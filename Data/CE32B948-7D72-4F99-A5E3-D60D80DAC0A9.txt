  
透水潛堤消波特性之研究 
 
Wave Damping Characteristics of a Submerged 
Porous Breakwater 
 
中文摘要  
    過去傳統上海岸保護工法是主要以防止海岸侵蝕災害發生及保護海岸安全為
目的，但是生態型海岸保護工法除了保護海岸，並應考慮到海岸開發與自然環境
保持平衡。潛堤是潛沒於水中，強迫大部分波浪於堤前碎波而削減波能，不僅如
此，還能養灘並確保砂不會因此流失，並可避免海岸灘線侵蝕，是一個值得推廣
的海岸消波結構物。潛堤為目前台灣保護海岸的重要設施之一，在保護環境生態
的要求下，潛堤的生態效果是值得研究的課題。 
    將傳統剛性結構物改為興建具有景觀、親水性海岸結構物也是未來海洋工程
發展趨勢，親水性結構物除了防災外，尚可以提供各種功能並具有海洋娛樂和增
加視野美化之功能。 
 
關鍵詞：生態工法、反射率、波力、透水潛堤、親水。 
Abstract 
  To prevent disasters from coastal erosion and to protect coastal security is the 
primary thing of traditionally near-shore protection. The main purpose of ecologically 
coastal protection method is to protect coastal, and it should be considered the balance 
between development behavior and coastal natural environment. A submerged 
breakwater has emerged as an effective structure, which can prevent beach erosion due 
to waves. A submerged offshore breakwater breaks large waves up and prevents 
nourished sand from flowing out. By now, the most important structure for protecting 
the coast of Taiwan is the use of submerged breakwater. To prevent the environment 
destruction, it is worth studying the effect with submerged breakwater. 
  To build harmonic promenade coastal structures instead of traditional structure is a 
new tread of marine engineering. Promenade coastal structures can provide various 
functions of disaster prevention, marine recreations, and landscape beautification to 
meet the increasing multipurpose requirements. 
 
Keywords：ecological engineering method, reflection coefficient, wave force,  
submerged porous breakwaters, promenade.

四、數值計算結果與討論  
一般傳統在計算孔隙板與透水潛
堤的問題，都是將孔隙板設計成直線型
的形狀，若將上方孔隙板改為曲面形或
圓弧形，則可增加景觀上的特殊視覺美
感，形成景觀、生態兩者兼具之結構
物。故本文將透水潛堤上方之孔隙板改
為圓弧形，並且設計成如圖 1 所示。 
 
固定的參數如下：透水潛堤的堤寬
與水深比(B/h)為 1.0。圓弧形孔隙板設
定為 1/4 橢圓，橢圓半長軸與水深比
(ra/h)為 1.0，半短軸與水深比(rb/h)為 0.5
的之圓弧形，透水潛堤的慣性係數(S)
為1.015，摩擦係數(f)為1.8，孔隙率(ε)
為 0.6，透水潛堤的堤高與水深比(hc/h)
為 0.5，形成一不突出水面的曲面孔隙
板，改變孔隙板透水參數 G 為 0.5、1.0
及 5.0，討論其反射率及波力變化。 
 
    由圖 6(a)中可知，反射率(|R|)隨著
kh 增大而下降，在 kh 大於 1.5 以後，
反射率值變化不大，漸趨於一穩定值，
其中下降幅度以 G=0.5 最大，並且明顯
看出透水參數 G 值愈大其造成反射率
亦會隨之增加。 
在波力方面，圖 6(b)顯示作用於下
方潛堤與後方不透水牆之無因次波力
會隨 kh 之增加而降低，而且改變 G 值
對無因次波力變化之影響並不太明顯。 
圖 6(c)、(d)、(e)則分別表示圓弧
型孔隙板無因次水平波力、垂直波力及
總合力。 
 
由圖顯示圓弧型孔隙板水平波力
遠小於垂直波力，當 G 值愈大則水平
及垂直波力均會顯著降低，而最大總合
力則發生在 kh 為 0.5 至 1.0 間。 
而圖 6(d)垂直波力最大的位置約
在 kh 為 0.75，無因次垂直波力為 0.8，
若提高 G 值至 1.0，則承受的垂直波力
就會降低至 0.57 左右。 
整體而言若 G 值越大則反射率會
上升，而 G 值越小則其所承受的波力
越大。

RESULTS AND DISCUSSION 
 
Effect of Submerged Permeable Breakwater Height 
 
The ratio of the submerged permeable breakwater height to the water 
depth ( hd / ) is one of the important parameters about the efficacy of 
the absorbing-type breakwater in this paper. In our numerical model, 
the value of the energy dissipation coefficient LE  used to estimate the 
wave energy dissipation by the absorbing-type breakwater, and it as a 
function of R  was described in Eq. 28. Thus the R  implies the 
energy. 
Fig. 5 shows the reflection coefficient R  as a function of kh  for 
different values of hd / . In this case, the value of porosity is 5.0=ε  
and 0.4/ =hB . Fig. 5 indicate that (excluding the ratio of hd / =1.0 
and 0.9) no matter how much the breakwater height ( d ) increase, the 
maximum and the minimum values of the reflections appear 
periodically. As 0→kh , the wavelength L  of incident wave is very 
long with respect to the water depth h . Thus the wave does not feel the 
porous plate and the submerged permeable breakwater, and a 
corresponding total reflection ( 0.1=R ) by an impermeable wall is 
noticeable. The variation of R  is seen to damp out for large values of 
kh  when hd /  approaches 1, that is, this case approaches the first 
limiting case (Madsen, 1983). However, the reflection coefficient 
versus kh  is periodical between 1 and 0 when hd /  equals 0, that is, 
this case becomes the second limiting case (Twu and Lin, 1990). The 
results of Twu and Lin (1990) indicate that the total reflection 
( 0.1=R ) is observed when the wave-absorbing chamber width B  and
