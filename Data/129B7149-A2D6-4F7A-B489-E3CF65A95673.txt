研究摘要(500 字以內)： 
在許多情況中,固體底材的表面的濕潤性調整是非常重要的,雖然已有許多
有關於高度拒水性表面的製造方法的文章被發表,但通常這些文章主要都是藉由
使用低表面能覆蓋結合特殊設計的表面型態而形成的,報導有關高度親水性的材
料文章卻很少,直到 1997 年二氧化鈦表面的超親水性行為才被發現,從此才開始
有一些親水性材料的研究計畫,雖然此項技術已完備,但是二氧化鈦表面的超親
水性狀態是需要在有自然光線中的紫外光存在下才會顯現,因此限制了室內產品
  
的結晶相,x射線電子光譜(XPS)分析顯示其含有N-Ti-O鍵結,而其組成成分氮的
含量x值為 0.369,薄膜經紫外光可見光(UV/VIS/IR)吸收光譜分析其吸收起始波長
範圍幾乎達可見光 500 nm之處。 
 
技術特點說明： 
本研究主要是以氮化鈦的靶材經磁控射頻濺鍍系統與通入氬、氮氣體,藉由
混合氣體比例的調整,濺鍍出氮氧化鈦化物(TiO2-xNx)材料,此材料具有親水防霧
的特性。 
 
可利用之產業及可開發之產品： 
防霧塑膠面板,防霧玻璃面板,抗菌玻璃面板, 抗菌塑膠面板等。 
 
推廣及運用的價值： 
本實驗計畫主要是針對聯享科技發展中的觸控式面板的防霧處理,此觸控式
面板的材料計有 PET, PC, PMMA,等,這些都是軟性塑膠材質,對於未來在軟性塑
膠電子元件的開發與製作,提供一種薄膜製作整合之可能性。

EXPERIMENTAL SECTION 
    TiO2-xNx thin films were deposited on 
PET substrates by sputtering a TiN target 
under a O2, N2, and Ar gas mixture. The thin 
films were deposited in plasma of argon, 
oxygen, and nitrogen with varying nitrogen 
content without heating temperature. To 
control the degree of nitrogen substitution, 
TiO2-xNx thin films were fabricated under 
various ratios of N2/O2 and N2/Ar gas flow 
and the total gas pressure was maintained at 
12 mTorr. The sputtering chamber was 
evacuated to a base pressure of 5×10-6 Torr. 
The power of ratio frequency input for TiN 
target was varied from 20 to 120 W.  
  The structure of TiO2-xNx thin films were 
determined by x-ray diffraction (XRD) and 
x-ray photoemission spectroscopy (XPS). 
X-ray diffraction (XRD) analysis with 
θ-2θ mode was performed under Cu Kα1 
irradiation at a scanning rate of 0.02° 2θ/s. 
The phase content of TiO2-xNx thin films 
was analysis from the integrated intensities 
of anatase (101) and rutile (110). The film 
thickness and morphology were determined 
using cross section and surface scanning 
electron microscopy (SEM) images, 
respectively. UV-Vis absorption spectra 
were recorded using a Hitach U-3010 
UV-Vis spectrophotometer. To investigate 
nitrogen states and to determine the degrees 
of x in the TiO2-xNx thin films, N 1s and O 
1s core levels were measured by x-ray 
photoemission spectroscopy (XPS). The 
photoinduce hydrophilicity was evaluated by 
measuring the water contact angle with a 
commercial contact angle meter while 
irradiating with UV (1 mW/cm2) and Vis 
light.

method. XRD pattern shows the (101) 
crystalline phase of anatase after PET was
