wastewater. The key role of the equipment to utilize 
the semi-conductor materials as the electrocatalytic 
electrodes for decolorizing printing wastewater. The 
degradation mechanisms and the factors of the 
electrocatalytic technique were investigated. This 
research takes several experimental stages including: 
1. quantity and quality investigation about printing 
wastewater such as COD, color, and conductivity； 2. 
operational parameters investigation about 
electrocatalytic technique such as voltage, electrode 
type, and pH； 3. decolorizing efficiency 
investigation during electrocatalytic treatment. 
Final stage is to find out the appropriate 
specification, treatment efficiency, and 
manufacturing cost. Accordingly, we can manufacture 
the prototype of the ＇auto-treatment equipment to 
decolorize printing wastewater＇. 
Due to the abundant organic pollutants in the 
printing wastewater, the OH radicals produced by the 
electrocatalytical system reacted with those organic 
pollutants. As a result, the pH value varied slightly 
and maintained around alkaline range. To the 
consideration of optimal electrical conductivity 
(EC), the EC of 5 mS cm-1 possessed better 
performance than that of other EC. Based on EC of 5 
mS cm-1 condition, the removal efficiency of TOC and 
color was 16% and 25%, respectively. Under the 
current density of 50 mA cm-2, the produced OH 
radicals, OCl- and HOCl could effectively degrade the 
TOC and color (the removal efficiency of TOC and 
color was 71% and 53%, respectively) in the printing 
wastewater. By two sets of electrodes, the removal 
efficiency of color could achieved 82%. When 
combining the activated carbons system, that could 
achieved 90%. For the proto type equipment, the color 
of printing wastewater could be removed effectively 
by 60-minute treatment.

電觸媒氧化有機物之降解機制主要分為直接氧化與間接氧化，如圖 1 所示。
直接氧化為施加能量至極板上，當污染物因吸附或擴散作用而接觸陽極時，經由
電子轉移反應而進行氧化反應；其間接氧化為施加足夠之能量於陽極板上時，價
電帶的電子躍遷至傳導帶而成為自由電子(e-)，且價電帶會產生帶正電之電洞
(h+)，進而形成電子-電洞對，此時水分子於價電帶之電洞碰撞反應，生成氫氧自
由基進行氧化反應【Herrmann, 1999】，如表 1 所示；另一間接氧化，是在系統
中添加中間物質例如 NaCl，在連續之電化學和分解反應中形成次氯酸與氯酸根
等物質氧化有機污染物，以達到礦化效果。【Güven et al., 2009；Chang et al., 
2009】。
