行政院國家科學委員會專題研究計劃成果報告 
以 Polycaprolactone diacrylate 寡聚物為交聯劑製備溫度/酸鹼感應性水
膠之性質研究 
Studies on preparation and properties of temperature/pH sensitive hydrogels 
containing polycaprolactone diacylate oligomer crosslinkers 
計劃編號：94-2216-E-036-012 
計劃期限：94 年 8 月 1 日至 95 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：李文福 大同大學化工系教授 
 
一、中文摘要 
    以NIPAAm、AA 及合成之
Polycaprolactone diacrylate 製備一系列溫度/酸
鹼感應性水膠，其中Polycaprolactone diacrylate
的合成是以acryloyl chloride 改質
Polycaprolactone diol。研究主要以改變單體組成
的比例來測量膠體膨潤度、熱性質、機械性質的
影響。結果顯示，隨著AA 含量增加膠體的膨潤
度上升，且CGTT 上升。而膠體的機械強度隨
著AA 含量增加降低。此系列膠體展現良好的溫
度及酸鹼敏感度。 
關鍵詞：PCL、NIPAAm、AA。di-acrylamide, 
N-Isopro。 
 
Abstract 
A series of temperature / pH sensitive 
hydrogels were prepared by copolymerizing 
N-isopropyl acrylamide (NIPAAm) , acrylic acid 
(AA), and polycaprolactone diacrylate, which was 
synthesized from polycaprolactone diol and 
acryloyl chloride. The effect of different monomer 
ratio of the NIPAAm / AA copolymeric hydrogel 
on the swelling ratio, thermal and mechanical 
properties was investigated. The results showed 
that the swelling ratio increased with an increase 
of the AA content in the copolymeric gel, but the 
critical gel transition temperature (CGTT) 
increased with the AA content. The compressive 
modulus also decreaed with AA content in the gel. 
The gels show good pH / temperature sensitive 
behavior.   
Keywords: PCL、NIPAAm、AA 
 
二、簡介 
水膠是一種交聯的高分子網目，在水中不會溶解
但會膨潤。水膠對於環境的刺激如：溫度、pH、
溶劑組成、光、離子強度等具有敏感性，並且會
對刺激做出應答[1-7]。由於感應性水膠具有上述
本質，因此可被應用於分離膜、生物感測器、人
工肌肉、化學閥以及藥物釋放機制[8]。NIPAAm 
之溫度感應特性，即是當水溫高於32 ℃時，網
目中水分子熱焓量增加，使水分子由氫鍵結態轉
成自由態，而從網目中釋放出，導致膠體收縮；
當溫度低於32 ℃時，膠體則會再度吸水膨潤，
此溫度稱為CGTT (critical gel transition 
temperature)。AA 帶有OOH，在鹼性環境中其
COOH 會解離為COO-及H+，COO-互相排斥使
得膠體網目撐開讓水分子進入，在酸中則相反，
故有酸鹼感應性。 
PCL，半晶型疏水性脂肪族高分子，其酯基可被
水解，具有良好的生物分解性及生物相容性。
Kweon 等人將PCL diol 末端改質為C=C 後交
聯成網目[9]。本研究使用PCLdA 作為交聯劑與
溫度感應性的單體NIPAAm 及pH 敏感性的AA 
交聯，期望 
做出一具有溫度、pH 感應性、可分解性及高相
轉移溫度(~37℃)的智慧型水膠材料，期望應用
在生醫工程與生物科技。

4.2 膨潤度及機械性質的影響 
Table (1) 顯示，不同單體比例影響膠體在去離
子水中的平衡膨潤度，結果指出，膠體的平衡膨
潤度隨著膠體中AA含量增加而提升。這是因為
其-COOH基團提供很強的水合能力，使得此複
合水膠更加親水。因此，膨潤度隨著膠體中AA
含量增加而提升。另外膠體強度藉由式(5)求得
之獲得。Table (2)為在PBS下膨潤之膠體的機械
性質結果，其中指出G值和有效交聯密度(ρ)隨著
膠體中AA含量增加而降低。膠體含水量大使得
膠體的結構脆弱，故其剪應模數(G)值越小。添
加越多親水性的AA減低了膠體間疏水段的作用
力，使得膠體內部越不緊密，讓交聯密度(ρ)越
小。膠體100/0則因無法壓碎，故其G及ρ值無法
量測。 
4.3 對膠體膨潤動力影響 
Fig4 為膠體在去離子水中的膨潤度對時間做
圖，n、K、D值藉由式(2)(3)算出且列在Table 2。
此結果顯示在去離子水中的膠體之擴散係數(D)
隨著膠體中AA 比例的增加而增加。因為AA 的
增加可提高膠體的親水性使得水較易擴散到膠
體中。按照Alfrey 等人的擴散機制分類，Table 2 
顯示結果指出此複合水膠的運輸機制為皆屬於
Fickian diffusion(n＜0.5)。 
4.4 膠體之酸鹼感應性 
Fig5 為改變單體比例在不同pH 下之膨潤度變
化，結果顯示，含AA 越多的膠體其感應性越
好，且在pH4~6 膨潤度的變化極劇
(50/50>75/25>85/10 
)，是由於AA 越多解離時COO-間互相排斥隨著
增加,更讓網目撐開讓水分子易進入膠體中。 
4.5 膠體的熱性質 
Fig6 為膠體的TGA 曲線圖，由圖顯示此系列膠
體於500℃時就即將燒完。而隨著AA 比例的提
升其耐熱性較好，是由於其含較多的羥基，需較
多能量來分解。 
4.6 膠體的溫度感應性 
Fig7 為膠體在不同溫度下的透光性，當溫度高
於CGTT 時NIPAAm 會排水成白色，此時透光
度為0，利用此特性測量NIPAAm/AA 系列膠體
CGTT。結果顯示，當100/0 有非常明顯示
CGTT=30℃，而隨著AA 的增加，其CGTT 越
往高溫偏移，但沒有特定的溫度點，這是因為添
加了AA 使得膠體在高溫時仍可藉由AA 的親
水性保水，故膠體的溫感性下降。 
 
五、結論 
只含NIPAAm 的膠體因以酯類寡聚物PCLdA 
作為交聯劑時增加了NIPAAm 疏水基的作用使
得水分子不易進入膠體中讓膠體極不含水。加入
AA 後其親水的COOH 基讓膠體變得較含水，
且隨著AA 量越多膨潤度越大，更提高水膠的

Synthesis of Dendritic Monomer 
The dendritic monomers, G0 and G1, were 
prepared via two procedures from scheme 1 and 
scheme 2: (1) Michael addition and (2) 
amidation where the reaction solvent was 
methanol [12]. The synthesized G0 and G1 were 
purified via vacuum distillation.
 
Preparation of IPN Hydrogels 
Dendritic monomers G0 and G1 with 
different molar ratio (0.25 mol%, 0.50 mol%, 
1.00 mol%) and 5 mol% of NMBA based on 
total monomer concentration content were 
dissolved in 10 mL of DMSO. To this solution, 
0.2 mol% AIBN, as an initiator was added, the 
mixture was immediately injected into the space 
between two teflon plates. Polymerization was 
carried out at 75°C for 4 days. After the gelation 
was completed, the gel membrane was immersed 
in 1.5 M HCl(aq) as a catalyst for 4 days to 
perform sol-gel reaction. Then the gel membrane 
was cut into disks and immersed in acetone and 
deionized water to remove unreacted monomers. 
Finally, the swollen gels were dried at room 
temperature for 1 day, and further dried in a 
vacuum oven for 1 day. The sample codes, feed 
compositions, and yields of the gels were listed 
in Table.1.

removed from the water bath, tapped with 
delicate task wipers to remove excess surface 
water, and weighed as the wet weight of gel (Ww) 
during the course of swelling at regular time 
intervals. The swelling ratio (Q) was calculated 
from the following equation:

CONCLUSIONS 
In this study, a dendritic monomer with 
amino group and triethoxy group in the end was 
synthesized, and a series of NIPAAm/dendritic 
monomer thermosensitive IPN hydrogels were 
prepared. The siloxane structure of the gel was 
carried out from catalysis of HCl(aq) and 
condensation of the ethoxy group. The swelling 
ratios and drug-loading amount were increase 
with the increase of dendritic monomer in the 
feed due to the increasing hydrophilicity of the 
gel. It was observed that the more dendritic 
monomer incorporated into gel, the higher the 
crosslinking density, Young’s modulus, and 
shear modulus of the gel due to the formation of 
siloxane structure. The increase in crosslinking 
density of the IPN hydrogels would cause the gel 
network more compact, and improve the gel 
strength.

Abstract 
Three different structures of organic-inorganic 
hybrid thermosensitive gels were prepared from 
N-isopropylacrylamide (NIPAAm), and N, 
N’-methylene-bis-acrylamide (NMBA) and 
tetraethoxysilane (TEOS) [N-IPN]; NIPAAm, 
3-(trimethoxysilyl)propyl methacrylate 
(TMSPMA) and TEOS [NT-IPN] ;and NIPAAm, 
TMSPMA and TEOS [NT-semi-IPN] by 
emulsion polymerization and sol-gel reaction in 
this research and the content of TEOS was 
controlled. The effect of different gel structures 
and TEOS content on the morphologies, 
swelling behavior, and mechanical properties 
was investigated. Results showed that the gel 
properties would be affected by the gel networks 
such as IPN or semi-IPN and existence of TEOS 
as the bridge between networks. The 
NT-semi-IPN gel had higher swelling ratio and 
faster diffusion rate because poly (NIPAAm) 
moiety in the semi-IPN gels was not restricted 
by networks. However, the IPN gels such as 
N-IPN and NT-IPN had better mechanical 
properties and lower swelling ratio and poor 
thermosensitivity due to the addition of TEOS 
into the gel system resulted in condenser link 
between organic and inorganic components. The 
morphology showed that IPN gels had partial 
aggregation (siloxane domain) and showed some 
condenser phases. Futhermore, the effect of the 
content of TEOS on the swelling ratios of the 
hybrid gels at different temperatures was also 
discussed. Swelling ratios of hybrid gels 
decreased as the content of TEOS increased.

the combination between organic and inorganic

just the interpenetration of networks and 
entanglement of polymers. Besides, by adding 
organic crosslinker NMBA to form gels with 
different crosslinking types: interpenetrating and 
semi-interpenetrating, discussed the effects of 
structure on thermosensitivity of NIPAAm gels. 
Unadding crosslinker NMBA caused the 
N-semi-IPN series unable to form a gel because 
organic component of NIPAAm cannot form 
water-insoluable crosslinking networks and 
generate covalent bonding with inorganic 
siloxane networks with absence of TMSPMA.

The effect of TEOS content on swelling ratios

Mechanical Properties 
Mechanical properties of hybrid gels were listed 
in Table 2. By compression model, the modulus 
G and effective ρx were estimated and calculated, 
and results showed that NT-IPN has highest 
strength and crosslinking density. This is 
because NT-IPN gel was composed of two 
crosslinking networks which combined with 
covalent bonding. Moreover, inorganic silane 
component also contributed to gel structure and 
resulted in higher gel strength. NT-semi-IPN gel 
was composed by only one network and 
polymers, its mechanical strength and 
crosslinking density were relatively low. 
Comparing N-IPN with NT-semi-IPN, although 
the former has two networks, there is no 
covalently bonded combination between these 
two networks, and consequently lower strength 
and crosslinking density than NT-semi-IPN. 
Considering the effect of TEOS content on 
modulus and crosslinking density, the result 
seemed that modulus and crosslinking density 
increase as the TEOS content increase when 
TEOS content higher than 10 mol%, but there 
existed no proportional relationship between 
TEOS content and mechanical properties.

行政院國家科學委員會專題研究計劃成果報告 
改變胺基矽烷含量進行 silica 之表面改質 
計劃編號：95-2221-E-036-027 
計劃期限：95 年 8 月 1 日至 96 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：李文福 大同大學化工系教授 
 
摘要 
改變矽烷與二氧化矽微粒之比例，以溶膠凝膠法(sol-gel method)製備一系列改質
之二氧化矽微粒，鑑定此改質微粒之化學特性，並量測改質二氧化矽微粒之粒徑
與介達電位等特性。改質後之二氧化矽微粒表面帶有胺基，進一步反應接載酸鹼
指示劑，並探討此改質二氧化矽微粒之諸性質，如粒徑、介達電位、酸鹼感應性
等。 
 
實驗部分 
實驗藥品 
Aerosil®200 (Degussa) (AE200)做為基材之親水性表面二氧化矽微粒，
3-aminopropyltriethoxysilane (ACROS) (APTEOS)做為二氧化矽微粒表面改質用
之矽烷，購買後直接使用。Methyl red (Wako) (MR)做為功能化二氧化矽表面微
粒接載之指示劑。Triphenyl phosphate (Acros) (TPP)做為接載methyl red之縮合
劑。Pyridine (Wako)與N-methyl-2-pyrrolidone (Tedia) (NMP)以氫化鈣(calcium 
hydride)乾燥 24 小時後，減壓蒸餾並保存在裝有分子篩的瓶中。 
 
以胺基矽烷改質Aerosil®200 (AE200) 
依 APTEOS/HCl/H2O/C2H5OH=x/4/16/6 之比例(x=0.5、1.0、1.5、2.0)，並以
APTEOS(mole)/AE200(g)=0.025, 0.050, 0.075, 0.100 之比例(表一)，配製矽烷溶
液，並秤取 AE200 分散其中，常溫攪拌 24 小時進行溶膠凝膠反應，反應完畢後，
過濾分散液，以乙醇與去離子水反覆清洗數次，再過濾。重複清洗與過濾的步驟
數次，洗去未反應之矽烷，置於 40℃烘箱中乾燥，製得一系列胺化之改質二氧
化矽微粒 AE200-APTEOS。 
 
接載 methyl red 製備功能化二氧化矽微粒 
設定酸鹼指示劑 methyl red 為 1mmol，取一三口圓底燒瓶，量取 2mL NMP、0.5mL 
pyridine、1mL triphenyl phosphate、0.27g methyl red、0.3g 改質 silica 
AE200-APTEOS_1.0，置入圓底燒瓶中，80℃攪拌反應 2 小時，反覆過濾、清洗數
次，40℃烘乾，製得功能化二氧化矽微粒 AE200-APTEOS-MR。

結果與討論 
二氧化矽微粒的改質 
本研究係以市售表面帶親水性氫氧基(-OH)之奈米級二氧化矽微粒Aerosil®200(簡
稱AE200)作為基材，透過溶膠凝膠法，將胺基矽烷在酸催化的條件下進行水解反
應，並與AE200 表面之氫氧基縮合形成矽氧烷鍵結(siloxane, Si-O-Si)，使帶有胺基
之矽烷接載於AE200 表面，將AE200 改質為表面帶有胺基之二氧化矽微粒。微粒
表面之胺基可以進一步與許多化合物進行反應，製備功能性之微粒，在本研究中
選擇具有酸鹼感應性之指示劑methyl red作為進一步改質，即透過微粒表面之胺基
與methyl red之羧酸基(-COOH)進行縮合而將methyl red接載於微粒表面，欲製備具
酸鹼感應性之二氧化矽微粒，其機制如圖一所示。其中AE200 與AE200-APTEOS
皆為白色微粒，而AE200-APTEOS-MR則為淡褐色微粒。

行溶凝膠反應達到二氧化矽微粒表面之改質，並比較酸鹼催化所製得之改質二氧

AE200-APTEOS、AE200-APTEOS-MR。由圖三(a)可得，以鹼催化之改質二氧化矽
微粒的粒徑比以酸催化者大，接載上 methyl red 後的粒徑差異更大，而酸催化後
製得之 AE200-APTEOS 之粒徑較 AE200 略小，可能原因如圖三(b)所示，酸催化
改質後之二氧化矽微粒之介達電位為 28.77mV，而 AE200 的介達電位值為
-16.46mV，改質後電性轉正且值較大，微粒間互斥作用力較大，因此微粒凝聚效
應較小，粒徑也較小，鹼催化製得之 AE200-APTEOS 微粒之介達電位為 4.90mV，
比起以酸催化製得微粒之介達電位值要小，微粒間互斥作用力較小，因此鹼催化
所製得之二氧化矽微粒粒徑，比酸催化製得之二氧化矽微粒粒徑要大，分散也比
較不均勻且容易沉降。

ABSTRACT:  
A series of organic-inorganic hybrid 
thermosensitive gels with three different 
structures and different content of 
tetraethoxysilane (TEOS) were prepared 
from N-isopropylacrylamide (NIPAAm), 
and N, N’-methylene-bis-acrylamide 
(NMBA) and tetraethoxysilane (TEOS) 
[N-IPN]; NIPAAm, 3-(trimethoxysilyl) 
propyl methacrylate (TMSPMA) as 
coupling agent and TEOS [NT-IPN]; and 
NIPAAm, TMSPMA and TEOS 
[NT-semi-IPN] by emulsion 
polymerization and sol-gel reaction in 
this study. The effect of TEOS content 
on the swelling behavior, mechanical 
properties, and morphologies of the 
present gels was investigated. Results 
showed that the properties of the gels 
would be affected by the gel networks 
such as IPN or semi-IPN, existence of 
TMSPMA as the bridge chain between 
networks, and content of TEOS. The 
NT-semi-IPN gel had higher swelling 
ratio because poly (NIPAAm) moiety in 
the semi-IPN gels was not restricted by 
NMBA network. However, the IPN gels 
such as N-IPN and NT-IPN had good 
mechanical properties and lower 
swelling ratio, but had bad 
thermosensitivity due to the addition of 
coupling agent, TMSPMA, into the gel 
system that resulted in denser link 
between organic and inorganic 
components. Increasing TEOS content 
would also reduce the thermosensitivity 
of the hybrid gels. The morphology 
showed that IPN gels had partial 
aggregation (siloxane domain) and 
showed some denser phases. 
Keywords: interpenetrating polymeric networks, 
thermosensitive, hybrid gel,
