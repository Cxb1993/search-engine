摘要 
本計畫已發展出一套能滿足台電現有機
組及獨立發電業不同運轉策略及不同運轉限
制條件的水火協調發電最佳調度策略，在兼顧
系統總發電成本以及使發電污染最佳化的考
量下，開發出「加強型差分進化法於同時考慮
閥門點現象與發電污染之水火協調系統最佳
排程」，可提供給台電公司及私人電力業界參
考。在以往的文獻中，雖然也有部份的研究加
入「發電污染」的考慮，但是只有少數的學者
將「閥門點現象」這個電力系統實際存在的情
況也一併加以評估。 
本計畫已整合「ε-限制技術」、「加強型
差分進化法」及「參數更新技術」，以整體多
目標規劃的策略，來分析水火協調系統排程問
題。本計劃所提出的演算法具有下列優點； 
(1) 考慮實際電力系統運轉操作情形（含閥門
點現象）的水火協調發電排程、 
(2) 開發出具有效能的加強型差分進化法僅
需使用少量族群來求解可降低運算時間
增加效率、 
(3) 可自動調適目標函數的處罰參數以處理
限制條件、 
(4) 考慮發電污染具多目標最佳化規劃。 
經過兩個例題測試比較，已驗證出本計畫
的確具有上述的優點，而且本計畫的成果應用
在水火協調系統排程規畫問題上，不僅具有更
優質的解答且完全滿足系統限制條件的要
求，確實具有效能及實用價值。 
 
關鍵詞：差分進化法、最佳排程，水火協調。 
 
 
 
Abstract 
This project has developed the enhanced 
differential evolutionary for optimal 
scheduling of hydrothermal coordination 
considering valve-point effects and power 
emission. Some investigations have been 
presented to solve the scheduling problem of the 
hydrothermal power systems. Unfortunately, few 
studies considered the effect of economic 
dispatch on emissions, and they failed to 
consider both of valve-point effects and 
emission level functions. This project is not only 
applicable for the practical operation of 
hydrothermal power systems, but also conforms 
to both of valve-point effects and emission 
constraints. This work is a useful solution to 
reduce cost and conform the standard of 
environmental pollution for the Taipower 
company and many private power enterprises. 
In view of the multi-objective programming 
for the best benefit, the proposed research has 
integrated the enhanced differential evolution 
(EDE), the multiplier updating and the ε
-constraint technique. This combination has the 
following merits: 
(1) applicable for the practical operation of 
hydrothermal power systems considering 
valve-point effects;  
(2) developing the efficient EDE and using 
only a small population for reducing 
computational time;  
(3) adaptive multiplier updating equipped to 
handle system constraints;  
(4) multi-objective programming considering 
emission.

電力經濟調度問題，其研究方法大致上可區分
為四個類型：(1)利用限制式方法[25, 27]、(2)
利用加權比重方法[24]、(3)分開個別計算方式 
[24]及(4)採用多目標整體規劃方式 [28]。 
對於上述第(1)個類型而言，此電力多目標
問題中的污染限制，被以「限制式方法」來處
理，將原本為多目標的型態降低為單目標問題
來討論。對於第(2)個類型而言，主要將各目標
函數各自乘上相當的「加權比重」值，來線性
組合成單一目標函數，如此做法對於具有不同
物理單位的各目標函數，可能會因為乘上一個
單位轉換因數，而喪失到其本身重要的特性及
精確度。對於第(3)個類型而言，「分開個別計
算方式」是對於求最大或最小值的個別目標函
數分別處理之，再整合出共同適合的答案。至
於第(4)個類型「採用多目標整體規劃方式」，
也正是本計畫將要採用的方法。 
 
1.4水火協調之傳統解法 
從電力系統運轉規劃的觀點而言，一個包
含有水力機組的系統遠較純火力機組的系統
來的複雜[29]。而水火協調必須同時處理水力
發電調度和火力發電調度二個問題。傳統的水
力發電調度的目的在於充分利用有限的水資
源，並在滿足系統負載需求及各種限制條件
下，訂出水力機組於各時段之發電量，以使得
系統總發電成本為最低。而當我們要分析電力
系統水火協調發電排程問題時，我們會遇到相
當多的限制條件式；如各水力發電機組的可用
水量、各發電機的總發電量必須滿足總負載量
及傳輸損失、各發電機的發電量上下限制、系
統備轉容量、勞工及維護成本…等等。目前大
部份求解水火力發電協調問題的方法，都是把
問題分解成水力及火力二個子問題[29]，火力
子問題中包含火力機組選定及經濟調度二項
工作、而水力子問題中則求解各水力電廠的調
度問題。因此需要一種能快速有效的演算法
則，來求解水火協調最佳排程、且完全滿足所
有電力系統限制條件的要求，才能符合「水火
協調系統排程」的最佳利益。 
 
1.5 水火協調系統之最佳排程(採用多目標整
體規劃方式-同時考慮閥門點現象與發電污
染) 
由上述的分析結果我們可觀察出，對於考
慮閥門點現象與發電污染的「水火協調最佳排
程」，以「多目標」的方式來規劃者卻是不多。
又水火協調的複雜度比單獨水力、或單獨火力
的經濟調度高出甚多。所以，本計畫採用一種
強健具有效率的全域最佳規劃法(加強型差分
進化法)來求解。因此，本計畫將以「水火協
調系統最佳排程」整體「多目標」規劃的角度，
來探索出符合整體利益，且完全滿足所有限制
條件的大型系統經濟調度最佳解，所以本計畫
將是一件相當重要、有意義且極具挑戰性的實
用性研究。 
 
2. 系統描述 
 
本計畫依真實的發電機成本曲線函數來
訂定目標函數，開發出考慮「閥門點現象」與
「發電污染」的「加強型差分進化法於同時考
慮閥門點現象與發電污染之水火協調系統最
佳排程」。水火協調系統排程通常均以某一時
間區段（Interval, 例如 tm小時）為單位，考慮
全部操作時間區域的整體最佳成本。本計畫將
分析兩個具有不同單位的目標函數-電力經濟
成本目標函數（Economic objective F1）與環境
污染目標函數（Environmental objective F2）。

5). 步驟五：遷移運算子（Migration Operator） 
為了有效增廣搜尋空間及降低族群數，

是否小於設定值，為此步驟執行與否的判斷標
準。

Improved Differential Evolution for Hydrothermal 
Power Systems 
Chao-Lung Chiang

Abstract—This paper develops an improved differential evolution 
based multi-objective approach for the optimal economic 
emission dispatch (EED) of the hydrothermal power system 
(HPS), considering non-smooth fuel cost and emission level 
functions. The improved differential evolution (IDE) equipped 
with an accelerated operation and a migration operation can 
efficiently search and actively explore solutions. The multiplier 
updating (MU) is introduced to handle the equality and 
inequality constraints of the HPS, and the İ-constraint technique 
is employed to manage the multi-objective problem. To show the 
advantages of the proposed algorithm, one example addressing 
the best compromise is applied to test EED problem of the HPS. 
The proposed approach integrates the IDE, the MU and the İ-
constraint technique, revealing that the proposed approach has 
the following merits - ease of implementation; applicability to 
non-smooth fuel cost and emission level functions; better 
effectiveness than the previous method; better efficiency than 
differential evolution with the MU (DE-MU), and the 
requirement for only a small population in applying the optimal 
EED problem of the HPS. 
Keywords- differential evolution; Hydrothermal power system. 
I. INTRODUCTION
Traditionally, in the short-term scheduling of a fixed water 
head, the variation of the net head can be ignored only for 
relatively large reservoirs, in which case power generation 
depends only on the discharge of water [1]~ [4]. The optimal 
EED problem of the HPS [5, 6], involves allocating generation 
among hydro-electric and thermal plants by minimizing the 
fuel cost and emission levels of thermal plants, simultaneously. 
However, these two objectives (economics and emissions) are 
in conflict with each other, since operating an HPS at the 
absolute minimum cost may cause emissions to exceed limits. 
The optimal EED problem of the HPS is in this case a non-
commensurable multi-objective constrained optimization 
problem, subject to various constraints on the hydraulic and 
power system network: equality constraints are power balance 
constraints at each subinterval of HPS and water availability 
constraints for every hydrothermal unit, and inequality 
constraints are set by the generating limits of thermal and hydro 
plants. 
Some investigations have been presented to solve the 
scheduling problem of the HPS. Powell’s hybrid method [2] 
has been applied to eliminate the divergence problem that 
arises when the Newton-Raphson method is used. A method 
[3] based on LU factorization of the matrix in Newton’s 
method formulation has been examined. It reportedly 
outperforms Powell’s hybrid method, but the matrix involved 
remains very large and so requires extensive computations. 
Another method [4] linearizes water availability constraints to 
solve the hydrothermal coordination equation. Unfortunately, 
these three cited works did not consider the effect of economic 
dispatch on emissions. They failed to consider non-smooth fuel 
costs and emission level functions, using approximations to 
limit complexity. Recently, Basu [6] modeled the HPS problem 
as a multi-objective problem and solved it using a weighted 
combination. Nevertheless, the weighting method linearly 
combined the objectives as a weighted sum. The objective 
function thus formed may lose significance because the various 
multiple non-commensurable factors are incorporated into a 
single function. This study employs the İ-constraint technique 
[7] to handle the multi-objective problem. 
DE was first proposed over 1994-1996 by Storn and Price 
at Berkeley as a novel approach to numerical optimization [8]. 
It is a population-based method and generally considered as a 
parallel stochastic direct search optimizer which is very sample 
yet powerful. The main advantages of DE are its capability of 
solving optimization problems which require minimization 
process with nonlinear and multi-modal objective functions. It 
employs a non-uniform crossover using parameters of child 
vectors to guide through the minimization process. The 
mutation operation with DE is conducted by arithmetical 
combinations which exploit the difference among randomly 
selected vectors, other than perturbing the genes in individuals 
with small probability. These special features make DE a 
precise, fast as well as robust algorithm. Furthermore, DE has 
been attracting more and more attentions from industry 
applications [9~ 11]. However, it has problems of converging 
onto local optimal solutions. Two additional operations of 
acceleration and migration are embedded in the IDE [11] to 
overcome such drawbacks for solving optimal control 
problems.

B. IDE 
In IDE [11], the one-to-one competition will have a faster 
convergence speed to give a higher probability toward a global 
optimum with much less computation time. It can use a small 
population in the evolutionary process to obtain a global 
solution. 
Generally, Evolutionary optimization involves two critical 
issues evolutionary direction and population diversity. As the 
evolutionary direction is effective in searching, the strong 
evolutionary direction can reduce the computational burden 
and increase the probability of rapidly finding an (possibly 
local) optimum. As population diversity is increased, the 
genotype of the offspring differs more from the parent. 
Accordingly, a highly diverse population can increase the 
probability of exploring the global optimum and prevent a 
premature convergence to a local optimum. These two 
important factors are here balanced by both employing the 
accelerated operation and migration in IDE that can determine 
an efficacious direction in which to search for a solution and 
simultaneously maintain an appropriate diversity for a small 
population. 
C. MU 
Michalewicz et al. [14] surveyed and compared several 
constraint-handling techniques used in evolutionary algorithms. 
Among these techniques, the penalty function method is one of 
the most popularly used to handle constraints. In this method, 
the objective function includes a penalty function that is 
composed of the squared or absolute constraint violation terms. 
Powell [15] noted that classical optimization methods include a 
penalty function have certain weaknesses that become most 
serious when penalty parameters are large. More importantly, 
large penalty parameters ill condition the penalty function so 
that obtaining a good solution is difficult. However, if the 
penalty parameters are too small, the constraint violation does 
not contribute a high cost to the penalty function. Accordingly, 
choosing appropriate penalty parameters is not trivial. Herein, 
the MU [16] is introduced to handle this constrained 
optimization problem. Such a technique can overcome the ill 
conditioned property of the objective function.

V. CONCLUSIONS
An efficient method for solving the optimal EED problem 
of the HPS was proposed. The proposed method yields optimal 
values, taking into account different objectives, and the pareto-
optimal set represents the trade-off between the objectives. The 
proposed approach integrates the IDE, the MU and the İ-
constraint technique, showing that the proposed algorithm has 
the following merits - ease of implementation; applicability to 
non-smooth fuel cost and emission level functions; better 
effectiveness than the previous method; better efficiency than 
DE-MU, and the need for only a small population. System 
simulations have shown that the proposed approach has the 
advantages mentioned above for solving optimal EED 
problems of the HPS.

轉限制條件的水火協調發電最佳調度策略，在兼顧系統總發電成本以及使發電

以往的文獻中，雖然也有部份的研究加入「發電污染」的考慮，但是只有少數
的學者將「閥門點現象」這個電力系統實際存在的情況也一併加以評估。 
本計畫已整合「ε-限制技術」、「加強型差分進化法」及「參數更新技術」，以
整體多目標規劃的策略，來分析水火協調系統排程問題。本計劃所提出的演算
法具有下列優點； 
(1) 考慮實際電力系統運轉操作情形（含閥門點現象）的水火協調發電排程、
