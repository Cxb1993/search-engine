多重電子施/受體對三苯胺系染料敏化太陽能電
池與電致色變元件之性能影響 
國科會計畫編號：NSC-98-2221-E-390-018 
楊乾信 
國立高雄大學 化工及材料系 
前言 
本研究利用 Vilsmeier-Haack reaction, Suzuki 
coupling reaction, Knoevenagel condensation 
reaction 等有機合成法製備有機染料。以 carbazole 
或 triphenylamine 當 電 子 施 體 ， 而 以
(rhodaniline-3-acetic acid 當電子受體。經光電性質
測試、電化學實驗、與理論計算，以及組裝成染
料敏化太陽能電池光伏元件。由實驗結果，可歸
納出元件的性能與電子施體/電子受體數目體有
密切關係，有最佳最佳性能存在於具有兩個電子
受數的染料。當額外增加電子施體 carbazole 的染
料並未呈現更佳的性能，，所組成的元件最佳的
效率可達 8.0%。相關成果已在 2010 化工年會
(APPCh) ，  7th Asian Conference on 
Electrochemistry in Kumamoto(ACEC 2010)國際研
討會發表。一篇 SCI 論文於 J. Phys. Chem. C, 
revised (2010). 一篇SCI論文於Renewable Energy, 
submitted (2010).  
 
研究目的 
    由於能源因素與地球環境的變化驅使更多科
學家著手於太陽能方面的研究，而染料敏化太陽
能電池(DSSC)，由於簡單的製程，不需昂貴設備
和高潔淨度的廠房設施，加上所需之材料便宜，
與目前廣泛生產的半導體固態模組來比，可大量
降低生產成本。其所需之材料包含二氧化鈦，碘
離子液體電解質，加上作為基材的 FTO(氟氧化錫)
導電玻璃，這在市場上都可以輕易的取得。但是
DSSC 在未來中要成為具商業競爭力，並普遍化
使用，還需有低成本染料合成，急需努力的研發。
本研究目的利用多種有機合成法製備多重電子施
體/電子受體全有機染料。探討其電子施體/電子受
體對敏化太陽能電池之光電轉化效率的影響。 
文獻回顧 
  Dye-sensitized solar cells (DSSCs) have also 
received attention for transferring clean solar energy 
into electricity over the past decade.1,2 The 
inorganic-organic hybrid DSSCs have been 
extensively investigated for the areas of designing 
more efficient dyes and electron mediators, 
fabricating better nanostructured films, and more 
deeply understanding the interfacial charge-transfer 
process. In a DSSC device, light is absorbed by the 
dye anchored on the TiO2 surface and then electrons 
from the excited dye inject into the conduction band 
of the TiO2, generating electric current, while the 
ground state of the dye is regenerated by the 
electrolyte to give efficient charge separation. Thus, 
the dye in DSSCs is essential for efficient light 
harvesting and electron generation/transfer. The 
electrolyte, containing a redox couple I3-/I- mediator, 
is injected into the slit between the anode and the 
catalytic Pt counter electrode. A dye sensitizer needs 
efficient charge injection with photoexcitation.2 To 
obtain high conversion efficiencies, it requires that 
the photogenerated electrons flow into the oxide 
film with minimal losses to interfacial 
recombination.3 
  Ruthenium-complexes sensitizers exhibit efficient 
charge injection after photoexcitation,2 which is 
grafted onto the semiconductor through anchoring 
groups, e.g. carboxylate that bind strongly to the 
oxide by coordination of surface titanium ions. On 
the other hand, room temperature ionic liquid-based 
electrolytes have been developed to contribute in 
this field.4 In this study, we synthesized 
polyurethane ionomers (PUIs) which were prepared 
pseudo-solid (gel-type) electrolyte in DSSCs.  
實驗方法與步驟 
   2.1.1. 4-Formyltriphenylamine (FTPA)   
A Vilsmeier reagent was first prepared as follows: 
phosphorous oxychloride (3.13 g, 20.38 mmol) was 
added dropwise to N, N-dimethylformamide (DMF, 
80 mL) in ice bath, and then the temperature was 
risen to 25oC and kept mixing for an additional 1 h. 
At 25oC, triphenylamine (5 g, 20.38 mmol) was 
slowly added over 15 min to the above Vilsmeier 
reagent and stirred overnight. The reaction was 
quenched by the addition of cooled aqueous sodium 
acetate solution (3M) in ice bath. The precipitate 
product was collected and recrystallized with 
ethanol. Yield: 4.57 g (82%).

結果與討論 
X-ray diffraction (XRD) patterns of the 
titanate-derived TiO2 are shown in Fig. 1(a), 
revealing almost pure anatase phase. 
Triphenylamine-rhodanine-3-acetic acid (TPAR) and 
carbazole-triphenylamine- rhodanine-3-acetic acid 
(CTPAR)-based organic dyes were synthesized and 
employed in dye-sensitized solar cells (DSSCs). We 
used several organic synthesis methods. Different 
numbers of aldehyde groups were first linked on the 
TPA molecule by using the Vilsmeier-Haack 
reaction. Then, two different CTPAR-based 
structures, which possess different numbers of 
aldehyde and carbazole groups, were obtained using 
the bromination of aromatic derivatives and the 
Suzuki coupling reaction,. Finally, these organic 
dyes (TPAR/CTPAR) were obtained both through 
nucleophilic addition and dehydration between the 
aldehyde groups on TPA/CTPA and the methylene 
of rhodanine-3-acetic acid using the Knoevenagel 
condensation reaction. Scheme 1 shows the 
synthetic sequences of TPAR and CTPAR. Fig. 2(A) 
shows the absorption spectra of as-synthesized dyes 
in ethanol. Table 1displays this data. TPAR dyes 
exhibit three distinct absorption bands at around 300, 
335, and 470 nm, respectively. The absorption peaks 
at around 335 nm which corresponds to the Π→Π* 
electron transition. Strong absorption peaks at 
around 470 nm can be assigned to an intramolecular 
charge transfer between the TPA-based donor and 
the rhodanine-3-acetic acid, providing efficient 
charge-separation at an excited state. Note, in Fig. 
2(B), that the absorption spectra of the adsorbed 
TPAR2/CTPAR2 dyes on the TiO2 surface exhibit an 
obvious red shift in comparison with those of the 
TPAR1/CTPAR1 dyes. Based on the Tauc relation 
[23], the energy gap (Eg) can be obtained by plotting 
(α hν )2 vs. hν  and extrapolating the linear 
portion of (αhν)2 to zero as shown in Fig. 3. The 
lowest energy band gap among these dyes indicates 
the red shift of the CTPAR2 dye. The two anchoring 
groups (rhodanine-3-acetic acid) in the CTPAR2 dye 
adsorbed on the TiO2 surface resulted in a more 
efficient excitation of electrons and led to a lower Eg. 
On the other hand, the Eg values of the CTPAR dyes 
were lower than those of TPAR dyes at the same 
concentration of rhodanine-3-acetic acid were, 
implying that carbazole, the electron donor, 
contributed to the lowering of the energy band gap 
in the dye molecules. In addition, the dyes with two 
rhodanine-3-acetic acids possessed lower Eg values. 
Cyclic voltammetry is a preliminary characterization 
technique to determine the redox properties of

[24] obtained experimental values for the highest

結論 
We have synthesized a new class of dyes 
featuring carbazole and triphenylamines as electron 
donors, and rhodanine-3-acetic acids as electron 
acceptors. These dyes were designed with differing 
contents of rhodanine-3-acetic acid (acceptor) in 
their molecular structures to exhibit differing 
photovoltaic performance. The performance 
achieved for the DSSCs constructed from this new 
series dyes is of significance. Incorporation of one 
carbazole and two rhodanine-3-acetic acids on a 
TPA molecular frame (CTPAR2 dye) was 
demonstrated to be beneficial in retarding electron 
transfer from TiO2 to the oxidized dye or electrolyte 
and to enhance the charge transfer efficiency in the 
excited state. It is likely that incorporation of 
appropriate numbers of extra electron donors and 
electron acceptors in the TPA structure will further 
improve the performance of the cells.

[成果所代表之意義]  
合成了含有一個額外的電子供體（咔唑）和電子受體（ rhodaniline - 3 -乙酸）對
triphenylamines（TPA）的有機染料。 
[成果所代表之價值]  
光物理，電化學，和理論計算方法，這些化合物進行了分類效應。納米二氧化鈦為基礎的
染料敏化太陽能電池（DSSCs）都採用這些染料分子作為捕光增敏劑。電池的整體效率 4.64
％，相對於一順二（氰硫基）-雙（2,2'-聯吡啶）-4,4'-二羧酸釕（Ⅱ）（N3 型染料）敏化劑
（7.83％）於製作與量測相同的條件下。在提高效率，咔唑電子捐贈的染料分子起到了關
鍵作用。兩個 rhodaniline - 3 -乙酸團體似乎有助於電荷由激發染料分子轉移傳達到 TiO2
導帶，導致更高效率的光敏染料。 
[影響或進一步發展之可能性]  
電化學阻抗支持這種染料的電荷轉移對提高在二氧化鈦的電子注入速率。計算結果亦證實
此染料化合物顯示出較大的電荷轉移效率的電子激發態。
