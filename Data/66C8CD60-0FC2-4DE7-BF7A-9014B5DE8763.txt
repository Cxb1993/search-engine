牙科瓷與純鈦金屬介面之性質研究 
Investigations of the interfacial properties between dental porcelain 
and pure titanium 
計畫編號﹕NSC 95-2221-E-027 -035 
執行期限﹕95 年 8 月 1 日至 96 年 7 月 31 日 
計畫主持人： 楊永欽 
國立台北科技大學 材料及資源工程系

中文摘要 
牙科瓷與純鈦介面間經高溫燒製會生成氧化鈦，此氧化鈦生成物於後續燒製
過程有溶入牙科瓷結構之現象。此外，介面處之牙科瓷中有純Sn與純Si之析出，
顯示活性較高之鈦原子會擴散至牙科瓷中，與其組成之SnO2與SiO2產生氧化還
原反應，導致鈦本身因而氧化並間接促使介面處之氧化鈦成長。隨著燒製時間
增加，介面之氧化鈦厚度與牙科瓷內之孔洞量亦增加，瓷層與基材之鍵結強度
與牙科瓷之內聚強度則下降。隨燒製時間增加，破裂起始有從牙科瓷轉移為介
面破壞之傾向，且介面破壞面積亦隨之增加，說明介面強度不佳為整個結構之
弱點。此外，其介面破壞面經分析後發現破壞發生之位置主要位於氧化鈦與純
鈦基材介面處，甚至是少量基材鈦表層。這主要是基材表層鈦固溶氧導致純鈦
表層強度劣化，以及介面處因氧化物形成留下缺陷，伴隨燒製過程壓縮應力作
用使得牙科瓷層與純鈦間產生裂紋，此為導致兩者間介面強度不足之主因。 
 
關鍵詞：牙科瓷、純鈦基材、界面性質、氧化層。

貴金屬低耐蝕性亦佳。不過主要的問題仍尚待解決，也就是鈦金屬在高溫狀態
下容易氧化的現象。 
牙科陶瓷與鈦金屬間的連結包含了機械固持與化學反應，其中機械固持主
要是增加基材的表面粗糙度，而化學反應的產生比率則與基材的表面積大小有
關。通常讓基材的表面越粗糙就可以獲得較大的接觸表面積，雖然如此，重要
的是要讓陶瓷能潤濕基材表面才會與基材接觸近而形成化學鍵結。由Pask等人的
研究指出，金屬表面氧化物的存在有助於陶瓷潤濕金屬基材[3]，因為陶瓷與金
屬兩者的物性與化性相去甚大，因此陶瓷很難去潤濕純金屬的表面。另外，Jach
等人也推斷出金屬與玻璃(陶瓷)間在沒有中介氧化層存在的話則不會有接觸的
情形[4]，由此可以看出陶瓷與鈦金屬間氧化物的存在是必要的。Wanger等人曾
在燒覆牙科陶瓷之前預先將金屬基材給氧化，結果卻是增進了鍵結強度[6]，這
是因為預先氧化的氧化層表面比較粗糙，因而增加陶瓷層跟金屬基材間化學鍵
結形成的機會。雖然有相關研究曾提及陶瓷與鈦金屬間的介面反應可能發生的
現象[6-8]，但沒有直接的證據顯示其介面化學反應的形成，因此進一步的研究
仍須進行。 
陶瓷層與鈦基材間的鍵結行為深受氧化鈦的影響，因為隨著燒製溫度的增
加，仍舊會導致界面鈦金屬氧化現象變得顯著，因而使得界面鈦金屬氧化物變
厚。事實上，除了溫度升高會造成鈦金屬氧化層明顯變厚之外，隨著燒製時間
的增加，陶瓷與鈦金屬介面氧化物也會增厚而影響其鍵結強度。雖然大部分的
研究都指出鈦過度氧化會使其與牙科瓷間的鍵結強度(bonding strength)下降
[6,9-12]，還有指出燒製後之鈦與牙科瓷間的破裂主要是發生在形成氧化物的介
面上[9,12-16]，然而，另有研究指出氧化鈦的存在是有助於鈦與牙科瓷間鍵結的
形成[10,11,14]，因此，氧化鈦在牙科瓷與純鈦基材間所扮演的角色是需要進一
步探討。在牙科復形體燒製的過程中，是採取將復形體由高溫直接空冷的方式，
此過程必然會造成復形體存在著殘留應力。而殘留應力的存在不論是拉伸應力
(tensile stress)或壓縮應力(compressive stress)都會對牙科瓷與鈦基材的結合造成
影響[17,18]其中尤其是壓縮應力對於塗層與基材的結合有負面的影響。 
本研究將使用真空燒製法將牙科瓷披覆於純鈦金屬上，探討牙科瓷與純鈦
金屬介面反應行為與鍵結特性，藉由微結構的觀察、介面組成的定量分析以及

2-3 牙科瓷層楊氏係數的量測 
計算殘留應力時將會用到牙科瓷的楊氏係數值，然而不同燒製溫度下的牙
科瓷層有不同的孔洞體積分率，將造成楊氏係數有所不同，因此，此部分試驗
將求出牙科瓷經燒製後真實的楊氏係數值。牙科瓷層楊氏係數量測使用之試片
尺寸為50mm × 10mm × 3mm。將試片以腐蝕的方式去除純鈦基材，此時即可取
下單獨的牙科瓷層，接著依據ASTM E-855所規範的標準三點彎曲試驗在萬能試
驗機中來進行。三點彎曲試驗是利用在不同的外加荷重下，準確量測牙科瓷層
的撓曲度(deflection)，經由所得到荷重-位移的關係即可求得楊氏係數，計算方

4. 結  論 
牙科瓷與鈦金屬間的鍵結強度隨著燒製時間的增加有下降的現象，同樣的
牙科瓷層本身的內聚強度也會因燒製時間增加而下降，主要是因為長燒製時間
下牙科瓷層內部孔洞含量增加的緣故。牙科瓷與鈦金屬間的破裂是內聚破壞與
界面破壞的混合型，其中主要以界面破壞支配著破裂特性以及鍵結強度，介面
破壞主要是發生在氧化鈦與純鈦基材界面，甚至包含表層的鈦為主，且隨著燒
製時間的增加，破裂發生在表層鈦的現象有增加的情形。鈦基材端之界面破壞
表面的成分分析顯示表層鈦有高含量的氧存在，以致表層鈦因而脆化，且隨著
燒製時間的增加而變得顯著。牙科瓷與鈦金屬間的破裂主要是跟氧化鈦生成過
程中產生的應力以及燒製冷卻過程在牙科瓷內形成的壓縮應力的作用有關，加
上表層鈦固溶氧造成機械強度劣化所致，而牙科瓷層內聚強度的下降以及界面
處氧化鈦厚度的增加並非是造成鍵結強度下降的主因。

Bond Strength Measurement 
The bond strength of the HACs was tested using a standard 
adhesion test (ASTM C-633). Each test specimen was an 
assembly composed of a substrate fixture, to which the HACs 
(180 ± 20 µm thick) were applied, and a loading fixture. The 
facings of the loading fixtures were grit-blasted and attached 
to the surface of the HACs using a special adhesive glue 
(METCO EP-15) with an adhesive strength of about 60 
MPa.The assembly was held perpendicularly and placed in an 
oven at 180°C for 2 hours. After curing and hardening of the 
bonding glue, the assembly was loaded in an Instron machine 
for measurement of the tensile bond strength. 
 
Results and Discussions 
 
Characteristics of HA coating 
The values of the roughness of HACs through six kinds of 
vacuum heating temperatures and the as-sprayed HAC are 
listed in Table 1; there is no apparent difference of roughness 
of HACs both before and after the heat treatment. The surface 
features of HACs sprayed under various conditions did not 
show significant variations of morphology, as show in Fig. 2. 
Figure 3 indicates the cross-sectional characteristics of the 
microstructure of seven HAC specimens and shows the 
obvious lamellar structure, i.e., micro laminar cracking. After 
vacuum heating, the HA coatings became well-bonded to the 
Ti-substrates, well except for the HAC900 sample, as shown 
in Fig. 3c.

 Residual Strain of HA Coatings 
By measuring the variation of length and curvature of the free 
HA coatings, we can measure their residual strain levels 
(Table 1 and Fig. 6). It is observed from Fig. 6 that the as-
sprayed HA coating exhibited compressive residual strain and 
the HAC400, HAC500 and HAC600 were exhibited tensile 
residual strain. The higher heating HAC700, HAC800 and 
HAC900 coatings exhibited compressive residual strain. In 
previous study [7], plasma-sprayed HA coating (HAC25) 
showed the compressive residual strain due to the complex 
coating deposition and cooling process during the plasma 
spraying. However, the consideration of the status pertaining 
to the residual stress of post-vacuum heating coating and 
substrate is much simple because during the process the 
coating and the substrate were under the same condition, after 
the post-processed specimen cooling down from the heating 
temperature to the room temperature, the status concerning the 
residual stress of specimens could be simply explained by the 
CTE mismatch. Theoretically, CTE of HA bulk is larger than 
that of Ti-alloy substrate [7]; therefore, according to the 
difference of CTE, the HAC will be induced a tensile stress as 
the specimen is through the post heating. But the porosity, 
crack and low crystallinity of plasma sprayed coating result in 
the difference between the CTE of HAC and the theoretical 
value of CTE of HA bulk. So the practical CTE of plasma 
sprayed HAC must be realized first if CTE were utilized to 
explain the generation and status of the residual stress.

The dilatation curve of HACs and Ti-substrate during heating 
is shown in Fig. 7, the average CTE of each temperature 
interval were shown in Table 2. Figure 7a and Table 2 
indicated that the coefficient of thermal expansion (CTE) of 
Ti6A14V substrate was between 9.9~10.5×10-6 K-1, higher 
than the theoretical value. Figure 7b showed the dilatation of 
HAC volume during heated from room temperature to 900ºC, 
which was separated into three intervals of temperatures. In 
the interval 100~400ºC (I) showed that HAC expanded with 
increasing temperature and according to the definition of the 
coefficient of thermal expansion (CTE) α: (δL/Lo) = α × ∆T, 
the value of the slope in Fig. 7 which could seen as CTE was 
14.24×10−6 K−1. In the interval 400~700ºC (II) showed that 
non-linear relation between the dilatation and the temperature; 
as the temperature was higher than 550ºC, the volumes of 
HACs were contracted with the increasing temperature. So we 
inferred that the interval II might be the major range of 
crystallization of the amorphous phase in HAC. As for the 
interval III (temperatures higher than 700ºC), the coating was 
subjected by the thermal expansion rather than the crystallo-
contraction and the curve of CTE represented the positive 
linear relation with the increasing temperature, the slope of 
which was 8.5×10−6 K−1. Comparing the slopes of the interval 
I and II, we confirmed the diversity of CTEs in accordance 
with the crystallinity of HA coatings.
