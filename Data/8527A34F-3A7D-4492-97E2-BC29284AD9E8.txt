2有用的電力，也無疑是消能設施的最佳應
用。如張等(2007)利用慣性搖擺結構的波浪
能吸收轉換法進行波能系統的研究，以理論
分析及實驗之方式探討結構中水中載體所
受到的波浪力與水動力。對目前國際上已成
功開發的波浪能技術主要可以分為幾大
類：垂盪式浮體發電裝置、垂盪與搖擺式浮
體發電裝置、縱搖式發電裝置、振盪水柱式
發電裝置與衝動式發電裝置，如 Duck、
CPelamis、OWC、 FROG、 PS FROG、
TAPCHAN及MOWC等。
波浪形成的主要動力為風，而風是來自
太陽對地球上溫度變化所產生的大氣對流
作用，因此利用波浪發電等於是間接的使用
太陽能。波浪發電即將波能蓄存起來加以利
用，由於波浪的能量與波長、波高及週期有
極密切的關係，同時波浪的能量與波高的平
方成正比，而波浪發電除了類似Wavegen、
PWP(Pelamis Wave Power)、 OPT(Ocean
Power Technology)等較大的研究與開發單
位外，在正式的商業用途上，迄今尚未能大
規模的開發利用，主要原因在於波浪本身的
不確定性，波浪的波高與週期等會因時空背
景之不同，如日、夜以及季節的變化等而有
極大之差異性，屬於穩定性較低、較不可靠
之能源，以台灣四周海域來說，北部沿海地
區大約在 9 月後受東北季風的影響才會出
現平均波高 1m以上的波浪，南台灣也只有
在6到9月間較可能出現平均波高較大的波
浪，且均集中在少數幾個月份，其他時間與
地區大多是風浪不大的情況。因此本研究擬
以迴力原理探討如何能在有限的波動條件
下利用棘輪裝置(ratchet wheel)將不同大小
之波浪運動轉換成為微小的變位蓄存起來
轉為彈力，再轉換成機械能來帶動發電機發
電。利用各種方法來增加有限環境與條件下
波浪之發電量已然成為近年來在國內外相
當受重視的一個再生能源之研究議題，如
Su(2002)、Thorburn(2005)、Orer(2007)等。
根據褚(2005)之內容描述及 Elliott(1996)：目
前世界上對於波能設備的相關研究有很大
的部份是英國所發展提出的 (Estelle,
2007)，而在英國能源部的研究報告指出，
未來對於大型近海的波能發電裝置在經濟
上的效益是不被看好的，而研究工作應集中
在小型組件的岸邊發電裝置上，並表示波力
發電最好是小規模的選擇在岸邊上或近岸
區進行。
本研究計劃應用迴力原理 (restoring
force) 來提昇波浪在微弱波動運動時之發
電效率以及效果，探討此法實際應用到不良
環境條件下之波浪發電或風力發電上之可
行性，將波浪條件不佳無法發電的少數能量
集中發電，所謂積少成多，目的在增加波浪
之功率以產生較大的效益。而迴力車在有限
的距離下往後拉回一小段距離後即可產生
極大往前的衝力與速度，故本研究計劃所稱
『迴力原理』即『迴力車原理』，為利用彈
性物質如彈簧或發條之回復力，來增加波浪
平均波高較小時的發電量。利用迴力之方式
先累積部份能量後，轉以較強勁之力量(彈
力或稱回復力)快速旋轉來產生電流，此時
瞬間所暴發出來的電量是遠較先前來回擺
動的搖擺式發電方式的好幾倍。本文先前研
究利用上述之原理，以迴力設備來進行水工
模型試驗，在斷面造波水槽內以水工模型試
驗，利用規則波與不規則波進行試驗，探討
不同波浪條件在迴力設備固定彈性模組條
件下，所引起的迴力對波浪能之產生與週期
性擺動之發電量比較，探討其增益性(參閱
Shih 等，2010)。本研究繼續以密合度較高
之機械設計模型與儀器來進行水工試驗，減
少系統之能量損失以求的更精確的波浪功
率(wave power)。

四、結論
本文以規則波與不規則波來進行應用
迴力原理配合棘輪裝置提昇波浪發電效率
之試驗研究，經試驗結果可得以下之結論：
1.本文試驗裝置採用較高精度之棘輪
裝置，雖因採用的塑膠葉片較輕質飛機木重
許多，但仍可測得相同條件下迴力原理所發
揮之提昇功效，其中短週期波雖然能量小，
單位時間通過發電裝置的波數較多，利用棘
輪可累積較多的能量，發電量之堤升效率反
而比長週期時大。另外，波高較小的波浪較
難推動葉片來發電，故搖擺式隨著波浪擺
動，每一次所產生的都是微小的發電量，而
利用棘輪將每一次所產生的微小能量累積
起來以較大的力矩快速的回轉來產生較大
的電量，反而比波高較大時之堤升效率大。
2.規則波試驗之平均發電結果較不規
則波大，主要係因不規則波所包含不同條件
之成份波，但在 H1/3=3cm、 2h/g <1.5之
部份可能因出現波高或波長較長的成份
波，亦即大於規則波 H=3cm 之成份波，故
得到的提昇效益也相對比較大。
3.本試驗雖以水輪車來進行試驗，但重
點不在水輪車之葉片結構，主要係探討迴力
原理配合棘輪裝置之提昇效益，利用迴力原
理技術來創造有利的波浪發電環境，不只適
用於搖擺式發電裝置，亦可實際應用於目前
已開發或開發中之各種發電設備來提昇發
電裝置之適用波浪範圍，應用到不利波浪發
電的不良環境條件下之波浪發電，可有效提
昇該系統在波浪條件較差時之環境的發電
量。
計劃成果自評
本計畫應用迴力原理(restoring force)
配合棘輪技術(ratchet wheel)提昇波浪在微
小波動運動時之發電效率以及效果，將波浪
不利於發電時之少數能量以棘輪之技術累
積成較大的變位以便集中發電，利用迴力車
之彈性以迴力之方式產生較大的效益。利用
斷面水槽來進行試驗，試驗模型採用浮式水
輪車波能發電裝置分別利用自由搖擺發電
及引用迴力原理發電之方式進行試驗，經水
工試驗後已初步檢驗此構想的可能性與正
確性，結果可以發現無論是利用規則波或不
規則波試驗，利用迴力原理時，小型發電機
所產生之電壓值都遠較相同波浪條下利用
來回擺動所產生之電壓值大，且波浪條件愈
差的環境下提昇發電之效益越好。本研究之
成果可實際應用於目前已開發或開發中之
各種發電設備來提昇發電裝置之效益，主要
應用到不利波浪發電的不良環境條件下之
波浪發電，有效提昇該系統在波浪條件較差
時之環境的發電量。故本研究成果與原先計
劃申請之預定達成目標大致相符，成果並將
於第 32屆海洋工程研討會中發表。

ABSTRACT  
 
The feasibility of extending the technique in practice to the 
development of wave power generation in an environmentally 
inadequate field are investigated by the application of restoring force 
theory to the increasing of wave power generation under the 
circumstances of weak fluctuations. Hydraulic model tests are carried 
out in the wave flume with regular waves as well as irregular waves to 
investigate over the efficacy of the wave power generation utilizing 
restoring force through elastic spring with ratchet wheel. The feasibility 
of the restoring device has been rudimentary confirmed by using a 
small-scale motor, tentatively, the ratchet wheel was adopt as a 
accumulation device to hoard up the amplitude of wave fluctuations 
with feeble energies to great potential, and the clockwork spring of a 
“pull-back car” then act as restoring device with sudden repercussions, 
which may create a greater quantity of wave power, the results are 
compared with that generate reciprocated by conventional oscillating 
movement, this improvement accrues at least twice power as much as 
that of original manner (reciprocation), the technique could be applied 
to the existing equipments for power generations to obtain a higher 
performance evaluation, especially in inadequate environment such as 
waves fields with weak fluctuations. The feasibility of the technique is 
applicable in practical applications to the development of existing wave 
power device, e.g. wave power and/or wind power generator.

INTRODUCTION 
 
Under the tendency towards environmental consciousness, clean 
regeneration energy such as solar power, wind power and ocean energy 
has becomes one of the research topics that are worth probing into. 
Considerable quantities of investigations in oceanographic engineering 
are conventionally invest in coastal protection branch, coastal hazard 
mitigation and their physical properties, such as wave breaking, wave 
absorbing or dissipating devices and constructions, ecological 
engineering method, etc. As a native proverbial saying ‘Water can float 
a boat, so can it capsize it’. It is time for us to think contrarily as 
‘Though water may capsize a boat, so can it floats’, i.e. to turn the 
hazardous wave power into useful renewable power might be the best 
way of coastal protection. Waves in the ocean contains a large amount 
of energies, this alternative energy source is one of the most typical 
representatives renewable power, how to develop effectively is 
therefore a popular topics for discussion. Wave energy is an alternative 
energy form of solar energy, which is proportional to the square of its 
height, as far as we may concern, such energy are not yet extensively 
developed as the result of its uncertainty, even though there are a 
considerable quantities of researches into the exploitation of wave 
power and are now in a pre-commercial stage, the nature and extend of 
environmental considerations remain uncertain, the wave heights and 
periods varies under different conditions, e.g. the dark night and 
daytime, winter and summer, etc., also, the circumstances of weak 
fluctuations are not feasible to build a large wave power plant, which 
are not able to produce sufficient motion and/or force to pump high-
pressure hydraulic oil for power generation. Hence, many a little makes 
a mickle, how to make the best used of waves under limited fluctuation 
by the accumulation of wave with feeble energy to great potential, 
which may drive the generator to generate electricity, and has become 
an interesting research topic of a renewable energy in recent years. 
There are presently many successfully developed wave energy 
converters demonstrated by pioneering scientists, such as Duck, 
CPelamis, OWC, FROG, PS FROG, TAPCHAN & MOWC, LIMPET, 
SeaFlow, Stringray, Archimedes Wave Swing, Pico, Oceanlinx, etc. 
(see João, 2008) and may be simply consolidated to four main 
technologies as OWC (Oscillating Water Column), AWS(Archimedes 
Wave Swing), Pelamis WEC(Wave Energy Converter) and Wave 
Dragon, the generation was achieved by hinged joints which the heave 
and sway motion pumps high pressure hydraulic oil. However, 
according to Elliott (1996), it was pointed out that in the UK’s 
programme, a smaller scale options have turned out to be more 
commercially relevant compared with large scale projects. The low 
cost-benefit of electricity acquired from wave power will only be 
competitive when the cost of the power generation is substantially 
reduced, small scale renewable generation may be the most cost-
effective way to bring electricity to remote villages. New innovations 
of different small hydro technologies are summarized and discussed. 
Although small hydro power devices may be a tendency to renewable 
energy generation, improvements and upgrading can still be 
worthwhile. Generally speaking, waves with great fluctuations are more

The laboratory testing were carried out in a 16m wave flume located at

was tested both in regular and irregular waves in order to confirm the 
efficacy of the design concept. Waves are measured using 5 
capacitance wave gauges with an adapter linked to the PC, the incident 
wave heights are measured with the first gauges while the 2nd and 3rd 
gauges are used for the calculation of reflection coefficient, and the 4th

wheel as shown in Fig.4 and Fig.5, which is a framework made of light 
wood and Polystyrene to decrease its weight. The blades on the rotor 
were directly driven by the circular water currents as well as the 
buoyant force (see Jobb and Faizal et al., 2009). The movement of the 
blades moves in back and force without clock spring in Oscillation case 
while in the Restoring Force System the blades moves unidirectional 
with clock spring; the spring was revolved tight by accumulated small 
displacement and released when it reached an applicable range.

In this study, the occurrence of electricity generations of waves mainly 
comprise two main stages in the system, the shaft work and power 
output, the first stage is the transition from wave energy to mechanical 
energy of some certain medium such as ratchet wheel, blades, shafts, 
spring, clockwork spring, piston, etc., the second stage is the 
conversion of power output through turbine mechanical energy via the 
generator (motor).  
Since waves provide the motive power that turns the wheel, a 
simply designed ratchet wheel equipment as shown in Fig.6 was 
adopted to maintain uniform direction while the wheel revolves, i.e. the 
shafts is equipped with a ratchet wheel that allows motion in single 
direction like a weather vane or an anemoscope, and is acted as a 
gadget of an accumulation of displacement by amassing the trivial 
fluctuations of the bladed rotor. Similar usage was also developed by 
Thakker et al. (2009) in their impulse turbine where they called it self-
rectifying turbines, that the turbines rotate in the same direction no 
matter what the direction of the air flow in the OWC, which are 
response to the need for turbines to extract power from bi-directional 
airflows. Moreover, the clockwork spring of a “pull-back car” was 
adopt as a device of the accumulation of potential energy by 
accumulating the wave amplitude of trivial fluctuations in all directions
