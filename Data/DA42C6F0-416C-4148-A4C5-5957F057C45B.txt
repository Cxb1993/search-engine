Abstract 
When mobile communication devices (such as cell-phone, PDA) combine with 
location-aware devices (such as GPS receiver), provides users capability of fetching regional 
information of their surroundings. However, the overflow of information and the difficulty 
of browsing and querying information with mobile devices, causes users not be able to get 
information on demand precisely. Therefore, how to integrate a variety of information 
sources to offer users while using the mobile communication devices, can rely on location 
information to get more abundant regional information guide and gather, namely becomes 
one of the considerably important subjects today. For this reason, we bring up a proposal of 
the mobile information integration system, quoting a XML Storage (XML Document Storage, 
called XDS), files or data can be encapsulated into a XML Object, and users can add some 
meaningful explanations to the XML Object. By these explanations, users can query 
information more precisely. We also set up a “Location tracker” to record user’s instant
