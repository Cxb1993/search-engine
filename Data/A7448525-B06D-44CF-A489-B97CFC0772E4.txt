二、前言 
 
  今年是三年期的計劃的第一年，今年主要完成磁場周期可調式聚頻磁鐵原型的研發。經
過一年來的努力，設計一台 1米長的磁場周期可調式聚頻磁鐵來產生寬廣的(Soft X-ray and 
X-ray範圍)及高亮度的光源。磁場周期可調式聚頻磁鐵,為了不影響電子運行軌道,注射操作
及壽命, 聚頻磁鐵的磁距設計為 7 公釐。磁場周期可調式聚頻磁鐵的磁路調整機制初步的構
想如圖七說明,巧妙的磁路安排及設計發現, 磁場周期為 2.0 公分的交錯式磁列由 1.2 公分
的鈷釩鋼磁極是和 0.8 公分的不導磁材料空氣所組成周期磁路。如果把 1.2公分的鈷釩鋼磁
極移動分 0.8 公分後，鈷釩鋼磁極緊密為 2.4公分和 1.6 公分的不導磁材料空氣所組成周期
磁路組成為 4.0 公分的周期磁路, 當要調整磁場周期時,只要移動一塊 1.2 公分鈷釩鋼磁極
相隔間距 1.6 公分的尺寸即可改變的磁場周期；調整的機制只有簡單又可靠的平移動作。 
 
三、研究目的 
 
磁場周期可調式聚頻磁鐵具有幾種特色: 
 
1. 在短的磁場周期亦能產生強的磁場強度。 
2. 磁場強度是以超導螺旋線圈的電流變化產生,當無磁場時磁列周期機械移動變形
誤差相當小。 
3. 磁場周期調整機構簡單,可維持磁列精密度。 
4. 善加利用不同磁場周期的產生的來產生寬廣的高亮度光源。

Abstract—Trim-iron-pieces and trim-coils were used to correct 
the magnetic field error in a superconducting undulator with a 
magnet period of 15 mm.  The main-coils were wound with 
NbTi wires of 0.77 x 0.51 mm2 rectangular cross section.  The 
entire iron pole array was electrically insulated from the 
main-coils using Teflon to avoid degradation of the 
superconducting wire when the main-coils were trained to a large 
current.  The trim-coils were wound with NbTi wires of 0.33 
mm diameter.  The trim-iron-pieces and trim-coils were 
mounted directly on the iron pole. The electric current of these 
trim-coils were generated by a single power supply with a 
constant voltage.  A variable resistor and two golden-case 
resisters were connected in parallel to adjust the current that 
passed through each trim-coil.   In this work, three 
trim-iron-pieces and one trim-coil were fabricated and mounted 
on each of the upper and lower arrays, and their effect on 
correcting the magnetic field error of the undulator is measured 
and compared to a model simulation. 
 
Index Terms—superconducting undulator, trim-iron-pieces, 
trim-coil, NbTi wire, magnetic field measurement. 
INTRODUCTION 
HE superconducting undulator has been extensively 
studied because it can produce highly brilliant x-rays up to 
25  keV photon energy from a medium energy, e.g. ~3 GeV, 
synchrotron storage ring to facilitate advanced x-ray 
microscopy, protein crystallography, nano-beam absorption 
and scattering experiments.  The short-period and mini-gap 
characteristics of the superconducting undulator increase the 
number of poles and the magnetic field strength, respectively.  
A maximum field strength of 1.4 T is presently obtainable 
using iron poles with vertically wound, racetrack-shape NbTi 
superconducting coils.  
Achieving high quality field and reducing radiation heat 
load are the two major issues to be faced by the design and 
fabrication of all short-period and mini-gap superconducting 
undulators. The field quality issue can be improved with 
precise winding of superconducting coils and field trimming 
by trim-coils [1].  The heat-load issue can be overcome by 
many ways, such as the use of a soft-end dipole magnet, a 
large-RRR conductor coating and a chicane mechanism, as 
well as by improving the design of the LHe cryostat [2-4]. 
However, shimming a short-period undulator is complicated 
because of the interaction of the magnetic fields among nearby 
iron poles.  In this work, the trim-iron-pieces and trim-coils 
were designed, fabricated and mounted on the outer-faces of 
iron poles to understand their trimming effect on the undulator 
magnetic field . 
Experimental Setup 
A 40 iron (low carbon steel 1006) pole array charged by  
superconducting coils each with 55 turns per pole (40P55T) 
was manufactured. A Teflon coating of  0.045±0.02 mm 
thickness on the iron pole array is used to provide an electric 
insulation up to 0.5 kV between the coils and the iron arrays.  
A 0.07 mm thick Kapton sheet was inserted between the 
arrays and the beam duct; this padding ensures satisfactory 
contact between their two surfaces.  The magnetic field of the 
40P55T was measured by a cryogenic mini-Hall sensor 
(AREPOC Ltd, HHP-MP type). The trim-iron-pieces and the 
trim-coils were mounted directly on the outer-face of the iron 
poles as a means to correct the magnetic field error of the 
undulator.  Figures 1 (a), (b) and (c) show the trimming 
control circuit of the trim-coils, the structure of the 
trim-iron-pieces and coils, and their assembly on the undulator, 
respectively.  In the design, all trim-coils are connected in 
parallel to one another and to a power supply. The trim-coil 
current was controlled using a ceramic variable resister (Rv) 
and two golden-case resisters (Ra and Rb). All control 
components were kept at room temperature; Fig. 1 (a) depicts 
that only the trim-coils were immersed in liquid helium at 4.2 
K.  In this study, three trim-iron-pieces and one trim-coil 
were mounted on each of the upper and lower iron pole array.  
The heights of the three trim-iron-pieces are 5, 15 and 25 mm, 
respectively, and their thickness is 4 mm. The trim-coils were 
wound around the 25 mm iron pieces with NbTi wire of 0.33 
mm diameter.  The height and thickness of the trim-coil were 
19 mm and 1.5mm with 222 turns, as shown in Fig. 1 (b). The 
5, 25 and 15 mm iron pieces were mounted on the outer-face 
of the 6th, 18th and 35th poles, respectively, using SS316L 
clamps as presented in Fig. 1 (c).  A pin indexes the position 
between the trim iron and the beam duct. The effect of 
trim-coils with 5 A (38.6 A/mm2), 10 A (77.2 A/mm2) and 15 
A (115.8 A/mm2) exciting currents was also studied.  To 
facilitate discussion, the pole with the 25 mm trim-iron-pieces, 
and its first and second nearest poles are labeled 25mm-0, 
25mm-1 and 25mm-2, respectively. This nomenclature is also 
applied to the poles with the 5 mm and 15 mm 
trim-iron-pieces.

形式的磁場可藉由將平面式聚頻磁鐵的磁極旋轉一角度而生，此一概念是由 ELLETRA 實驗室
的 Walker所提出。較短週期長度的橢圓偏振聚頻磁鐵能在固定的電子束設施中產生較高能
量的偏振光束，聚頻磁鐵的週期與磁列間隙比決定了最高可達磁場的強度，若週期越短，所
能達到的磁場強度越低，如何能在合理的磁列間隙與所需的光束能譜限制下提高橢圓偏振聚
頻磁鐵磁場強度便是重要的課題。同步輻射中心已針對利用超導線線圈製作橢圓偏振磁鐵的
方式進行相關研究，即使該磁路設計不難達成，採用超導體所會面臨的相關問題卻非短時間
內所能解決，例:感應電流、輻射熱負載、磁場誤差修正等。 
本計畫中採用了複合式磁塊材料、交錯磁列結構的方式來提升橢圓偏振聚頻磁鐵的磁場
強度，此結構之磁列可置於真空環境中，而超導線圈結構磁列則不行，因此種結構可有一優
勢:日本所提出的利用低溫永久磁鐵特性增強磁力的機制，需要將磁列置於真空環境，即可引
用於此結構當中。圖一說明了複合式磁塊材料交錯式橢圓偏振聚頻磁鐵的結構，此磁列將放
置於一螺線管磁場中操作。 
此一複合式磁塊材料交錯式結構也能應用水平偏振聚頻磁鐵，透過 RADIA 磁場模擬程
式，若將此結構與傳統複合式磁塊材料相較，可發現磁場明顯提升。我們同時將 Holmium材
料做為鐵心，進行磁場模擬，此材料的飽和磁場在液氦溫度下可高達 3.8T，藉由了解採用此
材料的磁場表現，可以幫助我們了解未來若此結構用於低溫環境時的發展性。圖二中描述了
磁列處於螺線管磁場下的磁場表現。 
若將複合式磁塊材料交錯式結構的磁極旋轉一角度，即可生成橢圓偏振磁場，其概念可
見圖三。在固定的磁塊尺寸下，垂直與水平兩方向磁場強度如何隨磁塊旋轉角度變化，已透
過模擬進行了解，相關結果列於圖四；固定的週期長度下、固定的塊材尺寸比例，垂直與水
平兩方向磁場強度如何隨磁極旋轉角度變化，也已透過模擬進行探討，見圖五；若採用 Holmium
磁極，此橢圓偏振聚頻磁鐵在台灣光子源儲存環中所能產生的光譜與該磁列所提供托園偏振
磁場之模擬結果可參考圖六、七，圖八為此磁列的橫軸方向磁場均勻度分析結果。 
三個週期的複合式磁塊材料交錯式結構橢圓偏振聚頻磁鐵的磁列原型已加工製造，該磁
列週期長度為 20 mm、磁極旋轉角度為 60o；磁列由 12mm厚釩鋼鐵塊與 8mm厚 鎳鐵硼磁塊交
替排列而成。同時已將此磁列置於螺線管磁場下進行測量，該螺線管設備在電流 180A下可提
供縱向磁場 0.37T；螺線管長度為 270mm、內徑 72mm、縱向 100mm內磁場均勻度達 1%。量測
系統由三軸平台、步進馬達與霍爾探針組成，磁場量測於室溫下執行。
