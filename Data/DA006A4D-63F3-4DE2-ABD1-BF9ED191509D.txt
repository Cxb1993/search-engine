repeatedly to reduce the broadcast latency. To the 
best of our knowledge, our work is deemed the first 
attempt to design such a multi-source broadcast 
algorithm with a derived theoretical latency upper-
bound. 
英文關鍵詞： interference-aware, distributed algorithms 
 
Our algorithms lead to a guaranteed latency upper- bound in terms of maximum hop 
distance. For our future work, power limitation is an important problem. We believe 
that we could extend this work and consider power limitation. Moreover, the unit disk 
graph model adopted in this paper could be replaced by more practical interference 
models such as two- disk or SINR (signal-to-interference-plus-noise ratio) models. 
 
Our results are to be published in IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed 
Systems [1].
