子此類著重三次非線性光學特性以及光折變材料上之研究。

1-2 研究動機 
  非線性光學材料研究主要著重於兩種(1)溫度穩定性及(2)高倍頻轉
換效率(d33)。在提高光電材料之高溫穩定性方面主要有兩個方式，其
一為改善熱塑性高分子之玻璃轉移溫度，如聚亞醯胺等。另外，利用
可交鏈型高分子之網狀交鏈結構亦為得到高溫穩定型非線性光學材
料之途徑，過去在這方面努力成果也相當多，這些結構包含網狀結
構、網狀互穿結構以及溶膠凝膠。對於高倍頻轉換效率NLO材料，早
先利用分子上設計引入強推拉電子效應來達成此目的，近年來藉由特
殊結構如Dendrimer、Dendronized Polymer、Organometallic…等材料
達到高效率倍頻轉換效率，前一章我們成功的合成出具極高熱穩定性
二維聚亞醯胺NLO高分子。

1-4-1 PVKNO2高分子之合成與結構鑑定 
高分子 PVKNO2 的製作是以 PVK 直接硝基化而成。Figure 1-6
為 PVKNO2之 NMR 圖譜，可知位置 b 與 c 受到硝基的拉電子效應使
得氫的電子密度變小而形成去遮蔽效應，使其化學位移會往低磁場方
向移動。Figure 1-7 為 PVK 與 PVKNO2之 FT-IR 圖譜，顯示經由硝基
化步驟後 PVKNO2於 1520cm-1與 1343cm-1位置出現強度高的 NO2對
稱與不對稱伸縮震動，從此確定結構上已含有硝基。經由元素分析各
元素所含有的重量百分比分別為 N：13.4％，C：54.49％，H：4.02
％。經由內插法估算其硝基化程度達 87.4％。 
1-4-2 PCzNO2高分子之合成與結構鑑定 
    以 2,7-dibromoethylhexyl carbazole 進行 yamamoto 反應形成共軛
高分子 PCz。Figure1-8(a)為共軛高分子 PCz 之 NMR 圖譜。由於主鏈
較長共軛結構，使得苯環上的電子更易流動並加強苯環產生的環電流
效應，使苯環氫質子之化學位移會往低磁場方向移動，藉由元素分析
N : 4.38％，C : 86.41％，H：8.39％與計算值接近，表示結構上的元
素比例正確，綜合以上確定合成出共軛高分子 PCz。接續將此高分子
進行硝基化反應。Figure 1-8(b)為高分子 PCzNO2 之 NMR 圖譜，與
Figure 1-7(a)相互對照下，原本的位置 j 之分裂峰消失顯示結構已接上
硝基。另外，位置 k 受到硝基的拉電子效應使其電子密度變小形成去
遮蔽效應，所以分裂峰會往低磁場方向移動。Figure 1-9 為 PCz 與
PCzNO2之 FT-IR 圖譜。PCzNO2明顯在 1517cm-1與 1329cm-1出現強
度高的 NO2對稱與不對稱伸縮震動。經由元素分析各元素所含有的重
量百分比分別為 N：10.64％，C：62.019％，H：6.2％。經由內插法
估算其硝基化程度達 87.4％。

熱示差掃瞄卡計分析 
非線性光學高分子之玻璃轉移溫度(Tg)是代表材料熱穩定性的
重要指標，也是高分子在配向極化過程中提供有效極化之參考溫度。 
對於玻璃轉移溫度的尋找，本研究利用熱微差掃瞄卡計(DSC)，
並採用兩階段升溫步驟，如下所敘： 
(1)第一階段升溫步驟：以每分鐘 10℃升溫速率升溫至 150℃並維持
30 分鐘。 
(2)降溫步驟：以每分鐘 10℃降溫速率降至-30℃並維持 10 分鐘。 
(3)第二階段升溫步驟：以每分鐘 10℃升溫至 250℃並維持 10 分鐘，
觀察此階段的玻璃轉移現象。 
Figure 1-16、1-17、1-18、1-19 分別為 PVKNO2、PCzNO2、
PCzCCNO2與 PCzCCSO2之 DSC 圖譜，並將數據整理於表 1-2。三種
高分子依照 Tg 值由高至低排列：PCzCCNO2＞PCzCCSO2＞ PCzNO2 
＞ PVKNO2。由於 PVKNO2 高分子其主鏈型態為柔軟的脂肪鏈段易
受熱刺激而擾動，故在 Tg 值呈現最低值；相反地，PCzNO2利用 2,7-
咔唑上的 N，將兩苯環緊緊扣住即可形成剛硬的高分子鏈在玻璃轉移
溫度上有較高的表現。過去研究顯示[13,14]高分子藉由剛硬的分枝結構
可提升高分子旋轉體積而達到增加玻璃轉移溫度的效果。然而
PCzCCNO2與 PCzCCSO2不僅具有主鏈的剛硬特性之外，藉由其延伸
出的 stillbene 結構來有效提升高分子間的嵌和效應(anchoring effect)
以及旋轉體積，因此高分子鏈需要較高溫度下才能進行局部性震動轉
動行為，故在玻璃轉移溫度上呈現最高值。PCzCCSO2由於磺醯基末
端較多柔軟之烷鏈段導致相對於 PCzCCNO2較低玻璃轉移溫度。

1-4-8 側鏈型全共軛非線性光學高分子排列程度之探討 
當分子的配向偶極矩平行於的永久偶極矩，在此條件下發色團會

降低入射光的吸收值，因此極化後高分子之 UV-vis 圖譜，其吸收值
會降低[22]。因此常利用此 Φ(order parameter)來描述高分子經極化後定
向排列的程度，其公式列於下所示： 
 
Φ = 1 – Ap / A0 
where A0：The absorbance of an unpoled system at λmax. 
AP：The absorbance of a poled sample at λmax. 
 
    雖然在極化回溫後，其 SH 值有回降現象，對於測量 Φ 有失初始
意義，但考量四種高分子均有此現象，因此測量 Φ 仍有其價值性。
將各高分子經極化過後的試片利用 UV-vis 測得吸收圖譜，如 Figure 
1-28 所示。並將極化前後的最大吸收峰數值結果整理於表 1-8 所示。

得發色團容易在外加電場誘導下進行定向排列並且使其 Φ 值較高，
反觀 PCzNO2 之發色團因高分子結構剛硬影響且受到鄰近發色團拉
扯，導致不易於定向排列，故其 Φ 值只達 0.099。對於 PCzCCSO2，
其 Φ 可達 0.153，表示此高分子之發色團受電場牽引下容易進行定向
排列。

1-4-11 非線性光學穩定性之研究 
利用四種高分子進行光學穩定性探討，但由於 PVKNO2 與
PCzNO2 兩高分子經極化後其二次非線性數值太小，故以下只針對
PCzCCSO2 與 PCzCCNO2單一高分子做探討。 
由於非線性光學高分子材料，利用外加電場的作用迫使發色團做
定向排列，進而形成非中心對稱之結構才具有非線性特性。然而此非
中心對稱結構容易受溫度及時間之老化過程而逐漸鬆弛。為了觀察非
線性光學係數之穩定行為，通常可由動態熱穩定性(dynamic thermal 
stability)及時間穩定性(temporal stability)兩種模式進行。以下針對各
光學穩定性測量分敘如下： 
 
動態熱穩定性探討 
對於材料熱穩定性之探討乃是藉著偵測材料之有效二次諧波係
數(effective second harmonic coeffecient；deff)隨溫度之變化來瞭解材料
適用溫度範圍。將完成配向極化之非線性光學高分子薄膜以每分鐘
2℃的加熱速率自室溫開始升溫，並同步紀錄 deff之變化。為了確實瞭
解材料之適用溫度範圍，因此定義 T0 值以供判斷，顯示的意義為在
此溫度所供給的能量足以使高分子鏈產生較大之分子運動而擾亂發
色團之排列進而造成倍頻效應降低。

形。PCzCCSO2 呈現較低 T0 值溫度(108oC)，可歸咎於較低玻璃轉移

不足以使發色團因受熱而改變分子定向排列。而當溫度超過 T0 時，
提供足夠動能使得高分子鏈裡少數發色團因受熱開始運動，導致原本

材料的緩和行為分析 
對於材料之緩和行為可以藉著 Kohlraush-Williams-Watts(KWW)
方程式加以模擬，方程式如下所示： 
deff (t) = deff (0)exp[-(t /τ)β]   (0<β≦1)      KWW equation 
其中 τ 為平均 SHG 緩和時間(average SHG relaxation time) ，也就是
deff(t)值衰退至 deff(0)值的 1/e 倍所需的時間，此 KWW 方程式的另一
參數為 β，其意義為緩和時間的分佈情形，此值介於 0 與 1 之間，若
β 等於 1，表示分子為單一緩和時間分佈，每個分子的緩和情形趨於
一致。 
為了尋求 τ 與 β 可將 KWW 式可經換算推得下列式子 
ln(ln(d(0)/d(t)))=βlnt-βlnτ 
由上式可知 lnt 對 ln(ln(d(0)/d(t)))做圖可得斜率與截距，即可推得
τ 與 β 值。分別將不同溫度下時間穩定性之數據帶入上式，得出如圖
1-34 與 1-35 所示，並將各重要參數整理至表 1-8。 
從表 1-10 得知隨著溫度上升 β 值也隨之遞增，表示提高溫度會
使分子緩和行為趨於一致。比較 85℃與 100℃之 τ 值兩者相差 82 倍，
表示於高溫時發色團受熱擾動情形增多故呈現較快速的鬆弛行為。

2-4-5 配向極化程序及非線性光學性質分析 
 
由前述之結果了解加入共軛高分子的賓主型系統能使發色團的
能隙降低，在此為了解共軛高分子對巨觀二次非線光學係數的影響，
須藉由實際極化結果量測二階諧波強度及公式計算後才能確定。極化
配向利用COPET (Corona-Onset Poling at Elevated Temperature)法以即
時檢測(in-situ)的方式，讀取每個溫度下穩定的二次諧波強度，以取
得最適極化條件。 
 
此系列所採用的極化條件在 7 KV 下，從室溫下開始以間隔 10
℃的方式逐漸昇溫，在每個溫度下停留 30 分鐘以量測其穩定值，當
測量的二階諧波強度已不在明顯上升時，以所測量到之最大之強度為
該系統之最大二階諧波強度。各材料的升昇溫結果如 Figure 
2-30~Figure 2-32 所示，並將各系列的最大二階諧波強度表列如 Table

學性質，其二階諧波之強度依序為 P3 > P2 > P1，本研究中所設計之
共軛高分子在極化初期就能發現其 SHG 訊號，比較文獻中所提及共
軛共高分子的非線性光學系統極化程序，當推拉電子基接在共軛高分
子上有可能會造成極化的困難情形並未在本研究中並未發生，但是回
溫時候由於高分子鏈段中之高發色團含量也發現與前一章節相似情
形，鄰近發色團們為了順應電場極化方向而轉動相同方向因此產生極
大分子內應力，然而極化回溫時無法提供充足活化能維持定向排列，
此時為了抒解分子內應力而回復原來高分子構形企圖達到一個穩定

RESULTS AND DISCUSSION: 
Synthesis of the Diblock copolymer PEOz-b-LPEI 
The synthesis of PEOz-b-LPEI is illustrated in scheme 1. Cationic ring-opening 
polymerization of 2-ethyl-2-oxazoline by using monofunctional initiator methyl 
p-toluenesulfonate is a typical method for synthesis of PEOz. The oxazolinium salt 
structure of the growing polymer can readily reacts with nucleophiles such as amines 
or water to introduce functional group to the end of polymer chain, quantitatively. 
Here we chose a thiol for coupling the PEOz and LPEI via disulfide bond. But the 
thiol was susceptible to oxidation to the formation of dimer, thus, the more stable 
form of thioacetate was desired. By adding potassium thioacetate to terminate the 
reaction, PEOz end capped with thioacetate functional group was obtained. The 
number average molecular weight and polydispersity index of PEOz-SAc determined 
by GPC were 10000 and 1.12, respectively. The LPEI was prepared by acid 
hydrolysis of PEOz.  
Particle Size and Zeta Potential 
After determining the amount of the different polycations for polyplexes formation, 
the particle size of the polyplexes was estimated by laser light scattering. In order to 
investigate whether the stability of the polyplexes will be affected by the ionic 
strength of the media, the polyplexes prepared in either aqueous solution of 
physiological salt concentration (150 mM NaCl) or distilled water were used for size 
measurement. During the measurement, we found that there existed a lowest P/D ratio 
for stable polyplexes formation to each polymer and the polyplexes that formed below 
this P/D ratio usually led to aggregation. The BPEI polyplex had an average diameter 
of 100 nm in water that somewhat consisted with the observation of Tang and Szoka. 
The sizes of LPEI and PEOz-b-LPEI polyplexes were around 190 nm. When the 
polyplexes dispersed in 150 mM NaCl solution, BPEI and LPEI polyplexes all 
aggregated to larger particles. However, the PEOz-b-LPEI polyplexes did not show 
any aggregation in the salt-containing media. 
The zeta potential measurement of the polyplexes also carried out by mixing the 
polyplexes at the lowest P/D ratio for stable polyplexes formation. BPEI and LPEI 
polyplexes all had moderately positive surface charges, but the zeta potentials of 
PEOz-b-LPEI polyplexes were almost neutral. However, when BPEI and LPEI mixed 
by coupling PEOz with LPEI and the physicochemical and biological characteristics 
were evaluated. The polyplexes of the PEOz-b-LPEI were shown to have neutral 
surface charge and stably sized particles, unlike LPEI and BPEI. These diblock 
copolymer polyplexes exhibited a pH-sensitivity that caused them to dissociate at pH 
values lower than 5.5, suggesting that they may release DNA intracellularly. The 
results also demonstrated that the PEOz-b-LPEI had low cytotoxicity and high 
transfection efficiency. Accordingly, the diblock copolymers have potential for 
application in nonviral gene therapy.

ABSTRACT SUMMARY: 
A novel mixed micelle that comprised of poly(N-isopropylacrylamide-co-methacrylic 
acid)-graft-poly(D,L-lactide) (P(NIPAAm-co-MAAc)-g-PLA) with methoxy 
poly(ethylene glycol)-b-poly(D,L-lactide) (mPEG-b-PLA) was developed for 
application in cancer therapy. The mixed micelle had an multi-functional inner core of 
P(NIPAAm-co-MAAc)-g-PLA to enable intracellular drug delivery and an extended 
hydrophilic outer shell of mPEG to hide the inner core. A change in pH deformed the 
structure of the inner core from that of aggregated P(NIPAAmco-MAAc), causing it 
to collapse. This change induced the release of a significant quantity of doxorubicin 
(Dox) from mixed micelles. This study presents not only a new micelle structure for a 
graft-diblock copolymer system, but also a method for overcoming some of the 
limitations on biomaterials used in intravenous injection.

RESULTS AND DISCUSSION: 
In this study, P(NIPAAm-co-MAAc)-g-PLA graft copolymer 
(MnP(NIPAAm-co-MAAc) = 10.5 kg/mol, MnPLA = 6.95 kg/mol, Mw/Mn = 1.3) and 
mPEG-PLA diblock copolymer (MnmPEG = 2.0 kg/mol, MnPLA = 0.37 kg/mol, 
Mw/Mn = 1.1) were synthesized. The mixed micelle was prepared using dialysis 
method against distilled water as the graft; the diblock copolymer was presentin 
dimethyl sulfoxide (DMSO) and dimethylformamide (DMF) cosolvent. In this solvent 
exchange process, P(NIPAAm-co-MAAc)-g-PLA and mPEG-PLA perhaps exhibited 
self-assembled by packing and 
associating of the hydrophobic PLA to form a mixed micelle, yielding a uniform 
particle distribution (101.1 ± 2.5 nm) and a low polydispersity index (PI = 0.073 ± 
0.008), as determined by dynamic light scattering (DLS). A micelle composed from 
P(NIPAAm-co-MAAc)-g-PLA graft copolymer with a particle size of 116.2 ± 3.3 nm 
and a PI of 0.091 ± 0.007 was used as a comparative sample to identify the outside 
surface of the mixed micelles. Doppler microelectrophoresis measurements reveal that 
the zeta potential of mixed micelle was shifted from -17.8 ± 1.8 mV for the micelle to 
-6.8 ± 1.1 mV. These results strongly indicate that the hydrophilic segments mPEG of 
the diblock copolymers were extended on the surface of mixed micelles and the 
diblock copolymers were successfully incorporated into the particles. The LCST of 
mixed micelle was at 28  ℃ for the sample in succinic acid buffer solution at pH 5.0. 
In contrast, the LCST of the mixed micelle in phosphoric acid buffer solution at pH 
7.4 exceed 50 ℃. The structural changed in the mixed micelles was determined by 
fluorescence spectrometry using pyrene as a hydrophobic probe. A higher I1/I3 of the 
pyrene emission spectra indicates more polar surrounding the pyrene probe. For 
mixed micelles at pH 5.0, the I1/I3 of the pyrene spectra decreasing began about 26 _ 
because of the pyrene molecules partitioning in the aggregation region, following by a 
rapidly increasing with temperature and time. The result indicates that the pyrene 
surrounding is transferred from mixed micelle inner core to the buffer solution by 
structural leakage. However, the I1/I3 depended weakly on temperature for the mixed 
micelles at pH 7.4, so mixed micelle were stabilized in neutral surroundings, 
regardless of the change in temperature. Dialysis method was also conducted on the 
Dox and copolymer solution, and mixed micelles with Dox were formed, exhibiting a 
uniform particle size of 165.3 nm. From UV/Vis spectrophotometer analysis, drug 
content of mixed micelle incorporated with Dox was around 19 %. At the acidic 
surrounding, Dox demonstrated a significant release amount of near 50 % from mixed 
micelles in the initial 1 h. That is due to the P(NIPAm-co-MAAc)s collapse and
