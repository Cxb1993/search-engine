1.中文摘要 
壓力殼為水下載具最重要之抗壓結
構，一般壓力殼之組成為球頭殼、圓錐殼及
圓筒型殼。圓錐型壓力殼截面以圓形為承受
外壓負荷之最佳結構，而進行圓錐型壓力殼
產製時，須進行板材彎板及焊接接合作業，
此將造成壓力殼變形，致無法達成完全圓形
之截面構型，故必須透過圓度規範來確保其
品質；本計畫利用 ABAQUS 為分析工具，
並考量流體-結構藕合現象，針對應用失圓
率為參數所建立失圓之圓錐殼，探討其承受
水下爆震負荷時之結構及環繞流場之反
應，本研究之成果可提供未來結構設計者進
行水下載具抗爆震分析與設計參考使用。 
關鍵詞：失圓率，失圓圓錐型壓力殼 
Abstract 
The circular cross section of conical 
pressure hull is the best way to resist the 
external pressure load. When the conical 
pressure hulls were made by bending and 
welding metal plates, such working methods 
will lead to deformations which make them 
unable to achieve ideal circular cross section 
configuration. It is necessary to ensure 
working quality control by means of 
specification. The out-of-round ratio is used to 
evaluate the circularity effect of pressure hull. 
The ABAQUS is used to study the response of 
imperfect conical pressure hull with external 
stiffeners subjected to the underwater shock as 
well as fluid-structure interaction (FSI) is 
considered. The imperfect conical pressure 
hull is established that refer to the 
out-of-round ratio. The results studies 
presented in this paper can provide structure 
designer with an effective tool at design stage 
and attempt to supply a reasonable 
comprehensive account, which will be used in 
designing structure so as to enhance resistance 
to underwater shock damage. 
Keywords: out-of-round ratio, imperfect 
conical pressure hull

進行研究；2004 年成功大學林忠宏教授[61]
利用工作壓力可達 120MPa之深海壓力模擬

大 學 李 雅 榮 教 授 等 [63] ， 利 用 德 國
U209-1400 型潛艦之ㄧ般佈置圖，重建壓力
殼模型，並利用 ABAQUS 有限單元軟體為
工具，進行壓力殼耐壓強度、抗爆震強度及
抗碰撞強度分析，研究發現此一壓力殼模
型，可符合設計潛深 400 公尺之要求，於水
深 250 公尺可承受距離壓力殼 15.8 公尺之
160 公斤 TNT 爆炸威力，在碰撞強度分析時
則發現，當此壓力殼以 7 節速度，2夾角碰
撞海床時，壓力殼結構亦可安全無虞。 
2.2 水下流場之爆炸分析 
水下流場爆炸分析方法主要可分為理
論實驗及數值分析模擬兩大類，在水下爆炸
實驗部份，由於涉及機密，各國大都祕而不
宣，目前已公開之文獻，簡述如后：1948
年Cole[64]利用流體力學之觀點探討不同炸
藥之組成成分對水下爆炸效應之影響，並利
用理論及實驗獲得了爆震波之半經驗公
式；1959 年 Hollyer[65]進行了爆震試驗，並
依所獲之實驗資料，建立半理論半經驗公式
來計算水下爆震波參數，如入射波之峰值壓
力值及爆震波時間衰減常數，以預測不同爆
藥形式來計算爆震波壓力之時間歷程；1961
年 Keil[66]描述了水下發生爆炸後不同階段
之物理現象，並提供船艦結構及裝備在不同
爆震環境下之反應及損壞模式；1973 年，
Young[67]利用不同類別之炸藥進行水下爆
破試驗，探討水下爆炸對海中環境之影響以
及描述水下爆炸現象；1979 年，Henrych[68]
應用應力波動理論探討不同傳播介質對於
爆震波之影響；1992 年，李翼祺[69]詳細描
述了水下爆炸之物理現象，並利用不同類型
炸藥之參數，推導爆震波相關參數之半理論
半經驗公式及氣泡球體湧動(Migration)現象
之相關參數，確認不同參數之適用範圍；
1994 年，Smith 等人[70]探討水下爆炸產生
之震波、氣泡爆炸能量分佈及水柱形成之原
因，並針對部分參數進行推導，建立半理論
半經驗公式；1995 年，Joseph 等人[71]進行
13 次有關水屏彈之實炸實驗，實驗內容包
含線串炸藥(Line Charge)之藥量、爆炸距離
以及連續或離散式線串炸藥於水下起爆產
生水屏(Plume)之影響；1996 年李世勤[72]
說明深水炸彈之戰術運用、種類以及引爆方
式；1997 年黃清哲等[73]利用三維邊界元素
法，求解拉普拉斯方程式及柏努力方程式，
模擬及分析氣泡球體之動態行為，其間包含
  8
轉換、傅立葉轉換及拉普拉斯轉換）等解析
技巧，推導波動方程式及殼運動方程式，再
以解析方法解之，但此僅適用於簡單幾何構
型（如平板、球殼與圓筒殼）。而利用實驗

各項參數，往往耗費巨大，故進行試驗前仍
必須經由數值方法先期多次模擬計算，方可
進行；在近年的發展趨勢中，利用數值方
法，已可解決構型較複雜結構物與流體之間
藕合問題。進行爆震分析之數值法可分類成
空間離散法(Spatial Discretization)及表面近
似法(Surface Approximation)，此二類方法各
有優缺點。近期發展之模組化流固藕合分析
軟體均結合以上的方法來進行數值模擬分
析。 
1. 解析法 
在圓柱殼與音響流藕合作用的研究方
面有，1951 年 Carrier[91]首先使用積分轉換
與級數法求解無限圓筒殼承受平面暫態波
之平面應變問題，由於轉換後之待求函數為
一 Bessel 函數，且 2-D 波動方程式含有特殊
之奇異性(Singularity)，造成求解的困難，無
法得到函數之反轉換，所以當時 Carrier 並
沒提出完整的數值結果。1960 年 Payton[92]
利用環向變數的傅立葉雙重積分轉換計
巧，求出了圓筒殼初期反應之漸近解
(Asymptotic Solution)。到了 1964 年，Peralta
和 Raynor[93]進而利用 Payton 所發展的方
法求得內部充滿液體之圓筒殼的平面應變
反應。1970 年 Huang[94]利用第二類的
Volterra 積分式，表示水下彈性圓柱殼與平
面音響波暫態交互作用的統御方程式，並以
有限的級數法求解。此後 Huang 與 Wang 等
[95-97]利用級數展開法(Series Expansion)、
拉普拉斯(Laplace)及傅立葉(Fourier)轉換技
巧，探討彈性圓柱殼與球面波之暫態藕合反
應。另 Geers[98-99] 以模態展開 (Modal 
Expansion)、拉普拉斯轉換技巧及殘餘速度
勢(Residual Velocity Potential)，研究彈性圓
殼承受震波作用之反應。 
在圓球殼與音響流藕合作用的研究方
面有，1957 年 Mann-Nachbar[100]首先對彈
性球殼與音響波之藕合作用進行研究。在
1969 年 Huang[101]以線性聲波理論、薄殼
理論及級數法等，推導出波動方程式及殼運
動方程式，以解析法求解彈性球殼承受平面
作用之暫態反應。隨後，Huang 等人[102]
將此方法推廣至球面波，比較球面波與平面
震波波的差異。 
具加強材之圓柱殼與音響流藕合作用
的研究方面有，1970 年 Berger[103]利用拉
普拉斯/傅立葉轉換技巧，研究具加強材之
無限長彈性圓柱殼與無限流體交互作用之
動態反應。另 Haxton [104]以模態法求解具
環向加強材圓柱殼承受水下爆震作用之線
彈性反應。對於此種問題的研究尚有
Berglund [105]，Crouzet-Pascal[106]等在水
下平板與音響流藕合作用的研究方面有，
1974 年 Huang[107]利用拉普拉斯(Laplace)
及漢克(Hankel)轉換技巧，解平板與圓形震
波的初期暫態反應，結果顯示，板上的彎曲
應力會高於波前壓力數倍。 
2. 數值法 
進行爆震分析之數值法可分類成空間
離散法(Spatial Discretization)及表面近似法
(Surface Approximation)，此二類方法各有優
缺點； 
其中空間離散法(Spatial Discretization)
的優點在於可以模擬整個流場及結構的反
應，相對的，其缺點為需耗費大量的電腦記
憶體容量及運算時間。其發展始於 1969
年，Zienkiewicz[108]以有限元素法將結構離
散成勁度矩陣方程式，而流體運動則以李維
-史塔克(Navier-Stokes)方程式表示，並藉由
給伽遼金(Galerkin)權值程序求解。1978 年
Marcus[109]利用有限元素法(NASTRAN)求
解水下懸臂板的自由振動反應。1983 年由
於 1970-1980 年代電腦運算速度及記憶容量
的限制，此法未被大量採用。直到 1990 年
代起，電腦及數值方法快速的發展此類方法
才逐漸地重新被廣泛使用。 1995 年
Huang[110] 運 用 有 限 差 分 法 及
Eulerian-Lagrangian 描述法，求解水下球形
殼承受震波與氣泡脈衝作用之暫態藕合反
  10
出壓力殼表面積分方程式，用以分析壓力殼
承受水下爆震波後之彈塑性反應；2001 年
洪振發等[133]，整合有限元素結構分析、邊
界元素流場壓力分析與氣泡動力雙漸進運
動方程式，建立船體受到水下爆炸後主爆震
波以及後續氣泡脹縮運動所造成之震顫反
應分析系統，並利用圓柱殼為對象進行研
究，發現主震波雖在極短時間對殼體表面產
生極大壓力峰值及加速度，而造成局部結構
振動，激發剛體模態為主之整體運動，但後
續之氣泡脈衝則對結構表面產生週期性壓
力脈衝，且作用時間較主震波作用時間來的
更久，同時發現以爆震因子做為結構損壞能
力評估標準，較適用於主震波作用造成結構
破損之評估，對於氣泡脈衝之影響則較不適
用； 2002 年洪振發等人[134]進行嵌板水下
爆炸衝擊試驗與數值分析，探討船體結構受
到水下爆炸時之相關參數值，並建立爆震波
壓力分佈及爆震對結構衝擊反應之預估方
法 ； 2003 年 洪 振 發 等 人 [135] ， 利 用
DYNA3D+USA 以空氣為背壓之平板為對
象，探討其受到不同爆震因數之水下爆炸
後，結構所產生之彈塑性佔態反應，分析時
並考慮材料應變率之影響，分析結果並與
Ramajecyathilagam[136] 之實驗結果相比
較；2005 年洪振發等人[137]針對錐狀物體
與船體結構撞擊所造成之剪切撕裂型態破
壞情形進行研究，歸納錐型體撞擊平板之破
壞模式與板塊結構撕裂之力學特性，建立撕
裂破壞模式，並推導簡化之半理論估算方
法，以推估破壞狀態下之能量消散及切割阻
力，並以 LS-DYNA 進行模擬比較；2007
年洪振發[138]等人庚續前述研究，以船體雙
層殼結構為對象，依受圓錐體撞擊後不同之
破壞模式推導消能及抗撞阻力估算式。 
3.流固藕合分析軟體 
目前可用以分析流固藕合相關問題之
電腦分析軟體，有 ABAQUS、STAGE/USA、
NASTRAN/USA，其中 ABAQUS 為美國
HKS 公司所發展的多功能有限元素軟體，
STAGE(Structural Analysis of General Shells)
為通用殼結構分析軟體，NASTRAN 為美國
MSC 公司發展的多功能有限元素軟體，而
USA 為美國洛克希德公司發展的水下爆震
分析軟體，此軟體由 Geers 利用雙向漸近近
似法(DAA)為架構發展而得。另近年有些結
構分析軟體，如 Bathe 所發展的 ADINA、
美國 MSC 公司發展的 DYTRAN 及美國
Century 公司所發展 AUTODYN 等，此三種
軟體在處理流固藕合問題上，使用的方法異
於前述諸種軟體，意即利用任意尤拉-拉格
坎奇法(Euler-Lagrange Method)將流固藕合
問題納入分析範疇。 
由前述的文獻回顧得知，流固藕合理論
自 1950、1960 年代的解析法，逐漸發展至
1970、1980 年的數值解，並於 1980、1990
年代發展成分析軟體，已漸趨成熟。上述文
獻且多偏向單一構型如圓筒殼等，且均假設
此種壓力殼均為理想圓，而未考慮圓度效
應，然而對於水下載具壓力殼的研究，同時
考量圓度效應、壓力殼承受動態爆震負荷之
流固藕合效應分析之文獻較為少見。因此本
計畫利用有限元素法(Finite Element Method, 
FEM)結合雙向漸近近似法[139]及空間離散
法[140]，建立一套計算程序，探討圓度效應
對於承受爆震負荷下之水下載具壓力殼非
線性反應行為。 
3.研究方法與步驟

3.1 水下爆炸效應理論 
爆炸屬於化學反應，反應發生時，會在
極短時間內將炸藥轉換成等質量、等體積之
高溫高壓氣體，一般爆裂物處於備炸狀態
時，均為不穩定之混合物，一旦引爆，即會
透過化學反應，立即轉化為較穩定之狀態；
傳統高爆彈頭或核子武器在水下引爆時，由
於能量急劇釋放，因而瞬間出現高溫及高壓
之氣泡球體(Gas Bubble)，同時引發爆震波
(Shock Wave)向外擴散，爆炸之瞬間壓力可
  12
態，則可由水面觀察到由爆炸中心點為中
心，以高速擴大之環狀陰影(Dark Ring)，此
稱之為水面凹凸效應（Slick），此現象係由
於水面凹凸不平所造成，其主因為震波在水
面移動速度高於本身傳播速度所致，而環狀
陰影(Dark Ring)之形成，則為水面凹凸不平
致光線折射或反射之緣故。 
(b)白變現象（Turn White） 
隨 Slick 現象之後，可發現水面顏色泛
白，形成白變現象，此係由於震波之反射波
為張力波，致水面下之壓力減少，因而產生
空蝕現象之緣故。 
(c)圓頂浪花(Spray Dome) 
白變現象之後，隨之發生圓頂浪花
(Spray Dome)，此係由於震波到達水面，受
到碰撞，使得水表面部份產生離散所發生之
現象，此時 Spray Dome 將持續上昇至昇力
等於重力與空氣阻力之總和為止，其中
Spray Dome 之水平直徑之最大值發生於氣
泡脈衝現象中氣泡膨脹結束並開始收縮之
瞬間；另當爆炸深度達一定深度且爆炸威力
足夠時，將由於氣泡球體之脈衝作用而產生
二次 Spray Dome；以承受 300 lb TNT 高爆
炸藥為例： 
 當最小爆炸深度為 50 ft 時，可產生二
次 Spray Dome。 
 爆炸深度達到 100 ft 時，首次 Spray 
Dome 結束後及發生二次 Spray Dome。 
 若爆炸深度達到 85 ft 時，則第三次
Spray Dome 亦將產生。 
 當爆炸深度達到 140 ft 時，最初震波
之Spray Dome若能發展完全並充分凹
入時，亦可發生第三次 Spray Dome。 
(d)水屏(Plume) 
Plume 為氣泡球體到達水面時所發生，
其形狀與氣泡球體到達水面之相狀態之不
同而有所差異，當其到達水面時若呈壓縮狀
態時，則 Plume 呈圓柱型態  (Columnar 
Form)，反之若呈膨脹狀態，則 Plume 呈不
規則灌木型態( Irregular Bushy Appearance 
Form)。如以 300 lb TNT 為例，在不同爆炸
深度時其水平狀態如表 2.所示；300 lb TNT
在不同爆炸深度時之水面效應實驗照片如
圖 12[64]所示。 
在水面以下亦將由於伸張波作用而形
成一空蝕區(Cavitation Zone)，故在水下某一
點之爆震波壓力峰值歷程，將因入射波之影
響使得其合成壓力低於周圍之靜水壓力，而
產生爆震波切斷效應(Cut-off Effect)（如圖
13(a)[66,131]），事實上，因水介質無法承受
張力，而且當壓力降至水之蒸汽壓時，將產

所產生負壓之現象並無法藉由感測器量測
得知（如圖 13(b)[66,131]）。

件的穩定積分，對於解的精度與所選擇的時
間步長直接相關，當時間步長 t 增加會導
致解的精度降低，而無限制的降低又將耗費
過多的 CPU 運算時間，因此時間步長必須
十分謹慎。

規範來進行壓力殼之品質檢驗，有關壓力殼
圓度之規範，由於各國之建造工藝、材料水
準、製造成本、設計理念以及資料技術保密
等因素，因此各國之規範均有不同，目前蒐
集文獻中，有關壓力殼圓度規範標準較重要
  20

7.6 流場域爆壓分佈歷程 
圖 47 為爆震波衝擊圓錐型壓力殼過程
中，流體域之爆震壓力分佈歷程；圖 47 可
以發現因流體域負壓所造成空蝕區域隨震
波前進之現象，造成局部空蝕的原因在於入
射衝擊波接觸圓錐殼時，會被反射成負壓，
導致流體與圓錐殼體介面間的動態壓力急

THE TRANSIENT RESPONSE OF IMPERFECT THIN-WALLED STIFFENED CYLINDRICAL SHELL 
EXPOSED TO SIDE-ON UNDERWATER EXPLOSION 
 
 
 
 
ABSTRACT 
The thin-walled stiffened cylindrical shells are usually 
applied in a submarine which takes the external pressure 
load, or in a boiler, pressure vessel or pipeline system which 
takes the internal pressure load. The thin-walled stiffened 
cylindrical shells under hydrodynamic loading are very 
sensitive to geometrical imperfections. This study is 
investigating an imperfect thin-walled stiffened cylindrical 
shell（out-of-round ratio is =2%） at a depth of 50m below 
the water level to see how it withstands sideward TNT 782 
kg underwater explosion loading so as to understand its 
structural transient response. ABAQUS finite element 
software is used as an analysis tool in the current study, 
meanwhile, during the analysis process, the Fluid-Structure 
Interaction (FSI) condition is employed. The structural 
transient response results of stress and displacement time 
history of the imperfect thin-walled stiffened cylindrical 
shell can be used as a reference for the anti-underwater 
explosion analysis and design of future submersible vehicles, 
pressure hulls or related structural designs. 
Keywords : imperfect stiffened cylindrical shell, 
underwater explosion 
 
1. INTRODUCTION 
Commercial and military vehicles usually adopt 
stiffened cylindrical shells in the form of a pressure-resistant 
structure. For military submersible vehicle, in addition to 
considering water pressure loading, the underwater 
explosion loading generated during the operation 
environment should be considered too. When a commercial 
submersible vehicle is located in an unknown underwater 
environment, it will unavoidably encounter the impact 
loading generated during the collision behaviour. Therefore, 
the nonlinear response generated by the submersible vehicle 
shell due to such dynamic transient is a very important factor 
to be considered when performing pressure hull structural 
design and analysis. Military submarine pressure hull mainly 
comprises of three traditional pressure hull structures such as: 
cylinder, dome end and conical pressure hull (as shown in 
Figure 1).  
 
Figure 1. A structural illustration of shell of submarine
 
Since stiffened cylindrical shell possesses good space 
planning and volume efficiency in the internal cabinet 
allocation, it thus occupies most of the space in a pressure 
hull. The circular cross-section is the best configuration for 
the stiffened cylindrical shell to resist the external pressure 
loading. In fact it is impossible for the stiffened cylindrical 
shell to have circular cross-section configuration owing to 
the construction technology or the cost. Therefore it goes 
without saying that this study is very important for the 
transient response of imperfect thin-walled stiffened cylindrical 
shell exposed to side-on underwater explosion. 
The numerical method for performing explosion 
analysis can be classified as spatial discretization and 
surface approximation, which have their own respective 
advantages and disadvantages. The major advantage of 
spatial discretization approximation is that it can relatively 
simulate the reaction between the entire flow field and the 
structure. Its disadvantage is that it takes massive 
computer memory space and operation time. Its 
development can be dated back to 1969, Zienkiewicz et al. 
[1] used finite element method to diverge the structure into 
stiffness matrix equation, and the fluid motion is 
represented by Navier-Stokes equation and the solution is 
found through Galerkin weighted value procedure. In 
1978, Marcus [2] used finite element method 
(NASTRAN) to solve the free vibration reaction of the 
underwater suspension plate. In 1983, due to the computer 
operation speed and memory space limitation in the era of 
1970-1980, this method is not massively adopted. Until 
1990, computer and numerical method was rapidly 
developed and such method was gradually adopted and 
used every now and then. In 1995, Huang and Kiddy [3] 
used finite differential method and Eulerian-Lagrangian

THE TRANSIENT RESPONSE OF IMPERFECT THIN-WALLED STIFFENED CYLINDRICAL SHELL 
EXPOSED TO SIDE-ON UNDERWATER EXPLOSION 
 
 
 
 
 
 
ABSTRACT 
The thin-walled stiffened cylindrical shells are usually 
applied in a submarine which takes the external pressure 
load, or in a boiler, pressure vessel or pipeline system 
which takes the internal pressure load. The thin-walled 
stiffened cylindrical shells under hydrodynamic loading are 
very sensitive to geometrical imperfections. This study is 
investigating an imperfect thin-walled stiffened cylindrical 
shell（out-of-round ratio is =2%） at a depth of 50m below 
the water level to see how it withstands sideward TNT 782 
kg underwater explosion loading so as to understand its 
structural transient response. ABAQUS finite element 
software is used as an analysis tool in the current study, 
meanwhile, during the analysis process, the Fluid-Structure 
Interaction (FSI) condition is employed. The structural 
transient response results of stress and displacement time 
history of the imperfect thin-walled stiffened cylindrical 
shell can be used as a reference for the anti-underwater 
explosion analysis and design of future submersible 
vehicles, pressure hulls or related structural designs. 
Keywords : imperfect stiffened cylindrical shell, 
underwater explosion 
 
1. INTRODUCTION 
Commercial and military vehicles usually adopt 
stiffened cylindrical shells in the form of a 
pressure-resistant structure. For military submersible 
vehicle, in addition to considering water pressure loading, 
the underwater explosion loading generated during the 
operation environment should be considered too. When a 
commercial submersible vehicle is located in an unknown 
underwater environment, it will unavoidably encounter the 
impact loading generated during the collision behaviour. 
Therefore, the nonlinear response generated by the 
submersible vehicle shell due to such dynamic transient is a 
very important factor to be considered when performing 
pressure hull structural design and analysis. Military 
submarine pressure hull mainly comprises of three 
traditional pressure hull structures such as: cylinder, dome 
end and conical pressure hull (as shown in Figure 1).  
 
Figure 1. A structural illustration of shell of submarine
 
Since stiffened cylindrical shell possesses good space 
planning and volume efficiency in the internal cabinet 
allocation, it thus occupies most of the space in a pressure 
hull. The circular cross-section is the best configuration for 
the stiffened cylindrical shell to resist the external pressure 
loading. In fact it is impossible for the stiffened cylindrical 
shell to have circular cross-section configuration owing to 
the construction technology or the cost. Therefore it goes 
without saying that this study is very important for the 
transient response of imperfect thin-walled stiffened cylindrical 
shell exposed to side-on underwater explosion. 
The numerical method for performing explosion

surface approximation, which have their own respective 
advantages and disadvantages. The major advantage of 
spatial discretization approximation is that it can 
relatively simulate the reaction between the entire flow 
field and the structure. Its disadvantage is that it takes

listed in the literature [6]. After the incident shock wave 
contacts the structure, the reflected pressure wave called 
scattered pressure wave, should be taken into account in the

5. CONCLUSIONS 
In this paper, ideal and imperfect stiffened cylindrical 
shells containing external ribs are used as the target and 
ABAQUS is used as a tool to study the response of these 
cylindrical shells at different out-of-round ratios by taking 
underwater explosion loading with the structure-fluid 
coupling effect taken into account. The results are 
summarized in the following: 
(1) During the action period of the explosion wave, the 
maximum acceleration for the ideal and imperfect 
stiffened cylindrical shell at water depths of 50m all 
occur at 1.0002s after the start of the explosion. The 
maximum acceleration of the ideal model is 46,004.2 
m/sec2 and the imperfect model is 60,267.9 m/sec2. It 
can be seen that the out-of-round ratio is increased by 
2%, but the maximum acceleration of standoff point of 
the stiffened cylindrical shell increases by about 31%. 
(2) During the action period of the explosion wave, the 
displacement of the standoff point forms a peak shape. 
The maximum value of the displacement for the ideal 
model occurs at 1.0032s after the start of the explosion 
and for the imperfect model occurs at 1.0026s. The 
occurred time of the maximum displacement of the 
standoff point for the imperfect model is lesser than the 
ideal model. The maximum displacement of standoff 
point of the ideal model is 10.4075 mm and the 
imperfect model is 21.4186 mm. It can be seen that the 
out-of-round ratio is increased 2%, the maximum 
displacement of the standoff point of stiffened 
cylindrical shell increases by about 105.80%. 
(3) On the outer surface, the larger the out-of-round ratio, 
the higher the reflected von Mises stress, during the 
shock wave action period. At water depth of 50m, the 
maximum von Mises stress of the standoff point for 
ideal model occurs at 1.0032s with a value of 317.716 
Mpa and for the imperfect model occurs at 1.0011s 
with a value of 641.871 Mpa. It can also be seen that 
the out-of-round ratio is increased by 2%, but the von 
Mises at the outer surface of the standoff point of the 
stiffened cylindrical shell increases by about 102.02%.
