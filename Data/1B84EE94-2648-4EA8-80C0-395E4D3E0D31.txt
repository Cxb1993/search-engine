一、 中文摘要 
本計畫為三年期專題研究計畫之第二年計
畫 ， 延 續 第 一 年 計 畫 運 用 微 機 電 系 統
(mircoelectromechanical systems, MEMS)成功
完成彎曲平板波(flexural plate wave, FPW)感
測元件之關鍵製程模組開發之後，本期
(Phase-II)計畫成功完成 (1) FPW 元件製程整
合；(2)開發 SAM 固定化技術以及沉積 IgE 抗
體；(3) ELISA 定量分析 IgE 抗原附著率&質量
靈敏度測試；(4) FPW 感測器量測平台建立；
(5) FPW 元件特性量測與分析。第三年計畫的
研究工作包含：(1)建立可精密控制矽薄膜厚度
之電化學蝕刻停止技術與系統；(2)建立 FPW
過敏感測晶片特性量測電路(雙埠聲波延遲線
振盪迴路)與系統；(3)進行 FPW 過敏感測晶片
之製程整合-完成「自我組裝單分子層(SAM)/
免疫球蛋白 E(IgE)抗體/抗原層」與 FPW 元件
之整合； (4)應用原子力顯微鏡 (AFM)觀察
SAM 每一單層與 IgE 抗體/抗原層的表面奈米
結構與吸附情形，並探討表面分子粗糙度與
IgE 抗體/抗原層專一性結合之關聯性；(5)進行
FPW 過敏感測晶片之封裝與靜態/動態特性量
測與分析。 
 
關鍵字：彎曲平板波、生醫感測器、免疫球蛋
白 E、自我組裝單分子層、微機電系統。 
 
英文摘要 
This project is the secondary phase of the 
three-year project. After developing the key 
process modules of flexural plate wave (FPW) 
sensor in first year, this research has successfully 
implemented (1) perform the process integration 
of FPW device and (2) apply bio-immobilization 
technology and nanotechnology to explore a 
SAM on chip for detecting the antigen 
concentration of IgE in human blood serum, (3) 
analyze the absorption of IgE antigen and mass 
sensitivity by ELISA immunoassay method, (4) 
build up a measurement platform and (5) 
proceed the characteristics analysis of the FPW 
device. 
In the third year, the project aims to (1) 
development of an electrochemical etching-stop 
technology and system for fine-thickness control 
application, (2) build-up a two-port acoustic 
wave delay-line-based oscillator loop circuit for 
the characterization of the FPW allergy 
biosensor, (3) integration of the self-assembly 
monolayer (SAM) and immunoglobulin E (IgE) 
antibody/antigen layers into the back-side cavity 
of the FPW device, (4) observation of the surface 
nanostructure and absorption of the SAM and 
IgE layers by using atomic force microscope 
(AFM) and (5) performance of the package and 
static/dynamic characterization of the FPW 
allergy immunosensor.

2、FPW 元件的頻譜分析 
頻譜響應是大部份聲波元件極為重要的
特性響應，本計畫利用中山大學電機系所提供
之 3GHz 網路分析儀(HP-8714ET)以量測 FPW
的四個 S 參數、元件波速、插入損(insertion

之關係圖三為目前所成功開發之 FPW 元件頻

性，元件中心頻率測得為 22.675MHz，此值與
理論值(20MHz)相當吻合，元件之插入損失約
為-40dB。

3、自我組裝單層之沉積 
分子的自我組裝是一種自發的組織行
為，分子會自動組成穩定無雜質、且表面具
有排序整齊的結構定義[9]。為了利用 FPW 元
件背面的腔體來作為檢測血清中 IgE 濃度的
環境，所以必須沉積一層 SAM 於 FPW 元件
背面的懸浮薄膜上，且為了增加 SAM 的黏附
性，必須先以電子束蒸鍍機沉積一層鉻/金薄
膜於 FPW 元件背面。其整體之製程如下：將
整個 FPW 元件的背面浸置於胱胺酸
(cystamine)溶液中一小時，並以去離子水(DI 
water)清洗晶片三次；於室溫下乾燥之後再浸
置 於 稀 釋 成 2.5%(v/v) 的 戊 二 醛
(glutaraldehyde，GA) 一小時，待晶片清洗與
乾燥之後即可完成胱胺酸-戊二醛自我組裝
單分子層的沉積製程。

四、 結論與討論 
本計畫的研究成果可總結為下列兩點： 
1、 FPW 元件結構分析與特性量測   
經實驗結果證明，FPW 元件於異向性蝕
刻製程時若可保留 2~3 μm 厚的矽懸浮薄板結
構，則可有效防止撓曲現象，並進而提高元件
製造良率與頻率響應的準確度；另一方面，目
前開發之 FPW 元件經證明其具有極佳的濾波
器特性，其元件的中心頻率為 22.675 MHz，
與理論值(20 MHz)相當吻合，且其元件的插入
損失僅約為-40 dB。 
2、 自我組裝單層與 IgE 抗體/抗源層之沉積 
  目前已成功研發出具有固定化 IgE 抗體、
抗原自我組裝單層之技術，且利用 ELISA 法
固定 IgE 抗體，於不同之 IgE 抗源濃度下
(0.0884, 0.1767, 0.3535, 0.707, 1.414, 2.828 
μg/ml)其線性度達 0.9937，證明其 IgE 抗體與
IgE 抗原具有優良之吸附能力。

3) Piezoelectric thin film deposition and patterning 
During the past decade, many piezoelectric films have 
been reported for the application of acoustic sensors. The 
three films most popular with the sensor industry are ZnO, 
AlN and PZT. In this study, a high-quality C-axis 
orientation ZnO piezoelectric layer is deposited on the metal 
ground plane by a RF-planar magnetron sputter and 
patterned by wet etch method (H3PO4 : CH3COOH : H2O = 
1 : 1 : 30). The advantage of planar magnetron sputtering is 
that electron bombardment of the substrate is greatly 
reduced, so that the wafer temperature can be better 
controlled with an external heater coupled to the wafer [8].

4) IDT electrodes deposition and patterning 
The metal film used to make the IDT must be thick 
enough to offer low electrical resistance and thin enough so 
that it does not present an excessive mechanical load to the 
acoustic wave [1]. In this paper, 200Å-thick Cr and 1500Å-
thick Au layers were constructed of the IDTs electrodes 
which are deposited by an E-beam evaporator and patterned 
by lift-off photolithography. 
5) Single-side anisotropic silicon etching 
After backside Si3N4 is patterned by RIE system and 
SiO2 is etched by BOE etchant, the silicon substrate is 
anisotropic etched in KOH solution for approximately 20 
hours and forms a floating FPW sensing membrane. The 
concentration of the KOH solution is 30% and the 
temperature of which is 60ºC.  
The top-view SEM micrograph of the implemented 
bulk-micromachined FPW device is shown in Fig. 2. The 
I/P and O/P IDTs of the FPW device are both constructed 
by 25 pairs Cr/Au fingers. The width and pitch of the Cr/Au 
electrode are both equal to 12.5 μm, so that the periodicity 
and the wavelength of IDTs are equal to 50 μm.
