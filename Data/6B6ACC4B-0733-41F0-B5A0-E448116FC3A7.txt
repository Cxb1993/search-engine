一、中文摘要 
 
磁旋行波管是藉由電子迴旋頻率的都
卜勒頻移而使電子束的能量轉換為毫米波
的高功率振盪器，並具有寬頻調節特性。
本 計 畫 探 討 使 用 散 佈 式 損 耗
(distributed-loss)磁旋行波管並操作在不同
的電流下，模擬結果顯示操作電流低於自
由起振電流，輸出功率隨著輸入功率線性
增加而增加且相位差為定值，屬於 amplifier
的操作區段。另一方面，當操作電流超過
自由起振電流時，非線性分析模擬結果發
現磁旋行波管在不同的操作下會有三種不
同的操作區間，分別是 amplifier、mode 
competing 和 injection locking 區間。在輸出
性能方面，在軸向電子發散速度是 5%時，
頻寬約有 7 GHz，飽和增益約有 90 dB，飽
和效率約有 27%。在橫向模式競爭方面，
初 步 結 果 顯 示 當 放 大 器 操 作 於
phase-locking oscillation 區間，其輸出功率
將不受 模式影響，進而再提昇操作電
流或增加作用長度，因此將此一機制運用
於低電壓操作之磁旋行波放大器，此放大
器將具有高操作電流或長作用長度的特
性，如此可彌補因操作電壓降低所造成作
用效率下降。

Abstract 
 
A gyrotron backward wave oscillator 
and traveling wave tube are a promising 
source of coherent millimeter wave radiation 
based on the electron maser instability. 
However, the requirement on phase 
coherence is strict for applications. In this 
plan, using distributed-loss Gyro-TWA and 
operating at different current, the simulation 
results of gyro-TWA driven by an external 
signal show that there are three roots exist. 
When the beam current is below free-running 
start oscillation current, the results of the 
nonlinear simulation are consistent with the 
results of the amplifier’s linear theory. This 
is an amplifier regime. When the beam 
current is above start oscillation current, 
there are three different operating regimes, 
amplifier regime, mode competing regime 
and phase-locking oscillation regime in a 
gyro-TWA driven by an external signal. The 
performance of gyro-TWA is predicted to 
yield the peak power of 200 kW corresponds 
to a saturated gain of 90 dB and saturated 
efficiency of 27%, bandwidth of 7 GHz and 
electron beam with an axial velocity spread 5 
%. In the mode competing, when the 
traveling wave tube operating in the 
phase-locking oscillation mode can 
effectively suppress the oscillation of 
non-operating mode. Using this mechanism 
in low-voltage operation of the Gyro-TWA, 
will have high current and intention length of 
long, so can compensate for low operating 
voltage drop caused by the efficiency of 
decrease. 
 
Keywords: gyrotron traveling wave 
amplification, cyclotron autoresonance maser 
 
二、緣由與目的 
 
毫米波段的高功率微波管廣泛應用於
通訊、工業及軍事，而操作於磁旋共振鎂
射 不 穩 定 (cyclotron resonance maser 
instability)的磁旋管(gyrotron) [1]，其輸出
連續功率在 110 GHz 可達 0.5 MW、效率約
為 35%[2-5]，適用於提供高功率毫米波
源 。 就 放 大 器 而 言 ， 磁 旋 調 速 管
(gyroklystron)可產生 94GHz 的最高功率
(peak power)80kW[6]及8.6GHz 的最高功率
80MW[7]，而磁旋行波放大器 (gyrotron 
traveling wave amplifier ; gyro-TWA)則可
提供 26.5％效率(efficiency)、70 dB 高增益
(gain)、3 GHz 的頻寬(bandwidth) [8]。
