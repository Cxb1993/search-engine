Piezoelectricity material is one of the 
most widely used sensing materials in the 
research of smart material systems. As one of 
the  piezoelectricity materials, PVDF film 
has received extensive attention for its 
property advantages of low cost, good 
mechanical ability, high sensibility and so on. 
In this project, research is done to study 
the strain sensing properties of PVDF film as 
the strain sensor.and PVDF strain monitoting 
system is developed based on the hardware 
and software of signal collecting. Finally, 
experiment on PVDF film is carried out to 
monitor structure cracks. The crack 
monitoring process of metal specimen, the 
crack monitoring process, including crack 
initiation, crack propagation and fracture 
based on PVDF sensor is executed and will be 
demonstrated. The pulsed signal generated by 
PVDF sensor, when the crack propagate 
across the PVDF sensor, can be used to reveal 
the useful information for safety evaluation 
and damage location
