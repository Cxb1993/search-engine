雷射動態量測儀在 1990 年首度在芝加哥工具機展覽館展示，後來 Leica 公
司將其改良成 Smart 310 型「雷射動態量測儀」[20]。其可對笨重複雜或不易
移動的大型物件（例如 20 公尺長機翼）進行 3-D 座標量測，同時也可以用在零
件組裝、校準機器人、輪廓外形及逆向工程與表面檢測。此系統的原廠規格為:
角度解析度 0.7 秒，距離分辨率為 1.26 mμ ，追蹤角度範圍為水平 0235± 垂直
045± ，追蹤距離範圍為 0.2m 至 25m。從以上資料可知此「雷射動態量測儀儀」
的性能應該比一般三次元量床(CMM)(例如 BHN710 型)要好。然而這款新的儀器
也存在著一些尚未解決的問題，使得此儀器的量測精度，尚無法與三次元量床
媲美。其原因有：(1)其旋轉鏡機構存在著連桿尺寸誤差，使得該儀器依然無法
取代 CMM；(2)若無精確地對系統的控制方程式建模，就無法求得量測尺寸的正
確值；(3) 反射器與其入射光束間的相對校準誤差也會影響光束行進的總長，
因而影響干涉儀的讀數。因「雷射動態量測儀」有潛力成為量測的重要儀器，
故激起本研究之企圖。國科會精密儀器中心於 93 年 10 月 26 日，將原價值 1162

ABSTRACT 
Laser tracker (e.g., Leica Smart 310 [1]) is one of the most potential apparatuses for 
3D coordinate measurement. Its basic technology was originally developed by a 
group at the US National Bureau of Standards, with further private development at 
Automated Precision Inc. (USA). Leica redesigned it and presented it at 1990 Quality 
Show in Chicago. Leica's specifications for the Smart 310 are: angle resolution 0.7 
sec; distance resolution 1.26 μm; tracking ranges of horizontal and vertical angles are 
respectively 0235±  and 045± ; tracking distance range is 0.2m to 25m. In other 
words, the Leica Smart 310 is capable of measurements one micron. Clearly, laser 
tracking system is potentially superior to CMM. However, the measurement accuracy 
of laser tracker is highly dependent on the accuracy of the link dimensions of its 
rotating mirror mechanism. Further, the alignment errors of the target retro-reflector 
with respect to its incoming ray also affect the optical path length and thus the 
interferometer readings. It is impossible to properly model the system's governing 
equations and obtain accurate measurement values without accurate dimension 
values of the mirror’s links. The literature offers little data regarding system 
modeling for laser tracking systems. We have studied Leica Smart 310 for three years 
by running a project sponsored by National Science Council. Due to our results and 
research capacity, Precision Instrument Development Center of National Science 
Council donated a laser tracker, model 1300A of Excel Precision Company, to our 
Laboratory on Dec. 26th of 1994. 1300A model is different from the Leica Smart 310, 
the one we studied last project. In this project the governing equations of 1300A were 
re-derived by using skew ray tracing method. Effects of mirror's mechanism 
dimension errors were also investigated by sensitivity analysis. Based on the

1.3 雷射追蹤技術之應用  
目前雷射追蹤技術已廣泛被運用於各種不同領域。例如在治療近視的雷射
手術中，被用來進行眼球的追蹤定位，可修正雷射手術中眼球不自主顫動所造
成之偏移誤差，提升精確度(如圖 1.4)。在軍事方面，被應用於測距和追蹤工作，
雷射追蹤系統提供武器控制所需三個座標，能有效對目標追蹤或反擊來襲的飛
彈，如美國砲兵使用之 AN/GVS-5 雷射測距儀(如圖 1.5)。此外，國內的漢翔
航空公司也將雷射動態量測儀用於執行大型物體精密量測、精密定位、運動軌
跡之量測，逆向工程及大型物體曲面模型建構(如圖 1.6)。
