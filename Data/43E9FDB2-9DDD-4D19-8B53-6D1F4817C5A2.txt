摘要 
本研究為探討摻和醇胺(2-氨基 2-甲基 
1-丙醇/對二氮已環)水溶液吸收二氧化碳之
氣液平衡量測研究，所探討之系统為： 
CO2/AMP/PZ/H2O。所量測之系统濃度範圍： 
CO2/AMP/PZ/H2O則為 0.5、0.1及 1.5 kmol/m3 
PZ 分別加入 2.0 及 3.0 kmol/m3 AMP 水溶液
中。溫度範圍為 30 至 80oC。所量測之氣液平
衡數據將由具理論基礎之電解質溶液理論
Cleeg-Pitzer model 來表示，模式中配對作用
力參數包括溶劑-溶質、溶劑-電解質、電解質
-電解質等將由所量測之氣液平衡數據來決
定。熱物性質將包括密度、黏度、亨利常數；
擴散係數量測將包括二氧化碳在摻和醇胺水
溶液之擴散係數及醇胺水溶液中之醇胺擴散
係數。 
計畫執行期間為一年。本年之工作項目
為(1) 電解質溶液理論 Cleeg-Pitzer model 來
計算 CO2/AMP/PZ/H2O 氣液平衡，程式撰寫
及測試計算結果。(2)氣液平衡設備架設及試
車。(3) AMP/PZ/H2O 之熱物性質量測：(a)密
度、黏度量測，(b)N2O 在 AMP/PZ/H2O 之亨
利常數及擴散係數量測，並由 N2O Analogy
推算 CO2 在 AMP/PZ/H2O 之亨利常數及擴散
係數，(c)以泰勒分散法量測 AMP/PZ 在水中
之交互擴散係數。(4) AMP/PZ/H2O 吸收二氧
化碳之比熱量測。(5)CO2/AMP/PZ/H2O 氣液
平衡量測，並由電解質溶液理論 Cleeg-Pitzer 
model 來計算 CO2/AMP/PZ/H2O 氣液平衡。
本年之工作已依進度如期完成。

Abstract 
    This research proposes the measurements 
of the vapor-liquid equilibrium, thermophysical 
properties, and diffusion coefficients for the 
absorption of carbon dioxide into aqueous 
blends of 2-amino-2-methyl-1-propanol (AMP) 
and piperazine (PZ) solutions. The 
concentration ranges will be: 2.0 and 3.0 kmol 
m-3 AMP; 0.5, 1.0, and 1.5 kmol m-3 PZ for 
CO2/AMP/PZ/H2O. The temperature will range 
from 30 to 80oC. The experimental vapor-liquid 
equilibrium will be correlated using the 
theoretical based model of Clegg-Pitzer. The 
interaction parameters of molecular-molecular 
pairs, ion-molecular pairs, and ion-ion pairs will 
be determined from the VLE data. 
Thermophysical properties such as density, 
viscosity, Henry’s constant and heat capacity 
will be studied. And diffusion coefficient of 
carbon dioxide in amine solution and the 
mutual diffusion coefficient in amine solutions 
will also be studied.

(D). 氣液平衡計算流程 
在液態中，有 8 個未知濃度值，即 CO2、H2O、
AMP、PZ、AMPH+、PZH+、 +3H O 、 3HCO−
等，應可解出液態中各物種之濃度。故本計 
畫之氣液平衡計算流程如下： 
(i). 在某一實驗條件下，由起始預估參數，
先由上述 8 個濃度方程式計算各物種在
液態中之摩爾分率。 
(ii). 由計算出之摩爾分率再與平衡常數進行
相平衡計算，由計算出之二氧化碳分壓
及液態摩爾分率。 
(iii). 計算之氣液平衡值和實驗值比較，經迴
歸方法(regression analysis)決定最終之
二物種較佳之作用力參數，如分子-分子
對(如 H2O-PZ、H2O-AMP 等)、分子-離
子對 (如 H2O (AMPH+ HCO3-)、AMP 
(PZH+ H3O+)等)。 
所得之二物種作用力參數將測試其計算敏感
度 ， 以 最 小 數 目 之 參 數 值 來 代 表
CO2/AMP/PZ/H2O 之氣液平衡行為。 
 
II、 氣液平衡及熱物性質量測 
(A) 氣液平衡量測 
氣液平衡數據量測通常在一控制溫度之
平衡槽內進行，氣相可循環以加速氣液平衡
之達成，氣相組成由連線之氣體層析儀分
析，當系統壓力、溫度和氣相組成保持不變，
可判定平衡達成，平衡達成後，液態取樣由
酸鹼滴定決定酸性氣體負載。本實驗之詳細
裝置及實驗步驟已列於文獻14。 
(B) 密度、黏度量測 
氣體吸收液之溶液密度、黏度之量測將
由密黏度儀(Anton Paar, model SVM 3000, 
Stabinger Viscometer)來量測。本密黏度儀溫
度可控制在±0.02oC；量測密度的正確性
(accuracy)為0.0005 g cm-3，量測黏度的正確性
為±0.35%。量測密度之再現性(repeatability)
為0.0002 g cm-3；量測黏度之再現性為±0.2%。 
(C) 比熱之量測 
吸收液之比熱可由示差掃描熱量計來量
測，本實驗使用示差掃描熱量計(Differential 
Scanning Calorimeter, DSC) 測AMP/PZ/H2O
之比熱值，量測的溫度範圍為30℃至80℃。
比熱之量測，DSC設備及實驗步驟可參考文
獻(Ho et al., 2007)。 
(D) 物理溶解度(亨利常數)量測 
本實驗是使用物理溶解度量測裝置來量
測亨利常數。此原理是使用一已知體積的氣
體吸收溶液與一已知體積的溶質氣體在定
溫、定壓下接觸，經由攪拌達到平衡，測量
被吸收氣體的體積變化推算出氣體溶於溶液
之物理溶解度。本實驗之詳細裝置及實驗步
驟已列於文獻16中。

Experimental Section 
 
Chemicals. 
All ILs used in this work were supplied by TCI Co. except for [Bmim][MeSO4] which 
were from STREM Co. with a minimum purity of 98 % by weight The ILs were used without 
further purification. A water purification system supplied by Barnstead Thermodyne (model 
Easy Pure 1052) was used to provide Type I reagent-grade water for the refractive index 
measurements with a resistivity of 18.3 (M⋅Ωcm) and with a total organic carbon content of 
less than 15 ppb. 
 
Density and refractive index measurements 
The densities of ILs were measured from (298.2 to 353.2) K while the refractive indices 
were from (298.2 to 308.2) K. The temperature ranges were different because of the 
refractometer’s limitation. 
The densities of IL were measured using a viscometer from Anton Paar Stabinger 
(model SVM 3000). The density was measured by the integrated density measuring cell, 
which used the principle of oscillating U-tube.  The integrated thermostat with cascaded 
Peltier elements and a platinum thermometer as well as the low thermal mass of the 
measuring cell, enabled rapid changes and exact adjustment of the measuring temperature. 
The repeatability of the density measurement was ±0.0002 (g⋅cm-3). The estimated 
uncertainties were: temperature ±0.002 K and density ±0.0004 (g⋅cm-3). The measurements 
were done in 3 to 5 replicate runs. 
The refractive indices of IL were measured using a commercial refractometer from 
Atago (model PAL-RI). The measurement uncertainty of the refractometer is ±0.0003 for 
refractive index (with water as reference at 20oC). The refractometer requires only 0.3 mL of 
the sample. A procedure called “zero setting” was always performed before the actual 
measurements of the sample’s refractive index, to ensure that the refractometer is working 
properly. Type I reagent-grade liquid water was used in performing the “zero setting” 
procedure. The measurements were done in 3 to 5 replicate runs.
