中英文摘要 
    In this proposal, it was aimed at developing a powder synthesis method with advantages such 
as energy efficiency, simple processing, high production rate, and easily obtainable reactants, 
products with superior properties, widely applicable by combing in the advantages of several 
different material processing techniques. These techniques (and their advantages) include: 
microwave techniques, solution combustion synthesis method, and carbothermal nitridation 
techniques. The synthesis method was developed for the preparation of nano size TiO2 
photocatalyst powder and AlN powder based on our forgoing research experience and results. 
Furthermore, the development of the synthesis method for solid oxide fuel cell (SOFC) and nano 
size AlN powder were also examined and tested for their properties.  
    本研究計畫之目的在於微波輔助-溶液燃燒合成奈米級可見光激發 TiO2 光觸媒粉體及結
合熱碳還原氮化法以進行奈米級 AlN 之製程開發。光觸媒材料由於奈米化所帶來的高比表面
積與量子效應，使其有極高的應用發展潛力。本研究以溶液燃燒合成法合成之 TiO2 在可見光
範圍內有相當優良之光觸媒效能，然而，溶液燃燒合成法，由於加熱引燃的過程反應物內溫
度分布極不均勻，因此，運用微波技術，結合溶液燃燒合成法，由於微波加熱之快速與均勻
性，當可解決溶液燃燒合成欠缺均勻性的問題。 
    在奈米級氮化鋁粉體的合成與生成機制之探討中，其研究的方法主要是以溶液燃燒合成
法結合熱碳還原氮化法來改善傳統熱碳還原法之反應物混合不均勻及反應溫度過高之關鍵問
題，本研究是以硝酸鋁搭配不同之還原劑：尿素及氨基乙酸，並以水溶性化合物蔗糖為碳源，
以反應物劑量比、鍛燒時間及溫度、氮化溫度為變數進行研究。

結果與討論 
一、燃燒現象 
燃燒反應中反應物依參數不同反應型態亦有所不同，當燃料比例低於化學劑量比時，反
應呈現無火焰多煙的緩慢燃燒現象，其反應中所釋放出來的熱量也較少。燃料與氧化劑恰為
劑量比時，反應先驅物中所含的氧氣量為燃燒反應的主要來源，因此當硝基反應成氧氣時即
快速地與燃料發生氧化反應，此反應現象幾乎是整體同時性的燃燒。而在高比例的燃料反應
中，須由外界提供燃燒時所需的氧氣，燃燒反應在反應前驅物的任一處被引燃後，燃燒波可
平穩地傳播前進，整個燃燒反應即可自行維持至反應前驅物完全燃燒為止。 
二、材料特性 
由本研究方法製備 TiO2 光觸媒，不僅省時節能且所合成之光觸媒在可見光下便可激發，
其 UV 特性吸收光譜如圖一所示，材料於 550nm 便有吸收值。溶液燃燒合成法因過程中會有
大量氣體產生，因此，使得材料具有多孔隙之表面結構，故其 BET 表面積可高達 280m2/g(表
一)。且因其瞬間的高溫反應致使晶相(anatase)易於生成，但卻又因反應後快速的降溫作用，
使得晶粒不易成長，因此，所獲得之產物皆為奈米級之結構，其 XRD 圖譜如圖二所示。圖三
為 TiO2 XPS 全譜圖，鍵結能在 458.4, 529.7 及 284.8eV 分別是 Ti 2p3/2, O 1s 及 C 1s 的鍵結，
更進一步探討 C 在 TiO2 鍵結型態，圖四為 C 1s 能階圖，由圖五可知能階峰出現在 284.4, 286.5
及 288.5eV，而 284.4eV 的 C 為儀器本身所含的碳水化合物，286.5eV 及 288.4eV 分別為 C-O
及 C=O 的鍵結，這是由於 carbonated 物質存在而形成的鍵結，由單一的 Ti 2p 軌域圖譜分析，
能階分別僅出現在 459eV(Ti 2p 3/2)及 464.8eV(Ti 2p 1/2)的位置，由此可知 Ti 2p 僅是以 4+狀
態存在，因此，可以推斷 TiO2 確實有 C 的參雜，因而形成氧的缺陷，而使得 TiO2 在可見光
可以被激發。

Solution combustion synthesis (SCS) was developed from self-propagating 
high-temperature synthesis (SHS) with combination of wet chemical techniques for the 
synthesis of metal oxide based materials. The process possesses both the advantages of 
SHS, which utilizes the in-built exothermicity of combustion of the reaction system to 
directly synthesize the required materials, and the advantages of wet chemical routes, 
which can produce a compositionally homogeneous mixture making both the ignition 
temperature and combustion temperature lower than those traditional SHS.  
SCS is a flexible technique where oxidizing and reducing precursors are mixed on 
the molecular level and, under unique conditions of rapid high-temperature reactions, 
nanoscale powders of desired compositions can be synthesized in one step. The 
oxidizer (typically nitrates or oxalates) and fuel ( e.g., hydrazine, hydrazide, glycine, or 
urea) are mixed in an aqueous solution to reach molecular level homogenization of the 
reaction medium. The solution is then heated until self-ignition, yielding a large volume 
of gas and converting the initial mixture to fine well-crystalline powders of desired 
compositions. Among the advantages of this process are low energy requirements, 
simple reactor setup, short reaction times, and molecular-scale mixing of precursors, 
leading to products of desired composition and microstructure. 
We have an ongoing research program, which is aimed at investigating 
mechanisms of combustion synthesis, a novel technique for the synthesis of TiO2 with a 
high specific surface area and small band gap that can function under visible light 
irradiation. The TiO2 was used to fabricate electrodes for DSSC and was tested for 
performance. 
 
Experimental 
 
Titanyl nitrate was used as a precursor to prepare the nano-sized TiO2 by solution 
combustion method. Titanium (Ш) n-butoxide was hydrolyzed to obtain titanyl 
hydroxide. Titanyl nitrate was then obtained by reaction of titanyl hydroxide with nitric 
acid. Urea or glycine( used as fuels) was added to the aqueous solution of titanyl nitrate 
and the solution was combusted to obtain TiO2 particles. For preparation of TiO2 
thin-film, TiO2 colloical suspensions were prepared by dispersing the TiO2 particles in 
distilled water with polyethelene glycol, (PEG, average MW of 20000) as binder. The 
films were fabricated by dropping the suspension on a transparent conductive oxide 
glass (TOC) by using a doctor blade technique. The films were then dried at room 
temperature and then sintered in air at 450  for 30min.℃  
The sandwich-type solar cell  was assembled by placing a platinum-coated TOC 
glass (counter electrode )beside the N719 dye-sensitized photoelectrode (working 
electrode ) and the edges of the cell were sealed with a hot-melt Surlyn spacer (SX 
1170-60, Solaronix ) by heating it at ~105 . The redox electrolyte was composed of ℃
0.1M LiI, 0.05M I2 and 0.6M 1-propyl-2.3-dinethylimidazolium, and was introduced 
into the cell through two small holes drilled on the counter electrode. The holes were 
then covered and sealed with a small square sealing sheet of microscope objective glass.
