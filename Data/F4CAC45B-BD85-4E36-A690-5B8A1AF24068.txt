三、文獻探討 
組織工程相關文獻 
組織工程是一門跨領域的學科，結合細胞生物學與化學工程學的原理來研究與開發生物性替代組
織(Replacement tissues)，來恢復、維持及改進組織功能。組織工程所採用的策略為使用生物可降解性
的材料做為支架（Scaffold），將捐贈細胞或包含生長因子培植於此支架上，直接去生成健康的組織。
組織工程的核心概念中，有幾個基本要素[1]： 
1. 細胞： 
在組織工程上，首先必須能夠分離出具分化能力，能產生組織的細胞。最終目的是能讓細胞有能
力在體內生長分化，而成為具功能性的組織器官。以骨組織工程為例，首先自病人身上取得骨髓細胞
培養成骨先驅細胞(Osteoprogenitor），亦稱做Osteogenic Cell，再將這些骨質原生細胞植入具三維支架
的生醫材料中，並刺激分化為成骨細胞，成骨細胞會分泌產生骨質母層（Bone Matrix），最後變成骨
細胞（Osteocyte）。 
2. 支架 : 
意即能攜帶細胞的材料。工程學家所製作出的生醫移植材料，如金屬、陶瓷、高分子材料等，雖
然機械強度好，但其缺點為生物相容性不佳，容易產生排斥反應，長期使用後易造成磨損、腐蝕，甚
至降解退化而導致毒性的產生，且無法被生物體所吸收並重新塑造，最後仍需進行二次手術取出。若
是選用生物相容性佳的支架，直接將具有生物活性因子的生醫材料直接植入活體，以材料本身的生物
化學性質去誘導組織及血管新生，待長滿骨頭組織的架構成形後，原先的生物相容性材料會在生物體
內自行降解成小分子後，經由代謝過程[2]而不需再另行開刀出，這種新方法可望為患者再造失去骨頭。
這些材料在填補骨缺損的位置之後，能使成骨細胞貼附、移行、分化與繁殖；具有這種性質的材料，
稱之為具有「骨傳導」(Osteoconduction)性質的生醫材料。以此種材料製成的支架，在支架內部應具有
孔洞結構；而孔洞結構就像是一間間的小房間，經細胞植入後，細胞先貼附在房間的牆壁上，然後慢
慢地往房間中央伸展，最後細胞及其製造出的細胞外基質(Extracellular Matrix; ECM)形成組織而占滿了
整個房間，細胞穿牆形成聯合的整體，長成我們所要的器官。 
3. 生物性的刺激(訊息因子)： 
意即在植入含有骨細胞的支架外，再提供一些具生物活性的因子，比如說像是能幫助細胞黏貼在
支架上的貼附因子，或是促進細胞正常生長的生長因子，以及能夠引導幹細胞分化為想要的細胞分化
因子等，通通都是屬於訊息因子的範疇。目前已知對於細胞有影響的成長因子大致如下：1α,25 dihydroxy 
vitamin D3、24,25 dihydroxy vitaminD3、Transforming growth factor β1(TGF-β1)、Fibroblastgrowth 
factors(FGFs)與Insulin-like growth factor 1 (IGF-1)等物質。這些物質能進一步促進成骨細胞之繁殖、分
化，並維持產生骨頭的生長環境。具有這類性質的物質，稱之為具有「骨誘導」(Osteoinduction) 性質。
其他一些物理因素，如機械應力或超音波等，也會對細胞的增生與分化產生正向的效果。從實際組織
修復的觀點而言，若將單獨細胞從捐贈者的組織先隔離出來，並在適當的支架上培養，再移植到受傷
之組織上，便可達到移植之目的。支架在活體外培養及後續的活體內移植時扮演著隔離細胞的支撐物
及黏著性基質之角色。細胞移植之成功與否，取決於基質材料對細胞培養的合適性。依據相關的研究
文獻，結果顯示生物分解性高分子可讓移植細胞在被細胞本身所分泌的骨骼基質取代前充當暫時性的
支架，並促成組織的再生。因此，利用天然或合成生物分解性高分子材料可使細胞移植技術應用於許

的支架，但孔洞的大小被侷限在13-35μm 的範圍，並不適合細胞的生長。 
( 4 ) 相分離(Phase Separation ) [11,12] 
將高分子聚合物PLLA 與Napthalene 加熱形成一均勻溶液，利用噴霧法形成霧狀累積在模具上驟
冷，在以低壓加熱使Napthalene直接昇華。此做法會使原本分散相的高分子聚合物轉變為連續相，而形
成孔洞。其優點為可藉由改變溶液中PLLA 的濃度調整孔洞尺寸，亦可藉由Napthalene 熔點以下的溫
度，控制支架成形後體積也不會有收縮現象。但此方法製得的支架具有機械性質強度不佳，孔洞結構
容易變形等缺點。 
( 5 ) 氣體發泡( gas foaming ) [13,14] 
將高分子聚合物預先壓製成厚膜，在將此厚膜置入耐壓器具中，再通入高壓二氧化碳使其滲入膜
的內部，隨後急速釋放氣體降低壓力，即形成有孔洞的支架。此方法具有製程快及步驟簡單之優點，
並且可藉由改變氣體的壓力及溫度改變孔洞的尺寸。但美中不足的是無法有效的控制孔洞均勻分布，
使得後續細胞植入結果呈現不均的現象。 
超臨界流體微細發泡相關文獻 
近年來超臨界流體微細發泡製程是一種新興的發泡製程，是利用氮氣或二氧化碳來做發泡劑不但
取得容易也具有環保，同時有減輕產品重量和節省原料使用量的優點。在氣泡尺寸的控制上優於傳統
發泡，因傳統發泡不能自由的控制其氣泡大小與密度，而超臨界流體微細發泡技術則可藉由控制壓力
降來達到均一的氣泡大小，故可逐漸取代傳統發泡製程。 
研究利用超臨界流體微細發泡製程於產品改善方面，2004年山田岳大[15]等人利用超臨界流體微細
發泡製程(Mucell)對於汽車內部塑膠零件部份製成發泡件，以達到提升尺寸精度的目的。材料為耐衝擊
性聚苯乙烯(HIPS)，以N2為發泡劑，觀察料量變化及皮層厚度來探討尺寸精度的影響。結果顯示，可
藉由控制皮層厚度與氣泡密度，達到尺寸精度的要求，且毛邊現象皆有大幅改善。之後在2005年
Kramschuster等人[16]探討微細發泡射出成型製程條件對產品的收縮與翹曲之影響。以聚丙烯為實驗材
料，並使用田口法於傳統和微細發泡作射出成型。結果顯示超臨界流體（SCF）含量和射出速度是影
響微細發泡射出成型製品的收縮、翹曲中最大的製程參數，然而傳統射出成型零件的收縮與翹曲有較
大的影響則是保壓壓力和保壓時間。 
一個超臨界流體微細發泡和傳統發泡塑膠不一樣的地方是氣泡大小，這兩者比較起來:傳統發泡有
較低之密度， 從103 ~106氣泡/cm3 且氣泡較大且不均勻，大於100 微米，因而其強度不好[17]，除此
之外，傳統發泡製程用化學發泡劑，像CFC 氟氯碳化物會破壞臭氧層或碳氫脂舫酸，像戊烷會有著火
之危險，因此有很強之要求要把這些發泡劑換成對環境不會有害之發泡劑像大氣中之氣體。 
微細發泡之射出成型之突起(現已商業化叫Mucell)能產生尺寸穩定且用較低之壓力，較短之生產
週期，和較少之材料，超臨界流體能有效的降低高分子之黏度[18]和玻璃轉換溫度[19]，因為此種原因，
使高分子能用較低的壓力和溫度來處理，且使它更能用於薄殼射出(0.3mm)，當這氣泡跑出氣液混合之
單相溶液，塑膠材料會回復其玻璃轉換溫度，而使其快速脆化，氣泡的成核和成長會吸熱而使熔膠溫
度降低，因此和傳統射出成型或結構發泡射出成型比較起來，微細發泡不必須要那麼多的冷卻和能量，
最後因為發泡所產生的壓力可以消除射出成型品之凹陷(sink marks)和改善尺寸之不穩定，因此它是一
個不需要飽壓的製程，且它也是合乎綠色製造的一種製程，可以節省能源，降低塑膠使用量，沒有公
害之問題。目前因組織工程支架之流行，也可應用於支架之生產上。 
從文獻中得知大部分支架製備的方式都會使用到溶劑，不僅會對環境造成傷害也會影響組織的成
長，因此使用微細發泡之射出成型來製備支架，不但能夠節省成本，也較適用於現今講求環保的時代。

Results and Discussion 
Characterization  
 
XRD is generally employed to characterize the 
interlayer spacing in clay particles, which is correlated 
with their extent of intercalation. Figs. 1 and 2 show the 
XRD diffraction patterns of nanocomposites in systems A 
and B, where the peaks correspond to the (001) plane 
reflection of the clay. It can be seen that the diffraction 
peak moved to a higher angle when the clay is blended 
with polymers, implying that the gallery spacing of the 
clay was reduced during the blending procedure. It might 
be that the organoclay used in this study is not compatible 
with MA grafted LDPE. Apparently, the modifier of the 
organoclay is released during the blending procedure, 
which induces the collapse of the interlayer of the clay. In 
Fig. 1, when the MA content was increased, it exhibited 
an XRD diffraction peak shifted slightly toward a lower 
Bragg angle, resulting in higher interlayer spacing, 
meaning that MA grafted LDPE can serve as a 
compatibilizer and improve the intercalation of the LDPE 
chain to the interlayer of the clay. However, In Fig. 2, 
further increases in the organoclay content did not 
decrease the Bragg angle of the nanocomposites. It might 
be that 2% MA employed as a compatibilizer was unable 
to disperse the clay effectively when the clay content 
exceeds 4 %.   
Since the XRD results do not give any insight into clay 
dispersion, TEM micrographs of LDPEgMA 
nanocomposites were prepared. Fig. 3 shows the 
micrographs of nanocomposite (MA 10%, MMT 5%), 
where the dark areas represent the clay particles and grey 
areas represent the continuous LDPEgMA matrix. It can 
be seen that the clay particles, ranging from 5 to 20 nm 
thickness, were dispersed uniformly in the LDPE matrix. 
 
Mechanical Properties 
 
   Fig. 4 shows the tensile strength of LDPEgMA-MMT 
nanocomposite after the conventional injection molding 
process. In system A, the MA showed less improvement 
on the tensile strength. For tensile strength, the best 
combination of the MA and MMT ratio is 3:1. However in 
system B, addition of the MMT resulted in higher 
modulus enhancement than in system A. It may be that 
MMT clay acts as a stiffening material, giving greater 
strength than LDPE. MA is a compatibilizer, with a higher 
percentage of maleic anhydride, which has no apparent 
effect on the tensile strength when the clay content was 
fixed at 1 %. Fig. 5 shows the tensile strength of 
LDPEgMA-MMT nanocomposites after the microcellular 
foaming process. The tensile strength is improved in both 
systems. It could be that the MMT clay and MA act as 
nucleating agents that lead to a smaller and more uniform 
cell structure during the foaming procedure. When the 
foam structure is homogeneous and small, the 
nanocomposites have a higher tensile modulus [9]. Figs. 6 
and 7 show the stress-strain curve of the nanocomposites 
molded by conventional injection process and 
microcellular molding process. In Fig. 6, the neat LDPE 
experienced the largest elongation. The addition of MA 
appears to improve the strength but lower the elongation. 
In Fig 7, the specimens were molded by microcellular 
foaming. The addition of MA improved the tensile 
strength and elongation. It may be that MA improved the 
dispersion of the MMT. Clay is a nucleating agent and its 
dispersion may result in a smaller cell size and higher cell 
density structure, inducing a higher tensile modulus. Figs. 
8 and 9 present the impact strength of solid and foamed 
LDPE-clay nanocomposites respectively. It was found 
that the addition of MA and MMT reduces the impact 
strength of the foamed LDPEgMA-clay nanocomposites, 
which is correlated to the tensile strength.  With higher 
clay content, the tensile strength improved but the impact 
strength reduced.  
   
Microcellular Foaming 
 
  Figs. 10 and 11 show typical SEM micrographs of the 
fracture surfaces obtained from the microcellular foaming 
specimens of system A and B. The images show the effect 
of MA and clay concentration on both cell density and 
cell size. The cell size decreased as the cell density 
increased. The dispersion of clay in the polymer clearly 
had an effect on cell nucleation [9]. In LDPEgMA-clay 
nanocomposites with higher clay content, the cell size was 
further reduced and all foams were homogeneous. Higher 
clay content correlates with increased tensile strength, 
decreased cell size, and increased cell density. This results 
in higher tensile strength. These results indicate that the 
clay serves as a heterogeneous nucleating agent during the 
foaming process. In the heterogeneous nucleating system 
containing filler particles, cells nucleate in the boundary 
between the polymer matrix and the dispersed filler 
particles. The remarkable rise in the cell density in the 
nanocomposites should be attributed to a dramatic 
increase in the effectiveness of the nucleating sites.
