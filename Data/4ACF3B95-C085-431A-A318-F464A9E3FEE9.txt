1. 前言 
   
    社群網站的在年輕族群的使用人口逐年上升，因此開始有研究調查社群網站的使用者行為，一個
針對92個大學生的社群網站使用行為調查發現，受調查的大學生每天大約花三十分鐘在使用facebook
的相關服務，他們會以一對多的方式將他們的訊息內容傳播給他們的朋友，同時他們會花比張貼訊息
更多的時間在關察其它人所張貼的內容，社群網站成為維繫既有網絡在離線時的溝通功能，以及成為
新興年輕族群確認身份標誌與意見表達的服務平台(Pempek, et al.)，再者社群網站推出社群遊戲來提
高社群網站使用人口，標榜可以增進網絡互動與擴展人際網絡，然而針對社群遊戲的使用者需求以及
使用行為研究至今仍十分缺乏，因此本研究從既有的網路遊戲使用行為研究作探討，比較網路遊戲使
用行為與社群遊戲使用行為之差異，並近一步整合科技接受模式(Technology acceptance model，TAM)
與服務品質(SQ)的研究觀點來建構研究模式，在1989年所提出的科技接受模式(Technology Acceptance 
Mode，TAM 1)試圖探討外在變數(External Variable)對於「知覺有用性(Perceived Usefulness)」與「知
覺易用性(Perceived Ease of Use)」的影響，並分析「知覺有用性」與「知覺易用性」對於使用態度(Attitude 
Toward Using)、行為意向(Process Behavioral Intention to Use)與使用行為(Actual System Use)的影響，
然而傳統的科技接受模式並沒有明確定義外在變數，因此在2000年所出的理論擴展科技接受模式
(Theoretical extension of the Technology Acceptance Mode，TAM 2)將外在變數分成社會影響程序(Social 
influence processes)與認知建構程序(cognitive instrumental processes)之兩程序，社會影響程序主要包含
主觀規範(Subjective Norm)、自願(voluntariness)與印象(image)之三個外在變項，而認知建構程序則
包含工作相關(job relevance)、輸出品質(output quality)、結果驗證能力(Result demonstrability)與認知
易用性(perceived ease of use)之變項(Venkatesh and Davis, 2000)，然而部分研究則認為像行動網路服務
的採用行為並不像一般爲工作目的的科技採用，因為其科技採用成本是由個人所承擔，故使用者會衡
量淨知覺價值，故應該採用價值基礎的採用模式(Value-based Adoption Model，VAM)與價值最大化觀
點來來解釋行動網路採用行為(Kim, et al., 2007)。由於服務是伴隨交易發生而且無法儲存的，使得服
務品質的衡量充滿挑戰性，早期對於服務品質的研究是多以一般的服務服務事業為主(Parasuraman, et 
al., 1985; 1988)，近年來由於網路服務事業的興起，使得多數研究者開始關注網路服務事業的服務品
質研究(Barrutia, et al., 2009; Bauer, et al., 2006; Fassnacht and Koese, 2006; Heim and Field, 2007; 
Herington and Weaven, 2009; Lages, et al., 2008; Parasuraman, et al., 2005b)。本研究分成兩個部份，首先
探討服務績效與玩家行為之間的關係，分析六個服務績效構面如何影響使用行為與忠誠行為，再者則
是分析社群遊戲的服務競爭力，本研究以模糊認知圖模式(Fuzzy cognitive map, FCM)來建構社群遊戲
的擴展網路關聯圖，透過擴展網路關聯圖，可找出影響玩家使用行為與忠誠行為的支配構面，透過網
路層級分析(ANP)來決定成份之間的權重關係，並利用VIKOR來評估各社群遊戲之服務績效與服務落
差，並透過網路關聯圖技術來找出服務改善策略，並藉此提升廠商服務績效以及改善產業整體服務滿
意度。 
 
本研究選擇栽培農牧類、餐飲經營類與寵物養成等三類行七款的社群遊戲，並藉由遊戲題材選擇
(CS)、服務與系統維護(SM)、遊戲內容規劃(CP)、聲譽與客戶關係(RR)、平台互動機制(AM)與遊戲
造成設計(SD)等六個服務績效指標來進行實證分析，研究發現具有吸引力的遊戲內容規劃(CP)與健全
的服務與系統維護(SM)將是社群遊戲服務商吸引玩家親睞與維繫玩家忠誠度的主要考驗，再者服務
與系統維護(SM)的優劣更直接影響社群網站的平台聲譽與客戶關係(RR)，更是社群遊戲服務商所必
須持續努力的，再者從社群遊戲的服務競爭力分析，在同時追求群體效用最大化和個別遺憾最小化(v

2.2 社群遊戲服務績效評估準則 
    本章主要介紹六大社群遊戲服務績效評估構面，主要包括遊戲題材選擇(TT)、遊戲造型設計
(MD)、遊戲內容規劃(CP)、平台互動機制(IM)、服務與系統維護(SM)與聲譽與客戶關係(RR)之六大

2.2.1 遊戲題材選擇(Selection of theme type, TT) 
    遊戲題材選擇(TT)構面主要在說明好的遊戲題材如何吸引玩家的親睞，有趣的遊戲題材能夠吸引
玩家的興趣，而遊戲流程設計的越容易理解越能引導新手玩家可以更輕易的融入遊戲，再者遊戲任務
指派越有挑戰性時，則更能吸引玩家的好勝心，此外遊戲的種類越多元化越能滿足各類型玩家的需
求，因此本研究透過專家訪談與文獻回顧，將遊戲題材選擇構面分為四個評估準則分別為題材類型
(TT1)、流程安排(TT2)、任務指派(TT3)與遊戲類型(TT4)之四個評估準則 
 
2.2.2 遊戲造型設計(Model design of game, MD) 
    遊戲造型設計(MD)構面主要在說明社群遊戲服務供應商如何透過遊戲造型設計來吸引消費者，
像是可依玩家個人偏好來配置場景的遊戲設計能夠提高玩家的參與感，而生動活潑的音效或配樂也能
吸引玩家的駐足，再者可供玩家選擇的人物性格與外形設計能夠提高遊戲本身的現實感，也讓玩家可
以依造自己的屬性來選擇自己喜歡得角色，此外社群遊戲中可供選擇或交易的物件越多元越能增加遊
戲的豐富性，因此本研究將遊戲造型設計構面(MD)分為場景規劃(MD1)、配樂選擇(MD2)、人物造型
(MD3)與物件總類(MD4)之四個評估準則 
 
2.2.3 遊戲內容規劃(Content planning of game, CP) 
    遊戲內容規劃(CP)構面主要在說明社群遊戲服務供應商如何藉由遊戲內容的安排與設計來提高
玩家的使用意願與維繫玩家的忠誠度，像是栽培農牧類型、餐飲經營類型、餐飲經營類型與寵物養成
類型的社群遊戲中提供適合玩家時間的遊戲時程安排可以增加玩家參與遊戲的彈性，栽培農牧類型社
群遊戲提供生長時間為八至十小時以上的作物，就可以讓一般上班族在下班後可以趕上收成，餐飲經
營類型設群遊戲提供多元烘焙時間的菜單種類可以讓玩家不用時時掛在網路上怕食物壞掉，而寵物養
成類型的社群遊戲提供可以八至十小時餵食週期的寵物或魚種可以讓上班族不至於因為太久沒餵食
而造成寵物餓死；再者提供適合的玩家的升級規劃與足夠的等級設計能夠提高玩家忠誠度，因為遊戲
升級速度太慢可能導致玩家興興致缺缺而不繼續完該款遊戲，然後太快的升級又會造成遊戲太快被完
成可造成玩家流失，因此足夠快的升級與夠多的等級設計有助於吸引玩家使用與維持玩家的高度忠
誠；然後有些玩家為了加快升級速度，會購買社群遊戲廠商所提供的虛擬貨幣來購買肥料，因此肥料
價格的波動往往會導致玩家成本的上升，因此遊戲中的交易條件設計越合理(如:肥料價格)越能維持玩
家忠誠度；最後則是社群遊潟的排名機制設計的越即時，排名範圍越完整，則遊戲的排名與計分系統
就會激起玩家的好勝心，而願意投入更多時間在社群遊戲上，以爭取好成績。 
 
2.2.4 平台互動機制(Interaction mechanism of platform, IM) 
    平台互動機制(IM)構面主要是說明社群遊戲服務供應商如何透過遊戲平台與玩家之間以及玩家
與玩家之間的互動關係來提高玩家的參與意願，像是遊戲網路交易系統越穩定越能提高玩家的信賴
感，玩家不用擔心被重複扣款或者扣了款而拿不到虛擬貨幣(農民幣)，再者增加玩家彼此的互動機會
(如:偷菜、偷寶物)可以增加遊戲的趣味性與互動性，但是過度的玩家互動也會造成的玩家彼此的排
斥、防備與敵意，此外透過玩家之間相互傳遞訊息有助於培養玩家之間的默契並增加社群遊戲互動
性，此外遊戲的導引越清楚而介面設計越人性化越能降低玩家的進入障礙，最後玩家在達成系統公告
的任務指派之後，系統是否能夠如期如質給付報償，也會影響玩家對於社群遊戲平台服務的信賴感，
因此本研究將平台互動機制構面(IM)分為交易系統(IM1)、玩家互惠(IM2)、訊息傳遞(IM3)、遊戲導引
(IM4)與任務信賴(IM5)之五個評估準則。

三、遊戲內容規劃 
1.時程安排 提供適合玩家時間的遊戲時程安排可以增加玩家參與遊戲的彈性 
2.等級設計 提供適合的玩家的升級規劃與足夠的等級設計能夠提高玩家忠誠度  
3.交易條件 遊戲中的交易條件設計越合理(如:肥料價格)越能維持玩家忠誠度 
4.排名機制 遊戲的排名與計分系統越能即時反應越能刺激玩家的好勝心 
四、平台互動機制 
1.交易系統 遊戲貨幣與真實貨幣的交易系統越穩定越能提高玩家的信賴感 
2.玩家互惠 增加玩家彼此的互動機會(如:偷菜)可以增加遊戲的趣味性與互動性 
3.訊息傳遞 玩家之間能夠互相傳遞訊息有助於培養玩家默契並增加遊戲互動性 
4.遊戲導引 遊戲的導引越清楚而介面設計越人性化越能降低玩家的進入障礙 
5.任務信賴 玩家達成系統公告的任務指派時系統是否能夠如期如質給付報償 
五、服務與系統維護 
1.更新頻率 遊戲的版本或者內容適時的更新有助於提高遊戲的新鮮感 
2.系統穩定 遊戲登入或連線服務以及平台的穩定度可以降低玩家的服務抱怨 
3.諮詢服務 良好的服務諮詢機制可提供玩家問題諮詢有助於提高服務滿意度 
4.系統安全 遊戲能夠避免駭客或是其他有心人士的入侵而造成玩家的損害 
六、聲譽與客戶關係 
1.客訴回應 提供給玩家健全的服務抱怨管道與客訴回應系統 
2.服務補救 當遇到玩家抱怨或罷玩情事能夠適時的處理或給予補償 
3.服務推廣 透過網絡邀請方式可藉由玩家的網絡來提高遊戲的擴散效果 
4.服務品質 遊戲平台是否願意維持其服務品質並能獲得玩家信賴

Service Selection for Social Games based on Players’ 
Behavior 
 
Chia-Li Lin  
Department of Resort and Leisure Management, Taiwan Hospitality & Tourism College, 
No. 268, Chung-Hsing St., Feng-Shan Village, Shou-Feng Township,  
Hualien County , 974, Taiwan 
 
Abstract- Due to the enhancement of web service and people’s 
love needs, social networking service is emerging and getting more 
and more popular in recent years. Recently, social games have been 
drawing much attention. Social games contributed more people to 
play online games, and to extend their social networks. The 
popularity of social games also changed the operation model of 
online games. Those high production cost online game have faced 
threatened by low-cost, website-recommended-only social games. 
Thus, it is very important for the on-line game service providers to 
find out the key factors influencing player’s decision. Currently, there 
are various kinds of social games, for instance, harvest and farm 
(Happy Harvest, Country Story, and Farm Vills), Pet breed (Pet 
Society and My Fishbowl), and the category of Restaurant and inn 
management (Restaurant City and Café World). In this study, the 
Fuzzy cognitive map (FCM) is used to construct the network relation 
map (NRM) and the extended NRM , the Principal component 
Analysis (PCA) is applied to categorize criteria based on their 
properties, the Analytic network process (ANP) is used to determine 
the weights of components, and the VIKOR (Vlse kriterijumska 
Optimizacija I Kompromisno Resenje) is adopted to evaluate the 
performance gaps of social games. Service provider of social games 
could clarify the relationship between the service performance and 
the behavior of use, and obtain improvement strategies of service 
performance for social game service. 
Keyword: Social game, Service performance, Behavior of use, NRM 
(Network  relation map), MCDM 
I. INTRODUCTION 
    In the past, people make a trip to visit relatives and friends. 
Nowadays, due to the technology advancement of internet 
service, people could visit others by social networking. Social 
networking service not only provides a new contact channel 
with others, but also a web-based disclosure of private 
information and messages. People could gain each other’ s 
recent messages, and small interested games are offered to 
enhance the interaction with relatives and friends. Therefore, 
the development strategy for social games is different from 
online game. Online game emphasizes the visional perception 
and scenario design, so service providers of online game must 
put in metal investment. In order to impress players on online 
games, some service providers spend huge money and time 
inviting stars or models to promote their online games and 
players would be attracted to play games. However, service 
providers of social games simplify the game and focus on 
funny, simple and well-interacted social content. Busy and 
lonely men and women can gain virtual gifts of game objects 
and accumulate their popularity index via inviting new players 
or present players. For the experienced population of social 
networking services grows continually in young group, some 
researches initiate to discuss the user’ s behavior in social 
networking services. Some researches considered that user 
behavior of mobile internet services differed from applied 
technologies. Users will score the net value received. This 
paper used VAM (Value-based Adoption Model) and 
maximum net value to analyze the consumer preference of 
mobile internet [1]. Because services happened when in 
transaction, the service quality is hardly to be measured. This 
research focus on service quality measurement in the early 
period. This study includes two parts, first the relationship of 
the service performance and user behavior would be discussed, 
and six service performance index influencing the use 
behavior and loyalty would be evaluated based on NRM 
(network relation map). Second, the service competitiveness 
for seven kinds of social game would be analyzed by using 
extension NRM. This study constructed the NRM (network 
relation map) and extension NRM based on the Fuzzy 
cognitive map (FCM). The domination aspects of service 
performance could be determined based on NRM, the 
weighted relationship could be foumd by using ANP (Analytic 
network process), and the performance and the gap would be 
discussed by using VIKOR (Vlse kriterijumska Optimizacija I 
Kompromisno Resenje). The NRM technique is applied to 
propose the improvement strategies to raise the players’ 
satisfaction for the social game service. 
        The remainder of this paper is organized as follows. In 
Section 2, the customer’s evaluation aspects for social game 
service are reviewed and discussed. In Section 3, an 
evaluation model for social game service is built. In Section 4, 
an empirical study of social game service based on novel 
MCDM technique is proposed. Finally, in Section 5, the 
conclusions and remarks are presented.

3.3 Analytic network Procedure (ANP) 
    The AHP (Analytic hierarchy Process) method proposed by 
Saaty in 1980 is an efficient multi-criteria evaluation method. 
AHP organizes strategic problems by the vertical hierarchy 
relation, and evaluates them through quantified process. The 
prerequisite for AHP is the independence between hierarchies. 
However, the problems are actually interrelated, and the 
complexity of interconnection becomes higher with 
involvement of increasing problems and elements. Therefore, 
idealized AHP is not applicable to problems in the real world 
and results in deviation of the evaluation results. Saaty (1996) 
proposed the concepts of ANP in 1996, to solve the issue that 
the AHP method is too ideal to correctly evaluate the 
problems. The ANP method [9-11] can deal with issues of 
inter-dependence and interrelation, and be more applicable to 
the problem resolution in reality. The ANP method is 
expressed by a unidirectional hierarchical relationship among 
decision levels [8, 12, 13]. The top element of the hierarchy is 
the overall goal for the decision model. The hierarchy 
decomposes to a more specific criterion, until a level of 
manageable decision criteria is met [14]. Under each criterion, 
sub-criteria elements relative to the criterion can be 
constructed. The ANP separates complex decision problems 
into elements within a simplified hierarchical system [15-17]. 
The steps of the ANP method are described as follows: (1) 
clarifies the questions and constructs the framework, (2) 
designs the questionnaires and the survey, (3) determines the 
relative importance of factors by pair-wise comparison to 
calculate factor weights by dependence and feedback, and 
checks the consistency of logical judgment, (4) calculates the 
supermatrix, and (5) determines the factor weights[15, 17]. 
    
3.4 Vlse kriterijumska Optimizacija I Kompromisno Resenje 
(VIKOR) 
      The VIKOR method can rank and determine the difference 
of negative and positive ideal solutions between 
services/utilities of the existing service systems of tourist 
package tours. When calculating the distance between the 
ideal solution and the proposed service systems of tourists in 
package tours, the score of every criterion should be 
summarized. The gap between the consumers’ most satisfied 
one and most dissatisfied one is also analyzed regarding 
services/utilities of the existing service systems of tourist 
prckage tours. The VIKOR method starts with the form of the 
PL  – metric , which is used as an aggregating function in a 
compromise alternativeming method and it develops the 
multi-criteria measure for compromise ranking [18, 19]. The 
VIKOR provides a maximum group utility of the “majority” 
and a minimum individual regret of the “opponent”. The 
compromised solutions could be the base for negotiation, 
involving the decision makers’ preferences by criteria weights. 
When both criteria are conflicting, the first criterion can be 
reached for the ideal value, but the second criterion has to be 
neglected for its performance, and vice versa. For 
simultaneously fulfilling these conflicting criteria, 
compromising is necessary.

Abstract—Nowadays, online and television (TV) shopping 
platforms, such as the shopping platform service, are becoming 
extremely popular. Despite these technological advances, there 
are some disputes resulting from the misunderstanding between 
real products and virtual ones. Consequently, many traditional 
physical sellers have been transforming their businesses to sell 
goods on TV. In recent years, the development of internet–
applied shopping, such as MOD (Multimedia on Demand) in 
Taiwan, has brought shopping platform services to another new 
milestone.  Shopping platform services have no limitations in 
relation to showcase space and business closing times. Therefore, 
shopping platform services are becoming more important than 
ever. Understanding customers’ attitudes about shopping 
platforms efficiently helps shopping platform service providers 
to improve their service qualities and increase their market size. 
In this study, a novel MCDM (Multiple Criteria Decision 
Making) approach is used to solve the decision of service quality 
for shopping platform services. By applying this analytical 
process, this study determines the critical factors of shopping 
platform services that customers care about and identifies the 
service aspects/criteria and their corresponding service strategy 
groups.  
 
Keywords— Shopping platform service, Service quality,

I.  INTRODUCTION 
In the past few years, there has been a significant increase 
in the number of products and services that were marketed 
with the shopping platform service (Online shopping or TV 
shopping). Therefore, the shopping platform service market is 
becoming more and more economically important. 
Consequently, many retail/service businesses have begun to 
sell their products and services via online shopping and 
TV/MOD shopping. This increase is found to be taking place 
in Taiwan’s retail market, including Books.com (online 
bookstores), Yahoo! Shopping (online shopping malls), Ez-
Travel (online travel agency) and EHS (Shopping platform 
service Mall). Some retail service operators have also begun 
to integrate their service businesses and provide diverse 
shopping platform services. One example is the Eastern 
Group that provide three kinds of shopping platform services 
for their retail service business: an ET Store (online shopping 
platform), an EHS (Shopping platform service platform) and 
an M-EHS (Mobile shopping platform). Today, more and 
more people purchase products and services on a shopping 
platform service channel or online service platforms, 
illustrating that the trade of shopping platform services and 
online shopping has became a part of life for modern people. 
There are several reasons that explain this change: (1) many 
customers work a long time, therefore, the traditional 
shopping style doesn’t satisfy today’s shoppers’ requirements 
of speed and convenience. (2) some customers prefer privacy 
when they are shopping; they also prefer a free shopping 
environment without any external interference. Since they 
don’t like other people influencing their shopping 
feeling/mood, they prefer a service platform that offers 
enough products and service information, letting them have a 
more enjoyable shopping experience. (3) some customers 
don’t like the trouble of overpopulation, so the shopping 
platforms can provide a comfortable and safe shopping 
environment. In this way, they can purchase a variety of 
products and services without ever leaving their house. (4) 
more and more service platform enterprises are starting to 
work with good quality suppliers and build long corporative 
partnerships.  In the past, many shopping platform services 
gave people a bad impression through their low costs and 
poor quality products. Nowadays, many shopping platform 
services offer high quality, attractive products and services 
with convincing brand names that attract the pickiest 
customers’ attention. (5) detailed product and service 
information was lacking in the traditional shopping 
environment. Some small traditional stores lacked 
salespersons with professional knowledge or detailed product 
information. The comparative information of product price 
didn’t provide enough disclosure for customers. These 
problems generally improved in the environment of the 
service platform, where customers could access the detailed 
product and service information they desired, including 
comparative product and service prices in different shopping 
service platforms. With the development of diversified 
platform services, the shopping experience is more 
professional and personalized, especially with interactive TV 
integrating information and the Internet becoming main 
stream. In this way, customers have better control and enjoy 
the shopping process more.
