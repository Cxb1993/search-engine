一、前言 
中文摘要 
a-Si/c-Si  結構異質接面(Heterojunction with Intrinsic Thin layer; HIT)太陽電池是目前太陽能電池
領域中最熱門的研究之一，主要是因為 HIT 太陽電池具有極高(~23 %)之轉換效率及極低(~200°C)之製
程溫度。本研究計畫主要以電漿化學氣相沉積系統製 p-a-SiC/i-a-Si/n-c-Si/i-a-si/n+-a-SiGe 結構之異質
接面太陽電池，研究內容包括：  
矽基板薄化與 Texturing 製程：矽基板的薄化可以大幅降低製作成本，Texturing 的設計可以增加電池
對光的利用率，提高效率。寬/窄能隙材料 SiC、SiGe 薄膜之製作與特性分析量測：a-SiC 光學能隙最
高可達 3.0 eV，可用於 HIT 元件的光入射層，因為較寬的能隙可以減少材料對光的吸收，進而增加 HIT 
太陽電池的吸光率，獲得較高電流，而使用高能隙的材料亦可獲得較高的開路電壓，SiGe 二元合金具
有高載子遷移率、能隙可調、折射率可變等優點，本計畫目的在於將 HIT 元件能隙匹配做最佳化，提
升元件對各段光波長的利用率以提升轉換效率。本實驗室在 SiC、SiGe 薄膜研究上已有初步的成果。  
a-Si/c-Si 界面分析與改善處理：氫氣鈍化及 a-Si(i)是填補單晶矽基板表面的懸浮鍵缺陷的重要技術。 
HIT 太陽電池經過 a-Si(i)填補過表面的缺陷後，可以減少電流在 a-SiC(p)與 c-Si(n)界面產生複合，可
望大幅提升開路電壓及短路電流。 以本實驗室優良機台製作 15×15 cm2 大面積元件，配合模擬 HIT 太
陽電池結構設計驗證達最佳化。透過材料開發與特性研究、製程技術與模擬最佳化設計整合研究，以
實現高效率 HIT 太陽電池元件。 
 
關鍵字：異質接面太陽電池、非晶矽薄膜、電漿化學沉積系統 
英文摘要 
The a-Si/c-Si (Heterojunction with Intrinsic Thin layer; HIT) is one of the most popular research in the 
area of solar cells recently due to it’s high efficiency (~23%) and low procession temperature (~200 °C). In 
this research plan, we’ll use the plasma-enhanced chemical vapor deposition (PECVD) to make the HIT solar 
cells of p-a-SiC/i-a-Si/n-c-Si/i-a-si/n+-a-SiGe structure .The following content is our research direction:  

    Silicon substrate thinning and texturing procession: thinner substrate could reduce costs and texturing 
could raise light utilization in order to enhance the efficiency. Procession and character analysis of wide band 
gap material SiC thin film: a-SiC optical band gap which is able to achieve up to maximum 3 eV could be 
used in window layers of HIT component.  
Because wider band gap is able to reduce light absorption of materials of HIT solar cells, moreover, cells 
could obtain the higher current density and higher open circuit voltage. Procession and character analysis of 
narrow band gap material SiGe thin film: SiGe alloy has lots of advantages such as high mobility, flexible 
band gap and changeable refractive index. It’s worth researching that we apply SiGe to HIT solar cells. 
Analysis and improvement of a-Si/c-Si interface: hydrogen passivation and a-Si(i) are crucial technologies to 
fill in the dangling bond defects. It could diminish the recombination rate in the interface after filling in the 
defects. Thus the Voc and Isc raise substantially. Our laboratory has great equipments ,and can easily make 
huge area component about 15 x 15 cm2, otherwise , we also use TCAD simulation software to optimize the 
efficiency of HIT solar cells. We are able to achieve high efficiency finally by combining material developing, 
character researching and simulation. 
Keywords: HIT Solar cell、Amorphous silicon thin-film、Plasma-enhanced chemical vapor deposition

圖 1、製造高效率異質接面太陽電池之製程步驟 
 
(1)選擇 n-type 矽晶圓並分析不同 Lifetime 之晶圓對於異質接面太陽能電池製作之影響，並配合 TCAD
模擬進行驗證，確認基板後接著進行 
(2) 異向蝕刻形成倒金字塔結構(Texturing)，一般在(100)矽晶片上進行異向蝕刻，主要原因在於該晶向
面蝕刻速率相對其他晶向面最為緩慢，因此在(100)矽晶片會蝕刻出 45°斜面也就是金字塔結構，當
光進入粗化表面後會產生二次折射吸收效果，提升矽基板對於入射光的捕捉，基板使用前需經過標
準 RC 晶片清洗步驟清潔並浸泡 2%氫氟酸去除氧化層，可減少 a-Si 與 c-Si 之界面載子復合損失，
提高光電流、提高元件效率 
(3)本計畫機台採用 ULVAC（日本真空）PECVD（CC-200）27.1 MHz)進行電漿的缺陷鈍化，改善界面
特性，減少因為界面缺陷而使懸浮鍵捕捉載子於此區域復合而降低光電流。  
(4) H2 電漿處理完接著沉積一層本質非晶矽薄膜(~5 nm)，此層的功用在於優化界面特性，提高開路電
壓。接著繼續沉積矽薄膜因本計畫採用 p 型矽基板沉積 n 型矽膜(PH3)、n 型矽基板沉積 p 型矽膜
(B2H6)，PN 接合產生電子電洞對，厚度控制在 5-20 nm左右，完成後將基板退出 load-lock 後翻面
再送入反應腔體繼續下一製程。此一步驟如步驟(3)及(4)所示，以 H2 電漿(對基板背面鈍化處理後，
接著分別沉積本質非晶矽薄膜，p型矽基板沉積 p+-a-Si 薄膜、n型矽基板沉積 n+-a-Si 薄膜，此層重
摻雜有助於提升內建電場，就能夠得到較高的開路電壓，並與背電極形成歐姆接觸，減少串聯電阻，
提升填充因子(FF)。 
(5)在基板正面利用本實驗室機台In-Line Sputtering製作透明導電膜選用靶材為ITO濺鍍厚度約100 nm。  
(6)接著電極製作，電極最佳化設計可減少光遮損失，提高光電流利用本實驗室太陽光模擬器(100 
mW/cm2，AM1.5)條件下測量電壓-電流特性。並利用TCAD模擬軟體驗證。利用此結構製作出VOC ≧ 
0.68 V 、 JSC ≧ 35 mA/cm2 、FF≧0.7 、η ≧ 18 % 大面積(15 × 15 cm2)高效率異質接面太陽能電池。

六、結論與建議 
目前最佳化HIT太陽電池之開路電壓VOC為0.57 V 、短路電流JSC為36.6 mA/cm2、填充因子FF
為0.7、轉換效率為14.7 %。研究中發現，在非晶矽薄膜內摻入碳原子形成碳化矽薄膜，雖然可以提
高材料能隙，卻嚴重影響薄膜電性及品質，製程上不易控制，未來考慮加入微晶製程，朝奈米晶矽
薄膜發展，因為奈米晶矽薄膜擁有高能隙，做為HIT太陽電池窗層，增加光吸收率，提高元件開路
電壓，且製程奈米晶矽薄膜需通入大量氫氣，有更高的機率填補矽晶圓表面缺陷，取代HIT太陽電
池
 i-layer，奈米晶矽薄膜與非晶矽薄膜相較之下，有更高的鍵結穩定性，缺陷也較非晶矽薄膜低，
為了有效提升電池元件的Voc 與 JSC，將依照模擬條件取得不同Lifetime (150 µs以上)或FZ等級
Wafer、製備高品質SiC(2.1 ev)材料以及同步進行具量子侷限效應之奈米晶矽薄膜開發，增加光利用
率，熱燈絲輔助化學氣象沉積HWCVD取代目前之27.1 MHz PECVD能避免電漿轟擊所帶來的介面損
失。

Fabrication and simulation of a-SiC:H, a-Si:H and a-SiGe:H thin film 
as graded absorber layer for Si-based thin film solar cells 
 
Chao-Chun Wang1, Shui-Yang Lien2*, Sheng-Jin Wang2, Chueh-Yang Liu2, Chia-Fu Chen2, 
Dong-Sing Wuu1 
 
1 Department of Materials Science and Engineering, National Chung Hsing University, Taichung 
402, Taiwan, Republic of China 
2 Department of Materials Science and Engineering, MingDao University, ChungHua 52345, 
Taiwan, Republic of China 
 
  The use of thin film technology for the fabrication of solar cells has gradually been increasing for 
lower production costs and acceptable efficiencies compared to other kinds of solar cells. The 
energy conversion efficiency of a solar cell can be significantly increased with the improvement of 
material properties and the designs or structures of the solar cells. In this paper, we combined 
experimental characterization with computer simulations software TCAD (Technology 
Computer-Aided Design) in order to gain a better insight of the transport mechanisms for the solar 
cell performance of single-junction pin structures where the a-SiC:H/a-Si:H/a-SiGe:H as graded 
absorber layer has been deposited by high-frequency (27.13 MHz) plasma enhanced chemical vapor 
deposition (HF-PECVD). The a-SiC:H, a-Si:H and a-SiGe:H thin film characteristics were analyzed. 
The result showed optical band gap of a-SiC:H films was varied from 1.85 to 2.1 eV by varying the 
percentage of methane in the silane gas mixture from 30 to 80%. On the other hands, the band gap 
of a-SiGe:H films was from 1.6 to 1.3 eV by varying the percentage of germanium in the silane gas 
mixture from 20 to 42%. Form these results, a new approaching for characterization of p-a-SiC:H 
(2.1 eV) /i-a-SiC (2.1 to 1.85 eV) /i-a-Si (1.7eV) /i-SiGe:H (1.6 to 1.3 eV) /n-a-SiGe:H (1.3 eV)
