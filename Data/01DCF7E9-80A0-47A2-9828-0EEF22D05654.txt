二、英文摘要 
Although Fitts’ law was developed to 
describe one-dimensional target aiming tasks, 
many studies have modified it as new models 
to predict the speed-accuracy tradeoff 
relationship while performing two- and 
three-dimensional aiming tasks.  However, 
these Fitts-type models have three limitations, 
including (1) lack of theoretical supports, (2) 
they cannot predict human performance in 
advance, and (3) they cannot provide 
separated performance evaluation of speed 
and accuracy while evaluating input devices.  
To provide an alternative methodology for 
evaluating individual differences and 
human-computer interaction designs while 
performing three-dimensional (3D) 
movements, this study tested the application 
of ballistic movement models [1].  In a 
darkened room, six participants conducted 3D 
ballistic movements of specific amplitudes 
using a personal computer, a self-developed 
laser emitter, and an electromagnetic tracking 
system.  The measured data of movement 
time and movement end-point variability were 
utilized to test the ballistic movement time 
model and the ballistic movement variability 
model.  The results showed that both the 
ballistic movement time model and variability 
model described well the measured data.  
This study showed the application potentials 
of ballistic movement models for evaluating 
3D movements.

4. Discussion 
With a self-developed 3D ballistic movement 
execution device and a 3D Guidance 
trakSTAR, this study successfully measured 
ballistic movement time and ballistic 
movement end-point variability when 
participant performed ballistic movement in a 
true three-dimensional environment.  As 
found in Lin and Drury [1], the ballistic 
movement time ranged widely (150 – 500 
milliseconds) than that reported by Gan and 
Hoffmann [8] because of the wide range of 
 values used as well as the experimental 
setup.  We were surprised that the main 
effect of hand had no significant effect on 
movement time.  Intuitively, our dominate 
hands should move faster than non-dominate 
hands.  Since our participants were all 
right-handed, we expected the movements 
performed by right hands were faster (i.e., 
shorter movement times).  However, as 
shown in  
Figure 2, only Participant 6 performed 
movements faster with his right hand.  We 
speculated that the reason might be due to the 
training effect.  Although every participant 
had the same practices for both hands before 
formal measurements, all the participants 
performed formal measurements with right 
hands first.  The formal measurements with 
right hands might also improve the 
performance of left hand.  However, the 
result was found different in movement 
end-point variability.  As shown in Figure 5, 
it was clear that movements were performed 
better using right hands no matter which axis 
of variable error was considered.  
Furthermore, as shown in Figure 6, we found 
that the amplitude of X-variable error was 
much larger than those of Y-variable error 
and Z-variable error.  The difference 
between the longitudinal error (i.e., 
X-variable error) and the lateral errors (i.e., 
Y-variable error and Z-variable error) 
increased rapidly as the movement distance 
increased.  This result was in line with 
Grossman and Balakrishnan [11] who 
reported that target size dimension along the 
primary axis of movement had a greater 
impact on performance than the other two 
dimensions.  While comparing the other two 
lateral errors that both measured 
perpendicularly to the movement direction, 
the Z-variable error was about 1.8 times 
larger than as the Y-variable error.

在過去 50 年中，Fitts’ Law 被人因工程與人機互動的研究學者及電子產品開發設計人員視
為非常重要且有效的評估量測方法。若在 Google Scholar 中以 Fitts’ Law 作為關鍵字，我們可
以輕易找到超過 17,000 篇相關的學術論文。這些論文主要是以 Fitts’ Law 量測人在使用不同
工具(徒手、筆、滑鼠、搖桿等)進行移動控制的能力比較。雖然 Fitts’ Law 的影響力重要到被
規範在 ISO 9241-9 中，但是 Fitts’ Law 所量測到的是進行移動控制時「速度」與「準確度」
的整合性結果。 
藉著國科會計畫這三年來的補助支持，本人積極提出以及證明一個比 Fitts’ Law 更有效的
方法 – 彈道式移動方法。本研究以人徒手執行三維空間中的瞄準移動為範例，證實以兩個彈
道式移動模型作為評估方法，能將原本 Fitts’ law 得到的整合性時間表現拆解為「速度」與三
個軸向的「準確度」進行獨立的評估與比較。 
現階段研究成果除了能作國際期刊發表，所提出的新評估量測方法將可繼續於未來研究
中，測試過去以 Fitts’ Law 作評估量測方法的相關研究，所得到的「速度」與「準確度」獨立
比較結果，將有助研究學者對於「人」的瞭解，並且協助電子產品開發設計人員發展出更好
操作的輸入設備。

工程所著重的「人」的角色，早已從勞力密集工業下的「作業人員」轉至站在消費角度的「使用者」；
而使用者所「使用」的也不再局限於有形的產品或器具，如今更含蓋了無形的「服務」。而不同於過
去的消費者，現在的消費者不再順從地去學習不考慮使用者需求下所設計、生產出來的工具或產品；
取而代之的是，消費者願意花較高的成本選擇容易學習(甚至是不用學習)及使用上不容易發生錯誤的產
品。也因為無慮於物質需求上的供給，消費者也進而期待服務上的創新來滿足精神上的享受。

An Application of Ballistic Movement Models for Comparing Ageing Differences while 
Interacting with a Touchscreen 
Ray F. Lin, Shin-Wen Shih, & Bernard C. Jiang 
Department of Industrial Engineering & Management, 
Yuan Ze University, Chung-Li, Taiwan 320 
Abstract. To make sure innovative touchscreen techniques can benefit well the elderly population, this study utilized ballistic 
movement models to evaluate the differences of movement speed and accuracy between elder and young adults while interacting 
with a touchscreen.  Six elder and six young participants conducted ballistic movements on a touchscreen monitor.  The 
measured data of movement time and endpoint error were utilized to test the ballistic movement models.  Our results showed that 
ballistic movement models fitted well young participants’ data.  However, elder participants performed movements with a 
conservative manner; their movement time and variable errors did not increase with increased movement distance as much as that 
of young participants’.  Future research will focus on enhancing experimental designs to obtain solid conclusions so that the 
results could help developing touchscreen design guidelines. 
Keywords: ageing, ballistic movements, ballistic movement models, aiming movement, touchscreens, interactive displays

在過去 50年中，Fitts＇ Law 被人因工程與人機互動的研究學者及電子產品開發設計人員
視為非常重要且有效的評估量測方法。若在 Google Scholar 中以 Fitts＇ Law 作為關鍵
字，我們可以輕易找到超過 17,000 篇相關的學術論文。這些論文主要是以 Fitts＇ Law
量測人在使用不同工具(徒手、筆、滑鼠、搖桿等)進行移動控制的能力比較。雖然 Fitts＇
Law 的影響力重要到被規範在 ISO 9241-9 中，但是 Fitts＇ Law 所量測到的是進行移動控
制時「速度」與「準確度」的整合性結果。 
 
  藉著國科會計畫這三年來的補助支持，本人積極提出以及證明一個比 Fitts＇ Law更有
效的方法 – 彈道式移動方法。本研究以人徒手執行三維空間中的瞄準移動為範例，證實
以兩個彈道式移動模型作為評估方法，能將原本 Fitts＇ law 得到的整合性時間表現拆解
為「速度」與三個軸向的「準確度」進行獨立的評估與比較。
