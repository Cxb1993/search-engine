2. 加工原理與磁力分佈 
2.1 加工原理 
平面磁力研磨的加工方式，是利用端面為平面
型之磁極與工件表面保持適當的加工間隙 1~10mm，
磁極與工件之間，充填具有磁化性質的鐵粉與研磨能
力的 Al2O3所結合而成的磁性磨粒，磁極之端面吸附
著磁性磨粒並與工件之夾具構成一個封閉的磁場迴
路。因此磁性磨粒在不均勻磁場的作用下，會沿著磁
力線方向產生相互的連結，形成具有撓性（Flexible）
可自由分離與連結的磁刷（Magnetic Brush）如圖 4
所示。磁力線所產生的力量使磁性磨料加壓在加工物
體上，利用磁刷作為研磨的工具。當磁極高速迴轉同
時與工作物相對運動時，磁刷尖端的磁性磨粒與工件
表面相互摩擦，此摩擦力會讓磨粒將工件表面凸出不
平的地方微量的磨除，配合工件（或磁極）在Ｘ-Y
平面上的移動，可達到平面研磨拋光的目的。平面磁
力研磨拋光加工示意圖如圖 5 所示。利用此一研磨工
具可用來加工平面、曲面、複雜機件的表面、工件毛
邊的去除等，均可達到相當良好的研磨效果。 
力 Fm會使得磁性磨粒飛離加工區域，但在高強度磁
場作用下，磁力 F 可防止此一現象產生，以確保磁力
拋光加工之正常進行。

4. 實驗結果與討論 
4.1 彈性磁性磨料製作 
磁性研磨材料必須具備有被磁化性質及有研磨
能力的複合磨料，磁化性良好的物質有鐵、鈷、鎳等。
研磨材的種類為氧化鋁、碳化矽、氮化硼、鑽石粉末…
等。 
本研究採用熱可塑性彈性樹脂結合法，依經驗值
選用適當粒度的鐵粉及碳化矽粉，及適當的重量比與
熱可塑性彈性體(TPE)顆粒均勻混合，加熱至 180℃
使 TPE熔化並充份攪拌，加壓通過篩網噴射至冷卻室
冷凝成顆粒狀，最後經由粉碎機製成實驗用磨料。以
SEM 觀察，在鐵粉表面均勻分佈著碳化矽粉，如圖
10 所示。 
磨料製程参數設定如下： 
1.鐵粉粒度 
    粒度較大的鐵粉與碳化矽粉結合後能夠得到較
強的磁性，單顆鐵粉所黏附的碳化矽顆粒也較多，但
可能會造成磨料難以粉碎至適當的粒度大小。本實驗
使用的鐵粉粒度值為 115m(125mesh) 
2.碳化矽粉粒度 
    碳化矽粉決定了切削作用的成形與否，其粒度大
小對於工件去除率與表面粗糙度有絕對的影響。粒度
較大的碳化矽粉擁有較佳的工件去除率，但表面粗糙
度則會變差。本實驗使用的碳化矽粉粒度值為
5m(3000mesh)。 
3.成分比例 
在磁性研磨拋光中，鐵粉的功用為吸附於磁極以
及透過磁場分佈對工件產生加工壓力，而碳化矽粉則
提供切削作用，較多的鐵粉雖然能夠提供較大的加工
壓力，但卻會使切削力量變小。本實驗使用的鐵粉對
碳化矽粉的重量比(Fe：SiC wt％)為 4：1。

Abstract 
Some of the stainless steel tubes such as bending 
tube, manifold and steel bottle for medical care and food 
usages have various narrow holes which are so slender 
that a conventional grinding tool is too big to insert into 
the tube to polish inner surface of the tubes. With the 
use of Magnetic Abrasive Finishing (MAF) to polish 
surfaces of the parts, roughness on the surfaces can 
reach a level like a mirror. 
We have devoted to the study of MAF for many 
years; the magnetic abrasive particle we have developed 
can yield a value of Ra 0.008µm, which is similar to the 
surface of a mirror. However, there is no way to absorb 
charging of abrasive particle during polishing for 
conventional stiff abrasive materials. Therefore, it is 
difficult to obtain the nano-level mirror surface. 
Such as the proportion between high polymer 
composite particle such as SiC and the magnetic metal 
powder, work piece rotational speed, work piece type, 
process time, particle size, etc. For the second year, we 
will use the high polymer composite elastic magnetic 
abrasive particle to carry on the magnetic polishing 
experiment using Taguchi method to obtain the optimal 
machining parameters and to polish surface of stainless 
steel. The success of the magnetic abrasive particle 
experiments will bring magnetic abrasive finishing 
technique to the new state of finishing technology.

Experimental Method  
Friction Drilling  
This study uses carbide as drilling tool material，carbide is a thermostable material and could 
be used in the thermal friction drilling。During the friction drilling，the drill encounter a high 
pressure from the tool running through the metal plate and created a temperature between 
650-750°C，simultaneously the temperature around the metal plate is going up instantly and 
therefore make the plate softened and then the friction drill will be easy to go through out the metal 
plate and to finish the drilling operations[1-3]。Figure 1 shows the friction drilling procedures，Step 
1: the drill just touch the part’s surface，Step 2: the friction drill enter into part under the high 
spindle force and speed，Step 3: the part melted and squeezed upward gradually under the high 
spindle force and speed， Step 4: the friction drill continues to enter into the part and bush starts to 
be formed，squeezed downward and planed by the up edge of the drill，Step 5: friction drilling 
finished and the bush length is about 3 times thicker than the plate size。The mechanism setup was 
showed in Figure 2。

Experimental data shows that Haynes-230 obtains better surface roughness than Mar-M247 and 
Inconel-718，since Haynes-230 has a better ductility which will make it easily to be processed 
during friction drilling and obtains a lower surface roughness；however，Ni-based surperalloys 
Inconel-718 has the highest destructive and yield strength among  three materials，therefore bush 
formation during friction drilling could abrade hole surface and result in a bad surface roughness。
