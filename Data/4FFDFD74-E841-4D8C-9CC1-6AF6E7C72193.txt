行政院國家科學委員會專題研究計畫成果報告 
 
低溫生長結晶性二氧化鈦奈米管陣列應用於染料敏化太陽能電池之研究 
Study on the Low-temperature Growth of Crystalline TiO2 Nanotube Array for 
Dye-sensitized Solar Cell Application 
摘要 
在目前太陽電池的領域中，一新興元件-染料敏化太陽能電池被發展出來，其中的核心
材料又以二氧化鈦最具代表性，由於二氧化鈦具有寬能隙，結合適當的染料後，成為染料
敏化太陽電池異質接面重要核心。目前諸多二氧化鈦層製作方法中，利用陽極處理法可製
備出排列整齊的二氧化鈦奈米管陣列，其將可有效增加吸附染料的比表面積，進而大幅提
昇光電轉換效率，但唯仍脫離不了鈦金屬基板的使用。因此，本計畫率先以成本低廉、高
附著性且高沉積速率的電弧離子鍍在透明導電玻璃預鍍鈦金屬層作為前驅層，再利用陽極
處理法將鈦金屬層置換成透明的二氧化鈦奈米管陣列電極，探討二氧化鈦奈米管陣列之微
觀結構，且經熱處理後並加以封裝成 ITO glass/[TiO2-nanotube (N3 dye)]/I2+LiI 
electrolyte/Pt/ITO glass 染料敏化太陽能電池組件，以了解其對光電轉換效率的影響。 
研究結果顯示；以電弧離子鍍製備鈦金屬模板時，施鍍時間為 45 min，可得厚度 5 μm
的鈦金屬層。在添加 3 g 氟化銨與 20 g/l 水之電解液中進行 2 h 陽極處理，可使鈦金屬層完
全置換成管長達 10.5 μm 的非晶二氧化鈦奈米管陣列，內、管徑分別為 92 nm 及 124 nm。
經 350 oC 退火熱處理可得銳鈦礦相的二氧化鈦奈米管，光電轉換效率明顯提昇。在大尺寸
測試面積(8 cm2)下進行測試，最高可達到 1.88%的光電轉換效率。本計畫將發展完成「低
溫生長結晶性二氧化鈦奈米管陣列於玻璃基板」及「正向式奈米管陣列之染料敏化電池」。 
 
關鍵字：鈦金屬、二氧化鈦、陽極處理、染料敏化太陽能電池 
Abstract 
In the field of solar cell, the most common DSSC under development is the titanium dioxide 
based type due to its wide energy band gap. It is well known that microstructure of titania 
electrode is essential to photovoltaic efficiency of the solar cells. With suitable dye 
absorption.Many techniques have been investigated in the preparation of efficient TiO2 electrode. 
Anodic oxidation is one of the methods to prepare highly ordered TiO2 nanotube array with larger 
specific surface area capable of dye absorbing efficiently and eventually photovoltaic efficiency. 
However, current anodizing precesses rely on the usage of metal titanium substrate to grow oxide 
layer. In this project, an arc ion plating (AIP) deposition system was employed to produce metal 
titanium pre-layer on transparent conducting oxide glass, followed by anodically oxidizing into 
TiO2-nanotube array electrode. Microstructure was examined. After thermal annealing, a 
dye-sensitized solar cell (DSSC) was then assembled as ITO glass/[TiO2-nanotube (N3 
dye)]/I2+LiI electrolyte/Pt/ITO glass. Photovoltaic performance was evaluated. 
Experimental results show that a 5μm thick titanium pre-layer can be obtained for 45 min 
AIP deposition. After anodic oxidation grown in the electrolyte for 2 h, metal titanium pre-layer 
can be completely convert into amorphous TiO2-nanotube array, which the tube length reaches 
10.5 μm with the tube inner and outer diameter 92 nm and 124 nm, respectively. Thermal 
annealing at 350oC enables the formation of anatase phase TiO2-nanotube array, which greatly 
increases the photo-conversion efficiency. Under the effective test area of 8 cm2, the device 
produced an ultimate photovoltaic efficiency up to 1.88%. However, low-temperature growth of 
crystalline TiO2 nanotube array on glass Substrate and Forward of nanotube array for 
dye-sensitized solar cell has been demonstrated in this project. 
 
Keywords: titanium, titanium dioxide, anodic oxidation, dye-sensitized solar cell

架構，即先使用電弧離子鍍沉積鈦金屬層於ITO玻璃基材上，接著進行陽極處理法製備出二

glass/[TiO2-nanotube (N3 dye)]/I2+LiI electrolyte/Pt/ITO glass，以探討其光電轉換效率。

六、結論 
本研究成功以電弧離子鍍預鍍鈦金屬層於 ITO 玻璃上，並經陽極處理生長二氧化鈦奈
米管陣列，經退火熱處理得到銳鈦礦相的二氧化鈦奈米管，並加以封裝成 ITO 
glass/[TiO2-nanotube (N3 dye)]/I2+LiI electrolyte/Pt/ITO glass 正向式結構染料敏化太陽能電
池元件，得出以下結論： 
(1) 電解液的氟化銨含量會影響二氧化鈦奈米管的生長。電解液的氟化銨含量越高其生長
速度越快，係因氟化銨含量影響溶蝕鈦金屬速度。 
(2) 光電轉換效率受奈米管徑和管長影響，管徑較小的奈米管其表面所產生的表面張力使
染料未完全吸附在二氧化鈦奈米管表面，而較長的管長降低試片的穿透度，故在含20 g
水和3 g氟化銨的電解液生長之二氧化鈦奈米管，可得最佳光電轉換效率。 
(3) 陽極處理生長出非晶二氧化鈦奈米管，經250oC後熱處理可得富含銳鈦礦相的二氧化鈦
奈米管，隨著熱處理溫度之增加，光電轉換效率亦隨之提昇。但當溫度達450 oC時，由
於ITO電阻亦隨處理溫度增加而增加，使光電轉換效率下降，故在350 oC可得最佳光電
轉換效率1.88%。 
(4) 本研究發展之低溫生長結晶性二氧化鈦奈米管陣列，將可作為正向式染料敏太陽電池
的正極，以有效提昇光電轉換效率。

Abstract 
 
The β-titanium alloys have been aware of their importance for applications on bone implant 
recently. In this study, micro-arc oxidation (MAO) was used to modify the surface of β-titanium 
alloy (Ti-13Cr-3Al-1Fe) using NaH2PO4 solution as electrolyte. The relationship of applied voltage 
and oxidizing time with microstructure of the oxidized layer were investigated. The cellular adhesion, 
proliferation and alkaline phosphatase (ALP) activity of MC3T3-E1 pre-osteoblast were measured to 
reveal cell compatibility of the treated specimens. Experimental results show that under an applied 
voltage of 350 V, the oxidized layer can be found to gradually increase layer thickness, pores size 
and surface roughness but decrease in pore number that exist in the oxidized layer as a function of 
treatment time. The anatase TiO2 presents in the oxidized layer but eventually replaced by the rutile 
phase by increasing oxidizing time above 30 min. Under an oxidizing time for 20 min, layer 
thickness, pore size and surface roughness of the oxidized layer increases and the anatase TiO2 exists 
but again rutile phase appears with increasing applied voltage up to 400 V. The MC3T3-E1 cell 
compatibility test shows that bare β-titanium alloy is superior to bare α-titanium (CP-Ti, Grade II). 
The MAO treated β-titanium specimen presents significantly better cell compatibility than the bare 
β-titanium alloy. Ultimate cell compatibility can be achieved by a MAO treatment using an applied 
voltage 350 V for 40 min. This study may provide an alternative for osteoblast cell compatible 
surface modification for β-titanium bone implant. The cell viability on the MAO treated β-titanium 
increases with increasing oxidizing time and applied voltage may be due either to the increased layer 
thickness, pore size, surface roughness or the controllable anatase-rutile ratio of oxidation layer. 
What is the most critical point in TiO2 for cell viability remains a mystery? Further studies to open 
the mystery involving  detail microstructure characterization, in vitro cellular calcification and 
mineralization analysis with in vivo histological observations, are currently in progress.

firm structure, during both the initial and subsequent growth of bone cells, are important endeavors.  
The surface treatment for titanium metal includes a preliminary thermal spray of hydroxyapatite 
[10,11], anodizing the titanium surface to grow an oxidation layer [12,13] and then employing an 
alkali treatment to generate a coating of calcium phosphate [14]. Among many surface treatment 
technologies micro-arc oxidation (MAO), also known as plasma electrolytic oxidation, receives the 
most attention. It uses an electrochemical technique to cover the anode surface with an oxidation 
layer that is crystalline, roughness [15], porous and has excellent adhesion to the substrate [16]. 
Some researches indicate that MAO treatment can form biocompatible thin film TiO2 structures 
on titanium metal surface [15]. Many β-titanium alloys have excellent biomechanical properties and 
corrosion resistance, yet progress in micro-arc oxidizing these materials is not reported in the 
literature; here a new β-titanium alloy CA-Ti (E= 85-90 GPa) will be subjected to micro-arc 
oxidation expected to produce thin film TiO2 that has compatibility with osteoblast cells. The 
microstructure of TiO2 and its morphological effect on biocompatibility will also be investigated. 
Opportunities for appling micro-arc oxidizing technology to β-titanium alloys in orthopedic implants 
are also sought.

This research employs the new β-titanium alloy CA-Ti from Japan Daido Steel as a substrate, 
the chemical composition of which is shown in Table I. The specimen dimensions are 20.0 × 20.0 ×

2.2. X-ray diffraction 
 
The prepared specimen is subject to grazing incidence X-ray diffraction (XRD) with incident 
angle fixed at 2o for analysis of crystal structure in oxidation layer by Bruker-D8 X, which uses Cu 
Kα (λ=1.5405 nm) as a light source and a scanning angle (2θ) ranging through 20~80° at a speed of 
3°/min. For the β-titanium substrate,  X-ray diffraction is performed.  
 
2.3. Scanning electron microscopy 
 
A Hitachi S-4800 cold field emission scanning electron microscope (FESEM) at an accelerating 
voltage of 15 kV is used to observe the surface and cross section morphology of TiO2 thin film on β- 
titanium specimens. 
 
2.4. Surface roughness 
 
The surface roughness is measured by means of surface roughness tester (Hommel werke Tester 
T1000). The average roughness (Ra) and ten points average roughness (Rz) are used to characterize 
the surface roughness of the specimens.  
 
2.5. Cell culture 
 
A murine pre-osteoblastic cell line MC3T3-E1 was obtained from Riken Cell Bank (Tsukuba, 
Japan). Cells were grown on the plastic cell culture dishes in 95% air 5% CO2 with 　-MEM 
(Invitrogen, Carlsbad, CA), which was supplemented with 20 mM HEPES and 10% heat-inactivated 
fetal bovine serum, 2 mM glutamine, penicillin (100 U/ml), and streptomycin (100 µg/ml) (pH 
test specimens (10,000 cells/cm2). After 2 days cells were washed twice with PBS and scraped in a 
0.2% aqueous solution of Nonidet P-40. Cells suspension was sonicated on ice for 30 s and 
centrifuged for 5 min at 4oC. Aliquots of supernatants were subjected to protein assay with the Pierce 
Coomassie Plus assay reagent (Pierce, Rockford, USA), and ALP activity in supernatant by ALP 
assay mixtures containing 0.1M 2-amino-2-methyl-1-propanol, 1 mM MgCl2, and 8 mM 
p-nitrophenyl phosphate disodium. After incubation at 37oC for 30 min, the reaction was stopped 
with 0.1 N NaOH and the absorbance was read at 405 nm (Bio-Tek, Winooski, VT).  
 
2.9. Cell morphology 
 
Cells on test specimens were washed twice in PBS and fixed with 3% glutaraldehyde in 0.1M 
sodium cacodylate buffer, dehydrated in serial ethanol and amyl acetate baths, and critical point 
dried. Specimens were sputter-coated with Au and imaged with a Hitachi S-3000N scanning electron 
microscope which operated with a secondary electrons (SE) mode to examine cell morphology under 
a 15 kV voltage [24]. 
 
2.10. Statistical analysis 
 
Results are expressed as mean ± standard deviation with n=5. Error bars represent standard 
deviations. Differences between the experimental groups were analyzed according to t-test, with 
P<0.05 considered statistically significant.
