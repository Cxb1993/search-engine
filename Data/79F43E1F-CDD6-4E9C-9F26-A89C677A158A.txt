一、 中文摘要
網路頻寬服務商品之特性包括: (1) 頻寬容量有
限, (2) 網路頻寬商品之需求高, (3) 網路頻寬設
施昂貴, (4) 網路頻寬商品之經濟價值高, 及 (5)
因財務及技術之限制, 網路頻寬設施之擴充有限。
由於這些現象,網路供應商所面臨之挑戰不僅包括
提升服務品質之問題,亦包括管控需求與供給之問
題。 許多學者已提出不同的呼叫允諾控制 (call ad-
mission control)的方法以增進服務品質,然而對於
控管網路資源的需求與供給問題方面之研究則稀
少許多。 為能刺激不同層面之需求以提升收益, 許
多網路服務供應商提供差異化的網路服務商品給
顧客。 然而, 採取差異化服務策略之網路供應商可
能面臨 應銷售何種等級之頻寬商品、 應分配多少
頻寬給各等級之頻寬商品、 各等級之頻寬商品之
銷售上限應如何訂定以確保服務品質等問題、 如
何快速的擬定決策以達決策制定之效率性。 本計
劃分三年執行以下三個議題: 第一年的子計畫中,
本計劃探討網路商品的選擇與頻寬分配問題。 第
二年的子計畫中,此計劃探討網路商品的超額用戶
訂購上限問題。 第三年的子計畫中, 此計劃發展效
率的演算法,於確保網路服務品質的情況下擬定頻
寬商品的頻寬分配與定價策略。 針對第一年之子
計畫,此研究發展以粒子群演算法為基礎的演算法
求解。 數值例結果顯示, 本研究所發展之演算法於
求解品質及時間方面皆優於著名的商用套裝軟體
CPLEX.針對第二年之子計畫,本計劃建立一個限
制條件的非線性規劃模型處理一個雙網路供應商
競爭條件下的超額銷售問題。 本研究針對本問題
之市場均衡價格及頻寬分配, 推導出封閉解。 針對
第三年之子計畫,本計劃建立一個數學規劃為基礎
的演算法求解一個服務品質條件下的頻寬商品的
定價與頻寬分配問題。 數值例結果顯示, 本研究所
發展之演算法之可求得近似最佳解。

供應商將希望知道如何定價以減少此差異進而增
加收益。 於第三年的子計畫中, 此計劃發展效率的
演算法,於確保網路服務品質的情況下擬定頻寬商
品的頻寬分配與定價策略。

Abstract 
 
Topics on reverse logistics have been discussed 
over the years.  Most of them are dealt with by 
assuming that facilities are kept open once they are 
established, and no inventory is stocked at 
intermediate recycling stations. By simultaneously 
relaxing these two assumptions, this paper studies a 
reverse logistics system with multiple items of which 
each consists of some components among a variety of 
spare parts. The purpose is to maximize the total profit 
by establishing a production schedule and reverse 
logistics framework over a finite time periods for a 
logistics system. The mathematic model established in 
this research is a constrained non-linear integer 
programming problem. A genetic based algorithm with 
the help of linear programming is developed to find 
solutions to this problem. Computational experiments 
show that the proposed approach is superior to the 
well-known CPLEX software in terms of both solution 
quality and computational effectiveness.

1. Introduction 
 
With the progress of science and technology, many 
new products are being developed and become our 
cherished possessions. These products play important 
roles for people since they make people's lives more 
convenient. For all their importance, some of these 
products are quickly discarded when the latest models 
become available. For example, fashion devices such 
as cell phones, personal digital assistants (PDAs) and

negative impact of the waste, the concept of reuse has 
become a trend today.  
Putting the concept of reuse into practice, many 
firms have incorporated the reverse logistics system 
into their manufacturer systems. This paper deals with 
a multi-commodity inventory problem in which a firm 
can obtain its materials/components from outsourcing 
or its reverse system. Usually, a reverse logistics 
system may involve the manufacturer, distribution 
center, customers, collector, asset recovery and 
recycler. In this paper, the reverse logistics system 
focuses on scenario of product return, asset recovery, 
distributions, inventory and use. The product return 
involves the collection, product mixing and transfer of 
returned products at collection points, the asset 
recovery of returned products through disassembly, 
repairs and refurbishing at disassembly centers, and 
remanufacturing at manufacturer. Holding products or 
materials incurs inventory holding costs while 
handling returned product incur not only inventory 
holding cost but also processing cost such as 
collections, refurbishments and repairs, transportation 
costs of returned products from collection centers to 
disassembly centers, as well as the transportation cost 
of refurbished parts from disassembly centers to 
manufacturer.

3.3. Decoding 
 
The heuristic initializes a population with 
popNum chromosomes. Each chromosome has CT 
genes and each gene is randomly generated by an 
integer number d within the range of [0,D]. The 
integer number d maybe a zero or a positive number. 
Integer number d =0 means that no collection-center 
is located at candidate site c. That is, ctx  =0. Integer 
number d >0 means that a collection-center is located 
at candidate site c , a treatment-center is located at 
candidate treatment site d and collection-center c is 
assigned to treatment-center d .

5. Conclusion 
 
The purpose of this work is to develop an 
optimization model and associated algorithm to 
design an integrated logistics network. The 
formulation to this problem was developed as a 
mixed integer nonlinear programming model with 
multi-periods, dynamic reverse logistics system, 
multi-commodity, capacitated facilities. Since such a 
problem belongs to a class of NP hard problems, a 
hybrid genetic heuristics have been developed in 
order to solve a realistically sized problem. 
Computational results indicated that HGA 
outperformed the modeling language CPLEX in 
terms of solution quality and computational times 
for middle or large-scale problems.  The proposed 
heuristic can be considered as a vehicle to solve a 
realistically sized problem.
