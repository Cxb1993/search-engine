Abstract 
In this year, the goal of this integrated project is to complete the whole system for the 
four-on-four middle-size robot soccer game. The system includes improvement and 
implementation of the architecture for robot soccer, establishment of wireless communication, 
design and implementation of the real-time image processing, visual servo systems, design and 
implementation of the servo controller, the intelligent motion tracking control and obstacle 
avoidance for multi-robot system, development the real-time role assignment system, intelligent 
IC design and behavior strategies, etc. We will integrate all the techniques mentioned above and 
implement them on the middle-size robot soccer players. The four-on-four middle-size robot 
soccer game is performed by autonomous robot, which plays with the opponent by executing the 
strategy by itself. For the middle-size robot soccer game, the most important thing is the 
competition with the opposite team and determination of the better techniques. Therefore, we will 
hold games for testing the feasibility and superiority of the techniques from each subproject. We 
will take the results as the reference to improve the design for the next year. 
Keywords: AI, Intelligent Control Strategy, Middle-size Soccer Robot, Obstacle Avoidance, 
Offence and Defense Strategy.
