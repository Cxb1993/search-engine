EXPERIMENTAL 
Materials 
3-Aminopropyltriethoxysilane (APTEOS) as a core of dendritic monomer was 
purchased from Acros Organics and was used after purification. Methyl acrylate (MA) 
and ethylenediamine (EDA) were used for the synthesis of dendritic monomer and 
were purchased from Tedia Company, Inc. N-Isopropylacrylamide (NIPAAm) was 
recrystallized in n-hexane before use in order to remove an inhibitor and was 
purchased from Tokyo Chemical Industry Co., Ltd. 3-(Trimethoxysilyl) propyl 
methacrylate (TMSPMA) as a coupling agent was purchased from Acros Organics. 
2,2′-Azobis(2-methylpropionitrile) (AIBN) as an initiator was purchased from Wako 
Pure Chemical Industries, Ltd. N,N′-Methylenebisacrylamide (NMBA) as a 
crosslinking agent was used as received and purchased from SIGMA Chemical Co.. 
Sulfanilamide, caffeine and vitamin B12 as model drugs were purchased from Fluka 
Chemical Co.. 
 
Synthesis of Dendritic Monomer 
The dendritic monomers, G0 and G1, were prepared via two procedures from 
scheme 1 and scheme 2: (1) Michael addition and (2) amidation where the reaction 
solvent was methanol [12]. The synthesized G0 and G1 were purified via vacuum 
distillation. 
 
Preparation of IPN Hydrogels 
Dendritic monomers G0 and G1 with different molar ratio (0.25 mol%, 0.50 
mol%, 1.00 mol%) and 5 mol% of NMBA based on total monomer concentration 
content were dissolved in 10 mL of DMSO. To this solution, 0.2 mol% AIBN, as an 
initiator was added, the mixture was immediately injected into the space between two 
teflon plates. Polymerization was carried out at 75°C for 4 days. After the gelation 
was completed, the gel membrane was immersed in 1.5 M HCl(aq) as a catalyst for 4 
days to perform sol-gel reaction. Then the gel membrane was cut into disks and 
immersed in acetone and deionized water to remove unreacted monomers. Finally, the 
swollen gels were dried at room temperature for 1 day, and further dried in a vacuum 
oven for 1 day. The sample codes, feed compositions, and yields of the gels were 
listed in Table.1.  
 
Measurement of Swelling Ratio 
The pre-weighed dried gels (Wd) were immersed in deionized water at 25°C until 
swelling equilibrium was attained. Each gel was removed from the water bath, tapped 
with delicate task wipers to remove excess surface water, and weighed as the wet 
a function of the model drugs concentration was prepared at 258 nm, 272 nm, and 360 
nm on the UV spectrophotometer for sulfanilamide, caffeine, and vitamin B12, 
respectively. Drug release experiment was conducted by immersing the above drug 
loaded gels into 10mL deionized water at 37°C. During the drug release experiment, 
the gel was taken out at regular time intervals then immersed into next 10 mL 
deionized water. 
 
RESULTS AND DISCUSSION 
Effects of Dendritic Monomer on Swelling Ratios  
    The effect of the content of hydrophilic dendritic monomer on the swelling ratio 
for the IPN hydrogels is shown in Figure 1~4. The results indicate that the swelling 
ratio of the IPN hydrogel will increase as the amount of dendritic monomer increase 
in the feed due to the increasing hydrophilic amino group of the IPN hydrogels 
(NG0100＞NG050＞NG025＞N, NG1100＞ NG150＞NG125＞N, NTG0100＞
NTG050 ＞ NTG025 ＞ NT, NTG1100 ＞ NTG150 ＞ NTG125 ＞ NT). The more 
hydrophilic monomer exists in the hydrogel network can absorb and hold more water 
and increase the swelling ratio of the hydrogel. Comparing the two different 
generations of dendritic monomers, G1 is more hydrophilic than G0, hence the G1 
series hydrogels show the higher hydrophilic than the G0 series. (NG1100＞NG0100, 
NG150 ＞ NG050, NG125 ＞ NG025, NTG1100 ＞ NTG0100, NTG150 ＞ NTG050, 
NTG125＞NTG025)

Many studies have been focused on the

the preparation of the hydrogel

Organic-inorganic hybrids are

mechanical and electrical properties, and

the hybridization of poly(NIPAAm) and 
inorganic compound improves the 
mechanical strength of poly(NIPAAm) 
gels16. The swelling behavior and 
mechanical properties were investigated 
from two series of NIPAAm-siloxane 
copolymeric hydrogels prepared from 
NIPAAm and 3-(trimethoxysilyl) propyl

prepared a series of hybrid gels with IPN 
or semi-IPN structures. the effect of 
vinyl silane monomer and gel structures 
on swelling behaviors, mechanical 
properties, and morphologies were 
investigated.

Results and Discussion 
Preparation of Organic-Inorganic 
Hybrid Gels 
To investigate the effect of the different 
gel structure on the properties of 
thermosensitive hybrid gels, a series of 
NIPAAm hybrid gels with different 
structures were prepared in this research: 
N-IPN, NT-IPN, NT-semi-IPN. The 
main difference between these gels is 
whether the combination between 
organic and inorganic components to be 
the Si-O covalent bonding or just the 
interpenetration of networks and 
entanglement of polymers. Besides, by 
adding organic crosslinker NMBA to 
form gels with different crosslinking 
types: interpenetrating and 
semi-interpenetrating, discussed the 
effects of structure on thermosensitivity 
of NIPAAm gels. Unadding crosslinker 
NMBA caused the N-semi-IPN series 
unable to form a gel because organic 
component of NIPAAm cannot form 
water-insoluable crosslinking networks 
and generate covalent bonding with 
inorganic siloxane networks with 
absence of TMSPMA. 
Scheme 1 shows the structures of hybrid 
gels: NT-IPN gel is an interpenetrating 
polymer network composed with 
organic network which crosslinked by 
NMBA and inorganic network which 
crosslinked by TEOS. These two 
networks were interconnected by 
covalent bonding that contributed by 
vinyl silane monomer TMSPMA; 
NT-semi-IPN gel is a 
semi-interpenetrating polymer network 
composed with poly NIPAAm and 
inorganic siloxane network, the polymer 
and siloxane network were 
interconnected by vinyl silane monomer, 
TMSPMA; N-IPN gel is an 
interpenetrating polymer network 
composed with organic crosslinking 
network and inorganic network, and 
those are no covalent bonding 
interconnection between two networks. 
From a structural point of view, types of 
networks and existence of 
interconnecton between networks would 
affect gel properties, for this reason 
these three types of gels are discussed in 
this study. 
 
Swelling Kinetics 
Swelling and deswelling kinetic profiles 
as a function of time for hybrid gels 
containing 5mol% of TEOS at 25℃ and 
37℃ were shown in Figure 1. From the 
results of the kinetic profiles, gels 
without crosslinker NMBA, i.e. 
NT-semi-IPN, have highest swelling rate 
and swelling ratio because the 
semi-interpenetrating network caused a 
looser gel structure, and the hydrophilic 
polymer wouldn’t restrict by networks. 
Comparing these two IPN gels, NT-IPN 
and N-IPN, the former gel has slower 
swelling rate and lower swelling ratio 
because it contains hydrophobic silane 
the hydrophobicity of gels increase with 
the addition of TEOS. When content of 
TEOS higher than 10 mol% there might 
be phase separation and the swelling 
ratio get higher than gels with lower 
TEOS content. 
 
Effect of Temperature on Swelling 
Ratios of Hybrid Gels with different 
TEOS Content 
The equilibrium swelling ratios of gels 
with different TEOS content at different 
temperatures are shown in Figure 7-9. 
Results showed that when TEOS content 
higher than 10 mol% (10 to 50 mol%) 
the thermoreversibility of hybrid gels 
would reduced. 
 
Mechanical Properties 
Mechanical properties of hybrid gels 
were listed in Table 2. By compression 
model, the modulus G and effective ρx 
were estimated and calculated, and 
results showed that NT-IPN has highest 
strength and crosslinking density. This is 
because NT-IPN gel was composed of 
two crosslinking networks which 
combined with covalent bonding. 
Moreover, inorganic silane component 
also contributed to gel structure and 
resulted in higher gel strength. 
NT-semi-IPN gel was composed by only 
one network and polymers, its 
mechanical strength and crosslinking 
density were relatively low. Comparing 
N-IPN with NT-semi-IPN, although the 
former has two networks, there is no 
covalently bonded combination between 
these two networks, and consequently 
lower strength and crosslinking density 
than NT-semi-IPN. Considering the 
effect of TEOS content on modulus and 
crosslinking density, the result seemed 
that modulus and crosslinking density 
increase as the TEOS content increase 
when TEOS content higher than 10 
mol%, but there existed no proportional 
relationship between TEOS content and 
mechanical properties. 
 
Conclusions 
A series of organic-inorganic hybrid gels 
with interpenetrating or 
semi-interpenetrating structures were 
prepared with changing the conditions of 
addition of crosslinker and silane 
monomer. Incorporation of vinyl silane 
monomer plays the role of bridge of 
combination between organic and 
inorganic networks. By free radical 
polymerization and sol-gel reaction, 
organic and inorganic networks not only 
interpenetrated but combined with 
covalent bonding. The existence of 
covalent bonding established via vinyl 
silane monomer would affect the 
swelling characteristics and mechanical 
properties of these hybrid gels. 
Considering two kinds of 
interpenetrating polymer networks, 
NT-IPN and N-IPN, the former 
contained vinyl silane monomer and 
resulted in more condense structure, 
more compact mechanical strength, 
lower swelling ratio and smaller 
swelling volume. Comparing

改變胺基矽烷含量進行 silica 之表面改質 
摘要 
改變矽烷與二氧化矽微粒之比例，以溶膠凝膠法(sol-gel method)製備一系列改質
之二氧化矽微粒，鑑定此改質微粒之化學特性，並量測改質二氧化矽微粒之粒徑
與介達電位等特性。改質後之二氧化矽微粒表面帶有胺基，進一步反應接載酸鹼
指示劑，並探討此改質二氧化矽微粒之諸性質，如粒徑、介達電位、酸鹼感應性
等。 
 
實驗部分 
實驗藥品 
Aerosil®200 (Degussa) (AE200)做為基材之親水性表面二氧化矽微粒，
3-aminopropyltriethoxysilane (ACROS) (APTEOS)做為二氧化矽微粒表面改質用
之矽烷，購買後直接使用。Methyl red (Wako) (MR)做為功能化二氧化矽表面微
粒接載之指示劑。Triphenyl phosphate (Acros) (TPP)做為接載 methyl red 之縮合
劑。Pyridine (Wako)與 N-methyl-2-pyrrolidone (Tedia) (NMP)以氫化鈣(calcium 
hydride)乾燥 24 小時後，減壓蒸餾並保存在裝有分子篩的瓶中。 
 
以胺基矽烷改質 Aerosil®200 (AE200) 
依 APTEOS/HCl/H2O/C2H5OH=x/4/16/6 之比例(x=0.5、1.0、1.5、2.0)，並以
APTEOS(mole)/AE200(g)=0.025, 0.050, 0.075, 0.100 之比例(表一)，配製矽烷溶
液，並秤取 AE200 分散其中，常溫攪拌 24 小時進行溶膠凝膠反應，反應完畢後，
過濾分散液，以乙醇與去離子水反覆清洗數次，再過濾。重複清洗與過濾的步驟
數次，洗去未反應之矽烷，置於 40℃烘箱中乾燥，製得一系列胺化之改質二氧
化矽微粒 AE200-APTEOS。 
 
接載 methyl red 製備功能化二氧化矽微粒 
設定酸鹼指示劑 methyl red 為 1mmol，取一三口圓底燒瓶，量取 2mL NMP、0.5mL 
pyridine、1mL triphenyl phosphate、0.27g methyl red、0.3g 改質 silica 
AE200-APTEOS_1.0，置入圓底燒瓶中，80℃攪拌反應 2 小時，反覆過濾、清洗數
次，40℃烘乾，製得功能化二氧化矽微粒 AE200-APTEOS-MR。 
 
改質 silica 之光譜鑑定 
改質後之 silica 以 IR spectrometer(Perkin-Elmer, Spectrum GX))鑑定改質 silica 之官能
基。

取少許改質與未改質二氧化矽微粒，分散於去離子水中，超音波震盪後，以雷射
結果與討論 
二氧化矽微粒的改質 
本研究係以市售表面帶親水性氫氧基(-OH)之奈米級二氧化矽微粒 Aerosil®200(簡
稱 AE200)作為基材，透過溶膠凝膠法，將胺基矽烷在酸催化的條件下進行水解
反應，並與 AE200 表面之氫氧基縮合形成矽氧烷鍵結(siloxane, Si-O-Si)，使帶有
胺基之矽烷接載於 AE200 表面，將 AE200 改質為表面帶有胺基之二氧化矽微粒。
微粒表面之胺基可以進一步與許多化合物進行反應，製備功能性之微粒，在本研
究中選擇具有酸鹼感應性之指示劑 methyl red 作為進一步改質，即透過微粒表面
之胺基與 methyl red 之羧酸基(-COOH)進行縮合而將 methyl red 接載於微粒表面，
欲製備具酸鹼感應性之二氧化矽微粒，其機制如圖一所示。其中 AE200 與
