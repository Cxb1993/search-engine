新型非同步跳時超寬頻系統於實際通道模型下耙狀接收器之設計 
The Rake receiver design of a novel asynchronous time-hopping 
UWB system in realistic channel models 
計畫編號：NSC 99-2221-E-228-002 
執行期限：99 年 8 月 1 日至 100 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：陳俊達   景文科技大學電腦與通訊研究所 
 
一、中文摘要 
 
在本計畫中，一個利用整個匡架去傳
送跳時及脈衝位置調變信號的高容量超寬
頻架構接收機將被提出。此系統是傳送大
調變級數之 M 位元正交脈衝位置調變，而
此方法是由我們之前於[1]提出的架構加
以延伸。此新跳時系統的優點是在 M 位元
脈衝位置調變訊符可以在一個匡架週期中
傳送更多的位元來增加系統容量。由於脈
衝位置調變之調變級數越大可以獲得越好
的位元錯誤率效能，我們所提出的超寬頻
系統可以比傳統的跳時超寬頻系統在相同
的位元傳輸率下有更好的位元錯誤率效
能，特別是在非同步的環境中。在[1]中，
我們使用傳統的超寬頻耙狀接收機，而其
在高斯環境下有最佳化的表現。但由於跳
時超寬頻系統的多重擷取干擾通常不呈現
為高斯分佈，我們必須考慮其他的多重擷
取干擾模型。在此計畫中，對稱穩定分佈
之干擾模型將被應用至最佳耙狀及次佳耙
狀接收機。要注意的是由於我們提出的系
統是採用 M 位元信號，因此上述的最佳耙
狀及次佳耙狀接收機的決定法則必須根據
不同的多重擷取干擾模型來重新推導。 
 
關鍵詞：跳時、超寬頻、耙狀接收器 
 
1. Abstract 
 
In this project, a novel high-Capacity 
UWB receiver that utilizes the whole frame 
to carry out time hopping (TH) and pulse 
position modulation (PPM) will be proposed. 
Consequently, the larger modulation level of 
M-ary orthogonal PPM signaling is utilized 
in the proposed TH scheme which is 
extended from our previous work further [1]. 
An advantage of the new TH system is that 
an M-ary PPM symbol is able to denote more 
information bits in one frame period which 
results in the increasing of system capacity. 
From the concept of larger modulation level 
of M-ary orthogonal PPM signaling can 
achieve better bite error rate (BER) 
performance, our proposed UWB system can 
achieve better BER performance than the 
conventional TH-PPM UWB system in the 
same bit rate especially in the asynchronous 
environment. In [1], the conventional UWB 
Rake receiver is used and optimized for 
Gaussian environment. Because the multiple 
access interference (MAI) of TH-UWB 
systems generally do not perform as 
Gaussian distribution, different model of the 
MAI must be considered. In this project, the 
MAI model  of Symmetric Alpha Stable 
(SAS) distribution will be applied to the 
optimum Rake receiver (ORR) and 
suboptimum Rake receiver (SRR). It is noted 
that the decision rules of ORR and SRR need 
re-derive with different MAI models 
especially when M-ary signals are considered 
in our proposed systems. 
 
Keywords: time hopping, UWB, Rake 
Receiver
