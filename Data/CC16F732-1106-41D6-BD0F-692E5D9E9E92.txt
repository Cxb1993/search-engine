2研究摘要：
鈦及鈦合金因具有良好的機械性質，且其生物相容性及抗腐蝕性均佳，目前已廣泛使
用在骨科、外科及牙科應用上。在牙科方面如純鈦及Ti-6Al-4V合金可被使用在牙冠、牙橋、
牙床、植體等，近年來一些不含Al、V元素具優良生物相容性之新型鈦合金被開發作為牙
科合金用途，主要是針對純鈦強度及鑄造性問題進行改善，但較少針對牙科用鈦合金的加
工性質進行探討，特別是牙科CAD/CAM系統的快速發展使得鈦合金的切削性質更凸顯其重
要性且漸漸受到關注。本研究探討一系列新型Ti-Sn二元合金的切削性，Sn的成分包括1、5、
10、20及30 wt %，並以商業用純鈦(c.p. Ti, Grade II)作為對照組。本研究的切削性評估為刀
具從試片邊緣朝另一側切削，計算試片3分鐘被切削的長度，根據各合金之平均切削長度，
作為合金切削性難易之評估。實驗結果顯示，本研究之切削條件下，Ti-Sn合金較c.p. Ti有
更佳之切削性，且當Sn元素含量增加時，其切削長度大幅提升，展現優良的切削加工特性。
Ti-20Sn合金於120 m/min切削速率下，荷重在200 gf或300 gf時，切削長度分別為c.p. Ti的1.3
及1.4倍；而Ti-30Sn合金於同樣之切削速率下，荷重在200 gf或300 gf時，切削長度甚至分別
高達c.p. Ti的1.7及1.8倍。本研究成功發展一套評估牙科用鈦合金CAD/CAM加工製程的切削
性評估系統，並開發一系列新型Ti-Sn二元合金，且實驗發現Ti-20Sn及Ti-30Sn合金於120
m/min切削速率下，試片之切削面沒有金屬切屑的黏附，相較於其他條件具有最低的表面粗
糙值(Ra)，因此極具潛力應用作為牙科CAD/CAM用鈦合金。
關鍵詞：牙科合金、Ti-Sn合金、CAD/CAM、切削性
The machinability of a series of binary Ti-Sn alloys with Sn contents ranging from 1 to 30 wt%
was investigated, using commercially pure titanium (c.p. Ti) as a control. The specimens were
slotted using an electric dental handpiece and end mills. Machinability was evaluated by the
cutting length, which traveled by the end mill from one edge of the specimen to the other edge.
For each metal specimen, a permitted cutting time of three minutes was used to determine its
average cut length. The experimental results indicated that alloying with Sn significantly
improved the machinability of c.p. Ti in terms of cutting length under the present cutting
conditions. The Ti-Sn alloys with a higher Sn concentration could be cut more readily. At 120
m/min, the lengths for Ti-20Sn were about 1.3 and 1.4 times higher than that of c.p. Ti at 200 and
300 gf, respectively. Additionally, the lengths for Ti-30Sn were about 1.7 and 1.8 times higher
than that of c.p. Ti at 200 and 300 gf, respectively. For Ti-20Sn and Ti-30Sn, there was no
adhesion of metal chips observed in the appearance of the cut surfaces at 120 m/min. Furthermore,
they had the lowest surface roughness (Ra) values at 120 m/min. Our research suggests that the
Ti-Sn alloys with Sn contents of 20 or 30 wt% developed here are viable candidates for
machining at the rotation speed of 120 m/min by the CAD/CAM method.
keyword: Dental alloy, Ti-Sn alloy, CAD/CAM, Machinability
