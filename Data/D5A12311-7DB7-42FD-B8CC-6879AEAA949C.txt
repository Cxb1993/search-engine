~46000 times (8.39×10-7 / 1.81×10-11) higher than that of the 
ST with O2 (sample E), indicating that THP plus ST with O2 
treatment significantly improves the corrosion resistance 
among all the THP +heat treated samples. In addition, samples 
C and E in which heat treatment associated with the O2 flow 
in (50 cc/min) exhibit better corrosion presumably due to the 
produced oxide layer. 
The oxide layer structures of above heat treatments are shown 
in Fig 3a, b, c, d and e respectively. In Fig. 3, it is observed 
that the annealing treatment (Fig.3b and 3c) shows finer oxide 
layers than those of ST treatment (Fig3d and 3e), hints that 
higher temperature results in coarsen oxide layer structure. 
However, Fig3e and 3c which all are with O2 condition exhibit 
the ranking 1 and 2 corrosion resistance performance 
suggesting that even under O2 containing condition, the 
corrosion resistance is not dominated by the observed oxide 
feature. 
Fig.4 illustrates the XRD results of ANN and ST treatments 
under with (Sample C and E) or without O2 condition(sample 
B and D. It appears that the α2(Ti3Al) phases were found in 
sample B and C at positions 2θ=36 and 54°  at ANN 
treatment. Raising the temperature to ST results in the 
increased intensity of α 2(Ti3Al). It is because elevated 
temperature leads to higher volume fraction of βphase where 
Al atoms known as αstabilizer are expelled fromβphase 
region enhancing the possibility of Ti3Al. This is in line with 
the XRD result in samples B and C where at relatively lower 
temperature(ANN), the intensities of α  phase are 
significantly reduced. 
  
Fig. 5 (a) shows the confirmed α phase HRTEM image 
(defects are arrowed) and Fast Fourier Transform indexed 
planes corresponding to the (101)α and (-110)α planes under 
ANN without O2 condition. This needle like α phase forms 
within the matrix with a width of 10 nm which are commonly 
seen even under no THP treatment.  
 
Fig. 6. (a) shows the HRTEM image ofα2(Ti3Al) under ANN 
with O2 condition associated with defects as arrowed. This is 
in line with the XRD result that the increased intensity of 
Ti3Al can explain the higher hardness of sample C than 
sample B in Fig.5.  
 
Fig.7. Under ST without O2 condition, the framed area is 
identified as β phase with the size about 9 nm in which no 
defects are found. It is interesting to note that this 
precipitation of nano particles exhibits the maximum hardness 
among the all the samples although no particular increased 
intensity of this phase is found. Further investigation of 
possible mechanism is needed.  
The present results can be summarized as follows: 
1. THP treatment plus solution treatment together with O2 
flows in at 50 cc/min significantly improves the corrosion 
resistance of Ti-6-4 alloy among all the THP +heat treated 
samples. 
2. The samples C and E being heat treated at ANN and ST 
condition associated with the O2 flow in (50 cc/min) can 
exhibit better corrosion resistance presumably due to the 
produced oxide layer.

摘要 
近期本研究室在 J. Mater. Res. [Wang,’09]發表乙篇有關 Ti-6Al-4V 合金在氣體
電弧銲接後經固溶處理及時效處理後，造成 TiO2, V2O5, and Al2O3 等氧化物在熔融區
大量析出，使得銲後合金具備較佳的抗蝕能力，而且非常有趣的是 Yu 等人研究熱氫
處理後發現會造成 Ti-6Al-4V 合金的晶粒細化成為奈米級結構[Yu,'07]，是以本研究將
探討 Ti-6Al-4V 合金在經熱氫處理及工業用熱處理後的氧化層及抗腐蝕性的相互關
係，並分析各種形態的氧化層，其可能的氧化層結構及形成機制將被討論。
