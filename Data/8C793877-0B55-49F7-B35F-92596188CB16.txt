關鍵字: 2-胺基噻唑、陽離子染料、偶氮、四級銨鹽、抗菌性
ABSTRACT
A series of symmetrical heteroaryl cationic azo dyes have been derived from 4-substituent-
2-chloroacetamide thiazoles which derivatives synthesized by condensation 4-
substituent-2-amino thiazoles with acetyl chloride. The six cationic thiazoles derivatives as
coupling components were prepared by reacting 2-dimethylamino-ethylmethacrylate and N,N-
dimethylbutylamine with 4-substituent-2-chloroacetamide thiazoles, respectively. Then, the
4-substituent-2-amino thiazoles as diazo components were diazotized with cationic thiazoles
derivatives as coupling components to give symmetrical heteroaryl cationic azo dyes. Then, these
symmetrical heteroaryl cationic azo dyes were dyed with polyester and polyacrylnitrile fabrics.
Characterizations of these dyes were carried out by spectral and their dyeing properties measured
were also evaluated.
Key Words：2-Aminothizole, Cationic dyes, Azo, Quaternary ammonium salts, Antimicrobial
研究方法:
先合成中間體 4-苯基-2-胺基噻唑，再與氯乙醯氯製備得另一系列中間體 4-苯基-2 氯乙
醯胺噻唑中間體，分別與不同四級銨鹽 2-（二甲基胺）甲基丙烯酸乙酯與 N.N-二甲胺基丁
烷反應，經反應後製得六支陽離子噻唑中間體。進而將中間體 4-苯基-2-胺基噻唑當重氮鹽，
在冰浴下分別與陽離子中間體偶合鹽進行偶合反應，製得如下兩大系列之染料。
1.以中間體 4-苯基-2-胺基噻唑為重氮成分，分別與 4-苯基-2-（二甲基胺）甲基丙烯酸乙
酯噻唑衍生物為偶合成份，在冰浴條件下行偶合反應後製得雜環陽離子偶氮染料。
2.以中間體 4-苯基-2-胺基噻唑為重氮成分，分別與 4-苯基-N.N-二甲胺基丁烷噻唑衍生物
為偶合成份，在冰浴條件下行偶合反應後製得雜環陽離子偶氮染料。
將上述之兩大系列染料分別對聚酯織物以及聚丙烯腈織物進行染色加工，進而將染織物
檢測其物性。
染織物檢測項目包括上色性 K/S 與均染性。
