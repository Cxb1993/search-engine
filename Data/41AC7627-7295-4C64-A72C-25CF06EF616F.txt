二、緣由與目的 
 
2.1 計畫緣由 
在能源日漸不足的狀況下，節能便成為關鍵的課題，空調系統中又屬冰水主機為最大
耗能，故提昇冰水主機之效率便成為主軸，而蒸發器的熱傳性能對於冰水主機之效率提昇，
有著極大的影響，常見的蒸發器有直膨式、滿溢式及噴淋式。直膨式蒸發器的冷媒是在管
內側蒸發，而冰水則在管外之殼側被冷卻；滿溢式蒸發器與噴淋式蒸發器的冷媒都是在殼
側，而冰水則在管側。噴淋式與滿溢式最大差別為冷媒之使用量，噴淋式噴淋至蒸發管上
使用薄膜蒸發的機制將熱帶走，而滿溢式則是將蒸發管浸泡在冷媒中，利用池沸騰機制將
熱帶走，故噴淋式蒸發器只需少量的冷媒即可與滿溢式蒸發器性能一致甚至獲得更優越的
性能。在一般冰水主機中，常會加入部份的潤滑油於冷媒當中，但潤滑油對於冷媒之熱傳
性能，有著部份影響，Webb and McQuade(1993)實驗研究中，發現冷媒添加潤滑油，其熱
傳性能會低於純冷媒，含油濃度愈高則沸騰效率愈差。因此，要設計高效率的冰水機必先
瞭解冷媒含油率對滿溢式蒸發器之對流沸騰、及滴淋式薄膜蒸發性能的影響，才能設計出
適用於滿溢式及噴淋式冰水機蒸發器所用之蒸發強化表面，進而提升能源使用效率。 
 
2.2 冷媒含油濃度對沸騰影響之文獻探討 
文獻中有關冷媒含油濃度(oil concentration)對沸騰效率之影響的研究，大多是在池沸騰
的狀況下進行，如：Jensen and Jackman(1984), Memory et al. (1993, 1995), Webb and McQuade 
(1993), Webb et al. (1995), Hsieh and Weng (1997), Wang et al., (1999）, Mohrlok et al. (2001), 
Zarnescu et al. (2000), Kedzierski (2001, 2002a, b)等。Tatara and Payvar (2000a, b, 2001)首度量
測了滿溢式蒸發器中，一種鰭片管與一種強化管之管集強制沸騰與含油濃度的關係，使用
的冷媒包括 R123 及 R134a。這些研究結果顯示：一般而言，在滿溢式蒸發器中，含油濃度
愈高則沸騰效率愈差；但在某些潤滑油與冷媒的特定混和比例下，沸騰性能反而較純冷媒
中的性能佳，如 Kedzierski(2002)的實驗顯示，在 R-134a 添加潤滑油可增加 Turbo-BII 強化
表面的沸騰性能。而在含油冷媒的理論分析方面，Mitrovic(1998)分析氣泡周圍的含油濃度
質傳現象，以解釋含油量的影響。筆者亦曾先後對於孔隙沸騰表面(porous surface)及結構式
強化沸騰表面(structured surface)的池沸騰效率與含油濃度的關係(Webb et al., 1995；
Zarnescu et al., 2000)，進行實驗研究，發現含油濃度愈高則沸騰效率愈差，而含油量的影響
程度也與沸騰表面的孔隙大小有關。 
Memory et al. (1993, 1995)進行單管及管集之含油冷媒沸騰熱傳性能測試，其結果與其
他學者有部份的出入。光滑管、結構管及多孔隙管皆呈現當含油率增加時熱傳性能下降，
但鰭片管之熱傳性能則反而隨含油率增加而提昇。主因是沸騰時會產生大量的泡沫，會有
以下兩種影響︰(1)在泡沫跟加熱表面之間產生一層薄膜，增強其熱傳性能。(2)泡沫離開表
面時，會迅速的填補至鄰近的液氣區域，產生二次沸騰。多孔隙管也有此現象，但卻對於
熱傳性能並未有提昇的效果，推測主因為︰(1)產生的泡沫不易進入陷入的孔洞。(2)當潤滑
油進入孔洞時，不易排出。(3)會有大量的油殘留於表面，因多孔隙管有效的將冷媒蒸發但
卻無法回填，造成積油的現象。 
Hambraeus (1995)針對 R-134a 與部份冷凍油量測蒸發器之熱傳性能，探討冷凍油密度、
混合性、表面張力、熱傳導係數、氣體壓力、黏滯力對於熱傳性能之影響。以下為冷凍油
各種性質對於熱傳性能影響之介紹︰

Zarnescu et al. (2002)以鰭片管及多孔隙管與含油率的熱傳性能比較，研究發現含油濃

2.3 噴淋式蒸發器之文獻探討 
噴淋式蒸發器與滿溢式蒸發器的冷媒側都是在殼側，而冰水則在管側。不同的是：噴
淋式蒸發器在管陣上方增加噴淋裝置，並且不使冷媒浸滿整個管集，則可以改變蒸發器之
蒸發熱傳機制，在圓管外形成液體薄膜，利用薄膜蒸發(Thin Film Evaporation)之高熱傳效
率而提升蒸發器的傳熱效率。對於水平排列的滴淋式蒸發管集而言，管排間的液體可能以
液滴狀、柱狀或片狀往下流，這些不同的流動型態必然影響其蒸發性能，因此 Mitrovic 
(1986)、Hu and Jacobi (1996)、Thome (2001)、Roques et al. (2002)等研究水平管集滴淋之流
動型態，並提出判斷各種流動型態的臨界值的計算公式；因為觀察管集流場的實驗設備，
不容易達到測試冷媒時所需之氣密條件，這類的實驗都是以水、乙二醇、油、酒精等在常
壓下進行實驗。至於有關冷媒的滴淋式水平管集，則以 R-11 為主，如 Fujita and Tsutsui (1995, 
1998)以管徑 25mm 的光滑圓管管集，進行 R-11 滴淋式薄膜蒸發實驗，分析管子在管集中
的排數之影響，並提出精確度約 20%的經驗公式。 
水平管上端受噴淋液體衝擊，其對流情況類似於加熱中的平面受噴嘴正向噴擊之二維
對流問題。Miyasaka and Inada (1980) 將加熱中的平面受正向噴擊之水注之流場分為
Stagnation flow, Impingement flow, Parallel Flow 三小部份。加熱面正對噴嘴的一小部分流場
呈近似停滯狀態，稱為停滯流場區域(Stagnation Flow Region)。流體通過停滯流場區之後改
變流向，加速朝兩側流動的區域稱為噴擊區(Impingement Region)。通過噴擊區之後，流體
則近乎平行地貼著加熱面往下游流動，即為平行流區域(Parallel Flow)。 
Owens (1978) 將 Chun ＆ Seban (1972) 所提出的經驗公式，再加入管集間的距離(H)
與管子半徑(R)為參數，而得到如下之經驗公式。

3.1.3 實驗程序 
本項池沸騰實驗之實驗步驟如下： 
(1) 實驗開始裝置前需先清洗紅銅製的鰭片管，先利用銅油擦拭鰭片管，以清除氧化層後
再用酒精清洗表面。 
(2) 實驗設備裝置完成後進行系統測漏，整個實驗約需約四個小時才可完成，故測漏極為
重要。若系統在測漏開始，經過站空24小時後仍維持壓力在96 kPa vac以下，則視為符
合實驗要求。 
(3) 利用真空泵浦將系統抽至真空約100kPa vac ，並持續約三十分鐘以期能將系統內水氣抽
掉。 
(4) 充填冷媒R-134a到覆蓋至最高的測試管後，再繼續充填到液面離最高測試管約3cm則停
止充填。 
(5) 以最大熱通量加熱工作液體，並使其溫度穩定在待測飽和溫度附近(5℃與10℃)。 
(6) 利用恆溫槽之溫度設定，來控制腔體內池溫穩定(±0.1℃內)且與待測溫度相差在±0.5℃

3.2.2 含油濃度的即時量測 
由於含油冷媒在冷凍循環當中對於冷凍能力具有相當大的影響，在冷凍系統中將含油
冷媒取樣出來，既耗時且會減少系統中的冷媒及冷凍油，故近來發展出採用折射率的變化
來量測含油濃度的技術。常見量測方式有以下幾種︰ 
1. 利用不同強度的光源打在媒介上產生不同的折射率 
2. 改變光學路徑 
3. 利用臨界角度的計算方式 
4. 利用干涉條紋 
Fukuta et al. (2010)使用雷射激光改變光學路徑以量測不同含油濃度所產生不同的折射率，
其原理如圖 3.8 所示，入射光經過空氣、玻璃兩種介面進入測試腔體，會因介面折射率不
同而造成入射角與反射角之偏移。實驗主要參數為 R-410A 搭配 PVE 冷凍油，R-134a 搭配
PAG 冷凍油，R-600a 搭配礦物油進行測試，搭配之壓縮機為螺旋式壓縮機，而冷媒含油濃
度則是使用體積比來表示，式子如以下公式(3.6)所示，

二、系統管路側漏 
實驗設備裝置完成後進行系統測漏，利用無油式空氣壓縮機打入 273.694kPa (約 2.7 大
氣壓之壓力)，經過 24 小時之站壓過程觀察壓力值之變化，若壓力值維持不變沒有洩漏，

實驗腔體及管路內無殘留之水分或氣體。 
三、系統絕熱 
絕熱之功用在於降低實驗系統與外界溫度進行熱交換，因此腔體與管路外圍均包覆保
溫泡綿，確保實驗過程中不與外界環境熱交換。 
四、工作流體填充 
確認腔體保持真空後，啟動恆溫水槽並將溫度設定為 5℃，藉此循環冷凝管路內之乙
二醇溶液將腔體內部溫度降至 5℃，達到設定溫度條件後，滴淋蒸發實驗則充填冷媒 R-245fa
至實驗腔體液面高度 3.0cm 處，填充量約 360mL；池沸騰實驗則充填冷媒 R-245fa 高於測
試管 3.0cm 處，填充量約 720mL。 
五、數據量測 
利用微齒輪泵調整實驗所需之循環流量，以最小熱通量加熱測試管，並藉由恆溫水槽
之溫度設定來控制腔體內部溫度達到待測飽和溫度(約±0.3℃)，維持 5 分鐘腔體內部狀態即
可擷取此數據記錄，資料擷取器設定為 5 秒記錄一筆數據，共取 10 筆數據，取其平均值為
所需之數據資料。逐次增加加熱棒之輸入熱量，重複上述步驟，本滴淋蒸發實驗及池沸騰
實驗皆以熱通量遞增之形式作為實驗的準則。 
六、結果分析

4.1 含油冷媒之高效能沸騰管之沸騰實驗結果 
為瞭解各種網目之影響，本研究先以各種網格纏繞於42FPI及50FPI之鰭片管，以量測
其沸騰性能。圖4.1是在池溫5℃時，纏繞80~120目網格的實驗數據。在圖中顯示，高熱通
量時，以#100b較好，但是在熱通量約低於16kW/m2時，則以#120b性能最佳，其原因為#120b
之網格因為氣孔較小，所以在低熱通量之沸騰性能極佳，但在高熱通量時則因為氣孔太小，
冷媒液體不易流入渠道，而使性能下降；#100bX2之網格的氣孔更小使性能更差。圖4.2為
60FPI、鰭片高0.4mm（編號60FPI_0.4H）之鰭片管，在池溫5℃時，纏繞 #100目黃銅（編
號60FPI_0.4H_100b）與雙層 #80目紅銅（編號60FPI_0.4H_80cX2）的數據，並與Webb and 
Pais(1992)與 Jung et al.(2004)所測試之Turbo-B以及Tatara and Payvar(2000)所測試之
Turbo-BII-HP比較。從圖中可以得知60FPI_0.4H_100b目與60FPI_0.4H_80cX2的熱傳係數，
皆明顯高於Turbo-B。其中又以60FPI_0.4H_80cX2性能最佳，在熱通量大於16 kW/m2以上
時蒸發熱傳係數比Turbo-BII-HP高，也比Turbo-B之熱傳係數平均增加1.3倍。 
表4.1為以鰭片管在含油濃度1%之性能為基準，在纏繞不同網格之後，其熱傳性能之
提升倍率。其中以纏繞黃銅100目網格之熱傳性能提升最佳，最高達到3.29倍。而鰭片管纏
繞上網格之後，的確有助於提升熱傳性能。

五、結論 
 本研究以計算流體力學軟體進行滴淋裝置多孔板之孔徑、間距等參數之模擬研究， 
以及滴淋蒸發熱傳實驗，研究結果獲得下列結論： 
1. 本研究之實驗證實：以適當尺寸之網格纏繞於鰭片管，可使鰭片表面形成氣孔，並使
氣體較容易殘留在渠道內，而大幅度增加熱傳性能。 
2. 本實驗結果顯示一或兩層之 #80 ~ 100 目為理想之網格。由 R-134a 之實驗結果顯示，
當飽和溫度為 5℃與 10℃，鰭片管纏繞 #100 目黃銅時，平均提升熱傳性能最佳；飽
合溫度為 26.67℃時，纏繞雙層 #80 目紅銅最好。 
3. 當冷媒 R-134a 含有適量潤滑油時，可增加網格式鰭片表面的沸騰性能；網格密度越小
或氣孔越大，最適量之含油濃度越大。 
4. 在純 R134a 冷媒滴淋蒸發實驗中，在滴淋流量較小(Re = 260)時才能在鰭片上維持薄膜
蒸發狀態，而得到較高之熱傳性能。在高滴淋流量時冷媒液體已經溢滿鰭片間之渠道
而呈核沸騰狀態，其熱傳係數與池沸騰相近。 
5. 在純 R245fa 冷媒實驗當中，光滑管在飽和溫度 20、5oC 時，滴淋薄膜蒸發的熱傳性能
與池沸騰之熱傳性能相近；鰭片管在飽和溫度 20、5oC 時，滴淋薄膜蒸發的熱傳性能
則是比池沸騰佳。 
6. 在含油冷媒實驗中，含油率對於熱傳性能之影響隨測試管的表面結構不同而異。光滑
管應用於池沸騰時，熱傳性能隨著含油量的增加而下降，而應用於滴淋蒸發時，則是

Abstract 
This study investigates a new enhanced boiling surface made by wrapping wire mesh on 
low fin tubes. Pool boiling performance of the new enhanced tubes was tested with 
refrigerant-134a at 5, 10, and 26.67oC saturation temperature for 6~48 kW/m2 heat flux. Brass 
or copper mesh of 80, 100, or 120 mesh per inch was wrapped on finned tubes of 42, 50 or 60 
FPI (fins per inch). The fin heights were either 0.2 mm or 0.4 mm. The test results show that 
mesh covered fin tubes yielded significantly enhanced boiling performance. The influence of 
fin geometry is more pronounced with mesh than without mesh. The preferred mesh size 
decreases with decreasing heat flux. The surface made by wrapping brass mesh of 100 mesh 
per inch on 60FPI tube having 0.4 mm fin height yielded the best boiling performance. It 
enhanced the boiling heat transfer coefficient by 3~8 folds at 5~26.7oC as compared with the 
smooth tube. 
Key Words: Nucleate boiling, mesh, tube, surface geometry, evaporator

1. INTRODUCTION 
Boiling heat transfer in boilers or chillers or any other similar equipment plays an 
important role in determining equipment efficiency. Almost all water chillers presently use 
enhanced tubes in flooded evaporators and falling film evaporators. The enhanced surfaces 
have high area density because of artificial nucleation sites. Webb (2004), Webb and Kim 
(2005) and Thome (1990) surveyed boiling enhancement techniques. The “structured 
surfaces” and “porous surfaces” are the most important types of enhanced boiling surfaces. 
The structured surface is manufactured by cold metal working to form reentrant nucleation 
sites and inter-connected channels on the surface. Commercial structured boiler tubes are 
made by forming reentrant shape cavities on low-fin tubes. Webb and Pais (1992) tested four 
commercial structured tubes with refrigerants R-11, R-12, R-123, and R-134a at 4.4oC and 
26.7oC. Tatara and Payra (2000) tested Turbo-B IITM tube with R-134a. Jung et al. (2004, 
2005) tested Turbo-BTM, Thermoexel-ETM, and a low fin tube (1023 fins/m) in many fluids at 
7oC. Wang et al. (1998) provided pool boiling data of Gewa-B in R-22, R-123, R-134a, 
R-410A, and R-407C. Ribaski and Thome (2006) tested two structured tubes 
(Turbo-BII-HPTM, and Gewa-BTM), one condensing tube (Turbo-CSLTM), and a porous coated 
tube (High FluxTM). Compared with a plain tube, they found the level of enhancement was 
2.4-5.2 fold for Gewa-BTM, and 1.8-7.0 for Turbo-BII-HPTM. 
Chien and Webb (1998a,b, 2001) designed an enhanced boiling surface by piercing pores 
 6 
on a thin copper sheet soldered on fin tips of a low-fin-tube. They performed systematic tests 
of geometric parameters, including surface pore diameter/pitch, subsurface tunnel height, 
tunnel width, and tunnel pitch in R-123, R-134a and R-22 at 26.7oC. Chien and Webb (1998c) 
performed visualization experiments on finned tube geometry that closely simulated the 
actual tube geometry. A transparent polypropylene tape pierced with pores in a fixed pitch was 
adhered on the fin tips. It was clearly shown that during saturated boiling, an active tunnel is 
vapor filled with liquid menisci in the corners. Their observation supports the 
suction-evaporation boiling models (Nakayama et al, 1982; Chien and Webb, 1998d). Chien 
and Chang (2004) investigated bend-fin surfaces having triangular cuts of specific size and 
pitch. The best of such a surface enhances boiling heat transfer about six times the plain 
surface in water and HFC-4310. 
Enhanced boiling surface can also be produced by wrapping metal mesh on tubes to form 
nucleation sites. Corman and McLaughlin (1976) conducted water boiling experiments using 
felt-metal nickel and copper wicking of various thicknesses (0.5 to 3 mm). The screens were 
held down by glass bar laid at the sides of the screens. At low heat flux (60 kW/m2), the 
boiling coefficient was improved about 70%, but the slope of the boiling curve was smaller 
than that for the smooth surface. At heat fluxes greater than 155 kW/m2, performance of the 
wicked surface was inferior. Kumar et al. (2006) wrapped stainless steel mesh on plain tube, 
and found that the mesh-wrapped tube improved the boiling heat transfer coefficient by

4. PRACTICAL SIGNIFICANCE 
Boiling curves of the two mesh covered tubes were compared with commercial tubes at 
5oC saturation temperature (Fig. 12). The 60FPI_0.4_100b tube yields significantly better 
boiling performance as compared with commercial boiling tubes, Turbo-BII-HPTM and 
Gewa-BTM from Ribaski and Thome (2006) at 4.8oC saturation temperature. As shown in Fig. 
12, boiling heat transfer coefficients of Turbo BII-HPTM and Gewa-BTM were similar in the 
experiment of Ribaski and Thome (2006). The data of Gewa-BTM in Wang et al. (1998) and in 
Ribaski and Thome (2006) are in good agreement. The boiling heat transfer coefficient 
reported by Tatara et al. (2000) is much higher than that reported by Ribaski and Thome 
(2006), although they were tested under similar experimental conditions. Boiling performance 
of the 60FPI_0.4_100b tube is much better than the Turbo-BII-HPTM data reported by Tatara 
et al. (2000) at q” > 20kW/m2, but it is about 10% less than that of Turbo-BII™ tube.

2. EXPERIMENTAL SETUP 
2.1 Boiling Test Apparatus 
Figure 2 shows the details of boiling test apparatus. A sight 
glass is made on one side of the test cell to observe the liquid 
level and boiling phenomena during the test.  Three tubes are 
fixed to one end of the rectangular boiling vessel.  An o-ring 
is inserted between the end flange and the test cell for sealing.   
All tubes are 100mm long, and have 19mm outside diameter 
and 9.0 mm inner diameter.  A cartridge heater is inserted in 
each of these tubes.  The total length of the cartridge heater is 
90 mm, but the actual heating length (Lh) is 70 mm long 
located in the middle of the heater.  The heat flux of the test 
tube is calculated based on the actual heated length of the 
heater and outer diameter of the tube.  The heat input of the 
cartridge heater in the test tube is controlled by a variable 
power transformer, and the current and voltage are measured to 
determine the heating power.  Saturation pressure is measured 
at the top (Ps), and the saturation temperature is calculated from 
the mathematic average of the two temperature measured at the 
top and bottom (TC-13 and TC-14).  During the tests, the 
difference of the saturation temperatures at the top and bottom 
is less than 0.5oC.  A Cole-Parmer 68001-24 pressure 
transducer (pressure range: 0-to-100 psig, accuracy: 0.028% of 
100 psig) is used for the tests. Heat is supplied through 
cartridge heaters inserted in the center tubes in the test cell.  
The function of unheated tubes on the top and bottom is to 
simulate the flow in a tube bundle.  A reflux condenser is 
located above the test tubes.  The cooling water temperature 
of the condenser is connected to a constant temperature bath.  
The saturation temperature in the boiling vessel is controlled by 
adjusting the cooling water temperature and flow rate of the 
reflux condenser. 
Figure 3 shows the end view and the side view of the 
instrumented tube.  The instrumented tube is fixed on the end 
flange with four 3.0 mm thick screws.  An O-ring is placed 
between the tube and the end flange for sealing.  Four 
thermocouple wells of 0.55mm diameter are made on the test 
tube at the top, the bottom and the two sides.  T-type 
thermocouples of 0.5mm sheath diameter are inserted in the 
thermocouple wells for tube wall temperature measurement.  
The thermocouple junctions are placed in the middle of the 
heated length.  The center of the thermocouple well was 1.5 
mm away from the tube surface of the plain tube.  One-
dimensional steady-state heat conduction equation in 
cylindrical coordinates is used to correct for the conduction 
temperature drop between the thermocouple and the boiling 
surface.  A heat sink compound is used to ensure good thermal 
contact of heaters and thermocouples with the tube wall. 
The new enhanced boiling surface is made by wrapping 
wire mesh on finned tubes.  The mesh are carefully aligned 
and wrapped with proper strength.  No solder or adhesive is 
used.   Details of the manufacture process are descried in 
Hwang [10].  Brass or copper mesh of 80 or 120 meshes per 
inch was wrapped on two finned tubes. The wire of the #80 
copper mesh is 0.1 mm thick, and the wire of the #100 brass 
mesh is 0.09 mm.  The 42 FPI tube has 0.32 mm fin thickness 
and 0.2 mm fin height in 42 fins/inch (1654 fins/m). The 60 
FPM tube has 0.21 fin thickness and 0.4 mm fin height in 60 
fins/inch (2362 fins/m).  The combinations of the tube and 
mesh in the present study are listed in Table 1. The boiling 
performance is expected to be improved by forming many 
pores of proper sizes on the top of the tunnels formed by the 
fins. 
2.2 Experiment Procedure 
All parts of the test facility were cleaned with acetone and 
pure water before each test.  The test tube was cleaned with 
acetone, and then it was thoroughly flushed with water.  Then, 
it was dried by blowing air. 
The boiling experiments were conducted at saturation 
temperature (Ts) = 5, 10, 20, or 26.6oC.  The boiling tests 
were performed with the following steps. 
 
1. A leak-tight test is performed before charging.  The 
system was considered leak-tight if the system is able 
to hold vacuum (less than 3 mmHg absolute pressure) 
for at least twenty hours. 
2. Charge the working fluid into the system until the 
liquid level is approximately 30.0 mm above the top of 
the highest tube. 
3. Turn the heater to the maximum output.  Keep the 
pool temperature at the desired saturation temperature 
by adjusting the cooling water temperature and flow

4.2 Effect of Fin Pitch and Fin Height 
According to the study of Chien and Webb [5, 6], the 
boiling heat transfer coefficient increases with increasing fin 
density or fin height.  The development of the commercial 
boiling tube also follows this trend. Developed from the 42FPI 
Turbo-B™ tube, the latest two versions, Turbo-BII™ and 
Turbo-BIII™, both have 2362 fins/m (60 fins/in) on the outer 
surface.  Hence, a mesh covered fin tube made on a 60FPI 
tube having 0.4 mm is expected to yield better boiling 
performance than the 42FPI tubes.  The #100 brass mesh is 
selected for the good boiling performance in Fig. 6~8.  The 
boiling curves of the 60FPI fin tube and the mesh covered tube, 
60FPI_0.4H_100b at 5oC saturation temperature are shown in 
Fig. 10.  As compared with the same mesh on the 42FPI tube, 
42FPI_100b, the 60FPI_0.4H_100b yielded much greater 
boiling heat transfer coefficients. As shown in Fig. 10, the 
60FPI_0.4H_100b tube yields better boiling performance as 
compared with data of Turbo-B™ from Webb and Pais [4] at 
4.4oC saturation temperature.  Its boiling performance is much 
better than the Turbo-BII™ data tested by Tatara et al. [11] at 
q” > 20kW/m2, although its performance is about 10% less 
than that of Turbo-BII™ tube.  Compared with the smooth 
tube, the enhancement ratio of the 60FPI_0.4H_100b tube is 
about 7~8 fold at 5oC. 
The boiling activities on the 60FPI fin tube at 16 and 26 
kW/m2 are shown in Fig. 11(a), (b).  The bubble sizes varie in 
a wide range.  The bubble nucleation sites increases as the 
heat flux increases from 16 to 26 kW/m2.  Compared with the 
fin tube, the bubble sizes are more uniform on the mesh 
covered tube as shown on the 60FPI_0.4H_100b tube in Fig. 
12.  The nucleation site density of the mesh covered surface 
(60FPI_0.4H_100b) is less than that of the fin tube without 
mesh, but the frequency of the mesh covered surface is greater 
than that without mesh.  In Fig. 12(a) where q” = 16 kW/m2, 
only a few bubble emerged from the mesh at the lower portion 
of the 60FPI_0.4H_100b tube, but many bubbles emerged from 
the top of the tube rapidly.  The bubble frequency and bubble 
density increased as the heat flux increased.  More bubbles are 
shown in Fig. 12(b), especially at the lower portion of the tube.  
The subsurface tunnels under the mesh are filled with vapor in 
both Fig. 12 (a) and (b).  The tunnels are in suction-
evaporation mode.  As described by Nakayama [12] and 
Chien and Webb [6], the liquid can be evaporates very 
effectively on the thin films on the tunnel wall in suction 
evaporation mode.  Therefore, the boiling heat transfer 
coefficient is successfully enhanced by wrapping a layer of 
#100 mesh around the fin tube. 
5. CONCLUSION 
 From the above experimental results and discussions, 
we conclude: 
 
1. Boiling performance can be enhanced by wrapping 
brass or copper mesh on a fin tube.  The preferred 
mesh size decreases with decreasing heat flux. 
2. For the 42 FPI fin tube, rapping the #100 brass mesh 
yields the highest boiling heat transfer coefficient at 5 
and 10oC saturation temperature. 
3. The boiling heat transfer coefficient increases with 
increasing fin density from 42FPI to 60FPI, and 
increasing fin height from 0.2 mm to 0.4mm. 
4. The 60FPI_0.4H_100b tube yields the best boiling 
performance.  Its enhancement ratio over the smooth 
tube is about 7~8 fold at 5oC.

Liang-Han CHIEN†   Rong-Hong CHEN

Summary 
 This study investigates the evaporation heat transfer performance of R-134a falling film on three horizontal 
tubes in a vertical column.  This study tested three kinds of tubes, a smooth tube, and a fin tube of 0.4mm fin 
height, 60FPI (Fins Per Inch). Experiments were performed at saturation temperatures of 10, 20 and 26.7℃. The 
liquid flows through a liquid feeder with a row of circular holes at a rate of 0.0075~0.0363kg/ms, and heat fluxes 
varied from 4.5 to 48.5kW/m2. The test results show that the heat transfer coefficient of the smooth tube increases 
with increasing heat flux, fluid temperature, and it increases slightly with increasing flow rate before dry-out 
occurs. At low flow rates (less than 0.015kg/ms) or Ref (≤ 255), the fin tube is in thin film evaporation mode, 
which is revealed by its high heat transfer coefficient, and at high flow rates (0.0225, 0.03, and 0.0375kg/ms) the 
falling film evaporation curves are similar to those in pool boiling. The fluid temperature and the flow rate have 
only minor influence on heat transfer coefficient before dry-out occurs. The fin tube enhances the heat transfer 
coefficient by 6.3~8.29 fold. 
  
Key words: falling film evaporation, spray, heat transfer, thin film, fin, tube, dry-out 
 
1. Introduction 
     
Falling film evaporation has been applied in many 
fields and showed high heat transfer efficiency.  
Falling film evaporation on vertical tubes has been 
utilized in many applications in the chemical 
industry.  Horizontal plain tube falling film 
evaporators have been used in desalination industry, 
and tested in Ocean Thermal Energy Conversion 
pilot plants.  Falling film (spray) evaporators have 
recently been used in the vapor-compression 
refrigeration chillers.  A falling film evaporator has 
greater evaporation heat transfer coefficient under 
many operation conditions and required much less 
refrigerant charge than a flooded evaporator for the 
same chiller capacity.  Therefore, one expects that 
it has the advantages of increasing chiller efficiency, 
reducing capital and operating costs, and reducing 
global warming gases. 
Earlier studies on falling film evaporation were 
conducted on vertical tubes.  Chun and Seban1 
modified the Nusselt’s condensation theory to 
predict their experimental data on falling film 
evaporation outside a vertical tube.  Chun and 
Seban1 categorized the flow regimes of falling film 
evaporation as: laminar flow, wavy-laminar and

4. Discussion    
 
Fig. 8 compared the heat transfer coefficient of 
falling film evaporation of three types of tubes.  
The finned tube yields the best performance.  The 
mesh tube tested by Chen11 was made by wrapping 
copper screen mesh on a 60 FPI tube.  The mesh 
tube has shown very high boiling performance as 
reported by Huang10.  However, the data of the 
mesh tube shows that the mesh tube only yields 
slightly better heat transfer performance than the 
smooth tube.  The data show that falling film 
evaporation performance of the smooth tube and 
mesh tube is dependent on heat flux, but not for the 
finned tube. 
For falling film evaporation, the tube surface is 
easier to be dry up than that in pool boiling mode.  
Hence, it is very important to predict the dry out heat 
flux.  The dry out heat flux (q”dry) data of the 
smooth tube is shown in Fig. 9.  As shown in Fig. 9, 
the dry out heat flux increases with increasing Ref. 






二、沸騰測試設備與實驗方法 
2.1 實驗系統之架設 
圖1為池沸騰熱傳之測試系統示意圖，容器內部上方為冷凝盤管，冷凝盤管
是由3/8英吋之銅管所構成，其管內之冷卻水連接至恆溫水槽以進行溫度控制，
使測試腔體內之池溫達到穩定控制。池沸騰測試腔體內置兩個熱電偶溫度計
(TS1、TS2)及一個壓力計，以量測工作流體之飽和溫度與壓力。測試腔體左側接
上充填閥以進行抽真空與充填工作流體，並且設置有四個觀測視窗，以便觀察測
試管池沸騰情況。 
如圖2為測試管連接容器端外觀圖，每根測試圓管的尾端分別以四個螺絲固
定於腔體壁面，並利用螺絲使增強管尾端壓迫O型環而與容器內側壁面形成氣
密；測試圓管中間之圓孔內插入加熱棒(heater)接至自耦變壓器控制其加熱量
(40W ~ 200W)；在每個測試圓管的管壁中，由連接容器的一端鑽出4個熱偶井
(thermocouple well)；在測試容器端板相對於各測試管之位置也鑽出加熱棒、熱
電偶、固定螺絲之開孔，如圖1所示。每個測試圓管插入四支0.5mm熱電偶
(sheathed thermocouple) 如圖1所示之TC1 ~ TC8，以便量測管壁溫度，並藉以外插
推算出測試表面之溫度。  
圖3為測試管設計圖，(a)為測試圓管尺寸示意圖，其外徑(ro)為19mm、內徑
(ri)為8.5mm、管長(L)為105mm，加熱棒長度(HL)為90mm，但加熱棒前後兩端各
有1㎝無加熱線圈，故實際加熱棒長度為70mm；(b)為熱電偶溫度深入銅管示意
圖，熱電偶溫度插入銅管深度(LT)為50mm；(c)為熱偶井配置位子圖，每個井的
直徑為0.55mm，距離管截面之圓心為r1=8mm；(d)為測試管之蓋板圖。 
2.2 網格式鰭片之纏繞方式  
網格式鰭片進行正式實驗前之纏繞方式經過多次嘗試，及多次反覆試驗，
發現網格間的重疊部分多寡、網格與鰭片管間的緊密程度、網格與鰭片管間是否
貼合無突起，都會對熱傳性能產生影響，將以上部份進行改善，並掌握到成功的
纏繞方式，將方法描述於下。 
纏繞方式分別有單層與雙層，其纏繞孔隙範圍為0.1168 ~ 0.268mm： 
(1) 單層纏繞方式：使用網格長60cm、寬1.2cm纏繞，一開始先用鐵製管束將鰭
片管與網格固定。在纏繞時網格與網格重疊約1 ~ 2mm，纏至管子末端後再
以鐵製管束加以固定。 
(2) 雙層纏繞方式：使用網格長60cm、寬2.0cm纏繞，一開始先用鐵製管束將鰭
片管與網格固定，並在纏繞時使前後兩圈網格重疊約1cm，而使整個纏繞區
均有兩層網格後再以鐵製管束加以固定，詳細纏繞方式請參閱黃浩林[4]。 
2.3 實驗程序 
本項池沸騰實驗之實驗步驟如下： 
(1) 實驗開始裝置前需先清洗紅銅製的鰭片管，先利用銅油擦拭鰭片管，以清除
氧化層後再用酒精清洗表面。 
(2) 實驗設備裝置完成後進行系統測漏，整個實驗約需約四個小時才可完成，故
測漏極為重要。若系統在測漏開始，經過站空24小時後仍維持壓力在96 kPa 
vac以下，則視為符合實驗要求。 
(3) 利用真空泵浦將系統抽至真空約100kPa vac ，並持續約三十分鐘以期能將系
統內水氣抽掉。 
(4) 充填冷媒R-134a到覆蓋至最高的測試管後，再繼續充填到液面離最高測試管
約3cm則停止充填。 
(5) 以最大熱通量加熱工作液體，並使其溫度穩定在待測飽和溫度附近(5℃與
10℃)。 
(6) 利用恆溫槽之溫度設定，來控制腔體內池溫穩定(±0.1℃內)且與待測溫度相
差在±0.5℃以內，維持五分鐘後便可開始連續擷取十筆以上之數值，取平均
值為所需之數據。 
(7) 逐次減少電源供應器輸入的熱量，每測試一組加熱量後，重複步驟6.與7.。 
明本研究之實驗過程與文獻 [4] 一致。圖 5 為本實驗前後兩次纏繞#100 目黃銅
網格增強管之再現性測試，選取 9.6kW/m2、20 kW/m2、36 kW/m2 三個熱通量，
兩次誤差為 2.2 ~ 11%，在 60FPI_0.2H_#100b 之實驗誤差範圍內(17%)，由此可
知本實驗之測試增強管具備合理之再現性。 
 
4.2 含油冷媒對網目影響之分析 
為瞭解冷媒含油濃度對各種網目之影響，本研究先以各種網格纏繞於
60FPI_0.4H之鰭片管，以量測其沸騰性能，再添加不同濃度之潤滑油以觀察其沸
騰性能變化。根據Webb and McQuade [2] 實驗研究，發現冷媒添加潤滑油之熱
傳性能會低於純冷媒，含油濃度愈高則沸騰效率愈差。而Kedzierski [3] 實驗數
據卻顯示，在R-134a添加少量潤滑油可增加Turbo-BII強化表面的沸騰性能，但含
油濃度超出1%時，性能即開始下降。比較Kedzierski [3] 與Webb and McQuade [2] 
所選擇之含油濃度，發現關鍵而重複的含油濃度為1%與2%，因此本研究選定含
油濃度為1%與2%進行分析。 
圖6為60FPI_0.4H之鰭片管在池溫為5℃與10℃時，不同含油濃度的比較，
由圖中可以明顯的發現，當純冷媒添加潤滑油濃度1%後，在潤滑油影響之下，
會導致熱傳性能開始下降。圖7 、圖8分別為在池溫5℃與10℃時，含油濃度為1%
之條件下，網格式鰭片管與鰭片管之比較，從圖中可以了解，60FPI_0.4H鰭片管
在含油濃度的影響之下，會造成其熱傳性能的下降，但將其纏繞上不同網格之
後，熱傳性能明顯獲得提升。 
表1為以鰭片管在含油濃度1%之性能為基準，在纏繞不同網格之後，其熱傳
性能之提升倍率。其中以纏繞黃銅100目網格之熱傳性能提升最佳，最高達到3.29
倍。而鰭片管纏繞上網格之後，的確有助於提升熱傳性能。 
圖9為60FPI_0.4H_#100b在池溫5℃時，含油濃度1%與2%的實驗數據，並與
Kedzierski [3] 在含油冷媒中所測試之Turbo-BII-HP比較，冷媒皆為R-134a，含油
濃度也都為1%與2%，差異為本實驗之熱通量從9.6 ~ 36kW/m2，Kedzierski [3] 所
測試之Turbo-BII-HP，熱通量從10 ~ 160kW/m2 。由圖中可以看出，在熱通量為
10 ~ 36kW/m2時，#100b在含油濃度為1%之熱傳係數，與Kedzierski [3] 在含油冷
媒中所測試之Turbo-BII-HP的熱傳係數相當接近，而#100b在含油濃度為2%時，
熱傳性能則低於Kedzierski[3] 在含油冷媒中所測試Turbo-BII-HP。 
 
4.3 含油冷媒與鰭片高度之分析 
    圖10與圖11為相同鰭片數、纏繞網格數與含油濃度下，在池溫為5℃與10℃
時，比較鰭片高度0.2 ~ 0.4mm之影響，黑色點曲線為鰭片高0.2mm；白色點曲線
為鰭片高0.4mm。當鰭片高增加時，可以增加工作流體與鰭片表面接觸面積，使
熱傳性能提升；圖中顯示鰭片高為0.4mm時，不論是否添加潤滑油，其熱傳性能
都較佳。表2為鰭片高度由0.2mm增加為0.4mm後之熱傳性能提升倍率，以
60FPI_0.2H_#100b為基準所計算。由該表可知，鰭片高增為兩倍所獲得之熱傳係
數增為1.5 ~ 1.7倍。 
從圖10與圖11中發現，60FPI_0.2H_#100b 與60FPI_0.4H_#100b的熱傳性能，
在含油濃度為1%之時最大，增加潤滑油含量後熱傳性能會下降，此趨勢大部分
與Kedzierski [3] 之實驗結果相符。但實驗數據顯示60FPI_0.2H_#100b在池溫
10℃時 ( 圖 11) ，含油濃度 2%的熱傳性能反而高於含油濃度 1% ，推測
60FPI_0.2H_#100b之渠道、氣孔等條件較適合含油率2%在10oC之物性，因此熱
傳性能會較好。

摘要 
本研究以實驗的方法探討不同測試管的滴淋薄
膜蒸發熱傳性能，使用圓管打孔滴淋裝置滴淋於三根
上下垂直排列之水平測試管以模擬滴淋式蒸發器，工
作流體為 R-134a，在飽和溫度 10、20、26.7℃下，
測試管種類為光滑管、鰭片密度 60FPI (Fins Per Inch) 
鰭片高 0.4mm 之鰭片管與纏繞黃銅 #100 目網格之
網格鰭片管，改變熱通量範圍為 4.5~48.5kW/m2，流
量範圍為 0.0075~0.0375kg/ms。 
實驗結果發現，光滑管在未發生乾枯現象前熱傳
係數受熱通量、溫度增加而上升，而流量只有些微的
影響；鰭片管在低流量(0.0075、0.015kg/ms)時可形成
薄膜蒸發之高熱傳效率模式，熱傳性能大幅增加，在
高流量(0.0225、0.03、0.0375kg/ms)時滴淋蒸發與池
沸騰的性能相當接近；網格鰭片管熱傳性能在未發生
乾枯現象前熱傳係數隨熱通量增加而上升，受溫度、
雷諾數影響不大。三種測試管中以鰭片管之性能最
佳，相對於光滑管的熱傳性能，在飽和溫度為10、20
與26.7℃時，低流量之鰭片管平均熱傳性能分別提升
8.29、6.32與6.55倍，高流量之鰭片管平均熱傳性能
則分別提升3.74、3.13與2.99倍。 
 
關鍵字：滴淋式蒸發器、薄膜蒸發、鰭片、乾枯現象 
  
1. 前言 
為了更進一步提升蒸發器的性能，採用各式各樣
的沸騰強化管，根據沸騰增強之理論，欲使表面的沸
騰性能增強需要適當大小之氣孔與渠道，Thors et al. 
[1]提出鰭片熱傳遞管的最佳設計參數為40~70 FPI，
Chien and Webb [2][3]則提出理想之冷媒沸騰所需氣
孔直徑約0.15~0.25mm；而目前國外性能優異之沸騰
強化管，以各種不同幾何形狀之氣孔表面，氣孔直徑
約為0.1~0.3mm，搭配鰭片密度約40~60FPI (Fins Per 
Inch)，鰭片高度約為0.4~0.5mm之渠道。由於熱傳機
制上的差異，薄膜蒸發與核沸騰所適用之冷媒側鰭片
理應有所不同；然而目前之蒸發管鰭片多依核沸騰之
增強理論所設計，有關滴淋薄膜蒸發之最佳鰭片則仍
有待研究。  
Moeykens and Pate [4][5]以冷媒R-134a在飽和溫
度2℃條件下，分別為smooth、Trubo-Cii、Turbo-B、
W-40FPI、W-26FPI、W-SC、W-SE等七種測試管，
進行噴淋薄膜蒸發熱傳性能實驗。實驗結果顯示在低
熱通量未發生乾枯現象前，熱傳係數僅受熱通量增加
而上升，而流量參數只有些微的影響 
 
2. 滴淋蒸發實驗設備與方法  
2.1 滴淋蒸發實驗設備  
本滴淋蒸發實驗之循環示意如圖 1 所示，實驗腔
體為一封閉密閉空間，各設備間以外徑 6.35mm 之銅
管與管件連接，其工作流體 R-134a 藉由微齒輪泵浦
提供循環之動力，首先與過冷裝置進行熱交換降低流
體溫度至過冷狀態，以確保其以完全液態通過流量計
量測其流量，通過流量計之流體進入滴淋分配管後經
由一串滴淋孔，使得均勻滴淋於水平測試管上方表面
形成薄膜液體，而表面的滴淋薄膜液體帶走熱量蒸發
成為氣體，該氣體將藉著腔體上方之冷凝盤管與來自
恆溫水槽的乙二醇溶液作熱交換，冷凝之流體與未蒸
發之滴淋薄膜液體將累積於腔體下方之儲液空間，然
後經過 #100 目(每英吋有 100 目數)之網格過濾雜質
殘餘物保持進入微齒輪泵浦液體之純淨，最後回到微
齒輪泵浦維持穩定流量，完成循環。 
2.2 實驗參數範圍 
本研究測試冷媒 R-134a 在不同測試管之滴淋蒸
發熱傳性能，表 1 為實驗參數設定總表。三種測試管
分別為光滑、鰭片與網格鰭片，其鰭片表面參數如圖
2 所示。實驗之熱通量藉由調整電源供應器輸出電壓

導途徑傳遞至測試管表面，最大發熱量約為 200W，
熱通量範圍自 4.5~48kW/m2符合冰水機之應用。實驗
之流量利用公式(1)，以最大加熱量(Q)為 600W，冷

4. 結果與討論  
本實驗光滑、鰭片、網格鰭片三種測試管在飽和
溫度為10、20、26.7℃改變薄膜雷諾數之滴淋蒸發性
能，並以上管(top tube)之數據與滴淋蒸發、池沸騰文
獻作比較討論。 
4.1光滑管之滴淋蒸發熱傳性能 
圖 4 為光滑管在飽和溫度 10℃之上管滴淋蒸發
性能曲線圖，由此圖可知，因為流量愈大管子表面愈
不易因液體蒸發而乾涸，在本實驗的三組 Ref之臨界
熱通量( )測試範圍隨 Ref增加而增加，當管子表面
未乾枯時，薄膜雷諾數對滴淋蒸發的熱傳性能並無太
大的影響。本實驗之光滑管與 Moeykens and Pate [5]
飽和溫度 2℃下 Ref=751 實驗結果相比較，熱傳係數
較差，但是兩者的性能趨勢相似。圖 5 與圖 6 則分別
為光滑管在飽和溫度 20℃與 26.7℃之上管實驗結
果，其熱傳性能趨勢與 10℃(圖 4)時相似。

而滴淋蒸發與Moeykens and Pate [4]、黃浩林、
簡良翰 [6]之池沸騰性能比較如圖4與圖6所示，本實
驗之滴淋蒸發性能較文獻[4]、[6]池沸騰低，但熱傳
性能趨勢一致；筆者認為本實驗的圓管打孔滴淋裝
置，在光滑管表面上所形成之滴淋薄膜厚度可能過
厚，過厚的滴淋薄膜厚度反而將薄膜蒸發的機制破
壞，造成滴淋蒸發的熱傳性能不如池沸騰。 
4.2鰭片管之滴淋蒸發熱傳性能 
圖 7 為鰭片管在飽和溫度 10℃之上管滴淋蒸發
性能曲線圖，從圖中發現 Ref=255 時性能有明顯提
升，這可能是因為鰭片管的熱傳性能較光滑管佳，因
此在低薄膜雷諾數時，熱通量造成流體蒸發使得表面
上的液體薄膜厚度變薄，達到薄膜蒸發的效果；而如
圖 8 飽和溫度 20℃，Ref=255 與圖 9 飽和溫度 26.7℃，
Ref=150、300 時的高熱傳性能原因相同；因為在低流
量時，測試管較易形成乾枯現象，故與高流量相比，
臨界熱通量相對的較小。 
圖 7 中，與 Moeykens and Pate [5]文獻比較不同
鰭片數之性能，本實驗之鰭片數為 60FPI，Moeykens 
and Pate [5]則為 26、40FPI、W-SC，當中 60FPI 之性
能趨勢與 26FPI、40FPI、W-SC 不同，本實驗 60FPI
之熱傳性能隨著熱通量增加而提升，熱通量的增加使
得熱傳機制改變。 
4.3滴淋蒸發熱傳性能比較 
圖10為滴淋蒸發熱傳性能分佈比較圖，性能趨勢
線1~4分別為：光滑管(smooth tube)、高薄膜雷諾數之
鰭片管(fin tube at high Ref)、低薄膜雷諾數之鰭片管
(fin tube at low Ref)及陳榮鴻、簡良翰 [7]網格鰭片管
(mesh tube)。在熱通量為10~48.5kW/m2，性能趨勢線
2與3中，鰭片管與網格鰭片管性能提升之原因為鰭片
與網格的表面結構作用相較於光滑管之平滑表面，表
面積的增加能增強其熱傳性能。而當熱通量大於
10kW/m2時，光滑管與鰭片管表面開始產生零星的微
小氣泡，隨著熱通量的增加，熱傳機制改變，對流沸
騰的作用漸增，故性能趨勢線2(高薄膜雷諾數之鰭片
管)之平均熱傳提昇倍率為3.1倍；反觀網格鰭片管，
雖然隨著熱通量的增加其對流沸騰的作用漸增，但網
格過小導致液體無法流入鰭片渠道內，造成滴淋薄膜
液體在網格表面即直接流走，因而熱傳係數上升斜率
不如鰭片管，所以性能趨勢線3(網格鰭片管)的平均熱
傳性能提升倍率為2.2倍。 
圖 10 中性能趨勢線 3 為鰭片管在低薄膜雷諾數
(Ref=150、255、280、300)時之滴淋蒸發熱傳性能，
此性能大幅提升之原因，推斷應是滴淋的流體量少所
以在鰭片管表面上形成之薄膜厚度較薄，熱通量造成
流體蒸發使得表面上的液體相當容易蒸發，達到薄膜
蒸發的效果，故性能趨勢線 3(低薄膜雷諾數之鰭片管)
之平均熱傳性能提升倍率為 6.86 倍，比起趨勢線 2
之性能又高出 2 倍。將圖 10 的高薄膜雷諾數之鰭片
管、低薄膜雷諾數之鰭片管、網格鰭片管性能分佈，
細分為不同飽和溫度時的平均熱傳性能提升倍率，如
表 2 所列，由表中可知較低的飽和溫度，性能提升倍
率較高。

摘要 
本研究主旨探討在含油冷媒中以不同的網格數
目纏繞於鰭片管上之池沸騰熱傳性能研究。本實驗工
作流體為R-134a，測試之飽和溫度為5℃與10℃，使
用之潤滑油為EMKARATE RL -150S，含油濃度為1%
與2%；鰭片管之鰭片密度為60FPI，鰭片高為0.2mm
與0.4mm；而纏繞網格目數分別有#80目紅銅與#100
目 黃 銅 ， 其 纏 繞 後 之 網 格 孔 隙 寬 範 圍 約
0.15~0.32mm。實驗結果發現：鰭片管纏繞 #80 ~ #100
目的網格後，可使鰭片表面形成許多大小適當的氣
孔，並且氣體較易殘留於渠道內，而大幅度增加熱傳
性能。當飽和溫度5℃與10℃時，在含油濃度1%與
2%，所有測試管中以60FPI (Fins Per Inch)、鰭片高
0.4mm之鰭片管纏上#100目黃銅網格效果最佳；相對
於為纏網格之60FPI鰭片管在含油濃度1%之熱傳性
能，飽和溫度為5℃與10℃時，其平均熱傳性能分別
提升2.77與2.57倍。 
 
關鍵字： 氣泡、沸騰、網格、鰭片、測試管、含油
濃度 
  
一、前言 
黃浩林、簡良翰 [1] 提出一種新型的網格式鰭
片管，將紅銅網目與黃銅網目纏繞於鰭片管上，發現
某些網目與鰭片管的組合，使得熱傳性能獲得大幅度
的提升，因此選用其中性能最好的組合來作為測試含
油冷媒熱傳性能的增強管。Webb and McQuade [2] 
實驗研究中，發現冷媒添加潤滑油，其熱傳性能會低
於純冷媒，含油濃度愈高則沸騰效率愈差。Kedzierski 
[3] 實驗數據則顯示，在R-134a添加少量潤滑油可增
加Turbo-BII強化表面的沸騰性能，但含油濃度超過
1%時，性能則開始下降。比較Kedzierski [3] 與Webb 
and McQuade [2] 所測試之含油濃度，可發現含油濃
度在1%與2%之變化最大，因此選定含油濃度為1%與
2%做為本實驗之測試範圍，並方便與文獻進行比
較。本研究並探討網格式鰭片管，及網格式鰭片管在
含油濃度影響下之熱傳性能變化，實際應用於空調主
機之蒸發器上的可能性。 
  
二、沸騰測試設備與實驗方法 
2.1 實驗系統之架設 
圖1為池沸騰熱傳之測試系統示意圖，容器內部
上方為冷凝盤管，冷凝盤管是由3/8英吋之銅管所構
成，其管內之冷卻水連接至恆溫水槽以進行溫度控
制，使測試腔體內之池溫達到穩定控制。池沸騰測試
腔體內置兩個熱電偶溫度計(TRS1R、TRS2R)及一個壓力
計，以量測工作流體之飽和溫度與壓力。測試腔體左
側接上充填閥以進行抽真空與充填工作流體，並且設
置有四個觀測視窗，以便觀察測試管池沸騰情況。 
如圖2為測試管連接容器端外觀圖，每根測試圓
管的尾端分別以四個螺絲固定於腔體壁面，並利用螺
絲使增強管尾端壓迫O型環而與容器內側壁面形成
氣密；測試圓管中間之圓孔內插入加熱棒(heater)接至
自耦變壓器控制其加熱量(40W ~ 200W)；在每個測試
圓管的管壁中，由連接容器的一端鑽出4個熱偶井
(thermocouple well)；在測試容器端板相對於各測試管
之位置也鑽出加熱棒、熱電偶、固定螺絲之開孔，如
圖1所示。每個測試圓管插入四支0.5mm熱電偶
(sheathed thermocouple) 如圖1所示之TCR1 R~ TCR8R，以
便量測管壁溫度，並藉以外插推算出測試表面之溫
度。

圖，其外徑(rRoR)為19mm、內徑(rRiR)為8.5mm、管長(L)
為105mm，加熱棒長度(HRLR)為90mm，但加熱棒前後

圖，每個井的直徑為0.55mm，距離管截面之圓心為
rR1R=8mm；(d)為測試管之蓋板圖。

四、結果與討論 
4.1 含油冷媒對網目影響之分析 
為瞭解冷媒含油濃度對各種網目之影響，本研
究先以各種網格纏繞於60FPI_0.4H之鰭片管，以量測
其沸騰性能，再添加不同濃度之潤滑油以觀察其沸騰
性能變化。 根據Webb and McQuade [2] 實驗研究，
發現冷媒添加潤滑油之熱傳性能會低於純冷媒，含油
濃度愈高則沸騰效率愈差。而Kedzierski [3] 實驗數
據卻顯示，在R-134a添加少量潤滑油可增加Turbo-BII
強化表面的沸騰性能，但含油濃度超出1%時，性能
即開始下降。比較 Kedzierski [3] 與 Webb and 
McQuade [2] 所選擇之含油濃度，發現關鍵而重複的
含油濃度為1%與2%，因此本研究選定含油濃度為1%
與2%進行分析。 
圖6為60FPI_0.4H之鰭片管在池溫為5℃與10℃
時，不同含油濃度的比較，由圖中可以明顯的發現，
當純冷媒添加潤滑油濃度1%後，在潤滑油影響之
下，會導致熱傳性能開始下降。 
圖7 ~ 8分別為在池溫5℃與10℃時，含油濃度為
1%之條件下，網格式鰭片管與鰭片管之比較，從圖
中可以了解，60FPI_0.4H鰭片管在含油濃度的影響之
下，會造成其熱傳性能的下降，但將其纏繞上不同網
格之後，熱傳性能明顯獲得提升。 
表1為以鰭片管在含油濃度1%之性能為基準，在
纏繞不同網格之後，其熱傳性能之提升倍率。其中以
纏繞黃銅100目網格之熱傳性能提升最佳，最高達到
3.29倍。而鰭片管纏繞上網格之後，的確有助於提升
熱傳性能。 
圖9為60FPI_0.4H_#100b在池溫5℃時，含油濃度
1%與2%的實驗數據，並與Kedzierski [3] 在含油冷媒
中所測試之Turbo-BII-HP比較，冷媒皆為R-134a，含
油濃度也都為1%與2%，差異為本實驗之熱通量從9.6 
~ 36kW/mP2 P，Kedzierski [3] 所測試之Turbo-BII-HP，
熱通量從10 ~ 160kW/mP2P 。由圖中可以看出，在熱通
量為10 ~ 36kW/mP2P時，#100b在含油濃度為1%之熱傳
係數，與Kedzierski [3] 在含油冷媒中所測試之
Turbo-BII-HP的熱傳係數相當接近，而#100b在含油
濃度為2%時，熱傳性能則低於Kedzierski[3] 在含油
冷媒中所測試Turbo-BII-HP。 
 
4.2 含油冷媒與鰭片高度之分析 
    圖10與圖11為相同鰭片數、纏繞網格數與含油濃
度下，在池溫為5℃與10℃時，比較鰭片高度0.2 ~ 
0.4mm之影響，黑色點曲線為鰭片高0.2mm；白色點
曲線為鰭片高0.4mm。當鰭片高增加時，可以增加工
作流體與鰭片表面接觸面積，使熱傳性能提升；圖中
顯示鰭片高為0.4mm時，不論是否添加潤滑油，其熱
傳性能都較佳。表2為以60FPI_0.2H_#100b為基準，
當鰭片高度增加為0.4mm後，其熱傳性能提升倍率。
鰭片高增為兩倍所獲得之熱傳系數增為1.5 ~ 1.7倍。 
從 圖 10 與 圖 11 中 發 現 ， 60FPI_0.2H_#100b 與
60FPI_0.4H_#100b的熱傳性能，在含油濃度為1%之
時最大，增加潤滑油含量後熱傳性能會下降，此趨勢
大部分與Kedzierski [3] 之實驗結果相符。但實驗數
據顯示60FPI_0.2H_#100b在池溫10℃時(圖11)，含油
濃度2%的熱傳性能反而高於含油濃度1%，推測
60FPI_0.2H_#100b之渠道、氣孔等條件較適合含油率
2%在10PoPC之物性，因此熱傳性能會較好。 
五、結論 
本研究以實驗方法，在池溫5℃、10℃之R-134a，
量測鰭片數為60FPI，纏繞 #80目紅銅與#100目黃銅
之沸騰熱傳性能，並且探討不同鰭片高(HRtR)與含油濃
度(質量分率%)的影響，綜合實驗數據分析可得以下
結果： 
1. 以適當尺寸之網格纏繞於鰭片管，可使鰭片表
面形成氣孔，並使氣體較容易殘留在渠道內，
而大幅度增加熱傳性能。在含油率 0 ~ 2%之
R-134a 中，其性能與商用沸騰管 Turbo-BII-HP
相近。 
2. 本實驗結果顯示，當飽和溫度為 5℃與 10℃，
添加含油濃度為 1%，以 60F_0.4H 鰭片管之熱
傳性能為基準，纏繞 #100 目黃銅，熱傳性能最
高提升 3.29 倍；纏繞#80 目紅銅，熱傳性能最
高提升 1.49 倍；#80 目紅銅雙層，熱傳性能最
高提升 2.79 倍。 
3. 鰭片管之熱傳性能提升，可藉由改變鰭片高(HRtR)
達成。在純冷媒時 60F_0.2H_#100b，鰭片高由
0.2mm增加為 0.4mm時，純冷媒之熱傳性能提
高 1.47 ~ 1.68 倍；含油濃度為 1 ~ 2%時，熱傳
性能提高 1.52 ~ 1.76 倍。

Abstract
This study investigates the evaporation heat transfer performance of R-245fa in pool boiling and falling 
film. Two kinds of tubes, a smooth tube and a fin tube of 0.4mm fin height, 60FPI(Fins Per Inch) were tested. 
Experiments were performed at saturation temperatures of 5, 10 and 20oC. The liquid flowed through a liquid 
feeder with a row of circular holes at a rate of 0.012 ~ 0.04kg/ms, and heat fluxes varied from 6 to 50kW/m2.
The results show that falling film heat transfer coefficient is better than pool boiling with smooth tube at low 
heat fluxes. The falling film heat transfer coefficient is better than pool boiling for the fin tube. The dry out 
heat flux increases with increasing falling film flow rate.  However, the falling film flow rate has negligible 
influence on the heat transfer coefficient when the surface is wet. The pool boiling heat transfer coefficient of 
R-245fa is similar to R-123. Hence, R-245fa is a good substitute of R-123. A heat transfer correlation of 
falling film evaporation on smooth tube, which predicts the data in this study within ±20%, is provided.

經過 24 小時之站壓過程觀察壓力值之變化，若壓力
值維持不變沒有洩漏，則符合實驗要求。釋放高壓空
氣，使用真空泵浦將實驗腔體內部抽至真空 10-3 
Torr，以確保實驗腔體及管路內無殘留之水分或氣
體。 
三、系統絕熱 
絕熱之功用在於降低實驗系統與外界溫度進行
熱交換，因此腔體與管路外圍均包覆保溫泡綿，確保
實驗過程中不與外界環境熱交換。 
四、工作流體填充 
確認腔體保持真空後，啟動恆溫水槽並將溫度設
定為 5℃，藉此循環冷凝管路內之乙二醇溶液將腔體
內部溫度降至 5℃，達到設定溫度條件後，滴淋蒸發
實驗則充填冷媒 R-245fa 至實驗腔體液面高度 3.0cm
處，填充量約 360mL；池沸騰實驗則充填冷媒 R-245fa
高於測試管 3.0cm 處，填充量約 720mL。 
五、數據量測 
利用微齒輪泵調整實驗所需之循環流量，以最小
熱通量加熱測試管，並藉由恆溫水槽之溫度設定來控
制腔體內部溫度達到待測飽和溫度(約±0.3℃)，維持
5 分鐘腔體內部狀態即可擷取此數據記錄，資料擷取
器設定為 5 秒記錄一筆數據，共取 10 筆數據，取其
平均值為所需之數據資料。逐次增加加熱棒之輸入熱
量，重複上述步驟，本滴淋蒸發實驗及池沸騰實驗皆
以熱通量遞增之形式作為實驗的準則。 
六、結果分析 
將實驗中所測得之數據統整，進行數據結果與討
論。

AN EXPERIMENTAL STUDY OF TWO PHASE MULTIPLE JET COOLING ON 
STRAIGHT-FINNED SURFACES USING A DIELECTRIC FLUID 
 
 
Liang-Han Chien Chin-Yao Chang Han-Yang Liu

National Taipei University of Technology,  
Taipei, Taiwan 
 
 
ABSTRACT 
In the present study, a jet cooling device for electronics 
was investigated using FC-72 as the working fluid.  The 
system temperature of jet cooling device was set at 50℃.  The 
jets were 5 mm above the test surface, and the heated area of 
the test surface was 12×12 mm2. The nozzle plate had 5 or 9 
pores of 0.24 mm in diameter.  The test surfaces included: a 
smooth surface and two straight-finned surfaces of either 400 
or 800μm fin height, 200 or 400 μm fin thickness and gap 
width.  The volume flow rate varied from 50 to 150 ml/min 
(Re = 1655 ~ 1860).  The results showed that the heat transfer 
performance increased with increasing flow rate, or increasing 
surface area enhancement ratio.  The finned surface of 800μm 
fin height, 200μm fin thickness and gap width yielded the best 
performance, which is about 250 % greater than the smooth 
surface at 150 ml/min.  As a promising chip cooling solution, 
the thermal resistance of spray cooling on this straight-finned

INTRODUCTION 
Spray and impingement cooling provides high heat transfer 
coefficients, maintains uniform temperature on modern devices, 
and dissipates heat in components of large heat fluxes, such as 
micro-processors and laser diodes.  Spray cooling can be 
implemented by means of liquid jets or liquid droplets (Fabbri 
and Dhir [1].  The droplet sprays can have the form of a mist, 
and impinge on the surface in sequences of streams.  If the 
frequency of the droplets streams is high enough, the droplets 
merge forming continuous liquid jets.  Impinging jets can be 
classified as either free-surface or submerged jets.  For  a 
free-surface jets, the liquid is ejected from the nozzle into 
gaseous environment before impinging upon the target.  As 
for submerged jets, the fluid is discharged from the nozzle into 
the same liquid.  For single jets, the local heat transfer 
coefficient has a bell-shaped distribution with the maximum 
value at the stagnation point.  The heat transfer coefficient 
depends on several parameters including Reynolds number 
(Re), Prandtl number (Pr), jet diameter (d), Nozzle leght-to-
diameter ratio (l/d), jet-to-tarjet spacing (hN/d), and nozzle 
geomtry.  Li and Garimella [2] proposed general correlations 
for confined and submerged impinginement of liqiuid and air 
jets with orifice diameters of 1.59~12.7 mm.  For multiple jets, 
jet sacing (S), and jet array arrangement have been found to be 
important.  Pan and Webb [3] closely examed the effect of jet 
spacing for S/d = 2 ~ 8 with water as the coolant.  They found 
the stagnation point heat transfer coefficient was independent 
of jet-to-jet spacing but dependent on jet-to-target scaping.  
Womac et al [4] investigated both free-surface and confined jet 
impinginement cooling using water and FC-77.  Both 2×2 and 
3×3 arrays of circular jets of 0.513 mm and 1.02 mm were 
tested.  Womac et al [4] correlated their data with an area-
weighted combinatio of impingement and wall jet regions.  
Experimental data of jet impingement of nozzle diameter less 
than 1.0 mm were relatively limited.  Fabbri and Dhir [1] used 
water and FC77 as working fluid to conduct experiments on the 
heat transfer under arrays of micro-jets of 69.3~250 μm 
diameter, and developed a heat transfer correlation of multiple 
jets for various jet spacing, diameters, and coolant flow rates.  
Robinson and Schnitzler [5] experimentally investigated liquid 
water jet impingement for both free-surface jet arrays and 
confined submerged jet arrays, which consisted of straight 
holes of 1.0 mm diameter with jet spacing of 3~7 jet diameters.

CONCLUSION 
From the present test results of spray cooling and 
discussions, we have concluded: 
 
1. For a fixed pore size, the impinging jets of nine 
jets array, having a smaller jet spacing, results in 
better cooling performance than five jets array. 
2. The dry-out heat flux decreases as the flow rate 
decreases for a fixed pore distribution. 
3. The cooling performance increases with increasing 
surface area enhancement ratio, γA.  Compared 
with the smooth surface at the same wall superheat 
and flow rate, the cooling rate of the C-822 
straight-finned surface is enhanced by 3~4 fold. 
4. This jet cooling device is found to be a promising 
chip cooling solution for its low thermal resistance 
using dielectric fluid, FC-72.  The thermal 
resistance of the C-822 surface is between 0.14 
and 0.15 K/W at 150 ml/min flow rate in the range 
of 60~120 W heat input.   
5. Correlations of multiple jets cooling on smooth 
and straight-finned surfaces are proposed.  The 
root means square of the prediction error is 17.6%.  
It predicts 94% of the present data are predicted 
within ±20%, for the data in the range of Re = 
1655 ~ 8960, Bo = 0.024 ~ 0.389, area 
enhancement ratio (γA) = 2.07 ~ 5.32, (S/d) = 13.7 
or 20.6.

AN EXPERIMENTAL STUDY OF TWO PHASE MULTIPLE JET COOLING ON 
STRAIGHT-FINNED SURFACES USING A DIELECTRIC FLUID 
 
 
Liang-Han Chien Chin-Yao Chang Han-Yang Liu 
Department of Energy and Refrigerating Air-Conditioning Engineering,   
National Taipei University of Technology,  
Taipei, Taiwan 
 
 
ABSTRACT 
In the present study, a jet cooling device for electronics 
was investigated using FC-72 as the working fluid.  The 
system temperature of jet cooling device was set at 50℃.  The 
jets were 5 mm above the test surface, and the heated area of 
the test surface was 12×12 mm2. The nozzle plate had 5 or 9 
pores of 0.24 mm in diameter.  The test surfaces included: a 
smooth surface and two straight-finned surfaces of either 400 
or 800μm fin height, 200 or 400 μm fin thickness and gap 
width.  The volume flow rate varied from 50 to 150 ml/min 
(Re = 1655 ~ 1860).  The results showed that the heat transfer 
performance increased with increasing flow rate, or increasing 
surface area enhancement ratio.  The finned surface of 800μm 
fin height, 200μm fin thickness and gap width yielded the best 
performance, which is about 250 % greater than the smooth 
surface at 150 ml/min.  As a promising chip cooling solution, 
the thermal resistance of spray cooling on this straight-finned 
surface varied between 0.14 and 0.15 K/W in the range of 60 ~ 
121 W of heat input when the present jet cooling system 
operated at 150 ml/min.  Correlations of multiple jets cooling 
on smooth and straight-finned surfaces have been proposed.  
For the data in the range of Re = 1655 ~ 8960, Bo = 0.024 ~ 
0.389, surface area enhancement ratio (γA) = 2.07 ~ 5.32, this 
correlation predicts 94% of the present data within ±20%. 
 
Keywords: jet, impingement, electronic cooling, straight fin 
 
INTRODUCTION 
Spray and impingement cooling provides high heat transfer 
coefficients, maintains uniform temperature on modern devices, 
and dissipates heat in components of large heat fluxes, such as 
micro-processors and laser diodes.  Spray cooling can be 
implemented by means of liquid jets or liquid droplets (Fabbri 
and Dhir [1].  The droplet sprays can have the form of a mist, 
and impinge on the surface in sequences of streams.  If the 
frequency of the droplets streams is high enough, the droplets 
merge forming continuous liquid jets.  Impinging jets can be 
classified as either free-surface or submerged jets.  For  a 
free-surface jets, the liquid is ejected from the nozzle into 
gaseous environment before impinging upon the target.  As 
for submerged jets, the fluid is discharged from the nozzle into 
the same liquid.  For single jets, the local heat transfer 
coefficient has a bell-shaped distribution with the maximum 
value at the stagnation point.  The heat transfer coefficient 
depends on several parameters including Reynolds number 
(Re), Prandtl number (Pr), jet diameter (d), Nozzle leght-to-
diameter ratio (l/d), jet-to-tarjet spacing (hN/d), and nozzle 
geomtry.  Li and Garimella [2] proposed general correlations 
for confined and submerged impinginement of liqiuid and air 
jets with orifice diameters of 1.59~12.7 mm.  For multiple jets, 
jet sacing (S), and jet array arrangement have been found to be 
important.  Pan and Webb [3] closely examed the effect of jet 
spacing for S/d = 2 ~ 8 with water as the coolant.  They found 
the stagnation point heat transfer coefficient was independent 
of jet-to-jet spacing but dependent on jet-to-target scaping.  
Womac et al [4] investigated both free-surface and confined jet 
impinginement cooling using water and FC-77.  Both 2×2 and 
3×3 arrays of circular jets of 0.513 mm and 1.02 mm were 
tested.  Womac et al [4] correlated their data with an area-
weighted combinatio of impingement and wall jet regions.  
Experimental data of jet impingement of nozzle diameter less 
than 1.0 mm were relatively limited.  Fabbri and Dhir [1] used 
water and FC77 as working fluid to conduct experiments on the 
heat transfer under arrays of micro-jets of 69.3~250 μm 
diameter, and developed a heat transfer correlation of multiple 
jets for various jet spacing, diameters, and coolant flow rates.  
Robinson and Schnitzler [5] experimentally investigated liquid 
water jet impingement for both free-surface jet arrays and 
confined submerged jet arrays, which consisted of straight 
holes of 1.0 mm diameter with jet spacing of 3~7 jet diameters.

CONCLUSION 
From the present test results of spray cooling and 
discussions, we have concluded: 
 
1. For a fixed pore size, the impinging jets of nine 
jets array, having a smaller jet spacing, results in 
better cooling performance than five jets array. 
2. The dry-out heat flux decreases as the flow rate 
decreases for a fixed pore distribution. 
3. The cooling performance increases with increasing 
surface area enhancement ratio, γA.  Compared 
with the smooth surface at the same wall superheat 
and flow rate, the cooling rate of the C-822 
straight-finned surface is enhanced by 3~4 fold. 
4. This jet cooling device is found to be a promising 
chip cooling solution for its low thermal resistance 
using dielectric fluid, FC-72.  The thermal 
resistance of the C-822 surface is between 0.14 
and 0.15 K/W at 150 ml/min flow rate in the range 
of 60~120 W heat input.   
5. Correlations of multiple jets cooling on smooth 
and straight-finned surfaces are proposed.  The 
root means square of the prediction error is 17.6%.  
It predicts 94% of the present data within ±20%, 
for the data in the range of Re = 1655 ~ 8960, Bo = 
0.024 ~ 0.389, area enhancement ratio (γA) = 2.07 
~ 5.32, (S/d) = 13.7 or 20.6.
