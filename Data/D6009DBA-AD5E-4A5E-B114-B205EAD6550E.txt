離子液體萃取廢水溶液中苯胺類化合物之研究 
 
中文摘要 
 
以往液相－液相萃取技術是以揮發性有機溶劑，如苯、甲苯、二氯甲烷及氯仿等。但
這些溶劑對於環境及人體具有高度危害性，故各國皆已立法限用。近年來，綠色溶劑室溫
離子液體(Room temperature ionic liquids, RTILs)已廣泛被使用，其優點如下：低揮發性、不
易燃性、低毒性、高穩定性且對環境友善。本研究即以室溫離子液體[1-丁基-1-甲基吡咯
啶][雙(三氟甲基磺醯基)醯亞胺]([bmpy][Tf2N])萃取水溶液中的有機染料，來探討取代具
有危害性之傳統有機溶劑的優越性。 
在合成染料中所選用為四種對於水溶解性相異之染料，親水性染料為番紅花紅
(Safranin O)及羅丹明(Rhodamin B)，疏水性染料為對位紅(Para red)及鎂試劑二號(Magneson 
II)。對位紅須溶於鹼性溶液加熱至 75℃，而鎂試劑二號則溶於鹼性水溶液中。以離子液體
[bmpy][Tf2N]萃取番紅花紅、羅丹明及對位紅趨近 100%效率，而萃取鎂試劑二號也有高達
99%以上之萃取效率。 
在親水性染料方面，就針對不同之酸鹼值，探討萃取率，發現番紅花紅於 pH=2～8，
其萃取率趨近 100%；於 pH=10 的條件下，略降至 97%；pH=12 時，其萃取率為 87%。至
於羅丹明方面，在 pH=2～8 的條件下，萃取率為 99%；於 pH=10 的條件下，略降至 98%；
pH=12 時，其萃取率為 97%。 
 
關鍵字：室溫離子液體、有機溶劑、染料、萃取率、綠色技術 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 1
 
一、前言及研究目的 
 
隨著科技産業蓬勃發展，提高生活水準及便利性，相對的製造出大量的污染物，然而
其中某些有機化合物對人體具有毒性及危害性，進而衍生層出不窮的環境問題。近年來，
環保意識抬頭，加上環境規範日趨嚴苛，已將污染控制及減量視為今社會的一大議題，因
此全球積極推廣綠色環保技術的發展，主要可由源頭上消除污染，並設安全、低毒性且對
環境衝擊較小的化學藥劑來取代危害性物質，以符合綠色化學的精神。 
傳統液相－液相萃取中，常以苯、二氯甲烷及氯仿等當萃取溶劑，而其本身是具高揮
發性、易燃性及毒性強，所以在操作上有諸多安全考量，且亦有産生揮發性有機物質(Volatile 
organic compounds, VOCs)之污染物生成，不僅構成操作人員安全疑慮以及額外製造環境公
害。近年來，新發展出綠色環保溶劑－室溫離子液體已廣泛被使用，其優越化學特性如下：
(1)趨近於零之蒸汽壓；(2)不易燃性；(3)具熱穩定性及化學穩定性；(4)具對有機和無機物不
錯的溶解性；(5)和部分有機溶劑、水有較低或完全不混溶的能力；(6)可回收再利用；(7)
針對陰、陽離子之調配可改變離子液體的物理化學性質等特性；(8)可溶解氣體(CO2、O2…
等)；(9)低毒性；(10)導電度高；(11)廣大的電化學電位窗(分解電壓)。因此，離子液體可以
作為對環境友好之介質與溶劑。 
本研究即以室溫離子液體[1-丁基-1-甲基吡咯啶][雙(三氟甲基磺醯基)醯亞胺]，
1-Butyl-1-methylpyrrolidinium bis (trifluoromethylsulfonyl)imide ([bmpy][Tf2N])當萃取劑，萃
取水溶液中的有機染料，來探討取代具有危害性之傳統有機溶劑的可行性。有機染料可分
為親水性染料為紅花紅(Safranin O)及羅丹明(Rhodamin B)，而疏水性染料為對位紅(Para red)
及鎂試劑二號(Magneson II)。並藉以改變萃取時間及 pH 值，探討液相－液相萃取技術運用
於有機染料，並且開拓離子液體處理染整廢水一項新的應用領域。

Recently, SCWO has been proven as a powerful technology for destruction organic solvents from a 
wide variety of toxic wastes [9-13].  The auto ignition temperature (AIT) is the temperature at 
which combustible materials ignite spontaneously in air without a spark or flame being present.  
However, at the AIT an activation energy supply is required to initiate the combustion of the 
substance.  Therefore, supercritical water oxidation (SCWO) creates a good condition for 
degradation of a substance at its AIT. 
Ethylene glycol (EG) is a manufactured chemical and widely used in antifreeze, de-icing reagent.  
EG has strong hydrogen bonding and very miscible with water is difficult to degrade by traditional 
water treatment.  PGMEA with chemical formula CH3COOCH(CH3)CH2OCH3  is a manufactured 
organic solvent and is frequently used as a photo-resist to clean the organic residuals on electronic 
wafers.  In a typical semiconductor manufacturing process, a large quantity of ultra-pure water is 
consumed in washing these photo-resists.  As a result, a significant amount of wastewater 
containing PGMEA is generated.  The present study focused on the degradation of EG and PGMEA 
by SCWO.  Some preliminary works were done for wastewater contains high concentration of EG 
and PGMEA.  In recent years a growing interest in the SCWO of organics in sub- and supercritical 
water has been developed.  However, less attention was paid in understanding the nature of AIT of 
organic solvent in SCWO reaction.  To the best of our knowledge no reports are available in 
detailed on the SCWO degradation of organic solvents related to their AIT characteristics.  
Therefore, for the past couple years in my lab, one of my SCWO studies’ goal is to find out the 
relation between combustion characteristics and the physical properties of organic solvent.  
Especially, effects of auto ignition temperature on the decontamination of waste water containing 
organic solvent by supercritical water oxidation. 
 
2 MATERIALS AND METHODS 
A continuous flow SCWO system is shown in Figure 1.  Ethylene glycol or PGMEA is mixed 
with water to simulate the waste and the investigated concentration of organics in SCWO reactor 
was controlled between 2.0 to 16.0%.  The removal of total organic carbon (TOC) and chemical 
oxygen demand (COD) are used to evaluate the destruction efficiency of either EG or PGMEA.  
The EG/PGMEA is pressurized and flows into the reactor system by ISCO pump.  The waste 
feeding stream is cooled by water before entering the reactor to prevent plugging due to the potential 
precipitation of inorganic salt and metal oxides in the wastes and the formation of various tars in 
reactor tube.  The pre-heated water is pressurized and fed into the reactor by a diaphragm pump.  
Compressed air supplied from a 10 hp air cylinder (10~90mL/min) is pressurized by a booster (AGT 
14/62, 17~42mL/min).  A constant pressure in the reactor was maintained at 23~27MPa through 
this experiment and sufficient air is used as the oxidant in this work.  The pre-heated water, air and 
EG/PGMEA are mixed in reactor, which is made of Inconel-625 with internal volume of 200mL in 
9
of aqueous PGMEA solution at different SCWO temperatures.  The average measured initial pH of
