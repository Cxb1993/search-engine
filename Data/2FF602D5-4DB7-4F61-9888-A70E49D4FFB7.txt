Abstract
The objective of this paper aims at making
use of the equivalent electrical and mechanical
system’s models to deduce and analysis the
relations between the dynamic behavior of an ac
electromagnetic contactor and different initial
phase angles of voltage source. By analyzing the
input electrical energy distribution in each
subsystem of contactor, we can further realize
the related dynamic characteristics and precede
the improvement of performance with the aid of
obtained valuable results. Based on easy
modeling and high calculating efficiency, the
magnetic circuit analysis method is selected as
establishing contactor simulation model
approach. Additionally, the system transient
features of contactor are analyzed from two
aspects such as electromagnet part and
mechanical part. The relations between different
initial phase angles and dynamic behaviors are
explored through simulation tests. From the
simulation results, exactly there exists an
optimizing initial phase angle of voltage source
leads to a desired energy transfer from the
voltage source. Moreover, the energy
distribution is related to each subsystem can also
be obviously observed from all the simulation
processes. Whereas the kinetic energy of
contacts prior to the collision is effectively
controlled, the using lifespan is prolonged and
the operating reliability is improved as well.
Keywords: Electromagnetic contactor,
Magnetic circuit analysis,
Mathematical model, Initial phase
angle, Energy distribution.
