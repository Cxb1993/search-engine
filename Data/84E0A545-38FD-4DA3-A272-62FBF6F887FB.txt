Key words: porous silicon, multi-layer, porosity, photovoltaic devices 
Recently, PS multi-layer structures with different porosity, including double 
layers PS structures and three layers PS structures, have received much interest 
because they possess the potential to realize the all-silicon optoelectronic integrated 
circuits (OEICs) by integrating PS light-emitting devices with other PS passive 
optical components. In this project, we are to prepare modulated-porosity PS 
multi-layer structures on Si substrates to check the poetntail for applications in solar 
cells.  
We had successfully prepared various porosity-modulated PS multi-layer 
structures on Si substrates by modulating the etching current in the electrochemical 
anodization processes. Photovoltaic properties of devices with the developed PS 
structures as the main absorption layers had also measured. Experimental results 
revealed that devices based on porosity-modulated PS layers achieved wider 
photo-responsivity and hence possessed high potential in development of photovoltaic 
devices.
