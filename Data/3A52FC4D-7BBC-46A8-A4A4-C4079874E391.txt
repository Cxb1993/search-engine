一、中文摘要
本研究製作一簡易型圓形治具進行壓合實
驗，使用材料為 7075 高強度鋁合金，實驗為兩塊
材料透過正向壓力進行銲合，探討在固定壓力和溫
度下，有無氣體保護對本體的銲合影響，透過治具
預製縫隙觀察溢料的銲合情況，分析材料銲合金相
組織、銲合強度等情形。研究結果發現，本體未成
功銲合是因為接合面氧含量較多。硬度分析顯示在
未加氣體保護的本體銲縫硬度值約為 77.6HV，加
氣體保護後本體銲縫硬度值可提升至 94.51HV，在
溢料部分銲縫硬度值為 106.59HV，此為 7075 母材
硬度值之兩倍。具銲縫之試片透過拉伸試驗後，得
知 在 無 氣 體 保 護 的 本 體 抗 拉 強 度 平 均 為
54.34MPa，有氣體保護者為 58.11MPa，溢料部分
為 410.32MPa，已達母材抗拉強度之 85.19％。
本研究藉由有限元素分析軟體 DEFORM 3D
進行模擬分析兩種不同銲合位置之方管擠伸成
形，Type(1)銲合位置在方管角偶處，Type(2)則在
方管側邊，藉此比較不同銲合位置對擠製成形負荷
以及模具應力、磨耗、壽命，等影響，再以萬能拉
伸實驗機針對兩副模具擠製出的方形管，進行擴孔
與拉伸實驗，以所得實驗結果比對模擬所得資訊，
探討方管在不同銲合位置下之擴孔與銲道接合強
度。研究結果得知方管四條銲道整體平均銲合強度
為 376MPa，約達母材抗拉強度值之 78.99％。
本研究另建立分子動力學分析程式，使用嵌
入式原子法模擬 FCC 結構之鋁、銅以及二元合金
(Al0.9Cu0.1)材料於奈米尺度下進行接合的特性探
討，並使用平行運算搭配國家高速網路中心之叢集
設備以增進模擬之效率。研究結果顯示︰壓合深度
會影響其接合的強度，較大的壓合深度會有較高的
接合強度。在速度效應的部分，快速拉伸會造成材
料較快發生斷裂的現象，且快速拉伸的最大應力值
會大於慢速拉伸的最大應力值，而拉伸速度的不
同，工件斷裂的形貌會有所不同，慢速拉伸的工件
拉伸至較細長才斷裂。溫度越高分子的動能越大延
展性較佳，故有較高的應變值。因為鋁的熔點較
低，在高溫(900K)已接近其熔點，原子結構已不再
是晶格排列(FCC)，所以造成強度大幅的減弱，而
三種材料的接合強度依序為︰銅鋁合金>銅>鋁。
關鍵詞：高強度鋁合金、7075、壓合實驗、銲縫、
接合強度、分子動力學、嵌入式原子法、平行運算、
擴散、空孔缺陷
The study used simple type round billets for
pressing and joining experiments where the studied
material is high strength aluminum alloy 7075 under
constant pressure and temperature conditions. Two
kinds of condition are varied, namely: the pressing
fixture with initial gap or not, and the condition in
protection environment or not. In order to investigate
the joining results in the main body and the flash part
of specimen, the microstructure, welding strength
and oxidation percentage have been analyzed by
using SEM/EDS and mechanical testing device.
Research results show that the specimen in main
body has better mechanical properties, such as tensile
strength and hardness in protection environment than
without protection environment. Specimen in flash
part has the best mechanical properties. Sample
element analysis by EDS found that the main body
failed to weld due to contain high oxygen percentage
in contact interface. Hardness analysis show that the
hardness value is about 77.6HV in the main body
near the welding seam without environment
protection, and 94.51HV in the main body near the
welding seam with environment protection, and
106.59HV in the welding seam in the flash part
which reached two times of the initial Al7075 billet.
Through the tensile tests for welding seam, the
tensile strength in main body is approximately
54.34MPa without protection environment,
58.11MPa with protection environment. The tensile
strength in flash part is about 410.32MPa which is up
to 85.19% of initial Al7075 billet.
This study use DEFORM 3D finite element
analysis software to simulate two different types of
hollow extruding die configurations for square tube
extrusion process, namely: type (1) welding position
at the corners of the square tube and type (2) at the
edges. The welding strengths of these two kinds of
extruded tube are studied by expanding test and
tensile test. The service lives of these two extrusion
die components are studied based on the die stress
and die wear information from DEFORM simulations.
Research result shows that the average welding
strength attends 376MPa which is up to 78.99% of
initial Al7075 billet.

三、實驗流程
本研究實驗治具參考文獻[7] Al7075壓合實驗
及其特性之研究進行製作，首先進行治具的設計並
將治具委外製作，再進行治具與胚料的測溫實驗，
得到胚料和模具加熱的溫度-時間關係圖，並以此
作為控制壓合實驗的條件依據，再進行壓合實驗，
把實驗完成的試片做金相分析與機械性質研究，最
後整理各實驗所得的結果。
3.1 實驗材料
本研究係以高強度鋁合金為主，因此使用鋁合
金 7075(Al 7075)做為實驗的材料，其試件規格為
直徑ψ30mm、高 20mm 之圓柱體，相關組成成份
與試件，如表 1。
3.2 實驗治具
本實驗壓合治具以圓形治具設計為主，採用
組合式內外兩層治具，材質為 SKD-61 模具鋼(經
熱處理)，內層為兩片半圓形組合式治具，厚度為
8mm，高度為 41mm；外層模具厚度 10mm，高度
40mm，內外層均有 3 度的拔模角，方便壓合結束
後可以非破壞組合式治具的方式取出材料，另一組
以相同設計方式並增加氣體保護裝置的上蓋，治具
組合時內層有預製縫隙 2mm，在上蓋鑽兩個孔做
為注入惰性氣體之管線連接處，治具組合示意圖，
如圖 1 所示。
3.3 測溫實驗
測溫實驗的目的是了解胚料在壓合實驗中升
溫變化，確保材料達到壓合實驗所需的溫度進行壓
合。實驗方式是取兩塊鋁合金 7075 胚料，一塊為
上胚料，另一塊為下胚料。在上胚料中心鑽一個
ψ7mm的貫穿孔，使熱電偶能夠穿過上胚料，與下
胚料接觸，並在距離上胚料中心 8mm 的左右位置
和下胚料中心位置各鑽一個 ψ3.5mm、深 8mm 的
孔，同時在加工三支ψ3mm、長度 12mm 的插銷，
為確保有相同的導熱係數以免造成誤差，所以插銷
的材質同樣使用鋁合金 7075，相關裝置與量測示
意圖如圖 2 所示。
3.4 壓合實驗
依據測溫實驗結果來控制壓合實驗的進行時
間，當溫度隨著時間增加，達到設定的壓合溫度，
操作萬能試驗機進行壓合實驗，機台與模具擺放實
驗示意圖，如圖3所示。圖3為無氣體保護壓合實驗
示意圖，有氣體保護壓合實驗僅在治具上方加入上
蓋與氣體注入管。
3.5 材料金相觀察試驗
壓合實驗後得到的試片，使用砂輪切割機進行
切割，對材料觀察面進行研磨，分別以不同號數
(#240、400、600、800、1200、1500、2000)之水
砂紙研磨，再以 0.3μm 的 Al2O3 粉水溶液進行拋
光，拋光完成的試片放入由 2g NaOH + 5g NaF +
93ml H2O 配製的腐蝕溶液中，浸泡約 30 秒至 1 分
鐘即可，腐蝕後的試片使用 OM 光學顯微鏡搭配
影像擷取裝置進行試片金相組織的觀察與拍攝。
3.6 試片表面與元素分析
使用掃描式電子顯微鏡進行試片表面樣貌拍
攝及觀察銲縫大小，並應用 SEM 所附加的 EDS 儀
器分析試片表面和銲縫元素分佈情況，了解試片在
接合後表面金屬樣貌及銲縫附近材料元素的含量
百分比。

4.2 模擬結果
4.2.1壓合深度分析
不同下壓深度的分析比較，在此先行定義速
度 2.5m/s 為快速、速度 1m/s 為慢速，並分別探討
不同深度對於接合特性的影響。因步階數固定所以
快速壓合的深度較深，因此分子間受到強烈的擠
壓，所以在快速下壓的條件下原子受到的作用力比
慢速下壓還要大。由模擬得知快速下壓的接合強度
較佳(圖 15 為各種元素之比較)，圖 15(a)為銅在不
同壓合速度的比較，此動態模擬圖是為了詳細說明
快速下壓的過程由(I)為持壓步階結束(Ⅱ)為拉伸
步階 23000 步時發生最大應力(Ⅲ)隨著步階數的增
加應力也大幅的降低由(Ⅳ、Ⅴ)因不斷的拉伸造成
頸縮的現象越來越明顯直到(圖Ⅵ)發生斷裂。
由圖 15(b)可看出合金的 case 因不同的下壓
速度其接合強度有很大的差異約有 3 倍的差距，而
且在慢速與快速壓合不同的條件下，慢速壓合的應
力曲線看不出最大應力的峰值。最主要的原因是可
由(圖 13b (Ⅰ)、(Ⅱ))來解釋，(Ⅰ)因慢速下壓的過
程，讓原子與原子之間有足夠的時間藉由相互震盪
並產生擴散的反應，因為鋁原子的間距較銅原子
大，所以銅原子會擴散至鋁原子間的空隙，造成空
孔的材料缺陷，(Ⅱ)為快速壓合的條件沒有缺陷產
生，所以接合強度才會有如此差異。

4.2.3溫度效應分析
4.2.3.1未經過冷卻直接拉伸
本節討論溫度對接合強度的影響，模擬參數，
尺寸 3nm、壓印速度 1 m/s 進行模擬溫度分別設
定為 300K、400K、500K、600K、900K 五種溫
度模擬壓合之動作，並觀察在不同的溫度下拉伸
的強度變化。
由模擬結果顯示圖 18(a)、(b)，可以得知經由
溫度的升高，抗拉強度會跟著減弱。最主要的原因
是在高溫的狀態下，分子間有較高的動能，所以流
動性相對於低溫時來的好，抗拉強度隨之降低。圖
19 的趨勢也和本研究相同，溫度越高，最大應力
值較低。
因為鋁的延展性比銅還高，故鋁的應變值約
為 2 時，材料才會發生斷裂，反觀銅的應變值約
1.5 時材料便發生斷裂。鋁的熔點 933K(660℃)遠
低於銅 1356K(1083℃)，以熔點的觀點便可以解釋
為何鋁在 600K 時最大應力的峰值已不如銅明顯，
當溫度升至 900K 時鋁已接近熔點，晶格已不再是
FCC 排列，所以應力-應變圖的趨勢已不同於其他
的溫度。銅也是相同的情況，900K 時已接近玻璃
轉換溫度，此時的狀態已不再是固態的形式所以其
應力值和應變值與其他溫度有很大的差異。因此，
本研究對於之後的探討，採用 300K、600K、900K
三種溫度，因為這三種溫度代表了材料當時不同的
狀態。
4.2.3.2經過冷卻後進行拉伸
本節要討論冷卻至室溫之後對於強度的影
響。經過冷卻過程後可以看出抗拉強度有大幅度的
增加，藉由應變值的減少可以推斷出冷卻後的延展
性較差，如圖 20。
比較三種材料在不同溫度下的強度變化，由
圖 21 顯示鋁的強度為最差，在 900K 已接近鋁的
熔點，所以在持壓步階時材料已經不再呈完美晶格
排列，故強度大幅的降低，如圖 21(c)。合金材料

Abstract 
Direct extrusion by port hole – bridge die configuration has been successfully used to fabricate products with hollow 
cross sections for 6000 series aluminum alloys. When these aluminum alloys flow through the upper die (with bridge 
and port hole) material flows separately. These separate materials contact together when they flow through the 
lower die (with welding chamber). The contacting and welding processes occurs naturally if the material 
temperature and contact pressure are suitable; then the product with hollow and complicated cross section will be 
obtained when the material flow through bearing regions in lower die. This solid welding process for 6000 series 
aluminum alloys is without any problem. However, if for 7000 series aluminum alloys this situation alerts since 
different alloy compositions such as Zn and Cu causing welding process in lower die failed. It will impede the 
success of industry application with light and high strength aluminum alloys.  
In order to determine the solid welding conditions during hollow extrusion with port-hole die structure for high 
strength aluminum alloy such as 7000 series, an easy tooling configuration has been designed. Based on this 
approach, two split and half die components with taper angle feature were inserted into an outer steel ring. In the 
beginning, some clearances happen between inner die and outer ring result from design in purpose. When the upper 
punch continues to press the testing billet, the clearance disappears gradually due to the designed taper angle of 
inner die and outer ring. However, when the pushing pressure from upper punch is over 350 Mpa and billet 
temperature is maintained at about 480C below melting temperature, small gaps between the two split half die 
components occur automatically. During this situation, two small flashes can flow into the opening gaps both from 
the upper and lower billets which then can weld together. However, these two upper and lower billets in direct 
pressing zone did not weld together. Several experiments at different pressure have been conducted and the best 
solid welding condition has been obtained. The proposed method (die configuration) is easy and cheap because 
there is no necessary to conduct experiment in controlled environment such as in vacuum chamber of Gleeble test 
or in a protective atmosphere. The grain size and grain structure as well as grain flow have been discussed in the 
proposed paper for testing parts in direct pressing zone and in flash zone. Some SEM photos and EDS analysis have 
been prepared and will be presented in this paper.

INTRODUCTION 
In recent years, the demand for high performance and light 
materials is increasing. Aluminum alloy is commonly applied 
to aerospace, precision and defense industries, given its high 
specific strength, light weight and good corrosion resistance. 
2000, 6000, 7000 and 8000 series are of heat-treatable 
aluminum alloy, which can be strengthened through 
precipitation hardening [1]. 
7000 series Al-Zn-Mg alloy is a lately developed series 
among the aluminum alloys for forging. It has advantages of 
low gravity, high strength, good processing behavior, and good 
recovery and reusability. It can be found from early 
applications to national defense, military and space industries to 
current materials for structural members of automobile 
industry. This series of alloy can be divided into two types: (1) 
Al-Zn-Mg-Cu alloy which has the highest strength among all 
aluminum alloys, it is non-weldable and applied to materials for 
airplane structure, and its representative alloys are 7050 and 
7075; (2) Al-Zn-Mg alloy without copper which is weldable, 
and its representative alloys are 7005, 7017 and 7039. Although 
the addition of copper can strengthen the material, it would 
reduce the corrosion resistance after welding [2]. 
As aluminum alloy has many excellent properties, such as 
good heat and electrical conductivity and corrosion resistance, 
it is widely applied to many industries. With the global 
economic growth, the development of building, traffic, electric 
machinery and mechanical industries and the extension of new 
uses, the aluminum alloy industry in the world still maintains a

When Al 7075 specimen is heat treated at temperature 
above 450 , the grains have clear black lines, and the black ℃

forming a new plane direction. 
The internal structure of material is instable after high-
heat treatment; it must be improved by aging treatment. The 
grains have many patterns with sharp angles before the 
specimen is aged. After the aging treatment, the patterns in 
grains are reduced obviously, almost disappear, and the grain 
shape is relatively round. The atomic structure inside the 
material is rearranged under the effect of low temperature 
ageing, removing internal stress, to complete the structure, and 
the material strength will also increase.  
 
XRD analysis of Al7075  
 
The material is analyzed by GID XRD (scanning angle is 
30-90°, incidence is 0.3°, scanning speed is 2°/min). The result 
shows the main crystal diffraction peaks of Al 7075 are (111), 
(200), (220), (311) and (222), as shown in Figure 12. The 
diffraction angle of maximum strength (111), (200) and (220) is 
compared with JCPDS Card # 04-0787. As seen, the Al 7075 
specimen used in this experiment has face-centered cubic 
structure.

CONCLUSIONS 
This study used high-strength aluminum alloy as the 
material for temperature measurement and pressing 
experiments, and analyzed its properties as the reference for  
solid welding in hollow and seam extrusion process. The 
following conclusions are obtained.  
1. In the temperature calibration test, the temperature of Al 
7075 was measured, so as to find out the temperature 
changes of the heated material. The result showed that the 
upper and the lower workpieces of Al 7075 almost have the 
same heating rate. The experimental repeatability is high, so 
the experimental pressing process can be planned based on 
the graph of relation between the measured heating time and 
temperature change.  
2. According to the results of pressing experiment, when the 
pressure is below 300 MPa, the material has no bonding.
