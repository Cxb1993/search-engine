A Study on Bifurcation and Chaotic Motion of the Multi-Mesh Gear 
Train with Time-Varying Stiffness Using Rack Cutter 
 
摘要 
 
本計劃主要研究齒條刀具創成之時變嚙合剛性於多齒對嚙合系統之分歧和混沌行為，其中多齒對
嚙合系統由兩對正齒輪組連結三個傳動軸組成，為分析嚙合行為對齒輪動態傳輸誤差的影響，傳動軸
與軸承假設為剛性體且兩者間的連結行為為剛性連接並忽略齒牙嚙合的摩擦效應。同時利用齒條刀具
創成齒輪齒廓，經由座標轉換及嚙合原理求出齒廓方程式，並搭配 Cornell [20] 所提出正齒輪撓度方程
式推導隨時間變化的嚙合剛性。最後，應用拉格朗至法推導具非線性時變嚙合剛性之齒輪系統運動方
程式，並使用簡諧平衡法與朗吉-庫塔積分法分別分析非線性系統之穩態響應以及分歧現象與混沌行
為，探討不同齒深與壓力角對系統動態響應之影響。 
由數值結果顯示，時變嚙合剛性明顯影響系統之簡諧響應與混沌行為，隨著齒深的增加造成單齒
對之嚙合剛性減少而導致單齒對嚙合過程中之動態響應值增大。此外接觸率的提升造成雙齒對嚙合時
間較長而使得系統較平順。同理，接觸率的減少隨著壓力角的提升而改變，較大的壓力角造成單齒對
之嚙合剛性增加而降低齒對分離時之動態響應值。另一方面，阻尼比明顯影響系統響應與混沌行為。
隨著阻尼比的提升將導致系統響應降低並且減少混沌行為的發生。 
 
關鍵字：簡諧平衡法、簡諧響應、時變嚙合剛性、混沌 
 
 
ABSTRACT 
 
This project studies bifurcation and chaotic motion of the multi-mesh gear train with time-varying 
stiffness using rack cutter. The multi-mesh gear train is formed by three rigid shafts connected to each other 
by two spur gear pairs. In order to analyze the influences of meshing behavior and gear dynamic transmission 
error, the shafts and bearings are assumed to be rigid, the gears are connected to the shafts rigidly and the 
frictions on gear flank are negligible. In this project, the gear profile is generated by using rack cutter, and the 
mesh stiffness is calculated by applying the generating theory and the deflection equation of spur gear. 
Furthermore, the equations of motion with nonlinear time-varying mesh stiffness model are derived using 
Lagrangian approach, and the dynamic response and bifurcation phenomenon of system are analyzed by 
applying Harmonic Balance Method and the Runge-Kutta method respectively. The influence of tooth depth 
and pressure angle on dynamic response are investigated in the project. 
According to the numerical results, the harmonic response and chaotic motion are significantly affected 
by the mesh stiffness, the mesh stiffness of single tooth pair is reduced by increasing the tooth depth, causing 
the dynamic response is strengthened when the single tooth pair meshing. Furthermore, because the contact

簡介 
近代研究齒輪傳動的主要方向大多集中於疲勞破壞及噪音問題上，其中噪音問題與齒輪的傳動效
率息息相關。本計劃提出一多齒對嚙合系統，以預測齒深、壓力角與嚙合阻尼等參數對系統動態傳輸
誤差與分歧行為的影響。有關齒輪嚙合的結構模型與分析之研究內容可概括分為兩方面討論，一是齒
輪系統模型，二是嚙合剛性模型。 
在齒輪系統模型方面的研究，Ozquven 和 Houser [1] 收集回顧1920~1980間近兩百篇的文獻，整
理齒輪嚙合系統之線性振動特性，並將線性模型歸類成簡單動態因子模型(simple dynamic fector)、齒部
撓度變形模型(tooth compliamce)、齒輪動態模型(gear dynamics)、齒輪轉子動態模型(geared rotor 
dynamics)及扭矩振動模型(torsional vibrations)等五大類別。Comparin 和 Singh [2] 使用簡諧平衡法分
析單自由度系統之頻譜響應，並比較兩種不同形式的外力對頻譜響應之影響。Cheo 和Lou [3] 研究一
具分段線性回復力之單自由度系統的外力振動，其中系統的穩態響應與回復力均用傅立葉級數近似，
並使用Hill’s variational equation判斷系統穩態解之穩定性。結果發現，減少系統阻尼比將出現高階的次
簡諧響應，且增加系統的外激振幅將導致高階的次簡諧響應發生混沌行為。Parker 等人 [5] 分析一單
自由度的齒輪模型並利用有限元素法模擬齒牙的變形，最後探討不同扭矩對系統的動態傳輸誤差之影
響，並與實驗結果相互比較。Blankenship 和 Kaharman [6] 研究一具齒背隙之單自由度非線性系統的
動態行為，並利用傅立葉級數模擬嚙合剛性與外力負載，探討不同剛性與外力負載對系統頻率響應的
影響。由數值結果顯示，某些區間中與實驗數據有些許誤差，乃是因為系統忽略傳輸誤差所造成之影
響。Vinayak 等人 [7] 亦將簡諧平衡法( HBM )使用於一多齒對嚙合系統的穩態響應解析上。
Raghothama 和 Narayanan [8] 使用增量簡諧平衡法(IHBM)研究一非線性齒輪轉子軸承系統的週期運
動，其中嚙合剛性的部分使用餘弦函數近似，並探討刺激頻率對系統運動週期變化的影響。Shen 等人 
[9] 亦應用增量簡諧平衡法( IHBM )分析一對正齒輪組的非線性動態行為，其中系統的嚙合剛性與靜態
傳輸誤差均用餘弦函數展開，並探討齒背隙( backlash )、時變嚙合剛性和靜態傳輸誤差等參數對系統動
態行為的影響。Kaharman 和Blankenship [10] 研究一考慮齒間隙( Clearance )的齒輪系統之穩態響應。
而為了估算最佳的齒輪嚙合率，Kahraman 與 Blankenship [10] 使用實驗數據探討扭矩、嚙合率與嚙合
頻率間相互關聯性，其中嚙合率可透過改變齒深的方式做調整，最後利用週期方塊波函數探討嚙合剛
性與嚙合率之關係。Liou 等人 [12] 探討了齒輪接觸比與動態嚙合力的關係，其中接觸比亦使用改變
齒深的方式來作控制。Tamminana 等人 [13] 發展一有限元素法模型和一簡化離散模型用以預測一對
正齒輪組的動態行為並與實驗結果作比較，探討系統之動態傳輸誤差與動態因子對頻率響應的影響。
Kaharman [14] 研究一螺旋正齒輪的多嚙合系統的動態特性，其中軸承的模擬為可變形體。Al-shyyab 
和 Kaharman [15] 研究一非線性時變之多齒對嚙合模型的次簡諧響應和混沌現象，其中系統模型為兩
齒對嚙合固定於三條剛性軸上，並使用傅立葉級數模擬嚙合剛性。最後應用簡諧平衡法分析系統的次
簡諧響應，並與數值積分法獲得之分歧與混沌行為作比較。同年，Al-shyyab 和 Kaharman [16] 亦使
用簡諧平衡法( HBM )研究相同模型之動態行為，探討不同剛性、外力與阻尼比對嚙合頻率的影響並分
析系統之單週期運動，最後再應用 Floquet 理論判斷系統穩態響應的穩定性。由數值結果顯示，外力
大小並不會改變穩態解之穩定性。Kaharman 等人 [17] 發展一套齒輪轉子系統的有限元素模型，並計
算該系統的臨界速度和偏心軸上任意點的響應。最後，Kaharman 和 Singh [18] 提出了一考慮齒背隙
( backlash )和時變嚙合剛性的齒輪轉子軸承模型並研究該模型的頻率響應。 
第二部份，在嚙合剛性模型方面的研究，Cornell 和 Westervelt [19] 提出一直線嚙合剛性模型，

WITH TIME-VARYING MESH STIFFNESS APPLICATION OF RACK CUTTER 
 
T. N. Shiau *,          J. R. Chang**,       K. H. Huang+,        C. J. Cheng * 
              Professor         Associate Professor      Manager             Graduate

ABSTRACT 
This paper studies the bifurcation and chaotic motion of a 
multi-mesh gear train with time-varying mesh stiffness 
application of rack cutter. The spur gear tooth profile using 
rack cutter is proposed, and the mesh stiffness at the position 
on line of action is calculated. The time-varying mesh stiffness 
with various parameters are discussed and compared with the 
experimental results of the literature. The equations of motion 
a multi-mesh gear train with the nonlinear time-varying mesh 
stiffness model are derived using Lagrange approach. The 
dynamic response of the system is analyzed by applying the 
harmonic balance method (HBM). Furthermore, Runge-Kutta 
method is employed to dig out the bifurcation phenomenon 
and chaotic behavior of system. According to the numerical 
results, the harmonic response and chaotic motion are 
significantly affected by the mesh stiffness with pressure 
angle. And the damping ratio can also significantly influence 
the system bifurcation phenomenon.

I.  NTRODUCTION 
In the geared system model group, Choi and Lou [1] 
investigated the forced vibration of a single-degree-of-freedom 
system with unsymmetric, piecewise nonlinear restoring force. 
Blankenship and Kahraman [2] used a multiple-term harmonic 
balance method (HBM) approach and developed a generalized 
analytical method for steady state solution. Raghothama and 
Narayanan [3] used the incremental harmonic balance method 
(IHBM) to obtain the periodic motion of a geared rotor system 
with a 3-DOF nonlinear model. Shen et al. [4] also used IHBM 
to analyze the nonlinear dynamic of a spur gear pair, where 
backlash, time-varying stiffness and static transmission error 
are all included. Tamminana et al. [5] developed a finite-
element-based deformable-body model and a simplified 
discrete model to predict dynamic behavior of spur gear pairs. 
Al-shyyab and Kaharman [6] used the harmonic balance 
method to obtain frequency spectrum on period-one motions at 
the same model. 
In the model of mesh stiffness, Cornell and Westervelt  
 
[7] proposed the rotational motion of geared system 
converted into the equivalent rectilinear motion, and the mesh 
stiffness is exhibited by using curve fitting. Cornell [8] 
described the formulation of compliance of spur gear; the 
compliance and stress sensitivity of spur gear teeth is 
investigated based on the simulate analysis and experiment. 
Ozguven [9] developed a 6-dof nonlinear model for the 
dynamic analysis of spur gears. In the computation of mesh 
stiffness on each mesh point, shear deformations, bending base 
rotation and Hertzain deflections are taken into consideration. 
Yoon and Rao [10] presented the static transmission error 
using cubic splines for gear tooth profile. Kahraman and 
Blankeship [11] studied the influence of involute contact ratio 
(ICR) on the tensional vibration behavior of a spur gear pair 
base on experiment by measuring the dynamic transmission 
error, and using periodic rectangular wave function to explain 
the relation between ICR and mesh stiffness. Wang etc. [12] 
formulated a generalized nonlinear time-varying (NLTV) 
dynamic model of a hypoid gear pair with backlash 
nonlinearity. The fundamental harmonic form of time-varying 
mesh parameters is used to study the effects of mesh parameter 
variations on the dynamic response, and the interactions 
between them and backlash nonlinearity. Walha etc. [13] 
investigated the dynamics of a two-stage gear system 
involving backlash and time dependent mesh stiffness. It 
mainly develops a 12 degree of freedom gear dynamic model. 
The nonlinear dynamic response of the system is solved using 
Newmark iterative algorithm. 
The researchers usually use contact value, multi-term 
Fourier series and periodic rectangular wave to approximate 
mesh stiffness, but it was not real meshing condition by 
involute tooth profile. Though the past researches have 
predicted the gear mesh stiffness, the generation of spur gear 
tooth profile using rack cutter is first proposed. The real 
involute tooth profile is developed in the proposed method. 
The nonlinear dynamic behavior of the associated gear train 
system is also investigated based on the predicted gear mesh 
stiffness.

Ⅳ.  NUMERICAL RESULTS AND DISCUSSIONS 
To verify the proposed procedure using rack cutter to 
obtain the time-varying mesh stiffness, a single degree-of-
freedom gear model is studied first. The numerical results by 
using the proposed procedure are compared with those of 
Blankenship and Kahraman [2] by using a multiple term 
Fourier series approach. Table 1 shows comparison of time-
varying mesh stiffness by using rack cutter procedure and 
Fourier series approach [2]. Using the parameter values of 
reference [2], the gears are of a unity ratio design having a 
module of 3 mm, 20 deg pressure angle, tooth number of 50, 
and backlash of 15 mm. The tooth width and tooth depth are 
unknown parameters in the cited reference. In Table 1, the 
mesh stiffness is calculated with three kinds of tooth depth 
with 20 mm tooth width. When the torque 300 Nm is applied 
at the system, the root mean square value of displacement u  
is calculated to compare with the results of reference [2]. 
Figure 7 illustrates the comparisons of the HBM solutions 
using three kinds of depth with experimental results. It is 
shown that the results by using the proposed procedure trend 
to those of reference [2]. Especially, the high depth gear model 
gets the better results. 
The contact ratio influences on dynamic behavior of the 
system with different pressure angle of the second gear pair 
are discussed. The pressure angle for the first gear pair is given 
as 20 . The system dynamic behavior is analyzed by 
changing the pressure angle of the second gear pair. The 
designed parameters with different pressure angle are given in 
Table 2. The contact ratio is 2.01, 1.82, 1.69 and 1.58 with 
four types of pressure angle. The single tooth pair meshing 
stiffness with different pressure angle is exhibited in Table 2, 
where the polynomial )(xq  is equation for the fitting curve 
of the mesh stiffness. The cross axis represents the contact 
ratio, where s is the contact length, and et  is the base pitch. 
The vertical axis represents the single tooth pair mesh stiffness 
at different contact positions on the line of action. The 
stiffness of multi-mesh tooth pairs is also shown in Fig. 8. It is 
seen that the case for the pressure angle of 5.14  has the 
largest mesh stiffness at three tooth pairs meshing. This 
implies that the small pressure angle increase the contact ratio, 
thus the summation of the individual mesh stiffness for one 
tooth pair brings out the large total mesh stiffness. For single 
tooth mesh stiffness, the case for the pressure angle of 5.22  
has the largest value.

   第二天七月二十九日(星期四)早上最主要的論文重點有1.A New Method to Determine 
the Weights of Multiple Objectives in Combinational Optimization Problems. 2. A Direct 
Mathematical Method to Calculate the Efficiency of Bore Hole Cylindrical Detectors. 3. Multi 
Sensor Data Fusion using Kalman Filter Techniques. 其中第一篇為多目標之最佳化問題，
主要提出一個新的模式。第二篇主要利用卡曼濾波原理來處理多感測器之數據問題。下
午論文發表重點有1. Comparison of Proportional Control and Fuzzy Logic Control to Develop 
an Ideal Thermoelectic Renal Hypothemia System. 2. AI Applications to Metal Stamping Die 
Design – A Review. 3. Technology-Supported Brain-based Learning Through Inquiring: A 
Model. 主要在不同技術之應用包括熱電系統控制之比較; 模具設計, 學習模式等。 
 
   第三天七月三十日(星期五)早上由日本Iwate Prefectural University, Prof. Bhed Bahadur 
Bista主持，其中發表之論文包括有1.Energy Efficient Reliable Cooperation Multipath 
Routing in Wireless Sensor Networks. 2. Image Transmission via Interactive Cellular-Turbo 
System. 3. To Study the Parametric Effects on Optimality of Various Feeding Sequences of a 
Multi-effect Evaporators in Paper Industry using Mathematical Modeling and Simulation with 
MATLAB. 整體論文研究重點著重在參數資訊分析及應用。下午論文主要有1. Fuzzy 
Expert System Design for Determining Wearing Properties of Nitrided and Non-Nitrided Steal. 
2. Comparison of MFCC and Cepstral Coefficients as a Feature Set for PCG Biometric 
Systems. 3. A New Biologically Inspired Pattern Recognition Approval for Face Recognition. 
論文主要著重在Bio Technology之應用。 包括臉型確認，特性確認，另有針對模糊專家
系統之設計應用於鋼材磨損研究。
