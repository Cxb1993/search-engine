ABSTRACT 
The purpose of this paper is to estimat  optimum reservoir drainage for keeping 
reservoir safe during typhoon peri d.  For dam safe, voiding flood and maintaining water 
resource, it is very important to find out optimumreservoir operati n.   
The invading typhoon data in 1996 to 2002 is c llected; Shihmen reservoir, its 
watershed and downstream Hsinhai Brdge a e taken as research area.  A 
?rainfall-runoff? model was develope  to estimate Sh hmen reservoir inflows for 
four-steps ahead prediction of hourly rainfall by using Artificial Neural Network?ANN?.  
Next, the water stage increments of the river due to eservoir drainage are an lyzed.  
Finally, Fuzzy Decision Analysis is used to determ n  the optimum drainage of r servoir 
during flood period.  It is ecommended that this paper can be used for online reservoir 
operation in the future. 
Keywords: Artificial Neural Network (ANN), Fuzzy Decision, optimum reservoir 
operation
