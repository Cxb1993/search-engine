摘要—本研究接續前次國科會陰極微孔層疏水處理之計劃,此次我們將陰極微孔層條件固定, 改
而探討親水性陽極(hydrophilic anode)對於質子交換膜燃料電池(PEMFC)性能的影響，我們分別
在陽極觸媒層、微孔層以及氣體擴散層作親水化處理，在不同進料相對溼度下進行研究。實驗
結果顯示，在進料無增濕情況下，親水性陽極增加觸媒的潤濕性，性能表現比一般未處理之陽
極膜電極組來的好。 
關鍵字：質子交換膜燃料電池,親水性陽極,二氧化矽粉末,乾燥 
 
Abstract—This research is a continued study of the previous project which dealt with the hydrophobic 
treatment of cathode micro-porous layer (MPL). This time, we focus on the hydrophilic treatment on 
the PEMFC anode under a specified cathode MPL condition. Hydrophilic materials were applied to the 
anode catalyst layer, micro-porous layer, and gas diffusion layer. This influence is evaluated under 
various inlet humidifier temperatures. The results show that better performance is obtained when 
hydrophilic treatment is applied to the anode catalyst layer as compared to that without hydrophilic 
treatment especially when the non-humidified fuel is fed in the flow channel. 
Keywords: proton exchange membrane fuel cell, hydrophilic treated anode, SiO2 particle, dry. 
 
一、前言 
能源是國家發展以及民生經濟的重要支
柱。隨著全球經濟不斷的成長，對能源的依賴
程度也越來越大。近年來石油的庫存量下降，
油價走勢屢創歷史新高，使得經濟發展受到不
小的衝擊；而石油在使用過程中會產生出大量
的溫室氣體，造成地球暖化，影響到地球生態。
為了不讓有限的石化能源被加速耗盡並避免地
球環境遭受嚴重破壞，各國相繼投入開發新綠
色能源，而質子交換膜燃料電池就是其中之
一。在質子交換膜燃料電池中，陽極進料氣體
通常都會進行增濕處理，若是進料溼度過低則
性能會受到影響。在本研究我們使用二氧化矽
粉末作為親水材料，分別摻入陽極觸媒層與微
孔層裡，並且對氣體擴散層添加 Nafion 離子
體，形成複合式親水性陽極，比較在不同陰、
陽進料增濕溫度下，陽極親水程度對於電池性
能的影響。

An Algebraic Model of Liquid Feed Direct Methanol Fuel Cell with Co-Current 
Channel Flow 
K.-M. Yin*, C.-Y. Yang 
Department of Chemical Engineering and Materials Science, Yuan Ze University, 
Neili, Taoyuan, Taiwan 32003, R.O.C.  
 
*(O) 886-3-4638800 ext. 2556 
(fax) 886-3-4559373 
email: cekenyin@saturn.yzu.edu.tw 
 
Key words: Direct Methanol Fuel Cell, Algebraic Model, Mixed Potential Theory, Crossover, Flow 
Channel 
Algebraic equations of a liquid feed direct methanol fuel cell (DMFC) with co-current 
channel flow are derived to correlate the literature experimental discharge data with the 
theoretically predicted polarization curve. Mass conservation of methanol and oxygen normal to 
the membrane electrode assembly is derived, and an averaging procedure over the channel length 
is carried out to give the overall cell performance. The influence of methanol crossover through 
the membrane is described as the result of diffusion, electro-osmotic drag, and the pressure 
difference induced permeation between anode and cathode. The influence of permeated methanol 
on the cathode electrode potential is demonstrated by the mixed potential theory. That is, the 
parasitic methanol oxidation reaction consumes part of the oxygen reduction current in the 
cathode catalyst layer, forming an internal short circuit, so that the resultant cell current density 
is less than the situation without crossover. This model is capable of predicting the concentration 
depletions of methanol and oxygen within the porous diffusion media of the membrane electrode 
assembly, and that along the respective flow channels. Nonlinear least square scheme, coupled 
with the algebraic model, retrieves the parameters of methanol diffusivity in the anode backing 
layer, exchange current density of methanol on catalyst surface, and proton conductivity in the 
polymer electrolyte membrane.

Conclusions 
An algebraic formulation of the liquid 
feed direct methanol fuel cell under 
co-current parallel flows is presented, which 
accounts the respective anode and cathode 
electrochemical reactions, the associated 
mass transport through the porous media, 
and the mixed-potential effect on cathode 
induced by methanol crossover. This model 
is based on the mass conservation in the 
normal direction of MEA through an 
averaging procedure along the channel 
length. The calculation procedure is simple 
and efficient, with the incorporation of 
parameter estimation scheme, to retrieve 
important MEA kinetic properties. Although 
algebraic approach is intentional, this work 
is virtually a pseudo-two-dimensional 
model.

薄膜，降低電解質的離子交換容量(ion-exchange capacity)。他們也研究含 NH3 污染物之不純
進料的影響。Chen and Fuller 延續他們以往的工作，探討陰極氧氣穿透薄膜至陽極反應產生
H2O2 的破壞效應，在長期的操作下，持續產生的 H2O2 會造成 Nafion 電解質的劣化以及觸媒
層/薄膜間的層間剝離，減少 MEA 的壽命。目前老化研究方面，由於相關現象與機制漸趨明
朗，在定量模擬方面的文獻也漸增加，Fuller 或 Van Zee 的研究群均是實驗與模擬同時並行，
也各具特色。 
    有關觸媒的研究，這次並無太多新觸媒的研發報告，而多在增進分散性方面。本人特別
注意到一些以電鍍法製備低負載觸媒的發展。Buratto 等人採脈衝電鍍法(pulse plating)，以
H2PtCl6 為鍍液在碳紙上得到低於 10μg/cm2 的 Pt loading。 Martin 等人以電化學還原法在
Carbon black 上得到 Co、Pt、PtCo 觸媒，他們發現採用高電位，低脈衝時間(10～50 ms)可得
到均勻分佈的成核晶種。這些研究顯示以脈衝電鍍法降低觸媒負載量，增進分散性，降低成
本等各方面都有潛力。要能夠成功的關鍵在於必須得到均勻分佈的 Pt particle(3～4 nm)，而且
還要能深入碳黑粉體或碳紙層的內部。這就牽涉到有效質傳的問題，這將是一個很有意義的
研究方向。
