    
 
 
 
Abstract: 
 
In this project, the characteristic properties and processing technology of thermoplastic 
polyimdes was studied, the whole product technology of thermoplastic polyimde parts including 
design, manufacturing process and property evaluation was investigated to enhance the 
thermoplastic polyimde product precision, and the training of the researchers and engineers of 
the cooperated company was carried out. The manufacturing technology of thermoplastic 
polyimde parts was investigated based on the evaluations of the characteristics of various 
thermoplastic polyimde grades and product requirements. The point was to decrease the 
manufacturing cost, to increase the precision and performance of the products, and to set up the 
application technology of thermoplastic polyimde parts. Furthermore, this project assisted the 
cooperated company to train the researchers and engineers, and provides consulting service 
about technologies in order to enhance the application technology of high temperature resistant 
engineering plastics. 
 
Keywords: Thermoplastic polyimides, Processing technology, Product application technology, 
Personnel training, Product precision.
