 
整合正逆向物流配送網路設計 
本研究整合正逆向物流配送，應用禁忌搜尋法（Tabu Search），設計配送網路，以作業成
本中車輛固定成本、車輛變動成本與物流中心回收產品的存貨成本，建構總成本目標函數，
求解過程分為兩個階段，第一階段應用節省法快速建構初始解，第二階段運用途程改善與回
收需求指派改善模組，進行初始解改善，以求出近似最佳解。 
為了驗證模式正確性與效率性，本研究以 Solomon(1987)國際例題為測試樣本，進行模擬
實驗，測試結果發現，與最佳化軟體 Lingo 8.0 獲得一致的結果，在模式的效率方面，並與
Toth(1999)研究進行比較，在 30個題組中有 25個題組，均明顯較佳，最後，並提出一些建議
與後續研究。 
關鍵詞：車輛途程問題、逆物流、禁忌搜尋法 
 
A Distribution Model with Integrated Forward and Reverse 
Logistics 
Abstract 
The logistic is one of important sources in creating value for business. Traditional logistic 
process is a one-way (forward) value-added activity. However, in the perspective of the whole cycle 
of the supply chain, reverse logistic have to be considered as well. This study proposes a distribution 
model with integrated forward and reverse logistic. The objective function includes three parts, 
namely vehicle fixed cost, vehicle variable cost and inventory cost. Application of the tabu-search to 
get nearly optimal routes for vehicles. The process of tabu-search includes two stages, firstly to get 
the initial solution then to improve it with inter-route and intra-route stage.To validate the proposed 
model, there are two simulation experiments to be conducted. First, this study used the examples of 
Solomon(1987) as data to conduct simulation experiments. The results shown that in case of small 
size problem, there is no difference with Lingo 8.0 solution. The second experiment aim to compare 
with Toth’s(1999) research. The results shown that are more efficient in most cases. Finally, this 
study propose some suggests for practices and further research. 
Keywords: Vehicle route problem, Reverse logistic, Tabu-search
