The main purpose of this project is to develop a monitoring mechanism 
of deterioration or churn in customer consuming behavior. The theories 
of our mechanism are Bayesian mixture model and cumulative sum 
charts. Recency is one of the key factors in the customer churn, but 
most research are neglect its influence. We will include the effect of 
recency in our study. However, to define the length of recency if a 
customer consuming behavior is deteriorating needs to solve. In our 
study, we use Bayesian mixture model to segment individual 
consuming behaviors into two groups, active one and inactive one. We 
define the inactive one to be the population of deterioration. If the 
recency of a customer falls in the deteriorating population under the 
statistical evidence, the mechanism will show this signal by cumulative 
sum chart. Cumulative sum chart can exhibit the statistical evidence by 
control chart which is visual and easily reading. Therefore, practical 
workers even without the training of statistics can use and operate it 
easily and correctively.

The main purpose of this project is to develop a monitoring mechanism 
of deterioration or churn in customer consuming behavior. The theories 
of our mechanism are Bayesian mixture model and cumulative sum 
charts. Recency is one of the key factors in the customer churn, but 
most research are neglect its influence. We will include the effect of 
recency in our study. However, to define the length of recency if a 
customer consuming behavior is deteriorating needs to solve. In our 
study, we use Bayesian mixture model to segment individual 
consuming behaviors into two groups, active one and inactive one. We
