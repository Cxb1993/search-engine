中文摘要 
關鍵詞：人工溼地、溫室氣體、甲烷、氧化亞氮、二氧化碳、廢水處理 
 
自工業革命後大氣中溫室氣體(greenhouse gases , GHGs)濃度顯著增加，使得大氣溫度上
升，對全球氣候、地形特徵和生物生存條件及空間皆造成了顯著影響。天然濕地及沼澤已被確
認是溫室氣體 CH4 及 N2O 的產生源。以 CH4 而論，文獻估計全球濕地每年約釋放 115~145 
Tg-CH4/year (P = 1×1015 g；T = 1×1012 g)至大氣中，約佔全球甲烷釋放總量的 20~25%。人工濕
地為模仿天然濕地的條件而建置，為一種廢水處理的生態工法。由於進流廢水，使人工濕地具
更高的潛能產生溫室氣體。然而，有關人工濕地溫室氣體釋放的相關報導仍相當有限。 
本年度計畫為執行三年計畫的第三年，以嘉南藥理科技大學校園人工濕地為研究對象，
使用密閉罩法測量濕地不同位置點因異營性呼吸作用產生的 CO2、厭氧分解產生 CH4 的釋放通
量及 N2O 通量，並同時監測濕地的廢水處理操作條件及水質淨化結果。N2O 與 CH4 自 2007 年
12 月起每個月採樣分析一次，CO2 則是從 2008 年 11 月起開始分析，亦為每月採樣分析一次。
人工濕地由兩種不同類型濕地：表面下流動(SSF)濕地(1450 m2)及表面流(FWS)濕地(2200 m2)
串連組成，進流經二級處理後的校園污水，研究期間平均進流流量為 316 m3/d，平均水力負荷
0.087 m/d，平均水力停留時間 3.14 d。本年度的主要研究目的包括：(1)探討人工溼地 CO2、CH4、
與 N2O 通量的時間變化，包括：季節、日夜間變化與月份變化；(2)探討人工溼地 CO2、CH4、
與 N2O 通量的空間變化；(3)探討有無水生植物存在對 CO2 與 CH4、N2O 釋放通量的影響；(4)
不同文獻報導人工濕地 CO2、CH4、N2O 釋放通量的綜合比較。 
本研究以靜置箱採氣及氣體層析法監測濕地的 CO2 與 CH4、N2O 釋放通量。結果顯示，
SSF 濕地的 CO2 釋放通量介於-182.5~777.18 mg/m2/h，FWS 濕地介於 12.8~402.9 mg/m2/h。SSF
濕地、FWS 濕地及整個 SSF-FWS 濕地的整年平 CO2 釋放通量分別為 157.77 ±152.08、
147.55±103.07 及 153.39±132.98 mg CO2/m2/hr。SSF 濕地的 CH4 釋放通量範圍介於-1.09~55.36 
mg CH4/m2/h，FWS 濕地介於-4.17~28.86 mg/m2/h。由於 CH4 通量的日夜間變化及有無植物存
在的監測值差異頗大，因此透過 α, β, γ 校正值估算出實際的平均通量，SSF 濕地、FWS 濕
地及整個 SSF-FWS 濕地的整年平均 CH4 釋放通量分別為 111.14、79.70 及 92.19 g C/m2/year。
FWS 溼地的所測得的 N2O 釋放通量介於 3.83～87.37 μg N2O/m2/h，FWS 溼地介於－6.10～
128.78 μg N2O/m2/h。 
三種氣體之通量的月變化觀察結果顯示，SSF 及 FWS 單元最高的 N2O 通量都發生在 2008
年 3 月與 10 月，最高的 CH4 通量發生在 2008 年 9 月中，最高的 CO2 通量發生在 2009 年 6 月；
而 SSF 及 FWS 單元最低的 N2O 及 CH4 通量則都發生在 2008 年 2 月，CO2 通量則發生在 2009
年 1 月。若以季節進行平均統計，發現，冬季整個溼地的 N2O 與 CH4 平均通量明顯低於春、
夏或秋季的平均值(p<0.05)，而春、夏及秋季之間的通量並無明顯差異(p>0.05)。CO2 氣體通量
在 2008 末至 2009 初的冬季有明顯較低的 CO2平均釋放通量。 
在空間變化方面，SSF 濕地的 N2O 平均釋放通量沿著人工濕地的流動距離而顯著下降
(p<0.05)，然而此空間變化在 FWS 濕地並不明顯。SSF 濕地進流端的 CH4 釋放通量明顯高於出
流端(p<0.05)，但是其餘各採樣點的 CH4 通量差異並不顯著。SSF 濕地的 CO2 釋放通量變化趨
勢並不一致，而 FWS 濕地反而發現 CO2 通量隨著水流距離而逐漸增加的趨勢，出流端的通量
明顯高於進流端(p<0.01)。 
有水生植物存在的溼地區域，其 N2O 與 CH4通量平均值均高於無水生植物存在的溼地區
域，其中以在 SSF 單元所測的 N2O 通量的差異性最顯著(p<0.05)。相反的，有水生植物存在的
溼地區域其 CO2 通量平均值均明顯低於(p<0.05)無水生植物存在的區域。本研究所調查的
N2O、CH4 及 CO2 通量值落於文獻中有關人工溼地處理廢水所報導通量的範圍。

第一章  前言 
自工業革命後大氣中溫室氣體(greenhouse gases , GHGs)濃度顯著增加，使得
大氣溫度上升，對全球氣候、地形特徵和生物生存條件及空間皆造成了顯著影
響。而陸域生態系統(例如：濕地)在全球暖化與氣候變遷中扮演何種角色，及全
球暖化與氣候變遷對生態系統會造成何種影響，排放這些氣體的可能來源、排放
速率與排放量、溫室氣體的轉換宿命、溫室氣體的減量技術及策略等。，是近年
來生態學家、氣象學家、及環境科學家們眾所關切的課題。 
人工溼地為一種低成本、省能源、容易操作、且兼具生態保育及景觀價值的
廢水處理程序，然而在水質淨化的生化程序中，無可避免的會產生氧化亞氮(N2O)
及甲烷(CH4)、二氧化碳(CO2)等溫室效應氣體。人工溼地在國內外應用的數目及
面積規模均有逐年成長的趨勢，因此溫室氣體排放造成衝擊的相關研究有其迫切
需要。溼地中的甲烷生成機制主要是將有機物進行水解酸化，甲烷菌再將中間產
物(H2、CO2、乙酸等)轉換產生 CH4 與 CO2。N2O 是細菌脫硝作用的中間產物、
或者是細菌硝化作用的副產物。其中自然界二氧化碳之主要來源為土壤微生物及
其他生物進行土壤呼吸（Armentano 和 Menges，1986）陸地上高等植物和水中
生物可經光合作用將二氧化碳轉變成有機碳，供異營性動物和不含葉綠素微生物
之有機營養來源。在好氣條件下，微生物可以分解簡單有機物、生物聚合物，在
嫌氣條件下，可以發酵及分解有機物。呼吸代謝物利用有機質產生能量，放出二
氧化碳。 
國內對於此類研究並不多，因此本研究選擇嘉南藥理科技大學校園人工濕
地為調查對象，除了監測濕地不同位置N2O及CH4、CO2的釋放通量外，也同時紀
錄廢水處理的操作條件及分析水質淨化結果。N2O及CH4研究期間是從2007年12
月至2010年6月期間，CO2研究期間則是從2008年11月~2010年6月期間。約每個月
進行一次的監測及分析。人工濕地系統由一個表面下流動式(SSF;1450m2)溼地連

3.2 人工溼地溫室氣體釋放通量之文獻報導 
在歐美國家人工溼地已發展有二、三十年，但有關於人工溼地處理廢水時的
溫室氣體排放之研究與發展仍然是剛起步的階段(Johansson et al., 2004)。Tanner et 
al. (1997)，在紐西蘭一座規模試驗人工溼地處理乳酪農場廢水進行測量甲烷氣體
的釋放通量，是最早期的報導。嚴格來說，在國內外有關於人工溼地產生溫室氣
體釋放通量之相關資料尚缺乏，整理如下。 
3.2.1人工溼地處理農業廢水之甲烷釋放研究 
Tanner et al. (1997)利用紐西蘭一座試驗規模人工溼地處理乳酪農場廢水，選
擇四個採樣點進行甲烷氣體產生的監測，觀察到甲烷釋放通量介於－2.08 ~ 59.38 
mg/m–2/h–1。人工溼地進流口的甲烷釋放通量顯著高於出流口(P < 0.05)，無種植
植物的釋放通量高於有種植植物得釋放通量，且最高的釋放通量也出現於無種植
植物區，這說明了植物根部的氧化作用扮演著抑制甲烷菌或使氧化甲烷的功能增
強。 
3.2.2人工溼地處理城市廢水之溫室氣體釋放研究 
Tai et al. (2002)在位於中國東部一座處理城市廢水之人工溼地中進行溫室氣
體釋放通量監測，測量其間為 1999 年 6 月~2000 年 8 月，濕地面積為 495,000 m2，
平均進流為 12,000 m3 / d 的都市廢水。人工溼地中甲烷產生的主要來源為城市廢
水，甲烷的平均釋放通量高達 5.22 g m-2 d-1，高於在同緯度天然溼地產生量的 250
倍之多。處理 1L 的廢水約會產生 227.8mg 的甲烷，高於二級處理廠處理污水時
所產生量的 700 ~ 1000 倍之多。

3.2.5季節性與溫度性的改變對人工溼地溫室氣體釋放之影響研究 
Liikanen et al. (2006)利用位於芬蘭境內的 FWS 人工溼地(2.4 ha)處理泥炭礦
脈採礦的進流水，長期調查溫室氣體排放進行研究。研究期長達 10 年的連續監
測中發現，在 1992 年甲烷的釋放通量為 140 mg m−2 d−1，到了 2002 年其釋放通
量增加到 400 mg m−2 d−1，增加了 185.7％。在 2001 年 8 月到 2002 年 8 月，其甲
烷釋放通量範圍在 0.4 ~ 4320 mg m−2 d−1 (0.02 ~ 180 mg m−2 h−1)，平均釋放通量為
380 ± 46 mg m−2 d−1 (15.83 mg m−2 h−1)。其甲烷釋放通量在秋天最高，冬天最低。
甲烷釋放通量與濃度在距離進流 30m 與 60m 處顯著高於 90m 處(p＜0.001)。
Liikanen et al.，發現初級生產量與植物種類組成的改變可能導致增加人工溼地的
碳循環與甲烷釋放的機制，1992~2002 這 10 年期間，植物生物量在人工溼地增加
了一倍。初級生產，提供了新鮮有機物的降解，是溼地控制甲烷釋放量其中一個
最重要的變數。微管植物也可以作為甲烷的導管，甲烷利用它們的通氣組織從厭
氧泥炭裡直接排氣到大氣中。因此，增加維管植物的範圍明顯是增加甲烷釋放通
量的原因。

4.3.1 採樣法 
溫室氣體釋放通量的量測採用文獻慣用的靜置箱採氣，配合氣體層析分析法
進行(Johansson et al., 1994; Liikanen et al., 2006)。乃以壓克力採樣罩(長 24cm、寬
24 cm、高 50 cm)，配合不銹鋼底座(長 25cm、寬 25 cm、高 20cm(或 40 cm))，組
合使用。採樣進行時先將採樣罩分別罩於不銹鋼底座之上，再以水封住凹朝接縫
處。確定水平後，啟動罩中風扇，靜待十分鐘，讓罩內外空氣平衡再以矽膠塞塞
住壓克力罩開口後開始計時。分別自第 0 分鐘以及第 30 分鐘時以拋棄式注射
針筒抽取罩內氣體樣品 100 毫升，注入事先抽成真空體積為 0.5 升之採氣袋
內。再攜回研究室以氣相色層分析儀分析氣體樣品中溫室氣體的含量，測定溫室
氣體在單位時間，單位面積上所釋放的量(mg m−2 hr-1)。在每月中旬進行採樣一
次，並選擇採樣日前 3～5 天無下雨的情況下進行採樣，以減少採樣的誤差。每
一採樣點均進行二重複試驗(即設置兩個採樣罩)。並固定在採樣日的上午 10~12
點之間進行採樣，氣體樣品在 12 小時內完成分析工作。每次採樣溫室氣體時，
同時測定溼地採樣點的氣溫、水溫、底泥溫度，光照及水質。

技術/ 創作名稱 人工濕地中溫室氣體釋放量的推估系統及削減溫室氣體排放的技術策略 
發明人/ 創作人 林瑩峰 
中文：（100~500 字） 
本研究三年計畫所獲得的成果預期可能開發出一個推估人工濕地溫室
氣體釋放量的運算系統，只要已知溼地的參數，例如處理水量、水質、
人工濕地類型、水生植物種植密度及種類等，即可透過此系統的運算模
式估算出整個溼地的溫室氣體排放量及季節變化。再者，亦可歸納出降
低溼地溫室氣體釋放量的設計指導方針及操作維護方法。 
技術說明 
英文： 
Based on the results obtained in this three-year project, a computation 
system will possibly be established to evaluate the emission rate and total 
emission amount of greenhouse gases from a constructed wetland used for 
treating wastewater. Through the computation system and the collected 
parameters of constructed wetlands, such as flow rate and strength of 
wastewater, types of constructed wetlands, density and species of 
macrophytes etc., gas emission rate and its variation due to seasonal change 
can be efficiently determined. Furthermore, this study may provide 
information on design guideline and operation & maintenance strategy for a 
constructed wetland to control the emission of greenhouse gases.

1. Introduction 
Aquaculture is an important industry in Taiwan, which provided about 0.33 million 
metric tons of food production that represented a mean market value of one billion US dollars 
annually in 2007 (ROCCOA, 2008). Mariculture contributed 39% of the production quantity 
and 37% of the total value. 
Most of mariculture farms in Taiwan conduct earthen pond culture and are located in the 
coastal area of the island, exploiting seawater near shoreline for culture and discharging pond 
effluent via tidal exchange with the sea. However, urbanization and industrial development 
has resulted in an increasing discharge of pollutants and nutrients from offshore land into the 
sea, which in turn deteriorates quality of the water resource for mariculture. Moreover, the 
effluent from mariculture itself typically contains particulates, organic matter, nitrogen, and 
phosphorous as a result of accumulation of feed residue and fish excreta during cultivation, 
which can further degrade the quality of receiving seawater. Under such circumstance, a cost 
effective water depuration approach is needed to purify the inflow water source as well as the 
final pond effluent so as to manage appropriate water quality for mariculture and protect the 
coastal marine environment. 
Constructed wetland (CW) is a natural system for water pollution control, which 
integrates physical, chemical, and biological processes into an ecological system to retain and 
stabilize pollutants from contaminated water or wastewater influent. As compared with a 
be utilized as potential filters of shrimp pond effluent (Gautier et al., 2001). In Taiwan similar 
to many tropical and subtropical countries, natural mangrove forests have been greatly lost for 
human activities including aquaculture development (Hsueh and Lee, 2000). Therefore, 
creating constructed mangrove wetlands adjacent to or in aquaculture farms to become an 
integrated aquaculture farm-mangrove depuration system is proposed in the study so as not 
only to manage the mariculture farm effluent but also to restore coastal mangrove ecosystems.  
There is still a problem in using mangrove plants for CWs treating mariculture farm 
effluent. Mangrove plants require frequent tidal flushing in their natural habitat, whereas CWs 
normally operate at a constant water depth with continuous flooding. Although some species 
of mangroves have been demonstrated growing well in freshwater without tidal flushing (Wu 
et al., 2008b), various native species existing in different regions of the world may exhibit 
different endurance of permanent inundation under saline aquaculture effluent, which needs to 
be investigated. 
In this study, CW microcosms planted with three species of native mangroves in Taiwan 
(Avicennia marina (Forsk.) Vieh., Rhizophora stylosa Griff., and Lumnitzera racemosa Willd) 
were established to receive mariculture fishpond effluent and operated at different hydraulic 
retention times (HRTs, 0.5, 1 and 2 day). The main objectives of the study were to: (1) 
investigate the effects of continuous flooding and HRT on mangrove growth, (2) examine the 
effects of HRT and mangrove species on performance of microcosms for depuration of 
tank. The microcosm influent was intermittently fed from the storage tank through pipelines 
and several small pumps with identical capacity to achieve a daily-average target flow rate of 
45, 22.5, and 11.3 L/d in order to maintain treatments at 0.5, 1, and 2 d of HRT, respectively. 
The different flow rates were adjusted by different pumping time sequences, which were 
controlled by timers. These twelve continuously flooded treatments were conducted to 
examine effects of HRT and mangrove species on plant growth and performance of 
microcosms for pond effluent depuration.  
The remaining nine microcosms comprised other three treatments, each also being 
performed in triplicate, which were planted with the three mangrove species as mentioned 
above. However, holes were drilled at the bottom of the tanks so that water was able to be 
drained out by gravity. The microcosm influent was fed twice a day (at 8 am and 8 pm) by 
pumping with a daily-average flow rate of 45 L/d, which was identical to that of the 0.5-d 
HRT treatment. These drained treatments were designed to simulate a situation that field 
mangrove forests received fishpond effluent and were affected by tidal flushing, and were 
employed to examine mangrove growth as compared with continuous flooding treatments.   
The wetland microcosms were set up on 20 March 2007. Before regularly receiving the 
influent on 20 April 2007, all microcosms were irrigated with the fishpond effluent twice a 
day for one month to promote acclimation of mangroves to the saline fishpond water. 
Treatments of regular influent to the microcosms lasted for 9 months and were ended on 22

Pollutant removal rate (g m-2 d-1) was defined as pollutant loading rate times the pollutant

4. Discussion 
4.1. Plant growth response to continuous flooding and HRT   
Soil redox potentials of mangrove pots have been observed to decrease with increasing 
waterlogged time, supporting the evidence that waterlogging led to oxygen deficiency in 
flooded soils as oxygen diffusion rates are much slower in water than in air (Ye et al., 2003; 
Visser & Voesenek, 2004). All the continuously flooded microcosms in the study even 
achieved negative levels of redox potentials or anoxic state in soil environment as compared 
with positive levels measured in the drained microcosms.  Different mangrove species could 
cause differential redox levels in soil environment. Krauss et al. (2006) reported that soil 
redox potentials of Rhizophora mangle pots were lower than those of Laguncularia racemosa 
and Avicennia germinans pots, and attributed this finding to poorer aerial root structures 
developed, thus less soil aeration, by the former species. Ye et al. (2003) found that soil redox 
potentials of Kandelia candel pots were higher than those of Bruguiera gymnorrhiza pots 
although there was no significant difference in root biomass between the two species, 
suggesting that roots of K. candel may transport more oxygen into soil than those of B. 
gymnorrhiza. In this study, the soil redox potentials of A. marina and L. racemosa 
L. racemosa was found in the 0.5-HRT flooded treatment compared with the drained 
treatment, and there was even an increase in RGR (approximately 4.4%) for L. racemosa (Fig. 
2). Such increase in RGR may imply the promoting effect on plant growth caused by 
increasing availability of water and nutrient out-competes the inhibiting effect due to oxygen 
deficiency. Consequently, we may conclude that A. marina and L. racemosa are more tolerant 
to continuous flooding with saline mariculture pond effluent than R. stylosa. A similar 
conclusion has been reached by He et al. (2007) who found A. marina had better tolerance to 
flooding than R. stylosa in a field trial with different tidal flat elevation treatments conducted 
in a diurnal tidal zone. 
HRT may also have two effects on mangrove growth. Firstly, shortening HRT represents 
increase in water replacement rate (or dilution rate) resulted from raising influent flow rate, 
which might somewhat prevent soil environment from being too anoxic, thus easing the stress 
of oxygen deficiency. That may be used to explain why 0.5-d HRT treatment groups had 
higher soil redox potentials than the 1-d and 2-d HRT treatment groups for A. marina and L. 
racemosa microcosms (Fig. 1). Secondly, less HRT means more nutrient supplies available 
for mangrove growth within the microcosms. In fact, growth responses to HRT for the three 
mangrove species were different. The RGR of A. marina in the 0.5-d HRT treatment was 
significantly higher than the 1-d and 2-d HRT treatments (Fig. 2). HRT exerted only a slight 
effect on the RGR of L. racemosa, and no effect on R. stylosa growth.

following sequential pattern: A. marina ＞ L. racemosa ＞ R. stylosa (Hsueh and Lee, 2000；
Yu, 2003；Tu, 2005). 
4.2. Performance of mangrove microcosms 
The surface flow mangrove microcosms removed pollutants from pond effluent with 
efficiencies of 5.7~27.1 % for SS, 4.9~36.3 % for BOD5, 18.7~29.9 % for TP, 21.2~49.8 % 
for NH4-N, and 5.4~37.7% for NOx-N. The performance of CWs using mangrove plantation 
has been increasingly reported, mostly focusing on treatment of pretreated municipal 
wastewater employing subsurface flow (SSF) wetland (Table 6; Jing et al., 2007; Wu et al., 
2008b; Yang et al., 2008) but rarely on treatment of aquaculture pond effluent with free water 
surface flow (FWS) wetland (Shimoda et al., 2005). The pollutant removal efficiencies 
obtained in this study were apparently lower than those previously reported findings (Table 6). 
There are three main factors that may have determined this discrepancy: strength and salinity 
of wastewater, and type of CW.  
Compared to aquaculture wastewater, the pretreated municipal wastewater typically 
contains higher strength of pollutants (Table 6), which could generate kinetic benefits to 
improve pollutant reduction rate, as pollutant removal in CWs is commonly recognized 
following first-order kinetics, and thus leading to higher removal efficiencies (Boonsong et al., 
2003; Jing et al., 2007; Wu et al., 2008b; Yang et al., 2008). In contrast, pond effluent had 
wetland microcosms and found that removal percentages of pollutants decreased 
approximately 15-22% with salinity increasing from 0 to 30‰.  
It is well known that CWs are divided according to water flow pattern into two types: 
free water surface (FWS) and subsurface flow (SSF) wetlands. While SSF wetlands are more 
expensive because of using large amounts of gravel as media, they are generally more 
efficient in removing pollutants due to provision of extra mechanisms (e.g., gravel filtration 
and more surface area for microbial attachment) to promote depuration of pollutants (IWA, 
2000). The FWS wetlands, in contrast, are less expensive and more functional in landscape 
esthetics and wildlife habitat enhancement, but normally less effective in wastewater 
treatment performance because of lacking those pollutant removal mechanisms. Thus, the 
lower pollutant removal efficiencies obtained in this study may be partly attributed to the use 
of FWS type of microcosms only. By considering the advantages and disadvantages of the 
both wetland types, a FWS-SSF hybrid system may be a suitable alternative for designing 
mangrove CWs for mariculture effluent treatment. Such system would be expected to 
improve its pollutant removal performance while maintaining functions of landscape and 
nature conservation with moderate capital cost. The FWS-SSF hybrid system, planting with 
salt non-tolerant macrophytes, has been demonstrated to be successful and applicable for 
depuration of brackish aquaculture pond effluent and recirculating aquaculture water (Lin et 
al., 2002a, 2002b, 2003, 2005, 2010).

While the highest removal efficiency (percentage) mostly occurred in microcosms 
operated at 2-d HRT, the highest mass removal rate was found in microcosms operated at 0.5-
d HRT for most pollutant parameters except BOD5. Although higher removal efficiency can 
be achieved with a longer HRT, it comparatively required a larger wetland area. In order to 
save costs and under the circumstance of satisfying effluent standards, a lower HRT system 
might be more suitable (Wu et al., 2008b). Operating with a shorter HRT means smaller land-
area requirements, which is a crucial factor for considering CW technique in a land-limited 
country such as Taiwan (Jing et al., 2002).

技術說明
(中文) 近年來，大氣層中溫室效應氣體(greenhouse gases)含量逐年增加進而導致全球
暖化及氣候 
變遷議的議題普遍受到國際間的重視。另一方面，人工溼地為一種低成本、省能
源、容易操作、 
且兼具生態保育及景觀價值的廢水處理程序，然而在淨化水質的生化程序中，無
可避免的也會 
產生甲烷(CH4)及氧化亞氮(N2O)兩種溫室效應氣體而引起關切。人工溼地在國內
外應用的數目 
及面積規模均有逐年成長的趨勢，因此溫室氣體排放造成衝擊的相關研究有其迫
切需要。 
本年度計畫為執行三年計畫的第一年，初步選擇嘉南藥理科技大學校園人工濕地
為研究 
對象，使用密閉罩法測量濕地不同位置點的溫室氣體釋放通量，並同時監測濕地
的廢水處理操 
作條件及水質淨化結果，自96 年12 月起每個月採樣分析一次。人工濕地系統由
一個表面下流 
動式(SSF)溼地連結一個表面流(FWS)溼地所構成，總濕地面積約3,800 m2。本年
度的主要研究 
目的為：(1)探討人工溼地溫室氣體釋放通量的空間與時間變化；(2)比較不同類
型人工溼地 
(FWS 與SSF 溼地)溫室氣體排放量的差異，並與國內已建立的天然溼地溫室氣體
排放資料進 
行比較；(3)利用單變數線性回歸法分析溫室氣體排放與溼地水質環境條件的關
係；(4)日夜間 
變化對人工溼地溫室效應氣體產量的影響。。
