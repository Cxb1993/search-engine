Mass Customization is one approach to deal with the continuous increasing demand for 
individualized products. Its major objective is to offer products with delivery times and prices 
comparable to mass produced goods. This plan addresses the design of an object-oriented model to 
allow an agile interaction between consumers and companies, toward the implementation of mass 
customization in the building sector.  We propose an approach to mass customization that is based 
on the collaborative product design process and agile manufacturing environment that mass 
customization can be effectively achieved through this integrated design and production system. 
The core of DFCM (Design for customization manufactur ing) is developed under the mass 
customization oriented architecture. Another core system, ACM (Agile customization 
manufacturing), is a novel production-system operational architecture. In mass-customization 
environments, ACM allows most adjustments for process to take place rapidly during production. 
Keywords?mass customization, design for excellent, agile manufacturing
