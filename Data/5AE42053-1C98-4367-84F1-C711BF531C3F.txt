 摘要 
本計畫的研究重點在釐清奈米碳管的微結構，包括： 
(1) 碳管外徑 
(2) 碳管層數 
(3) 碳管長度 
(4) 碳管長徑比 
(5) 碳管重量百分比 
(6) 基材性質 
與奈米碳管複合材料之剛度與熱傳導係數的「微結構─宏觀性質」定量關係。拉伸實驗顯示，
當使用直徑小於 20 nm 之多壁奈米碳管時，5 wt%-多壁奈米碳管/環氧樹脂的拉伸強度提昇
106%，楊氏係數提昇 61.1%。當使用直徑介於 40~60 nm 之多壁奈米碳管時，5 wt%-多壁奈
米碳管/環氧樹脂的拉伸強度提昇 82.3%，楊氏係數提昇 54.1%。至於熱傳導係數，當使用
直徑小於 10 nm 之多壁奈米碳管時，5 wt%-多壁奈米碳管/環氧樹脂的熱傳導係數提昇
62.3%，當使用直徑介於 40~60 nm 之多壁奈米碳管時，5 wt%-多壁奈米碳管/環氧樹脂的熱
傳導係數提昇 27.8%。 
 
關鍵詞: 奈米碳管，複合材料，機械性質 
 
ABSTRACT 
 
The proposed work studies in a systematic manner the macroscopic stiffness, and coefficient 
of thermal conductivity, of carbon nanotube-(CNT-)reinforced polymeric composites to obtain the 
mechanistic understanding of the quantitative microstructure-property relationship. 
 
 The objective of the project is to delineate the quantitative relationship between the carbon 
naotube-(CNT-)reinforced nanocomposites’ microstructure, specifically, CNT diameter, length, 
aspect ratio, number of layers, CNT weight percentage, and matrix properties, and the 
macroscopic stiffness, and coefficient of thermal conductivity.  The tensile test results show that 
the tensile strength of 5 wt%-MWCNT/epoxy with a diameter D < 20 nm increases 106.0%, and 
the Young modulus increases 61.1%.  Adding the same weight fraction of MWCNT with a 
larger diameter D = 40 ~ 60 nm into epoxy, the tensile strength of 5 wt%-MWCNT/epoxy 
increases 82.3%, and the Young modulus increases 54.1%, in comparison with those of pure 
epoxy, of which the tensile strength is 25.67 MPa, and the Young modulus is 2.83 GPa. On the 
other hand, the increase in the thermal conductivities of 1 wt.% and 5 wt.% MWCNT/epoxy 
composites range from 16.1% to 62.3% and from 2.9% to 27.8% with CNT diameter less then 10 
nm, and between 40 to 60 nm, respectively. The thermal conductivity of MWCNT/epoxy 
composite increases with a decrease in diameter of MWCNT and an increase in MWCNTs 
loading. 
 
Keywords: carbon nanotube, CNT, composite, mechanical property
