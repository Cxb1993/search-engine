結合觸媒氧化與奈米過濾之複合技術應用於降解含雙酚 A 廢水之
研究 
Preparation of NSC Project Reports 
計畫編號：NSC 99-2221-E-041-016 
執行期限：99 年 8 月 1 日至 100 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：陳世雄    嘉南藥理科技大學 環工系 
 
中文摘要 
本研究已成功開發觸媒氧化/奈米過
濾複合系統並應用於含雙酚 A 廢水處理，
研究結果顯示奈米過濾系統增進觸媒氧化
處理單元降解效率，有效減少異相觸媒之
流失並有效進行水回收率、回收水質。本
計畫以非均相鐵型活性探觸媒(本研究室
鐵型活性碳氧化觸媒專利 ,中華民國 I 
264416)與奈米過濾膜組成複合系統降解
污染物，以奈米過濾系統有效濃縮污染物
並促進氧化降解效率及有效降解非均相反
應之污染物，研究中改變異相氧化複合系
統降解條件與回收廢水率，其廢水系統中
中雙酚 A 轉化率可達 100%，而 COD 轉化
率可達 90%以上。非均相鐵型活性探觸媒
可經掃流過濾而回流至反應槽，而奈米過
濾將未反應物質濃縮而循環再進一步反
應，本研究所開發出之技術可有效處理高
毒性廢水產業廢水並能有效將廢水回收再
利用。 
關鍵詞: 觸媒氧化、水回收、奈米過濾、
複合系統、雙酚 A 
Abstract 
This project successfully developed a 
catalytic oxidation/Nanofiltration hybrid 
system for the degradation of bisphenol A 
from wastewater. The results indicated that 
membrane unit effective facilitated the 
efficiency of oxidation system and enhance 
the bisphenol A conversion in the effluent 
wastewater and this result benefited the 
wastewater reused. The effect of NF 
operating parameters on catalytic oxidation 
with heterogeneous carbon catalyst with iron 
embedded (I 264416, Taiwan) for wastewater 
treatment had investigated. It was found that 
the NF system concentrated the pollutant and 
enhanced the oxidation efficiency by the 
membrane system and degraded bisphenol A 
by the heterogeneous oxidation in the reactor. 
The heterogeneous catalytic oxidations in 
hybrid system were carried out by 
considering the operating parameters and 
recycling the intermediates into oxidation. 
This study showed that a completely 
bisphenol A conversion can be achieved and 
over 90% COD removal was found under the 
suitable conditions. In this study, the 
catalysts were recycling into the oxidation 
unit in the hybrid system and the pollutant 
and intermediates were concentrated to 
facilitate pollutant oxidation in the reactor. 
Finally, the effective catalytic oxidation and 
nanofiltration hybrid system is successful to 
apply for the toxic wastewater treatment and 
water reuse and recycling the water resource.    
Keywords:  catalytic oxidation, water 
reuse nanofiltration, hybrid system, 
bisphenol A

過氧化氫濃度測定: 
過氧化氫濃度是以高錳酸鉀滴定法測
定，用稱量瓶精確稱取試品2.5克 ，用少量
水攪勻後，將全部溶液和洗液注入500毫升
的量瓶中，並加水稀釋到500毫升的標線。
用50毫升的單標移液管移取試品溶液50毫
升，置於300毫升的燒杯中，加水100毫升
及6N硫酸溶液10毫升，然後以0.1N高錳酸
鉀液慢慢滴入，並時加攪動，滴至粉紅色
經久不褪時為止並由滴定劑量換算其濃
度。 
COD濃度測定: 
化 學 需 氧 量 （ Chemical Oxygen 
Demand，簡稱 COD）是以密閉迴流滴定
法測定。測定的程序是在消化管中依序加
入過量之重鉻酸鉀，硫酸及水樣後，於密
閉消化管中在 150 ℃下加熱迴流；待反應
完成後，以硫酸亞鐵銨滴定溶液中殘餘之
重鉻酸鉀，由所使用之硫酸亞鐵銨體積，
即可換算求得水樣中之化學需氧量。 
X射線繞射儀 (X-ray Diffraction， XRD): 
    X射線繞射儀以繞射角θ照射於樣品
上，樣品產生相干散射，依照布拉格公式
（Bragg’s  law）nλ＝2dsinθ，當n為一定
值時，任何一個晶體只有一個θ繞射角。
因此在測定XRD時，通常從0°～90°轉動樣
品，讓晶體能各自對應2θ產生繞射，再由
探測器接收，記錄系統顯示。在本研究對
於活性碳表面晶體繞射，繞射角以0.02°
/min由10°～110°轉動。 
比表面積分析儀 
比表面積分析儀是以惰性氣體在特定
相對壓力（P/P0）由零到一範圍內之等溫
平衡吸、脫附體積，再由所得之吸脫附體
積經由理論經驗式計算出活性碳之比表面
積、微或中孔體面積、總孔體積、孔洞大
小分佈、孔洞直徑、孔洞形狀等。本研究
所使用之儀器是 Micromeritics Instrument

結論 
1. 本研究完成非均相鐵型活性探觸媒與奈
米過濾膜系統氧化系統之測試並有效降
解溶液中雙酚 A 污染物｡ 
2. 觸媒氧化降解廢水中雙酚 A 等污染物之
反應參數、影響變因可最佳化用以有效
分解污染物。 
3. 含浸鐵鹽顯著影響非均相鐵型活性探觸
媒氧化過程表面孔隙與化學特性變化。 
4. 氧化系統之於最適條件下可完全氧化降
解雙酚 A 及溶液 COD。

Pervaporation of ethanol/water mixtures using ion-exchange polysulfone membrane 
containing ammonium 
 
Shih-hsiung Chen 
*
, Rey-May Liou, Cheng-Lee Lai, Mu-Ya Hung, Cheng-Hsien Huang 
 
Department of Environmental Engineering and Science, Chia-Nan University of Pharmacy and Science, 
Tainan, 717, Taiwan, Tainan, Taiwan, Tel. 886-6-2669090; Fax 
886-6-2669090;email:mshchen@mail.chna.edu.tw 
 
Abstract 
 
The ion exchange polysulfone membranes were prepared and utilized for dehydration of ethanol/water 
mixtures by pervaporation. This paper presents an original investigation on the influence of the ammonium 
containing on the hydrophilic and swelling properties of ion-exchange membranes. The identification of 
ammonium was carried out by Infrared ray (IR) spectra. It was found that the containing of ammonium did 
not significantly change the membrane surface morphology but strongly decreased the water contact angle of 
membranes.  The decline in the degree of swelling in feed solution (90% ethanol solution) implied that the 
content of ammonium on the polymer did not prefer ethanol to swell the membranes and it was also 
confirmed the hydrophilicity enrichment of ammonium containing in the polysulfone in pervaporation. The 
optimum ammonium substitution in membrane achieved the high pervaporation performance of dehydration 
of ethanol mixtures. An over 99% permeate water was obtained in the pervaporation process. The excellent 
dehydration performance of ion exchange membranes was obtained by sulfonation and ion exchange 
technology with polysulfone. All the PSI values of ion exchange membrane were over 10

 with suitable 
preparing condition with ion exchanged polysulfone membranes.  
 
Key words: ammonium, ion exchange, polysulfone, membrane, pervaporation, ethanol/water 
 
Introduction 
Dehydration of water/organic mixtures by pervaporation process becomes more attractive than 
traditional separation processes in industrial applications due to thier economic considerstion on the energy 
comsumption. The preparation and modification of dehydration membranes are the both two biggest 
challenges for development the high performance pervaporation process (1). Many works were focused on the 
improving of separation performance of pervaporation membrane by considering the permeate sorption and 
diffusion properties. The water-permselective pervaporation membranes usually focus on the improvement of 
the sorption selectivity rather than diffusion selectivity of permeates. Highly water-permselective membranes 
(2-5) can be achieved by increasing either the sorption selectivity or the diffusion selectivity of water to 
ethanol. The Introduction of hydrophilic moiety in polymer matrix to enhance the hydrophilic properties of 
membrane is an effective method. But some of the investigations failed in the attemp of improving  
performance of pervaporation membrane, the selectivity loss mainly due to the high degree of swelling of  
membranes with inducing the hydrphilic moiety increased their free volume and enhanced both of the 
permeation of water and ethanol transporting through the menbrane.  
In the case of  swelling state, the pervaporation membranes is proposed to present a high free volume 
due to the hydration of water molecules around ionic group in the membrane matrix. In a hydration state, the 
clusters of water molecules is interconnected and finally to form a channel which allowed the permeates 
transporting through the membrane. Therefore, the water selective membrane presents a good separation 
performance in the pervaporation for dehydration of ethanol/water mixtures. However, the various 
morphology chnages can be found due to the swelling state in the pervaporation membrane and resulted a 
significant impact on the separation performance. So it is necessary to control the degree of swelling in the

Sorption measurements    
The dried membranes were immersed in the ethanol-water mixture for 24 hours at 25 ℃. They were 
subsequently blotted between tissue papers to remove the excess solvent and placed in the left tube of a twin 
tube set-up. The system was evacuated while the left tube was heated with hot water for 30 minutes and the 
right tube was cooled in liquid nitrogen. The composition of the condensed liquid in the right tube was 
determined by G.C. The sorption selectivity was calculated as follows: 
sorp=(Yw/Ye)/(Xw/Xe) 
where Xe , Xw  and Ye ,Yw  are the weight fractions of ethanol and water in the feed and membranes, 
respectively. 
Contact angle measurements 
The water contact angle was measured with a FACE contact angle meter CA-D type (Kyowa Interface 
Science Co. Ltd., Japan). 
 
Swelling measurements 
Due to the difficult to determine the true swelling properties of porous structure of asymmetric sodium 
exchanged membranes, the degree of swelling of dense membranes is used to describe the swelling behavior 
of the skin layer of sodium exchanged membranes. The degree of swelling of dense membranes was 
determined in distilled water and in aqueous ethanol solution at 25 ℃. The weight of dry membrane (Wdry) 
was first determined. After attaining equilibrium for the swelling measurement in water or ethanol solution, 
the fully swollen membrane was blotted with tissue paper and weighed. Since the ethanol evaporated very fast, 
it was difficult to read the correct weight directly. The weight of the membrane was measured every 5 seconds, 
and it was plotted as a function of time for 30 seconds from the time the ethanol solution soaked membrane 
was blotted with tissue paper. The weight at time zero could be extrapolated and was taken as swollen weight 
(Wwet) of the membrane. The degree of swelling was calculated with the following equation:   
Degree of swelling (%) = (Wwet – Wdry)/Wdry × 100% 
 
Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM)  
Hitachi S4100 SEM was used to observe the surface and cross-section structures of asymmetric 
membrane. In preparation for taking the SEM images, membranes samples were first immersed and then 
fractured in liquid nitrogen. Using a sputter coater, gold was then deposited on the samples. 
 
Results and discussion 
Morphology of ion exchange membranes 
Typical surface morphologies of ion exchange membranes with various ammoniums contain are shown 
in Fig. 2. It can be seen by the observation of surface SEM micrographs that at lower ammoniums content, 
membranes show more uniform and homogeneous, but at higher ammoniums loading, the membrane surface 
is somewhat non-uniform nodule on the surface. In the case of dry phase inversion process, polymer 
concentration and solvent evaporation rate dominated the membrane formation pathways. If the solvent 
evaporated uniform and phase inversion of casting solution was happen while the polymer concentration 
entered the solidification area, the polymer chains orderly rearranged and form the dense homogenous 
membrane structure. On the other hand, the diverse structure was formed on the surface of ion exchange 
membranes with high ammonium ion content in the matrix. It is indicated the ion exchanged changed the 
membrane formation path way and formed a different surface morphology. It is believed that the ion exchange 
between ammonium ion and sulfuric group played an important role on the membrane formation. The nodule 
formation implied the entanglement of polymer chains were formed during the membrane formation. The 
possible reason resulted the polymer entanglement was the ion crosslink between the polysulfone while the 
ammonium ion was high enough to form the inter polymer chain cross linking. If the polymer entanglement

The effects of ionic group content on permeation flux and separation factor of ion exchanged 
membranes in pervaporation are shown in Figure 8. It can be seen that the similar permeation flux were 
observed by increasing the ion group content in membranes. It is reasonable to explain this behavior by the 
swelling measurement of ion exchanged membranes. Due to the ion crosslinking between the polymer chain, 
the permeation rate did not significantly affected by the hydration effect of the permeate in pervaporation. It is 
interesting to note that the separation factor showed a strong increase with the increasing in ion groups 
inducing in membranes matrix. Generally, the permeation behavior of pervaporation is dominated by the 
sorption and diffusion selectivity in pervaporation process. The increase in separation factor of ion exchanged 
membrane may be contributed by the promotion in permeate diffusion or sorption behavior. In order to 
exactly clarify the transport behavior of permeates in pervaporation, the diffusion selectivity through a 
pervaporation membrane and the permeate content in membrane were made.  The sorption selectivity can be 
calculated based on the sorption test. The sorption measurement of dense ion exchanged membranes are 
applied to calculate the sorption selectivity, the diffusion selectivity (αd) can be defined as the ratio of 
permeation selectivity (αp) and sorption selectivity (αs): [25 ] αp = αd × αs. The sorption selectivity of water to 
ethanol was calculated by dividing the amount of water content by the amount of ethanol content in sulfonated 
membranes.  
Figure 9 shows the relationship between the ion group content and sorption selectivity and diffusion 
selectivity of ion exchanged membranes in 90wt% ethanol solution at 25℃. It can be seen that the sorption 
selectivity of water to ethanol increased with increasing the ion group content in the membrane. On the other 
hand, the diffusion selectivity slightly decreased with increasing in ion group content in membrane. It is 
implied that the increased in ion group content in membranes benefits water selective property in all 
membranes. On contrary, the increase in ion group content in membrane lost the diffusion selectivity. The 
diffusion selectivity decreased may be contributed by the coupling effect between the water and ethanol in the 
pervaporation. The swelling measurement also confirmed the above viewpoint. Base on the swelling 
measurement, the decline in degree of swelling of ion exchange membrane indicated the smaller diffusion 
path were form by the ion crosslinking in membrane matrix. This significant sieving effect can be observed 
and enhanced the diffusion selectivity of water to ethanol. As showing in Figure 9, the diffusion selectivity 
did not increased but decreased with increasing the effect of ion crosslinking in membranes. Therefore, the 
partial coupling effect in the non-crossliking area contributed the loss of the diffusion selectivity. Thus the 
sorption behavior showed a strong enhancement by the ionic group contribution but slight decline the 
diffusion selectivity. It is conclusion that the ionic groups induce in membrane enhanced the performance of 
pervaporation membrane. But the balances of ion crosslinking and hydration effect of ionic group in 
membrane are the dominated factors of the separation performance.  
Effect of ammonium content on the PSI value of ion exchange polysulfone membrane was shown in 
figure 10. It can be seen that the PSI value of ion exchange polysulfone membrane were enhanced by the 
increase in ammonium content in membranes. Due to the less change in permeation flux was observed in the 
previous section, the increase in PSI value mainly contributed by the enhancement on diffusivity selectivity of 
ion exchange membranes, which was contributed by both of the hydration and ion crosslinking effect in the 
membrane. It was indicated that the increase in the degree of ammonium substitution in membrane lead to an 
enhancement in diffusion selectivity of membranes. This result also implied that the permselectivity of ion 
exchange membranes were strongly affected by the permeate swelling in the feed solution. 
 
Conclusion 
Ammoniums containing ion exchange membranes were successfully prepared and applied for 
dehydration of water and ethanol mixtures by pervaporation. Ammoniums substitution improved the 
hydrophilicity of ion exchange membrane and it was confirmed by the water contact angle and IR spectra 
measurements. It was found that the ionic crosslinking inhibited the swelling effect of permeate in 
pervaporation and promoted the separation performance. The sorption selectivity and diffusion selectivity 
analysis of ion exchange membrane were made and indicated that the diffusion selectivity was the important 
role to dominate the separation performance. Based on the result of this study, it can be concluded that a high 
selective membrane could be achieved by controlling the optimum swelling condition with appreciate 
ammoniums containing in ion exchange membranes.
