摘要 
 
  本研究以離子束沈積法(Ion beam assisted deposition, IBAD)，在5088鋁鎂合金基材上鍍
上一無電鍍鎳磷層，並於製程中改變沉積條件，分別沉積不同厚度的類鑽薄膜(Diamond-like 
carbon﹐DLC)後，再沉積相同厚度(1nm)的碳氮膜，以探討這兩種不同沉積條件薄膜在0.5M 
H2SO4溶液中的耐腐蝕性質之影響效應。首先利用拉曼光譜儀、微奈米硬度量測儀與原子力
顯微鏡(Atomic Force Microscopy, AFM)等薄膜結構分析法，對各種類鑽薄膜之原子鍵結、
表面硬度與表面形貌做一分析與討論，並且利用電化學極化法，分析薄膜鍍層之耐腐蝕性
質。研究結果發現，類鑽薄膜與類鑽薄膜+碳氮膜沈積於具有鎳磷鍍層之5088鋁鎂合金基材
上，均能顯示優異的耐腐蝕性，而只有類鑽薄膜的耐腐蝕性，隨著薄膜厚度的增加而增加。
兩種型式薄膜的表面奈米粗糙度均隨薄膜厚度增加而減少，而類鑽薄膜再沈積碳氮薄膜由
於具sp2的石墨相較多，使得耐蝕性與表面硬度、楊氏模數等機械性質比類鑽薄膜小，因此
有較好的延性與導電性。 
關鍵詞：類鑽薄膜；碳氮膜；拉曼光譜儀；原子力顯微鏡；微奈米硬度量測儀。

Abstract 
 
Diamond-like carbon films posses high hardness, high wear resistance and low friction 
coefficient, and widely used in mechanical, electronic and biomedical industries. The purpose of 
this study is to evaluate the corrosion resistance properties of Diamond-like carbon (DLC) and 
DLC with carbon nitride (CN) films with various thickness in 0.5M H2SO4 solution deposited on 
5088 Al-Mg alloy substrate by ion-beam assisted deposition (IBAD) method. The atomic bonding

度的碳氮膜(1nm)，分析比較不同性質條件兩組薄膜。以拉曼頻譜(Raman Spectrometry)分
析薄膜之微結構、微奈米硬度量測儀測量薄膜硬度、腐蝕行為方面在 0.5M H2SO4水溶液中，
進行電化學動態極化實驗，電化學極化使用[EG&G Model 273A]恆電位儀進行電化學動電
位極化掃描，曝露面積為 1.75cm2，量測範圍由OCP下方 250mVSCE以 1mV/s掃描速率向上
遞增至 1600mVSCE。電化學量測使用之參考電極與輔助電極皆分別為飽和甘汞電極
(Saturated Calomel Electrode ,SCE )與白金(Pt)線。薄膜經腐蝕試驗後，再以掃描式電子顯微
鏡(Scanning Electron Microscopy ,SEM)對破壞表面進行觀察。 
 
3. 結果與討論 
 
3.1 拉曼光譜分析 
 
  將拉曼光譜圖解析，可得到G band和D band位置、半高寬以及I(D)/I(G)比值，結果如表
1 所示。I(D)/I(G)被稱為石墨化指數(Graphitic Index)，因比值越大表示奈米結晶類石墨相的
增加[12-14]，而在G band位置的偏移值越趨近較低的波數時，可得知類鑽石的特性增加[15,16]。
由圖 1 也說明類鑚薄膜再沉積碳氮膜，其I(D)/I(G)比值增大之現象，可得知類鑚薄膜增加碳
氮膜會較趨向石墨化。 
 
3.2 微奈米硬度量測儀 
 
    表 2 為不同厚度的類鑽薄膜及類鑽薄膜+碳氮薄膜之表面奈米硬度與楊氏模數。將表面
奈米硬度與楊氏模數對薄膜厚度的關係作圖，結果如圖 2 及圖 3 所示。由圖 2 及圖 3 可得
知類鑽薄膜及類鑽薄膜+碳氮薄膜的硬度，均隨著薄膜厚度增加而增加，但類鑽薄膜的表面
奈米硬度與楊氏模數都比類鑽薄膜+碳氮薄膜碳氮薄膜高。由此可得知在類鑽薄膜再沉積碳
氮薄膜會有較高的延性，可能有助於耐磨耗。H. Q.Lou[17]等學者也使用奈米硬度量測
(nano-identation)證實CN薄膜比TiN薄膜有明顯較高的彈性恢復(elastic recovery)能量
而有較大的延性。本試驗結果可由拉曼分析說明，卽沉積碳氮薄膜會增加I(D)/I(G)比值，
使得鍍膜內之鑽石相(sp3 bond)減少，石墨相(sp2 bond)增加，導致鍍層性質趨近於石墨，而
使得鍍層硬度降低、延性增加。 
 
3.3 原子力顯微鏡 
 
圖 4 為不同鍍層厚度類鑽薄膜與類鑽薄膜+碳氮薄膜之AFM表面形態，顯示一個三度空
間的平滑表面，將所量測的表面平均奈米粗糙度Ra及Rms整理如表 3 所示。結果發現類鑽
薄膜與類鑽薄膜+碳氮薄膜的粗糙度均有隨著薄膜厚度的增加而降低，類鑽薄膜厚度增加可
以薄膜中的微細缺陷，如在薄膜成長時的氣孔及針孔可能消失[18]。而類鑽薄膜+碳氮薄膜的
粗糙度會降低，是由於sp3含量的不同。J. Y. Chen[19]等學者發現以較高的FC2H2/FAr流量比值
獲得表面較粗糙的類鑽鍍膜，是由於sp3含量的增加。若FC2H2/FAr流量減少會弱化類鑽薄膜
的形成，即降低sp3含量，而從拉曼光譜分析則會有較大I(D)/I(G)比值。

2. Experimental 
The substrate material was a commercial grade 5088 Al-Mg alloy. After 
electroless depositing a Ni-P interlayer of 10 μm, the diamond-like carbon 
nitride thin films of various thickness of 1.5, 2, 2.5 and 3 nm were deposited by 
ion beam assited deposition technique. A mixture of C2H2 and N2 (60%) gas 
was used as processing gas. The measurement of film thickness was 
evaulated by a transmission electron microscope (TEM), and measuring 
results as shown in Fig. 1. Structures of diamond like carbon nitride thin films 
were characterized by Raman spectrometry, Also the carbon and nitrogen 
atom of the CN thin films was analyzed by ESCA, evidenced that the ratio of 
carbon and nitrogen is 0.095 ± 0.02, as shown in Fig. 2. 
 11
   The wear corrosion tests were conducted in a 1M NaCl+1M H2SO4 solution 
of 4 L using the block-on-ring method [16,17]. The dry wear test was 
conducted in an ambient environment without lubricant, while the wear 
corrosion test was conducted. Specimens were placed in contact with a 
rotating ring, which was made of sintered Al2O3 ceramic with an outer diameter 
of 22.5 mm and a width of 13.5 mm. The applied load (5 mN) and sliding speed 
(0.06 m ⋅s-1, or 50 rev ⋅min-1) were fixed in wear corrosion tests. The sliding 
distance of wear corrosion was maintained constant at 216 m. The wear 
corrosion current density  was determined during sliding through a distance of 
216 m by varying the applied potentials of -400, 0, 400, 800 and 1000 mVSCE. 
All of the specimens before and after the tests were cleaned and weighed 
according to the ASTM G1-90 standard method [18]. A potentiostat (EG & G 
Model 273A) with analysis software were utilized to determine the corrosion 
current density and the potential of tested thin films immersed in 1M NaCl+1M 
H2SO4 solution. In the three electrode system used in the potentiodynamic 
polarization test, the specimen was the working electrode, with platinum wire 
as the counter electrode and saturated calomel as the reference electrode. 
The potentiodynamic polarization curves were obtained by scanning from 250 
mVSCE below OCP to 1600 mVSCE, at a scanning rate of 1 mV⋅s-1. The surface 
morphologies of the specimens following wear corrosion testing were 
examined by scanning electron microscopy (SEM).

3.4 Wear- corrosion properties 
Wear-corrosion tests were conducted to evaluate further the wear-corrosion resistance of the DLC 
nitride thin films. Fig. 6 plots wear corrosion current density versus applied potentials  for all specimens 
immersed in 4L 1M NaCl+1M H2SO4 solution. The current density in all specimens exhibits a similar 
tendancy, which increases with the potential. The result implies that with increasing corrosion rate 
accerlates the wear action, resulting in a quickly increase of the wear rate [25-28]. The drop in the wear 
corrosion current density of both 2.5 and 3.0 nm films in the anodic passive region is assumed to be 
caused by corrosion protection by a passive film. Moreover, with increasing film thickness causes the 
pass ive  f i lm  to  adhere  e f fec t i ve ly  and  min im izes  the  cor ros ion  cur ren t  dens i t y.
