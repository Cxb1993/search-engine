四、結果與討論 
依本研究設定之避難場所為辦公室而言，屬於避難時間較為短暫之人員工作場所，於
「各類場所消防安全設備設置標準」中，被歸類於乙類場所，內部避難時間經簡易二層法
計算約為 84 秒，因此本研究之採樣與紀錄時主要觀察為 180 秒以內之結果，並加以分析。 
進行一系列之實驗過程後，首先針對傳統式與新式排煙系統運轉之熱煙試驗結果比
對。由透過分割影像記錄系統可明顯看出實驗過程中於煙霧試驗後 60 秒時，由於 NY11 
（1.0CMM/m2 排煙風量）、NY21 （0.8CMM/m2 排煙風量）與 NY31 （0.5CMM/m2 排煙
風量）之新式排煙系統，由於天花板上方有蓄煙空間，所以煙層下降速度較為緩慢。 
120 秒時 TY10 之傳統排煙系統的煙層已開紿下降且有亂流情形發生。圖 7、8、分別
可看出在 180 秒後 TY10 之傳統排煙系統煙層已經瀰漫整個房間，而 NY11 （1.0CMM/m2
排煙風量）之新式排煙系統仍然將煙層控制在天花板下方附近，因此在高度 1.8m 高度處，
人員依然有良好之避難環境。

If the RTI value is relatively large, the thermal 
sensitivity of the sprinkler head toward the 
surrounding heat flow is slower. [14,15], Therefore 
some time is required for the sprinkler system to start 
operating after the fire source has been ignited. The 
factors that affect the RTI value include: the airflow 
velocity in the high temperature, the convection heat 
transfer coefficient, the mass of the thermal detection 
component, the specific heat and the surface 
area[16,17].  
Thus, installation at a different position would affect 
the convection heat transfer coefficient (h) and the 
airflow velocity (V), which primarily will affect the 
RTI value. This research analyzed these two 
parameters and its effect on the sensitivity of sprinkler 
head. 
 
FIRE SIMULATION ANALYSIS 
The fire scene was simulated using the analytical 
simulation software Fire Dynamic Simulator (FDS) in 
Computational Fluid Dynamic (CFD) mode. CFD was 
developed by the National Institute of Standards and 
Technology/Building and Fire Research Laboratory 
(NIST/BFRL) in the USA. FDS is a set of fire 
simulation software, adopting Large Eddy Simulation 
(LES). It is able to simulate the air flow phenomena 
actuated by buoyancy force [18]. The simulation 
space is an office. 
3. Full Scale experiments 
The full-scale experiment was carried out in a space 
with a length of 10.4m, a width of 9.6m and a height 
of 2.7m. The experimental space was an office room. 
The fuel was placed in the central of office and 
combusted for 360s. To understand the tendency of 
temperature distribution inside the space after the fire, 
this full-scale experiment was carried out in order to 
measure the fuel combustion practically. The 
full-scale experiment was adopted, referring to 
Australian Standards[19], “AS 4391-1999, Smoke 
management systems-Hot smoke test” with an A3 size 
of fire tray and combustion area of 0.125 m2. The 
combustion fuel was methanol with purity of 98% and 
its capacity was 1.0 liters. 
 
RESEARCH PROCEDURE 
This research focused on the thermal sensitivity of the 
sprinkler head, positioned at a height of 2.2m when 
the fire source was placed at horizontally distances of 
1.0, 2.1, 2.3 and 2.6m away from the sprinkler head. 
As the height of the experimental space was 2.7m, an 
absorber plate must be installed. We analyzed whether 
the establishment of an absorber plate would affect 
the thermal sensing of the sprinkler head.

high thermal detector in the sprinkler head. This 
research simulated the temperature variation around
