Abstract 
 
This proposal applied organic electrolytes preparation of titania nanotubes array 
in dye sensitized solar cells (Dye-Sensitized Solar Cells, DSSCs) by anodization 
method. Find the preparation of titania nanotubes array optimal conditions via H3PO4 
/ NH4F, ethylene glycol / H2O / NH4F and formamide / H2O / NH4F electrolyte, 
different of F- electrolyte concentration and the water content of titania nanotube 
array morphology impact. 
   Experimental results show that the anodic oxidation time 8 h, applied voltage 
is 40 V, NH4F concentration of 0.3M in the H3PO4 / NH4F electrolyte, its inner 
diameter is 62 nm and the tube length is 0.75 μm; in formamide / H2O / NH4F 
electrolyte 0.5 wt% NH4F and 2 vol% H2O, its inner diameter is 93 nm and the tube 
length was 12.031 μm; in ethylene glycol / H2O / NH4F electrolyte 0.3 wt% NH4F 
and 2 vol% H2O, its inner diameter is 85 nm and the tube length was 15.69 μm. 
ethylene glycol / H2O / NH4F electrolyte preparation TiO2 nanotubes arranged in a 
more compact and uniform. 
Keywords: anodization method, titania nanotube array, dye-sensitized solar cells.

二、實驗方法	 	
2-1 基材前處理 
    本研究使用純度 99.5 %的鈦片，厚度約為 0.25 ㎜。先將鈦片剪裁成 2 × 2.5
㎝ 2大小的長方形，將裁好的鈦片分別以清潔劑、丙酮、乙醇、去離子水及硝酸
溶液，超音波震盪 20 分鐘，最後再以去離子水超音波震盪 20 分鐘，即完成試
片清潔工作。 
2-2 陣列式二氧化鈦奈米管的製備 
    本研究採用兩極式電化學陽極氧化法製備陣列式 TiO2奈米管。將清洗過的
鈦片做為陽極與 keithley 2400 電源供應器連接，陰極為白金片，兩電極面對面
置於反應槽中，相距約為 1 cm。攪拌速率為 150 rpm，分別放入兩種不同的電解
液中，以定電壓 40 V，氧化時間為 8h，溫度控制在室溫。將反應後的陽極氧化
試片以去離子水於超音波震盪 5 分鐘，震盪完後放置於烘箱乾燥。此步驟是為
了將粘稠的電解液沖洗乾淨，減少雜質殘留。最後於高溫爐中 500 ºC 煆燒 2 h
（升溫速率為 10 ºC/min），如圖 1實驗流程圖。

四、結論 
成功使用陽極氧化法製備出二氧化鈦奈米管其結構具有高度整齊排列的奈
米管陣列。經實驗得到在乙二醇電解液中將條件設定:反應電壓 40 V，0.3 wt% 
NH4F + 2 vol% H2O + 乙二醇，氧化 8 h，煆燒 500 ºC 恆溫 2 h，升溫速率 10 
ºC/min，可以得到形貌最佳之陣列式 TiO2奈米管。

ABSTRACT
Highly-ordered TiO2 nanotube arrays were successfully prepared by electrochemical
anodization in formamide based electrolyte containing 0.5 wt% NH4F and 2 vol% H2O
at 30 V for 24 h. The effects of calcining temperature, calcining time and heating rate
on formation of TiO2 nanotube arrays were investigated in detail. The morphological
changes and phase transformation of TiO2 nanotubes were analyzed by X-ray
diffraction, scanning electron microscopy and transmission electron microscopy. It is
found that calcined temperature and calcined time determine crystal phase, while
heating rate is only beneficial for crystallinity. For application to dye sensitized solar
cells, maximum conversion efficiency was achieved at 500 ℃ for 2 h with 10℃/min
heating rate, which is ascribed to the high anatase crystallinity and fewer surface
defects of nanotubes. The optimum calcination conditions help to retard the electron
recombination rate to increase Voc, and allow higher dye absorption to increase Jsc.

Conclusions
When calcination temperatures increased to 800 ℃ or annealed
times achieved at 12 h, the TiO2 nanotubes are destroyed, and only
rutile phase were observed. For application to dye-sensitized solar
cells, the highest conversion efficiency is obtained under calcined at
500℃ for 2 h, with 10℃/min heating rate.
With increasing calcination temperature from 300 ℃ to 500 ℃ or
annealed times up to 2 h, the conversion efficiency of dye-sensitized
solar cells were enhanced due to formation high anatase crystallinity ,
and then decreased sharply due to presence rutile phase.
