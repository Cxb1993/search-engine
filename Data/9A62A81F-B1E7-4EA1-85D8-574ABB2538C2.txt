中文摘要 
廢水處理所產生的污泥數量極為龐大，其減量或進而資源化為目前國際性之課題。台
灣地區污水下水道之普及為國家重要之永續指標，其所產生之生質污泥也需給予適當利
用，方合乎永續發展之國際潮流。生質污泥進行厭氧處理可以產生氫，而氫燃燒不會形成
溫室氣體 CO2，是所謂的乾淨能源。此外，從可產生充足氫量供燃料電池使用之應用觀點
而言，厭氧醱酵產氫之速率快，已經被評估是最具開發價值之生物產氫程序(Levin et al., 
2004)。因此，基於可處理廢棄物及產生能源之永續考量，申請人係以多年的厭氧生物處理
經驗為基礎，探討廢棄活性污泥(WAS)之裂解效率及其適合反應之厭氧菌的生長需求及操
作環境特性，以研發生質污泥再能源化產氫之技術。 
本計畫第一年探討污泥經鹼前處理，於中溫(35℃)厭氧微生物分解廢棄活性污泥的產
氫行為。由污泥前處理鹼劑濃度試驗，尋求較適合進行醱酵作用的溶出物濃度及比例，並
利用植種來源的選擇，找尋適當微生物族群。分別進行 So/Xo 因子探討，藉由初步研究評
量廢棄生活污泥能源化之可行性。第二年利用鹼前處理，破壞 WAS 中的細胞，使其醣類及
蛋白質溶出，進一步利用溶出物質作為培養產氫菌之碳源及氮源。以前的實驗是利用鹼前
處理污泥進行產氫，在培養前將 pH 調整至偏酸性條件，此法使用龐大藥劑，且在操作上
十分複雜及耗時。利用高 pH 環境進行產氫試驗，不但能有效減少鹼劑的使用，且操作簡
單省時。由於 WAS 不易被微生物分解，若能與易分解之有機質進行共醱酵，則可望增加其
分解性並藉此增進能源之產生量；故第三年以 WAS 為基質，藉由有機質的添加進行共同厭
氧醱酵，期能促進 WAS 分解並產生氫氣，並探求各種有機質混合比例對生物產能之影響。 
依據實驗結果得知，黎明污泥菌種有較廣的產氫 pH 範圍，有利於後續系統的選
擇；當 So/Xo 為 0.3，VSS 降解情形嚴重，係因系統未能提供足夠基質導致微生物解體；
最大產氫量為 3.8 mL，耗氫較不明顯，此結果可作為調控基質進料比例的下限。在前處
理方面，鹼前處理污泥比其離心液有更高的產氫量，其遲滯期也較其離心液短；而且，
固定 pH 攪拌污泥 30 分鐘無法有效溶出醣類及蛋白質，提高 pH 或連續添加鹼劑，則能
提升溶出效果。在菌種方面，使用酸篩菌比熱篩菌有更佳的產氫效果。

第二章 文獻探討 
2.1 前處理與基質型態對 WAS 醱酵產氫之影響  
有機物之厭氧分解可分為四個步驟：水解、酸化、乙酸化與甲烷化。大顆粒或固態
複雜有機物之生物水解效率較差，常被稱為厭氧分解之速率限制步驟(rate-limiting step; 
Tiehm et al., 2001; Wang et al., 1999；Li and Noike, 1992)。WAS 是成分複雜之生質體
(biomass) ，是不容易被生物分解(refractory)之物，因此把 WAS 進行 bioenergy production
之操作時，爲減少速率限制步驟對厭氧分解效率之影響，有必要先對 WAS 進行前處理。 
目前對 WAS 進行前處理之常用方法包括熱前處理、酸或鹼前處理、超音波前處理、
熱化學前處理…..等。Stucky and McCarty (1978)研究化學法處理 WAS，提出脂質易於酸性
或鹼性條件下水解成脂肪酸；蛋白質於酸性條件下可水解成醣類與正磷酸鹽，水解效率較
加鹼水解效率高；碳水化合物則裂解成小分子量的多醣體。酸或鹼前處理已被廣泛研究如
何改善厭氧消化效率及水解特性，如台灣大學 Wang C. C. et al. (2003)以 pH 3 (HClO4)處理
民生污水廠的廢棄活性污泥其 SCOD/COD 增加了 4~5%，可以產生 1.8 mg/g-COD 的氫氣。
Kim J. et al. (2003) 以 pH12 測試不同鹼劑 (NaOH，KOH，Mg(OH)2，Ca(OH)2)對 WAS 降
解的情形，得知 VSS 之降解最高可達 29.8%(添加 NaOH 有較佳效果)。交通大學 Lin J. G. 
et al. (1999)以 40 meq NaOH/L 前處理廢棄污泥，其 SCOD/TCOD 可增加 55% 。Chiu Y. C. 
et al. (1997)也以 40 meq NaOH/L 處理工業廢水廠污泥，其 SCOD/TCOD 增加 36.3%。類似
的研究還有國際上(Tanaka et al. (1997)；Ray et al. (1990)；Rajan et al. (1989))均報導前處理
能有效地增加水解及甲烷生成效率。 
熱前處理包括低溫處理，其範圍為 60~100℃（Hiraoka, 1984），中溫處理 100~175（Kim 
J. et al. 2003；Tanaka et al. 1997；Hiroka et al.1985）和高溫處理 175~225℃(Haung, 1983)。
超音波前處理則可打碎細胞璧溶出細胞內物質。Kim J. et al. (2003) 以 42 kHz，10-120 min 
可使 VSS 降解 38.9%。不同頻率的超音波前處理(Wang C. C. et al. (2003)用 20kHz，20min；
Wang Q. et al. (1999)用 9kHz，200W，40min；Tiehm A. et al. (1997)用 31kHz，64sec，3.6kW) 
都有相當的 VSS 降解效果。熱化學前處理也有處理效率，如 Vlyssides and Karlis (2004)以
pH 8~11 和 Temp. 50~90℃為範圍，處理 WAS 結果顯示最佳組合為 pH11 加熱至 90 ℃，可
使 VSS 降解率達 45%，溶出 COD 為 70,000 mg/L，其效率高於其他前處理方式。Tanaka S. 
et al. (1997)則以 130℃5min+0.3 g NaOH/g-VSS 處理廢棄污泥，其 VSS 降解可達 40~50%
之間。廢水處理廠之廢棄活性污泥，含水率約 99~99.5%，組成成分主要為微生物細胞，
含有 40~60%蛋白質、25~50%醣類、10 %脂質(Confer and Logan, 1997)。 
在厭氧生物水解方面較為少見，有交通大學 Lin J.G. (1999)以控制 HRT 及 pH 之方式
利用 anaerobic hydrogen reactor 對 WAS 進行液化之研究，指出 HRT 5 天以及 pH 5.0 是最
用污水污泥植種產氫之最佳範圍(Chen et al., 2002；Lin and Jo, 2003；Chang and Lin, 2004；
Lin and Lay, 2004)。因此進行 WAS 再能源化技術研發時需再對此重要之環境因子進行分
析研究。中溫厭氧發酵產氫習慣採用 30~35℃(Chang and Lin, 2004；Chang et al., 2002； Yu 
et al., 2002)，但是也有高溫厭氧可以發酵產氫之報告（Hawkes et al., 2002）。例如 Ueno at al. 
(2001)以牛肉汁和 peptone 及纖維粉末混合發酵產氫其溫度控制在 60℃；Zotemeyer et al. 
(1982)曾以 52℃研究葡萄糖之厭氧酸化也獲致不錯之成果；Yu et al. (2002)以酒廠廢水在
20~55℃探討產氫效率結果以 55℃可達較高之產氫效率。 
 
 3.2.4 菌種篩選方式對鹼前處理污泥於高 pH 環境產氫之影響  
(1)基質：取味丹及中興污泥。將污泥 pH 固定在 11±0.1 及 12±0.1 攪拌反應
12 小時。  
(2)菌種：熱篩 --取正隆紙廠污泥，經 95℃熱篩 45 分鐘，靜置 12 小時後使用。
酸篩 --取正隆紙廠污泥，將 pH 控制在 3±0.1，靜置 12 小時後使用。 
3.2.5 鹼前處理污泥及其離心液產氫之影響  
(1)基質前處理：取味丹污泥、中興污泥及黎明污泥。將污泥 pH 控制在 10±0.1
及 11±0.1 攪拌 5 分鐘後，停止添加鹼劑，並攪拌反應 12 小時。12 小時後
取其基質及其離心液進行培養。  
(2)菌種：取黎明污泥，經 95 ℃熱篩 45 分鐘，靜置 12 小時後使用。  
3.2.6 有機質、WAS 及菌種混合比例對生物產能之影響  
添加有機質及 WAS 基質體積比分別為 0： 5、 1： 4、 2： 3、 3： 2、 4： 1
及 5： 0， pH 調整至 6.7，不經任何前處理，濃度皆控制為約 20 g COD/ L。 
(1)基質：包括味丹食品之糖蜜廢液及台中縣豐原清潔隊提供之廚餘以及糖廚
餘醱酵液；糖廚餘或糖蜜可被二次利用，廚餘則為廢棄產物。WAS 來自南
投縣中興新村都市廢水終沉池污泥之生污泥。  
(2)菌種：糖蜜醱酵液進行厭氧產氫之 CSTR 醱酵出流液。  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
第 12 小時，pH 11 的都市廢水污泥較其他條件有較高的醣類溶出量，而且其 12 小時尚無

4.3 各種菌種在不同 pH 之產氫量  
 
圖三、各菌種在各 pH 之產氫量 
 
污泥水解溶出物仍屬複雜基質，並非能完全被分解；為了得到較好的醱酵產物，故
需尋求適合的菌種，因此選擇三種菌種來源進行探討。圖三為各菌種產氫情形，各菌種的
最佳產氫 pH 皆不同。在測試的菌源中，經過馴養的蔗糖菌種在 pH 5.5 時呈現最佳產氫量，
約為 30mL，但其適合的 pH 範圍狹小，易限制 pH 的選擇。黎明菌源適合產氫 pH 範圍最
大(5.5~8.0)，且產氫量在各 pH 變化較小；福田菌產氫能力不佳。依此結果，後續的批次
試驗乃利用黎明菌源探討鹼劑濃度對產氫的影響。

第五章 結論與建議 
5.1 結論  
1.黎明污泥菌種有較廣的產氫 pH 範圍，有利於後續系統的選擇。 
2.當 So/Xo 為 0.3，VSS 降解情形嚴重，係因系統未能提供足夠基質導致微生物解體。
最大產氫量為 3.8 mL，耗氫較不明顯，此結果可作為調控基質進料比例的下限。 
3.鹼前處理污泥比其離心液有更高的產氫量，其遲滯期也較其離心液短。 
4.固定 pH 攪拌污泥 30 分鐘無法有效溶出醣類及蛋白質，提高 pH 或連續添加鹼劑，
則能提升溶出效果。 
5.使用酸篩菌比熱篩菌有更佳的產氫效果。 
6.高 pH 的環境會使 CO2 溶解在污泥中，有助於氫氣的萃取。 
7.WAS 添加有機質可促進共醱酵作用。

Disclosed herein is a process for enhancing anaerobic 
biohydrogen production, comprising: 
providing a first organic waste comprising hydrogen-producing
anaerobic bacteria; 
subjecting the first organic waste to a treatment such that the 
hydrogen-producing anaerobic bacteria contained therein become 
predominant; 
subjecting a second organic waste possibly containing undesired 
hydrogen-consuming bacteria to a heat treatment at a temperature 
ranging from 40  to a temperature that does not cause the second ℃
organic waste to boil, so that the hydrogen-consuming bacteria 
contained in the second organic waste are diminished; 
adding the treated first organic waste and the heat-treated second 
organic waste into a reactor that is made anaerobic; and 
allowing the first organic waste to anaerobically ferment the 
heat-treated second organic waste, so that hydrogen gas is produced.

2. Materials and methods 
2.1. Substrate molasses  
The substrate molasses concentration was 40 g-COD/L and was prepared using condensed 
molasses fermentation soluble from a food factory (Vedan Corp, Taiwan). Condensed molasses 
fermentation soluble (360 g-COD/L) is a by-product from the microbial fermentation of molasses. 
Other than the original nutrients contained in molasses, it also has additional elements such as 
microbial protein, amino acids, organic acids, vitamins and coenzymes produced by bacteria during the 
fermentation process. No extra nutrients were added. 
 
2.2. Bioreactor system and seed microflora 
Fermentation experiments were conducted using an up-flow anaerobic fermenter (UAF) with a 
working volume of 2.0 L (interior dia. 7 cm, height 50 cm). The operating pH was 5.5. The seed 
microflora was the biomass sludge collected from a continuously-stirred tank reactor fermenter 
(CSTRF; [5]). The UAF reactor was started-up with seeding with 1 L of biomass sludge and feeding 
with 1 L of substrate molasses. The hydrogen production experiments were conducted in two stages. (1) 
At the first stage, the fermenter was fed on raw substrate molasses without pretreatment. The operating 
hydraulic retention time (HRT) varied from 8 to 0.5 h. (2) At the second stage the influent substrate 
molasses was pretreated at a temperature of 70  for 10 min. The HRT was℃  started-up at 8 h for a few 
days and then reduced to 4 h for hydrogen production. For each experimental condition at this stage, 
the fermenter was restarted-up with newly-collected seed sludge from the CSTRF.

Environmental factors for efficient fermentative hydrogen production of 
granular sludge 
 
F. Y. Chang and C. Y. Lin※ 
BioHydrogen Laboratory, Graduate Institute of Civil and Hydraulic Engineering, Feng Chia University, P.O. 
Box 25-123, Taichung 40724, Taiwan 
 
Abstract  This work used screen method and conventional experimental design to confer the 
important environmental factors for fermentative hydrogen production of granular sludge. Based on 
the accumulative hydrogen production (mL/d) and signal-to-noise ratio, the important environmental 
factors were pH and temperature; the effect of metal ions was also obvious.  Peak hydrogen 
production occurred at 100 mg-Ca2+/L with the specific hydrogen production rate 
(SHPRmax) and hydrogen yield reaching 420 ml H2/g-VSS-d and 2.72 mol H2/mol-sucrose, 
respectively. The order of SHPRmax value was Ca2+ > Al3+ > Fe2+ > Fe3+. An overly-high dosage of 
200 mg-Ca2+/L (the specific dosage 46 g/g-VSS) inhibited the bioactivities; Fe3+ was higher than 
Fe2+ in toxicity. The acidogenesis relative bioactivity increased by 210% at 200 mg-Fe2+/L. 
Keywords Environmental factors; experimental design; hydrogen production; metal 
 
 
Introduction 
Hydrogen gas is expected to become an alternative to fossil energy because it is clean, renewable 
and has a high energy yield. Biological hydrogen production processes have the advantages of 
being less energy intensive.  Fermentative biohydrogen production gives high hydrogen 
production rates and is capable of converting organic wastes into more valuable energy resources 
(Hawkes et al., 2002). Biohydrogen production reactors have been studied on various 
environmental factors by one-factor-at-a-time experiments (Bai et al., 2001; Kumar and Das, 2001; 
Zheng and Yu, 2004). Few studies used experimental designs to investigate the environmental 
factors (Lay JJ, 2000; Lin and Lay, 2004). 
The environmental factors affecting hydrogen fermentation include seed microorganisms, 
temperature, pH, metal and toxic matter (Hawkes et al., 2002; Kumar and Das, 2001).  Microbial 
metabolism and growth rate were affected by pH variations. Dabrock et al. (1992) studied the effect 
of pH on growth and product formation; the results showed that growth started to decrease at pH 
below 5.5. The suitable pH range was from 5.5-7.0, which had slight effect on the shift of soluble 
microbial products (Fang and Liu, 2002). The culture temperature affects the growth rate and 
metabolic activity of bacteria. Most of the biohydrogen-producing reactors were operated at 
mesophilic (35℃) or thermophilic (50℃) temperatures. Metal ions play an important role in 
microbial aggregation in granule reactor system. However, the impact of toxic metal has always 
been a potential problem for the operation of anaerobic digesters.  Heavy metals are one group of 
toxins which have received considerable attention, but this has mainly been related to methane 
production systems; few reports have considered the biohydrogen production system (Zheng and 
Yu, 2004).  
In this work, we used experimental design to screen fifteen environmental factors to find the 
important ones, and used a conventional experimental method to investigate the effect of metal ions. 
Furthermore, suitable metal ions were used to form granule but avoiding toxicity. 
 
Materials and Methods 
 
Screen method experiment 
Biomass   
Two seeds were obtained from a municipal sewage treatment plant and a paper wastewater 
treatment plant. They were collected from final sedimentation tanks and then screened with sieve 
No. 8 (2.35 mm) to eliminate large particulate materials. Before being seeded into the serum vials, 
the seed sludge was heat-treated at 100℃ for 45 min to inhibit the methane-producing bacteria 
activity.

The order of SHPRmax values was Ca2+>Al3+>Fe2+>Fe3+. Dabrock et al. (1992) reported that iron

A. C. VFA (%) = (Vm/Vc)*100%

Conclusions  
1. pH and temperature were important environmental factors, but the effect of 
calcium, aluminum, and iron on hydrogen product ion was also obvious. 
2. The hydrogen yield peaked at calcium 100mg/L with 2.7 mol-H2/mol-sucrose.  
3. The order of Rm and SHPRmax values was Ca2+ > Al3+ > Fe2+ > Fe3+.
