在全球激烈競爭與經濟分工下，企業「瘦身」僅保留部分核心員工，而將外圍
業務改由派遣人員提供的風潮已經瀰漫全球且已行之有年。以萬寶華（Manpower）
企管顧問公司來說，每年平均在全球六十一個國家中，派遣二百七十多萬名員工，
勝過 IBM、微軟等國際大型企業，而成為全球最大雇主。且根據 2003 年的調查數
據，在美國每天人力派遣公司聘僱的派遣人才達到 290 萬人，且過去五年之中，
人力派遣公司創造了約 100 萬個新工作機會。且其中約 79%的派遣人力派給全職工
作，此與企業一般正職員工的工作型態相同。同時，全球人力派遣市場創下一百
七十億美元的營業額，歐洲各國採用『非典型勞力』平均約佔總體勞動市場之十
五%，荷蘭更高達五十%，反觀國內，台灣勞動人口約有一千萬人，但國內企業使
用短期派遣人力僅約十餘萬人，包括科技廠商、金融機構、以及各種客服中心等
企業，然而，據人力派遣公司調查，卻約有三分之一企業願意使用派遣人力，且
國內現已成立五、六十家人力派遣公司，因此，待『勞動派遣法』立法通過後，
未來國內企業『要派』人力尚有很大的成長空間。由此可見，此種用人趨勢不但
方興未艾且有持續擴大之勢。 
 
然而，人力派遣雖可能解決企業短期人力缺口，但當景氣趨向熱絡、或業務或
投資計畫非預期擴張時，亦可能面臨派遣人力短缺和/或人力派遣費用大幅上揚的
風險，由於選擇權本身的避險設計特性，且具有遞延決策時點的功效，因而人力
派遣選擇權應可做為規避此一風險之有效工具。此外，為進一步提昇企業人力需
求彈性、延長人力需求決策時點以爭取決策時間及降低避險成本，本研究於是提
出人力派遣複合選擇權之創新構想，將複合選擇權運用到人力派遣契約，建立一
套選擇權式的人力外包契約模型，主要基於人力派遣複合選擇權除了具備人力派
遣選擇權的避險功能外，更能彈性因應與有效規避未來業務計畫或投資計畫，因
景氣波動所造成人力需求的不確定性風險，以協助相關企業作成正確的人力資源
決策，簡言之，人力派遣選擇權主要可用於規避未來人力短缺及派遣價格不確定
風險，而人力派遣複合選擇權除了可規避此兩風險外，亦能規避未來投資計畫或
業務計畫變更(撤消、擴張或緊縮)所造成之人力需求的不確定風險。

人力派遣複合選擇權應可適用於所有欲聘雇派遣人力且具高度人力需求波動
性的產業，包括製造業與服務業。以下試舉一例說明人力派遣複合選擇權的使用
時機，以半導體產業為例，由於預期未來半導體產業景氣將會復甦，假定台積電
欲擴大其十二吋晶圓廠的投資計畫，且一旦投資計畫付諸實行，勢必須引進大量
的製程設備，並立即需要相當數量的機台維護人員，為節省人力成本，台積電打
算使用派遣人力。假定台灣應用材料公司為半導體設備專業維護人員的派遣公
司，專門供應台灣眾多半導體製造公司之製程設備維護人員，此一情況下，台積
電公司即可與台灣應用材料公司簽訂一份人力派遣複合選擇權契約，並支付權利
金，以預先取得與確保投資計畫中所需的機台維護派遣人力。隨著時間過去，當
資訊更趨明朗，並評估景氣狀況將不如預期，因而很可能放棄投資計畫時，台積
電公司即可選擇放棄履約，此時僅損失當初所支付的權利金。反之，若相當確定
半導體景氣將趨暢旺，因而很可能執行投資計畫時，台積電公司即可要求履約並
與台灣應用材料公司簽定另一份人力派遣選擇權，當人力派遣複合選擇權契約到
期，且台積電公司決定啟動投資計畫時，此時，台積電公司即可要求履約並與台
灣應用材料公司簽定另一份人力派遣契約。由此例可知，藉由運用人力派遣複合
選擇權，台積電公司即可成功規避未來投資計畫、計畫需求人力及派遣費用的不
確定風險。

and Folta (2002) 曾以實質選擇權 (real option) 分析法分析人力派遣之彈性價值，
其認為當企業面對的不確定性愈大、勞動市場愈嚴格、正式職員的薪資及福利愈
好的情況下，運用派遣人力將可增加投資擴張或緊縮的機會。Pinker and Larson 
(2003)更直接指出，不論外在環境是否變動，當企業需求不確定時，就可運用暫時
性人力，故全球使用暫時性人力的比率有逐年上升的趨勢。以上學者的論點與本
研究之研究動機與目標可謂不謀而合，透過人力派遣複合選擇權將可進一步提高
人力資源策略彈性。 
 
2.2 複合選擇權的應用 
自從 Geske (1979) 提出複合選擇權評價模式後，使得許多的財務與方案評估
等問題，可以更接近事實的模式作為分析及評價的基礎，以下即列舉出與複合選
擇權相關的若干應用文獻。McConnell and Schallheim (1983)曾將複合選擇權應用在
租賃合約的評價上，此兩學者嘗試推衍出一個以複合選擇權為基礎的架構，以分
別對七種不同的租賃合約進行評價，其研究結果認為標準的租賃合約本質上其實
就是一個複合選擇權。Boot, et al. (1983) 曾對複合買權的定價模型進行大規模實證研
究。Cortazar and Schwartz (1993) 將複合選擇權應用於實質資產之評價上，該研究
假設企業之生產流程為兩階段式，試圖去建立一個複合選擇權評價模式，以協助
解決一個有產能瓶頸的企業其在產能及生產率方面的問題。 Francesco (2001)應用
複合選擇權在評估道德風險，他認為在公司裡股東與管理者之間存在著很大的利
益衝突，可以藉由建立一個像是混合了絕對及相對績效指標的多重可能性選擇權 
(multicontingency option) 的獎金制度來大幅地降低。另外，Cassimon et al. (2004) 亦曾
將 6 階段複合選擇權應用於評估新藥之開發價值。 
許亮嵐 (1991)曾將複合選擇權應用於存款保險費率之訂定上，該研究選取
1986 年至 1991 年之三商銀（彰化銀行、第一銀行、華南銀行）及新竹區中小企業
銀行等四家銀行為樣本，進行分析。主要計算下列三項保險費率值，其分別為五
年期美式賣權之保險費率、三年期複合賣權之保險費率以及單純一期歐式賣權之
保險費率。在到期日期間不超過五年之假設下，五年期美式賣權之保險費率與三
年期複合賣權之保險費率可視為的保險費率的理想上、下限。然而，在考量相關
費用或成本後，此適當的費率實質上亦應介於二者之間。研究結果提出複合賣權
才是存款保險合約的本質。

5. 結論與建議 
由於人力派遣有助於企業降低企業人事成本、提高勞動彈性，亦可透過專業分工的方
式，聘請專技人才從事專業工作，使公司能專注於發展核心能力，建立競爭優勢，因此，
人力派遣型態的企業人力資源供需模式已日漸盛行，且因景氣循環波動加劇及競爭日益激
烈所帶來的未來業務/投資計畫的不確定性持續升高，此一情況連帶使得未來人力需求的不
確定性風險增加，本研究提出將複合選擇權運用在人力派遣交易機制上的創新性構想，複
合選擇權具有延後決策時點、提昇決策彈性及降低避險成本的優點，因而建議可透過在派
遣公司與要派企業間發行人力派遣複合選擇權，以同時規避要派企業未來業務/投資計畫變
更、人力短缺及人力派遣價格上漲的不確定性風險，用以協助對景氣波動敏感度高之相關
企業，建立一套能靈活運用以降低企業經營風險的新型人力外包合約。 
  本研究中除了提出行人力派遣複合選擇權的發行構想與發行機制外，亦說明了人力派
遣複合選擇權的評價方法，並進行人力派遣複合選擇權的實證研究與分析，根據所選取之
三纇製造業的研究實證結果可得知人力派遣複合選擇權的有效性與實用性，以及避險成本
的合理性。同時，藉由敏感度分析可瞭解人力派遣複合買權權利金對主要參數變動的影響
程度，分析結果可知權利金與標的資產價格波動率(人力派遣價格波動率)、標的資產價格(人
力派遣價格)及合約期間長短三者皆呈現正向變動關係，但就影響程度高低來看，依序為標
的資產的價格波動率、標的資產的價格、人力派遣複合買權合約期間及人力派遣買權合約
期間，因此，敏感度分析結果應可提供給人力派遣複合選擇權合約之買賣雙方當進行合約
條款與規格設計時的參考依據。

In nature, compound options is the optional contract for demanded items ’ optional contract, therefore 
it is also called “The optional contract of options”, depending on sales options of the target items and its 
differences, it can be separate into “a call on a call”, “ a put on a call”, “a call on a put” or “a put on a put”. 
Compound options provides risk avoiders the options to enter either “actual risk prevention” or “certain 
risk prevention” therefore it is suitable for situations where one is unsure “whether or not to accept the 
risks at the moment”. The questions that this research sets out to solve is similar to this situation and 
therefore the application of compound options should be able to appropriately resolve the problem of 
uncertain human resource demands for associated businesses. Furthermore, to satisfy the propose of risk 
prevention and avoid as much as possible the interferences to human resource agencies and dispatch 
projects, this research suggests using “European compound option on temporary workers”, where the 
target item for compound options on temporary workers is the options on temporary workers, this means 
that after the buyer of compound options on temporary workers contract (companies demanding labor 
services) pays the fees to the sellers (temporary workers agency), they are entitled the rights to demand 
the seller to provide another copy of optional temporary workers contract with the promised contract price 
(the optional temporary workers fee) within a set date. This contractual amount is the wage that the buyer 
agrees to give to the seller for a unit of labor provided and it is effective as soon as the compound option 
is issued. Usually it can be set to be to the human resource market price level during the time of issue, 
which is also the “at the money option”, therefore we can see that compound options on temporary 
workers can avoid the risk of uncertain human resource demand and worker price simultaneously. Also, 
when options on temporary workers contract is due, it transfers into temporary worker contract and actual 
cash flow intake/outtake usually happens when appointed jobs are complete or paid in installments during 
employment, therefore the compound options on temporary workers are similar to option on future. 
Furthermore, the maturity date of the options on temporary worker’s contract can be set to approximately 
the date when workers are needed.  In general, the fees for compound options on temporary workers are significantly lower than the 
target options therefore it has the effect of lower risk costs and as mentioned previously, compound 
options provides the risk preventers the choice to enter “actual risk prevention” or “certain risk 
prevention”. Using compound options on temporary workers as an example, the following strategic 
policy stages are possible in the future: 
 1.) The buyer and seller signs the compound options on temporary workers contract and the buyer pay 
the seller a specific amount of fees. When the specific time period of the initial stage completes 
and the economical or sales growth are not as expected, the buyer will choose to forfeit its right 
to fulfill the contract and lose only the fees paid at the beginning. In contrary, when the economy 
or sales growth is as expected, the buyer will choose to fulfill its contract and requests the buyer 
to sign an option on temporary workers with the promised price and the buyer will pay the seller 
the agreed fees for options on temporary workers and acquire the right to enter the next stage of 
waiting. 
2.) When the future second stage completes and the economical or sales growth are not as expected but 
the market price for temporary workers are lower than the contracted prices for options on 
temporary workers, the buyer will still choose to forfeit its right to fulfill the contract because 
they are capable of sourcing other resources of labor with lower costs. In contrast, when both of 
the above are satisfied, the buyer will choose to negotiate a labor contract and acquire the 
necessary labor quantity with contracted price. 
We can see from the previous statements that when choosing to fulfill the contract using compound 
options on temporary workers, the fees associated with compound options on temporary workers plus 
options on temporary workers will be higher than simply using options on temporary workers. In another 
words, when the buyer choose to forfeit its right to fulfill the contract when it is due, the compound 
options fee will be far less than the options on temporary workers fee. Therefore when assessing whether 
to use compound options or just options, the company should consider the risk level of future “certain risk 
prevention. When the risk potential is high, they should choose options; when it is low, they should use 
compound options. This research offers the concept of compound options and also the appropriate 
method of assessment.

the labor agency to negotiate more effective contract with risk prevention ability and flexibility. Through 
the assistance of compound options, the company should be able to solve the future economical
