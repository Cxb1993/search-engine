一、中文摘要
因相對於傳統致動器，壓電致動器具有更高的能量密度，因此在過去十多年壓電致動
器被眾多學者專家及研究單位來進行研發，並應用在多種不同的領域。壓電致動器有多種不
同的型態，如積層型、單膜片彎曲型、雙膜片彎曲型、RAINBOW 型、 THUNDER 型、Moonie
型、Cymbal 型、雙膜片雙放大器型等。其中 Cymbal 型因具有高有效壓電係數、高輸出力、
高靜水壓優值、高位移、高加速靈敏度及體小量輕等優點，因此其用途十分廣泛。
本計畫主要探討壓電陶瓷無鉛材料，以研發出高平面耦合因數及高壓電常數的應用-壓電
致動器(actuator)。本計劃選定 (Na0.5K0.5)NbO3- (Bi0.5Na0.5)TiO3 壓電陶瓷材料為主系統，所
開發出的壓電陶瓷無鉛材料系統，將藉由軟性掺雜、硬性掺雜或加入穩定劑來產生液相燒結
成份，藉以降低燒結溫度，並提升機電耦合因數及介電性質，這些都是本計畫要探討之處。
關鍵字: 致動器、壓電陶瓷 、燒結、極化、煆燒
Abstract
Piezoelectric actuators have been heavily researched in the past decade because of their high
power density compared with conventional actuators. Piezoelectric actuators are being investigated
for use in a variety of areas. Piezoelectric actuators have many types, such as stacks, unimorph
bender, bimorph bender, RAINBOW, THUNDER, Moonie, Cymbal, bimorph-based
double-amplifier etc. In which, the Cymbal type has demonstrated high effective piezoelectric
coefficient, high generative force, high hydrophone figure of merit, high displacement, high
acceleration sensitivity, light weight and compact size.
The aim of this project is to investigate high coupling factor and piezoelectric constant for unlead
ceramic actutors. The (Na0.5K0.5)NbO3- (Bi0.5Na0.5)TiO3 ceramics with an additive of doping were
investigated in this system. This project will investigate liquid phase sintering to decrease the
sintering temperature and promote the properties of the electromechanical coupling factor and the
dielectric constant.
Keywords：Actuator； Piezoelectric Ceramics；Sintering； Poling；Calcining

就是硬性摻雜時，有助於介電損失降低、機械品質因數提高，但機電耦合因數會降低、介
電常數也會降低。
