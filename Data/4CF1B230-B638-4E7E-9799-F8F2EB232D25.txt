the solar irradiance. Afterward, by adding adequate 
aluminum contents into the barrier layers, the 
polarization-induced (including both piezoelectric 
and spontaneous polarization) charges at the 
heterointerfaces caused enhanced built-in electric 
fields in the active layer, preventing photogenerated 
carriers from being scattered or recombined by the 
charged defect states in InGaN/sapphire compounds. To 
improve material qualities, the patterned sapphire 
substrates (PSSs) were used to deposit InGaN 
epitaxial layers. By decreasing the influences of 
nonradiation recombination from charge-related defect 
states, the open-circuit voltage of solar cell 
devices could be dramatically enhanced, and hence the 
power conversion efficiency. InGaN photovoltaic 
characteristics at high-concentrated solar irradiance 
(AM1.5G solar spectrum, 100 mW/cm2) were also 
studied. Solar cell devices with blue-band GaN/InGaN 
multiple-quantum-well (EL peak wavelength at 448 nm) 
absorption layers exhibited a high open-circuit 
voltage value (2.49 V) and a high fill factor (0.71) 
at 100-sun concentrated conditions, corresponding to 
a power conversion efficiency of 2.23%. Although the 
InGaN-based solar cells were in their infancy and 
still limited in material qualities, our research 
provided that InGaN-based solar cells have excellent 
potentials in the high-concentrated operation, 
providing basic knowledge and future study in this 
field.

摘要 
本研究中，我們利用有機金屬氣相磊晶法（Metal-organic vapor-phase 
epitaxy）在(0001)藍寶石（sapphire, Al2O3）基板上沉積氮化銦鎵（InXGa1-XN）
系列太陽能電池磊晶結構，初期針對磊晶材料特性與太陽能電池元件作一系列
的分析與探討。目前三族氮化物因技術及成本考量係將磊晶層沉積在晶格不匹
配的藍寶石基板之上，由於磊晶層中氮化鎵與藍寶石基板間在應力釋放後會產
生大量的晶格缺陷而無法產生光伏（photovoltaic, PV）效應。因此元件設計上，
為了避免過厚 InGaN 塊材吸收層超過其材料臨界厚度而崩解的情形，我們首
先利用短週期的 GaN/InGaN 超晶格（superlattice）主動層來吸收太陽光；隨後，
我們在超晶格結構中加入適當含量的 AlGaN，利用其自發與壓電極化現象來
提高 InGaN 電池的內建電場，可減少主動層中光載子被高密度缺陷所散射或
復合的機率，進而提升元件效率；為了改善磊晶品質，我們更進一步將 InGaN
電池結構成長於圖形化藍寶石基板（patterned sapphire substrate, PSS）之上，
隨著材料內部非輻射復合機制減少的影響，由於漏電流影響減少，元件在光電
轉換特性上開路電壓因而大幅提升；我們亦研究 InGaN 電池在高聚光條件下
的光電特性，利用 InGaN/GaN 多重量子井（multiple-quantum well, MQW）為
吸收層之 InGaN 電池結構，在 100 倍太陽光（AM1.5G 頻譜，100 mW/cm2）
聚光強度下，光電轉換特性表現於開路電壓與填充因子分別高達 2.49 V 與 0.71，
其光電轉換效率可達 2.23%，是目前在藍光波段 InGaN 電池中的最佳表現。雖
然目前 InGaN 太陽能電池研究仍在萌芽起步階段，我們證實其在高聚光操作
條件下具有相當好的發展性，並提供未來該領域的基本知識。 
 
一、背景簡介 
  隨著 2002 年以來半導體材料氮化銦(InN)被確認其能隙約落在約 0.7 電子

計從 0.7 eV紅外光波段到 3.4 eV紫外光波段能隙最佳化的串接式(tandem)光伏
元件，此單一材料系統三族氮化物(InXGa1-XN)便廣泛預測來作為全波段太陽能
電池［1-3］。我們熟知的單一接面太陽能電池在熱力學限制之下的轉換效率
為 31%［4］，然而，在照射 200 倍集光系統的標準頻譜之下，雙層串接是太
陽能電池在堆疊的上半部高能隙的子電池(1.68 eV)與下半部較低能隙的子電
池(1.1 eV)，理論上可以達到 38%的轉換效率［5］，而這樣的結構設計即可用
單一材料系統 InXGa1-XN 半導體來完成。InXGa1-XN 化合物半導體同時具備直

中內建電場之研究 
  儘管 InGaN 材料的高線缺陷密度(1×108到 1×1012 cm-2) 並不敏感到影響藍
綠光發光元件的發光特性，然而材料品質對於光伏元件的應用卻嚴苛受限於晶

10%，為了增加載子蒐集能力，元件電極配置為 p 與 n 電極互相交錯的指叉狀

情況下，所量得的 VOC值會比在一倍太陽光下的所量得的值要來得小，這是由

（subcell）中穿隧接面（tunneling junction）所影響。在單接面 InGaN 太陽能
電池元件中，InGaN/GaN 系列的 p-i-n 異質結構有較大的能帶不連續（band 
discontinuity）和低導電率的 p-GaN 接觸層，因而導致較高的 RS。另外，氮化
物材料的穿隧接面對於未來 InGaN/GaN 系列太陽能電池元件的發展是非常重
要的，然而這個領域目前仍處於發展初期的萌芽階段。除了 RS 相關議題外，
磊晶的材料品質也非常的重要。在傳統的太陽能電池元件中（如
InGaP/GaAs/Ge），半導體磊晶層間的晶格不匹配程度並不大。一般而言砷化鎵
系列太陽能電池元件中的缺陷密度夠低，所以能夠有良好的特性表現。而三族
氮化物系列太陽能電池元件成長在藍寶石基板上受到與藍寶石之間相當大的
晶格不匹配影響，進而導致高密度的結構缺陷（例如：線缺陷），這大大降低
了 InGaN 太陽能電池元件的圓鍵操作特性。在二極體元件中，從電流與電壓

Future ultra-high efficiency (>50%) and full-solar-spectrum-absorbed tandem solar cells are possibly 
achieved by the group III-nitride InXGa1-XN, which has tunable bandgap from 0.7 to 3.4 eV, high absorption 
coefficients (>10
5
 cm
-1
) and excellent irradiance resistance.
1-2 
It has drawn much attention recently.
3-5
 Due to 
the unique quantum-dot-like localized-states in indium-rich regions, InGaN has prospective emission 
properties in laser and light-emitting diodes and shows little sensitivity to structural defects.
6-7 
In a 
photovoltaic (PV) device, the separation of photocarriers is based on the built-in electric-field (Ebi) within 
junctions. However, lattice–mismatched hetero-epitaxy (eg., GaN/sapphire) results in high background 
concentrations and threading dislocations (TDs, usually 1×108 – 1×1012 cm-2) that might become the 
photocarrier killers and thereby degrade the power conversion in present InGaN PV devices.
3-5
  
To identify the transit of photocarriers, photocarrier 
generation from the active layer to external circuits were 
studied. We simultaneously prepared both fabrication 
processes in lateral and vertical type arrangement (see 
Fig. 1) of InGaN PV devices. The epitaxial structure, 
grown on c-face sapphire substrates by an MOVPE 
reactor, was depicted in figure 1. The main absorption 
layer was a 10-pair undoped InGaN/GaN (2 nm/14 nm; 
EL peak position at 446 nm) multiple quantum well 
structure. The fabrication of lateral device is similar to 
our previous work.
5 
 
Figure 2 shows the solar response of both devices 
performed under AM1.5G test conditions. In conventional 
InGaN/sapphire devices, the Ohmic contact is laterally 
fabricated. Once the non-conducting sapphire is removed 
by laser-liftoff and bonded to conducting carriers (eg., Si), 
the photocarrier transit distance could be greatly 
shortened from, at least, hundreds of microns to 
bonding-type devices with only several microns. This is a 
dominant factor that photocarriers could be easily 
scattered or recombined in longer distance with high TD 
density epitaxy. Besides, the resistance is proportional to 
transit lengths and the reciprocal of cross-sectional area. 
This is responsible for the reduced series resistance (RS) 
in the vertical-type InGaN PV device. In vertical-type 
fabrication, however, several minor drawbacks should be 
carefully concerned, including blue-shifted absorption layer,
8
 strain-relaxed generation of TDs and reduced 
polarization-related Ebi. More detailed results will be discussed and presented in the near future.
