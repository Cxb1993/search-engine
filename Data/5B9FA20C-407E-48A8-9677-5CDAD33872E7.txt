摘要
本研究旨在藉由改善壓接鉗的設計，提升此類手工具在進行壓接端子作業的績效及
品質。市面常見之壓接鉗其握距高達為24公分，遠大於石裕川等人在1996的研究所建議
的最大握距(13公分)，若握距在不能縮小的情況下，本研究希望藉由增加壓接鉗下手把的
面積，並調整使用者壓接動作，開發新型改善壓接鉗。因此本研究採用「兩因子完全隨
機化集區設計」之實驗設計方式，藉由在兩種工作平面下(桌面及牆面)，探討壓接鉗設計
（3個水準，無輔助握把，3 cm 及5.5cm輔助握把）在兩種常見線材直徑（細2 mm 及粗
5.5 mm）的使用績效及品質(壓線總數及不良總數)，結果發現無論是粗線或是細線，桌
面壓線總數優於牆面。為減少壓接不良，在桌面進行壓接作業時應使用5.5cm寬輔助握把
壓接箝；而在牆面進行壓接作業時，則使用3cm寬輔助握把之壓接鉗。
關鍵詞：手工具設計，非動力手工具，壓接鉗
Abstract
The study aims at to increase the efficiency and quality of terminals crimping job
through the improved design of terminals crimping tool. The handgrip span of the tool is 24cm
which is much longer than 13 cm which is suggested by Shih et al.(1996). The improved design
will increase the area of lower handle or adjust the movement of users if the handgrip span cannot
be reduced. Hence, the study adopts two factors completely block design to investigate the design
of terminals crimping tools (3 levels, no auxiliary handle, 3 cm or 5.5 cm wide auxiliary handles)
and two common wire diameters (2mm and 5.5mm) when working at tables or walls on terminals
crimping efficiency and quality (number completed per minute and defectives). The study founds
no matter what diameters the wire, crimping terminals at tables is more efficient than working at
walls. To reduce crimping defects, the study suggests adopting 5.5 cm wide auxiliary handles
when working at tables and 3 cm auxiliary handles when working on the walls.
Keywords: Hand Tool Design, Powerless Hand Tool, Terminals Crimping Tool
