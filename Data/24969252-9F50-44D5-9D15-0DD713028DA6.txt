二氧化鈦奈米管應用於葡萄糖感測器之研究 
“The study of titanium oxide nanotubes applied to glucose sensor” 
計畫編號：NSC100-E-211-003 
執行期間：100年 8月 1日 至 101年 9月 30日 
主持人：林智玲 華梵大學電子工程系 副教授 
1. 中文摘要 
本計畫提出以電化學陽極氧化處理的二氧化鈦奈米管作為電流式葡萄糖感測器電極薄膜，設計
實現出一高靈敏度、專一性佳及低檢測試劑的葡萄糖感測器。利用陽極氧化法，改變處理製程參數
得到具導電性、特性佳的二氧化鈦奈米管，管徑約 85~130 nm，管壁約 20 nm。。以膠體包埋法
將葡萄糖氧化酵素以膠體聚乙烯醇光聚合物固定在電極表面上，藉由酵素專一的催化能力達到對特
定物質之偵測之目的，並於膠體包埋中添加電子傳導介子，二茂鐵，以降低電極表面的氧化或還原
過電壓。葡萄糖量測呈現兩段式的反應特性，以電路架構得到的特性當葡萄糖濃度<150 (mg/dL)，
靈敏度為15.1 A/(100 mg/dL)、濃度在200~400(mg/dL)時，靈敏度為1.01 A/(100 mg/dL)。 
 
關鍵詞： 電化學陽極氧化、二氧化鈦奈米管、電流式葡萄糖感測器。 
 
2. 英文摘要 
In this proposal, titanium oxide nanotubes fabricated by electrochemical anodized technique had been 
applied to amperometric glucose sensors. Nanotubes were grown by potentiostatic anodization of titanium 
in an electrolyte of ammonium fluoride, ethyl alcohol, and high-purity deionized water (DI water). 
Nanotube samples had, respectively, inner diameters of 85~130 nm; wall thickness about 20 nm.  
The amperometric glucose biosensors will be developed by using gel entrapment to immobilize glucose 
oxidase (GOx) on a titanium oxide electrode. The glucose measurement response is based on the H2O2, 
which is produced from the reaction catalyzed by the enzyme, can reach the electrode. The H2O2 can be 
electrochemically oxidized on the sensing electrode. Therefore, the glucose concentration can be 
determined by measuring the current in the sensing electrode. However, oxygen is extremely active and 
the oxidation or reduction over-potential of H2O2 on an electrode surface is large, and electronically active 
components are generated that interfere with the reaction. Therefore, a small electron transduction 
mediator molecule, ferrocene, must be added to the enzymatic electrode to reduce the over-potential on 
the electrode.  
Experimental results exhibit that the glucose sensors show typical CV characteristics. Amperometric 
readout circuit including two operational amplifiers and a resistance was used of glucose sensors. The 
results exhibit two linear region for glucose concentrations of between 0 and 150 mg/dL, and between 200 
and 400 mg/dL, with the corresponding sensitivity are approximately 15.1 A/(100 mg/dL) and 1.01 
A/(100 mg/dL), respectively. 
 
Keywords: titanium oxide nanotubes, electrochemical anodized technique, amperometric glucose sensors.
