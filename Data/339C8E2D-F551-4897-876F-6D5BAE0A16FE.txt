 
中文摘要： 台灣掩埋場大部分以穩定期為主(掩捚齡>10 yr)，穩定期之掩埋
場滲出水中常含有難分解有機物，如腐植酸(Humic acid, HA)與
磺酸(Fulvic acid, FA)等物質。因腐植物質可能佔水中自然有機
物之 50%以上，常造成掩埋場放流水之 COD 超過法規容許值 
(COD< 200 mg/L)。此外，滲出水中之腐植物質也可能與滲出
水中重金屬形成金屬錯化物而加速重金屬於地下水之傳輸與污
染，也造成後續處理上之困擾。 
因此本計劃之主要目的為探討利用奈米雙金屬-活性碳(BM-
GAC)複合程序處理掩埋場滲出水中之有機物(腐植物質)。本計
畫為三年期計畫，依序分年探討利用 BM-GAC/O3、BM-GAC/ 
H2O2 與 BM-GAC/O3/H2O2等程序處理滲出水之可行性。本研
究共製備三類 BM-GAC催化劑(24種)，分別是(1)錳/鈰-活性碳
(MnCe-GAC)、(2)鐵/鈰-活性碳(FeCe-GAC)及(3)鐵/錳-活性碳
(FeMn-GAC)。製備條件包括不同鹽類金屬溶液 (氯鹽與硝酸鹽
類金屬)、製備之煅燒溫度(200、300℃)、pH(pH＜2.5、pH 10)
等條件。並利用場發射掃描式電子顯微鏡(Field Emission 
Scanning Electron Microscope, SEM)與高解析度比表面積分析儀
(High Resolution Surface Area and Porosimetry Analyser, BET)等
分析其表面特性。 
第一年之計畫是探討利用 BM-GAC 結合 O3 (BM-GAC/O3)來處
理垃圾掩埋場滲出水。雖然 O3 有很強之氧化能力，但是對滲
出水中腐植物質效果不佳，而且其反應常受限於水中 pH 值、
鹽類與 O3之溶解度影響，因此單獨使用 O3 處理滲出水效果有
限。所以本研究將探討以 BM-GAC促進 O3產生氫氧自由基(‧
OH)特性，先將廢水中有機污染物吸附並濃縮於 BM-GAC上，
再經 BM-GAC與 O3產生之自由基來快速氧化被吸附污染物。
其中以 FeCe-U-200-Cl/O3(FC1/ O3)對 HA之去除率為最高
(97%)。此外，鹼性時(pH 9)之處理效果較中性與酸性佳。 
第二年之計畫是探討利用 BM-GAC結合 H2O2 (BM-
GAC/H2O2)來處理垃圾掩埋場滲出水。本方法結合 BM-GAC
對污染物吸附能力與其催化 H2O2產生氫氧自由基性質，快速
氧化被吸附污染物，其中以MnCe -U-300-NO3/H2O2 
(MC7/H2O2)對 HA之去除率為最高(98%)。此外 (BM-
GAC/H2O2)於酸性(pH 3)條件下，對 HA去除效果最好。 
第三年之計畫是探討利用 BM-GAC結合 H2O2與 O3(BM-
GAC/O3/H2O2)處理垃圾掩埋場滲出水。並利用 H2O2可有效
促進 O3產生‧OH自由基之特性，希望能進一步強化處理的效
率與降低處理之成本。BM-GAC/O3/H2O2以酸性(pH 4)時處理
效果較佳，O3/H2O2莫耳比則以 11/1為最佳。 
最後本研究並綜合比較以上各程序對實場垃圾掩埋場滲出水處
理效率。去除效率依序為 BM-GAC/O3/H2O2 > GAC/O3/H2O2

II 
Abstract 
In Taiwan, most of the landfills are in the stable stage (landfill age>10 years) and the 
landfill leachates contain organics that are hard to be biodegradable, such as humic acid (HA) 
and fulvic acid (FA). These humic substances might contribute more than 50% of organics in 
the leachate.  As a result, the effluent of landfill leachate might excess the COD limit of 250 
mg/L.  
Therefore, this project develops the nano bi metal oxides granular activated carbon 
(BM-GAC) processes for the removal of organic matters in landfill leachate. This project spans 
for three years to investigate BM-GAC/O3, BM-GAC/H2O and BM-GAC/H2O2/O3 processes. 
Three types of BM-GACs were prepared. Preparation conditions include (1) types of metal 
salts (chloride and nitrate salts) (2) temperature (200 and 300℃) and (3) pH (<2.5 and 10). 
Surface characteristics of the BM-GACs were examined as well. 
The first year’s project focuses on BM-GAC/O3 process for the treatment of landfill 
leachate. The ozone by itself has limited removal ability for organic contents of landfill 
leachate. By combining BM-GAC and O3 processes, the oxidation ability of O3 can be 
significantly improved.  FeCe-U-200-Cl/O3 has the highest HA removal efficiency (97%) 
among the tested BM-GAC/O3.  In addition, a higher pH has a higher HA removal efficiency.  
The second year’s project focuses on BM-GAC/H2O2 process. Utilizing the catalytic 
ability of BM-GAC to enhance the oxidation ability of H2O2 through the generation of 
hydroxyl radical. MnCe-U-300-NO3/H2O2 has the highest HA removal efficiency (98%).  In 
addition, a lower pH has a higher HA removal efficiency.  
The third year’s project focuses on BM-GAC/H2O2/O3 process. In additional to the 
advantages proposed in the first and second years’ methods, H2O2 could react with O3 to 
generate additional hydroxyl radicals to enhance the reaction efficiency. BM-GAC/H2O2/O3 
process at lower pH has a higher HA removal efficiency. Optimum ratio of H2O2/O3 is around 
11/1.  
Finally, this research tests the real landfill leachate and the removal efficiencies are in the 
order of BM-GAC/O3/H2O2 >GAC/O3/H2O2> O3/H2O2。For example, the TOC removal 
efficiencies of MnCe-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2, GAC/O3/H2O2 and O3/H2O2 TOC are 68, 54 and 
43%, respectively. In conclusions, BM-GAC processes have good potential for removal of 
organics from landfill leachate. 
 
Keywords: nano bi metal oxides-granular activated carbon (BM-GAC), hydrogen peroxide, 
ozone, adsorption, humic substance, heavy metals

三、 研究方法 
 
研究方法流程圖呈現於圖 1 中。 
 
3.1. 活性碳(GAC) 
 
本研究選用之GAC為F400 (Calgon Carbon Corp.)，其N2 BET表面積約為800~900 
m2/g，填充密度(apparent bulk density)約為0.54 g/cm3，粒子密度(particle density)約為
0.78 g/cm3和孔隙率(pore volume fraction)為0.82。GAC使用前需先篩選至一定大小 
(例如20 x 40 mesh size)，之後用蒸餾水(DDW)清洗並置於200 °C之烘箱內烘乾後備
用。 
 
3.2. 奈米雙金屬氧化物覆膜活性碳(BM-GACs)製備 
 
本研究製備奈米雙金屬氧化物覆膜活性碳(BM-GACs)，包括錳/鈰-活性碳
(MnCe-GACs)、鐵/鈰-活性碳(FeCe-GACs)與鐵/錳-活性碳(FeMn-GACs)等，製備方法
簡述如下： 
以錳/鈰-活性碳為例，將MnCe莫耳比 = 6/4之MnCl2‧2H2O 與CeCl3‧7H2O 加
入去離子水充分混合，再加入GAC 於溶液中均勻混合過夜，依條件需求調整pH 值
後，靜置4小時，以105 ℃烘乾，置於加蓋之坩鍋中，減少與氧氣接觸，再分別於200
與300 ℃煅燒3小時，再以去離子水清洗，烘乾備用。製備方法係參考相關雙金屬之
配製法(Imamura et al., 1996；Hamoudi et al., 1998；Chen et al., 2001；Pestunova et al., 
2002；Liu et al., 2007；Feng et al., 2006)：其它BM-GAC之製備法與上述程序類似。 
本研究亦探討不同製備條件對BM-GACs吸附特性影響，探討項目包括：金屬鹽
類種類 (如：氯鹽類(Cl-)金屬 (氯化錳、氯化鈰與氯化鐵)與硝酸鹽類(NO3-)金屬(硝
酸錳、硝酸鈰與硝酸鐵)，本研究以代號表示製備金屬種類(氯鹽類為Cl、硝酸鹽類為
NO3-)、pH值(pH＜2.5、pH 10)與煅燒溫度(200與300℃)。詳細BM-GAC製備條件列

3.8 不同複合程序對實場滲出水去除率測試 
 
本實驗將實場滲出水(100 ml)置入反應器中，並視組別需求加入 GAC 或
BM-GAC 和 H2O2 後，開啟臭氧產生機通入臭氧，實驗期間，視組別需求再加入 H2O2，
待反應結束後，取樣分析廢水參數(含 pH, TOC, COD, HA 等項目)。 
去除率指標包括 HA 及 TOC 與 COD 等。 
 
四、 結果與討論 
 
4.1 BM-GACs 表面特性分析 
 
4.1.1 比表面積與覆膜量分析 
 
本研究製備的 BM-GACs 之比表面積及金屬氧化物覆膜量列於表 2 中。 
(1) MnCe-GACs:  
Mn 與 Ce 覆膜量分別介於 52.93~77.27 mg/g 與 9.68~12.54 mg/g。其
中以 pH < 2.5 製備之 MnCe-GACs 具有較高之 Mn 覆膜量，整體而言，總
覆膜量(Mn+Ce)以 pH < 2.0 硝酸鹽類製備為最好(85~90 mg/L)。  
(2) FeCe-GACs:  
Fe 與 Ce 覆膜量分別介於 46.67~84.75 mg/g 與 4.71~79.98 mg/g。Fe
覆膜量以製備時 pH < 1.5 硝酸鹽類之製備法為最高(約 84 mg/g)，Ce 覆膜
量則以 pH 10 氯鹽類製備法為最高(70~80 mg/g)，整體而言，總覆膜量
(Fe+Ce)以 pH 10 氯鹽類製備法為最好(125~150 mg/L)。  
(3) FeMn-GACs:  
Fe 與 Mn 覆膜量分別介於 29.29~99.70 mg/g 與 2.84~69.91 mg/g。  
Fe 與 Mn 覆膜量皆以 pH 10 硝酸鹽類之製備法為最高，(約 86~99 mg/g
與 62~70 mg/g)，整體而言，總覆膜量(Fe+Mn)以 pH < 1.5 硝酸鹽類製備
法為最好(145~170 mg/L)。  
 
BM-GACs 之比表面積與未覆膜任何金屬之 GAC 比較，比表面積均有下
降趨勢，可能由於覆膜雙金屬導致金屬氧化物微粒堵塞部分活性碳孔隙所致。

4.2 BM-GAC/O3 去除腐植酸(HA)實驗 
 
本研究比較不同程序(O3、GAC、FeGAC、MnCe-GAC、FeCe-GAC、FeMn-GAC、
GAC/O3、FeGAC/O3、MnCe-GAC/O3、FeCe-GAC/O3與FeMn-GAC/O3)處理水中腐植
酸(HA)能力。實驗條件如下：初始濃度(100與250 mg/L)、BM-GAC劑量(2 g/L) 、
pH(4、6與9)、臭氧電壓(40 volt)與流量(2 L/min)。不同BM-GAC(MnCe-GAC、
FeCe-GAC與FeMn-GAC)實驗結果分別呈現於圖9~15。整體而言BM-GAC對O3有明
顯催化效果，其結果分述如下： 
(1) MnCe-GACs 
O3、GAC、GAC/O3、MnCe-U-200-NO3、MnCe-U-200-NO3/O3、
MnCe-A-200-NO3、MnCe-A-200-NO3/O3、MnCe-U-300-NO3、
MnCe-U-300-NO3/O3、MnCe-A-300-NO3 與 MnCe-U-300-NO3/O3 對 HA 去除結果
分別為 30、8、31、19、50、22、49、8、42、10 與 57%(圖 9)，以 MnCe-A-300-NO3/O3
去除效率較佳(57%)。 
(2) FeCe-GACs 
O3、GAC、GAC/O3、FeCe-U-200-NO3、FeCe-U-200-NO3/O3、
FeCe-A-200-NO3、FeCe-A-200-NO3/O3、FeCe-U-300-NO3 與 FeCe-U-300-NO3/O3
對 HA 去除率分別為 30、8、31、12、59、6、50、8 與 54%(圖 10)，以 FeCe-U-200- 
GAC/O3 去除 59%較佳。 
(3) FeMn-GACs 
O3、GAC、GAC/O3、FeMn-A-200-NO3、FeMn-A-200-NO3/O3、
FeMn-A-300-NO3 與 FeMn-A-300-NO3/O3 對 HA 去除率分別為 30、8、31、24、
60、19 與 59%(圖 11)，實驗結果顯示，FeMn-A-200/O3 與 FeMn-A-300/O3 去除率
較佳，分別為 60 與 59%。 
以上三種不同種類BM-GAC/O3 (MnCe-GAC、FeCe-GAC與FeMn-GAC)去除HA
實驗結果綜合列於圖12，實驗結果顯示添加O3對BM-GAC有明顯助益。 
本研究亦探討不同金屬鹽類對HA處理效果之影響，以兩種鹽類金屬(-Cl與-NO3)
製備BM-GAC。以FeMn-GAC為例，實驗結果如圖13所示，FeMn-A-200-N O3/O3與
FeMn-A-200-Cl/O3，去除效果無明顯差異(60與61%)。但在FeCe-GAC方面，
FeCe-GAC-Cl/O3 (97%)對HA去除率則明顯較FeCe-GAC-NO3/O3 (58%)為佳(圖14)，可
能原因為處理HA廢水時產生氫氧化物膠羽，而提高HA去除效率，在溶液中也有測
出Fe與Ce離子的存在。綜合以上結果得知，HA去除效率依序為BM-GAC/O3> 
GAC/O3> O3 > BM-GAC> GAC。

4.2.1  pH 對腐植酸(HA)去除效率影響實驗： 
本研究探討 pH 對 HA 去除效率影響，分別在 pH 4、6 與 9 條件下進行實驗，
結果呈現於圖 15。各處理程序(O3、GAC/O3、FeMn-A-200-NO3/O3 與
FeMn-A-300-NO3/O3)中，pH 值對 HA 去除效率皆依序為 pH 9 > pH 6 > pH4。以
FeMn-A-300-NO3/O3 為例，在反應時間 60 min 時，pH 4、6 與 9 下對 HA 的去除
效率依序為 29、36 與 39%。 
 
4.2.2 BM-GAC 重複使用實驗： 
本研究探討BM-GAC重複使用之可行性，實驗結果如圖16所示，結果顯示，
去除效率並無明顯下降。以MnCe -A-300-NO3為例，第一次與重複使用
MnCe-A-300-NO3對HA的去除效率分別為56與55%。 
 
4.2.3 不同 HA 濃度對去除效率影響實驗： 
本實驗探討腐植酸(HA)濃度對去除效率影響，結果呈現於圖17中，以
FeMn-GAC-A-200-NO3/O3為例，當HA濃度為250與100 mg/L，去除率分別為38 與
60%，當HA濃度愈低，去除效率愈高。 
 
4.2.4 Cu(II)對 HA 去除效率影響實驗： 
滲出水中除HA外也可能存在微量重金屬，因此，本實驗探討滲出水中含Cu(II)
時對HA去除效率之影響。結果如圖18所示，HA溶液中Cu(II)的存在有助於提升
BM-GAC-NO3/O3對HA之去除效果。以FeMn-A-200-NO3/O3為例，添加Cu(II)濃度
分別為0與1 mg/L，其去除率由為38提升為44%。

4.3 BM-GAC/H2O2 去除腐植酸(HA)實驗 
 
本研究利用不同BM-GACs結合H2O2處理腐植酸(HA)，並比較各方法對HA之去
除效率，三種BM-GAC (MnCe-GAC、FeCe-GAC與FeMn-GAC)實驗結果分別呈現於
圖19~27，實驗條件如下：HA初始濃度(250 mg/L)、0.01 N NaNO3、吸附劑(0.5與1 
g/L) 、pH (3、6與9) 、H2O2劑量(1 ml/L)與反應時間為3與24小時。BM-GACs於不
同pH值(3、6與9)之HA去除效率分述如下：  
 
(1) MnCe-GACs：  
MnCe-GACs 及 MnCe-GACs/H2O2 對 HA 之去除效率顯示於圖 19~21。  
pH 3 時(圖 19)，對 HA 之去除效率以 MnCe-U-200-NO3、
MnCe-U-200-NO3/H2O2、MnCe-U-300-NO3 與 MnCe-U-300-NO3/H2O2 為最
佳，分別為 92、91、97、98%，整體而言，H2O2 有助於 MnCe-GACs 去除
HA。  
pH 為 6 時(圖 20)，H2O2 對 MnCe-GACs 去除 HA 無明顯助益，但其中
MnCe-U-200-Cl 與MnCe-U-300-Cl 單獨使用時，HA 有相當高之去除效果  (93
及 94%)，其他 MnCe-GACs 對 HA 之去除效率則小於 22%。  
pH 9 時(圖 21)，  H2O2 對 MnCe-GACs 無明顯催化效果，大部份
MnCe-GACs 對 HA 之去除率均小於 12%。  
 
(2) FeCe-GACs：  
FeCe-GACs 及 FeCe-GACs/H2O2 對 HA 之去除效率顯示於圖 22~24。  
pH 3 時(圖 22)，整體而言，H2O2 對氯鹽類製備之 FeCe-GACs 
(FeCe-U-200-Cl/H2O2 與 FeCe-U-300-Cl/H2O2)去除 HA 助益較明顯，但硝酸
鹽類製備之 FeCe-GACs/H2O2 則效果不明顯；其中 FeCe-U-200-NO3、
FeCe-U-300-NO3 單獨使用，對 HA 有相當高的去除效果(90 與 97%)。  
於 pH 6 時(圖 23)，  整體而言，H2O2 對 FeCe-GACs 去除 HA 均無明顯
助益，但單獨使用 FeCe-U-200-Cl 及 FeCe-U-300-Cl 亦可達到相當高之去除
效果(99 與 96%)，而其他 FeCe-GACs 之去除率不高(約 27%)。  
pH 9 時(圖 24)，H2O2 對 FeCe-GACs 去除 HA 無明顯助益；當單獨使用
FeCe-GACs 去除皆不佳。  
 
(3) FeMn-GACs：  
FeMn-GACs 及 FeMn-GACs/H2O2 對 HA 之去除效率顯示於圖 25~27。  
當 pH 為 3 時(圖 25)，H2O2 對 FeMn-U-300-Cl 之 HA 去除率提升之助益
最為明顯，HA 之去除率由 9%提升至 73%；但對 FeMn-U-200-NO3 及
FeMn-U-300-NO3 助益較不明顯。

4.4 BM-GAC/O3/H2O2 去除腐植酸(HA)實驗 
 
本節探討BM-GACs/O3/H2O2、BM-GACs/O3、GAC/O3、GAC/O3/H2O2、
O3/H2O2及O3等程序處理HA之可行性，分別呈現於圖31~32。實驗條件如下：
HA初始濃度250 mg/L、BM-GAC劑量  2.0 g/L、pH 6、臭氧劑量16 mg/L、
O3/H2O2莫耳比11/1與反應時間30 min。其實驗結果分述如下。  
 
(1) MnCe-GACs/O3/H2O2 
(a) HA 去除率  
各程序對 HA 之去除效率除 MnCe-A-300-Cl /O3/H2O2 去除率較低外
(<60%)(圖 31)，其餘去除率均高於 80%。  
(b) TOC 去除率  
對 TOC 去除效率依序為 BM-GAC/O3/H2O2 > BM-GAC/O3 > 
GAC/O3/H2O2 > GAC/O3 ≒  O3/H2O2 > O3，其中對 TOC 去除效率最高的程
序為 MnCe-U-200-Cl /O3/H2O2(約 87%)。  
 
(2) FeMn-GACs/O3/H2O2 
(a) HA 去除率  
各程序對 HA 之去除效率介於 67~95%(圖 32)。除了
FeMn-A-200-Cl/O3/H2O2 較差外(約 67%)，其餘程序對 HA 去除效果皆大於
80%。  
(b) TOC 去除率  
對 TOC 去除效率同樣以 BM-GAC/O3/H2O2 最佳(圖 32)，對 TOC 去除
效率最高的程序為 FeMn-A-200-Cl /O3/H2O2 (約 92%)。

4.4.1 pH 對 BM-GAC/O3/H2O2 去除腐植酸(HA)之影響 
 
pH為去除HA及TOC之重要參數之一(Sánchez-Polo et al., 2006; Oguz et al., 
2006)，故本研究進一步探討不同初始pH(4、6與9)對去除效率的影響，因
MnCe-U-200-Cl與FeMn-U-200-Cl對HA與TOC去除效果良好，因此作為後續所
有影響因子之主要探討對象，溶液pH以HNO3及NaOH調整。  
(a) HA 去除率  
初始 pH 對 HA 去除效率的影響如圖 33 所示。pH 對各程序去除 HA
之去除率影響不大。  
(b) TOC 去除率  
以 BM-GACs/O3/H2O2 程序對 TOC 的去除效率最佳(如圖 34 所示)。其
中 MnCe-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2、FeMn-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2 及 GAC/O3/H2O2 等
程序初始 pH 低(pH 4)時會有較高的去除效率，分別為 90、86 及 61%；反
之，MnCe-U-200-Cl/O3、FeMn-U-200-Cl/O3 及 O3 等程序，於較高初始 pH 
(pH 9)時去除效率會較佳。  
 
4.4.2 臭氧濃度對 BM-GAC/O3/H2O2 去除腐植酸(HA)之影響 
 
本研究中亦探討臭氧劑量(2、11、16與31 mg/L)對去除HA與TOC效率之影
響。  
(a) HA 去除率  
不同程序對 HA 之去除效果差異不明顯(圖 35)，一般而言，臭氧劑量
越高時，對 HA 的去除效率亦隨之增加。  
(b) TOC 去除率  
臭氧為催化此程序產生氫氧自由基之重要因子，臭氧濃度對不同程序
去除 TOC 有影響明顯(圖 36)。一般而言，臭氧濃度越高，對 TOC 去除效
率越好。其中以 BM-GACs/O3 及 BM-GACs/O3/H2O2 等程序之影響最為明
顯。當臭氧濃度高於 16 mg/L 時，MnCe-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2、
FeMn-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2 程序的去除效率明顯優於其他程序(>81%)。

4.4.5 Cu(II)對 BM-GAC/O3/H2O2 去除腐植酸(HA)之影響 
 
滲出水中也可能含有銅離子(Cu(II))等重金屬，故本研究亦探討不同濃度
Cu(II)( 0、1及2 mg/L)對去除其HA與TOC的影響，實驗結果分述如下。  
(a) HA 去除率  
圖 41 中，FeMn-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2 及 FeMn-U-200-Cl/O3 等程序加入 2 
mg/L 之 Cu(II)後，其 HA 之去除效果明顯降低。去除效率分別由未添加
Cu(II)時之 95 與 94%降低至 51 與 54%。MnCe-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2 及
MnCe-U-200-Cl/O3 程序亦受影響，去除效率分別由 93 及 96%降低至 69
及 70%。  
(b) TOC 去除率  
Cu(II)存在對 TOC 去除之影響顯示於圖 42 中。實驗結果顯示，  
BM-GACs/O3/H2O2 及 BM-GACs/O3 等程序因 Cu(II)存在，而對去除 TOC
有明顯下降趨勢。例如  2 mg/L 之 Cu(II)存在時，會明顯抑制
BM-GACs/O3/H2O2 及 BM-GACs/O3 等程序對 TOC 之去除效果。例如，
MnCe-U-200-Cl/O3、FeMn-U-200-Cl/O3、  MnCe-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2 及
FeMn-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2 程序之 TOC 去除率分別下降約 20、16、20 及 31%。

下。 
(1) 硫酸鹽(Sulfate)

(2) 硝酸鹽(Nitrate)

4-5 複合程序去除實場滲出水實驗 
 
為了解本研究所開發之複合程序(BM-GAC/O3/H2O2)程序對處理實場垃圾掩埋
場滲出水之效率。本研究特針對效果較佳之兩種BM-GACs (FeMn-U-200-Cl及
MnCe-U-200-Cl)進行測試。實場滲出水來自台灣中部某一垃圾掩埋場，掩埋年齡
約為11年，滲出水原水之特性為pH 8.47, COD 292 mg/L 與BOD 12 mg/L。實驗條件
如下， BM-GAC劑量2 g/L、pH 8.47、臭氧劑量16 mg/L、O3/H2O2莫耳比11/1
與反應時間30 min，其實驗結果分述如下。  
 
(a) HA 去除率 
對於不同處理程序對實場滲出水之 HA 去除效率影響，以有臭氧參與反應
之去除效率較高(>60%)，其中 O3、O3/H2O2、GAC/O3、GAC/O3/H2O2、
FeMn-U-200-Cl/O3、MnCe-U-200-Cl/O3、FeMn-U-200-Cl/O3/ H2O2 與
MnCe-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2 之去除效率分別為 73、67、81、91、82、83、92 與
92%。其他處理程序則去除效果有限(約 40%)(圖 47)。 
(b) TOC 去除率 
各處理程序中，以 MnCe-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2 對實場滲出水之 TOC 去除效
率較高(68%)(圖 47)。例如，MnCe-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2、
FeMn-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2、GAC/O3/H2O2 與 O3/H2O2 之去除效率分別為 68、55、
54 與 43%。一般而言，去除效率依序為 BM/O3/H2O2 > GAC/O3/H2O2 > O3/H2O2。
故本研究開發之 BM-GAC/O3/H2O2 之方法能有效提高實場滲出水中有機物去
除。

五、 結論 
 
1. 本研究完成製備三類共 24 種奈米雙金屬活性碳 BM-GAC (MnCe-GAC, 
FeCe-GAC 與 MnCe-GAC)。 
2. 本研究完成製備 BM-GACs 之各項表面特性分析工作(BET、Coated amount 與
SEM)。 
3. 對腐植酸(HA)之去除率而言，依序為 BM-GAC/O3/H2O2>BM-GAC/O3＞
GAC/O3> O3> BM-GAC>GAC。 
4. BM-GAC/O3 對 HA 去除效率，以鹼性(pH 9)時為最佳，BM-GAC/O3/H2O2 則以
酸性(pH 3)時為佳。 
5. BM-GAC /O3/H2O2、O3/H2O2 及 GAC/O3/H2O2 最佳處理條件之 O3/H2O2 莫
耳比分別為 11/1、8/1 與 5/1。 
6. 一般而言，臭氧濃度越高時，BM-GAC/O3/H2O2 去除 HA 效果越佳。 
7. Cu(II)存在會抑制 BM-GAC/O3/H2O2 程序對 HA 之去除效率。 
8. 實場垃圾掩埋場滲出水之測試結果顯示，TOC 去除效率依序為
BM-GAC/O3/H2O2 > GAC/O3/H2O2 > O3/H2O2。例如，MnCe-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2、
GAC/O3/H2O2 與 O3/H2O2 之去除效率分別為 68、54 與 43%。以
MnCe-U-200-Cl/O3/H2O2 之去除效率最高。 
9. 本研究開發之 BM-GAC/O3/H2O2 程序，能有效提高實場垃圾掩埋場滲出水之有
機物之去除。

II. METHODS  
A. Analysis Methods 
The solid waste samples were collected seasonally from 
April 2002 to March 2003. There were 220 solid waste 
samples obtained from 55 different administrative districts 
including villages, towns, cities and remote islands. The 
sampling procedures were complied with the methods 
defined in the American Society for Testing and Materials 
standard methods (ASTM D75). The characteristics of the 
samples were analyzed using the ASTM methods including 
the moisture content (ASTM E949) and physical 
composition (ASTM E889). The energy contents of samples 
were measured using the Bomb Calorimeter (ASTM E711). 
The analysis results of physical composition and LHV of 
wastes are listed in Table I. 
B. Data Used In SOM 
The relationship between physical composition and LHV 
of waste was obtained by stepwise multiple linear 
regressions. The most important factors influencing the LHV 
included contents of water, plastic, wood and non-
combustible materials with the coefficient of determination 
(R2) of 0.87. This result indicted that these four factors 
explain 87% variance of LHV. Therefore, these four factors 
were used as the input variables of SOM for future 
classification. Before splitting the four physical composition 
data into SOM, all the data were normalized by the 
following equation [4].

C. Two-Level Approach (SOM Neural Network Then K-
Means Clustering) 
The classification procedures of SOM involved the 
delineation of the homogenous physical composition of 
wastes clusters in which within-cluster distance is minimized 
and the between-clusters distance is maximized. In this work, 
two-level approach (SOM neural network then K-means 
clustering) was adopted to classify the physical composition 
of waste. The normalized data of physical composition is 
grouped firstly by a large set of prototypes vectors from 
SOM, each normalized data vector belongs to the same 
group as its nearest prototype. Then the K-mean clustering 
algorithm is used to partition the prototype vectors of SOM 
into different clusters [4, 8]. The main advantage of the two-
level approach is the noise reduction of the data since the 
prototype is the averaged values and less sensitive to random 
variations than that of the original data.

neural network consists of the input layer which is 
constituted by 4 nodes (one by physical composition) 
connected to the 220 samples, and the output (Kohonen)

the adjusted weights (prototype vectors). The output layer is 
constituted by 81 (9 9) neurons which are laid out on a 
hexagonal lattice. In SOM, the high-dimensional input 
vectors are projected in a non-linear way to a low-
dimensional map (usually a two dimensional space). Every 
neuron of the output layer acts as a virtual site, and is

A. Results Of SOM Neural Network 
Figure 1 shows the distribution of samples on the 
Kohonen map [11] and the S1~S220 represent the sample 
codes. The properties of waste physical compositions are 
similar if the waste samples located in the same or adjacent 
virtual site. For instance, the samples of S52, S134, S190, 
S203 and S212 were located in a single virtual site (VS1) on 
the bottom of right side of Kohonen map the characteristics 
of waste physical composition of these five samples are very 
close. Therefore, the Kohonen map allows us to classify 
waste samples according to the similarity of waste physical 
compositions. The site of VS2 contained samples S57, S104, 
S180, S197 and S200 which located on the bottom of left 
side of Kohonen map is far away from VS1, revealing that 
the characteristics of samples in VS1 and VS2 have large 
discrepancy.  With the help of component planes of SOM, 
the associations among waste samples can be observed 
visually. For example, Figs. 2(a), 2(b), 2(c) and 2(d) are the 
component planes of normalized data for contents of water, 
plastic, non-combustible material and wood, respectively. 
The dark area represents the dominance of individual 
physical composition. The associations of physical 
compositions among different waste samples are easily 
observed from Figure 2. The VS1, VS2, VS3 and VS4 were 
found to be contents of water, plastic, non-combustible 
material and wood dominated, respectively. Compared the 
samples in VS1 and VS2, the samples in VS1 can be 
defined as high-water content with low plastic, non-
combustible material and wood contents, and the samples in 
VS2 can be defined as high-plastic content, middle water 
content, low non-combustible material and low wood 
contents. Therefore, the large discrepancy of physical 
composition assemblage exists between VS1 and VS2 that 
causes the distance between VS1 and VS2 as shown in the 
figures.

B. Results Of Principle Component Analysis (PCA) 
Principle component analysis (PCA) is one of the 
commonly used data ordination methods for simplifying 
data. PCA is used in this study for comparison. The four 
physical compositions were also normalized by (1) before 
using the PCA technique. The PCA result shows that the 
first two principle components can account for 68.2 % of the 
total variance of the input data. The correlation (loading) of 
individual physical composition with first two principle 
components (PCI and PCII) are listed in Table II. The 
loading with value greater than 0.5 can be used as cutoff 
value.12 From Table II, the water content is negatively 
correlated with component I, and plastic and wood are 
positively correlated with component I. It is also found that 
the non-combustible material is positively correlated with 
component II. Figure 3 shows the plot of first two principle 
components scores. The larger blank circles, blank squares, 
dark circles, and dark squares symbols in PCA plot 
represent the samples in VS1, VS2, VS3 and VS4 on 
Kohonen map, respectively. It is found that the well 
separated samples on four corners (VS1~VS4) of Kohonen 
map are also well separated in the PCA plot, indicating that 
the PCA can be used for data classification as well. For 
instance, the samples in VS1 are water dominated and low 
plastic content (Figure 2(a) and 2(b)) which located at the 
left side of component I of PCA since the component I score 
is positively correlated with plastic and is negatively 
correlated with water. In addition, the samples in VS1 are 
low in non-combustible material (Figure 2(c)) and located at 
the bottom side of component II since component II is 
highly positively correlated with non-combustible material. 
Therefore, the samples in VS1 of Kohonen map with water 
dominated, low plastic and low non-combustible material 
contents located in the bottom-left side of PCA plot. In 
contrast, samples in VS2 with plastic dominated, middle 
water and low wood, and low non-combustible material 
would have larger component I and lower component II 
scores. The VS2 is located on the bottom-right side of PCA 
plot as predicted. Nevertheless, both of the SOM and PCA 
techniques can be used for data classification. However, the 
positive or negative associations of physical composition of 
wastes among samples can be easier to be observed for 
SOM than that of PCA. In addition to the advantage of 
visualization, the map of SOM will be unaffected if new 
samples were added on the Kohonen map.5 The new 
samples only need to be compared with the prototype 
vectors of SOM to obtain the values of BMU. Finally, the 
new sample is represented on the map corresponding to this 
BMU.

In conclusion, the performance of LHV values in 
different clusters is highly correlated with the physical 
compositions. In order to test statistical difference of LHV 
among different clusters, one-way analysis of variance 
(ANOVA) and Waller-Duncan k-ratio t-test (k = 100, P = 
0.05) were used. The null hypothesis H0: the mean LHV 
values are equal, and the alternative H1: at least two of the 
means are unequal for four or eight clusters conditions. The 
F-statistics from one-way ANOVA are 60.6 and 39.8 for 
four-cluster and eight-cluster conditions, respectively. The 
results indicate that the clustering solutions are all highly 
significant (P < 0.001), and suggest that at least two of the 
means are unequal. The Waller-Duncan k-ratio t-test was 
applied to test the difference between means of individual 
cluster being significant or not. The means of individual 
cluster and results of Waller-Duncan k-ratio t-test are shown 
in Table III. The same letters on the column of Waller 
grouping adjacent to the column of means indicate that these 
clusters are not significantly different at the = 0.05. From 
Table III, the Waller groupings for four-cluster condition 
show that each cluster is significantly different from each 
other except the clusters of A2 and A4. Although both A2 
and A4 have lower LHV, the reasons causing lower LHV in 
A2 and A4 are different. The lower LHV for A2 and A4 are 
resulting from the higher non-combustible material and 
water contents, respectively. For the case of eight-cluster 
condition, the results of Waller grouping are also shown in 
Table III, and only four well-separated Waller groups were 
found. For instance, the highest LHV in B6 is significantly 
different from the rest of the clusters; the cluster pair of B4 
and B5 cannot be separated from each others, but can be 
separated from the rest of the clusters. B8 and B3 can be 
classified as one group while B2, B7 and B1 can be grouped 
into as another group as well.
