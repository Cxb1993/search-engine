Abstract 
The Affinity chitin chromatography with enzyme-modified chitin was employed for 
purification of chitinase. The kinetics of chitinase was investigated for different forms of the 
chitin under 15?, pH5 and 4h. Therefore, through the inference of Langmuir isotherm and by 
observed the Q and k values, demonstrated that the chitin through modified by ferment could 
actually increase the specific absorbing surface for chitinase and its absorbing capability. 
 
  
Keyword: fermentation modification, chitin, chitinase, purificatication.
