1. 中文摘要
非晶矽薄膜提供了一種低成本，高產出的技術作為替代能源之解決方案。然而，與多/
單晶矽材料作為太陽能電池材料之比較，非晶矽薄膜卻有著比較低效能及長期使用不穩
定性的缺點。過去的文獻研究發現，這種的不穩定性與光誘導的未鍵結價鍵(dangling
bonds)有關，它會導致再結合區域(recombination part)的產生，使得它的效能降低。為了
改善元件的效能，本計畫除了非晶矽以外，將使用碳矽化合物之高頻帶(wideband gap)
材料，作為非晶矽薄膜的緩衝層，一方面可以增加元件的開路電壓(Voc)，以增加效能，
另一方面，可以增加能帶的吸收範圍。因此，對於非晶碳矽以及奈米碳矽的研究，是此
計畫的重點之一。此計畫利用化學回火(chemical annealing)的方式來作減低微縫隙
(microvoids)以及的化學鍵。又研究發現化學回火的氣體選擇，扮演了未鍵結價鍵的重要
因素。一般過去的實驗大多使用氫氣加電漿作為處理，但是它並無法有效的將非晶結構
有效的轉換成微(或奈米)晶體結構，然而利用氦氣比較能達到此一目的，可行性的解釋
應該是氫氣回火只將未飽和氫鍵取代，而氦氣回火能將弱氫鍵取代使鍵結穩定，可形成
微(或奈米)晶體結構。然而計畫中使用碳矽作為 p 極的的太陽能電池，雖然使開路電壓
增加，卻有短路電流過低的問題，有可能為接觸的問題，這個部份仍極待克服。
2. 英文摘要
Amorphous thin film provides a low-cost, high throughput solution used for renewable
energy. However, as a photo absorption layer of solar cell, it is a relatively low efficiency
techniques compared to crystalline material. Besides, its long-term degradation is another
disadvantage for the use on solar cells. Researchers found that the instability is caused by
light-induced dangling bond. The microvoids of the film leave recombination parts that
decrease the performance as a photo absorption layer. To improve their efficiency, a wide
bandgap material will be introduced along with amorphous silicon in this project. Besides,
this project used chemical annealing to eliminate microvoids and weak chemical bonds. The
gas choice as chemical annealing in the work is found to be critical to transfer amorphous
film into nanocrystalline one. Helium is found to be more efficient than hydrogen as reactive
gas in this work. The possible reason is that helium is more effect to remove weak chemical
bonds rather than only to replace bonds as hydrogen does. However, this work found the solar
cells have low short-current even they can effectively increase open voltage so far. A possible
reason is high resistivity of contact or quality of silicon carbide.
