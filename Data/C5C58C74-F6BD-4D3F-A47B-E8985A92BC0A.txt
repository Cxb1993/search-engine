一、 中文摘要 
 
本計畫 “高波速鑽石表面聲波元件之研製”，
主要是針對不同指叉電極與金屬層位置於層狀結
構(100)AlN/diamond 之表面聲波元件特性進行研
究，計畫時程為二年。本計畫第一年之主要(1)在
鑽石基板上沉積具(100)軸向之氮化鋁壓電薄膜並
(2)理論分析四種層狀結構(1. IDT/AlN/diamond 2. 
IDT/AlN/metal thin film/diamond 3. 
AlN/IDT/diamond 4. Metal thin 
film/AlN/IDT/diamond)之表面聲波特性，進行不同
指叉電極與金屬層位置之表面聲波波傳特性模擬
分析，求取其最佳化之表面聲波波速、機電耦合
係數等表面聲波特性。本計劃 “高波速鑽石表面
聲波元件之研製(Ⅱ)”為延續之第二年計畫，其目
的為研製薄膜層狀表面聲波元件與特性分析，主
要工作為：(1)發展層狀結構之鑽石表面聲波元件
電腦輔助設計程式，(2)設計射頻層狀結構鑽石表
面聲波元件，(3)進行元件製作與特性量測，並與
模擬結果進行比較，來進行元件特性修正。 
結果顯示，氮化鋁薄膜最佳製程參數為濺鍍功
率350W、濺鍍壓力9mTorr、基板溫度200℃、氮分
率50%時，可成功沉積出(100)優選取向氮化鋁薄膜
並作為高波速鑽石表面聲波元件之應用，薄膜表面
平均粗糙度(average roughness)為4.311 nm。另外量
測結果得元件共振頻率為4.375GHz並經公式轉換
求得表面聲波波速為 8,750 m/s。

Abstract 
This project entitled “Fabrication of High Velocity 
Diamond Based Surface Acoustic Wave Device” was 
planned to run in the two years. In the first year, we 
have prepared the (100) oriented AlN thin films on 
the diamond substrate and analyzed the surface 
acoustic wave (SAW) properties of four structures 
(IDT/AlN/diamond, IDT/AlN/metal thin 
film/diamond, AlN/IDT/diamond and metal thin 
film/AlN/IDT/diamond). This project entitled 
“Fabrication of High Velocity Diamond Based 
Surface Acoustic Wave Device (II)” will run in the 
next year. The goal of the project is the fabrication of 
layered diamond SAW device. First, we will develop 
the computer-aid method of layered diamond SAW 
devices and use this method to simulate the device 
properties to find the optimal design parameters. 
Finally, we will fabricate the SAW devices and set 
up the measured system. The results will be 
compared with the simulations and modify the design 
method. 
The result shows the optimal sputtering 
parameters for (100)-oriented AlN films were found 
to be RF power of 350W, pressure of 9 mTorr, 
substrate temperature of 200
o
C, Nitride 
concentration of 50%, and the average roughness is 
4.311 nm. The resonance frequency and the velocity 
of the Diamond Based Surface Acoustic Wave 
Device are 4.375GHz and 8,750 m/s, respectively. 
 
Keywords: (100)-oriented, AlN, Diamond, RF 
magnetron sputtering, Layer SAW filter.
