I摘要
台灣處於亞熱帶海島型氣候及地勢複雜之島嶼國家，主要受到太平洋高壓及
大陸高壓系統影響，在台灣中部地區空氣品質之時空分布及變異特性也會因季節
性氣象特徵而變化，在過去的幾年裡，台灣地區空氣污染物管制奏效，各污染物
平均濃度有下降之趨勢，但平均臭氧濃度卻逐年增加，尤其在春、秋兩季濃度較
高，而中部地區地形複雜，受海陸-山谷所產生之局部環流及混合層高度等大氣
環境因素影響污染物傳輸機制，因此，對氣象與地形因素探討將可深入了解高污
染事件的成因。
本研究將使用繫留探空監測技術，選擇不同季節為期數日之連續監測，主要
監測近地面大氣垂直剖面氣象因子及不同高度之空氣污染物(O3、NOx、NMHC、
CO、SO2)之濃度分布，分析高空污染物擴散關係，並配合澳大利亞研發之空氣
污染模式(TAPM)模擬中部地區不同季節之大氣環境特性，同時，再利用空氣污
染模式(TAPM)模擬污染物傳輸擴散情形，並修正及彌補衛星觀測資料之不足，
以達到模式模擬大氣環境之準確性，另外，再藉由軌跡模式運算，可推估污染物
之來源及其經過之地區所造成影響範圍。
在不同的縱觀天氣型態及地表特性下，皆會影響大氣邊界層之大氣環境有所
變化，進而導致污染物時空變化及高濃度事件之發生，因此，本研究將探討台灣
中部地區適合發生高臭氧濃度時之污染物擴散傳輸機制，未來可提供在污染物管
制決策作為參考依據，改善中部地區空氣品質。
經模式模擬結果顯示， TAPM 於水平氣象場模擬部份，春季溫度與觀測值
之相關系數皆可高達0.8， IOA 值介於0.8~0.9；風速方面，相關系數皆可高達
0.6，IOA 值介於0.4~0.9。秋季溫度與觀測值之相關系數皆達0.9 以上，IOA 值
於0.6~0.9 ；風速方面，相關系數皆可高達0.86 以上，IOA 值介於0.67~0.93。
垂直方向之模擬，TAPM 可掌握與實際監測之變化。TAPM 於不同季節所模擬
混合層高度之不同，造成污染物濃度之嚴重程度亦有不同。由模式模擬探討中部
地區之大氣環境，可以彌補觀測資料之不足外，可了解大氣環境及台灣地區地形
之複雜性。
本研究結果於2006年 SETAC Asia/Pacific國際研討會發表論文”Ozone
episodes associated with typhoon Vicente near Taiwan.”。英文已撰寫”Ozone

2.1大氣邊界層
都市地區所排放之空氣污染物，其水平和垂直的傳輸及擴散作用通常都在行
星邊界層(Planetary Boundary Layer, PBL)中進行，行星邊界層通常指地表以上至
一公里處的對流大氣，其中包括自地表面起至50 公尺之表面層、因夜間輻射冷
卻造成逆溫層及白天對流旺盛發展之混合層等，在300 至500 公尺高度之混合層
中，常發生風速隨高度急遽增加且風向呈現順時鐘旋轉之大氣現象，稱此層面為
艾克曼層(Ekman layer)，至邊界層上方則為自由大氣層。
邊界層以下之氣流受到盛行風向及地表磨擦的作用，地面排放之空氣污染物
則主要受限於100 公尺以下之大氣所影響(Ouattara and Robert, 2002；Seinfeld and
Pandis, 1998；Stull, 1988)。
日間因對流混合層高度及夜間會因為逆溫層的存在，使污染物於垂直方向的
擴散能力有重大的影響；氣流垂直的運動主要受到地表面太陽日照加熱、水平氣
流之輻合或輻散作用、水平氣流遇地形之抬升作用與氣團中水氣之凝結釋放潛
熱。
行星邊界層中之混合層高度泛指日間受太陽輻射加熱產生之熱力作用
（Thermodynamic）造成向上之旺盛對流所達之高度，該高度即為近地表空氣污
染物於垂直擴散之上限。夜間則因地表輻射冷卻所造成之輻射逆溫(Radiation
Inversion)現象抑制了污染物於夜間垂直向的混合，而只剩機械力（Mechanical）
的混合作用(Fatogoma and Jacko, 2002；Stull, 1988)。混合層頂高度可由大氣垂直
溫度剖面結構加以判斷，當大氣垂直溫度結構出現明顯逆溫現象時，即大氣為下
冷上熱之情形，使當時大氣難發生垂直混合交換之現象，即大氣呈現穩定之狀
態，而此逆溫層高度即為大氣垂直擴散頂點，亦即混合層頂之高度；若混合層高
度降低，往往使污染物不利於大氣環境中擴散與稀釋，導致空氣品質惡化，許多
嚴重空氣污染事件與當地發生近地面或低空持續性的逆溫現象有關（Seinfeld and
Pandis, 1998；彭啟明與林松錦，1995；陶等，1995；張等，1992）。受高壓系
統影響下，混合層高度較低，抑制污染物的垂直擴散能力，容易導致近地面污染
物高濃度事件之發生；許多嚴重空氣污染事件與該區發生地面或低空發生持續性
之逆溫現象有關，因夜間逆溫層使高空之污染物無法向下傳送，地面污染物也被
侷限於逆溫層下，不利污染物擴散與交換，因而容易導致空氣品質惡化（Helmiga
et al., 2002；Gerd and Platt, 2002；Cheng, 2001a；林能輝，2000；Seinfeld and Pandis,
1998；Katsoulis, 1996；Kassomenos et al., 1995；Ziomas et al., 1995；Liu et al.,
1994）。

4（三）衛星遙感探測( remotely sensed thermal data )
利用衛星上搭載的高解析度輻射計（ advanced very high resolution
radiometer , AVHRR）進行地面溫度觀測，其觀測不會受到移動觀測法時
間差的影響，只有觀測時受限於衛星運轉軌道及角度的關係，觀測時間比
較沒有彈性，而且也受到雲層的影響。用衛星來評估都市的熱島效應
(Gallo,1995)。利用 NOAA 衛的 AVHRR 來觀測德州休士頓的空氣氣溫結
構(Streutker,2003)。
（四）電腦模式模擬（computer modellings）
電腦模式模擬仍然需要上述的其他觀測方法資料的輔助，進行電腦模式的
建立與基本參數估計，及模式的驗證。利用三維模式模擬都市環境和熱島
的發展情形(Saitoh et al, 1995；Troude et al, 2001；Lemonsu and Masson,
2002； Luhar and Hurley, 2003；Fan and Sailori, 2005)。
本研究採用了上述的固定式監測法和電腦模式模擬方法，主要是利用固定氣
象監測站的資料能直接觀察到明顯的熱島效應的趨勢變化，再利用模式模擬掌握
更明確的熱島發展，然後再進一步做垂直方向的探討。再者，汽車移動觀測需要
儀器的設備以及人力的調度，資料彙集不便，而衛星遙感探測則考慮到資料取得
不易，加上目前利用遙測所拍攝的解析圖並沒有辦法完全清楚的描述水平和垂直
方向都市熱島效應的趨勢發展，而且容易受雲層影響，因此本研究選取固定式監
測法和電腦模式模擬方法，再加上繫留探空實際監測垂直大氣結構，以便能確實
掌握都市熱島效應的發展形態。
首先，本研究收集彙整台灣環保署中部地區環境監測站、中央氣象局氣象監
測站以及台中縣環保局自設測站資料，從中利用溫度場數據呈現地面都市熱島效
應情形，運用歷年數據推估不同季節熱島現象發生的區域，然後，選定熱島中心
做模式模擬，都市熱島的水平方向及高空垂直狀況，並且進一步比對實際監測值
與模式模擬值之間的一致性和相關性，希望能掌握台中地區都市熱島效應的發展
以及模式對台中地區的適用性。

3.2.1 繫留探空儀(Tethersonde)

4.2 垂直氣象場之監測與模擬分析
本文利用Tethersonde 於台灣中部內陸地區進行春季及秋季之繫留探空監
測，並且選擇發生污染物濃度較高時之氣象場，與TAPM 模擬結果進行比較。
（一）春季
本文選擇監測時間2004年4月24至2004年5月2日於南投縣草屯鎮進行繫留探
空監測為案例，模擬時間選取則為監測時間中2004年4月25至2004年4月30日，分
別對水平向及垂直向做比較分析。
2004 年 4 月 24 日至 5 月 2 日的綜觀天氣型態主要受到大陸高壓系統南下及
鋒面所影響。由中部地區環保署各測站所量測的臭氧濃度發現，自 4 月 28 日及
4 月 29 日，台灣中部地區臭氧值即開始增高，環保署竹山站地面測站臭氧濃度
值高達 148.7ppb，因此將針對 4 月 28 日的大氣及污染物垂直結構進行分析。
4 月 28 日，受大陸高壓系統南下出海及鋒面遠離之影響下，台灣地區呈現
晴朗炎熱的天氣型態。28 日清晨 00 時，地面 NO 濃度值高達 22ppb，隨時間與
O3 產生滴定效應的消耗，使 NO 及 O3 的濃度值降低，而 NO2 濃度值增加；於清
晨 06 時，人為活動的汙染物排放量的增加，NO 濃度回升，同時，O3 的光化反
應生成速率，卻不及臭氧被消耗的速率，NO2 濃度值持續增加；09 時，NO2 受
紫外線分解，臭氧濃度隨光化反應速率增加而增加，直到 15 時，臭氧濃度達
108ppb，呈現單高峰型態，而 NO2 也明顯被消耗而有最低值；隨光化反應減弱，
NO2 再次因滴定效應消耗臭氧而增加(圖 10)。
圖 10. 2004 年 4 月 28 日 O3, NO and NO2 濃度時間序列

（二）秋季
本文選擇監測時間2004 年10月21 日至2004 年10 月25 日於南投縣草屯鎮
進行繫留探空監測為案例，模擬時間選取則為監測時間中2004 年10 月21 日至
2004 年10 月24 日，分別對水平向及垂直向做比較分析。
2004 年 10 月 21 日至 10 月 24 日的綜觀天氣型態主要受到大陸高壓系統南
下，台灣地區主要受到東北季風的影響，北部及東部地區主要風場為東北風，西
半部地區則位於背風面，天氣型態穩定。監測採樣時間從 21 日至 24 日四天，
綜觀天氣型態為東北季風、高壓出海，持續受到低壓系統接近台灣之外圍環流氣
流影響，台灣西中部地區形成與高壓迴流天氣型態下相同之背風側相對低壓系
統，大氣狀態亦較為穩定，風速微弱晴朗炎熱的好天氣適合熱島效應的發展。因
此將針對 10 月 22 日的大氣及污染物垂直結構進行分析。
10 月22 日1200LST，由Tethersonde 監測結果顯示（圖12），風速隨高度
遞減，至約300公尺處，風速達最小值約2.3 m/s，300 公尺至400公尺風速遞增至
4.2m/s，400公尺以上至約750公尺處則隨高度增加而減少至1.5 m/s，750公尺以
上風速又隨高度兒遞增，而TAPM 於地面10 公尺風速約6 m/s，並且隨高度增加
而遞減至2.9 m/s。實際風向則於近地面至200 公尺為北風轉呈西北風，200 公尺
至400公尺又呈現北風，400公尺以上為西北風，TAPM 模擬結果則呈現西北風。
於垂直溫度變化，地面實際溫度為27℃，並隨高度增加而遞減，TAPM 亦有相
同之趨勢變化，但其溫度則略高實際溫度1℃。

Abstract
Previous studies pointed out that, whenever the weather pattern of a Tropical Depression
approaching northwards toward the island, ozone episodes would likely occur (over 80% of
statistically) in central Taiwan. The present study intended to examine the boundary layer structure
including thepolutants’ vertical profiles and horizontal distributions in the region during the 15-19
September 2005, when typhoon Vicente emerged about 1200 km southwest of Taiwan.
During this ozone episode, the tethersonde was raised at Nantou, situated inland of central
Taiwan, to monitor air pressure, temperature, humidity, wind direction and speed. Air samples at
different heights were collected by sampling pumps, and were analyzed for concentrations of O3,
NOx, NMHC and CO. Then, the Air Pollution Model Version 3 (TAPM V3.0)–a three-dimensional
prognostic model –was used to simulate the meteorology and pollutant distributions during this
high ozone period. The model simulation was subsequently verified with the data obtained from
the tethersonde monitoring as well as the meteorological and pollutant data from the Taiwan
Environmental Protection Administration (TEPA).
The ozone concentration in central Taiwan reached over 120 ppb on September 17, 2005.
During this ozone episode, the NO2/NOx values measured from the inland stations (e.g. Tali,
Nantou and Chushan) were above 0.7, which were noticeably higher than those measured from the
coastal stations (e.g. Shalu and Hsitun). The concentrations of the secondary pollutants (NO2 and O3)
quickly built up, suggesting pollutant accumulation in the inland regions. Also, between the
nighttime of September 16 and the midnight of September 17, the NO2/Ox values in Tali, Nantou
and Chushan were approximately 0.8, indicating that ozone was mostly titrated by NO to form NO2.
The NMHC concentration measured from the coastal station (Hsitun, >0.3 ppm) was higher
than that from the inland station (Nantou, <0.1 ppm) on September 17. While the time-variations of
NMHC/NOx in both Hsitun and Nantou exhibited the same trends with their NMHC variations on
the morning of September 17, the NMHC/NOx value in Nantou and Hsitun increased to 10 and 30,
respectively, by noon as a result of the reduction in NO2 concentrations. Since the NMHC
concentration in the inland region was less than that in the coastal region, it could be deduced that
the ozone concentration in the inland region was primarily controlled by the variation of NOx
concentrations.

41. Introduction
During the summer and autumn, typhoons frequently affect the Taiwan region. The influence
on air quality from the peripheral circulation of a typhoon varies with the intensity and the position
of the typhoon when it approaches Taiwan. Based on the past air quality data, when the center of
a typhoon locates at a distance about 1000 to 2000 km southwest or southeast from Taiwan, the
wind direction is typically a easterly wind and thus the western half of Taiwan is located on the lee
side of the central mountain ridge. Consequently, the current converges in the west half and the
synoptic patterns easily cause high Pollutant Standards Index (PSI>100), especially if ozone is used
as the index pollutant (Cheng, 2001).
Typhoon Vicente provides a recent example of such scenario as it approached Taiwan during
16-18 September 2005. During this period, the location of Typhoon Vicente was southwest about
1200 km out to sea from Taiwan, and it caused a maximum ozone concentration of over 120 ppb for
three monitoring sites. In addition, other monitoring stations also observed ozone concentrations
exceeding 100 ppb, demonstrating that the peripheral circulation of the typhoon already caused air
quality to worsen in central Taiwan (Cheng, 2002). This study therefore intends to discuss the
cause of ozone episodes occurred during this period based on interpretation of air quality data and
model simulation.

From the above-mentioned hypothesis we can make a preliminary conclusion. In the
coastland and urban areas, NOx is the main pollutant source, but NMHC is also one of the pollution
sources. The pollutant with the most obvious influence is NO2, especially for the inland areas.
In addition, the inland NMHC pollutant concentration is obviously lower than the coastal area, but
high ozone concentration values occur. In other words, one of the reasons that may cause the
inland concentration to accumulate is the pollutant transmission from outside.
4.2 Vertical pollution analysis
The vertical wind field and vertical virtual potential temperature, as well as the vertical profile
of pollutant concentration are shown in Fig. 9 and Fig. 10, respectively. Under the influence of the
regional circulation, the wind direction was south southwesterly on September 15 early morning.
The wind speed increased rapidly, and the largest wind speed was 8 ms-1, preventing concentration
of ozone precursor pollutants. After 15:00 p.m., the wind direction transferred to north and
northwesterly and the wind speed was about 7 ~ 8 ms-1, causing pollutant transport to produce O3
concentration of 82 ppb inland from the urban area. In another incident, after 12:00 noon on
September 16, the wind direction again transferred from south north and northwesterly and mixing
layer height was only about 570 m, which was relatively unfavorable to the vertical diffusion of
pollutant, and O3 concentration increased to 82 ppb. Because the wind direction at higher altitude
was weak southwesterly in the night, NO2 concentration accumulated to about 35 ppb (Fig. 10 (b)).
Furthermore, heavy traffic on the weekend caused CO concentration to reach a maximum of 1.8
ppm between the surface and about 100 m. As Typhoon Vicente approached, wind direction was
weak southeasterly and its wind speed reduced with increasing altitude on the morning of
September 17. The sunshine was strong at 12:00 ~ 15:00 in the afternoon, and NO, NO2 (Fig. 10
(a), (b)) accumulated on September 16 by undergoing photochemical reactions. The O3 hourly
concentration value was up to 121 ppb at the surface. Subsequently, as the typhoon moved away
on the morning of September 18, the average wind speed reduced and mixing layer height
developed to about 1000 m, which was beneficial to the vertical diffusion of pollutants. During 19
September 2005, a continental anticyclone moving eastwards over the East China Sea had its center
located northeast of Taiwan, with weak easterly winds covering the area (Lin et al., 2004). The
strong photochemical reaction was vigorous at 12:00. Therefore, the O3 hourly concentration
value rose to 102 ppb.

4.4 Trajectory model
Because there was no large fixed pollution source in Nantou, therefore only local moving
pollution source existed. The air mass trajectory at 15:00 (the simulation time is hour 111) on
September 17, 2005, in Fig. 15, shows the air parcel was located on the sea surface in the early
morning, then after sunrise the solar radiation warmed up the surface to induce a landward
sea-breeze within 2~3 hours. The observed and simulated wind speed and temperature are shown
in Table 4 and Table 5. The air parcel has already been moved from sea to the coastal area after
13:00 (the simulation time is hour 109), causing the near-shore hourly ozone concentration value to
reach 93 ppb in Changhwa, and the pollution already had started to accumulate at this time in
Nantou. The sea-breeze air parcel with added ozone precursor blew this urban Taichung pollutant
to the inland at 15:00, causing the hourly ozone concentration value to reach 125 ppb in the Nantou
area.

cannot resolve the complex Taiwan terrain completely. The entire simulation has an average IOA
value of 0.67. For temperature, TAPM simulated temperature has a tendency similar to
observation, with an IOA value between 0.77 and 0.93. For surface ozone, it grasps the observed
concentration variation tendency, average correlation coefficient at 0.57. It may be subject to the
influence of the synoptic meteorological field, so unable to represent it accurately.
The model in this research does not include conditions of vegetation, deep soil volumetric
moisture content, sea surface and deep soil temperatures and terrain parameters suitable to Taiwan
island. The model result with meteorological field and ozone is correct, but it cannot express
accurately the rapid variation of topography. If we can more thoroughly incorporate
meteorological factors and emission data in future revision of the model, it can be regarded as a
reference tool applied to environmental impact assessment of Taiwan in the future.
