     這該是我最後一次利用國科會專題計畫出國開會了，退休以後就不能享有這份資格了。 
     近十年來，我的一個研究主題是結構防震液流阻尼器之研發及應用。自己覺得，這項研
究已經可以暫時劃下一個句點，可以進入推廣應用的階段了。所以，除了參加ICCET2011研討
會，我又為自己安排了一場演講，這場演講除了獲得一個實際與大陸研究生們進行實際溝通
交流的機會外，宣導吾人自製的結構防震液流阻尼器實為安排這次演講的重要目的。 
     汶川地震無疑是大陸近年來頗受注目的一次強烈地震，基於此，我很想把這場演講放在

Abstract. For the sake of safety of structures subjected to abnormal loadings, damper could be one of 
the effective devices to reduce the responses by the energy dispassion functions. Key points in the 
process of development and application of damper and aseismatic engineering are narrated. Due to 
the highly nonlinear behavior properties, most of the case studies aim on these kind of dynamic 
analysis only working on structures with elastic linearly behavior systems. In addition to the 
nonlinearly behavior of damper devices, structures behave into nonlinearly as well when subjected by 
abnormal excitation, for instance, severe earthquakes. Materials respond into nonlinearly beyond 
yielding or plastic hinges occurred somewhere in the controlled structures. Proper software package 
is used to calculate the responses of simple portal frames which are controlled with dampers allocated. 
Both of structures and damper devices are considered and behaved according to its nonlinearly 
properties. Responses beyond yielding of material are rationally analyzed and revealed in time 
history expressions. 
Introduction 
What we have for our civil structural design stays in static system design concept yearly ago. In this 
several decades, our designing concept is not satisfied static, but also emphasizes dynamic design, 
particularly when the structures subject to abnormal excitations such as earthquake loadings. For the 
sake of safety of structures, reducing the responses of structures for sure is the goal for designers. The 
method to reduce the responses of the excited structures is denominated as structural control. 
Designers face the problem is only to reduce the deformation of the structures, try to reduce the 
displacement at different degree of freedoms when the design is statically considered. It is not only 
displacement, but also velocity, acceleration, mode shapes, frequencies of vibration, … are all 
responses should be detected when the design is dynamically considered, that leads the structural 
control becoming much and much complex then it is only statically considered.

 Finite Element Numerical Analysis 
Before the control system can be industrial publicly accept. Predictions of the responses of the 
excited structures would be very important to convince people to use the system. Prove the 
advantages in advance, to show the difference of the excited structures with and without control 
system would be one of the duties for structural engineers to prepare. In case of structures subjected 
to severe earthquakes causing damage, structures, including structural control devices if it is installed, 
could be yield most probably. No more linear analytic response reflects the true phenomenon. We 
need to provide the time history of nonlinear dynamic responses even the structures under 
consideration goes beyond yielding point, and with plastic hinge induced as well. Proper finite 
element software package, including SAP2000 and ANSYS, is use to find responses for both 
structure and control system devices are nonlinearly responded. It is clear to find that structural 
control with viscous damper plays very good role as an effective control design system. There are two 
examples of a simple portal frame installed with damper system subjected to El Centro earthquake as 
shown in Fig. 14. Responses reduced remarkably. Fig. 15 shows the structural configuration in the 
first example analyzed by SAP2000. Bi-linearly material stress-strain properties (α=0.0055) are 
assumed as shown in the Fig. 16. Reponses such as displacement, velocity, and acceleration, etc. are 
shown in Figs. 17, 18, and 19, respectively [6]. Fig. 20 shows the structural configuration in the 
second example analyzed via the package ANSYS. Material stress-strain properties assumptions are 
depicted in the Fig. 21. Difference of maximum stresses of the analyzed frame, without and with 
damper installed, is depicted in the Fig. 22. Reponses such as displacement, velocity, and acceleration, 
etc. are shown in Figs. 23, 24, and 25, respectively [7].
