Abstract 
 
The purpose of this study is combining multi-walled carbon nanotubes with the traditional 
fibers to develop conductive fiber materials of carbon nanotubes with high added value. 
 
Carbon nanotubes are the thinnest tubes people know now. Besides the properties on 
physical and chemical, they also have outstanding representation. The properties of carbon 
nanotubes are high thermal conductivity, high strength, good electrical conductivity, high 
flexibility, and chemical stability. The study will treat carbon nanotubes with various parameters, 
and introduce them into the fibers with different concentrations. Utilizing the structures and 
properties of carbon nanotubes, the new functional fibers combining with carbon nanotubes are 
developed. The fibers contain the properties of carbon nanotubes such as high thermal 
conductivity, high strength, good electrical conductivity, and high flexibility. With connecting to 
the power supply, it can be also used as heated textile fibers, which can increase the value of 
fibers in the applications of clothing, decoration, and textile products. 
 
In this study, the electric property of composite increases with the addition of as-prepared 
MWCNTs. For now, the MWCNTs have been added up to 4% which shows better electric 
property and electromagnetic shielding. 
 
Keywords: carbon nanotube, conductive fiber, electromagnetic shielding 
 
人才培育成果說明： 
 
    此次參與的研究學生，除了學得奈米碳管的純化製程與技術，且培養出奈米碳管的改
質與分散方法，以及檢測奈米碳管及導電纖維的 SEM、TEM、Raman 和電學性質等分析技
術。學生對於材料已經具有可以進行微觀分析與特性量測的能力；以掃描式電子顯微鏡及
穿透式電子顯微鏡觀察材料內部結構的分佈情形及完整性；以拉曼光譜儀分析材料的結
構；以電性量測儀器分析材料的電學性質，並探討材料的結構、分散情形對於材料的關係。
最後，能綜合所有的實驗結果，歸納出最佳的製程條件，以得到具有較佳特性。這些技術
及訓練有助於學生之後就業時的能力，或進一步成為優秀的研究人才。 
 
技術研發成果說明： 
 
1. 完成開發奈米碳管之純化技術(酸洗及氧化處理)。 
2. 完成純化奈米碳管之微結構、物性與化性之分析。 
3. 完成開發奈米碳管之分散技術。 
4. 完成開發導電型纖維之紡紗製程技術。 
5. 完成導電型纖維之微結構、物性與化性之分析。

態導電纖維之最佳製程條件。
