CONCLUSION 
 
In summary we presented a simple approach to control the microdomain orientation of 
BCP-based supramolecular thin films. The approach involves the use of a variety of solvents 
with different solubility parameters to anneal thin films. The orientation of microdomains is 
tailored by the distinct interactions between solvents and the blocks of BCPs. We also 
provided a new route to obtain non-equilibrium nanostructures in BCP thin films by 
arranging a precursor supramolecule into a morphology different from its equilibrium 
morphology. This templated route should be applicable to other BCP-based supramolecules 
and may lead to novel non-equilibrium structure.
