Abstract 
Physical and chemical properties of LPD-TiO2 
films have been analyzed and show the good 
morphology and interface. The leakage current 
of 30nm LPD-TiO2 is 1.01x10-7A/cm2 at 1MV/cm. 
By C-V measurement, the calculated dielectric 
constant is 24.4 which is characteristic of high-k 
materials and minimum Dit is 7.48x1011 cm2eV-1. 
AlGaN/GaN MOSHFET with LPD-TiO2 gate 
dielectric is fabricated. It shows lower leakage 
current, higher breakdown voltage and gate 
swing voltage and can be operated at higher 
gate voltage to get higher IDMAX. At the same 
time, we have succeed grow Al2O3 on GaN with 
LPD method. We can use the annealing to 
achieve the minimum (rms=1.025 nm) surface, 
and small leakage current. 
 
Keywords: GaN, MBE, heterostructure FET, 
LPD, TiO2, Al2O3
