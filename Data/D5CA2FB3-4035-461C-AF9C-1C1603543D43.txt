(中文) 許多精密設備在機台上具有可移動之載具，會造成機台以隔振系統自然頻率晃動
而很慢才

然頻率的晃動， 
然而此會降低隔離地板振動的效果。本計畫擬開發的高性能雙氣室氣墊隔振器，
將具有獨特的 
動態特性，其在低頻範圍具有高阻尼常數，可以有效抑制因機台龍門移動引起之
隔振系統自然 
頻率的晃動，而在中高頻的範圍則具有低阻尼特性，即可達到隔離地板高頻率振
動的良好效果。 
首先進行雙氣室氣墊隔振器的理論模型研究，建立雙氣室容積、雙氣室之間的節
流閥孔隙、活 
塞面積、橡膠薄膜勁度等等參數與隔振器動態勁度與阻尼的理論關係式。接著研
究橡膠薄膜製 
作技術，改善現有橡膠薄膜的側向勁度問題，提升隔振器水平方向的隔振性能。
而為了確實掌 
握雙氣室氣墊隔振器的動態特性，建立動態性能測試系統以提供隔振器驗證能力
與改善方向， 
最後將實驗驗證隔振效能與動態特性。
(英文) Oscillation of advanced facilities at natural frequency of vibration isolation system will 
be induced by moving stage on them. It will affect the operation of these facilities if 
oscillation damps slowly. In general solution, traditional damper is used to retard the 
natural frequency oscillation. But it will deteriorate the performance of vibration 
isolation. This project is mainly developing a high performance dual-chamber pneumatic 
vibration isolator which possesses high damping character near the natural frequency of 
isolation system in order to dissipate the oscillation rapidly and low damping character at 
high frequency range to retain the high performance of vibration isolation from floor. The 
first step of this research is to design the mechanism of system and derive the theoretical 
equations which govern the system vibration behavior. Following, the complex stiffness 
of the diaphragm or a rubber membrane and fluid dynamics across and in the capillary 
tube should be studied.
