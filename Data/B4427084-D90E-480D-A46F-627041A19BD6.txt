台灣吊橋約有 300 座之多，多數為已經使用數十年以上的舊橋，但吊橋安全問題
一直未受到應有的重視，本文以新竹縣政府縣轄內之(人行)吊橋(約 30 座)為研
究對象，主要目標是： 
1.規劃基本資料建置所須的格式，用來簡化及統一吊橋管理資料，便利爾後管理。
2.討論目視安全檢測作業所須的檢測項目，用來有系統的瞭解吊橋長期劣化情
形，並進一步建立優選排序方法、維修補強經費表，以利後續管理及維護作業的
完整性。 
3.計提出「吊橋基本資料表」、「吊橋目視檢測表」、「吊橋優選排序方法」、「吊橋
儀器檢測方法」、「吊橋維修經費表」等 5個主要建議項目，供吊橋檢測及維護管
理的參考。 
這對提高吊橋公共安全的保障將有所助益。

The exploration and project of suspension bridge inspection and basic 
information generation. 
Abstract 
There are approximately 300 suspension bridges in Taiwan. Many of these old bridges 
have been in operation for decades. However there has been a lack of focus on the safety of 
these bridges. This study focuses on suspension bridges (Pedestrian) within the jurisdiction of 
the Hsinchu County Government (approximately 30 bridges). The main focus of this study is: 
1. The planning and creation of the data format in simplifying the management and of basic  
information for the facilitation of on-going information management. 
2. To discuss the required items in the operation of visual safety inspections. In a systematic  
approach, this will be used to understand the long-term deterioration of the bridges and 
allowing a further understanding in selection the optimal inspection method and maintenance 
cost format. This will also allow the integrity of long-term facility management.  
On some bridges however, the extent of damage cannot be simply determined by visual 
inspections. In these cases the appropriate instrumentation must be used in the inspection 
procedures to ensure the proper understanding in the integrity, damage and deterioration state 
of the bridge. This allows for the determination whether public safety has been affected. 
Therefore this study proposes further planning after visual inspections. Bridges can be sorted 
by deterioration ranking and recommendation (and handling) of equipment based inspection 
operations in allowing for the determination of the causes and the extent of the damage 
sustained by the bridge. In addition, the maintenance, reinforcement (or reconstruction) funding, 
limited and temporary maintenance can be effectively utilized allowing for the increase in 
public safety. This study surveys bridges within the Hsinchu Country jurisdiction. However 
through the analysis of results from this study, five recommended formats are proposed: 
suspension bridge basic information format, suspension bridge visual inspection format, ideal 
ranking methods for suspension bridges, Usage of suspension bridge inspection equipment and 
suspension bridge cost format. This allows reference information for bridge inspection and 
managements. It is anticipated that information on suspension bridge construction and 
inspection will allow for the following of effective maintenance and reinforcement and 
ultimately allow for the protection of public safety.  tourism.  
Key words：suspension, basic information format, visual inspection format, ideal 
ordering method, maintenance cost format.

本文於檢測、調查、研究過程中發覺部份值得建議事項： 
1.吊橋管理方式可不需與 TBMS 結合： 
目前吊橋管理單位並未統一，若以(方便各單位自行)維護管理角度，建議爾後各管理
單位只將自管吊橋單獨建檔，應可以不考慮與 TBMS 結合。但管理者亦可視需要，將資
料輸入 GIS 系統圖層中，而達到管理應用的目的。 
2.新橋應保留竣工圖及歷史資料： 
本計畫調查檢測過程中發覺部份吊橋原始設計資料(竣工圖、主索索力、抗風索索力、
設計長度等)蒐集不易，因此建議後續興建之吊橋應該保留竣工圖電子檔。而未來進行
之各檢測歷史資料也應妥善保管。 
3.除新竹縣之外，建議其他各縣市亦應著手轄區內吊橋的檢測及資料建置，終究吊橋使 
用者的生命財產安全，不應小於公路橋梁的規定。 
4.鋼索內部狀況無法以目視檢測確認，建議未來後續研究可探討如何檢測鋼索內部狀況。 
5.吊橋基本資料應加上設計資料，如設計載重、設計風力等，建議修正為表 6-1 所示。
