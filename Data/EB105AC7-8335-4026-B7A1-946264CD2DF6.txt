中文摘要 
 
如何達成有效率的混合對於許多運用於生物科技、分析化學以及醫學科技等相關領域的
微流體裝置來說，是非常重要的。通常被動式微混合器靠著適當的流道幾何結構與放置於流
道中之障礙物，可將流體分離與再次地結合，如此流體不但可減少擴散路徑更可增進混合的
效能。本研究目標為探討經由放置於混合器流道邊界之凸出結構物，以增強兩不同測試流體
的混合效果。數值計算中，兩流體被視為、不可壓縮、可溶混層流，其傳輸特性均勻固定且
忽略重力的影響與溫度的變化。理論模型建立於三維暫態之質量、動量守恆方程式、及物種
傳輸方程式；並採用迭代數值解法 SIMPLEC演算法於求解統御方程式以計算各流場參數。
不同軸向位置之所預測之混合效率結果將與相關文獻之測量結果作比對驗證；此外，另將詳
細探究微混合器內混合發展過程之暫態流場及所需要之時間。研究結果指出邊界凸出物之鄰
近區域呈現顯著的渦度產生，故沿著流場方向增強其混合效率。為了獲得微混合器設計最佳
化，數值實驗將進一步研究微混合器內邊界凸出物的寬度、長度、擺放之位置、及形狀對增
強混合效率的影響。 
 
 
關鍵詞：微流體、微混合器、CFD模擬

微混合器之混合過程只能依靠分子間的擴散

著混沌移流之橫向渦漩來增強其混合效能。 
二、研究目的  
由前文獻回顧中可發現多數學者採用多
樣化阻塊置放於流道內之被動式微混合器，
其混合效能主要取決於阻塊大小、形狀及其
所擺放之位置等參數，而研究結果指出菱形
阻塊為最佳之障礙物，且需以交錯式放置方
可獲得良好混合效果；相對地卻只有非常少
數學者延伸研究微混合器擺放阻塊後，再更
配合安置邊界凸出物設計對整個微流體裝置
混合效率的進一步影響。本論文研究重點：
(1)針對微混合器-1(無配置邊界凸出物)進行
實驗與數值計算以驗證微流道內嵌對稱式與
交錯式菱形阻塊微混合器之混合性能優劣比
較；(2)提出一新型配置邊界凸出物之微混合
器-2 設計，並依據理論分析以探究此被動式
微混合器內部流場特性及其幾何與操作條件
改變下對混合效果影響。本文將利用計算流
體力學(Computational Fluid Dynamics, CFD)
軟體 ACE+®以進行數值模擬，計算結果將與
Bhagat et al. [30]實驗數據比對以驗證理論模
型和計算機程式之精確度，另將設計出交錯
式菱形阻塊結合邊界凸出物之微混合器構型，
改變邊界凸出物之寬度、長度、擺放位置、
以及其形狀等參數以完成微混合器之最佳
化。 
三、研究方法  
3.1基本假設及統御方程式 
本研究針對邊界凸出效應對於被動式菱
形阻塊微混合器的混合流場解析，數值模擬
軟體則採用 ACE+®程式為分析工具。為了簡
化處理複雜混合流場所發展之理論模型，本
研究所做的基本假設如下： 
1. 三維(3-Dimensional)、穩態(Steady)且為不
可壓縮(Incompressible)之混合流； 
2. 工作流體為牛頓流體(Newtonian Fluids)； 
3. 層流(Laminar Flow)、等溫流場(Isothermal 
Flowfield)； 
4. 不考慮物體力(Body Force)； 
5. 混合過程不考慮化學反應產生； 
6. 擴散係數 (Diffusion coefficient)、密度
(Density)及黏滯係數(Viscosity)為定值，不
隨濃度分佈而改變；  
7. 壁面與流體之間無滑移現象。  
基於以上的假設，並假設流體性質不隨
著濃度變化而改變，故我們可以推導出所需
要的統御方程式，如下：

3.5實驗步驟 
全域照亮式微流場可視化實驗步驟： 
1. 調配相同容積的去離子水與微量玫瑰紅
(Rhodamine B)染色液，並以燒杯將其倒
入至直徑 22 mm之針筒中並放置於注射
幫浦上。 
2. 將微混合器固定至顯微鏡的三軸量測平
台上，並將注射幫浦兩端(去離子水與玫
瑰紅染色液)與微混合器連接。 
3. 調整注射幫浦之設定至與實驗參數相
同。 
4. 開啟可錄影式類比訊號黑白 CCD。 
5. 將雷射光束導入所要觀察的微流道區域
進行全域照亮。 
6. 以 10 倍物鏡(工具距離為 33.5 mm)找到
顯微鏡下最清楚的焦距。 
7. 打開影像擷取系統軟體。 
8. 啟動 NE-1600針筒式幫浦。 
9. 待微混合器流場達到穩定後，移動量測
平台並開始拍攝微混合器從入口至出口
的混合情形。 
10. 將所擷取之影像，匯入至分析軟體並判
讀其灰階分佈。 
11. 將所得之數據代入混合指標之公式並求
取其效率值。

量、壓力等基本流場參數及濃度分佈，故須

本文所探討之邊界凸出物外形，分別為方形、

μm、寬度(w)為 40 μm，並在相同模擬條件
(Re=10)下，進行混合效率相互比較。圖 24 為
不同幾何外形的邊界凸出物沿軸向距離之混
合效率比較圖，結果顯示加入邊界凸出物之
微混合器，其混合效率評比分別為方塊形邊

文將對方塊形邊界凸出物之微混合器做進一
步最佳化參數研究。 
首先我們探討方形邊界凸出物之長度(l)
對混合之影響，因此我們將基本長度(50 μm)
分別減少 40%及增加 40%，即改變邊界凸出
物長度至 30 μm與 70 μm做相關探討，圖 25 
展示方形邊界凸出物之長度變異對混合效率
影響(a)Re= 10 下軸向混合效率分佈圖(b)在
0.1<Re<100下, x= 5 mm之混合效率圖，由匯
流處至出口之效率分佈，其中最大差別 3%發
生在 x= 5 mm處；而邊界凸出物長度 30 μm、
50 μm、70 μm在雷諾數範圍 0.1~100下於 x=5 
mm處之效率圖，其中最大差異約 2.7%，且
發生在雷諾數為 100。綜括而論，改變邊界
凸出物之長度對於提升整體微混合器之效率
沒有助益。圖 26 為方形邊界凸出物寬度變
異對混合效率影響，Re= 10下軸向混合效率
分佈圖，此處基本 40 μm 寬度也分別減少
50%為 20 μm以及增加 50%至 60 μm，結果
顯示在邊界凸出物寬度為 60 μm時，其混合
效率於 x= 5 mm時，約只有 68%，而 20 μm
與 40 μm卻已到達 82%之效率。不僅如此，
在不同雷諾數下(x= 5 mm)時，20 μm的寬度
在各個雷諾數下皆能擁有較高之混合效率，
其對 40 μm及 60 μm所造成的最大影響差異
在各雷諾數下(0.1~100)分別為 10.5%、13%、
20%、以及 4.8%。因此可看出在中低的雷諾
數範圍下邊界凸出物之寬度對於微混合器之
混合效果提昇有相當大的助益。

In a simple T-sensor, Kamholz et al. (1999) 
resolved the concentration of a target analysis by 
measuring fluorescence intensity with an analytic 
model presented to determine the mixing behavior 
from the analytic indicator and analytic-indicator 
complex diffusion coefficients as well as from the 
complex formation kinetics. Gray et al. (1999) 
formulated a novel technology based on a 
combination of silicon fusion bonding and deep 
reactive ion etching to manufacture fluidic circuit 
boards. A multi-level laminating mixer was also 
realized to demonstrate this technology. Good 
agreement was obtained between the simulated 
and measured mixing properties for different dyed 
water streams at a flow rate of 60 µl/min. 
Ismagilov et al. (2000) observed the diffusive 
mixing phenomenon at the interfaces between the 
phases in laminar flows. A scaling law was 
proposed to predict the spatially resolved 
transport processes to design passive 
micromixers. Wu et al. (2004) utilized a nonlinear 
model to optimize microfluidic chips related to 
diffusive mixing in a microchannel. To achieve 
rapid mixing in passive micromixers, obstacle 
structures were inserted into microchannels to 
enhance the advection effect by splitting, 
stretching, breaking and folding liquid flows 
throughout the mixing progression (Mengeaud 
et al., 2002; Kockmann et al., 2006; Ansari et al., 
2010; Kittisak and Ramana, 2010; Park et al., 
2010). To develop mixing at low Reynolds 
numbers (Re), Bhagat et al. (2007) also designed, 
simulated, fabricated and characterized a planar 
passive microfluidic mixer with integrated 
diamond-shaped obstructions within the 
microchannel to break-up and recombine the 
flow. Simulated and measured results of the 
proposed micromixer demonstrated good mixing 
operations over a wide range of Re values 
(numerically: 0.01<Re<100, experimentally: 
0.02<Re<10). 
Most previous studies have examined the mixing 
properties of passive micromixers with diverse 
geometric shapes of obstructions arranged in the 
flow channel. Furthermore, previous numerical 
studies of the mixing process have emphasized on 
elucidating the relationship between the vortices 
and rapid mixing. For example, Wong et al. 
(2003) performed computer simulations and 
found that rapid mixing of two liquids can be 
achieved with the inclusion of boundary 
protrusion elements at the outlet channel of the 
cross-shaped micromixer. Wong et al. (2004) also 
observed that the fast mixing can be explained by 
the asymmetrical flow conditions at the inlets and 
generation of vortices and secondary flow at the 
junction only. However, the present study extends 
to investigate the flow-turning induced vortices 
around the corners of obstacle and protruded 
boundaries as well as to perform optimization of 
the boundary protrusion for mixing enhancement. 
The goal of this paper is to explore the influence 
of boundary protrusion structures on the mixing 
process of a micromixer with diamond-shaped 
obstacles in microchannels. To simulate the 
inherently peculiar transport phenomenon in a 
passive micromixer with a geometrically complex 
design configuration. The predicted H2O molar 
concentration contours and mixing indexes at 
different longitudinal locations were compared 
with the measured results by Bhagat et al. (2007) 
for software validation. Numerical experiments 
were extended to investigate the effects of shape, 
length, width and placement of boundary 
protrusions on mixing enhancement of a passive 
micromixer with diamond-shaped obstacles.

4.3 Description of mixing flowfield

implanted with boundary protrusions, Fig. 5

obstructions, the main stream velocity appeared to 
accelerate as the flow turned. The level of turning 
and varied cross sectional area controlled the 
degree of acceleration, and in turn affected the 
vorticity strength of the induced vortex. Elevated 
vorticity values were identified around the corners 
of obstacle and boundary protrusions because of 
the flow turning effect. Thus, the fluids were 
expected to mix effectively by vortices agitation, 
which folded and stretched the fluids spirally to 
increase the interfacial area. This flow 
acceleration phenomenon became even more

Micromixers have been commonly utilized in the areas of 
analytical chemistry, biotechnical engineering and medical 
industry. In practice, passive micromixers are dependent on 
the apposite arrangement of channel geometric shapes with 
obstacles in microchannels to break-up and recombine the 
flow and decrease the diffusion path to improve the mixing 
performance. In this study, we proposed a novel application 
of employing the shape memory alloy (SMA) to change the 
cross section of flow channel, and thereby develop a tunable 
mixer for mixing control. With activation or deactivation of 
SMA serving as a switch, the mixing of different fluids in a 
microchannel can be proceeded or discontinued in response 
to operating needs. To demonstrate the feasibility of design 
concept before realization of the proposed device, numerical 
simulations were performed to examine the mixing process 
of two test fluids flowing through a passive micromixer with 
in-line obstacles produced by a pair of truncated cone SMA 
samples. The predictions revealed that the mixing efficiency 
of 71% was attained at the baseline case with the diameter 
and height of truncated cone protrusions of 25 and 15 μm.  
1 Introduction  
Shape memory alloys (SMA) have been successfully applied 
in different areas. As compared to the traditional design using 
large-scale pattern change of the SMA module, the application 
with the local shape change of SMA is rarely seen for design 
uses. The local shape change of SMA is usually introduced by 
the indentation of spherical or conical indenter on the surface of 
SMA [1]. The indentation depth on the surface varies reversibly 
as the SMA endures temperature variation associated with the 
martensite-austenite phase transformation. This two-way shape 
memory effect (TWSME) is related to the preferred nucleation 
and growth of the directional martensite variants with the SMA 
cooled from the austenite phase [2,3]. Alternatively, the more 
recently discovered magnetically-driven ferromagnetic shape 
memory alloy (FSMA) has received growing interest because it 
can produce a large strain and faster response without change in 
the external temperature [4,5].  
In this study, we proposed a novel application of using SMA 
to change the cross-sectional configuration of a microchannel, 
and thereby develop a tunable micromixer for mixing control. 
With activation or deactivation of SMA serving as a switch, the 
mixing process of different fluids in a microchannel can be then 
proceeded or discontinued in response to operating needs. The 
goal of this paper is to investigate the effect of pair cone-shaped 
obstructions (formed by circular protrusions of SMA samples) 
on the mixing process for verifying the conceptual feasibility of 
design before realization of the device. The computational fluid 
dynamics (CFD) software ACE+® was utilized for conducting 
numerical calculations. The mixing properties were computed 
for unilateral circular protrusions of SMA at different heights 
and diameters.  
2 Design  
Insertion of obstacles in microchannels can breakup flows to 
induce the transverse velocity, and thereby reduce the diffusion 
path for mixing enhancement [6]. To enhance the mixing effect, 
this study applies the SMA to generate circular protrusions for 
forming obstacles within the microchannel. Fig. 1shows (a) the 
circular protrusions after an indented and planarized bulk NiTi 
sample heated to the austenite phase [3], and (b) schematic of 
circular protrusions in a SMA-tuned micromixer. Pair of SMA 
100-μm diameter round samples was vertically placed at the top 
and bottom flat surfaces of the channel with concurrent circular 
protrusions facing each other directly to develop a connected 
intrusive micro-pillar in the channel, causing the disturbance of 
fluids along the flow course for mixing improvement. Similar 
to the design of Zhang et al. in Fig. 1(a) [3], the diameter and 
height of the truncated cone protrusions were prescribed as 25 
and 15 μm for the baseline simulation.

Abstract 
Micro-scale mixing is crucially important for chemical or biological analytical systems since it is this mixing 
which produces biochemical reactions necessary for their successful operation. However, rapid and effective 
mixing in miniature fluidic devices remains a continuing challenge. To facilitate the design of a novel vortex 
mixer, the CFD simulations serve as a powerful tool to provide insights on the inherently complex swirling 
dynamics of mixing realized with our simple semispherical geometry. Pair of streams tangentially flowing 
through the inlets into the mixer can generate a rolling vortex resided upright in the core region for mixing 
enhancement in the mixer. The predictions also indicate that the mixing efficiency of the vortex mixer can 
attain 88% at Re= 500.

三、 實驗方法 
過往相關研究所提出之微混合器元件腔體結構
與製程均相對複雜，且因低雷諾數操作條件之擴
散混合致使工作時間較長[12-14]。本文提出新型
模組化渦漩混合器設計，圖 1所示為混合器上下
兩件式翻模製作模具組合示意圖，此腔體結構簡
單，開孔處為混合器的流道，基本上備置兩個入
口和一個出口，且利用高雷諾數對流方式進行混
合，故而大幅減短混合時間。有別於一般微機電
製程無法製作出球狀腔體的限制，本研究採取翻
模製具方式以達到容易脫模且可以長期重複使
用為目標，故可製作出如矩形體、圓柱和球體等
各種形狀的混合腔體設計。模組化渦漩混合元件
尺寸為 20×20×10mm，流道直徑為 0.8mm，實際
成品陳列於圖 2。混合器本體係以 PDMS (10:1 w/ 
w, Sylgard 184 silicone elastomer, Dow Corning)製
作，材質具有極佳可塑性及透光性、固化後亦顯
現良好彈性和與生物相容的特性。實驗分析主要
是利用針筒式幫浦驅動淡藍色與紅色染劑至微
混合器腔體，接著使用高速攝影機拍攝出口管於
x= 1mm 之截面及混合器腔體，分析不同流速下
腔體內各截面的混合流場現象。至於拍攝影像尚
可利用 Matlab®程式轉成灰階值，再代入式(4)計
算出混合指標 σE。 
 
四、 結果與討論 
模組化渦漩混合器設計過程中，本研究使用計算
流體力學軟體分析流場混合機制，達到數值模擬
輔助混合腔體設計目的。圖 3為矩形體衝擊式混
合器之網格圖，其混合腔體長×寬×高尺寸為
6×6×8.5mm。兩端入口與出口皆位於高 1.5mm水
平面，圓管之直徑與長度分別為 0.8 與 10mm，
而數值網格總數為 391168格點。

五、 結論 
本研究以 CFD輔助設計一半球體渦漩混合器，使
入出口端與腔體配置成切線入口流與垂直出口
流設計，可成功達到強烈渦旋的極佳混合效果。
考慮穩態流操作條件與 1≦Re≦1000範圍內，數
值模擬分析結果顯示此新穎混合器於雷諾數 200
時即達 83%混合效率。平均 Re= 500時混合效率
可達約 88%；而高雷諾數時幾可高達完全混合。
本研究提供一高混合效率渦旋式混合器設計，隨
著此高便利性之模組化微流元件開發，可實質助
益未來化學或生醫領域上之應用。

14.本申請案之特色(一)：發明領域及創作目的(Field and Purpose of the Invention) 
(本發明(創作)技術之應用範疇及創作目的或可達成之功效) 
近年來生物晶片、化學分析晶片的持續研發已經帶動微流道裝置相關的研究工作，小於
1mm 甚至是 500 微米以下的裝置微小化與整合，使得如信用卡大小的檢測裝置具有減少分析
時間、減少樣品體積、降低檢驗成本及增加檢驗速率等特點。但檢驗的成功與否卻因為在此尺
度下的擴散係數不高，常與控制流體的流動與混合有極大的關係。在微尺度下流體間的快速混
合常因雷諾數太低、擴散係數太小而顯得困難，尤其是流體中混有大分子量之溶質。一般而言，
流體的黏滯力造成在微尺度下為層流流動，主要的混合機制為擴散，流體在微尺度下的混合所
需的時間需在數十秒之譜。換句話說，低雷諾數下常使紊流混合難以進行，但快速混合又是在
生化檢測技術上不可缺少之要素，這些困難對生化晶片而言是一項極大的挑戰。 
15.本申請案之特色(二)：欲解決之問題及其技術特點(Advantages of the Invention) 
(本發明(創作)技術所能解決之習知技術的問題及其與 習知技術不同之特點) 
基於上述問題，陸陸續續有不同之混合器設計裝置被提出。根據應用之需求與設計原理，
一般微混合器可分為主動式(active)與被動式(passive)，一般被動式微混合器利用流道幾何形狀
的設計去增加流體間之混合接觸介面，提升混合效果。主動式微混合器主要是設計有作動機件
或如壓力、電場力等外力所驅動之裝置。有作動機件之混合器在製造上一般難度相當高，目前
也僅限於半導體矽製程。而部分使用電場產生電泳、電滲透驅動之混合裝置，也可有效增加流
體間之介面積、加強混合效果。 
前述習用設計中，被動式設計使用時雖不需外加驅動能源，但混合效果一般較主動式為
差，且若有操作中變換混合方式之需求，則無法提供控制之彈性。具有運動件之主動式微混合
器設計，雖然混合效果較佳，但如前所述目前僅限於以半導體製程製作，材料材質受到限制，
且製作上複雜程度也高。因此，一種具有主動式混合器之作動件，可達到增加混合效果，但相
對可以較簡單之製程完成製作，且控制簡單的微混合器設計就可以顯現其優點。
