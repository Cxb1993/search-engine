一、 動機與目的 
1. 研究動機 
本研究將探討動機區分為二大面
向，包括：「研究特殊性」及「經營管
理階段之缺失」等，進而針對各要項
進行概論原由說明，並輔以相關統計
資訊與現況缺失，突顯出我國現存問
題的重要性與亟待改善處，相關說明
如下所述。 
(一) 研究特殊性 
本研究針對國內現行之維護管理
與設施管理等課題，進行初步之了解
後得知，其工程之屬性大多為建築工
程類別，由於建築工程雖然普遍因工
程種類複雜、建築型態多元化以及機
能需求轉變之故，進而衍生出諸多繁
雜且極度專業性的重要課題，但就我
國目前於維護管理之研究領域內，仍
有諸多的研究課題已具相當發展優勢
與雛型；但進一步檢視之，就其研究
內容與實質課題上，卻未有針對「大
型展演場所」之相關研究，更遑論其
相關細部問題之研究與作業流程。 
因此，本研究乃針對大型展演場
所進行初步統計後得知，國內於此類
研究課題顯為極少數外，於大型展演
場所之研究內容而言，亦僅止於委外
經營與績效指標、公共政策與制度規
範、日常節能與結構設計、BOT 之決
策與應用、規劃設計與選址以及服務
品質與顧客滿意度等課題之探討(圖
1)，對於大型展演場所之設計管理階
段、經營管理乃至資產管理等關鍵階
段，亦無相關可供參考之範本與計
劃，因此本研究將針對此課題之黃金
轉換期進行深入了解與探討，並以物
業管理作為輔助研析之理念，如何透
過機制之建立與策略的管理作為改善
當前之窘境，即為本研究之關鍵課題。

本研究將以案例分析的方式進行
探討，主要以台北小巨蛋作為研究範

探討之案例如下所述。 
(一) 台北小巨蛋(15,000 人座) 
為提昇國人的生活品質和增進健
康體能，並追求多元且均衡發展之
故，以帶動文化體育產業與週邊相關
經濟效益，進而提昇台北市於區域整
合發展與國際地位，臺北市政府於
2005年斥資47億餘元興建一座符合國
際標準的現代化大型多功能展演場
所，並期望透過全台最大且新穎之國
際性多功能體育場館落成之際，冀能
成為我國國際運動賽事、藝文活動、
商業展期之產業平台，進而活絡國內
之休閒運動風氣與文化觀光產業，以
及帶動社會繁榮及相關經濟效益。檢
視其建築構造與相關設備均相當複雜
且多元，對於建築物及其相關附屬設
施之維護管理，更顯重要，甚至於建
築物資產價值之維護與提昇，關鍵均

即為本研究之推論觀點，因此，將針
對此部分進行深入之探討。

1. 研究理念與執行依據 
將依據建築物管理預算的編列架
構做分類探討，探討內容包括：目前
所執行之大分類是否適切以及有所缺
漏，小分類是否足夠照護建物整體或
有多餘項目之產生，即對分類的原則
與邏輯進行流程思考，並以建築實例
進行評估與成本調整，嘗試建構其最
佳化之評估項目，以落實應用效果。 
進而透過生命週期應用流程之比
較與分析，於各階段流程探討生命週
期之變動與影響因子；由於建築物之
年度預算編列是動態性質，均可能因
為管理機關、不同屋齡階段、不同建
築構造、建築設備的有無、服務品質、
使用類型差距、管理模式之異質性等
因素，而產生相當金額差距之決預算。 
因此，本研究認為應於架構、流
程、項目上重新檢視之，進而以作為
考量的重點所在，回饋至預算預測之
應用模式，除可更有效掌控標準與品
質外，亦能發揮高效能之作業。

(三) 台北捷運公司經營管理階段【OT
模式】 
台北小巨蛋在台北市政府接管一
年後，即委由台北市政府的轉投資公
司台北捷運公司進行接手管理（97 年
9 月至 99 年 12 月），經本研究實地訪
查後得知，因機電、空調設備為場館
營運的重點管理維護項目，遂委由具
備機電工程背景的台北捷運公司負責
管理。 
台北捷運公司主要負責場館的現
場管理、服務管理、機電及空調設備
維護，而主場館的檔期安排及副館冰
宮營運，亦由文化基金會的組織成員
繼續負責。 
(四) 小結 
綜上所述，對於三個不同單位的
經營管理階段，可得知於東森集團經
營時期，組織介面較簡單，組織形成
亦較為明確，且相關事業體多，能因
應場館功能上的需求；台北市政府經
營時期，因屬緊急臨時編置，沒有所
謂的磨合運作時期，對於場館的營運
作業可視為僅是維持現況；台北捷運
公司經營時期，與市府較大不同點僅
為，現場管理由原本的營管中心轉變
為台北捷運公司，組織成員大多是原
班人馬，此舉只是凸顯公辦公營模式
在行政作業上的手法轉換。

THE STUDY OF DEVELOPMENT TREND FOR THE  
TAIWAN’S PROPERTY MANAGEMENT INDUSTRY 
 
A. Chang-Sian, Li*, B. Sy-Jye, Guo** 
 
*Ph.D. Candidate, Department of Civil Engineering, National Taiwan University, Taipei, Taiwan 
**Professor, Department of Civil Engineering, National Taiwan University, Taipei, Taiwan 
 
 
ABSTRACT: With the help of advanced technology and diversity demand for living environments, building has also 
been refined and specialized in the direction towards evolution. The Simple building clean, mechanical and electrical 
maintenance and safety management and other routine management services were unable to meet the needs and changes 
of the users or owners of buildings. Therefore the property management service is derived. As Taiwan move toward and 
synchronize with the world in the property management industry, its innovative and visionary concept will lead Taiwan 
to the replacement and major changes for the existing concept of property management and even cause the changing 
and transferring problems of the industrial structure. 
The study will focus on the characteristic, implementation strategy and development profile of the Taiwan's property 
management industry. The SWOT method is then applied for analyzing and understanding the strength, weakness, 
opportunity and threat of the property management industry. This will help analyze the development potential of the 
property management and even do the difference analysis including the number and type of the company, the number of 
working population, the production and etc. In view of the current market mechanism and the related execution 
mistakes for studying. Hope to come up with the structural integrity of information related to property management 
improvement advises. So that related departments or owners will use this study as a reference material when 
implementing the service procurement of the property management. 
 
Keywords: Property Management, Industrial Structure, Operation Mechanism, Enforcement Strategy. 
 
1. INTRODUCTION 
Property management is aimed to do the 
maintenance management and omni bearing operation 
for the building hardware and other software that 
provides services for the community and living 
environment. In another word, property management is 
the management of all the related activities of the 
building. Its aim is to make certainty for the building to 
be clean, healthy, comfort, convenient and to be 
environmental friendly. Discussion is given on the 
development trend and industry status of property 
management. 
 
1.1 THE SERVICE FOR PROPERTY 
MANAGEMENT 
According to the conclusion from the property 
management industry meeting of the Ministry of the 
Interior, the property management industry is defined 
into three groups: 
(1) First group: The maintenance and management of 
the building environment, which provide the 
building with environment maintenance, cleaning, 
security, public safety inspection, overhaul of the 
fire safety equipment and accessory equipment and 
other services. 
(2) Second group: The Life and business support 
services, which provide the Property agents, 
advisory industry, life service, life product, 
business support and other service. 
(3) Third group: The property management, which 
provide real estate business advisory, leasing and 
investment management.

DEVELOPMENT STRATEGY 
According to No.2914 property management 
industry development and planning meeting of Executive

strategy for the development and planning of the 
industry.

(1) Confirm the area of property management 
service. 
(2) Place importance on personnel training. 
(3) Provide environment incentives and 
development stage. 
(4) Establish an innovative research award. 
(5) Expanding the international market. 
(6) Review the laws and regulations. 
 
3.2 DEVELOPMENT AIMS 
(1) The employment population of property 
management industry will increase from 250 
thousand to 350 thousand within five years. 
(2) To provide 60 thousand professionals for the 
property management services within five 
years.

5.3 DIFFICULTIES OF DEVELOPMENT 
The study show that the difficulties of 
development for the property management company are: 
(1) The quality of the services provided by the 
property management company can not 
satisfy the owners who aspire to improve 
living quality. As the grows of economy, 
living standard increased. The importance is 
place on having a safety and comfortable 
environment for the community. 
(2) The implementation of property 
management is a system transform. It will 
make great changes to the interests between 
different groups. This change will cause 
chaos. 
(3) Policy which is behind schedule will cause 
contradiction. 
(4) The Aberration of Public opinions and 
society recognition make difficulties for the 
property management.
