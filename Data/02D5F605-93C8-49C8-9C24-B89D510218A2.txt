一、 摘要 
 
利用用脈診儀的脈波圖形協助中醫進行醫學診療判是目前中醫現代化的趨勢，然而現有脈
診儀脈象偵測的感應器選用上有先天上缺失，一者就是它所測得的是垂直脈管壁的壓力脈
波動訊號，它無法捕捉由心血管傳遞出來的全部壓力脈動訊息，此為心臟功能與血管功能
的重要指標；其次是由此壓力脈波訊號所得的傅立葉轉換分析頻譜不夠完整，無法完整反
映出由心臟驅動之人體血流動力學系統中的全部器官與組織之供血狀況。有鑑於此，本研
究根據血液流體動力學原理完成脈聲儀系統之開發，利用微型感測器透過皮膚測量撓動脈
脈波變化，此訊號中包含了人體血流動力系統中任何的動態壓力變化訊息；由分析此訊號
之原始波型配合心電圖與心導管測得之血壓圖同步比對可辨別此波型所代表之心臟與血管
方面的各種動態功能訊息，以及人體血流動力系統中連接器官與組織的各個分支血管之相
對供血動態狀況，此兩方面的資訊將可協助醫師提供更詳實的人體心血管疾病的醫學診療
與判斷，此兩方面的資訊將可協助醫師提供更詳實的人體心血管相關功能的醫學診療與判
斷。 
 
關鍵字: 脈聲儀、血流動力學、微型感測器、心血管疾病 
 
 
Abstract 
 
  The application of an radial arterial pulse diagnostic instrument for medication is the state of 
art in Chinese medical examination technology. However, owing to the improper selection of the 
sensing element in these instruments, it can only detect the mean radial arterial pressure wave and 
can not catch the detailed pressure wave signal of the cardiovascular system. The spectrum 
obtained by Fourier transformation of it can not offer the complete hemodynamics behavior of 
the cardiovascular system. On resolving this problem, an Audiocardiography system has been 
developed and tested based on the hemodynamics theory. The sound of blood pressure wave 
which is originated from hart and being propogating along the radial artery has been measured 
and examined. This signal contains the detailed hemodynamics information in a cardiovascular 
system. This data is vital for the diagnostic on the cardiac disorder. The digital signal analysis of 
the data obtained can unveil the detailed hemodynamics behavior. The analysis of signal in-line 
with ECG wave and mean arterial pressure wave delivers detailed information corresponding to 
the cardiovascular system. This device can offer an early diagnostic on the cardiac disorder.  
 
Keywords: Audiocardiography、hemodynamics、micro sensor、cardiac disorder
