計畫中文摘要 
牙鑽手機是引進高壓空氣進入牙鑽後，高壓空氣便會衝擊空氣渦輪葉片並帶動轉子旋
轉，這時渦輪轉子帶動鑽頭產生扭力矩以輪磨牙齒；一般渦輪轉子轉速高達幾十萬轉，治
療過程中，渦輪轉子葉片高速轉動摩擦就有噪音出現，必須設法減低噪音或防噪音產生，
以降低病人的緊張。 
本計畫依據 ISO 7785-1提及的牙鑽手機噪音量測規範，建立一套牙鑽手機噪音測試分
析系統，針對目前市售國外與國內自製之牙鑽手機進行噪音測試分析，主要目的為評定各
廠牌牙鑽手機噪音的聲壓位準，客觀比較同類產品的性能，此外最重要是設法找出牙鑽手
機之主要噪音源，作為其噪音控制之參考依據。 
測試結果顯示各牙鑽噪音皆無超過標準值 80 dBA，僅有國內 T廠牌超過規範的建議值
65 dBA，再由頻譜相關法比對牙鑽於相同轉速下之噪音與振動頻譜，發現轉子轉動產生的
結構振動為主要噪音來源，因此顯著改善噪音之方法為提高牙鑽轉子的平衡以減少機件摩
擦所產生的高頻結構噪音。 
關鍵字：牙鑽手機、高壓空氣、渦輪、噪音 
計畫英文摘要 
Air turbine aeroacoustics has been the key to improve the noise problem and performance of 
dental handpieces. The objective of this study is to establish a systematic approach for dental air 
turbine aeroacoustics testing and analysis. The dental air turbine testing consists of an air supply 
system, dental handpiece support system, a dynamic noise mesurement system, and a PC based 
data acquisition system.The dental air aeroacoustics analysis starts with an unsteady aerodynamics 
model to calculate the forcing function acting on the turbine blade passing frequecncy, which can 
be used to predict the noise level of the dental handpiece. This testing and analysis can provide 
ISO 7785-1guidance for dental handpiece noise reduction and performance upgrade. 
The existing dental handpieces will be used as the baseline-testing vehicles for aeroacoustics 
testing purpose(80dBA). Extensive measurements of the handpieces can provide a representative 
database for the current technology level(65dBA). With air turbine geometry and flow condition 
as input to the unsteady aerodynamics model, the unsteady forcing functions can be directively 
calculated. The sound pressure level can then be calculated to compare with the corresponding test 
results. Once we have a systematic model established, the model can be used to perform 
parametric study of the current air turbine design. Not only this approach can provide solutions to 
industry dental handpiece noise problems, but also provide guidance for future dental air turbine 
design to improve the current performance. 
 keyword：dental handpiece, air turbine, rotor blade, aeroacoustics, unsteady aerodynamics

五. 結論 
本計畫除了建立一套牙鑽手機噪音測試與分析系統外，並且由頻譜相關法比對牙鑽於
相同轉速下之噪音與振動頻譜，發現轉子轉動產生的結構振動為主要噪音來源，所以顯著
改善噪音之方法為提高牙鑽轉子的平衡以減少機件摩擦所產生的高頻結構噪音。 
除以上所述之外，實驗過程中軸承使用狀況亦是本研究關切之要題，諸如軸承造成的
阻力或磨擦會耗損掉一部分之能量，致使轉速與噪音等性能受其影響甚大，因此實驗前軸
承的潤滑以及隨時注意軸承使用情形是相當重要之工作。
