中文摘要 
 
本計畫進行自蔓延燃燒合成(SHS)之熱導度模式及燃燒模式研究，所採用的手段為透過熱導度及
SHS 之實驗量測數據與現存之理論模式結果比對及評估，並嘗試求得較佳之模式，以進行若干關鍵參
數之 SHS 過程數值模擬，其結果將與實驗值比對，以驗證模式的準確性。本研究所採用之數值方法包
括控制體積法、Crank-Nicolson 算則、Runge-Kutta 算則、TDMA 法等。鑑於先前權威論文明白指出，
熱傳導性質及熱化學反應動力學為反應物之異質微結構影響 SHS 動態最鉅之說法，本計畫所進行之研
究方向應為正確無誤。本計畫之研究目的為藉由針對 SHS 試片進行熱導度之實驗量測，來評估前人模
擬 SHS 過程所採用之熱導度模式，以及近年學者所提之異種介質熱導度模式，嘗試從中挑選出適當之
熱導度模式。此外，藉由與 SHS 實驗數據比對，來評估前人模擬 SHS 過程所採用燃燒模式之適用性，
並參酌此些模式嘗試提出新的燃燒模式。經研究發現試片之熱傳導度對於 SHS 之模擬而言，扮演著關
鍵角色，而較複雜的燃燒模式是必須的。

英文摘要 
 
In this project, existing thermal conductivity models and combustion models for self-propagating 
High-Temperature Synthesis (SHS) were compared with experimental data to evaluate their validity and the 
models were tested to find the optimal ones. Then, the optimal models of thermal conductivity and 
combustion were incorporated to explore the numerical simulations of SHS process with consideration the 
effects of several key parameters. For the numerical model, the finite volume method, Crank-Nicolson scheme, 
Runge-Kutta Scheme, and TDMA method are employed. According to the disclosure of authoritative 
literature, microsturcture of heterogeneous reaction mixture influences combustion process via variations in 
thermal conductivity and kinetics of exothermal chemical reaction. The direction in this study seems to be 
correct.. In the goal of the project, the existing thermal conductivity models of previous used in SHS 
simulation or new proposed in literatures, were evaluated via comparing with the measured value of SHS 
specimens. The optimal thermal conductivity model was adopted for the further numerical simulation. By 
comparing with SHS experimental data, existing combustion models were evaluated and modified to improve 
their accuracy of prediction. The simulated results showed that the thermal properties of reactants play a key 
role in SHS simulations and more sophisticated combustion model is needed. 
 
Keywords: Self-Propagating High-Temperature Synthesis, Numerical Simulation, Experimental 
Measurement, Thermal Conductivity Model, Combustion Model

過程有兩種方式，其一為介質異質的本性將影響放熱化學反應之動力學(Kinetics of Exothermal Chemical 
Reaction)，其二為異種介質本身熱傳導性質的變異，將影響高溫燃燒生成物傳向低溫未反應物之熱能
傳遞，如此將影響燃燒波之傳遞。因此，就 SHS 之理論模式而言，適當之燃燒反應模式及熱導度模式，
對於 SHS 過程之模擬將扮演著舉足輕重的角色， 
由以上之文獻回顧並參酌各研究所採用之熱導度模式(Mukasyan & Rogachev, 2008)及燃燒模式
(Arimondi et al., 1997；Cuadrado-Laborde et al., 2003)可以發現，所有的數值模擬結果定性上均與實驗結
果之趨勢相吻合，但各模式在反應混合物之微結構變化上，仍有改進的空間。 
 
三、 研究目的 
根據以上之文獻回顧以及本計畫申請人過去從事 SHS 模擬之經驗，本計畫之研究目的為藉由針對
點火前後之 SHS 試片進行熱導度之實驗量測，來評估前人模擬 SHS 過程所採用之熱導度模式，以及近
年學者所提之異種介質熱導度模式，嘗試從中挑選出適當之熱導度模式。此外，經由與共同主持人所
提供之 SHS 實驗數據比對，來評估前人模擬 SHS 過程所採用燃燒模式之適用性。

Study on Theoretical Models for Simulation of Self-Propagating High-Temperature 
Synthesis 
 
P.W. Hwang1*, C.L. Yeh1, C.C. Wang2 
 
1 Department of Aerospace and Systems Engineering, Feng Chia University, Taichung, Taiwan 
2 Department of Mechanical and Computer-Aided Engineering, Feng Chia University, Taichung, Taiwan 
 
Abstract 
 In this study, the existing thermal conductivity models of previously used in SHS (self-propagating 
high-temperature synthesis) simulation and new proposed in literatures recently, are evaluated via 
comparing with the measured value of SHS specimens in this work. The selected models of thermal 
conductivity were incorporated to explore the numerical simulations of SHS process with consideration 
the effects of several key parameters, i.e., porosity, ignition temperature, heat lost, heterogeneity in 
reactant, and the addition of diluents, etc. For the numerical model, the finite volume method, 
Crank-Nicolson scheme, Runge-Kutta Scheme, and TDMA method are employed. According to the 
disclosure of authoritative literature, microsturcture of heterogeneous reaction mixture influences 
combustion process via variations in thermal conductivity and kinetics of exothermal chemical reaction. 
Therefore, the direction in this study seems to be correct. By comparing with available SHS 
experimental data, existing combustion models were also evaluated and modified to improve their 
accuracy of prediction. The simulated results showed that the thermal properties of reactants play a key 
role in SHS simulations and more sophisticated combustion model is needed. 
 
Keywords: SHS, Thermal Conductivity, Experimental Measurement, Numerical Simulation 
 
1. Introduction 
Self-propagating high-temperature sysnthesis 
(SHS) is an energy-efficient combustion 
method or producing refractory, ceramic, and 
composite materials from their constituent 
powders. [1] The SHS method usually involves 
mixing and compressing the powders into a 
green compact. The compact is then 
synthesized into the final product by an 
exothermic self-propagating reaction wave. The 
initiation of the exothermic reaction occurs by 
an external energy source in the form of a 
heated filament, spark or laser at one end of the 
compact. The phenomenon is shown 
schematically in Fig. 1 [2]. Figure 2 shows the 
typical SHS combustion processes [3]. 
For the modeling the rate of heat generation 
can be estimated through the formation rate of 
intermediate phases in terms of bulk diffusion, 
whereas the heat transfer is controlled by 
thermal conductivity [4-5]. Some of physical 
properties required for understanding and 
predicting the SHS process have been compiled 
and listed in the literature, but other 
information is quite limited [6-11]. Among 
them available data on the thermal conductivity
