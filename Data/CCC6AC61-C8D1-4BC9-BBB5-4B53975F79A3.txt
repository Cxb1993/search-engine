一、 研究計畫中英文摘要：請就本計畫要點作一概述，並依本計畫性質自訂關鍵詞。 
（一） 計畫中文摘要。(限 400個中文字) 
關鍵詞：(限 400個中文字) 
光學薄膜、軟性基板、低溫製程、結構多層薄膜、離子助鍍、干涉濾光片 
 
本研究計畫目的在於開發以軟性材質之塑膠基板上製鍍多層薄膜來製作干涉濾光片。
在開發軟性基板的鍍膜技術時，所遭遇到最重要的問題將會是薄膜與軟性基板之間的應力
問題、低溫製程問題、同時伴隨著薄膜附著性的問題。因此，為達成此研究目的，本研究
計畫選用低能量或脈衝式離子源進行離子助鍍膜（IAD），不但能降低製程溫度且可以得到
較好的光學特性，除此之外，還利用結構多層薄膜之自我複製法作為製作元件的方法。 
離子束濺鍍是藉由動量傳遞將材料以一顆顆原子或分子沈積於基板上，由於碰撞散射
的機會小，指向性良好，原子撞擊效應，使得遷移率增高，膜質更加緊密，故大多呈現壓
應力。因此在適當的離子源參數下可以得到較小的應力。而在結構多層薄膜之自我複製法
上，蒸鍍的光學薄膜工作波長根據不同元件設計將在深紫外光、紫外光、可見光到近紅外
光波段等。 
（二） 計畫英文摘要。（五百字以內） 
 Key words： 
Optical thin films, Flexible substrate, Low temperature process, Ion assisted deposition, 
Structural thin film, Optical interference filters 
 
This is a proposal regarding the deposition of thin films on flexible substrates for 
optoelectronics applications. The solution for the several problems such as stress, low 
temperature process and adhesion was proposed. To achieve the purpose, the low ion-voltage ion 
source or pulse ion source will be applied to execute ion-beam assisted deposition (IAD) to 
reduce the process temperature and improve the quality of films. Moreover, autocloning method 
will be also applied to fabricate structural thin film to develop the low-stress optical thin films. 
When using IAD process, the coating material is bombarded by the ion to obtain high 
kinetic energy. So, the structure of the film is dense and behaves from compress stress to strain 
stress according to the energy of the ion. Based on the property the stress of the film can be 
controlled by varying the parameters of the ion source. By another way of reducing the stress of 
films, autocloning method will be also applied to fabricate structural thin film to develop the 
low-stress optical thin films. The periods of the structures must be in the scale of sub-wavelength. 
Therefore, the E-beam writer is the optimal lithography system for an application when the 
working wavelength is from UV to near IR.

除此之外，利用結構多層薄膜(Structured thin film)也會改變薄膜應力。所謂結構多
層薄膜指的是包含奈米結構的多層薄膜，製作結構多層薄膜的方法之ㄧ為自我複製法
(autocloning)，此技術為為製作三維週期性光子晶體結構的重要技術之一，1999 年由日
本東北大學的 Shojiro Kawakami 教授[29]提出以電子束直寫的微影圖案結合多層薄膜
技術可製造出三維光子晶體的奈米薄膜元件，其結構如圖一所示，製作方法為在週期性
結構基板上鍍上高低折射率材料交替的多層膜，形成的多層膜形狀為「之」字型(zigzag)
的結構，目前此項技術已經被廣泛的應用，可以製造出的奈米薄膜元件，例如:偏振器
[30]、光波導[31]、帶通濾波器[32]、光積分器[33]等。

(四) 研究方法： 
本研究計畫之計畫名稱為”多層光學薄膜及結構多層薄膜元件設計與製作”，其研究
方法將以低能量或脈衝式離子源進行離子助鍍膜（IAD）作為製程之研究方法，除此之
外，還利用結構多層薄膜之自我複製法作為製作元件的方法。 
 
薄膜製程與應力的關係 
表 1 是各種鍍膜方法所產生的應力，在物理氣相沈積(PVD) 的鍍膜製程中我們考
慮 電子槍蒸鍍法(Electron Beam Gun Evaporation)、離子助鍍法(Ion-Assisted Deposition)、 
離子束濺鍍法(Ion Beam Sputtering Deposition, IBSD)及雙離子束濺鍍法(Dual Ion Beam 
Deposition, DBIB)。由於電子槍蒸鍍法製鍍的氧化膜，堆積動能不夠大，原子表面的遷 
移率(mobility)偏低，膜成長的比較鬆散，結構較疏鬆，而呈現張應力的現象，如果加 上
離子助鍍法製鍍氧化膜，因為藉著離子的能量而提高了沈積分子的動能、動量及分子 結
合的機率，使沈積分子具有較高的遷移率，增加了膜結構的緊密性，空隙減少，隨著 IAD 
轟擊的能量增加，膜應力由張應力轉為壓應力。離子束濺鍍是藉由動量傳遞將材 料以
一顆顆原子或分子沈積於基板上，由於碰撞散射的機會小，指向性良好，原子撞擊 效
應，使得遷移率增高，膜質更加緊密，故大多呈現壓應力。因此在適當的離子源參數 下
可以得到較小的應力。 
 
結構多層薄膜之自我複製法 
本計劃將利用電子鎗(electron gun)蒸鍍、離子束濺鍍(Ion Beam Sputtering)和磁控濺
鍍(Magnetron Sputtering)等不同方法將光學薄膜製鍍在二維奈米成型圖案上，再輔以加
熱或離子源助鍍(ion assisted deposition)，以製作出自我複製方式的奈米薄膜元件。 
由於一般蒸鍍的薄膜若不採基板加熱或輔以離子源助鍍，膜層堆積不夠緻密，易
造成薄膜膜質穩定性不夠，在奈米薄膜元件的自我複製現象中將隨著薄膜層數增加而逐
漸失去自我複製能力。因此如何能找到適當溫度及離子助鍍製程參數，將有助於提升可
達成的薄膜層數，以增加奈米薄膜元件設計的彈性及機制。 
在應用方面則利用上述之研究結果製鍍各種濾光片，例如以有機材料製作的軟性
顯示器(OLED)需要 UV cut off filter；軟性顯示器或是軟性照明系統在色彩調整或校正上
所需的 Color Compensator；軟性顯示器(TFT)需要的偏振分光鏡、相位板；在太陽能控
制上的 Solar energy controlled filter；及各種抗反射膜、高反射鏡、帶通濾光片等都屬於
此領域之濾光片。

量測結果顯示電子鎗蒸鍍法製鍍的氧化膜，堆積動能不夠大，原子表面的遷移率
(mobility)偏低，使得膜質結構較鬆散，而呈現張應力的現象。若改用離子助鍍的方法製
鍍氧化膜，藉增加離子的能量以提高沉積分子的動量、動能及分子結合的機率，而沉積
分子具有較高的遷移率，使膜結構中的孔隙(voids)減少、分子堆積更緊密，可使膜應力
變化由張應力變成壓應力。因此，IAD轟擊的能量對膜質應力有一定程度的影響。離子
束濺鍍(IBSD)是利用Kaufman離子源以700V到1200V之離子束轟擊金屬靶材表面，經由
動量傳遞將靶材表面的粒子以一顆顆原子或分子沉積於基板上，被濺射的粒子在高真空
的環境中，受到碰撞散射的機會小，指向性良好，故能以最初被濺射出來的能量(約數
10eV)到達基板。此外，通常輔以Ar轟擊導致原子撞擊效應(atomic peening effect)，使得
膜質更加緊密，因此濺鍍薄膜總是呈現壓應力。

首先，我們討論結構角度與高低折射率材料比例對光子能隙之影響。使用FDTD模
擬的方式來計算二維光子晶體之頻帶結構(bandstructure)。圖二十為一般二維自我複製
式光子晶體之頻帶結構圖，接下來的研究將只探討圖中灰色區域之光子能隙變化，因為
其隨結構參數改變之變化較大。為了簡化設計上之難度，我們將結構角度與一對高低折

分開討論厚度及角度對光子能隙之影響，減少了一個影響的變數，在設計上只需知道基
板週期並將其代入，即可得到最佳之結構角度與膜層厚度，可大幅降低設計上的複雜度。

的問題，因此利用子計畫三的協助以電漿處理及表面改質的方法，來改進光學薄
膜與塑膠或軟性基板之間的附著力問題。當製程溫度高時元件的熱應力會較大，
此時製程參數與應力之間的關係可交由子計畫一量測分析，同時相關的製程參數
也可以提供子計畫一作為數值分析的依據。而當製程應力較小時，精密的應力分
析可由子計畫二的白光干涉技術來進行量測與分析。在本計劃中，我們嘗試利用
結構多層薄膜來降低應力的產生，但是由於軟性基板要製作奈米結構，在目前的
方法上並不容易，因此，初期我們先用玻璃基板製作奈米結構，用來對奈米薄膜
設計做先期之探討，在後期我們就利用奈米壓印，在軟性基板上壓出奈米結構，
這些設計的結構與量測的結果可以回饋到製程的改善並做修正，進行更深入研究
以及技術上的提升與推廣，如此交互研究，逐步完成本計畫。在表面電漿處理部
份，由子計畫三提供電漿處理技術，而製程中相關應力消除的製程均由子計畫一
與二來配合，而得到不錯的成果。

Abstract: Based on the ion beam sputter deposition, the negative-charge material- aluminum 
oxynitride has been fabricated to be passivation layer. The effective lifetime of C-silicon solar 
cell is improved 3 times using the negative-charge material. 
©2010 Optical Society of America 
OCIS codes: (310.6870) Thin films, other properties; (310.1860) Deposition and fabrication 
 
1. Introduction 
Solar cells are gathering increasing attention, because of the lack of energy. Especially, high efficiency solar 
cells have advantage of cost reduction. To improve the efficiency of solar cells, it is an important problem to 
reduce the carrier recombination on the surface of solar cells. Traditionally, Si3N4 or SiO2 have been applied to 
create a surface passivation. However, SiO2, the oxide layer is fabricated using thermal oxidation under about 
900 to 1100℃ [1]. The high temperature thermal process could cause the decreased of the bulk lifetime [2]. 
Recently, some reports have shown the surface passivation fabricated using negative charge material Al2O3 by 
atomic layer deposition (ALD) or plasma enhanced chemical vapor deposition [3][4] to reduce the carrier 
recombination. But both of them are very expensive and unsuitable for mass production. In this research, we 
chose low cost and low temperature fabrication method- ion beam sputter deposition (IBSD) to deposit high 
dielectric negative material aluminum oxynitride (AlON) to replace SiO2, the positive charge material. AlON is 
the material with high density of fixed negative charges at the interface of AlON and silicon substrate. Besides, 
AlON layer can also be used for field induced surface passivation which can create effective back surface field 
(BSF) layer at the silicon interface [5]. The experimental results of AlON films show that the effective lifetime 
of the C-Silicon with AlON passivation layer can increase the intensity of back surface field (BSF) and reduce 
the surface recombination to increase the solar cell efficiency. 
 
2. Fabrication process 
First of all, AlN layers were fabricated by an IBSD system. The ion source is a 500W Kaufman-type RF ion 
source, made by Veeco Ion Tech Inc. The ion beam voltage was kept at 1100V when depositing. Figure 1 
schematically depicts the coating system. The coating chamber was pumped using a cryopump to a base 
pressure of 8x10-6 torr before the thin-film process. The working pressure during the thin-film process was fixed 
at 1.8×10-4 and 2.3×10-4 torr by controlling the nitrogen flow. The substrate is a 4×4 cm2 p-type <100> silicon 
wafer and the resistivity was 10 Ω-cm, thickness was 525 μm. It was placed about 10 cm away from an 
aluminum target. The target was a 99.999% pure aluminum disk 3 inches in diameter. After the film deposition, 
AlN film will be annealed with oxygen to achieve AlON passivation layer.

3. Conclusion 
In conclusion, the lifetime of the silicon wafer before coated AlN is 1.41 μs. It was increased to 1.49 μs after 
coated AlN passivation layer when nitrogen gas flow at 10.56sccm. And it was about 3.6 μs after annealing 
under 500℃ in the oxygen ambient, that’s about three times than the original lifetime. When the temperature 
was higher than 600℃, it would break the balanced of the interface impurities which caused the reduction of the 
effective lifetime. When coating AlN, the nitrogen gas flow should have enough nitrogen atoms that could lead 
the Al-N bond strong enough. After that, the oxygen atom replaced the nitrogen atom could induce much more 
impurities from it and create much more effective charges and it could increase higher effective lifetime.

evaporation with ion-beam-assisted deposition. Symmetric structure designs have been

1. Introduction 
Photonic crystals are nanostructures with one dimensional, two dimensional or three dimensional periodic 
structure which including some optical properties such as anisotropy, high dispersion and photonic band gap. 
The photonic band gap has been reported by Eli Yablonovitch and Sajeev John [1]. Some electromagnetic wave 
can pass in some regions, and some are reflected. The reflected regions are called band gap of the photonic 
crystals. Polarization beam splitters (PBSs) separate TE and TM mode polarization light into two different 
propagation directions. The conventional PBSs such as multilayer dielectric PBSs based on Brewster’s angle 
can separate polarizations. However, the multilayer dielectric PBS is too large to be applied to a compact optical 
system. Recently, the compact PBSs have been reported [2-5], but all of them have the disadvantage of the 
difficult fabrication process. 
In this research, we have fabricated 2D photonic crystal PBS using autocloning technique. Autocloning 
technique is one of the process techniques to fabricate two or three dimensional photonic crystals. The concept 
of autocloning technique has been proposed by Kawakami in 1999 [6]. The autocloned photonic crystals using 
rf bias sputtering [7-9] which consist of high and low refractive index dielectric materials stacked alternately on 
periodic substrate. We utilize the autocloning technique to fabricate nanostructure films, but replace rf bias 
sputtering by electron beam gun evaporation with ion-beam-assisted deposition (IAD). Because of using the 
electron beam gun evaporation, the deposition rate is higher than rf bias sputtering’s. In this research, we 
deposited TiO2 (H) and SiO2 (L) films alternately on periodic substrate using electron beam gun with IAD to 
fabricate 2D photonic crystal PBS. Besides, we utilized the symmetric structures to reduce the ripples and 
broaden working wavelength range. 
 
2. Design 
Since the Finite Difference Time Domain method (FDTD) has been adopted to solve Maxwell’s equations in 
complex geometries [10, 11], we utilize FDTD to design 2D photonic crystal PBS. The structural parameters are 
as follows: the period of substrate is 600 nm, the thickness of alternate TiO2/SiO2 is 625 nm (TiO2:250 nm, 
SiO2:375 nm). The first layer is the shaping layer of about 830 nm in thickness. There are 14 periods of alternate 
TiO2/SiO2 on the shaping layer. Figure 1 shows the simulated result of TE and TM transmittance spectra of 2D 
PBS. There are two working wavelength ranges in near-infrared (1000-1100 nm) and infrared (1100-1300 nm) 
regions. However, there are some ripples of TM and TE modes. To reduce ripples and broaden working 
wavelength range, anti-reflection (AR) coating was applied.

4. Conclusion 
We deposited TiO2 and SiO2 alternately to fabricate autocloned polarization beam splitter using electron beam 
gun evaporation with IAD. The ripples can be reduced by symmetric structure. Moreover, 2D PBS with 
autocloned symmetric structure, sub/ (0.5L H 0.5L)14/ air, has wide working wavelength range about 200 nm 
(1100-1300 nm) in infrared region.

Fabrication of anodic aluminum oxide with periodic nano structures
using nanoimprinting pre-patterned technique
國立中央大學 陳昇暉,李正中,陳君閣,陳煌坤
本發明係為一種陽極氧化鋁製作方法，應用於一壓印模板與一鋁箔上，壓印模
板之表面具有一第一凹凸結構，該第一凹凸結構包含一第一條形凸模。該方法
包含下列步驟：將壓印模板對鋁箔進行加熱壓印，而將第一凹凸結構轉印至鋁
箔上，以使鋁箔之表面形成一第二凹凸結構，該第二凹凸結構並包含對應第一
條形凸模的一第一條形凹痕；以及將壓印模板自鋁箔上脫離，並將鋁箔置於一
電解槽中進行電解與陽極氧化，進而在第二凹凸結構上形成一第一陽極氧化鋁
層，該第一陽極氧化鋁層並包含一第一孔洞結構；其中對應第一條形凹痕之位
置上係包含第一孔洞結構中之多個孔洞的一第一孔洞組，該第一孔洞組所包含
的孔洞數目係和第一條形凹痕之尺寸產生關聯。
