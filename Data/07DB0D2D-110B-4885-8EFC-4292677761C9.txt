21. 前言
高分子的樹脂材料如epoxy、phenolic、
polyethylene（PE）和polyamide等等，常被
當作接著劑與填補材料來使用，雖然高分子
材料具有良好的物理與化學特性，但對於如
高強度、高剛性、高阻尼、高抗磨耗特性、
高導電性與優良動態特性等特殊要求，高分
子材料仍需透過補強材的加入才能達到上
述特定需求。奈米碳管的機械與物理特性均
優於纖維與一般常用的補強材料，因此在許
多研究中，選擇奈米碳管為補強材料，藉由
奈米碳管的加入，發展出更高性能的奈米複
合材料。
1.1 文獻回顧
奈米碳管具有優良的機械性質，研究人
員[1-3]發現，奈米碳管楊氏模數與抗拉強度
均高於傳統材料。許多研究[4-7] 發現以奈
米碳管為補強材的材料其機械特性均有明
顯提升。加有奈米碳管的複合材料[8-10]對
於動態特性上的表現有提升效果，主要的原
因是碳管與基材間優良界面特性。加有黏彈
材料或高阻尼材料的層狀結構[11-14]，在動
態特性與噪音的吸收能力也可獲得明顯改
善。
1.2 研究目的
本研究以電腦模擬方式分析不同的奈
米複合材料之懸臂樑結構的自然頻率，再以
實驗的方式量測其自然頻率，並與模擬數值
印證，進而由實驗數據推算材料阻尼特性。
再將奈米複合材料加入三明治複材樑結構
核心材料中，以不同核心材料與不同纖維排
列的表面材料進行結構自然頻率與阻尼特
性研究，進而得知三明治結構之動態特性。
文中探討各種高分子基材與不同碳管重量
百分比之奈米複材，以及在三明治複材樑結
構中，以不同纖維排列的表面材料與各種奈
米複材之核心材料，以懸臂樑結構型式探討
自然頻率與阻尼特性。
2. 研究方法

3.3 三明治複材樑結構動態特性探討
本研究以碳纖維疊層板為表面材料，高
分子奈米複材為核心材料製作三明治動態
實驗試片，將試片以懸臂樑結構形式進行強
制振動實驗，量測三明治複材樑結構自然頻
率與阻尼特性。所探討的參數為（1）核心
厚度（2）表面材料之纖維疊層角度（3）不
同核心材料，藉由這些參數探討，研究出最
佳動態特性三明治複材樑結構與使用材料。

/環氧樹脂試片是屬於脆性破壞；由較高放
大倍率 SEM 圖可知，強化材－碳管有會聚
集現象，此現象是造成碳管含量較高試片對
於材料機械與動態特性提升效果較不明顯
的原因；在放大五萬倍 SEM圖（圖 15(b)）
中，可明顯看到碳管表面被環氧樹脂完全包
覆，這表示多壁奈米碳管與環氧樹脂間有良
好的界面特性，此現象可提升高分子樹脂機
械與動態特性。

MWNTs/epoxy Nanocomposites. The thermosetting epoxy resin, E120-H100, was used as the
matrix. The hot press method was used to fabricate the MWNTs/epoxy nanocomposite specimens
[7]. Different weight percentages of MWNTs were added to the epoxy compound and mixed by a
magnetic stirrer for 10 minutes at 60 oC. The nanocomposites compound was then poured into a
steel mould in an oven at 60 oC for 1-hour heating. The mixed compound was followed by
hot-pressing at 85oC under pressure 1.03 MPa (150 psi) for 2 hours to fabricate the MWNTs/epoxy
nanocomposite specimens.
MWNTs/phenolic Nanocomposites. The thermosetting phenolic resin, PF-650, was used as the
matrix. In preprocessing, the MWNTs were prepared first by sonicating them in a water bath with
methanol solution for 30 minutes to separate them in the solution. The MWNTs were then added to
the phenolic resin and mixed by magnet stirrer for 30 minutes. The mixed compound was placed in
a vacuum oven at 75 oC for 24 hours. Then the mixed compound was placed in a steel mould frame
and hot pressed under 9 MPa (1300 psi) at 170°C for 2 hours to make MWNTs/phenolic
nanocomposites specimens. The nanocomposite specimens were post-cured at 200 oC under vacuum
for 12 hours to improve the cross-linking in the microstructure of the nanocomposites [8]. At least
three specimens were prepared for each case.
Sandwich Beam Specimens. The face material of the sandwich beams is graphite/epoxy laminate
fabricated by the usual hot press method. After fabricating the face and the core materials, the face
and the core materials were cut into a size of 180mm×12mm and the sandwich beams were made by
adhering them together. The adhesion layer was controlled within 0.2 mm thickness according to
ASTM E756-98 standard [9].The sandwich beams were hot pressed under 0.35MPa (50psi) for 5
hours to make the adhesive layer even. Finally, the edges of the specimens were trimmed to remove
the spilled adhesive to complete the fabrication of sandwich beam specimens.
Bending Experiment of Sandwich Beams
The three-point bending experiment was used to find the bending properties of the sandwich beams.
The testing facility includes a load cell, a displacement transducer and a data acquisition system
equipped with a personal computer. Fig. 1 shows the schematic of a three-point bending experiment.
The load cell was used to measure the load applied on the specimen from which the bending
moment could be calculated. The displacement transducer was used to measure the deflection of the
sandwich beam under bending.
