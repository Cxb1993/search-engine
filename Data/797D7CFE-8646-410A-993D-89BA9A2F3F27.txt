摘要 
液化澱粉芽胞桿菌（Bacillus amyloliquefaciens）是目前被認為具有良好拮抗質物
性病源真菌功能，其對檬果炭疽病（Glomerella cingulata）也有抑制能力。本研究探討
不同的碳源、氮源與添加物一級因子對液化澱粉芽胞桿菌產生菌數、iturin A 、surfactin、 
chitinase活性、glucanase活性等五項二級因子對檬果炭疽病之抑制活性的最適培養條件
探討。研究顯示以 1% yeast powder為氮源者較佳，1% molasses做為碳源可使 Iturin A
的濃度上升到 131 mg/L。在添加物方面，0.2% isolated soy protein的添加則使 chitinase
活性從 0.009 IU上升到 0.33 IU，菌數也增加至 4.1×1011 CFU/mL。0.1% polysaccharide
的添加使glucanase 活性從 0 IU增加到 0.12 IU。以1%molasses、1%yeast powder與 0.2% 
isolated soy protein培養基中添加 1%發泡煉石可使 surfactin的產量從 772.3 mg/L上
升至 1869.5 mg/L，添加 1%纖維素可使 iturin A的產量從 131 mg/L上升至 220.7mg/L。
根據因子試驗方程式系數共設計出八組陡升路徑實驗，八組實驗中以第四組的二級因子配
方，218.7mg/L iturin A、160 mg/L surfacitn、0.27IU chitinase活性、0.22 IU glucanase
活性、log(CFU/mL)為12.2時的配方有最佳的抑制活性達到100 %。 
 
前言 
 液化澱粉芽孢桿菌(Bacillus amyloliquefaciens)於 1943 年由日本學者 Fukumoto 發
現，此菌種可產生大量的α-amylase(Fukumoto,1943)。在一開始時，由於此菌種外觀及
表現特徵和枯草桿菌(B. subtili)極為相似，因此將液化澱粉芽孢桿菌列為枯草桿菌的亞
 4
為植物病原真菌作用因子，並以檬果炭疽病病原菌作為防治對象為模式，進行對其抑菌活
性最適化產程的開發，此一研究成果在液化澱粉芽孢桿菌抗生物質製劑終極的應用上，可
以不同組成比例的調整，開發防治不同的植物真菌病害最適化成分配方。 
 
文獻探討 
 液化澱粉芽孢桿菌對病原真菌和細菌具有拮抗作用，可以產生許多代謝產物和抗生物
質。曾有文獻指出，枯草桿菌至少有 66 種不同的抗生物質，但近年已發表在期刊中的資料
顯示，其中只有 11 種有確定的結構與功能。產生抗生素的枯草桿菌除可將菌體直接噴灑植
物葉片保護其免受葉部病害危害外，並可製成粉劑或純化出其抗生物質，進行種子覆被或
土壤處理。枯草桿菌屬產生的抗生物質其分子量範圍在 2700~4500 Da 之間的胜肽類抗生
素，而同一種枯草桿菌常可同時產生分子量相近的多種抗生素，其組成結構為多個胺基酸
以環狀結構聯接，故其較不易被動植物的蛋白酸水解，本屬微生物產生抗生素的種類依其
所在細胞位置及分子結構可分為三類，分別為胞內環狀胜肽脂類( Intracellular cyclic 
peptido-lipids)、胞外環狀胜肽脂類(Extracellular cyclic peptidolpids)及胞外胜肽環狀類
(Extracellular cyclic peptides) ，其所產生不同類之抗生素可對不同的病原菌產生抑制作用。
一種稱為「iturin A」的抗生物質，這種化合物會與病原真菌細胞膜的固醇分子作用形成複
合物，使得離子傳導孔隙增大，改變細胞膜的滲透性，讓鉀離子迅速流出，進而導致病原
真菌菌絲分解並抑制孢子發芽，達到防治病害的效果。Iturin A 擁有良好的兩性物質，因此
可發展成生物界面活性劑（Fiechter，1992）。其極性部份形成是因 peptide cycle 存在，其親
水基常來自蛋白質之極性基、醣脂之醣基及磷酸脂之磷酸部份。疏水部份由 β-amino acid
的烷基鏈形成，通常來自一個或多個長鏈之碳氫化合物。其可降低臨界微胞濃度，是一有
效之生物界面活性劑（Maget-Dana，1994）。生物界面活性劑依其化學結構特性可分為五大
類，分別為：（1）醣脂（glycolipids）、（2）磷脂（phospholipids）、（3）脂胜肽（lipopeptides）、
（4）脂肪酸（fatty acids）及（5）高分子生物界面活性劑（polymetric biosurfactants）（Fiechter，
1992）。相同的生物界面活性劑，可由不同微生物所合成而生，如枯草桿菌及液化澱粉芽孢
桿菌皆產生 Iturin A。多數微生物均能產生界面活性物質，其相異處在於利用之培養基質
（substrate）不同所生成之不同種類及濃度。iturin A 由　-amino acids 長鏈及 7 個　-amino 
acid 殘基組成之環狀胜肽脂類抗生素，Iturin A 在目前主要應用於生物防治的功用上
（Besson et al.，1979；Heins et al.，2000； Peypoux et al.，1979），Iturin A 可對抗的病原
真菌及細菌有：Cercospora kikuchii、Verticillium dahliae、Fusarium oxysporum f. sp. 
lycopersici、Alternaria mali、Rhizoctonia solani、Pyricularia oryzea、Xanthomonas oryzea 及
Pseudomonas lachrymans 拮抗範圍廣，為一具生物農藥發展潛力的菌種。此外亦可抑制會
產生黃麴毒素之真菌生長及阻止其產生黃麴毒素（Klich et al.，1991；向明，1998）。Moyne 
等人在 2004 年的報告中更發展出新的技術來控制真菌產生黃麴毒素，這些真菌像
Aspergillus flavus 和 Aspergillus parasiticus。在溫室藥效部份，它對甜椒疫病也有防治效果。
Iturin A 在 in vitro 試驗中能抑制不同植物病源菌，在田野試驗中針對蕃茄（tomato）與甜
瓜（melon）的種子所作之生物防治是有效的（Ohno et al.，1996）。近年來也有部份處理人
與動物的黴菌病之用途（Klich et al.，1994），Maki 等人在 2001 年的報告中也指出 B. subtilis 
能有效的對抗 Rhizoctonia solani 引起的植物葉片黃化。或者是由 Gloeosporium 
gloeosporioides 造成蔬果的炭疽病(Cho et al.,2003)等。 
 Iturin A 的產生是典型的二次代謝產物，從先前的研究得知其產生的時間都為第三天以
 6
的三角錐形瓶中(此錐形瓶中含有200 mL以3﹪Glucose 及0.75﹪Bacto peptone 比例配成
的培養基，在滅菌釜中以121 ℃, 1.5 atm高溫高壓滅菌20分鐘後取出)，之後將接種完之
三角錐形瓶置於30 ℃、200 rpm的振盪培養箱中培養10小時。 
 
分析方法 
（1） Iturin A 的 HPLC 分析方法 
 標準品之前處理，將 1 mg ＂iturin A＂ 溶於 1mL methanol 中，在 220 nm 波長的條
件下以 HPLC 分析。B. iturin A 之萃取，由 NA 培養基中挑單一菌落接種於 5 mL LB 小量
隔夜培養，以接種量 1﹪的比例轉養於 100 mL LB，於 30℃ 200 rpm 下培養 12~16 小時，
接著在 4℃下，以 8,000 rpm 離心 20 分鐘，去上清液，菌體回溶於 100 mL Potato sucrose 
broth (PSB)（含 2﹪sucrose）中。再於 30℃下培養 6天，然後在 4℃下，以 8,000 rpm 離
心 20 分鐘，取上清液以濃鹽酸滴定至 pH = 2 ±0.5。在 4℃下，以 8,000 rpm 離心 20 分
鐘，取沉澱物，以 25 mL methanol (100%)將沉澱懸浮於分液漏斗，用力振盪使＂iturin A＂
萃取於有機相中，重複萃取二次，過濾，並藉由真空濃縮儀將有機溶劑揮發，此沉澱物即
為粗（crude）＂iturin A＂。將粗＂iturin A＂溶於 NaOH（pH 8）溶液中，過濾去除未
溶解的不純物，收集濾液再度以濃鹽酸使其沉澱，用 methanol (100%)萃取，真空濃縮，
以 2.5 mL methanol 回溶，並以活性炭脫色，最後經 0.22 μm 過濾膜過濾，便可進行 HPLC
分析。HPLC 分析條件，利用 Lichrocart 250-4 Lichrospher 100 RP-18 column，以
acetontril：10 mM ammonium acetate＝2：3(v/v)為沖堤液，流速為 1 mL/min，偵測器
以 photodiode array (PDA)在 210~430 nm 掃描波長的條件下進行 HPLC 分析。 
 
（2）生物活性檢定方法 
 液化澱粉芽孢桿菌可產生多種拮抗物質，其中 iturin A 對 Fusarium oxysporum
（Hiraoka et al., 1992；Huang et al., 1993）及 Saccharomyces cerevisiae（Latoud 
et al., 1987；1990）可藉由改變細胞膜的滲透性達到拮抗作用，本研究測試植物病原真
菌檬果炭疽病病源菌(Glomerella cingulata；GC)。對峙培養法－真菌：分別將測試植物
病原真菌檬果炭疽病源菌之菌絲塊（直徑 1公分）置於馬鈴薯瓊脂培養基（potato dextrose 
agar, PDA）上，於 25℃下培養，待長滿培養皿。將測試菌株以接種環由 NA 培養基中挑取
單一菌落後，實驗方法是將真菌塊置於 PDA plate 中央培養，待其生長兩天後，在離菌絲
0.5 公分處貼上濾紙片，並滴上發酵液 30μl，總共做四重複，於一天及五天後觀察其結果。
於 PDA 培養基（9 cm）中央劃一直線，觀察病原真菌的生長情形，並測量抑菌距離。對照
組則是無菌水，照上述步驟進行實驗，於一天及五天後觀察其結果。上述生物檢定方法為
定性並可初步判定抑菌活性，進ㄧ步取得抑菌活性量化生物檢定，則詳述於下，
β-glucanase 抗 真 菌 活 性 測 試 採 用 此 一 方 式 進 行 。 1. 培 養 7 天 Bacillus 
amyloliquefaciens 培養液離心 10000 轉、10 分鐘。2. 將離心好的上清液取至無菌操作台，
以 0.22μl filter 過濾得到濾液。3. 將得到的濾液以無菌水分別稀釋 100 倍與 1000 倍。
4. 將步驟 3兩個濃度的濾液分別加入 55℃、70℃與 100℃未凝固的 Potato Dextrose Agar
中，搖晃均勻，並潑於 9 cm 之平板上，以上實驗均三重複。4. 待 PDA 凝固之後，以 0. 5 
c m 的 G. C. 菌塊貼於平板正中央。5. 觀察 5天後及 7天後之 G. C.生長情形，並丈量其
生長半徑。6. 將得到的生長半徑進一步的換算成不同的抑制百分比。7. 百分比抑制活性 
( % ) = ( 1- 生長半徑 / Blank ) * 100

部分蛋白包括 chitinase, glucanase 可能被 protease 分解以供菌體存活所需。在過去文
獻指出 Trichoderma harzianum 在目標基因被誘發大量表現時卻也被同時表現的
protease 分解，而使的該蛋白質的表現量降低。所以其可以經由蛋白質純化之步驟取得較
純的蛋白質，才可較長時間保存其蛋白質的存在。

Abstract 
Bacillus amyloliquefaciens is considered a potential bio-pesticide against many plant fungal 
disease. Carbon sources,  nitrogen sources and several additives were used to study the 
production of secondary metabolites include iturin A, surfactin,  the enzymes activity of 
glucanase and chitinase, and also on cell concentration of B.amyloliquefaciens. And, those 5 
factors were consider as the main role to inhibit many plant fungal diseases. Using the 1% 
molasses as carbon source was found to increase the production of iturin A to  131mg/L. 
However, the most effective increase iturin A production was found in 1% pottery addition and 
the production of iturin A was increased from 131mg/L to 220.7 mg/L. On the other hand, 1% 
expanded clay addition in the MY medium has the highest production of surfactin and it increases 
from 772.3 mg/L to 1869.5 mg/L. For enzymes activity of glucanase and chitinase, the activity of 
chitinase increase from 0.009 to 0.33 IU and the activity of glucanase increase to 0.12 IU in the 
MY medium with 0.1% isolated soy protein addition and 0.1% polysaccharide addition, 
respectively.  
 
Introduction 
 Synthetic chemical fungicide has long been served as power agents for reducing the 
incidence of plant disease; however, they are costly, can cause environmental pollution, and may 
induce pathogen resistance. Therefore, the conventional use of chemical pesticides has been 
seriously questioned [1, 2]. To cope with the problem associated with chemical fungicide, 
biological control agents, which include effective microorganisms and microbial products, have 
been attracting attention recently as alternatives to chemical pesticides for the management of 
plant disease. The biological control of plant pathogens which is without the negative aspects of 
chemical control has become an important aspect of the sustainable agriculture.

Materials and methods   
 Microorganism--The strain B. amyloliquefaciens s3 used in this study was isolated from an 
infected lily plants provided from TACTRI (Taiwan Agricultural Chemicals and Toxic Substances 
Research Institute, Council of Agriculture, Taichung, Taiwan). To prepare seed culture, a loop of 
S3 cells from a slant culture of fresh nutrient agar was inoculated into a 500-ml flask containing 
100 ml Luria broth. The flask was incubated on a New Brunswick rotary shaker (Model G24) at 
30℃ , 200 rpm for 24 h and used as a seed for submerged cultivations.  
 
 Shake-flask fermentation 
The standard spore suspension inoculums 5% (v/v) were used for shake flask cultures to 
evaluate the effects of carbon source, nitrogen sources, inorganic salts and plant oil on the 
production of iturin A from B. subtilis S3. Cultures were grown at 30 °C and 200 rpm in a rotary 
shaker incubator (Yih-Der, LE-539., Taiwan), in 500 mL  Erlenmeyer flasks with 100 mL of 
production media, and no pH control was applied for all cultivations. The production medium for 
cultivation of B. subtilis S3 consisted of 1% of one of the following carbon sources, i.e. fructose, 
glucose, sucrose and maltose, 0.5% of one of the following nitrogen sources, i.e. corn steep 
powder, soy bean protein, yeast extract and peptone, 1-2 % of potato powder and maltodextrin, 
1.5mM MgSO4·7 H2O, 0.75mM KH2PO4. In addition, to test the effect of plant oils, 0.5-1% of 
peanut oil, olive oil, soybean oil and canola oil (president food co., Taiwan) was also added. 
Submerged cultivations were performed in three replicate experiments, and the analyses were 
carried out at least in duplicate.

 Path of steepest accent (descent).  
The method of steepest ascent (descent) is a procedure for moving sequentially along the 
path of steepest ascent (descent), that is, in the direction of the maximum increase (decrease) in 
the response. Based on the results obtained from the factorial design, the fitted first-order model 
is  
               k 
      Y=β0 + Σ βi xi                             (1) 
                      i =1 
Y is the predicted response; β0,βi, are constant coefficients, and xi is the coded independent 
variables or factors. 
 
The direction of steepest ascent (descent) is the direction in which Y increases (decreases) most 
rapidly. This direction is parallel to the normal to the fitted response surface. One usually takes as 
the path of steepest ascent (descent) the line through the center of the region of interest and 
normal to the fitted surface. Thus, the steps along the path are proportional to the regression 
coefficientsβi. The path of steepest ascent (descent) started from the center of the first design. To 
move away from the first design center along the path of steepest ascent (descent), maltodextrin 
(X1), glucose (X2),  CSP (X3), shaking speed (X4) and area of aeration (X5) were moved -0.034, 
0.0565, 0.0966, 14.798, 0.398 (expressed as diameter of rubber eraser) units in each step, 
respectively. These new units were determined from concentration range of unity level from first 
design and estimated coefficient ratio from equation (1). The design and experimental results 
along the path of steepest ascent is shown on Table 3. It should be noticed that the pH was 
excluded in path of steepest ascent design because it showed little effect on iturin A production 
according to the results of fractional factorial experiments which will be described below.
