Abstract 
Electrochromism uses an external electric field to produce a change in material to a 
colored/bleached state, thus influencing the transmittance effect of external light. The advantages of 
screen printing technology lie in its fast preparation speed, ease of control of material constituents, 
and its ability for continuous production. It can also allow for the rapid preparation of the required 
coating layer in environments of low temperature. This study uses the sol-gel method to prepare 
WO3 glaze, and then use screen printing technology to print the WO3 glaze thin film onto ITO glass, 
which has great potential for use in the future development of energy-saving windows. This study 
combines sol-gel method and screen printing process to make tungsten oxide thin films, and also 
explores their electrochromic properties. First, we used the sol-gel method to make acetylated 
peroxotungstic acid powder and formulate it into solutions using different percentages of ethanol. 
Then, we added oxalate dihydrate and ethyl cellulose ethoce to change it into a paste form. To make 
the coating paste, the based solid materials of precursor will be dissolved in isopropyl alcohol and 
critric acid will be used to as an organic ligand. Finally, the paste will be screen printed on ITO glass 
substrates to form WO3 thin films. The experimental results presented that it can be successfully 
made WO3 film through the screen printing process when using the appropriate paste formula. The 
test results showed that the surface appearance of the thin film made with a ratio of acetylated 
peroxotungstic acid powder to ethanol of 4:6 is more compact; while cyclic voltammetry and 
UV-VIS-NIR spectral analysis showed that the thin film made with these parameters has excellent 
electrochromic properties, such as better reversibility and greater change in light transmittance. 
Keywords: sol-gel method, screen printing, tungsten oxide thin film, electrochromic property

4.1.4 循環伏安法(cyclic voltammery)分析 
由圖 4.5 之循環伏安曲線比較，可得未
退火之 4 wt.%二水草酸氧化鎢薄膜有較大電
荷反應面積與較佳可逆性。圖 4.5(a)當電壓從
正電位往負電位掃描，在氧化鎢薄膜在電壓
-1.5V 著色完成，其電流 i=-5.82mA 為最大還
原電流，在電壓 0.23V 時有一個寬廣氧化
峰，有最大值氧化電流 i=5.03mA，接著再驟
降至電壓 0.5V，之後電流慢慢趨近零，這是
指氧化鎢薄膜已達到完全去色，而整個電致
色變反應可逆性，可藉由通過陽極抽出與陰
極注入的電荷容量的比值(Qanode/Qcathode)加以
分析，其可逆度為 0.92。 
圖 4.5 循環伏安法曲線可觀察出結晶態
氧化鎢薄膜電荷反應面積比非晶態較小，圖
4.5(c)為 450℃熱處理氧化鎢薄膜，在還原峰
電壓-1.5 V，有最大電流 i=-1.05 mA 與非晶
氧化鎢薄膜相比，有較小還原電流，故其著

    對於電致色變薄膜而言，著去色狀態之

產生著去色狀態，進而量測薄膜之光穿透

WO3 薄膜之 UV-VIS-NIR 光譜圖，在紫外光
波段穿透率為零，這是由於 ITO 在紫外光區
有一定阻隔能力。圖 4.9(a)為溶液比例 3:7 製
造之 WO3 薄膜的光譜，可看出在初鍍及去
色態時有不錯之穿透率，但著色效果不明
顯，穿透率差只有 16%。圖 4.9(b)為溶液比
例 4:6 製造之 WO3 薄膜的光譜，可觀察初鍍
曲線在可見光區段之間有 60~65%穿透率，在
近紅外光區隨著波長增加，穿透率遞減。當
處於著色時，光譜曲線明顯往下偏移，在 560
