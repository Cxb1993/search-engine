摘要 
    本計畫主要產出技術係自行設計水壓式壓密儀器設備及控制軟體。硬體技術，
包括設計、組裝及測試，其子系統有(a)單軸壓縮試驗儀器(b)步進水壓缸(c)感
測器訊號擷取等；軟體技術，係以 VB.NET為開發平台，並完成五大子系統開發；
包含(a)自動開關 (b)自動加載減壓 (c)數據擷取與記錄 (d)繪圖與計算以及(e)
錯誤辨別等。 
    經本研究產出之軟、硬體整合、測試，並與傳統壓密儀器試驗比較驗證，證
實本計畫產出技術可應用於土壤壓密自動化試驗，而可供業界使用，以節省人力
成本。 
 
關鍵字：壓密儀器、步進水壓缸、壓密自動化 
 
Abstract 
The main development technical of this project is to design a hydraulic automated 
consolidation testing system for applying on soil consolidation test. In hardware 
technology, the design, assembly and testing were executed completely including 
subsystems such as (a) uniaxial compression test instrument, (b) stepping hydraulic 
cylinder, (c) sensor signal acquisition. In software technology, the VB.NET as the 
development platform was developed to five major subsystems software, for instance 
(a) automatic switch system, (b) automatic loading and unloding system, (c) data 
acquisition and recording system (d) graphics and computing system , and (e) error 
detect system. 
According the soft and hardware integration, testing, validation and comparison the 
test results with the traditional oedometer test, it was confirmed the developed 
technology in this research can be applied to soil consolidation test at automation, and 
available for use by the geotechnical engineering industry, in order to save on labor 
costs. 
 
Keywords: oedometer equipment, stepper hydraulic cylinder, automated consolidation 
testing system
