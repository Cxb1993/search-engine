一、中英文摘要 
 
關鍵詞：臨床生物資訊，生物資訊資料
庫，疾病導向，個人化醫學  
 
我們提出此計畫，主要在建立疾病導向的
臨床生物資訊資料庫，提供臨床醫師在診
斷與治療疾病時能隨時取得生物資訊。第
一年我們已建立了疾病導向的臨床生物資
訊資料庫網站(http://www.bmidoc.com/)。
疾病相關基因資料庫主要源自於NCBI的
基因銀行(GenBank)、GeneCards 及其他公
用的資料庫。疾病的分類採用目前醫院最
常用的ICD-9-CM 國際疾病分類法。計畫
的第一年，我們建立了32 種癌症相關的基
因資料庫。網站的首頁包括癌症的種類，
癌症相關的基因，及重要的相關網站連
結。基因資料庫內容有基因的主要名稱、
別名、種類、主要的基因功能及參考文獻。
內容力求精簡扼要，讓使用者能在短時間
內了解癌症相關基因的作用。 
 
第二年我們持續更新基因資料庫的內容，
使癌症相關基因資料庫能提供臨床醫師最
新的生物資訊。同時建立癌症相關的蛋白
質資料庫及SNP資料庫。臨床生物資訊資
料庫的應用及評估則因第三年計畫案未獲
通過補助而暫時做罷。 
 
Abstract 
 
Keywords: clinical bioinformatics, 
bioinformatics database, disease-oriented, 
personalized medicine 
 
Physicians take much time to retrieve the 
demand biological information from the 
public databases for medical practice. Also 
it needs a lot of analytical process to let 
these data become applicable for clinical 
diagnosis and treatment. Therefore, we try 
to create the disease-oriented clinical 
bioinformatics databases to provide 
biological information for their clinical 
decision making. 
 
In the first year, we created the 
disease-oriented clinical bioinformatics 
databases at the web site 
(http://www.bmidoc.com/) and clinical 
physicians can retrieve biological 
information via the Internet. The web site 
includes 32 cancer-oriented gene databases. 
The gene databases created at the web site 
originated from the GenBank of NCBI, 
Genecards, PubMed and other public 
databases. The list of diseases came from the 
International Classification of Diseases, 
Ninth Revision, Clinical Modification 
(ICD-9-CM). The ICD-9-CM is based on 
the World Health Organization's Ninth 
Revision, International Classification of 
Diseases (ICD-9). ICD-9-CM is the official 
system of assigning codes to diagnoses and 
procedures associated with hospital 
utilization. 
 
The contents of the cancer-related gene 
database created in the first year include 
gene name, aliases, gene type, information 
summary and references. The reference can 
link to the PubMed home directly.
