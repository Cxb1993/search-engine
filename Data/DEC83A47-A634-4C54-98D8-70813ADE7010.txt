Abstract 
Since the energy price is increased very much, the energy-saving 
air-conditioning systems are considered important gradually, especially on the 
application of adsorption cooling systems in the market of commercial 
air-conditioning systems. The modification of the key component of the heat 
exchanger in the adsorption cooling system is studied by improving the property of 
the adsorbent on the fins of the heat exchanger. The adsorbent of silica gel with higher 
surface area and smaller pore size distribution is prepared in this study to improve the 
adsorption capacity and the performance of the adsorption cooling system. 
SiO2 nanoparticles were prepared from tetraethylortho-silicate (TEOS) by using 
a sol-gel process. The effects of composition of reactants and reaction conditions were 
discussed. It was found that the size of silica nanoparticles would be changed with 
those parameters. Basically, particle size would be increase with the TEOS and NH3 
concentrations, but decreased with the reaction temperature. Furthermore, particle size
