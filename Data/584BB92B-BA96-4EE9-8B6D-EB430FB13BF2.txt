Abstract 
This project proposed a standard analysis procedure so that the engineers can easily and 
rapidly detect the problem, essentially to reduce the defect rate. Data Mining technologies and 
several statistical analysis tools are adapted to resolve the relationship between machine 
combinations, EDC data, WAT parameters, and Yield rate. Results indicate that the methodology 
is feasible and effective. Results suggested that if the support percentage is between 70% and 
80% and confidence percentage equals 85%, via the association rule, thirteen stations affecting Vt 
parameter more are picked up quickly out of thirty-nine stations. Also four abnormal machines 
are identified in the later half manufacturing process of that factory. Verified by engineers, the 
result coheres with the current situation of that factory. The association rule defining abnormal 
machines by this research is not only used to detect machine during manufacturing process of 
semiconductor product, but also used to provide references for engineers to analyze and schedule 
the production. 
 
Keywords: Machine assembling, Control chart, ANOVA, Association rule
