 The main objective of this study was 
to develop a high performance 
non-halogen flame retardant with low 
smoke emission. In this study, a 
combination of P-N type intumescent 
flame retardant with smoke 
suppressant such as antimony trioxide 
(Sb2O3)/sodium borate(Na2B4O7) was 
employed to develop a high 
performance non-halogen flame 
retardant. Oxygen index meter and 
cone calorimeter were used to 
evaluate optimum ratio between 
intumescent flame retardant 
(PNFR)and smoke suppressant. 
Finally, effect of addition high 
performance non-halogen flame on 
thermal stability of LDPE foam was 
evaluated by TGA.
