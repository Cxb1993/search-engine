摘要 
 
    硫酸化軟骨素(Chondroitin sulfate，CS)為一已知天然多醣體，具有良好的生物相容性
（Biocompatibility）和生物可降解性（Biodegradability）。本實驗使用硫酸化軟骨素來製備水膠以作
為遞送蛋白質藥物的載體。實驗首先準備三種不同分子量的聚乙二醇二丙烯酸酯（Poly ethylene 
glycol-diacrylate）和甲基丙烯酸酯基（Methacrylate，MA）接枝率 0.28 的硫酸化軟骨素-丙烯酸甲
酯，並使用這四種不同分子量的 PEG-DA 與不同 CS-MA / PEG-DA 比例來製備水膠。進行水膠的
性質分析，如重量損失測量、膨潤程度測量及交聯密度測試，並使用掃描式電子顯微鏡（Scanning 
Electron Microscope，SEM）觀察水膠的表面形態，最後利用水膠包覆 DNA 或蛋白質作為藥物傳
遞載體評估。 
 
關鍵詞：硫酸化軟骨素-丙烯酸甲酯（CS-MA）、聚乙二醇-二丙烯酸酯（PEG-DA）、藥物傳遞 
 
 
 
 
Abstract 
 
    The goal of this work was to utilize a naturally biodegradable polymer, chondroitin sulfate (CS), 
formed hydrogels for a protein or plasmid DNA drug delivery. Polyethylene glycol diacrylate (PEG-DA) 
with the various molecular masses of PEG (1.0K, 3.4K, 8.0Kand 20K g/mol) and methacrylated 
chondroitin sulfate (CS-MA) with 28 mol% of methacrylation onto CS were prepared. Hydrogels with 
different mole ratios between PEG-DA and CS-MA were synthesized and their effects on weight loss, 
swelling, compression modulus, and sustain-released profiles of BSA or DNA were tested.

前言 
    水膠，交聯且親水性的高分子，近年來在
生醫材料及的醫學領域上佔有相當重要的地
位，由於水膠含有柔軟的觸感及黏性使它非常
像天然的組織，使得其作為細胞生長的有利條
件。近年來發展的水膠不計其數，就因它具有
優越的特性，在醫學領域上有相當傑出的應
用，例如隱形眼鏡（Contact lenses）、生物感應
器（Biosensor）以及藥物投遞的載體（Drug 
delivery devices）。 
水膠在藥物釋放之應用可謂非常之廣泛，
一般都是利用壓克力系水膠含浸藥物後再作釋
放之動作，像 pH 值敏感型水膠及溫度敏感型水
膠這兩種水膠系統，在環境改變下水膠之外觀
也會隨之改變，利用此一特性可釋放各種藥物。

same final aim to use these nanoparticles as protein drug or vaccine carriers. Dr. Okano

cell or oral mucosal epithelial cells. Another example is using cardiac cell sheet engineering

growth hormone. Dr. B Chu stated they have developed a novel core-shelled DNA

mixture and subsequent encapsulation of the condensed DNA in a triblock copolymer, 
PLA-PEG-PLA, in the same solvent environment. The polylactide shell protects the 
encapsulated DNA from degradation during electrospinning of a mixture of encapsulated 
DNA nanoparticles and biodegradable PLGA to form a nanofibrous non-woven scaffold 
using the same solution mixture. Dr J. Kopecek delivered the hybrid hydrogels 
self-assembled from synthetic polymers and protein domains, self-assembly of graft 
copolymers, and self-assembly of triblock copolymer. Dr. S. C. Kim gave information about 
how to make hydrophobic-hydrophilic IPN’s with controlled morphology. They used 
biodegradable hydrophobic polyurethane (PU)/ hydrophilic poly(ethylene glycol diacrylate)
