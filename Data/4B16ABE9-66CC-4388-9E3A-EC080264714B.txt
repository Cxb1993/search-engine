Abstracts 
 
This project aims to suppress the methanol crossover and improve the utilization efficiency of 
methanol fuel using a layer of Pt49-Ru35-Ir6-Os10 or Pt58-Ru32-Os10 or Pt50-Ru50 or Pt50-Sn50 alloy 
catalyst directly deposited into proton exchange membrane (PEM) surface via the 
impregnation-reduction (IR) method. The proposed layer in Nafion membrane surface acts as a 
reactive methanol filter. This study investigates the influences of different catalyst deposited into 
the Nafion membrane surface on the suppression of methanol crossover and improvement of MEA 
performance. The mechanism for suppression of methanol crossover is also studied. SEM, X-ray, 
EDS and potentialstat were adopted to determine the surface microstructure and cross-sectional 
view, the phases and composition, methanol electrocatalystic activity of the resulted electrocatalyst 
layer. The rate of methanol crossover was determined by detecting the amount of CO2 in the 
cathode exhaust at steady-state. Experimental results show that the existence of a layer of alloy 
catalyst deposited into Nafion membrane surface do suppress the methanol crossover and enhance 
the MEA performance. Among the four samples, the MEA with Pt58-Ru32-Os10 ternary alloy catalyst 
layer in PEM maximizes the suppression of methanol crossover and the MEA performance up to 
39% and 12.4% than the MEA without catalyst layer in PEM anode side, respectively. This catalyst 
layer in Nafion membrane surface serves both as an methanol filter and an electrode. Its dual-role 
contributes to the suppression of methanol crossover, and improvement of both cell performance 
and the utilization efficiency of methanol fuel, both of which are dependent on the catalyst activity 
of the methanol electro-oxidation over the thin catalyst layer directly deposited on Nafion 
membrane surface. This demonstrates that the idea of incorporation of a layer of alloy catalyst in 
PEM surface do efficiently suppress the methanol crossover and improve the cell performance, 
which offering another important route to reduce methanol crossover. It also discovers that the 
nanometer-sized alloy catalyst particles can only obtain for the Pt58-Ru32-Os10 or Pt50-Ru50 or 
Pt50-Sn50 through the IR method, which benefit to the high catalyst activity of the methanol 
electro-oxidation. 
 
Keywords: Impregnation-Reduction method, methanol crossover, reactive methanol filter, 
nanometer-sized spherical particles, alloy catalyst

文獻探討 
直接甲醇燃料電池(Direct methanol fuel cell, 簡稱 DMFC)具有系統結構簡單、體積能量
密度高、啟動時間短、運轉可靠性高及燃料補充方便等優點，特別適合各種移動式或可攜式
動力源的用途，近年來，由於在可攜式電子產品電源的應用潛力而引起廣泛研究[1-4]，而其
必須具備高功率密度和低電催化觸媒負載量才能達到減少電池組重量、體積與價格的商業化
需求[2-3, 5]，因此，研究如何降低觸媒使用量並提高電極的性能一直是 DMFC 的重要研究課
題。目前，DMFC 效率仍偏低主要是因陽極的電催化動力學較弱，同時存在甲醇竄透(Methanol 
cross-over)現象降低甲醇利用率與增加陰極極化等問題[3-4, 6-8]，故仍有許多研究致力於改善
這兩關鍵問題。 
針對陽極的電催化觸媒電極動力學較弱的問題，主要是因甲醇反應物在陽極上的電催化
氧化反應，要比氫氧燃料電池的陽極反應來的複雜，其中一個甲醇分子經氧化反應生成二氧
化碳(CO2)必須進行六電子的轉移過程，此一過程必須藉助電催化觸媒開闢新的反應途徑來加
速反應的進行[4, 6]。目前，有能力進行甲醇電吸附反應的觸媒並不多，在酸性電解質中，只
有以鉑為基體的觸媒可以達到反應所需的活性和化學穩定性[4]。當以純鉑為觸媒，於電催化
氧化反應過程中產生的一氧化碳將會與鉑鍵合形成 Pt－CO 中間產物；此時，鉑本身並無法
再進行任何電催化反應而產生鉑觸媒毒化的現象。解決之道是設法提供活性氧以促成 CO 的
氧化反應，即將鉑觸媒表面上的 CO 氧化成 CO2 後脫附離開觸媒表面，如此下一個甲醇分子
才能夠在鉑觸媒表面繼續進行氧化反應；而活性氧原子來自於水，但當陽極電位過低(＜0.4V)
時，水分子無法在鉑觸媒表面進行吸附與活化，因此，必須加入第二觸媒以協助水分子的活
化與解離，目前作為 DMFC 第二觸媒的金屬大都為稀有金屬或貴重金屬，例如錫(Sn)、釕(Ru)、
錸 (Re)、鉬 (Mo)、鎢 (Wu)等 [4, 9-11] ，其添加的方法以兩種觸媒熔煉成為鉑基合金
(Platinum-base Alloy)的形式方可有效增強電催化氧化活性，其中以 Pt-Ru 及 Pt-Sn 合金效果最
佳。至於雙元合金觸媒中第二金屬觸媒含量對 DMFC 性能之影響目前仍是眾說紛紜。Gasteiger
等人[12-13] 探討 Pt-Ru 合金成份比例對 DMFC 性能之影響，實驗結果認為於 0.5M CH3OH 加
上 0.5M H2SO4 的溶液中，溫度在 25℃與 60℃時，Ru 在鉑基合金中的最佳原子數比例分別為
10%與 30%，然而，有更多的研究顯示 Pt-Ru 合金電極之鉑釕原子數比接近 1:1 時是具有最高

另一造成 DMFC 效率偏低的原因是於質子交換膜(PEM)的甲醇竄透現象，這現象是部分
甲醇尚未反應即通過 PEM 向陰極滲透，如此不僅降低甲醇的利用率，還會造成陰極極化的大
幅增加，進而降低 DMFC 性能。於是許多研究致力於避免甲醇的竄透現象發生以提昇 DMFC
的性能[3]。Tricoly 等 [23] 於全氟磺酸型膜 (如 Nafion®膜)中加入 Ce4+離子研究其對甲醇竄
透的阻隔效果，結果發現適量 Ce4+離子濃度可有效降低甲醇的滲透。Pu 等[24]利用表面塗佈
技術於 Nafion®膜表面塗佈一層抗甲醇滲透導質子的 metal hybrid 材料(如 Pd)，研究發現此三
明治結構確實對甲醇竄透有非常好的阻隔效果，卻同時也增加質子傳導阻力使電池性能下
降。Wainright 等 [25-28] 開發新型酸摻合的聚苯並咪唑(Acid-Doped PolyBenzimidazole, PBI)
固態電解質膜，此膜的甲醇竄透率比 Nafion®膜低一個數量級數，而輸出功率密度可達 100-300 
mW/cm2；此外，由於 PBI 膜能夠在 130-150℃的高溫下傳導質子，故可使用氣態甲醇作為陽
極燃料，同時陽極甲醇的氧化速率也因操作溫度的增加而提高。由以上文獻查考及討論指出，
降低或避免甲醇竄透現象的發生，不外乎於 PEM 上製作一阻隔甲醇滲透層及發展新型抗甲醇
竄透的 PEM。事實上，若能於 PEM 陽極表面內製作二層高氧化活性的三元(如 Pt-Ru-Os)或四
元(如 Pt-Ru-Ir-Os)合金奈米粒子層，由於三元或四元合金觸媒有較強電催化能力，再加上奈
米合金粒子具有高觸媒表面活性及電化學活性面積，對滲入 PEM 內甲醇有很強氧化活性，使
PEM 內甲醇迅速被氧化，導致到達 PEM 內的甲醇濃度幾乎為零，自然不會有甲醇竄透現象
的發生。本研究擬透過於 PEM 表面內的二層三元或四元合金奈米粒子層結構設計以過濾進入
PEM 內甲醇，以達到避免甲醇竄透現象發生同時提昇甲醇整體氧化速率、燃料利用率及觸媒
使用率，並改善 DMFC 性能。

還原與侷限空間還原，其中 Brust 等人[41]發展的相轉移合成法(phase transfer method)就是自
由空間還原法，並用以製作奈米金粒子。此法對於奈米粒子大小和形狀都有很好的控制。而
IR 法是屬侷限空間還原，Pt 與 Ru 離子是植入於 PEM 內，以 NaBH4 為還原劑擴散進入 PEM
內進行還原反應，控制反應溫度、反應時間及使 PEM 膨脹溶劑，應可於 PEM 內製得奈米 Pt-Ru
合金粒子或奈米層。目前，本人不僅成功利用此法於 PEM 內製作出二層不同成份含量觸媒
層，還利用固定反應物濃度及溫度，控制還原反應時間製作出二層 Pt-Ru 奈米粒子層，經測
試於 60℃的極化曲線顯示性能較 Fujiwara 等人[36]的膜電極組要好約 3.5 倍(0.40V 對應
0.23A/cm2)，而甲醇燃料供應下可獲得 OCV 值高達約 0.85V。IR 法只需控制適當還原反應時
間，便可輕易於 PEM 內獲得奈米 Pt-Ru 合金粒子或奈米層；而於還原反應中加入甲醇則可使
沉積 Pt-Ru 合金顆粒呈多孔性。因此，從所獲得觸媒層的特性、易製作奈米金屬粒子或奈米
層及製作技術的方便性這些觀點，本研究擬採用化學植入-還原技術於 PEM 表面內製作二層
三元或四元合金觸媒奈米粒子層，臺灣國內並未發現有這方面的研究。 
目前，以 IR 法製作 DMFC 電極，反應區域不足及 PEM 內觸媒層沒有導電子介質是主
要原因；若能將二維反應區拓展為三維結構反應區，且觸媒是奈米 Pt-Ru 合金粒子或奈米層，
並於 PEM 內觸媒層引入導電子介質，如利用直印法直接塗佈導電子觸媒層於經化學植入-還
原處理過之 PEM 上，再配合 MEA 製作步驟之熱壓碳布或碳紙擴散層到導電子觸媒層上，可
使導電子層嵌入到 PEM 表面觸媒層內，進而連通 PEM 內電子通道，改善歐姆過電位的問題，
將可大幅提高電極的性能。然而，過去直印法是先將 H+型 PEM 轉換成 Na+型，再塗佈一層
導電子觸媒層，接著再將 PEM 轉換成原來的 H+型 PEM。根據上一年度計畫的結果指出此法
缺點是導電子觸媒層對 PEM 黏著性不佳，且 最後於酸溶液處理程序易使觸媒脫落；另外，

3. 甲醇竄透及 MEA 性能 
圖十二是各 MEA 的陰極 CO2 濃度隨電流密度變化圖，其中空心符號代表 60℃，實心符
號代表 80℃。圖中可觀察到，所有設計 MEA (即於 PEM 內沉積四元或三元或二元合金觸媒
層)於陰極 CO2 量於 60℃均比對照組 normal-MEA 小；對 80℃，normal-MEA 與 MEA-Pt-Sn
的 CO2 量均超過 10000ppm(超過 CO2 偵測器偵測範圍)，同樣的，所有設計 MEA 的 CO2 量均
小於 normal-MEA，顯示 PEM 內含有一合金觸媒層可降低甲醇竄透量，其中含 Pt-Sn 合金可
降低甲醇竄透量約 18%，Pt-Ru-Ir-Os 合金可降低甲醇竄透量約 31%，Pt-Ru 合金可降低甲醇
竄透量約 35%，而 Pt-Ru-Os 合金可降低甲醇竄透量約 39%。此趨勢與峰電流密度大小類似。 
圖十三是各 MEA 於 60 及 80℃穩定 CO2 濃度下所得極化曲線。在 60℃及 80℃下，所有
MEA 於 PEM 內含有沉積合金觸媒層的性能均優於 normal-MEA，顯示 PEM 沉積合金觸媒層
可提昇性能，若以 100mA/cm2 為基準，其中於 60℃，MEA-Pt-Sn 性能提昇約 2.1%，
MEA-Pt-Ru-Ir-Os 性能提昇約 4.8%，MEA-Pt-Ru 性能提昇約 9.5%，MEA-Pt-Ru-Os 性能提昇
約 11.1%；而於 80℃，MEA-Pt-Sn 性能提昇約 3.3%，MEA-Pt-Ru-Ir-Os 性能提昇約 7.5%，
MEA-Pt-Ru 性能提昇約 12.2%，MEA-Pt-Ru-Os 性能提昇約 12.4%。綜合以上結果，於 PEM
內沉積合金層可有效降低甲醇竄透量及提昇 MEA 性能。 
由於 PEM 內沉積合金層可氧化甲醇同時合金層為層狀結構，推論其降低甲醇竄透並提
昇性能的機制如圖十四所示。竄透甲醇於 PEM 內沉積合金層進行氧化反應產生 CO2、H+及
e-，其中 CO2 由陽極端釋出造成陽極端 CO2 濃度增加(與實驗觀察結果符合)，而 e-及 H+分別
經由外接電路與 PEM 由陽極傳送到陰極，產生電流提昇性能。另外，由於 PEM 內沉積合金
層可於 PEM 表面與陽極界面扮演電極角色，再加上降低甲醇竄透量，減少陰極極化程度。因
此，在此三效應下，本研究設計的 MEA 可降低甲醇竄透量同時提昇 MEA 性能。 
文獻上結果顯示，觸媒催化活性依序是 Pt-Ru-Ir-Os > Pt-Ru-Os > Pt-Ru > Pt-Sn。但本研
究結果顯示，於 PEM 內所合成的 Pt49-Ru35-Ir6-Os10 觸媒催化活性不佳，與 Pt50-Sn50類似，而
Pt50-Ru50 卻有最佳觸媒催化活性，其次是 Pt58-Ru32-Os10；這是由以 IR 法較易合成均勻的
Pt50-Ru50 以及 Pt58-Ru32-Os10，而這兩合金層均呈現均勻奈米顆粒，同時此合金層密度小於 2.6 
g/cm3 。故其抗甲醇竄透能力與性能提昇依序是 MEA-Pt-Ru-Os ≈ MEA-Pt-Ru > 
MEA-Pt-Ru-Ir-Os > MEA-Pt-Sn。

4. Conclusions 
The nucleation rate of Pt is greater than the growth rate of Pt particle in the process of 
chemical platinization on a Nafion membrane. Therefore, extension distribution of nanosphere 
particles to three-dimensional zone in Nafion membrane has occurred in all growing time. The 
resulted platinum layer is formed by the nanosphere particles. At the reduction time between 10 and 
15 minutes, one can obtain the platinum layer formed by the two-dimensional growing of 
nanoparticle and the nanospheres particle growing from Pt nuclei. The three-dimensional growing 
in the thickness of platinum layer occurred in the reduction time greater than 15 minutes. The 
growth mechanism of platinum on a Nafion membrane using the chemical platinization is similar to 
that of the nanoparticle growing from the solution. The only difference lies in the formation of Pt 
layer at the reduction time exceeding 10 minutes. The variation of the concentration of Pt precursor, 
reaction temperature and concentration of reductant (NaBH4) do influence on the formation rate and 
distribution of Pt in PEM surface, but do not affect the growth mechanism of Pt on a Nafion 
membrane. Accordingly, one might obtain the nanometer-sized Pt particle or the Pt film with 
different thickness by easily controlling the reduction time in the chemical platinization process.

In this paper, a modified technique to prepare Pt-Ir catalyst layer on the proton exchange 
membrane (PEM) surface using the impregnation-reduction (IR) method is proposed to improve the 
electrocatalytic activity as well as the life cycle of the bifunctional oxygen electrode (BOE). The 
resulted electrocatalysts were characterized by the Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM), X-ray 
Diffraction (XRD), Electron Probe Micro-Analysis (EPMA), and Transmission Electron 
Microscope (TEM). The electrocatalytic properties of the Pt-Ir layer on PEM surface for the oxygen 
reduction and water oxidation reactions as well as the life cycle of MEA were investigated. 
Experimental results showed that the Ir particles were dispersed densely in the platinum layer 
through the modified IR technique. The atomic ratio of Pt over Ir elements was 9:1, and the resulted 
thickness of the obtained Pt-Ir catalyst layer was about 1.0μm. The Pt-Ir catalyst layer was 
composed of Pt layer doped with Ir nano-particles comprising nano Pt-Ir alloy phase. The large 
surface area of Ir core with Pt shell particles and the presence of nano Pt-Ir alloy phase led to a 
higher electrocatalytic activity of BOE. Due to the good binding between the Nafion® membrane 
and the Pt-Ir alloy catalyst, as well as the composite structure of the resulted Pt-Ir, the life cycle of 
Unitized Regenerative Fuel Cell (URFC) is improved through this novel BOE. 
Keywords: Pt-Ir catalyst layer, electrocatalytic activity, oxygen electrode, impregnation-reduction 
method.

ABSTRACT 
This paper deposited a layer of nanometer-sized 
Pt50-Sn50 catalyst particles in the proton exchange membrane 
(PEM) surface by impregnation-reduction (IR) method to 
suppress the methanol crossover and improve the utilization 
efficiency of methanol fuel. The suppression of methanol 
crossover and the performance of membrane electrode 
assembly (MEA) for the proposed structure were compared to 
those with normal-PEM structure. The possible mechanism of 
the suppression of methanol crossover was investigated. SEM, 
X-ray, EDS and EPMA analysis were used to characterize 
microstructures, phases, chemical composition and 
distributions for the obtained electro-catalyst layers. Methanol 
crossover rate in a DMFC was determined by measuring the 
CO2 concentration at the cathode exit in real time. 
Experimental results demonstrate that the PEM with a layer of 
nanometer-sized Pt50-Sn50 catalyst particles suppresses 
methanol crossover by up to 18% more than the normal-PEM, 
and yield a 8% better MEA performance than the 
normal-MEA. The combination effects of Pt50-Sn50 in PEM as 
a methanol filter and electrode contribute to the suppression of 
methanol crossover and improvement of cell performance as 
well as the utilization efficiency of methanol fuel.  
Keywords: Methanol crossover, nanometer-sized catalyst 
particles, impregnation-reduction method, 
utilization of methanol fuel, Pt50-Sn50 alloy. 
1. INTRODUCTION 
Direct methanol fuel cell (DMFC) is a promising energy 
conversion device especially attractive for the portable 
application. However, methanol crossover from the anode to 
the cathode was a very serious problem that severely reduces 
the cell voltage, current density, fuel utilization and hence the 
cell performance [1-2]. The issue concerning the prevention of 
methanol crossover from anode to cathode through the PEM 
must be addressed to increase the efficiency and stability of 
DMFCs before this technology is commercially viable. 
Numerous attempts [3-8] had been proposed to mitigate the 
methanol crossover, such as the developments of new proton 
exchange membrane (PEM) with low crossover rate of 
methanol, the barriers of methanol crossover containing Pd 
metal deposited on the PEM surface to form a sandwich 
structure, ion-exchanging appropriate amount of Ce4+ into the 
PEM et. al.  
The bimetallic electrocatalyst such as Pt-Sn have been 
investigated toward ethanol electro-oxidation and exhibit 
better activity than pure Pt and Pt-Ru catalysts [9-12]. The 
electrocatalyst activity of Pt-Sn toward methanol 
electro-oxidation is inferior to that of Pt-Ru. However, the 
Pt-Sn is cheaper than the Pt-Ru.  
This study proposes an active catalyst layer containing 
nanometer-sized Pt50-Sn50 catalyst particles deposited on the 
PEM anode side surface using the impregnation-reduction 
method (IR) to mitigate methanol crossover and improve the 
fuel utilization as well as the cell performance. Figure 1 shows 
the proposed PEM and MEA structures. The influences of a 
layer of nanometer-sized Pt50-Sn50 catalyst particles deposited 
on the PEM anode side on the suppression of methanol 
crossover and the MEA performance are investigated.

2-1. Preparation of a layer of Pt-Sn deposited on PEM 
surface by IR method 
Water was used as the solvent in all reactions. The 
pre-treatment PEM was placed on the armed side of a reactor 
designed in-house in order to offer anode side only deposition. 
The ion-exchange process of Sn precursor was conducted by 
adding 14ml of methanol to 80ml of SnCl2 (0.1191g ) solution 
at 70℃ for 3 hours. Following by adding of [Pt(NH3)4]Cl2 
(0.0971g) into the SnCl2 solution for 40 minutes. The 
reduction reaction was performed by adding 0.3625g of 
NaBH4 into methanol (3.8M) solution after no residue Pt and 
Sn ions were detected in the reactor. The solution was then 
heated to 70℃ for 30 minutes. The resulting PEM was treated 
with 1M H2SO4 at 80℃ for 2~3 hours to replace the 
non-reacted Pt and Sn precursors with protons to yield the 
fully H+ form of PEM. Finally, the catalyst layer of Pt-Sn on 
PEM was obtained when the H2SO4 had been fully washed out 
of the PEM. The resulting Pt-Sn loading was approximately 
0.90 mg cm-2, as shown in table 1.

4. CONCLUSION 
A facile procedure for the preparation of a layer of 
nanometer-sized Pt50-Sn50 alloy catalyst particles deposited on 
the PEM anode side surface is developed using the IR method 
with the designed experimental conditions. The average size 
of Pt50-Sn50 nano-spheres is 50nm. A layer of Pt50-Sn50 catalyst 
particles with thickness of 1.30μm concentrates on the PEM 
surface that connecting the direct-printed Pt50-Ru50 layer. The 
existence of a layer of nanometer-sized Pt50-Sn50 alloy catalyst 
particles in PEM mitigates the methanol crossover up to 18% 
and improves the cell performance up to 8% as compare to 
that of the PEM without treatment. This is because the 
crossed-over methanol can be simultaneously oxidized by the 
nanometer-sized Pt50-Sn50 alloy catalyst particles in PEM to 
form the electrons, protons and CO2. Since the layer of 
Pt50-Sn50 alloy catalyst particles adjacent to the direct-printed 
Pt50-Ru50 layer, the produced electrons transfer via 
direct-printed layer to the external circuit and reach the 
cathode. The protons directly conduct to cathode through the 
PEM and the produced CO2 evolve at the anode. The Pt50-Sn50 
alloy catalyst layer also serves as the anode electrode at the 
interface between the direct-printed layer and Pt50-Sn50 layer. 
As a result, it suppresses the methanol crossover and improves 
the utilization efficiency of methanol fuel as well as the cell 
performance.
