ZnSe/CdSe 與 ZnSe/ZnCdSeTe
多量子盤成長與特性
洪上超 副教授
實踐大學資訊科技與通訊學系
摘要
本研究指出在氧化矽基板上成長高密度的 ZnSe/CdSe, ZnSe/ZnCdSeTe 多量子盤。結果發現成長的奈
米尖端由立方閃鋅礦和先鋅礦結構混合而成。也觀察到 ZnSe/ZnCdSeTe 多量子盤發光強度大於同質的
ZnSe。此外並發現 ZnSe/ZnCdSeTe 多量子盤的活化能井寬分別為 12 奈米 189 毫電子伏特、16 奈米
205 毫電子伏特、20 奈米 292 毫電子伏特、24 奈米 240 毫電子伏特。
The growth of high density ZnSe/CdSe and ZnSe/ZnCdSeTe multi-quantum disks on oxidized Si
substrate have been reported in this study. It was found the as-grown nanotips were tapered with
mixture of cubic zinc-blende and hexagonal wurtzite structures. It was also found that
photoluminescence intensities observed from these ZnSe/ZnCdSeTe multi-quantum disks were much
larger than that observed from the homogeneous ZnSe. Furthermore, it was found that activation
energies for the ZnSe/CdSe multi-quantum disks with well widths, Lw, of 12, 16, 20 and 24 nm were 189,
205, 292 and 240 meV, respectively.
關鍵詞 –多量子盤、奈米尖錐
Index Terms - multi-quantum disks, nanotips
1. 簡介
ZnSe 是一種重要的寬能隙半導體, 在室溫下擁有直接能隙 2.67 電子伏特與 21 毫電子伏特激子束
縛能。二維的 ZnSe 晶層生長在 GaAs 基板也達到匹配，因這些特性讓 ZnSe 在近幾年來受到重視。除
了二元 ZnSe，它可能成長與三元/四元薄膜，而達到 ZnSe 為基底的異質結構。根據這些結構，ZnSe
為基礎的二維發光二極體【1】，雷射二極管【2】和光檢測器【3】都得到證明，它也可能成長一維 ZnSe
奈米線藉由不同方法【4-8】。一維半導體奈米結構擁有較大的表面積對體積比，展現獨特性質運用再
新設備的應用令人滿意。其中以一維 II-VI 族半導體材料 ZnO 被廣泛的研究。例如： ZnO 奈米線可以
運用在氣體感測器【9-10】。也表示 ZnO 奈米線光檢測器展現出高光導電增益【11】。然而，眾所皆知
P 型 ZnO 是非常不穩定。結果顯示，ZnO 基底 P-N 接面元件擁有好的可靠性是難以實現，相似氧化鋅，
ZnSe 也是一種寬能隙的 II-VI 族半導體和較激子束縛能【12】。
相反的， P 型 ZnSe 透過適當的參雜，異質結構也已經被廣泛運用在 ZnSe 為基底的二維元件。同

Abstract
A number of new technologies require the use of conical and sharp tips to serve as electron
emitters in the vacuum microelectronics. In this paper, we improved radius of curvature, height and
cone angle of emiters, in order to reach the enhancement result of field enhancement factor (β). In this 
experiment, we developed a fabrication process to improve geometry of emitter by employing isotropic
dry etching in pure SF6 and a mixture of SF6 and O2 followed by thermal oxidation technique. In the
end, we successfully achieved excellent conical emitters with 5-10 nm radius of curvature, 4.4 um
height, 60° cone angle. The conical silicon emitters current-voltage characteristics shows that Eto = 4.8
V/um (turn-on electrics field) compare with 10 uA/cm2, maximum current density J = 60.4 uA/cm2 at
E = 8.14V/um (Voltage:1100v).Keywords：Silicon Field Emitters, Dry etching
