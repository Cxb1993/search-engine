摘  要 
本研究主要以坪頂淨水場之淨水污泥作為複合金屬的觸媒材料，經燒結及加鹼活化後
應用於異相臭氧催化反應來進行水中有機物的降解反應，期望藉由污泥觸媒表面的催化機
制來加速水中溶解臭氧的分解，並產生反應力更強之氫氧自由基，以有效加速有機物的降
解效率。 
本研究分為兩個階段進行，第一階段是污泥觸媒的備製，第二階段則為異相臭氧高級
氧化程序降解水中草酸及苯甲酸的探討。經分析結果可知淨水污泥主要成分為 SiO2、
Al2O3、Fe2O3等金屬氧化物；而污泥經由 500℃、650℃、800℃、950℃燒結後會使比表
面積下降。研究中改變鹼（NaOH）的濃度對淨水污泥進行活化，由 SEM 結果顯示經活化
過後之觸媒表面較為細緻且可明顯看出表面為片狀堆疊，而污泥經加鹼活化後，亦有效增
加表面鹼性官能基（1.8 mmole/g 提升至 2.6 mmole/g）。另外，本研究亦嘗試以備製完成
之觸媒進行異相臭氧催化反應，實驗結果顯示在相同觸媒劑量下，經鹼活化的觸媒對草酸
去除效果有明顯的提升，隨著加鹼濃度的增加，其對草酸的去除效果分別為 29%、53%、
84%、87%。其主要原因推斷是觸媒表面經加鹼活化後，觸媒表面氫氧官能基可有效提升；
由於表面氫氧官能基所佔之活性位置較多，因此可促使更多臭氧分子在觸媒表面的活性位
置產生氫氧自由基，使溶液中草酸分子被分解，甚至進行進一步的礦化作用。另ㄧ個影響
草酸降解速率的因素為溶液的 pH，當草酸溶液之 pH 與觸媒的 pHpzc相近時，有利於觸媒
表面活性位置與反應物結合而形成反應性較佳的分子，使反應物更容易被降解，因此未加
鹼活化之觸媒在 pH 中性下有較佳的效果，而經以 2.5 N、5.0 N、10.0 N 等不同氫氧化鈉
濃度活化後的觸媒則在 pH=3 時效果最佳。此外，在臭氧催化苯甲酸實驗中，藉由 t-butanol
的添加可知臭氧對苯甲酸有很好的直接臭氧氧化效果，但對苯甲酸降解產生的大量中間產
物，僅有少部份被臭氧氧化去除。倘若以經 5.0 N 氫氧化鈉活化之觸媒搭配臭氧進行異相
臭氧催化苯甲酸，相對於單純使用臭氧氧化，添加 0.25 g 觸媒，反應 30 分鐘後，即可使
苯甲酸去除效果由 58%堤升至 83%，TOC 的降解效果亦可由 15%提升至 52%。歸納上述
實驗結果可知，淨水污泥經由 800℃燒結、以 5.0 N NaOH 活化備製成為觸媒，搭配臭氧
對草酸及苯甲酸進行異性臭氧催化反應，可使草酸有效降解且完全礦化成二氧化碳及水；
用於苯甲酸及其中間產物的去除亦有相當的去除效率。 
 
關鍵字：淨水污泥、複合金屬觸媒、臭氧、異相臭氧催化程序 
 
Abstract 
In this research, wasted chemical sludge generated from Ping-Ding drinking water plant was 
employed as novel catalyst materials. By modifying the surface characterization of sludge using 
calcination and alkaline activation, ozone molecule in water would be decomposed and produced 
more free radicals with high oxidation potential on the degradation of organic substances. 
Two phases have been involved in this project including catalyst preparation and 
heterogeneous ozonation for oxalic acids and methyl benzoic acids. According to the research 
results, a new, efficient, and stable sludge catalyst has been successfully prepared and applied in 
oxalic acid (OA) decomposition by combining with heterogeneous ozonation at acidic pH, where 
the self-decomposition of ozone can be substantially neglected. The catalyst was characterized 
using various analytical techniques. X-ray diffraction (XRD) and inductively coupled plasma 
optima optical emission spectrometer (ICP-OES) analysis showed that sludge catalyst as prepared 
contains SiO2 as the major constituent and Al2O3, Fe2O3, MgO, K2O, CaO, NaO as minor ones. 
Scanning electron microscope (SEM) analysis revealed that non-crystal infrastructure was formed 
主要混凝之機制是由於不同正價解離鋁離子、聚合物或單體在溶液不同 pH 值形成氫
氧化鋁之固體沉積物，其影響如圖 2.1 所示。

淨水污泥的成分會隨著水質，混凝劑種類、混凝操作因素而改變，其無機元素種類包
含有矽、鉀、鋁、鐵等，除了鋁、鐵來自於淨水處理所添加的混凝劑，其他元素因與該淨
水場取水區的土壤元素類似。Schmitt et al.（1975）就以質譜儀分析混凝沉澱池污泥及反
沖洗廢水之元素，發現主要為矽、鉀、鋁、鐵等，其中除了鋁為淨水處理過程中所添加之
混凝劑外，其餘元素則與集水區域土壤之元素相似。陳宜晶（2004）研究所使用的淨水污
泥取自於豐原淨水場經機械脫水後堆置於曬乾床中之污泥餅，主要化學組成為 SiO2、
Al2O3、Fe2O3等成分，其中以 SiO2比例最高，乃因自來水集水過程中夾帶大量砂土礦物，
經混凝沉澱後收集而來。而 Babatunde and Zhao（2007）之研究也指出，鋁鹽污泥除含
有 Al、Si、Fe、Mg 及 Ca 等金屬外，亦檢測出微量的 P、Zi、Pb、Cd、Ni、Cu、Cr 及
Co。 
 
2.2 金屬氧化物的表面特性 
選擇觸媒時必須注意的金屬物理及化學特性包括：表面積、密度、孔隙體積、黏滯度、
孔隙率、化學穩定性、活性位置、路易斯酸鹼位置。決定金屬氧化物催化特性的主要參數
是酸度和鹼度。金屬氧化物表面的氫氧官能基形成布魯斯特酸位置，路易斯酸及路易斯鹼
位置則分別位於金屬陽離子及未飽和氧上，而路易斯酸位置與布魯斯特酸位置皆被認為是
金屬氧化物表面的活性位置之所在。另ㄧ個用來評估金屬氧化物離子交換能力的參數是
pHPZC，即金屬氧化物在零價電位點時的 pH。ㄧ般而言，金屬氧化物的離子交換能力取決
於表面氫氧官能基在不同 pH 下所產生的反應： 
在酸性條件下     M-OH + H+     M-OH2+  K1int 
在鹼性條件下     M-OH + OH-     M-O-+H2O  K2int 
K1int、K2int 為解離常數，pHPZC 與解離常數有關：pHPZC＝0.5（pK1int＋pK2int），當 pH
＜pK1int時，金屬氧化物具有陰離子交換的能力；而當 pH＞pK2int時，金屬氧化物則具有陽
離子交換的能力（如圖 2.2）。大部分的金屬氧化物都具有兩性離子的交換能力，相較於矽
的 pHPZC較低，因此只具有陽離子交換的能力。

3.1 淨水污泥觸媒備製 
淨水污泥備製流程如圖 3.2 所示。取 20 g 淨水污泥烘乾研磨，過篩使其粒徑小於 1

四、結果與討論 
4.1 污泥特性分析 
本研究使用之污泥來源為高雄坪頂淨水場，其引高屏溪之溪水做為主要水源。研究中
取得之污泥餅，經由去離子水洗淨數次後烘乾，並研磨過篩使其粒徑小於 1 mm，而後經
由燒結、加鹼活化等程序將污泥備製成為觸媒，並以 SEM、XRD、ICP-OES、BET、TOC
等儀器分析其表面特性之變化。 
4.1.1 坪頂淨水場污泥成分特性分析 
坪頂淨水場污泥成分特性以 SEM、EDS、XRD、ICP-OES、BET 及 TOC 進行分析。
圖 4.1 為淨水污泥經掃瞄式電子顯微鏡於不同倍率下觀察，由圖中可看出淨水污泥表面為
不規則、片狀堆疊，且結構較為鬆散；而經由能量散佈光譜儀（EDS）觀察污泥表面之半
定量分析（表 4.1）結果顯示，坪頂污泥表面無機物成分以 Si 為主，並有少部分的 Al，此
成分來源主要為進流水所含之礦物質以及在淨水單元所添加之多元氯化鋁（PAC）。X 光粉
末繞射儀（XRD）分析污泥之結果（圖 4.2）可看到在 2Θ=26.6 處有明顯波峰，根據 JCPDS
資料庫標準圖譜（JCPDS card No. 86-1630）比對可知其為結晶型態之 SiO2，其餘較弱之
波峰則可能為 Al2O3、Fe2O3、MgO、CaO、K2O 等繞射強度較弱之晶體結構。淨水污泥經
微波消化系統消化後，以感應耦合電漿光譜儀（ICP-OES）分析污泥組成，結果如表 4.1，
Si 及 Fe 的含量最高，約 362,750 mg/L 及 405,050 mg/L，其餘亦有 Al、Na、K、Mg、Ca
等成分，此結果顯示坪頂淨水場之污泥組成中含有大量之礦物質；然而藉由 EDS 及
ICP-OES 分析結果可知，污泥表面成分為 Si 及 Al，但污泥內部則包覆有大量之 Fe 成分。
此外，坪頂淨水場污泥比表面積（BET）為 46.374 m2/g，總有機碳（TOC）含量則為
18,836.72 mg/kg。

4.1.3 P800 污泥加鹼（NaOH）活化後之表面特性分析 
淨水污泥燒結後置於 2.5 N、5.0 N、10.0 N 等三種不同濃度的氫氧化鈉溶液中進行污
泥表面活化以備製成為觸媒（簡稱 P8002.5 N、P8005.0 N、P80010.0 N），經 SEM、XRD、BET、
FTIR、ICP-OES 及表面鹼性官能基測定可得知，其污泥主要金屬成分並無太大改變，比表
面積亦無變化；但在掃描式電子顯微鏡下（圖 4.5），在 5,000 倍拍攝到污泥表面，相較於
經過 800 度燒結後的污泥，隨氫氧化鈉濃度由 2.5 N 提升至 5.0 N，其表面片狀結構更為明
顯且更為緻密，顯示當氫氧化鈉濃度提高至 5.0 N 或更高時，污泥表面結構較不緊實的片
狀組織會遭強鹼而破壞。 
本研究中，加鹼活化的目的為期望在不改變淨水污泥原有的金屬成分下，藉由氫氧化
鈉的添加，增加觸媒表面的氫氧官能基（hydroxyl group，-OH），以利於在後續異相臭氧
催化實驗中，可加速水中溶解臭氧降解產生氫氧自由基（hydroxyl radical，‧OH），以提
升有機物降解之成效，故在此以金屬表面鹼性官能基（-OH）測定法及傅立葉變換紅外光
譜儀（FTIR）測定加鹼活化後之觸媒表面是否可有效增加觸媒表面之氫氧官能基。以 FTIR
進行分析，也可由圖 4.7 看出，在波長約 970 cm-1處有明顯波峰，根據相關文獻（莊連春，
2004；李中光，2006；黃富昌，2004；許振宏，2001）推測該波峰可能為 Si-OH、Si-O
的伸縮震動峰，而隨氫氧化鈉的添加，該處的穿透峰亦有小幅增加，推測加鹼之後，增加
了污泥表面 Si 與 OH 官能基鍵結造成此結果；而由測定觸媒表面鹼性官能基（-OH）可知
（如圖 4.6），添加氫氧化鈉活化後之觸媒，其表面鹼性官能基（-OH）可由原本的 1.8 
mmole/g 提升至 2.6 mmole/g，而其中 5.0 N 及 10.0 N 氫氧化鈉活化後之觸媒其表面鹼性
官能基濃度僅差異 0.1 mmole/g，推估原因為觸媒表面可鍵結氫氧官能基的活性位置有限，
故即使再提高氫氧化鈉濃度亦無法有效增加觸媒表面鹼性官能基的濃度。 
4.2 溶解臭氧降解反應 
由於氫氧自由基與有機物的反應是造成有機物降解的重要機制，而本實驗中氫氧自由
基的生成主要來自水中溶解臭氧的分解，故期望透過 P800、P8002.5 N、P8005.0 N、P80010.0 N
觸媒的添加能夠加速水中溶解臭氧的分解並在短時間內產生大量氫氧自由基，而使目標有
機物能被大量的降解。圖 4.8 為 pH=3 的情況下，添加觸媒與單純臭氧自解效率的比較，
結果指出在 10 分鐘時，添加觸媒的飽和臭氧水中臭氧濃度已分別降低 48%（P800）、77%
（P8002.5 N）、89%（P8005.0 N及 P80010.0 N），而在 20 分鐘後，皆降低 80%以上，高於臭
氧自解反應的 50%，顯示出經加鹼活化後的觸媒能有效地提升水中溶解臭氧的分解速率。

4.3 草酸降解反應 
4.3.1 觸媒對草酸的吸附反應 
由 Legube and Karpel Vel Leitner（1999）所提出的異相臭氧催化機制中，有機物的
降解反應通常是來自觸媒對有機物的吸附行為，但並非所有被吸附的有機物都會被氧化劑
所降解，有少部份則因被觸媒吸附而去除；故於本研究中，在 pH=3 的條件下添加 0.25 g
觸媒以測試觸媒對草酸吸附的去除效果。經過一小時的吸附試驗，0.25 g P800 及 P8002.5 N
對草酸的吸附量約為 10 mg/L，而 P8005.0 N及 P80010.0 N對草酸的吸附量則約為 17 mg/L，
顯示本研究使用之污泥並未能有效吸附草酸分子，即使加鹼活化後的觸媒，其吸附能力也
僅有少許的提升而已。 
4.3.1 氫氧自由基捕集劑（t-butanol）之添加 
為了區分本研究中草酸降解的主要因素為臭氧或氫氧自由基作用，故於反應中添加
0.68 mM 的 t-butanol，比較添加自由基捕集劑時，對草酸降解的影響。在未添加觸媒的情
況下，添加 t-butanol 於臭氧降解草酸的實驗，由圖 4.9(a)可看出添加 t-butanol 並不影響草
酸的降解效果，故可知當 pH=3 的情況下，無自由基被 t-butanol 捕捉，顯示臭氧並未自行
降解產生自由基，而水中的溶解臭氧仍以臭氧分子存在；經 60 分鐘反應後，20%的草酸降
解效果來自於臭氧直接與 HC2O4－反應，進而使草酸降解為二氧化碳及水。若加入不同觸媒

4.3.4 觸媒劑量之影響 
B. Kasprzyk-Hordern et al.（2003）中提到ㄧ般異相臭氧催化反應有三種可能的機制： 
z 觸媒表面吸附之臭氧可形成活性分子與有機物反應 
z 觸媒表面吸附有機物進而促進有機物與臭氧反應

七、計畫成果自評 
 
本年度研究主要延續上年度計畫以淨水廠之鋁鹽污泥作為複合金屬的觸媒材料，經加
鹼改質及熱處理後應用於臭氧異相催化反應來進行水中有機物的降解反應，藉由污泥觸媒
表面的催化機制來加速水中溶解臭氧的分解並產生反應力更強之氫氧自由基，以有效加速
水中有機物的降解效率。經分析結果可知淨水污泥主要成分為SiO2、Al2O3、Fe2O3等金屬
氧化物；而污泥經由500℃、650℃、800℃、950℃燒結後會使比表面積下降。研究中改變
鹼（NaOH）的濃度對淨水污泥進行活化，由SEM結果顯示經活化過後之觸媒表面較為細
緻且可明顯看出表面為片狀堆疊，而污泥經加鹼活化後，亦有效增加表面鹼性官能基（1.8 
mmole/g提升至2.6 mmole/g）。另外，本研究亦嘗試以備製完成之觸媒進行異相臭氧催化
反應，實驗結果顯示在相同觸媒劑量下，經鹼活化的觸媒對草酸去除效果有明顯的提升，
隨著加鹼濃度的增加，其對草酸的去除效果分別為29%、53%、84%、87%。其主要原因
推斷是觸媒表面經加鹼活化後，觸媒表面氫氧官能基可有效提升；由於表面氫氧官能基所
佔之活性位置較多，因此可促使更多臭氧分子在觸媒表面的活性位置產生氫氧自由基，使
溶液中草酸分子被分解，甚至進行進一步的礦化作用。另ㄧ個影響草酸降解速率的因素為
溶液的pH，當草酸溶液之pH與觸媒的pHpzc相近時，有利於觸媒表面活性位置與反應物結
合而形成反應性較佳的分子，使反應物更容易被降解，因此未加鹼活化之觸媒在pH中性下
有較佳的效果，而經以2.5 N、5.0 N、10.0 N等不同氫氧化鈉濃度活化後的觸媒則在pH=3
時效果最佳。歸納上述實驗結果可知，淨水污泥經由800℃燒結、以5.0 N NaOH活化備製
成為觸媒，搭配臭氧對草酸及苯甲酸進行異性臭氧催化反應，可使草酸有效降解且完全礦
化成二氧化碳及水且用於苯甲酸及其中間產物的去除亦有相當的去除效率。

Abstract 
A highly efficient, carbon free rice husk ash green catalyst has been 
successfully used as a catalyst in Oxalic Acid (OA) decomposition at pH 3. The 
catalyst was characterized by using various analytical techniques. X-Ray Diffraction 
(XRD) analysis confirmed that the as-synthesized RHA is pure silica, X-Ray 
Fluorescence (XRF) analysis showed that RHA catalyst contains 92.8% of silica and 
EDX analysis confirmed absence of other impurities. Cold Field Scanning Electron 
Microscope (FESEM) analysis revel non uniform particle size and presence of 
external pores on the catalyst surface. Elemental Analysis (EA) indicated that RHA 
contain negligible percentage of carbon content. Oxalic acid degradation as well as its 
TOC removal was studied at various physic-chemical conditions. Adsorption and 
ozonation processes are not effective for oxalic acid and its TOC removal. RHA 
catalyzed ozonation causes 76.2% of decomposition and 75% of TOC in 60 minutes. 
0.5 g/l of RHA dosage was found to be optimum for effective removal.  The 
efficiencies of RHA was compared with other commercially available catalysts and 
the order was found to be RHA ≈ TiO2–P25> FeOOH> SiO2> Al2O3>ZnO. When the 
inlet concentration increases from 40 to 120 mg/l, the oxalic acid decomposition 
increases from 33.3% to 76.3% and TOC removal increases from 33% to 75%. 
Catalytic ozonation process has been studied at three different pHs and found that 
catalytic ozonation process is more pronounced than ozonation process at all pHs. A 
plausible mechanism of oxalic acid decomposition has been proposed. It is concluded 
that RHA is an efficient green catalyst for oxalic acid removal.  
Keywords: Rice husk ash, ozone, oxalic acid, catalytic ozonation, TOC.

of human beings have also led to the presence of new compounds in the effluent 
streams. Growing concerns about the environment have resulted in the development

green chemistry research. Rice is common crops grown allover the world and upon its 
separation from the paddy, one-third of its mass remains an agricultural waste 
material. The Rice Husk (RH) is another waste material which is generated in the 
milling process when the grain is separated and it is very cheap and available in large 
quantity. Earlier research had shown that rice husk is composed of 20% ash, 38% 
                                                 
  
various catalysts [21-30]. The primary objective of this study was to investigate oxalic 
acid degradation and to test the catalytic activity of RHA catalysts. To the best of our

2. Experiments. 
2.1. Chemicals 
           Anhydrous oxalic acid obtained from SHOWA chemical CO (Japan), 
was of analytical grade and used without further purification. HPLC grade 
Acetonitrile, t- butanol (99%) from Merck chemical Co in Taiwan. Indigo dye from 
ACROS organics was of analytical grade and used as received. The surface properties 
of RHA are presented in table.1. All other chemicals α-Al2O3 (purity 99.9%), SiO2 
(purity 99%) SHOWA Chemical Ltd., Japan; TiO2-P25  from Degussa Germany; 
ZnO (purity 99.86%), TEDIA chemical  USA, α-FeOOH (98%) from local 
manufacturer in Taiwan, were used as received and the particle size of these catalysts 
measured by TEM were 50–100 nm, 100–500 nm, 30 nm,  50–100 nm, and 50–100 
nm, respectively. For all experimental work, deionized water Milli q-Plus, resistance 
= 18.2 M Ώ was used. The pH of aqueous solution was not adjusted using any acid or 
base in the catalytic ozonation process. Oxalic acid is prepared in deionized water 
before the experiments.     
2.2. Catalytic ozonation experiments 
Ozone was generated from dried pure oxygen by corona discharge using an 
ozone generator (Ozonair RXO-5), which can produce 6% ozone concentration (w/w) 
in the oxygen enriched gas stream. To better maintain the performance of the 
ozonation system, the oxygen was dried using a molecular sieve before being entered 
into the ozone generator. A 0.5-l semi-batch reactor, which was made of glass with 
the dimensions of 9 cm diameter and 15 cm tall, was used to facilitate the operation of 
all catalytic oxidation processes (Fig. 1). Ozone was introduced through a porous 
fritted diffuser that can produce fairly fine bubbles with diameter less than 1 mm, 
which had been determined using a camera with close-up lens and image analysis 
software Matrox Inspector 2.0 [32]. The gas flow rate was regulated by a mass flow 
controller (Brooks 5850E). The gaseous ozone concentrations in the inlet and outlet 
stream were determined and monitored spectrophotometrically by the absorbance of 
ozone measured in a 2 mm flow-through quartz cuvette at the wavelength 254 nm. An 
extinction coefficient of 3000 M-1 cm-1 was used to convert absorbance into 
concentration units [32]. Ozone applied dosage is typically defined as the product of 
gas flow rate, ozone concentration, and ozonation period, divided by the reactor 
volume. A 500 ml solution containing appropriate concentration of oxalic acid and 
RHA catalyst was placed in a reactor and inorder to make homogeneous slurries, the 
solution was magnetically stirred at the speed of 900 rpm. After few minutes of 
stirring, ozone was introduced into the reactor. All experiments were operated at 25 
ºC using a water jacket around the reactor.  
2.3. Oxalic acid analysis

3.2. Aqueous ozone decomposition in the presence of RHA 
The aqueous ozone decomposition experiments were performed at various 
RHA concentrations to determine the role of RHA in the aqueous ozone 
decomposition at pH 3.  The effect of catalyst dosage on ozone decomposition was 
investigated from 0.05 to 0.5g/l and the results are shown in Fig.4. Increasing the 
catalyst loading from 0.05, 0.1, 0.2, 0.5 g/l increases the decomposition efficiency 
from 61.8 %, 71.3 %, 77.1% and 85.2% at the time of 15 minutes respectively. It is 
shown that an increase in the RHA concentration significantly increased the 
decomposition, demonstrating the effectiveness of RHA as a catalyst in reaction. The 
decomposition rate of ozone at the various RHA concentrations was found to be 
linearly related with the ozone decomposition rate, because the increased amount of 
RHA provides more active sites for ozone adsorption and decomposition. Our results 
are good agreement with earlier reports on ozone decomposition [36, 37]. It is 
concluded that RHA catalyst is an efficient catalyst for aqueous ozone decomposition. 
3.2. Adsorption and degradability. 
 Figure 5a&5b depicts oxalic acid decomposition and its TOC removal in various 
processes under identical experimental conditions. Adsorption experiments showed 
that about 2.7% of degradation in 60 minutes of contact time, which imply that 
adsorption process is not very effective process for oxalic acid removal from 
wastewater. Similar oxalic acid adsorption results were also reported in the literature 
in various metal oxide catalysts. Beltran et al., noted that 8% of oxalic acid removal is 
noted in adsorption process after 4 hrs treatment time on activated carbon catalyst in 
acidic medium [24]. Similarly, only 15%, 7% of adsorption removal is noted on 
TiO2/Al2O3, Fe2O3/Al2O3 catalysts respectively [30, 27]. Oxalic acid removal in 
ozonation process is also not very effective, only 14.3% of decomposition, 14% of 
TOC removal is noted at the end of 60 minutes of reaction time. The low removal rate 
in ozonation process is due to slow reaction of ozone with oxalic acid and the reported 
direct rate constant values (kD) were << 0.04 M-1S-1[38]. Ozone reacts with organic 
compounds in water directly through molecular or selective reactions with specific 
functional groups (double bond).Since oxalic acid does not possess any specific 
functional groups in its molecule, the direct reaction with ozone is very low. As we 
can see from the figure oxalic acid removal is effective in RHA catalyzed ozonation, 
about 76.2% of decomposition and 75% of TOC removal is observed. This 
enhancement of both removal rates is due to generation of hydroxyl radical by RHA 
catalyzed ozonation process which eventually increases the removal rate. The rate 
constant of hydroxyl radical with oxalic acid (k•OH) is 7.7× 106 M-1S-1[39], which is 
higher than kD values. Hence it would be expected more oxalic acid removal in the 
presence of hydroxyl radical.  The formation of hydroxyl radical in catalytic 
ozonation is also confirmed by using t-butanol as a radical scavenger. A separate 
experiment has been studied in the presence of 50 ppm of t-butanol and the results are 
also presented in figure 5a. Obviously, oxalic acid decomposition rate decreases in the 
presence of t-butanol due to scavenging of hydroxyl radical generated in the reaction. 
In conclusion, RHA is an effective catalyst in catalytic ozonation process for removal 
of oxalic acid in simulated wastewater.

3.5. Effect of inlet ozone gas concentration 
  Another important variable that influence catalytic ozonation process is the inlet 
ozone concentration.  The experiment has been studied at 200 ml/min of ozone gas 
flow rate and varying the ozone concentration from 40 to120 mg/l. Figure 7a & 7b 
depicts effect of inlet ozone concentration on oxalic acid decomposition and its TOC 
removal as a function of reaction time respectively. Obviously, inlet ozone 
concentration influenced both removal rates positively.  When the inlet 
concentration increases from 40 to 120 mg/l, oxalic acid decomposition increases 
from 33.3% to 76.3% and TOC removal increases from 33% to 75% in 60 minutes. 
This enhancement of removal rate is due to the generation of more hydroxyl radical as 
well as increase the direct ozone decomposition when the ozone gas concentration 
increases. Similar results also reported by Beltran et al in TiO2 catalyzed oxalic acid 
decomposition and found that oxalic acid degradation increases when the inlet ozone 
gas concentration increases from 7 mg l-1 to 55 mg l-1[23]. However, the same author 
reported different results in iron type catalyst and noted that oxalic acid degradation 
increases when the inlet ozone gas concentration increases from 15 to 30 mg l-1 but a 
further increase up to 45 mgl-1 did not increase the removal rate [27].  Furthermore, 
these results imply that the influence of inlet ozone gas concentration is depends on 
other experimental condition used in catalytic process and nessacerry to optimize for 
energy saving.  
 
3.6. Effect of pH 
The pH of solution may influence the surface properties of catalyst, charge of 
the pollutants and generation of free radicals. Hence, the study of pH influence on 
catalytic ozonation process would help to understand the mechanism of the process as 
well as for higher degree of removal.  In all catalytic ozonation process using RHA 
has been studied at pH 3, without using any buffer or mineral acids for pH adjustment. 
However, for neutral and basic medium experiments have been studied using K2HPO4 
and Na2B4O7 buffer respectively. The oxalic acid degradation experiments were 
studied at pH 3,7,10 in ozonation as well as in catalytic ozonation process and the 
decomposition, TOC removal results are shown in figure 9a & 9b respectively. 
Catalytic ozonation process is more efficient than ozonation process in all these three 
pHs. Obviously, catalytic ozonation process is more pronounced at pH3 than at pH 
7.10. About 62.9%, 18.5%, 14.8% of decomposition, 63.6%, 20%, 13.9% of TOC 
removal enhancement is observed at pH 3,7,10 in catalytic ozonation process over 
ozonation process. In ozonation process, oxalic acid degradation and TOC removal 
rate is increased when the solution pH increases from acidic to alkaline medium due 
to generation of free radicals at neutral and alkaline medium hence more 
decomposition is noted at higher pH.  It is concluded that catalytic ozonation at pH 3 
is most efficient for oxalic acid removal.
