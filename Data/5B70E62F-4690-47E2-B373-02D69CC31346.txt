Abstract
Acrylic bone cement is used as a grouting agent to anchor an artificial joint in total joint
arthroplasty. Bone cement typically consists of powder (pre-polymerized polymethyl methacrylate or
co-polymer, initiator) and liquid (methyl methacrylate monomer) portions. When the two
components are mixed in a dough state, free radical polymerization occurs instantly and a solid
hardened material forms after several minutes. From our previous study, acrylic bone cement
reinforced with carbon nanotubes provides excellent mechanical properties such as toughness, tensile
and compressive strength. In this paper, we measured wear behaviors of our novel acrylic bone
cement reinforced with carbon nanotubes. In order to obtain dynamic tribological behaviors of bone
cement, we used a reciprocating friction tester with a measuring system. A reciprocating friction
tester was able to precisely record friction change instantaneously and provides the information of
the relation between sliding distance and friction coefficient.
Key word: Acrylic bone cement, methyl methacrylate monomer, carbon nanotubes, reciprocating
friction tester

技術說明
(中文) 丙烯酸骨水泥是用來作為灌漿劑，用來固定人工關節與骨頭的填充材。通常由骨
水泥粉（預聚合的聚甲基丙烯酸甲酯共聚物、起始劑）和液體部分（甲基丙烯酸
甲酯單體）所組成。當這兩種成分混合，自由基聚合發生，幾分鐘後即硬化成堅
硬的物質形態。從我們以往的研究中，用奈米碳管強化的丙烯酸骨水泥提供了優
良的機械性能，如韌性，抗拉和抗壓強度。在本文中，我們主要探討以奈米碳管
補強丙烯酸骨水泥的磨耗性質研究。為了獲得骨水泥的動態摩擦學行為，我們採
用往復摩擦試驗機測量系統。因為往復摩擦測試儀能夠精確地記錄瞬間的摩擦變
化，滑動距離和摩擦係數之間的關係。
