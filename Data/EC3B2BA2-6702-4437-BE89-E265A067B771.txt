三、具體成果 
(一) 結構最佳化之修正 
    修正後模型之二維能帶與電位圖如圖一
與圖二所示。與舊模型之二維能帶與電位圖結
果相似。相對於傳統 HEMT，MOS-HEMT 不
同之處在於其閘極橫向電位VGB比傳統HEMT
較小，主要因為MOS-HEMT於閘極與蕭基層
之間，還有一層氧化層緣故，因此從能帶圖與
Poisson’s equation所推導出之 VGB才會比傳統
HEMT小。圖三與圖四為修正後模型之二維橫
向與縱向電場圖。 
圖五為修正後模型之通道層之橫向電位
圖。在低汲極偏壓下，通道層電位隨著汲極偏
壓增大而增大，這與舊模型之結果相同。在高
汲極偏壓下，當汲極偏壓加到 2 V時，其通道
層內電位幾乎與外加汲極偏壓 1.5 V時重疊；
表示MOS-HEMT元件在汲極偏壓外加至 2 V
時，已經趨近飽和遷移速度。通道之橫向電場
如圖六所示。在低汲極偏壓下，其通道層之橫
向電場並不因外加汲極偏壓增加而明顯增
加，主要是因為多了一層閘極氧化層緣故。在
此與舊模型之模擬結果並不相同，所以修正後
之模型更能觀察細微部份之變化情形。當外加
高汲極偏壓時，其汲極端之橫向電場便隨著汲
極偏壓而增大，當汲極偏壓外加至 2 V時，其
與閘極接觸之橫向電場與外加汲極偏壓 1.5 V
之橫向電場相互重疊，僅在未與閘極接觸處有
增大之趨勢，這意味汲極偏壓加至 2 V時，遷
移速度已趨近飽和，這與橫向電位觀察到之狀
況相同。圖七為低偏壓下通道層二維電子雲濃
度分佈。在低偏壓下，二維電子雲濃度隨著汲
極偏壓增大而增加。 
    本年度根據所模擬結果修正元件結構以
符合增強型金氧半高電子移動率電晶體之要
求。即除了蕭基層厚度外，摻雜濃度與預留之
欲氧化厚度皆是要考量的重要參數。根據修正
後的元件結構，吾人已可以準備在 GaAs, 
AlGaAs, InGaP, InGaAs, InAs等材料上研製增
強型或擬增強型金氧半高電子移動率電晶體。 
 
(二) 增強型金氧半高電子移動率電晶體 
LPCEO 氧 化 法 可 應 用 於 增 強 型
InGaP/InGaAs MOS-HEMT之閘極氧化層製作
上。圖八(a)為超薄液相氧化層之 TEM影像，
厚度約為 5奈米。藉由本氧化法的作用，可將
空 乏 型 之 HEMT 轉 變 成 增 強 型 之
MOS-HEMT。除此之外，最大閘極可忍受之
偏壓可到達 3 V 以上，其轉導特性如圖八(b)
所示。除了上述優點，還包括較高的崩潰電
壓、較小的閘極漏電流(如圖八(c)所示)、較小
的次臨界擺幅與改善的電流開/關比率。由目
前的實驗結果發現，此低溫液相氧化法對於在
高速與低雜訊元件的應用上極具潛力。目前元
件之汲極電流特性、雜訊與高頻效能仍持續改
進中。

AlGaAs/InGaAs Pseudomorphic High-Electron- 
Mobility Transistor with a Liquid Phase  
Deposited SiO2 as Gate Dielectric 
 
Kuan-Wei Leea,*, Hsien-Cheng Linb, Fang-Ming Leeb, and  
Yeong-Her Wangb 
a Department of Electronic Engineering, 
I-Shou University, Kaohsiung, Taiwan 
b Institute of Microelectronics,  
Department of Electrical Engineering,  
Advanced Optoelectronic Technology Center, 
National Cheng-Kung University, Tainan, Taiwan 
*E-mail: kwlee.isu.edu.tw 
 
InGaAs-channel transistors, such as pseudomorphic 
high-electronic-mobility transistors (PHEMTs) are 
attractive for achieving enhanced electron mobility. 
Recent progress in AlGaAs/InGaAs PHEMTs with a low 
Al-content AlGaAs Schottky layer grown on top of an 
InGaAs channel has shown promising features and 
performance. However, in spite of these excellent results, 
the Schottky barrier devices may still suffer from gate 
voltage swing, breakdown voltage, and gate leakage 
current issues. Insulators are the common choices grown 
on the III-V compound semiconductor to fabricate the 
metal-oxide-semiconductor (MOS) gate with a larger gate 
swing voltage and lower leakage current.  
The silicon dioxide (SiO2) is one of the commonly 
used dielectrics for III-V semiconductor device.1 There 
are many methods have been successfully deposit SiO2 
films such as low pressure chemical vapor deposition 
(LPCVD),2 plasma-enhanced chemical vapor deposition 
(PECVD),3,4 electron cyclotron resonance chemical vapor 
deposition (ECR-CVD),5 sputtering,6 rapid thermal 
oxidation (RTO),7 and liquid phase deposition (LPD).8,9 
Compared with other methods, the LPD process has many 
advantages which include low cost, low temperature, and 
selective deposition. Additionally, neither anodic 
equipment nor an assisting energy source is needed in the 
LPD system. Our previous works have demonstrated 
LPD-SiO2 on Si,10 GaAs,11 GaN,12 and AlGaAs.13  
The schematic cross section of the AlGaAs/InGaAs 
PHEMT structure is shown in Fig. 1. For the 
AlGaAs/InGaAs MOS-PHEMT, the GaAs capping layer 
was etched to expose the AlGaAs Schottky layer, and 
then the LPD-SiO2 was applied in the gate region. Finally, 
the 1 100 m2 gate MOS-PHEMT having a gate oxide 
thickness of 60 nm was fabricated by conventional 
photolithography. Fig. 2 shows the drain current density 
versus the drain-to-source voltage of the studied device. 
The inset shows the forward gate leakage current density. 
Base on the measurement results, the gate swing voltage 
is increased, the breakdown voltage is increased, and the 
leakage current is improved for the AlGaAs/InGaAs 
MOS-PHEMT as compared to those of Schottky-gate 
PHEMT, which will be beneficial to the power 
application.
