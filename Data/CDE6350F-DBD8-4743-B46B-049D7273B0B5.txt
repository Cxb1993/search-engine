中文摘要 
首先利用硝酸純化處理過後的奈米奈管，經由微波電漿輔助化學氣相沉積系統所產生
之氧電漿或氟電將表面處理過後，將其運用於酒精氣體感測系統上。經表面處理過後之
奈米碳管有著在室溫下 50-500ppm 的感測能力。且隨著不同時間的氧和氟電漿處理，能
產生不同的感測敏銳度。也進一步證實利用微波電漿輔助化學氣相沉積系統能有效改質
奈米碳管，且運用於氣體感測系統，未來更能有效運用於以碳主體之衍生性應用產品上。

2.2. Characterizations
The morphologies of the plasma modiﬁed MWCNT ﬁlms were
characterized by ﬁeld emission scanning electron microscope
(FESEM, JEOL JSM-6500F). The Raman spectra of the plasma modi-
ﬁedMWCNTswere characterized byRaman spectrometer (HORIBA
Jobin Yvon LabRAM HR800 UV) with a 632.8nm (1.96 eV) He–Ne
laser. The elemental composition change and relevant chemi-
cal bonding of the MWCNTs during the plasma treatments were
investigated by X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS, ULVAC-
PHI PHI Quantera SXM) with a hemispherical capacitor analyzer.
A monochromatic Al K line (h =1486.6 eV) was used as the pho-
ton source, and photoelectrons were collected at an angle of 45◦
relative to the sample surface. The energy resolution of system
(source + analyzer) was 0.49 eV and the elemental detection limit
was 0.1 at.%. In the spectrum analysis, the background signal was
subtracted by Shirley’smethod. Before XPSmeasurements, all sam-
ples were presputtered by argon ion beam in order to remove
surface contamination (C and O) and acquire true elemental com-
position.
2.3. Gas-sensing measurements
The alumina substrate (5mm×5mm) with MWCNTs coating
were painted with silver paste as silver ﬁlm electrodes [23]. The
two electrodes are both 1mm×5mm on the edges of the alumina
substrate. Silver wires were used to contact the silver electrodes
and the electrochemical workstation (JIEHAN ECW-5000) which
can be used to detect a very low current (∼10−12 A).
During the gas-sensing test, the resistance (or current) response
with a ﬁxed bias of 2V was measured with three ethanol con-
centrations. The test ethanol concentration (50–500ppm) was
obtained by volatile ethanol and subsequently injected into the
chamber. After reaching a steady current response, the chamber
was purged with air and the experiment was repeated for the
next cycle. All sensing experiments were done at 25±0.5 ◦C, and
humidity was kept at 60±5% RH. The sensor response (S) was
deﬁned as S=Rgas/Rair, where Rgas and Rair were the resistances
in an environment containing ethanol vapor and in air ambient,
respectively.

MWCNT-based sensors are still long [23].
