碳酸二苯酯製成之相平衡與離子液體破共沸研究 
 
洪錫勳 
義守大學生物技術與化學工程系 
國科會編號：NSC 99-2221-E-214-062 
 
 
摘要 
碳酸二苯酯(diphenyl carbonate，DPC)為一個重要
的化工中間體，低毒性且無汙染，其後續反應可以進
行鹵化、硝化、水解、氨解等反應，以製備許多有機
化合物與高分子材料，最近幾年發現，透過熔融酯交
換反應，取代光氣(phosgene)，DPC可與雙酚A(biphenol 
A)反應可以合成聚碳酸酯(polycarbonate) 而備受注目
，使得 DPC 的生產成為一個極有潛力值得開發的製程
，本研究探討酸甲酯、碳酸二甲酯、乙酸苯酯，兩兩
之間的汽液相平衡與甲醇跟碳酸二甲酯汽液相平衡，
及用離子液體[Bmim]BF4來破除共沸現象。 
關鍵詞：離子熔液、汽液相平衡，碳酸二苯酯 
 
Abstract 
Diphenyl carbonate (diphenyl carbonate, DPC) is 
an important chemical intermediate, with low toxicity and 
non-polluting. DPC can undergo a wide range of 
reactions, such as halogenation, nitration, hydrolysis and 
aminolysis reactions, to form many organic compounds or 
synthetic polymers. Recently, it was found that, in 
polycarbonate production, DPC can be used, instead of 
phosgene, to react with bisphenol A by melt 
transesterification to produce polycarbonates. This makes 
DPC become an economic and environmental potential 
product worth in industrial applications. Therefore, we 
select three major ingredients in DPC process, methyl 
acetate, dimethyl carbonate and phenyl acetate, to study 
the binary vapor-liquid equilibrium system. Their NRTL 
parameters for the VLE information are reported as well. 
The ionic liquid, B[mim]BF4, was also added to the 
binary mixture containing dimethyl carbonate and 
methanol to change the azeotropic phase behavior. 
Keywords: ionic liquid, VLE, Diphenyl carbonate  
 
前言 
由日本 Asahi kasei 公司與義大利之 EniChem 公
司開發出以DPC與雙酚A作為原料來合成聚碳酸酯之
後，此技術相較於以光氣與雙酚 A 合成聚碳酸酯對於
環境破壞之影響減低甚多，又工業上對於 DPC 之需求
量大增，使碳酸二苯酯之研究與開發成為發展重點。
碳酸二苯酯之合成主要分為光氣法與非光氣法，光氣
法為目前工業界使用最多之方法，此方法為使用光氣
與酚做為原料合成 DPC。但是光氣法由於光氣具有劇
毒與強腐蝕性，使得此法不利於環境生態，目前正在
逐步被淘汰。非光氣法主要包括氧化羰基化法[1]與酯
交換法[2]兩種方法合成碳酸二苯酯，其中氧化羰基化
法是以酚、一氧化碳、氧氣作為原料，在貴金屬觸媒
的作用之下直接合成碳酸二苯酯，雖然此法具有無汙
染性且不需要溶劑即可合成碳酸二苯酯之優點，但因
觸媒價格高且活性低，且反應生成的水會使碳酸二苯
酯發生水解之問題，導致此方法侷限於實驗室規模，
離工業的應用上仍不足。至於以酯交換法合成 DPC 有
三種途徑: 
1. 酚與碳酸二甲酯轉酯化方法: 
這一個方法是由無毒的碳酸二甲酯來代替劇毒
的光氣與酚進行反應，碳酸二甲酯與酚的總反應式如
(1-1)所示 
2Phenol+DMC <=> DPC+2Methanol  (1-1) 
2. 酚與草酸二甲酯(Dimethyl oxalate，DMO)轉酯化
方法: 
由酚與草酸二甲酯轉酯化合成草酸二苯酯(Diphenyl 
oxalate，DPO)，此總反應式如(1-2)所示 
2Phenol + DMO <=> DPO +2Methanol  (1-2) 
其中草酸二苯酯(DPO)需要再脫羰基才會得到碳
酸二苯酯 
3. 乙酸苯酯(Phenyl acetate，PA)與碳酸二甲酯轉酯
化方法:

 requirements. A building integrated photovoltaics (BIPV) system integrates photovoltaic modules 
into the building envelope, such as the roof or the façade [2]. By simultaneously serving as building 
envelope material and a power generator, a BIPV system can provide savings in materials and 
electricity costs, reduce the use of fossil fuels and emissions of greenhouse gases (GHG), and add 
architectural interest to the building. In addition, the shaded face of a BIPV system can also be used 
as shade from the sun, reducing the absorption of heat by the building and thus, decreasing the room 
temperatures. In this way, energy can be saved.  
However, the conventional BIPV modules with a high investing cost for construction, complex 
mounting, and a lack of design variations are obstacles to the marketing of BIPV system. Recently, 
the research objectives for PV modules in building design have primarily focused on increasing the 
power generation efficiency and decreasing the cost. In this study, we introduce a new type of PV 
module with high visible light transmittance integrated with building material. The aim of this paper 
is to demonstrate that the proposed high color rendering index (HCRI) BIPV module with excellent 
thermal and optical properties can be produced at a low cost using simple deposition methods 
2. Experiments 
2.1 Sample preparation 
The high color rendering index (HCRI) PV module designed in this study is one kind of the a-Si 
module, which is the main material to be used to replace the window side. The a-Si solar cell was 
laminated between two glasses, which has 30% visible light transmittance that can easy to lead into 
the daily light from sustainable resources (see Fig. 1). The power efficiency and electrical 
performance of HCRI-BIPV module based on IEC 61215 test standard [3] are shown in Table 1. 
For the production of HCRI-BIPV module, we have built a six-steps procedure of the line system 
regulation, as shown in Fig. 2. The first step is coating the transparent conductive oxides film (TCO) 
on the clean glass. The second step is scribing the TCO film by laser. The third step is using the 
PECVD method to deposit the a-Si film. The fourth step is scribing the a-Si film by laser. The fifth 
step is depositing the metal film. The sixth step is scribing the metal film by laser to become a 
semi-finished product. Finally, HCRI module is completed as a semi-finished solar battery is 
laminated with the other TCO glass by EVA or PVB.

 Saving energy, reducing installed cost and increasing the daylight usage through the design of 
BIPV system are significant concerns for building design. In this work, the method of PECVD 
(Plasma-enhanced chemical vapor deposition) can be applied to produce satisfactory high color 
rendering index (HCRI) BIPV module of good quality with visible light transmittance as high as 
26.9%, solar radiation absorptance below 60% and shading coefficient equal to 54%. On the other 
hand, the experimental results by a small-sized hot-box test with a solar simulator lamp indicated 
that the surface temperature of HCRI-BIPV module was higher than that on the surface of Si-based 
BIPV and HCRI-BIPV module prolonged the propagation of heat into building. This can have 
important practical implications on the fabrication of low-cost, high visible light transmittance of 
BIPV module. The efficiency of this proposed HCRI BIPV module is only 4.2%, the challenge for 
improving the power efficiency is certainly worth further work.
