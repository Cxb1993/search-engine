and spend too muchtime that makesit even more 
difficultto maintainquality. 
In this study, try to use 6σ concept and DMAIC steps 
into the problem analysis and    solving to improve 
flow and combine with statistical techniques (DOE, 
SPC, MSA, Process Capability Analysis, ⋯etc). The 
actions are including, to collect the data of defect 
rate in the process, to find out the key factors, to 
minimize the variations of the process and to compare 
the different outputs between original and improved. 
The major contribution of this study is to apply an 
efficient improving cycles which will obtain accurate 
results, establish optimum process conditions and re-
fine standard operation procedures (SOP), aligned 
with the organizational strategies. This study uses a 
raw material manufacturer in the semiconductor 
industry as a case study to prove the overall 
strategy with methodologies＇ applicability and 
suitability.

人才培育成果說明： 
此計畫總共訓練本校三位碩士研究生及合作廠商的業務主管、作業生產主
管及進出貨主管，學會運用六標準差 DMAIC 步驟與統計技術，而來建立製程
條件最佳化。 
 
技術研發成果說明： 
運用六標準差 DMAIC 步驟與統計技術建立製程條件最佳化，確實能提昇
整體企業經營績效。 
 
技術特點說明：  
結合 DMAIC步驟與實驗設計（DOE）改善良品率的過程，這種方法能有助於
對明確因子以及它們交互作用的影響力。結果也表明了 Resin (wax content)和
Silane (kneading RPM)比其他因子對良率改善有較大的影響，必須以管制圖對其
最適化允差進行持續監控。 
 
可利用之產業及可開發之產品： 
就其他製造業而言，若研究的成本高居不降，則需最有效地進行實驗，亦
必須使用科學方法來設計。本研究中有系統的應用實驗設計的步驟，獲得最佳
之控制因子，使不良率將可以獲得明顯改善，能從而檢討實驗設計的規劃，本
研究是可作為借鏡。
