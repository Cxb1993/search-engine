預熱處理對氧化鋅溶凝膠薄膜微結構及光學特性之影響 
Effect of preheating treatment on the microstructure and optical properties of 
ZnO sol-gel derived thin films 
 
計畫編號：NSC 95-2221-E-035-006 
執行期限：95 年8 月1 日至96 年7 月31 日 
主持人：蔡健益 逢甲大學 材料科學與工程學系 
計畫參與人員：范凱雄、王敏吉、陳錦誠、黃冠維 
 
摘要 
寬能隙金屬氧化物薄膜在可見光頻段具有高透光性且可藉由金屬離子的摻雜來調整載子濃
度或熱處理製程參數的最佳化來增進其結晶特性及光穿透特性，已廣泛應用在綠色能源及
光電領域；其中氧化鋅材料因價廉且薄膜的成膜特性佳，近年來被導入應用於太陽電池及
平面顯示器等具產值高成長性產業。本研究係以溶膠凝膠技術搭配旋鍍法製備具高透光性
之氧化鋅薄膜，實驗中將合成氧化鋅溶膠過程中最被普遍使用的醇類溶劑---乙二醇甲醚以
乙醇取代，避免將製程導入量產工廠時造成公安方面的疑慮與困擾。實驗結果顯示藉由熱
處理程序的調整可改善氧化鋅薄膜的平整度，並且將氧化鋅薄膜的光穿透率由60％大幅提
昇至92％。 
關鍵詞：金屬氧化物材料、氧化鋅薄膜、溶膠凝膠法 
 
Abstract 
Wide bandgap (> 3 eV) transparent oxides have been used in green energy and optical-electrical 
applications. Among these materials, ZnO is one of the most promising candidates for the novel 
device applications since it possesses high transparency and has a widely range conductivity from 
metallic to insulating. In this study, transparent ZnO thin films were deposited onto glass 
substrates by sol-gel method and crystallinity, microstructural and optical properties affected by 
preheating treatment conditions were investigated. 
Keywords: Metal oxide materials, ZnO thin films, Sol-gel method 
 
(1) 前言 
氧化鋅(ZnO)為 II-VI 族之化合物半導體材料，具纖鋅礦(wurtzite)型六方體結構(a=3.25 
Å，c=5.12 Å)，在室溫下的能隙約為 3.35 eV。因其價格低廉、化學穩定性高且具有生物相
容性(Biocompatible)，早已被廣泛使用在變阻器[1]、微感測器、表面聲波元件[2]、透明導
電薄膜(掺雜硼、鋁、鎵及銦等元素)[3]；以及太陽電池(視窗層)[4]、平面顯示器(薄膜電晶
體陣列)[5]等產值高成長性產業或雷達波吸收等新型用途，成為最有多領域應用潛力的金屬
氧化物材料之一。由於氧化鋅薄膜易形成氧空缺與鋅間隙原子(Zn interstitials)，這些點缺陷
在氧化鋅能帶中引入施體能階(Donor level)，使氧化鋅薄膜呈 n 型半導體特性[6]。另外，因
氧化鋅為寬能隙材料故在可見光範圍具有高透光性且在可見光的照射下並不會影響其導電
特性。若能應用於透明電子元件[7]中更能拓展在可攜式與穿戴式電子產品中新穎性與多樣
性之應用。

(3) 結果與討論 
溶膠-凝膠法是以無機聚合反應為基礎，由金屬醇氧化物或無機金屬鹽類為前驅物來
源，以水為水解劑、醇為溶劑來製備無機材料溶液。溶膠-凝膠製程有兩個主要步驟: 水解
與縮合，這兩個步驟和溫度、前驅物、氣氛、溶劑等參數有相當的關係，而網狀聚合體的
結構、形態則取決於水解、聚縮反應的結果。實驗中是將溶膠均勻塗佈於玻璃基材上，當
溶膠經適當乾燥後，得到一層氧化物的凝膠薄膜之後進行退火及薄膜特性檢測與分析。

段，第一階段為低溫溶劑的蒸(揮)發，第二、第三階段則為水的蒸(揮)發及有機高分子成份
的裂解與燒除。以乙二醇甲醚(EGME)配製之溶膠其低溫溶劑的蒸(揮)發速率最快，但其因
沸點較乙醇(Ethanol)為高，故需於較高溫度才能揮發完全。兩種溶劑所配製之溶膠在經

配製之凝膠在接近 400℃才有明顯放熱現象發生。

substrates by sol-gel method and crystallinity, microstructural and

Abstract 
ZnO thin-film transistors (TFTs) have attracted considerable R&D interest due to their high 
transparency and low photosensitivity compared with typical a-Si:H TFTs. The electrical 
characteristics of ZnO thin films may be controlled by doping with ternary element, for 
instance Al, Ga, In, Mg, etc. In this study, Zn1-xMgxO (x = 0 to 0.36) thin films were deposited 
on glass substrates by spin coating. The as-deposited films were baked at 300 oC for 10 
minutes and then annealed at 500 oC for 1 hr. The results show that, addition of Mg-species in 
ZnO films markedly enhanced the uniformity of film thickness and improved optical 
properties. The Zn0.8Mg0.2O film exhibited the best transparency of 92%, an increase of ~15% 
over a pure ZnO film, and the rms roughness value decreased to 1.63. The Zn1-xMgxO TFTs 
were demonstrated to have n type enhancement behavior. The optimum device with 
Zn0.8Mg0.2O channel layer had a mobility of 0.1 cm2/Vs, a threshold voltage of 6.0 V, and an 
on/off ratio more than 107.  
Keywords: Zn1-xMgxO films, Sol-gel method, Thin film transistors

2. Experimental 
Zinc acetate dehydrate (Zn(CH3COO)2．2H2O)  and magnesium acetate tetra-hydrate 
(Mg(CH3COO)2．4H2O) were dissolved in 2-methoxyethanol, and then monoethanolamine 
(MEA) was added to the solution. The concentration of metal ions in Zn1-xMgxO sols were 
controlled at 0.75M and Mg+2 was varied from 0 to 0.36 (for x values). The solution was 
stirred for 2 hr at 60 oC until a clear and homogenous sol was obtained. The Zn1-xMgxO thin 
film was deposited on alkali-free glass (Corning 1737) using the spin coating method, using 
3000 rpm for 20 s. These as-coated films were dried at 300 oC for 10 minutes, and then 
annealed at 500 oC for 1 hr in air. 
Thermal analysis of the Zn1-xMgxO sols used TG-DSC (TA Instrument, SDT 2960) to 
identify the evaporation, decomposition and crystallization points of the Zn1-xMgxO solution 
material. The crystal structure and microstructure of these thin films were examined using 
X-ray diffractometry (XRD, MAC Science MAXP3) and scanning electron microscopy (SEM, 
HITACHI S-4800), respectively. Surface morphology of the Zn1-xMgxO thin films was 
observed by scanning probe microscope (SPM, Digital Instrument NS4/D3100CL). Optical 
transmittance spectra in the visible range of these films were examined by a 
spectrophotometer (Mini-D2T, Ocean Optics, Inc., USA). 
The typical bottom-gate structure device of Zn1-xMgxO TFTs was fabricated by a hybrid 
method that combined the standard micro-electrical fabricated process and sol-gel method.

our research.

4. Conclusions 
Preparation and characterization of thin film transistors with Mg-doped ZnO films for 
the active channel layer were investigated. The results show that Mg+2 doped ZnO films of the 
form Zn1-xMgxO had markedly improved surface texture (reduced roughness), optical 
properties and finer microstructure than ZnO films. The Zn0.8Mg0.2O film exhibited the best 
transparency at 92%, increasing by 15% value for the un-doped ZnO film (80%), and its rms 
roughness value decreased to 1.63. When the Mg content exceeded 0.3 (x value) MgO phase 
segregation would occur. This impurity phase caused the film quality to degenerate. In this 
study, the Zn1-xMgxO TFTs were fabricated using the sol-gel method and exhibited n-type 
enhancement mode. The optimum device with Zn0.8Mg0.2O channel layer has a mobility of 0.1 
cm2/Vs, a threshold voltage of 6.0 V, and an on/off ratio more than 107.
