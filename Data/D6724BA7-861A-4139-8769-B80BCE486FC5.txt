的計算出結果。 
 
 
 
 
技術說明 
The present study employed Monod equation, including substrate 
inhibition, to simulate ethanol production using glucose as carbon 
source. The initial glucose concentration was 150, 250, 350 g/L. The 
parameters were estimated by trial & error. The experimental results 
for initial glucose concentration of 150 and 250 g/L could be well 
simulated. We also used a non-theoretical model to simulate the same 
experimental data. The glucose consumption, ethanol production, and 
cell growth were simulated very well by the non-theoretical model for 
the initial glucose concentration of 150, 250, 350 g/L.

Abstract 
The present study employed Monod 
equation, including substrate inhibition, 
to simulate ethanol production using 
glucose as carbon source. The initial 
glucose concentration was 150, 250, 350 
g/L. The parameters were estimated by 
trial & error. The experimental results 
for initial glucose concentration of 150 
and 250 g/L could be well simulated. We 
also used a non-theoretical model to 
simulate the same experimental data. 
The glucose consumption, ethanol 
production, and cell growth were 
simulated very well by the 
non-theoretical model for the initial 
glucose concentration of 150, 250, 350 
g/L. 
Keywords: glucose, high concentration 
ethanol fermentation, kinetic parameters, 
mathematical modeling.

4.2 實驗數據與數據回歸-非理論模
式 
圖一~圖三為厭氧發酵之實驗數
據與數據回歸之圖形，起始之葡萄糖
濃度分別為 150, 250, 350 g/L，點為
實驗數據，線為回歸之圖形，從結果
可知數據的回歸結果相當良好。厭氧
發酵之酵母菌細胞的生長較差，所需
發酵的時間比較長，在葡萄糖起始濃
度高達 350g/L 時，實驗點的數據呈現
不穩定狀態。 
圖四~圖六為利用通氣控制ORP在
-155~160mv 之間的發酵之實驗數據與
數據回歸之圖形，起始之葡萄糖濃度
分別為 150, 250, 350 g/L，點為實驗
數據，線為回歸之圖形，從結果可知
數據的回歸結果相當良好。 
非理論模型每一條曲線使用三個
參數，由於模型適用有反曲點的線
型，使用在發酵之葡萄糖代謝、細胞
生長、酒精生產的數據模擬本就非常
適合。因為細胞的生長在對數生長前
期（logarithmic growth phase）為
營養富足，生長生長不受限制，在對
數生長後期，營養開始匱乏，細胞生
長陷入趨緩，兩時期之間的細胞比生
長速率（specific growth phase）便
有反曲點。其它如葡萄糖代謝，酒精
生產也有類似的反曲點。雖然非理論
模型並沒有理論基礎，但對於實際工
廠日常操作，卻也是能用預測工具。
