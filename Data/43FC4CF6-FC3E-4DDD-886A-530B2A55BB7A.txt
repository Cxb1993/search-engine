研究摘要(500 字以內)： 
 
為了減小元件尺寸，從而達到整體提升應用元件性能之目的，本計畫依
由一個絕緣勢壘和兩個二維量子井組成之 RTD，其中絕緣勢壘位於兩個量子
井之間。為使元件正常工作，量子井和勢壘障壁厚度都很小。因為勢井厚度
很小，勢井可看成是二維的，電子運動除被限制在井平面內外，亦由於量子
力學隧道效應，電子可從上量子井(Top quantum well)隧道穿過勢壘層到達下
量子井(Bottom quantum well)。其中 NDR 之斜率大小對通訊光學元件之量
產與光學性質為一相當重要的因素。尤其 p-n 接面是太陽能電池 EHP 結合
使能階改變的位置，透過 p-n 接面的結構設計改變可提升轉換效率
(conversion efficiency)，若在 p-n 接面上建立多重量子井則可增加光能轉換
成電能之比例。最後依上述階段最佳製程條件，除將 RTD 整合於 SC 應用元
件以提昇整體發光效率外，並冀望充分應用於其他太空電力、獨立電源、民
生用消費性產品…等光電系統。由於此整合技術有極大的發展性，故具前瞻
性之研發動機。

1、 Introduction: 
In recent years, resonant tunneling diodes (RTDs) have attracted a great deal 
of research attention, such as analog/digital (A/D) converters, microwave 
oscillators, multi-state memories, switching devices and frequency divider, which 
characteristics have been developed and simulated. [1]. 
In this paper, we propose a parameter extraction method for parallel RTD's 
DC characteristics [2].The results are compared to experimental data on the 
extracted different parameter. Negative differential rectifying (NDR) effects are 
directly observed in the current/volage (I-V) characteristics, while corresponding 
conductance peaks are also identified at the same voltage positions in the 
conductance–voltage characteristics. In this paper, we demonstrate a room 
temperature deposition quantum well structure in silicon material. In the past, the 
material of NDR is almost selected in GaAs, but this paper thus provides a good 
basis for the observation of resonant tunneling and NDR effects in the silicon 
material. 
The Al/SiO2/nc-Si/SiO2/n-Si/SiO2/Al DWRTID structure is fabricated on the 
substrates of n-type crystalline silicon (100) using DC sputter deposition system at 
the growth temperature of 400°C, and therefore the deposition layers on silicon 
substrate is about 1um thickness. However, the I-V characteristics of 
Al/SiO2/Si/SiO2/n-Si DWRTID structures would be affected by the ohmic contact of 
metallization electrodes.

4、 Conclusion:
