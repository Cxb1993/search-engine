英 文 摘 要 ： This is a two years project 
First year: 
In this project, we will apply a gettering method 
which can improve the quality of a wafer。 
Two different wafers, CZ and multi-grain wafer will 
be chosen for comparison. This gettering 
method comprising two steps, first grow a thin 
strain-layer at the backside of the wafer at low 
temperature around 250℃ to 450℃, and annealed the 
wafer at 900~1100℃. The metal 
elements can be easily diffused to strain-layer and 
stay in the defects in the interface due to 
the strain generated by the strain-layer. The 
backside poly-silicon layer will be moved 
chemically or mechanically for further examination. 
In the mean time, the backside gettering 
wafer will be used for solar cell to investigate 
their gettering efficiency. During different 
thermal stages, the warpage of wafer (strain of 
wafer) and metal concentration profile will be 
detailed recorded for gettering mechanism study. 
Second year: 
In the second year, we will apply the extrinsic 
gettering technique for high impurity 
contamination wafer. Based on the results of first 
year, the higher annealing temperature and 
longer annealing time for be applied. The wafer 
surfaces will be intentionally contaminated 
with specific metal elements, such as, Ni, Fe or Cu 
with concentration of 1013-1014 cm-3 range. 
We expected to develop an economic process with a 
reasonable thermal budget to recycle 
used wafers collected from IC industry. In this 
project, we will also applied an oxide buffer 
layer beneath strained amorphous (or poly) layer to 
study gettering efficiency. The metal 
gettering process (high temperature) induced grain 
growth (size change) and strain change 
will be also investigated.

主持人：貢中元 學生:傅書豪、劉正淇   
單位：國立中興大學 電機工程學系 
E-mail：cykung@nchu.edu.tw  電話：(04)22850359 
 
計畫簡介 
利用利用外部去疵法提升晶圓品質技術並製
作異質接面型太陽電池;晶圓(wafer) 是製作光電元
件晶片，而在一般的加工過程中、加工的環境雜質
污染等製程，都會導致晶圓容易受到重金屬的汙
染，而這些存在晶圓內的摻雜元素(例如硼、砷、
磷)以及金屬雜質(例如鐵、鎳、鈷)，都會影響到半
導體光電元件，例如製成太陽能電池時的電特性。
而外部去疵則是在晶圓的其中一表面上形成一層
應力層，利用該應力層來補捉存在於晶圓內部的金
屬雜質。因此，外部去疵法將是一種非常有用的技
術，可以將矽基材內大部分的金屬污染物去除。本
計畫首先以鋁當做去疵效果及去疵的機制。使用的
晶片類型有兩種，一種為 P 型 Cz 太陽能等級的晶
片，未經蝕刻切片(As-cut)，電阻值為 1-100Ω-cm、
生命期約為 2µs、厚度為 220µm；一種為 N 型 Cz
晶圓、單面拋光、電阻值為 1-5Ω-cm、生命期為
20µs、厚度為 250µm。實驗中，晶片上鋁膜的厚度
有 0.3µm 及 1µm 二種。以不同的去疵熱處理溫
度、熱歷程研究不同狀態下的去疵效果。去疵效果
以量測少數載子生命期及載子遷移率為主, 霍爾
量測為輔。

結論 
    P 型單面鋁膜的試片在 800℃熱處理後有最好
的吸雜效果；N 型晶片單雙面鋁膜吸雜在 600℃有
最好效果，載子生命期較未處理的晶片提升十倍。
升降溫速率對載子生命週期有影響，以快進緩出的
效果最為顯著。
