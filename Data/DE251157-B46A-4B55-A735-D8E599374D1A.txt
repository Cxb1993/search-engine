2Abstract
This study fabricated micro PDMS (Polydimethyl Siloxane) PEMFC (Proton Exchange Membrane
Fuel Cell) by using the MEMS (Micro Electro Mechanical Systems) technology. The material used
is Polydimethylsiloxane; PDMS), which can improve the fragile features caused by the traditional
used graphite bipolar plates. Such also has the excellent performance for light-weight, impact
resistance and good drainage. In the processes, the Si-based mother mold is made by using MEMS
technology, after that the PDMS flow field is micro molded from the Si-based mother mold. The
active area of the membrane-electrode-assembly (MEA) is 2cm ×2cm, the parameters include open
ratio of flow field plates, clamping force, current collector shape, respectively. According to
experiment results, this study can obtain the following conclusions:
(1) open ratio of flow field plates: 75%>67%>50%; (2) The performance of the micro fuel cell
increases with an increase of the circle numbers from one to twenty-five on the current collector
under the same current collector open ratio; (3)natural convection of 4-cell, the maximum power
density is 1.9 watts; (4) A torque of 2.0 Kgf‧cm is recommended in the PDMS PEMFC; (5) As the
curent density increases up to 1100mA, the fourth cel’s performance becomes the worst, and the 
temperature is the highest; (6) The micro PDMS fuel cell and cell stack can maintain a stable power
output for a long time use up to 14 hours; (7) The PDMS-based PEMFC has a better performance
than that of the Si-based; (8) PDMS is a better material than MEMS for micro fuel cell because it
has less water flooding effects and possesses a better performance.

2010 年，Yuhao Lu 等人[13]在微型燃料電池設計製作方面，Lu 和 Reddy 設計四種不同的型

300um，其反應氣體流量分別為 15、30 及 50sccm，在比較完不同流量後發現 50sccm 相較於
其他兩者有較好的性能表現。
電池堆的設計，尤其是微型燃料電池堆，為了達到高功率密度、高單位能量、高能量轉
換率與高實用性等目的，依舊需要突破兩項關鍵技術，分別為電池堆組裝技術與電池流道設
計，如此才能使得組裝後的燃料電池達到最佳性能。而除了目前主要的三種組裝方式[14]：(1)
雙極板（bipolar plate）；(2)準雙極板（pseudo-bipolar electrodes）；(3)單極電極（mono-polar
electrodes）之外，亦有許多研究單純嘗試創作其他新型態組裝方式。
2000 年 Manhattan Scientifics 公司以圓柱狀堆疊方式[15]設計出微型電池堆，此設計可減少電
池堆中的移動件，方便電池堆組裝，同時也減少了整體重量，但此設計加工不易。
2002 年 S.J. Lee 等人研究 flip-flop 組裝方式[16]，此種組裝方式將電池堆組裝在單一膜電極組
上，減化了薄膜的處理過程與集電板連接的設計，卻也同時增加了電池堆流道的困難度。
2004 年 R. Dillon 等人採用組合件(flat-pack)燃料電池組裝法[17]，此法雖能減少電池的材料消
耗與整體體積，但卻會使得歐姆極化增加。此六種組裝方式之比較列於表 2。
