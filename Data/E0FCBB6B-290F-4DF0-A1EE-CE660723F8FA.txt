2二、英文摘要
The disaster evacuation has been highly regarded and recognized by all nations over years, and
the first forty-eight hours considered as golden time for saving lives after the catastrophe occurred.
Through the observations from global major disasters such as the great earthquake of Osaka and Kobe
in Japan, the 921 Earthquake in Taiwan and tsunami in Southeast Asia, one can envision the magnitude
of unknowns and difficult accessibility to the disaster zone devastated by such natural cause. Therefore,
based on the actual demand of disaster evacuation, there are researches and development of rescue
robots studied by the researchers and experts all over the world. The design and operation
characteristics of current rescue robots, apparently, are more on overcoming the ground motion of
disastrous terrain with a single operating method, and thus its movements are greatly confined by the
geographical complexity of disaster zone. Therefore, the proposal of “A Reconfigurable Robot Design
for Complying with Rough Terrain” is not only for orientating to disaster zone motion, but also for
overcoming the diversities of complex geographical compositions. This proposal provides innovative
research development in a reconfigurable robot design for complying with rough terrain with three-year
action plans, each year, to materialize research progression of each significant module of rescue robot,
control technique, actual testing and function improvement. The descriptions of projected goal of each
annual action plan as follows accordingly:
Year 1: Complete a prototype single-body rescue robot. In this year, the project aims to build
hardware and software systems for a single-body rescue robot including mechanical
design and manufacture of rescue robot, sensing and control design of motion systems,
electrical and mechanical testing of rescue robot.
Year 2: Complete a prototype multi-body rescue robot. In this year, the project aims to build
hardware and software systems for a multi-body rescue robot including design and
fabrication of multiple interlinks, motion testing and analysis of cascaded robot,
electronics system testing and motion controller design of multi-body rescue robot.
Year 3: Complete a lean designed prototype multi-body rescue robot. The main objective of this
year will be focused on the results of on going research and analysis from the previous
two years. In this year, the project conducts revision and improvement of the
reconfigurable robot design for complying with rough terrain, including lean structure
design of rescue robot and fabrication, on-site terrain detection and related research of
controller parameter, disaster surroundings simulations and analysis.
The research result of this proposal, aside from the innovative rescue robot prototype, the proposition of
rescue robot design offers faster and diverse applications of rescue efforts in the disaster zone. Moreover,
the execution of this project will create opportunities for people involved to learn and to realize the
knowledge and skills of disaster rescue engineering and robotic engineering, which can enhance the
research quality and quantity of the domestic institute.
Keywords: Reconfigurable robot, Rescue robot, Robotic technology, Motion control technology.

3.4.1 重組式機器人機構設計
重組式機器人整體設計理念以履帶式傳動作為設計主軸，因為不論在砂石、泥地、草地等特殊地面，
履帶式傳動皆能正常行走，相對於輪型傳動還是具有相當的克服地面能力。並且在重組式機器人的關
節設計，是以抬舉方式以及轉向方式的兩個自由度作為關節的作動方式，抬舉的自由度可以達到地面
高低落差時的克服，轉向的自由度可以增加對地面的適應性。機器人主要機構設計概述如下：
• 雙驅動鏈輪設計：兩驅動輪的設計是採用同一鏈條作為驅動，並且前後驅動輪大小尺寸完全相同，
所以必須精確的保持前後驅動輪的轉速相等，以避免因為前後輪轉速不同而造成鏈條的擠壓或拉
扯而產生損壞。因此在單邊的傳動機構設計上，使用單顆傳動齒輪同時驅動雙鏈輪的設計，確保
兩驅動輪相同轉速。圖 1 所示為單邊驅動機構設計。
• 驅動馬達上下並列設計：為發揮最大的空間效益，設計將驅動左右邊履帶的馬達以上下並列的方
式排列，如此可有效的減少平台的寬度尺寸，由於寬度減少，所需的高度也相對降低，整體尺寸
就容易縮小，圖 2 所示就是雙邊驅動馬達的並列設計實體。
• 齒輪傳動的抬頭與旋轉機構設計：如圖 3 所示，抬頭機構及旋轉機構的設計都是將最終的輸出端
固定於大齒輪上，而且由馬達端輸出經過小齒輪傳動大齒輪的帶動方式，作為與下一節機器人的
連結方式。由於馬達連接到較大的齒輪，強度也較高，且可以配上載重較大的大軸承，因此有效
的減少傳動時的磨損，降低對控制所造成的影響。
• 抬頭機構設計：抬頭機構的旋轉軸與其中一驅動鏈輪同軸，因此抬頭機構旋轉時便不容易與平台
本體發生衝突，迴轉角度便可增大至與鏈輪接觸角相等的 180°。如圖 4 所示，由於本體內部寬度
相當於馬達減去輸出軸之長度，因此設計將馬達固定於可動部分且置於本體外部，如此便可不用
將動力垂直傳動或是增加本體寬度。將馬達固定在可動部分的另一好處是可動部分使用之傳動齒
輪為重量較輕之小齒輪。
單節機器人的整體機構如圖 5 及圖 6 所示，是由本體左右邊的鏈輪驅動機構及前端與後端的抬頭機構
與旋轉機構所組合而成。每一單節機器人的前端都安裝抬頭機構且後端也安裝旋轉機構，在抬頭機構
以及旋轉機構的末端平板上，設計可以將抬頭機構的平板與旋轉機構的平板作組合，藉由此處的結合
設計，將每一單節的機器人作串連，以抬頭機構及旋轉機構構成兩個自由度的關節。圖 7 所示為三節
機器人組合而成的重組式機器人整體，圖中可顯示出單節與單節之間的關節作動時的形態，藉由調整
機器人的姿態來達到克服地面不平或障礙之影響。

技術說明 （英文）
This invention provides a chain-typed mobile robot mechanism
comprising two caterpillar band modules, one rotating mechanism
module and one lifting mechanism module. Each caterpillar band
module has a chain with blocks; the rotating mechanism module is
pivoted between two caterpillar band modules and has a connector
forward the front of two caterpillar band modules. Two caterpillar
band modules, the rotating mechanism module and the lifting
mechanism module are driven by driving motors; besides, a
plurality of robot mechanisms can be connected serially by multi-
degree of freedom mechanisms, multi-degree of freedom actions
and chains with blocks. The present invention can achieve purposes
of simple mechanism, smaller volume and adaptation of any terrain
to widely rescue.

The moving part starts forward motions from the return

increments. In each experiment, the cyclic motions between
the return positions A and B are performed several times so as

infrared thermometer is employed to measure the temperature
around the moving table, and the temperature is controlled
within the range from 28 °C to 29 °C during all experiments.

torque value that actuates the tested feed drive
servomechanism, and motion time. The units of the acquired
position, velocity, and acceleration signals are encoder
increments, rad/s, and rad/s2, respectively; the unit of the
acquired torque value is Nm. Usually, a large number of data

and therefore, long experimentation is mandatory.

V. CONCLUSION
This paper presents identification and spectral analysis
methods for the static friction of a feed drive servomechanism
in CNC machine tools. The breakaway experiment was
conducted to obtain the data values for analyzing the static
friction of a tested feed drive servomechanism. The
experimental results demonstrate that the identified static
friction is fluctuant significantly depending on the angular
position of the motor shaft, and thus, the identified static
friction is clearly position-dependent. Therefore, the
simplified friction characteristics usually used in motion
control design to reduce adverse effects induced by static
friction limit the motion accuracy for feed drives of CNC
machine tools. The spectral analysis using an FFT algorithm is
thus applied to analyze the frequency characteristics of the
identified static friction. Then, the resulted FFT components
are further analyzed to demonstrate the origin of the static
friction. According to the spectral analysis results, a low-pass
filter is used to filter out the random friction components and
the filtered static friction is applied to develop a friction
compensation lookup table for compensating the static
friction. Several experiments and motion tests were carried
out on a three-axis CNC milling machine. The experimental
results of motion tests indicate that the friction-compensated
motion control system with the friction model obtained in this
study achieves a reduction of 36.94% (the best case) and
24.90% (the worse case) in the MAX values of tracking errors.
Moreover, the RMS values of tracking errors are reduced, and
the rate of reduction achieved is 40.44% (the best case) and
19.28% (the worse case) in the experiments. The experimental
results thus demonstrate the feasibility of the static friction
identification and analysis methods developed in this study for
application to motion control design of CNC machine tools.
