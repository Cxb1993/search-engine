2磁場分量，對於斜板上電導性流體的弱非線
性表面波，呈現不同的穩定效果，同時指出
在臨界雷諾數下二維波長之穩定區。
Baumgartl 等[11]以實驗方法來探討磁場作用
於電導性流體之效應，發現藉磁場可阻止不
穩定狀態發生。Renardy 等[12]也指出磁場具
延遲流動不穩定之效果。Tsai 等[13]及 Cheng
等[14-16]以微小參數法探討磁場作用下直立
平板凝結流、直立圓柱黏彈性、冪次律及擬
塑性薄膜流之穩定性，結果指出磁場效應可
增加流動之穩定性。Eldabe 等[17]指出磁場在
黏彈性普修葉流 (Poiseuille flow)具穩定作
用，在柯提流(Couette flow)則表現不穩定行
為。利用適當的外加磁場，所產生的磁性流
體力能導致流變特性改變，因此合宜的控制
磁場能增加系統的穩定性並得到最佳化情
況。且應用磁場來穩定性統最主要有兩大優
點。一是機械元件可以不直接和流體接觸；
二是在科學技術上有關控制方面，磁場的應
用是簡單且可行的。
對於工業界實際應用，如齒輪、軸承及
凸輪潤滑液等，於工作中具動態旋轉效應，
如旋轉軸承之潤滑系統及旋轉塗層[18]，其液
膜非線性穩定性分析及控制理論，在工業應
用上為一非常重要的課題。由於磁場、旋轉
效應及幾何外形在液動穩定性上扮演非常重
要角色，本計畫的重點在於藉由線性與非線
性穩定性之理論分析，以數值模擬結果，探
討磁場大小(漢特曼數)、旋轉參數(羅斯比數)
及圓柱曲率對流體薄膜系統的穩定機制有何
影響。

四、結論
1.漢特曼數 m 增加及羅斯比數減小時，流場
穩定區將隨之擴大，愈易表現穩定狀況。
2.亞臨界不穩定區中的臨界振幅隨著 m 值的
增加及值減小而增大。
3.超臨界穩定區中的平衡振幅隨著 m 值的增
加及值減小而減小。
4.不穩定波之無因次波速，隨外加磁場增加而
減小，但與圓柱半徑及旋轉速度無關。
5.隨旋轉速度增大時，R 值愈大，流場愈不穩
定，此結果與圓柱靜止時之結果相反。
6.外加磁場、旋轉速度及圓柱半徑R 對薄膜流
的穩定性有極大的影響，外加磁場具與流動
方向反向之磁力，因此消散更多的外來干擾
能量，使系統更加穩定；而旋轉離心力增加
擾動能量，使系統更加不穩定。

ABSTRACT
To broaden the application of inverse estimation, the
purpose of this study is to estimate the unknown temperature
boundary condition of the complex or irregular shape, like the
high pressure gas tank. An inverse algorithm based on the
sequential method and the concept of future time combined
with the finite element method is proposed to solve the two
dimensional irregular shape heat conduction problems. Special
features about the proposed method are that the stiffness matrix
of the irregular shape can be solved from the finite element
method and used by the inverse algorithm. The estimated
results are quite accurate with the consideration of future time
in the different measured errors, the various sensor’s number
and the sensor location. These results show that the proposed
method is an accurate, sturdy, and efficient method for solving
several realistic applications.
Keywords: Inverse Method; Heat Conduction Problem; Future
Time; Finite Element Method

It is an important issue to obtain the heat transfer phenomena in

disclosed when the temperature or the heat flux of the complex

In the inverse heat conduction problems, the surface
conditions or the thermal properties of a material are estimated
by utilizing the temperature measurements within the medium.
These problems have received much attention and numerous
papers have been devoted to this topic of research. The inverse
problems are known as ill-posed, hence the estimation is very
sensitive to the measured errors of the input data. To overcome
the instability of the inverse problem, different methods have
been developed. Several studies investigated the inverse heat
transfer phenomena related to this topic [1-4].
The inverse method has been exhibited its potential for
dealing with many kinds of heat problems. Indeed, a few
researches have been solved the heat problems by the inverse
method successfully. For example, Dong et al. [5] proposed a
method of fundamental solutions for solving isotropic inverse
heat conduction problems. The results of their study for several
numerical examples are presented to demonstrate the efficiency
of their proposed method. The maximum entropy method [6] is
applied to estimate the surface temperature by Kim et al.. The
inverse heat conduction problem is reformulated for MEM and
a three-phase solution method using the successive quadratic
programming. Chen et al. [7] presents an input estimation
method to estimate the time-varying heat flux and the
temperature of the inner wall in the chamber recursively. Their
simulated results show that the proposed method exhibits a
good estimation performance and promotes a practical
implementation highly. Carvalho et al. estimate the temperature
profile generated in the cutting processes by the finite volume
method and the variation of the thermal properties [8]. In
addition, Chen et al. [9] compare the performance between the
application of the whole-domain function specification method
and the sequential function specification method in the inverse
problem of transient conjugate heat transfer of laminar forced
convection in a circular pipe. The two inverse methods are used
to estimate the time-dependent inlet temperature and the
outerwall heat flux simultaneously on the basis of temperature
measurements. Gao and He [10] present a new inverse analysis
approach for identifying material properties and unknown
geometry in the multi-region problems by using boundary
element method. In their study, the material properties and
coordinates of the unknown region boundary are taken as the
optimize variables, and the sensitivity coefficients are

In the proposed method, an inverse algorithm based on the
sequential method, the concept of future time and the finite
element method is used to estimate the time-dependent and
space- dependent boundary condition of the irregular shape in
this study. The accuracy of this investigation is discussed under
the different measured error, the future time, boundary
condition, and sensor number within the medium of the gas
tank. It can prove that the temperature distribution boundary are
estimated feasibility in this study. Furthermore, the results of
this study show that the estimation of temperature distribution
boundary in irregular shape by inverse method is robust and
effective.
CONCLUSION
This study has presented an inverse scheme based upon
the sequential method and the finite element method to estimate
the space-dependent and time-dependent wall temperature
distribution in the heat conduction problem of irregular shape.
An inverse solution is deduced from the method which is based
on a numerical approach and the concept of the future time and
no sensitivity analysis is needed in this algorithm. To make
changes in various parameters are used to prove the accuracy of
the method, just like different measured error, future time, and
sensor number on the results of the inverse analysis. In general,
the results have shown that the solution scheme yields
satisfactory results for the boundary temperature profile. It has
also been shown that the accuracy of the estimated results can
be enhanced by suppressing the error in the measurement data
or by the more temperature sensors. We combine the traditional
inverse method and the finite element method to obtain the
excellent results under the irregular shape condition.
From this study, it can be concluded that the proposed
method is an accurate, robust and efficient method to determine
the unknown temperature boundary conditions on the gas tank
in inverse heat conduction problem of irregular shape.
