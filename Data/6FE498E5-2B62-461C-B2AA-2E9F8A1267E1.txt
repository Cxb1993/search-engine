中文摘要 
本計畫是國科會配合經濟部技術處補助下執行在地型學界科專計畫「智能化攻牙中心
機技術研發計畫」(Intelligent Tapping Center) [2008/8/1~2011/7/31]所補助的儀器設備
費，目標是建立國內工具機產業在智能化主軸、智能化螺桿與智能化 CNC 等關鍵零
組件的技術深度，並以軟體和機電整合技術來提升國產工具機可靠度、穩定性和高加
工效能，除了提高國產工具機之附加價值外，更協助國內工具機廠切入汽車與 3C 電
子生產線上的高稼動率工具機市場。目前執行成果包括 21 項可供技術移轉項目、18
發明專利(申請中)、國內外期刊論文 24 篇與研討會論文 45 篇；在實際成果應用上，
已有 10 件技術移轉（285 萬）；衍生委託研究計畫計 23 件總金額為 2,655 萬元。 
關鍵字：智能化攻牙中心機, 智能化主軸, 智能化螺桿, 智能化控制器 
 
Abstract 
The goal of this project is to develop the smart machine tool technology especially for a 
three-axis tapping center.  This project includes three subprojects, which are smart spindle, 
smart ballscrew and smart CNC focused on the mass-production line of automotive and 3C 
components.  Smart machine tool can be achieved by integration of the computer, 
microelectronic, on-line process sensing and control, and metrology technologies.  
Besides, integration of human knowledge of dynamic system, design, automation and 
machining with on-line machining process monitoring and control is a core concept on 
building the smart machine tool.  The achievements of this project include 21know-how, 
18 pending patents, 24 journal papers and 45 conference papers.  We have also 
accomplished 10 technology transfer item and 23 granted research projects. 
 
Key words: smart tapping center, smart ballscrew, smart spindle, smart CNC
