英文摘要 
The main purpose of the proposal in the second year includes two main objectives, enhancement each 
layer reliability and demonstration the preliminary combination results of solution-processed organic 
transistors. To realize the all solution-process organic transistors, it is inevitable to develop the all necessary 
components for the fabrication of flexible organic device including plastic substrate, electrode, active layer 
and dielectric layers. There are six main research issues in second year’s project implementation, which 
consist of (i) The research and development of novel p- and n-types solution-process organic conducting 
layers. (ii) The research and development of MMT and liquid phase deposition silicon dioxide stacked on 
the plastic substrate (PET) to prevent the moisture and oxygen from penetration. (iii) Nano-silver particles 
with an average diameter smaller than 50 nm via chemical reductions were successfully synthesized. (iv) 
Comparison with the fabrication via pattern-ability inkjet-printing technology and mask-free spin-coating of 
high-k dielectric layer with nano-particles. (v) The research and development of novel solution-process 
high-k dielectric materials. (vi) Investigation and demonstration the preliminary results of all solution 
process organic transistors.  
First, in the organic conducting layer’s part, a novel and simple [X+1+Y] one-pot synthetic route for 
p-type DTT (dithenothiopehene; three fused-ring system), TTA (tetrathienoacene) and PTA 
(pentathienothiophene) and n-type tetrathienoacene were synthesized. Then, in the air blocking layer’s part, 
there are two main epoxy layers include montmorillonite and liquid phase deposition SiO2 developed in this 
year. In the electrode’s part, the conductivity of the prepared silver nano-particle was confirmed and it 
should be available for the designing of printing silver ink. In the dielectric layer’s part, all materials were 
chosen in high-k property such as PVP/nano-TiO2, strontium titanate, and strontium zirconate titanate and 
could be processed by spin-coating or pattern-ability inkjet-printing technology. Finally, the preliminary 
results of all solution process organic transistors were demonstrated and investigated. All results will be 
helpful for the fabrication and combination with each layers to realize the all solution-process organic thin 
film transistor in the third year. To sum up, the implementation and performance of this proposal in second 
year are successful. 
  
Keywords : Organic thin film transistors, all solution-process, the air blocking layer, high-k materials, silver 
electrode, inkjet-printing technology.

(C)、開發塑膠基板阻氣層的結構： 
  本年度延續第一年的環氧樹酯系統外，另外增
加使用矽氟酸還原的液相沉積制膜法來改善阻
氣膜的透明度。 
   在環氧樹酯的系統裡為了達到高阻氣性的效
果，我們必頇將蒙脫土剝離，進一步達到所需的
奈米分散型態。因此必頇破壞蒙脫土本身層與層
之間的結構，由於蒙脫土層狀結構，由於其層間
距僅 1~2 nm，一般高分子難以進入，故改以小分
子二氧化矽前驅物(TEOS)進入層中，藉由二氧化
矽前驅物網狀聚合的過程所形成的空間立體障
礙破壞蒙脫土的結構。最後調整 pH 值來控制水
解和縮合反應的速率，pH 2 左右是等電點
(isoelectric point)，是一個穩定的區域，使得凝膠
時間變長，類似長晶的過程，最終形成網狀結
構，藉此剝離蒙脫土，由圖六可以明顯的看出改
質前後蒙脫土對延長氣體擴散路徑的效果差異。 
  最後將改質後的蒙脫土混入KM-U型硬化樹酯
中，以高阻氣的樹酯為主體，藉由蒙脫土的層狀
結構導致氣體擴散路徑的變長，達到其加強阻氣
的效果。

(D)、可溶液式製程鈦酸鍶與鋯鈦酸鍶高介電常數
絕緣層有機薄膜電晶體： 
  本研究團隊利用可溶式製程鈦酸鍶與鋯鈦
酸鍶高介電常數絕緣層成功應用於五環素有機
半導體上，製作成具有高電流密度、高載子移動
率、以及低驅動電壓之有機薄膜電晶體。 
 
溶液配置 
  鈦酸鍶溶液之配置主要分成兩個部分，第一
部分主要是形成氧化鈦溶液：將 0.76 毫升氧化鈦
前驅物四異丙醇基鈦與0.51毫升乙醯丙酮溶於3
毫升之二甲氧基乙醇。第二部分主要是為了得到
鋇離子：將 536.8毫克醋酸鋇溶於 5毫升冰醋酸
中，使其完全溶解為止。將第二部分溶液以滴定
的方式滴入第一部分溶液，最後加入 0.73 毫升
二甲氧基乙醇使其最後溶液濃度為 0.5 莫耳濃
度。 
    鋯鈦酸鍶溶液之配置方法與鈦酸鍶溶液相
似，同樣分成兩個部分。第一部分將 0.7 毫升氧
化鈦前驅物四異丙醇基鈦、62.5微升正丙醇鋯與
0.47毫升乙醯丙酮溶於3毫升之二甲氧基乙醇形
成氧化鋯與氧化鈦溶液，第二部分將 536.8 毫克
醋酸鍶溶於 5 毫升冰醋酸中，使其完全溶解為
止。再將第二部分溶液以滴定的方式滴入第一部
分溶液，最後加入 0.83 毫升二甲氧基乙醇使其
最後溶液濃度為 0.5莫耳濃度。

Abstract 
 
Compact frequency doublers for operation in millimeter-wave bands are demonstrated. These frequency doublers adopt two types of 
slow wave structure in Marchand balun, thereby reducing the chip size.  The results demonstrate low conversion loss and good 
fundamental signal suppression with a wide operation bandwidth. These doublers fabricated by GaAs PHEMT process, with a size 
dimension of less than 0.288 mm
2
, could thus be obtained. In comparison with the conventional Marchand balun, more than 48% of 
reduction in the length of the coupled line can be achieved. 
 
 
1. Introduction  
 
In the RF front-end system, the signal sources can be obtained either by high frequency local oscillators or by doublers from a lower 
frequency source. Doublers are used more often than local oscillators because they allow stable and high-quality oscillator work at a 
lower frequency, as well as provide high performance for system application simultaneously. In addition, the balanced topologies 
allow for high conversion efficiency and effective suppression of fundamental signals [1]-[2]. The balun is an integral part of the 
balanced doubler, in which the outputs need to provide equal amplitudes, 180-degree phase difference, and wide operation 
bandwidth [3]. Although the existing research results have demonstrated good performance of balanced doublers, the size of 
Marchand baluns with λ/4 multi-coupled line is too large, thereby limiting flexibility in circuit design [4]. Moreover, the balanced 
doublers have been reported to rely on three-dimensional or coplanar waveguide transmission line topology to achieve its small chip 
size [5]-[6]; however, these complicated structures are difficult to design. Thus, the balun circuit still requires further study in 
doublers so as to improve design flexibility and miniaturize the circuit of the balun. 
In this paper, two balanced frequency doublers based on slow wave structure are presented. The first doubler employs the additional 
open stub to increase capacitors and reduce the coupled-line length in the Marchand balun. Based on this concept, the second 
doubler used extra coupled line to increase capacitors that also offered the slow wave effect to reduce the length of balun. 
Accordingly, these doublers are able to achieve a small chip size and wideband performance ranging from 25~44 GHz.

2. Circuit design 
 
2.1 Reduced-size Balun Design 
The conventional Marchand balun typically uses two λ/4 coupled line sections that occupy most of the chip area. To overcome the 
problem, the proposed balun with the compensated capacitors is shown in Fig. 1. Given that the compensated capacitors supply each 
coupled line with different phase velocities, it can reduce the length of the coupled line section and maintain the good performance 
for balun. The compensated capacitor can be realized easily by capacitive stubs or capacitive coupler line.

2.2 Doubler-1 and -2 Implementation 
By using the Marchand balun with compensated capacitive stubs, the implemented balanced doubler-1 was thus simplified (Figure 
4(a)). In addition, the implemented balanced doubler-2 with the compensated capacitive coupler line is shown in Figure 4(b). These 
Marchand-like baluns were used to produce the 180-degree phase difference, while the stub and coupled line A were used to reduce 
the length of the λ/4 coupled line. In general, the value of the λ/4 coupled line length is 860 µm for Ka-band application. In the 
doubler-1 structure presented here, the length, width, and spacing for the λ/4 coupled line of the proposed balun were 350, 7 and 7 
µm, respectively. In the doubler-2 structure, the length, width, and spacing for λ/4 coupled line of the proposed balun were 420, 16, 
and 5 µm, respectively. 
To design these doublers, the layouts were verified using the advanced design system (ADS) and Zeland IE3D software, and 
the harmonic balance method was applied to simulate the frequency doubler. The doubler-1 and doubler-2 were fabricated on 76.2 
and 100 µm-thick GaAs substrate. These chips were implemented by Transcom 0.25 µm PHEMT and WIN 0.15 µm PHEMT 
processes (Figure 4); the sizes of these chips were 0.4 x 0.72 mm and 0.41 x 0.68 mm.

3. Doubler Results 
 
The fabricated frequency doublers were attached on carrier plates for testing. The measurements of the input and output signals 
were provided by the coplanar GSG on-wafer system based on the Agilent E4446A spectrum analyzer that was calibrated by the 
E44198 power meter. Meanwhile, the losses of the probes and cables were calibrated by the PNA E8364A network analyzer. 
Figure 5 shows the measured and simulated conversion loss of the doublers with an output frequency ranging from 20~44 GHz. The 
measurements and simulations were performed for doubler-1 and doubler-2 with input power levels of 11 and 15 dBm, respectively. 
The measured conversion loss of doubler-1 ranged from 9~14.3 dB from 25~44 GHz, and the measured conversion loss of 
doubler-2 proved to be better than 14.5 dB from 20~44 GHz, with the best conversion loss of 8.4 dB at 25 GHz. 
Figure 6 shows the fundamental suppression as functions of input frequency. The fundamental signal suppression of doubler-1 
ranged from 24.9~37.5 dB at the input power of 11 dBm. In addition, the fundamental signal suppression of doubler-2 ranged from 
29~57.1 dB at the input power of 15 dBm. As Figs. 5 and 6 indicate, the proposed baluns provided two outputs signals with 
equivalent amplitudes and differential phase, resulting in good conversion loss, fundamental signal suppression, and broad 
bandwidth.

4. Conclusion 
The balanced frequency doublers incorporated with two novel miniaturized baluns have been presented. The 25~44 GHz frequency 
doubler-1 has been realized. By using the compensated capacitive stub, the traditional Marchand balun has been easily designed for 
its small size. In addition, the doubler-2 has been used to prove the compensated capacitive coupler line is another feasible slow 
wave structure. Based on the capacitive coupler line, the size and operation bandwidth of doubler-2 are relatively compact and wide. 
These doublers achieved conversion loss from 8.4~14.5 dB, while fundamental signal suppression reached more than 24.9 dB from 
25~44 GHz. As the measured results show, these baluns not only provide wideband performance for designing a frequency doubler 
but also reduced the entire circuit size. These capabilities are very attractive for RF front-end applications.

In this paper, the dielectric film with the admixture was made by inkjet printing to avoid the photography 
process. By studying the characteristics of insulating films at different parameters of ink-jet printing, 
including voltage, frequency and waveform, we successfully printed dielectric layout, accomplishing the

Finally, we realized a low leakage current density in the nanocomposite dielectric and high current ratio in 
the pentacene-TFTs device with top contact structure. These devices exhibited p-channel TFT characteristics 
with a higher saturation field-effect mobility and a lower voltage operation (<6 V) comparable to the spin 
coating (SPC) process, indicating that these NC-dielectric materials can be used in low-cost and 
high-performance printable electronics. Atomic force  microscope (AFM) and Raman spectroscopy are used 
to analyze the fundamental properties of PVP/TiO2 NC-films by inkjet printing and the carrier mobility 
relation to the semiconductor layer films, respectively. It was found that a higher capacitance of dielectric 
layer, improving the polarization at the boundary between the semiconductor and the dielectric films can 
induce higher carrier density of to accumulate in the channel to increasing output current. The greater 
intermolecular coupling will enhance the overlap of πconjugated electronic cloud of the pentacene layer, 
which raises the efficiency of carrier transport in the semiconductor. However, the leakage current density of 
insulator layers was deteriorating with increasing the TiO2 ratio in the MIM structure due to the enhancement 
of leakage conduction path related to the surface roughness.
