摘要 
本計劃的目的，首先要確定傳統氣壓剎車增壓器的罩門現象及其影響之後，驗證補救
措施的具體效果，然後進行主要零件的研發。再以最簡單最經濟的方式消除危險罩門，以
確保行車安全。目前，市面尚未見任何『氣壓剎車預警系統』出現。本研究根據廠商所提
的條件，設計一台可以自動偵測氣壓剎車增壓器的行程，且當氣壓度不足時或行程超過 2/3
最大行程時，可自動啟動警告警示燈號，提供煞車間隙調整時機，避免產生煞車失靈的危
機。 
關鍵詞：氣壓剎車，預警系統，煞車失靈 
 
ABSTRACT 
The purpose of this project is to determine the disadvantages of the conventional pneumatic 
air brake booster and its related impacts, validate the effectiveness of the remedy measure, and 
develop the key components, in order to eliminate the defects with the simplest and most 
economical way, and ensure driving safety. Until now, there is no pneumatic brake safety 
early-warning system available in the market. This project attempts to apply for partial financial 
support at your association. Based on the specifications stated by the manufacturer, this project 
aims to design a mechanism to detect the mileage usage of pneumatic air brake booster, so that 
when the air pressure is insufficient or the mileage has exceeded 2/3 of the maximum, limit, the 
mechanism sends a warning signal automatically, provide braking adjustment timing, in order to 
avoid the risk of braking failure. 
Keywords：Pneumatic Brake, Early-warning System, Brake the malfunction

(中文) 引擎的真空度已不足使用於大型汽車的煞車輔助，因此大型汽車皆採用壓縮空氣
及氣壓作動缸輔助腳踏的力量，通稱為氣壓剎車增壓器（補助器）。此裝置最大
的罩門就是行程有限，當增壓器行程到達極限時煞車將失去作用，煞車頓時失效，
影響行車安全甚巨。最近，有許多調查報告顯示：大型車於出發前檢查煞車時狀
況均良好，但旅途中因摩耗超出限度未能即時查覺而導致煞車失靈者居多。因此
本計畫擬在增壓器中增設位置感測器，當增壓器行程到達某位置時會自動顯示警
告，提供駕駛者適時調整煞車蹄片與煞車鼓間隙，避免行車途中到達最大極限而
無查覺引發危險。 
 本計劃首先要確定傳統氣壓剎車增壓器的罩門現象及其影響之後，驗證補救措
施的具體效果，然後進行主要零件的研發。期待可以以最簡單最經濟的方式消除
危險罩門，以確保行車安全。
(英文) Engine vacuum is no longer sufficient to function as a braking aid to large vehicle, so all 
large vehicles use compressed air and air pressure actuators to assist the stepping force, 
generally referred to as pneumatic brake booster (compensator). The main disadvantage 
of this device is its limited mileage duration.so that when the brake booster is used to an 
extent, it sends off a warning automatically, and reminds drivers to adjust the braking 
clearance, in order to prevent the risks when the mileage has reached a maximal limit.
