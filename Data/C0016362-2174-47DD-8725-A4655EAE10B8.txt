研究摘要： 
近年來，由於半導體元件產業的蓬勃發展，於是進而帶動了 LCD 製造商、
積體電路專用錫球製造商、鋼鐵業板模製造商、軟性電路板製造商等產業的復
甦；然而這些製造商在製造產品的過程中，在品管控制上的偏差度或精確度就成
為製造過程中相當重要的一個參考指標。因此在製造過程中就必須利用測量系統
分析儀所量測的數據資料統計。所以本計畫主要目的就是結合影像量測技術開發
一系列跨平台量測套件，提供製造業之管理資訊人員套用及建構一套為符合上游
廠商要求術數據統計格式之報表。 
 
The LCD manufacturing and semiconductor industries are emerging due to the 
blooming development of IC components industry recently. However, an important 
indicator for quality control of those manufacturers is in the field of product’s 
deviation or precision during the manufacturing process. To attain the indicator, they 
must base on the statistical data obtained from the analysis of the measurement 
system. Hence, the purpose of the project is to utilize image measurement technique 
to develop a cross-platform measurement packages, which can provide the required 
statistical format tables for managerial and information workers in the firms.
