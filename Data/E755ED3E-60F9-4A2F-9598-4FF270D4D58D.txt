The project develops a multi-period newsboy model to deal with the 
replenishment decision of a short life cycle product within a retailer 
and a manufacturer. This model uses wholeprice pricing and revenue 
sharing mechanisms to increase the profits of the retailer and the 
manufacturer. Then, the mechanisms can increase the profit of the total 
channel in the supply chain. Additionally, consumers can also pay less 
money to buy this product.
