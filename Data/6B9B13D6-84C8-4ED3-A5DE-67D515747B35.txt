Abstract 
Owning to geography conditions in Taiwan, only eighteen percent of rainfall is fully 
recycled and used. This is why Taiwan becomes one of the most shortages of water areas. 
The characteristics of mesh pipes are to recycle rainfall efficiently and to down the cost. 
This research expected to find out whether mesh pipe would lead to some degrees of 
changes on the length, width, leaves, ph of the plants. We designed the experiment 
including two groups, such as experiment (mesh pipe) and control (traditional container), 
and four different types of plants with multiple variances.  The variables are recorded by 
researchers each week. Experimental results show that water spinach is the most 
appropriate plant for mesh pipe cultivating. Others are appropriate for traditional 
container cultivating. 
Keyword: Mesh pipe, Container cultivation, Multivariate analysis.

Abstract 
Agriculture business owners seldom have financial plans in advance or make the 
financial report public afterward, thus there is few financial information for reference. 
Therefore, this study proposes the 5-year capital budget decision plan for the greenhouse 
planting of safety agriculture. The financial plan is calculated based on the area of 9.7 
acres greenhouse, and plants cauliflowers for 3 turns and melongena (eggplants) 2 turns 
each year. Each year there is NT 707,560 sales, except for the first year with NT 565,248 
due to the lead time. Every year the cost of goods sold ranges within NT 440K to NT 
446K, and cost for selling, general and administrative is NT 60K. The capital expenditure 
for the greenhouse is delivered NT 757,050 in the 0-th year, and is allocated to 5-year’s 
depreciation. The earnings before interest and taxes (EBIT) in 0-th and first year are 
negative, and are NT 51K annually for other years. The net present value (NPV) of this 
project is NT 5,088 under discount rate 2%. The internal rate of return (IRR) is 2.19%, 
while payback period is 4.7 years. If there is no lead time in the first year, the sales in that 
year is as high as that of other years and the NPV rises as NT 131,001, while the IRR is 
7.31% and the payback period is 4.07 years.  
 
Keyword: Greenhouse planting, Financial Management, Cost Analysis
