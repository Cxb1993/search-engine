Fig.  2. The J-V characteristics of the devices and the performance of devices  w/o BCP buffer layer. 
To verify the improvement of Voc, the device with structures shown in Figure 2(a) are fabricated. As 
compared to the structure commonly used ITO/PEDOT:PSS/P3HT:PCBM/Al (device A), the device B with 
BCP insertion between active layer and aluminum cathode exhibit obvious improvement. The device 
efficiency increases from 1.636 % to 3.606 % due to the enhancement of open circuit voltage and short 
circuit current. The open circuit voltage of device with BCP layer increases from 0.42V to 0.61V. In most 
studies of BHJ organic solar cells, the Voc of devices can be greatly improved by post-annealing treatment. 
But in this study, the device with BCP insertion did not need this treatment and can also achieve the 
comparable Voc value. The increase of Voc by about 0.2 V as compared to the device without BCP  could thus 
be clearly attributed to the increased Vbi in device B, which is nearly close to the value of the LUMO level 
shift of BCP tuned P3HT:PCBM active layer.
