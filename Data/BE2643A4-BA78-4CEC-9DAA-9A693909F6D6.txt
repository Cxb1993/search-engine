English Abstract 
  
      The process of membrane bioreactor (MBR) has been progressively applied to 
wastewater treatment nowadays. MBR combines active sludge systems and membrane filtration 
technologies, which can lead to high quality of effluent. However, membrane fouling is formed 
during MBR operation process. It is known that the major source of fouling is extracellular 
polymeric substances (EPS). EPS consists of protein, carbohydrate, humic acid, nucleic acid, 
lipid, etc., and the main components are protein and carbohydrate. Recent research studied the 
total amount of protein and carbohydrate, but rarely did qualitative analysis and systematic 
analysis of characteristics (i.e. molecular weight, hydrophobicity, and isoelectric point of 
proteins). Therefore, this study aimed to identify the species of fouling protein and carbohydrate, 
and investigated the relationship between materials of membrane and characteristics of fouling 
protein and carbohydrate. At first, submerged MBR was inoculated with dominant bacteria 
screened from active sludge and different membrane materials (PTFE, PAN, PVDF) were set into 
the same reactor. Then, we collected EPS from each membrane and extracted protein and 
carbohydrate. Results showed difficulties for carbohydrate separation and limitation for detection, 
while species of protein fouling was related to the surface morphology of the membrane (i.e. pore 
size and roughness). Larger pores cause higher ratio of relatively smaller proteins to aggregate on 
its surface, and more hydrophobic membrane surface was attached by high ratio of hydrophobic 
proteins. Most fouling proteins are located in the cytoplasm, and their functions are related to 
translation, stress response, protein folding etc. This study operated MBR in batch recirculation 
mode, which may cause stress to the microorganisms and lytic of cells. We will operate MBR in 
other modes and study the relationship between materials of membrane and characteristics of 
fouling protein further, and these information may help to build optimal operation mode of low 
protein fouling.

3.2 薄膜積垢分析  
於 MBR 的薄膜積垢中萃取出 EPS，之後以硫酸胺法沉澱出蛋白質，再進行後續的定量
(Bradford 法)和定性相關處理(濃縮、SDS-PAGE、In-Gel digestion、MS/MS)。鑑定出
蛋白質的種類後，於 Genbank 的資料庫蒐集比對積垢蛋白質的特性。  
碳水化合物的定量用酚硫法，之後以 HPLC 配合陰離子交換管柱做定性分析。
本研究以葡萄醣 (glucose)、果醣 (fructose)、蔗糖(sucrose)和乳糖(lactore)為主要分析對
象。

(五 ) 結論與建議 
本研究於 Ralstonia 純菌反應槽中置入 PAN、PVDF、PTFE 三種薄膜，分析薄膜積垢
物 EPS 中的蛋白質和碳水化合物，歸納出以下的結論。  
1.  積垢蛋白質分子量的平均值分別是 52.4 KDa(PAN)、49.6 KDa(PVDF)、44.8 
KDa(PTFE)，而膜孔大小分別是 0.035 M(PAN)、0.200 M (PVDF)、0.220 M 
(PTFE)，顯示在較小膜孔上吸附的蛋白質是分子量較高的。 
2.  顯示蛋白質親疏水性的 GRAVY 平均值分別是 -0.16(PAN)、 -0.17(PVDF)、
-0.01(PTFE)，顯示 PTFE 上吸附的蛋白質是最疏水性的。而膜 contact angle 分別
是遠大於 57.6 o (PAN)、81.1 o (PVDF)、127 o (PTFE)，材質上 PTFE 是最疏水性的，
顯示越疏水性膜材越會吸附疏水性蛋白質。 
3.  蛋白質大部分為胞內產物，與壓力反應和蛋白質摺疊等功能有關，顯示在這次的
無線迴流反應槽操作下，有明顯的細菌老化及水解現象發生。 
4.  碳水化合物的定性偵測有訊號重疊和偵測極限的困難，必須更進一步
純化並濃縮樣品中的碳水化合物。  
未來可以設計不同的反應槽操作條件，以探討對積垢蛋白質組成的影響，碳水化合
物的分析則需要分離出高純度的樣品，若能配合靈敏度更高的儀器預期能有好
的結果。
