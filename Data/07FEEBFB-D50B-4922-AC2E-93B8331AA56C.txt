摘要 
我 們 利 用 bis(4-(N-(1-naphthyl)pheny- 
lamino) phenyl)fumaronitrile (NPAFN)做為平面
異質接面有機太陽能光伏打電池之施體層，並
結合傳統的 C60 為受體層，製作出一具有高開
路電壓高達 1.03 V 之元件，且其開路電壓相較
於傳統的 copper phthalocyanine (CuPc)/C60 之
元件，增加了 100%，雖然其短路電流密度受
到 NPAFN 本身吸收深度的限制，從 4.85 降至
3.65 mA/cm2，但最後效率還是可達到 1.97%，
而 CuPc 的元件效率僅 1.31%。此外，NPAFN
元件在順向 1.5 V 之偏壓下，可以觀察到來自
於 NPAFN 激子複合放出的電激發紅光。 
 
關鍵詞：有機太陽能、高開路電壓、電激發放
光。 
I. 前言 
有機光伏打元件近幾年來受到相當大的
矚目，其優點有低成本製程、適合捲對捲製程
以及短小輕薄的特性[1,2]。有機光伏打的原理
為光和電的轉換，光子被施體和受體吸收形成
激子，激子擴散至施-受體介面，而被拆解成電
子和電洞，再被各別的電極所收集而形成光電
流。而在過程中，材料的能階也扮演著相當重
要的角色，施體的最高佔據分子軌域(highest 
occupied molecular orbital, HOMO)和最低未佔
據分子軌域(lowest unoccupied molecular orbital, 
LUMO)必須高於受體，以達到高的激子拆解效
率[3]。此外，元件的效率也和HOMO與LUMO
之間的能階差有相當程度的關連性[4]。為了增
加元件效率，subphthalocyanine (SubPc)被用來
取代傳統的施體材料 copper phthalocyanine 
(CuPc)，達到較匹配的能階[5]。由於芳香族羥
胺基之有機材料具有高電洞遷移率及極高的
HOMO，因此也被認為是相當有潛力應用在有
機光伏打元件上[6-8]。 
本 研 究 利 用 bis(4-(N-(1-naphthyl)phenyl- 
amino) phenyl)fumaronitrile (NPAFN)作為有機
光伏打元件之施體層[9]，其高電洞遷移率和極
高的HOMO，使元件的開路電壓比傳統CuPc
製作的元件高出兩倍。然而，由於NPAFN的吸
收波段和C60重疊，且其吸收深度也不長，使得
短路電流密度略低於CuPc之元件。最後，因為
其開路電壓的大幅增加，效率還是有顯著的提
升。此外，NPAFN其本身就是一個不需參雜就
可以放出紅光的材料，因此它可以製作單層的
有機發光二極體。在本研究中，我們施加順向
偏壓時，有機光伏打元件也可以放出紅光。從
應用的角度來看，這是一個具有雙功能的有機
光電元件(結合有機光伏打及有機電激發放光) 
[10,11]，由於有機光伏打電池可以以吸收從正
向入射的外界光，因此可以減少外界光的反射
來增加對比度，有如黑電極般[12]，且吸收了
外界光可以轉換為電流驅動有機發光元件，達
到能量的最佳運用。

一、出席會議經過 
SPIE為國際光電子元件學會的縮寫，每年固定在暑假期間約八月份時在美國加州聖地牙
哥的研討會中心(Convention Center)舉行，今年是在八月份的12~16日共計五天的研討會行程，
而我所參與的會議是屬於有機光電子元件學門的年會(補充SPIE共有分成四大主題：太陽能源
光電元件、生醫電子、光學、奈米科學與電子元件)，該會議的總主持人是Z. Kafafi教授，並且
會議內容含括有有機發光二極體、有機薄膜電晶體、有機太陽能電池、液晶光電、有機材料合
成、大型電子元件製作技術、有機半導體材料，該會議預計吸了全球超過500以上致力於光電
元件原理與技術的專家參與五天的研究成果發表及討論，也提供許多的機會讓大家交流意見。
而除了正式的研討會議外，SPIE也安排短期元件物理課程、專案討論會、廠商展覽會等...

dissociation sites, where the excitons can be converted into free carriers and contribute to the photocurrents [6,7]. In 
particular, in the planar-mixed heterojunction (PM-HJ) devices, in which the mixed layer provides efficient exciton 
dissociation and the planar layer provides better charge transport, can significantly enhance the device performance [8].  
Besides, a proper acceptor material also plays an important role in the optimization of device performance. Forrest 
et al. proposed the use of C60 in small-molecule OPV devices to improve the efficiency due to its long exciton diffusion 
length and outstanding ability for electron transfer [9,10,11]. C60 is also known for its high carrier mobility, which is 
expected to be more efficient for charge collection [12]. However, the hole mobility of the common donor materials is 
less than the electron mobility of C60, resulting in the charge imbalance condition [13,14]. This was previously suggested 
to be the cause of a low fill factor (FF) in OPV devices [15]. Recently, the emergence of C70 has promoted extensive 
studies on the further improvement of small-molecule OPV devices due to its wide absorption band and high extinction 
coefficient compared to the conventional acceptor C60 [16,17,18]. For example, Leo et al. has fabricated an OPV device 
of zinc phthalocyanine:C70 with a high external quantum efficiency (EQE) of over 50%, leading to an improvement in 
JSC [16]. Sakai and his coworkers studied the device performance as a function of annealing treatment. They suggested 
that the molecular packing density increases with the annealing process and improves the charge transport property, and 
therefore a higher JSC and FF are obtained [17]. In addition to the enhancement of power conversion efficiency (PCE) 
gained by using C70 as the acceptor, Xi and his colleagues proposed that the degradation of OPV devices in air can be 
alleviated compared to when C60 is used as the acceptor, due to the instability of C60 in oxygen [18].  
Here, we use a high HOMO material SubPc in order to obtain the high VOC. At the same time, by employing C70 as 
the electron acceptor with a complementary absorption spectrum, JSC can be further improved as more light harvested. 
The EQE spectrum showed a considerable enhancement of 350 to 550 nm when C70 was used as the acceptor in 
comparison to when C60 was used. Moreover, the FF was also increased when C70 was the acceptor. This was determined 
by using the differences in carrier mobility between C60 and C70 with the space-charge limited current (SCLC) 
measurement. The hole mobility of SubPc was found to differ from the electron mobility of C60 by orders of magnitude 
but was in the same order to C70. Therefore, blending SubPc with C70 may provide a better charge balance condition than 
using C60, leading to an overall improvement in device performances.

4. CONCLUSION 
We have demonstrated that C70 is more suitable for the acceptor in OPV devices than C60 due to its superior optical 
properties, such as high photo-responsiveness and a broad absorption band. By using the PM-HJ structure, the 
SubPc-based OPV device exhibited high performance with a PCE of 4.6% due to the significantly increased JSC without 
compromising the VOC. In addition to the enhanced JSC, the FF of the SubPc:C70 device was much higher than that of its 
SubPc:C60 counterpart. This was attributed to the better charge balance condition when using C70 with the SCLC 
measurement. Therefore, the use of C70 together with donor material that absorbs at long wavelengths is a promising 
method of advancing the performance of SubPc OPV devices.
