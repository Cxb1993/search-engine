中文摘要 
電阻記憶體之材料系統以過渡金屬單元氧化物過渡金屬氧化物(也稱TMO)，其結構最
簡單，成分控制相對容易，因此有利於商業發展，早期發表材料包含，TiO2、NiO、ZnO 以
及Cu2O…等，根據能隙數值判斷，大部分材料應具透光特性，但文獻上的探討不多。 
本研究計畫之關鍵技術研究主要以TiOxNy 材料系統作為電阻記憶體之電阻層材料，計
畫內容包含： 
1. 利用真空濺鍍法製作透明電阻記憶體之各層薄膜，將探討薄膜製程參數，對於其電
學與光學特性的影響，並找出製程最佳化條件。 
2. 該材料系統(TiOxNy)搭配金屬電極或透明導電薄膜電阻層轉換機制之研究。 
本計畫之元件之上、下電極以直流濺鍍方式獲得，電阻層則利用反應性濺鍍系統鍍製，
藉由改變基板溫度，反應性氣體之氮、氧氣比值，及不同電阻層厚度，進行製程參數最佳
化，研究結果發現：元件經 3000 秒stress後之高低電阻值的比值都能夠維持 103以上。 
英文摘要 
The resistive switching behavior, which signifies a drastic change in resistance in some 
materials, has reportedly been found in ferromagnetic oxide materials, doped perovskite oxide 
materials, and organic materials, as well as in simple binary transition metal oxide(TMO) 
materials(NiO, NiO, ZnO, and Cu2O).  
In this research, a resistance film of Titanium Oxynitride (TiOxNy) as the testing vehicle to 
study some key technologies of transparent RRAM including: 
1. The thin film is deposited and produced multi-layers by sputtering process. The 
correlation among the thickness， index of each film, and electrical properties of titanium 
oxynitride films will be studied in detail. The process optimization of the resistive layers of 
TiOxNy was estimated. 
2. The conduction mechanism of L- and H-state will be observed, whereas the switching 
mechanism from L- to H-state for TiOxNy resistive layers with metal electrodes and/or 
TCO(Transparent Conductive Oxide) electrodes will be studied. 
In this research, the resistance ratio between two memory states is above three orders of 
magnitude, which is stable over 3000 sec at 0.3V stress. 
一 前言 
平面、硬式的電子產品在攜帶上較不便利，為了讓電子產品更容易攜帶，並更服貼在
任何應用環境中，所以軟性電子技術乃孕育而生。軟性電子技術讓電子產品可以接受彎折、
捲曲，並在整個電子產品使用過程中仍可正常運作。全球諸多廠商均投入研發能量，進行
軟性電子技術的開發，除了少數已被實現、量產的軟性電子產品之外，絕大部分產品均還
處於研發階段。預估至 2018 年，全球軟性電子的市場值將達到 352.2 億美元，其中應用於
消費性電子產品的軟性顯示器將佔該市場比重之最。

及微結構佳、高整度以及可製備高方向性之薄膜。缺點：濺鍍過程中各組成的揮發性差別

5. 結論 
本次實驗經過電性量測後，發現薄膜阻抗交換現象是屬於雙極電阻轉換效應(bipolar 
switching)。TiOxNy薄膜可於室溫下利用磁控濺鍍法鍍製，適當的基板溫度可調整TiOxNy薄
膜內氧空缺，而達到元件開關電壓的調控。元件阻抗比值(on/off ratio) 可高達 104。該系統
將有機會應用於透明非揮發性記憶體。

2. Experiments 
The 150 nm-thick CCTO films were deposited on Pt/Ti/SiO2/Si substrate at 200oC by RF 
sputtering and annealed at various temperatures. The Ni top electrode was deposited by 
sputtering to fabricate Ni/CCTO/Pt structure. All the top electrodes were formed at ambient 
temperature with a radius of 300μm patterned by the shadow mask process. Aglient B1500A 
semiconductor parameter analyzer was used to measure the current-voltage (I–V) 
characteristics of the CCTO films device. The measurements were performed at 25, 50, and 
75 oC. Veeco D3100 atomic force microscopy was used to examine the surface roughness of 
CCTO films. Grazing incident-angle X-ray diffraction (XRD, Philips X’Pert Pro MRD) was 
adopted to characterize the crystal structure of CCTO films.

4. Conclusions 
In summary, the switching resistance switching behavior of the CCTO films prepared by 
RF sputter with various annealing temperatures is studied. VSET, VRESET, and leakage current 
density were decrease as the films annealed temperatures increases. The conduction 
mechanisms about LRS and HRS are Ohmic behavior and Pool-Frenkel emission, 
respectively. And the Ni/CCTO/Pt capacitor exhibits the strongest resistance switching effect 
with on/off ratio about three orders of magnitude.

The key technologies of low temperature processes for transparent
nonvolatile resistance random access memory
明志科技大學 張麗君,張剛樺
關鍵技術研究主要以TiON 材料系統作為電阻記憶體之電阻層材料，內容包含
：1.利用真空濺鍍法成功的製作電阻記憶體元件之各層薄膜。該材料系統
(TiON)搭配透明導電薄膜電阻層之製備。利用反應性濺鍍系統鍍製電阻層之過
程，藉由改變反應性氣體之氮、氧氣比值之調控，可以改善元件開關電壓，達
到省電的目的。元件含一般玻璃之透光性可達70%。2.調變製程參數，可於低溫
(基板無需加熱)即可成功的製造透明電極，及透明的電阻層，該技術可將電阻
式記體製作於矽基版、一般玻璃或塑膠上，進而應用到軟性電子產業、太陽能
或面板產業。
In this research, a resistance film of Titanium Oxynitride (TiON) as
the testing vehicle to study some key technologies of transparent
RRAM including:1. The thin film is deposited and produced multi-
layers by sputtering process at room temperature. The fabricated
TRRAM has a transmittance of 70%  including the substrate  in the
visible region and an excellent switching behavior under 3 V.
2. The conduction mechanism of L- and H-state will be observed,
whereas the switching mechanism from L- to H-state for TiOxNy
resistive layers with metal electrodes and/or TCO(Transparent
Conductive Oxide) electrodes could be produced.
電機及電子機械器材業；光學及精密器械製造業
電子產業、非揮發性記憶體
台灣的記憶體產值年約達到新台幣3000億元，而非揮發性記憶體早已廣泛應用於各種
產品中。比如，我們平時用的手機。然而快閃記憶體目前面臨瓶頸，當元件越做越小
，卻發現漏電的情況越來越嚴重，而導致記憶體無法長期保存資料，所以必須要逐步
開發可取代的新型記憶體。而電阻式記憶體是目前業界相當看好的形式之一。
