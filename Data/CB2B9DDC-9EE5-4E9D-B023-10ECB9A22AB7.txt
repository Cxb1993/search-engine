摘  要 
 
Chen 與 Liu(2007)已提出考量生產者與
購買者雙方達成最大利潤的供應鏈純採購系
統運作模式，然而在其模式中並未考量較完
整的製造成本模式與涵蓋有關消費者使用成
本的問題，因此，Chen與 Liu模式應加以修
正以獲取最佳的相關產品與製程參數。本研
究將提出建立兩階段篩選程序下的修正
Chen與 Liu模式，研究中與可直接量測品質
特性呈高度線性關係的相關變數將於第一階
段進行量測，若第一階段的產品篩選決策無
法決定則將進行第二階段產品實際品質特性
值的量測，至於消費者使用成本將採
Taguchi 的二次品質損失函數來衡量。本研
究模式在達成買賣雙方最大期望利潤條件
下，以期獲得最佳採購數量、零售價格、製
程平均數與產品篩選界限。 
 
關鍵詞: 採購數量，製程平均數，零售價
格，篩選界限，田口二次品質損失函數。 
 
Abstract 
Chen and Liu (2007) presented the 
optimum profit model between the producers 
and the purchasers for the supply chain system 
with a pure procurement policy. However, their 
model with a simple manufacturing cost did not 
consider the used cost of the customer. In this 
study, the modified Chen and Liu’s model will 
be addressed for determining the optimum 
product and process parameters. The author 
proposes a modified Chen and Liu’s model 
under the two-stage screening procedure. The 
correlated variable having a high correlation 
with the measurable quality characteristic of 
interest will be directly measured in the first 
stage. The measurable quality characteristic of 
interest will be directly measured in the second 
stage when the product decision cannot be 
determined in the first stage. The used cost of 
the customer will be measured by adopting 
Taguchi’s quadratic quality loss function. The 
optimum order quantity, wholesale price, 
process mean, and screening limits will be 
jointly determined by maximizing the expected 
total profit of the buyer and the supplier. 
 
Keywords: Order Quantity, Process Mean, 
Wholesale Price, Screening Limits, Taguchi’s 
Quadratic Quality Loss Function. 
 
1. Introduction 
 
In modern supply chain system, the trade-
off problem between the producers and the 
purchasers is an important topic. The producers 
need to determine the product price and the 
manufacturing cost for obtaining the maximum 
profit. The purchasers need to determine the 
order quantity and know the customer’s used 
cost of product for obtaining the maximum 
profit. Hence, the supply chain system needs to 
solve the problem of ”how to get a trade-off 
between them”. Goyal (1976) first proposed the 
integrated inventory model  with the total costs 
from the producers and the purchasers and 
obtained the optimum production quantity and 
order cycle. Lu (1995) considered the problem 
of an integrated inventory model with a single 
producer and multi-purchasers and presented a 
heuristic method for obtaining the model’s 
optimal solution. Hill (1997) modified the 
Goyal’s (1976) and Lu’s (1995) models for 
determining the optimum production quantity 
and schedule under the minimum expected total 
cost. Goyal and Nebebe (2000) proposed the 
optimum production and transportation policy 
with a minimum cost between the producers 
and the purchasers. Goyal (2000) further

5. Conclusions 
 
       In this paper, the author has presented a 
modified Chen and Lu’s (2011b) with the 
quality loss of a product and a two-stage 
screening procedure. The optimum process 
mean, wholesale price, order quantity, and 
screening limits are simultaneously determined 
in the modified model. The extension to the 
modified Chen and Liu’s (2007) model with a 
normal distribution for the demand of the end 
of  customer is left out for further study.
