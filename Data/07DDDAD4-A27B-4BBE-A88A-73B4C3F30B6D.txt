摘要  
氣候變化是人類面臨的重大挑戰，為了
延緩及避免全球暖化的趨勢，企業須在產品
設計及生產管理時將綠色議題納入考慮。由
於大量客製化的發展趨勢，使得產品結構不
再是固定不變的，在實務上為了要符合客戶
之需求，企業普遍會採用替代用料，而替代
在各個行業上存在已久，產生替代的因素，
會有許多考量的角度與不同的面向。本研究
從成本、品質、時間、彈性及環境等五大構
面，蒐集及整理出21個與替代料之選用相關
的考慮因素，並以分析層級程序法(AHP)分
析各替代料選用考慮因素之重要性。本研究
共訪談十九位專家(分別來自尚未實施環境
政策、已實施環境政策與長期實施環境政策
等三類公司)。研究結果顯示，若從主要準
則來看，大多數公司以品質因素作為選用替
代料之最主要的考慮準則；而已實施環境政
策與長期實施環境政策的公司，在進行替代
料件之選用時，環境因素則躍居首要之考量
準則。若從21個選用因素分析，一般而言，
當企業面臨替代料之選用決策時，「供應商
供貨之能力」及「料件品質提升」是其考慮
之主要因素，若針對已實施環境政策之企業
進行分析，結果顯示替代料件是否符合節能
要求是替代決策之主要考慮因素；對於已長
期實施環境政策之企業，對環境議題抱持持
續重視的態度，因此，建議其在選用替代料
之決策時，以選用「符合綠色設計」之料件
為主要考量，如此可在環境衝擊與企業效益
上達成雙贏之整體效益。 
關鍵字：替代料、分析層級程序法 
英文摘要 
Abstract 
To avoid global warming, climate change 
is a major challenge of human being. 
Enterprises shall take environmental issues 
into consideration when they engage in 
product design and operations management. 
Due to the trend of mass Customization, the 
product structure is no longer fixed. Actually 
it’s a common strategy to adopt alternative 
materials to fulfill customers’ needs. However, 
there are many considerations when making 
the decision of adopting alternative 
components. This study concluded twenty-one 
factors for checking the decision effectiveness 
in materials substitution. These factors can be 
classified into five catagories: cost, quality, 
time, flexibility, and environment. We use the 
21 factors to construct an AHP model and 
analyze their impact weighting. The results 
indicate that ‘quality’ is the most important 
criterion for all of the studied companies when 
makes decision for material substitution. The 
items that used to be the significant factors in 
selecting alternative material decision are ‘the 
supplier’s capability’ and ‘quality 
improvement’. From the viewpoint of 
enterprises which have engaged in sustainable 
policy for a long time, the major factors used 
to decide the material substitution is whether 
the alternative component meets the 
requirements of green design. 
Keywords: Alternative materials, Analytic   
Hierarchy Process

2.3 綠色設計 
隨著經濟全球化的高度發展，對於現今
氣候變化之異常，使國際間對於環境議題更
為重視，進而在產品製造過程中考慮種種減
少環境汙染之策略。而在環保意識抬頭的現
代社會，世界各國開始制定產品開發相關之
環境法規。 
從產品生命週期的角度來看，如
William McDonough (2008)在提到綠色設計
之概念與思維，其若想實現真正的繁榮，必
須要減少資源之消耗，使資源有效之利用，
如同搖籃到搖籃之系統，且系統不存在廢棄
物的概念。綜合上述，可得知環境衝擊對於
企業所面臨之問題，進而推動綠色設計之思
維，期望減少對環境之汙染。因此，本研究
將在選用替代料之環境因素納入為考量需
求，以提升企業決策之整體效益。

To meet global competition in a quick-response age, enterprises must place focus on rapid 
research and the development of diversified products. Timely implementations of product data 
management systems can effectively manage increased research and development projects, as well 
as be efficient instrument for increasing enterprise competitive power. This study is aimed at a 
product data management system application of a Taiwanese precision machinery manufacturer, 
evaluates the product data management system, and uses an analytic network process to analyze the 
key factors considered in the applications of the product data management system. Then find out 
some PDM’s critical functions from ninety surveyed lists which explored from the analyzed key 
factors. The results indicate that ‘system integration costs’, ‘maintenance service’, ‘software 
debugging and updating’, as well as other ‘support services’ of software vendors, are the important 
factors regarding software evaluations. The most import functions are ‘engineer drawing 
management’, ‘electronic work flow’, project management’, ‘engineering change management’, 
and ‘quality planning management’. 
 
Keywords: Product data management system, analytic hierarchy process, information system

the enterprise, a PDM system for employees was selected.

meeting users’ demands and increasing company profits.   
Function and technology aspect– comprising the features and functions of PDM systems.   
Vendor aspect – represents supplier’s ability to provide after-sale service and support.

4.4 WEIGHTS ANALYSIS 
The results of questionnaire analysis are as shown in Table 2:   
(1) Importance analysis of the aspects  
Cost: the system integration cost had the greatest importance, and thus, application of the 
PDM system required considerations of the costs of integrating PDM with other systems (such as: 
AUTO CAD, ERP).    
System: experts considered a PDM system must guarantee synchronization and timeliness of 
information transfer. During product design, if confusion of the design version can be eliminated, 
the product manufacturing lead time can be reduced. Thus, flexibility and integration of the system 
were the most important considered factors for a PDM system.   
Function and technology:  for users, electronic sign-off processes in a PDM system is the 
key function. Users can timely follow up product defect correction, make classifications, change 
analysis, and statistical inquiries, according to their needs.   
Vendor: the experts considered the follow-up maintenance service, debugging and updating of 
software and other support services of software vendors were important upon system 
implementation.   
(2) Weight measurement of the overall aspects 
The architecture comprised 17 considered factors, all with different reasons and dependence 
relations. During the priority sequencing of the overall perspectives, the “after-services” for the 
‘Vendor’ aspect was the most important factor, followed by “software stability” for the system 
aspect, and “basic construction costs” for the cost aspect.   
The experts considered whether many bugs existed, under stability upon system 
implementation, as this problem may cause inconvenience for users. Thus, it was hoped that 
suppliers can help users to solve program bugs or provide regular maintenance services; Table 2 
ranks the weights of critical factors according to their importance.
