一、前言 
隨著二十一世紀的半導體積體電路與微機
電系統(Micro electro mechanical system, MEMS)
製程技術蓬勃發展且漸趨成熟，許多衛星元
件，甚至皮米衛星等級的推進次系統都成為發
展趨勢。近十餘年來，以 MEMS 製造技術進行
太空微推進器研發，隨著衛星微小化的發展趨
勢，已廣受先進航太國家重視，特別是奈米級
衛星推進器更是研發重點，許多相關的研究報
告顯示 MEMS 製造技術已被大量應用。以微推
進器操作原理分類，包括有：液態汽化方式
(Vaporizing liquid)[1-3] 、 冷 氣 體 方 式 (Cold 
gas)[4]、固態燃料方式(Solid propellant)[5,6]、
電驅動方式 (Electrical)[7,8]、化學反應方式
(Chemical reaction)[9-11]等微推進器型式。由於
汽化液體式微推進器具有操作簡單、無燃料質
變、高可靠度及安全與環保等優點，十分適合
做為太空微推進之用途。然而，截至目前國際
間的研究團隊針對汽化液體方式之微推進器的
研究皆無法於低溫(< 200oC)及較低加熱功率
(Heating power)的條件下獲得不錯的液體汽化
或是全汽化反應，以至於太空用汽化液體方式
微推進器的輸出推力仍然偏低。 
本研究計畫已經成功整合 (1)微機電系統
(Micro-electro- mechanical systems, MEMS)技術
與(2)金屬輔助化學蝕刻(Metal-assisted chemical 
etching, MACE)[12, 13]技術完成太空用微機電
微推進器之液體汽化腔體內壁含有矽奈米線表
面之晶片研製，並進行初步熱流場觀察實驗。
利用金屬輔助化學蝕刻技術(MACE)蝕刻形成
矽奈米線表面，乃使用硝酸銀 0.02M(莫爾濃度)
與氫氟酸 5.0M(莫爾濃度)混合溶液蝕刻矽奈米
線，可獲得約 200nm 高準直性之均勻線徑矽奈
米線陣列。200nm 高準直性均勻線徑之矽奈米
線表面利用誘導偶合電漿化學氣相沉積
(ICP-CVD)技術沉積 C4F8 薄膜，可獲得表面接
觸角(Surface contact angle)高達 158.8o 之超疏水
表面。本研製之微推進器晶片能夠於加熱溫度
150oC 及去離水流量 0.5cc/min 之條件下產生完
全液體汽化的現象。由於沉積 C4F8 薄膜之矽奈
米線陣列表面具有高比表面積、表面形貌類似
奈米多孔性結構及其超疏水等特性，因此可大
為增加去離子水的吸熱面積，能夠迅速、均勻
且高效率地將微推進器液體汽化腔體底部之熱
量傳遞給去離子水，使去離子水迅速受熱，增
強液體汽化腔體內部的水沸騰，產生大量細小
之熱氣泡，甚至達到完全汽化液體狀態。 
 
二、微推進器晶片製造步驟 
微推進器晶片之製造步驟如下所述： 
(1) 以標準 RCA 清潔法[SC1=NH4OH:H2O2:H2O, 
1:1:5, 80oC, 10min; SC2=HCl:H2O2:H2O, 
1:1:6, 80oC, 15min]清洗(100) p-type 六吋矽
晶片，再用 DI Water 沖淨後去水烘烤。 
(2) 以電子槍金屬蒸鍍機(E-gun evaporator)，蒸
鍍鉻(Chromium, Cr)金屬薄膜於矽基板表
面，其薄膜厚度為 2000Å 作為 ICP 深反應
性離子蝕刻(Deep reactive ion etching, DRIE)
及硝酸銀濕蝕刻的蝕刻阻擋層。 
(3) 接著對鉻(Cr)金屬進行圖樣化(Patterning)製
程，定義微推進器微流道之外觀圖形，如圖
1 所示。 
(4) 隨後進行微推進器之第一階段利用誘導偶
合電漿反應性離子蝕刻(ICP-DRIE)技術蝕
刻微推進器汽化腔體結構，通入蝕刻反應氣
體為 SF6/O2 之混合氣體，蝕刻深度 100µm，

矽表面(Silicon surface)原本僅有單純的矽-
氧鍵(Si-O bond)，此時矽表面會呈現疏水性表
面，若利用 HF 溶液蝕刻去除矽表面的原生氧
化層(Native oxide layer)，即可觀察到其表面不
沾水的特性。然而，當矽表面暴露於大氣中約
數十分鐘之後，矽表面與大氣中的水氣反應，
原生氧化層會再度生長出來，其厚度約為
20-30Å，此時的矽表面鍵結會改變為矽-氫氧鍵
(Si-OH bond) ，此鍵結即是矽表面親水性
(Hydrophilic)的來源。因此，若可有效去除矽表
面的氫鍵即可將矽表面改質為疏水表面。因
此，矽表面放置於大氣後，若未經過任何表面
疏水改質處理，則多呈現為親水表面，如圖
6(a)、(c)所示。其中，因為矽奈米線表面具有高
比表面積，其表面特性甚至呈現為超親水特性。 
本研究計畫採用兩種方法進行矽表面疏水
性改質處理：(1)300oC 燒結持續 2 小時，(2)利
用 ICP-CVD 沉積 C4F8 薄膜。經過 300oC 燒結
持續 2 小時，可去除多數矽表面氫鍵(H-bond)；
若使用 ICP-CVD 沉積 C4F8 薄膜，則可完全覆
蓋住矽表面的氫鍵，可獲得較理想的疏水特
性，如圖 6(b)、(d)所示。若於矽奈米線表面再
沉積上 C4F8 薄膜，其表面接觸角可高達
158.8o，如圖 6(d)所示。流場觀察結果顯示，利
用 300oC 燒結持續 2 小時的晶片，於 150oC 底
部加熱溫度，0.5cc/min 去離子水質流量條件
下，微推進器中的流場仍呈現射流(Jet flow)，
如圖 8(a)、(c)所示。大部分的去離子水在這兩
種晶片(有奈米線與無奈米線表面)情況下皆無
法有效地蒸發。然而，具矽奈米線表面的微推
進器，其水汽化情況仍較無矽奈米線表面的微
推進器為佳，此可看出矽奈米線表面已發揮了
其高比表面積的不錯吸熱能力。兩種晶片同樣
經過了 ICP-CVD 沉積 C4F8 薄膜處理後，微推
進器汽化腔內部的射流現象已完全消失，如圖
8(b)、(d)所示。超疏水矽奈米線表面的晶片，
甚至可達完全汽化去離子水的狀態。由此可知
結合(1)高比表面積與(2)超疏水兩種特性之矽
奈米線表面是可均勻且迅速的將熱量傳遞給去
離子水。去離子水受熱後，快速形成大量沸騰
氣泡，並快速地脫離加熱面，造成較好或完全
地液體汽化的現象。

五、結論 
本研究已成功利用 MEMS 與 MACE 技術完
成太空用液體汽化式微推進器之液體蒸發腔體
內壁具有超疏水矽奈米線表面之晶片研製，並
完成初步熱流場觀察實驗。利用 MACE 技術，
使用 0.02M 硝酸銀(AgNO3)與 5.0M 氫氟酸(HF)
混合溶液於室溫(25oC)蝕刻矽奈米線(SiNWs)，
可以獲得約 200nm 高準直性且均勻線徑之矽奈
米線表面。內含有 200nm 高準直性且均勻線徑

技術沉積 C4F8 薄膜可獲得接觸角高達 158.8o 之

1.  INTRODUTION 
In the recent years, there has been a growing interest in MEMS micropropulsion due to the 
decreasing launch costs and increasing mission reliability. The vaporizing liquid microthruster is 
very suitable developed for space micropropulsion application due to its many advantages of 
simple operation, no fuel aging problem, high device reliability, nature and safety [1]. However, 
the vaporizing liquid microthrusters developed do not provide a well- or fully-vaporized liquid 
design to be easily operated at a low heating temperature (< 300oC). In this study, the vaporizing 
liquid MEMS space microthruster grown with silicon nanowires (SiNWs) inside was proposed. 
The present work can readily occur the fully liquid vaporizing due to the high specific surface 
area, nano-porous structure and hydrophobic surface of SiNWs, which can rapidly and uniformly 
generate a large amount of vaporized air bubbles to enhance the boiling of vaporizing liquid 
microthruster. 
2.  EXPERIMENTS
The microthruster layout was shown in Fig. 1. The width and depth of channels were set to be 
constants of 1 mm and 100 μm, respectively. The outlet width of 400 μm was selected in this 
study. The experiments were performed with the wall temperature ranging from 150-300 ƕC and 
mass flow rate from 0.125 to 1 cc/min, respectively. The chamber of micro-thruster with a depth 
of 100 μm was formed by using DRIE method and SF6 as an etching gas. The SiNWs array was 
grown by vapor-liquid-solid (VLS) method with a gas mixing ratio (SiH4/H2) of 100 sccm/200 
sccm under a process pressure of 350 mtorr at 620 oC for 60 minutes [2]. The SiNWs array has an 
average diameter of 200 nm and an average length of 6 μm as shown in Fig 2 and Fig. 3. Finally, 
the Pyrex7740 glass with two inlets was served as the top cover to be bound with Si wafer using 
anodic bonding technique and the SiNWs based MEMS micro-thruster chip was completed. The 
hydrophobicity is a very important parameter in a micro-scale two-phase thermal hydraulic 
system for boiling enhancement due to the interfacial force between the liquid and solid face [3]. 
The contact angles of the raw silicon and SiNWs wall with and without the heating treatment at 
300 oC for 2 hours were measured as shown in Fig. 4. Under a heating treatment done before 
experimental, the snake flow inside the chamber of micro-thruster almost disappeared as shown 
in Fig. 5. The stronger hydrophobicity of SiNWs surface inside the chamber can obtain the well- 
or fully-vaporized DI water with a lower wall heating temperature. 
3.  SUMMARY
In this study, the present work can readily occur a well liquid vaporizing under a heating 
temperature of 200 oC and a DI water flow rate of 0.25 cc/min. This is due to the high specific 
surface area and hydrophobicity surface of SiNWs can rapidly and uniformly generate a large 
amount of vaporized air bubbles and effectively transfer a large amount of heat from the heating 
chamber wall to DI water flow to enhance the boiling in the microthruster.

1. INTRODUTION 
This paper presents the synthesis and characteristics of silicon quantum dots (Si QDs) film. Our 
fabrication of such material is motivated by the findings [1] that silicon nitride film embedded with Si QDs. It  
has the relatively lower barrier (2.0 eV) for carrier injection, and more intense light emission than those of silicon 
oxide. The ways to form embedded Si QDs in silicon nitride or oxide are almost the same. The only difference 
is the films composition [2-3]. In our study, we synthesized silicon quantum dots from hydrogenated silicon rich 
nitride film by different annealing conditions. Finally, we got the Si QDs which size is 3 to 10 nm. 
 
2. EXPERIMENTS 
The silicon rich nitride films of about 25 nm were both deposited onto silicon and quartz 
substrates in a conventional PECVD system. The films were prepared using a gas mixture of SiH4 
and NH3. The flow ratio of SiH4 and NH3 was maintained to be a constant of 2. The total pressure 
and the plasma power were fixed at 0.5 torr and 15W, respectively. The growth temperature was 
maintained at 300oC. After films deposition, the samples were annealed at 900oC, and 1000oC for 60 
min in a horizontal quartz furnace under N2 atmosphere, respectively. Another experiment is varying 
annealing time with the same temperature of 1000oC. Figure 1 shows the Raman spectra of samples 
that annealed at dissimilar temperatures. The higher spectrum with annealing temperature of 1000oC 
exhibits that higher temperature would make better crystallization of Si QDs. Figure 2 is the GIXRD 
of the silicon quantum dots films. The samples were characterized by a set of X-ray peaks at the 
angles 2θ = 28.40, 47.38, 56.08 corresponding to (111), (220) and (311) silicon crystal planes. This 
data also prove that these films consisted of silicon nanocrystals (Si NCs). Figure 3 and Figure 4 is 
the TEM morphologies of annealed film with different temperatures. From the observation, we can 
find that lower annealing temperature will lead to sparse density of Si QDs. Figure 5 is the HRTEM of 
film that annealed at 1000oC, and it shows that the dots were crystallized with size about 5 nm. With 
various annealing time in the temperature of 1000oC, the FTIR analysis of the as-deposited and 
annealed films has shown in Figure 6. It shows the composition change of annealed film. 
 
3. SUMMARY 
In our report, we have examined various annealing conditions on hydrogenated silicon rich 
nitride films with the same deposition process. From these analyses of thermal annealing samples, we 
could observe the different precipitations of Si QDs. The achievement has been realized through 
higher temperature annealing with proper time which effectively change the dots size, density, and 
surface chemistry. Detailed discussion will be presented in the conference.
