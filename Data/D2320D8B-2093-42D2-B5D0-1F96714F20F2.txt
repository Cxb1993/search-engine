計畫中文摘要
近年來直播衛星事業蓬勃發展，節目頻道不斷增加，且用戶數快速成長，因此對提
升衛星地面接收系統的品質與功能逐漸受到重視。
本計畫針對衛星降頻器前端所需的集波方式，提出以平面雙極化天線陣列做為饋入
結構以取代傳統的金屬波導結構。以平面電路做為天線的架構，具有降低材料成本、製
作容易、減少重量、易與後級電路整合等優點。本計畫採用微帶線與孔徑耦合的激發方
式，做為集波饋入結構。利用相同陣列天線所產生的兩種不同的極化電磁波，分別經由
上下兩層微帶線饋入降頻器。孔徑耦合方法亦有降低天線與降頻器互相電磁干擾的作
用。另外一方面，當頻寬需求較窄時，可將兩種極化的信號饋入電路作整合設計，使互
為垂直的兩種極化波能由同一個饋入端進入降頻器，減少信號的路徑損耗，並提供產品
設計的多樣性。
以平面陣列設計集波器，可依照不同型式的反射面需求，作信號相位與電流分布的
調整設計，增加天線與降頻器的整體效能。本計畫的平面集波結構，除了降低業者製作
與設計成本、增加產品多樣化與增強產品效能外，將可提升與培植企業對天線與降頻器
整合設計的研發能力。
關鍵字: 衛星電視，降頻器，饋入，天線陣列，孔徑耦合
Abstract
The planar dual-polarization feeding structures for the low noise block down
converter (LNB) are proposed in this project. The planar feed-antenna array instead of the
conventional feed-horn for LNB provides the advantages such as easy fabrication, cost
down, reducing weight, low profile, and easy to combining with other devices in the LNB.
The energy from the antenna is transmitted to the microstrip that feeds the energy into the
LNB through the apertures on the common ground plane between the top antenna layer
and the bottom feeding microstrip layer. The dual polarizations are achieved by the
feeding microstrip lines on the different layers. In this case two different polarized
electromagnetic waves go through microstrip lines on the different layers. The aperture
coupling method also isolates the interference between the antenna and the microstrip. In
other way, for the narrower bandwidth application, by properly arranging the feeding
microstrip, the two polarized waves can be coupled through the apertures into the same
feeding microstrip and be selected by the controller switch. Besides raising the efficiency
of the feeding structure, these two kinds of the feeding methods offer versatile designs for
integration of the antenna and the LNB.
Keywords: satellite TV, LNB, feed, antenna array, aperture couple
