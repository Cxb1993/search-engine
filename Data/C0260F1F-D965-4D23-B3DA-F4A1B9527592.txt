一. 緒論 
 
鑑於國內一般從事馬達控制方法及
電路設計、高壓功率晶體設計、功率因數
控制及低壓數位積體電路設計之等各別
領域之工程師或研究人員，大多數彼此皆
很難有跨領域的設計能力，因此，長久以
來，存在許多重覆的研究能量投入、成本
無法降低及缺乏關鍵零組件之設計能力
的問題，再加上目前高壓功率晶體之製造
技術皆掌握在國外大廠，導致相關產業競
爭力無法有效提昇。因此，本計畫旨在建
構一結合智慧型高壓功率模組、功率因數
改善單元與無刷直流馬達控制單元之創
新整合設計平台。此創新整合設計平台將
以易於設計、快速產出為目標，所設計的
積體電路將以具有低成本且易於使用之
設計主軸。透過此平台之建立，相關廠商
可以很容易地設計具有特色之控制電路
及系統，並可經由積體電路之製程降低成
本，且易於應用於實際之民生系統上，藉
其符合未來趨勢之省能源特性，以提昇我
國相關產業之未來競爭力。 
本計畫除總計畫外共分為四個子計
畫：子計畫一、整合馬達控制晶片、智慧
型功率模組與功因改善之系統晶片與設
計平台研發。子計畫二、無刷直流馬達控
制與驅動晶片之設計。子計畫三、AC-DC
高功因整流器模組研製與節能家電應
用。子計畫四、節能馬達控制模組應用平
台-嵌入式智慧型省能乾衣機。總計畫關
係圖如圖 1.1，功能方塊圖如圖 1.2。

2-2-4、FPGA 數位邏輯控制模組設計 
本 計 畫 使 用 XILINX Spartan-3 
700AN FPGA 系列晶片如圖 2.37 控制模
組以發展控制程式，進而利用其週邊功能
以控制驅動器。XILINX Spartan-3 700AN 
FPGA 是週邊功能相當完整的處理器。使
用 VHDL 語言來設計邏輯電路，FPGA 元
件可以說是最佳的實驗目標。FPGA 它是
在一顆超大型積體電路中，均勻配置了一
大堆的可程式邏輯單元(稱之為 CLB)。每
個 CLB 都擁有基本的組合邏輯和順序邏
輯，而在 CLB 和 CLB 之間也均勻配置了
一大串的可程式配線(Routing)，控制這些
配線就可以將一個個單獨的 CLB 整合成
完整而複雜的大型電路；最後再利用分佈
於外圍的可程式輸入輸出阜 (稱之為
IOB)，提 FPGA 和外部電路的介面關係。

2-4-3、自動停機控制設計 
鑒於前述之缺點，本計畫提出自動停
機控制之方法，將吸入的空氣，透過加熱
器加熱後，成為中高溫並相對濕度低的乾
燥空氣，再與被馬達帶動的乾衣槽內之衣
物做混合，使得衣物中之水份會被大量乾
燥熱空氣蒸發，此時帶有大量溼度的空氣
會被迅速排出，而逐漸將潮濕的衣物烘
乾。 
如圖 2.92 所示，乾衣機將吸入的室溫
空氣視為（狀態 1），透過加熱器加熱後，
成為相對濕度低並中高溫的乾燥空氣視
為（狀態 2），此時相對濕度高並帶有大量
水份的衣物放在乾衣機內，當乾衣機運轉
時，衣物將會和高溫的乾燥空氣混合，使
得衣物中之水份會被大量乾燥熱空氣蒸
發，蒸發後被排出的空氣視為（狀態 3），
此時（狀態 3）的相對濕度將極接近
%1003 =Φ ，溫度 3T 也遠較（狀態 2）的
溫度 2T 低。隨著乾衣機的運轉而潮濕的衣
物也將漸漸的被烘乾，當衣物含水量漸漸
降低時，排出乾衣機外的空氣，相對溼度
也漸漸下降，則（狀態 3）的相對濕度 3Φ
將沿著等濕球溫度線向（狀態 2）靠近；
即由點 3 至點 '3 ，再由 '3 至點 ''3 ，以致於
由點 ''3 到點 2 為止。由此得知，其相對濕
度 3Φ 漸漸降低至 2Φ ，而排氣溫度 3T 也漸
漸升高至 2T 點。當衣物全乾時，便完全等
於狀態 2 之排氣溫度，程式便可判斷衣物
已乾而自動停機。

2-4-6、防皺、除靜電之原理 
 
圖 2.96 防皺處理示意圖 
圖 2.96 所示當衣服烘乾時，濕衣服內
的水會蒸發成水氣，體積也會跟著膨脹約
數百倍以上，此時膨脹的水氣會將衣服之
纖維撐開，若再以冷空氣快速冷卻衣服，
使衣服纖維收縮速度變慢，不易產生皺
褶。當相對溼度低於 40%以下時，靜電較
容易產生；空氣溫度愈高相對溼度愈低，
靜電也愈多；當乾衣機乾衣完成時，乾衣
機內溫度甚高可能產生許多靜電，此時灌
進大量冷風，當溫度降至接近室溫時，相
對溼度也愈靠近室內相對溼度，進而達到
防皺、除靜電、不燙手等優點。

Supervisory FNN Control for Two-Wheeled Mobile 
Vehicle System 
  
Tien-Chi Chen              *Tsai-Jiun Ren              Yu-Ren Chen 
Department of Electrical Engineering, Kun Shan University, Tainan, Taiwan 
*Department of Information Engineering, Kun Shan University, Tainan, Taiwan

Abstract-A two-wheeled mobile vehicle (TWMV) system 
design based on supervisory fuzzy neural network (FNN) 
control is proposed in this paper. The TWMV system is driven 
by two independent wheeled brushless DC motor. The 
supervisory FNN controller is composed of a supervisory 
controller and a FNN controller, and the parameters of the 
controller are tuning on-line automatically to stabilize and 
control the TWMV system. The laboratory-typed TWMV 
system is constructed. The experiment results are revealed to 
demonstrate the feasibility of the proposed controller and 
show the performance of the proposed laboratory-typed 
TWMV system. 
  
I.  INTRODUCTION 
With the accelerated pace of life, it is important for 
humans to have convenient and fast transportations. With 
the trend, the electric motorcycle, electric bicycles, and 
electric vehicles are invented and become populated 
recently. Two wheeled mobile vehicle (TWMV) evokes a 
lot of interest. TWMV is regarded as two wheeled inverted 
pendulum which is well known as a new powerful 
transportation vehicles, and at least one commercial 
product, Segway [1], is available. The Segway has brought 
a new and useful transportation style for people who need 
short-distance traveling. Segway is made with 
high-technology components, such solid-state gyroscopes, 
NiMH batteries, brushless servo motors, gear box, motor 
drivers, wheels, safety features, and one digital signal 
processor. Comparing to other vehicles, the price of 
Segway is too high to accept by the general populations, so 
the Segway market is limited. 
The JOE [2] is composed of a chassis with DC motors 
coupled to the planetary gear box for wheels, a digital 
signal processor board used to implement the controller and 
necessary sensors to measure the state of the vehicle and 
the radio control unit. A low-cost self-balance scooter is 
constructed using off-the-shelf parts like wheelchair motors 
and RC car batteries [3]. It also doesn’t need complex or 
high-performance software, although the scooter was not 
constructed with safety features. 
As mentioned above, the TWMV can seem as one kind 
of energy saving and population-free vehicle. The TWMV 
can be constructed by off-the-shelf parts which like sensors, 
wheeled-motors, digital signal processors and the software, 
good control algorithms, and be cost down to be popular. 
This motives this paper to construct a new TWMV with 
common-technology. 
The control system in the JOE is using the 
state-feedback equation. Designing the state feedback 
equation needs the exact parameters of the JOE, so this 
method is hard to be used generally. The TWMV is not like 
the JOE, the parameters of the TWMV change all the time 
according to the weight, height, and center of gravity of 
pilots, so using the state feedback as the controller for the 
TWMV is not a good choice.  
The control method of the self-balance scooter 
mentioned above is almost an open loop control. The control 
method is measuring the pitch angle of the scooter and 
applying  the mathematical function to calculate the output 
torque to control the speed of motor and balance the scooter, 
at last, judging the output torque, whether the output torque 
is too large or not. Using this method as a control method of 
the TWMV is so dangerous that the pilot would be hurt 
easily. 
Unlike the controller of the JOE and self-balance 
scooter mentioned above, a supervisory fuzzy neural 
network controller is developed in this paper. The proposed 
control scheme consists of a fuzzy neural network (FNN) 
[4]-[7] controller and a supervisory controller. FNN is used 
on the controlling field, and has good controlling ability on 
the nonlinear, unknown parameter, and parameters varying 
systems [8]-[10]. The supervisory FNN controller 
parameters are tuned automatically, on-line, to overcome the 
disturbances and parameter variations of the TWMV.  
The Brushless DC (BLDC) motor has some advantages, 
such as high efficiency, large torque in low speed, small size, 
good heat dissipation, no rotor copper lose, no carbon flash, 
and large load capability. The BLDC motor for the TWMV 
design is developed in this paper. For controlling the 
TWMV smoothly and staying in stability, a suitable motor, a 
good driver design, sensors used to measuring states of the 
TWMV and an advanced control algorithm are all important 
issues.
