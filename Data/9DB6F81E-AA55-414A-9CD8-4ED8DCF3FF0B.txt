具奈米結構之光學薄膜在光電元件上之應用 
Optical thin films with nanostructures for  
optoelectronic devices applications 
執行單位：台灣大學材料科學與工程學系暨研究所

中文摘要： 
關鍵字: 光學薄膜、奈米結構、表面電漿、
修正式照明、漸變結構、極化器 
在本計畫中，我們探討具奈米結構之
光學薄膜的特殊光學性質，並且結合薄膜干
涉特性及表面電漿效應，進而製作出具奈米
結構光學元件。利用光學及電子束微影、電
漿蝕刻、導納軌跡法、及 couple-wave theory
等方式來製備及分析奈米尺寸的光學薄膜
如濾波片和極化器。我們並利用光學系統的
離焦曝光方式，配合後續乾式蝕刻製程，成
功地得到寬頻波段低反射率的抗反射漸變
結構。除此之外，我們並利用修正式照明架
構如四極孔光源，找出次波長漸變抗反射結
構的製作方法。 
 
Abstract 
Keywords: optical thin films, nanostructures, 
surface plasma effect, modified 
illumination, gradient structures, 
polarizer 
In this project, we investigate special 
optical characteristics of optical thin films 
with nanostructures. Combining with thin film 
interference and surface plasma effects, thin 
films with nanostructures are designed and 
fabricated for novel optical devices. Optical 
lithography, electron lithography, plasma 
etching, admittance diagram and couple-wave 
theory are used to fabricate and analyze 
nano-scale optical thin films. High quality of 
optical filters and polarizers are also 
fabricated and designed by these technologies. 
We also demonstrate a simple method, which 
is combining modified illumination and 
defocus techniques to fabricate 
sub-wavelength antireflective structures. The 
optimum gradient resist and silicon profiles 
can be obtained after exposure, development 
and common dry etching processes. Patterning 
sub-wavelength structures should use the short 
wavelength combining defocus exposure or 
using a suitable modified illumination 
exposure system. The optimized gradient 
structures are simulated in dosage-focus 
matrix with different types of light source. 
Results show the quadrupole modified 
illumination system with large process latitude 
is suitable for patterning sub-wavelength 
gradient structures. 
  
Introduction 
The phenomena of surface plasmon 
polariton are widely studied in recent years, 
because its special property can be candidate 
for novel optical devices such as color filter 
and polarizer. The perforated metal films have 
an extraordinary transmittance enhancement 
are caused by surface plasmon. In our study, 
we found two interesting phenomena, one is 
that the extraordinary high transmittance of 
perforated metal hole-arrays are dependent on 
the periodic structure, another is that the 
transmittance spectra of TE and TM polarized 
light have different effect are observed by 
varying incident angle. The different 
polarization effect in TE and TM polarized 
light with varying incident angle can be for 
polarizer application. The surface plasmon 
polariton (SPP) is a phenomenon describe that 
the surface charge density occur at a 
metal/dielectric interface and the 
exponentially decay at each medium. By the 
theory of SPP, we can fabricate the suitable 
designed the patterns hole-arrays/period as 0.5, 
the correspondent hole diameter is 0.3μm 
(solid curve), 0.4μm (dashed curve) and 0.5
μ m (dotted curve). The maximum 
transmission peak position is increased as 
increasing the periodic of metal hole-arrays. 
The maximum transmittance efficiency 
measured is 56.4%, 68.6% and 72.6% at 
wavelength is 1030 nm, 1290 nm, and 1550 
nm for hole diameter is 0.3μm, 0.4μm and 
0.5 μ m, respectively. The maximum 
transmittance peaks position is mainly 
affected by periodic parameter and the 
dielectric constant of metal and dielectric 
medium.  
We measure the spectra of TE and TM 
polarization by varying incident angle. There 
are some interesting phenomena in the TE and 
TM polarization. We chose different incident 
angle in our measurement like 00, 100 and 
250,as shown in figure 3, and the sample hole 
diameter is 0.3μm, period is 0.6μm. For TE 
case, as the upper graph shown in figure 3, as 
we increased the incident angle from 00 to 250 
only the transmittance efficiencies decreased. 
The maximum transmittance peak didn’t shift 
as the incident angle changed for TE 
polarization. The maximum peak position is at 
1030 nm and the transmittance efficiencies 
decreased from 56.4% (solid curve) to 51.9% 
(dashed curve) and 39.7% (dotted curve) for 
incident angle are 0°, 10° and 25° respectively. 
It seems that TE waves didn’t couple with the 
field exponentially decaying toward to the 
medium of metal or dielectric, so the 
maximum peak of TE polarization didn’t 
change. For TM case, as the bottom graph as 
shown in Figure 3, as increasing the incident 
angle the maximum transmittance peak were 
shift to IR regime. The incident angle 
increased from 0 °  to 10 °  and 25 ° , the 
maximum transmittance peak were shift from 
1030 nm to 1085 nm and 1190 nm, and the 
transmittance efficiencies are decreased from 
56.4% (solid curve), 51.3% (dashed curve) 
and 46.7% (dotted curve). We suppose that 
the TM polarization was coupled to the field 
of metal and dielectric medium.

Conclusion 
In conclusion, we demonstrated the 
polarization effect on metal perforated films 
by surface plasmon polariton theory and 
derive equation to verify the polarization 
phenomena what we observed. And the 
polarization selectivity at specific incident 
angle is the simple application for surface 
plasmon polariton. It can be as polarizers by 
perforated hole-arrays on aluminum films for 
display device application. We can change the 
maximum transmittance peak position by 
changing periodic hole-array pattern on metal 
films. The mainly transmittance enhancement 
are caused by surface plasmon polariton. 
We also demonstrate a simple method, 
which is combining modified illumination and 
defocus techniques in optical lithography to 
fabricate sub-wavelength antireflective 
structures for solar cells. Patterning the 
sub-wavelength texturing structures for 
eliminating the diffraction order light is 
important. Patterning sub-wavelength 
structures should use the short wavelength 
combining defocus exposure or using a 
suitable modified illumination exposure 
system. Results show the quadrupole 
illumination system with large process latitude 
is suitable for patterning sub-wavelength 
pyramid structures. This method is also 
suitable for the fabrication of antireflective 
structures on various kinds of solar cell 
materials.

技術特點 
本計畫利用控制表面電漿現象及光學微影製程中曝光系統的修正
式照明技巧，製作出最佳化薄膜結構。此方法的特色為省去傳統結
合乾式及濕式蝕刻的繁複步驟，並增加結構最佳化的可重複性。

可重複性之外，對於推動本技術至產業界將不會有任何量產上之障
礙。

射光穿透小於光波長的孔徑時，根據古典繞射理論，此時會有極低的穿
透率；而Ebbesen等人卻發現在奈米等級的尺度下，在金屬薄膜上製作奈
