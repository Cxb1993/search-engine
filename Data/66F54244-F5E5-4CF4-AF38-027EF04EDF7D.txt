軟性高分子電磁波遮蔽奈米複合材料之研究
The Preparation and Investigation of Flexible Polymeric
Nano-Composites for EMI Shielding
計畫編號：NSC 98-2221-E-036-001
執行期限：98 年 08 月 01 日至 99 年 07 月 31 日
主持人：黃繼遠 大同大學材料工程所
計畫參與人員：劉建偉、吳靜宜
中文摘要
本研究利用膨脹石墨及奈米碳黑雙導電填充材提升乙烯醋酸乙烯酯（Ethylene-vinyl acetate, EVA）
複材之抗電磁干擾能力，針對奈米石墨薄片進行酸洗、混煉剪切率、超音波處理、碳黑種類及鈦系偶
合劑來改善複材抗電磁干擾能力。並利用 4 點探針、掃描式電子顯微鏡、能量散佈光儀、X 光繞射儀、
網路分析儀、頻譜儀對複材進行相關分析。研究結果得知，奈米石墨薄片複材之滲濾值約是 40phr。
藉由酸洗純化來改善其導電性，提高混煉剪切率與超音波震盪來分離奈米石墨薄片，但仍無法提升複
材電磁波遮蔽值，故添加碳黑(XC-72 或 BP-2000)與石墨薄片間產生導電通路連結，其中以 XC-72 添加
20phr 即有 99.7﹪之入射能量遮蔽較佳，再利用 Lica 38 鈦系偶合劑進行表面處理，可降低碳黑顆粒間
作用力，使複材混煉扭矩下降約 2~3 N.m 並保持原有電磁波遮蔽值。本研究使 EG/EVA 複材 2D 導電通
路藉由添加碳黑而形成 3D 連續導電通路，以提升電磁波遮蔽值，從原本 8dB 提升至 40dB，並可達 10
﹪能量吸收。
關鍵詞：膨脹石墨、碳黑、可撓性、電磁波遮蔽效應、酸洗、偶合劑
Abstract
In this study, the conductive fillers of expanded graphite and nano carbon black (CB) were applied to
enhance the electron magnetic interference shielding effectiveness (EMI SE) for EVA (Ethylene vinyl acetate)
composites. Using acid purification of graphite nanoplatelets (GNP), blending of shear rate, ultrasound
treatment, CB type and titanate coupling agent for improving of the EMI SE composites. Analyses were by
4-point-probe method, SEM, EDS, XRD, VNA and EMI measurement, respectively. From results, the
percolation threshold value of GNPs in the conductive composite was about 40 phr. The acid purification of
GNP increased the conductivity and methods of shear rate at blending and ultrasound treatment separated
GNPs but composites still show a poor EMI SE, so we add CBs (XC-72 or BP-2000) to bridge GNPs in
conductive pathway of composite. The content XC-72 of 20phr was added into the composites of GNPs/EVA
in which the power of incident wave was shielded in 99.7% and then Lica 38 titanate coupling agent was used
the surface modification. It reduced the interforce between carbon black and the torque value decreased about
2~3 N.m. In this study, the 2D conductive network of EG/EVA composites were formed 3D conductive
network by adding CB and the EMI SE of composite enhanced from 8 to 40 dB remarkably and the power of
incident wave was absorbed in 10%.
Keywords: Expanded graphite; Carbon black; Flexible; Electron magnetic interference shielding effectiveness;
Acid purification; Coupling agent
1. Introduction
Electromagnetic interference (EMI) could be defined as electromagnetic radiation which is emitted by
electrical circuits carrying rapidly changing signals, as a by-product of their normal operation. These
undesirable EMI signals of interference or noise could cause (1) noise signal and even malfunction of the
electronic appliances and (2) irradiative damage of the human body [1-4]. The importance of EMI shielding
relates to the high demand of today’s society on the reliability of electronics and the rapid growth of radio
frequency radiation sources [5-8]. While metals have good EMI shielding properties, they have disadvantages
such as heavy weight, easy corrosion, and poor processing for shielding materials. Due to the desire for light
weight for EMI shielding systems, polymer-based conducting composites are increasingly attractive to
supplement the disadvantages of common metals for EMI shielding [9-13]. Graphite is a layered material
consisting of one-atom-thick sheets of carbon. The carbon atoms are bonded sp2 in a hexagonal arrangement

A three- dimensional network of conductive filler particles is needed. Two-dimensional (2D)
nanostructures such as GNP, have widely used filler in polymer matrix and it has been shown that they
provide high electrical conductivity, but it cannot build up a continues conductive network by melt blending
process, so the composites show a poor EMI SE. In order to solve this problem we used hybrid filler method
to decrease the volume resistance of composites and improve the EMI SE of composites. The idea of using
hybrid filler comprised of two or more traditional filler that improved composites performance can be
achieved by combining the advantages of each fillers [21]. In the literature [18], it mentioned that
nanopatrticle (like carbon black) arose easily form agglomerative nature resulted in the failure that
three-dimensional network of conductive filler particle was formed in the composites. Figure 5 shows the
SEM of EVA/EG composites. The GNP was formed as EG compounded with EVA. Furthermore, as CB was
added into EVA/EG (Figure 6) were clearly formed. It was because that the shear stress of processing was
increased with increasing CB content. In the case of hybrid filler the 0D-carbon black were bridging adjacent
2D-graphite nanoplatelet for a short distance electron jump between graphite nanoplatelets forming a 3D
conductive network and providing additional channels for the electron jump in the polymer matrix, decreasing
volume resistance and enhancing the EMI SE from 8 to 25dB(Figure 7).
(5) Processability
Figure 8 shows the processing torque value of various kinds of composites. It shows that the processing
torque value was increased with increasing CB content. It was because that the CB possesses large surface
area and increased the friction force as compounding. In contrast, the processing torque value was decreased
with increasing EG content. There are two reasons to cause this result. The first, the graphite acts as kind of
lubricant and offer lubricating property. The second, the EG formed GNP as EG compounded with EVA. The
lubricating efficiency of GNP was better than that of NG or CB as processing. Figure 9 shows the EMI SE of
various kinds of composites. The EMI SE of composites was only 10~15dB even though 60 phr EG was
added. The EMI SE of composites was up to about 25 dB if substitute CB for EG. It was because that the
distance between conductive fillers of CB was shorter than those of EG. However, the torque value of former
was low than that of latter. The EMI SE of EVA/EG/CB (100/20/40) composite was 35 dB. It was because
that the processing torque value of former was high than that of latter. The high shear stress caused the EG to
form GNP completely and the 3D conductive network was easy formed.
(6) Carbon black surface modification
The nano fillers is regular in shape and agglomerated due to interparticle force, which depend on filler
surface area. The agglomeration of filler causes the formation of air voids [22]. In the literature [23], it
mentioned that nanopatrticle (like nano carbon black) arose easily form agglomerative nature resulted in the
failure that three- dimensional network of conductive filler particle is formed in the composites. Figure 10
shows the SEM of CB with or without titanate couple agent treated. It was found that, after couple agent
treatment could reduce the agglomerative phenomenon. Figure 11 shows the processing the torque value of
various kind of composites. Fig4-36 shows that the processing torque value of EVA/EG/CB (100/20/40) was
higher than that of EVA/EG/CB (100/40/20). It was because that the EG content of former was lower that of
latter. On the other hand, the CB content of former was higher than that of latter. For these two reason, the
processing torque value of former was lower about 5~6N.m than that of latter. The processing torque of
composites could further decreased as the CB was treated by coupling agent. It was because that the CB could
be dispersed homogenously as CB was treated by coupling agent. And then the friction behavior could be
decreased. In Figure 12, the EMI SE of composites was increased about 5~10dB as the CB (BP-2000)
substituted for CB (XC-72). It was because that the particle size 15nm of BP-2000 was smaller than the
particle size (30nm) of XC-72. As the same amount of CB was added into EVA, the number of BP-2000 was
higher than xc-72. And then, the average distance between BP-2000 CB was shorter than those of XC-72.
