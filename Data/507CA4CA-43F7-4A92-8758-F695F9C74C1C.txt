一、中文摘要 
由於生活水準提高以及醫療衛生進步，使
得人類平均壽命不斷延長，台灣早已於 1993
年正式邁入高齡化社會，受到文化及經濟因素
的影響，獨居或老年夫妻同住的居住型態已逐
漸成為趨勢，因此銀髮族的日常生活照護，將
成為未來居家照護產業中最重要的一環。家事
服務是所有居家照護服務中使用頻率最高的
服務類型，顯見家事服務在銀髮族居家照護需
求的重要性，因此本研究將以家事服務中的烹
煮行為為研究標的，利用服務體驗工程方法，
從不同角度了解家事服務的供給及需求狀
況，進而發展出創新且符合銀髮族需求的家事
服務模型，以達到銀髮族自立生活的目標。 
關鍵詞：服務體驗工程、服務設計、家事服務、
銀髮族 
Abstract 
The purpose of this study is to design an 
innovative housework service system. The high 
rising living standards and advances in medical 
care have eventually increased the longevity of 
the Taiwanese population. Living alone or living 
with spouse only become the most concerning 
topic for arranging elderly people’s life in the 
future. Therefore, housework service becomes 
more and more popular for elderly people. This 
study used the service experience engineering 
(SEE) approach to find out the gap of 
housework service and design an innovative 
housework service model. Participants, 10 older 
adults and 3 enterprises, were invited in this 
research. The Service Quality Function 
Deployment (Service QFD) was developed as 
result of this study. 
Keywords: Service Experience Engineering 
(SEE), Service Design, Housework Service, 
Senior

四、結論與建議 
本研究利用服務體驗工程方法，透過服務
價值網絡與服務體驗需求洞察的方式，從不同
角度了解家事服務中，烹煮行為的供給與需求
的情況，如此不僅可以對家事服務與銀髮族需
求有更深一層的了解，還可以進一步設計出符
合銀髮族需求的創新服務，達到銀髮族自立生
活的目標，以提升銀髮族的生活品質。 
在實務建議上，當銀髮族進行烹煮行為
時，有許多潛在的需求，都可以提供產品設計
上的創新及改良；例如在菜餚保溫及防止燙傷
的需求部份，可以研發不易導熱材質的熱食盛
裝器皿，這樣不但可以改善容易燙傷的潛在危
險，也可以達到菜餚保溫的效果。在工具方
面，可以依照不同的功能性需求，設計出專屬
銀髮族的刀子、鍋子、碗盤等工具。在廚房的
設計上，也可以與室內設計業者合作，針對材
質、動線規畫做調整，更符合銀髮族的需要。

ABSTRACT 
The purpose of this paper is to design an innovative housework service system. The high rising living standards and 
advances in medical care have eventually increased the longevity of the Taiwanese population. Living alone or living 
with spouse only become the most concerning topic for arranging elderly people‟s life in the future. Therefore, 
housework service becomes more and more popular for elderly people. This study used the service experience 
engineering (SEE) approach to find out the gap of housework service and design an innovative housework service 
model. Participants, 7 older adults and 3 enterprises, were invited in this research. The Service Quality Function 
Deployment (Service QFD) was developed as result of this study. 
Keywords: Service Experience Engineering (SEE), Service design, housework service, senior citizens 
 
 
1. INTRODUCTION 
 
With the highly developed medical health 
technology, people are living longer. It‟s a trend for 
ageing population in the world. According to the 
statistics, Japanese aging index is no. 1 in 2007 
(176.92%), and then is German, 142.86%
（ Directorate-General of Budget, Accounting and 
Statistics, 2009）. Taiwanese aging index have become 
65.05% in the end of 2009. Although the index was less 
than other country, but the rate is foremost. 
Same as many other countries, changes in social and 
family structures in recent years have altered traditional 
living arrangement in Taiwan; living alone or living with 
spouse only become the most concerning topic for 
arranging elderly people‟s life in the future (Yang, et al., 
2008). Therefore, Aging in place becomes more and 
more popular for elderly people to choose their retiring 
life. In response to the trend of aging in place, 
understand the needs and characteristics of elderly 
people are very important. 
Aging in place provides two care services: home 
care service and community care service. The home care 
service helps seniors aging at home by providing care 
services to seniors‟ home. The personal care services to 
be delivered to seniors‟ home. The home care service 
provides the most convenient care and flexible living 
location for seniors. According to research data (Lin, 
2005), the housework frequency is the highest in home 
care service. Seniors use housework services 17 to 32 
hours a month (third times a week). It is quite obvious 
that senior is concerned about their live, food and 
environment problems. 
This research intends to design an innovative 
housework service model for seniors by using in-depth 
interview and service experience engineering (SEE) 
approach to understand seniors‟ housework requirements. 
Then, find related service providers from home care 
industry. Use in-depth interview to know supply-demand 
situation, discover the service gap and service issue.           
Finally, through service quality function 
deployment (Service QFD) method, this research 
provides a SEE-based housework service model               
 
2. METHODS 
 
According to Lin(2007), there are many difficulties 
in service innovation in Taiwan. For example, lack of 
research capability to discover customer‟s demands and 
lack of process or tools to assist developing and 
designing services are most serious problems in Taiwan‟s 
service industry. To solve this problem, Innovative 
DigiTech-Enabled Applications and Services Institute 
(IDEAS) introduced the Service Experience Engineering 
(SEE) methodology in 2007. SEE combined Fraunhofer 
IAO service engineering methods and American IDEO 
customer experience methods as fundamental skills, 
emphasis a systematic way for achieving experience 
innovation. 
As noted the research motivation in the study, it can 
be understood that housework service should need a 
systematic service system innovation model to 
improving the effectiveness of the housework service. In 
accordance with this view, an innovative model for 
housework service is developed in this study, integrated 
with the priority of the seniors‟ original requirements and 
SEE methodology. 
In general, the service experience engineering (SEE) 
approach uses models, methods and tools for design and 
development of services in a systematic way. Through 
exploring the needs and designing the services, SEE 
provides counseling services to industries and helps them 
to create and design new services. In the next section, the 
SEE methodology will be explained.

3.3 Service Design (Service Product Model) 
 
After confirm the demand and supply, this study use 
the Service QFD (Service Quality Function Deployment) 
method to form the innovative housework service model. 
Figure 2 presents Service QFD for Housework Service. 
As shown in the figure, the aim was to investigate 
the requirements of seniors in housework services and 
the services that service providers supplied in housework. 
The left-hand panel in the service QFD refers to the 
seniors‟ requirements for housework service.  
The right-hand panel in the service QFD refers to 
service providers competence in home care industry, the 
number in column means the strength of competence. 
The top panel refers to the exist services in 
housework service. 
The down panel refers to the innovative services in 
housework service. The center panel of the service QFD 
refers to the correlation factors in requirements and exist 
services. “Blank space” means no correlation, “1” means 
low correlation, “3”means median correlation and “9” 
means high correlation. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
  
4. CONCLUSION 
 
This study used Service Experience Engineering 
(SEE) approach to find out the gap of housework service 
and design an innovative housework service model. 
The results of this study not only can help service 
providers to design suitable service contents to serve 
seniors, but also can help service providers to build up 
feasible business model for the aging future.
