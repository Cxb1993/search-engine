摘    要 
    本實驗係利用添加導電劑(銅)於鐵電極中，利用建立一導電網路以增進其電池性能。實驗中合成
鐵外部包覆銅的鐵/銅複合物電極以增進鐵微粒之利用率，其循環穩定電容量可達到800 mAh/g-Fe。 
    鐵電極自放電研究方面，除了利用添加具有較大氫氣超電位的銅以延緩自放電之外，亦選擇利用
添加緩蝕劑的方式，選擇(CH3CH2CH2)4NBr、Na2MoO4‧2H2O、Na2HPO4、Na2Si3O7、EDTA、Cr2O3、
NaNO2 等添加物添加於鐵電極中，添加物會與鐵微粒產生鍵結以減緩鐵電極自發性的腐蝕反應，進而
改善自放電。 
    實驗中亦以固定鐵重量只改變添加的銅含量之方式，作不同鐵銅重量比之循環穩定度與大電流測
試，其中，當鐵銅重量比為三比一時，具有最佳之循環電容量(800 mAh/g-Fe)，大電流測試方面，放
電密度分別為 0.5 C(200 mAh/g-Fe)、1 C(400 mAh/g-Fe)、2 C(800 mAh/g-Fe)、4 C(1600 mAh/g-Fe)、8 
C(3200 mAh/g-Fe)與 9.6 C(3840 mAh/g-Fe)，當鐵銅重量比為二比一時，其大電流放電測試之電池性能
最佳，其第一階段放電電容量皆可維持約 400 mAh/g-Fe。 
目前本實驗室利用奈米鐵微粒於製備鎳鐵電池上之電容量高達 800 mAh/g，則能量密度可達 95 
Wh/kg 以上，鎳鐵電池性能與鎳氫電池不相上下，而成本卻大幅降低，該能量密度與鋰錳化合物鋰電
池相同，為一般鉛酸電池的三倍，十分適合作為輕型電動交通工具使用，而成本的優勢則非鋰離子電
池所能比擬，期望鎳鐵電池能為電動車產業開闢新的契機。  
 
Abstract 
In this study, copper was added to the iron electrode to enhance the electrochemical performance by 
forming a strong conductivity net. The Cu-coated Fe composites complex electrode synthesized exhibited 
the highest capacity about 800 mAh/g-Fe. 
In the study of self-discharge, we used copper additive with larger hydrogen overpotential to reduce the 
self-discharge of iron electrode. We also used corrosion inhibitor to retard the corrosion of iron electrode via 
bonding with iron particles. The corrosion inhibitor included (CH3CH2CH2)4NBr, Na2MoO4‧2H2O, 
Na2HPO4, Na2Si3O7, EDTA, Cr2O3, and NaNO2 in this study. 
We also studied the cycle stability and high C-rate performance of iron electrode with different weight 
ratios of iron and copper. It gave better cycle capacity about 800 mAh/g-Fe as weight ratio of iron and 
copper were three. We examined with 0.5 C(200 mAh/g-Fe), 1 C(400 mAh/g-Fe), 2 C(800 mAh/g-Fe), 4 
C(1600 mAh/g-Fe), 8 C(3200 mAh/g-Fe), and 9.6 C(3840 mAh/g-Fe) in high C-rate test. The first discharge 
capacity was 400 mAh/g-Fe of nickel-iron battery in high C-rate test showed the best performance as weight 
ratio of iron and copper were two. 
    At present, the capacity of nickel-iron battery using nano-Fe particles as the iron electrode could deliver 
about 800 mAh/g, i.e. up to 95 Wh/kg in energy density. The nickel-iron battery was comparable to those of

第二章    研究目的 
目前大型電池研究仍面臨困境，再檢視可能的能源材料仍有其必要性，而且大型電池需使用大量
材料，從地球資源蘊藏角度來看，唯有使用資源豐富的材料才不會因大量使用而發生短缺問題。目前
油電混合車多以鎳氫電池來儲存電力，而鎳鐵電池之負極使用鐵，其在資源、成本與安全性上有更大
的優勢，此外鐵電極還有：循環壽命長、耐過度充電與過度放電、無污染等優點。過去由於鐵電極的
容量不高使得整體電池能量密度僅約 50Wh/kg，功率密度僅約 100W/kg，再加上自放電較大，在 70年
代之後漸漸被其他電池所取代。由鐵電極過去的發展歷史來看，容量與放電功率低為最主要的缺點，
這部分應能藉由增大反應表面積獲得改善，因此我們研究的的方向為製作高表面積之活性鐵，同時亦
與其他導體材料共同形成複合微粒以維持較佳之循環壽命。

首先，觀察兩種電極材料之初始狀態可知鐵微粒表面較光滑，粒徑約為 80-150 nm，而鐵/銅複合活性
材料表面較為粗糙，這是因為銅是以置換方式沉澱於鐵微粒外表面所致，與釋迦外貌相似，另外，銅
/鐵複合物的粒徑較鐵微粒大，約為 100-250 nm，因為鐵被銅置換後不只會形成鐵離子而溶於溶液中，
也會以氫氧化物之形式存在，因此造成鐵/銅複合活性材料之粒徑較大，另外，製備鐵/銅複合活性材
料之實驗步驟最後係再以過量的硼氫化鈉還原氧化的鐵與少量未反應的銅離子而生成較小的顆粒，因
此鐵/銅複合物的粒徑分佈也較廣。

    圖 10 為奈米鐵微粒於放電態與充電態之循環伏安測試圖，皆顯示兩明顯之氧化還原對，分別標

Ox2/Red2 對應 Fe(OH)2 氧化為 Fe(III)，即鐵電極進行第二階段放電反應[Caldas et al.,1998][Ujimine et

    比較鐵電極放電態與充電態之循環伏安測試圖可發現，收集放電態之產物其還原峰(Red1與Red2)
較充電態之產物明顯，線下面積也相對較大，因其一開始測試為鐵氧化物，即 Fe(OH)2 或 Fe(III)，因
此其能還原成零價 Fe 的量較多，相反地，若一開始收集的為充電態產物，則其氧化峰(Ox1 與 Ox2)
較明顯。 
    除此之外，隨著充放電次數增加，對應之氧化峰與還原峰(ΔE)則相差越遠，代表於充放電進行時，
電池內之阻抗增加，反應趨向不可逆，由先前 X 光繞射圖得知，放電會產生 Fe(OH)2，而增加阻抗之
原因應為 Fe(OH)2 為絕緣材料，散佈於導電之鐵微粒周圍而降低電極之導電度，產生失效的 Fe 或
Fe(OH)2 而致，因此電池極化使得電池電容量迅速下降。 
    圖 11 為鐵/銅複合材料(鐵銅重量比為三比一)於放電態與充電態之循環伏安測試圖，圖中表現出

現兩還原峰(Red1 與 Red2)，即 Fe(OH)2 還原回 Fe 與 Fe3O4 還原回 Fe(OH)2，這可能是因為銅之還原

第六章    結論 
1. 由於銅具有導電與保護鐵微粒之作用，因此添加銅於鐵電極中可改善電池性能，其中以鐵外部包
覆銅之電容量較高，自放電也較慢，鐵銅重量比為三比一時電池性能最好，而鐵銅重量比為二比
一時，大電流放電結果較佳。  
2. 由XRD得知鐵/銅複合物電極的放電產物為Fe3O4，而鐵電極的Fe3O4量相對較少，主要組成為
Fe(OH)2。 
3. 由SEM觀察鐵電極添加金屬銅後，鐵/銅複合物電極顆粒成長較慢，因此銅可能扮演鐵進行溶解
再結晶時的成核點，使電容量較為穩定，另外，銅亦增加了電極之導電性，提升電極之電容量。  
4. 由CV曲線相比發現，隨著充放電次數增多，電極趨向不可逆，與SEM和XRD得到的Fe(OH)2及
Fe3O4失效有關。但鐵/銅複合物電極相對於鐵電極之可逆性較佳。  
5. 自放電改善方面，緩蝕劑能改善自放電，但幅度不大，包含Na2MoO4˙2H2O與(CH3CH2CH2)4NBr。

2. Experimental 
 
2.1 Synthesis of nanosized iron/copper material 
Chemical reduction method was used to synthesize nanosized iron particles and iron/copper composites 
by sodium borohydride (NaBH4). Ferrous sulfate hydrate (FeSO4·7H2O, Showa, Japan) of 0.025 mole was 
first dissolved in 200 ml D.I. water, followed by a dissolution of 0.05 mole of NaBH4 (Lancaster, England) 
in 50 ml D.I. water. The FeSO4 solution was then pumped into the ice-bathed NaBH4 solution at a rate of 3 
ml min
−1
. Here iron nanoparticles would be obtained, and they were then washed by hot water (>90℃) and 
separated by a magnet three times. The obtained nanosized iron particles were dispersed again in 300 ml D.I 
water under an ice bath. Copper sulfate hydrate (CuSO4·5H2O, Showa, Japan) of 0.009 mole (Fe:Cu = 3:1, 
in weight ratio) was dissolved in 200 ml D.I. water, and then pumped into the nanosized iron particle 
solution at a rate of 3 ml min
-1
. After that, the NaBH4 solution was then pumped into above solution at 3 ml 
min
−1
 to reduce all residual iron ions into nanoparticles. Finally, the as-prepared samples were washed by hot 
water (>90 ◦C) and separated by a magnet several times to obtain iron/copper composites. Different amounts 
of copper sulfate were tested in this study.

copper precipitates formed. The particles of the pure iron system had a smooth surface and sizes of about 
80-150 nm, while those of the iron/copper composite system had rough surface and slightly large sizes of 
100 to 250nm.

4. Conclusions 
     
An anode material made up of an iron/copper composite by a chemical reduction method is successfully 
demonstrated to be highly reversible in this work. The copper nanoparticles in the composite construct a 
stable network to transfer electrons during the reduction/oxidation of the iron electrode. It therefore helps to 
stop the quick drop of electrical capacity due to the formation of non-conductive Fe(OH)2 from iron particles. 
When the copper addition is 1/3 (weight ratio) of iron, the composite electrode delivers a stable capacity of 
about 800 mAhg
-1
 (Fe) at a current of 200 mAg
-1
. When the copper addition is 1/2 that of iron, the electrode 
can still deliver 750 mAhg
-1
 at a very large current of 3200 mAg
-1
. This fact thus opens the possibility of 
future applications requiring large current outputs.

Manufacturing method of a battery electrode
國立清華大學 周更生,高振裕
本發明係一種電池電極材料製作方法，包含提供還原劑、提供導電助劑，導電
助劑係選自由金屬鹽類、金屬微粒、金屬化合物及含碳導電物質所組成之群組
其中之一；提供含鐵離子溶液，並將該導電助劑加入含鐵離子溶液形成第一混
合溶液；將第一混合溶液與還原劑混合形成第二混合溶液，其中導電助劑及含
鐵離子係經還原劑還原形成複合微粒，複合微粒包含有鐵微粒；自第二混合溶
液分離出複合微粒；提供黏結劑，將複合微粒與該黏結劑混合後形成塗料，以
及將塗料塗布於金屬網以製成電極。
This invention discloses a manufacturing method of a battery
electrode. The method comprises the following steps: Providing a
reducing reagent, a conductive adjuvant, and a solution comprising
ferric ion. The conductive adjuvant is selected from the group
consisting of a metallic salt, a metal particle, a metal compound and
a carbon conductive substance. Applying the conductive adjuvant into
the solution comprising ferric iron to from a first mixture solution,
followed by mixing the first mixture solution with the reducing
reagent to from a second mixture solution. The conductive adjuvant
and the ferric ion are reduced by the reducing reagent to from a
composite micro-particle comprising iron micro-particle. Isolating
the composite micro-particle from the second mixture solution.
