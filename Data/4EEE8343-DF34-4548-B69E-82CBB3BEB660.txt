英文摘要 
This project is divided into three parts. First, we investigate the whispering-gallery modes of 
Si microspheres and the fabrication of Si microspheres. Secondly, we report electroluminescence 
from ZnO nanoparticle-based devices prepared by the dry-coating method. Third, we demonstrate 
the fabrication of the Si nanowire/polymer solar cells. 
In the beginning, the resonant characteristics of Si micropheres are analyzed. The refractive 
index of Si is higher than that of silica. This leads to a large angle of total reflection at the Si/air 
interface. To achieve the same Q-factor, the size of Si microspheres is smaller than that of silica 
microspheres. The small size causes it suitable to be integrated into the high density 
optoelectronic circuits in the future. In this work, we theoretically analyze the sharp resonances 
and resonance shifts in very small silicon microspheres. To fabricate Si microspheres, the Si rods 
of different diameters were illuminated by high energy pulsed laser. The melt silicon rods could 
form spheres due to the surface tension. The Si would re-crystallize to form Si spheres. 
Experimental results show that single crystalline of Si with lattice plane spacing of about 0.24 nm 
was formed after the transformation of the Si rod into the sphere. It gives promise for integration 
in high density optical circuits. In addition, high quality factor of silicon spheres might be applied 
in bio- or chemical sensor and optical communications.  
Next, we analyze the optoelectronic properties of ZnO. Zinc oxide (ZnO) is a promising 
semiconductor with a wide direct band gap of 3.37 eV. Due to its large exciton binding energy 
(60 meV), the excitons in ZnO are thermally stable at room temperature, which is a significant 
advantage for ultraviolet (UV)-blue light-emitting devices. It is suitable to integrate with 
silicon-based optoelectronics devices, and it is potential to produce high-efficiency ZnO 
electroluminescence (EL) devices in silicon-based material. 
Finally, the combination of Si nanowires and conducting polymer was investigated. The Si 
nanowires and polymer form heterojunction devices. We investigated the current-voltage 
characteristics. The silicon nanowires and polymer heterojunction shows good diode behavior. 
The well-aligned Si nanowires were coated with a polymer thin film by using capillary 
mechanism. The Si nanowires and polymer formed core-sheath structures. The structure can 
collect carriers efficiently. Thus, it is suitable for photo-detection and photovoltaic applications. 
The power conversion efficiency of the silicon nanowire solar cells achieves 6.51%.

三、研究方法 
A、矽球共振腔 
矽材料的能階為 1.1eV，因此在紫外光的吸收係數極大，利用這一點，我們使用高能量
脈衝式雷射來使矽熔化，高能量雷射光會在矽材料中轉換為熱能，熱能便可以使矽熔化，
而熔融態的矽會因為表面張力，而使矽形狀產生改變，利用這個物理機制，我們將可以使
矽的柱狀結構熔化重新結晶成球形結構。製程方式如下，第一步，利用電子束微影設計不
同尺寸的圓形結構，蒸鍍鉻在圓形結構之中，將光阻移除留下圓形的鉻遮罩。第二，將表
面有鉻遮罩的矽晶圓(silicon-on-insulator)用乾蝕刻的方式蝕刻矽，蝕刻成不同直徑的矽柱狀
結構。第三，使用波長 248 nm 的短脈衝雷射照射矽柱狀結構，雷射光學架設如圖一，照射
在試片上為均勻的雷射光，試片放至於真空腔體中，照射雷射一發就可以形成矽微米球結
構。利用電子顯微鏡觀察矽球共振腔結構，並以高解析穿透式電子顯微鏡觀察其結晶特性。

四、結果與討論 
A、矽球共振腔 
為了計算矽微米球的共振模態，我們引用了 Maxwell equation 來解球形共振腔結構的
模態。假設矽的折射係數為 3.4，且放置在真空中，真空折射率為 1。解得矽微米球的第一
個模態的 a/λ(半徑/共振波長)為 0.141，當 a/λ值增加時，共振模態跟著線性增加，圖五中的
黑色曲線每個角動量量子數(l)下第一個模態的 a/λ值，隨著角動量量子數呈現線性的增加趨
勢，每個角動量量子數的模態也跟著增加。在第一個模態中，以通訊波長1.55 um為例，silicon 
microsphere 的半徑為 0.218 um，這個尺寸的 silicon microsphere 遠小於文獻上的 silica 
microsphere [7]，其原因在於 silicon 的折射率為 3.4，而 silica 約為 1.45，這個差異使得 silicon 
microsphere 在尺寸很小的時候就有共振模態產生。但是這裡要考慮到 silicon microphere 的
Q 值，尺寸再小的 silicon microsphere 若沒有相對應的高 Q 值，將沒有辦法應用。 
Q 值對於設計 silicon microsphere 是很重要的參數，Q 值的取法是取頻譜的半高寬
(Q=λ/Δλ)，不同的模態會對應到不同的 Q 值。我們計算了 Q 值隨 a/λ 變化的趨勢，發現在
第一個模態的 Q 值只有 10，在這麼低的 Q 值下，silicn microsphere 的應用會相當困難。隨
著 a/λ 值的增加，Q 值會急劇的增加，如圖六所示 Q 值接近指數函數的增加趨勢，在 a/λ
為 1.06 的時候 Q 值增加到 4×1012。當 a/λ超過 1.06 的時候 Q 值不再增加，其原因在於矽之
中的自由載子吸收效應(free carrier absorption)，這會造成光的損耗，使得 silicon microsphere
的 Q 值會有極限，但是在這麼高 Q 值下，其應用已經相當的足夠，下面我們將展示在這 Q
值下對於外界折射率變化的靈敏度。在 a/λ為 1.06 之下，波長 1.55 μm 時 silicon microsphere
的半徑為 1.64 μm，相較之下文獻中的 silica microsphere 的半徑要大於 20 μm 才能達到極高
的 Q 值。 
Silicon microsphere 藉由對外界折射率對共振模態的變化將可以作為生物或化學感測
器，或者光調變器以及可調波長的濾波器。Silicon microsphere 因為 WGM 而對表面的折射
率變化很靈敏，折射率變化將會使共振模態產生位移，而這個位移的最小值取決於頻譜的
半高寬，也就是 Q 值的大小，我們將計算外界最小的折射率變化使頻譜移動其半高寬的距
離。如圖七所示，最小折射率的變化將隨 a/λ減少，在 a/λ為 1.06 的時候，最小的折射率變
化為 1.2×10-12。這種微小的折射率變化在未來將可以作為生物或化學感測器，可以測量到
極微量的化學物質。

五、計畫結果自評 
關於矽微米球的共振模態，我們來解得球形共振腔結構的模態，在 a/λ 為 1.06 的時候
品質因數可高達 4×1012，而最小的折射率變化為 1.2×10-12。這種微小的折射率變化在未來
將可以作為生物或化學感測器，這個特點將可以應用到偵測微量的氣體或溶液中的分子
等，甚至是分辨單一分子的特性。 
利用雷射退火方式我們製作了矽球共振腔結構，其結構擁有很好的結晶特性，目前尚
未有研究人員利用此方法製作矽球形共振腔，在前面我們曾經用理論計算球形共振腔品質
因數(Q-factor)，發現矽球形結構有很高的 Q 值，高的 Q 值對於光有很高的靈敏度，未來將
應用這種矽球形結構於光電感測元件上。除了光電感測方面，此球形結構的製程與一般半
導體製程接近，將有可能結合於光電積體電路中，可以達到很高的元件密度。 
另一方面，我們利用乾式塗佈法製作單層氧化鋅奈米粒子電激發光元件,單層的氧化鋅
奈米粒子可降低因奈米粒子堆疊的孔洞缺陷,以提高氧化鋅奈米粒子的發光效率,在實驗中
分別使用二種不同的電洞傳輸層 PF與 PVK及電子傳輸層Alq3,並以掃描電子顯微鏡觀察氧
化鋅奈米粒子的單層結構,並以電流-電壓特性量測無電子傳輸層的元件及有電子傳輸層的

rough surface is dissolved to be liquid by absorbing energy of the laser, 
and then is becoming smooth by surface tension of the surface. When 
the surface is cooled to be solid, a smooth surface of the optical device 
is formed. The invention can smoothen a rough surface of an optical 
device to increase the resonating and wave guiding effect.

英文：  
The present invention generally relates to a method for maintaining 
a smooth surface of material, and more particularly to a method for 
maintaining a smooth surface of crystallizable material. 
Generally speaking, in the forming process of crystallizable 
materials, it is common to form a seed layer which is used as a crystal 
growth center. Then, a target crystallizable material is further formed 
based on the seed layer. The materials of the seed layer and the target 
crystallizable material can be the same or different. 
The dimension of the zinc oxide nanorods is related to the 
crystallization of the zinc oxide seed layer. When the grain size of the 
zinc oxide seed layer is larger, coarse zinc oxide nanorods can be 
formed; when the grain size of the zinc oxide seed layer is smaller, 
smaller zinc oxide nanorods can be formed. In order to adjust the grain 
size of the zinc oxide seed layer, an annealing treatment can be 
implemented to the zinc oxide seed layer for adjusting the 
crystallization of the zinc oxide seed layer. When the temperature of 
the annealing treatment is higher, the grain size of the zinc oxide seed 
layer is bigger; when the temperature of the annealing treatment is 
lower, the grain size of the zinc oxide seed layer is smaller. By 
controlling the temperature of the annealing treatment, the dimension 
of the zinc oxide nanorods can be controlled thereby. 
However, when the temperature of the annealing treatment is 
higher, the grain size of the zinc oxide seed layer is larger, leading to 
the rough and uneven surface of the zinc oxide seed layer. The 
subsequently formed zinc oxide nanorods will not grow towards 
substantially the same direction and will not form ordered array. 
For the disadvantages of the prior art mentioned above, there is still 
a need to provide a method for maintaining a smooth surface of 
material. The surface of material can maintain smooth after the 
annealing treatment. High quality nano-structure can be formed by 
using the smooth surface of material as a crystal growth center.

Jing-Shun Huang1, Chen-Yu Chou1, Kao-Hua Tsai1, Chung-Hao Wu3, Meng-Yueh Liu1, Yu-Hung Lin1, Wen-Hen Lin1, 
and Ching-Fuh Lin1,2,3,a)

a)Corresponding author. Tel: 886-2-3366 3540. FAX: 886-2-2364 2603. Electronic mail: cflin@cc.e.ntu.edu.tw 
Abstract 
Organic/inorganic hybrid solar cells are attractive due to the great potential for large-area, 
light-weight, flexible, and low-cost devices. The performance of the polymer/ZnO nanorod hybrid 
solar cells based on poly(3-hexylthiophene) and methanofullerenes is improved with the enhanced 
optical absorption by increasing the thickness of the photoactive layer and introducing a 
solution-processed interlayer. The dependence of the optical absorption on the thickness of the 
photoactive layer is studied as a function of the spin-coating rate, which controls the time taken by 
the solvent to dry after the spin-coating process. With the slower spin-coating rate, the photoactive 
layer is thicker, and the polymer chains have longer time to self-organize and more effectively 
infiltrate into ZnO nanorod spacing. In addition, a solution-processed vanadium oxide interlayer 
between the photoactive layer and Ag-electrode is introduced to modify the anode. With this 
interlayer, the optical absorption of the photoactive layer increases due to the optical-spacer effect. 
Our investigations show that the power conversion efficiency (PCE) is improved from 1.6 % to 2.7 
% with the thickness of the photoactive layer from 240 nm to 350 nm by slowing the spin coating 
rate of the photoactive layer. This method improves the infiltration of the photoactive layer into 
ZnO nanorod spacing, resulting in the higher crystallinity of the polymer and light harvesting 
without sacrificing the carrier transportation. Moreover, the PCE is further improved to 3.5 % by 
the vanadium oxide interlayer. We also demonstrate the devices fabricated on flexible substrates 
(polyester) with a PCE of 3.6 % and a fill factor of 0.63.

3. Conclusion 
 Efficient inverted PSCs with paired solution-processed modifications are demonstrated by utilizing the metal 
oxides near the anode and cathode. The pair of metal oxides can reduce the leakage current and guide the carriers to

To compare the reflectance of SiNWs fabricated with and without a pre-surface treatment, the solar-weighted 
reflectance of SiNWs was estimated as shown in Figure 3. The solar-weighted reflectance of the single-polished 
silicon wafer is about 40%. As SiNWs formed on the silicon surface, the solar-weighted reflectance greatly 
decreases. At the same SiNW length, the SiNWs fabricated with a pre-surface treatment reveal lower 
solar-weighted reflectance than those fabricated without a pre-surface treatment. It has a solar-weighted 
reflectance of 3.3% for a length of 0.87µm. To achieve the same reflectance, the SiNWs fabricated without a 
pre-surface treatment should be increased in length to 1.812µm. The result indicates that the density of SiNWs 
with sub-micrometer lengths predominantly influences the reflection. The antireflection effect of SiNWs is due 
to the multiple scattering of incident light between SiNWs. The multiple scattering enhances the absorption of 
light in materials, leading to suppression of reflection. As the length of SiNWs decreases, the scattering between 
SiNWs will decrease and the reflection will increase. However, if the density of SiNWs increases, the scattering 
between SiNWs with sub-micrometer lengths can be enhanced and the reflection can be reduced significantly to 
as low as that in the SiNWs with length of above micrometers. 
We demonstrate the strong antireflection properties of SiNWs with sub-micrometer lengths fabricated by 
metal-assisted etching. A silicon surface treatment before metal-assisted etching increases the density of silicon 
nanowires. The improvement reduces the solar-weighted reflectance to as low as 3.3% for silicon nanowires 
with a length of only 0.87µm. In comparison, the silicon nanowires fabricated without a pre-surface treatment 
have to be as long as 1.812µm to achieve the same reflectance.

The solar cell performance under AM1.5 global one sun (100 mW cm-2) is measured with a solar simulator 
(SUN 2000, Abet Technologies Inc.) at 25 °C. The photovoltaic characteristics of hybrid cells, including open 
circuit voltage (Voc), short circuit current density (Jsc), series resistance (Rs), shunt resistance (Rsh), fill factor 
(FF) and power conversion efficiency (PCE) are presented in Table 1. The use of GaAs NWs in hybrid cells 
could provide a larger area of p-n junctions and shorten exciton diffusion distance, and therefore greatly increase 
exciton dissociating probability. Compared to the planar hybrid cells, the PCE improves from 0.13 % to 3.46 %. 
In addition, the length of GaAs NWs also greatly influences the optical properties of GaAs NW arrays. Stronger 
light trapping effect induced in longer GaAs NW arrays is expected. Therefore, the PCE of hybrid cells could 
increase as the increase of GaAs NW length. As shown in Table 1, the Jsc of GaAs NW/PEDOT:PSS cells 
increases from 7.80 mA cm-2 to 10.24 mA cm-2 as the length of GaAs NWs increases from 270 nm to 820 nm. 
Thus, the PCE increases from 2.44 % to 3.46 %.
