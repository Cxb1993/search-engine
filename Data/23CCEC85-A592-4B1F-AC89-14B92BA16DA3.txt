內含奈米結構陣列之太空用高效率汽化液體式微推進器之研製 
Fabrication and Study of Nanostructures Array Based High Efficiency 
Vaporizing Liquid Microthruster for Space 
計畫編號：NSC 98-2221-E-224-076- 
執行期間：98 年 8 月 1 日至 99 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：劉建惟 國立雲林科技大學機械工程系暨研究所 
E-mail: liucw@yuntech.edu.tw 
 
Abstract 
In this study, the MEMS space 
microthruster grown with silicon nanowires 
(SiNWs) on the surface of reaction chamber was 
fabricated and proposed. From the experimental 
result, the present work can readily occur the 
fully liquid vaporizing under a heating 
temperature of 200 oC and a DI water flow rate 
of 0.25 cc/min. This is due to the high specific 
surface area, nano-porous structure and 
hydrophobic surface of SiNWs can rapidly and 
uniformly generate a large amount of vaporized 
air bubbles and effectively transfer the heat from 
the heating chamber to DI water flow to enhance 
the boiling of SiNWs based MEMS 
microthruster. 
 
Keywords: MEMS, space microthruster, silicon 
nanowires (SiNWs), liquid vaporizing, boiling 
 
中文摘要 
本研究已成功完成太空用微機電微推進
器之反應腔體內壁含有矽奈米線陣列之晶片
研製，並進行初步測試。實驗結果顯示，本研
製之微推進器晶片能夠容易於 200 oC 加熱溫
度及 0.25 cc/min 去離水流量的條件下產生完
全液體汽化的現象。此一完全汽化液體之現象
成因與微推進器腔體內壁的矽奈米線陣列有
關。由於矽奈米線陣列具有高比表面積及疏水
等特性，且其表面形貌類似奈米多孔性結構，
因此可大大增加去離子的吸熱面積，能夠迅
速、均勻且高效率地將微推進器腔體底部表面
的熱量傳給去離水，使去離水迅速受熱，增強
反應腔體內的沸騰，而產生大量細小的熱氣
泡，甚至達到完全汽化液體。 
 
關鍵字：微機電，太空微推進器，矽奈米線，
汽化液體，沸騰 
 
1. Introduction 
In the recent years, there has been a 
growing interest in micropropulsion due to the 
decreasing launch costs, decreasing flight times, 
and increasing mission reliability [1-9]. 
Therefore, MEMS is the key technology to 
develop the complex miniature instruments, 
components, and sensors required for 
micro-spacecraft. The vaporizing liquid 
microthruster is very suitable developed for 
space micropropulsion application due to its 
many advantages of simple operation, no fuel 
aging problem, high device reliability, 
environmentally friendly nature and safety. 
However, up to now, in many research groups, 
the vaporizing liquid microthrusters developed 
do not provide a well- or fully-vaporized liquid 
design to be easily operated at a low heating 
temperature (< 200oC) or a low heating power 
condition. The thrust output of vaporizing liquid 
microthrusters demonstrated and proposed in the 
literatures is still lower [1, 2]. Therefore, how to 
effectively enhance the liquid vaporizing in the 
chamber of microthruster to obtain a higher 
thrust output is a very important research and 
development topic. In many works done for the 
effective boiling enhancement by surface 
modification and micro/nano-strucrures [10-13]. 
Therefore, this study is to fabricate and study the 
silicon nanostructures array based high 
efficiency vaporizing liquid microthruster for 
space applications.

However, under a wall heating temperature of

disappeared as shown in Fig. 6(b) and 6(d).  
The stronger hydrophobicity of SiNWs 
surface inside the chamber can obtain a well- or 
fully-vaporized DI water with a lower wall 
heating temperature. The SiNWs surface with a 
very high specific surface area can transfer a 
large amount of heat from the bottom heating 
wall of chamber to the DI water rapidly and 
uniformly. Next, a large amount of vaporized 
boiling air bubbles are formed and grown rapidly. 
Then, the vaporized air bubbles are separated 
from the stronger hydrophobic surface of SiNWs 
surface to cause a well or fully liquid vaporizing 
phenomenon.  
 
4. Conclusion 
In this study, the MEMS space 
microthruster grown with silicon nanowires 
(SiNWs) on the surface of vaporizing chamber 
was fabricated and proposed. From the result, the 
present work can readily occur a well liquid 
vaporizing under a heating temperature of 200 
oC and a DI water flow rate of 0.25 cc/min. This 
is due to the high specific surface area and 
stronger hydrophobicity surface of SiNWs can 
rapidly and uniformly generate a large amount of 
vaporized air bubbles and effectively transfer a 
large amount of heat from the heating chamber 
wall to DI water flow to enhance the boiling of 
SiNWs based MEMS microthruster.

二、與會心得 
目前本研究小組正積極Advanced boiling enhancement technology與Advanced solar 
cells等技術的研發。對於如何改善太空用汽化液體式微推進器之效率與其相關可應用之
技術乃為下階段的研究重點。目前本研發之微推進器乃利用熱源加熱去離子水，使其沸
騰蒸發為熱汽方式產生推力，然而在太空中如何得到額外的加熱源是個棘手問題。本研
究小組目前考慮以太陽能加熱或是太陽能發電使微加熱器加熱的方式進行。另外，根據
本研究目前的研究結果顯示利用奈米線結構輔助增強蒸發腔體內壁水沸騰的效果十分
不錯，將可應用於太陽能加熱之應用，因此參與本次會議最主要的目的就是進行Solar 
thermal與Photovoltaic的技術訊息收集。 
Solar thermal部分，本會議的論文多以太陽能加熱系統或熱幫浦(Heat pump)為主，
較少針對吸熱材料的研究論文發表。太陽能加熱系統方面則大多針對太陽能聚光的設計
做最佳化研究。另外，觀察相關於Photovoltaic主題的論文，歸納發現多以CIGS太陽能
電池為主，結晶矽太陽能電池次之。CIGS太陽能電池為目前薄膜型太陽能電池效率最高
的結構，電池效率約達18%，這部份國內的TSMC亦積極投產中，較具經濟價值。論文
中較多針對CIGS四元材料的製作配方做討論，此也是影響電池效率的最關鍵。結晶矽太
陽能電池中則以日本SANYO公司的HIT太陽能電池為市售中最高電池效率，約23%。本
會議中SANYO公司也針對薄型結晶矽太陽能電池製造時所面臨的電池翹曲問題作研
究，如圖五所示。經過三明治薄膜結構改良可有效抑制薄膜結晶矽電池於製造時的翹曲
問題。III-V太陽能電池則以多層堆疊型結構(Tandem structure)及聚光型電池研發為主。 
本會議經驗對本人確實受益良多，也收集到不少新的技術資訊，特別在Photovoltaic
技術發展方面。本會議除了進行學術交流，獲得許多相關Renewable Energy的研究知識
與國際間各國的發展訊息外，也有很大的感觸，發現世界上主要的科技研究仍多集中在
美、歐、日等區域，目前亞洲地區已成為世界的最大需求市場，如何急起直追世界各國
之研究技術，創造研發出更多的經濟產值乃是當務之急。本次利用出席國際會議的機會
發現本研究小組目前正在積極發展的Advanced boiling enhancement technology與
Advanced solar cells等技術方向十分符合國際間目前的研究趨勢，因此將彙整本會議所收
集到的重要技術訊息來作為未來本小組的研發目標。
