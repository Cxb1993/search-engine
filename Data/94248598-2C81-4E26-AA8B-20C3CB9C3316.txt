In this project the inverse scattering 
problem for recovering the shapes of multiple
conducting cylinders with the immersed 
targets by genetic algorithm. Two or more 
separate perfect-conducting cylinders of 
unknown shapes are buried in one half space. 
Based on the boundary condition and the 
measured scattered field, a set of nonlinear 
integral equations are derived, and the 
electromagnetic imaging problem is 
reformulated into an optimization problem. 
The improved steady state genetic algorithm is 
used to find out the global extreme solution. 
Finally, the original images are compared with 
the reconstructed images to verify the 
reconstructed quality.
