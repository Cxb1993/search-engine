（一）計畫中文摘要。 
關鍵詞：介電常數、介電損耗、微波測量、混合定律。 
本研究主要在探討不同物質混合後在微波頻率之介電性質（介電常數與介電損耗）特性
與介電性質之混合定律（mixture rules）間之相互關係。並將研究之結果應用於微波介電性質
之量測上。 
現有之微波介電性質測量技術主要在針對整塊樣品，對於粉末樣品之量測方法較為付之
闕如，本計畫擬經由將欲量測之樣本粉末與 polyethylene (PE)粉末混合後，由於 PE之低溶點，
可利用高溫將混合之粉末溶鑄成為一整塊之 composite 樣本以利於量測。由不同成份百分比
之 composite樣本之介電性質進而推論出百分之百成份之欲量測之粉末樣本之微波介電性質。 
量測出之不同百分比的混合樣本之介電性質將應用十種常見之 mixture rules，並以外插
法得出欲量測粉末之介電性質，並試圖從現有理論或發展出新的理論觀點來解釋其結果。並
擬將粉末與整塊陶瓷樣品之介電性質做比較，以發現粉末之介電性質與整塊樣品之介電性質
是否有其差異，進而由粉末樣本之微波介電性質直接得知整塊陶瓷樣品之微波介電性質。 
 
 
（二）計畫英文摘要。 
Keywords： Dielectric constant, Dielectric loss, Complex permittivity, Loss tangent, Microwave 
measurements, Mixture rules. 
This research is to investigate the relationship between the mixture rules of dielectric 
properties and the microwave dielectric properties of mixed composite materials. The results of this 
research will be applied to the measurements of microwave dielectric properties. 
The existing measurement techniques of microwave dielectric properties are mainly for bulk 
samples. It is lack of the measurement methods for the microwave dielectric properties of powder 
samples. In this research, by mixing the powder of measured material with the powder of 
polyethylene (PE), the composite bulk can be easily made under high temperature because of the 
low melting point of PE. By measuring the microwave dielectric properties of composite samples 
with various volume percentages, the dielectric properties of pure measured powder can be 
deduced. 
The dielectric properties of composite samples with various volume percentages are compared 
with ten different mixture rules, the dielectric properties of pure measured material is then obtained 
from extrapolation. We will try to explain experimental results by existing theories or developing 
new theories. The dielectric properties of powder are then compared with those of the bulk ceramic 
sample to find out the differences on microwave dielectric properties between the powder and bulk 
samples. We then can obtain the dielectric properties of bulk ceramic sample by simply measuring 
the powder sample.

In this work, a simple field model for the waveguide
reflection dielectric resonator is developed. This field model
was firstly developed for the parallel-plate dielectric resonator
[13-16] and, in this research, it is modified to be applied to the
closed structure in Fig. 1. An eigenvalue equation is derived
from the boundary condition between the dielectric and the air
for the application of dielectric constant measurement. The
conductor loss of the metal cavity is also studied. The loss
tangent ofthe dielectric sample can then be computed from the
measured unloaded quality factor and the calculated
conducting loss. Finally, experiments are conducted by this
reflection waveguide dielectric resonator method and other
measurement methods to confirm the measurement accuracy
on both loss tangent and dielectric constant measurements by
using this modified field model.
