基板注入遲滯式氧化層崩潰後金氧半場效電晶體特性之研究
“The Study of the Characteristics of MOSFETs after Hysteresis-like Oxide Breakdown under Substrate
Injection”
計畫編號：NSC94-2215-E-262-003
執行期間：94年 8月 1日 至 95年 7月 31日
主持人：黃嘉宏 龍華科技大學電機工程系助理教授
一、中文摘要
本計畫探討基板注入氧化層發生遲滯形
式崩潰後對金氧半場效電晶體電特性的影
響。因此計畫將以遭受熱輻射光能量生長氧化
層與氧化後退火增加氧化層發生遲滯形式崩
潰之機率，藉此廣泛研究閘極氧化層發生遲滯
形式崩潰後對金氧半場效電晶體特性的變
化。將經由各種不同的電性分析，如電流-電
壓(I-V)、電容-電壓(C-V)、電壓施加引起漏電
流(SILC)、時間相關介電崩潰(TDDB)等特性來
檢視此崩潰後對金氧半場效電晶體的臨界電
壓、基底電流、轉導、遷移率、雜訊敏感度、
功率消耗與可靠度之影響。並以所觀察之結果
與遭逢熱載子效應後之金氧半場效電晶體特
性做比較分析。期能深入完整地瞭解遲滯形式
崩潰對金氧半場效電晶體操作特性的影響，並
建立模型。最後的成果將提供給學術界及工業
界參考。
英文摘要
The purpose of this study is to investigate
the characteristics of MOSFETs with ultra-thin
oxides subjected to substrate injection after
hysteresis-like breakdown. In this study, by
means of the illumination during rapid thermal
oxidation and post oxidation annealing, the
occurrence of hysteresis-like breakdown in
ultra-thin oxide films is enhanced. The effect of
hysteresis-like breakdown on MOSFETs is
examined in electrical properties. In terms of
various electrical analyses, in the meanwhile,
such as current-voltage (I-V),
capacitance-voltage (C-V), stress-induced
leakage current (SILC), time dependent dielectric
breakdown (TDDB), we will widely investigate
the variations in threshold voltage, substrate
current, transconductance, mobility, noise, power
consumption and reliability for MOSFETs
subjected to hysteresis-like oxide breakdown.
Then we will compare these characteristics with
those of MOSFETs subjected to hot carrier stress.
It is a great aid for us to understand the
characteristics of MOSFETs with ultra-thin
oxides subjected to hysteresis-like breakdown
under operating condition. The finally found
results should be useful for the reference of
technology development in both industry and
academic point of views.

三、研究方法及成果
本計畫研究的主軸，在於探討氧化層在發
生遲滯式(BD-H)後對 MOSFET 之電特性的影
響，因此超薄氧化層於生長與後退火過程中均
承受熱輻射光能量以增加氧化層遲滯式崩潰
的機率[22]，之後藉助於 NDL之佈植設備完成
MOSFET 元件源極與汲極之佈植製作完成
MOSFET各接觸電極，其中 MOSFET 之 L =
10μm，W = 20μm。測量與評估 BD-H 對
MOSFET電特性之影響。
