表現；尤其是市面上的 LED 在許多照明應用上常會發現空間中色溫分佈不均勻的現象，即黃暈或藍
圈的產生，導致 LED 在照明應用上的困難；因此，有鑑於此，本團隊即從多年豐富的光學研究經驗
出發，了解螢光粉內外的光學特性，發展精確的螢光粉模型，以期達到能預測螢光粉之光學及色彩的
表現，並據之設計擁有空間極小色溫偏差的螢光粉塗佈參數。 
 
（二）研究方法 
    在計畫的第一年使用本團隊建立的中場擬合法[7]來建立多晶片白光LED光源模型。由於多晶片白
光LED內部的光學行為複雜，若考慮真實的情況，其計算會變得困難且耗時。解決途徑包括簡化其內
部參數或利用空間對稱以加快計算速度。若能建立一個具有彈性、準確與計算快速的模型，將有助於
相關子計畫中的參數設計。因此第一年的研究重點為精確計算多晶片白光LED光源模型的發展與複雜
的內部光學行為之研究並利用封裝實作加以驗證。計畫第二年之重點為精密螢光粉模型之建立。螢光
粉的光學與色彩特性複雜，包含吸收、激發、波長轉換及散射等複雜行為；我們將以Monte-Carlo光追
跡法及Mie散射理論為基礎，藉由實驗驗證複雜螢光粉之光學特性，並配合子計畫四之色彩量測與混色
分析之技術，發展精密螢光粉模型並進行封裝驗證。精密螢光粉模型除了可預測螢光粉之光學及色彩
行為外，亦將可用於白光LED光源模型之建立。在計畫的第三年，我們將以第二年之螢光粉模型為基
礎，預測不同螢光粉塗佈對LED封裝之光學特性以及分析其空間中的色彩表現，並藉由實驗驗證進行
空間低色偏的螢光粉塗佈設計與優化，改善現有LED技術會出現黃暈之現象。最後，配合其他子計畫
的相關技術，發展擁有高光學及色彩品質的新世代LED照明光源。 
 
（三）結果與討論 (為精確表示，本段以英文書寫) 
(1) 多晶片白光 LED 模型： 
 This approach has been implemented to simulate a single-chip white LED [7]. Although useful to model 
the color angular distribution, this method demonstrated that there is practically no difference between using a 
complex phosphor model or a simple Lambertian model for the stimulation of the radiation pattern. However, 
for a phosphor-coated multi-chip LED, the traditional Lambertian model does not accurately reproduce the 
experimental measurements. The problem is depicted in Fig. 1 for an 8 chip LED with sizes: chip side 1 a.u., 
phosphor disc diameter 6.5 a.u., chip spacing 0.5 a.u., and spherical cap with both an 8 a.u. radius and a 4.2 
a.u. tip height. It can be noted that the conventional method does not agree with the off-axis data. As we 
explain in the next section, the main consideration missing for this problem is the energy distribution across 
the phosphor surface, which depends on geometric conditions, phosphor layer thickness, phosphor particle 
diameter, absorption of phosphor and so on.

(2) 超精確螢光粉模型: 
 
The phosphor in the study is a silicate-based chemistry of (SrBaMg)2SiO4：Eu
2+
, which absorbs blue light 
and emits the lights covering the spectrum in visible range but with green-yellow dominant and thus is useful 
for white light generation with blue GaN dies. The optical modeling process in this study is illustrated in Fig. 
4, where we did the measurement of the scattering lights, absorption coefficient, and conversion efficiency of 
the phosphor plate in different concentrations and thickness. Then we build up an optical simulation model 
with Mie scattering in a simulation program with Monte Carlo ray tracing. The model is aimed to simulate the 
outside power ratio of the blue light by the GaN die and the yellow light by the phosphor pumped by a blue 
light source. Finally, the successful model is applied to predict the color behavior of LEDs.  In the following, 
we will describe the details. 
The characterization of the phosphor starts from the measurement of the particle size, which is a key 
parameter in determining the fraction obscuration and the cross-sectional area of the phosphor mixed with 
silicone.[22] The measured particle size is in a Gaussian distribution from 2 um to 30 um with the peak at 
around 17 um. Then we calculated the particle number and the concentration of the phosphor with the density 
of Dp=4.83g/cm
3
. Thus the parameters for Mie scattering are determined.

(3) 超低空間色偏之設計與驗證 
 
Based on the precise phosphor model, we can control the chromatic performance of white LEDs. The 
objective here is to design a package to perform low CCT. As understanding, low CCT can perform high 
uniformity in spatial CCT owing to multiple scattering in the phosphor and over there less blue lights are out 
coupled. However, when the CCT reaches 6000 K and above, the blue lights become dominant and spatial 
CCT is difficult to control. We measured the spatial CCT of several commercialized samples. The result is that 
most samples perform  1000 K and specific samples perform  500 K. If we cannot reduce the spatial CCT 
variation, the color presentation of such LEDs cannot meet request by high-quality lighting.  
 The design started from the geometry of phosphor, which should perform equal optical path for the blue 
lights as shown in Fig. 7. In regarding a surface light source of LED dice, we design a half-sphere shape of 
phosphor plate to meet the requirement of equal optical path. Incorporating with the precise optical model for 
phosphor, we adjust the shape of the phosphor plate with aspherical coefficients to minimize the spatial CCT 
variation. The picture of one of the samples that we made is shown in Fig. 8. the measurement of the spatial 
CCT distribution is shown in Fig. 9, where all the four LEDs were operated at 350 mA injection, and the 
CCTs are around 6500 K. The measured spatial CCT variation are around  150 K rather than  50K in 
optical design.

在本計劃中，我們如期地達成計畫書中所制定的目標，其成果如下： 
(1) 國際上第一個對多晶白光 LED 建立精確的光學模型； 
(2) 國際上第一個建立白光 LED 之超精確螢光粉模型，其色座標值之誤差在 0.05
以內； 
(3) 提出白光 LED 之封裝設計，可以達到在暖白光±50K，與冷白光±150K 的國
際最佳記錄。 
上述相關研究大多已發表於期刊文章，其中包括 5 篇 Optics Express，並有 8 次
國際會議之 invited talk。 此外，相關技術移轉簽約金額超過 3,000 萬元，計劃
期間共獲得 OSA Fellow、國科會傑出研究獎、經濟部大學產業貢獻獎與國科會
傑出技術移轉獎等榮譽。 
因此，本計劃已圓滿達成，成果豐碩。
