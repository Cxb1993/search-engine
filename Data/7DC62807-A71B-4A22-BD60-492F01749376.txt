Abstract 
This is the final report of a 3-year research project, entitled “Raman Tweezers for Microbes Detections”, 
supported by National Science Council, for the biomedical applications of Raman spectroscopy in general, and Raman 
tweezers based analysis of antibiotic resistance of bacteria and single-cell detection of microorganisms in particular. 
Specifically, due to our increasing interest in clinical applications, we have expanded our scope in the 3rd year to cover 
resonance Raman methods for normal vs. oral cancer tissue discrimination, serum carotenoid detection, and 
surface-enhanced Raman detection of micro-volume pH change by silver nanocubes coated with 4-aminothiophenol 
(4-ATP). Our accomplishments can be briefly summarized as follow. In the 1st two years, we have identified the 
Raman signatures associated with specific molecular structures in the bacterial outer membrane which may play a 
critical role in their antibiotic resistance. By comparing the Raman spectra of two different strains of Salmonella 
enterica serovar Typhimurium, the wild-type (WT 01-4) and the R200 (which exhibits 200-fold antibiotic resistance of 
ceftriaxone compared with the WT 01-4), we have substantiated that the difference in relative Raman peak intensity 
of the two strains at 1452 cm-1 (a characteristic Raman signature of lipid structure) and 1355 cm-1 (a characteristic 
Raman signature of protein secondary structure-amide III) may provide some important information in antibiotic 
resistance of bacteria. Further comparison of Raman spectrum and of SDS-PAGE result of lipopolysaccharide (LPS) 
extracted and purified from two strains of E. coli K-12, the wild-type strain (BW25113) and the outer membrane 
lpp-knockout strain (JW1667), indicated that the relative Raman intensity of the peak at 1452 cm-1 may be correlated 
with the length of O-antigen of LPS. In addition, the comparison of the intensities of the two Raman peaks at 1452 
cm-1 and 1355 cm-1 of WT 01-4 and the OmpD-knockout, 01-4(ΔOmpD), indicated that OmpD-knockout may have 
less effect on the amount of lipid (characterized by the 1452 cm-1 peak), but may be associated with the reduction in 
amide III (characterized by the 1355 cm-1 peak). Therefore, the antibiotic mechanism of R200 strain may be due to the 
shorter length of LPS and then change the structure and function of outer membrane porin protein, OmpD. In 
summary, we have demonstrated that the change in lipid structure in R200 and the signal reduction in amide III, 
associated with OmpD knockout in our test, may contribute to the antibiotic resistance of the R200 strain. In the 3rd 
year, we also applied Raman microspectroscopy to analyze biopsy samples of oral cancer tissue, and to detect serum 
carotenoid and pH change of micro-volume samples and cancer cells to search for diagnostic markers of related 
diseases. 
 
Key Words: Optical tweezers, Raman microspectroscopy, Gram-negative bacteria, Antibiotic resistance, Oral 
cancer, Serum carotenoids, Micro-volume cell pH.

5-2 延伸部分 
 
5-2-1 拉曼光譜在口腔癌的應用 
 
(1) 研究背景及目的 
口腔癌的死亡率有逐年上升的趨勢，口腔癌病人的存活率主要取決於早期的正確診
斷，而使用光學方法來做癌症診斷是近來新興的方法之一。我們藉由觀察拉曼光譜在口
腔癌組織及正常組織的不同，試圖找出可供鑑別的指標，提高臨床上診斷的準確性及便
利性。 
  拉曼光譜已被用來分辨惡性腫瘤與正常組織 [21]。目前拉曼光譜的分析常使用主成
分分析法 (Principal Component Analysis, PCA) 等統計學方法。PCA 的優點是光譜資料庫
一旦被分析並分類，則新的光譜只要與此分類模型做比較，便可知道它是屬於癌症或正
常組織；但此種分類純粹只是統計上的分法，並無法確切的指出，是哪些分子種類在癌
症及正常組織的分佈會有所不同。在病理上，角質蛋白 (keratin) 已經被廣泛的視為癌症
的一個重要標記，因為角質蛋白在一般正常組織與癌症組織會有不同的表現 [22]。所以
目前我們研究的主題將著重在找尋拉曼光譜所代表的分子指標以鑑別癌症，我們應用非
負矩陣分解分析法 (Non-negative Matrix Factorization, NMF) 以補主成分分析之不足。 
 
(2) 研究結果與討論 
非負矩陣分解分析法已經可以成功的將原始光譜的資料庫，分解出幾個主要有物理
意義的光譜來表示，如自體螢光及玻片的光譜。我們發現口腔癌組織 (Squamous cell 
carcinoma) 具有非常相似於角質蛋白的拉曼光譜，而在正常組織裡則為一般典型的蛋白
質拉曼光譜，此兩種組織的光譜號有明顯的不同。角質蛋白的表現在臨床上為口腔癌的
指標之一，拉曼光譜結合非負矩陣分解法將可提供一個便利快速分辨口腔癌與正常組織
的有用工具。 
 
5-2-2 以共振拉曼光譜檢測採血管內血清類胡蘿蔔素 
 
(1) 研究背景與目的 
  類胡蘿蔔素是常見於彩色蔬菜或水果中的天然色素群，同時也是人體內重要的抗氧化
劑 [23-27]，類胡蘿蔔素的攝取不足可能導致心血管疾病或是眼球黃斑部病變等疾病。傳
統上檢測類胡蘿蔔素的方法是高效液相色譜法 (HPLC)，但卻耗費時間及成本昂貴。此研
究中我們建立共振拉曼光譜學快速檢測平台，用來檢測採血管內血清樣本中類胡蘿蔔
素，不需費時的樣品前處理只需採血與離心。 
 
(2) 研究結果與討論 
  為了利用共振拉曼效應 [28, 29] 加強類血清胡蘿蔔素的拉曼訊號，我們使用多波長雷
射拉曼系統尋找適合的激發波長；儘管類胡蘿蔔素在血清內濃度很低，在 488.0 nm、514.5 
nm 與 530.9 nm 波長之雷射激發下，我們仍能觀察到完整的類胡蘿蔔素拉曼光譜。同時意
外地發現碳與碳之間雙鍵拉伸的拉曼訊號 (ν1) 位置跟著激發波長改變而隨之改變。我們
假設這是由於不同種類的類胡蘿蔔素在不同的雷射波長激發下，會有不同的共振拉曼增
強效果所造成。首先，我們將不同種類的類胡蘿蔔素之拉曼光譜，依其共振波長 (由吸收
光譜得知) 的相似度概為二種主成分。番茄紅素為第一種成分，而葉黃素、β-胡蘿蔔素、
玉米黃質以及 α-胡蘿蔔素等四種則為第二種成分。接著，我們檢測正常健康人及相關病
人的血清樣本，透過軟體進行全球分析 (global analysis) [30]，得到這兩種主成分的組成
比例，並以共振拉曼係數進行定量分析，希望能有助於相關疾病的診斷方法之建立。

Discrimination of malignant against normal oral tissues by Raman microspectroscopy with 
non-negative matrix factorization analysis 
 
Po-Hsiung Chen,1 Rintaro Shimada,2 Masahiro Ando,3 Chiou-Tzu Chang,4   
Yong-Kie Wong,4 Arthur Chiou,1,5  Hiro-o Hamaguchi,2* 
1Institute of Biophotonics, National Yang-Ming University, No. 155, Sec.2, Linong Street, Taipei, 112 Taiwan; 
2Department of Applied Chemistry and Institute of Molecular Science, National Chiao Tung University, 1001 
Ta Hsueh Road, Hsinchu, 300 Taiwan; 
3Consolidated Research Institute for Advanced Science and Medical Care, Waseda University, 513 
Wasedatsurumaki-cho, Shinjuku-ku, Tokyo, 162-0041, Japan; 
4Department of Dentistry, Taichung Veterans General Hospital, No. 1650, Sec. 4 Taiwan Boulevard, Xitun Dist., 
Taichung, 407 Taiwan; 
5Biophotonics & Molecular Imaging Research Center (BMIRC), National Yang-Ming University, No. 155,

E-mail: hhama@nctu.edu.tw 
 
Raman spectroscopy for the discrimination of malignant against normal oral tissues has been developed in 
recent years. Principal component analysis (PCA) has been used in conjunction with some statistical multi-
parameter analysis. However, the physicochemical meaning of PCA discrimination is always unclear. The 
objective of the present study is to utilize a Non-negative Matrix Factorization (NMF) [1] analysis to supplement 
the PCA analysis by providing additional physicochemical information, based on the principal constraint that 
all the elements in the decomposed matrices must be non-negative. Squamous Cell Carcinoma (SCC) and 
normal oral tissues are used as samples. In order to achieve the best decomposition, we adjust three variable 
parameters for the NMF analysis, 1) the number of independent spectral components N, 2) L1 norm (lasso 
regression) and 3) L2 norm (ridge regression). For the present analysis of oral tissues, we determined N=3 from 
the number of dominant singular values in Singular Value Decomposition (SVD). We have found that L1=10
-4 
and L2=0 for the SCC tissue, and L1=10
-3 and L2=100 for the normal tissue produce smooth spectra without any 
artificial dips. NMF successfully decomposed raw spectral data set into three components, two broad 
fluorescence/glass spectra and a Raman spectrum. The Raman spectrum 
from the SCC tissue is very similar with the standard keratin 
spectrum (Fig.1 (a)(b))[2] and the spectrum from the normal tissue (Fig.1 
(c)) is a typical protein spectrum that is clearly 
different from that of the SCC tissue. In pathology, keratin is one of the 
most widely used marker for oral cancer. Raman microspectroscopy based 
on NMF analysis provides a sure tool to discriminate malignant and 
normal oral tissue without biopsy.
