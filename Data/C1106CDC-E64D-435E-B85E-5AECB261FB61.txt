2不同轉軸上，由徑向磁化之圓柱形結構之
磁極組成之磁力齒輪，透過非接觸式磁力
耦合以傳遞動力扭矩，在特定用途上，它
可用以取代機械傳動齒輪以降低不必要之
振動或噪音。
近年來，在永磁機械的相關研究中興
起了一股”Halbach arays”的熱潮，其應用
包括旋轉馬達、線型平面或管型馬達、磁
浮軸承等永磁磁極之設計，其特色為可以
提升氣隙之磁通密度、減少背鐵（導磁軛
鐵）的使用以降低可動件之慣量等優勢。
本計畫將針對永磁式扭力傳動裝置扭矩漣
波現象之改善提出創新磁極設計、磁場分
析、充磁方法探討、傳動實驗驗證，乃至
導入實際應用以改善非接觸傳動之扭矩漣
波現象。
三、研究結果
非接觸傳動可以減少因接觸所導致之
摩擦與振動，這對於磁性聯軸器而言，因
其主、從動軸間之傳動屬於同步式傳動，
確可因隔離傳動而減少振動的產生；但對
於磁性齒輪，因係利用主動與從動件磁極
間之作用力而傳動，當從一個磁極轉至另
一磁極時，傳動扭力的變化將形成扭矩漣
波(torque ripple)現象。為了探討在特定尺寸
下不同磁極數對於傳輸扭矩與扭矩漣波之
影響，首先採用如表一之尺寸數據做二維
之有限元素分析。圖一為氣隙 0.5 mm 條件
下，不同磁極對數之磁性正齒輪旋轉 0~30
度之扭矩變化，顯示在特定氣隙下磁極對
數增加反而導致磁漏增加、傳輸扭矩下
降；圖二則顯示增加磁極對數可以減少輸
出扭矩之變動量，亦即可以減少扭矩漣波
現象。

A. 2-D FEM analysis of magnetic spur gear
A comparison study of magnetic spur gear for different
pole configurations by 2-D FEM is shown in Fig. 3. The
results show that the innovative pole configurations for
magnetic gear can transmit higher torque than that of a
conventional radial magnet array. It is noted that iron
yokes are added to the inner ring of conventional radial
type magnetic gears to enhance the density of flux lines.
Different configurations of Halbach array were
employed to design magnetic gears array. Figure 4
reveals that the maximum torque is higher for the pole
configuration of Halbach array with 2-magnet segments

airgap smaller than 3 mm, yet at larger airgap the
magnitude of torque inverts. This can be recognized that
larger pole pitch for Halbach array with 4-magnet
segments per pole can not bring into full play at short
range of magnetic gears at closer airgap. Hence, Halbach
array with 2-magnet segments per pole is employed to the
design and proto-type of our magnetic bevel gear not

B. 3-D FEM analysis of magnetic bevel gear
For the transfer of perpendicular motion, a magnetic
bevel gear was designed and fabricated with well
magnetized permanent magnet poles. Due to better
magnetization and utility rate of the poles, bevel gears
can transmit higher torque than helical gears. For
parametric analysis, 3-D FEM package was used for
calculating the magnetic torque. Figures 5 and 6 show
the computer generated drawings and vector plots from
the 3-D simulation package. The schematic illustration
of the magnetic bevel gear is shown in Fig. 5. It is noted
that the Halbach arrays still need a non-ferrous mounting
structure such as those made of aluminum or ceramic, but
the analysis assumed they were mounted to “air.”
A bevel gear can be regarded as an expanded form of a
spur gear that has an incline face. The larger pitch
diameter side has wider clearance due to the bar magnet
poles. The effect of different pole configurations was
simulated as depicted in Fig. 7. The improvement over

increasing airgap from 0.5 mm to 5 mm. As the
