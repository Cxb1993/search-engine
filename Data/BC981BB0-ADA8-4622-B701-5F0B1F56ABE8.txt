二、 計畫的緣由與目的 
近幾年來以矽(Si)或矽鍺(SiGe)為材料的
電晶體，隨著奈米元件與製程整合技術的突飛
猛進，讓以矽或矽鍺的元件與電路之操作頻率
已經進入了 GHz 的頻段。使得 III-V 材料為主
的元件，需要往更高頻率與更高功率的應用上
做發展。其中以氮化鎵(GaN)材料為主的元件
在固定的頻率之下，具有較高的崩潰電壓。這
是因為氮化鎵是一個寬能隙的材料，它由於材
料的特性在惡劣的操作環境下依然可以有非
常好的功率輸出。現今最熱門的應用是以 GaN
材料的高電子移動率電晶體 (High Electron 
Mobility Transistor, HEMT)使用於油電混合式
動力車中的高電壓、高功率的電路裝置。 
另一個與 GaN 能隙相近之半導體：氧化
鋅(zinc oxide，ZnO)也是寬能隙半導體，其結
構 為 纖 鋅 礦 結 構 (Wurzite hexagonal 
structure)，屬於六方最密堆積。其中鋅原子位
於六角柱形結構的節點上，而氧原子則是位於
四個鋅原子所組成的四面體的中心位置。其能
隙寬度(optical band gap)約為 3.37eV，在可見
光範圍具有高穿透率。未摻雜的氧化鋅薄膜其
電阻值相當高，具壓電效應，傳統上多應用於
壓電材料。其內部傳導主要是由化學計量比的
偏差產生，帶電自由載子由氧空缺(oxygen 
vacancies)及格隙鋅原子(interstitial zinc)之淺
層施體能階(shallow donor levels)所提供。為了
提升氧化鋅薄膜的導電率，通常會摻入異價元
素，如：鋁、鎵、銦等，其導電性會大大提升。
由於摻雜入異價的元素可以提高氧化鋅薄膜
之載子濃度，因而增加了氧化鋅薄膜的導電
率。其主要原因是摻雜之異價元素取代氧化鋅
晶格中的 Zn2+離子的位置或是格隙的位置，使
得晶格中傳導電子的增加，進而提升其導電
率。但摻雜入的異價元素如 Al3+、Ga3+等，在
薄膜中除了提供傳導電子外，亦是離子化雜質
散射中心(ionized impurity scattering centers)，
同時也可能佔據晶格結構中之格隙位置而使
結晶變形，導致電子移動率下降，因此所摻雜
入異價的元素需有一適當量。

的應用於發光二極體 (LEDs)的透明電極之
上。除此之外，因為 ZnO 能隙與 GaN 相近，

Physical properties of MgZnO film grown by RF magnetron sputtering 
using ZnO/MgO (80/20 wt %) target  
 
Kuang-Po Hsueh,* a Chun-Ju Tun,b and Hsien-Chin Chiuc  
 
a Department of Electronics Engineering, Vanung University, Chung-Li 32061, Taiwan  
b National Synchrotron Radiation Research Center, Hsinchu 30076, Taiwan   
c Department of Electronics Engineering, Chang Gung University, Tao-Yuan 33302, Taiwan  
 
ABSTRACT  
 
This work investigates the physical properties of the MgxZn1-xO films. MgxZn1-xO films were deposited by RF 
magnetron sputtering system using a 6 inch ZnO/MgO (80/20 wt%) target. The XPS, Hall measurement, and transparent 
performance are measured. The XPS results show that there is high Carbon element content on the surface of MgxZn1-xO 
maybe due to the contamination and the average of the Mg content is about 25 at. %. The XRD results indicate that the 
appearance of only (111) peaks for as-grown MgxZn1−xO film is a sign of the cubic single phase. In this study, the 
MgxZn1-xO film show high transparency with transmittances over 90 % in the visible region (400 ~ 700nm) and the sharp 
absorption edge is visible in UV region due to the Mg content. Therefore, the Hall measurement of MgxZn1-xO films 
which were deposited at lower RF power show the higher doping concentration, the lower resistivity and higher mobility 
as a function of the annealing temperatures. The experimental results indicate that MgxZn1−xO film with 800 °C 
annealing contains more oxygen vacancies which play the role of donor. Since oxygen vacancies generate states in the 
band gap and cause an increase in conductivity.     
 
Keywords : ZnO, MgO, Transparent conductive oxide, Sputtering system, X-ray diffraction, Transparent performance, 
X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy.

Figure 3 shows the XRD patterns of as-grown MgxZn1-xO sample. Based on the diffraction peaks of ZnO (111) and 
MgO (111) are 36.326 and 36.888 degrees, respectively, the pattern of as-grown MgxZn1−xO film shows a strong 
MgxZn1−xO (111) diffraction peak of 36.21 degree coupled with a slight MgO2 (200) diffraction peak of 37.46 degree. 
This suggests that the appearance of only MgxZn1−xO (111) peaks for as-grown film is a sign of the cubic single phase on 
sapphire substrate. Figure 4 shows the transmittance spectrum of MgxZn1-xO film. In this study, the MgxZn1-xO film show 
high transparency with transmittances over 90 % in the visible region (400 ~ 700nm) and the sharp absorption edge is

The experimental results indicate that MgxZn1−xO film with 800°C annealing contains more oxygen vacancies which 
play the role of donor. Since oxygen vacancies generate states in the band gap and cause an increase in conductivity.
