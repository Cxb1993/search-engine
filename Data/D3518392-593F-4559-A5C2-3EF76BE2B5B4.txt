摘要—本計畫進行甲基第三丁基醚 +甲醇+正庚烷三成份系統及其相對應二成份系統之汽液平
衡研究，同時進行相穩定分析。計畫中利用活性係數模式，關連所量測得到之汽液平衡數據。
但是由於甲醇+正庚烷二成份系統為部份互溶，因此在決定此系統之二成份作用參數時，以穩定
分析結果發現系統產生兩液相分離(x1介於 0.1689至 0.8470間)，將改用較合理之汽液液平衡計
算方法，來決定更為可靠的二元交互作用參數，以期望順利推廣至三成份系統之計算上，最後
再將理論所得到之結果與實驗數據作一比較及相互驗證。 
關鍵字：汽液平衡、汽油添加劑、穩定分析 
 
Abstract—This project is the first year part of a two-year project. The objective of this research project 
is to study the vapor-liquid equilibria (VLE) and phase stability analysis for the ternary systems of 
gasoline additives + methanol + n-heptane. Oxygenated compound can be used as gasoline additives 
because of their good anti-knocking properties. The accurate VLE data of these additives with alcohols 
and hydrocarbons are required to develop calculation methods for gasoline reformulation and also to the 
design of separation process. The VLE for the ternary systems of MTBE + methanol + n-heptane and 
constituent binary systems will be carried out. The VLE data will be correlated by using activity 
coefficient model. Because of the partial immiscibility occurred for the system of methanol + n-heptane, 
the phase stability will be examined and these binary interaction parameters can be determined with a 
VLLE calculation method. It was found that the liquid phase splitting occurs at x1=0.1689 to 0.8470. 
Thus, the determined binary parameters can be extended to the ternary systems more reasonably and 
reliably. The obtained correlated results can be employed to compare and verify the experimental ones. 
Keywords: vapor-liquid equilibria, gasoline additives, stability analysis. 
 
一、前言  
由於甲基特丁基醚 MTBE 與特戊基甲基醚
TAME 等帶氧化合物，具有優異的抗震爆性
質，因此自 1973 年起，其逐漸被廣泛地應用
於添加在無鉛汽油中，以提高汽油的辛烷值，
而成為相當重要的化學品。因此在發展汽油重
組計算過程以及設計分離程序時，則須依賴正
確之汽液平衡數據。Oh 與 Park 自 1997 至
2005 年[1-3]進行了一系列有關汽油添加劑系
統汽液平衡之研究。在 1997年[1]，其在 323.15 
與 333.15K 恆溫條件下，對於 TAME 與醇類
（C1 至 C4）之二成份系統進行汽液平衡之量
測，利用Wilson、NRTL、UNIQUAC 與修正
型 UNIFAC 模式計算所量測之汽液平衡數
據。在 1998 年[2]，其在 333.15K 恆溫條件
下，對於 ETBE 與醇類（C1 至 C4）之二成份
系統進行汽液平衡之量測，也應用活性係數模
式來關連汽液平衡之性質。在 2005 年[3]，其
在 313.15K 恆溫條件下，對於 MTBE+甲醇+
甲基環己烷（或正庚烷）三成份系統以及其對
時， SPix 相當接近於液液平衡點，因此可將 SPix
做為液液平衡計算時之起始猜值。再利用活性
係數模式計算混合 Gibbs 能，在固定濃度下，
對上述之不穩定系統繪製混合Gibbs能對組成
關係圖。在曲線上兩個穩定點之間有一內凹現
象，對於落在內凹範圍之任一組成，皆可於曲
線上找到另一組穩定點，由此可知，對於內凹
範圍內的所有組成，皆會產生另一個新液相，
形成液液平衡；相對地，當系統之 Gibbs 能則
沒有內凹的情形發生，代表為完全互溶之系
統。

四、結果與討論 
本計畫進行了在常壓下，汽油添加劑 MTBE+
甲醇與甲醇與正庚烷之二成份系統之汽液平
衡，為驗證實驗設備與實驗步驟之準確性，將
MTBE+甲醇系統所得到之汽液平衡數據與文
獻[7,8]作一比較於如圖 2所示，可看出實驗值
與文獻值趨勢相當吻合，可證明實驗之可靠
性。 
同時，本研究所得到之汽液平衡相圖，可觀察
到在常壓下均存在一共沸點，共沸點經過內差
法得知，對於MTBE(1)+甲醇(2)系統之共沸組
成 azx1 為 0.6820，與文獻[8]共沸組成 azx1 為
0.685相當接近。再者，對於甲醇(1)+正庚烷(2)
系統之共沸組成 azx1 為 0.7537，同時此一系統
亦產生液液相分離之現象，亦即必須改採汽液
液平衡計算，根據本研究相穩定分析結果發
現，常壓下，在液相組成 x1 介於 0.1689 與
0.8470。再利用 NRTL 活性係數模式最適化
後，將計算結果表現於圖 2與 3中，可以看出
利用此種計算方式與實驗結果相當吻合。並期
望再第二期計畫中能在不需要增加任何第三
可調參數下，即可準確的預測三成份 MTBE+
甲醇+正庚烷系統各相中的組成及溫度，同時
亦可完整地描述相產生相分離之現象。

Experimental 
Chemicals 
 All chemicals were used without further purification. The chemicals are PGME (Panreac, 
99.5%), methanol (Fluka, 99.8%), ethanol (J.T. Baker, 99.7%), 1-propanol (Panreac, 99.5%), 
2-propanol (Panreac, 99.8%). Water used here is do-ionized water.  
 
Equipment and procedures 
 The VLE experimental measurements were determined by using a circulation-type still 
(ILUDEST VLE 100M, Germany). The apparatus was mainly consisted of a feeding funnel, 
an evaporator, an equilibrium chamber, a mixing cell, three condensers and a quartz heater. 
The schematic apparatus was shown in Figure 1. In the beginning of the measurements, the 
prepared liquid sample in the feeding funnel was introduced into the evaporator and the 
mixing cell. The sample volume to be filled in was followed by the way of the liquid level in 
the evaporator being located 2-3 cm above the immersion heater.  A heat source was supplied 
by heating rod to boil the liquid sample. To prevent the change in the liquid composition 
caused by vaporization during operation, two condensers were connected to the equilibrium 
chamber and the condensed vapor components were circulated and remixed with liquid 
solution by a stirrer in the mixing cell. The equilibrium chamber enveloped with a silvered 
vacuum jacket can be operated adiabatically and retain equilibrium condition. A heat source 
was supplied by the heater H to boil the liquid sample and kept at the equilibrium temperature 
for 3 h. After reaching equilibrium, the bubbling temperature was measured by a Platinum 
probe D1 (Hart Scientific Co., U.S.A.) with an accuracy of 0.1 K. The vapor-phase sample 
was then collected through port E, and liquid-phase sample was drained from port F. The 
atmospheric pressure was recorded by a Fortin barometer (Sato Keiryoki Co., Japan) with an 
accuracy of ±4 mmHg. 
 The analysis of the compositions in the vapor and liquid phases was performed by gas 
chromatograph with a thermal conductivity detector (China chromatography Co., 8700T). The 
chromatograph column was packed with Porapak QS. The flow rate of carrier gas, helium, 
was maintained at 25 ml/min. The operating conditions were the following: injection 
temperature at 443.15 K, oven temperature at 463.15 K and detector temperature at 443.15 K. 
The calibration curve was obtained from a set of synthetic samples, and the curve was used to 
calculate the mole fraction of PGME in unknown samples. The average error in the 
measurement of the mole fraction was ±0.0005
