 This project investigate: (1) micro-gap heat sinks having square fins with reentrant cavities, 
and (2) flat heat sinks integrating micro-channels with multiple jets for chip cooling. Dielectric 
fluid FC-72 was the working fluid. Based on the boiling enhancement theory, reentrant cavities 
were made on square fins arrays in a flat channel of 200 m channel height by MEMS process.  
The multiple jet-micro channel heat sink has 12×12 mm2 heated surface with 11 micro-channels, 
each channel was 0.8 mm high, 0.6 mm wide, and 12 mm in length. There were 3 or 5 nozzles 
install on each micro-channel. The nozzle diameters were 0.24 or 0.4 mm, and the nozzles 
spacing varied from 1.5 to 3 mm. The experimental result showed that heat transfer performance 
increased with increasing flow rate for single phase heat transfer. However, in two phase heat

二、緣由與目的 
 
2.1 計畫緣由 
電腦運算速度與功能不斷成長，使電子元件的散熱功率、熱通量不斷攀高。傳統的氣
冷散熱器已無法滿足散熱的要求，而CPU整合微渠道的液冷式散熱器將是未來主流。目前
的微渠道研究朝：低氣泡起始成核溫差、維持流場穩定、與噴擊冷卻相整合等三方向發展。
有鑑於此，本計畫將研究壓損較小的扁平微渠道，並將依據氣泡呈核理論，設計低起始溫
差之氣孔，測量其對流沸騰熱傳性能，並完成整合噴擊與微渠道之高效能扁平液冷散熱器。

3.2.5 實驗誤差分析 
在本實驗中，熱傳係數 h 受到測試表面過熱度（ΔTwi）與輸入熱通量（q”）之影響。
而 ΔTwi之誤差大致可分為兩個部份，其一為量測熱電偶本身之誤差；其次則為實驗中分別
量取壁面溫度（Tw）與流體入口溫度（Tin）所產生之差異。另一方面，輸入至薄型軟式熱
電片之加熱量，主要來自於交流變壓器，因此熱通量（q”）之誤差主要來自於兩部份，其
一為電錶量測加熱瓦數之精確度，另外為加熱投影面積之量測精準度。彙整上述熱通量誤
差與測試表面之過熱度誤差，即可求出熱傳性能係數之計算誤差，如方程式(4.12)所示，四
種表面之熱通量誤差為 1 ~ 9.7 %，而熱傳係數之誤差為 3.56 ~ 25.1 %。噴擊雷諾數之誤差
皆在 5.5 ~ 5.514％之內，主要皆為浮球式流量計讀值之誤差。

2.3 Experimental procedure 
In the present tests, heat transfer measurements were carried 
out in increasing heat flux. All parts of the test facility were 
cleaned with acetone and pure water before each test.  Boiling 
surfaces were cleaned with hydrochloric acid solution before 
installing into the test vessel.  They were immersed in 
hydrochloric acid solution (15% HCl + 85% HO2) for five 
minutes, and then it was thoroughly flushed with water. The 
previous cleaning and air dried steps were repeated twice. 
The boiling test procedures are: 
1. The boiling vessel was evacuated for more than two hours.  
A pressure gauge was connected to check the system 
pressure for leak-tight test.  The system was considered 
leak-tight if the system is able to hold vacuum (less than 3 
mmHg absolute pressure) for at least twenty-four hours. 
2. Adjusting shaft to control the boiling surface orientations 
from horizontal to vertical. 
3. After the leak-tide test, approximately 180 g of the 
working fluid was charged into the system from the 
charging/evacuation port. 
4. After charging, the system was boiled at test temperature 
for 1 to 2 hour.  Then, the control valve was open for 
about two seconds to release the non-condensable gases. 
5. After charging and de-gassing processes, the system was 
boiled at the maximum heating power and kept the test 
temperature at for more than 30 minutes before taking data. 
6. For a fixed heat flux, record the data after the system 
temperature remains steady for at least five minutes. The 
images were obtained with High speed-camera.

4. CONCLUSIONS 
 
Based on the above experimental results and discussion, the 
following conclusions are drawn: 
 
 The heat transfer coefficient of finned surface is higher 
than plain surface because of the increased surface area 
and the number of nucleate site density.  
 The departure diameter of the pin-finned surface was 
0.3~1.0 mm, which is smaller than that on a plain surface.  
The bubble frequency and departure velocity of the pin-
finned surface were greater than those of the plain surface. 
 High-speed camera and PIV technique were used to 
investigate the flow field and growth of vapour bubble in 
a dielectric fluid without particle tracer. Velocity vectors 
have been acquired for four stages of boiling on the pin-
finned surface, including: growing in a subsurface tunnel, 
bubble detachment, acceleration, and rising in bulk fluid.
