微透鏡陣列元件之滾壓成型技術之建立 
為有效利用氣體等向性均壓的特性，
氣輔平台的設計是將氣體封在一個可移動
的空間內，來達到滾動式的氣體輔助轉印
的目標。製作時，需要先建立一個裝填空
氣的腔體，該腔體需要能透光以便進行樹
脂轉印，而且該腔體與圓柱模接觸的一方
需具可撓性，才能將氣體均壓的情況施壓
於圓柱模。圖 7 為氣體輔助壓印製程示意
圖。圖 8 為機台整體結構。 
以大小約 395 mm×290 mm×15 mm 的
壓克力板(PMMA)作為平台本體，並且在這
壓克力板上製作出裝填氣體用的凹槽，其
凹槽尺寸約為 255 mm×120 mm×5 mm，再
用軟墊覆蓋於壓克力平台的上方(軟墊的
選擇詳見第六章)。覆蓋好軟墊後，用外框
大小 395 mm×290 mm×10 mm 且鏤空 255 
mm×120 mm×10 mm 的中空鋁框壓在軟墊
上，並將鋁框固定在壓克力平台。當氣體
充到此腔體後，鋁框與壓克力板之間的夾
持力可將軟墊夾住，把氣體包覆在凹槽
內，完成氣輔平台的製作；若需要改變軟
墊厚度時，可加入橡膠片來解決夾持不足
的問題。

1. 氣體壓力對微透鏡陣列成型探討 
    本實驗將熱壓溫度固定為 150°C，加
壓時間固定為 120 秒，以不同氣體壓力

10µm 至 35µm 之間，數據歸納整理如圖 9
所示。 
2. 熱壓溫度對微透鏡陣列成型探討

170°C)，探討熱壓溫度對微透鏡成型之影

Direct fabrication of microstructures on metal roller using stepped rotating lithography and electroless 
nickel plating 
 
Tzu-Chien Huang1, Sen-Yeu Yang1, Po-Hsun Huang1, Shuo-Hung Chang 1, 2 
1. Department of Mechanical Engineering, National Taiwan University, Taipei 106, Taiwan 
2. Industrial Technology Research Institute, Hsinchu 310, Taiwan 
e-mail: syyang@ntu.edu.tw 
 
Roller embossing has been attracted more attention due to its rapid, continuous production for mass production. The fabrication 
of microstructure rollers is the key technology. Most of the microstructure rollers fabrication methods are very complicated and 
costly, needing high-end facilities. Furthermore, mold sliding and warping problems are frequently encountered because of the 
weak adhesion between molds and rollers. Soft rollers with microstructures have problems including poor strength durability and 
temperature endurance. 
 
This work proposes a novel fabrication method of roller mold with microstructure on the surface using novel stepped rotating 
lithography and electroless nickel plating. The complete procedure used in this roller fabrication is illustrated in Fig 1. First, a 
thin nickel layer is electroplated on the surface of aluminum roller (the diameter is 80mm, and length is 120mm) and the 
photoresist is coated onto the roller surface using dip coating. Then the photoresist on the roller surface can be patterned using 
stepped rotating lithography, followed by PR developing. Subsequently, the roller is treated with electroless nickel plating. 
Finally, the residual PR is removed by acetone. After that, a roller with rigid nickel microstructures can be obtained.  
 
The fabricated microstructures on roller are observed by optical microscopy (OM). Fig 2 shows the OM image of the fabricated 
roller surface with rigid nickel microstructures. In upside of fig 2, the microstructures with a pitch of 57.5 µm are fabricated by 
stepping ten minutes. While the downside of fig 2 shows the microstructures with a pitch of 115 µm are fabricated by stepping 
five minutes. As shown in fig 3, the average sag height of the microstructure is 1.1 µm, and the line width is 23 µm (original line 
width on mask is 23.6 µm) measured by surface profiler.  
 
Result proves that the microstructures can be successfully fabricated onto the metal roller using proposed stepped rotating 
lithography and electroless nickel plating. In addition, the microstructures with various pitches on single metal roller also can be 
fabricated by adjusting the rotating degree of stepped rotating lithography.
