一、摘要
近年來環境荷爾蒙(或內分泌干擾
物質，endocrine-disrupting
compounds；EDCs)對人體健康的可能
影響普遍受到重視，即使在住家的環
境中，許多被廣泛使用的化學物質亦
被陸續被認定為EDCs，如:鄰苯二甲
酸酯(塑化劑)、鄰-苯基苯酚(消毒
劑)、壬基苯酚(清潔劑代謝產物)及農
藥等；然而評估各種EDCs的可能健康
危害時，其所需的暴露資料卻非常缺
乏。
由於國人暴露於各種環境荷爾蒙
的情況極為普遍，而許多研究亦懷疑
不同環境荷爾蒙對健康的危害可能具
有加成作用，因此評估來自室內環境
空氣及灰塵中之EDCs的濃度分佈狀
況、釐清可能污染源、及相關之健康
風險有非常重要的公共衛生意義；然
而現有的評估方法卻不夠理想，更可
能限制了重要暴露資料的收集。因
此，為改善現有採樣分析方法的不方
便性，本研究嘗試以固相微萃取(solid
phase microextraction; SPME)技術發展
室內空氣中不同低濃度鄰苯二甲酸酯
的被動式採樣方法。
本研究首先將適用之SPME纖維
暴露於含有濃度808 ng L-1的鄰苯二甲
酸酯類蒸氣的氣袋中，於不同暴露時
間(30, 60, 75, 90分鐘)的條件下進行鄰
苯二甲酸酯類的採樣分析，並對於不
同溫度(4℃, 25℃, 35℃)和溼度
(RH=10%, 80%)之影響因子進行測
試；完成採樣之後，將纖維置於氣相
層析儀之注射口進行熱脫附，接著以
質譜儀進行化學物質定性與定量之分
析。
本研究結果發現，100μm
Polydimethylsiloxane (PDMS)纖維為最
適合採集鄰苯二甲酸酯類之SPME纖
維，其對於鄰苯二甲酸酯類具有較好
的採集效率與穩定的採樣率。不同的
鄰苯二甲酸酯其採樣率範圍為
0.0165~0.307 cm3 min-1，而在不同溫
度下其採樣率略有不同，但在不同溼
度條件下則無差異。結合PDMS纖維
之採樣率與可用採樣時間與濃度範圍
大之優點，具有未來應用於量測室內
空氣中鄰苯二甲酸酯類時量加權平均
濃度(Time Weighted Average)之潛
力。
關鍵字: 固相微萃取、被動式採樣、
鄰苯二甲酸酯、氣相層析質譜儀
Abstract
Many widely used chemicals are
identified as endocrine-disrupting
compounds (EDCs) even in the indoor
environments, such as phthalates
(plasticizers), o-phenylphenol
(disinfectant), 4-nonylphenol (detergent
metabolite), and pesticides. The
widespread interest regarding the health
effects with EDCs has made exposure
assessment for these compounds a
priority. For example, the most
susceptible group, children, will expose

lower than the present indoor
concentrations [13]. It has been shown
that the phthalates concentrations in
indoor air ranged from 0.3 to 13907 ng
m-3 [5]. Generally, it is agreed that the
main sources of phthalates in air came
from indoor environments rather than
outdoors (such as the wearing of tires).
[14]. Published levels of phthalates in
the indoor and outdoor environments are
shown in Table 2.
Due to the adverse health effects of
phthalates on human reproductive and
developmental system, also because of
the considerable importance of
phthalates contained in air to total
exposure, previous studies have
developed several air sampling methods
for phthalates.
Solid-phase microextrction (SPME)
is a solvent-free preparation technique
and presents many advantages over
traditional analytical methods by
combing sampling, preconcentration,
and the transfer of the analytes into a
standard gas chromatograph (GC).
Therefore, the objective of this research
is to develop a more efficient and
environmental friendly method for the
determination of phthalates in
atmosphere by using SPME. The
designed method is expected to be rapid
and sensitive enough to apply in field
time-weighted average sampling of
phthalates in the future.
三、Results and Discussions
Selections of Fibers
The four commercially available
fibers were tested to extract the
phthalates. The main parameters that
affect the absorption and desorption
processes in SPME were determined.
Desorption temperature was the first
parameter to be studied (Figure 1).
Table 3 shows data obtained from
each fiber by gas bag extraction of
phthalate vapors. The highest amount
collected on different SPME fibers of
the same compound in each column was
set as 100%, others were shown as
relative amount. For DMP and DEP, 85
μm PA fiber had the best results. 
However, for the rest of the phthalates
(DBP, BBP and DEHP), 100 μm PDMS 
fiber had the best extraction efficiency.
In addition to different sorts of fiber
coatings, the ability of the coating phase
to retain the analyte also depends on the
coating thickness and the molecular
weight of the analytes [15]. For
phthalates with higher molecular weight,
a thicker coating possesses larger liquid
volume and thus extracts more of a
given analyte. In contrast, for lower
molecular weight, the gas-liquid
equilibrium dominates the extraction
process [15-16]. Nonpolar coatings (e.g.,
PDMS) retain hydrocarbons very well,
while polar fiber coatings (e.g., PA and
carbowax) extract polar compounds such
as phenols and carboxylic acid very
effectively [16]. Therefore, PDMS fiber
is suitable for extracting phthalates,
6fiber coating may be more advantageous
[16].

除了針對不同濃度之採集效果測
試外，本研究將持續探討更多變項對
採集效果的影響(例如其他物質共存的
影響)，同時也將與現有之主動式採樣
分析方法進行平行比對，以了解所設
計被動式採樣器的使用限制。
