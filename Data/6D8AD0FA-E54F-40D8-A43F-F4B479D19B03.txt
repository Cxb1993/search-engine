技術說明 
（中文） 
目前，模擬電力系統的雷擊特性主要採用電磁暫態程式 EMTP，依
照 EMTP 規格，地網係表示成為等效的 RLC 電路，目前的文獻
卻缺少該電路的架構分析與電路參數的估計方法。為彌補此項缺
失，本計畫的申請人曾設計適用於 EMTP 的 RLC 電路模型及模
型參數的估計方法，乃是根據地電極洩漏電流的原理，設計 RLC 
等效電路架構，隨而運用 CDEGS 程式模擬地網內各分段導體的
地電位昇與電流分佈情形，利用阻抗串、並聯轉換公式，求取各
分段導體之等效 RLC 電路參數；本計畫以台電 69kV公館-錦水線
之鐵塔地網進行注入衝擊電流試驗，並與模擬結果相比較。 
（英文） 
Power system lightning is commonly simulated by using the 
Electromagnetic Transient Program (EMTP) which presents the 
ground grid as an equivalent RLC circuit; however, the present 
simulation lacks an effective tool to assist in the modeling of ground 
grid, including the estimation of circuit (or model) parameters. To 
overcome this difficulty, this project proposed a modeling method for 
the ground grid equivalent-circuit including circuit parameters. The 
method makes use of the stray current characteristics to formulate the 
equivalent-circuit model and CDEGS ( Current Distribution 
Electromagnetic Fields, Grounding and Soil Structure Analysis) to 
simulate the lightning current distribution and the ground potential rise 
(GPR) of the ground grid, by which to derive the equivalent-circuit 
parameters. The project selects the tower  of Taiwan Power Company 
69 kV Kungkuan – Jin Hsuei line to inject the impulse current into 
the ground grid of tower, and compares the experimental with the 
simulation results.
