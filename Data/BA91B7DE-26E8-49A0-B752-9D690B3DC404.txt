16S rDNA of thirty-four isolates were amplified by using the primer set: F8
?5’AGAGTTTGATCMT- GGCTCAG3’?and R1488?5’CGGTTACCTTGTTA- 
GGACTTCACC3’?. The 16S rDNA-RFLP of the isolates was accomplished by 
using four endonucleases including DdeI, EaeI, RsaI and ScaI.  The result 
showed that 14 genotypes were categorized from the multiple restriction patterns. 
According to the 16S rRNA gene sequencing results, these isolates could be 
classified as the species of genera Thalassobius, Marinobacter, Vibrio, Shewanella, 
Pseudoalteromonas, Halomonas, Pseudomonas or Prolixibacter. Phylogeny based 
on 16S rRNA gene sequences, together with data from physiological, 
morphological and chemotaxonomic characterizations, indicated that the eight 
isolates could be classified as representatives of five novel species belonging to 
Gammaproteobacteria.
