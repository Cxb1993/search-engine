行政院國家科學委員會補助專題研究計畫成果報告 
 
空時碼多載波分碼多工多供輸入多輸出系統之 
盲目通道估測及接收機設計 
Blind Channel Estimation and Receiver Design of Space-time  
Coded MC-CDMA MIMO Systems 
計畫編號：NSC 95－2221－E－030－006－ 
執行期間： 95 年 08 月 1 日至 96 年 07 月 31 日 
計畫主持人：余金郎  副教授 
計畫參與人員： 李美貞、施志遠、紀建安、吳俊賢 
 
中文摘要 
多載波調變技術如多載波分碼多工存取
(MC-CDMA)廣泛地應用於寬頻無線通訊系統。空時
編碼技術近年來廣泛地被討論，而它與 CDMA 系統也
已成為第三代寬頻行動通訊標準技術之一。空時碼使
用空間分集特性配合特殊的編碼，以提供衰落通道下的
分集增益。但是若是在多路徑衰落通道環境下，ISI
的效應將導致空時碼特性被破壞，增加系統設計的
困難度。空時碼 MC-CDMA 系統於是被提出解決頻率
選擇衰落通道問題並提供空時分集增益。 
本研究計畫主要討論在頻率選擇衰減通道下
四傳多收以及二傳多收空時碼 MC-CDMA 系統的設
計、通道估測與分析。為了分便描述，此處研究方
法以四傳多收系統為例。我們先建立四傳多收空時
碼 MC-CDMA 系統的訊號模型，繼而利用空時碼特性
開發新的子空間盲目通道估測技術。對於接收端而
言，為了要偵測發射端所傳送出來的訊號，一般需
要知道多路徑衰落的通道脈衝響應向量；而此通道
向量的獲得，可以利用訓練時序(training sequence)
或利用盲目通道估測技術來達成。但若考慮通訊容
量，尤其在快速衰落通道中，若能發展盲目通道估
測而減少訓練時序，將有助於提昇通訊的使用效
率。我們分析在有限取樣資料下，利用奇異值分解
(SVD)或特徵值分解(EVD)計算的非理想子空間對通
道估測之均方誤差值(MSE)。其次我們利用 Capon 接
收機進而設計出特徵空間濾波器，並利用空時碼特
性分析接收端濾波器權重的關係以減少計算量。我
們也利用 Forward-backward averaging 的技術來增
加取樣資料以提升系統的效能。 
關鍵詞：空時編碼，多路徑衰落，通道估測，多使用
者偵測器，多輸入多輸出，多載波分碼多工存取，
特徵空間濾波器。 
 
英文摘要 
Multicarrier modulation such as orthogonal 
frequency division multiplexing (OFDM) and 
multicarrier code division multiple access (MC-CDMA) 
have been widely developed for wideband cellular 
communications. In addition, space-time coding 
technique has been received lots of attentions for years. 
The combination of CDMA and space-time coding is 
considered as one of key technologies for 3G standard. 
The space-time coding technique utilizes the spatial 
diversity (with multiple transmit/receive antenna) with 
specific form of coding. It provides the diversity gain to 
the receiver in the multipath fading channel. However, 
the system design becomes very difficult due to the 
multipath fading environment which destroys the 
property of space-time codes.  
This project is mainly to study the blind channel 
estimation and receiver design of the space-time coded 
MIMO (including 4IMO and 2IMO systems) 
MC-CDMA systems in frequency-selective fading 
傳空時碼系統，並建立適用於[9]-[11]的四傳多收
空時碼MC-CDMA系統的訊號模型。接著我們發展其通
道估測，利用整理好的訊號模型，我們擬用子空間
盲目通道估測技術來估測通道，其原理乃利用等效
通道向量正交於接收訊號的雜訊子空間的特性，即
可形成一齊次方程式(Homogeneous equations)，並
利用最小平方法(least square)來計算出在有雜訊
誤差下的最佳解。一般雜訊子空間可以由接收訊號
的資料矩陣執行奇異值分解(SVD)或由接收訊號的
自相關矩陣執行特徵值分解(EVD)求得。我們擬分析
在有限取樣資料下，利用SVD或EVD計算的非理想的
雜訊子空間對通道估測誤差之均方值(MSE)。其次我
們設計多使用者接收器，我們擬用Capon接收機及特
徵空間接收機[51][52]並討論兩者效能的優勢。最
後我們擬針對上述的接收機提出改良的方法。傳統
中，由於使用空時碼技術，接收端需設計四組接收
器分別估測4IMO空時碼的四個訊符，我們擬討論此
四組估測器的關係以簡化系統。其次我們利用
forward-backward平均法[53]，提出增加取樣資料
的有效方法，以提高系統的效能。

通道估測誤差知均方值(MSE)。為了方便，本計畫書

VI. COMPUTER SIMULATIONS 
The sample correlation matrices are used instead of 
ensemble correlation matrix rR  for reality. The signal-to- 
noise-ratio (SNR) and normalized mean square error 
(NMSE) of channel estimation defined as those in [8]. The 
ambiguity matrix C is obtained either by using pilot symbols 
at the first subcarrier [8] or by using the true channels, which 
is computed by 
( )-1 1ˆ ˆ ˆ H H=C H H H H                            (36) 
where Hˆ  and 1H  are defined by (17). The notations ‘pilot 
SS’ and ‘optimal SS’ in the figures indicate that the 
ambiguity matrix is computed by pilot symbols or (36), 
respectively. The optimal receiver means the true channel is 
used in the MMSE filter. Rayleigh fading channels are 
generated for each Monte Carlo run.  
Fig. 1 and Fig. 2 show the NMSE and BER versus SNR 
when K=2, J=3, N=32, L=7, 2pN =  and =200sN . With the 
estimated channels, the MMSE equalizer in (30) is used for 
detecting symbols. It is obvious that the proposed channel 
estimation works very well for complex input signals. 
Besides, the forward-backward averaging technique (FB) 
improves both NMSE and BER performance because of the 
proposed backward signal vectors.  
Fig. 3 and Fig. 4 show the NMSE and BER versus the 
number of samples sN . Clearly, as the number of samples 
increases, the RMSE and BER decrease. Again, the FB 
technique provides tremendous improvement for both NMSE 
and BER. Finally, we show the near-far effect on the BER in 
Fig. 5. The power of user 1 is 10 dB lower than that of user 2. 
It is found that the user gets a lower BER if the user has more 
power. Similar results are also obtained in [8]. Besides, the 
proposed FB technique enhances the performance of MMSE 
filter, especially for the user with higher power. 
VII.     CONCLUSIONS 
In this paper, the novel channel estimation is proposed for 
MIMO STC-OFDM system with complex input signals. 
With the modified Alamouti’s STBC, the drawback of 
channel estimation in [8] for complex with symmetric is 
cured. The identifiability analysis is executed and reveals that 
the channel responses can be computed by the proposed 
channel estimation up to two ambiguity matrices. 
Furthermore, the FB technique reduces the NMSE and BER 
significantly. Computer simulation demonstrates the 
correctness of channel estimation and efficiency of FB 
technique for the MIMO STC-OFDM systems.
