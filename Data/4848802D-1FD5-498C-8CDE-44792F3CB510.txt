This study aims to examine the electrohydrodynamic (EHD stability of a poorly 
conducting couple stress fluid in a horizontal channel in the presence of a transverse 
electric field. The poorly conducting fluids imply that the electric conductivity 
possesses a spatial variation that the dependence of conductivity on space leads to 
the change of electric charge, resulting in the electric force within the fluid. The 
EHD instability causing in-homogeneity is important in material science processing. 
With the knowledge of the mechanism of EHD instability due to the combined effect 
of an electric field and shear of horizontal velocity, the in-homogeneity phenomenon 
can be properly controlled.  
The physical properties of fluid which contains small particles or suspensions may 
be quite different from those of the Newtonian fluid owing to the fact that 
microstructure may be deformable and rotating, resulting in an asymmetric stress 
tensor. This class of fluid is referred to as micropolar fluid. When the microrotation 
vector is constrained equal to the bulk vorticity throughout the fluid, the micropolar 
fluid is now termed as the couple stress fluid. 
  We derive the equations of motion of the couple stress fluid with the electric force, 
a kind of body force, formulated from the Maxwell’s equations. Then the basic 
solution is obtained. Substituting the normal modes of perturbation together with 
basic solution into the governing equations, the stability equation, after linearization, 
is found. By using the energy method, the sufficient condition for stability is sought

當較重的流體於較輕的流體之上方的表面穩定性問題，稱為 Rayleigh-Taylor 
instability, 而兩流動剪切層之表面受剪應力作用的表面不穩定稱則為 Kelvin- 
Helmholtz instability，兩流體層受到震波作用的表面不穩定稱為 Richtmeyer-

文獻之中。 
    另外有一個種類的表面的不穩定問題，即電流體不穩定(EHD instability)問題
也逐漸被討論到，該種不穩定性主要源自於弱導電性流體於電場作用下，導電係
數或介電常數非常數而具備空間梯度的相依性，引致額外電力出現，導致於流場
或界面發生不穩定之故，古典的表面不穩定問題常出現於不相混合的弱傳導流體
間、或壓電材料的製造等等。 
EHD 界面不穩定問題曾被 Jolly[1], Melcher & Taylor [16]及[17-18]所討論與
回顧，但大多數考慮到的對流 EHD 不穩定，並沒有考慮到弱導電性流體之平行
流動，受到電場作用下的相關不穩定問題，例如導電係數受層變影響的空間變化、
合金混合物的濃度影響、溫度差的影響等等因子。[19]-[20]考慮了具空間梯度導
電係數變異受剪應力及旋轉效應作用的流動不穩定。有些物理現象則與弱導電性
流體有關，例如智慧材料的性質、在大氣與地球物理的過程，都可能包含著弱導
電性無黏或黏性流體與導電係數的空間梯度變異。 
特別是人工器官在生物醫學設計效能、材料科學中的固化程序、障礙物的熱

動脈疾病(CAD)的內皮(endothelium)都與弱導電性流體的運用有關，另外在關節
中關節液與人工軟骨間的表面、CAD 中動脈裡體液與內皮組織間的表面等的界

Flow and Heat Transfer of Nanofluids Near a Rotating Disk by I.C. Liu, H. H. 
Wang and C.N. Liu 已發表於 EI收錄之期刊 Advanced Materials Research Vol. 664 
(2013) pp 859-865, doi:10.4028/www.scientific.net/AMR.664.859. 
 
論文摘要 
Abstract. The study of boundary layer flow and heat transfer near a rotating disk with 
nanofluids is investigated numerically. Three types of nanoparticles, namely, silver Ag, 
copper Cu and alumina Al2O3 with water as the base fluid are considered. The results 
show that the momentum boundary layer thicknesses shortens as the nanoparticle 
volume fraction increases, whereas thermal boundary layer thickness elongates for 
increasing ϕ. It is found that the reduced skin-friction coefficients and heat transfer 
rate at the rotating surface increase linearly with nanoparticle volume fraction 𝜙. The 
surface heat transfer rate for Cu-water nanofluid is higher than those of the others 
when 𝜙 > 0.02, even though the nanoparticle Ag has higher thermal conductivity 
than that of copper Cu.
