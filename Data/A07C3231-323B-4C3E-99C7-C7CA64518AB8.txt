技術說明
中文：
在本專利中，先使用熱退火去除奈米碳管樣上非晶質含碳粒
子，再採用酸楚理，完全去除原來用來成長碳管用且存留在
奈米碳管中之金屬觸媒。在酸處理過程中，硝酸會將奈米碳
管增加奈米碳管微孔中孔吸附位置，並作為氫儲存之吸附部
位。再將純化完成之奈米碳管置入含氯鉑酸之乙二醇溶液，
以微波聚醇法將氯鉑酸中鉑觸媒粒子披覆在奈米碳基材料
上，微波加熱可快速溶解氯鉑酸，同時將鉑金屬觸媒快速還
原沉積在碳電極材料上，增加附著度，增加 Pt 金屬觸媒粒子
在碳管中之吸附量，以增加奈米碳管之儲氫能力。改善一般
以濺鍍法將 Pt 金屬觸媒粒子沉積在奈米碳管上需濺鍍機設備
以及昂貴之白金鈀材。或溶膠凝膠法將 Pt 金屬觸媒粒子沉積
在奈米碳管上，會有金屬觸媒粒子分散在碳管表面，附著度
差，附著量少，製程費時之缺點(製程需 3~4 小時)。以微波聚
醇法將氯鉑酸中鉑觸媒粒子披覆在奈米碳基材料上，微波加
熱只需 1.5 分鐘，白金粒子附著效果佳，溶液中白金粒子殘
留量低，以微波加熱還原白金效率高果。再利用氣體吸脫附
儀(BET)來分析未純化和純化後及含有 Pt 金屬之奈米碳管氣
體吸脫附量；利用穿隧式電子顯微鏡(TEM)、掃描式電子顯微
鏡(SEM)來觀察鉑金屬粒子分散在奈米基材之情況，以XRD 與
XPS 鑑定碳管上之觸媒，以 ICP 與 TGA 鑑定白金觸媒在碳材
料含量。CV 量測電化學特性。
附件二
以微波聚醇法被覆鉑粒子在甲醇燃料電池奈米碳基電極材料上特性探討
計畫編號:NSC 97-2221-E-159-001
執行期限:97/08/01~98/07/31
主持人:陳密教授 明新科技大學材料科學與工程系
國科會計畫編號：NSC 97-2221-E-159-001
摘要
燃料電池電極之主要組成元件有陰極、陽極、電解質、觸媒層與燃料傳導層
等。觸媒層鉑觸媒對於燃料的催化效率為控制電池效率的重要因素。由於白金相
當昂貴，降低鉑觸媒的使用量，有效提高鉑觸媒附著量、均勻分散鉑觸媒、提升
陽極還原反應速率，為直接甲醇燃料電池(Direct methanol fuel cells，DMFC )發展
之瓶頸與極待解決與探討的重點。
本專題研究採用微波聚醇法將氯鉑酸溶液內之鉑粒子還原並均勻被覆在奈米
碳管上。利用微波聚醇法快速將鉑觸媒粒子被覆在奈米碳管，微波輔助加熱可快
速還原金屬觸媒，因氯鉑酸易溶於醇類溶液中，因此分別採用不同醇類還原劑並
改變製程溫度，進而探討溶劑、製程溫度、對鉑粒子被覆於多壁奈米碳管上之效
應。並探討適當之漿料濃度、漿料的成分、漿料的噴塗技術、漿料之均勻性以製
備燃料電池半電極。研究結果顯示，採用微波聚醇法，以乙二醇為還原劑，製程
溫度 140℃所合成之白金粒子約 4.5nm，其粒徑分佈集中且均勻分散於奈米碳管。
添加界面活性劑 PVP 以及 SDS 可以有效分散鉑粒子，而不會產生聚集現象。微波
合成時間僅需 1.5 min，即可被附覆約 50wt%之白金觸媒在奈米碳管上。利用刮刀
方式可簡單將披覆鉑觸媒的奈米碳管與碳布製備成電極，比熱壓方式所得到電化
學特性佳。刮刀法製備電極其最佳條件為 Pt/CNTs 與 Nafion 重量比以 1:5~1:7 的電
化學性質最佳。以 40-60nm 奈米碳管披覆鉑觸媒，其電化學特性優於其他管徑之
奈米碳管。利用刮刀法製備燃料電池半電極，其電流密度約可提高兩倍，還可以
提高電流穩定性。
關鍵字：鉑觸媒、甲醇燃料電池、微波聚醇法、奈米碳管
一、前言
直接甲醇燃料電池由於可以微小
化，無環境污染問題，相關材料及其製
程的開發吸引許多學者投入探討。影響
甲醇燃料電池工作效能的主要因素是觸
媒層的效能，鉑觸媒對於燃料的作用為
Pt觸媒粒子會分解 H2分子為 H原子吸附
在碳電極材料上，為控制電池效率的重
要因素，而由於白金價格相當昂貴，能
夠有效控制白金顆粒之大小，增加白金
被覆量及均勻分散性，為發展直接甲醇
燃料電池之重要技術。
近年來，許多研究者相繼投入探討
以碳基材料如奈米碳管、碳纖維、機械
研磨石墨材料等來取代傳統燃料電池之
觸媒載體[1-2]，大部分之研究探討在高
壓 (1-16MPa) 以 及低溫 (88-133K) 條 件
下，碳材料之吸氫能力，理論上，奈米
碳管中空管內腔以及管壁與管壁間之間
隙都可允許氫進入，並作為氫儲存之吸
附部位，奈米碳管豐富的奈米孔隙可儲
存較多之氫。儲氫容量高低為儲氫材料
應用於燃料電池之重要技術指標，氫為
燃料電池之能源，觸媒在燃料電池之電
極上，可催化氫分子與碳材料之分解作
用，少量約 0.2~2 wt% 之過渡性金屬分
散在碳材料，氫分子可分解為氫原子
[3]。2004 年, Matsumoto 等人[4]使用 Pt
金屬分散在碳管上材料成功的製成功能
訊號產生，證明有奈米碳管上有白金粒子
存在。由實驗結果顯示，在反應物中加入
少量的水有助於白金粒子的沉積，但因無
添加任何界面活性劑而使白金粒子巨大
化。 Teranishi[8]等人發表在合成白金過
程中，使用醇類當還原劑時，所形成奈米
粒子極容易受到醇的種類與濃度所影
響，所以選擇適當還原劑與濃度是控制白
金粒子粒徑的重要因素，因為還原劑濃度
與種類可以影響[PtCl6]-2 還原成 Pt0 之還
原速率，而還原劑沸點愈高愈容易於短時
間內在奈米碳管表面產生白金晶核 (Pt
nuclei)進而促進白金粒子成長，當[PtCl6]-2
還原速率加快時，便會產生愈小且均勻分
散之白金粒子。本實驗所使用還原劑乙二
醇沸點為 198 ℃可在較高溫製程反應下
還原白金粒子加速白金還原速率且其介
電常數和介電損失較高，因此在微波輻射
下能快速被加熱[9]，得較佳之白金粒子被
覆效果。乙醇與異丙醇介電常數、介電損
失和沸點低較不適合做為還原劑。

3.4 合成時間對白金粒子被覆奈米碳管之
效應
合成溫度 140℃，並添加等比例 PVP 與
SDS 於離子溶液中，以微波加熱方式被覆
白金粒子於奈米碳管表面，探討不同合成
時間對奈米碳管表面白金粒子之效應。持
溫時間 10min與 30min所形成白金粒子粒
徑約 4.5nm，當合成時間為 90min 時白金
粒子間彼此會堆疊在一起，粒徑約為
4.3nm；說明當合成時間過長只會造成白
金粒子相互堆疊，不會使白金粒子粒徑變
大。合成溫度 140℃持溫 0，0.5，5min 條
件下合成白金粒子於奈米碳管上之 TEM
影像圖，圖 7 中顯示微波聚醇法可於極短
時間內(升溫 1.5min 持溫 0 min)便可快速
將白金粒子被覆於奈米碳管表面，此結果
顯示白金粒子在升溫階段即已大量還原

I. Introduction
Microwave plasma chemical vapor deposition (MPCVD)
has been widely used for the growth of carbon nanotubes
and diamond film in H2 and CH4 mixture and H2 and C2H2
mixture. Since carbon nanotubes (CNTs) were discovered
by Iijima[1], many kinds of synthetic techniques have been
developed, such as arc discharge, laser ablation, pyrolysis,
plasma-enhanced chemical vapor deposition, thermal
chemical vapor deposition [2-7]. Some groups emphasized
the control of carbon nanotube orientation and synthesized
well-aligned carbon nanotubes by chemical vapor
deposition for various catalysts, gas sources and substrates.
Dai et al. [8] and Fonesca et al. [9] have studied the use of
Fe, Mo, Co, Ni as catalyst particles particularly on Si and
Al-based substrates. The catalyst, reaction temperature, and
reaction gas play important roles in controlling growth
quality of CNTs. Larger size catalyst particles with low
density and broad diameters are formed at higher
temperature [10]. Rodriguez [11] and Jose-Yacaman et al.
[12] have studied that the diameter of tubes was controlled
by the metal catalyst particles which activate the carbon
nanotubes growth. Kukovitsky et al. [13] have investigated
the correlation between mental catalyst particle size and
carbon nanotubes growth. Some groupies reported
pretreatment for varying and controlling the diameter of
carbon nanotube and investigated effect of metal and the
mass production methods [14-16 ].
In our previous studies, microwave plasma chemical
vapor deposition was successfully used to synthesize
multi-walled carbon nanotubes by carbon oxide and
methane gas mixture [17]. We found that by substituting
carbon oxide for hydrogen in CH4-CO2 gas mixture a high
yield vertically aligned carbon nanotubes with multi-walled
could be synthesized at low temperature [18].
In the present work, the deposited Fe-catalyst Si
substrate was used to grow CNTs using CH4 and CO2 gas
mixtures by MPCVD process We investigated the various
H2 plasma pretreatment effect on Fe catalyst and CNTs
morphology. The diameter of CNTs is controlled by the
metal catalyst particles. A field emission properties of
CNTs with different diameter was also investigated.
II. Experimental Procedures
The Fe catalyst nanoparticles were deposited on n-type
Si (100) wafer by sputtering method. The thickness of Fe
catalyst was 10nm. The experiments were carried out in a
microwave plasma chemical vapor deposition (MPCVD)
reactor. The 20x20 mm deposited metal-catalyst substrate
was pretreated using hydrogen gas with the flow rate of
200sccm. The H2 plasma pretreatment time was adjusted
from 0 to 15 min. The microwave power was maintained at
400W and total pressure at 15 Torr. After the H2 plasma
pretreatment, a mixture of CH4 and CO2 was used as
sources gas. The gas flow rate of CH4 and CO2 were set at

carbon nanotubes which causes the blunt morphology of
carbon nanotubes.

3.4. Field Emission properties
Figure 5 show the field emission properties of CNTs of
sample A1,A2, A3, and A4 respectively at different
pretreatment time. It can be seen that the CNTs exhibit high
field emission current by using CO2-CH4 gas mixture. Here,
the threshold field Ethr is defined as the field where current
density 10 mA is obtained. The Ethr are about 5.47
V/μm ,4.77 V/μm, 4.56 V/μm, and 3.86 V/μm for sample 
A1,A2,A3, and A4 respectively. The turn –on fields, Eon,
defined at the emission current density of 1μA/cm2 are
about 2.63 V/μm , 2.74 V/μm, 2.51V/μm and 2.28 V/μm 
for A1,A2,A3, and A4 respectively. The field emission
current density of all carbon nanotubes are larger than
10mA/cm2 at 5 V/μm and the value is known to be 
sufficient for flat panel displays. The corresponding
emission current densities are about 0.995 mA/cm2,
1.045mA/cm2 , 2.386 mA/cm2, and 10 mA/cm2 at the
electric field of 3.86 V/μm forA1, A2, A3, and A4
respectively. There are really good field emission properties.
Higher emission current density was obtained for sample
A4 with H2 plasma pretreatment of 15 min. This may be
higher yield and densely carbon nanotubes form at this
condition.
Figure 6 is a Fowler-Nordheim (F-N) plot of the field
emission data. A linear relationship between ln (J/E2) and 1/
E can be obtained from Fowler-Nordheim equation [4-21].
A F-N plot is used to verify the field emission
characteristics. Figure 6 shows the corresponding F-N plot
of carbon naotubes. Nearly straight line for sample A1, A2,
A3, and A4 has been obtained, but has a tendency to
deviate from F-N behavior at high electrical field. It
exhibits the field emission phenomena of all samples.
However the field emission has a tendency to deviate from
F-N behavior at high electrical field. for multi-walled
carbon nanotubes. Satio et al. [22] reported the same
behavior for the multi-walled carbon nanotubes. The
deviation of the field emission current may derive from the
space charge effects and non-metallic behavior as a result
of the presence of tip defect and catalyst particles on the
tubes tip [23].
So carbon nanotubes by the microwave plasma chemical
vapor deposition of methane and carbon dioxide gas
mixture have good field emission properties. The
relationship between field emission properties of carbon
nanotubes and the H2 pretreatment time is illustrated by
raising the current density of carbon nanotubes with
increasing the H2 pretreatment time. Hong et al. had
reported the higher emission current densities obtained
from the denser and sharper aligned carbon nanotubes [24].
Dependence of emission characteristic on H2 pretreatment
time may be attributed to the geometry and denseness of
carbon nanotubes but it needs further investigation.
