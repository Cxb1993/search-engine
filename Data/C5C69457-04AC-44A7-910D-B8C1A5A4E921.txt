摘  要 
本研究以幾丁聚醣搭配動物明膠與聚乙烯醇形成複合溶液後，藉由靜電紡絲技術將之
製成奈米級纖維支架。研究中探討各種複合纖維成絲的最佳條件(複合溶液組成及濃度、溶
劑種類、溶液黏度與導電度、外加電場強度、溶液擠出速率等)對纖維外觀與型態的影響，
並針對各種纖維基材進行材料性質測定及崩解速率比較等。由研究結果顯示幾丁聚醣、動
物明膠、聚乙烯醇以 2:2:4 重量比例混合並以 20%醋酸為溶劑，配合相對較低電壓與溶液擠
出速率(15 KV, 0.15 ml/hr)可製作出高品質的奈米纖維，再以戊二醛交聯增加奈米纖維穩定
性，發現經戊二醛蒸汽交聯 12 小時有最大的抗張強度，若交聯時間過久，基材的抗張強度
反而下降。此外，DSC 結果顯示複合纖維中各成分均勻混合，而以冷凍凝膠法製作出的海
綿狀基材則有相分離的現象。在細胞相容性測試方面，將間葉幹細胞培養在纖維狀基材上
有良好的增殖現象，然而在海綿狀基材上細胞則幾乎沒有增殖，至於細胞分化特性的分析
探討則受制於本研究計畫期限及經費均被減半而無法進行，可考慮列為未來研究方向。總
結本研究指出，幾丁聚醣/動物明膠/聚乙烯醇複合溶液可以藉由靜電紡絲技術成功製成奈米
纖維，再以戊二醛交聯可使纖維基材在材料性質方面有顯著的改進，亦可大幅調控的其崩
解能力，且具有相當良好的細胞相容性，因此幾丁聚醣/動物明膠/聚乙烯醇複合奈米纖維具
有生醫相關的應用潛力。

Abstract

composite solutions. Then, these solutions were used to fabricate fibrous scaffolds by 
electrospinning. This study investigated the effects of various parameters (such as the

20% acetic acid aqueous solution, the applied voltage of 15 KV, and the feed rate of 0.15 ml/hr.

Fabrication of Chitosan/Gelatin/Polyvinyl alcohol Composite 
Nanofibers by Electrospinning 
H.J. Hsieh*, R.Y. Tsai, T.Y. Kuo, Y.H. Chou 
Department of Chemical Engineering, National Taiwan University, No.1, Sec.4, Roosevelt 
Rd., Taipei 106, Taiwan 
Introduction 
Electrospinning utilizes high voltage electric field to induce the formation of nanofibers. The 
structure of a nanofibrous scaffold is similar to that of an extracellular matrix and thus the 
scaffold has great potential in tissue engineering-related applications. Chitosan (Chi) and 
gelatin (Gel), both being natural biopolymers, can be dissolved in an acetic acid aqueous 
solution. However, it is difficult to fabricate chitosan/gelatin composite nanofibers by 
electrospinning due to the high surface tension of the solution. Therefore, researchers sought 
other more expensive and toxic organic solvents such as hexafluoro-isopropanol and 
trifluoroacetic acid to dissolve chitosan and gelatin. In this study, we found that, by adding 
polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) to a chitosan/gelatin mixture solution, it was feasible to fabricate 
chitosan/gelatine/polyvinyl alcohol (Chi/Gel/PVA) composite nanofibers by electrospinning 
even though an acetic acid aqueous solution was used as a solvent. Our method avoids the use 
of expensive and toxic organic solvents and thus is more environmentally friendly. 
 
Materials and Methods 
Chitosan (M.W. 300 kDa, Kiotech, Taiwan) and gelatin (Sigma-Aldrich, USA) solutions were 
prepared in an acetic acid aqueous solution. Polyvinyl alcohol (Chang-Chun Plastic, Taiwan) 
solution was prepared in deionized water. To optimize the fabrication of nanofibers, 
Chi/Gel/PVA mixture solutions with final concentrations of Chi/Gel/PVA as 3/0/2, 3/1/2, 
2/2/2 and 1/3/2 (in wt %) were prepared. The Chi/Gel/PVA solution for electrospinning was 
fed into a 3-ml syringe (needle: 23 gauge). The distance between the needle tip and the 
aluminium collector was 12 cm and the DC voltage of 10 to 25 KV was applied. The 
electrospinning was carried out at a feed rate of 0.2 ml/hr. The SEM images of the obtained 
Chi/Gel/PVA composite nanofibers were taken using JEOL JSM-5310 (Japan).

The electrospinability of Chi/Gel/PVA mixture solutions was investigated. Chi/Gel/PVA 
composite nanofibers could be fabricated by electrospinning at 20 KV from mixtures with
