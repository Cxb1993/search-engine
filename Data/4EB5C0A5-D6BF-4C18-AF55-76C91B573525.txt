二、緣由及目的
第一個由Bergveld所做出之離子
感測場效電晶體(ISFET)為經修改配
置之場效電晶體(MOSFET)，在醫學領
域的應用上，其金屬閘極之功函數可
對應其不同之離子濃度做調整。延伸
自ISFET擴展閘極場效電晶體(EGFET)
將可以取代 ISFET。
隨著感測層材料之發展，因為低
成本，和矽兼容性高體積小不同之高
介電係數材料如 Si3N4、Al2O3和 HfO2被
發展出取代 SiO2 做為感測層。無論如
何，高介電係數材料和其製造過程有
高度之相關，我們將深入研究不同之
處理和不同材料之替代可達成更佳的
離子感測表現、較小之遲滯、較低之
飄移和較高穩定度已達到商業以及生
物醫藥產業之應用要求。
作為閘極，氧化钆(Gd2O3)有約
14~16 之介電係數、較寬之能隙
(~5.8eV)和較大的導帶偏移。該化合
物也可以作為一種保護、耐腐蝕功能
塗料，因其高化學和熱穩定性。
在本研究中，我們使用堆疊在複
晶矽且經快速熱處理(RTA)之氧化钆
(三氧化二钆)作為 PH 感測膜，此感測
膜可以做為高靈敏度、高線性度、低
遲滯電壓、和低漂移之擴展閘極場效
電晶體(EGFET)，其可作為未來之生物
醫療設備應用。
三、實驗方法
高介電常數之Gd2O3 和Gd2TiO5被
製作在n+複晶矽/氧化物/P型矽基板
上，首先在P型矽晶圓上做標準之RCA
清洗並使用稀釋之氫氟酸去除其原生
氧化物，接著堆疊600奈米之氧化層，
在溫度625oC下使用低壓化學氣相沉
積法(LPCVD)堆疊300奈米之複晶矽，
使用RF濺鍍機堆疊25奈米的高介電常
數Gd2O3感測層在經稀釋的氧環境下，
Gd2TiO5感測層則是使用RF濺鍍機堆疊
20奈米之Gd2O3在複晶矽上其氬和氧之
比例為20比5，功率為150瓦，壓力為
2×10-2托爾再接著堆疊5奈米之鈦金

1. Introduction
The first material of silicon dioxide (SiO2) had been proposed as a chemical transducer element of pH sensitive mem-
brane around the early of 1970s. In 1981 Matsuo et al. proposed Ta2O5 as pH sensing membrane [1]. Start from that moment,
many materials have been wildly investigated, e.g. Al2O3, SnO2, WO3.
The rare–earth oxide, Samarium oxide (Sm2O3), is an attractive material to substitute the previous high-k materials be-
cause of its high dielectric constant(15-30) and good chemical and thermal stability with Si material. In this paper, the elec-
trical and physical characteristics of the high-k Sm2O3 polyoxides by RF sputtering system deposited on the polycrystalline
silicon were studied. It can be seen that the high-k Sm2O3 polyoxides with post rapid thermal annealing (RTA) can show
lower trapping rates and higher dielectric breakdown fields. Furthermore, the EGFET device with a high-k Sm2O3 sensing
membrane deposited on polysilicon through reactive sputtering was proposed for high sensitive pH sensing.
2. Experiment
At first, the p-type Si wafers were thermally oxidized to have an oxidized layer of 600nm. Then, a amorphous-si film of
120nm was deposited at 550°C by low pressure chemical vapor deposition (LPCVD) system. It was implanted with phos-
phorous at a 5×1015 cm-2 dosage and 30KeV energy and then annealing 600°C 24hour in an N2 ambient to amorphous-si be-
come the poly-si. A thickness of 25nm high-k Sm2O3 dielectric layer was deposited on the polycrystalline silicon by RF
Sputtering system, and then post-annealed by rapid thermal annealing for 30sec in an N2 ambient at different temperatures
from 600-900°C to improve thin-film quality. An TiN gate of a thickness of 50nm was deposited on gate dielectric films and
then coating the electronic conductive. Finial, the zone of sensing membrane was defined by SU8-2005 epoxy as photoresist.
The schematic polyoxide process flow was shown in Figure 1 and Figure 2.
3. Results and Discussion
Figure 3 shows the XRD spectra of the high-k Sm2O3 film at various temperatures. The as-deposited films show weak pat-
terns, indicating poor crystallinity, where the films after RTA 700°C clearly exhibits an stronger (400) peak. Apart from the
strong (433), (603), and (205) peaks, minor ones are present in the films. Thus, our study shows that the texture of the Sm2O3
film on polyoxide is
strongly dependent on the RTA condition. The XPS results showing the variations in the Sm2O3 films as a function of an-
nealing temperature are discussed. In Figure 4(b), it can be noticed that the Sm3d5/2 peak slightly shifted to a higher binding
energy region compared with the reference position of 1084.3 eV when the annealing temperature was above 800°C, reveal-
ing the formation of Sm silicate. In Fig. 4(a), the peak composition occurred at 530.4, 531.8 and 533 eV is indicative of the
formation of Sm2O3, SmSixOy and SiO2, respectively. The SiO2 layer grew monotonically at the film/substrate interface due
to the oxygen diffusion toward the interface from the crystal defect and nonstoichiometry structure with the annealing tem-
perature [2]. Figure 5(a) and (b) show the J-V characteristics under both polarity for–Vg and +Vg, respectively. The break-
down voltage is increased as increasing post rapid thermal annealing temperature, and the leakage current is also slightly
suppressed under the high electrical voltage. This improvement of characteristics is believed to be due to the post-RTA an-
nealing treatment can passivate the dangling bonds and traps in the high-k Sm2O3 dielectric and the interface between the
high-k Sm2O3 dielectric and polysilicon. Figure 6(a) and (b) show the gate voltage shift ( Vg) versus stress time u△ nder
5μA/cm2 and -5μA/cm2 constant current stress for the above samples. It can be seen that the samples with RTA at 700°C in
N2 for 30s treatment had smaller gate voltage shifts in comparison with the control sample. The Figure 7 shows the IV
curves response of Sm2O3 dielectrics after RTA at 700°C when inserted into solutions with pH values from 2 to 12. The inset
shows reference voltage as a function of pH at room temperature. The result demonstrated that the total shift of reference
voltage for the Sm2O3 sensing membrane annealed at 700 °C is about 482mV from pH 2 to pH 12. Besides, the sensitivity of
such pH detection is calculated to be 48.2 mV/ pH and the linearity is 98.27%, exhibiting a better detection sensitivity and
linearity than the others, as also shown in Figure 8. The poly-Si EGFET device with the Sm2O3 sensing film fabricated at the
RTA 700°C showed a lower value of 7.14 mV, in sharp contrast with the value of 17.31 and 12.23mV occurred at the an-
nealing temperature above 600°C and 800°C, respectively.

leakage current and better constant current stress characteristics. Furthermore, the EGFET device with a high-k Sm2O3 sens-
