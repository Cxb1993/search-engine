中文研究摘要(500字以內)： 
 
聖約翰科技大學2006年成立精密儀器中心，精密儀器中心內含有一座Class 
1000之無塵室，以及多項奈米級材料量測設備 (包括：能量分散式X光繞射
儀、原子力顯微鏡、掃描式電子穿隧顯微鏡、掃描式光激發光譜儀、3D表面
輪廓儀、傅立葉轉換紅外光譜儀、拉曼光譜儀、高溫氧化擴散系統、電壓-
電流特性量測設備 ......等 )。提供鄰近半導體產業在薄膜特性許多檢測之工
具。然而受限於設備之昂貴，空間之有限，許多遠距離之合作實驗室或合作
廠商皆需耗時於車往返之間。因此本計畫結合數位網路產業之公司，利用半
導體薄膜檢測的影像資訊，藉由遠端網路控制，將測試影像藉網路IP攝影機
傳送技術，使遠端半導體檢測使用者可超越距離限制，進行即時影像觀看及
紀錄。傳統IP攝影機往往受限於設定程序過於繁複，無法提供多位研究者同
時操作，限制精密檢測儀器遠端共享的機會。為此，本計畫將深入探討網路
IP攝影機之設定方式跟遠端控制。研發重點針對上述半導體工程師之需求提
出解決方案，提供實用之遠距精密檢測平台，將可簡化工程師遠端IP攝影機
設定程序，達到遠端實驗室可即時研究討論之目的。 
 
英文研究摘要(500字以內)： 
 
The precision instrument center (PIC) of St. John’s University was founded in 2006 to 
support the measurement service for semiconductor related industry of adjacent area. It 
is consisted of a main part of 1000-class clean room and several 
manufacturing/measuring instruments (including X-ray diffraction (XRD), atom 
scanning microscopy (AFM), scanning electron microscopy (SEM), scanning 
photoluminescence (PL), 3D-profiler, Fourier transforms infrared spectroscopy (FTIR), 
Raman spectrum, high temperature dispersing furnace, IV/CV semiconductor analyzer 
et al). For the related industries and its business development in Taipei and vicinity, the 
complete and convenient services would be provided by our PIC department. However, 
the convenience of user’s service was limited by the generic problems such as PIC’s 
maximum user capacity, service overloading and customer’s riding time. For PIC’s 
business development and its service quality, the measurement technology developed on 
digital transmission configuration is an effective approach to improve the above 
problems. Based on the digital technology integration including network terminal, IP 
webcam apparatus, remote control and image transfer software, the custom homework 
goal could be achieved. Furthermore, the diversification services such as customization 
measurement and engineer’s special solution would be also performed. Hence, this 
proposed project is highly focused on the technology development including: 
integration between measurement apparatus and digital network software, simplifying 
webcam control procedure and enhancement of control quality.
