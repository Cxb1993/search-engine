技術說明
(中文) 本發明之土壤壓力試驗分析方法，係供一使用者應用一壓力分析系統來對一待測
土壤進行試驗，其包括有數據試驗：自該壓力分析系統取得一垂直荷重值與一垂
直位移值；數據計算：利用垂直位移值作為一垂直應變百分比值，來計算該待測
土壤之應變面積值，並將該垂直荷重值除以該應變面積值，來取得一最大垂直應
力值；破壞量測：量測該待測土壤破壞面與水平面的夾角；結果輸出：透過該夾
角與最大垂直應力值計算該待測土壤之凝聚力與內摩擦角，並輸出一垂直應力與
垂直應變百分比值的關係圖及一剪應力與垂直應力的關係圖，以作為該待測土壤
強度的參考。 
(英文) An analysis method for a soil pressure test is used for a user to test a soil by a soil 
pressure analysis system, which comprises following steps: getting a vertical load value 
and a vertical displacement value from the soil pressure analysis system; calculating a 
strain area value of the soil by using the vertical displacement value as a vertical strain 
percentage value, and getting a maximum vertical stress value by using the vertical load 
value divided by the strain area value; measuring an angle between a fracture plane of the 
soil and a horizontal plane of the soil; and calculating a cohesive force of the soil and an 
internal frictional angle of the soil by the angle and the maximum vertical stress value, 
and outputting two relationship diagrams for a reference about the strength of the soil, 
wherein one relationship diagram is about a vertical stress and the vertical strain 
percentage and another relationship diagram is about a shearing stress and the vertical 
stress.
