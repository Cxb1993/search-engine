2.1 實驗步驟與參數 
 
    本實驗利用RF射頻磁控濺鍍法在不同
基材基板上沉積三氧化鎢薄膜。實驗流程
圖如下圖一所示。在靶材方面使用陶瓷三
氧化鎢為靶材(直徑為 2吋，純度
99.99%)。基材清洗是將基材分別依序浸入
異丙酮、丙酮與甲醇中，均以超音波震盪 5
分鐘後，再以去離子水清洗震盪 5分鐘，
試片取出後以氮氣槍吹淨試片上的水氣，
放入烘乾箱調至 80 度靜置 30 分鐘。進行
濺鍍時，腔體須先抽至 5x10-6mtorr 在通入
濺鍍需要的氣體。本實驗是以不同的濺鍍
瓦數、濺鍍時間為參數。而使用的濺鍍氣
體為固定流量比 Ar:O2 為 5:10。

In general, under room temperature only amorphous Ta2O5 thin film can be formed. There are two ways to 
obtain 
crystalline Ta2O5 thin film: one is by elevating the substrate and the other is to sputter amorphous Ta2O5 thin 
film 
first and then anneal the film at higher temperatures. However, both ways require high temperature processing. 
The 
annealing temperature ranges from 600 to 900°C and it can the done with the assistance of gas or plasma. As 
in 
Beckage et al. [15], the DC magnetron sputtered amorphous Ta2O5 thin film was annealed at temperature 
above 
600°C for extended time small discrete crystallites in amorphous matrix start to form. Laser assisted rapid 
annealing was also applied by [Nakagawa and Igarashi]. Nakagawa et al. [16] was the first to report the 
piezoelectricity in Ta2O5 thin film. By the same DC magnetron sputtering method, minimum substrate 
temperature 
to achieve piezoelectric preferred oriented Ta2O5 thin film was 450°C although better quality crystalline 
structure required higher temperature. Although most of the researches and applications were on amorphous 
Ta2O5 thin film, however, we did find crystalline Ta2O5 thin film has higher dielectric constant, optical 
index, and 
piezoelectric properties. However, the heating process is favorable for electric applications due to the durable 
substrate temperature it's preferred to develop a lower temperature deposition process. 
Most crystalline Ta2O5 thin films reported were in &#945; or &#946; phase orthorhombic structure [Chen13, 
Nakagawa16] with some exception of monoclinic and &#948;-hexagonal structure. These structures have 
asymmetric axis and share the property of piezoelectricity [Jaffe et al.71]. 
The experimental procedure is the same as described in [3]. The ceramic target is prepared as follows. 
Tantalum 
oxide powders (99.993%, density 8.2 g/cm3, Alfa Aesar) in 26g are ball milled with isopropyl alcohol for 
three 
hours. After being oven-dried the powders are pressed into a 2.5" dia. mold under 24MPa pressure. The 
molded 
target is then sintered at 1250°C for 6 hours and naturally cooled to room temperature. The target is shrunk 
and 
then ground to 2" dia. The completed ceramic target is then placed at 2" distance from silicon (p-type [1 0 0]) 
substrate. The substrate is heated and controlled at the designated temperatures during the sputtering 
processes.

Abstract 
Tantalum pentoxide (Ta2O5) thin film has high dielectric constant and stable chemical properties. It has been 
a 
good solution for tunneling effect incurred by the reduction of gate length in semiconductors. In addition, it 
has 
high refraction coefficient and band gap, thus has potential applications on solar cells. From literatures, as 
grown 
in preferred (2 0 0) axis crystalline structure it also has even higher dielectric constants and good piezoelectric 
property (high k33) and could be used in MEMS, resonator, and filters (FBAR, surface acoustic wave 
devices). 
In this study, RF magnetron sputtering process was applied to deposit Ta2O5 crystalline films with ceramic 
Ta2O5 
target with 400°C substrate temperature without annealing. TEM and ellipsometry measurements were carried 
out 
to identify the micro-crystalline structure and optical indices. The Ta2O5 thin film was identified in &#946;- 
phase 
orthorhombic structure. The optical index (maximum value of 2.6 at 550nm wavelength) is strongly affected 
by the 
film quality, i.e., the more compact the higher. 
Key words: tantalum pentoxide, thin film, RF magnetron sputtering, orthorhombic structure

感測器的發展史中，亦如近數十年來產業之發展歷程；產業自電機、機械、化工產業逐漸
朝向電子業，感測器之發展即朝向結合電子電路之智慧型感測。 
 
感測器共有四個組件： 
 
1. 感測元件 
2. 訊號處理 
3. 訊號加工 
4. 訊號表現 
 
化學感測器起步晚，但也已經有可觀的成果，尤其在環境監測之應用也逐年擴大規模，以
電壓，電流量測為基礎之電化學也引入新近發展的電學原理，化學感測不確定之撰寫被承
認是化學感測之基本要求；化學感測器研發中目前最大的限制條件是量測對象的多重成份
與其待測對象其分佈的非均勻性。 
 
 壓電晶體化學感測器為一種對質量變化非常靈敏之化學感測器，可偵測空氣及液體中非
常微量的質量變化及組成成份，可應用在環境污染、生醫化學、工業、食品、家居安全、
化學分析及化反應研究各方面。
