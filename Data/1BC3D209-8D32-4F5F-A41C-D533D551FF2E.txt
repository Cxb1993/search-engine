The availability and cost of precursor materials for biotechnological products are influenced 
by numerous factors including; climate, species, cultivation methods, and market demand. 
Fermentation technology offers an alternative production model by which we can duplicate 
products which are used in the food, beverage, cosmetic, pharmaceutical and other industries. 
The research and development of fermentation tanks has become a major direction of 
biotechnology industry. 
Some agricultural products contain special ingredients which are healthy for human (Ex: 
Ȗ-Aminobutyric acid of mulberry leaf), but these components are necessary to be operated by 
anaerobic fermentation process. So, in this project novel aerobic and anaerobic fermentation 
equipments are designed and manufactured including dimensions of tank, shapes and position of 
impeller, and baffles. Then, the study will also analyze the dynamic behavior of fluid inside the 
tank by numerical simulation. The results will be the guideline of manufacture for potential future 
large-scale fermented tank production.

a Novel Fermentation System
遠東科技大學 汪正祺
由於生物科技產品的原物料多數取自於大自然，生長環境容易受氣候、品種和
培植方法的影響，許多高單價或不易取得的原物料成分可利用發酵製程技術來
加以複製生產，除可運用在食品、飲品、醫療藥品和美容保養品等產品外，更
可應用在生產高經濟價值的生技產業。由於有些本土農產品中具有對人體助益
相當大的成分(如：桑葉當中的γ-胺基丁酸)，但需要在厭氧狀態下進行發酵程
序，因此在本技術主要包括好氧式及厭氧式發酵槽，涵蓋槽體尺寸、攪拌翼片
形狀、配置位置及檔板大小數量等，以製造適合各種不同需求之發酵設備儀器
。
The availability and cost of precursor materials for biotechnological
products are influenced by numerous factors including; climate,
species, cultivation methods, and market demand. Fermentation
technology offers an alternative production model by which we can
duplicate products which are used in the food, beverage, cosmetic,
pharmaceutical and other industries. The research and development of
fermentation tanks has become a major direction of biotechnology
industry. Some agricultural products contain special ingredients
which are healthy for human (Ex: γ-Aminobutyric acid of mulberry
leaf), but these components are necessary to be operated by anaerobic
fermentation process. So, this technique is associated with novel
aerobic and anaerobic fermentation equipments designed and食品、飲料及菸類製造業
本技術所開發之相關設備，可配合不同產品的研發導入最佳化製程，同時具有大型槽
體的擴充能力，甚至達到量產的階段。不僅可應用在健康食品的製備外，對於飲品、
美容保養品與醫藥工業皆具有實際應用的價值，並能提高企業的生產力與競爭力。
目前正與廠商洽談合作技轉事宜，包括將製程、儀器等相關技術進行轉移，技轉可行
