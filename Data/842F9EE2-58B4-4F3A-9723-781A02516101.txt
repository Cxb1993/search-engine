catalyst in the modern and future chemical

Polypropylene is an important plastic raw material 
and has been widely used to manufacture a lot of 
commodities. Some unenvironmental friendly plastics, 
e.g. PVC, were already replaced by PP polymer. The 
global annual demanding of PP raw materials thus 
keeps in a high amounts and leads to a large quantity 
of post-consumer plastics wastes produced. From the 
point of view of protecting natural environment and 
the limited carbon resource on the earth, how to 
treat these wasted plastics products in a more 
resourceful way is very important. The main treatment 
method of wasted plastics nowadays is physical 
instead of chemical, however, the latter one is more 
resourceful and to be developed in the future. 
    This project is going to study the degradation of 
PP over some neutral, acidic, and basic ionic liquids 
to screen the most active and follow by using some 
instrument analysis methods, such as 1H-NMR, FTIR and 
UV-visible, to characterize the self-made and 
modified ionic liquids. Finally, the most active 
ionic liquid is applied on PP degradation to find the 
optimal reaction conditions. The possible degradation 
parameters include temperature (110-170oC), time (1-
16 h), and the weight ratio of ionic liquid to PP 
(0.25-3.0). The results demonstrate the optimal PP 
degradation condition over ionic liquid [Hmim]Cl is 
at 150oC for 4 h using the weight ratio of IL/PP 
equal to 1.0.

摘要 
    由於離子液體其具有諸多特點，如熱穩定性高、低蒸氣壓、可調整對有機物、無機物和聚合
物的相容性、可重複多次使用等，是一種具環保概念的綠色溶劑及觸媒，因而有關其製備、性質鑑
定與應用，已有相當多的研究。聚丙烯是一種具有優異物理和化學性質的塑膠原料，已被廣泛使用
於多種日用產品的製造上，且已取代多種傳統材料及部分較不具環境友善的塑膠材料，全世界的需
求與使用量都極高。如何資源化處理大量的廢棄塑膠，一直是資源有效應用的熱門課題。有關廢棄
塑膠的化學處理方式極多，但以離子液體降解塑膠的較為少見。 
    本聚丙烯降解反應是在常壓下，於桌上型 50 ml 高壓反應器內進行，首先比較數種酸性、中性
與鹼性單一離子液體對聚丙烯降解轉化率的影響，由此篩選出最具降解活性之離子液體後，再行製
備及進行其結構與性質鑑定，最後探討數個可能對聚丙烯降解結果會造成變化的因素影響。實驗結
果顯示，以中性離子液體[Hmim]Cl 對於聚丙烯降解最具活性，這與傳統聚丙烯觸媒裂解反應所常
使用的固態酸有所不同；而所製備的離子液體以 1H-NMR 及 FTIR 分析結果與標準品比對，具有正
確的結構。此外，將所合成的中性離子液體與以酸性功能化，也顯示可成功合成酸性離子液體。在
使用單一離子液體下，有關降解反應因素對聚丙烯降解轉化率的影響，如:降解溫度(110-170oC)、
時間(1-16 h)、離子液體與聚丙烯的使用重量比(0.25-4)等，本實驗也都有進行探討。結果顯示，當
離子液體與聚丙烯使用重量比為 1.0、溫度為 150oC、反應時間達 4 h 的條件下，聚丙烯會有最適之
降解效果。

III 
     廢棄塑膠的化學回收方式，有水解(Hydrolysis)、醇解(Alcoholysis)和裂解(Cracking or pyrolysis)
等方式，但屬加成性聚合物的聚丙烯，多以裂解進行並以獲得再生燃料為主要目的。裂解是使單一
或混合塑膠在高溫缺氧下，利用熱或觸媒使其降解成分子量較低的燃料，但屬自由基反應的熱裂
解，轉化率較差且需較高的裂解條件，產物分佈廣且偏向大分子量碳氫化合物，較不具實用價值。
若為陽/陰離子觸媒裂解，可利用觸媒的活性或選擇性，獲得較好的成效。以聚丙烯為例，其觸媒
裂解所使用的觸媒以固態酸最具活性，尤其是酸性沸石觸媒如: HY、HZSM5及silica-alumina等；但
該程序有觸媒易失活及與產物不易分離和必須消耗碳源的問題。因此，開發有效且具環境友善的酸
性綠色催化製程，是處理廢棄聚丙烯塑膠的重要方向。 
 近年來，利用環境友善的離子液體進行塑膠降解，已開始出現在被應用至塑膠降反應上，在
適當降解條件下，塑膠降解轉化率及單體回收都有不錯的成果，但尚未受到大家的注意。目前為止，
並沒有以離子液體應用在降解聚丙烯塑膠的相關研究報導。本實驗除比較數種常見的中性、鹼性及
酸性離子液體對聚丙烯的降解活性之外，也針對數種離子液體自行合成並加以鑑定其結構和性質，
同時探討最具活性之離子液體對聚丙烯的降解轉化率及產物組成的影響。 
研究目的 
由文獻結果顯示，以離子液體為溶劑或/及觸媒進行聚合物的降解反應是可行的，只要篩選出
適當的離子液體和反應條件，可使聚合物進行降解並回收聚合單體或其他化合物。如有需要，離子
液體亦可經由酸性功能化後，使其應用至化學反應及程序上。由於過去在聚丙烯觸媒降解反應，固
態酸是一種相當有活性的觸媒，但卻面臨觸媒容易失活及觸媒與產物不易分離等問題的困擾。因
此，如能利用離子液體所具有的特點，將其應用在聚丙烯降解反應上，並進行離子液體的合成、性
質鑑定以及探討降解反應條件對聚丙烯降解轉化率及產物組成的影響，相信對聚丙烯回收的資源化
處理有其價值性，並提供一種有效而且符合綠色概念的廢棄塑膠處理方法與程序，對地球永續發展
與經營也會有所幫助。

 研究方法 
1. 離子液體的製備 
本實驗用來製備離子液體所需之化學試劑均為市售試藥級以上之產品，使用前並無經過特殊處
理，而直接使用。製備所得之離子液體如有市售相同商品可做為比對用之標準品，均於製備完成後
與標準品做結構比較，而市售離子液體 (如 : [Emim]CF3COO、 [Bmim]PF6、 [Bmim]OH 與
[Bmim]CH(CN)3)亦可做為催化活性篩選之用。中性離子液體製備過程以 1-己基-3-甲基咪唑氯鹽
(1-Hexyl-3- methylimidazolium chloride，[Hmim]Cl)為例，將 1-甲基咪唑(1-Methylimidazole，41.055 
g，0.5 mol)加入裝有加熱(油浴)回流之 250 mL 雙頸燒瓶內，再緩慢加入 1-氯己烷(1-Chlorohexane，
60.31 g，0.5 mol) 反應溫度為 70℃，在攪拌下，反應 72 小時。反應結束，以 40 mL 乙酸乙酯沖洗
混合液三次後，經減壓濃縮機，加熱至 70℃、蒸餾 2 小時以去除未反應之原料，再於真空烘箱溫
度 70℃烘乾 48 小時，即可得到 1-己基-3-甲基咪唑氯鹽離子液體。酸性離子液體製備過程則以 1-
丁基-3 甲基咪唑磺酸鹽(1-Butyl-3-methylimidazolium hydrogensulf，[Bmim]HSO4)為例，將 1-丁基-3
甲基咪唑氯鹽(1-Butyl-3-methylimid-azolium chloride，8.2 g，0.1 mol)加入裝有冰浴之 250 mL 雙頸
燒瓶內，再緩慢加入硫酸水溶液(Sulfuric acid，10 g，0.1 mol + 10 ml 水)，在劇烈攪拌下 30 分鐘，
硫酸滴完後將冰浴移除，以 70℃反應 2 小時。反應結束後，將混合液先以乙酸乙酯清洗 2 次再經
減壓濃縮機，加熱至 70℃、蒸餾 2 小時以去除未反應之原料，再於真空烘箱溫度 70℃烘乾 2 小時，
即可獲得。其餘[PSPy]HSO4、[Hmim]CF3COO、[Bmim]BF4、[Hmim]HSO4、[Emim]HSO4 等離子
液體的製備與上述過程相似，但[Bmim]BF4 則是將前驅物 KBF4溶於丙酮中，其餘皆利用酸性水溶
液(硫酸或三氟醋酸)。 
2. 離子液體的鑑定 
所製備的離子液體主要利用:1H-核磁共振光譜儀(1H-NMR)及傅立葉紅外光譜儀(FTIR)等儀器設
備鑑定其結構，並以電位滴定法鑑定酸性離子液體的酸度。1H-核磁共振光譜分析是以 Bruker 公司
製造型號為 DBX200 的核磁共振光譜儀進行，所用的檢測 D 溶劑為 D2O 或 CDCl3，分析步驟則依
標準程序進行。傅立葉紅外光譜分析所使用的紅外光譜儀為 Perkin Elmer 公司型號為 System 2000
型之儀器，測量範圍可從 200～4000 cm-1，操作步驟則依一般程序即可。 
3. 聚丙烯的降解反應 
本降解反應是在 50 mL 的桌上微型高壓反應器內進行，升溫以加熱包配合溫度控制器準確控制
反應器內部溫度。首先將已知重量比的反應物:聚丙烯、離子液體加入反應器的玻璃內襯中，以溫
度控制器控制反應器內部溫度並加以攪拌，待反應器內溫度升至反應所需溫度即設為反應起始時
間。反應結束後，將反應器由加熱包移出，使其降溫至室溫後，先以氣體收集袋於取樣閥收集氣體
產物，以 GC 或 GC/MS 分析其組成與濃度。再以 20~25 mL 乙醚沖洗反應後之混合物，由於乙醚
可溶解產物且不與離子液體相溶，因而可將產物與離子液體分離。經過濾後，附著於濾餅上之固體
物質，分別再以乙醇及去離子水清洗數次，去除可能殘留於表面的離子液體。固體經乾燥秤重後，
可分析其組成並計算聚丙烯降解的重量損失百分率；液體部分，則可利用 GC、GC/MS 或 HPLC
分析其組成。

結論 
    由本實驗的結果可知，聚丙烯以離子液體進行降解時，酸性離子液體的活性並非最佳，而以中
性[Hmim]Cl 最具活性，這與聚丙烯觸媒裂解反應中，使用酸性固態觸媒具有最佳活性的情形不同。

ABSTRACT 
Hydrogen production by partial oxidation of ethanol (POE) is studied for a series of different Pt loading on 
various support catalysts under several catalytic conditions at atmospheric pressure. The catalysts were 
prepared by chemical reduction, with ethylene glycol (EG) as a reducing agent. Of these prepared catalysts, 
the Pt/CNTs catalyst was an excellent catalyst for selective and effective production of hydrogen in the POE 
reaction. The purity, surface and pore structure of carbon nanotubes were improved after acid treatment and 
proved beneficial to the POE reaction. The acid treated carbon nanotubes and the prepared catalysts were 
characterized using N2 physisorption, micro-Raman analysis, thermogravimetric analysis (TGA), 
and transmission electron microscopy (TEM). The particle size and dispersion of the metal clusters on the 
prepared catalysts, as well as the hydrogen selectivity in POE, depends on the pH value of the reducing 
agent and the calcination temperature used in the preparation of catalysts. Under the best preparation 
conditions (pH value 10 and at 120oC) for a 5wt% Pt/CNTs catalyst, metal clusters of 2 nm were obtained. 
The catalyst’s activities in the POE reaction were further investigated in a fixed bed reactor. Using the catalyst 
prepared under the best preparation conditions and reacted at the optimal oxygen to ethanol molar ratio of 
0.5 and reaction temperature 400oC in the POE reaction, the conversion of ethanol, hydrogen selectivity and 
hydrogen production rate ate 98.2%, 97.5% and 202.3 mmol s-1 kg-1
 
, respectively. 
 
INTRODUCTION 
Hydrogen provides energy with high efficiency and low emissions to generate power at the point of use. If 
hydrogen production in situ can supply the needs of power generator, the emission of carbon oxides and air 
pollutants due to transportation can also be avoided. It will be both economical and environmentally beneficial 
(Balla and Weitschel, 2009). 
In contrast to hydrocarbon compounds derived from fossil fuels, bio-ethanol is a promising renewable 
untreated material that can be readily and easily produced in great quantities from the fermentation of 
biomass to generate hydrogen very efficiently (Fatsikostas et al., 2002). It also produces less greenhouse 
gases. Compared with other possible hydrogen producing liquids, such as methanol, ethanol is not only more 
easily handled, stored and transported, but also has lower toxicity and higher energy density. Ethanol is a 
capable and attractive source for hydrogen production (Youn et al., 2010).  
Catalytic steam reforming (SR), partial oxidation (PO) and auto-thermal reforming (ATR), which combine 
steam reforming and partial oxidation, are the three primary processes used to produce hydrogen from 
ethanol or other hydrocarbons (Holladay et al., 2009). Each process has its own advantages and 
disadvantages. In the case of ethanol as a hydrogen source, a main advantage of the partial oxidation 
process is that the reaction can be carried out at a lower reaction temperature and without an external heat 
supply, because it is an exothermic process. Many studies have focused on hydrogen production by SR 
(Kazama et al., 2010; Domok et al., 2010; Chen et al., 2010; Duhamel et al., 2010; Mendes et al., 2010) and 
ATR (Youn et al., 2010; Cavallaro et al., 2003). Hydrogen production from ethanol by partial oxidation over a 
series of Ni-Fe catalysts has been studied for various O2 and C2H5OH molar ratio at 473-573 K and showed 
high activities, especially for Ni50Fe50 (Wang et al.,2002). Mattos and Noronha (2005) studied the effect of 
the nature of the support on the performance of Pt catalysts, using Al2O3, CeO2, ZrO2 and Ce0.5Zr0.5O2 as 
supports for the partial oxidation of ethanol. They found that the distribution of the products obtained was 
related to the redox properties of the catalysts. Acetic acid was the main product using a Pt/Al2O3 catalyst, 
whereas methane and acetaldehyde were the only products detected for Pt/CeO2, Pt/ZrO2 and 
Pt/Ce0.5Zr0.5O2 catalysts. Hsu et al. (2008) also investigated the partial oxidation of ethanol using Pt/Al2O3, 
Pt/ZnO and Pt/ZrO2 catalysts and reported that the conversion of ethanol was greater than 50% for all 
catalysts at the low reaction temperature of 370 K. The selectivity for hydrogen and carbon monoxide varied 
for the different catalysts. The Pt/ZrO2

Characterization of acid-treated CNTs 
The TEM images of the untreated CNTs and acid treated CNTs used in this study are shown in Figs. 1(a) and 
(b), respectively. The images show the curved shape of the dense and very tangled CNTs before acid 
treatment. This curvature is caused by the existence of a five-member ring or seven-member carbon ring (sp3 
bonding), instead of a six-member carbon ring (sp2
 
 bonding) structure on the CNTs surface. In addition, the 
Van der Waals forces on the CNTs surface lead to the twisting together of most of the CNTs. This figure also 
shows a lot of metal particles protruding from the end of the nanotubes, like whiskers, which may be the 
residues of the catalyst used in the CNTs manufacturing process. The outside diameter and length of 
nanotubes were estimated to be between 25-40 nm and 250-300 nm, respectively. If the CNTs are dried 
directly in air and without further ultrasonic treatment in ethanol after the acid treatment, they aggregate 
together much more and are very tangled. When the acid-treated CNTs are further treated with ethanol, 
however, the TEM image of the CNTs in Fig. 1 (b) shows a very loose and obviously less tangled structure. 
The image also demonstrates that this treatment not only shortens the length of nanotubes and increases the 
roughness of the nanotubes outer surface, but also effectively removes the metal residue that protrudes from 
the end of the nanotubes. The energy dispersive X-ray analysis (EDX, Hitachi S-2600H) shows that the Fe 
and Al atom content in untreated and acid treated CNTs was reduced from 0.31% and 0.92% to 0.02% and 
0.21%, respectively.

CONCLUSIONS 
CNTs-supported platinum catalysts were prepared by a chemical reduction method and successfully applied 
in the partial oxidation of ethanol. The purity, surface and pore structure of the untreated carbon nanotubes is 
obviously improved by suitable acid treatment, which is also beneficial to the POE reaction. The particle size 
and dispersion of the platinum on the prepared catalysts and the POE activity depends on the pH value of the 
reducing agent and the calcination temperature during catalyst preparation. The POE performance for 
hydrogen selectivity and the hydrogen production rate for Pt/CNTs catalyst is much better than that for Pt  on 
the other supports. Both reaction temperature and the platinum loading of CNTs-supported catalysts are 
important factors in the POE reaction. It was also observed that POE with a lower molar ratio O2/C2H5OH of 
0.5 produced better hydrogen selectivity and a higher hydrogen production rate than higher ratios. 
Decreasing the molar ratio O2/C2H5OH from 1.5 to 0.5 causes a dramatic increase in the maximum 
hydrogen production rate from 137.9 mmol/kg/s to 202.3 mmol/kg/s and the hydrogen selectivity increases 
from 46.1% to 97.5%, at a reaction temperature of 400o
 
C. The best hydrogen production rate of 321.2 
mmol/kg/s for the 15wt% Pt/CNTs catalyst is attributed to a higher active metal loading and a uniform metal 
distribution on the CNTs surface. The as-prepared catalyst may have good potential for the production of 
hydrogen from ethanol.
