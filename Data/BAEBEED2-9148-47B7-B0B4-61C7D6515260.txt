二. 研究摘要： 
二段旋轉式寶特瓶吹瓶機的吹瓶模座機構是由吹瓶輪以及開關模座連桿與凸輪機構
所組成，其動力特性影響寶特瓶製程的穩定性。本研究利用運動曲線設計、連桿尺寸設
計及配重設計，針對開關模座連桿與凸輪機構進行動力特性分析和改善，以降低機器震
動和噪音問題為首要目標。 
本研究首先利用傅利葉級數與B-spline曲線作為運動曲線之設計手段，設計新型運動
曲線，接著透過向量迴路法配合運動係數法分析各個桿件之運動特性。根據運動分析的
結果，利用包絡線原理與壓力角定義，求得凸輪輪廓外型與壓力角數值。再根據d’Alembert
與牛頓運動定律，建立運動靜動力分析的數學模式，得到輸入扭矩、桿件接頭作用力、
搖撼力及搖撼力矩。最後根據分析結果，針對開關模座連桿機構進行尺寸設計，透過最
佳化方法，最大壓力角改善53.63%，輸入扭矩之方均根值改善25.95%，搖撼力方均根值
改善38.63%，搖撼力矩方均根值改善46.04%。 
本研究之成果可改善開關模座連桿與凸輪機構之運動與靜動力特性，降低開關模區間
所產生的衝擊力，亦降低開關模凸輪機構的壓力角與輸入扭矩，進一步提升吹瓶機之運
轉速度與產量。

Abstract 
The blow mold station of the two stage 
rotary type blow-molding machine is consist 
of a blow wheel, a pair of open-close mold 
linkage mechanism and an open-close mold 
cam mechanism. The productivity of the 
machine is dependent on the dynamic 
characteristics of the blow mold station. By 
utilizing motion curve design, optimal 
dimensional design and counterweight design, 
the dynamic characteristics can be improved, 
and the vibration problem can be thus 
reduced. 
In this study, firstly, the motion curves 
are designed by two novel design approaches, 
namely Fourier series and B-spline curve. 
Secondly, by using vector loop approach and 
the kinematic coefficients, the kinematic 
analysis are carried out. According to the 
kinematic analysis, the cam profile is 
synthesized based on the envelope theorem, 
and the pressure angle value is thus defined. 
Then based on the d’Alembert’s principle 
and Newton’s laws, the input torque, the 
bearing force, the shaking force, and the 
shaking moment analysis are performed. 
Finally, by utilizing the optimal design, a 
novel dimensional design is obtained, and the 
maximum pressure angle value is lowered by 
53.63%, the root mean square value of the
