摘要 
 本研究之共軛高分子係以具有電子傳遞性質的順丁烯亞醯胺(Maleimide)衍
生物與具有電洞傳遞特性之噻吩(Thiophene)，透過 Suzuki Coupling 進行共聚合
反應，並以甲苯進行分子鏈末端終止反應(End Capping)，合成出具有推拉電子 
(D-A；Donor-Acceptor) 結構之主鏈型順丁烯亞醯胺共軛高分子(PTML)；此外，
以具有乙烯基(Vinyl groups) 之交聯基團的Maleimide與Thiophene共聚合成出具
有不同交聯度之兩種交聯型順丁烯亞醯胺高分子(PTMLA-1:1、PTMLA-2:1)。將
所合成之三種高分子進行結構之鑑定，並對其溶解性、熱穩定性、電化學性質、
光學性質、高分子薄膜之表面型態及光伏特性加以分析探討。主鏈型高分子材
料，因導入具有耐熱性質之順丁烯亞醯胺，使之具有高的熱裂解溫度(Td 為
398.7℃)；交聯型高分子材料，不僅含有剛硬結構之 Maleimide 且因加熱使成為
網狀交聯結構，明顯提高熱裂解溫度(Td為 431.86℃)。高分子 PTML、PTMLA-1:1
與 PTMLA-2:1 之薄膜態的紫外光-可見光最大吸收波長分別為 454、449、448 
nm。螢光光譜最大放射波峰位置分別為 601、600、603 nm。在高分子太陽能電
池之應用中，以 PTML、PTMLA-1:1、PTMLA-2:1 分別摻混不同 PCBM 含量之
複合材料作為高分子電池之光電轉換膜，所製作之太陽能電池其光伏特性以 1:8
摻混比例最佳，光電轉換效率分別為 0.162 %、0.076 %、0.135 %。以交聯型高
分子 PTMLA-1:1、PTMLA-2:1 分別摻混 PCBM 所製作之太陽能電池，雖然光電
轉換效率不如 PTML 之元件，但交聯型高分子元件於照光之下，因材料結構較
為剛硬穩定而使分子鏈段不易被破壞並且光電轉換薄膜之表面型態較穩定，故電
池操作穩定性表現較佳。

3.3.1 紫外光-可見光光譜(UV-vis)分析 
    圖3.4為高分子溶液態與薄膜態 
(20mg/mL)之紫外光-可見光吸收光譜
圖。從圖中得知三種高分子於溶液
態、薄膜態皆有兩個主要的吸收峰且
吸 收 光 譜 極 為 相 似 。 P T M L 、
PTMLA-1:1、PTMLA-2:1 於吸收光譜
中，長波長的吸收為 Maleimide 基團 
n-π﹡能階躍遷所造成，而短波長的吸收
則為共軛高分子主鏈的 π-π﹡能階躍遷
所造成。此研究結果與文獻[12-13]相符
合。PTML、PTMLA-1:1、PTMLA-2:1 
於溶液態之兩個主要吸收峰，長波長
分別為 447 nm、442 nm、445 nm，而
短波長分別為 329 nm、324 nm、326 
nm；而薄膜態之兩個吸收峰，長波長
分別為 454 nm、449 nm、448 nm，短
波長分別為 331 nm、327 nm、329 
mm。此三種高分子於溶液態之紫外光
-可見光吸收光譜，並沒有顯著差異，
據推測此系列高分子之主鏈於溶劑中
的扭曲 (Twist) 現象近似，使三種高
分子保有相同程度的共平面性，故 
UV-vis 吸收波長相近。而固態薄膜移
去溶劑效應後，使得高分子間的距離
靠近，導致分子間作用力增強，因此
主鏈間產生鏈聚集與推疊之現象，導
致吸收範圍往長波長方向位移與變
寬。圖3.5～3.6分別為PTMLA-1:1、
PTMLA-2:1 高分子交聯前後之紫外
光 - 可 見 光 吸 收 光 譜 圖 ， 其 
PTMLA-1:1、PTMLA-2:1 之交聯態兩
個主要吸收峰，長波長分別為  443 
nm、441 nm，而短波長分別為 322 
nm、335nm。由圖譜中發現高分子經
過  200℃、90分鐘的加熱交聯反應
後，UV-vis 吸收波長有輕微藍位移現
象，推測可能因為高分子形成網狀交
聯結構後，使高分子主鏈產生輕微的
扭曲現象，進而導致紫外光-可見光吸
收產生藍位移現象。並且，交聯後之 
PTMLA-1:1、PTMLA-2:1 在短波長的
吸收皆較交聯前之  PTMLA-1:1、
PTMLA-2:1 高，推測高分子因交聯後
使得分子與分子間排列更為緊密，相
當於薄膜中單位長度的濃度提高，根
據 Beer Lambert Law[14]可知此現象導 
致紫外光-可見光吸收強度增加。

圖3.18～3.19分別為交聯型高分

比例最佳，光電轉換效率分別為 0.076

化學結構、物理性質與 PTML 高分子
相類似，故摻混不同重量比例之
PCBM，其元件所獲得之光伏特性變化 
趨勢與 PTML 元件一致。 
 由 PTMLA-1:1、PTMLA-2:1 與 
PTML 掺混 PCBM 製作成高分子太
陽能電池之 I-V 圖，比較後得知，交
聯型高分子之光伏特性與PTML 高分
子有明顯的差異。P T M L A- 1 : 1、
PTMLA-2:1 與  PTML 以  1:4 的 
PCBM 摻混比例於相同條件下比較，
其光電轉換效率分別為  0.049 %、
0.096 % 、0.148 %，而造成兩種交聯
型高分子之光電轉換效率較低的原
因，據推測可能為高分子薄膜於加熱
交聯後，會造成高分子鏈與 PCBM形
成嚴重的團聚現象，使電荷載子無法
有效的傳遞與分離，導致光伏特性降
低，且高分子薄膜在高溫及長時間下
交聯聚合時，雖然加熱過程中高分子
薄膜亦會同時達到熱處理效應，使分
子排列更趨於整齊，改善高分子薄膜
之缺陷密度，但並沒有因此提高元件
之光電轉換效率，推測原因為交聯聚
合完成後於降溫過程中可能會導致高
分子產生冷結晶型態，造成高分子鏈
段產生堆疊，導致主動層之表面粗糙
度上升，使得光伏元件串聯電阻上
升，進而造成元件之電流密度與效率
下降。PTMLA-1:1、PTMLA-2:1 摻混 
PCBM 之元件，其填充因子 (FF) 較 
PTML 之元件低，推測可能為加熱交
聯過程中，薄膜之表面粗糙度上升，
造成表面不平整現象，導致主動層與
金屬電極無法緊密的結合，使得漏電
流與串聯電阻上升，影響了電荷載子 
的傳遞能力。

發表論文題目: Electroluminescence Properties of Novel Hyper-branched Light-Emitting 
Polymers Based Devices 
  
  
論文發表人: Rong-Ho Lee*, Wei-Sheng Chen, and Ying-Yu Wang 
 
 4 
Electroluminescence Properties of Novel Hyper-branched Light-Emitting Polymers Based 
Devices 
 
Rong-Ho Lee*, Wei-Sheng Chen, and Ying-Yu Wang 
 
 Department of Chemical and Materials Engineering, National Chung Hsing University, Taichung 402, Taiwan 
E-mail: rhl@nchu.edu.tw 
 
Abstract  
A series of hyper-branched polyalkylfluorenes (HBPFs) with an electron-conducting group, triazine and a 
hole-conducting group, triphenylamine as molecular cores were synthesized by the Suzuki coupling reaction.  This series of 
HBPFs with different conjugated lengths were end-capped with a hole-conducting group, triphenylamine, and a rigid moiety, 
tetraphenylsilane.     
1. Introduction 
Alkylfluorene copolymers have proven to be the most promising due to their combined desired properties, such as high 
fluorescene quantum yield, and good film-forming and hole-transporting properties.  However, PF application in PLEDs has 
been hampered because of an undesired long-wavelength excimer emission band formation.  Excimer emission from aggregates 
formed by polymer chainstacking in PFs usually results in lower efficiency and a light emission red shift from blue to 
blue-green.  Recently, a series of tetraphenylsilane based hyperbranched blue light emitting alkylfluorene copolymers have been 
developed by He et al. [1].  No excimer formation in long wavelength (500-600 nm) was observed for such hyperbranched PFs.  
Color purity of blue emission was shown to be improved by reducing the aggregates formed by polymer chainstacking in 
the solid state of PFs.  The presence of the hyper-branched structure reduced aggregation and excimer formation of PF [1].   
Hyper-branched LEP with electron- or hole-conducting groups as the molecular core, in addition to excimer effect 
reduction, responds favorably for balance charge injection and transporting properties of PLEDs [2-3].  Hyper-branched PFs 
with electron-conducting moiety triazine as the molecular core have been reported by Wen et al. [2].   Introducing triazine into 
Pfs improves EL performances and suppresses green emission.  Chen et al. reports that charge injection and transporting 
properties must be varied as desired to further improve device performance for triazine or aromatic amine moieties as the core for 
the oligofluorenes [3]. Electron- or hole-conducting groups, on the other hand, have also been used as end-capped groups of 
hyper-branched LEP for tunable photo-physical performances. He et al. develops hyper-branched LEPs with 2,5-dimethyloxyl 
substituted for phenylene vinylene as connecting units, and different electron or hole conducting groups, such as pyridyl 
phenylene or dimethylaniline phenylene as end-capped groups.  The report shows PL and EL properties affected by end-capped 
groups.  Hyperbranched PPVs are highly soluble in common organic solvents, with good film-forming properties.  In 
particular, hyper-branched PPV thermal stability is extremely higher than that of linear PPVs. Hyper-branched LEPs with a 
different molecular core or end-capped groups have been studied based on the above mention.  Nevertheless, compared with 
linear LEPs, hyper-branched LEPs are limited in number [2-3].  Core or end-capped group influence on photo-physical 
properties of hyper-branched PFs have not been thoroughly examined.   
This study synthesizes a series of hyper-branched polyalkylfluorenes with electron-deficient group triazine and 
hole-conducting group triphenylamine as molecular cores using the Suzuki coupling reaction.  This series of hyper-branched 
polyalkylfluorenes with different conjugated length are end-capped with hole-conducting group triphenylamine and a rigid 
moiety tetraphenylsilane, respectively.  Eight hyper-branched blue light emitting polymers are therefore synthesized, namely 
PTFA5, PTFA10, PTFS5, PTFS10, PAFA5, PAFA10, PAFS5, and PAFS10.  Figure 1 shows chemical structures of the

The amplitude of excimer emission was not pronounced in the PL spectrum, indicating the the electrical field caused the 
aggregation of alkyfluorene chains.  However, the electrically induced excimer effect was not observed for the other 
hyper-branched LEP based devices.  This may be due to the fact of that the existence of the large end-capped group

The current density, brightness, and current efficiency versus applied voltage of the hyper-branched LEP based devices are

(PTFA10 and PTFS10) with long conjugated length as compared to the polymers (PTFA5 and PTFS5) with short conjugated 
length based devices.  In addition to the conjugated length effect, excimer quenching led to the low brightness and current 
efficiency of PTFA5 and PTFS5 based devices.  Moreover, the triazine core containing LEP based devices show a higher 
current density, larger brightness, and greater current efficiency than the triphenylamine core containing LEPs based devices.  In 
most conjugated polymers, holes are preferred over electrons.  The incorporation of -deficient moiety triazine was favorable 
for the balance charge injection and transporting properties of PLEDs.   Therefore, better EL performances were observed for 
the triazine core containing LEP based devices than the triphenylamine core containing LEPs based devices.   Furthermore, a 
thinner light emitting layer thickness yields the higher current density of PAFA5 and PAFA10 based devices, compared to the 
other LEPs based devices.  Particle presence on the ITO glass surface and ITO surface roughness cannot be smoothed down by 
a thin polymer film.  As a result, electrically leakage could have occurred during PLED operation processes because of defects. 
Consequently, a thin light emitting layer thickness resulted in low brightness and current efficiency in PAFA5 and PAFA10 based 
devices.  In addition, the EL quenching of electrical induced excimer yielded low brightness and current efficiency in PAFS5 
and PAFS10 based devices.  
4. Conclusion 
Electroluminescence performances of hyper-branched LEPs could be tuned by incorporating electron- and hole-conducting 
groups as the core or end-capping group.
