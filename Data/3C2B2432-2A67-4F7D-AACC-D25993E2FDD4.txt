assured. This intelligent controller can overcome the 
nature of nonlinear dynamics in magnetic levitation 
system. However, the neural networks, in general, are 
easily misled by either process noise, or measurement 
noise, or both. Noises are treated as parts of the 
system. As a sequence, PID parameters are in really 
bad shape and magnetic levitation system is out of 
control. Kalman filter is introduced to take care of 
the noise problems. Kalman filter can get an optimal 
estimation out of noise interference. In this thesis, 
this new design is called self-tuning ＇Radius Basis 
Function Neural Network PID controller with Kalman 
filter＇. The proposed Kalman filter and RBF-PID 
controller surpasses the performance of fixed-
parameter PID controller or self-tuning RBF-PID 
controller by a variety of Matlab simulations under 
noise interference. The output transient response and 
steady-state error of magnetic levitation system can 
be assured while the other two controllers are out of 
control.

（一）研究計畫之背景及目的： 
 
隨著科技的發展，高科技產業對設備的需求是穩定、精確、無塵…等，但是提供這些高科技設備
的傳統產業，機械運轉時所帶來的噪音、震動、摩擦所產生的熱…等，總是令人詬病，然而隨著磁浮
系統的出現與逐漸普及，這些問題有了解決之道。磁浮系統是一種利用電流通過線圈時產生電磁力，
此電磁力對物體的作用，因其作用力的方向，可以是浮力或是斥力，適當的操控電磁力，可以讓物體
飄浮在空中並且自由活動，由於物體是飄浮的，避免了機械元件在運轉中，帶來的噪音、震動和摩擦，
對於需求更嚴苛的高科技產業而言，磁浮系統因為具有非接觸式的特性，不會有粉塵的出現，若控制
得當，可以精確的定位，基於這些優點磁浮系統被廣泛的應用，如：磁浮列車、磁浮平台、磁浮軸承、
磁浮風洞等[1]-[16]。 
一般而言，磁浮系統由感測器、控制器、驅動器和受控體等四個部分組成，受控體為不穩定之非
線性系統，因此對於控制工程而言，選擇有效的控制方法，設計適當的控制器，使得系統達到所需的
要求，是困難的挑戰，因此除了傳統的 PID 控制器外，通常會選用智慧型控制器；另外，磁浮系統而
言，位置感測器扮演非常重要的角色，受控體的位置，是由感測器的量測輸出取得，磁浮的位置變化
非常小，通常僅達數毫米（mm），雜訊或量測上的不確定性，對於感測器的位置量測輸出影響相對極
高，而此位置量測輸出，直接影響到控制器的運作，造成磁浮系統位置控制的問題；而且智慧型控制
器因為依賴受控體的位置資訊，當做學習法則或判斷法則的依據，當位置資訊遭到雜訊干擾或量測上
的不確定性時，反而會造成嚴重後果，因此降低位置量測的確定性對於磁浮系統非常重要。 
本計畫提出「運用卡爾曼濾波器改善磁浮系統位置控制」的架構，以徑向基函數類神經網路的自
調式 PID 控制器解決傳統 PID 在控制參數上的調整問題，可以因應受控體線性化後帶來的系統不確定
性，與動態閉回路控制的需求，並增添卡爾曼濾波器，改善磁浮系統位置量測上，雜訊干擾或量測上
的不確定性的問題；因為磁浮球位置變化僅止於操作點附近數毫米之範圍，如果受到位置量測雜訊的
干擾，將對閉迴路的氣隙位置控制產生極大影響，甚至使磁浮球無法穩定控制；在本計畫的前置模擬
研究中，提出使用卡爾曼濾波器對系統內部與外部雜訊干擾進行濾波，使磁浮系統在受到干雜訊擾時，
徑向基函數類神經網路的自調式 PID 控制器仍然能穩定操控，模擬證實，有無使用卡爾曼濾波器，的
確有非常明顯的改善，另外，模擬亦證實，智慧型 RBF-PID 控制器受到位置量測雜訊干擾或量測上的
不確定性，會有嚴重影響。本計畫擬自行設計建構一套磁浮球系統的研究教學平臺，以資料擷取卡與
LabVIEW 程式完成 RBF-PID 控制器與卡爾曼濾波器的軟體撰寫，驗證「運用卡爾曼濾波器改善磁浮
系統位置控制」的效果。

(1)、卡爾曼濾波器理論 
 
卡爾曼濾波器由 R. E. Kalman 於 1960 年提出[17]，使用狀態方程式模型發展而成的遞迴式濾波
器，從已知的狀態初始值和其共變異矩陣(Covariance Matrix)，當取得新的量測資料時，只需根據預先
的狀態估計值，運用誤差最小平方運算法則，尋求狀態最佳估計值，再利用動態方程計算出其新的共
變異矩陣和新的狀態變數估計值，因此不需記錄先前的狀態或輸出量測資訊，可視為無記憶性的演算
法則；卡爾曼濾波演算法是一種高效率的遞歸濾波器(Auto-Regression Filter)，它能夠從不完全的訊號
 4
卡爾曼濾波是一種遞歸的估算，只須獲知上一時刻狀態的估計值，以及當前狀態的觀測值，就可
以計算出當前狀態的估計值，因此不需要記錄觀測或估計的先前資訊，因此可視為無記憶性之演算法
則。卡爾曼濾波器與大多數濾波器不同之處，在於它是一種純粹的時域濾波器，它不需要像低通濾波
器等頻域濾波器那樣，需要在頻域設計後，再轉換到時域實現。卡爾曼濾波器的運算程序包括兩個階
段：預測與更新，請參考圖三，在預測階段(A Priori Estimator, or Predictor)，使用上一狀態的估計，做
出對當前狀態的估計；在更新階段(A Posteriori Estimator, Corrector)，濾波器利用對當前狀態的觀測值，
在預測階段獲得的最佳之預測值，以獲得一個更精確的新估計值。卡爾曼濾波器演算法則可以下列之

因雜訊干擾造成的定位誤差都很明顯；RBF-PID 控制器則完全無法達成定位控制。 
 
(C) 研究結論 
 
在增加卡爾曼濾波器之後，磁浮系統的追蹤與定位控制模擬中，可以得到以下結論： 
(一) 磁浮球位置變化僅止於操作點附近數毫米之範圍，受到雜訊的干擾時，將對閉迴路的氣隙寬度控
制產生極大影響，甚至使磁浮球無法穩定控制，而無卡爾曼濾波器之 RBF-PID 控制器在雜訊干擾
下，反而因為線上學習機制，將被干擾的輸出響應視為受控體的一部份，誤導 PID 參數調整機制，
導致不能正常運作；針對雜訊干擾的問題，加入卡爾曼濾波器後，確實可以排除雜訊的干擾。 
(二) 由於卡爾曼濾波器可以正確估測出真正的磁浮球位置，排除干擾的雜訊，才能使得徑向基函數類
神經網路的自調式 PID 控制器正常工作，達成良好的暫態響應與消除穩態誤差，幾乎不受干擾雜

Abstract—The goal of this project is to design and build a 
dual-axis drive system for an electric bicycle. The computerized 
control for the electric bicycle utilizes a firmware-based system, 
with the vehicle’s electronic control unit (ECU)and most of the 
signal processing circuits  implemented using a Programmable 
SoC (PSoC). This special dual-axis driven electric bicycle has 
many advantages over electric bicycles currently available on 
the market. To be able to have both wheels drive the vehicle 
simultaneously, the speed controller must be designed with a 
very high degree of accuracy so that it has the ability to 
compensate for speed difference that might exist between the 
front and rear wheels. In addition, both wheels have breaks and 
kinetic energy recovery for decelerating or downhill riding. The 
vehicle has automatic cruse capabilities and can automatically 
switch to single front or rear wheel drive at medium or high 
speeds. This saves power and avoids any single motor driver 
overheating because of long term driving.  
I. INTRODUCTION 
N recent years, oil prices have frequently fluctuated 
dramatically, resulting in substantial disruption to the 
global economy. On July 11, 2008, the price of a barrel of oil 
in New York financial market surged to an unprecedented 
$147.27 US Dollars, resulting in the worst global energy 
crisis and economic recession since the second energy crisis 
in the 1970’s. The recent political unrest in the Middle East 
and the nuclear crisis in Japan have further contributed to the 
price of Brent Crude Oil from the North Sea surging to 
$126.5 US Dollars, pushing countries around the globe, many 
of which who have been seeing only slow economic recovery 
since 2010, into another economic and energy crisis. In 
addition, in today’s highly environmentally oriented 
atmosphere, it is becoming everyday practice to lessen the 
amount of air pollution for everyone on the planet. Ever since 
the main industrialized nations signed the Kyoto Protocol [1], 
carbon dioxide emission standards in countries around the 
globe have become more and more strict. According to the 
Environmental Protection Administration, Taiwan, 87.8% of 
domestic air pollution comes from moving pollution sources, 
which shows that vehicle emissions are the main culprit of air 
pollution in Taiwan. This is why the production of electric 
vehicles has become so popular. Out of the various types of 
electric vehicles, electric bicycles have the advantages of a 
high energy conversion efficiency, a simple mechanical 
design, diverse sources of electric power sources, and zero 
pollution during usage, meeting global requirements for a 
safe, efficient, and non-polluting recreational vehicle [2]. Due 
to their environmentally friendly and power-saving 
characteristics, electric bicycles have become the new 
favorite of the 21st century bicycle industry. This is why 
many countries have already engaged in the research and 
development of electrical bicycles [2]-[8]. Recognizing the 
huge potential of the electric bicycle market, both foreign and 
domestic bicycle manufacturers in Taiwan are in a race to 
develop electric bicycles. Large foreign companies such as 
Yamaha and Sanyo in Japan and ZAP in the United States, 
and domestic companies like Giant and Merida in Taiwan are 
all in active production of electric bicycles. This has pushed 
forward the development of key components such as batteries, 
resulting in new types of batteries being developed (such as 
Li-ion batteries and LiFePO4 batteries). Therefore, problems 
such as electric bicycles being too heavy, or not having 
enough power capacity for long-distance usage will likely 
soon be solved, and it can be predicted that enormous growth 
in the production and sale of electric bicycles will be 
prevailing. 
In light of this trend, this project introduces the design and 
production of a dual-axis drive system electric bicycle that 
solves the problem of the wheel-hub brushless DC Motor not 
having enough power and at the same time saves energy and 
has the ability to automatically switch to single front or rear 
wheel drive mode during medium to high speed operation 
with a heavy load to avoid damage due to overheating [5][6].  
This dual-axis drive electric bicycle is different from the 
electric bicycles that are currently commercially available. It 
is both front and rear wheel driven, with decelerating and 
downhill regenerative braking system. The dual-wheel drive 
system offers excellent traction between its tires and ground, 
even when on muddy or sandy roads, providing protection 
against possible sliding or skidding. Additional safety 
functions such as soft start and speed limitation are also 
available. The safety and ease of use of this electric bicycle 
makes it perfect for the office commute, shopping, and 
recreational usage by seniors and household wives.[9][10]

D. The Vehicle Electronic Control Unit (ECU) 
The vehicle electronic control unit is designed using a 
System-on-Chip (SoC). It has powerful hardware 
input/output interface capabilities and very flexible software 
control functionality, providing a powerful yet simple and 
reliable drive system; functionalities supported by the SoC 
include the throttle grip, brakes, reading of the battery voltage, 
reading of the battery current, vehicle speed control, speed 
difference control, power saving control, calculation and 
estimation of remaining battery power, etc. The vehicle 
electronic control unit block diagram is as shown in Figure 1 
and Figure 3. Its functionalities and design concept are 
described in the sections below.

E. Soft Start Function 
The soft start function is designed to protect the safety of 
the bicycle rider by preventing the sudden unintended 
acceleration (SUA) when the vehicle is started up. As shown 
in Figure 4, the yellow curve is the throttle grip signal and the 
green curve is the speed signal during loaded operation. The 
left side represents slow and steady acceleration, the right 
side represents sudden vigorous acceleration. After a system 
time delay of 0.2 seconds, both show a smooth and steady 
acceleration curve, even though the slope values of the 
acceleration curves differ quite dramatically; neither produce 
any uncontrollable acceleration at startup. Avoiding sudden 
uncontrolled acceleration during vehicle startup is a major 
concern to protect the bicycle rider, especially the elderly and 
women.

F. Vehicle Speed Control, Speed Compensation, and the

wheel to achieve a specified vehicle speed, thereby forming a 
dual closed-loop speed control system for the front wheel.

brushless motors to perform the various experiments, and a 
Yokogawa 4 Channel DLM 2024 digital storage oscilloscope 
with Techtronix TCPA300 current probes, which are used to 
test and record voltage, current, and other signals. The 
important test results are listed below:
