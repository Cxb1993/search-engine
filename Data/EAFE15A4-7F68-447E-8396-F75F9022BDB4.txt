中文摘要
本案乃延續前一研究案(變動環境中氯離子滲透混凝土之分析及中部河口氯離子濃度
調查 NSC96-2221-E-224-058)。
氯離子滲透混凝土，造成 RC 結構內鋼筋銹蝕，如橋墩的下部，在臺灣沿海及離島問題
最為嚴重，氯離子滲透混凝土可以 Fick 滲透理論來描述，理論公式內包含兩個重要參數，
混凝土內鹽分的飽和濃度值及滲透系數，外界環境條件會影響滲透量、深度及速率，外界
環境條件最主要的是鹽水濃度及環境溫度，前案實驗顯示鹽水濃度主要影響混凝土內鹽分
的飽和濃度值，並已推導出鹽水濃度隨時間變動時，滲透到混凝土內鹽分的解析解，也針
對中部四條典型河川出海口附近橋樑(台南縣急水溪五王大橋、台南縣嘉義縣交界八掌溪八
掌溪橋、嘉義縣朴子溪東石南橋、嘉義縣雲林縣交界北港溪雲嘉大橋)，現地取樣測定鹽水
濃度，作成一整年實驗記錄。
本案乃探討環境溫度對鹽分滲透混凝土的影響，進一步推導環境溫度隨時間變動時，
滲透到混凝土內鹽分的解析解，並繼續監測該四條河川出海口附近橋樑下鹽水濃度。
本研究的目的是要延續前案，以完成變動環境條件下之滲透理論模式，配合現地實際
狀況，以分析鹽分滲透造成 RC 結構中鋼筋腐蝕問題，用於預估出海口附近橋墩使用年限。
關鍵字：氯離子、滲透、混凝土、溫度
Object
This proposal is the extension of a previous project (Chloride Diffusion in Concrete under
Fluctuating Environmental Conditions and Investigation of The Temperature and Chloride
Concentration of The Water in Middle Taiwan Estuaries NSC 96-2221-E-224-058).
Chloride diffusion in RC structure, such as the lower part of the pier of a bridge, induces
corrosion of the rebar. The coast and the islands of Taiwan have more serious corrosion problem
than other areas do. The diffusion of chloride can be described by Fick's diffusion law involving
two important parameters, saturated concentration and diffusivity. The chloride concentration in
the concrete, diffusion depth, and rate are dependent on the environment conditions, such as
environmental chloride concentration and temperature of the water. The result of the project
reveals that the chloride concentration of the surrounding water directly dominates the saturated
concentration in the concrete and led to the solution of the chloride concentration in the concrete
due to the chloride diffusing from the surrounding water with fluctuating chloride
concentration. The project also includes collecting the data of the chloride concentration of the
water around four bridges close to the estuaries of four rivers in middle Taiwan (1. WuDaWang
Bridge , ChiSwei River (Tainan), 2. BaZan Bridge, BaZan River (Tainan-ChaYi), 3. DonSuNan
Bridge, PuZu River (ChaYi), 4. YunChi Bridge, BayGun River (ChiYi-YunLin)). The data are
collected to be a one year's record.
This proposal proposes to study how temperature affects the chloride diffusion in concrete,
derive the exact solution of the chloride concentration in concrete due to chloride diffusion from
surrounding water under fluctuating temperature, and monitor continuously the chloride
concentration of the water at the four locations chosen in the previous project.
The purpose of this proposal is to extend the project to develop a complete mathematic
model for fluctuating environmental conditions based on local environmental data for predicting
the life against corrosion of a pier close to estuary.
Keywords: chloride, diffusion, concrete, temperature
Theoretical Background
When temperature does not effect the diffusion, and the saturated content of the diffusion
material is stepwise or continuously time dependent,
The experiment at 5C is too slow to be completed in one year, which is not practical to be
studied and be abandoned. cand D seem not to have obvious dependency on temperature by
Arrhenius equation [2] in the temperature range of this study. In the temperature range, cand D
seem to be almost constant.
Since temperature seems has no obvious effect on the diffusion, no consideration is needed.
Only chloride of the water is considered. The data of cw(t) and T(t) of the water under four
bridges at the estuaries of four rivers in middle Taiwan were collected. They are (1). WuDaWang
Bridge , ChiSwei River (Tainan), (2). BaZan Bridge, BaZan River (Tainan-ChaYi), (3)
DonSuNan Bridge, PuZu River (ChaYi), and (4). YunChi Bridge, BayGun River (ChiYi-YunLin).
The T(t) will be used to evaluate D(T) and the cw(t) and T(t) will be used to evaluate c(T).
