ABSTRACT:   Poly(vinyl acetate)-montmorillonite 
(PVAc-MMT) exfoliated nanocomposite latexes were 
prepared by soap-free emulsion polymerization and 
directly applied to fabricate the PVAc-MMT 
nanocomposite films. These nanocomposite films were flat 
and transparent, where the MMT nanoplatelets were 
uniformly dispersed in polymer matrix and also flattened 
parallel to the film. The exfoliated MMTs were able to 
enhance the  chemical resistance of polymer and decrease 
the water vapor permeability.  On the basis of Nielsen’s 
permeability equation. the aspect ratio of exfoliated MMT 
platelets was calculated as 200:1, similar to that observed 
and estimated directly by AFM.    
Keywords: MMT, PVAc, nanocomposites, permeability.

Introduction 
    Polymer-clay nanocomposites1 are nowadays one of 
the most important advanced materials in nanocomposite 
fileds. Not only can many properties be improved by 
exfoliated clay, but also the manufacturing procedures are 
more facile and cheaper. Since nylon6-montmorillonite 
(MMT) nanocomposite2 was invented by Toyota research 
group, various polymers, clays, processes, and 
characterizations associated with the polymer-clay 
nanocomposites have been studied in recent years3-7. 
Generally, only a few weight percents of exfoliated clay 
with high aspect ratio can effectively improve strength,5 
thermal stability7, and gas or vapor barrier properties6. 
    Soap-free emulsion polymerization8, 9 is a clean, safe, 
and prevailing method for synthesizing the monodisperse 
sizes of polymeric latexes. It is also easier to dissipate heat 
than other polymerization processes. Recently, we have 
employed soap-free emulsion polymerization to fabricate 
the exfoliated poly(methyl methacrylate)-MMT  nano- 
composites latexes.10 During emulsion polymerization, we 
exclusively found that the polymerizing chains were easily 
penetrating into the interlayer regions of MMT for 
micellation and exfoliated MMT.      
Poly(vinyl acetate) (PVAc)12 is a tasteless, odorless, 
and nontoxic polymer, with moderate strength and good 
flexibility. It has been employed as an adhesive for wood, 
paper or porous materials. However, although some 
research groups have fabricated poly(ethylene-vinyl 
acetate)-clay nanocomposites by melt-blending method13 
and by in situ polymerization,14 no one in the literature has 
employed the soap-free emulsion polymerization to 
fabricate the PVAc-MMT nanocomposite latex.  
    Therefore, we prepared PVAc-MMT nanocomposite 
latexes with soap-free emulsion polymerization. The 
prepared PVAc-MMT nanocomposite latexes with different 
weight percentage of MMT were directly cast into films. 
The film quality, chemical resistance and vapor 
permeability of PVAc-MMT nanocomposites were then 
investigated in terms of the dispersion of exfoliated MMT 
in polymer matrix.  
 
Experimental 
    9.5 g VAc monomer was added into a three-neck flask, 
which has been loaded with 125ml water and 0.3852g KPS 
or KPS-MMT (1, 3, 5, 10 wt% MMT for PVAc-MMT 
nanocomposites) at room temperature. The solutions were 
maintained at 50oC with stir for 50 hrs. Then, the solution 
was heated to 70oC to complete the polymerization. The 
conversion of polymerizaiton was roughly about 80%, and 
the resulting molecular weight was listed in Table 1. Next, 
these PVAc-MMT nanocomposite latexes were cast into a 
15cm×45cm aluminum foil and heated to 50oC in an oven. 
After 12 hrs, it was further heated to 70 oC to remove 
residual water or monomer until the PVAc-MMT 
nanocomposite film was formed.

技術/創作名稱 形成黏土/高分子奈米複合材料乳液的方法及其應用 
發明人/創作人 林金福、簡安廷、顏銘翬、李佳欣、翁頂翔、周顓正 
中文：本發明揭示了一種形成黏土/高分子奈米複合材料乳液的方法，首先提供並混合
一層狀黏土材料與一水溶性起始劑在水中形成一中間溶液，使得層狀黏土材料可以吸附
或插層水溶性起始劑。然後，加入至少一種乙烯酯  (vinyl ester)、丙烯酸酯(acrylic 
monomer)或或丙烯醯胺(acrylamide)單體，以及選擇性加入其他可參與共聚合之單體體如
丙烯酸(acrylic acid)、馬來酸酐(maleic anhydride)、氯乙烯(vinyl chloride)、乙烯(ethylene)
等單體於中間溶液中，直接進行一無乳化劑乳化聚合反應，其中，吸附或插層的水溶性
起始劑分解形成一起始劑自由基與附近單體反應形成一寡聚物自由基，且層狀黏土材料
陸續吸引單體與寡聚物自由基進入黏土層間進行聚合反應，使黏土完全脫層，以形成脫
層型黏土/高分子奈米複合材料乳液，可直接應用於防水阻氣材料或阻氣塗料的製備，或
利用溶劑大量移除複合材料乳液中的聚合物，製造單片厚度的奈米黏土溶液，塗佈成薄
膜後奈米黏土含量超過 75wt%時具半導體性質，可應用於電子材料或有機-無機奈米複合
材料多層膜的製備。 
 
技術說明 英文：The present invention discloses a method to prepare the clay/polymer nanocomposite 
latex.  At first, a clay and a water-soluble initiator are mixed in water to form an intermediate 
solution, in which the initiator is absorbed or intercalated into the clay.  Then, at least one 
monomer from vinyl ester, acrylic and acrylamide derivatives is added into the intermediate 
solution for soap-free emulsion polymerization, which may also be added with some other 
monomers such as acrylic acid, maleic anhydride, vinyl chloride, ethylene, et al. for 
copolymerization. During soap-free emulsion polymerization, the radicals from the dissociated 
initiators will react with the monomers to form the oligomer radicals, which tend to diffuse into 
the interlayer region of clays for further polymerization and eventually exfoliate the clays.  
The prepared exfoliated clay/polymer nanocomposite latex can be cast into a 
vapor-impermeable film so that it can be used as a sealing material or coating.  The exfoliated 
clay solutions can also be prepared by removing the polymer matrix from the nanocomposite 
latex with solvent. As the exfoliated clay solution is cast into a film with the clay content >75 
wt%, the film is semi-conductive. Thus, the exfoliated clay solutions can be applied to the 
electronic materials and organic/inorganic multilayer composite films.
