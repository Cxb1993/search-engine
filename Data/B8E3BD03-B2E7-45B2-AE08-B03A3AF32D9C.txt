Abstract 
Recently, as an efficient method of 
describing the microscopic phenomenon, 
the molecular dynamics simulation 
technology which derived from statistical 
physics attracts people’s more and more 
attentation and successfully applied to many 
microscopic region such as nanotechnology. 
With the development of MD simulation 
technology, the study of simulation 
algorithm has attracted many people’s 
attentation. It is approved that the 
symplectic algorithm is a high resoluted and 
convergent algorithm. The advantage of 
symplectic algorithm is proved through the 
example of single pendulum, Through the 
simulation of nanomatric manufacturing, 
the accuracy of symplectic algorithm is 
justified. 
Keywords: Molecular dynamics, symplectic 
algorithm, Nunerical integration, 
Nanomanufacturing, Hamiltonian 
system.
