are very important issues for designing ASIC 
(Application Specific Integrated Circuits) and 
SOC (System On a Chip). This project aims to 
develop a signature-based Boolean matching 
algorithm which can be applied in technology 
mapping and logic verification.

equivalence of two functions under input 
permutation and input/output phase assignments, 
so called NPN-class. For the application in 
technology mapping, it can be used to map a 
sub-circuit to a logic cell in cell library. In
