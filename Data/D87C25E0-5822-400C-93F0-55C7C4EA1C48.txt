1. 室內無線定位感測網路 
 
  The market for real-time location systems for assets and personnel tracking is expected to reach $1.26 bil-
lion by 2011 [7], and $2.71 billion in 2016 [8]. For widespread adoption and everyday use of real-time loca-
tion systems in households and commercial buildings, the systems must be able to provide accurate and sta-
ble location estimations with little delay. 
Most indoor localization systems employ an RSSI-signature-based approach which exploits temporal stabil-
ity in the received signal strength indication (RSSI) from a set of predeployed beacons at identified locations, 
which is referred to as the RSSI signature. When a target carrying a receiving tag enters the space, the re-
ceived RSSI values are compared to the RSSI signatures. The corresponding location of the closest RSSI 
signature identifies the location of the target. Methods of ensuring robust mapping between the measured 
RSSI values and the pre-recorded RSSI signature have been studied intensively in recent years [9][10][26]. 
Such methods are needed to minimize localization errors induced by unstable RSSI values. An often over-
looked problem is the increasing use of wireless transmission of RF frequencies in the everyday environment. 
Bluetooth (IEEE 802.15.3), WiFi (IEEE 802.11) and Zigbee (IEEE 802.15.4) all operate in the .4x GHz fre-
quency band. The stability and availability of RSSI information for WiFi- or Zigbee-based localization sys-
tems may vary depending on interference from other WiFi, Bluetooth, and Zigbee sources. Aiming at a better

MEASUREMENT RESULT 
We conducted a sequence of experiments to determine the effect of WiFi noise on localization accuracy. In 
our experiments, we generate WiFi traffic at different levels, and collect the following data: (1) background 
WiFi traffic, (2) beacon messages received at the receiving tag and (3) beacon messages received at other 
beacons. In the 5 sets of experiments, the averages of observed background WiFi traffic rates are 68, 264, 
1308, 1705, and 2835 kbps. We analyze the collected traces and present the results in this section. 13 Figure 
3 depicts the cumulative distribution function (CDF) of the localization errors. The test results show that the 
localization errors are influenced by the background WiFi traffic. In the 50th percentile, the errors increases 
from 53cm to 81cm (53% increase) as the back- ground WiFi traffic increases. The increase in the 80th per-
centile error from 160cm to 385cm (141% increase) is particularly large. This indicates that, as background 
traffic increases, the localization error and variance also increase. 
 
FREQUENCY HOPPING MECHANISM 
To make a RSSI-based localization system robust to background noise, this study proposes a frequency hop-
ping mechanism consisting of (1) an initialization process for the sensor network formed by the beacon 
nodes, (2) a diagnostic test to detect whether the sensor network is substantially influenced by background 
noise, and (3) a protocol for signaling all nodes to hop to the next channel. The following subsections de-
scribe in detail the mechanism components and design rationale. 
Beacon Node

the base value at the system startup time. One obvious criterion is the beacon message reception rate at the 
receiving tag. However, the receiving tag may appear anywhere in the tracking area. In some regions, partic-
ularly those at the corners where the network connectivity is relatively weak, the receiving tag can only re-
ceive a small number of beacon messages even when the channel is quiet. Hence, the number of beacon 
messages received at the receiving tag is location dependent. For a localization system aiming at tracking 
mobile targets, a diagnostic test based on this criterion would not accurately reflect the interference level of 
the operating channel. On the other hand, the number of received beacon messages at beacon nodes will not

Like the process that a magnet (sink) attracts metallic objects (data), the data sink establishes a magnetic field across the 
WSN, with decreasing magnetic charges radiating from it. Sensor data, in turn, travel the network from lower magnetic 
charges to higher magnetic charges until reaching the sink. If there exist multiple shortest paths in the WSN topology, 
all of such paths will be discovered by the mechanism. As a result, MD tends to have low end-to-end delay. However, 
the redundancy also causes high packet transmission overhead and many collisions, resulting in the decrease of delivery 
rate.

(XD), whose MAC is a hybrid of TDMA and CSMA to avoid collisions. The TDMA mechanism utilizes the magnetic 
charges established by MD. In XD, nodes of the same magnetic charge send data at the same time slot. There are three 
time slots. A node with magnetic charge C transmits data only at time slot C mod 3. This prevents hidden terminal col-
lisions from different levels. Furthermore, the CSMA mechanism is applied to avoid data collisions among the nodes of 
the same charge. The TDMA and CSMA combined could properly

MAC Mechanism 
XD uses a hybrid TDMA-CSMA MAC mechanism that utilizes information established by MD. The TDMA part of the 
design eliminates hidden terminal collisions from two magnetic charge levels apart and the CSMA avoids collisions 
between neighboring nodes in the same magnetic charge level. The design objective is to get a good balance between 
high delivery rate and low end-to-end delay. To elaborate in more detail the design rationale, we classify collisions in 
MD networks into six canonical types by the sending nodes from different magnetic charge levels. In Figure 0.2, node 
A and B are sending nodes and the packets collide at node C. The number on the node indicates the assigned magnetic 
charge. Type I, II and III are cases where sending nodes are at the same level. The sending nodes can be one level high-
er, at the same level, or one level lower than the receiving node. Type IV and V are cases where sending nodes are of 
adjacent magnetic charges. Type VI is the case where the two sending nodes are two levels apart. The data traveling 
along the magnetic field, from low-charge to high-charge, should not be lost by collision since they are effective data 
disseminated to the sink. On the contrary, the data traveling against the magnetic field, to the same charge or from 
large-charge to low-charge, are not critical because they are away from the sink. As a result, Type I, II and IV collisions 
can be ignored, and in the following we focus on Type III, V and VI collisions. Type III collisions can be reduced by 
applying CSMA at nodes within the same magnetic charge level since nodes at the same level tend to be nearby. Note 
that, there still remains a slight chance that nodes at the same level are away from each other’s carrier sense range. 
However, these nodes may be too far away to affect each other. 
Type V collisions can be reduced by CSMA to a certain degree because nodes with adjacent magnetic charges can hear 
each other relatively easily. A significant amount of the collisions can be further reduced by separating the sending time 
of the nodes at different levels, hinting the benefit of a TDMA strategy to the design. In Type VI, nodes of higher and 
smaller level become hidden terminals to each other. These collisions may not be eliminated by carrier sense. The 
sending time of nodes two levels apart needs to be separated to avoid collisions. To avoid type V and VI collisions ef-
fectively, TDMA based on the magnetic charge levels is applied. XD uses three time slots for the TDMA schedule. The 
slot assignment is based on each node’s magnetic charge. In the interest broadcast phase of XD, the interest messages, 
originated from the sink, carry magnetic charges decremented at each hop. When a node receives the interest message, 
the node records the magnetic charge in the message and the TDMA slot for the node is assigned to n mod 3, where n is 
the recorded magnetic charge. In XD, we assume the transmission only affects the nodes in the adjacent levels, higher

先信號是帶通的，所以周期性的頻譜將不會與原先信號造成混疊失真，並且還會落到基頻，
達到降頻(Downconversion)的效果。所以對一個帶通信號而言，其取樣速度將可以遠低於取樣
理論的要求，這個現象稱為次取樣。一般而言，無線通信系統所接收到的射頻(Radio frequency, 
RF)信號均為帶通的，因此我們將可以利用次取樣理論來對信號進行取樣以及降頻。 
如圖二所示，傳統上連續時間式接收機架構中所使用到的吉伯特(Gilbert cell)混頻器將可
以被一個次取樣混頻器所取代。由於次取樣混頻器的輸出信號為離散時間信號，所以後端的
濾波器將可以使用離散時間式濾波器來實現，這也是為何此種架構稱為離散時間式接收機。
次取樣混頻器的輸出取樣速度將與射頻載頻頻率(Carrier frequency)相當。舉例來說，ZigBee
的射頻載頻頻率約為 2.4GHz，然而其一個頻道(Channel)的頻寬僅僅只有 2MHz。要以 2.4GS/s
的取樣速度去實現一個頻寬只有 2MHz 的頻道選擇濾波器(Channel-selection filter)是非常不實
際的，會耗費大量的功率以及成本。因此，大部分的離散時間式接收機設計均會把取樣速度
降到較低的取樣速度(Decimation)後再進行頻道選擇。然而降低取樣速度將會造成混疊失真，
所以在每個抽取器(Decimator)前均須加上抗混疊濾波器(Anti-aliasing filter)來防止混疊失真，
形成了抽取濾波器(Decimation filter)的架構。

1.5 薄膜電晶體式微懸臂樑生物感測器之運作原理 
當微懸臂樑接上生物待測物時，會因表面應力而彎曲產生應變，而此時在微懸臂樑上的
薄膜電晶體便會受到應變影響，由於前面提到的電晶體受應變所產生的晶格扭曲、能障改變、
缺陷出現、載子散射率改變等變化，便會使得薄膜電晶體的載子遷移率改變，進而表現在輸
出訊號電流上。微懸臂樑的彎曲程度會隨著待測物檢體的濃度增加而變大，故待測物濃度越
高，產生之電流變化量也會越大，因此當電晶體的電性與電晶體對待測物的靈敏度確定後，
便可由輸出的信號變化去判斷檢體中的待測物濃度，達到生物感測器的效果。整個薄膜電晶

1.6 電晶體受溫度效應變化理論 
溫度效應對於微懸臂樑感測器而言是很重要的，其所受溫度效應之影響原理，將分成雙
膜效應、電阻溫度效應、雜質散射理論、微懸臂樑之熱消散效應作一簡述。雙膜效應由於材
料組成的不同，在溫度升降的過程中，將因為不同的熱膨脹係數，而導致材料引發熱應力與
力偶矩，進而使材料彎曲如圖二所示。首先將複合材料樑分成Ｎ個單元，由第一層到第 N 層，
當溫度調整 ΔＴ後，各層材料伸長量不一，假設每層材料均為彈性變形。在某溫度下，將各
自不同長度的懸臂樑作合併，為了維持懸臂樑每一層間不產生相對滑動的情況甚至崩壞的情
況，各層材料將引發軸向力量。組合後懸臂樑的長度均為定值且在端點的縱軸方向合力為零，
其所引發的力量不平衡，將導致懸臂樑如同有一力矩而彎曲。在僅考慮彎曲應變下，其所造
成的軸向應力在中性面上的端點合力為零，其所推出的微懸臂樑因熱膨脹係數不同而受溫度

1.8 薄膜電晶體式微懸臂樑之製作 
微懸臂樑在設計上有一重要的考慮因素，就是中性軸的位置，中性軸是微懸臂樑在受應
力而彎曲時，所受應力為零因而沒有應變產生的位置，也就是說在中性軸的地方在微懸臂樑
彎曲前後，其長度不會有所改變，因此若電晶體的通道設計在中性軸位置，將無法感受到任
何變化，故設計時要避免將電晶體通道設計於中性軸所在。從圖 11 中可看到，若微懸臂樑向
下彎曲，在中性軸上方所受到的應變為正，而在中性軸下方則是受負的應變，且離中性軸越
遠應變變化量越大，因此設計時應盡量讓電晶體的通道遠離中性軸，在相同大小的彎曲下才
能感受到最大的應變量。

4. 生醫微感測器介面電路系統晶片之研究 
 
架構論述 
    本章是實現一個微感測器介面電路，以偵測生物訊號的改變。在介面電路的實現方式中
主要有以下幾種：1.利用低雜訊放大生物感測訊號，主要是用電阻變化產生電壓差(voltage 
domain)。 2. 把感測器的變化反應至頻率域(frequency domain)、訊號脈寬域(pulse width do-
main)的偵測、時間軸域(Time domain)。第 2 點的方式當中，主要都是利用電阻與電容充放電
的方式，來產生不同的脈寬、頻率或週期。 
    由於隨著製程技術的演進，低功率的架構已成為 IC 設計的主流，在此種條件狀況下，使
用電壓來偵測訊號的方式，在先天上會受到低電壓的限制。反觀在頻率域、訊號脈寬域、時
間域上的偵測，比較不會受到供應電壓的限制，在偵測上能有較大的自由度。故選擇的偵測
架構，主要以後者為主，又最後選擇以震盪器的方式來實現。 
    在前端的感測電阻，主要是用來感測 DNA，在感測到 DNA 後會產生電阻值的變化(預計
在 0.02%~0.4%的範圍)，而本計畫所實現的電路架構，就是要去偵測微小的電阻變化。 
    本計畫利用在頻率域上的偵測，達到偵測電阻值改變的目的。提出的架構如圖 3.1，在前
端是一個跟感測器結合的振盪器，把電阻變化反應到頻率域上(即圖中的 fin)，然後藉由混波
器(Mixer)把感測後的頻率降至低頻(圖中 fout)，最後在低頻中去偵測頻率變化。

調變技術介紹 
  在一般 PSK 的調變中，為了達到較好的頻譜效能，通常會選用 QPSK 的方式來實現，但因
QPSK 本身在訊號切換時，所造成的相位變化過大，使得資訊所佔用的頻譜範圍分佈過大，
並且過大的相位變化，可能會因為電路本身的頻寬限制，會造成在訊號切換時，使的訊號有
失真的情況出現，若是在高傳輸效率的情況下，此訊號失真會對傳輸的效能造成嚴重的影響。
因此，對於 QPSK 的調變方式存在的問題，通常會使用 Offset QPSK 來解決，實現方式就是
把 I 與 Q 的訊號在時間軸上做分離，讓 Q 訊號領先 I 訊號達半個 symbol period，也因此在訊
號變換的時候，永遠只有 90 度的變化，不會有 180 度的狀況發生，也因此能達到較好的頻譜
效能。 
但 O-QPSK 的調變方式在某些通訊應用上仍是無法滿足使用者對頻譜表現的要求，因此會
在基頻(base-band)上做濾波的效果，以讓基頻上對應到的相位訊號是成連續性的變化[1]，而
非離散的資訊。通常使用濾波器的形態有 Half-sine 或是 cosine 的等多種選擇。由 cosine 
funcction 來實現的稱為 MSK，而由 sinc 方式來實現的稱為 HS-OQPSK。 
在下圖中的 signal constellation 上可看到每種調變模式的差異。QPSK 可看到相位會有 180
度的轉變，OQPSK 中相位則是以 90 度的差異在變化，而對基頻訊號做濾波的調變方式(如
HS-OQPSK)，它的 signal constellation 則是呈現一圓弧的形態。而從圖中可看見 HS-OQPSK
的調變方式中，基頻頻譜訊號較 OQPSK 來的更集中，且 side lobe 跟 main lobe 的高度差足足
比 OQPSK 低了 10dB。 
二種調變模式其實都是 constant envelope 的特性，適合運用功率較低的非線性功率放大器
(non-linear power amplifier)來實現。

1.臨界電壓 (Threshold Voltage, VTH)

截距 VGS，再扣掉二分之一 VDS，此電壓即為臨界電壓 VTH.

雖然消耗功率不低，這原因主要是因為前級 24 億赫茲的正反器使用 180nm 製程與 1 伏特的

常大的功率，如果轉移到更先進的製程這樣的表現會更好。本次使用 180nm 製程完成一個接
收器的測試版本，並且在量測後得到合理的工作表現，因為是以數位電路為主的接收機，所
以在製程轉移上是比較方便的，而功率消耗與面積的表現上也會因為製程的進步而向上推進
一個等級。總結本次的作品提供了一個相當好的測試版本，也證明了離散式收發機的實作是
可以實現在晶片上。 
    在高敏度場效電晶體嵌入之微懸臂樑於心衰竭腦利鈉胜肽原的部分成功地針對目前微懸

寬為 200nm、厚度為 100nm，使用從上到下(top-down)相容於目前標準半導體製程方法，發展
出多晶矽奈米線場效電晶體穩定的製作流程。將開發的多晶矽奈米線場效電晶體實際應用於
生物分子感測之用。本子計畫成功於計畫執行期間開發多晶矽奈米線場效生物分子感測元件。
經由 APTES 與一連串化學步驟對多晶矽表面進行修飾，使探針 DNA 固定在多晶矽上，利用
微流道的方式，使多晶矽奈米線曝露在不同濃度或是序列的目標 DNA 溶液環境中，對元件
進行靈敏度和選擇性的測試與探討。當不同濃度或序列的目標 DNA 與多晶矽奈米線表面的
探針 DNA 進行雜交時，會造成多晶矽奈米線元件的臨界電壓的改變，介此證明目標 DNA 存
在之目的。本計畫開發的元件可感測的 DNA 濃度的極限可達 100fM，而選擇性則可以區分至
1 bps mismatch 序列。
