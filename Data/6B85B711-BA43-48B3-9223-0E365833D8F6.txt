3.1 風速特性分析 
本研究根據 Prandtl 之對數法則，將臺中港區所做之試驗所測得之風速風向資料進行分析，並經
由對數直線迴歸及相關係數分析所得直線方程式，可藉由此關係推算摩擦風速 *U 及粗糙長度 0Z 。本研
究風速觀測係利用於氣象觀測中，所使用之多層式時序列風速計進行風速量測，多層式時序列風速計
設置高程為 m2.0 、 m5.0 、 m0.1 、 m5.1 以及 m0.2 ，量測間隔為 10 min，其時序表中之風向都以 N 為主，在
飛沙的發生情況下，自地面至高度 2 m 範圍內之風速垂直分佈符合對數函數分布，本年度風速垂直分
佈如圖 3a 所示。圖中結果顯示，試驗所得之風速垂直分佈皆呈現良好之對數關係， 2R 介於
0.8651~0.995% 之間。往昔學者針對飛沙量之影響因子，都以摩擦風速 *U 代表(Bagnold, 1954；江和張，
1978)，由於前人所做的試驗多為試驗室所的資料，對於野外所得之試驗經由吳 (2006) 研究指出，為
此因子將難以表示為風速垂直變化之最好指數。本研究所推算之摩擦風速 *U 與 2.0U 風速之關係圖如圖
3b 所示。

今年 2012 土木、離岸及環境工程國際會議是基於 2010 年 ICSBI 國際永續發展建築和基
礎設施在吉隆坡會展中心舉行，會議的成功促使主辦機構今年再召集在同一地點，針對土木、
離岸及環境工程等不同的主題，強調了國際社會對環境的永續發展的關注。今年總共發表論

會議室，此次發表的主題為臺灣中部西海風附近沙丘的調查。臺中港受東北季風影響嚴重，
依據臺中港第二期擴建堤頭測站資料，2007 年 7 月～2008 年 1 月風力觀測 7～8 月間以 SSE
向為主要風向，風速大約為 5～10m/sec。9 月至翌年 1 月風力較強，風速約大於 20m/sec，風
向以 NNE 向為主。根據風速資料顯示，臺中港附近海域的風速已到達可驅動飛砂運移的風速
條件。因此，造成臺中港北防砂堤與北防波堤間飛砂淤積嚴重。本研究為探討臺中港灣近岸
所面臨的飛沙產生機制淤積原因。主要以蒐集現場相關海、氣象資料以現地試驗方式對海岸
飛砂量進行研究，藉以建立飛沙機制模式並探討飛沙與海岸侵淤的相關性，並分析提出飛沙
對附近港池可能造成的淤積威脅。會場與馬來西亞、香港等教授提出相關飛沙問題，相互討
論及關切此一重要問題。

II. METEOROLOGICAL PARAMETERS AND SAMPLING DATA 
Taichung Harbor is located in central Taiwan. The winter 
monsoons hit Taichung in September-April. The winds in 
the monsoon season are strongest in October-March and 
come mostly from the NNE. These winds occur 
approximately 80% of the time. The strongest recorded WS 
was a grade 10 on the Beaufort wind scale. In summer, most 
winds are from the south and the WSs are typically <10m/s. 
The mean air temperature was 297.19K, the relative 
humidity was approximately 74.93%, and the wind speed 
exceeded 4.47m/s; the wind came primarily from the NNE. 
The average sunshine duration and visibility was 8.32hr and 
11.93km, respectively. During the winter monsoon, 
northerly (N) wind blows in Taichung Harbor and 
southeastern (SSE) wind blows during the summer. Over all 
the year, with 44.4% of chances to have  wind velocity 
greater than 5m/s.

III. RESULTS AND DISCUSSIONS 
Several factors probably contributed to the coastal dune, 
including changes in the wind/wave climate and 
meteorological parameters were concluded in this paper. 
The volume results relating to the erosion/accretion status of 
the beach is shown in Figure 2, based on the LIDAR survey. 
The changes in the sand volume of regions I, II and III are 
shown in Table 2. 
The changes in the sand volume of regions I, II and III 
from 2010/09 to 2010/10 are shown in Table 2. Region I 
(beach) is the zone from mean low water line to the inner 
edge of landward limit of effective wave action. Region II is 
the zone nearby the coast line. Region III is the sand fence 
area. Three different types dune morphology are 
distinguished in this paper. During this period, the accretion 
volume values of the dune added in this region was about 
56,642.57 3m . Analysis of the volume of Region I derived 
from LIDAR data, it shows that the volume of Region I was 
accretion and the volume values was about 33,288.25 3m . 
The system of Region I is complex which contain waves, 
currents and wind interaction with available sediment at the 
wide water-land interface. The accretion reason may cause 
by the Ta-Chia stream provides enough sediment supply for 
this system. The system of Region II is nearby the coast line 
which contains the breakwaters and wind interaction with 
available sediment at this zone. In Region II, waves and 
currents were not easily to arrival under the flood-tide. 
During the winter monsoon period, the volumes decrease 
about -1,215.71 3m .  In Region III, sand fence was settled to 
prevent the sand flux advancing and reduce the wind-blown 
damage. The system of Region III is above the coast line 
which contains the wind shelter wall and wind interaction 
with available sediment at this zone. Overall this observed 
period, the volume values of the dune added in the sand 
fence area was about 24,570.03 3m . 
The changes in the sand volume of regions I, II and III 
from 2010/10 to 2010/11 are shown in Table 3. During this

values 2010/09~2010/10 and 2010/10~2010/11, the values 
of dune volume in 2010/10~2010/11 is higher than above 
observed values. Compared with three regions, region I has 
large difference in deposition volume values. Owing Region
