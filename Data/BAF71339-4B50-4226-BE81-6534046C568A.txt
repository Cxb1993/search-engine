中文摘要 
在本篇論文我們提出了，使用平行無超車光延遲線來建構一個最大延遲為d的flexible光延遲
線。當這些無超車光延遲線皆滿足先進先出的順序，我們證明了在固定個數的無超車光延遲線，
使光封包遺失個數最少的最佳的策略為：將來的光封包指定到最大剩餘服務時間的無超車光延遲
線。以此最佳策略為基礎，我們證明了要模擬flexible延遲線，需要⌈(d + 1)/2⌉條無超車光延遲線。
我們也證明了如果可以容忍一些封包遺失的可能性，我們只需要O(√d)條的無超車光延遲線。在這
種狀況，我們證明了無超車光延遲線剩餘的服務時間的行為狀況如同均勻的隨機變數。 
關鍵字：光儲存器，可變光延遲線，無超車(non-overtaking)光延遲線，最佳策略，次序統計 
 
英文摘要 
In this paper we propose to construct an flexible delay line with maximum delay d by parallel 
non-overtaking delay lines. We show that for a fixed number of non-overtaking delay lines, an optimal 
policy to minimize packet losses is to assign arriving packets to the non-overtaking delay line that has 
the largest residual service time while maintaining the FIFO order for each non-overtaking delay lines. 
Based on this optimal policy we show that to exactly emulate an flexible delay line, one needs ⌈(d+1)/2⌉ 
non-overtaking delay lines. We also show that if one can tolerate a small packet loss probability, one just 
needs O(√d) non-overtaking delay lines. In this case, we show that the residual service times of the 
non-overtaking delay lines behaved as if they followed the order statistics of uniform random variables. 
Index Terms—optical buffer, flexible delay line, non-overtaking delay line, optimal policy, order 
statistics
