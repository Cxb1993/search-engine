Large-Scale Dynamic Economic Dispatch for Considering Valve-Point Effects and 
Power Emission using Improved Particle Swarm Optimization

摘要 
本計畫已發展出一套動態最佳經濟調度
演算法(IPSO_MU)，在兼顧實際電力系統操作
的閥門點現象、總發電成本以及使發電污染最
佳化的考量下，開發出更具求解效能的改良型
粒 子 群 優 法 (Improved particle swarm 
optimization, IPSO)，並應用於「同時考慮閥門
點現象與發電污染之動態經濟調度」(dynamic 
economic dispatch for considering valve-point 
effects and power emission)問題上，可提供給
台電公司及民營業者參考，對國內產業界及學
術界作出貢獻。 
本計畫先針對傳統的粒子群優法的效率
加以改良，加入「遷移運算子」以提昇其效能，
開發出改良型粒子群優法。同時在以往的文獻
中，雖然也有部份的研究加入「發電污染」的
考慮，但是只有少數的學者將「閥門點現象」
這個電力系統實際存在的情況也一併加以評
估，而且以動態經濟調度(dynamic economic 
dispatch, DED)來分析並適用於大型電力系統
(large-scale power system)者更是缺乏。因此，
本計畫的執行不但採用真實系統的運轉操作
狀況設計，且將更能符合發電污染要求與具備
求解效能，相信本計畫的執行必能為國內的各
發電業者帶來更具實用性的「大型動態電力經
濟調度且符合現代環評標準」之解決方案。 
本計畫整合了「ε-限制式技術」、「改良
型粒子群優法」(IPSO)及「參數更新技術」
(multiple updating, MU)，以整體規劃的策略，
來分析大型動態經濟調度問題。本計畫所提出
的演算法具有下列優點；1)考慮實際電力系統
運轉操作的閥門點現象、2)可自動調適的處罰
參數以處理限制條件、3)開發出改良型粒子群
優法，僅需使用少量族群來求解可降低運算時
間增加效率、4)考慮發電污染具整體最佳化規
劃、5)和適用於大型電力系統之最佳動態經濟
調度問題。經過四個例題測試比較，驗證本計
畫的成果應用於 DED 問題上，確實具有效能
及實用價值。 
 
關鍵詞：動態經濟調度，大型電力系統、粒子
群優法、閥門點現象。 
 
Abstract 
This project proposes the large-scale 
dynamic economic dispatch for considering 
valve-point effects and power emission using the 
improved particle swarm optimization. Many 
works have been presented to solve the 
dispatching problem of the power systems. 
Unfortunately, few studies considered the 
dynamic economic dispatch with power 
emissions, and they failed to consider 
valve-point effects and emission level functions. 
This project will be not only applicable for the 
practical operation of large-scale dynamic 
economic dispatch of power systems, but also 
conform to emission constraints. This study is a 
useful solution to reduce cost and conform the 
standard of environmental pollution for the 
Taipower company and many private power 
enterprises. 
The integrated proposed algorithm 
(IPSO_MU) combines the ε -constraint 
technique, the improved particle swarm 
optimization (IPSO), and multiple updating 
(MU) using the evolutionary strategy to analysis 
DED problems. Many merits of the proposal 
have been presented herein: 1)applicable for the 
practical operation of power systems taking 
valve-point loadings into account; 2)adaptive

研究方法大致上可區分為四個類型：1)利用限
制式方法（The constraint method）[18, 19]。2)
利用加權比重方法（The weighting method）[15, 
20, 21]。3)分開個別計算方式（The separate 
runs method）[12, 22]。4)採用多目標整體規劃
方式（The multi-objective method）[16]。 
以第一個類型而言，此多目標問題中的污
染限制，被以「限制式方法」來處理，將原本
為多目標的型態降低為單目標問題來討論。對
於第二個類型而言，主要將各目標函數各自乘
上相當的「加權比重」（Weighting）值，來線
性組合成單一目標函數。如此做法對於具有不
同物理單位的各目標函數，可能會因為乘上一
個 單 位 轉 換 因 數 （ Non-commensurable 
factors），而喪失到其本身重要的特性（Loss 
significance）及精確度（Accuracy）。對於第三
個類型而言，「分開個別計算方式」是對於求
最大或最小值的個別目標函數分別處理之，再
整合出共同適合的答案。至於第四個類型採用
「多目標整體規劃方式」，也正是本計畫所採
用的方法。 
 
1.3動態經濟調度 
動態經濟調度（DED）[7, 23~30]的操作
規劃更是電力系統中非常實際的運作需求。在
動態電力經濟調度中，發電業者通常都會將各
發電機組的發電動態成本限制（Generating 
ramping rate limits: ramp-up and ramp-down）加
入考慮，而且以某一時間區段大小（Interval, 
例如 1小時）為單位，來整體考慮全部操作時
間區域（例如若分成 24 個時間區段，則全部
時間區域為 24 小時）中的整體最佳經濟成
本。所以動態電力經濟調度的求解困難度比靜
態的經濟調度更為艱鉅。本計畫運用參考文獻
[6]的（靜態 PED）實際成本模式，將其修正
為動態經濟調度成本模式，由「動態電力經濟
調度」整體規劃的角度，來研究出符合整體利
益，且完全滿足所有限制條件的電力系統動態
經濟調度最佳解，提供給電力系統業者運轉調
度之規劃參考。 
 
1.4 同時考慮「閥門點現象」與「發電污染」
之大型動態經濟調度 
截至目前為止，很少有文獻來同時考慮閥
門點現象與發電污染對動態電力經濟調度的
影響，事實上這兩個現象會同時存在於電力系
統實際操作中。由前述幾節(第 1.1-.1.3 節)的
分析結果我們可觀察出，對於考慮閥門點現象
與發電污染的「動態最佳經濟調度問題」，以
「多目標」的方式來規劃者卻是不多，尤其是
可適用在「大型電力系統」者更是缺乏，而無
論是「多目標」或「大型電力系統」都是事實
存在且需要解決的問題。又動態經濟調度的複
雜度比電力系統中靜態的經濟調度高出甚
多，所以為了可適用於(大型系統)的動態經濟
調度問題上，本計畫採用一種強健具有效率的
全域最佳規劃法-改良型粒子群優法來求解。
因此，本計畫運用以「多目標」整體規劃的角
度，來探索出符合整體利益，且完全滿足所有
限制條件的大型系統動態經濟調度最佳解，所
以本計畫是一件相當重要、有意義且極具挑戰
性的實用性研究。

3.2 改良型粒子群優法 
粒子群優法(particle swarm optimization, 
PSO) [33~ 35]，為一種近年來備受工程界關注
的最佳化演算法，PSO的優點為架構簡單，收
斂快速。PSO 為多代理人(multi-agent)演算
法，每一粒子即一代理人，其粒子數量及各參
數富有彈性，能依系統的大小來調整，而最後
總搜尋運算量及時間將可平均分配至粒子群
中的每個粒子，且由於採用認知模式及社會模
式使得其解能夠快速收斂至較佳的可行解。如
同基因演算法(Genetic algorithm, GA)一樣，
PSO以隨機解式作為起始於的世代，其發展係
依據觀測動物社會行為，如鳥群、魚群和群聚
行為理論，在 PSO 中的每個個體依據自身和
其群體的飛行經驗，被指定隨機化的速度值，
而這些個體或被稱粒子隨後飛越超空間。與
GA相比 PSO吸引人的特性為其具有「記憶」
功能，所有的粒子保有解決方案的知識(資
訊)，但在 GA 中，前述相關知識會隨世代更
替而被摧毀。另外，粒子間有結構性合作關
係，群體內粒子彼此分享所知的資訊。近年來
已有許多文獻提出將粒子群優法應用於電力
工程領域上，例如;配電狀態估測[36]及經濟運
轉排程[37, 38]等問題上，都能快速地獲得滿意
的解。所以利用 PSO 其快速收斂的特性，非
常適合應用於最佳電力機組運轉問題的求解。 
因為粒子群優法(PSO)是從鳥群覓食行為
所演化出的進化演算技術，藉由其量化能量函
數，來求得能量最小之最佳參數組的最佳化演
算法，具有架構簡單且易於實現的優勢，此演
算法具有以下兩項特點：1)粒子群優法為採用
貪婪搜尋的多代理人演算法，在每次疊代裏，
利用多個粒子搜尋解空間的多個區域，每一個
粒子都各自負責一部分的區域最佳化的參數
搜尋。2)粒子群優法中的粒子群藉由粒子群中
最佳的粒子給出訊息給其他的粒子，所以粒子
並非完全盲目的自我尋解。因為此種更新法
則，讓整個群體均勻的往最佳區域移動，使得
粒子群優法能更快速收斂至最佳解。 
所以粒子群優法這種仿生物技術，結合了
認知模式及社會模式[8, 34]，在每一次疊代之
中粒子透過跟蹤兩個極值pbest與gbest來更新
自己的參數組，來更新自己的速度和新的位
置，如下式所示，

4. 例題測試 
 
為了完整呈現本計畫所提之整合型最佳
化演算法(IPSO_MU)對於電力系統 DED 最佳
化問題的實用性，本節將以四個例題，來驗證

4.4 例題四:大型電力系統最佳折衷 
最後例題四則是大型電力系統最佳折衷
問題的討論，主要用來展現本計畫所提之整合

同時滿足每區段的負載平衡等式限制(h1~ h24)

3.  The Proposed HDE-MU 
3.1. HDE 
In HDE [9], the one-to-one competition will have a 
faster convergence speed to give a higher probability toward 
a global optimum with much less computation time. It can 
use a small population in the evolutionary process to obtain a 
global solution. 
Generally, Evolutionary optimization involves two 
critical issues evolutionary direction and population diversity. 
As the evolutionary direction is effective in searching, the 
strong evolutionary direction can reduce the computational 
burden and increase the probability of rapidly finding an 
(possibly local) optimum. As population diversity is 
increased, the genotype of the offspring differs more from the 
parent. Accordingly, a highly diverse population can increase 
the probability of exploring the global optimum and prevent a 
premature convergence to a local optimum. These two 
important factors are here balanced by both employing the 
accelerated operation and migration in HDE that can 
determine an efficacious direction in which to search for a 
solution and simultaneously maintain an appropriate diversity 
for a small population.

5.  Conclusions 
The HDE-MU for solving the CED problems has been 
proposed herein. Complication of the CED problem lies in 
the constraints imposed by the mutual dependencies of 
multi-demand and Heat-Power capacity. The HDE helps the 
proposed method efficiently search and refined exploit. The 
MU helps the proposed method avoid deforming the ALF and 
resulting in difficulty of solution searching. The proposed 
algorithm integrates the HDE and the MU that has the merits 
of taking a wide range of penalty parameters and a small 
population. Simulation results demonstrate that the proposed 
algorithm has more advantages for solving the CED problems 
than the previous methods. The contributions of this paper are 
the MU effectively handles the Heat-Power feasible region

HYBRID DIFFERENTIAL EVOLUTION FOR COGENERATION ECONOMIC 
DISPATCH PROBLEM 
CHAO-LUNG CHIANG , CHIA-AN WANG  
 Depart. of Electronic Engineering, Nan Kai University of Technology, Nan-Tou 542, Taiwan. 
E-MAIL: t129@nkut.edu.tw, t156@nkut.edu.tw 
Abstract: 
This paper proposes a hybrid differential evolution with 
multiplier updating (HDE-MU) to solve cogeneration economic 
dispatch (CED) problems. The hybrid differential evolution 
(HDE) has the ability to efficiently search and actively explore 
solutions. Multiplier updating (MU) is introduced to avoid 
deforming the augmented Lagrange function (ALF), which is 
adopted to manage the system constraints of CED Problems. 
The proposed HDE-MU integrates the HDE with the MU. Two 
cogeneration examples are employed to demonstrate that the 
proposed algorithm has the benefits of straightforwardness; 
ease of implementation; better effectiveness than previous 
methods; better effectiveness and efficiency than the traditional 
differential evolution (DE); automatic adjustment of the 
randomly assigned penalty to an appropriate value, and the 
requirement for only a small population when applied to CED 
operations. 
Keywords: 
Cogeneration; Differential evolution; Economic dispatch 
1. Introduction 
Recently, cogeneration units have played an increasingly 
important role in the utility industry [1~5]. Complexity arises 
if one or more units produce both power and heat. Such a 
case, both the power demand and the heat demand must be 
satisfied. Cogeneration units provide both electrical power 
and heat to customers. The heat production capacity of most 
cogeneration units, depend on the power generated, and vice 
versa. The mutual dependency of multiple-demand and 
Heat-Power capacity of those units introduce complexities in 
the integration of cogeneration units into the economic 
dispatch (ED) problems. 
Although the cogeneration system is popular, few works 
have been conducted in the field of the CED problems. Wang 
et al. [1] used a stochastic model for CED problems based on 
an improved particle swarm optimization. Song et al. [2] 
presented a novel ant colony search algorithm (ACSA) to 
solve the CED problems. Wong et al. [3] proposed an 
evolutionary programming (EP) approach for the combined 
heat and power system. Guo et al. [4] decomposed the CED 
problem into two sub-problems the heat dispatch and power 
dispatch. Song et al. [5] employed an improved penalty 
function formulation for the genetic algorithm (GA) to solve 
the CED problems. 
DE was first proposed over 1994-1996 by Storn and 
Price at Berkeley as a novel approach to numerical 
optimization [6]. It is a population-based method and 
generally considered as a parallel stochastic direct search 
optimizer which is very sample yet powerful. The main 
advantages of DE are its capability of solving optimization 
problems which require minimization process with nonlinear 
and multi-modal objective functions. It employs a 
non-uniform crossover using parameters of child vectors to 
guide through the minimization process. The mutation 
operation with DE is conducted by arithmetical combinations 
which exploit the difference among randomly selected 
vectors, other than perturbing the genes in individuals with 
small probability. These special features make DE a precise, 
fast as well as robust algorithm. Furthermore, DE has been 
attracting more and more attentions from industry 
applications [7~ 9]. However, it has problems of converging 
onto local optimal solutions. Two additional operations of 
acceleration and migration are embedded in the HDE [9] to 
overcome such drawbacks for solving optimal control 
problems. 
Michalewicz et al. [10] surveyed and compared several 
constraint-handling techniques used in evolutionary 
algorithms. Among these techniques, the penalty function 
method is one of the most popularly used to handle 
constraints. In this method, the objective function includes a 
penalty function that is composed of the squared or absolute 
constraint violation terms. Powell [11] noted that classical 
optimization methods include a penalty function have certain 
weaknesses that become most serious when penalty 
parameters are large. More importantly, large penalty 
parameters ill condition the penalty function so that obtaining 
a good solution is difficult. However, if the penalty 
parameters are too small, the constraint violation does not

the outer loop toward producing an upper limit of La. When 
both inner and outer iterations become sufficiently large, the 
ALF converges to a saddle-point of the dual problem [13]. 
Advantages of the proposed HDE-MU are that the HDE 
efficiently searches the optimal solution in the economic 
dispatch process and the MU effectively tackles system 
constraints.

設計，且將更能符合發電污染要求與具備求解效能，相信本計畫的執行必能為國內的各發
電業者帶來更具實用性的「大型動態電力經濟調度且符合現代環評標準」之解決方案。
