Abstract 
In this project, tetraoctylammonium bromide (TOAB) was spin-coated onto the surface of a 
green-emissive poly(9,9-dialkylfluorene) derivative (G-PF) to fabricate cathode-independent polymer 
light-emitting diodes (PLEDs). The electroluminescence efficiencies were 15.4, 11.4, and 9.1 cd/A for TOAB 
with Al, Ag, and Au as the cathode, respectively, which are better than that of the device with Ca/Al as the 
cathode (6.1 cd/A). The molecular nanomorphologies of TOAB deposited on G-PF were investigated using 
synchrotron X-ray diffraction. Results show that TOAB molecules nucleated on the hydrophobic G-PF 
surface and self-assembled into a highly ordered lamellar structure during the spin-coating process. This 
unique structure produces suitable molecular dipoles between N+ and Br-, significantly improving the 
electron-injection ability from stable metals to G-PF.  
Keywords: Self-assembled; Lamellar structure; Electron injection; Tetraoctylammonium bromide

Results and Discussion 
Device performance:  Figure 1a plots the J-V curves for devices with various cathode materials. The 
current turn-on voltages (VJ-on) for devices with Al, TOAB/Al, and NH4Br/Al are 1.6V, 2V, and 1.4V, 
respectively. VJ-on is related to △Φ+ψ, where △Φ is the built-in potential, which is the difference between the 
work functions of the anode and the cathode, and ψ is the majority carrier barrier height9. Figure 2 shows that 
a high electron-injection barrier is caused by the mismatch between the WF of Al (4.2 eV) and the lowest 
unoccupied molecular orbital (LUMO) of G-PF (3.3 eV). This indicates that the majority carriers for these 
devices are holes. Since no substantial change in the hole injection properties occurs at the interface between 
the anode and G-PF, the high VJ-on for the TOAB-based device results from a decrease in the WF of the 
cathode. However, a lower VJ-on was obtained for the NH4Br/Al-based device, which indicates that the WF of 
the cathode for the NH4Br/Al-based device is higher than that for the Al-based device. The change in the WF 
of the cathode was verified by photovoltaic measurements. In Figure 3, the TOAB/Al-based device exhibits 
an open-circuit voltage (Voc) of 1.8 V, which is much larger than that of the Al-based device (1.3 V). This is 
due to the attainment of a higher built-in potential27. Since the WFs of the anodes for the two devices are the 
same, the WF of the cathode decreased due to the presence of the TOAB layer. The Voc of the 
NH4Br/Al-based device is 1.2 V, which is slightly lower than that of the Al-based device. From our previous 
study8, this result indicates that the NH4Br layer is most likely an insulating layer. The photovoltaic results are 
consistent with the VJ-on results.

二、與會心得 
 UCLA 材料系 Prof. Yang 與 UCSB 化學系 Prof. Bazan 的演說相當精采，令人印象
深刻。Prof. Yang 提出以金屬氧化物取代酸性的 PEDOT:PSS 作為連接層，例如五氧
化二釩、三氧化鉬等，將兩個利用不同能隙之高分子材料製作的太陽能電池串聯之
構想，增加其穩定性。而相較於 PEDOT:PSS，金屬氧化物薄層的高穿透率，使吸收
光譜的範圍能夠變寬，以提高有機太陽能電池之效率。Prof. Bazan 將共軛聚電解質
應用於有機光電元件之中，包括有機太陽能電池、高分子發光二極體等，控制高分
子材料與金屬電極間的界面現象。其中有機太陽能電池具可撓曲、方便製作與製作
成本低廉等優點，目前 Prof. Yang 已將有機太陽能電池的效率提升至 8%左右，日本
也宣稱效率可突破 10%。現今此領域成長快速，或許能在不久的將來應用於生活中，
太陽能電池的生產有機會像印製報紙一般簡單、快速。台灣的能源相當缺乏，在替
代能源的研究與發展須再努力。
