摘要 
本研究旨在探討全潤濕性梯度表面上微液滴傳輸行為及其遲滯現象與黏著性之變化。首
先運用靜電逐層組裝技術在玻璃基板表面製備出兼具透明及超親水二氧化矽奈米粒子薄膜，
然後使用矽烷溶液藉由前進溶液法成功地在薄膜表面上創造出一全潤濕性譜 (從超疏水到超
親水，接觸角：165° ~ 4°) 的梯度化表面，並觀察微液滴在此梯度表面上之運動行為。實驗結
果顯示，奈米粒子薄膜表面雖然具有較大的接觸角梯度，但是並無法驅動微液滴；相較之下，
平板玻璃潤濕性梯度表面雖然接觸角梯度較小，但卻足以驅動微液滴。此一現象促使本研究
進一步探討奈米粒子薄膜及平板玻璃表面，由低至高的矽烷疏水改質程度下之遲滯現象及黏
著性。由實驗結果得知，在平板玻璃上的遲滯現象及黏著性都較小且很相近；反觀在二氧化
矽奈米粒子薄膜上則展現出先由小至大，再由大至小之極大的變化。本研究亦提出造成此一
差異的可能機制。這些不同黏著性的表面，皆具有極大的應用價值。此外，超疏水/超親水楔
型圖案化表面亦可成功地驅動微液滴自發性傳輸。 
 
關鍵字：超疏水-超親水梯度化表面、超疏水/超親水圖案化表面、微液滴自發性傳輸、潤濕性、
遲滯現象、黏著性 
 
1、前言 
超疏水與超親水表面皆具有一表面自潔 (self-cleaning) 功能之特性，而此特性在奈米科
技時代引起了廣泛的研究，其概念分別源自於蓮花效應 (lotus effect)[1-2]帶動仿生製備超疏水
表面與結合二氧化鈦的光催化與光誘導超親水特性[3]，使表面皆具備自潔的效果，將此類自
潔表面應用於大樓帷幕玻璃、建築塗料、衣物具撥水與撥油性、光電元件與太陽能面板等，
不僅可大量節省許多物品的清潔維護成本，更可提升效能以及創造龐大的商機。超疏水與超
親水此兩種表面，分別處於潤濕性的極端地帶，若在同一表面上創造一全潤濕性梯度化表面，
亦是一個值得研究的主題，例如在一個表面上創造漸進的化學組成變化或是漸進的物理結構
變化，進而產生漸進的潤濕性變化。此種具有潤濕性連續變化的單一表面可以有效地應用在
細胞的附著和生長、蛋白質的吸附、觸媒的吸附、及微液滴的自發性傳輸等，有相當大的發
展潛力[4]。另有非洲納米比沙漠的 Stenocara 甲蟲，它利用翅膀捕捉朝霧中的濕氣維生，甲蟲
前後轉動翅膀迎向風，霧中的小水滴會聚集在翅膀上直徑約 100 μm 的親水性突起，當水滴
達一定重量後便因重力而滑落，順著翅膀上的疏水區導入甲蟲嘴中。美國麻省理工學院的研
究人員模仿甲蟲翅膀結構[5]，製作出具有超疏水/親水的圖案化表面，這種仿生表面將可廣
泛應用於集水、藥物釋放控制、開放式微流裝置及芯片實驗室。此外，經由表面型態/結構的
設計及表面化學修飾，除了超親水、超疏水，更可進一步產生超雙親 (superamphiphilicity)、
超雙疏 (superamphiphobicity) 等表面性質並進行其間的轉換[6]，也提高了表面潤濕性研究的
應用潛力。

The realization of transparent and superhydrophilic/superhydrophobic surfaces by silica nanoparticulate thin
ﬁlms was exploited in this work. An aqueous electrostatic layer-by-layer assembly process was utilized to
fabricate nanoparticulate thin ﬁlms with adhesion/body/top layer structure on glass substrates by using SiO2
nanoparticles and polyelectrolytes. The effects of volume ratio of differently sized silica nanoparticle solutions
for the body layer deposition on transmittance in visible light region and surface wettability of the
nanoparticulate thin ﬁlms were systematically studied. The experimental results revealed that both optical
transparency and superhydrophobicity/superhydrophilicity can be achieved on the same SiO2 nanoparticu-

layer deposition, and with and without silane treatment in the fabrication process. The high contrast of
wettability that can be achieved by this way suggests the possibility of the creation of superhydrophilic/
superhydrophobic patterning and superhydrophilic–superhydrophobic gradient on the same surfaces.

1. Introduction
While a surface is usually called “superhydrophobic” when the
advancing contact angle of water on it is larger than 150° with a very
low contact angle hysteresis (the difference between advancing and
receding contact angles) [1,2], a surface is called “superhydrophilic”
when the advancing contact angle of water on it is less than 5° [3–6]
or the time for complete wetting by small droplets of water has been
observed to be less than 0.5 s [6,7]. It is well-known now the
wettability of naturally occurring solid surfaces with liquids is
governed by both the chemical properties and the microstructure of
the surface. Plant leaf surfaces that exhibit unusual wetting char-
acteristics of superhydrophobicity have been investigated and
documented [8,9]. In these leaves (including the lotus leaf) even
dew and fog, but especially rain, lead to the complete removal of
particulate contamination. This self-cleaning or Lotus effect [8,10] is
caused by both the hierarchical roughness of the leaf surface from
micrometer-sized papillae having nanometer-sized branch like
protrusions and the intrinsic material hydrophobicity of a surface
layer of epicuticular wax covering these papillae [11,12]. The natural
surfaces have inspired researchers to consider biomimetic approaches
for generating superhydrophilic, superhydrophobic, and superhydro-
philic/superhydrophobic artiﬁcial surfaces. A recent review of the
design and creation of surfaces with special wettability such as
superhydrophilicity and superhydrophobicity is available [13]. More-
over, the preparation of artiﬁcial superhydrophilic/superhydrophobic
patterned surfaces can then be possibly realized based on these
methods.
While surface roughness is necessary for surface wettability,
surface roughness must be minimized to reduce the light scattering so
that light transparency can be achieved. Establishment of the
appropriate surface structure length scale, therefore, is crucial to the
fabrication of thin ﬁlms that exhibit both properties. Xiu et al. [14]
demonstrated that a sol–gel process using a eutectic liquid can be
invoked to form superhydrophobic, optically transparent ﬁlms on
glass slides. Previous investigations of the optical transparency of
superhydrophobic ﬁlms have also been reviewed by Xiu et al. [14].
Among them, an electrostatic layer-by-layer (ELbL) processing
scheme that can be utilized to create transparent superhydrophobic
ﬁlms from SiO2 nanoparticles of various sizes has been demonstrated
by Bravo et al. [15]. Their ﬁlms consisted of three main parts:
adhesion, body, and top layers. Bilayers of cationic and anionic
polyelectrolytes were deposited to create the adhesion-promoting
multilayer. For polyelectrolyte–silica body layers, two different sized
silica nanoparticles were used. Furthermore, top layers containing
only one-sized silica nanoparticles were also added to enhance the
two-scale roughness necessary for superhydrophobic ﬁlms. The ﬁnal
assembly was rendered superhydrophobic with sintering and silane
treatment. Optical transmission levels above 90% throughout most of
the visible region of the spectrumwere realized in optimized coatings.

While the solid sample is held in a ﬁxed position by the electro-
balance, the wetting liquid contained in a beaker scans along the solid
surface at a constant speed via a computer-controlled stage. The
meniscus formed at the interface is characterized by the dynamic
contact angle at the interface. Calculations from the Wilhelmy
technique are derived directly from the Young equation. A simple
equation may relate the cosine of the contact angle to the magnitude
of the wetting force recorded by the balance, the surface tension of the
probe liquid, and the wetted perimeter of the solid sample. Contact
angles are measured in two directions—in one direction as the stage
moves up, advancing the liquid across the solid surface, and in the
opposite direction as the stagemoves down, receding the liquid across
the previously wetted surface. The difference between these two
extreme contact angles is known as contact angle hysteresis, and is a
universal property of most surfaces. By running multicycle of
immersion-and-emersion on the same sample, the dynamic contact
angle can be used to readily distinguish between various kinetic and
true thermodynamic hysteresis effects.

Design and fabrication of antireﬂective nanoparticulate thin ﬁlms with
superhydrophilic self-cleaning properties on glass substrate
Shu-Hsuan Lin, Yi-Ni Wu, Yu-Chu Lin, Yu-Min Yang *
Department of Chemical Engineering, National Cheng Kung University, Tainan 701, Taiwan
1. Introduction
By utilizing the photocatalytic and superhydrophilic properties
of TiO2 (Feng and Jiang, 2006; Fujishima and Honda, 1972;
Fujishima et al., 2000; Fujishima and Zhang, 2006; Hashimoto et al.,
2005; Lee et al., 2009; Liau et al., 2008; Ng et al., 2008; Parkin and
Palgrave, 2005; Wang et al., 1997, 2009; Wantala et al., 2010; Yu
et al., 2007), considerable success in the realization of self-cleaning
surfaces has been achieved. Because of the wide range of possible
applications, TiO2-based self-cleaning coatings may become an
important labor-saving device. TiO2-based coatings can be easily
applied on transparent substrates such as glass to provide a self-
cleaning function. The coatings, however, usually enhance the
surface reﬂection of transparent glass (refractive index: 1.52) due
to the large refractive index of TiO2 (refractive index: 2.52 for
anatase; 2.76 for rutile). According to the simpliﬁed Fresnel’s
equation for normal incident light (Yoldas, 1980), the reﬂection at
the air–glass, air–anatase TiO2 and air–rutile TiO2 interfaces are
4.3%, 18.6% and 21.9%, respectively. This is a great disadvantage to
the various applications such as glazings, display devices, optical
materials, and solar cells. Coatings with antireﬂective (AR)
properties are therefore most desirable.
The principle of antireﬂection is interference of the reﬂected
light from the medium-coating and coating-substrate interfaces.

Design and fabrication of transparent and superhydrophilic self-cleaning thin ﬁlms on glass substrate by
7 nm TiO2/22 nm SiO2 all-nanoparticle coatings via aqueous electrostatic layer-by-layer assembly

properties. This was achieved by using appropriate periodic calcinations rather than only once
calcination of the assembled nanoparticulate thin ﬁlms. Average refractive index of 1.30  0.03 at 633 nm
and average bilayer thickness of 20.18 nmwere determined for the as-fabricated nanoparticulate thin ﬁlms.
Furthermore, average transmittance values higher than 93.5% were exhibited by all the nanoparticulate thin
ﬁlms and the experimental results were only at the verymost of 2% lower than those predicted by the theory.
The self-cleaning properties of the nanoparticulate thin ﬁlms were revealed by the decomposition of two
model contaminants under UV illumination. The experimental results showed that the photocatalytic
decomposition rates are increased with the increase of bilayer number of the nanoparticulate thin ﬁlm.

3.3. Calcination and periodic calcination of nanoparticulate thin ﬁlms
The as-assembled nanoparticulate thin ﬁlms were calcinated in
a furnace under air atmosphere at 550 8C for 2 h to obtain sintered
and stable thin ﬁlms. As mentioned above, however, drastic
decrease of transmittance was found for thin ﬁlms with TiO2/SiO2
bilayer number larger than 40. In these cases, a periodic calcination
process by which the assembled thin ﬁlms were calcinated
periodically after every 20 or 30 bilayers assembly of TiO2 and
SiO2 nanoparticles was used instead. For example, a 60-bilayer
TiO2/SiO2 thin ﬁlm can be prepared by three procedures. First, the
thin ﬁlm was calcinated after the assembly of 60 bilayers (TiO2/
SiO2)60. Second, the thin ﬁlm was calcinated after the assembly of
the ﬁrst 20 bilayers (TiO2/SiO2)20, calcinated again after the
assembly of the second 20 bilayers (TiO2/SiO2)40, and calcinated
once more after the assembly of the last 20 bilayers (TiO2/SiO2)60
*.
Third, the thin ﬁlmwas calcinated after the assembly of the ﬁrst 30
bilayers (TiO2/SiO2)30 and calcinated again after the assembly of
the last 30 bilayers (TiO2/SiO2)60
*. For thin ﬁlms prepared by the
periodic calcination process, asterisk (*) was indicated on the
bilayer number.

The mechanical integrity is important for practical application
of any coating. The as-assembled (TiO2/SiO2)x all-nanoparticle
multilayers, however, show poor mechanical properties due to the
absence of polyelectrolytes that glue the deposited particles
together within the multilayers. The mechanical properties of the
all-nanoparticle multilayers can be signiﬁcantly improved by
calcinating the as-assembled multilayers at high temperature
(550 8C) for 3 h, which leads to the partial fusing of the
nanoparticles and better adhesion of the coatings to glass
substrates (Lee et al., 2006). Asmentioned above, the as-assembled
multilayers were calcinated in a furnace under air atmosphere at
550 8C for 2 h in this work. A simple semiquantitative evaluation of
adhesion of the ensuing coatings to glass substrate was carried out
in this work by the tape peel test (Lacombe, 2006). A dyed coating
of (TiO2/SiO2)120* was cut into eighty (8  10) 2.5 mm  2.5 mm
squares with a sharp knife and thereafter subjected to peel test by
using a 3 M Scotch tape. None of the squares were able to be
removed by the tape. This indicated that strong enough adhesion
can be achieved through the calcination process applied in this
work.

一、參加會議經過 
 
        本會議已有 34 年的歷史。自從 1976 年，Kash L. Mittal 教授在美國紐約州奧爾班尼
(Albany)舉辦第一屆界面活性劑溶液會議以來，每兩年都舉辦一次。1978 年及 1980 年分別又
在美國境內的 Knoxville 及 Potsdam 舉行過兩次會議。自此界面活性劑溶液會議走向全面的
國際化，在世界各地舉辦：瑞典 Lund(1982)，法國 Bordeaux(1984)，印度 New Delhi(1986)，
加拿大 Ottawa(1988)，美國 Gainesville(1990)，保加利亞 Varna(1992)，委內瑞拉 Caracas(1994)，
以色列 Jerusalem(1996)，瑞典 Stockholm(1998)，美國 Gainesville(2000)，西班牙
Barcelona(2002)，巴西 Fortaleza(2004)，南韓 Seoul(2006)，德國 Berlin(2008)。本次第 18屆
會議則在澳洲墨爾本舉行。這麼多年來，雖然會議主題涵蓋的範圍愈來愈廣，但系列會議的
名稱都一直維持「界面活性劑溶液會議」，沒有改變。 
 
        報告人曾於 20年前，第 8屆會議在美國 Gainesville(1990)佛羅里達大學舉辦時，規
劃前往與會。但是，後來因故不能成行，一直引以為憾。事隔 20年，本次會議得以成行，
也了了一件心願。此次會議台灣與會人員除了報告人之外，同系尚有張鑑祥、侯聖澍教授；
另外，報告人與張鑑祥教授同時還帶了三位博士班研究生—李蓉、邱俊瑋、李承軒—一同與
會參加口頭及海報論文發表。 
 
        近年來，膠體及界面科學這一門跨領域的學門隨著物理學、化學及生物學的不斷進
展，已進入一個進步而成熟的發展階段。因此，其應用也涵蓋了許多不同的層面，例如：化
學工程及奈米技術，生化及生物醫學技術，製藥技術，食品技術，微電子及光學，石油技術，
環境科技等。而新穎界面活性劑的發明、界面活性劑溶液性質的了解及應用，與膠體及界面
科學的進程息息相關，可以說是膠體及界面科學能夠持續進步的核心原因之一。此外，在全
球化的衝擊下，國際間學術界與工業界菁英的參與和交流的範圍也與日俱增。在界面活性劑
科學與膠體及界面科學領域中，不管是基礎或應用方面也已有長時間的發展經驗，而且不乏
工業界的熱烈回饋。這次會議也獲得澳大利亞國家科學與工業研究組織(Commomwealth 
for Colloid and Surface Science, ASCASS)主辦的亞洲膠體暨界面科學會議(Asian 
Conference on Colloid and Interface Science,ACCIS)，也是每兩年召開一次的同領域國際會
議。第一屆、第二屆及第三屆會議分別是在 2005 年、2007 年及 2009 年由日本名古屋大
學、中國山東大學、及南韓工業及工程化學學會承辦；第四屆將於 2011 年在台灣台南成
功大學舉行。ASCASS 的成立其實是反應亞洲地區對膠體及界面科學領域跨國交流合作
的需求。亞洲膠體暨界面科學會議的召開，是希望藉由定期會議的舉行，促進亞洲地區
膠體暨界面科學領域學術界與產業界的交流，並提升其發展與應用。日本的膠體及界面
科學領域是日本化學會下的一個分支；在中國，膠體及界面科學領域除了也是化學會下
的一個分支之外，第二屆會議的承辦單位亦是中國教育部設置的膠體及界面化學重點實
驗室；南韓似乎並沒有相關組織；在台灣則有一個獨立的界面科學學會。台灣界面科學
學會雖然規模不大，但已有穩固的基礎，學術界與產業界皆積極參與；而且自 1978 年起
亦發行界面科學會誌，已歷三十餘年，似乎大有可為，應積極推動台灣界面科學學會與
亞洲膠體及界面科學學會之間的交流，並透過這個平台與亞洲各國進行相關合作，再進
一步與世界其他界面科學學會交流合作。
