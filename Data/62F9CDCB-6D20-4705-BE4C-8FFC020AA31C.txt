摘要 
本研究考慮具擋板的彎曲微流道以及具擋板及側注入的彎曲微流道之混合
器中流體的混合行為。我們以聚二甲基矽氧烷，利用光微影技術製造微混合器。
並以光學顯微鏡、CCD攝影機與影像擷取卡配合個人電腦，組成本研究的影像
擷取系統，以觀測流體混合的過程。同時本研究也利用數值軟體 CFD-ACE+模
擬微混合器中流體的混合行為。本研究探討流速，擋板的設置，排列與寬度大
小，彎曲流道的曲率半徑以及設置雙側流道對混合效率的影響。本研究結果顯
示，擋板的設置為促進流體混合最主要的影響因素，隨著擋板的寬度增加混合
越好，但也伴隨著需要較高的壓力輸入。壓力問題可藉由將兩個側注入側流道
設置在擋板前或擋板之間來改善，使之不用提高入口的壓力輸入即可得到好的
混合結果。另外，離心力產生的徑向流動及設置多個寬度較小的擋板也可以增
進混合效率，以上方法可相互搭配達到高混合效率及低輸入壓力的設計。 
 
關鍵字：微混合器，側注入，擋板，彎曲流道，混合效率 
 
Abstract  
In this work, we consider mixing behaviors of fluids in a curved micro-mixer 
with baffles and side injections.  We fabricate the micro-mixer with 
polydimethysiloxane by a micro-photolithography.  An image capture system, 
including an optical microscope, a CCD camera, an image capture card and a 
personal computer, has been set up.  The visualization of the mixing process and 
the gray value of the mixing fluids are obtained by the system.  We also study the 
mixing flow by a software, the CFD-ACE+.  Mixing efficiency is evaluated by 
calculating gray value distribution of the fluids flowing through the micro-mixer.  
The effects of the flow velocity, the arrangement and the width of the baffles, the 
radius of the curved channel, and the arrangement of side injections on the mixing 
efficiency are investigated.  The results show that the baffles dominate the mixing 
process and the increase of the width of baffles improves the mixing efficiency, but 
it also increases the pressure applied.  The increase of the pressure applied can be 
remedied by arranged side injections either before or between the baffles; the results 
of a micro-mixer with two side injections show the high mixing efficiency can be 
obtained without increasing pressure applied.  Furthermore, the transverse 
advection arose from centrifugal force and arranging many smaller width baffles 
also increases the mixing efficiency.  Micro-mixers with the combination of the 
above designs may mix liquids well with less increase of the pressure applied.  
 
Key word：micro-mixer, side injections, baffles, curved channel, mixing efficiency

1. 緒論 
1990年 A. Manz等人[1]提出微型全分析系統（micro total analysis system, 
µ -TAS）的概念，希望應用微機電系統的加工技術，讓流體在微管道中執行實
驗室操作的各種程序，並整合在一片晶片上，使其較傳統的檢測程序，具有反
應速度增加，高靈敏度、高再現性（repeatability）、低污染、低成本與減少操作
時的誤差等優點。 
    由於流體的混合與反應幾乎是同時發生，因此流體混合的好壞，明顯影響
反應的完成程度。流道微型化後的流動傾向於層流（laminar flow），不易利用
紊流促進流體的混合，流體的混合主要依靠分子間的擴散作用。 
回應增進流體混合的需要，出現許多關於微混合器的研究，至今已發展出
多種巧妙的設計。微混合器可分成下述兩大類： 
(1)主動式混合器（active mixer）：藉由放置可動結構或外加的力場來產生流體
的擾動，增進流體的混合。像 Vivek與 Kim[2]製造扇型的致動器，產生側向橫
波來促使流體混合。Tsai 與 Lin等人[3]在微管道中產生氣泡，藉由氣泡的爆炸
使兩種流體間的接觸面成波浪形，增加流體的接觸面積以促進混合的效果。主
動式混合器由於放置可動元件或外加力場的設計，使其在製程上較為複雜，成
本也較高，但能提供較佳的混合效果為其優勢。 
(2)被動式混合器（passive mixer）：除了利用流體的流動與擴散作用達成混合
外，無外加裝置與力場，因此其研究大都在於如何設計微流道的幾何結構，以
增加流體間的接觸面積與縮短分子的擴散距離，或造成流道對流場的擠壓，使
原本的層流流場產生徑向流動或渦流等效應。像 Schwesinger 等人[4] 設計上
下左右交相分合之立體微流道，以增加接觸面積促進混合。Liu 等人[5]設計立
體彎曲微管道，使流場產生混沌移流（chaotic advection）的現象，促進流體的
混合。Jiang 等人[6]利用彎曲流道離心力產生的徑向流動，促進流體的混合吳
青峰[7]利用加設檔板使流場產生渦流（vortex）與壓縮擴張的效應，增進流體
的混合效果。被動式混合器製程較主動式混合器簡單，成本也較低，但流道內
複雜的幾何結構增加了流場的阻力，使其混合時間較長，同時混合效果也不如
主動式混合器。 
於此，本研究之目的在於利用微機電系統技術，製造一種操作步驟簡單，
且能在較短時間內達到混合的被動式微混合器裝置，並從實驗與模擬中觀察兩
種互溶液體（miscible liquids）在不同幾何結構的微流道內的流動與混合的現
象，討論結構尺寸、流率以及對應之雷諾數對混合的影響，進而提供高效能被
動式微混合器之較佳設計方向。

3. 製程與實驗

    本研究著重於被動式微混合器幾何結構影響流體混合的探討。微混合器的
實驗主要以流場觀測為主，定性地描述流體流動與混合的行為，同時混合的過
程中，因為流體濃度的不均勻，使流場產生灰階值的分佈。本研究中以光學顯
微鏡、CCD攝影機與影像擷取卡配合個人電腦，觀察並拍攝後混合器中流體的
流動與混合，取得影像後比較模擬結果,以評估其準確性，並做定性與定量的分
析。圖 4.1顯示為兩流體流經無擋板以及具有三個擋板，流道寬度為 400 mµ ，
深寬比為 0.325 的彎曲流道微混合器的流動與混合。圖中數值方法模擬出的混
合情形與實驗觀測的結果非常相似，顯示本文數值模擬相當可靠。

為 0.075、9、81的多組曲率半徑為 1800 mµ 的彎曲流道微混合器，並與流道中
心線長度相同的直型微混合器比較流體的混合情形。圖 4.2-4.5所示為流道寬度
260 mµ ，深寬比為 0.5的彎曲流道微混合器，在θ = o45 、 o90 、 o135 以及 o180 的

最低，這是因為側流道流體的注入方向與渦流轉旋方向相同，因此僅需較少的
壓力降即可將流體經由側流道注入微混合器內。 
本研究為探討側流道的側注入角度對流體混合的影響，以設置側流道與擋
 15
圖中顯示，擋板寬度是影響壓力降的主要原因，而擋板數目並未對壓力降造成
明顯的影響。於是可設置較多寬度較小的擋板，利用產生的持續擾動來促進混
合，同時因為影響壓力降的因素主要是擋板的寬度，因此可產混合效率與降力
降的較佳化設置，例如本研究中，設置十個寬度為 200 mµ 的擋板的彎曲流道微
混合器，除了在雷諾數為 9時，其混合效率皆較設置二個寬度為 350 mµ 的擋板
的彎曲流道微混合器好。在本文所選用的雷諾數範圍中，其混合效率都較設置
二個寬度為 300 mµ 的擋板、三個寬度為 300 mµ 的擋板的彎曲流道微混合器好，
同時除了雷諾數為 3 與 9 的流動情況外，其混合效率皆較設置四個寬度為
300 mµ 的擋板的彎曲流道微混合器好。另外在雷諾數為 81 時，設置十個寬度
為 200 mµ 的擋板的彎曲流道微混合器，利用流場持續擾動的疊加，其混合效率
甚至還較設置三個寬度為 350 mµ 的擋板，以及設置五個寬度為 300 mµ 的擋板
的彎曲流道微混合器好。只是在雷諾數不高不低的流動絛件時，還是需要寬度
較大的擋板設置來促進混合。 
 
6. 結論與計劃成果自評 
    本研究考慮具擋板的彎曲流道微混合器以及具側道及擋板的彎曲流道微混
合器中流體的混合行為。本研究以聚二甲基矽氧烷，利用光微影技術製造微混
合器，並以影像擷取系統觀測流體的混合過程，並驗証 CFD-ACE+數值模擬結
果的準確性。結果顯示離心力產生的徑向流動，以及側流道的設置可以增進混
合效率，以擋板的設置對混合的影響最大，隨著擋板的寬度增加混合越好，但
也伴隨著較高的壓力降，壓力問題可藉由設置雙側流道來改善，使之在較小的
壓力降即可得到均勻的混合結果。雙側流道設置的位置及側注入角度需要考慮
徑向流動的方向以及渦流旋轉的方向，才可得到更佳的混合。另外設置多個寬
度較低的擋板，利用流場持續疊加的擾動達到均勻混合，可與所需的壓力降搭
配達到較佳化的設計，但在雷諾數不高不低的流動條件時，仍需較寬的擋板設
置才能獲得均勻的混合。 
    本研究遵循原計劃執行，相符程度近於十分相符，所得結果摘要如上一
段所述，除影像擷取系統只能自上方觀測流體的混合過程，無法獲得流道截面
濃度分佈稍感不足，預期目標大致達成(以實驗獲得流道截面濃度分佈並不非原
訂目標)。接著我們將以英語改寫本報告，擬投稿相關國際期刊。

Abstract 
 
In this work, the discrete ordinates method has been adapted to solve transient radiative transfer in 
an anisotropically scattering slab with a spatial variable refractive index.  The medium considered 
has a refractive index increasing in the direction of irradiation and is exposed to a short laser pulse 
at one of the boundaries.  The time-resolved hemispherical reflectance and transmittance of the 
slab are obtained for a variety of optical thicknesses, scattering albedos, scattering phase function 
coefficients and gradients of refractive index.  The discrete ordinates method adapted for transient 
radiative transfer has been proved to be effective and capable of generating accurate results.   The 
time-resolved transmittance has a peak mainly due to the direct pulse and a plump tail due to the 
scattered radiation.  The former appears later for the case with a larger refractive index, because 
the radiation energy propagates in a smaller speed in a slab with a larger refractive index.  The 
latter may have a local maximum, when the scattering is strong.  The variable refractive index may 
have contribution to the scattering, because the gradient of refractive index makes radiation stream 
in a curve path and increases the internal reflection.  Thus, the local maximum increases with the 
increase of the gradient of refractive index.  It is found that the reflectance of the slab decreases 
with the increase of the gradient of refractive index.  The reflectance curve eventually merges with 
the corresponding transmittance curve as the time increases.  The merging process takes more time 
for the case with a lager gradient of refractive index.  Finally, it is found that the influence of 
anisotropically scattering on the time-resolved transmittance increases with the increase of the 
gradient of refractive index.

1.  Introduction 
 
Radiative transfer in a semitransparent medium with a spatially varying refractive index can be 
found in the atmosphere and many applications, such as optical imaging of biological tissues, 
plasma diagnostics and heating of glass and optical coatings.  Interest in the radiative transfer 
problems incorporating the inhomogeneities of refractive index is obtaining more attention recently.  
Ferwerda (1999) has derived the transient radiative transfer equation for a scattering medium with a 
spatially varying refractive index; Ben Abdallah and Le Dez (2000) have developed a curved 
ray-tracing method to analyse the quasi-steady radiative transfer in an absorbing-emitting slab with 
a spatially varying refractive index.  In these cases, due to the spatial variation of the refractive 
index, the speed of light is a function of the position and the radiation streams in curved paths rather 
than straight lines.  Researchers have developed more methods, including a combined curved 
ray-tracing and pseudo-source adding method (Huang et al., 2002), the discrete ordinates method 
(Lemonnier and Le Dez, 2002), a Monte Carlo curved ray-tracing method (Liu et al., 2003) and the 
finite element method (Liu, 2005), for solving the quasi-steady radiative transfer problems

The positive gradient of refractive index ( 0nns > ) makes the trajectories of some of the 
back-scattered radiation tend to an internal location, where the radiation is reflected back toward the 
right interface.  Thus, the reflectance of the slab at the left interface decreases with with the 
increase of the gradient.  The reflectance curve has a steep descent following the gradual descent 
after the peak.  The change from the gradual descent to the steep descent becomes less obvious as 
the gradient of refractive index increases or as the optical thickness increases.   
 
The time-resolved transmittance has a peak mainly due to the direct pulse and a plump tail due to 
the scattered radiation.  The former appears later for the case with a larger refractive index sn , 
because the radiation energy propagates in a slower speed in a slab with a larger refractive index.  
The latter may have a local maximum, when the scattering is strong.  The positive gradient of 
refractive index may have contribution to the scattering, because the positive gradient makes 
radiation stream in a curved path and increases the internal reflection.  Thus, the value of the local 
maximum increases with the increase of the sn .  The reflectance curve eventually merges with the 
corresponding transmittance curve as *t  increases.  The merging process takes more time for the 
case with a lager refractive index sn .  Finally, it is found that the influence of anisotropically 
scattering on the time-resolved transmittance increases with the increase of the sn .
