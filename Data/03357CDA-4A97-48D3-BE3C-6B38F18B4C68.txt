一、 中文摘要 
本研究中我們提出一個理論模型計算矽鍺
淺摻雜受子能階隨不同應力變化的情形。我們
不僅考慮對矽鍺[001]方向外加一個單軸應力
的情形，也考慮了成長於(001)矽基板之矽鍺
磊晶層中受子能階隨應力變化的情形。我們的
理論是以 Luttinger-Kohn 之考慮六條價帶的
理論出發，並利用 Bir-Pikus 應力理論來描述
系統受應力的情形。藉由群論的技巧我們可以
將雜質態的波函數分成不同的對稱性並且得
到不同對稱性之雜質態角度部份的解。如此不
但有助於理論分析也可以減少電腦計算的負

Abstract 
A theoretical model has been developed and 
used for the calculation of shallow acceptor 
states under a uniaxial stress which parallel to 
[001] direction in bulk silicon and germanium. 
We also consider the case of strained SiGe 
grown on (001) Si substrate. The theory is based 
on the Luttinger-Kohn 6-band model included 
the strain effect by using Bir-Pikus stress theory. 
By using the group theory, we can classify the 
wave function of acceptor state into different 
symmetries, and get its angular part belonging to 
different symmetries. It is not only beneficial for 
the analysis, but also reduces the loading of 
computation. The behaviors of acceptor states 
which change with the magnitude of stress have 
been investigated. In the unstrain case, our result 
is in good agreement with other calculations and 
some other experiments, while for strained SiGe 
case there has been no experiment data which 
have been shown before. Therefore our research 
would be valuable for the related researches in 
the future. 
二、 介紹  
有關研究動機請參照去年度報告。在我們
的研究當中，我們探討不同的晶體(Si 或
Ge)，不同的受子摻雜(B、Al 或 Ga )以及兩
種不同受應力的情形。(1. 直接外加一個[001]
方向的單軸應力(uniaxial stress)。 2. 在
(001)矽基板上長矽鍺合金的磊晶層。) 由於
篇幅的限制，我們僅針對 Ge(Ga)的系統下，
外加[001]方向的應力的情形討論。相關的研
究目前正處於論文寫作的階段，預計在明年度
的報告中會作一個完整的呈現。
