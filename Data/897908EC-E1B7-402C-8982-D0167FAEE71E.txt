 
中文摘要：本研究係探討在大氣環境下以不同的溫度與時間，將電漿輔助
化學氣相沉積法(PECVD)所沉積之非晶矽薄膜(厚度 100nm)轉變為多晶矽薄膜
過程中，不同厚度之鎳金屬薄膜(2~12nm)所造成的影響。根據掃描式電子顯微
鏡(SEM)、原子力顯微鏡(AFM)、拉曼光譜儀(RAMAN)與四點探針(FPP)所顯
示的實驗結果可知，增加金屬膜層厚度可有效阻絕氧氣，進而可於相同的退火
溫度、時間下獲得較大的晶粒，但就薄膜電性而言則具負面效應；另亦觀察以
不同的溫度(500、550℃)在長時間(20~60hr)大氣爐退火後之多晶矽薄膜的結晶
性狀(晶粒大小、結晶特性與薄膜電性)。根據實驗結果可知，延長退火時間並
不會使晶粒增大，但可進一步改善多晶矽薄膜之結晶性與電性。最後則透過與
鈍氣環境所得知結果比較後，成功歸納出不同鎳膜厚度在大氣中退火時的行為
模式。 
 
關鍵字：多晶矽、金屬誘發退火、再結晶 
 
英文摘要：This research was aimed to study the recrystallization behaviour 
involved during the production of poly-silicon thin films using metal-induced 
annealing technique. The required amorphous silicon thin films with the thickness 
of 100 nm were deposited onto the glass substrate using PECVD technique. In 
order to realize the nickel-induced annealing process, the nickel coatings of 4 - 
12nm – thick were subsequently sputter-deposited on the amorphous silicon. The 
characteristics of the obtained poly-silicon thin films such as grain size, 
crystallinity and electrical resistance were systematically investigated using SEM, 
Raman and Four-Point-Probe techniques. The results showed that the increasing of 
the annealing duration up to 20 hours had the effect in forming poly-Si grains with 
the average grain size higher up to a maximum of 320nm. As the annealing 
duration was set longer than 20 hours, the average grain size was shown not 
increased, however, the crystallinity and electrical resistance of poly-silicon thin 
films appeared to be improved. Based upon the obtained results of this study, the 
nickel-induced recrystallization of amorphous silicon thin films were recommended 
to be conducted at the temperature of 500℃or 550℃ with the annealing duration 
controlled at around 20hours. By comparing the results of annealing of amorphous 
Si films operated in air and in Ar, it is successful to build up the annealing behavior 
model involved for Ni-induced annealing of amorphous silicon thin films. 
 
Keywords: poly-Si, metal-induced annealing, recrystallization 
 
 
 
2. 實驗步驟 
 
首先，將委由工研院利用 PECVD 製作之 a-Si:H 均勻薄膜 (厚度為
100±5nm，玻璃基板採用 NEG OA10，450℃去氫烘烤 2hrs)，浸泡於丙酮液中
施以超音波震盪清潔 3 分鐘，DI Water 震盪 5 分鐘，並以氮氣吹乾後，藉由
RF Sputter 均勻濺鍍不同厚度(2~12nm)之鎳膜後，施以不同溫度(500、550℃)、
時間(5~10hrs)與氣氛控制(Air、Ar)使非晶矽薄膜透過 MIC 之機制轉變為多晶
矽薄膜。 
 
實驗中為了降低薄膜表面金屬(氧化物)殘留造成分析誤差，在量測拉曼光
譜與薄膜電性時先以王水(69% HNO3:36% HCl=1:3)腐蝕 30 秒，而利用 SEM 分
析粒徑時則再以新鮮之 Secco Etchant(49% HF:0.15M K2Cr2O7=2:1)腐蝕 20 秒。 
 
3. 結果與討論 
 
3.1 粒徑分析 
 
圖 1 與圖 2 分別為不同鎳膜厚度在經過 500℃或 550℃長時間大氣爐退火
後之 SEM 及 AFM 照片；圖 3 則為其平均粒徑分析。由於當鎳膜厚度為 2nm
時，Ar 環境下退火析出之晶粒均大於大氣環境下退火所得之晶粒(500  5hrs ℃
2nm air 時多晶矽粒徑約 80nm；500  5hrs 2nm Ar℃ 時多晶矽粒徑約 390nm)，
亦大於 550  10hrs 4nm air℃ 時之平均粒徑(~320nm)；而當厚度超過 4nm 且溫度
與時間分別為 550  10hrs℃ 時，晶粒大小均不再有明顯之不同(~320nm)。此現
象應為金屬鎳薄膜聚結成核點，故可於鈍氣環境（Ar）中獲得較大的晶粒，而
於大氣下，因氧氣的負面影響而使粒徑降低。此外，在大氣環境下增加金屬鎳
膜的厚度可於結晶析出的初期增大晶粒的粒徑(加工參數為 500℃ 10hr 時，若
鎳膜厚度由 2nm 變為 12nm，則平均粒徑可由~130nm 提升至~270nm)。 
 
3.2 拉曼光譜分析 
 
由圖 4 的結果顯示，保護氣體確可減少產生結晶所需時間(大氣環境下
500℃需 10 小時，保護氣體下 500℃僅需 5 小時)，且增加鎳膜厚度時，不論處
於何種退火環境下(Ar、air)均可獲得結晶性較為優良的多晶矽薄膜，這部分與
Soo 等人[10]的研究成果相似。 
 
3.3 片電阻分析 
 
由圖 5 可知，鎳膜厚度的增加會些微影響薄膜電性，故若需進一步提升薄
膜電性降低鎳金屬膜層厚度，使金屬的殘量減低，是一可行之方法。

6. 計畫成果自評 
 
為了有效控制變數(含環境氣氛因子)，我們多在退火過程中充以氮氣或氬
氣來降低大氣的影響。而 Lin 等人[8]於 2003 年針對氧原子提出了一個熱力學
的說明：由於氧化物的形成，雖然與鈍氣氣氛處於同一退火溫度仍能誘發再結
晶，但因為氧化鎳（NiO）須先還原為鎳原子，此一過程需要時間完成變態，
所以整個退火過程將會延遲約 4 小時。所以為了能有效掌握結晶的均勻度並進
一步降低各項製造成本，其中以金屬誘發再結晶製程結合大氣退火技術是一可
行且有效之方法。

Abstract. This research was aimed to study the recrystallization behaviour involved during the 
production of poly-silicon thin films using metal-induced annealing technique. The required 
amorphous silicon thin films with the thickness of 100 nm were deposited onto the glass substrate 
using PECVD technique. In order to realize the nickel-induced annealing process, the nickel coatings 
of 4 - 12nm – thick were subsequently sputter-deposited on the amorphous silicon. The characteristics 
of the obtained poly-silicon thin films such as grain size, crystallinity and electrical resistance were 
systematically investigated using SEM, Raman and Four-Point-Probe techniques. The results showed 
that the increasing of the annealing duration up to 20 hours was able to produce round poly-Si grains 
with the average grain size higher up to a maximum of 320nm. As the annealing duration was set 
longer than 20 hours, the average grain size was shown not increased, however, the crystallinity and 
electrical properties of poly-silicon thin films appeared to be improved. Based upon the obtained 
results of this study, the nickel-induced recrystallization of amorphous silicon thin films were 
recommended to be conducted at the temperature of 500℃or 550℃ with the annealing duration 
controlled at around 20hours.

3.2 Raman Analysis 
The results of Raman spectrum analysis of the a-Si films with sputtered nickel films of 4 nm-thick, crystallized at the 
temperature of 500℃/550℃ with the annealing time controlled ranging from 20 to 60 hours is shown in Figures 3. The 
results with three peaks at the phonon band centered at ~520cm-1 are clearly shown, indicating that poly-Si films had 
been successfully produced in this study by metal-induced-annealing processing. It also showed that the intensity of the 
peak due to the formation of poly-Si grains were increased by a factor of approximately 2 to 3, which proved that the 
crystallinity of poly-Si films could be improved by increasing the annealing duration during MIC process.  
 
3.3 Electrical resistance analysis 
It is shown in Figure 4 that a-Si films, which normally has the electrical resistance of about 9.3Ω/cm2, had been 
successfully transformed into poly-Si films with electrical resistance, being reduced, ranging from 2.5 to 4.25Ω/cm2 
after Ni-induced annealing using a heating duration around 20 hours at 500 and 550℃ respectively. The extension of 
annealing duration from 20 hours to 60 hours was shown to have effect on improving the electrical properties of poly-Si 
films. However the electrical resistance of poly-Si films is shown to be increased with the increasing of the Ni film 
thickness, which is probably due to the formation of NiO films while using a thicker Ni films. It needs an incubation 
time [8] for NiO to be reduced to Nickel which was necessary during the process of Ni-induced annealing of a-Si films. 
4. Conclusions 
The results of this research aimed to characterize poly-Si films produced using Ni-induced annealing technique can be 
summaried as follows： 
1. The Ni-induced recrystallization of a-Si films was shown able to be achieved resulting in round poly-Si grains with 
average grain size of about 320nm at the temperature of 500℃or 550℃ with the annealing duration controlled at 
around 20hours.     
2. The Raman analysis revealed that the crystallinity of poly-Si films could be improved by increasing the annealing 
duration involved during re-crystallization of a-Si films.   
3. The increasing of annealing duration from 20 hours to 60 hours was shown to have effect on improving the electrical 
properties of poly-Si films.. 
4. The increase of Ni film thickness resulted in increasing electrical resistance of poly-Si films, which is probably due 
to the formation of thicker NiO films needing an incubation time[8] for NiO to be reduced to Nickel.
