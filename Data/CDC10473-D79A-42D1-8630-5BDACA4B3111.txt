一 、 中英文摘要
雖然專案管理在企業的研發管理上扮演很重要的角色，其被廣泛應用於專案規劃與
管制的技術—PERT/CPM 卻自 1950 年代發展以來就沒有改進過，甚至於面對大部分的專
案是無法在預定時程、成本與規格的情形下也束手無策。Dr. Goldratt 根據其 TOC 之思維
程序所發展出來的「關鍵鏈專案管理（CCPM）」已經被證明其對於專案時間的規劃與管
控可以獲致很好的效果；然而，CCPM 在理論上與實務上尚有其有許多待釐清之處。
本多年期計畫於第一年提出建構穩健的新產品研發專案管理機制的必要條件與程
序，並利用模擬驗證其可行性。計畫第二年著重於 CCPM 理論面的議題詳加探討，主要
在規劃機制的建立，並專注於最佳化（或近似最佳）關鍵鏈排程演算方法之建立，以提
升資源規劃的效能，並提供更穩健、更短的專案時程。計畫第三年著重於探討 CCPM 企
業在應用專案管理實務面的問題，主要在設計一個能解決人性面因素並適用於全公司且
一致遵循的專案派工關鍵績效指標（KPI），以利管理機制的運作順暢，並且達到最終專
案績效的提升。
關鍵詞：限制理論、關鍵鏈專案管理、新產品開發、專案評核術/要徑法
Although project management plays an important role in modern organization’s new 
product development, the most widely used technique for managing project since its origin in
the 1950s, the well known as Program Evaluation and Review Technique/Critical Path Method
(PERT/CPM), has not been significantly modified and improved either in its application or its
assumptions. The majority of all development projects fail to meet their time and cost targets,
with the overrun typically between 40 and 200%. Thinking process and Theory of Constraints
(TOC) developed by Dr. Goldratt were used to develop Critical Chain Project Management
(CCPM). Application of CCPM in industries has been proven as successful. However, as a new
approach, Critical Chain method as its current stage of development is still not a perfect
solution to project management.
First year of the project, we proposed and exanimate the necessary conditions and
procedure in establish the robust CCPM system. The second year, we focused on developing
thetheoretical side of CCPM’s critical issues, such as optimized CC schedule, bufer sizing and 
buffer management. The third year, we devoted the effort to investigate the factors of human
side of implementation of CCPM and to design a Key Performance Indicator (KPI) for aligning
the human behavior to ensure the project successful.

TOC為以色列物理學家及企管大師Eliyahu M. Goldratt博士在1986年所創立的，其理
論的發展起源可以追溯到1970年代末期，Dr. Goldratt針對製造業產能問題所提出的最佳生
產技術（Optimized Production Technology, OPT）[McMullen, 1998]。TOC認為任何一個組
織或系統皆有其限制的存在，而所謂的「限制」係指阻礙任何組織或系統達到其最高目
標的事物。TOC是透過一種直覺性的架構（Intuitive Framework）對於整個組織或系統進
行一連串的持續改善程序。同時認為組織、系統的績效強弱應該是決定於其中最弱的一
環，而不是最強的一環。若能針對系統最弱的一環（核心、關鍵問題）加以處理，則系
統的主要問題就能迎刃而解，反之若未能針對核心問題加以處理，則系統整體的績效將
很難獲得改善[Goldratt, 1994]。TOC為了確保管理措施能針對系統之限制進行重點管理，
以協助組織進行連續性的改善與管理的變革，發展出一套獨特的思考程序（Thinking
Process, TP）以及系統化的方法做為解決之道，並以此為發展各項企業經營解決方案之基
礎輔助工具[McMullen, 1998]。
由於組織無法避免因人而產生的變動，且還隱含著一種對績效、限制作連續改善的
程序而產生的現在與未來的變動，而為了保證連續改善程序能測底執行，首先必須解決
人在處理變動上的問題，因此TOC創立具心理學特性的管理方法，從人性的角度切入進
行管理。透過TOC的思考程序，讓人員知道什麼需要改變？要改變成什麼？如何改變？

驟[Goldratt, 1994]為：（1）確認系統中的限制所在（Identify）；(2)如何利用限制改善系統
績效(Exploit)；(3)根據第二項之決策，擬定其他資源全力配合（Subordinate）；(4)提升系

三、第一年計畫之研究方法及結論—建立並驗證 CCPM的基本程序
3.1 CCPM方法之建構
關鍵鏈專案管理是應用限制理論的管理邏輯於專案管理上的概念，為專案管理開創
了新的方法。以有限的資源與消除不良的工作行為的概念進行專案時程的規劃，並利用
集中管理專案緩衝時間的觀念保護整個專案的執行，以期能順利或提早專案的達成。
關鍵鏈專案管理有關鍵鏈排程、同步化機制(Synchronization)、緩衝管理 (Buffer
Management, BM) 等三大機制，此三大機制的作用主要是改變專案成員的不良工作行
為，效法接力賽跑者（Relay Runner）的精神一棒接著一棒的傳下去，使得專案在時程管
控中能夠順利完成。仿效接力賽跑者的精神應用在專案工作上就是：工作可以開始時盡
早開始（接到棒子要馬上跑）；工作不要間斷，一直做到完成（全力跑完全程）；當任務
的標準完全符合實力及宣告完成（跑完立即交棒）。本研究將闡述關鍵鏈三大機制的詳細
作法來建構穩健的專案管理方法。
3.1.1 關鍵鏈專案排程之建構程序
所謂關鍵鏈就是考慮任務間可能存在的任何相依關係（資源相依或任務相依），所形
成之最長路徑。關鍵鏈是專案規劃與控管上之最重要路徑。傳統專案管理著重在要徑
(Critical Path)，並未考慮到影響專案的資源衝突。關鍵鏈排程的基本原則如下：
(1) 解決資源衝突；
(2) 非必要時不要任意開始一項作業；
(3) 提供明確的優先順序；
 任何管控以關鍵鏈為主；
 其他作業依「先進先服務」原則進行，除非獲得緩衝管理機制的指示；
(4) 把焦點從個別任務放到專案整體上；
(5) 保護該保護的地方。
關鍵鏈排程的施行步驟如下：
步驟一：確認關鍵鏈；
(1) 以 50-50 法則縮短任務的時間；
(2) 確認專案的網路；
(3) ALAP(愈晚開工越好)；
(4) 化解資源衝突；
(5) 找出相依事件中最長的鏈－關鍵鏈。
步驟二：保護關鍵鏈－以整體的專案緩衝 (Project Buffer, PB) 來保護專案的交期；
步驟三：所有非 Critical Chain 任務、路徑及資源皆全力支援步驟二所做的決策；
(1) 加入匯流緩衝 (Feeding Buffer, FB) 來保護關鍵鏈；
(2) 化解加入 FB 後的資源衝突。
步驟四：以增加資源的方式（在某一時間內），化解衝突任務，縮短專案時間；
步驟五：回到步驟一，不要讓惰性成為限制。

成功的專案有固定的組織規則、執行程序和相關的環境要素限制，但管理的技術亦
是佔有相當大影響的一個成功要素，因在複雜度、專案大小、問題的判定以及持續性不
同時其所需的技術也不同[Pinto & Covin, 1989]。也因此本研究將限定在複雜度，來模擬
專案對完成時間的影響。在代表不同屬性的專案型態中，複雜度代表著專案網路的結構，
包含作業數目及任務間的順序與關聯。Anthony A. Mastor 曾就專案排程上的最佳化進行
模擬[Mastor, 1970]，並對專案排程網路的結構，依據不同之複雜度進行分類，複雜度S之

實際專案計劃的情況。在設計專案網路作業間順序的複雜度為S=0.10、S=0.15、S=0.20三

5.2 實驗假設
5.2.1 模擬基本假設
本研究多專案情境模擬之基本假設如下：
(1). 因考慮結合公司實際之專案所需花費成本及時間過長，且專案的重覆性低，因此專
案個數、時程、資源係以現有的多專案模型作為本研究之模擬模型。
(2). 模擬情境為 CCPM 在多專案環境下，各專案之排程皆以 CCPM 方法排定，且任務之
網路順序、交期與其關聯性及資源指派皆為已知，且前續任務未完成前，下一項任
務即無法開始作業。亦不考慮臨時插單等人為因素。
(3). 本研究主要的績效指標是以專案完工時程作為衡量基準，因此各專案皆不考慮專案
成本的問題。
(4). 為讓資源產生嚴重衝突，因此本研究之 CCPM 排程不考慮產能緩衝的排定，且所有
專案任務時程估計皆以高風險狀態作為設定基礎。
(5). 一般專案的時程估計皆採用右偏的β分配，因此，本研究之專案時程分配亦採用傳
統 PERT 技術的β分配。
(6). 為了要讓專案具代表性，因此本研究專案資源設定為有限資源，且資源為各專案所
共用。
(7). 每項任務所需工期以「天」為單位，由定義的分配範圍亂數產生。在模擬資源衝突
決策法則時，以每五天作為檢查單位，產生衝突時則配合本研究所提出的法則作為
衝突之依據。

遲比例。

H1：CC 被保護比例 ≠ 專案延遲比例 ；若 Reject H0 ， 則 FCFS ≠ CC被保護
比例 ≠專案延遲比例。

H1：FCFS ≠ 專案延遲比例 ；若 Reject H0 ， 則 FCFS ≠ CC被保護比例 ≠專

Abstract
Traditionally, answer the questions of “when to replenish”and “how many to replenish”using varies
forecasting methods are commonly seem in the supply chain inventory management. However, the
uncertainty with changeful demand often makes various kinds of prediction methods became less efficient.
To solve the problem, the demand-pull and buffer management of TOC replenishment method developed
by Dr. Goldratt have been applied as solution in this research. Using a leading company of TFT-LCD
industry in Taiwan to be the case to evaluate the feasibility and validity of the solution, the result shows that
TOC solution provides a better performance in TDD and IDD than traditional methods.

1 INTRODUCTION
For a long time, the way that supply chain operates begins
by placing the forecasting orders via retailers. The orders
are then collected by the regional or distribution center and
sent to the manufacturer for production. After manufactur-
ing, the finished products are delivered back to the
regional or distribution center and then re-distributed again
to the retailers. This operation model is called “Push”
method. Since forecasting is usually inaccurate and has
other uncertainties, such as lot-size orders, cost fluctua-
tion, and long production/replenishment time, the variation
in demand becomes more significant as it moves towards
the supply end. This is the so-called Bullwhip Effect
[9][10], which leads to high inventory in regional ware-
house yet lacks the varieties demanded by the customer.
Most of the traditional replenishment policies make use of
replenishment to initiate the system, which maintains the
inventory level to meet customer’s satisfaction. Basically,
the system conducts appropriate replenishment at the right
time, such as the development of EOQ at the early stage
to (s, Q) replenishment policy, (s, S) replenishment policy,
(R, S) replenishment policy, and (R, s, S) replenishment
policy. The parameter settings for these replenishment
policies are influenced by demands. In the past, many
studies have reported ways to find the optimal parameter
setting, such as mathematical method, heuristic or simula-
tion method, etc [1][15][16][18]. However, these methods
only emphasize on “partial”inventory problem without
taking the real-time information into consideration. Fur-
thermore, these processes are too complicated; as the
result, applying these models in inventory management is
not appropriate under such dynamic environment.
The supplier adopting Continuous Replenishment Program
(CRP) must consider first the retailer’s order. After taking
into account other relevant factors, the order is adjusted
and ready for distribution. In this case, the process only
activates when the retailers have placed an order. On the
other hand, the suppliers adopting Vendor Management
Inventory (VMI) judge retailer’s demand from their
previous-established information and subsequently per-
form replenishment adjustment to improve the perfor-
mance of inventory management. Both CRP and VMI
must be built on top of adequate information and mutual
trust between members of the supply chain. Moreover, the
maintenance of the system is more complicated
[2][3][4][7][13][14][19]. Although implementing modern
information technology will speed up information transfer
and facilitate supply chain integration, the problems within
the supply chain cannot be efficiently resolved [11][12][14].
Most people believe that the problems occurred in supply
chain are caused mainly by inaccurate forecasting,
unreliable vendors, and long replenishment time, which
are difficult to overcome. Although solving these problems
seemed to be difficult from the perspective of traditional
thinking, they are indeed much simpler to deal with if they
are considered from a different point of view [6][17]. From
the calculation of statistical variation, individual calculation
variation is always far greater than overall calculation
variation; therefore, as the forecasting step moves closer
to the demand aggregation, the accuracy of forecasting
will also increase comparing to those performed and
collected for each individual retailer [5]. Dr. Goldratt
believed that the main inventory should be located at
places where the forecasting is most accurate, that is at
the aggregation source. He also believed that the ways of
operation should be changed from the original “push”
method to the “pull”method that takes customer demand
as the top priority. The amount consumed in the down-
stream is pull back from the upstream. The inventory is
moved from places near the customer to the source. This
not only is advantageous for making distribution that
satisfies downstream demands but also separate the
supply systems for the independent demands that are
unstable. As the result, the only time that remains is the
delivery time, which significantly reduces the replenish-
ment time and enhances the replenishment reliability
[8][20].
In this study, empirical analyses were conducted for the
Cell assembly of a leading Thin-Film Transistor Liquid
Crystal Display (TFT-LCD) manufacturing company in
Taiwan. Utilizing the concept of Demand-Pull (DP) and
Buffer Management (BM) derived from TOC, Demand-Pull
Replenishment Model (DPRM) was proposed to establish
the inventory that is efficient and adequate to meet
customer’s demand; consequently, reduces the cost of
inventory and achieves the ultimate goal of maximizing the

inventory buffers should be set in the factory upstream
(source), whereas the regional warehouse downstream
only needs to maintain the quantity needed for replenish

model of the upstream cell assembly will have a direct

The cell assembly process in TFT-LCD can be further

CutPolarizerGap formerCell test. Due to the
