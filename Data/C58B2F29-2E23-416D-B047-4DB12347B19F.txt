 
中 文 摘 要 ： 近年來，有鑑於狀態相關之 Riccati 方程式(state-
dependent Riccati equation，SDRE)技術所具有的諸多優
點，已有許多學者專家積極的投入研究應用此技術於非線性
系統控制之相關議題。SDRE 技術之優點包括有 i) 觀念簡單
及易於掌握，它直接在每一非零狀態套用線性 LQR 設計技
術； ii) 透過調整性能指標(performance index)中狀態及
控制之權重，此設計方法可事先預知性能表現之趨勢。例
如，工程師可藉由調高性能指標中狀態之權重來加快反應速
度，但相對的必須付出較大的控制成本；iii) 此 SDRE 技術
擁有另一項額外的設計自由度可用來提昇控制的性能表現，
此自由度來自於分解非線性漂流項(nonlinear drift term)
之狀態相關係數矩陣(state-dependent coefficient 
matrix)的不唯一性； iv) 此設計法雖然在每一非零狀態採
用線性 LQR 技術，但它並沒有拋棄任何非線性項，也就是說
此設計仍然考慮全部動態而非如線性化技術只是針對部分動
態進行設計。此外，目前文獻上已有許多成功利用 SDRE 設計
技術之實際且有意義之報告不斷的被提出，這些研究結果普
遍都具有極佳的性能表現，但都缺乏有效的理論推導及證明
來支撐其有效性。因此本計畫針對 SDRE 設計技術進行其有效
性之理論分析與全域穩定之探討。

狀態相關之係數矩陣、全域穩定。

The study of nonlinear system control by 
the state-dependent Riccati equation (SDRE) 
technique has recently attracted 
considerable attention, because of the many 
benefits of the approach. Those benefits 
include i) the concept is intuitive and 
simple, which directly adopts the linear LQR 
design at every nonzero state, ii) the design 
can directly address system performance 
through the specification of the performance 
index by adjusting the state and the control 
weightings with predictable results, for 
instance, the engineer may tune up the 
weightings on system state to speed up the 
response at the expense of more control 
effort, iii) the scheme possesses an extra 
design degree of freedom arose from the 
non-uniqueness of the state-dependent 
coefficient (SDC) matrix representation of 
the nonlinear drift term, which can be 
utilized to enhance controller performance, 
iv) the approach preserve the essential 
system nonlinearities, since it does not 
truncate any system's nonlinear term. 
Besides, many practical and meaningful 
applications which are successfully 
performed by the SDRE design have been 
reported. Among these reports, however, few 
are addressing the global stability and 
optimality of the SDRE approach from the 
theoretical viewpoint. In this project, we 
will investigate the global stability and 
optimality issues of the SDRE design for 
nonlinear system to support the validity of 
the approach from the analytical viewpoint.  
Keywords: Nonlinear system control, 
state-dependent Riccati equation, LQR design, 
state-dependent coefficient matrix, global 
stability.

二、研究目的 
誠如申請人在本期末報告之“背景說明及計
劃的重要性”中提到目前雖然已有許多非線性系
統控制的研究成果，但這些研究結果大都不是從以
設計目標為導向著眼。因此，應用工程師期待能有
一套同時照顧到理論與實務，能兼顧穩定性、穩健
性、性能表現、實際計算之方便性及易於分析與實
現的整合工具。而 SDRE 方法正好具有能有效改
善這些缺點的潛力。事實上 SDRE 方法還具有如
下之優點： i) 由於直接在每個非零狀態沿用線性
之最佳化 LQR設計程序，因此具有設計概念直覺
簡單且易於實現的特點； ii) 相較於非線性最佳化
必須求解 Hamilton-Jacobi 方程式的計算困難度，
SDRE 只需求解對應的代數 Riccati 方程式的解，
而且目前已有相當完備及有效率的計算軟體來執
行此一計算工作； iii) 可透過調整系統狀態和控
制力道的權重矩陣直接調整系統的性能表現以符
合實際控制的需求，例如工程師可以藉由調高系統
狀態的權重來加速系統狀態的反應，但相對的必須
付出消耗較多控制成本的代價； iv) 由於非線性
漂流項拆解方式的不唯一性，工程師可利用此額外
的自由度來提升系統的性能表現； v) 由於整個設
計過程中並未刪除任何非線性項，因而 SDRE 設
計方法能夠保留系統的非線性特性。此外，目前文
獻上已有越來越多成功利用 SDRE 設計技術之報
告不斷的被提出。基於上述優點，SDRE設計法則
的潛力及未來發展是相當值得期待的。 
雖然 SDRE 設計法具有上述許多優點及潛
力，且目前已有相當多效果很好的實際應用實
例，但這些例子多半都沒有理論基礎來保證應有
的性能表現，例如，全域穩定性，性能優越性（最
佳化），甚至連 SDRE 策略是否能被持續執行的
數學符號（symbolic）檢驗條件（即在特定狀態
下，狀態相關之係數矩陣的可控性與可觀測性）
目前都還需要進一步的研究。因此，SDRE設計
法的理論研究與相關之應用開發將會是現階段
國內外學者專家積極投入的重要項目，而這也是
本計畫之主要研究目的。此外我們也經由此計畫
的執行能為國家培養相關之電機控制研究人
才，並藉由研究訓練、技術累積和研發成果能對
國內學術的研究發展與工業的實務應用能有所
助益。

項具有無限多種拆解方式，這些拆解方式可以提供
設計上的自由度以提升系統之性能表現；在最佳控
制律所必須滿足的 costate 方程式方面，論文中也
證明了 SDRE 控制策略是以二次的收斂速率
（ quadratic convergence rate ） 來 漸 近 滿 足
（asymptotically satisfied）此對應的 costate 方程
式；論文還證明了在閉迴路系統之 SDC 表示式
Ac(x)是對稱矩陣的情況下，SDRE控制策略可達
到全域漸近穩定之性能表現；最後，作者也以四個
例子來驗證所提出的理論結果，並特別強調如何調
整 SDRE 控制策略之權重矩陣以避免控制輸入及
系統狀態達到飽和（saturation）。論文[21] 發表
在國際控制聯合大會(IFAC)，此篇論文考慮常見的
非線性仿射系統，利用Lyapunov直接分析法（direct 
method）以及選取適當形式的 Lyapunov函數及權
重矩陣提出了 SDRE 控制策略能夠使系統全域漸
近穩定的四個條件，並提出一個能夠在線（on-line）
有效率計算的演算法則。論文[27]探討非線性系統
之漂流項利用 SDRE 策略拆解的局部線性可控性
（factored controllability）與系統真實可控性（true 
controllability）之比較與關連性，並提出這兩種可
控性局部等價（local equivalent）與全域等價（global 
equivalent）的條件。最後並以一個五階之非線性
dual-spin載具系統來做說明。論文[29]將 SDRE控
制策略應用在R. T. Bupp [45]所提出之基準點問題
（benchmark problem）上，在與利用數值分析方法
所得到的開迴路最佳控制律比較後，發現如果不考
慮雜訊和誤差的話，SDRE控制策略的性能表現非
常接近開迴路最佳控制律的性能表現，另一方面，
透過模擬發現 SDRE 控制策略對於參數變動
（ parametric variations）和漸弱的諧波干擾
（attenuate sinusoidal disturbance）也具有一定程度
的穩健性。除此之外，這篇論文也展示了 SDRE
控制策略能夠有效的防止控制輸入達到飽和的極
限。論文[30]發表在美國控制研討會，論文提出給
定任意的非線性平滑狀態回授控制律k(x)，存在
適當的 SDRE 控制策略使其所形成的控制律也是
k(x)（也就是 recoverability of k(x)）的充分且必
要條件。論文[34]發表於 IEEE Transactions on 
Automatic Control，分析非線性漂流項拆解方式的
多樣性，並利用 S-procedure 的方法證明如果考慮
的非線性系統存在有穩定控制律且具有所謂
star-convex level sets形式的 Lyapunov函數，那麼
就一定存在有非線性漂流項的拆解方式使原點在
相對應的 SDRE設計流程下漸近穩定。 
由於利用 Hamilton-Jacobi 方程式處理的非線
性最佳化控制策略無可避免的會在計算求解上遭
遇重大的困難，1996 年兩位加州理工學院的學者

處理方法）最佳控制策略論文[37]。這篇論文提到
SDRE 策略在計算上的優勢以及漂流項拆解的多

健特性來設計基於 T-S 模糊模型的順滑模態控制
律。T-S模糊模型其原理是利用多個近似的線性化
模型透過權重來近似原始的非線性模型，由於 T-S
模糊模型的許多系統參數都可以以離線(off-line)
的計算方式來獲得，在線實現控制律時則可以由查
表的方式來快速獲得所需計算的資訊，因此以 T-S
模糊模型來近似原始非線性動態將可大幅減少實
現控制律所需的計算時間。所獲得的結果我們已發
表在相關的期刊[2]中。我們不但證明所提出的方
法可以有效的減輕在線計算的負擔，同時也保留了
順滑模態控制所特有的穩健特性，所獲得的理論結
果也已應用在機器手臂的模擬中來驗證其有效
性。接著我們將此 T-S模糊模型與變結構控制結合
的技術用來進行可靠度控制設計，所獲得的理論分
析結果與數值模擬均顯示閉迴路系統不但可以容
忍部分致動器發生故障，同時可以有效的縮短在線
的計算負擔，達到穩健穩定的目的，也就是說此可
靠度設計能夠同時保留 T-S 模糊模式及變結構控
制技術的優點。相關的研究結果已發表在 IEEE 
Transactions on Industrial Electronics中[1]。

the SDRE solvability condition is violated, which may

resentation that makes the SDRE (3) solvable and the
resulting ACL(x) matrix a Hurwitz matrix.

Nonlinear Reliable Control with Application to a
Vehicle Anti-Lock Brake System
Yew-Wen Liang, Member, IEEE, Chih-Chiang Chen, Der-Cherng Liaw, Senior Member, IEEE, and Yuan-Tin Wei
Abstract—This paper explores the design of active reliable
control systems for a class of uncertain nonlinear affine systems
using an integral-type sliding mode control (ISMC) scheme. The
presented scheme not only maintains the main advantages of
the ISMC design, including robustness, rapid response and easy
implementation, but it can also tolerate some actuator faults
when fault detection and diagnosis information is available. In
this study, the uncertainties and/or disturbances are not required
to be of the matched type; however, when they are matched,
the state trajectories of the nominal healthy subsystem and the
uncertain faulty system are identical. As a result, engineers
can predictively address the matched uncertain faulty system
performance in light of the performance of the nominal healthy
subsystem. The analytic results are also applied to the study
of a vehicle brake reliable control system. Simulation results
demonstrate the benefits of the proposed scheme.
Index Terms—Reliable control, robustness, integral-type slid-
ing mode control, anti-lock brake system, state-dependent Riccati
equation
I. INTRODUCTION
The study of reliable (or fault-tolerant) control has recently
attracted considerable attention and become of great impor-
tance [1]-[16] because it is often not possible to provide
repair and maintenance promptly, particularly for the systems
in aerospace missions. The main goal of reliable control is
to design a controller whose closed-loop system can tolerate
the abnormal operation of specific control components while
retaining overall system stability with acceptable system per-
formance. Existing reliable design approaches are based on a
variety of methods, including coprime factorization [8], the
algebraic Riccati equation [9], the linear matrix inequality
[10], the Hamilton-Jacobi equation (HJE) [11], [12], sliding
mode control (SMC) [13]-[15] and integral-type sliding mode
control (ISMC) [16]. Among these reliable control methods,
only the HJE, SMC and ISMC approaches deal with the
reliability issues for nonlinear systems. However, because
the HJE-based approach is implemented using optimization,
reliable controllers using this approach depend on the solution
of an associated HJE that is difficult to solve. Although a
power series method [17] can reduce the difficulty by using

reliable designs based on SMC or ISMC do not require
the solution of an HJE, and they retain the advantages of
SMC schemes. These advantages include rapid response, easy
implementation, and low sensitivity to model uncertainties
and/or external disturbances (MUED) [18].
In addition to having the benefits of the SMC-based schemes
mentioned above, the ISMC-based schemes also possess four
advantages. First, it has been reported that SMC schemes can
be sensitive to MUED during the period when the system
state has not yet reached the sliding manifold [19]. Unlike
SMC schemes, the system states of ISMC schemes start
in the sliding manifold and remain there, which improves
robustness. Second, the maximum control magnitude required
by ISMC is generally smaller than that by SMC, in which it
usually occurs at the beginning of the period before the sliding
manifold is reached. Thus, the physical control magnitude
constraints imposed by ISMC designs are easier to fulfill
than those by SMC designs. Third, the effect of unmatched
MUED can be minimized through the selection of sliding
manifold parameters [19], [20]. Finally, the state response of
the matched uncertain system is identical to that of the nominal
system if the system state remains on the sliding manifold. The
last property gives engineers greater flexibility in designing
an appropriate controller for the nominal system by creating a
desired system trajectory for the state of the uncertain system
to follow. Thus, the ISMC design allows engineers to predict
the performance of the uncertain system, which is in general
not easily implemented by other nonlinear control techniques.
In light of the many benefits of ISMC schemes, this study
will investigate reliable controller design using the ISMC
approach. With this approach, engineers can achieve better
system performance not only under normal condition but also
under a variety of uncertain fault situations. In [16], a reliable
control design from the ISMC viewpoint has been exploited.
However, it only dealt with a special structure of nonlinear
systems. In this paper, we will extend that previous study to
those for more general dynamical systems.

states the problem and the main goal of this paper. Section III
describes the design of the ISMC reliable control law. Section
IV discusses the application of the analytic results to the anti-
lock brake control of a vehicle. Finally, Section V provides
the conclusion.

that the range spaces spanned by B and GH(x) are identical,
then the functions hH(x) and DH(x) given in Eq. (21) can be

follows from the discussions that the ISMC reliable design
scheme allows engineers to predictively address the system
performance based on the performance of the nominal healthy
subsystem. The engineers may use any kind of optimal control
action they prefer for the nominal healthy subsystem and also
achieve good system performance in uncertain fault situations.

Abstract—This paper studies the design of a robust guidance
law for missiles in the presence of uncertainties, which may
include inaccurate prediction of target maneuver. Under the
design, the closed-loop guidance system is shown to be robust
to the uncertainties. Additionally, the scheme presented possesses
the flexibility of allowing the engineer to select a desired guidance
law for the nominal system (i.e., system without uncertainty) so
that the state response of the overall uncertain guidance system is
close to that of the selected nominal closed-loop guidance system.
The simulation results demonstrate the benefits of the presented
scheme.
I. INTRODUCTION
Due to the growing demand to minimize the threat of
missile attacks, the investigation of guidance laws for anti-
missile systems has attracted considerable attention [1]-[6]. A
main objective of missile guidance design is to intercept the
target with small miss distance while optimizing the energy
consumption to enlarge the interception range. Among the
existing guidance studies, the proportional navigation (PN)
law and its generalization (e.g., ideal PN [1] and realistic
true PN [2]) have been broadly used in the homing phase
of flight for most missile systems with non-maneuvering or
low-maneuvering target due to the advantages of simplicity
and easy implementation. Specifically, the PN law has been
shown to be an optimal design in minimizing miss distance
for the non-maneuvering target [3]. However, the performance
of PN and its generalization will become worse and lack of
robustness as the target’s maneuverability increases [4]. To
overcome such a disadvantage, various control techniques have
been recently employed to construct suitable guidance laws
for the missile interception such as the sliding mode control
(SMC) based design [5]-[6], the optimal control based scheme
[3] and the state dependent Riccati equation based guidance
laws [7].
Among the existing guidance designs, the SMC approach
is known to have the advantages of rapid response, easy
implementation and robustness to model uncertainties and/or
external disturbances [5], [6]. In fact, the SMC scheme has
been widely applied to control a variety of systems [8], [9]
because of the many benefits stated above. In contrast to
the SMC scheme, the Integral Sliding Mode Control (ISMC)
design was recently shown to be very effective and powerful
from a theoretical point of view [10]-[11]. ISMC not only
maintains the benefits of SMC as stated above, but also
exhibits the following three additional characteristics [10]: 1)
the ISMC scheme usually requires smaller maximum control
magnitude than the SMC scheme, because the SMC scheme
generally requires its maximum control magnitude at the onset
before the system state reaches the sliding manifold, while the
state of the ISMC scheme remains on the sliding manifold
from the beginning. Therefore, the physical constraints on
actuator outputs for ISMC are easier fulfilled than those
for SMC. As a result, the ISMC improves the robustness
performance of the SMC; 2) the ISMC scheme will not
amplify the effect of the unmatched uncertainty provided that
its sliding manifold parameters are appropriately selected; and
3) the state responses of the nominal system and the matched-
type uncertain system under the ISMC scheme are identical
if the uncertain system states keep staying on its sliding
manifold. The last two properties are useful since they give
engineers an additional degree of freedom on the design of
a suitable nominal system controller and creating a desired
system trajectory for the trajectory of the uncertain system
to follow. Thus, the ISMC design provides the engineer the
opportunity to predict the the uncertain system performance,
which is, in general, not easy to be fulfilled by other nonlinear
control schemes.
On the other hand, it was reported that the state-dependent
Riccati equation (SDRE) design, which is known adopted
linear quadratic regulator scheme at every nonzero state, is
very efficient in many practical applications [12]. The SDRE
scheme is known to include the following benefits [12]: First,
the concept is intuitive and simple, and directly adopts the
LQR design at every nonzero state; Second, the design can
directly affect system performance with predictable results by
adjusting the state and the control weightings to specify the
performance index (for instance, the engineer may modulate
the weighting of the system state to speed up the response,
although at the expense of increased control effort); Third,
the scheme possesses an extra design degree of freedom
arising from the non-unique state-dependent coefficient (SDC)
matrix representation of the nonlinear drift term, which can
be utilized to enhance controller performance; and finally the
approach preserves the essential system nonlinearities because
it does not truncate any nonlinear terms. Many practical and
meaningful applications successfully performed by the SDRE
design have been reported. Due to the many advantages of

V. CONCLUSION
A robust guidance law for a missile undergoing inaccurate
prediction of target maneuver was presented using a combi-
nation of the SDRE and ISMC schemes. It was shown that
the presented guidance law can effectively compensate for
the effects of uncertainty. Additionally, the design possesses
a remarkable feature that allows the engineer to select in
advance an appropriate guidance law for the nominal system
according to the interception requirements, such that the state
responses of the overall uncertain system is close to that of the
nominal system. Simulation results have clearly demonstrated
the benefits of the design.
