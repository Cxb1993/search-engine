Abstract 
In this project, the tunable fiber couplers based on the conventional fiber couplers immersed 
in the thermo-optic materials are proposed. The tunable devices can not only increase the 
freedom of the optical fiber communication system design and optical network management, but 
also surport the development of the novel device or module. In this project, we use the 
conventional fiber couplers fabricated by the fusing method to be immersed in the 
index-matching oil. Finally, the modulation of the optical coupling efficiency will be performed 
by changing the temperature of the index-matching oil. This tunable fiber coupler is fabricated by 
applying the conventional fabrication of the commercial fiber couplers in stead of the difficult 
fabrication of the side-polish.  The tunable fiber coupler can be also packaged by using the 
mature package technique of the fiber couplers in order to improve its reliability. In this project, 
our major work include: (1) to study the performance of the tunable fiber coupler fabricated with 
various fusing and twist parameters and different index-matching oils, (2) to respectively find the 
optimal fabrication parameters of a fiber coupler with the tunable coupling ratio and a WDM 
coupler with the tunable channel spacing, and (3) to improve the package process and the 
temperature control technique of the tunable fiber couplers. 
Keyword : Thermo-optic material, Tunable fiber coupler, Fusing method, Index-matching 
oil.

Conclusions 
We have theoretically and experimentally demonstrated 
a new design of the photosensitive-cladding fiber that is 
suitable to fabricate slanted FBGs for gain-flattening 
applications. The slanted FBGs with low backreflection 
(-30dB) and narrow-band symmetric spectral property 
(FWHM = 7.5nm) are presented. The new designed fiber 
make slanted FBGs the most promising filter to be used 
for DWDM systems.
