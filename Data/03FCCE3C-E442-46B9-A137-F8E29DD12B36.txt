摘要 
將廚房融入居家生活空間，是新的生活空間體驗與概念。由於地球環境變遷所帶來的衝擊與影響，
如何能達到高效率、節能、與兼顧綠色概念等需求，成為重要的設計考量。LED 光能兼具上述之優
勢，具有市場發展的潛力與龐大的未開發市場，更可以具體實踐未來廚房設計趨勢中的綠能觀念(節能)、
情境營造(氛圍)和功能性(光照)等目的，進而達到未來廚房不再只是廚房的趨勢，也讓 LED 光照技術
亦不再只達成 LED 光照的目的，取而代之的是一個整合性的觀念，本計畫成果除能提供合作廠商未
來於廚具規劃設計上的創新構想，並發展出廚房相關 LED 光照周邊創意商品設計模式，達成廚房規
劃設計的整體目標。 
本計畫首先透過產業趨勢報告資料，針對廚房與 LED 照明產業的趨勢進行調查，並結合專家之
意見形成創意趨勢的主軸，再將主軸轉化成為刺激原，藉由工作營過程中的創意激發，將刺激源導入
廚房 LED 光照周邊創意商品，最後透過設計方法與概念具體化的流程，以獲得創新具體商品的成果。
最終的成果，再經由專家於過程中適時討論與給予修正，將使開發商品更趨於兼具成熟性、實用性及
價值性等內涵，並期望經由產學之合作，歸納並建構出廚房綠能照明之創新產品設計概念。最終完成
之創新燈具有以下三種：微型警示滅火式燈具、造型噴墨投影式燈具和拼接式組合式燈具等三種。

Abstract 
Incorporating the kitchen function into a whole living space is a new experience and concept of 
domestic interior design. With the greenhouse impact of the Earth's environment, how to achieve high 
efficiency, energy-saving, and taking into account the green concept in the living space has become an 
important design consideration. The technology of LED lighting equips above advantages, and also creates a 
huge and potential market. The concept meets the practice of the future kitchen design and trends to green 
energy, atmosphere creation and lighting function purposes. Therefore, the kitchen is no longer just the 
original function of kitchen, and the LED light will no longer be the traditional way of lighting in the kitchen. 
It is expected to achieve a total concept of kitchen design goal, and this project results not only provide 
innovative kitchenware design ideas, and also develop a model of creative peripheral product design to the 
corporate. 
In this practical design study, trend reports will firstly be reviewed and analyzed to investigated the 
kitchen design and LED lighting industry. Delphi technique is then conducted to explore the product 
opportunities gaps (POGs) by experts. Design Workshop methods will be employed to conceptualize the 
creative design ideas and then concluded those ideas by way of Focus Group method. Function-analysis and 
Form-construction are used to concretization and development of innovative design concepts to the practice. 
It is anticipated that the results of the study will help the Corporation, LED lighting application and kitchen 
industry to make profits and enhance the competitiveness.
