architectures, structurally well-defined BCPs are able to self-
organize into ordered nanopatterns, which have found diverse 
nanotechnological applications. To date structurally well-defined 
polyolefin-based diblock copolymers can betypically prepared by 
sequential polymerization of different monomers via living or 
controlled polymerization routes. Unfortunately, living or controlled 
polymerization methods typically fail to facilitate effective 
stereoregularity control during polymerization of α-olefins； thus, 
stereoregular BCPs, which has been demonstrated to self-organize 
into unusual nanomorphologies due to the stereo-interaction 
between stereoregular entities, are unable to prepare directly by the 
commonly used living or controlled polymerization technologies.  
The difficulty confronted in the syntheses of stereoregular diblock 
copolymers has been have been circumvented recently through 
postpolymerization of stereoregular end-functionalized 
prepolymers. Nevertheless, syntheses of stereoregular BCPs via the 
postpolymerization of stereoregular end-functionalized prepolymer 
route still suffer the following problems. First, stereoregular end-
functionalized prepolymers can only be generated by employing 
stereoregular catalysts to mediate the simultaneous selective chain 
transfer reaction during stereospecific polymerization of α-
olefins； thus, structures of stereoregular BCPs have been 
drastically limited by the difficulty in the syntheses of stereoregular 
end-functionalized prepolymers. Second, the linking efficiency for 
connecting the stereoregular end-functionalized prepolymer with the 
second block is highly dependent on the reactivity of the terminal 
functional group； thereby, it is highly desirable for developing a 
new chain transfer technology that offer the successful preparation 
of stereoregular end-functionalized polymer containing a highly 
reactive terminal group. 
  This project not only offers the new synthetic route for 
preparations of stereoregular diblock copolymers but also provides 
the structurally well-defined stereoregular diblock copolymers for 
self-assembly studies.

一、前言 
    嵌段共聚物1為具備不同化學組成之嵌段結構並在不同嵌段間以化學鍵相接
連，也因為嵌段共聚物之特殊化學組成，結構均一(e.g., 具備均一之分子量、分
子量分怖及立體規則性)之嵌段共聚物可在熔融態及溶解態行分子間之奈米結構
自我組裝，以形成有序之奈米微結構(Ordered Nanomorphologies)。2對於非結晶
性之嵌段共聚物而言，有序之奈米微結構組裝，主要肇因於不同化學組成嵌段間
彼此互不相容之Segregation Strength。近年來之研究則發現除了Segregation 
Strength之外，奈米結構之自我組裝行為亦可藉由結晶行為3、及分子間之立體規
則性(Stereoregular Interaction between Stereoregular Entities)獲得控制。需要特別
注意的是，最近之奈米結構之自我組裝行為研究被發現，立體規則性嵌段共聚物
之奈米結構自我組裝，除了可以形成特殊之奈米微結構外4，更可以誘導學術上
相當特殊之Soldier-Sergeant Effect(士兵-士官長效應)之特殊奈米結構排列行為
5。也因此針對立體規則性嵌段共聚物新材料之合成與自我組裝研究
(self-assembly studies)成為，近年來高分子化學與高分子物理領域最熱門之研究
課題。

之問題，我們認為必頇藉由發展，新型、而且能適用於各種單體之選擇鏈轉移反

1. Heteroatom Containing Chain Transfer Agent 因為具備不同於 C, H 之雜元

四、結果與討論 
 在針對含雜元素之新型鏈轉移技術之開發研究當中，我們主要研究開發之新
型鏈轉移劑有如下兩種： 
1. 含鋁元素之三烷基鋁(e.g., trimethylaluminum, triethylaluminum). 
2. 含 矽 元 素 之 乙 烯 基 矽 化 物 vinylsilane (e.g., trimethylvinylsilane, 
phenyldimethylvinylsilane, triphenylvinylsilane). 
以下我們將針對應用含鋁元素之三烷基鋁為鏈轉移劑及含矽元素之乙烯基
矽化物 vinylsilane 為鏈轉移劑在以 metallocene 觸媒進行配位聚和 α-olefins 時，
此類新型鏈轉移劑對聚和反應及對所生成之 poly-α-olefins 在化學結構上之影響： 
 
4.1 應用含鋁元素之三烷基鋁為鏈轉移劑之聚和反應與嵌段聚合物和成研究 
4.1.a 應用含鋁元素之三烷基鋁為鏈轉移劑之聚和反應研究 
在使用不同之對位選擇性ansa-metallocene觸媒(e.g., Me2C(Cp)(Flu)ZrCl2 ; 
Me2Si(Cp)(Flu)ZrCl2)進行(1)對位選擇性聚合丙烯單體聚合與(2)冰片烯和乙烯兩
種單體共聚而成時，三烷基鋁化物(e.g., Trimethylaluminum ; Triethylaluminum)之
加入與濃度之增加，除了造成聚合物分子量之下降之外，更造成所生成之對位聚
丙烯(syndiotactic polypropylene, sPP)與環烯烴共聚物物(cyclo olefin coplymer，
COC)之末端化學結構之改變(See Table 1 and Table 2).從Figures 1 and 2 之1H 
NMR圖譜分析我們發現以此聚合反應條件，所生成之對位聚丙烯，可能生成兩
種不同之化學結構：包括vinylidene-capped sPP及OH-capped sPP，值得特別說明
的是vinylidene-capped sPP之vinylidene end-group源自1-2加成之丙烯單體，而後進
行β-Hydride Elimination之鏈轉移反應；相對的OH-capped sPP之hydroxyl 
end-group則源自1-2加成之丙烯單體，而後進行chain transfer to aluminum之鏈轉
移反應，所形成之末端帶鋁烷基之對位聚丙烯則可藉由進行氧化反應(O2/H2O2)
