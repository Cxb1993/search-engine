2中文摘要
環境污染物檢測方法可分為化學分析法和生物偵測法，前者主要是利用儀器定量分析污染
物的濃度，但無法得知污染物對生物造成毒害的綜合性影響，而生物法除了可偵測污染物之存
在與否，並可同步呈現污染物的生物毒性。此外，生物偵測法有別於物化法，具有低成本、低
污染、高效率之優勢，近年來隨著環境永續概念的發展，生物方法用以偵測環境污染物益顯重
要，然而，由於污染物種類繁多，且偵測方法各異，如何尋找與開發適用於多種環境污染物的
生物偵測法，仍是環境工程及檢測人員應該努力的方向。
本研究利用位於台南某污染場址下游之底泥土壤，於戴奧辛(Dioxin)污染後之生物復育環
境中，利用生物偵測法，將特定核酸片段放大，並配合瓊脂膠電泳分析，探討萃取後 DNA 之
完整性及強度，同時使用高解析度氣相層析/質譜儀(HRGC/MS)進行戴奧辛定量分析，再依其
結果與生物偵測法相互對應。期間，利用等溫迴圈核酸擴增法(LAMP)技術做特定 DNA 片段擴
增，並與廣泛使用之聚合酶鏈鎖反應方法(PCR)比較其核酸放大之效果。結果顯示，LAMP 放
大所得到之核酸分子量，最小約 100Da；最大約 1000Da 以上，且能夠擴增特定分子量之核酸；
而 PCR 放大所得之分子量，僅落在 200Da~300Da 之間，且反應中雖然擴增特定分子量之核酸，
但靈敏度不及 LAMP 佳。兩者方法之靈敏度比較，LAMP 與 PCR 相差 1000 倍。此一研究結果
顯示，利用 LAMP 做為核酸擴增工具，並以其為生物偵測之基礎，應為可行之生物偵測方法之
一環。

2. Materials and Methods
2.1 Inoculum soil
The soil in this study was sampled from Tainan An-Shun plant which is a chemical factory for produced
pesticides include dioxin and PCP. The site was announced become a real contaminated site polluted by PCP
and dioxin. To confirm the contamination of the soil, the dioxin and PCP concentration of the sample was then
investigated.
2.2 The screening of the bacteria strains
2g of soil was obtained from the site, and mixed with 20 ml of minimal salt basal medium (MSB). It was
prepared and added 10μg/L 2,3,7,8-TCDD, and put to a 150 ml satirized flask. The composition of the MSB
included 5.5 g Na2HPO4‧7H2O L-1, 2.7 g KH2PO4 L-1, 0.5 g (NH4)2SO4 L-1, 0.15 g KOH L-1, 0.6 g
MgSO4‧7H2O L-1, 0.2 g nitrilotriacetic acid L-1, 67 mg CaCl2 L-1, 2 mg FeSO4‧7H2O L-1, 0.815 mg
(NH4)Mo7O2‧4H2O L-1, and 1 ml of trace metal solution. The trace metal solution consisted of 2.5 g
Na2EDTA‧2H2O L-1, 11 g ZnSO4‧7H2O L-1, 5 g FeSO4‧7H2O L-1, 1.54 g MnSO4‧H2O L-1, 0.4 g
CuSO4‧5H2O L-1, 0.25 g Co(NO3)2‧6H2O L-1, and 0.18 g Na2B4O7‧10H2O L-1. All the reagents were
purchased from the Merck Chemical Co. (Darmstadt, Germany) at a purity of 98% to 99.5%. The growth
medium and the dilutants for serial dilutions were sterilized by autoclaving (121℃, 1.5 kg cm-2 for 30 min)
for the pure sub-culture experiments. The flask was cover with the permeable silicate stopper. The flask was
incubation in the 30℃, 150 rpm for 60 days. After the acclimation, the supernatant of the isolation culture was
took and added to the solid medium contain 1.5%Agar, 10μg/L 2,3,7,8-TCDD in 1L MSB. After 10 folder
sub-culture, the plate of the isolated strains were identified the genus and species by the identification of 16S
rDNA sequence and Biolog method.
2.3 The isolated strain identification and phylogenetic analysis
The 16S rDNA sequence alignment (Bochner, 1989) and Biolog carbon source utilization (Ausubel et al.,
1990) were employed to identify the genus and species of the strain which isolated from this study. The
phylogenetic analysis for the strains of this study was using the NCBI database BLAST software by 1000 time
calculation of the similarity. Additionally, the visual microscope (Olympus CH-2) was using for observe the

strain Ochrobactrum anthropi obtains the closer genetic relation to the genus of Sphingomonas species with
the Bootstrap value of 95. The phylogenetics analysis of these four strains indicated that the the strains in this
study achieved the biodiversity since the diversity of the genetics relations among these four strains.

3.3 The proteomic and the assay conditions analysis for the strains
In order to understand the variation of dioxin biotransformation protein in the culture medium, the strains of
Agromyces sp. and Achromobacter xylosoxidans were using prior to conduct the proteomics analysis. The
strains were inoculum to the culture with volume of 100 ml for 48 hours incubation and then concentrated.
The protein was then going to load to SDS-PAGE using 10% gel concentration. Every well load 25ul of
sample, using 120 voltages for 60 minutes to run the SDS-PAGE. The result was shown in figure 2, and found
that all the proteins smaller than 25K were disappeared and the different among the proteome cannot be
identified under this gel condition.
In order to obtain the feasible assay for the proteome analysis, the gel concentration of the SDS-PAGE was
modified to 12.5% with 25ul of sample, using 120 voltages for 60 minutes to run the SDS-PAGE. The
proteome display in the PAGE was more clear than last condition mention before. The SDS-PAGE was shown
in figure 3. The protein smaller than 25K molecular weight in this study can be identified more precise than
ever. It indicates that the gel concentration for those two strain’s proteome analysis need the gel concentration 
increased more than 12.5% than the proper result can be obtained.
12
After increase the gel concentration to 15% for the conduction of SDS-PAGE, the result was shown in figure
4. The results in figure 4 not make too much different compared to figure 3. Thus, the gel concentration
increase not make different when the concentration greater than 12.5%. It suggest the proper gel concentration
of 12.5%.
In order to approve the quality of the SDS-PAGE, the assay condition was still modified in this study. The
culture volume of the strains were then increased to 200 ml. the gel concentration use 12% for running the
SDS-PAGE. After 48 hours incubation, the proteome analysis was conducted using 120 voltages for 60
minutes. The result was shown in figure 4, that the protein with molecular weight 27K can obviously
distinguish and the protein with 15K has been clearly display. In this experiment, the gel running still has
some drawback that due to the blue line in the gel which suggest the proper staining process for the
SDS-PAGE conformation.
