研究摘要(500字以內)： 
二氧化氯（ClO2）是一種帶有辛辣氣體的黃綠色氣體，可用作強氧化劑及殺菌劑。它

是漂白粉、氯液等氯制劑最佳替代產品。目前已被世界衛生組織（WHO）列為A1 級安全
高效綠色消毒劑，而且美國環境保護署（EPA）已批淮用於醫療衛生業儀器設備的消毒。

製造技術，因此，本計劃將延續研究成果，並基於現有基礎，協助業者進行研發多功能二
氧化氯產生機之製造，針對高純度二氧化氯作為提供空氣清淨之用，以及食品相關產業之

效，而在200-300 ppm範圍，對除循環空氣異味消臭有顯著成效。

Abstract 
Chlorine dioxide (ClO2) is a pungent yellow-green gas that can be used as a strong oxidizer 
and bactericide. It have the best bactericidal efficiency and not have any by-products 
trihalomethanes (THMs). It has not polluted and has any adverse impacted for neture 
environment. It can substitute for bleaching powder and chlorine liquid preparations as 
bactericide on food inductry and medical treatment. Now, World Health Organization (WHO) 
already has been as safe and efficient green A1 class of disinfectants, and United States 
Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has been approved as bactericide and disinfection 
equipment for medical and health industry. The current Food Hygiene Regulations will also be 
chlorine dioxide (ClO2) as second category fungicides. Since chlorine dioxide has excellent 
deodorant and bactericidal properties, and it can be used for medicine, engineering, agriculture, 
livelihood and other industries. Our technology currently already had chlorine dioxide 
manufacturing technology. Therefore, this study will continue and be based on the existing 
system to support the production of chlorine dioxide for various manufacturing that produces 
high purity chlorine dioxide and food-related industries used for efficient sterilization and clean 
air of the new products. Present results in the standardization process through the production can 
be obtained for each batch solution production of 3000 ppm chlorine dioxide (purity ≧ 
99.0%), and with pure water ≦ 500 ppm detergent (pH 5~6). The results showed that 50-500 
ppm of ClO2 would have significantly bactericidal effect in the car; and 200-300 ppm for odor 
deodorizing effect.

技術特點說明： 
目前國內市售二氧化氯商品成分含量低，而且品質不均一，多為化學A、B劑所生產，
均偏酸性有害人體健康。本計畫所研發設備，經定性定量分析後，在標準化生產製程下，
均可製得濃度 3000 ppm 二氧化氯原液 (純度99.0%以上) 120 L。其設備所生產二氧化氯原
液，目前以純水配製成≦500 ppm的空氣清淨劑 (pH 5~6)。並透過車內落菌數實驗及除循環
空氣異味消臭實驗，在50-500 ppm範圍，對車內落菌數有顯著殺菌成效，約可減少循環空
氣總生菌數約九成，而在200-300 ppm範圍，對除循環空氣異味消臭有顯著成效，在氨水10 
ppm可達消除率100%。未來可以推廣使用在車用、室內及食品殺菌等相關產業的空氣消毒
清淨製程之用。
