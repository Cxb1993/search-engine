一、 中文摘要 
在此篇報告中，我們展示了使用
氧化鈰(CeO2)奈米微晶粒當作儲存電
荷媒介的新穎 SONOS 型式非揮發性快
閃記憶體的特性。可看出形成的氧化
鈰(CeO2) 奈米微晶粒形狀近似於球形
且具有高密度 5 × 10 11cm-2 的相同大
小。氧化鈰(CeO2) 奈米微晶粒是由兩
個步驟形成：使用蒸鍍槍系統沉積較
薄的氧化鈰(CeO2)薄膜約 2~3nm，接下
來在900℃氧氣的環境中作1分鐘的快
速升溫退火。製作成的記憶體元件呈
現出良好的電性特性，有足夠大的記
憶 窗 口 (>2V) 、 寫 入 / 抹 除 速 度
(10ms/0.1ms)、儲存資料持久時間到
達 104秒時只有 5%的電荷損失、在 105
次寫入/抹除後有較小的記憶窗口縮
小且可使用於二位元操作。這些特性
表示使用氧化鈰(CeO2)奈米微晶粒當
作儲存電荷媒介的新穎 SONOS 型式非
揮發性快閃記憶體有潛力應用在未來
的快閃記憶體中。 
英文摘要 
In the study, we demonstrated 
the characteristics of the non-
volatile Silicon-Oxide-Nitride 
-Oxide-Silicon (SONOS) type 
memories using Cerium Oxide (CeO2) 
nanocrystals as the charge storage 
agent. We characterize that the 
shape of formed CeO2 nanocrystal is 
nearly spherical and their sizes 
are almostly identical with quite 
high density of 5 × 10 11cm-2. Such 
CeO2 nanocrystals were formed by 
two steps: using evaporater-gun 
system deposites a thinner CeO2 
film ca. 2~3 nm, followed by rapid 
thermal annealing in O2 ambient at 
900 °C for 1 min. The fabricated 
memory devices show good elec-
trical properties in terms of a 
large enough memory window (> 2 V), 
program/erase speed (10 ms/ 0.1 
ms),retention time up to10 sec with 
only 5% charge loss, endurance 
after 105 P/E cycles with smaller 
memory window narrowing and 
two-bit operation. These features 
suggest that the nonvolatile 
SONOS-type memories with the CeO2 
nanocrystals trapping storage 
agent have the potential in the 
application of future flash 
memories.
