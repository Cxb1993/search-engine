4一、前言
鈦合金具有非常好的機械性質，但其骨引導性(osteoinductive)卻不佳。且在植體植入體
內初期，鹼性離子會受到化學及熱的影響，而由鈦合金表面釋放出來，進而影響到植體周圍
軟組織的健康狀況 【1】。
氫氧基磷灰石(hydroxyapatite, Ca10(PO4)6(OH)2, HA)，常被廣泛應用於披覆在金屬生醫材
料表面上，因為 HA 具有非常好的生物相容性(biocompatibility)及骨傳導性(osteoconductivity)
【2】，當 HA 與鈦合金基材接觸後會產生化學鍵結(chemical bonding) 【3】。而且先前的臨
床研究報告指出，CaP 陶瓷植入人體內後會直接與骨頭產生鍵結，同時進行骨整合作用
(osseointergration) 【4】。
然而，HA 仍然不能被單獨使用於植體方面，一些研究報告指出，HA 披覆在鈦表面其
鍵結強度不足，而且植入人體內後，很容易有 HA 顆粒脫落的現象產生【5】。因此，在植
入人體內後，經長期的體液與血液侵蝕，披覆層可能會產生剝離現象，造成金屬離子釋出，
進而引起人體過敏等不良反應【6】。此外，也有些研究發現 HA 披覆在 Ti6Al4V 的植體表
面，經由破壞韌性的測試，亦可發現類似的結果【7】。
為避免這些問題產生，研究發現可以在 HA 鍍層與鈦基材間，增加氧化鋯陶瓷薄膜(ZrO2)
介層，ZrO2 的特點為具有很高的機械強度、破壞韌性及常被運用於增加其他陶瓷的強度【8,
9】。在電漿噴塗的製程中，利用氧化鋯作為 HA 與金屬基材間的結合層，發現可以提高 HA
塗層的附著力【10】。
二、研究目的
本計畫希望在純鈦表面批覆兩層薄膜，第一層 ZrO2 陶瓷薄膜，目的作為鈦金屬與 HA
薄膜的界層，增加其鍵結力；第二層 HA 陶瓷薄膜，使表面具活性促進骨細胞之貼附、生長
與分化。在鈦表面製作這兩層薄膜，期能夠縮短鈦植入材與骨組織整合之時間。
本研究分為兩階段進行，第一階段利用電化學方法，製作 ZrO2 及 HA 鍍層，找出最佳
的鍍膜條件，包括電解沈積的電位及時間。第二階段進行鍍層的性質分析，包括鍍層表面形
態、成份、相組成、橫截面、耐蝕性及生物適應性等。

計畫成果自評
本研究藉由電化學沈積的方法，在不同電位下可在鈦金屬表面沈積片狀及針狀的 HA。
研究結果證實，當沈積時間設定在 3000 秒時，可在鈦金屬表面得到較均勻且緻密的片狀及
針狀 HA/ZrO2鍍膜。比較 X 光繞射分析 HA 的波峰強度，顯示針狀 HA/ZrO2 鍍膜會比片狀
HA/ZrO2 鍍膜具有較強的波峰強度。鍍膜的橫截面觀察結果顯示，片狀及針狀 HA/ZrO2鍍層
可以很緻密的貼附於純鈦基材表面且沒有裂痕產生，而批覆鍍膜的厚度約為 50μm。在人工
模擬唾液環境下進行試片的耐蝕性測試分析，顯示片狀 HA/ZrO2 鍍膜比針狀 HA/ZrO2 鍍膜
具有較低的鈍態電流密度。拉伸測試結果顯示，片狀 HA/ZrO2 鍍膜試片具有較高的鍵結強
度。細胞共培養的分析結果顯示，片狀及針狀 HA/ZrO2鍍膜皆可促進 MG-63 骨母細胞的貼
附生長，表示其皆具有良好的生物相容性。細胞增生評估分析，證實片狀及針狀 HA/ZrO2
鍍膜皆可有效提升細胞的增生速率，並且能使細胞穩定生長。

Introduction
Hydroxyapatite (Ca10(PO4)6(OH)2, HA) coatings on
titanium have revealed good fixation to bone and
increased bone ingrowth to the implant[1]. Electrolytic
deposition method was applied in this study because of
several advantages, such as flexibility of coating highly
irregular objects, arbitrary control of coating thickness
and chemical composition [2].
The purpose of this study attempted to fabricate
different morphologies of HA coating on titanium
substrate by electrolytic deposition method. HA coating
was obtained by controlling at proper deposition
potential and time.
Materials and Methods
Commercially pure titanium (grade 2) rod was cut into
disk with diameter of 16 mm and thickness of 1.0 mm.
The surface of each specimen was ground with SiC
sandpapers, polished with 0.3 μm Al2O3 powder, and
ultrasonically cleaned in distilled water and acetone for
10 minutes respectively.
HA coating was fabricated in mixed solution of
Ca(NO3)2•4H2O and NH4H2PO4. The electrodeposition
of calcium phosphates was executed by a potentio stat
(AUTOLAB PGSTAT 30, Eco Chemie BV,
Netherlands). Specimens were naturally dried for 1 day
and then annealed at 350℃ for 1hr in the argon
atmosphere after electrochemical deposition.
The surface morphologies and compositions of the
coating layers were observed using scanning electron
microscopy and energy dispersive spectroscopy
(SEM/EDS, HITACHI S-3000N SEM, HORIBA
EX-200 EDS). The crystalline structure of the substrates
was determined by X-ray diffraction (XRD, MAC
MO3X-HF Diffractometer) with Cu Kα radiation. 
Results and Discussion
A limiting current density of 2 ×10-3 A/cm2 was found
around -1.5 V in cathodic polarization curve. From the
electrochemical reaction, Ca2+ ions could react with PO3-
and OH- ions on titanium surface to form HA.
Fig. 1 shows the SEM micrograph of different
morphologies of HA coating on titanium substrate. In
this study, a uniform and dense HA layer could be
successfully coated on the titanium substrate by
electrolytic deposition. The plate-like HA crystals were
formed on the titanium substrate in the Ca and P mixed
solution at 65 ℃at potential -1.3 V for 3000 s, and
acicular-like crystals were formed at potential -1.4 V
for 3000 s. The cross-section indicated the thickness of
coating layer was 50 μm approximately. The SEM/EDS
analysis of HA coating exhibited Ti, O, Ca and P peaks,
that confirmed the coating possessed Ca and P elements.
X-ray diffraction patterns were subjected to examine
the plate-like and acicular-like HA coating on titanium
substrate for annealing at 350 ℃ for 1 hr. The two types
of crystallization were observed (002), (211) and (112)
faces respectively. The acicular-like HA coating
appeared stronger peak intensity than that plate-like
coating in XRD patterns.

Plate-like and acicular-like hydroxyapatite could be
formed on Ti substrates by electrolytic deposition
