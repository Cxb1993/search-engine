Maintenance Policy and Number of Lease Periods for Leased Equipment with A

一、中文摘要 
本計畫主要研究探討長、短租期設備的維修策
略與租期數。第一年研究探討短租期設備在維修門
檻值下一階段維修策略與租期數對利潤模式之影
響。對於可維修之租賃設備，當設備在租賃期間內
故障時，出租人給予設備小修處理來恢復設備的正
常運作功能。若設備的故障維修時間超過租賃契約
內制訂之維修時間時，則出租人需給予承租人適當
的金額賠償。為了降低設備故障所帶來之維修成本
與賠償金，租賃契約中將加入設備的預防保養與預
防性置換策略。以租賃公司的觀點來看，其主要的
收益來源為設備租金收入，而設備續租租金折扣優
惠是租賃公司出租設備一個極重要的行銷方式。當
設備老舊或不再符合客戶需求時，設備的租期數與
續租租金折扣問題對租賃公司是一個值得探討的
主題。因此在維修成本模式中加入設備租金、設備
續租租金折扣與租期數等出租設備可獲得之利
潤，將會有助於出租人與承租人雙方在擬定租賃契
約條款內容時的一個具體參考指標。針對設備在租
賃期間內建構出之利潤數學模式，推導出最佳的維
修門檻值，維修策略與租期數，使得出租人獲得總
利潤為最高。最後針對不同維修成本函數與租金折
扣，來進一步分析比較最佳維修門檻值，維修策略
與租期數對總利潤模式之影響。 
關鍵詞：預防保養、預防性置換、租期數、小修、
門檻值 
Abstract 
In this project, the maintenance policy and 
number of lease periods for leased equipment with a 
Long-run is investigated in the first year. For 
repairable leased equipment, the lessor performs a 
minimal repair for the equipment to restore normal 
operation state when equipment failed within the 
lease period. If the repair time of failed equipment 
exceeds a pre-specified tolerable time in the lease 
contract, then there is a penalty cost for the lessor. 
The preventive maintenance (PM) and preventive 
replacement (PR) policies are performed in order to 
reduction maintenance and penalty cost in the lease 
contract. Since the income of the lease company is 
rent, the discount of rent will be an important policy 
for sale. We will study the effects of maintenance 
policies, threshold value of maintenance and number 
of lease period for profit model under different 
preventive maintenance cost and rent discount in the 
lease contract. 
Keywords: Preventive Maintenance, Preventive 
Replacement, Number of Lease Periods, 
Minimal Repair, Threshold Value  
二、緣由與目的 
本計畫為國科會個別型二年計劃「長、短租期
設備的維修策略與租期數之研究」，第一年研究短
租期設備在維修門檻值下一階段維修策略與租期
數對利潤模式之影響，相關背景介紹如下。 
自1990年代至今，科技產品的快速發展及產品
功能的不斷提昇，致使製造商需以組合式系統設備
或不同生產設備來生產產品。然而，組合式系統設
備或生產設備的維修與保養需要不同的專業技術
維修人員，因此製造商除需花費昂貴成本採購設備
外，還需花費時間與成本來訓練專業的維修技術人
員。為了減少公司的成本費用支出，製造商傾向以
租賃來取代購買設備。 
在一般租賃契約下之出租設備大都是可維修
設備，在可維修設備之維修策略中有故障狀態已知
和故障狀態未知的情況。依據設備故障狀態已知和
未知的維修策略之研究，在過去的文獻中已有相當
廣泛的討論。對於故障狀態已知的情況下，維修策
略有小修(Minimal Repair)、預防保養(Preventive 
Maintenance)、不完美維修(Imperfect Repair)和置換
(Replacement)，而在故障狀態未知的情況下之維修
策略是將檢驗策略加入其中，再與上述之維修策略
結合。維修策略中，設備故障的小修概念最早來自
於Barlow與Hunter (1960)。所謂「小修」係指設備
故障經維修後恢復正常運作但其設備的失效率與

四、結論與討論 
    由數值例子的結果可以得知，若預防保養成本
增加時，則保養時間間隔較長，所需的預防保養次
數較少，設備可出租的期數將縮短，總利潤也將降
低。 
 
五、計畫成果自評 
    本研究完成第一年短租期設備在維修門檻值
下一階段維修策略與租期數對利潤模式之影響。對
租賃設備所建構之利潤模式，推導出最佳維修策略
與租期數的性質，透過數值分析結果，獲致相關於
契約決策制訂之重要性質，佐證維修策略與租期數
對利潤模式之影響。

因應近代科技迅速發展，產業界近年來採取以租賃方式取代購買設備來減
少公司在購買昂貴設備、設備的維修與訓練專業技術維修人員成本費用支出之
趨勢。因此，對於可維修的租賃設備，租賃公司在租賃契約內容中所提供之維
修策略將影響出租雙方之權益。本計畫之研究成果，在學術研究方面，可解析
有關租賃設備維修保養策略、設備續租租金折扣之制訂與出租期數之研究對制
訂租賃契約內容之影響，並具有推廣租賃契約可適用範圍之貢獻。由本研究之
成果可知，若租賃設備的預防保養成本增加時，將拉長保養間隔時間，所需的
預防保養次數變少，設備可出租的期數將縮短，所獲得的總利潤也將降低。實
務上，本研究成果可作為產業界之設備出租雙方對於長、短租期設備的租賃契
約內容之制訂的重要決策參考依據。

 Optimal Number of Leased Periods and Maintenance Policy with a 
Threshold Value on Age for Leased Equipment 
 
Wen Liang Chang† 
Department of Information Management, Cardinal Tien College of Healthcare & Management,Taipei, Taiwan 
Email: D9101402@mail.ntust.edu.tw 
 
Ruey Huei Yeh 
Department of Industrial Management, National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, Taipei, Taiwan 
Email: rhyeh@im.ntust.edu.tw 
 
Hui-Chiung Lo 
Department of Business Administration, Tamkang University, Tamsui, Taipei, Taiwan 
Email: hclo@mail.tku.edu.tw 
 
 
Abstract. This paper investigates the optimal number of lease periods and maintenance policy for leased 
equipment. In order to have steady revenue, the lessor (owner) of the equipment may provide a discount to 
encourage the lessee to sign a lease contract with a longer lease period. However, as the lease period 
increases, the maintenance cost also increases due to the deterioration or usage. Therefore, there is a need to 
derive an optimal number of lease periods and a corresponding maintenance policy for the leased equipment 
from the viewpoint of the lessor such that the expected total profit is maximized. In this paper, the following 
maintenance scheme is considered. When the leased equipment fails, a minimal repair is performed to bring 
the equipment back to an operating condition. Furthermore, when the age of the equipment reaches a certain 
threshold value, an imperfect preventive maintenance (PM) is carried out to avoid possible failures. Under 
this maintenance scheme, the mathematical model of the expected total profit is developed and the optimal 
number of lease periods and the corresponding optimal maintenance policy are derived. Finally, numerical 
examples are given to illustrate the effects of the number of lease periods and the maintenance policy on the 
expected total profit.  
 
Keywords: Lease Period, Discount, Preventive Maintenance, Minimal Repair.  
 
 
1. INTRODUCTION 
 
Due to consumers' variable requirements on the 
functionality of a product and expensive production 
equipment, it is not economical for the manufacturer to 
purchase the expensive production equipment to product 
the multi-function products and satisfy consumers' demand. 
In order to reduce cost, many manufacturers usually lease 
production equipment rather than purchase them (Desai and 
Purohit 1998). Under this situation, the lease payment and 
maintenance service of production equipment are very 
important items in the lease contract for the manufacturer. 
For repairable leased equipment, the maintenance 
methods can be classified into two major categories, (i) 
Corrective Maintenance (CM) and (ii) Preventive 
Maintenance (PM). CM actions are used to rectify failed 
equipment back to its operational state, and PM actions are 
performed to improve the operational state of the 
equipment to avoid failures. Minimal repair is often 
adopted CM action to restore failed equipment (Barlow and 
Hunter 1960). After minimal repair, the equipment is 
normal operation but the slope of failure rate remains 
unchanged. The PM can be classified into two major 
categories, (i) perfect PM and (ii) imperfect PM. Perfect 
and imperfect PM methods are often adopted in the 
maintenance model to reduce the number of the equipment 
failures and to maintenance the normal operation of the 
equipment. Many PM policies have been proposed and 
studied under various situations such as finite or infinite 
horizon (Jayabalan and Chaudhuri 1992a, Jayabalan and 
Chaudhuri 1992d, Jayabalan and Chaudhuri 1995, Malik

5. CONCLUSIONS 
 
In this paper, we construct a mathematical model of 
profit for leased equipment and found the optimal threshold 
value of age is equal to the degree of PM. That is, the 
equipment should be restored to its original state after each 
PM action. Furthermore, we show that there exists a unique 
optimal maintenance policy and number of lease periods 
within the lease period such that the expected total profit is 
maximized. When the failure rate of equipment is a strictly 
decreasing function, it is not worthwhile to perform PM 
actions for leased equipment within the lease period. 
Otherwise, the maximum profit of leased equipment 
decreases when PM cost increases.
