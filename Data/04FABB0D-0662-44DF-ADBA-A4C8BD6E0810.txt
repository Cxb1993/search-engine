 
摘要 
 
本研究主要是探討中性及鹼性電解液對
鋅空氣燃料電池鋅陽極及電池性能之影響以
及解決電解液易洩漏之問題。在鋅空氣極製
備上則以銅網為集電網，並且在空氣極的擴
散層及催化層中分別適量聚乙烯醇(PVA)，所
製備之空氣極可以解決電解液易洩漏之問
題。將所製備之空氣極、電解液分別為飽和
之氯化鈉(NaCl) 水溶液、6M 氫氧化鉀(KOH)
水溶液及 6M 氫氧化鈉(NaOH)水溶液，以鋅
板為陽極組成鋅空氣燃料電池，並在定電壓
下進行電池放電測試，由實驗結果得知：電
解 液 為 氫 氧 化 鉀 水 溶 液 時 其 放 電 電 流
值 最 高 約 在 45 mA 左 右，而 電 解 液 為 氯
化 鈉 及氫 氧 化 鈉 水 溶 液 時 其 放 電 電 流
值 大 致 相 同 約 在 22 mA 左 右 。 為 了 探討
鋅在不同電解液中之腐蝕行為，則藉由塔弗
極化技術進行實驗，由實驗結果得知：鋅在
氯 化 鈉 水溶液之腐蝕電位高於在氫氧化鉀
及氫氧化鈉水溶液中的腐蝕電位約 700 
mV。就腐蝕電流而言，鋅在 6 M 氫氧化鉀，
6 M 氫 氧 化 鈉 及 6 M 氯 化 鈉 水溶液中之
腐蝕電流都有相同的次方(order)，由此可知，
鋅在氯化鈉水溶液中具有較佳的抗蝕性。 
 
關鍵詞：鋅空氣燃料電池、電解液、定電壓、
腐蝕、塔弗極化技術 
 
Abstract 
 
The main aim of the present study was to 
investigate effects of electrolyte on the 
zinc anode and cell performance of zinc/air 
fuel cell. The air- electrode consists of two 
catalyst layers through the small addition of 
poly (vinyl alcohol) (PVA) bonded to each side 
of a copper mesh has been prepared and was 
effective to prevent leaking problem of 
electrolyte. In Zn /air battery using both 
alkaline (potassium hydroxide, 6 M KOH, and 
sodium hydroxide, 6 M NaOH) and neutral 
brine electrolytes (6 M NaCl) respectively, the 
cell performance test was carried out at constant 
potential condition. With the potassium 
hydroxide electrolyte, the discharge current has 
the largest valve about 45 mA. However, the 
other electrolytes have the same discharge 
current about 22 mA. Corrosion behavior of 
zinc anode at different electrolytes was studied 
using Tafel polarization technique test. The zinc 
in 6 M NaCl solution has an extremely large 
positive displacement of 700 mV in the 
corrosion potential relative to the value for the 
zinc in the other solutions. It means that zinc in 
6 M NaCl solution showed excellent corrosion 
resistance. 
 
Keywords: Zn/air fuel cell; Electrolyte; 
Constant potential; Corrosion; 
Tafel polarization technique

  Although the dimension of the nail is controlled precisely through the wire flat rolling process, but uneven thickness 
is often found when put together. Uneven thickness of nails lead to the seizing-up of nailer. If we can rolled several 
wires (for example, 10, 25 or 50 wires) at the same time, then the thickness of the nails may be more consistent. This 
method could also be used to increase the production rate. 
  This paper is pointed to simulate the flat rolling process of multi-wire, by using the finite element analysis software 
DEFORM-3D [9, 10]. We can understand the difference between single wire flat rolling and multi-wire flat rolling from 
the simulation results. These results can be applied to roller design and further study.

3.3 Forming load 
  About the forming load, wire diameter of 0.9, 1.18 and 1.575 mm are used to simulate the flat rolling. Fig. 14 is the 
forming load variation of single wire flat rolling. 0.9 mm wire has the biggest forming load and 1.18 mm is the smallest.

that the forming load of 5 wires’ flat rolling is almost five times of single wire flat rolling. Therefore, we can said that 
the forming load of 50 wires’ flat rolling might be fifty times of the single wire flat rolling.
