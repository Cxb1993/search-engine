中文摘要 
 
田口方法廣為企業界所愛用，然而西方統
計學者對其方法有諸多的批評，例如： 
Montgomery 批評其實驗方法只是 pick up 
the winner。因此深入探討田口方法在直交
表上的應用，了解其型I風險，以避免對最
佳製程參數水準之誤判對業界極為重要。
已過對田口方法的批判，諸如內外側直交
表、訊號雜訊比、順序類別型資料的累積
法等均有詳細的探討。然而對於田口方法
的型Ｉ風險卻未有深入的探討，因此，本
研究利用模擬方法來產生隨機變數作為品
質特性值，並假設品質特性值呈常態分
佈，且其平均值與變異數均相等。再利用
田口方法含(1)訊號雜訊比(2)合併誤差(3)
對訊號雜訊比或品質特性值做ANOVA和
(4)F值等於4或等於5%的顯著性等諸項準
則，加以評定直交表上各欄(各因子)之顯著
性。研究結果發現型Ι錯誤風險相當大，因
此必須結合田口之觀念與有效的運用統計
方法來改善品質。 
 
關鍵詞：田口方法，訊號雜訊比，型 Ι 錯
誤，直交表，模擬。 
 
1. Abstract 
 
   Taguchi method is an approach for 
improving product quality, manufacturability, 
and reliability at low cost using simple 
statistical techniques; as signal-to-noise ratio, 
pooling-up technique, and F test. These 
techniques have been discussed and criticized 
by several researchers. This research 
proposes an extension to on going research 
by determining the probability of 
misidentifying nonsignificant factors as 
significant, or so called “alpha risk” for the 
larger-the-better (LTB) type quality 
characteristic for various orthogonal arrays 
using simulation. It is found that alpha risk 
values are very high. As a result, more valid 
and effective statistical techniques are 
required to be combined with Taguchi’s 
well-founded engineering concepts.   
Keywords: Taguchi Method, Signal-to-noise 
Ratio, Alpha Risk, Orthogonal 
Array, Simulation 
 
2. Purpose of the Study 
The purpose of this paper is to investigate 
the alpha risk of Taguchi method for LTB 
type quality characteristic using simulation 
for orthogonal arrays.
