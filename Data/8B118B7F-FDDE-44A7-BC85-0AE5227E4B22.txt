DISCUSSION 
If the number of the stator lobe is one, the 
center of the rotor and the center of the stator 
are not concentric. However, if an air guide 
plate instead of valves is used, the center of 
the rotor and the center of the stator have to 
be concentric. Therefore, the number of the 
stator lobe to be one is not satisfactory. 
Moreover, if the number of the stator lobe is 
increased, the pressure angle increases 
rapidly. Therefore, the number of the stator 
lobe to be three is selected. If the radius ratio 
as shown in Fig. 6 is small, a large tool can 
be used to fabricate the stator inner profile. 
The pressure angle that the maximum value 
is less than 22 degree increases gently as 
shown in Fig. 7. When the rotational speed 
increases, forces Fn, FR and FL  increase 
rapidly as shown in Figs. 9, 10 and 11, 
respectively. Compared the results with 
Table 1, the case I, II and III exist, but the 
case IV does not exist at these rotational 
speeds. The efficiency increases as the gap 
decreases as shown in Fig. 12. The rotations 
of the rotor and the vane of the preliminary 
compressor are smoothly; therefore, the 
design of the compressor is acceptable. 
CONCLUSION 
A rotary single sliding-vane compressor 
with three lobes of the stator is designed and 
fabricated. The operations of the rotor and 
the vane are smoothly. Therefore, the design 
of the compressor is acceptable.

乙. 與會心得 
油源日漸稀少，面臨高油價時代的來臨，既環保又省油的車輛成為各大
車廠生產集團競相研究的方向，此次參加展覽會，增加了解世界有關車輛
研究之潮流與方向，了解環保、性能與休旅為未來車輛設計之重點趨勢概
念。獲取許多有關節能環保之省油車、電動車、混合動力車輛、燃料車的
最新知識，增加許多新產品之概念。同時還獲取許多有關發展、了解融合
科技與智慧，發展與加強優良之性能設計、車體外型、車門、燈光、車室
內裝配與空間設計、和電子儀器設備，獲得寶貴之經驗，獲益良多，對自
己之將來之研究與教學更有鼓舞之作用。
