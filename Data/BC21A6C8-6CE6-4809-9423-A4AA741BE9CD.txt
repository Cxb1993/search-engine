(二) 計畫英文摘要 
Objective：To improve the local control rate of cancer patients and reduce the complications, the patient 
should get the right dose and which should be delivered at the right position, and this is always the goal of 
the radiation oncology teams.  
 
Method and Material：To accomplish this verification, we presented a specified method with the Isocentric 
beam checker, which is a common QA (quality assurance) device in the department of radiation oncology. 
The checker was placed on the simulator couch with its center ball coincident with the simulator isocenter, 
and one axis of its cross marks parallel to the axis of gantry rotation. The gantry of the simulator was rotated 
to make the checker behave like a three-dimensional array of balls. After exposing and digitizing the films, 
the position of each steel ball on the checker was reconstructed and compared to its true position, which can 
be accurately calculated. After the verification test, we analyzed the positioning error and deviations of the 
source at each axis of the referred coordinate with the statistic methods and tools. Furthermore, we 
calculated the dose error caused by the positioning uncertainty, according to the TG43 report. We also 
verified the dose error by using the solid water phantom with the EBT2 films. 
 
Result and Conclusion: The differences between the theoretical source position and the measured source 
position is -0.2 mm ± 0.4 mm, 0.1 mm ± 0.3 mm and 0.1 mm ± 0.4 mm for the x, y and z axis respectively in 
our facility. Due to the position measurement uncertainty, the dose difference could be up to 7% through the 
dose calculation by TG43. However, the dose differences between our measurement by EBT2 film and the 
calculation by TG43 or by our planning system are less than 3%. Finally, our method could be used for the 
routine QA of the HDR dose verification.

B.結論與建議 
本研究計畫發展出一套校正方法，能夠使用一般醫學物理師常用的校正工具—等中心射束驗證盤來做
系統誤差之評估，並利用 KS test (Kolmogorov-Smirnov Z test)與 JB test (Jarque-Bera test)統計方法，提
出在本治療機構迪卡兒座標三個座標軸上之誤差值，其分別為-0.2 mm ± 0.4 mm、0.1 mm ± 0.3 mm 以
及 0.1 mm ± 0.4 mm，其他臨床醫療單位可應用本方法了解該機構之後荷治療射源絕對位置之相關誤差
或參考應用本方法於其他治療技術之研究。 
 
本報告亦將上述研究之結果帶入美國醫學物理學會第 43 號後荷治療劑量計算之工作報告(Nath et al., 
1995)來計算隨機誤差所造成的劑量誤差，我們發現因為射源位置的不確定性，可能導致 7%的劑量誤
差。而我們實際量測的結果與電腦治療計畫與 TG43相比，誤差在 3%之內，顯示臨床治療病人所得到
的實際劑量與預估得到的劑量差異不大。我們的整套技術，可成為臨床治療機構在後荷治療劑量驗證
部分，一個品保的重要方法。
