2摘要
關鍵字：磁性奈米顆粒、溫熱法、四氧化三鐵、布朗損失、尼爾損失、比吸收率
超順磁性(superparamagnetic)奈米顆粒，具有高發熱能力及低毒性之優點，近年來在治
療腫瘤上成為深具潛力之發展方向。為了合成符合臨床應用上之磁性奈米顆粒，必須針對
料物理變因測試材料發熱能力，並以模擬生物體蛋白質、黏度及電解質環境評估包覆材料
選用方向，以期建立適當之材料合成方法。本研究以自製之高功率交流磁場產生裝置進行
發熱能力試驗，試驗中以材料單位重量之發熱量(SAR，即比吸收率)作為評估之依據。由實
驗結果得知，粒徑尺寸需控制在 12nm~17nm 左右，濃度 0.05g/mL 以下，藉由提高磁場強
度及磁場頻率可達到高發熱效果。包覆材方面，應避免選用易受生物體環境影響之離子性
長鏈脂肪酸，可使用生物可相容之幾丁聚醣及聚乙烯亞胺作為包覆材料。
Abstract
Keywords：superparamagnetic iron oxide nanoparticles, hyperthermia, specific absorption rate
The hyperthermia using superparamagnetic nanoparticles, magnetite (Fe3O4), as a thermoseed is a
promising solution for tumor therapy. The aim of this study was to investigate the heating of
superparamagnetic nanoparticles influenced by physical and environmental factors under alternating
current field. Nanosized magnetite was prepared by chemical precipitation method using Fe2+, Fe3+ salt,
and ammonium hydroxide under a nitrogen atmosphere. The nanoparticles were characterized by
transmission electron microscopy, X-ray diffraction, Fourier transformation infrared spectroscopy and
superconducting quantum interference device magnetometer. The specific absorption rate (SAR) values of
aqueous suspensions of magnetite particles with different diameters varying from 7.7 to 13.4 nm were
investigated by measuring the time-dependent temperature curves in the magnetic fields of various
strength (3.0-4.7 kA/m) and frequency. (93 - 243 kHz). Results indicate that the SAR values increase as
the incensement of magnetic fields strength and frequency. The linear dependencies were also obtained
between SAR and the square of the field strength. As magnetite diameter increased from 7.7 to 13.4 nm,
SAR value increased from 0.7 to 3.7 W/g Fe3O4. Aggregation behavior of magnetite in physiological
media (high saline concentration, protein concentration and viscosity) is essential for biomedical
applications. This parameter varied strongly with the nature of coating. While laurate-stabilized magnetite
quickly precipitated in physiological media, PEI-stabilized magnetite stayed in solution for indefinitely
long time. SAR value reduced from 10.5 to 6.0 W/g with addition of 6.25 mM NaCl in the sample as well
as SAR value reduced to 8.0 W/g with addition of 0.125 g/mL BSA. As the viscosity of magnetic solution
increased by addition of glycerol, SAR value of the sample decreased. SAR value reduced to 6.6 W/g with
addition of 50% (v/v) glycerol. Nevertheless, for magnetic nanoparticles enveloped by PEI, its SAR
values were not significantly influenced by addition of NaCl, BSA and glycerol. In conclusion,
PEI-stabilized magnetite nanoparticles appear to be promising as a potential media for hyperthermia
applications under higher magnetic field frequency and strength applied.

乙烯亞胺為包覆材之顆粒而言，雖然其發熱能力較月桂酸包覆者為低，但由於兩者均為
帶正電之高分子，推論發熱能力不會受到白蛋白所影響。
(5) 在模擬生物體內黏滯度環境方面，在低濃度樣品條件下，將甘油濃度從 0%增加至 75%，
黏滯度從約 0.9cP 上升至約 29cP，樣品之發熱量從 10.0W/g 降至約 6W/g。根據推論下
降之部份主要是為布朗發熱機制所造成。另外值得注意的是，甘油加入量 25%及 50%
之樣品，其發熱量分別為 7.6W/g(降 24%)及 6.6W/g(降 34%)，由於黏滯度範圍與正常全
血者類似，因此以布朗損失為發熱機制之奈米顆粒非常容易受到外界之影響。對於以幾
丁聚醣及聚乙烯亞胺為包覆材之顆粒而言，雖然其發熱能力會隨甘油濃度上升而下降，
但根據回歸分析結果得知，兩者發熱能力下降量較月桂酸包覆者為低。
(6) 在模擬電解質環境方面，即使僅加入低濃度 6.25mM 之氯化鈉，顆粒之發熱量也會從
10.5W/g 降至 6W/g 程度。由於樣品在配置過程中產生快速聚集與沉現象，推論原因可
能來自遮蔽效應所致。對於以幾丁聚醣及聚乙烯亞胺為包覆材之顆粒而言，由於高濃度
NaCl 會造成幾丁聚醣產生膨潤作用，推論此現象會增加顆粒之水力半徑，造成以布朗
機制之發熱能力受到影響，因此在 150mM 濃度以上，幾丁聚醣包覆之顆粒發熱能力會
受濃度提高而降低。至於以聚乙烯亞胺為包覆材之顆粒而言，則不會受到 NaCl 所影響。
