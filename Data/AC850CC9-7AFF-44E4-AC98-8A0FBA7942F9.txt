本計劃的目的為研究利用奈米陶瓷粉末生產毫米尺度複雜形狀工件之技術。本計畫擬採用本實驗
室特有的疊層加工方法：漿料選擇性燒結法來製作工件的可行性。 
本計劃有三個製程上的難題要解決：就是(1)漿料分散、(2)鋪層快乾不裂、(3)支撐自動溶解。本
計劃將(1)採購特別的設備(超音波攪拌機)，與開發特別的方法(循環超音波震盪分散法)，以充份分散
奈米粉末；(2)要設計特別的設備(無段層厚模板)與開發新漿料配方(黏結劑成及分比例)以快速檢驗出
快乾不裂所需的層厚與漿料配方；(3)要設計新式的材料配方(黏結劑與崩解劑配對)，使本計劃製程的
支撐可以自動溶解，解決前述的三個問題。 
本研究利用矽酸膠液，加入黏結劑 BP-20S，因為矽酸膠液本身黏度太低，需加入 PVA幫助顆粒與顆粒
之間的黏結才能進行鋪層。在鋪層過程中，溫度控制方面，不能超過 30度，以防薄層產生裂痕。減少
含水量有助於增加鋪層厚度，以及鋪層後之薄層表面並沒有氣孔產生，工件燒結最高溫度到 1250℃，
緻密度燒結前到 62%，燒結後到 98%。

一、前言 
 
申請人十多年來研究的主題多數為雷射輔助快速原型陶瓷製程及其應用。在 1998 年度提出 “陶瓷
雷射熔結法”[1]的製程，在 2001 年度提出 “陶瓷雷射膠化法”[2]；在 2003 年提出結合陶瓷雷射熔結法
與陶瓷雷射膠化法優點的 “陶瓷雷射硬燒結法”[3]，2007 年提出＂陶瓷雷射軟燒結法＂。其中的陶瓷
雷射膠化法就是利用「奈米級陶瓷粉末」以雷射局部乾燥膠化的效應來連結粗顆粒的陶瓷粉末。但是
前述的製程並未曾以奈米粉末作為主要的結構粉末。申請人的製程係以漿料為起始材料，理論上可以
使用微細粉末，奈米粉末也包含在內。但因一直沒有適當的潛在應用來支持奈米粉末的研究需求，所
以本實驗室只研究使用次微米粉末的製程。近來，半導體封裝測試公司提出以快速原型製造陶瓷治具
的需求，因半導體製造的積體電路越來越小，陶瓷治具的尺寸也會越來越小，陶瓷微細特徵的精密製
造已經是現在實際的需求了。積體電路測試用治具用量小，但種類多，使用傳統製造法成本較高，這
是典型的適合用快速原型的產品。同時尺寸越微細，傳統加工方式的精度越低，快速原型的製程適合
製作小工件，若精度能達到將有機會與 NC 切削等傳統加工方式競爭。 
Rahaman在陶瓷製程一書[4]中提到” 粉末數量和成分(composition)標準差成反比”因在小陶瓷工件
中的粉末顆粒數量較大陶瓷工件少，所以製造多個小陶瓷工件時，這些工件的成分差異會較大陶瓷工
件為大。若我們在小工件中使用較小粉末，就可以提高粉末數量，也就是減小成分差異。假設現用0.5
微米的顆粒若改用100奈米的顆粒，顆粒數增為125倍，其成分差異為原來的11分之一。我們相信若成
分差異減小，則小工件的密度差異也會減小，就有機會做出精度較高的小工件。所以本計劃擬利用本
實驗室的漿料選擇性燒結法以奈米粉末為結構主材料來做出複雜形狀的陶瓷毫米尺度工件，然後與次
微米粉末做出工件作一比較。 
本計劃的目的是研究利用奈米粉末生產毫米尺度複雜形狀之陶磁工件之技術。

2.乾燥：步驟(4)以加熱器將鋪層加熱乾燥，使其固化成犧牲部分。 
3.雷射掃描：步驟(5)以雷射依切層檔案對犧牲薄層進行選擇性掃描，經過掃描的部份經由有機黏結劑
熔解，彼此混合連結無機粉末成形，未被掃描的犧牲薄層則成為自然形成之支撐。 
4.重複：工作平台下降一個鋪層厚度後，重覆步驟(2)至步驟(5)的動作，直到所有薄層堆疊完畢，步
驟(6)。 
5.清料：步驟(7)以崩解劑去除未經雷射掃描之犧牲部分，即可取出三維-陶瓷材料的工件。 
將前述的陶瓷材料的工件在燒結爐中施以適當溫度與時間燒失有機成分，再施以高溫緻密化燒
結，成為緻密化的陶瓷工件。

The advantage of the wet process is the capability of casting layer thinner than that of 
powder-based processes and results in a better surface roughness of the green parts. Theoretically, 
layer manufacturing will lead to staircase effect on an inclined plane or a curved surface of a 3D 
object. Such phenomenon will influence the surface roughness of the part [15]. The thicker the layer, 
the more serious of the staircase effect. Kuo [16] used the slurry composed of silica powder and silica 
sol to fabricate ceramic green parts and reported the minimum thickness of layer casting was 100 μm. 
On account of the large particle size and other unknown reasons, if the layer thickness is less than 100 
μm, the surface of the gel-like surface is damaged by the shear force occurred between the scraper and 
the previous layers. Liu [17] employed selective laser gelling (SLG), which is identical to CLG, to 
fabricate 2.5D parts with layer thickness of 100 μm, but the staircase effect was not mentioned. In 
CLG, not only the amount of binder but also the water content can result in the variety of the viscosity 
of slurry. If the binder content is fixed, high viscosity (low water content) will cause the difficulty of 
layer casting; on the other hand, low viscosity (high water content) will lead to poor consolidation 
between the layers, because a definite amount of laser energy is not sufficient to vaporize the 
excessive amount of water.  Thus, it can be seen that water content and laser energy are the crucial 
parameters of CLS. The aim of this paper is to demonstrate that optimizing the parameters (water 
content in the slurry and the laser scanning speed) can fabricate ceramic green part with the layer 
thickness of 40 μm, by which the staircase effect can be improved. 
Experimental method 
Ceramic Laser Gelling. CLG process is shown in Fig. 1. The fabricating steps include (A) mixing 
ceramic powder, dispersant, deionized and binder to form a slurry; (B) forming a thin slurry layer 
with a scraper; (C) scanning the slurry layer selectively with a laser beam; (D) descending platform 
for one layer thickness, and repeating steps from (A) to (D), until (E) completing a three dimensional 
ceramic part; (F) removing the un-scanned slurry to obtain a green part. 
 
Fig. 1 Schematic of CLG process 
The silica sol will be gelled or solidified, if its water content is reduced.  The CLG takes this effect 
and employs the energy from a laser beam to directly vaporize the liquid in the slurry so that the 
solidified silica will connect the ceramic powders and a gelled network can be formed. The gelled 
network cannot be dissolved in water. However, the un-scanned slurry, which adheres to the object, 
retains its initial water-soluble property. The green part can be obtained after removal of un-scanned 
slurry with water flushing. To completely bind the adjacent layers, a minimum solidified thickness by 
laser irradiation should be no less than 1.5 times of the casting thickness.

Results and Discussion 
Table 1 illustrates the relation between the formability and water content in the slurry. The 
preliminary results revealed that fabrication of the part was impossible when water content was 54 
wt% even though the scanning speed was reduced to 60 mm/s. Delamination did not occur when 
water content was 50 wt% even if the scanning speed was greater than 130 mm/s. On other hand, due 
to the high viscosity of the slurry, imperfect layer occurred when water content was less than 29 wt%. 
However, the advanced study revealed that the part could not be fabricated when water content was 
greater than 50 wt% even though the scanning speed was reduced to 60 mm/s and imperfect layer 
occurred when water content was less than 32 wt%. As a result, the water content should be within 32 
wt% ~ 50 wt%. However, sedimentation led to poor surface and poor binding of the layers when 
water content was too high. On the other hand, the increased shear force, which was increased with 
reducing of water content, between the scraper and gel-like layer damaged the part under constructing. 
Eventually, the final result showed that the parts fabricated with the slurry containing 34 wt% water 
and aforementioned scanning parameters could achieve the best surface and structure. Fig. 3

PVA幫助顆粒與顆粒之間的黏結才能進行鋪層。在鋪層過程中，溫度控制方面，
不能超過30度，以防薄層產生裂痕。減少含水量有助於增加鋪層厚度，以及鋪層
後之薄層表面並沒有氣孔產生，工件燒結最高溫度到1250℃，緻密度燒結前到
62%，燒結後到98%。

adding PVA into silica sol can enhence the connection between ceramic particles. During 
paving period, temperature should be controlled below 30  in order to preventing℃  
cracks. Decresing water content has advantage for increasing layer thickness. There is no 
babble on the surface of the green layers.The sintering temperature was 1250 . The℃  
density before sintering was 62%, after sintering was 98%.
