摘要 
歐盟於 2006年 7月公佈危害物質禁用指令(Restriction of Hazardous Substances Directive 
2002/95/EC, RoHS)。面對這波綠色風暴，我國政府早於 2005年由經濟部提出「寰淨計畫」，
協助產業鏈建置以中心廠為主，供應商為輔的綠色供應鏈電子化體系。 
然而政府與參與該計畫的企業大多著重在綠色供應鏈體系導入及資料傳遞等電子化技
術建置上，卻忽略了產品開發流程中前期的設計階段在綠色產品管理中的重要性。事實上，
根據 CIMdata研究報告指出，產品於研發時，若在前期的設計階段即早發現問題進行變更，
相較於在後期的量產製造階段進行變更所耗費的成本有極大之差異。因此，如果可以在產
品開發設計階段即管制綠色產品管理中與研發設計相關的作業，便可減少產品開發流程的
後期可能發生的設計變更，如零件物料的選用不符綠色環保標準所導致的不必要之變更。 
目前在國內產業已廣為應用在產品研發管理的 PDM系統，為企業內研發設計人員對
產品相關資訊相當重要的管理資訊系統，然而，市售的 PDM系統對於研發設計在綠色產品
管理上卻未提供例如管理多種法規的機制，零組件物料符合多法規的狀態管理，產品結構
對指定法規的驗證檢查等較完整的功能，導致企業無法在產品研發設計階段提早就綠色產
品加以管理。經本研究訪談企業後發現，目前產業為因應此現象，皆僅以綠色或非綠色零
件的二分法加以區分，而綠色零件則依最嚴格的法規限制為統一的標準，此種作法雖然提
供了簡單的管理方法，卻也造成零件成本較高與法規變動時很難區分零件符合哪一個法規
版本的問題，因此有必要在 PDM系統內提供較完整的綠色產品管理功能以利研發設計人員
可以在研發設計階段提早就綠色產品加以管理。 
有鑑於此，本研究 96 年度的計畫執行內容，主要的是提出了一套方法，透過流程分
析的手法，協助企業找出企業內的 PDM系統所需的綠色管理功能，以利研發設計人員可透
過 PDM系統在產品設計開發前端管理好綠色產品資訊。例如在產品開發時即挑選正確的物
料，便能減少在後期製造時可能發生的重工及變更的風險，減少變更所耗費的成本，進而
增加企業的競爭力。另外，為了驗證此流程分析手法所得到的資訊系統功能設計規格之可
行性，本研究也利用實際的 PDM系統，以 LCD-TV產品開發流程為例，實際建置這些功
能，以驗證其可行性及實用性。 
 
關鍵字: 綠色產品設計、產品結構、產品資料管理 
 
Abstract: 
OEM/ODM/EMS manufacturers are facing a complicated situation in the mixed production 
environment, in which green and non-green products are fabricated simultaneously. They are 
losing competitiveness as a downstream supplier due to lacking of a cost-effective approach to 
managing product variations compliant with different green directives. This research provides a 
methodology based on business process analysis method to find out the green product 
management requirement for needs of R&D people on new product development process. To 
validate the green product management requirement for use with R&D people, this research also 
implements the functional specifications analyzed by the above methodology within PDM system 
on LCD TV product development process.

 流程分析

 客戶/法規的綠色環保要求 
 綠色零件/物料管理 
 綠色製程 
 綠色供應鏈 
有了評估所需的綠色影響因子之後，即可針對產品研發作業流程進行分析與評估。圖

圖 2. LCD TV研發作業流程圖 
 
以前述的綠色影響因子評估圖 2的研發作業流程，可得到受影響的研發作業包括： 
 料件建立申請作業 
 料件承認作業 
 設計審查/驗證作業 
此處的料件包括零件、物料與製程，因為 LCD TV的研發部門以製程研發為主，因此
 經過上述的 IDEF 0分析後，可以獲得以下的主要功能需求： 
1. 產品必須可以因應不同客戶/法規的需求。 
2. 料件資訊必須具備綠色法規所規定的限用物質之各項物質與其含量資訊。 
3. 料件資訊必須記錄目前所符合的法規與符合狀態。 
4. 必須驗證整個產品的 BOM,對於指定的法規之符合狀態。 
5. 必須可以管理供應商所上傳的綠色承認資料，同時此承認資料必須與料件資訊關
聯。 
 
 功能規格制定 
此步驟的目的是以資訊系統建置為目標，將前一個步驟所分析出的功能需求，進一步

前述步驟所分析出的功能需求，在此步驟經過整理後，可以歸納為下列 PDM 功能規
格模組： 
1. 供應商綠色承認資料管理模組 
此模組主要的功能為供應商上傳之料件承認資訊可透過上傳資訊內容的剖析，進
而將承認內容中的限用物質含量，轉換為料件綠色資訊中的各項限用物質與其檢
驗出的含量。 
2. 法規資料庫管理模組 
為因應不同客戶或不同國家對限用物質的種類與含量有不同的規定，必須將各類
的法規與其對應的限用物質及其含量，逐筆詳細記錄，以便在料件申請與設計審
查/驗證時，可以明確記載當時所符合的法規與符合狀態。 
3. BOM驗證模組 
在設計審查/驗證的過程中，可以提供審查者快速驗證整個產品 BOM中所使用的
所有料件，是否符合指定的法規?

 Abstract - The automotive industry is like other globalization 
industries facing the challenges from the market maturity and 
high customer awareness. The vehicle makers in original 
equipment manufacturing environment are especially 
bottlenecked after World Trade Organization, a.k.a. WTO is 
getting more government agreements. Vehicle assembly thus 
becomes complex, more then 10 thousands parts are put into one 
vehicle, which results in the high inter-influence between the 
members in the supply chain. Therefore, the vehicle assemblers 
or vehicle makers would target for greater integration of their 
supply chains for cost-down and market share. 
To exchange messages in automotive industry more 
efficiently, governments or industrial organizations have adopted 
message platforms, such as Covisint® in North America, 
eSumtech® in Korea and Taiwan Automotive Network Exchange 
System., a.k.a. TANX®. Covisint®, which has GM, 
DamilerChrysler, Ford, Nissan and Mitsubishi as the center 
factories, now operates in 96 countries and has 30,000 company 
members. eSumtech® in Korea has Hyundai, DAEWOO and 
Saang Yong as the center factories. In Taiwan, TANX® has 
Taiwan Nissan, China Motor (Mitsubishi), Taiwan Sang Yang 
and Motor Power as the center factories. 
Unknown to most industrial practitioners, the construction 
of the collaborative model is crucial in the business process 
efficiency. Specifically, such efficiency refers to the processes of 
business communication. However, the abovementioned message 
platforms are merely assistant tools which can, to some limited 
extent, enhance the efficiency of business processes. Therefore, 
the efficient automobile manufacturing should depend on the 
collaborative model. 
This research applies the “Lean Production” concept to 
explicate the inadequacies of the current applied information 
exchange technique. In addition, the development of future 
automotive industrial information exchange platform from the 
perspective of Taiwan TANX® with the background of original 
equipment manufacturing environment is proposed. And the 
professional collaboration in automotive industry supply chain is 
analyzed here. We also argue that without reengineering the 
complexity of inter-business operation model by using 
information technology but adopting information technology on 
the current process, the expected result for enhancing the entire 
industry efficiency would not reveal. The research would propose 
the concepts and methods on strengthening the application of 
industrial information exchange platform by reengineering the 
supply chain business model, and eventually bringing a synergy 
of the entire supply chain.

I.  RESEARCH BACKGROUND AND PURPOSE 
A.    Research Background 
Automotive industry is highly capitalized and technical 
intensive. Making one vehicle requires over 10 thousands 
parts. The process combines materials of metal, plastic, textile, 
rubber, electronic, chemistry and machinery parts. For the 
huge amount of vehicle materials and parts, several industries 
are influenced by automotive industry such as metal, rubber, 
machinery, electronics, glass, and paint industry. The 
automotive industry supply chain structure is shown in Fig. 1. 
Fig. 1 illustrates the companies trading in the automotive 
industry interact as many-to-many. This makes the business 
communication difficult. A major vehicle maker or vehicle 
assembler is surrounded by several Tier 1 suppliers, which can 
be deemed as a center-satellite factory organization (C-S 
organization), and is followed by the Tier2 and Tier 3 factories 
and so on. The major vehicle brand or assemblers are the 
center factories. Then the Tier 1 suppliers would have to meet 
different requirements from the strong vehicle assemblers; 
meanwhile, the message requirements and the message 
contents. 
The globalization automotive industry is challenged by 
the shorten product lifecycle, diversified designs for less 
quantities, and localization. Therefore, the vehicle makers 
have put vast investment on the business innovation in order 
to survive. A good example is cost-down manufacturing and 
agility in operation while the market price competition is 
threatening them.

III.  SOLUTION 
In current practice, the industrial information exchange 
platform cannot effectively integrate the inter-business 
operation process so that the message standards cannot be 
adopted and the current operation model remains outdated. In 
order to solve the issues, by discussing the complexity of 
collaborative operation process, the condition of the 
diversified C-S organization operations, and the technology of 
defining process, we propose the concepts and methods of 
improving the industrial information exchange platform. 
A. The Complexity of Collaborative Operation Process 
The companies must co-work on the basis of 
synchronization engineering to shorten the time and enhance 
the operation efficiency in the highly competitive industrial 
environment [6]. Automotive industry serves as a good 
example in their interchange of data and information. In the 
current C-S organization, the vehicle assembler and supplier 
are highly inter-depending; the bundling contracts are 
commonly used for assurance of inter-business operation 
process integration. 
Fig. 2 shows the production model in the vehicle 
assembler manufacturing. The collaborative operation is 
complex so that it is necessary to build an information 
technology foundation and to construct the supply chain 
synchronic production model. 
As in Fig. 2, the vehicle assembler would provide to its 
suppliers with the production plan and would request them to 
deliver on the scheduled time. However, the vehicle assembler 
would avoid the early delivery of bulky parts that take up 
space of manufacturing floor because moving and logistics are 
costly. Those suppliers would be requested to deliver Just-in-
Time (JIT). The bulky parts are delivered more frequently and 
economically so as to spare the vehicle assembler the 
inventory and management costs. Furthermore, frequent 
delivery and small scaled quantity production will lead the 
continuous improvement and will enhance the entire product 
quality.

IV.  CONCLUSION AND SUGGESTION 
Industrial information exchange platform has reached its 
goal of information exchange standardization for the supply 
chain; however, how it can be adopted in the automotive 
industry to solve the issues of inter-business operation process 
integration is still on the way. 
In order to address and to provide solution for the 
application of industrial information exchange platform in 
automotive industry, the research discusses the complexity of 
collaboration operation process, the fact of heterogeneity of 
the C-S organizations, and the technology for defining the 
inter-business operating processes. The authors hope to 
address these issues and provide the concepts and methods to 
strengthen the industrial information exchange platform. Not 
only the current applied message content standards, but also 
the private business operation process for individual C-S 
organization must be provided. Thus the business process 
innovation needs can be met while the C-S organization 
operates. 
The research takes the concepts and goals industrial 
information exchange platform as the foundation of study. A 
vehicle assembler must consider its operation and competition 
need to develop its way of collaboration on the industrial 
information exchange platform. However, by using the 
exchange environment, the vehicle assembler manages not 
only the suppliers’ internal processes, but also reaches the goal 
of direct integration of inter-business operation process 
electronically. This would be a further issue for future study.
