英文摘要 
 
 The printed circuit board industry is heading toward the circuit design with fine line, multilayer, and high 
density in recent years. A Molded Interconnect Devices (MID) technology molds an injection molded 
thermoplastic part with 3D integrated circuit traces and allows for the integration of mechanical and electrical 
functions in the molded part. Nowadays, the 3D-MID processing technology including Two-shot molding, 
Laser Direct Structuring (LDS), In-Mold Decoration (IMD), 3D-Photoimaging, Hot Embossing and Capture 
Decal. LDS is one of the most industrially adaptive production methods for MID processing, but the apparatus 
cost is expensive. In addition, IMD processing also faces some challenges such ink washout, deformation of 
film, adhesion between the hot melt and plastic film. Therefore, in this study we will use IMD processing 
combine with 3D thermal forming and the Two-shot molding for MID molded part. The main purposes of this 
study discuss such: (1) electrical properties, (2) two-shot molding.  
 
 
Keywords : Molded Interconnect Devices (MID), In-Mold Decoration (IMD), Electrical specifications, Thin 
Film Deformation, Ink Washout

MID技術，從相關應用產品設計觀念、塑料選擇、模具設計、電路設計、與傳統射出成型比較，
其整合成型技術屬於高等級。由以台灣PCB板市場佔有率高，因此製程值得研究。電子產業工程師，
專長於電子功能研發，而對於結構設計、塑膠射出及模具設計則涉獵少。因而在設計上，採舊有方式
及思維，無法作大幅度變更。而今，當新製程技術開發後，如MID技術，則舊有設計將有所變化，因
此團隊合作能開創輕薄短小且環保的電子產品。採用MID技術，以射出成型技術直接以塑膠為基板，
並結合機械與電子功能，完成三維電路設計，亦即所謂3D-PCB設計，可得到下列優點:(1)可大量製作；
(2)可回收；(3)減少零件數；(4)減輕重量；(5)3D造型之生產技術。 
 
研究目的 
目前電子產品的設計製造慢慢朝向輕、薄、短、小趨勢，電路板也明顯朝細線路化、多層化發展，
在產品體積的控制上，則講求省空間及合理化的要求。在PCB市場，3D-MID技術能夠成型具有三維立
體網狀電路之PCB板，使其產品外型及功能更具有優越性，對於傳統PCB市場將會產生很大的影響，
並發現採用此技術將可節省20%至40%之加工成本。在國內業界或研究單位對3D-MID製程研究與技術
應用甚少，但随著國內汽車電子業和通訊電子業的發展，3D-MID的應用勢必將越來越廣泛。 
由於國內現今3D-MID常用加工技術有雙料射出成型、雷射切割加工、模內裝飾射出成型、照像印
刷成型、熱壓加工成型、顯像蝕刻等。目前最常見為雷射切割加工，但設備成本較高。模內裝飾射出
成型最常遇到問題有油墨沖刷、薄膜變形、塑膠與薄膜結合等問題。因此，本計畫將應用模內工程射
出成型技術，以導電油墨印刷在塑膠薄膜上，再經由真空成型加工使塑膠薄膜成為3D立體形狀，最後
置入模穴內做射出成型，以及探討雙料射出成型的相關技術。本研究主要在探討(1)印刷在塑膠薄膜上
的導電油墨經真空成型加工、射出成型加工後，其電氣特性的變化；(2)雙料射出成型的相關技術。

1. 薄膜模內射出成型

2. 雙料射出成型 
雙料射出成型實驗中，為了簡化研磨的工序，如何增加基材平整度與充分了解二射模穴深度與基
材厚度的密切關係，都是研究的重要課題。由一段射出實驗中，可發現適度增加保壓壓力可以減少收
縮的產生，主要收縮原因有兩個，第一表面結構物肉厚不均的因素產生中央凹陷收縮，第二壓降的因
素導致基材厚度與澆口距離成反比。實驗利用調整充填參數與降低基材厚度變異值。再進入二射充填
實驗之前，詳細探討二射模穴深度與基材厚度密切關係，詳細闡述厚度干涉值與渠道尺寸變化關係。

簡單電路板和防護性的外殼，從成本效益與創新的觀點，是具有強大的優勢。傳統 PCB 板主
要受限於二維的平面應用，然而 MID 可以使用於三維的曲面上，並且可依零件機械外殼設計，
進行電子線路的變更與改善，產品外觀更增添了許多設計自由度，結合這些觀點可有效節省

究中油墨添加不同比例導電材（銅粉、鐵粉、碳黑及銀漿）經網板印刷於 PC 薄膜，並比較不
同線寬與不同線長對電阻之影響，並完成模內裝飾射出成型的製程。其次藉由實驗室對微射
出成型技術了解，帶入兩段式之 MID 製程裡，規範出產品設計趨勢與規範。以利各產品製作。

the Composite Wind Turbine Blade

Wen-Hsiang Wu 
Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics

Abstract—The wind turbine blade sustains wind loadings 
during the operation and parking states. Due to the increasing 
size of the wind turbine blade, it is important to utilize the 
composite materials in a sufficient way to reach the material 
strength. Most of the composite blades are made of glass fibers 
composites while carbon fibers are also employed in recent years. 
Composite materials have the advantages of high specific 
strength and stress. This study develops a GUI interface to 
construct the blade model for the stress analysis using ANSYS. 
With the aid of visualization interface, the geometric model of the 
blade can be constructed by only a few data inputs. Based on the 
numerical stress analysis of the turbine blade, a simple iterative 
method was proposed to design the structure of the composite 
blade. 
Keywords—Wind turbine blade, stress analysis, structural 
design, composite blade 
I.  INTRODUCTION 
Glass fiber or carbon fiber reinforced polymer composites 
are currently the most popular materials used for the wind 
turbine blade due to their light weight and superior mechanical 
properties. A wind turbine blade may sustain various loading 
during the operation and parking states. Due to the increasing 
size of the composite wind turbine blade, it is an important 
issue to arrange the composite materials in a sufficient way to 
reach the optimal utilization of the material strength. The fiber 
direction and thickness distribution of the composite are two 
important design parameters in the turbine blade structure.  
The outer shape of a wind turbine blade is usually 
determined by the consideration of aerodynamic efficiency, and 
is not subjected to change in general. The structure of the 
composite blade is composed of the composite skins bonded 
together with a spar or foam core. The design problem thus 
becomes the determination of the fiber orientation and 
thickness distribution on the composite skin. There are many 
reports that address the design of composite plate with simple 
geometry. However, limited studies are reported in the 
structural design of composite with the complex blade 
geometry. Giguère [1] divided the blade into several sections 
and determined the stiffness of each section as the basis for 
material distribution. Tangle [2] pointed out that composite 
material could be used at the wind turbine blade to efficiently 
sustain the complex loading for increasing size of blade. Veers 
et al. [3] addresses the issues in design and fabrication of the 
wind turbine blade. Minimization of the blade weight was the 
design goal in their study. It was also reported that suitable 
design of the fiber directions and usage of unidirectional carbon 
fiber to replace the glass fiber can reduce the total weight of the 
turbine blade, while maintaining the same strength and stiffness 
[4]. Berry [5] introduced the fabrication processes of a large 
size wind turbine blade using mixed glass and carbon fibers to 
reduce the total weight.  
This study develops a GUI interface to construct the blade 
model for the stress analysis using ANSYS. With the aid of 
visualization interface, the geometric model of the blade can be 
constructed by only a few data inputs. Based on the numerical 
stress analysis of the turbine blade, a simple iterative method 
was proposed to determine the thickness distribution of the 
composite skin of the blade structure. The thickness 
distribution of the composite skin is selected based on the 
concept of uniform loading. In other word, with the applied 
wind loading during the parking state, the turbine blade will be 
designed to have the same safety factor in each station by 
applying the failure criterion of the maximum principal stress.  
II. CONSTRUCTION OF WIND TURBINE BLADE MODEL 
Stress analysis of the wind turbine blade must be 
conducted in order to determine the stress field for the failure 
criterion. A program with a graphical user interface based on 
Matlab is developed to facilitate the construction of the 
geometric model of the blade. The program will generate the 
geometry data that can be imported to the ANSYS for 
following finite element meshing and stress analysis. Figure 1 
shows the graphical interface of the program. With the input of 
airfoil geometry, skin thickness, chord length, pitch angle, 
distance to the center of each station, the blade geometry can 
be generated.  
The mechanical properties of the composite material, 
including Young’s modulus, Poisson ratio, fiber stacking 
layers and angles, can also be designated in the interface 
program. The geometric model can be output as a text file and 
imported to ANSYS. The final geometry of a sample turbine 
blade is as shown in Figure 2.

V. CONCLUSIONS 
A wind turbine blade geometry constructing interface is 
developed in the study to facilitate the stress analysis using 
ANSYS. Three different methods, BEM, FAST, CFX, are 
employed to estimate the wind load at the extreme wind speed 
while the blade is at the parking position. The BEM is shown 
to be an efficient method in calculations of the wind load with 
certain accuracy. Based on the maximum principal stress 
failure criterion, the composite blade skin can be determined 
under a specified safety factor. A uniform stress loading on the 
blade can be derived with this design methodology.
