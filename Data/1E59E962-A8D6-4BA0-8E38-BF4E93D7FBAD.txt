4.2 實驗儀器 
基本實驗架構是在光學隔震桌（DAEIL SYSTEMS）放置倒立式光學顯微鏡（Nikon EPIPHOT 
200），並將 Z軸移動載台（Z-axis translation stage, Sigma Koki SHOT-202）安置在倒立
式光學顯微鏡的平台上，利用 Z軸移動載台移動光學玻璃半球（Edmund Optics）。此外，在顯
微鏡平台上架設一個可量測施力的天平；使力傳遞於電子秤上（METTLER TOLEDO AB204），經
過一力傳遞係數的校正（詳見張智傑[8]），即可透過電子秤讀數的變化得知另一端施力之大
小。實驗過程中的影像則由安裝於倒立式顯微鏡的 CCD 數位相機（JENOPTIK GERMANY ProgRes○R  
C3）傳送至電腦，並利用視野放大鏡(Nikon)提升實驗觀察之範圍。Z軸移動載台的控制和電
子秤數據的讀取都以儀器控制軟體 Labview（National Instrument）做精密控制。

符合。若微柱深寬比相同時，實驗結果則是微柱排列面密度較高的試體呈現較高的有效彈性模
數E。 
而從數值分析結果亦發現，隨著微結構 /h d 值逐步增加， 0/E E 值會逐漸降低，整體勁
度S 也會隨之減少；隨著微結構面密度值逐步增加， 0/E E 值會逐漸提高，整體勁度S 也隨
之逐漸增加，隨著 /H R值逐步增加， 0/E E 值會漸漸降低，而整體勁度S 也隨之逐漸降低。

Introduction 
Fingering instabilities have been observed in 
experiments not only in viscous system but also in 
elastic layers. Shull et al [1] explored the fingering 
phenomena at the separation between a confined 
elastic layer and a spherical indenter. Similarly, the 
formation of stationary fingers with a well-defined 
spacing has been found in the experiments of Ghatak 
et al [2] with elastomeric films confined between a 
plane rigid glass and a curved glass plate. A related 
theory developed by Sheony and Sharma [3] is on the 
surface instability of a thin elastic film subjected to 
surface interaction from another surface in its vicinity. 
The purpose of this research is to investigate the 
stability condition of the perturbed front of a planar 
crack between two symmetric layers from the 
viewpoint of stress intensity factor. In this research, 
the stability condition of wavy crack front will be 
developed based on Rice’s theory [4] of first-order 
variation in elastic field due to variation in location 
of planar crack front.
