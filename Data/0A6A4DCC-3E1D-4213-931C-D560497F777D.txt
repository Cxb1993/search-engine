英文摘要 
Aromatherapy is a branch of complementary medicine developed based on the historical 
experience of using essential oils.  Application of essential oils has become ever more popular as 
these aromatic products are also widely found in miscellaneous goods adopted in daily activities.  
While beneficial properties of using various essential oils, either in pure or mixed form, have been 
claimed, few studies in literature have aimed to elucidate the specificity of the effects of essential 
oils and the mechanisms of their bioactivities.  With increasing use of essential oils and fragrant 
compounds, elevated exposure is likely to result in environmental impacts and health implications 
in decades to come. 
Aroma-therapists are the group of people with most frequent contact with essential oils.  
Moreover, aromatherapy often takes place in enclosed indoor environments with little ventilation 
where accumulation of emissions from essential oil can easily occur.  Our study, an occupational 
health investigation on aromatherapy clinics and their workers, therefore aimed to better (1) 
characterize the exposure profiles, working patterns, and general health complaints, and (2) 
determine the effects of aromatherapy procedures on general indoor air quality and potential 
secondary gas and particle phase pollutants with co-existence of oxidants in the air. 
The prevalence of palpitations (OR: 2.034; 95%CI: 1.186~3.518), vomit (OR: 2.396; 95%CI: 
1.419~4.086), poor appetite (OR: 3.347; 95%CI: 1.216~10.731), headache/migraine (OR: 1.898; 
95%CI: 1.129~3.212), unknown cause of being tired (OR: 5.010; 95%CI: 1.222~33.690) and 
dysmenorrheal (OR: 1.893; 95%CI: 1.091~3.313) is significantly higher in subjects of high 
exposure group than those in low.  There was no significant difference for other symptoms or 
diseases between these 2 exposure groups.  In addition, secondary pollutant levels appeared to 
always increase, even at low ozone levels during the aromatherapy.  The aroma-therapists reported 
worse health than the administrative personnel, presumably attributable to the different levels of 
exposure to emissions of essential oils.  Future investigation should design to further analyze, both 
qualitatively and quantitatively, the emission profiles and later to construct the parameters for 
biological monitoring and toxicological assessment. 
 
Key words: Aromatherapy, Essential oils, exposure assessment, volatile organic compound

三、文獻探討 
  一般大眾普遍認為精油的使用是具有放鬆、抒解壓力、抗菌等功效。且過去有文獻證實，
精油具有抗菌、鎮靜、幫助記憶、維持工作效率 (Khan et al., 2002; Shafi et al., 2002; Gerhard et 
al., 1993; Rasch et al., 2007; Sakamoto et al., 2005)等優點。然而，近年有研究指出，精油的使
用會對人體產生不好的影響，例如：皮膚炎、氣喘、鼻炎、痙攣 (Galdi et al., 2004; Woolf, 1999)
等疾病，且亦有部分的精油產品以註明孕婦不宜使用，詳細之健康效應如表一所示。因此，
精油的使用似乎存在著健康上的風險。

  比較過去關於精油分析的文獻，發現精油大多數的成分屬 terpene 類的物質，且 terpene
類物質因其含有雙鍵結構，故十分容易與強氧化劑 (如臭氧、氫氧自由基等)反應，而產生二
次氣狀與粒狀污染物(二次有機氣膠)。 
  二次有機氣膠 (Secondary Organic Aerosol, SOA)是大氣中具反應性的有機氣體 (Reactive 
organic gases, ROGs)之氧化產物。SOA 之生成過程，ROGs 必須先氧化成半揮發性有機物 
 
 4
(semi-volatile organic compound, SVOC)，因此空氣中必須存在強氧化劑 (如臭氧、氫氧自由基
等)，接著 SVOC 會分割而變成氣溶膠相 (aerosol phase)。SOA 形成的條件有二：(1) ROGs
之氧化過程必須快速；(2) 在低飽和蒸氣壓需有足夠半揮發性有機物之產生。若是 ROGs 氧
化過程太慢或飽和蒸氣壓太高就不會產生氣膠。芳香烴(Aromatics)為 SOA 最重要的前驅物 
(Grosjean and Seinfeld, 1989)，芳香烴如 terpene 類物質及 sesquiterpenes 類物質，此二者會造
成 SOA，且同時也為精油的主要組成份，也就是說，使用精油的過程中，若空氣存在強氧化
劑，極有可能會生成二次有機氣膠，使空氣中的微粒濃度上升。雖然二次氣膠在大氣中存在
已久，但 terpene 類化合物之氧化與行程 SOA 之機制仍然還有很多的未知數有待研究。 
  在一般常見的強氧化物中，臭氧為室內常見之污染物，其一般室內的濃度約為 20-40 ppb 
(Sabersky et al., 1973)，加上影印機、列印機、傳真機等事務機器的使用，也會使室內臭氧濃
度上升 (Weschler, 2000)。若於室內空間中使用精油，會產生大量的揮發性有機物 (Volatile 
organic compound, VOC)，這些 VOC 會與臭氧反應，生成二次污染物，其大多為甲醛與懸浮
微粒。此外，美國環保署及國際癌瘤協會 (IARC)已將甲醛列為〝人類可能之致癌物質〞，且
會造成眼睛、鼻子、喉嚨等器官發炎及造成喘鳴等症狀 (USEPA Health and Environmental 
Effects Profile for Formaldehyde, 1988; WHO. Environmental Health Criteria for Formaldehyde, 
1989)。懸浮微粒的部分，也有研究指出會對人體產生不好的健康效應 ((Pope et al. 2004; Pope 
and Dockery 2006)。綜合上述所言，使用精油所產生的 VOC 及其與臭氧反應所產生之二次污
染物，可能會造成人體健康危害，因此，高精油暴露族群之健康風險有其研究之必要性。
