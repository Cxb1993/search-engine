 
Yb:fiber laser is compact, light, high efficiency and 
good for heat dissipation. Even with high output 
power, it keeps the beam quality M2 close to 1. The 
line-width can be smaller than 0.1 nm, which is very 
suitable for blue laser generation by using frequency 
doubling technique. Although the emission spectrum of 
Yb:fiber covers from 900 nm to 1200 nm, but the 
shortest laser wavelength is 974 nm. While the 
wavelength is shorter than 976 nm, the emission cross 
section becomes smaller than the absorption cross 
section, and is closer to quasi-three-level laser 
scheme which causes serious re-absorption loss. This 
is why it is difficult to generate short wavelength 
Yb:fiber laser.  
 
In this plan, LAD software was used to simulate the 
Yb:fiber lasers, and 920~960 nm Yb:fiber lasers were 
simulated successfully. For example, while the 
reflectance of input FBG is 99.8% and the reflectance 
of output FBG is 90%. The 920~960 nm lasers were 
generated with proper fiber length. If 1W threshold 
was fixed, the slope efficiency were 80.1%, 57.8%, 
25.0%, and 1.25% for 960 nm, 950 nm, 940 nm and 920 
nm Yb:fiber lasers, respectively. We tried a lot of 
methods to solve the difficulties, including (1) 
pumping method, (2) fiber length, (3) FBG reflectance 
and (4) fiber cutting angle. A 960 nm Yb:fiber laser 
with 20.1% slope efficiency and 0.7 nm line-width was 
generated. The most interesting is that the slope 
efficiency increases with increasing temperature. 
This research result is published by IEEE Photonics 
Journal at December, 2012.

Keywords: Laser display, Laser projection, Yb:fiber, Fiber laser, Blue laser 
 
Energy saving and environment protection has attracted a lot of attention worldwide. Using red, 
green, and blue lasers instead of CCFL has been an important issue for display. So far, the wavelength of 
InGaN semiconductor blue laser is 400~420 nm, which is too short for display. The ZnO semiconductor 
blue laser suffers from the low output power and short lifetime. Therefore, the blue laser is nowadays the 
bottleneck of laser display development.  
 
Yb:fiber laser is compact, light, high efficiency and good for heat dissipation. Even with high output 
power, it keeps the beam quality M2 close to 1. The line-width can be smaller than 0.1 nm, which is very 
suitable for blue laser generation by using frequency doubling technique. Although the emission spectrum 
of Yb:fiber covers from 900 nm to 1200 nm, but the shortest laser wavelength is 974 nm. While the 
wavelength is shorter than 976 nm, the emission cross section becomes smaller than the absorption cross 
section, and is closer to quasi-three-level laser scheme which causes serious re-absorption loss. This is 
why it is difficult to generate short wavelength Yb:fiber laser.  
 
In this plan, LAD software was used to simulate the Yb:fiber lasers, and 920~960 nm Yb:fiber lasers 
were simulated successfully. For example, while the reflectance of input FBG is 99.8% and the reflectance 
of output FBG is 90%. The 920~960 nm lasers were generated with proper fiber length. If 1W threshold 
was fixed, the slope efficiency were 80.1%, 57.8%, 25.0%, and 1.25% for 960 nm, 950 nm, 940 nm and 
920 nm Yb:fiber lasers, respectively. We tried a lot of methods to solve the difficulties, including (1) 
pumping method, (2) fiber length, (3) FBG reflectance and (4) fiber cutting angle. A 960 nm Yb:fiber 
laser with 20.1% slope efficiency and 0.7 nm line-width was generated. The most interesting is that the 
slope efficiency increases with increasing temperature. This research result is published by IEEE 
Photonics Journal at December, 2012.

這次到韓國釜山參加研討會的感覺就非常不同，飛行時間短就輕鬆很多，七月份
去天氣也很好跟台灣差異較小；我最懷念的是韓國的美食，BEXCO 會議中心附近就是
號稱全韓國最大的新世界百貨，百貨地下室與地鐵相接十分便利，玲瑯滿目的飲食街
提供的食物大部分都很和我們的胃口，會議中心附近的一家烤肉店我更是光顧了兩次，
烤肉搭配的小菜超讚，韓國人跟台灣人一樣，天天喝咖啡，一點都不誇張，因為十家
店家裡面就有四家店是咖啡店，一出飯店旁邊就有五家咖啡廳看得我眼花撩亂不知該
挑哪一家，喝咖啡搭配鬆餅也是韓國的特色；韓國的地鐵也是出了名的階梯很多，因
為是早期蓋好的，地鐵挖很深光是爬樓梯也是一件不算小的勞動；另外讓我印象深刻
的是在韓國英文並不是很好使用的語言，就連在會議中心裡的行政人員，也是一副很
害怕聽到你講英文的樣子，十分逗趣；韓國人並不像我印象中的那樣兇悍，我遇見的
人都非常有禮貌而且很和善。 
 
OECC 與 CLEO 相較雖然規模較小，但是我認為也辦得非常好，討論的議題雖然
沒有 CLEO 涵蓋的那麼廣泛，但是更接近我的研究，例如第二天到第四天的議程，討
論的是 Fiber amplifier、Fiber sensors、Fiber lasers、Multi-core fibers、Fiber grating…，
對我而言都是非常重要的內容，有時候需要換會議廳的時候需要跑場，還好會議廳的
範圍不是太大跑場比較快速方便；另外讓我印象深刻的是研究進展有時候看似緩慢有
時候卻進展得十分快速，在我當學生時才開始興起的 crystal fiber 與 multi-core fiber，
經過三五年現在都已經發展得更加成熟，整個生長抽拉技術與應用性都已被開發，很
多場 crystal fiber、multi-core fiber 的演講都非常精采；光纖雷射相關的演講也很多，主
要是高功率雷射或是脈衝雷射方面的研究，讓我一邊聽演講也一邊激發靈感，獲益良
多。 
參與研討會是激發我做研究的靈感與提升研究動力的最佳方式，看到研究的進展
並學習新知識是一件令人歡欣鼓舞的事，再加上一杯咖啡就是人生五星級的享受了；
這五天的議程讓我感覺 OECC 是非常值得參加的研討會，我已經以此次豐富的內容鼓
勵我的研究生做研究並踴躍投稿，希望下次是我的研究生自己投稿，我能帶他們一起
參加會議。
