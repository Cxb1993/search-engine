中文摘要 
溶劑熱與水熱法成功製備低壓激發的奈米紅光螢光粉 SrTiO3: Pr, Al。在奈米晶種控制下，
熱退火後沒有明顯的晶體成長，溶劑熱法製備的螢光粉比水熱法製備者擁有更計量比與立
方性的 SrTiO3 結構，溶劑熱 200°C/24 h 製備的螢光粉具有約 50 nm 的立方晶大小。1000°C/1 
h 熱退火後，溶劑熱 200°C/24 h 製備的螢光粉之紅色陰極發光（CL）強度為水熱 250°C/24 
h 製備者之 9 倍。雖然溶劑熱製備的螢光粉比傳統固態氧化物製備者具有較低活化劑 Pr 與
增感劑 Al 含量，但可以在 1kV 低電壓激發下，得到波長 615 nm 的高陰極發光強度。晶種
控制的溶劑熱法結合熱退火可以製備奈米螢光粉，並能更均勻及有效率的低電壓激發分佈
在主晶格中的活化劑與增感劑，達到高發光強度。此外，微波煆燒 1000°C/10min 即可獲得
比傳統 1300°C /2h 煆燒所得螢光粉具有更高的 CL 發光強度。 
 
關鍵詞：SrTiO3 奈米螢光粉；陰極發光；水熱法；溶劑熱法；成核晶種 
 
英文摘要 
The low voltage excited nano-size of SrTiO3: Pr, Al red cathodoluminescent phosphors are 
successfully prepared by hydrothermal and solvothermal processes. Crystal size is without 
obviously growth after post-annealing process under the nucleation seeds control. The phosphors 
synthesized by the solvothermal process exhibit more stoichiometric and cubic SrTiO3 structure 
than the hydrothermal ones. SrTiO3: Pr, Al phosphors synthesized by the solvothermal process 
with 200°C/24 h possess a cubic crystal size of about 50 nm. The red cathodoluminescent 
intensity of 200°C/24 h-solvothermal phosphors is nine times of the 250°C/24 h-hydrothermal 
ones after 1000°C/1 h heat treatment. The stoichiometric SrTiO3: Pr, Al nanophosphors by 
solvothermal preparation with less amounts of Pr activator and Al sensitizer than by conventional 
solid-state preparation can achieve high cathodoluminescence at a wavelength of 615 nm exciting 
at a low accelerating voltage of 1 kV. The solvothermal combined with post-annealing process to 
synthesize nanophosphors proves to distribute and excite activator and sensitizer more 
homogeneously and efficiently in host structure of SrTiO3 so as to enhance luminescence. 
Microwave calcination also proves high CL intensity of separate fine phosphors to be obtained 
with low treated temperature and time of 1000°C /10min.  
 
Keywords: SrTiO3 nanophosphor; cathodoluminescence; hydrothermal; solvothermal; nucleation 
seeds

1999 年日本 S. Itoh [7]等人以固態反應法合成出 SrTiO3:Pr3+;Al3+螢光體粉末。指出當鈦
酸鍶螢光體因 Al3+或鎵 Ga3+的添加，大大提升了整體鈦酸鍶螢光體的紅光發光效率，約原
來的 200 倍。同年日本 S. Okamoto [8]等人，同樣以固態反應法合成出鈦酸鍶系粉末，主要
探討鈦酸鍶系紅光螢光體系統間能量如何傳遞以致於產生紅光發光之可能機制。在 Al3+添
加至 23 mol%時，為未添加時增加了 200 倍，且因鋁離子的添加在 617 nm 產生新的波峰，
為三價鐠離子 1D2Æ3H4 的電子遷移所產生。 
2000 年韓國 J.K. Park [9]等人，以 Dexter’s 理論探討固體發光材料中，發光中心間的
能量傳遞過程，證明系統能量傳遞從 Al3+至 Pr3+最接近的臨界距離(critical distance)為 17Å，
而 Pr3+至鍶離子及鈦離子最接近的臨界距離分別為 18Å 及 38Å。如果兩個中心間有近場力
的相互作用，一個在激發態的中心有可能把能量傳給另一個中心，而使前者於激發態回到
基態，後者以基態變為激發態，激子與其他中心之間通過再吸收、共振傳遞的機制釋放出
激發能，另一方面激子本身的運動，也會直接把它的激發能以晶體的一部份傳送到晶體的
另一部份，兩者於鈦酸鍶螢光體中，形成複雜能量傳遞機制而發光。 
2000 年日本 H. Yamamoto, S. Okamoto[10, 11]等人，分別發表相關的研究，主要的論
點有：（1）鐠離子與鋁離子於母體中，三者間的能量如何傳遞；（2）鍶離子與鈦離子的莫
耳比率不為 1.0 時，鋁離子於鈦酸鍶螢光體合成時對系統的影響。發現如鍶離子濃度高於
鈦離子，可觀察到 XRD 繞射線變寬，如圖 1 所示，其添加的鋁離子也必需隨之增加，若
是沒有，會影響結晶性，導致晶體之結晶特性變差，合成出之鈦酸鍶螢光體發光效率不佳。

添加的鋁離子對於鈦酸鍶系紅光螢光體的影響有下列幾項：可降低晶體缺陷，提高結
晶性。有助於鈦酸鍶母體與激活劑鐠離子間的能量傳遞，提升發光效率。 
同在 2000 年，韓國的 K.A. Hyeon [12]等人，發表有關添加不同計量比之鋰離子與鑭
離子，主要是兩者於鈦酸鍶系紅光螢光體的合成中，取代鍶離子的晶格位置，波長位於
611nm 處，同為鐠離子的 1D2Æ3H4 電子遷移所產生。2001 年韓國 S.H. Shin[13]等人，探討
改由摻雜鎵離子(Ga3+)於 SrTiO3：Pr3+。Pr3+於鈦酸鍶系螢光體中取代鍶離子後，多出一個
電子，Ga3+則從 Ti4+得到一個電子後形成一個電洞，當電子及電洞再結合時而發光。2003
年韓國 S.H. Shin[14]等人利用陰極發光(cathodoluminescence)測得之實驗結果，深入探討鈣
鈦礦的晶相結構與發光之關係，他們發現母體的晶體晶格參數 a=b=c 時，會放出藍-綠色
光，此為 Pr3+的 3P0Æ3H4 電子遷移所產生；如果母體的三結晶軸夾角 α=β=γ 時，則為 Pr3+
的 1D2Æ3H4 的電子遷移，放出的光即紅光。

四、研究方法 
本計畫選擇稀土元素鐠 (Pr)做為激活劑（ activator）；鋁 (Al)做為增感劑
(sensitizer)，將兩者摻雜於鈦酸鍶 (SrTiO3)母體晶格 (host)中，合成鈦酸鍶系紅
光螢光體。特別的是以奈米二氧化鈦（ ~20nm TiO2）為晶種，利用核殼成長
機制促進螢光體的成核與成長，並且藉以控制溶解析出作用，用以控制其晶
粒大小與結晶性。以水熱法及溶劑熱法兩種合成方法合成 SrTiO3:Pr, Al螢光體，
改變製程參數，尋求最佳合成條件，探討其核殼結構對螢光體成核 /成長之影
響，以及其晶形、晶粒大小、結晶性與顯微結構對發光強度與效率之關係。
使用傳統熱處理與微波煆燒製程，做為增進結晶性、修飾結晶缺陷，提升發
光效率之手段。藉由探討晶種協助成核 /成長與限制晶粒成長，微波快速處理
控制晶粒成長之機制，發展奈米級螢光粉體製作之另一環保節能創新路徑。  
 
五、結果與討論 
水熱法處理 200°C/3h 可得到 SrTiO3 (JCPDS 35-0734)結構，但仍有晶種 TiO2 的銳鈦礦
相存在。當水熱法處理 250°C/24h 則可得到完全純鈣鈦礦相，使 SrTiO3 螢光體的主體晶格
發展完全。為了移除水熱製程所可能導致的晶格缺陷及進一步改進結晶性與粉粒外觀形
狀，水熱所得粉體經500~1300°C/1h的退火熱處理，結晶性隨熱處理溫度升高而增加， 900°C
以上，結晶性增加更明顯，如圖 1 所示。螢光粉體發光強度與結晶性息息相關，較高熱處
理溫度的較高結晶度顯示較高的陰極發光（CL）強度，如圖 2，在 900°C 到 1000°C，CL
強度大大增加，但粉體晶粒尺寸並沒增加太多。當熱退火處理超過 1100°C，螢光粉體結晶
度與發光強度大大降低，已觀察到晶粒明顯成長及粉體顆粒表面形狀圓化現象。

表1與圖7也說明Sr/Ti需要計量比以發展充分的鈣鈦礦主晶格以及限制高溫熱退火的
晶粒成長，活化劑Pr的含量及晶格中存在有效的Al增感劑將決定熱退火後紅色CL的發光效
率。螢光粉含有更多活化劑Pr，將結合更多增感劑Al進入螢光粉中而增進CL的發光。當Al
加入SrTiO3: Pr中，藉由Al3+取代Ti4+，可以有效抑制晶格缺陷的產生。添加Al可以增進主晶
格到活化劑能量轉換的效率[11]。因此，計量比的主晶格ST及發展良好的晶粒大小，如溶
劑熱法200°C/24h繼而1000°C/1h熱退火條件，是奈米螢光粉在1kV低激發電壓，能獲得高CL
發光強度的基本要求。

七、計畫成果自評 
本計畫原規劃兩年，探討製程參數對螢光體成核成長之影響，以及其晶形、
晶粒大小、結晶性與顯微結構對發光強度與效率之關係。第一年計畫著重在核
殼結構與溶解析出成長的探討；第二年計畫則著重在限制奈米晶粒成長與成核成長機制
之探討。雖然研究申請僅通過一年計畫，在本計畫成果中，除了完成第一年計畫要達成之
目標外，第二年要進行的微波熱處理亦已提前進行，也有初步成果。接著希望對溶劑熱與
微波製程限制奈米晶粒成長與成核成長機制進行探討，但國科會計畫申請兩年經費，往往
只核定一年，使得研究的後續進行缺乏計畫資助而需捉襟見肘及人力安排的困難，這實在
是對深入研究探討者的一項痛苦指數。本計畫成果發表二篇 SCI 論文，二篇國際會議論文，

ABSTRACT 
CeO2 materials doped with the di- or tri-valent metals 
possess high oxide ionic conductivity at low temperature for 
potential electrolyte use in intermediate temperature solid oxide 
fuel cell (SOFC). However, multi-elements doped CeO2-based 
electrolyte, (La1-x-ySrxBay)0.175Ce0.825O2-δ (LSBC) in this work, 
with pure phase is difficultly synthesized at low calcination 
temperature. High sintering temperature, e.g. > 1500°C, is also 
needed in conventional mixed oxide method. In this work, 
nanoparticles less than 50nm of LSBC can be prepared by 
solution-evaporation method at constant temperature. Pure 
fluorite crystal structure can be obtained lower than 700°C. The 
optimal mole ratio of LSBC/citric acid in prepared solution is 
1/2 to achieve homogeneous composition and pure phase of 
LSBC. Small grain size of about 1μm average is observed for 
1300°C-microwave sintered LSBC by solution-evaporation 
method. The ionic conductivity of 1400°C-conventional 
sintered and 1300°C-microwave sintered LSBC prepared by 
solution-evaporation method is about 0.006 S/cm at 600°C but 
less than 0.004 S/cm at 600°C even for 1500°C-conventional 
sintered LSBC prepared by mixed oxide method.  
Keywords: CeO2, SOFC, solution-evaporation, ionic 
conductivity 
 
INTRODUCTION 
 Solid oxide fuel cell (SOFC) used in high power system 
possesses the highest efficiency of power conversion among 
various fuel cells. SOFC converts the chemical energy of fuels 
into electric energy directly. Generally, it operates in the range 
of 800-1000°C for zirconia-based electrolyte [1]. Lowering the 
operating temperature below 800°C (better below 600°C) 
facilitates the use of fuels of methane and butane without pre-
forming. The benefits for lower operating temperature include 
wide selection of the materials, long-time stability, interface 
matching of system and reduction of the manufacture and 
operation cost.  
One important bottle-neck for development of 
intermediate temperature solid oxide fuel cell (ITSOFC) is the 
low oxygen ionic conductivity of solid electrolyte at such a 
temperature, e.g. 600°C. Reduce the electrolyte thickness and 
use high ionic conductivity composition of materials are the 
studying target to ITSOFC. CeO2 materials doped with the di- 
or tri-valent elements possess high oxygen ionic conductivity 
for potential use as electrolyte at intermediate temperature [2, 
3]. Sm or La and alkaline earth co-doped CeO2-based 
electrolytes with high effective index have small micro-
domains around 1–3 nm in the microstructure. Then, the multi-
elements doped CeO2-based electrolyte improves oxygen ions 
passing through the oxide lattice [4].  
However, multi-elements doped CeO2-based electrolyte, 
(La0.75Sr0.2Ba0.05)0.175Ce0.825O1.891 (LSBC) in this work, with 
pure phase is difficultly synthesized at low calcination 
temperature. High sintering temperature, e.g. > 1500°C, is also 
needed in conventional mixed oxide method [5] to obtain pure 
fluorite structure and high density. Such high sintering 
temperatures may lead to the reduction of Ce4+ to Ce3+ and 
accompany the formation of oxygen vacancies [6]. The 
tendency of formation of oxygen vacancies retards 
densification and further lowers the mechanical stability of the 
material [7, 8].  
Generally, nanoparticles show higher catalytic activity, 
better sinterability, better conductivity and other unusual 
properties in contrast to bulk materials [9]. Improvement of 
ionic conductivity of solid electrolytes is expected upon 
reducing the average grain size of crystallites, if possibly in 
submicron range (0.1–1μm). The fine-grained solid electrolytes 
possess a developed intercrystalline boundary that is likely to 
substantially affect the charge transport [10]. Many chemical 
methods have been developed to synthesize CeO2-based

CONCLUSION 
The solid-state (SS) reaction of LSBC proceeded among 
350-1000°C. Solution-evaporation (SV) prepared powders can 
be decomposed completely up to 400°C. An endothermic peak 
higher than 1300°C existed for both SS and SV prepared 
powders. Pure phase of CeO2 fluorite structure of SS powders 
can not be obtained even at 1000°C/2h calcination. The pure 
CeO2 structure occurs as low as 600°C for SV powders. 
Homogeneous particle size is achieved smaller than 50nm after 
calcining SV prepared powders. Higher sintering temperatures 
enhanced Ba diffusion into fluorite structure of SS samples. 
The conductivities about 0.004 S/cm at 600°C were for various 
conventional-sintered SS samples and showed no difference in 
the range of intermediate operating temperature 400-600°C. 
The same conductivity as conventional-sintered was achieved 
by 1400°C/10min MW-sintering SS samples. Microwave 
sintering SS sample for ten minutes could get the same high 
conductivity as SV conventional-sintered bodies for same 
sintering temperature, e.g. 1400°C/6h. However, 
1300°C/15min MW-sintering SV samples can get the 
conductivity of 0.006 S/cm measured at 600°C. Rapid sintering 
of microwave led to stress-confinement in grains and fine 
microstructure about 1μm grain size.
