1.1 研究背景 
隨著二十一世紀的到來，人類因科技的不斷發展和創造而在物質生活上享有
前所未有的便利與舒適，但也因此加速了資源的消耗和環境上的破壞，其中以溫
室氣體排放所造成之氣候暖化與氣候變遷之衝擊最為廣泛與深遠（林唐裕、柯亮
群，2010），隸屬於聯合國的跨政府氣候變遷小組（Intergovernmental panel on 
climate change, IPCC）對地球之大氣層、冰川、海洋和冰帽歷時六年的研究，在
2007年公佈「IPCC Forth Assessment Report （AR4）」證實全球氣候暖化已是不
爭之事實。而在世界發展報告書 2010（World Development Report 2010）（圖 1-1）
可以得知十九世紀末以來大氣中 CO2 濃度已從約 280ppm 增至超過 380ppm；根
據 IPCC統計資料（Climate Change 2007 Synthesis Report）表示，在一百年內（1906
至 2005年）全球平均氣溫已上升 0.79°C。

1.4 研究限制與範圍 
由於各網站、企業和國家所發佈之EPD種類繁多，其揭露程度、描述方式、
採用方法學、標準和使用生命週期評估軟體亦不盡相同，故本研究採用日本產業
環境管理協會（Japan Environmental Management Association for Industry, JEMAI）
所發行之EPD，而為使其迴歸分析具有實質意義，故產品類別之EPD若不滿10篇
則不在研究範圍之內，而在回收效益之研究中則因某些類別之EDP並為揭露該方
面資料料易被排除在研究範圍外。 
 
1.5 論文架構 
本研究分為五大章節，其架構如下： 
第一章 緒論：敘述本研究之背景、動機、目的，最後是研究限制及架構。 
第二章 文獻回顧：首先介紹環境產品標製與宣告之種類和各國發展現況，接著
介紹碳足跡之定義及現況以及產品碳足跡及其計算方法、標準以及國
內外學者相關之學術著作，最後介紹。 
第三章 研究方法：首先說明研究架構、方法以及研究流程，接著介紹及瞭解統
計迴歸分析、Student化殘差之理論及方法，最後敘述。 
第四章 結果與討論：針對各種類別之產品Student化殘差後之EPD迴歸分析，包
括產品重量與碳足跡（包含生命週期五大階段）、硫足跡、能源足跡之
比較分析跡；產品總碳足跡、硫足跡與能源足跡之間之比較分析；產
品重量與單位重之CO2、SO2排放量與能源消耗之比較分析，並比較各
類產品之回收效益，最後以小結分析歸納得到初步結果。 
第五章 結論與建議：說明本研究知結論與期許，並提出後續研究之建議。

第三章 研究方法 
 
本研究主要是透過文收集 JEMAI所出版之 EDP相關資訊文獻，彙整出各類
產品，並加以統計分析，將可歸納出各類產品的規律性與方向性之趨勢，得以審
視各類別產品重量、單位重量和碳排放、硫排放與能源消耗之間的相關性，並透
過 SPSS統計軟體之殘差分析篩選樣本，使迴歸分析之方程式更為精準，藉以檢
視產品於生命週期各階段尚需加強減碳之階段，並比較各類產品回收之效益，期
望能作為國內企業產品減碳等各項議題時之參考依據。 
 
3.1 研究架構與流程 
本研究架構主要共分四階段說明（如圖 3-1），包含第一階段為「EPD 蒐集
與彙整」，此階段主要針對產品碳足跡之相關期刊論文及 JEMAI公告於網站上之
各類產品之 EDP 進行蒐集與彙整，以瞭解各類產品之資本資料與碳排放（碳足
跡）、硫排放（硫足跡）和能源消耗（能源足跡）。 
第二階段為「殘差分析」（residual analysis），此階段為依據第一階段所彙整
分析之資訊，應用 SPSS統計軟體對各類產品進行殘差分析，去除極端值，使產
品之重量、碳足跡、硫足跡和能源足跡特性和趨勢更為精確。 
第三階段為「迴歸分析」，此階段主要為針對文獻蒐集之產品環境宣告（EPD）
案例，進行迴歸分析，個別分析產品重量與碳足跡（包含生命週期五大階段）、
硫足跡、能源足跡之比較分析；產品總碳足跡、硫足跡與能源足跡之間之比較分
析；產品重量與單位重之 CO2、SO2排放量與能源消耗之比較分析，以得出各類
產品之排放特性與其趨勢。

3.2 殘差分析 
線性迴歸之統計前提假設，假如資料不符合其統計假設前提，則迴歸模式所
得之結果亦無意義，故檢定這些統計假設前提是非常重要的；由於是利用殘差進
行分析，故稱之為殘差分析。本研究採用 student 化殘差分析（studentized 
residuals）。 
Student化殘差是標準化殘差（standaridized residuals）的一種改進。標準化
殘差往往會忽略自變數之值與與預測值之間的誤差之間的關聯性，故 Student 化
殘差變是針對此問題所作的改進：在對原始殘差進行轉化之過程中，將每個原始
殘差與其平均數相減後再除上一個依賴樣本的自變數取值所計算而來的標準差

3.4 迴歸分析 
決策管理的應用上經常會需以兩個或兩個以上變數間的關係為基礎（陳光
華，1999；Chiang，2003），例如本研究之「產品重量」與「CO2排放量」，而在
許多情境下，不同變數之間，常會存在相互影響的關係（楊維寧，2007）。通常
會將較易測量或是掌控之變數稱為「自變數」（independent variable），而將另一
變數稱作「依變數」（dependent variable），以本研究為例，產品重量較 CO2排放
量更易取得其資料，故產品重量作為自變數，CO2排放量作為應變數（Montgomery 
and Runger，2002；Ross，2004）。

1. Introduction
With rapid growth of industry and commerce, highly toxic heavy metals or
compounds in the processes cause serious pollutions to natural water bodies,
deteriorate the quality of life of human beings and further jeopardize our health. As
the awareness of environmental protection is increasingly enhanced in Taiwan, laws
on emission standard of heavy metals in wastewater become more stringent.
Therefore, how to effectively control emission of pollutants becomes an urgent task.
Chromium is widely used in the industries, including electroplating, PCB, motor,
textile industry and special chemical industry. Currently most of treatments of
chromium wastewaters are to reduce Cr(VI) to be Cr(III) by the cementation method
for reducing the toxicity. However, there are still a lot of sludge after treatment which
may cause pollution of the environment mankind lives to some degree [1-3].
This study treats the Cr(VI) wastewaters by the batch photoreduction process.
Commercial P-25 TiO2 is used as the catalyst, with EDTA, NTA, Citric acid and
Oxalic Acid as the hole scavengers for capturing the valance band holes and
decreasing the possibility of electron-hole rebinding. The purpose is to reduce the
Cr(VI) wastewaters by the photocatalysis process. Quite many related work reports
that the influencing factors of UV/photocatalyst process include solution pH value,
photocatalyst dose, initial concentration of reactant, types and doses of scavengers etc.
As a result, these operating factors will be discussed in this paper to understand the
pollutant removal rate and the effects of reaction behaviors, put forward the possible
reaction kinetic equation to simulate kinetic behaviors of species in the reaction and
further assess the overall efficiency of the whole photoreduction process.
2. Experimental Instruments and Method
2.1 Experimental instruments
In this research, a typical annular UV photoreaction system is employed mainly
comprising thermostatic photoreactor, UV lamp tube, variable transformer, pH meter,
circulating thermostatic water tank, and electromagnetic stirrer etc. The photoreactor
is made entirely of Pyrex glass (outer diameter 6.50 cm) with an effective volume of
2.0 liter and consisted of one 2.15 cm inner diameter quartz tube housing a
UVP-XX-15S 254 nm or 365 nm low pressure mercury UV lamp with approximately
5.3 W maximum output. The UV irradiance of the UV lamp was adjusted by a
variable voltage transformer and detected by a Spectroline model DRC-100X digital
radiometer combined with radiation sensors. Temperature control was maintained for
the reaction by containing the entire apparatus setup in a constant temperature water
bath.

3.2 Treatment of Cr(VI) aqueous solution by UV/TiO2/EDTA process
3.2.1 Effects of solution pH value
In the UV/TiO2/Cr6+/EDTA system, the effect of different solution pH values on
the Cr(VI) removal rate is as shown in Figures 3 and 4. In the figures it can be
observed that the Cr(VI) removal rate is gradually declining with the increase of pH
value. When pH=3.0, the removal rate is about 80% after 60 minutes of reaction, but
when pH=11.0, the removal rate is just 7.5%. Different pH values will influence the
surface charge of TiO2 and Cr(VI). At high pH value, TiO2 surface has negative
charge which will not adsorb negative charge of Cr(VI); at low pH value, TiO2
surface has positive charge which adsorbs HCrO4- and Cr2O72- ions with negative
charge and the adsorption of Cr(VI) is possible. Further, in the reaction process,
solution pH value is slightly increased. This means the hydrogen ions will be
involved in the reduction of Cr(VI). Under acidic conditions, the lower the pH value
is, the higher the concentration of hydrogen ions is, thus facilitating the reaction; vice
versa. The reduction potentials of different solution pH values are also different. It
becomes large as the pH value is lowering, so the lower pH value has better reduction
capacity of electron and further promoting the reduction of Cr(VI).

3.2.3 Effect of initial concentration of pollutant

processes. Here the reduction efficiencies of different initial concentrations of Cr(VI)

4. Conclusions
Addition of EDTA may facilitate the photoreduction of Cr(VI) and EDTA may
effectively capture holes to avoid rebinding of electron and hole; in addition, the
adsorption of Cr(VI) on TiO2 surface can be increased. The effects of hole scavengers,
such as EDTA, NTA, Citric Acid and Oxalic Acid, on the Cr(VI) removal rate and
solid chromium generation rate are discussed. The experimental results show that
adding the above-mentioned scavengers can promote the photoreduction. EDTA as
hole scavenger may get the highest Cr(VI) removal rate, and NTA and Citric Acid
may get the highest generation rate of solid chromium. The reaction kinetic equations
of different types of hole scavengers are deduced respectively by the pseudo-first
order removal rate constant of Cr(VI), hole scavenger doses and changes of Cr(VI)
concentration. The values of Cr(VI) removal rate constant and reaction order are the
largest when adding EDTA as the hole scavenger. This results indicate that adding
EDTA as the hole scavenger has optimum promotion effect of Cr(VI) reduction
efficiency.
