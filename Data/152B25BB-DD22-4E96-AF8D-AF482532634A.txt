研究摘要(500 字以內)： 
破碎岩體之單封塞抽水試驗系統之研發的基本理論主要是利用封塞阻隔裂隙，量測其
水力特性求得地下岩體間之水力傳導係數，為了解破碎岩體裂隙構造之水力性質與台灣地
區岩體之透水係數隨深度的變化趨勢，有必要發展單封塞孔內抽水之儀器及技術。 
為為此目的，本研究設計、組裝與校正試驗儀器，其包括捲揚系統、抽水系統、攝影
和電阻測量系統以及擷取系統等四個子系統。同時，以金門花崗岩島所鑽探直徑為 3.5 英
寸鑽井孔進行測試。結果顯示，電阻測量技術不僅可以測量地下水水質，也可以用來評估
岩體 RQD 值。然而，因攝影光源設計不佳、光線不足，而不能有效評估岩體破裂。此外，
該系統設計開始時僅限於使用 NX 的鑽探，選抽水機抽水能量不足，使抽水效果受限。  
    總之，雖限於補助經費，缺失無法立即改善，但參與研究的學生和廠商均已學習到寶
貴的知識和技術，也累積了產品化的潛在能量。 
關鍵詞：水力傳導係數、裂隙岩體、單封塞抽水試驗。 
 
The basic theory of development and research for single packer pumping test in fracture rock 
mass is based on packer to prevent fracture and on measurement to obtain its hydraulic 
characteristics of the underground rock hydraulic conductivity. In order to understand the 
hydraulic characteristics of structure of the fracture rock and the relationship between the depth 
of water and permeability coefficient in Taiwan region, it need to develop a single-packer test 
system as the borehole pumping equipment and technology for investigation the hydraulic 
conductivity in rock mass. For the purpose, the project is applied. 
For the purpose, the instrument, included four subsystems such as the winch system, 
pumping system, photography and resistance measurement system as well as acquisition, was 
designed, constructed, and calibrated in this study.  A bore wells with the 3.5 inch diameter was 
tested on granite Islands in Kinmen. The results showed that resistance measurement techniques 
can not only measure groundwater quality, it can also used to assess the rock mass RQD value. 
However, the amount of photographic light source was designed is not good and insufficient light 
strength, it can not effectively measure get the value of rock rupture. In addition, the system 
design at the beginning limited to the use of NX drilling, selection of pumps energy is not enough 
to make the pumping effect is also limited. 
Anyway, although limited to the funds, missing not immediately improved, but 
participating in the study of the students and the manufacturers to learn valuable knowledge and 
technology, but also the cumulative product of potential energy. 
 
Keyword: hydraulic conductivity, fracture rock, single-packer test system.
