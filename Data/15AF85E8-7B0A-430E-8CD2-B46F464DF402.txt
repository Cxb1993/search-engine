摘   要 
    本計劃的重點是利用電泳披覆(EPD)製程應用於質子交換膜燃料電池(PEMFC)的領
域，使用奈米級 Pt/C 觸媒粉體，經電泳披覆製程製作觸媒層於碳電極表面，主要的研
究課題包含： (a) 選擇溶劑種類及比例，以調控 EPD 懸浮液中顆粒表面列塔(Zeta)電位
以達良好懸浮液穩定性，(b) 藉由 EPD 製程參數及不同電泳裝置之相對電極的設計與電
泳槽之非均勻電場的原則，控制披覆效率、披覆層完整性及觸媒粉體披覆均勻性，(c) 分
析不同之碳基材經電泳披覆觸媒層所得之碳電極之 PEMFC 的發電效率，(d) EPD 過程
中使用 Nafion 溶液，改善 PEMFC 發電效率。(e)以六氯鉑塩(H2PtCl6)為合成 PEMFC 之
奈米 Pt 觸媒前驅物，配合乙二醇(EG)為溶劑，以多壁型奈米碳管(MWNT)為觸媒載體，
製備 PEMFC 之 Pt 觸媒粉體。(f) 添加少量(5 wt%)疏水性表面改質劑如 Nafion 及 Teflon
於奈米活性碳顆粒(Vulcan XC-72)，製作 PEMFC 陰極中觸媒層與微孔層，並改善其疏
水性。(g)電泳披覆碳黑粉體於碳纖維布表面形成微孔層(MPL)，再利用二次電沉積技術
將奈米級 Pt/C 觸媒粉體沉積於薄層 MPL 表面製成所需之微孔層/觸媒層複合電極。 
當 EPD 懸浮液呈鹼性(pH＞10)時，奈米級 Pt/C 觸媒粉體之分散效果獲得改良，且
EPD 懸浮穩定性表現相當良好。吾人發現以碳布之發電效率最佳，因該基材電阻值最
低；反觀疏水性碳布則因含有 PTFE 處理，表面電阻值大幅提升而導致整體的發電效率
不彰。碳紙雖結構較不利流體之擴散，但具有高比表面積之特點，亦使得觸媒分布效果
頗佳，故能獲得滿意之發電效率。在電池壽命測試中，吾人以 EPD 製程所得之碳電極，
因 Pt/C 奈米觸媒粉體分布型態的影響，造成陰極疏水性質無法彰顯，發電效率在初期
有一明顯下降趨勢，直至電池內部有水的生成與排出兩者達到動態平衡時，則發電效率
始呈現穩定輸出。利用 EG 法還原之 Pt/MWNT 觸媒粉體，相較於以相同前驅物使用甲
醇為溶劑之合成反應製程，更能在 MWNT 奈米材料獲得分散較佳與密度較高之 Pt 顆
粒，優點為奈米觸媒之粒徑分布較集中。反應溶液中水分的含量與 H2PtCl6 鉑鹽添加速
度均會影響奈米鉑金屬顆粒粒徑。本實驗所合成之 Pt/MWNT 觸媒粉體不僅在於提升電
池的發電效能，加上配合利用電泳披覆製程，可以改善 PEMFC 陰極端的水氾濫問題。 
此外，添加少量 5 wt%之疏水性表面改質劑如 Nafion 及 Teflon 於奈米活性碳顆粒
(Vulcan XC-72)配方中，製作 PEMFC 陰極中觸媒層與碳纖維布氣體擴散層間之微孔層，
並進行其疏水性改質。實驗結果顯示經過 250℃/30 min 熱處理之 5 wt% Teflon 的微孔層
在電池電位 0.6V 有較高的電池效率 0.26 A/cm2。藉由電泳披覆機制將 Vulcan XC-72 活
性碳黑粉體沉積於碳纖維布表面形成微孔層(Microporous Layer; MPL)，再利用二次電沉
積技術將奈米級 Pt/C 觸媒粉體沉積於薄層 MPL 表面製成所需之微孔層/觸媒層複合電
極。使用高壓電電源供應系統提供電泳披覆製程中輸出電壓及披覆時間的即時監控，以
便於奈米活性碳及 Pt/C 粉體在碳纖維表面沉積出最佳化厚度之 MPL 薄層。

ABSTRACT 
The aim of this project was to employ the electrophoretic deposition (EPD) route on 
preparing the proton exchange membrane fuel cells (PEMFCs) by coating the Pt/C 
nanocatalysts on the carbon-based electrodes. In this study, there were several aspects 
including (a) analysis of solvent types and mixing ratios to attain good stability of EPD 
suspension by means of controlling the zeta potential of the nanoparticles within, (b) design 
of various of counter-electrodes with uneven electrical field to modify the EPD yield and 
deposition uniformity, (c) evaluation of power performance of PEMFCs regarding the use of 
different kinds of carbon-based electrodes, (d) use of Nafion solution to improve the 
electrical conduction and power performance of the fuel cell, (e) synthesis of Pt nanoparticles 
on carbon carriers or multi-walled carbon nanotubes (MWCNTs) by H2PtCl6 precursors and 
ethylene glycol solvent, (f) surface modification of carbon particles (Vulcan XC-72) with 
small amount of Teflon (5 wt%) to obtain hydrophobicity in order to prepare the microporous 
layers (MPLs) and catalyst layers (CLs), and (g) Preparation of MPL/CL composite on gas 
diffusion layer (GDL) by both EPD of nanoscaled carbon black and electrodepositon of Pt/C 
nanocatalysts.  
As EPD suspension was in the basic condition (pH > 10), the Pt/C nanocatalysts were 
dispersed smoothly in the EPD suspension with satisfactory stability. The power performance 
resulted from the carbon cloth electrode was much better due to the minimum electrical 
resistance of the electrode material. Whereas the hydrophobicity treated carbon cloth with 
Teflon coating had poor power performance owing to its increased surface impedance. 
Though the carbon paper had microstructure conflicting with good flowability, yet it had 
satisfactory power performance based on its high specific surface area. It was interesting to 
note that the carbon-based electrode with the Pt/C catalysts layer by EPD technique did not 
show the desired hydrophobicity to be in conjunction with initial fall-off of power efficiency 
until the dynamic equilibrium of water generation and removal was reached for the a stable 
power output.  
The multi-walled carbon nanotubes (MWNTs) were treated through a chemical 
oxidation by mixture of nitric acid and sulfuric acid. Nearly monodispersed nanosized 
platinum catalysts (2~3 nm) for PEMFCs were synthesized by the reduction of 
hexachloroplatinic (IV) acid or H2PtCl6 precursor with ethylene glycol (EG) solvent at 50℃, 
which were simultaneously distributed over the surface of the carriers of MWNTs. Regarding 
to the Pt nanocatalyst synthesis associated with the same H2PtCl6 precursors, the direct

2. 文 獻 探 討 
表 2 為燃料電池發展史，燃料電池的起源可追溯至十九世紀初的歐洲，燃料電池電
化學效應最早的發現者是瑞士 University of Basle 的理化教授 C. F. Schönbein，在 1838 
年 Schönbein 本人寫給友人的信中提到他所建立的實驗裝置可不像一般化學電池需要
進行化學充電，且能夠直接產生電流。次年他便將實驗結果公佈在  Philosophical 
Magazine 上，內容強調氫氣與白金電極上的氯氣或氧氣產生反應並能夠放出電流，
Schönbein 將此現象解釋為極化效應（Polarization Effect）。

電泳的披覆過程為利用分布於電極板間電場的影響，來驅動懸浮液中帶電顆粒朝
向與其相反電性的電極板方向泳動。因此，任何顆粒於溶劑中能被分散且其顆粒表面易
於攜帶電荷者，皆可利用電泳法披覆於具複雜形狀的導電性基材表面。電泳披覆法對於
製造精密陶瓷及電子陶瓷鍍膜是一種有效的技術，適用於披覆陶瓷生坏於導電基材表
面，且具有數優點：(1)製程簡單，(2)設備成本低，(3)可利用在製作披覆層於各種複雜
形狀之電極基材，(4)披覆厚度可獲精確控制，不須使用有機黏結劑或塑化劑之漿料配
方中，且生坏之孔隙較小有助於陶瓷坏體燒結性質的提高，(5)適用於披覆各種氧化物
及非氧化物披覆層或複合材料製作，(6)披覆速度較其他鍍膜方法(如 CVD、PVD)高。

4. 研 究 方 法 
本實驗中應用電泳披覆法(EPD)製作奈米 Pt/C 觸媒薄層，並以多次複合電泳披覆
方式，其中包括調製出具高穩定性質及高披覆效率之 Pt/C 觸媒粉體電泳懸浮液，經由
實驗結果以取得最佳之懸浮液濃度、披覆時間、基板面積、基板形狀、外加電場等製程
條件。 另外，於有機溶劑系之 EPD 懸浮液中加入 Nafion 溶液、pH 值調整劑，如氨水
(NH4OH)和醋酸(Acetic Acid；CH3COOH)等添加劑，以供進一步觀察探討電泳披覆行為
及披覆所製作之質子交換膜燃料電池(PEMFC)之中碳電極 Pt/C 觸媒層的顯微結構和電
氣特性。本實驗所使用之即時高電場調控 EPD 技術製作奈米 Pt/C 觸媒碳電極流程陳列

吾人發現某些披覆層顯微結構形成為一多層殼狀結構，並發生了斷裂剝落的現象。吾人

阻值隨而升高，導致粉體受到之電泳驅動力趨於微弱，使得最上層之披覆結構顯得鬆
散，其粉體間的附著力不足以承受觸媒層之重量負荷，故產生剝落之缺陷行為。

6. 結 論 
本計劃的目的是利用電泳披覆(EPD)製程應用於質子交換膜燃料電池(PEMFC)的
領域。使用奈米 Pt/C 觸媒粉體，經電泳披覆製程製作觸媒層於碳電極表面。重點為下
列諸課題：(a) 選擇不同溶劑種類及混合比例、改變懸浮液酸鹼度值，探討對懸浮液之
穩定性及分散性狀況，(b) 藉由改變 EPD 製程參數探討對披覆效率、披覆層完整性及
觸媒粉體披覆均勻性的影響。(c) 比較已披覆觸媒層之不同碳基材對發電效率的影響。
(d) 在懸浮液中加入不同比例之 Nafion 溶液，探討 Nafion 含量對發電效率的影響。(e) 選
擇水系溶劑混合最佳比例之觸媒及離子聚合物，改變懸浮液酸鹼度值，探討對懸浮液之
穩定性及分散性狀況；(f) 比較並探討 EPD 製程與傳統熱壓製程製備的膜極組(MEA)
之發電效率優劣；(g)水分的濃度與前驅物添加速度對 Pt 奈米觸媒粒徑之影響(h)商用
E−TEK 20 wt% Pt/C 與本實驗製備之 18.6 wt% Pt/CNT 之發電效能比較。吾人在電泳披
覆奈米 Pt/C 觸媒製程中加入奈米碳管，已成功獲得交錯排列的奈米碳管於 Pt/C 觸媒粉
體之間形成之 3D 結構，藉此奈米級 Pt/C 觸媒粉體得以更有效的分散均勻，且奈米碳管
亦能提供更豐富的觸媒粉體與碳電極之間電子之傳導路徑。依上述各點，吾人有信心預
期以電泳披覆製程披覆奈米碳管觸媒粉體，將可對燃料電池之發電效率能有令人滿意的
進展，吾人以合成之 Pt/CNT 觸媒粉體與商用 E−TEK Pt/C 觸媒粉體做一電池效能比較，
從 SEM 顯微圖像證實 Pt/CNT 觸媒電極的孔隙率較 Pt/C 觸媒粉體電極來得高，使得 Pt
觸媒使用率獲得提升，換言之，電極反應三相點增加，電池效能亦隨之提升。茲將本研
究工作所獲之重要實驗結果彙整並做出如下之結論： 
(1) 以異丙醇(IPA)為溶劑配製奈米 Pt/C 觸媒粉體懸浮液，並調整其 pH 值為 10 時，粉
體表面 Zeta 電位值為-36.54 mV，粉體之分散效果頗佳且懸浮穩定性亦良好。當奈
米 Pt/C 觸媒粉體懸浮液偏向酸(pH = 5~9)時，因懸浮液中粉體表面吸附之 OH–離子
較少，電雙層厚度變薄，粉體凝聚情形變得嚴重且分散性不理想。 
(2) 在電場強度 500 V/cm，披覆時間 60 sec 的工作條件下，進行奈米 Pt/C 觸媒粉體的
披覆製程，可得到一均勻包覆於碳纖維表面之鍍層，其中又以環式電泳裝置具有非
均勻電場，使得粉體具有一側向的泳動路徑，故所得之奈米 Pt/C 觸媒粉體鍍層均勻
度及表面平整度較佳，本製程可獲得 0.16 mg/cm Pt，且無多層結構剝落等現象之鍍
層。

7. 計 畫 成 果 自 評 
本研究計劃已成功將電泳披覆(EPD)製程應用於質子交換膜燃料電池(PEMFC)之
膜極組製備並在研究的過程中發現不同溶劑 pH 值對電泳披覆製程有重大的影響，以異
丙醇(IPA)為溶劑配製奈米 Pt/C 觸媒粉體懸浮液，並調整其 pH 值為 10 時，粉體之分散
效果頗佳且懸浮穩定性亦良好。為了使奈米觸媒粉體有更好的分散性及懸浮效果我們可
以添加部份的離子聚合物來配製奈米 Pt/C 觸媒粉體懸浮液。吾人在懸浮液中加入不同
比例之 Nafion 溶液，所得之結果以 NFP = 40 wt%時具有最佳之發電效率，當溶劑中離
子聚合物為 23.2 wt%固含量時可獲得最佳的觸媒電化學活化面積(EAS)。同時，吾人也
發現電場環境對電泳披覆奈米粉體之過程中有一定程度的影響，當電場強度 500 V/cm，
披覆時間 60 sec 的工作條件下，進行奈米 Pt/C 觸媒粉體的披覆製程，可得到一均勻包
覆於碳纖維表面之鍍層，其中又以環式電泳裝置具有非均勻電場，故所得之奈米 Pt/C
觸媒粉體鍍層均勻度及表面平整度較佳，本製程可獲得 0.16 mg/cm-Pt，且無多層結構
剝落等現象之鍍層。燃料電池膜極組中有許多可用來製作為氣體擴散層之材料，其中可
分為碳紙及碳布兩種，又以碳布基材之發電效率為最佳(81.9 mW/cm2)。 
吾人對於觸媒合成也有重大發現，當反應溶劑中去離子水的含量從 0−30 vol%將造
成 Pt 奈米觸媒粒徑分佈由 2.4~4.2 nm，此現象顯示反應溶劑的些許變化將造成奈米觸
媒粉體在粒徑上的重大影響。本研究除了在電泳披覆製程及奈米觸媒粉體合成有重大成
就外，在燃料電池膜極组中的氣體擴散層元件也有驚人的發現。吾人在氣體擴散層之奈
米活性碳微孔層內部添加表面改質劑由於熱處理所造成高分子黏性流動，而改變其分佈
型態與組織，使顯出微孔層的親/疏水性差異。其中，含 5 wt% Nafion 高分子表面改質
劑之微孔層在 150~250℃熱處理條件下均保持不錯的疏水性，而含 5 wt% Teflon 改質劑
者，則需提高熱處理溫度 250℃方可些微提升其疏水性。吾人藉由電泳披覆製程成功製
備微孔層/觸媒層複合結構，根據實驗結果所得之氧化還原機制差異，我們建議以單層
微孔層/觸媒層複合結構製備 PEMFC 用膜極組，並於 DMFC 搭配使用雙層複合結構。

by Electrophoretic Deposition of Pt/MWNT Nanocatalysts 
 
Rong-Fuh Louh and Felix Tsai

The multi-walled carbon nanotubes (MWNTs) were treated through a 
chemical oxidation by mixture of nitric acid and sulfuric acid. Nearly 
monodispersed nanosized platinum catalysts (2−3 nm) for PEM fuel 
cells were synthesized by the reduction of hexachloroplatinic acid 
precursor with ethylene glycol solvent, which were simultaneously 
distributed over the surface of MWNT carriers. In the preparation of 
Pt/MWNT nanocatalysts, ethylene glycol solvent, as compared to 
formaldehyde, was an ideal chemical to obtain improved dispersivity, 
narrower particle size distribution, and platinum loading capability on 
MWNT carriers (18.6 wt% Pt on MWNT). The evenly distributed 
loading of Pt/MWNT nanocatalysts on the carbon-based gas diffusion 
layers (GDLs) for the PEM fuel cells was carried out via the 
electrophoretic deposition (EPD). The cell performance of PEMFC 
with both synthesized Pt/MWNT and Pt/C (E-TEK, XC-72) 
nanocatalysts and the influence of hydrophobicity by using MWNTs 
and carbon black carriers for the Pt nanoparticles on GDLs were 
evaluated by cyclic voltammetry and contact angle measurement, 
respectively. The power density (mW/cm2) of PEMFC fabricated by 
electrophoretic deposition of Pt/MWNT nanocatalysts was increased 
by ~36% as compared to the same Pt loading (0.16 mg-Pt/cm2) of Pt/C 
nanocatalysts.

Conclusion 
 
Existence of well-distributed sites of functional groups on the MWNTs is an essential 
element for achieving a decent dispersion of Pt particles from the direct reduction of H2PtCl6 
precursor. Since there is a discrepancy in the effect of surface activation of MWNTs by 
merely using sulfuric acid or nitric acid, the surface treatment of carbon nanotubes using 
mixed acidic solution would offer better results. The higher H2PtCl6 precursor concentration 
renders a relatively larger particle size (2−10 nm) and agglomeration due to the highly 
localized self-growth of Pt particles after nucleation, which is the similar result when using 
greater water content in EG solution. The electrophoretic deposition (EPD) technique has 
been demonstrated as a simple and effective measure for obtaining a uniform distribution of 
Pt/MWNT and/or Pt/C nanocatalysts on carbon-base electrodes for manufacturing 
cost-effectiveness of PEM fuel cells. For same power performance level for PEM fuel cells, 
the EPD process can save more than 50% of Pt loading per unit area of electrodes (0.16 
mg/cm2), as compared to conventional screen printing (0.4 mg/cm2). Cyclic voltammetry test 
of half-cell samples is an alternative for us to grasp a quick idea on how the catalyst 
utilization, oxygen reduction reaction (ORR), impedance analysis of electrodes, and 
evaluation of electrochemical active surface (EAS) of Pt catalysts behave rather than the 
strenuous work to assemble the complete fuel cells.

孔層/觸媒層複合電極。 
技術說明 
英文：The key of this technique is to employ the electrophoretic deposition (EPD) route on 
preparing the proton exchange membrane fuel cells (PEMFCs) and direct methanol 
fuel cell (DMFC) by coating the Pt/C or Pt/MWNTs nanocatalysts on the 
carbon-based electrodes. In brief, this technique has several aspects including (a) 
analysis of solvent types and mixing ratios to attain good stability of EPD 
suspension by means of controlling the zeta potential of the nanoparticles within, (b) 
design of various of counter-electrodes with uneven electrical field to modify the 
EPD yield and deposition uniformity, (c) use of Nafion solution to improve the 
electrical conduction and power performance of the fuel cell, (d) synthesis of Pt 
nanoparticles on carbon carriers or multi-walled carbon nanotubes (MWNTs) by 
H2PtCl6 precursors and ethylene glycol solvent, (e) surface modification of carbon 
particles with small amount of Teflon (5 wt%) to obtain hydrophobicity in order to 
prepare the microporous layers (MPLs) and catalyst layers (CLs), and (f) 
Preparation of MPL/CL composite on gas diffusion layer (GDL) by both EPD of 
nanoscaled carbon black and electrodepositon of Pt/C nanocatalysts.
