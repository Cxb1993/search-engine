Abstract 
In general, multi-junction Silicon thin film solar cells use Plasma Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition 
(PECVD) in deposition of various interfacial structures on Amorphous silicon (a-Si) thin film. However, 
PECVD has generated a lot of ion bombardments on interface of the film and then needs to follow by various 
process treatments to remove these interfacial defects caused by ion bombardments. This project will 
incorporate with Optical Science Center of NCU in developing Electron cyclotron resonance and inductively 
coupled plasma chemical vapor deposition (ECR/ICP CVD) to replace PECVD for improvement of silicon 
thin film processes. In order to have a technology breakthrough in processing a low cost with high efficiency 
solar cell, a subsystem by reduction of using imported key components for ECR/ICP CVD will be developed. 
This subsystem is to carry out a plasma reaction mechanism study. The subsystem will include a precision 
control of heating module, two dimensional with innovative multi-parallel detections of plasma Langmuir 
probes and an integrated optical emission spectroscopy (OES). The electron temperature (Te) and plasma 
density (Ne) are determined by Langmuir probe and the relative concentrations of plasma species are obtained 
from OES. Results indicate that high density plasma can be obtained by increasing ECR microwave power 
even if the plasma density is near to the theoretical cutoff value. As the result of substrate heated by high 
concentration of electrons, the hydrogen precipitation occurs and hydrogen content is decreased by 46%. The 
result of Langmuir probe shows that Te is rarely influenced by microwave power. When the hydrogen dilution 
ratio (H2/SiH4) increases, the Ne increases. This study demonstrated that the method for integrating in-situ 
Langmuir probe and OES can receive more reliable plasma characteristics in CVD process.

(c)氫稀釋比對電漿與薄膜特性之影響 
圖十四及圖十五為探針量得之電子密度與電子溫度，電子密度隨著氫氣分壓的增加，而呈現上升
的趨勢；電子溫度則不受氫稀釋率影響，此現象亦可由圖十六的 OES 光譜中 Hβ/Hα趨勢佐證。吾人
推斷，這是由於 H 的電離係數(ionization coefficients)大於 SiH4，故在相同的能量下，電子密度將隨著
氫氣分壓升高而上升。該文獻中同樣指出，混合氣體的電離係數無法直接由氣體混合比例計算。因此
本研究證明，過去 Langmuir probe 相關研究中採用純氫氣或者氬氣做為製程中電漿參數指標的方式顯
然有誤，需以如同本研究中直接以實際製程的氣體比利量測，始能得到較為可信的結果。 
由圖十七 OES 來觀看，Si*、SiH*隨 SiH4分壓減少而等比例下降，而在氫氣分壓比例達 50%之前，
由 Hα、Hβ對 Ar 的比值，可得知氫自由基於電漿中的相對濃度並不受混氣比而有明顯變化。圖十七同
樣可觀察到，當氫氣分壓比例高於 50%後，氫自由基之比例則大幅上升。我們推斷，其原因係因在高
SiH4 情況下，當電漿物種由共振區擴散至基板的過程中，會發生如下式，將解離之氫原子還原為氫分
子的反應 
SiH4+H→ SiH3+H2 
此類反應之速率，遠高於氣態氫原子相互反應或與腔壁上氫原子的表面化合反應。因此，在高 SiH4 環
境下，提升氫氣分壓亦不會顯著改變氫自由基的相對濃度。

Experiment 
 
The experimental setup is schematically shown in Fig 1. The 2.45GHz microwave power 
is TE mode and output power ranges from 200W to 2kW. Microwaves are radiated into a 
cylindrical plasma chamber (D200mm*H200mm) across a quartz glass plate 90mm in 
diameter and 15mm in thickness. The distance from quartz window to substrate surface is 
425mm. Argon, silane and hydrogen are used as working gases. We investigate the gas 
interaction and their dissociation energy, so the hydrogen and argon gases are injected 
into the plasma chamber from upstream.  Downstream gas is for SiH4. The OES is 
installed outside the process chamber. The fiber angle can be adjusted by rotating 
platform adapter to maximize the signals from emission spectroscopy. 
The deposition of a-Si thin films is under different magnetic field combinations. 
Aside from the resonant main coils, we installed a sub-magnetic (auxiliary) field for inner 
and outer coils under ECR-CVD process chamber. By modulating resonant main coil 
current, we can create ECR plasma in expected loction. The relationship between main 
coli current and plasma location are shown in Fig 2

Conclusion 
 
The OES is a real time and non-intrusive diagnosis tool, which can measure the 
atomic, diatomic molecule, and free radicals emission spectra in plasma. Because of the 
high chemical activity of free radicals, it plays an important role with intermediate forms 
in chemical reactions, so the OES diagnosis technology would provide key information to 
resolve the mechanism of ECR silicon thin film deposition. In ECR-CVD process, the 
microwave power enhances almost entirely dissociation and ionization but not electron 
temperature. The electron temperature drops greatly from 3 mTorr to 6 mTorr. After 6 
mTorr, the reduction of spectra intensity is dependent on electron density. When the 
working pressure increases six times, Ar* spectrum intensity decreases 90%. The ECR-
CVD has no surface-reaction limited regime however it is limited by mass transfer. In
