一、中文摘要 
近年來發展迅速的創新構思問題解決法
(TRIZ)為一系統化創新性問題解決理論，其技
術方法中的矛盾矩陣(Contradiction matrix)廣
泛使用在取捨問題(Trade-off)解決，能協助發
明者快速找到更佳的研發創新思考途徑，最後
依照途徑探訪欲了解的專利資訊；目前的中華
民國專利仍以國際專利分類為主，使用產品別
與產業技術別做為分類依據，並無法直接提供
TRIZ 使用者與創新研發者使用。因此，本研
究計畫第一年將使用國際專利分類為 A 部之
“生活必需品”之 1000 筆中華民國專利為標
的，以文字探勘技術(Text mining)為主要手
法，針對專利文獻內容之專利名稱與摘要進行
TRIZ 40 創新性原則映射分類。首先，本研究
計畫第一年將利用中研院線上斷詞系統將資
料文句斷成數個字詞，並以人工方式加以適度
標籤，再經由程式計算字詞頻率(TF)與反轉文
件頻率(IDF)，且透過卡方統計與相關係數文
字特徵擷取方法，篩選並排序與各 TRIZ 創新
性原則具強關聯性之相關字詞，接著計算這些
字詞之字詞頻率反轉文件頻率(TFIDF)與權重
-字詞頻率反轉文件頻率(w-TFIDF)值，藉以作
為本研究計畫第二年支持向量機(SVM)與倒
傳遞類神經網路(BPN)分類器之幾何輸入向
量，進行專利檢索於 TRIZ40 個創新性原則。 
關鍵詞：創新構思問題解決法(TRIZ)；40 創
新性原則; 文字探勘；中華民國專
利分類與檢索；卡方統計;相關係數; 
頻率反轉文件頻率 
 
Abstract 
In recent years, TRIZ have been 
developed rapidly and known as a useful tool 
to solve the contradictions on engineering 
problems. The aim of this research is to map 
R.O.C patents into TRIZ applications by 
using text mining technique for further 
innovative product concepts and solutions of 
conflicts. In the first year, using on-line 
auto-tag system provided by Academia Sinica 
to break every sentence in a document into 
several keywords and label these keywords 
manually. Calculating text frequency (TF) and 
inverse document frequency (IDF) in the 
corresponding document. Then, chi-square 
statistics and correlation coefficient approaches 
are used to select and sort word features which 
are highly correlated to 40 TRIZ innovative 
principles. Then, calculating the TFIDF and 
weight-TFIDF values for selected words, these 
values are inputs for further classifiers such as 
support vector machine (SVM) and BPN in the 
second year project This research would like to 
significantly help R&D personnel to solve 
difficult trade-off contradictions by fast 
searching the correlation between TRIZ and 
existing R.O.C patents, which could reduce 
time-consuming for creative thinking and 
time-spent for new product introduction. 
Keywords：TRIZ; 40 Innovative principles; 
Text Mining; Patent Classification 
and Mapping; R.O.C patents; 
TFIDF; Weight-TFIDF

三、研究方法 
創新問題解決理論(TRIZ)，源自蘇俄工程
師兼發明家 Genrich Altshuller (1926-1998)與
他的團隊於 1946 年從事專利文件分析所發展
而成的一套創新理論，Altshuller 生於 1926 年
10 月，14 歲即獲得他第一個蘇聯專利，20 多
歲時開始發展 TRIZ，譽為 TRIZ 之父。將其
原俄文：теории решения изобретательских 
задач 縮寫「ТРИЗ」， 翻譯為「解決發明家
任務的理論」；用英語標音可讀為 Teoriya 
Resheniya Izobreatatelskikh Zadatch，TRIZ；
英 文 說 法 ： Theory of Inventive Problem 
Solving，TIPS）。在研究之初，Altshuller 的
假設為「存在一些萬用發明原則，可作為發
明創新高級新技術之基礎，假使這些發明原
則能加以確認與整理，並用來教導從事發明
者，則其發明過程更可預期。TRIZ 系統化創
新流程，即透過某事物需要被改善的認知
時，以 TRIZ 為工具或方法進行問題之最適解
決方法探索，其主要由定義問題、選擇工具、
解決問題、評估四個步驟來進行。而在第二

字詞集與主要特徵 
    由於專利文獻屬非結構化資料，因此在進
行專利映射 TRIZ 創新性原則分類前，須篩選
合適的字詞集與字詞特徵以利文字探勘工作
之執行。其中，字詞集的決定一般經由語意分
析方法進行，將文章斷成數個字詞，並標示各
字詞之詞性(如附錄 B 所示)；而主要特徵以文
件分類文獻中提及之卡方統計與相關係數方
式進行，可藉此篩選出各類別中較具相關性之
字詞，以利有效減低文字探勘程式運行之複雜
性。 
自動斷詞 
    依據文字探勘流程中所述，字詞集的決定
一般經由語意分析方法進行，將文章斷成數個
字詞並標示各字詞之詞性，依此以利分析工作
進行，而由於語意分析(斷詞規則與詞性區辨
等)工作繁複且非本研究計畫研究重點所在，
故本研究計畫實驗中，採用中央研究院所開發
之自動斷詞工具(Auto tag tools) (如下圖三與
圖四所示，分別顯示輸入與輸出之情形)，進
行專利名稱與摘要之自動斷詞，並為減低文字
特徵處理之複雜度，僅將斷詞後之字詞擷取出
名詞詞集(Na、Nb 及 Nc)與動詞詞集(VA、VC、
VG 及 VJ)兩大詞類為主，並外加研究人員做
適當標籤組成本研究計畫所需之初步字詞
集，亦即由原始專利資料中斷取出所需的合適
字詞(如下圖五所示)中，額外加上適度標籤後

    本階段中，主要有文件特徵的擷取及文
件特徵權重計算兩個重要的工作： 
(1) 文件特徵擷取(Feature extraction) 
    文件特徵擷取，主要是將能代表文件之
字詞擷取出來，做為文件分類之基準，當一
份文件描述一個特定主題時，一些具代表性
的字詞就會出現頻繁，而擷取這些關鍵字，
即可代表該文件之主要意涵。通常英文文獻
資料於此階段，由於字詞均已獨立，因此所
需之工作僅為去除停字(Stop-word)與字根還
原(Word stemming)部分；反觀中文文件，由
於每一段文句均連續無分離，因此要達到正

與文辭剖析(Parsing)等，而這些技術仍有許多
尚待突破的關鍵點，也是現今許多研究者極
力投入的研究方向。本國之中央研究院對這
些議題做了許多相關研究，其自行研發之自
動斷詞系統(Auto-tag system)便整合了上述相
關技術，因此本研究計畫亦以此一系統為初
步文字探勘前處理之重要工具。

四、結果與討論 
本研究計畫第一年利用自動斷詞方法將專
利內容轉為數個特徵字詞，並利用卡方統計與
相關係數特徵擷取方法，將專利內容中與 40
個 TRIZ 創新性原則有關聯性之字詞篩選出
來，經由此一機制流程及重複實驗測試後， 針
對本研究計畫第一年專利映射TRIZ創新性原
則之分類結果，每一個創新性原則各 25 筆(共
1000 筆)專利資料進行實驗，以支持向量機分
類器與倒傳遞類神經網路分類之。結果顯示利
用卡方統計和相關係數進行特徵擷取，當擷取
特徵越少，卡方統計有稍微較好的績效結果，
而擷取特徵越多，相關係數則有稍微較好的績
效但兩者差異並不大。 
藉由本研究計畫第一年實驗過程中可得以下
嶄新見解： 
(1) 自動斷詞技術能將專利內容斷成數個特
徵字詞，然而有些字詞組合方式不符所
需，仍須以人工方式進行調整，如前章所
提及之「預先反作用」、「預先作用」及「預
先緩衝」等和 TRIZ 創新性原則較有關聯
性之字詞，於自動斷詞過程仍儘能斷成「預
先」、「反作用」、「作用」與「緩衝」等字
詞特徵，可能造成分類工作進行時的部分
影響。 
(2) 過去績效較佳之卡方統計特徵擷取方
法，較最近提出的相關係數特徵擷取方法為
佳，其原因可能在於卡方統計方法同時截取出
對該創新性原則強烈正相關或強烈負相關之
字詞，不論於未來本研究計畫第二年 SVM 或
BPN 之分類工作下，其準確性能較相關係數
為佳。  
五、計畫成果自評部份 
過去傳統的 TRIZ 應用，透過問題描述過
程將問題中之改善參數與惡化參數訂定出
來，並透過矛盾矩陣表尋找合適的解決方法-
創新性原則，這些創新性原則提供一些建議與
方針，然而在目前知識膨脹的時代下，資訊、
知識、問答集、案例探討與專利，這些資源均
能提供一些具體的方向，比起這些仍須經過腦
力激盪之思考方針，著實更為明確。也因此，
本研究計畫第一年之主要目標，便在建立一專
利映射 TRIZ 創新性原則之分類機制，冀望能
透過此一機制將專利發明內容加以關聯，作為
提供TRIZ使用者或產品創新研發者更具體且
完整的思考途徑，亦為本研究計畫第一年最主
要之目的與貢獻。

basis to resolve contradictions successfully. It includes 
contradiction analysis, idle resources utilization, increasing the 
ideality of final result, prediction for the evolution of both

Abstract—This study develops an image-processing filter to 
enhance the visual defects such as particles, stains, and uneven 
intensity on polarizers in TFT-LCD industry. Each pixel in the 
subimage of a polarizer is initially processed to calculate its 
standard deviation (SD) of gray level, which is sampled by its 
neighbors within a window. The gray level of each pixel is re-
scaled by the maximal and minimal SD values on entire subimage 
to determine its new gray level. Real polarizers with visual defects 
are tested in this study. Experimental results show that the 
proposed filter achieves better performance than conventional 
image enhancement filters do. Moreover, the proposed image 
enhancement scheme provides more information for potential 
defect detection and classification alternatives. The proposed filter 
is simple, straightforward, and requiring no high-resolution image. 
Therefore, it is practically suitable for large-polarizer 
manufacturers to increase inspection speed.

electrical products such as projector, PC-monitor, notebook, TV, and 
so on. The demand of TFT-LCD has increased dramatically in this 
decade [1]. To reduce the unit cost by getting more LCD panels from 
one glass substrate, the trend for larger size of TFT-LCD (e.g. 5th – 7th 
generations) is unavoidable. However, different types of defects occur 
more frequently because larger area of TFT-LCD is involved. Once 
the fault probability exceeds a pre-defined limit, an expensive TFT-
LCD has forced to be scraped. Therefore, a fast and accurate 
inspection system using machine vision becomes a critical issue to 
TFT-LCD manufacturers and their component suppliers. 
     Important elements in a TFT-LCD include TFT array, color filter, 
back-lighting module, liquid crystal, flexible PCB, driver and control 
ICs, and polarizer (see Figure 1). Most of TFT-LCD manufacturers 
make TFT arrays and purchase other elements from their vendors. TFT 
array suffers from point, line, curve (cross-talk effect on a group of 
TFT cells), and area (uneven intensities on a group of TFT cells) 
defects [2]. Current vision inspection system for TFT array scans the 
pixels on the image row by row. It records the locations of pixels that 
are significantly different with their closest neighbors in gray level. 
Based on the locations, machine inspection system is able to further 
judge the types of defect. The most expensive purchased component 
for a TFT-LCD is color filter. Color filter occupies 25 % material cost 
of a TFT-LCD panel. The defects on color filter can be mainly 
classified into micro and macro defects [3]. Micro defects such as 
pinholes, particles, and cracks are very small and usually inspected by 
machine vision system. Macro defects including contamination, mis-
alignment of cell, and “mura” are manually inspected currently. 
Similar to the area defect on TFT array, a mura defect on a color filter 
is defined as the unevenness of color or spatial noise [3-4]. The mura 
defect is considered to be the most challenging inspection problem in 
TFT-LCD industry since it can be visualized only under a uncertain 
viewing angle. Statistical approaches solving the problem of uneven 
intensity on a color filter had been developed [4, 5]. However, these 
approaches require a lot of data to realize the accuracy of estimated 
parameters and the impact on the detection results.  
   Another crucial component occupying 10% material cost of a TFT-
LCD panel is polarizer. Polarizers strongly affect light reflection, LCD 
contrast, optical performance, and available viewing angles received 
by human eye [6]. A pair of crossed polarizers are respectively at the 
outer and the inter layers of a TFT-LCD panel. It is possible to 
minimize the unwanted reflections by polarizing light at an appropriate 
angle. Therefore, perfect polarizers contribute to more customer appeal 
and less visual fatigue [7]. Polarizer suffers from crack, particle, stain, 
and uneven intensity defects as well. However, rare studies have 
discussed on the inspection of polarizers in TFT-LCD industry.  
   This study proposes a simple and straightforward filter to 
enhance the visual defects such as particles, contaminations, and mura 
defects on a polarizer. For each pixel in the subimage of a polarizer, its 
local standard deviation (SD) in gray level is initially sampled by the 
intensities of the pixels surrounding it. Then, base on the difference of 
maximal and minimal SDs among entire subimage, a re-scale process 
(e.g. similar to equalization process) is performed to determine the 
new gray level of every pixel. These pixels with transformed gray 
levels are used to reconstruct the subimage of a polarizer. The 
proposed image enhancement technique is named “SD&R” filter in 
this study. Even in a low-resolution subimage, the contrast of visual 
defects such as particles, stains, and uneven intensities on a polarizer 
become much higher by using SD&R filter. Therefore, the visual 
defects are easier to be addressed by further detection approaches. The 
proposed image enhancement filter for polarizers is simple, 
straightforward, practical, and requiring no high-resolution image. It 
could be more acceptable for polarizer manufacturers in TFT-LCD 
industry since it is easier to implement. In addition, the subimages of 
real polarizers are tested in this study to validate the practice of 
proposed filter. 
   This paper is organized as follows: In section 2, the 
performances of traditional image enhancement techniques such as 
Robert filter, Sobel filter, Sharpening filter, and Thresholding for real 
polarizers are evaluated. In section 3, the proposed SD&R filter is 
described and compared with conventional ones. Experimental 
verifications using real polarizers are indicated in section 4. Finally, the 
conclusion is given in section 5.

IV.  EXPERIMENTAL RESULTS 
   The proposed SD&R filter for enhancing the particle, 
stain, and mura defects on polarizers is validated in this section. 
A white LED with diffuser in back-lighting direction and a 
50mm lens with 45mm extension ring are used to increase the 
visibility of polarizers. The proposed SD&R filter is edited in 
the C language and executed on the vision package software 
named ‘Optimas’ using a personal computer with Pentium VI 
CPU. 
   There are four testing subimages of real polarizers to 
verify the proposed filter in this section. They are captured in 
120 × 120 image resolution and 6mm × 6mm field of view. 
The window size k is set to 5. Each of the testing samples 1-3 
contains a mura defect at different position. Testing samples 1, 
2, and 3 are shown in Figures 4(a), 4(d), and 4(g), respectively. 
However, each of the testing samples 1 and 2 has a darker (blur) 
mura defect but testing sample 3 has a light mura defect. There 
are simultaneously particles, a stain (a dead and small bug on 
top-left side), and a much lighter mura defect on testing sample 
4. Testing sample 4 is indicated in Figure 4(j). The results by 
SD&R filter for testing samples 1-4 are respectively 
demonstrated in Figures 4(b), 4(e), 4(h), and 4(k). The 
corresponding histograms for Figures 4(b), 4(e), 4(h), and 4(k) 
are respectively shown in Figures 4(c), 4(f), 4(i), and 4(l). The 
skmaxvalues for testing sample 1-4 are 1.487, 2.525, 9.025, and 
10, respectively. The skmin values for testing sample 1-3 are 0. 
The skmin value for testing sample 4 is 0.124.    
   According to Figures 4(a)-4(f), the proposed SD&R filter 
successfully separates the darker mura defects from 
background in testing samples 1 and 2 since both histograms 
suggest high contrast images. Notice that very few white pixels 
shown in Figures 4(c) and 4(f), so they are not indicated clearly 
due to large scale of frequency. Concerning testing sample 3, 
The histogram in Figure 4(i) has approximately 45 white pixels 
and 810 black pixels. Most of pixels are located between 10 to 
170 after SD&R filtering process. It suggests a lighter mura 
defect occurrence, which covers a large portion of entire 
subimage. A stain and particles in testing sample 4 are 
enhanced and enlarged to be fully white in Figure 4(k). Except 
the pixels at the stain and the particle defects, many light pixels 
exist in Figure 4(k) and no black pixel exist in Figure 4(l). It 
suggests that there is a light mura defect, which covers 
uniformly on entire subimage. Based on the experimental 
results from testing sample 1-4, the proposed SD&R filter 
performs well in enhancing particle, stain, and mura defects on 
the images of polarizers. In addition, the proposed filter 
provides richer information for setting constraints on further 
defect detection and classification. 
   Figure 4(k) is further executed by Multiple-Thresholding 
process. The resulting images by thresholds TSDR2 and TSDR1 
are respectively shown in Figures 5(a) and 5(b). The stain 
defect and particles are clearly indicated by threshold TSDR2. 
The mura defects connects with the stain defect and particles to 
form many blobs by threshold TSDR1. Therefore, if there are 
many white pixels after SD&R filtering process, stains or 
particles defects can be detected by minimum variance 
threshold (TSDR2). More and more blob occurrences by 
decreasing threshold imply that there is a mura defect on the 
polarizer. 
V. CONCLUSION 
   This paper exploits an image enhancement scheme for 
visual defects such as particles, stains, and mura on polarizers 
in TFT-LCD industry. The proposed filter initially uses the 
standard deviation of gray level to represent the contrast of a 
pixel relative to its neighborhood. Then, the new gray level of a 
pixel is determined by its re-scale factor based on all SD values 
from entire subimage. The proposed SD&R filter is better than 
traditional image enhancement filters including Robert, Sobel, 
and Sharpening in the application of polarizers. Moreover, the 
proposed filter provides more information for potential defect 
detection and classification approaches. One is able to further 
judge the locations and types of defects by simple geometrical 
descriptors. The proposed image enhancement technique is 
simple, practical, straightforward, and does not require high-
resolution image. Therefore, it is more acceptable for real 
implementation in TFT-LCD industry.
