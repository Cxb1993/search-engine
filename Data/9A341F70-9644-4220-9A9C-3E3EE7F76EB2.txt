關鍵詞：零價鐵、一氧化氮、二氧化硫、流體化床、X光繞射、化學分析電子儀。 
 
    本計畫分為三個階段，第一年研究一氧化氮(NO)與零價鐵(zero valent iron, 
ZVI)在填充床(packed bed)管柱之化學反應機制。ZVI在高溫的狀況下，對NO有
很強的還原能力。故在空床接觸時間(empty bed contact time, EBCT)為0.0225秒到
0.0679秒下，考慮五個實驗反應溫度(573 K~773 K)，結果指出NO可被ZVI去除1.0 
mg NO/g ZVI (低溫和較短停留時間)到11.2 mg NO/g ZVI (高溫和較長停留時
間)。將使用過鐵粉經X光繞射儀(X-Ray powder diffraction, XRD)及化學分析電
子儀(electron spectrocopy for chemical analysis, ESCA)分析結果，由ZVI核心到外
層的鐵氧化物分別是Fe0 (ZVI)、FeO、Fe2O3和Fe3O4。研究結果顯示只有少於百
分之二的ZVI與NO反應，根據化學反應的理論，斷定反應只是在ZVI表面上發
生，而不是佔整個ZVI的大部分。本研究也在三個不同反應溫度下，對二氧化硫
(SO2)在ZVI填充床管柱的去除效果加以研究。研究顯示，SO2可被ZVI去除0.03 mg 
SO2/g ZVI (低溫)到0.64 mg SO2/g ZVI (高溫)，因此ZVI對SO2的去除效果隨著溫
度提高而增加。 
    研究中顯示ZVI在高溫下，對於單獨處理NO及SO2皆有良好之處理效率。故
第二年本研究係以實驗室之流體化床(fluidized bed)管柱實驗，探討ZVI併同處理
NO、SO2之可行性研究。從不同反應溫度實驗得知，在NO、SO2進流濃度分別為
500 ppm，鐵粉添加量為0.5 g，通量為0.6 L·cm-2·min-1，在反應溫度為573 K的反
應條件下，並無任何的NO、SO2累積去除量，當反應溫度在623 K、673 K時，NO
去除率可達100%，而SO2的去除率可達90%以上。若提高反應溫度至723 K、773 
K時，則NO、SO2的去除效率均可達100%。並且單位鐵粉量對NO、SO2去除量隨
反應溫度提高而增加，兩者皆呈線性關係，且迴歸係數均為大於0.97。NO、SO2
累積去除量隨鐵粉添加量增加而提高，但與通量無明顯的相關性。NO、SO2穿透
時間會受鐵粉量及通量所影響，由於鐵粉重量/通量的比值(W/F)與NO、SO2穿透

 Abstract 
 
Keywords: Zero valent iron, nitric oxide, sulfur oxide, fluidized column, XRD, ESCA 
 
    The three-year project incorporates three stages. In the first year, chemical 
reaction between nitrogen monoxide (NO) and zero valent iron (ZVI) was studied in a 
packed bed column process with high temperature based on the strong reducing ability 
of ZVI. Exhaustion test was conducted in five controlled temperatures ranging from 
523 K~773 K, with 400 ppm of NO and under short empty bed contact time (EBCT) 
(0.0225 sec to 0.0679 sec). The results indicated that NO reduction by ZVI (mg NO/g 
ZVI) varied from 1.0 mg NO/g ZVI (lowest temperatures and shortest detention time) 
to 11.2 mg NO/g ZVI (highest temperatures and longest detention time). X-Ray 
powder diffraction (XRD) and electron spectrocopy for chemical analysis (ESCA) 
were performed to analyze the crystal structure and oxidation state of the reacted ZVI. 
Four layers from the ZVI core to its surface can be identified: Fe0 (ZVI), FeO, Fe3O4 
and Fe2O3. Since only less than 2% of the ZVI was used to remove NO, it can be 
concluded that the heterogeneous reaction occurred only on the ZVI surface instead of 
the entire ZVI bulk. Another exhaustion test was conducted in three working 
temperatures for sulfur dioxide (SO2) reduction, and the results indicated that SO2 
reduction by ZVI varied from 0.03 mg SO2/g ZVI (lowest temperatures) to 0.64 mg 
SO2/g ZVI (highest temperatures). 
    In the second year, a new research to simultaneously remove NO and SO2 by 
ZVI was conducted. Two major parameters, temperature (623 K, 673 K, 723 K and 
773 K) and flux (0.5, 0.6, 0.7 and 0.8 L·cm-2·min-1) were tested in fluidized column 
studies containing 500 ppm of NO and SO2. Under the ZVI dosage of 0.5 g at flux 0.6 
L·cm-2·min-1 in the temperature of 573 K, there was neither NO nor SO2 reduction. In 
temperatures of 623 K and 673 K, 100% removal for NO and more than 90% removal 
for SO2 were achieved. In temperatures of 723 K and 773 K, 100% removal was 
achieved for both NO and SO2. When the temperature was controlled respectively at 
623 K, 673 K, 723 K and 773 K, NO reduction was 6.33, 22.65, 46.28 and 57.52 mg 
NO/g ZVI while SO2 reduction was 11.43, 30.03, 65.55 and 109.47 mg SO2/g ZVI. 
The result indicated that the amounts of NO and SO2 reduction (as mg NO/g or SO2/g 
ZVI) increased as temperature increased, and linearity was found in both cases as the 
correlation coefficients exceeded 0.97. The mass reduction of NO and SO2 increased 
with the increased in ZVI dosage but stayed constant regardless of flux variation. The 
breakthrough time was affected by flux and ZVI dosage. A parameter ZVI weight/flux

 第一章  前言 
 
1.1  研究緣起 
零價鐵(ZVI)金屬併同處理NOX及SOX，為本研究首先提出且有其可行性。目
前使用的NOX/SOX處理技術，不論是SCR、SNCR或FGD，均針對單一污染物進
行處理，除了各有利弊之外，均無法同時處理NOX及SOX。然而目前常用的石化
燃料以煤和重油為最多，其燃燒廢氣之氣狀污染物中，均同時含有NOX及SOX。
以發電廠為例，欲降低NOX及SOX濃度，就必須各裝置一套處理設備，不僅提高
了設置成本，也需要較大的場地，就經濟面而言，有再考量的必要。本研究整理
比較現行處理技術與ZVI處理技術去除效率列於表1.1，可發現以處理效率而言，
ZVI除了去除率高於其它方法，且能併同處理兩者之外，若CO存在於系統中，反
而對ZVI更有幫助，因CO會與鐵金屬表面已反應之氧化鐵作用，形成鐵元素，反
而輔助金屬鐵之再生。

2.1.2  氮氧化物污染特性 
氮氧化物污染產生的環境問題，可分為五類：(1)光化學煙霧(2)在對流層形
成臭氧(3)促進 PM2.5 (直徑少於 2.5 微米的超細懸浮固體)的生成(4)形成酸雨(5)
造成酸沉降。 
目前在國際上最為重視的氮氧化物污染問題首推酸雨，酸雨造成的危害主要
有下列六點[13]: 
A. 造成土壤酸化，以至農作物種植受限，導致農作物減產。 
B. 對建築材料的侵蝕，由於低 pH 值的影響，使建築物壽命減少並增加維護成
本。 
C. 樹葉直接受到侵害，使樹木壽命減少，尤其對於針葉樹的影響甚鉅。 
D. 溶解土壤中的金屬離子，造成土壤過度肥沃現象，影響植物生長速率，使年
輕林木過度成長，而導致土質鬆軟，不耐極端氣候及昆蟲之侵襲。 
E. 使土壤中所含毒性礦物溶出，污染地表水，透過食物鏈，間接影響人體健康。 
F. 使湖泊酸化，降低水中生物數量，破壞生態。 
在環境問題越來越受到重視的今天，全世界科學界及產物界均積極投入氮氧
化物控制與減量技術的研發，並利用新技術逐年降低氮氧化物的排放，期待能在
工業發展與氮氧化物排放間取得平衡點。 
 
2.1.3  氮氧化物控制技術 
人為氮氧化物的生成主要為燃燒行為所產生，因此控制氮氧化物的方法，主
要為下列三個方式[14]: 
1. 選用含氮量低(或不含氮)的燃料或減少燃料含氮量： 
    含氮量低的燃料(如天然氣或低含氮量之油、煤)，或以前處理方式減少
燃料中含氮量(如加氫脫氮處理 HDN)，均可減少燃料氮氧化合物的生成。然
此種方法成本昂貴，且無法減少熱力氮氧化物，因此成效有限。 
2. 改進燃燒程序： 
    在燃燒過程中，將空氣或燃料分段注入燃燒室內，以降低燃燒時之溫
度、減少燃燒區的氧濃度及減慢釋熱速率來減少氮氧化物的生成，是最經濟
有效的氮氧化物控制方法，且不致產生二次污染，但其改善效果(去除率
30~50％)有限。 
3. 煙道氣的處理： 
    燃燒後產生的 NOX在排放至大氣前，裝設處理設備使 NOX濃度降低，
減少排放量。

2.3  零價鐵及鐵氧化物在環境工程之相關研究 
2.3.1  零價鐵之應用面 
零價鐵的應用，近年來大部分以水相中污染物的處理為研究主軸，由於零價
鐵有很強的還原能力，1972 年 Sweeny 和 Fischer 首先即將零價鐵的技術應用在
TCE 等含氯有機物的去除[23]，並得到顯著的效果，到 1990 年代初期，零價鐵
處理技術再度引起重視，但仍多應用在處理含氯的有機化合物[24]，近年來發現
零價鐵對於硝酸鹽氮的去除亦有顯著效果[25]，因此零價鐵金屬牆或零價鐵管柱
應用於地下水中含氯有機物及硝酸鹽氮處理，不管在實務應用或理論方面，均有
許多相關的研究。而零價鐵在空氣污染方面的應用，在氮氧化物處理方面，相關
研究發現零價鐵可將 NOX 還原為氮氣，而零價鐵則氧化成為氧化鐵，去除效果
則隨反應溫度及鐵粉表面積增加而提高[26]。 
 
2.3.2  零價鐵處理 NOX方面 
    目前工業界製程所衍生出之空氣污染物NOX，多半使用選擇性觸媒還原法
(SCR)，其處理效雖果佳，不過由於更換觸媒費用昂貴，因此多數廠商在使用多
年後因不願更換觸媒而造成去除效果大為下降，為此，近年來國外學者新興之研
究課題即為利用鐵化合物(iron-containing additives)作為添加劑，用來去除煙道氣
體中之NOX，以下列舉兩篇具代表性之文獻作一說明。在Lissianski et al.[4]之研
究中，即分別探討不同種類的鐵化合物在加入鍋爐中對於NOX的去除效果有何影
響，此外並配合其它參數條件(添加鐵藥量、是否經過二次燃燒、鐵粉投入位置)
做交叉比對，以獲得最佳控制條件。另外，為了解廢鐵(iron waste)再利用之可能
性，因此本篇文獻也將其納入研究範圍。茲將其結果整理如下： 
其實驗方法為利用300 KW的蒸氣鍋模擬設備(boiler simulator facility)，在溫
度為1,590 K，NOX之進氣濃度為600 ppm時，於燃油中加入不同種類之鐵化合物
(1,000 ppm)送進主燃燒室中，以了解NOX之去除效果。所使用的鐵化合物有金鐵
（metallic iron,  MI）、Fe2O3 (試劑級)、廢鐵(iron waste, 80% of Fe2O3 and 20% of 
Ca(OH)2)及Fe3O4等四種。結果顯示，直接噴入鐵粉方式，其NOX去除率約20%，
若再配合二次燃燒(reburning)，則去除率將會大幅提升。若是將這些鐵化合物(添
加量1,000 ppm)加入二次燃燒用的燃料時，其NOX去除效果分別為Fe2O3 ≒ 
ironwaste > Fe3O4 > MI；其中因廢鐵主要成份與Fe2O3相同，故其去除效果相當，
 13
綜合上述兩篇文獻之比較，可發現Lissianski et al.[5]是由控制燃燒程序之不
同與添加多樣的觸媒藥劑進行對NOX去除效果的比較，並發現普通廢鐵渣的效果
相當不錯，而焦炭與煤的效果則十分低落，並在最後做出兩點結論，(1)只要在
燃燒過程中加入鐵觸媒，無論是否有經過二次燃燒，NOx皆有去除效果；(2)在有
加入觸媒鐵劑的情形下，無論燃燒室有無通入過剩空氣量(OFA)，NOX皆有不錯
之去除效果。而Hayhurst and Ninomiya[3]之研究則是以不同的燃燒溫度為條件，
進行以觸媒燃燒去除NOX之機制探討，結果顯示溫度越高NOX的去除效果越好，
效果之比較詳見表2.6。而在目前現行多數之觸媒還原反應結果顯示，以觸媒還
原法進行NOX的去除時，反應物必須經由質傳與擴散到達觸媒表面，並在適當的
操作溫度與空間速度下，觸媒表面孔隙內擴散對反應的影響最大，孔隙內的化學
反應次之，影響最小的是質傳到外圍表面，也因此觸媒還原法反應在目前之改良
與使用上，因表面作用仍佔大部分結果，故實廠使用仍以蜂巢狀的觸媒為主，在
考量合適的壓差範圍內，盡量提高觸媒單位面積下的孔口數，但是仍有程度上之
極限，所以由該研究結果顯示，若在成本許可下，提高燃燒溫度除了可以增加觸
媒表面之多孔性，更能夠將反應由表面帶入觸媒深層釋放電子的反應，得到更佳
的NOX去除效果。

3.4  分析方法 
3.4.1  XRD 試驗 
本研究之 XRD 試驗乃送至清華大學貴重儀器使用中心受測，X 光粉末繞
射光譜儀(X-Ray powder Diffraction, XRD) 試驗，是利用 X 射線在結晶物質中
的繞射現象來分析物質的晶體結構及組成，其原理為不同化合物均具有特定之
晶格結構，在入射 X 光的激發下，不同繞射角度下會產生不同強度之繞射光
譜，藉此鑑定區別不同之化合物。實驗分析時如樣品顆粒過大或分佈不均易造
成繞射圖譜發生偏移現象，因此樣品於分析前須先經篩分、研磨、烘乾等前處
理作業；且待測物有須為結晶型態及樣品濃度須大於 5 %的限制。樣品之 X
光繞射分析圖譜須藉由 JCPDS (Joint Committee on Powder Diffraction 
Standards)之標準圖譜進行比對工作，來確認其所含的物質。

原成 FeO，則反應優勢溫度至少需要在 650 ℃以上[31]，推論其還原之生成物種
也不會含有 FeO。因此經由上述的推論結果得知，其鐵氧化物經由 CO 氣體還原
之生成物種應為 Fe3O4，其 CO 氣體對鐵氧化物的還原反應機制應為(5.5)式，反
應式如下： 
3Fe2O3 ＋ CO  2Fe3O4 ＋ CO2    → (4.17)
 
由(4.16)式和(4.17)式可發現當 Fe3O4 被 NO 氧化成為 Fe2O3 時，此時 CO 會
與 NO 進行競爭，將 Fe2O3 還原成 Fe3O4，然而從實驗結果可以發現，添加 CO
氣體於零價鐵填充床處理 NOx 之實驗中，並沒有產生無限的反應時間，其可能
原因是 CO 氣體雖然能將 Fe2O3 還原成 Fe3O4，但其還原速率卻比 NO 對 Fe3O4
的氧化速率來得慢之緣故。 
根據上述的結論，因此將(4.15)式和(4.16)式合併可得知在沒有添加 CO 氣體
的條件下，其 NO 與 ZVI 之間的反應機制(4.18)式；若將(4.17)式和(4.18)式合併，
則可得知在有添加 CO 氣體於零價鐵填充床處理 NOx 的反應機制為(5.7)式，其
反應式如下： 
4Fe＋6NO  2Fe2O3＋3N2    → (4.18)
6Fe＋10NO＋CO  3Fe2O3＋5N2＋CO2 → (4.19)

 第五章  結論與建議 
 
5.1  結論 
    本零價鐵並同處理NOX、SOX及其再生性之研究計畫，於單一污染物之研究
中，研究中指出ZVI單獨處理NO及ZVI單獨處理SO2皆能有良好之處理效果，且
NO去除效率皆隨者反應溫度增加而提高，最佳之反應溫度為773 K。在ZVI單獨
處理NO研究中，比較高氣體流量(較高EBCT)和低氣體流量(較低EBCT），結果
顯示在低氣體流量的狀況下，其反應的穿透時間和反應體積，是在高氣體流量狀
況下的兩倍。而在ZVI單獨處理SO2研究中，對不同鐵粉量所對應之EBCT而言，
去除率降至50%之反應時間雖均隨EBCT增加而延長，但並非線性相關，因此用
EBCT來預測SO2之去除效率不可行。 
    而於複合污染物中，併同處理 NO/SO2 以及添加還原性 CO 氣體和煙道氣
含氧探討，還有添加氧化鐵結合 CO 併同處理 NO/SO2 研究跟模場試驗之結論
列於以下： 
ZVI 併同處理 NO/SO2 反應： 
1. 反應溫度在 673 K~773 K 時，NO、SO2 於反應初期均可被完全去除，而 SO2
僅只能達到的 90%去除率，NO、SO2 完全被去除的時間及穿透時間，皆隨
著反應溫度增加而延長。 
2. 反應溫度與單位鐵粉處理 NO 能力呈現線性關係，隨著反應溫度上升，處
理能力也急速增加，且趨勢線均為迴歸係數大於 0.97 的線性關係。 
3. 當鐵粉量為 0.5 g 時，其鐵粉累積去除量並未隨通量增加而有明顯差異，但
穿透時間則隨通量的增加而減少。當鐵粉量為 0.25 g~1.0 g 時，於通量 0.6 
L·cm-2·min-1 的情況下，NO、SO2 穿透時間與 NO、SO2 累積去除量均隨鐵
粉添加量增加而提高。 
4. 零價鐵併同處理 NO、SO2 較零價鐵單獨處理 NO、SO2 效果佳；但不管是
零價鐵單獨處理 NO、SO2 或零價鐵併同處理 NO、SO2，其 NO、SO2 排放
濃度均隨反應時間增加而急劇上升。 
5. 零價鐵併同處理 NO、SO2 中，而 SO2 有部份走氧化路線，且 NO 的 O 提
供 SO2 氧化生成 FeSO4。而 NO 是進行還原反應生成 N2，其總反應機制如
下：

5.2  建議 
本計畫主要僅針對利用零價鐵併同處理 NO、SO2 做探討，並研究添加氧
化鐵以及還原性氣體 CO 來增加處理效率，由實驗結果可知此方式可有效處理
NO、SO2，惟實際煙道氣之成分複雜，除 NO、SO2、CO 外，尚有其它成分，
如 HC、O2、H2O……等因子均尚未加入本研究範圍，其綜合效應需待更深一
步的探討。另於實際應用時，由於考慮到實際情況，反應溫度與發電機負載有
一定侷限，故不能無限之升高溫度已達到良好的處理效率，此為須考慮之問
題，另外，能否以工業產生之廢鐵屑來代替本研究所使用的氧化鐵粉(Fe2O3)，
如此更能符合資源再利用趨勢，並且可以大幅降低在污染控制方面的操作成
本，未來可朝向以上的測試條件，進行更深入之研究，以作為將零價鐵/氧化
鐵粉應用於空氣污染防治上的重要處理技術之依據。

Simultaneous Removal of NO and SO2 by High-Temperature
Fluidized Zero-Valent Iron Processes
Shiao-Shing Chen, Chih-Yu Cheng, Chung-Cheng Wei, and Chao-Heng Tseng
Institute of Environment Engineering and Management, National Taipei University of Technology,

ABSTRACT
A new approach to simultaneously remove nitrogen mon-
oxide (NO) and sulfur dioxide (SO2) by zero valent iron
(ZVI) was investigated. Three different parameters, tem-
perature, flux, and ZVI dosage, were tested in fluidized ZVI
column studies containing 500 ppmv of NO and SO2,
respectively. Under the ZVI dosage of 0.5 g at flux of 0.6
L/cm2min for temperature 573 K, there is neither NO nor
SO2 reduction. For 623 K and 673 K, complete removal for
NO and 90% removal for SO2 were achieved. For tem-
peratures of 723 K and 773 K, 100% removal was achieved
for both NO and SO2. The amounts of NO or SO2 reduc-
tion (as milligrams of NO or SO2 per gram ZVI) increased
as temperature increased, and linearities were observed
with both correlation coefficients 0.97. Compared with
NO, SO2 had earlier breakthrough because of a slower
diffusion rate and less reactivity but higher mass reduc-
tion because of a higher molecular weight for SO2 (64
g/mol for SO2 and 30 g/mol for NO). At same tempera-
ture, both NO and SO2 reductions (as milligrams of NO or
SO2 per gram of ZVI) were constant regardless of either
flux or ZVI dosage variation, but breakthrough time was
affected by both flux and ZVI dosage. A parameter weight
of ZVI/flux (W/F) was developed to represent these two
parameters at the same time to assess the breakthrough
time of NO and SO2. Higher breakthrough time was
achieved for higher W/F value. Moreover, interestingly,
longer breakthrough time and more NO and SO2 mass
reduction were achieved for combined NO and SO2 than
individual NO or SO2 treated by ZVI, and both oxidation
and reduction reactions occurred instead of a reduction
reaction only. Chemical reactions among ZVI/NO, ZVI/
SO2, and ZVI/NO/SO2 were also proposed and verified by
X-ray diffraction analyses.
INTRODUCTION
Nitrogen oxides (NOx) and sulfur oxides (SOx) are gener-
ated during combustion process, and most NOx and SOx
from stationary combustion sources are emitted as NO
and SO2. NOx leads to photochemical smog, SOx causes
acid rain together with humidity in the air, and both of
them are harmful to the human body and our environ-
ment. Therefore, the reduction or even zero discharge of
NOx and SOx from the flue gas out of industrial or power
plants has always been a critical issue. To deal with the
problem of NOx and SOx, two control methods are typi-
cally needed. For NOx, both selective catalytic reduction
(SCR) and selective noncatalytic reduction can remove all
NOx effectively; however, both techniques have with
their drawbacks of excessive use of chemicals or high cost
of a catalyst in the SCR process.1 For SOx, flue gas desul-
furization is the most common method, but the gener-
ated sludge requires further treatment. Consequently, a
new approach to simultaneously remove NO and SO2 by
zero valent iron (ZVI) was conducted, because ZVI is ca-
pable of removing both NO and SO22–7 without other
chemicals, and only one piece of equipment is needed
instead of two pieces.
There is no available literature regarding ZVI removal
of NOx and SOx together. Some literature has reported
using ZVI to remove NOx. The earliest reference on the
ZVI/NOx reaction is US Patent No. 4325925.2 NOx at
270 ppmv was removed by elemental iron at a water
volume of 1–15% and temperature 473 K for sufficient
contact time in this study. Some literature also reported a

m) in a 300-kW of Boiler Simulator Facility at a temper-

ABSTRACT
Chemical reaction between nitric oxide (NO) and zero
valent iron (ZVI) was studied in a packed-bed column
process with high temperatures based on ZVI strong re-
ducing abilities. For six controlled temperatures of 523–
773 K and 400 ppm of NO (typical flue gas temperature
and concentration), under short empty bed contact time
([EBCT] 0.0226–0.0679 sec), NO was completely removed
for temperature of 573–773 K but not for 523 K. Break-
through curves were conducted for the five working tem-
peratures, and the results indicated that NO reductions by
ZVI were varied from 2 to 26.7 mg NO/g ZVI. Higher
temperature and longer EBCT achieved better NO re-
moval efficiency. X-ray diffraction (XRD) and electron
spectroscopy for chemical analysis (ESCA) were con-
ducted to analyze the crystal structure and oxidation state
of the reacted ZVI. Three layers of iron species were de-
tected by XRD: ZVI, Fe3O4, and Fe2O3. ZVI was the most
prevalent species, and Fe3O4 and Fe2O3 were less from the
XRD analysis. By ESCA, the oxidation state on the reacted

fied as Fe2O3, which is the most oxidizing species for iron.
Therefore, three layers from the ZVI core to the ZVI sur-
face can be identified: ZVI, Fe3O4, and Fe2O3. Combining
the results from XRD and ESCA, the mechanisms for ZVI
and NO can be proposed as two consecutive reactions
from lower oxidation state (ZVI) in the core to higher
oxidation state on the iron surface (Fe2O3):

Effects of Temperatures (with Constant EBCT)
The experiment started with 523 K, but there was no NO
reduction. Apparently, higher temperature is needed.
When the temperature was increased to 573 K, 100%
removal was achieved but only lasted for very short time.
With every 50 K of temperature increases, in addition to
complete NO removal, the breakthrough time was also
increased. Breakthrough here is defined as when the ef-
fluent concentration is higher than the allowable concen-
tration, which is commonly 5% of the influent concen-
tration or 20 ppm in this study. The breakthrough curves
for five working temperatures (573–773 K) for flow rate of
0.1 L/min and EBCT of 0.0679 sec are shown in Figure 2.
Apparently, no NO reduction was observed under the
temperature of 523 K, but better efficiency (in term of
operation time) was achieved for higher temperature.
Thermodynamically, the reaction between ZVI and NO is
an exothermic reaction, because the enthalpy (H) of NO is
positive (90.3 kJ/mol), ZVI and nitrogen gas are zero, and
the resulting iron species (either FeO or Fe2O3) is negative
(272 or 1115.7 kJ/mol) assuming that eq 1 or 2 is
followed. Therefore, the enthalpy change of a chemical
reaction (H) is negative, and heat was evolved during the
reaction. Based on the Van’t Hoff equation, equilibrium
constant would be decreased and the reaction would not
be favored to the right side when temperature is increased
if H is 0. Thus, the increase for equilibrium constant at
higher temperature is not the reason for longer break-
through time and volume. It would contribute to the
higher diffusion or migration for the ZVI atom to contact
with NO at higher temperature according to the vacancy
diffusion theory.10 From the vacancy diffusion theory,
more vacancy would occur because of more atom migra-
tion at higher temperature, resulting in more ZVI atom
diffused to react with NO.

CONCLUSIONS
In this study, temperature, BV, flow rate, and EBCT, as
well as fate of ZVI were determined for the chemical
reaction between ZVI and NO. For six controlled temper-
atures of 523–773 K and 400 ppm of NO (typical flue gas
temperature and concentration), under short empty bed
contact time (0.0226–0.0679 sec), NO was completely
removed for temperature of 573–773 K but was not re-
moved at all for 523 K. Higher NO mass reduction was
shown for higher temperature and EBCT. The fate of ZVI
was determined by chemical identification of remaining
byproducts. Three layers of iron species were detected by
XRD: ZVI, Fe3O4, and Fe2O3. ZVI was the most prevalent,
and Fe3O4 and Fe2O3 were less from the XRD analysis. By
ESCA, the species was identified as Fe2O3, which is the
most oxidizing species for iron. Although the occurrence
of FeO was specified in other literature, it was not ob-
served in these analyses. Therefore, the three layers from
the ZVI core to the ZVI surface can be identified as ZVI,
Fe3O4, and Fe2O3. Combining the results from XRD and
ESCA, the mechanisms for ZVI and NO can be proposed as
two consecutive reactions:

SUMMERIES AND CONCLUSIONS 
 
For the study of concentration of 400 ppm NO 
removed by ZVIR for four different operated 
temperatures, the experimental results showed that 
when the temperature was increased, the reaction rate 
was increased, and first order reaction kinetic can be 
determined accordingly. The reaction constants k for 
these four reaction temperatures are determined as 
28.51 s-1 for 623 K, 87.88 s-1 for 673 K, 153.24 s-1 for 
723 K and 263.90 s-1 for 773 K. These rate constants k 
can be theoretically fitted into Arrhenius equation. 
Therefore, the activation energy (Ea) and frequency 
factor (A) for the reaction between ZVIR and NO was 
determined by plotting Ln k versus 1/T and shown as 
13.7 kcal/mol and 2.1×106 s-1, respectively. The 
calculated activation energy (Ea) is slightly lower than 
the theoretical calculation by EQS4WIN (19.45 
kcal/mol), which is a thermodynamic equilibrium and 
kinetic effect computation software, but is higher than 
the result shown in other literature (10.8 kcal/mol). 
The time needed for breakthrough was longer 
when the reaction temperature was increased. Total 
amount of NO removal by ZVIR (as mg/g) can be 
determined by the area over the breakthrough curve 
accordingly. The results showed, the total amount of 
NO removed by ZVIR (as mg/g) is also affected by 
the reaction temperature. When the reaction 
temperature was increased, the total amount of NO 
removed by ZVIR was also significantly increased. 
Moreover, there were only less than 1 % ZVIR used to 
remove NO comparing to theoretical ZVIR used based 
on stoichiometric ratio. Therefore, it can be concluded 
that the heterogeneous reaction was only occurred on 
the ZVIR surface instead of bulk of the ZVIR. ESCA 
(Electron Spectroscopy for Chemical Analysis) 
analysis was conducted to analyze the resulting 
species on the ZVIR surface and FeO was shown on 
the ZVIR surface. Therefore, the mechanism between

Methods  
A synthetic water and a groundwater with high silica and hardness were utilized in this study.  
The synthetic water was prepared using reagent grade sodium silicate, Na2SiO3, to make the 21 mg/L silica

same silica concentration. No extra alkalinity and hardness was added to this synthetic water since the 
effects of alkalinity and hardness were evaluated by adding calcium hydroxide and sodium carbonate. The 
brackish groundwater was obtained from a 600 CMD drinking water treatment plant located in Penghu 
County, Taiwan. This water treatment plant uses RO membrane process to treat the brackish water, but 
silica is the limitation for water recovery due to the possibility of scaling. The raw water qualities were 
listed in Table 1. As seen in Table 1, the water has very high hardness, especially for magnesium. Silica is 
not extremely high but can easily exceed the solubility (120 mg/L) at high water recovery. Standardized jar

optimizing conditions for hardness and silica removal. Rapid mix was performed for 30 sec at 100 rpm,

speeds for rapid and slow mix correspond to velocity gradients (G) at 25 oC of roughly 33 and 350 s-1, 
respectively. Samples for analysis were collected from each jar following a 5 minute settling period. These 
samples were collected at roughly 10 cm below the water surface with the jar. Upon collection, the samples 
were filtered with 0.45 micron glass fiber filter paper. There is no silica standard in the drinking water, but 
for solubility of 120 mg/L for silica and typical more than 90 % recovery, 10 mg/L of silica is the treatment 
goal to set in this study. All chemicals used are of reagent grade. pH was adjusted  by either caustic soda or
