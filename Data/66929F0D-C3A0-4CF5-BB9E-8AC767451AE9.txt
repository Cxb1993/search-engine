for knowledge management in construction 
enterprise, analyzing and discussing its 
components, pertinent supplementary techniques, 
and KM implementation status for construction 
projects. This study addresses application of 
knowledge management in the construction 
phase of construction projects and proposes a 
Construction CAD-based Knowledge 
Management (CCBKM) concept and system 
for contractors.  The CCBKM system is will 
applied in selected cases study of a High-Tech 
factory building enterprise in Taiwan to verify 
our proposed methodology and demonstrate the 
effectiveness of sharing knowledge special in the 
construction phase.  This research integrates 
present KM-related theoretical literature and 
practical implementation methodologies to 
develop with a generic model for KM 
frameworks integrated with knowledge map. 
During knowledge planning, top managers’ 
support will increase the possibilities of KM 
implementation. In addition, construction 
enterprise can take advantage of information 
flow and KM activities to help employees to 
clearly understand where KM is through 
knowledge map.  By effectively using 
information and web technologies during the 
construction phase of a project, knowledge can 
be captured and managed to benefit future 
projects.  The combined results demonstrate 
that, a CCBKM-like system can be an effective 
tool for construction knowledge managements in 
construction industries by utilizing the 
CAD-based knowledge management approach 
and web technology.
