In response to the demand over a higher efficiency for a power plant, various 
types of Generation IV nuclear reactors have been proposed. One of the new 
generation reactors adopts supercritical light water as the reactor coolant. While 
current in-service light water reactors (LWRs) bear an averaged thermal efficiency of 
33%, the thermal efficiency of a supercritical water reactor (SCWR) could generally 
reach more than 44%. For light water, the supercritical state in a system is attained 
when the system pressure is greater than 22.1 MPa and the system temperature is 
greater than 374 oC. Under this state, the coolant in an SCWR would remain in one 
phase and could therefore be spared from the incorporation of a recirculation system, 
a pressurizer, a steam generator, a separator and a dryer, as commonly seen in LWRs. 
Accordingly, the size of an SCWR is about one half of that of an LWR, the 
construction cost can thus be reduced by 30 to 40%, and the operation cost can be 
decreased by 30%. For LWRs, degradation of structural materials has mainly been 
resulted from stress corrosion cracking (SCC). Similar or even worse degradation 
phenomena are expected to appear in the structural and core components of an SCWR. 
It is therefore worth looking into the issue of finding suitable materials for SCWR 
applications during the design phase of an actual SCWR project. 
Specimens fabricated by Inconel 625 and Type 304L SS were exposed in a  
supercritical water loop with a dissolved oxygen concentration of 8.3 ppm at 600 °C 
and 24.8 MPa (3600 psi) for periods up to 1000h. The immersion test results indicated 
that the weight gain of the Inconel 625 specimen was less than that of Type 304L SS 
specimen. The mass change approximately follows a parabolic law of w2.21= 1.4×10-5t. 
It was also found that pits appeared on the surfaces of the specimens after the 
immersion tests and were resulted from MC carbide inclusions. In addition, duplex 
oxide layers were observed on the surfaces of the specimens. The outer layer 
consisted of sub-micron spinels of Ni(Cr,Fe)2O4, and the continuous inner layer was 
composed of spinels of Ni(Cr,Fe)2O4 and Cr2O3 with grain sizes of tens of nm. Some 
specimens were subject to proton irradiation. The oxide layers of the irradiated 
specimen exhibited similar oxide phases and thickness to those of the un-irradiated 
one. However, γ” coarsening occurred and was only seen in the matrix of the 
irradiated Inconel 625 specimen.

2.2實驗程序 
選定鎳基超合金Inconel 625及奧斯田不鏽鋼304L作為此次實驗材料。在超臨
界水環境腐蝕實驗之前，試片先進行製作加工與處理，試片大小為2 cm×2 cm×0.1

拋光液拋光，接著在含10%過氯酸之甲醇溶液內施加10V進行10秒之電解拋光，
可得到表面平滑之試片。之後將材料置於超臨界水環境的腐蝕實驗。腐蝕實驗設
定進口水溶氧量為8.3 ppm，導電度低於1 μS/cm以下，在溫度450℃至600 °C間與
壓力3600 psi的超臨界水環境下進行，之後使用精準度為0.01 mg之電磁天秤量測
試樣在實驗前後的重量變化。為了解在超臨界水環境下材料之腐蝕行為與特性，

3.3.3 XRD分析 
利用 X光低掠角繞射分析(GIXRD)分析氧化層以標定 Inconel 625在 600℃
超臨界水環境下生成之氧化物，掃描角度由 5°~100°。實驗 24、100、300、600
及 1000小時的結果如圖 17。在 100小時後 spinel Ni(Cr,Fe)2O4的峰值明顯，並且
在 600、1000小時有較明顯的 Cr2O3，和 TEM分析結果相符。 
由熱力學的自由能考慮，在 600℃時， 
2Ni+O2→2NiO     ΔG= -320.32kJ/mol， 
4/3Cr+O2→2/3Cr2O3   ΔG= -605.58kJ/mol 
Cr2O3的自由能雖然比NiO低，但是Inconel 625的成分中Ni占了約62%，Cr約25%，
Ni約為Cr的2.5倍多，因此必須考慮Ni的活度較大，因而在氧化層成長初期先有
NiO和Cr2O3的出現，並且混成Spinel NiCr2O4，氧化層持續成長後，因為Cr在氧
化層的擴散較慢，因此累積在氧化層內部，並和由外向內擴散的氧形成Cr2O3。
在 Tawancy的研究中[17]，Ni-22Cr合金在高溫氧化時，因為Ni占較多的含量，因
此在氧化初期NiO比Cr2O3多出許多，當繼續拉長氧化時間，為了降低自由能，
NiO會跟Cr2O3反應形成一固溶結構spinel NiCr2O4，之後在氧化層內部形成連續
的Cr2O3。Han的研究中[18]認為，鉻在氧化物內擴散較鎳慢，因此會在氧化層內
層累積鉻含量，鎳在氧化鎳中的擴散活化能為234 kJ/mol，而鉻在氧化鉻的擴散
活化能為420kJ/mol [19,20]，並且和經由晶界或是空孔從氧化層外向金屬內擴散的
氧形成Cr2O3[21]，此說法與Inconel 625於600℃超臨界水腐蝕後，利用XRD分析的
結果近似。

3.4.3氧化鋯參考電極在高溫超臨界水環境下測試 
目前氧化鋯參考電極在超高溫臨界水環境下仍在持續進行一系列的組裝改
良測試，維持參考電極在更高溫、高壓腐蝕環境中的穩定性與持久性為目前工作
重點。就電極穩定性而言，可採用更高效率的熱交換裝置，避免過大的溫度梯度
造成電位量測的誤差，或加長鋯管的長度來避免 Ag/AgCl電極末端過熱的問題。
在持久性方面，透過鋯管末端封口螺栓的精密設計可有效控制離子交換的速率，
飽和 KCl 電解質溶液的使用亦可增長電極的使用壽命，加大銀線末端氯化銀球
體的表面積亦是可行方法之一，但必須注意避免太接近高溫爐體而影響電位量測
的穩定性。現有以氧化鋯為護套的 Ag/AgCl 參考電極預期可應用於超臨界環境
中，未來將用以量測不同材料在超臨界水環境下的 ECP 值，以利後續材料腐蝕
