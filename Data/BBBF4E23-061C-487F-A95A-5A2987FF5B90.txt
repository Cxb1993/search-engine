研究摘要(500字以內)： 
 
本報告包含四個部份：首先，我們探討圖案化生長氮化鎵奈米柱及其接合再生長過程中
線性差排之演化。透過隨深度變化之X-光繞射及截面穿透式電子顯微術之結果，我們發
現線性差排密度與奈米柱大小成正比關係。利用最小之奈米柱及柱間距可以得到最低線
性差排密度及最高發光效率。經奈米柱生長及接合再生長之氮化鎵層，其材料與光學品
質都較原本之樣板者高。於第二部份內，我們以實驗及數值模擬顯示於表面電漿子與發
光二極體量子井耦合結構中，若我們插入一層如二氧化矽之低折射率介電質層，可產生
金屬損耗下降及表面電漿子消散場往外延伸之效果。雖然表面電漿子之能階密度也會下
降，但整體而言，表面電漿子與量子井耦合效果更佳，發光二極體發光效率更高。於第
三部份內，我們利用表面電漿子與發光二極體內量子井耦合效果來降低效率下滑效應，
經由最高外部量子效率所在電流密度及效率下滑斜率大小之觀察，可以發現具有較強耦
合效應之發光二極體樣品內，其效率下滑趨勢就較不明顯。最後在本報告第四部份內，
我們提出一套較完整之內部量子效率評估程序，雖無法提出內部量子效率數字，但可評
估各個影響內部量子效率因素之強弱，包括費米能量、高銦顆粒位能、量子侷限史塔克
效應及缺陷密度等。

技術研發成果說明： 
 
Part 1 
Patterned GaN Nanocolumn Growth and Coalescence Overgrowth   
 
High-quality coalescence overgrowth of patterned-grown GaN nanocolumns on c-plane 
sapphire substrate with metalorganic chemical vapor deposition is demonstrated. From the 
atomic force microscopy (AFM) measurement, the surface roughness of the overgrown layer is 
as small as 0.411 nm, which is only one-half that of the high-quality GaN thin-film template 
directly grown on sapphire substrate (the control sample). Based on the AFM and 
depth-dependent X-ray diffraction measurements, near the surface of the overgrown layer, the 
dislocation density is reduced to the order of 107 cm-2, which is one order of magnitude lower 
than that of the control sample and 2-3 orders of magnitude lower than those of ordinary GaN 
templates for fabricating light-emitting diode. Also, the lateral domain size, reaching a level of 
~2.7 m, becomes three times larger than the control sample. Meanwhile, the ratio of 
photoluminescence intensity at room temperature over that at low temperature of the 
overgrown sample is at least six times higher than that of the control sample. Also, threading 
dislocation (TD) evolution during patterned GaN nanocolumn growth and coalescence 
overgrowth with metaloragnic chemical vapor deposition is studied based on the comparisons 
of nanocolumn and coalescence overgrowth samples of different nanocolumn cross-section 
diameters and spacing sizes. From the measurement results of depth-dependent X-ray 
diffraction and cross-section transmission electron microscopy, it is found that the TD density 
in an nanocolumn depends on the patterned hole size for NC growth. Also, the TD formation at 
the beginning of coalescence overgrowth is related to the nanocolumn spacing size. Although 
the TD density at the bottom of the overgrown layer is weakly dependent on nanocolumn and 
spacing sizes, at its top surface, the TD density strongly relies on NC size. Among the 
overgrowth samples of different nanocolumn diameters and spacing sizes with a fixed 
nanocolumn diameter/spacing ratio, the one with the smallest size and spacing leads to the 
lowest TD density, the largest lateral domain size, and the highest photoluminescence 
efficiency. Also, the optical and crystal qualities at the surfaces of all the overgrowth samples 
are superior to those of a GaN template. 
 
 
Part 2 
Surface Plasmon Coupled LED (I) 
 
The improved emission enhancement in surface plasmon polariton (SPP) coupling with an 
InGaN/GaN quantum well (QW) by inserting a SiO2 layer of lower refractive index between 
the deposited Ag and GaN layers is experimentally and numerically demonstrated. The 
inserted SiO2 layer leads to reduced SPP dissipation rate, increased evanescent field intensity 
beyond a certain depth in GaN, and decreased SPP density of state. The combination of these 
factors can result in further emission enhancement of QW through SPP coupling. For 
light-emitting diode application, the elongated evanescent field coverage can release the 
constraint of thin p-type GaN for effective SPP coupling. More importantly, the reduced SPP 
dissipation can result in more effective emission in such an SPP-QW coupling mechanism.

技術特點說明： 
 
氮化鎵奈米柱的生長對發光二極體之發展具有如下之優點：(a)可降低氮化鎵內之線性差
排，提昇晶體品質；(b)可以產生散射，提高汲光效率；(c)於生長氮化銦鎵時，因側向
應變釋放，可提高銦成分；(d)可在奈米柱上進行接合再生長，以製作高品質之氮化鎵
樣板。若以自組成方式來從事氮化鎵奈米柱生長，奈米柱之大小高低及分佈位置相當雜
亂，品質並不佳，進行接合再生長時，結果不好，若以圖案化結構來生長，可得整齊劃
一且品質良好之奈米柱。目前世界上只有三個研究團隊可以生長排列整齊之奈米柱，一
個在日本，一個在美國，一個就是本研究團隊，但本研究團隊又有兩項特色，一為我們
以奈米壓印方式製作圖案，適合業界量產，另外，全球到目前為止只有我們接合再生長
成功，製作品質極高之氮化鎵樣板，線性差排密度降至107cm-2等級。同時，我們也在其
上成功的生長製作LED，藍光LED效率可增加將近100%。另外，我們發明在發光二極
體表面製作金屬奈米結構來產生表面電漿子與量子井耦合的效果，這種耦合效果可以加
強發光二極體整體發光效率，尤其針對原本內部量子效率不高的量子井，效果更明顯。
由於這種耦合效果可以分散載子發光管道，提昇復合效率，可用以減緩發光二極體在高
注入電流下之效率下滑趨勢。同時因表面電漿子具有偏振特性，這種耦合效果亦可用以
製作具偏振特性之發光二極體，這種發光二極體適合用於液晶顯示器之背光源。
