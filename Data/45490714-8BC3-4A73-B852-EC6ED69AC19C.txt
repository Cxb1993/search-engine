Abstract 
There are many different types of forms and manufacturing methods for pedal of bicycle 
in the current markets. The purpose of this research is mainly to innovative design a 
forging-press die of pedal of bicycle for the company’s needs. We first design a die with 
simulation analysis of bicycle’s pedal by utilizing a commercial finite element package software 
during research process. Moreover, a series of simulation analyses with the variables depended 
on different press velocity, temperature of billet and friction factor of the die is performed to 
evaluate the methods of light quantification in the forging press for bicycle’s pedal. The study 
modifies general pedal and highway pedal sizes. Under various forging conditions, it analyses 
the effective strain, the effective stress, the wear, heat transfer and the die radius load 
distribution of the billet. Moreover it used simulative software to analyze its forging process 
microstructure materials changing. It is assumed that the die is a die steel and heating in the 
analyses, furthermore, pedal forging temperature thermal conductivity due to temperature 
change as the mold. The material used the Aluminum (A6061 and A7075) to analyze simulative 
data. Next, the optimal parameters to control are obtained with Taguchi methods and Immune 
Genetic Algorithm, respectively. Finally, we identify the results of simulation analyses with 
design the experimental forging die to lower deformation behavior of bicycle’s pedal (including 
general pedal and highway pedal) 
Keywords：Bicycle chain wheel, Finite element, Forging, Taguchi method, Immune Genetic 
Algorithm.

1-3 文獻探討 
模具在工業產品中廣泛的被運用，因為它具備有快速、重複性高和大量生產價格便宜
等優點，幾乎所有的工業產品都必須依賴模具才能大量生產，依照沖壓模具之標準作業[1]，
從設計、試模直到開模都有一套嚴謹的規範，而本文自行車踏板設計參照模具設計基礎[2]、
模具設計問題[3]，應用模具使材料鍛壓成形，也就是所謂的金屬成形(metal forming)，於此
塑性變形被使用於將工件的形狀改變，金屬成形的工具通常為模(die)，用它施予超過金屬
降伏點的應力，金屬就會依模的形狀變形，施於使金屬塑性變形之壓力通常為壓縮性的，
隨之產生塑流應力[4]，為了能夠成功的成形，金屬必須具有降伏強度及高延展性[5]，然而
這些性質受溫度影響甚大，又可分為冷作(Cold Working)、溫作(Warm Working)、熱作(Hot 
Working)，本次之踏板模型就是使用熱鍛製程(Hot forging)[6]。 
 近年來有關鍛造的相關研究如下：楊俊彬和陳允修[7]以模擬軟體分析成形中模具所承
受之應力應變及材料流動之情形，找尋最佳化成形參數。黎琇瑩和李衍慶[8]認為鍛造的優
點是晶粒組織在鍛造的過程中會傾向緻密化，內部氣孔也可因此消除或是減少，此外連續
的晶粒流動所導致的機械性纖維化狀態，可使產品能因此得到最大的方向性強度。許光城
和黃英哲[9]使用有限元素法(DEFORMTM3D)模擬分析具孔形非軸對稱鍛件的成形與模具
設計之最佳化，並針對塑性加工製程中常見的實際應用問題，如負荷大小、材料尺寸與位
置、體積分配、溢料等現象加以分析研討，並針對缺陷來改善。施議訓和邱士哲等[10]實行
鍛壓加工時，材料內部會產生應力應變，此種塑性流變須用精密的塑性理論去計算，不過
在實際加工中其材料的變形狀況因受溫度、速度、潤滑的影響，會呈現出非常複雜的流變。
Wu和Hsu[11]探討有限元素法和實驗模式在擠製鍛造之流動情形。Forcellese和Gabriellei[12]
使用不同的步驟分析鋁材熱鍛造後的變溫變化。WeronAski[13]使用有限元素法分析鋁鍛造
後之結構變化。Skunca et al. [14]則用有限元素分析於改善冷鍛壓金屬成型技術。Zhang et al. 
[15]運用有限元素方法於 7075 鋁合金複雜的溫精鍛工件成形，並改善工件本身精度。 
 在鍛造加工配合有限元素分析軟體 DEFORMTM 3D 研究方面包括：陳狄成等人[16]研
究自行車踏板製程中之塑性應變與模具設計以及[17]自行車齒盤鍛壓在不同條件下的塑性
變形行為，尤麒熊[18]整合 QFD、TRIZ 及田口法研發破壞性創新之自行車鏈輪製程設計，
陳志明[19]研究 CPU 散熱片擠製配合使用田口方法設計模具。

2-2 有限元素與模擬軟體簡介 
2-2-1 有限元素法(Finite element method)之應用 
本研究分使用有限元素法來分析鋁合金踏板於鍛壓加工之成形性設計，有限元素法有
彈塑性有限元素法、剛塑性有限元素法。前者考慮應力、速度所有的關係式，後者不考慮
應力，以與能量法相同的原理定式化。分析塑性變形之有限元素法乃按照邊界幾何形狀及
加工形態，將工具及材料分割成若干元素，求得各元素勁度矩陣。並結合所有元素之徑度
矩陣，可得到全域勁度矩陣(Global stiffness matrix)，再考慮材料的應力-應變模式得到本構
方程式(Constitutive equation)，配合應力平衡關係式、應力及邊界條件，透過電腦運算求得
應力、應變分佈及外力荷重等。有限元素法的精確性與元素分割技巧、元素多寡、邊界條
件設定等有關，並且需要較多的運算時間。 
2-3 DEFORM 軟體之架構[20] 
DEFORM 是一套專門用來模擬材料受到工具作用時，產生塑性變形過程的有限元素法
軟體。DEFORM 2D 可模擬平面應變問題、軸對稱問題，而 DEFORM 3D 應用於三維實體
變形問題。基本上 DEFORM 是由多個模組組成，主要有前處理器(Pre-processor)、模擬引
擎(Simulation Engine)、後處理器(Post-processor)以及實用模組(Utility module)。DEFORM 的
分析範圍包括：(1)冷、溫、熱鍛之金屬成形；(2)玻璃、聚合物之固態成形；(3)粉末鍛造；
(4)變形中之模具撓曲與應變；(5)輥鍛。 
2-4 田口方法(Taguchi Method) 
2-4-1 田口式品質工程簡介 
本研究將使用工業上較具實用性之田口式品質工程法做為實驗規劃之方法，並且根據
品質特性資料特徵選定適當計算方式。田口式品質工程亦稱為田口方法，於 1949 年，田口
玄一博士於電信實驗室(Electrical Communication Lab.；ECL)為了改進傳統實驗設計之成本
效益問題，所發展出的實驗設計方法。田口認為品質代表社會成本、損失之結合。因此，
期望藉由二次損失函數(loss function)計算產品品質成本，進而得知品質良窳，經改善以達
品質最佳化[21]。 
田口式品質工程分為線上(On-Line)品質管制與線外(Off-Line)品質管制兩部份如圖 2-1

異及產品零件、材料劣化等所引起的產品機能變異上具有防範功能，因此對於產品的環境

2-6 延性破壞準則之分類 [24] 
在金屬成形過程中，由於受到塑性變形之影響，造成被加工物中缺陷的產生大都是因
為材料延性破壞所導致。金屬材料的脆性破壞是以結晶裂開面間，原子相互結合破壞為主
的微視現象，即劈開破壞(Cleavage fracture)且直到材料破壞之前，產生的應變量極小甚至
沒有塑性變形且無預警的形成破壞；而延性破壞則是屬於結晶滑移所致的剪斷破壞(Shear 
fracture) [25]。延性破壞可以被定義成是塑性變形所引起，以材料中存在的非金屬介在物、
析出物等微細粒子當作起點，形成空洞(Void)，並進一步成長、合體乃至最終破壞。而且造
成延性破壞的主要原因為加工參數和成形條件如變形率、潤滑和摩擦力等，其它影響破壞
的因素還包括其化學組成成份、微觀結構、表面粗糙度及均勻性等。

狀，主要是採用有限元素法(Finite element method)進行模擬分析，在塑性變形之假設中，有
限元素法可將胚料設定為剛塑性體或彈塑性體等性質，前者考慮應力、速度所有的關係式，
後者不考慮應力，以與能量法相同的原理定式化。剛塑性有限元素法雖然不能解決殘留應
力等問題，但可增大增分量，因此常用於大變形的解析。此法不需像彈塑性列式那樣求解
應力增量，而是在每一時間增量都直接求出應力，因此剛塑性有限元素法並無應力增分的
積分誤差等問題，所以反覆解析穩定變形時，收斂也良好。

Abstract—There are currently many different forms and 
manufacturing methods for bicycle seat post on the market. This study 
uses aluminum alloys (A7075) as research materials, and utilizes finite 
element analysis software along with Taguchi method to design and 
analyze in order to develop appropriate extrusion dies.  A series of 
simulation analyses in which the variables depend on different 
thicknesses of the long axis and short axis of the seat post are reveal the 
effectiveness of decreasing the warp and force deformation in the 
bicycle seat post extrusion process. We used the multi-quality 
characteristics (effective strain, effective stress and mold load) of 
Taguchi method to find the optimal design parameters of design factors. 
The simulation analysis provided insight into the optimal processing 
conditions of the sectional shape of an aluminum alloy (A7075) bicycle 
seat post. The experiment with simulation analysis shows satisfactory 
results, such as process, mould dimension and mould parameters are 
obtained. 
 
Keywords- Bicycle seat post, Finite element analysis, Taguchi method, 
Multi-quality characteristics. 
 
I. INTRODUCTION 
The industry of bicycle is currently prosperous, and 
Taiwan’s market mainly focuses on the production and 
development of high-price parts of various sectors. Aluminum 
alloys are light, highly intense and easily recycled. There are 
currently many different forms and manufacturing methods for 
bicycle seat post on the market. For the extrusion processing 
method, Lee and Yang [1] developed a steady-state finite 
element method and automatic mesh generation technique for 
hot extrusion through square dies, to provide a theoretical basis 
for optimal die design and process control. Metal flow pattern 
analysis is also very important in extrusion processes.  This 
experimental approach and finite element analysis were 
employed to study the effects of the process parameters on the 
multi-hole extrusion of aluminum-alloy A7075 tubes using the 
indirect extrusion process by Chen et al. [2]. Peng and Sheppard 
[3] used FORGE3TM FEM software to investigate the 
correlation between the number and distribution of the die holes 
in a multi-hole extrusion process and the optimal extrusion 
parameters. 
Zhou et al. [4] utilized a numerical simulation approach to 
investigate the isothermal extrusion of 7075 aluminum billets 
using a varying ram speed. Based upon the simulation results 
obtained from a series of constant ram speed extrusion runs, the 
authors presented two ram speed profiles to enable the 
isothermal extrusion of the aluminum billet at temperatures of 
480oC and 500oC, respectively. The author [5] employed rigid-
plastic finite element DEFORMTM 3D software to investigate 
the plastic deformation behavior of Ti-6Al-4V titanium alloy 
during its indirect extrusion through a single-hole die in the 
seamless tube fabrication process. Finally, we [6] used 
DEFORMTM 3D software to investigate the plastic deformation 
of Ti-6Al-4V titanium alloy during its indirect extrusion through 
a four-hole die. 
The current study uses DEFORMTM 3D software to 
investigate the plastic deformation of A7075 aluminum alloy 
during its extrusion through an elliptic mandrel die of bicycle 
seat post. This study also uses the Taguchi method to determine 
the optimum design parameters. The experiment with 
simulation analysis shows satisfactory results, such as process, 
mould dimension and mould parameters are obtained. 
 
II. SIMULATION PROCESS ANALYSIS AND TAGUCHI 
METHOD 
This study adopts the following assumptions: (1) the 
mandrel and extrusion die are rigid bodies; (2) the aluminum 
alloy (A7075) is a rigid-plastic material; and (3) the friction 
factors between the workpiece and the mandrel and extrusion 
die remain constant during the extrusion process. This study 
uses the shear friction equation for finite element analysis. The 
Taguchi method, a well-known robust-design technique, 
provides a comprehensive understanding of the individual and 
combined effects of various design parameters based on a 
minimum number of experimental trials. Its aim is to establish 
parameter settings that achieve a robust product that is 
unaffected by unavoidable variations in external noise.

II.FINITE ELEMENT METHOD 
The rigid-plastic finite element (FE) DEFORMTM 3D 
software is based on the design and forming processes for 
analytical models of two dimensional and three dimensional 
rigid-plastic deformations. Variations in forming processes 
appear in the control model, constant critical damage value, 
temperature after plastic deformation, effective stress, effective 
strain, plastic flow rate, and die radius load distribution of the 
work piece. Using this software simulating model not only 
saves time and money in tooling and mold testing but also 
provides highly critical information. Rigid-plastic finite 
element (FE) DEFORMTM 3D software is widely used in 
forging, extrusion, pulling, rolling, stamping, upsetting, and 
other forming processes of precision metals. This software is 
composed of multiple modules. The main structure can be 
divided into pre-treatment modules, simulation engine, 
post-processor modules, and multifunction modules.
