abstract 
        In order to know the influence of ultrasonic vibration on micro U-bending process, ultrasonic 
vibration was applied on U-bending die to produce the vibration during forming process. A1uminum 1050F 
foil with 0.15mm thickness was chosen for conducting this experiment. Through this research, the parameters 
including the punch load reduction, the spring back reduction, and the influence of different amplitude on 
punch load were acquired. This result could be the reference for the industrial application. 
KeywordsHultrasonic vibrationu springbackumicro U-bending process
