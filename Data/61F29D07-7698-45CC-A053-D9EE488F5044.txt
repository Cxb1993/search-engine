The mold life is very important to AG40A zinc die casting 
because of high production rate and wide use of the alloy. The 
experimental methods include PVD and nitriding treatments on the 
mold materials of SKD61, TDAC, P20 and S50C. The results show 
that mold materials treated with (Al,Ti)N-PVD coating have  best 
resistance of soldering, oxidation and hot checking. While mold 
materials, with nitriding treatment, have better oxidation-resistant 
and worse hot checking-resistant than with PVD coatings.
