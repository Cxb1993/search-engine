CuO/CexZr(Sn)1-xO2/Al2O3觸媒於富氫燃料中之CO選擇性氧化反應研究 
NSC 95–2221–E–008–140– 
摘要 
本研究以懸浮/共沈澱法將氧化鋁引入Ce1-xSnxO2固溶液中形成Ce1-xSnxO2/Al2O3混合氧
化物擔體，以臨濕含浸法製備CuO/Ce1-xSnxO2/Al2O3觸媒，詳細鑑定並應用於富氫進料中
之CO選擇性氧化反應。引入Al2O3能增加Ce1-xSnxO2的分散性，但不會改變Ce1-xSnxO2的
主結構。CeO2中引入錫能增加晶格氧的移動性，促進 7%CuO/ Ce1-xSnxO2/Al2O3(20%)觸
媒之CO選擇性氧化反應活性，適量錫的引入量為 1–x = 0.1–0.5。引入適量錫及Al2O3所
得之 7%CuO/CexSn1–xO2–Al2O3(20%)觸媒能應用於PEMFC燃料電池之PROX單元將CO
移除，不僅具有與貴金屬觸媒 5%Pt/Al2O3相當的良好活性，選擇率更遠高於 5%Pt/Al2O3。 
關鍵詞：一氧化碳、選擇性氧化、 CuO觸媒、CeO2、CexSn1–xO2–Al2O3 
 
Abstract 
The solid solutions of CexSn1–xO2 incorporated with alumina to form CexSn1–xO2–Al2O3 mixed 
oxides, by the suspension/co-precipitation method, were used to prepare 
CuO/CexSn1–xO2–Al2O3 catalysts for the selective oxidation of CO in excess hydrogen. 
Incorporating Al2O3 increased the dispersion of CexSn1–xO2, but did not change their main 
structures and did not weaken their redox properties. Doping Sn4+ into CeO2 increased the 
mobility of lattice oxygen and enhanced the activity of the 7%CuO/CexSn1–xO2–Al2O3 catalyst 
in the selective oxidation of CO. The selective oxidation of CO was weakened as the doped 
fraction of Sn4+ exceeded 0.5. Incorporating appropriate amounts of Sn4+ and Al2O3 could 
obtain good candidates 7%CuO/CexSn1–xO2–Al2O3(20%), 1–x = 0.1–0.5, for a preferential 
oxidation (PROX) unit in a polymer electrolyte membrane fuel cell system for removing CO. 
Its activity was comparable with, and its selectivity was much larger than, that of the noble 
catalyst 5%Pt/Al2O3. 
Keywords: CO; Selective oxidation; CuO catalyst; CeO2 oxide; CexSn1–xO2–Al2O3 mixed 
oxides

計畫成果自評 
本研究適度修正原預定計畫，以錫替代鋯，完成下列研究項目及目標： 
1.完成Ce1-xSnxO2/Al2O3擔體及CuO/Ce1-xSnxO2/Al2O3觸媒製備與鑑定。 
2.完成Ce1-xSnxO2/Al2O3擔體redox性質分析。  
3.完成CuO/Ce1-xSnxO2/Al2O3觸媒於CO選擇性氧化反應測試。  
4.評估H2O/CO2對CO選擇性氧化反應與觸媒穩定度的影響。 
5.完成CuO/Ce1-xSnxO2/Al2O3觸媒處理氣體容量之測試。 
6.完成CuO/Ce1-xSnxO2/Al2O3觸媒應用於燃料電池系統PROX反應單元之實用性評估。 
7.本計劃成果投稿於 International Journal of Hydrogen Energy (2006 Impact Factor: 
2.612)。 
 
綜合評估 
以最佳之 7%CuO/Ce0.9Sn0.1O2/Al2O3(20%)為觸媒，在含有 15% CO2及 10% H2O進
料於T100的反應溫度 160℃下(轉化率 100%，選擇率 90%)進行觸媒穩定性測試，經 200
小時反應後，轉化率僅由 100% 微降至 96%，且選擇率仍維持 90%以上，顯示
7%CuO/Ce0.9Sn 0.1O2/Al2O3(20%)觸媒為一具有高穩定性的觸媒，能克服商業Pt觸媒耗
損大量燃料氫氣的缺點，具有替代商業Pt觸媒應用於燃料電池系統PROX反應單元之實
用潛力。
