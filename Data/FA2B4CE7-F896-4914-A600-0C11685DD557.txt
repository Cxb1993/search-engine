Abstract
The research was based on the relatived
literatures collected from domestic and
overseas resources. Through inducting and
arranging the information ,a self
-administered questionnaire was developed.
After consulting opinions of thirty-three
experts, the self-administered questionnaire
turned into the expert questionnaire, and
preliminary performance scale of safety and
hygiene was obtained. The structured
questionnaire was developed to conduct the
assessment of safety and hygiene
performance. The recovery rate of effective
questionnaires was 82.5%.The reliability of
effective questionnaires was 0.847?
Keywords?occupational safety and health
management system?passive performance
indicator of safety and health , active
performance indicator of safety and
health ,voluntary protection program for
safety and health.
