摘要 
從新菌種的研究與PHAs醱酵生產，到功能性高分子材料的開發與應用，本計畫執
行的重點包括新型的PHAs生產與鑑定、高分子薄膜、奈米纖維的製備與結構分析，及
生醫應用研究。研究中，成功利用Cupriavidus taiwanensis 184生產新型PHAs─聚硫酯
(Polythioester, PTE) 。 另 外 ， 以 電 紡 絲 技 術 製 備 poly(3-hydroxybutyrate-co-3- 
hydroxyhexanoate)/ poly(D,L-lactic acid)摻合奈米纖維膜，利用XRD及DSC研究膜材結晶
結構及熱力學，並與緻密膜進行比較。同時亦製備非順向與順向奈米纖維膜，建立模型
解釋纖維膜的機械性質影響變因。在高分子薄膜應用性質方面，引入正子消散時間光譜
儀(PALS)，測定PHAs膜材自由體積，探討材料結晶結構對自由體積的影響，以及與滲
透性質的關聯性。除了材料物理性質的研究，生物相容性的測試亦為探討重點。將老鼠
的小腦顆粒狀神經元細胞培養於PHAs薄膜上，結果顯示細胞在PHAs薄膜表面生長良好
且呈現貼附行為，說明PHAs電紡膜於生醫材料應用上的發展潛力。

3. 結果與討論 
3.1 PTE結構與組成 
首先，對於發酵後得到之產物進行化學組成的確
認，因此利用紅外線光譜儀來判斷其產物的官能基。
Fig.1所示為使用Cupriavidus taiwanensis 184菌種所發酵
的產物之紅外線光譜(IR)圖譜。對於Fig. 1 [I]中所出現的
吸收峰，與文獻上PHB 的吸收峰比較[3]，由此可以得
知沒有添加第二碳源的高分子為PHB，而於1724 cm-1的
訊號峰代表為酯基中之C=O鍵結，並且在Fig.1 [II] 、 [III] 
中，都有出現位於1724 cm-1的訊號峰；而[II]與[III]之光
譜，位於1724 cm-1的訊號峰附近出現1687 cm-1訊號峰，
此為thioester 的C=O鍵結，由此可知，使用Cupriavidus 
taiwanensis 184菌種所發酵並在發酵過程中添加第二碳
源所得到的產物，含有酯基中之C=O 鍵結與thioester 的
C=O 鍵結，但是由於訊號並不是相當明顯，所以需再經
由其他測試來得証。由凝膠層析儀檢測得到發酵所得到
的產物，其重量平均分子量大約為600,000~800,000 Da，
而聚合分佈指數(PDI)為1.1~1.8 的單一型態的分布，其
數值表示於Table 2中。 
Fig. 2 為使用氣相層析質譜儀來檢驗添加第二碳源
為20 g/L的產物，(a)為S-2的樣品之氣相層析滯留時間
圖，並由資料庫的比對，得知滯留時間(RT)為3.59 min 得
訊號為3HB 的片段；而滯留時間(RT)為4.31 min 的訊號
峰為3MP 的片段。並且由氣相層析質譜儀的測試，證明
產物確實含有3HB與3MP的片段。而(b)為3HB與3MP 的
滯留時間放大圖；再把這兩個訊號峰經過質譜儀的分
析，(c) 與 (d) 各別為 3HB與 3MP 的質譜分析。 由
資料庫的比對與質譜的分析得知由在發酵過程中添加第
二碳源所得到產物，為同時含有3HB 與 3MP的片段的
高分子。經由前兩項測試，可以證明樣品中含有硫的官
能基與片段，而樣品結構上的碳、氫鍵結位置，則須經
由核磁共振儀之測試來證明。Fig. 3為使用Cupriavidus 
taiwanensis 184菌種發酵的高分子之500 MHz液態核磁
共振-碳(13C)譜 (a) 和 500 MHz 液態核磁共振-氫(1H)

和20 ppm的訊號峰，各別代表P(3HB-co-3MP)結構中的
C-4、C-5、C-6 和C-7；於77 ppm 為CDCl3的訊號。而

P(3HB-co-3MP)中2-H與1-H；在1.26、2.56 (2.46) 和5.24 
ppm 訊號峰代表為P(3HB-co-3MP)中5-H、3-H和4-H

Cupriavidus taiwanensis 184 菌種所發酵的產物為
P(3-HB)和P(3HB-co-3MP)。由元素分析儀(EA)測定樣品
硫的含量，呈現於Table. 2 中，其S-1與S-2 的樣品其硫

PHBHHx/PDLLA blends were immiscible in the 
amorphous state. To observe the melting behaviors is 
another method for determining the miscibility of blend 
polymer. The Tm of PHBHHx in the cast membranes are at 
133 and 150 oC, the Tm of PHBHHx in the electrospun 
membranes are at 151~153 oC, and both of the Tms do not 
change with the composition in the blends. These results 
also indicate the blend systems were immiscible. Zhang et 
al. showed that PHB and PDLLA blended system is also 
immiscible [2].  
In this study, the blend systems are immiscible and the 
PDLLA protion can still crystallize slightly although it is 
amorphous mostly. The theoretical enthalpy of fusion of 
PHBHHx can be calculated from  
ΔHm (J/g) =ΔHm,PHBHHx × rPHBHHx + ΔHm,PDLLA × (1- rPDLLA ) 
where ΔHm,PHBHHx and ΔHm, PDLLA are the enthalpy of 
fusion of pure PHBHHx and pure PDLLA , ΔHm is 
theoretical enthalpy of fusion of blends, rPHBHHx is the 
weight fraction of PHBHHx in the blend. The relationship 
of PHBHHx content and enthalpy of fusion in the blending 
systems can be identified. The experimental and theoretical 
values of enthalpy of fusion were very close. This 
suggested that one can control the PHBHHx content to 
obtain blending membranes of different crystallinity. The 
enthalpy of fusion of the blends decreased from 82.8 to 
23.4 J/g and from 62.9 to 18.6 J/g, respectively, with 
decreasing PHBHHx content from 100% to 25% in the cast 
and electrospun membranes (Fig. 1). The enthalpy of fusion 
of the electrospun membranes was always smaller than the 
cast membranes with any blend ratio. This because 
electrospun membranes had size limit which effected the 
crystalline spherulite growth so that the crystallinity 
decreased [5]. In our previous study, it was found that there 
were two melting peaks on the melting curve of the 
PHBHHx cast membranes due to the melting- 
recrystallization-remelting (mrr) mechanism and only one 
melting point on the melting curve of the electrospun ones 
because the melting was simply from the recrystallization 
during the heating run [6]. Similar results were obtained in 
the blended membranes. As PDLLA content increased, the 
double melting peaks became clear (Fig. 2). Secondary 
crystallization of PHBHHx could occur in the preparation 
of cast membrane; therefore, the melting peak at about 130 
oC became clear due to the melting of the less stable 
secondary crystallization portion.  
 
3.2 Crystallization behavior of blend membranes 
Fig. 3 shows X-ray diffraction patterns of cast blended 
membranes. PHBHHx membrane exhibited crystalline 
peaks at (020), (110), (111), (031), (040) and (002). The 
electrospun PHBHHx membrane only showed crystalline 
peaks at (020) and (110) (Fig. 4) due to the molecular 
orientation along the axial direction of the fibers [6]. The 
pattern of PDLLA had a very broad profile, which 
indicated that PDLLA was an amorphous polymer. The 
intensity of those peaks gradually decreased as the fraction 
of PDLLA increased in both cast and electrospun blend 
membranes. The intensity of those peaks gradually 
decreased as the fraction of PDLLA increased in the blend 
system; therefore, the crystalline structure didn’t change in 
the blend membranes.  
 
3.3 Mechanical properties  
The elastic modulus of pure PHBHHx electrospun 
membranes was smaller than that of blend membranes (Fig 
5). It indicated pure electrospun PHBHHx membrane is 
softer than the electrospun blended membranes. As PDLLA 
content increased, the elongation at break of blend 
electrospun membranes increased from 270 to 540%, the 
tensile strength increased from 6 to 8 MPa, and the 
toughness increased from 1,300 to 2,800 J/m3 (Table2). The 
blend membranes became more flexible, tough and ductile 
as the PDLLA content increased. Because the glass 
transition temperature of PDLLA is abort 57 oC, which is 
above the room temperature, the mobility of polymer chain 
is restricted. The electrospun membrane of pure PDLLA is 
too brittle to be cut into a complete membrane for tensile 
teat. This result suggests that the mechanical properties of  
PDLLA improve a lot when just 25% PHBHHx is added. 
 
4. Conclusions  
The blend system of PHBHHx/PDLLA is immiscible 
because there are two individual glass transition 
temperatures in the PHBHHx/PDLLA blend systems and 
the melting point of PHBHHx does not increase or decrease 
with increasing PDLLA content. Although PHBHHx and 
PDLLA blending were immiscible, PHBHHx wound 
improve the mechanical properties of PDLLA and enhance 
the application properties of PDLLA electrospun 
membranes. PDLLA is an amorphous polymer; it is 
expected that PDLLA will promote the degradation rate in 
the blending systems. The study of the degradation 
properties of the blends will be carried out in our future 
works.

cellular behavior were modulated by adjusting the 
characteristics of PHA films fabricated from various 
process. RCGNs on the HX-ES film performed better 
spread and higher viability than those on other films.

本研究為「永續性生物聚酯高分子材料特性與其應用」三年計畫之第一年，進行環
境低負荷高分子材料的生產發展與應用性開發。從新菌種的研究與 PHAs 醱酵生產，到
功能性高分子材料的開發與應用，預期完成的工作項目與成果自評如下：  
 
1.  微生物醱酵生產 
成功利用台灣本土根瘤菌─Cupriavidus taiwanensis 184菌種，經由兩階段式醱酵生產
生物聚酯Polythioesters (PTEs)。醱酵程序中第一階段進行菌數累積，第二階段則提供含硫
的碳源(3,3'-thioipropionic acid，TDP)當作額外的營養源，以提供菌種在醱酵過程中所需
要的硫的來源，生產Poly(3-hydroxybutyrate-co-3-mercaptopropinate) (P(3HB-co-3MP))。 
P(3HB-co-3MP)中硫含量與醱酵過程中添加TDP的含量成正比，目前可生產3MP含量
為0-15 mol%的copolymer。由熱性質分析可知，共聚合物中3HB與3MP會各自形成結晶，
而PTE整體結晶速率隨3MP增加而下降，但熱穩定性不受3MP含量影響。此外，以
Cupriavidus taiwanensis 184 菌種醱酵生產之PHB與PTE系列的熱穩定性皆優於市售之
PHB。 
 
2. 高分子薄膜與奈米纖維 
利 用 電 紡 絲 技 術 ， 成 功 製 備 Poly(3-hydroxybutyrate-co-3-hydroxyhexanoate) 
/Poly(D,L-lactic acid) (PHBHHx/PDLLA)摻合電紡膜。由熱性質分析可知兩種高分子摻合
屬不互溶系統(immiscible)，摻合後擁有兩個高分子各自的Tg，因此認為PHBHHx與PDLLA
摻合在無定型區是不互溶的(immiscrible)。 
在奈米纖維膜的結晶行為研究上，由於無定型的 PDLLA 被排除在 PHBHHx 之結晶
層板堆之外，故 PDLLA 的添加不影響 PHBHHx 之結晶生成，結晶熔融溫度不受 PDLLA
添加影響。由 XRD 結果探討結晶結構，可知奈米纖維的結晶受一維奈米尺寸效應影響，
而沿著纖維軸向堆疊生長，具有順向性，此與緻密膜的球晶結構截然不同。除此之外，
奈米纖維結晶速率較慢，故結晶度較低。 
 
3. 高分子薄膜應用性質 
鎔鑄膜與電紡膜之機械性質研究上發現，電紡膜受到分子鏈排列的影響，有較佳的
延展性。同時製備無定向以及具有方向性的電紡纖維膜，進一步探討非順向與順向纖維
膜其機械性質的變化，纖維膜的拉伸率與韌性受到材料孔隙度與纖維的順向性影響，而
由順向性的纖維膜可間接獲得纖維本身的機械性質。PDLLA 之添加使纖維膜延展性上
升，變的較為柔軟，使材料的應用範圍更廣。後續的研究將延續本計畫的第二、三年期
的內容，進行電紡纖維膜的生物相容性、降解行為等的研究。 
利用正子消散時間光譜儀(PALS)測定高分子膜材自由體積，探討不同製程 PHAs 膜
材結晶結構對自由體積與氣體滲透性質的影響。相關內容已於近期發表於期刊中。 
 
5. 生物相容性

6.會議心得  
薄膜（membrane）相關知識與技術可說是發展相當迅速，其運用領域也越
來越寬廣，包括廢水處理、蛋白質吸附、細胞培育基材、水質淨化以及最近相
當火熱的燃料電池等。在歐洲，因為薄膜技術的發達，其薄膜不但有高效率的
過濾品質且價格低廉，於是使用薄膜來淨化水質已經相當普遍的事。使用薄膜
來淨化水質來取代傳統用氯來消毒，不但可消除氯對人體的危害，增加食用的
口感，以及能更有效率的達成過濾的品質，而反觀台灣在這條路上還有很多需
要改善的空間，雖然部分家庭可自費購買薄膜過濾裝置，但其價格相當昂貴。 
在現今環保意識抬頭，廢水處理這環節已經受到公司相當程度的重視，而
利用薄膜技術來處理，不但可以讓廢水可以進行再利用，其效能也遠遠高於其
他使用化學藥劑吸附的方式，同時其時間更短，效益更高，例如國內中原薄膜
中心就有協助許多廠商的經驗，不但為中心帶來許多研發經費，且也為保護台
灣的環保盡份心力。只是台灣在這領域上，仍需要許多廠商的投入，才有更高
的效益。 
今日石油價格的高漲，更促使人類更積極尋求替代性能源的動作，其中燃
料電池便是被寄於厚望，而薄膜在燃料電池中扮演舉足輕重的角色，不但具有
高質傳、低阻塞率、高效能還需具有低成本及耐高溫等特點。在此研討會中，
不難發覺歐洲各國在這方面的研究進度相當快速，只是其報告內容是屬於實驗
室成果，無法得知其產業界的實際發展情況。
