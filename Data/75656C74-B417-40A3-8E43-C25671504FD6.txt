行政院國家科學委員會專題研究計劃成果報告 
以微波加熱觸媒(Pt/Fe2O3或 Pd/Fe2O3)固定床焚化石化業廢水處理廠含揮發性有機物廢氣流之研究 
Catalytic incineration of the VOCs gas flow from the wastewater treatment plant in petrifaction 
industry over the Pt/Fe2O3 or Pd/Fe2O3 packed bed by microwave heating process 
計劃編號：NSC 96-2211-E-127-002 
 執行期限：96年8月1日至97年7月31日 
 主持人：李炳楠 高鳳技術學院休閒事業管理系 副教授 
 
一、中文摘要 
(關鍵字：揮發性有機物、石化工業、微波、觸
媒焚化) 
本研究將α-Fe2O3改質使其具有觸媒功能，
藉由降低VOCs反應活化能之方式，可在較低溫
度(＜300 )℃ 下進行觸媒焚化處理。依據實驗數
據顯示，Pt/Fe2O3或Pd/Fe2O3觸媒固定床(直徑4.8 
cm、高度16 cm)以微波(2450 MHz、385 W)進行
加熱時，其不同高程位置的溫度可在15分鐘內
自室溫快速上升至350-610℃之間，且持續加熱
時溫度變化量維持在±10℃之範圍內，接近一般
工業製程常用相關揮發性有機物(VOCs)的燃點
溫度，因此具有將該等VOCs焚化的功能。 
本研究共製備完成6種觸媒，分別依Pd或Pt
之含量(x wt.%)標記為x-Pd/Fe2O3或x-Pt/Fe2O3，
如含0.76%之Pd/Fe2O3則標記為076-Pd/Fe2O3。經
結合微波加熱在相同操作條件(PMW=525 W、
R.H.=90% 、 GHSV=5240 hr － 1) 下，針對苯
(Benzene)、甲苯(Toluene)和丙酮(Acetone)進行
焚化處理，且以破壞去除率(DRE)為評估基準，
篩選出023-Pt/Fe2O3觸媒對該三種VOCs具有最
高之DRE值。此外，在操作VOCs之去除效率高
於99.9%之條件下，微波加熱023-Pt/Fe2O3觸媒固
定床所需之電功率可低至385 W，相較於一般傳
統觸媒焚化法至少約需550 W。因此，本研究所
發展之微波加熱觸媒焚化揮發性有機物技術，
將可有效減低處理石化工廠廢水處理場廢氣流
中VOCs之能源成本。 
Abstract 
Keywords: Volatile organic compounds (VOCs), 
petrifaction industry, Microwave irradiation, 
Catalytic incineration. 
In this study, α-Fe2O3 was modified to be as 
a catalyst in order to perform the incineration of 
VOCs well at a lower temperature (＜300℃) due 
to it can reduce activation energy barrier for 
catalytic incineration of VOCs. Basis on the 
experimental results, temperature on the catalyst 
(Pt/Fe2O3 and/or Pd/Fe2O3) surface is various at 
different location of the fixed bed, which is in a 
diameter of 4.8 cm and with a height of 16 cm, 
during a microwave-induced heating process. It 
was also observed that the fixed bed can be 
heating up to a range of 350-610℃ from room 
temperature in 12 min, and their temperature 
variations were found to be kept within ±10℃. 
There six kinds of catalysts are prepared and 
is named as x-Pd/Fe2O3 or x-Pt/Fe2O3 in relation 
to its metal content, such as 076-Pd/Fe2O3 
represents a catalyst with 0.76% of Pd. The fixed 
bed with the prepared catalysts were heated by 
microwave irradiation under the same operating 
condition, at a power of 525W, a moisture content 
of 90% and a GHSV of 5240 hr － 1, to do 
incineration of a gas mixture of benzene, toluene 
and acetone. The named 023-Pt/Fe2O3 catalyst was 
observed to have the highest DRE value of the six 
catalysts. In addition, the microwave-induced 
incineration process over a 023-Pt/Fe2O3 fixed bed 
needs a power of 385 W only, which is much 
lower than a 550 W power needed for a traditional 
catalytic incineration process. Therefore, the 
microwave-induced catalytic incineration of VOCs 
process developed in this research can be a 
promising technology for saving energy cost in 
treatment of the VOCs gas flow from the 
wastewater treatment plant in petrifaction industry.

4.流氣體空間流速對處理 VOCs 效能之影響 
由於進流氣之GHSV值高低直接影響VOCs
在觸媒固定床停留時間的長短，因此分別採用
三種GHSV值(3600、5280和8000 hr－1)，探討此
項變因對微波加熱觸媒固定進行床焚化VOCs
之影響程度。由實驗數據(如圖2)得知，雖然進
流氣之GHSV值為3600 hr－1時幾乎可將VOCs完
全去除，但是當GHSV值為5280 hr－1時，三種
VOCs的DRE值皆可達99.9%以上。

2.觸媒種類對處理VOCs效能之影響 
為了解觸媒種類對處理VOCs效能之影
響，分別將上述6種觸媒填充為固定床(直徑1.8 
cm、高度12 cm)，在相同操作條件(PMW=525 W、
GHSV=5240 hr－1)下，結合微波加熱觸媒固定床
方式針對苯 (Benzene)、甲苯 (Toluene)和丙酮
(Acetone)進行焚化處理，且自反應器的尾端收
集出流氣，再以氣相層析儀-火焰離子偵測器
(GC-FID ； HP 6890 ， Hewlett-Packard Co., 
Alpharetta, GA, USA)檢測該三種VOCs的濃
度，且以破壞去除率(DRE)為評估基準，篩選出
023-Pt/Fe2O3觸媒之處理效能較佳(三種VOCs之
DRE皆高於99%)，且利用它來進行後續實驗，
探討微波輸出功率和進流氣體空間流速(GHSV)
等對微波加熱觸媒焚化VOCs效能之影響。
