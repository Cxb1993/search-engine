2摘要
本文於彎道動床試驗中利用雙排「成功筐網(CKPB)」導流工法來保護彎道凹岸。試驗過程為清水
沖刷，設置參數為設置角度( 30 、 60 、 90 )，列間距與 CKPB 投影長度的比值 DL (0、1、2、3、4、
5、6、7)。經由試驗結果發現，在最佳的設置(設置角度、列間距)下，雙排成功筐網(CKPB)製造流速
緩慢區於筐網群下游形成落淤，並且將主水流導往渠道中心線。結果顯示，在相對曲率為 4.17、福祿
數為 0.45 等條件下，最佳雙排 CKPB 設置(位於渠道彎角約 25 與 31 度，且設置角度同為 30 度)，對於
原刷深點(位於約渠道彎角 49 度處) ，雙排 CKPB 最佳組之沖刷減少率可達到 104%，而對於面積沖刷
減少率，雙排 CKPB 亦達到 57%；單排 CKPB 最佳組之沖刷減少率為 93%，面積沖刷減少率為 51%，顯示
雙排 CKPB 設置保護效果良好，其最佳設置的效果亦比單排 CKPB 明顯。另外，結果也顯示雙排 CKPB 改
變第二排設置角度後的效果，不如設置角度同為 30 度的情況下明顯。。
關鍵字：成功筐網、彎道。
ABSTRACT
This project is about protecting the concave bank by Double rows of Cheng-Kung Porous Basket
(CKPB) in a curved channel. In this channel, no sediments were added during the experiments. The setting
parameters are: setting angles  of 30 , 60 and 90 ; and the ratio of the distance between the double rows
to the projected length of the CKPB DL equal to 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 and 7. From the experiments, we found
that the best set of double rows of CKPB (in terms of angle and ratio of distance between rows), can make a
slow flow velocity area behind it, and also guide the main stream away from the concave bank. In the
conditions of a relative curvature of 4.17 and Froude number equal to 0.45, the results show that for the best
set of Double rows of CKPB (located on 25and 31bending angles of the channel and the setting angle of
30), the scour reduction comes up to 104% to the original deepest point in the concave(located on 49
bending angle of the channel), and an area scour reduction of up to 57%, also a scour reduction of 93% to the
original deepest point in the concave, and a scour reduction of the area in the concave to up to 51% in the best
set of single row of CKPB. These results show that the Double rows of CKPB provide excellent protection
against girdle scouring, and in the best set, the Double rows of CKPB are better than single ones. Besides, the
effect of changing the setting angle of the second row CKPB is not better than having both the same.is studied
the flow field of the porous cylinder (PC) in one group. Experimental conditions are including the velocity
V=23.2 cm/sec, the water depth H=16cm, the ratio of the porous area and total area, ε=0.62. The
experimental results are shown that the velocity u and v will be increase in the outer area of the porous
cylinder as the number of the PC increased. For the number increased it brings about the flow field thrown
into unstable state.
Keyword：Bend channel, permeable/porous structure.

四、結論
一、單排 CKPB 設置不同位置與角度之影響
由試驗(Case-A~Case-I)發現，將成功筐網設置於彎道凹岸，對於原刷深點 s=240(cm)(渠道彎角約
為49o之處)，以設置位置 s=120(cm)(渠道彎角為25o 之處，距原沖刷點約兩倍渠寬的距離)與設置角度
30 的組合，效果最佳，原沖刷點沖刷減少率可達 93%，凹岸垂向面積沖刷減少率達 51%。
二、雙排 CKPB 設置不同無因次列間距之影響
評估雙排成功筐網設置，其無因次列間距 0DL ，也就是第二排 CKPB 設置於第一排 CKPB 末根中
心點垂直投影至凹岸處，保護效果最好，原沖刷點沖刷減少率可達 104%，呈淤積狀態；而凹岸垂向面
積沖刷減少率為 57%。
三、改變第二排 CKPB 設置角度之影響
無論是以沖刷減少率或者面積沖刷減少率來評估，改變第二排筐網群設置角度的效果都不如雙排
CKPB 設置角度皆同為 30 的情況下好。
四、雙排 CKPB 與單排 CKPB 之最佳組比較
雙排 CKPB 最佳組之無因次列間距為 0，無論是對原沖刷點沖刷減少率或者凹岸垂向面積沖刷減少
率，雙排 CKPB 最佳組的值皆比單排 CKPB 最佳組的效果好。(雙排 CKPB 分別設置於渠道彎角為25o 與31o
處，單排 CKPB 設置於渠道彎角為25o )
