Abstract 
In a multi-domain environment, after a user 
logins to a domain, she needs to login to another 
domain if she wants to access the resources in 
that domain.  Thus, it will be more convenient 
to the users, as well as increases the degree of 
automation, to have a single sign on (SSO) 
mechanism.  In this project, we proposed to use 
a Multi-Domain Controller (MDC) mechanism 
to coordinate access control in different domain, 
to map roles in a domain to another domain, so 
that a user does not have to login another domain 
in order to access the resources in that domain.
