行政院國家科學委員會專題研究計畫成果報告 
雙模態體聲波質量感測器系統之研製 
Fabrication of dual mode bulk acoustic resonator mass sensor system 
計畫編號：NSC 98-2221-E-237 -002 
執行期限：98年8月1日至99年7月31日 
計畫主持人：鄭建銓 教授（德霖技術學院電子工程系） 
 
中文摘要 
本研究使用具有雙模態之氧化鋅(ZnO)
壓電薄膜以研製高品質體聲波質量感測
器。ZnO壓電薄膜係採用反應性射頻磁控濺
鍍法，以離軸方式成長，經由X光繞射儀
(XRD)與場發射式掃描電子顯微鏡(FE-SEM)
分析得知所成長之C軸傾斜ZnO壓電薄膜，
具有縱波與剪波雙模態壓電效應；接著利用
反應性離子蝕刻法(Reactive ion etching， 
RIE)，以研製完成背蝕型薄膜體聲波共振器
(FBAR)。之後再進行縱波空氣質量測試、
剪波液態質量測試與酸鹼測試，以獲得高品
質及耐酸鹼侵蝕之雙模態體聲波質量感測
器。至於FBAR元件的頻率響應係採用
HP8720網路分析儀與CASCADE高頻探針
座量測之。實驗的結果顯示剪波模態在水中
的品質因子仍可維持高Q值，顯示其可應用
於液態感測。另外在本研究範圍內FBAR元
件的耐酸鹼特性還不錯。 
 
關鍵字：氧化鋅、體聲波、質量感測器、品
質因子 
 
Abstract 
In this project, we focused on the 
fabrication of high-performance mass sensors 
based on dual mode film bulk acoustic 
resonators (FBAR). The piezoelectric layer, 
zinc oxide (ZnO) thin films, was deposited by 
off-axis technique in a reactive rf magnetron 
sputtering system. The crystallization and 
microstructure of ZnO thin films were 
investigated using the X-ray diffraction (XRD) 
and Field-emission scanning electron 
microscopy (FE-SEM), respectively. The 
results showed that the c-axis tilted ZnO had 
the capability to excite the dual-mode 
resonances, namely, the longitudinal and shear 
mode resonances. The resonant cavity of 
back-etching type resonator was manufactured 
using the techniques of RIE to develop the 
FBAR devices. The mass loading was 
developed by shear wave and longitude wave 
in air and liquid, respectively. The sensitive 
area of mass sensor was also investigated to 
endure acidity and alkalinity. The frequency 
responses of FBAR devices were measured by 
the HP8720 network analyzer and CASCADE 
probe station. The experimental results 
indicated that the quality factor (Q) of shear 
wave in liquid was also kept at high value. On 
the other hand, the tolerance of resonators 
to acidity and alkalinity was excellent in 
this study. 
 
Keywords: ZnO, film bulk acoustic wave, 
mass sensor, quality factor 
 
一、前言 
自從 1965 年表面聲波(Surface acoustic 
wave, SAW)成功的應用在壓電基板後，各種
與 SAW 相關的技術與元件不斷的被開發，
在通訊的應用上呈現許多優異的特性，例如
體積輕薄短小、功率損耗低、工作頻率高以
及可大量運用半導體技術生產等。由於聲波
的傳遞速度與其傳遞路徑上的介質有關，所
以 SAW 也被用來作為各種材料的感測器，
例如氣體感測、生物醫學感測和壓力感測
漂移係數低且導電性佳的白金 (Pt)作為
FBAR 元件之頂電極與底電極，並使用鈦(Ti)
金屬作為成長白金電極的晶種層，藉以改善
白金電極與基板之間的附著度與粗糙度；利
用雙靶濺鍍系統成長鈦晶種層與白金底電
極，鈦晶種層與白金電極的厚度分別為 12 
nm 與 900 nm [15]。 
ZnO 壓電薄膜的成長的方式係採用室
溫兩階段反應性射頻磁控濺鍍法，首先沉積
一層表面粗糙度低的緩衝層，第二階段使用
高 C 軸優選的濺鍍參數，以獲得低表面粗糙
度與高 C 軸優選的 ZnO 壓電薄膜，濺鍍參
數如表一所示[16]。另外為了成長 C 軸傾斜
的 ZnO 薄膜，將晶片放置偏離靶材中心正
下方，藉以調整濺鍍離子團由靶材入射至基
板表面的角度，而偏離靶材中心正下方的距
離為 4 cm 如圖二所示[17]。

四、結論 
本研究採用離軸式反應性射頻磁控濺
鍍法，成功的沉積具有雙模態的 ZnO 壓電
薄膜，並將其製作成具有雙頻響應的 FBAR
感測元件，由頻率響應量測結果得知，剪波
模態於液態中的品質因子衰減率較小，故適
合應用於液態感測。另外雙模態 FBAR 感測
器經硫酸 (H2SO4)及過氧化氫 (H2O2)侵蝕
後，結果顯示對於元件之共振頻率並無明顯
的影響。未來將於 FBAR 元件上結合單分子
自我組裝法，並配合微量自動滴定系統，發
展在液態感測之生醫感測應用上。

Introduction 
Proton exchange (PE) in lithium niobate (LiNbO3) is a very simple and convenient technique 
for the fabrication of optical and acoustic waveguides. Immersion of LiNbO3 into molten proton 
sources [1-18] result in the exchange of the Li+ ions by protons, and the new crystalline structure 
Li1-xHxNbO3 is established. This process is a low-temperature operation, which is inexpensive and 
easy to control. The advantages of PE LiNbO3 optical waveguides over the conventional 
Ti-indiffusion counterparts are a nearly step-like refractive index profile, larger increase of the 
extraordinary refractive index, higher threshold of the optical damage and relatively ease of 
fabrication. 
It has been showed that annealed proton-exchanged (APE) LiNbO3 waveguides exhibit lower 
propagation loss than PE waveguides and the restoration of the electro-optic effect [19], and the 
nonlinear coefficients [20]. Therefore, the APE LiNbO3 waveguides are now widely used in 
integrated optics. In terms of acousto-optic interaction, surface acoustic wave (SAW) plays an 
important role in the optical waveguides used in analog signal processing such as a spectrum 
analyzer. It is well known that PE LiNbO3 waveguides may change the surface acoustic wave 
(SAW) properties [7-9], which offer new applications in the fields of guided SAW and
