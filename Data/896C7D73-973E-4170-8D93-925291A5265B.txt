Abstract 
    There are a lot benefit of save energy environmental protection, and durable for LED 
to replace CCFL in backlight system. In recent years, the LED backlight system is developed 
for large size. It is prone to produce dark band in backlight system, because LED is point of light 
source. The uniformity of the backlight system is arrived by a lot of LEDs in light bar, 
but heat is increased at the same time. In this study, the linear LED light fitting 
included microstructure is fabricated to the refractive film of Al, and flexible light 
guide of PDMS. It increases uniformity light source of back light. There is light bar 0f 
18 LEDs, which can be decreased to 3 LEDs. The uniformity of microstructure linear 
LED light fitting of is 47.9%. The uniformity of back light system is arrived 75% by 
measuring of night point.
