一、中英文摘要 
    新產品開發時程長短與上市時機是一
家公司能否掌握市場脈動與制勝的關鍵。
本計畫參與公司(以下簡稱 A 公司)為了能
讓新產品提前上市，常會讓多專案同時進
行；各專案經理人為了讓自己的專案能夠
準時結案，常會搶研發工程師的資源，因
此會有人力不足與專案排擠效應出現，使
得產品開發時程嚴重延遲，錯失了上市時
機與主導市場價格的機會。本計畫應用 Dr. 
Goldratt 的觀念於 A 公司的產品開發專案
管理上，用思維程序(Thinking Process)找
出 A 公司在產品開發上的核心衝突為「開
案數量多寡」，因此提出一套專案的「開
案、停案、關案、結案」機制去實際運用
在專案運作上，以提升專案管理的績效。
經 A 公司實際運作一年的結果顯示，適當
減少並控制開案總數量不僅可以使資源利
用率大幅提升，在專案準時達成率、專案
結案率及專案工時縮短的績效指標上更顯
示至少提昇 40%以上。 
關鍵詞：思維程序、關鍵鏈、開案機制、
產品研發 
 
Length of development schedule for 
new products and timing of reaching 
markets are whether a company is able to 
know the current market conditions and the 
key to win. Companies participating this 
program (here referred to as A Company) in 
order to fight for new products be in markets 
timely, they will start a number of projects 
at the same time; in order to make their own 
project closed on time, projects managers 
need more R&D engineers, as a result; the 
problems occur in shortage of manpower 
and crowding effect happen owing to 
multi-invested, even worse, this serious 
situation would delay new products’ 
development schedule, missed timing to 
market and the chance to lead the market 
price. This project used Dr. Goldratt's ideas 
in A Company's project management of 
product development, and with the thinking 
process to find out its core of the conflict ion 
on product’s development as "the number of 
open projects," , therefore, we proposed a 
set of project of  "the rule of opening 
project, stopping project, closing project and 
finishing project" to run on projects of 
practical application for the reasons of 
enhancing management performance. A 
year’s actual operations show that 
appropriately reduce and control the total 
number of open cases can significantly 
enhance the utilization of resources and have 
good result in project achievement rates or 
close rates, moreover, the performance 
indicators in working hour’s reduction show 
that it could at least raise to 40% more than 
ever. 
Keywords: Thinking Processes, Critical 
Chain, The Rule of Opening Project, 
Product Development

驗證二：每月完成專案實際工期與預估工
期完成比 
    每月專案任務實際工期與預估工期完
成比自97年7月起實施開案管制與減案動
作後發現，專案任務實際工期與預估工期
完成比，從以前最高的 3.2 降至 98 年 4 月
的 1.3，降幅高達 40.6%。因此，可看出實
施減案與管制開案方法有助於提昇專案準
時或是提早完工機會，圖 1 中 6 月沒有資
料是因為當月沒有任何專案結案，因此無
法進行統計。98 年 5 月工時比數突升至
2.3(藍色箭頭)原因為 A 公司在 98 年 1 月
進行公司組織重整，改組後的部門因為公
司業績與營業額考量並未繼續執行此專
案，以致於不良效應在 98 年 5 月時開始浮
現。
