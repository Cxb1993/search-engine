Abstract 
 
Fermentation process is a kind of non-linear time-variant system, in which the control variables are 
closely interconnection. In this study, hybrid system was proposed combined Taguchi quality engineering 
with grey relation to forecast the Optimization of medium composition, operation conditions and kinetic 
behave for production of Monacolin K and red pigments by Monascus was studied. Yields in 
submerged culture were affected by medium composition, pH and agitation. Application of Taguchi 
method, Genetic algorithms (GAs) coupling neural networks (NNs) were developed for the condition 
optimization of Monascus spp. fermentation scale up process. The fermentation predictive model of the 
combined effects of independent variables (pH, temperature, inoculum volume) for maximum fungi 
production, maximum color value and minimum Citrinin from a newly isolated Monascus spp. The 
applicability of the noninvasive sensor was tested in the fermentation of Monascus spp. The positive 
effect of Monacolin K was observed when the cultivation was added with KH2PO4 and Soybean oil. 
The optimum compositions were fund to be the japonica-type rice as carbon source, MSG, Peptone, 
pH=3. The results showed as that the strain being cultivated in this optimum medium composition, the 
maximum Monacolin K concentration was 151.06 ppm, red pigment was 259.97 u/g in intracellular fluid 
and 233.94 u/g in extracellular fluid, respectively.  
 
Keywords : Monascus, Grey relation, Taguchi quality engineering, Optimization.
