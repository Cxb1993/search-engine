一、中文摘要 
 
消防單位過去皆保存著各種消防事故
紀錄以及處理經驗，卻缺乏整體、適當的
意外事故分析及分享尤其跨部門知識獲取
不易，如此無法相互分享這些經驗與知
識。因此必須將消防事故資料根據情境分
析萃取完整、關鍵的變項。進而，若能將
救災人員經歷過消防事故搶救後的經驗及
知識若能完整的建立成資料庫提供給其他
人參考，則救災知識能快速累積及分享。 
因此本計畫除了有系統的分類過去的
消防經驗與知識，並以建立一套知識管理
系統，以強化消防知識分享，並提升意外
事故預防機制。此系統若能導入成功，除
可增加消防單位知識分享的時效與績效
外，將成為其他防災導入知識分享的主要
參考範例。此研究欲完成工作包括：確定
消防事故知識地圖類別、建立消防事故知
識地圖流程、建立消防事故專業知識地圖
與構面、盤點個人消防事故知識、建立消
防事故知識分享系統。 
 
關鍵詞：情境分析、消防事故、知識分享、
知識管理系統 
 
Abstract 
 
Different records of fire accidents and 
experience of management kept by the fire 
department are lack of complete and 
appropriate analysis. Particularly, the 
information preserved across different 
organizations is not easy to acquire, so that 
they can not share useful information with 
each other. Therefore, if we can analyze the 
accident scenarios and extract the important 
options based on aggregated fire accident 
data, then together with the corresponding 
rescue experience learned from fire 
department mentors, the complete knowledge 
system will be established. Consequently, not 
only certain knowledge regarding how to 
relieve the victims of a disaster can be 
quickly accumulated, but the corresponding 
experience and information can be shared 
publicly. 
For this reason, this proposal concerns 
the set up of a knowledge management 
system, in order to improve the fire 
knowledge sharing and enhance the 
prevention system of the accidents. We plan 
to assort the past information and further 
create a knowledge management system. If 
this system can be built and preformed 
successfully, an increase of the knowledge 
sharing prescription should be expected, thus 
resulting in a major suggestion model for 
other prevention system. 
The system will be generated through 
the category confirmation, built-up process, 
and construction for the fire accident 
knowledge map, the investigation of the 
individual fire accident knowledge, and the 
formation of the accident problem-solving 
scheme. 
 
Keywords: Scenario Analysis, Fire Accident, 
Knowledge Sharing, Knowledge 
Management System.

五、結論與建議 
 
知識地圖的貢獻在於，可將消防事故
共同性且經常發生的問題及解決方式，或
是救災知識、設備應用、管理等資訊加以
系統化分類成一資料庫，藉由消防事故知
識地圖，以圖層展開的模式表達與查詢資
訊，並利用縮減範圍之功能加以確定尋找
的方向，透過情境分析及知識編碼方式，
提供使用者一個更方便查詢的環境，將查
詢的結果視覺化及人性化，達成組織內外
部知識交流的標準。 
在消防局的組織中之資深人員的專業
技術與工作經驗，大多是隱性知識，無法
有效做到經驗傳承。新進人員僅知道作業
流程中的每一個步驟，而且知道組織內擁
有許多的表格、文件、參考資料，但是不
知道作業步驟與手上的參考資料有什麼樣
的關連，在瞭解關連的過程中，大多由新
進人員自己參與任務執行來累積經驗，方
能有效利用手上的資源。對於組織外部資
訊知識，較不知道可以從哪裡去取得學習
的資訊或管道。 
因此消防機關該如何將災害防救知識
作妥善的保存、利用與傳承，透過探討了
解知識管理的架構和理論基礎，提出合適
的知識管理系統模式，將其應用於災害防
救上，是非常重要的課題。

三、發表論文 
 
（一）題目：The Study of the Taiwan Power Company Employee and Contractor 
Occupational Accident。 
主旨：本研究根據台電公司各單位以及所轄承攬商自 1997 年至 2006 年間所發生
員工工作傷害個案（包括死亡及失能傷害），試圖由統計分析資料中，找出台電
員工及承攬商員工工作災害事故的主要肇因及其潛在的危害因子，以瞭解發生災
害之特性與影響降災之障礙。本研究之統計顯示，台電公司承攬商勞工十年間職
災的案例共 331 件，其中死亡人數高達 158 人，職災比例遠高於台電員工，由此
可得知台電已將部分高危險的工程外包給所轄承攬商。故本研究建議除員工必頇
確實做好個人防護並遵照安全作業標準，企業也應加強員工安全訓練，雙管齊下
必能有效降低職災發生。 
（二）題目：Reduce Injury for Ice Hockey Skating Player。 
主旨：探討分析 2000 年～2008 年 NHL30 個隊伍共 877 個球員的受傷模式，並
驗明球員的 5 個變項（1）年齡 （2） 加入 NHL 的時間 （3） 球齡 （4） 打
擊偏好（左/右） （5） 打擊位置 對受傷次數及因受傷所失去上場比賽這兩個構
面的關連性。此研究並分析了每個身體部位所受傷的次數分配， 根據研究結果
顯示，在這所有身體受傷的部位中， 腳的部位受傷的比重最高。再者，因受傷
部位而失去上場比賽的部位中，腳的部位也是佔了最大的比重。

2 Method 
This research is conducted by interviewing design experts of ice hockey skates. Nine factors were found to influence the 
concepts of ice hockey skate design and development (Nike Bauer, 2007). Upper features contain quarter package, lining 
material, heel support, ankle padding, tongue construction, foot bed, thermo formable as well as outsole and blade holder with 
runner of lower features. Total four skate design experts are interviewed with questionnaires. The questionnaire was designed 
in the following manner. The importance is each of 9 factors regarding safety and high performance on each part of foot area 
on ice hockey skate design. Note: 9 factors included a. quarter package, b. lining material, c. heel support, d. ankle padding, e. 
tongue construction, f. foot bed, g. thermo formable, h. outsole, i. blade holder and runner. 5 parts of foot area contact: A. 
instep, B. ankle, C. heel, D. side wall, E. toe (figure 2).

is the area where players get the most pressure while skating. Dry-grip moisture management is design on foot bed to proven 
slippery and better control (Mean = 4.25). Most high end ice skates use full upper thermo formable to bring the comfort to the 
players. Players with suitable, accommodated skates usually have a better control when skating. Correct fit of skates is also 
vital to the players’ comfort. If the boot is too tight, the players could get cramps, on the other hand, if the boot is too loose, the 
player’s feet could blister (Choice, 1992). The side wall area of the foot is also important (Mean = 4.00). Outsole is the 
foundation of the skate, which is used to assemble the skate boot with runner, beveled and injected outsole, and allows players 
to have tighter turns and maximum push-off power. Outsole also transfers the energy to the blade, which protects the side wall 
area of the foot (Mean = 3.25). The blade holder and Runner are designed on the performance aspect, which is to help players 
accelerate, maximize their speed and turning ability.  Blades are generally made of tempered steel and are coated with a high-
quality chrome, which are important for the heel area of the foot (Mean = 2.25).  
 
Conclusion 
This study demonstrated the significant points on ice hockey skates design. The nine influence factors of ice hockey skate 
design are able to increase the players’ safety and achieve higher performance. However, according to the injury report, there 
are still some injuries on ankle and feet location. This shows that other factors still might need to be discussed, such as violent 
and aggressive behaviors, training, characteristics of ice hockey, new construction of skate, different materials sourcing and 
new technology innovation.
