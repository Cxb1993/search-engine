ABSTRACT 
Sea dike is the most common 
structure adopted in coastal area. Except 
for relating to wave height and freeboard 
of dike, the overtopping rate should also 
have related to wave spectrum and wave 
train. Therefore, investigating the 
relationship between overtopping and 
characteristics of wave spectrum, wave 
train etc., could improve the calculation 
of overtopping rate. 
In this research, laboratory 
experiments under different wave 
conditions are performed to investigate 
effects of bandwidth, narrowness and 
peakedness parameter of spectrum, as 
well as groupiness factor, on the wave 
overtopping. It reveals that groupiness 
factor is a very significant parameter 
affecting on overtopping. The 
overtopping increases as the groupiness 
factor get large. In contrast, the 
bandwidth, narrowness and peakness 
parameter of spectrum are insignificant 
to overtopping. In addition the 
relationship between groupiness factor 
and reflection of dike sloping face is 
also discussed in the study. It finds that 
the group effect should affect the wave 
reflection.
