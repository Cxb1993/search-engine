ㄧ、中文摘要  (關鍵詞: 抗彎矩鋼構架,梁柱
接頭,地震載重,韌性需求,性能評估) 
 
 1994年美國北嶺地震與 1995年日本兵庫
縣南部地震 (即所謂的阪神大地震) 的災後
調查報告均顯示：在近域型地震的作用下，抗
彎矩鋼構架可能缺乏足夠的塑性變形能力，其
梁柱接頭甚至有脆性斷裂之虞。考慮到台灣與
美、日等國的鋼結構相似之處頗多，本研究搜
集相關的資料進行分析研究，以作為國內外規
範未來修訂之參考。 
 本研究參考美國規範 FEMA1995，對國
內過去試驗的 66 組全尺寸的鋼製銲接式梁柱
接頭，進行變形能力與破壞模式之交叉分析。
研究也對 4 個典型的中高層抗彎矩鋼構架，與
145 組近域型地震加速度記錄，進行非線性動
力歷時分析，以瞭解抗彎矩鋼構架與其使用的
銲接式梁柱接頭之變形需求。 
 研究發現，43%的梁柱接頭試體在梁翼與
熱影響區間破壞，而其最大塑性轉角平均為
3.03%，滿足規範要求。接頭破壞後有強度‧
勁度衰減之問題，需要注意。另外，分析中的
結構基本週期與地震脈衝週期相近時，抗彎矩
鋼構架與其梁柱接頭的變形需求會變大。除此
之外，抗彎矩鋼構架與其梁柱接頭之變形需
求，幾乎都在目前規範的推定範圍內。 
 
英文摘要  (Keywords：  Steel moment-resisting 
frames, Beam-to-column connections, Seismic 
loadings, Ductility demands, Performance evaluation) 
 
 The post-earthquake investigation shows that 
when subjected to near-fault ground motion shaking 
like the 1994 Northridge and 1995 Hyogoken-Nanbu 
(Kobe) earthquakes, steel moment-resisting frames 
might fail for lack of deformation capacity, and steel 
welded beam-to-column connections might suffer 
brittle fracture. There are certain similarities in U.S., 
Japan and Taiwan’s steel structures. Considering that, 
this study collected and analyzed the relevant data, 
for future reference to the codes.  
 In reference to FEMA 1995, reanalysis was 
made on the results of 66 past experiments in 
Taiwan, as to compare the connection rotation 
capacity across different failure modes. Using 
nonlinear time history analysis, the deformation 
demands of typical 4 middle-and-high-rise steel 
moment-resisting frames and welded beam-to- 
column connections were evaluated, for a total 
of 145 near-fault ground motion records. 
 43% connection specimens were found to 
have fracture between the beam flanges and heat 
affected zones. The median maximum plastic 
rotation of the connections is 3.03%, fulfilling 
the code requirement. The connections suffered 
stiffness degradation and strength deterioration, 
and the issues also deserve attention. On the 
other hand, when the analyzed structural periods 
are similar to the pulse periods of the near-fault 
ground motions, the deformation demands of 
steel moment-resisting frames and welded beam- 
to-column connections will increase to a great 
extent. Except for the case, the deformation 
demands of steel moment-resisting frames and 
welded beam- to-column connections are almost 
below the code expectation.  
 
二、計畫緣由與目的

強震的主要機構。然而，在 1994 年美國北嶺
地震與 1995 年日本兵庫縣南部地震 (即所謂
的阪神大地震)中卻發現，依照規範設計的抗
彎矩鋼構架可能缺乏變形能力，其梁柱接頭甚
