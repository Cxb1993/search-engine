摘要 
本期研究計劃的預設目標乃在於建立耐溫型之 EHDA (電液動霧化)系統及供料系統消音
器(膨脹室)之設計與裝設，同時以水、酒精與食鹽混合溶液進行 EHDA 實驗並以 EHDA 過程
中微滴模式之穩定操作為主，針對此模式特徵頻率、特徵尺度、液滴帶電量等與各操控變因
間關係進行觀測。鑒於先前相關計劃中因金屬毛細管高熱傳效率導致系統工作溫度無法有效
控制，本計劃改採近端混合式水浴配合紅外線溫度監測並成功達成準確及廣範圍之溫度控制
(5℃~50℃)。然計劃執行期間發現微流量下微滴模式穩定區間並不顯著，故追加進行微尺度
質傳觀測。結果顯示其與微滴模式存在區間對應流率差距在一個數量級之內，證實微尺度下
質傳對有效流率影響無法忽略。另，由於特定成分工作流體之相關物理特性如電導率等大多
無法由文獻中計算而須自行設計實驗取得，建議 EHDA 相關研究應以整合型計畫方式進行較
為恰當。

Abstract 
This project set primary goals on the design 
of an all-new EHDA system with wide operating 
temperature range, together with its own-damper 
along the liquid feeding system. At the same 
time attentions were paid on the microdripping 
mode stable-operating through EHDA 
experiments with solution of ethanol,  sodium 
chloride, and RO water; observations were made 
to attend the characteristic lengths, frequencies 
and the charging degrees. In order to avoid heat 
loss problem along the piping a two-stage hybrid 
water bath was installed down stream and this 
way precision, wide range(5 ℃ ~50 ℃ ) 
temperature control was achieved after all. 
However it was found that the existing region of 
stable microdripping mode suddenly shrinks 
below some certain flow rate, which implied that 
mass transfer takes parts in. Additional 
observations of mass transfer were performed 
and showed that mass transfer being less than 
half an order smaller than the typical flow rate, 
thus proves mass transfer significant while 
considering micro-scale cases.  
For the sake that most of the associated 
physical parameters of the ethanol-salt-solution 
like electrical conductivity, cannot be estimated 
accurately and need to be measured repeatedly, 
we strongly recommend that any 
EHDA-associated research be planned in 
integrated-project form in the future.

在固定全操作變因下仍不具重複性，排除系統
振動與環境擾動因素後吾人歸因於未確認操
作變因之存在。由於差異發生於使用溫度相異、
同黏度且表張力相近之異成份工作流體時，吾
