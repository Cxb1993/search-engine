色素貯藏色素殘留率之影響
pH 4 樣品 15℃貯藏 95 天後，兒
茶素者和苯甲酸者的色素殘留率為
26.48% 和 17.71% ， 較 控 制 組 的
16.25% 高。35℃貯藏 10 天後，兒茶
素者的色素殘留率為 18.83%，較控制
組的 13.29% 高，而苯甲酸者 13.74%
則與控制組相近。pH 6 樣品於 15℃貯
藏 27 天後，沒食子酸者、兒茶素者、
漂木酸者和香豆素者的色素殘留率分
別為 30.88%、 28.35% 、 17.52%和
14.04%，較控制組的 12.50% 高。在
35℃貯藏 8 天後，添加沒食子酸、兒
茶素、漂木酸和肉桂酸的色素殘留率
分別為 25.14%、12.47% 、10.72% 和
6.34%，較控制組的 5.89% 高 (圖 1)。
學者研究甜菜色素在不同 pH 值經
90℃ 加熱後其半衰期之差異，結果顯
示在氧氣條件下，pH 4 的半衰期長達
23.3 分鐘，較 pH 6 的 12.6 分鐘長；而
氮氣條件下，pH 4 的半衰期則長達 115
分鐘，也較pH 6的41分鐘長 (von Elbe
and Schwartz 1996)。由此可知，甜菜
色素在 pH 4 時較為穩定。
二、添加酚類化合物對於火龍果果皮
色素貯藏色澤品質之影響
在色澤品質方面，以 pH 4 樣品為
例，結果如圖 2 所示。15℃貯藏時，
其 a 值持續增加至第 10 天左右，漂木
酸者除外；而 35℃時，a 值則僅持續
增加至貯藏第 1 天，之後 a 值呈下降
之趨勢。此與上述 pH 4 樣品於 15℃和
35℃貯藏時，因貯藏過程中再生現象
仍持續進行，色素殘留率持續增加，
故 a 值也持續增加之情形相符。然而
隨著貯藏時間的增加，色素含量降
低，a 值則隨之下降，當中以兒茶素
者的具有較高的 a 值。而 b 值與 a 值
呈相反趨勢，隨著貯藏時間增加，b 值
呈上升之趨勢，如 pH 4 及 15℃貯藏
由第 0 天到第 10 天時，b 值無明顯的
變化，之後隨著貯藏時間增加 b 值呈
上升之趨勢；35℃貯藏時，第 0 天到
第 1 天無明顯變化，而後也呈上升之
趨勢。此結果也與前述結果相符，因
有再生現象產生，故隨著色素的再
生，a 值增加而 b 值則下降。而在ΔE 
方面，隨著貯藏時間的增加，ΔE也隨
之增加，色澤差異也更為明顯。
三、添加酚類化合物對於火龍果果皮色
素貯藏期間抗氧化力之影響
以統計分析貯藏前和貯藏後的
DPPH 自由基清除能力，結果如圖 3
和 4 所示， pH 4 樣品 15℃貯藏 95 天
後，自由基清除能力皆下降。當中以
肉桂酸者下降最多，貯藏第 0 天到 95
天由 30.59% 下降至 10.36%，下降了
20.23%。兒茶素者的自由基清除能力
下降最少，由 21.70%下降至 18.55%，
下降 3.15%；在 35℃時，貯藏前和貯
藏 10 天後，控制組和肉桂酸者的自由
基清除能力則無明顯變化，香豆素和
苯甲酸者則下降，香豆素者 26.05％下
降至 21.41%，而兒茶素者、漂木酸者
和沒食子酸者則增加，如兒茶素者由
15.16% 增加至 20.51%。在 pH 6 樣品
15℃貯藏前和貯藏 27 天後，除漂木酸
者和苯甲酸者無明顯變化外，其他的
酚類化合物自由基清除能力皆下降；
而 35℃時，除控制組、肉桂酸者和苯
甲酸者下降外，其餘皆無明顯變化。

者、肉桂酸者和苯甲酸者在貯藏後
期，自由基清除能力幾乎是減少的。

並隸屬於農業化學食品組，除到達的第一天領取資料外，次日即聽取口頭發表
奈米相關之論文共四天。本會議參加人次破萬，參展廠商數百，規模龐大。主

備，也有非常大的突破，對往後本人之研究有相當大的助益。

本年三月，參加第 234 屆美國化學學會年會(ACS)，該年會主題係以奈米

心得：
本次大會與往年最大不同在於以”奈米”為主，從奈米在生物、環境、分
析方法、抗癌、衛生安全等出發，強調”綠色奈米”減少地球污染，由一些危害人
體健康的有機物成分，改成 GRAS 成分，將環境中有毒物，利用奈米棒去檢測，
或以排除。奈米屬於細胞內的科技，利用纖維棒之改進，由 receptor 之建立，再
利用影像相關儀器之監控之傳輸，可以有效運用在各類生醫化學上。食品微膠囊
方面，也利用奈米食材去連接一些生物活性物質(bioactive)，或利用微膠囊去控
制 drug release。顯然這十年來奈米，已在各領域上發光發熱，故有本大會的共
襄盛舉。
例如奈米影像組，乃將生化結合工程，利用一些模型來強化奈米纖維的結構
(structure)。主要原理是，化學物經適合鍵結可以形成有結構的東西，如兩個碳
圓環可形成一個鑽石，此結構化模型可以利用所謂的 Torsional mode，經此結構
以後 T 型角力量度為原來的 1.57 倍，半軟的可以變成硬的，奈米後之顆粒經不
同溫度聚合可形成不同 Tg 的物質，甚至形成紫色的膜，利用此特性尋找新的物
質材料，作成可以新的膜形成，作為生物感應碳針(Tip)，將平面的數條線以該膜
型作成立體球狀的生物膜，目前哈佛大學奈米化學感應小組正朝開發新的生物感
應 Tip 介努力。另外，奈米級(10nm)的分子聚合後，可行成網狀 proton 結構生物
探針，並強化螢光使不受干擾，利用條件是 1. 10 nm 左右 2.單細胞 3.在水溶液
中可以呈像，並探討溫度之影響。另外有利用核苷酸去合成 dimer 或 trimer 使其
聚合後形成膜，再用 AFM(atomic force microscopy) 去做 imaging，其吸光特性並
使細胞產生 autophagy 而死亡，也利用電泳證明不是一般常見的 apoptosis 或
necrosis 機制。此外，喬治亞大學 Univ. Georgia 則以蛋白質代謝不正常，所引致
不正常糖類代謝(glycation)之前提，去探討中濕性產品(水活性 0.736)蘋果一綠茶
複合物，抑制 glycation(此涵蓋一些醣代謝物如梅納反應等產物)之效果。食物中
可抑制蛋白酸 glycation 的物質，有綠茶中的 EGCG 或橘絡等類黃酮。一般而言，

Introduction
Djulis (Chenopodium formosanum Koidz.) is an aboriginal cereal plant in
Taiwan. The whole plant including flowers and grains are full of color ranged
from yellow to red. Besides high content of dietary fiber and starch, they also
possess high protein and abundant of essential amino acid, which make this
plant a very good source of food supply, especially for vegetarian (Wu, 2007).
On the other hand, it has been used for inflammation and insect repellant,
which was believed to be attributed to its antioxidant capacity just like other
Chenopodium plant (Bhargava et al., 2006). Phyotchemicals such as
polyphenols and pigments are important contributors of biological functions in
many plants. Water soluble antioxidants are especially highlighted in recent
years for their availability. For example, anthocyanin and betnanin are
causative index of color and source of antioxidant capacity. Betanin, the main
pigment of Djulis, was reported to inhibit lipid peroxidation of membranes.
Purified betanin were even more effective than Trolox at scavenging free
radical (Kanner et al., 2001).
Nono-technology is one of the important scientific approached nowadays.
They are defined to be the research development at the molecular levels in
1-100 nm range (Weiss, Takhistov, & McClements, 2006). Nutrient with
particle size in nm is considered to be more easily absorbed by human body
(Chau, Wu, & Yen, 2007). Curcumin nanoparticles showed a better DPPH
scavenging ability and chelating ability of ferrous ion (Wu, 2005). However,
betanin is very susceptible to heat (Huang, & von Elbe, 1986), oxygen and pH
(Herbach, Stintzing, & Carle, 2006). How the color and antioxidant capacity of
this natural pigment will be after the Djulis grain was milled into nanoparticle
size? How they change during the storage when compared with the intact grain?
The aim of this study is to establish the characteristic data of Djulis powder
with different particle size and use it as fundamental information for further
application in food industry. Intact granule (IG), microparticle (MP) and
nanoparticle (NP) were used to compare the changes of pigment and
antioxidant capacity after grinding or storage (5 to 35 for 56 days).℃ ℃

Results and Discussion
In this study, we investigate the influence of grinding methods on the
functional components and antioxidant capacity of Djulis.
1. Particle size of Djulis powder after ground by nano-machine
As shown in Fig. 1, the intensity of particle distribution was higher
while the grinding time increased. But the particle size distribution was
focus around 400 nm. The best curve of particle size distribution occurred
in the sample ground for 60 minutes, which shift to around 100-200nm.
Particle size increased to 300nm after the grinding time increased to 120
minutes. This might be attributed to the coagulation or stacking of the
protein, starch or dietary fiber inside the particle. Further observation of the
particle size was conducted by the TEM. As demonstrated in Fig. 2, the
particle size of the primary particle (a) and secondary particle (b) of
nanoground Djulis powder was 20 nm and 75 nm respectively, which
confirmed the nanoparticle size of the NP samples used in this study
(Weiss et al., 2006; Chau et al., 2007).
2. Effect of grinding methods on the pigment and antioxidant capacity of
Djulis
Color, antioxidant capacity and total polyphenol content of Djulis in
intact granule (IG), microparticle (MP) and nanoparticle (NP) with different
particle sizes were illustrated in Table 1. Obviously, pigments content
(betanin and isobetanin) and polyphenol content, as well as antioxidant
capacity increased when the particle sized decreased. For example,
A530 ,betain and isobetain raised from 0.5, 1.13 mg/g and 0.92 mg/g
respectively in IG to 0.9, 1.61 mg/g and 1.22 mg/g in MP and then 1.2, 2.04
mg/g and 1.40 mg/g in NP. Obviously, decreasing the particle size during
grinding process may favor the coloration of the pigment in Djulis. For the
antioxidant capacity, FICA chelating power was highest in NP and followed
the degradation of betanin with different particle size. The changes in
betacyanin content vs time of samples heated at 50℃,60℃,70℃,80℃ and
90℃ was determined and the degradation rate was used to calculate the
activation energy. As shown in Figure 5, the active energy of IG, MP and NP
was 4.05, 3.84 and 2.37 kcal/mol, respectively. It suggested that the pigment in
particle with smaller particle size degraded more easily and only smaller
amount of energy ( 2.37 kcal/mol for NP) was enough to cause the degradation.
This is in accordance with the tendency of change of the pigment. It indicates
the close relationship between the pigment and antioxidant capacity.
7. Correlation analysis
As listed in Table 2, antioxidant capacities are significantly correlated with
pigments (correlation coefficient were 0.98 and 0.97 for FRAP and DPPH
scavenging ability respectively) and polyphenols content (correlation
coefficient were 0.972 and 0.99 for FRAP and DPPH scavenging ability.
Similar relation was also found in the a value or A538. It confirmed that both
betanin and polyphenol were causative source of antioxidant capacity in Djulis.
This is in accordance with the report of Wu (2008).
Conclusion
Djulis with micro or nanoparticle size exhibited higher pigment content and
antioxidant capacity than intact granule. However, they are more unstable
during storage due to the oxidation susceptibility and low active energy of
degradation. Therefore, further protection should be provided for these
products.
