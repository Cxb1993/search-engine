Abstract 
 
    This project studies the anti-plane crack problem of two bonded functionally 
graded piezoelectric material (FGPM) strips. Each strip contains an arbitrarily 
oriented crack. The crack surface condition is assumed to be electrically impermeable 
or permeable. The piezoelectric material has 6mm symmetry and the material 
properties of the strips are assumed in exponential forms varied in the direction 
normal to the interface. After employing the Fourier transforms, the unknowns are 
solved from the interface conditions, boundary conditions and the condition on the 
crack surfaces. The problem can then be reduced to a system of singular integral 
equations, which are solved numerically by applying the Gauss-Chebyshev 
integration formula to obtain the stress intensity factors at the crack tips. Numerical 
calculations are carried out to obtain the energy density factor and the energy release 
rate. The effects of edge boundary conditions, crack lengths, crack orientation, and the 
non-homogeneous material parameters on the intensity factors, energy density factors 
(S), and energy release rates (G) are discussed.  
    In impermeable case, the energy release rate has been shown to be negative as 
the electric loads are applied. The using of energy release rate criterion for predicting 
the crack growth is inappropriate because of the energy release rates become negative. 
The energy density theory becomes a good choice in evaluation the safety of the 
cracked FGPM. The positive definite characteristic of the energy density factor makes 
it possible for predicting the fracture behavior of the cracked structure. The influences 
of the non-homogeneous parameters and crack orientation on the energy density 
factors at the crack tips are discussed in detail.  
    The results show that the energy density factor at the crack tip will be increased 
when the crack tip is located within the softer material. In general, the factors are 
larger when crack tips are located in stronger material. Also, the factors increase as 
the crack is oriented in the direction normal to the interface. The conclusions made in 
this research can be used to evaluate the safety of two bonded strips once the cracks 
exist inside the structure. 
 
Keywords: Arbitrarily oriented crack; Singular integral equations; Energy density 
factors; Functionally graded piezoelectric material; Energy release rate.

參數為 1 a10=-0.4，裂縫角度θ1旋轉到400左右時，能量密度因子會相等(Sa2= Sb2)，
表示裂縫尖端 a2 受到裂縫間的相互作用力逐漸變小所致。

第六章 結論 
 
本計畫研究雙功能梯度壓電材料條板接合的面外裂縫問題，利用傅立葉轉換
法，將其轉換為奇異積分方程組，以數值方法求解裂縫尖端的應力與電位移強度
因子，探討條板邊界條件、裂縫長度、裂縫方向及非均質材料參數對於強度因子
的影響，再計算能量密度因子與能量釋放率來判斷裂縫成長的情形。綜合以上討
論，由各退化問題所得到的結論如下： 
1. 含雙任意方向裂縫雙結合功能梯度(FGM)條板: 
a. 當右邊條板寬度 h1 越來越大時與右邊裂縫中心位置 c1 不改變的情況下，
裂縫尖端(a1, b1, a2, b2)的強度因子皆會變小。 
b. 當裂縫角度 θ1 由 0°轉到 85°時，作用在裂縫 1 表面上的負載將逐漸減少，
因此所有 h1/a10 的例子中，其單位量化強度因子皆會逐漸變小。 
c. 當兩裂縫間距離 c1/a10 = c2/a20（a10=a20）越大時，應力強度因子由大到小
的順序為 2211 baab kkkk  。 
2. 含雙任意方向裂縫雙結合功能梯度壓電(FGPM)條板 
a. 從能量釋放率 G 的表示式中(4.58)，觀察此項( )/D( 00  , 200 )/D(  )，可
得知施加的正或負電場對能量釋放率 G 的正負值來說是很重要的角色。 
b. 當單位量化電位移值 D* >2.8 與 D*< -0.3 時，能量釋放率皆會出現負值，
此現象不符合物理意義，預期裂縫在此情形下不會成長，因此不適合利用
能量釋放率來探討壓電材料方面破壞行為。 
c. 當條板 1 的非均質材料參數 1 a10＞0 時，此材料性質為空間座標的指數型
函數將沿著 x 軸方向增加，可發現裂縫尖端的能量密度因子較大時，代表
此裂縫端位於材料性質較弱(soft)處。 
d. 在 δ1a10=0.3 時，表示條板 1 的材料性質越往邊界時，材料性質越強，因
此在裂縫 1 的能量密度因子 Sa1 與 Sb1 值之間的變化比均質材料小，且也發
現能量密度因子 Sa1 總是大於 Sb1，其原因來自於材料性質。 
e. 當裂縫 θ1 由 00 旋轉到 1800 時，在裂縫 1 表面上的機械負載與電負載將逐
漸減少，最後能量密度因子 Sa1 與 Sb1 會趨近於零，從不同的非均質材料參
數 1 a10 亦皆可得知此現象。

4.2.   DOUBLE-LAYERED HOLLOW DISK    
 
The new results of the double-layered FGM disk under 
the rotation are obtained in this study. The following data 
are used in the analyses: Po=Pi=0, 1 2 0.3   , R1= 0.6 m, 
R2= 0.8 m and R3=1 m. The material constants of the inner 
disk are: E01 = 210 GPa, 01=7850 kg/m3 and 1=0. Table 1 
lists the data of the outer disk. The initial interference 
displacement  between two disks is 0.5 mm.  
The contact pressure P between both disks are defined 
as P=P+P, where P and P are the contact pressure due 
to interference and angular velocity, respectively. Under 
the angular rotation, the two disks will be apart when P=0. 
At this condition, the critical angular velocity is obtained 
and it is shown in Table 2. If the press-fit between two 
hollow disks is necessary, the condition P=0 should be 
avoided. The critical angular velocity is an important 
parameter for the double-layered FGM hollow disk.  
From the data in Table 2, when using the same material 
of the inner disk, the critical angular velocity only depends 
on the value of 2.

5. CONCLUSIONS 
 
The stresses, critical angular velocity, inhomogeneous 
properties and structural behaviors of the hollow disk made 
of FGM have been analyzed and discussed. The stress 
solutions of this paper can be reduced to those of simple 
problems in previous studies. The critical angular velocity 
is an important parameter for the double-layered hollow 
disk under the rotation.
