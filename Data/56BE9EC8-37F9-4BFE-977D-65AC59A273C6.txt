Abstract 
Video streaming has been prevalently used in Internet today for servicing real-time 
multimedia services, such as, Video-on-Demand, distant learning, Web TV and E-commerce. 
However, the video streaming is suffered from exceeding initial delay and unstable 
transmission quality because of the limited bandwidth, long network delay and the best-effort 
transmission service offered by most wide-area-networks (WAN) deployed today.
