encouraging people to plant trees. However, the disadvantage of woody 
plants is slow growing rate and the impact on human living place. 
Therefore, a more feasible option seems to be the cultivation of herbs 
because of the fast-growing herbs, and scaffolding can be used when 
planting, which will make planting into a three-dimensional type. This 
policy is particularly advantageous for Taiwan＇s scarcity of land. 
    For the above reasons, we use oxalate-related monomer as materials 
to synthesize aliphatic polyoxalate with different carbon length by 
process of industrial melt-phase polycondensation. However, we find that 
polyoxalate has the disadvantage of poor thermal stability, so we 
introduce some monomer with rigid structure or longer chain as 
improvements.  
    Through this study, it will establish domestic first polyoxalate 
research group and its technical database. At the same time, we will 
integrate the implementation of the domestic industry, the ITRI and the 
resources of the agricultural sector to participate in research and 
development. Therefore, this integrated program based on the addition to 
academic study has considerable commercialized potential, and will also 
enhance the effectiveness of Taiwan's status in the carbon reduction. 
 
Key words: Carbon reduction, Poly(oxalate)s, biomass materials  
 
3、計畫目標 
脂肪族聚酯由於其具有相當優異的環境親和性、生物相容性以及
生物可分解性目前越來越受重視，其中最知名的全脂肪族即為聚乳酸
(Poly lactic acid)，其優異的性質，如可耐受消毒處理、易於加工成型
等，但由於其由乳酸(lactic acid)聚合或丙交酯(lactide)開環聚合單一成
分所製成，故其物性與化性調控上不易，僅能以後加工方式補強其性

10. 推廣及運用的價值：  
藉由此計畫將草酸結構導入聚酯材料中，不但能大幅降低石化原
料的使用，而且能大幅的減少二氧化碳的生成，其在全球環境綠能化
上的項獻將是相當可觀；同時，還能將台灣的紡織業、塑膠業及橡膠
等產業全面綠能化，塑造成世界綠能材料的典範。此外本計畫已針對
上游草酸單體材料取得，包含乙二醇與碳酸二酯類等單體之規畫，分
別與中國鋼鐵、台灣石化、中國石油化學(大陸)與天津大學等進行可
行性評估洽談，並針對草酸系共聚酯材料與工研院、台南紡織等進行
相關應用規畫討論，其對本計畫所開發之草酸系共聚酯具有相當高度
之興趣，並對於此一構建完整的垂直產業鏈規畫均保有相當高的意
願，未來將可開發一聚草酸系共聚酯材料下游應用網，將可有效吸引
帶動上述上游端之廠商與技術之投入。
