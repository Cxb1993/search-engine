一、中英文摘要 
 
關鍵詞：臨床生物資訊，生物資訊資料
庫，疾病導向，個人化醫學  
 
由於生物資訊的資料庫相當龐大，分析方
法多樣而複雜，非一般臨床醫師在診斷與
治療疾病時能輕易運用。因此，我們提出
此計畫，希望能建立疾病導向的臨床生物
資訊資料庫，提供臨床醫師在診斷與治療
疾病時能隨時取得生物資訊。 
 
第一年我們已建立了臨床生物資訊中心的
網站(http://www.bmidoc.com/)及疾病相關
的基因資料庫。疾病相關基因資料庫主要
源自於 NCBI 的基因銀行 (GenBank) 、
GeneCards 及其他公用的資料庫。疾病的
分類採用ICD-9-CM 的國際疾病分類法，
ICD-9-CM 的疾病分類也是目前醫院最常
用的疾病分類法。計畫的第一年，我們已
建立了32 種癌症相關的基因資料庫。 
 
臨床生物資訊中心網站的首頁包括癌症的
種類，癌症相關的基因，及重要的相關網
站連結。基因資料庫內容有基因的主要名
稱、別名、種類、主要的基因功能及兩篇
參考文獻。內容力求精簡扼要，讓使用者
能在短時間內了解癌症相關基因的作用。 
 
第二年我們除了擬新建癌症相關蛋白質的
資料庫外，將持續更新基因資料庫的內
容，使癌症相關基因資料庫能提供臨床醫
師最新的生物資訊。 
 
 
 
Abstract 
 
Keywords: clinical bioinformatics, 
bioinformatics database, disease-oriented, 
personalized medicine 
 
Physicians are hard and take much time to 
retrieve the demand biological information 
from the public databases. Also it needs a lot 
of analysis and process to let these data 
become applicable for clinical diagnosis and 
treatment. Therefore, in this project, we will 
create several disease-oriented clinical 
bioinformatics databases to provide 
biological information for clinical 
physicians to facilitate their clinical decision 
making. 
 
In the first year, these disease-oriented 
clinical bioinformatics databases have been 
placed at a web site 
(http://www.bmidoc.com/) and clinical 
physicians can retrieve biological 
information via the Internet. The web site 
includes 32 cancer-oriented gene databases. 
The gene databases created at the web site 
originated from the GenBank of NCBI, 
Genecards, PubMed and other public 
databases. The list of diseases came from the 
International Classification of Diseases, 
Ninth Revision, Clinical Modification 
(ICD-9-CM). The ICD-9-CM is based on 
the World Health Organization's Ninth 
Revision, International Classification of 
Diseases (ICD-9). ICD-9-CM is the official 
 
 
 
Link to the PubMed to show the abstract. 
 
 
 
 
Some important websites are placed on the 
right side for link if the user wants to know 
in detail, e.g. link to GeneCards. 
 
 
 
In the first year of this project, we have 
created a cancer-related gene database on 
the website. In the second year, we will 
create cancer-related protein database on the 
same site with the same model. The third 
year, we will create the SNP database and 
evaluate the accuracy of gene, protein and 
SNP databases. After the completion of this 
project, these disease-oriented biological 
data including gene, protein, and SNPs, can 
be retrieved, analyzed, processed and 
managed easily from the web site. We also 
would like to evaluate the utility of the web 
site in diagnosis and treatment of diseases 
using the clinical data and biological 
information in the third year. 
 
四、計畫成果自評 
 
此計畫的第一年，我們建立了完整的癌症
相關的基因資料庫，工作已如期完成。接
下來的第二年，我們將陸續完成癌症相關
的蛋白質資料庫。雖然初期建立癌症相關
的基因與蛋白質資料庫並不難，但較困難
的是以後更新資料庫內容的工作，勢必要
有更多人來維護。因此，我們考慮在完成
整個生物資訊資料庫後，將資料庫分成幾
個固定小單位，徵求志工來長期維護與更
新。
