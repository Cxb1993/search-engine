may be important to future nanodevices in 
resistive switching. 
 
三、研究方法及成果 
In order to investigate the resistive switching 
behaviors of gold-in-Ga2O3 core-shell nanowires, 
we have fabricated the single nanowire devices 
on the heavily doped n-type silicon wafer 
covered with 500 nm thick thermally grown SiO2 
layer as the insulating layer. On the top of it, we 
have employed photolithography to define 
platinum electrodes. Single nanowire is picked 
up onto the measurement chip with a three-axis 
oil hydraulic micromanipulator having glass tips 
via static electricity under optical microscope. 
The titanium contact metal and gold antioxidant 
metal electrodes are deposited to connect to the 
platinum electrodes by e-gun evaporator system 
and defined by electron beam lithography. The 
electrical characteristics are measured with a 
Keithley 4200 in atmospheric air at room 
temperature.

四、結論與討論 
我們已經成功量測到在單根金-氧化鎵核
殼狀奈米線中，其操作電壓固定，不隨電極距
離改變。我們認為操作電壓不變的原因是由於
電子傳導路徑均是由氧化鎵殼層和金核部分
所構成，而金核傳導速度極快。透過此不隨電
極距離改變的電壓特性，在奈米線上可做出多
個記憶體，也使得未來製作高密度電阻式記憶
體元件成為可能。 
相較於其他薄膜及單根奈米線元件，金-氧
化鎵核殼狀奈米線具有可觀的操作電場。(5 × 
10
4
 V/cm for set process and -8.5 × 10
4
 V/cm for 
reset process) 2V 的操作電壓即可驅動元件，開
關比超過 1000 倍，在只有 0.2V 的操作電壓
下，更還可維持 10 倍的開關比。此外，其記
憶時間超過 3 × 104秒、耐久度超過 100 次循
環。
