Vapor-liquid equilibrium (VLE) measurements were 
conducted at 101.3 kPa using the Hunsmann type 
equilibrium still with circulation of both vapor and 
liquid phases.  All experimental VLE data were used 
to calculate the liquid activity coefficients from 
the modified Raoult s law and to study the relative 
volatility for the azeotropic mixture.  Except for 
the systems involving PEG-200, the liquid activity 
coefficients were calculated using the modified 
Raoult s law and correlated with the Margules, 
Wilson, NRTL, and UNIQUAC models. The binary 
interaction parameters were used to predict the 
ternary VLE behavior. 
The densities, viscosities, refractive indexes, and 
surface tensions of binary systems were measured at T 
= (298.15, 308.15, 318.15, and 328.15) K and those of 
ternary systems were measured at T = 298.15 K under 
atmospheric pressure. Densities were determined using 
a vibrating-tube density meter. Viscosities were 
measured with an automatic microviscometer based on 
the rolling-ball principle. Refractive indexes were 
measured using a digital Abbe-type refractometer. 
Surface tensions were determined by the Wilhelmy-
plate method. These results are used to calculate 
excess molar volumes, deviations in the viscosity, 
deviations in the refractive indexes, and deviations 
in the surface tension for binary mixtures.  These 
calculated excess quantities were fitted to the 
Redlich-Kister equation.

1. INTRODUCTION 
Ethanol is increasingly being used as a large-scale 
fuel, and ethanol is usually produced using 
conventional processes of batch fermentation of 
sugars with yeast, followed by distillation to recover 
the ethanol.  2-Propanol is usually used as a 
dehydration solvent to clean the parts and electric 
circuit in the process of the electronic industry. The 
purification of this aqueous 2-propanol to recover 
2-propanol is a necessary step to reduce the pollution 
problem and the capital cost.  Hence, separation of 
aqueous ethanol and aqueous 2-propanol azeotropes is 
a very important operation in the biochemical and 
electronic industries. Unfortunately, aqueous mixtures 
of ethanol or 2-propanol display markedly non-ideal 
behavior, forming an azeotrope with a minimum 
boiling point temperature.

an automatic Anton Paar RXA-156 refractometer, 
which runs with the wavelength of 589 nm

scale with a precision of 0.01 mg.  The uncertainty in 
the composition is estimated to within ±1×10-4 mole 
fraction.  
All liquids were thermostatically controlled to 
within ±0.01 K, ±0.05 K, ±0.03 K, and ±0.05 K for ρ, 
η, nD, and σ measurements, respectively.  All

±3×10-5 g⋅cm-3, ±0.004 mPa⋅s, ±0.00005, and ±0.15 
mN⋅m-1, respectively.

本計畫工作的完成將有如下的貢獻與價值： 
1. 發酵程序產製之乙醇濃度約 8~12vol%，因此需利用蒸餾予以提昇濃度，但兩種化合物
混合在一起，達到一定比例會有共沸現象，而無法分離，但可有效的利用共沸或萃取蒸餾，
加入第三成分來克服。 
2. 一般半導體業界之製程所產生之含水異丙醇（含水率約 5wt%），須要回收純化來減低污
與成本。然而異丙醇的水溶液出現顯著的非理想性，使用分離劑進行共沸蒸餾或萃取蒸
餾，也是一種有效的分離方法。 
3. 本研究所使用的分離劑為無毒的藥用溶劑：1,2-丙二醇、1,3-丙二醇、與聚乙二醇
-200，研究的完成可以降低對乙醇與異丙醇水溶液之純化，事後溶劑的回收的成本，並減
少對環境的污染。 
4. 汽液平衡研究的研究的結果，在乙醇水溶液方面，只須要在進料中加入所提的萃取試
劑 30 wt%，便能達到共沸點的破除蒸餾純化目的。在異丙醇水溶液方面，則須要在進料中
加入 50 wt%所提的萃取試劑，可達到共沸點的破除蒸餾純化目的。
