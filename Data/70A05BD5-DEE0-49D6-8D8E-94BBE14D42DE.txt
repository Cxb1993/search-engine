低溫多晶矽高壓橫向雙擴散金氧半場效電晶體之製作與穩定度探討(II)  
“Low-temperature poly-Si HV-LDMOSFET fabrication and stability study (I I)” 
計畫編號：NSC96-2221-E-211-022 
執行期間：96 年 8 月 1 日 至 97 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：林智玲 華梵大學電子工程系 副教授 
 
1.中文摘要 
在本計畫中我們結合高功率元件與
薄膜電晶體之製程技術，製作出低溫多
晶矽橫向雙擴散金氧半場效電晶體（
LTPS LDMOSFET），並以步階式離子
植入與準分子雷射退火技術提升其元件
特性，最後利用Keithley 4200 and HP 4284A 
LCR meter、高壓量測系統，量測低溫多
晶矽功率元件之直流特性、崩潰電壓與
電容特性，分析此低溫製程技術製作高
功率元件之可行性。根據結果得到在經
過雷射處理的開關電流比為 1.28×106，
漂移區長度 35 µm和步階式摻雜情況下
，經過雷射處理的崩潰電壓最高可達
286V，特徵導通電阻為 9 Ω-cm2。此外分
析此元件之電容特性，包含均勻參雜、
步階式參雜與漂移長度的影響。
 
關鍵詞：橫向雙擴散金氧半場效電晶體
、薄膜電晶體、步階式離子植入、準分
子雷射退火技術  
 
英文摘要 
Low-temperature polysilicon thin-film 
transistor lateral double diffusion metal oxide 
semiconductor field effect transistors (LTPS 
LDMOSFETs) were fabricated by combining a 
thin film transistor with the power structure, 
three-step drift doping and excimer laser 
annealing. Simultaneous excimer laser annealing 
and substrate heating at 400℃ LTPS transistor 
will enable integration with the driving circuit of 
a system-on-panel (SOP) and other circuits in the 
future. The maximum breakdown voltage of 
three-step doping LTPS-LDMOS after excimer 
laser annealing is 286 V with Ldrift=35 µm. The 
specific on-resistance could be low about 9 Ω 
cm2 and the ON/OFF current ratio is about 
1.28×106 with Ldrift =15 µm. The subthreshold 
swing (SS) was about 1 V/decade showing low 
number deep states in excimer laser annealed 
polysilicon. In this research, the dependences of 
parasitic capacitances (Coss/Ciss/Crss), 
including step or uniform doping, drift region 
length, and channel length are compared 
experimentally. 
Keywords: LTPS LDMOSFETs, SOP, excimer 
laser annealing 
 
2. Introduction 
  Power devices and related products have been 
unceasingly studied and improved for 
applications in many field, recently. In particular, 
lateral power devices are continuously being 
developed to improve system-on-chips (SOC) 
and to facilitate integration with other circuits on 
complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor 
(CMOS) process. Lateral power devices include 
conventional lateral double diffused metal oxide 
semiconductor (LDMOS), super-junction 
LTPS-LDMOS power device on SOI.  
 
4. Discussion 
Figure 2 presents the basic output 
characteristics of ELC LTPS-LDMOS. The 
device dimensions and doping concentration 
were W=600 µm; Lch=9 µm; Ldrift=15 µm; 
Ndrift=7×1011 cm-2 (Ndrift-step= 2.3×1011, 7×1011, 
1.3×1012 cm-2); Tsi=0.1 µm; Tbox=1 µm.  
Fig. 3 shows the breakdown voltage 
distribution. The breakdown voltage variation is 
small when the drift region length is designed at 
15 µm. But the variation obviously increases 
when the drift region length is large than 25 µm. 
The long drift region length indicates large drift 
region area. If the excimer laser power is not 
uniform, the crystallization uniformity of large 
drift region will be poor. It is the reason why the 
drift region length is excess 25 µm, the variation 
of breakdown voltage is large. The phenomenon 
also appeared for specific on-resistance. The 
distributions of specific on-resistance of various 
drift region length are shown in Fig. 4. The 
channel length less than 15 µm owns low 
specific on-resistance and low variation. The 
variation range increases following drift region 
length extension.  
Capacitances of a conventional vertical power 
MOSFET present including gate source (Cgs), 
gate-drain (Cgd) and drain-source (Cds) parasitic 
capacitances. These capacitances contribute to 
the input capacitance (Ciss= Cgd+Cgs), output 
capacitance (Coss= Cgd+Cds), and reverse transfer 
capacitance (Crss= Cgd). Capacitances of

5. Conclusions 
In this work, LTPS-LDMOSs were 
implemented in three step doping and by 
combining silicon on insulator (SOI), low 
temperature with excimer laser annealing thin 
film transistor technology and a power device, 
showing the possibility of power devices on 
system-on-panel (SOP). The step doping 
LTPS-LDMOSs show good DC performances, 
such as breakdown voltage, on resistance, and 
on/off current ratio. They also have reasonable 
input, output, and reverse transfer capacitances 
comparing with c-Si SOI-LDMOSFETs.

一、參加會議經過 
 
這次所發表的兩篇論文，其一為 pH 值與 UV 光感測器多功感測器，二方面量測都有
極佳的靈敏度及穩定度，並以實際量測驗證，成功的結合 pH 值與 UV 光感測器於一體：
其二為二氧化鈦薄膜葡萄糖生物感測器，經由循環伏安儀 CHI 627C 以及應用我們自行整
合的電流式讀出電路實際量測得到的結果。最後將實驗結果整理並比較出碳與白金為基
底材料的優劣性，投稿於第七屆東亞化學感測器研討會(EACCS 2007)，此次研討會於新
加坡克拉克碼頭的 Novotel 飯店舉行。所發表的論文被安排於該會議第二天下午張貼發
表，並接受議廳裡有興趣的人員提出問題討論，於此過程中得到許多新的資訊以及新的
想法，預期在之後設計及研究時有更新穎的想法，可以研究出更實用並同時兼顧靈敏度
及特性之感測器。 
此次會議中並邀請許多各個領域有名的人做演講，針對不同感測器研究主題方向做
討論，未來展望以及有可能實踐的方式，演講主題有三: 
1.An Approach to the Detection of Volatile Organic Compounds.    
2.Optical Detection Technologies Developed for Point of Care Bioassay Devices. 
3.Crystalline Nanowires of Semiconducting Metal Oxides as a New Generation of 
Selective and Stable Gas Sensors. 
第一個主題是由日本 Nagasaki 大學教授 Egashira Makoto 主講，對於由早期至今易揮
發有機化合物量測的方法及歷史作一個完整的整理與簡介，並針對該研究在發展該項技
術時遇到的問題作一個概要性的講解，使與會者有較深刻的了解。 
第二個主題是由新加坡南洋理工大學教授 Swee Chuan Tjin 主講，對於光學感測日益普
及的今天，對光學儀器於生物感測器相關的應用作一完整的描述，並就生醫的角度對光
學感測未來在醫療輔助的未來展望，提出諸多的遠見及光學設備的發展重點，受益良多。 
第三個主題是由義大利 Brescia 大學教授 Giorgio Sberveglieri 主講，提出一新型感
測材料_晶體奈米導線，適合應用於氣體感測，並以實驗證明其穩定性，此新材料相信未
來會被廣泛的運用於化學感測。 
而整個會議內容可分為兩大方向來說明，一方向是對目前感測器領域裡較經成熟的
部分利用各種新式的方法或材料做先進的改善，另一方向就是提出新物質的量測可行的
方法，提供了與會人員新的動機以及思維。為期三天的會議過程裡，許多有趣的主題演
講或是發表文章，可以讓我們了解各項感測器領域研究的進行狀況，獲益匪淺。
