Keyword:  Chaos theorem, data compression, encipher/decipher, discrete wavelet, ZigBee  
2.4G RF local network, Field Programmable Gate Array 
 
The research of transmission of data is a very important topic in the both academic and industrial 
community. The transmission ratio will depend on the transmitting data-size; the transmission 
ratio decrease as the amount the transmitting data is large. Hence, the encryption and 
compression of data is important issue in the data communication. This proposal will focus on the 
security of data by using chaos theorems and on the data compression by using discrete wavelet 
theorem. Six research issues shown blow will be studied in this proposal: 
 
z Chaos theorem has very important advantages in security of communication system, while 
chaos control and chaos synchronization must be handled with for the secure communication. 
In the past decade, our laboratory had devoted to these issues and made several important 
contributions in these areas. Now, this proposal will develop a general chaos system that has 
multi-dynamic frequency responses, which can be viewed as FM modulation techniques by 
nonlinear theory analysis, robust control, adaptive control, ∞H and LMI (linear matrix 
inequalities) theorems. Synchronization and security of the new developed master-slave 
chaotic systems, and its hardware implementations will be studied. 
z New digital data enciphers/deciphers based on digital chaotic algorithm (DCA) will be 
studied. Traditional encipher/decipher algorithms like DES, RSA and IDEA are complicated 
and require a large amount of calculations. These algorithms will reduce the transmitting 
speed and waste the usage of bandwidth. DCA has many important properties, such as the 
sensitive dependence on initial conditions (keys), noise-like, pseudo-random and 
non-periodicity, etc. DCA can achieve those characteristic at short time, that is suitable for 
encipher/decipher system. 
z To store or convey data directly is not desirable. In order to reduce the data size, the 
compression technique is necessary. For this target, the processing time is another important 
issue with compression ratio and reduction. The discrete wavelet theorem has high 
compression ratio and efficiency; and it is suitable for data compression. After compressing, 
the data size will be reduced, it will be accelerate the speed of encipher/decipher, and 
increase the efficiency of integral system. 
z On the contrary, the ZigBee has several advantages, such as low electromagnetic wave and 
high transmission-rate. For this reason, the proposal will construct the local wireless 
network based on ZigBee system. Data encipher/decipher will be carried and implemented 
in ZigBee system. 
 
To implement the proposal, that has several points: 1. Using ZigBee module to construct 
peer-to-peer architecture to be local wireless network. 2. Implement the discrete wavelet 
theorem and digital chaos algorithm on Field Programmable Gate Array. 3. Implement the chaos 
control and synchronization problems via analog circuit, and apply to audio signal processing.

3. ZigBee 2.4G 無線傳輸技術的運用 (完成協定設計並應用於無線影像傳輸系統) 
嚴格說起來，本研究計畫當初提案時，預計是期望利用藍芽傳輸技術來完成無線資料傳
輸，但後期整個 2.4G 低功率傳輸技術上漸漸讓 ZigBee 技術逐漸嶄露頭角，同時也比較了
一下 ZigBee 傳輸技術與藍芽傳輸技術的差異性，ZigBee 是一種近距離、低複雜度、低功耗、
低資料速率、低成本的雙向無線通信技術，主要適合於自動控制、傳感、監控和遠端控制
等領域，可以嵌入各種設備中，同時支援地理定位功能。IEEE802.15.4 工作組定義了一種
廉價的供固定、便攜或移動設備使用的極低複雜度、成本和功耗的低速率無線連接技術。
ZigBee 聯盟在制定 ZigBee 標準時，採用了 IEEE802.15.4 作為其物理層和媒體接入層規範。
在其基礎之上，ZigBee 聯盟制定了資料連結層（DLL）、網路層（NWK）和應用編程介面
（API）規範，並負責高層應用、測試和市場推廣等方面的工作。 
ZigBee 的系統複雜要遠小於藍牙的系統複雜性，ZigBee 協定較為簡單，實現相對容易，
需要的系統資源也較少，據估計運行 ZigBee 需要系統資源約 28Kbps；藍牙協定相對複雜，
它需要系統資源約為 250Kbps。ZigBee 定義了兩種類型的設備：全功能設備 FFD（Full 
Functional Device）和簡化功能設備 RFD(Reduced Function Device)。網路為主從結構，一個
網路有一個網路協調者(Coordinator)和最多可達 65535 個從屬設備。網路協調者必須是
FFD，它負責管理和維護網路，包括路由、安全性、節點的附著與離開等。一個網路只需
要一個網路協調者，其他終端設備可以是 RFD，也可以是 FFD。RFD 的價格要比 FFD 便
宜得多，其佔用系統資源僅約為 4Kb，因此網路的整體成本比較低。另外 ZigBee 無線傳輸
協定上也提供了三個工作頻段：2.402∼2.480GHz、868∼868.6MHz、902∼928MHz 共 27 個通
道，在通道接入方式採用 CSMA-CA，能有效地減少了每個封包的衝突。更重要的一點是
ZigBee 的低功耗，在一個典型的 ZigBee 無線網路中，3.3V/500mAH 電池可以供 ZigBee 設
備工作六個月到兩年，因此在計劃執行選用無線傳輸技術時，基於上述優點，本研究團隊
便將原計畫中所提到的藍芽傳輸改成以 ZigBee 無線傳輸技術為主，也是很成功的應用至無
線影像傳輸系統中。 
 
4. 應用離散小波演算法於數位資料壓縮 (由於設計上與硬體限制問題，無法完成此部分) 
近年來離散小波轉換(Discrete Wavelet Transform)[15,16]在多重解析度分析和數位影像
處理方面有非常廣泛的應用，相較於 FFT、DCT 等方法，小波轉換具有較高的壓縮率以及
自我相似特性的資料結構，因此所呈現出來對於數位影像資料的壓縮效率也較一般壓縮來
得高。例如近年來所盛行的 JPEG2000 壓縮格式[17]也是建構在離散小波轉換的基礎上。因
此本研究計劃為了致力於提到無線影像傳輸的效能，期望利用硬體具高速運算的優點，將
小波壓縮理論以硬體的方式實現，採用硬體描述語言將小波壓縮理論模組化，將運算單元
與儲存單元分離處理，同時以 FPGA 作為壓縮運算的核心，配合外部記憶體儲存運算的結
果與資料，來達到更快速更有效率的資料壓縮。經過這一年來的進行，離散小波系統已經
完成 MATLAB 驗證模擬與 FPGA 初步的硬體架構實現，對於一張 480640× 的數位 BMP 影
像也可提供 50%的壓縮率，但由於之前在同一個 FPGA 系統平台[18]中已經植入了 Logistic 
map 演算法，在後期驗證時發現當兩系統需要整合時，會產生記憶體空間與邏輯元件空間
嚴重不足的問題；評估同時修改兩種演算法的硬體架構會耗太多資源與時間，為了儘快在
計畫期間內將完整無線影像保密傳輸系統先架設出來，很遺憾的此部分部未能如期完成，
因此在接下來的計畫報告中便不提及有關於離散小波演算法的相關資訊。

第 4-4-3 節 Second Operation Initial value 模組 
此模組是用來計算第二次 Logistic map 運算所需要的初始值，由於此部分其實是由很
多模組所組成，在此節將探討其主要的架構，圖二十一為此模組的架構示意圖。此部分模
組主要是實現(16)式至(17)式的計算，其動作流程如下為：K1、K2、K3 透過 buffer 組合成
一組 24bits 的資料線後與 Y02 相加Æ相加後的值為 10 進位，但由於後續的模組需要做浮點
運算，所以要經過一個十進位轉換 IEEE 754 浮點數運算的模組(Decimal To IEEE754)對數值
做格式上的轉換Æ經過轉換後的數值再透過浮點數的乘法運算；實現時，(16)式中的模數運
算以使用比較器、減法器和多工器來完成，而不必透過除法器來實現。(17)式的模數運算為
模 1 運算，所以此時必須執行減一的動作後，再將運算結果輸出。另一種則是範圍介於(0,1)
之間，在此時則不需做任何改變，可直接將運算結果輸出。

第 4-5 節 ZigBee 無線傳輸 
在前述文中已經提起有關 ZigBee 的技術優勢，經本研究團隊在業界中比較各家廠商所

此晶片可達成 2.4GHz 的射頻(RF)、實體層(PHY)、基頻(baseband)與媒體存取控制層(MAC)

制器，超框架(Superframe)構造，接收框架(frame)過濾器，安全引擎和數字信號處理模塊組

第六章 結論與未來發展 
本研究計畫為期兩年，主要的目的是以混沌理論系統為基礎，利用混沌的纇雜訊且可
控制的特性，分別就連續時間形式與數位形式的混沌系統提出可實現的目標；針對連續時
間下的混沌系統，過去多數的論文都是強調在系統同步後再進行加解密資料的進行，但實
際上本研究計畫實現時發現連續時間下的類比混沌系統之系統頻率響應是不夠快速的，若
直接利用一般論文的加解密方式，所傳輸的資料很容易被攻擊者利用低通濾波器將資料過
濾出；因此在本研究計畫中提出了一種調整因子 k 可用來調整連續時間型態混沌系統的頻
率響應，並將整個混沌控制系統推導出可利用類比電路實現，同時應用至類比訊號直接加
密，在本研究論文中以一 MP3 播放系統作為系統實現，由驗證結果確實可達到以類比電子
電路完成混沌電路並且可達到即時性的加密系統。 
對於數位資料無線傳輸的保密性，在本研究論文中也成功的利用可規劃邏輯陣列系統 
將一數位混沌系統 logistic map 實現硬體化，並且與 2.4G ZigBee 無線傳輸技術、CMOS 數
位影像資料及 TFT LCD 影像面板顯示整合，用以呈現無線傳輸的數位資料加解密之效果。
整個數位系統所有的環節(包含數位影像資料擷取系統、2.4G ZigBee 無線傳輸協定、1.8” 
TFT LCD 即時影像顯示以及一維 Logistic Map 演算法硬體化實現)皆由本研究團隊完成。當
中最大的突破莫過於利用 FPGA 硬體平台將一維 Logistic Map 演算法以 pipe-line 方式設
計，大幅提升了一維 Logistic Map 演算法在 FPGA 硬體中的執行效能，如此可讓數位資料
再加解密過程中很接近即時處理的效果。但美中不足的是在本研究計畫提案計畫中原本預
計利用離散小波系統期望能即時壓縮(提供 40%的壓縮效能)以換取無線傳輸中，可顯示在
TFT LCD 面板上有更高的 frame rate，但主要的障礙礙於在既有的 FPGA 平台中已經植入一
維 Logistic Map 演算法的 pipe-line 架構，剩餘的空間不足夠在植入離散小波演算法，因此
在為期兩年的計畫開發過程後期處理無法將離散小波系統一併整合至 FPGA 中，此為本研
究計畫需要改進的地方。 
 
總觀為期兩年的國科會專題研究計畫，總結對於本研究完成且具有貢獻的項目匯整如下： 
 
1. 對於傳統的連續時間型混沌系統提出一可調整系統頻率響應的演算法，並在 SCI 期刊上
發表同步控制器設計方法論以及利用類比電子電路實現，並應用至一 MP3 加解密系統。 
2. 研究數位型式混沌系統(一維 Logistic Map)並具體以 IC 設計的方式實現，同時在 FPGA
硬體平台上完成驗證，確實可以達到數位資料的加解密。

Abstract—An efficient active queue management (AQM) controller for a class of stochastic TCP communication networks 
is proposed in this study. By applying the Lyapunov–Krasovskii functional approach and the linear matrix inequality (LMI) 
technique, a static-state-feedback controller (SSFC) capable of both achieving the desired queue size and guaranteeing 
asymptotic stability of the operating point is presented. A series of numerical simulations are discussed in various network 
environments to evaluate the proposed scheme. The simulation results show that the proposed schemes surpass other AQM 
schemes. 
I. INTRODUCTION 
INCE the explosive growth and the development of traffic in today’s Internet, communication network has become 
a fundamental part of many science and engineering applications. However, traffic congestion is one of the main 
communication problems because of the increasing end user demands for more bandwidth to consume the available 
capacity and force the limits of technology. Moreover, it has been observed that the behaviors of TCP-based 
communication networks could be highly complicated due to TCP’s nonlinear additive increase multiplicative decrease 
(AIMD) congestion control mechanism and stochastic properties of Internet traffic [1]. In addition, via the different 
methods of linear and nonlinear time series analyses, the Internet traffic model is belonged to a nonlinear stochastic 
process [2]. Consequently, owing to the highly complicated nonlinearity in TCP communication networks, the 
congestion control mechanisms design is a very important issue. 
AQM technique is one major congestion control scheme to reduce the source sending rate by dropping or marking 
packets, when congestion is detected. Particularly, an earliest well-known AQM scheme, random early detection (RED), 
was proposed to decrease the flow-synchronization problem and regulate the traffic load to achieve minimal packet loss 
and queuing delay, high utilization, as well as remove biases against burst traffic. Unfortunately, RED easily causes 
oscillations and instability due to the parameter variations. [3]. Therefore, efficient modified methods are proposed, 
such as FRED [4], SRED [5], BLUE [6] etc. However, above-mentioned control schemes are normally based on 
intuition and heuristic design, thus the system stability cannot be guaranteed. Hence, various congestion control 
schemes based on control theory have been extensively proposed to the analysis and design of TCP networks [7]-[10]. 
Due to the variations of TCP sessions and round-trip time (RTT), these effects make the random 
arrival and departure of packet transmission and then cause the characteristics of stochastic [10]. 
Further, the packet loss in the network is a stochastic process, and the loss events from this 
stochastic process dominate the congestion window size. Hence, the evolution of the congestion 
window becomes coupled to the packet loss process, and TCP performance is derived in terms of 
properties of the stochastic loss process.  
Additionally, a saturating non-linearity of the system actuator frequently exists in several practical engineering 
systems. If the controller is not designed appropriately, the instability of the system and the serious degradation of the 
network performance will be caused [11]-[12]. Since the control input of TCP/AQM as packet dropping or marking 
probability is limited to between 0 and 1. 
Therefore the result of the stochastic properties of TCP system and the saturating actuator must be considered in the 
design procedure of the controller. 
Furthermore, inspired by the developments of congestion control outlined above, this study 
designs an AQM controller for a time-delayed stochastic TCP system with input saturation. The 
static-state-feedback controller has been developed for AQM to support the TCP based on the LMI 
technique. The remainder of this paper is organized as follows. In Section 2, the principals of TCP 
model is presented. Section 3 illustrates the AQM stabilizing controller design based on the 
proposed feedback control scheme. The simulation results performed to validate the proposed

VI. INTRODUCTION 
IN the last two decade, due to the potential characteristics of chaotic systems such as highly 
complex dynamics, broad-band Fourier power spectrums and strong attractors, the researchers were 
interested in the fundamentals of chaos dynamics [1, 2]. Many effective methods based on the 
theoretical analysis and experimental implementation for synchronization problems have been 
thoroughly investigated [3~5]. On the other hand, several mechanical systems with chaotic 
phenomena have been also observed in recent years [6, 7]. However, not as electronic circuits, to 
establish a mechanical system for researches costs much time and money. Therefore, how to replace 
the mechanical systems by the corresponding electronic circuits with the same dynamics is an 
important issue. Horizontal platform system (HPS) is one of the mechanical systems with chaos 
behavior that can freely rotate around the horizontal axis and extensively applied in offshore and

Lyapunov exponent (LE) [13] is a familiar method for identifying a chaotic system or not. 
It describes the growth and contract rate of vibrations in different directions. When the least LE, 
λ, is positive, this attractor becomes chaotic after extreme moment. There three statuses for λ: 
1. λ<0, the orbit attracts to a fixed point or stable periodic orbit. 
2. λ=0, the orbit is an eventually fixed point. 
3. λ>0, the orbit is unstable a chaos.

III. CONCLUSION 
Based on the OP-Amps and nonlinear circuit design, an electronic HPS is proposed in this paper. The dynamics and 
chaotic behavior of the proposed electronic HPS has been shown to be identical to the mechanical one. Thus the 
developed electronic HPS emulator can be used directly to replace the mechanical HPS for further research work 
and the cost and time can be reduced.
