7.結論 
1、設備在保證期限內與外，實施適當程度的預防保養確實可達到節省總成本的目的。 
2、固定比例模式所求得預防保養節省總成本比率比完美預防保養所節省的總成本比率低，
但較符合實務。 
3、在保證期限外，固定保養程度比例 p 的模式下。當韋伯分配 21 << β 時，失效率上升的
程度會逐漸緩和，我們所使用的保養程度與比例皆與保證期限內相同，使得保證期限外
保養與小修理的總成本逐漸下降，明顯與實務不符。 
4、本研究提出決策模式，讓供消費者由設備價值與預防保養成本做決策，求得符合消費者
需求的預防保養次數與使用期限。

I.  INTRODUCTION 
In this modern world, the skills of technology 
are also changing day by day. With this changing 
world, the customers’ requirement of the qualities 
and reliabilities toward equipment are getting higher 
than before. So as to dealing with this part, the 
manufacturers need to cater for all the customers for 
maintaining or increasing the sales volumes. In the 
situation of a lot of competitive opponents and the 
promotion of the buyer’s consciousness, that 
warranty has become the manufacturer’s important 
strategy in marketing. In the fundamental, the 
warranty is an important contract between the 
manufacturer and buyer. Therefore, when the 
buyer’s product occurs failures in the specific 
interval, manufacturer has the responsibility for the 
buyer to repair, replace, or refund the product. For 
the buyer, warranty is a guarantee and rights. 
Whereas, due to the manufacturer supplies the 
service, so the warranty cost is occurred.  
The amount of warranty cost would produce 
the affection for the profit and price of the product. 
For the manufacturer, the warranty cost must be 
minimum in the warranty period will promote the 
competitive of the market (Glickman and Berger, 
1976; Nguyen and Murthy, 1984; Jhang, 2005). 
Using Failure Rate with Fixed Reduction finds out 
the optimal maintenance policy for rent equipment 
(Chan and Shaw, 1993; Yeh et al., 2009). However, 
the equipment will not be suspended after the 
warranty period ended in pragmatic way. The 
manufacture will not have been requested to their 
equipment whose qualities and reliabilities must be 
within its warranty period. That is, the failure rate 
of equipment always increases with time. The 
customer is charged for repairs with the condition 
that the equipment loses its efficacy outside of 
warranty period. (Barlow and Hunter, 1960；Sheu, 
1999) Hence, how to use the best methods to 
promote the value of the equipment and how to 
reduce simultaneously the failure rate will be a very 
essential issue between manufacturers and 
customers.
