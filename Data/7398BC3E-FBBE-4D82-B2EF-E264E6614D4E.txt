摘要 
本研究計畫「以動覺感性互動為基礎的人與機器人系統的設計開發模式」企圖尋找一種強調身體動覺與
動作愉悅性的互動設計模式，作為未來開發家用服務型機器人與消費型智慧產品的基礎架構。研究之基本動機
在於結合今日的數位科技與人類既有的行動能力與情緒感知，提出一符合今日使用者對豐富多元的人機互動經
驗需求之創新設計典範，將未來服務型機器人的互動品質從純粹功能性、使用性上的滿足，提升至感性與動覺
美感的互動經驗層次。本研究計畫擬於三年期間，以寵物型陪伴機器人為例，建構家用服務型機器人的動作感
性設計系統的理論架構、設計程序與評估模式。第一年工作內容包括：參考國外先進機器人互動研究團隊之研
究程序，整合感性工學與相關動作分析理論提出動覺互動的設計開發模式。因此，本研究的重點在於探討機器
人的動作與使用者所感受到的動作意涵之關連研究，特別是透過機器人動作元素的解析、以及動作元素與語意
關連性的探索。研究方法主要是利用拉邦動作分析(LMA)理論與感性工學為基礎，透過動作相關的語彙搜集、
群聚分析、實驗設計，來解析機器人動作構成要素與使用者感受到的語意或感性訊息之關連性。並提出運用此
一「動作-感性」關係模式的HRI的動作設計建議。作為後續之研究計畫內容之修正依據。 
關鍵字：家用機器人，感性工學，互動設計，動作模式  
 
壹、背景與動機  
隨著科技的普及與智慧化產品的推陳出新，具備智能與行動能力的智慧型機器人逐漸進入今日人類的日常
生活之中。要讓過去屬於科學研究或產業應用的機器人融入人類的活動空間，進而提升人們的生活品質(Brooks, 
2003)，這使得使用者與機器人的人機互動（Human-Robot-Interaction, HRI）形成了一個新興的互動設計議題(游
曉貞等, 2009 & 2010；You and Deng, 2010)。在此大前提下，由於機器人與一般電腦產品最大的區別在於它具有
行動能力與擬人的特性，因此如何利用機器人動作來傳達訊息？機器人與其使用者之間的人機互動能否跟人際
間互動一般，藉由動作來傳遞 HRI訊息或是表現情緒反應？諸如此類的問題引發了本研究提案的動機。為此本
研究團隊旨在探討一般的居家使用者對於不同機器人的動作的詮釋與感受，以作為未來機器人動作指令的設計
建議。主要議題有三：(1) 探索使用者對家用機器人在生活脈絡中的 HRI互動需求；(2) 探索如何利用動作來強
化人機之間的資訊傳達；(3) 建構以動作做為 HRI的互動設計方法。本研究主要以機器人的肢體動作為研究重
心，期望能藉由肢體動作來傳達 HRI 所需的訊息。但市場上之「個人」與「家用」機器人種類型式繁多，且
機器人開發技術的日趨完善，機器人可藉由聲音、顯示螢幕、燈示、肢體動作等方式與人進行互動。本研究主
要以機器人的肢體動作為研究重心，期望能藉由肢體動作來傳達 HRI 所需的訊息。本研究以 Robotis 公司生產

肆、結論與討論 
本研究為「以動覺感性互動為基礎的人與機器人系統的設計開發模式」第一年計畫，參考國外先進機器人
互動研究團隊之研究程序，進行整合感性工學與相關動作分析，企圖提出一動覺互動的設計開發模式雛型。本
研究第一階段的「仿人機器人的動覺感性研究」中企圖從拉邦動作分析(LMA)的四項動作質地要素－時間、空
間、力度、順暢來探索這四項要素對使用者意義感受的影響，故進行一連串的實驗與資料分析。根據上述結果
本研究發現受測者對於某些機器人的肢體動作有著共同的情緒感受佐證本研究之基本假設，並提出運用本研究

COMPREHENSIVE ASSESSMENT 
In this section, the assessments of service design method are divided into three parts to explain the 
application of it. First, the purpose of this method point out the problems that design service might 
encounter. Second, in order to ensure the method is work, there were reminders of implementations for 
share. Third, we review the effect of using method and note the strengths and weakness of it. 
3.1 The intention of this method 
With the progress of science and technology, it gets easy to develop various products and service function. 
However, the new functions do not bring up the great experience anymore, because its process of using is 
just like the training of operation. So, the point of this co-design method is to design service from user's 
perspective, deeply realize the needs of user, plan the service function that fit user's wish. For achieving 
above objectives, holding co-design workshops is one way. 
The following are reasons and intentions this method concern about. 
1. 	 To generate concepts from user's experience 
After exploration stages, the findings of user's life should arrange to concept that lead design 
extending. To design concept with user insight is the aim of this method. While using mood puzzle, 
there're amount of pictures for opening participant's imagination, improving user to express their life 
experience, and help designer present their concept. Because pictures contain more infonnation that 
can't describe in words, designers and users have things to think through this visual tool, they could 
use same graphic language to communicate. This co-design method expects to help better 
imaginations and communications between user and designer. 
2. 	 To facilitate participants extending ideas with each other 
In order to design service experience, the second activity, journey story, is created to guide 
participants design it by planning steps of process. And it ' s also helps the collaborations between 
designers and users. That means their discussion is like connecting ideas and context, user proposing 
contexts and designer extending ideas with it. In this way, the journey that presents service functions 
or items could be close to user's expectation. 
3. 	 To specify service concepts step by step 
In the beginning, mood puzzle aims to design service from overview, then the journey story narrow 
down to design possible ways of service supplied. And then, in order to generate usable ideas, touch 
point pattern aims to help participants define the components of service specifically.

4 DISCUSSION 
In order to figure out potential problems of the u-service method using amI better understand the causes of 
them, we hold workshops, interview with participants and propose a paradigm for discussion the effect of 
using. An effective service co-deign method is worth to be explored for deeply realizing user's mind and 
controlling new design elements of service. 
In a co-design workshop, users are expected to bring up context or innovative insight from their 
experience and the designers are part of facilitators that help ideas generation. To bring their role into full 
play, it relies on the technique of facilitating, and knowing the methods and tools of collaboration will be 
useful to it. 
Because services only exist at the moment of being used, the interactions between people and service are 
not easy to prototype while early stage in design process. The method we proposed above help 
participants knowing how to think and deliver a service concept. 
The good service should always put people first, and this service co-design method is dedicated to 
understand user's need and respect user insight by co-designing with them. Through our method 
exploration, we hope to provide the chance for further discussion on service method design.
