摘要 
蛾眼的仿生結構能提供抗反射特性於寬廣光譜範圍，此結構具有漸變的折射率，
能降低空氣與基板的折射率差異，因而能應用於提高太陽能電池的吸收。一般而
言，反射能隨著此奈米結構的長寬比而降低，然而，高長寬比存在高表面積，短
擴散路徑與較差的機械強度等缺點。另一方面，若此漸變層厚度不足，入射光將
無法分辨整層的抗反射層，致使反射提高。在本研究中，我們製造了兩層的奈米
-奈米結構，乃利用氫電漿在電子束微影的奈米柱表面蝕刻出奈米草。結果顯示，
奈米柱與奈米草的抗反射特性能藉彼此而有所提升。 
 
 
Abstract 
Biomimetic moth-eye nanostructure gives a solution to broadband antireflection. This 
structure is characterized with a graded refractive index profile to mitigate the 
difference between air and substrate, thus can be used to enhanced the absorption of 
solar cell device. In general, the reflection decreases with an increasing of the aspect 
ratio of nanostructure. However, high-aspect-ratio profile has problems of high 
surface area, short diffusion length and weak mechanical strength. On the other hand, 
if the graded layer is not thick enough, the incident light cannot identify the whole 
layer, and partial reflection will arise by the difference of reflective index. In our study, 
we fabricate a two-level nano-on-nano structure to overcome these problems. 
Nanograss made by hydrogen plasma was created on the surface of e-beam-defined 
nanopillar array. The results show that both antireflection performance of nanograss 
and nanopillar can be enhanced by each other.

結論： 
此計畫揭露了一種與半導體製程相容的奈米-奈米結構。藉由奈米草的微小結構，
非正向反射能被抑制，而在長波長的反射能藉由下層的奈米柱結構所抑制。另一
方面，奈米柱的反射也能藉由奈米草而下降，並不需要改變幾何特徵。此結果將
有助於軸向接面奈米線太陽能電池的發展。
