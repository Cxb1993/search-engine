GRAFTING POLYMERIZED N-ISOPROPYLACRYLAMIDE into 
ELECTROSPUN POLYCARBONATE FINE-FIBROUS

一、中文摘要 
 本研究將聚碳酸酯高分子(PC)以電氣紡絲製備具有高孔洞率之纖維狀薄膜，在製備的過
程當中，控制不同的變因以調控出不同的強力及孔洞率；之後並在 PC 薄膜上以電漿進行表
面改質，讓 N-isopropylacrylamide 進行接枝聚合。薄膜的特性以偏光顯微鏡、SEM、孔洞測
定儀、接觸角測試等儀器進行分析。由實驗結果中發現輸液速度 6ml/hr、PC濃度 12.5wt%的
電紡薄膜，其孔洞大小可控制在 3~8μm，再將所製備好的 PC 薄膜進行電漿處理接枝上
PIPAAm，以此薄膜代替 trans-well insert上的 PET薄膜，進行細胞的培養，能培養出的細胞
為層狀，而非一般的懸浮液，所培養出的層狀細胞其結構性都可保持完整。 
 
關鍵詞：細胞工學、聚異丙基丙烯醯胺、碳酸聚酯、電氣紡絲 
 
Abstract 
 In present study, the electrospinning was utilized, and polycarbonate was used to fabricate 
fine-fibrous membrane. We also prepared membranes with different features by controlling factors 
to investigate more strong and more highly porosity PC membrane. Then, the PC membranes were 
treated by plasma to change the property of the surface. After the modification, poly(N-isopropyl 
acrylamide) [pNIPAAm] were grafted in PC membrane for cell culture. The PC solution 12.5wt% 
in a mixture solvent of Dichloromethane, Methanol, Acetone, 1,4-dioxane and the feeding rate keep 
in 6ml/hr. The solution was electrospun into non-woven fiber mesh with fiber diameter ranging 
from 1.1 to 2.5μm and pore size was measured 3~8µm. PNIPAAm was immobilized onto PC 
non-woven surface. This composites material was considered replace the PET membrane in 
trans-well insert. 
Keywords: cell engineering, polycarbonate, poly(N-isopropylacrylamide) pNIPAAm, 
electrospinning

四、結果與討論 
◆濃度與溶劑對於電氣紡絲的影響 
在不同溶劑的部份，先以 DCM : DMF : Acetone = 80 : 5 : 15開始進行實驗，所配置的溶質為
PC 以 10、12.5、15與 20w/v%四種濃度分別對上固定的電 30kV(正電壓 25 kV負電壓 5 kV)
的電紡測試，固定外加電場為 12.5kV 收集時間為 20 分鐘，金屬管徑以 1.07mm、收集距離
15cm與供液速率 0.08ml/min。當 PC的濃度達到 15w/v%以上時，發現到 PC的顆粒無法在所
配置的溶液中溶解，因而不能進行電氣紡絲。在 10 w/v%與 12.5 w/v%的濃度當中，發現到在
進行電氣紡絲的過程當中，只有在 PC濃度為 12.5w/v%都有成絲的現象，因此之後的實驗變
固定此溶質的濃度進行電氣紡絲。但以 12.5w/v%此濃度進行電氣紡絲時發現到會有成絲但無
法從收集板上收集下來的問題，及在電紡紡口會產生嚴重堵塞的問題，為了解決這些問題，
因此要改變溶劑的配方。因此在溶劑當中加入了1,4-Dioxane這種物質，則溶劑配方變為DCM : 
Methanol : Acetone : 1,4-Dioxane = 80 : 15 : 2 : 3，由於其沸點高，可降低溶劑的揮發速度可使
成電紡絲的製作過程降低產生 beads的機率，如此可使的所製作出來的絲具有強度及延展性。
在光學顯微鏡及顯微鏡下的觀察，發現到纖維成絲的情況較之前的溶劑更為良好，但在由收
集盤取下時仍較為不易撕取，撕取時仍容易成片狀；所成的絲其強度及延展性因此再進行溶
劑細微的調整，調整完後的溶劑配方為 DCM : Methanol : Acetone : 1,4-Dioxane = 80 : 10 : 2 : 8
再進行電氣紡絲。 
◆輸液速率與纖維直徑關係 
    為了解輸液速率與纖維直徑關係，實驗當中固定的電壓30kV(正電壓25 kV負電壓5 kV)、
外加電場為 12.5 kV，溶液濃度為 PC 12.5w/v%，溶劑配方為 DCM : Methanol : Acetone : 
1,4-Dioxane = 80 : 10 : 2 : 8，金屬管徑以 1.07mm、收集距離 15cm，再改變其供液之速率，共
有 0.08ml/min、0.16ml/min、0.24ml/min三組，將所製備好的薄膜收集並拍攝 SEM照片，由
SEM圖得知當供液速率為 0.08ml/min時，所得之纖維直徑差異性並不大，直徑大小範圍落於
2μm 左右。但當輸液速率開始上升後，會發現纖維直徑會有小幅度的上升，最後形成和緩之
趨勢，但纖維直徑差就會呈現明顯上升趨勢。 
    當輸液速率為 0.16ml/min 時，由 SEM 圖發現到經由電氣紡絲所製出的纖維直徑開始有
粗細不一致的現象產生，並且有些纖維開始呈現捲曲狀；當輸液速率為 0.24ml/min時，由 SEM
圖發現到纖維直徑的粗細大小差異性非常的大，細的纖維呈現連續的捲曲狀，其中參雜了粗
細大小不一致的纖維。供液速率所得的結果可見，當供液速率在 0.08ml/min時，所得之纖維
直徑非常一致。而當供液速率上升後，纖維直徑差就會有呈現明顯上升的趨勢；這是因為當
供液速率上升後，被推出的高分子溶液變多，但由於外加電場與電壓固定的關係，故能被電
場電壓射出的高分子溶液之量固定，形成過多溶液累積在射出口，而使電場電壓無法消化過

上升。

五、結論 
1. 用電紡薄膜製作的 PC薄膜較 PET薄膜鬆散，更適合培養基的通過。 
2. PC薄膜上其孔洞大小可藉由收集板的轉速進行控制。 
3. PC薄膜確實可進行表面改質的動作且可成功的接上 pNIPAAm。 
4. 經由接觸角的實驗發現，PC薄膜在接枝上 pNIPAAm後，呈現親水性，與原本呈現的疏水
性完全不同。 
5. 改質後的 PC薄膜，保有了 pNIPAAm的特性，且為親水性，更適合用於細胞培養。

GRAFTING POLYMERIZED N-ISOPROPYLACRYLAMIDE into ELECTROSPUN 
POLYCARBONATE FINE-FIBROUS MEMBRANE as A MATRIX for CELL CULTURE 
 
How TSENG1,2, Liang-Yu CHANG3, Ming-Thau SHEU4, Ko-Shao CHEN5 
 
1. Department of Biochemstry, 2. Graduate Institute of Medical Sciences, 3. Graduate Institute of Biomedical 
Materials & Engineering, 4. Graduate Institute of Pharmaceutical Sciences, Taipei Medical University, Taipei 11031, 
5. Department of Materials Engineering, Tatung University, Taipei 10452, Taiwan, ROC.  
Email: tsenghow@tmu.edu.tw 
 
ABSTRACT SUMMARY 
 The polycarbonate (PC) was used to fabricate 
fine-fibrous membrane by electrospinning. Then, the 
electrospun PC membranes were treated by plasma 
to subsequently grafting-copolymerize with 
poly(N-isopropylacrylamide) (pNIPAAm) for cell 
culture. Improved porosity and hydrophobicity of this 
composite for cell culture was considered to be 
replaceable of PET membrane in trans-well insert. 
 
INTRODUCTION 
 Cells connect to one another via cell-to-cell 
junction proteins and ECM and simultaneously 
adhere to culture surfaces by cell membrane 
receptor and adhesive protein when cells are 
cultured confluently in conventional TCPs culture 
system. Normal tissue culture dishes could absorb 
ECMs resulting in cell attachment and cell 
proliferation. Contrast to cell attachment, enzymatic 
digestion are usually utilized to harvest cell from the 
TCPs, but cell membrane receptor and adhesive 
protein are also disrupted resulting in the cell detach 
with considerable damages. Recently, Okano et al. 
have developed an alternative method for 
regenerative therapies by applying the use of 
temperature-responsive (TR) culture dishes [1]. The 
pNIPAAm exhibits the lower-critical solution 
temperature (LCST) near 32°C and a remarkable 
hydration-response to a relatively small change in 
temperature. This TR transition results in not only the 
formation of hydrogen bonds between polymer 
networks and water molecules but also hydrophobic 
interactions, of which also can bring cell attaching 
and detaching when the NIPAAm were grafted onto 
a culture dish [2]. 
 In physiological environment, the nutrient 
supplying of ectoderm-derived epithelial tissue are 
bottom-up from stromal layer. The culture-well-insert 
in “air-lifting” technique is always used to simulate 
the nutrient supplying model for the reconstruction of 
multilayered squamous epithelial structure. However, 
both of NIPAAm grafted TCPs and culture-well-insert 
of PET cannot offer a suitable way to supply the 
nutrient for epithelial tissue cultivation because of low 
porosity. In this study, a matrix membrane for using 
as the culture-well-insert was optimally developed by 
employing the electrospinning to fabricate a series of 
highly porous PC membrane, and the resulting 
membrane was subsequently grafted with NIPAAm 
after plasma treatment.  
 
EXPERIMENTAL METHODS 
 PC (CALIBRE 302-5, LG-Dow Co., Korea) was 
dissolved in a quaternary solvent system composed 
of CH2Cl2, methanol, acetone and 1,4-dioxane 
(12.5%, w/v). Electrospinning conditions were as 
followed: the delivery capillary has an inner diameter 
of 1 mm, a DC motor with 12.5 cm in diameter of 
stainless disc as a collector located about 15 cm 
apart from the capillary tip, feeding rate of PC 
polymer solution set in the range of 1-10 ml/hr, and 
the applied voltage of positive and negative charged 
in the range of 7-22 kV and 2-8 kV, respectively. 
Electrospun PC membranes were individually placed 
inside a bell-jar reactor with parallel electrode. The 
plasma reactor (13.56 MHz) was pumped to a base 
pressure of 30 mTorr before plasma pretreatment. 
After the pressure was adjusted to 100 mTorr by 
continuous flow of the oxygen gas, the plasma 
pretreatment was initiated under 50 W for 10 min. 
Then, ammonium persulfate (APS) (SIGMA Co.) was 
added as an initiator to grafting-copolymerize 10% 
(w/v) NIPAAm (ACROS organics) into the oxygen 
plasma-treated electrospun membrane [3]. 
 Morphology of the obtained fibrous membranes 
was observed under SEM (S-2400, Hitachi) at an 
accelerated voltage of 15 kV. The pore size and pore 
size distribution of electrospun membrane were 
measured by Capillary Flow Porometer 
(APP-1100AX, Porous Materials Inc.). The contact 
angle was performed by contact angle and surface 
tension analyzer (FTA188, First Ten Angstroms). 
 For biocompatibility testing, NIH 3T3 cells were 
seeded on the top of membrane in 15 mm culture 
well. The media was composed of DMEM, sodium 
pyruvate and L-glutamine supplemented with 10% 
FBS and 1% penicillin-streptomycin. Culture plates 
were placed in an incubator for 3 days. The WST-1 
test was used to estimate the cell viability. 
 
RESULTS AND DICUSSION 
 The micro-fibrous PC electrospun membranes 
as fabricated were illustrated in Fig.1 (A). The 
morphology of those membranes was significantly 
affected by the proportion of each solvent. Only if 
CH2Cl2 of low boiling point were used, it resulted in
