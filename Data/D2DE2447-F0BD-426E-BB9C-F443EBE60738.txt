Abstract 
This project was inspired by Drude-Zener Model.The model advocates that electrons in the metal were 
accelerated by electric and magnetic fields, also decelerated by some friction.By this phenomenon, they 
defined the electron relaxation time that is related with damping. Since these phenomena is similar in 
aqueous ion solution in the AC field, some of the parameters are very suitable for ion movement of which 
are appropriately converted. So we had to try this new idea. Currently, it seems a very high chance of 
success. 
We utilized rapid prototyping technology to fabricate the resin substrate with a PMMA cover slide as a 
close-ended chip to avoid evaporation. And we applied AC voltages in ionic solution which lead to regular 
charge recycling, and avoid charge accumulation, which ensures noise lowering and S/N ratio improvement. 
By measuring ionic phase delay during frequency shift, we acquired data in a complex form, that is, real and 
imaginary currents. The measurement implemented in lock-in amplifier system which can realize 
high-sensitivity. We demand to develop a novel molecular database establishment from this novel idea. 
We derived a model to explain the ion movement in the AC electric field, and we made a comparison 
with experimental results and theoretical values. It appears that the trends are quite match, and We 
discovered the Collective Coulomb’s Force exists in ions with the increase of the concentration, which 
attracts positive and negative ions, which is applicable to explain the crystallization phenomenon in high 
concentrations. Using a new physical version to illustrate the ion movement behavior in the solution. We 
found that massive ions possess small damping factor, otherwise, low-mass ions possess large values. The 
preliminary conclusion can be assisted to understand the concept of hydration energy that adopted in current 
chemical society, like effective mass changes of ions in water, and the different resistance that massive and 
low-mass ions faced in the water. In the experiments and simulations, we chose a set of completely 
dissociated strong electrolyte: NaCl, KCl, NaBr, and KBr. The real and imaginary current of four samples 
measured by lock-in amplifier are very close to theoretical calculation, it shows good accuracy of the 
simulation currently. And the damping factor does not change with applied frequency, the result is also 
reasonable in physics. 
 
Keywords: Drude-Zener Mode, relaxation time, damping, ion movement, rapid prototyping, 
close-ended chip, lock-in amplifier, Collective Coulomb’s Force, crystallization, hydration energy.

一、本計畫發展目的 
 
我們希望能夠發展出一套醫學上「更為精確」的檢測技術。以奈米電子及物理的觀點，現今醫
學常用之量測，其界面（interface）與界面間存在著很多複雜的物理機制，足以覆蓋掉疾病發生初期
所產生的微弱信號，致使這些信號為外界所偵測到時，病患的症狀往往已經很嚴重！因為量測界面普
遍存在位能能障（potential barriers），信號傳遞到此處都會被反彈，如此一來，一些微弱信號都被阻
礙掉了，要解決這個問題，方法並不單純。所以我們提倡在信號很微弱時就要能及早精確量測到，，
這是本計畫最重要的理念。在計畫裡，我們將會以電學量測分析為主，並加入光學量測來輔助電學方
面的研究，期望這套技術能建立新的生物分子 ID 資料庫，將生物分子如 DNA、蛋白質等之分子量
及結構等，辨認得更精確，同時確認這些加入離子化生物分子量測系統的光學、電性機制是否會產生
交互作用。我們想研究這些離子在生理環境中，濃度增加到接近結晶時，離子間微觀的一些物理參數
可否為我們提出的新模型所觀測到，這些研究長遠來對於研究慢性病（即體內長期化學汙染所造成）
的成因等相當重要。 
 
在本計畫中，我們利用外加電場驅使離子運動，然而在直流電場持續供應之下，可能會使得電
荷累積在電極附近，意味著量測環境將隨之同時變化。因此，不同於傳統，我們將直流電場更動為交
流電場，以避免電荷持續累積之問題，希望使量測環境不隨時間而改變。根據外加電場所測得到的離
子電流，我們推導了一個模型來解釋離子在交流電場下的運動行為，並且將理論值與實驗結果做一個
對比，發現曲線的趨勢相當的接近在我們的模型推導之中，我們發現了一個離子之間有一個隨著濃度
增加而增加的庫倫作用力，此力會將正負離子吸引在一貣，我們稱之為「群聚庫侖作用力（Collective 
Coulomb Force）」，也希望用此來解釋，當溶液濃度高到一個程度時，溶液會有結晶析出的現象。
並且我們用利用新的物理觀點，來解釋離子在溶液中的運動行為，在解釋模擬參數的過程之中，我們
發現，具較大質量的離子，阻尼因子較小；反之較小質量的離子，阻尼因子較大，這項發現可以解釋
在化學界中，利用水合能的觀念，來解釋離子在水中的等效質量變化，以及大小離子在水中感受到不
同的阻力。
