2摘要
一般傳統速可達機車在行駛的過程中遇到不同路況時，無段變速系統會因為加速或減速的需要而進
行變速；當減速比發生變化時，因傳統無段變速系統為被動式變速，主要由離心滾子的作動改變減速
比，這會使得內燃機工作區域處於不穩定狀態，且造成動力傳動系統的衝擊和內燃機的油耗增加。為
了使內燃機維持在一個較佳經濟燃油消耗區域之輸出狀態，利用電子控制式無段變速系統，能讓內燃
機維持在低油耗、低污染之最佳操作點來運轉達到提升燃油經濟性與機車操控之性能。
本研究計畫也著重於運動性及安全性，使得內燃機能維持在較佳操作點來運轉之外，在運動性方面也
因為可主動調整減速比、使得速可達機車可以在相同的車速上卻有著更高的內燃機轉速，以提昇機車
之運動性能。在安全性方面也因為可以主動式控制減速比，在緊急煞車時可獲得適當之內燃機煞車，
減少煞車時所需的力量，避免車輪鎖死、事故的發生。此外，提高變速系統應用於實車上的可行性，
及降低開發成本的花費，也是研究的目的之一。

Abstract
A continuously variable transmission system of a traditional scooter will change the speed as a result of
the acceleration or deceleration when facing different road conditions. Since the speed changes passively the
traditional continuously variable transmission system and the change in the gear ratio is from the movement
of the centrifugal roller, the working area of the internal combustion engine will become unsteady when the
gear ratio changes；therefore resulting in the power transmission system being damaged and the engine to
wasting a lot of oil. Electronically control continuously variable transmission system is employed to enable
the internal combustion engine to operate at an optimal fuel economy condition. E-CVT can provide the
vehicle to work at the best operating point of low fuel consumption and low pollution .
This research also focuses on the sports and safety. In the concept of sports, the E-CVT in the current
state not only adjusts gear ratio actively, but also allows for the internal combustion engine to run at higher
revolutions in our E-CVT with the same speed and there for the higher motors’ performance. In the aspect
of safety, the system with an appropriate anti-lock braking system in the current state can reduce the minimum
needed power for braking, and allowing the wheels to avoid being locked-up during the case of an emergency
braking. Furthermore, other purposes in this study are to increase the probability of practical applications on
an actual car and to reduce the cost of development.

無段變速系統(CVT)意指減速比呈連續無段差變化的變速器，它能提供車輛動力傳遞的連續性、降
低零組件種類、維修更為簡易和提高駕駛舒適性。然而電子式無段變速系統(E-CVT)於機車上的使用，
雖已經有廠商各別量產，但因成本過高及體積過於龐大的情況下，尚未普及。以下將介紹無段變速系
統(CVT)、電子式無段變速系統(E-CVT)特點與發展。
無段變速系統的特點
無段變速系統(CVT)最早的設計概念是由 Worley 在 1955 年時所提出[2]，當時為改善設計農業工具
機，Worley 觀察無段變速系統的運動模式，並配合經驗的修正，建立了皮帶張力與可動徑輪軸向推力
之間的關係式。1958 年由荷蘭的 DAF(Van Doorne's Automobile Fubrick)公司，首先將橡膠的 V 型
皮帶無段變速系統(CVT)傳動技術運用於小型汽車上，如圖 2[3]所示。早期的無段變速系統大多使用
於二輪雪車上，直到 1980 年 HONDA 才將無段變速系統應用於速可達上，如圖 3[4]所示。在 1981 年
時山葉機車也首度引進名為“跑速樂”的速可達機車，是台灣第一輛使用 CVT 變速系統的機車、在當
時風靡全台灣，也開啟台灣的速可達王國之路。
圖 2.裝置於 DAF66 型的 CVT[3] 圖 3. 1980 年 HONDA TACT 50[4]
由於無段變速系統具有連續無段控制減速比、操作簡易、機構簡單、製造成本低廉、換檔無頓挫
感等優點，有別於其他傳動變速機構。一般傳動變速機構只能在固定的傳動減速比下運作，速可達上
的無段變速系統(CVT)則是利用旋轉效應使得離心滾子產生側向推力的原理推動可動徑輪，改變皮帶在
可動徑輪上的半徑，可以在有限的範圍內做出無限多組的傳動減速比，使用在車輛上、內燃機轉速與
車速之間的關係，可以在連續範圍內做調整，如圖 4[5]所示。

Abstract. Drilling is the most economic and convenient operation of secondary machining for hole making of
fiber-reinforced materials owing to the need for structure joining. Delamination is commonly recognized as a
major defect after drilling composite materials. In general, the delamination is an irregular shape and size,
containing long and fine breaks and cracks at the exit of the drilled hole, especially in the drilling of carbon
fiber-reinforced plastic (CFRP). The conventional delamination factor ( aF ) is not appropriated to characterize
the damage area because the shape of the delamination after drilled CFRP is not a regular representation of
the damage magnitude. In this paper, a novel approach of the equivalent delamination factor ( edF ) is
proposed and compared with the adjusted delamination factor ( daF ) and the conventional delamination factor
( aF ). The experimental results show the edF obtained is considered suitable for characterizing delamination
at the exit of a hole after drilling composite materials.
Introduction
Carbon-fiber reinforced composites have gained much attention for their superior mechanical properties and
are attractive for use in aerospace, defense and transportation applications. However, these materials possess
peculiar characteristics governing their behavior during machining. The mechanism of machining composite
materials has been identified as fundamentally different from that of homogeneous metal removal [1]. Based
on experimental observations, little plastic deformation of composite materials occurs during cutting, and the
fracture resistance is ten to one hundred times lower than that of common steels. Drilling is the most
economic and convenient operation of secondary machining for hole making of fiber-reinforced materials
owing to the need for structure joining. Various cutting tools are available for hole making, but the twist drill
with two cutting edges on its working end is by far the most common. However, the efficiency of the cutting
action varies, being the most efficient at the outer diameter of the twist drill. The delamination damage caused
by the cutting edges of twist drill bits is one of the major concerns during drilling. Many references of the
drilling of fiber-reinforced plastics state the quality of the cut surfaces strongly depends on the drilling
parameters [1, 2]. Kobayashi stated the larger the feeding load, the more serious the cracking around the exit
edge of the hole [3]. On the other hand, damage development and detection, new tooling design, and the
influences of cutting conditions in drilling composite materials have been studied [4-9]. Several
non-traditional machining processes, such as laser-beam drilling, water-jet drilling (with or without abrasive
additive), ultrasonic drilling, electro discharge machining (EDM), have been reported as alternatives.
Nevertheless, conventional drilling still continues to be used widely for practical purposes.

in this paper. daF and edF had better discrimination of delamination damage compared to aF , which was
identical at the hole exit. Additionally, the trend is almost similar to that of daF and edF . daF is more
clearly larger than edF . However, daF is null for minimal and maximal delamination area. On the other hand,
the image digitalization and processing is also an essential method to obtain the delamination area after
drilling a hole on composite laminates. The edF obtained through digital image processing is considered
suitable for characterizing delamination at the exit of the drilled hole.
