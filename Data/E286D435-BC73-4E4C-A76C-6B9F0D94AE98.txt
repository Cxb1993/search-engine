低溫多晶矽高壓橫向雙擴散金氧半場效電晶體之製作與穩定度探討(I)  
“Low-temperature poly-Si HV-LDMOSFET fabrication and stability study (I)” 
計畫編號：NSC95-2221-E-211-021 
執行期間：95 年 8 月 1 日 至 96 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：林智玲 華梵大學電子工程系 副教授 
 
1.中文摘要 
在本計畫中我們結合高功率元件與
薄膜電晶體之製程技術，製作出低溫多
晶矽橫向雙擴散金氧半場效電晶體（
LTPS LDMOSFET），並以步階式離子
植入與準分子雷射退火技術提升其元
件特性，最後利用半導體參數分析儀、
高壓量測系統，量測低溫多晶矽功率元
件之直流特性與崩潰電壓，並與 SPC製
程做比較來分析此低溫製程技術製作
高功率元件之可行性，根據結果得到，
在Vd s=10 V和Vd s=30 V下，經過雷射處
理 的 開 關 電 流 比 分 別 為 5.98×104 和
2.65×105，比起 SPC製程 1.86和 2.27大了
大約 105倍之多；此外在漂移區長度 35 
μm和步階式摻雜情況下，經過雷射處理
的崩潰電壓最高可達 260V；在漂移區為
15μm和步階式摻雜情況下，經過雷射處
理的特徵導通電阻為 6.9 Ω-cm2，比起
SPC製程的 7.6×105 Ω- cm2少了大約 105
倍。  
關鍵詞：橫向雙擴散金氧半場效電晶體
、薄膜電晶體、步階式離子植入、準分
子雷射退火技術  
 
英文摘要 
   Low-temperature polysilicon thin-film 
transistor laterally lateral double diffusion metal 
oxide semiconductor field effect transistors 
(LTPS LDMOSFETs) were fabricated by 
combining a thin film transistor with the power 
structure and excimer laser annealing. 
Simultaneous excimer laser annealing for and 
substrate heating at 400  ℃ LTPS-LDMOS will 
enable integration with the driving circuit of a 
System-On-Panel (SOP) and other circuits in the 
future.  The maximum breakdown voltage of 
step doping LTPS-LDMOS after excimer laser 
annealing is 260 V with Ldrift=35 μm. The on/off 
current ratio is 2.06×105. It exceeds that of solid 
phase crystallized (SPC) LIGBT, by a factor of 
about 106 at Vanode=30 V with Ldrift=35 μm. In 
Ldrift=15 μm and step doping condition, the 
specific on-resistance after laser treatment is 
about 6.9 Ω-cm2 which is lower about 9.4 times 
than uniform doping. 
Keywords: LTPS LDMOSFETs, SOP, excimer 
laser annealing 
2. Introduction 
In recent years, plasma display panels and 
liquid crystal displays have been developed on a 
large scale. Accordingly, the need for high 
voltage circuits in these devices has increased. 
Low-temperature polysilicon high-voltage thin 
film transistors (LTPS HVTFTs) will become 
increasingly important in the future as SOP and 
3D circuits become integrated.  
In this investigation, LTPS-LDMOS was 
implemented in three steps of doping and by 
to 260 V. Figure 6 shows the relationship of 
specific on-resistance and drift region length 
under uniform and step doping of drift region. 
The specific on-resistance of step-doping after 
laser treatment is about 6.9 Ω-cm2.  
Figure 7 shows the gate transfer 
characteristics of LTPS-LDMOS between SPC 
and excimer laser treatments for uniform and 
three-step doping.  
5. Conclusions 
This work presents the three-step doping 
drift region of LTPS-LDMOS following excimer 
laser annealing. The step doping drift region that 
was treated by excimer laser annealing was 
herein linearly doped instead. The breakdown 
voltage of LTPS-LDMOS depends on not only 
the drift region length but also the doping profile. 
The maximum breakdown voltage of 
LTPS-LDMOS is 260 V.
