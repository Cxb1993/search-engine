ABSTRACT 
A lot of exist apartment communities in Taiwan, because the lane is got narrow and small, in 
addition, the parking space is insufficient, the cars and motorcycles are filled up with the whole 
lane to get, in case the fire occur, it is almost unable to enter the narrow and small lane to carry on 
the disaster relief work to rescue. Since in initial stage of the fire accident, the households have to 
depend on their own fire safety devices in the scene of a fire. But regrettable, the fire safety 
devices in the old apartment, only having several fire extinguishers. Though there is drafting, the 
effect is not good of relevant policy. 
After the staircase of the apartment usually takes place for household's fire, the ones that 
only stored only fled for one's life and walked, very unfortunate, passway of flee hither and 
thither by smoke too often. In the case of a fire, the most dangerous situation does not suffer to 
danger of fire directly, but is smoke. The more airtight building, the more obvious the stack effect 
is. The apartment garret is used to putting on the building at present, but has not almost held the 
smoke space in the narrow and small passway, so once the fire takes place, usually the garret 
household is injured the most serious. This research confirms the emergence of the stack effect, 
and put forward the concrete and feasible improvement scheme, it is time-consuming or stop up 
the difficulty that find it difficult to walk on vast day not to have, can offer the relevant unit and 
apartment household to consult. 
 
Keywords: Apartment, Fire, Stack Effect, Smoke
