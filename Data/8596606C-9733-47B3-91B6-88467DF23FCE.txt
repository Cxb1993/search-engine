摘要 
在肘關節與外固定器結合之結構中，均向牽引與牽引損失之機制仍未被釐清。此外，就
橋式肘關節而言，其關節表面間之正確牽引距離在文獻報告中並未一致。本研究以現有固定
器設計及肘關節角度的方式觀察牽引損失之機制。本文發展四個肘關節屈曲角度為 90°，
120°，150°及180°之模型。藉由有限元素分析肱骨端及尺骨端牽引裝置於肘關節中央之均向
牽引貢獻度。在橋式結構中之牽引損失則沿於肘關節兩側之特定負載傳遞路徑所探討。在四
肘關節模型中，均向牽引及非均向牽引同時發生於肘關節中央。牽引損失總是存在於橋式肘
關節之關節牽引。相較下，屈曲角度為120°之肘關節模型顯示出更有效率之均向牽引。對於
120°之肘關節模型的牽引損失則最高。牽引損失主要是由於固定骨釘之外側撓曲及牽引裝置
所牽引長度於垂直方向之轉換所引起。固定骨釘之外側撓曲則為骨釘及關節周圍組織強度之
函數。因此，肘關節解剖幾何、肘關節角度及固定器在肘關節表面間之有效關節牽引的垂直
轉換扮演著重要之角色。

Abstract 
The mechanisms of concentric distraction and distraction loss within the elbow-fixator-pin 
construct remains unclear. Furthermore, the literature reports are inconsistent regarding the correct 
distraction distance between the articulating surfaces of a bridged elbow. This study investigated the 
mechanism of the distraction loss in terms of the relevant fixator design and elbow angle. Four 
elbow joints flexed at 90°, 120°, 150°, and 180° were developed in this study. The contribution of 
each humeral and ulnar distractor to the concentric distraction at the elbow center was evaluated by 
the finite-element method. The distraction loss within the elbow-fixator-pin construct was studied 
along the specific load-transferring paths on both elbow sides. Among four elbow models, both 
concentric and eccentric distractions simultaneously occur at the elbow center. The distraction loss 
always exists in the joint distraction of the bridged elbow. Comparatively, the 120° elbow model 
showed the more effectively concentric distraction. For the 180° elbow model, the distraction loss 
was the highest. This distraction loss was mainly attributed to the lateral deflection of the fixing 
pins and the vectorial transformation of the distracted length of the distractors. The lateral 
deflection of the fixing pins is a function of the stiffness of both pins and periarticular tissues. The 
spatial relationship of the elbow anatomy, elbow angle, and fixator frame plays a significant role in 
the vectorial transformation of the effective distraction between the elbow articulating surfaces.

Discussion and Conclusion 
針對肘關節的均勻牽引，本研究呈現出肱骨及尺骨牽引之向量分析。不論在肱骨端或尺
骨端透過骨釘夾鉗與拉伸裝置產生之牽引負載，其方向主要為沿伸縮套筒之長軸方向。然而
對於均勻牽引之詳細計算，固定器架構、骨骼長軸及尺骨尖端處連線皆應該納入考慮。倘若

Residual stresses occur in the deposited thin films are unavoidable. The 
mismatch of lattice and thermal expansion between the ITO thin film and flexible 
substrate induces large amount of residual stresses [5, 6, 7]. Residual stresses can be 
either beneficial or detrimental to the thin film performance, which may improve the 
film stiffness or distort its structures, resulting in cracking in the case of tensile 
stresses or buckling in compressive stresses. It is thus necessary to systematically 
investigate the residual stresses of thin films on flexible substrates. 
Nanoindentation is the most common used method in measuring mechanical 
properties of thin films, which provides a depth-sensing indentation test that has been 
successfully utilized to characterize the hardness and elastic modulus of micro 
structures [8]. The hardness and elastic modulus of the thin film can be directly 
determined by the load-depth curve of the indentation. However, the nanoindentation 
is difficult to avoid the substrate effect, especially for the flexible substrates. Besides, 
the stress state of the thin films also affects the indentation results. 
In this work, the effects of the residual stresses on the measured elastic modulus 
and hardness are discussed. Three types of ITO thin films are deposited on 
polycarbonate (PC) flexible substrates by a radio frequency magnetron sputtering 
machine. The residual stresses of the thin films are measured by the sin2ψ method of 
X-ray diffraction (XRD). Furthermore, the elastic modulus and hardness are measured 
by the nanoindentation. Linear correlations between the residual stresses and the 
measured mechanical properties are found in the final.
