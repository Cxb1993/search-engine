以固態黏著型共振器建構雙頻之π階梯式與耦合式體聲波濾波器 
The fabrication of dual-band π ladder filter and coupled resonator filter  
using the solidly mounted resonator 
計畫編號：NSC 96－2221－E －237－002 
執行期限：96年8月1日至97年7月31日 
主持人：鄭建銓教授  (德霖技術學院電子工程科) 
 
一、 中文摘要 
本計畫以高聲阻抗的鉬薄膜及低聲阻
抗的二氧化矽薄膜，交替堆疊形成布拉格反
射器，再利用反應性射頻磁控濺鍍系統沉積
氮化鋁薄膜作為壓電層，構成固態黏著型諧
振元件，其中並搭配c軸傾斜的壓電薄膜與
不同頂電極質量負載，探討雙頻率諧振響應
與頻率調變之關係。 
為建構雙頻之聲波元件，採用偏軸成長
法研製c軸傾斜的氮化鋁薄膜，其聲波元件
具有激發出雙頻率諧振的能力，對於高頻無
線通訊以及高感度感測器有獨特之應用層
面；本計畫並利用質量負載效應來調變雙頻
諧振器的頻率，分成精細調整與大範圍頻率
調整，精細調整是為了後續階梯式濾波器的
調頻控制，而大範圍頻率調整則是為了探討
負載效應對於頻率響應的關係。 
本計畫成功地獲得優異之雙頻率諧振
響應，對於頻率之負載效應亦有充分且完整
之探討。 
 
關鍵詞：射頻濺鍍，氮化鋁，固態黏著型共
振器，雙頻率響應，質量負載 
Abstract 
In this project, we focused on the 
fabrication and frequency-modulation of λ/4 
mode dual-band solidly mounted resonators. 
To accomplish the Bragg reflector, the RF/DC 
magnetron sputtering system with dual targets 
is adopted to deposit alternating layers of 
quarter-wavelength Mo and SiO2 thin films. 
The piezoelectric layer, aluminum nitride 
(AlN) thin films, is deposited on the Bragg 
reflector by means of reactive RF magnetron 
sputtering. We alter the distance between 
substrate and target to deposit AlN with 
various c-axis tilting angle which results in 
longitudinal and shear acoustic waves at the 
same time. Furthermore, we use mass loading 
effect to modulate the resonance frequency. 
The loading mass is finely modulated for the 
latter fabrication of ladder type filters, and the 
loading mass is also extensively modulated for 
the clarification of the relationship between 
the loading effect and the frequency 
responses.  
We successfully fabricated SMRs with 
dual frequency responses and investigated the 
frequency modulation thoroughly. 
 
Keywords: RF magnetron sputtering, 
Aluminum nitride, solidly mounted resonator, 
dual frequency response, mass loading 
 
二、 計畫緣由與目的 
本計畫之目的是製作雙頻固態黏著型
體聲波共振器 (Solidly mounted resonator, 
SMR)及濾波器，其元件之規格也依照所設
計之規範來製作。首先採用c-軸傾斜壓電薄
膜之特性，在單一材料中激發出縱向及剪向
的體聲波，研製具有雙頻率響應之體聲波元
件，並開發雙頻固態黏著型共振器的頻率控
制製程，利用質量負載效應(Mass loading 
effect)來調變共振器的共振頻率，並嘗試將
共振器組合成帶通濾波器；製作完成之元件
將進行封裝測試，再將封測前後之頻率響應
軸排向之AlN薄膜，而為了成長c軸傾斜的
AlN，將晶片放置偏離靶材中心正下方，藉
以調整濺鍍離子團由靶材入射至基板表面
的角度，而偏離靶材中心正下方的距離分別
為0~7 cm，如圖二所示；所成長之AlN薄膜
則以X光繞射儀(X-ray diffraction, XRD)與
場 發 射 式 電 子 顯 微 鏡 (Field-emission 
scanning electron microscopy, FE-SEM)，來
進行薄膜晶相與表面特性之分析。

五、 結果與討論 
在先前的研究中[19]，對於¼λ模態之
SMR元件的研製方法與元件特性已有初步
地探討，由其研究結果顯示，為了降低多層
薄膜介面間的聲波散射效應，利用調整薄膜
製程參數的方式，減少各層薄膜的表面粗糙
度，有助於提升元件之頻率響應。本研究搭
配原子力顯微鏡的量測，以最佳製程參數所
成長之七層Mo/SiO2布拉格反射器，其表面
粗糙度可達2.282 nm，並且由圖三SEM剖面
圖所示，可清晰地看出Mo以及SiO2薄膜的
介面交替成長情形相當良好。另外，最頂層
的Mo薄膜除了形成聲波箝制面外，對成長
高c軸優選的AlN壓電薄膜具有極大的幫
助，而且Mo薄膜亦為本結構之底電極，在
Satoh的研究中顯示高聲阻抗的底電極可以
增加元件之品質因素，並且建議底電極的聲
阻抗應高於壓電層的1.5倍[20]，以本研究而
言，Mo與AlN的聲阻抗比達到1.8，將可確
保元件之高品質因素。 
AlN的壓電性源自於其不對稱的晶格
結構，可藉由適當之製程條件使得AlN具有
c軸優選取向，而成長高c軸優選排向之AlN
薄膜，是獲得高機電耦合係數的重要關鍵之
一；另外，成長具有傾斜c軸之AlN薄膜，是
獲得剪向聲波的必要條件[21]；因此同時成
長出具有高c軸優選排向與傾斜c軸之AlN薄
膜，為本研究重要的方向。

六、 計畫成果自評 
本研究利用七層Mo/SiO2薄膜研製布拉
格反射器，藉由適當地調變濺鍍參數，製作
出平整的布拉格反射層，降低了聲波能量的
散射；並採用了適當的濺鍍壓力與基板溫
度，以成長出具有高c軸優選與傾斜的AlN
薄膜。 
對於雙頻率元件的研製方面，利用偏軸
成長的方式成長出不同傾斜角度的AlN薄
膜，驗證出剪向波的產生與c-軸傾斜角度有
關，並且隨著c-軸傾斜角度的增加，剪波訊
號隨之增強，亦即其剪向的K2值越強；傾斜
c軸AlN的元件在1.4 GHz激發出剪波諧振訊
號，元件獲致顯著之縱波與剪波諧振訊號。
做為感測器的應用方面，此雙頻率響應元件
將可拓展感測器之應用範圍，而在無線通訊
的應用方面，亦完成了單一元件實現雙頻率
響應之目標。 
在頻率細調上，中心頻率線性的向下漂
移，符合Sauerbrey方程式，其頻率調變度約
為3494.21 Hz-cm2/ng，而隨著負載的增加，
頻率調變度有逐漸下降的趨勢；本研究完成
了頻率調變的探討，奠定了後續階梯式濾波
器調頻控制的基礎，亦詳細地探討了負載效
應對於頻率響應以及頻率調變度的關係。 
整體而言，本研究已達成預期的目標，
與原計畫預期完成的工作項目相符合，成功
的研製特性更為優異之固態微型共振器元
件，並朝向研製雙頻之濾波器元件邁進。

1. Introduction 
In recent years, the issue of energy shortage is being considered with increasing attention. 
Countries worldwide are actively researching for substitutions of smaller size, lighter weight, 
higher density in power, better in economics, security, and environment. Recycling power comes in 
various types, including water, wind, solar, tidal, and oceanic.  In particular, solar power is the 
most frequently used.  However, the application of solar power is restricted by day-and-night limit.  
Therefore, development of green energy should be able to amend for the disadvantage of solar 
power. Other types of recyclable energy are also being used.  Wind power was one of the earliest 
employed energy.  Before Industrial Evolution, wind power was the source of mechanical power; 
the use of such energy was therefore not restricted by time of day.   
Many scholars have conducted studies on piezoelectric power generators, mainly focusing on 
PZT piezoelectric material [1-3].  However, the production of the material causes serious 
environmental pollution.  In this study, the researchers adapted ZnO as element of piezoelectric 
thin film due to two main factors: 1) that it has relatively higher piezoelectric coupling coefficient, 
and 2) that the production of ZnO thin film does not cause serious environmental pollution.  
Under appropriate control of sputtering parameters, the production process could be done in room 
temperature, which is a critical component in the production process of flexible PET substrate.  In 
this study various sputtering parameters were considered to grown ZnO thin film with piezoelectric 
properties.
3. Result and Discussion 
Under various RF power the surface of ZnO thin film appears as shown in Figure 3.  As RF 
power increases the size of grain increases along, while the distribution of grains on the surface 
becomes less even.  ZnO obtains more energy to deposit when power increases, but when the RF 
power is higher than a threshold value, the deposition is likely to be inferior, causing the quality of 
the thin film to degrade.  From the SEM diagrams in Figure 3, the grain distribution is optimum 
when thin film was deposited under 75W of RF power. At RF power of 150W, the deposited thin 
film did not exhibit compact grain structure, even grain size, nor low coarseness of grain [6]. 
Illustrated in Figure 4, both intensity of refraction and c-axis orientation of grain growth are 
stronger as RF power increases.  However, the XRD result shown from sample deposited under 
150W of power demonstrated a non-c-axis orientation, which is similar to what was shown in SEM.  
RF power of 150W is too high for production of good ZnO thin film. Physical analysis based on 
SEM diagrams in Figure 3 showed that the deposited thin film, produced by 75W of RF power, has 
a more even distribution of crystal grains.  The compactness is higher, and that the angle obtained 
from diffraction diagram approaches 34.5 degree, and therefore the stress received was smaller. A 
sample of 0.6x0.6 cm2 was produced from the thin film produced under 75W of RF power, into a 
piezoelectric power generator.  When attached to a stable source of vibration of 120Hz, the 
measurement of power generator is as shown in Figure 5. The maximum open-circuit voltage 
produced reached 3V, 27μW/ cm2.
