several years. However, the past two years the 
upstream polysilicon materials suffered from a lack 
of overall industrial chain caused by soaring prices, 
the biggest issue of the silicon solar cell is the 
price of material-partly because of polysilicon 
feedstock purification process require large-scale 
plant, consuming a lot of energy, so the unit cost is 
not cheap, nor can quickly increase capacity in 
response to market needs； partly due to the physical 
nature of the restrictions, the thickness of the film 
on silicon solar cells is currently at least 200μm, 
thus needs the thickness of the film on large amount 
of raw materials. 
Thin-film solar cells can not only save material (the 
thickness of the silicon wafer solar cells may be 
reduced more than 90%), can also use inexpensive 
glass, plastic or stainless steel as substrates, and 
can use roll to roll mass production process for 
large area solar cells. However, low conversion 
efficiency of the current thin-film solar cells need 
more R&D on new materials and fabrication process. 
CIGS thin-film solar cells have better conversion 
efficiency, but have some disadvantages, such as 
expensive equipments, complicated processes, and the 
release of toxic fume. Electrospinning process can 
effectively cover these disadvantages and 
substantially improve the application performance and 
market competitiving. Electrospinning is the current 
process for making nano-fibers with less raw 
materials, and simple processes. 
We try to use the electrospinning process to 
fabricate CIGS solar cells to reduce investment 
costs, material consumption, environmental pollution 
and the release of toxic substances also simplify the 
processes and to enhance the coversion efficiency.

1. 計畫目標 
     CIGS 薄膜太陽電池生產方法很多，大致可分為真空與非真空製程，真空是以共蒸鍍及濺鍍為
主，非真空製程則以電鍍或合成各式化合物漿料，再以印刷塗佈方式於基材上。目前主要的是採用濺
鍍法的方式生產。現有 CIGS 製程(真空與非真空)均有其缺點，且無法避免 CdS 之毒性汙染，又有量
產速度慢與生產成本高的缺失。因此本計畫擬以靜電紡絲取代濺鍍法，並在塑膠、玻璃或是金屬基板
上製備 CIGS 太陽能電池，不僅可以降低投資成本減少材料損耗，也可簡化製程並降低環境污染與有
毒物質之逸散同時可大幅提升生產速度。
