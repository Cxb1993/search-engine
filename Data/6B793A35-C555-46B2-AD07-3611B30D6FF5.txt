計畫名稱： 奈米鑽石調質潤滑油使用於精密機械之潤滑特性探討—子計畫二：主軸及
導軌潤滑油添加奈米粉體之流變性質及磨潤影響探討 
計畫編號：NSC 95－2221－E－019－025－ 
執行期間： 95 年 08 月 01 日至 96 年 07 月 31 日 
計畫主持人：周昭昌  助理教授 
計畫參與人員：李思憲 
中文摘要 
 本計畫在於探討奈米鑽石懸浮潤滑油的流變性質及磨潤性能。這其中包括該流體的黏
度值及其受溫度影響的狀況都被加以討論，並同時提供了一個迴歸的流變關係式。透過粒徑
大小分佈的量測，可以得知懸浮顆粒團聚的狀況，較佳的分散方式也可以比較出來。此外，
這種潤滑劑的降低摩擦力與減少磨耗的性能可以一系列的磨耗試驗去加以比較而來。試驗是
以鉻鋼珠和碳鋼 AISI1045, AISI1025 以及鋁合金 6061 的對磨來加以進行，從試片的磨痕以
及接觸條件，例如摩擦力和摩擦係數，可以展現出來。碳鋼和鋁合金的差異提供了進一步瞭
解奈米鑽石懸浮液之潤滑機制的線索。 
 
關鍵詞：奈米鑽石; 添加劑; 流變學; 團聚 
Abstract 
 This project studied the rheology property and tribological performance of a 
nanodiamond-dispersed lubricant. The viscosity as well as the dynamic and thermal behavior of 
this lubricant was investigated and a rheological relation was obtained by statistical regression. 
Basing on the inspection of aggregation condition via particle sizing, a proper mechanical 
dispersing process was also proposed. Furthermore, the anti-friction and wear-reduction 
performance of this lubricant was explored by conducting a series of tribological tests. Carbon 
chromium steel ball rubbing with various materials such as carbon steels AISI 1045, AISI 1025, 
and aluminium alloy 6061 were implemented and compared. The tribological behavior of the 
lubricant was portrayed by the observation of the wear track and the test data of the contact 
condition. The different results of carbon steel and aluminium alloy gained an insight into the 
lubrication by means of nanodiamond suspension. 
 
Keywords：nanodiamond; additive; rheology; aggregation

一、前言 
在機械產業中，潤滑劑的應用量與產
值本來就很高，再加上環保與精密度的需
求日益嚴苛，奈米粉體的添加改質方式，
已是頗具潛力的應用方向。傳統的潤滑在
高温和邊界潤滑條件下，油性劑(oiliness 
additive)和極壓添加劑 (extreme-pressure 
additive 或  EP additive)是不可缺少的
[1]。油性劑是透過凡德瓦力或化學鍵結在
對磨的金屬表面上形成牢固的定向吸附
膜，防止金屬直接接觸，可起減摩抗磨的
作用。但在高温下(環境溫度或接觸溫度的
提高都有可能)，油性劑失效；在極高負荷

奈米鑽石粉的製造方法，可分為細分
散金鋼石和超分散鑽石。细分散鑽石是用
衝擊波作用到石墨或碳黑(soot)與炸藥的
混合物上製取的，粒徑大小分別為 10~150 
nm 和 20~800 nm。超分散鑽石在目前國際
上最流行的是負氧平衡法(negative oxygen 
balance) ，最早是由 Gneiner 等人[9]所發
展出來的。它是利用高能炸藥產生的瞬間
高温高壓而使部分非晶形碳(amorphous 
carbon)轉化成 1~20 nm 大小的鑽石。Tao
等人[7] 曾針對其結晶結構與合成過程所
產生的碳黑成份，以 TEM 做過仔細的探
討。但這樣的奈米鑽石容易團聚。只有採
取有效措施使這些奈米微粒呈超分散狀
態，才能使它們的優點充分發揮出来。根
據 Tao[7]等人的研究結果認為這種具有優
異的承載能力與抗磨損、降低摩擦特性的
奈米顆粒，可能的潤滑機制有兩項：其一
是奈米顆粒的表面效應。超分散鑽石的比
表面積可高達 400 m2/g 左右，它的表面原
子可占原子總數的百分之幾十。因而具有
吸附力强、表面活性高的特點。這些奈米
微粒既可被吸附在摩擦表面，同時又可牢
固地吸附油分子。另一潤滑機制就是將滑
動摩擦轉變為滾動摩擦。由於具有天文數
字的奈米鑽石微粒是承載能力極强的微
滾珠，從而使摩擦係數明顯的下降。 
雖然這種奈米材料的優異特性逐漸
被發掘出來，奈米粉體的應用潛力受到了
極大的重視。然而奈米粉末團聚的現象一
直是普遍存在且難以排除的問題，這使得
奈米粉末不易顯現出其原本優異的性
質。在種種分散技術中，除了化學分散技
術外，比較可行的是超音波分散技術。雖
然奈米鑽石運用的超分散技術似乎可以
避免奈米粉末團聚的現象，這種技術通常
會受限於時效與某些操作條件。故如何破
除奈米粉體之間團聚的情形，使粉末可被
均勻地分散於不同的介質材料當中，是落
實潤滑劑產業奈米化的關鍵因素之一。超
音波分散技術是將需要分散的粉體直接
置於超音波儀器中，是種強度很高的分散
技術。但好的分散效果並不是無止盡的提
高震盪頻率[10]，實驗證明對於分散懸浮
體存在最佳的超音波震盪頻率[11]，而頻
率之值決定於分散粒子的尺寸，當粒子尺
寸增加時其震盪頻率應降低。而震盪功
率，則提高相同粉體的分散效果，但是功
率高會帶來溫升效應，使顆粒重新團聚，
有其侷限性，需考慮震盪時間與散熱等問
題。

在進行磨耗實驗之後，針對各材質之

微滾珠軸承的效應以達到抗磨減摩的功

其它材料應該也可以適用。但是在此鋁合
金卻展現出不一樣的狀況，所以他的解釋
應該只適用於碳鋼材質才對。 
六、結論 
1. 使用之鑽石調質潤滑油，基本上均可
以 Bigham Plastic，Herschel-Bulkley 和
Casson 這三種流變模型來表示，但其
中以前兩者回歸效果較佳。由於線性
效果極明顯，建議以 Bigham Plastic 
model 做為分析時使用的流變模型。奈
米調質之潤滑油在較高的溫度時，超
音波分散的效應相對不明顯，而不再
反映出分散效果對黏度的影響。 
2. 從實驗之結果顯示，分散良好的奈米
調質潤滑油可以有效降低碳鋼之啟始
摩擦力，以及最終摩擦係數。但是對
鋁合金而言，這兩者卻反而是增加的。 
3. 奈米鑽石調質潤滑油對於鋁合金因為
進行類示拋光的磨耗機制，使得摩擦
係數及磨耗量都同時增加；然而，對
於碳鋼材質的試片雖達到抗磨減摩的
作用，卻也同時增加了表面粗糙度。
其中又以含碳量高的 AISI1045 具有較
好的改善效果，這種因含碳量所改變
的因素，有待將來進一步的探討。

RHEOLOGICAL BEHAVIOR AND TRIBOLOGICAL PERFORMANCE OF A 
NANODIAMOND-DISPERSED LUBRICANT  
 
C.C. Chou and S.H. Li 
 
 
Department of Mechanical and Mechatronic Engineering, National Taiwan Ocean 
University, Taiwan, ROC; email: cchou@mail.ntou.edu.tw 
 
 
ABSTRACT 
 
This paper studied the rheological property of a nanodiamond-dispersed lubricant. The 
viscosity as well as the dynamic and thermal behavior of this lubricant was investigated and a 
rheological relation was obtained by statistical regression. Basing on the inspection of 
aggregation condition via particle sizing, a proper mechanical dispersing process was also 
proposed. Furthermore, the anti-friction and wear-reduction performance of this lubricant was 
explored by conducting a series of tribological tests. Carbon chromium steel ball rubbing with 
various materials such as carbon steels AISI 1045, AISI 1025, and aluminium alloy 6061 were 
implemented and compared. The self-repair ability of the lubricant was portrayed by the 
observation of the wear track and the test data of the contact condition. The different results of 
carbon steel and aluminium alloy gained an insight into the lubrication by means of 
nanodiamond suspension. 
 
KEYWORDS: nanodiamond; additive; rheology; aggregation 
 
1. INTRODUCTION 
 
Oiliness and extreme-pressure (EP) additives are utilized extensively and frequently in 
boundary and EP lubrication [1]. Oiliness additive forms an adsorption layer on metals’ surfaces 
to protect the direct contact of rubbing pairs. It fails when the temperature becomes higher and 
the load reaches an extreme status. EP additive usually begins to be active when the load and/or 
temperature becomes very high. It reacts with the metals to yield a chemical reaction film on the 
contact surfaces to resist the shear stress between them. Most of the EP additives are 
phosphorous, sulphate, and chlorine types. The effective antiwear performance makes them very 
popular in industry applications. Nevertheless, they are environmental hazard materials. There is 
a high demand to find a new type of additive which is cost-effective and environmental friendly. 
Many efforts have been made in the past few years to apply two-phase lubricants yet have rather 
contrary results [2].  Some studies [3-5] found solid lubricants (such as MoS2 or graphite) 
dispersed in oil were beneficial to reduce the wear and friction force. On the other hand, some 
drawbacks such as increase in the wear rate and friction coefficient were found [6-9]. 
Nanoparticle as a solid additive has become an innovative way to resolve the unsteady problems 
of the former microparticles. Metal nanoparticles (Cu, Fe, Ni, and Co) [10] and Si3N4 [11] as 
well as Ti3(BO3)2 [12] were all studied. Among them ultradispersed nanodiamond is the 
promising candidate to reduce the wear and friction economically and effectively. Tao [2] 
investigated the tribological properties of the diamond nanoparticles synthesized by the 
detonation of explosives with negative oxygen balance process[13]. These particles, exhibiting 
spherical or polyhedral shape [14], were dispersed into oil as additives. The mechanism of 
reduction of wear and friction was discussed. However, one key problem for the application of

3.1 Aggregation of nanodiamond-dispersed lubricant 
Using the original lubricant as a benchmark, the rheological curves as shown in Fig.2 
reveals that the nanodiamond-dispersed lubricant after six month’s settle-down duration behaved

distribution (PDD) of the settle-down sample after passing through a 0.45 mm filter appeared an 
undetectable state. All the particles in this sample were aggregated to larger ones and their 
diameters were much greater than 0.45 μm. However, when the same oil sample was treat by a 
20-minute supersonic process. The PDD as shown in Fig.3 illustrated itself smaller particle sizes. 
The mean diameter was 350 nm. It was rather large compared with the original diamond 
particles. The obviously greater viscosity obtained from the slope of this suspension in Fig.2 
notified that such a re-dispersed suspension had a better lubrication performance than the 
original lubricant even the aggregation still prevailed to some extent.

4. CONCLUSION 
 
(1) The shear stress-shear strain rate relation of nanodiamond-dispersed lubricant in this study 
can be demonstrated by Bingham Plastic, Herschel-Bulkley, and Casson- all the three 
proposed rheological models. However, the first two are more statistically significant than 
the last one. As the linear behaviour of this suspension is rather obviously, Bingham Plastic 
model is recommended for the analytic work in hydrodynamics. 
(2) Generally speaking, a well-dispersed nanodiamond suspension has a lower yield stress 
parameter which is beneficial to reduce the starting resistance of an operating machine. 
(3) Nanodiamond in a lubricant can be dispersed by the supersonic process, which promotes the 
suspension’s viscosity. On the other hand, stirring process doesn’t. From the rheological test, 
no hysteretic behaviour was found from the nanodiamond-dispersed sample. This situation 
is still valid under a higher lubricant temperature, 50℃. It is verified that changing the shear 
strain rate, i.e. applying a mechanical stirring process, cannot reduce the aggregation 
phenomena of the suspension nanoparticles. 
(4) In the tribological test, the nanodiamond particles can make the wear tracks smooth by 
polishing the contact asperities. But only for carbon steel pairs does this additive reduce the 
friction force and the wear as well. There is a significant relationship between the carbon 
content of steels and the nanodiamond particle itself. It needs an intensive investigation to 
explore this connection.
