 
以微機電製程技術研製微 X 型熱線 
“Development and Fabrication of a MEMS Based Micro X-Type Hot-Wire” 
計畫編號：NSC95-2221-E-013-001 
執行期間：95 年 8 月 1 日 至 96 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：張政仁 空軍軍官學校航空太空工程系副教授 
 
一、 中文摘要 
熱線在流場量測上是極為重要的工具，因
其具有高靈敏度及反應速度快等優點，且可量
測到流場的速度擾動、旋度、紊流強度及能譜
等，對於了解流場的特性有極大的幫助；但傳
統的熱線在空間解析度及反應頻率上對於高
雷諾數流場仍有所不及。因此，本研究計畫旨
為應用微機電系統製程技術於微 X 型熱線之
開發；利用微機電系統製程製作之微熱線，其
截面積及長度均較傳統熱線大幅的減小，其空
間解析度及反應頻率可因此大幅提昇，對於高
雷諾數流場物理機制的探討將有極大的助
益，同時由於同一批次可生產大量熱線晶片，
製造成本可因此大幅降低。 
本計畫的研究重點，主要著重於微 X 型熱
線晶片的研發，目前的研究成果中完成了微 X
型熱線晶片的開發、封裝及定電流電路製作，
並於風洞中進行初步的定性測試。 
關鍵詞：微機電系統、微 X 型熱線、雷諾數 
 
英文摘要 
Hot-wire is an important tool for flow-field 
measurement because it possesses high 
sensitivity and high frequency response.  It can 
measure the velocity fluctuation, vorticity, 
turbulence intensity, and power spectrum of the 
flow-field.  It carries out significant assistance 
for understanding the flow-field characteristics.  
However, the spatial resolution and frequency 
response of conventional hot-wire are not 
enough when it is applied to the high Reynolds 
number flow.  The purpose of this project 
develops micro X-type hot-wire using 
micro-electro-mechanical-system (MEMS) 
technology.  The cross-section and length of the 
micro hot-wire are significantly small compared 
with those of conventional hot-wire, therefore, 
the micro hot-wire has better spatial resolution 
and frequency response than the conventional 
hot-wire.  The micro hot-wire provides an 
opportunity to investigate the mechanism of the 
high Reynolds number flow.  In addition, the 
cost of micro hot-wire can be obviously reduced 
because a large number of micro hot-wire chip 
can be made in a fabricated process.   
This project focuses on the development 
and fabrication of the micro X-type hot-wire. 
The present efforts have finished the design, 
fabrication, package, and control circuit of micro 
X-type hot-wire. In addition, the preliminary 
tests of micro X-type hot-wire chip were also 
conducted in low-speed wind tunnel.  
Keywords: MEMS, micro X-type hot-wire, 
Reynolds number
