一、中文摘要 
波浪傳遞過程會對海床產生動態的壓
力，當壓力傳遞到海床的內部，會使得海
床土壤同時受到垂直應力、水平應力、孔
隙水壓及扭剪應力的影響，讓海床土壤產
生不穩定。 
因此，本研究為深入探討模擬出波浪
在真實情況下，對於海床土壤受力的情
形，將原發展三軸向動力三軸系統，加以
改裝設計結合扭剪應力之功能，本試驗系
統模擬之結果能更符合現況。 
研究主要探討在颱風碎波點之波壓作
用、季節風和颱風之波浪、平常波壓與碎
波點波浪之波壓作用等四種不同波壓條件
下，不同深度海床土壤，受到垂直應力或
扭剪應力，孔隙水壓之變化有何差異之處。 
由颱風碎波點之軸向與扭剪試驗結
果，試驗後兩者孔隙水壓之激發，軸向試
驗孔隙水壓立即激發機制產生，而扭剪試
驗之孔隙水壓激發機制則較為緩慢。本研
究所建置動力三軸結合扭力試驗系統與初
步之試驗結果，期能對於後續海床土壤受
波浪動態作用之相關研究，能有所助益。 
關鍵詞：颱風波浪、土壤動態強度、海床
穩定、評估模式、扭剪應力。 
Abstract 
When the wave progressive will be 
transmitted in the sea bed and induce the 
dynamic pressure. The wave induced 
pressure could be transmitted to the soil of 
sea bed, and will induce the vertical stress, 
horizontal stress, pore water pressure and 
influence of torsion shear stress at the same 
time. The soil of sea bed will become 
unstably under wave loading. 
In order to deep understanding and 
simulation the true behavior of sea bed soil 
under wave loading, the purpose of this 
study is try to establishment the modified 
tri-axial cyclic triaxial testing system(TCTS) 
jointed with the torsion shear stress function. 
The development of testing system could be 
simulated the results for situ wave loading 
condition. 
In this study, the four different of wave 
induced pressure such as the wave pressure 
induce by the typhoon induce wave break 
point, the wave pressure induce by the 
typhoon and season wave, season wave 
induce pressure and wave induced wave 
break point, were discussed to evaluate the 
various difference with vertical stress, 
torsional shear stress, and pore water 
pressure of the sea bed soil under the 
different depths and wave loading. 
From the results of axial and torsional 
shear tests under wave pressure induce by 
typhoon induce wave break point, it could 
be indicated that axial tests was exciting 
pore water pressure immediately tendency 
and torsional test was exciting pore water 
pressure slowly tendency. Based on this 
study establishment of triaxial cyclic testing 
system jointed with function of torsional 
shear stress and initial testing results, it 
could be provided the later reference of 
dynamic behavior of sea bed soil under 
wave loading. 
Keywords: Typhoon Waves, Dynamic 
Strength of Soil, Seabed Stability, 
Assessment Model, Torsional.Shear Stress.

4.2 土壤動態特性 
Hardin ＆ Black（1966）、Drnevich 
＆ Richart（1970）就土壤承受反覆荷重之
“應力-應變”行為提出： 
當土壤之應變為 0.001％時，大多表
現出純彈性之力學行為，而應變振幅加大
時，於加壓及解壓過程中，將使土壤殘餘
愈來愈大之永久變形。 
而應變量介於 0.01、0.2 到 0.3％之
間，土壤將表現出彈塑性之行為。其彈塑
性變形主要是因為阻滯效應所產生之能量
損失，阻滯效應一般以阻尼比表示，當土
壤承受更大應變時，土體內部結構將因破
壞而產生塑性變形。

5.6 試體準備方式 
為模擬自然海床土壤的沉積特性，避
免因不同的試體準備方式，對土壤動態強
度造成影響。 
故本研究之試體準備方式，採用「過
水霣落法」來施作。

7.4 颱風碎波之試驗分析結果  
在颱風碎波點時的波壓作用之下，其
深度 3.6m 與 10.8m 之海床土壤，在波浪
作用次數還不到一下時，試體就直接發生
破壞，其原因可能為海床土壤所能承受之
波壓已到達極限；而深度 7.2m 之試驗，
經過波浪作用 6 次後才產生破壞，可能為
試體壓密後相度密度較高、試體強度較強。

ABSTRACT 
 
In this study, the field monitoring of the pore water pressure of the seabed soil under the wave action is investigated. 
From the coastal geotechnical engineering viewpoint, the wave-induced pore water pressure of seabed under 
irregular wave action and wave height in-situ were discussed, and also comparison with the laboratory simulation 
test to understand the relationship properties. The monitoring of pore water pressure system is jointed with the 
run-up of wave monitoring pile system in Golden Coast of Tai-Nan. In order to obtain the profile of various 
pressures induced by wave loading, the monitoring depth set up from -0.5m to -3.5m in step of 1m below seabed. 
Concerning above the monitoring of wave induced the pore water pressure within the seabed, the results of 
calculation of Yamamoto wave theory and the cyclic triaxial tests in laboratory could indicate that by using of the 
wave theory formulae derived under limitless depth seabed and pore water completely saturation, to evaluate the 
seabed of dynamic pore water pressure after the wave through the break zone. It found that the dynamic pore 
pressure induced by wave loading is underestimated tendency. But the decay curve of Δufield / P compared with the 
monitoring data and theory calculation have same tendency. The ratio of Δufield / P is decreased as depth 
dimensionless of z/L increasing. The monitoring results and analysis could be provided to the reference of the 
related seabed stability and coastal prevention disaster. 
 
KEY WORDS: Field monitoring, pore water pressure, seabed soil, wave loading.

Methodology and Monitoring Results 
 
About progressive waves induced the magnitude of stress within the seabed, details 
of previous investigations of wave-seabed-structure interactions were summarized in Jeng (2003).In his paper, 
the researches about waves propagating over a porous seabed were approached 
theoretically or experimentally. The theoretical (including analytical and 
numerical) approaches are primarily developed from the general theory of 
three-dimensional consolidation proposed by Biot (1941), under different 
assumptions of the properties of waves and seabed soil, including the linearity 
of wave profiles, and the thickness, saturation, isotropy, homogeneity, porosity, 
elasticity, Young’s modulus E , shear modulus G , Poisson’s ratio ν , and 
permeability k  of seabed soil. In order to compare with theoretically model, the 
normalized stress and the normalized depth (z/L) were discussed, and the monitoring 
data which was used to analyze statistics and solution of theory to illustrate 
separately. On the other hand, the simulation test was performed on laboratory. 
The selection of analytical solutions 
Sumer and Fredsøe (2002) proposed a simplified analytical solution which the condition 
of the horizontal seabed of limitless thickness. this popular analytical solution 
of the stresses for isotropic, poro-elastic media of seabed under the assumptions 
of fully saturation, and linear wave’s actions developed by Yamamoto et al. (1978). 
Therefore, in this study, we adopted the theory solution of Yamamoto et al. (1978) 
to assess the in-situ pore water pressure induced by wave loading, although this 
method simplify comparatively, but the influence on every stress of every parameter 
is easier to observe in the formula, and state of Gold Coast of Tainan that also

The Results of Field Monitoring and Laboratory Tests 
 
Comparing with the analysis results of monitoring data, the theoretical shear strength 
of season waves and the results of static triaxial tests, it was found that laboratory 
test values was greater than theoretical values, and which was greater than monitoring 
values in-situ. The shear strength of in-situ wave was greater about averaged 2.1% 
than seabed soil. The shear strength of pore water pressure was greater about averaged 
3.7% than seabed soil. The shear strength of season waves was greater about averaged 
12.4% than seabed soil, as shown in Fig.22. The results presented the seabed soil 
at 2.5m below surface of seabed on nearshore at least wasn’t easier developed to 
shear stress failure, and it was found that the shear stress was greater than other 
depth at 1.5m. It may be possible to have fine soil contents at this depth to cause 
this phenomenon.
