本研究探討在氬氣中引入水氣，溫度 1100℃於 Si 基板上以熱碳還原 GeO2粉末生成 Ge-GeOx
奈米線的影響。引入少許水氣便有顯著催化奈米線生長的現象，在此生成的奈米線為
Vapor-Solid (VS)機制，結果顯示水氣除了扮演擔任氧化劑也同時有還原作用。其還原作用在
以熱碳還原 GeO2粉末生長 Ge-GeOx奈米線的方面扮演一重要角色，而使得 Ge-GeOx奈米線能大量
生長。另外，在鍍金和銅膜 Si基板上，水氣影響趨勢與在純 Si基板上所生長的 Ge-GeOx奈米線
奈米線相同，不同的地方在於相同條件下，有金膜催化後其奈米線變細且在同樣單位面積內的
奈米線個數變多，在此生成的核殼奈米線為 VS 與 Vapor-Liquid-Solid (VLS) 機制共存。用 VLS
機制去生長SiGeO以及Ge-SiGeO奈米線液滴的化學組成和大小對於決定其形狀及結構扮演一個
很重要的角色。
利用熱蒸鍍法，將 Ge 粉末放置在 950℃並通以載流氣體 Ar和在基板的沉積銀顆粒反應形成
筆直的GeNWS，鋸齒狀的GeNBS和六方形的GeNTS已經被研究過了，在550-600℃生長GeNWS
和 GeNBS 以 top-growth 模式，為 VSS 生長機構。在 700-750℃生長 GeNTS 以 bottom-growth 模式，
為 VLS 生長機構，由上述結果顯示利用沉積銀顆粒促進 Ge奈米結構生長會受溫度影響，在高溫
時，大尺寸 AgGe 液滴聚集會促進 GeNTS 生長以 bottom-growth 模式，另外，這篇文章會討論 Ge
奈米結構的生長機構
關鍵字：核殼奈米線、水蒸氣、金、銅及銀催化膜
Effects of moist Ar and Au film as a catalyst on enhancing the growth of Ge-Si1-xGexOy
core-shell nanowires (Ge-SiGeO NWs) and Si1-xGexOy nanowires (SiGeONWs) on Si substrates via the
carbothermal reduction of GeO2 powders at 1100˚C at a flow rate of 200 sccm were studied. The
amount of Ge-SiGeO NWs and SiGeONWs increased with the volume of water in the range of 0.5-2
ml, but decreased with more water because of the exhaustion of more graphite powders. During growth,
the water vapor simultaneously serves as a reducer and an oxidizer. Au and Cu films coated on the Si
substrates further enhanced the growth of Ge-SiGeO NWs and SiGeONWs. The growth of SiGeONWs
follows the vapor-liquid-solid (VLS) process, while that of Ge-SiGeO NWs follows both the
vapor-solid and VLS processes. For the VLS growth of SiGeONWs and Ge-SiGeO NWs, the chemical
compositions and size of the droplets seem to play an important role in determining their morphologies
and structures.
The Ag-catalyzed growth of straight Ge nanowires (GeNWs), serrated Ge nanobelts (GeNBs),
and hexagonal Ge nanotowers (GeNTs) by thermal evaporation of Ge powder at 950˚C in Arwas
studied. The growth of GeNWs and GeNBs at 550-600 C˚ followed the top-growth mode via the
vapor-solid-solid process, while that of GeNTs at 700-750 C˚followed the bottom-growth mode via the
vapor-liquid-solid process. This result shows that the growth mode of Ge nanostructures catalyzed by
Ag nanoparticles is temperature-dependent. The larger size of AgGe droplets assembled at high
temperatures is beneficial to the growth of GeNTs with the bottom-growth mode. In addition, the
growth mechanisms of Ge nanostructures are discussed.

3.水氣對純 Si基板 SiGeO 奈米線生長之影響
在純 Si 基板，水量為 0.5-2ml，氬氣流量在
200~300sccm 時，便有紡錘狀及管狀奈米線叢
聚生成於試片如圖 12、13。在奈米線頂端有顆
粒含化學元素 Si,Ge 和 O。由於 Ge 溶點約在
938℃且在 1100℃可形成 SiGe 液態合金。故富
含 Ge 的球形顆粒在 1100℃應為液態；此顯示
SiGeO奈米線的生成是藉由 SiGe液滴來催化的
VLS 機制。由於 Si 基板位於 GeO2 粉末上方，
且試片中心相較於周圍部分，其曝露於較高
GeO(g)濃度的氣氛中，此為生成於試片中心的
Ge-GeOx奈米線中只有少量 Si 成份的原因。在
SiGeO 奈米線生長期間，SiO(g)主要是藉由水氣
氧化 Si 基板而來的；在 1100℃下，較高氬氣流
量能混合 GeO(g)和 SiO(g)且傳輸它們至 Si 基板
上形成 SiGe 液滴；當 SiGeO 液滴過飽和時，
SiGeO 奈米線便開始成核和生長於液滴的下層
表面。最後將 SiGeO 液滴舉離 Si 基板。

結論
1.於 1100℃無添加金屬催化劑下，藉由熱
碳還原 GeO2 粉末，同時引進些許水氣於氬氣中
能在 Si 基板生成 Ge-GeOx核殼奈米線及 SiGeO
奈米線。奈米線的量隨水量(0.5-2ml)增加而增
加。
2.於 1100℃於 Si 基板上鍍上一層金膜當催
化劑，藉由熱碳還原 GeO2粉末，足夠的氬氣流
量(200sccm 以上)，並引進些許水氣於氬氣中，
能在鍍金 Si 基板生成 Ge-SiGeO 核殼奈米線及
SiGeO 奈米線。
3.在濕氬氣中，Si 基板上增加金膜厚度，
核殼奈米線的生成量會增加，但有變細變短的
趨勢，此為 Au-Ge 液態合金增加了奈米線成核
位置。
4.純 Si基板上Ge-GeOx奈米線的生成遵守
VS 機制，SiGeO 奈米線為 VLS 機制；但在鍍
金基板上Ge-SiGeO核殼奈米線的生成遵守VS
與 VLS 機制。
5. 溫度 1100 ℃，在鍍銅 基板上生長
Ge-GexNWs，隨著銅厚度的增加，Ge-GexNWs
生長的量有增加的趨勢，表示隨著銅膜厚度增
加，CuGeO 液滴的數目以及Ge-GeOx NWs的
成核數目也隨之增加。另外值得注意的，在不
同銅膜厚度下，Ge-GeOxNWs頂部成分的組成
原子百分比，隨著銅膜厚度增加，頂部顆粒銅
元素佔的比例會高。Ge-GexNWs 的core主要
成分Ge，shell 成分包含Ge、O。由實驗結果
得知，銅膜厚度過會變成cluster-like(叢聚)而
Ge-GexNWs會傾向聚(agglomerate)生長，因此
過厚的銅膜，對於奈米線的生長反有阻礙。

The IUMRS-ICA 2008 Conference was held from December 9 to 13 in
Nagoya, Japan. Nagoya is located on the Pacific coast in central Japan, roughly
two hours away by train from Tokyo. Nagoya's main industry is automotives.
Many Japanese automotive companies are based in it. The IUMRS-ICA
conference has been held periodically from 1993 and organized by one of the
MRS regional societies in Asia, including China-MRS, MRS-India, MRS-Japan,
MRS-Korea, MRS-Singapore, and MRS-Taiwan. This conference was organized
by MRS-Japan and attracted about 1,500 attendees from 35 countries, including
38 symposia with about 1,900 presentations. The 38 symposia mainly focus on
the science and technology of advanced materials, covering oxide composites,
green energy materials, soft materials, materials for living, carbon nanotubes,
advanced ferroic materials, thermoelectric materials, ceramic materials for
phenomenon of TiO2-coated glass for self-cleaning surfaces, TiO2-coated air
filters for air cleaning systems to deodorize and disinfect. One fascinating
application is glassed-in smoking areas in Japanese Shinkansen trains to keep
them clean. He also presented medical applications for clean and antibacterial
surfaces, and killing tumors.
Prof. Stupp gave an interesting talk “Self-assembly of Supramolecular
Materials for Applications in Regenerative Materials”. Stupp and his team are
exploring medical areas such as spinal cord and retina regeneration, heart
regeneration after infarction, neuron repair, cell therapies for diabetes, cartilage
repair, and growth and universal repair of bone fractures. Essentially,
self-assembling systems are molecularly programmed to form nanostructures and
hybrids with both soft and hard matter. The basic idea is that cylindrical
nanofibers invade extracellular space for cell signalling, targeting, and delivery.
Several studies on applications of this technology in medicine were presented by
Stupp.
