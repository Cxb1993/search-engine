一、中、英文摘要 
 高深寬比微結構目前多以 LIGA、類 LIGA、蝕刻和部分的微機械加工法製造，這
些技術可製出極其微小的外型且具高深寬比的微結構，但相對的也需較高的成本，部分
製程亦僅適於特定材料，本計畫主要目的在提出一製造高深寬比微結構的製程技術，並
探討其可行性。製程中先以一般泛用型的精密線切割放電加工或以總計畫開發之微細加
工機中微高速銑削加工、精密研磨加工等方法切出微細溝槽，再以精密蠟油充當犧牲層
填入其中。接著以精密微電鑄的技術基使界面層成長一層電鑄層，最後以熱水浸泡溶出
蠟而獲得高細長比微流道結構。實驗證實，此製程可製出槽寬 220µm 深寬比 300 之微
流道，表面粗糙度可達 Rmax 0.5µm。與 LIGA、類 LIGA、蝕刻製程相比，計畫中提出
之製程更具彈性、所需時間較短且成本低，具有可行性。另外，亦可配合需求製作出不
同斷面形狀及多層之高深寬比微結構。 
 
關鍵詞：高深寬比、線切割、微銑削、電鑄 
 
Abstract 
The micro parts with high aspect ratio are most often fabricated by LIGA, LIGA-like, 
etching, and micro machining methods at present. However these techniques are costly since 
a high initial investment is required. Besides, some processes are only applicable to certain 
specific materials. In this project, a process that can produce a micro structure with high 
aspect ratio is proposed and its feasibility is study. The micro slots are first obtained by any 
conventional machining process such as precision wire-EDM, or high speed micro milling, 
precision grinding in micro CNC machine developed in the main project. The slots are filled 
with wax, and precision electroforming is then conducted to deposit a layer of the substrate 
material on the top of the part. Finally, the wax is removed and super high aspect ratio micro 
structure is obtained. Applying the proposed technology, micro channels with the slot width 
of 220µm and aspect ratio of 300 have been fabricated successfully. The surface roughness of 
the micro channel achievable can be as small as Rmax0.5µm dependent on the machining 
method used. Comparing with LIGA, LIGA-like and etching processes, the developed 
technology is more flexible, requires less time and it is cost effective in fabricating micro 
channels. For practical application, micro channels of various cross section, or multi-layer 
micro channels can be fabricated by the proposed approach as well.  
 
Keywords：High aspect ratio, wire-EDM, micro milling, electroforming

1.2 研究目的 
    微流道的應用範圍非常廣泛，不但能提供學術上熱流場的微觀研
究，更能應用於實際的工業產品，如微型噴油器 (Miniature oil 
sprayer)[1]、微型油霧器(Miniature oil atomizer)[2]、微混合器(Miniature 
mixer)[3]、微熱管(Miniature heat pipes)[4,5]、微冷凝器(Miniature 
refrigerators)[6]及微霧化器(Micro-Droplet Spray)[7,8]等微型結構。這
些微結構廣泛被用於驅動、散熱、冷凝、分流、混流及液體保存的場
合。它們具有體積小、細長比高及反應速度快的共同特點。流道的細
長比愈高，製造愈形困難。現今，高細長比微流道多以LIGA[9]、類
LIGA[10]和蝕刻[11]加工法製成，雖然這些技術可製出極其微小的微
結構，但需搭配如陽極接合或擴散結合等製程，因此所需成本也相對
提高，部分製程亦僅適用於特定材料，且流道形狀及長度也有所限制。 
 
綜觀過去微流道製程的相關文獻中，包含著許多不同種類之製作
方法，其中主要可概分為半導體製程及機械式製程兩類。首先在半導
體製程方面，柯金輝[12]利用紫外光雷射類深刻模造技術製造微流
道，所製作的生醫微流道使用SU-8厚膜光阻，以紫外線為SU-8曝光
光源，直接使用紫外光雷射書寫機（UV Laser Writer）直接書寫微流
道的幾何形狀，取代光罩曝光造成的尺寸誤差；許綜升[13]以LIGA
製程製作熱管之微溝槽及其毛細性能分析，開發低成本、高性能之微
型熱管，以理論分析與實驗量測，分別探討各種設計參數對三角形、
梯形及矩形等三種形式微溝槽之毛細性能所造成的影響；謝建文[14]
進行新型紫外光厚膜光阻合成及類LIGA製成進行之微混合器製作研
究，適合壓鑄方式可成功製作厚度500µm以上的光阻膜，達最小線寬
15µm，深寬比10的光阻微結構，利用此厚膜光阻可製作出微流到寬
度40µm的微型混合器光阻結構，完成微混合器元件組裝與微流體分
析系統架設，最後注入不同顏色流體進行混合特性的觀察；陳泓志[15] 
研製出輻射狀微流道熱管，運用雙面曝光技術並配合KOH非等向性
濕蝕刻於40wt%、1.8µm/min條件下，於4吋之P-type晶片上製作5×5平
方公分之平板輻射狀微流道熱管陣列，晶片經光罩圖形轉移與流道蝕

本研究之目的為發展一簡易可靠且成本低廉之微流道加工技

1.3 研究方法 
本研究使用線切割放電加工，配合電鍍成型技術來完成微流道的
製作，其製程流程如圖1-1所示。線切割放電加工的特性為最小加工
尺寸可控制在2µm內，故可先利用線切割放電加工法加工出非常微細
之溝槽，以隔離層填滿溝槽後，再利用電鑄方式往上電鑄出一層金屬
材將溝槽封閉；電鑄成型後再將先前填充之隔離層去除，藉以完成空
心的微流道結構。因本製程使用電鑄法來生成材料，故可在完成之電
鍍層上接續再製作另一層微流道結構，所以可在一塊基材上做出相當
多層之微流道結構，為本製程之最大優點。此外本製程可製作出高深
寬比的微小通道及緊密的通道排列，藉以達到提高熱傳導面積密度之
要求。 
 
在本製程中隔離層選用主要為不導電材料、例如光阻、防鍍漆或
蠟等…。將隔離層填滿於溝槽，電鑄完成後再將隔離層去除，成為空
心結構。與光阻塗料法相比，不止可降低成本，更大大降低了有機溶
液污染的風險；另一方面，本製程並不會受限於矽材料，所以並不會
有以上矽材料所產生的缺點和極限。此製程將可改善舊有微流道系統
之效率，使得效能較以往更佳，並克服使用半導體製程所產生的一些
限制。此外，利用本製程所製作出之緊密微流道結構，將可成為效率
良好之微小散熱機構，預計能有效改善現今電子產品微小化而產生內
部散熱不易和排熱困難之問題。

4、 鑄液的組成：鑄浴的組成包括總電解質、添加劑及錯合劑等。通
常電解質濃度較低時比較容易得到細顆粒且緊密的鍍層，如此可
得到較高的電鍍均厚能力。 
5、 攪拌：鍍浴經適當攪拌後可使鍍液中離子濃度更均勻，則在陰極
上沉積金屬就易均勻而提高電鍍均厚能力，尤其是當電流密度高
時，攪拌可即時補充陰極處因電沉積所減少之金屬離子，而減少
氫氣之生成。 
 
6、 電極效率：電鍍時所通過的電流，有時在陰極上未必完全作用於
析出金屬，其中有一部分會在陰極上放出氫氣或其他反應；而在
陽極上大部分用於溶解金屬以補充在陰極上所減少的金屬離子，
一部分則造成陽極上形成氧化物或不溶性鹽類。故電極效率往往
小於 100% 。電極效率小則不易得到良好的電鍍均厚能力。而提
高電極效率的一般方法是增加電鍍溫度、增加金屬離子含量(例如
加入該金屬離子的可溶性鹽類)、加予攪拌、或降低電流密度。 
 
7、 金屬離子濃度：鑄液中的金屬離子濃度可分成兩方面來討論，總
離子濃度和各金屬離子比例。當總金屬離子濃度太低時，在高電
流密度區易燒焦；若金屬離子濃度過高時，鑄液過於濃稠，氣泡
不容易移除造成鑄層表面出現針孔，此外電鑄成本也會過高。

4.2 微溝槽塗佈填充物 
 
(1) 使用防鍍劑 
防鍍劑不易填滿微溝槽，防鍍劑中因含有水分在蒸發後產生收
縮，因此微溝槽在經過防鍍劑塗佈後依舊會有無法填平的情況產生。
24 
 (4)使用低熔點蠟（熔點約為 60℃～80℃） 
  蠟本身不溶於水，故沒有水份蒸發收縮的問題，而且流道中的
空氣會浮到蠟的表面，不會造成坑洞，且塗佈過程容易。經研磨
後表面平整，獲得良好的表面性質，放置於電鑄液中也不會融化

4.5 電鑄 
 
(1) 電鑄成分說明 
 
鹽化鎳：促進陽極溶解並增加鍍液導電度，濃度低則鍍液導電度低
且電流效率低。量太多則鍍膜柔軟度下降。 
硼  酸：為鍍液 PH 值之緩衝劑，另可保持鍍膜密著性及延展性。 
添加劑：添加應力消除劑可減少鍍膜內應力，添加潤濕劑、針孔抑

Abstract 
A pulse type discriminating system to monitor the process of Micro-EDM is developed and implemented. The 
pulses from a RC type power source are classified into normal, arc, transient short circuit, and complex types 
based on their extracted features. An approach to discriminate the pulse type according to three durations 
measured at three pre-determined voltage levels of a pulse is proposed. The developed system is verified by 
using simulated signals. Discrimination of the pulse trains in actual machining process shows that the pulses 
are mainly normal type for micro wire-EDM and micro-EDM milling. The pulse type distribution varies during 
the micro-EDM drilling process. The percentage of complex pulse increases monotonically with the drilling 
depth. It starts to drop when the gap condition is deteriorated. Accordingly, an on-line micro-EDM drilling 
process monitoring strategy is proposed.

1 INTRODUCTION 
The nowadays tendency of smaller and more complicated 
products has made the process to fabricate miniature 
parts more and more important. The micro Electrical 
Discharge Machining (micro-EDM) has long been applied 
for making micro/miniature parts due to its material 
removal mechanism and high precision attainable. At 
present, RC pulse generator is generally applied as the 
power source of micro-EDM. It provides concentrated 
discharge energy and results in a low material removal 
rate suitable for precise machining. But the charging 
characteristic of the capacitance plus no effective servo 
feed control of the process lead to very low machining 
efficiency. As the sparking takes place in a very narrow 
gap between the electrode and workpiece, it is 
conceivable that the machining process is closely related 
to the gap condition. Hence identification of the gap 
condition is essential for the design of a servo feed 
control strategy.   
The “gap condition” is a vaguely defined term. It is 
generally accepted that the information within the gap is 
contained in the pulse trains of the process. The average 
gap voltage which takes the average effect of pulse trains 
has been applied most often to reveal gap condition for 
the servo feed control purpose. The average gap voltage 
is easy to obtain in practice. On the other hand, it 
overlooks subtle but pertinent information of the process 
as well. For this reason there had been many studies of 
pulse trains based on pulse type discrimination in the 
past. The pulse types were classified into open circuit, 
normal discharge, arc, and short circuit by CIRP scientific 
technical committee [1]. The effects of these pulse types 
on metal removal rate and relative wear ratio were 
investigated by Coğun [2]. More detailed classification of 
pulse types in die sinking EDM had been studied by 
Snoeys and Cornelissen [3] based on two voltage 
threshold values obtained through many investigations. 
Coğun [4] found that the probability distribution of ignition 
delay time of the pulse trains was closely related to metal 
removal rate, and a regression model between metal 
removal rate and segmented ignition delay time 
probability distribution was established. By comparing the 
open circuit voltage, discharge voltage and current with 
pre-determined corresponding values, Rajurkar and 
Wang [5] proposed that the pulse could be discriminated 
into four types: normal spark, transient unstable arc, 
harmful arc, and short circuit. Furthermore, a system to 
measure the ignition delay time of high frequency 
discharge and discriminate pulse type of wire electrical 
discharge machining (Wire-EDM) was developed by Liao 
and Woo [6]. Based on this system the characteristics of 
pulse trains in the Wire-EDM process under various 
machining conditions were studied. 
All the above mentioned works are for the pulse from a 
transistorized power source. They are not applicable for 
the discrimination of pulse trains from a RC type power 
supply commonly employed in micro-EDM and micro 
wire-EDM. It is also noted that the average gap voltage is 
taken for servo feed control of micro-EDM and micro wire-
EDM at present [7]. Hence it is essential to develop a 
pulse discriminating system for better understanding of 
the micro-EDM related process and for the design of a 
more appropriate servo feed control strategy.
