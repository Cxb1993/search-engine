壹、 前言 
互補式場效應金氧半導體電晶體與記憶體元件在目前各項電子產業應用中
擁有相當重要之地位。隨著電子元件輕、薄、短、高密度以及可攜式與低耗電量
的要求下，下世代的記憶體元件的研究與開發，是相當重要的課題。目前主要的
研發對象為奈米微晶，磁性隨機讀取記憶體、相變化記憶體、或電阻式記憶體。
其中，內嵌於絕緣層之奈米微晶在下世代的記憶體的應用受到廣泛的注意。在本
研究計畫中，我們預計利用一個與目前先進半導體製程相容的方法來成長高密度
的自組式磊晶矽鍺量子點技術，其在後續熱處理所形成之鍺奈米微晶，並研究其
在互補式金氧場效應電晶體與記憶體元件中的各項應用可行性研究。主要利用矽
鍺磊晶設備來成長高密度小尺寸的自組式鍺奈米點與適當厚度的矽保護層於矽
基板上，配合適當的臨場退火，以形成調變矽鍺量子點。利用原子力顯微鏡觀察
自組式鍺奈米點與適當厚度的矽保護層之形貌變化，使用此調變量子點來成為虛
擬基材之初始基材。同時，利用接續的快速退火氧化製程將矽保護層氧化成二氧
化矽，同時控制其下方之穿遂氧化層與利用鍺沉澱法形成適當的矽鍺奈米微晶。
接續精準控制退火條件與氣氛以形成尺寸小，密度高，且不連續的鍺奈米微晶。
利用上述製程所形成的鍺奈米微晶與下方穿遂氧化層為基材，製作適當的金氧半
導體電容元件，以研究此一結構的電容與電壓，並著重其在記憶體元件應用上的
可行性。同時嘗試尋找其他非揮發性記憶體的可能候選者。 
貳、 研究目的 
用自組式磊晶矽鍺量子點技術，同時配合矽之摻雜以及臨場熱處理與成長後
退火氣氛所形成的調變矽鍺與鍺沉澱與還原析出，藉此進行研究其成為虛擬基材
之底面應力源，可成長有用的虛擬基材，同時以鍺量子島為電荷捕捉與釋放中
心，研究其所製作之金氧半導體的電容特性，以探究其在記憶體應用之可行性。
此外並尋找其他非揮發性記憶體的可行技術。 
參、 文獻探討 
對於使用鬆弛矽鍺層作為底部應力源主要有下列幾項技術。有研究群使用低
溫成長的矽或矽鍺來當成緩衝層[1]。另外有人成長矽鍺或鍺薄膜在絕緣層上的
矽，利用上下兩層膜的交互擴散與二氧化矽與矽薄膜間介面不匹配的關係[2]。
此一做法亦可改善表面形貌和降低穿遂差排的密度與使用較薄矽鍺薄膜厚度即
可達成相當的鬆弛程度。近來有些研究群利用氣態離子佈值的方法來製作鬆弛矽
鍺薄膜[3,4]，他們使用氣態原子例如氫和氦離子佈植到應變矽鍺/矽基材的下
方，此一結構經過熱處理之後氫和氦離子會形成所謂的孔穴，此孔穴會釋放錯位
差排來促進矽鍺薄膜的鬆弛度。藉此改善表面形貌和矽鍺薄膜厚度。我們的研究
團隊也嘗試使用將矽鍺薄膜成長在漸變成份量子井，矽/矽鍺量子井，矽碳/矽鍺
量子井與矽鍺量子點[5,6]。上均可在完全鬆弛的矽鍺薄膜上有效改善表面形貌
和矽鍺薄膜厚度。但成長於矽鍺量子點，由於應力集中效應較大，易於在後續矽
鍺磊晶薄膜中，造成過多之穿遂差排[6]。因此，改善量子點的生成與分佈，對
於形成高品質，低穿隧差排密度之鬆弛矽鍺磊晶薄膜是相當重要的。

伍、 結果與討論 
在圖 1(a)-(d) 所顯示的是使用超高氣相化學沉積系統所成長的鍺自組式量
子點磊晶的一系列厡子力顯微鏡之形貌圖。鍺自組式量子點的厚度大約為 13.1
等效厚度。 在沒有任何矽之覆蓋層加入之前，其主要量子點為 75%的圓頂型，
25%的金字塔形貌的量子點。然而加入 14 等效厚度矽之覆蓋層後，其形貌轉變成
85%金字塔形貌的量子點，其主要原因為矽之覆蓋層會與鍺自組式量子點交互擴
散，維持金字塔形貌的量子點以得到最低的表面能量態。持續增加上層矽之覆蓋
層厚度(例如 20 nm 厚的矽之覆蓋層，圖 2)會維持金字塔形貌的矽鍺量子點形貌。
然而加入僅加入 28 等效厚度矽之覆蓋層，同時加上 3 分鐘的臨場熱處理後，因
為矽與鍺原子的晶格常數相差 4%，與矽覆蓋層厚度較薄，過多的之矽覆蓋層會
使下層鍺自組式鍺量子點藉由交互擴散作用的產生鬆弛效應；藉此效果，矽鍺量
子點形貌轉變成類似火山形狀的形貌。從上述實驗結果可以得知，加入適量的矽
於鍺量子點之上，可以有效混合鍺自組式量子點以降低其應力狀態與尺寸。此
外，在足夠的臨場退火時間下(10 分鐘)，其矽鍺量子點形貌表面轉變成平坦的
形貌，其平坦度為 0.3 奈米(圖 1d)，此平坦度與矽基板相距不遠。因為此矽鍺所
產生的鬆弛效應所形成的薄膜，可以成為有效的矽鍺奈米微粒與底層應力源的基
板，此結合鍺自組式量子點磊晶與 28 等效厚度矽之覆蓋層加上臨場熱處理所形
成稱為調整矽鍺量子點。圖 3(a)為 13.1 等效厚度鍺加上 24 奈米的矽之覆蓋層
之厡子力顯微鏡之形貌圖。其形貌為有許多 mound 形狀，此形狀會導致後續矽
鍺磊晶的產生過多的穿遂差排。因此，調整矽鍺量子點加上 20 奈米的矽之覆蓋
層的表面形貌如圖 3(b)所示，其粗糙度可以有效的降低 (比較圖 3(a)與圖
3(b)，此兩個試片之矽與鍺的量為相同)。由此可以證明調整矽鍺量子點可以有
效改善其表面粗糙度；因此，我們成長 10 層的調整矽鍺量子點加上 20 奈米的矽
中介層，其表面形貌如圖 3(c)所示(粗糙度為 0.74 奈米)。然而 20 層的調整矽鍺
量子點加上 20 奈米的矽中介層其粗糙度增加為 1.5 奈米。而在多重的調整矽鍺
量子點/矽介層結構中，調整矽鍺量子點的密度隨層數增加而下降。我們使用前
述的多重的調整矽鍺量子點/矽介層結構成為底部應力鬆弛來源。在其上成長一
層不同厚度矽鍺薄膜（鍺濃度為 20%）。其上矽鍺薄膜的鬆弛程度與多重的調整
矽鍺量子點/矽介層結構的層數與其厚度有關。在厚度為 500 奈米的均勻矽鍺薄
膜在 10 層的矽鍺量子點/矽介層結構之上，其鬆弛程度為 89%。與傳統的成分漸
變鬆弛矽鍺層相比較，厚度可以降低 75%。

EXPERIMENT 
 
The test cells consisting of Pt/HfOx/TiN/Si or Pt/Al2O3/HfOx/TiN/Si metal-insulator-metal 
capacitor (MIMCAPs) were fabricated in this work. A TiN film was chosen as the bottom 
electrode, and it covered the 8-inch Si wafer by a sputtering system. a nonstoichiometric HfOx 
with different thickness (15~30 nm) was deposited on the prior substrate by ALD system (ASM, 
polygon 8200) at the temperature of 300 °C. Hafnium tetrachloride (HfCl4) and water (H2O) 
were used as reactants for HfOx deposition. Some of HfOx films with the same growth 
parameters were grown on the Si with capped with a 3-nm-thick chemical oxide. Surface 
morphology of HfOx on two types of substrate were examined by Sekio SPI atomic force 
microscopy (AFM) in tapping mode, and the composition depth profile of HfOx was examined 
by X-ray photon-electron spectroscopy (XPS). Grazing incident angle X-ray diffraction 
(GIAXRD) was adopted to characterize the crystal structure of the HfOx films. Some of 
HfOx/TiN/Si samples were deposited with an Al2O3 film of 1-nm by use of the ALCVD system. 
To finish the RRAM device, Pt top electrode was deposited by sputtering system through a 
shadow mask at room temperature. Top electrode Pt was 60 nm thick and has circle patterns with 
radius from 100 to 800 µm. Microstructure of Pt/HfOx/TiN/Si was characterized by the cross-
sectional transmission electron microscopy (XTEM) operated at 200 keV (Philip F20). Electrical 
properties of memory device were measured by HP4284 LCR meter and HP4156 semiconductor 
parameter analyzer. During measurement, the voltage is applied to Pt top electrode with TiN as 
grounded electrode. For the as-fabricated device, the dielectric constant and the current density is 
~21 and ~ 1×10-8 A/cm2 at 1V, respectively. 
 
DISCUSSION  
 
Materials properties of HfOx on TiN or Si 
  Figure 1 shows the AFM scanned image of 20-nm-thick HfOx film on TiN/Si. The root 
mean square surface roughness with the scanning area of 1 × 1 µm2 for is around 1.93 nm, 
which is higher than that of HfOx on Si with the same thickness of HfOx. The surface roughness 
in HfOx on TiN/Si increases as the increase of the thickness in HfOx. The surface morphology 
of HfOx appears the characteristics of polycrystalline, as also confirmed by GIAXRD analysis, 
as shown in Fig. 2. The diffraction pattern indicates that the HfOx has the mixture crystal 
structure of monoclinic and tetragonal phase. A 20-nm-thick HfOx by ALCVD on SiO2/Si 
samples show a small fraction of tetragonal phase with plane {111} in the matrix of monoclinic 
phase. This result is consisted with the report of Triyoso et al. [9]. The HfOx volume ratio with 
plane {200} over the whole HfOx film on TiN/Si is higher than that of on SiO2/Si.

Device performance of HfOx based MIMCAPs 
  The soft-breakdown like voltage (forming voltage) of the HfOx based devices show 
linear dependence with their thickness. However, the MIMCAP with a HfOx of 15 nm after 
forming process has the characteristic of the ratio of high resistive state (HRS) to low resistive 
state (LRS). Fig. 5(a) shows the typical resistive switching I-V curve for one cycle of the device 
with HfOx of 30 nm after the electrical formation process. In this figure, the set process, which is 
clamped at some current compliance (100 µA), changes the resistive state in device from HRS to 
LRS, while the reset process transforms the state from LRS to HRS. The maximum operation 
current of the HfOx films based ReRAM device is around 100 µA, and the operation power is 
less than 0.4 mW. The reset currents seem insensitivity with the thickness of HfOx in the range 
of 20 to 30 nm.

TiO2. The devices of Pt/Al2O3/HfOx/TiN in this work show the similar results. 
 
CONCLUSIONS 
 
We have successfully prepared non-stoichiometric HfOx films by ALD method. An 
obviously difference in crystal structure of HfOx on TiN or Si was found. High ratio of HRS to 
LRS state and 1 V of operation windows between VSET and VRESET of Pt/HfOx/TiN capacitor 
with repetitively switching cycles was demonstrated. An Al2O3 thin film on the edge of anodic 
side results to the unstable set and reset voltage in the Pt/Al2O3/HfOx/TiN MIMCAPs.
