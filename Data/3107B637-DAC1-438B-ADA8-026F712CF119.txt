二、研究目的及文獻探討
由於服務業的蓬勃發展及全球性競
爭，提昇服務品質已成為管理上重要的課
題。 而資訊科技(Information Technology)
的快速發展與服務效率等的種種考量，使
得服務作業流程之電子化已一直受到服務
業所廣泛接受及應用(Nath, 1992; Bitner et
al., 2000)。 而其中的顧客服務(customer
service)作業更是透過資訊科技使與顧客
接觸的服務人員可以直接透過電腦(即客
服支援系統)執行資訊搜尋及顧客資料建
立與查詢等服務作業。此服務人員及其所
使用的客服支援系統 (customer support
systems) 所 組 成 的 人 機 系 統
(human-computer system)的整合表現績效
對於顧客服務品質有著決定性的影響
(Chen, 2000)。 而從人因工程的觀點，人
機系統的績效往往取決於其人機介面的適
用性(usability)(Konradt et al., 1999)。
事實上，關於客服作業 (customer
service operations)的研究，人因工程的分
析時有所見(例如 Hoffman, 1994; Drury &
Drury-Barnes, 1999; Ferreira & Saldiva,
2002 等) ，就其服務支援之相關資訊系統
的適用性的評估分析也已被提出 (例如
Pose et al., 1996; Terazzi et al., 1998 等)；而
客服作業之服務品質分析並不虞匱乏(例
如 Soteriou & Chase, 1998; Lee & Hing,
1995; Negash et al., 2003 等)；甚至也已經
開始有相關研究針對客服作業進行人因工
程分析及服務品質關聯的探討 (例如
Martinez de la Teja, 2001; Narasimhan et al.,
2003 等)。 然而這些研究大都只側重於適
用性分析或服務品質分析之中單一方面的
探討；就連如上述嘗試橋接人因與客服品
質關聯的研究在人因工程的分析方面也只
針對工作環境或心智負荷的層面作連結。
而對於此客服人機系統所提供的服務品質
(service quality)與其服務作業最主要的設
備工具－支援資訊系統－之人機介面適用
性(usability)之間的關聯卻未見受到討論
及重視。所以對二者的同步探討應可來了
解人因工程與服務品質之間的關聯，不但
能建立人因工程研究在客服支援資訊系統
改善對於服務品質的意義，也可以提供人
因工程在客服作業之服務品質改善上具體
的因果關聯及建議。
服務品質的評估工具及相關理論，最
廣 為 產 官 學 界 所 應 用 的 首 推
Parasuraman、Zeithaml 及 Berry 所提出的
SERVQUAL 及 GAP 模型(Parasuraman et
al.,1985; 1989)。 而對於所訪查的對象，
大多數的服務品質相關研究皆以一般顧客
為主。以顧客為服務品質的訪查對象是最
能契合以顧客導向(customer oriented)的當
代品管概念。但實際上也有研究將服務品
質研究的對象指向服務人員。 例如以
Bitner 及 Booms 為首的一系列服務品質研
究(Bitner et al., 1985; 1990; 1994 等)以對
於服務人員及顧客雙方面的訪談作為其分
析的基礎，以求取較全面完整的對照及連
結。 Chen(2000; 2002)所作的客服品質研
究也以客服人員及顧客雙方面為訪談調查
的對象。以服務人員為服務品質評量的對
象之優勢在於服務人員相對於顧客對於服
務過程的認知更具專業性也更有經驗，其

四、結論與建議
綜觀兩階段資料，本研究分析結果建
議如下: 1)就服務品質評估而言，客服人員
之內部評估明顯較顧客之外部評估為保
守；2)關懷性服務品質是各服務品質向度
之中唯一普遍顧客評估低於客服人員評估
的向度，應是客服品質改善的首要方向。
從內部支援系統人機介面適用性而言應以
畫面設計為主要改善切入點，而改善客服
人員態度及專業能力則是外部品質呈現所
應強調的重點；3)反應性服務品質的內部
支援系統適用性改善可由所有適用性向度
達成，而外部品質表現仍應稍重客服人員
的表現，尤其是服務態度及專業性方面，
但同時服務人數及系統速度方面也不容忽
視；4)可靠性品質的內部支援系統適用性
改善可由系統用語及回饋、學習、系統能
力等適用性向度切入，而外部品質表現則
應系統及客服人員並重，尤以人員服務態
度、專業性、與系統速度方面為改善要點；
5)保證性品質方面並無強烈相關內部系統
適用性要項，但外部服務表現仍應稍重客
服人員的表現，可由服務態度及專業性方
面努力。

Therefore, in order to discover potential opportunities for ergonomic intervention
in customer service quality improvement, the primary purpose of this research is to
establish the mapping between the usability of the support computer systems and the
consequent service quality attribute for customer services, as shown in Figure 1. It is
also aimed to discover those HCI issues of the computer interfaces for supporting
customer service operations that have significant quality impacts.
Method
As shown in Fig.2, there are two phases in this research. Phase I investigates both the
usability evaluation and the service quality evaluation from the service personnel’s
perspective along with mapping analysis. Customer service representatives from four
different companies (i.e., two banks and two cell phone service companies) in Taiwan
were surveyed. Each participant was asked to evaluate the usability of the computer
system used while interacting with customers and to also rate the quality of the
service delivered through questionnaires. The usability questionnaire is established
based on QUIS (Chin et al., 1988) with the Overall dimension and four major
dimensions as Screen Design, Terminology & System Information, Learning, and
System Capability. The service quality questionnaire is modified from the well-
known SERVQUAL (Parasuraman et al., 1988) with four major dimensions of
Reliability, Responsiveness, Assurance, and Empathy. The results from the two
surveys were then mapped primarily by the correlation analysis for every dimension.

Results and Conclusions
First phase study focuses on the judgments from the customer service representatives’
perspective. The analysis on the service quality ratings shows that the average ratings
on all quality dimensions for all four surveyed companies are positive, which are
ranged from 3.46 to 3.96 in a 1(worst) to 5(best) scale. Further analysis reveals that
there is no statistically significant difference among quality dimensions across either
four individual surveyed companies or the paired industry types. Therefore, the
evaluations on service quality in general are acceptable and no particular dimension
as the immediate candidates for quality improvement can be concluded. For the
analysis on the usability ratings, similar results were found. The average on every
usability dimension for every surveyed company is positive (ranged from 5.30 to 6.88
in the 1 to 9 scale) and no significant differences were concluded among usability
categories.
These rating data of service quality and system usability were further mapped and
correlation analyses were performed to uncover the interrelationships. The
summarized results of these correlation analyses are shown in Fig. 3.

Uses for Internet Shopping
An-Che Chen, Lung-Jung Tsai
Mingchi University of Technology, 84 Gungjuan Road, Taishan, Taipei 24301, TAIWAN
anche@mail.mit.edu.tw
Abstract. Internet shopping has not only become one of the crucial components
in modern business but also been a major activity in consumers’everyday life.
Previous research has already engaged profound studies and discussion in
internet shopping, especially in the user interface issues. The literature in HCI
usability and e-commerce service quality tend to suggest the critical role of
procedural information in the website uses for internet shopping. Therefore, an
in-depth investigation on the influence of procedural information to user
behavior and perception may provide useful guidance for the user interface
design in internet shopping. This research first investigates the common
patterns of user operating procedures for internet shopping. A two-stage
experiment with interviews are then conducted to understand the influence of
the procedural information to the operating performance, the quality evaluation
of the website, and the critical information demands by the users. Some of
major findings are: 1) operation times do not significantly correlate with quality
ratings; 2) inexperienced internet shoppers have greater tendency in utilizing
and depending on the procedural information provided to assist them to
complete the shopping missions designated, compared to the experienced ones;
3) for resolving disputable situations such as not receiving merchandise on
time, most of the subjects choose to contact the customer service personnel
directly, rather than going through automated service alternatives, to obtain the
necessary information regarding the resolving process.
