1. Introduction 
Flow metering devices are one of the most important apparatus to measure and control fluid flows in pipelines. 
In places, such as chemical/petroleum plants, residential/municipal facilities and in many other places and 
applications; the flow metering devices are of extreme importance. As an example, water loss is an extremely 
important issue for human beings. The control of water loss has been being an activity associated with water 
distribution since the systems were built [1]. Since Roman times many advances have been made to control 
water loss, but, still, in the latest distribution systems, leakage occurs. Today leakage engineers require a 
variety of equipment and techniques to measure, control and reduce leakage on water supply networks [2]. In 
average, in the water distribution process, 20% water is loss. In developing countries, water loss stands over 
50%. 
 
 In recent years, the monitoring systems have advanced to digital era with the development of science and 
technology. However, they require electricity to perform their function. In many areas, it is hard to accomplish 
the measuring task because is difficult to provide with a reliable, if any available, power supply to flow 
metering systems (Figure 1). The use of solar panels to power a regular flow meter is inefficient due to the 
lack of security, if any, the accessibility for maintenance of the equipment, vulnerability due to natural 
exposure, the requirement of batteries make solar panels’ size inappropriate for most application. In urban 
areas, flow meters do not face the same issues as in remote areas; but still, the maintenance process is still 
hard and expensive. 
 
 
Figure 1: Pipelines in remote areas, underground at urban areas, at hazardous areas and deep waters 
 
To change the power supply of the flow metering devices, we will take a tiny portion of the dynamic energy 
of the flowing fluid in the pipe to generate energy and power the device. This idea can change the need of the 
device to have batteries or be connected to the electric grid.

5. Conclusions and Outlook 
In this study, three Pipe Flow Generators are been developed for different pipe sizes. From our research, for 
small pipes (<50mm), the best design parameters is seven semi-spherical blades with radian curvature of H/L = 
0.5. For pipes of medium size (50mm – 300mm), simulation shows that the larger attack angle, the higher the 
torque, within the range of 0° - 36°. For big pipes (>300mm), several prototypes were made and tested, with 
double propeller turbine performed the best. Moreover, rotor design seems one of the most important factors, 
mainly for power generation efficiency. 
 
At the present, new improvements are being made to the generators for medium and big pipes. New designs 
and the simulation cases are being created to test higher angle of attacks and new blade geometries. Further 
study will also include optimization of the generator stator and rotor.

技術說明
(中文) 本創作揭示於一種葉輪同軸向管流發電裝置，包括：一管體、一發電單元、一葉
輪以及一軟軸。主要是透過管體內部流體流速的指數型分佈，使其最大流體流速
都位於管體內部的中心軸線，能夠在大管徑下的管流發電裝置達到最大的轉速，
運用上述葉輪與軟軸的組合設計，將葉輪軸向平行於管流軸向，使該葉輪軸向位
於管體內部中心軸線，當管體內部流體轉動葉輪扭矩，透過軟軸傳動至發電單元，
使該發電單元獲得更高的電壓伏特和更大的電能功率。
(英文) The “Co-Axis Pipe Flow Generator” is proposed for the situation of medium or lager 
pipes, where the blade axis is parallel to the direction of the pipe flow. For transporting 
the torque to the rotor direction of the generator, a soft-shaft or a universal joint will be 
applied. Moreover, other structures of shaft and impeller are explored as well.
產業別 自來水供應業；溫泉取供業
技術/產品應用範圍
有鑑於目前現有管流發電裝置存在相關問題，進而精心鑽研及多次改良，本創作之主要
目的在於提供一種葉輪同軸向管流發電裝置，運用葉輪與軟軸的組合設計，將葉輪軸向
平行於管流軸向，當管體內部流體轉動葉輪扭矩，透過軟軸傳動至發電單元，使該發電
單元獲得更高的電壓伏特和更大的電能功率。為了實現上述之目的，本創作提供一種葉
輪同軸向管流發電裝置，包括：一管體，該管體上設置有一盤座與蓋板，其盤座與蓋板
之中央部形成一通孔，且該蓋板係與該盤座相組合，一發電單元，該發電單元係設置於
該蓋板上，一葉輪，一軟軸，該軟軸一端連接該發電單元，其另一端連接該葉輪。上述
之該軟軸更包含至少一萬向聯結器。 
可能應用之產品：各類水、空調、機電、化學、石油等管路檢測產業、光電綠色節能產
業、生醫等流量監測/檢測/管理器材產業。
