3. Results and Discussion 
Fig. 1 shows the XRD patterns of (a) GO, (b) MWPG11, (c) 41, (d) 91 and (e) 
MW-PANI.  The shift of GO (0 0 2) plane at 2θ= 9.92°, 9.52°and 8.76° (MWPG11, 
41, 91) represents the d-space increases as increasing ratio of PANI/GO reveals that 
PANI grew successful between the GO layers.  And 2θ= 20.6° and 25° are 
corresponding to PANI (0 2 0) and PANI (2 0 0) planes, indicated that no additional 
crystalline plane in composites.   
Fig. 2 shows the IR spectra of (a) GO, (b) MWPG11, (c) 41, (d) 91 and (e) 
MW-PANI.  The peaks of GO around 1631, 1731 and 1408-1089 cm-1 are attributed 
to the C=C, C=O in the COOH, and C-O in the C-OH/ C-O-C functional group, 
respectively.  It indicates several oxygen-containing groups distributing on the 
graphene.   The composites show the similar strong adsorptions as MW-PANI at 
about 1559, 1482, 1297, 1143 and 800 cm-1, assigned to the stretching vibrations of 
C-N in N-Q-N, N-B-N, B-NH-B, B-NH+=Q (Q, quinonoid ring; B, benzenoid ring) 
and C-H, respectively.   
The FE-SEM images of the composites of MWPG91 (Fig. 3c, d) and MWPG41 
(Fig. 3e, f) exhibit a similar morphology of nanofibers to the MWPANI (Fig. 4-11a,b).  
The planar morphology of GO is not observed revealing that GO was exfoliated and 
dispersed in the PANI matrix.  Different from other composites, MWPG11 (Fig. 3g, 
h) shows a planar shape as similar as GO indicating that the PANI could form the thin 
film structure and cover on the GO plane.   
Fig. 4 shows the CV curves of as-prepared electrode of (a) GO, (b-d) MWPG11, 
41, 91 and (e) MWPANI at scan rate of 2 mVs-1.  The CV curve of GO shows the 
regular form, reveals that the electrode of GO mainly rely on double-layer capacitance.   
Besides, PANI has two couples of redox peaks in CV curve, which indicated the 
transition of PANI during the CV cycling. (i.e. the leucoemeraldine – emeraldine and 
emeraldine – pernigraniline transition).  The specific capacitance at different current 
density and the ESR value were calculated from galvanostatic charge-discharge 
cycling (Fig. 5) and electrochemical impedance spectroscopy (Fig. 6, 7).  All the 
value listed in Table 1.  According to the Table 1, the composites show the fewer 
specific capacitance decreasing than pure PANI, which indicates that GO could 
enhances the electrochemical stability.  Beside, all composites exhibit a synergistic 
effect with higher capacitance than the individual component.  The MWPG41 and 
MWPG91 show the higher capacitance, 340Fg-1 and 326 Fg-1, and the ESR value of 
MWPG41 and MWPG91 are 30 and 3.39 Ω.

 二、與會心得 
會議焦點主要在有機太陽能電池(OPV)的發展，特別是商品化應用與生產，在未來
可見的幾年，將有超越非晶矽的有機太陽能電池商品化。在過去幾屆會議熱門的有
機發光二極體(OLED)技術研發，隨著韓國三星已將其應用在 Galaxy Note 手機及其
他的手持電子產品，在本屆會議已較少研發技術報告。取而代之的是 OLED 照明應
用，以及有機場效電晶體(OFET)在 RFID 與感測器應用，在輕薄短小的訴求之外，
有機材料的柔軟特性結合優異的電子與光電性能，已躍上合成金屬科技研發的主流，
大會期間所發表的成果，引來 MSNBC 在「明日平板電腦的美麗新世界」專題報導
的廣泛注意。值得注意的研發趨勢還包括：合成金屬有機材料的結構/物性/固態物
理的機制解析、與量子點/奈米材料的混成材、自旋電子特性/元件、石墨烯材料的
應用、儲能元件的應用、以及生物感測器應用等。在此學術、研發與產業交流的盛
會中，不僅汲取最新的重要科技研發進展成果，也掌握未來的發展趨勢，更能在報
告解說的互動中，有廣泛且深入的學術交流。經過此科學饗宴的洗禮，對個人的研
究視野有啟發與擴大的效應，也刺激在未來研究方向的發想，包括：(1)奈米碳元素
材料的整合研究(小分子/高分子/共軛高分子/生物高分子/奈米碳材)；(2)奈米碳材的
合成/機能化/混成化與奈米結構控制；(3)奈米碳材在能源轉化/儲存的應用，均是未
來值得投入研發能量的新興跨領域研究。

MICROWAVE-ASSISTED SYNTHESIS AND CAPACITIVE PERFORMANCE OF 
POLYANILNE/GRAPHENE OXIDE NANOCOMPOSITES 
 
C.-G. Ye, C.-S. Liao 
 
Department of Chemical Engineering and Materials Science, Yuan Ze University, Taoyuan 320, Taiwan 
csliao@saturn.yzu.edu.tw 
 
A graphene nanosheet (GNS) is a single molecular layer of sp2 carbons.  GNSs have a high 
conductivity and a high specific surface area and are therefore potentially useful as electrode 
materials of electrochemical capacitors (ECs) [1]. We successfully prepared polyanilne/graphene 
oxide (PANI/GO) nanocomposites by microwave-assisted synthesis in a short reaction time. 
 
Figure 1:  FE-SEM images of PANI/GNS nanocomposites: (a-b) MW-PANI, (c-d) MWPG91, 
(e-f) MWPG41, (g-h) MWPG11. 
The PANI/GO nanocomposites were prepared in an aqueous 
dispersion of aniline monomer and graphene oxide (GO) in the 
presence of oxidant KIO3 with a microwave irradiation power of 10 
W at 25 ºC for 10 min [2].  The PGMW91, PGMW41 and PGMW11 
were designated for mass ratio of aniline monomer to GO in 9:1, 4:1 
and 1:1, respectively.  The SEM images (Figure 1) reveal that the 
diameter of PANI nanofiber decreases with increasing content of GO 
and occurs a transformation from nanofiber to sea cucumber shape.  
But for high loading of PGMW11, the morphology of nanofiber 
disappears and becomes as a flat coverage on GO nanosheets. 
The specific capacitance of PANI/GO nanocomposites were 
calculated from charge/discharge curves using a three-electrode 
electrochemical measurement.  The capacitances for GO, PANI, 
PGMW91, PGMW41 and PGMW11 are 43, 286, 326, 340, 238 F/g at 
a current density of 0.1 A/g, respectively.  The results reveal that the 
loading content and morphology of PANI on GO nanosheets affect 
significantly on the capacitive performance.  The microwave-assisted synthesis is a fast and 
efficient procedure to modulate the morphology and thus the electrochemical capacitive 
properties of PANI/GNS anocomposites.
