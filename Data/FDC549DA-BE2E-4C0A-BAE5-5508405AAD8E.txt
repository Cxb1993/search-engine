以鐵電薄膜製作非揮發性記憶器及其元件可靠度之研究(I) 
“The fabrication and device reliability of ferroelectric memories” 
計畫編號：96-2221-E-131-017 
執行期間：96 年 8 月 1 日 至 97 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：明志科技大學材料工程系助理教授 
 
一、 中文摘要 
本計畫的目的是針對未來超大型積體電
路非揮發性記憶體中所需要的鐵電及高介電
常數材料薄膜的研究。由於鐵電材料具有高介
電常數及電滯曲線的特性，可以應用於非揮發
記憶元件。高介電常數材料主要是作鐵電薄膜
與矽接觸時的阻障層。計畫分為四部分及三種
記憶體結構：(1)探討使用高介電常數薄膜，作
為鐵電材料之阻障層。(2)製作金屬/鐵電薄膜/
金屬 (MIM)結構電容器。(3)製作金屬/鐵電薄
膜/絕緣層/半導體 (MFIS)金氧半電容器和電
晶體。(4)研究鐵電元件的可靠度問題。我們將
利用濺鍍法研製以鈦鋯酸鉛 Pb(ZrxTi1-x)O3 與
鉭酸鍶鉍 SrBi2Ta2O9 為主的鐵電材料，以及現
今熱門的高介電常數材料如 HfO2、CeO2、
ZrO2、Y2O3 等與鑭系元素如 La2O3、Dy2O3等
之氧化薄膜材料，比較其電學性質以及可靠度
方面的研究。另外，我們將嘗試使用不同方法
製備鐵電材料如凝膠法等，以及利用金屬氧化
物電極來取代傳統金屬電極的可能性。試圖尋
找各種不同結構中，資料維持 (data retention)
與可運作週期 (endurance)的能力及元件的最
佳組合。以探討各種不同的鐵電材料結構在記
憶體元件方面的應用。 
另具有多重鐵磁特性的鉍鐵氧 BFO 
(BiFeO3)，亦是我們研究的重點。因鉍鐵氧漏
電流較一般鐵電材料大，我們將探討以各種製
程條件，設法使鉍鐵氧製作之記憶體元件的特
性達到最佳值，所用製程條件包含：電漿氬氧
比、退火溫度、絕緣層厚度、絕緣層種類等等，
試圖找出特性與可靠度佳之記憶體元件。漏電
流機制亦是我們研究的重點，溫度相依的電流
行為在此計畫也有詳盡的的分析與結果。  
二、英文摘要 
The goal of this research project is to study 
the ferroelectric and high dielectric-k films for 
future nonvolatile devices. The large dielectric 
constant and hysteresis switching characteristics 
of ferroelectric materials can meet the 
requirements for nonvolatile storage devices. 
The purpose of the high-k insulator layer is to 
prevent the reaction and inter-diffusion between 
the ferroelectric layer and semiconductor 
substrate. Three nonvolatile structures which are 
metal / ferroelectric / metal (MIM) capacitors, 
metal / ferroelectric / insulator / silicon (MFIS) 
capacitors and transistors will be fabricated. The 
focus of this research can be divided by four 
parts：(1) the study of high-k binary dielectrics 
such as HfO2、CeO2、ZrO2、and Y2O3 and 
lanthanide oxides such as La2O3、Dy2O3, etc. for 
MFIS structure (2) the fabrication of MIM 
structure for the capacitor-type FRAM (3) the 
fabrication of MFIS structures including 
capacitors and transistors for the transistor-type 
FRAM (ferroelectric random access memory) (4) 
the reliability properties of these devices. We 
will use RF magnetron sputtering technique to 
deposit the ferroelectric films such as PZT 
Pb(ZrxTi1-x)O3 and SBT (SrBi2Ta2O9) and wide 
  
  
metallization annealing (PMA) was performed at 
4000C in N2 ambience for 30 seconds. The C-V 
characteristics were measured with a Agilent 
4980 LCR meter. The area of the capacitors is 
2.8x10-3 cm2. The relative dielectric constant of 
HfO2 obtained from our Al/HfO2/Si (MIS) 
capacitors is 15. Thus, the dielectric constant of 
BFO can be calculated to be about 70 and 51 at 
the Ar/O2 gas flow ratios of 10 and 5, 
respectively.

semiconductor (MFIS) capacitors with BiFeO3

(HfO2) insulator layer have been successfully 
fabricated. The size of the memory window as a 
function of argon to oxygen (Ar/O2) gas flow 
ratio was compared with different insulator 
thickness and annealing temperature. The 
memory window affected by the charge injection

on Technological Advances of Thin Films & Surface Coatings (Thin Films 2008)，由於現場講題安
