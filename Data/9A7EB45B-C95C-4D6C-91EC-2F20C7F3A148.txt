To further promote the drive current and on/off current ratio of LTPS TFTs, this 
investigation uses a new self-aligned process to form raised source/drain TFTs (Structure ?) 
without additional polysilicon deposition or selective epitaxial growth process. Then, the 
RSD-TFTs with ONO gate dielectric will be fabricated to further greatly improve the 
electrical characteristics and reliability of the proposed RSD-TFTs. 
 
This project consists of three phases. The phase 1 is to design the stacked gate dielectric 
and develop its fabrication methods, and to find the optimized processing conditions. The 
phase 2 designs the multilayer gate dielectric, fabrication technologies and the optimized 
thickness ratio of multilayer dielectric. In the phase 3, the self-aligned raised soruce/drain 
TFTs will be developed. In addition, the structures I and II gate dielectrics will be combinated 
with the RSD-TFTs to further improve the device performance and reliability.
