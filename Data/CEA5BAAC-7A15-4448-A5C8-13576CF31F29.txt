1.2 實驗動機 
為了改善聚丙烯的親水性及染色性，關於聚丙烯纖維改質的方法頗多，例如
將已紡製之纖維予以極性單體接枝、鹵素元素氧化處理，或使用極性單體如丙烯
酸（AA）或甲基丙烯酸（MAA）為單體加以熔融接枝處理。但由於聚丙烯為非
極性的脂肪族結構，當以極性單體接枝時，因其相容性差，故其接枝率低，且易
產生相分離現象，造成紡絲之困難（如易塞紡嘴等）；此外以極性單體接枝時，
其纖維雖可以陽離子染料加以染色，但其染色堅牢度甚差，僅 1～2 級左右【4~7】。 
本研究採取高分子熔融摻合法，在聚丙烯製成纖維前，先進行改質，藉由聚丙烯
接枝馬來酸酐（PPgMA）為相容劑【8~10】，使聚丙烯與聚酯熔融摻合，過程中
並添加熱塑性聚烯烴（TPO）為抗老化劑【11】。添加聚酯的目的是為了增加聚
丙烯的染色基座，期望以化學方法以改善聚丙烯之染色性。此外在摻合過程中添
加奈米二氧化矽，這些奈米顆粒將會分散在聚合物中，形成孔洞，染料分子將可
藉由這些孔洞迅速擴散進入聚丙烯內部，期望以物理方式增加摻合物之染色性，
而達到深染之效果【12, 13】。 
 
1.3 文獻回顧 
1.3.1 聚丙烯共混改性之發展概況 
聚合物共混的本意是指兩種或兩種以上的聚合物材料、無機材料以及助劑在一定
溫度下進行機械摻混，最終形成一種宏觀上均勻的新材料的過程。廣義的共混包
括物理共混、化學共混和物理/化學共混。 
聚丙烯工程材料及合金始於 20 世紀 60 年代末 70 年代初，經歷了以下幾個發展
階段：【14】 
1951 年製成了結晶聚丙烯，此後發展了 PP/PE 共混物。 
1969 年製成了 PP/EPDM 共混物。 
1972 年 PP/EPDM 部分硫化熱塑性彈性體研製成功。 
1976 年 PP/EPDM 合金用於汽車保險桿。 
1981 年 PP/EPDM 動態硫化熱塑性彈性體工業化。 
1991 反應器合金 USI Petrothene TPO’S 研製成功。 
對聚丙烯進行共混改性，可以改變聚丙烯的以下性能：物理力學、透明性、
著色性、抗靜電性及降低成本。 
目前聚丙烯的改性方法除了傳統技術外，還發展了許多新技術：【14】 
（1） 微波輻射技術：微波輻射填料，使其吸收微波升溫，熔融其周圍的聚丙烯
樹脂，並在界面層產生等離子體效應，進一步增強聚丙烯樹脂與填料的界
面相互作用。 
（2） 原位複合技術：以熱致液晶高分子增強聚丙烯製備原位複合材料，使液晶
高分子微纖在基體聚丙烯樹脂中分散、潤濕及黏合良好，從而使聚丙烯具
 4

1.3.3 高分子/二氧化矽奈米複合材料 
過去幾十年來，奈米複合材料已經被廣泛的研究，其中大部分為高分子/無
機物奈米複合材料，所使用之無機物通常為奈米黏土，主要應用在材料的阻燃性
上，而本研究主要添加二氧化矽期望能改善材料的機械性、耐熱性及染色性等。 
在 2006 年期間，Feng Yang 和 Gordon L. Nelson 研究指出利用單螺桿擠出機
製備高分子/二氧化矽複合材料時，PMMA/二氧化矽奈米複合材料的機械性質會
隨著二氧化矽添加量增加而有顯著的改善。而就 PS 和 PC 而言，改善並不顯著；
在熱性質方面，高分子/二氧化矽奈米複合材料會隨著二氧化矽添加量增加，熱
穩定性有較好的表現【24】。Y. Lin 和 M. Kontopoulou 研究發現在製備聚丙烯/二
氧化矽奈米複合材料時，添加聚丙烯接枝馬來酸酐可以使二氧化矽有較好的分散
性，當加入 TPO 時，聚丙烯/二氧化矽奈米複合材料在抗張模數、衝擊和彎曲性
質可得到較佳的補強效果，並且在低量的奈米二氧化矽下有所改善，顯示在剛性
和韌性之間有好的平衡【25】。J. Zhang 等人以改質過的黏土製備 PP/Clay 奈米複
合材料以及 PE/Clay 奈米複合材料時，隨著 Clay 的增加熱穩定性較佳；在機械
性質方面，PP 以及 PE 奈米複合材料的抗張強度都是下降的，PE 奈米複合材料
的模數比純 PE 大，而 PP 奈米複合材料則是下降的【26】。

下面將介紹物理共混改性的一些方法。 
 按照廣泛的聚合物共混概念，共混改性的基本類型可分為物理共混、化學
共混和物理⁄化學共混三大類。共混改性的方法又可按共混時，物料的狀態劃分，
分為熔融共混、溶液共混、乳液共混等。此外，近年來還有新的共混方法問世，
如釜內共混。【34】 
(1) 熔融共混：是將聚合物組分加熱到熔融狀態後進行共混，應用極為廣泛。工
業上，熔融共混採用密煉磯、開煉機、擠出機等進行的，是一種機械共混方
法。工業應用的絕大多數聚合物共混物都是採用熔融共混方法。 
(2) 溶液共混：溶液共混是將聚合物組分溶於溶劑後，進行共混。此方法簡單易
行、用量少等特點，特別適合於實驗室中進行的某些基礎研究工作。另外此
共混法也可以用於工業上一些溶液型塗料或黏合劑的製備。 
(3) 乳液共混：乳液共混是將兩種或兩種以上的聚合物乳液進行共混的方法。在
橡膠的共混改性中，可以採用兩種膠乳進行共混。如果共混的產品以乳液的
形式應用，亦可考慮乳液共混方法。 
(4) 釜內共混：又稱釜內合金化，為近年來發明的方法，是以兩種（或兩種以上）
聚合物單體同在一個聚合物中完成聚合過程，聚合的同時也完成共混。此種
方法，得到的聚合產物也是共混產物，可省去獨立的共混工藝，有其優越性，
但釜內共混對聚合體系有特殊要求，指適用於某些體系，仍不可取代熔融共
混等方法。

2.3.3 相容性的測定 
    摻合物性能之優劣，主要決定於組成份間的相容性(完全相容、部分相容或
不相容)，此處介紹的幾種測定高分子間相容性的方法，他們所能反映的相容尺
度並不具有相同水準，但各具特點。可根據研究工作的性質與要求，選用不同的
測定方法【38】。 
（1） 共同溶劑法 
將摻合物溶於共同溶劑中，若發現有相分離產生，則表示這兩種聚合物不
相容，如果成均相溶液，則代表這兩種聚合物相容。這種方法由於受到溶
液濃度和溫度的影響，因此不夠精確。 
（2） 玻璃轉移溫度法 
摻合物的玻璃轉移溫度 Tg 可已有三種情況：第一種顯示只有一個 Tg 轉
變，表示兩聚合物相容；第二種顯示出兩個 Tg 轉變，而且兩個 Tg 與原
來聚合物的 Tg 位置完全一致，表示該摻合物完全不相容；第三種顯示出
兩個 Tg 轉變，但兩者 Tg 位置已經變化，相互接近，表明該摻合物呈現
部分相容。測定 Tg 轉變有許多方法，例如：動態機械分析(DMA)、示差
掃瞄量熱分析(DSC)、介電法及熱光分析等。 
（3） 紅外線光譜法 
紅外線光譜法也可以用於共混組份的相容性研究。對於具有一定相容性的
共混體系，各組分之間彼此相互作用，會使共混物的紅外光譜譜帶與單一
 10
此無法採用一般含水率較高的纖維所用之染料和染色方式，其難染色的原因為： 
1. 主鏈為酯基結合，亦含有芳香族環，使分子排列非常緻密，染料難以滲
透。 
2. 含羧基的比例雖約與醋酸纖維相等，但因主鏈為酯基結合，故吸水性比
醋酸纖維差（醋酸纖維之公定含水率為 6.5％）。 
3. 具有較高的 Tg 點（一般約在 78℃），且不具有任何反應性基團可以和
染料結合，故直接性染料、酸性染料以及其他分子量大的水溶性染料，
難以滲透進入纖維內部，分子量較小的分散性染料則需在高溫或使
用導染劑，促使染料滲入纖維內部。 
分散性染料是分子比較小，染料結構中不具有親水性基團，染料微粒難溶於
水，呈現懸浮狀，必須藉由分散劑，使染料均勻分佈在染浴中【36】。

Introduction 
 
Polyurethane (PU) is a thermoplastic polymeric material that comprises a repeating hard 
and soft segments； therefore, it possesses good mechanical properties, such as medium 
tensile strength and high elongation. It is used widely in synthetic leather, fiber, and 
adhesive.1-2 For shape memory material, it began to develop in the 1960’s. Scientists 
have found that the shape memory alloy (SMA), like nickel-titanium alloy, will start to 
restore its former shape after the temperature moves to higher temperature. Therefore, 
tubes of different sizes of SMA metal can perfectly be jointed after being one side 
enlarged, connected and finally thermally heat treated. 3-5 
For the polymeric materials, the polyurethane like SMA alloy possesses the shape 
memory properties. This is owing to its possessing the different states prior to and post 
the vitreous state transition point. Shape memory high molecular material falls generally 
into three categories, i.e. temperature controlled shape memory, solvent shape controlled 
memory and electric field controlled shape memory. 6-8  In the article we are mainly 
concerned about the polymer material with temperature controlled memory. 
The first found temperature controlled shape memory of high polymers is polynorbornene 
(PN), which “memorizes” its shape through the entanglement and twisting of the high 
polymer molecular chains. The vitreous state of transition temperature of the 
polynorbornene is nearly in the range of 35 to 45 , and it can be used in medical ℃
application. 9-12 
Shape memory material of polyurethane consists of two states, i.e. one being frozen state 
(or fixed state) and another being reversible state (or the state that changes from soft 
phase to hard phase or vice versa). The frozen state of polyurethane is the vitreous state 
or the crystal state, and the reversible state corresponds to the transition between vitreous 
state and rubber state at Tg or the crystal molten state at Tm , respectively. 13-16  
The purpose of this article is attempts to change the soft and hard chains of polyurethane 
structures and to improve the polyurethane physical, chemical properties and its shape 
memory property. In addition to the application in biomedicine and functional material, 
shape memory material is also applied to textile industry, e.g. for the manufacture of 
brassiere, the lining or the collar of suit etc., where shape memory function is needed.

Shape Recovery Properties  
  
For a shape recovery material, it is consisted of recovery and fixed state. Among them, 
the phase transition temperature in recovery state requires to possess a higher 
temperature than the operation temperature in order to frozen the material in the 
operation environment. Moreover the characteristic temperature of fixed state requires 
to have even higher temperature than both of the transition temperature of the recovery 
state and the operation temperature to stabilize the structure in the operation condition.  
The measurement of a shape recovery property is expressed by its recovery ratio vs. its 
operation temperature. The recovery ratio is defined as the ratio of recovery angle to 
its original deformation angle under a constant increasing rate of temperature at 
3 /min. As seen in Figure 6℃ -9, except P1S and P2S in the P1 and P2 series, they 
demonstrate 100% recovery. It is explained that there are two phases in structure i.e., 
soft and hard domains in the PU. The hard segment can contribute the ratio of recovery, 
i.e., the elastic restoration force for recovery as it is deformed. The soft chain can 
provide the elongation. After the presence of benzoyl group in the main chain. The 
benzoyl structure is included in the hard segment region to make it stiffer in the 
structure. It exhibits a better shape recovery property than that of linear structure with 
1,4 BD as chain extender. Besides, the P1 series (PTMG=1000) possess shorter chain 
length in soft segment. The glass transition temperature in soft segment (Tgs) is high, 
but it requires higher temperature to start the recoverable deformation. Moreover, as 
seen in Figure 9-11, the addition of benzoyl or naphthalate structure shows a better 
shape recovery property than that in PU only extended with 1,4 BD. This is believed 
that the aromatic structure can contribute better potential for shape recovery property, 
thus it requires higher temperature to start the restoration. Besides, in the P2 series, it 
contains more amount of soft segment, the glass transition temperatures in soft and 
hard segment is lower than those in P1 series. It starts to restore at lower temperature, 
since the molecule can move at lower degree of temperature. 
In addition, from the recovery ratio-temperature curve, a tangent line can be drawn at 
point in the dominant recovery ratio region (10-20%) to intercept with x-axis to obtain 
the initial recovery temperature, T0 , and T1/2 can also be obtained at temperature of 
half recovery ratio. As seen in Table , after addition of benzoyl or naphthalate group, Ⅴ
the initial and half recovery temperature is higher than that of 1,4 BD as extender. This 
indicates that the modified PU gives stiffer molecular chain. It reinforces the elastic 
recovery force, but requires higher temperature to start the recovery. The polymer with 
benzoyl or naphthalate group shows an increment of recovery temperature as more of 
hard segment content increased. Moreover those with naphthalate group demonstrate 
even higher recovery temperature than those with benzoyl group.

本計畫之目的在於改善聚丙烯之染色性。先將不同重量百分比之聚丙烯
（Polypropylene﹐PP）和聚酯（Polyester﹐PET）以雙螺桿擠出機熔融摻合，並
添加聚丙烯接枝馬來酸酐（PP-g-MA）當相容劑，以改善 PP/PET 摻合體的界面
作用，並藉此提高 PP/PET 摻合物的相容性，而熱塑性聚烯烴類（TPO）在實驗
中為增韌劑，摻合過程中並添加奈米二氧化矽，再利用熔融紡絲方式紡製成纖
維。探討不同比例之聚丙烯、聚酯以及 SiO2 奈米複合材料對其染色性之影響，
對改質的聚丙烯進行表觀及物性的分析，包括穿透式傅立葉紅外線分析儀
（FT-IR）、掃瞄式電子顯微鏡（SEM）、熱重分析儀（TGA）、機械測試及染色動
力學等。 
   結果顯示，由 FTIR 發現，本研究中 PPgMA 會與 PET 做反應，但因只有少
量的反應，故仍視為物理摻合；由 TGA 測試中發現，PP/PET 的初始裂解溫度比
PP、PET 低，隨著 PET 含量增加，初始裂解溫度隨之增加，且 PP/PET/SiO2 摻
合物中二氧化矽添加量為 2wt%時，初始裂解溫度最高；由拉伸測試中可以得知，
PET 及二氧化矽的加入將會降低 PP/PET 及 PP/PET/SiO2 摻合物的機械性質；從
SEM 圖中可知，二氧化矽分散於 PP/PET 摻合物中；從染色動力學數據中，隨 PET
含量提高平衡吸附量，速率常數也提升。

Introduction 
 
Polyurethane (PU) is a thermoplastic polymeric material that comprises a repeating hard 
and soft segments； therefore, it possesses good mechanical properties, such as medium 
tensile strength and high elongation. It is used widely in synthetic leather, fiber, and 
adhesive.1-2 For shape memory material, it began to develop in the 1960’s. Scientists 
have found that the shape memory alloy (SMA), like nickel-titanium alloy, will start to 
restore its former shape after the temperature moves to higher temperature. Therefore, 
tubes of different sizes of SMA metal can perfectly be jointed after being one side 
enlarged, connected and finally thermally heat treated. 3-5 
For the polymeric materials, the polyurethane like SMA alloy possesses the shape 
memory properties. This is owing to its possessing the different states prior to and post 
the vitreous state transition point. Shape memory high molecular material falls generally 
into three categories, i.e. temperature controlled shape memory, solvent shape controlled 
memory and electric field controlled shape memory. 6-8  In the article we are mainly 
concerned about the polymer material with temperature controlled memory. 
The first found temperature controlled shape memory of high polymers is polynorbornene 
(PN), which “memorizes” its shape through the entanglement and twisting of the high 
polymer molecular chains. The vitreous state of transition temperature of the 
polynorbornene is nearly in the range of 35 to 45 , and it can be used in medical ℃
application. 9-12 
Shape memory material of polyurethane consists of two states, i.e. one being frozen state 
(or fixed state) and another being reversible state (or the state that changes from soft 
phase to hard phase or vice versa). The frozen state of polyurethane is the vitreous state 
or the crystal state, and the reversible state corresponds to the transition between vitreous 
state and rubber state at Tg or the crystal molten state at Tm , respectively. 13-16  
The purpose of this article is attempts to change the soft and hard chains of polyurethane 
structures and to improve the polyurethane physical, chemical properties and its shape 
memory property. In addition to the application in biomedicine and functional material, 
shape memory material is also applied to textile industry, e.g. for the manufacture of 
brassiere, the lining or the collar of suit etc., where shape memory function is needed.

Preparation of Thermoplastic Polyurethane 
 
    A two-stage melting polymerization process was employed to synthesize the thermally 
moldable polyurethane. Before the reaction, PTMG was first added into a four-necked 
reactor and heated to temperature of 105 to 110  with pressure of 3 to 4 mmHg for 1.5 ℃
to 2 h to remove moisture. Then the MDI (diisocyanate) (based on NCO/OH=1.05) was 
added to proceed the prepolymerization reaction at 90  for 1.5 to 2 h and then the chain ℃
extender of different types was added. Agitated the solution for 1 to 3 minutes and poured 
it into a preheated flat mould in a vacuum oven at 100  for 20 h. Finally the samples ℃
were removed from the vacuum oven and the reaction was completed. The reaction

Measurements 
 
Infrared spectra of PU of polyurethane with different chain extenders were obtained by 
Hitachi Model 260-50 Type (Hitachi, Tokyo, Japan) over a range of 700 to 3500 cm-1. 
Intrinsic viscosity was performed by a Connon Type viscometer (Rheotec Co., Radeburg, 
Germany) in a constant temperature water bath at 25 . And, the value of inherent ℃
viscosity (ηinh) was calculated as ηinh = (㏑(t / t0 )) / C , where t0 is the flowing time of 
pure solvent; t, the flowing time of polymer solution; and C, a constant (g dL). 
Mechanical properties was measured using an Instron Mode 1122 testing instrument 
(Instron Corporation, Canton, Massachusetts, U.S.A) at a cramp rate of 20 ㎜/min; a 
paper extension rate of 10 ㎜/min with the specimen of 50 ㎜ length, 10 ㎜ width, and 
0.1~0.2 ㎜ thickness; and a full load of 20 ㎏. 
Differential scanning calorimetry (DSC) was performed by a Du Pont 2200 (Wilmington, 
Delaware, U.S.A) at a heating rate of 20 /min under a nitrogen atmosphere to obtain the ℃
glass transition temperature (Tg) and the  
melting point, respectively.

Thermal Properties of Polyurethanes 
 
The thermal properties of all types of PU polymers, which were measured by DSC, are 
typically shown in Figure 5 and are listed in Table . In the table, it is found that the Ⅲ
glass transition temperature of P1 series (PTMG=1,000) with more hard segment 
content  
show a higher value than those of P2 series (PTMG=2,000). Furthermore the glass 
transition temperature is higher with benzoyl or naphthalate structure due to its steric 
hindrance of chain rotation. Also, although both of PBE and BES chain extenders 
contain the benzoyl structure, however the BES with the –S02– structure is considered 
to be stiffer than –C(CH3)- of BPE, thus higher Tgs resulted. Moreover the increase of 
soft segment length will decrease the steric hindrance, the separation of Tgs and Tgh is 
increased in P2 series, promote the phase separation.  
As shown in Table , in the hard segment region, P1 series show a higher glass Ⅲ
transition temperature than do P2 series. It increases with higher content of hard 
segment. In the same series, the Tgh is varied with the different contents of hard 
segment. It is increased with the increment amount of biphenyl in the extender. 
Besides since the benzoyl or naphthalate group is in the main chain, the heat-
resistance is increased. The difference of Tgh – Tgs ( T△ g) can be used as a measure 
of phase separation in PU. As seen in Table , the TⅢ gh of copolymer is increased 
with the addition of the amount of BES, BPE or ND. For the melting point, it is found 
that there is no Tmh except P1-S in P1 series. The reason is attributed to the presence 
of benzoyl or naphthalate extender which makes the steric hindrance being great. The 
structure is not enough compact to form the crystalline. Nevertheless the P1-S is easy 
to form H-bonding and folded into crystalline due to its only aliphatic structure. There 
is the same tendency in P2 series. Moreover, P1 series do not show Tms, the melting 
point in soft segment. This is due to the polyglycol in P1 series only 1,000 in 
molecular weight. It is too short to fold into crystalline, especially the chain also 
affected by the hard segment. Nevertheless the P2 series always show the crystalline 
structure of Tms. It is in the range of 14.6-24.2  and increases with increasing ℃
amount of hard segments.

In comparison of the different chemical structure of extenders to their mechanical 
properties, it is demonstrated that the BES exhibits the best strength, but the 
elongation is low. This is probably related to the polarity of extender structure. The –
S02- group in BES is a polar group, whereas the -C(CH3)- in BPE is a non-polar 
group. The interaction of –S02- with ester groups in the main chain will enhance the 
mechanical properties. The BES behaves the most strength, but BPE shows better 
elongation. The strength and elongation of ND series lie in between the two series. 
The reason is believed although the naphthalate group has the better structural stability 
than that of the benzoyl group, the strength might be increased, but the solubility is 
poor. The formation of film is considered to be difficult, thus a lower behavior in 
mechanical properties resulted.
