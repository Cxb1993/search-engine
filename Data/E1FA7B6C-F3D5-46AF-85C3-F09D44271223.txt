high efficient flexible solar cells and to increase the process 
throughput. The solution processable hole blocking layer was 
prepared with doctor blading, which we found to produce even more 
efficient OSC than spin-on process. We also studied the underlying 
mechanisms and developed high throughput R2R compatible 
substrate processing and passivating technology. 
 
With this project, we understood the carrier transport mechanism in 
ALD ZnO inverted organic solar cells, and thereby developed 
highly efficient electron-selective layer free inverted organic solar 
cells with continuous light soaking. Since the electron-selective 
layer was not required, the processing of inverted organic solar cells 
are greatly simplified. We also have 6 journal publication with this 
project and was invited to give an oral presentation in the 2o11 
FlexAliance Conference in Phoenix.

一、中文摘要： 
有機薄膜太陽電池(OSC)短中期主要針對的市場是mobile IT產品以及部分可用於外
加式建築整合太陽能發電，其主要著重點為 效率/價格、安全、應用性，壽命則約
以三至五年為目標。OSC近數年有極大進展，目前在玻璃上Konarka已有NREL認證效
率達到8.3%之lab cell成果，但大面積只有Konarka有5.2%大面積(1cm2) OSC，軟性
基板上亦僅Konarka有效率僅1.5%之Power Plastic。為達到mobile IT應用性及安全
性並降低成本，roll-to-roll(R2R)製程為OSC必然之發展趨勢，目前國際各團隊都
積極發展中。R2R製程必先確定元件結構、材料與製程，本計劃之目標即在開發合適
R2R製程之大面積OSC元件結構、製程與相關技術，以裨後續年度R2R整體製程開發。 
 
本計劃是我們基於我們所開發目前文獻上軟性基板上最高效率之反結構電池，尋找
可達到與ALD ZnO相同效果電洞阻擋效果之溶液製塗佈層，且兩者配合降低全程ALD
需求以提高throughput。溶液塗佈將使用我們基於過去研究所發現，比旋轉塗佈可
得更高效益且適合R2R製程之刮刀塗佈，並開發適合連續製程之元件結構。 
 
本計劃執行結束，我們發現ZnO反式結構有機太陽電池之電荷傳播機制，並由此開發
出藉由照光而獲得之不需電子選擇層之高效率反式有機太陽電池。由於不須製作ALD 
ZnO，因此整體製程降更形簡化。在論文發表方面，共獲得六篇期刊論文發表，並在
國際研討會中口頭論文報告一篇。

Abstract： 
   Organic solar cells (OSC’s) are promising alternatives to conventional solar cells and are 
currently aiming at applications with mobile IT products and parts of building-integrated-
photovoltaics. As a result, the market driving factors for OSC’s are power conversion 
efficiency (PCE) / cost, safety, applicability and lifetime, for which 3 to 5 years are required. 
Although Konarka has already demonstrated NREL verified 8.3% lab cell on glass, only 
Konarka has demonstrated large-area OSC (1cm2) on glass with PCE of 5.2%. As to flexible 
OSC, only Konarka can deliever 1.5% Power Plastics. Flexible OSC are imperative to 
achieve the required applicability and safety and to use cost-effective roll-to-roll (R2R) 
processing technology. However, the device structure and materials must be fixed for R2R 
process development. The goal of this project is to develop the required device structure, 
processing and substrate engineering as well as passivating technology, which are 
compatible with R2R processing.
