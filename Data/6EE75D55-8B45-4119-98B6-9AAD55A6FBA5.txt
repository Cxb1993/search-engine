Abstract 
The gray-dead buffer layer between the p-layer and i-layer would enhance the 
amorphous silicon solar cell performance. The hydrogenated amorphous silicon (a-Si:H) 
thin film solar cells with different buffer layer have been fabricated by high frequency 
(27.12MHz) plasma enhanced chemical vapor deposition (HF-PECVD). We discussed 
the three condition in the p/i interface with no buffer layer、buffer layer and gray-dead 
buffer layer. The influences of the performance of the solar cell with the different buffer 
layer are investigated. As the results, the solar cell with gray-dead buffer layer has 
higher efficiency compared the cell with no buffer layer and buffer layer. We used a 
Gray-dead buffer layer to enhance the efficiency of the solar cell. It improved the higher 
Voc and FF has usually been attributed to a reduced interface density of recombination 
near the junction. Finally, the a-Si thin film solar cells with Voc of 0.81 V, Jsc of 15.4 
mA/cm2, F.F. of 68%, and η of 8.57%. were obtained.  
 
Keywords：Thin film solar cell; PECVD; amorphous silicon; buffer layer

4. Conclusions 
The band gap and absorption coefficient of thin films solar cell with different buffer 
layer depended on the carbon concentrations. The optimized solar cell performance on 
Voc, Jsc, FF and ηwere 0.81 V, 15.46 mA/cm2, 68% and 8.57% were obtained at 
gray-dead buffer sample due to the carrier recombination was blocked in the p/i 
interfaces.

程材料研討會”、2007 年 11 月在臺灣新竹成功召開了“第三屆海峽兩岸工程材料研討會”。 為
了進一步繁榮兩岸在工程材料方面的研究、開發及應用，現定於 2008 年 9 月在南寧召開“第四
屆海峽兩岸工程材料研討會”。本屆研討會的主題為新興產業與工程材料。研討會將包括大會特
邀報告、分主題邀請報告和交流報告等。會議還將安排豐富的學術參觀、技術訪問等活動。 
參觀廈門大學，学校已与英、美、日、法、俄等国家和港澳台地区的 150 多所高校建立了校际
合作关系，与美国华盛顿大学等共建“全球十校联盟”的多边国际大学合作组织。在对台交流方
