  
摘  要 
 
電視新聞畫面上使用動態文字訊息的現象已很普遍，其中以新聞和購物頻道出現的頻率最
多。畫面之動態文字訊息以不同的速度、不同的位置與不同的字體同時呈現在畫面右方或
左方與下方。多變之訊息顯示方式都可能影響著使用者訊息讀取的理解力與視覺舒適度。
鑑於新聞節目是大多數民眾每天接收國內外時事的主要資訊來源，為提供電視資訊設計之
參考，本計劃以國內新聞節目作為研究對象。主要探討雙行動態訊息之距離、位置與速度
因子對於讀取理解績效與視覺疲勞的影響。 
研究結果顯示：(1) 動態訊息置於不同的位置對受測者的閱讀績效有顯著的差異。當動態訊
息的方向置於下方時，其閱讀績效優於左方。(2) 當動態訊息雙行並列時，則不同的距離並
不會造成受測者閱讀正確率的差異，介於 0.5cm~2cm 之距離為受測者可接受之距離。(3) 配
速的不同對於受測者閱讀績效有顯著的影響，當爬行式雙行動態訊息的配速設定在
200/85wpm、85/200wpm 和 85/85wpm 時受測者的閱讀績效優於 200/200wpm。當爬行式雙
行動態訊息的配速介於 85/200wpm (上慢下快) 和 200/200wpm 之間時，上方的訊息越慢，
則雙行訊息方向應置於下方，反之上方動態訊息越快應置於螢幕左方，則有助於提升閱讀
績效。本研究結果將可提供電視媒體動態資訊製作與相關研究之參考，以提升電視畫面上
動態訊息之視覺呈現品質與使用者的訊息讀取績效。 
關鍵詞: 雙行動態訊息、閱讀理解力 
 
Abstract 
Displaying dynamic information on television, particularly on news and shopping channels, has 
gradually developed over recent years. Instantaneous dynamic information is associated with 
various speeds, positions, and ways of display, for example, dynamic information displays on the 
right, left, or bottom of the screen. Various ways of display may influence users’ reading 
comprehension and visual comfort. Therefore, identifying the appropriate speed, position, and 
manner of display is important for information design. This study examined the factors (line 
distance, speed, and position) affecting viewers’ comprehension and visual fatigue pertaining to 
the reading of double-lined dynamic information on an LCD screen. A three-way factorial-design 
experiment (4 x 4 x 4) using different speed combinations of up-line/below-line (85/85 wpm, 
200/200 wpm, 200/85 wpm, and 85/200 wpm), positions (bottom and left side of screen), and 
distances between the up-line and below-line (0.5, 1.25, and 2 cm) as independent variables was 
conducted to test the comprehension and visual fatigue associated with reading information on a 
leading display. 
The results indicated the following: (1) normally, double-lined dynamic information positioned 
on the bottom of the TV screen was better than that on the left side of the TV screen. (2) There 
were no differences among the distances of 0.5, 1.25, and 2 cm between the up-line and 
below-line. (3) There were differences on account of the various speed combinations. The 
double-lined speed combinations with 200 (up-line) wpm/85 (below-line) wpm, 85 (up-line) 
wpm/200 (below-line) wpm, and 85 (up-line) wpm/85 (below-line) wpm were better than the 
speed combination with 200 (up-line) wpm/200 (below-line) wpm. These results could be used as 
a reference for websites and TV screen designers for identifying the appropriate levels of speed, 
position, and field length. 
Keywords: Double-lined dynamic information, reading comprehension.
II
 三、文獻探討 
網頁上常用之動態訊息呈現的方式有很多。其中以前導式(leading) (Juola, 1995;Wang and 
Chen, 2002; Wang et al., 2003; 許, 1996)、捲動式 (scrolling) (Granaas, 1984)及快速連續呈
現式 (Rapid Serial Visual Presentations, RSVPs) 最常被使用。而根據本研究觀察電視上
之動態訊息呈現的方式，亦同樣已此三種呈現方式最常被使用。在Juola (1995) 的研究
中定義所謂前導式動態訊息呈現，即一串文字資訊由右至左連續移動呈現；而快續連續
呈現式是指呈現完整或被分割之部分句子，由下往上捲動呈現；Granaas (1984) 則是將
由下往上移動之文字呈現方式稱為捲動式 (scrolling)，藉以與前導式有所區別。 
因此，吾人可得所謂快速連續呈現式和捲動式其實在動態訊息之呈現的本質是一樣的。
其中捲動式 (scrolling) (Granaas, 1984)，又分連續捲動式 (continuallyscrolling) 與出現然
後停住捲動式 (start-and-stop scrolling) (McCrickard et al., 2003) 兩種。而經本研究初步
調查結果，電視新聞畫面上最常使用的動態訊息呈現方式為爬行顯示方式 (crawling 
display) (McCrickard et al., 2003)。爬行顯示又可劃分為水平文字走向和垂直文字走向。
水平文字走向又以由右至左顯示為主  [稱為爬行顯示方式  (crawling display)] 
(McCrickard et al., 2003)；垂直文字走向則以由下往上顯示為主。因為中文與英文字母在
字型結構上有所差異 (Cai, 2001)，因此文字走向的顯示方式僅適用於華人文字，吾人將
此顯示方式稱為垂直爬行顯示 (vertical crawling display)，另一種則為水平爬行顯示 
(horizontal crawling display)。 
四、研究方法與步驟 
4.1 受測者 
本實驗之受測者最基本門鑑：母語為中文、教育程度在大學以上、矯正後視力為0.8以上
且無色盲以及在實驗前的閃光融合閾值(Critical Fusion Frequency, CFF)不得低於35HZ 
(CFF正常值範圍介於35~40HZ之間)，在滿足以上條件後方可進行本實驗。20位受測者
參與實驗，由亞洲大學研究生與大學生所組成，年齡介於20~28歲，受測者的慣用手均
為右手，矯正過後的視力在0.8以上且無色盲或其他眼疾。並要求受測者填寫一份個人基
本資料。 
4.2 實驗設計 
本實驗主要探討受測者在雙行動態訊息環境下對閱讀理解力。因子包括：動態訊息之的
方向：以垂直的方式呈現於螢幕的左邊和以水平的方式呈現置於螢幕的下方，兩個水
準。動態訊息的速度組合：85/85wpm、200/200wpm、200/85wpm和85/200wpm，四個水
準。動態訊息的距離：0.5公分、1.25公分和2公分，三個水準。實驗的測量變項有答題
正確率 (每一任務共有6個與跟該顯示的動態訊息內容的息內容相關的問題，每答對一
題則表示此配方的閱讀正確率就提升16.67%，而訊息讀取正確率越高則代表受測者對此
配方的閱讀理解績效越佳)、及視覺疲勞度CFF值之測量。為測量在爬行式雙行動態訊息
的環境下，閱讀時間的長短是否會影響到受測者的閱讀績效，因此將20位受測者分為兩
組進行實驗。第一組：10位受測者。一次性完成24個任務；CFF測量方式：實驗前與實
驗後(2次)。第二組：10位受測者。每完成6個任務則休息五分鐘；CFF測量方式：每次
執行6個任務的實驗前後各量測一次 (8次)。圖1顯示雙行動態訊息呈現的畫面。

 中心點的距離為 25 公分，螢幕與桌面呈 90 度，螢幕與受測者眼睛的距離為 3 公尺，受
測者的下巴與桌面距離為 27.5 公分，受測者的下巴被固定於支撐架上。實驗室空間的環
境週遭照度為 270lx。 
4.4.2 閃光融和閾值儀器的使用 
本研究使用 LAFAYETTE Model 12021 測量受測者實驗前與實驗後視覺疲勞值的變化情
形。首先調整光源亮度為 100%，選擇 automatic model 軟體模式，光點模式選擇為雙眼
同時活動，閃爍頻率為每秒 1Hz。閃光融和閾值的測量共分為三個階段： 
第一階段：光點由低頻率至高頻率，開始時受測者會感覺到兩個光點不停的同時閃爍，
直到光點不閃爍、穩定成兩個光點時，此閃爍至不閃爍的臨界點稱為 Fusion。 
第二階段：光點由高頻率至低頻率，開始時受測者會看到兩個實體的光點，直到光點呈
現閃爍時，此從不閃爍到閃爍的臨界點稱為 Flicker。 
第三階段：此由電腦自動完成，取第一階段之 Fusion 值與第二階段之 Flicker 值，兩數
的平均值而得閃光融和閾值 (CFF)。 
4.5 實驗流程 
受測者開始實驗任務時必須將注意力集中在螢幕中央，才點擊”play 鍵”開始任務，且要
求受測者在一個循環內閱讀雙行動態訊息，以較慢的動態訊息播完後立即點擊”STOP
鍵”結束該任務。閱讀任務結束後，請受測者回答任務內容相關問題，問題以六個多項
式呈現於書面上。限制受測者於實驗前一小時，禁止做與螢幕或使受測者產生視覺疲勞
的任何相關活動，以防止視覺疲勞度的增加而影響實驗閱讀績效。限制受測者在實驗進
行時，務必將下巴緊貼支撐架以免影響實驗的公正性。 
5. 結果 
實驗結果均利用 SPSS(Statistical Package for the Social Science)統計分析軟體分析，閱讀
績效的正確率對各因子和不同組別間進行單因子變異數分析(ANOVA)，取 p<0.05 來判
斷因子造成結果是否顯著，如果顯著則各因子間做 Duncan 多重比較檢定，探討各水準
之間的差異。而閃光融合閾值變化及受測者主觀視覺疲勞變化則進行受測者間的獨立樣
本 T 檢定分析，取 p< 0.05 來判斷因子造成結果是否顯著。 
5.1 閱讀績效 
5.1.1 方向對閱讀績效的影響 
爬行式雙行動態訊息方向置於的下方的正確率為 73.2740，比方向置於左方的 65.0109
高。由表 5-2 方向變異數分析得知，方向對受測者的閱讀績效有顯著的影響(F=16.150, 
P<0.05)，Duncan 多重檢定顯示，方向置於螢幕下方時，受測者的閱讀績效較佳。 
4
 閱讀正確率的差異。本研究在視覺疲勞所得結果發現，休息時間的長短對於受測者在閱
讀爬行式雙行動態訊息時並無顯著的視覺疲勞影響，但對受測者的閱讀正確率有顯著影
響。 
6.1 顯示方向 
本研究結果發現動態訊息置於不同的方向對受測者的閱讀績效有顯著的差異。當動態訊
息的方向置於下方時，其閱讀績效優於左方。這與 Wang(2003)對於觀測受測者在閱讀
動態訊息與學者Wang(2006)在VDT環境下動態/靜態訊息的位置是否影響觀測者的閱讀
理解力的研究結論不同。可能的原因在於，動態訊息由單行變為雙行，使受測者閱讀時
注意力分散，但當動態訊息置於下方時受測者的注意力較易集中在螢幕下方。這與為期
一個月的動態訊息調查中發現的現象相符，電視新聞節目中以水平的方式呈現在螢幕下
方的比例最高。 
6.2 雙行訊息之間的距離 
本研究結果發現不同的距離並不會造成受測者閱讀正確率的差異。可能的原因是
0.5cm~2cm 之間為受測者能接受的雙行動態訊息距離，對於注意力的影響不大。但如果
動態訊息間的距離更加緊密或是距離更遠，是否會造成受測者主意力的感擾或分散而影
響到閱讀績效則更需進一部的研究。 
6.3 配速 
本研究結果發現配速的不同對於受測者閱讀績效有顯著的影響，當爬行式雙行動態訊息
的配速設定在 200/85wpm、85/200wpm 和  85/85wpm 時受測者的閱讀績效優於
200/200wpm。這與 Wang and Kan(2003)和 Wang(2006)在研究動態訊息速度的結論稍有
不同。可能造成的原因有二：第一單行動態訊息改為雙行動態訊息，當單行時動態訊息
的速度設定為 200wpm 時，為受測者最適的單行動態訊息閱讀速度，但改為雙行時，因
兩行動態訊息皆為 200wpm，而促使受測者的閱讀速度無法負荷；第二兩行動態訊息皆
相同時，配速設定太快造成受測者無法負荷。 
6.4 視覺疲勞 
本研究結果發現不同的休息時間對受測者的視覺疲勞無顯著的影響。這與 Thackray and 
Touchstone(1985)的視覺疲勞研究結果有差異，可能造成的原因有二：第一是本研究的
實驗是一般人所能承受之較高集中注意力的視覺工作；第二是本研究的實驗時間尚未達
兩小時，因此無法造成顯著的閃光融合閾值變化。 
6.5 爬行式動態訊息設計建議 
(1) 當在爬行式雙行動態訊息的環境下，動態訊息的方向置於下方時有助於提昇使用
者的閱讀績效。 
(2) 爬行式雙行動態訊息間的距離，設定在 0.5cm~2cm 時，對觀測者的閱讀績效不會
有太大的差異。 
(3) 在爬行式雙行動態訊息的環境下，動態訊息的配速設定應將其中一行動態訊息的
速度改慢，則有助於閱讀績效的提升；如爬行式雙行動態訊息的配速 設定為相
同時，配速設定不宜太快，越低於 200/200wpm 時，則有助於提升閱讀績效。

For Chinese, beside written from left to right (shifting text form right to left across the display so 
that new information comes continuously from the right side, while older information disappears 
from the left side on screen), the Chinese characters are also written from top to bottom (shifting 
text form bottom to top across the display so that new information comes continuously from the 
bottom side, while older information disappears from the top side on screen) (Fig. 1). Generally, the 
position of Chinese lending display is placed on left, right, bottom or top of a screen (Fig. 1). 
Schaik and Ling (2001) studied the effects of frame layout and differential background contrast on 
visual search performance in Web pages. The study indicated that both accuracy and speed 
measures of the frames at the top or left of screen were better than that at bottom or right of screen.  
The other factor of speed is a key determinant in terms of reading and understanding information. 
Leading display speed is measured by word per minute (wpm). Chen and Tsoi in year of 1988 
examined the English leading-display for the users’ comprehension. Their study results indicated 
that the speed has a significant effect on comprehension in reading English leading-display 
information. Juola (1995) conducted an experiment of sentences presented on a small display using 
RSVP and leading formats. The result indicated that sentences presented with RSVP format were 
more accurate than that with leading format (Juola, 1995). The other factor affecting comprehension 
in leading displays is length of display field. In this study, the text displayed in short (6 characters), 
medium (12- and 18-character) or long field length (24 characters) with a limited area. Koler, et al. 
(1981) indicated that the display field length is a significant factor for reading comprehension with 
RSVP, the reading comprehension of field length with 12.5cm and 18.7cm are better than that with 
6.2cm. Regarding the Chinese leading display on websites or TV screen are little discussed, 
therefore this present study was hold. 
2 Aim 
In order to provide reference for websites and TV screen designers to identify appropriate levels of 
display speed, display position, and display field length. This study examined the factors affecting 
comprehension and visual fatigues of the dynamic information reading on LCD screen. 
3 Method 
3.1 Subjects 
Sixteen volunteer graduate students (8 males and 8 females) participated in the experiment. The 
first language of all participants was Chinese. The subjects aged from 23 to 28 years with a mean 
age of 26.06 years (SD=1.61 years). All of them were selected with a corrected visual acuity above 
five-point scale. 1 point represented “not at all” and 5 point represented “very much”. The four 
questions were selected from a research (Heuer et al., 1989), among them are: (1) I have difficulties in 
seeing after seeing dynamic information. (2) I have a strange feeling around the eyes. (3) My eyes 
feel tried. And (4) I feel dizzy looking at the TV screen. The fewer average points indicated a higher 
level of satisfaction. The reading comprehension and visual fatigue were recorded for the further 
analyses. 
 
3.3 Experimental materials and equipments  
The experimental task in this study was reading dynamic information on an LCD TV. A software 
program written in C language was used to manipulate the display speeds, display positions and 
display field lengths to display information. The Chinese characters were displayed in Hei font with 
24 point on a 32-inch screen (1366 x 768 pixels, width-to-height ratio of the screen is 16:9) since it 
was widely used for dynamic messages display on TV in Taiwan. The size of the LCD TV without 
mounting and frame was 76.5 (length) x 11.4 (width) x 54.8 (height) cm, and was placed on the 
centreline of the desk. The brightness was 500 cd/m2, and the contrast ratio was 1000:1. Additional, 
the distance from the tabletop to the screen centre was 30.35cm. The distance from the subjects’ 
eyes to the screen was 300cm and the viewing angle towards the screen is 14.62 degree, and the 
viewing angle of the targets (the width of characters on the screen is 2.2cm) is 0.42 degree. The 
screen was free of glare or reflection. 
3.4 Experimental procedure 
Subjects filled in a basic data form that included their name, age, and years of experience of 
watching TV. They then were asked to take visual acuity test for ensuring normal visual ability. The 
experimental purpose and procedures were then explained to the subjects. Each subject practiced 
trials until they were familiar with each task and experimental procedures. At the beginning, the 
experimenter instructed participants to sit upright with their back against the chair, hold their chin 
on a static chin-rest. Each subject was asked to perform 64 trials in a random order. When subjects 
had completed 64 trials of reading task, they were asked to answer their subjective rating of visual 
fatigue with four questions.

Analyses of variance (ANOVA) were performed to test the performances. Duncan 
multiple-comparison tests were performed to determine the significant main effects.

A design guideline for determining optimum combination of speed, position and field length of 
leading display was found. This study results indicated that taking comprehension and fatigue into 
consideration, the leading speed should be set at between 85 and 195 wpm, and the high speed of 
325 wpm should be avoided. This result of Chinese leading-display is consistent with the study of 
Chen and Tsoi (1998), where speed has a significant effect on comprehension in reading English 
leading-display information. Furthermore, the display field length of 18-character was significantly 
better than the 12-, 24- and 6-character. The field length of 24-character and 12-character were in 
equal performance, worse than the 18-character and better than 6- character. This may because of 
the 24-character was too long for the viewing field and need to move eye to read it, however this 
should be verified in the future. The field length of 6-character in single line display was 
significantly inferior to the other levels (18-, 12- and 24-character). The field length of short 
(6-character) sizes leads inferior reading comprehensions. This finding is similar to the study of 
Koler, et al. (1981), where indicated that the display field length with 6.2cm reduces users’ reading 
comprehension with RSVP format. Moreover, the information displayed on the top or left sides of 
screen were better than that displayed on the bottom or right sides of screen. This finding is similar 
to the study of van Schaik and Ling (2001). Their study results showed that when the information 
located at top or left of screen leading to better performance. Analyses of visual fatigue results 
showed that dynamic display certain increase user information visual load and leading visual 
fatigue. The results study could be used as a reference for information design of websites or TV 
screen layout design.

Abstract 
Leading display is one of the ways for displaying dynamic information. Display speed, position, 
and length of display field are important factors for leading display. In order to investigate the 
proper condition for leading display on TV screens, sixteen volunteer graduate students participated 
in the experiment. A three-way factorial design experiment (4 x 4 x 4) using speed (85, 195, 275, 
and 325 wpm), position (top, bottom, left, and right side on screen), and field length (6, 12, 18, 24 
characters) as independent variables was conducted to test comprehension and visual fatigue of 
information reading. The results of the study indicated that the leading speed should be set between 
85 and 195 wpm, and the high speed of 325 wpm should be avoided. The display field length of 
18-character in single line of leading display was significantly better than the other three levels. The 
information displayed on the top or left sides of the screen were better than that displayed on the 
bottom or right sides. These results could be used as a reference for designers to determine 
appropriate levels of speed, position, and field length.
