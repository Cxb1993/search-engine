 1
物流業能源使用效率評估模式之建構與應用 
Establishment and applications of energy efficiency 
evaluation model for logistics industry 
 
 
中文摘要 
在能源使用效率分析上，現行是以因素
分解法為經濟面之主要分析方法，分析結果顯
示在部門總體與個別企業能源使用效率上呈
現落差，即是現行的衡量方式較無法真實反應
出各別企業之能源使用效率情況，導致雖個別
企業有實施節約能源措施，但在運輸部門層級
所彙整的整體資料卻無法反應出個別企業的
節能措施，而與整體調查結果有脫勾現象。故
本研究動機為建立運輸部門行業別之作業層
效率指標估算模式，並推估出實際節能量或耗
用量，以瞭解在物流作業中能源耗用情況。透
過應用函數理論（Function theory）建構一套
兼具經濟面與作業面之運輸部門能源使用效
率評估模式（ Energy efficiency evaluation 
model, EEEM），藉此評估模式除可分析產業
能源使用效率及推估能源節省或耗用量，提供
能源使用及管理作業改善的參考依據。為聚焦
產業通路特性，本研究以台灣某家第三方醫藥
物流業者為此評估模式之驗證對象，供以作業
面為基礎的能源使用效率分析之先導性研究。 
關鍵詞：能源使用、能源效率評估、第三
方物流 
  
Abstract 
For the analysis of energy efficiency, factor 
decomposition is currently the standard for 
economic analysis within energy consumption 
indicator analysis. Though industries 
implemented energy conservation measures, this 
data application method fails to take into 
account the differentials of each industry’s 
energy efficiency circumstances, to the point 
that it overlooks these variables. Confronting 
this difficulty, the objective of this research is to 
establish an operational evaluation model for the 
transport sector, specifically for the logistics 
industry. By applying function theory, the 
energy efficiency evaluation model (EEEM) can 
encompass both the economic and operational 
aspects, allowing for greater practical reduction 
and more accurate waste estimations, thereby 
providing a better overall understanding of 
resource misuse in the logistics industry.  The 
application of EEEM not only allows for energy 
efficiency analysis, and energy economization 
and waste estimates, but also provides a basis 
for administering operational improvements. 
This research uses a third-party medical logistics 
firm to test and verify the model, and thus lay a 
foundation for further energy consumption 
analysis. 
Keywords: Energy consumption, energy 
efficiency evaluation, third party logistics

五、結論與建議 
本研究透過EEEM模型來建立物流業能源
使用效率的評估架構，此模型具有資料蒐集簡
單、模型運算容易的優點，經與設計運能與設
計能耗的比較，可評估出能源使用效率的六種
能源情景，可明確判斷在某時段內在物流作業
中能源使用效率好壞，並透過能源減少或增加
的估算公式，可掌握物流作業的能源節約狀
況。根據模式評估結果案例公司能源使用效率
之發現如下： 
(1) 運能利用率越大則越節能之說法並不
完全正確（6 月及 8 月有此情況發
生），然此情況並非有規則的，因為尚
有許多因素可能影響到運能利用率之
變化如產 品重量、油價上漲、配送距
離等。 
(2) ECPU 值越小顯示在配送單位件數能
耗上越小，則能源使用效率越佳，反
應出在物流系統中有較佳的配送效
率。 
在國際間追求節能和減碳的趨勢下，如何
提高能源使用效率、降低二氧化碳排放量將是
產業所面臨艱鉅挑戰。本研究主要貢獻在以居
台灣碳排放量第二大的運輸部門中，選擇某家
醫藥物流業來建立能源使用效率的評估模
式，透過模式建立與評估可掌握配送作業在能
源使用效率上的情況，可提供能源使用及管理
作業改善的參考依據，提供作業面為基礎的能
源使用效率分析之先導性研究。對於未來
EEEM 模式之後續研究有三點方向： 
 實務上較難決定設計能耗值，若無法在
系統開始運作時決定，則可利用歷史資
料利用灰預測（Grey prediction）推估
設計能耗值，提高模式估算精確性。 
 進一步分析配送作業之能源使用結構
因素，將有助於釐清因內部因素（配送
件數、商品屬性、駕駛行為、路徑規劃）
所造成之能源耗用情況。 
 面對實施浮動油價機制後，物流業如何
提升能源使用效率和油價漲幅如何反
映在運費計價調整上。

II. METHODOLOGY 
A. Model Development 
The EEI at the process level is usually chosen to be 
the ECPU. The factors that affect the magnitude of ECPU 
are multiple, which include: (a)process equipments; 
(b)operation method; (c)energy category; (d)feeding 
material; (e)system management; (f)energy saving activity; 
and (g)utilization of production capacity, etc. By treating 
ECPU and factors as function and variables respectively, 
a mapping relationship can be constructed where domain 
consists of the set of all seven variables and range is the 
set of all real positive number. In practice the ECPU of a 
process can be easily obtained via measurements, the 
interest is then to find out what are the values of variables 
that cause the ECPU, specifically the variable of energy 
saving activities. Nevertheless, because: (1) the factors are 
hard to be quantified as variables; (2) some variables are 
inter-related and there is no way to make them separated 
from each other, this approach is almost impossible to be 
carried out.
To simplify the problem, the seven variables 
mentioned above are categorized into two groups: the 
interior and exterior variables. The interior variable is 
defined as quantity that is energy related, while the 
exterior variable is not. In this sense the first six factors 
can be lumped together to become a single variable, and 
the last factor remains to be the exterior variable. The 
ECPU now is reduced to be a function of two variables 
only. Mathematically, the two variables can be defined as 
follows: (1) the utilization index of process production 
capacity, expressed inD ; and (2) the variation index of 
process energy utilization, expressed inȕ. If symbol f  is

.Ҋ  CONCLUSION 
A structure model for Energy efficiency indicator 
(EEI) at the process level of industry sector has been 
developed in this research. Using Taylor series expansion, 
the model is developed on the premise that the design 
parameters of the process are used to compare against the 
operational parameters for energy differences. Utilization 
index of production capability and variation index of 
energy utilization are formulated in the model to describe 
the differences between EEIs. Both qualitative and 
quantitative methods for energy efficiency and energy 
savings analyses are derived from the model. Through 
structural analysis, the model has shown that while the 
EEI is inversely proportional to the process utilization 
index of production capability, the energy savings may 
develop along the opposite direction to that of EEI. This 
helps to explain, at least in one way, the inconsistency 
between EEI and energy savings. The energy that has 
been saved or overused corresponding to particular EEI 
can be computed from the model.  
To demonstrate the application of the model, an IC 
wafer manufacturing process in a semiconductor plant in 
Taiwan had been selected as sample for energy efficiency 
evaluation. Numerical outcomes from the model clearly 
show that not only the process efficiency can be evaluated, 
but also the saved energy or overused energy can be 
computed. The sample also shows that the energy savings 
may be developed along direction opposite to that of EEI, 
in conformation with the prediction of the model.  
This model provides a solution to the difficulty in 
evaluating energy efficiency at the bottom process level. 
Strength of the model lies in the fact that any process that 
depends on utilization of production capability can be 
applied. However, an interface between evaluations at the 
bottom and the top level is still lacking. Given that such 
an interface be developed in the future, this model will 
significantly contribute to the improvement of energy 
efficiency evaluation system. Success of such work
