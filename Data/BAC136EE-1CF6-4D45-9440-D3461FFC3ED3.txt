1.1 研究背景與動機 
隨著科技的進步與國民生活水準提高，電力需求也逐年增加，對
於電力系統供電的品質與可靠度要求也相對提高。在政府推動產業升
級趨勢下，台灣逐漸發展生物科技、精密機械、半導體等產業，這些
高科技產業用電量高且需要穩定的供電系統，若當系統發生故障且無
預警的斷電，將會對設備及製程造成巨大的損失[1]。 
為了確保供電系統正常，輸電線或高低壓設備的性能及可靠度為
主要關鍵。電力設備普遍由導電、導磁與絕緣材料所構成，當設備常
處於過載狀態或是年份已久的設備，其絕緣會出現老化現象。另外像
是施工不當所引起的人為疏失也會破壞設備的絕緣狀態，根據相關文
獻的統計，電力設備運轉中有大部分的事故是由絕緣材料的老化或故
障所引起的[2]。 
在電力設備保養中，維護輸配電系統正常運轉日益受到重視，均
投入大量人力與物力研究設備的故障診斷技術與維護，其中應用於電
力設備故障診斷技術常見的有紅外線影像、油中氣體分析、介質損耗

建立完善的診斷資料庫，提供與量測的局部放電圖譜做比較、辨識，
可以預防設備無預警故障發生，防止大規模停電所造成的事故與損
失。早期局部放電辨識是由有經驗的專家依據橢圓軌跡圖來判斷，隨

2.2.2 局部放電相關名詞 
IEC60270 局部放電量測標準中，為了對局部放電信號有更明確的
表示，對局部放電常見相關名詞定義如下[4]: 
1. 局部放電脈衝(partial discharge pulse) 
待測物於試驗時所產生局部放電之電流脈衝信號或電壓脈衝信
號，利用專為測試而加入測試電路中的適當檢測電路，予以測量求
得脈衝信號。 
2. 視在電荷(apparent charge) 
在待測物兩端瞬間注入電荷量，使待測物兩端的電壓變化和局部放
電所引發之電壓變化相同，所注入之電荷量即為視在電荷，一般以
pC(pico-coulomb)為單位。 
3. 脈衝重複率 n (pulse repetition rate) 
在選定之時間間隔內，局部放電脈衝總數與該時間隔時間的比值，
即每秒鐘局部放電脈衝之平均數。 
4. 局部放電起始電壓 Vi (PD inception voltage) 
對待測物施加電壓，由觀測不到局部放電的較低值逐漸增加電壓，
直到待測物首次觀測到重複性局部放電信號出現時的電壓。

2.3 局部放電的原理與種類 
2.3.1 局部放電的原理 
電力設備製造過程中可能在絕緣材料中殘留空洞(cavity)、裂痕
等，絕緣材料會因為內部電場的不同而產生游離現象，在絕緣處引起
局部放電，長時間會導致絕緣崩潰，造成電力設備故障。局部放電之
特性與絕緣介質及雜質的大小、形狀及位置有關。其中，絕緣材料內
部空洞若為氣隙是最容易發生局部放電，因為氣體的介電常數與臨界
擊穿電場比液體和固體小，且在交流電場作用下，電場強度與介電常
 17
此時 Vg瞬間下降至 V+ (一般 V+約小於 100V)，於氣體放電時間(一般約
小於 10-7 秒)產生電壓變化量ΔV+，ΔV+是崩潰電壓 U+減去 V+的值。
當外加電壓又增加時，氣隙電壓 Vg也隨之上升，直到達到崩潰電壓 U+
時，又出現放電，這樣的情形在一週期內可能出現多放電現象。 
於氣隙首次放電後，氣隙中氣體會發生游離產生正離子與負離
子，並在電場作用下移至氣隙兩端，形成了與外部相反之內部電壓。
當外部電壓開始下降與氣隙內部電壓相互作用達到崩潰電壓U -時發生
放電脈衝，相同情形重複發生，可發現有規律的重複放電現象[14]。

2.4.2 局部放電的檢測方法 
局部放電的檢測方法有很多，主要是依據局部放電過程中伴隨的
電氣信號，或像是光、聲音、熱和化學反應等非電氣信號，來作為局
部放電檢測信號。因此，局部放電主要分為電氣檢測與非電氣檢測兩
類，電氣式常見檢驗法有電流脈衝法(pulse current method)、無線電干
擾法(radio influence voltage method)等，非電氣檢測常見檢測法有光學
檢測(optical detection)、超聲波檢測(ultrasonic detection)及油中氣體分析
檢測(dissolved gas-in oil analysis)等方法，其中電流脈衝法靈敏度高，
也是 IEC60270「局部放電量測標準」中推薦方法，應用最廣泛，下列
為各方法介紹。 
1. 電流脈衝法 
(1) 量測原理 
局部放電產生的電荷交換，使得待測物兩端出現視在放電電荷，將
待測物所產生於試驗迴路上的電流脈衝，經由檢測阻抗轉換得到電
壓脈衝，電壓脈衝正比於待測物上的視在放電電荷，可由局部放電

5. 油中溶解氣體分析 
油浸式電力設備於運轉時受潛伏性因子影響，導致絕緣油化學成分
發生變化而降低絕緣能力。可利用此方法定期抽取油品取樣分析，
檢出溶解於油中之氣體，如氫氣、甲烷、乙烷、乙炔、乙烯與一氧
化碳等氣體，可由數位法診斷其相對應故障關係。此方法適合設備
長期監控，並可由糠醛(furfural)分析推斷變壓器(以油為絕緣介質)
之生命週期[19]。 
2.5 本章結論 
局部放電檢測目的為了解設備故障程度及故障種類，根據相關文
獻指出，不同種類的放電模式其放電特性會不同，不同的檢測方法適
用於不同電力設備。若同一設備其放電起始和熄滅電壓越高則危害越
小；反之若放電起始和熄滅電壓越低，表示設備危害越大。 
局部放電檢測對於電力設備健康是一重要指標，若電力設備絕緣
材料中有瑕疵，則會發生局部放電現象。局部放電會導致絕緣系統老
化，更進一步會導致設備故障，所以對電力設備例行週期性檢測有其
必要性。

4.2 分形理論概述

5.3 可拓類神經理論 
5.3.1 可拓類神經理論概述 
可拓類神經網路(extension neural network, ENN)理論是結合可拓理
論與類神經理論，可拓理論與類神經網路皆屬於人工智慧之範圍，而
類神經網類似人類的神經系統，故其非線性運算之基礎是利用神經元
(neural)與神經元間眾多的連結所組成，並且神經元是採用平行且分散
方式進行運算。類神經網路能藉由訓練資料來呈現此理論之學習
(Learn)、回想(Recall)與歸納(Generalize)能力[36]。其中「學習」為建
構神經元之間的連結模式，並且於學習過程中即時的修正連結神經元
的權重；「回想」則是指類神經網路之輸入端接受到一個刺激時，進而
利用網路結構產生一相對應之輸出值，故對於網路的效能與強健性將
有所影響；而「歸納」則是以局部範圍來描述整體性系統，係類神經
網路最不可或缺的部份，藉由歸納推演出適合全部類別的方法。本計
畫將以可拓類神經網路方法應用在電力電纜局部放電的故障診斷研究
上。此理論包含了可拓距離數值運算、類神經網路之平行運算能力及
其具有學習、回想與歸納等特性，非常適合應用在特徵辨識上。

6.5 本章結論 
本章主要將分形維數、間隙度及平均放電量作為 3D 圖譜的三個特
徵，輸入至各種辨識方法當作輸入特徵，並利用相同的條件對各種辨
識方法做訓練，且加上±10%~±30%的隨機雜訊，測試各種辨識方法的
抗干擾能力。於本章的測試結果比較，不加雜訊時各種辨識方法都能
達到 100%，但是當隨機雜訊增加至±20%以上時，可拓、可拓類神經
及類小腦辨識方法，其辨識都優於傳統類神經網路，且這組類神經網
路是經過試驗時獲得最佳辨識結果的網路架構。 
可拓、可拓類神經及類小腦辨識方法不但在抗干擾測試中優於類
神經網路，且所花費的運算時間約為 0.2 秒內，比起類神經網路約為
10 秒快上許多。由實驗測試得知確實是比類神經方法更有效率，其中
又以可拓類神經辯識率最高。

2. Practical PD field measurement 
PD is a forced phenomenon occurring in insulation parts 
in a power apparatus. When the intensity of electric field 
exceeds the breakdown threshold value of a defective 
dielectric, PD occurs and results in a partial breakdown in the 
surrounding dielectrics. PD is a symptom of insulation 
deterioration. Therefore, PD measurement and identification 
can be used as a good insulation diagnosis tool to optimize 
both maintenance and life-risk management for power 
apparatuses. 
The new standard IEC60270 [13] for PD measurement 
has been published in 2001, which establishes an integral 
quality assurance system for PD measurement instead of the 
old standard IEC60060-2 published in 1994. The standard 
IEC60270 ensures accuracy of measuring results, 
comparability and consistency of different instruments and 
measuring methods. Moreover, the new standard provides 
digital PD measuring recommendations as well as the analog 
measuring. In this work, all PD experiments are based on the 
new standard IEC60270.  
A PD experiment laboratory, including a set of precious 
instrument (Hipotronics DDX-7000 Digital Discharge 
Detector), has been set up in the St. John’s University 
according to the standard IEC60270 recommendations. The 
constitution of the PD experiment laboratory includes a PD 
analyzer, a high-voltage control panel, an isolation 
transformer, a high-voltage generator (step-up transformer), a 
calibration capacitor, and a coupling capacitor. Figure 1 
shows the practical PD field measurement in the shielded 
room. 
The experimental objects are epoxy-resin power 
transformers in this work. The rated voltage and capacity of 
the transformers are 12 kV and 2kVA, respectively. For 
testing purposes, four experimental models of power 
transformers with typical insulation defects were purposely 
manufactured by a power apparatuses manufacturer. These 
typical PD models include a low-voltage coil PD (Type A), a 
high-voltage coil PD (Type B), a high-voltage corona 
discharge (Type C), and a healthy transformer (Type D). 
The voltage step-up procedure of the PD experiment, 
according to the standard IEC60270 recommendations, is 
shown in Figure 2. First, the high-voltage generator generates 
a rising-voltage from 0V to 18kV, which is the 1.5 times of 
the rated voltage, in 50 seconds. This high-voltage will be 
maintained for 1 minute to trigger discharges. Then, the 
voltage will descend from 18kV to the rated voltage in 20 
seconds. The 12kV rated voltage will be kept and the PD 
detector starts to measure and record the PD signals for 2 
minutes. During the experimental process, all of the 
measuring analog data are converted to digital data in order 
to store them in a computer.

5. Conclusions 
This paper presents a new pattern recognition 
methodology based on a four-layer feed-forward ANN for 
solving the insulation diagnosis problem of power 
transformers. The input data for the recognition system is the 
field measuring 3-D (φ-Q-N) patterns and PD-fingerprints. 
The proposed approach is considered a general tool because it 
can be easy implemented on the popular MATLAB software. 
Moreover, the approach is considered a flexible tool because 
it can be also applied to other high-voltage power apparatuses 
such as current transformer, potential transformer, cable, and 
rotation machine. Experimental results demonstrated that the 
proposed novel recognition system has better average 
recognition rate than the existing methods.

4. Optimal tuning of PID controllers using genetic 
algorithm 
As a mathematical means for optimization, GA can 
naturally be applied to the optimal tuning of PID controllers 
[6], [7]. With reference to a step input signal, the entire 
system will generate an output step response. The role of the 
PID controllers is to drive this output response within the 
user’s specifications. Obviously, the parameter settings of the 
PID controllers should be fine-tuned so as to meet as high 
requirements as possible. 
Optimization of PID controllers firstly needs design the 
optimization goal, and then encode the parameters to be 
searched. Genetic operator is running until the stop condition 
is satisfied. The decoding values of the last chromosome are 
optimized parameters of the PID controller.

5. Dynamic simulation and discussion 
Firstly, the PEMFC dynamic model is built based on the 
following assumption [8]: 
1 The improve Nernst equation is applied. 
2 The gases are ideal and obey the perfect-gas law. 
3 The water management and the humidification system 
are not considered. 
4 The temperature of fuel cell stacks is regulated at 80℃. 
5 The oxygen flow rate is determined by the 
hydrogen-oxygen flow rate from the reformer. 
This paper uses the Matlab/Simulink to build a PEMFC 
dynamic model and searches for fine-tuned PID controllers 
by GA. A set of selected PID controller parameters is chose

6. Conclusion 
This paper uses MATLAB/Simulink to design a PEMFC 
dynamic model. The dynamic model of the PEMFC with 
reformer is presented. Moreover, we design the PID 
controllers, which are installed for controlling the hydrogen 
flow rate of the reformer and the output voltage of fuel cell, 
using GA to achieve better transient and steady-state 
performances of PEMFC. The transient response of the 
PEMFC with fine-tuned PID controller has less overshoot, 
and it also settles to the reference faster.
