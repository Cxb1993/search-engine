Abstract  
This study attempts to determine the hand surface area and the percentage of body surface area 
by 3D measurement technology. The hand surface area is an important reference value in many 
applications, such as medicine and industrial hygiene. In general, the hand surface area can be 
referenced as whole hand surface area(HSA), and palm surface area(PSA). Nevertheless, in the past 
the measurement methods used to obtain and estimate HBSA were inaccurate, and the resultant data 
are significantly varied among different researches. Thus those data are misused in many practical 
applications. Due to the development of 3D measurement technology, we intend to use 3D human 
scanner to measure the hand surface area by taking 270 samples. We will apply the software to 
obtain the hand surface area and the proportion of body surface area(BSA) to establish hand body 
surface area database. We found that the HSA is 4.56% of BSA and PSA is 0.89%, which were 
different from traditional data. In addition, we devised the estimation formulae of HSA (HSA?2.47
?L?B) and PSA(PSA?0.97?L?B). 
Keywords: hand surface area, 3D anthropometry, database.
