2中文摘要
光學鍍膜有兩個重要課題，一、開發具有良好光學特性、機械特性及環境穩定性的薄膜材料，二、
開發新的光學多層膜設計方式；而新薄膜材料的開發有助於此兩課題研究，利用兩種現有薄膜材料混
合出所需特性的混合膜是一個不需對製程作大幅改變之簡易及低成本的薄膜材料開發方式，而本計劃
使用射頻離子束濺鍍系統製備混合膜，為單一離子源同時轟擊兩個靶材，並利用步進馬達控制兩靶材
面積比例，以決定混合膜的成份比例；研究分為兩部份，一、Ta2O5-SiO2 混合膜材料的研究，混合膜
之折射率及消光係數分佈於兩種單一材料薄膜間；二、準漸變折射率皺波濾光片之研究，使用移動靶
材方式精確控制混合膜折射率變化，製鍍出準漸變折射率皺波折射率濾光片；本計劃將探討兩單一材
料薄膜混合對折射率、消光係數、表面微觀結構及殘留應力的影響，及經熱退火後薄膜的氧化還原及
薄膜結構改變之研究，並研製準漸變折射率皺波濾光片，搭配膜層結構控制及熱退火處理，使準漸變
折射率皺波濾光片具有低吸收損耗、低散射損耗、低表面缺陷、低殘留應力及非晶結構等特性，完成
以混合膜取代傳統單一材料薄膜作為高品質光學鍍膜材料，並設計與製鍍出準漸變折射率皺波濾光
片，作為下一世代光學薄膜設計及製程方法之研究。
關鍵詞：光學薄膜、混合膜、五氧化二鉭、二氧化矽、準漸變折射率皺波型濾光片、射頻離子束濺
鍍、熱退火、殘留應力
Abstract
There are two important topics on optical coatings. First, we have to develop thin film materials with
good optical properties, mechanical properties, and environmental stability. Second, we need to develop new
methods to design optical multilayer coatings. The development of the new materials is helpful to study these
two topics. Using two pure materials to develop a suitable composite thin-film material, the composite
thin-films need to be highly stable and mechanically strong. Also, they should have low absorption, and, last
but not least, low cost. In this project, the radio frequency ion-beam sputtering deposition (RF-IBSD) system
was used to prepare composite films. An ion source was used to bombard two targets simultaneously. The
composite proportions are determined by the area ratio of two targets and the area ratio could be adjusted by
moving the targets with a step motor. There are two parts in this experimental. First, study of the material of
composite films prepared by high and low indices of the pure material layers. The refractive index and
extinction coefficients of the composite films were found to be between those of the high and low index pure
material layers. The structure of the as-deposited films was amorphous, and lower surface roughness. The
composite films have lower residual stress. The structure of the composite films after annealing was
amorphous, with low surface roughness and decrease the influence of mechanical properties of composite
films by thermal annealing. Second, study of the graded-index-like rugate filters. As the target moved laterally
positions, the sputtered material changed gradually from high to low index material, and we use materials
with high thermal stability. In this study, we try to decrease residual stress by changing a period of layer
number of graded-index-like rugate filters. Moreover, the influence of optical properties and residual stress of
graded-index-like rugate filters was investigated after thermal annealing. In this project, we would replace the
conventional pure material films by composite films for high quality optical coatings, and the composite films
would have good optical properties and mechanical properties. To design and prepare the graded-index-like
rugate filters could be a method to make next generation optical thin film filter.
Key words：Optical thin films, Composite thin films, Tantalan pentaoxide (Ta2O5), Silcon dioxide (SiO2),

RF ion-beam sputtering. The influence of thermal annealing on residual stress of single layer 
composite films and graded-index-like films has been studied.

1. Introduction: 
Rugate filter is designed as a film with graded index of refraction varying as a sinusoidal shape [1]. Film with 
rugate filter can be made by two independent sources to co-evaporate of high and low indices material either with 
two thermal sources [2] or with two sputtering sources [3, 4] or one ion source with two sputtering targets [5]. 
Alternatively, using single source with reactive gas of variable amount to produce the rugate filter has also been 
described [6, 7]. However, few researchers investigated the effects of thermal annealing on stress behavior of rugate 
filters prepared by ion-beam sputtering, and no comparison between the stress of quarter-wave stacks and 
graded-index-like films has been made. In this article, we have used RF ion sources to deposit Ta2O5-SiO2 
composite films and graded-index-like films by using Ta-SiO2 target. The influence of thermal annealing on 
residual stress of Ta2O5-SiO2 composite films, two composite layers, and quasi-sine form of graded-index-like films 
with different layers are analyzed. 
2. Experiment: 
A 6cm RF ion-beam sputtering process was used to prepare Ta2O5-SiO2 composite films [5]. Prior to the deposition 
process, the chamber pressure was 1x10-4 Pa. Ta (99.95% purity) and SiO2 (99.99% purity) targets were placed side 
by side on the same target holder. The 6cm ion source was tilted at 45 degrees to bombard the targets. We produced 
variously proportioned (Ta2O5)X(SiO2)1-X thin films. The “x” values represent the Ta2O5 proportion. The area ratio 
could be adjusted by moving the targets from 0cm to 15cm with a step motor. When the targets were at 0cm a pure 
SiO2 film was produced and at 15cm a pure Ta2O5 film was produced. The working gas for ion source was argon 
with 6 sccm flow rate. Oxygen was fed at near the targets with 19 sccm. The background pressure during the 
deposition process was 6×10-2 Pa. The substrate temperature was maintained at 100oC. 
During the annealing process, the sample was placed in an oven and heated to 200oC for 3 hrs then cooled 
down to room temperature in air. After this process was completed, the optical properties and residual stress of the 
resultant films were measured. The same process was subsequently used to heat samples to 300oC, or 400oC. 
We evaluated the residual stress σf of the thin films by measuring the change of their curvature, according to 
Stoney’s expression [8]:

Figure 3 shows the residual stress for different Ta2O5 proportion of two layer stacks and the annealing 
temperatures. The residual stress of as-deposited samples A1 and A2 were -477 and -310, respectively. This result 
shows that 2 layers stack of pure Ta2O5 and SiO2 films had larger residual stress than 2 layers stack of composite 
films with 41% and 81% of Ta2O5 proportion. The smaller Ta2O5 proportion difference between pure Ta2O5 and 
pure SiO2 had lower influence on residual stress. In fig. 3 the influence of the annealing temperature of different 
Ta2O5 proportion of two-layer stacks is presented. As the annealing temperature increased from 100oC to 400oC, the 
residual stress of sample A1 and A2 increased from -477 to -940Mpa and  -234 to -378MPa, respectively. The 
residual stress of sample A1 and A2 changed rapidly as the annealing temperature increased from 100oC to 400oC. 
The residual stress of samples A1 and A2 were lower than single layer composite films as the annealing temperature 
increased. It was caused by an interface creation. The interruption of the film growth is presumed to form an 
interface. The interface stops the accumulation of residual stress. The thermal stress of films was increased as the 
annealing temperature increased, but the residual stress could be reduced by the interface stress. 
Figure 4 shows the residual stress for different layer stacks, sample B1 and B2, with different annealing 
temperature. The residual stress of as-deposited samples sample B1 and B2 were -159 and -137MPa, respectively. 
As the annealing temperature increased from 100oC to 400oC, the residual stress of sample B1 and B2 increased 
from -159 to -708MPa and -137 to -609MPa, respectively. The residual stress change of sample B1 was smaller 
than sample B2. Sample B1 and B2 are 11-layered stack and 51-layered stack, respectively. More layers resulted in 
more number of interfaces. It indicated the residual stress of graded-index-like films can be reduced by dividing the 
film stack into many very thin layers. The residual stress of sample B1 and B2 changed rapidly than sample A2. The 
annealing process would create enough kinetic energy to the grain growth. The grain boundary was formed, and the 
resulted to tensile stress. The increase in grain size led to intrinsic tensile stress decreased. Therefore, the annealing 
process resulted in residual compressive stress increase. 
4. Summary: 
Composite films of (Ta2O5)x(SiO2)1-x was fabricated single layer composite films and graded-index-like films by 
using RF ion beam sputtering. The residual stress of single layer composite films were increased as the annealing 
temperature increased. From sample A1 and A2, the larger Ta2O5 proportion difference resulted in higher influence 
on residual stress. Furthermore, the residual stress could be reduced by the interface stress as annealing temperature 
increased. From sample B1 and B2, the residual stress of graded-index-like films of 51-layered stack was smaller 
than 11-layered stack. The residual stress of graded-index-like films changed rapidly than 2-layer stack as annealing 
temperature increased. During the annealing process, the residual stresses of two-layer stack and graded-index-like 
films were influenced by interface and grain boundary.

熱退火對混合膜與準漸變折射率皺波型濾光片之影響研究
唐謙仁
98 -2218-E -159 -001 - 光學工程
使用單一射頻離子束濺鍍法製備光學混合膜及準漸變折射率濾光片
Using Single RF Ion-Beam-Source to Prepare Optical Mixed Films and
Graded-Index-Like Filters
明新科技大學 唐謙仁
光學鍍膜有兩個重要課題，一、開發具有良好光學特性、機械特性及環境穩定
性的薄膜材料，二、開發新的光學多層膜設計方式；而新薄膜材料的開發有助
於此兩課題研究，利用兩種現有薄膜材料混合出所需特性的混合膜是一個不需
對製程作大幅改變之簡易及低成本的薄膜材料開發方式，而本技術使用射頻離
子束濺鍍系統製備混合膜，為單一離子源同時轟擊兩個靶材，並利用步進馬達
控制兩靶材面積比例，以決定混合膜的成份比例；技術可分為兩部份，一、精
確控制混合膜之折射率及消光係數分佈於兩種單一材料薄膜間；二、精確控制
混合膜折射率變化，製鍍出準漸變折射率皺波折射率濾光片；利用本技術可提
供將兩單一材料薄膜混合而成之混合光學薄膜材料據有高折射率穩定性、低消
光係數及低殘留應力，並研製出準漸變折射率皺波濾光片，搭配膜層結構控制
，使準漸變折射率皺波濾光片具有低吸收損耗、低散射損耗、低表面缺陷、低
殘留應力及非晶結構等特性，完成以混合膜取代傳統單一材料薄膜作為高品質
光學鍍膜材料，並設計與製鍍出準漸變折射率皺波濾光片，作為下一世代光學
薄膜設計及製程方法。
There are two parts in this technology. First, precision control the
material of composite films prepared by high and low indices of the
pure material layers. The stable refractive index, low extinction
coefficients, lower surface roughness and low residual stress of the
composite films were found to be between those of the high and low
index pure material layers. Second, study of the graded-index-like
rugate filters. As the target moved laterally positions, the
sputtered material changed gradually from high to low index material.
We can decrease the residual stress by changing a period of layer
number of graded-index-like rugate filters. In this technology, we
would replace the conventional pure material films by composite films
for high quality optical coatings, and the composite films would have
good optical properties and mechanical properties. To design and光學及精密器械製造業；機械製造業
本技術提出混合膜及漸變折射率濾光片，可提供給將要使用單層混合膜取代傳統單一
材料或使用漸變折射率製程生產光學濾光片之廠商作為前期研究及評估之依據。
利用單一離子源轟擊兩平行移動之靶材可較兩濺鍍源之製程穩定性佳，混合膜之膜質
混合比例穩定且混合比例之控制更為精確，可提供更好的光學薄膜材料，且利用單一
膜堆多層切割之方式可達到降低殘留應力之效果，使光學濾光片於製鍍後的形變降低
，可協助鍍膜產業於多層干涉式濾光片之製鍍品質及薄膜之環境穩性之提昇，其技術
移轉可行性高，對光學鍍膜產業於材料及新型光學薄膜設計方式研究開發有極高之效
益。

本研究計劃內容包括混合薄膜製鍍後及高溫退火後處理微觀表面結構形態、光學特性的觀
測結果、應力行為量測結果、應力與混合膜及退火處理的影響及改善之方法等；再者，經
此一年之栽培碩士生 3名，研究成果發表於 2010年 6 月美國光學工程學會在 Tucson 舉行
的 OIC 國際會議及 2009年 12 月在台灣師範大學舉行的台灣光電科技研討會、一篇審查中
2010年的 Applied Optics 期刊文章，而其它研究成果也將陸續投稿至學術期刊與研討會，
光學混合膜於漸變折射率光學濾光片設計、製作與特性分析的等基礎研究，於過去製鍍混
合膜之折射率控制的精確度與重複性不佳，因此應用混合膜作為光學薄膜設計與製作之研
究較少，現今高精密光學鍍膜設備之開發及光學濾光片要求提高，將不可避免使用混合膜
於光學濾光片的製作中，本計畫提出光學混合膜及準漸變折射率濾光片製鍍方式，可提供
給要使用混合膜取代單一材料或使用漸變折射率生產光學濾光片之廠商作前期評估之依
據，可進行研發成果技術推廣，漸變折射率濾光片可應用於高功率雷射、投影顯示濾光片、
色彩補償濾光片及各項光電研究所需之各項鍍膜研究等，因此將會提昇我國整體的研究能
力、促進我國學術地位及工業水準。
