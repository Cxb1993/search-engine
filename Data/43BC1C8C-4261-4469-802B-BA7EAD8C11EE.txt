醫療特用LED照明系統設計分析與驗證 
The design and verification of LED illuminance system for medical treatment 
計畫編號：NSC 99-2221-E-492 -010 
執行期間：99 年 08 月 01 日至 100 年 07 月 31 日 
計畫主持人：陳峰志 
共同主持人：陳政寰 
協同研究人員：施至柔 
中文摘要 
本計畫中，以高演色性與低紅外熱能的白光發光二極體為燈源，利用幾何光學的方法設計出一組適當
的無影燈具，設計的目標係根據 ISO 牙科用手術燈為規範。發光二極體的光源部份，透過各種不同類
型的白光 LED 組合出適當的光源模型，進行光譜量測，並根據 CIE 的規範計算出演色性。燈具的部分
依照機台規格進行自由曲面反射鏡的設計與製作。所製作出的無影燈經過檢測與驗證，找出了最佳設
計方案。 
關鍵詞：無影燈、發光二極體、自由曲面 
 
Abstract 
In this project, conforming ISO standard are designed using geometrical optics. The goal of this design meets 
the requirement of the dentistry ISO. The light source is LED module which has the optical properties of high 
color rendering and lower infrared illuminance. The optimum light source modules consist of different types 
of white light LED. The optical spectrum of the light source modules is measured. The color rendering index 
based on CIE standard is also calculated in the same time. The freeform reflector based on the requirement of 
dimension is designed and fabricated. Finally, the fabricated shadow-free reflectors is measured and certified. 
The optimum design of the shadow-free reflector is given. 
Keywords: Shadow-free reflector, Light emitting diod, freeform mirror.

（中文）

high color rendering and lower infrared illuminance. The reflector is 
based on the multi-segments free-form mirror. The shadow-free 
reflector consists of the LED module and the reflector.
