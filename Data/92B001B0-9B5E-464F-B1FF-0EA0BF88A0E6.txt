Abstract
Coenzyme Q10 which locates in the mitochondria is a fat-soluble antioxidants. It seems that
these endogenous substances resembling drugs are synthesized in human. Coenzyme Q10 can
help prevent or treat cardiovascular disease in clinical trial. This formation of coenzyme-Q10
microemulsions were preparated as potential drug delivery. The stability and penetration of
CoQ10 microemulsion to be enhance bioavailability were demonstrated.
Keywords: Coenzyme Q10, microemulsions, drug delivery
