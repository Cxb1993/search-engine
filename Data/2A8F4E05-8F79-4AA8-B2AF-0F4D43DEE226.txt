使用帶拒技術之多頻帶印刷開槽孔天線設計 
Design of the multi-band open slot antenna using band-rejected 
technique

一、中文摘要 
 
在本計劃中提出在寬頻λ/4開路槽孔天
線上設計雙頻及多頻帶天線。此種設計不
僅可以保持印刷槽孔天線的低姿式及容
易製造的優點，同時可以達到雙頻及多頻
帶操作之特性。在本計劃中，所設計的天
線包括可應用於WLAN、WiMAX的開槽孔
天線設計。考慮在實際應用中，寬頻天線
必須使用帶拒濾波器抑制不必要的頻段
導致成本增加。先提出ㄧ具有寬頻特性
(2.3~6.1 GHz)的小型化開槽孔天線，在寬
頻天線中加入矩形金屬片去抑制不需要
的頻帶，便可得到雙寬頻設計符合操作頻
率在2.4/5GHz的無線區域網路應用。此
外，在槽孔中加入兩條不同尺寸的矩形微
帶金屬片去抑制雙頻帶，便可得到一符合
2.5/3.5/5.5 GHz三頻操作可應用全球微波
存取互通的天線設計。相關的最佳設計準
則，我們將進行實驗及理論模擬分析。相
關的最佳設計準則，我們將進行實驗及理
論模擬分析。相關的最佳設計準則，我們
將在本論文中展示。 
 
關鍵詞：印刷槽孔天線、雙頻與多頻操
作、開路槽孔、小型化天線、無線區域網
路天線、全球微波存取互通天線 
 
Abstract 
 
In this project, a small-size and dual-band 
and multi-band antennas of λ/4 open slot 
antenna are studied. The open slot antenna 
retaining low profile and easy fabrication 
advantages is proposed. We proposed open 
slot antenna designs, including band-rejected 
and enhance bandwidth for WLAN and 
WiMAX applications. In practical applications, 
broadband antenna must use filter to suppress 
dispensable bands lead to increase cost. First, 
the broadband characteristic (2.3~6.1GHz) of 
open slot antenna design with small size is 
proposed. Then, by inserting strip on printed 
open slot of the broadband antenna to reject 
dispensable bands, a dual-broadband design of 
the open slot antenna with small size can be 
obtained for WLAN applications in the 
2.4/5GHz bands. Furthermore, inserting two 
strips of different length on open slot antenna 
to rejected dual-band, a three-band antenna is 
achieved. By adjusting the dimension of two 
strips on open slot antenna, a three operating 
modes frequencies at 2.5/3.5/5.5 GHz for 
WiMAX (Worldwide Interoperability for 
Microwave Access) application has been 
obtained. The optimal design criteria of the 
proposed design have been studied. 
 
 
Keywords: printed slot antenna, dual-band 
and multi-band operation, open slot, small-size 
antenna, WLAN antenna, WiMAX antenna 
 
二、緣由與目的 
 
開路槽孔天線來達成天線縮小化設計[21]
可以得到更多的操作寬頻及效能。 
在上述設計中，藉由λ/4開路槽孔天
線來來達成天線縮小化及寬頻設計[21]
之饋入結構是微帶線饋入。而利用此種
結構之槽孔天線設計寬頻效能較佳。所
以吾人將特別注意此天線設計發展。 
 
寬頻帶槽孔天線頻帶函蓋範圍太
多，可能會與已存在的無線系統產生互
相干擾之效應。所以在槽孔天線中加入
一個機制以產生頻帶抑制作用。接著，
討論在印刷天線設計裡，使用帶拒的技
術，以做為本計畫之參考。在微帶線饋
入的印刷單極天線的輻射元件中加入U
形[22]或倒U形[23-24]槽孔形成帶拒作
用。也有使用修正的倒U形槽孔[25]的
印刷單極天線被研究。在寬頻印刷天線
背面加入一個窄金屬微帶[26]可以達成
良好阻抗匹配及以形成帶拒作用。在寬
頻印刷天線背面加入一個窄環形寄生
金屬微帶[27]可以形成帶拒作用。在寬
頻印刷天線的背面輻射元件附近，加入
兩個寄生金屬微帶[28]可以形成帶拒作
用。在共面波導饋入的印刷單極天線中
使用兩個向下開口的窄槽縫[29]形成帶
拒作用。也有使用倒U形槽孔的共面波
導饋入印刷單極天線[30]被研究。使用
圓環形槽孔[31]於印刷單極天線也可以
達成帶拒作用。 
 
至於在印刷槽孔天線帶拒設計方
面，相形比教較之下就顯得少多了。一個 
倒U形槽孔植入圓形槽孔內部圓形金屬調
整珠[32]上，可以達成帶拒操作。一個長
方形槽孔天線的背面微帶線端連接碎形
調整株(fractal tuning stub)[33]達到帶拒作
用。一個Koch碎形槽孔天線[34]小形化效
能及帶拒操作都可以達成。漸增式槽孔
(tapered slot)天線[35]開口端兩測嵌入兩個
槽縫形成帶拒操作。寬頻槽孔天線加入一
個開路的金屬株[36]形成帶拒作用。單平
面對數週期槽孔天線[37]利用加入一對金
屬調整株達成帶拒操作。 
以上這些設計結構多樣且不同，所產
生的帶拒效果也不同，使用上端視實際不
同的需求而有其不同應用價值。亦可以看
出小形化帶拒印刷天線為當前印刷槽孔
天線研究重點。 
由於新的無線系統不段的被提出及推
出應用，如圖一所示，在眾多的無線應用
中，如何設計適用的天線是相當不容易。
在本計畫中構想以一種寬頻天線使用帶
拒技術來設計適用於數個無線通訊天線。 
所以本計畫中使用具有縮小化及寬阻
抗頻寬的不對稱接地面之λ/4開路槽孔天
線 (如圖二 )來做為帶拒天線設計的平
台，並且利用嵌入一個或數個窄金屬微帶
(narrow metal strips)於不對稱接地面之λ/4
開路槽孔天線來設計適用於2.4/5.2/5.85 
之WLAN、2.5/3.5/5三頻之WIMAX、及應
用於未來之多頻帶之帶拒天線，使得此槽
孔天線更具實用價值。 
 
為了有系統地研究利用不對稱接地
面之λ/4開路槽孔天線設計應用於無線區
域網路、全球微波存取互通及應用於未來
之多頻帶之帶拒天線，本計劃以在不對稱
接地面之λ/4開路槽孔天線(簡稱為開槽孔
天線)嵌入一個或數個窄金屬微帶(narrow 
metal strips)於開路槽孔天線的內部金屬面
(接地面之一邊)，來設計適用於2.4/5.2/5.85 
之雙頻帶WLAN天線及多頻帶天線。一天
線設計幾何結構如圖三所示，在一般開路
槽孔天線由左邊邊緣嵌入一個長度為Ls
寬度為Ws的窄金屬微帶。藉由此金屬微
帶的引入，會在特定頻帶產生共振，使得
電流多集中窄金屬微帶周圍，於此使的該
特定頻帶的能量集聚在此不能有效輻射
出去，即產生天線增益在此帶拒頻帶中驟
降。並且由於電流多集中窄金屬微帶周圍
使得原寬頻天線在此帶拒頻帶產生不匹
配現象。

微帶(narrow metal strips)於開路槽孔天線
的內部金屬面(接地面之一邊)，來設計適
用於2.4/5.2/5.85 之三頻帶WiMAX天線。

1. Introduction 
 
Since the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) approved, the frequency spectrum 
from 3.1~10.6 GHz is for Ultra-wideband in 2002. Many configurations have broadband 
characteristic for UWB antenna design, including wide slot, CPW-fed monopole, bow-tie slot, and 
smooth circular monopole structure. The traditional slot antenna is about half-wavelength resonance, 
which is the large antenna size and unsuitable for mobile devices. In [1], the open slot antenna has 
quarter-wavelength resonance but the bandwidth is not enough to satisfy UWB applications. In 
addition, there are many techniques for bandwidth enhancement in the antenna designs are proposed, 
such as three different slot shapes, straight, L and inverted T, cut at the center of the finite ground 
plate edge [2], but these antenna configurations are difficult to enhance bandwidth for UWB 
requirement. Moreover, using notch constructions on radiating path or ground plane to improve 
bandwidth are proposed, such as cutting two L-shape notches and attaching two stubs to the 
radiating patch [3], cutting a notch at the ground pattern opposite the microstrip line [4].  
In this letter, we proposed a novel design to further enhance impedance bandwidth by 
varying the shape of the open slot. The measured bandwidth of the proposed antenna defined by 
return loss >10 dB is about 130%, from 2.3 to 10.9 GHz. The proposed open slot antenna will be 
implemented, and the detail antenna design and experimental results are presented and discussed. 
 
2. Antenna design and Experimental results 
 
 The geometry of the proposed antenna is shown in Fig.1. The antenna is printed on a FR4 
substrate with a thickness 0.8 mm and relative permittivity 4.4. The rectangular patch having 
dimension 1L × 2W  is fed by a 50Ω microstrip line of width 1.53 mm. On the ground structures, an 
invert-L shape slot is etched and two notches (notch A and notch B) are cut along the edge of slot. 
This antenna has a small size of dimensions 30×35 mm2. The only invert-L shape open slot on 
ground plate produces wideband impedance characteristic (2.2~6 GHz) has been proposed [5]. And, 
the detail parameter studies of open slot and rectangular patch were discussed. In order to improve 
the bandwidth for UWB, by adding two notches connected with open slot in the ground plate, the 
large impedance bandwidth of the antenna is generated. This is probably that the introduction of the 
two notches A and B increase the resonant path at high frequency, which leads to improve the 
bandwidth of the open slot antenna. 
Fig.2 shows the measured return losses with different length of L3. It is seen that the optimal 
value for L3 is 4mm. The measured return losses with and without notches of the open slot antenna 
are shown in Fig 3. It is observed that the notch B is the most important parameter determined the 
impedance matching and the notch A is used to improve the bandwidth. By simultaneously adding 
two notches (notch A and notch B), the proposed antenna can achieve a large bandwidth. By 
adjusting the dimension of the slot and notches properly, the operating frequency from 2.3 to 10.9 
GHz has been achieved. A broadband design of the open slot antenna with small size for WLAN 
and UWB applications is obtained.
