英文摘要
This study describes the effect of bath temperature and current density on iron content,
crystal structure and internal stress of Ni-Fe deposits prepared from a sulphate bath. It is
obvious that both increasing the bath temperature and decreasing the current density can
eliminate the anomalous codeposition of Fe and Ni. At low bath temperature of 298K,
increasing current density has very limited effect on cathode current efficiency and results in a
slight increase of iron content from 80wt% to 90wt%. However, at the highest bath
temperature of 353K, the increase of current density results in significant increases of both
cathode current efficiency and iron content. Severe internal stress causes cracking in the
deposits. The density of the crack is close related to the iron content of the deposits. The
deposits having an iron content of about 60% possesses a highest crack density. This iron
content also corresponds to a divide of the crystal structure of the deposits. Deposits having
iron contents lower than 60wt% have an FCC crystal structure, otherwise they have a BCC
structure.
Keywords：electrodeposition、Ni-Fe alloys、sulphate bath、bath temperature、cathode
current efficiency、residual stress、microstructures

八、計畫成果自評
本計畫已達成原定：「以硫酸鍍液系統為基礎，釐清鍍液成分和電鍍製程條件對鎳鐵
鍍層成分、相組成、顯微組織的影響，以建立穩定的電鍍製程」的目標。研究結果首次釐
清鍍液溫度對電鍍鎳鐵合金成分、內應力與顯微組織的影響。可以作為進一步瞭解鐵鎳異
常共鍍的現象，以及具BCC結構之高鎳合金的熱膨脹特性與相穩定性的基礎。因此本研究
成果兼具學術或應用價值。研究結果將分別撰寫論文兩篇（”Thermal expansion and thermal
stability of a BCC Fe-36wt% Ni alloy prepared by electrodeposition”and“Electrodeposition of
Ni-F alloys from a sulphate electrolyte: relationship between bath temperature and structural
characteristics”）投稿於學術期刊。

物的性質與應用，所發表之論文涉及的製程包括軋延、燒結、鑄造與電鑄等。其中本人之研
究係本研討會中唯一採用電化學方法製備具有奈米晶粒之鎳基合金。電鑄合金具備成長速度
快、成本低廉且強度高的優點，但是不易推廣的原因之一，是鍍層殘留應力不易控制，本研
究特別針對影響鍍層應力的製程與組織參數作了深入的探討、因此獲得部分參與者之興趣與
詢問。
