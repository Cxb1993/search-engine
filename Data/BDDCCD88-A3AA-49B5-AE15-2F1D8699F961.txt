應用添加劑於二階段靈芝醱酵生產多醣體之探討 
計畫編號：NSC 95－2221－E－005－030  
執行期限： 95 年 8 月 1 日至 96 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：劉永銓    國立中興大學化工系 
 
摘要: 
本研究利用油脂的添加進行靈芝菌絲體之深層培養，研究結果顯示，橄欖油的添加
有助於菌體乾重的提升，而玉米油的添加對於多醣體的產量是有提升的效果，為未添加
的 2.1 倍。於第 0 天添加 2％玉米油、1％橄欖油及起始 pH 值 6 下，以轉速 100 rpm 的
條件進行培養，其結果發現，菌體乾重及多醣體之產量都有提升的效果，多醣體的產量
約為未添加時的 4.74 倍，可達 1620 mg/L，而菌體乾重約為未添加時的 3 倍，可達 16.5 
g/L；另一方面，於第 12 天提昇轉速至 200 rpm，其多醣體產量於第 13 天可達 1810 mg/L，
約為未添加時的 5.3 倍。 
關鍵字：靈芝、多醣體、液態醱酵、油脂、界面活性劑 
Abstract 
  The object of this study was to enhance mycelia and polysaccharide production in 
submerged culture by addition of oils. In experiments of oils supplementation, addition of 
olive oil was favor to mycelia growth. In contrast, supplementation of corn oil increased the 
polysaccharide production (2.1 times compared to control). Based on the above results, 
fermentation was carried out by adding 2% corn oil and 1% olive oil, and the initial pH and 
rotation speed was adjusted to 6 and 100 rpm, respectively. The results showed that the 
mycelia and polysaccharide production was dramatically improved to 3.0 times (about 16.5 
g/L) and 4.74 times (about 1620 mg/L) compared to control respectively. On the other hand, 
to enhance polysaccharide production, experiment was conducted by adjusting the rotation 
rates from 100 to 200 rpm at 12 days’ cultivation to alter the share force exerting on the strain. 
When the rotation rate was changed, the results showed that polysaccharide production 
increased to 1810 mg/L at day 13, a 5.3 times increase as compared to that of control.  
Keyword: Ganoderma lucidum, polysaccharide, submerged culture, lipids, surfactants

1. 油脂添加對 G. lucidum 生產菌絲體及
多醣體之影響 
由圖 1 與圖 2 可知，橄欖油的添加，
有助於 G. lucidum 之生長，其菌體乾重最高
可達 10.9 g/L 約為控制組（5.5 g/L）的 2

2. 不同時間添加油脂之影響 
微生物在不同階段的需求不一，本實
驗以不同生長階段做玉米油的添加，找出
最適合之添加時間。由圖 3 與圖 4 可發現，
於第 0 天添加玉米油之效果最好，隨著添
加的時間越晚效果則越不明顯，由圖 3 可
發現，於第 0 天添加玉米油至第 13 天有高
菌量（12.9 g/L）明顯比控制組（5.5 g/L）
高，若於第 5 天添加玉米油醱酵至第 7 天，
其菌量（5.6 g/L）和控制組相比並無明顯的
差異，但培養至第 13 天則出現最高的菌量
（11.8 g/L）；多醣產量方面，由圖 4 顯示，
於第 0 天添加玉米油至第 13 天有最高多醣
產量（1351.9 mg/L）明顯比控制組（582.8 
mg/L）高，若於第 5 天添加玉米油醱酵至
第 11 天則出現最高的多醣產量（1038.1

比第 0 天添加玉米油之多醣產量低。由以
上結果得知，於 G. lucidum 醱酵初期添加玉
米油對於菌量和多醣的產量皆有提升之效
果。 
3. 添加第二種油脂的影響： 
由添加不同油脂之實驗發現，單一油
脂對菌量與多醣產量之提升不一，如 Park 
et al.[8]研究發現，添加葵花油能夠明顯提
升 Cordyceps militaris 之多醣產量，但對菌
量之提升有限；而添加橄欖油則能有助於
菌量之生產。由實驗 4-1 中可知，以 G. 
lucidum 生產多醣時，玉米油為第一選擇。
但圖 1 指出，添加 2％橄欖油之菌量（10.9 
g/L）明顯比添加 2％玉米油之菌量（8.2 
g/L）高，故本實驗利用橄欖油做為第二種
添加之油脂。由圖 4 可發現油脂之添加於
醱酵初期有較佳之效果，因此選擇 0、1 和
2 天為添加時間，由圖 5 發現，於第 0 天添
加橄欖油可得到較高之菌量（10.6 g/L），而
第 1 天和第 2 天添加橄欖油其菌量並無明
顯差異；同時可以發現，於第 0 天添加 2
％玉米油和 1％橄欖油與添加 3％玉米油之
菌量分別為 10.6 g/L 和 8.5 g/L，證明於醱

由圖 6 可發現，於第 0 天添加 2％玉米油和

2％玉米油和 1％之橄欖油對於菌量與多醣
的產量皆有提升之效果。

The assay procedure was originally described by Chen and 
Lee (Chen and Lee 1995). The reaction mixture, which 
consisted of 1.5 ml enzyme solution, 2.5 ml of 1 % 
colloidal chitin in 0.075 M, pH 7 sodium phosphate buffer, 
was incubated at 50 , 100 rpm for 10 min in a water bath. ?
After the enzymatic reaction, 1 ml of DNS reagent (3, 
5-dinitrosalicylic acid 10 g/l, phenol 2 mg/l, Na2SO3 0.5 g/l, 
and NaOH 10 g/l) (Miller 1959) was added, followed by 
heating of the mixture at 100  for 5 min. Then the solution ?
was cooled for 5 min, diluted with l ml of distilled water, 
and centrifuged at 3000 g for 15 min to remove the 
precipitate. The absorbance was measured in a 
spectrophotometer (Jasco, Japan) at 540 nm. One unit (U) 
of enzyme activity was defined as the amount of enzyme 
required to produce 0.5 µmol of N -acetyl-D-glucosamine 
for 1 h. 
3 RESULTS AND DISCUUSION 
 
3.1 AERATION EFFECTS 
 
The production of chitinase was conducted in a 5-L STBR 
with temperature maintained at 34.3 ? . The effect of 
aeration rates (namely 1, 2, and 3 vvm) was investigated 
with a constant agitation of 200 rpm and the results are 
shown in Fig. 1. The maximum chitinase activities for 
aeration rates of 1, 2, and 3 vvm were 3.9, 7.8, and 11.8 
U/ml, respectively. Under these three aeration rates, the 
highest chitinase activity appeared at 56 h for aeration rate 
of 3 vvm. The enzyme activity was considerably affected by 
the oxygen supply. At aeration rate of 1 vvm, a DO 
shortage was obviously observed. Meanwhile, DO profile 
of aeration rates of 2 vvm was lower than that of 3 vvm 
(data not shown).

3.2 AGITATION EFFECTS 
 
To study the effect of agitation rates on the yield of 
chitinase, the fermentation was carried out at 34.3  and 3 ?
vvm with different agitation rates (i.e. 100, 200, 300 rpm). 
Time courses of chitinase activity profiles under different 
agitation rates were shown in Fig. 2. The chitinase activity 
increased from the beginning of the cultivation and reached 
to a maximum level, then, decreased. The highest chitinase 
activity appeared at 56 h under the 200 rpm agitation rate.  
The chitinase level was a little lower at agitation rate of 100 
rpm. While at agitation rate of 300 rpm, the chitinase 
decreased rapidly. This indicated that the production of 
chitinase is limited to certain agitation and aeration rates. 
Increasing the agitation rate may result in the rupture of 
cells, which indirectly cause the decrease in chitinase 
activity.

3.3 pH EFFECTS 
 
The influence of fermentation medium pH was also studied 
in a 5-L STBR. While in the fermentation with different 
aeration and agitation, the pH varied between 6.8 - 7.8. In 
the fermentation with pH controlled, the chitinase activity 
produced was varied and is depicted in Fig. 3. It was found 
that chitinase activity profiles changed with pH controlled 
at 6.0 to 9.0. The optimal value of pH of the media for 
chitinase production was 7.0 (9.1 U/ml) at 80 h. It seemed 
that pH value affected both the cell growth of Paenibacillus 
sp. and chitinase production. However, the highest chitinase 
activity was found at that with pH uncontrolled. This meant 
the microbial could adapt to the environment in the whole 
process. The process with pH controlled might force it to 
change its metabolic pathway and cause a decrease in the 
chitinase activity during the cultivation.
