英文摘要 
To realize the all solution-process organic electrics, it is inevitable to develop the all necessary 
components for the fabrication of flexible organic device including plastic substrate, electrode, active layer 
and dielectric layers. In the first year, five research works implemented in this year consist of (a) a thin epoxy 
layer or the mixture of epoxy and MMT was stacked on the plastic substrate (PET) to prevent the nitrogen and 
oxygen from penetration. (b) The conductive polymer blend of silver nanoparticle (20nm~70nm) and 
photosensitive polymer has been studied for the application of the ink-jet printing process. (c) The noble 
soluble n- and p-type material has been synthesized. (d)The developed composite polymer dielectrics exhibit 
the smooth surface, high stability and low leakage current. (e) Due to the flexible organic electrics, it is 
necessary to investigate how the bending will influence the output characteristic of organic transistor. The 
x-ray diffraction and Raman spectrum have been adapted to study the correction between the bending and 
electrical characteristic of transistors. 
The implement of this year including in plastic substrate preparation, the conductive polymer blending, 
noble n- and p-type material synthetic, composite polymer development and research of output characteristic 
of “bending＂ device have shown the solid results. And all results will be helpful for the advanced research 
to realize the all solution-process organic thin film transistor in the following year. 
 
Key word : the air blocking layer, silver nanoparticle, plastic substrate, n-type organic material, all 
solution-process

  薄膜檢測： 
改質後的蒙脫土混入 KM-U 型硬化樹酯
中，在其與硬化劑固化的短暫凝膠狀態(gel state)
將之塗佈在 PET 基板上，我們選用三種方法分
別為旋轉塗佈、刮刀塗佈、重力壓膜機壓膜。 
旋轉塗佈(Spin coating)是將凝膠狀的蒙脫
土混合樹酯置於 PET 基板上，控制轉速於
800rpm，旋轉 20 秒鐘，使其均勻分散在基板
上，以轉速來控制膜的厚度，藉此得到塗佈均
勻的膜。 
刮刀塗佈(Doctor blade)是在 PET 基板周圍
貼上鐵氟龍(Teflon)膠帶，將凝膠態的蒙脫土混
合樹酯置於基板上，藉著刮刀刮過基板得到一
個與鐵氟龍膠帶同樣厚度的膜。 
重力壓膜機壓膜是凝膠態的蒙脫土混合樹
酯置於基板上，上方置一片玻璃，控制重力在
10k 牛頓下，剝離玻璃基板後可得到我們所需
的薄膜，所得的蒙脫土/樹酯膜之膜厚可控制在
約 35um，也因玻璃基板平整的表面，使得膜的
表面也相當平整，達到我們所需求條件。其裝
置如圖 7 所示。

計畫成果自評 
(A)研究內容與原計畫相符程度以及達成預期目標情況 
本計畫第一年度著重於有機薄膜電晶體之基板、電極、主動層、絕緣層之材料的開發
與適用於溶液式製程，完成 N2與 O2零滲透率阻氣層於基板，可以提高未來元件的操作壽命；
亦完成感光硬化行導電奈米油墨，可適用於元件電極，也適用於溶液式製程；本團隊亦開
發多種新穎 n-與 p-type 主動層材料，遷移率分佈在 10-3~10-110-1cm2/Vs 之間；亦完成高介
電常數、低漏電流、可適用於溶液式製程的絕緣層，可以解決 OTFTs 高偏壓的問題；最後
本團隊也建立一個元件撓曲後電性變化的模型，對未來元件彎曲電性變化非常有幫助。 
這些結果非常符合原計畫所提的第一年度的目標，也是第二年度所有成果整合的最佳
支柱。 
 
(B)研究成果之學術與應用價值 
(1)學術成果 
  第一年度完成之 OTFTs 元件所需要之所有材料的開發，並且適合於溶液製程，雖然市
面上早已有全溶液製程的材料，但本團隊並非使用市面上材料，而是發展出新材料，或是
將舊材料加以改善，像阻氣層是以蒙脫土取代樹酯來分散二氧化矽，不只成本低還可達到
N2及 O2零滲透率的境界。本團隊亦開發出一系列 n-與 p-type 主動層材料，p-type 主動層
材料遷移率可達 10-1cm2/Vs，四併環噻吩 n-type 材料，目前電性可達 0.3 cm2/Vs，這是目
前所有多併環噻吩材料中 n-type 電性最高者，本團隊在第一年度發表的期刊論文入下所

近年來離散式的電路架構已經有許多文獻探討。傳統離散式主動次諧波混頻器，
會因為前級的 balun 電路，侷限了後級離散式架構的頻寬，將提出一個改良式的離散
式寬頻次諧波混頻器。此次電路架構於 RF 訊號輸入後，經由離散電感架構分別輸入
三階離散式電晶體 M1-M3的閘極，此電晶體操作在飽和區，主要作為轉導級，提供 RF
訊號予開關級電晶體 M4-M9的源極，其中 M4-M9偏壓接近截止區，主要作為 LO 的開關
級，藉以混出兩倍 LO 訊號與 RF 訊號產生混頻的效果。而 LO 訊號輸入後，經由離散
電感架構分別輸入三組開關級電晶體 M4、M6與 M8的閘極，另一端的電晶體 M5、M7與 M9
的閘極則接到 bias 電路，對高頻而言提供一個接地特性，可以產生 LO 的帄衡訊號，
並且將兩端的相接後，可以用來產生二倍頻訊號，在與由轉導級輸入的 RF 訊號混頻
後，可以混出 IF 訊號，並且經由 IF 埠前端的低通濾波器，完成組抗匹配與提高隔離
度。因此，這個電路架構可以簡單的由單端輸入，產生帄衡的 LO 訊號，省去巴侖電
路簡化電路設計，所以可以利用到較完整的離散式頻寬，而不會被巴侖電路侷限頻寬
範圍。在此綜合介紹本電路架構的特點: 
1. 利用單端輸入的方式實現電路，相較於傳統差動輸入的次諧波混頻器，可以
省去 LO 端的巴侖電路，進而提升混頻器的頻寬與縮小晶片面積。 
2. 晶片面積 0.7 mm2，與其他離散式相關參考文獻相比，是相當節省面積的設計。 
3. RF 頻帶範圍介於 C- to K- band，可達到寬頻的特性。

Abstract-A single-balanced distributed broadband 
sub-harmonic mixer (SHM) is proposed to satisfy 
broadband operation and to minimize chip area. 
The proposed SHM consists of transistors, 
distributed inductors, and low-pass filters. A 
single-ended LO port and RF port structure is used 
to reduce chip dimensions and to simplify the 
circuit design. The proposed SHM is designed and 
fabricated on a 0.18 μm CMOS process. The 
CMOS SHM exhibits an operating bandwidth of 
7.5–20.5 GHz (92.9%), with the smallest chip area 
of 0.58 mm × 1.2 mm. Compared with previously 
reported SHMs, the bandwidth of the proposed 
mixer can be widened by distributed technique and 
retrenching the balun with 30% size reduction. 
The measured conversion loss was 9.1–13 dB from 
7.5 to 20.5 GHz. The isolation at each port is better 
than 23.5 dB under an operation bandwidth of 7.5 
to 20.5 GHz.  
 
Index Terms- CMOS, Single-balance, Distributed 
technique, Sub-harmonic mixer 
 
I. INTRODUCTION 
Mixers play a very important role in transmitters 
and receivers of communication systems because 
they can convert signals from one frequency to 
another. Given an increase in local oscillator (LO) 
operating frequency, implementing an LO with 
low phase noise and high output power is 
difficult. However, the subharmonic mixer (SHM) 
allows the LO operating frequency as half the 
frequency of the fundamental mixer. The phase 
noise and output power are superior to the 
fundamental source signal when the LO 
operating frequency is relatively low. The SHMs 
are then very suitable in millimeter-wave range 
applications. Moreover, the essential issue lies 
with investigating compact, low-cost, low-power, 
broadband, and high-performance CMOS 
transceivers in the modern MMW realm. 
The quality of output signals is strongly 
dependent on the isolation of the subharmonic 
mixer. Thus, designing a good performance 
mixer for radio frequency (RF) circuits is a very 
important issue. Many SHMs using anti-parallel 
diode pairs (APDPs) configuration have been 
demonstrated[1–5]. In conventional configuration, 
the quarter-wave transmission lines were used to 
implement good port-to-port isolation [1, 2]. An 
SHM monolithic microwave integrated circuit 
(MMIC) with small chip size is necessary for 
reducing production cost. However, these 
quarter-wave transmission lines have to occupy a 
larger chip size with regard to its required 
operating frequency. A miniature quadruple 
SHM with lumped frequency diplexer can be 
found in [3]. However, its operation under 
overlapping RF bands and LO signals was 
limited by the frequency diplexer. The APDP 
configuration has several advantages, such as the 
ability to suppress any even harmonics of the LO, 
and the ability to operate without dc power [4]. 
However, the directional coupler is not suitable 
for SHM designs because of its undesirable LO-
to-RF isolation. Numerous SHM MMICs have 
been demonstrated in CMOS processes [5–8], for 
an example, a 10–40 GHz broadband CMOS 
SHM uses a two-stage Wilkinson power 
combiner to achieve broadband features [5]. 
However, the two-stage power combiner is 
limited LO-to-RF isolation and occupy large chip 
dimension. The sub-harmonic mixer employed 
Gilbert-cell to achieve broad operating
