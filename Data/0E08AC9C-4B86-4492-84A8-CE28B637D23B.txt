Abstract 
The purpose of the research is analyzing the technique of designing photovoltaic(PV) 
integratedwith horizontal sunshade and redirecting devices, finding the optimal model of PV 
integrated with sunshade,based on the viewpoint of using redirecting to enhance the illuminance 
interior, using sunshade to reduce solar heat gain and using solar PV to lighting. 
First, the theories of application and evaluation on PV integrated with sunshade and 
redirecting willbe analyzed, such as the predicting of PV power, performance of sunshade, 
method of using redirection.Second, the technique of PV integrated with sunshades and 
redirecting devices on building envelope,construction and equipment will be analyzed. By the 
combination of theory of PV application, sunshade andredirecting, the criteria for PV integrated 
with sunshade and redirecting will be obtained. Considering the criteria of PV application, the 
optimal designing of PV integrated with sunshade and redirecting devices will be approached. 
Third, the experiment of the optimal model for PV integrated with sunshade will be conducted for 
evaluating and proving the performance of PV system, shading and redirecting. According to the 
data collecting, reading PV regulation, interview with professional expert and PV case study, the 
technique of PV integrated with sunshades and redirecting devices will be concluded. 
The results of this paper will include 1) the analysis of theories for predicting of PV power, 
performance of sunshade, performance of redirecting, 2) finding the optimal model for PV 
application on sunshades and redirecting devices, 3) evaluating the performance of PV 
integration with sunshades and redirecting, 4) collecting the technique of PV integrated with 
sunshades and redirecting. 
 
Keywords: photovoltaic(PV), BIPV, sunshade
