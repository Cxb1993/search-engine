英文摘要 
        Nuclear power is the most economical way of generation of electricity and it is carbon-
free. When operating nuclear power plants, “reactor safety” and “ radiation safety”are the 
most important issues. Having enough atomic energy regulatory personnel possessing 
knowledge of reactor safety and radiation safety is indispensable for safe operations of nuclear 
reactors. Due to the anti-nuclear environment for the past ten years, nuclear technology is 
facing the crisis of deficiency of nuclear engineers. We have to mend this situation in no times. 
       This project is a sub- project (Project I) of “Educational Planning for the Atomic Energy 
Regulatory Personnel”. The project is aiming on the issue of education and training of atomic 
energy regulatory personnel in the areas of reactor safety and radiation safety. It is a 3-year 
project, the courses related to reactor safety and radiation safety will be organized, turned into 
electronic documents(1
st
 year, this year), edited into short-course materials for on-job 
training(2
nd
 year), and developed into long-distance learning system(3
rd
 year). A short-term 
training program of the intern operator of THOR research reactor is especially included in this 
project to expose students to hands-on training of reactor operation. 
       The course materials established through this project will be helpful in the continuity of 
education of nuclear technology. Electronic documents completed can be used conveniently 
through the mail or website to avoid misunderstanding while facing discussions of safety 
related issues. The in-time education of young generation nuclear experts can prevent us from 
facing the problem of deficiency of human resource in this field in the years to come. 
Keywords: Atomic Energy Regulatory Personnel, Educational Planning, Reactor Safety,                   
Radiation Safety
