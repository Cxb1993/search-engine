摘 要 
本研究旨在探討蜂巢格網加勁砂土之力學
特性，試驗結果所得的參數將可提供蜂巢格網加
勁擋土結構之穩定性與變形性分析。本期之工作
項目包括：相關文獻收集與討論、蜂巢格網專用
夾具研發與製作、利用直剪試驗儀完成材料界面
摩擦特性試驗、及利用三軸試驗儀完成單格與多
格蜂巢格網的壓縮試驗。由已完成的試驗結果發
現，蜂巢格網層間的摩擦角與砂土的摩擦角相近，
此表示加勁層與層之間的摩擦性質由土壤所控
制。而地工格網拉拔試驗結果顯示，低正向應力
時，加勁材拉力隨正向應力呈線性分布，破壞模
式以拉出為主，當正向應力大於加勁材料的抗張
強度時，破壞模式則為拉斷破壞。此外，利用三
軸壓縮試驗探討單格與多格蜂巢格網的承壓應
力傳遞行為，已完成的試驗結果顯示，格網的圍
束效應限制了土壤的側向位移，明顯地降低試體
的體積變化趨勢，且圍束效應引致較高的軸差應
力阻抗。換言之，以蜂巢格網加勁能增加土壤的
視凝聚力，並因而提高土壤的抗剪強度。 
 
關鍵詞：蜂巢格網、直剪試驗、拉拔試驗、三軸
壓縮試驗。 
 
Abstract 
The aim of this research is to assess the 
stability and deformation of geocell-reinforced 
retaining structures, including reinforced walls and 
slopes. Laboratory tests were employed to 
investigate the mechanical characteristics of 
geocell-reinforced sand, and the parameters 
obtained from the test results will be used for 
numerical analysis on geocell-reinforced retaining 
structures. The content of this report includes 
literature review, development of a large clipper for 
geocell samples, modification of the large direct 
shear test equipment, conducting experiments on 
interfacial properties between different materials, 
including geocell/soil, geocell/geogrid, and 
geocell-reinforced sand layers. In addition, triaxial 
tests were also performed on single-cell and 
multiple-cell reinforced sands. The test results 
showed that the interfacial friction angle between 
the geocell-reinforced-sand layers was close to the 
friction angle of the sand. It means the frictional 
behavior was predominately by the soil rather than 
the reinforcement. The results of pullout test 
demonstrated that the failure mode under low 
normal stress was due to insufficient anchorage 
force, and that w under high normal stress was due 
to breakage of reinforcement. Moreover, the triaxial 
compression tests on geocell-reinforced sand 
showed that the confinement effect provided by the 
cell increased the shear strength but decreased the 
volume change of the sample. 
 
Keywords: geocells, direct shear test, pullout test, 
triaxial compression test. 
 
一、研究目的 
蜂巢格網(geocells)一般是以高密度聚乙烯
（HDPE）或聚乙烯（PE）材料，加抗化學酸鹼、
抗紫外線、耐老化等成分，經熔接法等加工程序
後，製成的蜂巢格室之結構，其具有許多優點，
初期以應用在加強路基之承載力（張家豪等人，
2004、謝啟萬等人，1999）。近年來，人們對生
態與環保的觀念提升，也更能接受新的工法，為
因應此一趨勢，同時兼顧加勁結構之穩定性及變
形性的考量，故針對一些問題需要作更深入的探
討: 
(1) 蜂巢格網加勁擋土結構之設計，目前常利用
邊坡穩定分析之極限平衡法分析，尚未考慮
到蜂巢格網的互制行為。此外，蜂巢格網加
勁擋土牆或加勁邊坡亦應有不同的設計理
念。 
(2) 因蜂巢格網格室較大，無法以傳統之三軸試
驗儀進行試驗，故其應力-應變行為及設計參
數不易求得，以致無法進行變形性分析。 
(3) 多格效應是否可有由單格試驗所得的結果加
以推估，仍未清楚。文獻中的多格室研究 
(Rajagopal et al., 1999; We sseloo et al., 2009) 
是在三軸試體同一截面積下分成許多小格，
與實際情形為每一格室之尺寸均相同並不符
合。

三、研究方法  
本研究之試驗內容分為兩部分，首先利用大
型直剪儀進行蜂巢格網之層間及其與土壤加勁
材間的界面力學特性探討，再利用三軸壓縮試驗
探討蜂巢格網圍束效應，並討論單格室與多格室
圍束效應的關係。以下針對已執行之第一期研究
成果說明。

四、結論 
    本階段完成工作項目如下：相關文獻收集
與討論、蜂巢格網專用夾具研發與製作、利用直
剪試驗儀完成材料界面摩擦特性試驗、利用三軸
試驗儀完成單格與多格蜂巢格網壓縮性的探討。
茲將各項試驗所獲致的結論歸納於後。 
1. 蜂巢格網加勁層間的摩擦角與純砂土的摩
擦角相近，此表示加勁層之間的摩擦性質主
要由土壤所控制；而試驗過程監測試體沉陷
量，發現加勁加勁試體能有效降低沉陷量達
40％。 
2. 地工格網之拉拔試驗於低正向應力時，加勁
材拉力隨正向應力呈線性增加，此時破壞模
式為拉出破壞，當正向應力大於18 kPa時，
加勁材於拉出過程會產生斷裂的現象，破壞
模式為加勁材拉斷破壞。 
3. 於三軸壓縮試驗中，由於單格蜂巢格網圍束
效應使土壤不論是於承受高圍壓或低圍壓
的狀態，相較於未加勁的土壤均能產生較高
的強度。在體積變化關係上，含格網的試體
於軸向應變大於 10％時產生一較為明顯的
反曲點，推測是因為加勁材的圍束效應限制
了土壤的側向位移。 
4. 在剪力強度參數方面，純紗與含加勁材料的
摩擦角均約為 φ＝37°，而有加勁試體的視
凝聚力 c＝62 kPa，此說明蜂巢格網的加勁
能增加土壤的視凝聚力，因而提高土壤的抗
剪強度。 
5. 於三軸壓縮試驗中，多格蜂巢格網試驗結果，
相較於單格試驗摩擦角(φ)提高 4°，視凝聚
力(c)降低 47.5kPa，此項結果與單格試驗結
果僅增加視凝聚力，而摩擦角不變的結論迥
異，將於後續研究作進一步的探討。

INFILTRATION 
RONG-HER CHEN*, KWO JANE KUO**, CHIH-MING CHANG*** 
*.Professor, Department of Civil Engineering, National Taiwan University, E-mail: rongherchen@ntu.edu.tw, 886-2-23629851

ABSTRACT 
Slope failure is commonly caused by rainfall infiltration due to increase in pore-water pressure within the slope. In order to understand 
the failure mechanism of granular soil slopes, a model slope subjected to rainfall infiltration was developed and employed to perform 
experiment on sandy soils. Different geological conditions, fines contents of sand, and rainfall intensities were considered as variables. 
Infiltration of rainfall was simulated by an overland flow infiltrating uniformly into a platform on the top of the slope. During the experiment, 
the variations in pore water pressure and volumetric water content in the soil were measured. The characteristics of the failure mechanism and 
the responses of pore pressure and water content in four model slopes were observed and discussed.  
Key Words: model test, slope, stability, granular soil, infiltration 
 
INTRODUCTION 
Many slope failures were caused by infiltration of water that 
induced increase in pore-water pressure and seepage force in soil 
(Lump, 1975; Brand, 1982; Iverson & Major, 1986; Chen et al., 
1999; Kim et al., 2004; Chen et al. 2004; Chen et al., 2009). The 
stability of an unsaturated soil slope can be dramatically altered as 
the soil becomes gradually saturated, even resulting in a slope 
failure. The collapsed soil may turn into a flow if there is plenty of 
water in the soil along with favourable geological condition.  
Using model tests to study rainfall induced slope failures have 
been conducted by a few research. Wang & Sassa (2003) 
performed tests on silica sand of different initial dry densities and 
fines contents to investigate the effects of grain size on pore-water 
pressure generation and the failure behaviour of rainfall-induced 
landslides. Moriwaki et al. (2004) conducted a full-scale 
experiment to clarify the failure process of landslide triggered by 
rainfall. The soil was loose sand with a relative density of 35 % and 
an initial water content of 8 %. The flume was subjected to rainfall 
of an intensity of 100 mm/hr. The sliding process of the slope was 
recorded and evaluated by measuring the displacement of slope 
surface, and the piezometric level and pore-water pressure within 
the soil. 
A set of laboratory-scale experiment for studying rainfall 
induced slope failure was performed by Tohari et al. (2007). The 
soils were sand and residual granite soil of various relative 
densities and initial water contents. They employed two types of 
water supply in the study; one was a rainfall simulator designed to 
produce an effective rainfall intensity of approximately 100 mm/hr, 
and the other was a water tank which could vary the rising rate of 
water level within the slope. Huang et al. (2008) also conducted 
model tests on sandy soil slopes to look into the rainfall-induced 
failure process in the slope. In addition, they observed and reported 
the characteristics of cumulative solid discharge versus time. 
Chen et al. (2004) investigated the triggering mechanism for a 
hazardous mudflow caused by a typhoon which delivered 
approximately 300 mm/day of precipitation. The destructive 50-m 
long mudflow rushed down a hill without any forewarning from a 
man-made platform of 30 m x 80 m right at the top of the mudflow. 
This funnel-shaped mudflow of a volume of 2,000 m3 damaged 
three houses below and resulted in five deaths. According to the 
test results of the samples taken from the erosion gully, most of 
soils belong to silty and clayey sand. The bedding, which appears 
in the erosion gully, has a dip angle ranging from 30 to 60° toward 
the toe of the slope. Besides, the cause of the disaster was found 
likely due to the malfunction of the drainage system around the
