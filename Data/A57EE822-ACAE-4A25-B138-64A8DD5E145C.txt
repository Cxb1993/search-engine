Ⅰ 
A STUDY OF SYNTHESIS AND LIGHT-EMITTING 
PROPERTIES OF NANO METAL IONS-DOPED 
STRONTIUM ZIRCONATE POWDER  
( PROJECT NUMBER：NSC99-2221-E-151-062)  
BAI-BIN WANG1 WEIN-DUO YANG1 CHING SHIEH HSIEH2 
 1DEPARTMENT OF CHEMICAL AND MATERIALS ENGINEERING, NATIONAL 
       KAOHSIUNG UNIVERSITY OF APPLIED SCIENCES  
2DEPARTMENT OF APPLIED CHEMISTRY AND MATERIALS SCIENCE, 
FOOYIN UNIVERSITY   
ABSTRACT 
THIS STUDY PREPARED MANGANESE-DOPED STRONTIUM ZIRCONATE 
PHOSPHOR AND EMPLOYED X-RAY POWDER DIFFRACTOMETER AND 
FLUORESCENCE SPECTROMETRY TO ANALYZE THE CRYSTAL STRUCTURE AND 
LIGHT-EMITTING PROPERTIES OF THE PHOSPHOR. WE EXPLORED THE 
INFLUENCE OF CALCINATION TEMPERATURE AND MANGANESE CONTENT ON 
THE EMISSION INTENSITY OF THE PHOSPHOR AS WELL AS THE MECHANISM 
ASSOCIATED WITH ENERGY TRANSFER. X-RAY POWDER DIFFRACTOMETER 
WAS USED TO IDENTIFY THE CRYSTALLINE STRUCTURE OF THE 
MANGANESE-DOPED STRONTIUM ZIRCONATE PHOSPHOR. FLUORESCENCE 
SPECTROMETRY IDENTIFIED PRIMARY BROADBAND PEAKS AT 
APPROXIMATELY 457NM IN THE EXCITATION SPECTRUM AND AT 
APPROXIMATELY 541NM IN THE EMISSION SPECTRUM. EMISSION INTENSITY 
FIRST INCREASED AND THEN DECREASED WITH AN INCREASE IN CALCINATION 
TEMPERATURE, REACHING A MAXIMUM AT 600℃. SIMILARLY, EMISSION 
INTENSITY INCREASED AND THEN DECREASED WITH AN INCREASE IN 
MANGANESE DOPING. AT DOPING OF 0.006 MOL, THE EMISSION INTENSITY OF 
THE PHOSPHOR REACHED THE MAXIMUM VALUE. THE MECHANISM OF 
ENERGY TRANSFER BETWEEN MANGANESE IONS WAS DETERMINED TO BE 
ELECTRIC DIPOLE AND QUADRUPOLE INTERACTION.   
KEYWORDS : MANGANESE-DOPED STRONTIUM ZIRCONATE PHOSPHOR, X-RAY 
POWDER DIFFRACTOMETER, FLUORESCENCE SPECTROMETRY, 
MECHANISM OF ENERGY TRANSFER, ELECTRIC DIPOLE AND 
QUADRUPOLE INTERACTION.

放射強度的影響與摻雜錳含量的改變對鋯酸鍶摻雜錳之螢光粉放射強度的影響，且根
據 Dexter 能量共振理論研究錳離子之間能量傳遞的特性。 
二、實驗方法  
本研究實驗流程圖如圖 1 所示。首先先將醋酸鍶 Sr(CH3COO)2．1/2H2O (99.99%)，
氯化氧鋯 ZrCl2O．8H2O (99.99%)和醋酸錳 Mn(CH3COO)2．4H2O (99.99%)精確的秤重，
然後分別溶於去離子水中，去得到一個含有(錳離子、鍶離子、鋯離子)的澄清溶液，接
著檸檬酸 HOOCCH2C(OH)(COOH)CH2COOH (99.99%)和少量的纖維素 (C6H10O5)n 
(99.99%)被秤重後，一併加入含有(錳離子、鍶離子、鋯離子)的澄清溶液之中，並混合
攪伴，最後得到一個白色溶液，白色溶液進行水熱處理 3 小時，在於 100℃的烘箱中乾
燥，將乾燥後的粉末放入氧化鋁坩鍋中研磨，在於 900℃的高溫爐中鍛燒 4 小時，爐冷
至室溫後得到產物。

四、結論 
本研究成功地合成了鋯酸鍶掺雜錳之螢光粉。在儀器分析上，使用了 X 光粉末繞射
儀和螢光光譜儀對鋯酸鍶掺雜錳之螢光粉的晶體結構與光譜特性進行分析，並去了解
不同的鍛燒溫度與不同的錳含量對放射強度的影響以及錳離子之間能量傳遞機制，研
究結果表明：（一）所合成的鋯酸鍶掺雜錳之螢光粉的晶體結構屬於正交晶系，具有空
間群 Pnma{62}，且晶格參數分別為 a=5.818 Å，b=8.204 Å，c=5.797 Å，v=276.7 Å3。（二）
在激發光譜中可以發現激發峰值的波長是位於 457 奈米。（三）在放射光譜中可以發現
放射峰值的波長是位於 541 奈米。（四）隨著鍛燒溫度的升高，螢光粉的放射強度也有
所增強，但當鍛燒溫度達到 600℃時，螢光粉的放射強度會達到最大值，而後提高鍛燒
溫度，螢光粉的放射強度已不隨溫度的提高而增強。（五）螢光粉的放射強度會隨掺雜
錳含量的增大而逐漸增強，當掺雜錳含量的莫耳數為 0.006 時，螢光粉的放射強度會達
到最大值，而後放射強度隨掺雜錳含量的增加而減小。（六）錳離子之間能量傳遞機制
經由 Dexter 能量共振理論證實為電偶極與電四極之間的交互作用。

Introduction
One-dimensional TiO2 nanotube arrays were fabricated by the
electrochemical anodization of Ti foils, to improve charge transfe
r and hinder charge recombination. Many research groups have
concentrated on the DSSCs of TiO2 nanotube arrays, with TiO2 n
anotubes as the anode and a Pt counter electrode as the cathode.
Dye-sensitized solar cells currently have two significant disadvan
tages - low photoelectric conversion efficiency and high loss. Con
siderable efforts have been expended on enhancing photo conver
sion efficiency. On increasing the tube length, modifying the TiO2
nanotube arrays, use of electrolytes and dyes, use of front-side ill
umination and treatment the TiO2 nanotubes have been attempte
d.

Conclusion
We have employed an optimal surface treatment technique to im
prove the efficiency of dye-sensitized solar cells, in which the surfa
ce of the TiO2 nanotubes were treated with titanium (IV) n-butoxi
de, using a hydrothermal method, in combination with oxygen pla
sma treatment. We note that dye adsorption is increased by TnB a
nd O2 plasma treatments from the UV-vis absorption spectra. The
effect of combined treatment can enhance the value of Voc and Jsc,
resulting in increased almost double the conversion efficiency relat
ive to untreated TiO2 nanotube arrays. This technique offers a fea
sible method to improve the efficiency of DSSCs, which provides a
good idea for further applications.
