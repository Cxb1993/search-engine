實驗結果如下：隨著加熱溫度及加熱

質則呈減少變化，a 值〈紅色度〉隨著

濁安定性，實驗結果如下：不同 pH 值
(pH2~10)酪梨汁的混濁安定性趨勢大
致與果膠含量與黏度趨勢有相關，越
往極端 pH 值的酪梨汁特別是酸性環
境下其混濁安定性越差。

Abstract 
 
Avocado juice has a bright yellow 
appearance, smooth mouth feel, 
excellent flavor and high nutritional 
value so that it is greatly accepted by 
consumers. But owing to its high 
content of crude lipid and crude protein, 
unstable phenomenon like phase 
separation, creaming and coagulation is 
easily produced with the treatment of 
bleaching, heating, homogenization and 
acidizing. This study examines the 
effects of different heating degree and 
heating time on physicochemical quality 
and the effects of acidizing or alkalizing 
on turbidity stability of avocado juice. 
The results show that increasing heating 
time and temperature causes the 
decreasing of browning index, turbidity, 
lightness, b value and pH value. In 
addition that raising the heating 
temperature cause protein denaturation, 
thus the content of soluble protein and 
particle size changed. The treatment of 
acidizing or alkalizing show that the 
turbidity stability is related to the 
content of soluble pectin and viscosity. 
Under the acidity condition, the 
turbidity stability is worse than the other

(圖五)，造成其含量下降。

加熱時間及溫度增加，其明亮度下
降。整體而言，酪梨汁經 70℃以上溫
度，對酪梨汁品質影響較大，此實驗
結果，未來可供業者對於酪梨汁殺菁
或殺菌條件參考。
