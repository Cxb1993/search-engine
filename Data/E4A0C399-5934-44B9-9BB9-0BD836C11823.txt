電子業去光阻液回收再利用之研究-離子液體萃取法 
 
A Study on the Recovery of Stripper Agent in Electronic Industries-Ionic Liquid 
Extraction Approach 
 
NSC 98-2221-E-159-009 
 
Jui-Tang Chen *, Shin-Wei Ho, and Mei-Hong Wu 
 
Department of Chemical and Materials Engineering, Ming-Hsin University of Science and Technology, 
Hsin-chu, Hsin-feng, 304, Taiwan. 
 
*Corresponding Author’s E-mail: dale_chen@must.edu.tw 
 
關鍵詞：液液相平衡、室溫離子液體、水溶液、雲點法 
中文摘要 
室溫離子液體(Room Temperature Ionic Liquids，簡稱 RTILs 或離子液體)由於幾乎沒有蒸氣壓，已
取代傳統揮發性有機溶劑被廣泛地應用至各化學化工領域上，如電化學、合成反應、程序分離…等等。
為了發展離子液體在上述領域的應用，研究離子液體與其他化合物或溶劑的相平衡是非常重要的。本研
究計畫已完成四組三成份含去光阻液與離子液體之液液相平衡數據量測，所完成的系統為水＋1-丁基-3-
甲基咪唑六氟磷酸鹽(離子液體，[BMIM+][PF6-])＋N-甲基吡咯酮(NMP)、二甲基亞碸(DMSO)、二乙二
醇單丁醚(DGBE)或單乙醇銨 MEA)等去光阻液(Stripper) 與離子液體之液液相平衡數據，量測條件為常
壓，溫度介於 283.15 K 與 323.15 K。相邊界曲線數據量測方法則採用雲點法來進行。 
 
Keywords: Liquid-liquid equilibria; Room temperature ionic liquids; Aqueous; Cloud point method  
 
Abstract 
 
Room temperature ionic liquids (RTILs) have been widely employed as potential green solvents to replace 
volatile organic solvents in electrochemistry, reaction, and separation processes due to they are almost zero 
vapor pressure. In order to develop ionic liquids for these applications, it is very important to understand their 
phase behavior with other compounds. In this research, liquid–liquid equilibrium data have been measured for 
four ternary systems of water + 1-butyl-3-methylimidazolium hexafluorophosphate ([BMIM+][PF6-]) + 
N-methyl-2-pyrrolidone (NMP), dimethyl sulfoxide (DMSO), diethylene glycol monobutyl ether (DGBE), or 
monoethanolamine (MEA) at temperatures from 283.15 K to 323.15 K under atmospheric pressure. The cloud 
point method was used to determine the LLE binodal curve data.
