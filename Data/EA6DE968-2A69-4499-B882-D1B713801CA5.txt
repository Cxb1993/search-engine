A virtual backbone has great benefits to routing and 
management of networks. We may impose a virtual 
backbone to support short path routing, fault-
tolerant routing, multicasting, and radio 
broadcasting, etc.  
 
    Hence, a network CDS with good network topologies 
benefits improvement of performance. In this 
research, we investigate into the minimum CDS of some 
specific networks, such as hypercubes and star 
graphs. In order to construct a virtual backbone with 
good topologies, we also investigate into the 
diagnosability, super connectivity, cycle embedding, 
and internally disjoint paths of some specific 
networks, such as star graphs, augmented cubes, 
hypercubes, folded hypercubes, and extended 
hypercubes. These results will help to construct a 
virtual backbone of a network with high performance.

一、 前言 
一個有線(wired)或無線(wireless)連結網路(interconnection network)的拓譜架構(topological 
structure)通常可用一個圖形(graph)G=(V,E)來表示，其圖形中的節點(vertices)集合 V 代表連結網路
中的處理器(processors)、而節點與節點之間的邊(edges)集合 E 代表連結網路中的鏈結(links)[56]。
圖形理論可用來分析許多重要的網路拓譜性質，例如連通支配點 (connected dominating 
set)[2,4,23,31,45,47,51] 、可偵錯(diagnosability)[7,33,34]、直徑(diameter)[48,53]、連通度(connectivity) 
[8,14,15,24,35,42,58]、漢米爾頓(Hamiltonicity)[9,16,17,29,49,53]、泛圈(pancyclicity) [16,30,46]、節
點間最大鏈結集合(maximum number of edges joining vertices)[1,59]等性質，這些性質在許多文獻中
曾被探討。以下我們使用標準的圖論定義[5]，並且交替使用網路(networks)與圖形(graphs)這兩個
名詞。 
本計畫為吾人 99 年度國科會計畫「連通支配點及其網路上的應用」(NSC 99-2221-E-159-014)
之延伸，執行 99 年度計畫過程中，發現關於「連通支配點」(connected dominating set)尚有許多相
關延伸的性質，例如可診斷值(diagnosability)、m-連通(m-connected)、r-跳躍 (r-hop)、k-倍支配點
(k-tuple dominating set)、邊-關鍵(edge critical)等，若能將這些性質加入連通支配點一併考量，對
於網路的繞徑、成本、及可靠度等，都有相當程度的助益，因此本計畫將探究以上連通支配點之
延伸相關問題於特定連結網路，關於連通支配點的問題描述如下：令 G=(V,E)為一個連通圖
(connected graph)，給定一組節點子集合 RV，圖形 G 中的任一節點 v 若不屬於 R，則屬於 R 中
某節點的相鄰節點，那麼我們就稱此節點子集合 R 為一組支配點(dominating set)，簡稱為 DS。此
支配點(dominating set) R 所產生的子圖(induced subgraph)(簡稱為 G[R])若為一個連通圖(connected 
graph)，則我們稱支配點為連通支配點(connected dominating set)，簡稱為 CDS。圖 1 為 CDS 的例
子。

三、 研究目的 
一個網路的虛擬骨幹(virtual backbone)若本身有較好的網路拓樸性質，將有助於網路整體效能
的提升，在本計畫中，我們對一些特定網路，例如超立方體(hypercube)與星圖(star graph)，求得其
最小連通支配點(minimal CDS, MCDS)；也對其他網路圖形，深入研究有助於建構良好虛擬骨幹的
網路拓樸性質，例如可診斷度(diagnosability)、超級連通度(super connectivity)、迴圈嵌入(cycle 
embedding)、可診斷的連通支配點(diagnosable CDS)、r-跳躍連通支配點(r-hop CDS)等，這些結果
將有助於建構網路較高效能的虛擬骨幹。

    另外，對於迴圈嵌入(cycle embedding)與繞徑(routing)相關議題，我們也有一系列的成果，這
些優良的網路拓譜性質，也將有助於網路骨幹的的繞徑與管理。在連結網路中迴圈嵌入(cycle 
embedding)是熱門的一個研究問題，在網路中嵌入不同長度的迴圈能夠有利於並行程序的執行效
率，曾有許多文獻討論嵌入各種長度迴圈[6,20,25,28,41,57]。我們對於(generalized recursive 
circulant graphs)，已能對其網路作漢米爾頓分解(Hamiltonian decomposability)，對於超立方體
(hypercube)與折疊超立方體(folded hypercube)，我們對其各種長度的迴圈嵌入得到部分的結果
[10,11]。而關於繞徑部分，我們針對超立方體的延伸圖形，例如延伸超立方體 (extended 
hypercube)，提出演算法，找出任兩個節點之間最多且最短的內部互斥路徑(internally disjoint paths) 
[50]。未來，我們希望產生更深入、更精闢的研究成果。

    今年的 The 2012 International Conference on Software and Computer Applications(ICSCA 2012)
在新加坡的 Quality Hotel舉行，此會議是屬於 International Association of Computer Science and

4. Concluding Remarks 
The hypercubes Qn and star graphs Sn are both recursively constructed networks, and they have many 
attractive properties. This paper demonstrates upper bounds of cardinality of the MCDS of hypercubes and 
star graphs. For the lower dimensional hypercubes and star graphs, we give lower bounds of cardinality of 
their MCDS. Therefore, we have conjectures that |MCDS(Qn)| = 2n−2 + 2 and |MCDS(Sn)| = 2(n−1)! for n ≥ 3.

(中文) 本計畫「連通支配點應用於網路之延伸研究」是以連通支配點(Connected 
Dominating Set) (CDS)為基礎的繞徑方式，把CDS當作該網路的一個虛擬骨幹
(virtual backbone)，對於網路的繞徑與管理有很大的益處，例如我們可以透過
虛擬骨幹來進行短路徑繞徑(short path routing) 、容錯繞徑(fault-tolerant 
routing)、多重播送(multicasting)、與無線電廣播(radio broadcasting)等等，
此外，虛擬骨幹於無線網路中也可減少通訊費用(communication overhead)、增
加頻寬效能(bandwidth efficiency)、減少能量的消耗(energy consumption)。
未來之網路或IC科技若能加入這些容錯拓譜性質，將有助於效能的提升。
(英文) This research “Extended Research of Connected Dominating Set and Its Applications on 
Networks” focuses on routing which is based on the Connected Dominating Set (CDS). 
The CDS can be served as a virtual backbone of a network, and it can be helpful to 
routing and management of networks. We may impose a virtual backbone to support 
short path routing, fault-tolerant routing, multicasting, and radio broadcasting, etc. 
Furthermore, a virtual backbone of a wireless network may reduce communication 
overhead, increase bandwidth efficiency, and decrease energy consumption. The results 
can be applied to network or IC technologies to improve their performance.
