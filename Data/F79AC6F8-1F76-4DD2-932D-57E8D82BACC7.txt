一、計畫中英文摘要 
豐田(Toyota)的煞車失靈事件確認了企業確保消費者安全的社會責任；鴻海(富士康)的員工跳樓事
件提醒了企業確保職工身心健康的社會責任，而英國石油(BP)的墨西哥灣漏油事件則是突顯了企業保
育環境的社會責任。的確，企業永續經營必須是兼顧組織經濟責任、環保責任、社會責任之三重底線
(Triple Bottom Lines)的管理理念，近年來備受產業與學術界的關注，也是政府發展產業政策的重要指
標，另一方面系統化的品質管理手法自90年代風行至今，儼然已是產業界追求營運升級的必要手段。
據此，運用系統化品質管理手法有效實踐企業的社會責任遂成為重要的課題。 
國際標準組織（ISO）已經於2010年5月發佈史無前例的企業社會責任管理標準最終草案ISO 
26000，欲導引組織運用系統化的管理來實踐企業的社會責任。本研究根據ISO 26000最新標準草案的
內容探討這一套全新標準對合作企業的應用內涵。研究過程中我們結合過程導向的品質管理模式及企
業社會責任的趨勢與原理，運用ISO 26000的系統模式與專家問卷調查的分析結果，有效推導企業社會
責任管理系統的過程模式，最後，藉由合作企業所聘請之產、學專家的焦點團體座談與研討來檢定所
發展企業社會責任管理系統的有效性與實用性。 
關鍵字：ISO 26000、過程導向、品質管理、企業社會責任、焦點團體座談 
The “brake incidence” in Toyota U.S. does confirm the Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) in 
delivering consumer safety; the “suicide jump” in Foxconn has just reminded us about the CSR in assuring 
employee health, and BP’s “Gulf oil spill” does alert business worldwide about CSR in protecting 
environment. Indeed, the sustainable development of a company lies on the Triple Bottom Lines of economic, 
environmental and social responsibility, and this has become a tendency in both academy and industry while 
governments always take it as priority. On the other hand the systematic principle of quality management has 
been effective in upgrading business performance ever since the 90’s. Accordingly, how to implement an 
effective system for managing CSR in an organization by systematic quality management has become an 
important subject. 
The International Organization for Standardization (ISO) has published an ever first standard for guiding 
CSR, the final draft of ISO 26000 in this May. We did provide organizations with system framework in 
implementing CSR, and our research project based this standard to explore the application domain for the 
joint-research company. In our research, we were employ the process approach of quality management and 
the principles of CSR incorporating the system model of ISO 26000 and the result of questionnaire survey by 
experts to develop the process model of CSR management in practices. At last, we will validate the 
developed model by the focus group consisting of those experts from both academy and industry who are 
designated by the joint-research company.
