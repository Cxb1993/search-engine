normal strength. Hence, fire protection in concrete 
tunnels is essential. This research presents a 
double–layer composite fire resistant concrete for 
tunnel lining. The outward layer composes of 
lightweight concrete (LWC) with P.P fibers and steel 
fiber to avoid the spalling of concrete and to reduce 
the input of the heat while it exposed to severe fire 
exposure. The inward layer is made of normal weight 
concrete (NC). In this project, the main experiments 
are: the splitting strength test and shear strength 
test of the boundary between LWC and NC, flextural 
strength test and compressive strength test of the 
composite concrete. The test results showed that the 
mechanics of the double-layer composite concrete cast 
in place has not much difference with that of the 
homogeneous concrete at the same water binder ratio. 
But the splitting strength and shear strength of the 
double-layer precast composite concrete showed the 
decline trend depending on the roughness of precast 
concrete surface. This research results showed that 
the double-layer composite fire resistant concrete 
for tunnel lining is feasible and can be used by the 
industry and government to enhance the tunnel fire 
safety.

四、 研究方法
雙層複合混凝土可分為場鑄及輕質骨料混凝土(LWC)預鑄兩種。場鑄的優點為
介面粘著力佳，但垂直介面及頂拱施工複雜。LWC 先預鑄，可代替隧道混凝土襯
砌的鋼模，外層 LWC 版組立完成後再澆置內層 NC，施工較易，但須作介面處理，
以增加其與 NC 之粘著力。本計畫特別著重 LWC 與 NC 介面粘著強度的測試，分為
場鑄及 LWC 預鑄兩大部分。
