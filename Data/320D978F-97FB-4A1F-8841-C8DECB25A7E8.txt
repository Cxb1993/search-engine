一、 中文摘要 
本專題研究係利用延伸式場效電晶體之架
構結合射頻濺鍍法及網版印刷法，於可撓式塑
膠基板上備製酸鹼感測器。於酸鹼量測顯示其
具良好之感測度50.9mV/pH及線性度0.998，並
藉由遲滯檢測得知遲滯量會隨著迴路時間
(Loop Time)與迴路路徑之增加而上升，且於鹼
性溶液中之遲滯量較酸性溶液之遲滯量大，此
外將鈣離子感測膜備製於酸鹼感測器上，於
1M~10-5M之氯化鈣水溶液中量測，其感測度高
達29.43 mV/pCa，線性度0.984，顯示本專題研
究計畫備製之鈣離子感測器不僅感測度佳、範
圍廣，且因採用網版印刷技術備製感測器，故
具有備製簡單、成本低、高量產及可拋棄式之
優點。 
英文摘要 
In this project, the pH sensor is prepared with 
the separative extended-gate field effect transistor 
(SEGFET) that based on PET substrate and 
fabricated by using radio frequency sputtering and 
screen printing technology. The sensitivity and 
linearity of the pH sensor are 50.9mV and 0.998, 
respectively. According to the measurement of 
hysteresis, the value of hysteresis is increased with 
increasing the loop time and loop route, and the 
value of hysteresis in alkali solution is larger than 
the acid solution. In addition, the calcium ion 
sensing film is based on pH sensor, and is 
measured in the CaCl2 solutions from 1M to 
10-5M, the sensitivity and linearity were 
29.43mV/pCa and 0.984, respectively. This 
project shows the advantages of a calcium ion 
sensor such as high sensitivity, wide measuring 
rage, manufactured easily, low cost, high yield 
and disposable.

度，分別為 1M、10-1 M、10-2 M、10-3 M、10-4 M、
10-5 M，再以去離子水(Deionized water , D.I. 
water)以倍數稀釋法調配之。其步驟如下所示： 
(1)  調配 pCa1 之鈣離子待測溶液，以電子秤
量取 0.7351g 之氯化鈣(CaCl2)加入 50ml
之離子水，以磁石攪拌器加以攪拌均勻，
此係 1M 之氯化鈣待測溶液。 
(2) 取 5ml 之 1M 氯化鈣待測溶液，加入 45ml
之離子水，以磁石攪拌器加以攪拌均勻，
此係 10-1M 之氯化鈣待測溶液。 
(3) 取 5ml 之 10-1M 氯化鈣待測溶液，加入
45ml 之離子水，以磁石攪拌器加以攪拌均
勻，此係 10-2 M 之氯化鈣待測溶液。 
(4) 取 5ml 之 10-2 M 氯化鈣待測溶液，加入
45ml 之離子水，以磁石攪拌器加以攪拌均
勻，此係 10-3 M 之氯化鈣待測溶液。 
(5) 取 5ml 之 10-3 M 氯化鈣待測溶液，加入
45ml 之離子水，以磁石攪拌器加以攪拌均
勻，此係 10-4 M 之氯化鈣待測溶液。 
(6) 取 5ml 之 10-4 M 氯化鈣待測溶液，加入

英文： 
A calcium ion sensing unit is provided.  The 
calcium ion sensing unit includes a substrate, a silver paste 
formed thereon, a carbon paste formed on the silver paste, a 
miniaturized reference electrode installed on the silver 
paste, a ruthenium dioxide film formed the carbon paste, a 
calcium ion sensing film formed on the ruthenium dioxide 
film, and an insulation layer covering the calcium ion 
sensing unit,  exposing the calcium ion sensing film and the 
miniaturized reference electrode.  The invention also 
provides a method for fabricating a calcium ion sensing 
unit,  and a sensing system including the calcium ion 
sensing unit .
