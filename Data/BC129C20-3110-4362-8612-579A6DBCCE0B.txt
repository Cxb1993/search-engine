to assist collaborators in making effective information shared understanding is becoming an 
important research issue in collaborative design. 
The research adopts human-centered approaches to explore the capabilities of 
information technologies in collaborative architectural design. This study focuses on how to 
achieve effective information shared understanding by incorporating information technologies 
from human-centered point of views. The research results include: 
1) To study human-centered communication interface design: The research explores 
the effective issues during asynchronous design communication from organization 
viewpoints. The research will also study the interface design issues by examining 
the theories of human communication interface (HCI). 
2) To develop human-centered information visualization tool: This study develops a 
visualization tool to filter and retrieve needed design information for design 
participants. The visualization tool is designed to enhance the shared 
understanding capabilities by providing a semantic web information structure. 
3) To develop collaborative design system with effective information shared 
understanding capabilities: The research develops a collaborative design system by 
incorporating visualization techniques with human-centered interface design. The 
protocol analysis will be adopted to evaluate the system performance from 
cognition science viewpoint. 
4) To propose a reliable mechanism for evaluating the effectiveness of information 
shared understanding: This study adopts semantic differential methods to evaluate 
the effectiveness of shared understanding that this research intends to achieve by 
conducting design experiments.
