含自組裝矽量子點之奈米孔洞氧化矽複合材料閘極之電晶體技術 
Transistor technology with gates of self-assembled nanostructures 
of silicon quantum-dots in mesoporous silica 
矽量子點近紅外光電晶體偵測器 
計畫編號： NSC 97 - 2221 - E - 492 - 014 
執行期間：97 年 8 月 1 日     至     98 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：謝嘉民 國家奈米元件實驗室研究員 
一、中文摘要 
含自組裝矽量子點之奈米孔洞氧化矽複合
材料閘極之電晶體技術應用於發展量子點
近紅外光電晶體偵測器 
本研究報告發展出一個完全以矽基材
料組成的金氧半場效電晶體應用於偵測近
紅外光。具矽奈米微晶奈米孔洞氧化矽複合
材料是經由奈米孔洞氧化矽材料填充矽奈
米微晶並將此薄膜夾在兩個氧化層中形成
一個完整的電晶體閘極介電結構，並利用多
晶矽薄膜作為閘極之電極，應用在金氧半場
效電晶體上做為光偵測器，在常用的通訊波
長1.55微米(μm)我們得到高達2.8A/W的光
響應度。如此高的響應度是由於電晶體電流
放大的機制所致。 
關鍵字：近紅外光偵測器，矽量子點，矽奈
米微晶，奈米孔洞氧化矽。 
二、英文摘要 
Transistor technology with gates of 
self-assembled nanostructures of Si QDs in 
mesoporous silica for near infrared Si QDs 
MOSFET photodetector 
A fully Si-based metal-oxide-semiconductor 
field-effect transistor is demonstrated for the 
detection of near infrared light. Si 
nanocrystals (nc-Si) are synthesized in the 
nano pore channels of mesoporous silica (MS) 
inserted between two oxide layers to form a 
complete gate structure of polycrystalline 
Si/SiO2/nc-Si-in-MS/SiO2 with a 
polycrystalline Si electrode. Illuminating the 
gate with near-infrared light, a 
photoresponsivity as high as 2.8 A/W at 1.55 
μm can be achieved. The improved 
photoresponsivity is attributed to from optical 
transitions via interface states and a current 
amplification mechanism of the device. 
Keyword： near-infrared detectors， silicon 
quantum dot ， silicon nanocrystals ， and 
mesoporous silica。 
三、計畫的緣由與目的 
矽光電子元件的發展是光電領域中最
備受關注的由於它能完全地相容整合於矽
微電子產業。然而，塊材矽因為能隙的關係
並不適合應用在通訊波長 1.3 微米到 1.6 微
米之間作為光通訊的光源偵測器。近來，利
用奈米技術發展出多功能性的材料引起許
多研究學者的興趣。基於這樣的概念，結合
元件的構造設計或是改變材料的結構，產生
一種新的領域—結構性光電學應運而生。 
矽奈米微晶(nanocrystalline Si, nc-Si )的
光電特性與塊材矽是截然不同的，雖然矽是
非直接能隙的半導體材料，但是我們在之前
的研究裡發現將矽奈米微晶(nc-Si)埋在奈
米 孔 洞 氧 化 矽 材 料 (mesoporous silica, 
MS)，具有高效率的光激螢光[1]並且可以增
強對可見光範圍波長的光電響應[2]。金氧半
結構所組成的高速矽光學模組元件已被發
展且被認為是矽光電最核心的元件[3]。依據
此理念，因此以矽基材料發展的近紅外光光

六、計畫成果自評部份 
我們首度利用自行組裝的奈米孔洞二
氧化矽當板模，於其中合成高密度三維分佈
的矽（或鍺）奈米量子點，形成矽奈米量子
點/二氧化矽新型奈米薄膜，發展出近紅外
光奈米晶矽金氧半場效電晶體光偵測器，突
破了矽材料的基本能隙限制，由於矽奈米微
晶與孔洞氧化矽間的界面能態使它具有能
吸收近紅外光(1.3μm<λ<1.6μm)的能力，而
由近紅外光產生的光激載子能增強閘極電
壓的影響力造成反轉層的電子濃度提升增
強通道導電性。我們成功的利用此光電晶體
的機制放大了在波長 1.55μm 近紅外光的光
響應度高達為 2.8A/W。

2008 IEEE Conference on Nanotechnology 國際學術會議論文 
Broadband anti-reflective structure with mesoporous silica of low refractive index in Si 
solar panels 
Jia-Min Shieh*, et al., oral presentation, 8th IEEE Conference on Nanotechnology (IEEE NANO2008). 
 
 
Abstract 
Mesoporus silica dielectrics, which revealed low 
refractive index (n~1.26), were introduced together 
with Si3N4 layers to be double-layer-based 
graded-refractive-index antireflectors for silicon 
solar-cells. Enhanced solar-light transmission 
increased conversion efficiency of Si solar panels 
by 4%. The degradation in photovoltaic efficiency 
of solar panels that were covered with Si3N4/MS 
anti-reflectors was as small as 0.54 % after 60 
hours of irradiation. MS films revealed wide 
band-gap of 6.4 eV and, therefore, negligible 
absorption in the solar spectrum, in response to 
highly stable photovoltaic efficiency during 
long-term irradiation. 
Introduction 
In past few years, enormous efforts have been 
made on development of high-efficiency Si solar 
cells, such as tandem structures by the 
introduction of various crystalline SiGe layers [1-3], 
nanostructures of quantum-dot superlattices [4], 
and broadband optical anti-reflectors [5] etc.. 
There are lots of methods to fabricate the 
anti-reflectors, such as the pyramid gratings [6], 
nano-rods [7], and graded-index structures [5]. 
Moreover, arrayed textures were normally formed 
on initial substrates in attempt to reduce reflection 
of solar-light by enabling multi-incidence and 
decreasing effective incident angle. However, 
Fresnel reflection at interfaces still limits 
light-transmission to a certain extent. 
Graded-refractive-index (n) layers [8-9] have 
broadband anti-reflective characteristics and J. Q. 
Xi et al [5]. demonstrated reflectivity of less than 
1% in the range of 400-1200 nm, regardless of 
light polarization and incident angle, while quite 
complex combinations of low refractive-index 
materials introduced. In the application of solar 
panels, structures and materials, however, should 
meet the criterion of reliability, low-cost, and 
mass-production. Therefore, the development of 
broadband anti-reflective structures is still a 
concern for the improvement of conversion 
efficiency. 
Mesoporus silica (MS) dielectrics revealed low 
dielectric constant [10-11], which was certainly 
introduced as insulators in metallization 
interconnects of electronic integrated-circuits. 
Furthermore, the porosity in such materials was 
also responsible for refractive index as low as 1.25, 
suitable for applications of anti-reflective layers in 
solar panels. Moreover, the refractive index and 
thickness of MS layer can be easily controlled by 
the introduction of various organic templates in 
precursors and calcination processes, and the 
adjustment of solvent concentration and 
spin-coating speed in synthesizing MS layers, 
respectively [10]. Such advantages facilitate the 
employment of MS materials as a part of 
multi-layer antireflective structures for solar cells. 
In this article, we integrate MS layers together 
with silicon nitride layers to be optical 
double-layer-based gradedrefractive-index (DLGRI) 
ant-reflectors on textured Si solarcells. Accordingly, 
enhanced solar-light transmission increased 
conversion efficiency of solar panels significantly. 
Moreover, the reliability of solar panels that were 
covered with Si3N4/MS anti-reflectors was also 
examined and highly reliable photovoltaic effect 
was explained by ultra-low absorption coefficient 
over the entire solar spectrum due to wide 
band-gap of MS materials.

C. Enhanced photovoltaic effect by DLGRI 
anti-reflector 
Figure 5 shows the measured I-V curves of 
samples with different anti-reflectors and their 
corresponding power output. The maximum 
conversion efficiency of three samples was also 
indicated. The conversion efficiency of solar cells 
with DLGRI structures was higher than sample 
coated with one-layer antireflector. Comparing 
current-voltage (I-V) characteristics of 5-inches 
solar panels coated by two-type DLGRI 
anti-reflectors revealed an enhanced efficiency of 
output-power for MS coated sample of 15.3 %, 
which was higher than 15.0 % for sample coated 
with conventional DLGRI structures of Si3N4+SiO2 
(Fig. 5). From the I-V characteristic curves, it was 
obvious that the short circuit current of samples 
covered with Si3N4+MS anti-reflectors under 
illumination was higher than that of samples 
coated with conventional Si3N4+SiO2 antireflective 
structures. Accordingly, we attribute increased 
photovoltaic efficiency of former panels to 
enhanced transmission of ultraviolet-to-visible 
solar energy due to broadband anti-reflective 
structure with mesoporous silica (See Figs. 3 and 
4). 
 
Figure 5: I-V characteristics of Si solar panels, which were 
coated with various anti-reflective structures, under illumination 
by solar simulator. 
The reliability of solar cell is also a concern. 
The degradation test for solar panels that were 
coated with Si3N4+MS anti-reflectors was shown in 
figure 6. Notably, the degradation in photovoltaic 
efficiency of this cell was as small as 0.54 % after 
60 hours of irradiation (Fig. 6). MS films revealed 
wide band-gap of 6.4 eV (Fig. 7) and, therefore, 
negligible absorption in the solar spectrum (Figs. 1 
and 7), in response to highly stable photovoltaic
