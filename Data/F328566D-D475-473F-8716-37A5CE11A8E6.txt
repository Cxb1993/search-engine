中文摘要 
本計畫利用分散聚合法（Dispersion polymerization），製備尺寸均一與分散性良好的高分
子微米粒。除了將此微米粒直接應用於擴散膜的應用外，另也分別利用高分子微粒上所帶
有螯合官能基配合化學沉澱法於高分子微粒表面形成具 UV 吸收光致發光的奈米 CdS 粒
子；以及將四丁基鈦與單體或微粒進行反應，製得內含有 TiO2 之複合粒子。再利用 CdS、
TiO2 奈米粒子可吸收 UV 光的特性，將此複合粒子添加於擴散膜中。本文以紅外線吸收光
譜儀分析(FTIR)及電子顯微鏡(SEM)分析所製得的高分子微粒的組成與型態；另以螢光光度
計(PL) 及 UV/vis 分別分析複合擴散粒於擴散膜中的光擴散率及光穿透度。結果顯示，利
用此複合微粒製得的光擴散膜之擴散率會隨著添加的擴散粒之重量百分量率增加而增加，
而全光穿透度則會隨之減少。利用內含 TiO2 微粒的光擴散粒製備光擴散板時可得最佳的光
擴散度 65%及光穿透度 65%。除此之外，若以含 CdS 或 TiO2 的複合微粒做為光擴散微粒
時，除可發現其光擴散度明顯提高外，再利用 UV/VIS 分光光譜儀量測，可發現製得的光
擴散膜有吸收 UV 光的功能。此特性可有效地降低一般背光模組中，因冷陰極管所發射出
的 UV 光造成的操作溫度提高的問題。 
關鍵字：光擴散粒、擴散板、奈米複合微粒 
 
Abstract 
Uniform polymer microbead were prepared by using dispersion polymerization and acted as 
the diffuser in the backlight module of TFT display in this project. Those polymer microbeads 
were mixed with acrylate resin and sprayed on the surface of polymer substrate to obtain the 
light-diffusion sheet.  Haze meter and UV/vis photometer was used to measure the light 
transmission and dispersion properties of the light-diffusion sheet, meanwhile, SEM electron 
microscope was applied to study the morphology of the sheet. Furthermore, the polymer 
microbeads with semiconductor nanoparticles, such as TiO2 and CdS, were also prepared via the 
surface modification and chemical deposition methods and applied in the light diffusion sheet in 
this project.  The light dispersion efficient of diffusion sheet is increased with the amount of 
diffuser, while the degree of light transmission is decreased. The best light dispersion efficient 
and light transmission of the diffusion sheet is 65% which is used the TiO2 composite microbead 
as the light diffuser.  On the other hand, the UV light absorption properties of both TiO2 and 
CdS nanocomposites were identified by using electron microscope, PL, and UV/vis photometer.  
This kind of UV absorption behavior can be efficiently descended the operation temperature of 
backlight module. 
 
Keywords: diffuser, backlight module, hybrid nanoparticles 
 
前 言 
近年來功能性高分子微米粒子的研究與應用發展續迅速，因為其具有相當大的尺寸範
圍、高表面積與多樣性的外貌，因而廣泛的應用在不同的商業領域( 1-7)。尤其是在 TFT 背光
模組中的擴散板中的高分子微米粒，這類的高分子微米粒剛好可以當做光的擴散點，因而
被大量的應用在液晶顯示器中背光模組的擴散板。而所製備出高分子微球在擴散板的主要
功能是將燈管所發出的線光源轉換成均勻的面光源，亦可具有遮蔽缺陷部分的功能。在國
內業者所生產的擴散板方式，都是以高溫熱壓成形的方式生產，其生產方式會使高分子微
粒子裂解，或者高分子微粒分布不均勻，成型所需要的高溫，會使的生產的成本提高。所
以本計畫以低溫的方式製備擴散板，並進一步地於擴散粒中添加無機粒子，透過這些無機
粒子的加入，除了增加擴散板的光擴散度與全光穿透度外，並利用這些無機粒子能吸收 UV
的特性，將冷陰極燈管所釋放出的部份 UV 光吸收，使擴散板的壽命增加，降低整体背光模
組的操作溫度。

自 評 
本計畫利用價格非常便宜的聚苯乙烯系來製備光擴散粒，且其擴散效果與市售日本帝
人的光擴散度與全光穿透度相當，其結果應稱良好。另值得一提的是，本計畫的擴散粒內
有添加 UV 光吸收微粒 TiO2，初步測試其的確對 UV 光具有吸收效果。因此，本計畫認為
此部份應可提供給業界參考，做進一步地測試是否能有效地降低 TFT 顯示器背光模組的操
作溫度。

Hollow spheres, especially for nanometer to micrometer dimensions, represent the special physical and chemical 
properties which have attracted great attention due to their potential applications [1].  However, the thermal 
oxidation of magnetic nanomaterials usually limits their performance.  Thus, how to prevent the oxidation of 
magnetic metals or alloy is an important topic? Recently, we have developed a simple process to prepare the 
magnetic composite hollow spheres covered with SiO2 nano-cluster [2, 3]. The SiO2
 
nano-cluster not only can avoid 
the aggregation of nanoparticles but also can inhibit the oxidation of metallic nanoparticles during high temperature 
calcinations. In addition, this method can be modified with various kinds of silane and make it easily dispersion 
within the polymer for other applications. Moreover, we present the preparation and magnetic behaviour of hollow 
spheres which are composed of SiO2 and CoFe2 magnetic alloys in this study. 
All chemicals were analytical purity and used as received. We used poly(methyl methacrylate-co-methacrylic acid) 
(PMA) microsphere (450 nm) as a core template which was synthesized by the soap-free emulsion polymerization 
method [2, 3]. In the polymerization, the potassium persulfate was used as an initiator. The reaction was executed 
under mechanical stirring and kept the reaction temperature at 75 °C for 4 hrs. The SiO2/CoFe2O4/PMA core/shell 
particles were prepared by the sol-gel method following the chemical co-precipitation [2, 3]. Firstly, 4×10-4 M Fe3+ 
and 2×10-4 M Co2+  were added into 100 ml PMA latex and magnetically stirred for 4hrs, then the Fe3+ and Co2+ were 
adsorbed onto the carboxyl-functional group PMA latex. Subsequently, NH4OH (33 wt %) was added into the latex 
solution. This process was repeated for 5 times, and then aged at 90 °C for 10 hrs. After purification and dried 
treatment of the CoFe2O4/PMA core/shell spheres, the tetraethyl orthsilicate was added into the CoFe2O4/PMA 
core/shell sphere powder with distilled water and magnetically stirred at 30 °C for 24 hrs. Finally, the hollow 
SiO2/CoFe2O4 composite spheres were formed by calcining the SiO2/CoFe2O4/PMA core/shell composite spheres in 
air at 450 °C for 4 hrs. To acquire the Co-Fe alloy involved in the hollow microspheres, the hollow SiO2/CoFe2O4 
composite spheres prepared at 450°C were further annealed at 900°C in a stream of 10% H2/Ar for 4 hrs and 8hrs. 
The morphology and microstructure of core-shell composites and hollow spheres was characterized by the TEM, 
XRD, and Fourier transform infrared (FTIR) spectroscopy. The vibrating sample magnetometer (VSM) was used to 
study the magnetic properties of the samples.

TEM images show that the surface of CoFe2O4/PMA core/shell spheres are roughly and their diameter is about 
480 nm (Fig. 1a-1). As SiO2 coated on CoFe2O4/PMA core/shell spheres, the SiO2/CoFe2O4/PMA composite (Fig. 
1b-1) has the uniform and smooth spherical surface. Furthermore, the hollow microsphere structure was obtained 
when the SiO2/CoFe2O4/PMA sphere (Fig. 1c-1) was calcinated at 450 °C for 4 hrs. Moreover, the TEM photograph 
of the cross section of the hollow sphere displays the thickness of the shell wall is about 50 nm (not shown in here).  
X-ray diffraction patterns show that the hollow SiO2/CoFe2O4 spheres, calcined in air at 450 °C for 4 hrs, have a 
cubic spinel ferrite structure (Fig. 2a). And samples further annealed at 900°C in the H2/Ar mixed gas for 4 hrs, the 
phases of fayalite (Fe2SiO4) and Co-Fe alloy coexist in the composite spheres (Fig. 2b). However, as the annealed 
times were extended from 4 hrs to 8 hrs only the α-body-centered cubic (bcc) CoFe2 alloy phase was observed (Fig. 
2c). In addition, the mean crystallite size of the CoFe2 nanoparticles, calculated using Scherrer’s equation, is 28.7 
nm.
