Abstract 
The objective of this experimental study has been aimed for the understanding of the 
effects of addition of dry brick powder to reservoir sludge mud (clay) in the preparation of 
lightweight aggregate as a mission for the recycling of the enormous deposits in the 60 
reservoirs of Taiwan. Addition of brick powders tends to promote the drying or dewatering 
rate of the feedstock material, being caused of by the pores originally present in the brick 
material. The strength of the lightweight aggregate product, prepared from clay-brick mixture, 
is significantly affected by brick powder addition. The highest strength is obtained for that 
with 10wt% addition, giving 38 MPa. 
Keywords?reservoir sludge, brick powder, lightweight aggregate, thermal behavior
