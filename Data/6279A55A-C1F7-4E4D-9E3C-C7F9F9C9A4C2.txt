新型味覺感測器及其訊號處理系統之研究、設計與實現 
“The design and implementation of new taste sensors and its signal processing system” 
計畫編號：NSC95-2221-E-239-043 
執行期間：95 年 8 月 01 日 至 96 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：陳宏偉 國立聯合大學電子系助理教授 
 
一、 中文摘要 
電子舌(electronic tongue)是新興的研究
領域，主要應用於液體分析， 是一種可以在
短時間內分辨和定量溶液中不同的味覺
(taste)或化學成份之儀器，辨識原理為模仿
生物體味覺系統之圖譜辨識。電子舌能夠快速
分辨 酸、甜、苦、鹹、鮮等五種存在於液體
中的味道的儀器，它的基本構造可分成三大子
系統：感測器陣列、前端信號處理、及後段資
料處理及味覺判定。分別類比於生物的舌頭、
神經系統及頭腦。 
本研究計畫之目的為完成一簡單且便宜
的電子舌，今年具體的目的有三：（1）研究
可用的新型電子舌感測器，本年度將著重於固
態感測器的研發及仿生材料的應用、(2) 嘗試
將所設計可用的的感測器模型化、加強感測信
號讀取電路、前端信號處理系統的研究及電路
設計、(3) 將電路以CMOS 技術實做，以利
將來和感測器陣列及後段資料處理及味覺判
定較整合成Sensor-System-On-a-Chip。 
電子舌的研究包含了奈米科技、化學工
程、材料工程、微電子工程、資信處理甚至包
含微機電等等各領域，它的應用更包含了飲料
檢驗、水質偵測、食品工業等民生相關工業。
我們覺得這是一個很值得研究的題目。感測器
在工業界的產值據信將有指數性的成長。因此
如能盡早研發相信必有助於國內工業的發展。 
 
英文摘要 
Electronic tongue is a new research field. It is 
an instrument thatemulates the human taste 
system to rapidly sense and discriminatethe taste 
substance in liquid; such as acid, sweet, bitter, 
salt, andumami. Basically, the electronic tongue 
system include three main subsystems which are 
the taste sensor array, the front-end analog signal 
processing system and the back-end data 
processing and taste classified system. 
There are three main purposes for this project: 
(1) research, design,and implement new taste 
sensors---especially in the solid state taste 
sensors and the sensors made by the mimic-bio 
material, (2) modeling the new design tatse 
sensors and dsign the front-end analog 
processing system, and (3) implement all the 
circuits by the CMOS technology. The third 
purpose is the most important for integration of 
all the three subsystems of electronic tongue to a 
chip to form the so-call 
“Sensor-System-On-a-Chip”. 
The study of electronic tongue includes many 
topics of research, such as nano-technology, 
chemistry, micro-electronics, IC design, and 
materials. The application of electronic tongue 
also important; it include environment-protection, 
food industry, and drink classification. We think 
the study of electronic tongue is meaningful for 
human being.
