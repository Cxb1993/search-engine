研究摘要(500 字以內)：
Combining wireless and fiber communication system is the most popular in the recent
years. It is easy to achieve high transmission rate and multi-media of cellophane service for
microwave transmission system in Passive Optical Network (PON).
we replaced the conventional time division multiplexing (TDM) in PON at upstream. First,
We use Arrayed-Waveguide Grating (AWG) in Subcarrier Multiplexing-Wavelength
multiplexing (SCM -WDM). And the other one is Code Division Multiplexing-Wavelength
Multiplexing (CDM-WDM).
The proposed system using SCM-WDM can overcome the problem of complicated
synchronization in Time Division Multiple Access (TDMA).And it is flexible to transmit analog
or digital for designer. But using SCM will cause the noise of Intermodulation Distortion (IMD).
When using CDM-WDM system, It wouldn’t have the problem of IMD in SCM. And It can
suppress the Optical Beat Interference in WDM system. For another thing, we only need to use
the single light source and Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) that can make many users using the same
driving source together utilized by multiplexing. In economical, the expensive light source cost
can be reduced effetely.
Finally we will consider many conditions such as temperature, distance power and
environment to get the best performance in our systems. And even more combining SCM-WDM
and CDM-WDM to compare their system performance.
