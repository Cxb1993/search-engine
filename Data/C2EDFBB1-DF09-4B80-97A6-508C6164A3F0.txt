一、參加會議經過 
2007 年國際電子電機天線傳播學會研討會今年於六月十日至十五日在美國夏威夷歐胡島
Sheraton Waikiki Hotel 舉行，共發表了超過1000篇論文。口頭方式報告分別在多個會場同步
進行，並且將每天壁報張貼的時間固定在中午用餐時間，且壁報場地與用餐地方規劃在同一
區域， 因此每天中午便可發現許多學者一邊用餐一邊彼此討論壁報內容。 
 
筆者於會議開始前一天(六月九日，星期六)中午抵達當地，而會議第一天(星期日)主要節目
是付費課程與註冊。筆者在第一天完成註冊後，便開始熟悉會場周圍環境。口頭報告及壁報
張貼於會議第二天正式開始，筆者也挑選幾個有興趣的主題參加，例如: 超寬頻天線設計興
應用，圓極化天線設計，多頻帶天線設計。另外，筆者也撥空參觀會場旁廠商所展示的相關
設備，包括有頻譜分析儀、網路分析儀、天線量測系統及各種電磁模擬軟體(CST, SEMCAD, 
HFSS, IE3D)，現場並有工程師介紹各項功能。 
 
筆者論文被安排在星期二中午以口頭方式報告與各國學者交流，題目是“雙頻天線設計應用
在GPS與UMTS”，對於筆者的論文，有一些國內外學者甚感興趣並提出相關問題及建議，
筆者均給予答覆及說明所提出之天線的實務應用，另外也有相關領域的外國學者希望筆者能
提供更完整的結果及參考文獻作為其研究方向。報告結束後，筆者也在接下來的兩天以比較
輕鬆的心態下參與其它的報告。 
 
除此之外，筆者也參加在(六月十三日)星期三晚上舉行的餐會，在餐會中的頒獎典禮介紹今
年榮陞IEEE fellow 的學者，其中包括了中山大學的翁金輅教授，且由陳文祥教授主持的IEEE 
AP 台南分會獲得年度最佳分會，並由陳宏圖教授代表上台領獎，筆者也與有榮焉。在會議
期間，也遇到許多本國學者、教授及業界人士，彼此也互相交換研究心得，並討論各校研究
環境及產業概況。會議共持續五天並於星期四結束，筆者於次日搭機返國。

1Department of Computer and Communication Engineering, Chienkuo Technology

Introduction 
 
In recent years, there has been an elevated interest in the antenna design of 
integrating the Global Position System (GPS) into terrestrial wireless communication 
networks such as; GSM [1], DCS [2], WLAN [3] and ETC [4]. For these designs, two 
radiating antenna elements with different performances in operating frequency, radiation 
patterns, and polarizations are required. Furthermore, they are usually arranged in a 
stacked configuration for compact size of the integrated antenna. However, the stacked 
structure would result in not only an increase in material cost but also more difficult to 
manufacture. In this paper, a new design of an integrated antenna for the applications of 
GPS and UMTS is proposed. A broadband monopolar patch antenna with conical 
radiation patterns [5] is used as a radiating element for UMTS operation, and its radiating 
patch is placed at the inner-slot area of a circular-polarized (CP) square-ring microstrip 
element for GPS operation. Consequently, the two antenna elements of the proposed 
design can be fabricated on a single printed circuit board. In order to obtain the isolation 
of more than 20 dB between the two input ports, a 3rd Order Butterworth low pass filter 
(LPF) is designed and placed at the input port of the GPS antenna element. Details of the 
proposed design and obtained results are presented and discussed. 
 
Antenna Configuration  
 
The geometry of the proposed dual-band antenna is depicted in Fig. 1. A square-
ring patch and an internal square patch with a side length of b are etched on the upper 
side of a FR4 substrate (thickness 0.6 mm and permittivity 4.4). Another square patch 
with a side length of c is fabricated on the bottom side of the same FR4 substrate, which 
is placed at a height of h above the ground plane. The square-ring patch has an outer side 
length of a, and the dimensions of the inner rectangular slot are L × W. To produce 
broadside right-hand CP radiation, the squarer-ring patch is with truncated corners of 
equal side length ∆l, and it is fed by a probe (radius 0.65 mm) located along the y-axis. A 
LPF is inserted between the probe and the GPS input port. As for the UMTS radiating 
element, it is composed by two square patches in which the upper patch is shorted to 
ground plane by two pins and the lower patch is connected to a 50 Ω microstrip line 
through a probe. The antenna structure has a broad impedance bandwidth and can radiate 
omni-directional patterns at a low elevation angle [5].
