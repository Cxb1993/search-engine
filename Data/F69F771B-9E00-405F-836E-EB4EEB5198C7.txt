結合超臨界流體萃取及Fenton 法去除土壤及地下水之五氯酚 
            計畫編號： NSC96 – 2221 – E – 324 – 012  
            執行期限： 96 年 8 月 1 日至 97 年 7 月 31 日 
            計畫主持人：劉敏信 博士   共同計畫主持人：羅煌木 博士 
            計畫參與人員：蘇益民、劉家福 
摘要 
爲維持環境基質的特性不變，又可徹底去除分解基質中的有機污染物，本研究計畫乃
串聯結合超臨界流體萃取法及 Fenton 法處理土壤及地下水之有機污染物。首先利用超臨界
二氧化碳配合修飾劑的添加作為超臨界流體，將土壤及地下水中五氯酚萃取移除後，再利
用 Fenton 試劑分解五氯酚污染物。研究中探討超臨界流體萃取五氯酚的最佳操作狀況與萃
取率之影響因子，而且在不改變介質特性及大幅降低 Fenton 試劑使用量之狀況下，求得
Fenton 試劑過氧化氫與亞鐵離子的最佳比例與瞭解其他影響因子。在超臨界流體萃取方
面，為尋求萃取時之最佳條件與個別的效率，本研究乃針對不同溫度、壓力以及甲醇修飾
劑之使用比率進行探討。土壤研究結果在未添加修飾劑甲醇情況下，以壓力 100 bar，溫度
70℃的狀態下，紅壤及沖積土皆可達萃取效率 60%以上，而添加 5%(v/v)甲醇修飾劑在壓力
100 bar，溫度 40℃的狀態下兩種土樣之五氯酚的萃取效率皆可達 70%以上。地下水方面之
結果顯示，在未添加甲醇修飾劑情況下，未飽和、飽和及過飽和五氯酚水樣中，以壓力 400 
bar，溫度 70℃操作條件下，五氯酚萃取效率可達 95%以上；而添加 5%(v/v)甲醇修飾劑情
況下，未飽和、飽和及過飽和五氯酚水樣以壓力 200 bar，溫度 70℃狀況萃取，最佳五氯酚
萃取效率皆可達 97%以上。最後將超臨界流體萃取出之五氯酚濃縮液進行 Fenton 氧化試
驗，數據發現萃取後之五氯酚無須很高濃度比例及劑量之過氧化氫及亞鐵離子藥劑，即可
達到完全降解之效果，比直接使用 Fenton 藥劑添加於土壤及地下水的需要量大幅減少，而
且效果較佳。 
 
關鍵詞：超臨界流體萃取、Fenton 氧化法、五氯酚、土壤、地下水 
 
Abstract 
In order to maintain the property of matrix during the process of degrading organic 
pollutants, a tandem system of supercritical fluid extraction and Fenton method was established 
in this research.  The main objective was to use supercritical carbon dioxide with modifier 
addition to remove pentachlorophenol (PCP) from soil and groundwater, thereafter the extracted 
PCP was degraded by using Fenton’s reagents.  The optimal operation conditions of 
supercritical fluid extraction and Fenton reaction were determined, and the quantity usage of 
Fenton’s reagents hydrogen peroxide and ferrous iron solution was reduced.  The parameters of 
temperature, pressure and addition percentage of methanol modifier in supercritical fluid 
extraction were studied.  The extraction for soil without modifier addition, the extraction 
efficiencies reached to 60% in the condition of 100 bar and 70°C, however the efficiency was 
氣態或液態，密度劇降，喪失親和力釋出溶
質。由於二氧化碳在常壓下屬於氣態，因此
在使用後只要減壓即會變回氣相，而和其他
固、液相的物質分離，故容易回收再使用，
以超臨界二氧化碳作為溶劑，在處理後不會
有殘留的問題，因而可符合環保及衛生法
規，也因此稱為綠色溶劑。超臨界流體相關
研究進行探討，超臨界流體應用於環境之領
域中包括活性碳之再生【5】，去除活性碳
所吸附之農藥、乙基醋酸與甲苯；以及廢液
或廢水有機污染物酚之去除【6】，其中修
飾劑具有對去除效率之影響性。研究中亦探
討一段式自廢液中直接以超臨界流體萃取
有機污染物，或者以二段式將廢液有機物吸
附於固體吸附劑上，再以臨界流體自吸附劑
中萃取有機污染物。有關廢液中有機物之去
除，於1982 年由Modell等人【7】 首先利
用超臨界水處理系統直接將有機物氧化，並
申請獲得專利。至於利用超臨界流體萃取介
質的有機污染物如多環芳香烴化合物，
Raymer等人【8】及Wright等人【9】就曾利
用超臨界二氧化碳由高分子吸收劑萃取回
收多環芳香烴化合物，並予以定量。空氣中
懸浮微粒之多環芳香烴化合物就曾由
Schantz等人【10】研究萃取，並與傳統的索
氏萃取法比較。而且Hawthorne等人【11】
曾使用非極性的二氧化碳及極性的一氧化
二氮(N2O)，探討在有無添加修飾劑甲醇的
狀態下，萃取多項環境介質的多環芳香烴化
合物，結果發現在添加甲醇的狀況下，可高
效率的萃取出多環芳香烴化合物。由於二氧
化碳臨界壓力與溫度相對其他氣體較低，在
加入少量之修飾劑之後即可萃取環境介質
中之有機污染物質，就文獻指出利用非極性
之二氧化碳及極性之ㄧ氧化二氮，配合甲
醇、丙酮等修飾劑的添加能成功有效的萃取
土壤中之農藥、多氯聯苯、氯酚及2, 3, 7, 8-
四氯戴奧辛【12-14】。反應過程中甲醇等
修飾劑既可作為Lewis酸，亦可以作為Lewis
鹼，在超臨界流體萃取過程中，可與溶質的
官能團相互作用，引起溶劑化之作用，增加
溶質溶解度而提昇萃取效率。 
2. 研究步驟與方法 
本研究中所選用的土壤為台灣常見的
台中科學園區預定地之紅壤土，與霧峰農試
所之沖積土為試驗土樣，並先對其含水率、
pH 值、導電度、氧化還原電位、有機碳含
量進行分析，藉以了解土壤特性對超臨界流
體萃取及化學氧化降解效率之影響。地下水
方面則預先控制地下水中 pH 值與氧化還原
電位以提高五氯酚萃取效果；研究首先採用
未受污染之地下水添加五氯酚，分別配置未
飽和、飽和及過飽和之水樣進行試驗。本研
究收集相關文獻整理外，對於五氯酚及
Fenton 法與超臨界 CO2 萃取技術之瞭解
後，主要部分分別為土壤前處理、土壤地下
水基本特性分析、配製五氯酚土樣與五氯酚
回收率試驗，五氯酚於地下水溶解度試驗，
Fenton 法實驗針對不同濃度之硫酸亞鐵與
過氧化氫濃度進行測試，超臨界 CO2 萃取實
驗則針對不同溫度、壓力、與是否添加甲醇
修飾劑作為試驗探討，最後作數據統計分析
整理，找出最佳的操作條件，實驗系統之超
臨界流體萃取土壤示意如圖 1 所示，超臨界
流體萃取土壤示意如圖 2 所示，本研究架構
流程如圖 3 所示。 
 
製備受五氯酚污染土樣之方法 
將0.2 g五氯酚溶於500 mL的甲醇中配
製 400 ppm 五氯酚溶液，取紅壤與沖積土各
1 g分別加入 1 mL配製 400 ppm五氯酚溶液
於離心管均勻混合後，於抽風櫃至風乾後儲
存，以作為受五氯酚污染之土樣。  
 
製備受五氯酚污染水樣之方法 
本研究所使用之五氯酚地下水水樣，飽
和水樣（15 ppm）取五氯酚 0.3 g 加水至 20 
L，並以機械攪拌器持續攪拌 6 天（600～
1,000 rpm），使五氯酚完全溶於水中。並分
別配製未飽和水樣 7 ppm 與過飽和水樣 55 
ppm 作為研究探討的濃度。 
 
土壤 Fenton 氧化法批次實驗方法 
配製不同濃度之過氧化氫及亞鐵離子
濃度後，分別加入亞鐵溶液、過氧化氫溶
液、1 g PCP 土樣於超音波震盪反應 10 分鐘
後，進行 5 分鐘的 2,500 rpm 的離心機固液
mM 倍數增加至 460.8 mM，過氧化氫以 51.5 
mM、103 mM 倍數增加至 824 mM，紅壤最
佳降解比例為過氧化氫濃度206 mM與硫酸
亞鐵濃度 57.6 mM，最差降解比例為過氧化
氫濃度 51.5 mM 與硫酸亞鐵濃度 460.8 
mM，而沖積土最佳降解比例為過氧化氫濃
度 824 mM 與硫酸亞鐵濃度 28.8 mM，最差
降解比例為過氧化氫濃度 51.5 mM 與硫酸
亞鐵濃度 230.4 mM。

3.4 超臨界二氧化碳萃取地下水中五氯酚結
果 
本研究所使用之五氯酚地下水水樣，分
別配製未飽和水樣 7 ppm、飽和水樣 14 ppm
與過飽和水樣 55 ppm 作為研究探討的濃
度。因此超臨界流體試驗以不同壓力 100 
bar、200 bar、300 bar、400 bar，並升高溫
度到 40˚C、50˚C、60˚C、70˚C，觀察其萃
取效率。在未飽和水樣 7 ppm 濃度下其萃取
效果如圖 16 所示，飽和水樣 14 ppm 濃度之
萃取效果如圖 17 所示，過飽和水樣 55 ppm
濃度其萃取效果如圖 18 所示。在三種濃度
水樣之萃取結果顯示，皆能達到 70%以上。 
由於二氧化碳乃屬於非極性物質，對萃
取效果有所限制，故研究中乃添加 5%(v/v)
甲醇修飾劑來增加超臨界二氧化碳之極
性，其三種濃度未飽和、飽和與過飽和萃取
結果如圖 19、圖 20、圖 21 所示，其萃取結
果皆有部分提升達到 80%以上。 
在 SFE 實驗中可得知在未添加甲醇情
況下，三種濃度水樣中萃取未飽和水樣中最
佳操作條件，以壓力 400 bar，溫度 70℃的
狀態下，可達最佳萃取效率 98%；飽和水樣
之最佳操作條件為 400 bar，溫度 60℃的狀
態下，可達最佳萃取效率 96%；過飽和水樣
之最佳操作條件為 200 bar，溫度 70℃的狀
態下，可達最佳萃取效率 96%。 
而 SFE 實驗中添加 5%(v/v)甲醇修飾劑
試驗中，在三種濃度水樣中萃取未飽和水樣
中最佳操作條件，以壓力 200 bar，溫度 70℃
的狀態下，可達最佳萃取效率 98%；飽和水
樣之最佳操作條件為 200 bar，溫度 70℃的
狀態下，可達最佳萃取效率 97%；過飽和水
樣之最佳操作條件為 300 bar，溫度 70℃的
狀態下，可達最佳萃取效率 98%。

造成與污染物鍵結及與污染物的競爭氧化
作用，所以需提高 H2O2 含量，但 H2O2 濃
度過高時，多餘的 H2O2 與．OH 產生反應，
造成氫氧自由基無法與污染物反應，因此並
非 H2O2 及 Fe2+濃度越高就越好。 
    土壤在 SFE 實驗中，壓力在 100 bar 時
提升溫度確實能提升萃取效率，但提升壓力
至 200 bar 以上時，溫度則非越高越佳，在
同壓力情況下，溫度越低其密度越大，密度
大小與批次的流體使用量確實會影響萃取
效率。土壤對五氯酚之吸附能力非常強，因
此在 SFE 實驗中萃取效果有限，添加 5% 
(v/v) 甲醇修飾劑，甲醇能提升二氧化碳之
極性，每批次具有提升 10%之萃取效果。 
    地下水在未添加甲醇情況下，萃取率大
體上隨者壓力與溫度的升高而提高；而添加
5%(v/v)甲醇修飾劑情況下，只需較低的壓
力與溫度的搭配皆可有高的萃取率，顯示甲
醇能改變超臨界二氧化碳的極性應用於地
下水中五氯酚之萃取。但是在不同壓力與溫
度試驗中，添加甲醇修飾劑在水樣中，卻也
有可能導致甲醇與五氯酚相互競爭，使得未
添加修飾劑條件下有較高的萃取效果，添加
修飾劑反而導致五氯酚萃取率的降低。 
   超臨界流體萃取出之五氯酚濃縮液進行
Fenton 氧化試驗中發現，萃取後之五氯酚
無須很高濃度比例之過氧化氫及亞鐵離子
藥劑，即可達到完全降解之效果，且此方法
不但不會破壞土壤及地下水中之特性與天
然成分，也不會有藥物殘留之問題。

Using Supercritical Fluid Extraction to Remove Heavy Metals from 
EAF dust in Steelmaking Industry 
 
Min-Hsin Liu1*, Chin-Tsun Hsieh1, Li-Ming Sun1 and Yi-Chen Chiang1 
 
1) Department of Environmental Engineering and Management, Chaoyang University of Technology, 
Wufong Taichung County, 41349, Taiwan 
jliu@cyut.edu.tw 
 
 
Abstract:  The components of EAF dust, produced from electric arc furnace in steelmaking industry, 
include not only organic pollutant polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons (PAHs), but inorganic pollutant 
heavy metals, these components usually lead the EAF dust classified as industrial hazardous waste.  
The objective of this research is to evaluate the efficiency of supercritical fluid extraction for removing 
heavy metals from EAF dust.  In order to enhance the removal efficiency of heavy metals, the 
chelating agents, including EDTA, citric acid and citric acid with chitosan solution were added.  In 
addition, hydrogen peroxide was added in order to increase the oxidation reduction potential in EAF 
dust prior to the extraction process, the purpose was to enhance the solubility of heavy metal in 
supercritical fluid or chelating agents, consequently increased the removal efficiency of specific heavy 
metals from EAF dust.  The results concluded a promising extraction efficiency of specific heavy 
metals from EAF dust by using the optimal operation condition.     
 
Keywords：Supercritical fluid extraction, EAF dust, Heavy metals 
 
1. GENERAL INTRODUCTION 
 
The EAF dust, produced from electric arc furnace factory, is collected from the air pollution control 
device such as bag house in steelmaking industry.  The components of EAF dust include inorganic 
pollutant heavy metals, these components usually lead the EAF dust classified as industrial hazardous 
waste.  However, the heavy metals contain some precious materials such as zinc oxide which can be 
further recovered.  The purpose of this research is to remove particulate heavy metals and remain the 
zinc oxide for future recovery.  The method used in this research was supercritical fluid extraction 
(SFE), carbon dioxide was selected as a supercritical fluid for the experiment, in addition methanol was 
added to increase the polarity of supercritical carbon dioxide so that the extraction efficiency can be 
improved [1].  Carbon dioxide is a fluid of non-toxic, non-flammable and related low critical pressure 
(73 bars) and temperature (31˚C).  In order to enhance the extraction efficiencies of heavy metals, 
some chelating agents such as ethylenediamine tetraacetic acid (EDTA), citric acid (CA), citric acid 
with chitosan were introduced during the extraction.  Also, the forms of heavy metals are based on the 
pH and oxidation reduction potential (ORP) of EAF dust, the mobility and solubility of heavy metals 
can be controlled [2], therefore various concentrations of hydrogen peroxide can be added during the 
extraction to control the ORP of EAF dust, that increase the solubility of heavy metals in chelating 
agents. 
Li et al. [3] had conducted the extraction of copper ions by using supercritical fluid, but no 
promising efficiency was found due to the weak bonding between supercritical fluid and metal ions.  
However, Laintz et al. [4] had utilized Dithiocarbamate as chelating agent to enhance the solubility of

3. RESULTS AND DISCUSSION 
3.1 Property of EAF Dust 
The properties of EAF dust is shown in Table 1.  Low moisture was identified, the pH indicated the 
EAF dust was in neutral with slight acid condition.  In particle size distribution experiment, the 
particle size percentages in the range of 0.074~0.149 mm, 0.149~0.297 mm and 0.297~1 mm are 
19.3%, 39.0% and 36.1%, respectively. It is concluded that the major particle sizes of EAF dust are 
between 0.074 to 1 mm.  The percentage of C, H, O and N were measured with element analyzer, the 
components of Ca, Fe, K, Mg, Mn, Cd, Cr, Cu, Ni, Pb, Zn, Ga, Na, Al and Ba were determined with 
ICP-OES.  Based on the data listed in Table 2, the major components of EAF dust were O, Fe and Zn.  
Besides, the other elements were below 2.5%, Si was not identified in this study.  
Individual concentration of heavy metal was identified using aqua regia digestion method.  In order 
to determine if the EAF dust is a hazardous waste, a TCLP test was performed.  The results for aqua 
regia digestion and TCLP methods are shown in Table 3.  According to the data of these two methods, 
the EAF dust contained large amount of zinc oxide.  In addition, the components of EAF dust were 
identified with x-ray diffraction method, the result is shown in Figure 2.  The major components in 
EAF dust are ZnO, MgO, Fe3O4, ZnFe2O4 and Mn3O4.  The metals of Cd, Pb and Zn exceed the 
criteria in TCLP method, therefore the main purpose of this research is to remove Cd and Pb and 
remain Zn by using chelating agents and supercritical fluid.

4. CONCLUSIONS 
(1) The redox potential was increased with the addition of hydrogen peroxide, which increased the 
removal efficiencies of heavy metals from EAF dust.  Based on the data, the forms of Cd and Pb 
are CdCO3 and PbCO3, respectively. 
(2) In the batch washing experiment, the optimal condition was to use EDTA as the chelating agent and 
introduce 0.14% or 14% hydrogen peroxide as pretreatment.  
(3) In SFE experiment, adding 5% methanol as modifier could enhance the removal efficiencies of 
heavy metals from EAF dust.  Also, adding hydrogen peroxide could increase the mobility of 
metals prior to SFE that enhance the extraction efficiency.  It is proved that the addition of EDTA 
as chelating agent and 0.14% H2O2 as pretreatment is the optimal operation condition.
