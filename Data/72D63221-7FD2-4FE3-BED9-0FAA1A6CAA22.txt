I摘 要
有機化學品及其混合物廣泛應用於各類製造及生產程序中，此類物質多具有毒
性、腐蝕性及可燃性等危害性，其中燃燒特性往往是最令人關切之危害性，對於燃燒
特性的描述一般使用閃火點作為參考指標。
閃火點可由實驗量測、查詢文獻或利用模式估算等方式取得，但前兩種方式皆有
其受限之處，使用者往往無法順利取得閃火點資料，因此本研究以基團貢獻法提出有
機物質之閃火點預測模式，本研究模式將 2003 年 Albahri 所發展的模式延伸應用至含
有氧、氮、硫及鹵素分子之有機物質，由 500 種有機化學品的閃火點建立出 32 種官
能基團的貢獻參數，而本模式的平均絕對誤差百分比為 3.68%。另外以燃燒能量平衡
方程式配合相平衡理論推導醇-烷混合物之閃火點預測模式，模式乃藉由醇及烷的閃
火點、熱容值、標準燃燒熱、蒸氣壓、活性係數參數等資料直接進行估算，本研究以
正辛烷+乙醇、1-丁醇、2-丁醇等 3 種混合物進行模式驗證，本模式的平均絕對誤差百
分比僅為 1.13%，相當優異。
關鍵字：閃火點、預測模式、基團貢獻法
Abstract
Organic substances and mixtures are used in manufacture process widely. These
substances have many hazardous characters, including toxicity, corrosion, and flammability
or combustibility. The flammability or combustibility has the most concern. Furthermore,
flash point is usually used as a flammable or combustible index for describing flammability
or combustibility of substances.
Flash point can be determined by test, literature or predictive model. The first two
methods above-mentioned are limited by various reasons, and resulting in user can’t obtain 
flash point successfully. So, this study proposed a predictive model of flash point for organic
substances. This model which is a group contribution method extended the model proposed
by Albahri in 2003 to apply to substances containing oxygen, nitrogen, sulfur, and halogen,
and established 32 group contribution parameters from 500 organic substances. The average
absolute error percentages (AAEP) of the model is 3.68%. Othewise, a predictive model of
flash point for alcohol+alkane mixtures is proposed by combining combustion energy
balance with phase equilibrium theory. The model uses flash points, heat capacities, standard
heat of combustion, vapor pressure data, and activity coefficients of alcohol and alkane to
determine flash point of alcohol+alkane mixtures. Four alcohol+alkane mixtures,
ethanol+octane, 1-butanol+octane, and 2-butanol+octane, were adopted as identifing the
precision of the model. The AAEP of the model is 1.13%, it is excellent.
Keywords：Flash Point、Predictive Model、Group Contribution Method
