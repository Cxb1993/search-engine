中文摘要 
本研究利用 Pluronic(Plu)和聚丙烯酸(PAA)，藉由原位成膠法製備具生物黏附性之 Plu-PAA 液態栓
劑，並搭配抗癌藥物草酸鉑(Oxaliplatin)，再將含有抗癌藥物之黏附性液態栓劑輸送至大腸直腸癌部位，
並藉由腸道溫度(37 ℃)和 pH 值(pH 7.4)在癌化部位形成凝膠，使抗癌藥物停留在我們希望治療的部位
持續釋放藥物，而達到專一治療的效果。由實驗結果顯示，添加 0.75 % AAc 之液態栓劑凝膠速度最快，
只需 170 s，且在 37 ℃以下即能成膠，還有適當的黏度(21000 mPa.s)、凝膠強度(> 300 s)和生物黏附力
(10168 dyne/cm2)。綜合以上結果，Plu-PAA 液態栓搭配抗癌藥物 Oxaliplatin 極有潛力發展成新型大腸
直腸癌藥物的定點釋放劑型。 
關鍵字:原位成膠、液態栓劑、生物黏附性、Pluronic、聚丙烯酸。 
英文摘要 
 
In order to avoid anti-cancer drugs undergo first-pass effect and reduce their toxicity, and to solve 
conventional suppositories defects, we developed in situ-gelling and injectable Pluronic-Poly(acrylic acid) 
(Pluronic-PAA) liquid suppository, which can gel fast in physiological state and has suitable gel strength and 
bioadhesive force. These results suggest that in situ-gelling and injectable liquid suppository for humans can 
be further developed as a more convenient and effective rectal dosage form. 
Keywords : In situ-gelling, Liquid suppository, Bioadhesive, Pluronic, Poly(acrylic acid).

壓及瘧疾等。由於液態栓劑具備適當生物黏附力及凝膠強度，使用後不會從肛門滲漏，也不會跑到結

膨潤試驗 
由於人體腸道內含有豐富的腸液，若想將液態栓劑運用在人體腸道，必須要有良好的膨潤程度和吸
水性。因此我們將Plu-PAA液態栓劑置於模擬腸液(37 ℃、pH 7.4)中進行膨潤度測試，並計算出含水率。 
本研究將 Pluronic 溶液之濃度固定為(14 wt %)提升 AAc 溶液之濃度(由 0.187 wt %上升至 0.75 wt 
%)，由圖 3 可發現液態栓劑的膨潤度和含水率都明顯的增加，因為 AAc 鏈含有能解離的親水性-COOH
基，解離後形成 COO-會與水分子產生鍵結。所以當 AAc 溶液濃度增加，會使得 AAc 單體中之官能基
COO-數量增加，導致 AAc 單體能夠抓取更多的水分子，因此，提升 AAc 溶液濃度有助於水膠增加含
水率。由表 3.1 得知，當 AAc 濃度由 0.187 wt %上升至 0.75 wt %時，其含水率由 85.99 %升至 90.16 %。 
SEM 型態觀察 
為了觀察添加不同AAc濃度是否對凝膠強度有所影響，我們由SEM圖來觀察水膠所呈現的孔洞結構
差異性，為了不破壞其結構而得到孔洞，我們將製備好的各成份之水膠，浸泡於模擬腸液，再以冷凍
乾燥機將膨潤達平衡之水膠，其所含水分排除，並以SEM觀察其截面。由圖4可看出水膠截面皆有孔洞
的結構，而添加的AAc濃度越高，其孔洞也越緻密。因為Plu-PAA水膠形成凝膠是由Pluronic所形成外
殼為PEO親水基之微胞利用水分子為介質與PAA網狀結構產生氫鍵而形成一個半互穿型高分子網狀結
構體，所以當AAc濃度增加，所含的COO-親水基數量越多，因此能和越多個PEO親水基產生鍵結，使
其網狀結構會越緻密。 
黏度測試 
為了探討液態栓劑在生理狀和非生理狀態下之黏度和添加不同AAc濃度對黏度的影響，我們利用
Brookfield Viscometer DV-III固定Shear rate 10 s-1，溫度從4 ℃升至37 ℃測量在不同溫度之黏度。由圖5 (a)
可看出當液態栓劑在< 10 ℃時呈現液體狀態，因為Pluronic的PEO和PPO在低溫時皆為親水性因此不會
成膠，所以黏度非常低。當溫度升高時Pluronic之PPO會變成疏水性。因此Pluronic之間的共聚物會自發
性的凝集成球型Micelles，當溫度繼續升高，Micelles之間相互糾結纏繞而使Pluronic形成凝膠，所以溫
度逐漸升高黏度也隨之升高，液態栓劑在> 15 ℃後則由液態逐漸形成凝膠如圖5 (b)，由此可證實液態
栓劑在人體體溫確實能成膠。另外，從圖可看到增加AAc濃度其黏度越高，因為高濃度之AAc含有較
多的COO-親水基，因此能和較多的水產生氫鍵，而當液體分子間若有氫鍵形成，則分子間引力會增大，
故黏滯性較大【19】。因此提升AAc濃度有助於增加其黏度。

體是否有遲滯現象(Hysteresis)發生。遲滯現象為施加一應力於黏彈性材料時，存於其中的應力會隨著
時間而衰減或增加。由圖 6 可以看出於生理狀態時，在低 Shear rate 下，因為溶液抵抗板子(Plate)的旋
轉動力，而使 Shear stress 突然速上升，一直到 Shear stress 達最高點時，溶液才開始流動，此最高 Shear 
stress 即為溶液之降伏應力(Yield stress)，在超過降伏點後，溶液之 Shear stress 隨 Shear rate 緩慢增加，
顯示此時溶液呈現凝塑性行為(Pseudoplastic behavior)【20】。由圖可觀察到添加三種不同 AAc 濃度之

四、建議 
從研討會得知目前國外在生醫材料、藥物制放及組織工程的研究方向。無論在國內或國
外，其研究方向都走在時代的尖端，企圖突破困境，研發更具生物相容性之生醫材料及能更有
效控制藥物釋放之膠體。國外的研究大都以整合型計畫為主，充分結合人力與物力，研究內容
深而廣，從基礎材料之研發，至活體外及活體內之研究，甚至做臨床上之試驗，面面俱到。因
此，所研發成功的產品具有商業化的潛力，能直接造福人類。反觀國內之研究大都為個別型計
畫，因為無法有效的整合人力與物力，研究內容只能集中於材料的開發及其物性的探討，至於
是否能真正應用於人體上，則有待商確。如此，無形之中對人力與物力是一種浪費，這是國內
研發人才必須痛定思痛的地方。我個人擔任生醫材料及藥物制放課程之教授，參加這次研討會
後讓我對生醫材料及藥物制放在理論與實用上有更深一層的認識，日後在授課上更能駕輕就
熟。另外，日後個人之研究也會積極朝生醫材料及藥物制放之方向邁進，期盼在教學與研究上
能相輔相成。

 Novel pH-sensitive hydrogel for nano-pearl powder delivery 
Hong-Ru Lin, Yu-Chaio Chen, Yiu-Jiuan Lin1 and Ming-Hung Ling 
Department of Chemical and Materials Engineering, Southern Taiwan University, Tainan 710, Taiwan 
1Department of Nursing, Chung Hwa University of Medical Technology, Tainan 717, Taiwan 
hrlin@mail.stut.edu.tw 
Introduction 
To protect the protein drugs from the harsh 
environment in the stomach before they can be absorbed 
in the intestine, pH-sensitive polysaccharides like 
chitosan and alginate have attracted increasing attention 
due to their favorable properties including 
biocompatibility, biodegradability, pH sensitivity, and 
mucoadhesive property. In this study, a novel 
pH-sensitive hydrogel system composed of chitosan and 
alginate was developed for delivering of nano-pearl 
powder.  
 
Experimental 
Alginic acid (3 wt%) was dissolved in distilled 
water and stirred continuously with a homogenizer 
(Polytron; Kinematica, AG, Switzerland) at 26,000 rpm 
for the desired amount of time. During homogenization, 
nano-pearl powder was added to the solution in order to 
prepare hollow hydrogel beads for the drug-release 
experiments. Calcium chloride (0.25 M) was dissolved 
in distilled water and N, O-carboxymethyl chitosan was 
added to the solution during homogenization to prepare 
CaCl2 solution. The alginic acid/ nano-pearl powder 
solution was then dropped using a hypodermic syringe 
into CaCl2/ N, O-carboxymethyl chitosan solution to 
obtain the hydrogel beads. The in vitro release profiles 
of hydrogel beads were studied in simulated gastric and 
intestine mediums, respectively. IEC-6 cells were 
seeded and cultured on the hydrogel beads to examine 
the biocompatibility of the carrier. In the in vivo studies, 
the nano-pearl powder levels (percentage oral dose) in 
blood with time after the oral administration of hollow 
hydrogel beads loaded with nano-pearl powder was 
compared with the levels after the oral administration of 
an aqueous nano-pearl powder solution in mice. 
 
Results and discussion 
As shown in Fig. 1, the hollow hydrogel beads 
incorporated nano-pearl powder exhibited low release 
profile and no erosion in SGF (pH 1.2). Such behavior 
is contributed to hydrogen bonding between –COOH 
from alginate and –OH from chitosan, leading to 
densestructure of hydrogel beads. The amount of 
nano-pearl powder released from hollow hydrogel beads 
was lower than 28 % at acidic environment. As the 
beads transferred to SIF (pH 7.4), their release was 
faster than in SGF. Since the –COOH groups from 
alginate were deprotonated to -COO- when the buffer 
pH was high than its pKa (3.3~3.5), leading to loose 
structure of hydrogel beads. Under basic environment, 
the amount of nano-pearl powder released from hollow 
hydrogel beads was increased up to 36~84 %. Fig. 2 
shows the result of the IEC-6 cells culturing  in the 
well, containing different kinds of hydrogel beads after 
1, 3, 5, 7 days culture. The result shows the viable cells 
number increased slowly from 1 to 5 day, because the 
samples were acidic. The viable cells number increased 
obviously at day 7. We confirmed that the hollow 
hydrogel beads made from alginate-chitosan should not 
be cytotoxic. Fig. 3 shows the means release rate of 
nano-pearl powder after oral administration of 
nano-pearl powder solution and loaded nano-pearl 
powder beads. At 6 hr, the release rate of nano-pearl 
powder solution was already reached maximum (81.7 %) 
and the release was terminated at 12 hr. However, the 
release rate of loaded nano-pearl powder beads was 
sustained for the next 6 hr (67.1 %) in comparison with 
solution. The in vivo release studies confirmed that 
hollow hydrogel beads have the controlled and 
sustained release ability. 
 
Conclusions 
The results suggest the pH-sensitive alginate-chitosan 
hydrogel system can be used as a promising vehicle for 
oral delivery of health nutrients.

技術說明
(中文) 本實驗用原位成膠方式在同時改變pH及溫度兩個參數下，將Pluronic及聚丙烯酸
(PAA)混合使之成膠。在非生理狀態下(25 ℃，pH 4.0)，由於Pluronic及PAA皆
為液體或溶膠狀態，因此兩者可完全混合成水溶液，可方便利用注射方式將此液
體移入直腸。在直腸時由於處在生理條件(溫度為37 ℃，pH為7.4)，此時兩者皆
會成膠。反應過程中Pluronic會形成外殼為PEO親水基之微胞，此微胞利用水分
子為介質與PAA網狀結構產生氫鍵，形成半互穿型高分子網狀結構體(Semi-
Interpenetrating Networks, Semi-IPNs)。
(英文) In this study, we developed an in-situ gelling and injectable liquid suppository which 
composed of Pluronic and Poly(acryl acid) (PAA) solutions. Pluronic is a copolymer of 
polyethylene oxide (PEO) and polypropylene oxide (PPO), which has reversible gelling 
properties upon change of temperature. PAA is a hydrophilic polymer which forms gel 
when pH is greater than its pKa (4.75). Mixture of Pluronic and PAA can form strong gel 
in the physiological condition (pH 7.4 and 37 °C) due to the dual phase transition of these 
two polymers.
