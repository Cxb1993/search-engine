 A study of Modified Manufacturing Process of Carbon Nano Fiber Absorbent Prepared by 
Electro-Spun Composite Yarn Technology  
計畫編號：NSC 100-2221-E-011-042  
執行期限：100 年 8 月 1 日 至 101 年 10 月 31 日  
主 持 人：蘇清淵

摘 要 
本研究採用靜電紡複合紗技術製備奈米纖
維複合紗織物，此技術結合奈米纖維及一般紗線
的特性，利用傳統紗線做為芯紗，提供複合紗良
好的強力性質，並保有奈米纖維的功能。 
在實驗中，我們以奈米纖維複合紗織物作
為前軀體，喂入連續式高溫爐，經熱處理加工製
備成活性碳纖維，過程中改變活化源量，製備出
數種不同條件的碳奈米纖維吸附材，並進行產品
特性的測試及評估。實驗結果顯示，經碳化活化
後，產率為 17%，雖然強度減少約 90%，但不影
響加工程序的進行，最終順利製造出奈米碳纖維
吸附材，其 BET 比表面積約 1100m2/g，奈米纖
維直徑約 100-150nm 。 
關鍵字:靜電紡絲、奈米纖維、活性碳纖維布、 
複合紗 
Abstract 
In this study we utilized an electro-spun 
composite yarn technology to produce nanofiber 
composite yarn fabrics (NCF). This technique 
combined electron-spun nanofibers with traditional 
yarn, and it utilized traditional yarn to be the core 
yarn. And then it retained the good characteristics 
of nanofibers and provided excellent strength on 
composite yarn.   
In the experiment, we used nanofiber 
composite yarn knitted fabrics as the raw material 
of activated carbon fabrics at different activator 
flow rates to prepare several different conditions of 
carbon nanofiber absorbent, and evaluate their 
performance. After carbonization and activation, 
the yield rate was 17%. Although it lost a lot of 
strength, the processes had not been affected. In 
addition, the specific surface area of BET of carbon 
nano fiber absorbent was about 1100m
2
/g, and the 
diameter of nano fiber was 100-150nm. 
Key words: Electrospinning, Nanofiber, Activated  
carbon fabrics, Composite yarn. 
一、前言 
奈米纖維膜與活性碳纖維都是具有高表面
積與多用途之材料，由於具有良好的吸附特性，
可以廣泛應用於廢水與廢氣之處理，但是若要將
奈米纖維膜製成活性碳纖維，使其效果更上層樓，
則必須克服許多本身強度甚低及熱處理後脆化
而斷裂的缺陷，為改善此情況，本研究將結合奈
米纖維與傳統紗線，製備出比奈米纖維薄膜更高
強力，但又保有奈米纖維特性的織物，再將此織
物進行氧化、碳化及活化工程，使其製備成具有
活性碳特質的碳奈米纖維吸附材，並克服以往奈
米纖維在加工處理時強度不足之缺點。

布標準試驗法進行測試，檢測結果如表 3-4所示，

因為活化源量越大，纖維表面被攻擊越嚴重，微
孔逐漸增大變成中孔及大孔，導致強度降低。但
即使強度降低約 90%，也仍比尚未熱處理加工之
奈米纖維膜強度高上兩倍之多，已達到本實驗改
善奈米纖維膜強度不足之缺點，並且在加工過程
中可順利進行。

3.4限氧指數分析 
為確定氧化工程後之奈米纖維複合針織布
為氧化纖維，故採用限氧指數測試，一般織物以
限氧指數作為材料燃燒性質與氧化作用之指標，
限氧指數越高代表在一大氣壓下所需的氧氣量
越高，越難燃燒；相反的，在一大氣壓下所需的
氧氣量越低，越易燃燒。本研究中所生產之奈米
纖維複合紗針織布，經過穩定化工程後隨即變成
耐燃纖維，限氧指數高達 41，成為不燃纖維，
而使之成為不燃纖維之原因是聚丙烯腈的線性
分子鏈經過分子內環化與分子間的交鏈反應後
形成足以耐高溫裂解的梯狀結構，其耐燃性與化
學結構關係密切。

結論 
綜合以上分析，奈米纖維複合紗技術可以雙
噴頭紡絲方式量產，奈米纖維包覆型態優良，並
且可以編織成布，以織布型態改善奈米纖維非織
物強力不足，克服奈米纖維膜無法順利加工製造
成碳奈米纖維吸附才的缺點，經過氧化碳化活化
處理後，比表面積也高達 1100 m2/g。可以將此
技術轉移至產業界，進一步開發合作，使其應用
在纖維或科技產業上。預計下次可以聚丙烯腈或
嫘縈系織物為基材，將電紡奈米纖維膜電紡於基
材上，結合奈米纖維與基材之特性，樣品保有織
布基材的強力外亦保有奈米纖維膜的高比表面
積，使奈米纖維應用性更加廣泛。

Effect  of  Carbonizat ion Temperature on Microstructure 
and ElectrochemicalPropert ies  of  Activated Carbon 
Fabric  Applied in  Supercapacitor  
Ching-Iuan SU, Wun-Ching Shih*, Chi-Ming Wang,Ching-Hsiang Lu 
Department of Material and Science Engineering, National Taiwan University of 
Science and Technology, Taiwan 
*M9804135@mail.ntust.edu.tw 
______________________________________________________________
_____________ 
Abstract 
This study used viscose rayon-based activated fabrics as 
electrode of supercapacitors. First, Rayon-based fabrics 
underwent oxidation, carbonization and activation in a semi-
open high temperature erect furnace to produce activated 
carbon fabrics (ACF). Then microstructure and electric 
properties of the ACF obtained under difference carbonization 
temperatures of 600-1000℃  were measured. By the X-ray 
diffraction (XRD), the crystallinity was improved as the 
carbonization temperature increased, resulting in reducing 
resistance (12.9 Ω-cm). In addition, the specific surface area 
increased with the carbonization temperature increased. The 
specific surface area was measured by the N2 adsorption 
analysis. Furthermore, by cyclic voltammograms measurement 
in 1 M sulfuric acid electrolyte, The ACF electrode (SN10) 
has outstanding capacitance (338 Fg-1) prepared in 1000℃  
carbonization temperature.

Morphology and microstructure of aggregates and gelation behavior of 
P3HT/PCBM composites in xylene solutions

ABSTRACT 
In this work, we investigate the morphology and microstructure of the aggregates, and 
gelation behavior of Poly(3-hexylthiophene) (P3HT) conjugated polymer blended 
with [6,6]-phenyl-C61-butyric acid methyl ester (PCBM) composites in xylene solu-
tion as functions of PCBM content and aging time by the means of ageing time test, 
wide angle X-ray diffraction (WAXD), scanning electron microscopy (SEM), 
UV-visible absorption (UV-vis) and photoluminescence (PL) spectra. The result re-
veals that the gelation time of P3HT/xylene solution decreases markedly with in-
creasing P3HT concentration. The photophysical properties; e.g., PL and UV-vis 
spectra, of the P3HT aggregates in P3HT/xylene solution increase as P3HT concen-
tration and ageing time are raised. It indicates that the well soluble P3HT chains in 
solution state assemble into stiff sheetlike aggregates, which associates by rodlike 
nanowhisker of P3HT polymers, promote by the P3HT content and ageing time. In 
comparison, the gelation mechanism of P3HT/PCBM/xylene solution changes signif-
icantly as PCBM is added in P3HT/xylene solution. The photophysical properties of 
the P3HT aggregate drop clearly, whereas, that of the PCBM associate promote 
markedly with increasing PCBM content. The morphology of the aggregates of 
P3HT/PCBM composites indicates that the self-assemble of the PCBM molecules and 
P3HT chains simultaneously to form the PCBM nanocrystals and P3HT nanosheets, 
respectively. Subsequently, the PCBM nanocrystals absorb the P3HT nanosheets to 
form the cabbage-like or core-shell structure. Therefore, themicrostructure and the 
thermal properties of the P3HT/PCBM nanocomposite prove that two different crystal 
structures coexisting in the dried P3HT/PCBM thin films. The thermal properties of 
the crystalline P3HT in P3HT/PCBM thin films lower than that in primitive P3HT 
thin film attribute to the crystallizability of P3HT is inhibited by the PCBM molecular 
forming an imperfect crystal of P3HT is observed in P3HT/PCBM thin films.
