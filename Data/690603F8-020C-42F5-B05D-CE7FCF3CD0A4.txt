中文摘要 
 
製備有機高分子薄膜可藉由利用TAN與MMA共聚物當作疏水性材料與無機矽粉末作
為表面粗糙度而被發展。我們選擇上述含有 70wt%的 TAN 共聚物溶液作為製備超疏水性
薄膜，這是因為它具有合適的接觸角度 119.4°。塗佈溶液若加入矽粉末於共聚物溶液，即
為一步驟法；若是加入矽粉末於單體中後再反應，即為二步驟法。藉由旋轉塗佈法將塗佈
溶液塗佈於基材上是很容易獲得的。薄膜性質的特性分析可以藉由 AFM、SEM、接觸角儀
與可視光光譜儀來觀察。 
結果顯示藉由一步驟法所製備之薄膜(塗佈溶液中含有 37.6%的矽粉末)接觸角大於
150°，但是可見光穿透度僅 30%。另一方面，利用二步驟法所製備之薄膜(塗佈溶液中含有
20%的矽粉末)接觸角大於 160°，並且其可見光穿透度大於 90%。 
Wenzel 模型之方程式也可分別藉由疏水性(TAN)與親水性(POA)材料來證實。這是由
於親水性高分子 POA 接觸角為 32.1°。但是超疏水與超親水性薄膜可以分別藉由導入粗糙
度於疏水性與親水性表面而獲得。 
關鍵字：含氟高分子共聚物，超疏水，接觸角，矽粉，蓮花效應 
 
 
 
ABSTRACT 
 
Organic superhydrophobic films were prepared by utilizing TA-N fluoroalkylate (TAN) and 
methyl methacrylate (MMA) copolymer as water-repellent materials and inorganic silica powder 
as surface roughness material has been developed. Coating solutions prepared by adding silica 
powders into copolymer solution directly (one-step method) and by adding silica powders into 
monomers and allowing them to react (two-step method). The results showed that contact angles 
of the films prepared by one-step method (37.6 wt % of silica powders in the coating solution) 
were greater than 1508, but the transmittance of the film at visible light was only 30%. On the 
other hand, the contact angle of films prepared by two-step method (20 wt % of silica powders in 
the coating solution) was greater than 1608 and the transmittance of the film was greater than 
90%. The contact angle of the film prepared by poly(octyl acrylate), POA, was 32.18, but while 
introducing silica powder into the system, the contact angle of the film was reduced to be smaller 
than 58. Thus, superhydrophobic and superhydrophilic films can be obtained by introducing a 
roughening material on the hydrophobic surface and the hydrophilic surface, respectively. 
Key words: fluorinated-copolymer; superhydrophobic; contact angle; silica powders; lotus effect

4 結果與討論 
4.1 不同高分子的接觸角 
    高分子的疏水與親水性質與極性有直接關係。一般所有的丙烯酸高分子有一些極性是
由於酯類的鏈與 C=C 雙鍵；然而，極性將大大地被高分子的骨架結構影響。為了調查這些
效應，不同的丙烯酸單體 MMA、t-BMA、n-BMA、TAN 與 OA 將各自被聚合。所有的高
分子薄膜是將合適的實驗條件利用旋轉塗佈於玻璃基材上而製造。高分子薄膜的疏水與親
水性質是利用接觸角測量，其結果顯示如表 1。表中顯示 PTAN 薄膜(含氟)有最大的接觸角
度 123.3°。PMMA 的接觸角大於 PBMA 是由於其酯結構具有較多的碳數而降低親水性。然
而，POA 的接觸角只有 32.1°。這暗示其酯結構(辛酯)的最大碳數並不會提升薄膜的疏水性。
雖然 PTAN 薄膜的接觸角是最大，但是 PTAN 具有最高的結晶溫度，這是很難溶解於有機
溶劑並且其本身也很昂貴。因此，為了增加在溶劑裡的溶解度與基材的附著力並減少 TAN
的使用量，TAN 將與其他單體形成共聚物。MMA 被選擇與 TAN 共聚合並且上述之共聚物
薄膜的實驗數據顯示如表 2。結果指出當 TAN 的含量超過 5%時，共聚物薄膜的交觸角將
大於 100°。甚至 TAN 的含量超過 70%(TM70)，共聚物的接觸角將維持約 120°。 
    不同組成的 TAN 與 MMA 共聚物其接觸角顯示如圖 1。這個圖透露了添加少量的 TAN
單體將使得共聚物的接觸角快速增加。這是由於添加 TAN 單體將導致表面氟原子比例的提
升。這將造成共聚物的表面能減少並且提升共聚物的接觸角。當 TAN 單體的含量到達 44%，
共聚物的接觸角將大於 115°。因此，為了降低主要成本的使用量並且加強含氟高分子的應
用，因此我們選擇 TM70 做為後續實驗的樣品。

可以同時並存的。藉由 TM70 在平坦基材上製備薄膜只存在著疏水性質。為了增加表面的
粗糙度，矽粉末將加入 TM70 溶液。這裡有兩個方法製備帶有疏水性質的塗佈溶液。其一

Fabrication of superhydrophobic silica-based surface with solvent-induced phase 
separation method 
 
Kuei-Chien Chang, Hui Chen, Yu-Kai Chen 
Department of chemical and materials engineering, National Central University, Taoyuan County 
32001, Taiwan, R.O.C. 
 
Introduction 
From the previous studies, superhydrophobic property must constitute two important 
elements: One is low surface energy substances and the other is surface roughness. As for low 
surface energy substances, because bare-fluorinated substances have expensive price and easily to 
pollute the environment, chemical modification of surface has been prepared by modification of 
CH3-terminated group [2].  
In general, many studies showed that preparation of superhydrophobic films by phase 
separation method was all most for organic polymers [3-5]. In this research, superhydrophobic 
silica-based films were prepared by solvent-induced phase separation method via sol-gel process. 
However, introducing sol-gel process into phase separation method would cause the films more 
hard. The roughness of surface of film is easily to prepare by removing organic part of film at 
high temperature. It was different from the common phase separation method to fabricate the 
ultra-water repellent thin films. 
 
Experimental 
   The organic-inorganic solution was prepared by two solutions, one was silica sol precursor 
solution that was prepared by mixing TEOS, water (pH=2) and EtOH (TEOS : H2O : EtOH=1 : 4 : 
4), then reacted at 60˚C for 4 hr. Another was PEG4000 polymer solution. The organic-inorganic 
solution was reacted at 60˚C for 2 min and then at 18˚C for 1 min. The films were prepared by 
casting organic-inorganic solution on the glass. In all cases, all coating films were reacted at 
250˚C for 2 hr and then raised the temperature to 500˚C for 4 hr. HMDS was chosen to reflux 
with as-prepared films at 110˚C for 6 hr. The process and parameters of preparation of 
superhydrophobic films were shown in Figure 1.  
  In order to increase the roughness of the films, the multi-layer of films were prepared by 
casting organic-inorganic solution on the glass several times or adding n-hexane (poor solvent) on 
the coating layer to cause early phase separation on the films.

Preparation of inorganic molecularly imprinted polymers by Sol-Gel method 
 
Hui Chen*, Hung-Sen Wei, Sung-Chuan Lee, Jia-Yi Wu 
Department of Chemical and Materials Engineering,  
National Central University, Jhong-Li City, Taoyuan, Taiwan 32054, R. O. C 
 
Introduction 
In the molecular imprinting process, functional monomers are either covalently [1] or 
non-covalently [2] interacted with a template. Most investigations of imprinted polymers were 
produced with pure organic polymers, there are limited reports on inorganic molecularly 
imprinted polymers (IMIPs). Sol-gels possess have several physical properties, as they exhibit 
porous, rigid, mechanical properties and chemically inert.  
In this study, the influence of varying parameters of sol-gel process on IMIPS is discussed. IMIPs 
were synthesized on model compound, caffeine by adding pore-forming agent and base catalyst 
sol-gel processes, respectively [3]. The template can be removed by extraction or calcination. 
Therefore, the influence of IMIPs prepared by these two removing template methods is reported. 
 
Experimental 
IMIPs prepared by adding pore-forming agent method 
Tetraethoxysilane (TEOS) (20.8g) and acidic water (pH=2, 7.2g) were well mixed then ethyl 
alcohol (14.4g) was added. The so-formed mixture was stirred in 60℃ for 3 hours to form 
precursor solution. Caffeine was dissolved completely in ethyl alcohol with or without lactic acid. 
The precursor was then added into the above solution. And allowed for reaction in 60℃ in close 
system for one day, then followed by reaction in open system for two days. The product obtained 
were ground and sieved (38µm~53µm.). Caffeine was removed by calcination at 600℃ or 
extraction. 
IMIPs prepared by adding base catalyst method  
Caffeine was added into a mixture of TEOS and acidic water as prepared above and stirred for 3 
hours at room temperature. Ammonium solution is then added to adjust the pH. It was placed in 
the 60℃ oven for one day for closed system reaction. The monoliths were ground and sieved 
(38µm~53µm). Caffeine was removed via calcination. 
 
Results and discussion 
In order to investigate the effect of introducing lactic acid in the system, quantity of lactic acid
