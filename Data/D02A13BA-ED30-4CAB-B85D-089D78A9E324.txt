缺血性中風已經是現代社會造成失能與死亡
的主要原因。近來對於缺血性中風的治療嘗試
逆轉因腦組織缺血帶來的不良反應，並有研究
指出近紅外光雷射在體內的環境下具有神經
保護的作用。本研究第一年的目標為建立一套
量測大鼠神經血管連結的標準流程，並用來當
作評估缺血性中風大鼠在近紅外光雷射治療
後腦部功能性恢復的指標。這套評估系統可以
以非侵入式的方式同時量測體感覺誘發電位
與事件相關的光訊號。在系統驗證的階段，總
頸動脈栓塞手術被用來驗證我們非侵入式量
測神經血管連結的實驗流程。健全的活底實驗
已經證實了我們目前非侵入式探頭的設計可
以從體外量測到體感覺誘發電位與當時血液
動力學的變化。時頻分析 (Time Frequency 
Analysis)也同時被應用在缺血性中風大鼠的
電訊號分析上。分析結果顯示出在缺血性中風
的同時，其誘發電位訊號有像高頻移動的趨
勢，這結果也符合先前的相關研究。這個系統
將被應用在我們未來近紅外光雷射治療的時
序評估的相關實驗，並同時搭配新開發的多通
道近紅外光血氧評估系統。

Ischemic stroke is a leading cause of

to establish a standard procedure for evaluating

In the validation trial,  the occlusion

Ⅰ. INTRODUCTION 
Ischemic stroke is that the thrombus 
obstructs vessels in the brain and causes the 
neuron and neuroglia of obstructed area 
suffering from the deprivation of nutrition 
and oxygen. Neuroprotective drug and 
thrombolysis agent are the most famous 
therapeutic strategies used in acute stage, 
but the time window is about three hours 
from the onset of stroke. The non-drug 
approach might provide new opportunities 
in addition to more traditional 
pharmaceutical therapies (Singhal et al., 
2008). Recently, it has been proven that near 
  
cerebral oxygenation change of both 
hemispheres at the same time.

The NILT system employed microprocessor to 
control auto-power control IC that can provide a 
feedback current to laser diode and ensure the 
stable power output. The NILT device in 
continuous wave (CW) mode of laser diode can 
transcranially illuminates the hemisphere

Animal Preparation 
All experimental protocols were approved 
by the National Cheng Kung University Medical 
College Animal Use Committee. Male Sprague- 
Dawley rats weighing between 280 to 350 g 
were housed under 12-hour dark/light cycle and 
fasted overnight but allowed access to water 
before the experiment. All rats were 
anaesthetized with 1% isoflurane mixed with 
oxygen. Body temperature was monitored by 
a rectal probe and maintained at 36.5°C to 
37.5°C by a heating pad. 
 
Measurement of neurovascular responses 
Each animal was put on a stereotaxic frame 
(Model 902, David Kopf Instruments, Tujunga, 
CA, USA) with the skull oriented horizontally. 
The surface recording probe was conducted to 
the bilateral S1FL (4.0 mm lateral and 0.5 mm 
anterior to bregma). After all before  
measurement, anesthesia was transition and 
maintained with 50 mg/kg of α-chlorolose 
(Sigma-Aldrich, USA) given intraperitoneally. 
The measurement was started after 45-mins later 
for transition. Forepaw stimulation was 
performed to evoke the SI cortex activity which 
the neurovascular responses occur when the 
SSEPs were evoked. A GUI program was 
developed with LabVIEW to control the 
stimulation protocols, and to monitor the raw 
data that is read from the DAQ device. All 
recordings were taken after a stable baseline was 
obtained. Both electrical signals and optical 
signals are sampled and saved concurrently with 
a data update rate of 20 kHz. 
 
Cerebral ischemia 
. To cause an acute cerebral ischemia in the 
somatosensory cortex, unilateral common 
carotid artery occlusion was done on the right 
side with the nylon suture ligation for two 
minutes, hemoglobin and deoxygenated 
hemoglobin were continuously recorded during 
forepaw stimulation. For observing the effect of 
vascular response in cerebral oxygenation, we 
withdrew the nylon suture back after 2 min 
artery occlusion measurement. 
For comparison of acute ischemia and 
chronic ischemia, middle artery occlusion was 
done on the right side with the 4-0 nylon suture 
with silicon coated round tip to block the origin 
of MCA, after 60, 90 and 120 minutes the 
reperfusion process was done by draw out the 
suture. 
 
 
Figure 3. The diagram of middle cerebral artery

Data Analysis 
For each electrophysiological measurement, 
coherent averaging was applied to construct the 
waveform of SEP with enhanced signal-to-noise 
ratio. The raw signals of cortical field potentials 
were analyzed with Matlab programs. The 
evoked potentials were subsequently analyzed 
with Time Frequency Analysis (TFA) to provide 
morphological change in frequency spectrum of 
SSEP caused by brain injury.

Ⅳ. DISCUSSION and CONCLUSION 
In this study, we successfully use our 
cranial probe to carry out non-invasive 
recording of SSEPs for evaluation of 
cerebral ischemia At the stage of system 
validation, common artery occlusion animal 
experiment was performed to verify the 
feasibility neurovascular response and 
neural response, which can evaluate the 
pattern information of brain function in 
small animal model. In addition to the 
traditional analysis, we have showed that 
TFA also could provide morphological 
change in frequency spectrum of EP 
potentials cause by brain injury (Braun et al., 
1997; Hu et al., 2000).  
Our results are in agreement with most 
related animal studies using diffuse optical 
tomography, LDF, or fMRI.  Our first-year  
study has non-invasively measured neuronal 
activity as well as hemodynamic parameters 
via noninvasive optical imagine provide a 
non invasive approach for evaluation 
cerebral ischemia The cerebrovascular 
responses to focal ischemia and evoked 
activation were both observed by our 
integrated surface recording SSEP-NIRS 
system.  
The combination of assessment of 
neurovascular coupling and NILT can be 
applied for monitoring the progressive 
recovery after treatment in ischemic animal 
model which would address a significant 
unmet medical needed. We will use the 
system established in our pilot study to 
research the neurovascular response of 
ischemic stroke animals under NILT in 
addition .to conventional approaches of 
neurobehavior tests. From analyzing the 
pattern of neurovascular coupling pattern 
between control group and treated groups, the 
self-recovery and treatment effect can be 
distinguished. 
Using multiple NIRS, we successfully 
detected absolute concentration of 
hemoglobin and tissue oxygen saturation of 
brain tissue before and after ischemic stroke. 
The oxygen level of lesion side of the brain 
was dramatically decreased after middle 
cerebral artery was occluded. This 
phenomenon was accompanied by function 
impairment, which was revealed in SSEP 
and EROS loss. There seems to be a 
correlation between SSEP activity and tissue 
oxygen level (Fig 12).

 此外，在會議中我們也分享神經復健的經驗，本研究建立了利用近紅外光光
譜儀偵測進行動態活動時大腦活性之可行性。其中風病患踩車活動之腦部造影結
果可應用於建立復健最佳化選擇之新策略。大多數的中風患者具有因動作控制能
力失調造成的功能障礙，進而影響其生活品質。雖然過去的研究證實復健對於恢
復中風病人的動作表現有其療效，但少有研究探討復健活動對於中風病患腦部皮
質重塑性的影響。由於過去腦功能造影技術的限制，大部分的腦功能研究著重於
上肢精細動作之探討，有關於下肢的功能性活動如踩車卻鮮少被探討。踩車活動
常被用於當作急性期中風病患的行走前訓練，也亦被證實對於增加肌肉力量、降
低痙攣以及改善神經肌肉控制有效果。由於近紅外光光譜儀（near infrared 
spectroscopy）為近年來發展之新型腦造影技術，且最大優勢為可用來偵測受試
者進行動態活動之腦部活化。我們的研究利用近紅外光光譜儀偵測因腦神經細胞
活化造成的腦組織血氧濃度變化去探測中風病患在進行踩車活動時的腦部活動
以及踩車訓練對於中風病患踩車表現、功能性恢復以及腦部重塑性的效果。在偵
測腦訊號的同時，踩車時股直肌之肌電訊號可以量化踩車的對稱性，以期瞭解中
風病患動作恢復之可能的神經機制。 
本實驗第一階段徵召健康年輕與健康老人進行功能性近紅外光光譜儀於偵
測進行踩車活動的腦部活性變化之適用性探討。其結果發現主動踩車時會活化感
覺運動皮質區。而健康老人在進行具有速度回饋的主動性踩車活動時，其踩車表
現會改善。其輔助運動區以及運動前區的活化程度也大於健康年輕人。第二階段
則徵召中風病患進行相同的踩車任務，量測其大腦活性變化與踩車表現。其結果
顯示，在進行主動踩車活動與被動踩車時，感覺運動皮質區的活化程度相對大於
其他皮質區域。但相較於主動踩車，在進行被動踩車時其健側的活化程度較小。
而速度回饋有助於改善中風病患踩車的對稱性與速度變異性，而健側運動前區的
活化程度增加推測為中風病患處理速度回饋訊號之代償機制，而受傷對側為主的
活化模式造成了不對稱的腦部活化。本研究第三階段為探討主動與被動踩車訓練
對中風病患踩車表現、功能性表現以及大腦皮質活性之影響。本實驗將十八位皮
質下中風患者隨機分配至主動踩車訓練與被動踩車訓練組，分別進行每次三十分
鐘、每周兩次，為期八周的踩車訓練。後測結果發現，主動踩車組其日常活動功
能、功能性表現與本體感覺較治療前進步，被動踩車組治療前後其功能與踩車表
現則無明顯差異。而主動踩車組之後測顯示健側運動輔助區與運動前區的活化程
度較前測增加， 而被動踩車訓練組則無明顯差異。

技術說明
(中文) 首先發展連續式進紅外光譜儀，以非侵入式方式偵測大腦組織血紅素含量變化。
以690nm以及830nm之雷射二極體作為光源，以不同載波調變後，於接收端經由雪
崩光電二極體接收，經過PLL電路解調，還原各波長之光學訊號直流成分，再以
比爾藍伯定理計算出組織內血紅素的變化量。而頭皮腦電圖儀之設計為，將經由
彈簧電極所接收到的電訊號，經過儀表放大器放大，並以低通濾波器過濾後，由
電腦接收。將光學系統搭配非侵入式之頭皮腦電圖儀，針對中風患部量測腦電波
以及神經耦合血管連結所產生之功能性血氧反應。
(英文) A noninvasive approach for recording somatosensory evoked potentials (SSEPs) and 
event related optical optical signals (EROS). The integration of SSEP recording 
electrodes and optical probe for surface recording were developed noninvasive. In the 
validation trial, the occlusion experiments were used to confirm noninvasive 
measurements. Robust animal study had been performed and our result shows that SSEPs 
and emodynamic responses can be acquired using surface recording probe.

在臨床上，中風的診斷仰賴神經行為量表，或是斷層掃描、磁振造影或正子影像等技術。
行為量表的敏感度不高，並仰賴醫師的經驗；而其他大腦影像上因使用成本高或暴露劑
量的疑慮，而無法對中風做連續時程的監測。近紅外光譜儀以及腦電圖儀皆有相關之市
售產品，技術門檻不高。本技術之優勢為，非侵入式且高安全性，沒有暴露過量的風險，
適合發展成為床側長期監測系統。技術關鍵在於，針對中風設計專一性條件以及測量方
式，並發展人體試驗以驗證功效。

1.我們這個研究實現了非侵入式測量體感覺誘發電位，並證明了可以另外使用
時頻分析提供除了型態學外額外的資訊。 
2.目前已經可以偵測到正常及缺血性中風大鼠模型在前腳電刺激下腦部血液動
力學的變化。 
3.缺血性中風大鼠模型的行為分析以進行基礎分析，其結果將為未來 NILT 治療
的基礎資料。 
4.利用矽膠包覆之尼龍縫線於中腦動脈產生拴塞兩個小時，可穩定形成缺血性
中風模式。 
5.以多距離近紅外光譜儀可成功地即時量測中風時，大腦組織內的血氧狀態。
6.以單距離連續式近紅外光譜儀，以及非侵入式頭皮腦電圖儀，可觀察經由腳
掌電刺激所誘發之體感覺皮質誘發電位，並作為中風損傷大腦之功能性指標。
7.本計劃成功整合光電量測技術，提供一非侵入式連續觀測中風進程之大腦影
像技術，對於中風病程的分析，以及治療成效的評估，將有莫大的助益。 
8.實驗內容已投稿至相關的研討會論文，後續成果預期在第一年完成期刊論文
投稿。
