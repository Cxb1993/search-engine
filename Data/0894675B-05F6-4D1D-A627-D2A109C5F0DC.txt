本計畫在使用stereoregular Metallocene 配位聚合觸媒系統，以控制聚合物之立體規則
性，並在使用特殊結構之轉移劑之條件下，誘導選擇性鏈轉移反應之進行以合成含末端官

block copolymers)。

自我組裝(Self-assembly)，探討結晶誘導之自我組裝行為，與自我組裝行為中立體規則性嵌
段間受立體規則性之影響，為近年來相當熱門之課題。然而具備化學結構均一並且可應用
於奈米結構自我組裝之立體規則性嵌段共聚物，目前並無有效之合成方法，因此
Stereoregular block copolymers之合成需仰賴新合成技術之開發。

Key words: Selective Chain Transfer Reaction, Stereospecific Polymerization, Stereoregular 
Block Copolymer, Self-assembled Nanostructures 
（一） 計畫英文摘要。（五百字以內） 
This project is aim to develop a new synthetic method that offers the successful preparations 
of structurally well-defined stereoregular block copolymers, which are capable of self-organizing 
into consistent nanomorphologies. Block copolymers are characterized by its unique chemical 
structures of combining different polymer blocks that are stung together by chemical bonding. 
Due to these unique chemical structures, block copolymers can self-organize in bulk or in solvent 
into unique ordered nanopatterns that have found nanotechnological applications as in modern 
devices. As a result, self-assembly studies of block copolymers have drawn considerable research 
attention in recent year.  
To date, block copolymers can be typically prepared by sequential polymerization of different 
monomers using living polymerization route (living anionic, free radical etc.). Unfortunately, 
stereoregular diblock copolymers can not be directly prepared by these methods as living 
polymerization routes fail to induce the stereosepcific polymerization of α-olefins because of 
their absence of chiral reactive center. In order to provide stereoregular control during α-olefin 
polymerizations, stereoregular catalysts (e.g., stereoregular metallocene catalysts) could be used 
in facilitating stereoregularity control. Of note, block copolymers are unable to prepare by the 
direct utilization of of metallocene catalysts because of metallocene catalyst’s non-living nature.

三、報告內容: 
 3.1 前言： 
嵌段共聚合物(Block Copolymers)為結構上相當特殊之聚合物。一般而言，嵌段共聚合
物由不同化學結構之嵌段加以組成，且在不同嵌段之末端 (chain end)以化學鍵相連；也因
此化學結構均一(structurally uniform)且具備不相容嵌段之嵌段共聚合物，可行奈米結構之自
我組裝(self-assembly)行為，並藉以形成特殊之有序奈米微結構(ordered nanomorphologies)，
也因此針對 Block Copolymers 之合成與奈米結構之自我組裝行為研究，為近年來高分子化
學與高分子物理最熱門之研究課題。 
目前，嵌段共聚合物之主要合成方法為運用living polymerization以進行不同單體之接續
聚合反應(consecutive polymerization of different monomers)，藉以成功的合成具備不同嵌段
之Block Copolymers，並控制其結構及分子量與分子量分佈之均一性。然而常見之living 
polymerization (如living anionic, living cationic, living free radical)其反應之活性中心(分別為
陰離子、陽離子與自由基)為非掌性結構，因此常見之living polymerization無法控制聚合反
應之立體規則選擇性以合成含立體規則嵌段之嵌段共聚合物(Stereoregular Block 
Copolymers)。因此Stereoregular Block Copolymers之有效合成需仰賴新合成技術之開發。
值得注意的是，含立體規則嵌段之嵌段共聚合物，因為具備特殊之立體規則嵌段，因此在
分子層級(molecular level)之奈米結構之自我組裝(self-assembly)行為中，被發現會因為立體
規則嵌段結構間之立體效應之影響而形成特殊之奈米微結構1。另一方面，由於立體規則嵌
段可形成結晶，因此運用結晶行為以誘導相分離之奈米結構自我組裝2，亦為使用
Stereoregular Block Copolymers以進行Self-assembly研究之優勢。由於Polyolefin-based
