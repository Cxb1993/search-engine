摘要 
   鐃鈸型壓電陶瓷換能器在受力作用時，所產生的電功率輸出受端帽金屬厚度及(頂端直徑/空腔直徑)
比所影響。當(頂端直徑/空腔直徑)比為定值時，開路電壓及短路電流會隨端帽金屬厚度的減少而增加。
當端帽金屬厚度為定值時，短路電流隨(頂端直徑/空腔直徑)比的增加而上升，但開路電壓會隨(頂端直
徑/空腔直徑)比的增加先增加而後減少。所產生的電功率會隨著(頂端直徑/空腔直徑)比的增加而上升，
當(頂端直徑/空腔直徑)比達到開路電壓折返時，電功率將不再增加。 
關鍵詞: 鐃鈸型換能器，壓電陶瓷，發電地板 
 
Abstract 
The electrical power output of PZT Cymbal type transducer depends dependent on the metal thickness and 
the (apex diameter/cavity diameter) ratio. With the same (apex diameter/cavity diameter) ratio, both the open 
circuit voltage and the short circuit current are increased with the decreasing of metal thickness. With the 
same metal thickness, the short circuit current is increased with the increasing of (apex diameter/cavity 
diameter) ratio. But the open circuit voltage decreased at first, and then increased with the increasing of (apex 
diameter/cavity diameter) ratio. The output electrical power increased with the increasing of (apex 
diameter/cavity diameter) ratio, but when the (apex diameter/cavity diameter) ratio reach to the value while 
open circuit voltage decreased, the output electrical power reach to the maximum value. 
Keywords: Cymbal type transducer, piezoelectric ceramics, powder platform  
 
前言 
人類生活的環境裡，存在有各種不同的能源，這些能源可能是自然生成(如太陽光所產生的光能、
溫差所產生的熱能)，或人為製造(如引擎工作時、人類行走時所產生的振動能)。這些能量大多消散在
自然界而沒有被利用，如果能將這些能量加以擷取使它轉換成可用的電能，則可減少對石油、煤等的
需求，因而減少二氧化碳的排放，並降低對環境的破壞。因此能量擷取(energy harvesting)這一個新興
的名詞，所代表的意義是將周邊環境中所未利用的能源如(太陽能、熱能、風能及振動能等)，將其轉換
成可用的電能，以達到能源回收再利用的目的，並減少對現有能源，如石油、煤等的需求，以減緩地
球暖化速度。 
 
在各種能源裡，振動能是最常存在於人類生活環境中，例如人們的行走、引擎或機器的運轉、海浪
的波動、風的吹動等，都可發現振動能的存在。同時振動能的擷取具有不受環境及天候等因素影響的
優點，例如太陽能發電在沒有太陽的時候則無法工作，而風力發電在風速低於某一低限時則無法工作，
且它們設置的地點會受到地理環境的影響，振動能的擷取則不會受到這些因素的影響。 
 
在自然界所產生的振動能可經由三種不同的方式來擷取以轉換成為電能，分別為壓電式、電磁式與
靜電式等三種[1-3]。其中壓電發電在振動能擷取及轉換上，具有最高的能量密度，且其構造最為簡單
可行。因此本計畫主要的研究就是以壓電陶瓷來擷取外界的振動能，並將它轉換成為電能輸出，以供
後續的負載使用。 
 
壓電式振動能擷取的研究是由上一世紀 90 年代開始，1996 年 Umeda 等人[4]以自由落球的方式來
打擊 PZT 壓電陶瓷片，以建立 PZT 壓電陶瓷片在受到衝擊機械能作用時轉變成為電能的等效電路模
型，他們同時也探討 PZT 壓電陶瓷片配合橋式整流器及電容器時的能量儲存特性。1996 年 Starner 等
人[5]探討如何將人腿部的運動及血壓所產生的機械能等經由 PZT 壓電陶瓷片來轉換成電能。1998 年
MIT 的 Kymissis 及 Shenck 等人[6]開發具有發電功能的鞋子，它是將壓電聚合物(PVDF)及 PZT 壓電陶
瓷分別裝設於鞋尖及鞋跟兩部份，藉由人在行走時，對鞋尖的彎曲與鞋跟的敲擊，經由壓電材料將機
械能量轉換成為電能，此一裝置在行走頻率在 0.8Hz 下最高已可連續提供 3V 與 1.3mW 的電能。 
 
到了本世紀壓電式能量擷取的研究不斷的增長，並達到某一程度的實用化。2007 年兩位 MIT 的學
生提出在都市區域內建立壓電地板的觀念[source: Christian Science Monitor]，他們的構想是在鬧區例如

結論 
鐃鈸型壓電陶瓷換能器在受力作用時，所產生的電功率輸出受端帽金屬厚度及(頂端直徑/空腔直徑)
比所影響。當端帽金屬厚度固為1mm時，短路電流隨(頂端直徑/空腔直徑)比的增加而上升，但開路電
壓會隨(頂端直徑/空腔直徑)比的增加先增加，當比值約為0.4後，開路電壓會隨(頂端直徑/空腔直徑)比
的增加而後減少。所產生的電功率會隨著(頂端直徑/空腔直徑)比的增加而上升，當(頂端直徑/空腔直徑)
比達到開路電壓折返時，電功率將不再增加而達到最大值。當(頂端直徑/空腔直徑)比為0.57的定值時，
開路電壓及短路電流會隨端帽金屬厚度的減少而增加。

III. Results and discussions 
   The piezoelectric resonator consists of a thin disc of piezoelectric ceramic material with an electrode on 
either side; and the disc is polarized in thickness direction. Apart from the behavior in the vicinity of the series 
resonance frequency of fundamental modes, every resonator is characterized by a number of overtones which 
are typical for the mode of vibration under consideration [9]. 
 
   The resonance spectrums of PZT ceramic resonators are shown in Fig.1. When the disc is fully electroded, 
besides the fundamental mode, some overtones exist. There are total four overtones appearing in the 
frequency range from 100kHz to 1MHz for fully electroded resonator in this study. But when the electrode 
sizes are reduced, some overtones are disappearing; such as the resonator with 80% and 40% of electrode 
sizes, the second order overtone is disappearing. When the electrode sizes are reduced, some small spurious 
resonance peaks exist among the main resonance peaks. These small spurious resonance peaks may be caused 
by the twist or bending mode.  
 
The series resonance frequency of fundamental modes of increases with the reduction of electrode sizes, 
as shown in Fig.2. 
 
   The planar electromechanical coupling factor kp of the resonator increased first with the reduction of 
electrode size, and reached a maximum value at 80% of the electrode size, and then decreased with the further 
reduction of the electrode size, as shown in Fig.3. The electrode of smaller size than the piezoelectric plate is 
commonly used to minimize the influence of the mechanical support at the edges so to reduce the leakage of 
vibration energy. For those resonators, the corresponding vibration energy is trapped in the vicinity of the 
electroded region [10-11] so to lead to the increasing of electromechanical coupling factors. When the 
electrode decreases further, the reduction of kp is due to the change of vibration modes. 
 
IV. Conclusions 
   When the disc is fully electroded, besides the fundamental mode, some overtones exist. When the 
electrode sizes are reduced, some overtones are disappearing; such as the resonator with 80% and 40% of 
electrode sizes, the second order overtone is disappearing, and some small spurious resonance peaks exist 
among the main resonance peaks. These small spurious resonance peaks may be caused by the twist or 
bending mode. The planar electromechanical coupling factor kp of the resonator increases first with the 
reduction of electrode sizes, and reaches a maximum value at 80% of electrode size, and then decreases with 
the further reduction of electrode sizes
