一、中文摘要 
刮齒刀的設計與製造由於設計理論複
雜，機具開發困難，因此，到目前為止擁有
刮齒刀設計與製造能力的國家並不多，國內
於近幾年才建立刮齒刀的設計與製造能
力，但由於未能充份掌握設計理論，故尚未
能有效掌握刮齒刀的品質。就刮削同一齒輪
而言，刮齒刀的設計可有許多不同選擇，何
者能有較佳的刮削效果、齒形控制、壽命…
等，一直為產業界與學術界共同追求的課
題。 
本研究將探討不同刮齒刀設計參數以
加工同一齒輪時之強度，並探討被刮削齒輪
之齒形。由於利用有限元素分析法模擬刮齒
刀刮削齒輪時網格、節點數眾多，為求得最
佳之刮齒刀設計參數，本研究首先利用田口
實驗法規劃，以進行具滾製根槽之刮齒刀在
不同設計參數下之刮削實驗配置，並進行有
限元素分析比較，以了解不同設計參數刮齒
刀之強度並減少被刮削齒輪之中凹現象。本
研究，首先不考慮刮齒刀之插槽，進行其刮
削模擬與被刮削齒輪齒形預測，隨後並考慮
插槽參數的影響，以了解插槽齒厚對刮齒刀
應力與被刮削齒輪齒形中凹的影響。後續針
對鑽孔與滾製根槽對齒形的中凹進行比
較，以了解根槽之外形的影響。本研究的成
果可以促進業界對刮齒刀設計之了解，縮短
刀具設計時間，提高刮齒刀設計、製造品
質、刀具強度與使用壽命。 
 
關鍵詞：刮齒刀、有限元素分析、田口法 
Abstract 
  At present, a few countries have shaving 
cutter design and manufacture ability due to its 
complex design theory and complicated 
machine tools. Recently, the local company has 
the ability of design and manufacture through a 
long-time development. Because the design 
theory is not well understood, the quality of 
shaving cutter cannot be well controlled until 
now. The shaving cutters may have different 
design parameters for the same shaved gear. 
The optimum design of shaving cutter with a 
long life cycle and ease of tooth profile control 
is highly demanded in the industry.  
  This project studies the strength of shaving 
cutters and the shaved gears tooth profile in the 
shaving process by using the finite element 
method. Due to the large amount of grids and 
nodes in this analysis, Taguchi Method is 
applied as the optimum tool to study the 
optimum design parameters of shaving cutters 
with novel relief portion. The phenomenon of 
concave tooth profile in the shaved gears is 
also investigated. Firstly, the shaving process 
between the shaving cutters without serration 
and the shaved gears is studied in this project. 
Secondly, the shaving process between the 
shaving cutters with serration and the shaved 
gears is studied. Then, the effect of the 
serration width can then be investigated. The 
concave phenomenons are studied by 
considering the relief portions manufactured 
by drilling and hobbing. In conclusion, the 
results of this project can help the local 
Fong [10] 研究直進式刮齒刀插槽對創生齒
輪齒面粗度的影響；在齒輪的應力分析方面
Tsay [11]分析螺旋齒輪之數學模式、接觸分
析與應力分析；Chen 與 Tsay[12]利用 FEM
作螺旋齒輪接觸應力與彎曲應力分析；Sahir 
與 Tamer [13]利用 FEA 作螺旋齒輪接觸與三
維應力分析。而在齒輪的加工方面 光灼華教
授[14]利用齒輪嚙合原理[2,15]推導瘤頭齒
條刀所創成之正齒輪方程式；Litvin [16]則利
用 CNC 刮齒機加工以產製出具修整外形之
正齒輪與螺旋齒輪；Koga 等人 [17]亦針對
修整的螺旋齒輪的刮削作用進行研究；
Chang 等人[18,19]利用漸開線齒輪小壓力角
容易產生過切的特性，巧妙的應用於圓柱狀
刀具的製造；Chang 等人[20, 21]利用瘤頭滾
齒刀可在齒根部位產生過切的特性，設計一
瘤頭滾齒刀以加工刮齒刀的根槽，新的加工
方式可提高刮齒刀的強度及使用壽命，降低
刮齒刀及齒輪之生產成本。 
由以上的文獻回顧得知，多數的研究
乃針對刮齒刀齒形及被刮削齒輪之數學模
式進行理論研究。本計劃主持人亦曾針對
具新型根槽之刮齒刀（圖三）進行強度分
析[20, 21]。惟目前文獻中仍鮮少探討刮齒
刀設計參數與刮削時之強度關係，及對齒
形中凹的影響。 
因此，本專題研究將探討不同刮齒刀設
計參數（加工同一齒輪）之強度與被刮削齒
輪之齒形。由於在探討實際刮齒刀刮削齒輪
之有限元素分析時，網格與節點數眾多，計
算相當耗時，為在眾多刮齒刀設計參數中求
得最佳之刮齒刀設計與被刮削齒輪齒形，本
專題研究首先利用田口法規劃實驗的進行，
以進行具有新型根槽但考慮不同刮齒刀齒
厚、齒數、螺旋角、外徑、根徑時的刮齒刀
之刮削實驗配置，並進行刮削之有限元素分
析，以了解不同設計參數之強度並減少或避
免被刮削齒輪之中凹現象。隨後並考慮刮齒
刀齒面上之插槽參數的影響（圖四），即考慮
插槽節距及插槽齒厚對刮齒刀應力與被刮削
齒輪齒形中凹的影響。 
綜觀整個研究主題，本專題研究完整地
針對刮齒刀設計參數與被刮削後的齒輪齒形
進行模擬與分析。計畫中考慮了刮齒刀的設
計，並以田口法規劃、進行實驗以驗證，配
合有限元素法進行實際刮削時之刮削模擬並
預測刮削後之齒形。因此，本專題研究對於
刮齒刀的設計理論能有較為全方面的了解，
提升國內廠商刮齒刀設計的能力，以提昇產
品品質及壽命。

三、研究計畫內容 
本研究計畫完整地針對刮齒刀的參數設
計，配合田口法與有限元素分析法，進行刮
削齒輪之模擬與分析。計畫中考慮了刮齒刀
的不同設計參數，針對加工同一齒輪進行研
究、比較，以有限元素分析法進行實際刮削
時刀具強度與被刮削齒輪齒形的預測。因
此，本專題研究對於具新型根槽刮齒刀的設
計與齒輪刮削進行了一系列深入而完整的探
討。

Abstract—Plunge shaving is the most advanced gear finishing 
technique which only needs radial infeed. Its advantages include 
increased productivity, accuracy, long tool life, and a simple 
machine structure. For the plunge shaving method, the gear 
tooth modification only depends on the surface geometry of the 
plunge shaving cutter. The analytical description of the gear 
with tooth modifications is firstly constructed by B-spline 
surface fitting. Then, the grinding wheel profile is 
parameterized and optimized for minimizing the surface 
deviations of theoretical and ground (from re-sharpening 
machine) tooth surfaces of the plunge shaving cutter, in which 
the topographic error has been reduced. The cutting trace of 
plunge shaving cutter has also been analyzed so that the shaving 
efficiency can be improved. 
 
Index Terms—Gear plunge shaving, Cone grinding wheel, 
Topographic error, Shaving cutter serration.  
 
I. INTRODUCTION 
  Gears are the most important components in transmission 
systems. Modifications of gear teeth can accommodate errors 
and deformations encountered in the manufacture, assembly, 
and operation of gear pairs. Litvin [1] provided a double 
crowned gear modified both in lead and in profile with 
improved transmission error. Wagaj and Kahraman [2] 
investigated the durability of helical gear affected by tooth 
modifications. Kahraman et al. [3] presented a gear wear 
model analyzing the influences of tooth modification. 
Gear shaving is one of the most efficient and economical 
processes for gear finishing after hobbing or shaping. Among 
the four basic shaving methods, plunge shaving is the most 
advanced gear finishing technique which only needs radial 
infeed. Its advantages include increased productivity, 
accuracy, long tool life, and a simple machine structure [4]. 
The basic meshing condition of 3D crossed-axis helical gear 
pair was first derived by Litvin [5], and it has been widely 
adopted as the fundamental assumption for simulation of gear 
shaving. 
In recent years, how to induce gear tooth modification by 
shaving has become an important subject of research. Chang 
et al. and Hung et al. [6]-[8] derived the mathematical model 
of traditional shaving machine and investigated the influences 
of parameters on tooth lead modifications. Litvin et al. [9]

IV. DESIGN OPTIMIZATION OF THE GEAR PLUNGE SHAVING 
CUTTER SERRATIONS 
Plunge shaving is characterized by a radial feed stroke 
without transverse feed. The serrations in consecutive shaving 
cutter teeth must be shifted longitudinally so that the cutting 
marks on the work gear move in a lengthwise direction, which 
can even the cutting residue and improve the surface 
roughness. As shown in Fig. 2, the design parameters for the 
serrations of the plunge shaving cutter are the serration pitch 
sp  and the serration shift SF between consecutive cutter 
teeth. 
  The trace of the plunge shaving cutter’s cutting edge on 
the work gear during the cutting process is illustrated 
schematically in Fig. 10. At the beginning of a shaving cycle, 
the cutting edge presses into the work gear surface, moves on 
its tooth surface due to the relative motion, and then removes 
chips when contact stress reaches local ultimate stress. 
Therefore, the front cutting edge produces the desired cutting 
action.

V. CONCLUSION 
For gear plunge shaving, the analytical descriptions of 
crowned gear and hence plunge shaving cutter have been 
constructed so that the grinding wheel can be optimized to 
minimized the topographic error. The cutting trace of plunge 
shaving cutter has also been analyzed so that the final real 
tooth forms can be predicted. The proposed mathematical 
model of design optimization can be use to calculate the 
optimum of grinding wheel profile and cutter serration, which 
can facilitate the design and manufacture of gear shaving 
cutter.
