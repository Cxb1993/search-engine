液皆為強鹼之氫氧化鉀(KOH)溶液，極易造

佈，甚至危及電極壽命。再加上燃料電池使
用過程中，KOH 電解液不慎洩漏出來時，

池，電池穩定性亦相當良好。

導電性，加上來源取得容易，製造成本低
廉，應當是非常良好的電解液材料。
2. 研究目的
無患子是一種木本植物，樹型高大，它
的果實也叫無患子，英文稱為 Soapberry 或
Soappnut。目前市面上無患子相關產品多為
其果實之果肉萃取液的衍生產品，而其果仁
之用途僅少數作為工藝品，多數作為堆肥。
以氣候條件來看，熱帶、亞熱帶、有乾旱的
季風區最適合無患子生長，它的主要分布在
華南、日本中部以南、琉球、印度，而台灣
由於氣候與地形適宜，在全台低海拔闊葉林
內皆能發現無患子蹤跡，尤其是西南半部的
淺山區域，更是主要的分布區。無患子是生
活力強的鄉土樹種，群族數量繁多，卻少見
形成獨佔優勢的群生純林。
無患子因含有高量的皂素，是一種天然
的介面活性劑、乳化劑、濕潤劑兼發泡劑，
因此具有清潔的能力。無患子果仁所含油脂
具導電性，加上來源取得容易，製造成本低
廉，應當是非常良好的電解液材料。
本計畫開發完成後不僅可以當作非常
好之替代能源，對於農民而言，對於種植無
患子之收益增加，農民會樂於種植，不會讓
農地荒廢，甚至山坡地之檳榔樹會有減少之
趨勢。對於政府而言造林政策得以推行，環
境綠化，山坡地不易發生土石流，農藥汙染
減少，二氧化碳減量目標得以達成。

Abstract
In wire flat rolling, wire of circular cross-section is cold rolled between the flat rolls in
one pass to achieve the desired thickness-to-width ratio. However, during wire flat rolling
process, the dimension accuracy of the wire was poor due to the effect of spring back. In
order to control the dimension accuracy, to know the quantity of spring back precisely is
very important. The DEFORM finite element analysis software was used to simulate the
wire flat rolling process with the elastic-plastic deformation model. This article is pointed
to the effect of reduction ratio, deformation load and side flow to the quantity of spring
back. If the variation of spring back under different rolling condition is known, the
dimension accuracy of the workpiece would be increased through the wire flat rolling
process.
Keywords:
wire-flat rolling, spring back, elastic-plastic deformation model, reduction ratio, dimension accuracy
Introduction
Rolling process is a process that uses roller to
stress the surface of metal, which bring plastic
deformation to the surface. The main purpose of
rolling process is to amend the micro geometry
of surface and increase brightness of the surface.
At the same time, the process itself leads to
changes in metallography, which increase the
beneficial residual stress and enhance
mechanical performance and life-span. In wire

Their study focus on single wire’s roling
process; however, the spring back after rolling
process is not studied.
