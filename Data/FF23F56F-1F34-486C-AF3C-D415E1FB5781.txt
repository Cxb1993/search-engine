附件二
4研究目的
基於不同壓迫所產生的細胞調控不同，進而產生的二次代謝產物亦有不同，故我們有
興趣探索不同壓迫因子對發酵產程中的二次代謝產物包含 iturin A、surfactin、glucanase、
protease、chitinase 等產量的相對關係。也由於抑制植物病原真菌的活性，與上述多個因子
有關，而非單一代謝物完成作用，計劃擬在培養 Bacillus subtilis 時給予不同特性的刺激或
壓迫(例如 C-limitation; N-limitation; P-limitation; Oxygen stress; Heat shock; Cold chock;
Osmotic pressure; Ethanol stimulation)，在不同時間點取樣細胞，再利用生物檢定的方法測量
抑菌活性的表現；另外利用反轉錄酶(Reverse Transcriptase)配合聚合酶連鎖反應(PCR)去測
定細胞內σB的 m-RNA 轉譯量，如此我們可以在發酵產程中針對各環境壓迫因之差異與不
同培養條件，對細胞內的壓迫反應指標做即時分析。將收集之數據再與最終二次代謝產物
的活性和產量進行統計相關分析，即可知此一分子指標σB，應用在饋料批次作為預測發酵
產能的有效工具。
文獻探討
枯草桿菌對病原真菌和細菌具有拮抗作用，可以產生許多代謝產物和抗生物質。曾有
文獻指出，枯草桿菌至少有 66 種不同的抗生物質，但近年已發表在期刊中的資料顯示，其
中只有 11 種有確定的結構與功能。產生抗生素的枯草桿菌除可將菌體直接噴灑植物葉片保
護其免受葉部病害危害外，並可製成粉劑或純化出其抗生物質，進行種子覆被或土壤處理。
枯草桿菌屬產生的抗生物質其分子量範圍在 2700~4500 Da之間的胜肽類抗生素，而同一種
枯草桿菌常可同時產生分子量相近的多種抗生素，其組成結構為多個胺基酸以環狀結構聯
接，故其較不易被動植物的蛋白酸水解，本屬微生物產生抗生素的種類依其所在細胞位置
及分子結構可分為三類，分別為胞內環狀胜肽脂類( Intracellular cyclic peptido-lipids)、胞外
環狀胜肽脂類(Extracellular cyclic peptidolpids)及胞外胜肽環狀類(Extracellular cyclic
peptides) ，其所產生不同類之抗生素可對不同的病原菌產生抑制作用。一種稱為「iturin A」
的抗生物質，這種化合物會與病原真菌細胞膜的固醇分子作用形成複合物，使得離子傳導
孔隙增大，改變細胞膜的滲透性，讓鉀離子迅速流出，進而導致病原真菌菌絲分解並抑制
孢子發芽，達到防治病害的效果。Iturin A 擁有良好的兩性物質，因此可發展成生物界面活
性劑（Fiechter，1992）。其極性部份形成是因 peptide cycle 存在，其親水基常來自蛋白質之
極性基、醣脂之醣基及磷酸脂之磷酸部份。疏水部份由β-amino acid 的烷基鏈形成，通常
來自一個或多個長鏈之碳氫化合物。其可降低臨界微胞濃度，是一有效之生物界面活性劑
（Maget-Dana，1994）。生物界面活性劑依其化學結構特性可分為五大類，分別為：（1）
醣脂（glycolipids）、（2）磷脂（phospholipids）、（3）脂胜肽（lipopeptides）、（4）脂肪酸（fatty
acids）及（5）高分子生物界面活性劑（polymetric biosurfactants）（Fiechter，1992）。相同
的生物界面活性劑，可由不同微生物所合成而生，如枯草桿菌及液化澱粉芽孢桿菌皆產生
Iturin A。多數微生物均能產生界面活性物質，其相異處在於利用之培養基質（substrate）不
同所生成之不同種類及濃度。iturin A 由-amino acids 長鏈及 7 個-amino acid 殘基組成
之環狀胜肽脂類抗生素，Iturin A 在目前主要應用於生物防治的功用上（Besson et al.，1979；
Heins et al.，2000； Peypoux et al.，1979），Iturin A 可對抗的病原真菌及細菌有：Cercospora
kikuchii、Verticillium dahliae、Fusarium oxysporum f. sp. lycopersici、Alternaria mali、
Rhizoctonia solani、Pyricularia oryzea、Xanthomonas oryzea 及 Pseudomonas lachrymans 拮
6PDA 以 25℃培養並於冰箱中 4℃下保存。本研究測試植物病原真菌檬果炭疽病病源菌
(Glomerella cingulata；GC) 利用 PDA 以 25℃培養並於冰箱中 4℃下保存。並定期以單孢
技術活化病源真菌。
分析方法
（1） Iturin A 的 HPLC 分析方法
A. 標準品之前處理
將1 mg ”iturin A” 溶於 1mL methanol 中，在 220 nm 波長的條件下以 HPLC 分析。
B. iturin A 之萃取由 NA 培養基中挑單一菌落接種於 5 mL LB 小量隔夜培養，以接種量 1
﹪的比例轉養於 100 mL LB，於 30℃ 200 rpm 下培養 12~16 小時，接著在 4℃下，以 8,000
rpm 離心 20 分鐘，去上清液，菌體回溶於 100 mL Potato sucrose broth (PSB)（含 2﹪sucrose）
中。再於 30℃下培養 6 天，然後在 4℃下，以 8,000 rpm 離心 20 分鐘，取上清液以濃鹽
酸滴定至 pH = 2 ±0.5。在 4℃下，以 8,000 rpm 離心 20 分鐘，取沉澱物，以 25 mL methanol
(100%)將沉澱懸浮於分液漏斗，用力振盪使”iturin A”萃取於有機相中，重複萃取二次，
過濾，並藉由真空濃縮儀將有機溶劑揮發，此沉澱物即為粗（crude）”iturin A”。將
粗”iturin A”溶於 NaOH（pH 8）溶液中，過濾去除未溶解的不純物，收集濾液再度以濃
鹽酸使其沉澱，用 methanol (100%)萃取，真空濃縮，以 2.5 mL methanol 回溶，並以活
性炭脫色，最後經0.22 μm 過濾膜過濾，便可進行 HPLC 分析。
C. HPLC 分析條件
利用 Lichrocart 250-4 Lichrospher 100 RP-18 column，以 acetontril：10 mM
ammonium acetate＝2：3(v/v)為沖堤液，流速為 1 mL/min，在 220 nm 波長的條件下進行
HPLC 分析。
（2）生物活性檢定方法
枯草桿菌可產生多種拮抗物質，其中 iturin A 對 Fusarium oxysporum（Hiraoka et al., 1992；
Huang et al., 1993）及 Saccharomyces cerevisiae（Latoud et al., 1987；1990）可藉由
改變細胞膜的滲透性達到拮抗作用，本研究測試植物病原真菌檬果炭疽病病源菌
(Glomerella cingulata；GC)。
對峙培養法－真菌：分別將測試植物病原真菌檬果炭疽病源菌之菌絲塊（直徑 1 公分）置
於馬鈴薯瓊脂培養基（potato dextrose agar, PDA）上，於 25℃下培養，待長滿培養皿。將
測試菌株以接種環由 NA 培養基中挑取單一菌落後，實驗方法是將真菌塊置於 PDA plate
中央培養，待其生長兩天後，在離菌絲 0.5 公分處貼上濾紙片，並滴上發酵液30μl，總共
做四重複，於一天及五天後觀察其結果。於 PDA 培養基（9 cm）中央劃一直線，觀察病原
真菌的生長情形，並測量抑菌距離。對照組則是無菌水，照上述步驟進行實驗，於一天及
五天後觀察其結果。
上述生物檢定方法為定性並可初步判定抑菌活性，進ㄧ步取得抑菌活性量化生物檢定，則
詳述於下，β-glucanase 抗真菌活性測試採用此一方式進行。1. 培養 7 天 Bacillus
amyloliquefaciens 培養液離心 10000 轉、10 分鐘。2. 將離心好的上清液取至無菌操作台，
以0.22μl filter 過濾得到濾液。3. 將得到的濾液以無菌水分別稀釋 100 倍與 1000 倍。4. 將
步驟 3 兩個濃度的濾液分別加入 55℃、70℃與 100℃未凝固的 Potato Dextrose Agar 中，搖
晃均勻，並潑於 9 cm 之平板上，以上實驗均三重複。4. 待 PDA 凝固之後，以 0. 5 c m 的
8實驗中使用常見培養花材如發泡煉石、活性碳、唐山石、樹皮、蛇木、珍珠石、蛭石、
蘭石、甘蔗纖維素等常見的園藝花材作為不同種類的固定化菌體介質。由結果發現，與文
獻記載類似，發泡煉石可以促使液化澱粉芽胞桿菌分泌 surfactin 產生（Yeh et al.,2005）。
此外，我們也意外發現到，固形物添加也可促使 iturin A 的產量上升。iturin A 與 surfactin
都是屬於脂胜肽類二次代謝產物，surfactin 產量上升原因，文獻指出，培養於液態發酵之
菌體因為想要附著於固形物上，因此促使其產生類似界面活性劑的 surfactin。iturin A 構型
與 surfactin 類似，都是環型的脂胜肽，因此 iturin A 的產量上升可能也是循此模式。此外，
在 1%樹皮與 1%蛇木添加發酵液，於靜置 7 天之後，iturin A 產量反而上升。這兩種添加物
都是由植物乾燥而成，因此於大膽推測，不可溶纖維素可以促進 iturin A 產生。
0.5、1、2%固定化菌體介質分別添加於 1% molasses、1%yeast powder 與 0.2% ISP 對 Iturin
A、Surfactin 產量影響，根據 2005 年的文獻，作者使用不同濃度之固定化菌體介質活性碳
觀察 surfactin 產生量是否有正相關情形。在不同濃度固定化菌體介質添加實驗中發現，唐
山石與纖維素的添加對 iturin A 產量有正相關，但是發泡煉石卻沒有此情形。但是在 surfactin
產量上，卻是發泡煉石與纖維素有正相關的情形，而唐山石則無。可以發現，0.5、1、2%
纖維素不管對 iturin A 與 surfactin 之產量都有正相關，在 iturin A 產量上，與前面的推測相
符。但是，在誘發 surfactin 產量上，卻是以發泡煉石為最佳。添加固定化菌體介質被認為
能夠促使菌體生長數量，因此其代謝產物也因此增加。Heptakis（2,6-O-dimethyl）beta－
cyclodextrin 被添加於合成培養基（Cyclodextrin solid medium，CSM）並發現對 Bordetella
pertussis Tohama phase I 的菌體生長數量有良好的相關性（Imaizumi et al.,1983）。纖維素添
加的培養基在靜置 7 天之後，iturin A 有下降的跡象，反而是 surfactin 有增加的趨勢，推測
菌體在產孢子時，有可能先合成 iturin A，之後才合成 surfactin，但需進一步實驗證實。文
獻指出，B. spp.菌體在產孢子時，會大量產生 iturin A（Mohammad et al.,2006）。

1. Introduction
Synthetic chemical fungicide has long been served as power agents for reducing the
incidence of plant disease; however, they are costly, can cause environmental pollution, and may
induce pathogen resistance. Therefore, the conventional use of chemical pesticides has been
seriously questioned [1, 2]. To cope with the problem associated with chemical fungicide,
biological control agents, which include effective microorganisms and microbial products, have
been attracting attention recently as alternatives to chemical pesticides for the management of
plant disease. The biological control of plant pathogens which is without the negative aspects of
chemical control has become an important aspect of the sustainable agriculture.
In the past few years, numerous microorganisms with antifungal activities and their
antifungal factors have been identified [3-8]; in addition, the mechanisms by which
microorganisms inhibit growth of potentially pathogenic fungi have been demonstrated [4, 7-13].
Bacillus subtilis, a representative Gram-positive and spore forming bacterium [11, 14-15], shows
antagonistic activities against several plant pathogens; thus, it is one potential bio-control agent.
Till now, several Bacillus subtilis strains and their derivatives were found to have broad
suppressive abilities over a variety of plant pathogens in vitro [16, 17] by producing the
lipopeptide antibiotics iturin A and surfactin [14, 18].
Iturin A is a cyclic lipopeptide containing a heptapeptide (L-Asn-D-Tyr-D-Asn-L-Gln
-L-Pro-D-Asn-L-Ser) cycled with a β-amino fatty acid; it is a small molecules yet displays strong
antifungal activity. In contrast, the other lipopeptide, surfactin which is a biosurfactant constituted
by heptapeptide cyclised with a β-hydroxy fatty acid, has week antibiotic activity. The strong
efficacy of iturin A against various phytopathogenic fungi is similar to the available chemical
pesticides [16, 17]. Along with its wide spectrum of antibiotic activity and surface activity, iturin
A confers low toxicity, low allergic effect on human and animals [19], high biodegradability;
these characteristics qualify itself as a candidate for a environmentally safe biological pesticide
[13].
Reagents for cultivation such as corn steep powder (CSP) was obtained from Merck & Co.,
Inc, potato powder and soy bean protein (Zer-Jen Co., Taiwan), peptone was from HIMEDIA
laboratory (India), yeast extract was from Taiwan sugar company, K2HPO4, KH2PO4, MgSO4·7
H2O, maltodextrin were obtained from Katamaya Co., Japan. The nutrient agar (NA), Luria
Bertani broth and carbon sources, i.e. fructose, glucose, sucrose, maltose, were obtained from
DIFCO Laboratories Michigan, USA. All other reagents used were of the highest grade available
unless otherwise indicated. Bacillus subtilis S3, isolated from soil, was obtained from Taiwan
Agricultural Research Institute, Wufeng, Taiwan. The strain was maintained on nutrient agar (NA)
slants. Unless otherwise mentioned, the slant was incubated at 30℃and then stored at 4℃, which
was subcultured every 4 weeks.
To prepare seed culture, a loop of S3 cells from a slant culture of fresh nutrient agar was
inoculated into a 500-ml flask containing 100 ml Luria broth. The flask was incubated on a New
Brunswick rotary shaker (Model G24) at 30℃ , 200 rpm for 24 h and used as a seed for
submerged cultivations.
2.2. Shake-flask fermentation
The standard spore suspension inoculums 5% (v/v) were used for shake flask cultures to
evaluate the effects of carbon source, nitrogen sources, inorganic salts and plant oil on the
production of iturin A from B. subtilis S3. Cultures were grown at 30 °C and 200 rpm in a rotary
shaker incubator (Yih-Der, LE-539., Taiwan), in 500 mL Erlenmeyer flasks with 100 mL of
production media, and no pH control was applied for all cultivations. The production medium for
cultivation of B. subtilis S3 consisted of 1% of one of the following carbon sources, i.e. fructose,
glucose, sucrose and maltose, 0.5% of one of the following nitrogen sources, i.e. corn steep
powder, soy bean protein, yeast extract and peptone, 1-2 % of potato powder and maltodextrin,
1.5mM MgSO4·7 H2O, 0.75mM KH2PO4. In addition, to test the effect of plant oils, 0.5-1% of
peanut oil, olive oil, soybean oil and canola oil (president food co., Taiwan) was also added.
performance of the culture in terms of iturin A yields are the levels of glucose concentration, corn
steep powder (CSP), maltodextrin, initial pH, shaking speed and area of aeration. Therefore, these
six factors were chosen for further optimization through RSM. Initially, a fractional factorial 26-3
design followed by the method of steepest ascent was carried out to find the general vicinity of
optimum conditions for iturin A production.
2.4. 1. Factorial design.
In the first experiment of this series, the ranges of the variables tested were 0.50-1.5%
glucose, 0.25-0.75% CSP, 0.5-1.5% maltodextrin, 4.5-7.5 initial pH, 100-200 rpm shaking speed
and 0.20-5.31 cm2 area of aeration. For the first phase of the optimization process in which the
region close to the optimum is to be approached, two-level factorial designs were chosen. In this
experimental design, the main effects and interactions of different factors, each at two different
levels, can be simultaneously investigated. Table 1 shows the six independent variables and their
real values at the different coded levels of the factorial design experiments. The six factors are
maltodextrin (X1), glucose (X2), CSP (X3), shaking speed (X4) and area of aeration (X5) also
expressed as diameter of rubber eraser, initial pH (X6); their upper and lower levels in this initial
design were chosen in reconciliation with the data of our previous work on production of iturin A
by Bacillus subtilis S3 [32]. For a 26-3 fractional factorial design with six factors at two levels,
eight experimental runs are required. The matrix corresponding to 26-3 fractional factorial design,
together with the observed experimental data is also shown in Table 2. To avoid bias, the total of
8 runs was performed in a random order (overall randomization).

2.4.4. Software for experimental design and statistical analysis
The computer software Statistica, version 5.0 (Statsoft, Inc., Tulsa, OK USA) was used for
the experimental design and regression analysis of the experimental data obtained. The quality of
fit of the model equation was expressed by the coefficient of determination R2, and its statistical
significance was determined by an F-test. The significance of the regression coefficients was
tested by a t-test. For analysis of the nature of the fitted response and for prediction of the
maximum point, the second-order equation was reduced to its canonical form [33, 34], which is
one part of the Statistica output.

3. Results and discussion
3.1. Shake-flask fermentation of Bacillus subtilis S3
In preliminary experiments, various carbon and nitrogen sources , plant oils were evaluated for
their suitability to sustain good production of iturin A by B. subtilis S3 [32]. The fermentation
medium, which is consisted of the following components: 2 % potato powder, 0.5% corn steep
powder (CSP), 2 % sucrose, 1.5 mM MgSO47 H2O, 0.75 mM KH2PO4, was grown at 30 °C and
200 rpm in a rotary shaker incubator. The time course of the cultivation is shown in Figure 1.
This preliminary study [32] showed that when B. subtilis S3 was cultivated on the
aforementioned condition, the highest iturin A production (47.19 mg/L) was obtained after 7 day
of cultivation. In addition, only 57 % increase of iturin A productivity ( from 47.19 mg/L to 74.22

From the first-order model equation (6) and judging from the regression coefficients and the

it was then omitted from the experiments of Path of Steepest Ascent and CCD in the study.
Based on the first-order model equation obtained, the path of steepest ascent was determined
to find proper direction of changing variables increasing or decreasing the concentration
according to the sign of the main effects to improve iturin A production. The path of steepest
ascent started from the center of the factorial design and moved along the path in which the
concentrations of glucose (X2), CSP (X3), shaking speed (X4) and area of aeration (X5) were
increasing; in contrast, the concentrations of maltodextrin (X1) was decreasing. The design and
results of the path of steepest ascent experiments are shown in Table 3. It is shown that the
highest production response is 123.02 mg/L where the concentrations of maltodextrin (X1),
glucose (X2), CSP (X3), shaking speed (X4) and area of aeration (X5) are 0.93 %, 1.11 %, 0.69
%, 179 rpm, 4.32 cm2, respectively. It suggested that this point was near the region of maximum
production response.
3.3. Central composite design.
To fully explore the subregion of the response surface in the neighborhood of the optimum,
an experimental design with more than two levels of each factor is required, so that a second
order approximation to the response surface can be developed. A CCD with five coded levels was
used for this purpose. The levels of the variables as shown in Table 4 for the CCD experiments
were selected according to the results of the previous experiments. The CCD design and the
combination for the iturin production was found to be following: glucose (X1) 1.11 %, CSP (X2)
0.7 %, shaking speed (X3) 180 rpm and area of aeration (X4) 4.35 cm2. The model predicted a
maximum response of iturin A production at 129.33 mg/L. Verification of the calculated
maximum was done with experiments that were performed in the culture media representing the
optimum combination found, and the iturin A production of 132.23 mg/L (average of three
repeats) was obtained. The excellent correlation between predicted and experimental values
justifies the validity of the response model. It is noted that 57 % increase of iturin A productivity
( from 47.19 mg/L to 74.22 mg/L) was observed using " one-factor-at -a-time" technique for
optimization as indicated above; however, 180% increase of iturin A productivity ( from 47.19
mg/L to 132.23 mg/L) was observed using RSM for the optimization.
Several previous studies used submerged fermentation [11, 36, 37], in addition to solid-state
fermentation [3, 20-23], to produce high concentrations of lipopeptide antibiotics from B. subtilis.
Recently, the iturin A yield has increased significantly through application of the solid-state
fermentation and RSM method [31]; the yield of iturin A produced by B. subtilis RB14-CS grown
on soybean curd residue in SSF under statistically predicted optimum conditions was 5.45
mg/g-wet solid material, a production 2.8 times higher than that observed in solid culture without
glucose or soybean meal, and 2.2 times higher than obtained in the basal medium [20]. The
application of RSM to optimize iturin A production in SMF is scant; however, Gu and coworkers
[30] have successfully demonstrated the optimization of medium constituents for the production
lipopeptide antibiotics by B. subtilis MO-01 in submerged fermentation (SMF) using response
surface methodology (RSM); the prediction lipopeptide yield was 1.712 g/L and the optimum
fermentation parameters were 22.432 g/L of sucrose, 2.781 g/L of ammonium chloride, 6.7879
um of ferrous sulfate and 0.0377 mM of zinc sulfate, respectively.
3.4. 5-L stirred tank fermentation
For the 5-L fermentor, the culture conditions, such as the agitation speed and aeration rate,
are essential factors for batch culture [37]. The effects of the agitation speed (100-300 rpm),
aeration rate (0.5-2.0 vvm) and different baffles on the production of iturin A were investigated.
The row of baffle inside tank directly influences the agitating status of fermentation, which
affect the oxygen mass transfer and also the shear stress on the cell. Three different baffles as
described above were inserted in the fermentor for the regulation of oxygen mass transfer and
shear stress. The effects of different baffles on oxygen mass transfer coefficient (KLa), and
production of iturin are shown in Figure 7 and Figure 8. It is seen that the addition of baffles in
the fermentor have influenced the oxygen mass transfer coefficient (KLa) significantly. Although
it appeared that the increase of KLa have favored the production of cell biomass (data not shown),
it did not correlate well with the production of iturin A. The main reason might due to the fact of
that the addition baffles increases not only the KLa but also the shear stress in the fermentor. The
impeller power consumption was found depended on the agitation speed or the type baffle used in
fermentor (Fig 4). The addition of baffle 3 ( 21cm length, 3 cm width) gave the highest KLa, but
it is the addition of baffle 1 (21cm length, 1.5 cm width) that gave a significant increase of iturin
A production, the highest is 142.13 mg/L at 6 d of fermentation. A interesting result was found
that the baffle 1 at 200 rpm and non-baffle 300 rpm have close power consumption and iturin A
production. On the other hand, the wide baffle was found to have higher power consumption and
lower iturin A production (Fig 4 & Fig 8). According this fact of that the higher power
consumption from baffle 2 and 3, the higher shear stress seem to be an inhibiting factor to iturin
A production. This result was consistent with the fact of that the higher iturin A was produced
with high oxygen mass transfer and non-high shear stress. The oxygen supply into the broths
constitutes one of the decisive factors of cultivated microorganisms growth and can play an
important role in the scale-up and economy of aerobic biosynthesis systems. In an baffled
bioreactor, the optimal agitating speed of the agitator and aeration must be determined to
facilitate cell growth and secondary metabolite production [39].

SMF were identified; they are the levels of maltodextrin, glucose, CSP, shaking speed, area of
