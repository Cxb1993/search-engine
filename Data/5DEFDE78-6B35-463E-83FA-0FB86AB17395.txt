本機台依實際滾珠螺桿接觸幾何加以設計試件外型，並可控
制負荷、速度參數、接觸角等參數，及量測摩擦力、油溫、試件
溫度、接觸電阻、位移等特性，實際驗證結果顯示試驗機確實能
夠完全說明滾珠螺桿接觸面特性變化。甚至可以測試實物機台不
能，未來滾珠螺桿急待克服的科技難題的實驗功能。 
    本機台具有四項專利保護，且具有設備不僅可提供滾珠螺桿
廠商作為實驗設備、也是齒輪、滾珠軸承或凸輪相關滾滑零組件
之實驗模擬設備。本機台具有並聯測試設計，可有效提供改善策
略的比對實驗驗證，亦可分析實物試驗機台無法了解之接觸角、
滾珠外形、預壓與磨損關係等現階段技術瓶頸之性能變化測試。
以致分析結果可以滿足廠商在不同操作環境下，零組件之參數設
定參考及半導體產業與奈米科技之潔淨室特殊使用環境需求，並
且可以建立使用規範，提升產業競爭力，有助於提升我國高負荷
高速滾珠螺桿之技術研發水準，而高超精密級、超大導程及潔淨
室專用滾珠螺桿是目前最新的應用趨勢，世界各主要廠商都全力
研發，爭取半導體產業與奈米科技業這特別高單價的產品。

of elliptical peaks on fractal microcontact areas and studied the change in G in areas of elastic, 
plastic, and elastic-plastic deformation. Carpinteri[11] proposed a unified interpretation of these 
size-scale effects according to fractal geometry, which represents the proper mathematical 
framework for the analysis of multi-scale friction of rough surfaces in contact. However, the effects 
of adhesion on the real contact area were not discussed in above papers. In 2008, Horng[12] 
considered adhesion effects in his fractal analysis of the microcontact area of spherical contact 
peaks. The results showed that the adhesion force is a dominant factor in maintaining stable motion 
under a low fractal roughness parameter value of G, a high adhesion work value of a and low 
contact load conditions. 
Menezes[13] observed that the coefficient of friction, the formation of transfer layers, and the 
presence of stick–slip motion depended primarily on the surface texture of hard surfaces. The result 
of Sheng’s fractal analysis[14] showed that a maximum friction coefficient occurs at a D value of 
1.6 for the rough surfaces. Most machined metal surfaces are oriented with respect to the direction of 
the motion of the cutting tools relative to the workpieces. In such cases, the profile of the asperities 
generally contains various curvatures for various directions. Moreover, in precision machines or 
MEMS, surface coatings have often been added to increase wear resistance and decrease the friction 
force or to improve appearances. The relationship between adhesion, surface roughness direction 
and the friction properties is important for the application of rough surface coatings. However, the 
combined effect of adhesion, the roughness direction, and the hardness curve of the film coating on 
friction has been not considered in past papers. This study used these three effects as a jumping-off 
point to discuss the friction coefficient of surface coatings.

because a high elliptic ratio caused the real contact area to decrease under the same load.[10] The 
friction and the real contact area had a positive correlation, as shown in equation (1), so the

decreased between P*=1×10-7 and P*=5×10-6 as the external load increased regardless of the value 
of . Conversely, when the value of P* was larger than 5×10-6, the friction coefficient increased as 
the external load increased. Figures 5 and 7 show that the friction coefficients approached a fixed 
value when the load increased for the different values of and film hardness. 
Figure 8 shows the effect of fractal dimension D on the friction coefficient with varying 
elliptical ratios when G*=1×10-10, m=0.5, c=2,a*=1×10-10, andP=1×10-5. It was shown that, 
regardless of γ, when D < 1.3, the friction coefficient was identical out to four decimal places. 
However, when D > 1.3, the friction coefficient increased as D increased to a maximum value 
(Dcritical) after which it declined. When D < Dcritical, a greater corresponded to a greater friction 
coefficient regardless of the value of D. The friction coefficient a maximum value and subsequently 
decreased, and the rate of decrease of the friction coefficient increased as increased. When D=1.9 
and =0.02, the friction coefficient was slightly greater than at =1.0. The maximum value of the 
friction coefficient was between 1.5 and 1.7 for the conditions used in this study. The smaller the 
value, the larger the Dcritical value at which the friction coefficient reached its maximum. From the 
above discussions, it is clear that if the fractaldimension D is kept at 1.3 or less while designing and 
manufacturing micro-components, a relatively low friction coefficient can be obtained. In contrast, 
if a component with a high friction force was required, such as a wheel or a brake pad, the fractal 
dimension D should be kept at approximately 1.60, which is where the greatest friction coefficient 
is obtained.

Conclusions 
(1) Whether the friction coefficient between rough surfaces decreased or increased with the  
external load depends primarily on the values of a* and G*. For the conditions used in this study, 
the value of G* is a key factor affecting the relationship of the friction coefficient and the 
external load. When G* was smaller, the coefficient decreased as the external load decreased. 
However, when the G* of a rough surface increased to a certain value, the friction coefficient 
increased as the external load increased. 
(2) The friction analysis showed that adhesion failure was more significant under a small load than 
under a large load. This was especially important when considering adhesion elements in 
microelements. 
(3) The friction coefficient decreased when m and c in the coating hardness curve increased.  
(4) Under the same load, a decrease in the elliptic ratio  of peaks causes a decrease in the real 
contact area, which decreases the friction coefficient between rough surfaces. 
(5) When D is lower than 1.30 in commercial mechanical components the relative friction 
coefficient is low and is not affected by changes in nondimensional adhesion or the elliptical 
ratio. When D is between 1.5 and 1.7, the largest friction coefficient is obtained under the 
conditions used in this analysis. The friction coefficient subsequently decreased as D increased 
for the different values of . The results will provide a deeper understanding of friction 
coefficients and will help to evaluate the manufacturing technology of precision machines.

一、參加會議經過 
    第二屆製造科學與工程國際學術會議將於2011年4月9-11日在桂林召開，本屆會議將
是製造科學領域的一場盛會，將為全世界材料與製造科學領域的學者、研究人員和科學
家提供一個交流思想觀點以及專業知識的機會。本次會議由廣西大學材料科學與工程學
院院長曾建民教授、桂林電子科技大學副校長古天龍教授、桂林電子科技大學機械學院
院長楊道國教授以及韓國海洋大學Yun-Hae Kim教授共同擔任主席。會議共包含來自全世
界的專家學者百餘人參加，共有198場Oral presentation session、8場 Professional keynote 
speech 共計發表學術論文約 210 餘篇。國際會議的內容有 Manufacturing Process 
Technology、New and Advanced Materials、Materials Processing Technology、Design and 
Manufacturing、Advanced Manufacturing System等。 
二、與會心得 
本人所演講論文為“Study of Contact Temperature in Polishing Surfaces” ，演講後與會學
者對於微接觸與工件精密磨削方面均感興趣與讚賞，其中共有多人發問，本人也加以詳
細解答，主持人亦對本人演講論文表示高度肯定，詢問國內研究之近況。當然，除了參
與自己所發表的論文之外，趁此機會認識各國專業研究人員，積極參與其他主題的論文
發表，以了解現今發展的主流以及相關研究並詢問問題，對於本人日後的研究有極大的
幫助，並且增加其他有利於創新的構想，綜觀本次的研討會參與，除了讓本人與許多外
國學者有進ㄧ步的互動外，藉由參加不同主題的研討也提升了個人在研究上的廣度與不
同的視野，相信日後將對本人的其他研究發揮重大的助益。由於本人亦是此會議之技術
委員會委員，因此洽談未來於台灣共同舉辦國際研討會之可能性。
