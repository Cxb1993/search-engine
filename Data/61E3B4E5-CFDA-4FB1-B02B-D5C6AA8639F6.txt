二、英文摘要 
 
The aim of this study was to examine the most detected compound through the use 
of consumer and personal care products, nonylphenol (NP) and triclosan (TCS), in

(四)研究方法 
2.1 藥品 
    壬基苯酚(4-n-NP)及三氯沙(TCS)均為最高純度之分析級標準品。另外，
有機溶劑乙腈(acetonitrile)、甲醇(methanol)、二氯甲烷(dichloromethane)等均為LC
級。斑馬魚胚胎細胞(ZEM2S)購自食品所生資中心。其他生物檢測之溶劑及藥品
均為分析級以上品質。 
  2.2 材料及分析設備 
    前處理設備及材料使用包括孔徑1μm及0.45μm玻璃纖維濾紙、固相萃取
管(Oasis HLB cartridge，6 mL)、固相萃取裝置、吹氮濃縮裝置(TURBO VAP  LV，
Cailper)及0.22μm Syringe Filter；分析則利用高效能液相層析儀(High Performance 
Liquid Chromatography，Waters Alliance 2695)搭配螢光偵測器(Waters 2475 Mulit 
λ Fluorescence detector)及 UV偵測器(Waters 2996 photodiode Array Detector)，均
購自美國Waters公司。 
  2.3.樣品採集 
本研究在99年8月至100年7月期間於台灣南部地區三座污水處理廠進行六
次樣品採集，採集方法依環保署公告之水質採樣通則(W101.54A)實施，採集樣品
後冷藏於4 ℃，並於一週內完成樣品前處理與分析。 
污水廠LK處理水源以生活污水為主，平均日污水處理量可達20,000 CMD，
本研究分別於污水廠進流口(LK-W-1)、終沉池(LK-W-2)、放流口(LK-W-3)、放
流水流出口(LK-W-4)及溪流匯流處下游(LK-W-5)處進行採樣。同時亦取得迴流
污泥、消化污泥及脫水污泥樣品進行分析。污水廠FS 處理水源以溪水及生活污
水為主，平均日污水處理量可達30,000 CMD，本研究分別於污水廠進流口
(FS-W-1)、終沉池(FS-W-2)、放流口(FS-W-3)及溪流匯流處下游(FS-W-4)處進行
採樣，而污泥則為消化污泥及脫水污泥樣品。污水廠DS處理水源以溪水為主，

終沉池(DS-W-2)、放流口(DS-W-3)及溪流匯流處下游(DS-W-4)處進行採樣，而

Results and Discussion 
We collected the raw and treated leachates from the landfills in order to determine the levels 
of PCDD/Fs and dioxin-like PCBs and examine the removal efficiency of leachate treatment 
process in Taiwan.  The results showed that the total PCDD/Fs concentration in collected 
samples ranged from 0.0585 to 3.23 pg TEQ/L. The highest PCDD/Fs concentration in raw 
leachate was found at landfill B (3.23 pg TEQ/L ) followed by landfill E (1.48 pg TEQ/L ).  
However, the relationships of the size between landfills and PCDD/Fs concentrations were 
not significant. Fig 3 and 4 illustrated the concentrations of 17 PCDD/Fs congeners in raw 
leachate between liquid and solid phases were ranged from 0.0348～1.24 and 0.0549～3.17 
pg TEQ/L with the highest concentration was 1,2,3,4,6,7,8-HpCDD.  In addition, Fig 5 and 6 
demonstrated the highest concentrations of PCDD/Fs in treated leachate liquid and solid 
phases was 2,3,4,7,8-PeCDF, respectively. It was obvious that the solid phases PCDD/Fs in 
leachate played a major role as expected.  The removal efficiency of PCDD/Fs for leachate 
between liquid and solid phases were varied among 5.70～95.6% and 41.2～97.6%, 
respectively,  except landfill A exhibited minus removal efficiency.  The results also 
indicated that over 90% of hepta- (landfills B,C,D,E) and penta-(landfill F) substituted 
PCDD/Fs in solid-phase were effectively removed by the leachate treatment process in 
landfill leachates whereas the levels of 17 PCDD/Fs were increased in landfill A.  Total
