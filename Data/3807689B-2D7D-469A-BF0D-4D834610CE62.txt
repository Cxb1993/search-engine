common in many parts of Europe and North American 
countries because of the high land cost and density 
of population in urban areas. There is limited 
research report in domestic relative to greenroof in 
reducing urban flood. The main purpose of this 
research is to develop the technologies to quantify 
the benefits of greenroof in urban flood mitigation, 
etc. and a mathematical model which combines the 
greenroof and rainwater harvest system to reduce 
water supply from city water. This project will 
collect and analysis the research results related to 
hydrology and hydraulic performance of greenroof from 
domestic and foreign countries. In the research, a 
physical model for greenroof will be build to 
experimental study the hydrological behavior of 
greenroof which includes runoff, evaporation, CN 
value, etc. Based the experimental results and 
hydrologic theory, a general model of rainfall-runoff 
for greenroof will be developed. This model will 
further be used to evaluate the effectiveness of 
urban flood mitigation for different space scale 
installing greenroof. Also, in the study, a 
mathematical model for estimating the storage volume 
requirement for rooftop rainwater harvest system 
combine with greenroof will be developed. It takes 
two years to complete. In the first year (99.08-
100.07), literature review is required focusing in 
the area of hydrology and hydraulic behavior of 
greenroof. Two physical models for rainfall-runoff 
and evaporation will be set up. Indoor and outdoor 
experiment will be carried out. With the experimental 
results and developed theory, a general model of 
rainfall-runoff for greenroof will be developed. 
英文關鍵詞： Greenroof, Water Management, Rainwater Harvesting, 
Urban Stormwater Management.

1. 國內外屋頂綠化相關工程水文水理文獻收集及彙整 
國外屋頂綠化減洪效益文獻回顧： 
A. Getter, Rowe, and Andresen(2007)針對傾斜屋頂綠化作暴雨貯流之定量研究，選定 12 個綠
屋頂並 4種角度(2%、7%、15%、25%)來分析。雨量分為輕量(<2.0mm) (0.08 in.)、中量(2.0–
10.0mm) (0.08–0.39 in.)、高量(>10.0mm) (>0.39 in.)。數據顯示，平均的貯留能力為 80%。
貯流量最少的發生在坡度 25% (76.4%)，最大的發生在坡度 2%(85.6%)。另外，所有坡度的逕
流也發生延遲的效應與逕流分部變為長延時。CN(Curve numbers)值為也從原本 98 降至約
84~90，表示傾斜的屋頂綠化較傳統屋頂可減緩逕流。 
B. 因著綠屋頂貯流雨水，可減低不透水層對逕流的影響。加拿大學者 Peck (2005)估計，若多倫
多有 6%的建築物能擁有屋頂綠化，可貯流的雨水量可等於修造一個 6億美金之涵管。同樣的，
美國學者 Deutsch 與其團隊(2005)提出，在華盛頓特區若能有 20%的建築物擁有綠屋頂，將可
貯流 7.1 億公升(1.9 億加侖)，並一年平均可以儲存 95.8 億公升(25.3 億加侖)。  
C. VanWoert, Rowe, and Andresen (2005)針對屋頂綠化與傳統屋頂在進行量化的分析，總體而
言，在平屋頂之屋頂綠化可達 82.8%之寶水量，若在傾斜斜屋頂施作屋頂綠化，則保水量與一
般屋頂較無明顯差異。 
D. 針對屋頂綠化是否能在 21 世紀作為控制降雨逕流之工具，Mentens, Raes, and Hermy(2006)
對德國 18 個地區報告中的原始數據進行分析，共 628 組數據。以 ANOVA 方法做統計數據分析，
並針對比利時首都布魯塞爾(Brussels)進行模擬分析。以全年逕流量尺度來看，傳統屋頂所產
生之逕流量為 98%，而精緻型屋頂綠化則可降至 15%；若針對不同季節進行分析，則可發現屋
頂綠化於夏天時對逕流減緩有較好的能力，其原因有可能是夏天蒸發散量較高；以暴雨事件來
看，Mentens 等人發現其數據讓無法建立降雨逕流之關係。針對布魯塞爾之模擬，設定 10%大
樓有設置屋頂綠化，則可減洪 2.7~5.4%流量。
