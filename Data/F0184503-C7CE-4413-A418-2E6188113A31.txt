Photopolymerization of Spiroorthoester  
 
Abstract 
The spiroorthoester, 2,3-tetramethylene-1,4,6-trioxa-spiro[4,4]nonane (TTN), 
underwent rapid cationic photopolymerization when exposed to UV light using  
diphenyliodonium salts as a photoinitiator. The polymer thus formed showed 
expansion in volume when comparing to the polymer obtained by the cationic 
polymerization of TTN initiated by a Lewis acid (e.g., BF3OEt2, CH3OSO2CF3, or 
SnCl4), which always showed shrinking in volume during polymerization. Both the 
cationic polymerization of TTN initiated by photoinitiator and Lewis acid caused 
the side reaction to afford by-products, γ-butyrolactone and poly(1,2-cyclohexene 
oxide) (PCHO) oligomer. The polymer thus formed was the copolymer, 
poly[(OCB)x-co-(CHO)y], of poly(2-oxycyclohexyl butanoate) (POCB) and PCHO 
segments. The volume expansion of the copolymer from photopolymerization was 
due to the lower content of the high volume-shrunk PCHO segment in the 
copolymer and, the most important, the higher mole ratio of trans isomer of 
2-oxycyclohexyl groups in the POCB segments of the copolymer.    
 
 
INRRODUCTION  
 
The study of expanding monomers that show low shrinkage or even expansion 
during polymerization is interested in applications as matrix resins for dental 
restoration, high-strength composites, adhesives, coatings, precision castings, and 
sealant materials.1,2 Volume shrinkage during polymerization is known due to the 
reduction of the longer van der Waals distances between monomer molecules to the 
shorter covalent bond length. The total value of the shrinkage during polymerization 
is determined by such factors as size of the monomer, degree of polymerization, state 
of monomer and resulting polymer, and type of polymerization.2,3 Double 
ring-opening polymerization is one of many strategies to reduce shrinkage during 
polymerzation.3 In this case, the shrinkage is compensated for every newly formed 
bond in the polymers by breaking the covalent bonds in the monomer. Acting as 
expanding monomers, spiroorthoesters have been proved to undergo cationic double 
ring-opening polymerization and show volume expansion during polymerization.4

Cationic photopolymerizations  
 
In the dry box, TTN monomer (0.50 g, 2.72 mmol) was mixed with DPI (1 
wt-% of TTN) and (if necessary) photosensitizer (0.5 wt-% of TTN). The mixture for 
photopolymerrization study was deposited onto the polypropylene plate at a thickness 
of 30 μm using polypropylene film as a spacer. Another 30 μm thick transparent 
polypropylene film was laminated on top of the liquid sample to prevent the diffusion 
of water. The sample was photopolymerized at 25 ℃ using a BLX-E254 crosslinker 
(Vilber Lourmat, France) with λmax at 254 nm. The light intensity at the sample 
position was 6.0 mW/cm2 as measured by radiometry (UVX-25 radiometer). The 
procedure for separating the PCHO and purifying the product was similar to the 
procedure mentioned above.

RESULT AND DISCUSSION 
 
Cationic polymerization of TTN initiated by Lewis acids   
 
     Cationic polymerization of TTN was carried out using BF3OEt2, CH3OSO2CF3, 
or SnCl4 as an initiator, and the reaction was monitored by FT-IR spectroscopy. In this 
kind of reaction, an inevitable built-in side reaction to produce γ-butyrolactone and 
PCHO were found. Fig,1 showed the change of the degree of conversion of TTN 
initiated by BF3OEt2 and the concentration of γ-butyrolactone with time at various 
temperature. The conversion of TTN and the concentration of γ-butyrolactone 
increase simultaneously with time. Fig. 2 was the IR spectra of the TTN initiated by 
BF3OEt2 at room temperature. The carbonyl absorption of both the γ-butyrolactone 
(1780 cm-1) and the POCB segment of the procuct (1731 cm-1) was found 
immediately right after the addition of the initiator into TTN (curve A), and both the 
absorptions at 1780 and 1731 cm-1 increased with time (curve B). While the IR 
spectrum of the POCB did not change at all, no absorption at 1780 cm-1 was found 
when product was stirred with BF3OEt2 in dichloromethane (curve C). The 
phenomenon suggests that γ-butyrolactone is produced as long as both the TTN and 
Lewis acid are present in the reaction system and nothing to do with the resulting 
POCB segment. After removing the γ-butyrolactone under reduced pressure, another 
by-product, PCHO oligomer, was obtained by extracting the reacted residual with 
water. The structure of the PCHO was identified by IR and NMR spectroscopy, and 
elemental analysis. The resulting polymer and by-products of the TTN polymerized in 
various initiators and reaction conditions are listed in Table 1.         
 
Cationic photopolymerization of TTN  
 
     When the TTN was exposed to UV radiation (λmax; 254 nm) in the presence of 
the DPI under water-free condition (laminate), the ring-opening polymerization 
occurred instantly. The reactions (in bulk or solution) are completed within 1 min. The 
by-products of γ-butyrolactone and PCHO oligomer are found with the amounts a 
little lowers than that of induced by the Lewis initiator (Table 1).  
The cationic photopolymerization of TTN can be achieved also by photosensitized 
decomposition of DPI at longer UV wavelength (365 nm) with isopropylthioxanthone 
(ITX)11or 2,2-dimethoxy-2-phenylacetophenone (DPA)12 as the photosensitizer. The 
γ-butyrolactone and PCHO are still found regardless of the species of the initiating 
cations (Table 1).

中文摘要 
     本研究將表面含有螺環基團單體(SOE)之改質二氧化矽奈米膠
體顆粒(CSOE)，與壓克力樹脂 bis-GMA/TEGDMA (GT)、光敏感劑 CQ 
(camphorquinone)、光起始劑 OPIA (4-octylphenyl phenyl iodonium 
hexafluro antimonate)進行混合製成高分枝熱固型樹脂(Hyper 
branches thermosetting resin);其在固化燈(λmax : 420 nm)的照
射下，GT 與 CSOE 同時分別進行自由基與陽離子光聚合反應形成一互
穿網 (interpenetrating polymer network,IPN)結構之高抗壓強
度、低收縮之壓克力/二氧化矽混成材料。以精密儀器如 FTIR、萬能
拉力試驗機等分析 IPN 結構混成材料之光硬化轉化率、體積收縮變
化、機械性質，並進而評估 IPN 結構對材料硬化收縮、抗壓縮強度之
影響。實驗結果發現，IPN 結構之壓克力/二氧化矽混成材料能有效
降低硬化收縮率及改善抗壓縮強度。

4.結論 
本研究以壓克力樹脂 GT 為有機相，含奈米二氣化矽顆粒 CSOE 為無機
相 ;將兩者以 20 :80 比例混合後加入光起始劑及光敏感劑 CQ ，進
行光聚合反應 ，製備成具有互穿網絡結構混成材料 GT20/CSOE80，
在測試其性質後，可以歸納出下列幾項結論: 
1.FTIR 的結果得知，GT20/CSOE80 在照光下分別進自由基和陽離子
光聚合反應 ，形成有機網絡與無機網絡相互穿隧之 IPN 結構 ，混
合材料中壓克力基團的轉化率皆達到 70%以上 ，螺環原酸酯基團的
轉化率約 66.07%。  
2.混成材料在硬化期間 ，IPN 結構的 GT20/CSOE80 能有效降低硬
化收縮率(5.23%)，優於市售的牙科綴補材料(SDI)之 7.2% 。 
3. 抗壓縮強度方面 ，GT50/CSOE50 以下與市售的牙科綴補材料相
較有較高抗壓縮強度。
