利用超臨界二氧化碳去除鋼鐵業集塵灰污染物之研究 
            計畫編號：NSC 95-2221-E-324-015 
            執行期限： 95 年 8 月 1 日至 96 年 7 月 31 日 
            計畫主持人：劉敏信 博士   共同計畫主持人：羅煌木 博士 
            計畫參與人員：謝金村、孫立明 
摘要 
電弧爐煉鋼業主要以廢鋼鐵原料為主，在煉鋼過程中廢鋼鐵於爐內高溫熔融還原後再
生為鋼水後煉製為鋼，其熔、沸點低之物質則於袋式集塵器集成飛灰。其中所含物質除有
機污染物多環芳香烴(PAHs)外，還包含無機重金屬污染物，此有害重金屬物質的濃度使得
集塵灰被歸列為有害事業廢棄物。因集塵灰含有大量鋅等有價金屬成分，若能將其中具經
濟價值之金屬氧化物進行回收，則可達到資源化再利用的目的。因此本研究乃利用單一批
次螯合劑淋洗、單一批次界面活性劑淋洗與超臨界 CO2 萃取試驗，將特定有害重金屬以及
多環芳香烴去除而殘餘之重金屬成分可進行資源回收再利用。在超臨界 CO2 萃取試驗中，
重金屬萃取方面還使用螯合劑包含乙二胺四乙酸(EDTA)、檸檬酸，以及檸檬酸添加幾丁聚
醣之溶液以增加萃取效率，過程中同時事先添加不同濃度之過氧化氫改變集塵灰的氧化還
原電位，增加重金屬之溶解度與移動性，以利集塵灰中特定重金屬之去除。在螯合劑淋洗
萃取重金屬結果發現，得最佳操作條件乃使用 0.14%過氧化氫進行前處理，再利用 EDTA
的溶液淋洗，其萃取重金屬鎘及鉛部分，萃取率可達 40%以上；在萃取重金屬鋅部分，其
萃取率在 5%以下，故為最有利後續進行氧化鋅之回收，再進行超臨界 CO2 萃取。超臨界
CO2 萃取結果發現，以添加 5% (v/v)甲醇修飾劑具有提升 5~10%之萃取效率，在溫度 60ºC
及壓力 300 Bar 之操作參數條件下萃取效果最佳。固定操作參數條件下其添加不同濃度之過
氧化氫結果發現，以過氧化氫 0.14%萃取效率最佳，在超臨界 CO2 萃取前添加微量之過氧
化氫濃度可提升 10~15%萃取效率；在螯合劑萃取結果發現，使用 0.14%過氧化氫進行前處
理並配合螯合劑 EDTA，超臨界 CO2 單一批次萃取對重金屬鎘、鉛及鋅去除率約可達 30%
以上其萃取效率最佳，故添加螯合劑具有明顯提升 5%以上之萃取效率。多環芳香烴萃取方
面，在單一批次界面活性劑淋洗試驗中，發現當界面活性劑必須達到臨界微胞濃度後，才
有將多環芳香烴從集塵灰中淋洗出來之能力，當界面活性劑濃度增加至 10 倍臨界微胞濃度
時，去除率可達 70%。在超臨界 CO2 萃取試驗中，當 CO2 達到超臨界流體之狀態時，以具
備去除集塵灰中多環芳香烴之能力，添加甲醇修飾劑與否與去除率較無明顯之關連。依最
佳操作條件下進行研究，其有利於後續進行氧化鋅之回收。 
 
關鍵詞：超臨界流體萃取、鋼鐵業集塵灰、重金屬、螯合劑、多環芳香烴、界面活性劑 
 
Abstract 
The electricity arc furnaces in steelmaking industry usually use waste steel and iron as raw 
materials mainly.  The waste steel is melted in steelmaking process while the low melting point 
materials is integrated to EAF dust and collected in the bag house of air pollution control device.  
以及多環芳香烴去除而殘餘之有價重金屬成分
可進行資源回收再利用。研究中使用之螯合劑
包含乙二胺四乙酸(EDTA)、檸檬酸，以及檸檬
酸添加幾丁聚醣之溶液，研究中同時利用不同
濃度之過氧化氫改變集塵灰的氧化還原電位，
增加重金屬之溶解度與移動性，以利集塵灰中
特定重金屬之去除。此 EDTA 為一種有機螯合
劑其當溶於水中和形成水溶液鹽類，毒性低【1
】，此外所使用的幾丁聚醣(Chitosan)為生物性
高分子，其在自然界中的含量僅次於纖維素且
製造的成本便宜，且具螯合的作用【2】。幾丁
聚醣與重金屬吸附具多重模式進行，其中包括
了離子交換、螯合、吸附等機制，針對不同情
況則有不同優勢機制產生【3】，幾丁聚醣的特
性呈弱鹼性陽離子性聚合電解質（pKa = 6.3）
，分子上具有高密度的官能基，可有效降低污
泥量【4】。另外氧化還原電位(Oxidation reduction 
potential, ORP)及 pH 值乃為重金屬型態價數之
指標，並影響重金屬之移動性、溶解度與污染
物之活性【5】，ORP 的改變可提供污染物的溶
解度，藉以提升不同螯合劑溶液萃取集塵灰中
重金屬的效率。所使用之界面活性劑為 Triton 
X-100 是一種非離子型之界面活性劑。非離子型
界面活性劑在水中不會解離出離子，因此稱其
為中性正是因為其不會解離產生帶電離子的關
係，因此毒性比其他種類界面活性劑中較低【6
】。界面活性劑淋洗集塵灰中有機污染物的機
制主要為溶化作用(Solubilization)以及乳化作用
(Emulsification)【7】。當界面活性劑存在水中
時，界面活性劑單體(Monomers)會均勻散佈在
水中，隨著界面活性劑濃度增加時，導致單體
將聚合成一個巨大的分子團，此時界面活性劑
的親油基會集合於內部，而將親水基朝外排列
，此分子團稱為微胞(Micelle)，而界面活性劑形
成微胞所需之最小濃度及為臨界微胞濃度
(Critical Micelle Concentration, CMC)，此時水溶
液的洗淨力、密度、表面張力、滲透壓、當量
電導度等物化性質會產生巨大之變化，如圖 1
。因此以臨界微胞濃度為一單位，進行集塵灰
之淋洗試驗，藉以去除集塵灰中多環芳香烴。 
    針對超臨界流體相關研究進行探討，關於
重金屬方面，利用超臨界二氧化碳萃取金屬離
子，已發現不具任何效果【8】，因為金屬離子
非電中性和溶質溶劑之間的結合力太弱所造成
。由於金屬離子本身不溶於超臨界二氧化碳中
，因此在超臨界流體中加入適當的螯合劑，此
螯合劑必須能溶解於超臨界二氧化碳中，而且
能提供電荷電性中和之能力，從而生成金屬螯
合物，進而溶解於超臨界二氧化碳中。金屬螯
合物於超臨界流體之溶解度，乃決定萃取效率
的重要因素之ㄧ，Lagalante等人【9】就曾利用
二酮類(β-diketone)螯合劑探討銅金屬螯合物於
超臨界二氧化碳之溶解度。存在於環境樣品中
之過渡金屬，如鑭系及錒系金屬，可利用溶解
於超臨界二氧化碳中之螯合劑輕易的去除【10
】。Glennon等人【11】則使用Hydrooxamic acid
作為螯合劑，利用超臨界流體萃取金屬離子。
在超臨界流體針對水溶液與矽膠顆粒的重金屬
Cu2+進行萃取，並有良好之萃取效果【12】，
超臨界二氧化碳添加LiFDDC萃取固相介質的
汞離子，結果顯示樣品基質中添加少量水份，
增加Hg(FDDC)2的萃取【13】，故本研究在超
臨界二氧化碳萃取時，在不同濃度之過氧化氫
進行前處理並配合淋洗液溶液萃取試驗。關於
有機污染物方面，超臨界流體應用於環境之領
域中包括活性碳之再生【14】，去除活性碳所
吸附之農藥、乙基醋酸與甲苯；以及廢液或廢
水有機污染物酚之去除【15】，其中修飾劑具
有對去除效率之影響性。研究中亦探討一段式
自廢液中直接以超臨界流體萃取有機污染物，
或者以二段式將廢液有機物吸附於固體吸附劑
上，再以臨界流體自吸附劑中萃取有機污染物
。有關廢液中有機物之去除，於1982 年由
Modell等人【16】 首先利用超臨界水處理系統
直接將有機物氧化，並申請獲得專利。至於利
用超臨界流體萃取介質的有機污染物如多環芳
香烴化合物，Raymer等人【17】及Wright等人
【18】就曾利用超臨界二氧化碳由高分子吸收
劑萃取回收多環芳香烴化合物，並予以定量。
空氣中懸浮微粒之多環芳香烴化合物就曾由
Schantz等人【19】研究萃取，並與傳統的索氏
萃取法比較。而且Hawthorne等人【20】曾使用
放射光譜儀(ICP-OES)分析重金屬，淋洗完後剩
餘之集塵灰，以去離子水淋洗兩次，目的為清
洗出殘餘的淋洗液，去離子水淋洗完之集塵灰
再置入烘箱烘乾後進行王水消化，其王水消化
之溶液再以感應耦合電漿光學放射光譜儀分析
，經由換算後可得知萃取率。每組樣本乃採用
三重覆分析，之後所得數據再進行統計分析。 
2.4  界面活性劑臨界微胞濃度與 Pyrene 回收
率測定 
 將界面活性劑配置不同濃度經表面張力儀
測定其表面張力後，經過計算後即可得知其臨
界微胞濃度。  
 配置 100 mg/kg 濃度之受 Pyrene 污染之集
塵灰，取 1 g 置入離心管中後，加入 10 mL 之
乙腈，以超音波震盪，震盪 10 分鐘後，取其上
層液，重複上述步驟兩次，分別將，上層液以
0.22 μm 之過濾膜過濾，將其濾液注入樣品瓶
中，再以高效能液相層析儀(HPLC)進行分析。
此實驗均進行二重複分析試驗，之後所得數據
再進行統計分析。 
2.5  批次界面活性劑淋洗萃取方法 
 集塵灰與界面活性劑之固液比為 1:10 進行
實驗，其作法是以一支離心管制置入配製污染
之集塵灰 1 g 與 10 mL 界面活性劑，分別以超
音波與水平兩種方式進行萃取後，在以 2,500 
rpm 離心 5 分鐘後，移除淋洗液，重複上述步驟
兩次，針對其殘餘量以乙腈為溶劑，使用超音
波震盪進行萃取，萃取液經 0.22μm 過濾膜過
濾後，再以高效能液相層析儀進行分析。每組
樣本乃採用二重覆分析，之後所得數據再進行
統計分析。 
2.6 超臨界流體萃取試驗 
2.6.1  重金屬萃取 
    利用螯合劑溶液配合不同濃度之過氧化氫
溶液的前處理，運用於超臨界流體萃取上，首
先將集塵灰以聚丙烯(PP)棉包覆，置於不鏽鋼萃
取槽中與過氧化氫溶液以 1:1 (w/v)方式添加不
需靜置時間，並以 1:1 (w/v)的方式加入螯合劑
，將兩端上鎖之後，置入恆溫箱。打開進流體
進流閥，以修飾劑泵浦輸送 5%(v/v)修飾劑甲醇
，之後再開啟流體鋼瓶閥送入二氧化碳，並加
壓至目標壓力ㄧ半後，打開加熱器加熱，待達
目標溫度後，將壓力加至目標值，關閉進流閥
。進行靜置萃取 1 小時。待 1 小時後，打開出
流閥與限流器。將萃出物排出收集器中，剩餘
之土壤置入烘箱乾燥，秤重進行王水消化，最
後萃取液與王水消化液以 ICP-OES 進行分析重
金屬含量分析。當實驗不需添加甲醇作為修飾
劑，則略過輸送 5%(v/v)修飾劑甲醇。超臨界流
體萃取流程如圖 5 所示，在超臨界流體之操作
條件設定方面乃採用 60˚C、100 bar，其超臨界
二氧化碳密度約為 0.84 g/mL。所有超臨界流體
萃取實驗均進行二重複分析試驗，之後所得數
據再進行統計分析。 
2.6.2 多環芳香烴萃取 
 將配置受 Pyrene 污染之集塵灰以聚丙烯
(PP)棉包覆，置於不鏽鋼萃取槽中，將兩端上鎖
之後，置入恆溫箱。打開進流體進流閥，以修
飾劑泵浦輸送修飾劑甲醇，若實驗不需添加甲
醇作為修飾劑，則略過輸送修飾劑甲醇。再開
啟流體鋼瓶閥送入二氧化碳，打開加熱器加熱
，待達目標溫度後，將壓力加至目標值，關閉
進流閥。進行靜置萃取 1 小時。待靜置 1 小時
後，打開出流閥與限流器。將萃出物經收集管
收集，其氣體經粒狀活性碳吸附後，始排出大
氣，剩餘之土壤待其乾燥，以乙腈為溶劑，使
用超音波震盪進行殘餘量萃取，萃取液經 0.22
μm 過濾膜過濾後，再以高效能液相層析儀分
析。本實驗均進行二重複分析試驗，之後所得
數據再進行統計分析。

3.2 氧化還原電位探討

還原電位，其不同過氧化氫濃度與時間之關係
如圖 11 所示。由圖中可了解當過氧化氫濃度增
加時，其氧化還原電位亦會隨之增加，當集塵

降趨勢，故本研究於後續試驗中在添加過氧化
氫後立即進行萃取實驗。

在。

規標準之有害重金屬Cd及Pb，並保留佔有全量
約30%之氧化鋅進行回收，以達集塵灰資源化再
利用。經由計算得知在萃取鋼鐵業集塵灰中以

ABSTRACT 
 
Selective catalytic reduction (SCR) is the most common method for treating nitrogen monoxide 
emissions, with hydrocarbons being considered as a substitute for ammonia as the reducing agent in 
SCR reaction.  In this research, metal catalysts of Pt/Rh and Pd/Rh combined with different types of 
zeolite were utilized for SCR reaction, with the optimal catalyst and operation conditions being 
studied.   
 
The results indicate using a metal catalyst with the addition of CeO2 reduced the conversion 
efficiency of nitrogen monoxide when either propylene or propane was used.  In the comparison of 
two metal catalysts, the conversion efficiency of nitrogen monoxide when using Pt/Rh catalyst was 
better than Pd/Rh regardless of CeO2 addition.  Of the two kinds of reducing agents evaluated, the 
best catalyst was Pt/Rh combined with FER zeolite, as opposed to CeO2.   
 
According to the Power-rate Law and Arrhenius equation, using oxygen had the highest activation 
energy (45.9 kcal/mole); followed by the reducing agent propane (32.5 kcal/mole).  Propylene had 
the lowest activation energy (20 kcal/mole). The data was fitted with Langmuir-Hinshelwood kinetic 
models to determine the kinetics of the reaction.  It was found that the existence of oxygen competed 
with reducing agent and nitrogen monoxide for the superficial activated site of catalyst.  Under an 
oxygen free situation, the reducing agent contended with nitrogen monoxide for the same activated 
site.  Consequently, maintaining a 1:1 ratio of reducing agent to nitrogen monoxide will produce a 
better reaction result. 
 
INTRODUCTION 
 
Nitrogen oxide, produced during the combustion process, can contribute to acid rain, greenhouse 
effect and reacts photochemically with hydrocarbon compound in the atmosphere.  Nitrogen oxide 
includes nitrogen monoxide and nitrogen dioxide which are harmful to human.  Selective catalytic 
reduction (SCR) is the most common method for treating NOx emissions, with hydrocarbons being 
considered as a substitute for ammonia as the reducing agent in SCR reaction.   
 
In SCR reaction, Burch and Millington1 had used hydrocarbon as a reducing agent to educe NO 
using precious metals, indicating the activation capacity order of Platinum, Rhodium, Palladium, 
and Iridium.  Moreover, the usage of Rh could prevent the conversion of nitrogen oxide into 
ammonia.  Oh and Eickel2 had studied the effect of cerium addition on CO oxidation kinetics for 
alumina-supported rhodium catalysts, wherein the results concluded that excess addition of cerium 
oxide reduced the activation capacity of catalysts.  In 1999, Pisanu and Gigola3 used Pd/α-Al2O3 to 
decompose and reduce NO with carbon monoxide, with more than 90% of NO being conversed 
when the temperature reached 240˚C.  Engler et al.4 had used Pt as catalyst to study the efficiency 
of different reducing agents including alkanes and alkenes.  Propane and propylene were proved to 
have a promising result for NO conversion. 
 
The metal catalysts of Pt/Rh and Pd/Rh containing different type of zeolite were utilized for SCR 
reaction in this study, and propane and propylene were selected as reducing agents, with the optimal 
catalyst and operation condition being studied as well.  The resultant data was fitted to the kinetic 
500˚C.  The optimal catalyst was then used to conduct the nitrogen monoxide conversion in 
various operation conditions in order to study the effect of each parameter.  The test conditions 
and evaluated are listed in Table 3.

mixed with Al2O3 (versal-150).  Thereafter, each mixture was dried and calcined at 350˚C for 2

made in 2 × 2Φ cm 400 cpsi monolith with wash-coat to fit into the reactor in the test furnace for 
further study.

The results in Figure 6 and Figure 7 indicate that the conversion of nitrogen monoxide increased 
when the temperature was increased and oxygen was absent, with the conversion rate reaching 
about 80% at 500˚C.  However, the conversion rate of nitrogen monoxide first decreased rapidly 
when oxygen was added, but was then increased by increasing the oxygen content further; the 
conversion reached to about 20% at oxygen content 12%.  It was found that the existence of 
oxygen competed with reducing agent and nitrogen monoxide for the superficial activated site of 
catalyst.  Adding oxygen in the reaction, the conversion of nitrogen monoxide increased with 
increasing temperature at the condition of below 300˚C, however the conversion rate was then 
decreased with temperatures above 300˚C.  According to the literature5, reducing agent did convert 
nitrogen oxide into nitrogen when the oxygen was absent, however the reducing agent was 
consumed when the oxygen was added, consequently reducing the activated sites of catalyst and 
conversion rate of nitrogen oxide.  In addition, the oxygen could react with NO forming NO2.  
The oxygen also could react with reducing agents to form intermediates and further react with NO24.  
Therefore the conversion of nitrogen monoxide could increase with increasing oxygen content.   
 
The results in Figure 8 show that increasing the reducing agent concentration did enhance the 
conversion rate of nitrogen monoxide.  The results in Figure 10 concluded the conversion rate of 
nitrogen monoxide was decreased with the increase of nitrogen monoxide concentration, while the 
conversion rate of propane was increased; the result is shown in Figure 11. 
 
Kinetics of Reaction 
 
Power-rate Law 
 
The regression results of Power-rate law for the SCR reaction when using propane as reducing 
agent are shown in Table 4.  The reaction order of NO increased when the temperature was 
increased, while the reaction order of propane decreased.  The regression results of Power-rate law 
for the SCR reaction when using propylene as reducing agent are shown in Table 5.  The reaction 
order of NO increased when the temperature was increased, while the reaction order of propylene 
was negative or negligible.  Based on the Arrhenius equation, the activation energy was calculated 
and determined to be 32.5 kcal/mole for propane as reducing agent, and the activation energy was 
20 kcal/mole for propylene as reducing agent.
