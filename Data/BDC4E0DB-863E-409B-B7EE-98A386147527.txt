具頻寬效率之非同調多層次編碼調變
Bandwidth-Eﬃcient Noncoherent Multilevel-Coded Modulation
計畫編號:NSC 98-2221-E-008-003-MY2
執行期限:98年8月1日至100年07月31日
主持人: 魏瑞益 國立中央大學通訊工程學系
一、 摘要
本報告包含三個部份: 非同調 (noncoherent) 多層次
(multilevel) 區塊編碼 (block-coded) 的三十二點正交振
幅調變 (quadrature-amplitude modulation,QAM)、 以
電腦搜尋之短非同調區塊碼以及用於廣義相差編碼 (gen-
eralized diﬀerential encoding)的非同調區塊編碼 QAM
和 TAPSK。 在第一個部份, 我們推導出用32QAM 之
區塊編碼調變的最小非同調距離, 而根據此結果提出非
同調區塊編碼32QAM(NBC-32QAM) 架構。 在相同資
料傳輸速率下的例子, 我們比較 NBC-16QAM 以及
NBC-32QAM 的最小非同調距離和錯誤效能。 而對於不
同區塊長度以及不同的構成碼,我們也分析並模擬 NBC-
32QAM 的錯誤效能。 然而, 我們發現 NBC-QAM 在
區塊長度小的時候錯誤效能並不理想,這是因為多層次編
碼造成最小非同調距離太小, 所以在第二個部份, 我們尋
找具有好的短非同調區塊碼。 因為短區塊的碼字 (code-
word) 個數不會太多,接收端可以一個碼字接一個碼字的
測試, 所以可以使用沒有結構的碼。 我們使用電腦進行各
種演算法的碼搜尋, 以找到最小非同調距離較大的碼, 並
且透過最佳的位元對應,讓具有較小非同調距離的碼字對
能對應到差異較少的位元, 進而得到比 NBC-QAM 更令
人滿意的的錯誤效能。 最後在第三個部份, 我們考慮非同
調區塊編碼 QAM 和 TAPSK, 使其能用於廣義相差編
碼。 在連續通道上, 非同調區塊編碼 MPSK 可以藉由旋
轉整個碼字的訊號點,使得第一個符元與前一次傳送碼字
的最後一個符元相同而不用傳, 以提升頻寬效率, 這稱為
廣義相差編碼。 然而, 之前所提的非同調區塊編碼 QAM
和 TAPSK 無法使用廣義相差編碼, 所以我們修改非同
調區塊編碼 QAM 和 TAPSK 的定義及星座圖的位元
對應。我們比較一些使用廣義相差編碼的非同調區塊編碼
16QAM 及16TAPSK 的錯誤率模擬。
關鍵詞: 非同調檢測, 區塊編碼調變, 多層次編碼
Abstract
This report includes three parts: noncoher-
ent multilevel block-coded 32QAM, short noncoher-
ent block codes searched by computers and nonco-
herent block-coded QAM/TAPSK for generalized dif-
ferential encoding. In the ﬁrst part, the minimum
noncoherent distance of block-coded 32QAM is de-
rived ﬁrst. According to the derived distance, non-
coherent block-coded 32QAM (NBC-32QAM) is pro-
posed. Then the minimum noncoherent distance and
error performance of NBC-32QAM is compared with
NBC-16QAM for examples with the same data rate.
For diﬀerent block length and code rate, we also an-
alyze and simulate the error performance of NBC-
32QAM. However, we found that NBC-QAM has poor
error performance when block length is small because
of its small noncoherent distance due to multilevel
coding. Therefore, in the second part, we look for
good short nonocherent block codes. Because a short
nonocherent block code does not have many code-
words and thus the recever can test codewords one
by one, codes without any structure are be used. We
complete various code-search algorithms by comput-
ers to ﬁnd codes with the largest minimum nonco-
herent distance. we also use an optimal bit label-
ing algorithm to let codeword pairs with small non-
coherent distance diﬀer the least labeling bits. The
resulting short noncoherent block codes have better
error performance than NBC-QAM. In the ﬁnal part,
we consider noncoherent block-coded QAM/TAPSK
for generalized diﬀerential encoding. For noncoherent
block-coded MPSK over continuous channels, rotat-
ing signal points of an entire codeword, such that the
ﬁrst symbol is the same as the last symbol of the pre-
viously transmitted codeword and thus can be omit-
ted, can increase bandwidth eﬃciency. This method
is called generalized diﬀerential encoding. Since non-
coherent block-coded QAM/TAPSK proposed before
cannot be applied to generalized diﬀerential encoding,
in this part, we modify the deﬁnition of noncoherent
block-coded QAM/TAPSK and the bit labeling of sig-
