Physiological Signals' Phase Shift Analysis in Traumatic Brain Injury Patients

一、中文摘要 
近年來，醫學科技的進步使得我們能夠
治癒世界上大部分的疾病，但是腦部損傷病
患的治療，手術的選擇，腦部複雜的結構使
我們在選擇治療方式上增添了更多的難
度，對於腦傷病患的腦死判定也因此顯得格
外重要，本篇的目的在於尋找一種較為簡易
的方法來建立嚴重頭部創傷病患的術後評
估指標以確定腦死亡，醫師依據本身臨床診
療的經驗判讀生理訊號的變化來診斷並預
測病患的術後狀況，以及判定同樣屬於嚴重
腦傷的不可逆之呼吸停止昏迷（irreversible 
apnoeic coma, IAC）病患，本研究希望透過
分析患者的即時數據，判定自主神經活性異
常帶來的不可逆呼吸停止病患的真實情
況，訂定醫療計畫，提高醫療品質與病患安
全。 
關鍵詞：不可逆之呼吸停止昏迷、嚴重頭部
外傷病患預後評估指標、自主神經系統 
Abstract 
Recently, because of medical technology 
progress, we could cure most of disease in the 
world. But, the treatment of the Traumatic 
Brain Injury patients still has some problems. 
The complex structure of the brain makes 
more difficult in select treatment, so the 
judicial brain death determination process 
becomes very important. The purpose of this 
study is to find out an easier way to diagnose 
the prognosis of the patients with severe head 
injury, and offer the medical staffs other 
information to determine brain death. Medical 
doctors depend on their experience to 
diagnose patients’ prognosis and some brain 
death patients with irreversible apneic coma 
(IAC) by observing the variation of different 
physiological signals. This study tried to 
judge the abnormal of activity of autonomic 
nervous system by analysis patients’ real-time 
data in order to make treatment plans for the 
neurocritical care to enhance patients’ safety 
and the quality of care. 
Keyword: irreversible apnoeic coma (IAC), 
brain death index (BDI), activity of 
autonomic nervous system
