ABSTRACT 
 
GABA tea ethanol extracts was prepared according to the optimum extraction conditions 
of the previous report (NSC 97-2221-E-241-001), and the purposes of this study were 
designed to investigate the active compounds, antimicrobial activity, microencapsulation and 
tablet processing of ethanolic extracts from GABA tea, and its applications in bakery products 
and pork patties. The results showed that (1) the extraction yield, total polyphenol, GC 
(gallocatechin), EGC (epigallocatechin), EC (epicatechin), EGCG (epigallocatechin gallate), 
GCG (gallocatechin gallate), ECG (epicatechin gallate), GABA, theanine and caffeine 
contents are 32.21 ± 0.91%?492.35 ± 2.87?2.54 ± 0.34?40.03 ± 2.43?19.52 ± 0.48?82.97 
± 2.92?1.77 ± 0.34?18.49 ±0.37?165.32 ± 2.98?5.79 ± 0.32?6.24 ± 0.37?68.01 ± 1.32 mg/g 
extract, respectively. (2) In general, antimicrobial activities increased when the amounts of 
GABA ethanol extracts increased. GABA tea ethanol extracts has potential antimicrobial 
activities effect on G (+) Staphylcoccus aureus and Bacillus cereus than that of Salmonella 
typhimurium, E. coli and E. coli O157:H7. In addition, the antimicrobial activities of GABA 
tea ethanol extracts are higher than those of potassium sorbate. (3) In the microencapsulation, 
the inlet temperature of spray drier affected the retention of active compounds of product. But 
the retention of active compounds of product was not affected by carrier concentration. Based 
on the results obtained, 130-150  of?  inlet temperature of spray drier was the optimum 
processing conditions. The product was packed by PET/Al/PE bag or PE can seal with Al foil, 
the qualities of product was not affected by storage temperature, humidity and light during 3 
months. In the development of tablet, the microcapsules of GABA tea extracts, excipient 
(including potato starch, methyl cellulose, dibasic calcium phosphate, magnesium stearate) and 
fruit powders (including kumquat, lemon, roselle) were pressed by tablet machine. The tablet 
of GABA ethanol extract was successful developed in the study. The product was packed 
by PET/Al/PE bag, the qualities of product was not affected by storage temperature, humidity 
and light during 2 months. (5) In bakery products, the hedonic scores of chiffon cakes 
prepared with 3.2% replacement of cake flour with GABA tea ethanol extract are the highest 
among chiffon cakes. (6) The hedonic scores of samples were prepared with 4-5 g GABA 
ethanol extracts addition for 1 kg pork patties are the highest among samples.
