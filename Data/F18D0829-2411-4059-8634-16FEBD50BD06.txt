Abstract 
The proposal program is a final year plan of three years project. The main objective in 
the project will investigate the dynamic characteristics of the soil behind the flexural wall 
during earthquake. Moreover the analytical method of lateral spreading of the soil was also 
discussed and developed. An analytical model about flexural unit-length beam theory was 
analyzed and compared with the results of shaking table tests. And effect of fines content on 
liquefaction resistance is also studied. Following past two years, the cyclic tri-axial tests were 
also sequentially conducted for the silty sands containing different fines content. The testing 
results in the literature were studied by means of inter-granular void ratio concept.
