Abstract  
   Diamond-like carbon (DLC), a-Si and silicon carbide nanocomposites (C/Si or 
C/SiC) will be fabricated by ultrahigh vacuum ion beam sputter (UHV IBS) system.  
In general, nanocomposite films have the potential advantages of super high hardness 
and high thermal stability as the film thickness is scaled down to a few nanometers.  
In this study, the film dimension can be easily controlled by atomic scale for the 
growth of DLC/SiC nanocomposites. Many DLC/SiC nanocomposites with different 
nanocrystalline microstructure will be formed by controlling the different Si/C 
multilayer thickness, layer number, substrate temperature, annealing temperature, ion 
energy and incident angle, gas flow and pressure.  Material analysis technologies 
such as X-ray diffraction, SEM/HRSEM, AES/ESCA, Raman and AFM are used to 
examine the nanocomposite composition, phase, microstructure, chemical bonding 
and morphology.  The correlation of nanocrystalline microstructure, process and 
formation mechanism of the nanocomposites will be established.  Since both DLC 
and SiC materials have the mechanical advantages of high hardness, good wear 
resistance and low stiction, we could prepare the C/SiC or C/Si nanocomposites with 
high hardness, high mouldulus, low friction and low stiction for the potential 
application to mechanical and MEMS/NEMS devices.  Mechanical properties of the 
nanocomposites will be measured by nanoindentation and scratch test. The 
relationship among the mechanical properties, microstructure and process of the 
nanocomposites will be discussed and established. 
 
Key words： nano multilayer, sputter, carbon, silicon, mechanical property, 
optoelectronic  property

disorder-induced peak (D-peak)。表 3-2列出了相對G-peak與D-peak的位置，半高

從室溫到 500 °C而增強，然後強度開始減弱，到 700 °C時，D-peak會完全消失。
此結果顯示了碳膜的 disorder state會隨溫度上升而增加，之後，碳膜開始與矽發
生反應大約在 600 °C，最後在 700 °C時反應完全。此推論也於拉曼位移 (Raman 
shift)在 900-1050 cm−1的不對稱 peak得到證實。從文獻可以知道，結晶矽 (c-Si-Si)
與矽、碳 (crystalline Si-C)的鍵結可以分別從 900-1050 cm−1與 950-1000 cm−1觀測
到 [21,22]。 所 以 在 900-1050 cm−1 的 不 對 稱 peak表 示 了 結 晶 矽 (c-Si-Si)與 矽 碳
(crystalline Si-C)的光譜重疊。當矽、碳 (crystalline Si-C)的鍵結增加時，此非對
稱性的 peak將越趨明顯。此外，由G-peak與D-peak的強度趨勢可以了解，其強度
會在 600 °C時減弱並在 700 °C時消失，此歸因於碳化矽 (c-SiC)的形成。

四、結論 
實驗探討單層、雙層、三層及五層碳 /矽奈米複合結構中，在室溫下及在不
同退火方式、溫度與退火時間下，結構反應與機械性質的關係。實驗結果發現
利用基板加熱的方式於單層碳膜中，當加熱溫度升高至 500 °C時，由於石墨化的
形成造成奈米碳膜的機械性質下降，當溫度持續升高使得碳與矽開始產生反應
並於 700 °C時反應完全形成碳化矽奈米網狀結構，而碳化矽與網狀結構的形成，
有助於機械性質的提升。而當利用真空退火的方式於三層及五層的複合膜結構
當中，發現當真空退火溫度在 700 °C時，硬度值有提高的現象，這可能是在 700 °C
時產生的碳化矽造成的。當真空退火溫度達 900 °C時，三層碳 /矽複合薄膜與五
層碳 /矽複合薄膜的表面粗糙度皆有明顯變化，這主要是多層薄膜中的矽因受退
火溫度影響，產生過度結晶化的結果。此外，實驗發現五層碳 /矽複合薄膜的硬
度比三層碳 /矽複合薄膜高，這與薄膜中碳佔有的比例較高有關；五層碳 /矽複合
薄膜的楊氏係數也比三層碳 /矽複合薄膜高，可提高材料的剛性，降低材料外力
變形量。實驗結果顯示藉由適度調整多層複合薄膜的厚度與層數，將可以改善
材料的機械性質。在另一方面，先沉積一層矽薄膜，將有助於減緩類鑽碳因形
成複雜且交鏈極高的網狀立體結構而產生的高應力，並且改善類鑽碳在基材的
附著性不佳問題。在抗磨耗試驗當中發現，當增加單層碳膜厚度時，其硬度與
楊氏係數的大小會隨著膜厚的增加而提升，相反地，彈性恢復率 (Elastic recovery)
會隨著厚度的增加而降低。當加入矽膜於碳膜上層時可以有效的提升硬度與楊
氏係數，然而，彈性恢復率卻會降低。三層結構在室溫及快速熱退火條件下的
機械性質討論發現，上下層矽膜的厚度越厚，將導致複合硬度的下降，因為非
晶矽膜本身的硬度只有 12.29 GPa、非晶碳膜硬度 18.35 GPa，所以上層矽膜厚度
低於 25 nm，將有助於複合硬度的提升。由於類鑽碳薄膜為碳原子所構成，所以
在一般的鋼材表面沉積類鑽碳薄膜時，會受限於鋼材的溶碳效應，也因其下層
結構為矽成份，可以改善與含碳基材的附著性，所以可以將此最佳性質結構薄
膜應用在刀具切削上。利用PECM的方法所製備之工具，其表面以及邊緣半徑可

both H and E variations are similar. Both increase with

FN2%. Both values maximize at nc-TiN/a-Si3N4

indicates that the nanocomposite nc-TiN/a-Si3N4 
microstructure at 5 FN2% exhibits higher hardness and 
modulus than amorphous microstructure at 10 FN2%. 
Moreover, experimental results showed that the 
mechanical properties of films were dependent on the 
preferred orientation of TiN as show in Fig. 6.  When Ti 
power increases, the intensity of TiN(111) and (200) 
peaks become higher and enhance the hardness. With a 
further increase of Si power, (111) and (200) peaks 
disappear and the mechanical properties of the films 
decrease accordingly .The maximum hardness of 24.86 
GPa occurs at Ti 150 W, Si 100 W and 5 FN2%.  
 
Conclusions 
The Ti-Si-N thin flims were prepared by reactive 
magnetron co-sputtering system whose microstructure 
and mechanical property were affected by the 
experimental parameters of nitrogen flow ratio, Ti power 
and Si power.The nanocomposite Ti-Si-N thin films have 
a quasi-amorphous microstructure with nanocrystalline 
grains embedded in an amorphous matrix at 3-5 FN2% 
and favors to form the amorphization of Ti-Si-N films at 
higher 10 FN2%. According to Scherrer’s formula, the 
grain size of films was below 5 nm. The surface 
morphology each film was very smooth due to fine 
grains. The maximum hardness was 24.86 GPa at 5 FN% 
which occurs at the transformation between 
polycrystalline and amorphous phases. The trend of both 
H and E ratio variations is similar. It increases first with 
FN2% then decreases. XRD spectra revealed that that 
texture(111) and (200) has a strong influence on hardness 
values. As the sputtering power of Ti increased from 75 
W to 150 W, the hardness of films also increased from 
18.95 GPa to 24.86 GPa due to the texture of (200) 
increased. With a further increase of Si power, (111) and 
(200) peaks disappear and the mechanical properties of 
the films decrease accordingly Consequently, the amount 
of grains grown along the (200) orientation which is the 
main reason for enhancement of mechanical properties 
on Ti-Si-N film.
