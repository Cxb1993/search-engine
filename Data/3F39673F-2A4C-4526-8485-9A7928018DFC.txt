一、 參加會議經過 
本次研討會自 2006 年 9 月 10 日至 2006年 9 月 14 日為止，約有近 3500 位各國
的學者專家參與本次研討會，本次研討會之會議主題包括： 
 
1. Appropriate and Non-Conventional Wastewater Systems  
 
2. Drinking Water Treatment  
 
3. Health and the Environment  
 
4. Integrated Water Resources and River Basin Management  
 
5. Operating and Managing Water and Wastewater Systems  
 
6. Strategic Management of Water in Urban Areas  
 
7. Wastewater Treatment
