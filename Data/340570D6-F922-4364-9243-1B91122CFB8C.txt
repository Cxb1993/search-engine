and civilian combustion facilities which burn 
hydrocarbon fuel need to be modified or redesigned to 
fit in with the demands for high-efficiency and 
low-emission combustion. 
The main objective of the present project is to 
develop a low emission and high efficiency 
ultra-lean-premixed burner. It is well known that 
lean premixed combustion is the most effective means 
to 
reduce the NOx emission and carbon monoxide from the 
hydrocarbon flame to-date. However, the practical 
application of lean premixed combustion technique is 
still limited and not much progress has been made due
to its unstable and transient nature of the flame and 
the difficulty in accurately controlling of the 
flame. Past 
studies have shown that a burner such as rich-lean 
flame burner whose lean-premixed flames are sustained 
by 
rich-premixed flames can be operated to achieve the 
goal of low emission and fuel-saving. Therefore, in 
the 
present project, the ultra-lean-premixed combustor 
for civilian application based on the concept of 
rich-lean 
combustor will be designed, built and tested. In 
addition, based on the results that will be obtained 
via 
proposed study, a complete database will be 
established and can be served as the design basis for
low-emission and high-efficiency ultra-lean flame 
burners.

技術說明
(中文) 本技術目的在於應用於開發高效率低污染的超貧油燃燒器，透過本技術以過去富
油-貧油燃燒器為基礎進行研發與修改，實現以穩定貧油燃燒成為導引火焰穩駐
超貧油火焰，設計可行的超貧油瓦斯燃燒器並且進行各種燃燒反應之測試。利用
所設計之燃燒器測試液化石油氣以及天然氣的操作參數資料庫，以作為後續工程
應用之基礎。本技術可以結合業界在燃燒器系統方面的經驗，結合學術界已開發
之技術與知識製作出高效率且低污染的新型超貧油燃燒器，用以取代現有的瓦斯
爐具之傳統爐嘴以及熱水器中的火排(燃燒器)。
