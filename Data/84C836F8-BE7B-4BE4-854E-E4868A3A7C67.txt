Planning, Design and implementation of e-voting mechanism and 
system—Trust and the E-voting Systems in Taiwan (II)  
 
Abstract  
     This research is the second sub-project of the general project--planning, design 
and implementation of e-voting mechanism and system. The main purposes in the 
study are (1) to understand voters’ trust toward using the e-voting system instead of 
traditional paper voting; and (2) to explore the influential factors on voters’ trust to 
use the e-voting system in Taiwan. According the first-year study, attitude toward 
using the e-voting system and security of the e-voting system are main elements to 
influence users’ trust to use it. Meanwhile, the attitude is influenced by ease of use 
and perceived usefulness. Therefore, while enhancing the e-voting system instead of 
traditional voting, users’ perceptions of security, ease and usefulness should be 
considered in this study.  
 
Key Word：E-voting System, Technology Acceptance Model, Trust

是一重要議題，牽涉到資訊科技安全與對資訊科技之信任。自學者Davis (1989)
提出科技接受模式(TAM)並說明適當使用資訊科技是有賴於使用者行為意向。
Davis et al. (1989)以理性行為理論(theory of reasoned action, TRA)為基礎修改而
成科技接受模型，是特別針對使用者之科技使用行為而發展，從使用者的認知與
情感因素，探討使用者與科技使用之間的關係，其目的在於解釋一般人對於科技
接受的決定因素，TAM可以用來解釋資訊科技之應用(Ma & Liu 2004)，此模型用
於解釋使用者使用科技的行為意向之態度。因此，此模型具有兩項重要因素為便
利性(perceived ease of use)與適用性(perceived usefulness)。「行為意願」意旨一個
人在表現某一特定行為時所展現出來的意願強度，此變相常被用來預測或解釋實
際行為的表現。「行為態度」是指一個人在表現某特定行為時所感受到正面或負
面的評價。此特定行為態度會受到其旅行該行為時所產生行為信念(behavioral 
belief)與結果評估(outcome evaluation)影響(李志成、姜齊，2006)。 
 依上述，科技接受模型提供一個理論基礎，用以了解外部因子對於使用者
的信念(beliefs)、態度(attitude)與意圖(intention)的影響，進而影響使用者對科技
使用的情形。

第四章 研究結果與發現 
    依回收有效問卷是368份(92%)。依性別分析，男性為217人（59%），則女
性為151人（41%）。受訪者年齡以20-25歲居多。基本上，受訪者都已有使用電
腦經驗，以5-7年居多。42.60%的受訪者有投票經驗；約67%的受訪者曾經使用
過網路投票(I-voting)，卻只有34.50%受訪者表示聽過電子投票方式。（見表一）
由此可知，若日後想推動電子投票方式，則需加強使用須知之宣導，讓使用者減
少疑慮。依可信度分析，五個變數其信度都超過0.70，因此，此問卷之問題具可
靠性（Cronbach’s α＞.60）。 
 依研究結果顯示，電子投票系統之便利性對此系統之適用性具有正面影響
（t=8.28, p＜.001）；而電子投票系統之適用性對使用此系統之態度具有正面影響
（t=8.52, p＜.001）。使用電子投票系統態度對此系統信任度具正面影響（t=5.67, 
p＜.001）。電子投票系統之安全性分別對使用此系統之態度與對此系統之信任度
皆具有正面影響（分別為 t=7.81, p＜.001：t=9.07, p＜.001）；但是，電子投票系
統之安全性對信任度之影響更高於對使用態度之影響。而電子投票系統之安全性
與此性統之適用性同時會影響到使用此系統之態度。 
 因此，依上述分析可得知，電子投票系統的安全性是影響對電子投票機制之
關鍵性因素。若日後要推行電子投票機制，則重要關鍵就是須讓使用者能信任此

經過實驗結果顯示，經過實際操作過電子投票之後，明顯使用者信心是上升，比

 依研究結果，日後若政府將推行電子投票進行選舉，本研究提出幾項建議： 
1. 中央選舉委員會須成為獨立自主一級機構，不附屬於任何機構之下，主要
領導者須不具黨派、政治色彩，以維持此機關之中立性質。 
2. 政府需設立一個安全且固定地方保存投票機制，避免機器軟硬體受毀損與
遭竄改之可能性產生。 
3. 須重新培訓選務人員，依國外經驗顯示，電子投票問題產生與人員素質有
關，故選務人員充分的資訊科技知識與操作能力將影響使用過程之問題發
生與避免。 
4. 依研究結果顯示，政府須有良好電子投票宣導，包括流程與安全保護，讓
人民知道如何操作與使用電子投票機器，才能確保人民對投票結果之信任
度。 
5. 政府須立即修法確保電子投票之合法地位，包括中選會之定位、放置機器
場地、選務人員職責與訓練與其爭議之解決。 
6. 因我國未開始實施電子投票，為讓人民瞭解與提升信心，建議可以從地方
小型選舉開始推動電子投票機制，在延伸到全國性選舉。

1. Introduction  
Since information technology (IT) has been applied to the voting system, our 
understanding of the role of electronic voting systems and its effect on the political 
environment has grown dramatically. Voting technology and ballot design in the 
e-voting system may influence election outcomes, affect how voters feel about their 
ability to exercise their right to vote, and influence voter willingness to accept the 
results of an election as legitimate. (Herrnson, et al, 2005; Alvarez, Hall & Llewellan, 
2006; Atkeson & Saunders, 2007, Hall, 2007; Magleby, Monson & Patterson, 2007) 
Therefore, when redesigning electoral processes in ways that better incorporate 
information technology, trust is one of the most frequently cited reasons for moving to 
electronic voting (Olson & Olson, 2000; Feng, Lazar & Preecee, 2004). Baier 
(1986:247) perceived trust functions as “a code of ethics designed for those equal in 
power and an offensive pretense of equality as a substitute for its actuality”. As the 
report from the Caltech/MIT Voting Technology Project (2001:42) noted, “people do 
not use things in which they have no confidence. Losing confidence in elections 
means losing confidence in our system of government.”  
According to a user-centered orientation, where technology is matched to user 
capabilities, the electronic voting system is designed to enable users to cast their votes 
with confidence and to conduct an efficient and effective election. This research aims 
to explore user perceptions of trust and security in the pilot e-voting system and to 
understand the properties of trust and security on e-voting in an Asian country, 
Taiwan. Asian countries, such as India, Japan and Korea, have begun to experiment 
with an e-voting system that Europe and the United States have utilized for the past 
decade (Trechsel & Mendez, 2005; Alvarez & Hall, 2006; Saltman, 2006). Attitudes 
toward e-voting vary in many ways within nations and across nations (Alvarez & Hall, 
2006), which makes this study of e-voting in Taiwan very relevant for increasing our 
initial understanding of e-voting in an Asian context. Hisamitsu and Takeda (2007) 
also state the security of e-voting systems and their operational procedures are 
important factors to assess the attitude of trustworthiness of e-voting practices in 
Japan. Therefore, the factors of trust and security are essential in discussing user 
attitude toward using the e-voting system.

conceptual framework. According to the Technology Acceptance Model and relative

two important beliefs—perceived ease of use and perceived usefulness—are 
instrumental in explaining the variance in intention (Agarwal & Prasad, 1999; Kramer, 
1999; Li, Valacich & Hess, 2004). ‘Ease of use’ encapsulates the degree to which a 
potential adopter views usage of the target technology to be relatively free of effort; 
‘perceived usefulness’ captures the extent to which a potential adopter views the 
innovation as offering value over alternative ways of performing the same task (Davis, 
Bagozzi & Warshaw, 1989; Karahanna, et al., 1999; Gefen, et al, 2005). In brief, ease 
of use refers to the property of a product or thing that a user can operate without 
having to overcome a steep learning curve. It expresses the relationship between 
experience and efficiency. In contrast, perceived usefulness means that a user believes 
in the existence of a positive use-performance relationship (Davis, 1989). Johnson 
(2005) stated that perceived usefulness influenced a manager’s attitude to invest in 
information security. The importance of perceived ease of use and perceived 
usefulness as determinants of user intention is indicated by their joint effects on 
attitude toward using the system, such as e-commerce, information technology and 
the voting system (Al-Gahtani & King, 1999; Xenakis & Macintosh, 2005). 
Without trust, it is difficult to establish a fair and legitimate electoral voting 
system. Alvarez, Hall and Llewellan (2006) were concerned with the attitudes of 
those who participated in the electoral process in the 2000 and 2004 U.S. Presidential 
elections. If citizens lacked trust in the electoral process that was used to select those 
who filled the offices of those institutions of government, it seems unlikely they will 
then have trust in the performance of those institutions themselves (Parent, 
Vandebeek & Gemino, 2005; Alvarez, Hall & Llewellan, 2006:4). Trust in the 
process is an important concern in designing the e-voting system, which also showed 
in the 2002 and 2003 U.K. e-voting pilots (Xenakis & Macintosh, 2005). That is to 
say, citizen trust in a virtual electoral process becomes an essential factor to influence 
their acceptance and usefulness of electoral transformation and electoral 
consequences, and further influence their intention or attitude to use e-voting system.

幾位台商。會談中瞭解到兩岸間交流是有助於台商在大陸之發展，尤
其廈門，在語言方面更是能與台商相通，因為除國語之外，彼此仍可
以閩南語溝通，讓彼此間溝通更顯容易與親近。蔣主任也提到，對於
台灣居民或商人到廈門觀光或從事商業行為，中心都將給予儘速協助
或拿到延長簽證，由於電子化協助，現今核發更快速。在廈門海關就
設有落地簽證，費用比在台灣簽證更便宜與便利。 
因戴小力教授專長於心理學，而本人亦在研究信心相關問題，與
戴教授有共同類似研究興趣，於是商議日後雙方進行學術合作，包括
共同指導研究生、合寫文章與從事兩岸電子化相關議題比較研究，由
無研發成果推廣資料
