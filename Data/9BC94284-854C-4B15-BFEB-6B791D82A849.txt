摘要 
 
隨著肢體行動不便患者對基本生活品質維護要求的提高，及護理人力之
短缺，所以醫療護理設備的功能與方便性亦應隨之提高，這不但對肢體障礙病患有
幫助，也能保護看護人員避免職業傷害，經過觀察目前醫院，為肢體障礙病人進
行翻身與移床是一項非常必要但又十分辛苦的護理工作。 
因此，為減少護士勞動強度，使病人舒適，並避免病人長期臥床引起併發症與病
人移床時之不便，本計畫提出研製一台『兼具側向翻身及移床功能病床』之實物原型，
設計一種能結合於現有病床之機構，不但可為臥床人提供定時側向翻身，並在需要移
床時以機構搬運方式將臥床人安全平緩地移置於床邊載具，完成之實物原型可以進行
安全測試與實務評估。 
本計畫第一年執行步驟與成果有：(I)概念設計評估，(II)機構運動與功能評估，(II)
零件之應力分析評估；完成之套件參加『參加 2008 高齡產業創意大賽』獲得『第二名』，
並以兼具側向翻身及移床功能之病床機構，於民國 98年 7月 21日獲得我國（第 I 312281
號）及美國發明專利（Patent Number 7562403 B2）。 
關鍵詞：病床，側向翻身，病人移床，防褥瘡床 
 
Abstract 
    Owing to the demanding of basic living quality of immobilized patients, the functions and the 
convenience of medical and nursing facility should be improved accordingly. In observation of 
the present hospital, nursing works such as doing lateral positioning or transferring of 
immobilized patients are very labor intensive. Therefore, the purpose of this proposal was for 
reducing the physical loading of nurse, and preventing from sequential illness, and relieving the 
uncomfortable feeling of prolonged bedridden persons. 
This project was carried out for a patient bed prototype with functions of change lateral 
position and transfer from bed to bed aside carrier for bedridden persons. These functions were 
proposed by a novel mechanism which can be incorporated with present hospital bed. 
   The major works done in the first year are: (I) evaluation of conceptual design, (II) kinematic 
and functional evaluations of proposed mechanism, (III) strength and stress analysis of parts in 
the prototype bed. The finished units won the Second Place Award of 2008 Senior Service 
Business Creativity Competition in Dec. 26, 2008. This concept was approved as invention 
patents in Republic of China （I 312281）and United States of America （Patent Number 7562403 
B2）.
