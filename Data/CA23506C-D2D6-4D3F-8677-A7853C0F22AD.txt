前言 
根據內政部建築研究所自 2007 年起所進行之相關「無線射頻技術（RFID）於建築生命週期之應
用」系列研究，主要著重在「建築生命週期」中，主要運用在「施工」階段中「營建施工品質及進度
管理」、「施工安全管理」及「物料管理」等三部分之系統建立。而在建築後續「使用追蹤」及「查核」
部分，目前仍無相關之研究結果。本研究乃基於台灣建材品質控管環境之特殊性、省思社會對於建材
品質確保的需求並參考震害相關資料檢討建築專業之責任、並配合台灣建材營運與建築生產品質的關
係及進行有關法令與制度之檢討，並以國家標準、建材品質檢驗機制運作、建材檢驗制度與建材管理
之關係等項目檢討所提出之研究課題為基礎，輔以科技之手法及無線射頻（RFID）技術之應用，解構
建材品質管理機制與建材品質之關係，並彙整前期相關研究成果建立我國綠建材品質檢驗機制模型的
建議方案，將我國綠建材應用之檢證及後市場追蹤管理作一整合性之探討以及系統化之建立，以尋求
邁向健全建材品質管理機制之關係解析及作為建置公平、合理之建材品質管理機制的依據，乃為本研
究計畫之目的。 
基於 RFID 未來的發展潛力，本計畫認為將其導入「建材管理」，對於提昇「效率」及後續建材「維
護管理」及「追蹤查核」方面會有相當大的助益，以一般建築而言，建築材料大多是樣式繁雜且數量
繁多，在現場點收、監控皆須耗費人力物力及時間；在擴大到整個「建築生命週期」而言，在其中的
任何一個階段，若可以導入 RFID 之管理機制，將有助於彌補現實狀況的盲點，例如今年建築技術規
則新修訂之新建物需有 30%綠建材之使用量，如何在建築物完成營造階段後進行查核，將是現行審核
使用管理執照上的一大挑戰。然而，國內有關 RFID 技術應用於綠建材與環境控制領域的研究領域，
迄今尚未有相關研究論述之出現。 
綜觀相關參考文獻發現，本計畫在建築產業中為一先導計畫，不僅在「施工階段」應用 RFID 技
術於建築材料上，在「使用階段」更是結合「維護管理」、「追蹤查核」等發展趨勢，有效將建築材料
之使用效率提高，並配合後端「資料庫」管理，積極將「營建自動化」、「管理電子化」、「生活空間智
慧化」等「創新技術」議題，導入建築產業及建材應用，藉以達到「環境永續」、「健康舒適」、「安全
便利」的便利新科技、智慧好生活目標。 
研究目的 
本研究主要是從智慧生活空間居住單元空間之「建材」面向，結合國內「綠建材標章制度」及「RFID
技術」應用，以建築生命週期後段之「使用維護」階段之建材管理為切入點，並嘗試建立一實用之建
材智慧化管理機制探討 ICT 產業應用於建築空間之「整合」之可能性。在研究進程內，將以規劃設計
階段的「現地查核」以及日常使用階段的「追蹤檢測」作為 RFID 的應用情境；以住宅空間、辦公建
築作為實證場域，研製開發「綠建材 RFID 現地檢測管理系統」，並建立現地查核與追蹤檢測及綠建材
後市場管理模態為本計畫創新之重點。

本研究以住宅類建築類型之室內裝修為研究對象，因此裝修材料視為本研究之實驗試體，由於裝
修材料依照使用需求以及設計差異，其材料、顏色、樣式、尺寸、規格…等均不同，致使裝修材料具
有方向性之特點。裝修材料基本規格為塊狀量體，其配置於天花板、牆體或樓地板之設計時，裝修材
料依照此設計變更其厚度或形狀，因此，本研究設定選用塊狀量體作為實驗室體，並且欲透過實驗分
析無線射頻辨識技術標籤設置於實驗試體時，其相對於讀取器之位置，何者具有較佳及較穩定之讀取
能力。由於裝修材料依據樣式及規格之不同，使其具有方位性，若無線射頻辨識技術之標籤黏貼於裝
修材料上，則應找出適當並且於實務層面可運用之黏貼位置，使此黏貼位置具有美觀以及讀取能力佳、
穩定等之特點。 
無線射頻辨識技術訊息之傳遞，是透過電波之原理為媒介，若將標籤運用於金屬類之物品，其會
擴大或縮小此技術之讀取能力，若標籤與水分接觸，則水份會吸收傳遞之電波，因此本研究將不探討
欲使用水泥或黏著劑固定之裝修工程，此外，若排除使用水泥或黏著劑固定裝修材料之裝修工程，則
裝修材料需要使用金屬或非金屬構件，使其固定於設定規劃之位置，因此第一階段實驗欲探討以及分
析本研究選定之無線射頻辨識技術標籤，於無環境影響因子之狀態，其運用於非金屬及金屬類之裝修
材料，測定選定標籤之讀取能力。此外，本研究亦需考量實際層面─商品成本之因素，因此本實驗預

綜合上述，本實驗之主要目的為，欲找出裝修材料於最單純的條件下，亦就是尚未賦予裝修材料
其實際意義，並且於無任何環境影響因子之狀態，測量本研究選用之無線射頻辨識技術標籤黏貼於非
金屬及金屬材料之實驗試體，並且分別測定各面相對於讀取器之位置，其不同距離之讀取能力；換句
話說，本階段實驗之試體，無黏貼於天花板、牆面或樓地板，並且於無任何材質傢俱之實驗場域內，
進行本階段實驗，並探討無線射頻辨識技術標籤黏貼位置相對於讀取器之位置及距離，較佳且穩定之
讀取能力，此為第一階段實驗─不同角度之標籤佈設相對位置對於讀取率之影響。 
第二階段實驗─讀取率與標籤重疊之佈設位置關係，此階段實驗之設計為：當裝修材料須透過構
件固定，若此時黏貼於裝修材料和構件之標籤，位於相同位置並且呈現重疊狀態，探討是否會影響標
籤之讀取能力。第二階段實驗分三個角度，以 X–Y–Z 座標系統而論，實驗探討當標籤重疊於 X–Y
平面、X–Z 平面及 Y–Z 平面之讀取能力，如圖 3-7 X–Y 平面測量圖、圖 3-8 X–Z 平面測量圖及圖
3-9 Y–Z 平面測量圖所示，分析不同方位對於標籤讀取率之影響模式，並提出實驗後之建議。 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
實際裝修環境中，若裝修材料固定於天花板、牆體或樓地板，裝修材料不僅固定於不同面向，亦
會固定於不同高度，則運用此技術進行材料管理時，標籤和讀取器會位於不同水平面上，因此，第三
階段實驗欲探討標籤位於 3-D 空間中之讀取能力。 
將空間概念帶入 X-Y-Z 座標進行探討，則天花板和樓地板於 X-Y 平面；牆體於 X-Z 平面及 Y-Z 平
面。本研究規劃探討裝修 X-Y 平面─樓地板及 X-Z 平面、Y-Z 平面─牆體，標籤黏貼於以固定之裝修
材料，則與讀取器呈不同相對角度，在此狀態下進行讀取能力試驗，分析此技術運用裝修材料管理之
可行性。 
實驗正式進行前，本研究將選用標籤佈設於相對讀取器之不同空間角度以及距離進行讀取能力試
驗，則較佳之讀取範圍於 250 cm 以內，並且考量實驗場域─祐生環控研究中心之高度，將於實驗體內
之 X-Y 平面─樓地板以及 X-Z 平面、Y-Z 平面─牆體，皆鋪設 60 cm × 60 cm 裝修材料，如：圖 3-10

1. INTRODUCTION 
In the 21st century, an age of sustainability, in response to the concept of 
Environmental Efficiency, building construction industry should develop “a 
method of assessment to building environment” in order to meet different 
building phases of the life cycle assisting to effectively cope with the issues of 
global warming and the risks of excessive consumption of ecological resources. 
A well-operated building has not only to maintain a constant “High-Quality” and 
“Low-Loading” status in its features, but to care about the users’ health, so that 
the post-occupied value of buildings can be generated to benefit both 
environment and economy. This study applied the concept of the “Sustainability 
in Building Construction-Framework - Methods of Assessment for Environment 
Performance of Construction Works-part.1: Building” covered in ISO 21931-1 
and the evaluation method of building Life Cycle Cost in ISO 156 86 to deduce 
an assessment model for evaluating the “the Existing Building Performance of 
Taiwan.  
Currently, to enhance the environmental quality and to low the environment 
load so as to extend the life cycle of buildings, Green Building has gradually 
become the most important future construction polity in Taiwan.  Since the 
establishment of the National Council for Sustainable Development Network 
under Executive Yuan in 1996, the Green Building Policy has become the key 
point for Taiwan to fulfill the responsibility of environmental protection in the 
international community as well as to pursue sustainable development. 
Evaluation Manual for Green Building Material was even formulated in 1999 to 
serve as a basis for green building evaluation in Taiwan. On the other hand, in 
order to actively promote green building, the Executive Yuan proposed the 
“Green Building Promotion Program” in 2001, and since 2002, it has been 
required for public buildings to have green building designs, and there have 
been excellent achievements so far.    
Nevertheless, the nine indicators for Taiwan’s green buildings primarily applies 
to newly constructed buildings, while the existing buildings take very high 
percentage (97%) in all buildings (Construction and Planning Agency, Ministry of 
the Interior, 2008). With the increase of the floor area of the buildings, a very high 
percentage of the existing building functions are more likely to be impacted by 
the emission of greenhouse gases and the dramatic climate change, and the 
deterioration of the buildings as well as the environmental quality are 
accelerated so that the environment and landscape are damaged and polluted.

5.1 The Analysis of the Sorting for the Results of Carbon Dioxide 
Reduction Calculation 
As to the improvement item for sound environment, the reduction appeared in a 
negative relation, meaning that there is no power consumption at the users’ end. 
However, as for the improvement item, the production of carbon emission mainly 
took place at the stage of material production and transportation. As sound 
absorption design was lower in the life-cycle cost at the stage of production and 
transportation stage as well as in carbon reduction, it is better for sound 
insulation design than in environmental reductions. 
As to the improvement item for light environment, carbon dioxide reductions 
for circuit design showed better performances, followed by the use of high 
efficiency lighting equipment, intelligent light control system and daylight use 
design, which revealed that if the control design of the lighting circuit could be 
incorporated with the space, more carbon dioxide could be reduced relatively. 
As to the improvement item for thermal environment, carbon dioxide 
reductions for automatic air-conditioning control system showed better 
performances, followed by the use of high efficiency air-conditioning equipment, 
temperature and humidity control, shading design and insulation design. Among 
them, as automatic air-conditioning control system could achieve the results of 
saving energy in operation due to automatic control variable frequency, it had 
better results of carbon reduction. Although at the improvement stage, insulation 
design was low in power consumption, since it failed to achieve complete 
insulation, the other air-conditioning equipment had to be used to reduce indoor 
temperature, and it failed to achieve the results of carbon reduction relatively. 
As to the improvement item for air environment, carbon dioxide reductions for 
natural ventilation showed better performances, followed by mechanical 
ventilation and the use of low-emitting decoration material. As natural ventilation 
used natural design, it had better results of energy saving and carbon reduction. 
However, the reduction for the use of low-emitting decoration material showed 
negative values, revealing the fact that there is no power consumption for user of 
the improvement item, but in terms of health, the use of low-emitting decoration 
material indoors achieved better health quality.
