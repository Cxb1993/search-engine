A strain-balanced multiple-quantum-well solar cell (MQWSC) of 
GaAs0.94P0.06/In0.2Ga0.8As is designed and analyzed.  Twenty quantum wells are 
inserted in the intrinsic region of the MQWSC with a well width of 80Å and a barrier 
width of 230 Å.  The performance of the multiple-quantum-well solar cell and a 
typical GaAs solar cell with a p-i-n structure is evaluated.  The intrinsic regions of 
both cells are 1.068µm.  The quantum efficiencies of the typical p-n junction GaAs 
solar cell and the GaAs0.94P0.06/In0.2Ga0.8As strain-balanced multiple-quantum-well 
solar cell are given in Figure 2.
