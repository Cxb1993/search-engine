前言與研究目的 
近年來由於半導體科技的高度發展，帶動了照明科技的長足進步，除了節能之外，
又有無汞的安全特點，非常符合現今注重環保的趨勢。然而，無機半導體製程設備昂貴，
生產耗時，導致成本較高，阻礙了固態照明的普及。溶液製程製作有機發光二極體簡單
迅速，材料利用率高，且容易製作大面積面板，在商業化量產的競爭力較高，相較真空
熱蒸鍍技術，在商業化量產的競爭力較高，因此有較大的發展潛力。為解決溶液製程製
作有機發光二極體可能會發生層與層之間互溶的問題，1995 年 Pei 等人提出的有機發光
電化學元件概念[1]。有機發光電化學元件在偏壓下可以形成導電性摻雜(conductive 
doping)，降低了載子由電極注入有機發光層的位障，大幅降低元件操作電壓，因此有
機發光電化學元件僅需單層結構即可具有一般多層有機發光二極體(含載子注入與傳輸
層)的功能，可以方便地以溶液製程製作，既降低成本也容易製作在可撓性基板上。其
低操作電壓與高量子效率，也就是高功率效率的元件特性在照明的應用上也極有潛力，
故本計畫研究的主題將以提升有機發光電化學元件效率的相關議題，如：載子平衡、主
客體系統和新型白光元件結構為研究重點。 
 
文獻探討 
    自 1995 年 Pei 等人提出有機發光電化學元件概念[1]之後，先後有許多研究團隊針
對此一主題進行研究工作，以材料系統來區分，可以簡單分為螢光導電高分子材料[2]
與磷光離子性有機過渡金屬錯合物[3-5]這二種。其中磷光離子性有機過渡金屬錯合物具
有較高的電致發光效率，各種光色的效率紀錄皆是由此種材料所製作的元件達到，因
此，我們也選用離子性有機過渡金屬錯合物來製作有機發光電化學元件，以達到較佳的
元件效率。發光電化學元件因為結構僅有一層，當 p 與 n 型摻雜區形成後，載子注入的
平衡有大幅改善，此時複合區在發光層內的位置決定於發光材料的電子與電洞遷移率的
相對大小，若是電子與電洞遷移率接近，就能使複合區靠近發光層中央，遠離電極以避
免激子猝熄，提升元件效率。然而，大部分的材料之電子與電洞遷移率並不接近，複合
區常會靠近電極，所以元件效率常常無法達到材料的理論極限。目前大部分相關文獻都
著重於發表新的材料與其相關的物理與光電特性，深入探討元件內部複合區位置與相關
的載子平衡仍然相當缺乏，由其是較為複雜的主客體系統，更是少見新穎的研究成果，
基於此研究主題的重要性，我們將針對主客體元件的載子平衡進行深入的研究，以提升

已發表於[Journal of Materials Chemistry, 21, 17855-17862, 2011. (SCI, IF 2011: 5.968,

B. Highly Efficient Double-Doped Solid-State White Light-Emitting Electrochemical Cells 
 
Chih-Teng Liao, Yu-Chun Shen, Hsiao-Fan Chen, Hai-Ching Su and Ken-Tsung Wong 
 
White organic light-emitting diodes (OLEDs) based on polymers and small-molecule materials have 
attracted intense attention due to their potential applications in flat-panel displays and solid-state lighting. 
Compared with conventional white OLEDs, solid-state white light-emitting electrochemical cells (LECs) 
possess several promising advantages. LECs generally require only a single emissive layer, which can be 
easily processed from solutions, and can conveniently use air-stable electrodes. The emissive layer of 
LECs contains mobile ions, which can drift toward electrodes under an applied bias. The spatially 
separated ions induce electrochemical doping (oxidation and reduction) of the emissive materials near the 
electrodes, i.e. p-type doping near the anode and n-type doping near the cathode. The doped regions 
induce ohmic contacts with the electrodes and consequently facilitate the injection of both holes and 
electrons, which recombine at the junction between p- and n-type regions. As a result, a single-layered 
LEC device can be operated at very low voltages (close to Eg/e, where Eg is the energy gap of the 
emissive material and e is elementary charge) with balanced carrier injection, giving high power 
efficiencies. Furthermore, air-stable metals, e.g. Au and Ag, can be used since carrier injection in LECs is 
relatively insensitive to work functions of electrodes. 
In this work, we report highly efficient solid-state white LECs based on a double-doped strategy, 
which judiciously introduces an orange-emitting guest, [Ir(ppy)2(dasb)]+(PF6─) (where ppy is 
2-phenylpyridine and dasb is 4,5-diaza-9,9’-spirobifluorene) into a single-doped emissive layer 
comprising of an efficient blue-green emitting host, [Ir(dfppz)2(dtb-bpy)]+(PF6─) (where dfppz is 
1-(2,4-difluorophenyl)pyrazole and dtb-bpy is [4,4′-di(tert-butyl)-2,2′-bipyridine]) and a red-emitting 
guest, [Ir(ppy)2(biq)]+(PF6─) (where biq is 2,2′-biquinoline) to improve carrier balance and thus to 
enhance the device efficiency. Photoluminescence (PL) measurements show that the single-doped (red 
guest) and the double-doped (red and orange guests) host-guest films exhibit similar white PL spectra and 
comparable photoluminescence quantum yields while device efficiencies of the double-doped white 
LECs are twofold higher than those of the single-doped white LECs. Therefore, such enhancement of 
device efficiency is rationally attributed to improved carrier balance of the double-doped emissive layer. 
Peak external quantum efficiency and peak power efficiency of the double-doped white LECs reach 7.4 
% and 15 lm/W, respectively. These efficiencies are among the highest reported for solid-state white 
LECs and thus confirm that the double-doping strategy is useful for achieving highly efficient white 
LECs.

本計畫研究成果主要有四項，分項自評如下： 
 
(一) 紅外光電化學元件 
以紅外光離子性銥錯合物製作紅外光電化學元件的文獻不多，元件效率也很低。為了提升
元件效率，本研究以高效率之商用紅外光雷射染料為客體，搭配適當的離子性銥錯合物為
主體，製作紅外光電化學元件，外部量子效率分別為 0.8 與 1.24%，這樣的效率在紅外光
電化學元件之中是非常高的，目前尚未有離子性銥錯合物紅外光電化學元件能達到相近的
效率。[Physical Chemistry Chemical Physics, 13, 17729-17736, 2011. (SCI, IF 2011: 
3.573, Ranking: 5/33, Times cited: 5)]
