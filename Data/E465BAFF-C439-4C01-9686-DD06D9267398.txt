一、中文摘要 
由於現今電腦使用的普遍性，及工作壓力大，「肌筋膜炎」為靜態上班族常見的一種
職業病。有鑑於背部肌筋膜炎患者日益增加，但對於「肌筋膜炎」對肌肉收縮功能所造成
的影響並未見深入的探討。有鑑於此，本研究招募 62 位受測者，包含 32 位上背肌筋膜炎
患者與 30 位正常受測者，並量測其上背部菱形肌的表面肌電訊號，藉以比較肌肉收縮功能
的族群差異性。結果發現病患組的動作電位之中值頻率比控制組來得低但施力強度卻比較
強，我們因而推論患有肌筋膜炎的肌肉中心頻率較低，表示處於較疲乏的狀態，因此在動
作時須施更大的力，來完成測試要求。此結果將有助於後續臨床研究探討「肌筋膜炎」的
成因。 
關鍵詞：表面肌電訊號、肌筋膜炎 
 
 
Abstract 
Nowadays due to the popularity of using computer and increasing working stress, 
“myofascial pain” (MP) has been a common occupational hazard. Although the prevalence of MP 
is increasing abruptly, the investigation into any induced muscular functional changes is rare. In 
this study, we recruit 62 participants including 32 MP patients and 30 normal subjects to measure 
their surface electromyography (SEMG) on both rhomboid muscles such that the functional 
differences between these two groups can be further analyzed. The results show that lower 
contraction frequency and higher contraction strength is associated with MP subjects, indicating 
that the muscles with MP syndromes tend to be fatigued and therefore need more strength to 
make contractions. We believe that the results can help further clinical studies to discover the 
etiology of myofascial pain. 
 
Keywords: surface electromyography signal, myofascial pain
