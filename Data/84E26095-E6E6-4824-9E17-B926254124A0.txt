四、結論 
本研究成功地藉由蒸鍍 Parylene-C 的方式
將以 ACCVD 方式成長於矽基板上的奈米
碳網抓住，再利用 Polyimide tape 將奈米
碳網轉印至 PEN 可撓性基板，此製程方式
簡單、快速且 IC 製程相容性高；並利用此
轉印方法設計並製作用於奈米碳網壓阻係
數測定之可撓式奈米碳網元件，並藉由拉
伸實驗了解奈米碳網受到應變下 (Max. 
Strain: 0.3%)的阻值變化並計算其壓阻係
數。在研究中也發現奈米碳網的疏密除了
造成元件阻值的差異，也會對壓阻係數大
小有所影響，未來將針對影響奈米碳網疏
密的製程參數做研究，藉由調控奈米碳網
成長參數用來成長不同疏密程度的奈米碳
網，並了解其壓阻係數之變化關係。 
SWCNNs/Parylene-C thin film 具有透光，
flexible、conductive 的特性，除了可作為
strain sensor 開發外，配合 PEN 基板也可
用以發展 flexible FET,TFT 等撓性電子元
件。使用 Parylene-C 做為碳網間的附著
層，讓奈米碳網可成功從矽基板轉印至可
撓式基板，這對於 Parylene-based 的微機
電元件與奈米碳網間的整合上提供了一個
直接轉印且製程快速的應用方向，有利於
奈米碳網應用於 biomechanical sensing 方
面的植入式撓性感測器開發。

ABSTRACT  
Many biomechanical engineering applications require new sensors for strain 
measurement. Carbon nanotubes (CNTs)/Polymer thin films have been used in some of these 
applications for their higher sensitivity and relatively small size than current metallic foil 
type strain gauges. However, previous process for fabricating CNTs/Polymer strain sensor 
is proven time consuming and is not compatible with standard IC process. In this paper, a 
rapid production and IC compatible fabrication process based on transfer printing 
technique is presented to demonstrate a flexible single-walled carbon 
nanonets(SWCNNs)/Parylene-C thin film composite structure can be used for strain sensing 
application. This prototype has been characterized with a commercial strain gauge using 
tensile test. The average gauge factor of the proposed sensor on the flexible PEN substrate 
was 1.61 with standard deviation of 0.15. The method to increase the sensor’s gauge factor 
will be also discussed.  
1  INTRODUCTION  
 
Strain sensing is important in many biomechanical applications, such as studying the onset 
of osteoporosis, tumors in bone [1] or the real time strain measurement on the bone surface 
[2]. If traditional type of metallic foil gauge is to be used in bio-type application, it is limited 
due to the low-sensitivity (gauge factor (GF):2.1) and relatively large size.  When the 
silicon-based strain gauge is to be used due to its higher gauge factor and smaller size, the 
cost is much higher and it is also suffered from its bio-compatibility issue.  More importantly, 
its flexible characteristics limits the long term implant capability while using it as the 
wearable bio-mechanical strain monitoring. Therefore, researchers are looking for new 
material and sensors that can be used for bio-mechanical strain sensing.   
For instances, the single or bundles of carbon nanotubes (CNTs) [3] are widely studied for 
their electrochemical, electromechanical, mechanical, electrical, thermal, and flexible 
properties.  They can also be applied to strain sensing in corrosive environment [4,5]. Piezo-
resistive properties of single or bundles of CNTs are also studied theoretically and

B. Characterization of SWCNNs Thin Film  
Characterization of the SWCNNs thin film is done by Raman spectrum analysis as shown in 
Fig. 5 using laser light source of 633nm wavelength with 17mW power.  Raman shifts 
appear in both low (150~300cm-1) and high (1550~1600cm-1) frequency regions. The low 
frequency mode is radial breathing mode (RBM), and the appearance of the RBM indicates 
that the CNN is single walled. The diameter of the nanotube can be estimated from the 
empirical equation d=248/ .  The peak of the RBM of this report is 195　 cm-1, and thus the 
diameter of nanotube is 1.27nm. The high frequency region is called the G-band (1590cm-1), 
and the relative intensity of the G-Band (IG) represents the degree of graphitic SWCNNs 
structure.   The intensity of D-band, ID (1350cm-1) on the left of the G-Band represents the 
defect of the SWCNNs thin film. In addition, the ratio, ID/IG can be used to determine the 
graphitization of SWCNNs. In this study, the SWCNNs thin film was grown by a high 
temperature ACCVD process, and the SWCNNs are well graphitized, and the ratio ID/IG is 
0.1. 
C. Tensile test results 
The acrylic specimen is tested by the Instron 1130, and it is illustrated by Fig. 6(a).  An 
enlarged inset photo shows the SWCNNs device is mounted on the acrylic specimen and a 
commercial metallic foil gauge (KYOWA) is also mounted on the backside of the specimen 
for stain measurement, Fig. 6(b).  The testing result is collected from the DPM-711B for 
strain value and Keithley 2701 for resistance change information of the proposed sensor, 
respectively.  The normalized resistance change of the SWCNNs device versus strain value 
are plotted in Fig. 6(c), and the gauge factor G, is then calculated based on the slope of the 
curves with the following equation:  
 
ΔR/R = G × Δ  (1) 
 
where ΔR/R is the normalized change in resistance, and Δ is the strain experienced by the 
strain gauge. The average gauge factor of the SWCNNs/Parylene-C sensor with a flexible 
PEN substrate is 1.61 with standard deviation of 0.15. 
 
4 CONCLUSION 
In this paper, a novel SWCNNs/Parylene-C thin film has been fabricated using a rapid and 
IC compatible method. The piezoresistive effect and the gauge factor of the 
SWCNNs/Parylene-C thin films on the PEN substrate under straining have also been 
measured and calculated. It has been shown that the SWCNNs/Parylene-C thin film can be 
easily peeled off from silicon substrate, and it can be used for flexible device fabrication 
without etching away the sacrifice layer.

本計畫原訂以兩年時間對單壁奈米碳網材料(SWCNN)，探討其壓組特性與其他場效應及應
用性質加以評估。審查委員僅建議執行一年，因此本研究對合成 SWCNN 之方法及後續應用
之製程與應用，以壓阻特性來評估。吾人製作與金屬應變規匹配之試片作一比較，隨著不
同 SWCNN 膜厚與疏密程度，對阻規係數有一定之影響。實驗結果顯示，較稀疏之奈米碳網
結構以較細之線寬(<30um)可獲得 Gage Factor>8 之成果，超越金屬應變規達 300%，未來
之應用可配合 Paralene 夾層開發生醫應用之感測元件。
