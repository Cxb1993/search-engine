<英文摘要及關鍵詞> 
Keywords: Yb:fiber, fiber laser, laser display 
 
Energy saving and environment protection has attracted a lot of attention worldwide. Many display 
factories and companies are looking for new light source instead of cold cathode fluorescent lamp (CCFL). 
Laser is a monochromatic light source that RGB (red, green and blue) lasers provide wider color gamut 
than CCFL and light-emitted diode (LED) in display. Besides, the high output power, high brightness, and 
good stability are also the advantages of lasers. Using laser in display saves the color filter cost and 
reduces 30~40% light loss. Using laser as the light source not only increases the display quality but also 
matches the goal of energy saving and environment protection. However, the wavelength of InGaN 
semiconductor blue laser is 400~420 nm, which is too short for display. The ZnO semiconductor blue 
laser suffers from the low output power and short lifetime. Therefore, the blue laser is nowadays the 
bottleneck of laser display development.  
 
Yb:fiber laser has been developed very well these years. It is compact, light, high efficiency and 
good for heat dissipation. Even with high power output, it keeps the beam quality M2 close to 1. The 
line-width can be smaller than 0.1 nm, which is very suitable for blue laser generation by using frequency 
doubling technique. Although the emission spectrum of Yb:fiber covers from 900 nm to 1200 nm, but the 
shortest laser wavelength is 974 nm as we know. While the wavelength is shorter than 976 nm, the 
emission cross section becomes smaller than the absorption cross section, and is closer to 
quasi-three-level laser scheme which causes serious re-absorption loss. This is why it is difficult to 
generate short wavelength Yb:fiber laser.  
 
Yb:fiber lasers have shown excellent performance in 980-1100 nm wavelength range. Below 980 nm, 
the small ratio between emission and absorption cross sections hindered its development. To find out a 
feasible solution for short-wavelength Yb:fiber laser, the temperature-dependent emission and absorption 
cross sections, fiber length, pumping method, and suppression of high-gain wavelengths are analyzed and 
discussed. It is interesting to find that elevated temperature of the gain fiber is beneficial for 
short-wavelength Yb:fiber laser generation which is opposite to general perception of quasi-three-level 
lasers. In this study, a 960-nm Yb:fiber laser with 20.1% slope efficiency is successfully developed at 101 
oC which is the shortest wavelength of Yb:fiber laser to our knowledge. And this 960-nm Yb:fiber laser 
can be applied in generating blue light for large screen laser display.

<結果與討論> 
成功產生 960 nm Yb:fiber laser，突破研究文獻 974 nm 的短波長限制。 
欲產生 960 nm Yb:fiber 雷射，恰當的光纖長度也是很重要的；我們模擬並量測 Yb:fiber (Nufern 
PM-YSF-HI)在不同長度下的 ASE 頻譜如圖二所示，當 Yb:fiber 長度短於 70 cm 時，輸出頻譜峰值落
在 emission cross section 最高的 976 nm，在長度約 45 cm 時功率達到最高，而後因光纖內的重複吸收
損耗隨光纖長度縮短而功率下降，當光纖長度大於 70 cm 時，頻譜變為由 1030 nm 主導。在雷射系統
中光纖光柵(FBG)僅能在 Yb:fiber 的兩端對 960 nm 以外的波長產生抑制，對光纖內部波長的功率成長
是難以控制的，所以要產生 960 nm雷射就必須和 976 nm 競爭增益，使用的光纖長度必須使 976 nm 的
single-pass gain 低於 FBG 的 suppression。由圖二可看出 976 nm 的光在光纖長度 20 cm 以下或 70 cm
以上功率都不高，表示 gain 很弱；但在長度 70 cm 以上時，1030 nm 才是主要的輸出頻譜，這表示大
量 2N 被 1030 nm 消耗，會使得 960 nm 無法 lasing。故欲產生 960 nm 雷射，Yb:fiber 長度應短於 20 cm。
