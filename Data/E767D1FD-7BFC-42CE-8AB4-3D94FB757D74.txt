Abstract
For the traditional electrode patterning by the photolithography technique, the
manufacturing instruments are expensive and the fabrication processes are complicated. This
project primarily develops the beam shaping technique to perform drilling, scribing and laser
electrode patterning on the glass substrate and the indium tin oxide (ITO) thin film of the plastic
substrate by using Nd:YAG UV laser processing system. The processing parameters and
properties for ITO will be measured by the optical microscope, the scanning electron
microscope, the α-step and the four probe setup. Finally, this project will analyze the laser
processing parameters and the fabrication improvement with Taguchi method. Implement period
of this project is three years, and the relative content includes:
The first year: utilizing the third harmonic Nd:YAG laser machine performs the beam
shaping technique. This project actually carries out drilling and scribing on the ITO of the glass
substrate. And then the experimental results for the beam shaping will be measured and
analyzed by the instruments.
The second year: the laser processing theory of the ITO on the glass and plastic substrate
will be constructed. The building model and the heat field analysis will be established with
ANSYS. At the same time, this project will analyze the processing parameters of the beams
shaped that perform the laser direct electrode patterning on the ITO of the glass and the plastic
substrate.
The third year: in order to obtain the optimal processing parameters and improve the
fabrication for the laser electrode patterning, this project utilizes the Taguchi method to examine
the patterning results of the ITO. In addition, all kinds of processing parameters and properties
of the ITO will be measured by the optical microscope, the scanning electron microscope, the
α-step and the four probe setup.
This project utilizes the third harmonic Nd:YAG laser processing system principally to
perform the laser electrode patterning technology for the ITO. Consequently, the fabrication can
be simplified and the utilization of the chemical dissolvent can be reduced. Besides, the laser
processing technology will be promoted greatly. This project could also transfer the relative
processing technique to others to enhance the international competition ability.
Keywords: Third harmonic Nd:YAG UV laser processing system, beam shaping, glass
substrate, plastic substrate, ITO, Laser electrode patterning, Taguchi method.

(2)玻璃基板電極成型
在 TFT LCD 電極成型製程中，於玻璃與塑膠基板表面上利用薄膜沈積與蝕刻技術製作

傳統電極圖案化製程技術於玻璃基板或塑膠基板上製作高解析度電極圖案需經過五至
六道製程步驟，且為濕式化學蝕刻製程。對於新興可撓式塑膠基板而言，其吸水性高、耐

可節省添購半導體製程設備所需要的昂貴經費，且可減少製作電極之步驟所花費的步驟、
時間與人力，同時亦可減少污染性化學物質的使用，朝向綠色加工的領域邁進。目前國內
研發機構與廠商所使用的雷射加工系統近幾乎仰賴國外進口。本計畫乃利用三倍頻Nd:YAG
UV 雷射加工系統，針對不同材質之透明導電薄膜於玻璃或塑膠基板上以雷射直接進行電極
成型之相關製程參數研究。期望未來能夠提供業界相關之技術協助，以提升國家整體競爭
能力。

四、研究方法
本計畫以光機電應用實驗室發展之三倍頻 Nd:YAG UV 雷射加工系統為架構，結合
雷射光束整型技術將雷射光束自高斯分佈修整為均勻光束，之後於玻璃基板與塑膠基板
上之透明導電薄膜，藉此完成 ITO 電極成型加工。本年度計畫係針對三倍頻 Nd:YAG UV
雷射光束進行整型研究，內容包括：(a)利用均質化光學元件進行光束整型。(b)發展光束
整型光路系統進行光束修整。(c)比較光束整型前後玻璃基板鑽孔與劃線結果。(d)玻璃基
板加工後孔形及劃線寬度品質量測。
雷射光束品質在雷射技術運用領域中是一項相當重要且關鍵的因子。包括：雷射材
料加工、光訊號處理、儲存與記錄、雷射醫學臨床應用等領域。上述之加工方法對於雷
射光束品質有較高的要求。在非線性光學頻率變換技術中，要求雷射光束強度均勻，在
高功率固態雷射中，若輸入的光束不均勻將導致非線性效應的產生，使得雷射光束品質
變差甚至損壞欲進行雷射加工之物件。因此，雷射光束品質不僅影響整體雷射效能，也

雷射光束品質在雷射技術運用領域中是一項相當重要且關鍵的因素。尤其在雷射材料
加工應用上。本研究主要利用雷射光束調變系統進行光束整型，其元件係由聚焦透鏡、散
焦透鏡、均質系統 (homogenizer)、與反射鏡所組成。功用為將雷射光束從高斯分佈形態調
變成均勻平頂光束分佈形態，同時將雷射光束放大至所需要的大小。圖 20 所示，為均質化
(homogenizer)光學元件，架設於雷射光路系統中。一般而言，雷射光束輪廓主要由雷射功
率密度分佈構成，因此產生比較均勻之雷射光束將有助於雷射加工品質，此雷射光束必須
藉由雷射束輪廓儀進行量測。

A few applications of laser materials processing have widely ablated the material of the workpiece,
include the dicing, the cutting, the drilling, the marking, the scribing and the lift-off technique. Two type
of laser, include the solid state lasers and the gas lasers, are usually adopted using in the material
processing system. The solid state laser are usually used the Nd:YAG crystal to emit the 1064 nm of
wavelength, and it is so-called fundamental laser wavelength of the Nd:YAG laser. In order to generating
other wavelength of the solid state lasers, the nonlinear optical crystals that include the KH2PO4 (KDP),
the KD2PO4 (KD*P), the KTiOPO4 (KTP), theβ-BaB2O4 (BBO), LiB3O5 (LBO), the CsB3O5 (CBO), and
others usually adjusted the laser wavelength [1-3]. In this paper, the third-harmonic Nd:YAG laser that has
been decreased the laser wavelength using two LBO crystals could generate the 355nm of wavelength.
Figure 1 shows a schematic diagram of the laser types versus the laser wavelength. In the laser materials
processing technology, the absorption of materials is a more important parameter in this procedure. Figure
2 indicates the relationship between the absorption of materials and laser wavelength, and it also shows
the optimal absorption of insulating materials is in the region of ultraviolet (UV) and the far infrared (FIR).
Although the insulating materials can be ablated using UV lasers and FIR lasers, the laser processing
usually use the UV laser to ablate the insulating materials due to its small spot size and small heat affect
zone (HAZ) [4-5]. Therefore, the UV lasers are widely adopted in the micromachining systems, and it is
used in ablating ITO films in this paper.
The formation of circuit pattern on the ITO films can be using the laser materials processing
techniques. O. Yavas et al. discussed the absorption rate of ITO films that used the Q-switch Nd:YLF laser
and flash lamp-pumped Nd:YAG laser to ablate ITO materials[4-5]. James G. Lunney et al. used the KrF
Excimer laser with a 248nm of wavelength to etch the transparent conductive thin films [6]. J.G. Liu and
C.H. Chen et al. used the CO2 laser ablating the silver on the epoxy resin substrate [7]. Dave Farson and
H.W. Choi et al. proposed the ablation of ITO films using the Ti:Sapphire laser with the 755nm of
wavelength, the 2kHz of repetition rate and the 150fs of pulsewidth [8]. H.J. Booth proposed the ITO
glass patterning on large plasma display panel (PDP) using the Bow tie scanning (BTS) technique [9]. The
research developed a third harmonic Nd: YAG UV laser processing system and ablated the ITO thin film
to form a circuit pattern on the glass substrates. The results of circuit patterns of the glass substrate are
measured using the OM and SEM. Therefore, the main parameters of laser direct writing processing
would be presented in this paper.

4. Results and discussion
The experimental specimen is the ITO glass which is produced by Merck Display Technologies Ltd.
in Taiwan. The ITO film is the 100nm of thickness, the 84% of transmittance, and the 20Ω/□of resistance.
The ITO glass has been washed using the ultrasonic cleaner and is dried the specimen using high-pressure
nitrogen before the laser patterning processing. The results of laser patterning on ITO glass are measured
using OM and SEM.
Figure 9 shows the result of the UV laser ablate the ITO film of glass substrate which was measured
by SEM. The width of cutting line is about 20 microns. The repetition rate of laser is 5 kHz and the
feeding speed of XY table is 10 mm/sec. In order to obtain the main parameter of laser patterning
processing, the feeding speed of XY table and the repetition rate have been changed in the experiment.
Figures 10 and 11 show the results of width of cutting line which used UV laser ablating ITO films with
different feeding speed and repetition rate. The results indicate the width of cutting line is decreased by
increasing the repetition rate of laser and feeding speed of XY table. In the Fig. 11, the width of cutting
line is increased by the repetition rate of laser, because the energy of laser has also increased. Therefore,
the influencing parameter of laser patterning is the laser energy. In addition, the width of cutting line
can be also increased by reducing feeding speed of XY table, because the overlapping rate can
increase the energy of unit area.
5. Conclusions
In this research, we developed a third-harmonic Nd:YAG UV laser processing system and provided a
method to ablate ITO films of glass substrate. The semiconductor manufacturing technique has usually
formed the circuit and widely generates electrode patterns on the glass substrate. In the recent year, the
laser direct writing techniques have used to etch the photoresist and make the circuit of devices. Therefore,
the investigation present a laser direct writing method can form the circuit of ITO glass and discuss the
influencing parameter of laser ablating ITO films of glass substrate.
In the experiment, the ITO films of glass substrate is ablated using the UV laser which used the
different repetition rate of laser and feeding speed of XY table. The results shows the repletion rate and
feeding speed would be affected the width of cutting line. In addition, the overlapping rate would affect
the quality of edge.

5. Conclusions
In this research, we presents a unique micromachining procedure which includes concentric circles method and trimming
circumference method, for this aluminum alloy and establish the temperature filed model with Finite Element Method (FEM).
At the same time, experimental results reveal the relationship of the laser average power, the spot size of beams, and the pulse
repletion rate from 1 w to 2.5 w, 70 µm to 100 µm, 2 kHz to 7 kHz, respectively.
In the results of simulation, the temperature filed was able to ablate materials with vaporization. However, with the
increasing of time, the depth and size of laser drilled hole gradually reduce due to the rapid increase of ionization. If we adopt
trimming circumference method, the superior quality of microvias will be improve clearly.
At the same time, the investigation shows that a few residue materials on the surface of the microvias occur and the
fabrication of taper microvias have been carried out. Consequently, the increasing laser fluence can rapidly accomplish the
fabrication of taper microvias with 300μm thick in aluminum alloys.
