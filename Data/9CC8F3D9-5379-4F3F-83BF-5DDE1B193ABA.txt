Abstract 
The continuous improving the solar cell devices performances are needed for low surface 
recombination, high shunt resistance, and low series resistance.  To achieve this goal, the high quality 
silver/aluminum paste was investigated to lower series resistance between AgAl and the p-type Si(100) 
interfaces. By modulated the various compositions of the silver and aluminum paste and post annealing 
conditions, the high quality silver/aluminum paste can be achieved for single-crystalline silicon solar cell 
(SSC) applications.  According to the electrical measurement and material analysis, the effects of the 
silver/aluminum electrode combined with various compositions of the silver and aluminum paste on the 
characterizations of the SSC devices were addressed.  The results show that (1) The resistivity of this new 
silver electrode with 5-7 mΩ/sq@10µm was achieved. (2) The silver/aluminum paste can run through the 
silicon oxide, (3) The viscosity of this new silver/aluminum electrode can be around 60-90 Pa.S.  (4) The 
optimum composition and post annealing conditions for the silver/aluminum paste were obtained.  
 
 
Keywords：Solar cell, silver/aluminum paste, post-anneling, conversion efficiency

鄭錦隆
技術說明
(中文) 本銀鋁漿應用於單(多)晶太陽能電池的背電極，其特性包含具備良好歐姆接觸、
高漿料覆蓋性、與焊錫具良好附著性及具長焊接黏著老化，因為這層銀鋁電極是
用來做為太陽能元件串聯的主要橋樑，一般單(多)晶太陽能的開路電壓為0.6伏
特左右，因此若要使用大電壓一定要將太陽能晶片串接，所研發的銀鋁電極之電
阻率(resistivity)為5-7 mΩ/sq@10μm；黏著(viscosity)為60-90 Pa.S；與商
業的其它銀鋁膠所使用的太陽能電池效率高出1-2%；低價的銀鋁電極材料，可應
用在太陽能電池元件之製作，促進太陽能電池元件之進一步發展。
(英文) The developed aluminum-silver paste was used on the backside electrode of the high-
efficiency single-(multi-)crystalline silicon solar cells. The characteristics of aluminum-
silver electrode include good ohmic contact, high paste coverage, good soderability or 
weldability, as well as high sodered aged adhesion. The aluminum-silver paste electrode 
was used for the connection between wafers and wafers. Therefore, the resistivity of the 
silver/aluminum electrode can be achieved around 5-7 mΩ/sq@10μm. The viscosity of 
the silver/aluminum electrode can be around 60-90 Pa.S. The conversion efficiencies of 
the solar cells with this silver/aluminum electrode are larger 1-2% than that the solar cell 
with the old silver/aluminum electrode ones. The efficiency of the solar cells can be 
improved by this silver/aluminum electrode.
