Assessment of environmental conditions of Bei-Gang River in Taiwan 
Tung-Jer Hu1, Hsiao-Wen Wang2 
1. Associate Professor, Dept. of Civil Engineering, Lanyang Institute of Technology 
2. Ph.D., Dept. of Civil Engineering, National Taiwan University 
 
 
Bei-Gang River (Figure 1) is one of the tributaries of Wu River in central Taiwan. Along Bei-Gang River, a 
pool-and-weir type fish passage at the Da-Chi Dam (Figure 2), established in 1997, is now destroyed due to 
big flood. In this study, by using one-dimensional hydraulic model, water surface profile was calculated 
(Figure 3).
