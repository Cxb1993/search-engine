邊界條件對含根土壤剪力強度評估影響之研究 
The Study on the Effect of the Boundary Conditions on the Shear Strength 
Prediction of the Soil-Root System 
計畫編號：NSC 96－2211－E－163 －002 － 
     執行期間： 96 年 8 月 1 日至 97 年 7 月 31 日  
計畫主持人：游新旺  私立中國科技大學土木工程系副教授  
共同主持人：陳主惠  私立中國科技大學土木工程系教授  
 
一、中文摘要 
計畫中將取不同植根土壤試體之個別
根，經由直接量測及拉力試驗尋求根之力
學性質；另藉由改良式之植生土壤直剪試
驗，使能在土壤受剪過程中觀察根在土壤
內之移動情形，以了解剪力帶厚度變化。
本研究依據根之彈性變形、斷裂現象及根-
土間握裹滑移等力學行為，建立含根土壤
之根力模式。計劃中將就文獻已有實例探
討松樹含根土壤在受剪過程中，因根系錨
錠狀況改變及模擬剪力帶厚度與根之側移
關係分別為常數、線性及二次曲線，探討
松樹植根土壤對土壤剪力強度增量之影
響。 
關鍵詞：根力模式，土-根系統、剪力帶  
 
Abstract 
The mechanical properties of roots, which 
come from different rooted-soil specimen, 
would be found through direct measurement 
and tensile strength test for individual root. 
In order to understand the variation of shear 
zone depth, the modified direct shear test 
equipment was made to observe the 
interaction behavior of soil-root system. An 
analytical model responds to the loading of 
root stretching, root breaking, and soil-root 
slipping was established in this study. The 
mechanic analysis models with experiment 
results were applied to the soil columns of 
pine in literature, the increment of the soil 
shearing strength would be discussed by 
changing the anchor system and the shear 
zone width versus lateral displacement, 
which were simulated in accordance with the 
different approaches in constant, linear, and 
quadratic curve. 
Key words：root strength model, soil-root 
system, shear zone
