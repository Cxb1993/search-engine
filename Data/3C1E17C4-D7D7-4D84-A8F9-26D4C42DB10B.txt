摘要 
本計畫旨在針對三維流道中載裝有間
隔凸塊熱源模組之系統，探討擋板對三維流
道中流體流動結構、溫度分佈及間隔凸塊熱
源模組之熱傳特性的影響。本計畫係以數值
方法進行嚴謹的模擬，另外，為驗證數值模
擬的正確性，本計畫建置實驗系統來量測熱
源模組之凸塊熱源表面溫度；比較實驗數據
與數值模擬結果的準確度，其相對誤差介於
10 - 20%。結果顯示，當 Re = 600、0.25 ≦ Bh 
≦ 0.35、7.1  B≦ x  ≦ 8.4、0.025 ≦ Hh ≦ 
0.225、15° ≦ α ≦ 90°和 30° ≦ β ≦ 180°
時，比較各類擋板造成最佳熱點溫度降溫效
果可發現，40°傾斜擋板能比其他擋板可多
增加約 11%的降溫效果。當 0.25 ≦ Bh ≦ 
0.35 時，垂直擋板可降低熱點溫度最佳可達
45.6%，而 40°傾斜擋板更可降低熱點溫度最
佳可達 57.5%，且 40°傾斜擋板比垂直擋板
有較小的壓力降比及較高擋板效益，其最佳
效益達 1.66。當 0.025 ≦ Hh ≦ 0.225 時，凸
塊愈高熱傳效果愈好，但凸塊過高會阻礙
40°傾斜擋板引起的縱向渦旋強度並降低熱
傳增進效果。當 200  Re  1000≦ ≦ 時，雷諾
數愈大，擋板的壓力降比與效益愈高。 
 
關鍵字：熱源模組、擋板、縱向渦漩、熱傳
增進、數值模擬、實驗量測 
 
Abstract   
This study aims to investigate the 
influences of baffle on the flow structure, 
temperature distribution and heat transfer for a 
discrete block heat source module in a three 
dimensional channel. The numerical 
simulation is rigorously performed in this 
study. In addition, an experimental system is 
set up to verify the results of numerical 
simulation. The difference in surface 
temperature of block heat source between the 
numerical and experimental data is within the 
range of 10 - 20%. The results show that, as 
Re = 600、0.25  B≦ h  0.35≦ 、7.1  B≦ x  ≦
8.4、0.025  H≦ h  0.225≦ 、15°  α  90°≦ ≦ 、
30°  β  180°≦ ≦ , the reduction in hot spot 
temperature of heat source module for the 
situation with 40° inclined baffle can be 11% 
larger than other arrangement of baffle. When 
0.25  ≦ Bh  0.≦ 35 the hot spot temperature of 
module can be reduced by 47.3% for vertical 
baffle, and 57.6% for 40° inclined baffle. In 
addition, the pressure drop is smaller and 
enhancement efficiency of baffle is higher for 
40° inclined baffle. The optimum enhancement 
efficiency of baffle can be up to 1.66 for 40° 
inclined baffle. When 0.025  H≦ h  0.225, ≦
the heat transfer characteristics is better for 
larger height of block. As 200  Re  1000, ≦ ≦
both the heat transfer characteristics and 
penalty of pressure drop owing to baffle 
installation increase with Reynolds number.  
 
Keywords: heat source module, baffle, 
longitudinal vortex, heat transfer enhancement, 
simulation, experiment

數，對於流體流動結構、溫度分佈及熱傳特
