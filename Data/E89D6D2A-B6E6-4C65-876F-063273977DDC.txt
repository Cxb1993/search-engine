Abstract
This three year project is focusing on the system application of the next generation ultra
broadband optical amplifier. Achievements of the third year are (i) studied the performance of
the long-haul RZ-DPSK system using the dispersion flattened fiber (DFF) and clarified the effect
of the dispersion map design using the DFF, and (ii) studied the performance of the orthogonal
frequency division multiplexing (OFDM) system and characterized the effect of subcarrier
numbers. These achievements can contribute to understand the system behavior of the
long-haul system.
