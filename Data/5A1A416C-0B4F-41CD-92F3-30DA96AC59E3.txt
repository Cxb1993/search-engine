Abstract 
    The present study investigated the microstructural evolution and recrystallization behavior of 
matrix and second phase in 95Pb5Sn solder strip, 95Pb5Sn/63Sn37Pb composite solder bump, and 
SnCu solder alloys under current stressing. The analysis of in situ synchrotron X-ray diffraction 
(XRD) reveals that the crystalline peak for Pb of the 95Pb5Sn solder strip was dominated by (222) 
facet and followed sequentially by (311), (200), (220), and (111). The diffraction peaks diminish 
gradually upon current stressing. The peaks of (200) and (111) disappear last and exhibit slight peak 
shift, 0.1° of 2θ, to the lower angle before they eventually vanish. Sn of the as-prepared solder strip 
exhibits (211) and (321) peaks which also disappear completely upon current stressing. The Pb (200), 
(220), (111) and Sn (211) facets gradually show up after current stops.  
    An in situ SEM/EDS analysis revealed the dissolution of Sn in Pb matrix that is attributed to the 
electron bombardment induced crystal expansion of Pb matrix under current stressing. The Sn 
concentration reached 9.3% near the cathode in a 95Pb-5Sn solder wire at a current density of 
1.0×104 A/cm2. The supersaturation results in the recrystallization of Sn into one-dimensional nano 
crystals when the current ceases.

specimen redistributed from the one side of solder joint to another side. The redistribution of Cu6Sn5

Pb 晶格常數增加，促使 Sn 原子固溶到 Pb 的晶格裡面，由於電子風力是連續長時間與原子相

行為，並在右半部基地內發現許多新析出的 Cu6Sn5。在通電時間持續至 50 小時後，位於接點
B左半部基地內只剩非常少量的Cu6Sn5存在，反之，位於接點B右半部基地內，新析出的Cu6Sn5
仍持續不斷的增加，如圖十四(c)圈圈內所示。在通電時間持續至 100 小時後，原本位於接點 B
左半部基地內的 Cu6Sn5 已完全溶解消失(圖十四(d))，而位於接點 B 右半部基地內的 Cu6Sn5，
仍同通電 50 小時的現象不斷持續的成長，尤其位於接點右下角的區域，Cu6Sn5有特別快速增
長的行為，此現象持續至最終的通電時間 200 小時，如圖十四(d)-(f)所示。因此藉由上述之結
果，大致可將銲錫接點 B 以紅色虛線劃分成兩個區域，用以區分通電中 Cu6Sn5 在基地內之行
為，其分別為左半部的溶解區域(Dissolution region)和右半部的析出與成長區域(Precipitation 
and growth region)，如圖十六所示。

這些溶解於基地中的 Cu6Sn5 會在溶解的同時，再次析出於相對在溶解區域的另外一側，如圖

第一階段：基地內 Cu6Sn5 溶解， 
第二階段：溶解後被釋出的 Cu 原子往接點內之某特定方向進行遷移， 
第三階段：Cu6Sn5 再次析出於相對溶解區域的另一側。 
 
基地內 Cu6Sn5 之溶解行為 
    外加的電子流對基地內的 Cu6Sn5 之溶解行為扮演著關鍵性的角色。文獻中指出，由於覆
晶銲錫接點試片之 daisy chain 結構影響，將使得流經接點內的電子流方向並不一定和箭頭標示
方向一致[10]。所以為了單一化流經接點內電子流的方向，避免其受幾合形狀效應(Geometry 
effect)之影響，研究中特別製備 Cu/Sn1.0Cu/Cu 棒狀結構銲錫接點。 
圖十七為 Sn1.0Cu 銲錫基材內的 Cu6Sn5，於電流密度 2.6×103 A/cm2、130oC 下，通電時
間 0-152 小時的微觀結構變化。其流經接點的電子流方向為由右側流向左側，如圖中黑色箭頭
所示。當接點被施加電流，於通電 79 小時後，Cu6Sn5 晶粒的右側區域(面向電子流的區域)開
始出現些微的溶解，但晶粒左側區域(背向電子流方向的區域)仍保持完好，如圖十七(b)所示。
當通電時間延長至 120 小時，位於 Cu6Sn5 右側區域的溶解現象變為更加嚴重，如圖十七(c)所
示，反之，Cu6Sn5 晶粒的左側區域則仍完全未出現任何溶解的跡象。當通電時間繼續延長至
152 小時，近乎右半個 Cu6Sn5 已溶解消失(圖十七(d))，但位於晶粒之左側區域仍維持與通電前
一樣之形貌，並未發生任何的溶解。因此綜合上述的實驗結果可推判，其 Cu6Sn5 極化溶解的
方向決定於電子流之方向，換而言之，在通電中 Cu6Sn5 會由面向電子流的區域向背向電子流
的區域逐漸進行溶解。 
    此外，藉由計算 Cu6Sn5 在基地內占據之面積對時間作圖可得知，在棒狀結構銲錫接點內(圖

(2) The Electrical Characteristics and Interfacial Interaction of Ti/Ni/Ag/Au 
Multilayers under Thermal Cycling Test: Fu-Jung Yeh; Tsung-Chieh Chiu; 
Kwang-Lung Lin; National Cheng Kung University 
The Ti/Ni/Ag/Au multilayers combination was chosen for the metallization of AlN 
submount of flip chip high power LED. The interfacial interactions and electrical 
characteristics of the Ti/Ni/Ag/Au multilayers, under various periods of thermal 
cycling between -40°C and 125°C, were investigated by depositing on Si substrates. 
The electrical properties of the multilayer thin films were determined by two-probe 
nanoelectronics measurement. The electrical resistance of Ti/Ni/(Ag,Au) and 
Ni/(Ag,Au) multilayer was founded to be 9MO and 8.5MO respectively, which shows 
that the Ni/(Ag,Au) determines the overall resistance of the multilayer combination. 
The results of XPS depth profile analysis indicate that Ag atoms diffused passing 
through the pure Au layer to the surface after thermal cycling. The outward diffusion 
of Ag atoms expands the mixed region of Ni, Ag and Au, resulting in higher 
resistance of the Ni/(Ag,Au) layer. 
 
(3) The Initial Reflow Interaction between Sn3.0Ag0.5Cu Solder and Ni 
Metallization: Yu-Wei Lin; Kwang-Lung Lin; National Cheng Kung University 
The early stage interaction between Sn3.0Ag0.5Cu solder and Ni metallization was 
investigated at soldering temperature of 250 for 5 seconds followed by rapid quench 
in liquid nitrogen. The interfacial HRTEM (High Resolution Transmission Electron 
microscope) image reveals that Ni lattice distortion, a few atom layers, took place 
immediately adjacent to the Ni metallization. An amorphous Ni-Cu-Sn diffusion zone 
was formed next to the distorted Ni layer. Nano-crystalline Cu6Sn5 and metastable 
NiSn compounds exists within the amorphous diffusion zone. Meanwhile, a little 
NiSn also exists immediately adjacent to the distorted Ni layer. Homogeneous 
nucleation was proposed for the nanocrystals in the amorphous region while 
heterogeneous nucleation for the NiSn adjacent to the distorted Ni layer. The TEM 
inverse FFT (Fast Fourier Transform) image further shows the formation of many 
short-range-order structures within the amorphous diffusion region, showing the 
preliminary stage for the nucleation of the interfacial IMC.
