一、研究計畫中英文摘要：
（一）計畫中文摘要。（5 百字以內）
本研究計畫的主要目的在於與機光股份有限公司共同研發新型有機材料，作為有機發
光二極體(OLED)元件相關研究之用。計畫之合作方式為機光科技提供新合成之有機材料，
而我們實驗室負責分析量測。首先為求實驗環境乾淨，將架設一大型手套箱以處理有機高
分子樣品，而此手套箱的設計將與實驗室既有之真空量測鎗體相容。材料部分大致分為高
分子聚合物、小分子有機電洞傳輸層、電子傳輸層與發光層，高分子聚合物將使用氬離子
轟擊以及紫外光臭氧技術做表面處理，所有動作將在所設計之大手套箱中完成並將樣品傳
輸入真空量測鎗體，再使用紫外光能譜儀、X 射線能譜儀作介面間的分析，最後蒸鍍出整
個有機元件觀察其性質；小分子有機材料的部分，將針對各種的電子電洞傳輸層、發光層
的材料做搭配，並一一使用介面能階分析技術並繪出其能帶圖，得出能障大小的資訊，另
外也將使用共蒸鍍技術製作單一有機元件並分析電流電壓曲線。針對每個段落所得出的結
果，與機光科技做討論並決定還需提供何種新材料，或者如何改良材料的性質，以達到研
發出穩定、低成本且具商業競爭性的有機新材料為目標。
關鍵字: 紫外光能譜儀、X 射線能譜儀、共蒸鍍、電流電壓曲線
（二）計畫英文摘要。（5 百字以內）
The main goal of this project is to develop new OLED materials with Lumtec. Lumtec develops
and provides us new organic materials and we carry out the material characterization on these novel
materials. The cooperation processes are following. First, we will design a big glove box that can
be combined with our vacuum analysis chamber in order to keep the experimental samples clean.
The organic materials given are either small molecules or polymer, such as hole transport layers
(HTL), electron transport layers (ETL), and emission layers (EML), and so on. X-ray
photoemission spectroscopy (XPS) and ultraviolet photoemission spectroscopy (UPS) will be used
to analyze the surface of the samples. Finally, the OLED device with these novel materials will be
fabricated and analyzed. For the part of small molecules, we will focus on the interface between
each layer, for example the interfaces between HTL and EML, or ETL and EML. The band
diagrams of the interface will be derived from XPS and UPS analysis. We can get the energy barrier
between the mentioned two layers and find a way to optimize it. We will work with Lumtec on the
result of every process and to decide the kind of material they still need to work on, and how to
improve the material characteristic to get stable, low-cost, and competitive new organic
materials.
Key words: UPS, XPS, J-V curve

特性及優缺點。
一般的有機元件實驗，通常都是做出一個有機元件，直接量測其發光效能、電流密度
對電壓曲線(J-V curve)等等，所得到的只是整體的元件性質，並無法得知內部各層結構間
的關係，就算知道每層材料個別的特性，如功函數、最高被佔據的分子軌域(HOMO)等
等，也無法知道當兩層材料結合後，這些性質是否會發生改變，而我們實驗室擁有的XPS
和UPS，就可以觀察到介面間的微妙變化。而這些介面間的變化，對應的才是有機元件效
能最真實的效能，因此和機光科技合作，我們將可對新合成的有機材料，做出最有效益且
最真實的分析。

目前廣為研究以及應用的有機發光二極體分為兩類: (1)小分子有機發光二極體
(smOLED),(2)高分子聚合物發光二極體(PLED)。其中小分子的 OLED 製作方法為將有
機材料一層層的蒸鍍到基板上，雖需要較高的真空蒸鍍技術，但為目前主流的模式，唯不
適合太大面積的製造；而高分子聚合物發光二極體的製作方式，通常為將液體狀態的導電
共軛高分子，經由旋轉塗佈的方式，使其附著在基板上，經由轉速的控制，就能鍍出所需
要的厚度，因此較適用於大面積的製造。整體而言，目前業界的發展較集中於小分子
OLED，但機光科技對於兩者的材料都持續在研發，而我們實驗室對兩種有機材料，都具
備充分的分析能力，且都能自行作出單一完整的元件，因此這次的合作案針對 OLED 和
PLED 都將一起研究。由於研究的材料眾多，且分析深入，產出結果可相當豐富，因此以
目前保守來估計，此次的合作計畫至少可產出兩篇期刊論文以及兩個專利。

In order to investigate interactions between SubPc and NPB, the core level spectra of these
samples are also measured. Figure 4(a) shows the boron 1s core level spectra of SubPc with NPB
using the excitation photon energy of 250 eV to avoid the diffraction effect in larger
photoelectron energy and to enhance the sensitivity23. The main feature of boron 1s peak does not
shift significantly during the deposition of NPB. However, a new feature was observed at the low
binding energy side of boron 1s spectrum, and its intensity increases with the thickness of NPB
increases. We further deconvoluted the boron 1s peak of SubPc with 0 Å, 0.2 Å, and 1 Å of NPB
to identify the new chemical state, as illustrated in the right hand side of Fig. 4(a). Compared to
the original peak, the new feature is found at lower binding energy by about 0.7 eV. At the same
time, there are additional features appearing at the high energy side in chlorine 2p core level
spectra, as shown in Fig. 4(b). No energy shift in other core level spectra, such as carbon 1s and
nitrogen 1s, were observed during the NPB deposition process. It implies that there is a bonding
change between boron and chlorine on SubPc molecules, which results in extra features in B1s
and Cl 2p core level spectra.
Previous studies indicated that the reactivity of subphthalocyanines can be divided into three
different categories: (1) axial reactivity, the reactive centers by the central boron atom, (2)
peripheral reactivity, the peripheral functional groups on the aromatic carbon atoms, and (3) ring
expansion reactions, the diiminoisoindolines-type core corresponding to the above reactivies14,24.
SubPc can be modified by both axial and peripheral reactivity, and the ring expansion is the result
of a loss of SubPc skeleton. Given that the photoemission spectra in C 1s and N 1s were not
affected too much throughout the deposition process, it suggests that the reaction of NPB on
SubPc comes from axial reactivity. SubPc in axial reactivity have been widely found with oxygen
nucleophiles, aliphatic, aromatic alcohols, and carbon nucleophiles to give rise to axially
substituted SubPcs14,25-27. In the neutral condition, SubPc has a positive atom charge on boron of
about 0.68 and a negative atom charge on chlorine with -0.1628. Combined with the core level
spectra shown in Fig. 4, it can be deduced that the bonding between chlorine and boron was
broken with the presence of NPB molecules, with boron atoms gaining back the electron that was
shared with chlorine and chlorine atom dissociating from SubPc and possibly being attracted by
NPB molecules. The reaction results in the new feature observed on the low binding energy of
