Abstract 
Because the sensitivity of pressure sensing is limited by bare fiber grating, the 
pressure sensed need to be transferred into axial extended-strain by package technology 
for practicability. However, the central wavelength of fiber grating will be shift by strain, 
and also will be affected by temperature. The accuracy of the experimental results is 
seriously affected by temperature when the sensitivity is greatly increased by package 
technology. 
This project proposes a novel fiber grating sensor with high sensitivity which can 
measure pressure and temperature simultaneously for accuracy of experimental results 
by the characteristics of the long period fiber grating and short period grating, and the 
advantage of lateral-pressure package for detecting the variation of the 
center-wavelength and reflection of fiber grating. The composition of this sensor has a 
long-short period fiber grating encapsulated in a polymer-half-filled metal cylinder. 
The project has finished the study in the characteristic of short period fiber grating 
and long period fiber grating. And, the fabrication and characteristics of the long-short 
period fiber grating in a fiber have been also investigated. Then, the temperature-pressure 
sensor based on fiber grating has been achieved. From the experimental results, the 
sensitivity of temperature sensors are 0.02nm/OC and 0.39 dB / OC and the pressure 
sensitivity are 7.5nm/bar and 7.76dB/bar. 
Finally, the sensor can be used water level sensor at different temperature, the 
reflection wavelength of the sensitivity are 0.007 nm/cm, and 0.12nm/ OC, the reflection 
intensity of the sensitivity are -0.011 dB/cm and 0.56 dB / OC 
 
Keywords: fiber grating sensor, polymer.

感測器提供新世代進一步改善整體結構安全性及新的監測功能。 
光纖光柵為近年來發展最快速的光纖被動元件之一，1978 年 K.O.Hill[2]等人發
現摻鍺光纖中的光感性，隨後便利用 514.5nm 波長的氬離子雷射由光纖兩端射入，
使此二束傳播方向相反的雷射光源在光纖纖核中產生干涉而造成駐波的發生，由於
透過摻雜鍺的光纖纖核中的光感性對光的吸收而造成光纖纖核發生折射率週期性的
變化，形成所謂的光纖光柵，此為世上第一個寫製完成的光纖光柵。不過由於此種
方式所產生的光纖光柵其折射率變化很小，主要由於光纖軸向的孔徑太小，以致於
雷射能量未能全部進入，並且在製作過程自此之後由於其具有許多獨特的優點，因
而在中易受到外界環境的干擾而影響到光纖光柵的品質，因而此製作方式並不適
用，其後主要的技術突破，是以兩道雷射光束以側照方式產生干涉條紋來寫製光纖

應用上可發揮可觀的量測能力，在及高溫鍋爐的壓力與溫度的同時量測應用上亦具
有高度的潛力。

六、計畫成果自評 
計畫執行已充分掌握主要元件長-短週期光纖光柵的寫製技術，在製作時須將
LPG 與 FBG 分別寫製，因此在寫製時要配合光纖的特性及寫製完光纖退火的參數
才能將 FBG 中心波長在 LPG 的負斜率區或正斜率區。另計畫執行也已完成溫 -壓
光纖感測器製作及感測器特性量測設備架設。將串聯式光纖光柵進行封裝，
對溫度及壓力進行實驗，可得到正負斜坡區的感測矩陣，對溫度感測靈敏度絕對值
為 0.02nm/ OC 及 0.39dB/ OC ，其靈敏度比未封裝前佳，除了光纖光柵本身的熱膨脹，
還有鋁質圓柱的熱膨脹；壓力感測靈敏度的絕對值為 7.54 nm/bar 與 7.76dB/bar。且
將感測器做不同溫度下的水位實驗，得到水位與反射波長及能量皆呈現線性關係，
且與在負斜坡區的趨勢相同且證明封裝後的靈敏度確實與未封裝前來的靈敏並具有
數倍以上的靈敏度。 
    串聯式光纖光柵的製造方法較為簡單，但還需提高共振波長的反射量，使其反
射能量穩定，且在封裝的過程中能有許多地方的需要改善，如聚合物的選擇、固定
光柵的黏著劑與剝除披覆層後光纖的結構…等，因此封裝技術與方法仍有許多改善
的空間。 
本計畫的串聯式光纖光柵，與新型封裝架構做結合，的確有同時執行壓力與溫
度的感測，並可實際應用於水位的量測。並期待串聯式光纖光柵能夠應用於其他的
感測上。

1. Introduction 
For the application of the fiber-grating intrinsic 
property and its different packaged techniques in 
humidity sensing, a polymer-coated fiber Bragg grating 
sensor was demonstrated with a maximum sensitivity 
of 5.6 pm/1%RH [1]. The sensitivity of a relative 
humidity sensors based on two-layer nano-coatings in 
fiber with a long period grating (LPG) has been 
improved to 0.44 nm/1%RH [2]. For the publications 
above mentioned, the long period grating is the key 
component whose basic operating principle is based on 
the forward-propagating core mode to be coupled into 
backward-propagating cladding modes to cause the 
energy loss dips at appropriate resonant wavelengths. 
the sensing mechanism is based on the shift of resonant 
wavelengths in LPG-transmitted spectrum caused by 
the change of the core index modulation. In this paper, 
a novel fiber sensor based on the calcium-chloride thin-
film to be coated on an air-gap long-period fiber grating 
(AG-LPG) is firstly proposed. The results 
experimentally demonstrated that the sensor have the 
maximum sensitivity of 1.36 nm/1%RH which is higher 
several times than that of using other sensing 
techniques of fiber grating sensors for our knowledge.

 Abstract-- A new fiber sensor by using a packaged fiber 
Bragg grating (FBG) a long-period fiber grating (LPG) for 
simultaneous measurement of pressure and temperature is 
proposed. 
I.  INTRODUCTION 
The core index in a fiber grating is the periodic change 
along the fiber axis, which can reflect and diffract 
specific wavelengths of light. By means of the different 
optical properties between the FBG and LPG, the sensor 
can simultaneously measure the pressure and temperature 
[1-3]. In this paper, the sensor is composed of an FBG 
and an LPG to be encapsulated in a polymer-half-filled 
metal cylinder with two openings on opposite sides of the 
wall of the polymer to sense the pressure. Due to the 
different response characteristics between the FBG and 
LPG, the variation of pressures and temperatures will 
cause the variation of the center-wavelength and 
reflective power of the FBG simultaneously. Therefore, 
the sensor can be used for measuring pressure and 
temperature simultaneously. 
II.  BASIC PRINCIPLES 
In this study, a series of an LPG and an FBG to form 
the sensing head is sealed with the silicon rubber polymer 
shown as in Fig. 1. The LPG and FBG used for this 
sensor is fabricated on a hydrogen-loaded single-mode 
fiber by using a phase mask and an amplitude mask 
writing technique respectively by using an KrF excimer 
laser of 248 nm. The center wavelength of FBG is
