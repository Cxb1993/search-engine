前言與研究目的 
本年度研究計畫案係依據本研究計畫
案主持人周至宏教授執行 2006和 2007年國
科會計畫案之研究經驗，以及本研究計畫案
主持人與其學生這幾年來在非線性動態系
統之最佳控制器設計的研究經驗(Ho and 
Chou, 2007; Hsu et al., 2008)，進一步針對非
線性不確定動態系統(同時考慮系統參數不
確定量及模式近似誤差等兩種不確定狀況)
之最佳控制器設計的問題，來作更深入與完
善的研討。在工程實際的控制系統中，受控
體(plants)通常是非線性的，而要使非線性動
態控制系統達到所要求的控制目標，其控制
器的設計是一個相當不容易的過程。因此，
有許多非線性動態控制系統之控制器的設
計方法已經被發展出來克服這個難題
(Slotine and Li, 1991; Marino and Tomei, 
1995; Chen et al., 1999; Tsao et al., 2002)。近
年來，由 Takagi 和 Sugeno(1985)應用軟式計
算(soft computing)中的模糊邏輯理論(fuzzy 
logic theory)，所提出的 TS 模糊模型方法
(TS-fuzzy-model-based approach)是一個非
常有效地來處理非線性控制系統的方法，此
方法在非線性動態控制系統的應用方面也
已有許多成功的應用實例(Babuska, 1998; 
Farinwata et al., 2000; Tanaka and Wang, 
2001; Tong et al., 2004; Li, 2006; Yoneyama, 
2007; Nachidi et al., 2008)。傳統的非線性動
態控制系統建模方法是以一個單一的動態
模型來描述非線性動態控制系統的整體行
為模式，而 TS 模糊模型方法是以結合多個
動態模型(特別是線性動態模型)，來描述非
線性動態控制系統的整體行為模式。因此，
當以 TS 模糊模型方法來建構非線性動態控
制系統時，其最大優點是可以應用線性系統
的控制理論方法來設計控制器(Tanaka and 
Wang, 2001)。在 TS 模糊模型動態控制系統
的每一模糊規則有一線性動態方程式作為
後件項，此表示每一模糊規則有其各別的動
態行為。然後，整體的動態模型由這些模糊
規則所合成而完成建模。 
在控制系統設計之研究課題方面，通常
我們感興趣的一個研究問題，是在要求二次
積分性能指標(quadratic integral performance 
index)最小化的控制目標下，完成設計二次
  4
分析的結果上，比較能夠降低其保守性，也
會使得在設計含有參數不確定性之動態控
制系統的控制器時，更容易且更具彈性
(Chou, 1995; Chen and Ren, 2001; Hsieh and 
Chou, 2004)。參數不確定性可導致 TS 模糊
模型動態控制系統發生不穩定(Tanaka and 
Wang, 2001; Hsieh and Chou, 2004)。因此，
探討具參數不確定性之 TS 模糊模型動態控
制系統的強健穩定性，對 TS 模糊模型動態
控制系統的研究而言也是一重要的議題。此
外，建構 TS 模糊模型動態控制系統有二種
方式:扇形建構法和線性化建構法。運用扇
形建構法雖然可以得到精確的 TS 模糊模式
，但是其模糊規則數會增加很多，因而增添
設計控制器的困難度。運用線性化建構法，
其模糊規則數很少，使得控制器的設計變得
非常容易，但是在設計控制器時，就必需考
慮近似誤差(approximation error)的問題。所
以，有一些學者專家(Chen et al., 1999, 2000; 
Kiriakidis, 1999; Uang and Huang, 2004; 
Hsiao et al., 2005; Tseng and Hwang, 2007)已
針對原始非線性系統與其轉換成 TS 模糊模
型系統後之間的近似誤差 (approximation 
error)的問題，應用線性矩陣不等式技巧來
解決 TS 模糊模型動態控制系統的強健穩定
性的問題，其中的近似誤差被視為是有界範
數的不確定量。因此，如果使用 TS 模糊模
型來描述具不確定量的參數之複雜的非線
性系統時，我們必須同時處理這兩個存在於
TS 模糊模型動態控制系統中的不確定量，
一部份為具有參數矩陣中各別元素不確定
量，以及另一部份為近似誤差有界範數不確
定量。但是就本研究計畫案主持人所知，就
目前現有的文獻中，迄今尚未發現有任何文
獻，針對同時具有參數矩陣中各別元素不確
定量以及近似誤差有界範數不確定量之 TS
模糊模型動態控制系統的二次有限時間強
健 最 佳 迴 授 控 制 問 題
(quadratic-finite-horizon and robust-optimal 
feedback control problems)，來探討設計其強
健最佳控制器的研究課題。 
所以，本研究計畫案之目的就是針對上
述所提及的研究問題，提出一新的軟式計算
和硬式計算之互補熔合的整合方法，在使得
二次積分性能指標最小化的條件下，受到強
健穩定性條件的限制，針對同時具有參數矩
陣中各別元素不確定量以及近似誤差有界
範數不確定量之 TS 模糊模型動態控制系統
，來解決二次有限時間強健最佳平行分配補
償迴授控制器的設計問題，其中，軟式計算
是使用本計畫案主持人與其學生所發展的
田口基因演算法(Tsai et al., 2004)，硬式計算
是使用正交函數計算法及線性矩陣不等式
技巧。在本研究計畫中也應用線性矩陣不等
式技巧推導出一個嶄新的強健穩定性充分
條件，來確保在同時具有參數矩陣中各別元
素不確定量以及近似誤差有界範數不確定
量之不確定情況時，閉迴路系統仍保持漸進
式穩定(asymptotical stability)；同時使用正
交函數之優雅的運算特性，將二次有限時間
強健最佳平行分配補償迴授控制器設計的
動態最佳化問題，轉換成為田口基因演算法
容易處理之代數方程式型態表示的靜態最
佳化問題。因此，針對具不確定性的 TS 模
糊模型動態控制系統，可順利地解決二次有
限時間強健最佳平行分配補償迴授控制器
的設計問題。

Robust Quadratic-Optimal Control of
TS-Fuzzy-Model-Based Dynamic Systems With
Both Elemental Parametric Uncertainties and
Norm-Bounded Approximation Error
Wen-Hsien Ho, Jinn-Tsong Tsai, and Jyh-Horng Chou, Senior Member, IEEE
Abstract—This paper considers the design problem of the robust
quadratic-optimal parallel-distributed-compensation (PDC) con-
trollers for Takagi–Sugeno (TS) fuzzy-model-based control systems
with both elemental parametric uncertainties and norm-bounded
approximation error. By complementarily fusing the robust sta-
bilizability condition, the orthogonal functions approach (OFA),
and the hybrid Taguchi genetic algorithm (HTGA), an integrative
method is presented in this paper to design the robust quadratic-
optimal PDC controllers such that 1) the uncertain TS-fuzzy-
model-based control systems can be robustly stabilized, and 2)
a quadratic integral performance index for the nominal TS-fuzzy-
model-based control systems can be minimized. In this paper, the
robust stabilizability condition is proposed in terms of linear ma-
trix inequalities (LMIs). By using the OFA and the LMI-based
robust stabilizability condition, the robust quadratic-optimal PDC
control problem for the uncertain TS-fuzzy-model-based dynamic
systems is transformed into a static constrained-optimization prob-
lem represented by the algebraic equations with constraint of LMI-
based robust stabilizability condition, thus greatly simplifying the
robust optimal PDC control design problem. Then, for the static
constrained-optimization problem, the HTGA is employed to find
the robust quadratic-optimal PDC controllers of the uncertain
TS-fuzzy-model-based control systems. Two design examples of
the robust quadratic-optimal PDC controllers for an uncertain in-
verted pendulum system and an uncertain nonlinear mass–spring–
damper mechanical system are given to demonstrate the applica-
bility of the proposed integrative approach.
Index Terms—Hybrid Taguchi-genetic algorithm (HTGA), lin-
ear matrix inequalities (LMIs), orthogonal functions approach
(OFA), quadratic-optimal control, robust stabilizability, Takagi–
Sugeno (TS) fuzzy model.

The main feature of a Takagi–Sugeno (TS) fuzzy model is to
express the local dynamics of each fuzzy rule by the linear
system model. There have recently been many successful ap-
plications of the TS-fuzzy-model-based approach to nonlinear
control systems [2]–[12]. Despite the success of applying the
TS-fuzzy-model-based approach to nonlinear control systems,
it has become evident that many research issues remain to be
addressed [5], [11]. In fact, in many cases, it is very difficult,
if not impossible, to obtain the accurate values of some system
parameters. The parametric uncertainties can be viewed to take
different forms like elemental and norm-bounded. Elemental
parametric uncertainties are those for which the elemental infor-
mation of the uncertain matrix is utilized and bounds on the indi-
vidual elements of the uncertain matrix are considered, whereas
norm-bounded parametric uncertainties are those for which only
a norm bound on the uncertain matrix is considered [13], [14].
The parametric uncertainties may destabilize the TS-fuzzy-
model-based control systems [4], [5]. Therefore, recently, some
researchers (see, for example, [4] and [15]–[25], and the refer-
ences therein) have employed the linear matrix inequality (LMI)
based techniques to investigate the robust stability and stabi-
lization problems of the TS-fuzzy-model-based control systems
with parametric uncertainties, where the considered paramet-
ric uncertainties can be categorized as norm-bounded uncertain
cases, except that Hsieh and Chou [20] and Ho et al. [23] consid-
ered the elemental information of parametric uncertain matrices.
Chou and coworkers [20], [23] have shown that if the elemental
information of the parametric uncertain matrices is considered,
the results will be less conservative than those results that do
not utilize the elemental information of the parametric uncer-
tain matrices. On the other hand, some researchers [26]–[31]
employed the LMI-based techniques to solve the robust stabi-
lization problem of the TS-fuzzy-model-based control system
with the approximation error between the nonlinear control sys-
tem and its fuzzy model, where the approximation error is as-
sumed to be a norm-bounded uncertainty. Since the TS fuzzy
models are usually used to describe complex nonlinear con-
trol systems with uncertain parameters, it is not unusual that,
at times, we have to deal with a TS-fuzzy-model-based control
system simultaneously consisting of two parts: One part has
only the elemental parametric uncertainties, and the other part
has the norm-bounded approximation error [28]. If the elemen-
tal parametric uncertainties are lumped with the norm-bounded
approximation error as a norm-bounded composite term

V. CONCLUSION
By using the OFA, an algorithm has been presented in
this paper to solve the nominal TS-fuzzy-model-based feed-
back dynamic equations. Then, the presented algebraic al-
gorithm is complementarily fused with the HTGA to design
the robust quadratic-optimal PDC controllers of the TS-fuzzy-
model-based control systems with both elemental parametric
uncertainties and norm-bounded approximation error such that
the control objective of directly minimizing a quadratic in-
tegral performance index subject to the constraint of LMI-
based robust stabilizability condition can be achieved. The
quadratic integral performance index is also converted into
algebraic form by using the OFA. Since, by using the OFA
and the LMI-based robust stabilizability condition, the robust
quadratic-optimal PDC control problem for the uncertain TS-
fuzzy-model-based control systems can be replaced by a static-
parameter constrained-optimization problem represented by the
algebraic equations with constraint of LMI-based robust sta-
bilizability condition, and since the proposed algorithm only
involves the algebraic computation, the design procedures of
the robust quadratic-optimal PDC controllers for the TS-fuzzy-
model-based control systems with both elemental parametric
uncertainties and norm-bounded approximation error may be
either greatly reduced or much simplified accordingly. There-
fore, this proposed approach facilitates the design task of the
robust quadratic-optimal PDC controllers for the uncertain TS-
fuzzy-model-based control systems. On the other hand, the prob-
lem of determining the robust stabilizability has been turned
into an LMI feasibility problem that can be easily solved by
means of numerically efficient convex programming algorithms.
The illustrative examples regarding 1) an uncertain balanc-
ing problem of an inverted pendulum on a cart and 2) an
uncertain nonlinear mass–spring–damper mechanical system
have shown that the proposed integrative approach is effective
for designing the robust quadratic-optimal PDC controllers of
the TS-fuzzy-model-based control systems with both elemen-
tal parametric uncertainties and norm-bounded approximation
error.
