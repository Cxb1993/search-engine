Studies on fabrications of high T embedded layers in 193 nm and simulations of its image
characteristics applied on immersion litho with a combination of TE polarized light, OAI and
AttPSM
計畫編號：NSC 94－2215－E－009－078
執行期間：94 年 8 月 1 日 至 95 年 7 月 31 日
主持人：龍文安 交通大學應用化學系教授
中文摘要
解像度增進技術一般可分為偏軸發光、
相移圖罩與表層成像三種。偏軸發光已屬微
影機台基本配備；表層成像製程複雜，業界
接受度不高；相移圖罩則仍有廣大之研究空
間。193奈米濕浸式微影，搭配橫電偏振光光
源與相移圖罩，為目前顯學，將主導全球未
來數年光學微影之研發。
減光型相移圖罩，可適用於248、193、
157、126、13、0.8奈米，涵蓋深紫外光、極
短紫外光、X-光，可謂全部微影波長皆可適
用；且尚有製備較易，適用於各式圖形之優
點，因此，其重要性無庸置疑。嵌附層正規
透射率T%~6%，高透射率通常定義T%~15-35。
本計畫為193奈米微影高透射率嵌附層
材料之研製，與模擬探討應用於橫電偏振
光、偏軸發光、減光型相移圖罩組合之濕浸
式微影成像特性。
本報告驗證，如使用適當光學參數，可
顯著改善解像度與增進禁止間距之焦深。
關鍵詞：微影、相移圖罩
英文摘要
In general, resolution enhancement
techniques consist of three methods, off axis
illumination, phase shift mask and top surface
imaging. Off axis illumination became basic
for exposurer, top surface imaging is
complicate, hardly accepted by industry, phase
shift mask still has vast space for research.
193 nm immersion lithography using TE
polarized light and phase shifting mask is the
current hot research issue and will dominate
the global research on optical lithography in
the near future.
The attenuated phase shifting mask
(AttPSM) is useful in 248, 193, 157, 126, 13
and 0.8 nm, covered all lithographic
wavelength from deep-UV, Extreme-UV to
X-ray, by the way, it also has advantages of
easy fabrication and suitability to all kind of
patterns, therefore, the importance is of no
doubt. For EAPSM, the normal transmittance
T often used is 6%, high transmittance is
generally defined as 15-35%.
The investigation item of this project is
the study on the fabrications of high
transmittance embedded layers in 193 nm and
simulations of its image characteristics applied
on immersion lithography with a combination
of TE (transverse electric) polarized light,
off-axis illumination and AttPSM.
This report verified that the resolution and
DOF of forbidden pitch were clearly improved
if proper optical parameters were selected.
Keywords: Lithography, Phase Shift Mask
