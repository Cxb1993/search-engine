Abstract 
 
This integrated project, “the development and application of nanostructural electrode 
materials”, combines material fabrication, electrochemistry, nanotechnology and material 
characteristics to create new electrode materials needed in the next generation. The results of 
sub-projects in this integrated project are as follows: 
In PEMFC project, the project 2 used MWNTs as the support of platinum catalyst for 
electrode material. The MWNT was modified using HNO3 heat reflux method to contain the 
carboxylic group grafted MWNT (cMWNT). The effect of different ratio of carboxylic group on 
the dispersion of platinum will be discussed. 
During the second-year course of project 3, fabrication of films with hierarchical pore 
structure of large area (> cm2), chemical synthesis of conductive graphite, and of platinum 
catalysis nanoparticles are our main research focuses. Additionally, hollow silica microspheres 
are also synthesized by use of the organic microspheres as a sacrificial template. An inverse opal 
structure with an enhanced pore volume becomes possible when the hollow microsphres are used 
as the building block for the colloidal crystals. Finally, platinum nanoparticles have been 
successfully synthesized via chemical means. These developed materials will be integrated into 
periodically ordered porous structures used as the catalyst support for fuel-cell membranes and 
their electrode properties will be examined. 
The research of project 4 includes three parts. The first one show that the gas diffusion 
layers prepared and surface treated by plasma technology. Second part mainly shows that the high 
surface area catalyst support prepared by a novel physical technology, and the third part, anode 
water management Improve by used the metal oxide materials and their electrode properties will 
be examined. 
 
 
KEYWORDS：Membrane Electrode Assembly；PEMFC；Catalytic

第三部分 MEA 關鍵技術的研究 
文獻曾提過陰陽極觸媒電極水管理也是燃料電池性能關鍵的重要一環。蒙脫土
(clay)，一般稱之為黏土或矽片，在子計畫 4 中主要使用蒙脫土固有的幾何分散特性以其單
獨片狀無機結構及其表面所具有的高離子數目，進一步與碳黑表面進行交互作用。並以片
狀結構阻絕碳黑彼此之間的凡得瓦爾吸引力，使碳黑彼此之間達到剝離且均勻分散效果。
除了利用掃瞄式顯微鏡進行微觀觀察外，在巨觀下也可看出碳管於奈黑矽片水溶液中會因
矽片介入進而由原先聚集沈澱狀態達到長期懸浮的性質。此現象為一般文獻中所未曾出
現，也是本實驗室創新之發明。預期可利用此現象進一步應用於燃料或生化電池之電極部
分，藉由奈米矽片進一步修飾電極表面碳黑分散性並同時發揮催化功效。

次的國際電化學學會第211屆會議(The Electrochemical Society 211th Meeting)，擁有與世界各地
學者交流的經驗。本次會議於今年05/06~05/10於USA,ILLINOIS,CHICAGO舉辦，今年所發表
之論文總篇數為1355篇。本次研討會內容包含36大主題，分別為Tutorials in Nanotechnology,
Electrochemistry of Novel Electrode Materials for Energy Conversion and Storage, Characterization
and Prevention of Failure Modes of Lithium Polymer and Lithium Ion Batteries in Transportation
Applications, Organic and Biological Electrochemistry General Oral and Poster Session, SOI
Device Technology, Electrochemical Processing in ULSI and MEMS等。ECS meeting 為國際材料
界一個重要且盛大的研討會，每半年在世界知名地點舉辦會議，是此領域中非常重要的研討
會，所以相關業界以及學界等研究人士皆會出席參加此項國際研討會。
學生的論文安排在第四天的下午兩點到六點，於HILTON飯店的Stevens 2,South Hall,
Lower Level,以口說的方式報告。由於平時於學校的Group Meeting中，薛富盛教授即要求以英
語口說方式進行報告，藉著平時累積的經驗，整個報告過程還算順利。論文報告後日本與會
人士詢問電漿處理過的氣體擴散層與鐵氟龍直接塗抹的氣體擴散層在後續的製程中有無差
異，針對此問題，學生說明電漿處理過的氣體擴散層非常柔軟對於後續的熱壓密合度有正面
的影響但鐵氟龍直接塗抹的氣體擴散層比較硬對於後續的熱壓易造成Nafion膜的皺折與破
損，圓滿地解釋並獲得對方的認同。
