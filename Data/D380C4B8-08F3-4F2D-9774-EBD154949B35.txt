一、 中文摘要
此計畫研究退化性存貨之訂購與生產排程問
題。 擬探討之範圍為商品之需求為價格之函數及
缺貨為部分後補之環境。 過去, 於退化性存貨之
訂購或生產排程問題研究中, 大部份學者皆假設
缺貨後補之訂單是不會遭到撤單的。 由於實際環
境中, 顧客會因為某些因素而取消其預約之訂單。
因此, 缺貨後補之顧客不會取消訂單之假設未必
能於所有環境皆成立。有鑑於此,本計畫於所探討
的退化性商品的生產存貨問題中, 除了容許缺貨
部分後補外, 並擬允許缺貨後補之訂單可被取消。
本計畫擬分兩期發展兩個決定 (1) 商品之訂購排
程與售價決策, 及 (2) 商品之生產排程與售價決
策之數學模型。 第一年本計畫對訂購排程問題推
導出有限計畫期之封閉解型式的 (1) 最佳訂購策
略、 (2)最佳訂貨週期、 (3)最佳預約價格及 (4)最
佳一般銷售價格。 第二年本計畫之目的包括透過
(1) 最適生產量及 (2) 最適預售價格之制定, 以獲
得計畫期或單位時間之最大利潤。本研究針對 (1)
完全預售制度下的生產排程及定價問題 (2) 預購
顧客之購買行為受等待發貨時間影響之完全預購
型式之生產排程及定價問題 (3) 同時容許預售與
現貨銷售的生產排程問題。 等三個問題建立數學
模型及推導最佳策略。
關鍵字: 存貨、 訂購、 定價、 超賣、 生產、
Abstract: This project investigates an inventory-
production scheduling problem for deteriorating
items with price dependent demand and partial
backlogging. It is assumed in most previous works
that backlogging customers may not cancel their
orders. However, in reality, customers may with-
draw or cancel their orders before receiving their
orders. Thus, the assumption that backlogging cus-
tomers may not cancel their orders is not always
true. Tacking the cancellation phenomenon into
account, this project tries to develop models for
(1) simultaneously determining the order schedul-
ing decision and the selling price, and (2) simulta-
neously determining the production scheduling de-
cision and the selling price for deteriorating items
having partial backlogging. The above two deci-
sions are to be separately analyzed within two years.
In the first year, this project determined the order
scheduling decision and the selling price. In the sec-
ond year, this project dealt with on determination
of production scheduling decision and the selling
price. The purpose of this project aims to develop
algorithms to derive optimal solution or near opti-
mal solutions to the proposed problem. In the first
year, this project derived the following decision rule
in closed form formulas for a finite planning hori-
zon: (1) optimal replenishment policy, (2) optimal
order sequency, (3) optimal advance sales price, and
(4) optimal regular sales price. In the second year,
our research This research shall aim at (1) fully ad-
dressing the production schedule and pricing strate-
gies issues related to the advance booking system,
(2) carefully examining the purchasing behavior of
consumers from the time they placed their orders
until the products are shipped out, and how their
behaviors are influenced by the production schedule
and pricing strategy, (3) determining how to opti-
mize the production schedule for both the advance
booking and goods in stock. Based on these three
issues, we shall aim to formulate a mathematical
model and an optimum strategy.
Key word: Inventory, Ordering, Pricing, Over-

(3) 存貨會議 I
Tamura, Kojima 及 Kumagai 博士等探討 Just
in time 系統下的 Kanban 問題。 Kanban 系統是
Just in time 成功與否的一個重要關鍵, 其功能是促
使採購部門的訂購量能剛好等於需求量。 Kanban 系
統成功的因素之一是需求率小。 當需求率大時, 傳統
的 Kanban 系統難以發揮其功效。
為能提升其效率, 於需求率大的情況下,Tamura 博士
等運用指數平滑的訂購法取代 Kanban系統。 其研究
結果顯示指數平滑的訂購法下的存貨成本低於 Kan-
ban 系統下的存貨成本。 此外,Tamura 博士等亦建
議此方法於多階段生產與存貨系統下更具效率。
(4) 存貨會議 II
Tas 及 Tarim 學者提出一個 (R,s,S) 策略的存貨
模型, 此 (R,s,S) 策略是結合動態 (s,S) 及 (R,S)
策略所形成。 此存貨模型類似成本績效下的 (s,S) 存
貨策略及敏感績效下的 (R,S) 存貨策略． Tas 及
Tarim 學者衡量其所提出的存貨模型之方法是透過
固定 (s,S) 或 (R,S) 來衡量未被固定的 (R,S) 或
(s,S) 之績效來評量。 Tas 及 Tarim 學者提出一個求
解演算法尋找所提出模型之決策。 研究結果顯示 Tas
及 Tarim 學者提出的求解演算法所搜尋的解近似最
佳解。
(5) 供應鏈管理之發展
過去已有許多學者提出如何持續性的或快速的提升供
應鏈效率的方法。 學習 (Learn) 及六個標準差 (Six
Sigma) 是最常被用到的。 Naslund 學者從三個層面
來討論關於供應鏈績效改善的方法。 首先, 他探討管
理風尚的本質,其次,他闡述剛好即時 (Just in time)
與學習生產, 及全面品質管理與六個標準差等兩對改
善方法的熱絡程度與共通性, 最後他衡量這四種方法
於文獻中被運用的頻率。 研究結果發現最近的研究文
獻中學習生產與六個標準差似乎有被取代的趨勢。
(6) 生產與運送同步化
結合生產與配送排程是達成將最終商品送交顧客的
一個相當重要的課題。 Ready Mixed Concrete
(RMC) 生產系統是結合工廠的物料及送至消費者
的最終產品的系統。 Fong 及 Piplani 學者針對
RMC 生產系統提出一個彈性作業排程方法, 以促使
生產與運送同步化． 此方法首先依據具體的運送容量
建立數學模型。 其模型中考慮多停靠點及時間限制。
Fong 及 Piplani 學者以 Branch-and-cut 演算法
求解。 其研究結果顯示即使是大型問題, 透過強力分
枝 (Strong Branching) 及最適評估法 (Best EW-
stimate rules) 皆可搜尋到很好的可行解。

Since a firm may use pricing strategy to spur demand. The inventory problems with price
and stock dependent demand cannot be ignored. Urban and Baker (1997) investigated a
deterministic inventory problem in which demand is a multivariate function of price, time
and inventory level. Their basic model with a single price is extended to a model with a
single price markdown.
It is noted that the literature herein rarely consider the cases with multiple price reduction
when demand is price and stock dependent. Since a firm may periodically reduce its sales
price to spur demand when consumers’ purchasing behavior are price and stock dependent,
this paper studies a single replenishment inventory model to deal with this problem. It is
assumed that a firm periodically reduce the sales price at a predetermined discount amount
under the condition that (1) the demand rate for the item may influence by price and
customers may expect a further price reduction when firms reduce sales price, and (2) the
demand rate is inventory level dependent. The purpose of this paper is to develop the
solution procedure for determining the optimal order size, optimal timing of price resetting
and the initial sales price.

4 Conclusion
This paper studied the times of pricing setting and ordering problem for an inventory system
under the condition that demand is linear price and stock dependent. Numerous inventory
models have addressed this problem. However, these models have rarely considered a situa-
tion in which the intersection of demand curve may move down, the selling period and the
times of price changes are controllable. Tacking these phenomena into account, this paper
developed a continuous-time model to deal with this problem. The times of price changes,
ordering quantity and initial sales price are jointly derived by a simple heuristic approach
studied in this paper.
