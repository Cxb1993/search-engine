Abstract 
   This study is to test in the Lab for 
the relationship between the consistency 
of debris flow and the retaining ability 
by check dams. These tests in the Lab to 
simulate the debris flows in situ, using 
equal-weight method and changing the 
content of fine-textured, with the 
concrete slit dams, steel grid dams and 
gravity dams. To measure and observe 
the conditions of different soils flowing, 
pile up, and the impulse to the dams; 
further, to analyze the result of three 
kinds of dams and discuss the effects of 
blocking and pile-up of different dams 
for debris consistency and ratio of 
fine-textured soil. 
         
Keywords: debris flow, detention rate, 
grain separation effect, 
fine-textured content
