摘要 
本研究目的在研發一種符合綠建材的新複合材料(軟木-石膏)並探討其防火性能。材料
改良方法係將軟木、蛋殼(碳酸鈣)及石膏等不同組合成分加壓成形。研究乃藉由材料的製作
與實驗的測試，在兼顧防火與材料需求特性下改良軟木裝修材料之合宜的應用價值並針對
不同組成的改良材料分別作比較及探討其使用效益。研究中運用 CNS 7332 與 ASTM E1354
試驗標準，圓錐量熱儀以 50kW/m2 輻射熱通量加熱，探討各種試片之熱傳導係數、吸水率、
剝離程度、熱釋放率性質、煙氣產生率及其著火性改善效應。研究討論部分，先針對綠建
材研發過程進行描述，以了解各種實驗變因對材料性能差異之影響程度；其次探討改良後
複合材料之材料性能改變程度與防火影響程度。研究成果顯示材料的基本性能及熱釋放
率、平均熱釋放率、熱釋放率峰值、總熱釋放率及平均有效燃燒熱、煙氣產生率及著火時
間等燃燒因子均因材料之組成成份改變而有所差異。綜合綠建材研發與性能改善結果以作
為未來室內裝修材料發展及火災危害度分析之參考。 
關鍵詞：綠建材、熱傳導係數、防火性能、軟木-石膏、熱釋放率 
 
Abstract 
   The purposes of this study are to analyze the fire performance of a new green building 
material and composite material, cork-gypsum composite. This study will investigate the 
manufacturing conditions and forming properties of mixing cork particle, eggshell powder, 
gypsum and water by using high pressure process. It is based on improving the suitable 
application value of fire safety/material demand characteristics in cork-gypsum composite 
materials. These characteristics will be determined and compared with other materials of different 
compositions. As a result of the additional analysis of the use benefit has been generated. We try 
to use CNS 7332 and ASTM E1354 test methods. Test methods were used to measure the 
material properties, heat release rate, smoke production rate and ignition time of the specimens at 
50 kW/m2 radiant power. This study will describe the procedure by which the composite 
materials have been developed and the difference of performance concerning the relative effects 
on testing variables. The results existed significant differences with changing material 
compositions. In addition, the fire safety effects and material performance changes of modified 
composite material will be discussed. The development results from the development of green

六、結論與建議 
    本研究中，藉由多孔性廢棄物研製綠建材經由 ASTM E1354 標準，在圓錐量熱儀下以
50kW/m2 輻射熱通量進行實驗，獲得各種不同成份組成之試片燃燒性，熱釋放率及著火性
分析用於評估其阻燃效果。結果顯示增加碳酸鈣粉成份於軟木—石膏板中，其燃燒特性優
於軟木比例高之板材，可見碳酸鈣粉之阻燃效果能明顯改善。 
   此外，研究結果亦藉由各試片的基本性能分析，以明確得知各種不同組成材料間之燃
燒行為與基本性能之關係，以作為研發與未來火災危害度分析及材料發展之參考。未來研
究可針對更多裝修材料進行研發，使裝修材料之燃燒特性更具研究價值，並能比較不同組
成裝修材料間之差異，進而研擬相關改善措施以提供業界設計與施工之參考。 
七、計畫成果自評 
本研究成果已完成預期之工作項目，研究中除了順利研製軟木－石膏裝修板，並使用
ASTM E1354 標準在圓錐量熱儀下取得多項試材之燃燒性資料外，本研究並對試片進行基
本性能測試，針對試材燃燒性、燃燒時間與基本性能探討相對關係，進行試材整體性能之
評估，此法亦藉由各項試驗對試材成份比例之成果效益進行評估，大幅提昇實驗佐證訊息
及試材防火性能之優劣比較，完成了整體分析比較，以選擇較佳之研發材料組成。如前所
述，本研究成果與當初預期目標相當符合，唯因經費之限制，無法延伸其他材料性能之測
試研究，未來亦將秉持現有研究成果，持續對材料應有之其他性能進行改善分析研究，研
究過程中發現研製的新材料確實達到當初預期之效果，並在研讀相關文獻中瞭解了更多相
關研究方法與材料研製，預期此將有助於未來的研究動能與思考邏輯，這些研究收穫與理
論論述將列為本研究之後續研究目標，並進行研究方向之規劃作業。本研究中所研發之軟
木－石膏材料防火效益，目前已完稿，擬發表於國際學術期刊。本研究之主要研究架構範
圍之相關成果，雖其研究方法常於期刊中被提出，然對於新材料的開發，尤其是符合環保
的綠建材研發實具研究應用價值。

Abstract 
This study will investigate the manufacturing conditions 
and forming properties of mixing cork particle, eggshell 
powder and adhesive by using high pressure process. 
We try to use ASTM E1354 test methods. Cone 
calorimeter was used to measure the heat release rate, 
smoke production rate and ignition time of the 
specimens at 50 kW/m2 radiant power. The results 
showed that the combustion factors of heat release rate, 
average heat release rate, heat release peak, total heat 
released, average effective combustion heat, smoke 
production rate and ignition time ,which do exist 
significant differences with changing material 
compositions. In addition, the fire safety effects and 
material performance changes of modified composite 
material will be discussed. 
Introduction 
The use of wooden materials in building has become 
prevalent in recent years. The important reasons for 
increasing the application value are the issue of green 
building material and the reduction of carbon dioxide 
for building’s design and construction. Cork products 
have been investigated for building application as 
acoustic and thermal insulation [1]. Its unique 
performance is just one part of the overall finishing 
material concern, but one that is not well understood 
with regard to how these material behave under 
ignitability and combustion properties. According to 
four combustion stages of indoor fire, the ignition stage 
is relatively important as compared to other for 
prevention. This fact significantly influenced overall 
finishing materials safety including the ignition time 
and fire-retardant behaviour. The necessity of fire 
performance becomes clear when considering the 
application of cork in building material [1]. It is clear 
that cork finishing materials have improved their 
features in order to achieve better fire performance. 
These features should include ignitability, flame spread, 
smoke generation property and rate of heat release 
[2,3,4].It is necessary to combine the porous material 
for keeping the original characteristic of cork. Previous 
studies have concluded that the eggshell is porous 
property from their micro-structure [5,6]. The 
incombustibility of eggshell powder is favorable when 
it is used as fire-retardant material. Cone calorimeter 
has been used to evaluate the fire performances in  
 
 
 
 
previous studies [7,8], the results showed that there is a 
close relation with the full-scale test in experimental 
data. Therefore, the objective of this study is to 
investigate a new green building material to provide the 
manufacturing conditions and forming properties for 
cork-eggshell composite. 
Materials and methods  
Preparation and pre-treatment of materials: 
Cork was obtained from a processing factory of the cork. 
It was first dried in sunlight and then cut into small 
pieces. The cork particle was screened to prepare 20 
mesh size particles (≒1mm). The cork particles was 
further dried at 50  in an oven for a period of 24 hours ℃
before use. Collect and wash waste eggshell from 
market. The dissolution of eggshell membrane was 
treated with 5% NaOH at a liquid to solid ratio of 20:1, 
70-80 , for 30℃  min. The pure eggshell was grinded to 
pass a screen of mesh size 50~100 and 200 for 
experiment.

The results of ignition time, maximum rate of heat

to peak in all test materials were described in Table.3. It 
was generally acknowledged that the CO didn’t the 
main composition bring about death, but still influenced 
people’s life index [17]. The peak heat release rate is 
believed by many fire scientists to be the major 
determinant of the onset of flashover in the real fire 
situation [13]. Furthermore, the longer ignition time, the 
material would be better [18 ]. From the observations 
above, it was clear that a lower ratio of maximum of 
heat release rate and CO production to the sum of 
ignition time and relative time to peak are expected to 
be good building material. Fig5 shows the ratio of 
HRRmax/t(kw/s ㎡) with different test samples. As can 
been seen from Fig.5, the sample D was relatively lower 
among the seven test materials. This means if a sample 
is suspected of having a larger particle size and more 
content for eggshell powder, the lower the value of the 
sample will provide a better fire-retardant and fire 
performance. The behavior in terms of CO production 
with CO maximum/time ratio is presented in Fig.6, 
lower value can be observed with samples D and G. It is 
clearly shown that the sample D had better fire 
performance than the others according to the higher 
HRRmax/t ratio from Fig.5.Based on the data presented 
in this paper, it is very important for further 
manufacturing process to produce such products with 
better fire performance and enhance fire-retardant in 
cork finishing materials. It can raise the ignition time, or 
reduce the carbon monoxide concentration to prevent 
fire spread and improve fire safety.

Conclusions 
In this study, fire performances of seven kinds of 
cork-eggshell finishing materials were determined by 
ASTM E1354 from small specimen of materials. Some 
combustion analysis, heat release rate, smoke 
production and fire performances of porous waste 
application with different content and particle size were 
investigated. Two ratio criterions were evaluated as 
applied to these materials: HRRmax/t and COmax/t. 
The results showed that higher eggshell powder 
contents and larger particle size would be favorable to 
the fire-retardant characteristics of materials. It was also 
observed that the other properties were decided on both.
