摘       要 
本研究探討利用奈米金屬-活性碳(NM-GAC)處理廢水中腐植物質與重金屬之可行
性。本研究製備奈米鐵-活性碳(NF-GAC)、奈米鋅-活性碳(NZ-GAC)及奈米鋁-活性碳
(NA-GAC)等三種新型吸附劑，SEM 結果顯示有明顯金屬微粒覆膜在活性碳表面。三種吸
附劑中以 NA-GAC 對鉛之吸附量最高，NF-GAC 則是對銅及鎘吸附量最高。NM-GACs 對
不同金屬離子的吸附能力均依序為 Pb(II) > Cu(II)> Cd(II)，本研究也探討雙成份重金屬競爭
吸附現象，親和力大小依序為 Cu(II) > Pb(II) > Cd(II)。 
水中含 Cu(II)離子時，有助於腐植酸(HA)與磺酸(FA)之吸附，但是對非腐植物質(NHS)
卻相對吸附降低。當水中有 HA 存在時，對 Cu(II)之吸附量影響不大；但是 FA 與 NHS 則
對 Cu(II)吸附量有相當助益，可能是形成 FA-Cu(II)與 NHS-Cu(II)錯合物，而此錯合物可吸
附於 NM-GACs 上。本研究也利用 Langmuir、Freundlich、IAST models 與 Sips 對以上各雙
成份實驗結果加以模擬，其中以 IAST model 之模擬結果為最佳。 
 
關鍵字：奈米金屬-活性碳、吸附、腐植物質、重金屬、活性碳 
 
Abstract 
 
The purpose of this research is trying to investigate using nano metal-granular activated 
carbon (NM-GAC) for removal of humic substance and heavy metals from wastewater. Three 
NM-GACs were prepared in this research, namely, nano Fe-GAC (NF-GAC), nano Zn-GAC 
(NZ-GAC) and nano Al-GAC (NA-GAC). Among these MN-GACs, NA-GAC has highest Pb(II) 
adsorption capacity and NF-GAC has better adsorption for Cu(II) and Cd(II). The heavy metal 
adsorption capacity for various NM-GAC are Pb(II) > Cu(II)> Cd(II). In binary metal systems, 
the sequence of affinity is Cu(II) > Pb(II) > Cd(II). 
The present of Cu(II) improved the adsorption amount of humic acid(HA) and fulvic 
acid(FA), but reduced the adsorption amount of non-humic substance (NHS). The present of HA 
did not increase the Cu(II) removal, but the present of FA and NHS increase the removal amount 
of Cu(II). This might due to the formation of FA-Cu(II) and NHS-Cu(II) complex, and this 
complex was favored to be adsorbed.  
Among four binary models (Langmuir、Freundlich、IAST models and Sips) investigated in 
this research, IAST was the best fit model. 
 
Keywords: nano metal-activated carbon, adsorption, humic substance, heavy metals

(3).等溫吸附曲線(adsorption isotherm tests) 
本實驗之目的在於研究 NM-GAC 對不同腐植物質或重金屬(含 Pb(II), Cu(II)與
Cd(II))之吸附能力。並探討其相互間之交互(競爭/干擾或增進)吸附作用。實驗簡述如

置於滾筒（shaker）中充分反應混合，並分別於不同時間，取 5 mL 水樣並測其濃度。

et al., 2002 and Newcombe et al.,2002)。各吸附模式簡述如下:

五、結果與討論 
NM-GAC 表面特性分析 
本研究所製備之 NM-GAC 以掃描式電子顯微鏡(SEM)分析。圖 1(a)為表面未覆膜
任何金屬之活性碳，表面相對比較光滑，圖 1(b)-(d)依序 NF-GAC、NZ-GAC 與
NA-GAC。SEM 結果顯示，有金屬微粒明顯附著於活性碳表面上。另外從 EDS 分析
結果顯示 NF-GAC、NZ-GAC 及 NA-GAC 所覆膜金屬元素重量百分比分別為 47.6 
(Fe)、48.9 (Zn)與 6.1 (Al)%。 
 
重金屬吸附實驗 
(1)單成分吸附實驗 
本研究分別針對不同吸附劑（GAC、NF-GAC、NZ-GAC 與 NA-GAC）對 Cu(II)、
Pb(II)與 Cd(II)進行吸附實驗（實驗初始濃度為 4 mg/L，pH 6）。實驗結果顯示於圖 2，
NM-GAC吸附量均高於GAC:活性碳表面覆膜奈米金屬能有效增加GAC對金屬離子吸
附能力。以 Cu(II)為例，不同吸附劑之吸附量依序為 NF-GAC＞NZ-GAC＞NA-GAC＞
GAC。當Cu(II)平衡濃度為 2 mg/L時，NM-GAC吸附量約為GAC的 2倍。不同NM-GAC
對 Pb(II)與 Cd(II)吸附量依序為 NA-GAC＞NF-GAC＞NZ-GAC＞GAC。 
NF-GAC 對 Cu(II)、Pb(II)與 Cd(II)吸附量依序為 Pb(II)＞Cu(II)＞Cd(II) (圖 3(a))。
NA-GAC、NZ-GAC 與 GAC 也有類似吸附傾向(圖 3(b)-(d ))。Li et al. (2003) 研究
Pb(II)、Cu(II)與 Cd(II)在奈米碳管上的競爭吸附與 Lv et al. (2005)研究 Pb(II)、Cu(II)與
Cd(II)在 ETS-10 之競爭吸附也有類似情形。 
 
(2)重金屬雙成分吸附實驗 
本研究也探討雙成份重金屬吸附現象。分別探討 NF-GAC、NZ-GAC、NA-GAC
與 GAC 對銅-鎘、銅-鉛及鎘-鉛三種系統(pH 值為 6)之競爭吸附情形。圖 4 為 NF-GAC
於雙成份金屬吸附實驗結果，圖 4(a)-(b)顯示於 Cu(II)-Cd(II)與 Cu(II)-Pb(II)系統中，
Cu (II)是最易被吸附之物種，Pb(II)及 Cd(II)之吸附量較低。Pb(II)-Cd(II)系統中，Pb(II)
之被吸附量較 Cd(II)為高(圖 4(c))。NZ-GAC 與 GAC 吸附情形與 NF-GAC 類似。此一
結果與 Li et al. (2003)、Lv et al. (2005)與 Qin et al. (2006)研究結果一致。 
Extended-Langmuir、Extended-Freundlich、Sips 及 IAST 等吸附模式結果列於(圖
5-7)，各模式中以 IAST 模式為最佳。 
 
有機物與重金屬吸附實驗 
本研究亦探討(NF-GAC)針對腐植酸(HA)、磺酸(FA)與非腐植物質(NHS)與重金屬
吸附實驗。以 NF-GAC 為例，HA 對 Cu(II)之吸附影響不大(圖 8)。但是 FA 則對 Cu(II)
之吸附量有正面效益，Cu(II)吸附量因為 FA 之存在而有數倍之增加(圖 10)。 
水中 Cu(II)離子存在有助於 HA 與 FA 之吸附，其結果分別顯示於圖 8-11 中。此一
結果顯示，水中同時存在 HA 與 Cu(II)時，對 HA 吸附量有助益，但是對 Cu(II)之吸附
量影響不大。當 FA 與 Cu(II)同時存在時，則二者之吸附量均有增加。可能是 FA 與 Cu(II)
二者形成錯合物後，此一新錯合物可吸附於吸附劑上。Cu(II)與 NHS 共存時，Cu(II)
吸附量增加，但是 NHS 吸附量卻相對較少(圖 12-13)。

SIMULTANEOUS DETERMINATION OF ADSORPTION EQUILIBRIUM 
PARAMETERS FOR BINARY-COMPONENT SYSTEMS 
 
1Huan-Jung Fan, 2Kohei Satoh, 1Hsin-Sin Yang, 3Eiji Furuya,  
 
1Department of Environmental Engineering, Hungkuang University, Taichung, Taiwan  
Email: fan@sunrise.hk,edu.tw 
 
2Research and Development Section, Organo Co. Ltd., Tokyo, Japan. 
 
3Department of Industrial Chemistry, Meiji University, Kawasaki, Japan. 
 
 
 
ABSTRACT 
 
Several methods have been proposed to estimate equilibrium 
parameters of the adsorption of multi-component onto 
activated carbon.  For example, Myers and Prausnits 
presented the Ideal Adsorbed Solution (IAS) theory for 
gaseous mixtures. Radke and Prausnits extended this 
method to dilute liquid solutions. Suzuki et al. applied IAS 
theory for Freundlich-type equilibrium systems for 
estimating multi-component equilibrium parameters.  
Furthermore, Furuya et al. used Suzuki’s technique to 
develop a method to determine the equilibrium parameters 
in multi-component aqueous samples as well. 
However, there is a major limitation of above methods when 
dealing with real wastewaters.  Industrial wastewater 
normally has only a fixed concentration ratio among 
different contaminates. And the ratio, unfortunately, can not 
be changed in most of the cases, because pure agents of 
these dissolved substances in wastewater are unavailable.  
Therefore, the methods mentioned above would not be 
useful for the estimation of adsorption capacity of adsorbent.  
There is a need to develop a method for simultaneous 
determination of equilibrium parameters from wastewater 
with a fixed concentration ratio among contaminates. 
Therefore, the main purpose of this paper is to investigate 
and verify the feasibility of simultaneous determination of 
adsorption equilibrium parameters for multi-component 
aqueous mixtures.  
Simultaneous determination of adsorption equilibrium 
parameters for individual adsorbate from binary phenolic 
compounds mixtures was proposed and investigated in this 
research.  Simulated wastewater samples composed of 
binary phenolic compounds were prepared and then diluted 
at different ratios with DDW.  Adsorption capacity tests of 
the diluted samples were performed under different 
adsorbent-liquid ratios.  Equilibrium concentrations of 
phenolic compounds were determined from UV absorbance.  
The Freundlich isotherm parameters were determined from 
Binary component adsorption data using the analysis 
technique developed in this research.  Experimental results 
indicated that values of Freundlich parameters obtained 
from binary component data were consisted of the values 
obtained from single component equilibrium data.  The 
method developed in this research would be useful for future 
application in wastewater treatment processes. 
  
Keywords: Binary component, IAS theory, Freundlich 
equation, parameter determination 
  
1 INTRODUCTION 
 
Adsorption equilibrium parameters are required for 
designing an adsorption process. For single component 
adsorption, adsorption isotherms such as Freundlich, 
Langmuir and Sips can be obtained through relatively 
simple bottle points experiments (Fan et al., 2005, Tseng et 
al., 2003).  However, most of the wastewaters contained 
more than one pollutant.  Therefore, the use of the single 
component isotherm is fairly limited.  Therefore, the 
methods to estimate individual component adsorption 
isotherm from wastewaters containing multiple components 
are important for field application.  Several useful models 
have been developed to describe the equilibrium adsorption 
of multiple organic compounds using activated carbon in 
aqueous systems.  For example, Frits-Schlünder’s (Frits et 
al., 1974) and Markham-Benton’s (Markham, 1931) 
equations based on Freundlich- and Langmuir-type 
adsorption, respectively, were developed and they are 
relatively simple to apply.  Equilibrium constants of the 
equations can be determined from experimental values for 
binary component systems (Frits et al., 1974, Furuya et al., 
1981).  However, in most cases, values of isotherm 
constants obtained from binary component systems were not 
equal to those for single component adsorption. 
Therefore, several methods were proposed to estimate multi-
component adsorption data by use of the single component 
adsorption isotherms.  For example, Langmuir extended 
model, Freundlich extended model and Ideal Adsorbed 
Solution Theory (IAST) were proposed to predict binary 
component adsorption systems using known single 
adsorption isotherms (Radke et al., 1972,  Suzuki et al. 1973, 
Furuya et al., 1986 , Al-Asheh et al.,2000 and Tseng et al., 
2003).  However, there is a major limitation of these 
methods, especially when dealing with real wastewaters, 
because these binary adsorption models would require 
known single component adsorption isotherms to predict 
adsorption capacities of each component in the binary 
system.  To obtain the single component adsorption 
isotherms, ones will have to prepare various initial 
concentration solutions with pure agents of the pollutants.
