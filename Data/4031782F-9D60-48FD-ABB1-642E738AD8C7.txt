中文摘要
本計畫已順利完成以小型高效率
批式堆肥槽，探討合成食品廢棄物中蛋
白質與脂肪等成份變化對堆肥過程的
影響計畫目標。
應用多變量迴歸分析，可以將堆肥
時間、酸化時間、最高溫度、最低 pH
值、最終 pH 值、揮發物、碳、氮損失
等主要堆肥結果與蛋白質與脂肪的含
量關係，以二次方程式表示。堆肥時間
直接與蛋白質與脂肪含量有關，蛋白質
含量愈多，堆肥時間愈短，脂肪含量愈
多，則堆肥時間愈長。此模式已成功地
預測實際廚餘堆肥化結果。
機械式堆肥系統細部設計雖已完
成，但自走部分機件設計與製作不良，
仍在改善中。
英文摘要
The effects of composition on the
food waste composting were studied.
Synthetic food waste made of human and
animal foods such as rice, wheat, fish
meal and soy meal was composted in a
200-liter laboratory composter.
Experimental results showed that the
composting rate and time were directly
affected by the protein and fat contents.
The more the protein content was, the
shorter the composting time was. The
more the fat content was, the longer the
composting time was. Muliti-variate
regression analysis and reaction curve
methodology were applied to illustrate
the relationship of the results and the
composition. The models were tested on
real kitchen food waste, and the results
were satisfactory.
The prototype of the mechanized
composting system is designed, but the
automatic moving part still needs
improvement.
