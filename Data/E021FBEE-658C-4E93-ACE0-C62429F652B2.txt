出色。因此，研發高導電率的鋰氧化物離子導體是相當具有探討價值的主題。 
 
2. 研究動機與目的 
    現今的離子導體發展之中，液態離子導體雖技術成熟且已商業化但仍存在電解液
易外漏且易發生化學反應的缺點，而之後發展的固態高分子離子導體其在離子導電性
方面也一直未能有所突破，因而發展出了無機固態之離子導體，此類離子導體具較強
之抗震性，亦不會因衝擊而有電解液洩漏的危險，其化學穩定性也較佳和電極間的接
面不會發生反應，在薄型化的程度上元件可達到比固態高分子離子導體更低的厚度。
因此，無機固態離子導體開始受到重視，是現今一種相當具有研究價值的主題。 
本實驗針對非晶玻璃系統離子導體中的鋰氧化物離子導體進行研究，因其在空氣
下之穩定性較好不易受潮變質，較符合應用上的需求。實驗採用 RF-磁控濺鍍法分別
對三種參數的靶材 ( Li2O : GeO2 : V2O5 = 1 : 2 : 1、1.5 : 2 : 1、2 : 2 : 1 ) 來進行薄膜的
沉積，並採用Ar為濺鍍氣體、N2為反應氣體，設定5種反應氣體與濺鍍氣體之比例 ( 全
Ar、N2/Ar = 0.5、1、1.5、2 )，藉此實驗設定探討靶材 Li 含量與反應氣體 N2對於本實
驗製備之薄膜離子導體之影響並探討最佳之參數。

patterns showed that all the deposited films were 
amorphous and no crystalline peaks were detected except 
for the film deposited at 100% RF portion of the total 
power. The attainment of minor crystalline ITO films 
deposited at 100% RF portion of the total power was 
related to high kinetic energy of the sputtered particles.

surface coupling with a secondary thermal effect could 
cause crystallization in these films. The variation in ion 
bombardment energy on the surface of growing film in 
term of discharge voltage upon RF implementation is 
characterized by different excitation mechanism between 
the DC and RF discharge modes. These intrinsic 
characteristics of the DC and RF discharge modes also 
lead to different microstructure developments.

The electrical resistivity, carrier concentration and 
Hall mobility of ITO films as a function of RF portion of 
the total power is plotted in Fig. 2. The electrical 
resistivity of the films almost linearly decreased from 
2.08 x 10 -3 to 4.58 x 10 -4 Ωcm with increasing the RF 
portion of the total power from 0 to 100%. The high 
energy flux arrived on the substrate increases with the 
increasing RF power portion and therefore assisted 
adatom mobility during film growth. More Sn ions 
originally located in grain boundary are supposed to 
dissolve into In atom sub-lattice sites and provided more 
extra free electrons. This leads to higher carrier 
concentration of 5.05x1020 cm-3 as films were deposited 
at 100% RF power. However, it is well known that 
electrical conductivity is not only dependent on the 
carrier concentration but also on the Hall mobility 
according to the following equation 
 
σ = 1/ρ = nqμ                                           
(1)   
 
Where, σ is electrical conductivity, ρ is electrical 
resistivity, n is carrier concentration, μ is carrier mobility 
and q is electrical charge. It is assumed that the grain 
boundary scattering is the predominating scattering 
mechanism, one would expect that the grain size affects 
the carrier mobility of the films. The increase in grain 
size allows the grain boundary scattering to decrease, 
thus raises the carrier mobility. As seen in Fig. 2 the 
carrier mobility increases with RF portion of total power 
from 20.8 up to 27.2 cm2V-1s-1. This is due to an 
improvement in grain size in these films as well as the
