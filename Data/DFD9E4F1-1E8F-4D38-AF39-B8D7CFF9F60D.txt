摘要 
 
本研究係研發一台銲接一般變壓器的多疊
層矽鋼片之高轉速且定負荷之摩擦攪拌銲接
設備。此設備係由銲接工具之轉動模組、擠壓
與退離模組以及多疊層矽鋼片工件之進給模
組等所組成。以一直徑 3 mm 碳化鎢圓棒的銲
接工具，在正向負荷(140 ~ 480 N)、主軸轉速
(12000 ~ 24000 rpm)及進給速度(0 ~ 1.58 
mm/s)的操作條件下，探討多疊層矽鋼片之銲
接特性與銲接機制，最後進行市售變壓器之銲
接。 
從長銲道的銲接實驗結果求出，表示正向
負荷(Fd)與主軸轉速(Ns)及進給速度(f)對最
大剪力(Ff)的影響之經驗公式，Ff = 40.6 
(Fd．Ns)1.123(f)-0.791。 (Fd．Ns)1.123(f)-0.791 表示
銲接工具在銲道上每單位移動距離之摩擦
功。此摩擦功的值愈大表示工件材料的生熱
愈高並且摩擦攪拌愈均勻，使工件的材料鍵
結強度增加，以致於銲道最大剪力愈大。 
從銲道橫截面的顯微組織照片觀察結果
得知，熱機影響區受銲接工具與銲道表面之
摩擦所產生的高熱作用，而呈現材料的塑性
流動，使疊層矽鋼片鍵結在一起。 
最後，成功銲接市售的變壓器，其品質符
合規範要求。並且提出銲接機及其夾持裝置
之發明專利。

Abstract 
 
A friction stir welding equipment with high 
rotation speed and constant load is successfully 
developed in this study to weld the multi - laminated 
silicon steel sheets widely used on regular 
transformers. This equipment consists of a spinning 
unit, a loading unit, and a feeding unit. A WC rod 
with 3 mm diameter is used as welding tool. Under 
different operating conditions, such as the normal 
load (140~480 N), the spindle speed (12000 ~ 24000 
rpm), the feeding rate (0 ~ 1.58 mm/s), the welding 
characteristics and the welding mechanism of 
multi-laminated silicon-steel sheets are investigated, 
and the commercial transformer is welded. 
The experimental conditions of the long - pass 
welding tests are selected to investigate the effects of 
normal load (Fd), the spindle speed (Ns), and the 
feeding rate (f) on the maximum shear load (Ff) of 
weld. According to the experimental results, the 
empirical formula is obtained as Ff = 40.6(Fd．
Ns)1.123(f)-0.791. In this formula, (Fd．Ns)1.123(f)-0.791 
means the frictional work per unit moving distance. 
With the larger frictional work, this represents the 
heat generation of the workpiece material is higher 
with more uniform friction stir, so that the bonding 
strength of the material increases and the the 
maximum shear load of weld is larger. 
According to the micrograph observations, the 
thermo - mechanically affected zone is significantly 
influenced by high heat action generated from the 
friction between the tool and the weld surface region, 
so that the plastic flow of the workpiece material 
occurs to cause the multi-laminated silicon steel 
sheets bonding together. 
The commercially available transformer is 
successfully welded, and its qualities fit the required 
specification. 
Keywords:  silicon steel sheets, friction stir welding, 
spindle speed, normal load, feeding rate 
 
 
一、 緒論 
 
摩擦攪拌銲接(Friction stir welding，簡稱
FSW)為一種固態接合技術。此技術的原理是
利用包含探針(Probe)及肩部(Shoulder)的攪拌
頭，亦稱銲接工具，以旋轉狀態的攪拌頭之探
針旋入欲對接銲件的接合線進行攪拌，並破壞
接合界面的氧化膜，且肩部對銲件持續施壓而
產生摩擦熱，使該銲件之接合面形成熱塑性半
熔融狀態，以進行擴散接合。與傳統電弧銲接

力計調到所需之壓力，最後再由進排氣控制閥
控制氣壓缸之作動，使高速主軸端的銲接工具

或正向負荷。
