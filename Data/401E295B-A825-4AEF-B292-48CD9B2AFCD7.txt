微型光纖電流感測器之設計 
Design of micro-type optical fiber current sensor

一、中文摘要 
由於電流之量測在自動化控制過程中
扮演極重要之角色，因此本計畫擬使用光
纖光柵設計微小型電流感測器。傳統電流
感測器皆以電訊號轉換為主，其缺點為必
須設計訊號轉換電路，使得感測器之體積
較為龐大，且量測微量變動之電流可靠度
較差，也容易受環境之電磁場影響使得量
測值產生誤差。光纖光柵之量測是利用光
之反射原理，較不受量測環境之電磁場干
擾，因此將應用光纖光柵為工具，利用光
纖光柵感測之較高靈敏度，配合電流流過
產生之磁場轉換成壓力，再將此壓力結合
壓力型光纖光柵感測器，經由光學儀器量
測光纖光柵波長之改變量，即可量測相對
之電流值。 
關鍵詞：電流感測器、布拉格光纖光柵 
 
Abstract 
Current measurement is an important part 
for automatic control process, the design of 
micro type current sensor using in-fiber 
bragg gratings (FBG) is proposed in this 
project. 
Electric signal conversion is used for the 
traditional current sensors. The main 
drawback is the electric signal conversion 
circuit is necessary for such equipment. This 
kind of sensor is large and low reliability 
and the measurement error is suffering the 
electric magnetic field of the environment. 
FBG is measured by the reflection of light 
and can avoid the electric magnetic field of 
the environment. The higher sensitivity in- 
fiber bragg gratings is used for the design of 
current sensor. The pressure produced by the 
magnetic field which is induced by the 
current flow is applied to the pressure type 
in-fiber bragg gratings sensor. The change 
of current is obtained by the change of 
wavelength measured by optic equipment. 
Keywords ： current sensor , in-fiber bragg 
gratings 
 
二、緣由與目的 
本計畫擬利用光纖光柵獨有之光學特
性並結合封裝結構的特殊設計以測量電流
的大小，由於光纖的發明與應用，使得傳
統利用銅導線做為傳輸工具及電子元件做
為感測器的方式有了重大的變革。因光纖
感測器被充分運用，也可達成如氣體，液
體的流量、壓力與溫度等量測。於是，對
於光纖光柵感測器的研究，將是步向全光
路監測系統實用化的一目標。因其具有質
量輕、耐久性、不易被腐蝕和不受干擾的
優點，而且其具有低損耗高效能的傳輸能
力，現在已經被廣泛運用在各個通訊網路
裡。利用光纖光柵完成之感測器，近十年
來亦已經成為在感測器領域上最受重視的
理論之一。 
相較於傳統機電式的感測器，由於其
纖細質輕、易於多工使用、且作為感測依
據的布拉格反射波長不會受到環境因素的
影響，因此可應用的範圍十分廣泛，如：
橋樑、水壩、電力工業等方面的非破壞性
結構感測，以及醫療、環保等方面。民生
與國防武器系統屬於動態的大型載台如船
艇、飛機等有關其操作系統中壓力、溫度、
流量等等之感測，目前均以電式感測器為
主。為了有效降低電磁波、電磁脈衝、環
境溫度、機械衝擊等等因素對其感測功能

1. 以 FLUX 2D/3D 軟體分析電流通過系統
所產生之磁場分布求得感測器最佳放置位
置。 
2. 在壓力感測器之感測點放置磁鐵，利用
同極性相排斥異極性相吸引之原理產生推
力與拉力，此力量將會對壓力感測器產生
作用，以精密光學儀器量測，可以得到待
測導線流過之電流。 
3. 量測電流流過所產生之磁力對光纖
光柵壓力感測器所產生之壓力與光纖
光柵因應力產生之波長變化之關係，如
果波長變化與電流變動呈線性關係，即
可以波長變化估測電流變動，此研究裝
置便可以當作電流計使用，配合光纖光
柵之靈敏度應可量測微小電流變動。

最後藉由電流大小所產生的磁力變化來影
響磁石，進而拉動光纖光柵，使中心波長
產生位移情形，即可觀察光纖光柵中心波
長位移與電流變化情況。

Measurement of AC current using an optical fibre sensor 
C. H. Cheng*, M. H. Chen, and W. F. Liu 
Department of Electrical Engineering, Feng Chia University, No.100, Wen Hwa Rd., Taichung 407,Taiwan 
Received zzz, revised zzz, accepted zzz

PACS 42.15.Eq, 42.81.Cn, 74.25.Gz, 74.25.Ha, 76.60.Jx 
In the power industry, current is measured for metering and protection purposes. For said measurement, we demonstrated 
an optical fibre sensor using a single Fibre Bragg Grating (FBG) for measuring AC current for this paper. The sensor head 
is based on an FBG encased in a polymer-half-field metal cylinder, embedded in a magnetic material at the measuring 
point with the characteristics of an all-optical high pressure sensitivity. The operating mechanism is that the sensor can be 
attracted by the induced magnetic force created by the solenoid along one radial direction only, and responds to an axial 
force on the magnetic rod attached to the round plate, creating an axial attraction on the FBG.

1 Introduction 
Optical fiber gratings are being used as sensors in a number of physical magnitudes, such as temperature, strain and 
pressure [1-2]; other magnitudes, such as electrical or magnitude fields can be detected by fiber gratings using the 
appropriate transducers [3]. In principle a fiber grating can detect magnetic fields through the Faraday Effect, and this 
mechanism is ideal because it does not need an external transducer [4]. 
Through the years a number of different optical systems for voltage applications have been presented but the main field 
of interest have been devices that can replace conventional voltage- and current-transformers [5] utilizing the 
nonconductive nature of the optical components. As optical measuring systems tend to be much smaller and lighter than 
their conventional equivalents it is possible with this technique possible to produce portable equipment [6-7]. 
In this paper, we demonstrate a new approach to the measuring processes of a AC current. The magnetic field of this 
sensor is created from the solenoid. The magnitude of the magnetic field in the solenoid can be measured the traditional 
way by utilizing the Faraday Effect. The design configuration of the sensor is based on our previous publication with a 
pressure sensitivity of 12102.2 −−× aMP as shown in Fig. 1 [8]. The positive pressure sensitivity of this sensor is 
obtained from the attracted force, created by the interaction of the forces between the magnetic field in the solenoid and 
the magnetic rod on the end of sensor, to be transferred by the axial attraction on the FBG.

This sensor has been demonstrated to have the wide range of dynamic measuring current capability. The sensor head, 
based on a FBG has good linear properties and can be applied to monitor the Bragg wavelength shifts caused by the 
magnetic forces created by the current in the solenoid. The proposed scheme for measuring current is a simple and 
compact configuration and can provide a portable current sensor for  an AC current measurement.
