一、中文摘要 
 
本研究以中心波長 800 nm、頻率 1 
kHz、脈衝時間 120 fs 的飛秒雷射，在不同
基材上加工次微米結構，並配合理論數值
模擬，了解雷射單一脈衝產生加工截面的
破裂形狀。主要加工材料為光阻 SU8、
AZ4620 與( 1 0 0 )、( 1 1 0 )、( 1 1 1 )三種
不同晶向之矽基材，透過不同雷射參數調
整，觀察與比較結構成形結果。另外，以
理論數值模擬雷射脈衝在矽基材內電子隨
聚焦位置、脈衝時間與深度的傳遞與分布
情形，與實際加工結果比較，相互映證。
根據模擬與單一脈衝加工孔洞之實驗結
果，本研究進一步將超快雷射應用於金屬
鎳之表面為結構加工，成功製作結構週期
約 100 μm、深度 37μm 之三角微結構。 
關鍵詞：飛秒雷射、SU8、矽、次微米結
構 
  
Abstract 
 
Sub-Micron-structure machining on 
silicon substrate is studied by direct writing 
system of femtosecond laser with the central 
wavelength of 800 nm, pulse duration of 120 
fs and repetition rate of 1 kHz.  Three kinds 
of experiments are studied: (1) the effect of 
photoresists on silicon; (2) machinability of 
different orientations of silicon; and (3) the 
size of micro-structure and the cross-section 
shape.  Photoresists SU8 and AZ4620 were 
coated onto silicon substrates in thicknesses 
of 100 μm and 30 μm specifically.  SU8 
remained on the silicon substrate while the 
silicon under the machined SU8 was 
removed after laser machining. AZ4620 and 
the substrate under exposed area were 
removed after laser machining. Orientations 
of (100), (110), and (111) silicon substrates 
were machined with the laser power of 60 
μW and the scanning speed of 3 mm/min.  
Spike morphologies were observed on all 
three orientations of silicon substrates 
without obvious directional difference of 
these spikes on different silicon substrates. 
The smallest hole structure with diameter of 
400 nm was obtained by single pulse at laser 
power of 60 μW.  In addition, the ablation 
threshold energies were also similar.  For 
the consideration of ablation energy, one 
numerical model of the machining 
parameters has been proposed to simulate the 
cross-section of the micro-structure.  The 
predicted shape by simulation can fit the 
profile of the cross-section shape well. Base 
on the hole structure machined by single 
pulse, the whole triangle-microstructure with 
the width of 100 μm and depth of 37 μm can 
be fabricated on metal Ni. 
Keywords: femtosecond laser、SU8、Si、
sub-micro structure

求，因此如何在相同元件上針對不同波長
製作不同特性的結構是一項重要的製程技
