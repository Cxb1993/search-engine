尺寸變小，屆時通過銲點的電流密度(current density)可能高達 104 A/cm2 以上。在此電流密
度下，電遷移(electromigration)效應對銲點可靠度之影響將變的相當重要。在目前發表的失
效機制中，皆是在銲點晶片端之電子流入口處，電遷移導致的孔洞（void）迅速生成，對
銲點可靠度造成影響。事實上，此區域除了銲料外還包含了一相當薄的 UBM（under bump
metallization）層，在銲點的可靠度中亦扮演相當重要的角色。本研究目的即在探討電遷移
效應對 Ni UBM 消耗及銲點失效機制兩者間的相互影響。實驗結果發現，晶片端靠近電子
流入口端處，局部 Ni UBM 被加速消耗。當局部的 Ni UBM 被消耗殆盡後，接著一多孔狀
結構在原來 Ni UBM 位置形成，因此電子流無法繼續通過此處。本實驗發現 Ni UBM 消耗
主導了銲點失效的機制即失效時間。因此擁有較厚的 Ni UBM 的覆晶銲點，有較長的通電
壽命。

近幾年來，覆晶（flip-chip）封裝技術已經成為高階電子元件封裝的最佳選擇。〝覆晶
封裝〞其名乃是一種藉由翻轉晶片並以錫球直接與基板作連接的方法而來。因此 I/O 可以
分布在整各晶片的表面，藉由此特性晶片尺寸可以大幅縮小。
然而，隨著元件尺寸的縮小，銲點的尺寸跟著變小。由於流經銲點的電流不變，因此
銲點中的電流密度將快速增加，而電遷移所導致的銲點失效將成為銲點可靠度重要的考量
[30-33]。在覆晶封裝的元件中，電流密度在導電線路中並非均勻分布。電子流由內導線流
入銲點時，電流密度將由高電流密度轉變為低電流密度。如此的電流密度轉變導致電子流
入口處產生嚴重的 current crowding 現象。此一現象將在覆晶銲點的失效機制中相當重要並
且決定了失效機制 [4-10, 32]。根據文獻的報導， current crowding 效應所導致失效的機制
目前有兩種。其中一個機制為孔洞的生成與發展機制[32]。另一個機制則是導致 Cu UBM
的快速 dissolution 造成銲點失效[7-10]。一般而言，與銲料反應時 Ni 抵抗 dissolution 能力
較 Cu 為佳。然而，覆晶銲點中 UBM 所使用的 Ni 膜通常小於 1 m ，因此，Ni UBM 的消
耗仍然是可靠度重要的考量之一。此外，銲點中的 current crowding 效應也可能加快局部的
Ni UBM 消耗。

3.2 Ni UBM consumption and the porous structure 
Figure 4 (a) showed that only the part of the Ni UBM near the entrance of the electrons had 
been consumed. Meanwhile, the Ni UBM at other location was still intact.  No Ni could be 
detected in those Cu6Sn5 beneath the intact Ni UBM. When the stressing time reached 300 hours, all 
the Ni UBM had been completely consumed and Ni could be detected in all (Cu1-xNix)6Sn5, as 
shown in Fig. 4 (b). The simultaneous disappearance of the Ni layer and the appearance of Ni in 
(Cu1-xNix)6Sn5 suggests that the dissolution of Ni into Cu6Sn5 was the main consumption 
mechanism for the Ni layer. At this stage, the so-called porous structure [15-16] was limited to the 
right side of the UBM, but after 400 hours of current stressing, as shown in Fig. 4(c), the porous 
structure had extended across the entire UBM.

4.2 Failure mechanism and the MTTF 
The void formation-and-propagation mechanism was reported to be a major failure 
mechanism in several different stressing conditions [2, 3, 10-12]. In this mechanism, a void forms 
in the solder at the location with the highest local current density, and the electrons are diverted 
around the void, making the solder next to the edge of the void become the highest current density 
region. This causes the void to grow toward the highest current density region. This process repeats 
itself, and the void gradually propagates until an open circuit is created. However, in this study, the 
role of void was replaced by the porous structure, which presumably was non-conductive. Once the 
porous structure formed, the electrons were diverted to nearby region, and caused the porous 
structure to extend toward that region. The joints failed when the porous structure extended all the 
way across the joint. In the void formation-and-propagation mechanism, one key factor determining 
the MTTF is the incubation time for the void nucleation [11]. Once the void nucleates, it 
propagation can cause the joint failure very quickly. In the Ni UBM consumption mechanism 
observed in this study, the key factor determining the MTTF was the time required to consume a 
certain thickness of the Ni UBM. As can be seen in Fig. 2, the thicker the Ni UBM, the longer was 
the MTTF.  
The role of the porous structure in the Ni UBM consumption mechanism was similar to the 
role played by the void in the void formation-and-propagation mechanism in that they were both 
non-conductive and could divert the electrons to the nearby region. The diverted electrons then 
caused the porous structure or the void to extend toward the new high current density region. These 
two mechanisms competed with each other, and the final failure mechanism depended on the 
chip/package design and the stressing conditions.
