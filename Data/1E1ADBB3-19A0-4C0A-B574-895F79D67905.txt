前言 
臺灣位於亞洲大陸棚邊緣，四面環海，海岸線長約 1,134 公里，加上離島共 1,566 公里，海岸土地
面積廣大，海水表面溫度變化較陸域穩定，就風場條件而言，提供了海域較為平穩、少亂流之風場環
境，利於風力渦輪機壽命之延長，相當適合發展離岸風田。加上近年來由於人口增加快速、全球化下
都市擴張及經濟的快速發展等等因素，使得土地的需求大幅度增加，以及用電量大增，陸地上可適用
土地原已不多，加上海岸線用地的限制級沿海遊憇區之發展，使得可設機組位置更顯不足，可預期未
來風田的推廣將面臨因土地取得與法規限制，而導致發展用地難求之窘境，面對著台灣這個小島寸土
寸金的趨勢與內陸地區的土地有限的情形下，轉向離岸進行大規模的風田開發為大勢所趨。所以離岸
風田在我國確實有其發展的潛力，尤其在核能發電成為各方關注的焦點時，不論是以二氧化碳減量的
觀點論之，或是以環保的觀點論之，利用風能發電時因不會排放二氧化碳（CO2）、硫氧化物（SOX）
等污染物質，再配合現今的土地利用，離岸風田應是一種可永續發展之再生能源方式。 
關於風能發電的發展，由於技術進步迅速，風力發電的單機發電量已由 1980 年不到 100 瓩，提
高至現在的 1.5 千瓩以上機組，目前 3.5 千瓩之機組亦已推出，而 4~5 千瓩之機組亦積極研發中，不
久即可問世，因此成本大為降低。再加上離岸式（offshore）風力發電機技術的成熟導入，風力發電將
是成長最快速的再生能源發電技術之一。離岸式風力發電機為陸地不足的海島型國家（如台灣），提供
一個頗具吸引力的發展遠景與潛力。在國外，丹麥、瑞典、荷蘭、英國已完成了 10 處以上離岸風田，
運轉極為成功；丹麥更於 2002 年底完成裝置容量達 160 千瓩的 Hons Rev 離岸風田，為目前世界上已
完成之規模最大離岸風田。丹麥已於 2008 年建造 750 千瓩離岸風田，預計在 2030 年擴增至 4,000 千
瓩；德國及其他歐洲國家亦皆有離岸風田開發評估、規劃或興建計畫在進行中(經濟部能源局)。而國內
有關風能的發展，在麥寮六輕風場、澎湖中屯風場、竹北春風風場、及核能一廠石門風場等地，都有
一般陸上風力發電廠之成立，而在離岸風田中，也有了適合場址之初步篩選(江榮城，2006)。 
然而，這些場址的考量只注重在風場分布，考量到平均風速、年發電量及氣候的影響，而未考慮
到離岸風田設置後，對環境及生態等狀態可能造成的衝擊。國外關於離岸風田可能帶來的環境及生態
衝擊已多有評估，而國內關於這方面的研究並不多。因此，本計劃之目標除了考量離岸風田可帶來之
能源外，同時以整合性海岸管理(Integrated Coastal Zone Management, ICZM)的角度，宏觀的考量離岸風
田之多功能發展，全盤思考離岸風田興建後對海岸地區可能會造成的問題，就海岸地區的海洋環境、
生態、航運、漁業、觀光遊憩、權益相關者等議題綜合考量，溝通協調不同層級、組織間的資源使用
衝突，以引導其合理利用，並結合多功能使用規劃，以期找出較適合之發展模式。

 離岸風田發展的研究 
近年來民間風力發電開發投資業者與台電公司已陸續進行投入風力發電場開發，目前已完工的設
置容量為 18.77 萬瓩，並有逾 70 萬瓩亦正積極籌設或興建中，然均為陸域風力發電場，尚未邁入離岸
風力發電開發。截至目前國內風力發電裝置容量前三名之風場分別為竹北春風風場 (3.5MW)、澎湖中
屯風場(2.4MW)與麥寮六輕風場(2.64MW)，全國總裝置容量不到 10MW，佔全國總負載容量不到 0.03%，
遠低於丹麥之 18%與德國 4.7%。且台灣地狹人稠，陸地上可適用以發展風力發電之土地原已不多，加
上海岸線用地的限制及沿海遊憇區之發展，使得可設機組位置更顯不足，可預期未來風力發電場的推
廣將面臨因土地取得與法規限制，而導致發展用地難求之窘境。相較於陸地，面積廣闊的海域為一風
能佳、平穩、少亂流之風場環境，提供台灣一個開發風力資源之可行途徑(張純美，2007)(張鼎煥，2005)。 
本計畫除了以台灣相關文獻做基本之外，亦參酌目前世界設置風力機組之經驗，並彙集 North Hoyle 
(Defra, 2005)(Mackinson et al., 2006)(DTI, 2003)、Kentish Flats (Mackinson et al., 2006)(DTI, 2003)、Setana 
Port (Naruse et al., 2004)、Horns Rev (Defra, 2005)(Cape Wind, 2005)及 Middelgrunden (Soerensen, 2000)
等五處近岸風力發電場設置後對選址區域附近環境的影響，為適切、合理的篩選出可行場址，首先必
須排除法律限制開發區並避開土地利用相衝突區域之後，再進一步針對初選場址之自然環境、人文條
件以及工程、經濟等因素，並考量疑似斷層帶 3 級以上淺層地震等有關安全及環保問題之區域排除，
逐一分析評定進行篩選出具潛力可行之場址。 
 海洋牧場發展現況分析 
台灣沿岸海域原為海洋生產力最高、生物最豐富之處所，隨著漁業科技的進步，漁業水域污染事
件頻繁發生，對漁業生態環境與資源造成嚴重破壞，使得漁獲量逐年下降，沿近海魚類資源已呈日漸
衰竭之危機。且台灣沿海陸上水產養殖引發一些環境的問題，因此近海淺海養殖近年來成為提高漁產
的一個新方向(俞克儉&蔡光照，2005)。 
過去養殖開展之初，皆以魚類箱網養殖為主，單一的養殖方式使海水營養鹽過剩，尤其是來自人
為營養鹽之輸入，赤潮時有發生(Danovaro et al., 2002)。近年來開始以魚、貝、藻混養的方法，箱網養
殖導致海水富營養化、浮游生物增多現象，可透過貝類攝食浮游生物的方法，大量飼養貝類；此外魚
類、貝類排泄物和排出的二氧化碳，可使用藻類等海洋植物的光合作用進行吸收、消化，提供海洋生
物必需的氧氣，海洋牧場不僅可達到生態平衡，亦可增加漁獲量，形成生態良性迴圈(Cripps& Aabel, 
2002)(Chopin et al., 2004)。

 系統動力學之應用 
根據栗文輝(2000)等人所描述，系統動力學最初應用於工業企業管理，1969 年則應用於城市社會
系統，主要在研究工業部門、房屋部門以及人口部門之間的互動關係。結果發現此動態模式一開始時，
因都市環境適合工商發展、居住條件舒適，人口便快速成長，但當都市內因人口成長而使得有限的土
地資源達到飽和狀態時，就會因房屋老化、工商發展停滯等因素而導致人口衰退。在環境生態上 Dudley 
(2008)利用系統動力學探討漁船數的管理策略對魚群總量的影響變化。 
 群體建模之發展 
System Dynamics (SD) is an approach to understanding the behavior of complex systems over time. It 
was created during the 1950’s by Jay Forrester of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (Forrester 1961). 
Its purpose was originally to help managers improve their understanding of industrial processes but with time 
it became used in a wide range of disciplines, including both natural and social sciences. As explained by 
(Winz, Brierley et al. 2007), stakeholder participation in System Dynamics projects is not a new phenomenon 
since stakeholders have for long been information and/or data providers. What is newer however, is the 
intensification of their involvement in the projects (from problem definition to model conceptualization and 
formulation), with the aim of improving their learning and supporting the implementation and use of models. 
This kind of activity, referred to as Group Model Building (GMB) interventions, has known an exponential 
development during the last twenty years: from the literature review by Rouwette et al. (2002), it appears that 
4 were published before 1970, 4 during the 1970’s, 16 during the 1980’s and 62 during the 1990’s (Rouwette, 
Vennix et al. 2002). 
Although group modeling activities have known a growing interest during the last two decades, only few 
researchers did actually contribute to its codification (Vennix, Andersen et al. 1997). Andersen and Richardson 
of the University at Albany in New-York and Vennix of Radboud Univeristy of Nijmegen have published 
numerous articles with that aim. Their contribution is great and covers several topics such as: group 
knowledge elicitation (Richardson, Vennix et al. 1989; Vennix, Gubbels et al. 1990), problem structuring and 
model conceptualization (Zagonel 2002; Vennix, Richardson et al. 2007), practical methods for conducting 
group modeling activities (Richardson and Andersen 1995; Andersen and Richardson 1997; Vennix 1999; 
Luna-Reyes, Andersen et al. 2004) or group model building effectiveness (Vennix, Scheper et al. 1993; 
Akkermans and Vennix 1997; Rouwette, Vennix et al. 2002). 
Concerning this study, we found particularly worthy the lecture of the four cases presented by (Costanza 
and Ruth 1998), the transportation problem examined by (Stave 2002), the fishery revitalization project 
discussed by (Otto and Struben 2004) and the nicely detailed methodology used by (Luna-Reyes, 
Martinez-Moyano et al. 2006) for the modeling of knowledge and information sharing in inter-organizational 
networks.

 系統動力學(System Dynamics) 
系統動力學(System Dynamics)是美國麻省理工學院(MIT)的 Jay W.Forrester 教授（1961）於 60 年
代開始發展。系統動力學結合控制理論(Cybernetics)、一般系統理論(General System theory)與決策理論
（Decision theory），利用電腦與模擬技術(Simulation Technology)，適用於解決非線性關係、因果循環、
資訊回饋、時間滯延等動態性複雜（Dynamic Complexity）之問題。 
謝長宏(1980)認為系統動力學之目標，應是顯示整個系統動態行為之特質，並透過建立管理實驗室
之方式，讓高層管理人員能夠試驗各種組織結構變更或是經營方針的改變，藉由此實驗模擬的結果，
更可作為管理政策研擬時之參考以及指導。綜合上述，謝長宏(1980)提出系統力學之目標具有顯示行為

Each step of the modeling process (1 to 6) is likely to require a certain amount of these types of task (A 
to C) but in a general sense, we can say that steps 1 and 2 (problems identification and definition, and system 
conceptualization) are dominated by the type of task A (eliciting information) because they require the 
modeler to produce a database of pertinent information and thus, the production of many different ideas. On 
the contrary, step 3 (model formulation) requires the modeler to focus on the functioning of the system and is 
therefore dominated by the type of task B (exploring course of action). Finally, steps 4 and 5 (model use, 
implementation and dissemination, and model testing and evaluation) are more likely to be dominated by the 
type of task C (evaluating situations). 
The analysis of the type of task required is especially important when we consider group model building 
activities because it has strong consequences on the way we should manage the meetings. Richardson et al. 
(1989) explain that the beginning phases of the group model building process (divergent thinking phases) 
should be less structured than the following, in order to keep the group inventiveness as high as possible 
(Richardson, Vennix et al. 1989). Moreover, in order to reduce the influence the group can have on individuals, 
they suggest that elicitation tasks should be performed by non-interacting individuals and the results of their 
brainstorming be pooled in clusters subject to debate. The Nominal Group Technique (NGT) (Delbecq and 
VandeVen 1971) therefore appears to be well-suited for these elicitation tasks. Convergent thinking phases 
should see the intervention of specialists, able to fix the technical questions while increasing the confidence 
participants place in the model. The Delphi Technique (Brown 1968), is often used during problem-solving 
tasks in order to obtain consensus of opinion of a group of experts (Rowe and Wright 1999). Table 2 shows 
the organization of the meetings and the techniques we used to go through the 6 steps of model building.

II. 經濟層面 
經濟層面的評估因子包含運輸以及漁業。運輸因子主要探討海上活動安全性、飛航安全性以及便
利區與可及性對整體環境的影響。具有良好的地理位置與安全的海上活動，不僅會提升漁民的安全感
與降低養殖成本，且便捷的交通有助於遊客的到訪，可提升當地休閒漁業發展(邱文彥&尚榮康，1999)。
然而，大規模多功能離岸風田發展的建造過程中，會有暫時性持續不間斷的船艦進行往返，提升了船

本計劃應用整合性海岸管理架構於多功能離岸風田發展，以環境的整體性來思考，有助於系統化

以系統方法建置適用於多功能離岸風田發展之評估指標，作為決策者評鑑其可行性之依據，並且分析

 結論與建議 
本計劃參考了第一年計畫的經濟指標，再利用系統動力學架構出離岸風田與多功能風田對於漁獲
量的影響及不同風機數下的經濟產出趨勢。由系統模擬結果可以推測出如不結合海上養殖而只放置人
工魚礁的話，因為施工期間造成海域內的魚群數量減少，因此需要一段時間才能呈現出保護區的效果，
短時間內效果並不明顯。當離岸風田結合海上養殖時提供了沿岸捕撈業漁民轉為養殖漁民的誘因，因
此在漁船總數不變的架設下達到了漁船減量的效果，魚群數量能因此有所回復，長期來看總捕獲量有
上升的趨勢。

As the catches (C) increase, fish biomass (F) decrease and so does the Catches Per Unit Effort (CPUE). 
However, even if decreasing, the CPUE remains good enough to incite new boats to enter the fishery. The 
number of boats skyrockets to 128 in 2007. During that time and under the increasing fishing pressure, the 
fish stock collapses (17% of the carrying capacity) and the CPUE becomes too low for any profit. The number 
of boats thus decreases as quick as it rose before, the pressure on the fish stock is relaxed and allow both fish 
biomass and CPUE to rise again, and so on. We can observe that catches first increase under the influence of 
the growing CPUE but when this one is maximal and begin to fall, they go on increasing under the influence 
of the increasing number of boats. This delay is due to the fact that the change in boats is thought to be 
function of a smoothed CPUE over the 10 past years rather than the actual CPUE. The damped oscillations 
pattern is characteristic of overexploited fisheries (Schaefer 1954; Dudley 2008). A steady state is reached 
after 170 years. The biomass of fish stabilizes around 60% of its carrying capacity and the number of fishing 
boats at 20. 
 Policy analysis 
The wind-farm is supposed to be built in 2010. Two different shapes are envisaged whether it 
encompasses a corridor for birds (shape 2) or not (shape 1). The size of the wind-farm may therefore vary 
between these two modalities. Shape 1 and shape 2 lead to 10 km2 and 15 km2 respectively. The whole 
modeled fishing ground is set to 1,000 km2 so that the ratio of the wind-farm area may change between 1% 
and 1.5%. 
Several policies are tested: scenarios 1 and 1’ only forbid commercial fishers to access the area for safety 
reasons, scenarios 2 and 2’ keep the same basic rule but add the construction of artificial reefs in order to 
increase the carrying capacity of the wind-farm and scenarios 3 and 3’ add the development of activities 
within the wind-farm, namely mariculture and/or recreational fishing. An overview of these scenarios is given 
in table 4. The policy analysis provided the group several interesting insights. 
First, as can be seen on Figure 21, the wind-farm may not automatically harm the fishery because it can 
export a significant amount of fish toward the fishing ground, especially if artificial reefs are built. After 2013, 
scenarios 1 and 2 always bring catches equal to or greater than scenario 0. The improvement is always better 
for scenario 2. 
Figure 22 shows the annual net exports of fish towards the commercial fishing ground. The sudden 
decrease in 2013 is due to the fact that, after the works are completed, the outgoing rate of fish (abnormally 
elevated during the construction) is reduced to its natural rate. In 2030, net exports are estimated to reach 2.6 
and 4.3 tons per year for scenarios 1 and 2 respectively. In scenario 1, this 2.6 tons net export in 2030 is 
almost equal to the increase in the catches the same year (2.3 tons) but for scenario 2, the net export (4.3 tons) 
appears to be greater than the increase in catches (3.4 tons) which suggest that artificial reefs may contribute 
to the replenishment of the harvestable fish stock. This result can be explained by the following

 Discussion and conclusion 
The whole process was dedicated to consensus building. We are glad our three participants totally 
accepted the multi-objective nature of the model and were not only preoccupied by their own interests. The 
satisfaction indicator has been built upon their request in order to compare situations which were a priori not 
comparable (for example a trade-off between birds and safety). It reveals that whatever the weights used, the 
actual situation (no wind-farm) seems preferable to the establishment of a wind-farm. It is important to remind 
that “all models are wrong” (Sterman 2002) and that our model may prove useful in the fact that it represents 
the state of mind of concerned local people. We suggest, in echo to our participants, that the wind-farm project 
may be moved to another place with less ecological constraints.
