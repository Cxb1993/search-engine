I中文摘要
前此研究中已證實，高熱衝擊之 CeO2 對含酚廢水濕式氧化反應 (Wet Air
Oxidation,WAO)具有很好的催化效能，且氧化鈰觸媒之催化活性與 CeO2 晶體顆粒結構
及大小有很大關聯。因此，我們以不同製備方法(過氧化氫還原法、沉殿法、草酸溶膠
法、聚丙稀酸溶膠法等)來製備奈米級的 CeO2 觸媒進行含酚廢水的催化反應。由研究結
果可以看出，在不同製備方法中，以 A 製備方法(過氧化氫還原法)所合成出來的氧化鈰
觸媒活性為最好，在 140℃即能顯現催化成效，而且觸媒經丙酮清洗後有很好耐用性，
實用之潛力相當大。在觸媒改良以降低成本方面，添加金屬助催化劑造成大量金屬離子
溶出，遠超過法規標準，所以不是可行方法。至於使用不同擔体，雖然 CeO2/-Al2O3 觸
媒活性最好，卻必須在 180℃以上才有類似前述 CeO2-A 觸媒之活性，因此也不是可行
的途徑；為降低觸媒成本，必須再測試其他擔體及進一步提升觸媒再生成效。
關鍵詞：製備方法、奈米級 CeO2、濕式氧化反應、含酚廢水、助催化劑、擔體鈰觸媒。

利用上述 CeO2 觸媒，我們更深入探討不同反應條件，諸如觸媒添加量、酚濃度、
氧氣壓力以及反應溫度等，對於酚類廢水轉化率及礦化率的影響。藉由研究成果，我們
發現最佳之操作條件如下：反應溫度僅需 160℃，觸媒添加量每公升為 1.0g，氧氣壓力
在含酚濃度低時 (400ppm 以下)，僅需約 0.5MPa，含酚濃度高時 (1000ppm 以上)，則需
1.0MPa 或甚至 1.5MPa (5000ppm 以上)。
雖然前述 CeO2 觸媒對於含酚廢水有很好的催化活性，但是因為鈰鹽價格並不便
宜，而且直接以鈰鹽煅燒所得氧化鈰，即使經過研磨，活性位址 (active sites)仍無法充
分分散。為降低觸媒原料成本以及提高活性位址分散度，92 年度所進行國科會研究中，
我們乃嘗試使用初濕含浸法(incipient impregnation)，將氧化鈰含浸於擔體上，繼續探討
擔體鈰觸媒對於含酚廢水 CWAO 反應之催化效能。在所測試擔體中(-Al2O3、SiO2、
TiO2、AlPO4-5、commercial CeO2)，吾人發現以 CeO2/-Al2O3 活性最佳，此可由 O2-TPD
顯示其具有較多可交換結構氧，H2-TPR 顯示其有較強之氧化反應活性位址，及較分散
之 CeO2 加以驗證。另外，觸媒耐用性亦經由使用過觸媒之活性測試加以評估，結果顯
示因為積碳關係，造成觸媒活性大幅降低(2 小時酚轉化率降至 37%)，此可由 EDS 結果
加以證明。前述使用過觸媒經由在空氣中煅燒，可恢復部分之活性(4 小時酚轉化率
86%)。比較 CeO2/-Al2O3 與純 CeO2 之催化成效，雖然前者較差，但在價格優勢下，
CeO2/-Al2O3 對於含酚廢水 CWAO 仍具可行性。
另外，經由測試不同鈰含量對於 CeO2/-Al2O3 活性之影響，吾人發現 20wt.%為
最適之鈰含量，與觸媒添加量無關。最適鈰含量之結論可由不同觸媒鑑定方法(O2-TPD、
H2-TPR)以及 CeO2 結晶大小之估算加以印證。相對於鈰含量對觸媒活性有顯著影
響，BET 分析結果則顯示表面積大小對於觸媒活性影響不大。至於操作條件
的影響，當使用最佳觸媒時，我們發現若酚濃度為 1000ppm，最適之觸媒添
加量為 3.0g/L，氧氣分壓至少需 1.5MPa，反應溫度則需180℃。惟若酚濃
度提高，則觸媒添加量與氧氣分壓均需再提高，才能達到較高之酚轉化率及
礦化率；這些研究成果均已發表在國際著名之期刊 [25-26]。
4三、研究方法：
(1) 觸媒製備：
CeO2與 CeO2/-Al2O3觸媒之製備：
(A) CeO2觸媒前驅物(precursor)之製備：
純 CeO2 觸媒前驅物分別依下列 A、B、C、D 四種方法製備︰
方法 A︰將 0.5M CeCl3•7H2O 與 35%H2O2 依 3:1 體積比充分混合，混合物加熱至 110
℃，定溫維持 4 小時，再將此溶液依以下(C)之升溫條件於空氣中煅燒。
方法 B︰於 0.5M CeCl3•7H2O 溶液中逐滴加入 1.0M NH3 水溶液，充分攪拌並量測 pH
值，直至 pH=9.0。此時溶液中形成凝膠狀黃白色沉降物，將其與溶液分離，
每次以約二倍體積 R.O.水洗滌，共洗滌二次，然後於 110℃將此沉降物烘
乾 4 小時，再依以下(C)之升溫條件於空氣中煅燒。
方法 C︰將 0.5M CeCl3•7H2O 與 0.5M 草酸(螯合劑)混合以形成溶膠(sol-gel)；混合時
金屬離子與草酸之莫耳比為 1:1。前述溶膠先於 80℃蒸發 4 小時，再於 110
℃烘乾 4 小時，所得觸媒前驅物仍以以下(C)之升溫條件於空氣中煅燒。
方法 D︰形成溶膠方法與方法 C 相似，但螯合劑改為聚丙烯酸(poly acrylic acid,
PAA)。在所形成溶膠中緩慢加入適量之 1.0M 硝酸，直至 pH=3.0。後續蒸
發與乾燥步驟同方法 C，所得觸媒前驅物依以下(C)之升溫條件於空氣中煅
燒。
(B) CeO2/-Al2O3觸媒前驅物之製備：
仿照前述 A、B、C、D 四種方法，製備 CeO2/-Al2O3 觸媒前驅物如下︰
方法A’︰將 0.5M CeCl3•7H2O 與 35%H2O2 依 3:1 體積比充分混合，再依預定之鈰含
量，將適量之-Al2O3 擔體加入混合液中，攪拌均勻後，加熱至 110℃，定
溫維持 4 小時。
方法B’︰依預定之鈰含量，將 0.5M CeCl3•7H2O 溶液加入適量之-Al2O3 擔體中，混
合均勻後逐滴加入 1.0M NH3 水溶液，充分攪拌並量測 pH 值，直至 pH=9.0。
此時溶液中形成凝膠狀沉降物，將其與溶液分離，每次以約二倍體積 R.O.
水洗滌，共洗滌二次，然後於 110℃將此沉降物物烘乾 4 小時。
方法C’︰將 0.5M CeCl3•7H2O 與 0.5M 草酸(螯合劑)混合以形成溶膠(sol-gel)；混合
時金屬離子與草酸之莫耳比為 1:1。於前述溶膠中，依預定之鈰含量，將適
量之-Al2O3 擔體加入，攪拌均勻後，先於 80℃蒸發 4 小時，再於 110℃烘
乾 4 小時。
方法 D’︰形成溶膠方法與方法 C 相似，但螯合劑改為聚丙烯酸(poly acrylic acid,
PAA)。在所形成溶膠中緩慢加入適量之 1.0M 硝酸，直至 pH=3.0。隨後再
依預定之鈰含量，加入適量之-Al2O3 擔體，攪拌均勻後，依方法 C 之步驟
蒸發與乾燥。
上列 CeO2/-Al2O3 前驅物之煅燒，仍依以下(C)之升溫條件於空氣中煅燒。
6反相(reverse phase)管柱，移動相(mobile phase)則為 35%甲醇及 65%去離子水之混合
液，流速為 1mL/min；經管柱分離的酚乃是由 UV 在 254 nm 波長下分析。此處所使
用之 HPLC，乃由本系環境中心提供，至於管柱及移動相則於計畫中編列預算購買。
另一方面，樣品之 COD 乃是依據環保署檢字第 21288 號公告之試管迴流法檢測水樣
COD 值，以評估含酚廢水之礦化程度。
(4) 觸媒鑑定：
不同製備方法及不同助催化劑添加條件對於觸媒活性之影響，可藉由 XRD 分析
及 H2-TPR 分析加以探討。XRD 分析可借助國科會南部貴儀中心之 XRD 分析儀
(Rigaku D/Max IIIV diffractometer)測定，所得圖譜再配合半高寬(FWHM)之計算，可
了解 CeO2 結晶情形及晶體顆粒大小，探討其對活性的影響。至於 H2-TPR，則可了
解可還原活性位址(reducible active sites)之多寡及活性位址氧化能力之變化。根據文
獻以及吾人之前對於 CuO-MoO3/-Al2O3 觸媒的研究結果[32-33]，擔體上非晶相
(amorphous phase) 活性位址較晶相(crystalline phase) 活性位址更具氧化能力；前述
觸媒是否有類似之結果，可配合觸媒鑑定及 CWAO 反應加以驗證。
對於觸媒之 XRD 分析，條件如下︰
分析範圍(2)︰20o~60o；掃描速度︰3o/min；金屬靶~Cu (=1.5405A)。
H2-TPR 之測定步驟則如下所述：
首先，在 30℃下以 He 氣體(30mL/min)流經約 0.1g 觸媒床，俟系統穩定，改通
H2 與 He 混合氣體(10%H2＋90%He, 30mL/min)，以 20℃/min 之速率升溫至 800℃。
所使用儀器為成大翁鴻山教授實驗室之 Micromeritics 2900 分析儀，H2 濃度則以
TCD 分析，藉此計算 H2 消耗量。
此外，觸媒活性亦可能受到比表面積及孔洞大小之影響，所以有必要測定觸媒
之 BET 表面積及孔洞分布，了解表面積及孔洞大小隨觸媒製備方法不同之變化情
形。BET 表面積分析儀為 Micromeritics ASAP 2010 機型，可由本系環境中心提供。
(5) 可交換結構氧之測定：

四、結果與討論：
本研究首先以含酚廢水 CWAO 反應測試不同製備方法對於 CeO2 觸媒活性之影響。
圖一結果顯示，CeO2-A 活性最佳，對於 1000ppm 含酚廢水，160C 下僅需約 2 小時即
可達到 100%酚轉化率及 95%礦化率；活性次佳的為 CeCl3•7H2O 直接煅燒之觸媒
(CeO2-E)，在相同反應條件下，必須超過 4 小時才能有類似 CeO2-A 之催化成效。另外，

CeO2-A及CeO2-E之誘導期分別為 1及 3小時，CeO2-D 則需 22 小時(圖二)，致於 CeO2-B，
在 160℃無明顯活性，即使在 180℃，誘導期亦長達 12 小時。

1. Introduction
In the field of environmental pollution control, methods developed to treat phenolic
wastewater include biodegradation (Chen et al., 2002; Godjevargova et al., 2003; Dias et al.,
2004), membrane separation (Kujawski et al., 2004), adsorption (Roostaei and Tezel, 2004),
and advanced oxidation processes (AOPs) (Esplugas et al., 2002; Kavitha and Palanivelu,
2004). The utility of biodegradation is limited by the difficulty of acquiring land necessary for
the process, especially in Taiwan, and by the extreme toxicity of phenol to microorganisms,
which makes biodegradation viable only at phenol concentrations < 500 mg L1. The
physico-chemical methods have their own limitations, including reaction inefficiency, high
energy consumption, production of sludge containing iron, and insufficient capacity. Catalytic
wet air oxidation (CWAO) is notable, therefore, as an economically and technologically
viable physico-chemical method, the high performance of which has been demonstrated
(Misha et al., 1995; Fortuny et al., 1998, 1999).
In studies of CWAO phenol is often chosen as a model pollutant because it is particularly
damaging and prevalent, being an important raw material widely used in dye, pharmaceutical,
petrochemical and plasticizer industries. Studies of the CWAO of phenol using CeO2-type
catalysts (pure CeO2 or supported CeO2) (Chang et al., 2002; Lin et al., 2002, 2003; Chen et
al., 2004; Chang et al., 2005) have shown that these catalysts, prepared under higher thermal
impact, exhibit excellent performance in the treatment of aqueous phenol solution.
Unfortunately CeO2-type catalysts are considered prohibitively expensive, and some
aspects of their catalytic performance, such as durability, still need improving. According to
previous studies (Oliviero et al., 2000; Neri et al., 2002), addition of a promoter, especially the
base metal oxides, may help to reduce these problems. Hence in this study various base metal
oxides were added into the CeO2/-Al2O3 catalyst to screen for the most effective promoter for
the CWAO of phenol. Durability of the optimal catalyst-promoter combinations, efficiency of
various methods for regenerating the used catalysts, and the influence of leached metal ions
was also assessed.

3.2. Regeneration of Deactivated Catalysts
The activity of the used catalysts Ce15Cu5-U and Ce15Mn5-U, shown in Fig. 11,
declined noticeably. Hamoudi, et al. (1999), and Besson and Gallezot (2003), reported that
this deactivation may be caused by deposits of carbonaceous compounds on the catalyst or
by leaching of active species of the catalyst. Comparison of the EDS spectra of fresh and
used Ce15Cu5 (Figs. 12a, b) and Ce15Mn5 catalysts (not shown) does reveal a C element
peak in the used catalyst spectra, absent in those of fresh catalyst. This peak was also
reported by Chen et al. (2004) in their study of CWAO of phenol using CeO2/-Al2O3.
Accordingly, deactivation by deposits of carbonaceous compounds may be demonstrated.
To regenerate the used catalyst it was rinsed with acetone, after which some was
calcined before re-use in CWAO of phenol and some was re-used directly. EDS spectra of
Ce15Cu5-A1 (Fig. 12c) and Ce15Mn5-A1 (not shown) revealed that the intensity of the
noted C element peak reduced noticeably. Moreover, the activity of these catalysts was
largely (Ce15Cu5-A1) or completely (Ce15Mn5-A1) recovered, as shown in Fig. 13. The
rinsing of used catalyst with acetone in order to remove carbonaceous deposits is therefore
clearly justified. Used catalysts that were calcined before re-use were much less active and
this may be because the calcination process stabilizes the catalyst structure. Yao and Weng
(1998) and Lin et al. (2002) have proven that in CeO2-type catalysts a more stable structure
leads to a less active catalyst.
A second regeneration (Ce15Cu5-A2) produced a less active catalyst. Deactivation of
this catalyst may be due to active species being further leached (Table 3) or higher levels
of carbonaceous material remaining on Ce15Cu5-A2 after acetone rinsing, the latter
phenomenon indicated by the EDS spectra in Fig. 13d. In an attempt to more effectively
remove carbonaceous material, an HCl or HNO3 acid solution was used in place of acetone
in the regeneration of used Ce15Cu5 and Ce15Mn5. This treatment, however, produced
regenerated catalysts that were less effective than their acetone-treated counterparts.
Regeneration of the catalyst, particularly a second regeneration, is a subject requiring
further research.
3.3. Effect of Leached Metal Species on CWAO of Phenol
Although comparable to activity of Ce15Cu5, the recovery in activity demonstrated by
Ce15Cu5-A1 was not complete. This may be caused by leaching of active metal species of
the catalyst, an event that is more likely to occur in acidic conditions (pH values of
reaction solutions catalyzed by Ce15Cu5 and Ce15Mn5 varied between 2.75~4.25 and
4.50~5.75, respectively). Concentrations of metal species in the reaction mixtures,
measured via ICP analysis, demonstrated that they clearly were leached from catalysts
(Table 3): at 180 C and after 4 h reaction, leached copper amounted to 35-146 mg L－1

Alternatively, Ce15Mn5 was less effective than CeO2/-Al2O3 as a promoter of CWAO of
phenol and leaching of Mn was more severe than leaching of Cu, making Mn also
unsuitable as a promoter of this reaction.
4. Conclusions
Various metal species were added as promoters to improve the activity of CeO2/-Al2O3.
Cu was the most effective promoter, followed by Mn, although the performance of
Mn-promoted catalyst was inferior to that of unpromoted catalyst. With Cu as promoter
(Ce15Cu5), CWAO of phenol was efficient at 160 C, whilst Mn-promoted catalyst
(Ce15Mn5) required temperatures higher than 220 C to obtain acceptable performance. The
activity of used catalyst (Ce15Cu5-U and Ce15Mn5-U) declined noticeably, due to
carbonaceous deposits and leaching of metal ions. Regeneration of used catalyst via acetone
rinsing was effective, leading to a significant recovery in activity in the Cu-promoted catalyst
(Ce15Cu5-A1). A second regeneration produced a catalyst (Ce15Cu5-A2) which was much
less active than Ce15Cu5-A1, however, due to remaining deposits of carbonaceous material
and extended leaching of metal species in the second run. Regenerating by rinsing with HCl or
HNO3 aqueous solution proved less effective than acetone. Leached metal ions accounted for
the majority of mineralization of phenol, while the solid catalyst played a dual role of initiator
and terminator of free radicals. Although performance of Ce15Cu5 was higher than that of
CeO2/-Al2O3, leaching of Cu2+ caused a severe decline in activity and poses the problem of
secondary pollution of treated wastewater. Therefore, addition of Cu, as well as other metal
species, is unfavorable in promoting the CeO2/-Al2O3 catalyst.

結論與建議：
本文主要有以下三點結論：
1. 在不同製備方法中，以過氧化氫中氧化還原法所得 CeO2 觸媒活性最佳，140℃即能顯
現含酚廢水礦化之催化效能，較大部份文獻中所發表者要低許多；觸媒耐用性則更加
顯示其實用之潛力。
2. 添加金屬助催化劑雖然能夠提升擔體鈰觸媒之催化成效，但這主要是來自溶出金屬之
催化所致。由於金屬溶出量遠高於法規所允許之標準，所以添加金屬助催化劑並不是
可行之方法。
3. 使用不同擔體所製備之擔體鈰觸媒，以 CeO2/-Al2O3 活性較佳，但其活性卻比 CeO2-A
觸媒低許多，所以現階段並無取代 CeO2-A 觸媒之潛力。
至於本文之建議，則是應朝向降低觸媒成本方向來研究，包括測試其他擔體及改進
再生方法等。
