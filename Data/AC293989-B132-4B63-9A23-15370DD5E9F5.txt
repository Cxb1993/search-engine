一、 中文摘要 
本專題研究計畫係以二氧化錫/氧化銦錫
玻 璃 (Tin Oxide/Indium Tin Oxide glass, 
SnO2/ITO glass) 為基礎，研製電壓式乳酸感測
器，此感測器架構係以酸鹼電壓為基礎，結合
半導體製程及酵素固定化技術，將乳酸脫氫酵
素(Lactate Dehydrogenase, LDH)及其輔酶菸鹼
醯胺腺嘌呤二核苷酸 (Nicotinamide Adenine 
Dinucleotide, NAD+)固定於二氧化錫 pH感測膜
外，完成具備低成本及可拋棄式等優點之乳酸
生物感測器。 
藉由酵素之專一性，乳酸分子藉由乳酸脫
氫酵素催化產生丙硐酸與氫離子，乳酸反應後
釋放之氫離子濃度改變造成感測元件表面酸鹼
值變化，再以二氧化錫/氧化銦錫玻璃為架構之
酸鹼感測器進行偵測。 
依據實驗結果，比較二種不同酵素固定化
方法，發現其量測偵測範圍之改變，其低偵測
極限可達到人體之血液乳酸濃度量測範圍(人體
正常範圍約 1mM 至 1.8mM)，並針對慢響應造
成之影響量測乳酸感測器的遲滯效應。 
 
關鍵詞：二氧化錫/氧化銦錫玻璃酸鹼電極、乳
酸脫氫酵素、電壓式全固態式乳酸生
醫感測器、遲滯。 
英文摘要 
This study presents a tin oxide/indium tin 
oxide glass (SnO2/ITO glass) electrode-based 
potentiometric lactate biosensor. The structure of 
the sensor is based on a SnO2 pH electrode. The 
lactate biosensor was fabricated by the 
semiconductor technology and enzyme 
immobilization method. Lactate dehydrogenase 
(LDH) and coenzyme nicotinamide adenine 
dinucleotide (NAD+) were utilized to achieve the 
lactate detection on the sensing surface of the 
SnO2 pH electrode. Moreover, it also possesses 
some advantages such as low cost and disposable 
capability.

selective property of lactate dehydrogenase (LDH) 
was utilized to achieve a lactate biosensor. The 
enzyme was immobilized on the surface of tin 
oxide (SnO2)/indium tin oxide (ITO) glass using a 
covalent bond method. LDH and coenzyme 
(nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide, NAD+) were 
co-immobilized on the SnO2 pH sensing window 
by 3-glycidoxypropyltri-methoxysilane (GPTS). 
The sensing mechanism was based on the 
reactions of enzyme, coenzyme and lactate, the 
resulting product, H+, is detected by the SnO2/ITO 
glass pH electrode.  
 
三、研究方法 
Reagents and materials 
Lacate dehydrogenase (LDC, EC 1.1.1.27, 140 
units/mg, from rabbit muscle) was purchased from 
the Sigma Chemical Company (USA). One unit of 
the enzyme activity is defined as that amount of 
the enzyme which oxidizes 1 µmol of L-lactate per 
minute at pH 7.0 and 25℃. Lactic acid 
(2-hydroxypropanoic acid C3H6O3, molecular 
weight=90.08, 1.209 g/mL) and nicotinamide 
adenine dinucleotide (NAD+, molecular 
weight=663.42) were obtained from Sigma 
Chemical Company as well. 
3-glycidoxypropyltrimrthoxysilane (GPTS) was 
obtained from the Aldrich Company (USA). All 
the others materials were used analytical grade. 
Standard solutions and lactate buffer solutions 
were prepared with deionized (D.I.) water for all 
experiments. 
 
Preparation of the SnO2/ITO glass pH 
electrode 
The SnO2/ITO glass pH electrode was 
fabricated by depositing SnO2 thin film onto an 
ITO glass substrate with thickness of 200nm. 
SnO2 thin films were prepared by the radio 
frequency sputtering system (SnO2 target: 99.9%) 
at a substrate temperature of 150℃. The ITO 
glasses, whose sheet resistance was between 50 ~ 
100 Ohms/square and the ITO coating thickness 
was 230Å, were purchased from the Mesostate 
LCD Industries Co., Ltd (Taiwan). First step, the 
ITO glasses were cleaned in methyl alcohol 
solution and D.I. water for 15 min, respectively. 
Second step, the sensing part of the pH electrode

(c) 2µL of GPTS was dropped onto the sensing 
part of pH sensor, and was cured at 40℃ for 
five hours. 
(d) LDH was dissolved in a phosphate buffer 
solution (PBS, 20mM, pH6.8). 
(e) The 1.5 µL of solution was dropped onto the 
GPTS membrane, and was storaged in 4℃ 
refrigerator. 
The cross section of SnO2/ NAD+ /GPTS/LDH 
modification method based on SnO2/ITO glass pH

Measurements 
Measurements were performed at the room 
temperature (25℃). The measurement system of 
the solid state lactate biosensor is shown in Fig. 3. 
The commercial reference electrode (model: 
S120C) was used as the standard reference 
electrode. Hence, the solid state lactate biosensor 
was immersed into the buffer solution with the 
commercial reference electrode during the whole 
measurement period. In addition, the output signal 
was obtained by the Agilent 34401A and 
presented in personal computer. 
 
四、結果與討論 
Performance of the SnO2/ITO glass pH 
electrodes 
Before immobilization of enzyme lactate 
dehydrogenase (LDH) and coenzyme nicotinamide 
adenine dinucleotide (NAD+), the performance of 
substrate (pH sensitive electrode) should be 
confirmed. Hence, based on the experimental 
results, the calibration curve of SnO2/ITO glass 
pH electrode is shown in Fig. 4. The sensitivity 
and linearity of the pH sensor are 58.73 mV/pH 
and 0.999, respectively. It is comfirmed that the 
pH sensor has excellent sensing performance. 
 
Potentiometric response of the lactate biosensor 
based on the modification method of 
SnO2/GPTS/LDH with NAD+  
The calibration of the lactate biosensor was 
carried out in 20 mM phosphate buffer solution 
with different lactate concentrations (5 mM, 10 
mM, 15 mM, 20 mM, 30 mM, 40 mM, and 50 
mM). As shown in Fig. 5, the experimental results 
showed that the sensitivity is about 61.92 
mV/decade (the linearity is about 0.9835) in 
detection range between 5 mM and 50 mM of 
lactate concentrations.

三、建議事項 
近年來生醫檢測逐漸受到重視，不論於前端之生醫感測元件，亦或是後端之
訊號處理系統皆有大量之研究被提出。生醫感測器可結合微流體結構，以取代複
雜之化學特性測試或分析儀器，將其微小化並感應人體生理參數或環境之變化，
故生醫感測器未來將對醫藥、醫療、環境保護和生醫科技等方面有廣泛之應用。 
生醫感測元件利用生物分子（如蛋白質、基因、酵素、抗體、核酸等）作為
辨識元件，生醫電子產品在傳統無機的半導體材料上（如矽晶片）或可撓式基板
等，放置特定生物材料（如蛋白質分子等物質），再與其他物質發生特異性之生
化反應後，訊息可即時定量，結合訊號傳送、訊號處理與運算於一體。 
隨著生醫感測技術之發展，生醫感測器成本大幅度降低，使生醫訊號即時監
控成為即將實現之目標。然而為達此目標，除了生醫感測器及生理訊號量測外，
迫切需要且不可或缺係開發生物檢體處理技術以自動處理、分析所量測之生醫訊
號，並擷取具代表性之訊號特徵。 
有鑑於此，未來國內可依據本研討會之相關資料應用於相關研究中，期望將
可提升國內感測器與微流體技術結合發展之競爭力。目前國內外已投入相當多資
源於生醫感測元件之製作，而藉由離子感測元件之研發，可利用微流體加工技術
處理製作各式生醫感測器，並應用於生醫醫療儀器之研究，並藉由生化、半導體、
