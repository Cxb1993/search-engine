一、研究計畫中英文摘要：請就本研究計畫要點作一概述，並依本研究計畫性質自訂關鍵詞。 
 
（一）計畫中文摘要。 
我們以窄頻寬鍍模反射片及再成像式的回授方式，成功在環境溫度變化於 0-50°C 時，將 808nm 雷
射二極體之波長鎖定在 806 到 809nm 的範圍內，波長漂移低至 0.06nm/°C。反射片角度之容忍值高至
5mrad 以上。將此項技術實際組成綠光雷射雛型品後，在環境溫度變化於 0-50°C 時，綠光輸出功率皆
可保持在 3.76 至 4.2mW 的範圍內。此成果非常適合應用於工業雷射及雷射顯示器之用途。 
（二）計畫英文摘要。 
For improving the temperature range of traditional diode-pumped solid-state laser, we successfully lock 
the wavelength of an 808nm laser diode with a narrow-band reflector and a re-image feedback scheme. With 
the ambient temperature varies from 0 to 50 °C, the wavelength of the laser diode was locked in a range 
between 806 and 809nm with a slope of 0.06nm/°C. The angle tolerance of the narrow-band reflector was 
measured to be as small as 5mrad.  With this technology, the output power of the prototype green laser was 
well-controlled to a range from 3.76mW to 4.2mW as the ambient temperature varies from 0 to 50°C. Such a 
laser is very suitable for applications such as laser leveler and laser display.

可由未鎖定的 797nm，鎖定到 806nm，也就是說鎖定的範圍至少可偏離增益頂點達 9nm 以上。
