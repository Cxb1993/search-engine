隨著行動電話的普遍，無線網路與衛星傳播的發展，使得高頻通信元件的需求增加。擁有體積
小及濾波效果佳等優點的表面聲波元件因而變得相當熱門。表面聲波元件的頻率取決於材料的波
速。由於鑽石的超高硬度，使其具有最高之波速，約略是傳統表面聲波元件基材之 2-3 倍，可大

藍寶石基板由於具有高聲速，因此常在其上沉積氧化鋅、氮化鋁或鈮酸鋰等壓電薄膜以作為高
頻表面聲波元件之應用。氧化鋁薄膜具有許多良好的性質；包括良好的熱及化學穩定性，高硬度，
高的阻隔及絕緣性，及良好的光學穿透性。由於氧化鋁的高硬度，使其具有較高的波速，加上成長
大尺寸之藍寶石基板仍有困難，且目前絕大多數的電子元件皆製作於矽基板上，因此本研究希望利
用沉積高聲速氧化鋁薄膜於矽基板上來取代價格昂貴的藍寶石基板。
本研究計畫將利用射頻磁控濺鍍法在具有高聲速的氧化鋁薄膜的矽晶片上成長氧化鋅壓電薄
膜，並製作高頻表面聲波元件。我們將改變高聲速材料之厚度以探討其對聲速改變之影響。期待
透過沈積高聲速特性的氧化鋁薄膜能有效提升元件聲速與操作頻率。

the layered SAW devices, as the thickness of the alumina
ﬁlm is smaller than the wavelength (20 mm) of the excited
SAW. Moreover, the presence of grain boundaries might
also markedly retard the propagation of SAW. Such a
hypothesis is supported by the phenomenon that higher
SAW velocity was obtained when the alumina ﬁlm used
for fabricating the layer SAW was thicker [cf.
Fig. 5(a)]. Such a phenomenon implies that, when
the wavelength of the SAW is small enough in high-
frequency devices, the phase velocity of the SAW
can be further increased. The experimental result is beneﬁ
cial to the replacement of the expensive
single-crystalline sapphire substrate with
e-beam-evaporated alumina ﬁlm, leading to a lower cost
of the high-frequency SAW devices. Moreover, the
results may also be useful for integrating the
semi- conductor and high-frequency SAW devices on the
same Si substrate. Further work on the theoretical
calculation of the SAW properties of the ZnO/Al2 O3 /Si
layered SAW devices
is ongoing.
4. Conclusions
ZnO thin ﬁlm with highly (0002)-preferred
orientation was deposited on an SiO2 /Si substrate
by RF magnetron sputtering with an
e-beam-evaporated alumina ﬁlm. The phase velocity
of the surface acoustic wave (SAW) device with a
8-mm-thick alumina ﬁlm was measured to be about
4733m/s, which is much higher than that (4069
m/s) of the ZnO/SiO2/Si structure and approaches
(89.6%) that
(5283m/s) of ZnO/sapphire. The coupling coeﬃcient
and
temperature coeﬃcient of the frequency of the ZnO/Al2O3/
SiO2 /Si structure with an alumina ﬁlm thickness
of 8 mm were close to those of the
ZnO/sapphire structure. The experimental result is
beneﬁcial to the replacement of the expensive
single-crystalline sapphire substrate with an e-
beam-evaporated alumina ﬁlm, leading to a lower
cost of the high-frequency SAW devices, and is
also useful for integrating the semiconductor and
high-frequency SAW devices on the same Si substrate.

本發明使用氧化鋁緩衝層，製造高聲速表面聲波元件，進而替代
昂貴的單晶藍寶石(sapphire)基板。
主要架構如下面所列：
(a)ZnO/Al2O3/Si；AlN/Al2O3/Si；LiNbO3/Al2O3/Si
(b)ZnO/Al2O3/SiO2/Si; AlN/Al2O3/SiO2/Si；LiNbO3/Al2O3/SiO2/Si
雖然氧化鋁為常見的材料，但要在表面聲波元件的應用下達到高
聲速，進而替代單晶藍寶石基板，氧化鋁緩衝層的厚度要在 10-20
μm的厚度。
