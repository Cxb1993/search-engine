1一、 摘要
質子交換膜燃料電池是一種乾淨的能源，而其中又以觸媒層及
交換膜為發展重點，一般質子交換膜燃料電池使用的觸媒是鉑觸媒
及鉑釕觸媒，其中觸媒皆是以碳黑為載體，碳黑是一種比表面積大
而傳導性又不差的碳載體，目前研究指出，當燃料電池運作時，碳
黑有可能因為腐蝕和氧化，使得吸附於其上的金屬觸媒顆粒產生凝
聚，故在觸媒層的研究一般致力於改善顆粒凝聚、二元合金的抗毒
化性質及載體的腐蝕性、傳導性。本實驗主要以自製人工石墨粉來
取代部分碳黑載體，因自製人工石墨粉中含有少量奈米碳管，希望
可以利用奈米碳管及立體結構石墨，使得金屬觸媒顆粒達到分散效
果和電化學效能的改進。在碳黑載體中加入 30 wt.%的自製人工石墨
粉，雖然不能使得觸媒顆粒成長的更小，但是由交換電流的大小可
以得知，在 Pt/C 及 Pt-Ru/C 兩組觸媒中，交換電流分別提高了 9 %
及 19 %。經由循環伏安法的結果可以得知，人工石墨粉中的奈米碳
管及立體狀石墨有助於改善觸媒的效能。
關鍵字：質子交換膜燃料電池，白金觸媒，奈米碳管，DPG 人工石

四、研究方法
1.觸媒製備
(1)在 100 ml 去離子水加入 5 克氫氧化鈉；50 ml 去離子水加入 12.72
克碳酸鈉；15 ml 去離子水加入 2 克硼酸鈉。
(2)在 55 ℃下，200 ml 去離子水加入 1 克六氯鉑酸跟 5 克亞硫酸氫
鈉，並攪拌均勻。
(3)先利用碳酸鈉溶液滴定回 pH 5，每分鐘滴定雙氧水 1 c.c. 50 次，
之後攪拌 30 分鐘。
(4)利用氫氧化鈉溶液滴定回 pH 5，之後攪拌 30 分鐘。
(5)加入 DPG 粉末並提高溫度到 95 ℃攪拌一小時。在 10-20 分鐘滴
定完硼酸鈉，再攪拌 30 分鐘即完成。
2.Cyclic voltammetry 量測
(1)先用600 μl去離子水混合200 μl Nafion Dispersion (5 wt.%, Aldrich)
和 21 毫克觸媒粉末製作成漿液。
(2)將含有 0.196 cm2 Glassy carbon 用鐵氟龍(PTFE)固定製作成旋轉
電極當作工作電極(WE)。
(3)滴定20 μl漿液於工作電極表面，再加溫到 70 ℃烘乾 10 分鐘。
(4)使用 Pt 薄片當作對電極(CE)；Ag/AgCl 當作參考電極(RE)。
(5)掃描範圍-0.65 V~0.65 V；掃描速率 10 mV/sec。

4. 循環伏安的分析
4.1 Pt/C
Fig. 28 至 Fig. 31 為使用不同比例的載體所製備的 Pt/C 觸媒在
10 mV/s 所做的循環伏安的結果，由圖中可以發現在-0.55 V 和-0.4 V
有兩個鉑對氫氣的脫附峰，在-0.55 V 的是一個較弱的脫附峰，而在
-0.4 V 是鉑對氫氣較強的脫附峰，而我們將循環伏安的還原曲線做一
個積分以得到在整個反應中總反應的還原電量，如 Fig. 32 所示，當
加入 30 wt.%的 DPG 時，可以得到總反應還原電量為 0.380 mC，而
未加入人工石墨粉 DPG 時總反應還原電量為 0.363 mC。加入了 70
wt.%的 DPG 之後，雖然有少量的衍生物可以提高觸媒的分散性，但
9用Scherer’s formula 計算 XRD 鉑的 (220) 峰所得到的平均顆粒大
小變化圖。當 DPG 粉末在載體中所佔的比例越高時，金屬觸媒顆粒
都會有變大的趨勢，而在加入越多的 DPG 粉末之後，因為吸附觸媒
顆粒的活性位置大幅減少，微量的衍生物不足以吸附多出的觸媒奈
米顆粒而造成分散性下降，使得顆粒有成長的趨勢。我們可以由
Scherer’s formula 計算出的結果來反應出不同粉末所繞射出不同比
例的 X 光強度，以 100 wt.% 所製作出的觸媒粉末為基準，使用線段
之間的比例及線性關係，可以大略估計出在 Pt/ C 及 Pt-Ru/C 觸媒中
的奈米碳管所貢獻的量約為 3-6%，不過所算出的量只是一個比較的
量，可以利用這個比較量來評估觸媒載體的性質。
我們將零電位以上的氧化曲線及以下的還原曲線積分之後，得
到了氧化電量及還原電量，而氧化電量及還原電量所帶的電性不
同，如果將兩者的面積相加起來，便可以得到反應的自然電量，也
就是在去除金屬觸媒顆粒的影響之下所得到的電量，如 Fig. 49 所
示，在未加入 DPG 人工石墨時，Pt/C 及 Pt-Ru/C 的自然電量相差很
多，這表示觸媒載體碳黑對整體觸媒的影響很大，加入 DPG 人工石
墨之後，會發現 Pt/C 及 Pt-Ru/C 的自然電量幾乎相等，而 DPG 人工
石墨的含量越多時，內含的 CNTs(carbon nanotubes)也會越多，而自
然電量幾乎相等的原因有可能是因為加入的 CNTs(carbon nanotubes)
並未對整體觸媒有很大的影響。
Fig. 47 為 Pt/C 和 Pt-Ru/C 的交換電流變化圖，由圖中可以看出
在加入 30 wt.% DPG 時，可以發現交換電流值在 Pt/C 和 Pt-Ru/C 系
統中分別上升了 9 %及 19 %，這表示了加入的 DPG 粉末中微量的奈
米碳管會使得觸媒的活性提高。我們可以經由計算交換電流的結果
來反應出不同粉末所貢獻出不同比例的電流強度，以 100 wt.% 所製
作出的觸媒粉末為基準，使用線段之間的比例及線性關係，可以大
略估計出在 Pt/ C 及 Pt-Ru/C 觸媒中的奈米碳管所貢獻的量約為
2-3%，在本公式中，是有假設在含有 Nafion 的狀況下進行估算，而
計算出的結果，也反映出加入了 Nafion 之後的確有助於整體觸媒的
導電度上升，而交換電流也隨之提高。而 Nafion 是一種固體超強酸，
在含有水的環境下具有強力的氧化還原作用，當量測觸媒的循環伏
安時，Nafion 也會對整體的氧化還原反應造成一定程度的影響，在
沒有加 Nafion 時，利用 XRD 的Scherer’s formula所計算出的奈米碳
管含量會比使用交換電流所計算出奈米碳管的量還要多，而加入的

七、計畫結果自評
由於實驗室氟氣設備尚未完全設置完成，故未能針對氟化後的
質子交換膜作進一步的分析，但對於 DPG 和碳黑的比例已有初步的
瞭解，期望在接下來的實驗中，可以將氟氣應用到實驗中，使用氟
氣處理質子交換膜和純化 DPG 中的奈米碳管，如果可以使用純化之
後的奈米碳管作為載體，相信可以提高觸媒的活性。

Boron addition that leads the boron-triggering

2. Experimental 
 
2.1 Dissolution Precipitation Graphite (DPG) 
 According to the papers proposed by Herfurth [27], 
Sadocha and Gruzleski [28], and Double and Hellawell 
[29], the presence of magnesium and cerium will 
contribute to a greater tendency of circumferential growth 
mode of graphene layers which assist the yielding of C60 
and CNTs in DPG. The nodular cast iron was melted 
totally at 1600
 o
C and 1500 ppm in total content of 
ferroboron were added in the melting solution. Amorphous 
carbons were induced to make carbon supersaturated.  
Then, magnesium oxide and cerium dioxide were added 
into the solution. After the carbon dispersed and mixed 
well in the melting solution, the temperature was lowered 
to 1200
 o
C. The precipitated graphite found on melted iron 
surface was named as Dissolution Precipitation Graphite 
(DPG). 
   Small size DPG powders were sieved with different 
screen meshes and treated by 7N HCl for 6 hrs. The 
microstructure and the compositions of the DPG samples 
were assessed via ICP-MS, BET, and field-emission 
scanning electron microscope instrument (FEG-SEM, 
LEO 1530). 
 
2.2 Lithium ion secondary battery  
DPG samples with different amount of MgO or CeO2 
mixed homogeneously with polyvinylidene fluoride 
(PVDF) 1300 as binder and N-methylpyrrolidone (NMP) 
as solvent were coated on copper foil as anodic active 
material of lithium ion secondary battery. The copper foils 
were heated at 150
 o
C until NMP solvent was entirely 
evaporated. In all experiments, the electrolyte contained 
1M LiPF6 /ethylene carbonate (EC) + ethylmethyl 
carbonate (EMC) (1:2 by volume).  The electrode tests at 
room temperature were treated by charge-discharge 
measurements (Maccor, Series 4000) under a constant 
current and voltage range from 1.8 to 0.01V. The electrode 
capacity was calculated according to the weight of active 
materials. 
 
2.3 Catalyst Preparation  
This method we used in our work was based on the 
paper that Watanabe et al. presented in 1987[26]. Four 
different ratio of DPG powder mixed with carbon black 
(XC-72R, Cobot). The mixing powder contained 0 wt.%, 
30 wt.%, 50 wt.% and 70 wt.% DPG powder. 
    TEM image of the Pt nanoparticles were obtained by 
using a JEM-2000FX at 200 kV. Electrochemical activity 
was investigated by cyclic voltammetry method at a scan 
rate of 10 mV s-1 using a three-electrode cell. A Pt foil and 
an Ag/AgCl electrode were counter and reference 
electrodes, respectively. 20 mg catalyst was mixed with 
0.2 ml Nafion solution (5 %) and 0.6 ml distilled water. 20 
µl of this mixture was spread on the surface of glassy 
 
8
supported catalyst could be very much simplified by 
decreasing the thickness of the Nafion film so that film 
diffusion becomes negligible. 
The cyclic voltammograms were recorded in 1N H2SO4. 
Fig.21 shows the two peaks in the cyclic voltammograms 
because hydrogen species adsorbed on the catalyst surface. 
First peak is the weaker adsorbed hydrogen peak at -0.55V. 
The stronger adsorbed hydrogen peak was found at -0.4V. 
Exchange current could be measured from the tafel plot. 
The exchange current (Io) is a good measure of the 
electron-transfer at the interface. Fig.22 shows the cyclic 
voltammograms result of  Pt/C and Pt-Ru/C catalysts.                                                 
 
Few CNTs in DPG powder also made the catalyst 
nanoparticles disperse well. And the DPG powder also 
could be used as support material. Fig.23 The CNTs

the conductivity better but also diminish the CNTs content

4. Conclusion 
 
From SEM images, tubular microstructures of rolled 
graphene layers observed which demonstrate the curling 
effect of magnesium and cerium on derivatives of DPG. 
Increasing pulverized structures appear in the sample of 
higher content addition of MgO or CeO2. Furthermore, 
BET specific surface area analysis confirms indirectly the 
increase of tubular and raised graphene layers because 
MgO or CeO2 was added. 
The first charging capacity had surprising amount 
which exceeded much more than theoretical capacity of 
graphite. The ICP-MS result shows other elements which 
can not produce enough electrochemical reduction to 
affect the great promotion of capacity expect carbon, 
oxygen, and silicon. For this reason, it is believed that 
purely microstructural cause affects. 
The average particle size of 10% Pt/C catalyst 
prepared on 30 wt.% DPG powder and 70 wt.% carbon 
black was 2.26 nm. By the Sherrer formula, the platinum 
particle size could be got. The equation that we used was 
showed below. The content of CNTs was 3.7-6.9% after 
calculating. The exchange current result was based on the 
conductivity of elements of catalysts. The content of CNTs 
was 2.1-3.2% after calculating. Few amount CNTs in DPG 
powder could make the platinum nanoparticles disperse 
well. The I0 increased after replacing carbon black support 
with the mixing powder support. And the DPG powder 
also could be used as support material.
