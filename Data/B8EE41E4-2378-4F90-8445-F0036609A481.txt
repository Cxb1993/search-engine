一、中文摘要 
本研究利用單一頻率深水波至中間性
水深波，水粒子運動軌跡型態與空間上之
演化特性，進一步探討當非線性度增加
時，其與質量傳輸速度之關係及動能、位
能間之影響。研究包含速度向量場、粒子
運動軌跡型態、平均質量傳輸速度與平均
動、位能等四項分析。從實驗結果可知，
在質量傳輸速度方面，發現靠近自由液面
及底床處有一向前之質量傳輸速度，而介
於其間之流場，則為向後之質量傳輸速度
分佈。整體而言，平均質量傳輸速率隨著
非線性度增加而加大，平均動、位能之量
測結果相當接近，且會隨著波高愈高其值
越大，但彼此間之影響並不隨著非限性度
增加而加大。 
關鍵字：粒子影像測速儀、粒子運動軌跡
型態、質量傳輸速度、動能、位能、粒子
軌跡重建法 
Abstract 
    The properties of monochromatic 
frequency wave for the cases varying from 
deep water to intermediate water wave were 
studied. The effect of wave nonlinearity on 
the mean drift velocity, kinematic energy and 
potential energy were investigated. The wave 
velocity field， particle trajectory，mean drift 
velocity，kinematic and potential energy were 
measured and analyzed. It was found that 
positive mean drift velocities exist near the 
free surface and the bottom of the flume and 
negative means occur in between. In general, 
wave nonlinearity increases the magnitude of 
mean drift velocity. The measured kinematic 
energy and potential energy were also found 
to be nearly the same. 
二、緒論 
在線性水波作用下，理論上受波動之
水中質點在一完整週期運動後不產生移
遞，而此種現象會因非線性（如 Stokes 
Wave、Cnoidal Wave 及包含複頻與起始生
成之瞬時驅動作用）增強而變得複雜。水
粒子因波浪運動及受底床剪力作用而產生
遞移效應，稱為質量傳輸。 
最 早 提 出 質 量 傳 輸 概 念 始 於
Stokes(1947)，在無黏性、非旋性的前進波
中，流場質點軌跡經過一週期後並非為一
封閉路徑，而是向水平波浪前進方向飄移
若干距離。其中 Caligany（1878）及 Mitchim
（1940）更進行實驗研究以証明質量傳輸
速度的存在。 
Isobe（1985）指出在橢圓餘弦波的條
件下，僅求解一階解之情況，波浪便具有
非線性與頻散性之特性，此種特性是線性
波所不具有的，因此橢圓餘弦波在研究波
浪非線性特性時，應會更切合實際之物理
現象，但橢圓餘弦波涉及複雜之橢圓餘弦
函數因此在應用上相對的也較為受限，但
隨著許多學者陸續提出橢圓餘弦函數的近
似級數展開式，使其更具應用之可行性，
如 Laitone (1960), Isobe (1985), Tanaka et 
al. (1998)等。 
 Adrian (2006)則針對 Stokes waves 粒
子軌跡進行探討，研究中證明對於一個自
由邊界週期性運動的問題，於非旋轉、非
黏滯流之平坦底床條件下，其粒子運動的
路徑呈現不封閉的軌跡。在一個週期內，
每個粒子皆會經歷前進與後退的運動，由
於波浪的質量傳輸，整體而言是一個向前
漂移的現象。

3.3 波形重複性試驗 
    由於攝影機之鏡頭拍攝的範圍有限，
然而所需拍攝的全部實驗區域相當大，實
驗無法一次同時記錄所有範圍之流場變
化，故必須採用分段拍攝的方式，最後再
將各區域之拍攝影像作連結而形成全域影
像。因此同一入射條件下，波浪之重覆性
就十分重要，故在此必須驗證造波機所造
出的波浪在時間及空間上之重現性是否良
好。 
3.3.1 造波機與取像時間之穩定性分析 
    以一週期為 0.5 s之入射波條件作驗
證，攝影機之位置在距離造波板 5.6 m處，
每次拍攝皆記錄 1364 張影像。由於攝影機
拍攝的範圍有限，所以拍攝完一個段面
後，必須移往至下一個位置拍攝，總共拍
攝四個斷面，才能夠將一完整之空間波長
記錄下來。再利用影像處理將四個斷面的
影像資料作連結，呈現包含了一個完整波
長之波浪空間資訊。在此波浪條件下每個
實驗位置分別於不同時間量測四次，再將
同組之四個斷面影像連接後隨機取樣，取
出第 965 張作比較，其比較結果如圖 3 所
示。由圖中可知，不同時間量測之空間波
形，其水位最大差異為±0.1 cm，其相位重
覆性也相當良好，故可以說明實驗之造波
機與取像時間之穩定性相當良好。

5.2 質量傳輸速度之分析 
往昔的學者在質量傳輸之實驗研究
中，證實的確有質量傳輸速度之存在。
Caligany (1878)與 Mitchim (1940)之實驗皆
發現在靠近自由液面和底床處，有正向之
質量傳輸速度，而介於此範圍間之內部流
場，則有反向之質量傳輸速度產生。另外
陳(2002)利用數值模式解析質量傳輸速度
剖面，在各個波浪條件下均是向後之質量
傳輸速度，由於數值模式採用開放性水槽
的方式進行分析計算，因此排除回流(return 
flow)的產生，推測此一後退量的發生，可
能來自 Piston-type 造波板所產生之高階影
響量。因此本節利用全域影像以及 PIV 粒
子影像速度量測法計算全域流場之質量傳
輸速度結果，以了解當波浪之非線性量增
加時，其與質量傳輸速度間之關係，並針
對粒子運動軌跡的型態於空間上之演化特
性做進一步探討。 
    圖 16(a)~(i)分別為不同實驗波浪條件
下，質量傳輸速度之分析結果，其中左圖

圖 2水波理論屬於弱非線性波，其中圖 16(a)

六、結論與建議  
本文利用 P.I.V.量測系統研究單一頻
率深水波至中間性水深波之質量傳輸與波
形間之關係並計算其動能及位能。在此將
實驗所得之結果歸納為下述幾點： 
1. 在速度場方面： 
    利用粒子軌跡重建演算法計算本
實驗分析速度場之用，其所得之速度
向 量 場 結 果 較 往 昔 學 者 所 用 之
Voronoï 演算法佳。在各個波浪條件
下，隨著波高愈高其水粒子速度愈
大，從全域瞬時速度向量場圖中可
知，在空間上同相位(如波峰與波谷處)
之速度一致性良好，且水粒子速度隨
著水深之遞增是呈現一遞減之趨勢。  
2. 粒子運動軌跡形態： 
    深水波浪運動下，粒子運動軌跡
呈現圓形曲線，隨著水深越接近底床
處，其軌跡圓的直徑呈現一遞減。中
間性水深波 (但因較靠近淺水波條
件)，其粒子運動軌跡呈現較橢圓的軌
跡型態，隨著水深的增加，其橢圓軌
跡則呈現愈趨扁平，軌跡的擺動幅度
亦隨非線性量而增加。水粒子運動軌
跡隨著波高增加，其粒子運動軌跡之
直徑也隨之增加，尤其靠近自由液面
處，經過一週期時間後有微量向前之
漂移產生，且軌跡已不再呈現封閉之
曲線。 
3. 在質量傳輸速度方面： 
    在各波浪條件下，實驗分析所得
之質量傳輸速度皆為負值，推測其可
能原因由於水槽為封閉性，在確認尚
未有反射波產生之情形下，水體感受
到水槽尾端水槽壁阻擋之壓力，進而
造成平均靜水位上升而產生質量堆積
之現象。一般而言，量測結果顯示，
靠近自由表面處，有一正質量傳輸速
度，而於中段水深附近，有逆向流產
生一向後之質量傳輸速度，靠近底床
部份又有一微量向前之質量傳輸速
度，且平均質量傳輸率會隨著非線性
量之增加而越大。其結果與 Caligany 
(1878)、Mitchim (1940)之實驗結果及
陳(2002)數值模式所得之結果趨勢相
當類似。 
4. 在平均動、位能方面： 
    實驗所得之平均動能與平均位能
之大小均很接近，且平均動、位能會
隨著波高愈高其值愈大，平均靜水位
經過一週期後皆上升，平均位能皆較
理論值為大。

ABSTRACT   
 
In this investigation, we considered the wave force on multiple large 
surface-piercing cylinders mounted on a shoal. Instead of conventional 
approaches, e.g. Helmholtz equation for constant water depth or 
ignoring rapid undulations of the bottom, the modified mild-slope 
equation (MMSE) was adopted to calculate the wave potential and the 
linear Bernoulli equation was used to estimate the wave force. About 
the numerical method, we employed the dual reciprocity boundary 
element method (DRBEM) to reduce the corresponding complicated 
domain integration. The numerical results were compared with the 
analytic solutions of Liu and Lin (2007) and MacCamy and Fuchs 
(1954), respectively. Comparisons between numerical results and 
analytic solution showed excellent agreements. We used two types of 
shoal with different slopes as numerical cases in this study. We also 
carried out numerical experiments for incident waves with normal and 
45-degree angles. We found that when the length of incident wave is 
twice the spacing between columns, larger wave height occurs at 
downstream columns, because of interactions between waves and 
columns. The wave force oscillates in sync with incident wave. 
 
KEY WORDS: Dual reciprocity boundary element method; 
wave force; modified mild-slope equation; large structure.  
 
INTRODUCTION 
 
The accuracy in predicting water wave interactions with near-shore 
structures on irregular bathymetry plays an important role in planning, 
designing, constructing and maintaining coastal facilities. Varieties of 
approaches, both theoretically and numerically, have been developed to 
predict the interactions. Cylindrical plies are widely used in coastal and 
ocean engineering, such as for breakwaters, wharfs, lighthouses, 
artificial islands and platforms for research or petroleum extraction. 
The cylindrical structures can be divided into two types by their relative 
diameters to the wavelength: small- and large-scales. The wave force 
exerted on small cylinder structure can be formulated by the Morison 
equation. However, the Morison equation is based on the assumption 
that the presence of the object does not affect the characteristics of the 
incident wave; this equation fails to predict the wave induced forces 
exerted on the large-scale cylinder. As the diffraction effects increases, 
the wave loads exerted on large-scale cylinders must be computed by 
the diffraction theory. The theoretical and numerical investigations into 
large-scale cylindrical structures with the diffraction theory are 
numerous. For example, MacCamy and Fuchs (1954) first derived the 
well-known analytical solution for linear waves force on a pile structure 
with a constant water depth. Besides, Au and Brebbia (1983) computed 
the wave force by using boundary element method (BEM). However, 
the non-homogeneous terms in BEM would lead to complex domain 
integration. The corresponding domain integration can be reduced into 
boundary integration again by using the dual reciprocity boundary 
element method (DRBEM) proposed by Nardini and Brebbia (1983).  
Zhu (1993a) successfully employed the DRBEM to solve the 
mild-slope equation, a well-known wave diffraction-refraction equation, 
and obtained reasonable results. Moreover, Silva, Borthwick and 
Taylor (2005) adopted the modified mild-slope equation (MMSE) 
derived by Chamberlain and Porter (1995) to demonstrate the 
monochromatic wave scattering by multiple cylinders located on a 
constant depth. In their works, the wave energy dissipation terms due to 
wave breaking and bottom friction are considered in the governing 
equation.  
In contrast to previous studies, we will evaluate the wave force on a 
group of four vertical surface-piercing cylinders, which located on a 
conical shoal, using the MMSE as the governing equation. To avoid the 
complicated domain integration, the DRBEM is applied in this 
investigation. By solving the MMSE, the wave potential is obtained and 
the corresponding wave force calculated by integration around the 
cylinder. Furthermore, we conduct a series of numerical experiments 
with different bathymetries and incident wave periods to study the 
difference between conventional MSE and MMSE.

CONCLUSIONS 
 
Based on DRBEM we developed a MMSE numerical model to 
calculate wave forces acting on cylinders mounted on two basic bottom 
profiles. We conducted different numerical cases, including single, 
double and four cylinders. The numerical model is verified with 
analytical solution proposed by Lin and Lin (2007) and MacCamy and 
Fuchs (1954). The maximum relative errors are 0.67% and 2.04%, 
respectively. In addition, comparisons with the experimental data by 
Linton and Evans (1990) also show good agreements.  
From our results we conclude that: 
1. The extended sill top, on which the double and four cylinders 
mount, could lead to larger wave forces. A larger sill slope also 
would induce significant effects by the slope-squared term. 
2. The shoal profile of this paper is a straight line. There are no 
effects of the profile curvature term. Thus, the results of MSE 
show good agreement with those of MSE +F1. 
3. When water waves propagate through multiple cylinders mounted 
on uneven bottom, the magnitude of the wave force on the 
upstream cylinder oscillates. This can be attributed to the 
interactions between cylinders.
