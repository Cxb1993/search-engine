壹、中英文摘要 
由於填充效應與波索蘭反應之優點，近年來水泥基複材添加奈米矽質摻料引起廣泛注
意。示差掃描熱量儀檢測氫氧化鈣之變化可以推演出成核生長、交互作用及擴散三階段水化
動力模式。本研究採用片狀奈米蒙脫土和球狀奈米二氧化矽，並基於環保之故，選擇聚羧酸
系強塑劑。根據水化動力方程式，相臨曲線微分一致之假設，可以獲得各階段間轉換時間。
測試結果顯示，成核生長至交互作用階段之時間與交互作用至擴散階段之時間，在添加奈米
蒙脫土後，分別增加 7 分 22 秒與 12 分 36 秒，在添加奈米二氧化矽後，則分別增加 3 分 32
秒與 7 分 21 秒。此外藉著測定界達電位，奈米卜作嵐材料與強塑劑最佳共同添加效果之比例
植基於個別最佳添加比例。 
Owing to merits of the filling effect and the pozzolanic reaction, Portland cement-based 
composites incorporated with nano siliceous admixtures become a great deal of interest in recent 
years. The change of calcium hydroxide detected by Differential Scanning Calorimetry is used to 
predict kinetic models of hydration in which its evolution is divided into nucleation/growth, 
interaction, and diffusion stages. In this paper, two kinds of nanoscale admixtures are introduced, 
one is the nano-montmorillonite (NM) in sheet shape and the other is the nano-silica(NS) in ball 
form. And for environmental protection, polycarboxylates is selected as superplasticizer. According 
to kinetic equations of hydration, experimental parameters and the assumption that differential 
values of the reactive curve between adjacent stages are equal, the transit time can be acquired. Test 
results show that both the transit time from the stage of nucleation/growth to interaction and the 
transit time from the stage of interaction to diffusion respectively increase 7 minutes 22 seconds and 
12 minutes 36 seconds with NM, and 3 minutes 32 seconds and 7 minutes 21 seconds with NS, 
respectively. By way of zeta potential examinations, the optimum ratio of nano pozzolanic materials 
with superplasticizer is based on best effects of individual additions.

1. Introduction  
Owing to results of the filling effect to reduce porosity as well as the pozzolanic reaction that 
consumes calcium hydroxide (Ca(OH)2) to yield calcium silicate hydrate, Portland cement-based 
composites incorporated with nano siliceous materials become a great deal of interest in recent 
years. Some efforts on strength improvement and permeability elimination of Portland cement 
pastes with nano siliceous admixtures have been reported. Furthermore, such composites contain 
more dense substance and more stable bonding framework through Mercury Intrusion 
Porosimeter (MIP) and Nuclear Magnetic Resonance spectrometer (NMR) examination[1, 2]. 
Besides the mechanical properties and microstructural analyses, the process of hydration being 
also one of major factors for establishing suitable kinetic models is definitively needed for further 
investigation. 
 
2. Instrument and Materials 
The hydration of Portland cement belongs to exothermic reactions, from which well 
simulations of such reaction under various temperatures and heat source with good results have 
been obtained in some researches [3, 4]. The hydration products, for instance, calcium silicate 
hydrates, calcium aluminate sulfate hydrates, calcium hydroxide and so on can be well studied by 
thermal analysis technology, for example, thermogravimetry (TG) method [5, 6]. Among these 
hydration products, the variation of calcium hydroxide happens to have a close relationship with 
the performance of cement hydration. Therefore, the change of calcium hydroxide detected by a 
more delicate thermal measuring instrument like Differential Scanning Calorimetry (DSC) is 
used to predict kinetic models of hydration of two types of nanoscale admixtures in this study.  
The first one is a nano-montmorillonite (NM) in sheet shape with a planar diameter of 100 nm 
(photo 1), and the other is a nano-silica(NS) in ball form with a spherical diameter of 20 nm 
(photo 2).

510oC, that is, transformation of calcium hydroxide exists in this territory [7]. After heating, 
calcium hydroxide of cement hydrates transfers into calcium oxide and water. The decomposing 
formula is described as the following equation.
