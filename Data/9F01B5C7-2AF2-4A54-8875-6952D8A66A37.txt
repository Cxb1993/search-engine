摘   要  
本計畫的目的以類 LIGA 技術製作微研磨工具，共分微影製程與
微複合電鑄製程兩部分探討，微影製程探討 JSR 光阻塗佈厚度、烘烤
時間、曝光時間與顯影時間等；微複合電鑄製程將探討鍍液組成、操
作條件、添加劑與攪拌方式等。最後建立各項製程參數，以得到最佳
製程條件。微型研磨工具進行實際研磨加工測試，根據實際加工結果，
進一步調整參數與加工條件，使微型研磨工具可以實際應用。 
第一年實驗結果顯示：微影製程部分，使用 JSR 光阻單次塗佈可
達 110μm，透過多次塗佈技術亦可得到更高膜厚，並且配合不同幾何
形狀的光罩，可得到不同幾何形狀微研磨工具。複合微電鑄過程使用
平滑劑與應力降低劑配合不同電流密度可獲得不同鎳鍍層硬度值，在
無添加任何添加劑所得鎳鍍層硬度平均值 HV220，添加 10cc 平滑劑與
5cc 應力降低劑時所得鎳鍍層硬度平均值 HV420。電解加工製程透過脈
衝頻率與低電壓控制能有效對高深寬比微孔進行有效電解蝕刻，且較
低電解蝕刻因子。最後建立用類 LIGA 技術製造微鑽石研磨工具的製
程及技術。 
第二年實驗結果顯示：透過微影製程製作光阻母模，再進行電解
加工製程，使用電解加工將鋁合金底部挖孔使鎳鍍層與鋁合金更深入
結合得到更強的黏著度。使用 1000 號砂紙進行磨耗試驗，微磨錠經電
解加工比沒進行加工者，微柱斷裂比例少 3 倍。顯示微磨錠經電解加
工黏著強度較佳。研磨矽晶片加工時，矽晶片表面大部分呈現延性磨
削行為，且粗糙度隨磨削速度愈快，其粗糙度較佳。 
關鍵詞：類 LIGA、微複合電鑄、電解加工、磨耗試驗、研磨加工。

1.3 研究動機與目的  
科技發展日新月異的今日，機械、電子、半導體等工業發展迅速，
所有產品的設計都朝向『短、小、輕、薄』發展，所以微小化製造技
術是必須克服的關鍵。而在現今各種微加工技術中所需要的各式各樣
微工具，能夠有效的生產製造又能達到耐用、節省成本是現今研究的
重點。由於 LIGA 技術具有可產生高深寬比的微結構，及大量生產複
雜、規則及重覆性形狀的能力，因此本研究將使用『類 LIGA』技術
的優點及『精密複合電鑄』技術來研發『微工具』，如微型鑽石磨棒、
微型鑽石研磨盤、微型鑽石刀片等微磨削工具。所以本研究將利用
LIGA-like 製程來製作『鑽石微研磨盤』。 
製作鑽石微研磨盤研究目的列出如下： 
1. 微影技術及精密複合電鑄應用於金屬基底之性質分析: 
(1) 設計與製造『金屬基材』之微影技術用治具、簡易曝光平台與
光罩。 
(2) 設計組裝與測試複合電鑄設備。 
(3) 建立微影與複合微電鑄製程參數。 
(4) 建立金屬基材之微影製程參數，軟烤時間、溫度、曝光時間、
顯影時間等，對光阻種類之微結構幾何形狀、強度等影響。 
(5) 建立鑽石及鎳合金之在複合微電鑄製程參數，如鍍液成分、添
加劑種類、攪拌方式、電流密度、時間，對複合材之微組織硬
度、黏著度等影響。

為了使圖案的轉移能夠完全且精確，曝光機投射在光阻上的圖
案，應該具有一定的焦點深度(Depth of Focus，DOF)，以便使整個光
阻分子，不論是在接近光阻的表面端或是接近晶片端(如圖 2-3 中的 A
及 B 兩點) ，曝光機所提供的解析度，應該至少能含蓋圖中光阻厚度
的 A、B 兩點，所以通常以 DOF 來表示曝光機所能提供的曝光深度，
如下式表示之： 
R＝K1‧λ/ NA                                    (2-1) 
DOF＝K2‧λ/ (NA) 2                               (2-2) 
式中 
R ：解析度 
λ：光源波長。 
K1 ：與光阻材料及製程條件相關的一個常數。

2.4.6 電鑄常見問題與其變因  
根據國內外微電鑄之相關研究，大致歸納出電鑄主要的
影響變因與解決方法 [34-37]：  
一、 鍍液之組成：一般傳統電鑄研究大致都針對鍍液配方，主要是
控制鍍液穩定性、溫度、pH 值、添加劑成分。 
（1） 鍍液穩定性：鍍液中兩種金屬離子的濃度對鍍層組成有直
接的影響。某種金屬離子的濃度增加，鍍層中此種金屬含
量也應增加；錯合物濃度提高，可以降低較穩定錯合金屬
離子在鍍層中的含量，故亦可以調整錯合劑的濃度來達到
合金鍍層要求的金屬比。 
（2） 鍍液溫度：提高溫度能增加金屬鍍層較不活潑金屬的含
量。溫度還將影響錯離子的不穩定常數及造成陽極極化的
各種因素。通常適時溫度控制，有助於電流密度更均勻與
使鑄件性質良好。 
（3）  pH 值：pH 值的控制對電沈積層的影響也非常重要，pH
值的控制對鍍層的外觀、應力、電極效率均有影響。若電
鍍液的 pH 值太低，則加速氫氣的放出並降低陰極效率，使
鍍層的硬度、應力及吸著性減少。pH 值的控制通常均由經
驗獲得，不同電鍍液需控制在不同的 pH 值。添加劑：適當
加入界面活性劑可以降低表面張力，改善鍍液與電極的濕
潤作用，還可以降低鑄件內應力與使鍍層平滑。 
二、 深寬比效應：在電鑄微細深孔時，因其內部電極之過電位達到
氫氣析出之電位時，陰極表面會析出大量氫氣所產生之氣泡容
 
40  
 
（electro-chemical machining, ECM），利用電化學將陽極溶出至陰
極，透過適當的遮蔽或母模，獲得所需形狀、尺寸與表面形狀。由阿
瑞尼（Arrhenius）電離說（theory of electrolytic dissociation）提出電
解質在液體中解離成正負離子，其正負離子跟所帶總電量會相同而使
溶液保持電中性。而且正負離子可在液體中自由移動，通入直流電

授受化學反應，在陰極發生還原反應，陽極發生氧化反應，此現象稱
為電解[38]。將被鍍物置於陽極上，侵入一含有金屬離子 M n+的電解
液中，其在陽極反應為 
M  → M n+ + ne-                             (2-5) 
陰極反應為金屬之解離，即 
    M n+ + ne-  →  M                         (2-6) 
與電鍍原理剛好相反，加工方式為被加工物與電極極小間距（0.02～
0.7mm），透過電解液使被加工物陽極溶出，電壓通常控制在 5 到 20V
之間，電流密度 30 到 200A/cm2，此電流通常大於電鍍。原因電流越
大時，加工速度越快，加工精度也越好，改善加工面粗糙度[39]。

（2）  由於電鍍過程中，在陰極（鋁合金基材）表面上會產
生氫氣泡，氣泡會因表面張力效應附著於陰極表面上，
使得複合鍍層中有氣孔的產生，將會造成複合鍍層強度
降低及表面龜裂現象發生，為了改善這個現象，以提高
陰極轉速使氫氣泡脫離陰極表面。  
2. 超音波振盪：利用超音波振盪產生高頻微幅的振動作
用，來分散凝結成塊的微細鑽石粉末，使得複合鍍層中
的鑽石粉末沒有堆疊現象產生；同時，亦可使附著於陰
極（鋁合金基材）上的氫氣泡脫離，避免於複合鍍層中
產生孔洞，造成複合鍍層強度降低。

2.2 End grinding tests
End grinding tests of the bronze-bonded
diamond composite tools were performed on a
vertical CNC machining center, its engagement
kinematics being shown in Fig.3. The grinding
conditions in the tests are given in Table 2. The
spindle speed/table speed used in the test was
1,000/15, 2,000/25, 3,000/35, and 4000/45
rpm/mm/min, and the depth of cut was fixed at 3m.
Throughout the tests, dry grinding was employed.
The workpiece material used was BK7 glass. For
each tool the available grinding distance between
two dressings and their responding grinding forces,
and surface roughness were measured to evaluate the
tool performance. For each test, normal, feed and
lateral grinding force components were measured by
means of a quartz piezoelectric type dynamometer
(KISTLER type 9257B). Wear behaviors of the
diamond particles and the bond and the appearance
of the ground glass after grinding were examined
using a SEM and a toolmaker’s microscope. Besides, 
the surface roughness of the workpiece after
grinding was examined using a profilometer.
3 Results and discussions
3.1. Grinding forces
The variations of the normal grinding forces
during dry grinding BK7 glass with the available
grinding distance between two dressings were
shown in Fig.4 for the bronze-bonded diamond
composite tools at the spindle speed of 2,000 rpm
and the table speed of 25 mm/min. It can be seen
that the tool C during the dry BK7 glass can
maintain the relatively longest grinding distance of
about 3200 mm between the adjacent two dressing,
and its normal grinding force progressively increases
like-sawtooth until the end of grinding. Causing this
phenomenon may be the effect of the chip removal
during the dry grinding without any aid. Finally, it
can’t work due to the high noise and chater or burn 
as shown in Fig.5. For tool B, the available grinding
distance can maintain to about 1900 mm during the
dry grinding BK7, and its normal grinding force
gradually increases like-sawtooth to about 26 N,
which causes the tool high noise and chatter or burn.
However, for tools A and D, the grinding distance
only can maintain to about 500 mm, which causes
the tools high noise and chater, thereby can’t work 
continuously. Although the grinding forces are
relatively low. But, the chip removal during the dry
grinding BK7 glass is poor. Causing the results of
these diamond composite tools may be attributed to
the difference of the amount of graphite and resin
added into the bronze bond. Because they can
provide lubrication, heat dissipation and chip
removal ability in the grinding zone during the dry
grinding. In the experimental observation the chip
removal ability in the grinding zone is very
important t during the dry grinding BK7 glass.
Fig.6 showed the available grinding distance
with four different grinding conditions for four
diamond composite tools. It can be seen that the tool
C is the relatively longest grinding distance obtained
at the spindle speed of 2000 rpm and the table speed
of 25 mm/min, next to at the spindle speed of 1000
rpm and the table speed of 15 mm/min. At the higher
spindle speed of 3000 rpm and 4000 rpm, the
available grinding distance during the dry grinding is
relatively shorter. This may be due to the higher
grinding temperature to cause the chip difficult to
escape. In addition, the order of the available
grinding distance obtained during the dry grinding
BK7 glass from high to low was diamond tools C >
B > D > A. It implies that the combination of
graphite and resin added into the bronze bond can
produce a better tool life of dry grinding
performance, which provides a multifunction of
lubrication, heat dissipation and chip removal ability
in the grinding zone during the dry grinding BK7
glass. Furthermore, the low spindle speed adopted in
the dry grinding can obtain a relatively better
grinding life.
3.2 Wear behaviors of diamond composite tools
The worn behaviors of the medium zone of
diamond composite tools A, B, C, and D after the
end life of dry grinding BK7 glass at the spindle
speed of 2000 rpm and the table speed of 15
mm/min were shown in Figs.7(a)-(d), respectively.
For diamond tool A, most of diamond grits on the
