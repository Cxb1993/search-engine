應用離子源濺鍍全彩濾光薄膜技術 
研製改善光電顯示元件之效能 
The Improvement on Optical Filter Obtained by a Reactive Ion-Assisted 
Sputter System in LCD Applications

一、中文摘要： 
 
目前市場對光電顯示元件 (opto-electronic 
display)之世代改革中，均以輕薄短小 (具撓曲
性)、快響應速度、低消耗功率、高對比與畫質…
等為主要訴求；尤其在人類視覺感官與光學投影
系統間，所扮演之介面功能則趨形重要。而直接
影響顯示器之彩色表示機制，則需更高品質之彩
色濾光片 (color filters)，其中以三原色 -紅綠藍
(RGB)控制之全彩濾光模組(full color module)所需
之光學薄膜製鍍技術，更決定生動顯示功能之重
要性。因此，如何研發一改善彩色顯示元件之影
像效能，實為本計畫之首要整體目標。 
為使顯示器之彩色影像更清晰生動，本計畫則
依過去研究之成果，以二階段時程目標來研製及
提升三原色(RGB)濾光模組之製鍍技術，以改善光
電顯示器之全彩濾光模組(full color filter module)
效能。吾人首先利用 TFcalc 模擬軟體設計藍(B)、
綠(G)、紅(R)波段的濾光多層膜，以達到能設計出
最佳全彩之濾光模組。其次利用直流磁控離子源
濺鍍系統(DC ion source sputtering system)，於未預
熱之 B270 玻璃基板，製鍍綠(G)色濾光膜，並尋
求綠(G)色濾光膜之最佳光學特性，再依次製鍍最
佳之紅(R)光及藍(B)濾光片。由於所使用之直流磁
控離子源系統，其鍍膜速率無一般直流反應式磁
控濺鍍氧化毒害的問題，並可預期其濺鍍速率較
快；又因此系統具有離子助鍍之效果，其膜質相
當穩定而能得到更高的穿透效果。由於此技術設
計目前尚無任何研究文獻，故具(量產)實用之創新
研發動機。 
同時為了比較出本系統的效益，除濺鍍速率與
透光率外，其他如粗糙度、環境容忍度…等，將
會與一般濺鍍系統比較。再依上述階段最佳製程
條件，研製出非各波段高透光且膜質穩定之光學
多層彩色濾光膜。最後期能從這些製程參數的變
化對薄膜表面的結構的影響的關係中，建立起相
關全彩濾光模組之參考依據，且冀望可充分應用
於新一代光電通訊、數位投影(像)機…等光電顯示
系統。 
 
關鍵詞：光電(顯示)元件(opto-electronic display)、
彩色濾光片(color filters)、全彩濾光模組(full 
color module)、直流磁控離子源濺鍍系統
(DC ion source sputtering system)、離子助鍍。 
 
Abstract 
 
In this paper, the optical properties and 
environment resistance of optical films, which 
directed to the substrate and sputtered both with 
oxygen flowing through and without flowing through 
the ion gun, were investigated and compared. We get a 
smooth surface and high speed deposition rate with 
ion-assisted, and the refractive indices of single-layer 
nb2o5 and sio2 films with ion-assisted were 2.36 and 
1,49, respectively, higher than non-assisted films. We 
also get lower extinction coefficient with ion-assisted. 
Besides, single-layer films with ion-assisted have 
more environment resistance than that without 
ion-assisted after we boiling 2 hours. The multilayer 
filter was baked at 200℃ for 12 hours. The multi-film 
shifts at 50% transmittance (t50) deposited with 
ion-assisted are less than those films without 
ion-assisted. These phenomena show that filter 
deposited with ion-assisted are more stable. Finally 
we deposited an excellent green filter with ion 
assisted.  
Keywords: Organic Light Emitting Device/ Flat 
Panel Display (OLED/FPD), DC Ion Source 
sputtering system, Hardness poly-carbonate (HPC) 
substrate, Anti-reflection (AR) film.

4. CONCLUSION 
The optical thin multilayer film of Nb2O5 and 
SiO2 prepared by reactive ion-assisted dc 
magnetron sputtering have lots of benefits. Pure 
argon and pure oxygen were used as the sputtering 
gas and the reactant, respectively. In order to 
avoid the oxidation of niobium and silicon targets, 
oxygen gas was introduced and ionized by the ion 
gun and toward the B270 glass substrate also 
make high deposition rate . The obtained 
single-layer NB2O5 and SiO2 films have higher 
refractive index and lower extinction coefficient. 
Moreover, These films with ion-assisted make 
surface smoothly with excellent environment 
resistance after 2h boiling. The multi-layer filter 
with ion-assisted shows a stable characteristics 
after baking from 1 to 12h. However, it is 
noticeable shift without ion-assisted, which may 
result from the refractive index of 
non-ion-assisted obviously change in first one 
hour, or ion-assisted make film more compact. We 
also get a better green filter with reactive 
ion-assisted deposition. The above results indicate 
that the reactive ion-assisted dc magnetron 
sputtering is a hopeful coating technique for the 
stable and firm films optical thin film, will useful 
on photoelectric industries.
