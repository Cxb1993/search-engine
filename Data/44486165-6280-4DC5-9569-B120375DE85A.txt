Part one: 利用酵素擠壓法處理高梁以生產酒精的研究 
 
一、摘要 
本研究探討擠壓法液化高梁澱粉之操作因素，擠壓機四段升溫條件控制在
70-80-90-100°C, 擠壓高梁的水分含量控制在35%(w/w)，α-澱粉水解酶的使用量
的變因為每100g 乾燥高梁使用0.6，0.3，或0.15g 的α-澱粉水解酶。本研究探
討5個操作變因進行高梁澱粉的擠壓作業，結果擠壓後的高粱澱粉之還原糖為
16~25g/100 sorghum。擠壓後的高粱(有加蛋白質水解酶)調製成40%(w/v)的高粱
醪並補充0.125g的蛋白質水解酶(Genencor Protex 14L)，16mM尿素，0.5%(w/v)
酵母抽出物進行酒精發酵，結果酒精的生產速率為3.6g/(Lh)，酒精轉換率為 
0.363 g-ethanol/g-sorghum。若發酵酒醪中不加入蛋白質水解酶，則酒精的生
產速率僅為2.15g/(Lh)。由於高梁澱粉被蛋白質包附住，加入蛋白質水解酵素有
助於水解高粱的蛋白質為游離胺基酸，這有助於發酵剛開始階段的酵母的生長。 
關鍵字：高梁，擠壓機，酒精發酵，α-澱粉水解酶，蛋白質水解酶 
 
Abstract 
The extrusion variables were studied to liquefy sorghum starch by a single screw 
extruder. The set point of temperature in the barrel zones were 70 °C for zone 1, 80 
°C for zone 2, 90°C for zone  3, and 100°C for the extruder die. Amylase (NOVO 
BAN 240L) dosage of 0.6, 0.3, or 0.15 (g/100 g sorghum) was investigated, and the 
moisture content was 35 % (w/w). The reducing sugar in five different extruding tests 
is in the range of 16 to 25 g/100 g sorghum. The extruded sorghum starch with 0.125 
g-protease/100 g DM (Genencor Protex 14L), 16 mM urea and 0.5% (w/v) yeast 
extract was employed for ethanol production under very high gravity (40%, w/v). 
Sorghum starch granules are imbedded in a protein matrix that could restrict starch 
gelatinization and enzyme accessibility. Analysis of fermentation broth indicated that 
yeast growth was enhanced by more free amino acids in the early stage of 
fermentation when sorghum starch was extruded with protease. The ethanol 
productivity and yield from sorghum grain (extruded with alpha-amylase and protease) 
was 3.63 g/(L•h) and 0.363 g-ethanol/g-sorghum respectivily. While, that extruded 
sorghum starch without using protease, the ethanol productivity was 2.15 g/(L•h).

4.1 利用擠壓機進行澱粉水解處理 
    影響擠壓機中高梁澱粉水解之還原糖的變因為含水率、擠壓之溫度變化與酵
素量。本研究先以 40%(w/w)含水率進行澱粉之擠壓，結果雖然澱粉水解良好，
但是水分含量太高，在擠壓過程中，會造成澱粉黏度增加，而延長澱粉滯留擠壓
機內之時間，使得進料速率減緩，增加實驗操作中之困難度，因此選擇較低含水
率進行實驗，可促使擠壓過程更順利，但是過低之含水率會減低酵素水解高梁澱
粉的程度，因此本實驗選擇 35%(w/w)含水率進行擠壓。 
    擠壓時轉速快慢會影響高梁澱粉於擠壓機中滯留時間，但是由結果顯示，轉
速快慢對高梁澱粉水解程度之影響不大，但低轉速會延長實驗操作之時間，因此
選擇 40rpm 轉速進行實驗。 
    本實驗使用之澱粉水解酵素為耐高溫型，可操作於 105℃以下之溫度，而溫
度越高，可促進酵素的水解效率，本研究用兩組溫度變因如 70-80-90-100℃以
及 60-70-80-90℃來進行。

1. Introduction 
 
According to the Hubbert curve of peak oil theory, the deposit of global fossil fuel 
will be depleted by 2050. It is imperative to search any alternate energy that can 
reduce the depletion rate. Although debatable in terms of net energy gain (Pimentel 
and Patzek, 2005; Farrel et al., 2006), the bio-fuel is a promising alternative to 
partially substitute petroleum-derived fuel, and more importantly to reduce CO2 
emission, which is regarded as one of the greenhouse gases causing global warming. 
To lower the production cost of bio-ethanol, use of locally grown agricultural crops is 
an imperative choice.  
 
Sorghum, cultivated in the arid area along the west coastline of Taiwan, was used, in 
part, to prevent soil erosion due to strong winds. It is an energy crop and does not 
compete for arable land, making it a potentially good alternative to sugar cane for 
ethanol production (Bulawayo et al., 1996). Sorghum is a starch rich grain, and its 
starch granules are imbedded in a protein matrix. Zhan et al. (2006) applied 
supercritical fluid extrusion to pre-treat sorghum powder followed by α-amylase 
liquefaction and glucoamylase saccharification, resulting in a 5% greater ethanol yield 
than that without supercritical fluid extrusion pre-processing. Additionally, the 
utilization and conversion of sorghum stalk and its bagasse to ethanol were also 
investigated (Gnansounou et al., 2005; Lezinou et al., 1995; Mamma et al., 1995).  
 
Several fermentation technologies have been implemented to convert carbohydrates 
hydrolyzed from sorghum to ethanol; including yeast immobilization onto sodium 
alginate (Liu et al., 2008; Liu and Shen, 2008), and simultaneous saccharification and 
fermentation (Lezinou et al., 1995; Mamma et al., 1996; Suresh et al., 1999). 
Laopaiboon et al (2007) reported that when sweet sorghum juice (18 oBX) 
supplemented with 0.5% ammonium sulphate, a 100 g/L and 120 g/L of ethanol could 
be attained by means of batch and fed-batch fermentation, respectively.  
 
To make ethanol an economically feasible biofuel, any fermentation technology that 
can increase ethanol productivity without major modification of existing processes is 
welcome. Very-high-gravity (VHG) fermentation, originally developed for converting 
corn and wheat mashes to ethanol, is such a fermentation process featuring a high 
initial glucose dose, expecting a higher ethanol yield. However, when yeast exposes to 
a high sugar environment, it suffers from the osmotic stress due to the replacement of 
intracellular water by glucose molecule. As fermentation progresses, the accumulation 
of ethanol in the fermenter results in the inhibition of yeast growth, consequently

2.3 Ethanol fermentation 
Fermentation was performed in a 5 L jar fermenter (Model: CMF-5, Chin-Chi, 
Taiwan) with a working volume of 2 L. The fermentation temperature and agitation 
rate was kept at 27 °C and 50 rpm, respectively. Four grams of active dry yeast was 
rehydrated with 50 mL distilled water before pitching. A stream of sterilized air 
(through a 0.2 μm air filter) was flushed to the headspace of the fermenter at a rate of 
100 mL/min during fermentation to provide the required oxygen for synthesizing 
unsaturated fatty acids and sterols needed to maintain cell membrane integrity. The 
exit gas was condensed at 4 oC before discharge to the atmosphere. During the course 
of fermentation, pH was controlled at 5.0 by the addition of 5 N KOH. 
 
2.4 Analysis 
A 10 mL of fermentation broth was withdrawn. One portion of aliquot was used to 
enumerate yeast cell population under a microscope. Viable and dead cells were 
differentiated by using methylene-violet staining. The yeast cell population counting 
was repeated three times for each sample, and the average and deviation of viable 
yeast cell was measured.  
The remaining portion of aliquot was centrifuged (8000x g, 4 oC) for 30 minutes. 
The decanted supernatant was used to quantify residue glucose by means of an YSI 
glucose analyzer (Model: 2700 Select, Yellow Springs, OH, USA). Ethanol was 
separated at 50 oC by an ion exchange column (model: ICSEP ION-300, 
Transgenomic Inc., Omaha, NE, USA) by using an HPLC (Model: 501, Waters Corp., 
Milford, MA, USA), and detected by a differential refractometer (Model: 410, Waters 
Corp.). The mobile phase consisted of 0.085 N H2SO4, operated at 0.3 mL/min. Free 
amino nitrogen (FAN) was analyzed by the ninhydrin method according to European 
Brewing Convention (1987). 
 
3. Results and discussion 
 
A 22 experimental plan was implemented to investigate the effect of fermentation 
configurations (saccharification followed by fermentation, symbolized as ‘S>F’ verses 
simultaneous saccharification and fermentation, symbolized as ‘SSF’) and the FAN 
content (supplementation verses no supplementation) on the sorghum utilization for 
ethanol production. Hence, four fermentation modes were tested and coded as: A, S>F 
in the absence of FAN content; B, S>F in the presence of FAN content; C, SSF in the 
 16
folds more efficient than mode A. In other words, fermentation mode B could produce 
12% more ethanol than mode A and the fermentation time was significantly reduced 
by 121h. When SSF was operated and the effect of FAN was examined, one found 
that the ethanol productivity of 0.93 and 3.03 g/(L h) was obtained for fermentation 
mode C and D, respectively. These results show that the importance of FAN 
supplementation to enhance ethanol production from sorghum for each respective 
fermentation configuration.  
In the absence of FAN-containing substances, fermentation mode A and C was 
compared (i.e., S>F verses SSF). Fermentation mode C could produce 144 g/L ethanol 
in 156h, meaning that 15.4% more ethanol was attained and 29h less time was needed 
with respect to mode A. When FAN-containing substances was supplemented, SSF 
configuration (i.e., fermentation mode D) could produce 5% more ethanol than S>F 
configuration (i.e., fermentation mode C); or, 39% more efficient in terms of ethanol 
productivity. Given that sorghum contains 69.5% starch on average (Jambunathan and 
Subramanian, 1987), and one gram of starch can be stoichiometrically converted to 
0.567 g ethanol, the maximum attainable ethanol is thus calculated to be 0.394 g. The 
fermentation efficiency (the ratio of actual ethanol concentration divided by the 
maximum attainable ethanol concentration, and multiplied by 100) for fermentation 
mode A, B, C, and D was estimated to be 79, 89, 92, and 94%, respectively. This 
indicates that SSF configuration in conjunction with FAN supplementation was the 
most efficient and effective fermentation mode in the current study.   
 
 
3.3 FAN utilization and viable cell profiles 
 
 
At the onset of the fermentation, a rapid FAN uptake was recorded for all 
fermentation modes. Particularly, the first 24h FAN uptake rate was 68, 84, 75, and 
91% for fermentation mode A, B, C, and D, respectively (Fig. 3). The FAN 
supplementation resulted in a higher uptake rate, indirectly implying an insufficient 
provision of FAN by sorghum mash alone. An initial decline followed by an increase 
in FAN level was observed for four modes. Notably, fermentation modes B and D 
have relatively fast responses in FAN level than modes A and C. This observation 
might partially be attributed to cell lysis, resulting from a rapid accumulation of 
ethanol (Bvochora et al., 2000; Jones and Ingledew, 1994).  
A high initial uptake of FAN might imply incomplete nutritional supply from the 
hydrolyzed sorghum in order to support active propagation of yeast grown under 
VHG conditions. The nutrient imbalance may partially be alleviated by supplementing 
 18
glucose level. Operating SSF supplemented with high FAN doses, a fast fermentation 
rate (or short fermentation time) and a high ethanol production (146 g/L in 48h) was 
attained.  
Utilizing locally grown raw materials for fuel alcohol production is not only an 
optimal choice to lower ethanol production cost, but also to reduce 
soon-to-be-implemented carbon taxes in order to control global carbon dioxide 
emission. In this article, a simple 22 experimental plan was used to investigate the 
applicability of a local crop for possible ethanol production under two fermentation 
configurations. The employed plan can also be used to evaluate the efficacy of other 
different fermentation configurations in the presence or absence of nitrogen 
compounds under VHG conditions.

evolving of the paradigm of agriculture as a supplier of energy and chemicals”. 
 
對於再生性生物酒精的原料來源，纖維素被認為對於糧食供給的衝擊最少，纖維
素來源的原料如乾草、芒草、木材、農業廢棄物如玉米桿、甘蔗渣等，先就生產
技術面不談，就以原料供給面來說，工廠需求面所定的價格仍然遠低於原料供給
面期望的價格，這些價差使得以纖維素原料來生產酒精的可行性降低許多，供給
面的成本包含原料的耕種、收集、運輸。John A. Miranowski, Iowa State University, 
“Biomass production and processing economics: Implications for cost of reducing 
carbon emissions via biofuel”.

除此之外，大量生產的農業是否為永續包括土地的使用、作物輪作、耕作方法、
常用食物的改變等，就是說看來永續性的燃料能否建築在非永續性的農業上。
