 I
摘 要 
台北盆地景美礫石層由於滲透性係數高，因此在一定水力坡降
下，將產生相當高的水壓力。在此等地盤進行深開挖時，常常必須抽
降地下水位以避免產生上舉力破壞。抽降地下水分析所需要的參數為
導水係數及儲水係數。由於台北盆地景美礫石層的不均勻性，各處之
導水係數及儲水係數將有所不同。此外，進行上舉力破壞分析時，常
常未考慮擋土壁與土壤之摩擦力，導致抽降大量的地下水，不但不經
濟，而且亦造成地盤沈陷等問題。有鑑於這些問題，本研究利用進行
中的台北捷運深開挖地下抽降地下水位的過程中，持續監測盆地各處
水位的變化，然後藉著數值方法及數學方法，進行景美礫石層抽水特
性之分析探討，並建立合適的開挖抽水分析方法。最後，本研究將進
行模型試驗，以探討抽降地下水之上舉力分析法，以合理的考慮擋土
壁與周圍土壤的摩擦力。 
 
關鍵詞：深開挖、景美礫石層、抽降地下水、上舉力破壞

2.1.2 台北盆地地下水位變化與補注 
台北盆地之地下水分佈，由於阻水層之存在概分為兩層，一為松
山層中之自由含水層（unconfined aquifer），一為景美層之受壓含水層
（confined aquifer）。早期台北盆地景美層地下水蘊藏豐富，民國前
16 年巴氏調查地下水壓力面時，水頭高於地面 2.1~4.5 公尺（吳建民，
1967、1968），相當於標高 8.5~10.5 公尺間。民國 46 年時則降低至
1.0~地面下 0.75 公尺之間。之後由於大量的鑿井抽取地下水，民國
46 年至 53 年間水位降低約 16~24 公尺平均年降幅為 2.3~3.4 公尺。
至民國 64 年 9 月達到最大洩降量，最低水位面位於標高-49.65 公尺
（吳建民，1987），如圖 2-3。但近三十年來政府管制抽水及地面水之
補注，含水層之水頭已大幅回升，至民國 82 年深層之水頭已回升近
30 公尺（賈儀平，1999），如圖 2-4。在由萬華女中的水位觀測紀錄
得知，民國 90 年時水位已回升至地面下 5 公尺，對於台北盆地深開
挖與隧道工程具潛在性的威脅。 
台北盆地主要含水層的地下水補注區是在新店溪、大漢溪的中游
地區，而基隆河則因坡降較緩，在汐止以西的基盤上方皆是泥層，阻
滯了河水或雨水的下滲，難以補注含水層；經由台北盆地周圍山區、
丘陵及台地深處岩層流向含水層的地下水則為次要的補注源；此外，
由於淺處阻水層之水位高於深處的含水層，也有部份地下水係由阻水
層下滲而來。

2.3.2 數值解 
目前地下水數值模擬分析程式中，較具代表性的地下水流模式有
屬於有限差分法的 MODFLOW（三維）、PLASM（二維），及屬於有
限元素法的 AQUIFEM-1（二維），ㄧ般而言，有限差分法較有限元素
法普遍被運用（Anderson，2000）。過去電腦計算速度緩慢，差分法
格網數量受到限制；但隋著電腦速度提高，格網數量受限越來越小，
也因此差分法優勢越來越大，特別是 MODFLOW，據統計，自 1992
年底以來，美國地質調查局運行的 22 種有關地下水流水量水質數
值模擬計算的程式中，MODFLOW 約佔總應用次數的 41.56%，而
其它的絕大多數模型的使用率都不超過 5.0%，MODFLOW 已經佔
據了最權威的地位（丁繼紅等，2002）。 
近年來台北盆地捷運新莊線施工，跨越淡水河兩側之站體（大
橋國小站、道岔段及台北橋站），都因開挖深度相當深，為了避免
底部因上舉破壞，需抽降景美礫石層水位。歐章煜等（2005）以
MODFLOW 數值程式對大橋國小站、道岔段及台北橋站進行抽降
水模擬分析，其中抽水分析結果顯示大橋站、道岔段及台北橋站，
在未考慮壁體摩擦力時分區開挖所需開啟水井數目最多分別為 8
口、12 口及 12 口水井，與原先設計水井數，分別為 22 口、33 口
及 32 口水井比較得知，研究成果降低許多施工成本。研究案中亦
考慮特殊狀況如抽水機械故障、遇颱風或其他突發狀況導致抽水機
無法運作之替代方案抽水模擬分析。此外，研究也針對大橋站、道
岔段及台北橋站在抽水時，因施工時間重疊可能造成之交互影響。

模組化三維地下水流模式（MODFLOW）是由美國地質調查所研

MODFLOW-96（Harbaugh and McDonald 1996a,1996b）等最初版本已
能夠用來模擬抽水、河川、排水、水位變化、補注和蒸發散量等相關
邊界條件的影響。MODFLOW 內各套件（packages）是由許多人所研
究發展而成，而每ㄧ套件可以是獨立程式碼或與 MODFLOW 互相合
併。一組獨立的套件可透過數據檔案與 MODFLOW 進行資訊交換，
如水平對流傳輸模式 PMPATH （Chiang and Kinzelbach 1994,1998）、
溶質傳輸模式 MT3D （Zheng 1990）和 MT3DMS （Zheng and Wang 
1998）、參數推估程式 PEST （Doherty et al. 1994） 和 UCODE （Poter 
and Hill 1998）都以此方式進行之。而溶質傳輸模式 MOC3D 
（Konikow et al. 1996）和反轉模式 MODFLOWP （Hill 1992）等則
是與 MODFLOW 互相合併。 
PMWIN（Processing MODFLOW for Windows）是由德國 Kassel

水係數 T 對於數值模擬的結果影響相當大，因此由單井試驗進行水理
參數推估之過程相當重要，不可不慎。 
本次以水平單層二維模式分析台北橋站之水位洩降量，在此案例
中以單一參數分析的結果與疊加值相當接近，更加驗證線性疊加之可

工程設計時可參考利用。

6.2 建議 
1. 本研究於單井試驗時，受到部份貫入井效應影響的僅有一口觀測
井，無法去探討部分貫入井影響的範圍及大小。在往後抽水試驗
時，可依照不同距離佈設更多的觀測井，藉此來探討部分貫入井
的效應。 
2. 由數值分析結果來看，分區水理參數所得結果與實際監測值差異
相當大，在加上本研究及過去前人所做之水理參數皆與分區圖上
之參數有差異，因此參數分區圖是否應重新劃分調整，仍值得探
討。 
3. 既然以公式線性疊加能得到不錯的成果，或許能建立一套簡單的
程式，再輸入所需的參數，如水理參數、導水係數、抽水量…等，
就可直接計算出洩降量，減少人為計算的誤差，也方便快速。 
4. 台北盆地試樁資料反推分析顯示，黏土與樁體摩擦力黏著係數基
本上會隨黏土不排水剪力強度增加而下降，不過範圍相當大，本
研究根據試樁資料建立變化範圍之上下限，其次本研究也提出一
保守的建議公式，可以作為評估黏土和連續壁界面摩擦力黏著係
數的方式。 
5. 本研究也設計一套室內試驗儀器來模擬開挖上舉破壞的行為，初
步的試驗結果顯示，重模黏土和混凝土版的黏著係數也介於試樁
資料反推黏著係數之上下限間，接近上限曲線，不過室內試驗並
未考慮穩定液的影響，因此實際的黏著係數會小於試驗結果。基
本上，上述之建議公式仍然可以用以計算黏土與連續壁間之摩擦
力。 
6. 本研究建立一套有限元素分析分法，可以合理地分析開挖引致之

本次大會的主題為「Development of Urban Areas and Geotechnical Engineering」，除此之
外，其目的為提供工程師和科學家一個機會能互相交換觀念、方法和經驗。大會於早上 9：
00分正式舉行開幕式。根據統計，本次會議學術界及工程界參加者約各佔一半，代表大地工
程這個領域注重工程實務的本質，也很符合大地工程的本質。
大會共有 13場 Plenary Session的專題演講.。6場的 parallel sessions論文宣讀，每一場的
主題分別為 Soil-structure interaction, Soil characteristics for geotechnical and geoenvironmental
purposes in urban areas as a basis for geotechnical design, Geotechnical aspects of reconstruction of
historical cities and monuments and preservation, Geotechnical challenges and underground
construction, Application of state-of-the-art geotechnologies in congested urban areas. Use of
monitoring and the observation method.
二、與會心得
國際大地工程學會有很多的技術委員會(Technical Committee)，每個技術委員會負責一個
主題.。本次會議是由 TC38及 TC41共同支持。在會期間，兩個委員會不斷開會討論。使得
許多與會者，白天參加研討會及論文宣讀，晚上參加各種會議，使得許多大師級的學者相當
忙碌活躍。在此也同時發現，台灣的學者不是很活躍，許多重要的場合不見台灣學者參與。
檢討起來，應該是台灣學者平時不常參加國際活動，例如研討會或是技術委員會的討論，以
致於在重要的場合，主事者不會想到台灣學者。但從另一方面來看，參加各種國際會議或技
術委員會不但需要經費支持，也相當花時間。如何在學術研究及參加會議間取得平衡，是一
個值得思考的問題。
在這次研討會中，與許多大師級學者討論新近的研究，聆聽演講，以瞭解近來的趨勢，
均收穫很多。除此之外，筆者也發現，國內學術水準也不差。以筆者的研究領域─深開挖工
程為例，筆者新近的研究成果，與國外學者相比，不僅不遜色，甚至還超過他們。

3. DESCRIPTION OF SOIL SPECIMEN
The soil used in this study was the Taipei silty
clay. The soil was ground into soil powder,
which then passed through ＃40 sieve to make
remolded samples. The reconstituted sample
was prepared by applying a consolidation
pressure to the remolded sample. The remolded
sample was made by adding a sufficient amount
of distilled deionized water to the soil powder
until its water content was 1.5 times greater than
its liquid limit. The remolded sample was then
thoroughly mixed in a mechanical mixer and
transferred in layers into the testing container.
The reconstitution pressure of 100 kPa was
used.
4. TEST PROCEDURE
A total of 8 electroosmotic tests were performed.
First, the soil sample was subjected to 100 kPa
vertical pressures until it reached 90% degree of
consolidation by observing porewater pressure.
Secondly, the effect of electroosmotic treatment
with the injection of chemical solution was
investigated by applying direct current using the
voltage gradients of 50 V/m under the vertical
pressure of 100 kPa. The soils were treated by
injecting chemical solutions at the anode and
drained from the cathode during electroosmosis.
After completion of injection, the
electroosmosis was still processed until finish of
treatment time. During treatment, the settlement
was monitored and the drained water was
observed at the cathode. The soil properties, at
different locations of the test specimen, as
indicated in Fig. 2, were determined before and
after the electroosmotic with injection test.

shear tests.

5.3. Effect of calcium chloride and sodium
silicate
In this series of study, the calcium chloride
solution was injected first, then following the
injection of the sodium silicate solution. The
test procedure was denoted as EO7_C2_N2.
The test result indicates that very stiff piles,
approximately diameter of 6 cm, surrounding
the anode were found, as shown in Fig. 7. Fig. 8
shows the test results for test of EO7_C2_N2
with regard to the distance from the anode, 0.05

electroosmotic test with the injection of the

than those of the only injection of calcium
chloride solution during electroosmosis
(EO7_C1hr). The average increase of undrained

6. DISCUSSION
When calcium ions and silicates are injected
into the soils, in addition to the effects of pure
electroosmosis, the increase of cations in the
soil due to injection of calcium ions and
silicates will result in the increase of the electric
conductivity, which causes more adsorbed water,
along with the cations, to migrate the cathode,
leading to increase of amount of drained water
and undrained shear strength. Moreover, the two
stabilization mechanisms- ion exchanges and
pozzolonic reactions are also responsible for
strength increases due to injection during
electroosmosis. The ion exchanges of calcium
ions in surface of soil particles lead to
flocculation and coagulation of soil particles
into larger colloids (Diamond and Kinter, 1965).
The pozzolonic reaction, resulting from a
chemical reaction between calcium and silicates
or aluminates, leads to the formation of
cementing agents, consisting of calcium silicate
hydrate and calcium aluminate hydrate
(Diamond and Kinter, 1965). The first process is
relatively fast but provides a minor stabilization
effect. The second process, i.e., cementation,
causes the clay particles to bind together and
hence there is an increase in the particle size of
the clay particles. Soils may be strengthened by
bonding with cementing agent (Calcium silicate
hydrate and Calcium aluminate hydrate. Hence,
very stiff piles with diameter of 5-6 cm,
surrounding the anode, will be formed for test
EO7_C2_N2. Moreover, the region of
improvement (from anode to cathode) can be
effectively extended to the cathode by using
increase of injection time for calcium chloride
solution and sodium silicate solution from 1 hr
to 2 day.
7. DISCUSSION
A series of laboratory tests for strengthening of
the soft silty clay using the electroosmotic
injection were performed to assess the
effectiveness of treatment. In this study, the
calcium chloride solution was injected, and then
followed by the sodium silicate solution during
electroosmotic treatment, which causes
cementation between soil particles under
electric field. The undrained shear strength of
the soil was increased significantly. Besides, the
following conclusions may be drawn:
1. Two stabilization mechanisms- ion
exchanges and pozzolonic reactions are
responsible for strength increases owing to
injection of calcium chloride solution and
sodium silicate solution during electroosmosis.
The ion exchanges of calcium ions in surface of
soil particles lead to flocculation and
coagulation of soil particles into larger colloids.
The pozzolonic reaction, resulting from a
chemical reaction between calcium and silicates
or aluminates, leads to the formation of
cementing agents, consisting of calcium silicate
hydrate and calcium aluminate hydrate. Hence,
very stiff piles, with diameter of 5-6 cm, were
formed near anodes due to the effect of
cementation between the two chemical solutions
and soil particles under the electric field. Except
areas of stiff piles, the average undrained shear
strength was increased by 195 % and the
improvement area can also be expanded to the
cathode for a treatment period of 7 days with a
voltage gradient of 50 V/m
2. The electroosmotic time can be certainly
shortened, the region of improvement can be
effectively extended to the cathode, and the
amount of drained water from the cathode be
increased by injecting specific volumes of
calcium chloride solution and sodium silicate
solution during electroosmosis and then pure
electroosmosis after injection, leading to
increase of efficiency of electroosmosis.
However, if the calcium chloride solution was
injected continuously until the end of
electroosmotic test, the strength of soil would
not be increased.Units of measurement,
