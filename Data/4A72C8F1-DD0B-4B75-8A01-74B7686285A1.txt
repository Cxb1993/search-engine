surface plasmon or waveguide modes, owing to the 
momentum conservation. Consequently, the absorption 
efficiency of the solar cell highly depends on the 
incident angle of light. Therefore, the angle 
resolved spectrum is needed for analyzing the optical 
properties as a function of photon energy and 
momentum, i.e. the optical band diagram. It presents 
a lot of resonance modes as the solar cell is 
patterned with micro/nano structure. The conversion 
efficiency of the photocurrent highly depends on the 
spatial distribution of each mode. Through the use of 
the Poynting theorem, the spatial distribution of the 
absorbed photons can be calculated. In other words, 
the spatial distribution of electron-hole pairs can 
be calculated. Combining the information of the 
electron-hole distribution and the Poisson’s 
equation, the relation between the photocurrent and 
the resonance modes with spatially inhomogenous 
distribution can be simulated. The angle resolved 
spectrum can present the information of the optical 
band diagram of a structured solar cell but cannot 
tell us where the mode is localized. Therefore, it 
cannot provide the information about such as ’the 
spatial distribution of the absorption’, ’whether 
the light is absorbed by active layer’ and ’the 
quantum efficiency for each resonance mode’. Thus, 
the relation between the resonance mode and the 
corresponding photocurrent is still deficient.

理上必須滿足動量守恆，使得該太陽能電

關係。在實驗上，角度解析光譜系統可以
提供奈微米結構的光學能帶資訊，但是無

能提供研究者「光子在太陽能電池被吸收

率」等重要資訊，所以無法有效分析共振
模態與光電流的對應關係。

將建立角度解析光譜儀及光電流頻譜系
統，也就是角度解析量子效率量測設備。

技術製作多層表面電漿太陽能電池，多層

Abstract 
    The aim of this project is to develop an 
angle-resolved quantum efficiency 
spectrometer for investing the relation 
between the optical band diagram and the 
quantum efficiency of a solar cell with 
nanoscaled light trapping texture. To couple 
the light into the active layer of the solar cell, 
the free space light with an additional 
momentum provided by the structure should 
be equal to the propagation constant of the 
trapped resonance modes, such as surface 
plasmon or waveguide modes, owing to the 
momentum conservation. Consequently, the 
absorption efficiency of the solar cell highly 
depends on the incident angle of light. 
Therefore, the angle resolved spectrum is 
needed for analyzing the optical properties as 
a function of photon energy and momentum, 
i.e. the optical band diagram. It presents a lot 
of resonance modes as the solar cell is 
patterned with micro/nano structure. The 
conversion efficiency of the photocurrent 
highly depends on the spatial distribution of 
each mode. Through the use of the Poynting 
theorem, the spatial distribution of the 
absorbed photons can be calculated. In other 
words, the spatial distribution of 
electron-hole pairs can be calculated.

BSF)的太陽能電池中，於背電極附近被吸
收的光子很容易產生表面復合，這個時候
在背電極附近的量子效率就會降低，也就
是說，光子轉換成有效電子電洞對的量子
效率是一個空間的函數，這個空間函數在
傳統不具有耐微米結構的太陽能電池大致
上可以忽略，然而，一旦引入了奈微米結
構，在共振的情況下太陽能電池中會產生
一些侷域場(Local field)，造成了吸收的空
間分布劇變，此時，吸收的空間分布以及
光電轉換量子效率的空間分布則變成分析
太陽能電池效率的一個重要課題。
