1中文摘要及關鍵詞
美觀、耐用及健康是牙科贋復體追求之極致目標。全瓷贋復體(all -ceramic
restoration)無論在透光性(translucency)或色調(shade)上均優於陶瓷—金屬贋復體
(ceramic-metal restoration)。歷史悠久之陶瓷—金屬贋復體因透光性不足，具生物相
容性風險，備受爭議，而漸漸被透光性良好且生物相容性較佳之全瓷贋復體取代。但目前
全瓷薄蓋冠(coping)材料相較於金屬仍嫌較脆，延展性亦不足，致使全瓷贋復體較易破
裂，預期使用壽命較短。在材料之改良未臻理想之前，如何利用現有之全瓷贋復體材料，
藉由適當設計之全瓷薄蓋冠厚度，以期達成全瓷贋復體美觀又耐用之目的，是我們關心的
課題。
因義齒需承受咬合力，故本計劃藉由三維有限元素分析法(3D finite element
analysis)評估經CAD/CAM製成薄蓋冠之全瓷牙冠(all-ceramic crown)及其支台齒
(abutment tooth)之應力分佈(stress distribution)模式,以得到較適宜的全瓷牙冠贋復
體復形方式，提供牙醫師做為CAD/CAM製成全瓷牙冠贋復體之參考依據。本研究將探討上
顎右側犬齒及第一大臼齒經特定之牙齒修形設計，再經CAD/CAM製成不同薄蓋冠厚度設計
之全瓷牙冠，在特定咬合力，不同咬合點作用下之應力分佈模式，依據此不同設計之結果
作交叉分析，進而訂定出較適宜之贋復體復形指標，以提供牙醫師做為CAD/CAM製成全瓷
牙冠贋復體復形之參考依據。
經由本研究依據不同設計之結果作交叉分析，於上顎右側犬齒可以得到以下結果:可得到
coping(薄蓋冠)材料的種類，厚度及咬合位置會影響全瓷牙冠(all-ceramic crown)及其
支台齒(abutment tooth)之應力分佈。在進行全瓷假牙製作時，Zirconia 作為 coping 材
料，在臨床上可以使用於應力高處或使用於牙橋製作，但須注意 cement 處位置的破壞。
在使用較薄的 coping(薄蓋冠)材料(切緣 0.7mm、四周軸壁 0.5 ㎜厚度)時，要減少唇側面
齒頸部應力集中，儘量避免此處成為瓷牙破壞點。亦可藉由增加 coping(薄蓋冠)材料厚度
(切緣 1.0mm、四周軸壁 0.7 ㎜厚度)，使 coping 最高應力值發生在舌側面咬合位置，避免
唇側面齒頸部的破壞。咬合位置越接近牙齒切端，會對於唇側面齒頸部造成越大的應力。
臨床上，可修正咬合位置，使其遠離牙齒切端(incisal edge)。另外，若咬合位置接近牙
齒切端，亦可增加 coping(薄蓋冠)材料厚度，以降低 coping 材料本身的應力值，避免破
壞。於上顎第一大臼齒可以得到以下結果: In-Ceram Alumina 作為 coping 材料，若增加
coping(薄蓋冠)材料厚度，可以降低 coping, porcelain(瓷粉),及支台齒(abutment tooth)
的應力值，降低全瓷假牙的破壞機率。
本研究計劃結果，除了可提供牙醫師做為CAD/CAM製成全瓷牙冠贋復體復形之參考依據，
並提供另二項子計劃(力回饋機構模擬系統及CAM系統全瓷牙冠加工)有關牙齒修形與復形
之軟體設計之重要依據，對於整體牙醫、醫工界發展牙齒修形及教育訓練力回饋機構模擬
3英文摘要及關鍵詞
ABSTRACT
Purpose: The purpose of this study was to analyze and compare, by finite element analysis, the
stress distribution in restored crown of right maxillary canine for the risks for failure and the
influence of different coping materials, thickness and loading contact areas. Materials and
Methods: The CAD models of multi-layer all-ceramic crowns for tooth 13 produced with
CAD/CAM technology were translated into a three-dimensional FEA program. Loading was
simulated by applying an occlusal force on palatal surface of maxillary right canine of 120 N at
distinct three positions, including centric occlusion (G1), transitional point (F1) and incisal edge
(E1) by mandibular anterior teeth. Results: Maximum von Mises stresses increased with
increasing Young’s modulus of the coping materials in the sort of preparation, coping material in
different occlusal contact positions. Meanwhile these stresses of coping materials for In-Ceram
zirconia in the veneering porcelain at the incisal edge of contact location were lower than
In-Ceram alumina. The maximum von Mises stresses were found adjacent to labial side of
cervical area in the thin coping group, however, the thick coping group were focused near the
palatal side of centric occlusion area. In the thin coping group these stresses in the sort of coping,
cement and preparation were higher at incisal edge (E1) than centric occlusion (G1), however, in
the thick coping group the maximum von Mises stresses of coping materials were found at the
centric occlusion (G1). Conclusion: FEA utilizing CAD/CAM data can be a successful tool to
develop design guidelines for all-ceramic crowns may increase the longevity of these
restorations.
Key words: finite element analysis, all-ceramic crown, stress, coping material

(一) 研究計畫之前言,目的,文獻探討
美觀及耐用是牙科贋復體追求之極致目標。透光性良好且生物相容性較佳之全瓷贋復體經
不斷研發改良，在美觀及機械性質上均有長足之進步，因此近年來於臨床之應用漸趨普
遍。但是全瓷贋復體材料之機械強度仍嫌不足，若欲加強全瓷贋復體之耐用性，則必須研
發新的機械強度較高的陶瓷薄蓋冠材料，以便支撐上層相對較脆弱的鑲面陶瓷(veneer
ceramic)1。在陶瓷薄蓋冠材料之改良未臻理想之前，利用現有之材料，藉由適當設計之陶
瓷薄蓋冠，達成美觀又耐用之目標，是我們研究的目的。
在考量全瓷牙冠機械強度的同時，有限元素分析法(finite element analysis, FEA)是有
效且能精準分析受力模式之研究方法。有限元素分析法應用於牙醫學研究也有三十多年歷
史，諸如Hojjatie及Anusavice2和Palamara等人3以三維有限元素分析法探討象牙質及贋復
體承受咬合力時之變化。Kamposiora等人4探討贋復體受咬合力時，黏合劑(cement)扮演之
角色。據此種種經驗，我們認為有限元素分析法是探討全瓷牙冠受力模式分析的適當工具。
要建立耐用且美觀之全瓷贋復體，必須先考慮所有可能的影響因素。影響全瓷贋復體耐用
程度之因素很多5，例如贋復牙齒的位置、瓷材之選擇、咬合力大小位置、牙齒修形之設計、
贋復體之設計等重要因素。
首先要考慮的影響因素是贋復之牙齒位置及瓷材之選擇。對前牙而言美觀是重要因素，但
是對後牙而言，材料之機械強度就變成較優先的考量。第一年本研究採用右上犬齒為研究
對象，因犬齒仍屬前牙，美觀因素不可偏廢；但其扮演犬齒引導(canine guidance)咬合
模式中之重要角色，其贋復體之機械強度亦非常重要，不可忽略。本研究將觀察於犬齒引
導中不同施力點導致之應力分佈變化，而有較全面之探討。目前市面上已有多種全瓷贋復
材料，但就機械強度考量，本研究採牙科陶瓷大廠Vita Zahnfabrik生產之In-Ceram
Alumina及In-Ceram Zirconia瓷塊為研究對象來建立模型。臨床上之使用是將瓷塊藉由
CAD/CAM方式切削成薄蓋冠，上面再以其搭配之瓷粉Vita VM7燒製鑲面。廠商宣稱此二種
薄蓋冠材料同時適用於前牙及後牙，可符合上顎犬齒所需之要求。第二年本研究將以右上
顎第一大臼齒為研究對象，因此處為人類咬合力最大之區域。在此位置之機械強度考量相
對較重要，因此薄蓋冠之材料將設定為In-Ceram Alumina。
接著要考慮的影響因素是咬合區域及咬合力大小。上顎犬齒位於牙弓之關鍵位置，無論是
在犬齒引導或群體功能(group function)咬合模式中皆扮演重要角色。本研究將設定120N6
施力於上顎犬齒之舌側咬合面中心咬合位置(centric occlusion) (F1點)、切緣位置(E1
點)、及舌側隆凸位置(cingulum) (G1點)。而上顎第一大臼齒因其咬合面積最大及所在位
置之故，是口腔中咀嚼功能最有效率之處。在正常功能或夜間磨牙狀態下，人類之咬合力
7本研究可評估出陶瓷薄蓋冠之適當設計，以達成全瓷贋復體美觀又耐用之目標。且藉由本
研究(子計劃三)所分析出的全瓷冠假牙復形指標，可提供其餘研究(子計劃一及二)之模型
建立依據。

(三) 研究結果與討論
(1)Zirconia 和 Alumina 不同 coping(薄蓋冠)材料之比較(於上顎犬齒可以得到以下結果)
用 Zirconia 作為 coping 材料，對於 preparation, coping, veneering porcelain 所產
生的應力皆較採用 Alumina 材料低，如表 3-1 所示，且 Zirconia 的彎矩強度(flexural
strength)為 600MPa，也高於 Alumina 的彎矩強度(500MPa)，因此就 coping 耐破壞強度而
言，Zirconia 會較 Alumina 的壽命較長，因此應較適合臨床使用於應力高處或使用於牙橋
製作。
採用 Zirconia 材料所產生 cement 最高應力較 Alumina 材料來的高，如表 3-1 所示，這兩
者最高應力值相差約 15~20%。因此若臨床發生全瓷牙破壞而言，Zirconia 造成 cement 破
壞機會較 Alumina 來的大。用 Zirconia 作 coping 材料時，須特別注意 cement 位置的破
壞。

(四) 結論
依據不同設計之結果作交叉分析，可得到 coping(薄蓋冠)材料的種類，厚度及咬合位置會
影響全瓷牙冠(all-ceramic crown)及其支台齒(abutment tooth)之應力分佈。
於上顎犬齒可以得到以下結論:在進行全瓷假牙製作時，用 Zirconia 作為 coping 材料，
對於 preparation, coping, veneering porcelain 所產生的應力皆較採用 Alumina 材料
低，且彎矩強度(flexural strength)也高於 Alumina 材料，因此 Zirconia 在臨床上可以
使用於應力較高處或使用於牙橋製作，但採用 Zirconia 會產生的問題是在 cement 層的應
力會比採用 Alumina 來的高，因此透過 Zirconia 作 coping 材料時，須特別注意 cement
位置的破壞。
在使用較薄的 coping(薄蓋冠)材料(切緣 0.7mm、四周軸壁 0.5 ㎜厚度)時，coping 最高應
力值皆發生在唇側面齒頸部，故唇側面齒頸部牙齒必需避免修成較尖銳的角度，以減少唇
側面齒頸部應力集中，儘量避免此處成為瓷牙破壞點。亦可藉由增加 coping(薄蓋冠)材料
厚度(切緣 1.0mm、四周軸壁 0.7 ㎜厚度)，使 coping 最高應力值發生在舌側面咬合位置，
避免唇側面齒頸部的破壞。
咬合位置越接近牙齒切端，會對唇側面齒頸部的 coping 材料和 cement 及 preparation 造
成越大的應力。臨床上在進行全瓷假牙製作時，要修正咬合位置，使其遠離牙齒切端
(incisal edge)，以降低唇側面齒頸部 coping 材料和 cement 及 preparation 的應力。另
外，若咬合位置接近牙齒切端，亦可增加 coping(薄蓋冠)材料厚度，以降低 coping 材料
本身的應力值，避免破壞。

1. Introduction
The objective of this study was to evaluate, by 3D- FEA, the influence of the various
coping materials, coping thickness and biting locations on the stress distribution in
CAD/CAM produced all-ceramic crowns. The coping material of all-ceramic crown
has the stronger strength than the veneering ceramic1. The finite element analysis 2-4
was often used in analysis the occlusal force and adhesives in the dental crowns. The
quality of all-ceramic crown were influenced by many factors 5, for example, coping
materials, occlusal force, biting place, tooth preparation type et al. The stress is
equally distributed in the thick coping10, but the thick coping will decrease the
aesthetics. Choosing the better coping materials, designing the proper coping
thickness in the different biting locations to increase the quality of the all-ceramic
crown are our objectives.

2.1. Conversion CAD to FEA models
In the CAD program, the multi-layer crowns are described by the surfaces of each
layer (preparation layer, cement layer, coping layer, and veneering porcelain layer).
The design structure looks like an umbrella with the crossing vertical and horizontal
lines forming surface elements. The horizontal lines have equal distances, giving a
nice looking model. However, in some places the horizontal lines fall together on the
outline, while the vertical lines come together in one or more points. As a
consequence, some surface elements are strongly deformed, some even with no
surface, or with one or two sides with no length. Therefore, two steps were performed
in order to transform the CAD model to FEA format.
First, the various separate surfaces in the Cicero CAD-program were connected in the
FEA model. Second, some crossing points were moved on the vertical lines, and some
points were left out, in order to get a model with well-shaped surface elements.

4. Discussion
4.1 The comparison between coping materials Zirconia and Alumina
highest stress is located at the lingual biting place. Only part of the stress will along
the biting direction, through coping to labial cervix.
When increases the coping depth, and the exerting location is more near the incisal
edge, for example at E1, F1 place, the coping stress will be decreased, as seen in table
2. Thus, the dentist can increase the coping depth for decreasing the coping stress
when manufacture the all-ceramic crown.
4.3 The influence of biting location
The biting location will effect the stress distribution. When the biting place is far from
the tooth cervix, for example E1 place, the stress in coping, cement, and preparation
layers of labial cervix is larger than F1, G1 place, as seen in table 2. Thus, when
biting place is more near the incisal edge, the coping, cement, and preparation layer of
the labial cervix has the bigger stress. In clinics, the dentist can prepare the biting
place not near the incisal edge, for decreasing the stress of the coping, cement, and
preparation layer at labial cervix.
If the coping depth was increased, the highest stress will be at the cingulum (G1), not
near the incisal edge(E1), as seen in table 2. It is because the increasing the coping
depth, the structure strength of coping will also increase. The stress was almost
absorbed by coping material, so the highest stress is located at the lingual biting place.
Only part of the stress will along the biting direction, through coping to labial cervix.
For this study it was assumed that the FEA models did exactly correspond with the
clinical crowns, although the clinically placed crowns might differ significantly from
When biting place is more near the incisal edge, the coping, cement, and preparation
layer of the labial cervix has the bigger stress. In clinics, the dentist can prepare the
biting place not near the incisal edge, for decreasing the stress of the coping, cement,
and preparation layer at labial cervix. If the biting place is near the incisal edge, the
dentist can increases the coping depth for decreasing the coping stress and avoiding
fracture.
This study indicates that following these specific design rules for full ceramic crowns
may increase the longevity of these restorations.
