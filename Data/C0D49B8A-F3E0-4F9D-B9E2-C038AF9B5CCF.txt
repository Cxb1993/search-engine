肆、 結論 
本研究所述之小型壓電馬達，其在相同共振頻率
與壓電陶瓷片厚度條件下，其轉速與驅動電壓成正
比。另外，在相同驅動相角的共振頻率與相同壓電陶
瓷片厚度條件下，由於模態變化的不同，使得順時旋
轉的轉速平均大於逆時旋轉之轉速。再者，在相同共
振頻率與驅動電壓件下，其溫升與壓電陶瓷片厚度成
正比。然而對於瞬時啟動與關閉之小型機電系統而
言，溫升對於轉速與共振頻率的影響可以忽略不計。
本研究應用於推動光學鏡頭之彈性元件，在相同壓電
陶瓷片厚度、共振頻率與驅動電壓條件下，其推動行
程與彈性元件厚度成反比。

Abstract 
This main purpose/motivation of this study is 
discussing a kind of small-scale piezoelectric motor, 
which it is mainly form by piezoelectric stator and 
spiral component. The small-scale piezoelectric motor 
makes use of oval distortion behavior of piezoelectric 
stator to produce rotation motion (clockwise or 
counterclockwise rotation motion), then rotation 
motion to change into translation or vertical (up or 
down) motion through spiral component or screw. 
And the importance of this study lies in this 
small-scale piezoelectric motor can be applied to 
precision position system or electromechanical 
system and done translation or vertical motion. As 
for this study mainly pastes piezoelectric ceramic 
slice separately on the surface in metal tube, let it 
form a kind of piezoelectric stator. In addition, attack 
the tooth to the metal tube in piezoelectric stator, 
make it can hold spiral component and do rotation 
and translation motion. While operating, so long as 
exert driving voltage, resonance frequency and 
different phase angle to piezoelectric stator, can let it 
produce oval distortion. The main results of this 
study lies in, the maximum clockwise and 
counterclockwise rotational speed is 1,415rpm and 
1,376rpm respectively under conditions of 140Vp-p 
driving voltage, 62kHz resonance frequency, 600 
phase angle and 0.5mm thickness of piezoelectric 
ceramic slice. Moreover, the small-scale piezoelectric 
motor can promote the elastic component of 0.3mm 
thickness to produce the displacement of 5.52mm 
under conditions of 140Vp-p driving voltage, 62kHz 
resonance frequency, 600 phase angle, 0.5mm 
thickness of piezoelectric ceramic slice and M3 spiral 
component. 
 
Keywords: Piezoelectric Motor, Piezoelectric Stator, 
Spiral Component, Metal Tube, Piezoelectric 
Ceramic Slice.
