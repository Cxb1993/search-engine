1.前言 
  應用光纖感測器於橋樑工程結構的監測在國內外已有廣泛的研究與應用於
基礎建設或各產業領域中，然而國內鮮少有任何產學界應用此項技術於瀝青鋪面
結構上，而且國外也僅用於單一面層之應變量測與研究。研發團隊應用長週期光
纖光柵(LPG) 感測器於溫度、應變與折射率量測等。光纖感測器由於其徑細質
輕、傳輸頻帶寬、不受電磁干擾、可撓性高、耐高溫及抗蝕性,且易於埋入結構
體、作分佈式多工網路監測，適合應用在鋪面結構智慧型檢測。LPG 之可靠性
和長期穩定測試為這個感測器由登上執行了它在一個具體標本的表面, 並且幾
個溫度週期以被設計的運作的溫度範圍由 110oC 決定是應用的至少 48 個小
時。小 rms 溫度變異(小於 1oC) 被獲得了和溫度的最大變異是 8% 。溫度描述
特性能潛在地使用監測或查出化學變化、腐蝕條件在混凝土結構裡面和惡化在被
加強的或鋼筋混凝土結構裡, 雖然一些改進需要在將來做。這類型緊湊和低廉
LPG 感測器能是高潛力貢獻經濟和安全監視運輸基礎設施, 特別是為混凝土結
構。 
2、研究目的 
計畫之目的在應用長週期光纖光柵(LPG) 感測器於溫度、應變與折射率量測
等。尤其應用在新瀝青材料、新施工法或特殊配合設計方面。計畫執行乃利用研
究團隊已發展出二氧化碳雷射照射方法之原型的 LPG 感測器; 應用於溫度、應
變或折射率量測等，尤其對鋪面材料溫度變化與土木環境中之溶液濃度改變之監
檢測方面探討。 
3、文獻探討 
  光纖技術在最近幾十年廣泛地被使用了為基礎設施監視,主要為結構溫度
和應變監視但最近, 他們被使用查出在混凝土結構裡腐蝕或結構變形[1]。為例
子,光纖感測器被埋置了在幾個修建過的基礎建設結構裡譬如橋梁,水壩和大廈, 
提供可貴的資料在靜態和動態應變 , 溫度變異、風或水壓和結構衛生狀況
[2,3]。混凝土材料或結構管理和安全通常要求週期性監視和維護。結構的行為
的一次準確和長期監視越來越重要因為新大廈和恢復技術被使用並且現有的建
築需要生存未損壞對附近的建築。光纖感測器好處包括光纖感測器由於其徑細
質輕、傳輸頻帶寬、不受電磁干擾、可撓性高、耐高溫及抗蝕性,且易於埋入結
構體、作分佈式多工網路監測。因而,光纖感測器是適當是理想的感測器為潛在
的智慧的結構和材料的應用。特別是可用於混凝土結構的長期健康評估。最近, 
大量的注意集中於長週期光纖光柵(LPGs)，因為 LPGs 的應用由於製造、低損
失、低反射、和特別是高溫、彎曲和裝載敏感性等。這些光柵提供了對光通訊
的寬申請和感覺系統譬如在光纖過濾器[4]和溫度、應變感測器[5]。LPGs 的製
造的幾個技術被展示了。LPGs 由紫外 Laser 的輻照區域通常製造在 193 或 248

3. EXPERIMENTAL 
 
Several methods of writing LPFGs in fiber have been demonstrated10-12. Among them, CO2 laser irradiation has provided 
a simple and highly controllable method for inducing periodic index changes on a bare fiber. The experimental setup for 
writing the LPFGs, as shown in Fig. 2, consisted of a computer-controlled CO2 laser associated with a high-speed optical 
beam scanner to direct and focus the laser at the desired location along the fiber. A translation stage was used to position 
the fiber in the alignment fixture. The CO2 laser (SYNRAD, 48-2) provided a full power of 25 W at a typical frequency 
of 5 kHz (200 µs period), which was probably the best frequency for energy absorption of silica at 10.6 µm wavelength. 
The beam scanner was constructed by a galvanometer mirror and F-theta lenses, capable of scanning the focused laser 
with a 290 µm beam spot across a field size of 110 mm. The two-beam position resolution of the system was about 30 
µm and the maximum speed of moving successive beam pairs could be up to 40 kHz (25 µs). A broadband amplified 
spontaneous emission (ASE) fiber source and a high-resolution spectrum analyzer (ANDO AQ6315A) were used to in-
situ monitor the transmission loss as the grating was written.

We have studied the use of this sensor to measure the concentration of chloride ions in sodium chloride solution. The 
result of LPFG loss peak as a function of concentration of sodium chloride solution was reported in Fig. 7. In this case, 
the experimental data can also be fitted by a liner function (correlation coefficient, R = -0.9924). The concentration 
sensitivity by wavelength shift was -0.058 nm /%. Based on the linear fit to experimental data, the lower limit of 
detection (LOD = 3 / mσ , σ  = standard deviation of transmission loss peak in measuring the blank sample, m = slope) 
of the LPG sensor in pure sodium chloride solution was estimated to be 0.0 4%. A weight concentration standard of 17.5 
% solution was used to determine the accuracy of LPFG sensor. Twenty measurements were taken and the concentration 
values were calculated using the linear fit function. The accuracy of concentration measurement, corresponding to rms 
concentration value obtained from the prediction of linear fit and the standard, was determined to be 0.6 %. The 
precision on the wavelength shift measurements in our experiment is about 0.02 nm. With the use of a commercially 
available optical spectrometer equipped with much higher resolution of 0.1 pm, the minimum detectable chloride 
concentrations as small as 0.0001% for this bare LPFG sensor can be achieved. As shown in Fig. 8, the measured 
response for sodium chloride solutions with the modification of colloidal gold nanoparticles was analyzed and the results 
showed that the sensitivity increased from -0.058 (nm/%) to -0.071 (nm/%).  
 
To study the performance of chloride ions measurement in a typical use conditions, a LPFG sensor, a thermocouple, and 
a chloride ion-selective electrode (ISE) were surface mounted in a concrete specimen for further tests. The thermocouple 
placed near the location of the LPG will be used to monitor the temperature changes during the course of measurement. 
Chloride ISE (CL-BTA,Vernier) was used to calibrate chloride ion concentrations in salt solutions, which were prepared 
by adding pure sodium chloride solid to enough distilled water for 1 L of solution. The relationship between chloride 
concentrations by ppm or mg/L and percent concentration by weight is measured and used for further calibration. We 
have measured the concrete sample immersed in salt water solution with different concentration ranging from 0% to 
20%. The results showed that the LPFG exhibited a linear decrease (R2 = 0.9988) in resonance wavelength shift when the 
concentration increased. The accuracy of salt concentration measurement with this LPFG sensor was calculated to be 
0.6% and the limit of detection for chloride ion was about 0.04 %. The reliability and long-term stability tests for this 
sensor were performed by mounting the LPFG sensor on the surface of the concrete specimen that was immersed in a 
20% salt solution for at least 24 hours. A chloride ISE was also used to compare the performance. The concentration 
variations as small as in the range of 0.1%-0.2 % was observed. The maximum concentration change was about 1%. 
When conducting the same performance tests for the gold-coated LPFG sensor, experimental results showed that the 
accuracy of concentration measurement was better than 0.2% and limit of detection can be improved from 0.04% to 
0.02%. Therefore, results presented in paper showed that gold nanospheres modified on the fiber grating can increase its 
sensitivity in detecting solution concentrations significantly.
