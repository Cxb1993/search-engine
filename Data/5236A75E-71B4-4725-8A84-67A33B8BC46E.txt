1. Introduction 
The effects of edge waves on coastal environments always attract a great dealof 
attention in past years. After Stokes (1864) and Ursell (1952) presented the 
oscillation mode of edge waves, edge waves have become one of the main topics in 
coastal studies.  
Stokes (1864) established the theory and the oscilating modes by considering the 
dispersion relation. Eckart (1951) applied the shallow-water theory to obtain the h-th 
dispersion relation. Ursell focused on the mild-slope case and tried to linearized this 
problem. Longuet-Higgins (1967) and Ball(1967) introduced the rotaional theory 
into the shallow-water theory to derive the exponential-decaying oscillating modes. 
More recently, Baquerizo et al. (2002) used the mode-matching method to acquire 
the head loss of the slope. Kurkin et al. (2002) applied the asymptotic method to 
define the dispersion relation and edge waves, and proved that the wave amplitiude 
will dominate the energy conservation. 
In this study, our main concern is to estabilsh the relation between the amplitude 
and thetopogtraphty. We hope the results can be applied to analyze the influence of 
coastal structures on the wave characteristics.

5. Results and conclusions 
 
Some results are found from our analytical solutions: 
 
1. The larger maximum amplitude of the slope will lead stronger edge waves.  
2. The larger curvature of the topography will result in higher amplitudes of edge 
waves.  
3. Distances between cylinders on the slopes also greatly influence the edge waves. 
In general, smaller distances will generate stronger edge waves.

計畫成果自評： 
 
本研究主要探討因沿岸斜面任意地形變化所引起之岸邊斜面波波動。首先
考慮一穩定均勻流在正弦波形斜面上之水波波動解，利用所得之基本解，配合
傅利葉積分，求得任意地形變化上之水波波動解。本研究目的是要建立之前所
提之地形與沿岸水域波高的關係，並說明其關聯性。希望這些成果可以提供海
洋相關研究人員在沿岸結構物對沿岸海域影響分析上有正面的幫助。 
 
 
自評成果為：本計畫之研究成果符合計畫書中所規劃的研究內容。
