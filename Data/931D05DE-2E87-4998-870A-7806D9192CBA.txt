Correlatively Coded OFDM
will be tend to interfere the adjacent channel. Most of practical applications adopt
the rectangular pulses duo to its suitability for combining with redundant block
transmissions and the fast Fourier transform (FFT) process. But the rectangularly
pulsed OFDM has comparatively large power spectral density (PSD) sidelobes fall
off as f−2, even with hundreds of subcarriers. In order to minimize the signal
distortion over bandlimited channels and diminish the power spilling from the ex-
pectative channels, the subcarriers at edge are not modulated in general. Though
some smoother pulses have been proposed for OFDM systems, these pulses were
not been widely adopted for the complexity of implementation of FFT.
There are lots kinds of correlative codes have been proposed to improve the
spectral performance of conventional OFDM signal with or without cyclic prefix
or zero padding. Correlative coding use correlation to the transmission data in
time domain, then the power spectrum of the transmission signals will be properly
shaped at band edge. In this report, a new correlative coding scheme is created
by Dr. Chung to shape the signal spectrum of the rectangular pulsed OFDM. For
the purpose of convenience, this new correlative coding scheme is denoted by CE-
OFDM throughout this report. The proposed L-th-order CE-OFDM signals have
extremely spectral performance that the equivalent lowpass PSD decays nearly with
a rate f−2(L+1) at spectral band edge. The spectral rolloff at band edge is very
fast even when L is a small postive integer. Motivated by this remarkable feature,
we can conjecture that the portion of spectrum overlap between each subcarrier
at receiving end may be degraded. After inducing ICI, the carrier-to-interference
radio (CIR) of coded OFDM at receiving end may be better than the uncoded one’s.
Moreover, we will be interest in the performance improvement of CE-OFDM after
adopting the ICI self-cancellation scheme of data-conjugate method (DCM).

subcarriers. The causes of ICI can be roughly divided into two parts . Frequency
offsets are generally caused by unmatched local oscillators at the two ends of the
communication system, Doppler effect1 or phase noise are introduced by nonlinear
channel condition. In fact, the FFT filter’s frequency response extend over whole

Conclusion
In this report, we made the ICI analysis for the CE-OFDM signals, including es-
tablishment of mathematical model for CE-CP-OFDM and CE-ZP-OFDM signals
both, making comparison with the uncoded OFDM signals according to different
signal parameters and making the conclusion tables for CE-CP-OFDM and CE-
ZP-OFDM. Afterward the DCM was adopted for reducing the negative influence
of ICI for both types of signals. These analyses are based on the signals definition
and PSD characteristics from [1].
From the numerical results, we can find that CE-OFDM has better CIR per-
formance than uncoded OFDM. In other words, the ICI problem can be effectively
reduced by adopting the CE-OFDM. And we can also find that the coding order L
is the major parameter that will effect the performance of average CIR or minimum
CIR. After adopting DCM, the CIR performance is much better than CE-OFDM
without using DCM and the CIR performance is also better than the uncoded
OFDM with DCM. But the CIR performance of CE-OFDM isn’t easy to make out
what’s the major parameter that will dominate the whole trends of average CIR
or minimum CIR. The reason may be the change of the PSD of CE-OFDM. Even
though the results are not obvious, that can still be a passably information for
constructing the communication system.

第二節 動機

會增加一個等級，而且每個狀態所殘存的分支數目為上一個消除階級的一半。

 第五章 
結論 
 
在本篇報告中，我們主要探討的是如何在相關性編碼正交分頻多工系統的編
碼籬笆結構上消除分支，而後得到一個新的且保有相關性編碼正交分頻多工訊號
優異的頻譜效能的的分支消除相關性編碼正交分頻多工的編碼籬笆結構。 
 在第二章中，首先一開始我們主要探討相關性編碼正交分頻多工系統的架
構，進而說明相關性編碼正交分頻多工訊號在編碼等級為 L 的時，為何其等效基
頻功率頻譜密度為何會隨著 衰減。再來我們探討的是如何用一個籬笆結構
來實現相關性編碼正交分頻多工系統的編碼過程。藉由這個編碼的籬笆結構，接
著我們可以經由集合分割的方法將編碼籬笆結構上的所有分支分配到多個不同的
子集合，使的每個子集合的最小的內部集合距離變大。利用這些分割後的子集合
所擁有的分支，再根據本章中第四節說明的消除分支的兩個規則去找到分支消除
相關性編碼正交分頻多工的編碼籬笆結構。在找到籬笆結構後，實際去由此籬笆
結構的自由距離去計算可能的得到相似編碼增益，而本章的最後，也秀出我們找
到的兩個例子。
