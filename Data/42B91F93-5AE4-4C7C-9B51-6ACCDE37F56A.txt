Abstract 
  Reformed methanol fuel cell （RMFC） technology uses a steam reforming reactor to 
generate fuel-cell-ready hydrogen from a highly concentrated methanol solution. For using 
PEMFC in portable electronic devices, a micro RMFC must be developed to provide hydrogen in 
such an application.  
  In this research, a micro reformer was built for experimental study, which was construed with 
three channels in serpentine shape and had the dimension of 100mm×120mm×15mm. The micro 
channels had a width of 0.75mm and two kinds of depth, i.e. 0.75mm and 1.5mm. Three lengths of 
the channel was designed as 633mm、 1015mm and 1392mm. Commercially available 
CuO-ZnO-Al2O3 catalyst was coated on the surface of the channels for steam reforming. The 
amount of the catalyst was about 0.08g for the top plate and 0.14g for the base.  
  The experimental results show that both the methanol conversion and hydrogen production 
rates increase with reaction temperature in the range of 200-280℃. At 280℃ and 0.005ml/min 
feeding rate of the methanol solution the conversion rate is nearly 100%. But when the feeding rate 
is doubled, the conversion rate drops to 83.4%. However, at this feeding rate 18.0sccm of 
hydrogen is generated, which may be sufficient for a micro PEMFC to provide power output of 
22.5W. The experimental results also show that the conversion rate decreases while the hydrogen 
production rate increases with the feeding rate of the methanol solution. It is also found that when 
the ratio between water and methanol is set at 1.4, optimal methanol conversion rate may be 
obtained. Increasing the length and the cross-sectional area of the channel will extend the time for 
the chemical reaction of the methanol solution, both the methanol conversion and hydrogen 
production rates will thus be increased.   
 
  Keywords: micro, reformer, methanol, steam reforming 
 
摘要 
  本研究目的在於提供微型燃料電池持續性氫源，於此建構一長效型微型重組產氫系統，
並藉不同參數探討其效應與尋求最佳化之契機。重組方法為甲醇蒸氣重組法，此實驗中反應
器本體設計為三上三下蛇行流道，其尺寸設計為100mm×120mm×15mm，單流道截面則有0.75 
mm *0.75 mm與0.75 mm *1.5mm，流道長度分別為633mm、1015mm與1392mm，觸媒選擇為
CuO-ZnO-Al2O3，其塗佈量上下蓋分別為0.08g與0.14g。 
  研究結果顯示在實驗設定之200-280℃區間，隨著反應溫度增加，甲醇轉化率與氫氣產
生率均大幅提升。在280℃時，甲醇進料率0.005ml/min，其轉化率接近100%，氫氣產生率
5.4sccm；若甲醇進料率0.02ml/min，其轉化率83.4%，但氫氣產生率18.0sccm，約可供給微
型質子交換膜燃料電池產生22.5W功率，為本實驗最高值。反應時間方面，在化學反應初期，
增加化學反應的時間能有效促進系統之轉化率，加長反應器流道長度與增大流道截面尺寸則
能增加反應流體之反應時間，提高蒸氣重組系統之甲醇轉化率與氫氣產生率。

1.前言 
  碳氫化合物產生氫氣的重組反應技術
可區分為蒸氣重組法（ steam reforming, 
SR）、部份氧化（partial oxidation）及自發
熱重組（ autothermal reforming, ATR 或
partial oxidative reforming）反應。甲醇蒸氣
重組法之產物氫氣濃度最高(75%) 、CO濃
度最低、甲醇轉化率最高，是為適合發展微
型燃料電池之技術。

取代傳統之二次電池。 
 
2. 研究目的 
  本研究所關切在於可攜帶性電子產品
之能源供應改善，根據文獻蒐集，結合微小
型甲醇重組器與質子交換膜燃料電池之甲
醇重組燃料電池（RMFC）能解決攜氫與儲
氫困擾、具有高氫氣轉化率、避免甲醇與其
它反應後附產品進入與毒化燃料電池等優
點，有較佳潛力成為可攜帶電子領域之替代
性新能源。 
  因此，此研究目的在於建立一高效率之
微型重組器，由改善微型化後所伴隨的效率
低現象，藉由文獻的探討，對舊有小型重組
器進行微型化，根據多方操作變數的設定，
對不同實驗現象進行探討，以求得系統最佳
化，並找出超微化（<1W）的契機。
