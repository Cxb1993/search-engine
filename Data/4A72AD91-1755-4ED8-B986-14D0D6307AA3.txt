2soil organic compounds. The research explained in
the present report was designed as two batch
lab-scale case studies. Case 1 study focused on the
effect of organic matter on biodegradation of total
petroleum hydrocarbon (TPH) in soils, and Case 2
study focused on the effect of sequestration (aging)
on biodegradation of TPH in soils. In both of the two
cases, 1-2 kg wet weight soils were operated in a
controlled laboratory to simulate biopile landfarming
in real sites. Duplicate analyses were conducted in
both of the cases. Case 1 soils were designed as
containing different amounts of total organic carbon
(TOC): P-series with 40.5% of TOC, ST-series with
5.2% of TOC, and ST-C (control) with 1.3% of TOC.
The results showed that the 1st order kinetic k
constants of P-series, ST-series, and ST-C, during
the 1st stage degradation, were 0.0106, 0.0188, and
0.0133 day-1; respectively. It was concluded that
when TOC in soil was relatively high, the 1st order
kinetic k constant of the 1st stage degradation was
relatively small. Also, the final TPH removal
percentages presented the same trend: P-series,
ST-series, and ST-C were 72.7%, 84.4%, and 85.8%,
respectively. When the biopile was operated the
162nd day, newly introduced rhamnolipid
biosurfactant enhanced the degradation in P-series. It
implied that when treating contaminated soils with
high TOC content, adding rhamnolipid could be a
useful option.
Case 2 was designed with artificially
diesel-contaminated soils “aged” for 0 day (ST0),
38 days (ST38), and 101 days (ST101). The results
showed that, with the increase of the sequestration
period, the 1st order k constant for the 1st stage
degradation became small (ST0: 0.0245 day-1, ST38:
0.0128 day-1, ST101: 0.0090 day-1). The same
performance can be found for TPH final removal
percentage: ST0: 87%, ST38:71%, and ST101: 58%,
respectively. Therefore, when soil is freshly
contaminated, bioremediation can be achieved in a
more efficient way.
ㄧ、前言
石油碳氫化合物的生物降解(Biodegradation)
被形容為土壤顆粒、污染物、水分、與微生物之
間的交互作用(Volkering et al., 1998)。特別是當土
壤顆粒與污染物之間的交互作用被強調會被土壤
中有機質(soil organic matters, SOM)所影響，其有
機質的量 (Hatzinger and Alexander, 1995)及性質
(Ortega-Calvo, et al., 1997; Piatt and Brusseau,
1998)，甚至土壤孔隙大小(pore size)與結構(Nam
and Alexander, 1998)都有影響。因此，在開始實施
ㄧ生物復育的計畫之前，如果影響石油碳氫化合
物的生物可及性的因子可以被得知或被預測，將
可決定較有效的處理方法而節省處理的時間。影
響生物可及性的因子當中，土壤中的有機物含量
與風化程度為其中之顯要。曾有文獻指出速率限
制步驟會決定最後污染物的殘餘濃度(residual
concentration)，然而殘餘濃度主要與土壤性質(soil
property)相關(Allen et al., 1997; cited in Nocentini
et al., 2000)，也受污染時間的影響(Williamson et
al., 1997; cited in Nocentini et al., 2000)。甚至有文
獻指出，風化(aging)的問題在當土壤中有機質
(Soil Organic Matters)愈多時會遇嚴重(Edwards et
al., 1957; Hatzinger and Alexander, 1995; cited in
Alexander, 2000)。簡言之，處理被有機化合物污
染的土壤時，土壤中有機質與 aging 的交互作用會
相當影響生物可及性。
造成生物可及性減少的機制在於，在 aging 的
過程當中，有機污染物會被緩慢的吸收
(sorption)，此過程為吸附(partitioning)於土壤中有
機質(soil organic matters, SOM)或穿透(penetration)
於土壤細小孔隙當中(Freeman and Cheung, 1981;
Brusseau et al., 1991, Nam, et al., 1998; cited in
Nam, Chung and Alexander, 1998)。這種有機污染
物的化學分子會緩慢的進入土壤的組成(matrix)當
中，而導致無法被微生物取得(Alexander, 2000)。
這上述的過程便稱為「封存」(sequestration)。特
別當有機污染物持續存在土壤中時間愈久，「封
存」的情形會愈嚴重。尤有甚者，當 aging 的問題
與「封存」於土壤中有機質(SOM)的情形同時存在
時，生物可及性受影響的情形更為值得探討

乳化指數為界面活性劑另一重要指標之一，
界面活性劑可藉由乳化作用，將非水溶性物質分
散於水體中，以達到提升非水溶性物質在水中的
溶解度。本研究參考將馬(2005)將 Cooper 等人在
1987 年發表之方法，量測界面活性劑對柴油的乳
化指數 (Cooper et al., 1987 見馬，2005)。操作步
驟如下:
1. 取 6 mL 柴油與 4 mL 界面活性劑混合，置於試
管中
2. 以 Vortex 強力震盪 2 分鐘
3. 靜置 24 小時後，量測其乳化層高度佔總液體
高度的百分比，即可得到乳化指數(emulsion
index, E24)
4. 若需量測不同 pH 下界面活性劑之乳化指數，
將以磷酸緩衝液配製不同 pH 之界面活性劑
