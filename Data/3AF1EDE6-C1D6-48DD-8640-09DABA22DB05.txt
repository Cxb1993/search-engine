chain. In the proposed model, the multi-level 
arborescent structure, multiple members in each 
level, and the finite planning horizon are 
considered. Appropriate order decisions are made in 
order to minimize the total supply chain cost, 
including the total ordering cost, the total 
purchasing cost, the total transportation cost, and 
the total inventory holding cost. A typical example 
is solved by a nonlinear mathematical programming 
software (i.e., LINGO) and a genetic algorithm 
software (i.e., Gene Hunter), respectively. In the 
experimental design, this project uses the typical 
example to analyze and set the values of the genetic 
algorithm parameters (i.e., population size, mutation 
rate, and so on). This study uses 10 test problems to 
compare the solutions and required CPU times obtained 
from Gene Hunter and LINGO. Finally, a case of 
Taiwanese agricultural product chains is studied. The 
second year of this project studies non-price 
coordination mechanisms. A questionnaire is designed 
and a survey aimed at investigating the situation of 
northern and central Taiwan automobile companies in 
implementing quantity flexibility contracts is 
conducted. Key factors that affect the performance of 
the supply chain are also explored. Apart from 
descriptive statistics, hypothesis test, chi-square 
test, analysis of variance, and factor analysis are 
used for the analysis. This project uses SPSS as an 
analytical tool. The results of the whole project can 
provide important references for practical and 
academic persons who intend to design and implement 
supply chain coordination mechanisms.

折扣協調機制才能降低整體供應鏈總
成本。  
(五)本計畫啟發式演算法輸入當期相關
參數，可求得各階層最佳訂購量、價格、購
買成本、運輸成本，能夠有效減少訂購行政
作業、避免不必要的運送車次、減少發生買
貴的情況，故本計畫協調機制能使供應鏈總
成本下降，提升整體供應鏈之競爭力。 
本計畫第二年可獲得五點重要結論： 
(一) 問卷量表之信度高，問項或題項充
份涵蓋研究主題，足以達成研究目標。 
(二) 「數量彈性契約」、「夥伴關係程
度」、「資訊分享程度」、「物流管理整合程度」
對於供應鏈績效 I (提升投資報酬率)、供應鏈
績效 II(降低物流成本)、供應鏈績效 III(提升
訂單滿足率)、供應鏈績效 IV(提升準時配送
率)與供應鏈績效 V(縮短購置前置時間)與供
應鏈績效 VI(提升存貨週轉率)均達顯著的相
關。 
(三) 由表 5-3 可知，H3：數量彈性契約
對於物流管理整合程度具有顯著正向影響性
是成立的；H4：夥伴關係程度對於物流管理
整合程度具有顯著正向影響性是成立的；
H5：物流管理整合程度對於資訊分享程度具
有顯著正向影響性是成立的；H6：資訊分享
程度對於供應鏈績效具有顯著正向影響性是
成立的；H7：物流管理整合程度對於供應鏈
績效具有顯著正向影響性是成立的；H8：數
量彈性契約對於供應鏈績效具有顯著正向影
響性是成立的；H9：夥伴關係程度對於供應
鏈績效具有顯著正向影響性是成立的。然
而，H1：夥伴關係程度對數量彈性契約有顯
著影響是不成立的，主要原因為鞏固的夥伴
關係是不需要利用數量彈性契約來加強交易

是數量彈性契約不會影響資訊分享程度。

二、與會心得 
     When the growth rate of the manufacturing industry has begun to moderate, 
booming growth in the service sector becomes one key driver contributed to the 
economic development. Service industry has hired the largest labor force and 
made significant contributions to the world’s economics. Over 70% (Taiwan 
67.8% and 73.2% in 2003 and 2009, respectively) of GDP comes from 
service industry in developed countries, and the proportion is more prominent in 
developing countries. This trend has attracted more and more professionals and 
practitioners to join together and present their studies on service systems and 
service management. The objective of the conference is to provide a forum for 
researchers and scholars to discuss and exchange their findings and applications in 
service systems and service management.

3. PROBLEM DESCRIPTION 
The configuration of a SC can be viewed as a network 
of facilities that deploys in a serial or arborescent way. 
Partners in a SC may include suppliers, manufacturers, 
distributors, wholesalers, and retailers. The SC performs 
the procurement of raw materials, transformation to 
intermediate and end-products, distribution and selling of 
the end-products to customers. Each member in the SC 
has limited resources, including workforce, capacity, 
storage space, and so on. Figure 1 shows an example of 
supply chains with multiple objectives.  
Figure 1 An example of multi-objective supply chains 
 
In Figure 1, each level of the SC has only one member. 
The wholesaler negotiates the required quantities with 
the manufacturer based on the demand of the wholesaler. 
The supplier provides the manufacturer with raw 
materials according to the manufacturer’s purchase 
orders. The demand of the wholesaler is deterministic 
and known. Therefore, according to the regular and 
overtime production schedules and the inventory levels 
of raw materials, the manufacturer releases orders to the 
supplier. It is extremely important for all members in the 
SC to closely integrate together in order to gain mutual 
benefits and increase the global competitive advantage. 
Now, the following basic assumptions are made: 
(1) All multiple objectives are transformed into fuzzy 
goals. 
(2) A manufacture produces several products and the bill 
of materials (BOM) of each product is known. 
(3) Only one supplier provides the manufacturer with 
raw materials required to produce each product. 
There is no shortage of raw materials. 
(4) The planning horizon is finite and known. 
(5) The inventory level at the beginning of the planning 
horizon and the minimum stock quantities in each 
period of each product and each raw-material are 
known. 
(6) Delivery lead time between two adjacent members is 
assumed to zero, and thus, the transportation costs of 
shipping raw materials and finished goods are not 
considered. 
(7) The manufacturer (the core member of the SC in 
Figure 1) has three objectives (i.e., maximize the 
profit, minimize the inventory level, and minimize 
the change in workforce level). The supplier or the 
wholesaler has only one objective that maximizes the 
profit. 
(8) The manufacturer uses three resources (i.e., workers, 
capacities, and warehouse space) and each of the 
other two members only considers the constraint of 
the maximum stock quantity. 
(9) The wholesaler’s demand is derived from customer 
needs and is deterministic and known. 
(10) Regular time production has higher priority than 
subcontracting. Subcontracting decision mainly 
depends on whether the unit overtime production cost 
is larger than the unit subcontract cost. It is also 
assumed that the capacity of subcontracting is 
unlimited. 
(11) Related cost functions are linear.

7. CONCLUSIONS 
This paper has modified Sakawa et al.’s approach 
and formulated a FMLMOPP model, in which multiple 
objectives are transformed into fuzzy goals. For this 
reason, the research problem becomes much more 
complex since Sakawa et al.’s model only considered 
one objective at the manufacturer level. The proposed 
model and the efficient two-phase solution procedure can 
be easily adopted by practitioners because it reproduces a 
real-world situation. Several important findings and 
managerial implications are drawn from the results of the 
supply chain negotiation case study. The results indicate 
that the interactive and adjusted process between 
upstream and downstream members is necessary. This 
can maximize the total supply chain profit and the 
satisfactory degree. Future research topics will consider 
arborescent supply chains, new solution procedures, 
and fuzzy parameters.
