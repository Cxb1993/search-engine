Abstract
Random copolymers based on terephthalate acid, succinic acid and ethylene glycol, with
thermally induced shape memory, were synthesized via melt polycondensation. The chemical
structures of these copolymers were ascertained by 1H NMR spectroscopy. The thermal and
viscoelastic characteristics of these copolymers were studied in terms of the acid content using
differential scanning calorimetry and dynamic mechanical analysis. The shape memory effects
of the copolymers were examined using the strain test. The experimental results suggested that
all copolymers exhibited shape memory above the glass transition temperature and that the
highest shape recovery rate was 90%. The shape recovery rates of all test samples declined with
the number of cycles. This decrease in the shape recovery rate may result from the change in
the degree of polymer orientation and/or crystallinity during repeated testing.
Key words: copolyester , shape memory, polycondensation

結果與討論
本研究避開聚合難度較高的寡聚合物方式來製備嵌狀共聚形狀記憶高分子材料 (block
copolymer)，而採用較簡易之aliphatic/ aromatic polyester隨機共聚法來製備具生物相容性與
分解性之新型形狀記憶隨機共聚酯 (random copolymer)。此隨機共聚酯將會藉本身玻璃轉
移溫度來啟動形狀記憶動作，並利用分子鏈間相互作用現象(物理交聯)來維持其在形狀記憶
啟動溫度時仍具高彈態性質。當溫度升溫至共聚物的玻璃轉移溫度之間時，結晶部分分子
鏈則藉較高的熔點溫度維持了整體聚合物高彈態，非結晶分子鏈因受熱鬆動並朝受力方向
排列， 降溫後非結晶分子鏈產生凍結，維持暫時的形狀，最後經由升溫過程回復起始狀態，
