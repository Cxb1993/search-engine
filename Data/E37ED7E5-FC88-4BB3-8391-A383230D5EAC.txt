行政院國家科學委員會補助專題研究計畫成果報告 
台灣西南海岸河川底質特性對海岸潟湖之衝擊影響及潟湖內底
質對螺貝類生物多樣性之相關性研究（1/2） 
The impaction of lagoon sediment properties affected by neighboring 
rivers in southwest coast Taiwan and the correlation of spiral shells biodiversity 
in Tapeng Lagoon 
計畫編號：NSC 95-2221-E-127-004 
執行期間：民國 94 年 08 月 01 日至 95 年 07 月 31 日 
計 畫 主 持 人 ： 廖 少 威   大 仁 科 技 大 學 環 境 資 源 管 理 系

摘  要 
    本研究主要目的是透過因子分析方法瞭
解本海域暨鄰近河川底質特性。結果顯示大
鵬灣暨遴近河川底質環境最主要的潛在因子
為「土壤質地影響因子」、「重金屬影響因
子」、「有機物質影響因子」及「營養鹽影響
因子」。再由因子得點等濃度圖來說明大鵬
灣海域底質污染特性之空間分佈情形：鎘的
含量在湖濱區相對高於其它區域；而鉛、
鉻、鐡、錳、鋁、鎳等含量在囊袋入口處北
端及潟湖東南區及東南湖濱區則高於其它地
區。再以因子分析算得的因子得點，應用群
集分析方法將採樣區分區。分析結果明顯的
可區分出五個群集，此四個群集為：主要
為：大鵬灣囊袋入口處(Ⅰ)、大鵬灣囊袋未
端處(Ⅱ)、林邊溪、高屏溪、東港溪。除少
數採樣點被錯分到林邊溪外，其它各群集都
有很明顯的分群效果。三條河溪的群集底質
特性主要係受到工業廢水、市鎮廢污水、農
事禽畜等養殖廢污水的排入所影響，而大鵬
灣群集除市鎮廢污水及養殖廢污水的排入
外又受到海域生物地質化學反應的影響。在
確定潟湖底質受鄰近河川（高屏溪、東港溪
及林邊溪）之污染影響上，經由典型判別分
析的結果，在建立的二個線性判別函數裏，
所含的判別變數大部份均有顯著性的貢獻。
此兩判別函數以因子結構係數來說明主要影
響參數為孔隙率、鎘、鉻、鋁、鉛，可說明
主要是「重金屬影響因子」，判別能力分別是
82.6%、17.4%。大鵬灣在污染自淨能力上因
只有一囊袋開口與外海流通，灣內外水體交
換能力嚴重不足，平均滯留時間約為 7 天，
可能受當地生物地質化學複雜反應的影響深
遠。故從底質特性的角度並沒辦法統計歸類
出大鵬灣海域底質主要係受何河溪之影響。
然此些方法及結果對於大鵬灣潟湖及鄰近三
河川在水資源之經營、生態保育及決策管理
上必有所幫助上。 
關鍵詞：因子分析、群集分析、判別分析、
大鵬灣潟湖、河川底質特性 
ABSTRACT 
This work investigated sediment samples 
collected from Dapeng Bay and neighboring 
rivers. Factor analysis was performed to 
explain the characteristics and variation of 
sediment quality. The result shows that the 
most important latent factors in Dapeng Bay 
are the soil texture-caused factor, heavy metal 
influence-caused factor, organic matter-caused 
factor, and nutrient-caused factor. From contour 
maps of factor scores it shows heavy metal 
accumulated in the lakesides especially 
southeastward, it was due to biogeochemical 
processes and uncorrelative with its geography. 
Cluster analysis was performed with factor 
scores computed from those latent factors.

4.1 因子分析 
將資料矩陣進行因子分析，整個資料矩
陣可以六個特徵值大於等於 1 的潛在因子

釋能力。本海域及鄰近河川底床環境潛在因
子分別為「土壤質地影響因子」及「重金屬
影響因子」、「重金屬影響因子」、「有機物質
影響因子」及「營養鹽影響因子」。對於大
鵬灣海域底質污染特性之空間分佈情形，以
因子得點等濃度圖來解釋。第一潛在因子中
鎘的因子得點呈現正相關，繪出圖二，第一
因子得點等濃度圖，因呈現正相關，所以圖
中實線愈粗代表濃度愈高虛線愈粗代表濃
度愈低。由圖及原始測值可看出除了 Q1 點
及潟湖入口處的 Q、L 點外，沿湖濱區鎘的
含量相對高於其它區域。第二潛在因子的
鉛、鉻、鐡、錳、鋁、鎳等因子得點亦呈現
正相關，故繪出圖三第二因子得點等濃度分
佈圖，由圖可看出在囊袋入口處北端即 L、
K、M、I 點，及潟湖東南區及東南湖濱區即
O、K、P、G 點處可看出其因子得點值高於
其它地區，顯示此些區域底質重金屬鉛、鉻、

亦呈現正相關，繪出圖六第六因子得點等濃

五、結果與討論 
    由因子分析可看出本海域底質環境潛在
因子主要為「土壤質地影響因子」、「重金屬
影響因子」、「有機物質影響因子」及「營養
鹽影響因子」。並由因子得點等濃度圖可看
出在湖濱區鎘的含量相對高於其它區域。而
鉛、鉻、鐡、錳、鋁、鎳等含量在囊袋入口
處北端及潟湖東南區及東南湖濱區高於其它
地區。再以因子得點為出發點，以群集分析
方法將採樣點作一區分。分析結果可明顯區
分出五個群集，主要為：大鵬灣囊袋入口處
(Ⅰ)、大鵬灣囊袋未端處(Ⅱ)、林邊溪、高
屏溪及東港溪群集。除 Q1、TK7、KP2 三樣
點被錯分到林邊溪外，其它各群集都有很明
顯的分群效果。三條河溪的群集底質特性主
要係受到工業廢水、市鎮廢污水、農事禽畜
等養殖廢污水的排入所影響，而大鵬灣群集
除市鎮廢污水及養殖廢污水的排入外又受
到海域生物地質化學反應的影響。經由典型
判別分析的結果，在建立的二個線性判別函
數裏所含的判別變數均有顯著性的貢獻。且
說明此兩判別函數最主要是「重金屬影響因
子」。這二個判別函數解釋了大鵬灣潟湖鄰近
三河溪底質的變異性，也建構出大鵬灣水域
鄰近三河系的判別函數模式，然從底質特性
角度來看大鵬灣海域底質與鄰近三河溪底質
特性不儘相似，可能受當地生物地質化學反
應的影響。本研究之方法及成果應用於其它
相同性質之棲境在環境資源之經營管理上，
將可提供莫大助益。

一、參加會議經過 
2006年 IWA年會於九月 10日中國北京召開，由 IWA主席 László Somlyódy與中國建設
部副部長仇保興共同主持。László Somlyódy以奈米污染物對水質影響，歐盟水權統一構想，
以及中國水資源迫切需求等為新議題，提出與來自世界各地學者專家共同分享。中國國務院
副總理曾培炎亦發表重要講話，並提出水資源對中國之重要性。此次年會分八大類別進行研
討，發表論文共超過 800篇，更有近 300篇海報論文與近 280家廠商參展。閉幕式則宣告下
一屆 2008年會，移師維也納舉行，而閉幕晚宴則於人民大會堂舉辦。 
 
二、與會心得 
會議期間，外國多家廠商共襄盛舉。亞洲國家則以韓國及新加坡最受矚目，韓國以水及
廢水協會(KWWA)為主體，而新加坡則以龐大民間企業，尤其是薄膜處理業者，此兩國家以
大型攤位參展，相當醒目，以此也可瞭解此兩國家進軍大陸水處理業之旺盛企圖心。另外韓
國及日本在論文發表數量上，亦見其研究動能之雄心。藉此，我國實應積極投入研究動力，
並刺激相關產業之成長。

Water quality assessment and apportionment source of 
pollution from neighboring rivers in Tapeng Lagoon 
(Taiwan) using multivariate analysis  a case study

Abstract: Factor analysis was conducted to explain the characteristics and the variation in 
the quality of water during the disassembly of oyster frames and fishery boxes. The result 
shows that the most important latent factors in Tapeng Lagoon are the ocean factor, primary 
productivity factor, and the fishery pollution factor. Canonical discriminant analysis is 
applied to identify the source of pollution in neighboring rivers outside Tapeng Lagoon. The 
two constructed discriminant functions (CDFs) showed markedly contribution to all 
discriminant variables, and that total nitrogen, algae, dissolved oxygen and total phosphate, 
combined in the nutrient effect factor. The recognition capacities in these two CDFs were 
95.6%, 4.4%, respectively. The water quality in the Kaoping river most strongly determined 
that in the Tapeng Lagoon the best is. And disassembling the oyster frames and fishery boxes 
improves the water quality markedly. However, environmental topographic conditions 
indicate that strengthening stream pollution prevention and to constructing another entrance 
to the ocean are the best approaches for improving the quality of water in Tapeng Lagoon, 
especially by reducing eutrophication. These approaches and results yield useful information 
concerning habitat recovery and water resource management.  
Keywords: canonical discriminant analysis; factor analysis; Tapeng Lagoon; water quality  
Introduction  
In this work, the data in the large database obtained during the development of 
disassembling the oyster frames and fishery boxes in the Tapeng Lagoon and in the 
neighboring rivers were analyzed using various multivariate statistical approaches to extract 
information on the similarities or dissimilarities among the sampling sites, the identification 
of the water quality variables that cause spatial and temporal variations in the quality of 
lagoon water, the hidden factors that govern the structure of the database, the effect of the 
neighboring rivers on the water quality parameters and the identification of sources of 
pollution from neighboring rivers. In conclusion, the quality of water in rivers and the lagoon 
were analyzed. Secondly, factor scores were determined from a factor analysis of water 
variables, which were used to determine spatial and temporal variations of water quality in 
Tapeng Lagoon. Thirdly, canonical discriminant analysis was conducted to construct the 
functions of monitoring parameters and identify to estimate the contribution of possible 
sources to the concentration of the parameters that are associated with neighboring rivers. 
The aim of this work is to perform multivariate analysis and demonstrate its applicability and 
effectiveness in environment research. This is the first study in Taiwan that takes such an

Discriminant analysis 
Total standardized canonical coefficients specify the joint effects of independent variables of 
a given CDF, so are more informative than the total canonical structure coefficients (Rencher, 
1992). Total standardized canonical coefficients can be misleading when independent 
variables are related (Cruze-Castillo et al., 1994). In this work, the total canonical structure 
coefficients were used to interpret the CDFs because significant correlations obtained among 
some of the independent variables (not showed in the text), and the total standardized 
canonical coefficients was used to yield the CDFs.  
A forward stepwise approach was applied to determine which variables could be 
incorporated in the model (StatSoft, 1996). Therefore, an F-test was conducted to identify the 
most discriminating variables. The process was terminated when the differences ceased to be 
significant. Table 2 shows that all of these discriminant variables were significant included, 
according to Wilk s Lambda test. The order of inclusion in the model, according to the F-test, 
was - TKN, chlorophyl-a, algae, DO, HPC, EC, UV-254, TP, T, pH, Cl, TS, SO4-2 and Alk. In 
these models the main factors were the ocean factor, the nutrient factor, the dissolved oxygen 
factor, the primary productivity factor and the environmental pollution factor. Accordingly, 
these rivers vary greatly. Some parts of these rivers were polluted by domestic wastewater
