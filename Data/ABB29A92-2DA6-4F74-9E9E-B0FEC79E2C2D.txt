(4)研究方法 
2.1實驗材料 
實驗中所使用的材料為 3mm 厚
Ti-15V-3Cr-3Al-3Sn合金板材，其化學成份為
75.70 Ti、15.10 V、2.92 Cr、3.05 Al、2.99 Sn、
0.071 Fe、0.016 C、0.12 O、0.014 N、0.021 S。 
2.2雷射銲接 
雷射銲接系統為 Rofin-Sinar RS850 5KW 
CO2 橫流式雷射加工機。其氣流方向、陰陽
極放電方向以及雷射共振輸出三軸相互垂直
之雷射車體。在銲接前，需清潔試片表面，
以避免銲接時有雜質、油垢污染銲道而影響
銲道品質。在銲接時，為避免銲件受熱應力
作用而變形，而使用銲接夾具固定其銲件。
同時，於銲道背面通入氬氣，防止銲件在高
溫中與氧反應而氧化。雷射銲接是在大氣中
進行，因此保護氣罩( Shielding Devivce )的使
用也是相當重要，吹驅氣體為氦氣，氦氣可
保護雷射聚焦鏡片不受銲接金屬蒸氣和濺渣
之汙染，而氣罩所通入之氬氣則可隔絕大
氣、保護銲道不受氧化。Ti-6Al-4V銲接參數
為 功 率 3200W 、 銲 速 800mm/min ；
Ti-15V-3Cr-3Al-3Sn 銲 接 參 數 為 功 率
3000W、銲速 1000mm/min。 
2.3時效熱處理 
鈦合金於高溫下極易氧化，進行時效熱
處理或銲後熱處理時，環境須在高真空下進
行。將 Ti-15V-3Cr-3Al-3Sn 板材進行時效處
理，時效時間為持溫 8 小時，其試片時效條
件為室溫、371℃(700℉)、426℃(800℉)、482
℃(900℉)、538℃(1000℉)、593℃(1100℉)。
試片皆在持溫後空冷至室溫。 
2.4高溫缺口拉伸試驗 
高溫缺口拉伸試驗使用 10噸MTS810材
料試驗機。測試試片為 Ti-15V-3Cr-3Al-3Sn
條件為原始母材、銲後原始狀態與母材和銲
件經 371℃/8hr、426℃/8hr、482℃/8hr、538
℃/8hr、593℃/8hr 五組不同溫度熱處理試
片。測試溫度為室溫、150℃、300℃及 450
℃四種環境溫度下進行缺口拉伸試驗，拉伸
速率為 0.75mm/min。 
2.5疲勞裂縫成長試驗 
疲勞裂縫成長試驗採用 10 頓 MTS 810
油壓伺服控制動態試驗機，試驗系統與電腦
連線以控制試驗參數並存取試驗數據，試驗
分析軟體為 MTS 公司發展的 759.40 Crack 
Growth Test。試驗材料為 Ti-15V-3Cr-3Al-3Sn
母材經不同時效熱處理之試件，疲勞裂縫成
長試驗依 ASTM E647-91標準規範製作。疲
勞試驗分成兩階段進行，第一階段進行疲勞
預裂(Fatigue Precracked)，預裂長度設定為
2mm，以頻率 20Hz及 Final Kmax遞減的方式
進行，選擇適當參數使裂縫儘可能接近自然
裂縫(Natural Crack)。在預裂過程中可修正最
終之 Kmax 以避免預裂時間過長。預裂完成
後，以頻率 20Hz、定振幅負荷(Constant load)
的方式(負載波型為正弦波)於大氣環境下進
行疲勞裂縫成長試驗(Crack Growth Test)，實
驗應力比(stress ratio)分為 R=0.1及 R=0.5兩
種條件。本實驗亦針對 Ti-15V-3Cr-3Al-3Sn
進行觀察在不同 ΔK值破裂面之形態。

2. Ti-15-3 板材的缺口拉伸試驗中，室溫環
境下，低溫時效試件其缺口拉伸強度與延展
性皆不佳，破斷面呈現大量劈裂面及混些許
窩穴狀破裂特徵，而高溫時效試件則相對有
較優異之強度與延展性，隨著環境溫度的提
升，低溫時效試件強度與延展性會有顯著增
加，而高溫時效條件則是受到高溫軟化的影
響，雖延展性持續小幅的增加，但強度則是
逐漸弱化，破裂面呈現大面積窩穴破裂特徵。 
3. Ti-15-3 銲件的缺口拉伸試驗中，巨觀破
裂特徵大體上與板材類同破裂趨勢，且所有
破壞面皆呈現銲件凝固組織之破壞形態，在
室溫環境下，受高溫時效處理後之銲件，會
有較高缺口拉伸強度及延展性，隨環境溫度
的提升，低溫時效處理之銲件受到延展性的
增加，缺口脆化效應下降，使缺口拉伸強度
均有增加，而高溫時效處理 593℃之銲件，則
受到高溫環境材料軟化的影響，缺口拉伸強
度有明顯的弱化。 
4. Ti-15-3 板材常溫疲勞裂縫成長試驗中，
頂時效 426℃/8hr 試件顯示最高之疲勞裂縫
成長速率，破裂特徵主要為穿晶混雜沿晶，
而 371℃/8hr 試件於 0.1R 時，呈現曲折裂縫
成長，因而有最低疲勞裂縫成長速率，破壞
面顯示在不同晶粒間會沿特定結晶面成長，
隨 ΔK 增加呈現大範圍劈裂狀及較深的溝狀
破裂特徵，時效溫度達 482℃以上時，抵抗裂
縫成長能力逐漸提升，斷面上主要可看到粗
大條紋狀的穿晶疲勞破壞特徵。可以注意到
的是，在 R 為 0.5 之 371℃試件其曲線斜率
(4.42)遠高於其它試件(2.31-2.61)斜率，原因
為當 Kmax趨近於 Kc，裂縫便不再遵循 Paris 
Law 而開始不穩定加速成長，因而造成此結
果。

 Fatigue crack growth tests according to ASTM E647-91 standard were performed on 
compact-tension (CT) specimens at room temperature in a computerized MTS hydaulic testing 
system. In fatigue crack growth tests, the loading frequency is 20Hz with constant amplitude of 
sinusoidal waveform at the stress ratio (R) of 0.1 or 0.5 throughout the test. Crack length was 
estimated on-line using a crack opening displacement gage mounted at the specimen edge. The 
compliance function provided by Saxena and Hudak [9] was used to determine the crack length and 
confirmed by a traveling microscope at a magnification of 30. The fatigue-fractured appearances of 
assorted specimens were examined by a Hitachi S4800 scanning electron microscope (SEM). The 
detailed microstructures of the specimens were examined by using a JEOL-2000EX transmission 
electron microscope (TEM). 
Results and discussion 
The as-received specimen consisted of coarse equiaxed β grains without any observable 
precipitates. SEM metallograph showed needle-like fine α precipitates formed in the matrix and at 
the prior β boundaries of the A426 specimen (Fig. 1(a)). TEM micrograph revealed lath α 
distributed uniformly in the matrix (Fig. 1(b)). The coarsening of α precipitates became more 
significant with an increase in aging temperature, especially above 538°C. In the A593 specimen, 
the coarsening of α precipitates as well as the decreased number and density of the lath-like α 
account for the lowered strength and hardness of the specimen (Fig. 1(c)). Besides, the prior β grain 
boundaries were profiled with α precipitates.   
The results of microhardness measurements indicated a minor increase in hardness for the 317°C 
aged specimen (Hv 280) as compared with the as-received specimen (Hv 270), which was due to 
the formation of a small amount of ultra-fine ω (hcp) precipitates. In case of the specimen aged at 
371°C, a fluctuation in hardness between Hv 340 and 365 was obtained. The results also indicated 
that the A426 specimen had the highest hardness (Hv 440) among the aged specimens. Over-aging 
the Ti-15-3 alloy at/above 482oC caused an obvious drop in hardness and was associated with the 
coarsening of α precipitates. Additionally, the hardness of the A593 specimen (Hv 310) was slightly 
higher than that of the A317 (under-aged) specimen. In previous study [10], the 371oC aged weld 
was most susceptible to notch sensitivity among the tested specimens due to severe cleavage-like 
fracture [10]. It was suggested not to use the Ti-15-3 alloy in the under-aged condition. In those 
welds aged at or above 482oC, the coarse columnar structure was prone to interdendritic fracture 
during notched tensile tests [10].   
The fatigue crack growth behaviors of aged Ti-15-3 specimens are shown in Fig.2. The results 
indicated the peak-aged specimen had the highest FCGRs, while, the 593oC aged one exhibited the 
best resistance to fatigue crack growth (Fig. 2(a)). It was noticed the difference in FCGRs between 
A426 and A482 specimens was minor at R of 0.1. At the R of 0.5, the FCGRs of all specimens 
raised as a whole (Fig. 2(b)). However, the FCGRs of the specimen tested at R of 0.5 showed the 
same trend as those specimens tested at R of 0.1, i.e., increasing the aging temperature caused a 
decrease in FCGRs of the specimen. It was worth mentioning the FCGRs of A482 specimen was 
greater than those of the A426 in the high △K range at R of 0.5.

ranges.  The macro-fracture appearance of various specimens was quite similar, showing a very 
irregularly rough surface (Figs. 3(a)&(b)). Sometimes, coarse stripes were more likely to be found
