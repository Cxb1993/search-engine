國家科學委員會專題研究計畫成果報告
不同輕質骨材對輕質骨材混凝土之耐久性影響
Durability of Reinforced Lightweight Concrete Subject to Different
Lightweight Aggregates
計劃編號：NSC-97-2221-E324-037
執行期限：97 年 8 月 1 日~98 年 9 月 31 日
主持人：李明君 朝陽科技大學營建工程研究所
摘要
本研究針對『不同輕質骨材對輕質骨材混凝土
之耐久性』進行探討評估，配比設計包括普通混凝
土、兩種輕質骨材混凝土與輕質骨材透水混凝土，
共四種混凝土配比，本期主要是針對輕質骨材混凝
土之鋼筋腐蝕研究，鋼筋腐蝕惡化環境包括海水(鹽
害)乾濕浸泡與二氧化碳加速碳化（中性化）反應二
種，於上述環境下進行相關測試，包括（1）抗壓強
度，（2）抗彎強度，（3）劈張強度，（4）碳化深度，
（5）鋼筋腐蝕電位量檢測，（6）吸水率。
研究結果顯示，使用比重較高輕質骨材之混凝
土其強度與單位重較高，但輕質骨材混凝土單位重
低於普通混凝土，在同一水灰比下，使用較高密度
之輕質骨材混凝土強度可較接近普通混凝土強度。
以二氧化碳加速中性化試驗中，混凝土會隨著時間
的增加，使碳化深度有逐漸增加趨勢；經加速中性
化 240 小時後，輕質骨材混凝土之碳化深度與普通
混凝土差異不大：海水乾溼循環試驗中，鋼筋腐蝕
電位值於 20 循環後皆低於-350mV，其腐蝕機率高於
90％，而經海水乾溼 20 循環後之試體並未出現龜裂
現象。
關鍵字：輕質骨材、吸水性、顆粒強度、耐久
性
ABSTRACT
In this research, the durability of reinforced
lightweight concrete subject to different lightweight
aggregates is investigated. Four mix design concretes
included ordinary concrete, two lightweight aggregate
concretes and pervious lightweight aggregate concrete.
The project aims to run the steel corrosion test for the
reinforced lightweight aggregate concrete in sea water
dry-wet immersion and carbonization tests. The study
will be tested and evaluated by the following items:
compression strength, bending strength, splitting
tensile strength, carbonated depth measurement,
corrosion potential method, water absorption.
The findings showed that the higher specific
weight of lightweight aggregate concrete exhibit
relatively high strength and unit weight. In the same
water-cement ratio level, the strength of higher density
of lightweight aggregate concrete would reach those of
normal concrete.
At the accelerated carbonation test, the
carbonation depth of concrete would increase with time
and there is a gradual increase in carbonation depth
trend. The carbonation depth of lightweight aggregate
concrete specimens was the almost same as compared
with those of normal concrete ones after 240 hours
accelerated carbonation test. In dry-wet immersion by
sea water test, reinforcement corrosion potential values
were less than-350mV CSE after 20 cycles, the
corrosion probability is higher than 90%. After 20
drying and wetting cycles of sea water immersion test,
the cracking of concrete specimens does not appear.
Keywords：lightweight aggregate, water-absorption,
grain strength, durability

2-2 測試齡期與環境
1.飽和石灰水養護
輕質骨材混凝土、普通混凝土及輕質骨材透水
混凝土拆模後，隨即放入飽和石灰水養護，待測試
齡期到 28 天時，自飽和石灰水中取出，置於空氣中
乾燥後進行各項試驗。
2.人工海水乾濕循環
人工海水乾濕循環是將混凝土置於人工海水水
槽中，利用抽水馬達，以及時間控制器，12 小時抽
乾人工海水一次，1 天為 1個循環，測試齡期為 0、
10、20、30 個循環。
3.加速中性化
加速中性化是將普通混凝土與輕質骨材混凝土
（粗、細）以及輕質骨材透水混凝土試體置於混凝
土透水儀試模中，透水儀試模分別貫入流量 4 L/min
及 8 L/min 的二氧化碳，比較貫入兩種不同流量的
二氧化碳，對四種不同材質混凝土之影響，測試齡
期為 0、30、60、90 小時之碳化時間。
2-3 試驗方法及步驟
（1）抗壓強度試驗
抗壓強度是混凝土品質檢驗最重要的標的，亦
是混凝土結構設計之依據。在抗壓試驗中，加載的
速率及時間與試體的含水率皆會影響混凝土抗壓強
度。若加載速率過快且時間過短，所測得的強度會
比正常高出約 15%；若加載時間過長，所測得的強
度則會比正常低約 30%。混凝土抗壓試體大小為
Φ15×30cm。
（2）抗彎強度試驗
混凝土抗彎試驗為評估混凝土抗拉強度的另一
種方法，抗彎試體大小為 10×10×35cm，根據 ASTM
C293-79 或 CNS 1234 中心點載重法進行抗彎試驗，
以每秒 8.75~12.25kgf/cm2 之加載速率加壓於試體，
直至試體破壞，記錄最大破壞載重。
（3）劈張強度試驗
藉由劈張試驗可間接求取混凝土之抗拉強度，
劈張試驗試體尺寸為Φ5×10 cm圓柱試體，根據ASMT
C469-96 或 CNS 3801 進行試驗，以每分鐘 7~14
kgf/cm2之加載速率加壓，直到圓柱試體裂成兩半，
求得材料張力強度。
（4）碳化深度量測
本試驗將普通混凝土與輕質骨材混凝土置於混
凝土透水儀中，分別以流量 4 L/min 及 8 L/min 的
二氧化碳加速混凝土中性化，混凝土試體經劈裂
後，以酚酞指示劑塗抹於混凝土試體內部，經由顏
色之變化，即可測得混凝土碳化之情形，當酚酞指
示劑塗抹於混凝土試體呈現紫紅色表示試體尚未碳
化，若試體無變色則表示試體已碳化。比較不同流
量經 0、30、60 及 90 小時的加速碳化，對不同材質
混凝土之影響。
（5）鋼筋腐蝕電位量檢測
鋼筋腐蝕分析儀主要檢測原理是利用鋼筋腐蝕
所釋放的電位，而檢測此電位來確定混凝土內部之
鋼筋是否腐蝕，腐蝕儀就像是一台伏特計，將鋼筋
及混凝土連接，鋼筋釋放之電子流過腐蝕儀，而測
得其伏特值。
（6）最大吸水率之含濕率試驗
試體須使用整塊混凝土空心磚，測定其質量 m０，從
水中取出以濕布擦拭表面測定其質量 m2，其次在溫
度 100~110℃之空氣乾燥器中乾燥 24 小時，測定其

三、結果與討論
依據本研究試驗計劃，將試驗結果區分為使用輕
質骨材之混凝土與常重骨材混凝土之比較、輕質骨
材混凝土之基本力學性質、輕質骨材混凝土與輕質
骨材透水混凝土耐久性試驗等，並比較輕質骨材混
凝土、普通混凝土與輕質骨材透水混凝土在海水乾
溼浸泡與加速中性化環境下，對其力學性質、鋼筋
腐蝕電位量及碳化深度之探討。
3-1 抗壓試驗
混凝土抗壓試驗比較未經過環境測試之四種不
同材料變數之混凝土，探討不同骨材顆粒強度在混
凝土中之差異，由圖 1 之比較可以看出，四種混凝
土中抗壓強度最高的為普通混凝土，其次是使用輕
質骨材顆粒較小之輕質骨材混凝土，而使用輕質骨
材顆粒較大之輕質骨材混凝土則比骨材顆粒較小之
輕質骨材混凝土之強度還弱，因輕質骨材體積越
大，其中單位重越低，強度越弱，所以選用顆粒強
度較高之輕質骨材來施作混凝土亦可以達到普通混
凝土之強度，而強度最差的是輕質骨材透水混凝土。

3-7 鹽害對不同混凝土試體鋼筋腐蝕電位試驗結果
試體經 28 天養護後，置於人工海水乾溼循環
槽，依設計循環次數（0 循環、10 循環、15 循環、
及 20 循環）分別取出，再加以量測其電位之變化，
以判讀鋼筋發生腐蝕之機率。
在未將試體放入人工海水循環槽前，除輕質骨
材透水混凝土試體外，其他三種不同配比之混凝土
試體電位值，皆大於-200mV，顯示發生腐蝕之機率
低於 10％，而輕質骨材透水混凝土之電位值已達
-207.4mV，電位值介於-200mV 至 350mV 之範圍，腐
蝕機率為 10~90％，是因為輕質骨材透水混凝土無細
骨材，內部孔隙多，水與空氣容易侵入混凝土中，
於未進入惡化環境期間，鋼筋腐蝕電位值較其他三
組配比混凝土試體高，如圖 9 所示，顯示輕質骨材
透水混凝土試體於 10 cycle 之電位值已達
-677.51mV，再人工海水乾溼循環後電位值已降了
470.11mV，明顯低於其他三種混凝土試體，抵抗化
學性侵害能力較差，普通混凝土試體於 10 cycle 之
電位值皆高於其它三種配比混凝土試體，至 15
cycle 時，普通混凝土試體電位值降至-604.88mV，
仍高於其它配比之混凝土試體，而兩種輕質骨材混
凝土（粗、細）試體之電位值皆大約在-655mV，輕
質骨材透水混凝土試體仍然落於-670mV 附近，輕質
骨材混凝土之電位值較普通混凝土略低，而在 20
cycle 後四種混凝土之鋼筋腐蝕電位值變化則趨於
平緩，在人工海水循環測試後，均落於腐蝕機率 90
％以上。

（3）海水（鹽害）乾溼循環耐久性
1.輕質骨材於海水鹽害乾溼循環初期，強度會略
降，循環增加則有略升之趨勢，但至今為止，試
體並未有龜裂現象產生。
2.混凝土吸水量方面，輕質骨材混凝土試體其吸水
量遠高於普通混凝土。
3.鋼筋腐蝕電位值之量測，輕質骨材透水混凝土電
位值之減少較為明顯，其次為輕質骨材混凝土
（粗）、輕質骨材混凝土（細）、普通混凝土，而
此四種混凝土經海水乾溼循環 10 回後，鋼筋腐蝕
機率皆在 90％以上。
4.混凝土試體之吸水量與混凝土抗壓、抗彎強度的
關係較為明顯，吸水量與強度成反比。
5.由水淬熔岩混凝土耐久性試驗結果得知，20％水
淬熔岩混凝土，經 3 階段凍融循環測試後，其強
度衰減幅度相較其取代量試體小；然而，30％熔
岩與 30％爐石混凝土試體，經 3階段凍融循環測
試後，其試體強度實為相近。
4-2 建議
1.海水乾溼浸泡環境測試池之效果明顯，未來可進
行其他化學侵蝕之乾溼浸泡環境。
2.量測鋼筋腐蝕電位值之試體，建議後續研究人員
可測試鋼筋腐蝕電位量與混凝土鋼筋拉拔試驗做
比較，方能得知鋼筋腐蝕對於混凝土與鋼筋握裹
力之關係。
3.輕質骨材透水混凝土若欲提高其強度，建議可調
整配比，添加適量細骨材。

The main aim of the work presented here is to assess the performance of steel coated with a nickel phosphorous alloy, and to evaluate
corrosion resistance and durability of its bond to existing reinforced concrete.
EXPERIMENTS
Two kinds of severe environment testing on coated steel specimens were carried out to evaluate their corrosion resistance and
durability of bond to existing concrete. One environment testing was done at Tai-Chung Harbor to test the corrosion resistance
under rich chlorine environment near the sea shore. The other was a freeze-thaw cycling environment testing, which was
performed to evaluate the specimen’s capability of resisting the freeze-thaw condition.
Materials
The cement used in this study was Type I Portland cement manufactured by a local Portland Cement Company. The coarse aggregate
used was a commonly-used crushed stone. Three types of fine aggregates used were 100% river sand, 50% river sand + 50% sea
sand, and 100% sea sand. The physical properties and the results of sieve analyses on the aggregates are shown in Table 1. The above
materials including cement, river sand, sea sand, crushed gravel and steel rebar were used to make the concrete cylinder samples.
Both No.6 reinforcing steel rebar and No.6 reinforcing steel rebar coated with electroless nickel-plated film were inserted in the
center of a concrete cylinder, 120 mm in diameter and 240 mm in height, as shown in Fig. 1. The thickness of rebar coating film was
about 10μm. The factors considered and the combinations of reinforcing concrete cylinder specimens are shown in Table 2.
Tests performed in this study included compressive strength, electric potential, steel bond strength and corrosion visual inspection.

EXPERIMENTAL RESULTS AND DISCUSSION
Compressive Strength
Compressive strength is the most commonly used property of concrete in the control of the quality of concrete. Compressive strength
test is one of the most convenient tests on concretes. Due to its importance as a major indicator of concrete characteristics and
performance, the compressive strength was included as reference in this testing program. The effects of sea sand replacement on the
compressive strength of concrete for 28-day moist curing are shown in Fig. 4. The compressive strength decreases as the amount of
sea sand replacement increases up to 100% replacement of river sand. In general, sea sand or seawater may be used for mixing plain
concrete for sea shore structures except for high-alumina cement. It should not, unless quite unavoidable, be used in reinforced
concrete, for it will greatly increase the risk of corrosion of the reinforcement.
Electric Potential
The highly alkaline environment inside concrete usually protects reinforcing steel bar from corrosion due to the formation ofγ-Fe2O3
as a thin passive layer on the steel surface. However, this protection breaks down and the electrochemical process of corrosion is
initiated in the presence of chloride ion from the concrete making materials or from the aggressive environment (Neville 1995).
In accordance with ASTM C876, there is a probability greater than 90% that no reinforcing steel corrosion is occurring when the
potential is numerically less than -200 mV CSE. Beyond the level of -350 mV CSE, the corrosion is occurring with a probability
higher than 90%. However, a simple comparison of the half-cell potential data with the ASTM guideline on steel reinforcement
corrosion probability could prove meaningless. For instance, a more negative reading of potential is generally considered to indicate

In general, a more negative reading of potential is considered to indicate a higher probability of corrosion. However, this general
“rule”may not always apply because of the complexity of today’s concrete andrepair technologies. Evaluation of rebar corrosion
from the “absolute” half-cell potential values may mislead engineers and cause errors in judgment if other factors are not taken into
account. It must be stressed that the half-cell potential measurement only reveals the corrosion probability at a given location and
time. Long-term monitoring of the half-cell potential reading is more meaningful (Manning 1996).
Steel Bond Strength and Corrosion Visual Inspection
In general, there were three different failure modes, namely split failure, bond failure and steel break off in the steel pullout test (Lee
et al 2005). The failure mode for this study was a split failure. Pullout tests were carried out to study the effects of reinforcement
corrosion on the bond behavior of reinforced concrete members. Steel bond strength for the reinforcing steel bars inserted in concrete
cylinders, exposed to outdoor, seawater and freeze–thaw cycles, are separately shown in Tables 6 through 8. The results show that the
bond strength attained was essentially the same as that attained by using either coated rebar or sea sand before and after outdoor,
seawater or freeze–thaw exposure. In general, the bond strength of plain bars and concrete initially increases with increasing
corrosion, then declines. The turning point depends on the cracking of the concrete cover. The bond strength of deformed bars and
concrete increases with corrosion up to a certain amount, but with progressive increase in corrosion, the bond strength decreases, and
the cracking of the concrete cover seems to have no effect on the bond strength (Fu and Chung1 1998).
It can be concluded that the cover of concrete cylinder specimens has no crack and the steel bond strength of plain rebar is almost
equal to that of the coated rebar after outdoor, seawater or freeze–thaw exposure. This indicates that coated steel has similar
performance as the conventional one. This means that the electroplating has no significant impact on steel bond strength.
