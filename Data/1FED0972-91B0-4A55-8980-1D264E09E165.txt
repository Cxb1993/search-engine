（二）計畫英文摘要。（五百字以內） 
This project plans to help the Jiu-Hua traffic industrial Co. to improve the all 
terrain vehicle (ATV) transmission system. The components of ATV transmission 
system are composed of clutch, gearbox, drive shaft and gear train. A new type of 
transmission structure will be designed to change the original inter-chain to shaft 
transmission in this research. In addition, a gearbox is installed to make the ATV 
forward and backward possible, then a proper spiral umbrella type gear train will 
be selected and incorporate into the transmission system, it is expected that the 
transmission efficiency can be improved. The main purpose of this project is 
performance function increased and transmission mode improved, where the 
research and development of gearbox is accomplished by Jiu-Hua itself, while the 
collocation of the umbrella gear train and transmission shaft design are carried out 
in this project.  
On the basis of the mechanic load analysis and material deform theorem, the 
transmission shaft is designed, where the shaft dimensions are calculated. For 
safety consideration, the shear energy theory and Goodman fatigue criteria will be 
adopted to determine whether the permanent service life dropping on within the 
safety design margin or not. It is worth noting that the spiral umbrella gear 
selection of gearbox is due to the advantages of it with a smaller inaccuracy of 
transmitting and a more precision point-contact type. 
As the design of transmission parts is completed, on the consideration of 
safety and prime cost, the standardized equipments will be selected to low down 
the fabrication expense, and then assembly testing is performed. Finally, after 
constantly road conditions cross-examination, the safely, easy-control, 
brief-assembly, facility- maintenance and long-run objectives shall be achieved for 
this newly design transmission mechanism. It is heartfelt to expect that the 
new-type transmission ATV developing by Jiu-Hua will be more competitive and 
have a best sale on the international market.
