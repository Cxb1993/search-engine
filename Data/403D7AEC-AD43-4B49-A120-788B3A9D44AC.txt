Abstract 
Assessment of personal respirable dust 
exposure is a critical step in 
eliminating dust-related occupational 
illness and disease. Currently, dust 
levels are measured by weighing dust 
collected on filters, which is used for 
compliance purposes, but results can 
take several weeks to process. The 
delay in receiving filter results 
prevents timely correction of excessive 
dust levels. Therefore, several types of 
dust monitors have been developed for 
assessing personal dust exposure 
which is essential in eliminating dust-
related occupational illness and disease. 
Most of these dust monitors measure 
dust concentration by sensing the 
scattered light intensity. They can 
provide instantaneous reading to alert 
workers when overexposed to dust, but 
do not provide true mass concentration. 
A miniaturized tapered element 
oscillating microbalance (TEOM) has 
been developed by the National 
Institute for Occupational Safety and 
Health to monitor the personal ‘true’ 
exposure to respirable dust. This 
Personal Dust Monitor (PDM) is 
composed of a 1~1.5 m long silicone 
rubber tube (inner diameter 5 mm), a 
cyclone with a 50% cutoff at 4 µm 
when operated under 2.2 L/min, a 
temperature conditioning unit, and the 
core TEOM unit. Air resistance caused 
by and aerosol deposition loss in the 
silicone rubber tube were evaluated in 
the present study.  
 
In order to simulate the effect of extra 
silicone rubber tube (diameter of 5.0 
mm) on both aerosol penetration and 
pressure drop across the sampling tube, 
three different coil diameters (17.5, 35, 
and 70 cm) were shaped manually. The 
total length of the silicone rubber tube 
was fixed at 100 cm, which was 
enough to form 4 x 17.5 cm, or 2 x 35 
cm, or 1 x 70 cm coil, while the rest 30 
cm straight tube could be used for 
connecting the sampling inlet and the 
entry to the cyclone. Micrometer-sized 
potassium sodium tartrate aerosol 
particles were generated by using an 
ultrasonic atomizing nozzle. Aerosol 
number concentrations and size 
distributions upstream and downstream 
of the silicone rubber tube were 
measured by using an Aerodynamic 
Particle Sizer (APS 3322, TSI Inc., St. 
Paul, USA). The pressure drop across 
the sampling tube was monitored by 
using an inclined manometer. A Tygon 
tube with a diameter of 9.5 mm was 
used for comparison, to investigate the 
effect of tube diameter. Flow rates 
ranging from 1 to 5 L/min were 
conducted to study the flow 
dependency. 
 
The total pressure drop caused by the 
straight tube was always lower than 
the bent or coiled sampling tube, 
regardless of the flow rate and tube 
configuration. Smaller coil caused 
lower pressure drop; however, 
pressure drop per unit length of 
smaller coil was higher than that of 
larger coil. Aerosol deposition in the 
straight vertical tube was almost 
negligible. Aerosol penetration 
through the sampling tubes of all 
shapes decreased with increasing flow 
rate, indicating that inertial impaction 
was dominant deposition mechanism. 
For 4 µm particles, two elbows (2 x 90 
degree bends) caused about 10% 
deposition loss when operated under 
2.2 L/min. Aerosol penetration data 
showed that aerosol deposition in the 
sampling tube increased with

的量測原理簡述如下。 
TEOM的量測方法為將空氣中
的粒狀污染物經由粒徑篩分器，以
適當的吸引量採集到濾紙上，濾紙
直接裝在擺動式錐狀微量天平上，
直接測出瞬間重量的變化，再經儀
器自動換算出即時濃度值。該方法
適用於空氣中粒徑在 10 微米(μm)
以下之粒狀污染物(PM10)濃度之自動
測定，其適用濃度範圍介於 0~5 x 
106μg / m3 (5 g / m3)。水氣可能形成
干擾，可以適當加熱控制機制排除
之。檢測元件之加熱控制機制可能
會造成某種揮發性成分散失(例如：
硝酸鹽)，在此狀況下可設定「大氣
溫度」進行採樣或自行評估差異
性。 
本直讀式粉塵質量監測儀
（personal dust monitor, 以下簡稱
PDM）係由美國國家職業安全衛生
研究所（NIOSH/USA, The National 
Institute for Occupational Safety and 
Health）和 Thermo公司(原 R&P公
司於 94年底被購併入 Thermo公司)
合作研發。該儀器即是利用縮小型
的 tapered element oscillating 
microbalance (TEOM)作為量測個人
呼吸性粉塵暴露濃度之偵測原件，
並首要規劃為進行採礦工人之粉塵
暴露評估之個人採樣器。PDM採樣
系統共包括一個固定於安全帽上的
採樣口、一條長約 1至 1.5公尺，內
徑 5公釐之可導電矽膠採樣管
（conductive silicone rubber tube）用
以連接採樣口與量測機體，該採樣
管需固定於勞工背部，及一個可懸
掛於勞工腰部腰帶上之量測機體。
該監測儀之量測機體包含三部分，
分別為（1）一在採量流量為 2.2 
L/min 時其 50%截取氣動粒徑為 4 
µm的旋風集塵器（cyclone）、
（2）一個加熱管、（3）一個
tapered element oscillating 
microbalance （TEOM）。該項設計
係利用旋風集塵器過濾出呼吸性粉
塵，使其沈積在擺動式錐狀微量天
平上的濾紙，測出瞬間重量的變
化，再經儀器自動換算出即時重量
濃度值，故所得之粉塵數據即為作
業勞工所暴露之呼吸性粉塵濃度。 
       由於 PDM為避免因攜帶採樣器
造成勞工作業干擾，所以體積與重量
都以盡可能減少為重要目標。所以，
在儀器有限空間內各連接管線與加熱
管皆無可避免有彎曲的地方。此外，
採樣系統需固定於勞工背後之採樣管
設計，亦可能因為勞工個人身材不
同，為求固定過長的採樣管而有不同
的彎曲程度的情況。故不論是採樣管
還是量測機體內的彎曲管路，其各種
彎曲情形對於所欲採集量測的粉塵是
否會造成不同沈積損失而導致無法呈
現環境真實的呼吸性粉塵重量濃度是
值得關注與確認的誤差來源。

結果與討論 
一、PDM組成元件之粉塵沉積分布 
PDM之旋風集塵器的粉塵沉積
結果如圖 3所示，在 2.2 L/min流量
下，其 50%截取氣動粒徑為 4 µm，
符合 ISO/ACGIH/CEN respirable 
convention等機構之要求，而另外兩
個流量其 50%截取氣動粒徑皆有明
顯的偏移。 
此外，若考量長時間使用後粉塵
負載可能對於旋風集塵器之微粒收集
效率有所影響，故再進一步在 2.2 
L/min 的操作流量下進行 300 分鐘的
負載測試，其結果如圖 4所示，可發
現該粉塵監測器之 90%截取氣動粒
徑在前 100 分鐘從 90%下降至
70%，但之後則趨向穩定。而其 50%
截取氣動粒徑在前 100分鐘從 50%下
降至 40%，但之後亦穩定於 40%。
而其 10%截取氣動粒徑則不隨時間
增長而有明顯的下降改變。
