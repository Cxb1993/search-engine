四、研究與實驗方法 
    為了達成上述研究目的，本計畫主要引用的研究方法與設備如下說明： 
一、飛灰採樣與備料 
對本研究擬採樣對象，基於實務上該焚化廠排出的飛灰為「鍋爐飛灰」與「袋式集
塵器收集飛灰」之混合物（習稱飛灰與反應生成灰），故本研究飛灰對象亦屬此等由現
場採集回來的等重量比例之混合物（即不分別探討鍋爐飛灰或反應生成灰）。因目前固
化處理皆是此等飛灰混合物。研究期間若有必要才分別探討鍋爐排出飛灰或反應生成
灰。故本研究所用的飛灰之採集悉依據「事業廢棄物採樣方法 NIEA R118.00B」，仿過去
研究經驗，本項工作仍以定期至實場採集飛灰樣（包括 boiler 飛灰或反應生成灰），並

三、飛灰基本性值 
基於飛灰的基本性質對研究其處理技術甚為重要，近年來本研究室在相關機構的資
助下（專案研究委託案）已建立了良好的採樣與分析技術，本計畫將一如往昔先建立此
飛灰基本質（如重金屬金含量，TCLP 溶出量，其部份所需經費可由校方配合款或其他委
託案支援）。 
 
四、戴奧辛與重金屬分析 
為了減低研究上的成本及實驗精密度要求，在處理 DXN 之微波消化的效率
（destruction & removal efficiency， DRE）上是檢測 DXN 之同分異構物，從目前國內
各焚化廠檢測所分析 DXN 的同分異構物有 17 種，此代表這 17 物種為 TEQ 含量排序最
高者，餘皆微乎其微或定為零，不足影響 TEQ 之計算。如北部某二廠(垃圾已有良好的
分類)是以前者為含量最高的物種，因本研究的飛灰對象「可能」取自此廠，故用於檢
視「微波消化」處理之 DRE 將以此 17 種為 DXN 的指標物，即在分析 DXN 上依 NIEA
標準方法處理後，以 GC/M 檢定飛灰中 DXN 的含量。 
戴奧辛的檢測分析工作非常的耗時、繁瑣、昂貴，因此極需發展一個有效的內部
控管機制（甚至於尋求外界支援，如精確度比對）來控制與戴奧辛分析品質，基此，本
研究參考分析結果作為計算戴奧辛 DRE 效率之基準。本研究主要項目分析方法

高解析質譜儀系統內均具即時質量漂移校正之參考物質如 PFK，由 NIEA M801.11B 表
六所列每一組監測群之精確質量離子均由該質量鎖定離子作即時監測。 
7.品管規範與品質保證：內標準品回收百分率：參考 NIEA M801.11B 方法之表三。淨化
標準品回收率：參考 NIEA M801.11B 方法之表三所列淨化標準品係於酸洗淨化效率，
其回收率須落在 30～130﹪範圍內。空白基質添加待測物標準品回收率：待測物添加
之回收率須落在 70～130﹪範圍內。 
8.精密度與準確度：本研究實驗室之精密度將以空白基質添加待測物標準品及 13C12-同
位素標幟標準品；實際樣品添加 13C12-同位素標幟標準品(詳見國家標準方法 NIEA 
M801.11B 之表十一)分析之（83~110%），準確度則以查核樣品分析之（詳見國家標

(二)鉛重金屬 TCLP 消化與分析 
1.樣品保存 
本研究所用的飛灰之採集悉依據「事業廢棄物採樣方法 NIEA R118.00B」，所有樣品依
據適當之採樣計畫收集：至少要收集三個代表性之樣品：第一及第二個樣品作初步
測詴用（如測詴固體顆粒尺寸，或使用何種萃取液），第三個樣品重金屬成份之萃取
用。無論是原飛灰或經 MDT 處理後之飛灰皆不添加任何保存劑於樣品，若樣品需冷
藏則以不超過 3天為原則。 
2.萃取溶液(B)製備 
在 1L 量瓶中，將 5.7mL 冰醋酸加入蒸餾水中；稀釋至刻度。此溶液之 pH 為 
2.88  ±0.05，使用前檢查 pH 值。萃取液並經常檢查是否有不純物質干擾。 
3.前處理步驟 
因飛灰可通過 9.5 mm 之標準篩網，故不需要減小顆粒可逕行至萃取液選擇步驟。秤取
固體 5.0 g，置於 500 mL 燒杯或錐形瓶中，加入 96.5 mL 之詴劑水，蓋以錶玻璃，
以磁攪拌器劇烈攪拌五分鐘，測量溶液之 pH 值並記錄之，因一般 pH  5.0，故加入 3.5 
mL 1.0 N HCl，攪拌成均勻狀，蓋以錶玻璃，加熱至 50 ℃並維持 10 分鐘，冷卻至室
溫後，測量溶液之 pH 並記錄之(但若 pH < 5.0，則使用萃取液 A；若 pH < 5.0，使

4.萃取 
秤取至少 100g 之樣品，紀錄其重量，將樣品之固相與濾紙一併置於萃取器中。緩
慢加入萃取溶液萃取容器中，旋緊容器瓶蓋，置於旋轉裝置，以每分鐘 30  ±2 之
轉速旋轉 18  ±2 小時，室溫維持在 2  2± 3℃（在萃取過程中，有些樣品會
產生氣體，而必須每隔 15 分、30 分或一小時，打開瓶蓋釋放氣體）。萃取完成後，
使用新的濾紙過濾，分離固相及液相，並收集濾液。紀錄其 pH，並立即分配以便
分析前消化或適當之保存（作金屬分析用之濾液應以硝酸調整 pH至小於 2）。 
5.分析前消化 
精取 100 mL或適量之具代表性混合均勻之上述樣品至 150 mL 燒杯內，加入 3 mL 
濃硝酸，以稜紋之錶玻璃覆蓋置於加熱板上，控制在 90 至 95℃之間，小心蒸發至約 
3 至 5 mL量。並小心不使樣品沸騰且不可使燒杯底部有任何乾掉現象。冷卻後再加入 
3 mL 濃硝酸，以無稜紋之錶玻璃蓋住燒杯，放回加熱板上加熱，提高加熱板溫度緩
緩迴流。繼續加熱，重複添加硝酸至消化完全（消化液顏色變淡或顏色不再變化），
將體積蒸發至約 約 3 至 5 mL，但小心不使燒杯底部有任何乾燥現象。冷卻燒杯，
加入U約 10 mLU 1/1（v/v）鹽酸，蓋住燒杯，迴流 15 分鐘，以溶解因蒸發產生之殘渣。
以水沖洗燒杯和錶玻璃，必要時則過濾或離心除去樣品中可能會堵住霧化器
（nebulizer）的矽酸鹽物質及不溶物（不溶物可能會堵住霧化器時，方進行過濾，過
濾前先以稀硝酸沖洗濾紙及過濾設備）。以水定容至 100 mL，使最終酸濃度為約 
10%，即進行下列分析。

III. EXCUTION INFLUENCE ON MSW 
CHARACTERISTICS 
 
A. Physical Composition 
 Typical seasonal variation in refuse components is 
moisture content, and management variation is physical 
composition. Through the field investigation with 
periodical sampling, the study found that much of the 
MSW generated in Taiwan is simply trash including 
rubbish or garbage. They include paper and plastics, wood, 
rubber and leather, old clothing, and a host of other 
products. But occasionally, a very few of these wastes are 
hazardous. By comparing the data obtained in 2005 and 
2008 as shown in Fig.1, the physical components of 
refuses in plants A and B were varied obviously due to the 
programs implementation described above.  
 In 2005, paper or wood waste was usually the largest 
portion in MSW, but after the aforesaid programs 
promulgating, plastic waste became the largest portion of 
the MSW stream and will be predicted to continue 
growing. During the program it was found that food waste 
was the component with the maximum variation among 
all physical components. This is attributed to the RKG 
program carried out very well in Taiwan. For plant A, the 
percentage of food waste by dry-based weight decreased 
from 20.5% in 2005 to 15.8% in 2008. The weighted 
portions of most incombustible components decreased 
after implementation of the aforesaid programs. However, 
plastic waste increased from 15.8% in 2005 to 23.5% in 
2008, and textile waste from 8.9% to 12.3%.  
 Of interest is that the total amount of plastic waste 
decreased significantly during the past few years mainly 
due to the RUP and PRP promulgation in Taiwan. It was 
estimated that the total amount of plastic waste in MSW 
reduced from about 135 tons/yr in 2005 to 110 tons/yr in 
2008. However, the weight percentage of plastic waste in 
incineration-feeding refuse increased even with the usage-
limitation policy. This result is attributed to two reasons. 
The first is that food eaten-out is usually taken out with 
plastic bags. This easily causes the bags to become dirty 
and discarded by the user. The second is that plastic usage 
is the custom in Taiwan and not easily changed. 
Merchandise plastic packaging is the norm due to the 
great amount of rain in Taiwan. By weight, plastic waste 
currently accounts for about 22% to 25% of the waste 
stream. Environmental conditions such as the rainy season 
also affects the quantity of plastic waste collected in 
Taiwan. In summary, the programs to meet the recent 
management strategy for MSW reduction and recycling 
have a significant influence on the physical composition 
of incineration-feeding, especially on food waste, paper, 
and plastics. 
 
B.  Chemical Properties 
 In general, about five-sixths of the MSW (on a weight 
basis) is comprised of organic or combustible materials. 
The remaining one-sixth is primarily metals, glass, and 
dirt. In other words, the weight of the combustible 
components is 83-86 wt.% (categorized by paper, 
wood/garden trimmings, cloth, plastics, food waste, 
rubber/leather, and others). The weight of the 
incombustible components is 14-18 wt. % (categorized by 
metal, glass, ceramic and other inert materials) on dry 
basis for the MSW generated in Taiwan. The averaged 
monthly variation in moisture content decreased from 
37.9% in 2005 to 36.7% in 2008. The ash content 
decreased from 15.4% in 2005 to 10.2% in 2008. 
However, the combustible matter increased from 44.7% in 
2005 to 51.9% in 2008, because of both moisture and ash 
decreasing.  
 It was found that the ash content decreased from 2005 
to 2008. This implies that the MSW recycling 
management strategy was promulgated successfully in 
Taiwan. Thereby most recoverable material can be 
recycled through collection system in communities. The 
recoverable material usually contains high ash content; so 
the programs, such as PUP and FRM, would lead the ash 
content decreasing in refuse. This achievement would 
give a benefit to cut down the bottom and fly ash disposal 
cost for annual incineration operation and maintenance. In 
addition, the result also shows that TKG programs can 
reduce the percentage of high-moisture food waste in 
refuse. This leads to decreasing moisture content. This 
attracts restaurant enterprises to make greater effort in 
food waste reduction, and causes the local governments to 
actively strive for establishing more concrete relative 
collection and recovery system programs for food waste 
in Taiwan. Because of the reduced moisture matter and 
ash, the combustible matter in MSW increased due to the 
programs implementation. 
 The complex waste diversion sources resulting from 
the recent MSW reduction management strategy may not 
be easily explored. As discussed in the above section, the 
TKG program affects percentage of food waste, and the 
RFM program decreases the incombustible material in 
refuse. It was found that the weight percentage of carbon 
increased from 21.8% in 2005 to 27.2% in 2008. This is 
consistent with the increase in combustible content found 
by approximate analysis. It was also found that the weight 
percentage of nitrogen increased from 0.85% in 2005 to 
1.13% in 2008. However, NOx emissions were enhanced 
in the flue gas from the MSW incinerator thus increasing 
the De-NOx cost. This is an extra problem and more 
attention must be given to this during program execution.  
However, the weight percentage of sulfur decreased from 
0.73% in 2005 to 0.43% in 2008. It will be helpful to 
control the acid flue gas emitted from the MSW 
incinerator. In other words, the waste diversion resulting 
from recent management strategy does not lead to an extra 
treatment load for controlling acid flue gas because of the

example, starting on July 2002, the EPA began the first 
stage targets for restricted use. The yearly elimination rate 
of plastic shopping bags was 5%, and the yearly reduction 
rate of plastic tableware was 3%. Starting on January 
2004, the EPA began the second stage targets for the 
restricted use. The EPA anticipated that the yearly 
reduction rate of plastic shopping bags and plastic 
tableware increased by 7% and 6% respectively.  
 Although the adoption and implementation of the 
programs have been conducted for a time, not only the 
characteristics of the MSW going to the incinerator 
changed, but some obstacles were also encountered and 
need to be solved. The Taiwan government still actively 
strives to establish more concrete policies and objectives, 
especially, understanding support of the general public, 
the cooperation of private sectors, as well as the open-
mindedness of the public sectors. What the management 
strategy must be kept going in tandem with the global 
trend to achieve a zero-waste-discharge country. 
Therefore, the characteristics of the MSW going to the 
incinerator will change. Also, it is important to consider 
the changes that may occur in the composition and 
characteristic of MSW with time in planning for future 
MSW management systems. However, it is believed that 
the total recycle ratio of MSW will approach to 100% in 
coming 2012 when the city government expects the MSW 
generated in the city can be all recycled and reused. In 
other words, exact management with good and available 
programs can reduce waste volume and reuse or recycle 
all MSW as possible. It will lead the city toward a zero 
waste society. 
 
V.  CONCLUSION 
 
 The programs to meet recent management strategy for 
MSW reduction and recycling have a significant influence 
on the physical composition of MSW, especially on food 
waste, paper, and plastics. In 2003, paper or wood waste 
was usually the largest portion in MSW, after the 
programs promulgating, plastic waste became the largest 
portion of the MSW stream and will be predicted to 
continue growing. The TKG and PYT programs can 
reduce the percentage of high-moisture food waste in 
refuse, then it leads the moisture content decreasing. 
Through the investigation of this work, it is found that the 
heating value of the MSW generated in Taiwan increase 
gradually about 5% per year after the programs 
implementation. These programs not only attract the 
restaurant enterprises to make more effort on food waste 
reduction, and cause the local governments actively to 
strive establishing better relative programs on the 
collection and recovery systems of food waste in Taiwan. 
A comparison on monthly variation of chemical elements 
indicates that carbon is the element with maximum 
variation among all elements in organic compounds 
through the programs implementation. The chlorine 
content in refuse was found to change obviously. This 
would be the result from the usage reduction in PVC 
plastic due to the RFM, and the food waste as well as salt 
content reduction due to the TKG. This achievement 
would give a benefit to reduce the dioxins emission from 
MSW incineration. Although the adoption and 
implementation of these programs have been conducted, 
some obstacles were encountered and need to be solved.. 
The management strategy must be conducted in tandem 
with the global trend to achieve a zero-waste-discharge 
country. Therefore, it is important to consider the changes 
that may occur in the composition and characteristic of 
MSW with time in planning for future MSW management 
systems
