The study of cyclic plastic deformation welding residual stress and the high

一、 中文摘要 
兩種雙相鈦合金 Ti64 和含 Mo 的
SP700，其低週疲勞結果顯示材料軟化的行為
和應變速率是沒有太大的關聯性。在 10-4 S-1
低應變速率的情況下，SP700 在塑性區的疲勞
壽命會優於 Ti64 合金。長片狀的雙晶產生於
低應變速率時的 α 相中，此長片狀的雙晶會
比在 10-2 S-1 高應變速率下產生的短片狀雙晶
更有助於延長疲勞壽命。兩種雙相鈦合金
中，β-rich SP700 的抗高溫乳變行為優於
Ti-6Al-4V。在 160 個小時潛變行為下兩者β
相的體積分率會呈現 5%增加量，然負乳變
下，晶粒會成長，晶粒尺寸大於穩態乳變。
銲接最大殘留應力發生於銲道中央，並隨著
距離遠離，應力下降成拉力壓力波動變化，
經鑽石鍍膜可清除殘留應力。 
Abstract 
    Analyses of the fatigue behavior of 
titanium alloys Ti64  and SP700 were 
performed to examine the trend of cyclic 
hardening or softening, hysteresis loops, and 
fatigue life. The cyclic stress-strain curves of 
Ti64 and SP700 alloy exhibit material softening 
behavior independent of strain rate. The plastic 
fatigue life of SP700 is superior to that of Ti64 
alloy at a low strain rate of 10-4 S-1. The long 
patches of twins that occur at a low strain rate in 
α phase are more beneficial to extension of 
fatigue life than the short segment twins that 
occur at a high strain rate of 10-2 S-1.The 
volume fraction of the β phase shows a 5% 
increase for 160 hours of creep, 2% increase for 
negative creep. The grain growth occurs during 
the stress drop incubation period.

五、實驗方法 
雙相鈦合金的Ti64圓棒中含有較多的α
相，而 SP700 是含有較多的β相。週期載重
會在 0.5%-2%之間變化且以兩種不同的應變
速率 10-2/sec 和 10-4 /sec 進行實驗研究。金
相光學顯微觀察是將硝酸，氫氟酸和蒸餾水
依一定比例混合溶液進行雙相鈦合金金相腐
蝕，α相和β相的鑑別是利用Hitachi S4100 
SEM 在 15Kv 下，以能量散佈光譜儀 EDS 來完
成。而再利用穿透式電子顯微鏡來觀察疲勞
後微結構。 
    固定負載潛變測試和應力遽降測試於空
氣中進行，初始應力為 100MPa，在恆溫 500℃
持續時間 160 個小時。使用 SLVC 來測量潛變
應變量。在穩定狀態下進行潛變試驗 140 個
小時後，將初始應力卸載降低至初始應力的
30%持續荷重，研究其變形行為。氣體保護鎢
極電弧銲(GTAW)法施工時，附加一氣體罩吹
入氬氣避免銲道氧化。殘留應力依 ASTM 
E837，分別在距熔融線(Fusion Line) 0 ㎜, 7.5
㎜, 16㎜, 28㎜, 35㎜, 42 mm不同位置測量殘
留應力值。

6.4 High temperature behavior 
    兩鈦合金 α-rich Ti64 和 β-rich 
SP700，之典型的穩態潛變行為見圖 6 。抗高
溫時間變形上 SP700 優於 Ti64，基於前者β 
phase 較多緣故。合金 Ti64 的最低潛變速率，
值為 3x10-4/hour 是 SP700 的最低潛變速率為
1x10-4/hour 值的 3倍。雙相 Ti64 包含較多的
六方最密堆積 α 相 83%，在溫度 500oC 應力
範圍為 291~520MPa 和溫度 600oC 應力範圍為
97~319MPa 的情況下潛變的變形主要是藉由
差排的滑移[20]。相較之下 SP700 鈦合金的
潛變則是由較多的β相變形所導致。穩態階
段潛變源自應變硬化和回復動態平衡。因而
內應力的大小與時間的關係是無關的。
Kruml[22]主張材料中所承受的應力可被分
離成兩個不同的部份，有效應力和內應力。
有效應力被認定取決於溫度和塑性應變速
率，材料的機械性質取決於顯微組織和測試
環境。Ahlquist 和 Nix[23]提出三個典型變
形機制及研究減少外加應力後的潛變行為。 
    量測結果與移動熱源有限元素分析結果
相符。發現若銲件表面經過鑽石鍍膜處理，
可減緩銲殘留應力分佈，不但有利於銲件之
保固與表面改質，同時銲件表面因平滑的鍍
層，可提供低抗腐蝕的保護層。 
 
七、結論 
    含鉬雙相鈦合金在低應變速率下的反應
最大應力振幅都比 Ti64 來得大。且疲勞壽命
均優於 Ti64 合金。兩種鈦合金於高應變振幅
的疲勞中會呈現軟化的趨勢。含鉬鈦合金中
α 相會有長板狀的雙晶變態產物，且應變振
幅增加時，會有更多的雙晶生成，增長其疲
勞延性與疲勞壽命。反之高應變速率下不含
鉬之雙相鈦合金，差排偏好地堆積在晶界上
並且使之粗化，是不含鉬雙相鈦合金疲勞壽
命比含鉬鈦合金較長之原因。  
6.5 Microstructure change and residual stress     於 500℃和 100 Mpa狀態下含鉬雙相鈦合
金的抗長期變形性優於不含鉬合金。β 相經
過長期變形其體積百分比呈現 5% 的增加。反
之負潛變則為 2% 的減少，且晶粒明顯大於穩
態潛變，基於新應力小於內應力的關係。 
    Ti64 於高溫長期變形到 5.5% 應變量的
變形顯微組織(圖 7(b))比原材顯微組織(圖 7 
(a))晶粒內有較多差排。同時大量的動態再
結晶的微細晶粒被嵌入混合於應變晶粒中。
然而應力遽降，產生負潛變的顯微組織會大
晶粒存在且為變形晶粒和再結晶晶粒所環
繞，次結構形成的稠密程度於與應變量之增
加有關[24]。相較之下含有鉬和鐵合金元素
的β相具有較佳的抗潛變性，慢速變形後會
有許多的動態再結晶微細晶粒形成；然而，
負潛變下會有等軸再結晶晶粒出現，顯微組
織的變異是滯彈性反應後產生動態回復導致
潛變速率和差排密度減少[25]。

ABSTRACT The microstructures of the duplex stainless steel (DSS), SAF 2507 and 
SAF 2205 were evaluated, after the dynamic impact test with two strain rates of 
8.5×102 s-1 and 5×103 s-1. Results indicate that the flow stress-strain curve and the 
microstructure are strongly affected by the impact loading rate. The value of 
microhardness for both the ferrite and austenite increases as increasing strain rate. For 
relative low strain rate of 8.5×102 s-1, tangled dislocations were observed in both the 
α and γ phase. In contrast, deformation twin occurs only in the γ phase for 
dynamic impact at the relative high strain rate of 5×103 s-1. 
  
INTRODUCTION: As-received duplex stainless steels (DSS) SAF 2507 DSS and 
SAF 2205 DSS have mixed structures composed of equal volume fractions of 
α-ferrite (bcc) grains and γ-austenite (fcc) grains. These structural materials will 
encounter both high strain rates in the working processes of manufacturing and 
dynamic collisions. High resolution microstructures of dislocation substructure, shear 
band quantity and characteristics, and α’ martensite fraction are correlated with 
macroscopic responses of 304L stainless steel under high impact loading. It is known 
that mechanical twining is another important deformation mechanism, especially 
under dynamic loading conditions and low temperatures. Previous studies have 
showed that the formation of mechanical twins is often found in micro-shear bands 
that consist of high density stacking faults, ε phase, and twin faults [Lee et al. 2002]. 
 
PROCEDURES, RESTULTS AND DISCUSSION: Two types of different duplex 
the needle-like micro twins appear in great numbers inside the γ-austenite phase of 
SAF 2205 DSS, as shown in the dark field transmission electron microscopic image 
and the diffraction pattern in Fig. 2d confirms the validity of the needle-like products 
as the twins. It seems that the deformation twinning nucleates and grows from the 
boundary. It is observed that dislocation density of α-ferrite grains increases with 
increasing strain rate in SAF 2507 DSS and SAF 2205 DSS. Deformation twins change 
only the crystal lattice position, but not the crystal structure. The occurrence of 
twinning depends on several factors: pressure, crystallographic orientation, 
stacking-fault energy, existing substructure and grain size. As the stacking-fault energy 
of fcc metals is decreased, the incidence of twinning increases [Meyers 1994]. In fact, 
the higher the nickel and chromium content, the lower the stacking fault energy 
[Whitcroft et al. 1969 and Najafi et al. 2005]. Vohringer (1976) suggested the formula 
may be written as: 
dε / dσ = (dεs + dεt) / σ                                                                                                     
(1) where dε/dσ is the inverse of work hardening, dεs is the strain contribution of 
slipping, and dεt is the strain increment of twinning. This equation points out that the 
inverse of work hardening must increase after twinning starts.
