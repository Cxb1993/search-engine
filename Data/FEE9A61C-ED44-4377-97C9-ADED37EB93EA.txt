information of various solid foods; 
furthermore, it is very important to develop a 
simple, accurate and instant apparatus for 
determination of electrical conductivity.  The 
investigation of electrical conductivity 
changes during food deterioration is very 
helpful to the detection of food quality.  In 
the first year of this 3-year project, the 
electrical conductivity of various solid food 
samples was determined by the traditional 
equipments in present lab in order to 
establish database.  Meanwhile the data of 
microorganism growth and electrical 
conductivity were analyzed to provide the 
relationship.  The results showed that in low- 
temperature storage, both electrical 
conductivity increase and microorganism 
growth were slow, and a linear propotional 
relation could be found.  However for room-
temperature storage, conductivity data were 
found unstable, and the linear propotional 
relation with microorganism growth could 
only be found in certain periods.  The reasons 
could be due to sample tissue collapse caused 
by quick deteration, which resulted in 
difficulty in determination of electrical 
conductivity.  This observation was found 
mainly in meat and cooked samples, but not 
often for vegetables. 
 
Key words: electrical conductivity, 
water mobility, solid foods, microorganism, 
quality index
