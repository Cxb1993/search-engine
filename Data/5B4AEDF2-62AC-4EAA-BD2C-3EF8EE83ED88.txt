2小米酒安定評估研究: (1)以 HPLC 分析參與混濁的多元酚類(2)以
DSC 熱分析小米酒混濁蛋白質之不安定性
摘要
本研究將小米酒中酚類與酚酸進行分離、鑑定與定量。將小米酒先經
solid-phase extraction，接著進行 HPLC with photodiode array detector 進行檢測，
檢測小米酒中 gallic acid (沒食子酸)、protocatechuic acid (原兒茶酸)、gentisic acid
(二氫氧苯甲酸)、(+)-catechin (兒茶素)、vanillic acid (香草酸)、caffeic acid (咖啡
酸)、syingic acid (丁香酸)、(-)-epicatechin (表兒茶素)、p-coumaric acid (香豆酸)、
ferulic acid (阿魏酸)、cinnamic acid (肉桂酸)和 quercetin (檞皮酮)。此分析結果除
可提供小米酒成份分析外，以了解這些酚類在 4℃儲藏期間是否發生變動，推斷
小米酒安定性的方法.。經和 12 種標準品比對後，所有 12 種酚類(酚酸)於 4℃儲
藏期間的酒液被檢測出。12 種被標定的酚類以(+)-catechin、gallic acid 與 quercetin
含量較高，除 catechin 有變動外其餘酚類變化不大（表 6），這些酚酸可能不是主
要參與混濁的酚類，而 catechin 可能是參與混濁的酚類。另外從表 7 得知總酚、
易生混濁酚(HA-polyphenol)和類黃酮量呈下降趨勢，推測其它屬於總酚與類黃酮
而未被 identified 的較大與複雜的 polyphenol，可能與混濁有關。
本研究第二主題，乃藉differential scanning calorimetry的量測，了解小米酒混
濁蛋白質不穩定度與熱動力變化。由於穀類飲料渾濁主要為蛋白質分子與多酚間
作用形成渾濁顆粒或甚至沉澱，醇溶性蛋白質可的做穀類飲料中渾濁蛋白質之
model protein。本實驗以小麥醇溶穀蛋白(gliadin)為model protein樣品，在
不同溶液環境(如與水或tannin混合)下進行熱分析試驗，以示差掃瞄熱分析法
（Differential scanning colorimetry）檢測緩慢升溫時，gliadin水溶液熱性
質變化，以及加入單寧後gliadin熱性質變化。由於分子間的交互作用，以及化
學或物理作用，使蛋白質的構造發生變化（變性denatured），因而改變蛋白質的
熱性質。此研究可應用在穀類釀造酒業界，提供判斷酒品質一種快速方法。
關鍵字: 小米酒、混濁、高效能液體層析、熱示差掃瞄分析（Differential scanning
calorimetry）
前言
本實驗室已發現小米酒在4℃冷藏20週期間，其酒液成份及一些物化性質發
生變化。其中酒液顏色、濁度、可溶性固形物含量、總糖、總氮，蛋白質與多元
酚濃度，被檢測出發生改變（Yang et al ;2005, 2006ab）。小米酒渾濁度隨儲存時
間增長而逐漸提高，乃是酒液中可溶成份發生變化之故。在4℃儲藏初期，濁度
只有些微變化，6週後酒液濁度驟增，而總酚與總蛋白質隨儲藏時間增加而降低，
4Chemicals, solutions
Standards of caffeic acid, p-coumaric acid, ferulic acid, gallic acid, gentisic acid,
salicylic acid, protocatechuic acid, (+)-catechin hydrate, (−)-epicatechin, quercetin
dihydrate will be purchased from Aldrich (Milwaukee, USA) or Fluka (Buchs,
Switzerland). Solvents such as acetone, ethyl acetate, dichloromethane, hexane,
acetonitrile, methanol for liquid chromatography will be purchased from Merck
(Darmstadt, Germany).
The stock standard solutions of each phenolic compound are prepared in methanol.
A mixed standard solution is prepared by dilution in methanol of the stock solutions.
All standard solutions will be stored in the dark at 4◦C. Waters 500 mg Sep-Pak C18
Plus cartridges (Mildford, USA) are used as solid-phase extraction minicolumns for
purification and concentration.
Taiwanese millet wine produced by different manufacturers in Taiwanese and by
my laboratory （Yang and Su, 2004）will be used for characterizing the proposed
method as well as for screening the presence of these phenolic compounds.
Chromatographic system and operating conditions
The analysis will be performed on a HITACHI HPLC L-7000 system equipped
with a degasser, pumps, and a photodiode array detector linked to a PC computer
running the software program HPLC LACHROM Software . The analytical column
(150 × 4.6mm i.d.) used is a Waters Symmetry 5μm C18 (USA). The guard column
(50 × 4.6 mm i.d.) is packed with dry 40μm Pelliguard LC-18 (Supelco). For HPLC
analysis, an aliquot (20μL) is injected into the columns and eluted at room
temperature.
Mobile phase eluent (A) is 0.6‰ aqueous perchloric acid, and eluent (B)
MeOH, and the flow rate is kept constant throughout the analysis at 1 ml min-1
（Guendez et al., 2005）. Injections are accomplished with a 20μl fixed loop. The
elution programme used is as follows: from 100% A to 78% A in 55 min, from 78%
A to 0% A in 10 min, and then isocratic for a further 10 min. For photodiode array
detection, the wavelengths of phenolic compounds at their respective maximum
absorbance wavelength can monitored at the same time. Detection and quantification
is done at 253 nm for quercetin; at 280 nm for gallic acid, (+)-catechin and
(−)-epicatechin; at 306 nm for caffeic acid, p-coumaric acid and salycilic acid; and at
330 nm for gentisic acid（Hayes et al., 1987a and 1987b; Es-Safi et al., 1999;
Montanari et al., 1999）.

Millet wine polyphenols extraction and purification
A 500 mg C18 Sep-Pak cartridge is conditioned with 5mL of methanol followed
by 10 mL of deionized water without allowing the cartridge to dry out. An aliquot of
the millet wine sample is passed through the cartridge. After the cartridge is dried,
phenolic compounds are eluted by acetonitrile. The organic eluate is then transferred
to a 25mL flask and evaporated to dryness on the rotary evaporator. The residue
obtained was dissolved in 1mL of methanol: water (50:50, acidified at 1% with acetic
acid). Homogenization of the final extract will be achieved with vortex agitation prior
to the chromatographic analysis（Yang, 2006d）.

Sample preparation for differential scanning calorimetry

(2006) as follows：Millet wine is dialyzed using a dialysis tubing of molecular weight

against the phosphate buffer（pH4.6, containing 8% ethanol） for 72 hrs at 4°C in a
cold room. Several changes of buffer, 500 ml each, are given for equilibration of the
protein sol. The protein sample is carefully collected from the dialysis tubing and
lyophilized. Samples are stored at -40℃ until use.

solution. The collected protein fractions are monitored at 280 nm absorbance. The

101-104℃間（Tp=102℃）有一狹長峰值，有個明顯的吸熱反應。兩個吸熱峰可
能表示小米酒混濁蛋白有兩個以不同溫度區間的吸熱現象，造成不同階段的熱性
質轉變。尤其在 32℃~69℃（Tp=61℃）的區間，可能使數各蛋白質表現一種”
混成”熱反應，而 101-104℃區間的吸熱峰較“敏銳”，推測可能是單一蛋白質

結論
10 mg 的小米酒混濁蛋白與 20μl 水混合在 70℃~98℃（Tp=94℃）之間以在
98-104℃間（Tp=100℃）有峰值。由於小米酒混濁蛋白是ㄧ群蛋白質，兩個吸熱
峰可能表示混濁蛋白有兩個以不同溫度區間的吸熱現象，造成不同階段的熱性質
轉變。尤其在 70℃~98℃（Tp=94℃）的區間，可能使數各蛋白質表現一種”混
成”熱反應；而 98-104℃區間的吸熱峰較“敏銳”（sharp），推測可能是單一
蛋白質熱變化被觀察到。添加單寧後，熱變化除了源自蛋白質熱變性，另ㄧ主要
因素為單寧加入之後，激烈的 protein-tannin, tannin-tannin(因為單寧是一
混合物)、與 protein-protein interactions 強烈發生。
