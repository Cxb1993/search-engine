RCS Reduction of a Composite AMC Structure 
 
 
Yueh-Lin Tsai*, Ruey-Bing Hwang 
Institute of Communication Engineering, National Chiao-Tung University, 
Hsinchu, Taiwan 
E-mail:  yuehlintsai.cm97g@nctu.edu.tw, raybeam@mail.nctu.edu.tw 
 
 
ABSTRACT: We studied the RCS reduction characteristics of a checkerboard 
structure configured with two different AMC unit cells with 180゜ reflection 
phase difference near its resonance frequency. A simple array factor formula is 
adopted to show the philosophy of the physical mechanism. Both the numerical 
and experimental results have been achieved and compared. Good agreement 
from results was obtained and the ability for RCS reduction rate of this composite 
AMC structure is over 10dB within 16-25GHz. 
 
Introduction 
 
Radar cross section (RCS) reduction has been studied extensively in recent decays 
due to the demand of military development. There are two main philosophies for 
the design of RCS reduction. One is to absorb the incoming wave and the other 
one is to redirect the incoming wave away from the incident direction. The most 
famous method for the former one is the use of Salisbury screen which provides a 
lossy screen to prevent the reflection of the incoming wave within certain 
frequency bands [1-2] and the latter one typically utilizes the variation of the 
shape to prevent the reflection. Recently, a new metallic electromagnetic structure 
has been proposed to have the characteristics of high impedance surface (HIS) 
near its resonance frequency [3] to reduce the thickness of the Salisbury screen 
[4]. As was well known, a surface having a high surface-impedance boundary 
condition which is defined as the ratio between the tangential electric and 
magnetic fields on the surface can mimic the reflection characteristics of a perfect 
magnetic conductor (PMC). Different techniques for the design of PMC unit cell 
have been developed in the literatures for the thin and broadband radar absorbing 
material (RAM) design [4-10]. Which include the application of the Micro-
genetic Algorithm to design the multiple frequency selective surface (FSS) 
screens [6] and the use of different arrangement of AMC cells to rearrange the 
reflection phases to redirect the incoming wave away from the incident direction 
within frequency bands [7]. 
    In this paper, we adopt the concept of two AMCs with different reflection 
phase characteristics arranged in a checkerboard configuration with 180o 
reflection phase difference to eliminate the reflection waves under normal 
incidence condition to reduce the RCS. By analyzing the simple array factor 
formula as described in equation (1) we could know that if the phase difference of 
the two elements maintain170o ~ 190o the RCS could be reduced above 10dB.

論文題目 EM Shielding Techniques Using Composite Dielectrics 
一、參加經過 
 
自從 2003 年由中日韓三國共同舉辦第一屆的亞洲閃電防護討論會(Asia Lightning Protection 
Forum, ALPF)開始，此會議也陸續在漢城、東京、廣州、濟州島、橫濱等地舉辦，ALPF儼
然成為亞洲區閃電防護的盛會。經過七年間各主辦單位的努力，其重要性從亞洲地區躍昇為
亞洲與太平洋地區，成為亞太國際閃電研討會。 
 
本次會議於星期二在成都的總府皇冠假日酒店開始。一行人搭乘星期二早上的班機抵達成
都，並馬上前往大會本部註冊，也參與大會所舉辦之晚宴。本次研討會主辦單位總共籌辦 10
場特別論壇和 15場技術論壇，由於時間限制，僅能選擇與本人研究相關的場次聆聽。 
 
我的兩個博士班學生也在這次會議中報告本實驗室的研究成果，分別是「由寬頻雷擊波激發
高阻抗表面之散射特性」和「使用複合性介質材料之電磁波防護技術」。第一篇論文探討當
寬頻雷擊波入射一般金屬和高阻抗表面的散射特性比較。我們結合兩種不同類型的人造磁導
體單元並以棋盤狀排列重新組合成一高阻抗表面。利用此一自行開發的高阻抗表面可將入射
電磁波散射到各個方位，並且可降低包覆在高阻抗表面下之電路的電磁干擾，使其維持正常
運作。除了數值模擬和實驗量測，我們也使用準確但簡易的公式估計其效果。第二篇論文提
出一種採用複合性介質材料的新型電磁防護技術，利用具特殊幾何形狀之複合性介質材料可
以改變入射電磁波之軌跡，使其轉彎而繞過欲保護之電路。我們設計了一個在矩形波導中的
實驗，並使用網路分析儀量測其反射係數和透射係數。利用自行發展的模態轉換理論可以清
楚解釋此技術背後之物理意義。
