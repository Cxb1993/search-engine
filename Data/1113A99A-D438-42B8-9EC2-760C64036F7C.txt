介孔氧化鋯感測器應用於自動化檢測氧氣體 
The application of the mesoporous ZrO2 for the automated 
testing of oxygen

一、中文摘要 
介孔材料具有高比表面積、均勻孔洞大
小和可控制孔洞大小等特性，是近年來相當引
人注目的研究領域之ㄧ。本研究是利用三嵌段
兩性共聚物作為模板，氯化鋯和氯化釔為反應
前驅物，以溶膠–凝膠法製備高比表面積的介
孔氧化鋯與介孔釔安定氧化鋯(YSZ) ，作為氧
氣體感測器材料。實驗是以TGA、XRD、TEM
及氮氣吸附/脫附曲線分析該材料之微結構及
比表面積，並探討各種合成因素例如改變三嵌
段兩性共聚物濃度與煆燒溫度、釔添加量及前
驅物中添加水量對介孔氧化鋯與介孔YSZ 之
結構影響。 
實驗結果顯示，使用三嵌段兩性共聚物的
添加量為 1.5 g，煆燒溫度 500 ºC，可合成出
比表面積約為 67.92 m2/g、孔洞直徑約為 69.77 
Å 的介孔氧化鋯。當釔添加量從 0YSZ 改變
至 12YSZ 時，經煆燒溫度 500 ºC，其比表面
積會從 67.92 m2/g 增加至 128.33 m2/g，孔徑
從 69.77 Å 縮小至 62.73 Å。經由 XRD 分析可
知在反應系統中水濃度的含量不會影響氧化
鋯之結晶相，可合成單一立方晶相的介孔
YSZ，而由 BET 分析顯示其比表面積隨著水
取代量越多則越小，以釔添加量為 12YSZ 經
煆燒溫度 500 ºC 為例，水取代量從 0 wt% ~ 
50 wt% 時，其比表面積會從 128.33 m2/g 縮
小至 109.85 m2/g，平均孔徑從 62.73 Å 減少
到 46.99 Å。 
關鍵詞：介孔、三嵌段兩性共聚物、氧化鋯、
溶膠–凝膠法 
Abstract 
Mesoporous materials have attracted 
particular attention because they usually show 
higher surface area, regular pore size and 
tunable pore structures compared with 
nanocrystalline materials. In this study, the 
mesoporous ZrO2 and Yttrium-Stabilized 
Zirconia(YSZ) were prepared by sol-gel process 
and the synthesis was accomplished by using 
block copolymer as the template, and ZrOCl2‧
8H2O, YCl3‧6H2O as the inorganic precursor. 
TGA, XRD, TEM and N2 adsorption-desorption 
isotherms were used to characterize the 
microstructure of the samples. We adjusted the 
experimental parameters such as the 
concentration of block copolymer, calcination 
temperatures, molar ratio of Yttrium to 
Zirconium, and additive of water to investigate 
their effects on the structure of mesoporous 
ZrO2 and YSZ.

由X-ray 繞射圖，可以觀察出純-氧化鋯
煆燒溫度在400 ºC 時，顯示出在晶相組成上

著煆燒溫度增加至500 ºC 時，開始有單斜晶
相氧化鋯生成，煆燒溫度增加至600 ºC 時，
有很尖銳的單斜晶相繞射峰出現，表示具有很
好的結晶性，而煆燒溫度增加至700 ºC 時，
其結晶性極佳，但可能仍具有介孔結構。 
利用 Scherrer equation 公式可計算出試
片的晶粒大小，使用 1 g 三嵌段兩性共聚物，
經 400 ºC、500 ºC、600 ºC 及 700 ºC 煆燒溫
度後，晶粒大小各為 5.1 nm、8.8 nm、11.7 nm 
及 23 nm，使用 2.5 g 三嵌段兩性共聚物，經
400 ºC、500 ºC、600 ºC 及 700 ºC 煆燒溫度
後，晶粒大小各為 9 nm、11.7 nm、15.5 nm 及
24 nm。由此可知當添加相同重量之三嵌段兩
性共聚物，其煆燒溫度對所合成的氧化鋯晶粒
大小有明顯的影響，且三嵌段兩性共聚物的添
加量也會影響其晶粒的大小。 
對這些顆粒觀察介孔氧化鋯必須使用 
TEM 。Figure 3 為使用 1.5 g 三嵌段兩性共
聚物所合成之介孔氧化鋯經煆燒溫度 500 ºC 
的 TEM 結構分析圖。由圖中可以觀察出有晶
格相，而且在繞射圖中有明顯的繞射環，說明
了在 500 ºC 時已具有很好的結晶性，其晶粒
大小分別約為 7 ~ 9 nm。由於介孔的型態非常
不明顯，在圖中很難辨別，所以此試樣之介孔
結構可利用氮氣吸附/脫附曲線分析所得到之
結果提供證據來探討之。 
由於 TEM 分析圖只能觀察到試片中局部
的區域，試片整體的孔洞形態可由氮氣吸附/
脫附曲線說明。本研究以三嵌段兩性共聚物為
天然模板的孔洞來源，當三嵌段兩性共聚物於
溶液中達臨界微胞濃度(CMC)時，可獲得大量
甚至有序之介孔結構與高比表面積之氧化鋯
粉末。以氮氣吸附/脫附曲線量測氧化鋯之比
表面積與孔徑分布(依據 BJH model 理論)。 
Figure 4 所示為使用 1.5 g 的三嵌段兩性
共聚物經煆燒溫度 500 ºC、600 ºC 及 700 ºC 
後，所得到之介孔氧化鋯粉末之氮氣吸附/脫
附曲線圖與孔徑分布圖，此試樣之三嵌段兩性
共聚物濃度於溶液中達臨界微胞濃度，故曲線
均具有 type Ⅳ 形狀[6, 7]，且圖中可明顯觀察
到 type Ⅳ 之遲滯曲線，表示產物中具有介孔
結構，即為孔洞結構存在之特徵。介孔結構未
消失前其遲滯曲線為典型 H2-type，而介孔結
構消失後則為 H1-type，插圖為 BJH 的吸附曲
線分析。

OXIDATION OF N-GAN BY ELECTROCHEMICAL ETCHING   
 
Lap Hong Chan* Weng-Sing Hwang* Ya Chi Shen** Po-Chang Chen** and Lay Gaik 
Teoh***  
*National Cheng Kung University, TAIWAN 
**Nan Jeon Institute of Technology, TAIWAN 
***National Pingtung University of Science and Technology, TAIWAN 
 
In this study, the formation of n-type GaN oxidation layer obtained under constant potential 
anodic process has been investigated. A 3-μm thick Si-doped n-GaN film was grown on a 
sapphire substrate by MOCVD for sample preparation. Some defect pits exist on sample 
surface as shown in Figure 1(a). The oxidation layer structure distributions strongly depend 
on electrochemical condition. In comparison to anodization of GaN with other traditional 
electrolytes, the anodic process with the nitric acid system, suggests more effectively and 
variety oxidation results. The mechanism of electrochemical etching including oxidation and 
dissolution steps taking place by turns. Nitric acid acts as a powerful oxidant agent to 
contribute to the oxidation of GaN [1]. Compare with bulk n-GaN shown in Figure 1(a), the 
anodized in low applied potential n-GaN reveals higher density submicron pits formed are 
found on the surface of the wafer as shown in Figure 1(b). For anodization with appropriate 
high applied potential range, surface is covered with thin oxidation layer as shown in Figure 
1(c). By SEM cross section image, Figure 1(d) shows the thin oxidation layer of n-GaN may 
lift off the sapphire substrate. Table 1 summarizes the EDX measurement results from the 
SEM top plan and cross section images. The results show the element atomic percent of Ga, 
N and O. It reveals the oxide is exist on the surface of n-GaN. The percentage of oxide on the 
surface nucleation layer is more than in the inner porous layer. The percentage of oxide is 
greater at higher rather than low potential. The results indicate that both the composition and 
the porosity can be controlled by the applied potential. The EDX analysis, performed by using 
SEM on surfaces, has demonstrated that this phenomenon implies a thin oxide layer exist on 
surface after anodization of n-GaN in nitric aqueous system. The oxide grown on GaN is 
useful for use in metal-oxide-semiconductor device [2, 3].
