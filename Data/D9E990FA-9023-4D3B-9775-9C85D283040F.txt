Abstract 
 
Solid oxide fuel cell (SOFC) is one of important power generation devices which are 
currently developed in the world. The fuel cell has several advantages, including high power 
transferring efficiency and low contamination. This study used planar-type design to prepare 
cell due to its simple structure and easy mass production. The design is also the recently 
focus of the field. The made fuel cells were sintered from the combination of 
anode-electrolyte –cathode layer structure. The surface might in contact with steel housing (a 
Crofer 22 alloy) when the cells were assembled to a stack. Therefore, several potential glass 
materials were developed, and the long-term sealing used between different interfaces was 
intensively investigated. The electrolyte materials, CeO2 and Bi2O3, the glass sealing in the 
tests of chemical properties, thermal properties, high temperature creeping property, and the 
evolution of all components during 5000 hr holding at operation temperature are investigated. 
The report has including eight subjects. There are   
(1) Assembly of anode tape and 1st and 2nd E-layer (rpt1); 
(2) Characterization of Ni foam and net for current collector (rpt-2); 
(3) Glass module used for gas channels (rpt-3, has been submitted for patent 
application); 
(4) Preparation and characterization of glass sealing for intermediate temperature 
solid oxide fuel cell (rpt-4);  
(5) Leakage test of solid oxide fuel cell (rpt-5); 
(6) Low temperature conductivity of porous Al2O3 foam for SOFC (rpt-6); 
(7) Creep of G1A5 glass ceramics the application of SOFC (rpt-7); 
 
Keywords: fuel cell, solid electrolyte, sealing, long-term test

Resistance of Ni-foam and Ni-mesh used for IT-SOFC 
S. E. Lin and W. C. J. Wei 
8/13/2010 
 
Abstract 
 Ni metal in the shape of foam or mesh structure can be used as current collector, 
gas distributor, and catalyst to reform methane or other hydro-carbon gases into 
smaller molecules, such as CO and H2., In order to meet the functions of a current 
collector for IT-SOFC, the resistance behavior of two commercial Ni-mesh and mesh 
were investigated in the conditions during heating and cooling from room temperature 
to 800oC at 5oC/min of heating rate. The results show that Ni metal oxidizes to NiO 
and the resistance increases dramatically at the temperature changing from 600oC to 
650oC. The investigation of microstructure variation of Ni-foam before and after 
oxidation and reduction at 800oC was also carried out and reported in this study. 
 
Keywords: Ni, current collector, oxidation, reduction

Synthesis and Long-term Test of Silicate-Based Sealing Glass for  
Intermediate Temperature Solid Oxide Fuel Cell 
 
Sung-En Lin*, Yi-Ru Cheng*, C. H. Hsueh*,#, and W. C. J. Wei* 
*Dept. Mat. Sci. Eng., National Taiwan University, Taipei, 106, Taiwan, ROC 
#Materials Sci. Tech. Div., Oak Ridge National Laboratory, TN, USA 
#Dept of Phys. Astro., Univ. Tennessee, TN, USA 
 
Abstract 
A series of silicate-based glasses, either BaO-SiO2-B2O3 ternary or 
BaO-SiO2-B2O3-Al2O3 quaternary systems, are prepared as sealing materials for 
intermediate temperature (500-650oC) solid oxide fuel cells (IT-SOFCs). Crystalline 
phase, glass forming ability, and thermo-chemical stabilities of the glasses are 
characterized. Additionally, the effect of B2O3/SiO2 ratio, Al2O3 and BaO contents on 
coefficient of thermal expansion (CTE) are discussed and compared. Among those glasses, 
one formulation, G6, has the CTE value of 10.1 ppm/K, which is close to those of 
Y2O3-doped ZrO2 (YSZ), Sm2O3-doped CeO2 (SDC), anode (YSZ+NiO) substrates, and 
Crofer 22 metallic alloy. Test results show that the G6 glass can fully wet those substrates 
at sealing temperatures higher than 975oC. The possible interface reactions of the glass 
with those cell components are investigated by element mapping, XRD, and SEM. 
Long-term sealing test holding at 650oC for 5000 hr and leakage test up to 650oC are also 
conducted. The results show excellent stable conditions of one glass (M-G6) compatible 
with the other fuel cell components.  
 
Keywords: Solid oxide fuel cell, glass, silicates, borates.

Low Thermal Conductivity of Porous Al2O3 Foams for SOFC Insulation 
 
 
Y. W. Lo*, W. C. J. Wei*,1, and C. H. Hsueh*,+ 
*Dept. Mat. Sci. Eng., National Taiwan University, Taipei, Taiwan, ROC 
+Materials Sci. Tech. Div., Oak Ridge National Lab., Oak Ridge, TN, USA 
+Dept. Physics and Astronomy, Univ. Tennessee, Knoxville, TN, USA 
 
Abstract 
Ceramic thermal insulator is one of the important thermal management devices 
for SOFC operating at medium temperatures (<800oC). This study used colloidal 
dispersion, direct foaming method, microwave drying, and sintering to prepare porous 
Al2O3 foams with porosity of 85%~95%. The measurement of thermal conductivity 
by a hot-wire method was conducted and also calibrated to an accuracy of +0.01 
W/m*K. The effects of various porous states of the structure on thermal conductivity 
are investigated. By optimizing porosity, grain size, and gas cell structures of Al2O3 
foams, low thermal conductivities of 0.05 W/m*K at room temperature and 0.14 
W/m*K at 800oC are achieved in this study.  
 
Keyword: thermal conductivity, ceramics, microstructure

2.1 參加口頭報告 
參加人報告的主題是燃料電池氧化鉍電解質的製程改善，主題為”用於固態
燃料電池之釔添加鈮酸鉍電解質(Y-doped bismuth niobate (Bi0.79Nb0.21O1.71) 
electrolytes for SOFC)”，內容是陳述 　-相氧化鉍具有很高的氧離子傳導性質，
也被視為固態燃料電池的潛在固態電解質材料之一。但這個氧化物需要相穩定
劑，在室溫至 650oC 的操作溫度之間維持相穩定。本研究選用氧化鈮，但每添加
一單位五價次的鈮離子，即減少一單位氧空缺。另方面，在此研究也有少量(<6 
mol%)的鈮被三價次的釔離子所取代。因為鈮離子遠小於鉍離子，所以這種固溶
的氧化鉍基(bismuth-based)雖然含有較少量的氧空缺，但其氧傳導性會有不錯的
表現。研究以膠粒製程(colloidal process)來達到均勻分散的目的，我們將三種粉
體分別分散，然後按不同次序混合，得到的生坯體可以在 760oC-800oC 燒結一到
二小時，達到緻密化，微結構僅有一種鈣鈦礦(fluorite)晶相。電導性方面，B21N
之中以 Y 取代 Nb，樣品內的氧空缺預期會增加一些，但是因為釔離子接近鉍離
子，所以氧的擴散需要較高活化能，因此當 6 mol%Y 取代 Nb，也就是 B15B-6Y
樣品，可以展現最高的電導特性。未來我們的研究將以電腦模擬方法，分析氧空
缺濃度(oxygen vacancy concentration)、取代離子尺寸(size effect)及離子結合(ion 
association)三方面，來了解雙重添加的氧化鉍的氧離子導電性為何能夠增加。

三、建議 
    1. 固態燃料電池的研究在美國仍然維持一定的能量，經費也維持高檔，但
研究方向部分還集中在高溫催化行為，以及使用碳合成氣(coal syngas)的應用研
究，研究目標清楚。但國內仍將 SOFC 規劃在氫能研究之下，並未將 SOFC 燃氣
的來源定位在可靠、低價的來源，例如沼氣，或是其他天然氣副產品，實為可惜。 
 
 2, 美國幾個重要研究單位，PNNL，NETL(National Energy Technology 
Laboratory)都有完整的單電池及電池組的測試能力，前者目前進行的單電池測試
以 2000 小時為目標，後者則著重在煤碳後端的應用。國內國家研究室都還沒有
完整的測試流程，國內技術明顯落後國外四、五年。

2.3  其他重要演講 
        Ed. Orton  紀念演講是在 1933 年美國陶瓷學會開始，Orton 先生曾在 1899‐ 
1917 擔任美國陶瓷學會秘書，1930‐31 擔任學會理事長。今年委員會邀請瑞士的
Gauckler 博士主講  “經由修飾固液相及固氣相介面進行陶瓷製程的創新研究”。
演講從膠粒製程開始，說明陶瓷顆粒表面可以經由界面劑的添加，改變陶瓷表面
電位能(potential energy)，或是經由乳化(emulsion)，改變顆粒反應型態，或是結
合高分子，進行不同材料的複合(synergize)化。 
 
        Gauckler 的演講主題中包括我們最近多孔材的研究，他將多孔材應用在飛機
及發電機的隔熱，對於結合微細乳膠粒在多孔壁(wall)內，提高孔隙率到 98%，
增加隔熱效果，證實有明顯助益。另一個是使用片狀晶(例如六角氧化鋁顆粒，
在氣泡表面平躺，以平板乘載，乾燥後的表面塗上黏膠，然後再鍍上一層片狀晶，
重複上述步驟，得到層狀複合材料，這複合材具有很好的強度，極佳的破壞變形
量，應變(strain)可以增加到十幾個百分比。
