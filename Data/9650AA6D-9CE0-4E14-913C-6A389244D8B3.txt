 migration coefficient of steady state and non-steady state, the concrete stored in room temperature is 1 
to 16 times higher than the concrete stored in different environment temperature, among this two states, 
the influence on non-steady state migration coefficient is more remarkable. The concrete adding 
mineral admixtures and then stored in different environment temperature resulted in increasing the 
chloride penetration time and degrade the steady state and non-steady state migration coefficient, 
moreover, the degree of deterioration in the mineral admixture concrete is higher than normal concrete, 
but the result between ponding test and ACMT has different trend, the concrete with mineral 
admixture can improve the deterioration which cause of the temperature effect. 
 
一、前言 
混凝土材料已經廣泛的使用於各種建設，世界各地皆有符合當地需求的標準及規範，加上
各種不同的掺料搭配使用，混凝土已經發展的相當成熟。混凝土本身雖然有高強度、經濟、耐
久等優點，但是處於高溫環境下可能引起膨脹使內部裂縫與孔隙連通，導致混凝土滲水，使得
氯等有害離子進入混凝土，進而腐蝕鋼筋，增加危險性。所以探討溫度對混凝土內部孔隙結構
的影響是相當重要的課題。由於緯度高低不同，各地區氣候上也有很大的差異，許多熱帶與亞
熱帶國家長期處在較高的氣溫環境，氣溫甚至高達 40℃以上，一般混凝土於過去的使用經驗中，
環境溫度變化對混凝土內部劣化方面的資料較欠缺。本研究為探討混凝土在各種不同溫度環境
下，對於混凝土之內部孔隙結構變化會有多大的影響。同時也依比例添加飛灰、爐石於混凝土，
與普通混凝土做比較，作為礦物掺料改善混凝土耐久性與耐高溫的效能評估。 
本研究之混凝土試體，採用不同水膠比(w/b)，並依照比例添加礦物掺料，貯存在室溫(25±2
℃)、40℃、60℃、80℃等不同溫度環境。主要研究目的為： 
1.比較不同溫度環境下混凝土力學性質與混凝土抵抗氯離子能力。 
2.比較一般混凝土與添加礦物掺料混凝土之力學性質與耐久性差異。 
3.以 ACMT、Ponding test 探討混凝土內部劣化程度。

2.4 Salt Pnding test浸漬試驗 
Ponding test 利用外在浸漬溶液中的氯離子濃度與混凝土內部之濃度差使其滲入混凝土試
體，進而利用其氯離子濃度與滲入深度評估混凝土之耐久性。試驗法參照 AASHTO T259 [3]試
驗，並採用直徑 10 cm、高 10 cm 之混凝土圓柱體；根據研究指出，混凝土試體烘乾後，混凝土
內部濕度極低導致氯離子快速侵入試體，且內部氯離子濃度點位紊亂，無法正確計算擴散係數，
固本研究之 Ponding test 試驗前先使試體內部飽和，試驗前處理參考 ASTM C1202 RCPT 的抽真
空步驟。Ponding test 驗步驟如下： 
 
1、將壓克力環安裝於混凝土試體上方，周圍以環氧樹脂塗封。  
2、參照 ASTM C1202 RCPT 裡的試體前處理抽真空，讓試體內部充滿水分飽和。 
3、將濃度 3 %之 NaCl 溶液到入上方壓克力環中，如圖 3，並以保鮮膜將其封起，避免蒸發。 
4、放置 91 天後將試體取下，使用鑽石切刀切割出 2.5×2.5×10 cm 的立方柱體，並由試體滲透面
開始往下間隔 0.5 cm 逐一切取試片，如圖 4。 
5、將 2.5×2.5×0.5cm 的試片磨成粉末通過 50 號篩，取其過篩的粉末。 
6、參照 AASHTO T260 酸溶法[4]，將 3g 的粉末置於燒杯中，加入 10 ml 之去離子水煮沸，再
倒入 1 ml 濃硝酸 HNO3，於加熱攪拌機上加熱 2 分鐘，在加入 50 ml 之沸騰去離子水，沸騰
5 分鐘後將燒杯自加熱器取下降溫。 
7、利用 Whatman #40、#41 號濾紙過濾溶液中之雜質細粒，留下溶液部份。 
8、利用電位滴定法量測各杯溶液之氯離子濃度。

化程度並探討其內部孔隙連通情形。

四、結論 
1. 室溫貯存與烘箱貯存的混凝土抗壓強度相對百分比大約都在 100~70％之間。由抗壓試驗的
結果顯示，80℃以內的貯存溫度對混凝土抗壓強度影響不大。 
2. 經由烘箱貯存的混凝土的穿透時間，C 組 AIR 所需的穿透時間較烘箱 40℃高出 1.56 h~9.62 
h，較 60℃高出約 0.19 h~6.72 h，較 80℃高出約 0.84 h~4.43 h，穿透時間在 40℃就有明顯變
化；在室溫貯存環境下 SF 組之穿透時間較 C 組低 8.3 h~23.07 h，經溫度效應後穿透時間與
C 組僅相差 0.34 h~2.52 h，SF 組部份配比之穿透時間低於 C 組，證實經過烘箱烘烤後，混
凝土即使添加礦物摻料，對降低氯離子穿透能力並無明顯幫助。 
3. C 組之非穩態傳輸係數在不同溫度之烘箱貯存增加 1.50~9.42 倍，SF 組增加 3.31~16.76 倍，
相同水膠比與溫度環境下 SF 組之 Rn值大於 C 組，表示 SF 組受到溫度效應後劣化較多，且
部分 SF 組之 Mnc明顯高於 C 組，說明溫度效應影響傳輸係數大於改變水膠比與添加礦物摻
料。

3. Results and discussion
3.1. Compressive strength and splitting tensile strength
From the obtained results in Table 3, it can be concluded that
normal weight concrete (C series) showed the higher compressive
strength and splitting tensile strengthwhen compared to the heavy
weight concrete (H series) with the same w/c ratios.
For the compressive strength of concrete in Table 3, the differ-
ence between control specimens and specimens subjected to 95 ◦C
isnot signiﬁcant for allmixes. The compressive strengthofC28, C32,
H28, and H32 mixes showed a minor difference of 0%, 1%, 6.3%, and
8.4%, respectively.
The splitting tensile strength of the control specimens is slightly
higher than that of the specimens subjected to 95 ◦C. The splitting
tensile strength of the control specimens for series C is higher than
that for series H with the same w/c ratios. After being heated, the
splitting tensile strength reduces to a limited range (4.13–4.96MPa
for w/c =0.28, and 3.31–4.14MPa for w/c =0.32). It seems to indi-
cate that the pore structure becomes the same for the concrete
exposed to 95 ◦C. The splitting tensile strength of C28, C32, H28,
and H32 mixes showed a reduction of 19.6%, 30.0%, 1.5%, and 16.4%,
respectively, when the specimens were subjected to 95 ◦C.

crete is a way to understand the permeability of these materials.

solution in the anodic cell and was the average of three individ-

non-steady-state, transition period, and steady-state with respect
to the changeof the chloride concentration. In thenon-steady-state,
the chloride ions are in the process of migrating through saturated
pores in concrete and have not yet reached the anodic cell. In this

3.4. Effect of 95 ◦C on the chloride migration coefﬁcient of
concrete
In the transition period, the chloride concentration vs. time for
C32mix at room temperature (control specimen) and 95 ◦C (heated
specimen) is shown in Fig. 4. It can be seen from this ﬁgure that the
breakthrough time (tb) is signiﬁcantly reduced for the specimens
exposed to 95 ◦C. The non-steady-state migration coefﬁcient (Mn)
is determined based on measurements of the time-span for chlo-
ride ion penetration through specimen. As observed from Table 4,
the non-steady-state migration coefﬁcients of specimens at 95 ◦C
are higher than that at room temperature. The non-steady-state
migration coefﬁcient of C28, C32, H28, and H32 mixes showed a
signiﬁcant increase of 11.4, 14.6, 5.1, and 4.6 times, respectively,
when concrete specimens were exposed to 95 ◦C.
In Fig. 5, theACMTresults of chloride concentration in theanodic
solution at room temperature (control specimen) and 95 ◦C (heated
specimen) are plotted as a function of time. In Fig. 5, the chloride
migration rate (K) at 95 ◦C is higher than that at room tempera-
ture. The steady-state migration coefﬁcient (Ms) is based on the
measurement of the constant ﬂux of chloride ions passing through
the specimen in steady-state. In Table 5, the steady-state migra-
tion coefﬁcients for all mixes at 95 ◦C are higher than that at room
temperature. As concrete specimens were exposed to 95 ◦C, the
steady-state migration coefﬁcient of C28, C32, H28, and H32 mixes
showed a signiﬁcant increase of 7.1, 5.3, 2.3, and 2.5 times, respec-
tively.
Concrete transport properties are controlled by the transport
properties of its constituent phases and their geometric arrange-
ment. The aggregate commonly used in concrete has much lower
transport properties than cement paste, if the bond between aggre-
gate andpaste is perfect, the cement-based compositematerialwill
be less transport properties than the matrix. Shah [15] and Dela-
grave et al. [16] pointed out that the inclusion of aggregates in the
hydrated cement pastmatrix has two opposite effects on the trans-
port properties. The dilution (blocking) and tortuosity (redirecting)
effects reduce cement-based materials transport properties while
the interfacial transition zone (ITZ) and percolation effects increase
the transport properties. The ITZ that develops between the aggre-
gate and matrix allowing accelerated ingress and movement of
chloride ions and increases the transport properties of concrete.
