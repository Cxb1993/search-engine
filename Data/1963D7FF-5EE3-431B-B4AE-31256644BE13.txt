Abstract
This is the first study about an online vehicle type classifier for road-side radar
detectors in multi-lane environments. The road-side radar detector is developed based
on frequency-modulated continuous wave (FMCW) radar with the carrier frequency
at X-band. A two-dimension Gaussian Mixed Model (GMM) is employed to develop
the online learning model for vehicle type classifier. Expectation maximization (EM)
algorithm is implemented to obtain the parameters of GMM. Vehicles are classified
into three major categories, large vehicle, small vehicle and motorcycles, and the
features used in vehicle type classification include average energy maximum and
average energy variance. In the field tests, the accuracy of online vehicle type
classifier can achieve about 60%.
Keywords FMCW, Gaussian Mixed Model (GMM), Expectation maximization (EM)
