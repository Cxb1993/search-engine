Abstract 
A series of high-carbon Cr-based hard-facing alloys were successfully fabricated on a substrate of S45C 
steel by gas tungsten arc welding (GTAW) process using various alloy fillers with chromium, chromium 
carbide (Cr:C = 4:1), silver and titanium powders. The analysis of the claddings consists of hardness test, 
chemical composition analysis, microstructure observation and evaluation of crack. This purpose is to discuss 
the effect of various Ti and Ag contents on the properties and crack improvement of the claddings. Crack 
length per area of the claddings substantially lowers from 1.7 cm-1 to 0.2 cm-1, as Ag content in filler ranges 
from 0 wt% to 0.5 wt%. As Ti content in filler increases from 0 wt% to 1.5 wt%, the crack length per area of 
the claddings decreases 58.6%. The microstructure of the hard-facing alloys with different Ag contents 
consists in great amounts of primary M7C3 carbides formed in the surrounding eutectic colonies [α+M7C3]. 
When the addition content of titanium in filler ranges from 0 wt% to 1.5 wt%, microstructure consists of 
primary (Cr,Fe,Ti)7C3 carbides and eutectic [α+(Cr,Fe,Ti)7C3]. When the addition content of titanium is 2.0 
wt%, microstructure contains the duplex carbides consisted of (Cr,Fe,Ti)23C6 outer shells surrounding 
(Cr,Fe,Ti)7C3 cores and eutectic [α+(Cr,Fe,Ti)23C6] phase. According to the results of WDS analysis, Ag 
element mainly dissolves into the eutectic α. Ti element mainly combines with Cr, Fe and C to form the 
(Cr,Fe,Ti)7C3 carbides and only a small part of Ti element dissolves into the eutectic α. According to the BEI 
observation, as the amount of Ag adds to 2.0 wt%, some tiny Ag particles uniformly precipitates on the

銲覆相當具有研究價值。本實驗所設計的過共晶高碳鉻基硬面合金中，碳的來源主要是藉著添加

3.10 裂縫改善成因探討 
本研究利用兩種方式來改善高碳鉻基硬面合金表面的脆裂程度，第一種方式是直接添加不同純元
素(鈦與銀)於合金填料中來改善銲覆層表面的脆裂程度；第二種方式則是添加不同助銲劑(螢石、松香
與硼砂)於合金填料中來改善銲覆層表面的脆裂程度。因本研究所添加的不同純元素與助銲劑會跟顯微
結構中不同相產生作用，因此要探討裂縫改善成因前，須先徹底了解所添加的物質在顯微結構中扮演
什麼角色。首先會先利用 FE-SEM 所附設的 EDS 進行成份半定量分析，探討所添加的物質是存在於初
晶相中還是留在基底相內，然後進一步了解這些元素對顯微結構中不同相的性質影響，再結合前面所
探討的裂縫產生原因，找出不同添加物對銲覆層裂縫改善的成因。 
3.11 建立裂縫改善機制圖 
當整個不同添加物對銲覆層裂縫改善的成因全部釐清之後，便可以進行裂縫改善機制圖的建立，
此圖的建立可以讓後續有興趣的讀者，快速了解不同添加物對銲覆層裂縫改善的機制，並將此機制應
用於不同系統的硬面合金中，倘若此機制可以有效地應用於不同的硬面合金系統中，這樣便可以將不
同硬面合金系統的表面性能提升。

次之；而當δ大於12%時是較無效果的。TiC為FCC結構，晶格常數a=0.46nm，M7C3碳化物為HCP結構，

的做為初晶碳化物M7C3的異質成核點，所以碳化鈦大部分析出在初晶M7C3與共晶組織的界面處。

2. Experimental details 
In this study, residual stress was made by welding process. 
After welding, vibrate these specimen with sub-resonant, 
resonant and wavelet frequency respectively, then measure 
their residual stress to compare which frequency have the best 
result. 
 
2.1 Welding Process 
The material used was as-received SS41 steel plate. The 
plate measured 450mm long x 300mm wide and was 14mm 
thick. Before welding, all specimens were annealed at 900℃ 
for one hour. After annealing, recorder the residual stress 
value of the stress free SS41 steel plate. Then weld without 
filler metal by GTAW. The welding current was 200A, 
voltage13V, and the traveling speed was 50mm/min. The 
additional parameters were tungsten rod, 3.0 mm in diameter, 
protective argon gas, 15 IPM. 
 
2.2. Vibration Stress Relief Setting 
Before determining the vibratory frequency for the welding; 
the welding table was vibrated continuously for 20 minutes 
until the system reached a stable condition. The entire 
vibration system setup was lay out at Fig. 1. Here, one 
vibration parameter determined by the meta-lax theory. 
Another vibration parameter was determined by the 
waveform. The criterion of waveform is to have the max 
acceleration per sec. 
The harmonic curve was determined by transducer with 
vibration controller and frequency & amplitude recorder and it 
was shown in Fig. 2. The system resonant frequency is 
51.1Hz. The sub-resonant frequency is 43.1Hz and the wavelet 
frequency is 49.7Hz. Waveforms were measured by an

enlarge the wave signal for observation at oscilloscope. The 
wave form of resonant, sub-resonant, and wavelet were list at 
Fig. 3a-c.

wave acceleration per second of resonant vibration is 1195.7 
g/s, with its long wavelength, the amplitude of resonant is the 
highest of the three vibrations unquestionably. If the vibration 
amplitude higher than the fixer can bear, rigid body vibration 
will appear. Wavelet can give high shear stress and low 
amplitude to prevent rigid body vibration. Otherwise, short 
wave length means stress relief will be more uniformly. In our 
tests, the degree of residual stress relieved from 28.5% (sub-
resonant) to 38.8 % (wavelet), more than 36% residual stress 
can be release by wavelet. 
The shape of the waveform should be an important factor in 
VSR treatment because of slope it means how large the force 
act on the workpiece in unit length but it was out of 
consideration in V.S.R. development. Besides, the value is 
more precisely to estimate the ability of a vibration. Actually, 
to determine the optima vibration condition always perplexes 
the users. However, V.S.R. will not become popular with a 
complex operation method. The use of waveform provides a 
fast way to determine the optimized vibration parameter to 
release more residual stress. 
Similar waveforms may also created by employing more than 
one vibrator. For instance, an eccentric motor with a high-
frequency magnetic vibrator can provide similar or a special 
waveform for more stress relief. Or distinctive waveform may 
created by vibrating in various ways. Thus, this paper is just 
the beginning of a new avenue of study into VSR.
