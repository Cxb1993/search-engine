湍流粗糙底床流量推估方法之研究與應用 
Study and Application of the Discharge Estimation for Rapid Channel 
Flow with Rough Bed 
摘要 
本實驗係採用光纖雷射杜卜勒測速儀（Fiber-optic Laser Doppler Velocimetry，簡稱 FLDV）
量測底床鋪設 8mm 玻璃顆粒之陡坡粗糙底床明渠流場。實驗目的主要在探討寬、窄流場於陡
坡粗糙底床水力特性與光滑渠流之異同，並且探討坡度及寬深比的變化對平均流速分佈之影
響。研究成果顯示，坡度、寬深比及垂線之位置等水流參數皆影響平均流速的分佈。平均速
度分佈已無法單用二維流場機制表達，垂線最大流速會發生於液面下，造就三維流況，故斷
面最大流速與其發生之位置是重要參數，其影響整個斷面流速分佈型態。此外，本文亦將三
種流速推估方法相互做比較，其所得推估流量結果皆頗為良好。以方便性而論，僅須於 Y 軸
上(通過斷面最大流速之垂線)量測得最大表面流速，再配合推估斷面積( estA )，則可快捷且精
確地獲致流量( maxest m est sQ A U A uψ −= = )。本研究完成後，可作為高效率流量量測方法建立之基
礎，快捷而有效地獲取所需之中高流量資料。 
關鍵詞：光纖雷射杜卜勒測速儀、寬深比、斷面最大流速、最大表面流速 
ABSTRACT 
The Fiber-optic Laser Doppler Velocimetry (FLDV) was adopted in this experimental study to 
investigate the characteristics of the steep open channel flow over a rough boundary, which was 
formed by gluing a layer of uniform 8 mm glass particles on the channel bottom. The experiments 
aimed at searching into the dissimilarities of the characteristics of the steep open channel flows over 
rough and smooth boundaries wide different channel widths. And both the influences of channel 
bed slope and aspect ratio (ratio of channel width to depth) on mean velocity profiles are 
investigated. In this study, the flow parameters including channel bed slope, aspect ratio and the 
location of measuring vertical affect the mean velocity profiles in a cross-section. The mean 
velocity profiles can not be expressed with a two-dimensional model. The maximum point velocity 
occurring below the water surface is an indication of three-dimensional flow. Thus both the 
magnitude and location of the cross-sectional maximum velocity are important indices influencing 
the pattern of velocity profiles in the whole cross-section. In addition, the results of comparing with 
three kinds of velocity estimation were very well. In contras with convenience, the proposed 
method, where only the maximum surface velocity (us-max) at Y-axis(vertical passing through the 
maximum point velocity of the cross section)need to be measured. The flow discharge (Q) was then 
computed by multiplying the estimated flow area (Aest) with mean velocity Um (ψus-max), which has 
the advantages of short operation time and high accuracy. The results of this study can be used as a 
basis of the high efficient flow discharge measurement, which can estimate the medium and high 
flow quickly and effectively. 
Keywords：Fiber-optic Laser Doppler Velocimetry (FLDV), aspect ratio (ratio of channel width to 
depth), cross-sectional maximum velocity, the maximum water surface velocity

摘要 
本實驗係採用光纖雷射杜卜勒測速儀（Fiber-optic Laser Doppler Velocimetry，簡
稱 FLDV）量測底床鋪設 8mm 玻璃顆粒之陡坡粗糙底床明渠流場。實驗目的主要在
探討寬、窄流場於陡坡粗糙底床水力特性與光滑渠流之異同，並且探討坡度及寬深比
的變化對平均流速分佈之影響。研究成果顯示，坡度、寬深比及垂線之位置等水流參
數皆影響平均流速的分佈。平均速度分佈已無法單用二維流場機制表達，垂線最大流
速會發生於液面下，造就三維流況，故斷面最大流速與其發生之位置是重要參數，其
影響整個斷面流速分佈型態。此外，本文亦將三種流速推估方法相互做比較，其所得
推估流量結果皆頗為良好。以方便性而論，僅須於 Y 軸上(通過斷面最大流速之垂線)
量測得最大表面流速，再配合推估斷面積 ( estA )，則可快捷且精確地獲致流量
( maxψ −= =est m est sQ A U A u )。本研究完成後，可作為高效率流量量測方法建立之基礎，
快捷而有效地獲取所需之中高流量資料。

ABSTRACT 
The Fiber-optic Laser Doppler Velocimetry (FLDV) was adopted in this experimental 
study to investigate the characteristics of the steep open channel flow over a rough 
boundary, which were formed by gluing a layer of uniform 8 mm glass particles on the 
channel bottom.  The experiments aimed at searching into the dissimilarities of the 
characteristics of the steep open channel flows over rough and smooth boundaries wide 
different channel widths. And both the influences of channel bed slope and aspect ratio 
(ratio of channel width to depth) on mean velocity profiles are investigated. In this study, 
the flow parameters including channel bed slope, aspect ratio and the location of measuring 
vertical affect the mean velocity profiles in a cross-section. The mean velocity profiles can 
not be expressed with a two-dimensional model. The maximum point velocity occurring 
below the water surface is an indication of three-dimensional flow. Thus both the 
magnitude and location of the cross-sectional maximum velocity are important indices 
influencing the pattern of velocity profiles in the whole cross-section.In addition, the 
results of comparing with three kinds of velocity estimation were very well. In contras with 
convenience, the proposed method, where only the maximum surface velocity (us-max)at 
Y-axis(vertical passing through the maximum point velocity of the cross section)need to be 
measured. The flow discharge (Q) was then computed by multiplying the estimated flow 
area(Aest) with mean velocity Um (ψus-max),which has the advantages of short operation time 
and high accuracy. The results of this study can be used as a basis of the high efficient flow 
discharge measurement, which can estimate the medium and high flow quickly and 
effectively.

三、實驗設備及方法 
3.1 實驗設備 
本實驗之主要設備為循環水槽及光纖雷
射杜卜勒測速儀（FLDV）系統，粗糙底床所
採用之玻璃顆粒之中值粒徑為 8mm，以蜂巢
排列方式將玻璃珠排列至壓克力板上。將金漆
均勻噴至玻璃珠上，利用金漆與玻璃珠間之表
面張力，使玻璃珠間無空隙，即告完成。 
3.2 試驗方法 
探討不同坡度與寬深比對底床為完全粗
糙之明渠水流流場之水力特性的影響為本實
驗之研究目的，因此以粗糙（rough）底床與
光滑玻璃邊壁之水槽進行穩定（steady）、均勻
（uniform）的流場試驗。本實驗之主要獨立
變數為：渠道坡度、水流深度及距離邊壁的垂
線位置。渠道坡度依據水流狀況採用 0.3%、
1%、2% 與 3%四種，水深範圍 22 mm ~ 54 
mm，而距離邊壁的垂線位置分別為 0.5cm、
2.5 cm、5.0 cm、7.5 cm、10 cm 和 12.5 cm。
