Studies on Instrumentation of Sensory Quality Analysis for Taiwan Tea 
Andi Shau-mei OU 
Department of Food Science, Central Taiwan University of Science and Technology 
 
Abstract 
 
Green tea as non-fermented tea, Song-Bo-Zhang-Qing and Dong-Ding Oolong as particial- 
fermented tea and black tea as full-fermented tea were used as the experimental materials. The 
purpose of this study was to examine the correlations between the sensory qualities (including color, 
aroma, taste and aftertaste) evaluated by 7 well-trained panelists unsing quantitatively descriptive 
analysis with the analyses data obtained from electronic tongue and nose for these Taiwan special 
teas in order to investigate the feasibility of replacing sensory panel by these instruments to 
examine the sensory quality of these special teas. The results showed that in term of sensory 
characteristics, the dried-fruity, ripen-fruity, sweet and roasted aromas in green tea were quite weak 
and the red color and sour taste were much stronger in black tea while the sweet aftertaste, aroma 
aftertaste, salivated aftertaste, smooth taste, sweetness and floral aroma in Song-Bo-Zhang-Qing 
and Dong-Ding Oolong tea were much stronger. For the physicochemical properties, the contents 
of total catechins, total polyphenols and EGCG were found highest in green tea. It was found that 
the black tea with the lowest pH value, darkest infusion color, strongest chroma and highest amount 
of caffeine. There were no significant difference in the pH, infusion color and other chemical 
compounds between Song-Bo-Zhang-Qing and Dong-Ding Oolong teas. Using the cluster analysis 
to examine the discriminating ability for these Taiwan special teas, the electronic tongue was the 
best followed by physicochemical data, sensory analysis and electronic nose. Furthermore, using 
partial least square regression analysis to investigate the correlation between sensory with other 
data, the results indicated that the total polyphenols, total catechins, EGCG, EC in physicochemical 
analysis data were highly positive-correlated with the bitterness and astringent aftertaste in sensory 
data while they were highly negative-correlated with the salivated aftertaste, aroma aftertaste, 
smooth taste and sweetness. The Hunter a and b values were highly negative-corrected with the 
yellowish and bright color. There were four sensors (SYg/CTl、P40/1、SY/gCT、SY/Gh ) of 
electronic nose were highly positive-correlated with the flora aroma while the BA sensor and BB 
sensor of electronic tongue were highly positive-correlated with the sourness and sweetness, 
respectively. In conclusion, the sensory analysis indeed has high correlation with electronic tongue 
and nose and it indicates that it is feasible using the electronic tongue and nose to examine the 
sensory quality of Taiwan special teas once these instruments are well-trained by the sensory data.   
 
Keywords: Taiwan special tea, sensory evaluation, physicochemical analysis, electronic tongue, 
electronic nose.

三、感官品評之定量描述分析 (廖，2005) 
茶葉沖泡以茶葉：水=1：50 比例配製，秤取 15 g 茶葉放入白瓷茶壺 (規格內徑 9.5 cm，
外徑 10 cm，高 12 cm，容量約為 750 ml )中，沖入 100℃ 750 ml 沸騰的熱水，加蓋靜置 5 min
後，先將茶湯倒入聞香杯 (內徑 4 cm，外徑 4.2 cm，高 5 cm，容量約為 30 ml )中蓋上品評杯 
(內徑 5.5 cm，外徑 5.2 cm，高 3.5 cm，容量約為 30 ml )，再將茶湯由聞香杯反轉倒入品評杯
中，進行品評，以聞香杯中之香氣品評茶湯之香氣部份，品評杯中之茶湯進行茶湯水色、香
氣及餘後感之品評。 
採用定量描述分析法 (Quantitative Descriptive Analysis, QDA)。就茶湯水色、香氣、滋味
及餘後感四大部分之尋找描述語，經確認最後的茶湯描述語。再以不同處理的樣品請 12 位品
評員針對各描述語評分範圍進行強度的訓練 (1 分為最弱；9 分為最強)，並熟悉評分法的使
用。每次品評固定以松柏長青茶 (SB1) 為標準品，作為其他茶樣的對照評分，進行強度訓練，
使品評員熟練品評及評分方法，最後採具再現性與一致性之九位品評員進行之。

酚含量僅次於綠茶，一般而言，發酵程度最重之紅茶中兒茶素的氧化作用最重，理當總兒茶
素要偏低，然而於本試驗結果並非如此，造成此結果之原因可能是茶葉本身的老嫩程度或是
其茶種 (台茶 20 號) 之差異所造成。總游離胺基酸含量部分則是以綠茶含量最高，松柏長青

在茶湯之咖啡因與個別兒茶素含量部分，綠茶與紅茶具有最多的咖啡因含量。游離型兒

烏龍茶 (有焙火和無焙火)個別兒茶素含量部分的含量最低，結果與廖 (2005)結果數據相近。

五、感官品評與物化分析結果之相關性探討 
為進一步瞭解物化分析對品評描述語之整體相關性，意即物化分析的測定結果是否可以
反應出感官品評的感受，將特色茶之物化分析測定值與定量描述分析結果進行部分最小平方
迴歸 (PLS2)分析，分佈圖如圖十二所示，茶湯物化分析之總多元酚、兒茶素類，位置與感官
的收斂感接近；咖啡因、兒茶素類則是與感官的苦味、澀味之相對位置相近，具有高度之相
關性，而與生津感、回香感、順口感、甘味呈負相關，此結果與林 (1999) 研究指出總多元
酚、兒茶素含量與風味之苦味、澀味、收斂感有高度相符。茶葉之酚類化合物是茶湯主要苦
味以及澀味之來源，兒茶素類的游離型兒茶素 CEC 等偏苦，酯化型兒茶素偏澀 (中國茶學辭
典編簒委員會，1995、蔡，1990)，為影響茶湯色、香、味品質之最重要成分。因此茶湯中多
元酚、兒茶素含量高使得茶湯苦澀味較強，且不具生津感、順口感、甘味。 
物化分析測定之 Hunter L 值與茶湯品評項目水色的明亮度、黃色於圖上位置相近，具有
較高之相關性，但 Hunter a、b 與黃色、明亮度呈負相關。Hunter a、Hunter b 與品評之水色
紅色、滋味的酸味有較高的相關性，由於茶樣發酵程度越深，會使得茶湯之水色較深且酸味
