一、中文摘要
近年來累積計數和管制圖已獲證實能有效
管理高品質（接近零缺點）製程，但對於製
程不合格率出現小幅變動的偵察能力較為
薄弱。為了改善這方面不足之處，本研究結
合變動抽樣時距與變動管制界限的技巧發
展另一新的累積計數和管制圖，並建置平均
訊息時間等統計績效指標。據此，可與現有
的累積計數和管制圖進行統計績效比較。本
研究也將應用於超低不合格率的奈米製
程，以評估其可行性及效率。
關鍵詞：累積計數和管制圖、變動抽樣時
距、變動管制界限、奈米製程管理
Abstract
Cumulative count of conforming (CCC)
chart has shown to be particularly useful for
high-quality processes in which the fraction of
nonconforming is very low at parts per million
(ppm) level. Traditional CCC chart is
generated using 100% inspection, but this
approach may lead to a relative high
inspection cost and time, and thus limit the
application of the CCC chart. In order to solve
this problem, a study of one possible approach
to use the CCC chart with the variable
sampling interval and control limits (VSIL)
scheme and the application to nanofabrication
processes is planning in this three-phase
research project. The research project aims at
deriving some performance measures and
evaluating the detection ability of CCC charts
with the VSIL scheme according to these
measures.
Keywords: nanofabrication, cumulative
count of conforming chart, variable
sampling interval, variable control limits

Most of these studies concentrated on
the control charts monitoring for continuous
variables rather than attributes [39]. However,
the scheme of adaptive parameters has been
shown as well to be capable of improving the
performance of the control chart for attributes
[40-43].
This project studies the CCC chart with
the scheme of the variable sampling interval
and control limits (VSIL). Following the idea
of Liu et al. [15], the sample size is equal to
one. The parameters of sampling interval
length and control limits are allowed to vary
between maximum and minimum values as a
function of the last point position. If this point
falls in the warning region, then the control is
tightened by shortening the sampling interval
for the following inspection until the
appearance of the next point, and plotting the
next point on the chart with narrow control
limits. On the other hand, if this point falls in
the safety region, then the control becomes
softer than usual by lengthening the sampling
interval until the appearance of the next point,
and plotting it on the chart with broad control
limits.
三、研究報告應含的內容
Results
(1) The average time to signal (ATS) an
out-of-control for the CCC charts with the
VSIL scheme have been derived based on the
Markov chain approach.
(2) In order to evaluate the statistical
efficiency of the modified CCC chart, it was
compared with the CCC charts with the
traditional sampling scheme. In doing so, an
improvement factor (IF), defined as the ratio
of the out-of-control ATS for the modified
CCC chart and the traditional one, was
employed. Accordingly, it was found that : (a)
when the shift in the defect rate increases, the
value for IF decreases and the modified
chart becomes better as compared with the
traditional one; (b) the larger the difference
between the lengths of alternative sampling
intervals the smaller the IF value, and the
better the performance the modified chart
related to the traditional one; and (c) The
larger the difference between values of
alternative control limits, the smaller the
improvement factor, and the better the
performance the modified chart related to the
traditional one.
(3) The modified CCC chart was further
compared with the CCC charts with the VSI
as well as the variable control limit (VL)
schemes under equal conditions (in-control
ATS). According to their improvement factors
(IF) respectively to the traditional CCC charts,
it is observed that: (a) the modified CCC chart
outperforms the ones with the VSI and VL
schemes for different process shifts. Similar to
the traditional chart, the main advantage of the
VL scheme is its convenient administration
because the sampling interval length is fixed.
However, with the modification on the control
limit it improves the speed of the traditional
chart in detecting a process change; and (b)
the VSI scheme seems outperforms the VL

二、 與會心得
很感謝國科會願意補助本人參與此次國際學術會議。在會中與不同地區學者的交談過程中，可以了解到他們對於學術領
域工作的積極投入及執著，以及來自不同文化的學者對於日常生活所秉持的態度及價值觀，其中有些優點的確值得我們學習。
本次會議進行節奏很快，雖然會議僅有短短三天，但主辦單位安排數量相當龐大的論文發表。因此，交流的時間比較不
足，另外，與過去曾參加過的會議不同，本次會議中並未邀請各領域知名 keynote speakers演講，此亦為美中不足之處。
此趟行程共在東京待了五天四夜，除了參與會議外，也參觀東京當地的一些景點，令人相當懷念。然而，韶光芢苒，為
期三日的國際研討會議終於圓滿結束。這段期間，個人學習到許多平日不易擁有的寶貴經驗與事務。再次感謝國科會在經費
上的協助，讓此趟東京的學習之旅得以滿載而歸。
