摘要 
本計畫針對在合理的時間延遲區間條件下的網路型奇異系統，進行系統可容許性分析與遠端迴授控
制器及濾波器設計問題的探討。以時間延遲問題為主軸，利用包括廣義李雅普諾夫方法，以及發展出新
式有限總和不等式等方法，連結奇異系統與網路型控制系統的問題，在具有特定延遲區間的時變延遲情
況下，發展低保守性的延遲相依型分析條件與生成條件，並以此延伸，將具有資料遺失或有限通道能力
等網路系統常見類型的問題，轉化成為時間延遲問題類型，進行分析和遠端控制單元的設計討論。並將
所有的條件結果，以線性矩陣不等式的形式呈現，以便透過電腦求取可行或最佳的數值解。 
以控制器設計問題來說，因網路型控制系統的應用，討論具有時間延遲狀態的控制器是合理也是必
須的，特別是時間延遲具有區間延遲的型態。因此，提供一個新式的有限總和不等式方法和相關輔助方
案，用來開發低度保守性的系統可容許性分析條件和控制器生成條件。 
以濾波器設計問題來說，因不可靠的網路傳輸或受限的頻寬，導致訊息傳遞延遲或封包遺失現象，
濾波器設計有其必要性。因此，利用伯努力序列概念，模擬資料遺失現象，並導入時間延遲項於濾波器
輸入端，形成對應的強健濾波器設計與分析問題。針對不同的系統設定，得到時延相依條件的成果。

networked singular system with possible lossy measurements and induced delay.  
Time delay is frequently a source of instability and is encountered in various engineering systems [Gu03] such 
as long transmission lines in pneumatic systems, networked control systems, and chemical processes. In general, 
two cases of delay should be considered. One is that the system itself is delayed, which is considered in most 
researches [WuS08]. Another is that the system itself is delay-free, but the delay may be induced by the 
transmission or communication channels. For such cases, systems with interval time-varying delayed 
controllers are natural and expectable. A typical example is a networked control system case [Pen08], whose 
far-end controllers are linked to near-end systems by communication networks. This also motivates our 
interests. On the other hand, even though many delay dependent analysis and synthesis conditions for singular 
systems have been developed, few concern about the problems for a delayed controller design and interval time 
varying delay simultaneously. To the best of our knowledge, for the control of discrete-time singular system 
with interval time-varying delayed controller, it still remains open and challenging.  
In this report, we will introduce a new sum inequality approach to apply to the control problems for discrete 
singular systems with interval time varying delayed controllers. The finite sum inequality approach provides a 
solution to deal with the cross items in the derivation of the Lyapunov functional candidate, and all possible 
useful information is maintained. Therefore, less conservative results may be expected.

Controller Design for Networked Singular Systems 
 
Controller synthesis conditions for different cases will be provided in this chapter. The new finite sum 
inequality approach attached in Chapter 2 will be utilized to prospect the (admissibility) analysis and (controller)

time-varying delayed controller which can be formulated as a far-end controller for networked control systems, 
respectively. The resultant delay dependent conditions are presented under the linear matrix inequality 
framework that can be solved efficiently. Corresponding conditions for singular systems can be easily 
simplified to the ones for normal state-space systems.
