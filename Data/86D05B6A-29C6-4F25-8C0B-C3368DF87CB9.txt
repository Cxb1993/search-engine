二、 緣由與目的 
在電子科技產品持續蓬勃發展下，傳統動/
靜態隨機存取記憶體(DRAM/SRAM)面臨縮小化
及資料存取速度的問題 [1-5]，具高集積密度
(integrated density)，高速存取資料及可長時間保
存資料的新型次世代記憶體為未來的發展趨勢
[6,7]，其中電阻式記憶體(resistive RAM, RRAM)
因結構簡單、低成本、高速度及低功率等特性，
同時在積體電路製程上，可微縮(scalability)及有
高集積度(integrated density)等優點，已被視為下
一世代新型非揮發性記憶體最有希望的技術之
一 [6-9]。目前已有很多薄膜材料可製成電阻式
記憶體，如以(1)TiO2 薄膜 RRAM，有不錯的雙
極特性，(2) TiN/TiOx/HfO2/TiN 結構的 RRAM 有
較佳的轉換速度，而(3) NiO 薄膜 RRAM 有良好
的單極電阻轉換特性。ZnO材料為常見且成本低
廉的氧化物材料，近年來許多研究人員使用 ZnO
薄膜來製做 RRAM，亦有不錯的單極電阻交換特
性。同時 ZnO材料摻雜其它元素(Co、S、Ti、Ta、
Mn、Mg、Ni 或 Cu)等，可改變 ZnO薄膜特性如
能隙 (energy band gap)、薄膜載子分佈、金屬離
子/氧空缺的濃度及薄膜阻值變化等等[10]。另
外，目前文獻 RRAM 常用的金屬電極一般為金
(Au)、銀(Ag)和鉑(Pt)等昂貴金屬，因為此類金屬
不易氧化，容易在金屬氧化物內進行金屬離子的
氧化及還原反應(reduction and oxidation, Redox) 
[10-12]。為了降低成本，低成本的電極開發為一
重要課題。使用 ZnO 薄膜製作 RRAM 有其獨特
的重要應用目的，因 ZnO可做成透明的(導電)薄
膜材料，未來可應用於透明的軟性電子
（ transparent flexible electronics）與軟性顯示
(flexible display)產品上。目前研究 ZnO 材料的
RRAM 與其它材料相比較，相對較少，同時應用
於軟性電子的相關文獻也較缺乏。至於 RRAM之
高 低 電 阻 轉 換 機 制 (resistive switching 
mechanism)，目前有許多文獻提出相關的電阻交
換 機 制 (mechanism of resistive switching) 
[13-19]，如為單極 RRAM，其高低電阻的轉換均
在同一外加電壓極性。另外，高電阻轉換為低電
阻的主要機制為導通燈絲路徑形成(conductive 
filament path)，然而由低電阻轉換成高電阻，其
主要為燈絲路徑被阻斷所至[13-17]。雖然目前有
許多機制提出，但目前尚無一個明朗的物理機制
與理論模型可以完全解釋，同時不同的金屬氧化
物材料有不同的燈絲路徑形成與阻斷機制。 
本計畫為期一年進行沉積特定品質的 ZnO
薄膜以製作性能優越高穩定度的電阻式記憶體
(ZnO RRAM)，共計有四項研究，(1)沉積具不同
氧組成成份之 ZnO 薄膜電阻式記憶體之特性研
究，此項研究經由調高氧氣比例，可增加 ZnO 薄
膜之氧成份，觀察其 RRAM 的特性，(2)退火處
理對 ZnO 薄膜電阻式記憶體之特性研究，(3)摻
雜型 ZnO薄膜之電阻式記憶體之特性研究，本計
畫摻雜 Al 或 Ti 雜質，研究不同摻雜對 ZnO 
RRAM 的特特研究，以及(4)低成本電極材料之
ZnO薄膜電阻式記憶體開發與特性研究，另外對
於沉積於玻璃基板之透明 ZnO RRAM 亦進行研
究。 
在電性分析上進行電壓-電流特性曲線量
測、電流機制探討、元件耐用性測試(endurance 
test)、電阻高低阻態比值分析、高低電阻阻態分
佈分析、Vset/Vreset操作電壓分佈分析、資料保存
能力測試 (retention)等等。在材料分析上進行
XRD、SEM 與 TEM 等微結構的分析，同時進行
傳導燈絲路徑的形成與阻斷機制研究。

Abstract-This work investigates the effects of N2 RTA treatment 
on characteristics of ZnO film as resistive RAM (RRAM). In 
addition, we also study the leakage current behavior and 
conducting mechanism in the low resistance state (LRS) and high 
resistance state (HRS) of ZnO RRAM. From the electrical 
characteristics, the resistance ratio (HRS/LRS) can be larger than 
1011, and the set and reset voltage is lower to around 0.3V and 2.2V, 
respectively. The RTA-treated ZnO RRAM reveals more 
improvement on the endurance up to 103 times. Presumably, the 
RTA treatment will induce more crystalline phases in ZnO film 
and rearranges and/or recover the oxygen vacancies and 
distribution in the film, and it is beneficial for the resistance 
switching. Low power and high resistance ratio (HRS/LRS) of ZnO 
RRAM can be finished for the RTA-treated ZnO RRAM with 
inexpensive Al metal electrode. 
 
Keywords：RTA treatment, Zinc oxide (ZnO), Resistive RAM 
(RRAM), Resistive switching, Switching cycle 
 
I. INTRODUCTION 
 
Resistive RAM has been considered as the potential candidate 
to replace the flash memory owing to its simple 
metal-insulator-metal (MIM) cell structure, high density and 
low power consumption [1-3]. Many research groups have 
investigated the RRAM using various transition metal oxides 
(TMO) [4, 5], or rare earth material [6]. But for the zinc oxide 
material is beginning from recent years [7-9]. Zinc oxide 
material can be used as the transparent conducting oxide (TCO) 
for solar cell application. And zinc oxide can be used for flexible 
electronics. For transparent and flexible electronics application, 
the zinc oxide is a promising material for future application. 
Some literatures have been investigated the characteristics of 
ZnO resistive RAM, but those papers used the expensive 
material as the metal electrode, such as Pt, Ag, and Au [13, 14], 
and the switching cycles is less [10]. Additionally, the switching 
mechanism is also not clear. In this work, we investigate the 
effects of rapid thermal annealing (RTA) on the resistive 
switching of zinc oxide resistive RRAM. The top and bottom 
electrode are used the inexpensive aluminium metal. In addition, 
the samples without RTA treatment are also fabricated for 
comparison. 
 
II. EXPERIMENTAL 
 
The starting materials for Al/ZnO/Al samples fabrication 
were p-type, (100)-oriented silicon wafers with a nominal 
resistivity of 1-10 Ω-cm. After a standard RCA cleaning, a 
200-nm-thick SiO2 layer was growth by furnace tube, after that, 
a 200-nm-thick Al bottom electrode was deposited by dc 
magnetron sputtering. The 60-nm-thick ZnO thin film was 
deposited by reactive magnetron sputtering system using Zn 
target (99.99% purity) in an Ar/O2 gas mixture at 7.6 mTorr. In 
this work, the Ar/O2 ratio of gas mixture is 2 (Ar/O2=24 sccm/12 
sccm). After ZnO film deposition, the samples were introduced 
into a rapid thermal annealing in nitrogen ambient at 400C for 
2 min. After RTA treatment, the top electrode of Al is deposited 
by dc magnetron sputtering system, and patterned by circular 
shadow mask with diameter of 200 m (area is 3.1410-4 cm2). 
The final sample structure and process steps were shown in Fig. 
1. The samples without RTA treatment (no RTA) are also 
prepared for comparison. The electrical characteristic of 
resistive switching of RRAM is measured by a HP4155C of 
semiconductor parameter analyzer. The endurance (switching 
cycle) and retention of ZnO RRAM are also evaluated by the 
HP4155. For retention measurement, we observe the 
characteristics of RRAM with and without constant voltage 
stress (bias) of 0.1V. Transmission electron microscopy (TEM) 
was employed to observe the cross-sectional image and the 
thickness calculation of ZnO film. Scanning electron 
microscopy (SEM) was used to observe the surface morphology 
of deposited ZnO film. Moreover, X-ray diffraction (XRD) 
analysis was used for phase identification of ZnO film with and 
without RTA treatment.
