導電性有機無機混成材料超臨界接枝反應研究 
Study of conductive organic-inorganic hybrid grafting in 
supercritical carbon dioxide 
計畫編號：NSC 97-2214-E-034-010 
執行期限：97 年 8 月 1 日至 98 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：曾梨子   文化大學化工系 
 
一、中文摘要 
 
本研究探討無機材料在超臨界流體中進行導
電高分子的接枝反應成為有機-無機複合材料，第
一年的研究成果，對於氧化矽基材在超臨界二氧化
碳中以聚 3,4-二氧乙烯塞吩(PEDOT)進行接枝反應
之探討，完成二氧化矽基材的分析、接枝反應之超
臨界溫度、壓力與反應時間之定性的探討，並以熱
重分析解析 PEDOT-SiO2產物的接枝比例，結果顯
示隨著超臨界二氧化碳壓力及反應時間的增加，可
得到較好的接枝效果，在本研究探討的溫度條件
下，323K 之反應溫度，相較於 313K 及 333K 具有
較佳的接枝效果。熱重分析的結果顯示，當固定反
應溫度、時間及反應物當量等條件，將超臨界壓力
由 120bar 提高至 200bar 時 PEDOT 的接枝量會由
28.3 %提高至 51.4%。 
 
關鍵詞：超臨界二氧化碳，導電高分子，有機-無
機 
 
Abstract 
 
The Grafting reaction of conducting polymer 
onto the inorganic substrate via supercritical fluid is 
investigated in this study. At the first year of the study, 
we investigate the grafting of 
poly[3,4-ethylendioxidethiophene] (PEDOT) onto 
silicon oxide(SiO2) substrate. The properties of 
several SiO2 substrates have been measured. The 
temperature, pressure and time period conditions of 
grafting reaction in supercritical carbon dioxide have 
been investigated. The thermogravimetric analysis of 
PEDOT-SiO2 sample has been measured to verify the 
percentage of grafting. The grafting percentage will 
increase as pressure and reaction time period. The 
reaction temperature of 323K gives better grafting 
result with respect to 313K and 333K. The 
thermogravimetric anaylsis shows that the grafting 
percentage will increase from 28.3% to 51.4% when 
the supercritical pressure increase from 120 bar to 200 
bar with fixed temperature and reaction time period. 
The organic-inorganic composites has been 
successfully synthesized in supercritical fluid. 
 
Keywords: Supercritical, conducting polymer, 
organic-inorganic 
 
二、緣由與目的 
 
隨著有機-無機混成(organic-inorganic hybrid)
材料研究的興起，憑藉有機材料本身的特性如高透
明性、良好之接著性、交聯性等，以及無機材料許
多優良的特性如高硬度、耐磨性、加工性、尺寸安
定性、低膨脹係數及熱安定性等，由於兩者具有互
補之作用，可充份的被開發與利用，解決各材料應
用領域的瓶頸，因此所開發出來的有機-無機混成
新材料，其材料特性將促使一波材料應用領域的革
新，將可在製程上局部取代邊框達成一次設計成型
(design-in)的新概念產品的目的，例如紫外線硬化
奈米塗料，因加入了無機材料而提高了塑膠材料表
面的耐磨性及硬度 (Wouters et al.,2004)。由於早期
的複合材料容易發生巨觀上的相分離現象，造成材
料的不均勻而發生局部崩毀的致命缺點，混成材料
主要以兩種方法來克服相分離的現象，其一為利用
奈米程度進行混成，其二為對無機材料進行表面改
質，使與有機相能夠相容。除了應用於有機無機混
成，無機材料因改質官能化，會造成的許多特性改
變而可應用到新型材料中，如半導體製程中的
SOG(spin-on-glass) 平坦化所用到的二 氧 化 矽
(Silica, SiO2)，其表面的氫氧基 (hydroxyl group)
在成膜時，會造成薄膜介電系數的提高；如將其表
面的氫氧基改成烷基，則可大為降低介電系數成
為理想的 low-k 材料；所以無機材料的表面改質為
有機-無機混成材料中的一個關鍵步驟，而受到許
多學者專家的研究探討，例如將高分子聚甲基丙烯
酸甲酯(polymethyl methacrylate, PMMA)接枝到奈
米 SiO2無機材料裡，當高分子與無機材料的表
面氫氧基反應後，可使無機材料作有機化的改
質，而每一研究主題，都先針對某些議題作深入的
探討，以尋求一理想的材料 (Hong et al.,2006)。學
者 Kim 曾利用紫外線誘導反應將 PMMA 接枝到奈
米 SiO2無機材料裡 (Kim et al.,2005)。當 PMMA
有效的分散在改質後的 SiO2 材料中，使其具
有良好的填溝能力 (gap filling)性質以及一
些物理性質的改變，而改變的物理現象包括材
料的親疏水性、帶電性、極性、表面化學組成、表
面粗糙度等。改質後的 SiO2 材料有許多應用範
圍，例如光觸媒材料 (Curri et al.,2003)、電子元
件(Wu et al.,2004)、印刷設備(Chen et al.,1998)、生
物技術(Ratner et al.,1993)等。有鑑於此，本計畫也

Experimental 
 
Phenylbutazone (C19H20N2O2) and nabumetone (C15H10O2) were purchased from Sigma-Aldrich 
with the purity greater than 99 %. Carbon dioxide with the purity better than 99.8 % (San Fu, 
Taiwan) was used as the anti-solvent. The schematic diagram of apparatus and procedures in this 
study is present in our previous study [3]. Applying the RESS process, solid phenylbutazone or 
nabumetone was firstly extracted by supercritical CO2. The supercritical solution was then 
depressurized to the atmospheric condition through a nozzle. The micronized phenylbutazone or 
nabumetone particles were formed in a short time period. The morphologies of particles were 
examined using the scanning electron microscope (SEM, JOEL JSM-5600). Particle size 
distributions of the particles were determined using image analysis software ImageJ. The crystal 
structures of particles were detected using the X-ray diffractometer (XRD, Philips X’pert 
diffractometer) where data were collected between 2θ = 5 o to 40 o
 
with a scanning rate of 5 o/min. 
Thermal behavior of the particles was studied using differential scanning calorimetry (DSC, DuPont 
TA 2010) with a heating rate of 5 K/min. 
 
Results and discussion 
 
Fig. 1 shows the comparison between the SEM images of the original and RESS treated 
phenylbutazone. The original phenylbutazone has the long bar shape with unfavorable flowability. 
The RESS-treated phenylbutazone showed irregular morphology with much smaller mean particle 
size. Fig. 2 shows the XRD patterns for phenylbutazone before and after the RESS treatment. 
Compared with the literature data [4], it is observed that phenylbutazone changed from the original 
δ form to β form after the RESS process. This result has also been presented by Moribe et al. [5]. It 
is also demonstrated in Fig. 2 that the XRD intensities of phenylbutazone decreased after the RESS 
process. This result can be beneficial to the increase in dissolution rate.
