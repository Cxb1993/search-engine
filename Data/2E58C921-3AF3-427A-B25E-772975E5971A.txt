中文摘要: 
汽車產業為全球目前極受重視的工業之一，汽車電子尤其獲得相當大的注意，因為
台灣有很完整的半導體電子產業，LED 產業亦發展成型，若搭配台灣很成熟的射出成型
與模具技術，這些所謂的傳統產業，或可在汽車電子的燈具照明部份佔有一席之地，事
實上，不少汽車產業所需之燈具皆由台灣廠商為其製作與代工，然而，其利潤卻相當微
薄，而且，由於大部份廠商均以中小企業產業規模運作，對旺季的大量訂單需求，或淡
季的無訂單狀況，公司都難以順利承接與營運。 
代工廠商為求永續經營，也逐漸研擬轉型，開始重視研發創新，並以申請專利保護
本身智財權，以期逐步擺脫代工之產業型態。為搭配與因應合作廠商需求，本研發團隊
協助合作廠商開發具特殊警戒效果之特色煞車燈，目前該合作廠商已獲部份相關專利，
並規劃以自身商品呈現，故本計畫即為協助合作廠商製作自身商品之雛型品，探討煞車
燈之電子控制電路製作，研製具特殊警戒效果之煞車燈。 
 
關鍵詞：汽車電子、LED 
 
Abstract： 
The automobile industry is a very important industry at present. Especially, automobile 
electronic engineering receive much attention. As there are a complete semiconductor 
industry and preliminary LED industry, they can combine with the traditional mold 
technology to achieve the automobile lighting technology in Taiwan. In fact, many small 
companies in Taiwan help the foreign companies to produce the lighting product now. The 
benefits of those companies are very limited because the core technologies of automobile 
lighting belong to the foreign companies. Those small companies with limited scale survive 
uneasily. 
For long-term business, some of those small companies try to change their original 
business model. They pay attention on research and patent to raise their values. In order to 
help the small cooperating company, we try to study the automobile lighting technology and 
survey the present related patents. We plan to develop and realize the prototype of 
automobile brake lamps with special warning effect. That will help the small cooperating 
company to have its own products. 
 
Keywords：automobile electronic、Light Emitting Diode
