以水熱法合成鈣/鎂複合氫氧基磷灰石粉末及表面鍍層之 
微觀組織解析與機械性質研究 
計畫編號：NSC 99-2218-E-150-045 
執行期限：99 年 8 月 1 日 至 100 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：楊崇煒 助理教授  國立虎尾科技大學材料科學與工程系 
本計畫探討方向含：(一) 以水熱法合成HA、鈣/鎂複合HAMg粉末，並歸納水熱法合成HA製
程最佳條件；(二) 利用水熱法在Ti6Al4V合金與AZ91鎂合金表面析鍍HA、HAMg複合鍍層，
進行微觀組織解析，並比較添加Mg對HA鍍層之影響；(三) 水熱法析鍍HA鍍層結合強度以及
鍍層可靠度評估。探討結果顯示，水熱法合成HA、HAMg複合粉末之結晶度會隨提高反應溫
度而升高之趨勢，水熱合成粉末呈板片狀結晶型態，且在偏中性水溶液下合成之HAMg的結晶
度較佳，本實驗以175ºC水熱處理為合成HA之最佳條件。根據XRD相組成鑑定與XPS對Ca 2p
之鍵結能偏移分析結果，水熱法有助於Mg元素嵌入HA結構並促進鈣鎂磷灰石相形成，FT-IR
分析OH−基、PO43−基吸收峰強度降低結果顯示，Mg經水熱合成嵌入導致含鈣鎂磷灰石相之
HAMg結晶性降低。水熱法成功地在AZ91與Ti6Al4V表面析鍍HAMg複合鍍層，在水熱析鍍過
程中，未經鎂鋁水滑石(MAH)鈍化處理之AZ91表面會先形成一層Mg(OH)2，HAMg鍍層則生成
於Mg(OH)2表面，且AZ91基材表面未經鎂鋁水滑石鈍化處理之HAMg鍍層析鍍效果較佳。鍍層
結合強度測試結果發現析鍍於Ti6Al4V基材之HAMg鍍層強度較析鍍於AZ91之鍍層強度高，韋
伯可靠度函數解析結果顯示，水熱法析鍍HAMg鍍層之破壞型態為包含鍍層/基材界面附著強度
及鍍層本身內聚強度之綜合破壞模式，皆屬於m > 3 之損耗型破壞模式，具工程上之可靠度。 
關鍵詞：氫氧基磷灰石、水熱法、表面鍍層、鎂合金、結合強度、韋伯統計解析、破壞機構 
Three subjects are included in this project, namely (1) to synthesis HA, HAMg composite powders 
through the hydrothermal method and further conclude the optimal synthetic condition, (2) to 
hydrothermally-precipitate HA, HAMg composite coatings on commercial AZ91 magnesium alloy 
and Ti6Al4V titanium alloy. The present study will evaluate the effect of Mg element addition on 
the variation of microstructural features, phase composition and chemical bonding properties of HA 
coating. The third subject is to study the shear adhesive strength and reliability of hydrothermally 
precipitate HA, HAMg composite coatings. Experimental results show that the crystallinity of HA 
and HAMg composite powders increased with increasing hydrothermal treating temperatures. The 
hydrothermally-synthesized powders showed a plate-like crystalline feature and the powders, which 
synthesized within a neutral solution, displayed a better crystallinity than that of within basic 
solutions. The optimal hydrothermal temperature to synthesize HA powders without impurity 
phases was controlled at 175ºC. The XRD and high resolution Ca 2p XPS analysis results showed 
that Mg2+-ions can be effectively substituted into HA crystal structure to promote the formation of 
calcium magnesium phosphate phase (HAMg) through the hydrothermal treatment. The decreasing 
peak intensity of OH−, PO43− groups by FT-IR analysis represents that the decrease of HAMg 
crystallinity is resulted from the substitution of Mg2+ into HA structure. The surface HAMg coating 
can be successfully hydrothermally-precipitated on AZ91 and Ti6Al4V alloys. During hydrothermal 
precipitation coating process, a surface Mg(OH)2 layer was formed on AZ91 substrate without 
Mg-Al hydrotlacite (MAH) pre-treatment, and HAMg coatings were then formed on the Mg(OH)2 
layer. HAMg coatings hydrothermally-deposited on Ti6Al4V showed a higher shear adhesive 
strength than that of deposited on AZ91 substrate. From the analysis of Weibull distribution 
function, the hydrothermal HAMg coatings were reliable materials with a wear-out failure model of 
m > 3 failure behavior. They showed a better reliability for further biological applications. 
Keywords: hydroxyapatite, hydrothermal synthetics, surface coating, magnesium alloy, adhesive 
strength, Weibull statistical analysis, failure mechanism

2.2. 金屬基材表面 HA 鍍層之水熱合成析鍍 
本研究採用商用Ti6Al4V (ASTM F136 
ELI)鈦合金與AZ91鎂合金進行水熱法析鍍
HA表面鍍層之基材，AZ91擠型材經分光分
析其合金成分如表1所列。先將Ti6Al4V與
AZ91基材裁切成適當大小之試片(30 × 10 × 
3 mm3，如圖2(a)所示)，Ti6Al4V基材表面先
進行噴砂處理，並控制其表面粗糙度(Ra)約
為2.0 ± 0.3 μm，經丙酮超音波震盪洗淨後，
即浸泡於體積莫耳濃度5M之NaOH水溶液
中，經150ºC，持溫2小時之水熱浴表面處理，
目的乃藉此活化Ti基材表面而促進HA表面
鍍層之析鍍。 
由於鎂合金表面活性較大，因此AZ91鎂
合金基材則先進行生成鎂鋁水滑石(Mg-Al 
hydrotlacite, MAH)表面鈍化處理[22, 23]。步
驟為在廣口瓶內盛500 ml去離子水，持續通
入100 sccm流量之二氧化碳氣體用以製備碳
酸水溶液，將AZ91基材置於此碳酸水溶液，
以50ºC持溫浸泡2小時後取出，再浸泡於1.5 
M，pH值約11.5之NaOH水溶液中持溫2小
時，即可在AZ91基材表面生成鎂鋁水滑石，
隨後便將此經表面處理之試片置於水熱釜，
進行HAMg複合鍍層之析鍍，另準備以#2000
砂紙研磨而不經水滑石表面處理之AZ91基
材作為對照組。水熱法進行HA表面鍍層之析
鍍條件為在150ºC反應溫度下持溫20小時，圖
1為水熱法析鍍HA鍍層裝置示意圖，HA鍍層
析鍍完成後，壓力釜亦緩慢爐冷至室溫。 
2.3. 相組成與微觀組織解析 
實驗以X-ray繞射分析HA與HAMg複合
粉末與鍍層之相組成，掃描範圍 2θ = 
20º~60º，掃描速率 1º/min，並以結晶度指數
(Index of crystallinity，IOC = Ic/Io × 100%)計
算粉末結晶度，Ic 與 Io 表示水熱合成 HA 與
標準 HA 樣品之主繞射峰積分強度。另取商
用高純度之 HA 粉末(99.99 wt.%，粒徑範圍
15~40 μm)作為水熱合成之 HA 粉末結晶度、
相組成分析之對照組。利用 SEM 觀察 HA 與
HAMg 複合粉末與鍍層表面微觀組織形貌；以
霍式轉換反射式紅外線光譜儀(FT-IR)檢測
HA 與 HAMg 的特徵官能基吸收光譜：OH−基
(hydroxyl groups) 與 PO43− 基 (phosphate 
groups)，採用 1 cm−1 的光譜解析度於 450 ~ 
4000 cm−1 的波數範圍進行掃描，以分析其晶
體結構；以化學分析光電子光譜儀(XPS)分析
HAMg 之鍵結能(binding energy)偏移：以 Mg
的特徵 X-ray (Mg Kα)為光電子激發源，電壓
15 kV，電流 20 mA，X-ray 入射角 45º，系統
真空度低於 1 × 10−10 torr，並以 C 1s 之束縛
能(284.6 eV)作為校正光譜。

此外，以較偏中性 pH 值水溶液所合成之

歸納以上之 XRD 分析結果發現，當水熱
合成製程添加 Mg 元素藉以形成 HAMg 複合

以Mg部分取代Ca的形式促進鈣鎂磷灰石相
的生成之外，HA 結構中存在之 Mg 元素亦可
能會使結晶結構不完整而造成結晶度降低現

到 Mg 嵌入之影響程度，即利用 FT-IR 分析
HA 特徵官能基 OH−基與 PO43−基之吸收光
譜，並以 XPS 分析 Ca 2p 軌域鍵結能(binding 
energy, BE)之偏移(shift)，藉此輔助評估。 
不同條件之水熱合成 HA、HAMg 複合粉
末 FT-IR 光譜分析如圖 9 所示，經過鑑定，
波數位於 630 cm−1 與 3572 cm−1 之吸收峰屬
於 OH−基之 stretching band [12]，波數位於
602, 963 cm−1 與 1030~1090 cm−1 之吸收峰分
別屬於 PO43−基(phosphate groups)之 P-O 鍵的
bending band 與 O-P-O 鍵的 asymmetric 
stretching band [1, 12, 27]，而波數介於
1400~3300 cm−1 之間則屬於其他磷酸鈣鹽類
或碳酸根(carbonate groups, CO32−)光譜區[27, 
28]。比較圖 9 之 FT-IR 光譜分析結果，發現
除了在 135ºC 水熱合成條件之 HA、HAMg 粉
末 OH−基與 PO43−基吸收峰強度較低之外，以
150ºC、175ºC 水熱合成 HA、HAMg 粉末皆有
顯著的 OH−基與 PO43−基吸收峰光譜，OH−
基吸收峰的出現是 HA 結晶性佳的指標，而
PO43−吸收峰尖銳化也代表 HA 的結晶度提高
[27]，顯示愈高水熱處理溫度所獲得之 HA 晶

OH−吸收峰，則屬於未反應完全殘留之
Mg(OH)2 結構的 OH−吸收峰。比較 HA 及
HAMg 粉末之光譜發現，HAMg 粉末之 OH−
基、PO43−基吸收峰強度略低，亦證明含鈣鎂
磷灰石相之 HAMg 複合粉末結晶性會略低。 
圖 10 為 HA 與 HAMg 粉末之 Ca 2p 軌域
高解析 XPS 光譜，而隨著水熱法 HA 結晶度
的提高，Ca 2p 軌域之峰值光譜會逐漸往高束

相之外，卻也導致整體結晶性的降低現象。

表面鎂鋁水滑石、Mg(OH)2 處理之表面鈍化
層在模擬體液之反應性、離子釋出及鈍化層
之保護效果亦需進一步了解，而對於 HAMg
鍍層本身在模擬體液之穩定性，以及鍍層經
長時間生物反應試驗後之 HAMg 鍍層強度與
可靠度，也是重要的探討指標，因此針對本
研究所開發之水熱析鍍 HAMg 鍍層的生物反
應、相容性探討有其必要性，且將在未來的
研究中作更進一步評估報告。 
3.4. 水熱 HA 鍍層之破壞面觀察 
圖 16 為立體顯微鏡觀察水熱法析鍍
HA、HAMg 鍍層之拉伸測試破斷面組織特
徵。AZ91 基材表面析鍍 HA 鍍層之破斷面如
圖 16(a)、16(b)所示，呈現島狀散佈之界面破
壞型態，由於 AZ91 表面在水熱析鍍 HA 過
程中，會同時生成 Mg(OH)2 表面層，因此殘
留 於 AZ91 基 材 端 之 鍍 層 可 能 為
HA/Mg(OH)2 混合層。圖 16(c)、16(d)所示為
AZ91基材表面析鍍HAMg鍍層之破斷面形貌
觀察，界面破壞之比例降低，顯示破壞路徑
除了部分發生於鍍層/基材界面之外，由於鍍
層與 AZ91 基材間結合力的提高，因此裂紋
傳播路徑會轉而穿過鍍層內部，並且呈現
Mg(OH)2 及 HAMg 混合型之鍍層破壞特徵，
如圖 16(d)所示。圖 16(e)、 16(f)所示為
Ti6Al4V 基材表面析鍍 HAMg 鍍層之破斷面
組織觀察，由於 Ti6Al4V 基材先以噴砂處理
使表面粗糙度提高後，再進行 HAMg 鍍層之
析鍍，粗糙度提高有助於強化 HAMg 鍍層
/Ti6Al4V 界面之機械咬合力，因此使鍍層具
有較高的結合強度，而破斷面型態亦顯示界
面黏著強度與鍍層內聚強度之混合型破壞。 
四、 結論 
  根據上述之實驗結果，本研究可歸納以下
幾點結論： 
1. 水熱法合成HA、HAMg複合粉末之結晶度
與相純度隨反應溫度上升而呈現升高之趨
勢，且在偏中性水熱合成環境所得之HAMg
複合粉末結晶性較佳。 
2. 含飽和水蒸氣之高壓水熱法製程可促進
Mg元素嵌入HA結構並獲得複合鈣鎂磷灰
石相(calcium magnesium phosphate)，但因
HA結構受到破壞而使結晶度略為降低。 
3. 水熱法可成功合成並析鍍一連續性HAMg
鍍層於AZ91鎂合金及Ti6Al4V合金基材。 
4. 未經鎂鋁水滑石鈍化處理之AZ91會形成
Mg(OH)2，水熱HAMg析鍍層則被覆於此
Mg(OH)2表面層；AZ91表面未經鎂鋁水滑
石鈍化處理之HAMg鍍層析鍍效果較佳。

3. Results and discussion 
 
3.1 Characteristics of AgInSbTe films 
Fig. 1(a) illustrates the film thickness tends to 
increase with increasing sputtering power. This 
phenomenon derives from the better sputtering yield 
as an increase of the ion energy in the plasma [6, 7]. 
But the average film thickness is slightly increased 
from about 250 nm to about 300 nm with increasing 
sputtering pressure. From Fig. 1(b), the film 
thickness significantly increases and shows a linear 
relationship between the film thickness and the 
sputtering durations. The estimated deposition rate at 
a specific sputtering power of 200 W is about 4.34 
nm/s based on the experimental results of Fig. 1(b). 
As a result from Fig. 1, it should be noted that the 
sputtering duration is the most effective parameter to

It is reasonable to suggest that the electron colliding 
between free surface and the reflecting surface (the 
interface between the film and the substrate) will 
reduce electrical conductivity [9, 12]. Additional 
scattering oriented in the grain boundary can be 
resulted from the dispersion of electrons [12]. A 
possibility is considered further to describe the 
overall sheet resistivity as a summary of ρMS + ρFS 
−ρ0, where ρMS is arisen from the quantum size 
effect, ρFS is arisen from the grain boundary effect 
and ρ0 is the bulk resistivity. Because ρMS and ρFS 
tend to decrease with increasing thickness, the size 
effect consequently gives an inverse relationship 
between sheet resistivity and film thickness [12]. 
As shown in Fig. 5(b), it is noted that the size 
effect for quasi-crystallized specimens is contrary to 
crystalline films. The increase of sheet resistivity ρs 
with increasing film thickness means that the size 
effect is not valid in the quasi-crystallized AIST 
films. Since the quasi-crystallized film possesses a 
high resistivity compared to the crystalline film, the 
quasi-crystallized film can be recognized as an 
insulator. In Table 2, the carrier density and the 
carrier mobility of quasi-crystallized films are 
significantly smaller than that of crystalline films. 
The combination of the above-mentioned size effect 
with the phenomenon of carrier reduction can result 
in a typically proportional relationship between the 
sheet resistivity and film thickness as shown in Fig. 
5(b). The capacitance effect for thinner 
quasi-crystallized AIST films is minor and the 
surface scattering plays an important role in the 
sheet resistivity, whereas the high capacitance is 
generated from the increasing of film thickness.

Since the transformed volume fraction of AIST 
films is correlated to the change in isothermal sheet 
resistance [5], a quantitative description of the 
isothermal crystallization kinetics is made using the 
Johnson-Mehl-Avrami formalism of Eq. (2). 
Isothermal sheet resistance measurements are 
applied at annealing temperatures of 423, 433 and 
443 K to obtain JMA curves and the regressive plots 
for Avrami exponents at these temperatures are 
shown in Fig. 6(a). The slope of each straight line is 
subjected to the Avrami exponents of each 
isothermal annealing temperature. The range of n 
value is from 1.1-1.4. It indicated that the crystal can 
grow freely and the sheet resistance will decrease 
dramatically after the occurrence of impingement 
effects. Another regressive line for calculating 
activation energy using Eq. (3) is subsequently 
plotted with different annealing temperatures as 
shown in Fig. 6(b). The slope of Q/R (R is the gas 
constant) gives an activation energy of 0.815 eV 
when adopting average n = 1.25. 
 
4. Conclusions 
The sputtering duration is an effective parameter 
to improve the extent of atomic rearrangements. The 
estimated nano-crystal size of quasi-crystallized 
films grows to 0.9 nm when sputtering after 600 s 
and the atomic rearrangements within a short range 
can occur. The sheet resistivity of quasi-crystallized 
AgInSbTe films is around four orders higher than 
that of the crystalline films. The thickness 
dependence on the sheet resistivity of crystalline 
films follows the classic size effect but that of 
quasi-crystallized films violates. The Avrami 
exponent (n) is about 1.1-1.4 and this value is 
smaller than 2.0-3.0 for typical transformation in 
thin films. It is suggested that impingement effects 
are the cause of the smaller Avrami exponent in 
isothermal sheet resistance measurement.
