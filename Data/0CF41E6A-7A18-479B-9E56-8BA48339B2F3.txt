一、 前言 
 
鐵基形狀記憶合金【1-5】雖只具有單向記憶效應，但具強度高、易加工、
價格低廉、使用方便(不需在低溫保存)等優點，在適當的成分設計及熱機處理
下，可以得到相當良好之形狀記憶效應，在工業上具有高度的應用潛能與市場競
爭力，故廣受國內外學術研究機構與工業界的重視。 
鐵基記憶合金中，又以 Fe-Mn-Si 基合金最受矚目，雖然其麻田散體變態並
非熱彈性型，但經由應力誘發麻田散體變態的微細麻田散平板，在變態過程中亦
具有可逆性，故可呈現形狀記憶之特性。 
在鐵基記憶合金的應用中，最具發展潛力的是製成套筒元件—即管接頭，用
於水電管路、輸油管、瓦斯管線等之接合。目前大陸方面已經將鐵基形狀記憶合
金製造的管接頭實際應用在油田工程管線【6】，如勝利油田的工程管線、華北油
田與中原油田的高壓注水管線，天津無機化學工廠與大慶煉油廠輸送酸、鹼介質
管線等之接合。 
Fe-Mn-Si 基形狀記憶合金之各種性質均有學者研究及探討，而備受關注的
仍在於形狀記憶性能之提升。而在提升形狀記憶性能之各種方法中，舉凡添加合
金元素以降低疊差能、析出硬化或晶粒細化以強化 γ 母相、避免產生 α’ 麻田散
鐵和熱機訓練等亦都有學者進行研究；惟在這些文獻中，絕大多數均探討單一之
強化機構，很少研究合成兩項強化機構之效應。 
本研究之目的乃針對 Fe-25Mn-6Si 及 Fe-25Mn-6Si-5Cr合金，施予適當之預
應變及隨後之退火處理，以結合細晶強化與有序排列之析出強化的雙重效應，有
效提升 Fe-Mn-Si 基記憶合金之形狀記憶性能，以提高該合金在工程應用上之實
用價值。

effective catalysts and combining the techniques of equal channel angular pressing (ECAP) and high energy ball milling 
(HEBM). Experimental results show that, after ball milling with no additives for 20 hours, the hydrogen capacities of 
Mg-Zn-Zr (ZK60) HSAs are still bad, only about 4.25wt%. By adding 5wt% carbon, the hydrogen capacities of ZK60 
HSAs could reach 6.12wt%. Besides, by adding 5wt% carbon and 0.5 wt% vanadium, the ZK60-5C0.5V HSAs exhibit 
excellent hydrogen capacity of 7.06wt%. After 200 cyclic hydrogen absorption/desorption, the ZK60-5C0.5V HSAs 
still maintain the hydrogen capacity of 4.41wt%.  
Keywords: Mg-Zn-Zr hydrogen storage alloys, equal channel angular pressing, ball milling.  
Introduction  
Hydrogen-storage alloys have become promising candidates as a hydrogen-storage medium for fuel cell vehicles, 
because of their high hydrogen capacities and safety characteristics. The severe plastic deformation of Mg-Zn-Zr HSAs 
was expected to introduce a high density of crystal defects and obtain a super fine grain size. These features would 
increase the hydrogen capacity of Mg-Zn-Zr HSAs to be higher than 5 wt% [1]. In this study, the techniques of equal 
channel angular pressing (ECAP) and high energy ball milling (HEBM) are applied to the ZK60 alloys to produce small 
particles and reduce their grain sizes. Additionally, some catalysts of the activated carbon and V metal powders are 
added to increase the reaction rate of hydrogen absorption/desorption.  
Experimental procedures  
The ZK60 alloy was melted and cast by vacuum furnace. Its alloy powders were prepared by using ECAP and HEBM. 
During the process of HEBM, 5 wt% activatedcarbon and 0~2 wt% vanadium were added. The material analyses were 
carried out by using ICP-AES, SEM and XRD. The hydrogenstorage characteristics, including the absorption/desorption 
kinetics and pressure–concentration temperatures (PCT), were measured by using a volumetric Sieverts apparatus.
