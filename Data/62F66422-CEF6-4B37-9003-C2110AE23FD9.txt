2、研究目的 
本計畫之目標在應用光纖光柵(FBG) 感測器於溫度、應變量測等之自動化量測
系統之雛型建構。尤其應用在交通領域之瀝青鋪面方面等。光纖具有徑細質輕、
傳輸頻帶寬、不受電磁干擾、可撓性高、耐高溫及輻射、抗蝕性等優點。本計
畫利用 FBG 感測器應用模擬鋪面於溫度、應變 (載重)量測，埋置光纖光柵感測
器於模擬之瀝青鋪面道路中，利用 LabVIEW 軟體與 GPIB – USB 連接線，連結
光譜分析儀 (OSA) 或光功率計 (Optical Power Meter)等進行溫度與載重之量
測，成果顯示以此方法進行鋪面狀態、力學行為等量測是可行的。 
3、文獻探討 
  光纖技術在最近幾十年廣泛地被使用了為基礎設施監視,主要為結構溫度
和應變監視但最近, 他們被使用查出在混凝土結構裡腐蝕或結構變形[1]。為例
子,光纖感測器被埋置了在幾個修建過的基礎建設結構裡譬如橋梁,水壩和大廈, 
提供可貴的資料在靜態和動態應變 , 溫度變異、風或水壓和結構衛生狀況
[2,3]。混凝土材料或結構管理和安全通常要求週期性監視和維護。結構的行為
的一次準確和長期監視越來越重要因為新大廈和恢復技術被使用並且現有的建
築需要生存未損壞對附近的建築。光纖感測器好處包括光纖感測器由於其徑細
質輕、傳輸頻帶寬、不受電磁干擾、可撓性高、耐高溫及抗蝕性,且易於埋入結
構體、作分佈式多工網路監測。因而,光纖感測器是適當是理想的感測器為潛在
的智慧的結構和材料的應用。

written in polarization maintaining photonic crystal fiber 
Jaw-Luen Tang1, Chien-Hsing Chen1, Jian-Neng Wang2, and Ping-Chang Jui3 
1Department of Physics, National Chung Cheng University, 168 University Road, Chia-Yi 621, Taiwan 
2Department of Construction Engineering, National Yunlin University of Science and Technology, Yun-Lin 640, Taiwan

1Email: phyjlt@.ccu.edu.tw; 2Email: wangjn@yuntech.edu.tw 
Abstract: We report on fabrication and characterization of a long period grating in polarization 
maintaining photonic crystal fiber. The sensing properties of the grating have been investigated and it was 
shown to possess insensitivity to temperature, a refractive index sensitivity of 15.2 nm/RI, and a bending 
sensitivity of 24.1 nm.cm.  
1. Introduction 
Long-period gratings (LPGs), which couple the fundamental core mode and forward-propagating cladding modes at the 
resonance wavelength in an optical fiber, have widely been used in various telecommunications and sensing applications. 
LPG-based devices offere several advantages, such as low back reflection and insertion loss, polarization independence, and 
ease of fabrication. Previous research mainly concentrated on LPGs inscribed in conventional single mode fibers. Recently, 
there have been considerable research efforts [1-3] on formation of LPGs in photonic crystal fibers (PCFs) that consist of a 
pure silica core surrounded with a regular pattern of air-holes running along the length of the fiber axis. PCFs and PCF-based 
optoelectronic components have attracted great attention due to their unusual optical properties and potential applications in 
networks and sensors. In this paper, we report the results of our recent investigation on LPGs based on a polarization 
maintaining PCF fiber (PM-PCFs), which possess distinct optical responses to environmental perturbations such as 
temperature, strain, refractive index, and bending. 
2. Experiments and results 
The PM-PCFs used in this work (Blaze Photonics, PM-1550-01) have a hole pitch (spacing between adjacent holes) of 4.4μm, 
a large hole diameter of 4.5 μm and a small hole diameter of 2.2 μm. The diameter of the hole region is 40 μm and the 
outside diameter of the fiber is 125 μm. Shown in Fig. 1 is a scanning electron micrograph (SEM) of the cleaved face of a 
PCF. A point-to-point technique was utilized to fabricate the LPGs in PM-PCFs by using a CO2 laser system, which has been 
reported elsewhere as a simple and cost-effective method of writing LPGs in conventional single mode fibers [4]. After the
