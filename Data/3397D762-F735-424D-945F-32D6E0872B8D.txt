2源，進行五氯酚與戴奧辛分解菌之篩選、分離與馴養以及生長特性之評估與強化。
三、 材料與方法
本研究主要針對已確定受五氯酚與戴奧辛污染之土壤，採集土壤及底泥樣品，首先分析
採集之土壤樣品已確認該樣品確實為受五氯酚與戴奧辛污染之土壤，再以確認污染之土
壤為微生物篩選之種源，進行以五氯酚與戴奧辛為碳源之污染物分解菌篩選，其後將所
篩出之分解菌以分子生物學方法進行菌種鑑定，並以單一碳源與輔助碳源交互應用，分
析微生物之最佳生長條件，並求得微生物對五氯酚與戴奧辛之分解濃度範圍，詳細研究
方法與材料如下所述：
1.採樣地點選擇
本採樣點依環保署的土壤及地下水污染整治網，相關公告污染整治及控制場址公告
資料為選擇依據，目前在五氯酚及戴奧辛的相關污染場址中，以中石化學工業開發股份
有限公司安順廠為較為嚴重及污染明確之污染場址，因此選定此地點之下游為本研究之
採樣點。樣品的採集及保存方法乃依據環保署公告之環境樣品採集及保存作業指引
(NIEA-PA102）所述進行，採集之樣品依採樣地點不同將樣品分為台南 1、台南 2、台南
3、台南 4、台南 5、台南 6等六個場址。
2.樣品中污染物確認
為了確認採集之樣品為確切受污染之土壤，本研究乃對樣品進行污五氯酚與戴奧辛的
污染濃度分析。在五氯酚的分析方面，首先利用五氯酚標準品配置出 100ppm、50ppm、
25ppm、5ppm、1ppm 的標準溶液繪製檢量線，同時將樣品進行萃取，其萃取方法是參
考環檢所公告之索氏萃取法進行 (NIEA M165.00C)，而分析方法則以液相層析儀
(HPLC)，以環檢所公告標準分析方法中的五氯酚分析方法分析之，並依實際情況調整分
析的相關參數，藉此達到最佳的分析結果，實際進行分析的相關參數與儀器請見表一。

六、 計畫成果自評
透過本次研究的進行，已從受污染的場址中篩選出四株穩定生長之菌株，且該四株
菌皆為革蘭氏陰性桿菌，菌種鑑定結果顯示，所篩出之菌種具備分解氯酚類化合物之能
力，並且可以毒化物戴奧辛為生長之唯一碳源；未來擬針對該四株菌，進行生長特性、
污染物降解特性與核酸與蛋白質層次之生理與生化特性之瞭解，探討之項目如下：
1. 針對菌株進行單一碳源與雙碳源的生長測試，利用此測試瞭解菌株在戴奧辛或五氯
酚為單一碳源，及加入輔助碳源時的生長情況。藉此獲得菌株的生長曲線與比生長
率。
2. 利用不同的溫度、pH或其他不同的環境培養菌株並觀察其生長情形，瞭解菌株的生
理特性。
3. 以污染物培養菌株並偵測污染物濃度變化，瞭解菌株對污染物的降解速率。
4. 利用分子生物學方法，分析分解菌在與毒化物共存之逆境中，探討其核酸與蛋白質
層次之生理與生化特性差異與微生物強化之方法。

ABSTRACT
Anti-depressant bioactive compound from Hyperricum perforatum (St.John’s wort)
was identified to hyperforin and hypericin and Pseudohypericin. To enhance the dry and
extraction efficiency would be improved the acceptance and medical value for the
implement of this plant in Taiwan. An indigenous plant, Hyperricum perforatum, in
Taiwan contains constituents of anti-depression was investigated in this study. Using
different drying and extraction processes, the extraction yield of bioactive compounds
was assessed. The results of equilibrium moisture content by air drying indicated that air
with 25℃ and 70℃ make of 20% and 10.2% was obtained, respectively. When the dried
Hyperricum perforatum was further process with Soxhlet extraction and ultrasonic
extraction, the yield of bioactive compound by ultrasonic extraction is higher than
Soxhlet extraction employed. On further inspection of the different parts of plant, the
concentration of anti-depression compound in the leaves (33.4 mg/g) is higher than in
the Rhizome (24.4 mg/g). The highest concentration of hyperforin is 39.2mg/g by
ultrasonic extraction combined with 50℃ hot air drying. This work provides an optimal
process for extraction of bioactive compound in Hyperricum perforatum. It is very useful
to the food and medicine manufacture, and makes patients improve universality and
convenience of receiving treatment.
Keywords: Hypericum perforatum, Hyperforin, Hypericin, Extraction process.
Plant Material Extraction
A 10g portion of ground Hyperricum perforatum was accurately weighted and extracted
with 200 ml of methanol for 24 hrs in a water bath at 75℃.Finally, the resulted extract
was concentrated to 1 ml in a rotary evaporator.
Analytical Method
HPLC analysis of the extract was performed using a Hitachi system (HITACHI L-7420,
JAPAN). Seperation were performed on a Lichrrosper 100 RP-18 column (4.0 mm ×
150 mm; 5 μm particle size ). The mobile phase consisted of 10 mM ammonium
acetate buffer adjusted to pH 2.5 with glacial acetic acid and a 0.3:90:9.7 mixtures of
acetonitrile and pure water. The flow rate was 1.0 ml/min, and the injection volume was
10 μl. Each run was followed by an equilibration period of 30 min. Mass spectra were
obtained using an API3200 LC/MS (Applied Biosystems Inc., ) Standard solution were
stored in the dark at 4℃.
RESULTS AND DISCUSSION
1.Effect of temperature on Hot air drying
In order to optimize the drying condition for Hyperricum perforatum extraction,
different temperatures were employed to evaluate the rate of water evaporation. The rate
of water evaporation, as shown in Fig. 1, increases as the temperature is raised from 30
to 70℃. Under hot air treatment with 70℃for 10 hrs, the water content of dried plant
was reached to 10.2%. Therefore, pre-treatment of hot air drying is actually improved
the time of water evaporation and water content of the plant. No doubt, the air-drying
efficiency was corresponding to the drying temperature with a propositional relationship.
The hot the air, the short the drying time schedule. It means the higher the heating
temperature can eliminate the heating cost by saving the heating time length. However,
in detection results of this study, the higher the drying temperature, the lower the
bio-active compounds remaining in the plant. It means the researcher and the
manufacturer have to choose the optimal way in reducing the cost and remaining the
compounds in the plant, and of course this issue was also intentioned in this dissicusion.

Conclusion
1. The heat drying temperature in 70℃ for 10 hours achieved the optimal drying
efficiency to the equilibrium moisture of 10.2%. Additional, the lower drying
temperature also reached an equilibrium moisture of 20% in the temperature of 25℃
for 22 hours.
2. In different portion of Hyperricum perforatum, the optimal concentration in the root
are 24.4 mg/g and 10.2 μg/g for hyperforin and hypericin, respectively. In the leaf,
there have 33.4 mg/g of hyperforin and 19.8μg/g of hypericin.
3. The optimal processes for preserving the bio-active component in the plant of
Hyperricum perforatum are 20℃air-drying process sequence to the soxhlet extraction
processes.
4. By accompany with different treatment processes, the couple of the heat-air drying
and ultrasonic extraction, the couple of low temperature air drying and soxhlet
extraction are the feasible integration respectively.
5. The 39.4 mg/g of hyperforin by low temperature 25 ℃ air drying and soxhlet
extraction, and 41.2μg/g of hypericin by low temperature 15 ℃ air drying and
soxhlet extraction are the best treatment results in this study.
