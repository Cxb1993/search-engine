Abstract 
 
In manufacturing system, a supply chain 
is a network of facilities that performs the 
functions of procurement of material, 
transformation of material to intermediate and 
finished products, and distribution of finished 
products to customers.  A better project 
management considers the complete scope of 
supply chain management, from supplier of 
raw material, through factories and warehouse, 
to demand in a store for a finished product.  
The supply chain concepts can perform a 
more applicable system for managing the 
uncertainty that plagues the performance of 
suppliers, the reliability of manufacturing and 
transportation processes, and the changing 
desires of customers.  One can always
