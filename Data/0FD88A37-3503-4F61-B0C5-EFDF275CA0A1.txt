中文摘要 
本 研 究 報 告 針 對 OFDMA(Orthogonal Frequency 
Division Multiple Access, OFDMA)無線通訊系統，探討於通
道具有遠近效應問題時，因不同的使用者在上傳訊號因時
間延遲所造成的多用戶存取干擾(Multi User Interference, 
MUI)，本報告探討部分平行干擾消除 (Partial Parallel 
Interference Cancellation, PPIC)之效能，在不同的NFR下，
我們找出其適當之部分消除權重 (Partial Cancellation 
Weight)，由模擬結果可知，我們提出之適應性消除權重
(Adaptive Weight PPIC, AWPPIC)確實能改善系統效能並提
升其系統用戶容量(User Capacity)。 
關鍵字：OFDMA，多用戶存取干擾，遠近效應，平行干
擾消除。 
 
Abstract 
In this report, we investigate the multiuser performance on the 
orthogonal frequency division multiple access (OFDMA) 
mobile communication systems is investigated. In multi-user 
systems, the interference degraded the system performance, 
especially in near-far effect environments. The parallel 
interference cancellation (PIC) scheme has been proposed to 
cancel the multi-user interference (MUI) for both Block 
Subcarrier Assignment (BCA) and Block & Interleaved 
Subcarrier Assignment (BICA) in the subcarriers allocation for 
users. Moreover, we propose an adaptive weight PPIC 
(AWPPIC) scheme to effectively cancel the MUIs with the 
proposed adequate partial cancellation weights (PCW) for near 
users and far users, respectively. Simulation results show that 
the proposed AWPPIC can improve the system performance 
and system capacity in highly near-far effect environments for 
both BCA and BICA OFDMA systems. 
Key words: OFDMA, MUIs, Near-far effect, parallel 
interference cancellation, PPIC, AWPPIC.  
一、前言 
正 交 分 頻 多 工 (orthogonal frequency division 
multiplexing, OFDM)可提供較高資料傳輸，已廣泛應用於行
動通訊系統中[1-3]。第三代無線行動通訊系統，採用了多
載 波 調 變 (Multi-Carrier Modulation, MCM)[4-6] 中 的
OFDM[7-8]技術。此技術在寬頻無線通道中，可以抵抗多重
路徑干擾，而為了提升系統容量，OFDM 的技術被結合應
用於 FDMA(Frequency Division Multiple Access)[7]技術，稱
為 正 交 分 頻 多 工 存 取 (Orthogonal Frequency Division 
Multiple Access, OFDMA)[9-11]。OFDMA 技術被提出，並
且應用在未來 4G 實體層的基本調變架構，其可以擁有高速
度的傳輸速率與對抗多路經衰減通道和可調整每個使用者
的頻寬特性，讓其成為無線傳輸中最重要的技術。 
在 OFDMA 系統下，不同使用者同時間利用分配到的
子通道上傳訊號，所以可以很容易的從頻域上分離出每個
使用者的訊號，但是不同的使用者在上傳訊號的途中必定
會產生不同的時間延遲(Time Delay)以及頻率偏移(Carrier 
Offset)，而時間延遲及頻率偏移會在接收端造成訊號的互相
干擾，這些干擾會破壞子載波之間的正交性進而產生子載
波之間干擾(Inter Carrier Interference, ICI)[12]和多用戶存取
干擾(Multi User Interference, MUI)[13-14]。此外，當某些使
用者距離基地台較近時，他們所發出來的訊號，經過通道
的衰減影響較小，所提供的多重存取干擾也會較多。所以
對於那些距離基地台較遠的使用者，效能將會大大的降
低，此類問題我們稱之遠近效應問題(Near-Far Problem)也是
必須克服。因此，本論文將探討在通道具有遠近效應問題
時，兩種多用戶之子載波配置方法，區塊子載波配置(Block 
Subcarrier Assignment, BCA)和區塊交錯子載波配置(Block 
and Interleaved Subcarrier Assignment, BICA)之效能。 
在 CDMA 系統中，許多線性與非線性多用戶檢測器已
提出來減輕多用戶干擾[15-17]，而平行干擾消除(Parallel 
Interference Cancellation, PIC)可用來消除多重存取干擾，不
過，不論多用戶之 BCA 或 BICA 方法，當通道具有遠近效
應時，隨著 NFR 越大代表收到某些用戶功率越低，檢測出
來訊號可靠度很差，這時拿來當估測值作 PIC 反而效能不
理想[18-20]。因此，我們將探討部分平行干擾消除(Partial 
Parallel Interference Cancellation, PPIC)之效能，在不同的
NFR 下，我 們找出其 適當之部分 消除權重 (Partial 
Cancellation Weight)。本報告中我們提出適應性消除權重平
行干擾消除(Adaptive Weight PPIC, AWPPIC)來改善系統效
能並提升其系統用戶容量。

B. 多重存取干擾分析 
我們提到了單用戶檢測器在檢測時會受到來自其他使
用者的干擾而造成系統效能的損失。因此，多重存取干擾
是影響 OFDMA 系統的容量和效能的重要因素之一。在
OFDMA 上鏈通訊系統中，由於各用戶傳送到接收端的時間
延遲不同，造成用戶間彼此無法達到完全正交，造成使用
者和使用者間產生干擾。因此若能降低此項所產生的干
擾，就能藉此改善系統效能。圖 2 為各用戶時間延遲小於
保護區間，在接收時只要移除 CP，便可還原 OFDMA 的符
元訊號。 
但如果用戶的時間延遲大於保護區間，則會產生多用
戶干擾的現象。如圖 3(a)、(b)、(c)及(d)所表示。圖 3(a)為
當 User1 是目前要取樣的符元，這時 User2 的時間延遲小於
CP，因此 User2 信號不會干擾到 User1，而 User3 和 User4
的時間延遲大於 CP，這時 User3 和 User4 的前一個訊框
(Frame)就會干擾到User1當前的Frame，稱為Previous Frame 
MUI (PF-MUI)。圖 3(b)為當 User2 是目前要取樣的符元，
這時 User1 的後一個 Frame 就會干擾到 User2 當前的
Frame，稱為 Next Frame MUI (NF-MUI)，User3 和 User4 對
User1 的干擾為 PF-MUI。圖 3(c)為當 User3 是目前要取樣
的符元，這時 User1 和 User2 對 User3 的干擾為 NF-MUI，
User4 對 User3 的干擾為 PF-MUI。圖 3(d)為當 User4 是目前
要取樣的符元，這時 User1、User2 和 User3 對 User4 的干
擾皆為 NF-MUI。

C. 遠近問題 
另一個影響 OFDMA 效能的重要因素是遠近效應
(Near-Far Effect)，它是由於使用者和基地台的距離不同導致
的。當使用者越靠近基地台時，基地台收到的訊號功率就
越大。相對的，距離遠的使用者所送出的訊號，因為經過
的路徑長，所以到達基地台的訊號功率較小。因此，在基
地台功率強的訊號便會對功率弱的訊號產生較強之干擾，
使功率弱的訊號錯誤率上升。

結論 
本報告針對 OFDMA 無線通訊系統，探討於選頻-時性
衰退及其遠近效應問題時，應用 PIC 方於不同的子載波配
置之效能，在遠近功率比 NFR 較高時，PIC 的效能不理想。
因此，我們提出應用 AWPPIC 於 BCA 和 BICA 方法，可以
改善應用 PIC於 BCA 和 BICA 方法的缺點，當 NFR 較小時，
PCW 可以調整到較高，當 NFR 較大時，PCW 可以調整到
小，因為估測值已經不準確了，這時應用 PIC 來消除干擾
會更差。最後，我們應用 AWPPIC 於 BICA 方法，在任何
環境都是最佳的，因此，我們提出的 AWPPIC 確實可以降
低錯誤率進而提升用戶容量。 
因此，本研究已獲得相當豐富的成果，已發表一篇國際
期刊論文[23]，六篇國際研討會論文[24-29]，及五篇國內研
討會論文[30-34]。

Performance of an MMSE based Indoor Localization 
with Wireless Sensor Networks 
 
Yung-Fa Huang1, You-Ting Jheng1 and Hsing-Chung Chen2 
1Chaoyang University of Technology, Dept. of Information and Communication Engineering, Taichung County,  
2Asia University, Dept. of Computer Science and Information Engineering, Taichung County, 
Taiwan 
 
   
  Abstract- This paper investigates the indoor localization based 
on minimum mean square error (MMSE) estimation for wireless 
sensor networks (WSN). With three received signal strength (RSS) 
of the unknown node, a trilateral localization is performed to 
position its coordinates. However, the mean square error (MSE) 
of localization is deteriorated by the shadowing effect and the 
MSE depends on the location of reference nodes. Therefore, in 
this paper, we investigate the effect of the location of reference 
nodes and the shadowing effect on localization MSE. Simulation 
results show that the distance variance of distances between 
reference nodes and unknown node increases the MSE of 
localization. 
 
I.    INTRODUCTION 
 
In the applications of wireless communication, the 
localization with global position systems (GPS) has become a 
very important technique for mobilization issues for the 
military and commercial [1] in the outdoor environment. 
However, in indoor environments, the GPS can not be 
effectively utilized with the house blocking the 
communication links. Therefore, the indoor localization 
methods become the important research topics [2]. 
With micro electro mechanical systems (MEMS) 
manufacturing technologies and the battery techniques, the 
wireless sensor networks (WSNs) are developing rapidly with 
the large amount of small, low power and low cost device.  
Moreover, due to the advantage and flexibility, the 
applications of WSNs have been spread widely. For examples, 
the public health and medical care, nation defense and force 
arrangement, and     environment surveillance, etc. 
In the field of wireless sensor networks, the localization 
technologies generally are divided into two types: namely, 
Range-Based [4-6] and Range-Free. The Range-Based 
essentially use the angle or distance information to estimate 
the location and distance measurements mostly through radio 
signals and ultrasound. APSAOA [5] the assumption that there 
are three reference nodes (beacon nodes) to rotate clockwise or 
counterclockwise, and emission signals, the number of point 
of rotation with the other two reference nodes can send 
messages, use this angle to calculate the vertex and the other 
in their own the angle formed by two points, the angle 
information to locate unknown node (Unknown Node) and the 
distance between the reference node and position, this 
technology must be able to distinguish the signal receiving 
antenna direction and angle, requiring high precision hardware 
support. 
The principal of the Range-Based Positioning algorithm 
has four methods used to estimate the distance [7], 
transmission time (time of arrival, ToA) method, passing the 
time difference (time difference of arrival, TDoA) method, 
incident angle (Angle of arrival, AOA) method, received 
signal strength method (received signal strength, RSS) method. 
This paper will use the received signal strength method [8, 9], 
because it has the advantage of estimating distance without 
additional equipment, without time synchronization problem 
as well. 
Now most of mobile communication equipments have 
been equipped with wireless transmission capabilities, which 
can be one of the most cost-effective and simple positioning 
system. Therefore, this article will focus RSS based location 
and distance estimation issues. Moreover, the minimum mean 
square error (MMSE) method can coordinates several 
estimation errors. In wireless channels, the signal will be 
subject to different shadow fading effect [10], thus this article 
will explore the effects of different shadow fading effects on 
localization estimation errors. 
The rest of this paper is organized as the followings. 
Section II will focus on various methods based on RSS for 
trilateral localization. The shadow fading and the MMSE 
algorithm are described clearly. Simulation results are 
demonstrated in Section III. In Section IV, some conclusions 
are given. 
 
 
II.   MMSE BASED LOCALIZATION  
 
In the Range-based localization method, we estimate the 
unknown location based on the RSSs received from three 
reference nodes. Then MMSE algorithm is used to locate the 
coordinates of unknown node values. Fig. 1 shows the 
schematic diagram for trilateral localization.  In the estimation 
of distance between the unknown node and the reference node, 
the RSS of the unknown node will be affected to varying 
degrees of shadow fading [10]. The errors make the estimation 
location is spread to an area as shown in Fig. 1. Therefore, the 
MMSE can be used to minimize the localization errors. 
In this article, we set up three reference nodes (A, B, C) 
and seven anchor points for the unknown nodes. At first we 
estimate the distance between the reference node and unknown 
nodes.  We investigate the localization performance of mean 
square error (MSE) by Monte Carlo simulation in different 
shadow fading environments.

IV.   CONCLUSION  
 
In this paper, we investigate the MSE of indoor 
localization based on MMSE method. Simulation results show 
that the distance variance of distances between reference 
nodes and unknown node is a factor to increase the MSE of 
localization. In our experiments, the unknown nodes of the 
central point in an equilateral Triangle, which exists three 
equal distances to the three reference nodes, can obtain the 
minimum MSE among the seven nodes interior the triangle.

PAPER: 74 
TITLE: Performance of Adaptive Fuzzy Spreading Coded Selection Tree Combining for 2-D Spreading OFCDM 
Communication Systems 
  
OVERALL RATING: 3 (strong accept)  
REVIEWER'S CONFIDENCE: 3 (high)  
 
In this paper, the authors proposed a selection tree for choosing a suitable combining scheme for 2-D spreading 
orthogonal frequency coded division multiplexing (OFCDM) communication system. Simulation results show that the 
authors' proposed selection tree adaptive combining (STAC) scheme can not only suppress the MAIs induced by the 
non-orthogonality for heavy system load (SL) but also exploit the diversity gain for light system load over time and 
frequency 
selective fading channels. The authors also proposed an adaptive fuzzy-based spreading coded (AFSC) selection tree 
combining scheme to further exploit the benefit of frequency and time 
diversity gain. I strong accept this paper.

This paper proposes a combiner selection scheme, the selection tree adaptive combining (STAC) scheme, for 2-D 
spreading orthogonal frequency coded division multiplexing (OFCDM) communication system which adaptively 
combines the symbol signals for various situations and system loads. Simulation results over time and frequency 
selective fading channels show that STAC is able to select EGC combiner for heavy system load and MRC combiner 
for light system load, respectively. Furthermore, STAC reduces the MAIs and achieves better diversity gain in cases 
of heavy system load and light system load, respectively. Additionally, an adaptive fuzzy-based spreading coded 
(AFSC) selection tree combining scheme is proposed to further exploit the benefit of frequency and time diversity 
gain. This paper is novel and well organized. However, many grammatical errors were found, which should be 
refined.

V. SIMULATION RESULTS 
In this paper, we assume a synchronous downlink channel, 
and then we discuss the BER performance of MC-CDMA 
systems with OVSF codes over frequency selective fading 
channels. To investigate the multiuser performance of STAC 
schemes in the 2D spreading OFCDM systems as shown in 
Fig. 2, we performed computer simulation. 
Fig. 6 shows the BER performance comparison of MRC, 
EGC and STAC combiner for OFCDM systems with fixed 
speed fd=10Hz, SNR=9dB and SF=32, respectively. We use 
the SF = 32 of the corresponding table compare to simulation 
environment, can be found when fd = 10Hz, and the system 
load under the 3/8, we adopt the MRC combiner. Moreover, 
when SL is lower than 4/8, the EGC combiner is used to 
achieve the best system performance. It is observed that even 
though the MRC can get more diversity gain, but the more 
MAI induced by high system load would degrade the 
performance.
