緒論 
 
在1990年中期，印刷電路板(Printed Circuit Board)成為台灣第二大
電子零組件產業，我國PCB產業成長主要原因ㄓㄧ是軟性印刷電路板
(Flexible Printed Circuit)，軟性印刷電路板的基材以聚醯亞胺為主。 
 一般高分子量的芳香族polyimides 薄膜通常具有韌性及可饒
性、耐溶劑、高Tg、好的熱穩定性等優點，但是polyimides 薄膜的顏
色通常介於黃色及琥珀色，因此勢必會有較高陽光吸收性。研究指
出，電荷轉移錯體(charge-transfer complex，CTC)的形成會導致
polyimides 的顏色變深。使用具有巨大基團的芳香族二胺可以有效減
低CTC 的形成，降低polyimides 薄膜的顏色。 
由於傳統之polyimide溶解度不佳，使得加工不易，且因苯環與醯
亞胺基造成的高剛硬性，使其韌性不高。本論文針對上述之缺點加以
改善，在芳香族二胺單體上導入Bulky的集團以增加聚醯亞胺的加工
性、溶解性以及韌性。

Flame-Retardant Epoxy Resins with High Tg by Novel Curing Agents 
 
Ching Hsuan Lin*, Sheng Xiong Cai, Tin Yu Hwang and Ya Ru Tsao

We synthesized a novel phosphorus-containing triamine (dopo-ta) from the 
nucleophilic addition of 9, 10-dihydro-9-oxa-10-phosphaphenanthrene 10-oxide 
(dopo) and pararosaniline chloride using triethylamine as an acid receiver (Scheme 1).  
We propose a mechanism for this reaction (Scheme 2). We confirmed the structure of 
dopo-ta by IR, Mass, NMR spectra (Figure 1) and elemental analysis. The dopo-ta 
was served as a curing agent for diglycidyl ether of bisphenol A (dgeba) and 
dicyclopentadiene epoxy (hp7200). Properties such as glass transition temperature, 
thermal decomposition temperature, flame retardancy, moisture absorption and 
dielectric properties of cured epoxy resins were evaluated (Table 1). The glass 
transition temperatures of cured dgeba/dopo-ta and hp7200/dopo-ta are 171 oC and 
190oC, respectively. This high Tg phenomenon is rarely seen in the literature after 
introducing a flame retardant element. The flame retardancy increases with the 
phosphorus content and a UL-94 V-0 grade can be achieved with a phosphorus 
content of 1.80 wt % for dgeba/dopo-ta/ddm and 1.46 wt % for hp7200/dopo-ta/ddm 
systems (Table 2). The dielectric constant for dgeba/dopo-ta and hp7200/dopo-ta is 
2.91 and 2.82, respectively, implying dopo-ta curing system exhibits low dielectric 
properties (Table 1). Dgeba/dopo-ta shows the lowest moisture absorption when 
compared with ddm and dds curing systems, and similar result is observed for 
hp7200/dopo-ta (Figure 2).
