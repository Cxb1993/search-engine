一、中文摘要 
    RFID 應用於物流供應鏈上貨物的追
蹤與追溯，以增加物流供應鏈上資訊的能
見度，已逐漸受重視。要達成這樣的目標，
除原有的RFID 電子標籤與讀碼器技術
外，背後還需要有一網路資訊系統來支
援，而EPCglobal 組織所推動的「EPC 
Network」正是為此網路資訊系統。EPC 
Network 定義了幾個主要的元件，如：
EPC(Electronic Product Code) 、 tag 、
reader、middleware、ONS、EPCIS、及EPC 
DS，並對這些元件的本身或其溝通的介面
制定了初步標準。但是，EPCglobal 至今
尚未定義完整各元件設計及其間資料的傳
遞與運作模式，更缺乏如何將 EPC 
Network 導入應用在物流供應鏈上，或以
何種方式導入，與導入之後的效益。因此，
本研究為探討導入EPC Network 於物流
供應鏈的運作方式，除研究每個元件的功
能及其涵意，並以一個物流供應鏈的情境
為基礎探討EPC Network 如何對供應鏈
上的物品進行追蹤與追溯功能，包含商品
在流通過程的資料如何的被記錄，以及供
應鏈上的各個成員要如何查詢完整的物流
運送履歷。 
關鍵詞：無線射頻辨識、電子標籤碼、供
應鏈 
Abstract 
    Applications of RFID system on the 
track and trace of logistic to improve supply 
chain visibility are becoming more popular 
recently. However, in addition to tag and 
reader technology, it is required a special 
designed information network system to 
support supply chain operation. The 
international organization EPCglobal has 
proposed an EPC Network to be considered 
as a standard for RFID-based track and trace 
in logistics supply chain system. The EPC 
Network is composed of several standard 
components, such as EPC(Electronic 
Product Code)、 tag、reader、middleware、
ONS、EPCIS、and EPC DS. Therefore, the 
objectives of this research are to investigate 
these components and then to present 
operational models to describe information 
relationship among these components. An 
illustration or demo system is presented to 
show the track and trace function of 
logistics. 
Keywords: RFID、EPC、Supply Chain 
二、前言 
最近的 RFID 應用熱潮有別於以往在
單一企業或工廠內部的應用，逐漸強調物
流供應鏈的產品追蹤與身份識別。在物流
供應鏈上的應用除了 RFID 電子標籤與讀
碼器的技術外，更重要的是背後的資訊網
路架構。此架構目前主要有 EPCglobal 組
織推動的 EPC Network 標準，其目的在於
建構一個全球性網際網路的物品識別環
境，”The Internet of Things”。EPC Network 
定義了幾個主要的元件，如： EPC 
(Electronic Product Code)、tag、reader、
middleware、ONS、EPC IS、及 EPC DS，
並對這些元件的本身或其溝通的介面制定
初步標準。雖然目前 EPCglobal 已經針對
一些基本元件定義了標準化的需求，但是
仍然有許多 EPC Network 標準尚在發展
中，此外，在應用系統 EPC Network 架構
的部份，文獻中雖然已有學者探討，但仍
缺乏對整體運作模式分析的研究，而且各
學者的解讀方式也不一致。不過對於欲早
期佈局市場的企業而言，卻已面臨即刻瞭
解其扮演的角色、功能、與運作方式的需
求。另外，在目前階段如果實際建構一個
真實系統，其風險過高且耗費時間；反之
如果沒有這樣一個示範性的情境系統，也

3.2 追蹤與追溯 
3.2.1 追蹤與追溯之定義、目的與效益 
追蹤與追溯，英文 Track and Trace，
簡 稱 T&T 。 P.Baglietto, L.Ballardin, 
S.Bedin(2005)，將追蹤與追溯定義為兩
種，一者為本地之追蹤與追溯，一者為廣
域地區之追蹤與追溯。本地之追蹤與追
溯，代表廠內或固定地點中，對物品動態
的掌握及回溯。廣域地區則代表在外在地
理區域之物品動態掌握及回溯。 
追蹤與追溯之目的是為了確保每一個
貨物都能在有效時間內送達正確的地方，
因此在運送過程中需要得知貨物的即時狀
態資訊。且可以追溯移動中貨物的運送履
歷，並檢測貨物狀態是否處在正常狀態及
是否在正確的運送途徑中。 
本研究針對廣域之追蹤與追溯討論，
其期望效益為透過 T&T 的技術，3PL 可以
取得即時的貨況資訊，提供即時貨況查詢
的整合性服務。由於資訊透明度的提升，
可以增進作業的正確性。

4.2 EPC Network 運作模式分析 
4.2.1 單一階層供應鏈之 EPC Network 運
作模式 
單一階層供應鏈情境說明 
在單一商品的流供應鏈上成員有製造
商A、流通業者B、與零售商C，如圖4.1 所
示。製造商生產的物品會先運送到物流中
心再配送至商店。製造工廠生產的物品皆
貼上電子標籤記錄著EPC 碼，包裝區裝設
讀碼器，當物品產出後會讀取並記錄，記
錄物品的產出地點與時間等資訊。當製造
商A 收到來自流通業者B 的訂單後，便會
將物品搬運上貨車，準備運往流通業者B 
的物流中心。製造商的出貨碼頭裝設有讀
碼器，物品通過時會被感應並記錄；同時
也檢驗貨物內容，比對物品是否與訂單上
的內容符合。記錄物品的EPC碼並產生
ASN (Advanced Shipping Notice)，並傳送
給物流中心。 
當貨車來到物流中心進行卸貨，物流
中心的進貨碼頭裝有讀碼器，這些物品通
過進貨碼頭時會被記錄下來隨後送到倉儲
區儲存。進貨過程透過讀碼器讀到的物品
EPC 碼與ASN 進行比對，項目不吻合時
能夠馬上發現並進行處理。流通業者B 收
到零售商C的訂單後，作業員就從倉儲區
搬物品並上貨車。這時出貨碼頭也裝設有
讀碼器，當物品通過時會被感應並記錄下
來，並同時比對物品內容是否與訂單需求
相符，另外也會傳送ASN 給商店。 
貨品到達商店時，作業人員使用手持
式讀碼器記錄所收到的物品，隨後搬到後
倉儲存。手持式讀碼器會預先下載ASN，
並將讀到的物品EPC 碼與ASN 中的EPC 
碼進行比對，當項目不吻合時能夠即時發
現與處理。消費者拿起欲購買的物品至櫃
檯結帳，櫃台利用手持式讀碼器為消費者
進行結帳作業，同時記錄了此物品已售出
的訊息。

皆處於 Readable Cycle，可被正確讀取到，
故資訊不會遺失。但也因為其是一個一個

需要所有承載物的 Tag 皆可讀取，至少在
多次讀取下可以被讀到一次以上。也因此
在一般情況下較為快速。可是所謂非一般
情況，即若讀取率過低時，卻反而會造成
讀取次數極高，甚至因某 Tag 一直無法被
讀取到，而遲遲無法完成作業。另外，相
較於 Model 2 必須一個一個移至讀取區讀
取後再上載具，Model 3 等上載具後再一
次讀取的作法，不會對舊有作業流程造成
影響。最後，Model 1，Model 3 還必須預
先知道棧板之編碼規則及承載物之個數。

Abstract—The paper studies the capacity planning problem in the TFT-LCD production chain. Due to two 
significant trends, the capacity planning decision gradually becomes an important strategic issue to which 
TFT-LCD industry paid attention. First is the increase of product types which causes a wide range of product 
groups, such as mobile, monitor, notebook, TV and industrial display are produced. The second trend is the 
advances of new technology which cause multiple generations of technologies coexist in each manufacturing 
stage and production site. This paper proposes a mixed integer linear programming (MILP) to formulate the 
capacity planning which considers many practical characteristics and constraints in TFT-LCD production 
chain. A heuristic algorithm is developed to solve the MILP model.  
 
Index Terms—Capacity Planning、TFT-LCD、Mixed Integer Linear Programming 
I. INTRODUCTION 
FT-LCD(Thin Film Transistor Liquid Crystal Display) has been an emerging industry in the 
past few years in Taiwan. The production chain in TFT-LCD panel industry consists of three 
manufacturing stages named Array, Cell and Module processes and each stage has multiple 
production sites. Array and Cell processes are all capacity-constraint and high-investment 
production environments. Effective utilization and planning of their production capacity are 
crucial for TFT-LCD industry. 
Due to two significant trends, capacity planning gradually becomes an important mid-term 
strategic issue to which TFT-LCD industry paid attention. First is the increase of product types 
which causes a wide range of product groups, such as mobile, monitor, notebook, TV and 
industrial display are produced. The second trend is the advances of new technology which cause 
multiple generations of technologies coexist in each manufacturing stage and production site. 
The decisions of capacity planning include how many of which product to produce in what site 
and how many new auxiliary tools of which product to purchase for expanding the capacity in 
what site.  
As a result of these motivations, this paper presents a mixed integer linear programming 
(MILP) to formulate the whole model of the capacity planning which considers many practical 
characteristics and constraints in TFT-LCD production chain. The model objective is to

III. HEURISTIC ALGORITHM 
This study develops a heuristic algorithm to solve the MILP capacity planning model. This 
heuristic algorithm uses the shadow-price (post-optimization) concept to decompose the capacity 
planning model into two iterative phases showed in Figure 5, capacity allocation phase and 
capacity expansion phase. Initially, capacity allocation phase only considers the available 
capacity for each site and each product group to decide the product mix and production 
quantities without capacity expansion decision. Since capacity allocation phase is a complicated 
MILP problem, we combine genetic algorithm and linear programming method to solve it. The 
GA-LP method not only determines the best product mix and production quantities but also 
generates the shadow price information of all site-product available capacity constraints which is 
the important input of capacity expansion phase. Then, according to the useful shadow price 
information, capacity expansion phase will recommend that the capacity of certain product group 
must be expanded at certain site in certain period. Once we have new expanded capacity of 
certain product group at certain site in certain period, the right-hand-side of the certain 
site-product available capacity constraints (7) will be increased and the capacity allocation phase 
must be resolved again. These two phases are continually iterated each other until the 
termination condition, no more capacity is worth expanding, is satisfied.

V. CONCLUSIONS 
 In this paper, we present a capacity planning problem in the TFT-LCD production chain. 
The decisions of capacity planning will determine how many of which product to produce in 
what site and how many new auxiliary tools of which product to purchase for expanding the 
capacity in what site. Then a mixed integer linear programming model is proposed to formulate 
the capacity planning which considers many practical characteristics and constraints in TFT-LCD 
production chain. This study also develops a heuristic algorithm to solve the MILP capacity 
planning model. Finally, the computational study shows the results of this algorithm have the 
best performances in the solution quality and CPU time.
