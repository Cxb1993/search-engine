二、研究目的  
本研究計畫之目的是以溶膠凝膠中其金屬氧化物材料特性與一般無機材料
相似，具抗拉、抗壓強度大、耐熱性佳以及熱傳導熱膨脹均低的特性；而高分子
材料通常耐熱性差，熱膨脹率高且易燃。故本研究希冀以 Titanium(IV) 
isopropoxide (TIPT)和 Tetraethoxysilane (TEOS)作為前驅物，藉由溶膠凝膠法製備
聚乳酸/二氧化矽與聚乳酸/二氧化鈦之奈米複合材料，改善原生物可分解性材料
PLA 之性質，進而探討此複合材料對材料型態、熱行為與結晶影響。 
 
三、文獻探討  
典型的複合材料是將兩種或兩種以上不同性質的材料，經由特定的加工方法
製成混合材料，其性質通常會介於兩種原材料之間，利用其配方成份之不同，可
得到較原有兩種材料更優越的性能之新材料。Bledzki 和 Gassan[18]研究如何將纖
維素作為基材的材料性能更為提升，以天然纖維作為高分子補強材的最大優點則
為他們具有的兼容性。Shanks[19]研究天然纖維亞麻(flax)和聚 3-羥基丁酯 poly 
(3-hydroxylbutyrate)(PHB)發現纖維附著在聚酯類的效果要比 polypropylene 更
佳，且造成 PHB 之玻璃轉移溫度和結晶度發生變化。 
    許多學者研究以 Poly lactide 作為複合材料基底的新式材料，Ray et al.[20]將
PLA/ 經有機改質後的層狀矽以融熔壓出成型方式作為複合材料，
oligo(caprolactone)(o-PCL)作為相容劑，從表面分析與機械性質作為主要探討方
向，發現經插層後的奈米複合材料在材料的固態與融熔態，對材料的性質有顯著
的改善。Pluta et al.[21]研究 PLA/ montmorillonite(MMT)奈米複合材料，以融熔混
摻的方式混合改質後之 MMT 與未改質之 MMT，從 XRD 可知改質過後的 MMT
與 PLA 彼此之間有良好的親合性，於複合材料中形成插層結構；從 TGA 也可得
知在空氣環境下，此複合材料改善 PLA 的熱穩定性。Chang et al.[22]研究兩種有
機黏土，以 hexadecylamine 進行改質後的 MMT(C16-MMT)和以氟素化合的雲母
(C16-Mica)，將兩種改質後的材料以溶液插層的方式分散於 PLA 中，結果顯示
當 C16-MMT 含量達 4wt%時，張力強度和彈性模數皆會提升，而 C16-Mica 則於
8wt%時也開始上升。 
    高分子/silicate 的奈米複合材料，比起現有的純高分子和傳統的複合材料，
展現更優秀的物理性質，包含提昇儲能模量，拉伸和彎曲的性質，並有降低滲透
和易燃的效果[23,24]。Linbo Wu et al.[25]將酸性矽溶膠(contain 25.4wt% SiO2)加入
Poly l-(lactic acid)(PLLA)中，以超音波震盪後，用減壓蒸餾的方式製得
oligo-(L-lactic acid) 和 nano-silica (OLLA/SiO2)後再進行縮合反應，反應後的產物
溶於氯仿中在離心乾燥得到 PLLA/SiO2 的奈米複合材料。S.Yan et al.[26] 將
L-lactic acid 單體溶於甲苯並置入 silica 奈米粒子，於氮氣環境下加溫攪拌，反應
三天後，離心取得產物後以氯仿清洗後去除溶劑得接枝型 g-SiO2/PLLA 奈米複合
材料，並證實此複合材料改善原材料的韌性與張力強度，且加入的 SiO2 奈米粒
子，可作為 PLLA 結晶的成核劑。有機無機複合材料顯著改善機械[27]，熱[28]，
電性[29]，和磁性[30]等性質。特別是在於奈米尺寸下的無機摻混所引起的有趣性
質，譬如光學透明度，由於此類材料的廣泛應用性，許多摻混材料已被商業化
[31]。近年來，鈦材料在摻混產品中獲得極大的關注[32,33]， 由於其特殊的性質（X

五、結果與討論 
    微分掃描熱卡計(DSC)分析 PLLA/TiO2 奈米複合材料之熱行為，由文獻[36]
可得知 PLLA 的熔融溫度 (Tm)在 172℃ ，而結晶溫度 (Tcc)為一個範圍在
100~150℃，玻璃轉移溫度(Tg)約在 60℃，各摻合樣品先升溫至 PLLA 熔融溫度
之上 10~20℃，200℃恆溫 3 分鐘後驟冷(Quench)至-30℃，接著以 10℃/min 做升
溫掃描。圖 1 為 PLLA/TiO2 奈米複合材料之熱分析圖，並將圖中所獲得之熱行為
資訊彙整至表 1，與 PLLA 性質做比較，複合材料比例不同時，Tg 並無產生明顯
地溫度偏移，表示此複合材料系統兩者之間可能不具有作用力；Tcc溫度則隨 TiO2
比例越高越往低溫位移，而從 Tcc 所顯示的結晶放熱峰可知，加入 TiO2 的複合材
料，比 PLLA 其結晶放熱峰的位置與峰值大小皆比 PLLA 更加明確，表示 TiO2
使 PLLA 更容易產生結晶；在 Tm的溫度變化也可看出 TiO2 比率越高熔點越往低
溫移動的趨勢，表示加入 TiO2 使 PLLA 進行結晶成長時，於單位體積內的結晶
物質比原 PLLA 結晶具有更高的表面自由能，因此，形成晶板厚度較小且較不完
善的結晶；當 TiO2 含量 3wt%時，有最高的ᇞHc和ᇞHm值，由熱力學的觀點，可
以知道熔點會依據熔融熱ᇞH 和熔融熵ᇞS 兩因素決定[37]，當 TiO2隨著重量比率
上升時，晶板厚度較小的結晶造成較高的ᇞH 值，而 TiO2 的重量比率也同時降低
熔融熵ᇞS，但隨著 PLLA 量越趨減少，形成結晶的數目也越少，過多的 TiO2開
始阻礙結晶行為，ᇞH 值逐漸變小，直至 TiO2 含量到達 56.5wt%時，已無法再由
DSC 偵測。圖 2 為 PLLA/TiO2 奈米複合材料穿透式電子顯微鏡圖。

同心圓消光環是徑向向外射出的晶板猶如緞帶狀地同時扭轉產生的結果，POM
下所觀察到的消光環間距與晶板扭轉周期相對應，Neat PLLA 在各溫度中也無出
現消光環形態，顯示 TiO2 的加入可能與 PLLA 產生某種作用力[38]，使球晶具有
能形成消光環的排列方式。當 TiO2 的量到達 49.4wt%時，在 POM 下已無法觀察
到結晶，因 TiO2 在系統中分佈過多，使 PLLA 無法順利形成球晶。 
為了進一步觀察 PLLA 球晶型態，POM 使用 530nm 彩色偏光板觀察 PLLA
球晶型態。圖 4 為 PLLA/TiO2 混成材料 TiO2 含量不同比率下，85℃恆溫結晶的
球晶型態。由圖中可知 PLLA 的結晶型態隨 TiO2 增加，因形成的晶核點多，在
低溫時結晶排列的方向較為混亂，使球晶雙折射改變而呈現顏色多樣性；隨著溫
度上升，PLLA 受到溫度的影響，高分子鏈運動性增加，成核後所成長的球晶開
始朝固定方向延伸，推測 PLLA 的結晶過程受到 TiO2 和溫度影響使高分子排列
方式產生改變。且在 530nm 濾光片下可以很明顯觀察到消光環的存在，當溫度
升至高溫 115℃時，消光環逐漸消失，球晶也開始扭曲，因是受到高溫熱擾動影
響，難以形成一完整型態的球晶。 
作用力分析 
POM 的實驗結果中，觀察到與 PLLA 結晶不同的結晶型態，推測 PLLA/TiO2
混成材料中，產生新的作用力造成結晶型態改變。觀察分子鏈之間的作用力變

前皆經過 200℃熱處理 1 分鐘後隨即以液態氮驟冷，再移至待測溫度環境之中。

六、結論與建議 
溶膠−凝膠法製備之 PLLA/SiO2 混成材料，由 DSC 分析可發現隨 SiO2 含量上升，結
晶完成所需的時間大幅減少；而混成材料的球晶型態，隨 SiO2 含量上升，球晶半徑在恆溫
溫度下逐漸增大，且球晶成長速率也明顯增快，皆顯示 SiO2 在結晶過程中具有形成晶核的
作用，改善 PLLA 結晶成長速度較慢的性質，但並非 SiO2 含量愈高效果愈好，過多 SiO2
將會阻礙 PLLA 結晶；而由廣角 X-ray 分析可知，PLLA/SiO2 混成材料並未使 PLLA 所具有
的結晶峰消失，且部分結晶峰具有增強趨勢，證實 PLLA/SiO2 混成材料使 PLLA 結晶具有
更強的結晶性。溶膠−凝膠法製備之 PLLA/TiO2 混成材料，由 DSC 分析可發現隨 TiO2 含量
上升，結晶完成所需的時間大幅減少，與 SiO2 具有相同的效果；而 PLLA/TiO2 混成材料的
球晶型態，球晶半徑在恆溫溫度下逐漸增大，且球晶成長速率也明顯增快，同樣顯示 TiO2
在結晶過程中具有形成晶核的作用；當 TiO2 含量到達 18.2wt%以上時，可發現恆溫結晶之
球晶出現消光環 (ring-banded)之型態，且隨恆溫結晶溫度升高，可觀察到球晶晶板逐漸扭
曲並呈現不規則型態，表示 TiO2 與 PLLA 產生一特殊作用力，造成球晶型態改變。進一步
以 FTIR 分析 PLLA/TiO2 混成材料官能基變化，觀測到新官能基之特徵峰，顯示 PLLA/TiO2
混成材料間有反應發生。由 ESCA 之實驗結果，根據其化學鍵結比例上的變化，得知
PLLA/TiO2 混成材料作用力之機制為交酯化反應；再經由廣角 X-ray 分析證實，TiO2 含量
越高會抑制 PLLA 結晶吸收峰，PLLA 因交酯化反應，使原分子鏈斷鏈並改變結晶方式，
造成原 PLLA 結晶吸收峰受到破壞。

The Influence of RF Power on the Characteristics of Sr0.6Ba0.4Nb2O6 Thin Film  
 
Y.S. Yang1, C.C. Su2, G.H. Wu2, Y.X. Lin3, C.F. Yang2* 
 
1Department of Electronic Engineering, Kao Yuan University, Kaohsiung, Taiwan, R.O.C. 
2Department of Chemical and Materials Engineering, National University of Kaohsiung, Kaohsiung, 
Taiwan, R.O.C.  
3Department of Electronic Engineering National Kaoshiung University of Applied Science, Taiwan, 
R.O.C. 
Abstract 
Sr0.6Ba0.4Nb2O6 ceramic targets are fabricated by 
ourselves and radio frequency (RF) depositing method 
is used to deposit Sr0.6Ba0.4Nb2O6 (SBN) dielectric thin 
films on the Pt/Ti/Si and Pt/Ti/SiO2/Si substrates as a 
metal-ferroelectric-metal (MFM) and metal- 
ferroelectric-insulator-metal (MFIM) structure. By 
careful control of sputtering parameters, the optimal 
sputtering parameters for depositing SBN thin films are 
substrate temperature of 500oC and chamber pressure of 
10 mTorr. The SBN thin films deposited on the optimal 
parameters have the acceptable depositing rate, uniform 
grain growth and acceptable roughness. It is found that 
the oxygen concentration and the RF power have large 
influences on the thickness (depositing rate) but have no 
apparent influences on the morphologies of SBN thin 
films. Furthermore, the influences of oxygen 
concentration and the RF power on electrical 
characteristics of SBN thin films, including capacitance 
versus voltage (C-V) curves and leakage current density 
versus electrical field (J-E) curves, are also investigated 
in this study. 
Introduction 
Many ferroelectric materials with high dielectric 
constant had been widely developed as ferroelectric thin 
films for the various possible applications of memory 
cell. For example, the thin films of (Ba,Sr)TiO3 and 
Ba(Zr,Ti)O3 were developed for their higher dielectric 
constant and lower leakage current density than those of 
BaTiO3 [1-3]. During the development of non-Pb high 
dielectric constant ferroelectric ceramics, the strontium 
niobium-tantalum (Sr(Nb,Ta)2O6) and strontium barium 
-niobium ((Sr,Ba)Nb2O6) ceramics were investigated 
[4-5]. Ferroelectric strontium barium- niobium 
(SrxBa1−xNb2O6, 0.2≤x≤0.8) are the attractive materials, 
which are widely considered very useful in diverse 
device applications, including memory devices, saw 
devices and so on. If rare-earth and alkali ions are 
doped into SBN crystals, the SBN thin films that will 
substantially enhance the electro-optic and 
photorefractive coefficients. Due to the anisotropic 
properties of SBN ceramics, it is desirable to obtained 
oriented SBN thin films. A number of studies were 
demonstrated that the c-axis oriented SBN thin films 
could be grown on (100) MgO substrates or on 
LixNi2-xO coated MgO substrates [6]. However, pure 
c-axis oriented SBN films prepared directly on silicon 
substrates have rarely been reported. Many ferroelectric 
materials with high dielectric constant have been widely 
developed as ferroelectric thin films for the various 
possible applications of memory cell. But so far, only 
few studies are focused on the SrNb2O6-based thin films 
for the application of requirement of Giga-bytes 
Dynamic Random Access Memory (DRAM) devices. 
Various methods could be used to develop the 
deposition of ferroelectric thin films, including 
chemical solution deposition, metal organic chemical 
vapor deposition (MOCVD) and molecular beam 
epitaxy (MBE). Among the thin films growth 
technologies, radio frequency (RF) sputtering was one 
of the most promising methods to deposit dielectric thin 
films because of the easier process control. The SBN 
thin films could be easily achieved by changing ceramic 
target compositions. In this study, the Sr0.6Ba0.4Nb2O6 
(SBN) ceramic is chosen as the dielectric material and 
the RF sputtering method is used to deposit the SBN 
thin films. We would deposit SBN thin films on Pt/Ti/Si 
and Pt/Ti/SiO2/Pt substrates with a conventional 
RF-magnetron sputtering method. Because the 
sputtering parameters have large influences on the 
characteristics of depositing thin films, we will find the 
optimal depositing parameters and then research the 
influences of different oxygen concentration and RF 
power for the physical and electrical characteristics of 
SBN thin films. 
Experimental 
Raw materials of SrCO3, BaCO3, and Nb2O5 were 
weighed according to the compositions of stoichiometry 
Sr0.6Ba0.4Nb2O6 (SBN). After mixing by ball-milling, 
the mixture was dried, ground and calcined at 1100oC 
for 2 hrs. The pressed targets of 2-inch diameter were 
sintered at 1340oC in an air ambient for 2 hrs, then SBN 
ceramic targets were obtained. The SBN thin films were 
deposited under substrate temperature of 500oC and 
chamber pressure of 10 m Torr with various deposition 
parameters such as RF power and oxygen concentration. 
To eliminate the impurity and particles of the chamber, 
the chamber base pressure was maintained at 1×10-6 
Torr. In order to remove the defects of ceramic target 
and obtain stable plasma during deposition time, pre- 
sputtering time of as-deposited SBN thin films was 
maintained for 2 hrs under argon ambient. The SBN 
thin films were deposited under various deposition 
parameters such as the RF power, chamber pressure and

Figure 5 shows the leakage current density versus 
electrical field (J-E) curves of the as-deposited SBN 
thin films under different oxygen concentrations. The 
leakage current densities of as-deposited SBN thin films 
first increase with the increase of applied voltage and 
saturates at 4 MV/cm. As Fig.5 shows, the leakage 
current density critically decreases as the oxygen 
concentration increase from 0% to 25%. During the 
depositing process, the oxygen vacancies will appear in 
the depositing SBN thin films. As the oxygen/argon 
mixtures are used as the depositing atmosphere, the 
defects and oxygen vacancies in SBN thin films are 
filled and compensated by oxygen gas, and the leakage 
current densities will decrease. But too much oxygen 
concentrations are not necessary, because the oxygen 
will format the inter-dopant and make the leakage 
current density to increase. For that, the smallest 
leakage current densities will reveal in the 40%- 
oxygen-deposited SBN thin films. As the applied 
electrical field of 1 MV/cm is used, the leakage current 
density of SBT thin films deposited at 40% oxygen 
concentration has a small value of about 3×10-8A/cm2. 
Fig. 5 also suggests that too much oxygen used as the 
depositing gas is not good for the SBT thin films. 
Because as the oxygen concentration is 60%, the 
roughness will increase, and that will result in the 
increase of leakage current density.

Because SBN thin films depositing under the 40% 
oxygen concentration have the minimum leakage 
current densities, for that the 40% oxygen concentration 
is used as the depositing atmosphere. Figure 6 shows 
the C-V curves of the as-deposited SBN thin films 
under different RF power with the MFM structure, 
measured from the positive 10 V to negative 20 V or 
from the negative 20 V to positive 10 V. As Fig.4 
shows, a small close hysteresis loop is also observed, 
that indicate SBN thin films exist the switching of the 
ferroelectric polarization. Fig.4 indicates that the 
threshold voltage windows for RF power 80W, 100W, 
120W, and 140W are 0V, 8.2 V, 10.3 V, and 0.8 V, 
respectively. This indicates that a threshold voltage 
windows much larger than 3 V window required for 
conventional flash memory devices. The large ΔVFB 
could be attributed to the remanent polarization of the 
SBN thin films. This result suggests that the deposited 
SBN thin films have the large polarization and can be 
used to fabricate high performance memory devices.
