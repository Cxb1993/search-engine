中文摘要 
本研究以薄膜旋轉電極方法探討 Pd-black 觸媒分別在 0.5M 硫酸以及 1M 過氯酸電解質
溶液中對甲酸分子的電催化氧化反應動力學。利用循環伏安法(CV)所測得甲酸分子在 0.5M
硫酸以及 1M 過氯酸溶液中的擴散係數(D)在高掃描速度區(500~75 mV/s)分別為 6.7×10-6 
cm2s-1 與 5.1×10-6 cm2s-1；而在低掃描速度區(50~5 mV/s)則分別為 3.9×10-7 cm2s-1 與 1.1×10-7 
cm2s-1，硫酸中的 D 值皆大於過氯酸。進一步以 i-t 法並利用 Cottrell 方程式所求得甲酸分子
在 0.5M 硫酸與 1M 過氯酸中的 D 值分別為 4.0×10-6 cm2s-1 與 3.5×10-6 cm2s-1，與以 CV 法在
高掃描速度區所求得之 D 值較為相近。所求得擴散係數值上的些微差異可歸因於實驗誤
差。本研究的結果有助於了解在不同酸性電解質溶液對電催化甲酸氧化反應動力學的影響。 
關鍵字：鈀、甲酸、電催化、薄膜電極、燃料電池 
 
英文摘要 
In this work, the reaction kinetics of electrocatalytic formic acid oxidation on a Pd-black 
catalyst in 0.5M H2SO4 or 1M HClO4 electrolyte was investigated by using the thin-film rotating 
disk electrode (TFRDE) methodology. By using the cyclic voltammetry (CV) on the 0.1M formic 
acid oxidation on the Pd-black electrode, the linear correlations of the diffusion coefficient (D) of 
formic acid were divided into two regions. The values of D in the region of high scan rates 
(500~75 mV/s) were 6.7×10-6 cm2s-1 and 5.1×10-6 cm2s-1, respectively in 0.5M H2SO4 and 1M 
HClO4 electrolytes; whereas in the region of low scan rates (50~5 mV/s) the D values were 
3.9×10-7 cm2s-1 and 1.1×10-7 cm2s-1, respectively. By using the i-t method for further 
confirmations on the D values, the D values were evaluated to be 4.0×10-6 cm2s-1 and 3.5×10-6 
cm2s-1, which are close to the D values obtained in the high scan rate regions by the CV method. 
This work might be useful in understanding the effects of electrolyte on the reaction kinetics of 
formic acid oxidation on a Pd-black catalyst. 
Keywords: Pd, Formic Acid, Electrocatalysis, Thin-Film Electrode, Fuel Cells 
 
一、前言  
從因應溫室效應的全球環境問題，到面對未來化石能源將會枯竭的挑戰，高效率的能
源轉換與使用及替代能源的開發，是目前迫切須要進行的工作。燃料電池為高效率、潔淨
的能源轉換裝置。從科技選擇、經濟及環境效益等角度考量，燃料電池科技是值得投入發
展的新興科技領域。 
燃料電池是一種新型的電化學能量轉化裝置，與傳統的電池概念完全不同。只要將燃
料(如氫氣、甲醇、甲酸或其他小分子有機物等)直接導入燃料電池系統，就能將燃料的化學
能直接轉變成電能。燃料電池具有低污染、高能源轉換效率、低噪音、用途多、免充電及
進料廣等特點。目前發展中的燃料電池，依電解質加以分類，可區分為鹼液型(AFC)、磷酸
型(PAFC)、熔融碳酸鹽型(MCFC)、固態氧化物型(SOFC)、質子交換膜型(PEMFC)及與
PEMFC 同樣使用質子交換膜(eg. Nafion)為電解質的直接甲醇型(DMFC)等(Vielstich et al., 
2003)。若以溫度分類，PEMFC(室溫~90ºC)，DMFC(室溫~130ºC)、AFC(室溫~90ºC)及
的溶劑揮發，藉之讓薄膜與觸媒之間更緊密接合而不易剝落。在進行電化學量測分析前，
電極必須浸泡在 0.5M H2SO4 或 1M HCO4 電解質溶液中 8~10 小時，以確保 Nafion 膜之潤
濕(Lin, 2005)。 
2.2 電化學分析實驗 
本實驗使用三電極系統電化學槽，包含 GC 工作電極、對電極以及參考電極，其中參
考電極使用的是飽和甘汞電極(SCE)，對電極由白金絲組成，電解質溶液為 0.5M H2SO4 或
1M HClO4 水溶液。測量的儀器為恆電位儀(Autolab PGSTAT30, Ecochemie)，利用電腦軟體
來控制並記錄實驗數據。本研究所有電位都是相對於可逆氫電極(RHE)。進行實驗測量前需
通入氮氣於溶液中，以除去系統中的溶氧；使用循環伏安法並設定電位在 0~1V vs. RHE 的
範圍，經過數次的掃描直到穩定的 CV 圖形出現，並藉此估算 Pd-black 觸媒的電化學活化
表面積(Electrochemical Active Surface, EAS) (Schmidt et al., 2003; Lin, 2005)。 
    進行 CV 法分析實驗前，先通入氮氣於電解質溶液中 15 分鐘以除去電解質溶液中的溶
氧，將薄膜電極置入電化學槽並進行循環伏安法掃描，待掃描圖譜穩定後，停止掃描。加
入甲酸使電解質溶液中 Formic Acid 濃度達到設定的濃度值(0.1M)，經由循環伏安法設定電
位在 0~1 V vs. RHE 的範圍，掃描速度在 15~500 mVs-1 之間，以得數個不同掃描速度下的循
環伏安圖譜數據。 
進行 CA 法分析實驗時與 CV 法相同，在得到待測電極穩定的 CV 圖譜後，加入定量的
甲酸，使電解質溶液中 Formic Acid 濃度為 0.1M。於固定電壓 0.2V vs. RHE 下，進行 1000
秒的電催化甲酸氧化反應測試，並紀錄電流與時間(i-t)的關係圖(Li, 2010)。 
三、結果與討論 
3.1 Pd-black 觸媒之電化學活化面積 
圖一是典型的薄膜 Pd-black 觸媒電極(Pd-loading: 204 µgcm-2；Nafion thickness: 8.0 µm)

vs. RHE 的區域可明顯地看出 Pd-black 對氫原子的吸附/脫附特性尖峰，其第一個吸附峰
(peak I)的位置大約在 0.07V，而與第二個吸附峰(peak II)的位置大約在 0.26V，分別表徵了
不同 Pd 晶面對氫原子的吸附特性。由氫原子吸/脫附峰曲線下的面積可知氫原子在 Pd-black
觸媒上的吸/脫附電荷量(QH)(Vielstich et al., 2003)，經換算後可得到電化學活性表面積
(EAS)，分別以相同條件製備 Pd-black 觸媒電極三次，並由 CV 實驗結果可得 Pd-black 觸媒
電極的 EAS 值為 22.8±0.1 m2g-1。

四、結論 
本研究以薄膜旋轉電極方法探討 Pd-black 觸媒在 0.5M H2SO4 或 1M HClO4 電解質溶液
中對甲酸分子的電催化氧化反應動力學。Pd-black 觸媒在 0.5M H2SO4溶液中所測得的電化
學活性表面積(EAS)為 22.8 m2g-1。在添加 0.1M 甲酸的 0.5M H2SO4電解質溶液中，Pd-black
觸媒之 Tafel 斜率為 154.8 mVdec-1。Pd-black 觸媒電極在 0.5M H2SO4 以及 1M HClO4 電解
質溶液中，利用循環伏安(CV)法所測得甲酸分子的擴散係數(D)在高掃描速度區(500~75 
mV/s)分別為 6.7×10-6 cm2s-1 與 5.1×10-6 cm2s-1；而在低掃描速度區(50~5 mV/s)則分別為
3.9×10-7 cm2s-1 與 1.1×10-7 cm2s-1，硫酸中的 D 值皆大於過氯酸。進一步以 i-t 法所求得甲酸
分子在 0.5M H2SO4 與 1M HClO4 中的 D 值分別為 4.0×10-6 cm2s-1 與 3.5×10-6 cm2s-1，與以
CV 法在高掃描速度區所求得之 D 值較為相近。 
本研究的結果有助於了解在酸性電解質溶液中的甲酸電催化氧化動力學，也有助於開
發鈀觸媒實際應用為直接甲酸燃料電池的陽極觸媒材料。

Effect of Chemisorbed Carbon Monoxide on Kinetics of Hydrogen Oxidation 
Reaction at Nafion-Coated Pt/C Rotating Disk Electrodes  
 
Ren-Bin Lina,*, Pei-Wen Hob, Shin-Min Shihb , Hung-Ta Lia, Wei-Liang Fangchianga 
aDepartment of Chemical and Materials Engineering, Chinese Culture University, Taipei, 11114, Taiwan 
bDepartment of Chemical Engineering, National Taiwan University, Taipei, 10617, Taiwan 
*Corresponding author: +886-2-28614011, lrb@faculty.pccu.edu.tw 
Abstract: The effects of chemisorbed carbon monoxide (CO) on the hydrogen oxidation reaction (HOR) on 
the Pt/C catalyst were investigated by the thin-film rotating disk electrode (RDE) methodology. By reducing 
the electroactive surface area of the catalyst through the adsorption of CO, the current density in the catalyst 
layer and thus the exchange current density (i0) for HOR on the catalyst can be measured. At the higher CO 
coverage, the HOR at the catalyst layer was the case of a mixture of reaction and diffusion control. The i0 
value of the 27.2wt% Pt/C in 0.5M H2SO4 was determined to be 5mA/cm2 (2A/mgPt). 
Keywords: Electrocatalysis, Hydrogen oxidation, Fuel cells.  
1. Introduction  
The development of new or modified catalysts for proton exchange membrane fuel cells (PEMFCs) 
requires the quantitative determination of their electrocatalytic activity. The catalytic activity of a high 
surface area catalyst and its electrochemical reaction kinetics had been studied by several investigators by 
using the RDE technique under fuel cells relevant conditions1. In our previous works2,3, the HOR kinetics at 
the Nafion-coated Pt/C or Pt-black electrodes was investigated using the RDE technique. However, the HOR 
at the Pt catalyst layer was proved to be reversible and the measurable current density was determined 
entirely by the mass transfer of hydrogen in the aqueous electrolyte and the Nafion film. In order to obtain 
accurate i0 using the RDE method, the Pt surface area per unit volume of Pt layer should be made very small. 
The purpose of this work was to obtain accurate i0 using the thin-film RDE methodology by reducing 
the electroactive surface of the catalyst through the adsorption of CO. The effects of chemisorbed CO on the 
HOR kinetics at Nafion-coated Pt/C electrodes were investigated. A theoretical description of the method for 
the determination of the kinetic parameters was presented, and the intrinsic i0 value was thus determined. 
2. Experimental  
Pt/C catalysts were prepared by using colloidal precursor technique4. The particle size of Pt for 
5.4~27.2 wt% Pt/C catalysts prepared, determined by XRD and TEM, was in the range of 3.1~8.9 nm, which 
are slightly larger than those of commercial Pt/C catalysts. The preparation procedures of the Nafion-coated 
Pt/C rotating disk electrode were similar to those described elsewhere2,3. Experiments for the oxidation of 
0.5% CO/H2 on the electrodes were performed in the reactant gas saturated 0.5 M H2SO4 electrolyte. The 
potential was hold at 0.05 V vs. RHE for various CO adsorption time intervals (tad), and then the potential of 
the electrode was changed between 0 and 0.2 V vs. RHE at a rate of 10 mV/s. The detailed procedures of the 
electrochemical measurement have been described elsewhere4.
