 一、 前言 
 
  鐵基形狀記憶合金的種類很多，有 FePt(1~3)、FePd(4)、FeNiCoTi(5、6)、FeNiC(7)、
FeMnSi
(8、9)等，其中又以 Fe-Mn-Si 基合金最受矚目。Fe-Mn-Si 基形狀記憶合金
由於製造成本相當低廉且有良好之加工性，故廣受學者的重視與研究。Fe-Mn-Si
基形狀記憶合金的應用，最具潛力的是製成套筒元件—即管接頭，用於水電管路、
化學管路、輸油管線、瓦斯管線等之接合。但由於其形狀記憶性能未臻於完美
(TiNi形狀記憶合金之形狀回復率為100 %，Fe-Mn-Si基合金最好只達90~95 %)，
故在工程應用上受到許多限制。若能結合製造成本低廉及良好加工性之優點，並
有效提升其形狀記憶性能，則 Fe-Mn-Si 基形狀記憶合金在工程應用上將可與
TiNi 形狀記憶合金相抗衡並廣泛被採用。增進合金形狀記憶性能的機構有許多種，
但目前絕大多數之文獻均只探討單一機構之效應，很少進行多種強化機構對增進
合金高記憶效應之研究。本研究計畫擬就添加稀土元素 RE 對 Fe-Mn-Si 基形狀
記憶合金之降低疊差能與晶粒細化雙重效應如何有效增進形狀記憶性能進行探
討。希望藉由本計畫之執行，可建立相關製程技術，完備降低疊差能與晶粒細化
之雙重強化效應對增進合金記憶性能之理論基礎，並開發多重強化型之高性能鐵
基形狀記憶合金。

3.4  RE 稀土元素對形狀回復率之影響 
 
  兩種合金經 20%冷滾軋後，再在 650℃退火之硬度值如表 4 所示，將表 4 之
數據以曲線表示如 Fig.11。合金均質化後經 20%之冷滾軋，將產生加工硬化，因
添加 RE 之合金可降低疊差能，滾軋過程將因產生大量疊差，有效阻礙差排之滑
移，故加工硬化更加顯著，其硬度值也明顯較高。如 Fig.6 中所示，Fe-Mn-Si-Cr-RE
合金經 20%冷滾軋後，在 650℃下 10min 即已完成再結晶，且從退火 10min 至 4
小時(見 Fig.10)，其晶粒因晶界相之存在，幾乎沒有晶粒成長。故表 4 或 Fig.11
中顯示，添加 RE 之合金，退火 30min 後，其硬度明顯下降，這是由於再結晶後，
原 20%冷加工之硬化效應已被消除，而退火 30min 至 240min，其硬度值僅些微
下降，是因為晶界析出物有效抑制晶粒成長之故。Fig.6 中亦顯示，Fe-Mn-Si-Cr
合金在 650℃下 20min 亦開始發生再結晶，而此合金在 650℃下退火 30min 之硬
度值與20%冷滾軋後相當，是由於(1)再結晶將消除冷加工硬化效應使硬度下降，
(2)再結晶後之晶粒細化所造成的強化效應將使硬度升高，此兩因素平衡之結果。
此合金退火期間，其硬度值僅略為下降也是因為晶粒幾乎沒有成長之故。

四、 結論 
 
1. 添加微量稀土元素 RE 可降低疊差能，使應力誘發麻田散鐵較易形成，
進而提升合金之形狀回復率。 
 
2. 添加 RE 因降低疊差能，合金經冷滾軋後將產生相對大量之疊差，有效
阻礙差排之滑移，使加工硬化更加顯著，晶粒內部應變能也相對較高，
故可促進再結晶更早發生。 
 
3. 添加 RE 在再結晶過程中，可促進晶界析出物更易發生，進而抑制晶粒
之成長，達到明顯之晶粒細化效果。 
 
4. 合金經 20%冷滾軋及 650℃退火後，Fe-Mn-Si-Cr-RE 因(1)降低疊差能及
(2)晶粒細化之雙重強化效應下，其形狀回復率明顯優於 Fe-Mn-Si-Cr 合
金，且可高達 95%以上。

1. Introduction 
The demands for renewable energy and environmental protection have continuously 
driven the development of fuel cells. Among the current fuel cell techniques, 
proton-exchange membrane fuel cells (PEMFC) is one of the most attractive power 
sources, especially for portable devices and vehicles due to the relatively lower operating 
temperatures and system volume [1-3]. The core of PEMFC is the proton conducting 
polymer electrolyte in the membrane electrode assembly (MEA). The requirements for a 
polymer electrolyte membrane used in PEMFCs are high proton conductivity, high 
stability against the attack of radical by-products and low cost. Currently, the Nafion® 
membrane, which was developed by DuPont Ltd. decades ago, is still the most adapted 
electrolytes either in academic researches or commercial applications [4-6]. In recent 
years, the numbers of researches in MEAs are gradually increased. Among these 
researches, three-phase boundaries (TPB) at the electrolyte-catalyst-electrode interfaces 
are most important due to the redox reaction of fuel cell occurring in the TPB [7-9]. The 
adhersion factors at the electrolyte-catalyst-electrode interfaces have a significant 
relationship to the resistance of the interfaces and thus fuel cell performances. There 
have been many reports regarding the surface modification including ion bombarding 
on the polymer electrolyte surface, which enhances the quality of catalyst on the 
membrane surface and thus increases the effective area of electrochemical reaction;

3. Results and Discussion 
The Fig.1a-d showed the morphologies of Nafion®117 through by ion bombardment 
under different condition. The profile of Nafion®117 had similar to valley caused by ion 
bombardment. The roughness of Nafion®117 was increased with the increasing of rf 
power. The average roughness of N-00, BO2-10W, BO2-20W and BO2-30W were 0.8nm, 
9.26nm, 38.42nm and 117.63nm show in Table.2, and which roughness was increasing as 
rf power increased. It proved the ion bombardment can increase specific surface area of 
Nafion®117.  Catalyst layer coated by self-breathing after ion bombardment showed in 
Fig.3a-d. The profile of Nafion®117 has become rough after ion bombardment, and 
which process can make surface from 2-dimension structure become 3-dimension 
structure. However, the 3-dimension structure can increased effective area, and enhance 
catalyst layer efficiency. Figure.4a-d showed C1s spectrum at the N00, BO2-10W, 
BO2-20W, BO2-30W, and identified C-C, C-S, C-O, COOH, CF2, CF3 and CF-O bonding, 
etc. [14-17] The COOH (289.3 eV), CF2 (291.6 eV) and CF3 (293.2 eV) was reduced with 
the increasing of rf power. However, C-S peak has increase in BO2-10W, because the 
main chain fluorocarbon, it will first be ion bombardment. As the rf power increases, the 
C-S peak was significantly reduced in BO2-20W, even in BO2-30W were disappearance. 
The C-S, COOH, CF2, CF3 peak were destroyed with the increasing of rf power, 
therefore C-dangling bonds [14] were produced. C-dangling bond with the oxygen, 
making the C-O (287.3 eV) and CF-O (292 eV), which were increasing as rf power 
increased. The properties of C-O and CF-O were hydrophilic [14]. It will help catalyst 
layer coated by self-breathing.
