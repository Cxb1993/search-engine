Abstract 
 
Keywords: Isotropic disk, Digital Photoelasticity,  Stress, Material fringe value. 
 
The full- field stress analytical solution of isotropic disk subjects to distributed compression on 
a finite area at rim had been derived by Hung and Ma, and it has a good agreement between 
theory and experiment of Digital Photoelasticity by estimating distributed compression on a small 
area at rim. Two methods are recommended to determine material fringe value on Photoelasticity 
experiment, one is tensile experiment method, and the other is circular disk loaded in diametral 
compression method. The circular disk is somewhat easier to machine and to load than the tensile
