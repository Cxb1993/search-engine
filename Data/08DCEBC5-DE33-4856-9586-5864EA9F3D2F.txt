奈米碳管強化無電鍍鎳複合鍍層之複合微動磨損之研究
計畫編號：NSC 96-2221-E-150-055-
執行期限：96 年 08 月 01 日至 97 年 07 月 31 日
主持人：黃 社 振
1. 中文摘要
微動磨損常以耐磨鍍層增強其磨耗性能，由於奈米碳管具有獨特
之物理、化學性質和機械性質，因此配置奈米奈米碳管作為強化無電
鍍鎳複合鍍層之微動磨耗與疲勞磨潤性能是工業界需求殷切之研究
工作。本研究以添加奈米碳管藉由不同分散相 Sic、Al 2 O 3等微米（固
體微粒）散佈強化於表面的無電鍍鎳鍍層，在邊界潤滑狀態下，以高
性能微動磨潤測試儀，在不同接觸負荷、接觸幾何、速度、振幅和不
同溫度下，研究其奈米效應之微動磨耗機理，由此建立奈米碳管配置
准則及分析微動磨耗模式。
研究動機與目的
奈米碳管具有特殊的電子及機械性質，因此他的生長及應用像炸彈爆炸一
樣，大幅的開展出來。奈米碳管可以是金屬或半導體，因此很容易形成金屬/半
導體或半導體/半導體異質接面元件。另外由於它具有極高的張力強度、楊氏係
數及其他機械性質，因此它可以運用在高強度複合材料上。無電鍍鎳為一氧化還
原且自身催化反應，當還原劑將鍍液中的鎳離子還原成鎳金屬，第一層鎳金屬析
出即可連續催化無電鍍鎳反應直到鍍液壽命停止。因此無電鍍鎳的膜厚與析鍍時
間成正比。若選擇次磷酸鈉為還原劑，所得的無電鍍鎳複合鍍層為鎳磷合金層，
由於磷在鎳的固容量很低，過飽和的磷會破壞鎳的晶體結構，隨著磷含量的變化
由微結晶、非晶質和兩相共存的組織均可能出現。隨著科技的進步，人們對於材
料的要求越來越高，而以往單一的材料往往無法兼具各項要求。結合兩種或兩種
以上材料所形成的複合材料，通常具有各材料的優點且彌補其缺點，因此廣泛被
利用。利用電化學或化學方法將金屬與不溶性固體微粒共同沉積而獲得複合材料
層得方法稱為複合鍍，藉此選擇不同性質的固體微粒可獲得不同功能性的複合鍍
膜。在原材表面披覆一層異質材料的表面鍍層處理方法包括物理氣相沉積(PVD)、
化學氣相沉積(CVD)、電鍍與無電鍍(析鍍)等等，其中無電鍍鎳表面處理具有易
操作、成本低且具有良好的膜厚均勻性及低孔隙度等特性，經常被應用於耐蝕性
和耐磨耗的環境，因此已廣泛應用在許多工業中。例如硬碟碟片表面硬化層、半
導體製程的銅導線擴散障礙層、另外在汽車工業、航空工業也有許多應用實例。
微動磨耗(fretting wear )是接觸兩表面受到振動或交互變動應力作用下因微小
振幅(微米級)之相對運動而引起磨擦磨耗之破壞模式[1]。易引起構件之咬合、
噪音，加速疲勞裂紋之產生、擴展與斷裂，降低疲勞壽命，甚至導致整個系統破
壞[1-4]。在精密機械、航空、電力(高空電纜和核電)、甚至人工器官植入等領
域之精密配合件中[3-5]，已成為關鍵零組件為主要破壞形式之工業癌症。為了
減少或消除微動破壞，有使用各種潤滑劑，表面修飾技術和鍍層等減緩方[6-15]。
隨著現代工業快速發展，對關鍵零組件之壽命、精密度及可靠度趨更嚴密之要求，

1. Introduction
Thermally sprayed WC–Co coatings are widely used to protect the engineering
surfaces against a variety of condition which include fretting, sliding, abrasion and
erosion wear resistant[1~18].
High-velocity oxy-fuel (HVOF) process has shown very successful in spraying
wear resistant WC–Co coatings , because the higher velocities and lower temperatures
experienced by the powder particles (as compared to plasma based routes) result in
less decomposition of the WC during spraying [1,3]. Consequently, this results in
higher density, superior bonding strength and less decarburisation. However,
high-temperature flame jet for heating and acceleration, decarburisation of WC may
decompose to W2C, zYx CWCo and the other amorphous phase or complex carbides
such as Co3W3C, Co2W4C, Co6W6C and Co3W9C4 [4,5] Several studies [6-10] have
attempted to relate the spraying parameters and the powder characteristics to the
microstructural features and the wear resistance of the coatings. Such an approach
aims at optimizing the spraying conditions to obtain an increased wear resistance.
The present study was to investigate and to compare Microstructure and
Tribological Properties of the thermal Sprayed WC-(Co, Ni, Cr) Coatings, obtained by
HVOF technology (High Velocity Oxy-Fuel). Experiments using a tribometer (ball
on disc configuration) under lubricated and dry conditions have been performed in
order to evaluate the friction and wear properties of the different coatings.

Abstract
Surface modification are often contain various additives to enhance
frettingwear performance. Nanometer tubes possess some special
physical 、 chemical and mechanical properties. Therefore,
Understanding their preparation and fretting wear and fatigue
performance of nanometer tubes as lubricating additives is essential
for the formulation of composite coatings.
This research is aimed to prepare carbon nanotubes using
electroless Ni composite coatings method and to study its nanometer
self-repairing effect tribological mechanism as reinforced additives in
boundary lubrication, were evaluated using a fretting wear tester at
various contact load, frequency, slip amplitude and temperature, and
thereby provide guidelines and analysis model of the formulation of
fretting wear performance for these designs.
Key word: carbon nanotube , electroless Ni, fretting wear

之理論能完整地闡述微動破壞之整個過程，因此其研究發展尚有很大之空間與潛
力。以添加奈米碳管的散佈強化應用電鍍鎳複合鍍層於之微動磨耗之研究，在國
內外也尚未見有報導。因此期能對其微動磨耗機構與微動疲勞能有新的認識與瞭
解。本研究實驗擬使用高性能之 SRV 微動試驗機，如圖一所示，在不同接觸幾
何模式、負荷、微動位移、頻率參數下，量測磨擦力，磨耗體積，經由試驗模擬
建立運行微動圖和材料塗層反應微動圖，確定微動數學模式。
二、實驗過程與方法
1. 奈米碳管強化無電鍍鎳複合鍍層方法
奈米碳管強化無電鍍鎳複合鍍層實驗係使用無電鍍鎳方法法添加氧化鋁、氧
化鋁加奈米碳管及碳化矽加奈米碳管固體微粒共沉積，試片前處理為鹽酸與純水
以 9:1 比例酸洗 3 min，將還原劑、安定劑、主要鹽類及緩衝劑依序加入純水中
以磁石攪拌，將 pH 直調至 9，加溫至 80 度，取適當固體微粒粉末加入無電鍍液
中，以數位攪拌器攪拌，使固體微粒粉末全部懸浮，並控制鍍液溫度，將已處理
的試片置入鍍液中析鍍，並控制析鍍時間，得到最佳無電鍍鎳複合鍍層厚度及最
佳粒子分佈狀況後取出其實驗參數，如表一所示。析鍍完的無電鍍鎳層是非晶形
構造，係磷溶解在微細晶狀鎳粒內，呈層狀排列，真空熱處理至 400 度一小時後，
有 Ni3P 晶相析出，使得鍍層表面硬度提高。

四、結論與建議:
本研究使用奈米碳管強化無電鍍鎳複合鍍層多功能磨潤試驗儀與高性能 SRV 微
動磨耗試驗儀以評估複合鍍層之微動磨擦與磨耗性能，綜合本研究其結果與建議如
下:
1.經由實驗可以得知在 400℃氬氣保護下熱處理一小時後，複合鍍層之無電鍍鎳
由非晶質結構轉換成結晶結構，因此鍍層硬度提高。
2. Sic+CNT/EN 和 Al 2 O 3+CNT/EN 以奈米碳管強化明顯比 Sic/EN、Al 2 O 3/EN、
複合鍍層，可提高鍍層硬度明顯增強磨耗性能。
3. 有系統地以實驗設計規劃建立反應曲面圖，由此建立資料庫做為材料設計之
參考。奈米碳管強化無電鍍鎳複合鍍層之摩擦係數隨摩擦行程與微動行程沒顯
著變化但卻隨負荷之增加而減少，是否奈米碳管作用產生摩擦係數降低作用值
得進一步詳細探討其產生磨擦機理。

Microstructure and Tribological Properties of HVOF Sprayed
WC-(Co, Ni, Cr) Coatings
Pi-Chuen Tsai a Shen-Jenn Hwang ,*b Jiing-Herng Lee a
a Department of Materials Science and Engineering, National Formosa University
b Department of Mechanical Design Engineering, National Formosa University
E-mail: hwang6@nfu.edu.tw
Abstract
In this study, four types of tungsten carbide base coatings with various binder
compositions were deposited on steel substrate by using a high velocity oxygen fuel
(HVOF) spraying technique. The coating microstructure was examined using optical
microscopy (OM), scanning electron microscopy (SEM), X-ray diffractometry (XRD)
and microhardness tester. The tribological properties of these coatings were evaluated
by the use of a ball on disk and block on ring experimental equipment. The friction
coefficient, wear rate, electrical resistance and microstructures of the test surfaces
were studied to evaluate the wear properties. An energy-dispersive spectroscopy
(EDS) equipped to SEM was used to analyze the elements of the coating surfaces in
an effort to relate the coating structure to tribological performance. The results show
that coatings containing Ni and/or Cr gave a superior tribological performance than
the others.

5. Conclusions
The microstructure and tribological behaviour of four kinds of cermet
HVOF-sprayed coatings have been studied. The results show that all HVOF-sprayed
coatings containing Ni and/or Cr gave a superior tribological performance than the
others. The HVOF-sprayed coating structure, wear track, and counterbody
investigation contributes in better understanding the coating wear performance.
