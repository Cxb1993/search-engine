The pitch plane linear tangent guidance can guide the launch vehicle back 
to its nominal flight. For the same simulation in the yaw plane, the 
disturbance force is in the local horizontal plane and pendicular to the 
velocity vector. It causes the heading angle to deviate from the nominal 
value. The yaw plane linear guidance can eliminate the deviation and guide 
the launch vehicle back to its nominal flight. Then in the three-dimensional 
linear tangent guidance simulation, a three-dimensional normal disturbance 
force is considered. Simulation results show that the three-dimensional 
linear tangent guidance law can guide the launch vehicle back to the 
nominal trajectory. Consequently, the purpose of precise orbit insertion 
can be achieved. 
Finally, the payload maximization problem for three-dimensional 
linear tangent guidance law is studied by using the parameter optimization 
method. When the mass of payload is increased, it will change the final 
inertial speed, the final flight path angle, and the final altitude. The ascent 
trajectory of the launch vehicle is deviated from the nominal trajectory. 
The linear tangent guidance law is then imposed to guide the launch 
vehicle from the deviated trajectory back to the nominal trajectory. Again, 
the purpose of precise orbit insertion can be achieved. The maximum 
satellite mass obtained is 1,200 kg. The case with satellite mass 
decreaseing is also studied. More understanding on the scope of 
application of the linear tangent guidance law has been obtained.

three-dimensional linear tangent guidance, optimal linear tangent guidance,

四、導引法則發展概況
 
    1972年的導引法則發展狀況如表4.1所示[1]。表2.1所列當時已經
在使用之三種導引法則，∆導引法則已經查不到進一步發展的文獻，
顯式導引法則(explicit guidance law)則仍然被廣泛研究；當時尚未開始
使用的三種導引法則，目前都已經逐漸進入使用階段。顯式導引法則
最初是由Duncan MacPherson推導出來的[2]，基本原理是求牛頓方程式
的顯示解或稱解析解，採逐節瞄準的導引方式，最後一節則瞄準衛星
入軌點。後續的發展如參考資料[3-8]所示。 
數值積分導引法則(numerical integration guidance law)：當初是使
用雷達量測資料來進行導引計算，因此計算量大而且計算速度要快，
使用雷達量測資料可以在地面執行計算，再將導引命令傳送到發射載
具，目前這些計算都可以在衛星上執行，主要參考資料為[9-10]。  
線性正切線導引法則最初由F.M. Perkins推導出來，如參考資料
[11]，當時並未開始使用，最近的研究實例如參考資料[12]，其優點是
不必每一節火箭去設定瞄準點；之後還有雙線性切線導引法則
(bilinear-tangent guidance law)的發展。 
雙線性正切偏航導引法則(bilinear-tangent yaw guidance law)：本導
引法則曾經被應用於美國高能天文實驗室(High Energy Astronomy 
Observatory)衛星的發射，為一種參數偏航把舵法則(parametric yaw 
steering law)，所使用的發射載具是Atlas/Centaur，它提供了發射載具
近乎最佳的偏航把舵操控，可以達到預先設定的軌道傾角與節點的入
軌，而如果是在均勻的地球引力場情況下，可以證明出來在俯仰平面
與偏航平面都是最佳的把舵法則，在適當條件可以簡化為線性正切法
則與等姿態法則(constant attitude law)。在參考資料[13]中，對於本法
則在旋轉座標系統內的運動方程式如何即時積分(real-time 
integration)，以及如何將程式植入飛行電腦，都有詳細介紹，其參數
導引方程式的顯式解(explicit solution)，必須在發射的飛行過程中，即
時求解兩個兩點邊界值問題(two-point boundary value problem)，也就是
在俯仰平面與偏航平面，其導引結果非常好，即使飛行方向與偏航平
面之角度已經達到50度，仍然能夠導引回來。 
近似最佳導引法則(approximate optimal guidance law)：本導法則使
用展開技術(expansion technique)來推導，其運動方程式有包括地球自
轉的影響，當假設發射載具推力與地球引力遠大於氣動力時，可以求
得Hamilton-Jacobi-Bellman方程式(或稱動態程式化方程式，dynamic 
programming equation)第零次項(zeroth-order term)的解，若把慣性力與
氣動力涵蓋進來，就必須求解展開式之較高次項的解，可以從第一階
線性偏微分方程式，經積分求得，因此可以在飛行中即時求得近似最
佳解，可用以建構發射載具之導引法則。[14]

九、從台灣發射之俯仰面 (垂直面 )的線性正切導引  
本章模擬從台灣發射之俯仰面（垂直面）的線性正切導引，此處
之垂直干擾力是在俯仰面上垂直於速度向量，正干擾力可以使飛行路
徑角增加，負干擾力可以使飛行路徑角減低；「較小」是以約一度之推
力不正來衡量，因此假設第一節火箭之垂直干擾力為48,000牛頓，第
二節火箭之垂直干擾力為8,000牛頓，第三節火箭之垂直干擾力為1,800
牛頓；「較大」則是以約兩度之推力不正來衡量，因此假設第一節火箭
之垂直干擾力為96,000牛頓，第二節火箭之垂直干擾力為16,000牛頓，
第三節火箭之垂直干擾力為3,600牛頓。本章第一節為引言，第二節為
俯仰面受較小正垂直干擾力之干擾軌道，第三節為俯仰面受較小正干
擾力加線性正切導引之軌道，第四節為俯仰面受較小負干擾力加線性
正切導引之軌道，第五節為俯仰面受較大正干擾力加線性正切導引之
軌道，第六節為俯仰面受較大負干擾力加線性正切導引之軌道。

壹、目的 
國際太空年會之英文名稱為 International Astronautical 
Congress，簡稱 IAC，由三個國際學術機構共同發起：國際太空聯
盟(International Astronautical Federation，簡稱 IAF)、國際宇宙航行
學院(International Academy of Astronautics，簡稱 IAA)與國際太空
法律學會(International Institute of Space Law，簡稱 IISL)，總部均
設在巴黎，年會每年由不同的城市主辦，會期六天。第一屆 IAC 於
1950 年在法國巴黎舉行，最近幾年已經舉辦或將舉辦的主辦城市分
別為：2007 年第 58 屆海德巴拉(印度)、2008 年第 59 屆格拉斯哥(蘇
格蘭)、2009 年第 60 屆大田(韓國)、2010 年第 61 屆布拉格(捷克)、
2011 年第 62 屆開普敦(南非)。 
中華民國航空太空學會成立於 1958 年 6 月 22 日，當時的會名
是「中國太空航行學會」(Astronautical Society of the Republic of 
China)。為保持與國際太空航行組織之密切聯繫與學術交流，本會成
立後即派員列席 1958年 8月國際太空聯盟(IAF)假荷蘭阿姆斯特丹舉
行之第九屆年會，並申請加入該聯盟，當時即獲得通過本會代表中華
民國為國際太空聯盟之會員，迄今已經 51 年。後來國內航空工業開
始發展，大專院校也紛紛開設了航空工程科系，為迎合時代的需要，
在 1970 年的年會中通過改名為「中國航空太空學會」 (Aeronautical 
and Astronautical Society of the Republic of China，簡稱 AASRC)，
並經內政部社字第 41075 號通知奉准備案，一直沿用至 2007 年 6
月。為了使得中英文名稱具有一致性，於第 48 屆年會暨會員大會會
議通過，修正本學會中文名稱為「中華民國航空太空學會」，並於 2007
年 6 月經內政部奉准更名及換發立案證書台內社字第 0960086857
號。 
中華民國航空太空學會自從加入 IAF 為會員後，每年均按時繳交
年費，並組團出席 IAC，發表論文及與世界各國太空專家、學者進行
溝通與交流，近二十年來更有多位會員獲選為學術委員會委員、國際
議程委員會委員、論文研討會主持人，以及獲選為 IAA 院士，是 IAF
的優秀會員之一，唯一直未曾主辦過 IAC。民國 95 年(2006 年)，陳
正興會員首先在理監事聯席會議提出建議，爭取主辦 IAC，經討論後
決議交由國際事務委員會辦理，因此由當時的理事長李羅權博士率
隊，在第 57 屆 IAC 於瓦倫西亞(西班牙)舉行時，展開初步拜會活動。
民國 96 年(2007 年)，李羅權理事長率學會相關人員拜會國科會、外
交部、教育部、台北市政府、台北市電腦公會等單位，尋求支持與協

二、九十六年度情形概述 
這一年將競標的是 2010 年第 61 屆年會的主辦城市，由於我們
事先已經知道將輪回歐洲舉辦，因此未準備投標，以觀摩與吸收經驗
為主；競標的城市有兩個：捷克布拉格與突尼西亞突尼斯，前者是捲
土重來，準備比以前充分，後者則是被邀請來的，因為 IAC 一直還
沒有在非洲舉辦過，大家都很希望近幾年能夠到非洲舉辦一次。也由
於突尼斯是被邀請來的，所以沒有做太多的準備，結果自然由捷克布
拉格勝出。事實上，捷克布拉格的勝出也早在意料之中，因為 IAF高
層團隊(IAF Bureau)中有一位重量級人物 Vladimir Kopal 為捷克籍，
他常年以來一直是 IAF 的法律總顧問(General Counsel)。

肆、建議 
中華民國航空太空學會從去年申辦 2011 年第 62 屆 IAC，到今年
參加第 60 屆 IAC 的經驗，大致可以歸納出下列三點建議：

switches the satellite to spare side and keeps it at acquisition, safety and hold (ASH) 
mode until the trouble shooting and recovery procedures commanded from ground 
have been completed. 
Generally speaking, FS2 is still at very good condition in its SOH. One of the

the three gyros remain operating smoothly. The battery of the FS2 has some 
degradation for several percent. This degradation is within and below the design 
prediction. There is also some degradation in the charged coupled device (CCD) of 
RSI.  
Three topics will be addressed in this paper: 1) FS2’s global contribution and

Especially, the mangrove can mitigate the effect of tsunami disaster. ﹝7﹞ There are 
few comparable protected coastlines within the tropics as pristine as St Lucia's.

2.4 ATMOSPHERIC TRANSIENT LUMINOUS EVENTS (TLES) 
A total of 11403 TLEs with global distribution as shown in Figure 4 have been

the first instrument dedicated to the TLEs observation from space, ISUAL has been 
providing a great wealth of images and photometric data to study TLEs and lightening. 
The ISUAL science team consists of space physics scientists from NCKU, UCB, 
Tohoku University and many other institutes has been analyzing these observation

3.3 Gyro Failure 
There are four gyros in the IRU with one of them as spare. After about two and half 
years in orbit, much higher noise level had been found in gyro A. Two AROs had been 
triggered by the high noise level. Therefore, gyro A was replaced by the spare one 
gyro D. It means no more spare gyro is available so far. Figure 7 shows the trending 
of X-axis gyro drift in 5 years. The discontinuity point in the plot is the time the 
replacement was made. Gyros of the other two axes are pretty much the same. 
Specification for FS2’s gyro drift is 3 deg/hr. It illustrates a very good stability of the 
gyro. Since gyro drift is the main performance index of IRU, it can be concluded that 
IRU has excellent performance with no degradation except the failure of gyro A. 
 
3.4 Battery Depth of Discharge (DOD) 
 
The maximum DOD (Max_DOD) of battery per day merged with its average value 
per month in the past 5 years is shown in Figure 8. Most of the Max_DOD values stay 
between 16 ~ 22% with some transient peaks up to 23%. Since the anticipation value 
is within 25%, the global ranges and the local peaks are all within the expectation. 
The seasonal effect can be seen from the DOD behavior tendency. While the solar 
flux in winter solstice is higher than in summer solstice, the satellite thermal 
consumption demand will be less in winter. The annual oscillation cycle demonstrates 
this seasonal effect. However, the DOD evolution is not only influenced by the season, 
but also considerably affected by the mission scenario of RSI (operates in sunlight 
side) and ISUAL (operates in eclipse side). 
 
4. FS2’S POST MISSIOM LIFE PLAN 
 
4.1 Life Extension for FS2 
Currently this is the most important issue: to extend the working life of FS2. Taiwan’s 
second remote sensing satellite is still at very early stage in its development. The most 
optimistic possible launch time is in 2013. Therefore it is expected that FS2’s working 
life can be sustained to that time. 
Based on the above SOH trending, the expectation could be achieved if the 
noise level of gyro does not cause further problem. The degradation in battery 
performance is slower than estimation. There is still more than 50 kg propellant 
onboard for orbit keeping. The RSI is still in very good condition while the ISUAL 
has very limited degradation. All these factors are advantageous to the working life 
 21 
another several years. Post mission life plan includes life extension, global coverage 
consideration, and the study of gyroless attitude control with life extension as the first 
priority.

ABSTRACT 
This paper presents the design of a 30 kg class experimental microsatellite TUUSAT-1B by a 
group of professors and students from 5 universities in Taiwan. It was more than 10 years ago 
the Taiwan Universities-United Team (TUUT) devoted to the microsatellite development was 
organized by a small group of professors and students coming from several domestic 
universities. At the beginning, TUUT established the basic technologies required for 
microsatellite development using very limited resources of manpower and budget under the 
support of the National Science Council (NSC) of Taiwan. Then the National Space 
Organization (NSPO) of Taiwan supported TUUT to develop and manufacture the Taiwan 
Universities-United Satellite No. 1A (TUUSAT-1A) from 2005 to 2007. It is the engineering 
model (EM) of an experimental microsatellite. Through the cooperation with NSPO, the team 
has built its very solid and mature technological capabilities in microsatellite development and 
manufacture. Therefore, NSPO intends to support TUUT the design and development of a 
flight model (FM) named TUUSAT-1B in the coming 3 years from 2010 to 2012. As an 
experimental microsatellite, there are 3 payloads, 4 instruments, and 5 verification missions. 
Thus it is also called a 3-4-5 experimental satellite (345ES). Although for many years, the 
members of TUUT have been changed due to temporal reasons. However, the core team 
members keep almost unchanged. Besides, the technological heritage of the whole team is 
well maintained. Currently there are 9 professors and 18 students from the following 5 
universities: China University of Science and Technology, Tamkang University, Taiwan Ocean 
University, Formosa University, and Chiayi University. All team members have very high 
interest and enthusiasm in the microsatellite development and test.

3. FIVE VERIFICATION MISSIONS OF TUUSAT-1B 
The five subjects to be verified by developing and launching TUUSAT-1B are as follows: 
1) Functions of TSLV: to verify that the launch is successful, to verify the dynamical 
environmental conditions of TSLV, etc. 
2) Capabilities of TSLV: to verify the payload weight which TSLV can launch, to verify the 
accuracy of orbit insertion of TSLV, etc. 
3) Applicability of COTS in space: to verify the COTS parts used onboard TUUSAT-1B are 
suitable for space application, including the commercial GPS receiver, etc.  
4) Academic and commercial value of TUUSAT-1B: to verify that TUUSAT-1B is academically 
and commercially worth to develop, etc.  
5)  Capability and capacity of developing and manufacturing satellite in the academic 
community in Taiwan: to verify the capability of TUUT established in Taiwan’s academic 
community devoted to the research and development (R&D) of microsatellite, to verify if the 
capacity of TUUT along with another group established in Cheng Kung University can meet 
NSPO’s requirements, etc.  
      These five subjects can be considered as the missions of TUUSAT-1B.

4.3 Thermal Control Subsystem (TCS) 
All satellites encounter extremely severe temperature which could be from -150˚ C to +150˚
C when orbiting in space. We need to design a thermal control subsystem to ensure the 
temperature is within safety range and all onboard equipments can operate normally. The 
thermal equilibrium equation of a satellite is 
sun er earth i u eq q q q q q+ + + = +
         (1) 
where sunq = thermal energy from the sun, erq = the thermal energy from the albedo, 
earthq = the thermal energy from the Earth’s infrared radiation, iq = the thermal energy 
generated inside the satellite, uq = the internal thermal energy change of the satellite, and 
eq = the thermal energy radiated to space from the satellite. In order to save the electric 
power, passive thermal control method shall be used. General procedures for thermal control 
design is as follows: 
1) To determine the requirements and constraints; 
2) To determine an equivalent sphere with surface area equals to the surface area of the 
satellite; 
3) To select the radiation characteristics of the surface;  
4) To compute the worst case hot and cold temperatures and compare with the working 
temperature ranges of equipments; 
5) To select the thermal control method and materials; 
6) To determine if there are special thermal control problems; 
7) To estimate the subsystem weight, cost, and power required; 
8) To document the reasons of selections; and 
9) To do simulations and check the design.

      The FORMOSAT-2 (or FS2, Formosa satellite #2, was previously called ROCSAT-2 
or RS2, Republic of China satellite #2) is the first remote sensing satellite (RSS) owned by 
Taiwan for the purposes of earth and upward lightning observation. It is a small satellite of 
746 kg mass for two remote sensing missions. Its mission orbit is sun-synchronous of 888.47 
km altitude for exactly 14 revolutions per day. The design life and mission life are 7 and 5 
years, respectively. For earth observation, the payload is an advanced high resolution remote 
sensing instrument (RSI) with ground sampling distance (GSD) 2 m in panchromatic (PAN) 
band and 8 m in four multi-spectral (MS) bands. For upward lightning observation, the 
payload is an imager of sprite and upper atmospheric lightning (ISUAL).﹝5-18﹞It was 
launched from Vandenberg, California on 20 May 2004. In other words, FS2 has entered its 
7th year mission operations (MO). 
     As a high resolution Earth observation satellite (EOS), FS2’s typical mission operations 
in disaster observation consist of the following processes:  
1) Acquisition of information: to know the type and place of the occurrence of disaster. 
2) Settlement of observation strategy: to pinpoint the observation area and arrange the 
satellite time slot for observation, including weather check of the observation area.  
3) Programming of command: to transform the observation strategy to command program 
that can be uploaded to FORMOSAT-2. 
4) Command uploading: to upload the command to FORMOSAT-2. 
5) Disaster observation: to observe the disaster and take images.  
6) Image downloading: to download the observed images. 
7) Image processing and distribution: to process the observed images and deliver to the 
rescue agency.

Cospas-Sarsat alert and location data.  
      The System is composed of:  
- about one million distress beacons operating at 406 MHz; 
- SAR payloads on 11 satellites in low-altitude Earth orbit and in geostationary orbit; 
- seventy-six ground receiving stations spread around the world;  
- a network of 30 Mission Control Centres to distribute distress alerts and location 
information to SAR authorities, worldwide. 
      After 28 years of successful operation, the Cospas-Sarsat System is entering an era of 
rapid evolution with the planned integration of new satellite constellations in medium-altitude 
Earth orbit. This evolution is challenging, both technically and from a management 
perspective. It is also expected to open opportunities for significant enhancements to the 
services provided to SAR authorities. There are about one million beacons currently in use 
worldwide and this population has been increasing at a swift rate in the last few years (+25% 
in 2008). Further information about the Programme and the System is available on the 
Cospas-Sarsat website at www.cospas-sarsat.org.  
      It is seen in Figure 2 that the Cospas-Sarsat System includes two types of satellites: 1) 
satellites in low-altitude Earth orbit (LEO) which form the LEOSAR System; and 2) satellites 
in geostationary Earth orbit (GEO) which form the GEOSAR System . 
      Cospas-Sarsat has demonstrated that the GEOSAR and LEOSAR system capabilities 
are complementary. For example the GEOSAR system can provide almost immediate alerting 
in the footprint of the GEOSAR satellite, whereas the LEOSAR system: 
- provides coverage of the polar regions (which are beyond the coverage of geostationary 
satellites); 
- can calculate the location of distress events using Doppler processing techniques; 
- is less susceptible to obstructions which may block a beacon signal in a given direction 
because the satellite is continuously moving with respect to the beacon.

4. GERSS 
Now, we have the COSPAS-SARSAT system, UNITAR, UNOSAT, and GEOSS, it is a proper 
time to consider the establishment of the GERSS. Technically it would be very feasible.  
      For the so many times FORMOSAT-2 had made contribution by providing Earth 
observation images, the shortest time duration from information acquisition to image 
provision is 7 hr. There are just one RSS FORMOSAT-2 and two ground stations Taiwan and 
Kiruna. The time distribution is as follows:   
1) Acquisition of information about the type and place of the occurrence of disaster: 0.5 hr. 
2) Settlement of observation strategy to pinpoint the observation area and arrange the 
satellite time slot for observation, including weather check of the observation area: 0.5 hr.  
3) Programming of command to transform the observation strategy to command program 
that can be uploaded to FORMOSAT-2: 0.5 hr. 
4) Command uploading to FORMOSAT-2: 0.5 hr. 
5) Disaster observation, image taking and downloading: 2.5 hr. 
6) Image processing and distribution: 2.5 hr. 
7) Initiation of SAR: not applicable 
With the establishment of GRESS, there should be many RSSs as well as many ground 
stations. Almost all processes could be done within the shortest time. Therefore, with good 
and smooth coordination as a prerequisite condition, the estimation of required time in each 
process from disaster occurrence to SAR initiation could be as follows:  
1) Acquisition of information about the type and place of the occurrence of disaster: 5 min. 
2) Settlement of observation strategy to pinpoint the observation area and arrange the 
satellite time slot for observation, including weather check of the observation area: 5 min.  
3) Programming of command to transform the observation strategy to command program 
that can be uploaded to FORMOSAT-2: 10 min. 
4) Command uploading to FORMOSAT-2: 20 min. 
5) Disaster observation, image taking and downloading: 30 min.
