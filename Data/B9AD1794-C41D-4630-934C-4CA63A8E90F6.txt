一、計畫之目的。 
由於奈米銀粒子其低電阻(1.47× 10-8 Ωm)之性質常調製成導電膠，應用於混
成積體電路組件(hybrid integrated circuitry)和多層陶瓷電容器之金屬批覆
(metallization of multiplayer ceramic capacitor)及其他厚膜用途上，除調製成導
電膠外，在織物或材料表面塗佈銀鈀薄層或摻入銀鈀奈米粒子，可製作隱形材
料。由於奈米微粉之高表面積比，使其具備高活性和高反應性之特質，故可用於
銀鈀金屬電極及高催化性觸媒反應之用途上。另外，將銀鈀奈米粒子塗佈在多孔
陶瓷或不鏽鋼基材上，可製備具有高氫氣選透性之無機薄膜，作為氫氣純化、分
離或觸媒膜反應(membrane reaction)之素材。這項研究能應用於燃料電池的開
發，也是我們開發這項計畫的重點。 
奈米銀具有許多的好處，但是這項優點有賴於粒徑與幾何形狀的控制，因此決
定以Lab-on a chip的觀念發展微形奈米銀製程晶片，以電脈衝法制成奈米粒子文
獻中已經被證實可行例如，以金做為觸媒在鹼性環境下之固液界面，對甲醇氧化
的催化活性跟奈米顆粒尺寸大小間的影響作探討。以電化學脈衝還原於碳基材製
備奈米金顆粒，前驅物的選用為三氯化金(III)，將其還原至零價的金屬態。改
變脈衝參數的時間及電位及supporting electrolyte的濃度，製備出所期望的高分
散度及粒徑分佈範圍集中的奈米金顆粒。並將製備好的奈米金顆粒應用在電催化
甲醇及一氧化碳氧化的反應，測試其電催化活性效能。以旋轉圓盤作為電化學電
極，分析奈米金觸媒在鹼性系統中對電化學氧化甲醇及CO的動力學行為。因此
將金顆粒利用脈衝的方式還原在RDE的glassy carbon-disc平板電極上，來達到控
制金顆粒大小及分佈，進而探討奈米金觸媒在鹼性系統中對氧化甲醇及CO的動
力學行為。而且此項設計能使奈米生成所期望的基材, 能有利於發展燃料電池的
薄膜, 甚至可以應用於其他金屬如白金奈米管等，這也是我們所研發的目的。 
主，晶片採用三層結構設計，當藥劑充分混合後便進入反應區進行反應。

Abstract
A novel TiO2 was coated on the module surface to achieve the self-cleaning purpose, and several
multi-layer SiNx thin films with lower reflectance indexes in various wave length ranges were coated to
increase anti-reflective effect. The first dual SiNx multilayer was formed by adjusting the Al/N2 ratio
during deposition process, and the third TiO2 layer is formed by sol-gel method. The triple-layer structure
increases the average transmittance, during the visible light range of 400 to 900 nm, successfully up to
95.4 compared with low-iron glass (91%). This kind of AR glass with antireflection coating have three
advantages, including lower UV damage, higher visible transmittance and self-cleaning. The detail of
analysis and fabrication of AR multi-layer is discussed in this paper.
1. Introduction
It is well known that the optic structure will influence the efficiency of a solar cell, especially, thin film
solar cell. All thin film solar module are depend on the texture structure of Transparent Conducting Oxide
(TCO) film to achieve antireflection effect [1]. The 3-4 % visible light is reflected from the top side of
glass surface. Since SiN (silicon nitride) thin film is a good optical material thus it has been widely
applied as anti-reflection layer in solar cell [2, 3]. Silicon nitride films can be produced by a variety of
methods such as CVD [4], PECVD [5], reactive evaporation [6], ion beam deposition [7], reactive
sputtering [8] and photo-catalytic degradation [9]. TiO2 is believed to be the most promising material
form currently known ones, due to its superior photo-reactivity, non-toxicity, and long-term stability [10].
The sodium glass has been widely used in solar cell module to reduce the cost. Meanwhile, the ultraviolet
light is also absorbed by sodium glass. Therefore, the UV energy absorb by TiO2 does not influence the
efficiency of solar cell. Thus, the TiO2 film can use the ultraviolet light energy to do the self-clean
function. Thus, the multilayer with dual SiNx and single TiO2 thin films lead to thin film have
antireflection effect and self-cleaning ability.
