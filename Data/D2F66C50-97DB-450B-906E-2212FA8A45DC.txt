算法(Genetic Algorithms, GA)、模擬退火法(Guided Simulated Annealing, SA)、蟻群演算法(Ant Colony 
Optimization)、禁制搜尋法(Tabu Search Algorithms, TS)等。然而各種啟發式演算法都有其應用特性，因
此，許多學者提出結合二種以上啟發式方法的混合型啟發式技術求解 NP-hard 問題(Glover et al., 1995; 
Ting et al., 2001)，藉由各啟發式技術的優點並選取其部分演算程序加以結合，以提高求解的效率。
Renaud et al.（1996）首度提出以禁制搜尋法求解 MDVRP 的研究，Cordeau et al.（2001）則首度將禁
制搜尋法應用於具時窗限制的 MDVRPTW。由於之前並沒有學者提出求解 MDVRPTW 的解法，因此
其演算法在 MDVRPTW 的測詴題目上著重於迭代數（求解時間）與求解品質的權衡上。Giosa et al.
（2002）探討 MDVRPTW，著重在顧客先指派給場站，運用的是先分群再排程的方法。Cordeau et al.
（2004）修正在 2001 提出的禁制搜尋法，將其法運用在探討具時窗限制的週期車輛路線問題（Periodic 
Vehicle Routing Problem with Time-windows, PVRPTW）、MDVRPTW 與具時窗限制的 SDVRPTW 之迭
代數與求解品質的權衡上，並與公布的最佳解做比較。Nagy 及 Salhi（2005）發展考慮同時收貨及送貨
的多場站配送模式（VRPPD），並結合多種啟發式方法求解。Bae et al.（2007）探討 MDVRPTW，以運
輸距離最小化為目標，先以分群模式將多場站問題轉換成單一場站問題，再以考量車的型態、速度、
容量、旅行時間等限制下之改善路線模式，最後以改善基因演算法模式的三階段求解。 
Jeon et al.（2007）探討多場站的物流配送系統，考慮多場站、多車種、切割配送，求總行駛距離
最小化。其建構數學規劃法求解小型的問題，再建構混合基因演算法與一般基因演算求解大型的問題，
比較後發現使用混合基因演算法可以找到比一般基因演算法還要更好的解。Shih and Ou（2007）考量
多場站、配送貨物、路線規劃與時窗限制下求配送成本最小之目標，以最近鄰啟發式演算法（Nearest 
Neighbor Heuristic, NNH）在時窗與車容量限制下搜尋可行解，接著發展混合基因演算法進行初始解路
徑改善。Ho et al.（2008）提出三階段方式求解 MDVRP，先指派顧客至場站，再對每個場站中的顧客
做路徑分配，最後對每個場站進行路徑排程。其提出兩個混合基因演算法（HGA1 及 HGA2）以有效
率處理 MDVRP 問題，兩者最大差異在於 HGA1 以隨機產生初始解，而 HGA2 則以節省法與最近鄰點
法合併產生。Ghoseiri and Ghannadpour（2010）以混合基因演算法求解 MDVRPTW，其以先分群再排
路線的方法，將多場站問題轉換為單一場站問題，再對每個場站中的顧客進行路徑排程。Mirabi et al.
（2010）以混合啟發式法求解以最少配送時間為目標之 MDVRP，此混合啟發式法包含以最近法、節
省法、和最近鄰法產生初始解，再以確定型、隨機型、及模擬退火法發展三種演算法以改善初始路徑。
Liu et al.（2010）研究指出運輸業者皆在尋找更有效的運作方式以反應客戶需求、高漲的燃料成本、貨
運保險、以及競爭激烈的市場。因此作者針對美國每天約有 18％的空車，研究如何減少空車比率來減
少總運輸成本，其以貨運業為對象建立整車貨運之多場站容量限制路徑問題（Multi-depot Capacitated 
Arc Routing Problem with Full Truckloads, MDCARPFL）之模式，並發展兩階段貪婪演算法求解現實情
況的大規模問題。 
如上所述，使用混合型啟發式方法能結合不同方法之優點，獲得較佳的解與降低配送成本，而使
用越多元化的混合演算策略，求解結果越好。然而，綜觀上述研究鮮少針對 MDVRP 中動態車輛巡迴
路線問題求解，因此本研究將結合 GA 與 TS，建構出求解考量時窗限制與即時需求之混合型啟發式演
算法(Hybrid Heuristic Approach)，以服務的總成本最小為目標，並滿足求解過程隨機產生的新需求點以
即時更新路網中物流車的行進路線，達成配送需求。為測詴所提出之混合型啟發式演算法之可行性與
效能，本研究以 Cordeau et al.（1997）設計之國際標竿（benchmark）例題為已知顧客需求點進行測詴。
評估求解效能後，再發展為可求解即時需求之線上更新演算法，使物流車輛配送路線更具實用性。 
本文於第二節說明本研究之問題定義與求解架構；第三節進行基礎情境實驗與相關參數調整及比
較；第四節進行即時需求之線上更新實驗與數值結果分析；第五節提出結論與建議。

由於 MDVRPTW 之求解目標為最小化配送總距離，而本研究為保持啟發式演算求解的彈性，針對
演算所得之解如存在放棄配送、違反車容量限制、違反最大行駛距離限制與延遲服務(於時窗結束時間
後才服務)之情形，則以附加懲罰值的方式來提高其配送成本。因此，本研究設定求解目標值最小化總
成本，其總成本為總配送距離加上發車成本與懲罰值形成的遺傳演算法之基因適合度值來表示。對於
求解過程中會發生之不可行解，除了重複配送外，都設置懲罰值使其可以加入求解，對於各項會提高
配送成本之參數說明如下： 
1. 放棄配送懲罰值：當演算法對於某需求點難以加入配送或配送成本過高時，則會將其判斷為放棄
配送，但放棄配送並非求解時所預期得到之情況，故加入懲罰值降低基因適合度值。此外，放棄
配送懲罰值不可為 0，否則演算法會趨向將所有需求放棄配送。 
2. 違反車容量限制懲罰值：每台配送車皆有其所能負載之需求量，如演算法求得之配送路線超過該
車負載能力，其為不可配送之情況，故對其基因加入懲罰值降低適合度。 
3. 違反最大行駛距離限制懲罰值：每台配送車還有其總配送距離之限制，如超過此限制，則加入懲
罰值降低基因適合度。 
4. 違反時窗懲罰值：每個顧客會提出自己的預期配送時間，配送車頇於時間內到達才能滿足顧客之
需求。違反時窗限制有兩種情況，一種為提早到達，則配送車頇於該處等待直到時窗開始時間才
能進行服務，但此情況並不影響服務品質，故不加入懲罰值；另一種為延遲到達，其為配送車於
時窗結束時間後才到達，此情況下會附加懲罰值於成本中，並且再根據延遲時間長度額外再附加
延遲懲罰值，此作法可使演算法逐漸地設法找出最少延遲甚至不延遲的配送順序。 
5. 發車成本：為得到以最少車完成所有配送的結果，故加入發車成本於適合度值中，每當發出一輛
配送車，總成本則會加入 1 次發車成本。於設置發車成本時，其值必頇小於放棄配送懲罰值，避
免演算法趨向不發車而放棄配送。 
 
2.3.2 遺傳演算相關參數與方法 
本研究所建構之混合啟發式演算法為以遺傳演算法為基礎，相關之參數與演算方法說明如下： 
1. 初始族群大小：演算法根據此參數建立初始族群，其中包含初始解基因與隨機產生之基因。 
2. 族群規模上限：此參數限制其族群成長規模，當子代基因隨演算不斷加入族群之中，會使族群不
斷擴大而降低演算績效，因此，當族群擴大到規模上限時，會進行淘汰程序，降低族群規模。 
3. 交配次數上限：於遺傳演算中，每進行一次交配，表示進入下一個世代，為避免演算無限制的交
配而演算時間無限增加，故設置交配次數上限。 
4. 成熟檢查範圍：成熟為遺傳演算法中用於檢查族群中基因間的差異程度，當族群中基因差異降低
到特定程度(由成熟差距值定義)，表示即使再透過交配也無法產生不同的基因，則視為族群成熟，
演算終止。 
5. 成熟差距值：用於定義適合度值差異於多少比例內視為相同基因，其比較基準為各族群中的最佳
基因適合度值乘以(1+差距值)，若檢查範圍內最差的基因適合度值落於比較基準內，則表示檢查範
圍內的基因差異度已降到特定程度，即結束演算。 
6. 複製方法：複製方法為選擇交配母代的方式，其典型的方式有下列三種： 
(1) 完全隨機：任意由族群中抽取母代基因。 
(2) 輪盤法：以蒙地卡羅轉盤(Monte Carlo wheel)發展而來，其選擇母代的方式會根據基因的適應
力大小決定其被選擇的機率，有越高適應力的基因將有越大的機率被選擇成為母代。

1. INTRODUCTION 
 
The business setting where enterprises used to independently compete with one another has 
now evolved into an environment of global supply chain competition.  In addition to 
consolidating the corporate body, it is necessary for a focal company to create strategic 
alliances to form a collaborative supply chain management system, so as to enhance 
competitiveness.  Logistics plays a significant role in integrating the supply chain of 
industries.  In order to enhance the enterprise’s competition capability and operation 
performance, many enterprises are outsourcing their logistics operations to a professional 
company, the third-party logistics (3PL) providers, to increase their competitive advantages 
(Razzaque, 1998).  Therefore, how to select an appropriate 3PL provider has become an 
imperative issue. 
 
The 3PL providers can improve customer service and reduce costs, and allow companies to 
concentrate on their core competencies (Vaidyanathan, 2005).  The relation between 3PL 
providers and companies can be viewed as a supply-demand relationship between suppliers 
and users.  A common problem in this kind of relation is that customers may be dissatisfied 
with the services provided by providers, which can further affect their loyalty to the 
companies and has a significant impact on the strategic alliances of logistics (Andersson and 
Norrman, 2002).  In fact, there are increasing numbers of companies realizing that logistics 
alliances can be mutually beneficial and can allow the outsourcing company to concentrate on 
the core competence, reduce the cost, improve the service quality, increase the performance, 
and restructure the supply chains (Bhatnagar et al., 1999; Hertz and Alfredsson, 2003; 
Skjoett-Larsen, 2000).  Several studies have extensively discussed the 3PL researches in 
different perspectives (Andersson and Norrman, 2002; Boyson et al., 1999; Hertz and 
Alfredsson, 2003; Jharkharia and Shankar, 2007; Lewis and Talalayevsky, 2000; Murphy and 
Poist, 2002; Razzaque and Sheng, 1998; Skjoett-Larsen, 2000; Sohail and Sohal, 2003; Van 
Laarhoven et al., 2000; Wilding and Juriado, 2004).  Among those studies, the evaluation 
and selection of 3PL providers has gained increasingly concern.  Therefore, how to measure 
performance and select appropriate 3PL providers is an issue of utmost importance for 
corporate executives (Razzaque and Sheng, 1998; Andersson and Norrman, 2002).   
 
Generally, the evaluation and selection of 3PL providers involves multiple criteria (attributes) 
decision processes.  Enterprises usually set up a team of analysts who have rich experience, 
knowledge, and expertise in logistics operations (Jharkharia and Shankar, 2007).  The 
analysts should determine the important evaluation criteria and identify their weights.  The 
evaluation criteria used for the provider selection problems have been discussed in many 
studies (Andersson and Norrman, 2002; Bhatnagat et al., 1999; Boyson et al., 1999; Hum, 
2000; Jharkharia and Shankar, 2007; Liu and Wang, 2009; Menon et al., 1998; Razzaque and 
Sheng, 1998; Stock et al., 1998; Van Hoek, 2000).  In addition to different evaluation criteria 
regarding various logistics providers, the adopted competitive criteria generally differ and the 
weights of measures are correlated to individuals with subjective preferences, i.e., there are 
substantial differences in performance measurement and selection.  Certain and uncertain 
information as well as multi-granularity linguistic preference terms used by individuals create 
a problem of aggregating the performance measurement.

1. Identify function of providers and related evaluation attributes  
From the perspective of a company, the competitive selection criteria generally differ for 
different roles of providers.  Multiple attributes and their related sub-attributes for measuring 
providers must be identified.  Also, both quantitative and qualitative attributes are included.  
 
2. Set criteria and generate alternatives for providers 
Based on the function of providers, the company identifies suitable attributes and sets criteria 
to evaluate potential providers.  A finite set of alternatives is generated for selection.  These 
alternatives are the acceptable providers and need to have their performance properly 
evaluated.  Since an effective and efficient method in performance evaluation and decision 
making is critical, a fuzzy multiple attribute decision method (FMADM) is applied.

4. Solution to the model 
To evaluate alternatives, quantitative attributes can be easily calibrated, and uncertain or 
qualitative information need special settings.   
 
(1). Quantitative attributes and normalization 
The quantitative attributes, which are measured using numerical values and different units, 
need to be normalized to represent the uniform information for aggregation. 
 
(2). Qualitative attributes and fusion of multi-granularity linguistic information 
The qualitative attributes are interpreted as linguistic values expressed in triangular fuzzy 
numbers.  Since analysts have multi-granularity linguistic scales on qualitative attributes, the 
linguistic values are converted into a specific linguistic domain, a basic linguistic term set 
(BLTS), to make the information uniform for aggregation.  BLTS is assumed 15 scales. 
 
(3). Aggregation of numeric values and multi-granularity linguistic information 
In order to further evaluate the alternatives, numeric values and multi-granularity linguistic 
information need to be aggregated for comparison.  Since analysts have different judgment 
in weighting, a fuzzy linguistic quantifier guided OWA operator (Herrera et al., 2000) is 
adapted to compute and aggregate collective attribute values for each alternative. 
 
5. Sensitivity analysis 
Performance measurement and provider selection is a continuous process, which consists of 
the past, present, and future of time horizons.  A sensitivity analysis could be adopted to 
demonstrate different results related to the company’s perspective.

applied to a case company.  The case company produces various products in Taiwan.  Since 
logistics outsourcing is a large investment, the company needs to select the best provider to 
satisfy various needs in quality, marketing skills, financial performance, commitment, and 
facilitating factors.  The proposed evaluation framework can help measuring the 
performance of providers, thus the company can increase its competition. 
 
5.1 Provider Evaluation and Selection for the Case Company 
1. Identify providers and related evaluation attributes 
The case company collects various data and information regarding the strength and weakness 
of the providers from different sources.  Based on the attributes described in Table 1, the 
attributes adopted to evaluate provider include five main attributes: quality, marketing skills, 
financial performance, commitment, and facilitating factors.  The five main attributes have 
received a great amount of attention in provider selection.  
 
2. Set criteria and generate alternatives  
The case company applies evaluation criteria that differ with attributes to emphasize the 
influence of different criteria on aggregation results.  Since quality and marketing skill are 
the most important criteria in evaluating providers, they are treated as critical attributes and 
the weighting values will be calibrated from fuzzy quantifier “at least half” to emphasize the 
great influence on aggregation results.  The attributes of commitment and facilitating factor 
are major attributes and the fuzzy quantifier “most” is used to emphasize the medium 
influence on aggregation results.  Financial performance is a basic attribute, and the fuzzy 
quantifier “as many as possible” is used to represent the basic requirement that the providers 
can fulfill.

5.2 Sensitivity Analysis 
Performance measurement is a continuous process, which consists of the past, present, and 
future of time horizons.  Since different strategy will affect the performance measurement, a 
sensitivity analysis is adopted to demonstrate different results related to the strategy of the 
case company.   
 
In Section 5.1, the case uses fuzzy quantifier “as many as possible” to indicate that the 
attributes of “financial performance” are basic criteria for performance evaluation.  
However, as shown in Table 8, provider C will be selected if the criterion of attributed 
“financial performance” has been changed to be the critical criteria by using the fuzzy 
quantifier “at least half”.  The results indicate that a different provider strategy will create a 
different selection decision of providers.

場站物流配送規劃上一個良好且實用性的結果。此混合型啟發式演算法可延伸應用於各種
車輛巡迴路線問題上，對於整合不同技術及演算法之改良上，可提供一參考之方向。 
2. 就社會影響上，本研究所開發之混合型啟發式演算法可運用於線上更新演算中，可求
解即時需求與動態物流配送規劃問題。在實務應用中，對於因應即時訂單的產生以及顧客
對於企業的回應需求速度之要求逐漸提升的狀況下，本研究所開發之即時需求線上更新演
算法頗具實用性與經濟效益。
