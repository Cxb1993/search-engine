中文摘要 
關鍵詞：光學循環器、雙折射晶體、空間偏離偏極器、光通訊、偏極。 
 
光學循環器為建構光通訊網路基本功能模組不可或缺之元件，例如：光學
存/取多工器、色散補償、光學放大器和光時域反射測量術。在大部分的系統應
用中均要求循環器具有高的效能表現，這些效能表現包含：高的隔離值、低的
插入損失、低的偏極相依損失、低的偏極模色散。此外，在一些系統的應用中
要求光學循環器具有多個傳輸埠，或者具有大的波長操作頻寬，這些特點都將
使元件在應用上更具有使用彈性，除了能使系統達成密集化與小型化的目標，
更能降低系統架設的成本。 
有鑑於此，本研究計畫提出了一種新型的多埠光學準循環器，此光學準循
環器是由串聯式的空間偏極模組，並且巧妙搭配偏極分光鏡與反射稜鏡來組
成。而結構中的空間偏極模組是由YVO4雙折射晶體、法拉第旋轉器與二分之ㄧ
波片所組成。在計畫的實際執行上，實際組裝 6-port的光學準循環器來驗證此
方法的可行性，實驗測量到所得之插入損耗可小於 1.9dB，反射損耗與隔離值
分別可大於 40dB與 49dB。此一架構設計的優點包括有架構簡單、低的插入損
失、反射損失與高的隔離值，且因為偏極分光鏡與反射稜鏡被巧妙的安排放置
在架構中，因此能有效的解決偏極模色散問題。此外，此一光學準循環器之設
計也能簡單的將傳輸埠擴充至多個。

此光學準循環器在設計上具有緊密、簡單的優點。在架構上由於配合了偏極分
光鏡(PBS)與反射稜鏡(RP)所組成的偏極模色散消除模組，來抵消光傳播時產生

入損失和高的隔離值。這些規格與一般商業產品的規格已相當接近，因此可以
實際的被運用於各種光學系統中。 
 
二、研究方法 
本研究計畫所提出的多埠光學準循環器之設計主要架構為空間偏極模組
(Spatial and polarization module, SPM)，其空間偏極模組所組成元件包括了空間
偏離偏極器(Spatial walk-off polarizers, SWP)、法拉第旋轉器(Faraday rotator, FR)
與二分之ㄧ波片(Half wave-plate, H)。下面則針對此一空間偏極模組之操作原理
作一詳細介紹。

2.2 多埠光學準循環器

要架構，並在兩旁經過特別的計算，在適當的位置上相對應的安置入偏極分光
鏡(PBS)與反射稜鏡(RP)來建構而成。其架構如 Fig.3 所示。為了方便解釋，我
們將訂定一個 x-z 之座標軸，此一座標軸原點定於串聯空間偏極模組之中心。
如 Fig.3(a)所示，當訊號由 port 1 入射後，先經過 RP 折射進入 SPM，經由雙折
射晶體之分光與旋光元件的偏振旋轉，再由雙折射晶體結合後出射串聯的
SPM，訊號光往+x 方向位移了 2L。再藉由 RP 折射後傳輸到 port 2。如 Fig.3(b)
所示，當訊號由 port2 入射，經 RP 折射後入射到 SPM，經 SPM 分光往-x 方向

ABSTRACT 
The feasibility of conventional polarization-selective substrate-mode holograms is usually limited by the finite refractive 
index modulation strength. Therefore, in this study, a novel design of polarization selective element with a large 
diffraction angle is proposed based on the coupled-wave theory. The polarization selective element for 632.8nm is 
fabricated with VRP-M silver-halide recording material. The diffraction efficiencies of s- and p- components are 83% 
and 5%, and the calculated extinction ratios are 5.58 and 275, respectively. Polarization selective elements fabricated by 
the proposed method have all the merits of conventional substrate-mode hologram but not limited by the finite refractive 
index modulation of common recording materials. 
Keywords: polarization-selective elements, substrate-mode holograms, refractive index modulation strength, splitting 
angle. 
 
1. INTRODUCTION 
Polarization-selective elements [1-5] capable of splitting an optical beam into two orthogonally polarized beams 
play an important role in different kinds of optical systems such as optical sensing [6-8], optical data storage [9, 10], 
imaging system [11], switching network [12], wavelength division multiplexing (WDM) [13], and optical circulator [14-
17]. In different applications these elements are called as polarization beam splitter (PBS), spatial walk-off polarizer 
(SWP), or polarizing mirror. Presently several kinds of method and technique have been proposed to implement this 
function such as substrate-mode hologram, optical multi-layer coating, birefringent crystal, and micromachined 
integrated optic. In spite of the mature techniques, the elements fabricated with multi-layer coating, birefringent crystal, 
and micromachined integrated optic have drawbacks of complex fabrication processes and high cost. Especially, the 
crystal-type element is hard to fulfill the demand of compactness. Though, the conventional polarization-selective 
substrate-mode holographic elements have many advantages. Its feasibility is usually limited by the finite refractive 
index modulation strength of a recording material. The common solution is to increase the thickness of the recording 
material in order to compensate the shortage of the refractive index modulation strength, in the phase modulation term. 
However, under the thickness condition of thick material, the distortion effect of interference fringe is worsened. An 
ideal holographic recording condition hinges on the thin thickness of a recording material with a high refractive index 
modulation strength. Actually these cannot be completed in both respects. 
Therefore, in this study we devoted to overcome the problems in holographic optical elements fabrications. Based 
on the coupled-wave theory and the structure of substrate-mode holograms, a special design of a polarization-selective 
element is proposed at a relatively large splitting angle near 90°. With this design, we need only a low refractive index 
modulation strength, which can be easily achieved with common recording materials. In addition, this design should bear 
all merits of conventional substrate-mode holographic elements such as compactness, plane structure, easily light 
collimation, easily fabrication, and low cost.
