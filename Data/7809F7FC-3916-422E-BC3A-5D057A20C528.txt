摘    要 
現有之土壤行為研究多建立在重模試體的試驗上，透過重模試體已建立許多砂土行為
模式。亦有學者經由觀察現地試驗行為與抗液化強度之間關係來建立二者之間的經驗法
則。現有研究指出砂土重模試體與天然試體間存在著極為顯著的差異，其中重模試體製作
方法更與重模試體的土壤組構以至於所表現出的行為息息相關。一般以水中沉降法試體較
能代表沖積土壤組構，麥寮砂及台灣中西部多數砂土為沖積土壤，根據經驗應以水中沉降
法或泥漿沉積法製作試體較具代表性，但這些方法應用在重達數百公斤的大型標定試體上
效率不佳且均勻性表現亦不理想，因此研發符合沖積土壤行為且能有效率的製作大型試體
的方法是必須的。濕噴法採氣流霣降與水霧沾附等方式，嘗試對粉土細砂的顆粒析離現象
做出改善，所研發的試體製作程序及設備在應用於製作大型試體上相對其他模擬沖積土壤
行為方法簡便。經實驗證實，濕噴法試體在三軸試驗上具有與水中沉降法相近的表現，說
明其能用以模擬現地沖積土壤，粉土細砂試體垂直向與水平向的篩分析結果也顯示濕噴法
試體良好的細料含量均勻性。目前對於粉土細砂製作方式對標定槽試體的製作影響所知相
當的有限，經簡易修改後的濕噴法將易於製作均勻試體，以了解砂土現地行為與室內標定
試驗間之關係。 
 
關鍵詞: 粉土細砂、重模試體、濕噴法。 
 
Abstract 
Fundamental investigations of soil behaviour going is based on the test results of 
reconstituted specimens. Through reconstituted specimens, many soil behaviour models have 
been built up. On the other hand, by autoptic insitu testing such as SPT/CPT/shear wave velocity 
measurement, empiricism linking insitu behaviour and cyclic resistance were found. Numerous 
studies have shown that differences in stress-strain-strength behaviour between undisturbed and 
reconstituted sand specimens may be dramatic. Soil behaviuor is highly dependent upon soil 
fabric produced by different reconstituted method. It's been reported that water pluviation method 
simulates the deposition of sand through water found in natural environments and have similar 
fabric and behavior. Series laboratory tests include monotonic and cyclic triaxial test and CPT 
calibration tests of Mai Laio Sand were performed. Most of it were prepared by moist tamping 
and pluviation. Where natural silty sands found in central Westen Taiwan like Mai Laio Sand are 
often associated with alluvial deposits. Base on experience, in order to represent alluvial 
environments, silty sand specimens should be reconstituted by water pluviation or slurry 
deposition method. But for calibration chamber specimens which substantially larger than those 
for typical triaxial tests these methods show less preparation efficiency and uniformity. It is 
bounden to find a method for reconstituting large specimens streamlined and also give same 
response as the alluvial sand. 
Own developed wet spray method using air jet pluviation with moisten both sands and fines 
attempt to handling and minimization of soil particle segregation in silty sand specimens.

由於現地砂土不易取得不擾動土樣，對於室內試驗多採用重模試體進行操作，然而試
體之製作必需考慮試體之均勻性、試驗之重複性，以及模擬現地砂土狀態[18]。關於三軸試

AP）、濕夯法（moist tamping, MT）、濕震法（moist vibration, MV）、水中沉降法（water 
sedimentation, WS）、水中震動法（water vibration, WV）、以及泥漿沈積法（slurry deposition, 
SD）。不同重模試體的製作方式，其砂土組構並不完全相同。Tatsuoka et al.[19]比較霣落法、
濕夯法、濕震法和水中震動法所製作試體的抗液化強度，其中以濕震法強度最高，而霣落
法強度最低。Amini and Qi[10]曾探討均勻（uniform）與分層（layered）試體的差異，對於
抗液化強度而言，其差別並不大。Yamamuro[20]以內華達砂（Nevada Sand）在相同的細粒
料含量及孔隙比操作試驗，發現水中沉降法呈現膨脹性行為，故不容易液化；反之，乾置
法則呈現壓縮性行為，容易產生液化。 
標定試驗的基本構想，是在試驗室中製作大型試體，進行現地儀器標定試驗。由於所
有的過程皆在試驗室中進行，在試體的均勻性和邊界條件，皆可做嚴謹控制。 
現行標定槽試體的準備，大多是利用固定式和移動式霣落器（pluviator）來製作。固定
式霣落器讓砂土由儲砂桶的底盤開孔落下一段距離後，經由分散器（sand diffuser）的作用，
使砂能夠均勻的分散在整個平面上，堆積在標定槽中。試樣緊密度主要是由儲砂桶底部的
多孔盤（porous plate）之開孔大小和數量及分散器底部至砂土表面距離來控制。移動式霣
落器的儲砂桶下方有一個長方形開孔，在砂土霣落的過程中，砂土可以經由儲砂桶之移動
而使之均勻分散在空間中，試樣的緊密度是經由長方形開孔的大小和儲砂桶移動的速度來
控制。在製作含細顆粒之砂土試體時，固定式霣落器會有以下的缺點（[21][22] [23]）： 
1.霣落過程中，在試體中央的試樣，較周圍的試樣相對密度小，會造成在空間中的不均勻。 
2.當相對密度為 15%或 90%時的試體，在試體各處的評估，是相當均勻。但在中等疏鬆砂
的狀況下，其試體較不均勻，尤其在同一平面上。 
3.底盤不論是使用單孔或多孔的開孔方式，均可看見細顆粒土壤形成粉土柱及顆粒析離
（segregation）的現象。 
移動式霣落器雖然可以降低粉土柱和顆粒析離的現象[23]，但是無論使用固定或移動式
霣落器，其沈積方式仍然是屬於乾砂之霣落法（air pluviation）。 
Rahardjo[22]使用泥漿壓密法（slurry consolidation）製作標定槽粉土質砂土試體。此方
法是將粉土質砂土與水在標定槽中攪拌後，以逐步施加圍壓之方式進行壓密，最終達到圓
錐貫入試驗前所設定之圍壓狀態。以泥漿壓密法製作標定槽試體相當耗費時間，對於 40%

以濕噴法所研發之砂土霣落系統包括砂土霣落器及水霧濕潤系統兩部分： 
一、砂土霣落器 
濕噴法砂土霣落器與傳統霣落器之差異在於透過氣流帶動，使不同粗細顆粒砂土
在霣落過程中以相近終端速度落下。造霧系統在砂土霣落過程中以均勻水霧使粗細顆
粒彼此相互沾粘而形成顆粒尺寸均勻之砂水顆粒，避免霣落過程中因顆粒大小不同導
致顆粒析離現象。 
砂土霣落器（如圖 4.3(a)所示）採用白努利定理以氣流帶動儲砂槽中之砂土向下霣
落，在儲砂槽中央設置中空螺桿之出氣管，管後端連接至調壓閥與空壓機以提供氣體
壓力，藉以調整與砂土混合之氣流大小。出砂口為環狀放射（如圖 4.3(b)所示），調整
環狀出砂口開口大小則可控制出砂量的多寡，進而控制土壤試體的孔隙比。試體製作
時，氣流自漏斗面窄端往寬端流動並由環形開口輸出，同時帶出砂土顆粒。氣流帶動
可使砂土中之粗細顆粒以一初始速度霣落而非自由落體霣落，減少顆粒析離現象。 
 
二、水霧濕潤系統 
水霧濕潤系統的組成主要包括儲水槽、調壓閥、與噴嘴三部分。於試體製作前，
儲水槽之儲水需先進行除氣（deair），並使噴嘴與霣落器中心軸線成 30∘夾角，調整
調壓閥之輸出氣壓為 4 kg/cm2，即完成水霧濕潤系統之準備工作。 
水霧濕潤系統是以定流量噴嘴製造顆粒均勻之水霧，並使噴出之水霧覆蓋砂土顆
粒下落之路徑。霣降砂土自霣落器出砂口脫離後即與自兩側所散佈之均勻水霧接觸，
並為其所包覆成為整體的砂水混合顆粒。

5.4 動態三軸試驗結果 
5.4.1 試體之重複性 
試體重複性試驗乃對相同孔隙比試體之力學行為做確認，此部分以動態三軸試驗做驗
證，以相同軸向應力對相同有效圍壓壓密與孔隙比之試體進行反覆荷重三軸試驗。當試體
初始液化時，比對所得之反覆荷重數(NC)，並觀察其不排水狀態下與所激發超額孔隙水壓
之情況，試驗結果顯示其重複性良好（如圖 5.4 所示）。 
5.4.2 試體製作方法對動態三軸試驗之影響 
由學者的研究得知，不同的重模試體製作方式，其抗液化強度會略有不同，主要原因
是不同的重模試體製作方式所製作出的試體之土壤組構並不相同，試體製作方式所造成的
差異程度因土壤種類而異。郭毓真[25]以 FC=30%之麥寮砂作為試驗材料，試體製作方法對
動態行為所造成的影響的研究中發現，在相同的孔隙比下，不同試體製作方法中以濕夯法
之試體抗液化強度最高，水中沉降法次之，與霣落法相類似的乾置法最低。 
以濕噴法所製作的麥寮砂試體在相近孔隙比下抗液化強度與郭毓真[25]水中沉降法試
體具有相同的趨勢，顯示濕噴法土壤組構與水中沉降法相類似。圖 5.5(a)(b)(c)表示麥寮砂

六、結論與建議 
本研究以製作大型粉土細砂標定槽試體之構想，研發並製作濕噴法之儀器設備與操作
流程，獲致結論如下： 
1. 試體分層與分區切片之試驗結果顯示濕噴法雖採固定霣落方法，但無論在水平方向或垂
直方向上其顆粒析離情況並不明顯，濕噴法確能達到減少顆粒析離現象的發生。 
2. 以動態三軸試驗做試體重複性試驗比較，兩組試驗結果在初始液化時，其反覆荷重數
(NC)與所激發超額孔隙水壓之情況相近，顯示其重複性良好。 
3. 同樣以麥寮砂作為試驗材料，在動態三軸試驗當中，濕噴法所製作的試體其受剪行為與
以水中沉降法所製作之三軸試體相近，顯示兩者之間土壤組構相似。 
4. 壓密不排水三軸試驗中，濕噴法與濕夯法所製作之試體反應不同的受剪行為。學者對試
體製作方法的研究顯示，濕夯法試體多是傾向收縮與應變軟化，但是與現地土壤組構相
似的三軸試體（非擾動取樣、泥漿沈積法與水中沉降法）則是表現出明顯的受剪膨脹與
應變硬化。濕噴法之壓密不排水三軸試驗結果顯示受剪膨脹與應變硬化行為，與現地土
壤組構相似的三軸試體研究成果相近。

A NEW SPECIMEN PREPARATION METHOD ON CALIBRATION 
CHAMBER IN SILTY SANDS 
 
Huai-Houh Hsu1, An-Bin Huang2, Yu-Jie Huang2 and Chung-Ci Chen3 
 
1Department of Civil Engineering, Chienkuo Technology University 
Chang-Hua, Taiwan 
2Department of Civil Engineering, National Chiao Tung University 
Hsin-Chu, Taiwan 
3Institute of Mechatronoptic Systems, Chienkuo Technology University 
Chang-Hua, Taiwan 
 
ABSTRACT 
The sand on the West Coast of Taiwan contains significant amount of silt. Due to lack 
of cohesion, it is difficult to obtain undisturbed samples for sand. In situ tests are 
often used to determine the engineering properties of sand. The cone penetration test 
（CPT） is a widely used in situ test. The interpretation rules of CPT data are mostly 
developed based on chamber calibration tests, and limited theoretical considerations. 
Most of the available empirical rules came from Europe or Northern America, based 
on tests in uniformly graded, clean sand. The difference between uniformly graded 
clean sands and silty sands can be significant. It is not desirable to directly adopt the 
empirical CPT interpretation rules developed in the West to the silty sand on Taiwan. 
The preparation of a uniform chamber specimen is important.  When the sand 
contains fines, the particle segregation during deposition process should be 
minimized. A new deposition method has been developed to prepare a uniform 
chamber specimen of silty sands.  This paper introduces this method and presents 
test results. 
Key words: silty sand, reconstituted specimen, wet spray method. 
 
INTRODUCTION 
The silty sand on the West Coast of Taiwan comes from soft rocks of shale and 
mudstone. Many coast structures are constructed on this kind of sand layers. When 
the fines content (FC, particles which pass through sieve No. 200 or smaller than 
0.075 mm) increases, the engineering properties will be changed. 
 
Many researches show that the fine particles in sand will affect its dilatancy, 
shear strength, liquefaction potential, and explanation of in situ test results. The 
calibration tests in sand usually use clean, uniform, silica or quartz sand, which is 
refered to as “academic sand”. Lots of empirical rules and analytical models are 
established which are based on the “academic sand” test results. The in situ sand 
layers are not “academic sand”, their engineering properties will be affected by the 
 7
affected by geological history, mineral contents, soil sampling process, and 
reconstitute specimen method. It is important to establish an explanation way for silty 
sand in Taiwan. 
 
ASSESSMENT OF RECONSTITUTED SAND SPECIMEN METHODS 
Due to lack of cohesion, it is difficult to obtain in situ undisturbed samples in 
sand. The reconstituted specimen is frequently used in the laboratory tests. However, 
the preparation of a remold specimen should consider its uniformity, repeatability of 
test result as well as simulate the field state (Kuerbis and Vaid, 1988).  
 
Sand Specimen of Triaxial Test 
Many methods have been proposed to prepare the triaxial sand specimen, these 
methods include (Ishihara, 1993; Kuerbis and Vaid, 1988; Tatsuoka et al., 1986): 
1. dry deposition (DD) 
2. air pluviation (AP) 
3. moist tamping (MT) 
4. moist vibration (MV) 
5. water sedimentation (WS) 
6. water vibration (WV) 
7. slurry deposition (SD) 
 
The sand fabric prepared by different method will not totally the same. Tatsuoka 
et al. (1986) compared the resistance liquefaction strength of different sand specimen 
reconstituted methods (AP, MT, MV, and WV), the MV method had the highest 
resistance strength and AP method got the lowest resistance strength. Amini and Qi
（2000）evaluated the differences between uniform and layered specimen. They found 
that the difference of resistance liquefaction strength was not obvious. Yamamuro
（2004）adopted WS and DD methods to prepare the specimen of Nevada Sand to 
conduct cyclic triaxial tests. Test results show the sand behavior was dilatant and not 
easy liquefaction while using WS method.  On the contrary, the sand behavior was 
contractive and easy liquefaction while using DD method. 
 
Chamber Calibration Specimen 
The concept of a calibration chamber test is to prepare a large sand specimen in 
the laboratory, consolidated to a desired stress level, and then perform the experiment 
under given boundary conditions. Since the entire experiment is conducted in the 
laboratory, the test quality can be readily controlled. The large sand specimen, with 
uniform deposition and known engineering properties, provides reference values for 
 9
drops from the opening that is mounted in the rectangular aperture. During pluviation, 
the pluviator moves back and forth horizontally above the open chamber and the 
falling sand is like a wide brush to sweep over the deposition surface. 
 
The stationary pluviator is economical and easy to operate.  However, when the 
sand contains fines, segregation can be significant. The sand or gravel sized particles 
spread over a wider area than the fine grains. Silt columns or silt concentration is 
observed right beneath the holes or nozzles of the rainer plate (Lo Presti et al., 1992). 
Although the traveling pluviator can reduce the phenomenon of segregation, both of 
them are air pluviation methods. 
 
Rahardjo (1989) used slurry consolidation method to prepare chamber specimen 
of silty sand. The silty sand and water are mixed together by a concrete mixer, and 
consolidated to the desire stress level of performing CPT. It takes a long time to form 
a chamber specimen by the slurry consolidation method. For a FC=40% chamber 
specimen, 1.5m in diameter and 1.5m high, it will take 18 to 20 days to complete the 
consolidation process to reach the confining pressure of 70 kPa.  
 
THE WET SPRAY METHOD 
The wet spray (WSp) method adopts the concept of both air pluviation and slurry 
deposition. During silty sand pluviation, sands pass through a water spray zone to 
make coarse and fine particles attach to each other, then move dowward through a 
film of water, and finally deposit inside the chamber. The wet spray pluviation system 
consist of a sand pluviation device and a water spray equipment. Fig. 2 demonstrates 
the schematic view of this system.
