sources of the sports injure obtained from the
fencing, badminton, weight lifting, Tae-kw
o-do and so on 5 representative teams of the
National Taiwan sports university (Taichung),
The number of contestants are 89 persons
altogether; the subjects ages are from
18.54-21.52 years old and the training periods of
them are accepted from 5.13-10.19 years.

the foot pressures measurements, those subjects
are elected due to the longer training in that term.
