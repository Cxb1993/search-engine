Improvement of package technique for high-absorption thermoelectric devices – 
applying to the associated solar thermoelectric with silicon solar cells (II) 
計畫編號：100-2623-E-002-004-ET 
計畫類別：■個別型計畫   □整合型計畫 
計畫主持人：王立民 
e-mail： liminwang@ntu.edu.tw 
計畫參與人員：陳彥齊 曾俊啟

摘要 
本研究利用高熱傳導氮化鋁(AlN)取代一般熱電元
件 (TEC1-3103-40×40 mm2, 3.4A/3.5V, TEC)的高溫熱
板，量測其對應之輸出功率 p 與轉換效率η改變，其結
果則與 TEC1熱電元件比較並討論之。此外，結合多晶
矽太陽能電池，在照光光度 215 mW/m2照射造成多晶矽
太陽能電池表面平衡溫度 33℃情形下，產生氮化鋁熱電
元件溫差 5℃與最大 4.1 mW 之功率輸出，而此時多晶
矽太陽能電池最大之輸出功率為 73.5 mW，亦即增加約
5.6%之熱能回收效率 R。 
關鍵詞：熱電元件、轉換效率、氮化鋁、多晶矽太陽能
電池。 
Abstract 
In this study, high-thermal-conductivity AlN was used 
to replace the hot plates commonly used for the package of 
thermoelectric devices (products of TEC1-3103-40×40 
mm2, 3.4A/3.5V, TEC). We measure the corresponding 
output powers poutput and transfer coefficients η of the 
AlN-packaged thermoelectric devices. The results are 
discussed and compared with those of the TEC1 
thermoelectric devices. In addition, by associating a 
poly-silicon solar cell with the thermoelectric device under 
a 215 mW/m2 irradiation, a maximum output power of 4.1 
mW for the thermoelectric device within a temperature 
difference of 5℃ was obtained, while a maximum output 
power of 73.5 mW for the poly-silicon solar cell with a 
surface temperature of 33℃  was observed. This is 
corresponding to an increase of ~ 5.6% in the 
thermal-electrical energy transform. 
Keywords: thermoelectric devices, transfer coefficient, AlN, 
poly-silicon solar cell. 
I. 前言 
熱，是一種能源，它無處不在；有別於太陽光電，
它隨時可得。熱電材料能直接將浪費的熱能轉換成有用
的電能，由於沒有動件，熱電產生器可長期可靠的工作
而無需特別維護；堅固、無雜音為其最大優點。雖然熱
電現象早於 1823年即被 Seebeck所發現，後歷經百餘年
之發展，目前利用熱電材料製成的裝置其效率（< 5%）
仍遠比傳統發電機小，擁有眾多優點的熱電材料應用發
展，仍面臨瓶頸。利用熱電材料製作的熱電產生器結構
