計劃名稱：全像分光光學元件於立體視覺顯示之應用 
計畫編號：NSC 100-2221-E-018-016- 
執行期限：100 年 08 月 01 日至 101 年 7 月 31 日  
主持人：蘇威佳  國立彰化師範大學光電所  
 
摘要： 
本研究提出以高分子分散液晶製作立體顯示用途所需要之全像式影像分光元件，透過
材料製作與量測，我們分析比較材料中不同組成成份比例對於分光元件繞射效率的變化，
藉以取得最佳材料組合比例並製作所需的基材，之後將元件設計貼附在2.4吋LCD面板表
面，依照人眼的明視距離計算所需的分光角度，設計新的拍攝架構並製作出全像分光元件。
經實驗結果可以發現，元件成功將LCD裡的插排影像分離且準確的分送至觀測者的左右
眼，而元件左右視角繞射效率分別為51%和55%，contrast ratio高於84%，且在製程與組裝
方式都表現出來良好的特性，證明出PDLC全像分光元件作為裸眼立體顯示的可行性。 
1.簡介 
近年來聚合物分散液晶發展的快速，光聚合材料的優點有，高的角度選擇性，高解析
度，折射率動態範圍大，製作簡便，組成多樣化及價格便宜等優點，有幾種類型的感光聚
合物全像記錄系統已被提出[1-4]，它們廣泛的被應用，如光學儲存[5]，光開關[6]，全像光
學元件[7,8]，reflective flat-pane displays [9]，switchable lenses [10]，application-specific lenses 
[11]和許多光電應用。 
而全像光學元件應用至裸眼立體顯示在2011年，Su等人已提出[12]，依據拍攝架構的不
同，紀錄上不同的光柵，光透過這些光柵便會產生繞射 [13]，進而利用此種特性可以將光
導向我們所需的位置，但繞射效率的表現僅43%。目前市場上最為廣泛的是微柱狀透鏡式
[14,15]及視差遮蔽式 [16-18]這兩種2D多工式技術，但微柱狀透鏡分光技術則會產生左右影
像分離不清，造成Cross Talk的問題[19] ，而視差遮障利用遮畫素的方式來達到分光，造成
顯示整體輝度下降至22.4%[20]，為了克服上述的問題在2008年，Chen等人利用鍍金屬反射
層於面板內部，藉此提高光的再回收利用率，將視差遮障式整體面板輝度提升至
43.9%[21]，2005年，Shieh等人提出繞射光學元件具有可避免產生輝度下降以及串音干擾，
但是繞射光學元件在製程上由於元件體積過小製作困難，所以會產生結構上的誤差 [22]。 
結合以上問題，本文章提出以PDLC製作HOE，並設計應用在2.4吋的LCD上作為立體
顯示分光技術。利用全像拍攝術來製作元件，將光柵分成奇偶畫素使不同方向的光柵記錄
在材料上，便可達到奇偶畫素分離至不同方向的效果，而Brightness是取決於材料的繞射效
率，本文章所配製的材料繞射效率達到58%，我們將材料製作成元件後左右視角繞射效率
分別是51%和55%，Crosstalk的對比度更大於84%，這樣的表現已經能突破以DCG製作的
HOE[12]，除了效率及對比度較高優點以外，還有元件製程簡單省時，減少一般全像材料
的顯影、定影、漂白等過程，且厚度約為1mm，更為適合貼附在LCD上作為裸眼立體顯示
技術的分光元件。

2-3. 繞射效率與時間曲線分析 
如圖4所示，隨著LC增加從10 wt%到30 wt%時，隨著曝光時間增加所對應的繞射效率
明顯的提升而呈現穩定狀態。當LC的含量從40 wt%個增加到60 wt%時，繞射效率在很短的
曝光時間就可衝到最高，但是隨著曝光時間的增加而逐步下降，所以LC的含量越來越多，
到達繞射效率最大值的曝光時間會更為縮短，且效率隨著時間下降速度更加快。研判這個
結果可能是因為過多的LC分子在相分離時造成過高負荷導致發生光柵崩塌，因為能夠支撐
光柵的交聯聚合物減少和折射率調製也下降。然而這種趨勢會隨著LC含量增加會更加顯著
[24]。由繞射曲線圖來看， LC 20 wt%這比例得到最高+1繞射效率58%，且隨著曝光時間增
加呈現穩定，因此本實驗選用此含量作為下段製作全像分光元件的基材。

2-5. 分光元件拍攝架構與拍攝過程 
本實驗拍攝架構與拍攝過程如圖7(a)所示，利用Beam splitter (BS)將雷射光源分兩道
光，兩道光分別利用Spatial filter (SF)，將雷射光調整成擴散且均勻的光源。同時兩道光分
別穿過Fourier lens (FL)，將球面波性質轉換成平面波性質，並使參考波分別與主波夾8度
角，兩道光以相同垂直極化來進行拍攝元件，為了得到可以分離左右影像的全像分光元件，
左邊影像的繞射光柵與右邊影像的繞射光柵必須分開拍攝，利用兩片mask前後貼附在材料
上，分別遮住材料的奇偶畫素，以達到左右影像繞射光柵分開效果，設計的mask大小為5cm 
× 4cm條紋間隔為140μm。功率探測器放置在+1階繞射點位置，以即時量測繞射點功率的變
化。

3.討論 
Fig.8所量測到的以PDLC製作的全像分光元件左右視角繞射效率分別為51% 和55%，
在Brightness的表現上，優於傳統視差遮蔽式的立體面板輝度22.4%，也高於高輝度立體顯
示面板的輝度值43.0%[20] ，先前本團隊利用DCG所製作的全像式分光元件，該繞射效率
也僅43.0%[12]。然而，PDLC材料組成多樣化容易提升材料繞射效率，使接近理想100%大
幅提升輝度[25]。 
Crosstalk現象在立體顯示技術中影響著影像的品質，只要左眼觀看的影像不受到右眼
影像的干擾，反之右眼影像亦然，為了評估Crosstalk優劣我們以Contrast ratio (CR)來做為判
別，如公式(1)所示。其中Leven代表當顯示一個視角影像時偶畫素中的光強度，Lodd代表
當顯示一個視角影像時奇畫素中的光強度，將所量測的左右視角影像光強套入公式(1)，結
果如表1所示。左右視角的CR值分別為91.01%及84.39%，遠大於所定義的基準值，超過了
DCG所製作的全像分光元件的CR值76.5%[12]。

本元件在製程方面，只需架設光學拍攝架構，且感光材料可自行調配低成本，製作時
只需曝光流程即可完成，製作時間短，而元件輕薄厚度約1.4 mm適合貼附在一般TFT顯示
器上。而DCG製作的HOE製作元件過程與本實驗相同，但材料曝光完需定影、清洗、烘烤
手續製作耗時間，高亮度視差遮障式，採用黃光微影製程手續，需要多道的微影曝光手續，
製作上較為複雜繁瑣，成本高且製作耗時間。 
PDLC與DCG分光元件組裝方式，都是採用After-Market概念，貼附於面板最外層，可
以配合各種面板規格設計製作，面板出廠後再貼附，配合立體影像的輸入，即可改裝成裸
眼立體顯示器，不須改變目前大廠面板生產線的手續；而高亮度視差遮障式，為面板內部
增加一層高亮度反射層，若要將此種方法大量生產，整體的面板製程手續必須改變，對於
面板大廠的固定生產線廠房必須耗費大量資金去增加這一道生產手續[20]。

101 年 4 月 15 日自桃園國際機場出發，於當地時間 4 月 16 日早晨抵達德國法蘭克福機場，
當日即搭乘歐洲火車前往會場所在地比利時布魯塞爾，並於傍晚到達。會議議程自 4 月 16 日
進行至 4 月 19 日，4 月 17 日完成報到程序後即參與相關會議之議程。本團隊發表之壁報論文
發表時間訂於 4 月 18 日下午。會議進行中與西班牙以及日本之全像研究團隊進行交流與對談，
此次會議有關全像技術的發表文獻來自於日本、比利時、西班牙、愛爾蘭以及義大利等國，本
次會期中充分呈現出本實驗室的研究成果。 
二、與會心得 
此次會議為國際性會議，吸引不少全球學者前來參與，會議內容涵蓋奈米科技、光子晶體、
光電系統、光子晶體、生醫光電、微光機電技術、光學模型與設計、有機光電、矽光電、雷射
科技、太陽能光電、非線性光學、光電半導體、影像處理以及光電多媒體等重要光電光學領域，
分成十八個會議議程個別舉行。本人所參與 optical modeling and design 議程即有不少歐洲學者
發表與全像相關的論文，顯示歐洲方面對於全像技術之發展十分重視。藉由參與此次國際學術
會議，本人得以進行學術交流並了解世界潮流與國際之研究水準，對於本團隊之研究主題極具
啟發性，獲益頗多。同時經由此次參與會議可以有效提升本計畫執行成效在國際上之能見度。

ABSTRACT 
A holographic optical element (HOE) simultaneously accompanied with light guiding and beam shaping 
function is implemented with edge-lit holograms in this study. This holographic optical element is generated 
in a polymer-dispersed- liquid-crystal (PDLC) film with 20μm thickness. In the holographic reconstruction 
process of the HOE, the wavefronts emitted from the light source will propagate to the HOE and a quasi 
collimation diffraction beam can be obtained from this device. We demonstrate two applications of edge-lit 
HOE in this study. One demonstration is a head-mounted display (HMD) system, and the other is an 
illumination device for display holograms.  
Keywords: holographic optical element, edge-lit holograms, beam shaping, light illumination. 
 
1. INTRODUCTION 
Holographic optical elements perform high potential for light guiding and beam shaping in applications of 
optical information processing [1-2]. In addition, edge-lit holograms have received increasing attention owing 
to their potential in compact configuration and insusceptibility from extraneous lights [3-5]. Applications of 
edge-lit holograms such as diffusers, reflectors, and back-scattering plates have been proposed [6]. In this 
paper, a holographic optical element implemented with edge-lit architecture is presented. The edge-lit 
holographic optical element is generated by one spherical reference beam with large incident angle and one 
plane signal beam with about normal incident angle on a polymer-dispersed- liquid-crystal film. The polymer-
dispersed-liquid-crystal (PDLC) film consists of 3-acryloxypropyltrimethoxysilane (APTMS, 50.4 wt%), 
trimethylolpropane triacrylate (TMPTA, 21.6 wt%), photoinitiator (PI, 3 wt%), photosensitizer (PS, 0.1 wt%) 
and liquid crystal (E7, 25 wt%), and the thickness of the material used in the experiment is 20 μm. In the 
holographic reconstruction process of the HOE, the wavefronts emitted from the light source will propagate to 
the HOE and a quasi collimation diffraction beam can be obtained from this device. The edge-lit architecture 
of the HOE makes it perform high potential in application of compact display systems. We demonstrate two 
applications of an edge-lit HOE in our study. One demonstration is a head-mounted display (HMD) system, 
and the other is an illumination device for display holograms. In demonstration of a HMD system, we located 
a display panel at the position of the recording spherical reference wave, and we found the edge-lit HOE 
diffracted a virtual image locating at infinity successfully. In demonstration of an illumination device, we 
used a light-emitting-diode (LED) as the original lighting source, and then the edge-lit HOE transformed the 
illumination wavefronts from the LED to become a quasi collimation wave. We have successfully used the 
diffracted quasi collimation wave as the reconstruction beam for a rainbow hologram which diffracted a 3D 
image.

3. MATERIALS  
The developed polymer-dispersed-liquid-crystal samples were fabricated by the following procedures. We 
dissolved 3-acryloxypropyltrimethoxysilane (APTMS, 50.4 wt%), trimethylolpropane triacrylate (TMPTA, 
21.6 wt%), photoinitiator (PI, 3 wt%) and photosensitizer (PS, 0.1 wt%) into the liquid crystal (E7, 25 wt%). 
Subsequently we stirred the mixture until the ingredients were dissolved and the solution was sealed in a glass 
cell with dimension of 30 mm × 30 mm. The thickness of the sample used in the experiment was 20 μm. Fig. 
2 shows our experimental setup for measuring the holographic recording property of the fabricated PDLC 
samples. The holographic grating was generated by two plane waves with 532nm wavelength in the prepared 
PDLC film. The full recording angle of the holographic grating is 16 degree in the air. The diffraction 
efficiency of the holographic grating is defined as the diffracted power divided by the incident power. Fig. 3 
shows our experimental measurement results on the holographic diffraction efficiency. The maximum 
diffraction efficiency can be obtained in our developed material is about 52% as shown in Fig. 3.

5. COLLIMATION ILLIMINATION BASED ON EDGE-LIT HOE 
Another application of the proposed edge-lit HOE is use it as an illumination device for the reconstruction of 
a rainbow hologram. Fundamentally, the reconstruction beam of a rainbow hologram is usually a plane wave, 
and accordingly an external light source is required for the observation of the diffracted 3-dimension image. 
Based on the concept of edge-lit HOE, a compact collimation illumination can be achieved and the whole 
device can be designed as an internal lighting source. The basic system setup is shown in Fig. 6. As shown 
in Fig. 6 (a), the edge-lit HOE will diffract a collimation wave when the spherical wave emitted from the 
LED. And then the diffracted collimation wave was used as the reconstruction wave for the rainbow 
hologram. A 3-dimension image can be observed through the illumination device. The practical 
experimental setup is also shown in Fig. 6 (b). Fig.7 shows the observed 3D image of a rainbow hologram, which 
is reconstructed by the collimation illumination from an edge-lit HOE.
