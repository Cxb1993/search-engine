Consumers — Wedding Cake for Example

National Taipei University of Technology, Taiwan

Tatung University, Taiwan 
Yung-Chin Tsao

ABSTRACT 
 
Package design is a part of marketing activities. The Chinese give the package design of 
wedding cake a significant symbolic meaning in the rite of passage. Since experiential marketing 
is recommended as a must for gaining competitive advantages, the current research, taking 
wedding cakes as an example, is aimed to study the impact of the design factors of a product’s 
commercial package on consumers’ product experience. The research results are useful for 
understanding the relationships between the images encoded in a product’s package design and 
the product experiences of consumers. Therefore, the research results can be further adopted as 
a reference to the future package design in experiential marketing.  
 
INTRODUCTION 
 
In Taiwan, there are approximately 140,000 to 180,000 couples engage each year. The market 
scale of wedding cake industry is about 4.5 billion NT to 5 billion NT in 1995. As a result of the 
change of life styles, the number of the new married couple decreases each year. However, due 
to the increase of consumption ability, the contracted couples are willing to pay more for their 
wedding cakes as long as they are products of value. Since price and product design are the two 
main consideration issues of purchasing wedding cakes, the wedding cake businesses must try 
their best to enhance the value of their products. 
 
According to Csikszentmihalyi (1997), a person's perception of a product is the result of an 
interaction between their mental faculties and environmental contexts. The product experiences 
derived from both media and direct encounters are organized by the consumer imagination, so 
that the consumer understands what is presented to the senses. In what has been called an 
emerging “Experience Economy,” products are defined as a source of memories, rather than 
goods (Pine & Gilmore, 1999, p.12). Prahalad and Ramaswamy (2004) suggest that future-
thinking corporations should shift strategic focus from managing resources and capabilities to 
managing the consumer experience as the primary source of value creation. The future of 
competition rests on enhancing the consumer experience through products at various dimensions.

(2)Texts 
There are three main kinds of texts on the commercial packages of wedding cakes: “brand label,” 
“product name,” and “auxiliary explanation,” (Figure 1). It is found that all product names of the 
wedding cakes are designed to stimulate emotional association, such as “love traveling case”

explanation is mainly used as an ornament. From table 4, it can be seen that the most of the texts 
shown on the packages of wedding cakes is the “brand label only”(42.9%) and the next is 
“product name together with auxiliary explanation” (28.7%). 
 
(3)Graphics 
There are three kinds of graphics on the commercial packages of wedding cakes: abstract, semi-
concrete, and concrete. From Table 5, it can be found that most of the graphics is the “realistic 
illustration” (43.3%) which are the expressions of realistic drawing of portraiture, flowers and 
birds. The portraiture figures with delicate drawing emphasizes on ladies who have aristocratic 
temperament, gentle smile and gorgeous dress attract many female consumers who have a dream 
of becoming a princess. The expression of flower or bird symbolizes wealth and splendor. 
 
(4)Color 
In the aspect of the color of the commercial packages of wedding cakes, the warm color is 
adopted most (86%). It is found that the red color which represents happy is still the most 
preferable color (72.4%) (Table 6). The color of purple is the secondary (14.6%) which may be 
due to its image of romance. The warm colors adopted for the commercial packages of wedding 
cakes are mainly red and yellow. The cold colors adopted are purple, blue, and green. The gold 
color is used a lot as an decoration to symbolize rich and splendid. The colors of pink and pale 
turquoise are also found as a breakthrough of the traditional usage of colors of the commercial 
packages of wedding cakes. 
 
(5)Material 
From Table 7, it is seen that most commercial packages of wedding cakes are made of paper 
boxes (58.9%), the next is of iron boxes (24.4%). The materials of cotton and silk textiles such as 
brocade, satin and embroidery are gradually adopted in the recent years (16.4%).

CONCLUSION 
 
Through investigation, most of the wedding cakes on the Taiwan market are of the Western style. 
Most of the external form of the packages of wedding cakes is square with one-decked structure. 
“Brand label” and “realist illustration” are the regular texts and graphics on the packages of 
wedding cakes. Drawings of delicate figures of ladies who have aristocratic temperament and 
drawings of flowers and birds which symbolize wealth and splendor are preferred by the parents 
of the contracted couples. Red is the most common color of the packages of wedding cakes 
because it represents happy atmosphere. Although paper and iron are the most familiar materials 
of the packages of wedding cakes, it is found that the materials of cotton and silk textiles such as 
brocade, satin and embroidery are gradually adopted in the recent years to enhance the value of 
packages. 
 
The factors that consumers take into consideration in purchasing wedding cakes are package, 
price, and flavor. The interviewees of both sexes share pretty much the same concern on this 
point. However, disagreement exists between the contracted couples and their parents. The 
parents prefer conventional Chinese style, while the couples take a fancy on the Western style, 
and hand-made cookie is especially desirable. The package design is the major factor that 
influences the decision of the contracted couples.  
 
The contract couples focus more on the whole package design of wedding cakes and the issue 
that whether the packages of wedding cakes are of enough additional value to be treasured as a 
souvenir after cakes are consumed becomes important. Since the aesthetic evaluation of the 
package design directly affects the reputation of the contracted couples, the design of exterior 
bag of the products of wedding cakes is another factor to enhance additional value. It is expected 
that the packages of wedding cakes would become a fashionable decoration for engagement. 
Therefore, the packages of wedding cakes should be design carefully to reflect the expectation of 
consumers. The most successful package design of wedding cakes is that one that not only makes 
the contracted people feel proud of but also makes the acceptors feel highly respected.
