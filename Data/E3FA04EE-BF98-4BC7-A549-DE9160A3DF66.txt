以兩種硫屬化合物合成之複合物的微結構及熱電性質探討 
吳慶成(C. C. Wu) 
國立東華大學材料科學與工程學系 
(NSC－98－2221－E－259－006) 
 
本計畫嘗試以高速搖擺與直接淬火(Direct Quench)的方式，在窄能隙材料(Bi2Te3)中形成寬能隙材料
(CdTe)的微結構-即備製兩成分(Bi2Te3)1-x/(CdTe)x 之硫屬化合物，其中 x 分別為 0.01、0.05、0.1、0.15、0.2。
藉由 X-ray 繞射分析(XRD)、電子顯微鏡（SEM）來分析成分及微結構，並在 30 至 400K 之間進行 Seebeck
係數、電阻率和熱傳導係數隨溫度變化的量測。本文中詳細討論(Bi2Te3)1-x(CdTe)x合金之顯微結構及熱、電性
質對溫度之相依性，並計算其熱電優值以評估其熱電特性。在電子顯微鏡(SEM)下觀察到：當 x 之成分由 0.2
改變至 0.01 時，析出的 CdTe 形狀會隨著成分改變由塊狀轉變成為條狀與點狀，並且平均尺寸由 15μm 減少
至 1μm 左右。並發現當 CdTe 的成分為 x=0.01 時，有較低的熱傳導係數及較高的 Seebeck 係數，高溫時顯示
較 Bi2Te3為佳的熱電性質，在 400K 時之熱電優值(ZT 值)為 0.64。 
關鍵字：熱電材料、碲化鉍/碲化鎘，兩成分複合物  
 
In this study, Bi2Te3 was selected as a matrix material and an immiscible semiconductor material CdTe with a 
wider energy band gap was precipitated to form microstructures in it. For this purpose, a series of two-component 
composites (Bi2Te3)1-x/(CdTe)x, with x = 0.01, 0.05, 0.1, 0.15, 0.2 were prepared by melting, high speed rocking and 
quenching technique. The composition and microstructure of these two-component composites were examined by 
X-ray and SEM. Thermoelectric properties including Seebeck coefficient, electric resistivity, and thermal 
conductivity were measured from 30 K to 400 K. The compositional and temperature dependence of the 
microstructure and thermoelectric properties of (Bi2Te3)1-x/(CdTe)x composites were discussed in detail. It is found 
that (Bi2Te3)0.99/(CdTe)0.01 exhibits a better thermoelectric performance than that of Bi2Te3 at elevated temperatures. 
The figure of merit (ZT) of (Bi2Te3)0.99/(CdTe)0.01 is about 0.64 at 400 K. 
Keywords:  (Bi2Te3)1-x/(CdTe)x, two-component composites, thermoelectric materials 
 
1. 前言 
熱電材料(Thermoelectric materials)是一種能將電能與熱能交互轉換的材料，可用來製造
熱電產生器(Thermoelectric generators)或是熱電致冷器(Thermoelectric cooler)，當提供給元件
一溫度差即能產生電流；反之，提供電流則能產一溫度差。評斷熱電材料優劣，可依據 ZT
值：ZT = S2Tσ/κ，其中 S 為 Seebeck 常數，σ為電導率，T 為絕對溫度，κ為熱傳導係
數。低溫熱電材料以 Bi2Te3(Bismuth telluride)及其合金為代表，一般來說低溫熱電材料以熱
電致冷器為主要應用方向，在室溫下 Bi2Te3 合金的優值約為 1[1]。熱電元件優點為[2]：可連
續工作，無污染、無動件、無噪音，壽命長，且體積小重量輕，能致冷，也能加熱，為主
動冷卻，而能致冷使溫度低於室溫。高精度的溫度控制、可局部冷卻、熱慣性小，致冷致
熱時間迅速、具發電能力(溫差發電)。目前以熱電材料製成的各種裝置，其最大的缺點就是
效率低，以 ZT 值為 1 而言其熱電產生效率約為卡諾效率的 20%，冷凍效率約為卡諾效率
的 10%，效率低造成其應用受限制。 
本實驗以熔融淬火的方式製備(Bi2Te3)1-x(CdTe)x (x=0.01、0.05、0.1、0.15、0.2)兩相之合
金。由三元相圖中可以發現，當 x=0.01、0.05、0.1、0.15、0.2 時 Bi2Te3 與 CdTe 皆不會互
融。本實驗依據這個特性，利用高速搖擺爐在高溫下快速的搖擺以達到 CdTe 均勻的混合在
Bi2Te3 的基材中，再利用淬火的方式使 CdTe 在合金中形成微結構，希望可以利用這些微結
構來降低熱傳導率以提升材料的優值(ZT Value) 。本文中詳細探討合金的晶體構造與熱電
性質，並與無添加 CdTe 之 Bi2Te3晶體之結構與熱電性質作比較，以說明 CdTe 的添加對材
料性質的影響。

相差不多。當 x 成分大於 0.05 時 ZT 值明顯的下降並且與 Bi2Te3 單晶相比少了一個級數，

Seebeck 係數是呈現二次方的關係，所以添加越多的 CdTe 最後呈現的結果並沒有提高
ZT 值的表現。

Abstract. In this study, Bi2Te3 was selected as a matrix material and an immiscible semiconductor 
material CdTe with a wider energy band gap was precipitated to form microstructures in it. For this 
purpose, a series of two-component composites (Bi2Te3)1-x/(CdTe)x, with x = 0.01, 0.05, 0.1, 0.15, 
0.2 were prepared by melting, high speed rocking and quenching technique. The composition and 
microstructure of these two-component composites were examined by X-ray and SEM. 
Thermoelectric properties including Seebeck coefficient, electric resistivity, and thermal 
conductivity were measured from 30 K to 400 K. The compositional and temperature dependence 
of the microstructure and thermoelectric properties of (Bi2Te3)1-x/(CdTe)x composites were 
discussed. It is found that (Bi2Te3)0.99/(CdTe)0.01 exhibits a better thermoelectric performance than 
that of Bi2Te3 at elevated temperatures. The figure of merit (ZT) of (Bi2Te3)0.99/(CdTe)0.01 is about 
0.64 at 400 K. 
 
Introduction 
The narrow gap semiconductor Bismuth telluride, Bi2Te3, is a well-known room temperature 
thermoelectric material used as direct heat-to-electricity converter (power generation) and 
refrigerator. In spite of a long history of research, there are ongoing efforts toward the improvement 
of thermoelectric properties of Bi2Te3 and its related alloys [1-5]. One possible strategy for the 
development of new materials is to synthesize M-Bi-Te type of ternary compounds, where M is a 
metal element such as Zn, Cd, Hg, Ga, In, Ti, V, Nb, Ta, Fe, Ni, etc. [6]. However, no new ternary 
compound in the M-Bi-Te system with a better thermoelectric performance was realized. In the past 
two decades, it has been shown theoretically and confirmed experimentally that an enhancement in 
the thermoelectric performance may be obtained in nanostructured materials such as quantum dot 
and quantum well superlattice as a result of the increase in the electron density of states near the 
Fermi level. However, it is difficult to attain large-scale applications of costly superlattice thin films 
used in power generator and refrigerator [7]. Therefore, the incorporation of nano-scale
