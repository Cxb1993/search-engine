Abstract 
In face of the coming aging society and the rapidly developed service science, how to base on 
physiological and psychological characteristics for the elderly population to develop suitable 
products and service systems to meet seniors’ needs, it is becoming an important issue for the 
industrial engineering. Thus the integrated program that has four sub-programs is proposed. The 
details of each sub-program are illustrated as follows: In recent years, the rising consumption 
consciousness makes the GDP value keep up-growing as the mainstream of the job market. In 
advanced countries, service industries affect many economic activities. Take United States for 
example, service industries occupy more than seventy percent of GDP in 2005. Taiwan is the same. 
We can see that from the change of the main focus; it is from manufacture industries to service 
industries. Thus disciplining outstanding service science talents is very important. Currently, the 
orientation of education in Taiwan is toward to service and mainly focuses on Department or 
Institute of Industrial Engineering. We guide the original industrial engineering curriculum to the 
field of service science. One the one hand, it is easier to lead into; on the other hand, it can help the 
service industry talents in Taiwan step into the trend of time earlier.  
In Taiwan, there are many universities gradually offer core curriculum related to service 
including service management, service marketing, consumer behavior, customer relationship 
management, and etc. However, with the rapid change of the industry, these courses are no longer 
meeting the market needs. The problem that the service industry is facing includes the improvement 
of service productivity, changes of service way, innovation of service, and multi-skilled talents. For 
solving this problem, we should reconsider “the meaning of service” for the industrial engineering 
education.  
Therefore, the aim of this project is to provide a new perspective suggestion for Department of 
Industrial Engineering in universities in Taiwan to improve the curriculum framework. We also 
hope this can promote the development of service science in Taiwan and help to connect with the 
world. By “On-line Delphi Method”, this project proposes a survey about guiding service science 
curriculum into the curriculum framework of Department of Industrial Engineering in Taiwan. The 
result of this study reveals that industrial engineering education in Taiwan focuses more on 
technical courses and less on humanities and culture, and information sharing or integrating. For 
above reasons, we should value and reinforce the training of related skills. Also, in order to initiate 
new service science courses and further to train more T-shaped people suitable for the market, the 
current curriculum framework should be organized more systematically. 
Sub-project 3 presents a service science investigation into describing a humanistic tele-health 
care service system for the elderly needs in Taiwan. The investigation was focused on the new 
generation elderly born in 1949 to 1964, which called the baby boomers. the correlations between 
the elderly’s attitude toward the tele-health care system, subjective norm, perceived behavioral 
control, and behavioral intention were discussed to emphasize the necessity of developing an 
innovative tour service model. 250 subjects of the new elderly generation in Changhua County took 
the questionnaire survey while 230 effective questionnaires were retrieved with return ratio 100%. 
The software SPSS12.0 was used to statistically analyze the data gathered and to verify the 
correlations between the variables. The findings were as follows: 
(1) The results for the new elderly generation of different ages, genders, financial status, and 
housing conditions were significantly different in attitude toward the tele-health care system. 
(2) The results for the new elderly generation of different ages, genders, financial status, and 
housing conditions were significantly different in subjective norm toward the tele-health care 
system.  
(3) The results for the new elderly generation of different ages, genders, financial status, and 
housing conditions were significantly different in perceived behavioral control over the 
tele-health care system. 
(4) The results for the new elderly generation of different ages, genders, financial status, and 
housing conditions were significantly different in behavioral intention toward the tele-health 
care system.

「服務科學」的發展背景與意涵 
上個世紀 90 年代，由於科技的蓬勃發展，使得服務業本質產生了革命性變化，尤其是自
動化與資訊化技術，更是使得服務業整體品質的穩定性、服務範圍與內容、服務即時性、服
務的儲存與運輸性、服務數量等均較過去大幅提昇。有鑑於科技乃至科學對服務的影響日益
加深，以及服務也日益需要科技與科學的協助，使其更加具有產業化特徵，故於本世紀初，
以資訊化與科學化為主軸的「服務科學」，逐步成為新的研究議題與領域。Spath(2006) 歸納
出服務業研究領域發展的里程碑，指出隨著服務需求的複雜化、科技與資訊技術進步、服務
品質與價值的重視、自助與自動化服務發展，均促進了不同時代的重點發展與研究。如 70 年
代著重服務管理與服務行銷、80 年代開始強調服務作業管理與服務品質、90 年代發展服務工
程議題等

圖 4-10 提升居家照護服務系統效功能分析圖－以改善自動化服務為例 
根據圖 4-10 功能分析圖，讓我們可以很清楚地了解，居家照護服務系統效能的提升，需
要有效率的方法提供照護服務滿足老人的需求，其最佳的方法即是提供自動化服務(包括：遠
端醫療系統、電子藥盒、電動輪椅等)，讓老人自己可以隨時服務自己。然而，提供自動化服
務卻會帶來許多的成本問題，包括：維護成本、設置成本以及硬體消耗成本(如圖 3 顯示 UF 3：
自動化服務、UF 4：設備維護會惡化 HF 1：增加居家照護服務成本，而影響 UF 1：居家照護
服務系統效能)。因此，我們針對居家照護服務系統之自動化服務、設備維護與成本，進行問
題公式化並進一步描述： 
(1) 找出一個方法解決矛盾，可以提供［UF 3：自動化服務］，又可提升［UF 2：立即滿足老
人需求］，而不會導致［HF 1：增加居家照護服務成本］的惡化。 
(2) 找出一個方法解決矛盾，可以改善［UF 4：設備的維護］，又可改善［UF 3：自動化服務］，
而不會導致［HF 1：增加居家照護服務成本］的惡化。 
(3) 找出一個方法，以改善［HF 1：增加居家照護服務成本］。 
根據上述圖功能分析圖與問題公式化之描述，幫助我們清楚了解居家照護服務系統內
［UF 3：自動化服務］、［UF 4：設備的維護］與其他功能之因果關係。在問題公式化描述之
第 1 項，清楚呈現［UF 3：自動化服務］與［HF 1：增加居家照護服務成本］產生矛盾現象，
其原因為自動化服務，雖然可以滿足老人的需求，但是自動化服務設備缺少整合服務系統其
他的功能，這樣反而無法達到節省成本之目的。例如，老人的生理檢測設備無法整合資訊系
統，居家照護機構之醫療人員還是必需前去老人家進行檢測。另外，在問題公式化描述之第
2 項，呈現出［UF 4：設備的維護］與［HF 1：增加居家照護服務成本］亦出現矛盾現象，
其原因為自動化服務的使用，居家照護機構常需要花費人員維護、交通以及補充耗材等成本。 
步驟 3：產生並評估解決方案 
根據前一步驟的功能分析圖與問題公式化描述，我們發現提升居家照護服務系統效率需
要自動化服務以及設備的維護，伴隨而來的成本問題則形成了矛盾問題。為了解決此矛盾問
題，本研究利用 TRIZ 矛盾矩陣表來產生創新法則，接著，本研究透過在地老化服務業者的
討論，發展改善服務系統的創新改善方案。 
首先，本研究根據在地老化服務系統之 TRIZ 參數對應表，將需要之改善設備的維護與
自動化服務，分別對應至 TRIZ 參數 No. 34－可修復性、No. 38－自動化，另外，會妨礙服務

創新改善方案 5： 
根據創新法則 No. 23－回饋，其定義為採用回饋之機制，來改進一個作業程序。建議管
理者可以整合醫療量測設備與資訊科技技術，隨時記錄老人生理變化，並將資訊回饋至居家
照護機構與醫院。這樣的機制，可以讓居家照護之管理者與醫生，可以快速地判斷老人的身
體狀況，之後，再指派照護人員去老人家，提供精準且快速的照護服務。另外，建議管理者
應要求照護人員，定期紀錄老人使用照護設備之情形，並回報給管理者，以了解照護設備改
善的方向。此創新改善方案，可以有效減少居家照護機構之管理成本，並且能提供即時性服
務，提升服務系統之效能。 
創新改善方案 6： 
根據創新法則 No. 28－替換機械系統，其定義為應用感覺(聲學、光學、熱能、視覺、味
覺與嗅覺)系統，來取代機械系統(現在的系統)。建議管理者多利用人的感覺設計居家照護設
備，包括：電子藥盒使用音樂與震動的方式提醒老人吃藥；老人洗澡完畢出門時，門口地板
會有熱氣散出來保持老人的體溫；利用聲學設計照明開關，當老人起床時發出聲音，臥房的
燈會自動開啟。

METHODOLOGY 
Research Method 
In view of the ages of the research subjects, difficulties were envisaged in collecting information through a 
traditional questionnaire survey. Therefore, this study adopted qualitative research method. Through 
semi-structured, one-on-one, in-depth interviews and observations would be a more appropriate 
data-collection methodology. This methodology allowed the interviewees to speak openly and freely, and 
thus facilitated identification of the demands and preferences of their leisure activities.  
 
Research Subjects and Procedures 
This study surveyed the elderly in Taipei aged 55 and above, and those who are financially sustainable as 
well as having the ability to move around. In all, a total of 24 subjects (20 for in-depth interview and 4 for 
observation) were invited to participate in this study. 
Before the formal interviews were performed, a pilot study was conducted among members of the research 
team to ensure the appropriateness of the proposed questions in the semi-structured interviews. The final 
version of the guidelines for the interviews included questions on: (i) interviewees’ personal backgrounds; (ii) 
A whole day’s schedule; (iii) Usual leisure activities; (iv) The demands in these activities; (v) The obstacle 
during these activities; and (vi) Dietary habits. The guidelines for the observations included: (i) People – the 
elderly aged 55 and above; (ii) Event – the type of activity; (iii) Time – write down the time of activity; (iv) 
Place – the places they go to the most; (v) Object – carry-on accessories.  
 
Data Analyses 
All interviews and observations were recorded and later transcribed. The transcriptions and observational 
records were then encoded into manuscript files for content analysis. In accordance with Weber (1990), 
word analysis, sentence analysis, theme analysis, paragraph analysis, and whole-text analysis were used for 
this content analysis. In particular, theme analysis is an appropriate approach for analyzing opinions and 
proportions, especially when discussing values, beliefs, and attitudes (Kassarjian, 1977). 
Two phases of content analysis were employed in this study. In the first phase, manuscripts of whole 
interviews were analyzed into units (themes). These themes were then placed into the appropriate category 
of the demand of leisure activities. In the second phase of content analysis, manuscripts of whole 
observations were analyzed into themes by Five Behavior Models which developed by the Innovative 
DigiTech-Enabled Applications & Services Institute (2010), which is expressed through drawing and the 
tool includes interaction, sequence, instrument, cultural, physical, and more. An overall understanding of 
elderly preferences, type, demands, and obstacles of leisure activities then developed from the results of 
these two phases of content analysis.

“Environmental demand” – When participating in leisure activities, the elderly will pay attention to the

SUGGESTIONS 
The elderly nowadays emphasizes on environment, social networking, and economy. That is why current 
industries should provide a clean and safe environment that is elderly-friendly. These environments should 
also play a role in releasing stress, increasing human interactions, maintaining a social network, increasing 
fun in life, and adjusting one’s mood. Therefore, body and mind are both very important to the elderly when 
doing such activities. Most elderly these days are very frugal. They will try to avoid unnecessary expenditure. 
So the pragmatic suggestion to the entertainment providers out there is to lower the price as much as they 
can.  
 
LIMITATIONS 
The followings are the problems and limitations we encountered during our study: 
1. Due to the time limit, our study could not do a continuous observation on research objects. 
2. Due to the limitations on the human power and funding, an in-depth investigation couldn’t be conducted 
throughout Taiwan. Samples were chosen only in Taipei.  
3. There are a wide variety of leisure activities for the elderly, our research only observed a selection of such 
activities. 
4. Leisure industry for the elderly is still immature. Related documents are not easy to find.  
5. There are communication issues during data collection. 
6. It is hard to gain complete trust from the respondents in a short amount of time. So part of the data only 
scraped the surface.
