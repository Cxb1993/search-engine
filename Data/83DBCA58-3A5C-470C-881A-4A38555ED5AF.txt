英文摘要 
In a living system, "road lamps" and " electricity pole " are the daily life of people with the closest one of 
the facilities, the two public facilities may be life or Taiwan because of the frequent typhoons and storms, as 
well as material deterioration of the natural aging To make it function and quality of service break to 
inadequate demand; to the two facilities to maintain normal and appropriate services, it must be to maintain a 
good management system. 
The life cycle of this study from the life-cycle management perspective, "road lamps" and " electricity 
pole " of two facilities in the planning design, construction and maintenance of the different stages of the life 
cycle of the current operation, and by the relevant conditions and the lack of analysis, To investigate further 
improve the management mechanism; alternative because of the geographic study of the Taipei City Parks and 
Street Lights of the Works Project Management Office and the Taiwan Power Company as the main study. 
This study will be by-the-spot investigation on the "road lamps" and " electricity pole " to do with the 
investigation records, documents and interviews and surveys to understand the life cycle stages as the use and 
management of performance evaluation, in order to re-induction After the classification of different levels of 
integration lifecycle management (LCM), the life cycle cost (LCC) and life cycle assessment (LCA), and so 
the idea of "road lamps" and " electricity pole " of the life cycle management mechanism. 
 
Key words : Street light、 Electricity pole、Life Cycle

(2)使用材料 
A.水泥含一般養生及高溫高壓養生：普通卜特蘭水泥第一種，應用新出廠貨品，未受潮濕結塊
者。水泥需符合 CNS 61 或 ASTM C150 或同等品規定，廠商並應提供製造廠家之出廠檢驗
結果報告書。 
B.水：混凝土拌合用水，其對混凝土及鋼筋有害之物質含量，不得超出標準，其 PH 值需介於
6 至 9 之間。             
C.砂：混凝土用砂需經洗砂機淘洗，並符合 CNS 1240 或同等品中細粒料之規定。 
D.石子：混凝土用石子應經洗石機篩洗，並符合 CNS 1240 中粗粒料之規定。其顆粒級配應能
符合 CNS 1240 中 20 公厘級之規定，顆粒最長不得超過 25 公厘。

(4)製造： 
A.鋼筋之預拉 
(A)一般養生電桿：鋼筋預拉後之最大應力不得超過鋼筋降伏點之 80%。 
(B)高溫、高壓養生電桿：鋼筋預拉後之最大應力不得超過鋼筋降伏點之 80%，或鋼筋極限
抗拉強度 70%，二者取小值。 
B.電桿之養護：電桿澆鑄完竣後，需隔二小時然後移置於蒸汽養護室，養護室之溫度應漸漸上
升，並應保持在 40℃以上，但不得超過 65℃，蒸汽養護（40℃～65℃）時間應在 10 小時以
上，蒸汽養護後待養護室溫度降至室外溫度時，始可移出拆模（一般養生之電桿之養護以溫
水養護可代替蒸汽養護），並送養生。 
(A)一般養生電桿：拆模後送水中養護池，在水中養生七天後，始可移入儲桿場。 
(B)高溫、高壓養生電桿：拆模後即送進高溫、高壓養生爐，養生爐之溫度、壓力應在 2 小
時以上的時間內漸上升，並維持溫度在 170℃~190℃，壓力在 8kg/cm2~12kg/cm2，其養生
時間應在 4 小時以上，高溫、高壓養生後待養生爐之溫度，壓力在 2 小時以上的時間內
降至常溫、常壓時，始可出爐移至儲桿場。 
2.設計： 
(1)預力電桿設計用最外纖維容許壓應力 
A.一般養生電桿：不得大於 0.45 f’c 即 225Kg / cm2，但 f’c 為 28 日離心機製混凝土抗壓強度
500Kg/ cm2。 
B.高溫、高壓養生電桿：不得大於 0.45 fc 即 315Kg / cm2，但 fc 為離心機製經高溫、高壓養生
後混凝土抗壓強度 700Kg/ cm2。 
(2)預力電桿設計用最外纖維容許拉應力 
A.一般養生電桿：不得大於 0.05 fc 即 25 Kg/ cm2。 
B.高溫、高壓養生電桿：不得大於 0.05 fc 即 35 Kg/ cm2。 
(3)考慮預力鋼筋之鬆弛及混凝土乾燥收縮對預力有 15%減少之影響，故設計電桿採用之有效預
力。 
A.般養生電桿：不得大於 0.68fy（即等於 0.8（1 - 0.15）fy），但 fy 為預力鋼筋降伏點應力。 
B.高溫、高壓養生電桿：不得大於鋼筋預拉值之 0.85 倍。

(B) 路燈 
市區道路照明主要是指路燈及其相關附屬設備；路燈提供照明功能，讓道路上車輛之駕駛與行人
有良好的視覺，達到交通安全的目的，同時促進市民生活便利，能防止夜間犯罪活動。要使路燈能維
持正常並提供適當之照明功能，就須有良好的路燈維護管理制度；同時在明確之照明功能要求下，能
確實執行路燈維護或定期更換作業。 
    國內的路燈維護方式大都採「隨報隨修」；並以「失明率」來評估路燈維修的績效，但是對於路燈
的維護機制尚未深入探討，以致維護成效無法彰顯。 
道路照明工程之規劃設計內容大概包括下列數項： 
(1)現況分析 
因道路幾何條件、路況、交通量及周圍環境等因素均可能影響照明設施之設置，故應調查並研
究分析所經辦之道路各項條件。 
(2)照明設計基本要求 
A.同一路段之照明設施設計應求一致。 
B.設計時應重視照明效率、使用壽命、經濟性及對當地氣候條件之適應性。 
C.燈座最好選擇可調整者，配合折射罩紋路，依實際路面寬窄調整選擇最適合之光束分配，俾能平
均分配於所照區域，不致產生黑暗或特亮等現象，而影響駕駛人之視覺。 
D.對汽車排煙污染燈具之問題應予考慮，以避免影響照明組件而失去應有之亮度。 
E.如管理上需要，設計時得採用自動點滅器依照明需要自動開閉啟用。 
F.多霧地區可考慮設置濃霧偵測器，連鎖自動開啟照明。 
(3)設計規範及準則

(5)名詞定義 
A.光束（ψ）(單位：流明（Lumen）)：1 流明係由點光源以 1 燭光均等光度放射光線至 1 公尺等距
離之半球面 1 平方公尺內之光量。 
B.光度（I）(單位：燭光 cd 或 cp)：由點光源在一立體角內所發射出來之光束為一流明時，則該光
源之光度為 1 燭光。 
C.照度（E）(單位：勒克斯（Lux）)：其最大光度之涵蓋範圍為 0 度~90 度。 
D.輝度（L）(單位：Nit=cd/m2)：由光源或反光面上之任一點朝觀測方向發射或反射之單位面積上
之光度值。

Applying ANNs to estimate the amount of chlorine in air 
Chen-you Jian1, Lee-kuo Lin2, Yu-sheng Chen3 & Chia-feng Chang4 
1.National Taipei University of Technology, Civil Engineering Department, Taiwan 
2.National Taipei University of Technology, Civil Engineering Department, Taiwan 
3.National Taiwan University, Civil Engineering Department, Taiwan 
4.Taiwan Construction Research Institute, Taiwan 
 
Abstract 
Structures in Taiwan are influenced by corrosion, because Taiwan is in subtropical zone, surrounded 
by sea, and wet climate. Thus, researches focusing on corrosion estimation to refer for the 
application of structures estimating or repairing are getting important. In order to estimate by 
scientific formula, it must consider what factors or which will influence corrosion process. Up to 
today, there are several algorithms applied in this research issue, but none of them can fully solve 
this issue.  
This research applies the Fick's law of diffusion to estimate chlorine infiltrate into concrete or steel 
structures. The key factor is to estimate the amount of adherent chlorine on ionic diffusion 
procedure. The experience formula is combined with several data to estimate the amount of chlorine 
on structure surface, such as distance between structure and sea, rainfall, humidity, wind velocity, 
and the ration of sea wind. This study gathers field data analysis by artificial neural networks 
(ANNs) to improve the accuracy of the experience formula.  
For practice illustration, this research collects the amount of chlorine and the environmental data at 
34 sites in Taiwan once each month periodically in 2 years. Then, this research uses the 
environment data as input data and the amount of chlorine as output data to analyze in ANNs. This 
study uses 80% of collection data as training data, and the others as testing data. In the network 
selection, we select two kinds of networks (Feed-forward backpropagation neural network & 
Feed-forward time-delay neural network) which use 1 input layers, 3 hidden layers (20 neurons) 
and 1 output layer. The application of the training result can input environment data (like position, 
distance, angle, and rainfall data) of any site into this training network to calculate the amount of 
attached air-borne salt to help engineer design and repair structures. 
Keywords: Artificial neural networks (ANNs), amount of chlorine, Fick's law, chlorine infiltrate, 
performance index, R square. 
 
1. Introduction 
Taiwan is in subtropics, and surrounded the sea, so the durability of offshore structures has been 
controlled by the corrosion of reinforcement mostly due to the air-borne salt attachment. So, how to 
design and maintain these structures is one of a topic which researchers study. In order to achieve 
the purpose, to predict the corrosion rate of these structures is an appropriate method. The corrosion 
rate is determined by environment around the structures (like humidity, oxygen, chlorine ion, 
pollutant in air, and dust). Generally, researchers use Fick's law of diffusion to calculate permeant 
rate of structures which attach noxious material (like chlorine ion). How to calculate the attached 
salt on structures is the first step to get the corrosion rate.  
Formula (2) and (3) are experience methods to calculate the attached salt. These are determined by 
some factors like the distance from the sea (X), rainfall (R), humidity (H), and wind velocity (S). 
These formulas only figure the chlorine ion in air (Cair). It must transfers by another formula (4) to 
get the amount on a structure (C0).  
In consideration of calculation which computes by empirical rule, this research sets up lots of 
measurement sites to collect attached salt, and uses neural network to analyze collection data to 
improve the prediction methods.

System Development and Performance Evaluation of Track Intrusion in

Lee-kuo Lin1, Chih-chian Chang 2 & Po-yen Wu3

Abstract 
Taipei has serviced metro rapid transportation (MRT) system since March 1996. It is an indispensable 
transportation and becomes the solution of the traffic jam in Taipei city. Up to today, the daily transportation 
is about 1.23 million passengers in average. However, many incidents are still happened from time to time 
during the operation period, such as personnel intrusion occasionally falling into the railway area, and etc.. 
These incidents always cause MRT system delay and even damage losses of lives and properties. Therefore 
the Taipei Rapid Transit Corporation (TRTC) tries to develop several prevention systems including a track 
intrusion detection system and installing automatic platform gates to effectively prevent personnel intrude 
the railway area.  
Because these two systems need to combine several techniques, such as computer software, wireless 
transmitting technology, hardware and so on. Therefore this article will introduce the system algorithm and 
operating structure of these track intrusion detection system and automatic platform gates system. Then the 
prevention performance based on cost analysis will also be discussed. 
Keywords: metro rapid transportation (MRT), prevention system, track intrusion, automatic platform gates, 
prevention performance.
