修平技術學院 歐崇仁
一種溫室裝置，搭配電冰箱使用，包含一設置於電冰箱頂面的溫室本體、一導
引電冰箱之廢熱進入溫室本體內的熱傳元件及一產生特定光源用於照射水耕蔬
菜的發光元件。該溫室本體包括一可種植水耕蔬菜的容室及複數與該容室連通
的氣孔。透過該熱傳元件導引電冰箱的廢熱來提供此溫室所需的熱量，讓水耕
蔬菜能在適當的溫度下生長，進而達到節省能源的功效並同時兼顧環保，並藉
由發光元件產生的特定光源來促使水耕蔬菜達到成長最佳化。本新型之功效在
於：藉由電冰箱的廢熱來提供溫室所需的熱量，達到環保的功效，藉由發光元
件所提供特定範圍波長的光線，達到促使植物生長最佳化的功效。
A greenhouse device to use with a refrigerator, including a set at
the top of the greenhouse refrigerator body, a guide of the waste
heat into the greenhouse of the refrigerator body and a heat transfer
device to produce a specific light source for light emitting devices
hydroponic vegetables . The body consists of a cultivable greenhouse
hydroponic vegetables and complex volume chamber connected with the
pore volume chamber. Guided through the heat transfer components of
the waste heat from refrigerators to provide heat needed for the
greenhouse, so that hydroponic vegetables can be grown under
appropriate temperature, and then to save energy, and taking into
account the environmental effect, and produced by light emitting
devices to induce a specific light source to grow hydroponic
vegetables in an optimize situtation.農業；光學及精密器械製造業
