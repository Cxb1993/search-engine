through the self-designed pultrusion facility. The furfuryl alcohol prepolymer were used 
directly and polymerized in the pultrusion die. It provides a new concept for in-situ pultrusion. 
To study the process feasibility of utilizing furfuryl alcohol resin as polymeric matrix to 
fabricate pultruded glass fiber composites, and to study the processing parameters by polymer 
kinetic analysis, mechanical properties and thermal properties of composites. This research 
project will include the following subjects: 
1. To study the process feasibility of furfuryl alcohol prepolymer: the relation between 
viscosity and temperature of resin, pot life of resin, reactivity of resin, and morphology 
between resin and fiber. 
2. To study the effects of resin formulation on pultruded processing condition and its 
composites. 
3. To obtain the basic kinetic constants of resins and heat transfer during the reaction process 
and to seek the optimum processing condition. 
4. To seek the optimum processing parameters for pultrusion, including pulling rate, die 
temperature, and postcure temperature and time, etc. 
5. To investigate the effects of filler type and content, and glass fiber content on composite 
properties. 
6. To manufacture the cheap and high performance (flame resistance, heat resistance, 
corrosion resistance, abrasion resistance, high strength etc.) composites by pultrusion, and 
to test the mechanical, flame, thermal and physical properties of composites.

幹部。 
2.培訓和提供化工與材料相關行業之工業界基本幹部兩名，提昇高分子複合材料業界人才
水準。 
 
技術研發成果說明： 
 
1.完成呋喃樹脂預聚合物加工之可行性參數：樹脂黏度與溫度之關係，樹脂操作時間，樹
脂反應性，樹脂與纖維形態學之探討。 
以黏度計對呋喃樹脂預聚合物做測試，由圖一可知呋喃樹脂預聚合物在 25~35℃下有
超過八小時以上的操作時間(Pot life)；由圖二膠化時間(gel time)的測定，得知呋喃樹脂預
聚合物具有高反應性，於高溫下其膠化時間相當短；由圖三 FTIR 可看出呋喃樹脂預聚合
物具有高度反應性；由圖四 SEM 之橫切面與破壞面圖形可看出玻璃纖維與呋喃樹脂之含
浸相當良好。基於以上幾點，可判定呋喃樹脂預聚合物具有優異的拉擠成型加工特性，因
此適合於拉擠成型加工。 
2.完成樹脂配方對拉擠成型加工條件與複合材料性質之影響：呋喃樹脂/催化劑(對甲苯磺
酸)重量比 = 100/2.25。 
 
3.由DSC所得之實驗值配合自催化反應方程式與反推算(複迴歸與數值分析)之自催化模擬
值相吻合，而得反應次數為 m=1.265，n=2.329 為一合理值，而 A(Frequency constant)為
1.72×109 min-1、E(Activation energy of reaction)為 64.61 kJ/mol。
