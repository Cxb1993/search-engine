一、中文摘要 
案例式推理系統是工程師開發新產品的
有力工具，以物件導向架構開發的案例式推
理系統可以適用於產品架構複雜的協同式新
產品開發。但是當新案例產生時需要工程師
手工人為的決定新案例應該存放的類別位置
及參數值。本計畫主要目的開發於新產品開
發獲得認證後，可以自動將新案例存放於物
件導向式案例式推理系統。本技術的需求是
因為物件導向式案例式推理系統除了案例的
關鍵參數值外，尚需決定新案例所屬的產品
類別。此一自動化儲存技術可分為兩大部
分，第一部分是決定新案例所屬的產品類
別。本研究運用知識本體論的工具分析新案
例中的關鍵文件以決定新案例所屬的產品類
別，此時文件中關於產品的重要參數值亦將
被解讀出來以供第二部分使用。第二部分會
將新案例依所屬產品類別及參數值存放於案
例式推理系統。 
關鍵詞：案例式推理、新產品開發、物件導
向、知識本體論 
Abstract 
Case-based reasoning system (CBRS) is 
regarded as a powerful tool for engineers in 
new product development. Object-oriented 
framework has made the CBRS versatile for 
collaborative product development for product 
with complex configuration. However, the 
filing of the new cases would involve the 
decision of proper class in which the new case 
should reside and the storage of attribute values 
at the proper slot. The objective of this project 
is to develop an automatic filing mechanism for 
new cases in CBRS to assist engineers filing 
new product cases after the product being 
certified for production. This mechanism is 
required due to the framework of CBRS is 
object-oriented. Therefore, new case needs to 
be properly placed in the corresponding class. 
The framework could be divided into two parts. 
The first step of the mechanism is to decide the 
proper class in which the new case would 
reside. Ontology technology tools were used in 
parsing and analyzing the key document of the 
new case. The attribute values of the new case 
also retrieved for later used at this stage. After 
key document being analyzed and the class 
being settled, the attribute values of the new 
case were put into the CBRS database to store 
the new case.  
Keywords: case-based reasoning, new product 
development, object-oriented, ontology
