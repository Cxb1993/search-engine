利用酵母菌 Saccharomyces diastaticus LORRE-316 於氣舉式反應器進行酒精氣提之研究
The research of ethanol stripping with Saccharomyces diastaticus LORRE-316 using
internal airlift
國科會計劃編號：NSC 96－2221－E－194－035－
執行期限：自民國 96 年 08 月 01 號起至民國 97 年 07 月 31 日
主持人：林昭任 中正大學化工系
摘要
本研究使用酵母菌 Saccharomyces diastaticus LORRE-316 在氣舉式反應器中進行酒精氣
提實驗。此氣舉式反應器為之前自行自行設計之高質傳效率的內環式氣舉式反應器，並裝
置有冷凝管、溶氧電極、感測探棒、滴定酸鹼液或消泡劑等。為了降低酒精抑制，在進行
發酵時，同時以氣提方式來進行產物分離，氣提的氣體種類為空氣和二氧化碳。在氣提發
酵時，使氣體與酵母菌所產生的酒精結合，促使酒精與發酵液分離，以減少酒精對菌體的
抑制作用，使發酵時間縮短進而增加產率和得到較純的酒精冷凝液。經實驗結果得到，不
論是全程通空氣或是厭氧發酵，使用氣提所得到的產率皆高於批次發酵。最後，以氣速
3.88cm/s 進行饋料批次實驗總共消耗了 1188 克的葡萄糖產生了約 548 克的酒精達到 90.3％
理論產量（約 240 克的酒精留在反應器內，冷凝液所收集到的酒精約 308 克）。
緒論
以製酒工業來說，菌體能不能凝聚是一個很重大的特性，在發酵後期菌體可藉由凝聚
而與產物分離，降低純化的成本，例如使用凝聚性菌種來進行連續式生產酒精,外加上菌體
循環回收,重複使用以減少細胞的損失，比起傳統的反應器有著較高的產率和較低的能源消
耗(Kuiyama et al.,1985)。然而在一兩百種的酒精發酵菌中，約只有 23 種菌有凝聚的效用，
而這之中只有約四種適合在工廠使用，在選擇的時候，值得注意（Takashi et al.,1996）。所
謂凝聚是指數千個菌團吸附聚集成絮狀物 (Bony et al., 1997; Stratford, 1989)，此現象會被培
養液的組成,溶氧, pH 值,發酵溫度和菌的保存狀態所影響，是可逆的。實驗發現溫度在
15-32℃之間對凝聚現象沒有影響；pH 值在 3.5-5.8 之間為最適 pH 值（Verstrepen et al.,
2003）。使用凝聚性菌體進行發酵的優點有二，一是容易達到高菌數（Grootjen et al., 1991），
特別是在使用氣舉式反應器（Vicente and Teixeira, 1995），二是菌體易於沉降，僅以沉澱槽
即可將菌體回收再利用，因此操作較簡易，可節省設備花費（劉和張, 1995）；缺點則是基
質跟產物會有擴散限制，當絮狀物的體積增加時，基質必須藉由擴散和消耗部分的氧等來
接近絮狀物中心的菌體，而代謝得到的酒精也會積在絮狀物中造成抑制，這使得整個發酵
過程所得到的菌數量和酒精產率都受到了影響(Sousa and Teixeira, 1991；Vicente et al.,
1998）。
本實驗所使用的菌種為 Saccharomyces diastaticus（LORRE 316），是由美國普渡大學的
曹祖寧教授所領導的再生資源實驗室成功改質而成的酵母菌，可以自行凝聚，並具有極高
的酒精容忍度，使用玉米澱粉為基質進行 24 小時的饋料批次發酵，最終可得到濃度為 17.5
％的酒精，遠高於許多工業生產菌株；再經由細胞融合基因改質，該菌株已經可以使用木
糖進行酒精發酵。使用葡萄糖為基質在攪拌槽中進行批次發酵所得到的產率可達 0.49，為
理論產率的 96％，其他相關研究也已發表。

冷凝器溫度設為-20℃；（3）打開空氣壓縮機，加熱該氣數之空氣到達 35℃。配製 3.6 升的
發酵培養基，將 3.6 升的發酵培養基放入反應器中，以濃度 1N 的鹽酸、1N 的氫氧化鈉溶
液調整 pH 值，接種 0.4 升的二次培養活化菌液，由電腦作溫度、pH 值等線上監測。反應
器溫度:35℃；通入氣體溫度:35℃；通氣速率:1.28 2.95 4.74 6.95cm/s；全程通空氣進行實驗；
每隔一小時取樣ㄧ次，注入高效能液相層析儀分析葡萄糖、酒精、甘油、等濃度，菌體濃
度則以離線方式由分光光度計測量。其操作有全程通空氣批次發酵，厭氧批次發酵，全程
通空氣氣提發酵，厭氧氣提發酵，饋料批次發酵。
結果與討論
全程通空氣批次發酵
改變不同的氣速進行全程通空氣批次發酵的個別結果，顯示了實驗中葡萄糖、酒精、
甘油和菌體濃度隨時間改變的情形。發酵時間皆為 16 小時。由圖 2 可以觀察到最終菌體濃
度隨著氣速上升而增加，最終酒精濃度隨著氣速上升而減少。在氣速小的時候，氣泡僅在
上升流動區內向上帶動液體循環，循環效果不好，且溶氧量低，使菌體無法獲得足夠的氧
氣生長而偏向生成酒精，因此在最低氣速 1.28cm/s 時，有最高的酒精濃度 82.48g/l 和最低
的菌體濃度 16.93g/l；隨著氣速越來越大，氣體被帶入沉降流動區的量也越來越多，甚至會
被回流的液體帶回上升氣流區，在混合良好且溶氧充足的情況下，會傾向於生長菌體而較
不生產酒精，在最高氣數 6.95cm/s 時，會得到最高的菌體濃度約 27.44g/l 和最低酒精濃度
50.656g/l。菌數隨著氣速增加而增加，表示本次實驗範圍內氣速所造成的剪應力並沒有對
菌體生長造成過大的影響。圖 3 和圖 4 表示全程通空氣批次發酵實驗，在不同氣速下所得
到的最終酒精產率與產量，由於使用的基質起始濃度都是 200g/l，基質轉化完全並無殘糖，
實驗結束時間也是 16 小時，因此酒精產量和產率和最終酒精濃度ㄧ樣隨著氣速增加而減
少，產量最高為 0.412，最低為 0.253，產率最高為 20.62g/h， 最低為 12.664g/h。

技術說明 英文：
In this study, we produced ethanol by gas stripping with the yeast
Saccharomyces diastaticus LORRE-316 in an airlift bioreactor. In
order to limit the ethanol inhibition, fermentation is carried out with
simultaneous recovery of product by air or CO2 stripping. This
stripping method can separate ethanol from fermentative broth, abating
the inhibition for biomass, reducing fermentation time and significantly
improved the process productivity. The ethanol yield of stripping
fermentation is higher than that of batch fermentation.

Studies of Thin Film Deposition of Organic Light Emitting
Diode by Using Rapid Expansion of Supercritical Solution
ABSTRACT
This research investigated the film deposition mechanism of organic material Alq3 through rapid
expansion of supercritical solution (RESS) to organic light emitting diode (OLED) application.
The depressurization of supercritical carbon dioxide (SCCO2) into ambient environment produced a
rapid supersaturated precipitation. The high speed particles imprinted the substrate and
aggregated to form a membrane. It was found that SCCO2 at 30.6MPa, 35℃ has higher solubility
to obtain finer particles and to form a thin film with 4.1nm roughness. Moreover, with a suitable
addition of co-solvent Ethyl alcohol to increase the solubility of Alq3, it can save up to 40% of
SCCO2 producing 70nm thickness of Alq3 thin film was re-agglomerated to reduce roughness to
3.2nm. For the OLED electrical test, layers of NPB and LiF were generated by evaporator to
improve the electron and hold transition ability. The OLED device (ITO/ NPB (35nm)/Alq3
(70nm)/LiF (0.5nm)/Al (100nm)) has turn on voltage at 6V and 300mA/cm2 current density at 10V,
which meets the requirements of the OLED device.
INTRODUCTION
Organic light-emitting diode (OLED) is a light-emitting diode (LED) whose emissive
electroluminescent layer is composed of a film of organic compounds. The layer usually
contains small molecules or polymers that allow suitable organic compounds to be deposited.
A significant benefit of OLED displays over traditional liquid crystal displays (LCDs) is that
OLEDs do not require a backlight to function. Thus they draw far less power and, when
powered from a battery, can operate longer on the same charge. OLED-based display devices
also can be more effectively manufactured than LCDs and plasma displays. Hence, it is an
important issue to fabricate the electroluminescent layer in less cost and effectiveness.
An OLED is composed of an emissive layer, a conductive layer, a substrate, and anode and
cathode terminals [1]. The layers are made of special small or organic polymer molecules that
conduct electricity. Their levels of conductivity range from those of insulators to those of
conductors, and so they are called organic semiconductors. Small molecules commonly used in
OLEDs include organo-metallic chelates (for example Alq3, used in the first organic
light-emitting device) and conjugated dendrimers. In the past, the ways of fabricating the layer
of organo-metallic chelating molecules include spin coating, ink jet printing, vacuum evaporation,
screen printing. However, each of these fabricating methods has its disadvantages, e.g.,
difficult to allocate color pixels for spin coating, high cost and time consumption for vacuum
evaporation, and solvent residual for ink jet printing and screen printing.

Effects of Pressure and Temperature of Extractor
Figure 2 shows the images of Alq3 thin film fabricated by different pressures and
temperatures of extractor. From Fig.2, the fabricated thin film becomes smoother as increasing
pressure to 30.6MPa for a fixed temperature. Although the large cluster existed in all pressure
cases, the number and the size of large cluster gradually decreased and smaller at higher pressure.
These phenomena can be explained by two mechanisms: First, at higher pressure the solution in
the nozzle has higher and more stable solubility preventing particle from precipitation before
expansion. On the other hand, at low pressure any fluctuation in pressure or temperature can
cause instable in solubility, leading to precipitate particle easily in the nozzle. Second, the
distance for producing mack number is longer at higher pressure. Hence, the precipitated
particles were collected earlier at the same collecting distance before they collide together to
form larger particles or clusters. Therefore, from Figure 3 scanned by AFM, the roughness
(RMS= 4.1nm) of the thin film at 30.6MPa is smaller than that (RMS= 17.9nm) of 10.2MPa.

Electrical test
For the OLED electrical test, layers of NPB and LiF were generated by evaporator to improve
the electron and hold transition ability, as shown in Figure 6. The OLED device (ITO/ NPB
(35nm)/Alq3 (70nm)/LiF (0.5nm)/Al (100nm)) has turn on voltage at 6V and 300mA/cm2 current
density at 10V, which meets the requirements of the OLED device.
