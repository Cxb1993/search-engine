2Abstract
This study is based on tests done on T/W blended fabric. They include alkaline treatment using
varying conditions, two-phase curing treatment using different concentration ratios of TiO2 and
coupling agent, and varying curing treatment conditions. FT-IR analysis, SEM observation, EDS
analysis, TGA and tests on the fabric’s anti-bacterial property, UV protection, stretching strength,
softness, shrink resistance, and washing durability were also carried out. The experiment results
show that when T/W blended fabric undergoes alkaline treatment and curing treatment, the
coupling agent and TiO2 will be integrated onto the T/W fabric. The temperature of pyrolysis for
the processed fabric will decrease as the concentration and temperature of alkaline treatment and
temperature of the curing treatment increase. T/W blended fabric processed with a 1/3
concentration ratio of TiO2 and coupling agent can suppress or fight bacteria, while processed
T/W blended fabric is good at shielding against UV light. The stretching strength and shrink
resistance of the processed T/W blended fabric decrease as the concentration and temperature of
the alkaline treatment increase. However, they improve as the temperature and duration of the
curing treatment increase; where stretching strength increases as the percentage of polyester
increases, while shrink resistance increases as the percentage of wool fiber increases. Softness is
enhanced as the concentration and temperature of alkaline treatment increase but deteriorates
when the temperature and duration of curing treatment increase, and when the percentage of
polyester increases. Processed T/W blended fabric has a rather good washing durability.
Key words: T/W blended fabric, photo-catalyst,TiO2, coupling agent, alkaline treatment
研究方法：
1. 研究方法:
T/W 織物(或經鹼化處理)→偶聯劑加工液之製備、奈米光觸媒 TiO2 溶液之製備→第
一階段加工：T/W 織物以偶聯劑加工液壓吸→熱處反應→水洗烘乾→第二階段加工：再
以TiO2溶液壓吸→熱處反應→水洗烘乾→T/W織物防縮檢測→T/W織物抗菌實驗→T/W
織物柔軟性試驗→T/W 織物力學檢測→T/W 織物抗紫外線檢測。
2. 進行步驟：
(1). T/W 織物之鹼化處理﹕
a.以 0.30%、0.45%、0.60%、0.75%濃度之 NaOH，液比為 20：1，在 50℃進行 60min
之鹼化處理。
b.以 40℃、50℃、60℃、70℃之不同溫度，0.45%之 NaOH，液比為 20：1，進行 60min
之鹼化處理。
c.上述鹼處理之 T/W 織物在 50℃烘乾 60min。
(2). 加工液之製備：
a. 偶聯劑溶液之製備﹕
以不同對應比例（4、3、2、1、0 等份）之偶聯劑，最高濃度為 5%，液比為 10：1，
調製偶聯劑溶液。
b. TiO2 溶液之製備：
以不同對應比例（0、1、2、3、4 等份）之 TiO2，最高濃度為 5%，液比為 10：1，
調製 TiO2 溶液。
