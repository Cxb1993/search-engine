合成複雜形態和功能性的材料，一直都是令人感到興趣投入發展和注意，
許多的生物有機體可以結合成(有機/無機)的複合材料，並可控制這些材料所形成
的形態，物理性質或是結構尺寸的組成。自然界很多材料例如骨頭，珍珠和二氧
化矽矽藻，被特定的蛋白質或是聚胺基酸礦化後形成，這些複雜的自然材料都具
有高層次且可控制性的結構，且在一般環境狀況下，即常溫常壓下就能形成。最
近幾年，模仿自然界生物的能力，設計多種聚胺基酸高分子自組裝，發展合成的
途徑模仿自然界礦化過程，用聚胺基酸高分子直接合成出類似生物的高層次結構
材料，已被廣泛的研究。
我們想找出一種簡單的途徑和普遍和多功能的方式，以聚胺基酸高分子來
製備中空管的氧化無機物奈米材料，利用同一種途徑來達到合成多種不同的氧化
物材料，並能利用此種途徑的優勢，來控制材料的特性，達到具有良好特性的複
合奈米材料，最後能根據材料特有的應用方面，做一些基本而有效的應用測試。

於研討會中我們發表研究共聚縮氨酸 (block copolypeptides) 的自組裝與仿生
材料之合成，摘要如下：
Here, studies of the supramolecular structures assembled by amphiphilic
diblock and triblock copolypeptides in different solution conditions will be
presented. These block copolypeptides form vesicles that can be manipulated
based on solution conditions, and the stimuli to induce these transformations
can be pH, salt, or anions (for example, perchlorate anion). These structural
transitions are shown to strongly correlate with conformation changes of the
lysine block, and are completely reversible. The size, supramolecular structures,
and folded states of the block copolypeptides at different solution conditions
were characterized by using dynamic light scattering (DLS), confocal
microscopy, and circular dichroism (CD). The results of this work show that
these materials have unique properties of relevance to numerous applications
including drug delivery, and biomineralization/biomimetic syntheses of hard
matter.
Biomimetic synthesis of inorganic materials using these block
copolypeptides as templates will also be presented. This study shows that
individual supramolecular objects (vesicles) formed by block copolypeptides
can be used as templates to form nanostructured hard materials. Inorganic
nanospheres, hollow nanospheres, and core/shell nanospheres can be
synthesized by rationally designing supramolecular assemblies of these block
copolypeptides. Also, polypeptide chemistry and solution conditions can be
used to control both the morphology and porosity of the hard materials they
assemble. Microporous silicas with different morphologies can be synthesized
using these block copolypeptides as templates. The results of this work show
that these materials have unique properties of relevance to numerous
applications including drug delivery, nanodevices, controlled released, and
encapsulation.
