利用槍鑽研磨裝置於深孔研磨加工表面多重品質改善 
 
計畫編號：NSC 95-2221-E-234 -003 - 
執行期間：2006年8月1日 至 2007年7月31日 
主持人：王士賓副教授  共同主持人：許榮宗副教授、陳峰元副教授 
Email: wsb@mail.lit.edu.tw 
摘要 
傳統深孔絞刀與內孔研磨或手動搪磨之拋光加工方式，對深孔加工施行不易，雖可改善
表面粗糙度，但往往無法兼具真圓度、圓筒度等等其它表面加工品質。為了達成深孔加工多
重品質改善的目標，本研究將利用槍鑽特有之刀具修改完成深孔研磨刀具，發展槍鑽深孔研
磨加工技術。 
本研究，設計一深孔研磨刀具，利用田口實驗設計規劃加工參數，將經槍鑽深孔加工完
成後之工件進行深孔研磨加工，量測其表面粗糙度、圓筒度、真圓度等等多重加工表面品質。
並利用田口方法針對多重加工表面品質進行最佳化加工參數分析。結果顯示，深孔研磨進給
愈小、主轉轉速愈大時，其多重表面品其愈佳，亦愈能改善加工表面粗糙度。此獨創之槍鑽
研磨(Gun Honing)加工方式，證實可有效提升槍鑽鑽孔加工後深孔精度使其面粗度達到Ra0.3
以下，幾近鏡面狀態。比較研磨前後，結果顯示，此加工方式亦能改善深孔之圓筒度。對真
圓度而言，雖其值較槍鑽鑽孔後稍差，但均能符合工作規範。 
關鍵字：槍鑽深孔研磨、多重表面品質、田口方法、最佳加工參數 
 
Abstract 
The manners by traditional reaming, inner-hole grinding or handle honing are difficult to be 
proceeded especially for the deep hole grinding.  Although, these working manners have the effect 
to improve the surface roughness of the workpiece, the roundness as well as the straightness can not 
be obtained effectively.  For the purpose to improve the multiple surface qualities of the deep hole 
drilling, a grinding technique, the deep-hole gun honing, is developed by the modification of the 
special gun-drilling tool.  
In this study, a grinding tool of deep hole is designed. Considering the working conditions, a 
sequence of experiments based on the Taguchi method is designed for the deep-hole gun honing. 
The multiple surface qualities for the roughness, roundness and straightness are measured. With the 
developed Taguchi model, a selection of the optimum working conditions is presented.  
The results revealed that a lower feed speed and a higher spindle velocity have better multiple 
surface qualities, as well as an excellent surface roughness. Thus, this manner of grinding is proved 
efficiently to improve the surface roughness by gun drilling even less than a degree under Ra0.3. 
Compared to the hole surface qualities before grinding, a this manner of grinding can also improve 
surface cylinder degree, an exception that a poor roundness under operating range is obtained.    
keywords: deep-hole gun honing, multiple surface qualities, Taguchi method, optimum working 
conditions

第一章、緒論 
在現今高科技產業的機器中，因機件要求高精度以發揮其高性能，故往往需高精度之深
孔加工機器機件，當這些機件之深孔加工精度不良時，常會使機件運轉時產生磨耗、振動、
噪音等不良結果而導至故障，降低機器之精度與壽命。故對於工業之發展，深孔加工技術扮
演了極重要之角色[1]。 
特別對目前台灣發展中精密塑膠模具業而言，往精密模具方向發展，為了提高競爭力，
其加工品質要求日益嚴苛。一些零件，如套筒、嚾嘴、流道等，均要求具有高品質之真圓度、
圓筒度、面粗度之深孔加工。這些深孔加工之產值全世界預估超過60億美元[2]。這些鏡面深
孔加工之零件孔徑約在Ф20mm 以下，孔長比L/D 大於5 以上，一直為業界所苦惱。最主要
原因是其加工時間過長，品質精度不均，成本過高等問題一直未能有效解決。尤其是對不銹
鋼、鎳鉻鉬合金鋼等難切削材更甚。是故，目前台灣產業，當使用到高精度模具零件時，往
往向國外訂購，如美國DME[3]、德國HASCC、日本FUTABU 或MISTUBISHI[4]。因此，如
何突破此深孔之加工要素，以提昇我國精密模具業之競爭能力，實為一重要課題。 
深孔加工品質精度的優劣，直接影響到精密零件的使用功能，由於模具零件之高精度要
求，一些特殊之深孔加工應用，如高精度之射出、微細小機件之射出，其成型機所使用之灌
嘴之面粗度甚至常要求達到鏡面加工程度，不能有任何刮痕存在。一些外國公司，配合各業
別所需這類產品之深孔表面精度，其深孔面粗度經常規定在Ra 0.8精度以下。雖以槍鑽進行工
件之深孔加工具有其優越性，但其加工精度也有其限界。王士賓［5］曾利用槍鑽進行鋁合金
T6061深孔加工之應用研究，其結果顯示深孔加工對硬度較低之鋁合金材料已可獲得高品質之
加工表面，表面粗糙度甚至可達Ra 0.5左右之鏡面加工。但對於合金鋼等硬度較高之材料而
言，如塑膠模具零件常用之H13(SKD61)熱作模具鋼，直接以槍鑽深孔加工，表面粗糙度大約
達到Ra 10μm左右。是故槍鑽深孔加工後，需配合其它加工拋光技術，以提高槍鑽深孔表面
粗糙度之品質使加工表面精度達到Ra 0.8以下。 
傳統槍鑽深孔加工後其深孔為達鏡面，會進一步利用深孔絞刀加工。然後再施以內孔研
磨或手動搪磨(handle honing)方式予以施行拋光。傳統深孔鏡面加工流程如圖一所示。為了施
以深孔絞刀加工，槍鑽深孔加工常需預留0.2mm~0.3mm裕度。之後進行內孔研磨，其加工深
孔大約也只能達到五倍孔徑深度，手動搪磨方式也只能達到十倍孔徑深度左右。故深孔加工
之內孔鏡面加工，受幾何形狀深孔限制加工相當不易。而且，磨石之鬆緊、加工回轉數之快
慢及作用力是否均衡均難以控制，甚至雖然表面粗糙度改善了，但真圓度、真直度卻降低了！
如此兩難之情況，對加工成品表面品質之穩定有相當大之影響。故對深孔鏡面加工之加工方
式應用，實有必要進一步設計開發新的方法去改善。 
本研究利用槍鑽特有之刀具修改完成深孔研磨刀具，發展槍鑽深孔研磨加工技術。由於
是對槍鑽刀具頭改裝研磨裝置，故當完成槍鑽深孔加工後可更換刀具，利用槍鑽同一機台進
行深孔研磨拋光。此過程中不用深孔絞刀加工，直接在深孔加工後進行研磨，較具效率，其
加工流程如圖二所示。利用此加工方式，孔加工深度可達到原槍鑽深孔加工深度，同時也可
改善真圓度、真直度難以維持之問題。 
 
槍鑽其特有加工方式為具有由切削油循環裝置提供之高壓油，它流經回轉給油器(Rotary 
union)入主軸頭(Main Spindle Head)，再流入刀柄(Shank)及刀刃(Tip)中間之油孔，長刀具藉由
防振座(Drill rest)，固定於回屑箱(Chip box)上之橡膠套(O. S. set) 及刀導套(Drill bush) 以支撐
並防振，加工之同時將鐵屑與高壓油經刀具上110°之V型槽排回至回屑箱，再將切削油與鐵屑
分離，而切削油經過濾後再回收使用。 
槍鑽深孔加工時，高壓油在加工過程中具有快速排屑，冷卻刀具切削所產生之熱量，並
提供有效之潤滑效果。由於加工為連續進給，可由電控裝置中依不同材質之最佳切削條件控
制刀具回轉數與進給量。槍鑽研磨刀具之設計，則是根據槍鑽深孔刀具鑽頭加以修改，取代
為研磨頭設計。其外觀與槍鑽刀具在鑽頭部位、刀柄不同，其示意圖如圖五所示。研磨工具
由研磨頭(Honing head)，刀柄(Shank)及驅動部(Driver)所構成。驅動部依槍鑽刀具同規格製
作，刀柄製作則配合較槍鑽刀具刀柄略小使用外徑之鋼管，將刀柄嵌入驅動部以銀銲固定，
而研磨頭係以螺絲方式固定於刀柄之前端。 
根據槍鑽深孔加工孔徑大小Ф12.7mm製作研磨頭，研磨頭之製作為取外徑12.3mm長
100mm之黃銅棒在中央位置銑寬度2.5mm長度57mm之貫穿槽，以六等分方式，其中銑4處半
徑2.5mm之對稱油槽，銅棒一端嵌入M8×30長之螺牙。將2片磨石(L10A5)[7]底部4處加工Ф 
2.5×3mm深之圓孔，各以緊密嵌入4條線徑0.4mm外徑2.5mm自由長10mm之彈簧，將此磨石組
插入上述之貫穿槽內部組成研磨頭，如圖六為研磨頭之構造示意圖。在不改變加工機台前提
下，槍鑽研磨利用原槍鑽機台之高壓油路系統將切削油由驅動部流入鋼管至前端與研磨頭對
稱之4個Ф1.5×10mm深之深孔噴入研磨頭之油槽，以達潤滑、冷卻及迅速帶出切削粉屑之功
能。實際製作完成之槍鑽研磨刀具實體如圖七所示，槍鑽研磨刀具頭實體如圖八所示。 
當研磨頭進入槍鑽削後之深孔，當刀具轉速增加時，磨石將因離心力向外甩出，此時彈
簧將被拉伸，彈簧彈性係數與壓縮量之乘積即為此拉回磨石之作用力。當槍鑽研磨工具快速
轉動時，磨石之重量亦因高速回轉而產生離心力，即質量與加速度之乘積。此拉力與離心力
二者之效應和即為實際研磨切削力之大小。此兩力的合力將決定磨石磨光深孔內孔壁力量之
大小。

ESTIMATION OF HEAT TRANSFER COEFFICIENT IN A POROUS
MODEL FOR SIMULATING A BIOLOGICAL TISSUE UNDER A 
HYPERTHERMIA THERAPY
PING YUAN*, SHIH-BIN WANG, SYU-FANG LIU,  MING-JUN KUO 
Mechanical Engineering, Lee Ming Institute of Technology,
2-2, Lee Zhuan Road, Taishan, Taipei, Taiwan, 243, ROC
*Corresponding author: pyuan@mail.lit.edu.tw
ABSTRACT
This study investigates an equivalent heat transfer coefficient between tissue and blood in a porous
model, which applies on the thermal analysis of a biological tissue under a hyperthermia therapy.
This paper used a finite difference method to solve the tissue temperature field in Pennes’ bio-heat
transfer equation and two-equation porous model, respectively, and then employed a conjugate
gradient method to estimate the equivalent heat transfer coefficient in the two-equation porous
model related to a known perfusion rate in the Pennes’ bio-heat transfer equation.  The results
indicate that the equivalent heat transfer coefficient is slightly affected by the perfusion rate, and is
independent to blood velocity and heating conditions, but apparently increases with a decrease in 
blood vessel diameter.
1. INTRODUCTION
Hyperthermia therapy is a kind of method to destroy or control the tumor. Since Pennes [1]
proposed a simple bio-heat equation model, many literatures discussed the thermal model focusing
the validation of the perfusion term, and the simplicity of one governing equation [2-6]. Arkin et 
al. [7] reviewed many new bio-heat transfer models by researchers from 1948 to 1994.  Their
comment was that Pennes model might still be the best practical approach for modeling bio-heat
transfer. In hyperthermia, most planning system also utilizes the bioheat equation to calculate the
temperature field because of the simplicity of this model [8-10], although this equation cannot
describe the direct effect of blood flow. Khaled and Vafai [11] made a review about the role of 
porous media in modeling flow and heat transfer in biological tissues. Xuan and Roetzel [12, 13]
used the porous media concept to analyze the steady and transient temperature fields of tissue,
artery and vein by a system of three energy equations. The porous model neglects the structure of 
blood vessel, and then used an energy conservation equation of blood with a porosity to treat the
heat carried by blood.  However, the heat transfer coefficient between the tissue and blood in 
biological porous media is lack in literature.  This lack induces an impropriety of porous model
applying on a biological tissue. 
Baish et al. used a parallel tube heat exchanger configuration to simulate the internal convection
effects of blood flow [14]. The measured thermal response of a prototype compared favorably
with the numerical solution of Pennes bio-heat transfer equation (PBHTE). The results provided
the relationships among the tube size, spacing, and the simulated perfusion rate. In order to find
an equivalent heat transfer coefficient in porous media, this study considers a tissue with a 
distribution of parallel straight vessels, which size is identical to those in [14]. This study

4. RESULTS AND DISCUSSION
This study selects the thermal conductivity of tissue and blood is 0.5 W/mк, the density of tissue
and blood is 1050 kg/m3, and the specific heat capacity of tissue and blood is 3770 J/kgк. The
heating time is 10sec and power density is 107 W/m3 for the tissue.  Because vessels are uniform
distribution in the tissue, the whole domain can be considered as an assembly of repeated hexagon
unit, as shown in Fig. 3.  Meanwhile, td is the diameter of an equivalent circle tissue, vd  is the
diameter of vessels, and L is the vessel distance. Figure 4 shows that the equivalent heat transfer
coefficient versus the perfusion rate at different blood vessel diameters.  Meanwhile, the solid,
dashed, and dash-dotted line represent the results when vd =1.14, vd =2.28, vd =4.56 mm,
respectively.  In this figure, the equivalent heat transfer coefficient obviously increases with a
decrease in vessel diameter, and is slightly affected by the change of perfusion rate. When the
diameter of blood vessel is 1.14, 2.28, and 4.56 mm, the average equivalent heat transfer
coefficient is close to 99, 173, and 285 W/m2к, respectively. Figure 5 plots the average
equivalent heat transfer coefficients with closed circles at dv=1.14, 2.28, and 4.56mm, which are
got from the Fig. 4. In this figure, the three data shows a linear relationship between the blood
vessel diameter and the equivalent heat transfer coefficient in the axis with logarithm scale.  This
study confidently uses curve fitting skill to find the relationship in terms of a first order and second
order polynomial curve, and confidently draws the curve to extend lower order of blood vessel
diameter.  The curve fitting formula in first and second order polynomial are as follows.
