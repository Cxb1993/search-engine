碳酸鈉存在下碳熱還原回收硫化鋅中的鋅 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
一、中文摘要 
 
本研究探討碳酸鈉存在下的硫化鋅碳熱
還原反應。我們將裝有硫化鋅、碳黑及碳酸鈉
混合粉末的坩堝置入通有氬氣的熱重分析儀
(TGA)中，進行化學反應。經過一段時間後，
將固體樣品取出，進行 X 光繞射儀(XRD)、原
子吸收光譜儀(AAS)、感應耦合電漿質譜儀
(ICP-MS)、元素分析儀(EA)、掃瞄式電子顯微
鏡(SEM)及表面積儀(SAA)分析。此外，由熱重
分析儀可得到固體樣品的重量變化。 
根據 XRD、TGA、AAS、ICP-MS、EA、SEM
及 SAA 的實驗結果，我們推測出機制及模式來
說明這個反應。 
原子吸收光譜儀的實驗結果發現，提高反
應溫度、初始 C/ZnS 莫耳比或初始 Na2CO3/ZnS
莫耳比可以提高鋅生成速率。另外，降低固體
樣品高度、碳黑凝聚團大小、碳酸鈉凝聚團大
小或初始固體樣品密度也會使鋅生成速率上
升。而氬氣流量的改變則不會影響鋅生成速
率。根據這些數據，我們迴歸出鋅的生成速率
方程式。 
 
關健詞：碳熱還原、碳酸鈉、硫化鋅 
 
Abstract 
 
Carbothermic reduction of zinc sulfide in 
the presence of  sodium carbonate  was 
investigated in this study. A crucible containing  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
podwer mixture of zinc sulfide, carbon black, and 
sodium carbonate was loaded in an argon flowing 
thermogravimetic analysis system (TGA). After a 
certain reaction time, the solid sample was 
removed and analyzed using an X-ray 
diffractometer (XRD), an atomic absorption 
spectrometer (AAS), an inductive coupled 
plasma mass spectrometer (ICP-MS), an 
elemental analyzer (EA), a scanning electron 
microscope (SEM) and a surface area analyzer 
(SAA). TGA gave us the instantaneous mass of 
the solid sample. According to the results of 
XRD, TGA, AAS, ICP-MS, EA, SEM and SAA  
a mechanism and a model were proposed to 
interpret this reaction. 
Results of AAS indicated that the rate of 
zinc yield could be increased by increasing the 
reaction temperature, the initial molar ratio of 
C/ZnS or the initial molar ratio of Na2CO3/ZnS. 
The rate was also found to be increased with a 
decrease in sample height, the agglomerate size 
of carbon black or sodium carbonate or the initial 
bulk density. The argon flow rate, however, was 
found not to change the rate of zinc yield. Based 
on these experimental data, an empirical rate 
equation of zinc yield has been determined.
