三、研究目的與文獻探討 
3.1 文獻探討 
質子交換膜燃料電池中的水包括氣態和
液態兩種，其來源包括陰極電化學反應生成
的水與加濕的反應氣體所帶入的水。其中燃
料電池反應所生成的產物，水，是在陰極由
H+、電子與 O2 在催化劑的作用下結合而成，
而對反應氣體進行加濕處理則是為了使電解
質始終保持最佳的濕潤狀態，從而使得電池
穩定運行。 
水在質子交換膜燃料電池內遷移主要表
現為兩種形式  [1]；一是在電滲力(Electro 
osmotic Drag)的作用下被水化的質子攜帶，由
電池陽極向陰極運動。質子交換模燃料電池
在工作過程中，陽極所形成的H+將越過膜來
到陰極，由於質子都處於水合狀態，當質子
進行遷移時，在電滲力的作用下必然將部分
水帶至陰極，越過膜的質子數愈多(電流密度
愈大)，每個質子攜帶的水分子也愈多(電滲係
數愈大)，則隨同質子從陽極遷移至陰極的水
也愈多。Zawodzinski et al. [2] 研究了質子交
換膜中電滲力對其水含量的影響，結果顯
示，對應於膜中水的不同存在形式，電滲係
數也不同，當水以氣態形式存在時，電滲係
數為 1.0 H2O / H+，而以液態形式存在時，電
滲係數則為 2.5 H2O / H+；另一種形式則是水
在濃度梯度下的擴散。由於電滲作用，膜的
陽極側水將減少，同時由於陰極區有電極反
應生成的水，造成水在膜兩側形成濃度差，
從而產生水由陰極向陽極的擴散，而擴散的
速度正比於濃度梯度。 
在電池中，除了膜電極以外，燃料電池
還需要雙極板來傳遞燃料與排出反應所生成
的物質，在雙極板的表面上會進行燃料的流
道設計，以達到傳遞燃料與反應物的功能，
一個優良的流道設計，可以有效的增加燃料
電池的工作性能，反之，一個設計不良的流
道，將會讓電池內部燃料與反應物的輸送受
到阻礙，降低電池的操作功率。在 1998 年
Nguyen[3]等人針對質子交換膜型燃料電池提
出一種新型態的流道設計，將此種交岔式的
流道設計和傳統式的流道設計做一比較。交
岔式流道設計可以提高反應氣體的交換速
率，增加水合作用以及 MEA 膜的導熱率，並
提高燃料電池在操作中對於過多水量的容忍
度。他們並在 1999 年對於交岔式流道設計進
行了理論分析[4]。在此分析中，雖然以質子
交換膜燃料電池為其適用對象，但是可以藉
由其理論分析了解電極厚度和阻礙物寬度對
於燃料電性能的影響。其結果表示當使用較
高的電極厚度時，燃料電池性能會降低，原
因為增加電極高度將會產生擴散層厚度增
加，使得氫離子通過 Nafion 時的距離增加，
同時也增加了 MEA 的內部阻抗值，因此造成
電流密度降低。當增加阻礙物厚度時，將會
使得氣體流量減少，此時因為反應物減少，
使得電池性能降低。 
直至 2002 年，Arico 等人開始將交岔式
流道設計應用在直接甲醇型燃料電池中[5]，
同時比較了傳統式流道設計和交岔式流道設
計對於甲醇型燃料電池性能之影響。Arico 提
出在傳統式流道設計對於甲醇Crossover的容
忍程度較交岔式流道設計為高，並且在低電
流密度中，其電池性能較交岔式流道設計為
佳。而交岔式流道設計則是可以提高甲醇燃

5.1 不同流道設計之電池性能影響 
實驗中，使用三種不同的流道設計，分
別為單蛇，指叉與雙指叉三種。圖三為電池
性能圖。在圖三中可以發現。使用雙指叉流
道設計可以提供最好的電池性能。其次是單
蛇，最後為指叉型流道設計。在單蛇和指叉
流道設計中，水分排除能力較雙指叉弱，所
以在高電流密度區中，蛇行和指叉流道之質
傳極化現象很明顯，雙指叉則是可以產生較
佳的電池性能。 
圖四(a)、(b)、(c)則是三種不同流道的
可視化結果。由圖四(a)可知，使用蛇形流道
，水分在 40 秒時產生，可以在流道中看見
少部份的水分排出，同時在其他的流道內也
可以看見水蒸氣在壓克力壁面產生。在後期
時可以看見多數的流道中充滿著水汽，但卻
無較明顯的水排出，造成氣體燃料輸送困難
，使得電池性能下降。 
圖四(b)則是指叉型流道的可視化結果
。在指叉流道中，水分的產生並不明顯，與
蛇行流道相似，但水分的產生較蛇行流道更
慢，經過 200 秒後，水分才明顯的分布在流
道中，和蛇形流道不同是在指叉流道中，液
態水的生成量較多，由觀察結果發現，水分
的排出會受到流道的阻礙。特別是在指叉流
道。當水分越來越多時，也發現水氣也同時
會累積在流道壁面，造成水份移除困難。 
圖四(c)則是雙指叉流道的可視化結果
。在雙指叉流道中，可以發現和上述兩組流
道不同的水分產生結果。由結果發現，使用
雙指叉流道時，在 40 秒時可以明顯的看出
水分的排出。並且在實驗後期，雖然也有水
蒸氣在壓克力壁面形成，但也因為雙指叉的
流道設計有利於水分的排除，所以電池性能
較佳。 
 5.2. 不同流道設計之定電壓放電性能影響 
在本實驗，以固定電壓 0.6、0.4 與 0.8V
進行定電壓放電測試，並且比較三種不同的
流道設計定電壓下電池性能差異。其實驗結
果如圖五與圖六所示。圖五為定電壓下 0.6V
至 0.8V 的不同流道電池性能，圖六為 0.6V
至 0.4V 下不同流道的電池性能。結果顯示
使用雙指叉流道設計，在 0.6V 與 0.4V 時其
電池性能均較其他兩組流道設計為佳。 
5.3 不同流道溫溼度測量 
在本實驗中，使用溫溼度感測器測量不
同流道內的溫濕度，量測點分別為前段，中
段與後段三個位置。表一為其溫度與溼度的
實驗結果。根據此實驗結果得知，在指叉流
道中，其溫度與溼度的分布較不平均，但使
用蛇行與雙指叉流道時，其內部的溼度分布
差異性較小，薄膜的濕潤程度較為均勻。並
且由於此實驗陰陽極流場是使用相對流
(count flow)的方式流動，所以在中央位置，
陰陽極的燃料濃度會最高，所以會有較佳的
電池性能，換而言之，該點量測到的溫度也
會較高。

OSCILLATORY DOUBLE-DIFFUSIVE CONVECTION WITH DIFFERENT 
BUOYANCY RATIOS IN A POROUS ENCLOSURE 
 
C.-J. Tseng, C.-L. Tseng 
National Central University, Jhongli, Taiwan 
 
Abstract 
 
The transient behaviors of oscillatory double diffusive convection of a binary gas mixture in a 
porous enclosure subject to opposing thermal and compositional buoyancies are investigated 
numerically by employing the SIMPLE algorithm. Effects of two important parameters, the solutal-
to-thermal buoyancy ratio N and the solid-to-fluid heat capacity ratio σ, on the heat and mass 
transfer characteristics are studied. Steady oscillations occur for σ = 0.9 and N = 0.85~1.00, and σ = 
1.0 and N = 0.75~0.95 as a result of the interaction between thermal and solutal buoyancy forces.  
For N < 0.75, the solutal buoyancy force is too small, and the flow oscillates initially and reaches 
the steady state after the transient period. For N >1.0, the flow is dominated by the solutal effect, 
and always circulates in the counter-clockwise direction. Therefore no oscillation occurs. For fixed 
σ, the frequency of oscillation decreases as N is increased due to reduced net buoyancy force.  
Time-averaged Nu and Sh also decrease as N is increased as a result of reduced convection effects.

1.  Introduction 
   
Oscillatory double diffusive natural convection has attracted some attention recently; for example, 
Feonychev (1995), Tang and Hu (1995), Nepomnyashchy and Simanovskii (1995), Lee and Chun 
(1997), Nishimura et al. (1997), and Wang and Kahawita (1998). It has wide industrial applications 
such as physical and chemical vapor transport processes (Komiyama, 1990), and crystal growth 
(Lee and Chun, 1999; Nishimura et al. 1992). Jiang et al. (1998) experimentally investigated natural 
convection in a shallow enclosure filled with electrolytic solutions of high Lewis numbers subjected 
to horizontal thermal and solutal gradients. Multilayer, secondary cell and mixture flow structures 
were observed for different buoyancy ratios. Similar results were also discovered by Weaver and 
Viskanta (1991) with low Lewis number in binary gases. It showed that the flow was unsteady and 
oscillating for opposing gradient cases.

3. Numerical procedure and validation  
 
Computations are performed numerically to solve equations (1)-(5). The SIMPLEC algorithm is 
employed. Grid independence test is performed by using 61×121, 81×161 and 101×201 grid-point 
systems. Results show that 81×161 is adequate and is used in this work. The time step size is set at 
10-4.  
 
The accuracy of the present unsteady numerical code is further checked by comparing with the 
results of Rudraiah et al. (1995) for Gr at 2×106 and Pr at 0.733 without the presence of the porous 
medium. Their model was based on finite-difference method. Results of the average Nusselt 
number Nu vs. time η for different Hartmann number are shown in Figure 2. For all Ha cases, the

4. Results and discussion

Referring to Figure 4, the mechanism of the oscillatory flow can be explained as follows. Initially 
(point a), the fluid near the left hot wall is heated and moves upwards due to the thermally induced 
buoyancy force. A large clockwise circulation occupies the whole domain. At point b, as high 
temperature region extends to the region near the right cold wall, an upward thermal buoyancy force 
is developed in that region. The circulation slows down due to this counter-directional buoyancy 
force.  At some point, the combined counter-clockwise thermal buoyancy force and solutal 
buoyancy force become greater than the clockwise thermal buoyancy force and the circulation 
reverses its direction. As time proceeds (point c), the counterclockwise circulation grows to its 
maximum that forms a large cell in the central portion. Two smaller cells exist near the left and 
right walls. Finally (point d), the high temperature region recedes back to the left side, and the 
induced buoyancy force near the right wall diminishes. The flow gradually returns to one large 
single cell in the clockwise direction. The process is repeated thereafter.  
 
For N = 0.70, the compositional buoyancy force is too small to sustain a periodic oscillation. For N 
= 1.00, the thermal and compositional buoyancy forces are approximately equal in strength. The 
oscillation is unstable and no constant period can be found. For N > 1.0, the flow is dominated by 
the solutal effects.  Therefore, the flow is always in the counter-clockwise direction.   
 
Figure 5 shows similar results for σ = 0.9.  Unlike the σ =1.0 case, periodic oscillation can be found 
for N = 0.85 to 1.00.  For smaller σ, less thermal energy is transported with the fluid motion. 
Therefore, the upward thermal buoyancy force developed near the right cold wall is smaller. Thus, 
it is more difficult for the oscillation to occur, and hence the higher N values. When the oscillation 
does occur, it is weaker for smaller values of σ .

The variation of the frequency of the periodic oscillation with the buoyancy ratio N for both σ =1.0 
and 0.9 cases is presented in Figure 8.  The oscillation frequency decreases as N is increased. As N 
is increased, thermal buoyancy force decreases while solutal buoyancy force increases. As a result, 
the net upward buoyancy force near the left hot boundary decreases, and hence the speed of the 
circulation.  It takes longer time for the hot fluid to be transported to the cold right boundary, and 
longer time for the counter buoyancy force to develop. Therefore, the period is longer and the 
frequency is lower.

both Nu and Sh decrease due to reduced net buoyancy force as explained in the previous paragraph.
