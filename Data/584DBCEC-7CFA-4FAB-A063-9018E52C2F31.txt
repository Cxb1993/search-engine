The fabrication of self-aligned E-mode HDCFETs (EHDCFET) started with mesa isolation. The gate 
metallization and lift-off technique were used to define a 1 m opening by conventional photolithography and 
evaporated the AuZn upon the p+-GaAs cap layer. After thermally annealing, the gate metal was directly used 
as a mask for removing the p+-GaAs cap layer by selective wet chemical etching (NH4OH:H2O2:H2O=3:1:50) 
with controllable undercut resulting in a T-shaped gate structure and a reduced gate length. Then, AuGe was 
deposited and alloyed as both drain and source Ohmic contact metal. Finally, gate feeder region was patterned 
and defined a window. Anew, selective wet chemical etching was used to remove the materials under the gate 
feeder and to form an air bridge gate. On the other hand, the fabrication of parasitic D-mode HDCFET 
(DHDCFET) also started with mesa isolation. After removing the entire p+-GaAs cap layer, AuGe metals were 
deposited through contact window and alloyed as both drain and source Ohmic contact metal. Finally Au 
metal is directly deposited upon the schottky layer as the gate metal. The gate width and drain-to-source 
spacing are 100 and 3 m, respectively. A cross-sectional view of self-aligned EHDCFET and parasitic 
DHDCFET are shown in Fig. 1. In the case of self-aligned EHDCFET, the length of 1 m gate metal was 
directly deposited after mesa isolation. Then, AuGe drain and source contacts were formed by directly 
deposited using the T-gate as the mask. In addition to the reduced gate to source and gate to drain spacing, 
AuGe metal overlapped upon the AuZn gate also features in improving the gate resistance since we use AuZn 
gate as a mask to form the T-gate structure. A SEM picture of self-aligned EHDCFET is shown in Fig. 2.

III. RESULTS AND DISCUSSION 
Figure 3 show the common-source characteristics of both fabricated self-aligned 
EHDCFETs (solid line) and parasitic DHDCFETs (dash line) base on the same epitaxial 
structure and the same chip. The devices demonstrate excellent pinch-off and cut-off behavior. 
When VGS = +1.5 V, the knee voltage of self-aligned EHDCFETs and parasitic DHDCFETs are 
0.62 V and 2.05 V, respectively. The self-aligned EHDCFETs have cut-off region at VGS = 0 V. 
Applying the same voltage condition with VGS = 0 V, the available drain current and knee voltage 
for parasitic DHDCFETs are 112 mA/mm and 0.71 V, respectively. The fabricated self-aligned 
EHDCFETs and parasitic DHDCFETs are suitable for low power supply application.
