optical array design product with area 120mm x100mm

material. One of optical feature dimension is 4mm x 
4mm, the maximum thickness is 1.8mm, and the minimum 
thickness is 0.5mm. The related optical design and 
gate/runner system of mold design will through CAE

shrinkage of thermoplastic elastomer product molding, 
the correlated processing conditions include melt 
temperature, mold temperature, injection speed, 
packing pressure and cooling time will be conducted 
in this study. In order to find out the optimum 
molding window and parameters for product molding, 
the Taguchi method also be introduced.Finally, it 
expected that the research results can prove a 
detailed understanding including material select, 
optical design, CAE technologies, mold design, 
molding operator and function confirmation of molding 
product via optimum molding condition to improve the 
research capacity.

佈預測有很大的幫助。
