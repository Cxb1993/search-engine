本專題計畫研究是從脈衝磁鐵文獻及過去在脈衝磁鐵相關研究整理,發展出高精密脈衝
磁鐵關鍵性的技術，其中包含磁鐵系統、真空系統、脈衝電源系統以及屏蔽系統的重要元件。
透過磁路設計,來最佳化脈衝磁場以及減低雜散磁場。高真空腔環境的設計，可以減少渦電流
所造成的能量損失，而提升電子束通過脈衝磁鐵的穩定性以及發散度。對加速器的注射效率
會有顯著的提升。完成脈衝磁鐵設計製造能產生除了提供國內科學基礎研究使用。並可將脈
衝磁鐵系統所發展的高精密脈衝磁場技術能在國內生根並推廣到工業應用與醫療用的質子加
速器癌症醫療儀器。

二、進度報告 
 
  今年是三年期的計劃的第一年，今年主要完成偏踢磁鐵(Kicker magnet)與隔板磁鐵
(Septum magnet)原型磁鐵的研發。加速器注射段的偏踢磁鐵，此磁鐵可以將電子以幾個mrad
的踢向電子束的軌道。其電子受偏踢磁鐵踢進軌跡的誤差僅容許為幾個微米(micrometer)，
以確保電子運行的穩定性。脈衝偏踢磁鐵系統為一組四個，四個的波形需要一致，才能使得
電子能準確的踢回設定的軌道中，因此偏踢磁鐵的設計與尺寸建造需要非常的精密，加工精
度需要達到0.02mm以上，才能控制磁鐵磁場的電感。電力電路的設計與配置亦需要高精準度，
以確實準確的控制磁鐵的脈衝磁場強度與脈衝時間，如此一來才能保證四個偏踢磁鐵有相同
的脈衝波形。偏踢磁鐵所需使用的磁鐵材料為Ferrite Ni-Zn (CMD5005)。磁鐵的設計採用H-
型的設計方式，此機構設計加強結構的剛性，線圈也較易固定於磁鐵上。磁鐵的磁路設計利
用Opera/Tosca, Opera/Elektra計算，考慮磁場在空間中的分佈情形，其均勻度需達ΔB／B
＜0.1％。在磁鐵邊緣所產生的三維邊緣場(Fringe field)會有較差的均勻度，因此需要了解
並加以修正。在磁場的計算中，須考慮短時間的脈衝所造成在磁鐵表面上的渦電流以及其所
產生的熱能量損耗。 
第一年研發的非真空式隔板磁鐵，鐵心的部分由矽鋼片薄片所組合而成以確保沒有渦電流在
鐵心的表面的流動且避免鐵心飽和現象的發生。鐵心的選擇上為了要減小脈衝磁場所造成的
雜散漏磁場，以銅的屏蔽薄片做使用。因此為了有效的消除雜散磁場，除了巧妙利用薄銅片
所產生渦電流來產生反向的磁場外(須考慮磁場不均勻性)，最好的方法是供應全正弦波形的
電流，利用負的脈衝電流來抵消正電流所產生的雜散磁場。利用此方法可以將積分的漏磁場
降至 10 Gauss．m。以負脈衝電流波形來消除雜散磁場的方法，並沒有比使用屏蔽材料(μ
-metal)來的有效，所以找到適合的屏蔽材料用於真空內為本研究的一個重點。電源系統部分
利用第一年設計的電源，將改變電路的配置，將正弦波形成為半正弦波形。這樣的設計，主
要利用負電流將正電流的雜散漏磁場抵消，且避免負電流脈衝波形產生不必要的雜散磁場。
經過一年來的努力，設計製造一台磁鐵來產生一個磁場內部非常均勻且低渦流損的脈衝磁場、
在磁鐵外部則是低雜散的漏磁場。進行步驟如以下: 
1. 利用電腦程式 "OPERA"軟體計算磁場設計一台0.8 ｍ長隔板脈衝磁鐵的磁路結構，以產
生0.8Ｔ的均勻縱向磁場，且高均勻度ΔB／B＜0.1％。有效的均勻磁場需達800mm 長、
20mm寬。 
2. 計算並最佳化半正弦波脈衝時間對於內部脈衝磁場以及外部雜散漏磁場造成的影響，以
及評估各相關電力電路設計的參數。 
3. 用Solid Work 製圖程式做脈衝磁鐵機構設計。分析脈衝振動所產生的機構內部應力與變
形。 
4. 計算渦流損失對磁場均勻度的影響及溫升效應分析。 
5. 設計屏蔽裝置以減低雜散漏磁場。

一、摘要 
 
今年度的主要工作是進行偏踢磁鐵(Kicker  magnet)與隔板磁鐵(Septum  magnet)的磁場
量測，以瞭解應用在加速器儲蓄環所能接受的脈衝磁鐵的磁場誤差。脈衝磁鐵系統，操作
在 300µs 半正弦波周期 8000A 大電流；銅導體可以產生在一個磁場內部非常均勻且低
渦流損的脈衝磁場、在磁鐵外部則是低雜散的漏磁場。本計畫所研發的脈衝磁鐵系統，
較易符合脈衝磁鐵要求精密又穩定的操作磁場。本年進行脈衝磁鐵的磁場計算與分析，
以三維磁場計算以設計優化磁路結構，不同的脈衝電流時間，不同形狀的屏蔽材料，技
術克服對於雜散磁場/漏磁場以及漏磁影響時間降低，磁場強度數值分析對漏磁場對電
子軌跡的影响。 
 
關鍵詞：脈衝磁鐵 
 
The septum magnet bends the injection beam before the kicker magnet. The septum magnet is 
located near the storage ring in the synchrotron accelerator. As the space from the magnet wall to 
the vacuum chamber of the ring is less than 3 mm, the leakage field of the septum magnet thus 
influences the stability of the electron beam in the storage ring. The main field and the leakage field 
of a septum of the direct-drive type are compared to one of the eddy-current type. The effects of the 
vacuum chamber and high µ -metal are simulated with full-sine and half-sine current pulses with the 
OPERA-2D TR analysis module; measurement results are presented in this work. In addition, the 
results of vibration tests in septum wall and influence of all of these on mechanical designs are 
discussed.

IV. CONCLUSION
This paper presents the simulation and experimental results
of the septum magnet for the TPS project. Choosing a septum
charge of the direct-drive type with a full-sine power supply has
a smaller leakage field. The pulse width is as short as possible
to minimize the leakage field. In experimental results, -metal
Netic S3-6 decreases the leakage field effectively for the TPS
septum magnet.

MEASUREMENT SYSTEM 
The measurement system consists of a pulse generator 
(DG535) and a digital oscilloscope (TDS3054B), shown 
in figure 4. The pulse generator provides two independent 
pulse outputs to a high-voltage power supply and a digital 
oscilloscope. The repetition rate of the output is 3 Hz. The 
digital oscilloscope has four independent channels, 
respectively using a BNC cable connected to a current 
transform (CT), a search coil or a long coil (CV) and a 
trigger signal.  
The search coil and long coil use the same passive RC 
integrator, which is composed of a 2.2-nF capacitor and a 
1-MΩ resistor. From equation (1), the sensitivity of the 
search coil is 37.35 G/mV, and of the long coil is 12.94 G

Abstract—Taiwan Photon Source (TPS) is a 3-GeV third-
generation light source that will be operated in a top-up injection 
mode. The injection magnets for the TPS storage ring comprise 
one pulsed septum magnet, a DC-mode septum magnet and four 
kicker magnets. These precise pulsed magnets are designed, 
constructed and measured in NSRRC. The pulsed magnets are 
described and analyzed to achieve a precise and stable 
performance of the magnetic field. The magnetic field is 
measured with a long-coil system and a search-coil system to

Keywords—septum; kicker; pulsed  
I.  INTRODUCTION  
Taiwan Photon Source (TPS) is a 3-GeV third-generation 
synchrotron radiation light source under construction at 
National Synchrotron Radiation Research Center in Taiwan. 
The injection system for the storage ring consists of four kicker 
magnets, a pulsed septum magnet and a DC pre-septum magnet; 
they are installed in a straight section (length 12 m) to inject 
the incoming electron beam into the storage ring. During the 
injection, kicker1 and kicker2 magnets generate deflection 4.5 
mrad; they can move the stored beam a distance 16.2 mm from 
the orbit center toward the septum magnet wall, at which the 
distance is only 2.8 mm from the septum plate. The stored 
beam merges the incoming beams at the exit of the pulsed 
septum magnet. Kicker3 and kicker4 magnets then bump the 
stored and injected beams to return to the stored-beam orbit 
center. The purpose of the septum magnet is to deflect the 
incoming electron beam in parallel, but displaced from the 
stored beam, into the storage ring. The kicker magnets are 
operated in a half-sine mode of pulse length 5.2 μs, which is 
equivalent to four consecutive revolutions of the storage ring; 
each stored beam thus passes through the kicker magnet four 
times during the injection period. The pulsed septum magnet is 
operated as a half sine of pulse length 300 μs. The stored beam 
is perturbed more by the septum pulsed stray field during the 
injection period. TPS will be operated in a top-up injection 
mode [1]. The perturbation of the stored beam is required to be 
less than or equal to 10 % of the beam transverse size (100 μm) 
during injections. The homogeneity and identity between the 
four kicker pulses are required to be better than 0.1 % and the 
integrated leakage magnetic field of pulsed septum on SR orbit 
center is specified to be less than 85 μT m. When the electron 
beam is being injected into the storage ring, the shutters of the 
photon beamline in a user’s experiment remain open; the 
pulsed magnets must hence be operated in a highly precise and 
stable manner to ensure an excellent and efficient rate of 
injection and to fulfill the requirements for radiation safety. 
Figure 1 shows the layout of the injection straight section in the 
storage ring. On the basis of experience of the top-up mode 
operation in SLS and TLS, the pulsed septum magnet with the 
vacuum chamber can be transversely moved in a small range 3-
5 mm to decrease the bumper distance of the kicker [2]. 
Moreover, each kicker magnet is mounted on a finely 
adjustable support to allow compensation for the magnet 
misalignment.

V. SEPTUM MAGNETS 
 
 The main integral field of the injection septum with the 
SUS chamber is 4.5 % less than the one without a SUS 
chamber. The influence of this chamber is demonstrated 
with the current output and field. The character existed 
also in the extraction septum magnet, but the appearance 
in injection is more obvious than for extraction. The 
leakage integral field of the injection septum decreased 
signally with shielding.

二、 與會心得 
本次會議有許多不同的插件磁鐵及加速器磁鐵與脈衝磁鐵技術主題，而
且在整個會議的重要研究單位的研究員都齊聚一堂，是一個非常好的學術交
流平台。在本次的 IPAC會議中除了與人討論外，另外也與製造公司的人討
論或所取一些更新的資訊。本人有機會參與此次盛會,除了將工作成果作整
理報告發表論文外,與各國專家,學者討教及學習收獲良多.在臺灣國家同步
輻射中心正在建造 3 GeV同步輻射光源及未來可能發展自由電子加速器計
畫 ; 有待我們的努力及政府長遠科技規劃的經費上的支持. 插件磁鐵、加
速器磁鐵及脈衝磁鐵技術相當專精與重要, 同步輻射應加強學習及研究插
件磁鐵技術。未來磁鐵技術將在加速器科學研究扮演關鍵性的角色, 雖然醫
學生物的科技應用的進展尚未達到具體全面市場化,但未來的發展及遠景是
指日可待. 臺灣想在磁鐵技術上急起直追與站穩技術的脚步 ; 應鼓勵年青
或國內專家.學者參與研究及參加國際研究機構的合作。此次參訪上海光源
對於加速器系統配置有更多的瞭解，印象深刻。 
三、 考察參觀活動(無是項活動者略) 
102/5/17 下午與中心同仁一同參訪上海光源。此次參訪上海光源主要儲存
環、增能環、線性加速器區，並在參訪前聽取上海光源概要簡介，上海光源
在 2009 年 5 月正式對外開放運轉，目前已有 7 條光束線運轉中，尚有 8 條
光束線正在規劃興建中，其主要操作參數 Storage Ring Energy：3.5 GeV 與
Natural Emittance：3.9nm-rad，產生很多篇 Nature, Science, PRL 等尖端論文，
印象深刻。上海光源建築外觀設計理念採鸚鵡螺構造，在空照圖中看起來相
當有幾何特色。
