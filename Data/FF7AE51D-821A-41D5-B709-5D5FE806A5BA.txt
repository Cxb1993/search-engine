ABSTRACT 
The functions of system based on the techniques of distribution database and data mining are created by the concept 
of web-generator, classifier, text mining and co-occur term knowledgebase, including uploading and clustering 
automatically knowledge document, co-occur term analysis and inquiring full-text of knowledge document, 
visualizing and coloring the front of system, and online reading and editing the co-occur term documents. Based on 
the functions of system, we can organize the relationship and find the gaps among documents in knowledgebase then 
we will build the future directions of research in our fields. Hence we can track the co-occur terms of knowledgebase 
following up the forward and backward. 
To reach both of safty and portability in scientific documents of digital contents, we propose a digital content plant 
form for scientific research. There are five functional modules: (1) Personal Portal Management Subsystem, (2) Data 
Management Subsystem, (3) Document Management Subsystem, (4) Content Management Subsystem, (5) System 
Management Subsystem. Finally we will create a prototype to illustrate our digital content plant form for scientific 
research based on the concept of system analysis and design in this project. 
 
Keyword: Portable, digital asset management, content management, knowledge management
