中文摘要 
雷射繞射術是一種非常有效的線距標準片量測方法。在大氣環境下，一個 Littrow 架構
的傳統雷射繞射儀是無法測量小於二分之一真空波長的柵距，此研究提出浸潤繞射術來提
高環境折射率，因此可將柵距量測限制降低到三分之一真空波長。為了驗證新方法的可行
性，以光源為 633 nm 氦氖雷射的浸潤繞射儀及光源為 543 nm 氦氖雷射的傳統繞射儀，量
測相同光柵，其柵距為 288 nm，比較兩種方法得到的柵距值，其偏差為 0.05 nm。除此之
外，此研究亦使用此浸潤繞射概念來測量水、甲醇及浸潤油的折射率，結果顯示最大量測
偏差約為 0.0016。 
 
關鍵詞：雷射繞射術, 浸潤繞射術, 光柵柵距, 液體折射率 
 
Abstract 
Laser diffractometer is an effective instrument for calibrating pitch standards of a grating 
structure. A conventional diffractometer based on Littrow configuration can not measure a grating 
with pitch below half the laser wavelength when the diffractometer is operated in atmosphere 
environment. This study proposes an immersion diffractometer to raise the refractive index of 
environment and thus the new method reduces the current limit of one-half wavelength to 
one-third wavelength. To demonstrate the feasibility and effectiveness of this technology, a 288 
nm grating was measured by the immersion diffractometer with a 633 nm laser and by the 
conventional diffractometer with a 543 nm laser. Comparing the results obtained by these two 
methods, the pitch difference is around 0.05 nm. Besides, the immersion diffractometry can be 
applied to measure the refractive index of water, methanol and immersion oil. Experimental 
results show that the maximum deviation is about 0.0016. 
Keyword：laser diffractometry, immersion diffractometry, grating pitch, refractive index of 
liquid.
