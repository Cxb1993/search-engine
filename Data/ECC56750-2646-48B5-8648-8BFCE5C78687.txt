Abstract 
For a company that produces switching power 
supply, research and development is the key point to 
raise the market share and profit. In the period of 
development of a new product was always delayed by 
too many modifying. This may fail to sale in the market 
in advanced and lose opportunity to earn the profit. 
Recently, many companies apply simulation 
software of electrical circuit design to develop new 
product for reducing debugging time. It indeed has 
improved efficiency of research and development. 
    To improve the accuracy of preliminary design and 
to raise the product market share and profit, In this 
project, IsSpice combined with Magnetics Designer are 
introduced to assist the preliminary circuit design. To 
improve the model accuracy, the model of device 
parameter built in IsSpice are modified by experiment 
circuit.
