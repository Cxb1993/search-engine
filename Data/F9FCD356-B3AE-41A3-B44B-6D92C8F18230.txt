2中文摘要：
本研究主要是合成兩性離子型的水膠，poly(acrylamide-co-CDP) (PAC)，探討水膠在純水與鹽水中
的吸水率，以及對於水泥漿、水泥砂漿性質的影響。
研究結果顯示 PAC可以成功的合成，在純水中的吸水率可達 400 g/g；在 0.1M NaCl(aq)、0.1M CaCl2(aq)
的吸水率分別為 39.3、31.1 g/g。添加 PAC 水膠於水泥砂漿中，能降低水泥砂漿的水分重量損失與乾燥
收縮；亦能增加水泥砂漿的濕度與抗壓強度；再者，添加 PAC 水膠於水泥漿中，能夠減少漿體表面裂
縫的產生。
關鍵詞：兩性水膠、合成、吸水率、砂漿、水分損失、乾燥收縮、濕度、抗壓強度、裂縫
Abstract：
An amphoteric hydrogel poly(acrylamide -co-CDP) (PAC) was synthesized. The swelling behavior of the
hydrogel in deionized water and salt solutions were measured. The effects of the hydrogel on the properties of
mortars and cement pastes were determined.
The results showed that PAC was prepared successfully; its water absorbency is 400 g/g in deionized
water and 39.3, 31.1 g/g in 0.1M NaCl, 0.1M CaCl2 solutions. Addition of PAC hydrogel in mortars could
lower the water loss and dry shrinkage, and increase the humidity and compressive strength.
Furthermore, addition of PAC hydrogel could also reduce the crack formation on the surface of
cement pastes.
Keywords: amphoteric hydrogel, synthesis, swelling ratio, mortar, water loss, dry shrinkage, humidity,
compressive strength, crack.
1. 前言
混凝土在澆置作業完成後，由於表面通常會曝露於外界空氣，故若未加以適當的養護，混凝土中
的水分便會從其表面揮發散失，而影響水泥的水化，和造成混凝土的乾縮、龜裂。養護的目的便在於
使混凝土保有足夠的水分，以確保水泥水化作用的進行而產生混凝土強度，並避免乾縮龜裂，以致影
響混凝土結構之耐久性。[1]
相較於一般混凝土，高性能混凝土的養護更加重要。高性能混凝土中由於有強塑劑的加入，使其
用水量較一般混凝土低，但卻仍具甚佳的工作性；且硬固後的結構緻密，外界水分難以滲入。也因此
養護不當的高性能混凝土，相較於一般混凝土，更易產生自體乾縮(autogenous shrinkage)以及影響強度
發展。
在工地最常使用的「外部」養護(external curing)方法如滯水法或持續灑水法等，此法需要人員週期
性的澆水、灑水、或噴霧等，不但養護費工費時，且在一些場地或時程有不便性。為改善這些缺失，
近年來一些研究便朝向「內部」養護(internal curing)的應用，即將具有保水/釋水性能的養護劑，加入
混凝土中，以達到自我養護(self curing)的目的。[2]
對於將混凝土進行自養護劑的概念，約在1990年代早期就已提出。ACI的「curing」技術委員會的
主席Mather指出養護(Curing)是一種保持早期混凝土內部有足夠的水分和溫度，以便發展出需要的特
性。當外部的養護方式或水氣、溼度和溫度條件適當，自不需採取其它措施；但是在某些情狀況下若
無法外加養護時，便有需要採取特殊的養護方式如內部養護技術。[3]
Viswanath曾發表文章review國外有關自養護劑研發的現況。[2] 自養護劑，一般可以分為無機材料
和有機材料兩大類，無機材料為輕質骨材或多孔性骨材；有機材料則為水溶性樹脂和吸水性樹脂或水
膠(hydrogel)。以無機材料作為自養護劑的研究機構包含有日本Hiroshima Univ, 加拿大Univ of Toronto,
4283HA (來自台塑公司，成分為聚丙烯酸酯)及 PAC 水膠，分別在去離子水中吸達飽和後，再置入
W/C=0.485 的水泥漿中的孔隙溶液中浸泡不同時間後，取出秤得水膠重量。定義原先吸水達飽和水膠
的重量(W3)與浸泡於孔隙溶液後的水膠重量(W4)之相對重量差為釋水率(water desorption ratio)，計算如
右式：釋水率 = (W3-W4)/W3 × 100%

圖 8 為添加不同 PAC 水膠劑量之砂漿試體的水分重量損失。結果顯示試體的重量損失隨著放置時

增加而先上升，然後趨於定值。添加 PAC 水膠之砂漿試體的抗壓強度高於未添加水膠之砂漿試體。在
放置 28 天後，未添加水膠之砂漿試體的抗壓強度為 33.6 MPa，添加 0.25wt% PAC 水膠之砂漿試體的
抗壓強度為最高(37.8 MPa)。
圖 10 為 PAC 劑量對於砂漿試體乾縮量的影響。結果顯示試體的乾縮量先隨著放置時間增加而上
升，然後趨於平緩。添加 PAC 水膠之砂漿試體的乾縮量均低於未添加 PAC 水膠之試體。於 28 天後，
未添加水膠之砂漿試體的乾縮量為 0.214mm，添加 0.25wt% PAC 水膠之砂漿試體的乾縮量為最少
(0.193mm)。
圖 11 為 PAC 劑量對於水泥砂漿試體內部濕度的影響。結果顯示試體的內部濕度先為定值，到某一
時間後濕度開始隨著時間增加而下降。添加 PAC 之砂漿試體的內部濕度在 9 天後濕度開始隨著時間增
加而下降，未添加 PAC 之砂漿試體在 8 天後濕度便開始隨著時間增加而下降。於 28 天後，未添加水
膠之砂漿試體的內部濕度為 60.7%，添加 0.25wt% PAC 水膠之砂漿試體的內部濕度為最高(74.9%)。
3.7 水泥漿試體表面的裂縫觀察
圖 12 為各組環形水泥漿試體在一定條件(風速為 4.5 m/s，室溫)，經過 1 天後於表面產生的裂縫情
形。可觀察出添加水膠的水泥漿表面產生的裂縫較少、裂縫指數(CI)值較低。未添加水膠的水泥漿之
CI 值為 0.54mm，添加 0.1wt% 283HA 水膠的水泥漿之 CI 值為 0. 36mm，而添加 0.4wt%283A 水膠的水
泥漿之 CI 值則降為 0.09mm。顯示添加 283HA 水膠於水泥漿中可減少水泥漿的裂縫產生，水膠加的越
多效果越好。而添加 0.25wt% PAC 水膠的水泥漿之 CI 值趨於零，證實添加 PAC 水膠比添加 283HA 水
膠於水泥漿中，能更有效的減少水泥漿表面的裂縫產生。
4. 結論
本研究成功的合成出一種兩性水膠–PAC，在純水中的飽和吸水率為 400 g/g，在 0.1M NaCl(aq)、0.1M
CaCl2(aq)的吸水率分別為 39.3、31.1 g/g。吸純水達飽和的 PAC 水膠放入砂漿孔隙溶液後，由於官能基
上發生了改變，使得水膠呈現先釋水而後吸水的現象。此結果顯示，PAC 水膠加入水泥質材料中，能
夠將較多的水分束縛在其內部，更能扮演蓄水庫的角色。添加適量 PAC 水膠於砂漿中，能降低砂漿的
水分重量損失與乾燥收縮量；亦能增加砂漿的濕度與抗壓強度；再者，添加 PAC 水膠於水泥漿中，能
夠減少漿體表面裂縫的產生。最佳的水膠添加量為 0.25wt%。

ABSTRACT: 
Hydrogel is a crosslinked polymer which is able to absorb a lot of water. In this 
study, a hydrogel was prepared and added into mortars. The effect of this hydrogel 
on the self-curing properties of mortars was studied. The results indicate that mortars 
with hydrogel could reduce the water loss from materials and maintain the humidity 
compared to those without any hydrogel present. Addition of proper amount of 
hydrogel could also make the resulting mortars with higher compressive strength. 
Keywords: hydrogel, curing, mortar, water loss 
 
1. INTRODUCTION 
Curing is to maintain the moisture 
content in fresh concrete. That is 
important in developing the concrete 
microstructure and pore structure, and 
achieve the desired mechanical 
properties and durability. Traditional 
curing methods of concrete include 
moist curing, membrane curing, and 
sprinkling curing, etc. These are 
considered as “outside curing” methods, 
that could fulfill the curing purpose. 
However, they are not always 
convenient in practical applications. 
Therefore, the concept of “self-curing” 
has been proposed and developed. 
Self-curing concrete is made by 
preparing concrete incorporated with 
self-curing agents, which is to reduce 
the water evaporation from concrete, 
and increase the water retention or 
humidity of concrete compared to 
conventional concrete [1,2]. 
 
Two types of self-curing agents have 
been considered [1-3]: (1) 
water-soluble polymers and (2) 
hydrogels. Water-soluble polymers 
such as polyvinyl alcohol can interact 
with water usually through hydrogen 
bonding, reduce the water vapor 
pressure, and hence decrease the water 
evaporation rate from concrete. 
Hydrogels are crosslinked polymers 
such as polyacrylate, which have 
network structure. The polymers used 
in concrete are often classified as 
superabsorbent polymers that can 
absorb and retain large amounts of 
water relative to its own mass. They act 
as water reservoirs to release water 
gradually, supplement the water loss 
and increase the water retention or 
humidity of concrete compared to 
conventional concrete. 
 The idea of using hydrogel as a 
self-curing agent of concrete becomes 
attractive recently. It is clear that the 
can be attributed to the reduction of 
osmotic pressure between the gel and 
the aqueous phase.

two factors need to be considered. First, 
incorporation of hydrogel in mortars

promotes the strength growth. Secondly, 
hydrogel becomes swollen when it 
absorbs water; the more water the 
polymer absorbs, the more swelling the 
hydrogel becomes. Compared with 
cement or sand particles, hydrogel is 
much weaker. The polymer becomes a 
weak point in mortar structure. 
Furthermore, when water was released 
from swollen hydrogel to maintain the

leave pores in the gel structure, that 
would decrease the strength of mortars. 
For mortars with lower hydrogel content, 
the first factor is more influential. In 
contrast, when mortars contain higher 
amount of hydrogel, the second factor 
becomes dominant over the first one.

4. CONCLUSIONS 
An amphoteric hydrogel has been 
prepared and tested as a self-curing 
agent in mortars The results indicate 
that 1 gram of hydrogel could absorb 
pure water up to about 300 g. The water 
absorbency decreases with salt 
concentration and ion charges. Mortars 
with hydrogel could reduce the water 
loss from materials and become more 
humid than those without any hydrogel 
present. Addition of proper amount of 
hydrogel could also improve the 
compressive strength of moratrs.
