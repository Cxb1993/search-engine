摘要
本研究以 Ti-6Al-6V-2Sn 合金板材為材料，首先利用物理氣態充氫的方式將氫固
溶至鈦合金中，在不同充氫條件下其影響參數包含:溫度、氫氣分壓以及充氫時
間，探討這些參數對於充氫的影響性。再來本研究針對最近開發的熱化學氣態充
氫 製 程 TCT(thermochemical treatment) 以 及 近 幾 年 所 稱 的 熱 氫 製 程
THP(thermohydrogen processing)，加以探討其顯微組織的變化情形以及比較晶粒
細化後機械性質的提升差異性。 
關鍵字：Ti-6Al-6V-2Sn 合金、充氫、晶粒細化。 
 
Abstract 
Hydrogen was regard as a harmful element for some general metallic materials in 
traditional concepts, but it can through different thermochemical procedures to 
enhance the mechanical properties of titanium alloys. By the presence of hydrogen in 
titanium to form solid solution or hydride, titanium and titanium alloys have large 
affinity for hydrogen and can be easily to remove by vacuum annealing. The 
dissolving and removing processes of hydrogen in titanium alloy is reversible, so that 
results grain refinement of its alloys.  In this study, that hydrogenation in different 
effective parameters has been discussed, including temperature, hydrogen pressure 
and hydrogenation time by gas phase hydrogenation. TCT and THP were applied to 
Ti-6Al-6V-2Sn alloy to evaluate their microstructure change, grain refinement and 
mechanical properties difference. 
Keywords: Ti-6Al-6V-2Sn alloy, hydrogenation, grain refinement. 
 
(1) 前言 
    以往傳統觀念認為氫在一般材料
中被視為一種有害的元素，現在已可
經在氫存在環境中由不同熱處理而改
善並提升鈦合金機械性質，本研究係
以利用鈦與鈦合金對氫元素之間具有
很高的親和力，而氫在鈦合金中主要
是以形成固溶氫或形成脆性氫化物的
形式存在，再利用氫原子與鈦之間的
可逆性，氫原子很容易在高溫高真空
的除氫製程中將氫除去，因而達到晶
粒細化的成效。

(5) 結果與討論 
本實驗利用充氫溫度、時間、氫

度(600、500℃、450℃)下進行充氫，
分別在不同進氣壓下升溫至 600℃、
500℃以及 450℃其氫分壓分別為
230psi 以及 350psi（450℃）、230 以及
380psi（500℃）、260psi 以及 380psi（600
℃），再來經由三種不同充氫時間下
(0.5、1、2hrs)進行充氫並加以探討。

和氫固溶量只有 100~150ppm 容易達

0.15，因此 α 相為氫化主要對象，但是
為了固溶更多的氫含量，基於兩相體

βH，但也基於一定體積分率比的關

而會有部分的 α 相殘留或重新析出以
維持一定比例，因此，隨著充氫量的
增加，βH 的繞射強度會下降而氫化物
的繞射強度會上升，當氫分壓增加到

70.5°之 α+H 的繞射強度也隨著增高。

Abstract 
The corrosion behavior of Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5 and (Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5)98Er2 bulk metallic glasses (BMGs) has 
been evaluated in 3.5% NaCl and 0.1N H2SO4 solutions and compared with that of commercial Zircaloy-2 alloy. 
The results of cyclic potentiodynamic polarization test showed the excellent corrosion resistance of the 
(Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5)98Er2 BMG in 3.5% NaCl and 0.1N H2SO4 solutions arises due to the formation of improved 
protective films, compared to Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5. (Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5)98Er2 has higher pitting potential in 3.5% NaCl 
and lower passivation current density in 0.1N H2SO4 solution, when compared to Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5. The addition 
of 2% erbium leads to improved corrosion resistance of Zr-baesd BMGs.

Pitting corrosion; C. Surface morphology

Results and Discussion 
 
Figure 1 presents the X-ray diffraction patterns of the Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5 and 
(Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5)98Er2 BMGs. The measurements show a wide-broadening peak (2θ = 37°) 
without any trace of crystalline peaks in the XRD patterns. Both Zr-based amorphous alloys 
are X-ray amorphous. The glass transition temperatures of Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5 and 
(Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5)98Er2 were 400℃ and 395℃, and the crystallization temperatures were 
479℃ and 467℃, respectively.  
 
The Evans diagram for Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5 and (Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5)98Er2 amorphous alloys 
and Zircaloy-2 in 3.5% NaCl and 0.1N H2SO4 solutions are shown in Figures 2 and 3. The 
corrosion data from polarization curves are summarized in Table 1. The corrosion rates were 
lowest for the (Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5)98Er2 specimens in 3.5% NaCl and 0.1N H2SO4 solutions. A 
wider passivation region and lower passivation current densities were observed for 
(Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5)98Er2 than Zr55Cu30Al10Ni5 in the test solutions. These results show that the 
addition of Er had a beneficial effect upon the corrosion resistance of Zr-based BMGs in 
aggressive solutions. Rosalbino et al. reported [19-21] that the addition of Er to Mg and Al 
alloys also greatly improved corrosion resistance. The presence of erbium oxides in the 
passive film appears to enhance corrosion resistance of Zr-based bulk metallic glasses in 
aqueous solutions.
