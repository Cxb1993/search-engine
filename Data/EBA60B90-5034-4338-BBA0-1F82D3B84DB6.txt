摘要
本研究係將AZ91D之擠型材（As-extruded），施以T6、T8及T9等不同的熱處理與熱機
處理（TMT）方式，再針對各處理試片，觀察固溶處理、時效處理及熱機處理後AZ91D鎂
合金中析出β相(Al12Mg17)之型態，進而了解熱處理與熱機處理改變Al12Mg17型態之效果，以
及不同Al12Mg17型態對AZ91D鎂合金硬度與抗拉強度之影響。實驗結果顯示，AZ91D鎂合
金在經過T6、T8 及T9處理後其硬度值會明顯的上升，特別是T8、T9處理，可有效地藉由
熱機處理直接使Al12Mg17相產生不連續的顆粒狀析出，其均勻分佈在鎂基地內，進而提升
析出強化的效果。
拉伸試驗之結果可知，經T6處理後，則以時效溫度200℃下可獲得明顯高於擠型材之抗
拉強度值，而經由T8、T9處理，亦可發現二者之抗拉強度均明顯提高，且更優於T6處理。
研究結果可知 AZ91D擠型材可藉由熱機處理，透過改變β相(Al12Mg17)的析出型態來
達到強化之效果，進而獲得高硬度及高強度。
關鍵字：AZ91D鎂合金、固溶處理、人工時效、熱機處理
Abstract
This research used different thermal treatment as well as thermal mechanical treatment to
modify the morphology of Al12Mg17 precipitates, and improve the mechanical properties of
AZ91D alloy.
The results show that the hardness of AZ91D treated by T6, T8 and T9 were increasing
remarkably. Especially, the Al12Mg17 precipitates could be segmented into small discontinues
particles distributing in the Mg matrix by T8 or T9 treatment. Therefore, the precipitation
strengthening effect of T8 or T9 treated AZ91D alloys are obvious.
From the tensile test results, the strength of AZ91D after T6 treatment aging at 200℃ is
higher than that of the extruded one. Besides, the strengths of T8 or T9 treated AZ91D are
superior to T6 treatment. Consequently, the morphology of Al12Mg17 precipitates can be
refined and uniform distributed in the matrix, and the mechanical properties can be improved by
mean of the thermal mechanical treatment.
Key word：AZ91D magnesium alloy, solid solution treatment, aging, thermal mechanical
treatment

1. AZ91D擠型材之顯微組織
圖二為A Z91D擠型材之光學顯微組織，因高溫擠型的關係導致擠製過程中會有動態再
結晶而形成等軸晶，且由於受力不均勻而使得β相(Al12Mg17)產生群聚的效應。
再以EDX定性半定量的分析其基地與第二相之成份，分別如圖三（a）及（b）所示，
結果可知，基地為α-Mg而第二相則為β相(Al12Mg17)之結構。以維克氏硬度試驗（Vickers
hardness）測試擠型材之硬度則為HV71。
2. 熱處理前後之硬度變化
2.1 T6處理後之硬度變化
固溶處理後之硬度為HV65，比擠型材的HV71低，其原因推測為固溶處理後，β相固溶
基地內，且晶粒略為成長及組織均一化(如圖四所示)。明顯地看出固溶處理之組織呈現出單
一相，顯示β相(Al12Mg17)已固溶於基地內。
圖五分別為200℃及250℃下之時效曲線，結果可知，時效硬度隨時間增加而提高，主
要原因為析出β相(Al12Mg17)的量增加所致。
然而，由時效硬化曲線可知，硬度達到最高尖蜂值後即呈現飽和現象，主要原因為
AZ91D之析出相均以層狀析出為主，當層狀析出的量析出完全後，硬度即達飽和值【12】。
同時可發現時效溫度200℃之尖峰時效（peak-aging）明顯高於250℃，此現象與一般析
出相同，在較低溫時（200℃）其成核速率大於晶粒成長速率，而析出較細之層狀組織，因
此其強化效果較250℃來的好。
2.2 T8處理後之硬度變化
先分別在200℃下，以30%、60%之總軋延率將AZ91D擠型材軋延成薄板，再分別於
200℃及250℃不同時效時間下時效處理。圖六（a）及（b）分別為其時效曲線。由圖可知，
T8處理後之硬度變化與T6處理相似，硬度達尖峰值後即呈現飽和，推測其層狀組織完全析
出後，將使硬度趨於飽和【15】。
其次，分別比較圖六（a）、（b）200℃及250℃的二條時效硬化曲線可知，軋延率60%
的試片其時效硬化的效果明顯優於軋延率30%及未軋延的試片，推測層狀之β相(Al12Mg17)
的析出型態已經改變，直接以顆粒狀的方式在鎂基地上析出，將對差排滑移行成更好的阻
礙效果。
另外，再行比較各時效硬化曲線的尖峰時效（peak-aging），如表二所示，可明顯發現，
尖峰時效隨著軋延率的增加而提前出現，究其原因，為軋延後之試片其內含有因冷加工所
帶來的大量應變能，在時效的過程中，該應變能便提供了β相(Al12Mg17)成核時所需的驅動
力（driving force），導致尖峰時效（peak-aging）提早發生。
2.3 T9處理後之硬度變化
將T6處理後所得之尖峰時效（peak-aging）試片，進行滾軋，其結果如表三所示，試片
經軋延後，其硬度值有明顯的提升，推測為試片透過軋延，將時效後所析出的層狀β相
(Al12Mg17)打斷，令其產生不連續的分佈，進而提高時效的強化效果。而總軋延率達60%的
試片，其硬度值明顯高於總軋延率達30%之試片，究其原因，推測為較大之軋延率，除可
使層狀β相(Al12Mg17)分斷外，更可使β相(Al12Mg17)均勻地分佈在鎂基地內，進一步的增加
時效強化的效果。
3. 熱處理前後之抗拉強度
3.1 固溶處理前後之抗拉強度
如圖七所示，可明顯地看出擠型材的極限強度及降伏強度皆高於固溶後的試片，其主

Experimental Procedures
The AZ91 billets, 7”in diameter, were first heated to 350C and then extruded into a sheet metal
100 mm in thickness. The sheet metal was further hot rolled at 300 ºC to a thickness of 2 mm.
Specimens of AZ91, 30 mm3 mm, were cut for photocatalytic deposition.
A thin film of TiO2 was deposited on the surface of the samples using DC magnetron sputtering
under the following sputtering conditions: substrate temperature, 250C, 470 volts, vacuum
pressure = 2 mm torr, argon gas flux = 40 sccm, oxygen gas flux = 7 sccm. Field emission scanning
electron microscopy (FE-SEM) and transmission electron microscopy (TEM) were used to observe
the microstructure of the deposited films and AZ91 substrate. The chemical composition of the
films and the substrate were analyzed using energy dispersive spectroscopy (EDS).
The phase structure of the thin film was examined using low angle (1.5) X-ray diffraction (XRD).
Testing for photocatalytic characteristics was conducted by measuring the contact angle. For contact
angle measurement, the samples of AZ91 substrate, both with and without thin film deposition,
were exposed to ultraviolet light, and the contact angles of water were measured at an interval of ten
minutes.
Corrosion test was performed by using a constant potentiometer to measure the potentiodynamic
polarization curves of TiO2-deposited and uncoated substrates. Each test was conducted in a
corrosion cell that contained 300 ml of 0.6 M NaCl solution at room temperature at a scan rate of
0.5 mV-1. All the electrochemical measurements were made using a Princeton Applied
Research model 263A potentiostat/galvanostat with its M352 software. Platinum gauze was used
as a counter electrode and silver/silver chloride (Ag/AgCl) was the reference

Crystal Structure of Photo-catalytic Thin Films
Of the three possible crystal morphologies of TiO2–Rutile, Anatase, Brookite–Anatase shows the
strongest photo-catalytic properties [14]. Figure 1 shows the low angle XRD spectrum for the
deposited film. The distinct crystalline Anatase phase with photo-catalytic characteristics can be
identified from the spectrum. Also present in the spectrum are Mg-peaks, which appeared because
and AZ91 substrate. The EDS analysis of AZ91 substrate and TiO2 thin film is shown in Figure 8,
which unambiguously shows the film composition to be TiO2. Figure 9 shows the TEM image of
the cross-section of TiO2 film. The columnar structure of the film indicated the crystallinity of TiO2.
From the above data, it can be stated that the morphology of TiO2 thin film was not fully dense with
many pinholes on the surface. Under corrosive environments, oxygen molecules penetrate into
AZ91 substrate and accelerated the corrosion rate. Prevention of pinhole formation will crucial in
ensuring the protective function of TiO2 films.
Conclusions
The following conclusion can be summarized from the present study:
1. Anatase-TiO2 thin film can be deposited on AZ91 substrate using magnetron-sputtering
technique.
2. AZ91 substrates, deposited with a thin film of TiO2, exhibit distinct hydrophilic property, as
illustrated by the contact angle measurements on samples exposed to UV light.
3. The corrosion potential of TiO2 thin film is higher than that of AZ91 Mg-alloy, suggesting the
corrosion-resistant property of TiO2 thin films.
4. The poor overall corrosion resistance observed in the TiO2-deposited substrate is caused by the
presence of pinholes on the deposited film, which was not fully dense and accordingly, gave rise to
Galvanic cell effect with accelerated corrosion. It can be predicted that prevention of pinhole
formation on TiO2 film will improve corrosion resistance and achieve photo-catalytic effect.
