三、基因演算法 
基因演算法主要是根據達爾文的「進
化論」觀念而來，用此來模擬生物界中「適
者生存，不適者淘汰」的生存演化法則[6]。
演化過程是藉由模擬生物基因的複製、交
配及突變等三種基因運算子的能力，用此
方式來找出擁有最好適應值的子代。 
基因演算法是以生物演化的機制所發
展出來的蒐尋法則，其較不容易陷入局部
最佳解(Local Optimum)的特性使得有較好
的 機 會 求 得 全 域 最 佳 解 (Global 
Optimum)，因此目前已應用在許多工程領
域的最佳化上[7-9]，圖 3 為基因演算法的
流程。

層神經元數目的總和。 
五、實作結果與分析 
本計畫所建構之非侵入式負載監測系
統在作法上是先將所輸入之原始特徵向量
即每個特徵值使用基因演算法做適當的取
捨及尺度化，以找出能使系統具有最大辨
識成效的特徵向量組合。實作系統架構圖
如圖 6 所示，本計畫是直接將市電接至自
耦變壓器，然後再由自耦變壓器接至匯流
排，匯流排的種類有 220 伏特或 110 伏特，
以降壓變壓器將電壓變壓後送入匯流排。 
於匯流排上，分別使用比壓器和比流
器量測電壓訊號和電流訊號，所量測到的
電壓訊號和電流訊號經過 AD 轉換盒和資
料擷取卡得到數位資料，並藉由撰寫好的
LabVIEW 程式與經由 ADX 3000 所量測到
的訊號做雙重的比對，以確認訊號資料的
準確性，然後再作適當的處理並儲存。

部分為程式方塊 MATLAB Script，如圖 7

載 2 及負載 3 的資料。第三部份為負載判

於 0.9 時才視為負載正在使用。

5.3 七負載組合的案例 
七負載組合時，每一種負載組合皆有
11 種電壓值，故資料總數共有 1397 筆。如
同三負載實驗，將此 1397 筆原始特徵向量
直接由類神經網路辨識系統做辨識，或將
此1397筆原始特徵向量送入基因演算法作
演化運算以求得最佳化特徵，再將所得的
新特徵向量輸入類神經網路辨識系統以獲
得各個負載組合情況的辨識率，以便於比
 10
果，並沒有如同三負載的實驗一樣，辨識
結果可以提升至所預期的八成以上。而三
負載的資料量較少，所以類神經網路的隱
藏層只需使用單一隱藏層，故運算時間較
短。當在執行七負載之案例時，由於資料
量較大，若只使用單一隱藏層，將使辨識
率大幅的降低，故七負載實驗所使用的是
兩層隱藏層，雖然辨識率可以提高，卻也
使運算時間大幅的增加。 
本計畫提出在非侵入式負載監測系統
上使用不同的電力特徵將影響負載辨識的
結果，案例一中不管使用何種電力特徵，
負載辨識結果皆為 100%，相對於案例二因
為其中負載為具有相同實虛功率之ㄧ致性
負載，因此若以實虛功率作為電力特徵
時，將使得負載辨識率大為降低。未來本
計畫的研究方向將持續尋找更能代表負載
的電力特徵，並提出應用小波轉換於各階
分析的結果，分析負載運轉的物理特性，
作為日後建立負載模型的依據與參考。

(1) 為了克服傳統非侵入式能源管理系統的缺點，本計畫提出使用傳統穩態的電

率與虛功率的情況下。

(5) 將所辨識出使用的各項家庭負載在其消耗電力能源的需量作計算，計算出負

Abstract— In response to the governmental policy of saving 
energy sources and reducing CO2, and carry out the resident 
quality of local; this paper proposes a new method for a non-
intrusive energy management (NIEM) system in smart home to 
implement the load identification of electric equipments and 
establish the electric demand management. Non-intrusive 
energy management techniques were often based on power 
signatures in the past, these techniques are necessary to be 
improved for the results of reliability and accuracy of 
recognition. By using neural network (NN) in combination 
with genetic programming (GP) and turn-on transient energy 
analysis, this study attempts to identify load demands and 
improve recognition accuracy of non-intrusive energy-
managing results. The turn-on transient energy signature can 
improve the efficiency of load identification and computational 
time under multiple operations. 
Keywords- smart home; load identification; artificial neural

VI. MULTI-LAYER FEEDFORWARD NEURAL NETWORK 
Most back-propagation (BP) neural network applications 
employ single- or multi-layer perceptron networks using 
gradient-descent training techniques, with learning by back 
propagation. These multi-layer perceptrons can be trained 
with supervision using analytical functions to activate 
network nodes (“neurons”) and by applying a backward 
error-propagation algorithm to update interconnecting 
weights and thresholds until proper recognition capability is 
attained. In the present study, the back-propagation 
classifier is generally used as a trainable classifier for a 
multi-layer feedforward neural network (MFNN). 
“Classification” in this context denotes a mapping from a 
feature space to the set of class labels – the names of 
commercial or industrial load combinations. 
A supervised MFNN is generally divided into three 
layers: input, hidden, and output, including neurons. The 
neurons are connected by links with weights that are 
selected to meet the desired associations between the input 
and output neurons. These weights should be trained with 
existing input-output pairs using an appropriate algorithm. 
An appropriate momentum and learning rate should be 
given during the training phase. The purpose of the MFNN 
in this paper is to identify loads of the NIEM system. The 
MFNN based on the back-propagation method is adopted in 
this paper and this ANN can identify the similarity between 
given data and know data [27]. The input, output and hidden 
layers of the ANN are described as follows: 
1) Input layer: the power signature information 
including the turn-on transient energy for an 
electrical service entry severs as inputs. 
2) Output layer: the number of output neurons is the 
same that of the identified individual appliances. 
Each binary bit serves as a load indicator for the 
ON/OFF status. 
3) Hidden layer: Only one hidden layer is used in this 
paper. Some heuristics have been proposed to 
determine the number of neurons in hidden layer [28]. 
The common number of neurons for the hidden layer 
is (number of input neurons + number of output 
neurons)/ 2 or (number of input neurons + number of 
output neurons) 0.5. The simulation results show no 
significant difference between these two alternatives.

An e-marketplace is a virtual market where Buyers and 
sellers meet just like in a traditional market just that in this 
case, all interactions are done virtually. E-marketplace 
includes b2b, b2c, c2c etc. 
E-Health is a wide and fast-growing field with many 
implications on how healthcare will be delivered in the 
future. The aged are large users of medical care. Most wish 
to remain at home rather than be in a hospital setting and e-
Health will assist in this. E-Health can enable a patient's 
home or elderly center to become a virtual hospital ward. 
Vital signs can be transmitted to monitoring centers, which 
can detect possible problems. A nurse, based in the 
monitoring centre could be available 24 hours a day to 
provide reassurance and advice. A specialist could be 
contacted if there are signs causing concern. In real time e-
Health, the classic example of which is video-consultation, 
the interaction between the participants is "live" and they can 
interact immediately [5]. 
The elder people can not leave from the elderly center by 
himself or herself for shopping for the reason of safety. A 
virtual supermarket is built in the elderly center using 
telecare e-marketplace system (Sellers). Each elder people 
has an e-Card that is deposited a few of money. The elder 
people (Buyers) can use this e-Card on personal data 
acquisition (PDA) to go shopping in this e-marketplace. The 
advantages of telecare e-marketplace system are as bellows: 
1. Reducing trading cost: the trading cost will be 
reduced 10% for organization for using this system because 
the process is a package deal, validity of order, a simple 
process of administration. 
2. Finding a new supplier: the buyers can evaluate and 
find a good supplier. 
3. Searching market information: the supplier can 
clearly know what the elder people need. 
4. Fixed the prices and reducing goods in the stock: 
the supplier can clearly know how many goods in the stock 
and to fix the prices. 
5. Fair competition for small organization of buyers: a 
small organization of elderly center can has these advantages 
of e-marketplace to build a fair competition.

B. ZigBee Wireless Transmission System 
ZigBee is based on 802.15.4, set by IEEE. It’s a new 
standard of short distance wireless transmission; it 
emphasizes low cost of money, electricity and two-way 
transmission. Its transmitting distance is 10 meters in the 
frequency of 2.4 Hz and 250kbps of transmitting speed. The 
characteristic of the frequency of 868/915 Hz is shown table 
2. The technique of ZigBee is a point to point internet serial 
link transmission in low speed, low power and short distance, 
it can be used to control the air condition, illumination, and 
fire extinguish system by single remote control or similar 
equipments [6]. 
ZigBee system is divided into four parts; Physical Layer 
(PHY), Medium Access Control Layer (MAC), Network 
Layer (NWK) and Application Support Sub-layer (APS). It 
piles up the information in all layers and put it in a packet [7]. 
The structure of packet is shown as figure 2. 
PHY, MAC, NWK and APS layer shows different 
information. The information is as below: 
1. PHY layer: IEEE staring address, system code, 
packet length, PHY information and destination address. 
2. MAC layer: MAC layer code, length, IEEE address, 
PAN ID, MAC information and examining code. 
3. NWK layer: NWK layer code, color of pin for 
equipment and IEEE staring address. 
4. APS layer: Destination endpoint, group address and 
source endpoint. 
The specifications of each layer are shown from figure 3 
to figure 6 [8]. The data transfer between equipments is as 
figure 7. The maximum speed is 128 kbps and system 
memory must be less than 32 kbps (8-bit controller). Each 
additional slave nodes need more than 30 milliseconds, 
ZigBee can control thousand of user to exchange information 
and back to stand by mode, so it can save its battery power. 
The characteristics of ZigBee technique are as bellows: 
1. Saving power: the time of sending and reading 
signals is short because low transmitting speed and capacity. 
ZigBee is very efficient in energy and the battery of this 
system can be up to 6 months to 2 years.
