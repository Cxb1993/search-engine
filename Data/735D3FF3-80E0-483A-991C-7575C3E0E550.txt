摘要 
本研究以電漿電弧為加熱源蒸發鎢靶材，並藉由氣凝合成機制及一套吹氣裝
置，分別探討不同電漿電流(70~90A)及腔體壓力(200~600Torr)對生成氧化鎢奈米
材料的影響，其間並針對奈米棒的光學特性進行分析與探討。研究結果顯示，
隨著電漿電流及腔體壓力的增加，氧化鎢形貌不但會由塊狀粒子逐漸變為棒狀結
構，而且奈米棒的直徑及長度也都會隨之增加，其中在電漿電流90A及腔體壓力
400Torr製程條件下所製備出的氧化鎢奈米棒具有最大的長寬比(12.7)。微結構分
析結果得知，不論在何種電漿電流或腔體壓力下所得到的氧化鎢結構皆為正方晶
體的W5O14相態，晶格間距(d-spacing)為0.38nm，晶面沿著[001]方向成長，成長
機制可透過氣-固相(Vapor-Solid, VS)法解釋。W5O14奈米棒在小於450nm波長時 
，開始會有光吸收特性產生，當進一步透過光激發光譜的分析，可發現分別會
在紫外光(364nm)和藍光(444nm)波段位置相對產生一個由帶間躍遷及氧缺陷所
造成的放射波長，能隙為3.4eV。 
關鍵字：氣凝合成法、氧化鎢、奈米棒、光激發光 
 
ABSTRACT 
In the present study, tungsten oxide nanomaterials were synthesized by a 
modified plasma arc gas condensation technique. The effects of processing 
parameters (plasma current ranged from 70 ~ 90 A and chamber pressure ranged 
from 200~600 Torr) on the preparation of tungsten oxide nanomaterials were 
investigated. The morphology of tungsten oxide nanomaterials was equiaxed and 
turn into rod-like nanoparticles with increasing plasma currents or chamber pressures. 
Generally, the relative amount, diameter, and length of tungsten oxide nanorods 
increased with increasing plasma currents or chamber pressures. The aspect ratio of 
the as-prepared tungsten oxide nanorods reached a maximum of 12.7 with a plasma 
current of 90 A and a chamber pressure of 400 Torr. X-ray diffraction results showed 
that all the nanomaterials investigated in the present study were W5O14 phase. High 
resolution transmission electron microscopy observation revealed that tungsten oxide 
nanorods grown along the [001] direction with a lattice spacing of 0.38 nm. 
Photolumeinscence emission at ultraviolet-visible (365 nm) and blue (444 nm) 
regions are found in the W5O14 nanorods, which is caused by band-band transition and 
oxygen vacancies, respectively. And the band gap of tungsten oxide nanorods is 3.4 
eV. 
Keywords ： Gas Condensation Technique 、 Tungsten Oxide 、 Nanorod 、
Photoluminscence

4.4 光學特性 
由於氧化鎢是為間接能隙半導體 
，其電子電洞的結合主要是透過對光
波向量補償所造成的聲子放射或吸
收，透過圖九的紫外光-可見光吸收光
譜可看到，W5O14奈米棒在小於450nm
波長時吸收度會開始大量加，這相對
於文獻 [20]所報導的WO3吸收邊緣
(absorption edge)為360nm是發生紅移
(red shift)現象。造成這樣的結果，推
測主要是因為本製程所得到的W5O14
奈米棒具有氧缺陷的結構，因此會使
得導帶與價帶間形成由氧缺陷(defect)
所構成的能階，以致於當原本位於價
帶的電子受光激發後，可能會受到氧
空缺的捕獲，而使得吸收邊緣產生紅
移，而這種缺陷能級所造成的光學能
隙縮減在文獻上也有相似的報導
[21]。此外藉由(αhυ)1/2對(hυ)作圖可求
得其光學能隙值(optical energy gap) 
，一般光吸收效應可藉由αhυ=(hυ-Eg)2
關係式來表示，其中α代表吸收係數，
hυ代表光子能量及Eg代表光學能隙，
能帶通常定義為(αhυ)1/2對(hυ)圖的截
距(α大於104cm-1)，由圖中可得知其能
隙約為2.8eV，此值推測應該為價帶與
氧缺陷能級的差距大小。 
    一般當半導體受可見光或紫外光
照射，其能量大於費米能量 (Fermi 
energy)，則位於價帶的電子受光激
發，會躍過半導體能隙跳至導帶，而
在價帶中產生電洞；當位於導帶中的
電子重回價帶，與價帶中的電洞重合
產生電子電洞對(electron-hole pair)

3 RESULTS AND DISCUSSION
3.1 Phase Characterization
It is interesting to note that the as-prepared
nanocrystalline powders exhibited different colors during
the processing. Since tungsten oxide exhibited various
stoichiometric (such as WO2 and WO3) and
nonstoichiometric (WO3-x) compositions, X-ray diffraction
was used to examine the phases of the as-prepared
nanocrystalline powders. Figure 2 shows the X-ray
diffraction patterns of as-obtained tungsten oxides prepared
using various processing parameters. It can be noted that

In addition to the X-ray diffraction analysis explored the
long range atomic order, synchrotron X-ray absorption near
edge structure (XANES) technique reveals the electronic
structure of the detected element. Figure 3 shows the
XANES spectra of the W LΙΙ-edge for the corresponding
tungsten oxide powders (i.e., WO3, WO3-x, and W5O14)
examined by XRD in Fig. 2. It can be noted that the main
absorption peak shifted to the right with respect to WO3,
WO3-x, and W5O14. The main absorption peaks are 10.2115,
10.2099, and 10.2080 keV, respectively. The tungsten
valence of the as-prepared WO3, WO3-x (W19O55 as the
major phase), and W5O14 powders is +6, +5.8 (calculated
from W19O55), and +5.6, respectively. The decreasing
absorption peak energy confirmed the decreasing oxidation
states of tungsten (i.e. valence) in tungsten oxide powders.
This shows a similar trend as reported by Khyzhun [15].
