英文摘要 
 
This project continued on the research of neural networks. We developed new networks 
to explain the mechanism of synaptic memory and recall of neurons. In the first year, the 
project accomplished applications in building computational cognitive systems. The 
important research issues in this year are as follows: 
1. Memory for sequential symbols 
2. Cognitive automata acquired through learning 
3. Investigate semantic indexing and build possible theoretical structure. Elman network 
has been used in achieving and accelerating the cognitive process and various functions 
of the mind through recursive reference. In the second year, the Elman network and 
feed-forward networks have been rewritten. The networks are applied on literal works 
and visualization. The applications includes, visualization, literature semantic 
categorization/indexing/search/coding, 
music perception. The third year we conducted comparison and evaluation on 
applications.  
 
Keywords: neural network, visualization, feed forword network, Elman network, 
semantic indexing
