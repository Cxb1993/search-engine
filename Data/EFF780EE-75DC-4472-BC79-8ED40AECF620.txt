一、摘要 
此為兩年研究計劃之報告，分析滑動葉片
頂點與其上軸承運轉之包絡線軌跡，設計
靜子內壁與兩側蓋板上之滑槽之軌跡，定
子內壁輪廓曲線。利用多變壓縮過程，以
求得氣室內氣體之壓力與溫度的變化情
形、體積流率，壓縮機模型製作完成，量
測壓縮機之進氣區與壓縮區在不同轉速下
之溫度與壓力。根據量測之溫度與壓力的
結果，由多變過程中溫度與壓力之關係，
可計算出指定多變過程之多變指數，以作
為後續研究之依據。求得葉片之位移及加
速度、與利用牛頓定律，推導作用於葉片
上與其上軸承之力、摩擦力、摩擦功率、
壓縮功率、容積效率，壓縮機之總效率為
0.59。 
 
關鍵詞：旋轉式壓縮機、單一滑動葉
片、軸承、包絡線軌跡 
 
Abstract 
This is a two-year study report. After the 
envelopes of the motions for the vane tip and 
bearings on the vane are derived, the stator 
inner contour and the slots on both cover 
plates are designed. The polytropic 
compression process is used to determine the 
pressure and temperature of the fluid and the 
volume flow rate. Based on the configuration 
of the compressor, the air temperature and 
pressure during intake, compression and 
discharge are measured. The polytropic 
constant that is based on the measured air 
temperature and pressure can be obtained. 
With the obtained displacement and 
acceleration of vanes and Newton’s law the 
forces acting on vanes and the bearings 
mounted on vanes, friction force, friction 
power, compression power, volume 
efficiency and mechanical efficiency of the 
compressor to be 0.59 can be obtained.  
 
Keywords: rotary compressor, single sliding 
vane, bearing, envelope 
 
二、前言 
1. 緣由 
甲. 市場前景樂觀 
壓縮機之應用很廣，關連產業效果大﹐所
以經濟部訂定其為重點發展項目之一。壓
縮機之種類很多，它可分為容積式壓縮機
與空氣動力式壓縮機，容積式壓縮機可分
為往復式、旋轉式、螺旋式與渦卷式，而
空氣動力式有離心式。往復式又可再分為
活塞式與斜板式。旋轉式又可再分為滑動
葉片式與滾動活塞式。螺旋式又可再分為
單螺旋式與雙螺旋式。 
各種壓縮機有其優缺點,旋轉式壓縮機運
轉平穩與輸出穩定，因此發展良好，而各
旋轉式的壓縮機中，滑動葉片型旋轉式壓
縮機因具有零件少、體積小、重量輕、壓
縮動作順暢、振動小、體積效率高、成本
低等優點，尤其受到青睞，已用於車輛空
調、真空泵及氣體壓縮等裝置。

實驗時，當轉速高於180 rad/s以上時，振

實驗時，於定子內壁、葉片與轉子滑槽間，
噴入些許的霧狀黃油以供潤滑，這些白煙
應是由於潤滑油之汽化所產生，然而由圖
10 得知實驗所量測之最高氣溫度為
338oK，低於黃油之容許溫度(約433oK)，因
此在未來實驗過程中，應可進一步量測定
子內壁的溫度情形，以作為整個壓縮機最
高溫度的參考。 
 
八、結論與建議 
本研究設計並製造出一新型、單一雙銷滑
動葉片旋轉式壓縮機，於葉片之銷上套入
軸承，側蓋板上有滾珠軸承運動之內軌
跡，促使壓縮機運轉時，葉片上銷之軸承
依據此軌跡而運行，減少摩擦功率，提高
機械效率。此設計具有構造簡單，在組裝
或維修方面，都方便。此壓縮機之轉速為
140 rad/s時，總效率為為0.59。當轉速高於 
180 rad/s以上時，出現異常的振動、噪音及
大量白煙冒出，為安全起見，實驗之最高
轉速只達180 rad/s，因此葉片之摩擦有進一
步研究之空間與必要。 
建議提供更多經費與人力，以加強與加速
研究與開發。 
 
九、計畫成果自評 
理論研究與設計皆優。此壓縮機由於
設計簡單，製造也相對的容易，融合學理，
可達成人才培育及技術提升的目標，進而
能達機械設計實作之目的，並提供一可實
用之滑動葉片旋轉式壓縮機，將對我國之
壓縮機產業有莫大之貢獻。 
研究內容與原計畫相符、達成預期目
標情況良好、研究成果之學術或應用價值
適合在學術期刊發表。

ABSTRACT 
This study investigated the transient temperature distribution, 
thermal gradient and heat flux distribution, thermal stress and 
deflection of a disk brake due to friction. The finite element 
method with a two-dimensional model was applied in the 
analysis. A specified initial uniform temperature of the disk was 
used to simulate heat transfer of the disk. Utilizing the transient 
temperature and pressure distributions, the stress and the 
deflection of the disk brake can be determined. Since the 
temperature of the disk brake inboard is higher than that of the 
disk brake outboard, the deflection of the disk brake inboard is 
larger than those at other locations. The disk expands radial 
outward and bends from the disk brake inboard toward the disk 
brake outboard. The maximum deflection of 0.4 mm occurs at 
the outer diameter of the disk inboard. The coning angle 
between the disk outboard surface and the original vertical disk 
outboard surface is 0.39o, which is comparable with the 
existing datum of 0.35o. The principal stresses at the lower 
mounting location are 184 MPa and 236 MPa. The calculated 
safety factor is 1.27 based on the modified Mohr theory used 
for brittle materials, and this disk brake is reliable for the 
application.  
INTRODUCTION 
The schematic drawing of a disk brake is shown in Fig. 1. 
Vibration, material, surface contact and thermal stress and 
deflection, etc., of various disk brakes have been studied to 
improve their performance. Mote [1] analyzed effects of heat 
on the natural frequency and the stress of a thin annular plate. 
Kirkhope and Wilson [2] utilized the finite element method to 
analyze the natural frequency of an annular plate with the free 
inner boundary and the simple supported or free outer 
boundary. Many researchers applied Mindlin’s concept and 
analyzed dynamic axisymmetric and asymmetric annular plates 
[3-6]. Mote and Angelo [7] proposed a model with no stress, no 
displacement on the boundary and a solid disk model to 
simulate the boundary condition of an annular plate to better 
predict natural frequency of a thin annular plate.

and aluminum, and they utilized Taguchi method to analyze the

CAE 
Because the disk brake is very complicated, this study 
simplified it into a two-dimensional model to investigate the 
thermal state, thermal stress and deflection. Using the 
sequential method with a given initial temperature on the 
boundary, the distributions of transient temperature, 
temperature gradient, thermal flux, and material properties of 
the disk brake can be determined. The sequential method needs 
two types of elements for the thermal analysis and the 
structural analysis. Substituting the temperature distribution 
and material properties into the structural model with the 
assumed pressure on the boundaries yields the thermal stress 
and deflection. Then they are substituted into next time step 
and the procedures are repeated.
