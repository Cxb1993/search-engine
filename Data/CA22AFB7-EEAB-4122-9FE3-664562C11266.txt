3.3 MWNTs/Chitosan 複合薄膜修飾於玻璃碳電極 
 將 4 mg/mL 多層壁奈米碳管加入於 0.25 wt%、Chitosan 水溶液中 pH 5，經超音波振盪
器使 MWNTs 完全分散於 Chitosan 水溶液中，可得到一黑色均勻懸浮液，取 6 μL 的
MWNTs/Chitosan 溶液滴在玻璃碳電極表面，在室溫環境下使其自然乾燥，待其乾燥後，
MWNTs/Chitosan 薄膜修飾在玻璃碳電極即製備完成。測試前，將 MWNTs/Chitosan 薄膜修
飾玻璃碳電極置於緩衝溶液中。 
 
3.4 MWNTs/Chitosan/LDH 生物複合薄膜修飾玻璃碳電極 
將超音波震盪後之 MWNTs/Chitosan 水溶液加入不同量的乳酸去氫酶（LDH）(分別為 6、
8、10、12 mg/ml)，將其以超音波震盪約 5 分鐘，形成均勻的 MWNTs/Chitosan/LDH 的水
溶液，取超音波震盪完之 MWNTs/Chitosan/LDH 水溶液 6 μL，將其滴於玻璃碳電極之上，
在室溫環境下使其自然乾燥，待其乾燥後，MWNTs/Chitosan/LDH 修飾玻璃碳電極即製備
完成。利用原子力顯微鏡(atomic force microscopy)觀察奈米碳管及 LDH 在 Chitosan 溶液中
分散的狀況。在作電化學測試之前，將 MWNTs/Chitosan/LDH 修飾玻璃碳電極置於緩衝溶
液中。

4.1 以 AFM 觀察 MWNTs/ Chitosan/LDH 奈米複合薄膜 
直接將複合物薄膜修飾於工作電極上，是改良電化學感測器最有效的方法之一，該薄
膜的均勻度在生物感測器的製備上非常重要，薄膜均勻度愈高，生物感測器的靈敏度會愈
高。本實驗以 AFM 觀察 Chitosan 與 MWNTs/Chitosan 與 MWNTs/Chitosan/LDH 奈米生物薄
膜修飾於玻璃碳電極，結果分別如圖 1、圖 2 及圖 3，其 root mean square 分別為 17.5 nm、
29.5 nm 及 15.2 nm。圖 2 中可清楚看到 MWNTs 均勻的分散在 Chitosan 中，該奈米碳管之
直徑約 60 nm，由 RMS 可看出在加入 MWNTs 後，薄膜的每個地方都有 MWNTs 分散其中，
造成其表面較為不平坦。而圖 3 比起圖 2，多了 LDH 之後膜變得較為平整，這是因為 LDH
與 Chitosan 的多醣鏈(polysaccharide chain)形成聚電解質複合物。在此證明了 LDH 可溶於
MWNTs/Chitosan 溶液中，而 MWNTs/Chitosan/LDH 均勻地塗佈在電極上，且 MWNTs 無明

峰電位負向橫移 0.2 V，且可提高電流密度。因此將 MWNTs 應用在本實驗之乳酸生物感測
器上，可在較低的操作電壓使 NADH 氧化，如此亦可降低干擾物對生物感測器之影響。

測結果。由圖 6 (a)可看到在 0~0.4 V 均無任何電流，直到在 0.5 V 下才有 NADH 的氧化電
流訊號出現，而圖 6 (b)在 0.3 V 已有氧化電流，可見該 MWNTs/Chitosan 複合薄膜電極僅需
施加 0.3 V 即可將 NADH 氧化，0.3 V 到 0.6 V 呈現持續上升的驅勢，直到 0.6 V 達到最高，
0.7 V 時電流訊號突然比在 0.6 V 下偵測到的電流訊號減少了四分之一，0.7 V 到 0.9 V 間電
流訊號又呈現上升的驅勢，該電流-操作電壓圖與 Musameh 等人[18]發表過的電流-操作電
壓圖相當類似，該研究的工作電極是全氟磺酸(Nafion)/MWNTs。由此可證明 MWNTs 的確
可以有效的提升電流訊號並降低操作電壓。為了獲得最高的靈敏度及降低可能的干擾，選
擇 0.6 V 作為該感測器之操作電壓。

五、結論 
本研究成功地以 Chitosan、MWNTs、LDH 製備出乳酸生物感測器，首先以 Chitosan
溶液分散 MWNTs，使用 AFM 觀察後證明 MWNTs 均勻的分散在 Chitosan 中，再以溫和的
圈入法將 LDH 混合進入 MWNTs/Chitosan 中，經由簡單的電極塗佈方法將該薄膜修飾於電
極之上。實驗證明加入 MWNTs 後，能有效催化 NADH 的氧化，提升電流密度、降低操作
電壓並能有效的防止電極毒化。以電流-操作電壓實驗決定操作電壓後，進行一連串的實驗
決定各種實驗條件之最適值(緩衝溶液之 pH 值、NAD+之濃度及 LDH 之最佳承載量)後，進
行長期穩定性測試。實驗結果證明該乳酸生物感測器有極短的應答時間、高靈敏度及低偵
測極限，且 LDH 與基質間有極佳之親和力，顯示該乳酸生物感測器有極大的應用潛力，未
來可用於血液及食物中乳酸濃度的檢測。

英文： 
   A composite of multiwalled carbon nanotubes/chitosan 
(MWNTs/Chitosan) was used as a matrix for entrapment of lactate 
dehydrogenase (LDH) onto a glassy carbon electrode in order to 
fabricate amperometric biosensor. The homogeneity of the resulting 
MWNTs/Chitosan/LDH nanobiocomposite film was investigated by 
atomic force microscopy (AFM). It shows that the enzyme is 
homogeneously immobilized within MWNTs/Chitosan/LDH. The 
inclusion of MWCNT within MWNTs/Chitosan/LDH exhibits the 
abilities to raise the current responses, to decrease the electrooxidation 
potential of β-nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide, reduced form 
(NADH), and to prevent the electrode surface fouling. The influence of 
several experimental parameters such as applied potential, solution pH 
value, NAD+ concentration, and enzyme loading was explored to 
optimize the electroanalytical performance of the biosensor. The 
optimized biosensor for the determination of lactate shows a sensitivity
of 0.0083 A/M．cm2 and a response time of about 3 s. The proposed 
lactate biosensor retained 65 % of its original response after 7 days.

技術特點 
本計畫以幾丁聚醣、多層壁奈米碳管、乳酸去氫酶製備出乳酸
生物感測器，我們先以幾丁聚醣溶液懸浮多層壁奈米碳管，再以溫
和的圈入法將乳酸去氫酶混合進入多層壁奈米碳管/幾丁聚醣中，

物感測器有極短的應答時間、高靈敏度及低偵測極限。

2.2 Preparation of Pt-Ru-MWCNT-Nafion nanocmposite-coated electrodes  
The optimum composition of MWCNTs/Nafion for electroanalytical applications has been reported 
in our previous study [9]. A 0.5 wt% Nafion solution was prepared by diluting the 5 wt% Nafion 
solution with 0.1 M phosphate buffer solution (pH 7), then a 10 mg MWCNTs was added in l mL 
0.5 wt% Nafion solution and ultrasonic agitation for 1 h to from a homogeneous solution. Before 
the preparation, the 3mm glassy carbon electrode was polished with 0.3 and 0.05μm alumina slurry 
and cleaned with deionized water in ultrasonic bath for several minutes. MWCNT-Nafion film was 
prepared by casting a 6 μL of 10 mg mL-1 MWCNT-Nafion solution on the surface of glassy carbon 
electrode. The solvent was allowed to evaporate at room temperature in the air for 1 h. The 
nanostructured Pt and Ru was electrochemically deposited within the MWCNT-Nafion matrix by a 
cyclic voltammetry in a 0.1 M phosphate buffer solution (pH 7) containing 1 mM K2PtCl6 and 0.75 
mM RuCl3. The potential was scanned between -1.0 to 0 V versus Ag/AgCl at a scan rate of 50 mV 
s-1.  
 
3. Results and discussion 
3.1 FESEM, TEM, and EDS characterization of Pt-Ru-MWCNT-Nafion nanocomposite 
Pt and Ru nanoparticles were successfully deposited within MWCNT-Nafion matrix by a cyclic 
voltammetry method. The formation of Pt and Ru nanoparticles was characterized by FESEM, 
TEM, and EDS. Fig. 1 shows the FESEM image of MWCNT-Nafion nanocomposite after 
electroreduction of Pt and Ru nanoparticles. Pt and Ru nanoparticles were deposited to the surface 
of MWCNTs where could form a passageway for electron to carry out the reaction of 
electrodeposition. The further information of the electrodeposition of Pt and Ru nanoparticles onto 
the surface of MWCNTs was investigated by TEM. Fig. 2 shows the TEM image of 
MWCNT-Nafion nanocomposite after electroreduction of Pt and Ru nanoparticles. It is clear from 
Fig. 2 that the Pt and Ru nanoparticles are grown on the MWCNTs after electrodeposition. The 
formation of Pt and Ru nanoparticles within MWCNT-Nafion nanocomposite is supported by EDS 
observation. Fig. 3 shows the EDS pattern of MWCNT-Nafion nanocomposite after 
electroreduction of Pt and Ru nanoparticles. It can be seen from Fig. 3 that C, O, F, Pt, and Ru are 
the major elements after electrodeposition of Pt and Ru. C comes from MWCNTs and Nafion and O 
and F are derived from Nafion. The appearance of Pt and Ru elements after the electroreduction 
demonstrated that the nanoparticles within the MWCNT-Nafion are Pt-Ru.
