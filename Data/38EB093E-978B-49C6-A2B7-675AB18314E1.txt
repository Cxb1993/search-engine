 在 1968年，Veselago預測左手介質(left-handed media) [1,2]存在於具電漿與
磁性共振特性的物質裡，其介電係數 ' i= +ε ε ε  (permittivity) 與導磁係數
' i= + ''μ μ μ  (permeability)的實部項同時為負值( 'ε <0, 'μ <0);然而左手介質材料
並不存在於自然界中;Pendry 研究團隊在 1996 年提出次波長結構的週期性金屬
奈米線結構，此結構具有負的介電係數 'ε <0[3]。Pendry並在 1999年提出等效奈
米結構的導磁係數μ模型[4]，並指出在特定頻率下其導磁係數的實部 'μ <0。2002
年Sarychev的研究團隊提出成對的奈米金屬線結構(nanowire pairs)在紅外光波段
下，具有負的介電係數 'ε <0 與導磁係數 'μ <0[5];當入射光的磁場振動方向垂直
於成對的奈米金屬線時，在此奈米結構中將引導出位移電流(displacement current)
與傳導電流(conducting current)，而成為一迴路系統，造成近場的磁共振現象。
本計畫實際製鍍多樣化之奈米金屬結構薄膜，並在可見光下量測其等效電磁參
數。 
 本研究團隊在真空鍍膜技術已有多年的經驗，近年來除製鍍具偏極轉換特性
(Polarization conversion reflection)的奈米介質斜柱(nanorod array)薄膜，更利用方
位角掃描技術(phisweep technique)有效調製非均向薄膜的多孔性。在2008年”Asia 
materials (NPG)”以”Silicon spikes soak up the rays”為標題，報導本團隊所製鍍的
高角度抗反射奈米錐結構。除了介電質材料，我們也成功製鍍了鋁奈米柱陣列結
構，並發現此金屬薄膜同時具有介電質的干涉行為及表面電漿共振現象(Appl. 
Phys. Lett. 91, 021109 (2007).)。在 2009年”physicsworld.com”更以”Technique to 
mass produce metamaterials”為標題，報導本團隊製鍍並發現具有負折射率的奈米
銀柱陣列結構薄膜。此外在奈米多層膜組態(S-shape structrue/Al film)中，本團隊
發現除了 p偏極光具有多模態表面電漿共振現象，s偏極光更有表面電漿共振行
為，此研究將打破表面電漿共振只能由 p偏極光激發的觀念。以上奈米金屬薄膜
所具有的特殊光學特現象，除有助於提升本計畫在雕刻奈米金屬的技術與研究其
特別之光學現象，更可有效提昇此種奈米超穎材料薄膜在實際的應用範圍。 
 由於銀奈米柱陣列薄膜具有非常大的等效相位差(p 偏極光與 s 偏極光)，在
量測上我們使用 PSA技術(polarized-sample-analyzer)，量測薄膜在可見光下的等
效雙折射特性，並探討其倒退波現象(backward wave phemonen);此外，由於縱向
電漿共振模態(longitudinal plasmon mode, LPM)在可見光波段可被 p 偏極光激
發，p偏極光在銀奈米柱狀結構的透射率遠小於 s偏極之透射率；本研究將利用
分離式干涉儀直接量測奈米金屬薄膜之等效電磁參數，以建立其物理模型。在製
鍍具三維銀奈米結構時(如鋸齒狀結構及 S 型結構)，本團隊使用交錯沉積技術
(bideposition technology)並搭配斜向角度沉積法製鍍多樣化奈米金屬薄膜;我們在
可見光證明其等效折射率在可見光時具有負折射率。再者，我們使用有限時時域
差分法(finite-difference time-domain, FDTD)模擬單個銀奈米鋸齒狀結構及 s型結
構的近場磁偶極(magnetic moment)共振現象，並討論鋸齒狀結構外型以及入射波
長對於磁效應之影響。

奈米銀陣列電漿共振模態 
 在 2006 年，Zhao 研究團隊利用斜向角度沉積技術 (glancing angle 
deposition)，製鍍出銀奈米柱陣列結構，並量測其吸收光譜[13,14]。圖 3.9(a)為 p
偏極光、s偏極光與奈米柱結構之相對關係圖;由圖可知，當入射光為 s偏極光時，
電場垂直於奈米柱結構，可在銀奈米柱之間激發橫向電漿共振模態(traverse 
plasmon mode, TPM);當入射光為 p偏極光時，由於柱子傾斜 β角，造成入射電場
分解成平行與垂直分量，其中平行電場可激發縱向電漿共振模態(longitudinal 
plasmon mode, LPM)。圖 3.9(b)與(c)為 p與 s偏極光在不同銀奈米柱長度(h=201nm, 
662nm, 783nm)之吸收光譜圖;由圖可知，當 h=662nm、783nm、入射光波長
λ=400nm~1500nm時，p偏極光譜由於 TPM模態的存在，其吸收遠大於 s偏極光;
當 λ<400nm時，s偏極光可激發銀奈米柱之 LPM模態，則其光譜吸收大於 p偏
極光。

有限元素差分法 
 有限差分時域法(FDTD)[37]為一對具奈米結構非均向薄膜的近場模擬方
法，直接對滿足電磁場之邊界條件作差分處理，來解決電磁波在電磁介質中傳播
和反射問題的算法，把整個計算區域分成許多不同的分析單位(cell)，由入射波
源的電磁場，交互計算隨時間變化的電磁場空間分布，可即時的得知電磁波在分
析空間中的傳播型態，以有限差分時域法(FDTD)模擬各金屬奈米結構薄膜的吸
收光譜和在電漿共振波長的電磁場分佈，提供一個直觀的模擬與驗證方法。
FDTD具有模式直觀簡潔、易操控與可顯現暫態電磁場分佈等優點，且結果分析
計算方便，而廣泛應用於奈米結構的光學模擬中。我們選用合適的模擬空間與分
析單位，採用完美匹配層(perfect match layer, PML)以避免在模擬邊界的人為反
射。根據 FDTD 模擬結果可得到奈米金屬結構內部及近場電磁場與電流密度分
佈，並進一步得到材料的共振特性與發生負折射的成因。 
 首先建置 FDTD 程式以及高速運算電腦叢集的搭配，透過程式模擬與分析
奈米金屬結構來提供製程所需之參數，並以理論分析探討奈米金屬結構可能發生
負折射的特性與成因。在不同材料以及結構的各種尺寸下，自由電子被電磁波激
發而導致振盪，結構中發生共振的區域以及震盪強度都不相同，透過 FDTD 模
擬不同的材料與幾何結構，得到的反射率與穿透率頻譜計算材料之等效介電係數
(ε)、導磁係數(μ)、折射率與負折射效益等光學參數，將探討其中之振盪強度、
入射波長、對與對間距、結構尺寸的近場性質之相依關係，當改變不同的幾何關
係，則不同的震盪強度以及發生共振區域也會隨之變化(可分析如圖 4.8(a)鋸齒狀
結構或(b)S 型結構等對應奈米結構)，藉由模擬反射率與穿透率頻譜，分析等效
電偶極與磁偶極，計算材料之光學常數，經模擬驗證，進一步設計各種結構達到
負折射效果。

1. Introduction 
Long-range surface-plasmon-polariton (LRSPP) waves are coupled modes of surface plasmon waves on both sides 
of a thin metal film [1]. The coupled wave with a lowest imaginary part of the propagation constant propagates a 
long distance when the thickness of the metal film decreases. Sarid proposed a symmetric multilayer structure for a 
very thin silver film (20 nm) surrounded by a low-refractive index material would excite such LRSPP waves [2]. 
The propagation length of the p-polarized LRSPP wave is enhanced over 300 mμ  at 632.8λ = . Recently, we 
proposed the periodic multilayer structure of exciting p- and s-polarized LRSPP propagation simultaneously at  
632.8λ = . The symmetrical film stack is introduced to be as a coupling layer to achieve the LRSPP waves of 
either the p- or the s-polarized waves [3].  
In this paper, the mechanism and the design method for exciting p- and s-polarized LRSPP propagation in the 
prism-coupling configuration {prism/ coupling layer/silver film (20 nm)/ Ta2O5 layer/ air} are investigated. Sec. 2 
is devoted to the description of the mechanism of exciting a typical LRSPP wave. A normalized admittance diagram 
(NAD) is set as the LRSPP design tool. The multilayer structure of exciting LRSPP for p- and s-polarization states 
will be realized and discussed at a designate angle of incidence i 65θ = °  at 632.8λ =  nm in the prism-coupling 
configuration in Sec. 4.

4. Conclusion 
Long-range surface-plasmon-polariton (LRSPP) waves for p- and s-polarization states are excited at the designate 
angle i 65θ = °  at the wavelength 632.8λ =  nm in the prism-coupling configuration {prism/ coupling layer/silver 
film (20 nm)/ Ta2O5 layer/air}. The coupling layer with a large and positive imaginary intrinsic admittance is 
introduced to achieve the LRSPP design in the normalized admittance diagram (NAD). The propagation lengths for 
p- and s-polarization states are enhanced to be 536.30 mμ  and 298.34 mμ  in the configuration {prism/ 
[Ta2O5(142.70nm) /SiO2(421.80nm)/Ta2O5(142.70nm)]18/silver film(20 nm)/Ta2O5 film(241.45 nm)/air} and 
{prism / [Ta2O5(84.50nm) /SiO2(247.45nm) /Ta2O5(84.50nm)]6  / silver film(20 nm)/Ta2O5 film(115.24 nm)/air}, 
respectively. The multilayer LRSPP design method would provide a new way of guiding lights for novel photonic 
devices in the future.

1.Introduction 
Nano-sculptured metal films have exhibited their extraordinary optical properties such as polarization-dependent 
absorption and the negative refraction phenomenon [1,2]. In our previous work [3], glancing angle deposition 
(GLAD) technique is applied to grow a thin film (about 250nm) comprising parallel, tilted silver nanorod (NRA) on 
a 2-inch square substrate of fused silica. The reflection and transmission coefficients of the thin film are measured at 
three wavelengths in the visible regime for normal-illumination conditions, using ellipsometry and walk-off 
interferometer [4]. For incident p-polarized light, the thin film is found to display an equivalent refractive index with 
negative real part.  
In continuation of that work, two silver thin films comprising parallel nanorod arrays with difference 
thicknesses. Under normal-incidence illumination, the longitudinal and transverse plasmonic modes are excited 
mainly by p-polarized and s-polarized light [1], the anisotropic absorption therefore becomes strong. The reflection 
and transmission coefficients of these thin films are measured for the two polarizations at the wavelength of 639 nm. 
According to our result, it is demonstrated that the refractive index and impedance are structure-dependent.

4. Conclusion 
In this work, it is demonstrated that a silver nanorod array deposited with GLAD exhibits a negative real refractive 
index under longitudinal plasmonic mode. The different thickness represents different shadowing effect among the 
rods. The magnetic effect will be strong as the rods are long and parallel enough. As the thickness increases from 
200±5 nm to 300±5 nm, the real part of the p-polarized equivalent refractive index varies intensively from -0.898 to 
-0.126 at the wavelength of 639 nm. The real parts of the equivalent refractive indices keep at a constant of 0.621 
±0.1 for s-polarized light.
