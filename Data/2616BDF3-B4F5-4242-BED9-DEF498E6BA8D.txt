Abstract
The vehicle routing problem (VRP) has
attracted considerable attention in the past, and
has accumulated a vast amount of research
results. The problem of assigning customers to
service vehicles of refuse collection systems
can also be considered as a variant of the VRP.
In this system the vehicle base (parking lot)
and the cargo base (incinerator) differ. In this
research we identify the problem as a
Dual-Base Vehicle Routing Problem, and
developed a solution heuristic for it. The
neighborhood-search based heuristic
exchanges customers between vehicles in each
of the iterations. For a given vehicle, we also
developed mixed integer programs to optimize
the selection of a customer to remove, and to
optimize the insert location of a new customer.
The programs also yield the optimal
corresponding departure time. Computational
testing results are promising.
