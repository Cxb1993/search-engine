(一)中文摘要: 
關鍵詞: 紫外光偵測器、光電晶體、氮化物、光增益、光響應、硫化處理 
      
    III 族/氮化物半導體系列材料，由於 MOCVD 磊晶技術的改良，利用 GaN、AlGaN 低
溫緩衝層成長於 SiC、Al2O3 基板上，克服缺陷的延伸，方使得藍光 LED、LD 以及紫外光
偵測器等相繼問世，促使微電子元件也逐漸朝向高溫/高功率操作發展，然而在製程技術上
卻充滿許多的變數有待克服；再者，因應環境的變遷、工業的發展與健康管理的重視，使
得紫外光偵測器的發展在國內外已頗受重視，雖然傳統光二極體型紫外光偵測器有許多文
獻與應用發展，紫外光偵測用光電晶體卻仍未隨著傳統化合物半導體光電晶體之發展而有
所進展，本計劃提出俾期建立各項製程技術，元件測試，以開發 III 族/氮化物 Npin 光電
晶體，第一年計劃之研究重點與完成項目成果如述：利用化合物半導體元件模擬軟體進行
併完成 Npin HPT 結構設計、特性分析。 完成具 AlXGa1-XN/GaN 超晶格基極之元件製作測
試。包括以 ICP 蝕刻完成元件製作、光性能測試、高溫測試。最後研究重點在製程技術之
改良，包括(1)ICP 後表面硫化處理最佳化，(2)積層金屬在 p 型歐姆層低阻值接觸之探討，
(3) 高溫回火等之最佳化。 
 
(二)英文摘要(English Abstract) 
Keywords: UV detector, phototransistor, nitride, optical gain, responsivity, sulfur treatment 
 
    The improved MOCVD epitaxial techonology made it possible to overcome the extended 
and point defects in III/Nitride semiconductor epitaxial growth via the existence of 
Low-temperature GaN or AlGaN buffer layer upon the SiC or Al2O3 substrate. The success on 
epitaxial techonology brought out the approach to blue light LED, LD, and UV detectors. The 
trend for microelectronic in high-temperature and high-power application is becoming promising, 
although the manufacturing technology remains some difficulties to be conquered. Furthermore, 
UV detectors have received interest due to the climate transition, highly industrial development, 
and the attention on health management. A lot of literatures and applications toward the 
conventional photodiode UV detectors already have taken place while the phototransistors still 
need more research. Therefore, we propose this project to establish standard fabrication process 
and device testing on the development of III/Nitride Npin phototransistor. The project in the first 
year is described as follows: The Atlas/Blaze SILVACO simulation software is used to perform 
the simulation of Npin HPT structure and its electrical analysis. Npin HPT structure, epitaxial 
growth, and dopant analysis are designed according to the simulation results to practically 
fabricate devices, optical performance testing, and high temperature performance testing. Finally, 
we focus on the process improvement including (1) optimum of surface treatment after ICP dry 
etch, (2) investigation of multi-metal upon p-type contact layer, and (3) optimum of 
high-temperature annealing. 
 
一、報告內容： 
(一)、前言： 
     
    III 族/氮化物半導體系列材料在過去二、三十年裡，一直被視為應用在藍光、紫外光
波段間半導體光電元件最具潛力者。以 Wurtzite 結晶而言，GaN、InN、AlN 分別在室溫有
3.4、1.9、以及 6.2 eV 的直接能隙，特別是晶格相當匹配的 AlxGa1-xN 更涵蓋了大部分可
見光波段到紫外光，這使得此一氮化物系統在 LED、LD、偵測器等的應用更令人矚目。
短波長紫外光偵測之應用甚廣，如紫外光天文學、燃燒工程、火焰感測、引擎控制、太陽
紫外線監測、污染監測等。III 族/氮化物在材料特性上的優勢，促使本人在此寬能隙材料
光偵測器之興趣，利用 pin 與 HBT 優點的結合，設計高光吸收量子效益與高電晶體電流增
益具備高速、高溫操作與高光響應之 AlGaN/GaN 超晶格式基極 Npin 光電晶體，並期開發一
實用之紫外線偵測電路應用工業上之紫外線監測以及紫外線指數之測定。在本人已研製成
功的單射極光電晶體分析中發現，唯有以電流源當基極偏壓源時，所得到的光增益表現才
會隨著入射光功率的提高而增加，反之，以電壓源為偏壓源時所得到的光增益在不同入射
光功率下是固定的值。本人即利用此提出計劃第一年建立完整之單射極光電晶體理論、製
程技術暨電路模型，進而研製壓控式雙射極光電晶體。首先在本成果報告書中將針對第一
年(96 年 5 月 31 日前)所完成之工作項目與成果作簡單的回顧，再接著依序報告第二年之
計劃內容，為兩年期之計劃作完整而有系統之說明。

(五)、結果與討論： 
 
Fig.2 shows the schematically plan view of the fabricated DEPTs. The emitter (E1) area is 
AE1=75×100 μm2 while the other (E2) is AE2=150×250 μm2. The sum of the AE1 and AE2 is 
equal to AE of the conventional phototransistor fabricated in the first-year project. In addition, 
the area of the absorption region for both the DEPT and a conventional HPT is 1.1×105 μm2. 
The light source module utilized for the optical measurement is equipped with a 365-nm light 
and a power meter specific to the wavelength.

In Fig. 3, the common-emitter characteristics of the HPT operated with a floating base (2T-HPT) 
are exhibited according to the incident optical power. When increasing Pin from 0.68 to 7.2 uW, 
the measured ΔIcp for the unpassivated- and the sulfur-passivated-devices at Vce=10 V increase 
from 0.08 to 2.01 uA and from0.15 to 4.16 uA, respectively. A twofold improvement in ΔIcp is 
observed for the sulfur-passivated device compared to the unpassivated one. The output 
performance is strictly impacted by the dc current gain characteristics. In addition, the maximum 
optical gains obtained, shown in Fig. 4, are only 2.46. It is not altogether disappointed that the 
sulfur-passisvated device exhibits a great improvement. The optical gain for sulfur-passivated 
device under Pin=2.2 uW is larger than that for the unpassivated on under Pin=7.2 uW. When a 
base voltage is applied to HPTs (3T-HPTs), the quiescent point is promoted but the optical 
performance is degraded as compared to the 2T-HPTs. As shown in Fig. 5, the enhancement due 
to the surface sulfur treatment is also observed. However, the measured ΔIcp are in the range of
