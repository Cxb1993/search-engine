scheme, this study focuses on the development of a fruitful technique to identify the starting time of the underlying 
disturbance in a gamma process.  The effectiveness and the superiority of the proposed approaches are 
demonstrated through a series of simulations. 
 The structure of this study is organized as follows.  The second section discusses the estimation of the change 
point of a gamma process.  The third section shows results from the simulations, performed under various 
conditions.  These conditions include the different shifts for the gamma parameters and different sample sizes.  
The research findings and future research direction complete this study.
