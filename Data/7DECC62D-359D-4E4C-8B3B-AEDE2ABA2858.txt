4semiconductor materials become suitable alternative candidates for flexible UV optoelectronic
devices. ZnO and its related alloys are promising for UV photodetectors due to ZnO’s direct
wide bandgap, as well as low processing cost and capability of operation in harsh environments
[16-17]. Due to the lack of reliable p-type ZnO, these types of UV sensors are mostly realized in
metal-semiconductor-metal (MSM) structure with Schottky or ohmic contacts [13-15], or
heterojunction pn or pin devices [16-18].
Among all types of oxides, ZnO and related compound based transparent thin film transistors
(TFTs) have attracted much attention due to their potential to replace hydrogenated amorphous
(a-Si:H) or polycrystalline silicon (poly-Si) TFTs, which serve the backplane devices for the
mainstream display industry. The development trend of oxide based TFT is toward totally optical
transparency, low processing temperature, and mechanical flexibility [19-22]. All the applications
mentioned above lead to the research on the properties of low temperature deposited TCOs, which
usually possess lots of defects and grain boundaries. This is one of the research targets in this
proposal.
MgO/MgZnO/ZnO is the chosen material system for this study. The bandgap of MgO is
about 7.8 eV; and the bandgap of ZnO is about 3.3 eV. The bandgap of MgxZn1-xO can be
tailored in between by changing the mixing ratios of these two materials [23]. The conduction
band offset for MgO/ZnO superlattice is as large as 3.83 eV [24]. By changing the compositions
of MgxZn1-xO, the band offset can also be varied. The other key issue is that MgO and ZnO are
not fully soluble. The maximum solubility of MgO in ZnO is about 4 at.% from phase diagram.
MgO will precipitate in the mixture when its concentration is high [25]. For high temperature
processed polycrystalline MgxZn1-xO prepared at 550 and 1200 oC, the solubility limits were
observed to be 15 % and 16% [26-27]. Furthermore, MgO crystal structure is cubic and ZnO is
wurtzite, bulk MgxZn1-xO experiences a phase separation from wurtzite to cubic when x exceeds
20% [28]. Metastable cubic structure of MgxZn1-xO is realized by using thick buffer ZnO buffer
layers for x up to 0.6 [29]. Recently, we have shown that the 2DEG can exist in the rf-sputtered
defective MgZnO/ZnO heterostructures [30-31].

旅程
本次由台北出發，搭機至日本轉機至紐華克機場，再轉車到波士頓。回程則由波士頓先
搭車至紐華克機場，再搭機至日本轉機回台北。
地點
MRS fall meeting 固定於Boston舉行。是一個很有歷史味道的城市。
會議
本次會議有五十一個子題，幾個大項包括：Information processing and sensing,
Nanoscience and technology, Energy and the environment, Materials across the
scales, Health and biological materials, 以及 General interest。內容十分豐富。
二、與會心得
以往都只參加 MRS spring meeting，這是第一次參加 MRS fall meeting。議程的重點
不太一樣，這次個人比較有興趣的子題為：Symposium C - Large-are processing and
patterning for optical, photovoltaic, and electronic devices II, Symposium H –
ZnO and related materials, Symposium L – Large-area electronics from carbon
nanotubes, graphene, and related noncarbon nanostructures, Symposium Q –
Photovoltaic material and manufacturing issues II, Symposium Z – Energy
harvesting – from fundamentals to devices,等等子項。本次主要是與台大光電所
陳奕君教授及其光電所學生、台大光電所吳育任教授及其學生、工研院材化所林澤勝博
士、工研院材化所邱國創博士共同發表論文。是一個人新嘗試的領域。報告安排在
Symposium H – ZnO and related materials。大會這一次請到了台灣中央研究院的吳
茂坤演講台灣的科技發展，很有意思。
Symposium H 的演講有幾個和電子元件有關的演講，和實驗室做的方向有高度相關，包
括 ZnO-based MESFET devices, ZnO UV photodetector with tunable quality factor,
Visible blind UV photocathode based on MgZnO thin film alloys and nanostructure
for space application。而在異質介面電子元件則有 p-NiO/n-MgZnO、p-NiO/ZnGaO、
ZnO 和 TGZO 的薄膜電晶體。有關 UV 的 Photodetector，在太空中有很多地面偵測不到
的紫外線，因此美國太空總署特別有興趣。另外有許多和 ZnO 水熱法的研究。ZnO 的發
展已有很長的一段歷史，不過，整個基礎和應用研究的活動仍在蓬勃發展，例如表面電
子的結構，激發子和材料結構及材料表面極性的關係，雷射激發發光的研究等等。
另一部分和實驗室相關的研究課題為多重量子井，也有許多相關的演講。例如一些拘束
效應、光激發光實驗、不規則原子排列對激發子的影響等等。和生物相關也有一些氧化
鋅的凝膠研究。
另外一個個人有研究興趣的課題為染料敏化太陽能電池，該電池的發明人 Michael
Graetzel 也應邀演講。另外一個相關的演講為一些和染料敏化太陽能電池相關的一些問
題，例如和水有關的議題，也很有意思。
