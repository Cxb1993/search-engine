Performance improvement of the flexible organic light emitting devices / flat plane

一、中文摘要： 
 
目前有機發光平面顯示元件(Organic Light 
Emitting Device/ Flat Panel Display, OLED/FPD)
效能不佳主要原因之一，即為高反射係數基板
所造成之內部全反射(Total internal reflection, 
TIR)效應，以致僅有少數比率之有機發光值可
透過基板；亦由於玻璃基板上波導及 TIR 效應
下，其 OLED 發光損失幾乎達到 90%。因此，
如何研發一改善發光效率之 OLED/FPD 應用
元件，實為本計畫之首要整體目標。 
因此本計畫依過去研究之成果，以探討此
可撓式 OLED/FPD 應用元件之效能改善。吾
人首先利用直流磁控離子源濺鍍系統(DC Ion 
Source sputtering system)，於未預熱之硬化處
理塑膠(Hardness Poly-carbonate, HPC)基板上
成長抗反射(Anti-reflection, AR)膜，且尋求其
最佳介電質薄膜(Nb2O5、SiO2)之光電特性資
料；並利用該氧化膜的折射率(n)、消光係數(k)
等相關光學特性，以 TFcalc 模擬軟體設計兩
種材料交疊而成的最佳化之四層抗反射膜，再
運用此模擬設計之膜層數據來製鍍出多層 AR
膜之最佳透光率。由於製鍍多層膜若每層膜層
之間有些微的吸收(k 值上升)，則極易造成非
截止波段的透光率下降；因此，為改善上述之
缺陷，吾人擬以四層 AR 膜，製鍍於應用元件
背面，藉以改善其透光率特性。 
 
關鍵詞：有機發光平面顯示元件(OLED/ 
FPD)、直流磁控離子源濺鍍系統、硬化
處理塑膠(HPC)基板、抗反射(AR)膜 
Abstract 
 
In general, for the organic light emitting 
devices/ flat panel displays (OLED/ FPD), 
without light efficiency enhancing mechanism, 
the total light efficiency is only 5-10 %. A major 
light loss comes from the traditional absorption 
type polarizer in each layer of LCDs. It only 
allowed less than one half of the light to pass 
through it. Therefore, how to develop an 
OLED/FPD for improving the light emitting 
efficiency is the first research goal of this project. 
In this report, we use the DC reactive 
magnetic with ion source sputtering system to 
deposit the high packing density and smooth 
interface Anti-Reflection (AR) films at 80℃ . 
And we use the AR Coating on the B270 glass 
substrate to improve the visible range of lights 
transmittance average from 91.2% advanced to 
95.2%. By using atomic force microscope (AFM) 
to identify the surface of the sputtered Nb2O5 
films, we found that films’ roughness is 0.185nm.  
Keywords: Organic Light Emitting Device/ Flat 
Panel Display (OLED/FPD), DC Ion Source 
sputtering system, Hardness poly-carbonate 
(HPC) substrate, Anti-reflection (AR) film.
