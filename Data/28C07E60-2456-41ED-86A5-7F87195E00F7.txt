2-1 乙烯 
2-1-1 乙烯在蔬果中之生成機制及其影響 
乙烯作為為一植物荷爾蒙，可調節植物生長、發育及逆境所引起的過程。這些過程包括
種子萌芽、水果熟化、花卉凋謝、葉子衰老及合成種種逆境所誘導的蛋白質。在高等植物乙
烯的合成如圖 1 所示:

轉化過程中，甲硫胺酸的第一個碳最後變成二氧化碳分子上面的碳，第二碳則轉化到蟻
酸上，第三及第四碳成為乙烯分子的碳，如式 1 所示

而甲硫胺酸分子中的硫仍留在植物組織內，甲硫胺酸先反應成「硫-腺核苷甲硫胺酸」
（S-adenosylmethionine，簡稱為 SAM），再分解成為甲基硫腺核苷酸與 1-胺基環丙烷羧酸
（1-aminocyclopropane carboxylic acid，簡稱為 ACC），在氧氣參與之下，ACC 迅速轉變成乙
烯。 
乙烯亦為促使水果熟化的關鍵因子[9]。在水果採收後的長時間儲存、運輸過程中，由於
水果所產生乙烯的累積造成催熟，水果會迅速熟化，進而腐爛；因此，如何避免包裝中乙烯
的累積，成為水果保存的重要議題。控制包裝中乙烯累積，可從二方面著手：一是水果的生
理反應，藉降低儲藏溫度減緩果實生理反應，或用化學藥劑、基因改良來抑制水果產生乙烯
的生化反應；二是針對乙烯處理，利用吸附劑、氧化劑、觸媒和 TiO2 光觸媒，移除乙烯。[1-10] 
 
2-1-2 蔬果採收後儲存環境之乙烯控制技術 
目前研究對於延長水果保鮮時間之方法分為物理、化學、生物三大部分。物理方法，改
變環境之溫度、氣體組成(Fellman et al., 2003; Gerasopoulos and Richardson, 1997)；二是化學
方法，改變水果基因，依水果特性不同進行不同之基因轉植或以化學藥劑抑制水果產生乙烯，
使其不易在運送過程中腐爛(Wills and Warton, 2004)；三是生物方法，以生物取代物化材料進
行降解反應，減少貯藏環境之乙烯濃度，以達延緩熟化之效果 (Martinez-Romero et al., 2007)。
但由於傳統方法效果不彰，又基因轉植及化學藥劑，對人體健康之影響與否仍存有疑慮。故
本研究試圖以吸附劑進行外部處理蔬果儲存環境中之乙烯，找出吸附劑效果最佳之狀態，使
其得以實際應用於蔬果的輸送，並需要考慮材料對環境之影響及回收再利用之可行性，以降
低環境資源之損耗。 
 
2-2 二氧化鈦光觸媒材料 
2-2-1 二氧化鈦結構特性 
二氧化鈦最為被應用在光催化反應上，乃由於二氧化鈦具理想能帶結構，以及不受光子
激發後而活性衰退等特性。若能隙太大，將造成激發電子時所耗能源變大；若能隙太小，則
被激發的電子－電洞對容易再結合而造成反應活性不佳。優點包括不產生光腐蝕、來源豐富
而成本低、高分解效率、低操作溫度、低能量消耗和高比表面積等等。

於可見光之光催化效率及其光催化作用機制。

(a) 溶膠－凝膠法（sol－gel method）

溶膠(sol)。凝膠(gel)則為經過水解(hydrolysis) 及縮合(condensation)反應，分子單元逐漸轉變
為大分子之凝膠狀態。圖 2 為溶膠凝膠法形成過程之示意圖：

比較所有合成方法，溶膠凝膠法具有簡便、低成本及不需在高溫下合成等優點，同時，
以此法所製備的二氧化鈦之物理、化學及電化學特性易被修飾，因此可提高其光催化活性，
並具有高純度、良好結構、高比表面積、利於控制粒徑、具有一致的孔隙結構等特性，因此，
此法被廣泛應用於金屬氧化物之製備。 
(b) 水熱法 (hydrothermal method) 
水熱法係指於密閉系統中，固定一溫度(>100℃)及水的自生壓力(>101.3kPa)，通常是在
不鏽鋼的密閉容器中進行，利用水蒸氣造成高溫( < 250 ℃)、高壓之反應環境，進而增加其
溶液之溶解度，然而最終產物於溶液中溶解後結晶再析出，最後重新排列組合為所需之晶體，
因此水熱法的的合成過程為一溶解—析出之反應過程(dissolution-precipitation reaction)。此反
應起使物通常是無定形 TiO2、鈦酸鹽或鈦酸無機鹽，如四氯化鈦（Titanium tetrachloride，
TiCl4），當四氯化鈦與水接觸時會進行快速的水解反應，對此水溶液加熱會幫助反應的進行
並使其聚合而讓二氧化鈦沈積下來。Park et. al.[3]以 TiCl4 在 70℃加熱水溶液合成二氧化鈦粉
體，並且可得到均一的型態分佈[4, 5]。而 Wei et. al.[6]則得到了奈米尺寸的二氧化鈦粉體。影
響因子為 pH 值、溶液濃度、水熱溫度及反應時間。低 pH 值助於 rutile 生成，中性和弱酸有
利於 anatase 生成，中性和弱鹼性則利於 brookite 生成；溶液濃度大使其水解緩慢，需要更長
反應時間或更高的水解溫度幫助反應進行；水熱溫度高有利於水解反應進行；反應時間增加
可得到均勻的奈米晶體。其優點包括設備簡單、、控制容易、容易、可連續生產及原料便宜
且無需進行段燒、所置備的粉體之晶粒發育完整、粒徑一般為微米級且分布均勻無團聚等等
特性。此法常應用於陶瓷氧化物粉體之合成。 
 
(c) 化學氣相沈積法(chemical vapor deposition；CVD) 
依能量來源可區分為三類，熱活化化學氣相沈積(thermal CVD)、光輔助化學氣相沈積
(photo CVD)、電漿化學氣相沈積(plasma enhanced CVD, PECVD)等等。 
由於 CVD 可於極短時間內均勻披覆大表面積之物質，因此廣泛地應用於工業製程中陶瓷
或是半導體膜製備。原理係藉由加熱基片或物體表面，通過一種或數種氣態元素或化合物，
並產生化學反應而生成不揮發的固體膜層，利用化學反應方式，將氣體反應物在特定區域內
反應並沉積於基材表面的一種薄膜技術。 
依化學反應形式不同分為兩類，熱分解反應沉積及化學反應沉積。熱分解反應沉積係指
化合物加熱分解，在基片表面得到固態膜層，為化學氣相沉積中最簡單的形式；而化學反應
沉積係指兩種或兩種以上的氣體物質在加熱基片表面發生化學反應而沉積成固態膜層。因此
其反應條件具備以下三項必要條件，(一)沉積溫度下反應物須具有足夠高的蒸氣壓；(二)僅
沉積物為固態，其它物質須為氣態；(三)沉積物本身蒸氣壓低，確保沉積過程使其固定於加
熱基片。 
而此方法之影響因子包括前趨物的種類、沉積速率、載流氣體之流速、基材之種類與溫
度、反應之溫度、反應系統之壓力等等。以沉積溫度而言，當溫度越高，則導致其沉積速率
越高，因此沉積物成膜越致密。然而，基片材質需選擇與沉積膜之間需具強親和力、相近的
膨脹係數及相似的結構為佳。

2-3-2-2 氧氣濃度 
氣相光催化反應中，氧氣與水分子一起吸附於觸媒表面，大大促進氧化反應的進行；因
氧氣為一與電洞反應而形成氫氧自由基之重要來源，並可氧化有機[14]或是無機之汙染物。
氧氣被發現在半導體光催化降解有機物時式不可或缺的。根據 Braun 等人的研究，他們發現
較高的氧氣含量將造成光催化反應速率的降低，此可歸因於高濃度氧氣的存在，使得二氧化
鈦表面高度的水化，造成競爭、抑制汙染物的吸附位址活性[15]。 
在沒有任何氧化劑和還原劑存在情形下，且處於穩態 (steady state) 時，電子和電洞達到
平衡。當氧氣存在時，吸附在 TiO2 表面的氧會參與反應並消耗電子，使電子和電洞的重組
受阻，促進氧化能力。Dibble 和 Raupp[1, 6]以 TCE 為研究對象，也發現氧濃度與反應速率呈
線性關係或零階反應，當氧濃度較低時，反應速率與氧濃度多呈線性關係，反之則斜率為零。
Raupp[1]以 UV 光催化分解丙酮，發現氧化速率隨著氧濃度增加而增加，直到氧濃度超過 10% 
(莫耳比)時才趨於定值。Wang[16]研究以二氧化鈦光催化分解 TCE，發現當氧濃度小於 500 
ppm 時，TCE 的轉化率介於 30~88%之間；當氧濃度大於 500 ppm 時，轉化率達到 86~91%。
轉化率增加的原因為氧濃度較高時，氧分子在二氧化鈦表面形成單層吸附達到飽和狀態，因
此增進 TCE 之分解。Hung[2]等研究氧濃度對 TCE 光催化分解的影響，發現氧濃度小於
1,000ppm 時，TCE 的轉化率隨著氧濃度增加而增加；但氧濃度大於 1,000 ppm 時，TCE 轉化
率並未明顯增加，認為在高氧濃度時，因為競爭氧化反應佔優勢，Cl•攻擊 TCE 反應會受到
明顯抑制。劉安治等[3]研究 PCE 的光催化反應，發現 PCE 轉化率隨著氧濃度的增加而增加，
在低氧濃度時，其轉化率增加較為明顯；但在高氧濃度時，則漸趨平緩。氧氣的存在並不會
阻礙反應物的吸附性，Jacoby[5]在光催化分解苯的研究中，在相同的濕度條件下，比較無氧
和有氧（含氧量為 21%）時對苯在二氧化鈦表面吸附性之影響，發現有氧時對苯的吸附量無
顯著的差異，因此認為氧氣的存在並不會阻礙苯在光催化劑表面的吸附性。 
Raupp以UV光催化分解丙酮，發現氧化速率隨著氧濃度增加而增加，直到氧濃度超過10 
%(莫耳比)時才趨於定值[1]。Hung等研究氧濃度對TCE光催化分解的影響，發現氧濃度小於
1000 ppm時，TCE的轉化率隨著氧濃度增加而增加；但氧濃度大於1000 ppm以上時，TCE轉
化率並未明顯增加，認為在高氧濃度時，因為競爭氧化反應佔優勢，Cl‧攻擊TCE反應會受
到明顯抑制[2]。 
Jacoby 等人(1996)在光催化分解苯的研究中發現，在相同的濕度條件下 (1000 ppm)，比
較無氧和有氧(含氧量為 21 %)時對苯在二氧化鈦表面吸附性之影響，發現有氧時對苯的吸附
量無顯著的差異，因此認為氧氣的存在並不會阻礙苯在光催化劑表面的吸附性[5]。

受限於質傳的例子較多。

3.3.4 傅立葉轉換紅外線光譜儀(Fourier-transform IR spectroscopy, FT-IR) 
紅外光光譜儀是近幾年來化合物鑑定及環境量測的重要工具之一。紅外光為一般的光能
中較低頻之能量（大約 400-4000 cm-1 或 2.5-25 µm），其頻率的能量與分子振動及轉動所需能
量相當，因此，分子能夠吸收與其振動或轉動所需能量相當的紅外光，而進行能階躍遷，此
一能量的吸收行為造成特定頻率的紅外光能量被吸收，同時吸收光的程度與分子的數量有關
係，因此利用各化合物對紅外光吸收特定波長之特性，比對紅外光吸收圖譜與標準圖譜可以
判定量測物之種類，物種濃度光譜吸收強度則遵循比爾定律（Beer’s Law）。傅立葉轉換紅外
光譜法（FT-IR），則利用干涉儀產生干涉波並照射至樣品後，再由電腦轉換成紅外光光譜，
相較於一般古典的紅外線光譜儀，FT-IR 提供更快、更準確，且更高靈敏度的量測。 
 
於傅立葉轉換紅外線光譜儀( Nicolet Nexus FT-IR spectrometer)分析前，需先進行校正，
分別針對濕空氣、乾空氣和 KBr 進行校正，校正完畢後完後取 0.001 g 之樣品與 0.2 g 之 KBr
混和，研磨後壓成片狀並確認外觀可透光之程度，置入 FT-IR 中進行分析，所分析之數據在
透由其控制軟體扣除背景值，濕空氣、乾空氣和 KBr 後轉存。

(a) 反應器測漏實驗

比較二個小時內的乙烯濃度差值，以檢驗反應器在該實驗條件下是否密合。 
 
(b) 載體吸附實驗 
同反應器測漏實驗，反應條件為 1atm，環境溫度為 25 ℃，流速為 200 mL/min，氣體通
過質量流量控制器及曝氣瓶加濕裝置，由露點溫度偵測器測量露點溫度，並透過露點溫度及
相對濕度的換算公式，將反應系統之相對濕度(relative humidity, RH)控制在 70 %，氧氣含量
為 21%，乙烯初始濃度約為 100 ppmv。在反應器的部分，反應槽內放入以 95 % 酒精經超音
波震盪清洗半小時之空白載玻片(長、寬、高分別為 7.5 cm * 2.5 cm * 0.1 cm) 進行為時兩小時
之空白載體吸附批次式實驗。表面披覆光觸媒之反應試片，亦進行載體吸附實驗，實驗條件
同空白載體吸附實驗，以得知披覆光觸媒之試片對乙烯是否有吸附情形。 
 
(c) 乙烯自分解反應 
測試氣體條件同載體吸附實驗，由面板調配好反應條件，經過反應器約半小時至一小時，

pollution of the irrigation soil and the sediment from the irrigation channel has drawn

processes of decomposition do not remove heavy metals. On the contrary, they may 
be enriched by organisms and be converted to organic complexes, which may be even

  Traditionally heavy metal pollution is assessed mainly based on total extraction. 
Since the toxicity of a metal depends more on its chemical forms than on its total 
amount, determination of metal speciation is essential to evaluate metal toxicity in the 
environment. Principally the metal solubility is controlled by pH, concentration and 
type of ligands, oxidation-state of the mineral components, and the redox environment 
of the system (Jain, 2004). It has been speculated that irrigation water is the major 
source of metal contamination on agricultural soils. Speciation of heavy metal in soils 
or sediments is an important factor governing their mobility, toxicity and 
bioavailability (Ko et al., 2007). Chemical speciation can be defined as the 
identification, quantification, or the description of the different species, forms or 
phases present in a material (Fytianos and Lourantou, 2004). However, the 
determination of specific chemical species or binding forms is difficult and often 
hardly possible (Fytianos and Lourantou, 2004). Therefore, determinations of broader 
forms, e.g. so-called mobile or carbonate bound forms, depending on operationally 
defined procedures can be a good compromise in terms of assessing environmental 
risks (Fytianos and Lourantou, 2004). The determination of extractable trace metals in 
soil or sediment is often used to gain an insight into chemical speciation. Therefore, 
sequential extraction of elements from soil or sediment is a widely used technique in 
environmental studies. The metal extraction procedures followed those of proposed 
by Tessier with modifications. A total of five fractions were extracted: exchangeable, 
carbonate, Fe–Mn oxides, organic material, and silicate residues. 
  In Taiwan, due to the close proximity between the agricultural lands and factories,

X-ray absorption spectroscopy 
This technique uses synchrotron radiation to photo-ionize the core electrons of Cr 
atom and provides useful information on the valance of chromium. When the 
increasing scanning energy is sufficient to eject the core electrons, the energy 
absorption abruptly increases (absorption edge). With the increasing oxidation 
state, the absorption edge shifts to higher energies and thus the valence state can 
be determined. X-ray absorption near edge structure (XANES) spectra of iron 
samples including metallic Cr and CrO were obtained on the Wiggler beamline at 
the Synchrotron Radiation Research Center (SRRC) in Hsinchu, Taiwan. The 
electron storage ring was operated with an energy of 1.5 GeV and a current of 
200 mA. A Si(111) double-crystal monochromator (DCM) was used to provide 
highly monochromatized photon beams with energies of 4 to 15 keV and an 
energy resolution of 1.9×10−4 (eV/eV). The data were collected in transmission 
mode with a Lytle detector in the region of the Cr K edge (5989 eV) at room 
temperature. The absorption spectra were obtained using ion chambers filled with 
helium gas. The photon energy was calibrated with characteristic pre-edge peaks 
in the absorption spectrum of an Chromium foil. Each sample had four scans. 
The fractions of iron species in the samples were deduced using least-square 
fittings of the XANES spectra. The height and area of the near edge band in the 
chromium spectrum are quantitatively proportional to the amount of Cr species.

4.0 Conclusion 
In agriculture environmental, trace metals undergo numerous changes during their 
transport due to dissolution, precipitation and sorption phenomena. High 
concentrations of Cr is thought to have resulted from anthropogenic influences, 
practically from industry used in agriculture, and are found to present a pollution risk. 
From the results of the present investigation, the following conclusions can be drawn. 
 
1. Metal speciation of sediment and soil has obvious differences in this site. For 
example, the Cr species in the channel sediment was present mostly in Fraction 5. 
However, results showed that in soils the Cr species were present in Fraction 3. 
 
2. Synchrotron radiation results showed that in sediments and soils the Cr species 
were seem to have different trend. 
 
3. Sequential extraction and synchrotron radiation results showed that the speciation 
difference of sediments and soils were seem to have the same trend. 
 
4. The Cr species in the channel sediments and soils were present mostly in trivalent 
chromium, while availability and mobility of Cr was low, and therefore it would 
represent a low risk for the environment and human health.

ABSTRACT 
Recently, the relationship between the heavy metals pollution of the irrigation soil and 
the sediment from the irrigation channel has drawn great attentions from the scientific 
communities. Traditionally, heavy metal pollutions are assessed by total extraction. 
Since the toxicity of a metal depends more on its chemical forms than on its total 
amount, determination of metal speciation is essential to evaluate metal toxicity in the 
environment. It has been speculated that irrigation water is the major source of metal 
contamination on agricultural soils. However, no information is available on the 
transformations of heavy metals, variations in metal speciation, and transport 
mechanisms between the sediment of the irrigation channel and the soil of the farm 
land. This research was intended to assess the speciation of chromium in soils and 
sediments of contamination sites. Two experiments, the sequential extraction 
procedure and synchrotron radiation, were conducted to assess the source of 
contamination. Irrigation channel sediments and agricultural soils were collected from 
sites in Central Taiwan in polyethylene bags. The samples were immediately air dried 
and stored in the laboratory before further study. Large debris such as stones and plant 
fragments were removed from the dried samples by screening using 2-mm sieve. The 
metal extraction procedure was modified from that proposed by Tessier. The 
sequential extraction produced five fractions: exchangeable, carbonate, Fe–Mn oxides, 
organic material, and silicate residues. Results of sequential extraction indicated that 
chromium speciation mostly occurred in the residual fraction, about 70% in the 
sediment whereas 20-45% in the Fe–Mn oxide fraction for the corresponding soil. 
Results of EXAFS spectra analysis indicated that the chromium in the sample of soil 
profile (0-15cm and 15-30cm) were the same species, and the chromium in other 
samples were the same species. 
1. INTRODUCTION 
Soil pollution is a serious environmental problem in highly industrialized countries. In 
particular, contaminated soils with heavy metals, such as Cr, Cu, Zn, Ni, Pb, and Cd 
have been recognized as the troublesome problem. In Taiwan, more than 50,000 ha of 
transport mechanisms between the sediment of the irrigation channel and the soil of 
the farm land. This research was intended to assess the speciation of chromium in 
soils and sediments of contamination sites. For this reason, this research focused on 
the transformation of chromium species in soils and sediments by sequential 
extraction procedures or by in situ analyses such as X-ray absorption spectroscopy 
(XAS). In particular, some important structural information could be clearly clarified 
through the analysis of extended X-ray absorption fine structure spectroscopy 
(EXAFS) such as bond distance, coordination number (CN) as well as the local 
structure of chromium species. The pollution of heavy metal of the agriculture soil 
mainly originates from the irrigating channel. However, the heavy metal was 
discharged from pollution sources to the channel. Irrigation water is the carrier to 
transport the pollution from channels to the fields. In the present study, we 
investigated the occurrence of chromium in agricultural soils and the sediments of the 
irrigation channels. Specifically, we evaluated the possible source of chromium 
contamination of agricultural soils in relation to the irrigation water. Therefore, the 
objectives of the present study were (1) to investigate the distribution of chromium in 
soils and sediments of irrigation channels using sequential extraction procedure, (2) to 
gain insight into the transformations of chromium in the agriculture system.
