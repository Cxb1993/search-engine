一、摘要(關鍵字：居家生活、生活作息、遠距溝
通、互動式溝通、情境設計)  
本研究計畫主要運用環境行為觀察法與實地
訪談法，探討家人生活作息活動上外出時、外出後
的溝通需求，例如「外出者對家中」、「家中對外
出者」，「外出者對外出者」等三種情境產生的遠
距型互動溝通需求。針對台灣地區年輕世代、中老
年世代，不同世代八個家庭研究案例進行現況調
查。藉以了解與分析現代家庭生活中，每個階段世
代家庭中，家人因工作或學習生活作息的不同，由
內而外或由外而內，出入家門時段有先後次序的差
異，因而產生斷續銜接的溝通需求所以需要留言、
備忘、記事、通訊影像之互動溝通平台。 
因此，本研究計畫針對不同世代家庭的互動溝
通問題與需求，提出未來互動式溝通情境設計：1.
個人行程訊息回饋 2.家人間相互提醒留言 3.生活
管理與保全需求；藉以了解家庭成員未來互動式溝
通環境使用情境。並且根據情境設計導引為基礎，
同時結合子計畫二共同提出四個未來互動式居家
服務機器人創新設計提案（如圖2），分別為分別
為：生活管家機器人－Sunny、居家看護機器人－
菲比、居家生活陪伴機器人－MIHO、兒童伴讀機
器人－Ｈug Touch，以建構未來生活互動式溝通環
境。 
    最後，本研究計畫成果以3D模擬情境以及具
體外觀模型，呈現互動式創新產品設計提案，同時
導入未來高科技相關技術，讓家人在不同的時間、
場所都能隨時進行互動溝通，為家庭生活帶來
更便利的溝通方式，提供未來科技生活中，發展互
動式溝通產品的參考依據。 
 
二、計畫緣由與目的 
2.1 研究背景與動機 
近三十年來，台灣社會隨工商業蓬勃與科技進
步快速，家庭生活形式已隨之轉變，從農村社會三
代同堂、親族同聚的大家庭，逐漸轉變為以兩代同
堂的核心家庭形式。而現代核心家庭生活，從新婚 
到兒女成長的不同階段，夫妻與子女的生活作息與 
互動溝通形式有很大的變化與差異。 
實際上，家庭生活因成員的年齡結構不同，家
人照料與生活起居的溝通需求也不一樣，藉助科技
支援的問題性也因人而異。例如：育幼時期夫妻，
夫妻生活溝通重點在幼小子女照顧工作輪替與教
導。而成長時期的夫妻，上有年邁的父母，下有好
動叛逆的青少年子女，要煩惱子女升學課業、叛逆
意識，與子女意見不合溝通困難問題。接著，成熟
時期夫妻，面對子女成長外出求學或工作的空巢
期，有遠距溝通聯繫的問題，與面對雙親年老疾病
的照顧問題。 
本研究從台灣家庭生活現況，審視不同時期家
庭成員生活作息與互動溝通問題。因應不同時期的
家庭生活需求提出未來互動式溝通的情境設計。另
外，如何巧妙運用科技、將科技產品融入人們的生
活，運用於家庭生活的貢獻度，也是未來產品設計
開發上很重要的課題。 
 
2.2 研究目的 
  本子計畫將延續第一年與第二年的研究成果
與發現，探討居家生活家人因工作或學習生活作息
的不同，由內而外或由外而內，出入家門時段有先
後次序的差異，因而產生斷續銜接的溝通需求，所
以需要留言、備忘、記事、通訊影像等互動溝通平
台。  
  主要應用環境行為觀察與實地訪談法，探討
家人生活作息活動上外出時、外出後的溝通需求，
例如外出者對家中、家中對外出者，外出者對外出
者等三種情境產生的遠距型互動溝通需求。透過劇
本導引模擬各種家人內外溝通的情境設計，以3D 
電腦模擬建構具體的互動溝通裝置與創新設計提
案，以達到實質提升居家生活家人內外互動溝通平
台系統之建置。

四、家庭成員互動溝通歸納分析 
本研究透過個案訪談現況調查分析的紀錄，歸
納台灣核心家庭居家生活成員，因日常生活作息活
動活動、行為之間影響的相關性資料與成員不同場
合下所產生不同行為的差異性。並以研究中的年輕
世代與中老年世代對象進行互動溝通之歸納分
析，就家庭生活形式、家庭生活型態、生活作息與
互動溝通，歸納本研究之發現。  
 
1.家庭生活形式： 
  依據本研究問卷與主計處統計結果，現代核心
家庭多為雙薪家庭，夫妻雙方的學歷越來越高，夫
妻年齡差距多在五歲內。本研究針對居住在公寓形
式的現代不同世代核心家庭作調查，佔大多數為社
區型大廈，居住面積約在 21-40 坪為最普遍，且
大多居住在 10-20 年屋齡的房子。年輕世代家庭來
說，家庭收入情形與職業別會影響家庭生活形式，
家庭經濟壓力與工作壓力。對於中老年世代，主要
針對日常生活的自理，未來生活起居溝通需求為主
要影響的因素。 
2.家庭族群描述： 
  本研究利用 AIOS 量表進行因素分析，利用
集群分析將年輕世代核心家庭區分為忙碌打拚
族、外向好客族、保守安穩族與積極守份族等四
族。另外中老年世代核心家庭區分為好學風尚
型、熱情豪放型、活潑健談型、知足常樂型、投機
好義型、孤單寡欲型，分析空巢期生活上之需要，
如：親友陪伴、起居協助、行程提醒、資訊收發。 
 
3.家庭互動溝通： 
由訪談案例中發現：現代核心家庭生活作息與
活動，不管平日或假日，因工作與求學限制顯得大
同小異。家庭成員平日互動溝通，易發生於每日必
要行為與約束行為。假日不同家庭儘管會進行不同
室內外休閒活動，家人互動還是多發生於自由性時
段內。家庭習慣性的活動，已成為家人之間重要的
互動溝通方式。 
(1) 家人互動溝通以吃早餐、晚餐時段是最主要的
方式，客廳和餐廳為主要溝通場所。 
(2) 家人互動場所主要為客廳，其次為餐廳。不同
處在於忙碌打拚族只有多書房一個互動空間，
顯示十分注重子女教養。 
(3) 現今年輕世代核心家庭外出者以先生居多，可
能是因為以先生為主要收入來源，常需在外打
拚。現代因手機通訊相當方便，家庭成員間有
事便隨時聯絡。 
(4) 家中成員作息時間不同，衍生老人看護、居家
保全等問題。 
(5) 家中年長者忘記吃藥和量血壓，需家人提醒。 
(6) 體力大不如前，需要藉助高科技協助或人力支
援，如傭人、幫手來分擔家事。

功能下巴部位採用軟材質，讓使用者頭部更加舒
適。 
 
七、結論與建議 
本研究以七個案例調查分析、情境設計與四個
完整創新機器人設計提案。針對整體研究成果，歸
納幾點結論： 
 
1. 透過環境觀察與文獻研究發現人與人的行為
不僅於言語的溝通，眼神、肢體、表情與行為
在家人的相處更為重要，家庭環境中的物品與
成員生活行為，與家庭溝通互動主要地點，
如：門、玄關，都是可善加運用感性設計的情
感凝聚媒介。因此未來高齡者互動溝通設計必
須讓人與物更緊密的結合以達到情感聯繫。 
2. 利用情境設計的導引，模擬未來互動式溝通裝
置使用情境，了解居家生活互動式溝通的需
求，如長者照護、居家安全、不同作息與不同
空間場域的聯繫等。 
3. 現代老年世代家庭因子女工作忙碌、作息不
同，面對面溝通的時間相對減少，以及因生活
空間樓層配置，所產生各種溝通需求；需要具
備整合留言、多媒體、語音辨識、聲控說話、
保全監控功能、家電操作以及互傳訊息之互動
溝通平台，為高科技產業實現商品化的參考依
據和消費市場所趨。 
4. 未來家庭互動式溝通裝置兼具有遊戲、休閒、
育樂的調劑功能，期許為未來家庭生活帶來更
方便的溝通模式、讓科技產品更富人性化，除
提供貼心、忠誠的支援與服務交流外，亦在發
揮科技人性化的實質功效；藉由多功能高科技
整合之互動式平台而能被家庭視為一份子。 
5. 藉由情境模擬的導引設計，模擬未來家庭互動
式溝通產品的使用情境，藉此了解家庭成員於
居家生活互動式溝通的需求，如不同作息與不
同場域間由內而外或由外而內的聯繫、時間的
斷續連結等，並藉情境模擬設計的發展，進行
互動式溝通產品的創新設計提案。 
6. 感謝國科會工程處此次「未來生活互動式溝通
環境之建置」整合專案，給予設計發展可能性
與工程科技整合並發揮的機會，期待創造未來
互動式產品的設計新視界。

 The Study to Parameters of Mineral Water Bottles’ Features 
and Establishment of Innovative Design Service System 
 
Ching Yang a and HsuanChu Chen b 
a yangj@yuntech.edu.tw, Department of Industrial Design,, National Yunlin University of Science 
and Technology,NO.,123 University Road, Section 3, Douliou, Yunlin 64002, Taiwan； 
b flychu@gmail.com, Department of Design Doctoral Program, National Yunlin University of 
Science and Technology,NO.,123 University Road, Section 3, Douliou, Yunlin 64002, Taiwan. 
 
 
Abstract:  
 
With a framework based on ICAID, this study adopted parametric design, carried out three stages of 
experiments, and established a generative service and consultation system of mineral water bottle design.  
At the first stage, the system data was collected through choosing 20 representative samples of 580-600ml 
mineral water bottles from popular brands in Taiwan; then, CATIA parameterization was used to construct 
four shape feature surfaces including bottle neck, body, ring type and bottom, which were combined as 
the basic prototype. This prototype was transferred into features parameter to construct 64 basic modular 
shape samples, and the number of test sample was reduced to 16 through Taguchi Methods. Expert focus 
groups chose six pairs of semantic adjectives including simple-complex, familiar-unacquainted, 
cheap-expensive, smooth-crude, soft-rigid and “easy-to-hold”-“difficult-to-hold”. SD (Semantic 
Differential) investigation was conducted among 30 testees, and regression analysis was used to get 
semantic evaluation formula and coefficient. At the second stage, this study established system interface 
and generative mode, utilized system data of the first stage, connected with interface through graphics, 
constructed parametric generative mechanism, and proposed two kinds of shape generation systems 
namely Top-down conceptual design and Bottom-up modular design. At the third stage of system test and 
evaluation, system operation interaction evaluating test and individual interviews were implemented 
among five experts through situational simulation. This study found the established shape-generation 
design service system could really provide designers brand market demands through combining 
parametric generative method with Top-down and Bottom-up thinking mode. Diversified water bottle 
shape designs, which are quickly obtained through 3D software, can be extensively applied to the shape 
design of drink containers, household appliances, electronic products, etc., and provide practical and 
pluralized functions of innovate design service and consultation.  
 
Key words: Mineral Water Bottle, Innovate Design, Shape Feature, Parametric, Semantic Differential  
 
 
 
1 RESEARCH MOTIVES AND PURPOSES  
 
Along with recent global warming, abnormal climate changes, gradually-drained earth resources and 
industrial pollution, people have been anxious about sources and problems of potable water in life. 
Therefore, credible mineral water brands are increasing on the market annually, water resource is seen as 
commercial “blue gold”, and people’s drinking habits and life styles are changed. Besides, the 
ever-improved CAID provides more than a graphics environment. If computer and designers can design 
jointly, offer reference information at proper time, and design integration is directly implemented through 
computer 3D, the designer will promptly enter design field.  
This study took personal mineral water bottle of 600ml sold in Taiwan as an example, established a 
service system of mineral water bottle design, used ICAID as basic frame, and conducted three stages of 
experiments: collecting basic system data, building system interface and generative mode, as well as 
system test and evaluation. The experiment steps are as follows. At the first stage, it constructed basic 
system data, collected mineral water bottles and adjective vocabulary for knowledge base, and used 
morphological analysis to cross assemble samples after summarizing and selecting. Then, Taguchi 
orthogonal array was used to reduce the samples of semantic differential experiment, and regression 
analysis was used to calculate semantic value of the samples. For component base, CATIA 
parameterization was used to establish the basic prototype of 3D components. At the second stage, system 
interface and generative mode was firstly constructed through evaluating the media software of system 
 3 RESEARCH METHODS AND EXPERIMENT DESIGN 
 
In terms of the research purposes, this study established “innovative design service system” of mineral 
water bottle through three stages: 1. system knowledge collecting; 2. system interface and generative 
mode constructing; 3. system test and evaluation. Exact steps were explained as follows: 
(1) System knowledge collecting: for system knowledge base, it firstly collected mineral water bottle Patt 
-erns and adjective vocabulary, then used Taguchi Orthogonal Arrays to reduce experimental samples 
of SD, and finally adopted regression analysis to predict utility value of original sample; for system 
component base, it constructed each 3D component by parametric means and assembled each basic 
prototype sample.  
(2) System interface and generative mode constructing: this study firstly evaluated media software of 
system frame, and used system interface programming to connect knowledge base with component 
base; then, it explored system processes in terms of user mode to make Top-down and Bottom-up 
operation models can correct each other’s shape through parametric generative method; finally, the 
system could generate mineral water bottle that confirmed to users’ imagination; new shapes could be 
stored in component base. Regression analysis and parametric generative method were used to predict 
image evaluation of new shapes and enlarge knowledge base.  
(3) System test and evaluation: system operation interaction test was conducted among testees by means 
of situational simulation. Testees’ interviews and suggestions were recorded as a reference for 
improvements in subsequent system.  
 
 
4  SELECTING BASIC DATA OF GENERATIVE DESIGN CONSULTATION SYSTEM

4.1 System Component Base Data Collection and E establishment 
 
This section established a system component base by classifying feature types and constructing 3D 
components.  
Feature type classification of mineral water bottle: according to “pollster” on-line brand investigation data 
of 2009/8/29, there are five key brands in Taiwan market: Yes, Vedan, Twist, UNI and King Car. From 
January 2010 till March 2010, 30 samples were collected from domestic convenience stores, chain malls, 
etc. Then, this study analyzed basic feature elements, classified these bottles in terms of their features, 
grouped and ranked them through KJ method, and selected 20 representative samples as shown in Fig.1. 
Finally, the mineral water bottle type area was divided into five parts. The shaping features of four sites, 
including A. bottle neck, B. body, C. ring type and D. bottom, jointly constituted 4×2×4×2=64 basic 
prototype samples as shown in Table 1.  
3D parametric establishment of mineral water bottle:The 3D model of this study was constructed under 
the CATIA graphics environment. Mineral water bottle shape was composed of the first item of each 
component. Parameter substitution can only change the shapes under the same condition. Therefore, 
under 2D Sketch environment, the quantity should be determined by node resulted from Bezier Curve 
when using Spine Curve to draw graphics by means of control point. The control points and restrictive 
conditions of all feature shapes should be the same, and the coordinate figure （x,y）of each node should 
be recorded. Nine parametric dimension definitions were obtained including bottle neck （x1,y1）~
（x3,y3）, body（x4,y4）~（x6,y6）and bottom（x7,y7）~（x9,y9）; shaft was used to construct the 3D 
model of mineral water bottle.

 5  ESTABLISHMENT OF SYSTEM INTERFACE AND GENERATIVE MODE 
 
After finishing the shape data value, emotional vocabulary value and coefficient of the first stage, system 
computation model was constructed at the second stage. Based on ICAID frame, this stage formed a 
generative system frame of design consultation that could be used as a reference for designers’ graphics

Fig.2 A chart about the generative system frame of design consultation 
 
5.1 System Media Evaluation and Information Process Establishment 
 
The overall system, constructed under CATIA graphics environment, was based on the system frame 
planned in Figure 3. This section respectively explored and expatiated on six kinds of particular media 
including information communication, computer graphics mode, design evaluation and judgment, basic 
product component base, and shape generation mode.  
(1) Information communication: CATIA releases two kinds of programming language-Java and Visual 
Basic-for external operation. Therefore, CATIA can store output parameters in Excel, and choose 
Visual Basic that is highly compatible with Excel to write external interface of the programming. 
Relevant details would be exhaustively explained in interface part of Sec. 5.2.  
(2) Computer graphics mode: CATIA was used as the system model presentation environment. 240 basic 
prototypes cross assembled by Morphological Analysis were the basic model data of this system.  
(3) Design evaluation and judgment: this part should combine design knowledge base with basic product 
component base, and these two bases were respectively connected through Excel and VBA. For 
external information communication, Visual Basic programming was used to connect with external 
interface. This framework was divided into Top-down (positive-going) thinking mode judgment and 
Bottom-up (negative-going) thinking mode judgment. The data flows were explained as follows:  
a. Top-down positive-going thinking mode judgment: firstly, the utility values of 64 basic models in 
design knowledge base were summarized as the Excel document in Fig.3. Using the total of 
difference value between system input value and adjective utility value to find out the model code 
that has the least total difference value, and this was the system output value. 
b. Bottom-up negative-going thinking mode judgment: user chose different components constitution. 
After transferring user’s choice into strings, searching was conducted to find out the model code 
that the user chose.  
(4) Design knowledge base: the system should make the design knowledge base correspond to basic 
component base. Basic product component base could be put in the Design Table function of CATIA, 
as well as connected and output to Excel. Therefore, the design knowledge base of this system was 
stored in Excel sheets so as to be connected with subsequent system.  
(5) Basic product component base: CATIA graphics environment was used to draw the basic components 
of component base and standardize each variable name and variable value. A total of nine parameter 
values were output including 4 parameters of bottle neck, 2 of body, 4 of ring type and 2 of bottom. A 
complete database was sorted out and stored by Design Table. Parameter changes could be used for 
changing different shapes.  
(6) Generative shape mode: generative mode aimed at making corrections can be implemented after 
design evaluation and judgment. Users made inspection by parametric generative computation, so as 
to achieve sensatory correction after design evaluation and judgment. The data flows were classified 
into shape parameter and new shape image value, which were respectively explained as follows. 
a. Shape parameter: used Visual Basic programming to judge the basic prototype that user chose. 
After computation through parametric generative method, the prototype would be sent back to 
Design Table as the parameter value of new shapes so that the CATIA could generate changes in 
shape.  
b. Image value of new shape: the semantic image value of new shape was predicted by the way that 
used for predicting color image value through regression formula coefficient and parametric 
generative method.

5.3.2 Use processes of Bottom-Up modular design system 
 
For Bottom-Up modular design, the design service system could be explained through six steps. Designer 
chose to enter modular design system by Top-Down way, changed the features of each component,

bottom control point Y9, which was 2 originally, was adjusted to 6. The parameter value of ring type
