一.前言與研究目的 
目前世界上鎂合金的應用產業以汽車零組件為主，其次為 3C 產業，其他像汔
動工具、自行車、器材工具、運動用品及航太國防也都在其應用範圍之內，而我國
為 3C 產業的重要生產國，鎂合金則多應用於 3C 產業。隨著國內筆記型電腦全球市
佔率達六成以上，有機會成為鎂合金筆記型電腦機殼最大供應廠商，相信鎂合金未
來在工業及電子產業上，將會佔有更重要的地位。此外，輕量化是軍品性能要求重
點項目之一，許多先進國家開始將部份軍品載具更換為鎂鋁合金以達減重目的，隨
著鎂合金的不斷發展，預估將會逐步導入國軍各項軍品上，取代國軍目前以鋁合金
及鋼材為主的裝備，因而鎂鋁合金之表面處理技術，應及早密切瞭解其發展狀況與
趨勢。我國傳統產業進入轉型的契機，經由財團法人鎂合金協會成員共同努力下，台灣鎂
合金成型產業已具雛形，但目前仍遭遇許多挑戰，包含有：(1)鉻酸鹽皮膜及塗裝限制使用，
(2)產品過度集中在筆記型電腦外殼，(3)生產技術尚未成熟，不良率過高卻惡性削價競爭，
(4)廢料無法在地處理，這些隱憂影響業者健全發展及對產品應用的信心，然而台灣企業形
態彈性大、適應力強，符合 3C 產品市場快速求變的特質，所以台灣仍具有很大的競爭優
勢，除努力在 3C 產品保有亞洲市場之外，更可再進軍歐美地區之汽機車及氣動手工具外
銷市場。 
鎂在酸性環境易形成鎂離子而加速腐蝕；而在鹼性環境下，則形成氫氧化鎂之鈍化保
護膜，可有效降低腐蝕速率，此層鈍化膜在 pH 小於 11 的水溶液中不穩定，無法保護鎂底
材免於鏽蝕。鎂合金的標準還原電位較其他金屬元素來的低，極易發生電化學腐蝕之金
屬，通常鎂及其合金在發生腐蝕時，多以點蝕或全面腐蝕，形成疏鬆多孔的灰黑色氧化鎂
(MgO)，且表面逐漸粉化。傳統以添加合金元素方式改善抗蝕性，如添加鋁及鋅金屬的 AZ
系列鎂合金。添加不同的合金元素雖可改善鎂合金之腐蝕特性，但目前鎂合金運用面臨的
最大挑戰是抗蝕能力不足，仍未能滿足市場應用的要求，特別是在戶外使用的時機，而提
供鎂合金防蝕保護最有效率的方法為披覆保護塗層，此保護層必須可在底材上形成均勻附
著、無孔洞(pore free)且當遭受外界機械力損害時具自我修復能力的緩衝層(barrier layer)。 
 
二.文獻探討 
次沉積形成 Cr(OH)3，阻止腐蝕繼續發生，因此增加在皮膜上的 Cr(OH)3 含量，即
可增加皮膜的抗蝕能力。通常皮膜的抗腐蝕性與其厚度成正比的關係，如要讓皮膜
保有防腐蝕的特性，不可以在高溫中處理（＞66℃），否則會減少皮膜的厚度和消除
皮膜的自我修復的特性，此鉻酸鹽皮膜含有自我修復的能力是因為皮膜為水合物，
如果皮膜在較高溫中維持一段時間，即可改善漆料在皮膜上的附著性。 
研究顯示鎂合金經過鉻酸鹽處理後，在鹽霧測試的抗腐蝕性有明顯改善，然而
此皮膜太薄不適合當最後的被覆，以抵抗外面環境的腐蝕。根據文獻鉻酸鹽皮膜的
抗腐蝕特性，主要是 Cr(OH)3生成物，提高鉻酸鹽的濃度就可以提高緻密層 Cr(OH)3
的量，即可改善皮膜抗腐蝕的能力。在鉻酸鹽溶液中加入 Zn+2，亦可以增加 Cr(OH)3
的量。 
 
磷酸鹽化成處理 
磷酸鹽的表面處理法主要運用於鋼鐵與鋁合金，作為塗裝前的前處理，近年來
著眼於磷酸鹽法經濟成本低廉與處理手續簡便考量，運用磷酸鹽皮膜處理作為取代
鉻酸鹽化成處理的方案，引起眾多學者致力於研究皮膜的抗蝕性及改善方法。磷酸
鹽具有三個鹽基，因此磷酸鹽可以分成三類：第一磷酸鹽(磷酸二氫鹽)，具有水溶
性；第二磷酸鹽(磷酸氫鹽)及第三磷酸鹽(磷酸鹽)，不具水溶性，磷酸鹽皮膜處理即
屬於不具水溶性。 
Lowle 所發表之磷酸鹽化成皮膜處理，底材所溶出之鎂離子會與磷酸根離子發
生反應，生成 Mg(H2PO4)2、MgHPO4、和 Mg3(PO4)2。AZ91D 及 WE43A 經磷酸鹽/
錳酸鹽化成處理之後，形成片狀均勻披覆於基材之膜，並且可利用 pH 值的調整，
改變 H2PO4與 HPO42-離子濃度，改變不可溶磷酸鹽皮膜之沉積速率。處理過後之磷
酸鹽皮膜相對於鎂底材雖有較佳之抗蝕性，與塗料的附著性也較鎂底材與塗料之附
著性好，但仍較鉻酸鹽皮膜之抗蝕性與附著性差，且對鎂合金底材之成份也較為敏
感。容易因底材成份變動，對皮膜的顏色以及附著性有極大的影響。過錳酸鉀具有
多價及較強的氧化特性，常被添加到非鉻系化成處理液中作為氧化劑。Hawke 等人
利用 NH4H2PO4 及 KMnO4,H3PO4 之水溶液，進行 AM60B、AZ91D 化成處理，指
液，溶液中的三架鈰離子氧化成為四價的鈰離子，所添加的過氧化氫在試片表面還原，並
且提升表面 pH 值增加化成皮膜的沉積速率，所沉積的氫氧化物皮膜，當取出至於大氣中
時，皮膜會發生氧化，隨之脫水皮膜產生裂紋。但針對鎂合金稀土金屬化成處理之機構問
題，仍然未能有相關之探討，稀土金屬之化成處理若能夠廣泛的應用在鎂合金上，首先必
須釐清皮膜的生長機構，方能助於後續的應用發展。

四.結果與討論 
對於 AZ31 鎂合金而言，釩酸鹽化成處理所得皮膜其抗蝕性優於鈰酸鹽與磷酸鹽/錳酸
鹽化成處理所得皮膜。釩酸鹽化成處理可以成功地提升 AZ31 鎂合金的抗蝕能力。 增加化
成液中釩酸鈉添加量可明顯提升皮膜抗蝕能力，而以添加 30g/l 釩酸鈉配製而成的化成液
所得化成皮膜抗蝕能力最佳。提升化成液溫度可增加皮膜生長的速率，但對於皮膜抗蝕能
力未見明顯的改變。實驗結果顯示在化成溫度 25℃，釩酸鈉濃度為 30 g/l 的化成液中處理
10 分鐘所得皮膜抗蝕性最佳。此化成液 pH 值為 8.0。釩酸鹽化成皮膜的厚度隨著化成時間
的增加而增加，然而過長的處理時間造成皮膜抗蝕能力變差。 
從釩酸鈉濃度為 30 g/l 的化成液中所得皮膜厚度均勻，但反應速率較慢，選用醋酸調
整 pH 值後所得皮膜有較佳的成膜性，皮膜抗蝕效果較佳，而在化成液 pH 值為 5 時所得皮
膜有最好的抗蝕性表現，反應速度增加造成皮膜厚度大幅增加，但厚度不均勻且因附著性
不良的關係，皮膜剝落的現象是其運用上的缺點。釩酸鈉濃度為 30 g/l 的化成液中處理時
間較短時，所得皮膜為單層的鬆散層結構，隨著處理時間增加，皮膜緻密化造成結構變成
雙層結構，包含與底材相鄰的緻密層與鬆散層，且緻密層釩的含量較鬆散層高，是提升皮
膜抗蝕性的主要結構。將化成液利用醋酸調整 pH 後，反應速率大幅上升，但皮膜結構較
為鬆散，並可見大量氫氣泡殘留的痕跡。皮膜結構隨著處理時間增加由單層的鬆散層轉變
成緻密層與鬆散層的雙層結構，但其緻密化程度遠不及從 pH=8 的化成液中所得皮膜。 
隨著化成時間的增加，化成皮膜厚度逐漸增厚，而腐蝕總阻抗在化成處理時間 10 分
鐘達到最佳狀態，隨之下降，顯示皮膜厚度增加對抗蝕性並無絕對性之影響。從電化學量
測結果可知，釩酸鹽化成處理系統以化成處理時間 10 分鐘所得皮膜具有最佳抗蝕能力。化
成皮膜結構初始為單層結構，隨著化成處理時間增加，皮膜緻密化後變成雙層結構，但皮
膜組成均為氫氧化鎂和氫氧化釩。圖 為含釩酸鈉 30g/l，pH=5 之化成液皮膜厚度量測圖，
當化成處理開始時，皮膜厚度迅速增厚，當時間增加到 1 分鐘後，皮膜增厚速率減緩，由
此可知皮膜是藉由擴散機制成長，當膜厚達到一定厚度後，擴散反應的路徑增加造成皮膜
生成速率減緩。整體而言由皮膜中偵測到釩的平均含量，亦發現皮膜與化成處理時間有關。

釩酸鹽化成皮膜老化機制 
鎂合金表層 Mg(OH)2在含有 S2O62-，Cl-，SO42-等特定離子溶液中會加速腐蝕，
因此我們嘗試將抗蝕性較佳的化成皮膜浸置於含有 0.1% NaCl 之水溶液中，以電化
學即時監控方式記錄混合電位(OCP)變化，觀察皮膜與氯離子反應後老化的現象，
並將浸漬於 0.1% 氯化鈉水溶液中 2.5 及 5 小時後之化成皮膜以電化學腐蝕交流阻
抗實驗評估其耐蝕能力變化，並配合 X 光光電子質譜儀(XPS)分析釩元素 2p 軌域訊
號峰在能譜圖之位移情況，以確認浸漬後之化成膜是否有新的釩化合物生成。

Green Process of Conversion Treatment on Magnesium Alloys 
 
Kuang-Hsuan Yang* 
 
Department of Chemical and Materials Engineering, Vanung University 
*Email: khy@msa.vnu.edu.tw
Abstract 
We first investigated AZ31 magnesium alloys treating 
with a vanadium bath, and the results indicate that the 
vanadium conversion coating that formed on the plate 
did provide good corrosion resistance. Various 
experimental parameters, including vanadium 
concentration, solution temperature, immersion time, 
and pH value, were explored to study the influence of 
this layer on the corrosion resistance of the conversion 
coatings. Electrochemical performances were evaluated 
by DC polarization and electrochemical impedance 
spectrum (EIS). The structural differences were probed 
by X-ray diffraction (XRD), and microstructural 
observations were carried out by scanning electron 
microscopy (SEM). The coating with best corrosion 
resistance formed in the solution containing 30gl-1 
vanadate for 10 min at 25℃. Reducing the solution pH 
value by adding acetic acid glacial will promote the 
growth of the coating by means of enhanced Mg2+ 
dissolution of the anodic reaction, and better corrosion 
resistance can be obtained from the bath containing 
NaVO3 with a concentration of 30 gl-1 for 1 minute at pH 
5. The main composition of the vanadium coatings are 
Mg(OH)2 and VO(OH)2. 
 
Keywords: Chemical conversion coating; Vanadate; 
Magnesium alloys; EIS measurement; Corrosion 
resistance

1 Introduction 
Chemical conversion coatings are widely used on various 
active engineering alloys to improve paint adhesion and 
corrosion protection[1]. With the increasing demands for 
corrosion resistance on magnesium from industry, the 
conversion bath containing hexavalent chromium ions has 
appeared as the most effective and convenient method to 
protect the surfaces of magnesium alloys[1,2]. However, 
due to the high toxicity of the ion and environmental 
concerns, many researchers have proposed trivalent 
chromium as a replacement for it. Bellezze et al. reported 
that the corrosion behavior of the trivalent passive coating 
with sealing treatment is comparable to that of the 
hexavalent passive coating coatings without sealing[3]. 
The new modification method will no longer be 
economical to use during manufacturing processes due to 
factors such as the higher conversion bath temperature 
(>50℃), longer immersion time, heating after treating, 
ventilation, and greater number of rinsing steps[4]. Most 
importantly, there is still some hexavalent chromium 
retained in the coating layer and solutions, and this will be 
forbidden by law in the future. Therefore, new generation 
technical solutions that are environmentally friendly need 
to be investigated immediately. 
Vanadate coating is generally used as a base coating for 
further surface modification in aluminum and its alloys[5]. 
We have shown that homogeneous, non-powdery and 
uniform coatings can be achieved from AZ61 magnesium 
alloys in a previous study[6]. Because the chemical 
composition of the matrix is as critical as the phase 
constituents to the corrosion performance of an alloy, we 
chose AZ31 magnesium alloys to reduce the β phase 
influence because it plays a role as microcathode in the 
magnesium alloy[7-9].

3.2 Effects of solution temperature 
The polarizing curves were measured in a solution 
containing 0.1wt% NaCl solution. Figure 3 shows the 
anodic and cathodic polarization curves of the samples 
processed by immersion in a bath for 5 min. The corrosion 
potential is about -1.25 VSCE in bare AZ31, but it is slightly 
lower in the samples immersed in a vanadium bath with a 
different solution temperature. That is, the samples 
immersed in a bath show a passive state in the anodic curve, 
with larger anodic polarization than bare AZ31, indicating 
that it has a superior corrosion resistance. Although no 
clear passive state appears in the specimen immersed in a 
bath with a different solution temperature, it has a 
corrosion resistance higher than that of bare AZ31, with a 
large anodic polarization. The corresponding SEM images 
of the surface conversion coating are shown in Figure 4. It 
is clear that the crack density decreases as immersion 
temperature increases. This indicates that the higher 
solution temperature will increase the growth rate and the 
width of the crack.

3.4 Effects of pH 
Without any addition of accelerator in the bath, it is not 
economical for a chemical conversion process. According 
to the Pourbaix diagram of Mg, increasing the growth rate 
is the pH of the treatment solution. As the pH is lowered 
from this point, we make a choice about what kind of acid 
to add into the solution. Four kinds of acid were employed 
to adjust the pH value of the bath: phosphoric acid, sulfuric 
acid, hydrochloric acid and acetic acid glacial. Effects of 
pH value and immersion time for the vanadium treatment 
were studied, and the results are shown in Table 1. In Table 
1, the seven grades referring to the performance are 
assigned according to the specimen immersed in the bath 
containing NaVO3 30gl-1(pH=8.0) at 25 ℃  for 10min 
(Resistance 12.5~13.5 Kohm/cm2). Hence, the results show 
the bath pH should be adjusted to 5 by acetic acid glacial 
during the process. As the sample is immersed into the bath, 
the phenomenon is different from in an alkaline (pH=8) 
environment because the film forms and dissolves in the 
acid environment and will induce the corrosion potential 
variation until the growth and dissolve rates achieve a state 
of balance. Figure 10 shows Nyquist plots for different 
immersion times. The maximum charge transfer resistance 
is about 8.9 kΩcm-2 with the immersion time of 1 min and 
is lower than the corrosion resistance of the coating formed 
in the alkaline environment. The coating formed in the 
alkaline environment will have a greater density because 
the growth rate is relatively slow. Figure 11 illustrates the 
phase components of AZ31 magnesium alloys after 
treatment in the solution. It is clear that the chemical 
conversion coating shows several peaks corresponding to 
Mg, Mg(OH)2 and VO(OH)2.
