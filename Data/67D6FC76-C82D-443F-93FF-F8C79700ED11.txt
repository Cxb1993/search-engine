一、摘要 
  「發光二極體背光模組之研製」係為一年
期之完整計畫，主要為研製以發光二極體當
背光源之背光模組。本計畫針對發光二極體
背光模組作探討與研究，就發光二極體穩態
與暫態特性、發光二極體串並聯方式、定電
壓與定電流驅動特性與多模組均流機制做
研究與探討。液晶平面電視與傳統映像管電
視最大的不同在於重量輕、體積小、低耗
電、無輻射等特性。然而液晶平面顯示器由
於本身不是自發光，必須靠背光模組（back 
light）產生光源，為維持一定的高亮度，大
尺寸的液晶平面電視所需的背光模組數亦
隨之增加。然而，傳統使用冷陰極管當背光
源，再經由三原色濾光片達成影像顯示方
式，不僅光源利用率低，且冷陰極管工作電
壓高，對系統的干擾與電氣隔離亦是一大考
驗。隨著高亮度發光二極體技術進步，在發
光效率不斷提昇，而且發光二極體壽命較冷
陰極管長的特性，發光二極體逐漸應用於液
晶背光源，掃描式三原色發光二極體驅動觀
念被提出，可省去三原色濾光片，增加光源
利用率，使發光二極體應用於液晶顯示器更
邁進一步。本計畫發光二極體背光模組採用
三原色發光二極體，探討發光二極體掃瞄驅
動架構，針對發光二極體串並聯的可靠度與
穩定度做分析比較，同時探討輝度與驅動電
流的關係，設計 LED背光模組滿足液晶背光
源之基本規範。 
 
關鍵詞：發光二極體、背光模組 
 
Abstract 
The purpose of this project is to design 
and development a LED back-light for LCD 
TV. LCD flat display market is one of the 
fastest growing markets today. The desired 
feature of an LCD-TV set is in large diameter. 
In such a large size of LCD panel, multiple 
CCFLs are required to provide sufficient back 
lighting. Recently, due to improvement in 
luminous efficiency, long life and wider color 
gamut, LED has gradually substituted for 
CCFL as back light .Although, LED back-light 
driving systems have been developed and 
introduced into the market, further reduction 
of power consumption, reliability and 
availability improvement, and overall system 
cost reduction are still strongly demanded, so 
as they can be broadly accepted to the market. 
In large scale of LCD panels, an LED 
back-light system needs a bunch of LED 
arrays to provide sufficient brightness, while 
reliability of the LED arrays is quite limited. 
Thus, an LED bypass circuit is usually 
required to prevent LED arrays from 
open-circuit, improving back-light reliability 
and availability. This research adopts a viable 
bypass circuit to the designed driving system. 
In addition to brightness, chromaticity is 
another important issue of LCD-TV. In this 
research, we discuss the relationship between

之輝度，而且各並聯的 LED串列之驅動電流

sharing)技術以達到以上需求。最後設計符合
液晶平面顯示器的所需之 LED背光模組。 
3.重要性 
前台灣在 TFT-LCD 面板市場佔有率已
超越日本，以量產能力，台灣與韓國已逐漸
擺脫和日本三分天下的局面，成為台灣與韓
國兩雄爭霸。由於國內已發展至 7.5 代廠，
奇美、廣輝、中華映管、友達…等公司均對
LCD TV 產品的開發投入相當的人力與物
力，而目前以 CCFL 為背光模組之發展為主
流，然而 LED的特性不斷改良，將有助於提
升 LCD 產品之色彩品質與使用壽命等特
性。國內背光模組廠商在筆記型電腦及 LCD 
監視器產品以累積相當的量產經驗，在設計
開發上具有相當技術基礎，相信在切入開發
LCD TV 用背光模組應有相當助力，然而若
缺乏自我研發技術的生產模式下，廠商的利
潤其實相當有限。仰賴國外研發技術轉移，
對國內相關產業研究開發相當不利，因此唯
有積極研發以及儘速的培養電力電子相關的
研發人才，才是當務之急。 
    LCD TV產品，由 2002年 264萬台的產
量倍數成長到 537萬台，年成長率達逐年上
升，其發展可從短、中、長期的發展來分析，
廠商逐漸對 20～40吋家用電視市場的銷售
進行規劃，如能在家用電視佔有一席之地
後，將逐漸擴大銷售面，配合六、七代廠產
能的陸續量產，且克服良率及系統整合問題
後，再佈局 40吋以上的數位家電視訊產品，
TTF-LCD可直接挑戰大尺寸 PDP TV產品在
市場的區隔界線，同時因數位視訊環境的建
構與普及化，將使大型化 LCD產品的利基市
場更加完備。目前韓國與日本，均已將 LED
背光模組商品化發展，台灣廠商目前正急起
直追，唯有積極研發發展高功率 LED技術，
並克服 LED間差異性的瓶頸，設計合適 LED
驅動電路，提升 LED背光模組的整體效率與
可靠度，早日獲得國內外背光模組廠的認證
及採用，方可在未來 LCD TV市場大餅中爭
的一席之地。 
三、文獻探討 
隨著液晶顯示器製造技術的提升，大尺
寸及低價格的趨勢下，背光模組在考量輕量
化、薄型化、低消費電力、高亮度及降低成
本的市場要求，為保持在未來市場的競爭
力，開發、設計新型的背光模組及射出成型
的新製作技術，是未來努力的方向。近年來
為因市場的需求，許多國外大廠如 
National、linear、 Micro Linear、Analog 等
均陸續開發 LED 背光電源的專屬控制 IC，
國外相關的研究，除在建立新的電路模式之
外，並朝開發、設計新型 LED背光模組等方
向前進，廠商目前有中強光電、瑞儀、大億、
輔祥、力信、全漢、達方等廠商投入背光模
組及與週邊相關產品發展。在國內學術界包
括台大陳秋麟教授、清大潘清財教授及廖聰
明教授、成大陳建富教授及梁從主教授、中
正吳財福教授、中山莫清賢教授、台科大謝
冠群教授、雲科大華志強教授、彰師大陳財
榮教授…..等多位教授陸續投入相關之專題
研究計畫或建教合作。但是目前發表之相關
論文數量並不多，所以仍有相當大的發展空
間，除此之外，大尺寸 LED的背光模組投入
研究的人亦較少，因此開發大尺寸 LED之背
光模組來因應大尺寸 LCD TV 的發展，也是
目前可以深入研究探討的課題。

技術/創作名稱 低功率損失且低成本之發光二極體串列旁通電路 
發明人/創作人 沈志隆 
中文： 
本創作提出一發光二極體(LED)串列旁通電路，當串列
中任一 LED 發生斷路故障時，提供了一條電流導通的旁
路，藉由所提電路，能維持其餘 LED之正常點亮，串列
不因個別之 LED故障而整組丟棄不用。所提旁通電路主要
係由一開關電路與一觸發電路所組成，因此，架構簡單，此
外具有功率損耗低、製造成本少、可嵌入 LED製程、並可
輕易地檢測出故障 LED位置等優點。 
技術說明 英文： 
The objective of this innovation is to design a bypass 
circuit for LED string. Once any single failure at an LED 
arrays occurs, the proposed bypass circuit will conduct to 
prevent the LED system from shutdown. With the bypass 
circuit, the reliability and availability of an LED system is 
promoted significantly. The circuit mainly consists of an 
active-switch circuit and a trigger so that the circuitry is simple 
and cost-effective. In addition, the bypass circuit can be 
embedded into an LED package and detect the failed one 
easily, which widens its application.

Abstract—In this paper a three-phase three-wire (3φ3W) 
half-bridge single-stage grid-connection PV power inverter is 
proposed. The inverter system can not only transfer solar 
power into ac power to supply loads or/and to feed utility but 
eliminates current harmonics and improves power factor. As 
compared with conventional PV inverter, the total number of 
active switches and current sensors in the power stage can be 
reduced so that its cost is lowered significantly. For control-
ling, the direct-source-current-shaping (DSCS) algorithm 
and the linear-approximation method for maxi-
mum-power-point tracking (MPPT) are applied to determine 
optimal current command to avoid complicated calculation. 
Simulation results and practical measurements demonstrate 
the feasibility of the proposed single-stage grid-connection 
inverter system. 
Index Terms—Grid connection, PV power inverter, cur-
rent harmonics, power factor. 
 
I. INTRODUCTION 
   Solar energy, a kind of renewable resources, is 
clean pollution-free and inexhaustible. Developing solar 
energy supply systems is a key to solving the energy crisis 
of exhausting in fossil fuel. Recently, photovoltaic arrays 
are widely used to supply loads [1]-[7]. PV inverter can be 
briefly classified into stand-alone system and 
grid-connection system. Owing to more flexibility in 
power conditioning, the study on the grid-connection type 
of PV inverter stimulates many interests. 
A block diagram showing the configuration of a con-
ventional grid-connection PV inverter system is shown in 
Fig. 1, which consists of multiple stages, leading to low 
efficiency, large volume and high cost. To improve part of 
the disadvantages mentioned above, some researchers 
have designed two-stage configurations, as shown in Fig. 
2. To further improve efficiency and reduce cost, the sin-
gle-stage structure has been developed [8]-[9]. The struc-
ture’s diagram is shown in Fig. 3. Even though the PV 
inverter has been simplified, a couple of active switches 
and current sensors are still needed in the power stage. 
To reduce the total number of active switches and cur-
rent sensors, a 3φ3W half-bridge single-stage PV power 
inverter system is proposed in this paper. The inverter 
system can not only transfer solar energy into ac power 
but improves power factor, eliminates harmonic currents, 
and lowers cost. For controlling, a di-
rect-source-current-shaping (DSCS) algorithm is applied 
to determine current commands, which avoids coordinate 
transformations and complicated calculations for the de-
termination of inverter-current commands. To draw the 
maximum power from PV arrays, a linear-approximation 
method is developed to complete maximum-power-point 
tracking (MPPT). As a result, the proposed PV inverter 
system is compact and cost can be reduced significantly.

II. CONFIGURATION OF THE PROPOSED INVERTER

single-stage grid-connection PV inverter system, which 
consists of a dc-bus filter, a half-bridge inverter, an output 
filter, and a system controller. The 3φ3W half-bridge in-
verter, which contains four active switches and two 
dc-voltage-dividing capacitors, can perform current har-
monics eliminating, improve power factor, and feed PV 
power into utility lines. The dc-bus filter suppresses 
dc-link voltage fluctuations and filters out ac components 
on the dc side for accurate MPPT, while the output filter

V. CONCLUSIONS 
A three-phase three-wire half-bridge single-stage 
grid-connection PV power inverter is presented in this 
paper. In the power stage, the total number of active 
switches can be reduced by two so that the system con-
figuration is simplified and the cost is lowered signifi-
cantly. The nonlinear programming method is applied to 
derive the optimal current commands. For controlling, the 
direct-source-current- shaping algorithm and the MPPT 
linear approximation method is proposed to determine 
current commands to avoid complicated calculations. 
Simulation results and hardware measurements have 
demonstrated the feasibility of the proposed inverter.
