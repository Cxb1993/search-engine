與螢光光譜等變化。最後也使用此高分子微胞作為癌症藥物的奈米載具，以探討其在控制藥物釋放的

Abstract： 
The utilization of polymeric micelles self-assembled from amphiphilic block copolymers as nanocarriers 
for hydrophobic biomaterials is of great interest, and its applications in bioimaging, biosensing, and cancer 
therapy have attracted great attention recently. However, the current studies of polymeric micelles for 
controlled release or target delivery have been focused on either active targeting using receptor-mediated 
endocytosis or passive targeting with stimuli-responsive micelles. It has seldom been investigated by 
combining both active and passive mechanisms in a single micelle system.  
In this study, we have synthesized a series of amphiphilic block copolymers successfully by atom 
transfer radical polymerization, and functional groups with specific characteristics, such as 
aggregation-induced emission, photo-cleavable moieties and thermo-responsive moieties, have been 
introduced into either the hydrophobic or hydrophilic block on the copolymers. The polymeric micelles 
self-assembled from these block copolymers have been prepared and investigated to correlate the effects of 
polymer structure and functional groups on the nanostructure and photophysical properties of these micelles. 
Finally, these micelles have been utilized as nanocarriers for cancer drugs, and their applications on the 
controlled release are investigated. 
 
Keywords：amphiphilic block copolymer, polymeric micelle, nanocarrier, controlled release

1. 雙親性嵌段共聚高分子聚合與化學結構鑑定

四、結論與建議： 
在本研究中使用含 Aggregation-Induced Emission (AIE) 特性的單體---PPS-HEMA 以及光敏性單體
NBMA 做 出 功 能 性 雙 親 性 嵌 段 共 聚 高 分 子 ： PEG-b-[PS-co-P(PPS-HEMA)] 及
PEG-b-[PNBMA-co-P(PPS-HEMA)] 並且使之在水溶液中自組裝形成微胞而發出螢光。此外，
PPS-HEMA 與藥物 Doxorubicin 會發生 FRET 效應，可作為藥物包覆的指標，表示藥物是否成功被包
入微胞核心。而使用 UV 光照射則可以誘發微胞崩解達到加速藥物釋放的目的。未來將進行細胞毒性
與 in vitro 的實驗，測試此藥物載體的實用性。
