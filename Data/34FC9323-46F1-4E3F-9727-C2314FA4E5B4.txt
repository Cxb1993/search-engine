RIN expression. The device parameters of differential 
gain and nonlinear gain compression factor are 
therefore extracted. The estimated maximum 3dB

analysis. Yet the linear, instead of proportional, 
dependence of resonance frequency versus square root 
of incremental current is observed and discussed for 
chirpily stacked QD lasers. 
For uniformly stacked quantum dot laser with GS 
lasing emissions of 1.3 μm, the K-factor limited 
bandwidth is 13 GHz. The extracted differential gain 
and gain compression factor are 1.7E-15 cm2 and 2E-16 
cm3, respectively. For chirpily stacked quantum dot 
laser with ES lasing emissions of 1.2 μm, the K-
factor limited bandwidth is 14 GHz. Yet the non-
proportional dependence between resonance frequency 
and square root of incremental current yields 
differential gain of 4.3E-15 cm2 and huge gain 
compression factor of 1.4E-14 cm3.

一、 中文摘要 
關鍵詞：相對雜訊強度、微分增益、增益壓縮
因子、量子點雷射 
半導體雷射的調變特性通常藉由量測小
訊號的頻率響應來評估；另一種方式是分析其
光輸出的相對雜訊強度的頻譜，而後者較不受
寄生效應的影響。量子點雷射之相對雜訊頻譜
的量測雖然散佈在少數的論文中，但對其頻譜
的分析卻幾乎找不到，這是由於量子點的響應
受到高度阻尼，使得其雜訊位準相當低，因而
量測上就顯得有點棘手。 
在本計劃中，我們量測了均勻堆疊量子點
基態（GS）發光及啁啾堆疊量子點激態（ES）
發光之相對雜訊強度的頻譜，藉由擬合其理論
公式來分離出調變頻寬、共振頻率及衰減因子
等動態特性，從而萃取其微分增益和增益壓縮
係數等元件參數，此種量測方法所推估的3dB
頻寬與與小訊號頻率響應所獲得的結果相當
一致。然而在啁啾堆疊量子點的量測與分析
上，我們發現共振頻率對於增量電流的平方根
呈現線性而非正比的關係。 
對於均勻堆疊量子點，基態發光波長在1.3 
μm波段，其K-factor的限制頻寬高達13 GHz，
而其微分增益和增益壓縮係數分別為1.7x10-15 
cm2 與 2x10-16 cm3。至於啁啾堆疊量子點，激
態發光波長在1.2 μm波段，其K-factor的限制頻
寬可達14 GHz、微分增益為4.3x10-15 cm2，但
增益壓縮係數因線性而非正比的關係而呈現 
1.4x10-14 cm3此一相當大的量值。 
二、 英文摘要 
Keywords: Relative Intensity Noise, Differential 
Gain, Gain Compression, Quantum Dot Lasers 
The modulation characteristics of 
semiconductor lasers are mostly studied by 
small-signal modulation (SSM) response. 
Another method to evaluate the direct 
modulation capabilities is by relative intensity 
noise (RIN) measurement, which is parasitic-free. 
The RIN measurements of quantum dot (QD) 
laser were scattered in recent publications, 
however, analysis of RIN spectra can rarely be 
found. This is attributed to the highly damped 
modulation response that leads to very low RIN 
levels which are critical in the measurement. 
In this work, the RIN spectra of uniformly 
and chirpily stacked QD lasers are measured for 
ground-state (GS) and excited-state (ES) lasing 
emissions, respectively. The dynamic properties 
of modulation bandwidth, resonance frequency 
and damping coefficient are resolved by fitting 
the theoretical RIN expression. The device 
parameters of differential gain and nonlinear 
gain compression factor are therefore extracted. 
The estimated maximum 3dB bandwidths are 
consistent with those from SSM analysis. Yet the 
linear, instead of proportional, dependence of 
resonance frequency versus square root of 
incremental current is observed and discussed 
for chirpily stacked QD lasers.

5. Conclusion  
In this study, we have characterized as-prepared and aged InGaN LEDs fabricated from two epitaxial wafers that are 
without noticeable difference. The noise spectral density is analyzed in two frequency ranges. The exponent in the 
current dependence of low-frequency 1/f noise amplitude and the corner frequency in the high-frequency g-r noise 
spectra are two possible indicators for reliability estimation. As a diagnosis tool, they are further confirmed by 
comparison with burn-in samples as well as commercial red LEDs.
