行政院國家科學委員會專題研究計畫成果報告 
具燒結式孔隙表面之多通道對流沸騰式電腦散熱器研究 
計畫主持人：簡良翰 
計畫參與人員：劉子銘、裴善永、廖文榮、王宏仁、李書哲 
 
一、中英文摘要 
本計劃以燒結式多孔隙表面沸騰研究為基礎，將燒結式孔隙表面應用在晶片散熱用
上，研究具燒結式孔隙表面的熱管或強制對流的多通道散熱器。研究的方法可分兩部分：
一、研究水力直徑約2mm之多渠道散熱器之管內對流沸騰熱傳、壓損性能及臨界熱通量
(CHF)之實驗；二、以具燒結孔隙表面的熱管實驗，研究其蒸發熱阻抗。實驗結果發現：在
純水中，兩種渠道都僅在最低流量時有氣泡產生；燒結表面因為增加流體擾動而可提升渠
道內之熱對流係數約兩倍。而在FC-72中，因為管內有沸騰現象，燒結表面可提升渠道內之
熱對流係數為光滑渠道的三至四倍。純水在光滑渠道的對流熱傳係數較FC-72高，但純水與
FC-72在燒結表面渠道的熱傳係數則相近。燒結式熱管熱傳性能實驗中，以純水50%充填量
時熱傳性能較佳；在甲醇25%充填量時的熱傳性能次之。 
 
關鍵詞: 對流沸騰、多孔隙表面、小管徑、平板式熱管、熱傳性能。 
 
 The present project will investigate the multi-channel heat sink having internal sintered 
particle coating, based on the previous sintered porous surface boiling study of the present 
author.  The research methods include: (1) experimental tests of heat transfer performance and 
pressure drop through the multi-channels having 1 mm by 2 mm channels; and (2) Heat transfer 
experiment of a flat heat pipe having sintered porous capillary structure.  The experimental 
results show that the porous structures enhanced the heat transfer coefficient in multi-channels 
by a factor of two for water, where boiling in the channel occurs only in the lowest flow rate.  
For FC-72, the ratio of heat transfer enhancement is greater (3~4 folds) because the boiling 
effect is the dominant heat transfer mechanism.  The flat heat pipe experiments showed that the 
50% charge of water in the heat pipe yields the best thermal performance, and the 25% charge 
of methanol results in the second best performance. 
Keywords: Multi-channel, small diameter, flow boiling, heat sink, flat heat pipe. 
 
二、緣由與目的 
電路集積程度的提高則使得熱更集中。因此，當前的電子元件正朝向高散熱功率、高
熱通量的方向發展。根據 ITRS(International Technology Roadmap for Semiconductors)的預
估，晶片熱通量將於西元 2010 年超過 70 W/cm2。熱管式散熱器已成為其散熱系統中不可
 4
的表面的沸騰熱傳性能最佳，約為光滑表面的十倍；此表面之臨界熱通量在測試範圍
（500kW/m2）內尚未出現。 
有關小管徑之單相與雙相熱對流，早期多半以冷凍與製程之板式及管式熱交換器為應
用，工作介質多為冷媒及水，如 Lazarek and Black (1982)、 Wangsganss et al. (1993)以 R113
測試管徑 3.15mm 及 2.92mm 之單管，測試 R-113。半導體工業的進步使電子散熱之應用
受重視，這類研究也朝向更小尺寸的方形管道發展，Bower and Mudawar (1994)以 R134a
測試管徑分別為 2.54mm 及 510µm 的管內對流沸騰，發現管徑 2.54mm 的管子的壓損
(0.01bar)小於 510µm(0.31bar)，且較不易阻塞。Warrier et al. (2002)以 FC84 測試水力直徑
0.75mm 的方形管。Ammerman and You (1998)以 FC87 測試方形管道。Qu and Mudawar 
(2003a)以去離子水測試 21 道 231 × 713 µm 平行通道，並歸納文獻中有關小管徑之雙相熱
對流實驗數據，發現文獻中九個經典的兩相管內對流經驗公式都不能有效計算微小管徑熱
對流實驗數據，主要因素是因為微小管子內環狀流的液滴較容易被氣體帶至下游，形成可
觀的 entrainment flow；而 Qu and Mudawar (2003b)則提出涵蓋此現象的新模型。 
在管內蒸發對流中包含了沸騰與對流兩種熱傳機制，在小管徑內增加微鰭片可增加對
流熱傳係數，但其壓損也相對增加，對於原已有壓損過大的小管子而言顯然並非理想的方
法；而 Ammerman and You (2001)則利用 O’Connor and You (1995b)發展出的微孔隙
(microporous)沸騰表面的製作技術，將粒徑 8~12µm 之工業鑽石粉末黏附於 2 × 2mm 小管
內壁，能增強其沸騰效應，因而增強多通道冷卻器熱傳性能一倍而壓降僅小幅增加。
Ammerman and You (2001)的研究以電腦散熱為考量，其多孔材質有非導電性之特點，但
其熱傳增強之幅度卻較傳統之金屬顆粒燒結而成的多孔表面相對的小了許多，例如 Ikeuchi 
et al. (1984) 以 0.115mm 之銅粒燒結於 17.05mm 的管內，而其在 R22 的對流沸騰性能為
光滑管五倍。 
近年來國內對於微小管之製作與管內對流的研究也不少，陳信男(盧定昶指導，2000)
以實驗方法探討 FC-72 於內徑為 4.35 ㎜及 3.17 ㎜之水平光滑圓管內，飽和壓力在 1.3 大
氣壓時之蒸發熱傳係數與兩相流動壓降特性，並藉由拍攝流譜的方式來探討兩相蒸發的熱
傳機制。林建順（楊建裕指導，2000）以實驗方法分析冷媒 R-134a 與潤滑油混合後，在
管徑 1.7 至 6.4mm 圓管內之絕熱壓降、凝結及蒸發熱傳性能比較。類似研究由劉亦堂（楊
建裕指導，2002）延續，測試 1.1mm 及 0.8mm 圓管。涂昆仁(杜文謙、康尚文指導，2001)，”
微管流之實驗分析”以去離子水為工作流體，在水力直徑為 153-508µm 的不同尺寸下進行
強制對流沸騰測試測試。潘欽及其學生 Lee et al. (2004) 、(馮士豪，2003）、Li et al. (2004), 
利用微機電技術於單晶片上蝕平行梯型矽質微通道，水力直徑約為 41.3 和 33.5µm，用除
氣後的去離子水為工作流體，透過高速攝影機探討微通道的沸騰熱傳與雙相流現象，包括
氣泡成長機制、沸騰熱傳、雙相流動形態與雙相流不穩定現象。 
 綜合前述各項文獻，筆者認為孔隙表面的高沸騰熱傳係數應可有效提升多通道冷卻器
的熱傳性能，而且以金屬材質之多通道冷卻器應採用燒結式孔隙表面，以得到比 Ammerman 
and You (2001)的微孔隙多通道冷卻器更高的熱傳性能；而通道的尺寸則不宜過小，以免壓
降過大。

四、結果與討論 
 
4.1 多通道管內對流沸騰實驗結果 
 本研究以純水及 FC-72 兩種流體進行測試，測試溫度介於 30~90oC。純水在光滑渠道
的測試流量介於 1.3 ~ 15.8 g/s，結果如圖七。由於水的沸點較高，除了在最小流量 (1.3 g/s) 
時在出口觀察到氣泡之外，都是在單相液態下進行，因此其對流熱傳係數(h)與熱通量
(82~289 kW/m2)沒有太大關係，而與 Re 數呈正比關係。純水在具燒結式多孔表面渠與光滑
渠道的 h 對 Re 呈現相似趨勢，但具多孔隙表面渠道的熱對流係數（如圖八）大約高出約一
倍；這可能是因為燒結表面粗糙度較高，使流體擾動增加而可提升渠道內之熱對流。 
FC-72 在光滑渠道與多孔隙表面渠道的測試結果分別如圖九、十。圖九與圖七之純水
數據相比，在低流量與低熱通量時 FC-72 及純水的熱傳係數相近，但在高流量或 Re 數時，
純水的 h 較高，兩者差異約可達兩倍，這可能是因為 FC-72 的熱傳導係數比水小所致。由
於 FC-72 的沸點比水低，在大部分的測試範圍中，都可觀察到氣泡大量由出口端冒出；而
圖十之實驗數據顯示熱通量(q)對於 FC-72 的熱對流係數影響比較大，；而流量的影響反而
較小，可見沸騰的效應高過強制對流。相較於光滑渠道，具燒結表面渠道的熱傳係數(h)約
是光滑渠道的三到四倍，而且燒結表面的 h 大致與熱通量 q 成正比關係（如圖十），可見燒
結表面確實比光滑表面容易產生氣泡。

一、 參加會議經過 
   
  國際熱傳會議(International Heat Transfer conference)是全球熱傳學界最盛大的學術會議，
自 1962 年開始每四年舉辦一次；由 AIHTC(Assembly for International Heat Transfer conference)
籌畫，會員國包括澳洲、巴西、加拿大、中國、法國、德國、印度、以色列、義大利、日本、
韓國、葡萄牙、俄國、南非、瑞士、荷蘭、南非、美國等十八個國家。這一屆（第十四屆）
的大會首度在南半球的澳洲雪梨市國際會議中心舉行，大會主席為澳洲新南威爾斯大學(U. of 
New South Wales)機械系的 Graham de Vahl Davis 教授。整個研討會為期六天，共有來自全世
界六十多個國家的近八百為學者專家參加，其中有三十三個由大會邀約的專題演講(keynote

THERMAL PERFORMANCE EVALUATION OF A SPRAY TYPE 
EVAPORATOR USING ELEMENTAL NTU-ε METHOD 
 
Liang-Han Chien, Tong-Hong Shih 
Department of Air-conditioning and Refrigeration 
National Taipei University of Technology, Taipei, TAIWAN 
 
Abstract 
 
This manuscript presents a computer solution methodology and the results for predicting spray type 
refrigerant evaporator performance.  A computer code based on elemental NTU-ε method was 
developed.  A falling film evaporation model based on the superposition model was used for the 
prediction of the refrigerant side heat transfer.  The predictions of this model agree with 
experimental data of a R134a spray-type evaporator at various water temperatures and evaporator 
pressures. At a fixed condenser cooling water temperature, the evaporator performance increases as 
the saturation temperature increases.  At a fixed condenser cooling water temperature, the 
evaporator performance increased as the chilled water temperature increased.  At a fixed chilled 
water outlet temperature, the evaporator heat transfer rate reduced as the refrigerant inlet vapour 
quality increased with increasing condenser cooling water temperature.

For an evaporator, the heat transfer coefficient of the refrigerant side usually depends on the local 
vapour quality and wall superheat.  Therefore, the evaporator needs to be divided into many 
elements for an accurate thermal performance calculation.  The elemental NTU-ε methodology was 
used by Webb et al. (1989a, b) for predicting the thermal performance of a flooded type evaporator.  
Their model under-predicted full-load heat duties of four evaporators by 6 ~ 11%.  They also found 
that a two-increment model predicts the results by 43% and concluded that a hand calculation is not 
likely to yield acceptable accuracy.  Browne and Bansal (2001) also adapted NTU-ε methodology to 
predict the performance of vapour compression liquid chillers that utilise flooded evaporator and 
water-cooled condenser. 
 
The NTU-ε methodology can also be used for the prediction of spray type evaporators, but a 
different refrigerant side heat transfer model is required.  The refrigerant flow in a spray type is 
quite different from that in a flooded type evaporator.  For a flooded type evaporator, the refrigerant 
flows from the bottom of the shell side.  Contrarily, the refrigerant was introduced into the shell 
side of the evaporator from the top.  Consequently, the heat transfer mechanisms of these two types 
are quite different.  For the flooded type evaporator, both liquid and vapour refrigerant flow from 
bottom to the top as a two-phase mixture.  Convective evaporation is the dominant heat transfer 
mechanism.  However, vapour and liquid flow in counter-current directions in a spry-type 
evaporator.  The refrigerant liquid passes through a fluid distributor and falls down on the tubes, but 
the evaporated vapour flows upward through the passages between tubes.  Falling film evaporation 
is the dominant heat transfer mode on the tubes.  In the present work, a computer code was 
developed based on the elemental NTU-ε methodology using the Chien and Cheng (2006) 
correlation to predict the heat transfer capacity of a spray type evaporator.

2.4 Calculation of the Refrigerant Side Heat Transfer Coefficient 
Three possible heat transfer modes may occur in a spray type evaporator.   
1. Nucleate boiling: For the tubes submerged in the liquid refrigerant, nucleate boiling is the 
dominant heat transfer mode.   
2. Falling film evaporation: For the tubes above the liquid-vapour interface, falling film 
evaporation is the dominant heat transfer mode. 
3. Single phase convection: If the vapour quality is greater than one, the refrigerant vapour flow 
across the tubes is in single phase convection mode.

3.2 The Spray Type Evaporator 
The R-134a chiller unit having a spray type evaporator was manufactured by ITRI.  Its evaporator 
had 99 enhanced copper tubes in three bottom-top water passes.  The shell diameter is 335.6 mm, 
and the height of the liquid level was kept at 1/4 ~ 1/3 of the shell diameter.  The tubes were 19 mm 
OD, 2.4 m long, in 15 rows of staggered arrangement.  The internal fin hight = 0.4 mm, helix angle 
= 40o, fin pitch = 0.48 mm, inner root diameter of the tube was 16.38 mm.  A refrigerant distributor, 
having pores aligned with the tubes, was installed above the tube bundle.  The refrigerant 
distribution was observed at six locations as shown in Figure 3.  From the observations, we found 
that the refrigerant liquid was uniformly distributed under steady test conditions.  This chiller 
equipped with a screw type compressor (RC-15L), which was controlled at a fixed volume flow rate.  
In the present study, full load and 75% load conditions were tested.  At full load (384 m3/hr), the 
designed cooling capacity is 80 tons. 
 
4.  Results and Discussion 
 
In the present study, full load and 75% load conditions at either fixed condenser cooling water inlet 
temperature or chilled water exit temperature were calculated by the present model. Calculations 
were run for one increment per tube row (Ny = number of tube rows =15) and 32 increments over 
the entire tube length (Nx = 32).  The calculation of Q for Nx = 8 was 1.5% smaller than Nx = 32, and 
there was less than 0.25% difference in Q for Nx = 32 and 64.  The boiling surface in the present test 
is similar to the W-B surface in Wang et al. (1998) study.  Therefore, the boiling heat transfer 
coefficient of the present spray type evaporator is interpolated according to its saturation 
temperature (3 ~ 12 oC) in the test from the boiling curve-fitted values of 0oC and 20oC of Wang et 
al. (1998) data.  The refrigerant inlet quality and flow rate were calculated from the operating 
pressures measured at the condenser and evaporator assuming an ideal vapour compression 
refrigeration cycle.

摘要 
本研究探討溝槽與網格對於熱管的熱傳性
能，研究中以紅銅平板上製作間距0.55mm深0.3mm
之V字型溝槽，另以凹槽式平板加上100或120目的網
格作為毛細組織。使用純水、甲醇做為工作液體，加
熱量在10~40W之間不等。實驗結果發現本研究所用
之熱管毛細組織以溝槽加網格較純溝槽式佳；經分析
其性能與其穿透率、有效毛細半徑有關。絕大部分數
據顯示工作液體以甲醇較純水好；經本研究分析，其
主因為甲醇在網格間的接觸角較小，毛細作用較強所
致。當熱管操作為水平放置時之性能比蒸發器向下傾
斜8度佳。傾斜角度(重力)對冷凝阻抗的影響較蒸發阻
抗大。在25%，33%，50%三種充填率中甲醇以25%
最佳，純水則以50%最佳。本研究提出包含接觸角效
應之優點數修正公式，在使用網格為毛細組織時，應
依流體之接觸角進行修正，才能有效反應熱管性能。

1. 前言  
邁入3G時代，高功率電子元件的發展與半導
體製程技術的進步，促使消費性電子產品的使用，也
能夠與時尚的生活型態做結合，因而目前許多電子產
品皆走向高性能、微小化的趨勢。早在 1965 年，英
特爾 (Intel) 公司的創始人之一摩爾先生 (Gordon E. 
Moore) 即預測晶片中指令數 （電晶體個數)將呈現
每隔 18 個月以兩倍數成長的走勢。事實上，半導體
製程技術已由 1970 年約 1,000 個指令數(4004 
Processor)，進步至現今約十億個指令數(Pentium 4 
Processor)；線寬方面，則於二十一世紀初可望進入
奈米紀元。然而造成電路集積程度增高，使得熱量更
為集中，熱通量更為提高，因此散熱問題遂成為電子
產品設計的重點之一。相較於傳統的散熱裝置，熱管
(Heat Pipe)對於此種情況的散熱提供一個良好的解決
方案。熱管的發展已由管式發展至平板式，而其毛細
組織大致分為凹槽式、粉末燒結式、網格式。

4. 結論  
1、本實驗中以溝槽式加網格優於純溝槽式；與其穿
透率、有效毛細半徑有關。 
2、工作液體絕大部分以甲醇較純水好，經本研究分
析，其主因為甲醇與網格的接觸角小。 
3、在25%，33%，50%三種充填率中甲醇以25%最佳，
純水則以50%最佳，特別是在高熱通量時較明顯。 
4、當熱管操作為水平放置時之性能比蒸發器向上傾
斜8度稍佳。傾斜角度(重力)對冷凝阻抗的影響較蒸發
阻抗大。 
5、本研究提出利用優點數修正公式在使用網格為毛
細組織時，需依流體之接觸角進行修正，才能有效反
應熱管性能。 
6、隨著加熱量增加，蒸發阻抗漸減，冷凝阻抗則增
加。

THERMAL PERFORMANCE EVALUATION OF A SPRAY TYPE

Liang-Han Chien, Tong-Hong Shih 
Department of Air-conditioning and Refrigeration 
National Taipei University of Technology, Taipei, TAIWAN 
 
Abstract 
 
This manuscript presents a computer solution methodology and the results for predicting spray type 
refrigerant evaporator performance.  A computer code based on elemental NTU-ε method was 
developed.  A falling film evaporation model based on the superposition model was used for the 
prediction of the refrigerant side heat transfer.  The predictions of this model agree with 
experimental data of a R134a spray-type evaporator at various water temperatures and evaporator 
pressures. At a fixed condenser cooling water temperature, the evaporator performance increases as 
the saturation temperature increases.  At a fixed condenser cooling water temperature, the 
evaporator performance increased as the chilled water temperature increased.  At a fixed chilled 
water outlet temperature, the evaporator heat transfer rate reduced as the refrigerant inlet vapour 
quality increased with increasing condenser cooling water temperature.

For an evaporator, the heat transfer coefficient of the refrigerant side usually depends on the local 
vapour quality and wall superheat.  Therefore, the evaporator needs to be divided into many 
elements for an accurate thermal performance calculation.  The elemental NTU-ε methodology was 
used by Webb et al. (1989a, b) for predicting the thermal performance of a flooded type evaporator.  
Their model under-predicted full-load heat duties of four evaporators by 6 ~ 11%.  They also found 
that a two-increment model predicts the results by 43% and concluded that a hand calculation is not 
likely to yield acceptable accuracy.  Browne and Bansal (2001) also adapted NTU-ε methodology to 
predict the performance of vapour compression liquid chillers that utilise flooded evaporator and 
water-cooled condenser. 
 
The NTU-ε methodology can also be used for the prediction of spray type evaporators, but a 
different refrigerant side heat transfer model is required.  The refrigerant flow in a spray type is 
quite different from that in a flooded type evaporator.  For a flooded type evaporator, the refrigerant 
flows from the bottom of the shell side.  Contrarily, the refrigerant was introduced into the shell 
side of the evaporator from the top.  Consequently, the heat transfer mechanisms of these two types 
are quite different.  For the flooded type evaporator, both liquid and vapour refrigerant flow from 
bottom to the top as a two-phase mixture.  Convective evaporation is the dominant heat transfer 
mechanism.  However, vapour and liquid flow in counter-current directions in a spry-type 
evaporator.  The refrigerant liquid passes through a fluid distributor and falls down on the tubes, but 
the evaporated vapour flows upward through the passages between tubes.  Falling film evaporation 
is the dominant heat transfer mode on the tubes.  In the present work, a computer code was 
developed based on the elemental NTU-ε methodology using the Chien and Cheng (2006) 
correlation to predict the heat transfer capacity of a spray type evaporator.

2.4 Calculation of the Refrigerant Side Heat Transfer Coefficient 
Three possible heat transfer modes may occur in a spray type evaporator.   
1. Nucleate boiling: For the tubes submerged in the liquid refrigerant, nucleate boiling is the 
dominant heat transfer mode.   
2. Falling film evaporation: For the tubes above the liquid-vapour interface, falling film 
evaporation is the dominant heat transfer mode. 
3. Single phase convection: If the vapour quality is greater than one, the refrigerant vapour flow 
across the tubes is in single phase convection mode.

2.5 Calculation Procedure 
The flow chart of the computer model is shown on Figure 2.  The calculation sequence depends on 
the water passes.  For a bottom/top three water passes evaporator, the calculation procedures are: 
 
1. Read the input data including water inlet temperature (Th1), water flow rate (Wh), refrigerant 
saturation temperature (Ts) and inlet quality (xr1). 
2. Calculate the number of tubes per pass (Ntp).  Identify the corresponding water pass and number 
of tube (Ntjy) for each row. 
3. Set the initial guessed values of the total heat transfer rate Qgu, and the vapour quality (xb) of the 
bottom row above the refrigerant liquid level. 
4. Calculate the initial values of the water exit temperature, (Th2)jx,jy = (Th1-Qgu/Wh)jx,jy, for each 
element and the refrigerant mass flow rate, Wr = Qgu/[λ·(1-xr1)]. 
5. Identify the heat transfer mode of the refrigerant side and calculate the heat transfer coefficients 
of each element, starting from the water inlet of the bottom row.  The calculation methods are 
described in section 2.4.  The fluid properties are calculated by a subroutine according to the 
inlet fluid temperature.  An iterative procedure is involved for the calculation of the nucleate 
boiling heat transfer coefficient (hnb) because hnb depends on the tube wall temperature.  In the 
iteration, the new tube wall temperature (Tw’) is calculated from ho and U of the previous step as 
given by Equation (21).

3.2 The Spray Type Evaporator 
The R-134a chiller unit having a spray type evaporator was manufactured by ITRI.  Its evaporator 
had 99 enhanced copper tubes in three bottom-top water passes.  The shell diameter is 335.6 mm, 
and the height of the liquid level was kept at 1/4 ~ 1/3 of the shell diameter.  The tubes were 19 mm 
OD, 2.4 m long, in 15 rows of staggered arrangement.  The internal fin hight = 0.4 mm, helix angle 
= 40o, fin pitch = 0.48 mm, inner root diameter of the tube was 16.38 mm.  A refrigerant distributor, 
having pores aligned with the tubes, was installed above the tube bundle.  The refrigerant 
distribution was observed at six locations as shown in Figure 3.  From the observations, we found 
that the refrigerant liquid was uniformly distributed under steady test conditions.  This chiller 
equipped with a screw type compressor (RC-15L), which was controlled at a fixed volume flow rate.  
In the present study, full load and 75% load conditions were tested.  At full load (384 m3/hr), the 
designed cooling capacity is 80 tons. 
 
4.  Results and Discussion 
 
In the present study, full load and 75% load conditions at either fixed condenser cooling water inlet 
temperature or chilled water exit temperature were calculated by the present model. Calculations 
were run for one increment per tube row (Ny = number of tube rows =15) and 32 increments over 
the entire tube length (Nx = 32).  The calculation of Q for Nx = 8 was 1.5% smaller than Nx = 32, and 
there was less than 0.25% difference in Q for Nx = 32 and 64.  The boiling surface in the present test 
is similar to the W-B surface in Wang et al. (1998) study.  Therefore, the boiling heat transfer 
coefficient of the present spray type evaporator is interpolated according to its saturation 
temperature (3 ~ 12 oC) in the test from the boiling curve-fitted values of 0oC and 20oC of Wang et 
al. (1998) data.  The refrigerant inlet quality and flow rate were calculated from the operating 
pressures measured at the condenser and evaporator assuming an ideal vapour compression 
refrigeration cycle.
