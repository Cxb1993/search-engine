Abstract 
Diabetes is a common chronic disease in 
the world, and there’s a high prevalence of 
diabetics in Taiwan as well. According to the 
medical statistics released by the Health 
Department, Executive Yuan in 2008, 7.03% of 
population in the age of 15 have diabetes. On 
the other hand, the cost of the diabetic 
treatment and care can be a very heavy burden. 
Besides taking medication to control diabetics, 
measuring blood pressure and blood sugar 
regularly is the most direct way for self-health 
management of diabetic condition. 
 
The development of this research, 
“Development of the diversified healthcare 
system for diabetics”, is different from the 
blood sugar management software developed 
by other domestic manufacturers of blood 
glucose meter. Besides the basic function of 
blood sugar management software and home 
telehealth system, we’ve been planning three 
different kinds of applications and two kinds of 
operating platforms which support wired and 
wireless transmission to provide a more 
completed home telehealth system and achieve 
different diabetics’ needs. Cooperating with 
BIONIME has helped us to apply the 
development of the diversified healthcare 
system for diabetics on BIONIME diabetic care 
management system, so that the results of this 
study can really be fulfilled. 
Keyword: Diabetes, Blood Glucose 
Management, Home Telehealth System, 
Distributed Data Server

本研究開發完成一「多元化糖尿病照護管理系統」，有別於國內外之血糖管理軟
體，除了擁有血糖管理軟體的基本功能（如資料分析與繪圖、報表列印等）與
遠距居家照護所需通訊核心功能外，並加入了下列多元化元素：(1)適合於單機
式、應用伺服器式和集中資料庫式等三種不同的服務應用類型，能符合不同使
用者多元之服務需求；(2)設計專用機及安裝於個人電腦的軟體版等兩種不同的
操作平台，提供電腦操作能力不同使用者所需；(3)支援有線與無線的血糖機傳
輸，符合未來醫療器材無線傳輸之發展趨勢。本系統提供多元的使用情境，滿
足不同糖尿病族群的使用需求，及更完整的遠距居家照護服務，讓使用者可以
依照自身的需求與電腦操作能力，選擇不同的照護類型、操作平台與血糖機，
讓遠距照護服務能夠更貼近使用者的需求。本研究與國內華廣生技股份有限公
司合作，成果已實際成為該公司生產血糖機所搭配的糖尿病健康管理系統。本
研究中所提出的功能定義與介面設計等，皆是與華廣生技所共同討論出來的結
果，依據其多年來的病患回饋經驗、醫師建議與自身廠商看法，提出非常多實
務性要求，讓本研究開發出來的系統更接近產品面。
