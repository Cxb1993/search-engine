In this project, the structuralized modelling of 
power flow analysis of complex compound 
vibration isolation systems, which is composed of 
single layer and multiple layer resilient mounts, is 
planning to establish. Based on the object-oriented 
viewpoint of power flow transmission path, the 
solution algorithms of the vibration power flow of 
complex resilient mount systems are discussed and 
studied. Meanwhile, in the structuralized modeling 
the non-linear damping will take into consideration. 
In which the nonlinear damping parameters have 
been characterized by using the result from the 
previous research project of NSC entitled 
“Modelling of Vibration Analysis of Hysteresis 
Damping System”. Thus, it is also intended to 
discuss the bifurcation and chaotic instability 
phenomenon in the nonlinear vibration transmission 
via compound isolation systems, and furthermore 
this nonlinear phenomenon can be used to assess 
the isolation stability of the compound isolators. 
Keywords: vibration isolation, power flow, 
object-oriented, transmission matrix, structuralized 
modeling
