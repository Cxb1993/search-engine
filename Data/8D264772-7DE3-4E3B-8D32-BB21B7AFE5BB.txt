1.Abstract
We describe a facile route for the
room temperature synthesis of poly
(amic acid)-zinc oxide (PAA-ZnO)
raspberry like composite particles were
prepared by heterocoagulation method.
The poly(amic acid) can be used as host
particle to modify ZnO optical proper-
ties. Theresulting materials were
characterized by below instrument. The
morphology of composite particles was
examined with a scanning electron
microscopy (SEM),transmission electron
microscope(TEM), The crystallization
structure of samples was characterized
with a multi-functionalities and differ-
ent-Thermo System X-ray Diffracto-
meter. The composite particles surface
element analysis by energy-dispersive
X-ray spectroscopy (EDX). The optical
spectra were measured using a
UV-visible spectrophotometer. The
optical properties of the poly(amic
acid)-ZnO core-shell composite particles
was found large redshift as compared
to the pure ZnO nanoparticles. The
effects of ZnO coating on the poly(amic
acid) particles structure, morphology
and optical absorption were also inves-
tigated.

本研究計畫製備聚醯胺酸/氧化鋅奈米複合顆粒做為光觸媒使用。先以分散聚合法製得單
一散度次微米級聚醯胺酸顆粒當作粒種，再加入氧化鋅前驅物將聚醯胺酸顆粒表面改質，
於 
聚醯胺酸顆粒表面形成氧化鋅殼層；氧化鋅前驅物含有氫氧基會與聚醯胺酸的羧酸基反
應， 
最後製備出具光觸媒活性的聚醯胺酸/氧化鋅複合顆粒， 於光學性質檢測發現複合顆粒的
吸 
收波長朝可見光方向移動跟單一氧化鋅顆粒吸收波長做比較，吸收波長越長更可使用低能
量 
能源去催化觸媒，在觸媒學術成就上提供了新的觸媒材料於技術創新上提供了新的方法去
合 
成有機無機複合材料，在工業上如能降低光觸媒光催化能量更可減低能源消耗並降低碳排
放 
量和生產成本使工業朝零污染方向邁進並為地球軟暖化盡一分心力，依照現有文獻所提供

移 
動，使本研究有了進一步發展的可能性。
