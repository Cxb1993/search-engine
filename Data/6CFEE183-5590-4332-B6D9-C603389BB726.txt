摘要 
 
本研究利用不銹鋼為基材，在表面以不同的
方式進行加工，產生不同的微結構，改變光線的
行徑方向，並以光譜的全/漫反射率為指標。另一
方面，利用物理氣相沉積法沉積薄膜，探討薄膜
的附著力。採用的加工方式有電化學加工與微噴
砂兩種方法，直接在不銹鋼基板上加工出不規則
織質微結構，探討不同加工參數下，產生不同的
粗糙度，量測粗糙度與光譜之間的關係。 
由光譜結果可得知，兩種加工後所形成的表
面織質結構，其漫反射率與原始的漫反射率相
比，約提升 10%~40%，經鍍銀後的漫反射率提
升 30%~80%。另一方面，蝕刻氧化鋅薄膜過後
所產生的織質結構的，其漫反射率比未蝕刻前提
升 10%~20%。 
在附著力測試部份，是以 ASTM-D3359 的規
範進行測試，利用鈦薄膜當不銹鋼基板與銀薄膜
的黏著層，能有效提高附著力，測試結果由 0B
提升至 5B。氧化鋅薄膜部份，沉積薄膜時利用
高溫製程，提供良好的附著力，其附著力測試結
果為 5B。 
 
關鍵字：光捕捉、織質結構、全反射率、漫反射
率、粗糙度 
 
ABSTRACT 
 
In this research, stainless steel is used for the 
substrate; the surface is processed by different 
methods to create different texture in order to alter 
the direction of the light. The total/diffuse 
reflectance within the spectrum is set to be the 
benchmark values. At the same time, Physical vapor 
deposition (PVD) is utilized to deposit films, and 
further to discuss their adhesion. The processing 
methods include Electrochemical Machining (ECM) 
and sand blasting to directly process on the stainless 
steel surfaces. Within this study, irregular texture is 
discussed. Experiments and researches were 
conducted to demonstrate the causal effects 
between the resulted roughness and different 
process variables, further discussion is also 
provided to show the relationship between the 
roughness and spectrum. 
From the spectrum test result, formed by two 
kinds of processed surface texture, the diffuse 
reflectance and that compared to the original diffuse 
reflectance is about 10% ~ 40% increased. The 
total/diffuse reflectance are both increased 
30%~80% after the silver coated. On the other hand, 
the diffuse reflectance of texture which etched of 
zinc oxide thin films is about 10%~20% increased 
than those without etching. 
Speaking about the adhesion test, 
ASTM-D3359 standards were followed to complete 
this assessment.  Ti film is used for the adhesive 
section between the stainless steel and the silver 
film which is able to strengthen the adhesion from 
0B to 5B according to the ASTM-D3359. Regard to 
the zinc oxide (ZnO) film adhesion test, it shows 
that by performing the process under high 
temperature will help to achieve better adhesive 
ability with a result of 5B in the test. 
 
Keywords: Light trapping, Texture Total reflectance, 
Diffused reflectance, Roughness.

3.3 鍍層附著力檢測 
附著力測試方面，本研究採用 ASTM D3359
附著力測試規範中的膠帶附著力測試法，測試薄
膜與不銹鋼基板的附著力，表五為測試後之結
果。由表中可知，銀膜與不銹鋼之附著力測試結
果為 0B，若利用鈦薄膜當銀薄膜與不銹鋼的黏
著層，附著力提升至 5B。另一方面，本研究利
用射頻濺鍍機沉積氧化鋅薄膜，製程中設定腔體
溫度為 200℃，在高溫下氧化鋅薄膜結構較緻密
與附著力提升，故測試氧化鋅薄膜與不銹鋼基板
的附著力結果為 5B。
