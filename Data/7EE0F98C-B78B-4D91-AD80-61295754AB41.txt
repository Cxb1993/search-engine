2中文摘要
關鍵字：協同設計、資訊分享平台、知識管理、知識語意網、知識數位化
台灣產業在經過數十年的政府與人民不斷的耕耘與努力，其經濟繁榮與科技
進步為世人所稱道，台灣製造不僅是科技的象徵，也是高品質的代表，然而臺灣
腹地無法像歐、美日等擁有廣大內銷與品牌市場，必須依靠外銷的方式來獲取生
存的優勢，面對全球化企業的盛行，大部份企業將製造基地移轉到成本結構最佳
化的區域，並積極全球布局以期望在下一世紀中成為佼佼者，所以台商西進與全
球分工已成為不可以擋的趨勢。
在大陸生產基地不斷量產後，為了當地內銷與在地化，採用當地幹部就地管
理已經是必然的結果，加上大陸等地子公司迅速成長與臺灣經濟衰退，許多臺灣
母公司竟然被大陸子公司反併吞，這樣的問題變成一個當出高階主管預料不到的
問題，如何在海外子公司成長之際迫使臺灣母公司產業升級成為一個創新與卓越
的企業，已經是一個刻不容緩的問題。
針對以上產業變革，本研究提出以協同設計資訊分享平台架構為基礎，在全
球化、西進大陸的過程之中，透過兩案三地的協同設計的運籌模式，把全球化人
才的設計知識，透過設計流程與知識數位化同步的概念，透過知識語意網來整合
全球化人才的設計資源，並同步完整的儲存與管理設計、製造、設計流程與知識
工程，其目地在於大量分享、平衡全球性設計人員的專屬性與不可取代性，讓服
務導向的設計資源融入企業文化之中。

6經導入了許多制式化的管理制度與開發控管方法與文件化制度，但是對於產品設
計公司，由於整個產業非常強調設計創意與設計過程並不強迫訴諸文件化指引的
方式(如軟體開發都已具備系統規格書與程式規格書等正式文件)，所以直到產品
設計完成後或該設計人員主管的過程參與等要素，其他設計人員非常難以了解該
設計人員的設計所有過程與思緒，導致了以下結果：
1. 人員調任它職，繼任人員需花費大量時間方可接替工作
2. 知識無法長存與企業之中
3. 人員離異，新進人員培訓需花大量成本
4. 新進人員接替前人工作，無法銜接的非常有效率
5. 所有人員的交接替換，其交接期需要非常長，甚至前人已離職多時，接
替人員仍無法掌握其前人的工作，造成資源斷層的品質低谷期
如果在設計專案進展的過流程中，可以將設計人員的知識與技術，同步轉化
成企業知識庫之中。隨著更多設計人員的知識分享，可以提昇技術水準並且讓企
業對人員異動的損失降低到最少，對於全球化的設計人員互動與輪調及異動，試
圖提出一個解決可行的方案。
在全球化產業佈局的趨勢下，台商西進乃是不可擋的必然結果，但是台灣的
科技人才是否在經濟、政治、文化等全球變局的強大壓迫下，能透完成蛻變的期
望結果嗎?這對於台灣未來人才的走向，將扮演著一個很重要的角色，
所以設計知識的外顯、儲存、散佈、內化、再利用的過程必須與設計過程整
合，這樣的需求，必須整合全球設計整合的平台，並且可以達到專案的控管機制
與設計流程的整合方是一個企業在全球化整合中，研發的能量不會被大陸人才所
攻佔的較佳防衛。然而標準化的過程之中，設計圖樣的標準化、流程的整合、設
計再利用的原則都是當前所必須考量的議題。
所以台灣產業的升級，研發人員不再是敝掃自珍，必須整合大陸設計研發人
才，進行協同設計與開發，並且整合資訊科技與知識管理的資訊能量，將設計知
識的獨特性透過全球設計人員的互動與參予來累積設計知識，並透過本體論
(Ontology)與語意網(Semantic)的方式來儲存設計知識，並利用人工智慧的方式
與機器學習技術，希望可以更進一步的設計出設計輔助系統，來達到輔助基礎或
重複性的設計知識與工作，讓台灣的設計人員可以有更多的資源可以將自己的資
源集中於創新的設計與前導性的研發。

8面對未來的短暫的設計時程與多變的設計問題，本研究運用在 2005 健康與
管理學術研討會所提出的『視覺元件庫為基礎之設計方法以加快設計概念提案：
以比雅久機車為例(A Study on Design Proposal of PGO Scooter based on
Database of Design Components)』來提升快速設計的方法論，並在比雅久機車
設計部門得到階段性的實證，然而設計研發人員在面對環保的設計議題，本研究
也在 2005 健康與管理學術研討會發表的『以視覺元件庫為基礎之綠色設計方法
以降低設計資源之耗用：以比雅久機車為例(A Study on the Methodology of
Green Design Based on Visual Components to Reduce the Waste of the Design
Resource for PGO Scooters)』來整合綠色供應練的規範來解決這個設計問題。
本研究團隊為了讓研究落實於企業設計部門之中，也為了讓研究可以以實際
的資料進行研究，於第一屆資訊管理學術暨專案管理實務研討會先行進行前導性
的研究，發表『企業導入以設計知識為基礎的專案型知識管理初探：以祥記實業
股份有限公司為例(A Study on Project Management Based on Knowledge
Management for Designers -A Case Study of Gallant Industries Co., Ltd )』
對袋包產業的特性與當前設計問題進行多方面的實務探討，讓整個研究可以在理
論框架上與實務上的應用可以得到雙贏的效益。
本研究團隊基於以上的先前的研究基礎，希望對於目前兩案三地所面對的全
球性的協同設計問題，來進行一個可以整合跨領域與跨企業的設計研發協同設計
平台的設計與開發，並透過這個平台希望能夠整合不同文化、語言、設計背景、
社會環境等等將大型設計研發的產品設計，可以整合起來，並可以透過整個平台
可以讓客戶、設計研發人員、設計研發主管、企業主管、股東與協同廠商在同一
個平台上得到整合的優勢。

研究方法及進行步驟
本研究會將整個研究時程區分為三個階段性的時程，並將每一階段性的主要
研究議題列舉如下：
第一階段: 延伸設計元件庫為樣式元件知識庫，並設計臺灣母企業協同設計
專案平台，並以袋包產業的龍頭-祥記企業設計部門導入設計平台來實證整個研
究。
第二階段: 將臺灣母企業協同設計專案平台為基礎，延伸到其印尼廠與大陸
廠，進行全球協同設計管理，並導入虛擬團隊控管的機制，並將設計過程用多媒
體、設計知識元件、設計意涵與設計流程等構面進行設計知識內涵的知識平台，
並將之整合進入協同設計輔助功能的專屬功能。
第三階段: 透過前兩年的設計案例進行知識關聯分析，並將知識元件的內涵
關聯透過語意網的分析，建立自動化設計知識的機制，以建立設計知識元件為基
礎的設計輔助支援系統.

解決動態路邊停車資訊即時存取裝置之研究
A Study on Instant Information Retrieving Device for the
Dynamic Road Parking Quota Information Problems
曹永忠 1*、薛新光 2、許智誠 1、蔡英德 2
1國立中央大學資訊管理學系
tyc6095@ms1.hinet.net, khsu@mgt.ncu.edu.tw
2靜宜大學資訊傳播工程學系

摘要
在台灣的大都會區，政府會設置便民使用的公有停車位，其中於道路旁邊所
劃設的停車格，其停車記錄收費的方式，則是透過檢查人員巡迴檢視，將最新的
停車費資訊填報在停車費用單內，再將單據置於停車者的車窗上。然而，由於人
力控管停車收費的品質維持不易，便常會有停車位未被準確輸入收費資訊、駕駛
人無法立即知道停車空位出現位置等情形發生，這些便成為道路停車位動態資訊
存取的關鍵問題。
商務人士駕車工作洽公、個人或全家開車出遊至餐廳用餐時，並非所有的公
司或商店皆可以提供足夠停車空間，故至路邊停車成為主要停車方式，但為找停
車位，駕駛人常繞著目的地周圍道路碰運氣直到找到停車位為止；如果可以動態
即時獲知可用道路停車位，快速找出最佳化路徑達到路邊停車的目的，將相對提
高行車油費與時間的使用效率。
本研究希望基於原有使用者行為與商業模式下，找出一個解決方案來滿足主
管機關常面臨停車收費問題，例如無法即時獲知停車空位、停車不開單就無法收
費、逾時停車無續開停車記錄等。透過衛星導航裝置使用，可以自動查詢停車位
資訊，運用動態路邊停車資訊，將能使駕駛人以最佳的路徑找到停車位，達到路
邊停車資訊即時存取的目的。
關鍵字： 路邊停車、動態路邊停車資訊即時存取、衛星導航裝置、停車位資訊

國外停車制度
在羅兆廷(民 89)碩士論文相關研究中，提到英國、法國、澳洲三國路邊停車
使用的方法：
(1).英國
在英國及其他歐洲各地，大量使用停車顯示(PAY-AND-DISPLAY )停車收費
計時器，此裝置是一種可以被 10~100 個停車位共用的停車收費計時器，包含功
能如下：IC 卡的讀卡機、IC 卡轉帳付費、列印停車時間證明單、列印停車收據。
(2).法國
在法國則使用一種個人停車計時器(PIAF)，其使用預付式 IC 智慧卡，特點
是停車時車主將預付式 IC 智慧卡插入其個人停車計時器內計時器才開始計時，
此計時器及依停車的時間單位逐次扣減智慧卡中的預付餘額，並在顯示版上顯示
卡內餘額，警方可以用 PIAF 號碼及私密驗證邏輯(private key)密碼，得知此停車
計時器是否在使用中，並正確地扣減停車費。自 1991 年 11 月起，巴黎開始將投
幣式停車計時器改為智慧卡讀卡機式計時器。
(3).澳洲
澳洲墨爾北市利用 IC 智慧卡作為停車計費工具。當車輛駛入停車場入口
時，會得到一張 IC 智慧卡停車證，且已記錄進入時問和停車場入口，車輛離開
時在停車場出口處將 IC 智慧卡插入讀卡機中，讀卡機即根據停車時間計算出停
車費進行收費。

A Exploratory Study on Cognitive Gap Model Based on Extension of TAM 
Applied in Conceptual Design among Designers, Purchasers & Users

Abstract 
The design processes for the developers usually confuse the purposes of the product development 
due to the unique characteristics of the developers' creative, the experiences & the subjectivity. 
Therefore, the selling to the customers always focuses on the problems including the overpricing of the 
overloading functions, the ambiguous requirements, etc. The enterprises will enhance the kernel power 
to grant the dept. of R&D & Product Development, some special privileges for the developers with the 
subjective viewpoint were required strongly and caused many products unable to fit the exact & 
internal consumers' needs. 
The study focus the ambiguous consumers' requirements in view of the marketing to distinguish the 
differences for the designers, the marketing, the consumers, the customers, the purchasers & the users 
including the thinking and the internal & external needs, each roles from the conceptual design to the 
selling have the individual benefit & constraint. Each role must consider the personal & optimal benefit 
to stand on their standpoints to confuse the real purchasing motive by the appearance, the functions, the 
pricing, the reuse, the marketing. The study uses the three designs – the electronic photo display 
products from the three viewpoint including the users, the potential users, the purchasers, the marketing 
as the cases to explain the Roles Cognitive Gap and try to propose the Cognitive Gap Model (CGM) 
Based on the Extension of Technology acceptance model: TAM applied in the conceptual design 
among the designers, the purchasers & the users to fit the research’s issues. 
The Cognitive Gap Model suggests the new approach to help the related roles for the products 
creation to measure the key factors of the product design and to change each constructs' weights to 
support some quantitative information as the guideline of the product design. 
Keywords: Cognitive Gap Model: CGM, Technology Acceptance Model: TAM, Electronic Photo 
Display, Product Development

This study uses two patterns of the photo-frame product

The second case is a pattern –‘Assemblage photo frame

based on reusable components for cost redundancy prompted 
by marketing-managers. The high ratio of marketing share 
depends on available pricing, so mass productions & economic 
scale productions are the keys to the goal. The best practices of 
standard & component-based assembly seems a better solution.  
 
Fig 6  The design based on reusable components 
The standard & component-based assembly Design (shown 
in Fig 7) is a new solution of whole production of digital 
frame because of reusable & component-based form for mass 
productions of the standard parts. The kernel unit is a firmware 
based on System on Chip (SoC) infrastructure by parametric 
software design and polymorphic software engineering theory. 
The mass productions based on the standard & 
component-based parts can optimize the economic-scale parts 
productions and simplify the processes of assembly. 
 
Fig 7 The standard & component-based assembly design 
The IT derivative designs usually use the central process 
unit (CPU) architecture as the kernel hardware combined 
with software as firmware. The functions of digital photo 
frame products rely on software embed in hardware, so 
different functions and product type may use the same 
software turned by internal parameters control. The changes 
of software usually change themselves without changes of 
hardware and version control & upgrade is easy by upgrade 
the firmware. Therefore, the kernel cost of products’ 
functions & values not only rely on firmware but also the 
forms of products manufactured by mass productions via 
standard & component-based assembly design (shown in 
Fig 8). The cost-related problems of forms’ parts & 
manufacture are the critical issues.

4.1 The Issues of Cognitive Gaps Problems 
The cases-study session propose uses two patterns of the 
photo-frame product design to derive the cognitive gaps’ 
issues by the thinking & the processes of the products design. 
The study finds some issues according the approaches 
described in cases-study session listing below: 
1. The price problems cause the consumers to consider the 
C/P (cost over performance) problems & benefits from the 
behaviors of the second case. 
2. The pricing problems affect the usefulness of the products. 
3. The cost of using products for users concerns the users’ 
satisfactions. 
4. The purchasers care the benefits of the users’ using 
products result for attitude to purchase intention & 
behavior. 
5. The users’ internal demands are the critical factors, which 
the purchasers concern with. 
6. The consumers are the composition of users & purchasers 
with different aspects. 
7. The purchasers’ internal desire form the using products 
feedback of the users are the potential motivation of 
attitude toward purchase the products. 
8. The values of the stakeholders for the relationships of all 
roles involved the products from designs to repeated 
purchasing. 
9. The technology may be a novel methodology to create the 
original idea to satisfy the new problems for the 
purchasers to motivation of attitude toward purchase the 
products. 
10. The pricing problems need the economic scale of mass 
productions faced with short product lifecycle problems in 
current environment, so the optimized parts problems are 
the critical factors. 
11. The daily & necessary needs for users are the core factors 
for purchasing, especially for something impossible 
without the products. 
12. The creative & novel attractions may be the first-eyes for 
consumers’ stimulus.  
The approaches mentioned above propose two constructs 
including values & usefulness for the stakeholders. In 
advanced, the issues affect the attitude toward purchase and 
behavior intention to purchase the real products. Finally, the 
actual products use & repeats the products make the real goal 
for the marketing-managers concerned with the survivability 
of the enterprises. 
 
4.2 The Proposed Cognitive Gap Model 
The proposed cognitive gaps issues extended from TAM 
constructs the hypothesis of the cognitive gaps model 
according the issues in session 4.1. The perceived usefulness 
replace the perceived ease of use in TAM with functions 
approaches for the basic foundation. The perceived value 
replace the usefulness in TAM with innovations approaches 
for the advanced considerations of the consumers. The two 
constructs have direct relationship between both and both have 
direct relationships to attitude toward purchase instead of 
attitude toward using. The attitude toward purchase will trigger 
the keys of the doors for behavioral intention to purchase due 
to the changed attitudes and closely to the motivation of the 
purchasing. 
In a view of marketing-managers, may think the missions 
finished after purchasing. But too many cases happened for no 
actual use & continuous use after the purchasing products are 
the real & critical factors for success of the products 
developments. For such issues, the relationship of attitude 
toward purchase to actual product use must be added the 
hypothesis model for validation of the products usefulness to 
the users’ accurate needs. So the construct of the actual 
product use add and measure the relationships of attitude 
toward purchase to actual product use as the mechanism of the 
feedback of model. The hypothesis of cognitive gap model

of the using products. The views turn into the usefulness and

conclusions are focused on the cognitions’ differences for all 
roles in aspects of the attitude, the behaviors of the real

For the reasons mentioned above, the study finds the

 2. Literature survey 
2.1 Technology Acceptance Model 
The researches about consumers’ cognitive behaviors in 
marketing field proposed on previous researches included the 
Theory of Reasoned Action: TRA (Ajzen, 1985). The results 
of the Theory of Planned Behavior: TPB, (Ajzen and 
Fishbein,1980) . Davis proposed a new approach derived to 
Technology Acceptance Model: TAM(Davis, 1989) for 
pointing the result of behavior intention influenced by 
consumers’ attitude. Many literature survey proceed many 
comparisons and discussions for these theories of TRA, TPB 
& TAM, valid the explanation of all by a way of empirical 
studies.  
The Davis (Davis,1989) proposed the Technology 
Acceptance Model :TAM (shown in Fig 1) on the discusses of 
technology acceptance criteria for users’ attitude & behaviors 
with comparisons of TRA & TAM theoretical models in the 
dissertation. The results by empirical studied validate the more 
complete explanation of the behavior intention than TRA.  
Although the results of Davis research can’t judge the 
discrimination of two models by the trial experimentation 
results for TAM, but the discrimination of the attitude & 
behavior intention with better explanations for TAM had 
proposed finally.  
For the reason mentioned above, Ajzen (Ajzen,1985) 
proposes Theory of Planned Behavior: TPB to enhance & 
modify the Theory of Reasoned Action: TRA. In advance, 
Mathieson (Mathieson,1991) & Taylor (Taylor and Todd,1995) 
compare with TPB & TAM in a view of attitude & behavior 
intention of information technology and prove the better 
explanations of TAM than TPB by the empirical studies. 
Mathieson’ researches support the better explanations of 
attitude & behavior intention of information technology, but 
the results can’t judge the excellence between all due to the 
scenery, time, objects & space different. The researchers agree 
the sound of all models, but which model fits the researches 
problems according to the characteristics of researches 
problems finally. 
Taylor (Taylor and Todd,1995) used empirical study for 
TAM & TPB to prove the differences of results of Mathieson’ 
researches (Mathieson, 199l)) and found the constructs about 
cognitive behavior control to enhance better explanation of 
attitude than TAM. For such results, Taylor and Todd propose 
that two constructs about objective norm & cognitive behavior 
control influenced behavior intention, in opposition to lack of 
two constructs for TAM. 
According to Literature survey for TRA, TPB & TAM 
arguments, the study wishes to apply the TAM as a foundation 
theory, because the study includes consumers’ behavior, 
cognitive behaviors, IT derived products and & IT 
characteristics concerned with each other.  
 
2.3.2 Introduction of Technology Acceptance Model  
Davis proposed the technology acceptance model based on 
TRA (shown in Fig 1) in his dissertation (Davis,1989) to 
discuss the influenced issues of  Perceived usefulness & 
Perceived ease of use for users of new information technology. 
TAM had haven the ability of the efficient prediction and 
evaluation for IS users’ behaviors including extensive 
explanations of IS acceptance behaviors like Email, enterprise 
applications. WWW, Web applications, Online game, etc 
(Dishaw and Strong, 1999; Karahanna and Straub, 1999; 
Lederer et al, 2000; Lin and Lu, 2000; Hsu and Lu, 2004)。 
by the thinking & the processes of the products design. The 
designers always think consumers as the key factors, but the 
differences of the users and the purchasers usually confuse the 
design-goals because of purchasing behaviors. Due to the 
ambiguous behaviors of uses & purchases as the satisfactions 
to the purchase fool the designers & marketing-managers in a 
way of tricky thinking like the functions & pricing to purchase 
or satisfactions to purchase, etc. In fact, the differences are the 
mutual-causal relationships. So the two cases describe the 
users’ oriented approaches and functional approaches by the 
users’ internal values instead of multi-functions and pricing 
problems. The main goals are to derive the constructs & the 
relationships among marketing-managers, users, purchasers, 
designers, external factors, etc. This study will extend the 
technology acceptance model (TAM) by using these constructs 
& relationships to construct a proposed Cognitive Gap Model 
(CGM) to evaluate the production designs’ core.

5.2 Implications 
Although the hypothesis of cognitive gap model are 
needed the questionnaire to valid the model robust and 
accuracy as validation of the model, but the empirical study 
is effective for measuring the weight of different aspects of 
each roles in products development & lifecycle 
management ( PDM & PLM). The important implications 
in the study are listed as following. 
1. The consumers’ roles may be the composition of 
purchasers & users. 
2. The value of purchasers & users is the key toward the 
attitude. 
3. The usefulness of the products is the basic desires of the 
purchasing motivation not only the original idea or 
creative but also the implicit internal needs of the users. 
4. The implicit internal needs of the users may be the 
intensive motivation and purchasing desire in aspect of the 
purchasers’ values. Because the behaviors of purchasing 
are the real powers of the products usefulness (Scott 
Weiss, 2002). 
5. The price of products is the barriers of the purchasing 
behaviors due to the consideration of C/P (cost over 
performance). 
6. The values of the consumers may be changed by novelty, 
creativeness, forms especially technical original for the 
engineers of IT related workers. 
7. The usefulness of the products are not ended after the 
purchasing behaviors affected by intention, because of no 
real use and repeated purchasing to feedback the 
validation of products success. 
8. The external environment factors can be condensed into 
two aspects including functions & innovations as the 
consideration of measuring the values & usefulness. 
The implications are the critical factors for the products 
development & lifecycle management ( PDM & PLM) for 
manufactures & developers to consider and measure the 
effectiveness of the whole products designs and selling.
