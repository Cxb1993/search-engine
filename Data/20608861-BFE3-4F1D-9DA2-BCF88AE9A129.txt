研究計畫英文摘要 
Tissue engineering and/or regenerative medicine has seen an increasing interest over the 
last years and great expectations have risen within the general public to its potential 
clinical application in the treatment of damaged functionaltissues-organs. However, in spite 
of the scientific advancements, applications to the patients is still very limited and it 
appears that to optimize the strategy for the tissue engineering in the clinical view, 
researchers have to strive for a new level of innovation which will bring together the main 
pillars of tissue engineering: namely (1) microsurgical manipulation, (2) cell and tissue 
transplantation, (3) material science of appropriate scaffold, and (4) gene or growth factors 
transfer. Thus, an innovative strategies able to provide extra support to the proper 
re-establishment of functional needs are warranted before a successful functional repair can 
be achieved. 
Advances in molecular biology have provided the tools necessary for the design of 
gene-based strategies for therapeutic and pharmacological purpose. Genetic engineering has 
emerged as a promising method to address the need for sustained and robust cellular 
differentiation (viz., stem cells) and extracellular matrix protein production. As a result, 
gene therapy has been established as a promising approach to enhance cellular activities for 
tissue repair and regeneration. The success of therapeutic and tracer targeting effect is 
most importantly depending on the development of the delivery carrier/vehicle. In out 
institute, we demonstrated that hydroxyapatite (Hap) is suitable as a basal material candidate 
for gene and protein delivery carrier development. 
Nanoparticles (NPs) of synthetic Hap will be rendered magnetic by treatment with iron 
ions using a wet-chemical process. Magnetic nanoparticles oriented target-delivery of hTERT 
related gene sets to somatic cells at the appropriate niches that favor the proliferation 
of uni-potent stem cells to cartilage. In this project, we try to develop an innovative method 
to deliver hTERT gene to somatic cells and then to identify and characterize of the properties 
of both the cartilage and somatic cells niche. The possibility and likelihood of different 
somatic cells involvement in tissue engineering will be elucidated.The translation of these 
new concepts into effective therapies will represent an outstanding challenge for 
regenerative medicine over the next decades. 
 
Key Word: somatic stem cells, Cartilage, gene transfection, magnetic nanoparticles, 
functional tissue engineering

進行編程轉化的過程，產生更具體有效的治療價值。

III. 於細胞先處理細胞外基質(ECM)再進行磁性轉染(Magnetofection)

胞之細胞外基質(ECM)對於基因轉染有很大的影響，必須解決細胞外基質(ECM)之影響，方能
提升軟骨細胞之轉染率；另外，我們也可以得到利用磁性氫氧基磷灰石奈米顆粒(mHAP)磁性
