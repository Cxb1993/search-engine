象。另一個方法則是採用一普通鏡片的攝影
機再加上一個特殊的鏡面(mirror) [6]，但此
方法需透過相當的數學推導來建立此鏡面
模形，其建構方法較為複雜。 
相較於採用單一攝影機來產生廣角視
訊的方法，植基於多攝影機設定是一常被採
用的產生廣角視訊的方法。廣角視訊中的每
一個影格(frame)可以利用適當的影像接圖
技術或影像拼湊(mosaics)技術來建立，然後
再對這些影格進行合併即可獲得所要的視
訊。 
本研究首先提出了一個可擴展性的多
攝影機模組，利用此攝影機模組可先將多視
訊流(video streams)擷取出來；接著再利用攝
影機的校正、透視投影轉換、影像扭曲、影
像縫合及影像調和等技術來獲得所需的廣
角視訊中的影格，最後再將這些影格進行合
併來得到所需的廣角視訊。其中，影格縫合
程序中，我們是先找出相鄰影像的重疊區域
後，再對重疊區域中尋找其轉折點(corner)
作為比對特徵點，然後再針對這些特徵點找
出其相對應對(corresponding pairs)。接著，
利用這些相對應對，透過 8-parameter model 
of perspective transformation [7]來求算出相
鄰影像的 homography transformation，則相
鄰影像便可以成功的進行縫合；緊接著再利
用我們所提出的兩階段影像調和方法[8]來
獲得無接縫的影格。因此，利用本研究所提
出的廣角視訊產生方法，可順利獲得無接縫
的視訊。

Creating Wide Field-of-View Video using Multicamera 
Wu-Chih Hu*, Chin-Hao Chang, Ya-Fang Liang, and Chen-Tsung Wu 
*Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, 
National Penghu University, Taiwan, ROC
wchu@npu.edu.tw
Abstract 
A panoramic video is a wide field-of-view (FOV) 
video, up to a full view of 360 degrees. In this paper, 
the model of wide FOV video is proposed based on 
images which are captured from three cameras 
without specially designed lens. The proposed model of 
wide FOV video can be up to a full view of 360 
degrees. Experimental results demonstrate that the 
proposed method for creating wide FOV video has 
good performance. 
1. Introduction 
In multimedia, video has popular and potential 
applications under capturing device and vast storage 
that are becoming cheaper and more popular. A 
panoramic video is a wide field-of-view (FOV) video, 
up to a full view of 360 degrees. Panoramic images of 
scenes in the panoramic video have interesting 
applications in computer vision because they contain 
full information about the scene from a given 
viewpoint, e.g. image-based virtual reality (VR) [1], 
panoramic image matching [2], content recognition [3], 
etc.
Panoramic views can be obtained by using a wide 
FOV lens, e.g. a fish-eye [4] or panoramic lens [5], but, 
the cost of specially designed lens is very high and the 
obtained panoramic images have distortions and loss 
of quality. Hence, they are drawback for applications 
in computer vision. Using video sequences from single 
camera is a solution for obtaining panoramic images [3, 
6]. Panoramic video is dynamic; scenes and objects in 
the video are synchronous, so methods for obtaining 
static panoramic images [3, 6] are not suitable for 
creating panoramic video. 
 In this paper, the model of wide FOV video is 
proposed to obtain the wide FOV video. The wide 
FOV video is created based on images which are 
captured from three cameras without specially 
designed lens, as shown in Fig. 1. The frames of the 
wide FOV video are generated by using image 
warping, stitching, and blending, as shown in Fig. 2. 
Furthermore, the wide FOV video is obtained by using 
these frames under seriating. Besides, the proposed 
model of wide FOV video can be up to a full view of 
360 degrees.

一、參加會議經過
本國際會議是第二屆舉辦，論文集則是由 IEEE Computer Society 出版，為 EI
刊物。本會議共計收到超過 1000篇論文投稿，只有 624篇論文被接受發表(論文錄
取率約六成左右)，共分成 54場次公開進行論文口頭報告發表。大會另安排兩場次
的 Plenary Speech，分別為 Innovative Computing for Homecares (Speaker: Prof. Harold
Szu, Fellow of IEEE)及 Logistics Cities: Infrastructural Nodes in Effective Supply
Chains-A Case Study (Speaker: Prof. Pieter Nagel, Victorial University, Australia)。
個人所發表的論文則是被安排在 9/6日(第二天)下午 13:30~15:00 進行的
Session B10: Intelligent Video Processing 的第 6篇論文發表。論文發表過程順利，也
同時和與會日本教授交換研究心得與相關問題討論。
二、與會心得
此次國際研討會是在日本熊本舉行，除了日本及台灣的學者外，亦有不少大
陸、加拿大、美國…等地的學者與會。除個人論文發表場次外，亦參與了其他場次
的論文發表，會中除了與論文作者有交談、互換研究心得。本次出席會議的台灣學
者(含研究生)都能如期出席發表論文口頭報告，是一次成功的學術交流。透過會後
休息時間，和與會的學者進行有關學術研究的深度討論，此次研討會亦有多位國內
學者參加，如高雄應用大學潘正祥教授、高雄大學洪宗貝教授、雲科大張傳育教授…
等等；參與此次研討會對於個人來說，將會對於個人在未來的研究上助益良多。
三、考察參觀活動(無是項活動者省略)
個人於會議空閒期間參加了日本熊本當地的旅遊，拜訪了熊本城、阿蘇火山、
水前寺等地。
