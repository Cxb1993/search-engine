3. Spectrum optimization with variable bandwidth effect  
 Most users prefer to use a continuous spectrum. A wiggler is hence always used to 
generate the required continuous spectrum. A drawback of that use is that much useless 
power radiated from the wiggler adversely affects the stability of optical components in 
the beam line and also requires more RF systems that increase the cost of the accelerator 
facility. Therefore, a large photon flux with a small radiation power is thus a necessary 
consideration. To obtain a small radiation power in an insertion device, the strength 
parameter of that device should be set appropriately to a small value such that the photon 
flux spectrum tends to become discontinuous. An algorithm code [5] for the optimization 
has been developed to solve this problem. For the undulator, the continuous spectrum can 
be generated on changing the magnet gap (i.e. change strength parameter) a little. An 
auto-optimization program code that can calculate a set of strength parameters of an 
insertion device with properties of small radiation power, a small angular cone and large 
flux was developed in aMathematica program that will be combined together with the 
LabView program. This program code readily produces the optimization results.

Development and testing of a prototype superconducting undulator 
 
J. C. Jan, C. S. Hwang*, F. Y. Lin and C. H. Chang 
 
National Synchrotron Radiation Research Center, Hsinchu Science Park, Hsinchu 
30076, Taiwan 
 
 Abstract 
A prototype superconducting undulator with a magnet array of length 1 m, a magnet 
period 15 mm and a magnet gap 5.6 mm was fabricated to test the performance of the 
magnetic field and to investigate related issues. The electric insulation was enhanced 
withKapton tape on the entire magnet array between the wire and the iron. A dummy 
beam duct was assembled with the magnet array and tested in the test dewar. A 
cryogenic Hall-probe measurement system with a rail guide was assembled with the 
test dewar to measure the field distributionon the axis. A heating-quenching 
experiment was performed to evaluate the heat load tolerance of SC wires. A UHV 
beam duct was developed and tested in a separate environment to ensure the 
reliability and to test the aging effect. In this paper, the concept of the magnet design, 
the related technical issues, the coil heat-load tolerance measurement, the 
performance of the magnetic field and the magnet training behavior isdiscussed.

期共四天，來自全世界各國家之同步輻射相關學者專家，大約近300人參加此次
的大會，就各項同步輻射儀器之開發與研究做廣泛的交流與討論。本次共有2篇
論文以壁報方式報告於大會中發表。 
大會除有五個相關的衛星會議為: 
Workshop1: The manipulation and characterization of FEL x-ray beams 
Workshop2: Coherence preserving optics for high brilliance light source 
Workshop3: Superconducting undulators and other new ID sources 
Workshop4: Advancing the frontiers of structural biology with micro beams, 
nano-crystals and picoseconds pulses 
Workshop5: Detector advances for light sources 
衛星會議之外，同步輻射儀器會議主要的學術分類為： 
1.  Sources and Instrumentation 
2.  Optical instruments and application 
3.  Detectors 
4.  Spectroscope 
5.  Biology 
6.  Timing 
 
  
除兩個場大會演講(Plenary Session)外，口頭報告(Oral Session)分四個場次，
一個壁報報告(Poster Session)安排於周三(9/22)下午五點到晚上八點。大會演講主
要為科學研究的新方向以及對新光源的需求，及各分類的主要進展報告，在口頭
報告部份，除了科學研究的成果以及配合實驗的儀器設備的發展與進展，儀器設
備主要為光學元件，特別是可調變型式之設計、開發，製造及測量，奈米級之精
密調整機構，偵測器之開發及應用等議題。 
二、與會心得 
1. ALS 的 upgrade 計畫 2009 年拿到經費準備 2012 年全部完成整個儲存環更新後的
emittance 可以從 6.3 nmrad 小到 2.2 nm rad，而垂直的 emittance 可以小到 5 pm 
rad。  他們利用 GLASS 以及 Genefic Algorithm (GA) 兩套軟體來優化儲存環，
並利用新的六極磁鐵來達到此目標，新六級磁鐵的設計概念與我們的 TPS 六極磁
鐵的功能一樣。優化後，其有很強的 focusing (即有很強的 chromatic 
aberration) 而得到很小的 dispersion (此因素使得六極場較無效率)。由於優化
後會有很短的lifetime以及beamsize的不穩定性。其中GLASS軟體是用來計算得
到 (1)在直段的小 emittance 有小的βx (2)在 low momentum compact 下同時有小
的 emittance 而 GA 是用來優化加速器的磁格。 
2. 王敏惠報告 SLAC PEP-X 儲存環設計結果，他們利用 90m 長的 damping wiggler 來
達到水平與垂直的 emittance 分別為 600 prad 及 8 pmrad，已經達到其 4.5 Gev
coherence 與 phase contrast 實驗在能量大於 30 KeV 以上的 brilliance 要比目
前大 1000 倍。另外 APS 的另一個重點計畫就是 protein structure 他們 protein 
structure 的研究在國際上是做的最好。希望 upgrade 之後還是要領先全世界，
還有在環境與綠色能源上的研究也是他們的重點。能夠利用同步光源來研究如何
來控制自然環境，以及能即時的來了解物質的真正生存的實際條件，例如製藥，
virus，biological，以及 geochemistry 等等。 
10. 另一大會演講也是由 Argonne 的 George Crabtree，他提到從 1850 年來到 2010 年
整個地球的水平面已經上升 20 公分，溫度上升 1∘C，而南北極的冰厚度減少 6mm，
因此他的演講內容也是在強調研究自然界與地球的能節減碳的材料開發。例如
quantum energyconversions 材料如光電化學電池(solarchemistrycell); Li ion 
cell (photo voltaic cell)；battery fuel cell，另外他又提到未來的重點實驗
有(1)新科學研究如何來控制地球上的material和chemistry。(2)利用pump-probe
來觀察時間的演化(3)soft x-ray microscope 做 image EXAFS 等來探討 local 
chemical selectivity。以及 SAXS 的 diffraction 與 granting incidence 
diffraction 等實驗。 
 
三、建議 
 此次參加會議人數約 300 人。借此會議展示 NSRRC 在插件
磁鐵上研究成績，並和他人互相交流、吸取他人經驗，仍是一件
有意義值得積極參與之事，若以後經費足夠的話，就可以讓學生
去發表並與國際人士互相討論。
