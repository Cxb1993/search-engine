瘤組織中但為數不多、具抗藥性且可再生新腫瘤組織的細胞，此種細胞被稱為腫瘤或癌症幹細胞
(cancer stem cell, CSC)。CSC在功能上類似於傳統的幹細胞，具備無限增殖和自我更新（self-renew）
的能力，並驅動腫瘤形成與生長(7-10)。目前癌症幹細胞已在神經膠質瘤 (11-13)、神經管母細胞瘤 
(14, 15)、乳癌 (16-18)、黑色素細胞瘤 (19, 20)、前列腺癌 (19, 21, 22) 以及大腸直腸癌 (23) 等腫
瘤中被分離出來並進行鑑定。CSC 除可以促使腫瘤生成外，也被認定是癌症惡性轉移的元兇。近
年醫界對於難以治癒與容易復發、轉移的癌症，提出癌症幹細胞，是造成癌症復發的關鍵，這說明
了只有消滅癌症幹細胞，癌症才有治癒的機會(5, 24)。目前常見的抗腫瘤治療方法並不能將腫瘤有
效根除，原因是CSCs 時常處於停止分裂的狀態，並且對於化療藥物有極高的抗藥性，其ABC運輸
蛋白的表現量更超過一般的腫瘤細胞，因而減弱甚至抑制化療藥物的毒殺作用，導致癌症在預後經
常出現復發的情形 (25, 26)。此外，在放射線治療上，由於癌症幹細胞內檢查點磷酸化酵素的表現
亦較一般腫瘤細胞強，因此在高能量放射線所導致的DNA破壞上，有很強且迅速的修補能力，使
CSCs不會走向細胞凋亡的路徑 (27, 28)。 
 
癌症幹細胞培養-生醫材料平台的建立 
 
由初級腫瘤(primary tumors)所分離純化出來的CSCs，僅佔整體腫瘤組織中的一小部分，且細
胞表面有細胞標的CD133之表現(29)。目前CSCs的分離可藉由免疫染色法將其分離出來，利用
CD133-免疫螢光抗體染色後，經流式細胞儀進行篩選。但最大瓶頸在於腫瘤組織中的CSCs含量過
於稀少，數目並不足以提供細胞抗藥性、抗細胞凋亡和生理上的大規模分析。為了增進CSCs方面
的各項研究，以利於癌症的治療，所以有必要發展一個使CSCs大量培養的穩定平台。 
 
癌症幹細胞的治療-光動力 
為了解決大腸直腸癌的癌症幹細胞對標靶治療藥物和放射線治療的抗性，達到徹底治癒大腸直
腸癌的目的，所以尋求一個有效殺死癌症幹細胞的治療方式是非常重要的。光動力療法
（photodynamic therapy, PDT）是一種受到矚目能夠破壞惡性腫瘤、癌前病變的新方法，它具有非
侵入性，以及能夠選擇性地使腫瘤細胞壞死，而不傷害週遭正常組織的特性(30-33)。目前光動力療
法於癌症幹細胞的毒殺效果上仍是未知的。而本研究之目的即是欲評估光動力療法於毒殺癌症幹細
胞的效果。藉由光動力治療的特性，讓光感藥物能大量累積於癌症幹細胞上，並且加以光激發，在
短時間內大量製造高能ROS與自由基，令自由基清除機制無法及時發揮功用。同時分析出癌症幹細
胞內最主要的自由基清除機制並加以抑制。由此更簡單、專一、有效的達成撲滅癌症幹細胞的目的。 
 
奈米診治系統的開發 
利用高分子微胞在藥物傳輸載體上極為普遍(34-36)，尤其對脂溶性藥物的包覆率特別突出。並
利用其具有的特性，例如奈米的大小、高溶解性、以及高穩定性，而具備enhanced permeabilityand 
retention (EPR)的特性累積於腫瘤組織，提高藥物在腫瘤的相對濃度(37)。此外，奈米微胞更可以包
覆MRI顯影劑，並且進行表面修飾，可發展成同時具偵測與治療的奈米裝置，未來的發展潛力無窮。

Cytotoxicity and cell uptake of SPIO@TPGS clusters 
To evaluate the cytotoxicity of SPIO@TPGS clusters, cells were incubated with various concentration 
of SPIO@TPGS for 24 hours and then quantitatively determined by MTT assay. Similar to commercial 
Resovist®, SPIO@TPGS cluster revealed almost non-toxic to the KB cells and the cell viability remained 
over 90% at the maximum iron concentration 25 μg/ml used in this experiment (Fig. 2). Thus, the 
hydrophobic SPIO can be processed using surfactant to form the hydrophilic SPIO nanoparticles or 
clusters as potential MR contrast agents without significant cell toxicity.  
Magnetic property of SPIO@TPGS clusters 
  Magnetic nanoparticles including Fe3O4 and γ-Fe2O3 with a size from 3 to 10 nm have been 
developed as contrast agents for MRI diagnosis. To evaluate the magnetic property of SPIO clusters, their 
response to an external magnetic field was measured at room temperature sweeping between -10000 to 
10000G. Fig. 3 shows the hysteresis loop of the as-prepared SPIO and SPIO clusters. Similar to Resovist®, 
the single SPIO and SPIO cluster showed reversible superparamagnetic behavior with very small 
coercivity (SPIO: 2.08G, SPIO@TPGS: 1.56, Resovist®: 8.79, Fig.3) that is important for MR 
applications. Moreover, the magnetization (Ms) with the same iron weight of SPIO, SPIO@TPGS cluster 
and Resovist® were 116.83, 137.04 ad 114.59, respectively. Thus, TPGS can not only stabilize the SPIOs 
and also enhance the magnetic performance of SPIO@TPGS clusters.

Abstract 
    In recent years, the hypothesis of cancer stem-like cells (CSCs) received striking attention. This hypothesis indicated CSCs possess stem-cell-
like properties, the ability to self-renew, proliferate infinitely. Besides, they also have the noticeable characteristic of anti-apoptosis and 
could induce the recurrence of tumors after chemotherapy or radiotherapy. These properties make CSCs more difficult to eradicate. Therefore, 
the mechanisms for CSCs which can escape from death after therapy merits further study. Here, colorectal cancer cells (CRCs) were studied by 
investigating the characteristics of drug-resistance and anti-apoptosis within colon CSCs and non-CSCs. First, colon CSCs and non-CSCs were 
separated by FACS. The cytotoxicity assay showed that CSCs were more resistant to clinic chemotherapeutic drugs than non-CSCs. Moreover, 
the result of real-time PCR indicated that the gene expression of CSCs on certain drug resistant and anti-apoptosis was at a higher level than 
non-CSCs. Therefore, these results gave clearer information on the characteristics of colon CSCs and the possible mechanisms to cause the 
CSCs escape from cell death after treatment. Our study provided practical information to design more effective therapeutic strategy for 
eradicating CRC and achieves better prognosis.  
Keywords: Cancer stem-like cell, colorectal cancer cells, Chemoresistance, Anti-apoptosis  
Conclusions 
       In this study, the colon cancer stem cell model was established. 
The characteristics of colon C S C s , such as drug resistance, anti-
apoptosis, were both studied. Cytotoxicity studies showed that colon 
CSCs were more resistant to chemotherapeutic drugs as compared to 
non-CSCs. The gene expression of drug resistance and anti-apoptosis 
genes in colon CSCs was also higher compared to these in non-CSCs. 
Our data indicated that colon CSCs might be easier to eradicate by 
inhibition of these dominant drug resistant genes and anti-apoptotic 
genes.  Further investigations are needed.

二、與會心得 
本次參與的國際會議是由Keystone Symposia所舉辦，主題是探討Stem cells, Cancer and 
Metastasis，地點是位於美國科羅拉多州的Keystone Conference Center。由於近來對於Cancer
生成分子機轉相關研究日熾，漸漸發現腫瘤的形成也如同身體內部的組織一般，由少數增生
和self-renewal能力較強，稱為cancer stem-like cells構成整個腫瘤組織，且是癌症惡性程度的一
項依據。因此，對於cancer stem-like cells特性的瞭解，將有助於控制惡性腫瘤的持續生長，
並且從中研發出毒殺cancer stem-like cells的方式。另外，如何抑制癌細胞的轉移也是項重要
的課題，分析其轉移機制將有利於控制腫瘤於特定區塊內而能完全撲滅。本次會議的宗旨即
是探討cancer stem-like cells和stem cells間的關聯性，並且瞭解細胞所存在之周遭環境訊息因子
對維持stem cell特性的影響，以及stem cell或是cancer stem-like cells在轉移的過程中所扮演的
角色和機制。 
近來漸趨熱門的EMT (epithelial to mesenchymal transition)在此會議中也成為眾所矚目的
焦點。目前在EMT研究上的翹楚—MIT的Weinberg教授也前來講授他們在EMT和cancer 
stem-like cells關聯性的發現。EMT不僅可讓cancer cell具有metastasis能力，讓其至新組織部位
落地生根，也可使cancer cell獲得更強的增生和self-newal能力，這代表著EMT可能使cancer cell
轉化為cancer stem-like cells。有鑑於此，許多引發EMT的分子漸漸被發現，例如TWIST､Snail､
Slug､TGF-β等，藉由這些分子的發現，我們可以誘導產生更多的cancer stem-like cells，這有
利於大規模的分析cancer stem-like cells的性質和篩選有效的治療藥物，再也不會為了無法獲
得夠多的cancer stem-like cells而苦惱。此外，在治療上，本次大會也有人提出抑制EMT的前
後訊息因子來達到壓抑腫瘤生長的目的，並且有不錯的成效。 
除了cancer stem-like cells外，stem cell研究也是重要主題，有許多研究是提及miRNA的調
控對於stem cell機制的影響，特別是分化路徑上。隨著miRNA類型不斷的出現，或許對於將
來stem cell的生成和特異分化上會有重要的突破。 
希望自己能加強這一部分，往後能以更快的步伐，追上別人的腳步。 
    藉由研討會，我更了解自己往後的實驗架構，並了解目前在此領域的走向，期待我
還能有其他機會，到其他研討會去吸收新知，與國際交流，提升自己應變與談吐的能力。
