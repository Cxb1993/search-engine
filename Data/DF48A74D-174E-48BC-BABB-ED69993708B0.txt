Abstract? 
Global synchronization in large 
populations of coupled identical chaotic 
systems have been studied in this report. 
The N )3N( ≥ chaotic systems are 
coupled in central-satellite-network 
system. The Duffing equation has been 
used to perform the chaos 
synchronization of N chaotic systems. 
The stability of the global 
synchronization of the N Duffing 
equations have been investigated by 
Lyapunov’s direct method. 
Further, numerical simulations 
have also been carried out to confirm 
the validity of the theoretical approach 
as N=4, 5 and 6. Those are verified 
theoretically and numerically. 
Furthermore, we must point out this 
report give not only a theoretical basis
