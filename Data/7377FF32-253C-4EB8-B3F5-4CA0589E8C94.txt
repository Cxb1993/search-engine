摻雜型鐵酸鉍薄膜之電性及應用於非揮發性鐵電記憶體之研究 
“The electrical properties of doped-BiFeO3 thin-films and the studies on the non-volatile 
ferroelectric memory applications” 
計畫編號：NSC 98-2221-E-131-026 
執行期間：98 年 8 月 1 日 至 99 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：阮弼群 明志科技大學材料工程系 副教授 
 
一、 中文摘要 
本計畫使用摻雜鐵酸鉍 (BiFeO3) 製作非
揮發鐵電記憶體，來解決鐵酸鉍漏電流及可靠
度的問題。多鐵性鐵酸鉍具有鐵電性與反鐵磁
性，為一受矚目的鐵電材料。高介電常數材料
主要作用為鐵電薄膜與矽接觸時的阻障層。計
畫內容將製作三種記憶體結構：(1)製作金屬/
鐵電薄膜/金屬 (MFM) 結構電容器。(2)製作
金屬/鐵電薄膜/絕緣層/半導體 金氧半電容器 
(MFIS capacitor)。(3)製作金屬/鐵電薄膜/絕緣
層/半導體 金氧半電晶體 (MFIS transistor)。
我們將利用濺鍍法 (sputtering) 研製以似鐵離
子與鉍離子大小之摻雜元素離子 La3+、Nd3+來
取代 Bi-site，以 Mn3+、Ti4+與 Cr2+摻雜元素離
子來取代 Fe-site 形成摻雜鐵酸鉍鐵電薄膜，以
及現今熱門的高介電常數材料如 HfO2、ZrO2、
Y2O3 等與鑭系元素如 La2O3、Dy2O3等之氧化
薄膜材料，比較其電學性質以及可靠度如資料
維 持  (data retention) 與 可 運 作 週 期 
(endurance) 的特性。以探討各種不同的摻雜
型鐵酸鉍鐵電記憶結構在超大型非揮發記憶
體的應用。 
另多重鐵磁特性的摻雜型鐵酸鉍 BFO 
(BiFeO3) 材料特性亦是我們研究的重點。因
鉍鐵氧漏電流較一般鐵電材料大，我們將探討
以各種製程條件，設法摻雜型鐵酸鉍製作之記
憶體元件的特性達到最佳值，所用製程條件包
含：電漿氬氧比、退火溫度、絕緣層厚度、絕
緣層種類等等，試圖找出特性與可靠度佳之記
憶體元件。漏電流機制亦是我們研究的重點，
溫度相依的電流行為在此計畫也有詳盡的分
析與結果。  
二、英文摘要 
The goal of this research project is applying 
doped-BiFeO3 as the ferroelectric layer to solve 
the leakage current and reliability issues of 
BiFeO3-based nonvolatile ferroelectric memories. 
Multiferroic BiFeO3 is a promising candidate 
coupled ferroelectric and antiferromagnetic 
properties. The purpose of the high-κ insulator 
layer is to prevent the reaction and 
inter-diffusion between the ferroelectric layer 
and semiconductor substrate. Three nonvolatile 
structures which are metal/ferroelectric/metal 
(MFM) capacitors, metal/ferroelectric/insulator 
/silicon (MFIS) capacitors and transistors will be 
fabricated. We will use the chemical solution 
deposition and RF magnetron sputtering methods 
to prepare the doped-BiFeO3 ferroelectric films 
on wide bandgap high-κ dielectric films for 
MFIS structures. The dopants include La3+、Nd3+ 
ions for Bi-site substitution and Mn3+、Ti4+、Cr2+ 
for Fe-site substitution due to similar radii 
compared to Bi3+ and Fe3+, respectively. The 
electrical and reliabilities such as the data 
retention and endurance of MFIS structures with 
different high-κ binary dielectrics such as 
HfO2、ZrO2、and Y2O3 and lanthanide oxides 
co-sputtering. The RF and DC powers are used 
for BFO and Ti/Mn targets, respectively. The 
thickness of Ti/Mn-substituted BFO thin film is 
250 nm. The Ti/Mn-substituted BFO/HfO2 
structures were then rapid thermal annealed 
(RTA) in pure N2 at 5000C-7000C for 30 s. 
Aluminum was used as the top electrode. A 
post-metallization annealing (PMA) was 
performed at 4000C in N2. The crystalline phase 
of ferroelectric film was identified by x-ray 
diffraction (XRD). The combination states of Bi 
4f, Fe 2p, Mn 2p and O 1s electrons were 
examined by x-ray photoelectron spectroscopy 
(XPS) (Perkin-Elmer ESCA system) with using 
Mg Kα line at 1253.6 eV, the pass energy of 46.9 
eV and the take-off angle of 900. The carbon C 
1s peak at 285.3 eV was used as the reference 
binding energy. The C-V characteristics were 
measured with a Agilent 4980 LCR meter. The 
area of the capacitors is 2.8x10-3 cm2. The 
relative dielectric constant εr of HfO2 obtained 
from our Al/HfO2/Si (MIS) capacitors is 15. The 
Ti/Mn substituting amounts for Fe-site in BFO 
films were determined by XPS. The Ti 
substituting amounts for Fe-site in BFO films 
were determined by XPS. The compositions of 
Bi(Fe0.65Ti0.35)O3, Bi(Fe0.54Ti0.46)O3 and 
Bi(Fe0.42Ti0.58)O3 are obtained from deposition 
process conditions of DC powers of 5 W, 10 W, 
and 15 W, respectively. The compositions of 
Bi(Fe0.92Mn0.08)O3, Bi(Fe0.85Mn0.15)O3 and 
Bi(Fe0.72Mn0.28)O3 are obtained from deposition 
process conditions of DC powers of 5 W, 10 W, 
and 15 W, respectively.  
 
五、結果與討論 
Fig.1 shows the XPS spectra scanned from 
700 to 740 eV of the Fe 2p peaks in the 
Ti-substituted BFO thin films deposited on 
HfO2/Si stack with DC power of 10 W. The 3/2 
and 1/2 spin-orbit doublet components are 
positioned at 713 eV and 725 eV, respectively, 
which are associated with the Fe3+ valance state 
[17]. The measured Fe 2p1/2 peak can be fitting 
into two peaks, which are Fe2+ and Fe3+ states. 
The slope of Fe3+ at annealing temperature of 
5000C is larger than that of 7000C. It indicates 
that the Fe3+ substituted by Ti4+ ion is efficiently 
taken place at higher annealing temperature. The 
amount of Fe atom calculated from the peak area 
in BFO films decreases with increasing the 
annealing temperature from 5000C to 7000C (a1 
> b1 > c1), though the percentages of Bi and O 
atoms almost keep the same. The results confirm 
that Fe ion is replaced by Ti ion after 
postannealing process. Fig.2 shows the XPS 
spectra scanned from 150 to 170 eV of the Bi 4f 
peaks in the Ti-substituted BFO thin films 
deposited on HfO2/Si stack with DC power of 10 
W. The 7/2 and 5/2 spin-orbit doublet 
components are positioned at 159 eV and 165 eV, 
respectively, which are associated with the Bi3+ 
valance state [17]. The measured Bi 4f7/2 peak 
can be fitting into two peaks, which are Bi2+ and 
Bi3+ states. The coexistence of both Bi2+ and Bi3+ 
implies that a different phase from BFO crystals.

Fig.9 shows the memory window as a 
function of insulator thickness at annealing 
temperature of 5000C. The inset shows the 
memory window is defined as the flat band 
voltage shift (△VFB) when the sweep voltage is 
swept from negative to positive voltage and 
swept back. According to the crystallization 
temperature discussed above, a sufficient amount 
of amorphous BFO remains uncrystallized and 
the ferroelectric polarization is very weak. When 
the insulator thickness is increased from 20 nm 
to 40 nm, the voltage drop across the insulator 
layer becomes larger. It implies that the the 
charge injection effect is enhanced. The voltage 
drop across the ferroelectric layer (Vf) and the 
insulator layer (Vi) can be expressed as 
Vf/Vi=df×εi/di×εf, where df, di, εf, εi are the film 
thicknesses and the dielectric constants of the 
ferroelectric and the insulator layer, respectively 
[20]. As a result, the memory window turns out 
to be counterclockwise or negative when the 
sweep voltages are larger than 6 V. When the 
annealing temperature is increased from 5000C 
to 7000C, the ferroelectric polarization is the 
dominant mechanism due to BFO crystallization. 
The memory window as a function of insulator 
film thickness is shown in fig.10. When the 
insulator thickness increases, the inter-diffusion 
between BFO thin film and silicon substrate is 
further reduced. The maximum memory window 
can be achieved as large as 2 V when the sweep 
voltage is 8 V and the HfO2 thickness is 60 nm. 
Fig.11 shows the memory window as a 
function of DC power for Mn at annealing 
temperature of 6000C. When the doping amount 
increases, the memory window becomes smaller.

nonperovskite phase of Bi0.37Mn0.03O0.62 is 
observed. Therefore, the ferroelectric 
polarization will be reduced. Fig.12 shows that 
the leakage current decreases with the ratio of

表，與會期間大會也很用心地在中午安排了一個小時半的午餐進行文化交流活動，以促進與
會學員間的了解與認識。除此，最後一天安排香港城市大學工程學院的實驗室參觀，實驗設
備的充足與完備，使我們覺得為何這學校有如此高的知名度且能進入世界百大，值得我們學
校需借鏡的地方。本人參加奈米電子技術研討會 IEEE International NanoElectronics Conference 
(INEC2010)，係將最近實驗成果整理後發表論文題目為：(1) 漸進式高介電摻雜型鉿鑭氧化
物薄膜之縱深與化學鍵結分析 (Depth Profiles and Chemical Bonding States of Graded Doping
