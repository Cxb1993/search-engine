氧化鋁模板製備是將鋁片( foil )與電解
液(硫酸、草酸、磷酸) 和陰極金屬(白金)搭
配形成電化學反應，反應中鋁金屬表面生成
氧化鋁膜，其特色為孔洞大小單一、孔洞均
一分佈，可經由調整參數而加以控制孔洞大
小。陽極處理進行時，陽極附近液層中會有
Al+3 含量增加，同時在陽極上也會有氧氣析
出，陰極則析出氫氣，並伴有溶液溫度上升
的現象，陽極產生如下反應： 
H2O → [O] + 2H+ +2e-           (1) 
2Al + 3[O] →Al2O3 + 熱          (2) 
酸性溶液對鋁金屬與生成的氧化鋁膜進
行著化學溶解，其反應如下： 
2Al + 6H+ →2Al3+ + 3H2          (3) 
Al2O3 + 6H+ →2Al3+ + 3H2O       (4) 
進行陽極處理時，氧化鋁膜的生成與溶
解同時併行，故只有氧化鋁膜的生成速度大
於溶解速度時，膜的厚度才能增長。綜上所
述，氧化鋁膜的生成是兩種不同的反應同時
進行的結果。一是電化學反應析出氧與金屬
鋁結合生成氧化鋁，二為化學反應，即酸性
溶液對氧化鋁膜的溶解。唯有電化學反應速
度大於化學反應速度時，氧化鋁膜才能順利
持續生長。然為獲均勻有序的多孔性氧化鋁
膜板則必須對其控製參數深入了解並熟知其
生成的變因。多孔性氧化鋁膜板製程其變因
包括︰電解液濃度17、電流密度、電壓18和時
間等，因此在製備多孔氧化鋁模板須確實掌
握製程參數。 
氧化鋁奈米模板有其發光特性。1999 年
W. L. Cai 以不同種類的陽極氧化溶液製作

都是落在藍光的頻段。在氧化鋁薄膜中，這
個光激發螢光的頻段顯然由單離子的氧空位
(F+心)所引起19。2003 年 T. Gao 發現多孔質氧

光中心從被合併的草酸元素改變，這就是發
470 奈米藍色的原因20 。2001 年 X. L. Wu 等
研究了矽基陽極氧化多孔性薄膜的發光來
源，將陽極氧化試片在不同溫度退火，觀察

有關的缺陷中心 F+中心為發光形成的原因

米孔陣列的陽極氧化鋁薄膜的 PL 光譜，薄膜
在草酸裡形成顯示兩個頻段所組成很強的藍
光，由光譜觀察與分析顯示出這兩個頻段是
由兩種不同的缺陷(F 與 F+中心)所組成，而不
是像先前報告的只有 F+中心，且缺陷被分布
在結構的孔洞中22。同年 X. L. Wu 研究了 0.5
莫耳草酸下多孔質氧化鋁模板發光性質，發
現 PL 在 565 奈米有一個很寬的頻寬，565 奈

有一些紫外光，紫光與藍光 PL 頻段的存在，

Deposition of nanoscale films on the nanopore-arrayed template by 
an in-situ spinning-precipitated technique

ABSTRACT 
A spinning-precipitated technique is proposed to fabricate nanoscale films. In this 
study, the deposition of MnZn ferrite on the template of nanoporous anodic oxide 
alumina (AAO) is demonstrated. The liquid solutions for precipitating MnZn ferrite 
were citrated in turn in the center of the spin plate, and impinged on the target due to 
the applied centrifugal force. The MnZn ferrite is precipitated on the plane of AAO 
with the island growth mode at the initial stage. With increasing the precipitation time, 
the precipitated area would extend to cover the nanopores via the side growth. As a 
result, the thickness of the deposited thin film can be well controlled in the nanoscale 
by the island and side growth mode. On the other hand, the nanopores of the template 
are free from the deposition of the MnZn ferrite due to the spinning.  
 
Keywords: MnZn ferrite; thin films; spinning; deposition; precipitation 
 
INTRODUCTION 
Spin coating is a widely used technique to deposit liquid films on substrates, 
especially in the manufacture of microdevices [1]. For example, the liquid photoresist 
are mostly deposited during the semiconductor processes for the mask exposure. After 
the development of the exposure, the patterns are residual on the substrate surface for 
selective etching. Regardless of the patterned or non-patterned areas, the patterned 
plane is fully deposited with the subsequent deposition processes. The pattern appears 
by removing the residual photoresist. Although there are various chemical and 
physical vapor deposition techniques to deposit thin films, the demand of a new 
deposition technique to reduce deposition process is desired. In this study, a 
spinning-precipitated technique is developed to deposit on patterns in one step. The 
approach is to join a chemical precipitation technique and a spinning technique.

The spinning-precipitated MnZn ferrite is collected to analyze its composition and 
microstructure. The x-ray diffraction pattern of the collected powder from the citrated 
solutions is shown in Fig. 5. The main peaks correspond to the single-phase spinel 
structure and some unidentified impurity. This result demonstrates that the 
microstructure of spinning-precipitated nanoclusters onto the surface of the 
nanoporous Al2O3 substrate is the spinel structure of MnZnFe2O4.[5] The 
paramagnetism of MnZn ferrite has been widely used on the magnetic. In this case, 
the MnZn with nanopore arrays contribute to the peculiar on the magnetism and 
would be fully discussed in the other place.
