一種發光二極體燈具，包括一發光二極體元件以及一反射燈
罩。反射燈罩具有一出光截面，且包括至少兩個部件，如一第一
部件以及一第二部件。第一部件具有一第一內凹曲面，而第二部
件與第一部件連接，並具有一相對於第一內凹曲面之第二內凹曲
面。第一內凹曲面的形狀不等於第二內凹曲面的形狀，且第一內
凹曲面的部分邊緣與第二內凹曲面的部分邊緣圍繞出光截面。發
光二極體元件配置於反射燈罩內。上述之發光二極體燈具能提供
具非對稱照度分佈的光束。 
 
英文摘要 
A light emitting diode lamp including a light emitting diode 
component and a reflector is provided.  The reflector has a light 
output section and includes at least two parts, such as a first part 
and a second part.  The first part has a first concave curved surface, 
and the second part is connected with the first part and has a second 
concave curved surface opposite to the first concave curved surface.  
The curvature of the first concave curved surface is not equal to the 
curvature of the second concave curved surface.   The partial edge 
of the first concave curved surface and the partial edge of the second 
concave curved surface surround the light output section.  The 
light emitting diode component is disposed in the reflector.  The 
said light emitting diode lamp is able to provide a light beam with 
asymmetric illumination distribution.
