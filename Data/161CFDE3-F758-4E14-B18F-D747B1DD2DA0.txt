一、中文摘要 
金屬氧化物半導體氣體感測器因其構造
簡單，靈敏度高，價格低，近年來己成為重要
的研究課題。本研究利用三嵌段兩性共聚物為
模板，以溶膠-凝膠法製備介孔 Ti/Cr/Al doped 
WO3，經 TGA、XRD、TEM 和 BET 分析微
結構和比表面積，並量測不同溫度對 H2S 氣
體感測性質之影響。實驗結果顯示，利用三嵌
段兩性共聚物製備之 Ti/Cr/Al doped WO3 經
250~350℃之煆燒後結構為單斜晶(微晶)。經
300 ℃煆燒 1 小時後之 WO3-3%TiO2 表面型
態為均勻的蟲狀組織，孔洞大小約為 7.6 nm，
比表面積為 125.5m2/g。在 H2S 氣體感測方
面，在較低的溫度下(<100℃)即具有靈敏度，
由此可知，介孔材料可有效降低感測器操作溫
度。 
關鍵詞：介孔、三嵌段兩性共聚物、氣體感測
器 
Abstract 
In recent years, there has been increasing 
interest in the study of metal oxide 
semiconductor gas sensor owing to simple 
construction, high sensitivity and low price. In 
this study, the mesoporous Ti/Cr/Al doped WO3 
have been prepared by using sol-gel process 
with block copolymer as template. The structure, 
morphology and specific surface area of samples 
have been investigated by TGA, XRD, TEM and 
BET. The sensitivity were measured at various 
temperatures. The structure of mesoporous 
Ti/Cr/Al doped WO3 was monoclinic structure 
between 250~350 ℃ . In addition, the 
morphology of mesoporous WO3-3%TiO2 which 
was calcined at 300℃exhibited wormhole-like 
structure with an average pore size of 7.6 nm 
and the specific surface area of 125.5 m2/g. It is 
noted that the sample showed a sensitivity <100
℃ . Therefore, the mesoporous material can 
reduce the operating temperature. 
Keywords: mesoporous, block copolymer, gas 
sensing

Effect of Block Copolymer and Additive Concentrations on the 
Structure of Nanostructured Mesoporous TiO2 
 
Lay Gaik Teoh1, Yi Hsuan Liu1, Yee-Shin Chang2, Yen Hsun Su3, Ya Chi Shen4 
1Department of Mechanical Engineering, National Pingtung Univ. of Sci. & Tech., 
Neipu, Taiwan 
2Department of Electronic Engineering, National Formosa Univ., Huwei, Taiwan 
3Department of Physics, National Cheng Kung Univ., Tainan, Taiwan 
4Nan-Jeon Institute of Technology, Yen-Shui, Taiwan 
E-mail: n5888107@mail.npust.edu.tw 
 
Abstract 
Mesoporous materials1 with tailored pore structures and high surface areas have 
gathered increasing attention because of their possible application as adsorbent, 
catalyst, and separatory materials. TiO2 in anatase phase has been used as a 
photocatalyst for photodecomposition and solar energy conversion because of its high 
photoactivity2. Two of the most important factors affecting the photocatalytic activity 
of TiO2 are its specific surface area and crystallinity. Thus, high surface area 
mesoporous TiO2 with controlled nanocrystalline framework is an important material 
in need of further investigation.  
In this study, nanostructured mesoporous TiO2 was prepared by sol-gel process, 
and the synthesis was accomplished by using block copolymer as the 
structure-directing agent and TiCl4 as the inorganic precursor. TGA, XRD, SAXS, 
TEM and N2 adsorption-desorption isotherms were used to characterize the 
microstructure, pore size, and pore structure after calcination. The powders were 
found to have a wormhole-like pore structures after calcination. When the additive of 
block copolymer increased from 0.1 to 1.5 g, the specific surface areas increased from 
113.23 to 284.75 m2/g, but the average pore sizes decreased from 7.4 to 4.8 nm, and 
an average crystallite sizes decreased from 9.0 to 5.5 nm after being calcined at 300°C 
for 5 h. These results show that the mesostructure and crystallinity of TiO2 can be 
controlled by the block copolymer.
