Study on the pervaporation performance of the organic mixtures and the 
characterization of the fine-structure through the novel interfacial polymerized 
polyamide composite membranes 
新型界面聚合聚醯胺複合膜滲透蒸發分離有機混合物效能與微結構鑑定之關聯性研究

Abstract 
To improve the pervaporation performance of polyamide membrane, the polyamide composite membranes 
were prepared via the interfacial polymerization of 1,3-diaminopropane (DAPE) and succinyl chloride (SCC) 
or trans-5-norbornene-2,3-dicarbonyl chloride (tNBDC) on the surface of modified asymmetric 
polyacrylonitrile (mPAN) membrane and were applied in the pervaporation separation of 90 wt% aqueous 
THF and 98 wt% aqueous EAc solutions at 25oC. To investigate the variation in the free volume of the 
polyamide active layer, the free-standing polyamide membrane was examined by conducting PALS 
experiments, in which a positron beam was used. Attenuated Total Reflectance-Fourier Transform Infrared 
(ATR-FTIR) Spectrometry, scanning electron microscopy (SEM), atomic force microscope (AFM) and water 
contact angle measurements were applied to analyze chemical structure, morphology, surface roughness and 
hydrophilicity of the polyamide active layer of composite membrane. From the result of PALS experiments, 
the DAPE-tNBDC polyamide active layer had the lower o-Ps lifetime τ3 (corresponding to the size of free 
volume) and the higher o-Ps intensity I3 (corresponding to the concentration of free volume) compared with 
the DAPE-SCC polyamide active layer. In the dehydration of the aqueous tetrahydrofuran (THF) and ethyl 
acetate (EAc) solutions, the DAPE-tNBDC/mPAN composite membrane had the higher permeation rate than 
and the same water concentration in permeate with the DAPE-SCC/mPAN composite membrane. The 
pervaporation performance was in agreement with the result of PALS experiment. 
 
摘要 
為了提升聚醯胺薄膜的滲透蒸發分離效能，利用二胺單體 1,3-diaminopropane (DAPE)與二醯氯單體
succinyl chloride (SCC)或 trans-5-norbornene-2,3-dicarbonyl chloride (tNBDC)，在改質的聚丙醯腈非對稱
膜表面進行界面聚合反應，製備兩系列聚醯胺複合膜，並將其應用於滲透蒸發分離 90 wt%四氫呋喃水
溶液與 98 wt%乙酸乙酯水溶液。為探討聚醯胺聚合層的自由體積變化，利用正子湮滅時間光譜實驗，
以正子束測試儀量測無支撐層的聚醯胺薄膜。全反射式傅立葉轉換紅外線光譜儀(ATR-FTIR)、掃描式
電子顯微鏡(SEM)、原子力顯微鏡(AFM)與接觸角測試儀被用來分析複合膜表面聚醯胺層的化學結構、
型態、表面粗糙度與親水性。由正子湮滅時間光譜實驗的結果可知，與 DAPE-SCC 聚醯胺層比較，
DAPE-tNBDC 聚醯胺層有較低的 o-Ps 湮滅時間 τ3 (與自由體積的尺寸相關聯)與較高的 o-Ps 強度 I3 (與
自由體積的濃度相關聯)。在四氫呋喃水溶液與乙酸乙酯水溶液的脫水實驗中，與 DAPE-SCC/mPAN 複
合膜比較，DAPE-tNBDC/mPAN 複合膜有較高的透過率與相同的透過水濃度，滲透蒸發效能與正子湮
滅時間光譜的實驗結果一致。

Introduction 
Interfacial polymerization is an effective technique for fabricating a composite membrane, which consists 
of a thin interfacially polymerized selective layer on the surface of a porous support membrane. This 
 4
support membrane. The cast membrane was precipitated by immersion in a bath of water. The resulting 
porous PAN membrane was washed in water several times for more than one day to remove the remnant of 
NMP solvent, and it was then stored in water. 
The porous modified PAN membrane was prepared by immersing the PAN support membrane in a 2M 
NaOH solution at 50oC. The partial -CN groups of PAN can be converted into -COOH or -CONH2 groups 
after the hydrolysis with NaOH solution. The porous modified PAN support membrane was washed in a water 
bath for several hours, and it was then stored in water before use for the interfacial polymerization. 
 
Preparation of interfacially polymerized polyamide composite membrane 
The active polyamide skin layer was polymerized via the interfacial polymerization technique. The mPAN 
support membrane was first immersed in a 1 wt % aqueous DAPE solution for 3 min. Then, the excess 
amount of aqueous DAPE solution on the surface of the mPAN support membrane was removed using a 
rubber rod. The mPAN support membrane soaked with the aqueous DAPE solution was contacted with a 
toluene solution containing 0.5 wt% acyl chloride for 3 min to carry out the process of interfacial 
polymerization. Finally, the resulting polyamide composite membrane was washed in methanol and then dried 
at atmospheric temperature. 
 
Preparation of free-standing polyamide membrane 
To perform the PALS experiment, the free-standing PA membrane was obtained at the interface between 
the aqueous DAPE and the organic acyl chloride solutions in a beaker through the interfacial polymerization 
at the atmospheric temperature. After it was taken out the beaker, the free-standing PA membrane was washed 
in methanol and then was dried under vacuum. 
 
Characterization 
Attenuated Total Reflectance-Fourier Transform Infrared (ATR-FTIR) Spectrometry (Perkin Elmer 
Spectrum One) spectroscopy was used to examine the chemical structure of the composite membrane. 
Scanning electron microscopy (SEM, HITACHI S4700) and atomic force microscope (AFM, Digital 
Instruments, DI-NS3a USA) were used to observe the morphology and the surface roughness of the 
polyamide composite membrane. In order to understand the surface hydrophilicity of the polyamide 
composite membrane, the water contact angle was estimated with the use of Automatic Interfacial 
Tensiometer (FACE Mode 1 PD-VP). 
 
Positron annihilation lifetime spectroscopy (PALS) experiment 
In order to investigate the interfacially polymerized polyamide layers’ free volume variation, the 
free-standing polyamide membranes were conducted by the PALS experiments in which a positron beam at 
the R&D Center for Membrane Technology, Chung Yuan University, Taiwan was used. The positron 
annihilation lifetime spectroscopy of the free-standing polyamide membranes were determined by detecting 
the prompt γ-rays (1.28 MeV) from the nuclear decay that accompanies the emission of a positron from the 
22Na radioisotope and the subsequent annihilation γ-rays (0.511 MeV). All of the PAL spectra were analyzed 
by a finite-term lifetime analysis method using the PATFIT program. 
 
Pervaporation measurement 
The pervaporation separation of 90 wt% aqueous THF and 98 wt% aqueous EA solutions through 
polyamide composite membranes was conducted. The feed solution was contacted directly with the 
membrane. The effective area of the membrane for pervaporation was 11.64 cm2. The operating temperature 
(feed solution temperature) was 25oC. The concentrations of the feed solution and permeate were measured by 
gas chromatography (GC; China Chromatography 8700 T). The permeation rate (P) was calculated from the

二、與會心得 
2004 年在中國吉林省長春市首次舉辦第一屆國際高分子化學會議，接
著，第二屆與第三屆分別於 2006年與 2008年在遼寧省大連市與安徽省合肥
市舉辦，今年在江蘇省蘇州市舉辦的第四屆國際高分子化學會議，投稿論文
多達二百七十四篇，其中亞洲地區佔了二百三十九篇，充分展現出亞洲地區
高分子化學研究能量的精進與壯大，不讓歐美各國專美於前。另外，亞洲地
區高分子化學研究的發展亦與世界同步，針對高分子合成、巨大分子構築、
功能性高分子以及生物相關與醫用高分子等四個學術主題，進行深入的研討
與交流，發表最新的學術思想和研究成果，共同探討相關領域的發展趨勢、
瓶頸及其解決之道。 
在參與此次國際高分子化學盛會期間，透過各地專家學者在高分子化學
方面的成果分享，使本人研究動力大幅提升，並經過與會學者的熱烈討論
後，讓本人的學術視野更開闊，且激盪出多元的研究思考，獲得諸多寶貴的
經驗，堪稱受益良多。本人期望將所獲得的經驗，應用於高分子薄膜研究領
域，包括薄膜結構設計與薄膜化學改質等，進一步應用於薄膜分離程序，例
如滲透蒸發與氣體分離等，期能有所助益。感謝國科會對於此行的贊助，讓
本人與會期間的經費不虞匱乏，順利完成此次中國蘇州的研究探訪之旅。 
 
三、建議 
透過國科會的研究補助，讓本人能在經費毫無後顧之憂的情形下，參與
本次中國蘇州的研究探訪之旅，不但增進自身的研究視野，也讓各地的學者
進一步了解台灣的研究成果，這對於台灣在國際學術上的聲望有極大助益。
在參與會議期間，發現許多中國大陸、日本、韓國等同為亞洲國家的學生，
出席此國際高分子化學會議，甚至亦有遠到而來的歐美國家學生，參與此盛
會。因此，深切期望能多提供一些補助方案，鼓勵我國的學生參與國際學術
會議，除可以增廣視野，促進英文聽講的能力之外，亦藉由學生研究成果的
競賽，激發學生的研究潛力，以提升我國的研究能量。
