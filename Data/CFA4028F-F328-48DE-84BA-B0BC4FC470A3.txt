以奈米粒子與量子點為電荷存取中心之矽奈米線快閃記憶元件研究(I) 
“Silicon Nanowire Flash Memory with Nanoparticles and Quantom dots as Charge Storage Centers” 
計畫編號：NSC96-2221-E-009-213 
執行期間：96 年 8 月 1 日 至 97 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：許鉦宗 交通大學奈米科技研究所系教授 
 
一、 中文摘要 
非揮發性記憶(nonvolatile memory)元件主要必
須符合高可靠度 (reliability)、低功率消耗
(power consumption)、與低工作電壓(working 
voltage)等等特性。由於元件尺寸快速 scaling 
down，即使以分散電荷儲存的 SONOS 結構也
面對了兩個彼此 trade-off 問題: 較長記憶儲存
時間與更高存取速率。本研究擬利用掃描式探
針顯微鏡微影技術與自組裝(self-assembly)沉
積技術在(100)絕緣矽(SOI)矽晶基材上研製矽
奈線快閃記憶元件。奈米線快閃記憶元件的主
結構是用掃描式探針顯微鏡將矽轉換成奈米
級矽氧化物，以此矽氧化物做為蝕刻罩幕
(Mask)進行非等向性溼式蝕刻後產生矽奈米
線為基礎。另外、利用自組裝沉積 AEAPTMS
或 MPTMS 單層分子膜於二氧化矽上，可以將
奈米金粒子或是半導體量子點(CdSe 等)選擇
性沉積在分子層上。結合上述兩個技術，便可
以研製以奈米金粒子或是半導體量子點為電
荷儲存中心的奈米快閃元件。於不同溫度
(4.2K~450K) 時，觀察矽奈米線快閃記憶元件
的存取穩定性、電流 / 電壓特性、不同
program/erase 的 穿 隧 現 象 如 channel hot 
electrons 或 Folwer-Nordheim 等等。另外、經
由沉積多種奈米金粒子或是半導體量子點，來
達 成 多 階 電 荷 儲 存 (Multiple-level charge 
storage)或多位元(multiple-bit)的元件也一併加
以研製探討。最後、本研究將於第三年將研製
單個(或數個) 奈米金粒子或是半導體量子點
做為懸浮閘的矽奈米線快閃記憶元件，並建立
相關理論模型。 
二、英文摘要 
Modern nonvolatile semiconductor memories 
possess properties like reliable, low-power, 
low-voltage performance. There are essentially 
two dominant technologies which compete for 
an ever-expanding world market: floating-gate 
EEPROMs and floating-trap SONOS. To date, 
the mass produced nonvolatile memory devices 
are based on the concept of a continuous layer of 
floating gate. However, it has faced the 
difficulties of consecutive scaling down due to 
the compromise between long-term nonvolatility 
and high operating speed. This research project 
focuses on fabrication and measurement of 
silicon nanowire flash memory devices on (100) 
SOI silicon wafer. The main structure of a 
nanoflash device is fabricated with scanning 
probe lithography (SPL); the hydrogen 
terminated silicon surface can be transferred into 
silicon oxide when a bias is applied between the 
SPM tip and the substrate. The oxide was then 
used as mask in orient dependent wet etching to 
generated silicon nanowires (SiNWs). On the 
other hand, self-assembly of AEAPTMS or 
MPTMS monolayers provide a linker to attract 
nanoparticles or semiconductor quantum dots on 
the silicon dioxide surface. Integration of two 
techniques described above, silicon nanowire 
flash memory device with nanoparticles or 
quantum dots as charge storage centers can be 
achieved. Retention time, electrical properties, 
thickness of the deposited iridium film is around 
1.5 nm. According to figure 1(a), the mean NCs 
size and NCs density of the Ir-NCs are about 15 
nm and 2.6×1011 ㎝ -2, respectively. Figure 2 
presents the cross section of the device structure 
and HRTEM image of MOS with Ir-NCs 
embedded between SiO2 and HfO2. Inset shows 
the schematic of MOS device structure with 
HfO2 as blocking oxide and SiO2 as tunneling 
oxide. Figure 3 shows the XPS spectra of Ir-NCs 
on SiO2 after high temperature thermal cycles. 
The variation of intensities is presumably due to 
the percentage of surface coverage after different 
annealing temperature. The binding energies of 
the Ir 4f levels are found almost invariant after 
the rapid thermal annealing in N2 ambient 
showing the excellent thermal stability of Ir-NCs 
in corporation into the CMOS process. Figure 4 
demonstrates the capacitance-voltage (C-V) 
curve of fabricated MOS capacitors with and 
without Ir-NCs. The gate voltage was swept 
from 10 V to – 10 V and back to 10 V, with a 
step of 0.2 V. A large flat-band voltage shifts 
(ΔVFB  2 V ) were observed in the sample ≒
with Ir-NCs embedded.

五、心   得   與   建   議 
 
奈米製程技術(22 nm 以下)會採用 Ion-beam E-beam 或是目其他技術前仍然意見紛紜，但各
國不無全力以赴。國內目前在奈米科技的投入在量上雖不少於國外，但具深度之研究即使與
曰本、韓國比較亦是不夠。不過,近兩三年由於奈米國家型計畫的推動,研究已漸顯成果。以
筆者為例,從本次會議過程中已經較少以往之失落感,同時亦感覺在研究上與國際一流團隊的
距離正快速接近中。因此，我相信在充分支持其研究經費下，國內研究成果必定有更進一步
的成效，也必定能加速縮小國內外之研究差距。 
 
一、奈米科技已是研究的潮流，如何在這個剛起步不算太久的領域中找出適合我們發展的少
數領域，應是值得深思的問題。 
 
 
二、如何整合國內已有的奈米研究團隊與設備，應是首先的迫切工作。 
 
 
三、在給與相對的人力與經費後逐年針對計劃成果的追蹤考核也是奈米研究能否開花結果的
關健。 
 
我國擁有世界上十分之三的半導体產量，卻只有十分之一的產質。其原因我想大多數的人都
知道。如何避免在這次奈米競賽中得到同一下場呢？我想研究環境與研究態度的建立是帶領
我們跳出此一能障的不二法門。目前國家在奈米計畫的推動似乎成功可行，但如何擬定長遠
穩定的方向,確切執行應是主管單位與學術界人士隨時注意的。
