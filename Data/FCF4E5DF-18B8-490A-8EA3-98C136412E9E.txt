 摘要 
 
生物薄膜系統為近年廢水生物處理中一嶄新的研究，由於利用薄膜單元取代傳統的沉
澱池，使其處理效能大幅提升，其中處理水更具回收再利用的潛能，因此實具發展的潛能。
然而薄膜積垢卻大大的限制生物薄膜反應器的廣泛應用，薄膜積垢會導致薄膜通量的衰
減，使得薄膜須時常清洗或更換，如此便增加生物薄膜程序的操作及維護成本。本研究探
討生物薄膜系統於次臨界通量操作下污泥特性及水流剪力對薄膜積垢的影響。結果顯示，
生物薄膜系統於次臨界通量操作下薄膜積垢的速率很慢，但在生物反應槽中若絲狀菌大量
的增生則會因汙泥中多醣類大量產生而導致薄膜積垢現象的急劇發生。研究發現薄膜積垢
的程度與胞外聚合物中多醣類的含量呈現高度的相關性。在批次試驗中阻抗的產生主要為
濾餅(汙泥中較大的顆粒，例如：膠羽，所造成的積垢)所造成，在操作上可藉由增加水流剪
力來減少濾餅所造成的阻抗。然而水流剪力對於汙泥中較小的顆粒，例如：膠體及溶解性
物質所造成的阻抗卻無明顯的影響。因此，汙泥中的巨分子物(例如：多醣類)在次臨界通量
操作下為薄膜積垢的重要影響因子。

一 、前言 
隨著日益趨嚴的放流水法規標準及水資源保護的重視，生物薄膜反應器在近年來受到
廣泛的研究及應用，其特性在於傳統的活性污泥程序中加入薄膜單元合成為一新的處理技
術，一方面仍保有生物處理的傳統功能，另外更加上薄膜本身有效固液分離的特性，其出
流水水質有達到水回收再利用標準之潛能，並易於自動化控制及減少廢水處理廠之佔地面
積。近年新加坡政府已成功將生活污水回收再利用於飲用水水源(稱為新生水, Newater)，其
所憑藉的技術便是一系列的生物及薄膜技術。儘管生物薄膜程序有眾多優點，但是薄膜的
積垢卻限制了生物薄膜反應器的廣泛利用。薄膜積垢的發生會使得薄膜通量衰減或透膜壓
力 (transmembrane pressure, TMP)增加，導致清洗薄膜的次數增多而縮短薄膜壽命，提高操
作及維護成本。薄膜積垢之特性和程度會受到薄膜性質、污泥特性及操作條件三種因素所
影響(Chang et al., 2002)。為了使MBR能長時間的連續操作，一般會將其於次臨界通量(sub-critical 
flux)下操作，以避免沉積物於薄膜表面生成。但是研究指出，即使在次臨界通量下操作，
經過一段時間後(critical time, tcrit)，TMP仍舊會快速地增加(Kaichang et al., 2003；Ognier et al., 2004；Pollice 
et al., 2005)。因此本研究將MBR於次臨界通量操作下，監測污泥性質與 TMP之變化。再藉由
截流式過濾系統 (dead-end stirred cell system)，模擬MBR中積垢行為，進而探討薄膜積垢
影響因子及其阻塞成因。 
 
二、研究目的 
本研究目的主要可分為兩部份，一為觀察MBR於次臨界通量操作下，污泥性質與 TMP
之變化，另一為利用截流式過濾系統，配合取自MBR之污泥，模擬MBR中積垢行為，進
而探討薄膜積垢影響因子及其阻塞成因。 
 
三、文獻探討 
在所有條件中，操作通量或 TMP 之大小為影響薄膜操作成敗最重要的因素(Nagaoka et al., 
1998)。Field et al.,(1995)定義臨界通量(critical flux, Jc)為MF 於掃流式過濾時存有之ㄧ通量，
當操作通量小於 Jc時，無衰減的情況發生，反之，若大於 Jc時則有積垢產生；臨界通量的
大小和系統水力條件(hydrodynamics)及眾多因素有關。臨界通量之定義可分為兩類：
(1)J/∆PT (permeability, K)等於清水操作之值(strong form) (2)J/∆PT維持一常數(weak form)。
一般在薄膜過濾的程序中，以後者之定義較易被達到。 
後續有許多學者提出臨界通量不同的概念，如：Howell(1995)指出，在臨界通量下操作，
膠體物質不會沉積在薄膜上；Chen et al.,(1997)指出，在臨界通量下操作，其值之增大或減
小，TMP 均可回復；Liu et al.,(2000)指出，在用顯微鏡觀察下，物質開始沉積於薄膜表面
之通量稱為臨界通量；Chang et al.,(2002)指出，以 TMP與通量做圖，斜率快速增加的那點
即為臨界通量。臨界通量最簡單的操作定義：為了使系統有效地運作，所施加的最大通量，
且於此情況下很少或幾乎沒有薄膜積垢發生(Choi and Dempsey, 2005)。臨界通量之量測可用來做為
水廠決定操作通量的依據，相當於傳統淨水程序中瓶杯試驗之功能(Choi and Dempsey, 2005)。理論
上來說，當溶液中顆粒受力達平衡時，此時之通量稱為臨界通量。在過濾系統中，顆粒移
動之方向主要受兩力影響，一是過濾所產生的拉力(permeation drag)，當操作通量或顆粒越
大時，受力越大，另一為顆粒脫離薄膜之力量(back-transport)，其是由濃度梯度或掃流式操
作所產生的，當掃流速度或顆粒越大時，受力越大(Bae and Tak, 2005)。

七、計畫成果自評 
本研究針對不同污泥性質(正常膠羽與絲狀菌)於次臨界通量操作下薄膜積垢特性的探
討。發現MBR系統於次臨界通量操作下EPS為中重要的積垢物，尤其是EPS中的多醣類更是
造成薄膜積垢的重要物質。文獻指出，傳統活性污泥系統中污泥的沉降性為主要影響出流
水水質之重要關鍵，但在MBR系統中因為以薄膜取代沉澱池，使得污泥沉降性不再是其限
制因子。但本研究結果顯示，儘管污泥膨化，MBR仍可維持良好的出流水水品質，但卻會
因為分泌大量的EPS而導致薄膜嚴重的積垢，導致MBR系統的操作失敗。因此，污泥特性
在MBR系統操作中，仍是一重要的操作參數。此發現對於MBR在實場的應用上提供了重要
的操作依據，在操作過程中控制污泥的特性，以減緩薄膜積垢的速率，提升薄膜的應用性、
經濟性，進而促成MBR的發展與普及。本研究計畫已完成一位碩士班學生的畢業論文並發
表於第四屆國際水協薄膜研討會，且經審查後獲Water Science and Technology期刊的接受通
知。

Among them, the imposed flux is the key parameter (Nagaoka et al., 1998).  Critical flux (Jc) is the 
flux below which membrane fouling can be avoided in cross-flow filtration.  Critical flux is 
affected by hydraulic condition and other parameters (Field et al., 1995).  In order to lengthen the 
operation period of MBR, operation under critical flux is often one of the strategies because it 
reduces the deposition of particles on/into membrane surface.  However, TMP increment is still 
observed after a critical time (tcrit) even under sub-critical flux operation (Kaichang et al., 2003, 
Ognier et al., 2004 and Pollice et al., 2005).  In this study, the characteristics of membrane fouling 
under subcritical operation was investigated and the fouling mechanism was studied by using a 
dead-end stirred cell system.  
Methods 
The MBR system comprised a bioreactor (30 L aerated tank) with submerged flat sheet MF 
modules (Kubota, hydrophilic polypropylene membrane of 0.4 µm pore size).  Concentrated 
simulated municipal wastewater was pumped into the bioreactor continuously at a constant rate to 
the MBR.  The operational condition was as follows: Organic loading, 1.2 kg COD/m3/day; HRT, 
8.3 h; SRT, 10 days; DO, 6 ~7 mg/L; pH, 6.8 ~ 7.2.  
 
The measurement of critical flux was conducted by flux stepping method after acclimation of 
sludge for at least 3 SRT.  The initial flux was operated at 12 L/m2h and maintained for 15 min 
(step height) before each increment in flux (6 L/m2h), and TMP was monitored. 
 
The amount of EPS was the sum of polysaccharides and proteins in this study and the total EPS is 
the sum of the supernatant EPS and extractable EPS.  The EPS extraction method followed the 
formaldehyde plus NaOH extraction method (Fang et al., 2002). 
 
A dead-end stirred cell system was established to analyze the contribution of sludge fractions and 
the effect of shear stress on membrane fouling.  The feed vessel was pressured by a nitrogen 
cylinder to push the influent through the membrane located under the small stirred cell.  A stirring 
bar located 3 cm above the bottom produced the shear stress. 
 
Resistance-in-series model was used to measure the resistance.  The sludge was separated 
into suspended solids, colloids and solutes.  The total resistance was assumed to equal to the 
sum of the three resistances caused by three components of the sludge: suspended solids, 
colloids and solutes.  
 
The sludge was centrifuged (4000 rpm) at 4℃ to separate the suspended solids and the 
supernatant .    The supernatant was then filtrated by 0.45 µm membrane (Advantech), and 
the effluent was considered the solutes.

Figure 4 shows the TMP profile under sub-critical flux operation.  The TMP increased quickly in 
the initial period, which could be due to the over growth of filamentous in the reactor.  The TMP 
dropped immediately after the aerobic selector was established, by which the MBR was operated 
for 100 days without serious fouling (data are not shown here).  Several references have 
discovered that even when MBRs were operated under sub-critical flux, TMP would still increase 
dramatically after a specific period (tcrit).  This phenomenon could be attributed to the adsorption 
or deposition of macromolecules on/into membrane surface, which reduced the effective membrane 
pores.  Meng et al., (2006) reported that if filamentous exist predominantly in bioreactors, the 
amount of EPS and the sludge viscosity would increase which then resulted in serious membrane 
fouling.  As shown previously, the concentration of polysaccharides in EPS increased with 
filamentous bacteria in this study.  Therefore, there seems to be a correlation between the 
membrane fouling and the concentration of polysaccharides.

In order to investigate the effect of shear stress on different sludge fractions, the stirred cell system 
was set at 40 and 100 rpm, respectively.  The result shown in Fig. 6 revealed that shear stress had 
great effect on Ras, minor effect on Rcol+sol, and no effect on Rsol.  As mentioned above, colloids 
and solutes were barely affected by shear stress.  Therefore, it can be concluded that even though 
the membrane was operated in sub-critical flux, membrane fouling could not be avoided due to the 
adsorption and/or deposition of colloids and solutes on/into membrane which might, therefore, play 
an important part in membrane fouling under subcritical flux operation.
