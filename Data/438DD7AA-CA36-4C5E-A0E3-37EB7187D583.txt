摘  要 
本計畫中針對異相鐵氧化物光催化 H2O2 反應與 Persulfate 氧化等高級氧化技術進行研
究，探討不同設計參數下處理 phenol、BPA 等環境荷爾蒙之效能。而在鐵氧化物對催化過
氧化氫降解酚的反應性比較研究中顯示，固定化的鐵氧化物（稱為 SiG1 與 SiG2）具有較
高的催化效率（可 100％降解水溶液中的酚）。當水溶液中含有酚的第一類氧化中間產物醌
類（如 catechol 與 1,4-hydroquinone）時，SiG1 與 SiG2 表面上的三價鐵可輕易的被還原成
亞鐵離子溶解出來。為了改善鐵溶出之問題以延長觸媒壽命故本研究亦提出使用光-流體化
床反應器，研究成果證實酚可在此系統當中成功地礦化（98% TOC 移除率）且觸媒表面所
釋出的鐵含量也大大的降低至 5mg/L 以下。而在 Persulfate 高級氧化技術方面，先利用一種
高硫酸複合鹽(peroxymonosulfate, PMS)所生成之高氧化電位的自由基(‧OH 和 SO4–‧)對
BPA 進行降解(化學計量比: [PMS]0/[BPA]0 = 2)。並鑑定於不同條件下所生成自由基之種類
及主導物種。研究發現該自由基氧化程序能部分礦化 BPA (Co2+/PMS 程序在 1 小時礦化率
約達 40%)。與相關文獻比較，本實驗所採用之活化劑和氧化劑為最小用量。之後再嚐試於
中性 pHi(起始 pH)處理條件下提出 UV-Na2S2O8/H2O2-Fe(II,III)兩階段氧化程序，應用於降
解或礦化 BPA。第一階段吾人利用另一種高硫酸鹽(persulfate，S2O82–)以生成高氧化電位的
硫酸根自由基將 BPA 氧化降解成較小分子(化學計量比: [S2O82–]0/[BPA]0 = 1)。接著再以
photo-Fenton 程序將此較小分子進行礦化，總礦化率可由 40%提升至 91%。 
關鍵字：固定化鐵氧化物、酚、Fenton、雙酚 A、高硫酸鹽 
Abstract 
    This project is aimed to study the heterogeneous catalysis of hydrogen peroxide with UV 
and immobilized iron oxides and the oxidation of persulfate process in the treatment of phenol 
and bisphenol A (BPA). Comparison study of the reactivity of iron oxides reveals that the 
immobilized iron oxides, namely SiG1 and SiG2, were efficient for the degradation of phenol in 
the presence of hydrogen peroxide (100% of phenol was degraded). SiG1 and SiG2 exhibited 
high reactivity for the reductive dissolution process in the presence of hydroquinones (catechol 
and 1,4-hydroquinone). To solve the problem of iron dissolution, photo-fluidized bed reactor 
(photo-FBR), was proposed. Successfully, the mineralization of phenol (about 98% TOC removal 
efficiency) and minimization of iron leaching (< 5 mg/L) were achieved in the photo-FBR. 
Moreover, persulfate process was also totally studied in this project. Firstly, the advantages of 
high oxidation–reduction potential of hydroxyl and sulfite radicals transformed from 
peroxymonosulfate (PMS) (stoichiometric ratio: [PMS]0/[BPA]0 = 2) as the oxidants were applied 
to oxidize BPA to less complex intermediates. The expected radicals were used to mineralize 
those compounds very efficient (TOC removal ~40% at 1 h). Secondly, a two–stage oxidation 
(UV–Na2S2O8/H2O2–Fe(II,III)) process was applied to mineralize BPA at pHi (initial pH) = 7. 
The high oxidation potential of sulfate radicals was considered and persulfate (stoichiometric 
ratio: [S2O82–]0/[BPA]0 = 1) was used as the 1st–stage oxidant to oxidize BPA to less complex 
intermediates. Afterwards, photo–Fenton process was used to mineralize those intermediates to 
CO2 (TOC removal was increased from 40% to 91%).  
Keyword：Immobilized iron oxide; Phenol; Fenton; Bisphenol A; Persulfate

過硫酸鹽 (persulfate) 此一強氧化劑已經被廣泛地應用在工業界，常被用來當作乳化聚
合反應的起始劑、游泳池清潔劑、毛髮漂白、銅印刷電路板的微蝕刻、以及最常被用來做
為TOC分析氧化劑[7]。近幾年來，才被用來當作氧化劑用於處理土壤、地下水污染之研究，
通常工業化生產的過硫酸鹽類型有Na2S2O8、K2S2O8、(NH4)2S2O8，其中又以鈉鹽(Na2S2O8)
最常被使用，因為其最具環境親合力[8, 9]。1956年，由Ball和Edwards兩位學者首先研究鈷
離子(Co2+)介質，作用於分解過硫酸氫鉀複合鹽(KHSO5)，之後陸續有些學者便著力於研究
此過硫酸複合鹽（peroxymonosulfate, PMS）系統的分解動力和機制[10]。 
Persulfate 鹽類溶解在水中會形成persulfate 離子(S2O82-)，其氧化還原電位(Eo = 2.01 V)
僅次於臭氧(O3) (Eo = 2.07 V)[11]，雖然說S2O82-對許多有機物質的氧化速率較慢，但是對於
整體“persulfate 氧化技術＂而言，乃主要利用此氧化劑被進一步活化之後所產生的中間衍
生物“硫酸根自由基(SO4-‧)＂ 來當作系統中所欲引用的強氧化劑，而其氧化還原電位高
達Eo = 2.60 V (詳見下表2-1) [12, 13]，僅次於氫氧自由基(Eo = 2.70 V)。 
而persulfate 氧化技術的最大優勢在於其可應用的pH 範圍廣，可以在酸性～鹼性範圍
下進行自由基生成反應[10, 14-16]，氧化有機有害物質，其實際應用效果更突破Fenton 
reaction 僅能在酸性條件下進行之侷限(因為鹼性下Fe3+會變成Fe(OH)3(s)沉澱)。因此，在適
合用於飲用水中低濃度有機污染物的處理。此外，相當值得重視的是在鹼性的條件下，
SO4-‧比‧OH 的氧化還原電位更高（如表2-2所示），亦即中鹼性條件下persulfate 是相當
有效的氧化技術。

4-1-3 光-流體化床反應器對酚之降解 
由於在利用固定化鐵氧化物來氧化酚的過程中會產生中間產物，而持續造成Fe(II)之還
原性溶出，因此更進一步設計出一水流式光－流體化床反應器，以鐵氧化物催化H2O2來礦
化酚並藉由紫外光（UV，波長365 nm）來減少釋出的鐵離子。而本部份研究所採用之觸媒
為SiG2。圖4-7為不同條件下（不加觸媒（UV/H2O2）、不加光（SiG2/H2O2）與光輔助催化
（UV/SiG2/H2O2））對酚及TOC之降解效果。而圖4-7(a)指出SiG2觸媒可有效活化H2O2進而
分解酚；另外亦可發現到反應初期之效率相當緩慢，原因為此階段Fe(II)與OH·生成之濃度
不足所導致。而當反應系統為UV/SiG2/H2O2時，可發現低降解速率的反應初期時間可有效
減少。圖4-7(b)為不同系統中之H2O2消耗濃度變化，可知當反應達240分鐘時，UV/SiG2/H2O2
系統之H2O2已幾乎完全消耗完畢。在TOC之降解效果上，其結果如圖4-8所示。經由
UV/SiG2/H2O2系統之反應，可知98% 的TOC (其最終濃度< 1.8 mg/L)可被去除。 
而降解過程之pH變化則如圖4-9所示。在系統的pH變化上，可知其pH由反應開始時之4
降至2.8，而其原因則為中間產物草酸生成所造成之影響（如圖4-10所示）。UV/SiG2/H2O2
系統中，其pH變化呈現先降後升之現象。pH降低之原因推測為中間羧酸產物所造成的，而
升高之原因則應為羧酸之H+轉換成H2O或被吸附於SiG2觸媒上，亦或有機物分解而產生之
碳酸所造成的（其pH範圍為5-6）。由於反應後之pH值較高，接近中性，故可降低用來調整

第五章 結論與建議 
環境荷爾蒙具有低濃度且高毒性之特點，傳統的生物處理法、吸附法、化學混凝法等
無法有效的處理此類污染物。本計畫中針對異相鐵氧化物光催化 H2O2 反應與 persulfate 氧
化等高級氧化技術進行研究，探討不同設計參數下處理 phenol、BPA 等污染物之效能。而
在在鐵氧化物對催化過氧化氫降解酚的反應性比較研究中顯示，固定化的鐵氧化物（稱為
SiG1 與 SiG2）具有較高的催化效率（可 100％降解水溶液中的酚）。當水溶液中含有酚的
第一類氧化中間產物醌類（如 catehcol 與 1,4-hydroquinone）時，SiG1 與 SiG2 表面上的三
價鐵可輕易的被還原成亞鐵離子溶解出來。而經由文獻回顧亦得知其他有機物氧化過程中
所產生之有機酸類亦會造成鐵離子持續溶出而減少觸媒壽命之問題，故本研究亦提出使用
光-流體化床反應器來克服此問題。然而，觸媒是否可重新再生（即再固定化）為未來一個
可探討的方向。 
而在利用高硫酸複合鹽(peroxymonosulfate, PMS)及高硫酸鹽(persulfate，S2O82–)氧化技
術來處理 BPA 方面，首先得知利用 Co2+/PMS 程序可在反應 1 小時內達約 40%的礦化率，
而 UV–Na2S2O8 程序效果不佳。但若使用 UV–Na2S2O8/H2O2–Fe(II,III)兩階段氧化程序來降
解 BPA，其礦化率可提升到 91%。此氧化技術之高礦化率未來應能實際推廣於現場使用，
亦或可應用為進入生物處理程序前之前置處理技術以降低生物處理程序之負荷。

means of oxidation and depth filtration processes. Oxygen and other oxidants, such as chlorine and 
potassium permanganate (KMnO4), are generally used for the oxidation of Fe2+ and Mn2+ [Ellis et 
al., 2000]. The solid products of oxidation (FeOOH·H2O and MnO2) are then precipitated or filtered 
through a granular bed, commonly green sand [WHO, 2006]. The main drawbacks of these 
traditional processes are the production of a large amount of sludge, the large size of precipitating 
tanks, the need for filters, and the need for the precipitates disposal. However, the first step in the 
treatment of Fe2+ and Mn2+ is the oxidation process. Aeration air is considered the cheapest oxidant 
for the oxidation process. The oxidation of Fe2+ and Mn2+ is strongly dependent on the pH value 
[Faust and Aly, 1999]. It is known that Mn2+ oxidation in the laboratory is slow (requiring days) 
when the pH < 9, and that the oxygenation kinetics are autocatalytic [Sung and Morgan, 1981]. 
Goldberg [Goldberg, 1961] first suggested the catalytic oxidation of manganese by oxygen on an 
iron oxide surface as a possible mechanism of ferromanganese nodule formation.  
Fluidized bed reactors (FBR) have been extensively applied in the removal of cations from 
wastewater. In a previous study, ferrous ions were oxidized using H2O2 and iron oxides were 
immobilized on supports in acidic conditions in an FBR [Huang et al., 2000]. It was found that the 
immobilized iron oxides effectively adsorbed heavy metal ions [Bailey et al., 1992; Edwards and 
Benjamin, 1989; Huang et al., 2007a; Huang et al., 2007b] and activated H2O2 for the degradation 
of organic contaminants [Hsueh et al., 2006a; Hsueh et al., 2006b]. Hence, the immobilized iron 
oxides were applicable for use as adsorbents and catalysts. The major limitation of adsorption 
process for the treatment of iron species is adsorption saturation. However, the immobilization of 
iron oxide on support media in an FBR can overcome the limitation of adsorption process. 
Furthermore, the advantages of iron removal in an FBR are that a smaller space is required, there is 
no necessary regeneration process and reusable byproducts (iron oxides) are produced. 
A new method for the removal of ferrous ions from aqueous solution has been developed 
[Huang et al., 2004]. Based on this method, the oxidant of Fe2+ was replaced by air in an FBR 
under neutral conditions. Immobilized iron oxide (SiG) was successful produced using this method. 
In previous work, silica sand (Si) and SiG were employed as support media to remove Fe2+ from 
aqueous solution in an FBR. Furthermore, the new oxidized iron species continuously immobilized 
on SiG and new immobilized iron oxides (FeOx-30) produced in the reactor [Lu et al., 2008]. In this 
study, the new immobilized iron oxide, FeOx-30, was employed as the fluidized media in the FBR 
for the treatment of the binary species, Fe2+ and Mn2+. The oxidation rates of Fe2+ and Mn2+, and the 
immobilization efficiencies of iron and manganese oxides, all strongly depend on pH value, so the 
variations in the pH of the solution is considered to be a major parameter. An optimum pH for total 
iron and manganese removal efficiencies should be found, and the system loading effect on iron 
removal efficiency is also a matter that requires investigation. The simultaneous oxidation and 
immobilization of iron and manganese species in an FBR is the main purpose of this study. 
 
2. Materials and methods 
2.1 Fluidized media 
Virgin silica sands (Si) were washed with deionized water several times and then treated with 
HCl (pH < 2) for 24 hours to ensure it was clean. Then, the silica sands were rinsed with deionized 
water and dried at room temperature. A new immobilized iron oxide, FeOx-30, was developed using 
a 10 L FBR in which ferrous ions were treated for 3 months [Lu et al., 2008].  
2.2 Experimental set up and Chemicals

measurements of dissolved oxygen (DO), temperature (T) and pH were monitored in the top of the 
FBR. Also, the samples were withdrawn from the top of the FBR with period of time.
