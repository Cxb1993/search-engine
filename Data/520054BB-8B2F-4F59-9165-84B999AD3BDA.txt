1.中文摘要 
本計畫使用2kW質子交換膜燃料電池堆作為研究對象並使用儲氫合金瓶作為氫氣儲
存裝置。因儲氫合金瓶釋出氫氣為一吸熱反應，當燃料電池所需氫氣量較大時，儲氫合
金瓶表面易出現結冰問題而使放氫反應終止。本計畫以風扇陣列回收燃料電池運作時所
發出之廢熱，並將此熱量以熱對流形式供給於儲氫合金瓶希望維持其瓶身溫度，以保持
氫氣供應。並且對於燃料電池輸出情形以實驗方式討論相對應風扇轉速、儲氫合金瓶排
列方式、以其訂出最佳運作參數，結果顯示若燃料電池運作情形為60°C、功率為
37.5W/cell、效率為60%，風扇流量CFM=348，以排列型式管陣回收廢熱，可回收約22.5%
之廢熱。 
 
關鍵詞：燃料電池、風扇、廢熱回收。 
 
Abstract 
The hydrogen is the major used for electrochemistry reaction in the fuel cell and 
generally kept in a steel storage cylinder. The generated heat by the electrochemistry reaction 
may cause a serious problem which influences the working performance of the fuel cell. Also, 
the electrochemistry reaction of hydrogen discharging from the cylinder is a kind of 
endothermic reaction which not only drops the temperature of the steel storage cylinder but 
decelerates the rate of hydrogen discharging. Therefore, it is essential for keeping the 
temperature of the fuel cell and storage cylinder moderate, stabling the fuel-cell cooling and 
recycling waste heat recovery to achieve the better performance of fuel cell system.  
By installing the fans array and controlling framework system in 2kW PEMFC stack, we 
may adjust the wind velocity to keep the temperature of fuel cell and storage cylinder 
appropriate with different operation conditions. Consequently, this succinct fuel-cell cooling 
and waste heat recycling system may have a stable operating situation and promote the 
performance of the 2kW PEMFC stack. 
The result shows that the performance of the heat recycling system is 22.5% under the 
condition of fuel cell operating temperature is 60°C, the power is 37.5W/cell, the efficiency is 
60% and the CFM is 348. 
 
Keywords: Fuel cell、Fan、Waste heat recovery.
