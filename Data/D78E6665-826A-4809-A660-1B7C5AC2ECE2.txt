Abstract in English and keywords: 
 
We provide an ending report for our project supported by NSC.  This project was proposed to study 
smart lighting and its application to photography with performance improvement.  The main 
approach is to employ the optical transfer function to analyze the full system and to explore the major 
features for performance improvement. 
 
Keyword: smart lighting/Photography

V.結果與討論（含結論與建議） 
We develop either an analytical or a semi-analytical model by the use of optical transfer function 
(OTF) and illumination formations and put emphasis on the study of imaging and non-imaging qualities in 
the incoherent imaging system.   A new approach for extending the depth of focus (EDOF) in an 
incoherent imaging system with a dynamically programmable shaped pupil using structured light has been 
demonstrated.  Our proposed research provides the connection between non-imaging and imaging systems 
for enhancing the image quality in the conventionally incoherent imaging systems such as light pipe, and 
photography, microscopy and projector with embedded illumination modulator.
