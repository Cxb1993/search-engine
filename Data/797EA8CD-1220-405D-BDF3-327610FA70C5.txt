一、中文摘要 
幾何代數或 Clifford-Grassmann 代數，
提供簡單及直接的方式探討空間機構運動
學問題。根據可動特性的類型，空間機構
大略區分為普通性、異常性及矛盾機構。
異常性及矛盾機構通常稱為過度拘束機
構。而矛盾機構為具有量或尺寸特殊限制
關係的過度拘束機構；有別於僅具有特殊
軸向幾何排列之仿射關係的異常性機構。
普 通 性 機 構 的 可 動 性 ， 一 般 以
Chebyshev-Gruebler-Kutzbach 或群論的包
含及包含於關係決定之。然而異常性機構
的可動性可以利用位移李群的相依性判別
之。為獲得空間機構矛盾可動的尺寸限
制，及克服大量符號運算的複雜性，本研
究成果以幾何代數將 3D 歐氏幾何元件及
特徵轉換為 4D 幾何代數元素及關係；接著
將運動位移表示為幾何代數的偶元素，推
導得整合旋轉及平移的位移運動變換通
式，用於探討空間機構矛盾可動性尺寸的
限制，並舉現有的矛盾機構:Bennett 基、
Delassus 及 Bricard 等機構為例，印證說明
非異常性過度拘束空間矛盾機構的尺寸限
制，以提供空間機構運動學探討矛盾可動
性之理論基礎及學術研究之參考。 
關鍵詞：幾何代數、異常性機構、矛盾機構、
過度拘束機構、雙向量、可動性、
尺寸限制。 
Abstract 
Geometric algebra or Clifford-Grassmann 
algebra allows studying and solving spatial 
kinematic problems in a simpler and more direct 
way.  Based on the mobility characteristics, 
spatial mechanisms can be generally classified 
into the trivial, exceptional and paradoxical 
mechanisms.  Exceptional and paradoxical 
mechanisms are usually regarded as the 
over-constrained ones.  The paradoxical 
mechanism is characterized by the specific metric 
or dimensional constraints.  It belongs to a 
particular overconstrained one and it is different 
from the exceptional overconstrained one which 
is only characterized by the specific 
axe-orientation affine geometric relations.  The 
mobility of trivial mechanism can be easily 
determined by the Chebyshev-Gruebler 
-Kutzbach formula or group-theoretic inclusive & 
exclusive relationships.  The mobility of 
exceptional mechanism can be identified by the 
group dependency of displacement Lie group.  
In this research work, we transform 3D Euclidean 
geometric entities to actual elements of 4D 
geometric algebra space by geometric algebra 
methodology so as to represent the motion 
displacements as even elements of geometric 
algebra.  Then, we derive the general formulas 
for the combined rotation and translation and 
further establish the dimensional constraints of 
paradoxical mobility for spatial mechanisms by 
using the derived geometric algebra approach.  
Moreover, taking the existing paradoxical 
mechanisms, Bennett-based, Delassus and 
Bricard mechanisms, as three examples for 
verifying dimensional constraints of 
non-exceptional overconstrined spatial 
paradoxical mechanisms.  The findings will be 
offered for the references of academic research 
and for the fundamentals of studying paradoxical 
mobility theory in spatial kinematics. 
Keywords:Geometric algebra, Exceptional 
mechanism, Paradoxical mechanism, 
Overconstrained mechanism, Bivector, 
Mobility, Dimensional constraint.

幾 何 代 數 重 要 運 算 之 一 稱 為 回 復
(Reversion)運算，以"+"符號表示；即反轉
向量的次序。經回復運算後，純量及向量
不變，但雙向量及三維的虛擬純常數
(Pseudoscalar) I (=e123)卻改變其正負符
號。在 3D 空間的幾何代數 G3，一般性多重
向量(Multivector)可以寫成 
M=α+a+B+βI (7) 
其中，α及β是常數，a 為向量且 B 為雙向
量。幾何代數的任意元素稱為多重向量。
對於所有的多重向量，相加具有交換性，
相加及互乘具有結合性，乘法對於加法具
有分配性，且唯一存在多重向量 0 及 1。任
意多重向量具有唯一的加法反元素。因
此，整個幾何代數空間具有封閉性，即任
意多重向量的和或積仍為多重向量本身。
根據定義，向量與叁向量總是具交換性，
虛擬純量 I 在奇數維空間與所有向量具有
交換性，但在偶數維空間卻具反交換性。
因此，得知 M 的回復以 M +表示為  
M+=α+a–B –βI (8) 
幾何代數另兩種最有力的應用為反射
(Reflection)及旋轉(Rotation)。在正交於單
位向量 n 的平面上，將任意向量 a 反射至
a′可以表示為 
a′=-nan (9) 
上式為反射的一般通式，適用於任意維數
的空間。利用向量間的純量積與幾何代數
純量部份的循環次序特性，很容易驗證此
種運算具保長及保角的特性。 
在兩個單位向量 m 及 n 所形成平面上旋
轉，可以藉由分別垂直於 m 及 n 平面的連
續兩個反射達成之。換言之，在垂直於 m
及 n平面上的兩個連續反射，將導致在m∧n
平面上的一個旋轉。根據簡單的三角幾何
關係，可以確認初始向量 a 與旋轉後向量 c
間的夾角為 m 與 n 間夾角的兩倍。而且定
義 R=nm(或 R+=mn)，故得旋轉的結果為 
c=RaR+ (10) 
此變換為處理旋轉的通式。適用於任意維
數的空間，亦適用於任意級別的多重向
量。將此 R=nm 稱為轉量(Rotor)，提供處
理旋轉方式，其對於幾何代數而言，是唯
一的。因為轉量為兩單位向量的幾何積，
故立刻得知 RR+=R+R=1。這也提供證明旋
轉變換具有保長及保角特性；可以雙向量 B
進一步表示為 
R=cosθ –Bsinθ (11) 
此相當於以單位雙向量 B 取代 i= 1− 的複
數尤拉公式。因此，以定義於級數的指數
函數，將其寫成 R=exp(–Bθ)。若回想此公
式是旋轉 2θ，則旋轉θ的適當轉量應為 
R=exp(–Bθ /2) (12) 
上式提供幾何重要性及獲得下列公式: 
a′ =RaR+=e–Bθ//2aeBθ//2 (13) 
此表示在 B 平面轉動θ，且轉動方向由 B 決
定之。而其反變換(Inverse transformation)
為 a= R+a′R。假設轉量 R1 變換向量 a 至 b，
再使用轉量 R2 轉動 b 至另一個向量 c 產生 
c=R2R1a(R2R1)+ (14) 
上式的合成轉量(Composition rotor)表示為
R=R2R1，屬於轉量的群組合規則，轉量因
此形成群，為連續群(Continuous group)或
李群(Lie group) [6]。

IV 以四維幾何代數探討矛盾可動性 
根據前幾節的研究結果，將一般三度空
間(3D)的歐氏幾何元件，如點、線、面、…
等，以 4D 幾何代數(簡稱 G4)的元素，如向
量、雙向量及多重向量等表示，擴充僅定
義於純向量空間的內、外積至適用於所有
G4 元素。因而有系統的歸納出幾何特徵在
G4 的運算數學模式，以 G4 幾何代數的偶級
元素表示剛體運動，獲得整合旋轉及平移
的位移運動變換通式，再將其應用於矛盾
機構可動性尺寸限制的探討。因此，以下
利用前述 4D 幾何代數 G4 及推導所得的運
動變換表示通式，以 Bennett 基、Delassus
及 Bricard 等矛盾機構為例，探討非異常性
過度拘束空間矛盾機構的尺寸限制，更進
一步驗證這些現存矛盾機構的可動特性，
且提供尋找真正新矛盾機構之参考。以下

3.  PRELIMINARY FUNDAMENTALS 
A group is a non-empty set endowed with a binary 
operation (often called a product) in the set satisfying some 
definition conditions [13-15] : closure, associativity, existence 
of identity and existence of inverse.  In the displacement 
group, the binary operation is the composition product of 
displacements.  By definition [15,16], in general group theory, 
the product of sets is the set of products.  A displacement is 
called also a rigid-body motion in mechanism theory or 
orientation-preserving Euclidean isometry in geometry.  The 
6-dimensional displacement Lie group denoted {D} is a set of 
point transformations.  In any orthonormal frame of reference, 
the displacement group is represented by a matrix group 
usually called SE(3) [17,18].  The Reuleaux lower pairs are 
represented by displacement Lie subgroups [3, 15, 16].  The 
set of relative motions produced by a revolute with axis (N,u), 
or a prismatic pair parallel to v constitutes the displacement 
subgroup {R(N,u)} or {T(v)}.  The identity transform set {E} 
corresponds to two rigidly-connected bodies and it contains 
only the identity E, i.e., the transformation that leaves 
unchanged all points in the space.  A more detailed 
explanation of nomenclatures is described in the previous 
section. 
A mechanical or kinematic bond is the mathematical model 
of a coupling between two rigid bodies; more precisely, it is 
the set of feasible relative displacements of one body with 
respect to the other. It is a subset of {D}.  A kinematic pair 
occurs if the coupling between the bodies results from a 
material contact between the body surfaces.  It is the simplest 
way to achieve a mechanical bond.  By choosing two rigid 
bodies in a single loop chain, we obtain two serial chains that 
connect in parallel the chosen bodies.  The displacement 
subset that is the mechanical bond between the two chosen 
bodies is the intersection set of the displacement sets 
generated by these two serial chains.  Let us consider a single 
loop chain with the six rigid bodies.  The set equation that 
explains the loop closure is  
{L(1,2)}{L(2,3)}{L(3,4)}{L(4,5)}{L(5,6)}{L(6,1)}={E}(1) 
in which the displacement subset of {L(i-1, i)} may be a 
subgroup {R(N, u)} if the body number i-1 is connected to the 
body i by a revolute R pair having the axis (N, u). 
Avoiding the misconstruction of local and full-cycle 
mobility is required. Local mobility corresponds to 
instantaneous mobility.  A local mobility has infinitesimal 
amplitude and, in fact, this is a geometric property that does 
not depend on the time.  The local mobility does not guarantee 
the full-cycle or finite mobility.  The degree of freedom (dof) 
of local mobility can be derived from the number of linearly 
independent kinematic screws or twists, which represent 
velocity fields produced by relative motions in the kinematic 
pairs.  Moreover, the local mobility depends only on the 
spatial positions of kinematic pairs.  But different kinematic 
chains can be built with the same system of joint axes and 
pitches.  In other words, for a given arrangement of pairs, 
infinitesimal mobility does not depend on the pair ordering in 
the chain whereas finite mobility may happen or change 
according to the pair linking.   
The dof can be regarded as the dimension of a set.  In many 
usual mechanisms, the motion of a body with respect to 
another body is a manifold contained in the displacement 
group and the manifold dimension is also called the dof of the 
mechanical bond.  The bond dimension is called also 
connectivity in mechanism theory [6].  Nevertheless, some 
special closed kinematic chains can produce a mechanical 
bond that is the union of two or several manifolds instead of 
being one smooth manifold. 
In the continuous mobility case, the displacement subset of 
relative motions between any couple of links has the algebraic 
properties of a smooth manifold.  On the other hand, in known 
cases of discontinuous mobility, the displacement subset that 
is the intersection set of relative motions between two specific 
links in the chain is not a manifold but is the union set of two 
or several sets, which may be distinct smooth manifolds, each 
of which having a dimension.  A mechanism with 
discontinuous mobility has singular poses that are uncertainty 
or change-point positions.  At these special configurations, the 
dof of the local mobility does exist and is the dimension of a 
vector (or linear) subspace of screws often called ″screw 
system″ in literature [6].  Nevertheless, passing through these 
singular poses, some different or extra global dofs are attained.  
Here, such a mechanism is called discontinuously movable 
(DM) mechanism.  DM single-loop mechanism can readily be 
synthesized when two or more subgroups that are independent 
are involved in the same chain loop.  Among them, some 
working modes prohibit the motion of the other ones and vice 
versa.
