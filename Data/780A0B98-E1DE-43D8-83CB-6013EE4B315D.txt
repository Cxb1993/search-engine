摘要 
 
本研究嘗試就應用於礫石層地質之施工方式（明挖及推進工法），以政府預算數量
編列方式，計算各管徑於不同埋設深度之施工成本，再藉回歸分析之統計方式推導相對
應之成本函數。本研究之成本函數，與現行台灣地區現有之成本函數作比較，其準確性
較高，顯示本成本模式可快速實際反應出工程設計之預算推估，有助於規劃單位年度預
算之精確編列，尤以台灣地區正積極推動以 BOT（Building Operator Transfer）方式辦
理污水下水道系統建設，若管線工程之推估成本可符合實際建設成本，將有助於廠商於
財務規劃時，計畫成本與收益之詳實評估，提供政府與廠商議定付費機制及委託服務費
用訂定之參考。 
 
關鍵字：成本函數；污水下水道系統；施工方法；礫石層 
 
 
 
 
Abstract 
In order to accurately estimate the construction costs of sanitation sewer system, this 
study analyzed the cost data of several sewer projects in Taiwan to develop cost functions 
based on the pipe materials and construction methods. The cost functions were built by 
regression analysis technique and were easily to use. The cost functions are able to efficiently 
and effectively estimate the budgets for constructions of sanitation sewer system. This study 
provides the decision makers helpful tools to draw up budget of construction designs, 
especially for the moment that Taiwan is carrying out many sanitation sewer system 
constructions by BOT (building operator transfer) policy. 
 
Keywords: Cost function, sanitation sewer system, construction method, gravel stratum

2.推進工法之成本分析 
推進工法有別於明挖工法，不採全面性開挖埋管方式，以設置工作井方式再將管線
逐一往前推進，由於所佔用路面積較小，對於環境衝擊（交通延滯、空氣及噪音污染與
燃料成本增加）相對降低，但其造價偏高與施工期過長為其缺點。推進工法之主要係於
埋設管線之兩端，構築與埋管深度相同之推進工作井及到達工作井，並於推進工作井後
方構築反力牆，利用置於該處之油壓千斤頂，在管後端一面將管線向前推進土層內，一
面將管內廢土以人力或機械挖掘、搬運出工作井外之施工方式（圖 2）。一般均於人孔
所在位置設置工作井，作為推進機具進出，及供人孔設置之用。本研究之工作井採預鑄
沉箱式（pre-cast casion construction method）（推進井 L×W=6.8m×2.8m、到達井

四、使用線性迴歸發展成本函數 
為了吻合實際工程建造成本，成本函數的建置需先依據不同的施工法分別彙集現有
的設計圖說，進行相關單價成本分析與歸納，因此本研究收集近三年來台灣中部礫石層
地質之施工設計圖說，以建置成本函數。 
其中明挖工法之成本將鎖定管線埋設費用（管材費、管座、挖方、棄方、回填方、
碎石級配、柏油鋪設等）及人孔設置費用（人孔材料費及吊裝費、挖方、棄方、回填方、
碎石級配、柏油鋪設等）。受擋土支撐方式限制，分別針對挖深 3 m 以下（木板檔土），
及挖深 3~8m（鋼軌檔土）兩者進行分析；而人孔則將依設計圖說，由施設管徑選擇適
合之人孔型式。 
推進工法之成本則鎖定用以作土壤支撐之工作井(推進井與到達井)成本、人孔（人
孔材料及吊裝費等）成本及推管費用（管材費、推進設備折舊費、推進費用等）進行計
算。

INTRODUCTION 
Municipalities have been responsible for planning and implementing MSW 
management, from collection and transport to treatment and disposal. Although 
facilities and programs for MSW management are already being operated by each 
municipality, the optimality and/or efficiency of the current systems is questioned 
when considering a larger multi-municipality perspective. Consequently, the 
strategies of MSW management operated among municipalities should be carefully 
designed to achieve the most economic and/or efficient solution. 
 
Economic optimization for solid waste management system planning was first applied 
during the late sixties1. Recently, considerable research efforts have been directed 
towards the development of economic-based optimization models for MSW 
flow/allocation from sources to treatment facilities such as waste recycling stations, 
incinerators, landfills and disposal facilities.2-9 Different types of mathematical 
programming models, for example linear programming, mixed integer programming, 
nonlinear programming, dynamic programming, and uncertainty programming, have 
been established for MSW management planning to assess the best 
alternatives.10,2-5,9-13
 
Several studies have examined the regionalization of MSW planning 
programs.2,4-9,11,14-16 These studies have made various suggestions for cooperation 
among different administrations under specific consideration. However, few of these 
studies have evaluated the influences of different management strategies, especially

ALTERNATIVE STUDY 
According to the current circumstances in the Taipei metropolitan area, this study 
proposed three alternatives from practical points of view to assess their economic 
impacts to the three municipalities.  The scenario of each alternative is described 
below: 
Case I: Each municipality treats its own MSW and incineration ash. 
Case II: Each municipality can pay other municipalities tipping fees to treat their 
MSW and incineration ash. 
Case III: The scenario is the same as Case II, but incinerator operating constraint, Eq 
11, are ignored.

CONCLUSIONS 
Linear programming techniques have been successfully employed in this article to 
solve the regional MSW management problems. The results of this study demonstrate 
that a regionalization policy is a good strategy for metropolitan MSW management. 
However, negotiations among the relevant authorities are essential for feasible and 
stable multi-county cooperation. Policies which are able to fairly balance the financial 
interactions between the authorities, such as adjustment of the tipping fees of 
treatment facilities or request for central government subsidies, need to be further 
evaluated.
