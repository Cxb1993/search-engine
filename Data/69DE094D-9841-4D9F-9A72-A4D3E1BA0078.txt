2摘要
本研究採用溶膠凝膠法(Sol-gel)來製作純度相當高、組成均勻的鋯鈦酸鉛(lead zirconate
titanate, Pb(Zr,Ti)O3，簡稱 PZT)壓電陶瓷微細粉體，以利往後壓電元件的製作。將 PZT 凝
膠粉末加以研磨，探討改變煆燒溫度、煆燒時間，而後改變燒結溫度、燒結時間，以阿基
米德法測其密度，X-ray 繞射分析鑑定其結構，SEM 分析表面結構，並將其極化，以求得
最佳化製程條件。於煆燒溫度 900oC、煆燒時間 30 分鐘，而後 1100oC 燒結 2 小時，可得到
密度 8 g/cm3 左右的陶瓷體。
Pb(Zr,Ti)O3 (PZT)薄膜是以旋轉塗佈法製作於 Al/Si3N4/Si (100) 基板上。經過 150oC 乾
燥，350oC 預烤，並以 600oC~700oC 來燒結，可將非晶 PZT 薄膜轉變為具有鈣鈦礦結構的
PZT 薄膜。本文同時探討膠體濃密及燒成條件對 PZT 薄膜的影響。在本研究裡最佳的製程
為以 150oC 乾燥 15min，以 350oC 預烤 10min，然後以 50oC/min 升溫速率升溫至 700oC 來
進行 30min 的燒結。所得到壓電薄膜的剩餘極化量 Pr 及頑固場 Ec 分別為 11.39C/cm2 及
84.52kV/cm。
關鍵詞: 鋯鈦酸鉛，溶膠凝膠法，旋轉塗佈法，壓電薄膜
Abstract
The sol-gel method was used to preparation the high purity lead zirconate titanate Pb(Zr,Ti)O3
(PZT) piezoelectric ceramics powders. To determine the optimum fabrication process, the
influence of calcined conditions and sintering conditions on the characteristics of PZT bulk was
studied. After calcined at 900oC for 30min, and then sintered at 1100oC for 2hr, the density of the
ceramics bulk nearly equal to 8 g/cm3.
The polycrystalline PZT thin film was preparation by using spin coating method. The PZT
thin films with lower gel concentration of 0.2M were used, and deposited on Al/Si3N4/Si(100)
substrate. After drying at 150oC, pre-baking at 350oC and sintering at 600oC~700oC, the
amorphous PZT thin films have been changed to perovskite-type crystal structure. The influence
of gel solutions concentration and heating conditions on the morphology of PZT thin films were
discussed. The cracking problem was alleviated by employing lower gel concentration of 0.2 M
with a drying at 150oC for 5 min and a pre-baking at 350oC for 10 min, and then sintering at
700oC for 30 min with a heating rate 50oC/min. The values of the remanent polarization (Pr) and
coercive field (Ec) are 11.39c/cm2 and 84.52 KV/cm, respectively.
Keywords : lead zirconate titanate, sol-gel method, spin coating method, piezoelectric thin film.

(A) PZT 粉體及陶瓷塊材
A-1. PZT 溶液之 ICP/MS 分析
為利於 PZT 塊材的製作，藉添加過量的醋酸鉛並以醋酸作為反應催化劑，改變各反應
先趨物加入後的回流時間，而得各種不同形態的 PZT 溶液，靜置後觀察其顏色變化情況，
並取微量的 PZT 溶液以感應耦合型電漿質普儀(ICP/MS)分析組成，如表-1 所示。由結果可
知:回流時間比 Pb:Zr:Ti=2:1:2 時，S5 的溶液呈透明淡黃色，無沉澱物產生，且 Zr/Ti > 1，
合乎須求。若偏離此製程條件則有類似針狀沉澱物產生，且 Zr/Ti < 1，肇因於 Zr 與 Ti 間的
反應並不完全，已使得 PZT 溶液偏離形變晶界組成，故有沉澱物產生。
A-2. X-ray 分析
將合乎需求的 PZT 凝膠製成微細粉末後，每次取約 20 公克粉末，裝在密封氧化鋁坩
堝內，以防止鉛的過量揮發，然後置於高溫爐中以不同溫度及時間來進行煆燒。當煆燒溫
度為 700oC，煆燒時間為 3hr~10hr，升溫速度為 10oC/min，再以 10oC/min 降溫冷卻至室溫
後取出，以 X-ray 繞射儀分析，得到如圖-1 所示的 XRD 圖。由圖上可知，粉體具有鈣鈦礦
PZT 結晶構造，但是焦綠石(pyrochlore)相亦伴隨產生，此時(110)面呈現寬廣繞射峰。焦綠
石相之存在是由於長時間的煆燒，致使 Pb 大量的損失所致。圖-2 所示，為溫度升至 800oC
煆燒 30min 後，以 1000oC~1300oC 燒結 2hr 時所得到的 XRD 圖，此時鈣鈦礦 PZT 結晶構
造更完善， (110)面分裂成兩個結晶面，即(101)與(110)面用時存在，於 1300oC 時焦綠石相
完全消失，整個 PZT 至此反應相當完全。當煆燒溫度升至 850oC 以上，而後以 1100oC 燒
結 2hr 時，焦綠石相完全消失，如圖-3 所示。由以上 XRD 的分析可知：焦綠石相為形成鈣
鈦礦 PZT 相的必須過程，而且可在較高的燒結溫度完全轉變為鈣鈦礦相。
A-3. 密度的測量分析
選擇煆燒溫度 700oC 及 900oC 的粉末，壓成直徑 12mm、厚度 2mm 的塊材，於不同燒
結溫度下測量密度的變化，如圖-4 所示。由結果顯示：當燒結時間增加時，雖可增加 PZT
晶粒的成長，但亦造成鉛的揮發，所以緻密性下降造成整體的密度亦隨之降低。煆燒溫度
900oC、時間 30min，燒結溫度 1100oC、時間 2hr 時，可得最大密度 7.93 g/cm3。可知於較
6升溫至 700oC 來進行 30min 的燒結，上述步驟重複三次。圖-10(b)的製程是以 150oC 乾燥，
以 350oC 預烤，此一步驟重複三次，然後以 50oC/min 升溫速率升溫至 700oC 來進行 30min
的燒結。圖-10(c)的製程與圖-10(b)相似，但升溫速率為 5oC/min。由圖-10 的結果可知，不
同的處理方式，會得到不同的焦綠石相含量。對圖-10(a)、(b)及(c)而言，其焦綠石相含量
分別為 4.03%、0%及 4.40%。
圖-11 所示為以 150oC 乾燥 15min，以 350oC 預烤 10min，然後以 50oC/min 升溫速率升
溫至 700oC 來進行 30min 的燒結所得到壓電薄膜的 P~E 曲線。此一薄膜的剩餘極化量 Pr

結論
以溶膠凝膠法所配製的鋯鈦酸鉛(PbZr0.53Ti0.47O3)(PZT)壓電陶瓷，改變燒結條件對 PZT
之特性影響甚大。當煆燒溫度升至 850oC 以上，而後於 1100oC 燒結 2hr 時，焦綠石相完全
消失，可知焦綠石相為形成鈣鈦礦 PZT 相的必須過程，而且可在較高的煆燒溫度消除焦綠
石相。當燒結溫度、燒結時間增加時，雖可增加 PZT 晶粒的成長，但亦造成鉛的揮發，焦
綠石相亦伴隨發生，所以緻密性下降造成整體的密度亦隨之降低。當煆燒溫度 900oC、時
間 30min，燒結溫度 1100oC、時間 2hr 時，可得最大密度 7.93 g/cm3。可知於較高煆燒溫度、
適當的燒結溫度及燒結時間時，PZT 粉末間流動性佳反應完全且緻密性高，所以密度增加。
由於整體的顆粒非常微細與均勻，並且成功的降低較傳統方法燒結溫度近 100~200oC，有
利於往後應用在微細高頻通訊元件的製作。
多晶 PbZr0.53Ti0.47O3 (PZT) 薄膜可利用溶膠-凝膠法製作於 Al/Si3N4/Si (100)基板上. 為
了要得到均勻且裂縫少的 PZT 薄膜，必須要適當調整凝膠的莫爾濃度、燒結溫度、升溫速
率等因素，同時在高溫處理時必須加入適為的 Pb 補償。在形成鈣鈦礦 PZT 相之前，會存
在有焦綠石相，但經過適當的燒結條件可以使焦綠石相完全消失。適當的製程控制可以得
到裂縫少的 PZT 薄膜，但因 PbO 的揮發，所以在薄膜的表面仍然存在有空孔。在本研究
裡最隹的製程為以 150oC 乾燥 15min，以 350oC 預烤 10min，然後以 50oC/min 升溫速率升
溫至 700oC 來進行 30min 的燒結。所得到壓電薄膜的 P~E 曲線較差，主要是因 PZT 薄膜
仍然存在有空孔，因此如何消除這些空孔，且製成特性良好的壓電膜將是本研究第二年的
工作重點。

3Introduction
For ferroelectric materials have ABO3 basic structure, perovskite-type.
Ferroelectric state possessed the coexistence of both ferroelectric phases FT and FR, by
splitting of (100), (110), (210) and (211) looked alike, where FT and FR were
designated to tetragonal ferroelectric phase and rhombohedral ferroelectric phase,
respectively. The ability of certain crystalline materials to develop an electric charge
proportional to a mechanical stress is known as piezoelectricity, discovered by Pierre
and Jacques Curie in 1880 [1]. Basically, all piezoelectric materials lack a center of
symmetry, hence, piezoelectricity is inherently an anisotropic phenomenon, and can

functions discovered by Jaffe et al. [2] and the basic components are the ferroelectric

known to have superior dielectric, elastic and piezoelectric properties including a high
electromechanical coupling factor, large spontaneous polarization, high dielectric
constant, low dielectric loss and low internal stress, and so on[3]. Ceramic processing
has been made to control the amount, uniformity and distribution of dopants by
modifying their ceramic characteristics. Modifications of the PZT materials and the
effects of dopants had been researched extensively to improve its piezoelectric
properties for various applications in gas sensor [4], MEMS devices [5-7],
ferroelectric random access memory (FRAM) [8], infrared detectors, actuators,
piezoelectric resonators, transducers, and micropositioners, etc [9-10]. Therefore, PZT
piezoelectric materials have become one of the most promising candidates for device
applications. Characteristics of donor doped PZT ceramics are termed as “soft”PZT
ceramic, for example, Nb+5, W+6 and Ta+5 substitute (Zr, Ti)+4, or La+3 , Sc+3,Mg+2
substitute Pb+2. Numbers of donor dopants (e.g., Nb+5, Ta+5) had been studied the
effects on features of PZT piezoceramics, in our earlier studies also reported Nb
doping as a donor dopant, the electromechanical coupling factor of PZT ceramics was
improved.

Samples for various PbO excess(wt%) were designated S0、S1、S3、S5. Calcined
powders at 850oC for 2 h were ground and die pressed into disk samples, and then
sintered at 1280oC for 2 h. Density variations of S0、S1、S3 PZT bulk sample could
obtain higher density and the relative value was 7.9g/cm3 close to 98% of the
theoretical.However,S5 density dropped to 7.5g/cm3. The volatility of Pb tends to be
deficient in PZT ceramics during firing at higher temperatures, thereby forming the
pyrochlore phase in the PZT ceramic, so suitable excess of Pb added to the bulk and
Pb atmosphere to compensate for the loss of Pb were necessary.
Fig. 1 appeared XRD spectra of PZT ceramics calcined at 850oC for 2 h, sintered
at 1280oC for 2 h. It showed the coexistence of both ferroelectric phases FT and FR in
all cases except S5, by splitting of (100), (110), (200), (210) and (211) looked alike,
where FT, FR was designated to tetragonal ferroelectric phase and rhombohedral
ferroelectric phase, respectively. Diffraction lines split into doublets revealed that the
coexistence of both ferroelectric phases at MPB was not changed by the oxide
additions. However, the (110) plane intensity increased gradually from S0~S3 and
reached strongest at S3, but S5 decreased. It could be deduced that 3 wt% was a
watershed for the PbO vapor pressure evaporation and recondensation equilibrium in
reaction[12], the densification maximum. However, an adequate excess of PbO
compensation was necessary to form pyrochlore-free phase. Though pyrochlore phase
in calcining stage was existed, whereas it transformed to perovskite phase when PZT
ceramics fired at higher temperature. Unfortunately, larger amount of PbO incurred
Pb dissolved in the grain, so pyrochlore-phase appeared compatible with S5 density
was decreased.
