壓電超晶格極子之能量分佈 
The Energy Distrubution of Polariton in Piezoelectric Superlaattice 
計畫編號：NSC 96-2212-E002-209 
執行期限：96 年 8 月 1 日至 97 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：周元昉  國立台灣大學機械工程學系 
 
中文摘要 
本計畫研究的對象為多晶 PZT 超晶格平
板的極子行為，此極子行為來自週期結構中
機械波與電磁波的耦合。在某些頻率此耦合
現象十分強烈，在分析上必須兼顧 Newton’s 
2nd Law 與 Maxwell’s equations，將此二者聯
立的數學模式以平面波展開法來解出週期結
構的極子頻譜與場形，由所獲得的場形可以
求得極子的機械能與電磁能的分佈。本研究
並探討 PZT 平板的週期極化方法。 
關鍵字：極子, 壓電超晶格, 頻譜, 能量 
Abstract 
The research targets in this project is the 
polariton in polycrystalline PZT superlattice 
plates. The polariton behavior in piezoelectric 
superlattice is originated from the coupling 
between mechanical wave and electro-magnetic 
wave. Since the coupling is very strong in some 
frequencies, both Newton’s 2nd Law and 
Maxwell’s equations have been included in the 
analysis. The simultaneous equations is solved 
by the method of planar waves expansion to 
obtain the spectrum and field patterns of 
polaritons. The acoustic energy and 
electromagnetic energy distribution of 
polaritons are found from their field patterns. 
The method of periodic polarization and 
fabricating periodically polarized specimen is 
proposed. 
Keywords: Polariton, Piezoelectric superlattice, 
Spectrum, Energy 
1. 緣起 
對於人造的週期結構中，某些材料經由
特定的安排，同一頻率的聲波亦可以與電磁
波耦合。在 1999 年，Lu[1]等人將兩種極化
相反的 LiNbO3 鐵電晶體以週期排列成超晶
格結構，此超晶格結構的振動與電磁波耦合
後導致材料中各種長波長的光學特性發生變
化，如微波的吸收、介電性質的非常行為、
以及激發出原僅存在離子晶體內的極子。Zhu
等人[2]在 2003 年探討在此種壓電超晶格內
電磁波的傳遞，由於壓電效應導致的縱波可
以引起橫向的極化變化，材料在適當的週期
極化方向之排列下，可在某些特定的頻率範
圍使機械波與電磁波發生強烈的耦合，如此
產生一種不存在於離子晶體中的新型極子。
另一方面，當超晶格振動時會使壓電材料產
生縱向或側向的極化，此種經調變的側向極
化會發出電磁波與原激發的電磁波相互干
涉，在此情況下縱向的晶格振動透過壓電效
用可以與電磁波發生強烈的耦合，結果在微
波的頻率範圍可以激發出極子導致異常的介
電性質。Zhang[3]等人在 2004 年探討壓電超
晶格內極子的能隙。同年他們也研究準週期
結構的壓電超晶格內極子的行為[4]。

ABSTRACT 
A piezoelectric superlattice is formed by intervallic polarizing 
oppositely along one direction in a piezoelectric solid. Due to the 
strong coupling between acoustic and electromagnetic behavior, 
Newton’s equations of motion and Maxwell’s equations have to be 
satisfied simultaneously. The polariton behavior in the superlattice is 
studied by plane-wave expansion method. Both band structure and 
field patterns are calculated. In the vicinity of the center of the first 
Brillouin zone, strong coupling of acoustic and electromagnetic 
energies are found in both passing and stopping bands. The kinetic, 
strain, electric, and magnetic energies of polariton are analyzed in 
detail. Correlation between energy allocation and field patterns is 
investigated.  
 
INTRODUCTION 
In 1987, Yablonovitch [1] proposed the concept of photonic crystals. 
Due to dielectric modulating in space, this kind of crystals has a band 
gap that the electromagnetic waves are prohibited. The phenomenon 
was showed experimentally and the relation between the dielectric 
constant and the band gap was explained by Yablonovitch and Gmitter 
[2] in 1989. By using the plane wave expansion method in 1990, Ho et 
al. [3] found the full photonic band gap in a diamond structure. They 
proved that a complete band gap in FCC structures dose not exist. In 
1991, M. Plihal and A. A. Maradudi [4] calculated the photonic band 
structure for a two dimensional photonic crystal with triangular lattice. 
They decomposed the electromagnetic waves into TE-mode and TM-
mode and got their absolute band gap respectively. 
In addition to photonic crystals, space modulated elastic structures 
were also studied. Similar band structure has been observed, but those OELECTRIC SUPERLATTICES

particular polarized electromagnetic waves. 
All of the previously mentioned works were focused on band 
structures and little attention was paid to the behavior of polaritons. In 
2007, Chou and Yang [15] studied the energy conversion in 
piezoelectric superlattices and showed that polaritons can radiate and 
receive electromagnetic waves. It implies the superlattice has the 
potential to be transducers. For getting better understanding, it is 
yearned to investigate their energy constituents in detail.
