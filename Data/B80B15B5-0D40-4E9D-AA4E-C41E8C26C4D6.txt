abstract 
The recent development in “intelligent building and life space” mainly focusing 
on the implement of technology based on the concept of tech-goes-first. Having this 
in mind, this design research is prompted via the reflective on the increasing situation 
of Hikikomori, to practice social care and preventive medicine. The project is 
emphasized with a view to reducing the extents to which the Hikikomori isolate 
themselves from the others, and enhance the active social care and enrich people’s 
interaction over a distance. The design work features on integrating the innovative 
design and the for-sight tech, such as affective computing, brain wave signal 
controlling system, and motion capture tech.  
The objectives of this research are. to develop a number of products and systems, 
potential allowing to integrate into the current “intelligent building and life space”, to 
improve the life of Hikikomori, and to enrich the life by creating the opportunity of 
active warm care, and walking out from isolation. 
Thee design concepts are proposed: city travel, pet runner, and travel badge.  
The applied technology includes AR, graphic recognition, bio sensor, GPS and micro 
projecting.
