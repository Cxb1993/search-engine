砷化鎵系列金氧半-高電子移動率電晶體之模擬與研製(II) 
The simulation and fabrication of GaAs-based  
metal-oxide-semiconductor high-electron-mobility transistors (II) 
計畫編號：NSC97-2221-E-214-063 
執行期間：97 年 8 月 1 日 至 98 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：李冠慰  義守大學電子工程學系助理教授 
 
一、 中文摘要 
承襲前一年經驗，吾人不斷修正已研發的
金氧半-高電子移動率電晶體的數值模擬程
式；並進一步將低溫的「液相化學輔助氧化法」
應用於砷化鎵系列(砷化鋁鎵、砷化銦鎵、磷
化銦鎵與砷化銦鋁等)的金氧半-高電子移動
率電晶體元件之氧化層最佳化與高頻特性製
作上。由於氧化層品質的改善，使得元件展現
出良好的飽和與完全夾止的直流特性且均能
達到增加閘極電壓操作電壓，增加崩潰電壓與
降低漏電流，甚至抑制離子衝擊效應，進而證
實可提供高功率元件之應用。由實驗結果發
現，經過「液相化學輔助氧化法」處理的高電
子移動率電晶體因為閘-源極電容 Cgs、閘-汲極
電容 Cgd 與表面復合電流減小的緣故，可增加
gm/(Cgs+Cgd)的比率，故表現出比同尺寸元件耐
更高的崩潰電壓、擁有更高的操作頻率與更低
的雜訊效果。 
 
英文摘要 
We have modified the reasonable 
MOS-HEMT model to predict the carrier 
transport within the 2DEG channel, and have 
fabricated the GaAs-based (AlGaAs, InGaP, 
InGaAs, and InAlAs etc) MOS-HEMT with 
oxide as the gate insulator prepared by the liquid 
phase chemical-enhanced oxidation (LPCEO). 
The high-frequency results show the stability 
and potential of the LPCEO for the fabrication of 
electronic devices. Good pinch-off and 
saturation characteristics are obtained. As 
compared to its counterpart HEMTs, larger gate 
swing voltages, lower gate leakage currents, 
higher breakdown voltages and improved impact 
ionization effect for the MOS-HEMTs make the 
proposed technique suitable for power device 
applications. In addition, the gate-source 
capacitance Cgs and the gate-drain capacitance 
Cgd of the MOS-HEMT is lower than those of 
the Schottky gate HEMT at the VGS with 
maximum gm. In other words, the reason for the 
improved of fT, fmax, noise figure and associated 
gain for MOS-HEMT may be partly attributed to 
the ratio of gm/(Cgs+Cgd). Besides, power 
performances are also improved for the 
MOS-HEMT, exhibiting its promising 
applications to millimeter-wave integrated 
circuit designs. 
 
二、 計畫的緣由與目的 
1978 年，首次 AlGaAs/GaAs 高電子移動
率電晶體 (high electron mobility transistor, 
HEMT)元件發表後，HEMT 元件材料及結構之
研究即蓬勃發展，相當多的研究團隊對傳統
HEMT 元件之製作與量測貢獻甚多，但尚無人
對真正的金氧半 - 高電子移動率電晶體
與 MHEMT 在 2.4GHz 之功率特性比較。由以
上實驗結果發現，此低溫液相氧化法對於在高
速、低雜訊與高功率元件的應用上頗具潛力。
目前元件之高頻特性/功率效能最佳化仍持續
進行中。 
 
四、 結論與討論 
本年度計畫中，我們將氧化物成長的參數
最佳化並應用在 GaAs-based MOS-HEMT 上。
所研製的 GaAs-based MOS-HEMT 能獲致高
速、高功率與低雜訊的優異效果。同時我們亦
利用自行研發的元件數值模擬程式來了解元
件內部載子的移動情形與修正實際元件的結
構以達到最佳效能。本研究群的相關研究結
果，2008 年共發表 1 篇期刊論文，2 篇國際會
議論文，2009 年共發表 2 篇期刊論文。

III. Results and discussion 
 
For device fabrication, the photolithographic 
process is necessary; consequently, the as-grown oxide 
has the opportunity to expose to the develop solution 
directly. In order to study the etching phenomena of the 
oxide in chemical solutions such as the developer, 
experiments on immersing the oxide into chemical 
solutions for a certain period and measuring the variation 
of its thickness by the ellipsometer were performed. Fig. 
3 shows a plot for the thickness of the as-grown InGaAs 
oxide without PR as a function of etching time in the 
standard buffer solution (pH = 10.0), the AZ 300MIF 
developer (pH = 13.25), and the diluted D-35 developer 
(pH = 12.80). The basis of the AZ 300MIF developer is 
TMAH, and the basis of the D-35 developer is NaOH. As 
shown in the figure, the oxide without PR starts to be 
etched in the diluted D-35 developer of pH = 12.80 for 5 
sec. These results seem to be similar with that on the 
In0.53Ga0.47As material by the same oxidation method. It 
clearly indicates the ingredient- and pH-dependent 
etching phenomena of the oxide in alkaline solutions. 
Figures 4(a) and 4(b) show the drain current density 
versus the drain-to-source voltage of reference MHEMT 
and MOS-MHEMTs, respectively. Clearly, good pinch-
off and saturation characteristics are obtained. The 
conventional Schottky-gate MHEMT may suffer from 
lower gate swing voltage and lower breakdown voltage, 
which may limit the applications of the device. On one 
hand, due to the higher energy barriers at the gate 
interface, the MOS-MHEMT can be operated at higher 
drain-to-source voltage VDS and gate-to-source voltage 
VGS than those of the conventional Schottky-gate 
MHEMT, which can enhance the current driving 
capability. On the other hand, the oxide film can also 
provide an improvement in the impact ionization effect in 
terms of the bell-shaped curve of the gate leakage current 
versus VGS characteristics [4], as shown in Fig. 5. 
Obviously, a remarkable increase in the peak gate current 
takes place when devices are biased at higher VDS. 
However, the peak gate current density for MOS-
MHEMT with 15-nm-thick In0.53Ga0.47As oxide is 
significantly improved by ~900% as compared with that 
of reference MHEMT. Thus, the electrons and holes 
generated by the impact ionization are decreased to 
further reduce the drain and gate current owing to the 
oxide layer, which is consistent with the results of Fig. 
4(b). 
 
IV. Conclusion 
 
A selective, low-temperature, low-cost, liquid phase 
oxidation of InGaAs using photoresist or metal as the 
mask has been proposed, and the application of InAlAs/ 
InGaAs MOS-MHEMT without gate recess has also been 
demonstrated. As compared to the conventional MHEMT, 
the MOS-MHEMT makes the proposed low-temperature 
and selective liquid phase oxidation suitable for high-
power device applications.
