摘要： 
本 計 畫 先 利 用 diphenylamine 與
4-fluoronitrobenzene 合 成 具 光 電 致 變 色 性
4-amino-triphenylamine (ATPA) 化合物，然後
ATPA再和acryloyl chloride行乙烯化反應形成具雙
鍵的4-acrylamidotriphenylamine單體(AATPA)。再
利用此單體與N-isopropyl acrylamide (NIPAAm)單
體共聚合製備一系列含Triphenylamine基團之熱感
應性膠體。本文主要探討AATPA單體含量對膠體
的物性、膠體的膨潤度、機械強度及藥物釋放行為
的影響。結果顯示，膠體中具有苯環基團的AATPA 
含量，比例由1至5mol%增加，膨潤度會由8倍降到
2倍，而在不同溫度下的熱應答效應也跟著不明
顯，機械強度及交聯密度則會隨著在乙醇中時
AATPA的量增加，而跟著下降2到3倍以上。另外
在不同溶劑下的膠體膨潤度也會隨著AATPA 的
量 增 加 而 有 所 改 變 。 此 外 在 本 研 究 中 將
NIPAAm/AATPA 做成共聚體並且溶於不同極性
的溶劑中，分別使用UV和PL分別測量最大吸收波
長及發光強度，但出現在不可見光區。 
關鍵詞：  UV irradiated；NIPAAm；Triphenylamine 
 
Abstract： 
The thesis includes two parts. In first part, 4-Amino 
triphenylamine (ATPA) was firstly synthesized by 
nucleophilic substitution of 4-fluoronitrobenzene and 
diphenylamine in this study, and then acylation of 
ATPA was carried out via acryloyl chloride to obtain 
4-acrylamido triphenylamine (AATPA). Then, a 
series of thermosensitive hydrogels were prepared by 
copolymerization of AATPA and N-isopropyl 
acrylamide (NIPAAm) by UV irradiation. The effect 
of AATPA content in the copolymeric gels on the 
swelling ratio, mechanical properties, and drug 
release behaviors of the gels was investigated. 
Results showed that the swelling ratios of the 
copolymeric gels decreased from 8 (g/g) to 2 (g/g) 
when the hydrophobic monomer AATPA content in 
the copolymeric gel increased from 1 mol% to 5 
mol% due to the decreasing hydrophilicity. In 
addition, the thermosensitive behavior obviously 
decreased with increasing of AATPA content in the 
gels, but the mechanical properties and crosslinking 
density of the gel swelling in the ethanol solution 
increased with increasing of AATPA content. The 
swelling ratios of hydrogels were changed by 
increasing AATPA content in the different solvents. 
In addition, the corresponding copolymers of 
NIPAAm/AATPA copolymeric gels, were 
synthesized. The UV absorption and PL emission 
were measured. The results showed that the 
maximum UV absorption wavelength and PL 
intensity of the copolymers and the AATPA 
homopolymer appeared in the invisible region. 
Key word： UV irradiated；NIPAAm；Triphenylamine 
 
簡介 
水凝膠是一種親水性的三維結構高分子。如果水凝
膠是放入水中，它不會溶解，相反地，它會膨脹並
吸收很多水。水凝膠的膨潤體積和膨潤度與其本身
和周圍的環境，包括溶液溫度，pH值，離子強度，
離子種類，甚至存在之電場有關。基於這個原因，
水凝膠可用於藥物釋放系統，藉由周邊環境的控制
來得到所預期的釋放速率。此外，水凝膠已被用作
人造組織，因為它們可以吸收和保持水分，使氧氣
及其他小分子營養物質通過。 
poly(N-isopropylacrylamide) (PNIPAAm)凝膠被稱
為溫敏水凝膠，在32℃的水中會急劇收縮，基於
此，其被廣泛研究於生物醫學、製藥、和其他應用。
但收縮過程通常需要很長時間，其取決於凝膠的大
小。假設其施加壓力並從內到外將水驅逐，則該形
成一層高密度表層在收縮凝膠的表面上，會導致顯
著地收縮率降低。最近，Hirotsu研究NIPAAm水凝
膠 /水/酒精系統的相行為，並解釋其 
thermoshrinking 是藉由破壞在水分子和NIPAAm
的NH或CO鍵之間的氫鍵。 
H. Masuhara and K. Sasaki 報 告 研 究 合 成
pyrene-labeled PNIPAM (Py-PNIPAM) 作為螢光
探針，顯示能優異的監測該聚合物結構。它們成功
地合成了高分子並利用光致發光（PL）光譜和激
光共聚焦顯微鏡下觀察螢光性質。Horie et al.報告
研究了一系列含 poly(N-vinyl carbazole) (PVCz)的
光導高分子。電致發光聚摻合物和含有 PVCz 光折
射材料作為電洞傳輸聚合物能應用成為原料，未來
可做為顯示器和記憶體。這個 PVCz 的電洞傳輸性
質能發揮重要的作用於摻合複合材料功能。這個功
能可能具有摻合複合材料的相關性與相容性。聚摻
合物的相容性被定義為在二聚合物同性質程度鏈段
獨立於這兩組成的比例。

結論 
在 這 部 分 研 究 中 成 功 地 製 備 了 一 系 列 的 
NIPAAm/AATPA 共聚物水凝膠和相應的線性共
聚物。在水凝膠或共聚物進行了膨潤行為、物理性
質、藥物釋放行為以及光致發光的研究。結果顯
示，越多的 AATPA 加入於共聚物膠體，共聚物膠
體的膨潤度會減少，膠體強度增加，在去離子水中
交聯密度增加。但是，當在乙醇中平衡的凝膠，最
大的膨潤度、凝膠強度和交聯度出現在共聚物凝膠
（AN1）中 AATPA 1 mo1％時，是由於溶劑對共
聚物凝膠的親和力。在這種凝膠體系，咖啡因的藥
物釋放行為和 indomethacin 藥物釋放行為顯示相
反的結果。此外，紫外吸收和螢光發射光譜的線性
共聚物出現在不可見光區。隨著溶劑極性的增加，
螢光發射光譜的紅位移更為顯著。

Abstract: Three series (D, T, O) of dendritic monomers were prepared from N-isopropylacrylamide 
(NIPAAm), acrylic Acid (AA), and dendritic monomers as crosslinkers. The influence of dendritic monomer 
content and different structure on the swelling behavior and mechanical properties of the three series 
hydrogels was investigated. 
 
Introduction  
Thermosensitive hydrogel, one of the environmental stimuli response hydrogels, is the most extensively 
investigated intelligent hydrogel at present; especially in the drug delivery system. 
Poly(N-isopropylacrylamide, NIPAAm) hydrogel is a well-known thermosensitive polymeric gel, nearly 
continuous volume transition and associated phase transition from low temperature, a highly swollen gel 
network, to high temperature. To maintain the three-dimensional structure of hydrogel, crosslinker was 
usually used. As present, poly(NIPAAm) hydrogels are formed by the covalent-crosslinking of 
poly(NIPAAm) chains with the dendrimer derivative. Dendrimers are a unique novel class of synthetic, 
nanoscopic, spherical macromolecules, which are composed of a central initiator core, branching units, and 
closely-packed surface groups. Poly(ethylene glycol amidoamine) (PEGAMAM) dendrimers were the first 
complete dendrimer family to be synthesized by using repeated sequence of protection-deprotection reaction 
cycles to produce a higher generation. Within the extensive literatures on dendrimers, comparatively little 
research has focused on the relationship between hydrogels and dendrimers.1 
 
Experimental 
Preparation of thermo/pH sensitive hydrogels (NAD, NAT, and NAO) series pH-thermosensitive gel. 
A fixed molar ratio of NIPAAm (70mol%), AA (30mol%) and three kind dendrimers and three molar ratios 
of PEG6 di-acrylamide (PEG/D-AAm), PEG6 tetra-acrylamide (PEG/T-AAm), PEG6 octa-acrylamide 
(PEG/T-AAm) (1, 3, 5 mol%) as crosslinkers were dissolved in deionized water to a total volume of 10mL. 
To the solution, 3 mol% APS and 1 mol% TEMED as redox initiators were added, and then polymerization 
was carried out at room temperature for one day. The influence of dendritic monomer content and different 
structure on the swelling behavior and mechanical properties of the three series hydrogels was investigated. 
 
Results and Discussion 
Three series of NA hydrogels containing dendritic monomers as crosslinker were prepared by 
copolymerization of NIPAAm and AA. PEG was used as the core of the dendritic monomers and synthesized 
by divergent method with C=C end groups. Comparing these swelling ratios of the three hydrogels, the 
swelling ratio is in the order NAD＞NAT＞NAO due to the higher functionality of NAO gel resulted in 
higher crosslinking density. The more the dendritic monomer incorporated into the gel, the higher the 
crosslinking density and the shear modulus of the gel due to the formation of denser structure. The increase 
in crosslinking density of the N series hydrogels would cause the gel network more compact and improve the 
gel strength. We found this phenomenon made the swelling ratio of the gel rapidly decreased at the CGTT
