（1）環境績效評估 
環境績效評估可作為策略或營運作業改善的有效工具，針對產品的生命週
期各階段所造成的環境衝擊進行詳細評估，如 Xerox 利用本身的資料庫及加入
生命週期的觀念而發展出一套績效評估的工具；此外 B&Q 針對供應商環境化
的程度來做環境改善評估系統；AMD 是利用資料庫來將一些環境衝擊因素的
數據標準化，再針對各個分公司在各年度的環境活動加以評估及改善。  
（2）綠色採購 
綠色採購的運作可藉著對供應商材料採購、零件或產品的限制，來減少有
害物的產生。產業因在供應鏈上下游角色的不同而有不同的綠色採購重點，在
供應鏈上游的廠商，如 AMD 只對一些辦公文具、設備來實行綠色採購；而在
供應鏈下游的廠商，如 IBM 則除了針對法規所禁止材料進行減量及限制，更
積極的完成一完整的限制材料或產品資料庫，透過網路和內部資訊系統連結來
對供應商採購之材料加以管制。  
（3）供應商管理 
面對全球運籌的競爭壓力下，如何管理自己的供應商以提昇在產品及服務
的競爭優勢，已成為企業所關注的焦點。大部分企業的做法為利用環境績效評
估、環境保護程度、活動或環境管理的程度來為供應商評定等級，選擇是否為
自己的綠色供應商，如 B&Q、Canon 及 Nokia 等著名企業即是如此，但也有
些企業有特別的做法，如 AMD 更進一步成立針對不同產業供應商的評估組
織，評估不同類型的供應商，而 Xerox 則提供環境教育方案來輔導自己的供應
商。這些例子都是利用網路和內部系統結合來整合供應商，或產生互動並加以
管理。  
（4）環境化設計 
環境化設計的觀念較普及，也是企業藉由環保產品而提昇競爭力的來源，
所以大部分的公司都已投入研究及發展，並進而取得環保標章。大部分的公司
針對能源、材料的節省、回收率增加及有害物的減少為指導方針來加以設計；
部分公司更導入產品在生命週期的觀念，甚至開發軟體來比較不同材料及過程
的環境化設計，使各方面考量更成熟。  
（5）產品回收 
由於法規及環境保護壓力下，產品回收幾乎是每家公司都早已投入的項
目，不過也因產業的不同使得回收方法也大大不同，一般公司的做法是針對個

A Hybrid Due-Date fulfilled Forecasting Based on  
Clustering and Decision trees
Sheng-Yuan, Hsu 1* 
1Department of Industrial Engineering and Management

No. 1, Chieh-Sou N. Rd., Changhua City, Taiwan, R.O.C.

Abstract -  In wafer fabrication, due date fulfilling is very 
important. How to timely delivering the goods to customer is 
very important. It will enhance customer service and 
competitive advantage. But the complexities of production 
system and re-entrant processes in wafer fabrication have 
raised the challenge for due date management. How to 
predict due date related performance is very important. In 
this research, a predict model of due date related 
performance based on decision tree has been modeled. Due 
date fulfilling performance will be classified to EE (extremely 
earliness), NE (Normal earliness), OT (On time), NL (Normal 
lateness), EL (Extremely lateness) based on the decision tree 
trained by a great quantity of historical data. Furthermore, 
different combination of order releasing rules (UNIF, TB, 
and WR) and dispatching rules (FIFO, EDD, CR, SRPT, and 
COVERT) have been considered in the simulation tests.
In the result of simulation and statistics, average rate of 
correctly classifying is more than 90%. Especially in the 
combination of TB*SRPT, UNIF*COVERT, and 
WR*COVERT, the rate are larger than 95%. Managers of 
the wafer fabrication plants can improve the decision quality 
for due date management and shop floor control by 
prediction the due date related performance. 
Keywords -  due date, decision tree, wafer fabrication 
. INTRODUCTION 
Semiconducting production is one of the critical industries 
in Taiwan.  The chip sets consists of miniaturized 
electronic components and their connections. There are 
five steps in semiconductor manufacturing process: wafer 
fabrication, wafer probe, device assembly, class test, and 
final test. Wafer fabrication is most technologically 
complex and capital intensive. Since the capital 
expenditure is extremely large, the quality decision on 
SFC (shop floor control) is very important for improving 
the system performance. However, it is very complexity to 
control the wafer fabrication process owing to the complex 
manufacturing technologies, re-entrant process, and 
ever-changing products yield. There are lots of researchers 
focus on this topic, including order review/releasing, 
dispatching, rework, and due date assignment. 
Due date assignment is the fist important task in shop floor 
control. The suitable due date assignment will improve the 
quality of SFC and enhance customer service and 
competitive advantage. Assigning due date is a difficult 
decision. As a job arrives at the pre-shop pool, a due date is 
specified indicating when the job is expected to complete. 
This assignment is complicated by the fact that each 
arriving job has processing needs on various machines in 
the shop, and by the further facts that each machine is 
experiencing different and varying levels of congestion, 
which change as the jobs flows through the shop. How to 
improve the quality of decision on due date and enhance 
the abilities to control the shop floor for improving system 
performance is very important. In the study, we will try to 
model an estimation model of due date related 
performance based on decision tree. The real time 
estimation will be used for due date negotiation and 
decision on shop floor control based on real time data 
collection.  
The remainder of this paper is organized as follows. The 
second section will summarize the relevant literature on 
due date assignment and decision tree. The third section 
will discuss the research methodology. The fourth section 
will discuss and analysis the forecasting. The conclusions 
and suggestions for future study will be included the last 
section. 
. LITERATURE REVIEW 
ADue Date Management in Wafer Fabrication 
There are four kinds of due date prediction rule, including 
direct procedures (conventional rule), Simulation method, 
analytical methods, and Statistical analysis (Chung, et al. 
1997). Direct procedures assign due date using such 
information as job characteristics, shop condition and 
dynamic shop condition (Smith and Seidman, 1993). This 
kind of methods is convenient and easily computing, but 
some parameters have to be pre-determined by other ways. 
Initially, due-date forecasting rules only considered job 
characteristics, like as TWK, SLK, and NOP. TWK is 
based on total works to forecast due date; SLK is given 
flow allowances that reflect equal waiting times or equal 
slacks. NOP predict due date considering the number of 
operations or steps to be performed on the jobs. For 
improving the quality of due date prediction, another type 
of due-date assignment rules was proposed for considering 
shop-status information. Like as, JIQ, where due dates are 
determined based on current queue lengths in the system 
(Eilon and Chowdhury, 1976). Besides, as the computer 
technologies’ advancing continuously, virtual 
manufacturing system will be modeled easily on computer 
for using on production decision. Simulation will be used 
for SFC analysis, including due date assignment. Vig and

. CONCLUSION AND FUTURE WORKS 
In this research, a predict model of due date related 
performance based on decision tree has been modeled. In 
this paper, due date related performance will be classified 
to EE (extremely earliness), NE (Normal earliness), OT 
(On time), NL (Normal lateness), EL (Extremely lateness) 
based on the decision tree trained by a great quantity of 
historical data. Furthermore, different combination of 
order releasing rules (UNIF, TB, and WR) and dispatching 
rules (FIFO, EDD, CR, SRPT, and COVERT) have been 
considered in the simulation tests. In the result of 
simulation and statistics, average rate of correctly 
classifying is more than 90%. Especially in the 
combination of TB*SRPT, UNIF*COVERT, and 
WR*COVERT, the rate are larger than 95%. Managers of 
the wafer fabrication plants can improve the decision 
quality for due date management and shop floor control by 
prediction the due date related performance. For 
improving the system performance, the managers should 
try to dynamic adjust SFC’s strategies. In the future, a 
dynamic scheduling system based on the prediction of due 
date fulfilling will be model.

本計畫透過問卷及訪談，針對中部地區織襪產業參與廠商進行探討分析，評估
整體產業競爭優勢，並透過競爭優勢分析模式，評估代表性廠商的整體核心能
力與產業定位，並依據其產業定位及競爭優勢，協助構建符合高運作效率及環
保法令要求的供應鏈架構，並透過供應商遴選機制的構建，協助廠商建立符合
現有競爭優勢，強化現有產業定位的綠色供應鏈體系，有效提升整體競爭力。
