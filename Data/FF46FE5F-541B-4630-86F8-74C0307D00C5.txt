information for diagnostic reference. To speed up 
computation and access efficiency, we use the mobile 
cloud computing environment to access the large 
amount of image and speech data in cloud databases.  
We use the video retrieval subsystem to access and 
track the patient’s profile and facial expressions. 
Audio data are analyzed via the audio retrieval

三.  研究目的  
在全球化的競爭下，人們的經濟負擔和生活壓力不斷增加，罹患憂鬱症的人
數隨之攀升，病患的年齡也擴展到各個年齡層。憂鬱症，也被稱為「心的感冒」，
雖然是常見的疾病，往往因為自身不自覺而忽略，或者一般人錯與精神疾病畫上
等號而不願接受，因而錯失及早治療的良機。導致憂鬱症的原因非常複雜，情緒
起伏變化和心情調適失去平衡等等都可能是致病的根源之一，因為憂鬱症所帶來
的健康問題和社會成本非常高。聯合國世界衛生組織更表示，憂鬱症在十年後將
會成為排在心臟病之後的第二大疾病。 
在外顯行為沒有明顯變化下，人們無法察覺自己是否有憂鬱症問題，等到發
現異常行為時，就必須接受治療了。治療憂鬱症的週期很長，從諮詢認識、接受
治療、癒後照護等非常辛苦。如果能夠及早篩選、偵測出徵兆加以預防或提早診
療，可以帶來很大的效益。但是傳統的問卷篩選作為輔助憂鬱症的檢測，是早期
識別憂鬱症一項很大的挑戰，因為通常用問卷取得的資料有準確度的問題，而且
資料蒐集、前處理與後處理的耗時與成本問題，導致早期鑑別的困難。  
我們研發一個結合影像與聲音辨識的憂鬱症醫療探勘系統，幫助使用者瞭解
自己的憂鬱程度，進而預防憂鬱症的產生，以及後續的復原與自我照護。為了能
加快計算與存取的效率，我們建立無所不在的行動雲端資料庫(Cloud Database)，
有效率地存取與處理使用者的語音和影像資料─特別是串流資料(Stream Data)─
並維持系統中各節點資料的一致性。之後，將這些錄音和錄影資料透過影像處理
擷取憂鬱症病患的臉部表情，透過聲音處理擷取講話聲音的特性來了解語音和情
緒的關聯性。經由這兩者的情緒相關性分析成果，可以協助我們認識使用者的反
應與行為、以及相關的情緒狀況。將所有的資料藉由資料探勘建立起憂鬱症相關
程度的事件，來回饋給醫生作為診療的參考，讓使用者知道其憂鬱症的情況和如
何自我照護，並讓家屬和醫事人員隨時瞭解和關懷病患的復原情形。  
本系統的實際運作是先透過影像擷取子系統取得人體輪廓與臉部表情，以及
透過聲音擷取子系統取得語音的特性進行資料探勘，來瞭解使用者的臉部表情、
聲音起伏與情緒反應等資料之間的相關性，分別建立臉部表情與聲音語調的辨識
模型，進而對應至情緒的關係產生有分類功能的雛型系統。整合可能患有憂鬱症
病患的資料分析結果資訊，依照相關性的程度做事件分類，再提供給醫生做為診
療的參考，系統進一步提供醫生的醫囑，讓使用者知道自己的憂鬱程度、照護方
法或恢復情況，並且讓家屬可以隨時查詢。

最鄰近分類法[9]是使用最直覺的方法來進行分類，就是找出此點最近距離

專業的背景知識，便不容易理解其意義，如果能將資料視覺化，則可以幫助人們
容易了解其中的意義。以分類演算法來說，在近幾年的發展之中，除了如何開發

因此許多學者，便針對不同分類演算法的結果，開發視覺化的呈現方法，如對支

為「悲傷」，以及它的起始與結束時間。但如果一段音調，同時有快樂跟悲傷的
情緒，有多種情緒交雜的時候，我們需要研究這些例外該怎麼處理，或者是交給
專業的語音辨識人員作診斷。將所有音框內的情緒事件辨識完畢，並記錄情緒的
起始與結束時間後，才能完成情緒辨識。

一個具備事件辨識之雲端影像探勘系統來輔助憂鬱症診療 
A Video Data Mining System in the Cloud to Identify Events for 
Assisting Melancholia Diagnosis 
黃丹琳 楊東麟  
逢甲大學資訊工程學系 
Tan-Lin Huang and Don-Lin Yang 
Dept. of Information Engineering and Computer Science, Feng Chia University 
 M0005720@fcu.edu.tw, dlyang@fcu.ed.tw  
摘  要 
在全球化市場的競爭下，人們的壓力倍增，憂鬱症病患的數量隨之攀升，而憂鬱症並沒有明
顯的外顯行為，導致人們無法察覺自己是否有憂鬱症問題，往往延誤治療時間。  
本研究目的是結合影像與資料探勘的憂鬱症諮詢系統，提供憂鬱症患者進行自我評估憂鬱程
度的一個平台，進而輔助憂鬱症的病情改善。在系統架構中，使用雲端資料庫來存取大量的影像
資料，並建構一個「影音雲」，以提升計算與存取的效率。 
這一個具備影像辨識之雲端憂鬱症醫療探勘系統，可以提供醫生診療的參考資訊，主要在預
防憂鬱症、幫助使用者了解自己的憂鬱程度，進而協助醫生指導患者自我照護。我們先透過影像
擷取子系統取得人體輪廓與臉部表情，來瞭解使用者的臉部表情與情緒反應等資料之間的行為相
關性，依照症狀相關性的程度做初步的分類。再整合可能患有憂鬱症病患的資料分析結果與相關
資訊，依照相關性的程度做事件分類，產生醫生診療的參考訊息，並且提供家屬有關病患的憂鬱
程度、照護方法和恢復情況，都可以隨時查詢。 
關鍵字: 事件辨識，雲端，影像探勘，憂鬱症，臉部辨識 
一、緒論 
在現今高度發展的社會中，隨著世界經濟與科技的快速發展之下，人們生活水準也逐漸提
高，伴隨而來的壓力也逐年增加，導致罹患憂鬱症的人口比例與日俱增，產生憂鬱症的原因相當
多，所以需要透過專業醫師才能診斷。憂鬱症也可能伴隨著其它問題，包括其他身體疾病或心情
受到重大影響，較嚴重者可能會自殺、傷害自己或別人，這些是我們必須要去面對和解決的問題。 
本研究主要希望能夠幫助人們預防憂鬱症的發生，可以及早發現憂鬱症，盡快接受專業醫
師的治療，讓人們避免掉許多精神上與經濟上的損失，也可以減少一些可預防且不應該發生的社
會問題；另一方面，對於憂鬱症患者，如果能夠提供醫師一個病患的長期歷史病情資料追蹤狀況，
讓醫師可以對病患的病況有充分的參考資料，以便了解病患的病情發展趨勢，並給予適當的專業
診療，對憂鬱症能有更好的防範與治療。

資料探勘的結果須要視覺化的呈現來輔助了解資料的意義，“A picture is worth a thousand 
words”指出了視覺化的重要，過去已有不少學者提出視覺化的優點及重要性[3] [24]；資料探勘
所分析產生的資料，往往都是相當複雜，若無專業的背景知識，便不容易理解其意義，如果能將
資料視覺化，則可以幫助人們容易去了解其中的意義。以分類演算法來說，在近幾年的發展之中，
除了如何開發分類演算法外，將演算法結果視覺化，讓人容易了解結果，也是研究重點之一。因
此許多學者，便針對不同分類演算法的結果，開發視覺化的呈現方法，如對支持向量機[7]、類
神經網路[11]，經由視覺化處理將所得到的結果加以圖形化、圖表化，以取代較難理解的數據資
料，如此使用者就可以容易的藉由圖表來判讀結果。此外，更有學者自行開發分類演算法並做視
覺化處理[25]。目前已有不少針對決策樹所開發的視覺化工具[12][16]，除了可以讓使用者容易的
利用圖形化的介面進行決策樹設定及資料分析外，更可依使用者的需求產生各類不同性質的圖
表。 
B. 影像探勘 
影像探勘 (Image or video mining)已發展多年，在學術研究方面，目前已有部分學者在對圖
像進行挖掘的研究，如從衛星圖片中辨別水中具有危害航行的水生植物區域[22]。 
Vector Quantization (VQ)  是一種很有效率的影像壓縮方法，但是大部分 VQ  演算法(例如  
SMVQ) 都只有考慮相鄰區塊來決定  Codeword，這將導致嚴重的偏移。[17] 利用關聯法則資料
探勘演算法找出相鄰區塊的關連法則，空間關係的關聯法則包含水平、垂直、對角三種規則，用
來預測影像區塊，以求得更好的影像品質。  
傳統的 Content-based image retrieval system，大都是利用顏色、形狀、紋理來做搜尋影像內
容的依據，但對於一般的使用者來說，利用這些低階的特徵來做搜尋是有困難的，而且大部分的
使用者比較偏好利用文字來做搜尋。可是目前影像的註解，大部分還是要依靠人力，非常的耗時，
如何對於影像下適當而且自動的註解是非常重要的。[4]提出一個方法可以對影像做自動的註
記，他們的方法是擷取出每一張影像的 Fractal features，再利用 Diverse Density Algorithm 做為訓
練分類的方法，讓使用者和系統可以做即時性的互動式學習，最後可以利用已經訓練好的 Models
對影像做自動註解。  
如何在影像表示法(Image representation)中表現物件間的方向關係，這個問題可以幫助解析影
像內涵意義。[5]提出 DLT Miner 演算法，將 Symbolic 影像中物件的空間關係分成 9 個方向，
利用 9 DLT Matrix 表示，並將 9 DLT Matrix 轉換成 9 DLT string，新的 9 DLT string 資料結構有
助於使用傳統資料挖掘演算法處理，並可得到物件間空間關係的 Association rules。9 DLT mining
是一個透過改進的資料結構表示法，進而可以套用資料挖掘演算法的成功例子。在應用上，影像
探勘的研究已有多方面成熟的應用，例如可針對相同性質的圖形來判別其中的細微差異，最為大
家所熟知的如字元辨識[19]、人臉辨識[15]等；近年來，在生物醫療應用方面，有如基因細胞辨
識、醫療診斷輔助系統[18]等；另外，在工商業應用上，有如公司商標專利辨識、車牌辨識等。
在工具軟體方面，已有一家 LTU 科技軟體公司(Http://www.ltutech.com/en/)，專門針對影像去做
搜尋、過濾，且已研發出如何針對圖像進行自動分類，如一大堆的圖像照片中，系統將能自動分
類哪些是人像圖、運動圖、風景圖等等。

Combining the above approaches, semi-automatic ontology 
construction method is the most common approach in which 
information extraction techniques are used under the 
supervision of human. Such methods include the learning 
modules to extract concepts and conceptual relations from 
domain documents. They require expert knowledge to verify 
obtained information and decide which information should be 
included in the ontology. In English, many frameworks and 
plug-ins have been built to help users construct ontologies 
semi-automatically. For example, TextToOnto [5] used 
generalized association rules to find out the co-occurrences 
between items and relations between them. OntoLT [17] is a 
Protégé plug-in that extracts concepts and relations from 
annotated documents for ontology construction.  
Typically, taxonomy is needed in ontology acquisition task 
to construct the concept hierarchical structure of the ontology. 
In English, taxonomy-based approaches often use WordNet as 
a super taxonomy to determine the conceptual relations 
between concepts. In Chinese, HowNet has been used with the 
same role in many researches. In case taxonomies are not 
available or due to other reasons, non-taxonomy approach is 
considered. For example, the concept hierarchy is constructed 
by learning algorithms as in [2][4][6].  
 To extract candidate terms, the well-known statistical 
measurement of TF-IDF can be used [2][7]. Association rules 
or frequent patterns are mined to discover the co-occurrences 
and semantic relations between terms [4][5][7]. Linguistic rules 
are also used in many researches [9][10] in which predefined 
lexical patterns were used to extract candidate concepts by 
bootstrapping mechanism. In Vietnamese, Chau Q. Nguyen 
and Tuoi T. Phan proposed a hybrid approach which combine 
lexical rule based and ontology based methods to extract key 
terms and phrases from Vietnamese text [8].  
III. PROPOSED METHOD 
We propose a system, called Vietnamese Text to Ontology 
– ViText2Onto, along with learning techniques to discover 
concepts and conceptual relations from Vietnamese text 
documents.  
Our contribution can be stated as follows. Given a set of 
Vietnamese text documents in a specific domain, the system 
should support users to construct the ontology using semi-
automatic approach. The result is an ontology that contains 
concepts and instances organized in an appropriate hierarchy. 
A. System architecture 
To construct ontology, domain knowledge must be 
discovered and organized in a conceptual hierarchical structure. 
ViText2Onto employs a semi-automatic approach where 
discovery methods are used in combination with human 
supervision. From this perspective, an interactive mechanism is 
established between the system and users where the 
construction process is iterative and cyclic. After each iteration, 
the conceptual hierarchy is extended and verified by users, such 
that the users can incrementally discovery more concepts and 
relations based on the accessed concepts. The system 
architecture is shown in Fig. 1, which includes the following 
components: 
 
Figure 1. System architecture 
1) Vietnamese Natural Language Processing Module is 
used to make the Vietnamese text documents ready for 
extraction algorithms. It is a set of Vietnamese processing tools 
to perform tokenizing, part of speech tagging (POSTagging) 
and chunking. The output of this module is annotated 
documents being stored in text files. A small converter is 
created to read these files and converts them into compatible 
format that can be used by GATE [12].  
2) Learning and Discovering Component is used for 
extracting concepts and conceptual relations from annotated 
documents. We use various learning and discovering 
algorithms, including pattern based, statistic based, and 
association based approaches. To implement pattern based 
learning, we use JAPE (a Java Annotation Pattern Engine), an 
element of GATE framework. JAPE provides finite state 
transduction over annotations that let us extract predefine 
patterns based on rules written in a specific grammar.  
3) Lexical Patterns contain lexical rules written by using 
JAPE grammar which are used for pattern based learning. They 
were constructed based on Vietnamese syntactic rules. 
Applying these rules on the corpus using JAPE, we can extract 
concepts and conceptual relations from the matched patterns. 
B. Vietnamese language processing 
We use Vietnamese language processing tools provided by 
the project Building Basic Resources and Tools for Vietnamese 
Language and Speech Processing (VLSP)[13][14] for 
preprocessing Vietnamese textual corpora. Processing 
components include the following features:

In our research, we use segmented sentences with chunking 
labels as input of sequential pattern mining. As the input 
sentences were segmented into frames which have specific 
grammatical roles, a concept is often belonging to only one 
frame. We consider each frame as a sequence, and each word 
as an item. By mining frequent sequences we can obtain word 
sequences that frequently occur together in a frame, and hence, 
they can be candidate concepts. For example: 
• Input: Điện thoại Iphone 4 màu trắng chưa được sản xuất 
• Meaning: White Iphone 4 has not been produced yet 
• Chunks: [Điện thoại Iphone 4 màu trắng]NP [chưa được 
sản xuất]VP 
Assume that one of frequent sequences is “Iphone 4,” we 
can see the candidate concept completely belongs to the chunk 
[Điện thoại Iphone 4 màu trắng]NP.  
After the mining stage, we only use maximal frequent 
sequences as candidate phrases. The set of frequent sequences 
is denoted by SF.

2)  Conceptual relation discovery 
In this phase, we use the combination of pattern based and 
association based learning to discover the relation between 
concepts. Using lexical rules in conceptual relation discovery 
has high reliability; however, predefined grammatical rules 
may not cover all cases of language usages. We take the 
advantage of association rule mining to overcome this 
weakness, in which, relations between concepts are mined 
without considering the semantic aspect of sentences. 
a) Lexical pattern based conceptual relation discovery 
In this part, we take into account the semantic relations 
between elements in sentences, as used in [8]. According to 
Vietnamese grammatical characteristics, there are some rules 
that reflex relations between nouns or noun phrases as follows: 
• Rule 1: {Noun phrase A} “là một” {Noun phrase B} → A 
is a B 
• Rule 2: {Noun phrase A} {Proper noun B} → B is an 
instance of A 
• Rule 3: {Noun phrase A} “có” {Noun phrase B} → A has 
a B 
• Rule 4: {Noun phrase A} “của” {Noun phrase B} → B 
has an A 
• Rule 5: {Noun phrase A} “thuộc” {Noun phrase B} → A 
is a subclass of B 
• Rule 6: {Noun phrase A} “bao gồm” {Noun phrase 
B},{Noun phrase C} → B and C belong to A 
• Rule 7: {Noun phrase A} (“và” | “hoặc”) {Noun phrase 
B} → A (and | or) B  
Based on these rules, we build a set of extraction rules 
using JAPE grammar. When matching process is running, 
matched concept pairs and the relations between them will be 
discovered. In this research, we focus on finding subsumption 
relations and instances of concepts. The set of lexical rules 
contains many language usage cases that imply isA and hasA 
relations. Building a complete set of rules seems not feasible, 
however, the rule sets can be enriched in further study.  
Heuristic 
Context implication: if A is a concept and B appears with 
A in the context {A} (“và” | “hoặc”) {B}, we can infer that: i) 
B is also a concept and ii) A and B have the same level of 
abstraction. For example: 
• Điện thoại HTC HerovàMotorola Milestone đều được cài 
đặt hệ điều hành Android 2.1 
• Both HTC HeroandMotorola Milestone are installed with 
the operating system Android 2.1 
In this context, if we already know HTC Hero is a concept 
mentioned as a kind of mobile phone, we can easily infer that 
Motorola Milestone is also a kind of mobile phone. 
Incremental learning: Obviously, each domain has some 
cornerstone concepts, said seed concepts, which occur in many 
documents within the corpus. The learning phase should be 
started from these seed concepts to discover concepts at lower 
level of abstraction and repeat the process in an incremental 
manner. 
Learning from instance: Typically, a class name rarely 
co-occurs with its subclasses or its properties in a sentence. 
Instead, instances of that class usually appear together with its 
related concepts. For example: 
• HTC Hero được trang bị màn hình cảm ứng điện dung 
4.3 inchesvà bộ nhớ trong 1.5GB

V. CONCLUSION AND FUTURE RESEARCH 
In this preliminary research, we propose a support system 
for Vietnamese ontology construction using the combination of 
lexical pattern based, statistic based, and frequent sequential 
pattern based methods. The hybrid approach can overcome 
different weaknesses of each individual method that may miss 
some concepts and relations during the discovery task. We also 
built a real Vietnamese ontology in mobile phone domain using 
our system. The constructed ontology can be used as a 
knowledge base on mobile phones in many applications such as 
a recommendation system, text classification, and information 
retrieval for anything about phones. Experimental results show 
that our approach has acceptable performance in concept 
discovery.  
Based on current results, in the future we would like to 
make the ontology construction process more automatic by 
considering both syntactic and semantic aspects of Vietnamese 
documents. First is to automate relation extraction algorithms 
by taking into account the semantic relations of selected 
concepts. This will reduce the manual proportion of the 
construction process. Second, better methods of measuring 
term importance will be considered to improve the automation 
of the concept identification task.  
Due to the absence of Vietnamese WordNet, some 
linguistic relations are not considered in this research, for 
example, synonym, antonym, hypernym, and hyponym. 
Otherwise, Vietnamese WordNet will have valuable 
contributions to the learning model, in which more concrete 
concepts and relations can be extracted accordingly. Currently, 
a small portion of Vietnamese WordNet (about 10,000 words) 
is under construction. When it is available, we would like to 
adopt it in our system to improve the construction of 
Vietnamese ontology within a given knowledge domain.

本研究是利用臉部與聲音的特徵辨識、資料分類與資料探勘技術來設計一個雲端的憂鬱症
醫療探勘系統，能夠幫助病患了解自己的心理狀況，協助家屬能夠更關心病患的進步情
況，提供醫師更多關於病患的資訊來對病患做診療。因此可以讓病患在保護隱私的情況
下，有機會能夠及早認識和接受憂鬱症的治療，系統進一步提供持續追蹤和紀錄管理，能
夠讓病患獲得更好的診斷與醫療照護。 
    我們訂定了一個有效的病患資料記錄格式，符合我們對於憂鬱症病患的臉部表情和聲
音辨識結果的紀錄和持續追蹤的需求，並有效地將龐大的影像資料做壓縮後放在雲端環
境。透過我們系統所分析的結果，不但可以幫助病患及病患家屬了解、追蹤病患的心理狀
態，同時也可以提供醫師更多關於病患的診斷參考資料。這些研究成果在資料挖掘和多媒
體影音處理上有學術價值以及產業應用性，更希望可以有效的預防或早期發現致病的傾
向，及早讓醫療人員協助病患正確認知憂鬱症，願意接受治療和復健的輔導，提高醫療效
能。以期能對社會日益嚴重的憂鬱情緒等相關症狀能有突破性的貢獻，可以帶來社會的安
定和發展，提高國人的身心健康和降低醫療成本。
