摘要 
本實驗主要是利用固態反應法製備 0.94(Bi0.5Na0.5)TiO3-0.06BaTiO3 (簡稱為 BNBT6)，並探討以不同煆
燒溫度與燒結溫度對 BNBT6 壓電材料之微結構影響。其中，主要的製程參數為：煆燒溫度之不同(700
℃、1100℃均持溫 0.5 小時)，與不同燒結溫度(900℃、1000℃、1100℃均持溫 2 小時)。根據 X 光繞射
儀(X-Ray Thin Diffraction meter, XRD)，掃描式電子顯微鏡(Scanning Electron Microscope, SEM)，阿基
米得浮力原理探討其該緻密燒結體之微結構及密度。 
 
 
The purpose of this study is to discuss the effects of various calcination and sintering temperatures on the 
microstructure of lead-free piezoelectric materials of 0.94(Bi0.5Na0.5)TiO3-0.06BaTiO3 (BNBT6) which is 
prepared by solid state reaction method. Among these studies, the major factors of process are different 
calcination temperatures (700℃/0.5hr and 1100℃/0.5hr),and sintering temperatures (900℃/2hr, 1000℃/2hr, 
and 1100℃ /2hr). To discuss the microstructure and density of lead-free piezoelectric materials of 
0.94(Bi0.5Na0.5)TiO3-0.06BaTiO3, the X-Ray Diffraction meter (XRD), Scanning Electron Microscope(SEM), 
and the Archimedes buoyancy theory are used.
