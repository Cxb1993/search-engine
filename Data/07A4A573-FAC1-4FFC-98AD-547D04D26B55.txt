到的優勢，但傳統上一般認為 late transition metal 之聚合活性較低(其 metal-carbon bonding
較強，不利於 chain propagation step)，但隨著 Brookhard Pd 、Ni 觸媒之發現 1 及日本學者

二、英文摘要: 
Key words: Chain transfer reaction, Block copolymer, Coordination polymerization 
（二） 計畫英文摘要。（五百字以內） 
The high oxophilicity nature of early-transition metal catalysts (e.g. Zieglar-Natta or 
metallocene catalysts) limited the utilizations of early transition metal catalysts for the 
polymerization of polar monomers.  In contrast, late transition metal catalysts were found can 
mediate the polymerization of polar olefins because of their high electron-rich nature. However, 
late transition metal catalysts were typically regarded as less active catalysts for olefin 
polymerization because of their strong carbon metal bonding.  Recently, Brookhart et al have 
reported the successful utilization of late transition metal catalysts to induce the polymerization 
of α-olefins with exceptional high activity. Their results may offer the opportunity for the 
preparation of new polymers because late transition metal catalysts were found can mediate the 
polymerization of unusual monomers (e.g. cyclic monomers, polar monomers). As a result, it is 
intriguing to study if these new synthetic tools can be used for the preparation of new diblock 
copolymers.  In this report, late transition metal catalysts, including Pd and Ni acetone nitrile 
complexes, have been used to induce the polymerization of cyclic olefins.  The alkene 
end-capped polynorborene has been successfully prepared by conducting the norbornene 
polymerization in the presence of a-olefins, which act as the chain transfer agents.  The 
generation of the alkene end-capped polynorborene is due to the occurrence of a selective 
chain-transfer reaction to 1-hexene or 1-octene (chain transfer to chain transfer agent) followed 
by a selective β-hydride elimination reaction. Thus, the alkene end-capped polynorborene can be 
used as the functional group end-capped prepolymer for the preparation of polynorbornene-b-PS 
 
 
 
 
 
     
 
 
值得注意的是縮合聚合雖然可以應用於合聚
物，然而縮合聚合之分子量分怖較寬(Mw/Mn ~ 2.0)難以進行 self-assembly studies 以形成單
有序奈米結構。反觀使用 chain reaction 之加成聚合反應以合成 block copolymer 時，則受限
於 chain reaction 中容易發生鏈轉移(chain transfer)與鏈終止(chain termination)反應之限制，
而無法串聯不同的聚合鏈段以合成 block copolymer。因此目前應用於合成聚烯烴類 block 
copolymer 之已知技術，皆須仰賴 Living polymerization(e.g. Living Anionic，Living Free 
radical polymerization)等聚合方法之介入，值得注意的是，上述 Living Anionic 及 Living Free 
radical 之相關技術僅能適用於相當有限類別之單體結構，因此能應用此一技術加以有效合
成的聚合物之種類亦相當有限，除此之外上述之 Living polymerization 之聚合技術並無法有
效控制聚合物之立體規則排列，也因此材料應用的空間亦明顯的僅能侷限於特定範圍。
