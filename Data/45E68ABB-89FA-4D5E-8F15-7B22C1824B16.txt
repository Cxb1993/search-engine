In this study, the aluminum nitride (AlN) 
thin film was prepared by r.f. sputtering on 
the ITO glass. Then the AlN/ITO glass 
sensing structure connected with the gate of 
MOSFET device and the extended-gate 
field-effect transistor (EGFET) was finished. 
Afterwards, the selective membrane was 
immobilization of ionophores on the AlN surface. 
In which, the polymerization condition was 
PVC : DOS : ETH9033 : TDDMACl = 33 : 66 : 
2 : 10 wt%. Afterwards, the pH sensitivity and 
chlorine ion response were measured which 
using the semiconductor parameter analyzer 
(I-V measurement, Keithley 4200) in the pH 
buffer solutions (pH=1~11) and KCl solutions
