Abstract	
Wave propagation and attenuation due to elastic cover on the sea surface is 
considered to be soft shore protection system in this paper. In the two-way 
coupling model for wave motion in a two-phase medium, “viscous fluid-elastic 
shirt”, accounting for the interaction between those phases is consided within the 
frame of a houndarylayer-type approximation. An asymptotic linearized model, 
including the Navier-Stoke equations for a viscous incompressible fluid and the 
deformable plate motion equations for an elastic cover, is analyzed within a wide 
range of governing parameters. The dispersion relationship for different Reynolds 
numbers shows different oscillation regions. The structure of the fluid flow is 
studied in detail for the two asymptotic limiting cases of relatively high and low 
impact ranges of the elastic cover on the surface wave propagation and damping. 
Furthermore, According to our analyses, the effect by wave steepness is similar to 
the literature. When Wave steepness increase, then the transmission coefficient 
decrease, energy loss increase. The difference by changed wave height are not 
obvious. And when the ratio of structure length to incident wave length increase, 
the energy loss increase too. We also find out the ratio affect more than the wave 
condition when the ratio value is bigger than four. As for the effect of water depth 
is not so clear, it is because all the wave condition in this experiment conducted in 
middle-water depth. Last, in the different elastic covers,the rigidity cover’s 
reflection coefficient is much higher than others, but the latex cover’s energy 
reducing is the best.

(3)學術交流。 
(4)交換學生。 
(5)出版物和其他共同感興趣教材的交流。 
(6)實現創新計畫領域的經驗交流。

Abstract 
  During September 28 to October 5, 2012, the President of National Cheng Kung 
University (NCKU), Prof. Hwung-Hweng Hwung , has led a delegation to visit 
Russia and signed Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with Saint-Petersburg 
State University (SPbU) and St-Petersburg Branch, P. P. Shirshov Institute of 
Oceanology of Russian Academy of Science, expecting to enlarge the Taiwan-Russia 
academic cooperation and invite two Russian research teams to join a transnational 
center of wave dynamics research and application in the future. 
 
    The NCKU delegation included President Hwung-Hweng Hwung, Director 
Yang-Yih Chen of Tainan Hydraulics Laboratory, Prof. Igor V. Shugan of Tainan 
Hydraulics Laboratory, Chief Ray-Yeng Yang of Research Center of Tainan 
Hydraulics Laboratory and Prof. Chi-Hsin Hsu of College of Marine Science, NSYSU. 
 
    The academic cooperation between Taiwan and United States is quite frequent. 
However, the academic exchange between Taiwan and Russia is extremely few. Thus, 
the establishment of the cooperative relationship between National Cheng Kung 
University and renowned academic and research institutions in Russia is particularly 
unique. 
 
    So far, Taiwan doesn’t have diplomatic relation with Russia yet. The SPbU is 
well-known for her basic scientific research and is claimed to be one of the cradle of 
Nobel Prize winners in Russia. Through the cooperation with top academic and 
research institution in Russia, it is believed that NCKU can increase the scientific 
research achievements on both sides and strengthen the relationship between Taiwan 
and Russia.   Signing the MOU between NCKU and SPbU is just the first step for 
the bilateral cooperation. A closer academic cooperation relation between Taiwan and 
Russia will be followed after this visiting. Furthermore in addition to research 
cooperation, NCKU and SPbU institutes will explore the opportunities for student and 
faculty exchange, joint curriculum development as well as research and academic 
materials exchange to mark a new milestone for the international cooperative 
relationship between top research in Taiwan and Russia. Meanwhile in the future, 
Tainan Hydraulics Laboratory, NCKU and St-Petersburg Branch, P. P. Shirshov 
Institute of Oceanology of Russian Academy of Science will cooperate on the 
research fields of the internal wave, freak wave, wave modulation and marine 
ecosystem simulation etc..

4. 此次參訪除了研討會與合作備忘錄簽署之外，對於俄羅斯國家科學院海洋研
究所聖彼得堡分所利用微脈衝光達在不同應用面的具體成果亦深感興趣。此
種微脈衝光達(MicropulseLidar) 是以雷射光束作為工具的一種遙測技術，在
發展初期最直接的分析方法便是觀察回波圖形，因為氣膠顆粒、空氣分子與
雲滴所造成的回波在強度上有明顯差異，故對於即時現象可以做第一時間的
解釋。此系統具有對人眼安全、高效能、對環境的高穩定性、可靠性、高重
複率、光子計數、多層雲狀結構與煙流(plume)偵測、動態獲取大量採集的時
空數據訊息等等優勢。微脈衝光達的回波訊號可以確實反應傳波路徑上的散
射因子，再加上具有大範圍的資料採集能力，已很普遍的被用於環境監測。
Pershin 教授他們的研究團隊有許多成功的應用，包含偵測油氣外洩、雲層結
構變化、樹高的測量等等，都是基於散射原理進行有效環境監測。此方法甚
至被用於地震前兆的討論。他們在 Georgia 的研究發現大氣中氫氣、氣膠微
粒在大地震前有大量降低的趨勢，雖然尚待驗證此種地震前兆的穩定性，但
此研究說明微脈衝光達觀測在地震預測上的應用面。本套 Lidar 系統預期亦
可應用於成功大學水工試驗所大型平面試驗水池之動床漂沙試驗模型底床高
程之量測。
