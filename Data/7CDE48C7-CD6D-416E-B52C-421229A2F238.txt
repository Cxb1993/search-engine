 III
abstract 
Lithium ion batteries have been commercialized successfully. They are widely 
used in mobile phones and notebooks. LiCoO2 is the most commonly used cathode 
materials for the lithium ion batteries, due to its stable electrochemical performance 
and well established process. However, because cobalt is expensive and non-friendly 
to environment, many researchers have been trying other to replace LiCoO2. LiFePO4 
is a popular cathode material for lithium ion batteries due to good electrochemical 
performances, low cost, high safety. Unfortunately, LiFePO4 has some drawbacks, 
such as low Li+ diffusion coefficient and poor electron conductivity, which limited its 
applications. 
The research here used pure LiFePO4 thin films as the cathode materials of thin 
film batteries. Based on our previous laboratory studies, we developed a new method 
that induced substrate effects and interdiffusion in the LiFePO4 thin films. In this 
study, we successfully indentified the microstructural evolutions in LiFePO4 thin 
films due to the substrate effects and improved the electrochemical properties of the 
LiFePO4 thin films. Further more, LiFePO4-Ag composite thin film were prepared by 
radio frequency magnetron sputtering, It was shown that the conductivity of 
LiFePO4-Ag composite thin films was greatly enhanced due to Ag mixing, the 
electrochemical analysis showed that LiFePO4-Ag composite thin films can be 
discharged at a specific rate as high as 10 C. 
 
Keyword：lithium iron phosphate、thin film battery、substrate effect、interdiffusion、
composite thin film.

逆的氧化峰是由鐵氧化合物所引起的，例如：基材上的 Fe3O4。鐵氧化合物於電
極中可容許鋰離子儲存於其中成為 LixFeyOz，並能藉由鐵的價數於 Fe2+/Fe3+轉
變，使鋰離子能嵌出嵌入，如此鐵氧化合物也能提供電容量。不過大多的鐵氧化
合物雖然氧化反應發生於 LiFePO4 操作電壓範圍，但是其還原反應發生於較低之
電位(3 V ~ 1 V)33，因此鐵氧化合物於 LiFePO4 操作電壓範圍內，只會有氧化反
應發生，而沒有還原反應發生，使得鐵氧化合物反應所提供的電容量和不可逆反
應一樣，於第一圈循環後就會消失。 
因為本實驗之膜重有所差異，因此將圖十八的氧化還原峰的電流對 LiFePO4
重量做正常化(normalization)後再作比較，得到結果如圖十九所示。當銀含量少
於 35 wt%時，每克的 LiFePO4 所能負荷的電流小於 100 mA；銀含量高過 35 wt%
後，每克的 LiFePO4 所能負荷的電流隨著銀含量增加而增加；當銀含量高過 65 
wt%，LiFePO4 所能負荷的電流隨著銀含量增加而下降。此結果和先前的推論相
吻合的，當銀含量少於 35 wt%時，銀不足夠披覆於 LiFePO4 上形成導電網路，
如表四所計算的銀體積，銀體積百分比少於 20 vol%，在一般的金屬與氧化物複
合物中是不會導電，金屬銀只有接觸到一些 LiFePO4 的顆粒，無法改善全部
LiFePO4 的導電性，因此銀含量低於 35 wt%的 LiFePO4-Ag 複合薄膜還是無法負
荷快速充放電。而當銀含量高於 35 wt% ( 20 vol%)，金屬銀可接觸到的 LiFePO4
的顆粒增加，可大幅改善 LiFePO4-Ag 複合薄膜整體的導電性，使得 LiFePO4-Ag
具備快速充放電能力。但是當銀含量過高時，銀會包覆住 LiFePO4 的顆粒，使
LiFePO4 的顆粒團聚變大，導電性雖極大幅的上升，卻會造成鋰離子擴散路徑變
長，如此狀態下 LiFePO4-Ag 複合薄膜的快速充放電能力會由原先受限於導電性
不良，轉變成受限於鋰離子擴散速率，所以銀含量大於 65 wt%後，單位重量的
LiFePO4 所能負荷的電流反而隨著銀含量增加而下降。

4、結論 
本實驗室已研發出可製作高純相不含碳的 LiFePO4 薄膜製程。實驗過程
中，我們了解並證實基材的種類與表面粗糙度明顯影響 LiFePO4 薄膜顯微表徵
與晶體結構變化。我們藉由金屬 Ag 薄膜基材的擴散並誘發 LiFePO4 薄膜顯微
結構變化，成功地研發出具有小尺寸晶粒的高導電性 LiFePO4/Ag 薄膜 ( 5×10-3 
Scm-1 )，電容量達 79 mAh/g。藉由 X-ray 光電子能譜儀 ( XPS ) 與 穿透式電子
顯微鏡 ( TEM ) 的成分分析，除了觀察出 Ag 有擴散行為之外，還可推測出電容
量不足理論電容量 170 mAh/g 一半的原因，主要是由於 Ag 以沿晶擴散的形
式，且不均勻的分佈於 LiFePO4 晶粒之外，導致於薄膜晶粒中大部分的 LiFePO4
仍然因導電性不佳，而無法充分發揮功用，而影響電化學表現。 
本實驗嘗試改以銀片貼覆於磷酸鋰鐵靶配合射頻磁控濺鍍的方式，將銀於濺
鍍過程中直接添加至 LiFePO4 薄膜中，所得 LiFePO4-Ag 複合薄膜導電性大幅度
上升，並展現出良好的快速充放電能力，於 10 C 高速率下仍可充放電，尤其是
銀添加量在 45 ~60 wt%間能達到最佳改善 LiFePO4-Ag 複合薄膜的快速充放電能
力的效果，且有不同放電電流計算得知，銀含量在 45 ~60 wt%的 LiFePO4-Ag 複
合薄膜有較低之極化阻抗。

Abstract  
Carbon-free LiFePO4 thin films have been sputtered from a pure LiFePO4 target onto Ag/SS, 
Ag/Si3N4/Si and Si3N4/Si substrates. All of the deposited films were annealed at 973 K for 1 
hr in H2/Ar (5 %) atmosphere. Substrate induced microstructural and crystallographic 
evolutions have been observed by a scanning electron microscope and X-ray diffraction. 
Energy dispersion spectra and x-ray photoelectron spectra revealed that Ag was mixed in the 
LiFePO4 films deposited on Ag under layers, the LiFePO4-Ag cermet form. The film 
conductivity (1 x 10-3 Scm-1) is therefore elevated by an order of six, compared with pure 
LiFePO4 (10-9 Scm-1). The electrochemical measurements of the LiFePO4-Ag films showed a 
flat plateau at 3.4 V (v.s. Li/Li+) and a reversible capacity of 80 mAh/g. Optimization of Ag 
contents may further improve the discharge capacity.
 3
In the RFMSD studies, Eftekhari et al. [12] deposited LiFePO4 thin film by rf sputter with 
transition-metal codeposition to enhance the LiFePO4 thin film battery performance. In 2007, 
Chiu et al. [13, 14] synthesized carbon-mixed LiFePO4 composite thin-film cathodes by bias 
sputtering and a high specific capacity was observed at low discharge currents. Dudney et al. 
[15] deposited the 1 μm thick film of LiFePO4 by RFMSD that can deliver over 150 mAh/g at 
a lower discharge current of 0.1 C. However, to our knowledge, there is no report on the 
substrate effects for the LiFePO4 thin films, and the roles of transition metal mixed in the 
LiFePO4 have not been systematically studied.   
In the present work, the carbon-free LiFePO4 thin films have been deposited on different 
substrates by RFMSD. Substrate induced microstructure evolution of the thin films has been 
observed. The conductivity of the LiFePO4 thin films is increased by the addition of Ag. 
These Ag-LiFePO4 composited thin film cathodes for lithium ion cells have been 
characterized. The study reveals that the dispersion of Ag inside the film body plays an 
important role in the electrochemical performance of the thin film cathodes. 
2. Experimental Details 
The carbon-free LiFePO4 were deposited on different substrates (SiNx/Si, Ag/SiNx/Si, 
and Ag/stainless steel (SS)) by RFMSD with RF power of 30W. The SiNx layer was used as a 
diffusion barrier of Si to LiFePO4, because our previous study [14] has found interdiffusion 
between Si and LiFePO4 under annealing.  The amorphous SiNx coated (100) Si wafers were 
provided by the Industrial Technology Research Institute. The Ag/SS substrates were 
prepared by sputter depositing 30 nm polycrystalline Ag films on 316L stainless steel discs 
(1.33 cm2). The sputtering target to substrate distance was 5 cm, and the substrate holder was 
electrically grounded. The sputtering was carried out under Ar/H2 (Ar/H2 = 95/5 in partial 
pressure) mixture gas with working pressure of 1.33 Pa. The gas flow rate was 10 sccm. The 
deposition rate was 1.5 nm/min and the films thickness was 370 nm. All LiFePO4 samples 
were post annealed at 973 K in vacuum for 1 hour by a radiation heater.

3.2 Crystallography 
Figure 1 shows the XRD patterns of the LiFePO4 thin films deposited on the different 
substrates, and the powder scrapped from the pure LiFePO4 target is also shown for 
comparison. The powder XRD patterns completely match the olivine structure as identified by 
JCPDF (40-1499) data base. The bare Ag/SS substrate after annealing shows diffraction peaks 
of Ag, SS and Fe3O4, owing to that the SS substrates were oxidized under high temperature by 
the residual oxygen or water vapor in the furnace during the annealing process [17]. 
All of the XRD spectra of the LiFePO4 thin films reveals that the films after annealing are 
well crystallized and free of second phases, except for the peaks from the substrates. Sample 
LiFePO4/SiNx/Si shows higher relative intensity of (020) peak. With the addition of Ag under 
layer, i.e. LiFePO4/Ag/SiNx/Si and LiFePO4/Ag/SS, the samples become slightly (011) 
textured. It is well known that sputter deposited films tend to develop certain preferred 
crystallographic orientation due to the influence of surface energy or volume strain energy of 
the film-substrate systems [16]. Interestingly, for the films with Ag under layers, the (011) 
peak is enhanced. It may be induced by the addition of Ag, which changes the balance of 
surface energy and volume strain energy during film growth and crystallization. Similar result 
has been found in our previous study of LiFePO4 films with Ti under layers, where (011) 
texture was also promoted. The metal induced (011) texture of LiFePO4 films may play an 
important role in future applications, since the (011) orientation provide ion transportation 
channels with no obstacles from FeO6 and PO4 of the LiFePO4 structure [15].

Electrochemical characterization was carried out on the film/substrate, which shows

phenomenon has been observed in other published reported. It was attributed it to the

The charge-discharge tests are presented in Fig. 5, the sample (LiFePO4/Ag/SS) showed a

theoretical capacity (170 mAh/g) and the capacity (~140-160 mAh/g) of carbon mixed

本研究選擇純LiFePO4作為薄膜電池正極材料，結合過往對LiFePO4的研究經驗
，並以基材效應與薄膜間交互擴散為理論基礎的新實驗方法，發展出不同
LiFePO4薄膜顯微表徵與提高LiFePO4薄膜正極的導電性。除次之外，更進一步
嘗試使用貼覆銀片磷酸鋰鐵靶及射頻磁控濺鍍製造LiFePO4-Ag複合薄膜，所得
之複合薄膜因為銀的添加而導電性大幅上升，因此可於10 C高速率下放電。開
發之LiFePO4-Ag複合薄膜之特殊製作方法，所得之複合薄膜因為銀的添加而導
電性大幅上升，因此可於10 C高速率下放電。
This research here used pure LiFePO4 thin films as cathode materials
of thin film batteries. Based on our previous laboratory studies, we
developed a new method that induced substrate effects and
interdiffusion in the LiFePO4 thin films. In this study, we
successfully indentified the microstructural evolutions in LiFePO4
thin films due to the substrate effects and improved the
electrochemical properties of the LiFePO4 thin films. Further more,
LiFePO4-Ag composite thin film were prepared by radio frequency
magnetron sputtering, It was shown that the conductivity of LiFePO4-
Ag composite thin films was greatly enhanced due to Ag mixing, the
