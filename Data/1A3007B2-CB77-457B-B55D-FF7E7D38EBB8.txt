Abstract 
An excellent idea is cardinal to a product’s success. Scenario based design is exploited 
comprehensively in product design region since a scenario based idea can not only provide user  
wonderful using experience but also find out potential using opportunity. Hence a scenario 
based idea is fit in with the requirement of user centered design (UCD).Our research aims to 
generate a user centered product idea. We design a computer aided product idea generation 
approach based on scenario based design?? activity analysis, case based reasoning (CBR), and 
Petri net (PN). It includes 4 phases: (1) scenario generation: generate a problem scenario and 
characters within it, (2) scenario analysis: construct use cases based on problem scenario and 
carry out 5W1H analysis,? (3)activity analysis: a complete activity contains subject, object, tool, 
rule, community, and division of labors. And an activity modality which contains manual, visual, 
auditory, speech, and automatic depict the interaction between users and tools in detail, (4) idea 
generation: generate new ideas via case based reasoning in this phase. The proposed method not 
only solve design problems but also assist organization to generate more new ideas and 
contributes to the overall competence of an organization’s innovativeness.    
?  
Key words: product idea generation, case based reasoning, scenario based design, Petri net, 
computer aided product development?
