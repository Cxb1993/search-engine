摘要 
 
本研究利用磁射直流濺鍍技術，製備複合氧化銦錫(ITO)與之二氧化鈦(TiO2)奈米複合
光觸媒薄膜(composite photocatalyst thin film)，探討此類光觸媒薄膜的特性（如：表面形貌、
結晶型態等），並進一步分析這些薄膜光觸媒在水裂解產氫與有機污染物分解等方面的能
力。另外，本研究於相關光觸媒鍍膜製備時，植入碳、氮等無機物，藉以提昇觸媒活性與
可見光的吸收。初步實驗結果顯示，摻雜微量探、氮之 TiO2 光觸媒，可提升對可見光光源
之吸收，不過對於可見光下之光生電流的提昇，則需要確認其添加量，摻雜過多的無機物
於光觸媒結構中，可能會導致不利於水裂解產氫之晶相觸線；另外，添加氮或氧化銦錫改
質後的複合光觸媒薄膜，對於有機物之氧化分解，具有較佳的催化活性，觸媒表面結晶形
貌、孔間隙大小、鍍膜厚度、能隙高低與相對應位置，皆為影響光觸媒活性的重要因素。
TiO2 經由氧化銦錫複合後，有助其氧化與還原能力的提升，其可能與其具有延緩電子-電洞
對再結合速率。另外，本研究結果也顯示，於工作電極端置入 DMSO 等有機物，其有助於
相對電極端氫氣的生成，係因為 DMSO 可消耗光觸媒上的電洞，可延緩電子/電洞對的再結
合速率，並增進水裂解成氫氣與氧氣。此外，對於較產氫效能較佳的電解質環境條件，於
最佳操作條件下，其獲致之光生電流值為，以單純 TiO2 類型之 T 薄膜的 177.8 µA/cm2 (以
365 nm 為光源，2.0 M KOH 為電解質)，以及 TiO2-ITO 類型之 TI 薄膜的 73.0 µA/cm2 為最
佳(以 365 nm 為光源，1.0M Na2S2O3 為電解質)。本研究確認藉由相關光觸媒之製備，可在
太陽光或可見光為光源下，進行水之裂解產氫反應。 
 
關鍵字：奈米光觸媒、濺鍍、二氧化鈦、氧化銦錫、氫氣 
 
Abstract 
This study developed series titanium dioxide/indium tin oxide (TiO2/ITO) composite thin 
film photocatalysts for organic pollutants oxidation and water splitting.  There were several 
kinds of thin films including simple TiO2 (TiO2-ITO), nitrogen doped TiO2 (N-TiO2-ITO), and 
carbon doped TiO2 (C-TiO2-ITO) accompanied with WO3 in the study.  The thin film 
photocatalysts were prepared in a DC magnetism spluttering reactor in which pure titanium foils 
work as titanium sources and the commercially available ITO glass as their deposition material.  
The nitrogen doping into crystal structures of TiO2 was to enhance the responsibility of 
photocatalysts to visible light. Some important results were observed in this study.  It was 
observed that TiO2 could absorb longer-wavelength light after being doped with nitrogen or being 
composed with ITO. Although simple TiO2-ITO thin films could achieve higher photocurrent 
than N-TiO2-ITO, carbon doping is beneficial for increasing their oxidation capabilities for 
organic pollutants.  The overall performance of the prepared thin films could be affected by

2.2.2. 薄膜觸媒氧化能力檢測 
相關研究文獻顯示，光催化反應之所以能夠迅速地進行，其最主要的原因在於，除了
被提昇的電子/電子洞對具有氧化/還原能力，其衍生許多反應性強的自由基（如：•OH），
更扮演重要的角色。本研究以 dimethyl sulfoxide (二甲基亞碸, DMSO)作為氫氧自由基捕捉
劑，藉由 DMSO 與氫氧自由基反應主要中間產物－甲醛(formaldehyde) 生成速率的量測，
藉以估算自由基的生成量。另外，本研究針對反應系統中微量甲醛的分析，係利用已建立
之微量甲醛分析方法，來加以定量之，其方式如下：取樣品後，加入磷酸緩衝溶液、NH3、
乙醯丙酮於試管中進行 Hantzsch 反應，使甲醛反應生成具有螢光特性的甲醛衍生物後，再
以 HPLC-螢光偵測器分析。HPLC 分析條件：Column：C-18、Flow rate：1.0 mL/min、Detector：
fluorescent lamp（激光 400 nm、發光 452 nm）、移動相：MeOH/H2O = 60/40(v/v)。 
2.2.3. 薄膜觸媒有機物分解速率測試 
本實驗使用批次反應器進行相關有機物分解測率的測試，此反應器高 5 cm、內徑 10 
cm，（Pyrex 玻璃為底槽，上蓋為石英玻璃，槽體體積為 700 mL），在反應器上方 5 cm 處放
置兩支 20W 近紫外燈管(λ= 365nm)為光源。反應器四周設置四個可密閉的開孔，以便於進
樣、採樣及安置入熱電偶溫度計(thermocouple)。另外，反應器內部將放置一顆攪拌磁石，
以增加混合效果。實驗時，反應器內先置入已批覆觸媒薄膜試片，並以微量注射針注入定
量的 MtBE，反應器外部包覆以可控制溫度之加熱帶，以恒定反應時的溫度。針對 MtBE 分
解速率之檢測，乃於實驗過程中適時採集樣品，再利用 GC/FID(HP 4890 Series GC System)
及 GC/MSD (HP 6890 Series，HP5973 Mass Spec Detector)等進行反應物與產物的定性與定量
分析。

3.4.光生產氫效應 
    就產氫速率而言，本研究發現，在 simple TiO2 光觸媒薄膜類型中，以 P1 薄膜的水裂
解產氫效率最佳，其原因可能與銳鈦礦 2θ= 55.06°(211)強度最強有關，而在 TiO2-C 光觸媒
薄膜類型中，以 Bc6 薄膜的產氫效率最低，亦即氧化電位高於還原電位，可能較適於進行
光催化氧化反應的應用。另外，在光催化水裂解產氫反應中，為了能有效提升產氫效能，
乃添加電子提供者(電洞清除者)，除有利於延緩電子-電洞對的再結合速率外，亦可以降低
氫氣與氧氣再結合的反應發生[1]。經上述的初步篩選實驗後，將從三種類型光觸媒薄膜中，
篩選出各個類型較具代表性之薄膜，進行有機物分解及氫離子還原產氫實驗。本研究所測
試之目標有機物為二甲基亞碸(dimethyl sulfoxide, DMSO)，圖3.12與3.13分別是添加DMSO
對光電流與產氫效能之影響。研究結果顯示，光觸媒薄膜於添加 DMSO 後，所產生的光電
流強度皆大於無添加 DMSO 時所產生的光電流值。以 P1 薄膜而言，可使光生電流強度從
239 提升至 262 µA/cm2，而產氫速率從 19.706 提升至 21.095 µmole/cm2hr；以 Tp3 薄膜而言，
可使光生電流強度從 60.9 提升至 67.3 µA/cm2，而產氫速率從 2.307 提升至 3.569 
µmole/cm2hr。以 Bc6 薄膜而言，可使光生電流強度從 2.44 提升至 8.9 µA/cm2，而產氫速率
提升至 0.183 µmole/cm2hr。另外，本研究也發現，DMSO 濃度亦隨反應時間之進行而逐漸
降低其濃度，且基本上仍循一階的反應進行。 
 
3.5. 有機物氧化分解速率 
    本部分實驗旨在於探討三種光觸媒薄膜的 MtBE 分解效率，研究發現 MtBE 的分解反
應呈一階反應動力關係，經進一步估算其一階分解速率常數，彙整於圖 3.14 中。可發現含
碳氮的二氧化鈦光觸媒薄膜(BC3)，具有最大的 MtBE 分解速率，含碳的二氧化鈦光觸媒薄
膜(BC1、BC6)次之，單純二氧化鈦光觸媒薄膜反應速率相對較低。摻雜碳氮的二氧化鈦光
觸媒薄膜(BC3)的氧化效能，其約為單純 TiO2/ITO 複合光觸媒薄膜之 3.4 倍，反應速率常數
約為 19.2 1/(min•m2)，這些實驗結果顯示二氧化鈦碳摻雜後，可提升其光催化活性。

3.6.1 光生電流強度之整體表現 
在本研究所製備的光觸媒薄膜類型中(單純 TiO2、TiO2-ITO、TiO2-C)，圖 3.20 至 3.28
為相關之實驗結果。首先如實驗方法所述，本研究先以「線性循環伏安法」進行光生電流
的量測，觀察反應過程中的電流值變化。圖 3.20 至 3.22 為 TiO2 薄膜類型之線性循環伏安
法量測結果。其中，T 薄膜試片在近紫外光、藍光 LED 或太陽光模擬器光源下，均在施加
偏壓 0 V 至 1.5V 時呈現平緩的趨勢；而 TI 薄膜試片在近紫外光及藍光 LED 光源的照射下，
於施加偏壓 1.0 V 時電流呈現上升的現象，而在太陽光模擬器光源下則呈現較平緩的趨勢；
另外，TC 薄膜試片則是在三種光源的照射下，均於施加偏壓 1.0 V 時電流有呈現上升的現
象。由於光觸媒本身特性會影響線性循環伏安法及光生電流之結果，因此各觸媒於各電解
質中之電流變化趨勢，即線性循環伏安法之結果，光生電流強度與外加偏壓之變化趨勢大
致相同，只是其電流表現高低有所差異(其他電解質 LSV 量測結果，請參考附錄 D 之 I-T 
Curve、LSV 掃描結果)；圖 3.23 至 3.25 為本研究於 2.0 M Na2CO3 電解質中，當施加 0.5 V
偏壓時所測得光生電流強弱的整體表現，由圖 4-20 至 4-22 之量測結果中可看出，無論是 T、
TI 亦或是 TC 薄膜在使用近紫外光(365 nm)，藍光 LED(470 nm)，或是太陽光模擬器(太陽
光)為光源時，皆以近紫外光光源具有較高的光生電流值，其次為太陽光光源，並以 LED
藍光可見光源所獲得之光生電流值最低。實際比較各薄膜試片其光生電流值於各光源下其
差異如圖 3.26 所示，為以單純 TiO2 薄膜具有較高的光生電流值，其次為 TiO2-ITO 光觸媒
薄膜，並以摻雜碳光觸媒薄膜(TiO2-C)所獲得之光生電流值最低。 
另外，本研究發現，在光觸媒薄膜製備過程中，添加微量的 ITO 薄膜與碳或氮進行 TiO2
薄膜的改質後，對於以 470 nm 藍光 LED 及太陽光模擬器為光源時，具有明顯提升光電效
能的作用。這些改質後的光觸媒薄膜，雖然可能會降低在近紫外光為光源下的光電效應，
導致所產生的光生電流反而較小，但對於未來直接引用太陽光作為光源，提供一個可能性。 
其次，就不同光源對光生電流強弱的影響，在所測試的三種光源中，以近紫外光(365 nm)
為光源時，其所產生的光生電流強度，遠大於以藍光 LED(470 nm)及太陽光模擬器為反應
光源時所產生的光生電流值，其差異可達 1~2 個數量級。此結果顯示光源波長的特性，對
於光電效應有明顯的影響，但基於考量未來新能源開發的實用性以及能源效率的考量，本
研究仍分別針對 365 nm 之紫外燈光源、470 nm 之藍光 LED，以及太陽光模擬器之太陽光
光源，進行更廣泛的探討。

3.6.2 不同光源對光生電流強度之影響 
圖 3.27 至 3.29 為本研究針對 TiO2 光觸媒薄膜類型，分別在波長 365 nm(近紫外光)、
470 nm(藍光 LED)及太陽光模擬器(太陽光)為光源下，施加 0.5 V 偏壓於不同電解質時，所
量測出光觸媒薄膜的光生電流值。其中，以近紫外光為光源的實驗結果而言，皆以單純 TiO2
光觸媒薄膜類型，具有較高的光生電流，其光生電流大小依序為：單純 TiO2 薄膜
(T)>TiO2-ITO 薄膜(TI)>TiO2-C 薄膜(TC)；而在以 LED 藍光為光源的實驗下，雖然仍以單純
TiO2 光觸媒薄膜類型，具有較高的光生電流，但卻對於 TiO2-ITO 薄膜之光生電流值有明顯
的提昇效果，尤其在 1.0 M NaI 電解質中，有接近於單純 TiO2 薄膜之光生電流表現。 
在以太陽光模擬器為光源的實驗結果中，雖然結果大都仍以單純 TiO2 光觸媒薄膜類
型，具有較高的光生電流，但和 LED 藍光光源下的結果相似，太陽光光源不但對於 TiO2-ITO
薄膜之光生電流值有明顯的提昇效果，在特定電解質中如：0.1 M KI、1.0M Na2S2O3、1.5M 
Na2SO4 及 0.5M Na3PO4 中，對於 TiO2-C 薄膜之光生電流值有著更顯著的提昇效果，尤其在
0.1 M KI 電解質中，TiO2-C 薄膜之光生電流值更高於單純 TiO2 薄膜。若比較各種類電解質
於不同光源其影響，由圖 4-26 至圖 4-28 可得知，於近紫外光下以碳酸根離子(CO32-)、氫氧
根離子(OH-)及含硫離子(SO32-、SO42-、S2O32-)所得到的光生電流值較佳，不過在 LED 藍光
光源下，各電解質對薄膜試片之影響卻較無明顯差異；另外，在太陽光光源下，各電解質
之影響雖然相似於 LED 藍光光源之結果，較無明顯差異。整體而言，平均光生電流值表現
則以磷酸根離子有較佳之影響。

可發現雖然 T 薄膜試片在可見光下效能較低，但仍為三種薄膜類型中具有較佳的光生電流
表現。另外，在近紫外光光源下，仍以 2.0 M Na2CO3 電解質有較佳之光生電流值產生，而
在 LED 藍光光源下，以 2.0 M LiOH 電解質有較佳之光生電流值產生，最後在太陽光模擬
器光源下，則以 0.5 M NaCl 電解質有較佳之光生電流值產生。 
此外，就 TiO2-ITO 薄膜 TI 而言，在近紫外光(365 nm)光源下，以 1.0M NaOH 電解質
有較佳之光生電流值產生；在 LED 藍光(470 nm)光源下，以 1.0M NaI 電解質有較佳之光生
電流值產生；在太陽光模擬器(太陽光)光源下，則以 2.0M KOH 電解質有較佳之光生電流
值產生。針對 TiO2-C 薄膜 TC 而言，在近紫外光(365 nm)光源下，以 2.0M LiOH 電解質有
較佳之光生電流值產生；在 LED 藍光(470 nm)光源下，以 2.0M Na2CO3 電解質有較佳之光
生電流值產生；在太陽光模擬器(太陽光)光源下，則以 2.0M LiOH 電解質有較佳之光生電
流值產生。 
若比較其溶液酸鹼度之影響與電解質測試結果，可發現，近紫外光之光生電流表現較
佳之電解質其溶液 pH 值範圍較寬(5.94~14.16)，太陽光光源之光生電流表現較佳之 pH 值範
圍則趨於鹼性(8.89~12.63)，據此推論溶液酸鹼度對於 T 系列薄膜試片之光生電流強度較不
顯著。比較之電解質導電度與各薄膜試片光生電流，其可發現當溶液之導電度愈高，光生
電流之平均表現也較高。如近紫外光下，導電度 85 mS/cm 以上之電解質，其光生電流表現
均優於導電度低於 85 mS/cm 以下之電解質。探討其離子強度及活性係數得知，以氫氧根離
子活性係數相同之電解質為例，活性係數大小為 Li+(0.826)>Na+(0.803)>K+(0.760)，於三種
光源下其光生電流並無明顯隨活性大小變化之趨勢，若再比較其他電解質之 Na+、K+離子
活性大小與光生電流關係，陽離子或許並非影響光催化水裂解效能之主要因素。

四、結果彙整 
    本研究利用磁控濺鍍技術製備奈米複合光觸媒薄膜，研究中分析探討光觸媒薄膜的表
面特性，並測試這些光觸媒薄膜在環境工程應用方面的適用性。實驗結果顯示，利用本研
究所開發的協同氧化/還原反應光催化反應系統，確實可同步進行 DMSO 的氧化分解與氫氣
的生成反應，深具開發價值。此外，本研究中也發現，當 DMSO 存在於反應系統中，可使
光生電流強度提升，而最佳的產氫速率也提升了 50% (從 2.3 提升至 3.6 µmole/cm2hr)，皆具
有不錯的光電效應及氧化有機物之能力。另外，就相關光觸媒薄膜的氧化分解有機物方面，
摻雜微量碳之 TiO2 光觸媒和摻雜微量碳、氮之 TiO2 光觸媒，可提升其對可見光光源之吸
收，已達初步紅移的功能。 
    另外，針對水裂解產氫效能方面，影響光催化水裂解光生電流表現及產氫效能之總體
因素而言，仍以光觸媒薄膜的本質最具影響性，亦即須篩選出較適合光催化氧化與還原反
應之光觸媒。影響光觸媒的催化能力者，包括觸媒結晶表面形貌、間隙大小、鍍膜厚度、
能隙大小、氧化還原力高低等因素；以單純 TiO2 類型薄膜而言，無論是單純 TiO2 或者經
由摻雜 ITO 或碳改質過後之薄膜試片均以氫氧根離子為主之電解質有較佳之光生電流產
生；本實驗於最佳操作條件下，其獲致之光生電流值為，單純 TiO2 類型之 T 薄膜的 177.8 
µA/cm2 (以 365 nm 為光源，2.0 M KOH 為電解質)，以及 TiO2-ITO 類型之 TI 薄膜的 73.0 
µA/cm2 為最佳(以 365 nm 為光源，1.0M Na2S2O3 為電解質)。當 T 薄膜及 TI 薄膜位於 1.0 M 
NaI 電解質中於不同光源下時，可使 TI 薄膜的光生電流強度從 T 薄膜(以 365 nm 為光源，
TI 薄膜的光生電流為 24.4 µA/cm2，T 薄膜的光生電流為 59.4 µA/cm2)的 1/2 提升至相同電

Among the developing control technologies of VOCs, photo-sensitizer assisted photocatalytic process is one of the 
most promising technologies for both clean energy production and VOC control6-10. Even though there have been 
many photo-sensitizers developed11, titanium dioxide (TiO2) is the most investigated semiconductor oxide due to its 
superior properties of effectiveness, cheapness and chemical stability6,12-14. But there are still some limitations for 
adopting pure TiO2-assisted PCO process to pollution control. The most important constriction is the band gap 
energy (about 3.0~3.2 eV) of TiO2 too wide, causing that TiO2 need absorb the light with the wavelength below 380 
nm for the photocatalyst activation. The solar light with wavelength below 380 nm is only about 3~5% of solar light 
radiation received on the earth. As a result, the research efforts on modification of TiO2 have been widely explored 
for extending its responsibility for visible light. These modification approaches may include metal doping 15, 
nonmetal doping16,17,18, transition metal compound or dye sensitizing19-22. In the early investigation 16, it has been 
reported that visible-light responsible TiO2 could be achieved by doping with nonmetal inorganic elements (e.g., 
carbon), which could cause the modification in visible light responsibility of TiO2. 
 
Accordingly, this study aims to investigate the photocatalytic oxidation of MtBE by using carbon doped TiO2/ITO 
composite thin films as the visible light responsible photocatalysts. Series of experiments for MtBE photocatalysis 
were conducted under the illumination of near-UV light (UVA) and visible blue light, respectively. Several typical 
affecting factors including MTBE initial concentrations, water vapor content and reaction temperatures were also 
evaluated in the study.

The TiO2/ITO composite films for the study were prepared via a magnetron sputtering process.  The thin films of 
TiO2/ITO were deposited on unheated glass slide (25×75×1 mm) substrates in a pulse arc plasma-aided twin DC 
magnetron sputtering a chamber with dimensions 300(W)× 300(D)×400 mm(H). This magnetron system consists of 
opposite vertical twin targets each of dimensions 400×100×6 mm and is operated in a class 1000 clean room.  In the 
study, the ITO films were initially deposited onto unheated rotating glass substrates using a pair of vertical ITO 
targets, 90 wt% In2O3 and 10 wt% SnO2 on one side of the chamber. The base pressure was set to 1.2×10-3 Pa, 
whereas the sputtering pressure of 0.11 Pa was maintained as the reactive argon-oxygen gas mixture (2% oxygen) 
was introduced.  Reaching the desired thickness of ITO film, the substrate holder was rotated 180o towards the other 
side of another pair of vertical titanium targets (99.5% purity) for the formation of overlaid TiO2 film. The deposition 
sequence is repeated as many times for making the photocatalysts, as it is desired. The base pressure was still set at 
1.2×10−3 Pa, whereas the sputtering pressure of 0.27 Pa was maintained during the overlaid TiO2 deposition. The 
target to substrate distance was kept at 60 mm during the layered TiO2/ITO film deposition. In addition, for carbon 
doped TiO2/ITO photocatalyst preparation, a graphite target was assembled in the sputtering chamber.

For the MtBE photocatalysis, it was observed typical heterogeneous catalytic reaction for the degradation of MtBE 
assisted by both simple TiO2/ITO and carbon doped TiO2/ITO composite thin films. It is also found that the 
photocatalytic degradation rate of MtBE enhances quickly with MTBE concentration while its concentration is low, 
showing a pseudo-first order reaction pattern.  On the other hand, the degradation rate approaches to a constant rate 
while MTBE concentration is above its adsorption saturation concentration by TiO2, which usually shows a zero 
order reaction. Besides, for the effect of water vapor content on MtBE degradation, the experimental results show 
that the degradation rate of MtBE increases initially with water vapor concentration while water vapor concentration 
is low, but the degradation rate further declines with water vapor concentration above 0.0125 µM. The similar 
reaction patterns could be observed in the photocatalysis of many organic compounds in the related investigations. 
Most of these studies consider that water vapor can either enhance or inhibit photocatalytic reactions. Water vapor is 
beneficial for the formation of hydroxyl radicals to be involved in the reactions. However, too much water vapor 
could retard the reaction rates due to adsorption completion by water vapor for activated sites on photocatalysts with 
organic compounds. A dual-site reaction kinetic modified from typical Langmuir-Hinshelwood heterogeneous 
reaction kinetics was also applied in the investigation, showing well fit with the experimental results. 
 
For the effect of carbon doping on the photocatalytic activities of the prepared TiO2/ITO composite thin films, the 
experimental results clearly demonstrate higher activities achieved when they were being doped with carbon (Table 
2). In the literatures, it was reported that the carbon elements in carbon-doped TiO2 was not only beneficial for 
changing the optical response properties contributing to the observed red shift of absorption edge like the results 
observe in this study (Fig. 4), but also could introduce a series of localized occupied states in the band gap to 
promote the photogenerated charge separation23,24. The photocatalytic activities were enhanced for the carbon doped 
TiO2/ITO composite thin films. Especially, the experimental results successfully demonstrated that MtBE 
decomposed fast while using carbon-doped TiO2/ITO thin film as a photocatalyst under illumination of visible light, 
which can promote the possibility for using visible light as a light source for pollution control while TiO2 can be 
modified by doping proper amounts of inorganic species.

INTRODUCTION 
 
Volatile organic compounds (VOCs) have been considered as the most important organic pollutants of human origin found in urban 
and industrial areas. They are not only capable of producing oxidants by reacting with nitrogen oxides in the atmosphere1-3, but also 
many VOCs directly cause harmful effects to human beings or induce odorous problems 4. Among the using VOCs, some 
oxygenated fuel additives are now increasingly used for the particular purpose for improving combustion of gasoline. However, 
some other problems are also concerned while these fuel additives used. Particularly, methyl tert-butyl ether (MtBE) was the most 
widely used motor fuel additive in the USA until 1998 and it is still the most common oxygenated additive nowadays in the world5. 
MtBE, nevertheless, has a stink smell even at a very low concentration, and it has been reported as a potential carcinogen as well. 
Due to improper handle or fugitive emission, MtBE is often observed in the air around bus stations and busy traffic area. For 
example, based upon our investigations in Taiwan, it has been found that MtBE had concentrations ranging from 30.3 to 104.7 
µg/m3 around gas stations and from 61.2 to 1527.6 µg/m3 around bus stations. Hence, some control technologies according to 
convince and effectiveness are being developed for solving the potential problem caused by MtBE release. 
 
Among the developing control technologies of VOCs, photo-sensitizer assisted photocatalytic process is one of the most promising 
technologies for both clean energy production and VOC control6-10. Even though there have been many photo-sensitizers 
developed11, titanium dioxide (TiO2) is the most investigated semiconductor oxide due to its superior properties of effectiveness, 
cheapness and chemical stability6,12-14. But there are still some limitations for adopting pure TiO2-assisted PCO process to pollution 
control. The most important constriction is the band gap energy (about 3.0~3.2 eV) of TiO2 too wide, causing that TiO2 need absorb 
the light with the wavelength below 380 nm for the photocatalyst activation. The solar light with wavelength below 380 nm is only 
about 3~5% of solar light radiation received on the earth. As a result, the research efforts on modification of TiO2 have been widely 
explored for extending its responsibility for visible light. These modification approaches may include metal doping 15, nonmetal 
doping16,17,18, transition metal compound or dye sensitizing19-22. In the early investigation 16, it has been reported that visible-light 
responsible TiO2 could be achieved by doping with nonmetal inorganic elements (e.g., carbon), which could cause the modification 
in visible light responsibility of TiO2. 
 
Accordingly, this study aims to investigate the photocatalytic oxidation of MtBE by using carbon doped TiO2/ITO composite thin 
films as the visible light responsible photocatalysts. Series of experiments for MtBE photocatalysis were conducted under the 
illumination of near-UV light (UVA) and visible blue light, respectively. Several typical affecting factors including MTBE initial 
concentrations, water vapor content and reaction temperatures were also evaluated in the study.

For the MtBE photocatalysis, it was observed typical heterogeneous catalytic reaction for the degradation of MtBE assisted by both 
simple TiO2/ITO and carbon doped TiO2/ITO composite thin films. It is also found that the photocatalytic degradation rate of MtBE 
enhances quickly with MTBE concentration while its concentration is low, showing a pseudo-first order reaction pattern.  On the 
other hand, the degradation rate approaches to a constant rate while MTBE concentration is above its adsorption saturation 
concentration by TiO2, which usually shows a zero order reaction. Besides, for the effect of water vapor content on MtBE 
degradation, the experimental results show that the degradation rate of MtBE increases initially with water vapor concentration 
while water vapor concentration is low, but the degradation rate further declines with water vapor concentration above 0.0125 µM.
