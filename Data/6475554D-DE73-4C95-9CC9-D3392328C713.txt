環上的兩個氮原子鍵結，形成一維配位高分子鏈，化合物 3 則是與配位基形成單
核金屬化合物，但化合物 1 ~ 3 分子間均利用 CH---F、NH---F、CH---O、NH---O、
CH---Ag、NH---N、NH---O 等氫鍵作用力以及 Ag---O、Ag---F、Ag---N 等弱作
用力，以及 ion-ion interaction、ion-dipole interaction、dipole-dipole interaction 等
多種超分子作用力形成多維超分子結構。並與廠商合作研發新型高分子樹脂材
料，並於其中添加新型耐米銀化合物並探討熱穩定度、拉張力、等多種材料物性。

Abstract 
 
    This report discusses the supramolecular chemistry of Ag(I) complexes 
containing polydentate nitrogen ligands. A series of A(I) complexes of the types 
{[Ag(L)](BF4)}∞, 1；{[Ag(L)](NO3)}∞, 2 ；Ag(L)2(ClO4) , 3, have been prepared by 
reaction of AgX ( X = BF4-, NO3-, ClO4-) with 2-amino-4-methyl-6- 
methoxypyrimidine (L) in EtOH / H2O. The structures of these compounds were 
identified by single crystal X-ray diffraction. Crystal 1： Monoclinic, P21/c, a = 
12.3354(10)Å, b = 10.8381(7)Å, c = 15.2790(12)Å, β = 95.138(7)˚, V = 2034.5(3)Å3, 
z = 8, R1 = 0.0409, wR2 = 0.0987. Crystal 2： Monoclinic, P21/c, a = 9.2373(10)Å, b 
= 12.2537(11)Å, c = 8.8925(11)Å, β = 107.17(6)˚, V = 961.66(18)Å3,  z = 4, R1 = 
0.0311, wR2 = 0.0721. Crystal 3： Orthorhombic, P212121, a = 6.7825(7)Å, b = 
17.444(2)Å, c = 29.925(5)Å, V = 3540.6(8)Å3, z = 8, R1 = 0.0349, wR2 = 0.0880. 
Complexes 1 and 2 form 1-D coordination polymer structures with pyrimidyl ligands. 
Complex 3 is a mononuclear complex containing two L and interact with another by 
NH---N hydrogen bonds. Both them form 3-D supramolecular structures which are 
linking through the types of CH---F, NH---F, CH---O, NH---O hydrogen bonds and 
Ag---O, Ag---F, Ag---N weak interactions.
