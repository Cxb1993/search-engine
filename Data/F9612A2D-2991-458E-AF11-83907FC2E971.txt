一、 前言： 
有鑑於國內微機電與加工製造業技術水準之提高，精密加工與材料發展技術皆直接
影響高科技產業之發展，又以表面鍍膜技術為一門不可輕忽之研發領域。近觀國內產業
受到國際間激烈競爭之影響，原料成本的漲價壓力下，必先逐步降低加工、人事等成本
之支出，始能平衡開銷與支出，延續產業之生存價值。因此，微奈米精密加工與鍍膜技
術必為台灣日後刻不容緩的發展項目。根據市場發展需求，在高溫應用及高速金屬加工
之工模具硬質鍍膜塗層市場，國內目前較缺乏具有相當競爭力之硬質鍍膜技術。唯國際
間已有朝奈米複合薄膜發展市場需求，故需進一步開發適合精密器件、模具與工具應用
之奈米複合塗層之技術，以因應市場規模與發展。 
在硬質鍍膜種類方面，為了達到高速化與精密化加工之趨勢需求，則以高硬度、耐高
溫氧化、耐磨耗特性之鍍膜為目前國際刀工具市場趨勢，工業界選用的硬質鍍膜大致上為 
TiN、TiAlN、TiCN、TiAlSiN、TiZrN、CrAlN、TiAlCrYN、TiAlCN、TiNbN、TiN/NbN、
TiN/MoN 與 TiCrN [1-6]，其中又以 TiN 用途最為廣泛之硬質膜材料，兼具耐磨耗性、耐
熱性與較佳之機械強度[5-8]。雖然 TiN 鍍膜在硬度的表現優於 CrN，而在成形工模具領
域的應用上，則是以 CrN 的使用效果最理想，磨耗過程中具有低摩擦係數之特性，有助
於減輕加工過程中產生的摩擦力，抑制腐蝕程度的能力遠比 TiN 鍍膜理想，並可抑制水
氣分子或脫模劑中內含腐蝕成份對於模具底材鋼料的侵蝕。而低摩擦係數與良好的抗黏著
性能有助於提升模具與被加工元件間的脫模能力，由於具有比 TiN、TiCN 等鍍膜有更佳
的抗黏著性與熱穩定性，因此可以得到更佳的工具保護效果與加工品質，且氮化鉻與工件
的附著性良好，可以承受加工過程所衍生的較大負荷，也可鍍製較厚的膜層[9-12]。而近
年來其他專業研究文獻針對 CrAlSiN、TiAlSiN 等複合超硬鍍膜的研究顯示，在 TiN、
CrN 等二元金屬氮化物鍍膜中添加鋁、矽等元素，形成三元或四元合金氮化物，不但能
提升機械性質，增加抗高溫氧化及抗蝕性[6, 13-20]，勢必能取代 TiN、TiAlN 等硬質鍍
膜，成為革命性的硬質鍍膜材料。 
因此，本計劃乃利用高效能大面積旋轉陰極電弧源沉積製備 TiAlSiN 、
TiAlSiN/CrAlSiN 奈米結構漸層(graded)與多層(multilayered)薄膜，探討此多元奈米結構多
層薄膜之高溫氧化特性與相變化模式。 
 
二、 實驗方法： 
本實驗採用陰極電弧沉積系統，設計製備側面旋轉柱狀靶材製備AlxTi1-xN/CrN、
TiAlSiN、CrTiAlSiN 薄膜，靶材分別為高純度鉻靶、鈦靶及鋁矽(Al88Si12) 合金靶，直徑為
80mm，薄膜沉積於矽晶片與高速鋼上。如表一所示，藉由靶功率與電流的控制，由鈦靶、鉻
靶和鋁矽合金靶的靶電流(70~120A)來控制薄膜的成份，沉積三種不同成分含量CrTiAlSiN 多
元合金薄膜，如圖一所示，製程前的腔體壓力至少為1.0 x 10-4 Pa以下，矽晶片基材固定於載
具上，轉速為5rpm，基材偏壓為-60V，通入反應氣體後的腔體壓力維持在5.0 × 10-2 Pa。在
薄膜沉積時，使用熱電偶靠近基材量測其溫度，並控制在430℃。在高溫氧化退火中，使用儀

 三、 實驗結果與討論： 
本研究首先設計製備AlxTi1-xN/CrN與奈米結構TiAlSiN之單層、漸層與多層薄膜，進行此
類薄膜之機械性質分析研究，相關研究成果發表於Thin Solid Films 之  SCI國際期刊-- 
“Mechanical properties of gradient and multilayered TiAlSiN hard coatings”, Thin Solid Films, 
517/17 (2009) 4934-4937. (SCI) 及發表於Surface & Coatings Technology 之 SCI國際期刊-- 
“Microstructural and mechanical properties of graded and multilayered AlxTi1-xN/CrN coatings 
synthesized by a cathodic-arc deposition process”, Surface & Coatings Technology, in press (2009). 
[21, 22]，接著研究進行AlSi之合金靶材、Ti靶與Cr靶以交錯位置配置，同時引入氮氣(N2)，製
備CrTiAlSiN之奈米漸層與多層結構合金膜，探討CrTiAlSiN奈米複合薄膜之高溫特性，並且
進行高溫氧化處理，最後分別觀察其高溫氧化前後之薄膜微結構、附著性、硬度及結晶相等
分析研究，分別對為CrTiAlSiN薄膜進行微結構分析、機械性質分析、高溫氧化行為之研究分
析。研究成果發表於Thin Solid Films 之 SCI國際期刊-- “High temperature oxidation resistance 
of multicomponent Cr-Ti-Al-Si-N coatings”, Surface & Coatings Technology, in press (2009). [23]

技術/創作名稱 陰極電弧沉積高硬度氮化鉻鋁鈦矽之奈米結構薄膜製程技術 
發明人/創作人 張銀祐 
中文： 
本研究計劃利用陰極電弧激發沉積法 (Cathodic-arc activated 
deposition)之技術，採用AlSi之合金靶材、Ti靶與Cr靶以交錯位置配
置，同時引入氮氣(N2)，製備CrTiAlSiN之奈米漸層與多層結構合金
膜，利用高離化率之系統設計可製備不同化合比之高硬度與抗塑性
變形之CrTiAlSiN、CrAlSiN之複合奈米結構薄膜。 
 技術說明 
英文：Nanocomposite CrTiAlSiN and CrAlSiN hard coatings can be
synthesized by cathodic arc evaporation with plasma enhanced duct 
equipment, using AlSi alloy, Ti and Cr targets as cathodes. The 
synthesized CrTiAlSiN and CrAlSiN multilayered coating possesses
high hardness (as high as 38 GPa) , low residual stress and high 
temperature oxidation resistance.

Mechanical properties of gradient and multilayered TiAlSiN hard coatings
Yin-Yu Chang a,⁎, Shun-Jan Yang b, Weite Wu b, Yu-Chu Kuo c, Jyh-Wei Lee d, Chaur-Jeng Wang c

Keywords:
Mechanical properties

Physical vapor deposition
Thin ﬁlms
Multicomponent coatings based on different metallic and non-metallic elements possess the combined
beneﬁt of individual components leading to further improvement of coating properties. In this study,
monolayered Ti–Al–N, multilayered Ti–Al–N/TiN, gradient Ti–Al–Si–N, and multilayered Ti–Al–Si–N/TiN
coatings were synthesized by using a cathodic-arc evaporation (CAE) system. In addition to Ti, Ti33Al67 and
Al88Si12 cathodes were used for the deposition of Ti–Al–N, and Ti–Al–Si–N coatings, respectively. The
gradient Ti0.50Al0.43Si0.07N, and multilayered Ti0.50Al0.43Si0.07N/TiN with nanograins separated by disordered
grain boundaries possessed lower residual stress (−2.8~−4.8 GPa) than that of monolayered Ti–Al–N
(−6.8 GPa) and multilayered Ti–Al–N/TiN coatings (−5.7 GPa). The highest hardness was obtained for the
gradient Ti0.50Al0.43Si0.07N (38±2 GPa) with Ti/(Ti+Al+Si) content ratio being 0.5. On the contrary, the
multilayered Ti0.50Al0.43Si0.07N/TiN possessed the highest H3/E⁎2 ratio of 0.182±0.003 GPa, indicating the
best resistance to plastic deformation, among the studied coatings.

1. Introduction
Industrial applications of monolayered TiAlN and multilayered
TiAlN/TiN coatings synthesized by physical vapor deposition (PVD)
are increasing rapidly due to their advanced mechanical properties
and high temperature oxidation resistance [1–4]. Those materials are
based on fcc TiN structure, where the addition of Al in the form of solid
solution has been shown to increase the oxidation resistance.
Consequently, the incorporation of aluminum in the cubic fcc TiN
structure leads to enhance thermal stability of the coating [5,6].
Recently, hard coatings such as TiSiN and TiAlSiN coatings have been
received more attention because these coatings possessed higher
hardness and better cutting performance [7,8]. It has been reported
that the addition of Si into TiAlN was observed to shift the hcp-AlN
structure to higher temperature and improve the hardness and
thermal stability of the coatings [8–10]. Therefore, gradient and
multilayered Ti–Al–Si–N can be used as wear-resistant coatings for
dry machining applications [11,12].
In the present study, cathodic arc evaporation (CAE) process with
titanium,Ti33Al67 andAl88Si12 alloycathodeswasused for thedeposition
of monolayered Ti–Al–N, multilayered Ti–Al–N/TiN, gradient Ti–Al–Si–
N, and multilayered Ti–Al–Si–N/TiN coatings. The microstructure and
mechanical properties (hardness and elastic modulus) of the deposited
Ti–Al–N and Ti–Al–Si–N coatings were studied.
2. Experimental details
Monolayered Ti–Al–N (TiAlN–SL), multilayered Ti–Al–N/TiN
(TiAlN-ML), gradient Ti–Al–Si–N (TiAlSiN-GL), and multilayered Ti–
Al–Si–N/TiN (TiAlSiN-ML) coatings were deposited on polished
silicon by using a cathodic arc evaporation system [11]. Titanium,
Ti33Al67 (33 at.% of Ti and 67 at.% of Al), Al88Si12 (88 at.% of Al and 12 at.
% of Si) alloy targets were arranged on the chamber wall to deposit the
coatings. In addition to Ti, Ti33Al67 and Al88Si12 cathodes were used for
the deposition of Ti–Al–N, and Ti–Al–Si–N coatings, respectively. A
base pressure prior to deposition was less than 1×10−3 Pa. The
samples were mounted on a rotational substrate holder for deposition
of the coatings. The Ti–Al–N and Ti–Al–Si–N coatings were deposited
at a N2 pressure of 2.0 Pa and substrate bias voltage of −80 V. The
coating thickness of the deposited coatings was controlled at 1.3±
0.2 µm. In order to control the composition, the cathode current of Ti,
Ti33Al67 and Al88Si12 cathodes ranging from 80 to 120 Awas used. The
chemical content ratios of Ti/(Ti+Al) and Ti/(Ti+Al+Si) were
controlled to be 0.4–0.5 for optimum mechanical properties.

6700F high resolution ﬁeld emission scanning electron microscope
equipped with secondary electron imaging (SEI) and backscattered
electron imaging (BEI) detectors. The coating structurewas investigated
by using a ﬁeld emission gun high resolution transmission electron
microscope (FEG-HRTEM, FEI Tecnai G2 20 S-Twin). Chemical composi-

3.2. Mechanical properties
Previous studies showed that the multilayered structure of Ti–Al–
N/TiN and Ti–Al–Si–N/SiNx can reduce residual stress and improve
adhesion [4,22]. In this study, the residual stresses of monolayered
Ti0.42Al0.58N (TiAlN-SL), multilayered Ti0.42Al0.58N/TiN (TiAlN-ML),
gradient Ti0.50Al0.43Si0.07N (TiAlSiN-GL), and multilayered Ti0.50Al0.43-
Si0.07N/TiN (TiAlSiN-ML), those were determined by the sin2ψ
method, are revealed in Table 1. The difference in magnitude was
related to the residual stress which composed of an intrinsic
component resulted from the growth process and an extrinsic
component (thermal stress) due to the presence of different thermal
expansion coefﬁcients between the coating and the substrate. The
monolayered Ti0.42Al0.58N (TiAlN-SL) coating possessed the highest
residual stress of−6.8±0.3 GPa. The incorporated Si atoms promote
crystallite reﬁnement and dense structure, however, lower compres-
sive residual stress (−4.8±0.5 GPa) was found in the gradient
Ti0.50Al0.43Si0.07N (TiAlSiN-GL) coating. With introduction of TiN
interlayers, the multilayered Ti0.42Al0.58N/TiN (TiAlN-ML) and multi-
layered Ti0.50Al0.43Si0.07N/TiN (TiAlSiN-ML) also possessed lower
residual stresses (−5.7±0.5 GPa and −2.8±0.5 GPa). The stress
behavior showed a relaxation effect of the multilayered coating with
TiN interlayers.

(TiAlN-SL), multilayered Ti0.42Al0.58N/TiN (TiAlN-ML), gradient Ti0.50-
Al0.43Si0.07N (TiAlSiN-GL), and multilayered Ti0.50Al0.43Si0.07N/TiN
(TiAlSiN-ML) coatings were measured as a function of indenter
displacement using continuous stiffness measurement method, as
shown in Table 1. The hardness and elastic modulus of the
monolayered Ti0.42Al0.58N(TiAlN-SL) coating were 35±1 GPa, 415±
30 GPa, respectively. Addition of Si into the TiAlN was helpful for the
increase of hardness and elastic modulus. The gradient Ti0.50Al0.43-
Si0.07N(TiAlSiN-GL) with nanocomposite structure possessed the
highest hardness of 38±2 GPa. Previous study by O. Nakonechna
