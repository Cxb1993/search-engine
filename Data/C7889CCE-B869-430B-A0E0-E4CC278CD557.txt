Abstract
In this study, the three kinds of the amiphiphilic
rod-coil block copolymers, PFn-b-P2VPm, PFn-b-PDMA
EMAm, and P3HTn-b-PSNm with the different coil lengths
were synthesized by ATRP. Further, the prepared block
copolymers were applied to produce the CdS nanoparticles
as a nanoreactor to prepare the PFn-b- P2VPm/CdS,
PFn-b-PDMAEMAm/CdS, and P3HTn-b- PSNm/CdS
nanocomposite thin films. The effects of various operating
conditions, including coil length, ratio of mixed solvents,
ratio of Cd2+ to S2- ions, heating temperature, on the
photophysical properties of films were also discussed.
In the last year, we had successfully synthesized the
PFn-b-P2VPm and P3HTn-b-PSNm. Their chemical structures
were confirmed by the analysis of GPC, FTIR, and NMR. In
this study, we extend the results of last year to synthesize the
novel photoelectrical rod-coil block copolymers
poly[2-(dimethylamino)-ethyl methacrylate] (PF-b-PDMA
EMA) by ATRP with three different coil lengths,
PF10-b-P2VP36, PF10-b-P2VP68, and PF10-b-P2VP85. The
effects of mixed solvents, DMF/MeOH, THF/MeOH, and
1,4Dioxane/MeOH, and PDMAEMA coil length on the
