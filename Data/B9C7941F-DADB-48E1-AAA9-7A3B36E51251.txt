一、中文摘要 
    本專題研究計畫係以二氧化錫薄膜為
基礎研製電壓及電流式葡萄糖感測器，而
此感測器架構係以氧化銦錫薄膜及碳電極
薄膜為基礎製作。葡萄糖生物感測器係藉
由酵素固定化技術，將葡萄糖氧化酵素固
定於二氧化錫薄膜，並具備可拋棄式及低
成本之優點。 
依據研究結果，可得知沉積二氧化錫薄膜
可減少干擾及提高系統之感測範圍、靈敏
度及線性度。此外，本研究計畫係藉由濺
鍍二氧化錫之薄膜研究研製電壓及電流雙
模架構葡萄糖生物感測器。由於成本低廉
及製造簡易等特點，本研究已成功的研製
以二氧化錫之薄膜為基礎的拋棄式雙模葡
萄糖感測器系統平台。 
     
關鍵詞：二氧化錫、葡萄糖感測器、氧化
銦錫、碳電極、葡萄糖氧化酵素、
雙模。 
 
一、英文摘要 
 
This study presents an amperometric 
and potentiometric glucose biosensor based 
on a tin oxide (SnO2) electrode. The SnO2 
electrode is prepared by depositing the SnO2 
thin film onto an indium tin-oxide (ITO) and 
carbon electrode. The glucose biosensors are 
fabricated by co-immobilizing glucose 
oxidase and electron transfer mediator 
(ferrocen-ecarboxylic acid, FcA) onto the 
SnO2 layer by polyacrylamide. Due to the 
hydrophilic characteristic of FcA, the 
mediator is mixed with glucose oxidase and 
polyacrylamide in a buffer solution. The 
mixture of the enzyme and the mediator are 
co-immobilized onto the surface of the SnO2 
electrode in one step without pretreatment, 
which made this fabrication process simple. 
Deposition of the SnO2 thin film onto 
the ITO and carbon layer reduces 
interference and increases the detection range, 
sensitivity and linearity. Additionally, this 
project is attempted to sputter SnO2 thin film 
onto a carbon electrode. Moreover, because 
of the low cost substrate and simple 
fabrication characteristics, this study has 
successfully created a dual-mode disposable 
glucose biosensor platform based on the 
SnO2 electrode. 
 
Keywords: Tin oxide, glucose biosensor, 
indium tin-oxide layer, carbon 
layer, glucose oxidase, 
dual-mode.

     The cyclic voltammetric behavior of 
the SnO2 amperometric glucose biosensor 
was investigated at a scan rate of 10 mV/sec. 
Figure 4 illustrates the current responses as 
the blank SnO2 electrode (Fig. 4, line A), 
SnO2 electrode was only immobilized with

solution (Fig.4, line B), SnO2 amperometric 
glucose biosensors immersed in buffer 
solution (Fig.4, line C) and 360mg/dl glucose 
solution (Fig.4, line D). According to the 
experimental results shown in Fig.4, similar 
profiles of cyclic voltammograms were found, 
since the SnO2 electrode was only 
immobilized with ferrocenecarboxylic acid 
(Fig.4, line B) and SnO2 amperometric 
glucose biosensors (co-immobilized with 
FcA and glucose oxidase, Fig.4, line C) were 
immersed in buffer solutions, respectively, 
indicating that the enzyme (glucose oxidase) 
and mediator (ferrocenecarboxylic acid) can 
be coimmobilized onto the surface of the 
SnO2 electrode without altering their own 
characteristics. Moreover, the cyclic 
voltammogram of the SnO2 amperometric 
glucose biosensor immersed in 360mg/dl 
glucose solution demonstrates that the 
maximum current response occurred at an 
applied potential of 300mV. 
The experiment results shown in Figs.5 
and 6 reveal that the SnO2 amperometric 
glucose biosensor exhibited a higher 
sensitivity and linear range than the carbon 
amperometric glucose biosensor. In 
particular, the SnO2 amperometric glucose 
biosensor could be operated at a lower 
applied potential of 150mV. Clearly, the 
merits of the carbon base electrode modified 
by SnO2 thin film are the high sensitivity, 
high linear range and low applied potential. 
 
pH effect 
The efficiencies of glucose oxidation, 
electron transfer and the decomposition of 
H2O2 to H2O and O2 affected the 
performance of the potentiometric glucose 
biosensor. The key factors in determining the 
efficiencies of glucose oxidation, electron 
transfer and decomposition of H2O2 were the 
enzyme activity and capacity of the electron 
mediator.  
To determine the optimal pH 
environment for the SnO2 amperometric 
glucose biosensor, the biosensor was 
immersed in different pH glucose solutions 
with a glucose concentration of 360mg/dl, 
which the experiment results are displayed in 
Fig.8. Although the current response 
obtained in pH7.5 glucose solution was 
lower than that in pH7 glucose solution, both 
current responses were very close. 
Additionally, considering the pH value of 
human blood around pH7.4, pH7.5 glucose 
solution was selected as the testing solution 
in this study. 
 
Interference effect 
Uric acid, urea and ascorbic acid were 
selected to evaluate the selectivity for the 
SnO2 amperometric glucose biosensor. Uric 
acid, urea and ascorbic acid concentrations 
fluctuating within the normal physiological 
range of 2~7mg/dl, 7~20mg/dl and 
0.4~1.5mg/dl were regarded as an acceptable 
level of control. The highest normal 
physiological uric acid (7mg/dl), urea 
(20mg/dl) and ascorbic acid (1.5mg/dl)

二、 與會心得 
 
由於中國逐漸成為世界舞台上之大國，重要性不可輕忽，而研究中國之議題
係對於台灣及中國雙方關係發展之重要課題。於海峽兩岸關係中，中國、台灣及
其他國家之間的橋樑建構，台灣之地位及議題將越來越重要，除了因為地區之和
平將對世界安全佔有重要影響外，目前台灣企業對於中國經濟的影響力更愈來愈
大，進而間接影響全世界經濟的走向，由此觀點切入，可易想見台灣的科技教育
的內容和成效是分常重要的議題。 
此次研討會亦進行社會福利政策相關議題之討論。相關資料顯示，台灣在人
口老化、家庭不穩定、所得分配不均的狀況下，又加上全球化帶來之生產結構改
變、經濟低度成長、貧富差距擴大、跨國人口流動，以及失業率攀高等全球風險
曝露的升高，更顯出社會福利的重要性，社會福利主要係用來保障人民之基本生
存、家庭之和諧穩定、社會之互助團結、人力品質之提升，期使國民生活安定、
健康、尊嚴。 
 
此次會議由大會邀請數位專家學者針對不同領域之專題進行專題演講，其中
美國加州州立大學洛杉磯分校（California State University, Los Angeles）Walter Y. 
Kiang 教授之講題為：『The globalization and child protection: Implication for 
Taiwan』，吾人整理分析其演講內容如下所示： 
Walter Y. Kiang 教授，講題為『The globalization and child protection: 
Implication for Taiwan』： 
由其講述內容表示，台灣的社會經濟及政治持續地發展，也間接衝擊了海峽
對岸中國人民的生活，並造成許多改變，如人民認知到可扮演並分享自己生命的
角色。在台灣方面，在重視教育的同時，也逐步開始研究兒童福利相關專業方面
的課題。Walter Y. Kiang教授表示目前台灣因應全球化問題及兒童保護的關鍵在
於現有台灣社會背景的改變，而成效更取決於台灣的兒童福利專家本身是否對小

孩童的權力已逐漸受到重視，Walter Y. Kiang教授再三強調，家庭尊嚴、家庭愛
護、管理照護、永久計畫、正規化、去機構化與預防是有助於兒童福利政策及實
施架構的形成。Walter Y. Kiang教授表示，社會各界及相關單位需具備遠見及勇
氣來面對及解決這個挑戰，如此一來兒童福利專家與其他相關專業所做的貢獻將
更有意義。
