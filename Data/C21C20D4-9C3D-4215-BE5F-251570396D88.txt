鋱離子濃度對 Y3Al5O12-Tb3Al5O12 固溶系統之結構與螢光性質影響 
Effect of Tb3+ Concentration on Structure and Luminescent Property in 
Y3Al5O12-Tb3Al5O12 Solid Solution 
 
李玄閔、陳威宇、吳佳穎、黃啟原 
Hsuan-Min Lee, Wei-Yu Chen, Jia-Ying Wu, and Chi-Yuen Huang 
成功大學資源工程系 
Department of Resources Engineering, National Cheng Kung University 
 
中文摘要  
  本計畫於 YAG 中添加 Tb (鋱) 離子取代 Y (釔) 離子來改善傳統以 YAG 為主體之螢
光粉，鋱離子除作為激活劑 (activator) 外，更進一步取代可形成鋱鋁石榴石 (terbium aluminum 
garnet, TbAG) 當做主體晶格，作為後續發展性佳的螢光系統。本研究重心放在 YAG-TbAG 完
全固溶系統之結構與螢光特性隨成分變化之關連性，建立因不同量之鋱離子取代而衍生出主體
轉變與濃度粹滅之關係，並探討鋱離子分散度、激發光能量、晶格缺陷、溫度效應與粉體型態
等對發光強度、效率與時間影響，並搭配利用晶體結構模擬與理論機制來闡述結構與螢光性質
之關係。 
關鍵詞：釔鋁石榴子石、濃度粹滅、螢光、結晶物理 
!
Abstract 
In this work, we substitute Tb3+ ion for Y3+ ion into the YAG crystal.  Besides being as an 
activator, more Tb3+ ion replaces Y3+ ion to form the host as terbium aluminum garnet (TbAG) 
structure.  TbAG holds the better perspective than YAG.  This study focuses on structure and 
luminescent property with different composition in YAG and TbAG complete solid solution system.  
By varying the Tb3+ concentration, the host transition and concentration quenching relation in 
YAG-TbAG phosphor were studied.  Also, the extent of Tb3+ dispersion, the exciting energy, crystal 
defect, temperature effect, and morphology of this phosphor were related with luminescent intensity, 
efficiency, and lifetime.  The most important of all, the crystal structure model and theoretical 
mechanism were used to establish structure-luminescence property. 
 
Keywords: YAG, concentration quenching, luminescence, crystal physics

四、結論 
 本研究利用固態反應法，在不同氣氛控
制下於1600oC持溫16 小時，成功合成出純相 
(Y1-xTbx)3Al5O12 (x = 0~1.0) 螢光粉體，因為
氣氛的控制而產生不同價數鋱離子。由研究
發現螢光粉中存在 Tb3+ 及 Tb4+ 量不同對於
晶格常數有些微影響，但從吸收、發光/激發
光譜及拉曼光譜可明顯觀察到異價對光學性
質的影響，同時發光性質也受到濃度淬滅的
產生而有強度的變化。

3. Results and discussion
Fig. 3 as (a), (b) and (c) shows typical X-ray diffraction profiles of
the obtainedmicro-scale YAG:Ce3+ powders. However, a pure cubic
YAG phase (JCPDS No. 33-0040) was obtained only with the Ce3+
content x = 0.025 (0.83 mol%) in (Y3!xCex)Al5O12 at any soaking
time. The other calcined phosphors of Ce3+ stoichiometric amounts
x = 0.050 and 0.075 (1.67 and 2.5 mol%) in (Y3!xCex)Al5O12 showed
up the CeO2 second-phase (JCPDS No. 34-0394) at 2u = 28.5 and
47.58. The result hinted that the doping limiting content of activator
Ce3+ in YAG lies in between 0.83 and 1.67 mol%. When the activator
exceeds the solid-solution limit in amount of YAG, this produces
another phase like CeO2. The amount of CeO2 impurity increases
with an increase of x in (Y3!xCex)Al5O12.
