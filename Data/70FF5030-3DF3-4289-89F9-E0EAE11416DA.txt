質子交換膜燃料電池陽極觸媒層親水性處理與陽極乾燥問題 
 
尹庚鳴、陳益鋒、張志鵬、林泓志 
 
元智大學化學工程與材料科學學系，桃園，320 
Department of Chemical Engineering & Materials Science, Yuan Ze University, Tao yuan Taiwan 320, ROC 
 
 
摘要—本實驗分別採用商用二氧化矽粉末(10nm)或 TEOS(Tetraethoxysilan)經由溶膠凝膠法所形
成的溶膠(SiO2 sol)當作親水性材料，對質子交換膜燃料電池的陽極微孔層與觸媒層作親水性處
理。實驗結果顯示，在低進料增濕溫度下，陽極觸媒層與微孔層因添加二氧化矽，使得陽極觸
媒的潤濕性增加而提升性能。我們並嘗試除了在質子交換膜燃料電池的觸媒層和微孔層加入 22 
wt.%二氧化矽更進而在氣體擴散層加入 5 wt.% Nafion，探討燃料電池陽極做不同程度的親水處
理，在不同的陽極進料增濕溫度下對於質子交換膜燃料電池的性能影響。實驗結果顯示，在電
池陰陽兩端濕度均不足的情況下，觸媒層，微孔層，擴散層三層皆有作親水性處理對於電池性
能的提升會有助益；但在濕度足夠的情況下，反而容易造成電池水氾濫、質傳阻抗增加的問題。 
交流阻抗測試發現電池阻抗半徑一開始會隨著電流增加而變小，但到達一個極值後，阻抗
半徑反而會隨著電流增加而變大因為此時質傳阻抗因素轉強。Sol-gel 做成的親水性陽極在低相
對溼度下，隨著觸媒層中二氧化矽含量的增加，性能也會提升；但當濕度足夠的情況下只會增
加電池的質傳阻抗與更多水氾濫的問題。 
關鍵字：質子交換膜燃料電池、二氧化矽、溶膠凝膠法, 親水性處理, 交流阻抗 
 
Abstract—This study is to incorporate commercial hydrophilic SiO2 particles (10nm) or SiO2 from 
TEOS in sol-gel process in the anode catalyst and micro-porous layers for the improvement of cell 
performance. The effect of hydrophilic anode is investigated at various anode humidifier temperatures. 
Under low humidified situation, the results showed that better cell performance is obtained when 
higher content of hydrophilic SiO2 particles is incorporate into anode micro-porous and catalyst layers. 
Besides adding 22 wt.% SiO2 particles into the anode catalyst and micro-porous layers, we incorporate 
5 wt.% Nafion ionomer into the anode gas diffuser for further investigation. The results showed that 
hydrophilic treatment on catalyst layer, microporous layer, and gas diffusion layer can increase the 
wettability of anode electrode under low humidifier temperature so that the cell performance can be 
improved. On the other hand, the cell performance is reduced if fully humidified gas is used.  
Impedance spectroscopy reveals that the impedance radius decreases along with the discharge rate 
till a minimum value is reached. Further increase of current results an increase of impedance as a 
consequence of mass transfer limitation. TEOS made hydrophilic anode enhances the cell performance 
under low humidified condition, while impedes the cell discharge due to mass transfer limitation and 
water flooding.  
Keywords: PEM fuel cell; SiO2; sol-gel, hydrophilic treatment, impedance 
 
三、結果與討論 
Fig. 1 在觸媒層與微孔層分別加入 0 wt.%、5 wt.%、22 wt.%、40 wt.%不同含量的二氧化矽
粉末，探討不同陰極進料(氧氣或空氣)與不同的陽極進料增濕溫度對於電池性能的影響。可以發
現當陰極進料為空氣時，因為空氣氧含量只佔了百分之二十，所以在電極表面無法保持足夠的
反應物濃度，則會發生濃度極化現象，使得電池整體性能明顯比氧氣作為陰極進料來的低；另
外，因為電池的電滲透作用使得陽極所產生的氫離子將水分帶往陰極，因而造成陽極缺水，所
以電池性能會隨著陽極端增濕溫度增加而提昇。如 Fig. 1(a)與 Fig. 1(c)所示，當陽極相對溼度較
低時，電池性能會隨著陽極二氧化矽的含量增加而增加，這是因為電滲透作用造成陽極缺水，
因此陽極需要較多的親水性來保持觸媒的濕潤，但過多的二氧化矽含量反而會因觸媒層與微孔
層過厚而影響到燃料的進入而增加質傳阻抗以至於性能下降；如果在陽極相對濕度足夠的情況
下，Fig. 1(b)與 Fig. 1(d)所示，當二氧化矽含量的增加其性能反而會降低，其原因是陰極還原反
應生成的水量與進料所挾帶的水量使得陰極產生水氾濫，而此時二氧化矽反而容易增加陽極端
的質傳阻抗，導致電池性能的不穩定。 
Fig. 2 探討陽極各層的親水處理對於質子交換膜燃料電池性能的影響，陽極的觸媒層以及微
孔層皆加入 22 wt.%的二氧化矽粉末且在陽極氣體擴散層有 5 wt.% Nafion 親水處理，在電池溫
度為 70℃，陰極進料未增濕條件下，比較在不同的陽極進料增濕溫度，陽極不同程度的親水處
理對於電池性能的影響。從 Fig. 2 可以發現，除了陰極進料使用氧氣會比使用空氣性能來的好
以外，隨著陽極增濕溫度的上升，使得質子傳導暢通，其性能亦會提升。另外，在陰、陽極進
料皆未增濕下，在陽極觸媒層與微孔層裡添加二氧化矽且氣體擴散層有 Nafion 處理具有較佳的
性能，原因是因為電池兩極的進料皆未增濕，陰極所產生的水就成為膜與陽極觸媒濕潤的唯一
來源，而 MEA 經過親水處理後，較能將水儲存在陽極，維持膜與陽極觸媒的濕潤性。 
Fig. 3 和 Fig. 4 的電池操作條件皆為 Tanode-Tcell-Tcathode =80℃-80℃-80℃，當陰極進料為氧氣
或空氣的情況下，隨著二氧化矽含量的不同，在不同的電流值下，所呈現的電池阻抗。Fig. 3 的
陰極進料為氧氣，由圖可看出，起初電池阻抗會隨著電流增大而阻抗半徑會越來越小，但當電
流值達到某一程度之後，阻抗半徑反而會隨著電流的增加而慢慢增加，如 Fig. 3(c)和 Fig. 4(d)
所示，在電流 200mA 時阻抗半徑為最大，然會隨著電流增加而慢慢下降，即阻抗半徑會越來越
小，在電流為 1500mA 時有最小阻抗半徑，之後阻抗半徑會隨著電流的增加而有遞增的趨勢。
這可能是因為氧氣之擴散過電位逐漸升高所造成。而 Fig. 4 的陰極進料則為空氣，與 Fig. 3 有類
似的趨勢，一開始阻抗半徑會隨著電流增加而減少，在 1000mA 時阻抗有最小值，而後會隨著
電流的增加阻抗半徑也會慢慢增加。使用空氣阻抗最小值發生的電流比氧氣為低，原因是空氣
的質傳速限發生的電流較低。另外從 Fig. 3 亦可發現當加入的二氧化矽含量越來越多時，其阻
抗半徑也會越來越大。 
Fig. 5 是將電池溫度與陰極進料增濕溫度為固定在 70℃時，電池性能會隨著陽極進料溫度
上升而提昇。但是當電池中膜與觸媒層的濕度均足夠的情況下，增加親水性的二氧化矽反而不
會有增濕的效果，而會影響到電池的質傳阻抗與加重水氾濫的問題，當觸媒層中含的二氧化矽
含量為 22wt.%與 40wt.%時，陽極增濕溫度的增加反而會使電池性能下降。

還發展了一套 hydraulic impedance 的技術求取 GDL 的 capillary networks
的特徵。 D. A. Caulk and D. R. Baker (GM) 發現 GDL 中氧氣輸送可分
為三區間, 即低電流密度之 dry region, 高電流密度之wet region, 以及介
於其間的 transition region。Transition region 之範圍很狹窄, 而輸送的阻
抗在 dry region 或 wet region 變化都極小 (雖然極限電流各不相同)。 
   我注意到 MEA 老化的研究愈漸重要,這一部分牽連極廣,所運用的研
究技術亦不盡相同。 在長期連續操作下, 觸媒 Pt 顆粒有可能溶解, 再度
微觀 DFT 模型,並能夠與電化學分析做動力學參數印證; (v)以高表面積
carbon fiber 做載體, 以電化學方式將其金屬化 (直流電或脈衝電流), 研
究增進觸媒分散性的關鍵步驟。 以上這些構想都建立在 RDE 的完整流
體力學的掌握之上, 因此我們得以專注在動力學的探討。

A Co-Current Direct Methanol Fuel Cell Model with 
Consideration of Gas Flow Effect 
Ken-Ming Yin 
Chemical Engineering Department, Yuan Ze University 
135 Yuan Tung Road, Chung-Li, Taiwan, 32003 
 
Direct methanol fuel cells (DMFC) is considered as the 
major candidate of commercial fuel cell system for its 
easy fuel storage and low operating temperature. However, 
DMFC is suffered from the low anode electro-catalytic 
property and the high methanol crossover rate through the 
solid electrolyte membrane. Further, the gas bubbles 
generated in the anode catalyst layer prohibit efficient 
mass transport of methanol across the anode diffusion 
layer [1]. It was shown that higher velocity in the anode 
channel reduces the gas blockage in the conduit and on 
the electrode but at the same time, increases the methanol 
permeation rate [2]. A proper flow bed design is crucial 
for the gas removal in the DMFC[3].     
 
 The present work is to develop a simple model 
of DMFC with consideration of the gas flow effect. Both 
anodic and cathodic electrochemical kinetics are included 
with the associated mass transport across the diffusion 
media. Methanol crossover effect is evidenced by the 
decreased cathode voltage, which is explained by the 
mixed potential theory. Mass conservations along the 
channel direction were made on the gaseous phase and 
methanol concentration. The model is validated with 
literature data through a nonlinear parameter estimation 
scheme [4], as shown in Figure 1.

methanol crossover and channel flow effect are avail-
able (Wang and Wang, 2003; Zhang and Wang, 2004;

and normally require excessive computation time. As
a result, it even takes more time to implement a pa-
rameter estimation scheme on these CFD based mod-
els. The present work aims to develop an analytical
formulation that includes all the important physical
features, while being simple enough to retrieve mean-
ingful kinetic parameters for system identification. This
model is simple compared to the above mentioned CFD
simulations because no excessive grid numbers have
to be assigned to the solution field. On the other hand,
the equations derived are based on the mass transport
phenomena and associated electrochemical reactions;
all the parameters are physically originated. This is
different from previous works, for example in
Squadrito et al. (1999) and Argyropoulos et al. (2002),
where two empirical constants are used to account for
the so called “mass transport overpotential.” In addi-
tion, the present algebraic model includes the axial
channel flow effect in contrast to the above mentioned
empirical models that only consider the mass transfer
normal to the membrane electrode assembly.
1. The Algebraic Model
The model region is sketched in Figure 1 with
the following assumptions:
• The cell is isothermal and operated in the steady
state.
• No methanol or oxygen concentration variations
are considered within the respective anode and
cathode catalyst layers normal to the MEA, due
to the thinness of such layers.
• Plug flows are fulfilled in the respective anode
and cathode flow channels.
• Due to the large cathode over-potential induced,
the crossed methanol is consumed instantaneously
at the membrane/cathode catalyst layer interface
(Garcia et al., 2004).
• Mass transports are described by the effective
diffusions through anode and cathode diffusers.
• Aqueous solutions are prevailed in the anode chan-
nel, anode backing layer, anode catalyst layer,
polymer electrolyte membrane, and cathode cata-
lyst layer; however, gaseous oxygen exists within
the cathode diffuser and cathode flow channel.
• Anode and cathode over-potentials (η
a
 and η
c
) are
regarded as uniform throughout the cell for a
specified cell voltage.

Conclusions
An algebraic formulation of the liquid feed direct
methanol fuel cell under co-current parallel flows is
presented that accounts for the respective anode and
cathode electrochemical reactions, the associated mass
transport through the porous media, and the mixed-
potential effect on cathode induced by methanol crosso-
ver. This model is based on mass conservation in the
normal direction of MEA through an averaging proce-
dure along the channel length. The calculation proce-
dure is simple and efficient, with the incorporation of
parameter estimation scheme to retrieve important
MEA kinetic properties. The experimental polarization
curves of Scott et al. (1999) correlated well using the
proposed model with estimated parameters. The meth-
odology in this research should be able to characterize
other systems without much difficulty. Although an
algebraic approach is intentional, this work is virtu-
ally a pseudo-two-dimensional model.
