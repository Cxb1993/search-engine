  
 
利用熱氧化法於氮化鎵基板成長單斜氧化鎵奈
米線並且應用於 solar-blind 紫外光檢測器與氣
體感測器 
 
 
 
計畫編號:NSC 100-2221-E-006-168 
執行期間:2011/08/01 ~ 2012/07/31 
報告類別:研究成果報告(精簡版) 
計畫類:新進人員研究計畫(個別型) 
 
計畫參與人員資料: 
張勝博 助理研究員

報告內容 
摘要 
    本實驗計畫描述了對氮化鎵藍寶石基板進行加熱以成長出β-Ga2O3奈米線並
且鍍上指叉型電極以製作成 β-Ga2O3 光偵測器，其 β-Ga2O3 奈米線的平均長度及
直徑分別為 10μm 和 100nm。而所組裝的光偵測器不受太陽光幅射干擾且其截止
波長為 255nm，在 10V 的偏壓與 255nm 的光照下所量測到的光響應值為
8.0×10-4A/W。 
緒論 
    紫外光(UV)光偵測器在各種不同的應用上已成為重要的元件，例如太空通
訊、臭氧層偵測及火焰探測。隨著光電元件之快速進展，使用氮化鎵為材料的
Visible-blind 光偵測器已經到了量產的階段，由於氮化鎵的能隙值為 3.4eV，因
此氮化鎵光偵測器的截止波長約為 365nm [1], [2]。減少偵測錯誤及高雜訊，偵
測紫外光不受太陽光幅射干擾是很重要的，為了達到“solar-blind”，需將材料截
止波長小於 280nm [3]。這表示可以利用鋁含量高的AlGaN合金來當作光吸收層，
但隨著鋁含量的增加，會迅速惡化 AlGaN 磊晶層的晶體品質。 
    近幾年來單斜 Ga2O3(β-Ga2O3) 材料引起了大量的關注，β-Ga2O3 薄膜已經被
應用於氣體感測器[4]、螢光粉[5]、透明導電膜[6]、透明電子元件[7]，而由於
Ga2O3 的能隙值為 4.9eV，其薄膜 solar-blind 光偵測器也已經被證實[8]。對於其
他薄膜光偵測器而言，不僅可以利用這一材料製作出一維(1-D)的奈米線光偵測
器，也由於增加的表面積提升電子電洞分離之效率而有較高的光傳導增益[8]，[9]。
最近 Feng 等人利用直接蒸鍍 Ga 在鍍金矽基板上成長出 β-Ga2O3奈米線[11]，為
了製備出 solar-blind 光偵測器，Feng 等放置單一根奈米線連接在兩個預製金電極
上，雖然得到高光暗電流比但並沒有描述其光譜響應。而本研究中，我們利用氣
液固(VLS)機制對氮化鎵藍寶石基板進行加熱以成長出 β-Ga2O3 奈米線並且製作
成 solar-blind β-Ga2O3奈米線光偵測器，而奈米線的物理性質及光偵測器光電性
質將被一一探討。 
實驗 
    本實驗中所使用的氮化鎵藍寶石基板是利用有機金屬化學氣相沉積法
(MOCVD)在藍寶石基板上先鍍上 30nm 氮化鎵低溫緩衝層再鍍上 2μm 氮化鎵
磊晶層[12] , [13]。一開始先將氮化鎵藍寶石基板浸泡在稀釋鹽酸(HCL : H2O = 
1:1)裡 5 分鐘以去除原生氧化層，再利用電子束蒸鍍機在氮化鎵藍寶石基板鍍上
3 nm 金薄膜。接著將基板放在陶瓷舟上一起推入石英爐管之中，在通入氬氣的
情況下將爐管溫度提升到 500 °C 並維持 20 分鐘以形成奈米金粒子[14]，隨後為
了成長奈米線將爐管溫度提升到 1100 °C 並且通入 0.8 sccm 的純氧 120 分鐘，製
備完畢後再將爐管自然冷卻至室溫。 
 
結果與討論 
去的研究可證實這些氧化半導體奈米線的光傳導增益主要是跟分子敏化機制及
電洞侷限效應相關[9]， [18]。而本論文的 β-Ga2O3 奈米線光偵測器應該也是類
似的現象，在未照光環境下，氧分子會吸附在奈米線表面上接著去捕獲自由電子
以形成 n 型 β-Ga2O3 [19]，因此在奈米線表面上形成一導電率低的空乏層，而在
波長 255nm 的紫外線照射下，電子電洞對將產生在這空乏層之中，照光之下所
產生的電洞將氧化吸附在奈米線表面上帶負電荷的氧離子，其餘在導電帶的電子
將增加其導電性，這些在奈米線表面上與氧相關的電洞侷限狀態防止了載子的復
合及延長光載子的生命週期。而本實驗的光偵測器並沒有進行特性最佳化的動作，
測量得出光響應為 8×10-4 A/W，相當於量子效率 0.39％。本研究認為低量子效率
與低載子蒐集率是相關的，這原因是由於製程中電極之間的間距 0.2mm 太長所
導致而成的，本實驗如果能將 β-Ga2O3 奈米線光偵測器的製程參數最佳化也許能
實現更大的響應及量子效率。 
結論 
    總結本論文描述了對氮化鎵藍寶石基板進行加熱以成長出β-Ga2O3奈米線並
且鍍上指叉型電極以製作成 β-Ga2O3 光偵測器，其 β-Ga2O3 奈米線的平均長度及
直徑分別為 10μm 和 100nm。而所組裝的光偵測器不受太陽光幅射干擾且其截止
波長為 255nm，在 10V 的偏壓與 255nm 的光照下所量測到的光響應值為
8.0×10-4A/W。

and electrical properties of Zn-In-Sn-O film

1. Introduction 
Annealed can improve the electrical properties of TCO 
films, but higher temperature may result in the deterioration 
of film crystallization [1-2].  In addition, some TCO films 
were treated by plasma ions bombarded to enhance the film 
conductivity [3-4].  However, the above treatments 
consumed more power and needed expensive equipments.   
The application of bias-crystallization mechanism 
(BCM) was a familiar technology that has been used in 
different fields in the past, such as electrical discharge 
machining [5], welding [6] and drawn wire [7].  Notably, 
the post-treatment of TCO film using a biasing technology 
has still not studied, and in particular, BCM process at 
room temperature is worthy of further investigation.  In 
this work, ZITO/In samples were treated by biasing 
technology to adjust the quality.  The effects of the 
bias-crystallization mechanism (BCM) on ZITO film were 
investigated to understand the contribution of ions diffusion.  
In addition, the efficiencies of BCM and the annealed in the 
furnace were discussed. 
 
2. Experimental procedure 
   The ZITO/indium double layers were deposited onto 
the glass substrate.  Firstly, the indium layer of 600 nm 
thickness was deposited by the DC magnetron sputter 
deposition as the current transmittal layer.  And then, the 
160 nm ZITO film was performed at a pressure of 1.9×10-1 
pa by RF power of 80 W (for ZnO target) and DC power of 
40 W (for ITO target) using co-sputtering system 
(ULVAC/Model ACS-4000-C3) [8].  Figure 1 shows the 
schematic illustration (Cross-section view and top view) 
and the photograph of bias-crystallization mechanism 
(BCM) for ZITO film.  The positive and negative poles 
were sited on the middle edge of the indium layer, 
respectively.  The electrical current passed through from 
positive pole to negative pole via the indium layer 
(impedance). 
 
3. Results and discussions 
   Table1 shows the resistivity of ZITO/In samples under 
the different biasing duration (0~30 min).  The resistivity 
of un-biased ZITO film was 3.08×10-4 Ω*cm.  As the 
biased of 4 V for 10 min, the resistivity of ZITO film 
reduced to 1.24×10-4 Ω*cm.  With increasing the biasing 
duration, the film resistivity gradually reduced and then had 
a stable value after biasing duration of 20 min.  This result 
shows the BCM can promote the indium atoms to diffuse 
into upper ZITO film.  Additionally, the reduction of film 
resistivity may attribute to the improvement of ZITO 
crystallization by BCM.  The un-biased ZITO/In sample 
and the ZITO/In sample with biased of 4 V for 20 min were 
subjected to XRD analysis as shown in Figure 2.  After 
biased, the intensity of In2O3 and In phases substantially 
decreased.  The main reason is that the BCM caused the 
indium to form In2O3 structures and embedded into ZITO 
matrix, and deteriorated the crystallization of ZITO/In 
sample.   
The depth profile of the elements distribution for 
un-biased and biased ZITO/In film (4 V for 20 min) was 
performed using SIMS (Figure 3).  After biased (Figure 3 
(b)), the variation of all elements distribution mainly 
occurred in the surface (Zone II) and the interface (Zone I) 
between ZITO film and indium layer.  In zone I, it clearly 
showed that the content of zinc, indium and tin ions in the 
bottom film region had increased.  In particular, the 
variation of zinc ions was more obvious, and its diffusion 
path was about 50 nm.  The variation of zinc, indium and 
tin ions content in zone II (in surface region) was affected 
and their diffusion path was about 38 nm.  These results 
were attributed to the BCM.  In addition to indium layer, 
BCM also caused the migration of other metal atoms.  
Due to micro SnOx phases combined with In2O3 phases 
easily, some tin ions had diffused from interface region into 
ZITO matrix. [8-9].
