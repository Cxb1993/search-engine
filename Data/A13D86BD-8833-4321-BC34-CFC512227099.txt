Study on the application of rockmass discontinuum model on granular debris flows

一、中文摘要 
台灣地區由於受到板塊擠壓，造就出陡峻
的地形與脆弱多變的地質環境，破碎之地
層造成斷層、層面、節理、張裂縫、或剪
裂縫等弱面幾乎無所不在，於自集集地震
後，台灣山區岩體受到劇烈擾動而更加破
碎，近幾年來數次的颱風事件，經常導致
山區發生大規模的山崩與土石流，導致各
地的受災範圍與程度均遠較過去為烈，嚴
重地危害到民眾生命與財產的安全。尤其
是 2008 年間之薔蜜、辛樂克、卡玫基颱
風等在台灣北中南地區均造成嚴重之崩塌
與土石流災害。而民國 2009 年 8 月 8 日
莫拉克颱風來襲，更造成南台灣包含南
投、高雄、與屏東等縣市嚴重的災害，此
次莫拉克颱風所造成人員與設施的傷亡災
害，為歷年罕見，兩千多公厘之豪大降雨
量造成南台灣地區多處崩塌、土石流、堰
塞湖潰堤、洪水等災害。 
傳統以連續體觀念發展之分析方法對台灣
地區破碎岩盤問題之適用性受到相當之侷
限，諸如中部地區大甲溪流域嚴重之崩
塌、落石與土石流問題，或是邊坡發生崩
塌後，其崩塌材料之移動範圍與可能影響
範圍等，因此針對裂隙岩體等非連續體問
題發展合適台灣地區之非連續體分析模式
有相當之必要性。本研究將以非連續體顆
粒力學方法進行土石流運動機制之研究，
非連續體顆粒力學方法將土石流視為不連
續體，土石流之組成為許多不連續的顆粒
材料堆積，於土石流運行中，土石顆粒間
可允許大變位及錯動，較適合台灣地區多
以礫石為主體之土石流運動型式。前人研
究成果顯示，非連續體分析模式可考慮塊
體間接觸與碰撞之力學特性，對於落石與
土石流等大變形問題之分析有其優越性。 
本研究計畫擬以不連續變形分析法為理論
發展以不連續變形分析法(Discontinuous 
Deformation Analysis, DDA)為理論之岩體
工程非連續體數值分析方法，除修改 DDA 
理論與分析方法之相關限制外，並進一步
推導理論與撰寫分析程式，申請者於先前
計畫中已初步完成顆粒版本之不連續變形
分析法理論與分析程式。 
本研究上年度已進行擬時間積分法
(Fictitious Time Integration Method, FTIM)
理論與數值方法研究，本年度進一步引入
擬時間動力分析法系列之擬時間積分法與
流形指數收斂演算法，此兩種方法皆是以
加入一個時間維度的概念所發展的方法，
不需計算 Jacobian 之反矩陣，使得數值方
法對於求解非線性問題上有更好的處理空
間，而在求解較為病態之非線性代數問題
時更是有效。本研究藉由擬時間動力分析
系列方法處理病態問題之優越能力與抗噪
之特性，嘗試與 DDA 方法進行結合，以
其改良 DDA 於處理土石流顆粒間之剛性
接觸碰撞問題所引致之病態問題，進一步
改良 DDA 法數值計算之穩定度，之後再
進一步將流體機制與不連續變形分析法進
行結合，並推導流體機制與不連續塊體顆
粒間之力學互制關係，最後將本研究所建
立之理論與分析程式應用於土石流問題之
運動機制之研究。 
 
關鍵詞: 土石流，顆粒力學，不連續變形
分析法，接觸碰撞，擬時間動力分析法。

台灣地區自集集地震後，岩盤受到劇
烈擾動而非常破碎，近幾年來數次的颱風

較過去為烈，嚴重地危害到民眾生命與財
產的安全。尤其是近年來受全球氣候變遷

4.1.2 擬時間動力分析法 
在數值方法蓬勃發展的現今，各類學
門對於求解線性與非線性代數問題之研究
方法不斷推陳出新，擬時間積分法即為最
新發展方法之一，此方法是由劉進賢教授
與 Satya N. Atluri 教授(2008)所發展，可以
使用於求解各類工程問題、探討許多自然
現象之描述等，其原理易理解且因不需計
算傳統牛頓法中 Jacobian 矩陣導數之問
題，故能大幅降低數值計算之時間並提高
方法之穩定性與精確性。 
擬時間積分法其最大之特色在於採用
了動力系統(dynamical system)的觀念來求
解非線性代數方程，使過去之數值方法需
利用疊代的方式進而求解方程式，於此法
為透過積分之過程進而求其解。因採用動
力系統時間積分進行計算，不但使此法之
物理意義清晰，也使之不需求解 Jacobian
矩陣導數，對於分析系統中之病態問題有
更佳的改善空間與求解能力。 
流形指數收斂演算法(Manifold-Based 
Exponentially Convergent Algorithm, 
MBECA)是用來求解非線性代數方程的問
題，此方法建立在創造出一個空間-時間的
流形，利用引入一虛擬時間函數，將代數
方程轉化為一常微分方程系統，MBECA
無須做 Jacobian 矩陣的反矩陣，並且它有
指數收斂的優點。 
本研究上年度已進行擬時間積分法理
論與數值方法研究，本年度進一步引入擬
時間動力分析法系列之擬時間積分法與流
形指數收斂演算法，此兩種方法皆是以加
入一個時間維度的概念所發展的方法，不
需計算 Jacobian 之反矩陣，使得數值方法
對於求解非線性問題上有更好的處理空
間，而在求解較為病態之非線性代數問題
時更是有效。

1  Introduction 34 
For solving engineering problems, numerical methods used in the computational 35 
mechanics, as demonstrated by Zhu, Zhang and Atluri [1], Atluri [2], Atluri and Shen 36 
[3], and Atluri, Liu and Han [4], lead to the solution of a system of linear algebraic 37 
equations for a linear problem, and of a Non-Linear Algebraic Equations (NAEs) 38 
system for a non-linear problem. Besides, to deal with many practical nonlinear 39 
engineering problems, the methods such as the finite element method, the boundary 40 
element method, the distinct element method, and the meshless method, etc., also lead 41 
to a system of NAEs. 42 
Over the past years, many contributions have been made towards the numerical 43 
solutions of NAEs. Among these methods, Newton's method, also known as the 44 
Newton–Raphson method, is perhaps the best known one for finding successively better 45 
approximations to the solutions of a real-valued non-linear system. Since it converges 46 
quadratically, Newton's method can often converge remarkably quickly if the initial 47 
guess is sufficiently close to the nonlinear solution. However, the Newton-like 48 
algorithm is sensitive to the initial guess of solution, and it is very expensive in the 49 
computations of the Jacobian matrix and its inverse at each iterative step. In addition, 50 
Newton’s method may stagnate at a point, at which the solution search may fail because 51 
a singular Jacobian matrix is encountered, and modifications of the Newton’s method, 52 
such as the arc-length methods or Jacobian-Free Newton-Krylov method [5-6] have 53 
been extensively developed for this purpose. 54 
For solving a large system of NAEs, a novel time integration method named the 55 
Fictitious Time Integration Method (FTIM) has been proposed by Liu and Atluri [7]. 56 
Based on a novel continuation method, the FTIM embeds the linear or non-linear 57 
algebraic equations into a system of nonautonomous first order ordinary differential 58 
equations (ODEs) by introducing a time-like or fictitious variable. Since many 59 
numerical methods lead to the solution of a system of linear or non-linear algebraic 60

In this paper, an Exponentially Convergent Scalar Homotopy Algorithm (ECSHA) for 97 
solving determinate/indeterminate system of non-linear algebraic equations is proposed. 98 
The ECSHA is based on the construction of a scalar homotopy function to transform a 99 
vector function into a time-dependent scalar function by introducing a fictitious 100 
time-like variable. Taking advantages of the time-dependent scalar function, the 101 
proposed ECSHA does not need to calculate the inverse of the Jacobian matrix for every 102 
time step, such that it can greatly reduce the cost of the computational time and has a 103 
great advantage, in that it is exponentially convergent. Moreover, the ECSHA can have 104 
the same important features as the FTIM for its ability to solve a large class of problems 105 
effectively and it is insensitive to the guessing of initial solution. It is thus easy to find 106 
the solutions through ECSHA. Since the ECSHA includes the first derivative of the 107 
target function in its solution search, the performance can be dramatically improved, 108 
even while a little computational cost is required to calculate the Jacobian matrix. 109 
Illustration examples demonstrate that the ECSHA can dramatically improve the 110 
convergence and has a better performance than the FTIM and Newton’s method. 111 
Several applications, including finding the solutions of NAEs in one, two, and three 112 
variables, the solution of underdetermined nonlinear equations, and the solution of the 113 
first-kind Fredholm integral equation, are conducted. Results obtained show that the 114 
presently proposed ECSHA is highly efficient to find the true solutions, and it can also 115 
significantly improve the accuracy as well as the convergence. 116

5  Conclusions 438 
In this study, an Exponentially Convergent Scalar Homotopy Algorithm (ECSHA) for 439 
solving determinate/indeterminate system of non-linear algebraic equations is proposed. 440 
The important fundamental concepts and the construction of the ECSHA are clearly 441 
addressed. Several applications, including finding the roots of non-linear algebraic 442 
equations in single, two, and three variables, the solution of underdetermined nonlinear 443 
equations, and the solution of the first-kind Fredholm integral equation, are considered. 444 
Findings from this study are drawn as follows. 445 
1. The ECSHA is based on the construction of a scalar homotopy function to 446 
transform a vector function into a time-dependent scalar function by introducing a 447 
fictitious time variable. With the novel formulation proposed in this study, the 448 
ECSHA includes the first derivative of the target function in its solution search and 449 
avoids computing the inverse of the Jacobian matrix, which can retain the 450 
numerical stability in cases where the Jacobian matrix is close to zero.  451 
2. Unlike Newton’s method, the proposed new ECSHA does not need to calculate the 452 
inverse of the Jacobian matrix for every time step and it may greatly reduce the 453 
cost of the computational time. Besides, the ECSHA has a great advantage that it is 454 
exponentially convergent. 455
