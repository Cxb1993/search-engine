 Abstract 
The unsteady two-dimensional Navier-Stokes equations and Navier-Stokes type 
model equations containing the convective inertial force and the viscous force for 
porous flows were solved numerically to simulation the propagation of water waves 
over a rippled porous bed.  The exact free surface and interfacial boundary 
conditions between the water and the porous media are in complete form.  A 
piston-type wave maker was set-up in the computational domain to generate the 
incident waves.  In present report we focus on the propagation of solitary wave over 
an impermeable or a permeable rippled bed.  Besed on the numerical results, we try 
to provide information for understanding the possible mechanism of sediment 
transport arround the rippled bed.  The main results showed that when the rippled 
bed is permeable, the size of the vortices generated at both the weather and lee sides 
of the porous ripples are smaller than those on the impermeable rippled bed, because 
the flow is allowed to penetrate into or through the porous bed.  The sediment 
transport under a solitary wave is in the opposite direction of the wave propagation 
direction.  This is different from the case of rigular wave where the sediment 
transport is in the same direction as the wave. 
 
Keywords：water wave, rippled porous bed, sediment transport

圖 3  追蹤質點於砂漣兩砂漣漣峰間的初始擺放位置 
 
承上所述，擺放追蹤質點（ 1P ~ 10P ）的初始位置展示如圖 3，並隨著流場移動（圖 4）。
當中，追蹤質點的軌跡標示數字（0,1,2）代表追蹤質點在各個時刻的位置，其時間間隔為
2/1)/(4 −ohg 。觀察圖 4 追蹤質點的軌跡，隱含圖 1 及圖 2 之往覆渦流流場變化情形，意即，
靠近砂漣底床之流體質點隨著順時渦流而抬升，並順著流（current）朝波浪前進之相反方向
傳輸。此現象有別於過去研究（規則波通過剛性透水砂漣），質點朝向岸方向（onshore）傳
輸的流場特性。在追蹤質點的移動距離方面，如同圖 1 之觀察結果，追蹤質點於透水砂漣底
床上之軌跡特性與不可透水者相似，由於透水砂漣底床附近之順逆時針渦流與形成的流
（current）皆較不透水者為小，因此大部份的追蹤質點移動較小的水平距離。有趣的是，儘
管不透水砂漣底床上所有的質點最後皆朝波浪前進之相反方向傳遞，然而在透水砂漣中卻有
部分追蹤質點（如 5P 、 8P 和 9P ）朝波浪前進方向前進。 
綜合以上流場現象可推知：當孤立波進入砂漣時，底床泥沙（sediments）將因往覆的
順時針渦流而捲起，形成懸浮載（suspension），並影響砂漣附近的輸砂機制，將砂礫帶往向
岸（onshore）或離岸（offshore）方向。
