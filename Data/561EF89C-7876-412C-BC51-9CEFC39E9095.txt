微懸臂造型設計於多重物理量感測性能的研究探討
Geometric design of micromechanical cantilever for multi-physical sensing
functions
計畫編號：NSC 99－2221－E－002－156
執行期限：99 年 08 月 01 日 至 100 年 07 月 31 日
主持人：黃光裕 國立台灣大學 機械工程系所
計畫參與人員：廖先順、丁仁峰、黃宣富
一、摘要
精密且緊緻的量測系統持續地被研究與應
用，在奈米尺度下量測各種物理量的機制儼然成
為奈米、生化、醫學等各領域研究的關鍵技術之
一。微懸臂樑結構由於其靈敏度高且構造簡單，
可被應用於各式感測技術，如力量感測、熱感測
器、黏滯力量測、質量感測、磁力、靜電力感測
等等 [1]。其中質量感測廣泛應用於生化領域
[2,3]，藉由感測生物分子與微懸臂表面鍵結造成
之質量變化來研究生物分子之特性。本計畫開發
一像散式量測系統用以量測微懸臂特性，探討微
懸臂之動態特性與質量變化之影響，並藉由設計
微懸臂之幾何形狀，成功提昇其質量解析度。此
外，生物感測器經常需要在溶液環境中量測，為
探討不同介質對微懸臂特性之影響，一適用於水
溶液環境之微懸臂載台被設計開發，可提供外部
激振強化訊雜比，用以偵測微懸臂在溶液介質之
振動特性。
關鍵詞：微懸臂樑、像散效應、質量感測器。
Abstract
More compact and precise sensor systems are
devoted unceasingly. Different physical quantitative
measurement in the nanoscale has become a key in
the nanotechnology, biochemistry and medicine
fields. Micro-cantilever is a sensitive and simple
structure. It can be designed for different transducer
like force, temperature, medium viscoelastic, mass,
magnetic, and static electric sensor [1]. The mass
sensor is often applied in the biochemistry field [2,3].
When chemical reactions bind molecules to a
cantilever, both static bending and dynamic
frequency response vary and are monitored for
analysis. A cantilever sensor system is proposed in
this project. Through an astigmatic detection system,
the affection of cantilever mass variation is
examined. The ge ometry of the cantilever is
designed for improving the mass sensitivity. Besides,
biosensor is usually operated in liquid envirnment.
For exploring the dynamic properties of cantilever
in different medium, a cantilever ho lder is
developed for liquid environment. An external
excitation can be applied to increase the signal to
noise ratio of the cantilever vibration for measuring
the cantilever spectrum in liquid.
Keywords:
Micro-cantilever, Astigmatism, Mass sensor

II. 水溶液用懸臂載台
當微懸臂浸入水中時，由於水之內聚力，水
與空氣之介面自然會產生弧面，雷射光路因此受

五、計畫成果自評
在此計畫中，我們成功架設一像散式量測系
統，在微懸臂感測器的應用中，具有光點小、高
頻寬、簡化校正等優點，同時為在溶液環境中量
測，開發之水溶液用懸臂載台可避免光路在溶液
中之變化，並提供外部振動方式激發微懸臂之撓
性以及扭轉方向共振模態。
在實驗方面，共振強化微懸臂能夠成功提升
其質量靈敏度，比較同樣尺寸的微懸臂結構，共
振強化微懸臂可提升 6.2 倍之質量解析度。利用
水溶液用懸臂載台，我們能夠量測溶液介質對於
微懸臂振動特性之影響。根據目前基礎實驗結
果，我們認為像散式量測系統配合微懸臂感測器
的改良設計是具有應用潛力之精密量測技術。

For comprehending the influence of the mass variation on the 
mass sensitivity, a 20µm×20µm square mass-tuning hole is 
designed on the free end of the cantilever as shown in Fig.1. 
According to its hole size and the cantilever density, a mass 
reduction ∆m of -932 pg can be realized. For analyzing the 
dynamic performance of the cantilever with a non-uniform 
cross-section, the elaborate finite element analytical (FEA) 
software (MULTIPHYSICS, COMSOL) is used to evaluate 
the influence on the variation of the resonant frequency. For 
creating a water environment, a 800 µm-diameter water 
sphere is set around the cantilever. Figure 2 shows the 
influence of the mass variation on the frequency shifts ∆fn of

Hz is about 77 times the frequency shift ∆f1 of the 
fundamental one. The mass sensitivity Sn can be defined by 
the frequency shift ∆fn per unit mass variation ∆m, and the 
mass sensitivities of fifth resonance and first resonance are

III. EXPERIMENTAL SETUP 
The optical lever technique applied in AFM is very 
sensitive to the angular deflection, which is induced by the 
higher order resonance on the cantilever.  Figure 3 shows our 
experimental setup with the AFM system (MFP-3D, Asylum 
Research) and its signal processing configuration. The 
cantilever is held between the glass and the base and 
surrounded by the water drop. A laser beam is focused on the 
cantilever, and it is reflected on the position sensing detector 
(PSD) with four segmented photodiodes (A,B,C,D). 
According to the beam energy distribution, the PSD deduces 
four signals UA, UB, UC, and UD. And through corresponding 
algorithmic signal processing, the bending deflection can be 
detected by UBend=((UA+UB)-(UC+UD)) and the lateral torsion 
by ULT=((UA+UC)-(UB+UD)).  Furthermore, the deduced 
signal is transformed by the 16 bits analog-to-digital 
converter (ADC) with 5 MHz sampling rate in the AFM 
controller. And the 131072 sampling data of the thermal 
fluctuation spectrum are processed by the fast Fourier 
transform (FFT). By using the simple harmonic oscillator 
(SHO) fitting curve, the resonant frequency and the Q-factor 
can be calculated from the derived spectrum data.   
 
Figure 4 shows the modified tipless cantilever (Arrow TL1Au, 
NanoWorld) with the mass-tuning hole, which is   formed 
through Gallium ions collision in the dual beam focused ion 
beam system (NOVA-600, FEI) under 10-6 mbar vacuum 
condition. The side length of the square hole is 20.78 µm, and 
its corresponding mass removal is 1006.1 pg.

V. CONCLUSION  
Through simulated and experimental verification, we have 
approved that the high-order resonances of the microscale 
cantilever in water possess higher mass sensitivity than its 
fundamental resonance. Regardless of environmental 
condition, only the simulated and experimental fundamental 
resonant frequencies can well match. In comparison with 
experimental data, the simulation resonant data also showed 
the correct tendency with the mode number. For realizing 
more precise simulation High-order resonant behavior, it 
needs more accurate geometric data and properties of 
cantilever and liquid. Utilizing the developed optical lever 
and the thermal fluctuation spectrum method, the first five 
order bending resonances in water can be easily acquired. The 
mass sensitivity -5.397 Hz/pg of the fifth order resonance is 
94 times that of the first order resonance of -0.057 Hz/pg. In 
comparison with the bending mode, the torsion mode induces 
less mass sensitivity reduction in water. For the applications 
in liquid environment, the torsion mode with less interference 
is also verified as a suitable method.
