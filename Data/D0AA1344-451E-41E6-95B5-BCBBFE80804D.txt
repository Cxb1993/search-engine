Abstract
Based on contact mechanics and friction theory, this work establishes a new three-body friction
model that considers the adhesive friction, summit deformation friction, ratchet friction, and deformation
friction of particles. In MEMS and precision machines, particles are often present at contact interfaces.
The adhesion force between those particles gives rise to the variation of adhesion and friction at the
contact region. In this paper, a three-body friction model for rough surfaces with particles is proposed to
evaluate the effects of particles between surfaces on contact characteristics.
The results show that the ratio of the particle size and roughness, x/Rq, dominates the friction for low
mean separation between rough surfaces at the same plasticity index. The total friction coefficient
decreases with decreasing plasticity index or the ratio x/Rq. Beyond the critical value of mean separation
(d/σ)C, the main factor that affects total friction is surface friction. The critical dimensionless separation
(d/σ)C decreases with decreasing plasticity index or the ratio x/Rq.

Introduction
The mechanisms of friction between two
solids have been extensively studied. However, a
complete explanation of the phenomenon is still
missing. The experimental work by Amontons in
1699 and Coulomb in 1785 showed that the
motion to the force holding the bodies together
seemed to be constant over a range of conditions.
A significant number of experiments under
different conditions have been performed, and this
friction model is usually applicable only to a
specific interface [1-4]. Blau [5] indicated that
friction coefficient is relatively easy to determine
in a laboratory, but the fundamental origins of
sliding resistance are not so clear. Tabor [6]
pointed out that the true contact area and
intermolecular forces of contact between rough
surfaces are important elements in the friction of
dry solids. Chang et al. [7] developed a new
definition of the friction coefficient:μ= Q/ Fex =
Q/( Ftotal -Fs) where Q is the friction force, Fex is
the external normal force, Fs is the total adhesive
force, and Ftotal is the total contact load of the true
contact area between the rough surfaces. Studies
on the contact and adhesion of rough surfaces are
essential for understanding the mechanism of
friction.
Several stochastic microcontact models of
rough surfaces have been proposed [8-13]. The
most widely used stochastic model is that
proposed by Greenwood and Williamson (GW
model) [8]. The experimental observations of
Pullen and Williamson (PW model) [9] showed
that, in the plastic deformation state, volume is
conserved by a rise in the non-contacting surface
under extremely high loading. Chang et al. [10]
proposed an elastic-plastic microcontact model

Conclusion
This paper established a simple three-body
friction model that considers the adhesive friction,
summit deformation friction, ratchet friction, and
deformation friction of particles. The analyses
results show that the particle deformation
coefficient of friction is the main component of
the total friction coefficient for small
dimensionless mean separation. In this contact
region, the friction increases with increasing of
plasticity index or the ratio x/Rq. The main factor
of the surface friction coefficient is the
deformation of the surface summit. The critical
dimensionless separation (d/σ)C decreases with
decreasing plasticity index or the ratio x/Rq. This
indicates that in order to reduce friction coefficient
before the value of (d/σ)C, decreasing the plasticity
index or the ratio x/Rq are effective methods.
However, it is necessary to increase the plasticity
index or the ratio x/Rq to reduce friction
coefficient when the dimensionless separation is

第五屆國際先進光學製造與檢測學術會議（AOMATT2010），於 2010 年 4 月 26-29
日在舉世聞名的世界經濟論壇 DAVOS 會址，大連世界博覽廣場召開。這是光學-製造科
學領域每年一次規模空前的國際學術盛典、國際高科技先進光學製造技術論壇。會議誠
邀國內外產學研各界，千人聚會。會議有安排國際名家大會演講、中國科學家院士論壇、
六大專題分會場報告交流、展覽大廳張貼報告專場以及世界著名廠商先進製造與檢測設
備專題講座等。這次會議主要分為六個專題如 1. Large Mirror and Telescopes. 2. Advanced
Optical Manufacturing Technologies. 3. Optical Test and Measurement Technology and
Equipments. 4. Design, Manufacturing and Testing of Micro and Nano Optical Devices and
Systems. 5. Opto Electronics Material and Devices for Detector, Imager, Display and Energy
Conversion Technology. 6. Smart Structure and Materials in Manufacturing and Testing. 總共
近 1200 份論文，包含台灣、中國、美國、日本、德國、法國、澳大利亞、馬來西亞、新
加坡等國家之學者。

具抗菌性之混合自組裝分子結構及其製法
國立虎尾科技大學 洪政豪,張勝,魏進忠,紀政穎,鄭
本發明係有關一種具抗菌性之混合自組裝分子結構及其製法，特別是指一種具
有正十八烷基三氯矽烷及抗菌藥劑之具抗菌性之混合自組裝分子結構，其兼具
抗污性佳及抗菌性佳等優點及功效。
[1]抗污性不佳。由於顆粒與水份在大氣環境下，容易沾在元件表面使其磨損與
透光性效應變差，因此如何在硬塗層上進行分子薄膜之表面改質，使其減少環
境對鍍層表面之緩慢傷害，並且不傷害本身之性能展現，為商品極待解決之問
題。
[2]抗菌性不佳。元件表面除了需要抗污性佳之優點外，抗菌效果亦為重點之一
；元件表面之抗菌效果係關係著真菌於元件表面之附著、生長，當表面抗菌效
果不佳時，真菌容易附著於表面成長，進而影響商品之效能。
This invention is related to a kind of mixed with antibacterial
powdered coal self-assembled molecular structures, in particular, is
a kind of positive octadecyl trichloro silane and antimicrobial
agents with antibacterial activity of a mixture of self-assembled
molecular structures, the combination of dirt Good and good
antibacterial activity of the advantages and effectiveness.

塗層具有抗黏附摩擦之功能可作為觸控面板表面處理方法之ㄧ，並也發現具有抗菌與
抗污現象。
