一、 中文摘要 
本計畫針對奈米壓印引導有機分子成長
的方法進行改良，以製作 OTFT，先對電極的
部份進行奈米壓印與蝕刻，形成溝槽後，再進
行介電層沉積，形成厚度均勻的介電層，不但
具有周期性奈米溝槽圖案引導有機分子成長
的優選取向成長，並改善 OTFT 的元件特性的
優點，正確估算與控制元件結構，將可有效提
升元件的載子移動率與電性，未來若整合捲軸
式壓印技術進行量產，將可製作出兼具低成本
與良好特性的軟性電子元件。 
 
關鍵詞: 奈米壓印技術、軟性電子元件、有機

英文摘要 
In this project, we will focus on the new 
technique for the inducement of pentacene 
molecular orientation of OTFTs by imprint 
technology. The nano groove for the gate 
electrode will be fabricated by NIL and reactive 
ion etching. Then the uniform dielectric layer 
and the OTFTs will be fabricated. The periodic 
structure will improve the molecular orientation 
of pentacene. The prediction and control of the 
device structure; the mobility and the 
performance of OTFTs will be also improved. 
The low cost and high performance flexible 
electronics will be realized by combined this 
technique and roll-to-roll technology. 
 
Keywords: Nano imprint, Flexible electronics, 
OTFTs 
 
二、 計畫的緣由與目的 
The organic thin film transistors (OTFTs) 
have become the potential candidates for 
low-cost and flexible electronics applications 
[1-9]. To fabricate the OTFTs with the high 
carrier mobility, numerous methods have been 
studied by introducing the additional 
source/drain contact materials, using the 
appropriate dielectrics, and optimizing the 
organic semiconductor growth conditions [2-6].  
For the growth of the organic semiconductor, 
e.g. pentacene, one of the promising ways is to 
grow the organic molecules with a suitably 
preferred orientation relative to the conducting 
current or source/drain electrode. The stable and 
uniform alignment method is necessary for the 
organic electronics applications. Using periodic 
groove patterns on organic dielectrics to improve 
the preferred orientations of organic molecules 
and device performances had been proposed 
recently [7-9]. The organic semiconductor was 
fabricated on the organic gate dielectric with 
periodic groove fabricated by nano imprint 
lithography (NIL) technology. Comparing to 
pentacene films that are not aligned by NIL, the 
field effect mobility of OTFTs has been 
improved when the optimal π–orbital overlap 
direction of organic thin film is parallel to the 
direction of current flow [7-9]. Besides, NIL

結論: 
The OTFTs with the nano-groove Al gate 
electrodes were fabricated by UV nano imprint 
technology successfully. The HSQ master mold 
with the line/space of about ~110/340 nm was 
fabricated successfully by E-Beam lithography 
and the patterns were transferred to ETFE 
successfully by the hot-embossing process. The 
nano-groove Al gate electrodes with the 
line/space about~ 95/355 nm were fabricated by 
UV nano imprint and reactive ion etching. The 
OTFTs with nano-groove Al gate electrodes 
have a lower threshold voltage, a higher 
ON/OFF current ratio, and a field-effective 
mobility than those with the single Al gate 
electrodes. The molecular orientations of 
pentacene can be improved and the proposed 
process will be suitable for the future roll-to-roll 
process of the flexible electronics.
