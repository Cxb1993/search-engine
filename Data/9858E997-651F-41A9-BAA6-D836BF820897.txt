摘要 
本計畫擬以三年時間利用二氧化碳超臨界流體萃取技術與快速膨脹技術來發展製備
微粒子技術，超臨界流體萃取技術係利用壓力、溫度及修飾劑的改變來控制溶解能力，有
效將物料中的成分萃取分離出來。而本計畫擬發展之超臨界流體快速膨脹系統，則利用操
作條件的變化來改變超臨界流體的物理特性，進而達到控制粒子尺度大小與純化的目的。 
本年度之研究方法主要是改變超臨界流體快速膨脹系統收集槽的環璄，利用溶解度的
改變使天然物中不同成份在不同的壓力與溫度中析出微粒子，可在一次流程中，將天然物
中不同的成份分離出來，同時製備成細緻化粉體的目標。 
本計畫以超臨界溶液快速膨脹法進行分離純化，當達到飽和狀態的溶液由噴嘴噴至一
大氣壓的收集容器時，溶液成為過飽和狀態，此時溶質開始成核、凝結並析出。本研究獲
得以下幾點結論： 1.當操作壓力在 2000psi 且預膨脹溫度在 45°C 時可獲得最佳粉體粒徑約
為 58 奈米。2.當操作溫度在 45°C 時，可獲得最佳微粒子生成量。3.當操作溫度於 55°C 時，
其顆粒變化的趨勢最為顯著。4.當預膨脹壓力越高，粒徑越小。5.當預膨脹溫度越高，粒徑
越小。6.微粒生成數量隨溫度升高而降少。 
 
關鍵字: 超臨界快速膨脹法、超臨界流體萃取、咖啡因、兒茶素 
 
Abstract 
In this project, a technique composed of carbon dioxide supercritical fluid extraction 
technology and Rapid Expansion technology of Supercritical fluid Solutions is using to develop a 
fine particle in material. Supercritical fluid extraction method adjusts pressure, temperature and 
modifier to control the dissolution rate of extraction and extracts the effective element in material. 
The Rapid Expansion of Supercritical fluid Solution System (RESS) method developed by this 
project will adjust the physical property of supercritical fluid to produce a fine particle and purify 
the particle.  
In this year, the research method is to change the environment of rapid expansion of 
supercritical fluid collection tank, the use of changes in the solubility of the different components 
of natural products at different pressure and temperature, precipitation particles. In a process, the 
different natural products separate ingredients, and meticulous preparation of the powder into the 
goal. 
In this project, the rapid expansion of supercritical solution were isolated and purified. When 
the saturation of the solution sprayed from the nozzle to the collection container at atmospheric 
pressure, the solution had become saturated, then solute nucleation, condensation and 
precipitation. This study obtained the following conclusions: 1. When the operating pressure at 
2000psi and the pre-expansion temperature at 45 °C for optimal when the powder particle size of 
about 58 nanometers. 2. When the operating temperature at 45 °C, the available amount of the 
best fine-particles generated. 3. When the operating temperature at 55 ° C, its particles most 
significant trends. 4. When the higher pre-expansion pressure, the smaller the grain size. 5. When 
the higher pre-expansion temperature, the smaller the grain size. 6. Fine-particles generated with 
the number of small temperature increases and decreases.

技術說明
(中文) 本技術利用二氧化碳超臨界流體萃取技術與快速膨脹技術來發展製備微粒子技術，
超臨界流體萃取技術係利用壓力、溫度及修飾劑的改變來控制溶解能力，有效將
物料中的成分萃取分離出來。本研究獲得以下幾點結論： 1.當操作壓力在
2000psi且預膨脹溫度在45°C時可獲得最佳粉體粒徑約為58奈米。2.當操作溫度
在45°C 時，可獲得最佳微粒子生成量。3.當操作溫度於55°C 時，其顆粒變化的
趨勢最為顯著。4.當預膨脹壓力越高，粒徑越小。5.當預膨脹溫度越高，粒徑越
小。6.微粒生成數量隨溫度升高而降少。

Supercritical fluid Solutions is using to develop a fine particle in material. Supercritical 
fluid extraction method adjusts pressure, temperature and modifier to control the 
dissolution rate of extract and extracts the effective element in material. This study 
obtained the following conclusions: 1. When the operating pressure at 2000psi and the 
pre-expansion temperature at 45 °C for optimal when the powder particle size of about 58 
nanometers. 2. When the operating temperature at 45 °C, the available amount of the best 
fine-particles generated. 3. When the operating temperature at 55 ° C, its particles most 
significant trends. 4. When the higher pre-expansion pressure, the smaller the grain size.
