極性與非極性蒸氣在帶高電量奈米微粒上之非均勻相核凝現象 
計劃編號：NSC 97-2221-E-006-087 
執行期限：97/08/01—98/10/31 
主持人：陳進成 成功大學教授 
計劃參與人員：周明翰 
 
一. 中文摘要  
大氣中因自然輻射使微粒帶電，電荷會
影響微粒在蒸氣下的凝結能力，而目前對於
微粒大小、帶電荷量及電荷極性影響微粒核
凝能力程度仍未充份了解。 
本研究以電噴霧法製備二氧化矽(SiO2)
奈米微粒，配合凝結電荷放大系統，讓單一
粒徑的 15nm、20nm、25nm SiO2微粒，帶 1~3
個單位負電量，並以流動型雲霧室來探討帶
高電量微粒在水蒸氣中所引起之非均勻相
核凝機構。 
在探討凝結電荷放大系統操作條件方
面，帶高電量微粒的濃度，會隨著水蒸氣流
速、電暈放電電壓大小、稀釋乾空氣流速、
同軸噴射混合器內外軸距離等改變。 
在電荷電量效應方面，單一粒徑的
20nm、25nm SiO2微粒，沒有明顯的電荷效
應。而單一粒徑的15nm SiO2微粒，隨著帶
電量的提升，所需之臨界過飽和度降低，定
性上與理論計算之趨勢相符合，定量上則有
差距。臨界過飽和度實驗值和理論值的差
距，可能原因為負離子附著在微粒表面之
上，成為核凝程序發生之中心位置(site)，降
低所需之臨界過飽和度。 
 
(關鍵詞：非均相核凝，奈米微粒，流動型
雲霧室，臨界過飽和度，帶電微粒，電噴霧)  
Abstract  
In the atmosphere, there are ions 
generated as a result of cosmic ray and natural 
radiolysis, and aerosol particles become 
charged due to the attachment of these ions.  
Charge can affect the condensation of 
vapor on particles. So far there are scant data 
on the effects of the amount of charge, charge 
polarity, and particle size on the condensation 
of vapor on nanoparticles; the phenomena are 
not well understood yet. In this study, an 
electrospray aerosol generator was used to 
generate SiO2 nanoparticles and highly 
charged nanoparticles were prepared by 
condensation charge magnification system. A 
flow cloud chamber(FCC) was employed to 
examine the effects of amount of charges.  
Each particle carried on the critical 
supersaturation for the condensation of a 
supersaturated water vapor on SiO2 
nanoparticles with diameters ranging from 15 
nm to 25 nm, and having up to 3 unit negative 
charges. 
    In the preparation of highly charged 
nanoparticles by the condensation charge  
system, the concentration of highly charged 
nanoparticles depends on steam flow rate, 
corona discharge voltage, dry air flow rate, 
and the distance between the coaxial jets. 
    For 20 and 25 nm SiO2 particles, the 
experiment results show that there is no 
charge effect. For 15 nm SiO2 particles, an 
obvious charged effect on Scr is observed, and 
the effect is much stronger than the prediction. 
This phenomenon may be caused by negative 
ion attached on particles surface to reduce the 
barrier of nucleation.

之去除效率。  
3-2 實驗步驟  
當微粒通過流動型雲霧室時，即使無核
凝現象產生，微粒亦會由於慣性衝擊與重力
沈降等因素而碰撞附著於壁上。因此為了瞭
解微粒是否因核凝現象而被去除，在每一次
做去除效率的實驗前必須先進行空白實驗
以做比較。以下為本實驗流程之簡介：  
1. SiO2微粒的製備：秤取0.21g的TEOS 
(Si(C2H5O)4)溶於30g的酒精中，配置濃度約
0.7wt%溶液。再利用電噴霧法將前趨物
(precursor)噴出，來產生微小液滴(droplet)，
利用載流氣體(Carrier gas)帶出後，進入高溫
爐，在高溫下(900℃)反應，得到SiO2奈米微
粒。 
2. 微粒篩選階段：利用電噴霧法所產生的
奈米微粒為帶高電荷量，其粒徑分佈為多粒
徑分佈(Polydisperse)，為了避免過高的電荷
量造成微粒因電力排斥 (charge dispersion 
force)而於傳輸過程大量損耗於器壁上，及
可能影響電力篩選器(Electrostatic classifier)
的電場運作原理，因此先將微粒通入微粒電
荷中和器(Aerosol Neutralizer)，藉由產生之
電荷使微粒之電荷分佈成為近似 Boltzmann
平衡分佈，再將這些微粒通入電力篩選器
(Electrostatic classifier)，利用其電場的調節
篩選出特定單一粒徑之微粒(Monodisperse 
particle)。 
3. 凝結電荷放大系統階段：凝結電荷放大
系 統 (Condensation charge magnification 
system)如圖二所示是利用同軸噴射混合器
(coaxial jet mixing chamber)混合較高溫且含
高蒸汽量之氣流，經由冷熱混合以產生過飽
和蒸氣凝結在微粒上，長成較大液滴藉著電
暈放電方式(Corona discharging)產生游離電
荷以調整液滴帶電量。液滴因體積遠比微粒
大，游離電荷較易附著因此可帶比原來微粒
較多電荷。接著混入乾淨(過濾且乾燥過)的
空氣稀釋氣流使液滴蒸發，蒸氣蒸發後液滴
所帶電荷會大量殘留在微粒上，產生帶高電
荷量之單一粒徑微粒。再通入另一個電力篩
選器以篩選出帶不同電荷量微粒，並與乾淨
空氣混合成2L/min之流量再進入雲霧室中。

4. 非均勻相核凝階段：本研究利用流動型
雲霧室來探討非均勻相核凝現象。流動型雲
霧室之上下板各利用一個恆溫循環槽控制
其溫度，上板高溫，下板低溫。同時以微量
幫浦(metering pump)控制不斷流入上板之
液體量，使雲霧室上板保持潤溼以隨時維持
足夠之過飽和度。微粒進出雲霧室之濃度利
用超細微凝結核計數器(CNC)來測量，以便
求得微粒之去除效率。流出雲霧室之氣流由
通風櫃排出。

4.3 空白實驗 
微粒於流動型雲霧室中會因重力沉
降、慣性衝擊、布朗運動及核凝現象之發生
而被去除，因此在每次實驗開始前，先控制
雲霧室上下液膜溫度相同，此時雲霧室中過
飽和度為 1，當微粒飄行通過雲霧室時，水
蒸氣並不會在微粒上發生非均勻相核凝而
形成液滴使微粒去除，量測微粒於進出口之
比值，即可得知微粒因”非”核凝現象(如重力
沉降、慣性衝擊、布朗運動等)而被去除之
比率，此為空白實驗之目的。 
圖六為本次實驗SiO2微粒帶不同電

失的影響不大，而在本次實驗粒徑範圍內粒
徑越小者，出入口濃度比值越低，表示微粒
粒徑越小者因非核凝現象造成微粒損失的
比率越高。

五.計劃成果自評  
本計畫之研究進度與內容皆依原計
劃目標進行，本實驗室成功的設計並達成
以凝結核電荷放大系統使粒徑放大並帶
高電量。同時利用此方法已成功證實對小
於 15 nm 的粒子有顯著的電荷量效應影
響。此研究成功的探討了電荷量對微粒核
凝的效應，此成果在此領域居於世界領先
地位。目前計畫未來研究方向可以針對下
列幾點進行： 
1. 探討不同種類的奈米微粒，帶高電量
對臨界過飽和度的影響。 
2. 探討SiO2奈米微粒帶高正電量，對臨
界過飽和度的影響，並和帶高負電量
的實驗值比較。 
3. 比較不同極性的蒸氣，SiO2奈米微粒
帶高電量對臨界過飽和度的影響。 
4. 針對電荷在微粒表面分佈情形，修改
原本的理論值公式，並能加以計算出
數值，以期能更適當的描述實驗值和
理論值的差異。

Introduction
Recently nanoparticles have attracted extensive studies. The small size 
makes them have different properties such as electronic, optical, magnetic, 
mechanical, electric, and chemical, etc., with respect to the bulk materials 
and suitable for new applications[1]. Various methods are used to 
characterize their particle size such as SEM, TEM, XRD, laser light 
scattering, etc. In the study, an electrospray system associated with a 
scanning mobility particle sizer (SMPS, Model 3934, TSI) was employed 
to analyse the particle size of CdS we synthesis and the commercial 
available CaCO3 nanoparticles. 
Electrospraying has been shown to yield small droplets with a narrow 
size distribution when operated in the cone-jet mode[2].  The powder of 
CdS (potentially used as light emitting quantum dots) and CaCO3 (widely 
used as filler in rubber, ink, and paints) was dispersed in ethanol or 
DMF(N,N-dimethylformamide, HCON(CH3)2) solvent assisted with an 
ultrasonic oscillator and then electrosprayed to form aerosol . Because, in 
the powder, particles may physically agglomerate together or may stick 
tightly to form neck connection, the initial form of the nanoparticles 
(powder or slurry), the solvent, and the intensity of ultrasonic wave all will 
affect their dispersion, and thus the measured particle size distribution.

Experimental
In the study, CdS nanoparticles are synthesized from cadmium 
sulphate and ammonium sulphide((NH4)2S, by sol-gel method with a 
surfactant added to avoid aggregation and a MW heating to control 
the diameter (3.82 nm) and size distribution (less than 10nm) and 
CaCO3 are obtained from two industrial suppliers in the form of 
powder and slurry, hereafter, referred as CC-501, CCR, and slurry.. 
The CdS nanoparticles were synthesized  by the following steps:
(1) 40.1mg CdSO4 + 20ml H2O + 0.5ml surfactant (cellesh 200)→
ultrasonically vibrated for 5 min
(2) 34ml (NH4)2S + 100ml Ethanol → ultrasonically vibrated for 
5min
(3) Mixing (1) and (2) →heating in microwave oven 
→ ice bath 5min → centrifugalized for 10 min
→ flushing with deionization water
→ centrifugalized for 20 min.
Figure 1 illustrates the set-up of the experiment for the SMPS 
particle size measurement . A syringe pump supplies particles 
suspended in ethanol or DMF solution through a capillary tube into 
an electrosprayer. Clean air carries the droplets out of the 
electrosprayer and enters the oven to produce a polydispersed aerosol. 
The aerosols enter an electrostatic classifier (Model 3080, TSI), 
classifying the polydispersed aerosol into a monodispersed aerosol 
whose concentration was counted by an ultrafine condensation 
particle counter (UCPC, TSI 3025A). Fig. 2 shows a typical result 
measured by SMPS. In addition, the CdS particle size was also 
obtained from the XRD patterns calculated using Scherer’s formula 
[3]. And for a comparison, the particle size was measured from the 
TEM pictures too.
