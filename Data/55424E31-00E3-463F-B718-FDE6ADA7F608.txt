Abstract 
The dynamic behavior of moderately 
thick plates in air has been studied for more 
than 40~50 years. But, limited investigators 
have examined stiffened moderately thick 
plates having contact with a fluid. In present 
paper use of Hamilton’s principle in 
conjunction with the Rayleigh-Ritz method, 
energy method and potential flow theory is 
developed as an approximation method for 
the determination of dynamic characteristics 
of stiffened moderately thick plates in air 
and having contact with a fluid. Calculation 
results are appropriate for engineering 
design applications. 
Keywords: stiffened plat, stiffeners, 
moderately thick plate, fluid-structure 
interaction, Timoshenko beam, free 
vibration.
