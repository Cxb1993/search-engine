一、 中文摘要 
本研究主在探討電腦作業系統 3D 情境式模擬設計，並整合專為華人使用者所開
發的網頁流覽器。回顧文獻探討，於電腦作業系統與網頁流覽器之歷史發展演
化，發現在使用者測試過程裡，普遍出現多數非英語系國家之使用者，有不同程
度之使用問題。再者，對於近年來快速增加的華人電腦使用者，本研究會予以探
討其可延申之文化社會相關議題(CCD)。本計劃特著重於調查 3D 網頁情境空間
與其相關之適用問題。嘗試運用情境故事虛擬法(SBD)之設計方法，與使用者量
化分析，針對華人使用者之偏好來設計，並進而改良電腦作業系統與網頁流覽器
之介面設計。主測試評估方面，會根據國際標準組織的 ISO 9186-1:2007 圖形符
號試驗方法之第一部分，用於測試易理解性的方法，來做可用度之衡量指標與驗
證。預期結果希望能呈現使用者其偏好並具特定文化之圖像樣式，甚於一般傳統
的微軟與蘋果電腦之圖像(icons)。再者，也希望能找出其使用者是否具高度圖像
辨識力與滿意度，性別差異與使用者經驗，及其它相關議題等。更者，本計劃也
希望能調查對於作業系統之隱喻(metaphor)使用偏好性，及具有文化特徵之情境
圖像樣式所產生的影響，相信兼具文化特色之介面設計開發及其應用，能在未來
電腦介面設計相關之研究領域裡，有深遠之影響。 
 
關鍵詞: 圖像認知，文化中心論，介面隱喻，圖像使用者介面設計

The average weekly surfing time of Chinese internet users is currently 19 hrs, with the 
largest professional sector within the Chinese internet market being Students with 76 
million users (30%). It is therefore no surprise that the 18-24 age group has the 
highest number of internet users. However, only 3.9% of people over 50 yrs use the 
internet, the biggest reason for not using the internet is stated as ‘Not having the 
necessary skill’ (43.3%). China clearly needs to reach out to this underutilized market. 
As of September 2008, 164 million people have downloaded the Maxthon browser 
since its launch in 2003. It has been reported that 14% of Chinese users have used the 
browser and 17% employ it for web searching through Baidu (the largest search 
engine in China) [3]. The reasons for its success are its customizable and innovative 
icon features, fast speed and the fact that it is built on top of the IE engine.

2.2 Research Questions 
• How will the rapid growth in non-English speaking internet users affect the 
balance of power in terms of the development of the internet? 
• How well can Chinese users associate IE 7.0 and Safari 3.0 web browser icons 
with their intended functions? 
• Do Chinese users think that these representations are appropriate? 
• Is it possible to design culturalised web browser icons for Chinese users?  
• What form would these culturalised web browser icons take? 
• Will Chinese users prefer to use culturalised web browser icons over the industry 
leading offerings from Microsoft Internet Explorer and Macintosh Safari? 
Does the gender, educational level, level of computer experience or computer 
platform of the user play a role in determining any of the above factors? 
 
 
三、 方法與測試 
The web browser icons chosen for this study were taken from Internet Explorer 7.0 
and Safari 3.0, since Microsoft IE and Macintosh Safari are the two most frequently 
used PC and Mac web browser platforms. We then compared these with 
Culture-Centred Design (CCD) (culturally specific) icons that aim to differentiate 
from standardised ones, and have been designed specifically for Chinese users [12]. 
Eight basic icon functions were selected from each of the latest IE, Safari, and CCD 
web browsers, i.e. Forward, Backward, My Favourite, Go/Visit, Home, Refresh, 
Search, and Stop. The order of these when presented to participants was deliberately 
mixed up to prevent guessing by test subjects.

In order to gauge the level of support amongst Chinese computer users for a web 
browser containing specifically designed culturalised icons, we compared icons 
developed using the Culture-Centred Design methodology with those of IE 7.0 and 
Safari 3.0. Analysis of our results shows that the CCD icons had perspicacity rates, 
which were almost equivalent to those of IE 7.0 and Safari 3.0, however, when we 
analysed the data on appropriateness, it was clear that several of these icons had high 
levels of either highly inappropriate or inappropriate: Go/Visit (69%), Refresh (65%), 
Favourite (62%), Forward (48%), Backward (48%), Stop (44%), Search (40%), and 
Home (38%). To some extent, this is comprehensible due to the high levels of PC 
users (98%) amongst the participants. However, we intend to further develop these to 
lower these levels in line with those of IE. 
 
 
五、 計劃成果自評 
The Chinese netizen community is expanding rapidly and has recently overtaken the 
USA to become the largest Internet user base in the world. By 2012, we estimate that 
there could be over 900 million Internet users in China alone. However, having 
conducted a thorough literature review, we have found very few citations with regards 
to web browser icon developments specifically for Chinese users. 
 
Of course it is true that Chinese culture is somewhat different between mainland 
China, Hong Kong Chinese and Taiwan Chinese, etc. However, in so far as they all 
communicate using a common ideographic language and share a similar cultural 
hegemony, we can consider them as a common group with shared cultural markers. 
 
Web browser icons should be intuitive, associative and easy to navigate, in supporting 
the comprehensibility of Chinese web users. With the rapid growth of usage of 
computers and the Internet, designers need to be culturally-sensitive to specific users 
needs [13-16]. 
 
The CCD icons used in this testing have been developed over several years, using 
Taiwanese participants, we believe that these show promise as alternative browser 
icons to both IE 7.0 and Safari 3.0. We fully accept that several of these require 
further enhancements to increase their perspicacity and appropriateness.

一、 參加會議經過 
   這次接受國科會補助參加國際研討會，主要是前往柏林出席於柏林藝術大學於02/15-02/17間所舉
行2009年國際設計研究研討會(2009 the Third International Conference on Design Principles and 
Practices)，除出席聆聽設計領域相關論文外，並於會議中提出論文發表。 
   此一研討會的投稿十分受到各國設計領域相關研究人員之重視，在經過各領域之專家學者的愼重
審稿後，總共篩選出約有450篇之研究論文，在研討會進行期間分別在61個場次作口頭方式發表，
並藉此機會與各國專家學者進行面對面的討論，吸收經驗。主辦單位將本次會議內容區分為下列
各大議題，分別是：Theme (1) Disciplines of Design: The design of processes and human systems; 
Digital, software and multimedia design; Designing information architectures; Design approaches, 
strategies, methodologies and tactics; Theme (2) Interdisciplinary Design: Values, culture and 
knowledge systems in design: the role of perspective, subjectivity, and identity; Methods of observation, 
frames of interpretation and criteria for assessment of design; Fundamentals of design across the design 
disciplines and professions; Theme (3) Collaborative Design: User-centred design and client-centred 
project management; Co-design: designing with users; Theme (4) Modalities of Design: Spatial and 
Visual Thinking; Modelling and representation: graphic, symbolic, logical and mathematical; Computer 
simulations and computational tools: conceiving new objects and spaces等。 
 
第一個Keynote Speaker為來自美國伊利諾香檳大學知名的Professor Bill Cope，他演講的題目為
“Cultural and Linguistic Diversity and New Technology Representations”，由於他多年致力於視覺語
言之研究，對於文化與溝通設計之應用層面有相當深入的解說與分享，尤其強調跨國與跨領域之
合作能激發創意設計。另一位Keynote Speaker為來自德國電信設計研發中心執行長的Professor Dr. 
Gesche Joost，她演講的題目為“Gender Design in Human Computer Interaction”，強調性別設計在
人機介面設計之重要性，及運用性別差異之優點來做適度的設計並應用其於設計上，她也以她多
年於業界之參與實務之研發經驗與成果分享給與會之聽眾。 
 
其中本人所提出之論文“Chinese users preference for Web Browser Icons＂被歸類在Session C
“Interaction/Interface”主題中，並在會議最後一天(02/17)上午11:00 ~ 11：45進行口頭報告。 
 
二、與會心得 
藉由參加此次會議的機會，除了可以瞭解各國學者專家的最新研究方向、題目與成果外，並經由
面對面自由討論的方式來獲取更多與更新的知識，這對於未來個人在研究計畫的擬定及研究論文
的撰寫都有極大的助益。 
 
三、建議 
這一次參與國家非常廣泛，而國內學者專家參與發表篇數約有十篇以上，不亞於相鄰的大陸、日
本與韓國。然而與會人士有許多是由教授帶領研究生前往聆聽觀摩學習，除可透過國際會議的機
會作學術交流外，亦可開拓學生對於相關研究領域的興趣，為使學生成長的好機會。希望往後國
內對於研究生，尤其是博士班學生能夠大量提供充分之經費補助，鼓勵其參與國際學術研討會，
藉由研究成果的發表與研究成果的交流，除可提昇研究品質外亦可增加台灣在國際上的學術地
位。
