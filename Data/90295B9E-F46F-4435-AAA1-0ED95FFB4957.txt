ABSTRACT 
This plan is aimed at the thermal solution of electronic components by providing the 
phase-change material (PCM). Solid-liquid PCM of different melting point is put with different 
contents in the cooling system to test the cooling efficiency respectively with forced and free heat 
convection. Forced convection cooling is used to solve the high power-density component, and 
relied mainly on larger heat convection coefficient and more surface area of protruded fin. So, the 
experiment with PCM shows no help to the forced convection cooling. The adding of PCM 
increases the thermal resistance instead and makes the cooling effect worse. The free convection 
cooling of low power-density component with PCM shows the usefulness to the thermal solution. 
Suitable PCM will improve the cooling effect of free convection. In this investigation, the PCM 
at the melting point of 59? and with the content of 0.55 g/cm2 will reveal the better 
improvement to the thermal solution. 
Keywords?forced convection, free convection, heat dissipation, PCM
