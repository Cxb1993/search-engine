(BIS) are collected and analyzed to find the

nerve. The first year is to collect the 
physiological signals of patients and analyze

modify the algorithms. The second year is to 
build a multi-dimensional physiological 
signal analysis and artificial intelligent 
diagnosis system. Then, we will adjust and 
modify the system with clinical experiments.

actual condition, and check the reliability of 
this system with online test. The finial goal 
is to provide an online diagnosis system to 
reflect the patients’condition and achieve the 
monitor function and guide the treatment. 
The research of this project include: 
1. The building-up of physiological database

EKG, EEG, BIS and other signals into one 
computer program and put the entire 
collected signal to SQL Server in the end. 
2. Analysis of signals To filtrate the noise in 
collected signals through digital filter. Then 
to set up a computer program to analyze the 
change of blood flow, heart rate and brain 
wave and find the relation between them. 
3. The setting of a windows analysis program 
By using Borland C++ Builder or VB to 
present the final result in humanity and 
efficiency. Through this program, users can 
easily get the statistics and discuss the project
