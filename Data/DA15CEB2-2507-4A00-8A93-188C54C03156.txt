三、研究目的 
Based on the above-mentioned manufacture method, the ZnO nanostructures must be synthesized in a 
high temperature environment. Nevertheless, the high-temperature process is not suitable to substrates with 
low glass transition temperatures (Tg), such as glasses and plastics. Therefore, synthesizing ZnO nanostrutures 
requires finding a low-temperature method. Our previous works propose supercritical CO2 (SCCO2) fluid 
technology to improve the dielectric properties of sputter-deposited films, including hafnium oxide and zinc 
oxide films, and passivate the defect states in hydrogenated amorphous-silicon TFTs (a-Si:H TFTs) at low 
temperature due to its liquid-like and gas-like double properties.
19–22
 Besides, the CO2 reused process is an 
attractive topic for greenhouse effect, because of its environmental compatibility, nontoxic, nonflammable, 
and exhaust fume reusability.
23
 The study investigated SCCO2 fluid technology for the first time to form a 
ZnO nanotube by oxidizing zinc thin film at low temperature of 60
 o
C. In this technology CO2 liquid with a 
flow rate of 50 ml/min was compressed to 3000 psi, mixed with 0.3 vol % deionized (D.I.) water and heated 
at 60
 o
C. The experimental results focus on material analysis of the sputter deposited Zn film to verify 
feasibility of the ZnO nanostructure synthesized by SCCO2 fluid technology at low temperature.  
 
四、研究方法 
The Zn films were deposited on a glass substrate by a reactive DC magnetron sputter under Ar ambience 
at room temperature. The average thickness of Zn films was 80 nm, measured by the N & K analyzer system. 
The system can calculate the thickness and the index of refraction and extinction coefficient from measuring 
the transmittance and the reflectance spectra of materials. Subsequently, these as-deposited Zn thin films were 
split into four groups and treated with different experimental conditions to observe the effect of SCCO2 fluid 
treatment on Zn thin films. In this study, all samples were placed in the pressure-proof stainless steel chamber 
of supercritical fluid system, and we filled different ingredients into the chamber for various treatments. The 
first group, labeled “untreated”, was the as-deposited sample, taken as a contrast sample in this work. The 
second group, labeled “pure SCCO2”, was treated by 3000 psi-SCCO2 fluid at 60
 o
C for 1 h. The third group, 
labeled “pure H2O vapor”, was treated with a 60
 o
C only pure H2O vapor without SCCO2 fluid for 1 h. The 
fourth group, labeled “water-mixed SCCO2”, was treated by 3000-psi SCCO2 fluid mixed with 0.3 vol % D.I. 
H2O at 60 
o
C for 1 h. After various treatment conditions for Zn thin film in the SCCO2 fluid system, the

This research demonstrates polycrystalline ZnO nanotubes with small diameter size by a SCCO2 fluid 
technology at a temperature as low as 60 
o
C. The diameter of the ZnO nanotube was in the range of 30–50 nm 
and its length can be up to several micrometers. The H2O oxidant may be easily carried into the Zn thin film 
at low temperature due to the high diffusivity and high pressure of SCCO2 fluid technology. This speeds up 
the formation of the ZnO nanotube in the SCCO2 fluid environment by oxidizing zinc thin film at low 
temperature. Because ZnO nanotubes have large surface area, this structure can be applied to active materials 
in the future as a catalytic agent, photo catalyzer, or gas sensor. Therefore, we believe that the SCCO2 fluid 
technology can be applied for fabricating devices with nanostructures on a low Tg substrate in the future.

成果歸屬機構
國立中山大學 發明人
(創作人)
蔡宗鳴,張鼎張,張冠張,徐詠恩,
李政樺
技術說明
(中文) 一種太陽能電池構造包含一基板、一奈米抗反射層、一p-i-n結構層及一透明導
電膜層。該基板具有一第一電極層，而該奈米抗反射層及p-i-n結構層設置於該
基板上，且該透明導電膜層設置於該p-i-n結構層上，以形成一第二電極層。該
奈米抗反射層用以阻止太陽光發生反射，以降低太陽光反射率，以便提升太陽能
轉換效率。 
為了達成上述目的，本發明之具奈米抗反射層之太陽能電池構造包含： 
一基板； 
一奈米抗反射層，其設置於該基板上，該奈米抗反射層提供奈米化構造； 
一p層，其設置於該奈米抗反射層上； 
一i層，其設置於該p層上； 
一n層，其設置於該i層上；及 
一透明導電膜層，其設置於該n層上； 
其中該奈米抗反射層之奈米化構造用以阻止太陽光發生反射，以提升太陽能轉換
效率，如第1圖所示。 
 本發明較佳實施例之該奈米化構造包含數個奈米柱或數個奈米管。 
 本發明較佳實施例之該奈米抗反射層成長於一金屬層上。 
 本發明較佳實施例之該奈米抗反射層係屬一氧化鋅層﹝ZnO﹞。 
 本發明較佳實施例之該太陽能電池可選自單晶矽太陽能電池、多晶矽太陽能電
池或非晶矽太陽能電池。 
(英文) A solar cell structure includes a substrate, a nano anti-reflection layer, a p-i-n structure 
and a transparent conductive layer. The substrate has a first electrode formed thereon. The 
nano anti-reflection layer and the p-i-n structure are provided on the substrate and the 
transparent conductive layer is provided on the p-i-n structure so as to form a second 
electrode. The nano anti-reflection layer is used to reduce a reflection degree of solar 
radiation incident so as to enhance an efficiency of solar energy conversion.

而具有提升太陽能轉換效率之功效。
