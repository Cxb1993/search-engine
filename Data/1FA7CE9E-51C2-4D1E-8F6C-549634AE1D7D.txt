長時，就容易造成疲勞或體力耗盡的現象，為避免因過度疲勞或體力不足造成身體傷害或意外發生，
工作中應適當安排休息時間。在不同的工作負荷下，一個人能持續工作，而不致產生疲勞或傷害之最
長工作時間，稱為最大可接受工作時間（Maximum Acceptable Work Duration, MAWD）。吳欣潔【2001】
指出，因為不同勞工的生理狀況並不相同，其在執行相同作業時，最大可接受的工作時間亦不相同。

3.1、工作指派流程 
本研究進行工作指派時，假設所有勞工皆為全能工班，即每位勞工皆可指派給任何作業項目，並

可行之工作安排，進而求得總工作時間與累積勞工超額能量消耗雙目標下之最適派工模式。茲將各步

3.3、演算參數 
在基因演算法中，若能選用合適之相關演算參數，如世代數（Generation）、群體數（Population）、
交配率（Crossover Rate）、突變率（Mutation Rate）等，就能有效提升演算之搜尋速度與效率。有關各
項合適演算參數的選用，必須進行敏感度分析，進而決定出適用之基因演算參數。敏感度分析是先將
所有參數項目之參數值，暫時以一可能之數值訂定之，再將其中某一參數值，於其較可能之變化範圍
內變動並演算之，再比較該參數於不同數值下之演算結果，取其最佳結果之參數值為該參數之最適合
參數值。重複進行比較，直到取得所有演算參數之最適合參數值為止。本研究針對各項演算參數進行
敏感度分析結果，合適演算參數分別為世代數:5000、母體數:100、交配率:0.3 及突變率:0.05。

HYBRID OPTIMIZATION MECHANISM 
 
In this study, we integrate genetic algorithms (GAs) and discrete event simulation (DES) techniques to 
optimize truck dispatch schedules for earthworks in order to minimize the transportation time. In this hybrid 
approach, DES is applied to simulate earthmoving operations and generate schedules for dispatching various 
trucks, and GA is used as the filter to screen out schedules that require long trips. Figure 1 shows this hybrid 
approach. First, a randomly generated string representing a particular truck fleet dispatch schedule is 
transformed to a CYCLic Operations NEtwork (CYCLONE) model. CYCLONE is one of the most widely 
used DES methodologies in the construction industry (Halpin and Riggs, 1992). The model is run, and the 
total transportation time obtained is used as the string’s fitness value. A GA uses the fitness value of each 
string to determine the probability of a string being selected in the next generation. GA operators, crossover 
and mutation, are then applied to the strings to produce a new offspring. The fitness value of the new 
offspring is again determined by simulating the CYCLONE model and then applying these genetic operators 
to the new offspring. This process is repeated until the termination condition is reached. 
 
CHROMOSOME STRUCTURE 
 
The chromosome structure discussed in this study utilizes a two-dimensional matrix, as shown in Fig. 2. 
The first row is composed of random numbers; the second row, of the number of project sites listed in order; 
and the third row, of the number of landfill sites. The chromosome length is the sum of truck loads required 
for all the earthwork projects. A random number in the first row is used to determine the sequence of the
