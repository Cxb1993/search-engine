一、中英文摘要 
超臨界濕式氧化是在控制溫度及壓力
超過水的臨界點(647.3 K，22.1 MPa)下進行
氧化反應，放射性有機廢液與氧氣在超臨
界水中能完全互溶，放射性無機鹽類的溶
解度則降為零，有機物分解為CO2及H2O。
因此，SCWO的高破壞去除率(DRE)很適用
處理放射性有機廢液。目前原能會核能研
究所待處理的低放射性有機廢液包括油類
廢液、閃爍劑廢液、乾洗廢液及除污廢液，
因種類繁雜且數量眾多，是目前核能所內
較難以處理的放射性廢棄物，主要是以苯
環類及含氯有機物為主。本研究之實驗是
在 673-823 K和 250 atm批式反應器下進
行，放射性有機物殘餘量、PAHs及其衍生
副產物以HPLC及GC/MS分析，實驗結果顯
示DRE＞90%，添加CuO/zeolites觸媒其
DRE＞99.9%，去除效果極佳。本計畫亦探
討添加草酸等鹽類或將少許無機放射性廢
液，來使放射性核種在超臨界水下形成不
溶性之無機鹽類及形成金屬鹽類沉澱之機
制，經FE-SEM、XANES及EXAFS分析結
果，發現可能有Fe2O3、FeO、Co2O3、NiO、
Cr2O3及CrO3等放射性無機鹽類結晶生
成。另外，CuO/zeolites觸媒在放射性有機
廢液SCWO中，具有副產物沸石孔洞選擇
特性，高分子量的PAHs在CuO/zeolites催化
分解下無法檢測出，因此，便於控制高毒
性副產物之生成；EXAFS及XANES光譜數
據指出Cu之氧化態為Cu(II)及Cu(I)；在
1.91±0.05 Å和 2.80±0.05Å分別具Cu-O及
Cu-Cu鍵結，顯示CuO/Y在SCWO反應中，
只扮演催化劑的角色，未參與抓取含氯有
機廢液中之氯的反應機制。 
 
關鍵詞：放射性有機廢液、超臨界濕式氧
化、觸媒催化分解、無機鹽類沉澱、延伸
細微結構 X 光吸收光譜、X 光吸收邊緣結
構光譜。 
 
Abstract 
Reduction of trace polycyclic aromatic 
hydrocarbon (PAH) by-products in 
supercritical water oxidation (SCWO) of 
radioactive organic liquid (ROL) wastes 
effected by CuO/zeolites and sodium cations 
have been investigated in the present work.  
In the channels of zeolites, due to a restricted 
environment, formation of undesired PAH 
by-products in the SCWO of ROL wastes 
was significantly suppressed. Only trace 
light-PAHs with a molecular size less than 6 
Å were formed in the SCWO of ROL wastes.  
The oxidations of ROL wastes were 
conducted in high-pressure quartz-lined 
batch reactor with/without metal cations 
and/or zeolite catalysts at 673-823 K and 250 
atm.  Experimentally, the 
selectivity-to-decomposition (S/D) ratio for 
SCWO of ROL wastes was enhanced in the 
presence of catalysts or Na+. Due to the 
extremely low solubility of metal salts in the 
supercritical water, abstraction of Cl in ROL 
wastes via an intermediate (R-Cl---Na+) was 
postulated. Formation of Fe2O3, FeO, Co2O3, 
NiO, Cr2O3 or CrO3 precipitates in the 
SCWO of ROL wastes were also identified 
by FE-SEM.  Since the formation of toxic 
highly chlorinated phenols and heavy PAHs 
was notably reduced, abstraction of Cl of 
ROL wastes by cations may occur in the 
early stage of the SCWO process.  The 
extended X-ray absorption fine structural 
(EXAFS) spectra also suggest that the copper 
oxides in the channels of zeolite Y may form 
CuO with Cu-(O)-Cu and Cu-O bond 
distances of 2.80 ± 0.05 Å and 1.91 ± 0.05 Å, 
respectively.  Furthermore, since the 
absence of Cu-Cl species in the EXAFS 
spectra, one would suggest that copper in the 
channels of zeolites is unlikely involved in 
the abstraction of Cl species from ROL 
wastes in the SCWO process. 
 
Keywords: Radioactive Organic Liquid 
Wastes, Supercritical Water Oxidation, 
Catalytic Decomposition, Precipitation 
Inorganic Salt, EXAFS/XANES.
