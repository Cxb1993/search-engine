can be observed, such as the considerations of nonlinear behaviors by Gardner et al.

the effects of non-Newtonian micropolar fluids by Chang-Jian and Chen (2006). But,
the surfaces of journal bearings are commonly roughened owing to the manufacturing
and treating procedures that have been utilized in various finishing and forming stages.
Traditionally, rough surfaces are assumed to be a random process. By applying the
concept of a stochastic process, an averaged Reynolds equation is developed by
Christensen and Tonder (1973) and is applied to analyze the mean film pressure of
bearings in the presence of rough surfaces. The results show that surface roughness
may considerably influence bearing characteristics depending upon the structure of
roughness patterns. Extended theoretical researches are also carried out by Chow and
Saibel (1978) to confirm this stochastic model. By applying the Christensen stochastic
model, several researches have investigated the influences of rough surfaces upon
journal bearing characteristics; such as the steady-state characteristics by Gururajan
and Prakash (2002); and the linear dynamic characteristics by Chiang et al. (2004).
However, the effects of surface roughness on the nonlinear characteristics of journal
bearings are not included in these studies. Recently, the effects of isotropic surface
roughness on weakly nonlinear dynamics of journal bearings have been analyzed by
Lin (2007). However, the influences of transverse surface roughness on the weakly
nonlinear bearing characteristics are absent. Therefore, on the ground of the Hopf
bifurcation theory derived by Hassard et al. (1981) and the stochastic model of rough
surfaces developed by Christensen and Tonder (1973), the Hopf bifurcations of short
journal bearing considering the transverse rough surfaces are investigated in the
present study.

計畫主持人：林昭仁
計畫編號：NSC 96-2221-E-253 –007 –MY3
學門領域：熱傳學、流體力學
技術/創作名稱 具橫向粗糙表面之非線性短頸軸承系統
發明人/創作人 林昭仁
技術說明
中文：
流體膜頸軸承廣泛應用於工程科學與實務工業之高速旋轉機
械上。因為軸頸之非線性運動方程式為複雜之非線性微分方程式，
流體膜力量常造成流體膜頸軸承系統之非線性震盪行為。另外，由
於製程與衝擊損傷等因素，常導致軸承套筒與頸軸之表面為不光滑
之粗糙表面。因此，考慮表面粗糙度之效應並了解流體膜頸軸承系
統之非線性分歧行為，對於工程實務上之應用便顯得非常重要。
本研究技術乃是在提供短頸軸承系統，在考慮橫向表面粗糙度
時之非線性霍普夫 (Hopf) 分歧特性技術。根據克利斯登森
(Christensen)與唐德(Tonder)之推測學模式並配合霍普夫分歧理論，
在不同系統參數值與不同橫向性表面粗糙參數值下，提供短頸軸承
系統(包含超臨界分歧區域，亞臨界分歧區域，超臨界分歧剖面，亞臨
界分歧剖面等)之非線性分歧特性。本研究技術成果，可做為工程界
設計短頸軸承系統在考慮橫向表面粗糙效應並同時顧及非線性動
態分歧行為時，一個非常有用與重要之參考依據。
