面間的熱彈不穩定性。Aldo 模型是由兩根等高的柱體構成，柱體的上、下兩端和剛體平面
接觸，下端剛體平面固定不動，溫度為 0；上端剛體平面受到往下的靜力作用，溫度維持
恆定。柱體頂端和上端剛體平面間有熱阻。熱阻值隨著柱體和上端剛體平面間的壓力和間
距連續單調變化，壓力無限大時熱阻為零，間距無窮大時熱阻無窮大。Barber 指出，系統
只可能存在奇數個平衡解。當存在多個平衡解時，穩定解和不穩定解交互出現。Barber 認
為只要熱阻函數滿足某些定性上的規範，則系統的行為和熱阻函數的詳細形式無關。然而
Barber 的推論並沒有經過嚴格的證明。在第一年的計畫中，我們考慮由 2 根等高柱體組成
的 Aldo 模型在給定熱阻函數下的行為。這個模型一定存在所有柱體都和上端剛體平面均勻
接觸的平衡解；除了這個解之外，隨著溫度的增加，也存在不均勻受力的接觸解，和只有
部分柱體接觸的平衡解。固定上端剛體平面的受力，我們探討這個系統隨上端剛體溫度變
化下的分歧行為。我們發現即使固定熱阻的函數形式，不同的參數也會導致截然不同的分
歧模式，對接觸面間的熱彈行為有顯著的影響。這個結果可以推廣到後續柱體具有碎形特
徵的 Cantor 集表面模型上，以增加我們對實際粗糙表面熱彈行為的了解。

pan of two years. Thermoelastic instability problems arise in diverse areas such as the study 
of automotive disk brakes, the analysis of heat exchangers and the production of metals. Barber 
employed the Aldo model to illustrate the thermoelastic instability at contact surfaces. The Aldo 
model consists of a rigid body supported by two rods of the same height. The upper rigid body 
with a fixed temperature is subjected to a downward load. The rods are rigidly joined at the 
bottom, where the temperature is maintained at zero. Between the top of each rod and the rigid 
body there exists a thermal resistance, which is a continuous monotonic function of the pressure 
or gap. Barber pointed out that this system permits an odd number of equilibrium solutions. If 
multiple solutions exist, stable and unstable solutions appear alternately. Barber claimed that the 
qualitative behavior of the system does not depend on the precise form of the thermal resistance 
as long as the thermal resistance function satisfies some qualitative requirements. However, 
Barber’s claim has not been proven rigorously. In the first stage of this project, we have 
investigated the behavior of the Aldo model composed by two rods with a given resistance 
function. In addition to the two-rod uniformly in contact solution, the system may also have the 
two-rod non-uniformly in contact solutions, and one-rod in contact solutions. For a given load, 
we studied the bifurcation behavior with the variation of the temperature of the upper rigid body. 
For a specific thermal resistance form, the change of the values of system parameters may result 
in qualitatively different behavior. The results will be extended to the study of a fractal Cantor set 
surface model to enhance our understand of the thermoelastic properties of real contact surfaces.

4. 結論 
在第一年的計畫中，利用 Aldo 模型和 Barber 熱阻條件，討論接觸面間的熱彈不穩現
像。在導入明確的熱阻函數後，詳細討論不同參數條件下，Aldo 模型的接觸行為隨溫度變
化的情形。結果顯示，Aldo 模型包含了較先前研究者所討論的更豐富的現像。我們使用的
模型包含兩根等高柱體，柱體下端固定，溫度為 0；上端支撐溫度固定的剛體，同時剛體
受到向下的負荷。柱體和上方剛體間有熱阻。Barber 等人的論文指出，所有柱體都和上方
剛體接觸且均勻受力的均勻雙接觸解永遠為穩定。當上方剛體的溫度達到分歧溫度後，出
現穩定的單接觸解。此時系統的穩態行為和初始條件有關。這樣的分歧屬於鞍點-節點分
歧。我們的參數分析結果顯示，除了鞍點-節點分歧，在某些參數下，均勻雙接觸隨著上方
剛體溫度的增加會有叉狀分歧。當溫度高於分歧溫度時，均勻雙接觸解變為不穩定，系統
逐漸趨向單接觸解。這類的分歧屬於叉狀分歧。我們同時發現，柱體的面積不同時，叉狀
分歧發生的溫度較柱體相同時為低。這個結果顯示，系統的行為會隨熱阻的實際形式而有
定性上的不同，這個結果和 Barber 的推論「系統的定性行為不受熱阻的函數形式影響」不
同。我們的結果顯示，如果系統包含多根柱體，在某些參數下，系統有叉狀分歧，此時隨
著上方剛體溫度的增加，負荷會逐漸集中在少數柱體，造成過高的應力和磨耗；相對的，
在某些參數下，系統有鞍點-節點分歧，此時均勻接觸解永遠為穩定，而負荷平均分配在每
個柱體上，系統較為可靠耐久。系統設計時，應保持在使得系統有鞍點-節點分歧的參數條
件下。這個結果有助於後續計畫中對 Cantor 集表面模型行為的了解。

Periodic Solutions of a Rotor Equipped with a Ball-Type 
Automatic Balancer
Chung-Jen Lu and Y.M. Lin
National Taiwan University
Introduction: A modified incremental harmonic balance (IHB) 
method was proposed to study the periodic behavior of a rotor system 
equipped with a ball-type automatic balancer. Methods: A ball-
type automatic balancer consists of several balls which are free to 
move in a circular orbit. Under proper working conditions, the balls 
can eventually position themselves to balance any eccentricity in 
the rotor. This particular equilibrium configuration is called the 
perfect balancing configuration. In addition to the perfect balancing 
configuration, two types of periodic motions have been observed 
in practice, which are called oscillatory and rotary periodic motions 
for convenience. Little attention has been paid to the rotary peri-
odic motions due to some numerical difficulties described below. 
In the oscillatory periodic motion, the balls oscillate in a small range 
of the orbit. On the other hand, in the rotary periodic motion, the 
balls circulate around the orbit continuously. The oscillatory peri-
odic motions can be determined by the powerful IHB method, in 
which the generalized coordinates and the corresponding incre-
ments are expanded into Fourier series. In a rotary periodic mo-
tion, although all the physical properties of the system are periodic, 
the angular coordinates of the balls are not periodic and can’t be 
expanded into Fourier series. Consequently, the IHB method can’t 
be used to determine the rotary periodic motions. To overcome this 
difficulty, we expand the angular velocities of the balls into Fourier 
series. The resulting series representation of the angular coordi-
nate of each ball is a Fourier series augmented by a term linear in 
time. The coefficient of the term linear in time is either one or zero, 
which respectively indicates the rotary and oscillatory periodic 
motions. In this way, both types of periodic motions can be deter-
mined in a unified formulation. Results: Periodic solutions of a 
rotor equipped with a ball-type auto-balancer were determined by 
the proposed modified IHB method. It is found that stable periodic 
motions may coexist with the perfect balancing configuration. 
Moreover, the basin of attraction of the perfect balancing configu-
ration entangles with that of the periodic motion and form a fractal-
like pattern. Conclusion: The modified IHB method can determine 
the rotary as well as the oscillatory periodic motions in a unified 
formulation. Global dynamical analysis of a rotor equipped with a 
ball-type automatic balancer indicates that the steady-state behavior 
of the system may be very sensitive to the initial states.
