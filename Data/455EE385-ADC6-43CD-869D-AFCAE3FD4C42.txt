利用變壓吸附程序製造無水酒精 
Production of anhydrous alcohol by using pressure swing adsorption process 
 
計畫編號：NSC97-2221-E-008-032 
執行期限：97 年 8 月 1 日至 98 年 10 月 31 日 
計畫主持人：周正堂   國立中央大學化學工程與材料工程學系 
摘要 
  在酒精和水的分離技術當中，由於酒精和水組成為 95.6wt％時，在 78.2℃會產生共沸點，而
吸附分離相較於傳統共沸蒸餾或萃取蒸餾為一種較節能的程序，因此被廣泛注意將其運用在酒精
脫水。本研究先利用實驗方式，藉由對純水和純酒精飽和吸附量的測量，使用 3A 和 4A 分子篩當
作吸附劑，得到不同的吸附平衡數據。再藉由 Langmuir 等溫線吸附平衡關係式利用數學分析的方
式，取得等溫吸附平衡曲線圖及其參數。最後利用模擬方式得到不同的貫流曲線，進料組成為
18.2mol％水蒸氣和 81.8mol％的酒精蒸氣的混合氣體，使用 3A 和 4A 分子篩當作吸附劑，在單一
吸附床中進行模擬。研究中藉由改變不同的操作條件（如進料壓力、吸附床長度及進料流速），探
討各種變因對貫流行為的影響。結果發現當進料壓力、進料流量增加時，貫流時間會減少。而當
吸附床長度增加時，貫流時間則會增加。 
 
Abstract-In the distillation separation of ethanol vapor and water vapor, it can form an azeotrope at 78.2
℃ when there are 95.6wt％ ethanol and 4.4wt％ water. Traditional azeotropic distillation and 
extractive distillation to obtain anhydrous ethanol need more energy than adsorption. Adsorption as a 
low energy consumption process has attracted attention to apply in ethanol dehydration. This study plans 
to obtain different adsorption equilibrium data by the adsorption experiment for water and ethanol using 
3A and 4A zeolite. Then this study obtain the isotherm curve and the parameters by numerical method. 
The adsorption is expressed by the Langmuir isotherm. The isotherm is applied to analyze the effect of 
the variables such as inlet pressure, bed length, and inlet flow rate on the breakthrough performance. The 
simulation results show that the breakthrough time decreased with increasing inlet pressure and inlet 
flow rate, and the breakthrough time increased with increasing bed length. 
 
關鍵字：酒精、乙醇、水、貫流、分離

研究方法 
實驗方法 
  本研究為了得到純水和無水酒精在不同壓力、不同溫度下，使用不同吸附劑進行吸附的吸附
平衡曲線圖，研究曾經嘗試使用 differential adsorption bed(DAB)對水蒸氣進行吸附平衡測試，但
由實驗過程發現不如預期，因此利用微量天平(Micro-Balance Thermo D-110)來進行吸附實驗。此
微量天平可操作的條件為：最高溫度 575℃、最大壓力 1500psi、最大容量為 100g、靈敏度可達 0.1
μg。本實驗選擇在固定壓力、固定溫度下量測在不同吸附條件下的平衡吸附量，進而判定不同吸
附劑對純水和無水酒精的吸附效果。實驗步驟如下： 
(1) 將所選吸附劑在高溫脫附超過 12 個小時進行吸附劑再生。 
(2) 將控制電腦、溫度控制器、D-110 電源打開，與 DRB 軟體作連線。 
(3) 在反應管內鎳鉻吊線上掛上空的石英吊杯，等量測重量穩定不再變動後，進行歸零的動作。 
(4) 將反應管卸下，在空石英吊杯上放入已再生的吸附劑，再將反應管旋上密閉。等重量穩定之
後，於所需溫度下，開始進行恆溫抽真空。當重量再次達到穩定的時候，停止抽氣並紀錄此
時的重量。 
(5) 緩慢通入氣體破真空，並將壓力調整至所需壓力，關閉通入氣體的閥，在恆溫恆壓的環境之
下進行氣體吸附。觀察微量天平的重量變化直至重量曲線穩定而不再改變，紀錄下此時的平
衡重量。 
(6) 重複以上步驟，改變不同的溫度、壓力以及不同的吸附劑，得到不同操作條件下的平衡重量。 
實驗完成之後，將取得的數據進行作圖，可得到不同吸附劑在恆溫下，壓力對平衡吸附量的
曲線圖。

結果與討論 
（一）吸附劑比較 
本研究利用不同的吸附劑對純酒精和純水進行平衡吸附曲線的量測，在不同溫度、不同壓力
的環境條件之下，得到平衡吸附的數據以及平衡曲線圖，如 Figure 1-4 所示。對於吸附劑的選擇
性，可由吸附劑的吸附量比值大小比較。在 150℃，0.548863atm 的條件之下，3A 吸附劑的對水
的平衡吸附量為 2.09536 mole/kg adsorbent，對酒精的平衡吸附量為 0.7994 mole/kg adsorbent，故
相對比值為 2.62116。而在相同的操作的條件之下，4A 吸附劑的對水的平衡吸附量為 1.74675 
mole/kg adsorbent，對酒精的平衡吸附量為 1.0976 mole/kg adsorbent，故相對比值為 1.59142。因
此，3A 相較於 4A 吸附劑在酒精和水的分離上分離效果較佳。以同樣方式比較其他壓力與溫度之
下，亦可得到相同的結果。 
（二）貫流行為的分析 
  經由實驗所得到的吸附數據，利用數學計算的方式，得到純物質在不同吸附劑中的最佳平衡
吸附參數，如 Table 1 所示。再利用這些平衡吸附參數，在單一吸附塔中進行吸附貫流行為的模
擬，其吸附塔及吸附劑的參數如 Table 2。試著改變各種不同的操作變因如 Table 3，對於模擬結果
進行分析。  
  Figure 5 和 Figure 8 分別為利用 3A 和 4A 分子篩，在固定壓力、塔長下改變進料流速對於貫
流行為的影響。其結果可發現當進料流速增加，則其貫流時間減少。因為體積進料流速增加，單
位時間進入床內的氣體相對增加，在分子篩吸附量固定的情況之下，貫流時間也會減少。當吸附
床內水蒸氣接近飽合時，由於酒精和水的相互競爭吸附的關係，使得進料流速高的曲線，貫流時
間增加。 
Figure 6 和 Figure 9 為 3A 和 4A 分子篩在固定塔長、進料流速下改變進料壓力對貫流行為的
影響。由 Figure 6 曲線圖中可發現當進料壓力由 1.4atm 上升到 5.4atm，則貫流時間由 125 秒減少
為 35 秒，且貫流曲線變得陡峭。因為進料壓力升高，則同一時間內流進吸附床內的氣體流量增加，
使得吸附劑可在較短的時間內，達到吸附飽和。

2. Modeling and PSA Process Description 
The model used to describe the PSA process is based on single square channel with a flat carbon coating onto the 
ceramic backbone and is derived from the mass balances, including the following assumptions: (1) The gas 
behaves as an ideal gas. (2) Isothermal operation is considered. (3) The axial dispersed plug flow through the 
channel. (4) Effective diffusional transport and adsorption equilibrium are considered in carbon-coated layer. (5) 
Pressure drop and radial diffusion through the channel are neglected. (6) The adsorbed amount of air is neglected.

3. Results and Discussion 
3.1 Influence of Carbon Coated Layer Thickness 
To increase the thickness of carbon coated layer decreased the void fraction of monolithic adsorber. Figure 3 
shows the effect of carbon coated layer thickness on three-step PSA process with constant Cv. The results in Figure 
3 show the purity and recovery increase with the thickness of carbon coated layer increase. The reason is that 
thicker coated carbon layer has more adsorption capacity and smaller void volume of adsorber, that make thicker 
one (εm = 0.37) to have lower adsorber pressure and more butane desorption than thiner one (εm = 0.96) after 
cocurrent depressurization step (step II) with time and valve values fixed. The more desorbed butane enhance the 
purity and recovery at countercurrent  production. The results for the pressure profile of adsorber with time is 
shown in Figure 4.  In our simulation, a line representing adjusting the valve value of εm = 0.96 is shown in Figure 
4. The valve value for εm = 0.96 is adjusted to make the profile of pressure similar to the one for εm = 0.37. The 
simulation results show that the lower pressure after step II can improve the purity from 36.8% (εm =0.96, constant 
Cv) to 46.3% (εm =0.96, variable Cv), but reducing the recovery from 4.3% to 2.5%. The recovery reduction is 
caused by more butane exhaust for decreasing pressure of adsorber at step II.

3.2 Influence of Feed Concentration 
The effect of feed molar fraction of butane on butane purity and recovery are exhibited 
in Figure 5.  As the feed molar fraction of butane increasing, more butane flows into the 
adsorber and more butane is adsorbed in the carbon, which yields higher purity of 
butane for production. However, the recovery decreasing with feed concentration 
increasing, due to more adsorbed butane desorbs and vents at cocurrent depressurization 
for butane enrichment. Figure 6 shows the pressure profile of adsorber on different feed 
molar fraction of butane with time. In Figure 6, the adsorber pressure for the butane 
concentration of feed ,butane/air = 0.5/0.5 and butane/air = 0.25/0.75, are 0.867atm and 
0.612atm at cocurrent depressurization end (t=18sec), respectively.  The reason is that 
high concentration feed make more butane adsorbed on carbon and the adsorbed butane 
can not desorb well to depressurize the pressure of adsorber at the same step time. 
 
3.3 Influence of Feed Pressure 
The effect of the feed pressure on butane purity and recovery is shown in Figure 7. 
While maintaining the same operating cycle time, increasing the feed pressure will lead 
more butane to adsorb on carbon and flow through adsorber. Besides, the high adsorber 
pressure makes an inefficient depressurization step for butane desorption and 
production at same step time, which results in butane purity and recovery decreasing. 
Figure 8 shows the pressure profile of adsorber on different feed pressure with time. 
After adsorption step (9sec), the high feed pressure causes high adsorber pressure, 
which makes it inefficient to depressurize to a lower pressure than low feed pressure.
