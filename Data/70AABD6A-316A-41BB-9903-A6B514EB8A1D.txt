The three-year plans focus on to enhance the efficiency of carbon nanotube
field emission study at first year. Carbon nanotubes (CNTs) have been recently
interested and focused in the area of filed emission applications due to their excellent
electronic and structural properties such as low diversion electric field, high shooting
electric current density and high stability. However, the field emission performance of
CNTs still impacted by some factors such as the ratio of CNTs, conductive paste,
dilute agent, and inorganic binder in the preparation of CNTs slurry. It is shown that
the field emission performance is poor if the surface of CNTs is covered with
conductive paste or inorganic binder. When a magnetic force perpendicular to the
CNTs plate is added on the CNTs arrays in case of 67 wt.% sodium silicate binder,
the CNTs arrays show a highly aligned state. This result in low turn-on electric field
intensity of 1.22 V/μm under a magnetic force of 129 mT for twenty minutes.
The second year in order to study the synthesis of white powder. The mainstream
of current solid-state lighting technology includes the employment of UV LED with
triple-wavelength RGB phosphors or blue LED with a yellow phosphor to produce
white light. In the former case, the white LED emission color is controlled solely by
the use of appropriate phosphors. This offers flexibility in designing white LEDs with
various color temperatures and color rendering index, as well as the potential for
better color uniformity in manufacturing. Recently, attention has been paid to the
development of single-phased (instead of combination of red, green, and blue)
phosphor joined to UV LED (or CNT-FEDs) for white-light generation. Oxide
phosphors have attracted a great deal of interest recently for their good physical and
chemical stability. Among oxide phosphors, Ba3MgSi2O8: Eu2+, Mn2+, a single-phase
full-color phosphor for UV LED, has been synthesized by solid-state reaction and
combustion process. In this work, Ba3MgSi2O8: Eu2+, Mn2+ nanophosphor was
prepared using citric gel method and the morphology, crystal structure and
photoluminescent properties have been examined as a function of dopant
concentration and preparation conditions. The Ba3MgSi2O8: Eu2+, Mn2+phosphor
which was synthesized by the citric gel method possessed a uniform fine grain size
about 150 nm and a smooth figure. The Ba3MgSi2O8: Eu2+, Mn2+ phosphor showed
three emission colors: blue band of 440 nm, green band of 510 nm and red band of
620 nm under near UV irradiation. The emission intensity was increased with the
increasing calcination time and annealing temperature increased. In particular, when
annealing temperature was raised to 1500 , three co℃ lor emissions changed into
green emission and luminescence intensity increased significantly. The emission
colors were tuned through dopant concentration variation of luminescent centers in
Ba3MgSi2O8: Eu2+, Mn2+ phosphor.

結論與建議
當受一垂直於添加有 67 ﹪的矽酸鈉無機黏結劑之奈米碳管陰極板向上的
磁力，在磁場強度 129 mT，並作用時間 20 分鐘之下，能獲得有效地控制高密
度垂直排列之奈米碳管，並具有相當低的奈米碳管場發射起始電場 1.22 V/μm。
利用矽酸鈉無機黏結劑黏結奈米碳管與導電膠，並且利用磁場吸引及排列含磁
性粒子之奈米碳管，進而提升奈米碳管的場發射性質，為一可行的奈米碳管場
發射製程。
有機導電膠為有機高分子及導電粒子所組成，但有機高分子日久會產生氧
化、裂解等問題，因而造成陰極板使用壽命下降，以致影響場發射效能。若將
有機高分子完全以無機黏結劑所取代，將可改善原本有機高分子所產生的問
題，進而促進場發射效能。奈米碳管場發射陣列表面的奈米碳管在受到磁場吸
引之後，部份未被黏結住的奈米碳管會有脫離陰極陣列表面現象。此現象對於
整體場發射環境來說，將造成表面場發射點不均，導致場發射性質極不穩定，
且因場發射極作工分配不均，某些場發射點因頻繁的電子穿隧，進而毀壞。所
以在利用微處理加工對奈米碳管場發射陣列表面做均勻齊面處理，將是重要的
改進目標。
