（一）前言及研究目的 
銲線接合(wire bond)是以一條細微金屬導線來連接 IC 的訊號傳輸、電氣傳遞方式，是最早被使用
的訊號傳送連接技術，評估一家封裝廠的技術指標，通常就看其銲線製程的能力。截至目前為止，就
技術、設備、良率、價格而言，銲線製程仍然是晶片互連技術的主要方式。以往，封裝廠的銲線製程
是以一條 4N(純度 99.99%)超細(直徑 1mil以下)的金線(gold wire)，將晶片上預留之鋁墊(Aluminum Pad)
與導線架/基板上對應之接點連接起來，用來傳輸電力與電子訊號，所以銲線接合處的品質，直接影響
到電子訊號環路是否導通，產品可否正常運作。2010 年底，黃金每盎司預計超過 1400 元美金，就成
本考量，尋找金線的替代材料以降低成本，以銅線製程代替金線製程將是不可避免，在現階段上，封
裝大廠已經進行銅線銲線製程而且學術界也深入銅線銲線的研發[1-9]。 
使用銅線取代金線之前，有幾個問題必須克服否則會降低銅線製程的可靠度，其一是銅線氧化問
題相當的嚴重，除了在空氣中很容易氧化外，在無氮氣保護下經放電結球之後，導致結球 FAB 的真圓
度不佳，且表面嚴重氧化導致表面產生氧化物影響表面粗糙度，進而影響銲線的鍵結強度，容易造成
假銲；其二是銅線經 EFO後，熱影響區(HAZ)之強度不穩定，造成接合過程中斷線的問題；其三是銅
線的材料的高硬度與強度，在接合過程中下壓時鋁墊的推擠與晶片底下的電路受損；其四是在作失效
性分析(failure modes and effects analysis, FMEA)，非常困難進行可靠度測試的開蓋(decap)檢測。最重要
的是Cu FAB與Al bond pad銲接接合面間的薄膜摩擦行為、界金屬化合物(inter metallic compound, IMC)
產生的機制，會直接影響到銲線的鍵結強度(bondability)。 
在先進的晶圓製程上，鋁墊下的空間需被充分的利用，而發展出 CUP(Circuit under Pad)微結構，
及 Cu/ Low K材料的導入，致使在 IC封裝上面臨極大的挑戰。使用銅線替代金線，做為電性傳遞與訊
號傳輸的橋樑，銅線的導電性比金線優良，但銅線的缺點在於其容易氧化，會造成銲線接合強度可靠
度問題；銅線材料硬度、強度均比金線高，在進行銲線接合時，容易造成晶片上的鋁墊推擠變形，會
影響鈍化層(passivation)以下 CUP微結構承受力過大，導致晶片裂紋的發生，進而造成晶片的損壞；另
外，銅線與鋁墊間銲線易脫落的問題。雖然，在之前國、內外已有相關研究，銅線銲線製程的可靠度
問題、缺點也有多所被報導。但隨著機台設備的更新調整、材料的不斷精煉，以銅線來進行銲線製程
有必要再深入研究。 
銅線銲線製程為避免氧化，通入 95%N2及 5%H2混合氣體保護，或是 100%N2氣體(在某些條件下，
純 N2 的氣體較能抗氧化也有較佳的鍵合力)保護，在經過 EFO 燒球後，在燃燒末端因重力及表面張
力而形成球狀之 FAB，在其上方因散熱形成一段再結晶區域，稱之為熱影響區(HAZ)，超過 HAZ 區長
度以外為熱穩定區 TSZ(Thermal Stable Zone)。因此，一條銅線銲線從結晶組織而言，分成三區域不同
機械性質的 FAB、HAZ、TSZ，從先期的實驗結果得知，由於 FAB 再凝固成柱狀晶(columnar)組織造
成鋁墊推擠、HAZ的粗晶粒會造成球頸高度(kink height)過高。其次是在熱超音波接合時，銅線對高頻
的銲針震動會產生較高的應變率，因此在銅線中就會有較高的應力，線就會因受到應變而硬化，較小
的變形，且有較多的能量傳送到銲接表面，較高的頻率會增加金屬交互擴散的比例，並得到較好的銲
接效果。從鋁墊銅線結合的鋁墊表面粗糙度及銅線的 IMC厚度層，雖然 IMC的機械性質比較脆硬，
但 IMC 層太薄對鍵合不利，而粗糙度較大的鋁墊表面(摩擦係數越大)，可增加結合鍵結的可靠度。

（五）結果與討論 
銅導線的取代目的是為了降低成本之考量，由於銅導線的性質直接影響銲接過程，而過程中因銅
導線強度造成銲墊推擠，嚴重至發生鈍化層以下晶片結構造成破裂電性失效。 
實驗所得材料性質代入數值模擬中模擬與分析之，以下幾點結論： 
1.材料經退火溫度後性質有明顯下降趨勢，二次燒球的性質比一次燒球低，而承受應力的能力皆下降
例如：楊氏係數、降伏強度、極限抗拉強度、硬化指數及強度係數，而材料的延展性增加趨勢，對
於材料組織軟化提升。 
2.採用奈米壓痕硬度實驗取得球體(FAB)的楊氏係數。以卸載之後的曲線找出斜率便可量測得剛性
(Stiffness，S=dP/dh。線材表面最先受到高溫產生相變化形成球體，再熱傳至球體中，造成外圍部份
的壓痕卸載(Unload)的斜率低、剛性低。 
3.從銅導線銲線接合數值模擬中，認為是受到衝擊過程中降伏強度與極限拉伸強度之間的影響，它們
的特性反應在強度係數與應變-硬化指數，因此銲墊無法承受應力很快就被推擠開來。因此可看出退
火處理 200℃的性質如：降伏強度、極限拉伸強度、強度係數與應變硬化指等性質下降，有助於降低
銲墊的推擠量。 
4.利用原子力顯微鏡接觸模式量測金線、銅線與鋁墊的摩擦係數，利用探針量測側向力，以及探針位
移距離算出正向力，進而推算出摩擦係數，經由量測發現一次燒球的銅球頭摩擦力與金線、鋁墊比
較起來相對較高，可是經過二次燒球後的銅球頭摩擦係數比一次燒球的摩擦係數低許多，經由材料
組織的軟化使得摩擦係數的下降。 
5.針對鋁墊的表面，也利用原子力顯微鏡輕拍模式量測，掃出表面形貌，求的中心線平均粗度、均方
根粗度值、斜度與旋度值，其中的旋度值指一粗糙單峰分佈曲線在高峰處的平坦或高聳程度，而量
測出其值小於 3，代表其表面粗糙度波形傾向於圓鈍的形式，稱為低濶峰(Platykurtosuc)，所以鋁墊為
一平坦面。 
6.在模擬銲接過程中，為了更加符合真實銲接情況，考慮摩擦係數對其影響，可以明顯看到考慮接合
過程中所產生的摩擦因素，有效的降低了銅線對鋁墊的推擠量，推擠高度從 1µm下降到 0.5µm，而
鋁墊的厚度為 1µm，模擬的結果得知銅線銲接打在鋁墊上，並不會打穿透過鋁墊而造成鋁墊的破損，
而影響到鋁墊下方的晶片，不會造成晶片的損壞。 
7.本研究首次運用分子動力學到電子構裝銅銲線製程中，探討 IMC層的鍵結強度，成功的模擬出原子
級應力應變拉伸圖。

Abstract 
In this paper, micro/nano-mechanical properties of ultra-
thin copper wire (ψ=0.6mil) and nanotribology along the 
interfacial surface between free air ball (FAB) and aluminum 
(Al) bond pad have been carefully investigated. For 
comparison, M-brand Pd-coated 99.99% (4N) Cu wire and no 
coating 4N Cu wire are selected as test materials. Thermal 
effects on bonding process were taken into account for all 
case studies. Tensile micro-mechanical properties were 
conducted through self-designed wire pull test fixture. 
Nanoindentation instrument was applied to obtained thin film 
surface elastic modulus on FAB. Interfacial frictional 
behavior along smashed FAB and bond pad was determined 
by Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM). AFM force-distance 
curve is utilized to measure the nanotribology properties. The 
interfacial coefficient of frictional force can be derived from a 
serial of calculations. A well-defined contact area is measured 
to study the frictional force and friction stress. The roughness 
of contact surface influences the contact between friction and 
surface forces. The study of roughness parameters 
corresponds to evaluate the friction and the interfacial 
strengths. Local variation in nanotribology is also measured. 
A series of comprehensive parametric predictions have been 
performed in this paper. 
Keywords: micro-mechanical, nanotribology, Atomic Force 
Microscopy, Pd-coated Cu wire. 
Introduction 
Copper wire on wirebonding has been recently focused 
and developed in semiconductor packaging industries for cost 
reduction. However, high rigidity and hardness of Cu wire 
would result in easily squeezing in Al bond pad around the 
smashed FAB. In addition, Nitrogen has to be gassed into a 
small chamber to avoid oxidation of EFO Cu wire. 
Manufacturing process becomes much more complexity. 
99.99% (4N) Pd-coated Cu wire and no coating Cu wire are 
commonly used in wirebonding process. Interfacial 
micro/nano tribology between Cu FAB and Aluminum (Al) 
bond pad plays an important role during wirebonding process. 
Bondability on ball bond (first bond) can be improved as the 
surface frictional force was increased. However, higher 
rigidity and strength of Cu wire would result in Al pad 
squeezing beneath the mashed FAB. Few papers published 
the micro interfacial frictional behavior along Cu FAB and Al 
bond pad. Engineers also appear to lack experience in the 
modeling of microscopic friction. These difficulties can be 
overcome and will be fully discussed in this paper. 
Interfacial nanotribology is crucial in describing 
manipulations of molecules behavior. AFM force-distance 
curve was exploited for the study of several nanotribological 
properties of materials and for characterization of the 
microscopic surface interactions. An AFM force-distance 
curve is a graph of tip-sample interaction force versus tip-
sample distance. Van der Waals force dominates the force-
distance curve at the atomic scale. Typical AFM setup 
consists of (1) a micro scale tip attached to cantilever (2) an 
optical detector measures deflection (3) sample is scanned by 
a 3D piezoelectrical scanner (4) constant force measures the 
feedback from the deflection sensor. The tips are commonly 
made of silicon or silicon nitride (Si3N4) due to their good 
mechanical properties and expendable cost. The signal of 
scanning sample under test is detected by a piezoactuator and 
the cantilever deflection is measured by an optical laser beam 
to reproduce the surface topography (2D & 3D images). The 
normal force is measured from the contact image and the 
lateral force can be evaluated through tapping mode. The 
coefficient of frictional force is defined as the ratio of normal 
force and lateral force.

displacement is 12.45 μm and the horizontal displacement is 2
