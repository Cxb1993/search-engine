ABSTRACT
This study was using a dioxin contamination site in southern part of Taiwan for
the bacteria strains isolation. The characteristics of the strains were investigated. The
total DNA of the strains were extracted and amplified by LAMP and PCR for the
comparison of the effectiveness. The differents of the accuracy and precision between
PCR and LAMP were also cpmpared.
The results of this study shown that soil samples collected from the contaminated
site at Tainan An-shan plant include Tainan#1, Tainan#2, and Tainan#3 obtain the
contamination with the concentration of dioxin 2. 3. 7. 8 TCDD. The most toxic
sample obtain the toxic equivalent of 2450 TEQ/kg.
The strains of bacteria isolated from this study and identified shown that the
strains were Achromobacter xylosoxidans, Agromyces sp., Ralstonia mannitolilytica,
Ochrobactrum anthropic. This study was trying to using the molecular bioassay to
evaluate the DNA amplification effectiveness for the improvement of the accuracy of
the community analysis. After the total DNA extracted from the cell of the bacteria,
the results was shown that LAMP achieved higher 1000 time precision than the PCR
for the DNA amplification. The DNA analysis through PCR and LAMP obtain the
molecular weight of 2500 and 3500 KD, respectively. The LAMP can amplified
various nucleic acid and the PCR can only amplified for 2 band of the nucleic acid
indication the LAMP real exist the potential for more precise for the cell nucleic acid
analysis.
Key words: LAMP, PCR, dioxin, biotransformation bacteria.
三、研究方法
1、土壤來源
本研究所使用之土壤來自於中石化工業開發股份有限公司之污染場址，採樣
地點於臺南安順廠下游鹿耳門溪支流竹筏港溪之底泥，採集點共有三處，分別為
臺南1、臺南2、臺南3 三個樣品，每個採集點各取兩次樣品，總共採六個樣品。
此外，本研究在部份的菌源中，額外添加50 mg/L的琥珀酸或其他營養源如
NB(Nutrient Broth)作為輔助碳源，藉此觀察所篩選出來之分解菌，其微生物生長
與特性是否有所差異。
2、戴奧辛轉化菌之馴養與分離
取2 g之土壤樣品、20 ml滅菌之無機營養鹽(MSB)與10μg/L的2,3,7,8-TCDD，
加入至經滅菌處理之125ml三角錐瓶中，馴化混合菌株中能以戴奧辛為碳源之微
生物，製備完將三角錐瓶瓶口塞上棉花，並於恆溫培養箱中，以30℃，125 rpm
馴化培養30天。馴養30天後，取出上澄液於含1.5 % Agar與10μg/L 2,3,7,8-TCDD
之MSB培養基，進行繼代培養與分離，經過大約10個繼代(sub-culture)培養與分
離後，進行菌種鑑定。
3、戴奧辛轉化菌之 DNA萃取與定量
將四株轉化菌株，以含 1000 mg/L葡萄糖與 10 μg/L2,3,7,8-TCDD之MSB
培養基培養 48小時。培養 48小時後，使用Minprep of bacterial genomic DNA方
法萃取，萃取完之 DNA需經過定量分析，取 10μl之 DNA溶液加入 TE buffer (或
純水)稀釋到 1000 µl，利用紫外光分光光度計，測其 A260與 A280，並比較在 260 nm
及 280 nm之讀值，濃度必須達到一定的範圍才可進行後續實驗，通常其比值約
在 1.7~1.8。

五、結論
1、 本研究篩出的四株戴奧辛分解菌株，其核酸序列以 NCBI及 Applied
Biosystem資料庫比對後，最後鑑定 Achromobacter xylosoxidans、
Ochrobactrum anthropi、Ralstonia mannitolilytica 和 Agromyces sp.為最接
近之菌種名稱。
2、 本研究經聚合酶鏈鎖反應與等溫迴圈核酸擴增法，擴增戴奧辛轉化菌，所
得結果得知，PCR能擴增特定分子量之核酸較少，且所得之強度也不高，
相對於 LAMP反應，能夠擴增較多特定分子量之核酸，且所得之強度也
很高，但 LAMP反應較為敏感，其缺點是較容易在反應過程中受到污染，
必須要先克服。

72. Materials and Methods
2.1 Source of the soils
The source of the strains were the soil from: (1) Yangmingshan National Park, the volcanic soil material.
The soil is soft and light with organic matter and mostly as small granular; (2) Hot spring soil taken from the
Beitou Hot Spring Valley, The hot spring temperature is about 90 ℃ ~ 100 ℃, that is a hydrochloric acid
spring water with pH of 1.6; (3) The soil beside the gas station, content much organic material with black
outlook.
2.2 Carboxymethyl cellulose (CMC)
CMC is a cellulose derivative, the polymer retains the structure of cellulose material which purchase
from the Merck company (Sigma-Aldrich, USA, 2010)
2.3 Isolation process for the bacteria
The screening method is using bagasse as the carbon source for microbial growth. The initial step was
taken 3g of bacteria sources soil and 5g of bagasse by adding 150ml of minimal salt medium (MSB) for
shaking incubation. The shaking speed is 150rpm, the temperature is 30℃ for 60 days. After 60 days
acclimation, the strain can using bagasse as the sole carbon source. The bacteria were empolyed to CMC
liquid culture to confirm that the bacteria can survive in liquid CMC. The survival bacteria then cultured to
solid-state CMC. After screening the subculture of bacteria, The growth of the bacteria was testing and strain
identification was followed.
2.4 The identification of the strains
The method is based on the isolation of 16S rDNA nucleotide sequences and compared to the sequences
in NCBI database. First of it was extracted the chromosome DNA form the cell and purified [6]. The primers
used in this study were F8 and R1510. The F8 is 5'-AGAGTTTGATCCTG-GCTCAG-3', and R1510 is
5'-GGTTACCTTGTTACGACTT-3'. Finally, it confirmed by agarose gel electrophoresis and then sent the
DNA fragments to MingShin company for the identification of the sequence. The NCBI database matching
is available on the website using NCBI BLAST program.
2.5 The growth kinetic analysis of the strains
Microbial growth kinetics is an important indicator of microbial activity. To further understand the
decomposition of substrate, the substrate concentration and time period corresponding to bacteria growth
was need to conduct. This study was use the isolated strain of Sphingomonas sp., Pseudomonas sp.M1,
Achromobacter sp., Pseudomonas sp.M2, and Stenotrophomonas sp. for bacteria growth kinetics analysis.
The analysis was using the model of a single carbon substrate (by Monod eqation) and high concentrations
inhibited ( by Haldane-Andrews model). The experiment is based on CMC and the MSB liquid medium for
bacteria culturing. The strains were incubated in 150 rpm, 30℃ temperature with shaking, and every four
hours to measure the culture turbidity using spectrophotometer with OD600 absorbance.
2.6 The measurement of the reduction sugar concentration
The determination of reduction sugar concentration is refered to DNS method. DNS reagent
(Dinitrosalicyclic acid reagent) in the culture medium was restored and then quantitative evaluate by
spectrophotometer[7]. The DNS reagent preparation is as follows (100 ml): first, a small amount of
deionized water, 1.6g NaOH solution adjusted to alkaline, add 30g potassium sodium titrate, then add 1g
3,5-dinitro-water Yang acid and quantitatively to the 100ml, stored in non-illumination at 4℃. To consider
the hydrolysis, the cellulose as substrate intermediate is mainly with glucose. The samples were collected
with approximately 2ml culture medium and centrifuged (6,000 rpm×5min). 1ml supernatant obtained by
adding an equal volume of DNS reagent mix to screw cap in the glass tube, placed in boiling water bath for
heating 10 min, rapid cooling to room temperature, the water was then empolyed to the visible light
spectrophotometer at 540 nm and the absorbance was measured.
