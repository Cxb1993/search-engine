Abstract 
   The project intends to study the Ni pillar 
contact growth rate / deposition condition 
relationship and the resulted microstructure in N- 
based semiconductor compounds device. Besides 
the concentrations of Ni ions, the high aspect ratio 
feature size and feature shape, e.g. rectangle, circle, 
corners angle, could result in local current density 
and, hence, microstructure variation in the deposit. 
This study will focus on using pulse electroplate to 
fabrication a Ni pillar contact on N- based 
semiconductor compounds device and analysis 
crystal orientation of Ni  deposition.  A It is 
expected that result of Ni microcontact deposition 
rate and properties can be co-related with the 
different feature aspect ratio / deposition condition 
relationship established.  
Keywords: N- based semiconductor 
compounds,pulse electroplate, electroless Ni, 
Ni/Au pillar contact
