II 
Abstract 
There are many kinds of processes to prepare CZTS, such as spray pyrolysis, sol-gel, 
sputtering, electron-beam evaporation, co-evaporation, photo-chemical deposition, and pulsed 
laser deposition etc. Among these methods, sol-gel is the easiest process with cost down, and 
large area etc. properties, and without needing any vacuum facility. However there exists the 
problems of pool sulfur in the CZTS resulting from all of the deposited methods. Sulfide 
annealing was adopted to solve the problem, and in most of the reports H2S was selected to be the 
compensated source for poor sulfur in the CZTS. During the use of H2S toxin gas in CZTS 
annealing process, more expensive vacuum instruments are not inevitable to be used for the 
safety consideration, and this make the process more complex.    
 
CZTS is a kind of p-type direct band gap semiconductor material. It has low cost, nontoxic 
and abundant properties with a band gap value suitable to be absorption layers for applications in 
thin film solar cells. In this work sol-gel deposition is used to fabricate solar-cell CZTS thin film. 
The effects of different sulfurization conditions on crystallization and photo-electricity of CZTS 
are characterized. Also, the characteristics of the Cu2ZnSnS4 solar cell’s at different sulfurization 
situations are examined. The solar conversion efficiency of 0.09% of the CZTS results from the 
processes of re-crystallization and sulfurization at 400℃. The open circuit voltage(Voc), short 
circuit photo-current density(Jsc) and curve fill factor(FF) are 0.18V, 1.48mA/cm2 and 34.19% 
respectively. 
Keywords: CZTS, solar-cell, absorption layer, sulfurization

計畫與自評 
我們藉由實驗結果得知：越高的硫化處理溫度可增加CZTS薄膜的結晶性，而CZTS薄
膜的結晶性又影響著CZTS薄膜太陽能電池元件的轉換效率，因此大致列出以下可改善
CZTS薄膜太陽能電池元件轉換效率的方向： 
1. 由於高溫硫化處理過程中，硫元素遇氧後易燃燒，建議建立更安全與完善的硫化退火設
備，進而研究更高溫的硫化處理製程對於CZTS薄膜結晶性之影響。 
2. 由實驗結果我們得知Voc值偏小，可能藉由調整材料比例，進而調整CZTS薄膜的載子濃
度，探討載子濃度與空乏區大小及轉換效率之影響。 
3. 研究各層之間的歐姆接觸效應，並試圖降低太陽能電池元件中的串聯電阻、提升太陽能
電池元件之並聯電阻，藉以提升FF填充因子。 
4.本研究所製備的太陽能電池具有材料低廉與製程成本低廉之兩大優勢，期許未來能夠加
以改善，並做出具有高水準的薄膜太陽能電池，為台灣的太陽能工業盡一份棉薄之力。 
5.經由本研究訓練研究所碩士班學生及大學部學生專題製作，對於太陽能電池製造技術人
才之培育，相信有一定的成果。 
6.經由本研究產出九篇學術論文及三項專利陸續申請中，相信對於技術轉移給國內太陽能
光電產業必能有所貢獻。
