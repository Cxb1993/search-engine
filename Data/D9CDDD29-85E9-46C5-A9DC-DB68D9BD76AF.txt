一、中文摘要 
本研究使用具有雙模態之氧化鋅(ZnO)
壓電薄膜以研製體聲波質量感測器，其中
ZnO壓電薄膜係採用反應性射頻磁控濺鍍
法，利用偏軸成長方式研製c-軸傾斜的ZnO
壓電薄膜，以成長具有縱波及剪波雙模態之
ZnO壓電薄膜，作為體聲波共振器壓電層之
用；電極材料的選擇則使用具有耐腐蝕的白
金 (Pt)薄膜；再利用反應性離子蝕刻法
(RIE)，完成背蝕型薄膜體聲波共振器
(FBAR)之研製；之後再進行縱波空氣質量
感測及剪波液態質量感測，以獲得高品質之
體聲波質量感測器。 
ZnO壓電薄膜之結晶性與微結構係採
用X光繞射儀(XRD)與場發射式掃描電子顯
微鏡(FE-SEM)來分析；體聲波質量感測器
之頻率響應則採用HP8720網路分析儀與
CASCADE高頻探針座量測之；實驗的結果
顯示元件在空氣中剪波的共振頻率為793 
MHz，品質因子為4470，而在液態中相同共
振頻率下，測得之品質因子仍然維持在
4062；另外縱波在空氣中的共振頻率及品質
因子分別為1769 MHz及955，而在液態中其
品質因子則下降到502，這顯示剪波模態於
液態中的品質因子衰減率較小，適合應用於
液態感測。

Abstract 
In this project, we focused on the 
fabrication of mass sensors based on dual 
mode film bulk acoustic resonators (FBAR). 
The piezoelectric layer, zinc oxide (ZnO) thin 
films, was deposited by reactive rf magnetron 
sputtering. We altered the distance between 
substrate and target to deposit c-axis tilted 
ZnO which resulted in longitudinal and shear 
waves at the same time. The platinum (Pt) thin 
flim was adopted as the electrodes. The 
resonant cavity of back-etching type FBAR 
was manufactured using the technique of RIE. 
The mass loading effect of mass sensor was 
investigated by longitudinal and shear waves 
in air and liquid, respectively. 
The crystallization and microstructure of 
ZnO thin films were investigated using the 
X-ray diffraction (XRD) and Field-emission 
scanning electron microscopy (FE-SEM), 
respectively. The frequency responses of 
FBAR devices were measured by the HP8720 
network analyzer and CASCADE probe 
station. The quality factor of FBAR shear 
wave in air and liquid were measured of 4470 
and 4062 at 793 MHz, respectively. The 
quality factor of FBAR longitudinal wave in 
air and liquid were measured of 955 and 502 
at 1769 MHz, respectively. The experimental 
results showed that the quality factor of 
longitudinal mode was decayed apparent and 
shear mode was kept at high value. 
 
Keywords: ZnO, film bulk acoustics 
wave, mass sensor, shear wave, quality factor

五、計畫成果自評 
本研究採用射頻磁控濺鍍法，成功的沉
積具有雙模態的 ZnO 壓電薄膜，並將其製
作成具有雙頻響應的體聲波質量感測器，由
頻率響應量測結果得知，剪波模態於液態中
的品質因子衰減率較小，適合應用於液態感
測，且經由可靠度測試得知，元件在水中具
有不錯的可靠度，未來使用於生醫感測可提
供一個可靠度的量測參考；本研究計畫之
FBAR 元件研製過程詳盡，係由各層材料的
物理特性進行分析，並針對元件所需之材料
特性進行材料製備，接著開發 FBAR 元件的
各項製程參數，並提出各項製程需注意的關
鍵，以建立完整的元件製程技術。整體而
言，本研究已達成預期的目標，與原計畫預
期完成的工作項目相符合，成功的使用雙模
態體聲波共振器研製質量感測器特性，未來
更積極的來改善感測器的特性，並朝向研製
一個質量感測系統而努力；相信本計畫的執
行除了成果可提供國內業界參考外，參與本
計畫的研究生亦可熟悉微機電及半導體的
製程技術，並對生醫感測有基本的認識，為
國家培育優秀的人才。

Highly Sensitive Ultraviolet Detector using a ZnO/Si Layered SAW Oscillator 
 
Ching-Liang Wei1, Ying-Chung Chen1,*, Chien-Chuan Cheng2, and Po-Shu Cheng1 
 
1Department of Electrical Engineering, National Sun Yat-Sen University, Kaohsiung, Taiwan, R.O.C.

Abstract 
This study elucidates the combination of an oscillator circuit with a high-frequency amplifier, a 
matching network and a layered surface acoustic wave (SAW) device, for detecting ultraviolet (UV) 
light. In this structure, a ZnO thin film is simultaneously used as an active layer for UV detection 
and a piezoelectric layer for exciting a high-order surface acoustic wave. The microstructure and 
crystallization of ZnO films were investigated using the scanning electron microscopy (SEM) and 
X-ray diffraction (XRD), respectively. The SAW oscillator shows a good performance with the 
resonance frequency of 751 MHz and phase noise of －94.7 dBc@100 kHz. The frequency 
variation of the oscillator steadily increases with the intensity of the UV light. An extreme 
frequency shift of 1017 kHz was obtained as the UV intensity reached 551 μW/cm2. The maximum 
sensitivity of 8.12 ppm/(μW/cm2) can be obtained in this detector. 
KEYWORDS: SAW oscillator, UV light detection, ZnO, sensor

3. RESULTS AND DISCUSSION 
Figure 3(a) is the X-ray diffraction (XRD) pattern of the ZnO thin film, which exhibits the 
diffraction intensity peak of (002) plane at 2θ=34.3o, showing a highly preferred orientation. The 
cross-sectional and top-view SEM images also show a highly c-axis oriented columnar structure 
and a fine pebble structure. The thickness of ZnO thin film is 3.23 μm, as shown in Fig. 3(b). The 
higher diffraction intensity and better crystalline structure of piezoelectric films are associated with 
the higher electromechanical coupling coefficient and better device performances [17-18]. 
Because surface acoustic waves are propagated in the vicinity of thin film surface, the surface 
roughness of piezoelectric films is vital for SAW device applications. The worse surface roughness 
of piezoelectric films corresponds to the poor frequency response of SAW devices. The root mean 
square (rms) value of 10.9 nm is fairly small compared with other studies [19-20], calculated from 
the surface roughness of the ZnO film, as shown in Fig. 4. Therefore, ZnO thin films in this study 
exhibit good properties and are suitable for fabricating SAW devices.
