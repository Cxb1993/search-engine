一. 中文摘要 
許多的不同種類的螺旋銑刀，是NC機器加工
各種各樣的模具和複雜的曲面必須的工具，螺旋
刀具外緣品質可改善機器加工效率與精度。但我
國目前研磨螺旋狀一體成型銑刀的製造產商，其
製造軟硬體皆為國外進口十分昂貴，致使螺旋狀
一體成型的銑刀單價十分昂貴，且對較特殊形狀
的螺旋銑刀，若國外軟體無此功能，則此類型的
銑刀常無法製造，影響製造業加工研磨與發展，
致使國內刀具製造的進步停滯不前。 
擺線複雜螺旋銑刀為一種多功能的新穎螺旋
刀具，其刀身外形多變化，由於此類刀具以側銑
加工最後精修為主，可將多個加工程式以一次加
工完成，可減少多個加工道次中換刀及不同基準
點的累積誤差，而且具有將多個加工道次成為同
軸心的功能，不但可增加加工效率並可提高加工
精度，因此對此特殊擺線螺旋銑刀的外徑變化要
求嚴格。由於研磨複雜擺線螺旋銑刀十分困難，
故研磨此類複雜擺線螺旋銑刀的外形外徑十分重
要，有必要對此複雜外形的擺線螺旋銑刀進行研
究探討，以提高加工工件的品質。 
 
關鍵詞：擺線螺旋銑刀；螺旋角；溝槽；刃口曲
線。 
 
Abstract 
Since many different kinds of milling cutters 
are generally required for NC machining of various 
mould and complex surface. Evaluating the contour 
quality of this kind of revolving toroid-shaped 
cutters is very important to its usage and improving 
the machining efficiency. Manufacture factories of 
spiral and one material shaping milling cutter have 
hardware and software from abroad, these are very 
expensive. The milling cutter with spiral and one 
material shaping is also more expensive. Special 
shapes of helical will not be manufactured if 
software doesn’t have this function; these are 
influential of developing on manufacturing. Thus, 
the skill of manufacturing cutters in Taiwan has 
been stopped from improving. 
Complex cycloid spiral cutter is a new kind of 
multi-funcation helical milling cutter. The body 
shape of the cutter is changeable. Since this kind of 
cutter is mainly used for side milling and finishing, 
it is capable of finishing many manufacturing 
processes by manufacturing once. By using this 
kind of cutter, the accumulated errors that are 
produced by changing cutters and slight shift of 
bench-mark during many manufacturing processes 
will decrease, and the center axis of many 
manufacturing processes has become the same axis. 
Thus, the cutter can increase not only the efficency 
but also the precision in manufacturing. As a result, 
the variation of outer diameter for this special 
cycloid spiral cutter is highly restricted. Because it 
is difficult to grind the complex cycloid spiral 
milling cutters, grinding the outer diameter of the 
cutter’s shape have become very important. As a 
regard, it is essential to do some researches and 
discussions on the complex shape of complex 
cycloid spiral milling cutter in order to increase the 
precision of the manufactured workpiece. 
Keywords： cycloid spiral cutter, helical angle; 
groove; cutting edge curve
