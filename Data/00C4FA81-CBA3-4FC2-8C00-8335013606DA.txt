1摘要
計畫名稱：『以離子交換樹脂承載奈米零價金屬薄膜還原降解水中污染物之程序開發』
關鍵詞：樹脂、奈米零價金屬覆膜、染料、2-氯酚、管柱試驗
奈米級零價鐵由於高比表面積與良好之還原能力，對於偶氮染料脫色及含氯有機物脫氯有
相當明顯的效果，然而在製備、收集、貯存與反應過程中極易與空氣或水中溶氧反應產生
氧化鐵覆蓋於奈米級零價鐵表面使之喪失反應力，此外由於顆粒細微除了以批次方式一次
性添加外，在填充塔或流體化床反應器型式上很難應用，也因此提高了實廠應用與尺寸放
大的困難度。本研究提出新構想並以實驗印證以離子交換樹脂承載奈米零價金屬薄膜
（Ni/Fe, Zn/Fe 與 Cu/Fe）複合材料進行偶氮染料的脫色、TOC 移除以及 2-氯酚污染物脫氯
之探討。在製備方面本研究室可以製成樹脂承載之鐵、鎳、銅單金屬奈米覆膜與鐵/鎳、鐵
/鋅、鐵/銅之雙金屬覆膜。除奈米銅覆膜外，奈米鐵與鎳均能在短時間內將目標污染物之偶
氮染料 AB113 脫色並有部份 TOC 的吸附移除，且鐵/鎳雙金屬對於 2-氯酚有相當高之還原
脫氯效果，由於樹脂的離子交換能力鐵、鎳等金屬於反應中的溶出並不明顯，不致產生二
次污染。奈米鐵鎳雙金屬覆膜可以減緩鐵表面的氧化與失去還原力。管柱試驗中奈米鎳覆
膜有較奈米鐵覆膜高的處理容量，以鹽酸再生較硼氫化鈉較具成效。
Title: Development of noval process involving ion exchange resin as nano zero-valent metal
film carrier to reduction degrade water pollutants
Keywords: resin, nano-bimetallic film, dye, 2-chlorophenol, column test
With high surface area and reactivity, nano-scale iron can effectively reductive degrade organic
pollutants. However, the drawback of nano-scale iron is the formation of iron oxide thin film and
losing its reactivity. Furthermore, particle size of the nano-scale iron is too small to effective
operated in large scale treatment process. Therefore, the preparation of resin bonded
nano-bimetallic (Ni/Fe, Zn/Fe and Cu/Fe) film complex materials is the key improvement to
decolorize azo dye, remove TOC and dechlorinate chloro-organic compounds. In this work, nano
scale single metal and bimetallic coverage on the supporting resin were prepared and tested.
Although, film complex materials are efficient in decolorizing azo dyes and dechlorinating
chlorophenol, they can only degrade total organic carbon really. Except the nano copper coverage
of resin, both iron and nickel nano coverage materials can effectively decolorize AB113 azo dye.
TOC was adsorptive remove at same time. Bimetallic Ni/Fe complex material can effectively
dechlorinate 2-chlorophenol. The dissolution of iron, nickel into the solution was adsorbed by ion
exchange resin and observed no significant discharge. The iron/nicke bimetallic coverage on resin
can resist to the oxidation of metal and can last longer time period. In column test, the
nano-nickel coverage performs better than iron coverage in treatment capacity. And the
regeneration of nano-nickle coverage was sufficient by hydrogen chloride than by NaBH4.
3程應用之可行性，
本研究室過去三年進行奈米零價鐵脫色染料廢水之研究已具備奈米金屬製備能力，並
已證實奈米零價鐵可有效對偶氮染料進行脫色。惟奈米零價鐵雖然反應性強卻容易在保存
環境與反應器中受到空氣或水中溶氧所形成氧化鐵膜覆蓋，進而失去其還原力。此外，奈
米零價金屬由於顆粒極為細小，除了以批次方式一次性添加外，在填充塔或流體化床反應
器型式上很難應用，也因此提高了實廠應用與尺寸放大的困難度。因此，本兩年期計畫以
強酸性離子交換樹脂為承載體在其表面製備奈米金屬薄膜或奈米雙金屬（Ni/Fe, Zn/Fe,
Cu/Fe）薄膜複合材料，作為新潁技術開發之基礎。
本研究從合成奈米雙金屬的經驗導入強酸型離子交換樹脂為承載體，並以奈米零價金
屬合成技術來形成樹脂表面的奈米薄膜。如此，不但可以提升奈米金屬的操作性也可以減
少反應後的金屬溶出。若在樹脂表面同時形成雙金屬薄膜則可以克服奈米零價鐵易於被氧
化的問題。雙金屬材料用於觸媒反應非常普遍，但是多數研究採用 Pd, Pt, Ag, Au, Rh 等貴
重金屬，成本非常高。本計畫採用平價、易回收、反應性強以及性質穩定之奈米雙金屬（Ni/Fe,
Zn/Fe , Cu/Fe）薄膜，研究其表面性質、金屬成份配比與水中 ORP、反應性之關係，並利
用實驗設計技術獲得最適操作條件，以進行偶氮染料分子在水相之還原性分解脫色及含氯
有機物 2-氯酚降解脫率。使用後之雙成份離子交換樹脂承載零價金屬薄膜複合材料由於操
作性良好且易於收集可回收與再生使用。由於離子交換樹脂承載零價金屬薄膜複合材料雖
然具有強還原力但應用於染料廢水處理時，僅有脫色之效果，對於染料廢水中有機污染物
（以總有機碳代表之）較無法以還原反應達到有效降解的目標。因此需配合其他高破壞力
之後處理程序。
二、研究方法
本計畫的主要目標為以強酸性離子交換樹脂為承載體在其表面製備平價、易回收、反
應性強以及性質穩定之奈米金屬薄膜（Ni, Fe, Zn, Cu）複合材料，研究其表面性質、金屬
成份配比與水中 ORP、反應性之關係，並利用實驗設計技術獲得最適操作條件，以進行本
實驗兩種目標污染物處理探討，分別為偶氮染料分子在水相之還原性分解脫色以及含氯有
機物 2-氯酚分解脫氯效率。在完成單一成份金屬薄膜測試後並製備雙成份金屬薄膜（Ni/Fe,
Zn/Fe, Cu/Fe）以測試其反應性與持續性是否因雙金屬成份而有提升。使用後之離子交換樹
脂承載奈米金屬與雙金屬由於樹脂為成熟之工業原料其填充與再生、回收均較奈米金屬顆
粒簡單容易。
本研究可區分成兩部份，第一部份為合成奈米單一金屬及雙金屬薄膜樹脂之特性分
析，離子交換樹脂承載零價金屬薄膜複合材料製備完成後，以場發射掃描式電子顯微鏡
(Field Emission Scanning Electron Microscope-EDS)鑑定表面性質與表面元素組成；使用比表
面積分析儀以 B.E.T 法量測自行製備之奈米級零價金屬薄膜樹脂之比表面積。

2-3-4 高效率液相層析儀(HPLC High Performance Liquid Chromatography)
層析的定義為混合化學的分析物進入固定相後在移動相的帶動過程中（亦稱為沖提過
程，elution）會依據分析物的化學結構特性和固定相所含的化學官能基之間作用力大小的
不同，造成不同分析物種因在系統中移動時間的不同，而依次被分離開來。由於液相層析
法應用範圍相當的廣泛，結合質譜儀的高靈敏度、高選擇性以及分析快速的特點，且可同
時進行定性與定量的分析。以高效率液相層析儀 HPLC (Agilent 1200 Series)分析目標污染
物氯酚及其中間產物之濃度，分析管柱為C18 (5μm,4.6×250mm)，移動相條件設定甲醇為
60%、去離子水為40%，流速為1 mL/min，波長設定為280nm。
2-3-5 離子層析儀 (IC Alert Ion Analyzer)
氯酚溶液反應脫氯後溶液中氯離子濃度，本研究利用離子層析儀 (Dionex ICS-1000)
檢測，利用其離子交換層析法(Ion exchange chromatography)分析氯酚溶液中氯離子之含
量，分析原理利用離子交換樹脂作為固定相，適當之流洗液作為移動相，利用各種陰陽離
子在固定相及移動相之間的平衡常數不同，且其對於移動相之親和力亦各不相同，使得各
種陰陽離子在管柱中各具有不同的滯留時間，達到分離的目的。
2-3-6 原子吸收光譜儀 (AA Atomic Absorption Flame Emission Spectrophotometer)
本研究利用原子吸收光譜儀(SHIMADZU AA-6200)分析染料溶液中總鐵含量，依環檢
所公告(NIEA W305.53A, 2008)水中溶解性鐵、錳檢測方法－火焰式原子吸收光譜法，將反
應後欲分析之污染物溶液以 0.45 μm 之濾膜過濾並加酸使水樣 pH < 2，經消化分解有機物
質後，直接以火焰式原子吸收光譜儀，選擇適當波長測定金屬濃度。
其分析原理是利用高溫燃燒爐將分析樣品內所含金屬元素還原成原子態，同時利用與
分析金屬具相似吸收寬頻輻射的放射輻射源，藉由放射輻射被原子化樣品吸收造成衰減的
強度，對照濃度與吸收度相關式並配合檢量線計算獲得正確分析金屬濃度。

3-2 樹脂承載奈米零價金屬薄膜還原反應
在樹脂承載奈米零價金屬薄膜與本研究設定之兩類目標污染物，分別為偶氮染料 Acid
Blue 113 溶液以及含氯有機化合物 2-氯酚進行還原反應，對於反應後各項參數如零價金屬
種類、反應時間、零價金屬覆膜劑量、目標污染物初濃度及初始 pH 值之影響分別進行探
討。
3-2-1 零價金屬薄膜種類影響 (偶氮染料 Acid Blue 113)
配製 Acid Blue 113 染料初濃度 100 mg/L 並利用化學還原法製備各種奈米零價金屬薄
膜樹脂(Fe、Ni、Cu、Fe/Ni)，覆膜劑量控制 50 mg/L，將 20 g 樹脂與 1 L 染料溶液均勻攪
拌混合反應，轉速控制 30 rpm，依固定時間取樣並以分光光度計進行全波長掃描，計算染
料濃度，染料濃度去除效率如圖 3-4 所示，由圖中可看出奈米鐵及奈米鐵/鎳雙金屬薄膜樹
脂對染料濃度皆能達到 100%之去除效率，單一奈米鎳金屬薄膜樹脂於 60 分鐘反應時間也
可達約 90%去除率，且反應相當快速約於 10 分鐘即趨於平衡，但零價銅覆膜樹脂對染料明
顯不具脫色效果。此外，除了觀察染料濃度變化，也同時監測反應過程中染料溶液之總有
機碳、酸鹼值、氧化還原電位(ORP)及金屬離子溶出濃度的變化情形，結果如圖 3-5~3-8。
圖 3-5 為總有機碳變化情形，除了奈米零價銅覆膜樹脂反應無法有效降解溶液中有機碳，
另外三種金屬薄膜樹脂在反應過程中，染料溶液中總有機碳濃度皆有下降的趨勢。圖 3-6
為染料溶液於反應中之酸鹼值變化情形，染料原始 pH 值約為 6，經反應後約可提高至
7~8.5。圖 3-7 表示反應過程中氧化還原電位先降後升，奈米鐵覆膜樹脂反應時變化最大，
約自 300 mv 下降至-600 mv，60 分鐘後，再升至-300 mv。圖 3-8 為反應過程中染料溶液金
屬溶出濃度的比較，得知雖然反應中有金屬離子溶出於染料溶液中，但隨著反應時間增加，
溶液中金屬濃度也隨之降低，由此可知，樹脂利用離子交換可減低反應後的金屬溶出。
3-2-2 零價金屬覆膜劑量影響 (偶氮染料 Acid Blue 113)

碳去除率如圖 3-10 可發現覆膜劑量與溶液中總有機碳之去除無顯著關係，圖 3-11 表示各種
金屬劑量與染料溶液反應過程中具有金屬溶出，金屬溶出量會隨著覆膜劑量提升而增加，
但隨著反應時間增長，溶液中金屬離子濃度成下降的趨勢，表示強酸型陽離子樹脂將於反
應過程中將所溶出之金屬離子吸附於表面。

3-2-5 初始 pH 值對 2-氯酚脫氯之影響
配製 50mg/L 之 2 -氯酚溶液(C6H4OHCl)於 1 L 燒杯中，用鹽酸(HCl)和氫氧化鈉(NaOH)
調整初始 pH 值至既定範圍(2、4、6、9、11)，製備 27.6 mg Fe 21.9 mg Ni/ g-resin 之陽離子
交換樹脂 20 g，分別在不同 pH 值之 2-氯酚溶液 1 L 燒杯中加入合成之樹脂 20 g，並以轉速
200 rpm 混合攪拌，定時取樣，每次取樣 10mL，利用高速離心機轉速 4000rpm 進行固液分
離 5 分鐘，離心後樣品進行 HPLC 分析。
針對 2-氯酚初始酸鹼值進行研究結果如圖 3-16 所示。原始 2-氯酚溶液 pH 值約為 6.0，
與奈米鐵鎳雙金屬覆膜樹脂反應 60 分鐘後，其最終 2-氯酚脫氯效率可達約 97.8%，而調整
至強酸或強鹼條件下，並無明顯脫氯效率，當 pH 值為 2 時，反應後脫氯效率約為 43.8%，
主要原因為零價鐵在酸性環境中加速氧化形成二價鐵並釋出電子，此時大部分之零價鐵氧
化成二價鐵且受離子交換作用迅速被置換於樹脂內，雖然污染物迅速接受電子但卻缺少足
夠的反應活性位置，而導致效率不佳；當 pH 值為 11 時，脫氯效率約為 11.2%，由於鹼性
環境產生氫氧根離子易與鐵表面溶出之二價鐵離子形成氫氧化鐵沉澱而佔據表面活性位置
導致阻礙反應發生。在本研究之操作條件下，2-氯酚最佳處理 pH 值範圍為 4~6。

為探討樹脂表面承載零價金屬劑量對色度去除率之影響，於合成中分別配製不同金屬
劑量由 10~50 mg/ resin(g)與 50 mg/L 之 2-氯酚溶液進行反應。以單一金屬以及不同雙金屬
(Fe/Ni)比例進行劑量影響探討，反應過程以轉速 200 rpm 混合攪拌，定時取樣，每次取樣
10mL，利用高速離心機轉速 4000rpm 進行固液分離 5 分鐘，離心後樣品進行 HPLC 分析。
實驗結果如圖 3-17。奈米鐵鎳雙金屬脫氯效率比單一金屬明顯較佳，而在奈米鐵鎳雙
金屬又以鐵佔之比例大時脫氯效率較佳，其反應時間 60 分鐘之 2-氯酚脫氯效率約可達
98%，顯示當覆膜鐵金屬劑量增高可提供較多表面活性位置，增加與污染物接觸之機會，
2-氯酚脫氯效率也隨之增加。反應溶液中金屬溶出濃度也為重要指標之一。圖 3-18 為溶液

放流水標準。

Laboratory prepared NZVI particles were prepared by slowly adding assigned volume of 0.6 M of
iron chloride solution into 250 ml 0.8 M of sodium borohydride solution in a homogenizer mixed
vessel with high speed stirring using a homogenizer. The synthesis method is modified from our

collected and separated from reagents by magnetic force. To characterize the NZVI, the supernatant
was decanted to collect the synthesized NZVI particles. Then, the NZVI particles were remained to be
dried for morphology and particle size distribution detection mainly within 50-80 nm by a JEOL
JSM-6700F Field Emission Scanning Electron Microscope (FESEM). The morphology of NZVI was
shown in Figure 1a. By EDS scanning, the surface of NZVI was determined major of iron metal and
litter oxygen absorbed and iron oxide formed on the surface of NZVI as presented in the Figure 1b.
The BET surface area was detected by a Micrometrics made Gemini V gas sorption system to be 76.7
m2/g, and the pore volume and pore size were measured to be 0.0247 cm3/g and 12.87 Angstrong,
respectively.
For the NZVI reduction stage, azo dyes were prepared into designated concentrations and reacted in
1.0 dm3 batch reactor, the stirrer speed was set at 100 rpm. At beginning of the experiments, various
dosages of NZVI were added into dye solution at different experimental runs. The experimental
variables such as reaction time, NZVI dosage, initial dye concentration, and pH were evaluated for
color removal. For AOP oxidation stage, the UV/H2O2 reactor utilized a thin gap annular UV
photooxidation tubular reactor was conducted in the experiments. A Sterilight low pressure
mercury arc UV lamp (wavelength 253.7 nm, input power of 14 watts) was mounted in the inner
quartz tube of reactor. The details of the reactor were reported in our previous work (Shu et al., 2005).
While irradiating the UV lamp, azo dye solution with certain concentration of H2O2 was pumped to
flow through the thin gap between quartz tube and outer stainless steel shell where produced
photo-oxidation process instantaneously producing very strong oxidizing hydroxyl and peroxyl free
radicals non-selectively attacking hydrocarbons resulting bond cleavage into some smaller molecules
or carbon dioxide. For the integrating process with NZVI reduction and UV/H2O2 oxidation, NZVI
particles were initially added and mixing with the original dye solution during certain time. Then is
the separation of NZVI particles from dye solution to obtain clear solution. Later, the clear NZVI
treated solution was then stored in a sample reservoir to continuously conduct the UV/H2O2 process
described as above for enhancing further oxidation of the residual dye and TOC after NZVI particles
addition. The water samples for various processes were withdrawn by time intervals to be measured
at their characteristic wavelengths by Hitachi U-2000 spectrophotometer. Total Organic Carbon
(TOC) was determined by an IO Analytical 1030W Total Organic Carbon (TOC) analyzer. Moreover,
the color strength of dye solution was measured by standard color detection procedure developed by
the American Dye Manufacturers Institute (ADMI) substituting transmittance data with 30
wavelengths from UV-VIS spectrophotometer (every 10 nm interval during 400-700 nm) followed
2120E of Standard Methods by applying the Adams-Nickerson color difference formula. The pH and
ORP were monitored by a Eutech PH5500 dual channel pH/ion meter with specific probes.
RESULTS AND DISCUSSION
NZVI reduction stage
Reductive degradation of azo dye, Acid Black 24, by ZVI and NZVI in aqueous phase has been
proven to be a fairly fast process from our previous works with the dissolution of ferrous ions and
electrons to react with the dye molecules on the iron surface (Chang et al., 2006b; Shu et al., 2007).
As reported in above mentioned papers, about 90% decolorization of AB24 (100 mg l-1) within the
4again from iron surface as soon as iron gradually dissolving into water by time extension. In the case
of TOC removal, the adsorption removal of AB24 organics was highest among three azo dyes.
UV/H2O2 oxidation stage
From a previous study, azo dyes with di-azo links were fairly difficult for treatment using either
ozonation process or UV-induced ozonation process (Shu and Huang, 1995). The degradation of azo
dyes such as, Acid Black 1, AB113, Direct Blue 199, and Acid orange 10 by UV/H2O2 process was
thoroughly studied by the authors (Shu et al., 2004; Shu et al., 2005; Shu and Chang, 2005a; Shu and
Chang, 2005b). In this stage, AB24 was investigated to use different hydrogen peroxide dosages. The
initial concentration of hydrogen peroxide is an important parameter for the degradation of azo dyes
in the UV/H2O2 process as concluded from above mentioned literatures. Theoretically, the higher
initial hydrogen peroxide concentration, the higher decomposition rate of the AB24 is. However, the
OH free radicals reach equilibrium with the concentration of hydrogen peroxide. Over the
equilibrium concentration, any increase of the hydrogen peroxide concentration cannot enhance the
free radical concentration. Figure 7 showed the AB24 initial concentration of 100 mg l-1, the
degradation of AB24 were implemented significantly, while the hydrogen peroxide dosage increasing
from 5.82 to 69.7 mM. In this operating condition range, reverse effect on degradation of AB24 by
increasing the hydrogen peroxide dosage was not observed. Therefore, up to the hydrogen peroxide
concentration of 69.7 mM, the degradation rate constant increased with increasing hydrogen peroxide
concentration. Besides, the UV/H2O2 process is very effective for mineralization (or TOC removal)
of AB 24 shown in Figure 7. In 120 minutes of UV irradiation with hydrogen peroxide dosage of 5.82
and 69.7 mM, 34.0 and 69.2% of mineralization rate were achieved, respectively. For 300 minutes of
irradiation time, in both cases of hydrogen peroxide concentrations the mineralization rate of 95%
were obtained.
Combination of NZVI and UV/H2O2 process
From the above results, UV/H2O2 process was proven to have abilities to not only degrade azo links
of dye molecule, but also mineralize the TOC effectively. However, TOC mineralization takes longer
time by UV/H2O2 process. On the other hand, NZVI reduction process performed high azo dye
removal efficiency but low TOC removal efficiency. To utilize the advantages of both processes and
avoid disadvantages, connecting NZVI reduction with UV/H2O2 oxidation process was proposed as
an emerging technology for rapid dye molecule degradation and mineralization of the residual TOC
in a whole. The idea was proved to be feasible by using ZVI with UV/H2O2 oxidation in our previous
work (Chang et al., 2006a). For NZVI stage in this work, the reaction was starred by addition of 0.2 g
l-1 of NZVI into 1.0 dm3 of different azo dye solution in 100 g l-1 and stirred in 350 rpm. For 30
minutes of reaction time, most of dye or color content was removed and 20-40% of TOC was
adsorbed on NZVI surface. Then the supernatant was withdrawn from NZVI reactor and separated
from NZVI. The supernatant was then stored in a vessel with 500 cm3 volume and pumped into the
UV/H2O2 reactor. The initial hydrogen peroxide concentration was set at 20.6 mM with 14 watts UV
irradiation for another 120 minutes. The results shown in Figure 8a presented effective removal of
dye by NZVI and litter help by UV/H2O2 process. All of three azo dyes can be completely decolorized
by 30 minutes of NZVI and 40 minutes of UV/H2O2 oxidation. The TOC removal by NZVI
adsorption and UV/H2O2 oxidation was shown in Figure 8b. Three azo dyes presented differently for
NZVI adsorption, AB24 was adsorbed more by NZVI than the other two dyes. By connecting to
UV/H2O2 process, all of three dyes were mineralized efficiently to reach 92.7, 92.8 and 94.6% TOC
removal efficiencies for AB113, DY4 and AB24, respectively. Thus, the results show using NZVI 30
minutes in conjunction with UV/H2O2 for 120 minutes can almost completely degrade AB113, DY4
and AB24 in both color and TOC.
In this combination process, the ORP dropped from 362, 356 and 372 mV down to -358, -443 and
