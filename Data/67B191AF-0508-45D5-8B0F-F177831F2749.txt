近岸影像之像素時序列分析 
本計畫使用高解析度攝影機拍攝小尺度近岸區域之連續影像，採用影像像素時序列分
析方法 (pixel time series analysis)，提供即時的波浪、流場與水下地形資訊。原本計畫預期
在二年內完成之工作項目包括：(第一年) 完成高解析度攝影機之實驗室內部參數校正，完
成西子灣現場影像觀測站架設工作及攝影機之現場外部參數校正，完成像素時序列波浪分
析模組，完成像素時序列流場分析模組。(第二年)完成利用影像分析的波速或頻率/週波數
二維波譜及線性波浪理論反算近岸水深模組，完成結合邊緣偵測與三眼立體攝影方法分析
波高模組。 
關鍵詞：遙測、光學、影像技術、近岸、波浪淺化、流 
英文摘要及關鍵詞 
Pixel time series analysis of nearshore image 
In this study, a high resolution camera was used to record a near continuous 
sequence of images of a small-scale coastal area, and pixel time series analysis was 
employed to provide the real-time wave, current and nearshore bathymetry information.  
The original proposed study will span a period of two years.  In the first year, interior 
calibration of a high resolution camera will be performed in laboratory, exterior 
calibration will then be conducted after its deployment in His-tzu-wan test site.  Various 
wave and current analysis algorithms from pixel time series will be developed.  In the 
second year, linear depth inversion from remote sensing of wave phase speeds and 2-D 
frequency-wavenumber spectrum will be conducted.  A combined edge detection and 
trinocular stereo imaging system algorithm will be developed for three-dimensional 
surface wave measurements. 
Keywords: remote sensing, optics, video technique, nearshore, shoaling wave, current. 
前言 
利用光學影像技術來遙測近岸環境已有近 25 年歷史，國外研究主要採用 OSU/Coastal 
Imaging Lab 所發展的 Argus 影像觀測系統，來進行近岸地形 (Bathymetry) 、水動力學 
(Hydrodynamics) 與海岸管理 (Coastal Zone Management) 等方面之研究。在水動力學方
面，主要探討近岸波浪淺化現象與沿岸流觀測。Argus 系統在水動力學之像素時序列分析 
(pixel time series analysis) 採用所謂的像素儀器 (pixel instruments) 即各種像素陣列 (pixel 
array) 上像素強度之時序列資料。以 Argus 系統為例，有以下各種像素陣列可分析波浪週
期、波向、方向波譜、水下地形、溯上、沿岸流等：單一像素可分析波浪週期 (Lippman and 
Holman, 1991)，Alpha array 與 Tess array 可分析波向與方向波譜 (Lippman and Holman, 
1991)，Bathy array 可分析水下地形 (Stockdon and Holman, 2000)， Run-up array 可分析溯
上 (Holland and Holman, 1991)，Vbar array 可分析沿岸流 (Chickadel et al., 2003)等。此外
PIV 影像技術也可用來分析灘面內流場 (swash flows) 的分佈 (Holland, et al., 2001)。目前
