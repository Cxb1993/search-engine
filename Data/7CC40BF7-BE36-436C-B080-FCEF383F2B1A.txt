二、 計畫的緣由與目的  
近幾年來以矽(Si)或矽鍺(SiGe)為材料的
電晶體，隨著奈米元件與製程整合技術的突飛
猛進，讓以矽或矽鍺的元件與電路之操作頻率
已經進入了 GHz 的頻段。使得 III-V 材料為主
的元件，需要往更高頻率與更高功率的應用上
做發展。其中以氮化鎵(GaN)材料為主的元件
在固定的頻率之下，具有較高的崩潰電壓。這
是因為氮化鎵是一個寬能隙的材料，它由於材
料的特性在惡劣的操作環境下依然可以有非
常好的功率輸出。現今最熱門的應用是以 GaN
材料的高電子移動率電晶體 (High Electron 
Mobility Transistor, HEMT)使用於油電混合式
動力車中的高電壓、高功率的電路裝置。 
另一個與 GaN 能隙相近之半導體：氧化
鋅(zinc oxide，ZnO)也是寬能隙半導體，其結
構 為 纖 鋅 礦 結 構 (Wurzite hexagonal 
structure)，屬於六方最密堆積。其中鋅原子位
於六角柱形結構的節點上，而氧原子則是位於
四個鋅原子所組成的四面體的中心位置。其能
隙寬度(optical band gap)約為 3.37eV，在可見
光範圍具有高穿透率。未摻雜的氧化鋅薄膜其
電阻值相當高，具壓電效應，傳統上多應用於
壓電材料。其內部傳導主要是由化學計量比的
偏差產生，帶電自由載子由氧空缺(oxygen 
vacancies)及格隙鋅原子(interstitial zinc)之淺
層施體能階(shallow donor levels)所提供。為了
提升氧化鋅薄膜的導電率，通常會摻入異價元
素，如：鋁、鎵、銦等，其導電性會大大提升。
由於摻雜入異價的元素可以提高氧化鋅薄膜
之載子濃度，因而增加了氧化鋅薄膜的導電
率。其主要原因是摻雜之異價元素取代氧化鋅
晶格中的 Zn2+離子的位置或是格隙的位置，使
得晶格中傳導電子的增加，進而提升其導電
率。但摻雜入的異價元素如 Al3+、Ga3+等，在
薄膜中除了提供傳導電子外，亦是離子化雜質
散射中心(ionized impurity scattering centers)，
同時也可能佔據晶格結構中之格隙位置而使
結晶變形，導致電子移動率下降，因此所摻雜
入異價的元素需有一適當量。  
近幾年來，以 ZnO 為主的材料已經廣泛
的應用於發光二極體 (LEDs)的透明電極之
上。除此之外，因為 ZnO 能隙與 GaN 相近，
兩材料之間有著絕佳的晶格匹配。所以也有些
是應用結合 GaN 與 ZnO 製作光電元件或是將
ZnO 當作半導體製作偏紫外光的發光二極

1. INTRODUCTION  
 
Zinc oxide (ZnO) transparent conductive oxide (TCO) is a well-known wide-band-gap material that can be used to 
form transparent contact layers (TCL) in light-emitting diodes (LEDs) [1-2]. MgxZn1−xO alloy, another potential 
transparent conductive material, has been proposed and widely investigated because MgO has a wider band gap of 7.7eV 
than ZnO [3]. Alloying MgO with ZnO produces MgxZn1−xO compounds with band gaps spanning from 3.4 to 7.7 eV. 
This study analyzes Al-doped MgxZn1−xO (AMZO) films deposited on sapphire substrates by radio-frequency (RF) 
magnetron sputtering at room temperature with subsequent annealing temperatures of 500, 600, 700, 800, 900, and 1000 
°C for 60 s in nitrogen ambient. This study also presents the temperature sensitivities of the electric characterizations of 
Al-doped MgxZn1−xO films. Hall measurements were used to characterize the Al-doped MgxZn1−xO films. The 
composition of the Al-doped MgxZn1−xO films was analyzed by x-ray diffraction (XRD). Transmission spectra were also 
measured to investigate the optical properties of Al-doped MgxZn1−xO films.     
 
 
2. DEVICE FABRICATION 
 
The Al-doped MgxZn1−xO (AMZO) films used in this study were grown at room temperature using the RF 
sputtering method on c-face sapphire substrates. The Al-doped MgxZn1−xO films were formed by a 4 inch 
ZnO/MgO/Al2O3 (76/19/5 wt %) target. Before being loaded into a deposition chamber, the substrates were cleaned in an 
ultrasonic bath with acetone, isopropyl alcohol and deionized water for 5 min. The targets were pre-sputtered for 15 min 
before growth. Sputtering was carried out at an argon flow rate of 9 sccm and a pressure of 1.2 × 10-3 Torr, at a sputtering 
power in the 150W and 200W. The distance between the target and the substrate was fixed at 7.2 cm. The growth 
thickness of the Al-doped MgxZn1−xO films was maintained at 330 nm. The thickness of the deposited Al-doped 
MgxZn1−xO films was measured from Veeco/Dektak 6M. The MgxZn1−xO samples were then annealed at 500, 600, 700, 
800, 900, 1000 °C for 60 s in a rapid thermal annealing (RTA) system for Hall measurement.
