中文摘要 
    Ti50Ni25-xPd25-yCux+y四元形狀記憶合金在 x,y≤ 10 at.%下其 Ms 變態點在 57~180℃且有不錯的形
狀記憶效應。Cu 取代 Pd 會降低其 Ms 溫度，但 Cu 取代 Ni 則提升其 Ms 點。而 Cu 取代 Pd 及 Ni
均會降低其加工性及格子常數，特別是 Pd 被取代時。經冷加工且在 650℃以下退火之 TiNiPdCu 四
元合金會有 Ti2(Ni,Pd,Cu)3及 Ti2(Ni,Pd,Cu)析出物，並使其 Ms 點及形狀記憶效應被壓抑。研究顯示
Ti50Ni20(Pd20Cu10)及 Ti50(Ni25Cu5)Pd20兩種 SMAs 之 Ms 溫度約在 110℃，兩合金之熱軋延性質優異，
冷軋延也可達 25%左右，經冷軋延後若在 500℃退火會有析出物之產生而使 SME 等特性變差，但
經 650℃ 3 分鐘以上之退火時，其 SME 則相當優異，彎曲試驗幾可達 100%。Ti50Ni20Pd30-xNbx四元
形狀記憶合金在 x≤ 2 at.%下其 Ms 點在 73~210 oC 且有不錯之冷加工性及形狀記憶效應，唯 Nb 取
代 Pd 對 Ms 點之降低遠比 Cu 取代 Pd 者來得大很多。Ti50Ni20Pd30-xNbx合金經冷軋延後退火者可得
較好之性質，唯退火溫度以 500 oC 者為佳。根據過去兩年之經驗，在第三年度配置
Ti50Ni15Pd24.5Cu10Nb0.5合金，其 Ms 溫度為 128.2oC，∆H 值 17.0J/g。研究發現其制振能、形狀記憶
效應、250oC 下之熱穩定性(熱循環、退火)皆相當優異，於 250oC 以下是具有相當工業應用價值的，
也符合計畫之目標。唯在使用時務需避免 400oC~650oC 間之熱處理，以免合金變態性質因
Ti2(Ni,Pd,Cu)、Ti2Pd 及 Ti(Ni,Cu)Pd 析出物之析出而快速且大幅度劣化。700oC 以上之熱處理可使晶
粒成長並獲得更高之相變態潛熱，也不會對此合金之變態性質產生危害。 
 
關鍵詞：TiNiPdCu、TiNiPdNb 及 TiNiPdCuNb 高溫形狀記憶合金、加工性、形狀記憶效應、退火、
析出物 
 
英文摘要 
Ti50Ni25-xPd25-xCux+y quaternary shape memory alloys (SMAs) with x,y ≤ 10at.% exhibit B2←→B19 
martensitic transformation and good shape recovery with Ms temperature in the range of 57oC to 180oC. 
The Pd substituted by Cu in these alloys significantly depresses Ms temperature but Ni replaced by Cu 
increases it. The Cu addition decreases alloy’s maximum thickness reduction during cold-rolling and 
affects its lattice constants of B19 martensite, especially when the Pd is substituted by Cu. Alloys annealed 
at 650oC form Ti2(Ni, Pd, Cu)3 and Ti2(Ni, Pd,Cu) precipitates. Ti50Ni20(Pd20Cu10) and Ti50(Ni25Cu5)Pd20 
SMAs have their Ms temperatures near 110℃. Their hot-rolling and cold-rolling formability is good with 
the cold-rolling thickness reduction being ≈ 25%. The annealing effects on the cold-rolled plates indicate 
that the 500℃ annealing forms the precipitates which deteriorate significantly the shape memory effect 
(SME). However, specimens annealed at 650℃ for more than 3 min have quite good SME and their shape 
recovery ratio can reach ≈ 100%. Ti50Ni20Pd30-xNbx quaternary SMAs with x ≤ 2 at.% also exhibit 
B2←→B19 transformation with Ms temperature in the range of 73oC to 210 oC and have rather good 
cold-rolling behavior. The effect of Pd substituted by Nb on Ms temperature is much more than that of Pb 
substituted by Cu. Cold-rolled and annealed Ti50Ni20Pd30-xNbx SMAs can improve their properties but the 
used annealing temperature can not be too high or too low in which 500oC is found to be the best. 
According to the past two years experience, Ti50Ni15Pd24.5Cu10Nb0.5 SMA is designed and has Ms 
temperature at 128.2oC with ∆H=17.0J/g. Experimental results indicate that this SMA exhibits rather good 
damping capacity, shape memory effect and has long-time thermal stability of martensitic transformation 
at 250oC, such as thermal cycling, annealing, etc. However, this SMA should be avoided to be aged in the 
temperature range of 400oC~650oC due to the precipitates of Ti2(Ni.Pd,Cu), Ti2Pd and Ti(Ni.Cu)Pd phases 
formed which will deteriorate the transformation properties. Therefore, it recommends that the annealing 
temperature should be conducted at ≧700oC to prevent the loss of its high temperature shape memory 
effect. 
 
Keywords : TiNiPdCu, TiNiPdNb and TiNiPdCuNb high temperature shape memory alloys, Ms 
temperature, cold-rolling, annealing, shape recovery, precipitate

自1987年起，紐西蘭的Yi及Moore針對TiNiPd合金系列的熔煉配置提出非傳統性(例如粉末反應
燒結)的合金製程[18-21]。本研究群也在1992年發現Ti50Ni50-xPdx（X＝20at％～50at％）合金系列均
具有高溫的形狀記憶效應 (SME) ，且其 Ms 變態點溫度範圍可控制在 76℃(Ti50Ni30Pd20) 至
532℃(Ti50Pd50)內，如圖2-1所示，為熱彈型的麻田散體相變態。該等Ti50Ni50-xPdx (x:20~50at.%) SMAs
經由DSC實驗結果發現，其變態之反應熱∆H隨Pd添加量(x量)的增加而明顯上昇，∆H值與DSC變態
峰值的溫度（M*，A*）呈一線性關係。而Ti50Ni15Pd35 SMA麻田散體在250℃經過長時間之時效處
理及Ti50Ni20Pd30SMA經冷加工之效應，皆會產生麻田散體的安定化現象。 
對於TiNiPd合金系統的機械性質，Khachin等人[22]對含37at.％Ni的TiNi-TiPd三元SMA在高溫下
進行扭曲(Torsion)試驗，在加熱後，可藉由應力誘發相變態(Stress Induced Transformation)和回復的
過程，使得此合金的應變回復率達4％。Lindquist及Wayman[23]則對Ti50Pd30Ni20合金在室溫下進行
拉伸(Tensile)試驗，在拉伸負荷達總應變的6％下，此合金的應變回復量約為2.5％；Yang及Mikkola[24]
對Ti50.7Pd29.0Ni22.3合金的形狀回復進行壓縮(Compression)試驗，在2～3％的變形後其形狀回復率約可
達90％。 
雖然TiNiPd三元SMAs是相當優良的高溫SMAs，但
Otsuka[25]卻發現其SME會隨著拉伸溫度的上昇而變差，其
原因是隨著溫度的上昇，此等SMAs滑移的臨界應力會隨之
下降。為了達到更佳的機械性質，則可使用時效、熱機處
理及添加第三合金元素等方法來改良之。Golberg等人[26]
對Ti50Ni20Pd30 SMA進行24～25％冷加工後，在高於As但低
於再結晶的溫度下進行退火，例如在400℃進行退火一小時
後，發現此合金可獲得最佳的形狀記憶性質。Suzuki等人[27]
則 在 Ti50Ni20Pd30 SMA 中 加 入 0.2at. ％ 的 硼 ， 並 比 較
Ti50Ni19.8Pd30B0.2與Ti50Ni20Pd30兩合金，發現加硼者在高溫下
其強度與延性都有良好的提升，但對於形狀記憶性質仍需
要進行適度的熱機處理方能獲得改善。Shimizu等人[28]則
對Ti50.6Ni19.4Pd30 SMA在500℃進行時效處理一小時後，藉由
Ti2Ni的析出造成析出硬化，以達到改善其SME的效果。

三、實驗方法及步驟 
 
    本研究之實驗方法及步驟，包括合金之配置、熔煉、軋延、固溶及時效處理、DSC/SME 等量
測，SME/OM 金相觀察、EPMA、XRD 結構分析等。其中合金之熔煉是使用 VAR，軋延加工有 900℃
熱軋延及室溫冷軋延，在冷熱軋延之間會做 900℃×1 小時之均質化處理。冷軋延之試片經切割後在
500℃~800℃之間作不同時間之時效，時效後做各種性質之測試，包括變態點、顯微組織、析出物
成分分析、形狀回復率及 XRD 結構分析等，最後作實驗結果之綜合討論等。 
 
四、實驗結果與討論 
4.1. TiNiPdCu 四元 SMAs 之研究 
 
    本研究以 Ti50Ni20Pd30 及 Ti50Ni30Pd20 SMAs 為本，加入 Cu 以取代 Ni 或 Pd。Ti50Ni20Pd30合金經
DSC 實驗後，得其 Ms 溫度為 238℃，As 為 243℃。今配置 Ni/Cu 及 Pd/Cu 比為 99/1 之合金，即為
Ti50(Ni19.8Cu0.2)Pd30 及 Ti50Ni20(Pd29.7Cu0.3) SMAs，經 DSC 量測後得前者之 Ms 為 233.5℃，As 為
236.9℃，後者之 Ms 為 226.8℃，As 為 237.5℃。顯然 Cu 取代 Ni 或 Pd 的量不高時其 Ms 溫度降低
的不夠多，若希望 Ms 能高於 100℃但又不要離 100℃太遠的原則來看，以 Cu 取代 Ni 或 Pd 的量有
必要加的更高，故本研究接著配置 Ni/Cu 及 Pd/Cu 比在 5/1~1/1 之間的 TiNiPdCu 四元 SMAs，並了
解其變態特性等之改變。

步討論這四種合金經退火處理後之性質。將經熱軋延及冷軋延之四種合金在 350oC、500oC 及 650oC

五、 結論 
     
本研究以探討 Ms 溫度在 100℃~250℃TiNiPdCu 及 TiNiPdNb 之四元高溫 SMAs，並同時擁有
不錯之加工性者。經以 Ti50Ni20Pd30 及 Ti50Ni30Pd20 SMAs 為本，加入 Cu 以取代 Ni 或 Pd，發現
Ti50Ni20(Pd20Cu10)及 Ti50(Ni25Cu5)Pd20兩 SMAs 之 Ms 溫度約在 110℃，並擁有一階之 B2←→B19 麻
田散體變態。這兩種合金之熱軋延性能優異，而冷軋延也可達 25%左右。經冷軋延後若在 500℃退
火會有析出物之產生使變態點大幅降低，SME 特性變差等，但冷軋延後經 650℃退火者，則經 3 分
鐘後已完全回復，而其 SME 相當優異，經 5~6 次彎曲試驗後可使其 SME 達 100%。本研究同時經
以 Ti50Ni20Pd30為本，加入 Nb 以取代 Pd 或 Ni，發現 Nb 之取代可大幅降低其 Ms 溫度(每 1 at.%Nb
取代 Pd 可降 91.3℃)，XRD 之結果發現此等 TiNiPdNb 四元合金仍為 B2←→B19 麻田散體變態。當
Pd 的量被 Nb 取代到 2at.%時，此等 TiNiPdNb 合金之軋延性並不變差，冷加工量均可達 35%。
Ti50Ni20Pd30-XNbX 合金經冷軋延後退火者可得較好之性質，但退火溫度太低(350oC)不足以進行再結
晶及晶粒成長，退火溫度太高(650oC)又會使得析出物過多造成變態峰的分離，故經冷軋延後施以中
溫 (500oC) 長時間退火者可得最好之性質。根據過去兩年之經驗，在第三年度配置
Ti50Ni15Pd24.5Cu10Nb0.5合金，其 Ms 溫度為 128.2oC，∆H 值 17.0J/g。研究發現其制振能、形狀記憶
效應、250oC 下之熱穩定性(熱循環、退火)皆相當優異，於 250oC 以下是具有相當工業應用價值的，
也符合計畫之目標。唯在使用時務需避免 400oC~650oC 間之熱處理，以免合金變態性質因
Ti2(Ni,Pd,Cu)、Ti2Pd 及 Ti(Ni,Cu)Pd 析出物之析出而快速且大幅度劣化。700oC 以上之熱處理可使晶
粒成長並獲得更高之相變態潛熱，也不會對此合金之變態性質產生危害。

Abstract 
 
SiC:H organic-like thin films are deposited on Ti50Ni50 shape 
memory alloy (SMA) by DC-pulsed plasma assisted chemical 
vapor deposition (PACVD) in the gas mixture of 
Hexamethyldisilazane (HMDSN) and Ar at room temperature. 
The deposition rate of the plasma-polymerized HMDSN 
(PPHMDSN) thin film is fast and the PPHMDSN thin films 
not only enhance the corrosion resistance but make the 
corrosion current density decay four orders. The surface 
morphology of the films is very smooth and uniform and the 
root mean square of surface roughness is in the range between 
3 and 5nm. The organic-like thin films containing peroxides or 
free radicals can be grafted with polymer acrylamide (AAm) 
which can improve the biocompatibility of TiNi SMA. 
 
Introduction 
 
TiNi-based SMAs have the excellent shape memory effect 
(SME), superelasticity and biocompatibility [1] and have been 
used as a functional material in a variety of industrial and 
medical applications such as the intracoronary stent [2], the 
ligaments’ replacement [3], the SMA microvalve to control 
drug delivery [4], the bone stamp and osteosynthesis devices 
[5], etc. The biocompatibility of metal implants is generally 
evaluated by the reaction between metal and the surrounding 
tissue. The intensity of the damage caused by metallosis is 
proportional to the quantity of toxic metallic ions produced by 
corrosion. In TiNi-based SMAs, titanium is not detrimental 
among the essential trace elements [6], while nickel proves to 
exhibit high carcinogenic and toxic potencies in vivo 
regardless the fact that Ni is an essential element to the human 
body [7]. Hence, the passivity current density of the implanted 
metal in a potentiostatic condition can be taken as an index of 
the ion release of the metal and this current density is 
sufficient to be an indicator of its biocompatibility. Since the 
corrosion resistance depends on the surface properties of the 
alloy, the coatings of PPHMDSN are employed in this study to 
improve the corrosion of Ti50Ni50 SMA and also to prevent the 
Ni ions releasing from Ti50Ni50 SMA. At the same time, the 
wettability, the relative wet friction coefficient and the 
feasibility to graft with polymer to improve their 
biocompatibility of PPHMDSN coatings on Ti50Ni50 SMA are 
also studied and discussed. 
 
Experimental Procedure 
 
Substrate Preparation 
The Ti50Ni50 SMA was prepared from titanium (99.8wt.%) and 
nickel (99.98wt.%) by using a vacuum/argon tungsten arc 
furnace. The casting ingot was hot-rolled into 1-mm-thick 
sheet at 900oC, and subsequently annealed for 30min. The 
rolled sheet was cut into 10×10×1mm specimens. The surface 
was ground on SiC papers to remove oxide and polished 
through a standard metallographic procedure to a final level of 
1.0µm alumina. Subsequently, all of the polished substrates 
were cleaned ultrasonically in cleaning reagents (Extran, 
MERCK Taiwan Ltd.), acetone and ethanol for 10min to 
remove grease or organic contamination on the surface and 
then rinsed with de-ionized water, and finally air dried. These 
specimens were stored in a dried box before use.

deposition was carried out under the given bipolar pulsed 
source for 10-30min. Frequency of the pulsed wave was 
2.38kHz. The experimental parameters are detailed in Table 1.

Surface Property of the Deposited Films 
Figure 4a shows the cathodic and anodic polarization Tafel 
curves for the TiNi substrate. The flat portion of the curve 
indicates that the TiNi readily forms a passive film. The open 
circuit potential and the corrosion rate are approximately 
0.3740V and 0.5696µA/cm2, respectively. However, the 
reproductivity of the breakdown potential is poor and gives 
widely scattered results ranging from 0.712eV to 1.299eV. For 
the PPHMDSN coatings with different deposition time, as 
shown in Fig. 4b-4d, the improvement of corrosion resistance 
is very remarkable. The PPHMDSN film on TiNi can reduce 
the current density by at least four orders of magnitude. The 
outstanding improvement shown in Fig. 4b-4d may be 
attributed to the higher resistance and the more homogeneity 
of the deposited film. The hydrophobic and homogeneity 
properties prevent the TiNi from water uptake, leading to 
lower ionic conduction by many orders of magnitude.

(d) 30min. 
 
The wettability of the deposited films can be evaluated from 
the water contact angle, as illustrated in Fig.5a. Table 4 lists 
the results of the water contact angle test. The water contact 
angle of as-polished TiNi alloy is 42° and after PPHMDSN 
deposition, the water contact angle raises to 87°, as shown in 
Figs. 5b and 5c, because the PPHMDSN film consists of more 
non-polar groups as methyl and methylene.
