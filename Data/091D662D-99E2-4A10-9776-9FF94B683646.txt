method is the frequently used technique to measure 
the dose contributed from photons and neutrons in the 
mixed radiation field. Because of the neutron cross 
sections are too small for TLD-700 relative to TLD-
600 for low energy neutrons, so that the contribution 
of neutrons to TLD-700 is usually ignored. But for 
the neutron energy is beyond 1 MeV, neutron cross 
section of TLD-700 is increased and close to TLD-
600’s. This means the contribution of neutrons to 
TLD-700 becomes important. Thereby, to use the dual 
TLD method exactly, the neutron spectra in mixed 
radiation field should be realized. The LiI (Eu) 
detector with Boron Sphere spectrometer, BSS, an 
often used method to obtain neutron spectra, was used 
in this study. BSS is a set of high density 
polyethylene spheres of different diameters: 0, 2, 3, 
5, 8,10 and 12 inches. The method used in this study 
was verified by measuring the neutron spectrum of Cf-
252 source. Besides, after verification of BSS 
method, the neutron spectrum which induced by the 
high energy medical cyclotron was estimated.

三、 結果與討論 
   本期研究(一年期計畫，99.8.1~100.9.30)利用國際上常使用的多球體中子能譜評估系統(Boron 
Sphere spectrometer, BSS)，以不同尺寸大小的聚苯乙烯(polystyrene)球與6LiI(Eu) 閃爍偵檢器，建立中
子能譜量測技術。由於不同尺寸的硼球會對影響不同能量的中子在球心處偵檢器所度量到的信號響
應，這些尺寸的聚苯乙烯球可測量中子的能量範圍可由0.025 eV至數百MeV，將置於不同大小球內
6
LiI(Eu)所測量到的計數率(Count rate)，由UMG 3.3 Unfolding軟體展開即可評估獲得中子能譜。 
   本計畫已完成利用多球體 BSS 與碘化鋰 6LiI(Eu)閃爍偵檢器進行中子能譜度量之測試與驗證，並
計算出不同中子能量對於不同尺寸的 BSS 與偵檢器的響應因子(Response factor, R)。利用國家游離輻
射標準實驗室之 Cf-252 中子射源以 Cf-252 中子射源進行照射 BSS 與碘化鋰 6LiI(Eu)閃爍偵檢器，做
中子能譜之度量方法驗證。將置於不同大小球內 6LiI(Eu)所測量到的計數率(Count rate)，以及用蒙地
卡羅模擬(Monte Carlo simulation)在相同的幾何條件下，計算 Cf-252 中子射源所發射出之不同中子能
量對於不同尺寸的 BSS 與偵檢器的響應因子(Response factor, R)(結果列於表 3.1)，再經由 UMG 3.3 解
碼(Unfolding)軟體展開即可獲得中子能譜。 
利用蒙地卡羅方法模擬每一單能中子經不同尺寸(0, 2, 3, 5, 8, 10 及 12 英吋)球體後至 6LiI(Eu)偵檢
器的響應(response)並建立響應函數(response function)。模擬的幾何條件為 6LiI(Eu)閃爍晶體的尺寸為直
徑 4 mm，長度為 4 mm 圓柱型晶體，偵檢器的材質設為 100 % 6Li，其他雜質元素含量低且對於中子的
作用截面也非常小，因此模擬的時候可以忽略不計。而晶體外用圓柱型的鋁殼包含著，其尺寸為直徑
14.5mm、長度為 16mm、鋁殼的厚度為 2 mm(如圖 3.1 所示)，偵檢器內為真空狀態，偵檢器的位置放
置於 BSS 球體內的中心點位置。模擬射源的位置是在各球體的表面 0cm 處，射源為一面射源(disk 
source)，射源大小與需模擬 BSS 球體大小的直徑相同，代表該射源均勻照射此球體。模擬的 BSS 球體
尺寸一共有 7 組(直徑 0, 2, 3, 5, 8, 10 及 12 英吋)，每一球體模擬的中子射源能量從 0.01 eV 至 100 MeV，
一共有 51 組(energy bin)，因此模擬計算之結果可獲得 51 組能量的單能中子經過各 BSS 球體及 6LiI(Eu)

四、 結論 
    本計畫利用一年的時間，利用國際上常使用的多球體中子能譜評估系統(Boron Sphere 
spectrometer, BSS)，以不同尺寸大小(0, 2, 3, 5, 8, 10 及 12 英吋)的聚苯乙烯(polystyrene)球與
6
LiI(Eu) 閃爍偵檢器建立中子能譜量測技術，利用蒙地卡羅方法模擬每一單能中子經不同尺寸(0, 
2, 3, 5, 8, 10 及 12 英吋)球體後至 6LiI(Eu)偵檢器的響應因子(response factor, R)並建立響應函數
(response function)。將不同大小聚苯乙烯球所測量到的計數率(Count rate)，以及用蒙地卡羅模擬
(Monte Carlo simulation)在相同的幾何條件下，計算出每一種的中子能量對於不同尺寸的 BSS 與
偵檢器的響應因子，再經由 UMG 3.3 軟體展開(Unfolding)即可獲得中子能譜。本研究藉由度量
評估 Cf-252 中子源之能譜進行方法驗證，並針對高能量醫用迴旋加速器設施在運轉過程中所產
生的中子輻射進行中子能譜量測，結果發現：小體積靶(Target 2)於迴旋加速器正前方屏蔽門外
15 cm 處的所產生之中子通量高於大體積靶(Target 1)的中子通量，此可推論於大體積靶本身對中
子的衰減緣故。

ABSTRACT 
 
Assessment of doses and neutron spectra induced by a high energy medical Linac 
 
Lin J.R.
1
, Hsu, F.Y.
1,2,*
, Hsu, C.H.
1
,  Liu, M.T.
3
, Huang, S.S.
3
, Chao, J.H.

, Huang, G.R.

, Yuan, M.C.
 4
 
 
1
  Department of Biomedical Engineering and Environmental Sciences, National Tsing Hua University, 
Hsinchu, Taiwan. 
2
 Nuclear Science and Technology Development Center, Tsing Hua University Hsinchu, Taiwan. 
3
 Department of Radiation Oncology, Changhua Christian Hospital, Changhua, Taiwan.
 
4 
Health Physics Division, Institute of Nuclear Energy Research, Longtan, Taiwan 
 
The medical linear accelerator (Linac) is the main therapeutic equipment used in modern radiotherapy. For 
the energy of photon beam produced by a Linac is higher than 10 MeV, probability of photonuclear interaction 
increases, therefore, neutron particles will be induced. To assess the induced neutron dose, 
dual-thermoluminescent dosimeter (dual-TLD, TLD600/700) method is frequently used. However, the 
accuracy of dual-TLD method depends on the energy spectrum of the induced neutrons. The sensitivity of 
TLD-700 chip to low energy neutron is negligible, but it will increase and become significant relative to 
TLD-600 chip as the neutron energy is higher. To estimate the neutron doses correctly, measurements of 
energy spectra of neutrons are necessary. This paper, thereby, assessed and discussed the energy spectra and 
doses of neutrons induced by a Linac used in radiotherapy.  
Gold foils inside the Bonner spheres with different diameters (2”, 3”, 5”, 8”, 10” and 12”) were used to 
estimate the neutron spectra induced by a medical Linac (with max. energy of 15 MV X rays). Response 
functions of the gold foil were calculated with Monte Carlo simulation Code (MCNP). By means of the 
response functions, activated-gamma counting rates of the gold foils in different size of spheres and the 
unfolding UMG 3.3 code, neutron spectra were estimated. Besides, the doses and neutron spectrum at 
different depths (from 0 to 20 cm depth) of solid water phantom were also estimated. The doses contributed 
from X rays and thermal neutrons and fast neutrons were assessed by using dual TLD(TLD 600/700) method. 
Two algorithms, considering (depending on the assessed neutron spectrum) and ignoring  the dose responses 
of TLD-700 chips to higher energy neutrons were applied and discussed. 
Two main peaks, thermal(<0.1eV) and fast (about 1 MeV) were showed in the assessed neutron spectra. 
For shallow depth, fluence of high energy neutrons is higher, ignoring the dose responses of TLD-700 chips to 
higher energy neutrons would overestimate the dose of thermal neutrons.
