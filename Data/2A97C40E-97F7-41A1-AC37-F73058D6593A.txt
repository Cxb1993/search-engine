（一） 計畫中文摘要
本計畫將利用矽酸鹽添加物和沸石的組合而成的過濾材料，並以不織布抽絲編
織的方式將其製作成特定孔隙大小的過濾材料。矽酸鹽添加物可以降低流經其表面
的水體的氧化還原電位。透過不同比例的組合，並配合物理性與化學性的表面修飾，
設計出兼具降低氧化還原電位和吸附陰陽離子能力的過濾材料。其最大的優點在
於，不需要額外提供電力，製作成小型化或攜帶型裝置過濾系統。藉由組合性機能
性纖維棉，任何過濾液體經由本濾膜濾芯後即可得到兼具抗氧化、除氯與除重金屬
功能之液體。本計畫之濾膜濾芯可處理水質至飲用水、沐浴用水或化妝用水等用途。
（二） 計畫英文摘要
This project will use SiO2 components and Zeolite to build a liquid filtering material.
We will develop several techniques, including nonwoven, hemstitch, and braid methods to
develop special and different pore size/hold filtering materials. SiO2 related components
will reduce the oxidative redox potential and increase pH value in the filtered water. By
use of different percentage of materials and components design and modification of
surface physical and chemical structure, we will develop a novel liquid filtering material
with both lowering redox potential and adhering anion/cation ions. The best characteristics
of these products will not need the electric supply and can be made a small-size and
portable liquid filtering material system. By the combination of functional cotton cellulose,
any liquids after this filtering setup will obtain antioxidant, de-chlorine and heavy metal
removing in the application. This project will develop a filtering membrane or setup to
treat the water and apply to drinking water, SPA water, cosmetic water, and even medical
water like dribbling solution or organ preservation fluids.
