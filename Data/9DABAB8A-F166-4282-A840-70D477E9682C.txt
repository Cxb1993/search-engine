XRD 繞縳分析
煆燒及燒結產物經 X光繞射儀分析，並與

掃描式電子顯微鏡分析
由 SEM 分析(Fig.2)可知，前導化合物非
常容易聚集在一起。此現象主要是由於分子間
的凡得瓦力，水氣的吸收，及粒子表面的缺陷
或電性不平衝所導致凝聚及吸附現象，但到達
煆燒溫度後，顆粒會連結，成長，當煆燒溫度
600 C又 6 小時(Fig.3),顆粒長大,聚集減少,
結晶性增加.

介電性質幾及電阻分析
量測在不同頻率下之交流電阻，其圖形整理
於 Figs. 4 & 5。由 Figs. 4 & 5 顯示出頻率
愈大其交電阻係數愈小，有些則成反比的現

則愈小。
由 Figs. 6 & 7 知介電常數部分也呈現頻率愈

samples，當燒結溫度愈高，頻率對其介電常
數的影響反而不甚明顯。
五、結論
(1) 由 TGA 和 XRD 分 析 得 知
LiAl1/3Mo1/3Mn1/3O2 及 LiAl1/3Mo1/3V1/3O2 的
較佳煆燒溫度是 600 ℃，持溫時間皆固定為
6 小時。
由 LCR 測量儀測量其電容值經公式換算成介
電常數知其介電常數均不甚高，
(2) 除了 1100oC 外,在較低頻、低溫燒結
下，其皆在 45 以下，屬低界電物質。但隨著
燒結溫度下降或量測頻率的升高，其介電常數
會隨之下降。
而在 1 kHz - 1 MHz, LiAl1/3Mo1/3Mn1/3O2
電阻為 10 的 3-6 階Ω-cm, LiAl1/3Mo1/3V1/3O2
電阻為 10 的 3-5 階Ω-cm。

The chemical equation of Li-Al-Mn oxides ceramic matrix composites in the
process of chemical coprecipitation can be written as follows:
LiOH + x Al(NO3)3 + (1-x)Mn(NO3)2  LiAlxMn1-xO2 where x = 2/3, 1/3
The chemicals used were all of high purity. LiOH, Al(NO3)3 and Mn(NO3)2 were
used as the starting reagents of Li(I), Al(III) and Mn(II) , respectively.
Figure 1 shows the flow chart for chemical coprecipitation. The sample index and
mole ratio of Li : Al :Mn for this study, LiAlxMn1-xO2 , are 1:2/3:1/3 and 1:1/3:2/3,
respectively.
RESULTS AND DISCUSSION
Lithium aluminum manganese oxide precursor
FTIR analysis
The FTIR patterns of the lithium aluminum manganese oxide precursor were shown
in Fig.2. It is clear from Fig. 2 that there are peaks at 1200-1450 cm-1 and 3000-3700
cm-1 for OH bending, around 1500-1750 cm-1 for NO3-, at 500-700 cm-1 for spinel.
TGA/DTA and XRD analyses of coprecipitated powder
There is a weight loss in the vicinity of 373 K for the Li-Al-Mn oxide in TGA/DTA,
owing to the vaporization of the water in the precursor from absorption heat. There is
also a weight loss in 493 K, because nitrate reacted into nitrogen oxide. In the vicinity
of 573 K the OH- reacted into water and the vaporization of water from absorption
heat. Heated to 1073 K, the product of LiAlxMn1-xO2 is complete for the reaction.
There are clear peaks from the XRD pattern of the powder in coprecipitation of
Li-Al-Mn oxide dried at 473 K for 24 h( Fig. 3). It is obvious that there is a crystal
structure LiAlxMn1-xO2 .
EDA Analysis and SEM images of LiAlxMn1-xO2
The EDA analysis of lithium aluminum manganese oxide precursor was shown in
Table 1. It is seen from Table 1 that when the ratio of Al : Mn is very different,
there is an appropriate ratio into the reaction.
The SEM images of lithium aluminum manganese oxide at various ratios were
shown in Fig. 4. It is obvious from Fig.4 that there is a clear phenomenon of
agglomeration. This phenomenon is attributed to van der Waals forces between
molecules, the absorption of vapor and the defect for the surface of the grain or
having no balance in electricity to produce aggregation and tendency of adsorptive
outer substances[4,5].
Particle Size Distribution of LiAlxMn1-xO2
The average particle sizes of LiAl2/3Mn1/3O2 and LiAl1/3Mn2/3O2 are 0.72 and 1.10m,
a function of temperature by SQUID. The magnetic susceptibility decreases with
increasing temperature. The magnetic intensity, I, with magnetic strength, H, is
written as I = XH, where X is magnetic susceptibility. And susceptibility is
proportional to 1/T as C/T [6,7]. SQUID was used to measure 1/X vs. T. The results
for the various sintered are antiferromagnetism.
Measurement of Resistivity for the Sintered Body in Air at Room Temperature
The electrical resistivity vs. frequency for the body of Al : Mn = 2/3 : 1/3 or
1/3 : 2/3 sintered at 1200, 1300 and 14000C in air for 6 h were shown in Figs. 8 and 9.
It is clear from Figs. 8-9 that the electrical resistivity decreased with increasing
frequency. The resistivity increased with increasing temperature.
Measurement of Dielectric Constant for the Sintered Body
Dielectric constant can be obtained byr = (ta cp) / (r2o ) [8], where ta is the
thickness of the sample, r is the measured radius of the holder, 2.5 x 10-3 m,o is the
dielectric constant in air, 8.854 x 10-12 F/m and cp is the measured capacitance. The
dielectric constant vs. frequency for the body of Al : Mn = 2/3 : 1/3 or 1/3 : 2/3
sintered at 1200, 1300 and 14000C in air for 6 h were shown in Figs. 10 and 11. It is
obvious from Figs. 10-11 that the dielectric constant decreases with increasing
frequency. The dielectric constant decreases with increasing sintering temperature.
And that the dielectric constant of high aluminum content is larger than that of low
aluminum content.
Charge and discharge curves of the lithium aluminum manganese oxide
The charge discharge curves of the lithium aluminum manganese oxide were
shown in Fig. 12. The initial potential was 4.4 V vs. Li/Li+, and charged after
discharged 15 h. From Fig. 12 it is clear that the potential drop was slow and
there was a longer time over the potential 4.1 V compared with LiAlO2.
Hence the LiAl2/3Mn1/3O2 is the best cathode material for Li-Al-Mn-O system in
the lithium ion battery.
