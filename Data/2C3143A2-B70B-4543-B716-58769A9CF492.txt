 1
以生物程序進行廚餘轉化產氫之研究 
A study on hydrogen production from kitchen waste with 
biological processes 
 
摘要 
氫能是一種環保與經濟兼具，且能永續供應之綠色能源，已經被國際公認為未來重要能源
之一。生物產氫可應用於生質體或有機廢棄物之資源化產氫，是合乎環保概念與資源回收
的能源產生方式，其中厭氧醱酵是目前被評估為最具發展潛能之生物產氫法。有鑑於廚餘
中含大量適合於產氫之米飯，因此本研究以海岸潮間帶污泥為生物觸媒，進行米飯澱粉醱
酵產氫之各項環境因子探討，企圖尋找產氫之最適培養條件，以應用於廚餘醱酵產氫。本
研究依序進行不同 pH (4.5-6.5)及不同基質濃度(10-40 g COD/l)之批次產氫實驗，並以修正
型 Gompertz 方程式探討其產氫行為。結果顯示該產氫污泥適合於酸性條件下培養，並以
pH 5.0 之產氫表現最佳；在各種不同基質濃度之產氫實驗中，發現以 40 g COD/l 進行培養
時呈現明顯基質抑制現象，並以 20 g COD/l 之產氫表現最佳。綜觀本實驗結果，最適培養
條件為 pH 5.0 及基質濃度 20 g COD/l，產氫速率與氫氣產率分別為 305 ml/l/h 與 8.38 mmole 
H2/g COD。 
關鍵字：生物產氫、廚餘、米飯澱粉、環境因子 
 
Abstract 
Hydrogen energy is clean, efficient, and recognized as a potential alternative to fossil fuels in the 
future.  Production of hydrogen gas from a more environmentally friendly means, such as 
biological processes, finds its potential to play a crucial role in global hydrogen supply.  In 
particular, hydrogen production via anaerobic fermentation of organic substrates is considered 
one of the most economically effective routes for biohydrogen production.  Kitchen waste is a 
carbohydrate-rich organic solid waste, thereby being a suitable substrate for biohydrogen 
production.  Two factors, namely, pH and substrate concentration, were intensively examined 
for their effects on H2 production activity.  The results of cumulative H2 production in the batch 
experiments were described by the modified Gompertz equation.  The results show that the H2 
production efficiency and the composition of soluble metabolites were found to be highly 
sensitive to the change in pH, as pH 5.0 seemed to give the best overall H2 production 
performance.  Using the preferable pH (5.0) for bioH2 production based on the foregoing results, 
the H2 production performance of cultures with different substrate concentrations of 10-40 g 
COD/l was investigated.  The results showed that there was strong substrate inhibition effect at a 
concentration of 40 g COD/l, causing a decrease in the rate of hydrogen production.  The 
maximum H2 production efficiency occurred when the substrate concentration was 20 g COD/l.

2.4 分析方法 
生物產氣及液相之組成分析均以氣相層析儀(SIMAZU GC14-B)進行分析，氣體組成分
析使用溫度傳導式偵測器(temperature conductivity detector, TCD)，其載體(carrier gas)為氬
氣，注射口及偵測器溫度皆為 140℃，管柱啟始溫度 50 (℃ 維持 1 分鐘)，升溫梯度 30 /min℃ ，
最終溫度 200 (℃ 維持 1 分鐘)。液相組成分析使用火焰離子偵測器(flame ionization detector , 
FID)，其載體(carrier gas)為氮氣，注射口及偵測器溫度為 185℃，注射口溫度為 175℃，管
柱啟始與最終溫度維持 145℃，過程中不進行升溫。 
化學需養量(COD)、揮發性懸浮固體(volatile suspended solid, VSS)及總醣濃度分析分別

發明人/創作人 林屏杰、李國興、張嘉修、楊立豪、賴志揚 
中文：台灣地區每日廚餘產生量約為 4,000 公噸，約佔一般家庭
垃圾量的 20~30%；由於廚餘含有大量的碳水化合物，適合作為
生物產氫之基質來源。基於垃圾減量與資源回收再利用之理念，
本研究旨在發展廚餘醱酵產氫技術，首先醱酵產氫之環境因子(如
培養溫度、基質濃度及 pH 等)已獲確認，並進一步建立出連續醱
酵產氫之最適操作策略。 
技術說明 
英文：As a major burden to the environment, the generation of 
kitchen waste amounts to 4000 tons per day in Taiwan, accounting 
for 20-30% of municipal solid waste.  Kitchen waste is a 
carbohydrate-rich organic solid waste, suits to be used as a substrate 
for biohydrogen production.  From the perspective of waste 
reducing and resource recycling, the objective of this study is to 
develop the technology of hydrogen production from kitchen waste. 
At first, the optimal control of environmental factors (temperature, 
substrate concentration, pH, etc.) has been identified.  Furthermore, 
the optimal operation strategies for continuous hydrogen production 
have been established.
