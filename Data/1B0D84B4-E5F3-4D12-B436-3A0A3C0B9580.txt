Research Center, one of famous graphic arts industries, Dr. Joe Webb had estimated the output value of 
commercial printing, under the largest floating condition, is 1,011 billion dollars in 2007, 904 in 2008, 843 
in 2009, and 777 in 2010.(Frank Na, 2007) 
 
    After financial tsunami, on the basis of System Innovation Theory, every country in the world makes 
efforts to increase international competitive advantage and get the leading position. (Sung M H, Ben-Jeng 
Wang, 2009) Also, industries must take customer need into account and focus on product differentiation. 
When faced with challenges and crises, they have to provide appropriate solution and strategy to increase 
enterprise profit timely. 
 
    Without doubt, if industries want to survive and thrive in the future, they have to take Innovative R & D 
and Value-Added service into account. 
 
    Through literary reviews, we understand the development status quo and problems faced in domestic 
Printing Industry. In order to implement the Triangulation, we apply in-depth interview method, interview 
the manager and research staff of Printing Industry, and make on-site observations. 
 
    On the basis of Empirical Analysis, this study explore the principle and strategy of TRIZ and its 
feasibility in the process of research and development in Printing Industry. In addition, our project is try to 
build a suitable process, create a innovative model, and provide advice to accelerate industry upgrade and 
transformation.
