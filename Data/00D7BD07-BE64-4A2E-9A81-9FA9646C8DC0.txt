計畫績效評估                                                                     第二部分 
 
二、 計畫實際達成度與預期目標之差異 
   (說明本年度執行的成效，以及實際成效與預期成效之差異說明。若進度  
   落後，請提出彌補方法與措施。) 
 
1. 以多層次模式建立三度空間數值模式。目前採用 4 層分層進行模擬，依據各地水深，
內部各層深度分別為 7m、15 m、25 m，任何格點底層下界皆為海底。多層次模式
之運算，採用外模式(External Mode)與內模式(Internal Mode)交互運用。外模式之程
式已建置完成，內模式之程式正在建置中。 
2. 現場實測的規劃已完成，預計在 100 年度在基隆海檻上分五測站，蒐集 3 次至少 15
日的時序資料，除能與 99 年度資料作長期間的比較，並試圖找出最合適測試潮流發
電機組位置。  
3. 由於該發電機必須置於海水之中，因此必須能有水密之功能，國內目前尚未建立生
產此等水密產品之技術，因此暫時求助國外有此等能力的廠家 Cusson Marine 
Technology。發電機之尺寸為直徑約 20cm，長度約 27cm，其外殼需要與海水直接
接觸作為冷卻，因此發電機可以設計安裝於外殼（casing）之內，此時需要讓 casing
內之海水是流動的。為了讓發電機可以方便安裝於軸對稱體之 casing 內，吾人亦將
發電機之支撐基座改成圓形。 
4. 水輪葉片（turbine blade）若能夠永遠朝向海流來向，方能使水輪葉片回收最多的能
量，為達此目的，本測試機組將於水輪轂部之後方設計箭形艉翼，可使水輪葉片永
遠面向潮流入流的方向。另外，整組發電設備將設置在一組滾珠軸承（ball bearing）
之上，能隨流向變化自由旋轉，使電力輸出線可順利經由類似電刷的傳輸器將電力
引出，而此部分水密亦需要有軸封的設計。 
5. 本年度擬以上一年度分析之設計波浪重現值進行發電機組受力分析，已完成 ANSYS
分析軟體之採購與安裝，簡化模型並已初步建立。規劃先行以規則波進行受力分析，
爾後再進行不規則波分析。 
6. 為了取得不同潮位及晴朗的天氣下的衛星影像，本研究所取得之衛星影像分布在不同
月份。而不同時間太陽光到達海面的強度會有差異。本研究將利用太陽與觀測點的幾
何關係，以降低太陽位置影響後，再求取不同潮位時淺海水深變化。 
 
 
 
三、 計畫架構(含樹狀圖)： 
本整合型計畫由一個總計畫和五個子計畫組合而成，如下所示，計畫目的為從事我
國自主潮流發電技術開發與研究。計畫工作目標為「評估台灣環島潮流發電潛能、開發
設計潮流發電機組雛形、從事推動潮流發電時所可能遭遇之相關問題研究」，以各子計
畫的研究結果探討潮流發電的各種問題，並尋求最佳解決方法。 
 3
計畫績效評估                                                                     第二部分 
 
相對於洋流(Ocean current)發電，潮流不但較容易預測(具有週期性)，且通常可以在近岸
規劃合適的潮流發電潛力場址，未來在廠站建置與電力的輸送利用上更具有吸引力。 
即便潮汐發電已被視為一種近乎成熟的技術，在它仍有諸多有待研究改進與克服的
地方，譬如如何提高發電效率、如何降低發電潮流流速門檻、如何減少天然環境(如颱
風)的破壞等，在不同的設置地點上，潮流發電方式也有它的特殊性存在。本計畫所研
發之潮流發電機可依比例尺放大至 MW 級之機組。而這些 MW 級之機組可裝置於本計
畫所找到的北部海域有發電潛能之場址。 
完成此整合型計畫後，預期可以獲得以下效益： 
1. 掌握我國沿海潮流狀態，瞭解環島潮流發電潛能。 
2. 建立我國自製 3 瓩級潮流發電機組，以及測試的實驗平台，提高我國國海
洋能研發能力。 
3. 從學術上研討海洋能開發技術，搭配工業界先進之工業技術，推動我國海
洋能開發產業。 
4. 瞭解海洋能在營運階段所可能面臨之問題與解決方法，包含對於自然外力
之衝擊，提供未來具體開發時之政策研擬參考。 
5. 培育海洋能科技研究人才，建立海洋能研究團隊。 
6. 長期推動海洋能科技研究，作為政府推動海洋能政策之技術支援。 
7. 深入瞭解基隆海檻上方潮流、地形等特性，建立國內潮流發電測試場址。

Positive Definite Advection Transport Algorithm (MPDATA) (Smolarkiewicz and Grabowski, 
1990) 的數值計算方法，用來模擬溫、鹽之輸送混合狀況。此數值計算方法的目的，在
於保證密度場在水團混合與輸送的過程中，維持其絕對正值的自然性質。結合上述的三
種數值計算方法，對於海洋混合層、密度鋒面結構及水團間混合、擴散過程的數值模擬，
有更進一步的改進。

控制方程式，但是運動方程式依然遵守質量守恆及動量守恆的法則，在卡式座標系統下

計畫第一年度(民國99年)建立了包含十種統計分布的頻率分析(Frequency analysis, 
FA)方法，頻率分析是指根據某水文現象的統計特性，利用現有水文資料，分析水文變
數設計值與出現頻率（或重現期）之間的定量關係 (詳見第一年期末報告)。頻率分析
流程為：(一)、擷取現有水文資料組成樣本時序列；(二)、套配不同統計分佈並加以檢
定以決定最合適的統計分佈，此為頻率分析主要的步驟，代表的意義是利用少數短期的
資料(樣本)來推估母體，而該母體可以以一個統計分佈函數來描述；(三)、藉由該統計
分佈函數推求相應於各種頻率（即發生機率，也就是重現期）的設計值。所套配的統計
分布函數中包含了若干的參數，這些參數可以利用最大概似法(Max. likelihood method, 
MLM)、動差法(Method of moment, MOM)或線性動差法(Linear method of method, 
LMOM)等方法求解，本計畫採用動差法求解統計分布參數。為了檢定統計分佈套配分
析資料的合適程度，本計畫採用卡方檢定(Chi-Square Goodness-of-Fit Test)和柯司
(Kolmogorov-Smirnov Goodness-of-Fit Test)檢定兩種方法，假使兩種方法都通過檢定，
則以均方根誤差(Root mean square error, RMSE)最小者為最合適的統計分布函數。 
本子計畫分析第二子計畫在基隆海檻所採集的波浪資料，量測水深分別位於 5m 與
15m 水深處，根據第二子計畫的初步結果與建議，測試用的小型機組將設置於 5~6m 水
深附近，未來大型機組則可能設置於 15~20m 水深處，這兩個波浪量測站正是評估潮流
發電機組承受波浪外力最符合實際狀況的資料，此波浪資料在 2010 年共有四次短期量
測，每次量測均約歷時 1 個月，量測時段分別是 2/11-3/12、4/20-5/21、7/2-7/20、
9/28-10/24，共約 103 天資料。 
由於海檻上方實測波浪數據並不算長，進行頻率分析所產生的不確定性很高，而
距離基隆海檻不遠處的龍洞海域有氣象局設置的資料浮標，其數據累積時間已經超過
10 年，是理想的分析對象。本子計畫分析基隆海檻與龍洞浮標波浪數據間的關係，分
析結果顯示當海面波高在 2m 以下時，基隆海檻上方深水處(15m 處)之波高與龍洞海域
波高大致上是接近的(如圖 5-1)；然而當海面波高超過 2m 時，應是受基隆嶼遮蔽效應
的影響，基隆海檻深水處(15m)之波高僅為龍洞海域波高之一半(53%)；另外，由於碎波
的關係，基隆海檻上方淺水處(5m)的波高在 1m 下時反而比深水處(15m)為大(如圖
5-2)，但當波高超過 1m 時，海檻上方淺水處和深水區之波高相當一致。

Workshop Organization 
 
The objective of this workshop is to provide a forum for East Asian researchers, 
engineers, and students to exchange their new ideas and to introduce their latest 
activities in the field of the marine environmental engineering and management. 
 
The workshop was originally organized by the Marine Environmental Committee, the 
Society of Naval Architects of Japan (MEC-SNAJ) & the Korean Society for Marine 
Environmental Engineering (KOSMEE) in 2003. In 2005, Taiwan joined the workshop, 
which was extended by East Asian Workshop for Marine Environments (EAWOMEN). 
In 2009, China joined the workshop. This workshop has been held every 2 years. 
Between workshops, we have sessions in the IEEE/MTS Oceans-Asia Conference 
like in OCEANS’06 Singapore, OCEANS’08 Kobe and OCEANS’10 Sydney.

Tidal power is a renewable energy source and considered as one of energy solutions 
in the context of global warming. The tidal currents measured at some stations over 
Keelung Sill (25.18oN, 121.78oE), which are located between Keelung Island and 
Keelung Harbor north of Taiwan, are above 1.5 m/s and more than 2.5 m/s at their 
maximum. This study is to design a 3-dimensional numerical model to simulate tidal 
current around Keelung Sill. The 3-d model is constructed by means of layered model 
(Simons, 1973). The implement of layered model is performed interchangeably 
through external mode and internal mode. The external model is to compute the 
volume transport in whole depth in each grid point within the model. The internal 
mode is to calculate the velocity shear between layers. The tidal elevation for driving 
water transport in external mode is computed by harmonic constants, which are M2, 
S2, K1, O1, N2 and P1 in this study. The harmonic constants of these partial tides are 
obtained from the wbsite (http://140.121.165.75/). At present we choose 4 layers in 
the layered model depending on the topography within this region. The depths of 
layer interfaces are 7m, 15m and 25m respectively. The surface layer (0-7m), the 
second layer (7-15m), the third layer (15-25m) and the forth layer (25m-bottom) can 
represent velocities at depths of 3.5m, 11m, 20m, and near bottom respectively.

The least-squares finite-element method (LSFEM) for approximating the 
solution of boundary value problems of partial differential equations has been studied 
at least three decades [[4, [8, [12, [15, [16, [17]. In recent years, LSFEMS have been 
receiving increasing attention in both engineering and mathematics communities. In 
the engineering literature, for solving Stokes and Navier-Stokes equations by the 
LSFEMs, the L2 LSFEMs are the most popular one because L2 LSFEM exhibit 
theoretical and computational advantages in the algorithmic design and 
implementation [[12, [15]. One main advantage of LSFEMs is that a single 
approximating space can be used for all variables [[4, [8], and its choice of 
approximating space is not subject to the Ladyzhenskaya-Babuska-Brezzi (LBB) 
condition [[11, [12, [15]; The other advantage is that the resulting system of equations 
is symmetric and positive-definite (SPD), therefore, can be solved efficiently with the 
preconditioned conjugate gradient method [[5]. 
We have applied the LSFEM to shallow-water equations in previous studies [[18, 
[19] where  method is used for time-stepping integration. In this study, we employ 
the space-time approximation for both space discretization and time integration. 1D 
shallow-water equations are described in the next section. The space-time LSFEM for 
the shallow-water equations and the associated condition number is then followed. 
The developed model is applied to flow past an elliptic hump and flow past a vertical 
cylinder. Based on the computed results, summaries and conclusions are made.

CONCLUSION 
Different least-squares finite-element formulations for shallow-water equations can be 
derived depending on the choice of norm, variables, interpolations, and possible 
including the treatment of boundary conditions. Based on the computed results of 1D 
shallow-water equation, We found condition number, as shown in Table 1, is 
proportional to  O h  instead of  2O h . Reduced-order approaches (UW-formulation) 
result in better conditioned system of equations, and therefore, more stable and 
accurate. However, UW-formulations also result in more dofs, i.e. larger system of 
equations to solve, hence, not as efficient as direct approaches (U-formulation). High-
order approaches are suitable to resolve large gradients and fine structure of 
flow/wave fields. They also result in more dofs and poor conditioned system of 
equations. Apparently, there are compromises for reduced-order (UW-formulation) 
approaches and high-order (U-formulation) approaches. Combination of the two 
approaches, UW-formulation with low-order interpolations, provides an attractive and 
efficient algorithm for the problems including high-order derivatives and large 
variations of solution.

Extreme Wave impact to the energy converters in the sea  
 
 
Dong-Jiing Doong 
 
* Department of Marine Environmental Informatics, National Taiwan Ocean University, Keelung, 
20224, Taiwan, doong@mail.ntou.edu.tw  
 
 
ABSTRACT 
The purpose of this study is to study the natural loadings to the ocean energy converter, including 
ocean waves and currents. Extreme statistical approach is applied to study the design wave heights 
as the condition for loading assessment. In addition, this study proposes the instantaneous freak 
wave in the ocean may bring huge impact to the ocean energy converter. It needs to be considered 
in the future assessment of natural loadings. Loading computation on a simplified bottom mounted 
tidal current turbine shows the upper side and blades of the device receive major loading. The joint 
effect of ocean wave and current changes the loading frequency, compared to unique action of 
waves. 
 
KEYWORDS 
 
Extreme wave; Ocean energy converter; Loading 
 
 
1. INTRODUCTION  
The oceans, covering more than 70 % of the Earth, have long been appreciated as a vast renewable 
energy source. The energy is stored in the oceans partly as thermal energy, partly as kinetic energy 
(waves and currents) and also in chemical and biological products. Numerous techniques for 
extracting energy from the sea have been suggested, such as the tidal energy, the Ocean Thermal 
Energy Conversion (OTEC), the energy extracted from marine and tidal currents, and the ocean 
wave energy. The kinetic energy present in marine and tidal currents can be converted to electricity 
using relatively conventional turbine technology. Harnessing the kinetic energy in waves presents a 
different set of technical challenges and a wide variety of designs have been suggested. Ocean 
thermal energy conversion is possible in locations with large temperature differences, extracting 
energy using a heat engine. Wave power is the transport of energy by ocean surface waves, and the 
capture of that energy to do useful work. Ocean power generation is not currently a widely 
employed commercial technology but many prototypes or research projects have been carried out in 
the sea. People believe that the ocean energy has the potential of providing a substantial amount of 
new renewable energy around the world in the near future. 
The ocean is constantly moving and stirred up by powerful natural forces such as sea winds and the 
gravitational pull of the moon. The forces of the ocean, on occasion, turn violent. Great cyclones, 
typhoons and stormwinds can form over large masses of water, wreaking destruction upon Man's 
property, including the ocean energy converters deployed in the sea Ocean waves, which feature

3.  ANALYSIS OF WAVE LOADING  
This study computes preliminarily the loading to the ocean energy converter from ocean waves. The 
ocean energy converter analyzed in this study is the bottom mounted tidal current turbine 
corresponding to the one described by Tsai et al. (2011). A simplified model is shown in Figure 6. It 
is a horizontal axis type turbine. The main case of the energy converter has the size of 4m×2.5m. 
The diameter of the blade is 3.6m. The device is assumed to be installed at the locations with water 
depth of 5m and 10m. The computation conditions are: incident wave height 2m from direction –y 
to +y (see Fig. 6); wave period 6.25s; tidal current velocity 2m/s. Uniform current velocity and no 
wind condition are assumed. 
Figure 7 shows the pressure distribution from the joint effect of ocean wave and current. It was 
computed by the ANSYS AQWA model. In the left side of Figure 7 shows the pressure distribution 
of the device when the wave phase is 3π/2. In the right Figure 7 shows the pressure distribution 
when wave crest pass through the device. It was found the upper side and blades of the device 
receive main loading. The loading for the mounting mast is relative small. This is the result from 
action of uni-direction regular wave and uniform current. Figure 8 shows the time series of the 
loading of joint effect of wave and current when water depth is 10m. Compare to the unique action 
of waves as shown in Figure 9, it is found the frequency of loading changes.   
 
 4. CONCLUDING REMARKS 
This article is a preliminary study for the assessment of loading to the energy converter in the sea 
from natural factors. The natural factors considered in this study are the ocean wave and current. 
The type of ocean energy converter analyzed in this study is the bottom mounted tidal current 
turbine that simplified from the one described in Tsai et al. (2011).  
Typhoon induced large waves are the main forcing to the energy converters in the sea. It is found 
more than 10m significant wave height occurs commonly during typhoons. Frequency analysis can 
be applied to study the design wave height as the conditions for wave loading computation. In 
addition, freak wave that occur momentarily should be considered to assess the loading but it is still 
no regular algorithm for this assessment. ANSYS AWQA model was applied to compute the 
distribution of pressure of the bottom mounted device and the time series of the loading by different 
wave phases. It is found the upper side and blades of the tidal current turbine receive the strongest 
loading from the joint effects of waves and currents. The effect of current loading to the device 
changes the frequency of loads.

台讓各國研究生相互認識、交流。筆者與同系與會教師共邀集了 9位研究生或助理與會，其中
3 位進行了口頭論文發表，4 位以海報形式發表，他們的表現均相當優秀，不論在講台上或海
報前的講解，超過筆者的期待。看起來，我們的學生缺少的一個舞台，他們的潛力無限，只是
沒機會表現而已。未來應持續提供任何的機會給予同學，或許能激發他們的潛力，創造任何可
能的未來。 
本次研討會合計 50篇發表的論文中約有 15篇與海洋能源有關，這是相當高的比率，顯示
出海洋能源研究在此時的熱門程度，筆者與同系與會教師也在發表了三篇相關論文，均是國科
會能源國家型計畫的成果。筆者參與 10 月 7 日中午的國際委員會議，會中決定下屆研討會由
中國大陸青島海洋大學舉辦，而下下屆(2015年)在台灣舉辦，同時為凸顯海洋能源研究的重要，
與會委員通過將協會名稱增加 Energy 一字成為 East Asia Network for Marine and Energy 
Environments，簡稱改為 EAMEN2。 
三、建議 
1.   未來舉辦研討會建議以中小型為主，僅安排一個會場，讓與會者可以聽所有論文發表，若
能限制在某特定主題則更佳。 
2.   舉辦國際研討會增加國際能見度，也讓老師同學增加參與國際合作事務與國際交流經驗，
