2摘 要
基於籠狀高能材料 HNIW (Hexa-nitrohexaaza-iso-wurtzitane, CL-20) 的衍生物  HBIW (2,4,6,8,
10,12-hexabenzyl-2,4,6,8,10,12-hexaazatetracyclo[5. 5.0.05,9.03,11]dodecane) 具有特殊結構及優良放熱性
質，本計畫成功製備一系列類似於 HBIW 結構，而在 2,4,6,8,10,12 六個位置上芐基苯有不同取代基
的對稱型 (Symmetry-Type)、混合型 (Mixed-Type) 及非對稱型 (Asymmetry-Type) 三種不同型的籠狀
衍生物材料。首先利用乙二醛分別與芐胺、4-甲氧基芐胺、4-甲基芐胺、3,4-次甲二氧基芐胺、3,5-二
甲氧基芐胺、3-三氟甲基芐胺、2-氯基芐胺及 4-硝基芐胺等胺類化合物進行縮合反應，製備出含不同
取代而具對稱型之 HBIW、HMIW、HMOIW、HPIW、HDIW、HTIW、HCIW 及 HNBIW 胺類籠狀
衍生物材料，此系列化合物在 2,4,6,8,10,12-六個接氮的位置上分別引入推電子或拉電子的各式官能基，
分子具對稱結構。混合型籠狀衍生物材料的製備，則是利用芐胺與 3,4-次甲二氧基芐胺二種胺類同時
和乙二醛進行交叉偶合反應，製得 2,4,6,8,10,12-六個氮上具有不同取代基的胺類籠狀衍生物。另外，
以醋酸酐當醯化試劑，活化鈀/碳為催化劑進行氫化去苄基反應，用乙醯基取代對稱型籠狀物中 2, 6, 8,
12 位置上的苄基，而 4,10 位置上則仍保存著苄基，製備出 TADB 及 TADNB 非對稱型胺類籠狀衍
生物材料。經 DSC 及 TGA 分析儀測試，本實驗室所合成 HNIW 鈍感高能物最大吸熱溫度為
172.1oC，在 5oC升溫速率條件下，最大放熱溫度為 243.3oC；以每分鐘升溫 5oC 速率下加溫至 400oC
進行熱重損失分析，於 310.27 oC 剩 15.39 % 含量， HBIW 籠狀物亦具優良放熱性質，DSC分析儀
測得爆燃點為 156.99oC。

ABSTRACT
Cage-structure compound HBIW (2,4,6,8,10,12-hexabenzyl-2,4,6,8,10,12-hexaazatetracyclo[5.5.0. 05,9.
03,11]dodecane), derivative of high energy high density material HNIW (CL-20, Hexanitrohexaaza-iso-
wurtzitane), exists special structure and high energy according to the differential scanning calorimetry
analysis. This proposal have prepared the HBIW-like amine-containing cage-structure derivatives. Three types
of cage-structure derivatives are synthesized : (1) In the presence of a catalytic amount of formic acid,
condensation of a series of substituted benzylamines with glyoxal in acetonitrile achieves HBIW, HMIW,
HMOIW, HPIW, HDIW, HTIW, HCIW and HNBIW symmetry-type cage-structure derivatives. This kind of
compounds have put the same activated or deactivated function groups at the positions of 2,4,6,8,10,12 in the
cages. (2) Cross-Coupling reactions of mixture benzyl amines with glyoxal have also afforded mixed-type
cage compounds. (3) A debenzylation reduced reaction have carried out in the presence of acetic anhydride
and a catalytic amount of palladium/carbon to produce TADB and TADNB asymmetry-type cage compounds
which put the acetyl group at the 2, 6, 8, 12 positions of the nitrogen atoms but keep the benzyl group at 4 and
10 positions. The cage-structure derivatives such as HNBIW and TADNB which exist six, four and two
nitro-groups at 2,4,6,8,10,12 position provided higher density and oxidation balance. From Differential
Scanning Calorimetry, the maximum endothermic and exothermic temperatures for HNIW are 172.1oC and
243.3oC. The explosive point of HBIW is 156.99oC.
Keywords: High Energy Materials, Condensation, Cross-Coupling, Activated and Deactivated group,

五、2012年美國臨床化學年會發表論文摘要 
 
Synthesize the Precursors of Nitro-Containing HNIW Cage Compounds  
as Inhibitors of AchE and BchE 
 
Abstract 
In the presence of a catalytic amount of formic acid, treatment of benzylamine with glyoxal in 
acetonitrile achieves high yield of HBIW which is a precursor of the cage materials HNIW. Substituted 
benzylamines such as 2-chlorobenzylamine, 3,5-di- methoxylbenzylamine and (3,4-methylenedioxy)benzyl- 
amine also react with glyoxal to afford HCIW, HDIW and HPIW, respectively. These precursors were used as 
the inhibitors of AchE (Acetylcholinesterase) and BchE (Butyrylcholinesterase). This paper is study on the 
inhibitory mechanism of AChE and BChE by using cage compounds as reversible inhibitors. From the rate 
constant (k2), inhibitory constant (KI) and biomolecular rate constant (overall inhibitory constant ki), the 
results showed that the inhibitory potency for the inhibitor to AChE and BChE was in the order of HNIW＞
HCIW＞HDIW＞HPIW＞HBIW. This research also illustrated that the inhibitory potency of cage compounds 
to the BChE was better than that of AChE. 
Keywords: Cage Compounds, Inhibitory rate, Cholinesterase, Steric/Electronic effect.
