Keywords?Intrinsic Permittivity, Material Measurement, Substrate, Transmission Line 
 
An RF and microwave evaluation methodology for intrinsic permittivity of planar 
substrates is developed that is based on the TRL calibration method. Based on this 
methodology, the characteristic propagation constants (γ) of transmission lines are exactly 
extract out first, and then the intrinsic permittivities of substrates are recovered through the 
experimental formula gained from electromagnetic numerical analyses. A complete 
permittivity characterization system that includes the frequency-domain multi-line 
propagation-constant characterization program, GPIB control program and facilities, 
network analyzer, and transmission-line fixture is developed. The effective permittivity is 
recovered from a characterization procedure of propagation constant γ of TEM or 
Quasi-TEM transmission line that makes this methodology have the advantages of wide 
bandwidth and conforming to the real application scenario. The experimental results show 
that this method can exactly characterize the intrinsic permittivities of substrates.
