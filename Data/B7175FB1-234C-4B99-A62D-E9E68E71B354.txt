英文摘要 
 
 
In the first year, radio frequency (R.F.) sputtering technology is presented for 
fabricating thin film of ruthenium dioxide (RuO2) based on seperative extended gate 
field effect transistor (SEGFET) in this study. This structure can be simulated as 
sensing electrode of semiconductor process by means of I-V characteristic curve. 
How to fabricate a good sensing film based on RuO2 is studied by optimizing R.F. 
sputtering process in the low pressure. The sensing membrane based on RuO2 has pH 
sensitivities ranging from 54 mV/pH to 58 mV/pH and good linear response in pH 
buffer solutions ranging from pH 1 to pH 13 at room temperature. By a series of 
measurements with Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM), Electron Spectroscope for 
Chemical Analysis (ESCA), Energy Dispersive Spectrometer (EDS) and X-Ray 
Diffraction (XRD), the compositions and structures of thin films can be observed. 
Moreover, Cyclic Voltammetry (CV) is employed to analyze the basic characteristic 
and electrochemical mechanism of the electrode. Sensitivity is a main factor for 
detecting the quality, and the stability of the membrane. In accordance with the 
above-mentioned, the RuO2 pH-sensing electrode is measured for drift effect and 
hysteresis effect, the characteristics of the electrodes can be obtained. 
In the second year, that adopts the RuO2 thin film was used as the substrate based 
on the penicillin enzymatically enzyme electrode, which has the characteristics of 
cheaper price and the simple fabrication. And the ruthenium dioxide (RuO2) thin film 
was prepared by means of R.F. sputtering system. The enzyme electrode can be 
developed towards as the disposable type sensor, so that can have the time-limited 
efficiency of the clinical medical treatment immediately. The enzyme penicillinase 
was immobilized directly onto the surface of the RuO2 bare electrode by 
3-glycidoxypropyltrime- thoxysilane (GPTS). Some characteristics of the biosensor 
such as sensitivity, linear range, hysteresis and drift, were also investigated. The 
achievements in the fabrication and characterization of the ruthenium oxide and 
enzymatically modified electrode with penicillinase are described in detail. 
In the third year, we designed a miniaturization with accurate front-end ion 
sensor by using the VLSI technology to improve the traditional readout circuit. The 
characteristics of sensing device were based on the experimental data. In the section 
of forward analog detect circuit was designed by using VLSI, and the single chip 
microprocessor was used as signal processor compensation circuit for ion sensor. A/D 
converter was used to convert analog signal to digital signal for the ion sensor.

4.1 Characterization of the RuO2 electrode 
After the electrode was completed, the electrode was tested to ensure whether 
having good electrical conductivity for detecting the substance further. The electrode 
was placed in an electrolyte solution to measure the redox reaction by employing 
cyclic voltammetric (CV). The electrolyte containing electron mediator (K3[Fe(CN)6]) 
would undergo the redox reaction. When the electron mediator was added with the 
various concentrations, the current response of electrode increases with the increasing 
concentration. For the CV measurement, the potential was swept from -0.6 to 0.2V 
with a rate of 25 mV sec-1. The RuO2 bare electrode has a reversible cyclic 
voltammogram, the two peaks which indicating the redox couple can be observed. 
The results demonstrate that the sputtered RuO2 electrode possesses the performances 
for the electrical conductivity and good linearity. Figure. 7 shows the cyclic 
voltammogram of the RuO2 bare electrode. The diffusion of hydrogen ion in RuO2 
thin film is of significant interest because of its importance in capacitor, sensor and 
catalyst applications. In electrochemical permeation method, hydrogen is generated 
by hydrogen ion reduction on the cathodic side of the membrane either at a constant 
potential or at constant current. Hydrogen then diffuses through the membrane, until it 
reaches the anodic side of the membrane where it is oxidized under potentiostatic 
control. The output signal is the transient oxidation current detected at the anodic side 
[52]. The results demonstrate that the sputtered RuO2 electrode possesses the 
performances for the electrical conductivity and good linear. The voltammetric 
behavior is shown in Fig. 7.

4.3 Hysteresis and drift effects 
From Fig. 14, the hysteresis width with sensing area of 1 mm2 was larger than 
other sensing areas. Moreover, the hysteresis width decreases as the sensing area 
increases. Bousse et al. [57] expressed that the hysteresis is considerably less when 
the acid excursion is done last. For the sensing area of 4 mm2, the hysteresis width of 
pH 7-3-7-11-7 and pH 7-11-7-3-7 cycles are 7.78 mV and 1.93 mV, respectively. 
The drift experiment for the bare RuO2 electrode starts in the initial solution (pH 
7) for 30 minutes, and then the drift of the pH-response is produced in the test 
solution (pH 8) by a long-term measurement, and the drift value was calculated from 
5 to 12 hours. According to the literatures [58-60], based on the simulation of the 
time-constant model, we can extract the time constant and amplitude from the 
response curve of the drift. The experimental results show that three time constants 
are 892 sec, 1795 sec, 15220 sec and magnitudes of three amplitudes are 0.0096,

抗黴生物藥劑，其作用方式係抑制細胞壁之合成。由於
青黴素結合蛋白質（Penicillin Binding Protein, PBP）係細

將導致青黴素結合蛋白質無法供給細胞壁合成時所需之
蛋白質，即細胞壁遭受破壞，無法合成正常細胞壁，最

測場效電晶體較離子感測場效電晶體具有下列優點：(1)
