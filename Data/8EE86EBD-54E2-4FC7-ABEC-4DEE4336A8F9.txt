第 2章 研究目的 
近幾年，行動裝置普及，已到達人手一機或是一人數機的狀態，人們的生活已與行
動裝置息息相關。行動手持裝置除提供一般的功能外，也多了許多應用與趣味性。影
音功能，現在已是行動裝置的基本設備了。多數廠商所生產的行動裝置將影音功能列
為不可或缺的功能；消費者在選擇行動裝置上也會考量到影音功能。因此多數人都使
用過手持裝置上的影音功能，來播放多媒體的資訊、音樂與影片。 
由於多數的行動裝置限制在電池容量的大小，較大容量的電池能提供較長的使用時
間，但是卻不適合攜帶。因此，如何在有限的電池之下達到最長時間的使用效率是很
重要的議題。 
目前，亦有許多學者，針對環保節能方面，發表多篇論文。近年來，由於環保意識
高漲，且綠色能源議題需要在開源與節流的方式並進，因此，我們針對在行動裝置上
作節能的分析，相信可以提供開發的廠商，或是廣大的使用者，一個較為適切可行的
方式去使用行動手持裝置，提升電池電力使用效能。 
本次的計畫，在行動手持裝置，包括廠商優化後的產品(PDA)與開發平台上做電力
測試實驗。希望能利用實驗的結果，以客觀的方式提供行動裝置的使用者與製造廠
商，一個提升電力使用效能的方式，使得行動手持裝置的使用時間能夠相對的延長。

3. OGG： 
OGG 是一個完全開放性的多媒體系統計劃的名稱，也是 OGG Vorbis 文件的擴展名。
OGG Vorbis 是一種類似於 Mp3 的有損音頻壓縮格式，但是它自由（免費、無專利限
制）且開放原始碼。Vorbis 為此種音頻壓縮格式的名稱。目前 OGG 計劃只實現了 Vorbis
的部分。OGG Vorbis 格式非常先進，雖然 Vorbis 也是有損壓縮，但是由於其使用了
更加先進的聲學模型，同樣 Bit rate (比特率)下的 OGG 文件比 Mp3 文件聽起來更好一
些。事實上，一些評測結果顯示，在 80k-256k 的 Bit rate 下 OGG 格式呈現出該 Bit rate
範圍下的相對於其他音頻格式（mp3pro,wma,aac）更高的保真度 (fidelity)。

MPEG-4 是一套用於音頻、視頻信息的壓縮編碼標準，由國際標準化組織 IEC 活動圖
像專家組（即 MPEG）制定，第一版在 1998 年 10 月通過，第二版在 1999 年 12 月通

MPEG-4 包含了 MPEG-1 及 MPEG-2 的絕大部份功能及其他格式的長處，並加入

2   Background 
We briefly introduce the relevant encoders and the stream server in our experiment in 
the following. 
2.1   MEPG-4 
MPEG-4 is an encoding standard used primarily to compress audio and visual digital 
data. The uses for the MPEG-4 standard are webs (streaming media), CD distribu-
tions, conversations (video phones), and broadcast televisions, all of them benefit 
from compressing the audio and visual stream. 
MPEG-4 absorbs many of the features of MPEG-1 and MPEG-2 and other related 
standards, adds new features such as the extended VRML support for 3D rendering, 
object-oriented composite files (including audio, video and VRML objects), supports 
for externally-specified Digital Rights Management and various types of interactivity. 
Most of the features included in MPEG-4 are left to individual developers to de-
cide whether to implement them. To deal with this, the standard includes the concept 
of "profiles" and "levels", allowing a specific set of capabilities to be defined in a 
manner appropriate for a subset of applications [6], [7], [8]. In this paper, the MPEG-
4 codec, MS mpeg4 v2 (Microsoft MPEG4 version 2) is developed by Microsoft. 
2.2   WMV 
Windows Media Video (WMV) is a generic name for the set of video codec tech-
nologies developed by Microsoft. WMV competes with other codec such as 
RealVideo, DivX, Xvid, and H.264, etc. Generally speaking, common usage of the 
term WMV refers to this codec only. WMV supports variable bit rate, average bit 
rate, and constant bit rate encoding. 
Although WMV is generally packed into an Advanced Systems Format (ASF) con-
tainer format, it can also be put into AVI or Matroska containers. When encapsulated 
in ASF file format, WMV can be supported and managed tools with several rights that 
are use for protecting the intellectual property right [9]. In the experiment, we adopt 
WMV 7 and WMV 8. 
2.3   MMS 
Microsoft's streaming server Microsoft Media Services (previously called NetShow 
Services) uses the Microsoft Media Server (MMS) protocol to transfer unicast data. 
MMS can be transported via UDP or TCP. The MMS default port is UDP/TCP 1755.

5   Conclusions 
In this paper, we use various codecs to encode films and adjust the encoding parame-
ters for video streaming. We replay the video files on the mobile device to analyze 
energy consumption to find out the most suitable energy-conserving way to encode 
video files for replaying on the mobile device. 
From the experimental results, we have following conclusions.  
Films encoded by using MS mpeg4 v2 and WMV1 codecs consumes less energy 
than films encoded by using WMV2 and WMV3 codecs. 
As for the file format, the results of experiment 2 demonstrate that it does not have 
obvious effect on energy consumption. Films encoded by the same codec but with 
different file format consume similar amount of energy. 
As for the parameters of codecs, the results of experiment 3 show that with similar 
amount of energy consumption, films encoded with higher bit rates and lower resolution 
have better image quality than films encoded with higher resolution and lower bit rates. 
In experiment 4, we find that when the bit rate of the film is higher, “Play as re-
ceived” mode is more energy efficient; and when the bit rate is lower, “Play after 
download” mode consumes less energy. So, when play back video streaming, you 
should select the playing mode according to the bit rate of the film.
