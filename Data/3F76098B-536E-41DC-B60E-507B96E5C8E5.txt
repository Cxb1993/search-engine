The Design and Numerical 
Simulation of the Relief Groove of the 
External Gear Pump 
Yu-Ren Wu
1
 and Shi-Ying Huang
1
 
Department of Mechanical Engineering,

NSC Project No.: NSC 98-2218-E-020-002 
Abstract  
The external gear pump commonly comprises a 
pair of conjugate involute gears meshing to each other 
around the respective axis. However, the compressed 
fluid enveloping within the housing and tooth surfaces 
may cause pitting on tooth surfaces and hydrodynamic 
noise at the discharge end. Therefore, the tooth profile 
design of the asymmetric involute gear is proposed in 
this paper to apply to calculate the pump flowrate. The 
design of the relief groove on the discharge casing plate 
is also proposed and used to reduce the discharge 
pressure. A mathematical model based on the laws of 
mass, momentum and energy conservation has been 
established to verify the validity of the design of the 
relief groove. 
Keywords: Gear pump; Relief groove; Involute spur 
gear
