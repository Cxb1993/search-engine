Abstract 
 
(Key Words: Digital Life, Digital Home, Home Network, Intelligent Services, Cognitive Computing, 
Affective Computing, Human-Machine Interaction) 
Digital life has been a hot IT research topic in both industry and academy. It associates with 
several implications: 
1. The service target of IT technology has been expanded from professionals to all people. 
2. The operational manner of IT systems should be changed to a more humanized style. 
3. Besides the development of unit core technology, the researches of IT technology are 
exploring to the multi-disciplinary integration due to the getting richer and plural 
application scenarios. 
4. IT technology is being applied to the innovative services that ask for multi-disciplinary 
know-how integration. 
This project is based on the research achievements in the CREDIT Center of NCKU by the 
same team to continue the development of integrated digital life technology. This project is expected 
to make valuable contribution for the academic and industrial sectors. In order to concentrate the 
research scope, the target of this project is focused on the development of digital home technology, 
which is the most important segment of digital life. The research goals of this project include  
1. The middleware of home service network based on semantic web technology (integration 
of intelligent agent and web service). 
2. The reconfigurable home service network structure based on the integration of wireless 
sensor network and wireless home backbone network. 
3. Integrated affective human-machine interaction based on the smart home service network. 
The research topics of innovative home service include 
1. Predictive user life activity support. 
2. Life and mind cares of elders who live alone.

1. 結合智慧代理人及網絡服務的智慧型家庭服務網路中介軟體成果 
A、 發展服務代理人平台與 OSGi 的整合架構用以協助開發情境感知服務 
發展情境感知服務時，本計畫期望達到下列兩個主要目的：(a)提供個人化的情境
感知服務(b)整合異質性家庭網路，達到服務自動化。為了達到這兩個目標，我們利用
服務代理人平台與 OSGi 的特性，來協助我們達到上述兩個目標，服務代理人平台將負
責服務個人化的目標，利用代理人的特性，滿足不同使用者對於服務喜好的需求，另一
方面，透過 OSGi 整合目前常見的家庭網路溝通協定，用以整合異質性家庭網路，並協
助達到服務自動化的目的。 
Agent Platform 
為有效提升各種不同作業系統內裝置設備的系統效能，目前大多採用代理人平台技
術在各主要的設備或網路節點等重要資料或作業的執行處，配置行動代理人（Mobile 
Agent）。行動代理人本身為程式，可移動於各裝置設備之間，並將使用者的請求透過裝
置設備間的相互合作來完成，最後將執行結果回覆給使用者。除此之外，行動代理人具
有自主分析能力，即使各裝置設備並未提供使用者互動介面，行動代理人依舊能收集各
裝置設備的執行狀態，自行調整適當的程式執行方式，藉此提升系統的執行效能，與提
供個人化需求。 
OSGi Framework 
開放式服務平台（Open Services Gatway Initiative，OSGi）之協定係由 OSGi 協會所
制定，主要是提供一個開放式的平台介面，主要由一服務閘道器與一個以上的設備組成。
服務閘道器內儲存有數個服務包，每一服務包皆包含有設備使用的設定參數或執行的程
式，服務包僅能由服務閘道器執行。使用者僅需透過服務閘道器啟動不同的服務包，以
控制各設備的執行狀態。同時，使用者可根據所需從廠商下載不同的服務包，並載入服
務閘道器。 
為了能提供自動化服務的機制，所以我們採用 OSGi(Open Source Gateway Initiative)

息告知。當 OSGi 架構接到服務執行需求，OSGi 將即時觸發 service bundle 去提供服務。

A、 整合性情意感知之情緒辨識混和專家模型 
人機互動是發展數位家庭 ubiquitous services 的核心議題之一。而人機互動有多種
模態可以實現，但各自有其適用之情境及限制。觀之人類與環境的互動，其實常借助多
模態(multi-modal)資訊的整合。近年來，許多研究藉由視覺、語音、生理訊號與 RFID
來辨識人類的動作語意。但資訊來源的豐富與龐大的資訊量，資料如何有效的萃取(Data 
Extraction)與融合(Data Fusion)已成為實現多模態人機互動重要的課題。 
 過去大部分語意辨識的研究，主要針對如何利用單一資訊建立一個準確的單一模型。
根據這些分類方法，其提升辨識率仍然是有限的。人類可透過多管道去認知某人之語意。
一些研究指出可以合併多個不同資訊所建立的模型以達到更佳的辨識率。然而，合併的
規則依應用層面應有不一的表現。許多研究者提出一些合併多模型的方法，如階層式合
併規則、模糊隸屬函數規則與人為定義每一模型的可參考機率值，但這些方法依演算法
或應用導向的不同，無法適用所有應用，或是需要有經驗地調整這些參數與規則。一個
通用的混和模型，能過濾出重要資訊，並且整合不同專家的貢獻是有其必要性的。

4 Conclusions and Future Works 
We have proposed a group-oriented and hybrid 
approach for key tree management, called as HFGOT. 
HFGOT is developed on both TGDH and DH 
algorithms and efficiently manage the group key tree. 
To improve a great deal of exponentiation 
computation of TGDH, the proposed HFGOT can 
reduce the computation cost of join operation, leave 
operation. From the simulation results and 
mathematic derivation, the proposed HFGOT has two 
advantages: (1) if group has more members and (2) if 
the group characteristic is stronger, the computation 
cost of re-key is less.

Abstract—In smart living spaces, a reliable context-aware 
application must adapt to the variable environment and should 
function to complete the user’s requirements. Therefore, if a 
context-aware application is to achieve the user’s requirements 
smoothly, it must understand the environment status according 
to trustworthy context information. However, the context 
information influenced by the environment status may be 
imperfect. If a context-aware application responds to the user’s 
requirements using the incorrect context information, this will 
lead to undesirable result. To resolve these problems, this paper 
proposes a Context Model (CM) to evaluate the reliability of 
context information and predicted the context information that 
context-aware application needs. When CM approves of the 
context information, it considers both the key features of context 
information, and also the different requirements of the context-
aware application. Finally, an experiment is proposed to verify 
the reliability of CM. The experiment verifies that the reliability 
of context information recommended by CM can represent the 
real status. In addition, the experiment verifies whether CM can 
provide appropriate context information according to the 
particular requirements of a context-aware application. 
Keywords—Smart living space, Context Model, actuator, 
sensor, context-aware application

context-aware applications can receive two kinds of context
