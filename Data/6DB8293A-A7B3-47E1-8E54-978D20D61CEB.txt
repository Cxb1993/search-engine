中文摘要 
鉍鐵氧化物(BFO)與鈦酸鍶(STO)超晶格磊晶薄膜藉由射頻磁控濺鍍系統
膜成功鍍著於 (001)單晶鈦酸鍶基板上。控制鉍鐵氧化物以及鈦酸鍶層之厚
度，我們得以將不同的應變量引入超晶格結構之內，並探討應變對於結晶結構
之影響。應用高解析 X光繞射(XRD)圖譜以及 X光反射(XRR)技術我們可以得
知超晶格結晶之微結構。X光反射(XRR)實驗結果顯示，鉍鐵氧化物/鈦酸鍶超
晶格結構之介面以及表面粗操度均會隨著週期增加而提高，此一結果與原子力
顯微鏡觀察結果相吻合。超晶格結構磊晶品質則可藉由 azimuthal (111) 方向 
phi scan量測。由 phi scan結果可以明顯觀察到超晶格結構呈現四軸對稱的結
晶分佈，證明超晶格結構具有優良的磊晶品質與對稱性。超晶格結構中 BFO
以及 STO 層結晶結構以及應力狀態則可藉由 in-plane[2 0 0] 以及
out-of-plane[0 0 2]方向之結晶柱(CTR)圖譜分析。CTR 圖譜顯示隨著 BFO及
STO層的厚度增加，超晶格結構呈現部份應力釋放的現象。 
關鍵詞：超晶格結構 射頻磁控濺鍍 鉍鐵氧化物。

I. 緣由 
鐵電薄膜材料因其具有特殊之自發極化與遲滯特性，而產生良好的介電、焦電、壓電、
電光等特性，且因半導體技術的快速發展以及薄膜製作技術的日漸成熟，使得鐵電薄膜能
製造出符合現代科技要求的元件，成為現今電子陶瓷材料發展之主流。鐵電薄膜的應用範
圍十分廣泛，可用於記憶體元件儲存電荷、紅外線感測元件、壓力感測元件、加速儀、微
馬達、微泵光閘元件、光導電記憶體顯示器、動態隨機存取記憶體(dynamic random access 
memory, DRAM)以及非揮發性記憶體(non-volatile memory)等應用[1-6]。因此，鐵電薄膜材料
與製程之開發也就成為現今最熱門的智能材料研發領域。 
在諸多的鐵電薄膜應用中，研究最多、成果最豐碩、而且最具市場潛力者，當屬非揮
發性之鐵電記憶體(FeRAM)[7-13]。鐵電記憶體需要擁有大的殘留極化值(Pr, remanent 
polarization)，如此才能於小電容面積及低矯頑電場(coercive field)的低電壓操作環境下使
用。目前較引人矚目者有 PbZrxTi1-xO3(PZT)、SrBi2TaO9(SBT)和(Bi,La)4Ti3O12(BIT)等三種
材料，似乎勉強可以滿足這種要求[14,15]。這三種材料中以 PZT 之殘留極化值為最高
(Pr~30µC/cm2)，BIT 次之(Pr~20µC/cm2)，SBT 最低(Pr~10µC/cm2)[14]。在這些鐵電記憶體
材料的研究當中，因鉛系之 PZT其 Pr最高，故最廣為大眾所探討。但因為鉛系的 PZT鐵
電薄膜含有鉛，在這講求環保無污染的二十一世紀裡，這又面臨一個大問題，因此現在各
方正積極尋找替代的材料。在目前所尋找的替代材料之中，以 BiFeO3 (BFO)的發現最被為
看好，研究最多。因為 BFO同時擁有鐵電性(ferroelectric)與反鐵磁特性(antiferromagnetic)，
係為一種多鐵性(multiferroic)材料[15-18]。自從 J. Wang[16]等人於 2003年在自然雜誌上發表
利用 PLD方式成長 BFO磊晶薄膜於 SrTiO3單晶基板上，發現厚度 200-nm的 BFO薄膜在
頻率 15 KHz下其 Pr值高達~ 55 µC/cm2，這個值遠高於塊材(Bulk)的最大值~6.1 µC/cm2
甚多，因此引起廣大研究者的投入[15, 18-24]，研究成果也相當豐碩，對於 BFO材料有更進
一步的認識。在所有發表文獻中，BFO的 Pr值最高可達 150µC/cm2 [20]，而且有相當低的
結晶溫度(<550℃)，可謂應用於鐵電薄膜的記憶體之潛力無窮。但目前 BFO還有一些困難
尚待解決，因為它的漏電流太大，還無法實際應用於記憶體上。一般認為，它的漏電流乃
由於 Bi 具有很高的揮發性，以致於在製作過程中會產生很多氧空缺，所以造成漏電流特
別大。欲解決此一問題，有研究者利用添加稀土金屬元素(Rare earth elements如 Nd、La)
來部份取代 Bi 的位置，減少它的氧空缺產生，進而降低漏電流，截至目前為止，這方面
的研究正如火如荼的進行著，雖有些進展但仍屬有限[25-31]。 
若吾人可以改從另一個方向來思考，即利用前述之人工超晶格的觀念。如果使用 BFO
與高介電材料，如 SrTiO3(STO)、BaTiO3(BTO)結合在一起做成超晶格的薄膜，此概念乃是

much attention.  Wang et al.[16] showed that epitaxial BiFeO3 (BFO) thin films on single-crystal 
SrTiO3 (STO) possess highly saturated ferroelectric hysteresis and great resistivity.  Epitaxial 
BFO thin films show a greater Pr value than bulk BFO material, but serious problems with BFO 
including a large leakage current and ferroelectric unreliability arise because of a deviation from 
oxygen stoichiometry, which results in an increase of free charge carriers.  Besides, defects such 
as Bi vacancies result from its substantial volatility.  An increased coercive field (Ec) and 
improved reliability are critical challenges for BiFeO3-based films as integrated microelectronic 
devices 
Several methods have been discussed to improve the performance of BFO films.  One 
approach has focused on the substitution of minor elements, for example, solid solutions of BFO 
with other perovskite materials[45-46] or substitution[47–50] of Bi and Fe elements in BFO films.  
The perovskite-type material with a tetragonal crystal structure has a larger c axial lattice 
parameter, and deviations of cations at the octahedral interstitial site induce ferroelectric 
properties.  Controlling the composition of films is one method to control the c/a ratio.  The 
cation and oxygen stoichiometry is the main factor to adjust the a- and c- axial lattice parameters, 
and an epitaxial artificial superlattice is another way to enhance the c/a ratio of films.  When 
material layers of perovskite type having disparate lattice parameters are combined with each 
other, a mismatch between the in-plane lattice parameters occurs; strain from lattice distortion is 
thus introduced into the artificial superlattices.  Recently, Bose et al., [51], prepared symmetric 
BFO and STO superlattices by pulsed laser deposition (PLD) to improve the ferroelectric, 
leakage properties of the BFO film.  The dielectric constant is also sensitive to the stacking 
periodicity.  Previous authors [52-55] have shown that a BaTiO3/SrTiO3 (BTO/STO) artificial 
superlattice has dielectric properties superior to those of single-layer BTO, STO films.

be scaled into large industrial coater production on a commercial scale [56].  RF sputtering 
technology is convenient for the preparation of oxide films.  Our objective in this work is to

3. Results and discussion 
3.1 Composition  
XPS spectra of Bi-4f, Fe-2p and O-1s in a BFO single-layer thin film are shown in Fig. 2. 
Two major groups of features occur in the Bi-4f spectra, due to Bi-4f5/2 and Bi-4f7/2 at binding 
energies 164.1 and 158.8 eV in the group of Bi-4f5/2 and at 162.3 and 157 eV in the group of 
Bi-4f7/2.  The Fe XPS spectra also comprise two peaks fitted at 710.9 eV of 2p3/2 and 724.4 eV 
of 2p5/2.  The atomic ratios of BFO films were determined on calculating the ratio of integrated 
areas of the features in the XPS spectra.  The atomic ratio in the BFO thin film was Bi: Fe: O = 
1.92: 2.1: 5.98, which approaches the stoichiometric ratio of the theoretical BFO structure.

Figure 6 (a) shows crystal truncation-rod (CTR) spectra along the [H 0 0 ] direction of the 
superlattice films having varied stacking periodicity.  For simplicity, values of H, K and L given

directly the in-plane strain effect between that film and the substrate.  Strain relief with 
increasing sublayer thickness was an observed effect.  The superlattice film with the smallest 
sublayer thickness has the smallest deviation between the diffraction peak of the BFO/STO 
superlattice film and the STO substrate.  With increased sublayer thickness, strain relief 
occurred, and separate diffraction peaks of the film and the substrate appeared. 
Figure 6(b) presents (0 0 L) CTR intensity profiles of superlattice films with sublayer 
thickness varied in a range 2 – 10 nm, which shows that the position of the peak shifted toward a 
smaller L-index with decreasing sublayer thickness, and the gap between BFO and STO substrate 
diffraction peaks decreased from 0.084 to 0.04.  These effects resulted directly from the 
elongation of the BFO lattice along the [0 0 L] direction due to the heteroepitaxial strain. 
4. Conclusions 
BFO/STO superlattice thin films with varied stacking periods were deposited on a (001) 
single-crystal STO substrate with a RF magnetron sputtering system.  XPS spectra of BFO films 
show that these films possess a composition ratio similar to that of the theoretical BFO structure.

IV. 檢討及展望 
本計畫最關鍵的部分為人工超晶格型薄膜之研製，目前均已一一克服困難，並已初步
達成計畫需求。本計畫施行至今，顯示利用便宜的 RF濺鍍方式也可以達到與 PLD或MBE
等得到相同品質之磊晶結構，至此已證實本計畫之可行，也建立本實驗室執行本計畫之能
力。而且 RF 濺鍍法為工業上常用之基本製程，因此本研究非常有實際應用價值，展望未
來，吾人相信對於後續之臨場同步輻射 X光實驗及其分析工作必能順利進行，確信在現有
基礎上，再加努力，定能達成計畫目標。吾人深信經由本計畫之執行，必能培養國內在鐵
電薄膜元件製作工程之技術人才，進而開發新一代可應用於鐵電記憶體的材料，相信本計
畫研發成果，對於國家科技能力之提升，將有直接的貢獻。 
V. 成果自評 
綜上所述，經由本計畫之推動，已成功地濺鍍出高品質的 BFO/STO人工超晶格型多鐵
性氧化物薄膜於 SrTiO3 (STO)基板上，經利用高解析度之同步輻射 X光檢測，發現確實具
有非常完美的超晶格結構，其磊晶結構係控制在原子層級。本計畫施行至今，顯示利用便
宜的 RF濺鍍方式也可以達到與 PLD或MBE等得到相同品質之磊晶結構，而且 RF濺鍍為
工業上常用之基本製程，因此本研究非常有實際應用價值。至此已證實本計畫之可行，也
建立本實驗室實行本計畫之能力。
