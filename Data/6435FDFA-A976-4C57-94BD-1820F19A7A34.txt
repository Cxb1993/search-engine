摘要 
在台灣水庫淤泥大部分仍儲存於水庫附近，而水庫空地已漸無法容納日益增加之水庫淤泥。水庫
淤泥其去處，在廢棄土所造成環境汙染之問題尚未解決之前，實無法得到解決。此外，由於國內砂石
資源日益枯竭，而採集河砂又受到法令所嚴禁。因此利用水庫淤泥用以製造砂石之替代品乃為解決水
庫淤泥去處之最好方法。 
本研究主要目的在於利用水庫淤泥製造高強度土壤材料，並應用於工程實務。利用由石門水庫所
採取之淤泥、爐石粉及砂土為材料，並依其不同配比，利用鹼激發劑聚合原理開發高強度生態土壤材
料，來進行工作性試驗、單軸抗壓強度試驗、抗彎試驗、衝擊試驗、水密性試驗、耐磨試驗、孔隙率
試驗、耐久性試驗、快速氯離子電滲試驗、X-Ray 測其化學性質、SEM 試驗等去探討外部力學性質與
內部微觀結構。研究結果預期可提供解決水庫淤泥去處方法，並探討高強度土壤材料之生產流程及相
關力學性質，以供工程界設計及施工參考。

3.3 高強度生態土壤材料 
傳統黏結材料的生產過程和資源問題的惡化，促使人們迫切需要尋找有效的生態材料以平衡自然
資源的開發和生存環境之間的關係。2004 年，Chen (2003) [9]所發展之高強度生態土壤材料（High 
Strength Ecological Soil Material, 簡稱 HSESM）即有著低耗能和材料可再循環利用的新世代材料。 
高強度生態土壤材料的生產方法，可分為下列四個主要步驟，如圖 1 所示： 
1. 將粘土礦物加熱至 700 ℃持續 4 個小時，將粘土礦物去氫鍵並使土壤處於介穩定狀態。 
2. 依粘土礦物 SiO2 及 Al2O3 成分高低，決定添加天然礦物飛灰、爐石或其他活性礦物，通常高嶺土
之 SiO2 及 Al2O3 含量較高可不用再添加天然礦物，但伊利土及蒙脫土因 Al2O3 之含量較低約 10
～16％，所以必須添加天然礦物飛灰或爐石作補充。 
3. 使用強鹼(如 NaOH)解聚 Si 及 Al 單體。 
4. 使用聚合物(如 Na2SiO3)將解聚後之 Si 及 Al 單體與氧原子重新聚合成新的聚合物。 
 
整理文獻[9]其主要研究結果為：高強度生態土壤材料之 7 天抗壓強度約為 28 天之 80%,且其強度
發展於 28 天後已達穩定；若依據 ASTM C 1202-97 所建議通過高強度生態土壤材料的電滲量來評估其
抗滲透性能力，則其抗氯離子滲透性能力等級分類為中等級；高強度生態土壤材料之孔隙率大約 30～
40 %左右，乾密度為 1.56～1.65 g/cm3，面乾飽和密度為 1.76～1.94 g/cm3，所以高強度生態土壤材料
屬於輕質多孔材料，這與此材料生成類似沸石結構的組合率有關。 
3.4 台灣發展土壤聚合物材料之問題 
由於 Davidovits 教授利用高嶺土成功的開發土壤聚合物材料之偉大貢獻，所以如何利用一
般黏土質土壤，以開發具有類似土壤聚合物材料功能之研究，均受到全世界各國之重視。由文獻
[5] [9]中得知，一般黏土質土壤處理成土壤聚合物材料大致可分為 2 類，第一類為以高嶺土為基
材；第二類為非以高嶺土為基材之土壤聚合物： 
1. 以高嶺土為主[5]，因其 SiO2之含量約 50~58 %，而Al2O3之含量約 35~45 % 左右，即SiO2 + Al2O3
大於 85 % 以上。所以使用 750 0C 以上煅燒 4～6 個小時，可將黏土中連結之氫鍵破壞呈不穩定
狀態，此時稱為蒙特高嶺土(metakaonlin)，再將其加入砂、石與鹼激發劑，可成為一高強度之土
壤聚合物。 
2. 非高嶺土之其餘黏土質土壤中[9]，因其 SiO2 之含量約 50~58 %，而 Al2O3 之含量僅約 10~16 % 左
右，因其 Al2O3 之含量不足，所以必須增加爐石、飛灰等工業礦渣以補充黏土質土壤中 Al2O3
含量，再與砂、石、鹼激發劑結合，形成土壤聚合物材料。

依據不同性質與行為，對試體進行各項試驗。本研究透過土壤基本性質試驗、初終凝試驗與坍流

4.2.1 試驗規劃 
1. 拌合階段 
在拌合階段是高強度土壤材料至為重要一環，土壤材料硬固後之性質，如強度、抗張力、滲透性、
耐久性等，均取決於配比條件，不同的配比下，其新拌性質也會產生不同反應。 
2. 強度方面 
在各種不同配比下所製造的高強度土壤材料試體，在第 1 天、第 7 天、第 28 天、第 56 天時進行單
軸抗壓強度試驗，比較土壤材料在不同配比下，隨著時間改變的單壓強度；在第 7 天、第 28 天時進
行抗彎強度試驗、水密性試驗、衝擊試驗、耐磨試驗、快速氯離子滲透試驗，比較土壤材料在不同
配比下，隨著時間改變之抗張力、滲透性、耐衝擊性、磨損抵抗的性質變化。單壓強度試體之尺寸
為 Φ5 cm × 10 cm 之圓柱試體，齡期為 1 天、7 天試驗者，採 2 組試體取平均，齡期為 28 天與 56 天
者，採 3 組試體取平均。抗彎強度、水密性試驗、衝擊試驗、耐磨試驗、快速氯離子滲透試驗，其
齡期為 7 天者，採 2 組試體取平均，齡期為 28 天者，採 3 組試體取平均。孔隙率試驗齡期為 7 天、
28 天與 56 天均採 3 組試體取平均。抗彎強度之試體尺寸為 4 cm× 4 cm × 16 cm，水密性之試體尺寸

12. WD-XRF 波長分散式-X 光螢光分析儀 
波長分散式-X 光螢光分析儀(Wavelength-Dispersive X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometry，WD-XRF 螢
光試驗機)在實驗中是用來檢驗粉體之內含氧化物成分與含量。由 X 射線管陰極產生之高速電子．
經高電壓加速衝擊金屬靶時，一部份電子放出能量而產生具有各種連續波長之 X 射線。當此一次
X 射線照射試樣所發生之螢光 X 射線（亦稱二次 X 射線）通過平行光管為平行光線後，照射一已
知距離(d)之分光結晶，依 Bragg 定理（nλ=2dsinθ）而分光，再經由檢波器及波高分析器選別所需
之能量並測定其強度。由於特性 X 射線強度與試樣中所含成份元素之量成正比，故可由測定某一
成份元素特性 X 射線之相對強度得知該成份元素之相對含量。

ABSTRACT   
 
Monopiles are used as foundation structures for offshore wind energy 
towers, which are open-ended steel pipe piles with large diameters. For 
a pile foundation, the scour caused by wave and current lead to the 
reduction of embedded pile length and may decrease the lateral 
resistance. In the practical design different requirements regarding the 
minimum embedded pile length are used to limit the pile deflection at 
the mudline and at the pile toe under static and in particular cyclic loads 
due to wind and wave. The “vertical-tangent” and “zero-toe-kick” 
criteria are usually used to guarantee the lateral stability of monopiles 
and to insure piles are entered deep enough into the ground. In this 
study, the different design criteria are compared with respect to 
monopiles of scour. By means of the p-y curves method the pile 
deformation under lateral load are quantified and different criteria are 
assessed. The minimum required embedded lengths of monopile 
foundations with respect to scour are evaluated and the suitability of 
adopting the mentioned design criteria for monopile foundations is 
discussed. 
 
KEY WORDS:  monopile, offshore wind energy; scour; embedded 
length 
 
INTRODUCTION 
 
Monopile foundations, the single pile with a diameter 3 to 5m, are the 
most common foundation type adopted in the existing offshore wind 
farm in Europe and are considered as the foundation in the offshore 
wind farm in the pilot project of Taiwan. The lateral loading comes 
from wind and wave load, is thus intensely cyclic and leads to an 
accumulation of permanent deformation of the pile. Due to the extreme 
sensitivity of wind energy converters to the lateral deformation, a 
sufficient embedded pile length is needed to limit the lateral 
deformation within the tolerance under cyclic loads. 
An important aspect in the design of offshore foundation elements is 
the potential development of scour (Fig. 1). For an offshore pile 
foundation, the scour caused by waves and currents may decrease the 
lateral resistance substantially, inducing excess lateral deformations 
under monotonic design loads as well as large permanent deformations 
under cyclic lateral loads. The scour depth to be expected around a 
single pile foundation has been investigated by many researchers based 
on laboratory tests (Dahlberg, 1983; Ansari and Qadar, 1994; Sumer 
and Fredsoe, 2001; den Boon et al., 2004; Whitehouse et al., 2006; 
Rudolph and Bos, 2006; Nielsen and Hansen, 2007). The suggested 
scour depth S is presented to be dependent on the pile diameter D, with 
S = 1.3 to 2.5 D dependent on system and boundary conditions. In the 
practical design, a relative scour depth S/D between 1.0 to 1.5 is 
usually adopted following the suggestions of the related design 
standards (DNV, 2004; Germanischer Lloyd, 2005). 
A sensitivity analysis of the lateral resistance of piles with a small 
diameter under monotonic load is presented by Diamantidis and 
Arnesen (1986) with p-y curve method. The maximum bending stress 
in the pile was found to increase almost linearly with the scour depth. 
Zaaijer (2002) investigated the effect of a scour hole on the natural 
frequency of support structures for offshore wind energy converters. 
For a special monopile system considered he obtained a reduction of 
the natural frequency of about 5% with respect to the case without a 
scour hole. The deformation responses of monopoles are investigated 
by Li at al. (2009) and Achmus et al. (2010) with finite element 
models. The scour around the monopiles lead to a reduction of 
embedded pile length and lateral resistance. A considerable lateral 
deformation may occur on the monopiles due to scours which are 
subjected to a certain loading conditions or the monopiles with 
specified geometries. The offshore wind energy converters are very 
sensitive to lateral deformation and in particular to coinciding pile 
rotations. To ensure the serviceability of wind energy converters, an 
additional pile length can be considered as an alternative method to the 
scour protection. However, the minimum required embedded pile 
length with respect to scour is not discussed intensively in the existing 
references. 
For a laterally loaded pile without a scour, there are some suggestions 
presented in the existing references for the minimum required

There is no general accepted method to allow scour in the p-y curves

THE MINIMUM REQUIRED LENGTH UNDER CYCLIC 
LOADING CONDITION 
 
Based on the laboratory test results of Hettler (1981), LeBlanc et al. 
(2010) and field test results of Alizadeh & Davisson (1970), the 
horizontal displacements of monopiles increase with load cycles when 
the lateral load is high enough. For the offshore wind turbines cyclic 
loading results from wind and waves, and excess lateral deformation 
could happen on the monopile foundation and imperil the serviceability 
of wind energy converters. In this section, the deformation responses of 
monopiles under cyclic loading are studied with the p-y curve method. 
The ability of monopiles with different pile length to limit the 
accumulation of lateral deformation is compared. 
For the cyclic loading condition, the deformation responses of 
monopiles without a scour under different loading conditions are 
presented in Fig. 11. The deformation responses of monopile are 
analysis with the critical length Lc,const, Lc,vt and Lc,ztk determined from 
Fig. 8 and Fig. 9. The increasing rate of lateral deformation due to 
cyclic loading ys,cyc/ ys,stc is defined here as the ratio of lateral 
deformation of pile at sol surface due to cyclic lateral load and the 
lateral deformation of pile at sol surface due to monotonic lateral load. 
The increasing rate of lateral deformation due to cyclic loading ys,cyc/ 
ys,stc is positive for different loading conditions in Fig. 11 and Fig. 12. 
Horizontal displacements of the pile top increase due to cyclic loadings. 
For the monopiles subjected to a lateral load without a moment arm 
(h=0m), a moment is added on monopiles due to the scour (H*S). This 
additional moment induces an increase rate of lateral deformation due 
to cyclic loading. For the monopiles with a scour S=7.5m, a pile length 
reach vertical tangent criterion may lead to a significant increasing rate 
of lateral deformation due to cyclic loading. The increasing rate of 
lateral deformation due to cyclic loading are identical for the monopiles 
with a scour when the embedded pile length exceeding the critical 
length Lc,const for the monopiles subjected to a lateral load without a 
moment arm (Fig. 12). When the monopile with a pile length meets the 
critical length Lc,const  shows the ability to limit the accumulation of 
lateral deformation due to cyclic loading under pure horizontal loading 
condition.  
Although the horizontal deformation of monopiles with a scour S=7.5m 
subjected a lateral with a moment arm increases due to scour, the 
increasing rate of lateral deformation due to cyclic loading ys,cyc/ ys,stc is 
smaller than the monopiles without a scour under the same loading 
conditions. This is due to the moment induced by scour that is 
relatively irrelevant for the monopiles subjected a lateral load with a 
large moment arm. For the monopiles foundations of offshore wind 
turbines, the lateral load due to wind and wave applied on monopiles is 
generally with a large moment arm.

CONCLUSIONS 
The effect of scour on the lateral deformation of monopile foundations 
is investigated with a state-of-art method (p-y curves method). The 
lateral displacement of the large-diameter monopile under monotonic 
and cyclic loading are affected significantly by scour. The scour around 
the piles lead to loss of lateral resistance of monopile. To ensure the 
serviceability of wind energy converters, a sufficient penetration pile 
length is needed. The critical length Lc,const, Lc,vt and Lc,ztk suggested by 
existing guideline and regulations can be considered to determine the 
minimum required embedded pile length. Under the monotonic 
loading, the critical length Lc,const increases with the loading level and
