傳送接收器設計 (Transceiver design) 的主流。 它利用
所謂的空間多樣性 (Spatial diversity) 有效的提升系統
品質及傳輸速率。再則，在傳送端我們若結合多天
線與時間-空間區塊編碼 (Space-Time Block Code; 
ST-BC) [2][4][5][15] ，則更能善用傳送多樣性 
(Transmit diversity)和編碼增益 (Coding gain)，對降低錯
誤率 (Bit-error-rate; BER) 與提升系統效能有明顯助
益，這也是多輸入-多輸出 (MIMO) 系統的核心精
神。 
此外，正交分頻多工 (Orthogonal Frequency Division 
Multiplexing；OFDM) 調變技術 [6]-[9]，它是屬於多
載波 (Multicarrier, MC) 調變技術的一種，在多載波的
系統架構下，整個通道的頻帶 (Bandwidth) 被分割成
相對應的幾個子載波通道，同時，所傳送資料也被
分割成大小一致的連續符碼區塊 (Block symbols)，對
多路徑傳輸較具強韌性 (Robustness)  和較佳的頻譜
效益(High spectral efficiency)等優點，已被廣泛的應用
在許多不重要的無線通訊系統，例如 IEEE 802.11a 
[16][17]、IEEE 802.11n 與 WiMAX/IEEE 802.16 等 
[23]。 通常使用正交分頻多工調變技術時，藉由加
入足夠的冗餘資料量 (Redundant symbols)或保護區間
(Guard interval) 於連續傳送的區塊資料中[11][13]，
可以有效抑制由通道多路徑所引發的符碼間干擾
(ISI);含區塊間干擾 (Inter-block-interference; IBI)，所造
成的影響。 如此可使系統效能達到預期品質，兼具
有能使用具低複雜度通道等化器  (Channel equalizer) 
的優點。 
在文獻中較常被使用的冗餘資料量加在所欲傳
輸符碼區塊 (Block symbols) 的方法有三類。 其中最
早被使用的是所謂的循環前置碼 (Cyclic prefix; CP); 
亦即在傳送端將所欲傳送的區塊符碼 (Block symbol)
附加一組冗餘資料量 (Redundant symbol) [11][13]。
通常是取區塊資料尾端四分之一將之複製置於前
面，且其長度不可小於通道脈衝響應階數，如此可
以維持子載波間的正交性 (Orthgonality) 並避免子載
波間干擾(Inter-carrier interference; ICI)。很顯然的，
這將會大幅降低資料傳輸速率假降低頻寬效益，如
何兼顧符碼間干擾消除能力與頻寬效益顯的格外重
要。然因為 CP-OFDM 系統對於通道零點 (Channel 
null) 的問題相當敏感。 為了改善其缺點另兩種加入
冗餘資料量的方法，文獻上也陸續有新的防衛區間
方法被提出； 即補零 (Zero-padding; ZP) [11] 與虛
擬隨機後置編碼 (Pseudo Random-Postfix; PRP) [13] 
的正交多頻分工(OFDM) 系統已經被提出。其中，
PRP-OFDM 具有與 ZP-OFDM 相同的優點，所不同
的是於 PRP-OFDM 系統中，我們可以利用所謂的後
置編碼 (Postfix sequence) 於接收端 (並假設信號之
一階統計特性為零) 做通道估測。 因不需額外的領
航符碼 (Pilot symbol)，不會降低系統頻譜效益 
(Spectral efficiency)，居於這個理由它也被稱之為半
盲蔽式 (Semi-blind) 通道估測方法 [12][13]。 本計
畫將強調結合 PRP-OFDM 技術與 ST-BC MIMO 系
統方面之討論，並改善其缺點。  
 
三、研究方法及步驟 
為了改善傳統虛擬隨機後置編碼正交分頻多工
(PRP-OFDM) 技術為基礎的 ST-BC MIMO 系統之缺
點，我們設計出新的所謂的虛擬隨機循環後置碼序
列 (Pseudo-Random Cyclic-postfix; PRCP)，並將其應用
到 ST-BC-MIMO-OFDM 系統，稱之為多輸入-多輸
出 (MIMO) 之虛擬隨機循環後置編碼正交分頻多工
(PRCP-OFDM) 系 統 。 同 時 ， 在 ST-BC-MIMO 
PRCP-OFDM 系統的接收端， 我們可以利用所提出
的循環後置碼序列 (PRCP) 結合其特定的時間-空
間編碼器編碼 (i.e. W(.))來做多輸入-多輸出系統 
(MIMO System) 的通道估測 (Channel estimation)。並藉
由虛擬隨機循環後置編碼之移除降低區塊訊號間之
干擾(IBI)。之後， 利用所估測出的通道資訊 
(Channel Side-Information; CSI) 設計等化器，就已移除
之所接收到的區塊訊號 (Block Symbols) 作等化 
(Equalization)。因為所提出之虛擬隨機循環後置編碼 
(PRCP) 較傳統之虛擬隨機後置編碼 (PRP)有較佳之
通道估測效果，可以有效提升系統品質  (Symbol 
error rate; SER)。

    總共有 36 個 Oral Sessions 及 12 個 Poster Sessions， 此外每天各有一場
Plenary Speech ， 詳細議程如附件(一)。 討論之議題從先進訊號處理技術至

將我國各項主流科技與產業向相關人士說明，完成一次柔性外交。 明年會議主
辦國為泰國地點為清邁。--  附上擬刊登在 IEEE eXPlore 之論文, 供參考。

 NEW ST-BC MIMO-CDMA TRANSCEIVER WITH AUGMENTED USER 
SIGNATURES 
 
Shiunn-Jang Chern1, and Ming-Kai Cheng2 
 
1Tamkang University, Taipei County, Taiwan {e-mail:sjchern@mail.tku.edu.tw}. 
2 National Sun Yat-Sen University, Kaohsiung City, Taiwan. 
 
 
ABSTRACT 
 
This paper presents new transceiver framework for the DS-
CDMA systems that use the multiple-input multiple-output 
(MIMO) antennas, with space-time block code (ST-BC). In the 
transmitter, new hybrid (prefix/postfix) zero-padding sequences 
combining with the desired user signature is exploited to form 
the hybrid augmented user signature sequences, with respect to 
different transmit-antennas. While in the receiver under the GSC 
framework a new Capon-like semi-blind MIMO-CDMA 
filterbank detector based on the linearly constrained constant 
modulus (LCCM) criterion, is derived. Also, the adaptive GSC-
RLS algorithm is developed for adaptively implementing the 
blind LCCM MIMO-CDMA filterbank of the receiver. With the 
specific design of the hybrid augmented user signature 
sequences, we are able to resolve phase ambiguity problem of 
blind channel estimation. Computer simulations verify the merits 
of the proposed new transceiver framework; it can be employed 
to achieve better performance, and is more robust against the 
discrepancy due to Capon channel estimation. 
 
 
1. INTRODUCTION 
 
With the multiple transmit-antennas (Tx) and receive-
antennas (Rx) (MIMO) technology, channel capacity of the 
direct sequence code division multiple access (DS-CDMA) 
systems can be increased, effectively, for wireless 
communications [1]-[4]. Also, the MIMO systems with space-
time coding (STC) make spatial diversity possible to be 
exploited in downlink transmission since it relies on multiple 
transmit-antennas, which is feasible at the base-station [3]-[5]. 
The multiple access interference (MAI) is known to be the main 
performance limitation of the CDMA systems. In addition, the 
self-interference (SI) (due to ST-coding) induced by multiple 
transmit antennas in the ST-BC framework is another important 
issue. The blind receiver is an alternative candidate employed to 
increase spectral efficiency, since the training sequence is not 
required. However, the conventional blind receiver has inherent 
phase ambiguity problem [6][7]. To solve this problem, in this 
paper, new transceiver framework of the ST-BC MIMO-CDMA 
system is proposed. With specific design of new hybrid 
(prefix/postfix) zero-padding sequences combining with the 
desired user signatures in the transmitter, it can be used to 
alleviate the effect of MAI and SI. Also, associated with a new 
Capon-like blind channel estimator in the receiver, it can solve 
the phase ambiguity problem. Next, under the generalized 
sidelobe canceller (GSC) structure the GSC-RLS algorithm is 
derived for implementing the blind MIMO-CDMA filterbank of 
the receiver, adaptively. 
 
2. NEW TRANSCEIVER FOR ST-BC MIMO-CDMA 
SYSTEM WITH LCCM GSC-RLS ALGORITHM

the user signatures cmk are exploited to alleviate the effects of 
ISI and SI in multipath channels. The transmitted signal vectors

3. SIMULATION RESULTS 
 
We consider a 10-user downlink CDMA system, with two 
transmit-antennas and N = 1 or 2 receive-antennas. The QPSK 
modulation and Gold code with length 31 and unit-energy are is 
considered. The noise coefficients are with zero-mean and the 
variances No are determined according to SNR defined as Es/No. 
Near-far ratio (NFR) is set to be 10 dB; it caused severe MAI, 
and the forgetting factor λ  is set to be 0.998. The MMSE 
detection scheme with perfect (or known) channel information is 
the optimal solution and is used as the benchmark for 
comparison. To be consistent with [9], the true channel vector is 
employed for scaling ˆ ( )th , such that the first initial element of 
channel vector is error-free, that is the inherent ambiguity 
problem was ignored. For comparison, we also derived the so-
called MV-GSC-RLS algorithm under new transceiver model for 
the ST-BC MIMO CDMA receiver, for adaptively implementing 
the LCMV-based blind Capon-type receiver. For fair 
comparison, the bit-error-rate (BER) of the proposed algorithm 
and MV-GSC-RLS algorithm [10] are computed by ignoring the 
first 500 iterations (corresponding to the 500 samples used in 
estimating the Ryy(t) for the blind Capon receiver) and ensured 
the proposed algorithm to converge.  
First, we consider the case without mismatch, for L=3 as 
evident in Fig.3 the proposed LCCM-GSC-RLS algorithm could 
achieve better BER performance over the conventional Capon 
approach and MC-GSC-RLS algorithm. It has about 4dB 
improvements against the blind Capon receiver, and is about 
2dB worst than the MMSE receiver (the benchmark) [12]. Next, 
the mismatch effect is investigated with similar parameters as in 
without mismatch case; the results are shown in Fig.4. In fact, 
the BER performances of the blind Capon receiver and MV-
GSC-RLS algorithm are unsatisfactory under mismatch effect. 
This is true, when the value of SNR increases, the BER becomes 
worse than the proposed algorithm.

MIMO-CDMA, where a specific hybrid augmented signature
