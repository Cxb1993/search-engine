佈的電阻和LED 晶片各層的幾何和物質電阻。通常，在LED 裡均勻分佈的電流能降低串聯電
阻。但是，在堵塞電流的電極和半導體層之間的一絕緣層的插入，因此產生更高的串聯電阻，
更多均勻的電流分布可能也降低I-V 特性或輸出功率[26,27]。有些研究指出垂直注入電流
(vertical-injection)型的GaN 系列發光二極體(vertical LED)有優良的潛能成為高功率和高效率
LED 元件[19,23,29,30]。這些LED 將可降低電力浪費或者有更高的光輸出功率以得到更高的
LED 效率。更低的電力浪費可透過電極圖案的適當安排來獲得。一種精心設計的電極圖案能導
致一個更均勻的電流分布和更好的電特性[16,17,21-24,31]。另一方面，增加光輸出的一種方式為
降低電極襯墊對於光的吸收。要避免電極襯墊對於光的吸收，就是要將電流有效地引入LED 發
光層的有效區域。插入一個電流阻擋層可增加注入到有效面積的電流量[23,26,27,32,33]，但是在
LED 裡的串聯電阻則通常變得更大，並且它的電流-電壓特性會變得更差且輸入電流的數量也
將減少。因此，以不接近電流飽和之假設情形下，LED 的電和光學特性被活化層(active layer)
裡的電流分佈所影響，而未轉成光的電能將造成熱能的產生，使得晶片產生溫度效應，根據
Shockley 方程式，磊晶層的電特性會受到溫度的影響而改變，這將會間接改變電轉光的效率，
而當電的特性轉變時，熱分佈又隨之而改變，這導致熱與電效應不停的互相影響，直到平衡為
止，由此可看出電與熱是密不可分的兩個因子，因此，在本研究計畫中將深入的探討關於熱電
耦合下的晶片設計。 
工研院於2005 年成功開發出藍光及綠光的AC 電源LED 製程技術[34]，讓LED使用的電源
不再受限於電池或是需經由交直流轉換器轉換的DC 電源，變成是可以直接用家裡電源插座供
應的交流電。可直接插電於110V 交流電壓使用之交流發光二極體(AC LED)，將可立即應用於
工業及民生產品，如指示燈、霓虹燈，目前全世界僅有美、韓有此技術，但晶粒體積較大約1mm3 
以上，工研院開發出體積為0.3-0.6mm3 的AC LED 技術不僅與國際同步，也是全球領先者之
一。直接使用交流電(AC)電源的LED，意味著應用範圍又進一步地擴展。一般而言，戶外用及
車用的LED 都用直流電(DC)，如果能直接用交流電(AC)，那麼家用市場就大有機會了。直接以
交流電操作，可以節省30～40%的電力，另外因為沒有「交流╱直流轉換器」，因此LED 燈具
之體積將會更小，成本也可望降低。可應用在工業及民生用小型指示燈，至於高壓低電流導通
的優點，也可擺脫霓虹燈及大樓外牆使用DC LED 時，線路高損耗造成須依賴電源供應器接續
的施工困擾及成本問題。此外，AC LED 能夠雙向導通，藍綠光LED 不會出現靜電擊穿(ESD)
的問題，而微晶粒技術更可大幅提高發光效率；由於AC LED 的功率因數提高與低電流控制，
預期將成為未來一般照明、LCD 背光面板產業及車用照明產業另一項更實用化的新替代光源技
術。

三、 研究方法 
高性能LED 需具備有高輸出功率和低驅動電壓。這些需求部分取決於電流分佈的均勻性與
轉換熱量的分佈。在這個領域中仍有許多工作需要達成。令人遺憾地，在實驗上對於各磊晶層
(n-GaN、p-GaN、電流阻擋層、透明接觸層與活化層)、電極的型式(包含電流分布與電壓變化)、
細部溫度量測及光輸出功率等項目的研究上是費時且具有困難的。因此，發展一個合適的三維
數値模型來幫助研究這些項目的影響是極為有用與需要的。而除了透過數值模擬的方法外，仍
須建構一套標準的LED量測設備，來針對實驗數據與數值模擬結果的相互驗證。 
1. 研究方法與進行步驟 
(a)熱電耦合模擬數值方法： 
在本研究計畫中，利用有限元素分析軟體COMSOL Multiphysics 來輔助整體模擬分析之研
究。整體分析流程如圖1-6 所示，其中包含幾何模型建立、相關物理參數設定(Physics condition：
Initial value、Material properties、Heat generation term、Volume force term、Heat Source/Sink、
Another boundary condition)、網格建立(Mesh)、求解器設定(Solve)以及求解後處理設定(Post 
process)等等。有限元素法是一個數值的程序，可以用來求解包含穩態、暫態、線性或非線性問
題的應力分析、熱傳遞、電磁與流體流動等廣泛的工程問題。一般而言，工程問題可轉化為物
理狀態下的數學模式，利用對應邊界和初始條件，以及藉由基礎法則和自然原理對系統或控制
體積而得到的微分方程式，這些統御方程式通常代表著質量、力或能量的平衡。在很多實際的
工程問題中，由於複雜的統御方程式或是難以處理的邊界和初始條件，使得我們無法獲得精確
解，在處理上只能以數值的近似值來與解析解做比對，解析解顯示系統內任一點精確的行為，
而數值近似值的解卻只存在於不連續的點，即為節點上。任何一個數值程序的第一步驟即是做
分離，其過程是將介質分成很多區域和節點，而一般常用的數值方法中，有限元素法使用積分
公式來創造一個代數方程式系統，以一個近似連續的函數來表示每個元素的解答，藉由連結或
組合個別的解，完整的解即可產生。

(b)發光二極體的工作原理：     
發光二極體晶粒的組成材料是半導體，其中含有帶正電的電洞(Hole)比率較高的稱為 p 型半
導體，含有帶負電的電子(Electronic)比率較高的稱為 n 型半導體。p 型半導體與 n 型半導體相接
處的接面稱作 p-n 接面(p-n Junction)，因為接面上的載子(Carrier)濃度梯度很大，造成載子擴散
的情形，所以電洞由 p 側擴散到 n 側，電子由 n 側擴散到 p 側，電子與電洞擴散的情形，讓接
面的 n 和 p 兩側會分別形成具有正離子及負離子的電荷空泛區(Depletion Layer)或稱為(Space 
Charge Region)，因為空泛區而產生一個內建電位勢(Built-in Potential,Vbi)。沒有外加電壓情況
下，費米能階(Fermi Energy)在 n 側與 p 側都保持一定，在發光二極體的正負極兩端施予順向偏
壓，造成空間電荷區(Space Charge Region)變窄，而且空間電荷區的位能障壁變低，n 側與 p 側
的費米能階也不盡相同，因此在 p 側與 n 側的主要載子分別注入 n 側與 p 側，使兩側之少數載
子(Minority Carrier)大量增加，因為少數載子的增加，在兩側能進行直接復合的電子電洞對大量
增加。在順向偏壓時，使電子由高能階掉回低能階狀態與電洞結合，將結合的能量以光的形式
發出，依使用材料的能階高低決定發光的波長，因此就會發出不同顏色的光，可發出紫外光、
可見光、紅外線區域內的自發輻射光。針對二極體的電流-電壓特性，由 Sah、Noyce 和 Shockley
在 1960 年代提出一個理想二極體方程式以描述一個 p-n 接面二極體的電流-電壓特性曲線(I-V 
curve) [41]。此一方程式因此被稱為 Shockley 方程式。如下式所示：

五、 結論 
因為 AC LED 與 DC LED 的操作方式不同，因此無法使用順向偏壓法量測 AC LED。一般
交流電系統皆是提供固定電壓均方根值，因此本文從 Shockley 方程式討論輸入電壓所對應之電
流與溫度之關係，發現輸入相同電壓時其對應之電流將隨溫度增加而上升，根據此特性製作出
用於量測接面溫度的溫度校正曲線。溫度校正曲線的製作是利用相同晶片之 AC LED 具有相同
的起始電壓之特性，由烤箱提供一熱源觀察輸入相同起始電壓時電流隨溫度之變化而製作出溫
度校正曲線。利用此溫度校正曲線之斜率值與電流差可計算出 AC LED 接面溫度，以及利用此
顆 AC LED 於直流操作時所量之熱阻推算 AC LED 於交流電操作時之平均接面溫度。使用熱阻
推算的方法來驗證本實驗量測的準確性。其結果顯示，驗證值與實際量測值誤差不超過 0.12℃。

因為在微製程技術不斷提升下，新開發的複合微晶片 LED 具有較好的光效率以外，還能配
合不同電壓的交流電來驅動。所以針對這樣的產品本研究利用數值方法和各種實驗的比對下來
與傳統的單晶片 LED 做比較，發現當在同樣大小的晶片和同樣的磊晶結構下，如果利用複合的
微晶片結構可以使面積上的平均電流密度變大，增加光輸出功率，此外因為微晶片的設計讓 p
電極到 n 電極的距離縮短了，所以每顆微晶粒上的電流密度都可以很均勻，再將這些電流密度
較均勻的微晶粒串成與單晶片一樣大時，可以發現在複合微晶片裡不會出現，在單晶片會造成
光效率較差的電流密度空泛區。在溫度分布的部分，由於複合微晶片中的電流壅塞會因多顆微
晶粒的設計被分散在整塊晶片上，所以在電流壅塞上所造成的熱點不會像單晶片一樣全部都聚
在一起且產生較大的熱源，使晶片上溫度的高低溫度差異很大，造成熱應力使晶片上的結構產
生破壞。此外溫度還會影響光飽和的速度，而由模擬得知複合微晶片因為溫度較低所以在光飽
和的部分比單晶片慢，且在 8W 時未達到光飽和的現象。 
    複合微晶片的設計除了在電流密度和溫度分布造成的影響以外，還有串聯所造成高電壓低
電流的特性，使得非活化層所產生的焦耳較低，尤其在越高瓦數下，傳統的單晶片與複合微晶
片在焦耳熱的差異更加明顯，這也是複合單晶片在高瓦數下能讓溫度較低使光輸出功率較好的
主要原因之一。但是複合微晶片在製作上比單晶片複雜，尤其是溝槽、絕緣層和晶片間的互連
導線的設計上都比都比單晶片困難許多，所以相對來說會增加製作上的成本，但是如果能克服
在成本上的問題，相信複合微晶片能利用高光輸出功率和多種驅動模式的這些優點下，在日後
的固態照明市場中獲得重視的發展機會。 
     此外本研究也利用這高準度的模擬結果，來估計當這樣大小的 LED 晶片在此封裝體下，
可承受溫度的電功率大約在 5W 左右，如果繼續增加瓦數會因為溫度過高造成封裝與晶片的損
壞，最後在 8W 時兩者晶片都已超出溫度承受極限。 
    此研究中，使用的熱電耦合模擬方法，可將一顆具有封裝體的 LED，從電流輸入至晶片中，
因為受晶片裡不同材料或結構的改變，所以得到不同電流密度的分布形態，而電流密度的分布
形態與溫場分佈互相有極大的影響，所以當本研究的數值方法能正確的模擬封裝體下的 LED
時，模擬的結果將更能貼近真實成品。其中本研究也把光功率衰退的現象考慮進去，這樣的方
式讓數值模擬更貼近真實 LED 在做功時的物理現象。藉由這套完整的系統，可以針對 LED 不
同結構上的設計在不同瓦數下，預測 LED 電性和溫度的結果，做晶片結構上設計的改變，或調
整其LED的封裝體，達到最好的產品設計。而本研究中所探討的複合微晶片除了能在直流電(DC)
驅動以外，還可以利用交流電(AC)或是 AC 經由全波整流器所得的全波電壓來點亮，因此未來
可以利用此研究所建立的數值方法來探討其在非穩態的高瓦數下去做探討。另外在螢光粉上的
影響如果也能考慮進去的話，那本套數值系統將能考慮螢光粉在封裝上對 LED 晶片的影響，將
可以分析從電能到最後輸出的白光過程中所有的物理現象，在依據其結果來設計最佳化的 LED。

junction temperature including microRaman spectroscopy,

The forward voltage method is most often used today because it
is easy to apply and has better accuracy [2, 9]. It is based on the
linear relationship between the forward voltage and junction
temperature, when the junction temperature is greater than
300 K [10]. The dependence of the forward voltage
temperature varies with the structure of the LED. During the
measurement process, a pulsed forward current is applied to
the sample LED which is placed in a temperature-controlled
oven. The slope of the linear relationship between the forward
voltage and junction temperature is obtained. In thermal
equilibrium under constant current input, the forward voltage
can be measured by a sudden reduction in the input current
to a small value in a very short period of time [11]. The
junction temperature of this LED under this current input can
be inferred from the forward voltage temperature relationship.
However, it is very time consuming to measure the junction
temperature using the forward voltage method. The thermal
resistance of an LED is usually defined as the temperature
difference between the junction and the lead frame divided
by the heat generation rate while light emission [12]. Thus,
the thermal resistance for a particular LED is determined by
the chip, submount, and heat dissipation structures and their
properties. When the thermal resistance is known, the junction
temperature can be deduced by measuring the temperature at
the lead frame. The data sheet provided by the LED maker
usually includes the thermal resistance, maximum allowable
power and the junction temperature limit. The forward voltage
method is easy to use with dc LED devices due to their constant
current input, but it is very difficult to extend the method
to measure the junction temperature of an ac LED, simply
because the ac LED device is driven by a time varying voltage.
To date, there is no well-developed method for measuring
the junction temperature of an ac LED. Hwu et al [5] have
proposed an indirect method for determining the junction
temperature of an ac LED device. In that method, the ac LED
is driven by only part of the chips in the full ac LED array
under dc operation. The junction temperature and the thermal
resistance are obtained based on the forward voltage method.
The junction temperature during ac operation can be inferred
from the temperature at the lead frame under ac operation and
the thermal resistance obtained during dc operation. However,
inaccuracy may arise when calculating the thermal resistance
based on the junction temperature obtained during dc operation
because in this case the temperature is invariant with time, even
after a long running time, while its behavior under ac operation
is periodic. This may also cause error when using the dc mode
instead of the ac one.
In this study, a method for measuring the mean junction
temperature of an ac LED is proposed. The linear relationship
between the temperature and the input current is derived from
the Shockley equation. Variation of the threshold current
with temperature under the threshold voltage is examined
experimentally to confirm this linear relationship. The
threshold currents at the initial and quasi-steady states are
measured for different ac voltages. The mean junction
temperatures are then determined from these threshold currents
and the linear temperature dependence of the threshold
current.
2. Measurement
2.1. Theory
An ac LED turns on when the instantaneous voltage reaches
the threshold voltage. Hwu et al [5] performed a numerical
simulation to predict the variation in the junction temperature
of an ac LED with time. Initially, the mean temperature is
equal to the ambient one and increases quickly with time,
but approaches a constant value after the ac LED has been
operating for a certain period of time. It is obvious that the
threshold current is not initially affected by the self-heating of
the ac LED. The variation of the forward current within a time
period becomes invariant when the mean temperature becomes
steady. If the relationship between the threshold current and
the mean temperature is known, the mean junction temperature
at the quasi-steady state can be inferred from the ambient
temperature, and the threshold currents at the beginning and

4. Conclusion
In the present study, a novel method for measuring the mean
junction temperature of an ac LED is proposed according to
the thermal and electrical characteristics of an ac LED which
is driven when the instantaneous voltage reaches the threshold
voltage. The present results show that the threshold current
increases linearly with temperature. Using this linear relation,
the mean junction temperature for different input Vrms can
be obtained by measuring the threshold currents at both the
initial and quasi-steady states. We obtain an accuracy with
this method for measuring the mean junction temperature
of an ac LED of about ±2 ◦C. The thermal resistance of
an ac LED under ac operation as predicted by the present
method is slightly different (only 3%) from that measured
by the conventional forward voltage dc operation method [5].
The advantages of the present method are that it can directly
measure the mean junction temperature of an ac LED under ac
operation, and the measurement process is simpler and more
time saving than the indirect method proposed by Hwu et al
[5].

1. Introduction
LEDs offer several advantages over conventional light sources,
including longer lifetime, low power use, no mercury, compact size
and more reliable. With the rapid development of high-power
white-light emitting diodes and its potential for general illumina-
tion applications, LEDs can be used in numerous illumination
applications, more than trafﬁc signals to decorative lighting, trafﬁc
signals, and backlights for small LCD.
As the efﬁciency improve further, LEDs will become the tech-
nology of choice for existing lighting application as solid-state
lighting (SSL). To satisfy the need for high luminous ﬂux in SSL,
the requirement for high current density and therefore high junc-
tion temperature operation of LEDs become more important. So
package designers concentrated on improving the conductive
thermal path to minimize the thermal resistance and transfer
more heat to the ambient [1–4]. With low thermal resistance,
the package conﬁguration is able to manage higher power dissi-
pation. However, a lot of high power LED suppliers produce single
package LEDs, parts of that as surface mount devices so that they
can be treated automatically during further processing. The main
drawbacks for single package LEDs are the overall size that does
not allow high packaging densities, and too much excessive con-
tent of slug, thermal conductivity glues, solder and board will
raise the thermal resistance. The thermal resistances Rhjb (from
the junction to the package base) of commercial high power LEDs
are in the range of 8–24 C/W [5]. The thermal resistance could
rise if the high power LED is mounted on a PCB, MCPCB (metal-
core printed circuit board) or Al plate owing to the two important
interfaces of chip to slug (or base) and slug (or base) to plate. An-
other approach to high power LED solutions is the chip on board
(COB) technology [5–10]. The COB technology proposes the direct
integration of LED chips on the MCPCB or other substrates, in-
stead of the package LEDs. Compare to package LEDs, the thermal
resistance would be low owing to the only critical interface of
chip to MCPCB.
At the mean time, except for the high efﬁciency and good reli-
ability of LED itself, the penetration of the SSL should learn more
from the progress of CFL-based products. That is, the LED-based
lighting and ﬁxtures with ease of installation, simplicity, good per-
formance and design may play a large part in transforming the
market for residential LED ﬁxtures [11]. However, to reduce ther-
mal resistance of high power LEDs by shorting the thermal path
from the die to slug and slug to Al plate, or enlarging the contact
area between the slug and Al plate, make most of the packages
of high power LEDs have a horizontal geometry as surface mount
structure like Luxeon or Golden Dragon. The thermal resistances
of the high power LEDs can be as low as 8 C/W and 24 C/W.
But these high power LEDs with horizontal geometry it is difﬁcult
to assemble to the light ﬁxtures without PCB, MCPCB or Al plate
and screws. So the lamp bases with the properties as ease of instal-
lation, simplicity, just like E27 or MR16 can do to the lamp sockets
might be important concerns for the LED used in SSL.
A new package structure of W5II based on the COB technology
is presented in this study. To provide an efﬁcient and easy method
of assembling high-power LEDs to sockets of the ﬁxtures, a vertical
design referenced from the typical Edison screw base found in

with single package LED on MCPCB and conventional COB, W5II

provides an efﬁcient and easy means of assembling high power
LEDs to sockets of the ﬁxtures, and can be utilized to enhance
the reliability and thermal fatigue capability of high-power LED
packages.

Light-emitting diodes (LEDs) offer many advantages 
over conventional light sources, such as their narrow 
spectrum, long lifetime, and good mechanical stability 
1).  High-brightness LEDs have demonstrated their 
excellent ability to function in back and general lighting 
applications 2).  There are two major methods to 
increase the light output of an LED chip: enlarging the 
area of a single LED chip and manufacturing an LED 
chip array 3).  When the area of an LED chip is enlarged, 
it results in an increase in the input current and the 
problem of heat dissipation may occur. Chakraborty et 
al. designed an interdigitated multipixel array (IMPA)

improve the high current LED performance 3).  An LED 
array can increase the energy utilization efficiency and 
reliability, and lead to greater compactness 4).  Another 
type of LED chip array is the alternating current (AC) 
LED 5, 6).  The AC LED chip can be individually 
operated, so that the device can eliminate the need for a 
current direction converter, which leads to significant 
cost savings 7). 
A new LED chip array, usually known as a high 
voltage (HV) LED, with microchips connected in series 
has been manufactured.  The HV LED can be driven by 
a half-sine-wave voltage, which is usually generated 
from half-wave rectification under AC operation. All 
microchips of the HV LED are simultaneously turned 
on when the threshold voltage is reached, which can 
improve the problem where only part of the radiation 
area of an AC LED is turned on in each bias direction.  
In this paper, we report on the numerical study of the 
thermal and electrical behaviors of an HV LED.

4.1.  接面溫度量測結果 
單晶片LED的起始電流值 約為1mA，複合微
晶片的LED因為多晶片串聯造成電壓會比較大，所以

0.6mA，經量測可得單晶片及複合微晶片的K factor
分別為 -1.456 mV/℃及-0.0216 mV/℃。隨後將LED

試箱當中，並以同樣的方法，同樣的儀器，同樣的帄

兩種LED的接面溫度結果如表二。而實驗的部份考慮
