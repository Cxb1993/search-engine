前言 
近年來隨著雲端計算應用與半導體產業蓬勃發展，電子零件的速度與效能不斷往上提
昇，另外，行動通訊裝置在產品越做越小而功能越做越多的情況下，許多意圖發射源之產品
擺放在相對小的空間導致相互干擾，進而影響無線通訊之性能。本研究以筆記型電腦為例，
在系統平台上有許多雜訊干擾源，像是距離無線通訊天線最近的Camera、液晶顯示面板以及
中央處理器、記憶體等等，還有一些控制電路部分所產生之電磁干擾頻譜，可能經由傳導耦
合或是輻射散出將雜訊耦合至鄰近無線通訊模組，載台雜訊將入侵在無線通訊頻帶上，降低
無線模組之訊雜比，影響產品整體性能，進而影響無線通訊性能。由於各種無線通訊系統與
高速數位電路的高度整合，因此導致了由快速時脈與訊號技術所產生的系統載台雜訊，當其
進入高敏感性的接收器頻段時，升高的雜訊位準將會使無線通訊系統的接收效能劣化，因此
分析數位無線通訊系統之IC干擾雜訊、內建式天線的型式與擺置佈線之雜訊位準、以及載台
機構對天線的寄生效應，對於提升無線通訊效能的設計便日益重要。 
 
 
 
 
研究目的 
過去在設計電路走線時，因為頻率較低，因此考慮的因素相對來說並不多，只要遵守一些基
本原則就足夠應付了，但隨著傳輸速度增加，工作頻率上升，漸漸的已無法防止電磁效應所產生
的干擾；同時， IC設計已進入到晶片系統(SoC)設計時代，因此電磁相容領域的研究最近這幾年
也漸漸演變運用至模組與 IC上。在其操作頻率上也漸漸提昇、供給的電壓也漸漸降低，在 IC的
電磁耐受度上面也逐漸遭遇問題。一般來說，電磁相容是定義一個電子系統或元件能工作在恰當
的電磁環境中而非在於不正常的干擾環境中而遭遇失效或性能劣化，這也是為什麼電磁相容對於
產業(尤其是電子科技與無線通訊產業)如此重要的理由。舉例來說，當筆記型電腦功能越來越強
的同時也在內部加入許多無線通訊模組，有 Wi-Fi模組、Bluetooth模組、GPS模組..等，由於除了
高速的中央處理器與記憶體都是嚴重的干擾源外，LCD與 CAMERA等影像訊號線路也是明顯的 
EMI干擾源，而隨著無線通訊接收電路的高敏感度要求，使得相對裝置逐漸增加而設計難度變的
更為複雜，倘若沒有考慮到載台雜訊（Platform noise）與射頻模組共存(RF coexist) 問題，在整合
完成後所面臨的將會使整個研發進度受到極重大的考驗。因此，為達成較高傳輸速率或較遠傳輸
距離與較低雜訊位準的要求，利用不同天線設計擺置技巧及改善方法，以降低成本、增加通訊速
度、使通訊無死角便成為本研究之主要目的。

Abstract—Since display panel becomes the largest part of a 
netbook or notebook computer, the built-in antennas for a 
variety of wireless communication systems are inevitably

I.  INTRODUCTION 
Due to the miniaturization of a variety of wireless 
communications products, the layout and trace routing of 
circuits and components become much denser than ever before. 
Meanwhile, with the development of IC technologies 
advancing toward nano-meter processing and higher operating 
frequencies in recent years, the systems of highly integrated 
high-speed digital circuits and multi-radio modules are now 
facing the challenge from performance degradation by more 
complicated electromagnetic noisy environment. The EM 
noises emitted by unintentionally radiated interference sources 
will severely impact the receiving performance of antenna, 
and thus result in the severe performance degradation of 
wireless communications platform. The causes for throughput 
or coverage reduction due to degradation of wireless system 
could result from conducted or radiated EMI noises from 
nearby digital components. Therefore, we have investigated 
and analyzed the noise effect of co-existing high-speed digital 
devices and multi-radios platform for further high-sensitivity 
wireless communications design. 
II. USE NOISE BUDGET ANALYSIS FOR LCD Panel 
A. Application Concept of Noise Budget 
     The concept of noise budget for devices on wireless 
communication product stems from the link-budget for RF 
Tx/Rx performance. It also borrows the idea from EMC testing 
requirements for automobile industry to identify the potential 
interference sources that might cause safety problem and thus 
to provide design guideline for compliance. The noise budget 
concept helps system designers to manage the EMC issues, as 
early as possible, such as coupling mechanisms, module 
placement, grounding, and routing for EMC test. The 
methodology is intended to develop modular architecture of 
analysis to accelerate system design and also provide the 
solutions for potential problems to improve performance in all 
aspects [1]. 
The preliminary goal of this study is to establish the noise 
budget for components and devices on laptop computer for 
further RF sensitivity analysis. To utilize the near-field EM 
scanner to detect the EMI sources on laptop, we can locate the 
major noise sources in 2D hot-spot distribution graph. From the 
emission levels and locations of the noisy components, we can 
figure out their impact on throughput and receiving sensitivity 
of wireless communication and find the solution to improve 
performance. The final goal of noise budget, however, is to 
establish the EMI limits for each digital components related to 
layout location, it would therefore help designers to choose the 
appropriate components for optimal placement and cost 
consideration to meet product requirement. The application of 
noise budget accompanied with near-field surface scanner not 
only can locate the EMI source and further to solve the 
problem, but also can utilize the EM analysis to improve the 
design efficiency and the performance of wireless 
communications.

A. Results of noise level measurement for different LCD

Variation of noise level for LCD Panel module at different 
operation mode is shown in Fig. 2 We can observe the 
significant variation of noise level in 2586~2600MHz

at 2405MHz and 2600MHz, the most significant noise level 
frequencies, respectively. Therefore, the receiving sensitivity 
and thus the communications performance in 2.4 GHz band are

B. Results of surface-scanning measurement 
From the results of noise level measurement, we first 
obtained the interference frequencies generated from LCD 
panel. We then used the magnetic near-field probe to observe 
the noise influence on wireless communication bands via 
antenna ports from LVDS cables, connectors, and driver ICs 
of the LCD panel control circuits when LCD panel was set to  
various operation modes, (such as off, standby, and H pattern 
mode). Figures 6-8 show the change of transmission 
coefficients between antenna port and LVDS cables of the 
LCD panel control circuits. The measured result on left is for 
horizontal orientation of near-field probe placement, and the 
one on right is for vertical orientation. When magnetic probe 
is placed in vertical orientation, it is in parallel with routing 
traces of LVDS connector and thus results in higher sensitivity. 
We also observed that the coupling level are much higher for 
LCD panel operated in standby or H pattern mode than shut 
off. 
Figures 9 and 10 show the change of transmission 
coefficients between antenna port and driver IC of the LCD 
panel control circuits. The measured result on left is for 
horizontal orientation of near-field probe placement, and the 
one on right is for vertical orientation. We observed that the 
noises coupled to antenna port are much higher for LCD panel 
is turned on or displays H pattern mode than shut off, because 
the control IC is activated.
