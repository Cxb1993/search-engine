（一） 計畫中文摘要。 
隨著消費性電子產品日漸普遍應用在家庭生活的每個角落，傳統的影像及視訊
多媒體系統中由於表示資訊的資料量不足，逐漸無法滿足現今數位家庭之需
求；因此，能夠更貼近真實世界的多媒體系統為世人所重視，高動態影像範圍
影像/視訊技術(High Dynamic Range)即是其中的一種解決方案。高動態技術
雖可解決傳統系統中色彩不足的問題，但也帶出幾個新的題目，如：資訊量爆
炸、目前的顯示設備無法相容等。在本計畫中，我們提出一個高動態範圍影像
/視訊的整合系統架構，高動態範圍影像/視訊由數位設備擷取，並將擷取出來
的影像/視訊修正為更接近人類視覺感受的色彩範圍；接著為使得高動態範圍
影像相容於目前的顯示設備，我們設計色調再生演算法使高動態範圍影像/視
訊能夠相容於目前顯示設備中，顯示效果針對人類視覺有與真實色彩相同的感
受；最後，考量高動態範圍影像/視訊資料量過大的問題，設計新紋理壓縮方
法來有效地降低儲存與傳送時所需的空間及頻寬，並提出增強影像/視訊方法
讓影像的感覺更貼近真實。本計畫的主要貢獻在提出一個完整高動態影像技術
之系統架構，除了在前面的顯示部分，更考慮影像中特有的雜訊與資訊量過大
的問題，並配合目前網路上常見的串流傳輸方式。 
關鍵字: 消費性電子產品、高動態影像範圍、人類視覺、色調再生、紋理壓縮

Design of a Filter against Ultrasound Image Noise for 
Remote Diagnosis 
Yen-Yu Chen and Wen-Chien Yan 
Department of Information Management 
ChungChou Institute of Technology 
 
Abstract 
Noise is an inherent property of medical ultrasound imaging, and it generally tends to reduce the 
image resolution and contrast, thereby reducing the diagnostic value of this imaging modality. 
The noise is mainly produced by the imaging instrument and compression during transmission for 
remote diagnosis. Some de-noise algorithm must be applied to enhance image quality. This study 
presents edge enhancement function, quad-tree partition and a set of morphological filters for 
reducing the noise of ultrasound images. Specifically, the presented morphological filters use 
predefined morphological operations, including structuring elements (SE) that include both 
dilation and erosion. The proposed voting strategy can be used to select the morphological filter 
for each block to optimize decoded image quality. Image quality can be enhanced by applying the 
appropriate morphological filter to each block. Simulation results demonstrate that the proposed 
technique enhances noise image quality compared to unprocessed JPEG2000 and SPIHT output 
at an equivalent bit-rate accounting for the side-information overhead, in terms of both peak 
signal to noise ratio (PSNR) and Human Visual System (HVS). 
Key words: noise, ultrasound image, JPEG2000
