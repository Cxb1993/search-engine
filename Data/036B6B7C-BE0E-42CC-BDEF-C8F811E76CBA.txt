一、中文摘要
本研究計畫為三年期。第一年擬針對
金屬板胚電磁成形時之材料流動及成形極
限進行研究，探討成形線圈之電流對成形
力、材料流動、成形極限、彈性回復、成
形後的工件形狀精度等的影響，並進而藉
此技術獲得電磁成形之模具最佳化之製程
參數設計。第二年擬針對金屬板胚電磁成
形時磁場控制模型之設計，進行磁場控制
模型之形狀尺寸、模型與工件之間隙、模
型固定方式、模型材料等對於成形後工件
形狀精度等的影響，並進而藉此技術獲得
電磁成形之磁場控制模型最佳化之製程參
數設計。第三年則研究如何利用高導電撓
性板材料作為成形驅動壓力片對低導電性
材料作間接的磁力成形，擬探討此驅動片
之設計要領，進行驅動片之形狀尺寸、撓
曲剛性性質、驅動片固定方式、驅動片材
料等對於成形後工件形狀精度等的影響，
並進而藉此技術獲得電磁成形之驅動片最
佳化之製程參數設計。由於計畫核定僅一
年，而本結案報告為第一年期。
關鍵詞：電磁成形、成形極限、磁場控制
模型、低導電性材料。
Abstract
The period of this plan is three years.
The first year, study on the influences of eddy
current on forming force, material flow,
forming limits, spring back and the precision
of the formed parts for the electromagnetic
forming (EMF) process. Then according to
the study, the optimizing process parameters
of EMF will be found. The second year,
study on the influences of the shape and
dimensions of the field shaper, clearance
between shaper and wokpiece, shaper fixed
types and shaper materials on the precision of
the formed parts for EMF with field shaper.
Then according to the study, the method of
optimizing field shaper design will be
obtained. The third year, study on the
influences of the shape, dimensions, and
rigidity, fixed type and materials of the
driving plate on the precision of the formed
parts. Then according to the study, the
method of optimizing driving plate design
will be found.
Keywords: electromagnetic forming, field
shaper, lower electric conduction
materials.
二、緣由與目的
由於市場對於輕薄小的需求，產品微小化
的趨勢已蔓延於電子、通信、航太、汽車
工業、醫學…等產業，依據 NEXUS 的預
估，最近幾年全球微系統產品的市場規模
將成長至每年 700 億美元，因應應用面及
市場的不斷擴大，對更穩定、快速、便宜
的精微生產技術的需求將不斷增加。由於
工業產品朝向輕薄短小的發展趨勢，3C 產
4此有需要對其進行有系統之深入研究。本
計畫擬進行金屬板胚電磁成形之成形極
限、磁場控制模型及對低導電性材料間接
磁力成形之三年期研究計畫。
第一年擬針對金屬板胚電磁成形時之
材料流動及成形極限進行研究，探討成形
線圈之電流對成形力、材料流動、成形極
限(圓筒引伸極限)、彈性回復、成形後的工
件形狀精度等的影響，並進而藉此技術獲
得電磁成形之模具最佳化之製程參數組合
設計。
第二年擬針對金屬板胚電磁成形時磁
場控制模型之設計進行研究，線圈的設計
利用標準線圈，加上磁場控制模型(Field
shaper)，以達到可隨時變動複雜幾何形狀
之成形，此係對於形狀較複雜且為適應市
場少量多樣之製造需求(較具變動性與經
濟之成形方式)。計畫擬探討此磁場控制模
型之設計要領，進行磁場控制模型之形狀
尺寸、線圈與工件之間隙、模型固定方式、
模型材料等對於成形後工件形狀精度等的
影響，並進而藉此技術獲得電磁成形之磁
場控制模型最佳化之製程參數設計組合。
第三年則研究如何利用高導電撓性板
材料作成形驅動壓力對低導電性材料作間
接的磁力成形。由於電磁成形對於導電性
高的材料擁有較佳的電磁成形性，因此對
於低導電性金屬(如鎂合金、鈦合金)則需在
工件表面上置放驅動片(如銅)，經由驅動片
與電磁感線圈間產生的感應作用力，帶動
低導電性材料成形。計畫擬探討此驅動片
之設計要領，進行驅動片之形狀尺寸、撓
曲剛性性質、驅動片固定方式、驅動片材
料等對於成形後工件形狀精度等的影響，
並進而藉此技術獲得電磁成形之驅動片最
佳化之製程參數設計組合。
由於計畫核定僅一年，故本結案報告

Conclusions
In this research, FEM was used to investigate the steady-state wire drawing process for strain-rate
sensitivity materials, and a series of simulations on the wire drawing using the program
DEFORM-2D were carried out. The influences of the process parameters such as drawing speed
and strain-rate sensitivity index of the wire material on the relative drawing stress, the relative
separation force, the effective strain-rate distribution, and the effective stress distribution of drawing
die have also been examined. From the results, the following conclusions can be drawn:
(1) The relative drawing stress increases with increasing strain-rate sensitivity index or drawing
speed.
(2) The relative separation force of wire drawing also increases with increasing strain-rate
sensitivity index or drawing speed.
(3) The maximum effective strain-rate of drawing wire for strain-rate sensitivity material increases
with increasing drawing speed.
(4) The maximum effective stress of drawing die for strain-rate sensitivity material increases with
increasing strain-rate sensitivity index.

Keywords: Finite element method, elliptic-shape flaring process, critical flaring ratio.
Abstract. In this paper, the finite element method is used to investigate the cold elliptic-shaped
flaring process of metal tubes with a conical punch from a tube billet. A series of simulations on
tube flaring was carried out using the FEM program DEFORM 3D. The influence of process
parameters such as tube wall thickness, punch conical length, aspect ratio of elliptic shape, friction
factor, punch fillet radius and strength coefficient of the billet material on the critical flaring ratio of
the tube are analyzed.
Introduction
A reducer (see Fig.1) reducing from elliptic-shape to circular-shape is recently used in exhaust pipe
in automatic industry in fashion, or is sometimes used in pipe fitting industry for special situations.
The reducer could be manufactured by tube flaring with a conical punch having a blending shape
from elliptic shape to circular shape. In this process, the conical punch is first pushed into a circular
short tube undergoing suitable traveling, then ejected out of the workpiece to have the rough and

Regarding researches on the tube
flaring process, Ichinose et al. [1]
investigated the inside-out inversion
process of aluminum tube under axial
loading by conical die, and examined the
influence of process parameters, such as
tube thickness, slenderness ratio, punch
conical angle and lubrication, on the
forming limits, forming load and tube
deformation. Manabe et al. [2,3] studied
the forming loads and the stress and strain
distribution in tube nosing and flaring
with conical punch. Chumadin et al. [4] evaluated the stress and strain distribution of the process of
flaring conical blanks. Kitazawa et al. [5,6] established a tube flaring or outward curing forming
criterion by using theoretical analysis and experiments, and proposed a pre-curing method to
improve the flaring limits. Lu [7] provided analytical expressions relating the tube flaring ratio and
tube end strain rate to the tool stroke and velocity in the tube flaring process. Although much work
has been done on the axisymmetric tube flaring as mentioned above, little research has been done
on the elliptic-shaped flaring of tube.

Results and discussion
Figures 4(a) to (d) show the deformation progress of the elliptic-shaped tube flaring. As can be
seen, the leading edge of the workpiece was gradually flared in radius during punch feeding. In
Fig.5, the relative position of the flared tube, and the wall thickness distributions of the flared tube
on the sections of major axis and minor axis are shown in Fig.6. AS shown in both sections, the
wall thickness is thinner in the portion of the leading edge of the flared tube than that in the other
portion.
The minimum wall thickness of a tube reducer for industry should not be less than 87.5% of
nominal thickness according to the product catalog [9]. Therefore, the minimum thickness should
exceed 2.38, 2.89, and 3.825 mm for a reducer tube of 2.8, 3.4, and 4.5 mm in nominal thickness,
respectively. A qualified product has to be fit the minimum wall thickness requirement and produce
no defect of buckling simultaneously.
