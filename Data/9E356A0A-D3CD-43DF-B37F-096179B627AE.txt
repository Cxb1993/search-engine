(英文) Wastewater treatment approach and its device
成果歸屬機構
美和科技大學 發明人
(創作人)
莊德豐
技術說明
(中文) 一種污水處理方法係包含：一分解步驟係藉由微生物厭氧分解污水中的有機物，
且釋出質子群及電子群，獲得初反應污水；一生電步驟係將該質子、電子群分離
而產生電能；一曝氣步驟係將該初反應污水進行曝氣，得次反應污水；及一沉澱
步驟係將該次反應污水進行沉澱而得一放流水；以及一種污水處理裝置係包含：
一生電組件，設有一陽極槽、一陰極槽及一通透膜，該陽極槽及該陰極槽分別設
有一陽、陰極板係以電性連接，該通透膜係設於該陽極槽與該陰極槽之間；一曝
氣槽係與陽極槽連接；及一沉澱槽係與該曝氣槽連通。
(英文) A wastewater treatment approach includes: one degradation process: microorganisms will 
anaerobically degrade onganic matters in wastewater and release protons and electrons 
and form pretreated wastewater, one power generation process: protons and electrons will 
be separated and produce electricity, one aeration process: the pretreated wastewater will 
be aerated and form secondary wastewater, one sedimentation process: the secondary 
wastewater will be settled and then discharged, one wastewater treatment device: This 
device includes one power generation components, one anodic chamber, one cathodic 
chamber and one proton-electron transport membrane. Two electrodes are installed in the 
anodic and cathodic chambers, respectively and connected by electric wire. Proton-
electron transport membrane is installed at a location between anodic and cathodic 
chambers. The anoxic chamber is connected to one aerobic tank, and the aerobic tank is 
connected to a clarifier.
