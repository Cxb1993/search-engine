中文研究摘要 
改善太陽能電池元件特性，最主要的三大重點為(1)降低表面復合(2)提高並聯電阻(降低製造缺陷
及設計多能隙材料)(3)降低接觸電阻。為了達成這些目標，本計畫與產業界合作開發銀電極用銀膠，
藉由改變不同的組成比及加熱時間與溫度等條件，開發高品質低電阻率高效率太陽能電池用銀膠。透
過電性及材料分析的量測，了解各種混成銀膠對太陽能電池元件的特性影響，本研究計畫之研究結果
顯示(1)所製作出來的銀電極之電阻率能達到 1.6x10-6 Ωcm，(2)銀膠能均勻滲透 SixNy並與射極接觸。(3)
藉由調整銀膠組成比搭配最佳加熱參數可得到最佳轉換效率。(4)新的銀電極材料之研發成功，可應
用在太陽能電池元件之製作，將促進太陽能電池元件之進一步發展。 
 
關鍵詞：太陽能電池元件、銀膠、熱處理、轉換效率 
 
 
 
 
Abstract 
The continuous improving the solar cell devices performances are needed for low surface 
recombination, high shunt resistance, and low series resistance.  To achieve this goal, the high quality silver 
paste was investigated to lower series resistance between Ag and the Si interfaces. By modulated the various 
compositions of the silver paste and post annealing conditions, the high quality silver paste can be achieved 
for the silicon solar cell (SSC) devices applications.  According to the electrical measurement and material 
analysis, the effects of the silver electrode combined with various compositions of the silver paste on the 
characterizations of the SSC devices were addressed.  The results show that (1) The resistivity of this new 
silver electrode with 1.6x10-6 Ωcm was achieved. (2) The silver paste can run through the silicon nitrides, (3) 
The optimum composition and post annealing conditions for the silver paste were obtained.  (4) The 
efficiencies of the solar cells were improved by this new silver electrode.   
 
 
Keywords：Solar cell, silver paste, post-anneling, conversion efficiency

(英文) The high quality silver paste was investigated to lower series resistance between Ag and 
the Si interfaces. According to the electrical measurement and material analysis, the 
effects of the silver electrode combined with various compositions of the silver paste on 
the characterizations of the SSC devices were addressed. There are some major works to 
be carried out in this proposal, (1) the silver paste can run through the silicon nitrides, (2) 
The effective contact areas of Ag/Si system can be increased , (3) The optimum 
composition and post annealing conditions for the silver paste cab be obtained. The 
expected results include, (1) The efficiencies of the solar cells with the new silver 
electrode are larger 1-2% than that the solar cell with the old silver electrode ones. (2) 
The efficiency of the solar cells can be improved by this new silver electrode. (3) The 
resistivity of this new silver electrode can be around 1.6x10-6 Ωcm.
