摘要 
 
本研究是藉由合成聚苯胺的同時，添加不同含量之聚乙烯吡咯烷酮(PVP)，來探討
PVP 對聚苯胺之型態及性質的影響。由 TEM、SEM 觀察可得知，PVP 含量在 0.1wt%
以下時，聚苯胺的形態為纖維狀。當 PVP 含量增加到 0.3wt%以上時，形態轉變為顆粒
狀，且粒徑隨著 PVP 含量增加而降低，可製備出不同尺寸從 57nm 到 280nm 聚苯胺之粒
子。聚苯胺之導電度會隨著 PVP 的含量增加而降低，這是因為 PVP 為非導電性高分子，
造成導電度下降。 
 
關鍵字：聚苯胺、纖維、顆粒、聚乙烯吡咯烷酮 
 
 
Abstract 
 
This research is to prepare polyaniline (PANI) by adding various polyvinylpyrrolidone 
(PVP) contents (0wt%, 0.1 wt%, 0.3wt%, 0.5wt%th, 1.0wt%, and 2.0wt%). Results of TEM 
and SEM show that the morphology of PANI is fiber when adding 0.1wt% PVP. However, if 
the amount of PVP is more than 0.3wt%, the morphology of PANI is spherical particle. PANI 
conductivity is decreased with the amount of PVP increased. Since PVP is non-conductive 
polymers, the conductivity of PANI is decreased. 
  
Key words: Polyaniline, fiber, particle, PVP

4. 導電度之分析 
由導電度之結果可得知，PANI 的導電度隨著 PVP 含量的增加   
而減少。因 PVP 本質上屬於非導電性高分子材枓，由於部份 PVP 的分子鏈和 PANI 的分子鏈之間形成
糾結纏繞的現象，使得 PANI 的產生導電通路的結構上之缺陷，不利於電子傳導，會使得導電度降低。 
5. TEM、HR FE-SEM 與光散射平均粒徑分析 
由 TEM、HR FE-SEM 與光散射平均粒徑分析之結果可得知，PANI 之平均粒徑隨著 PVP 含量增加
而減少，這是因為當 PVP 含量增加時，其分散效果增加及顆粒變小，使粒子之間的聚集愈少所造成。

Abstract 
This study introduces polyimide/γ-Al2O3 (PI/γ-Al2O3) hybrid films based on 4, 
4’-oxydianiline (4, 4’-ODA) and pyromellitic dianhydride (PMDA). They were prepared by 
incorporating various amounts of γ-Al2O3 in a PI precursor, poly (amic acid) (PAA). 
Dispersion included an ultrasonic treatment followed by high-speed homogenization to form a 
homogeneous PAA/γ-Al2O3 solution, and thermal imidization of PAA at controlled 
temperature produced the corresponding PI/γ-Al2O3 hybrid films. The PI/γ-Al2O3 hybrid films 
exhibited a pale yellow color and good transparency. TEM micrographs revealed that most of 
the γ-Al2O3 nanoparticles had been homogenously dispersed throughout the PI matrix. The PI 
film had been simultaneously strengthened and toughened by the γ-Al2O3 nanoparticles. In 
addition, the thermal conductivity of PI/γ-Al2O3 hybrid film had significantly increased, as the 
amount of γ-Al2O3 nanoparticles increased in the PI matrix. The thermal conductivity of 
PI/5wt.% γ-Al2O3 hybrid film was approximately 120% higher than that of pure PI film. 
 
Key words: polyimide, Al2O3, mechanical properties
