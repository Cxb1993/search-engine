特高壓地下環路供電系統之潛在風險及其對供電品質影響與改策對策(Ⅱ) 
Hidden Risks of the VHV Underground Distribution System with Loop Scheme 
And Their Affections on Power Quality and Mitigation Strategies (Ⅱ) 
                          
計畫編號:NSC 94-2213-E-027-062 
       執行期間:94 年 8 月 1 日至 95 年 7 月 31 日 
              計畫主持人:周至如 國立台北科技大學電機工程系 
      計畫參與人員: 官軍偉、曾盈昌、蕭智鴻 
 
摘要 
特高壓地下環路供電系統，由於其環路架構及電纜電容很大，使其電氣特性具有相當
潛在風險，主要包括接地故障轉移、諧波共振、保護協調失當、開關突波及低頻諧振之傷
害。本研究主要針對諧波共振及保護協調問題所造成的影響進行探討。在諧波共振方面由
於地下環路之電纜充電電容很大，與電力系統之阻抗共振頻率較低，若共振頻率降到一般
用戶之諧波頻率則諧波共振將會發生，對系統及設備危害很大，本研究將探討典型的 161kV
地下環路供電系統之諧波特性考慮系統短路容量之變化及用戶諧波的條件，分析頻率響應
及諧波響應並評估其影響，包括檢討對電力品質的影響是否超過台電諧波管制標準及其他
不良的影響。在保護協調方面將檢討目前環路架構下的保護缺失及相關問題，並提出改善
對策以供參考。 
 
關鍵詞: 特高壓地下環路、潛在風險、諧波共振、保護協調、供電品質 
 
Abstract 
   The very-high voltage(VHV) underground distribution system(VHV loop system) have 
some hidden risks due to their loop scheme and large cable capacitances. Main hidden risks 
include ground fault transfer(GFT), protection coordination problems, harmonic resonances, 
switching surges and low frequency resonances damages. This study is aimed to the problems of 
harmonic resonances and protection coordination problems. The system resonance frequency 
will decrease due to large cable capacitances which may fall into the spectrum of load harmonic 
currents. Thus, the harmonic resonance will appear to damage the system and equipments. To 
survey this problem, a typical closed-loop 161kV underground distribution system with 6 
customers are analyzed for its frequency response of system impedance and harmonic response. 
The system short circuit capacity (SCC) and the harmonic situations of each customer are 
considered for the harmonic analysis. The affections of harmonics on the power quality are 
evaluated based on harmonics standard of Taipower company, and other detrimental effects are 
assessed. For the problems of protection coordination, the defects of loop scheme and the 
associated problems will be investigated and their improvement strategies are proposed.  
Keywords: VHV underground Loop, Hidden Risks, Harmonic Resonances, Protection 
Coordination, Power Quality

（2）各用戶使用保護電驛特性不同 
各用戶保護電驛的廠牌、型式、設定等很可能有差異(如電磁機械(EM)式、電子式、
數位式…等)其特性很難一致，雖然在正常的穩態時可能沒問題，但在異常或暫態時，誤動
作就可能發生，若電驛有遠端跳脫(Remote Trip)，則誤動作的範圍加大。 
（3）環路架構解聯
