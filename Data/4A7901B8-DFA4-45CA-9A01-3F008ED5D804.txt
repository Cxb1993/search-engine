微機電整合聲覺晶片(I) 
MEMS Integration Acoustic Chip (I) 
 
計畫編號：NSC 99-2218-E-415-002 
執行期限：99 年 8 月 1 日至 100 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：江政達助理教授 嘉義大學 電機工程學系 
 
ABSTRACT 
 
In this work, a MEMS integration acoustic chip is newly proposed. It is attractive due to the 
fact that the sensor and all the circuits are robustly and compactly integrated. Another innovation 
of this proposed chip is that it could be operated without charge pump circuits. Thus, the sensor 
and circuits can be performed at the same power supply. Measurement results have successfully 
verified the correct function and performance of the proposed chip. The proposed MEMS 
integration acoustic chip is suitable for mobile phones, laptops, PDAs, and hearing aids, etc. 
 
Keyword: Sensor interface, silicon condenser microphone, sensor transducer, CMOS, capacitive 
sensor

I. Introduction 
 
Silicon condenser microphones play an increasing role in applications such as mobile phones, 
laptops, PDAs, and hearing aids etc [1]. There are several types of microphones such as 
piezoresistive, piezoelectric, and capacitive microphones, etc. Among them, the inherent 
limitations of mechanism of microphones should be noticed. For example, temperature variations 
affect piezoresistive microphones easily. The fabrication procedures of piezoelectric microphones 
are complex, and can not make the whole processes cheaper for the industry. Capacitive 
microphones are attractive due to the low temperature effects, low cost, and easy combination 
with circuits. Until recently, some products based on capacitive mechanism have previously been

IV. CONCLUSION 
 
A MEMS integration acoustic chip is newly proposed. The proposed chip is attractive due to 
the fact that the sensor and all the circuits are robustly and compactly integrated. The core 
functionalities of the proposed chip are successfully obtained and may be applied to the consumer 
products. In the future, the developed techniques will be adaptively designed into mobile phones.
