一、  中文摘要 
 
PEO-PPO-PEO 團聯共聚高分子(Pluronics)
因可降低界面張力，常用於製藥的乳化劑與穩
定劑，也可應用在藥物釋放中。本研究係針對
Pluronics 共聚物於液/氣界面上之吸附行為進
行探討。藉由調控一系列具不同分子量及親疏
水性 (PO/EO 比例)之 Pluronics 共聚物以掌握
上述效應對其吸附行為之影響，並分析吸附之
Pluronics 分子隨表面濃度不同所產生之相變
化行為。本研究利用表面膜壓裝置進行線上動
態膜壓曲線之量測同時利用橢圓儀量測膜
厚。藉由分析平衡膜壓與膜厚所構成之等溫吸
附曲線掌握 Pluronics 在液/氣界面吸附之相變
行為，並根據相轉變模型 (Phase transition 
model)進行量化分析。本研究比較 PO 比例較
高(L62、L72、L92)、及高 EO 比例 (F38、 F68、
F88、F98、F108)情況下之分子量大小效應、
固定 PO 鏈段改變 PO/EO 比例(L61、L62、
L64、F68)以及相同分子量不同比例 (L35、
L44、L62)的 Pluronics 的平衡膜壓及膜厚，藉
此了解 Pluronics 於液氣界面的吸附機構。研
究結果顯示，平衡膜壓及膜厚所產生之相變化
行為可以分成三個區段：其中膜壓及膜厚等溫
吸附曲線分別在溶液濃度在 10-6~10-5mM 及
10
-4
~10
-3
mM 等兩個區域出現轉折，當溶液濃
度小於第一個轉折區濃度， Pluronics 分子皆
以展開的方式吸附於界面上，膜壓與膜厚的變
化都極低；隨著濃度增加此狀態之吸附在第一
個轉折濃度達飽和，當濃度跨過第一個轉折
區，Pluroics 中之 PEO 鏈段逐漸被排入水相

成 PEO 鏈之伸展而導致膜厚持續上升。

Abstract 
 
PEO-PPO-PEO copolymers (Pluronics) can 
reduce interfacial tensions and are commonly 
used as an emulsifier or a dispersion stabilizer in 
pharmaceutical industry. This study investigates 
the adsorption behavior of Pluronics at air-water 
interfaces. In order to understand the adsorption 
behavior of Pluronics, a series of Pluronics with 
different molecular weight and hydrophobicity 
are studied. We analyze the phase transition 
behavior of Pluronics, at various surface 
concentrations by measuring dynamic surface 
pressures and film thickness in-situ. We also 
analyze the equilibrium isotherms to delineate 
the phase transition behavior of Pluronics 
molecules. A phase transition model is applied to 
quantitatively describe the phase transition 
behavior of Pluronics. The observed phase 
transition behavior of Pluronics can be described 
by different corresponding adsorption 
conformations: for very low concentration 
(<10
-6
mM), Pluronics might adsorb on the 
interface as an extended form without obvious 
increasing surface pressure and thickness. 
Followed the increases in solution concentration 
(10
-6
~10
-5
mM), part of PEO chains might 
protrude into the aqueous solution. At 
concentration ranging from 10
-4 
to 10
-3
mM, 
Pluronics turned into “brush” configuration and 
the PEO chains are completely expelled into the 
aqueous solution while the PPO region is 
remained on the interface. Further increasing 
solution concentration, the adsorbed PPO chain 
might become more coiled or the PEO coils in 
solution might be compressed and become more 
extended and resulting the increases in the 
adsorbed film thickness. 
Key Words：Pluroinics、PEO-PPO-PEO、
Pluronics adsorption/desorption 
 
二、  前言 
 
PEO-PPO-PEO 團聯共聚高分子(Pluronics)
為兩端親水中間疏水的結構，靠疏水端與疏水
性表面產生作用力而吸附形成緊密穩定的薄
膜。藉由改變 PEO 與 PPO 的比例可調控其親
疏水性及對應的界面活性。Pluronics 分子由於
生物相容性良好，同時俱有良好之界面活性，
以簡單之吸附方式，進行表面修飾，將可應用
於生醫材料、生物感測器或藥物微粒之表面處
理。由於 Pluronics 之表面修飾特性與其表面
吸附行為，息息相關，因此本研究將探討此高
分子在液氣界面上之吸附特性。
