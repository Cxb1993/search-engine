2.2 實驗菌株 
2.2.1 Aspergillus niger 
黑麴菌(Aspergillus niger，BCRC 
32817)自生菌中心購得後，因其事先為
冷凍乾燥保存，故遲滯期(Lag period)
會較長，必須等培養兩倍時間後才能
生長出。所以需經一、二次繼代培養
(Subculture)活化後才能正常生長使
用。活化操作步驟如下，將-80℃保存
之菌液移出，先放置-20℃下三十分
鐘，再放置室溫中完全熔化成液態。
倒入 Potato dextrose broth (簡稱 PDB)
之液體培養基中。取 0.1-0.2mL之菌體
懸浮液於 Potato dextrose agar (簡稱
PDA)活化平板培養基上，經培養一段
時間後，以檢視菌種純度及活化情形。 
 
2.2.2 Bacillus sp. 
Bacillus sp.分離自台南弘遠紡織
廠的活性污泥池，在分離出單一純菌
時，需先大量保存於-80℃，其活化操
作步驟如下，將-80℃保存之菌液移
出，先放置-20℃下三十分鐘，再放置
室溫中完全熔化成液態。倒入 Nutrient 
broth (簡稱 NB)之液體培養基中。取
0.1-0.2mL 之菌體懸浮液於 Nutrient 
agar (簡稱 NA)活化平板培養基上，經
培養一段時間後，以檢視菌種純度及
活化情形。 
 
2.3 菌株的共培養 
將已培養好的 Bacillus sp.和已培
養好的 Aspergillus niger 進行混菌，各
取 1 毫升加入培養基，培養於 35℃及

抹棒，塗抹於 NA及 PDA上，於 35℃
培養 24 小時，取出觀察菌相。

2.7.3 發芽率

第三組為二次水。

度為室溫。在第五天分別計算根的長
度與發芽率，比較數據的差異。 
 
3. 實驗結果 
3.1 染料脫色菌種的分離與篩選 
將收集來自紡織廠活性污泥池中
的樣品進行篩菌，在第一次篩菌時發
現只有一種菌落。將這一種菌落使用
NA 與 PDA 中各別分離出來，先將這
一種菌落分別培養於 NB 與 PDB 中，
培養條件為 37℃及 100rpm，培養一天
後取菌液讀取OD600的值為 0.6-0.8之
間，即可進行脫色的效率測試。使用
50ppm的 reactive red dye HE3B進行24
小時的脫色率測試，發現此一菌株的
脫色效率極佳。 
將菌株進行菌種鑑定，發現菌屬
為 Bacillus sp.，由過去的文獻得知此
菌屬是具有一定的脫色效率，但是需
要較長的脫色時間，所以研究中藉由
細菌與真菌共培養的方式期望達到快
速的脫色效率。

lignocellulose using Phanerochaete

Wen-Ta Su and Yu-Hsuan Liu 
Department of Chemical Engineering and Biotechnology, National Taipei University of Technology, 
Taipei, Taiwan. f10549@ntut.edu.tw 
 
Abstract-The effects of surfactants were tested on the production of bioenzyme and lignocelluloses hydrolysis 
using Phanerochaete chrysosporium. The results showed that 0.00625% PEG 4000 nonionic surfactant added 
to the cultivated medium can enhance the activities of endo-β-1,4-D-glucanase, β-1,4-D-glucosidase and 
cellulase were 165.57 U/mL, 40.58U/mL and 0.5U/mL for 96 h culture. These data were increasing 2.05-fold, 
4.52-fold and 3.13-fold respectively than those no surfactant added. The hydrolysis product, reducing sugar, 
can measure as 3.03 mg/mL, and increase 2.05-fold than that no surfactant addition. 
 
Index Term – surfactant; hydrolysis; lignocelluloses; Phanerochaete chrysosporium 
 
I. INTRODUCTION 
Lignocelluloses, a common biopolymer which includes agricultural residues, paper waste, wood 
chips, etc., is an ideal inexpensive, renewable, abundantly available resource. Rice straw, consisting 
cellulose and hemicelluloses in close association with lignin, is abundant in Asia because of its growth 
condition. The basic hydrolysis process of lignocelluloses is usually catalyzed by hydrolytic enzymes, 
because enzymatic hydrolysis of the cellulose produces better yields than acid-catalyzed hydrolysis 
(Pan et al. 2005). However, the high cost of cellulose enzymes and hydrolysis rate decrease rapidly 
often restricts the large scale application of enzyme in the bioconversion of lignocelluloses biomass. In 
order to make cellulose hydrolysis economically feasible, many studies have researching and analyzing 
the inhibiting factors in the enzymatic hydrolysis of lignocelluloses. It has been shown that the addition 
of surfactants can significantly increase the enzymatic conversion of cellulose to soluble reducing 
sugars (Castanon et al. 1981, Ooshima et al. 1986, Helle et al. 1993, Pardo 1996, Reddy et al. 1999). 
Various mechanisms had been proposed for the positive effect of surfactant addition on the enzymatic 
hydrolysis of lignocelluloses. The surfactants can adsorb to the lignin and prevent enzyme bind to the 
lignin, thereby producing higher yield and better recycling of enzymes.  
In the present study rice straw degradation was assessed by a representative of the white-rot fungi, P. 
chrysosporium. The main focus of this study is to investigate the conversion of straw cellulose into 
reducing sugar with surfactant addition, and compare the effect of different type of pretreatment 
process for enzymatic hydrolysis. Through an enzymatic hydrolysis process, rice straw can be 
converted to reducing sugars, which can subsequently be fermented to bio-ethanol and lactic acid.

Chitosan conduits effect the ECM protein expression in 
Schwann cells 
Wen-Ta Su1*, Chuang-Yu Lin1,2, Li-Tzu Li2 
1. Department of Chemical Engineering and Biotechnology, National Taipei 
University of Technology, Taipei 106, Taiwan 
2. Institute of Cellular and Organismic Biology, Academia Sinica, Taipei 115, Taiwan 
 
Background introduction 
Schwann cells play a critical status in the nervous system, especially in the area of 
injured peripheral nerve regeneration. Schwann cells express numbers of ECM 
components, one of these are laminins. Laminin is the most active molecules in ECM 
proteins which regulate Schwann cell proliferation and myelination of peripheral 
axons. There are many studies shown the chitosan-scaffold structure can be a 
suitable bridge for axon regeneration. There are many qualitative investigations of 
laminins in Schwann cells. However, the quantitative analysis of laminins expression 
in Schwann cells grown on the biomaterials is not shown. 
 
Materials & Methods 
Fabricate chitosan conduit by lyophilization. Schwann cells seed into chitosan 
conduits in a rotary culture system. ECM protein expression quantify by ELISA. 
 
Results and discussion 
  Chitosan conduit appeared porous structure that promoted cellular adhesion and 
medium pass through. Based on the results, the bio-compatitable chitosan conduit is 
appropriate for cell attachment and proliferation. Schwann cells cultured on plane 
produce more Laminin protein in cells, but secreted more Laminin protein into the 
medium on chitosan conduits. So the chitosan conduit can effect the protein 
expression of Schwann cells into the medium or intracellular accumulation. 
 
Conclusions 
Schwann cells grew on chitosan conduit show well attachment and spreading. The 
ECM glycoprotein, Laminin expression is affected by micro-environmental 3D 
construction.
