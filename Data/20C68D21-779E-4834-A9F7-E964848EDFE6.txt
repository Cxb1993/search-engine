隔振墊圈；內容皆是以抑制光碟機的結構振動行為為

機已日漸普遍，市售光碟機之馬達轉速高達
10000RPM，然而在馬達轉速升高的同時，由質量分佈
不均的光碟片所導致之振動效應將會被凸顯放大。由
於振動的產生，光碟機之讀寫行為勢必受到影響，挑
片便是其一現象，更嚴重者，瑕疵之光碟可能因此而
碎裂。為了避免振動所衍生之問題，工程師藉由振動
學、動力學原理設計了不同類型之制振機構以減少光
碟機的振動。這些設計分別利用動態迴旋原理、多自
由度振動特性、增加系統慣性等方法來抑制不平衡質
量之振動，其中吸振器便是利用振動學之原理，藉由
在原系統外加吸振器之方式，達成振動抑制的效果，
能同時具有解決外界的擾動問題與光碟機運轉時所引
起的振動問題。第二年研究便是以使用吸振器為主
體，利用有限元素分析模擬軟體 ANSYS 建立旋轉機
械之虛擬原型，分析真實模型設計參數之效能變化，
以改善旋轉機械之各種振動；第三年是以實驗驗證所
設計之旋轉吸振器是否能有效的抑制碟片的徑向振
動；另外在光碟機上放置麥克風，量測光碟機之聲壓
值，共設計兩種吸振器用於不同轉速範圍的光碟機
下，探討其是否能對光碟機噪音及振動問題有所改善。 
在探討光碟機被動式負剛性隔震器可行性方面，
傳統光碟機隔震器依其元件功能可以簡化為彈簧、阻
尼和質量塊代表其所組成的結構系統，此種結構可以
應用於降低地面震動對於光碟機的位移傳遞率
(Displacement transmissibility)。隔震的效果在震盪頻率
與自然頻率的比值大於 2 時才會發生，且阻尼越小則
隔震效果越好。而當自然頻率與環境震盪頻率相同時
則會產生共振現象，此現象發生時位移傳遞率為最大
值，非但不會減震反而還會將震盪效果放大；由上述
原理得知傳統被動式隔震器之缺點為其受限於結構之
自然頻率大小，隔震器只能對外力頻率大於 2 倍自然
頻率之範圍隔震，而結構自然頻率又受限於整體結構
剛性，故傳統被動式隔振器無法有效地對低頻範圍進
行光碟機隔震為其缺點，而被動式隔震器由於成本低
且不耗能為則為其優點。根據隔震原理，可明白當剛
性越小可適用之隔震範圍越廣，若加入負剛性結構來
降低整體剛性，形成負剛性被動式隔震器，理論上可
適用之隔震頻率範圍則越廣。負剛性形成之基本原理
是利用預壓彈簧提供一種負值之剛性，當負剛性與傳
統正剛性並聯則可得到近似零剛性之結構，由於這種
結構其自然頻率極低，故其隔震之頻率範圍會較傳統
式隔震器大上許多，低頻範圍亦能進行隔震。透過負
剛性與其他設計參數間的關係可調整體結構之剛性降
低至最佳值，連帶地將結構自然頻率降低以增大隔震
之頻率範圍；本計劃於第三年先對於具負剛性光碟機
隔震器進行可行性評估 
    Mizuno[7]等提出將負剛性結構與正剛性結構兩者
結合可應用在隔震系統，其結合類型主要分為兩類，
第一類是將兩者並聯得到極微小的剛性，主要應用在
防止由地面傳遞至平台的震動，第二類則將兩者串聯
得到極大的剛性，主要應用在防止直接加壓在平台上
所造成的震動。且利用實驗證實後者的效果。Lee[8]
等提出震動範圍在 0.5-5 Hz 是對人體最有危害的頻率
範圍，故將負剛性結構應用在汽車座椅的防震上，使
駕駛者得以不受到低頻震盪危害健康，文中利用薄殼
來形成負剛性以減小結構整體剛性，並且達到有效隔
震的目的，令駕駛者得以預防受到低頻震盪所造成的
危害。Robertson[9]等提出永久磁鐵之磁力作用可等效
於正剛性、負剛性的彈簧效應，其中將一磁鐵固定於
隔振器中之上方，利用磁力吸引下方異極磁鐵的形式
可等效於負剛性效應，將一磁鐵固定於隔振器中之下
方，利用磁力排斥上方同極磁鐵的形式可等效於正剛
性效應，而將此兩種效應結合則可得出零剛性效應，
文中還將磁力造成的彈簧效應進行數學分析求出其等
效剛性。Akoun[10]等介紹 3D 空間中平行位於上下方
之長方體磁鐵其相互作用的能量、磁力與等效剛性之
數學分析式，且求出它們與其他參數間之關係式，因
此得以設計出所須之負剛性效果。Nijsse[13]等設計磁
力隔震結構，其特點並非利用一般直接與平台接觸式
的隔震系統，而是利用磁力效果來造成非接觸式之隔
震器，且令整體結構剛性近似於零，而得到低頻的隔
震效果。 
基於上述文獻之研究成果顯示，傳統式隔震器之
優點為其成本低廉，而其缺點則為只能有效地進行高
頻隔震，基於隔震原理只有當外在環境頻率為自然頻
率的 2 倍以上才能有減震效果，故傳統式隔震器不能
有效的在低頻範圍進行隔震；而主動式隔震器卻可有
效的在任何特定頻率範圍進行隔震，但是由於需要能
量進行回饋作用且製造之成本昂貴，故在價格上的考
量成為其缺點。被動式之負剛性隔震器則是綜合上述
兩者之優點，它可利用負剛性結構降低整體剛性，以
至於自然頻率跟著降低，而使可隔震之範圍增大，其
效果連低頻範圍亦能有效減震且其成本耗費低，並不
需能量進行回饋控制，故本計劃第二部分主要研究被
動式負剛性隔震器於光碟機隔振實施的可能性。

負剛性隔震器 
一般對於剛性的定義為施加外力作用於物件使
其產生形變的難易程度。負剛性其物理涵義自然不同
於一般所提之正剛性。當外力作用於正剛性結構時，
結構的作用力與施力方向相反，於是產生一種欲使結
構恢復原狀之特性；當外力作用於負剛性結構時，結
構的作用力與施力方向相同，於是產生一種欲使結構
加深變形之特性。Park[11]等提到負剛性結構可利用能
量法推出等效剛性，負剛性結構如表 9 所示，垂直支
撐之彈簧為正剛性結構，水平彈簧則為此系統之負剛
性結構，圖中質量塊受限只能作單一自由度的上下移
動，並且假設在質量塊移動範圍內，水平彈簧皆為預
壓狀態，則當質量塊遠離平衡點的情況中，負剛性結
構與正剛性結構對質量塊所造成的力量方向相反，正
剛性結構的作用方向是將物件拉回平衡點，負剛性結
構的作用方向則將物件推離平衡點，兩者綜合作用下
會使系統整體剛性減小。本研究為使隔震系統能夠進
行低頻範圍之隔震，加入負剛性結構以降低整體剛性。

FOLLOWER REST DESIGN USING VIBRATION ABSORBERS FOR 
BALL SCREW GRINDING 
Chih Chun Cheng, Fu Ching Wang, and Wen Nan Cheng 
Deparment of Mechanical Engineering, National Chung Cheng University 
Chia-Yi, 621, Taiwan, R.O.C. 
zzs1003@hotmail.com 
Abstract 
A novel design of follower rest used in ball screw grinding machine is proposed. This 
follower rest is designed to be a vibration absorber installed on the grinding wheel holder. The 
vibration absorber is used to suppress the ball screw vibration at the position close to the 
grinding force and thus acts as a ball screw follower rest. Influences of design parameters of the 
absorber on the transient response of the ball screw such as the absorber mass and the absorber 
damping are examined in detail. Bandwidths of the vibration absorber and its interaction with 
the ball screw are formulated using impedance method, which is critical when the rotational 
speed of the grinding wheel may fluctuate during a grinding process. Results show that the 
absorber damping has a negative effect in suppressing the ball screw vibration; however it has 
little influence on the frequency bandwidth. It concludes that the absorber should have little 
damping but larger mass to have a better performance in suppressing the ball screw vibration. 
1. INTRODUCTION 
The grinding wheel used in the ball screw grinding possesses a certain degree of 
imbalance and deviation in roundness due to material inhomogeneity and manufacturing 
tolerance. Even for a carefully balanced grinding wheel, the imbalance and the deviation from a 
perfect circle increase due to the wear of abrasive in a grinding process. This imbalance and 
lack of roundness cause severe ball screw vibration and then results in undesirable surface 
roughness on the ball screw. In order to suppress the excessive vibrations caused by the 
grinding wheel especially on a long ball screw, steady rest are utilized as shown in Fig. 1. They 
are fixed on the machine bed and distributed periodically along the ball screw. The installation 
of these supports is time-consuming especially when one needs to change the ball screw with a 
different length. Moreover, from the energy point of view, the vibration power from the 
grinding wheel is transferred to the machine through the steady rest instead of dissipating it and 
thus one may expect that the grinding machine vibration may increase.  
In this paper, an alternative ball screw support made of vibration absorber is introduced. 
The dynamic vibration absorber is a traditional but effective device to reduce the undesired 
vibration of a machine [1–4]. Generally, the absorber can be regarded as a spring-mass-damper 
(SMD) system and has its best performance in vibration reduction when the excitation 
frequency coincides with its natural frequency. That is, the SMD creates a vibration node on the 
location where it is bonded. Under this circumstance, the machine vibration can be reduced due 
to the node acting as a fixed support for the machine. On the other side, the SMD possesses 
most of the vibration power and avoid it flowing into the machine. Therefore this SMD acts as 
a vibration power absorber. 
Based on the vibration absorber technique, a novel design of ball screw follower rest is 
proposed. The follower rest is designed to be a SMD installed on the grinder wheel holder; and 
its free end or driving point is attached to the ball screw at the location close to the grinding

7. CONCLUSION 
A novel design of follower rest used in ball screw grinding machine is proposed. Installed 
on the grinding wheel holder, the follower rest is designed to be a vibration absorber with its 
driving point attached on the ball screw. It is used to suppress the ball screw vibration at the 
position close to the grinding zone and thus acts as a follower rest. It provides an alternative in 
counteracting the ball screw vibration during a grinding process besides the traditional steady 
rest fixed on the machine bed. Frequency bandwidths of the moving absorber as well as its 
interaction with the ball screw are formulated using impedance method. The available 
frequency bandwidth is critical when the rotational speed of the grinding wheel fluctuates 
during a grinding process. Results show that the damping of the proposed follower rest has a 
negative effect on the vibration reduction of ball screw, but it has little influence on the 
frequency bandwidth. To quantify the proposed follower rest bandwidth, an absorber with a 
mass ratio 1/10 has a bandwidth of 0.3Ω, where Ω is the grinding wheel rotational speed. It 
concludes that the proposed follower rest proved to be useful in suppressing the ball screw 
vibration when the ball screw has a greater length-diameter ratio. Moreover, the proposed 
follower rest should have little damping but larger mass in order to have a better performance 
and large working frequency bandwidth.

三、與會內容與心得 
  為期 5 天(7/6~7/10)的研討會，筆者口頭報告之時間被規劃在第 4 天(7/9)，組
別為 Machinery Noise and Vibration，而題目則為”Follower Rest Design Using 
Vibration Absorbers for Ball Screw Grinding”，主要內容為應用吸振器原理設計一
移動吸振器(Follower rest)，其隨著砂輪研磨而移動，藉此抑制砂輪作用於滾珠導
螺桿上時產生之振動，並探討移動吸振器的參數設計對於振動抑制效果。筆者口
頭報告之場次為 PM04:40~PM06:20，共五場演講，主持人為一南韓教授 Dr. Tae 
Kil Ahn，而筆者為此場次第一個上台演講者，演講過程一切順利，口頭報告後，
在座之學者亦提出一些問題與建議，對於若往後在相關研究領域繼續鑽研時，提
供相當大的幫助。

Vibration、Passive Techniques in Vibration and Acoustics Attenuation、Vibration and 
Control of Smart Material Systems、Modal Analysis 以及 Signal Processing，其中
