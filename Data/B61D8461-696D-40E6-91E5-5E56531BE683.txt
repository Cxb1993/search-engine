4前言
氮化鎵系列材料在光電元件與電子元件的研究上具有極大的潛力與無限的商機。氮化鎵系列材
料具有高的崩潰電壓、良好的熱傳導性、高的電子移動率與高的電子飽和速度，故此類材料極適合
應用於光電及高功率、高溫與高頻操作之元件上。由於目前沒有適當的基板來成長氮化鎵薄膜，使
得界面的晶格匹配相形困難。目前，使用有機金屬化學氣相沉積磊晶法(MOCVD)已經可以將氮化
鎵薄膜成長於藍寶石基板上。藍寶石材料耐高溫，可承受腐蝕性氣體的侵蝕，且容易獲得。但藍寶
石與氮化鎵之間因晶格常數不匹配與熱膨脹係數之差異，導致長晶時產生大量的差排缺陷，影響了
薄膜的品質，間接地影響到元件的性能表現。
氮化鎵磊晶薄膜中最常見的缺陷為沿著 c 軸成長之貫穿式差排（threading dislocations, 如圖一
及圖二所示），其密度約為 1010∼1012 cm-2。由於差排缺陷在半導體元件中經常扮演少數載子殺手的
角色，對於發光效率及載子遷移率有很負面的影響。在典型的異質結構系統中，異質介面差排缺陷
的存在，是造成元件退化的主要原因。在已退化之元件上作顯微組織分析時，經常可清晰地觀察到
缺陷差排的產生。這些差排缺陷一般會產生在異質介面附近，這是由於該介面處是屬於較不穩定且
具有高應變能的區域，因此任何的外在誘因，如溫度提升熱能會促使差排的形成，以降低整體在異
質介面上的總應變能，這些差排便嚴重地影響半導體元件的功能。由於這些存在晶體內部之差排，
是很活躍的載子陷阱（traps）及再結合中心（recombination centers）。它們的存在會大幅地減低了
少數載子(minority carrier)壽命，對於發光元件而言，差排的存在會降低元件之發光效率;對於電子
元件而言，差排的存在會使載子受到散射機制(scattering mechanism)的影響而降低載子遷移率。除
了對少數載子佈下陷阱外，差排也提供載子快速擴散的途徑。針對一具特定載子分佈的元件而言，
載子沿著差排作快速擴散或是載子偏析(segregation)於差排附近，均會導致元件設計上載子分佈的
錯亂。差排型態之中，又以邊緣差排(edge threading dislocation)影響元件功能最甚，其應力場的分
佈在晶體內部可能促使載子依附在邊緣差排週遭，造成元件之特性與功能變差。因此，降低氮化鎵
磊晶薄膜中的貫穿式差排密度是製作高特性及高效能氮化鎵半導體元件的首要目標。

研究方法
我們使用金屬有機化學氣相沉積系統在藍寶石基板(0001)面上成長磊晶層。TMAl, TMGa 和
NH3 分別為鋁、鎵和氮原子的來源。首先我們先將基板置入充滿氫氣的成長腔體內，溫度控制在
1120℃，以去除基板上的汙染源，接下來將成長溫度降至 600℃ 成長 30 nm 的低溫氮化鎵緩衝層。
接著於 1050℃ 成長1.75 μm高溫氮化鎵磊晶層。然後在 500℃ 成長 100 nm 低溫氮化鋁鎵中間置
入層(鋁: 30%，鎵:70%)。最後成長氮化鋁鎵(鋁: 25%，鎵:75%)/氮化鎵異質結構，其中氮化鎵磊晶
層的厚度為 0.25 μm，氮化鋁鎵磊晶層的厚度為 20 nm。另外亦磊晶成長一不含低溫氮化鋁鎵中間
置入層的對照組結構以茲比較。圖三為試片磊晶結構圖。為了確定磊晶結構，我們使用二次離子質
譜儀解析試片的縱深分布，如圖四所示。由圖四鋁原子的訊號可準確判別結構中每一磊晶層之厚
度，含量比例與分布。

8結果與討論
(1) 以低溫中間置入層結構改善其上氮化鎵磊晶薄膜之製備與特性研究:
圖七顯示以低溫氮化鋁鎵中間置入層製作氮化鋁鎵/氮化鎵異質接面(即高溫氮化鎵/低溫氮化
鋁鎵/高溫氮化鎵三明治結構)之穿透式電子顯微鏡截面影像圖。由圖一可清楚觀察到，大部分差排
式缺陷幾乎都被阻擋在低溫氮化鋁鎵下，而沒有向上竄升至上層的高溫氮化鎵磊晶層。可能的原因
如下:
1. 由於低溫成長無法提供足夠的動能，使得氮化鋁鎵形成奈米尺度的 mosaic 結構，其 grain
boundary 能提供足夠數目的缺陷截止點使差排缺陷終止於此處。由於成長完氮化鋁鎵後機台會先
升溫，以成長後續的高溫氮化鎵層。此升溫之過程會使氮化鋁鎵層出現〝再結晶(recrystallization)〞
的現象，而將大部分缺陷截止點覆蓋於下方，並提供一較平坦的表面以利後續高溫氮化鎵之成長。
2. 於上層高溫氮化鎵與低溫氮化鋁鎵界面處，差排缺陷可能發生彎曲的現象，差排缺陷間彼此由
於應力產生抵銷(annihilation)與減化(de-multiplication)交互作用，因而減低差排缺陷往上竄升之機
率。

圖十五 圖十六
圖十六為元件的電容-電壓曲線圖。此曲線同樣與場效電晶體的操作原理一致。在小偏壓時，
元件電容值保持固定，此時電容的來源為處於二維電子氣通道中的載子。隨著偏壓的增加，電容值
緩慢下降。當偏壓小於截止偏壓時(V<Vp)，偏壓逐漸跨壓於負電極，此時的電容為負電極空乏區所
主導。隨著偏壓慢慢增大，此空乏區亦逐漸擴張，往下穿越二維電子氣通道，而擴散至下層之氮化
鎵通道層，關閉了整個二維電子氣通道，造成電容的急遽下降。當偏壓大於截止偏壓時(V>Vp)，電
容又回復為負電極空乏區主導，此時主要受到通道中存在的背景施子(background donor)所影響。而
吾人亦可由圖十六可看出，使用高溫氮化鎵/低溫氮化鋁鎵/高溫氮化鎵三明治結構之元件其閘極控
制能力優於對照組元件，此現象再度與電流-電壓曲線的結果前後呼應。
圖十七顯示兩組元件於外加負偏壓下之電場分布圖。於金屬-半導體-金屬結構中，吸收層中的
電場強度會隨著距離表面(電極)愈遠而減少，在吸收層深處產生的光載子需要傳送更長的距離或時
間才能到達電極處被收集(如圖十七(a))。另一方面，在高溫氮化鎵/低溫氮化鋁鎵/高溫氮化鎵三明
治結構中，電場大部分被侷限在低溫氮化鋁鎵中間置入層以上的區域，又因為低溫成長之氮化鋁鎵
其電阻率相當大，造成於兩電極間流動的載子被迫侷限於一個較窄且電場強度較強的區域，增加了
載子側向的流動速度，因而能預期其光電流值將提高，進而提高元件之光響應度。

電晶體與紫外光檢測器研發成果名稱

成果歸屬機構
崑山科技大學 發明人
(創作人)
張品全、李凱璿、張守進
（中文）本研究中，吾人以有機金屬化學氣相磊晶法成長高溫氮
化鎵/低溫氮化鋁鎵/高溫氮化鎵三明治結構，應用於異質接面高電
子移動率電晶體與紫外光感測器。此種新式結構比起傳統的異質
接面結構於電晶體特性上有更高的汲極電流與崩潰電壓，更低的
閘極電流、二維電子氣濃度與轉導值。這提供了一簡單的方式成
長高品質的氮化鎵薄膜，以應用於高頻、高功率與低雜訊元件。
同時，此結構亦可用於製作高效率金半金紫外光感測器，元件的
NEP=2.65×10-10 W，D*=1.74×109 cmHz0.5W-1。由於其提供了一電
場強度較強的區域予光載子的流動，使元件之光響應大幅增加。
此結構結合光元件與電元件，可進而實現光電積體電路化的目標。
（200-500 字）
技術說明
（英文）AlGaN/GaN heterostructure high electron mobility transistors
(HEMTs) based on HT-GaN/LT-AlGaN/HT-GaN structure by
metal-organic vapor phase epitaxy have been investigated and
compared with the standard HEMT. The new configuration
outperformed the one without in several aspects, exhibiting higher
drain current and breakdown field, lower gate and buffer leakage
current, and larger two-dimensional electron gas concentration and
transconductance. This provide a simple method to grow high quality
epitaxial GaN for high-stress, low-noise, high-frequency, and
high-power HEMT applications. In the meanwhile, larger
photoresponsivity can be achieved due to the enhanced electric field
strength by utilizing such a structure to the application in
metal-semiconductor-metal photodetectors. The NEP and D* for the
photodetectors with a sandwich structure are 2.65×10-10 W and 1.74×
109 cmHz0.5W-1, respectively. This structure combine the
optoelectronic and electronic devices to realize the goal of
optoelectronic integrated circuits (OEIC).

 Effect of Growth Temperature on the Indium Incorporation 
 in InGaN Epitaxial Films 
P. C. Chang1, a, C. L. Yu2, Y. W. Jahn2, , S. J. Chang2,b, K. H. Lee3  
1Department of Electro-Optical Engineering, Kun Shan University, Yong-Kang 71003, TAIWAN

Keywords: InGaN, metalorganic chemical vapor deposition (MOCVD)   
Abstract. InxGa1-xN epilayers have been grown by metalorganic chemical vapor deposition 
(MOCVD) at different temperatures between 740°C to 830°C. The thickness of InGaN film is 50nm 
for all samples. The incorporation of indium is found to increase with decreasing grown temperature. 
The optical properties and film quality of the samples have been investigated by photoluminescence 
(PL) system and X-ray diffraction (XRD). The Full Width at Half Maximum (FWHM) of PL and 
XRD decreases with increasing the grown temperature. We also found that the peak emission of PL 
shifts with changing the grown temperature. The effect of temperature on the film properties was 
determined. This understanding will lead to better quality control of the optoelectronic devices.  
Introduction 
III-nitride Semiconductor has attracted much interest in both optoelectronic and electronic devices 
[1-2]. It is well known that the direct band-gaps of GaN, AlN, and InN are 3.4eV, 6.2eV and 0.8eV, 
respectively. Therefore, the wavelength of the alloys and heterostructure of these materials can be 
turned from the visible to the ultraviolet regions. Among the III-nitride compound semiconductors, 
InGaN thin film has played an important role in optical devices due to its strong band to band 
emission efficiency and its bandgap tenability from 0.8eV to 3.4eV[3]. With the emission in the 
blue-green spectral region, a reliable red emission from InGaN films would make it possible to 
realize high-brightness full color displays or white LEDs using only one material system. Even 
though the growth of InGaN with high indium content was possible at a low temperature of around 
500°C, the relatively large lattice mismatch with GaN have prevented the growth of high quality 
InGaN thin films by metal-organic chemical vapor deposition (MOCVD). Besides, InGaN thin films 
are needed for fabricating electronic devices such as GaN/InGaN (AlGaN/InGaN) heterostructure 
field effect transistiors (HFETs), high-speed electronic devices and operating them at high 
temperature and hostile environments [4-5]. Therefore, the attempts to increase the efficiency of 
indium incorporation without structural deterioration of InGaN thin films have been reported by 
several groups [6-7]. However, most researches are dedicated to the studies on the optical 
characterization of this material system. So, it is necessary to further investigate the relationship 
between growth conditions and structural properties of InGaN films. In this study, we investigated 
the effects of growth temperature of InGaN films grown by MOCVD. X-ray diffraction (XRD), 
photoluminescence (PL), and the atomic force microscope (AFM) were used to evaluate the 
characterizations of these epilayers. This study provides evidence to show that the growth 
temperature plays an important role in the incorporation of indium in InGaN thin films grown by 
MOCVD. The indium composition of the InGaN was estimated by x-ray diffraction analysis.

(英文) AlGaN/GaN heterostructure high electron mobility transistors (HEMTs) based on HT-
GaN/LT-AlGaN/HT-GaN structure by metal-organic vapor phase epitaxy have been 
investigated and compared with the standard HEMT. The new configuration 
outperformed the one without in several aspects, exhibiting higher drain current and 
breakdown field, lower gate and buffer leakage current, and larger two-dimensional 
electron gas concentration and transconductance. This provide a simple method to grow 
high quality epitaxial GaN for high-stress, low-noise, high-frequency, and high-power 
HEMT applications. In the meanwhile, larger photoresponsivity can be achieved due to 
the enhanced electric field strength by utilizing such a structure to the application in 
metal-semiconductor-metal photodetectors. The NEP and D* for the photodetectors with 
a sandwich structure are 2.65×10-10 W and 1.74×109 cmHz0.5W-1, respectively.
