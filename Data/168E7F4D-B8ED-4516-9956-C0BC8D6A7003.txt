二、英文摘要 (Abstract) 
The three-dimensional particle size 
segregation for binary mixtures of 6 different 
size ratios in a rotating drum were studied. The 
information of the interior of the particle bed was 
obtained by “freezing” the bed, slicing and 
image analysis. Except for the binary mixture of 
particle size ratio 2.01, the size of the core 
increases with the increasing particle size ratio at 
the same bed depth in the range studied. The size 
and shape of the segregated structure is a not a 
simple function of the particle size ratio although 
the particles of the binary mixture are only 
different in sizes. The degree of mixing at the 
bed surface and close to the cylindrical column 
wall can be realised by the drift-diffusional 
model and is greatly influenced by the particle 
dynamic angle of repose. 
Keywords: Segregation, Drum, drift-diffusional 
model 
三、本文 
1. Introduction 
The rotating drums are commonly used for 
solids mixing, drying, and granulation operations 
in chemical, pharmaceutical and food industries. 
However, most of the work addressed the 
granular behaviours at the surface of the bed 
from the end views (Khahar et al., 2001; Hill et 
al., 2005) or from the top views (Choo et al., 
1998; Charles et al., 2006). The particle 
distributions/behaviour inside the bed were 
either less discussed, or indirectly obtained from 
radioactive experiments using the magnetic 
resonance imaging (MRI) techniques (Nakagawa 
et al., 1997; Kawaguchi, 2010), the positron 
emission particle tracking (PEPT) techniques 
(Ding et al., 2001; Ingram et al., 2005; Kuo et al., 
2005a) or computational simulations (Rapaport, 
2007; Arntz et al., 2008; Iwasaki et al., 2010). In 
this work, binary mixture of different size ratios 
was mixed in a specially designed rotating drum. 
The internal structure of the drum was directly 
investigated and the three-dimensional bed 
segregation structure was constructed. 
There are several reasons causing the 
granular mixture to mix or to segregate in the 
drum. When particles with different particle sizes, 
shapes, densities, roughness or elasticity are 
mixed in a drum, they would tend to segregate 
(Williams and Khan, 1973; Kuo et al., 2006a). 
When binary granular mixture with different 
sizes is mixed in a rotating drum, high 
concentration pure smaller particles are collected 
in the bed centre and larger particles are in the 
periphery after several revolutions. Following 
the formation of the segregation core, alternative

For the case shown in Figure 2(b) and 
Figure 3(b), the difference of the angles of 
repose of the two particles cannot be neglected: 
the particles at the large-particle rich band are 
more easily to roll down on the surface of the 
small-particle rich band at the upper part of the

drift currents of the two type particles are shown

the diffusional current occurs from the particles 
of higher concentrations to lower concentration, 
the diffusional currents are shown as the two thin 
arrows in Figure 3(b). As the size ratio and hence 
the difference of the angle of repose of the binary 
mixture increases, the diffusional current is 
suppressed and the drift current is enhanced. 
 
4. Conclusion 
Segregation of binary glass beads with 6 
size ratios (SR) in a horizontally rotating drum 
was studied. The three-dimensional segregation 
structure was developed using the gel-slicing and 
imaging-editing method. The shapes of the core 
are different for the binary mixture with different 
SR, including a small bump next to the middle 
segregation band, an axe shape and a 
hemi-sphere shape. Except for the binary 
mixture of SR=2.01 with the unique axe core 
shape, the size of the core increases with the 
increasing SR value at the same bed depth. The 
degree of mixing at the surface and at 4 cm deep 
in the bed can be explained by the

四、計畫成果自評 
本研究應用六種不同粒徑比之二元玻璃珠研究滾動鼓中的偏析結構，並應用影像分析技術
建立三維偏析結構，且應用 Savage drift-diffusional model 成功說明三維偏析結構形成原因。應用
三維結構，找出無(弱)偏析核之粒徑比結構，可應用於無偏析核之銅-氧化鋁-銅功能性梯度材料
製備，達成原擬定之計畫目標。目前的研究成果已經投稿於 AIChE Journal，應用偏析方式形成
之銅-氧化鋁-銅偏析商業化功能性梯度材料也在整理論文中，將投稿於 Powder Technology 期
刊。目前本實驗室於新一年的國科會計畫中，進一步應用 CFD 模擬動態偏析系統，以能初步模
擬出偏析之形成機制，利於相關產業實務推廣之運用。

本屆研討會於印度新德里的 Ashok Hotel 舉行，第四屆亞洲粉粒體技術會議為延續已經
舉辦於泰國曼谷、馬來西亞檳城、中國北京的前三屆的亞洲粉粒體技術會議。此會議由印度
金屬協會(The Indian Institute of Metals)與印度礦業工程協會(Indian Institute of 
Mineral Engineers)主辦，大會主席為 TATA 集團的 Dr. Pradip。屬於中型研討會，參加人
數約四百人，發表論文約兩百五十篇。所參與之國家，包含日本、英國、美國、韓國、台灣、
泰國與新加坡等二十個國家，下屆會議(2012)將於新加坡舉行。本次會議定調為創新的粉粒
體技術(Innovations in Particle Technology)，特色為大會演講多達 9個，邀請演講多達
65 個!研究主題分為 13 個。 
大會演講中，有幾個題目本人深感興趣：日本 Prof. Y. Fukumoi 於大會演講中，說明
癌症用藥的粉體設計要點，對於奈米粒子的粒徑控制極為重要，可分辨正常與癌症細胞；英
國 Professor R. Williams 於大會演講中，以商業化觀點設計高產量的乳液與膠囊，著重於
噴嘴的開口設計；澳洲的 Prof. R. Batterham，以能量消耗觀點，他指出全球約 2-3%的能
源應用於礦業粉碎製程，礦業發展的新方向，或許以有效礦石研磨的節能方向為重點研究仍
大有可為；美國佛大的 Prof. B. Moudgil，以醫藥用粉體研究與醫學中心合作為例，說明粉
體研究需重視團隊合作之整合型計劃；美國麻州理工學院的 Prof. F. Stellacci 以創新研
究方法，毋需昂貴之 AFM 儀器設備，即可得到物體表面微結構。在生物奈米研究上，印度的
Prof. D. Khakhar、Prof. M. Sastry 與加拿大的 Prof. G. Walker 也都給予的精采的大會
演講。總觀，本屆的粉體技術會議以生醫用粉體設計、奈米粉體應用為主要課題，在能源應
用、介面科學也多所著墨。在本人所參與的研究口頭報告中，有許多研究學者，以 CFD 與 DEM
模擬約佔 60%，實驗方面約佔 30%。本實驗室研究方向亦兼顧 CFD 模擬與實驗，符合趨勢。
