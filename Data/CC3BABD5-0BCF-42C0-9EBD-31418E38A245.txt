2A Study on Some Key Issues Concerning the Mechanisms of
Eutrophication in Reservoirs
Key words：reservoir, eutrophication, limiting factors, nutrient, light
Abstract
Due to watershed development and high nutrient input, reservoirs in Taiwan are
commonly suffered from severe eutrophication. The problem can be worse in southern
Taiwan due to high water temperature and intense solar radiation in this region.
According to Liebig’s Law of the Minimum, algal growth is limited by the most scarce
resource available (limiting factor). Possible limiting factors for algal growth in fresh
water lakes and reservoirs are nutrients, typically phosphorous or nitrogen, and light.
Reservoirs in southern Taiwan are characterized as nutrient rich and turbid, leading to
the high probability of light limitation. In this study, the limiting factors for algal
growth were investigated for two major reservoirs in southern Taiwan, Tsen-wen and
Nan-hua. Preliminary evaluations using historical water quality data indicated that
algal biomass didn’t corelate wel with either phosphorous or nitrogen in Nan-hua
reservoir. Algal biomass in Tsen-wen reservoir correlated well with nitrogen, indicating
a possible nitrogen limiting situation. Bioassay using samples spiked with two different
levels of nitrogen and phosphorous confirmed that no nutrient limitation has occurred
except nitrogen in Tsen-wen reservoir. Another bioassay was also conducted to study
the response of algal biomass under different light intensities and photo periods.
Optimal light intensity for algal growth was found to be 393μmol quanta m-2 s-1 for
both reservoirs, and that a minimum daily light exposure of 3-hours was required for the
maintenance of algal population. Since both reservoirs are turbid with photic depths of
well blow 0.5 meter, compared with a characteristic mixing depth of 2-3 meters, algal
growth in both reservoirs are expected to experience strong light limitation as indicated
by the results of bioassay.

c. 水庫光照限制探討
湖泊與水庫藻類隨著蓄水在混合層上下循環，其接受光照僅限於在光照層
停留的時期，因此水體中藻類每日接受的光照量與該水體光照層及混合層的相
對深度有關。本研究光照週期試驗結果顯示，兩水庫都須每日 6小時以上光照
才有藻類數量增長的情況。南化與曾文兩水庫有限的調查數據顯示，水庫混合
層深度在 2-3 m之間，而由於蓄水混濁，光照層厚度大都在 0.5m以下，因此
藻類每日接受的光照時數，僅表面日照之 1/4至 1/6。以夏季最長日照時數 14
小時為例，兩水庫藻類暴露於光線下的每日時數僅 2.3至 3.5小時。根據光照
週期試驗結果，藻類在此光照條件下僅能維持其既有數量，無法有明顯的增
長，呈現光照限制的情況。
四、結論與建議
優養水體的浮游植物量主要受到營養鹽與光照因子的控制，本研究透過長期
水質監測數據的分析以及藻類生物試驗，探討曾文以及南化兩水庫可能的藻類生
長限制因子，研究取得之主要結論包括：
一、 曾文與南化兩水庫葉綠素長期平均濃度均由上游往下游遞減，曾文水庫葉綠
素濃度範圍在 6.4至4.4 μg/L之間，南化水庫在 3.6至3.2 μg/L之間。根據
US EPA單一參數優養指標判定，曾文水庫為普養，南化兩水庫則為貧養狀
態。
