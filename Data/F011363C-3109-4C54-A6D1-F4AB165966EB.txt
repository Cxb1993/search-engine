英文摘要
To prevent hypertrophy of muscle and bone due to long-time staying in the bed,
patients with bone fracture or total joint arthroplasty were usually encouraged to
exercise as soon as possible. However, in clinical rehabilitation, there were still no
suitable criteria to evaluate the effect of these rehabilitation processes. This study is
trying to establish a biofeedback rehabilitation system. This biofeedback system
would help patients to active control the weight bearing during rehabilitation process.
This research will implement and design the hardware of biofeedback system.
The system will be calibrated by motion analysis and ground reaction force
measurement. In the project, it will divide two groups of treatments that the loading is
subject’s weight of 1/3 and half. It can provide the quantization results of
rehabilitation the femur fracture patient for clinical rehabilitated physician rapidly
during the treatment course and rehabilitate efficiently with alarm for patient in the
overload condition.
This biofeedback system can be more and more completed through above
researches. Eventually, the system will be commercialized to advance the
development of rehabilitation engineering and establish the landmark in internal
medical industry.
Keyword: bone fracture, quantum rehabilitation, biofeedback, trauma

而腳底壓力量測已有相當多學者加以研究，如：1985年 Soames [9,10]以鞋
底埋設 15個壓力轉換器藉以量測腳底不同區域之壓力分佈情形。而在 1987年，
Cavanaugh [20]將腳底壓力的分佈情形詳加描述(如圖 3.1)結果發現在赤足的情形
下，腳跟受力佔全部的 60％，中足區佔約 8％，前足區佔 28％，而腳指的部分
佔 8％；而腳跟的最大腳底壓力(如圖 3.2)約為前足區的 2.6倍。2001年Wearing [17]
將足底分成多個不同的區域分別量測，發現有足部缺陷的病患其腳底壓力分佈情
形與正常人明顯不同。雖然腳底壓力的研究很早便開始，但是多半是在赤足的情
況下量測，無法真正表示病患在日常生活行走時的足底壓力變化，同時在穿鞋與
赤足的情況下足底壓力的分佈亦會有所不同，故近年來的學者大多針對穿鞋後的
足底壓力加以研究，同時可幫助不同使用者達到舒適且避免傷害的功能。在 2001
年 Lawless [18]針對足部骨折及骨折後穿戴輔具的病患分析其穿鞋後足底壓力的
不同，結果發現穿戴輔具後，病患的足底壓力可較平均分佈，避免負荷過度集中。
此外，根據 Barnett[19]的研究指出，利用 Pedar足底壓力量測系統與力板量測的
結果互相比較，印證利用足底壓力量測系統所得到之結果不僅與力板分析之地面
反作用力有一致性，並且可以量測出更多步態中的暫態參數。由以上的文獻回顧
中可發現，足底壓力的量測將有助於分析病患的下肢載重能力的改變，同時亦可
提供復健療程中之所需。
