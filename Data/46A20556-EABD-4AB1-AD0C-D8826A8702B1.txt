〈II〉 
Abstract 
The study is to enhance the low temperature dyeing properties between reactive 
dyes and polypropylene(abbre. PP) fabrics. At first year, using different plasma 
conditions, cross-linking and acrylamide(abbre. AAm) concentration and UV 
exposure time to deal with polypropylene fabrics. The hydrophilic property and 
dyeing ability of polypropylene fabrics treated by plasma and grafted with AAm were 
significantly improved. The second year, utilized above grafting technology, the AAm 
was replaced by containing diamine compound ,i.e. 2,6-diaminoanthraquinone, 
grafting on PP fabrics, and compared the effects of physical and dyeing properties 
with AAm. The results reveal that the grafting ratio and dyeing ability were increased 
with increase of treating power and time of plasma and concentration of monomer. 
The K/S value of dyed PP fabrics were improved by grafting with AAm and 
2,6-diaminoanthraquinone, the effect of the letter was better than the former. From the 
contact angle test, we can find the sample treated by plasma and grafting with the 
AAm and 2,6-diaminoanthraquinone, the hydrophilic properties of PP fabrics were 
obviously increased. Strength of modified PP decreased with the power and time of 
plasma. The surface of PP fabrics treated by plasma and grafting have been observed 
in SEM microscopy. In addition, the strength of modified PP fabrics after cross 
linking using UV-curing method were increased . N atoms were obviously appeared 
on surface of modified PP grafted by AAm and 2,6-diaminoanthraquinone in ESCA 
analysis . That reveal the amine groups have been grafted on surface of PP fabrics and 
are of great advantage to dye with reactive dyes at low temperature. 
 
Keywords: Polypropylene、UV curing graft polymerization、Plasma、Acrylamide、   
2,6- diaminoanthraquinone、Cross-linking agent

第二章  理論  
2-1 電漿處理理論 
電漿稱為物質的第四態。電子在電場的加速後，與氣體分子或原子產生非
彈性碰撞使其產生離子化反應、解離反應或者是被激發至激發態。此團由電子、
離子、激發態粒子、自由基及中性氣體分子所組成的部份離子化氣體，即為電
漿。 利用帶有能量的離子化氣體，與材料表面相互作用使表面產生刻蝕或化學
斷鍵使其產生活性基[6-8] 。 
2-2 電漿誘導接枝理論 
電漿後 PP 針織布表面的自由基與空氣中的氧會形成過氧化物，再經由熱
處理或氧化還原劑的作用，使得過氧化物的氧原子與氧原子鍵結(O-O)斷裂形成
自由基，以進行單體接枝[7,8]。

(A)第 1 年改變 AAm 濃度及 UV 光照射時間對織物 K/S 值之影響 
以氬氣、150W、5min、壓力 0.32mbar 電漿、Efactive Red 5BS o.w.s 0.5%
為處理條件，對 PP 針織布進行處理以探討不同交鏈劑種類和濃度及 UV 光照
射時間對接枝率及 K/S 值之影響。由表 9 得知，於未添加交鏈劑時，其接枝率
與 K/S 值會隨著 UV 光照射時間的增長進而提升，且照射時間為 20min 時達到
最佳的效果。因 UV 光照射時間至 20min 時 AAm 已反應完全所以接枝率和 K/S
值已無增加。而添加水性 PU 交鏈劑會隨著交鏈劑濃度和 UV 光照射時間的增
長而提升其接枝率和 K/S 值，當交鏈劑濃度為 2wt% UV 光照射時間為 25min
時有最佳成效，是由於增加反應時間能使水性 PU 交鏈劑反應更為完整。使用
NMBA 其接枝率和 K/S 值也隨著交鏈劑濃度和 UV 光照射時間的增加而提升，
當交鏈劑濃度為 1.5wt%、UV 光照射 25min 時可得最佳的效果，但 NMBA 添
加過多會使得聚合物易脆裂剝落，以致降低接枝率進而 K/S 值也下降。

由 ESCA 測試結果證明，經由蒽醌接枝處理後，同樣有氮元素的產生，證
明單體接枝於纖維表面。
