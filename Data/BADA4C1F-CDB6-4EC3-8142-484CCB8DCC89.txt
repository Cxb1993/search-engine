Laser Capture Microdissection (LCM) is a technique that permits rapid and reliable 
procurement of pure population of cells from tissue sections. It is a powerful tool to analyze 
the DNA, RNA, and some protein in molecular medicine. The study follows as the plan which 
is working in 2004 to design and create a new type of LCM using near filed fiber probe. The 
system successfully uses an IR laser injected through the fiber probe to heat a plastic film 
(EVA) placed above a nano section of the sample. It has been capable to capture the nano 
particles monolayer by the EVA film melted by the laser beam from near field fiber probe. We 
try to apply the system to the capture of bio-ample, and newly develop the key material in 
LCM, that is EVA film. Therefore we complete two targets in the plan; one is to make the 
EVA film with its mount in different thickness, the other is to take the application of the new 
type of LCM system into biotechnology, such as bacteria.  
 
Keywords: Laser Capture Microdissection (LCM), ethylene vinyl acetate (EVA)
