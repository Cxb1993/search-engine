中文摘要 
本計畫考量在 IEEE 802.16m OFDMA Frame
架構下，提出一  Downlink Burst 排程機制 : 
Transmission time Interval Grouping and Exchange 
Rules (TIGER) Scheme 規 劃 IEEE 802.16m 
Downlink Subframe 中 Default Transmission Time 
Interval (TTI)之使用。希望藉由 TIGER 的適當規
劃可以提升 Downlink Subframe 資源利用率。由於
不當的分配與決定 TTI 會造成嚴重的外部碎裂問
題，同時亦會增加 Advanced MAP (A-MAP) 
Control Overhead 而造成 Downlink Subframe 資源
利用率下降，因此，TIGER 藉由調整 Default TTI
的配置位置，降低外部碎裂之問題，同時亦可降
低嚴重 A-MAP Overhead 問題。 
關鍵詞- IEEE 802.16m, 正交分頻多工存取, 傳輸時間間隔, 
全球互通微波存取 
Abstract 
This project proposed a Transmission time 
Interval (TTI) Grouping and Exchange Rules 
(TIGER) scheme to allocate the downlink bandwidth 
in the IEEE 802.16m OFDMA systems. Because 
inappropriate TTI allocations will degrade the 
network throughput, the goals of this proposed 
scheme is to increase the bandwidth utilization as 
well as to decrease Advanced MAP (A-MAP) 
control overhead in the downlink subframe. The 
simulation results show that TIGER outperforms 
other related work in the external fragmentation 
problem and the Advanced MAP (A-MAP) control 
overhead.  
Keywords-IEEE 802.16m, OFDMA, Resource Allocation 
Algorithm, Transmission Time Interval (TTI), WiMAX。 
1. 前言與研究目的 
隨著高速多媒體服務的發達與無線通訊技術
的快速成長，無線語音服務、資料通訊及各項行
動影音多媒體的需求也如雨後春筍般地增加。然
而目前的無線區域網路 (Wireless Local Area 
networks, WLANs)面臨涵蓋範圍不足及傳輸速率
不足等瓶頸，此外，在 WLANs 環境下，對於後
置網路(Backhaul Networks)的資料傳輸須藉由有
線網路的支援，因此，無線網路業者尚須額外負
擔有線網路之建置成本。有鑑於此，WiMAX 無
線寬頻網路逐漸成為新一代無線網路架構的新興
寵兒[1] [2] [3]。 
一個 IEEE 802.16m 網路是由基地台(Advance 
Base Station, ABS) 與多個移動裝置 (Advance 
Mobile Station, AMS)所組成。ABS 集中式管理整
體網路頻寬資源，網路頻寬會以時間的方式進行
切割成數個訊框 (Frame)，圖 1 所示為 IEEE 
802.16m 定義之訊框(Frame)架構，該訊框是由數
個 OFDMA Symbol Time 與 Subchannel 所形成的
二維空間。依據不同的資料傳輸方向以時間的方
式 區 分 成 Downlink Subframe 與 Uplink 
Subframe，AMS 會在 Downlink Subframe 接收來
自 ABS 傳輸之資料且會在 Uplink Subframe 傳送
資料給 ABS，由於本計畫探討 Downlink 資料之傳
輸與頻寬之分配，因此，爾後本計畫會針對
Downlink Subframe 進行詳細之說明。 
Burst 為 ABS 配置給單一 AMS 在 Frame 中的
基本傳輸與接收資料之單位。因為，每個 AMSs
在不同的 Subchannels 會因為環境中的障礙物而
導致訊號溢散，間接造成每個 AMSs 在不同
Subchannel 之間有著相異的雜訊比，導致各個能
用以紀錄 Downlink Burst 的位置與傳輸時所需的
Burst Profile，這些重要的 Downlink 排程資訊
Subchannel 所支援的調變技術不盡相同。由於不
當分配與決定 Burst 位置將會造成嚴重的資源浪
費，同時亦會降低整個 Downlink Subframe 的頻寬

增方向在單一 Subchannel 上進行配置，直到滿足
一個 AAI Subframe 的寬度(6 個 OFDMA Symbol

Burst 的調變為符合最低調變，而使用低調變進行
資料傳輸。 
3. 問題描述 
3.1 Internal Fragmentation Problem 
由於每個 AMS 的 Downlink 頻寬需求有所不
同，所產生對應的 Burst 大小也不盡相同，但因需
要符合 IEEE 802.16m 規格定義的矩型配置原則，
ABS 會配置超過實際需求量大小的矩型空間，如
圖 4 中所標示 Internal Fragmentation 區塊所示。以
Burst 1 為例，內部碎裂的空間該 Burst 由於已經
滿足需求區塊大小，不須再使用，而其他 Burst
也無法 Overlap 使用，就造成了頻寬的浪費，本計
畫 定 義 此 一 問 題 為 內 部 碎 裂 問 題 (Internal 
Fragmentation Problem, IFP)。  
針對 Default TTI 資源的配置，由於資源配置
被侷限在一個 AAI Subframe 中，因此，因內部碎
裂所造成之 Slot 浪費數量將會少於單位 AAI 
Subframe 的 Symbol Time，換句話說，該 Burst
所造成之浪費數量會少於 6 Slots。而嚴重的內部
碎裂將會大幅度的降低 Downlink 頻寬之利用
率，進而降低網路效能，因此，一個設計優良
Downlink Burst 排程機制必須將問題納入考量。

圖 5、外部碎裂問題。 
不當的 Burst 配置，除了會產生上述的內部碎裂情
況，亦會有外部碎裂問題之產生。如圖 5 所示，
由於每個 Burst 都希望在最好調變的 Subchannels
上傳輸，所以可用空間空間。若是另有一個 Burst 
8 欲配置於 Frame 中，雖然剩餘可用空間的資源
區塊總合符合 Burst 8 的需求量，但是，由於矩型
配置的限制，導致 Burst 8 無法配置於 Frame 中而
造成資源之浪費，此資源浪費情況，在本計畫定
義 為 外 部 碎 裂 問 題 (External Fragmentation 
Problem, EFP)。倘若能將可用空間聚集在特定
Subchannels，且可以滿足此配置需求，使得原先
不能配置的 Burst 變得可配置其中，這將可大大提
升網路的資源利用率。 
解決外部碎裂的問題，直覺的作法是將 Burst
分割為數個大小不一的矩形配置，使用分割的方
式增加排入 Frame 中的機會。雖然這樣的做法暫
時可以解決外部碎裂的問題，但每個 Burst 都需要
使用 Assignment IEs 定義配置位置與調變等資
訊，而每個 A-MAP 內有越多的 Assignment IEs
時，A-MAP Control Overhead 如此將增加。由於
A-MAP 的宣告位置是在每個 AAI Subframe 之
中，若是有過多的 A-MAP 時，反而會讓 Burst 減
少可用空間配置資料。此情況使得更多 Burst 需要
分割為更細小的區塊來填補可用空間，以減少外
部碎裂問題，不僅帶來新的 A-MAP Control 
Overhead 問題，更加重外部碎裂的情況。所以在
進行資源配置時，還需要考慮 A-MAP 使用量問
題。

圖 8、Bucket 配置方式。 
 
由於 ABS 會基於每個 AMS 所回傳不同的
Channel Quality Information (CQI)，決定合適的調
變與編碼(Modulation and Coding Schemes, MCSs)
機制傳送資料給各個 AMS，所以每個 AMS 皆期
望可使用較好的 MCSs 進行資料傳輸。因為使用
高調變進行配置資源時所使用的 Slot 數量會少於
使用低調變的方式配置，也就是說同樣資料量大
小，若該 Burst 的資料若能安排在能夠使用較高調
變的 Subchannel 上進行傳輸時，其單一 Slot 能夠
傳輸較多的資料。 
因此，TIGER 優先考量資料需求量較大的
AMS 傳送，其原因在於該需求量較大的 Burst 能
夠 有 高 的 機 會 安 排 在 可 支 援 較 高 調 變 的
Subchannel 上進行傳輸，此配置方式不但可以提
高 Subframe 利用率，還可以避免使用低調變傳送
所增加額外的空間浪費。而對於每個 AMS 連續可
用高調變 Subchannels 數量不一的情況，會優先配
置較少連續可用高調變 Subchannels 的 AMSs，讓
有較多連續高調變 Subchannels 之 AMS 來配合配
置。 
STEP 1. Burst 分群及排程順序 
基於上述的理由，每個 AMS 都被分配在適合
的 Subchannel 上時，可以有較佳的 Slot 利用效
率，但是，當每個 AMS 都以利己的角度為基礎
時，會產生如前述的外部碎裂問題。所以 TIGER
演算法在 STEP 1 時，會預先找出相似配置位置的
Burst，並且使其分群，目的在於將這些性質相似
的 Burst 分別在不同的 AAI subframe 上進行配
置，以符合 IEEE 802.16m 規格中，不可有 Burst
重疊配置的限制。由於分群的動作可以針對每個
AMS 的 Subchannel 特性分類，可使後續的調整步
驟更容易達成。

     圖 11、Network Throughput。 
碎、外部破碎、ABS 可服務的 AMS 數量以及
Network Throughput 進行探討以驗證本論文所提
出之 TIGER 演算法。 
圖 11 為 Network Throughput 表現，橫軸為
AMS 數量，縱軸為 Throughput，單位為 Mbps，
其計算方式為 Downlink 實際傳輸資料量與傳輸
一個 Frame 所需時間，由結果發現本計畫提出之
TIGER 演算法可以達到不錯的產能，另外也可以
發現 SDRA 的 Throughput 是最差的，這是由於
SDRA 的 Burst 排程演算法是採用跨越所有
Subchannel 配置，因為 Burst 只能使用一種調變與
編碼，ABS 在選擇調變技術時必須使用最差的調
變以確保資料傳輸正確，因此使得每個 AMS 所佔
用的資源上升，換句話說，也就是每個 AMS 所使
用的 Burst 因此而增加使用的 Slot 數量，進而使
其它 AMS 可以使用的資源減少，然而 Raster 演算
法採用由左至右的資源分配，事實上本計畫提出
的方法類似 Raster 的配置方式，但是 Raster 在
IEEE 802.16m 環境下，將會有內部碎裂增加以及
減少 Subchannel 利用率的問題。 
由於 TIGER 演算法的概念混合了 Bucket 配
置的精神，讓有較多好 Subchannel 的 AMS 資源
可以集中在一個 AAI Subframe 中，提高 Frame 利
用率，這也是本計畫所提出之作法勝過 Raster 的
理由，由圖 11 中可以看到當達到 15 個使用者時
本計畫所提出的方法在產能上有下降的趨勢，這
是由於本計畫所提出之 TIGER 作法是採用配置
Subchannel 接著才藉由調整 Burst 的位置，當 ABS
分配資源給多個 AMSs，越後面配置的 AMSs 其
可使用的資源會越靠近 Downlink 右邊界，造成每
個使用者資源的AMSs其Burst所含資料量可能無
法滿足 AMSs 的需求，造成 Burst 的數量變多，使
得 A-MAP 變大，進而壓縮到其他 AMSs 可使用
的資源，當達到 20 個使用者時，Downlink 
Subframe 中的可用資源已經趨近飽滿，此時
Network Throughput 將會持平。 
7. 結論與討論 
本計畫考量在 IEEE 802.16m OFDMA 網路
架構下，提出一  Downlink Burst 排程機制 : 
Transmission time Interval Grouping and Exchange 
Rules (TIGER) Scheme ， 來 規 劃 Downlink 
Subframe 中 Default Transmission Time Interval 
(TTI)的 Burst，Default TTI 是 IEEE 802.16m 
Downlink Burst 配置之模式。為了增加 Downlink 
頻寬利用率及減少內、外部碎裂所造成的資源浪
費，本論文利用規格所定義的 Default TTI 配置之
特性，同時亦考量此種 Default TTI 配置之限制，
利用樹狀結構調整 Default TTI 的配置位置來減少
外部碎裂之資源浪費，本計畫是少數考量在 IEEE 
802.16m OFDMA Frame 架構下，Default TTI 之規
劃與配置。由實驗結果顯示，TIGER 演算法相較
於目前 Burst 分配之相關研究(SDRA 與 Raster)，
有較高的 Throughput 與 Frame 利用率。

In IEEE 802.16m  networks, each frame is divided into eight 
advanced air interface (AAI) subframes, composed of Logical 
Resource Units (LRUs). According to stations’ requirements, 
advanced base stations (ABSs) allocate bursts constructed by 
contiguous LRUs to stations. If ABSs cannot efficiently schedule and 
allocate bursts, bandwidth resource will be wasted in or among bursts, 
respectively called internal fragmentation and external fragmentation 
in this paper. In this study, this project formulates a downlink burst 
allocation problem, considering both internal fragmentation (IF) and 
external fragmentation (EF), and then proposes a transmission time 
interval grouping and exchange rules scheme, called TIGER, to 
determine the position and the number of required LRUs of each burst 
in the downlink (DL) AAI subframe, subject to minimizing the 
bandwidth wastage. Simulation results show that TIGER outperforms 
other related approaches, SDRA and Raster in the network throughput.

Abstract—The paper investigates the downlink burst
scheduling problem (BSP) in IEEE 802.16 OFDMA systems.
To solve the problem, a subchannel-aware burst fragmentation,
packing and scheduling (BFPS) algorithm is proposed to
schedule the position of each burst with high subchannel
utilization and low DL-MAP overhead. BFPS is composed of
the burst allocation scheme, burst fragmentation and packing
scheme, and the burst swapping scheme. The burst allocation
scheme and the burst fragmentation and packing scheme are
to reduce the wasted and unused slots in and among bursts,
and the DL-MAP control overhead. Moreover, in order for the
channel compensation, the burst swapping scheme is used to
adjust the positions of bursts. The simulation results highlight
that BFPS outperforms other related approaches in the network
throughput, DL-MAP IE efficiency, service ratio, and the
bandwidth utilization ratio.
Keywords—IEEE 802.16, OFDMA, WiMAX, Burst Allocations.
I. INTRODUCTION
Worldwide interoperability for microwave access
(WiMAX), based on IEEE 802.16 standard [1], is an
on-going debate for the research community and the industry
due to its capability to support high transmission rates
and large-scale coverage. Among the physical layer (PHY)
specifications, orthogonal frequency division multiple access
(OFDMA) is attractive as a key technology to support reliable
and non-line-of-sight (NLOS) wireless transmissions. An
IEEE 802.16 OFDMA frame combines time domain and
frequency domain. Fig. 1 shows the IEEE 802.16 OFDMA
frame, which is composed of OFDMA symbols in the time
domain and subchannels in the frequency domain.
Basically, an IEEE 802.16 network consists of one base
station (BS) and multiple mobile stations (MSs). BS allocates
bandwidth called burst to MSs. One of the main restrictions
for the burst allocation in the downlink subframe is an ar-
bitrary rectangular region made up of a number of OFDMA
slots, each of which composed of subchannels and OFDMA
symbols. With the lack of a well-designed burst allocation
and scheduling scheme, some OFDMA slots in or among
bursts are unable to be used due to the rectangular mapping
constraint and inappropriate position allocations of bursts in
Fig. 1. IEEE 802.16 OFDMA Frame Structure.
the downlink subframe, as shown in Fig. 1. Therefore, not
only downlink bandwidth utilization, but also the network
throughput degrades.
In addition, the downlink bandwidth utilization will de-
creases due to the increase of the DL-MAP overhead. Since
the DL-MAP control message contains DL-MAP Information
Elements (IEs) to record the burst allocation information of
the downlink subframe, BS broadcasts the control message
with the most robust modulation and coding type, e.g., QPSK
1/2, to ensure the success of the transmissions from BS
to MSs. When the number of DL-MAP IEs increases, the
map overhead increases. The DL-MAP overhead might even
occupy up to of the frame [2] and the available bandwidth
will decrease without a well-designed burst allocation and
scheduling scheme.
Recently, much related work [3]–[8] has contributed to the
design of downlink burst allocation mechanism in the IEEE
802.16 OFDMA system. Most of them do not consider the
unused OFDMA slots in or among bursts. Additionally, the
related contributions allocate more than one burst for each
MS, and lead to high DL-MAP control overhead and less
number of available OFDMA slots. Therefore, the paper in-
vestigates the burst scheduling problem (BSP) and proposes a
burst fragmentation, packing and scheduling (BFPS) algorithm
for the bandwidth utilization gains and the control overhead
alleviations.

III. BURST FRAGMENT, PACKING AND SCHEDULING
(BFPS) ALGORITHM
As mentioned above, the wasted slots caused by IF and EF,
and the additional DL-MAP overhead degrade the network
throughput. The paper proposes a heuristic algorithm, named
Burst Fragment, Packing and Scheduling (BFPS) Algorithm,
to solve BSP in IEEE 802.16 OFDMA systems. Algorithm 1
shows the generic structure of BFPS. The output of Algo-
rithm 1 is the scheduled information of bursts.
BFPS contains three schemes, including the burst allocation
scheme, burst fragmentation and packing scheme, and the
burst swapping scheme. The burst allocation scheme is used
to determine the burst profiles, shapes, and the temporary
positions of bursts. Because EF may substantially results in
a large number of unused slots, comparing to that caused by
IF. Therefore, unlike our previous work, the burst allocation
scheme tried to minimize the unused slots caused by EF firstly.
Then, through the burst fragmentation and packing scheme,
the wasted slots caused by IF can be minimized. The DL-
MAP overhead is also decreased by the scheme. Finally, the
burst swapping scheme adjusts the positions of bursts for the
channel compensation in the OFDMA system. The details of
the schemes are described in the following.

Finally, the percentage of downlink bandwidth usage is eval-
uated. The utilization ratio are mainly composed of the ratio of
the OFDMA slots for data transmissions and the unused and
wasted OFDMA slots. Fig. 7(d) illustrates the utilization ratios
of BFPS and the compared protocols. From the simulation
result, BFPS has some wasted OFDMA slots caused by IF. It
is because that some bursts with wasted OFDMA slots caused
by IF are not alleviate by the fragmentation scheme and some
unused slots among bursts are not filled. However, the ratio of
OFDMA slots for actual data transmissions of BFPS is still
higher than the other protocols, except for MAT. Although
MAT performs a little bit better than BFPS, the network
throughput and subchannel utilization of BFPS are better than
that of MAT because of using high-order modulation schemes.
In summary, the performance of BFPS outperforms to the
other protocols in terms of the network throughput, service
ratio, DL-MAP IE efficiency and the utilization ratio.
V. CONCLUSIONS
The paper considers the downlink scheduling problem in
IEEE 802.16 OFDMA system. Since each MS has different
receiving condition in each subchannel and without an ap-
propriate burst scheduling algorithm leads to lower network
throughput, this paper provides a subchannel-aware burst frag-
mentation, packing and scheduling (BFPS) scheme for the sub-
channel utilization and the network throughput improvement.
By using burst fragmentation scheme, the wasted OFDMA
slots caused by IF can be released to other bursts and the
fragmented bursts are also can fill up the unused OFDMA slots
among bursts. However, the fragmentation scheme causes the
additional DL-MAP control overhead. Therefore, BFPS also
takes the additional DL-MAP control overhead and released
wasted OFDMA slots into account. The simulation results
show that the BFPS outperforms other protocols in the network
throughput, service ratio, IE efficiency, and the subchannels
utilization.
