利用微生物法由帶鱗之吳郭魚皮製備水溶性膠原蛋白 
(Preparation of soluble collagen from tilapia skin with scale 
via microbial fermentation) 
計畫編號：NSC 96-2221-E-022 -008   
執行期限：96 年 8 月 1 日至 97 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：郭建民    執行機構；高雄海洋科技大學水產食品科學系  
 
一、中文摘要 
本研究利用 Meiothermus pigtungensis 
sp. nov. I40 探討分解魚鱗之發酵條件。
首先利用 Plackett-Burman 設計法篩選出
對於培養 I40 菌體量、水解魚鱗及蛋白
酶產量之影響較為顯著的因子依序：fish 
scale、tryptone、yeast extract。根據 PB 
design 之結果，續進行兩次反應曲面法
探討 I40 水解魚鱗及生產蛋白酶的最適
發酵條件，結果以蛋白酶為反應值所對
繪製出反應曲面圖， R2＝0.9755。獲得
最高蛋白酶產量的最佳培養基配方為：
tryptone 0.160 %, yeast extract 0.267 %, 
fish scale 1.81 %, temperature 51.7˚С。比
較實驗設計前後的蛋白酶活性，由原來
的 1.5 U/mL 提高為 20.8 U/mL 增加了
13.9 倍，而且微生物培養時間由原來的
5 天縮短為 2 天；然而 yeast extract 使用
的濃度由原來的每升 0.25 g 提高為 2.67 
g，fish scale 的用量由原來的每升 5 g 提
高為 18 g。I40 生產之蛋白酶最適作用
pH 為 8.5，最適溫度為 70˚С。該蛋白酶
貯存於 4˚С 或-20˚С 之酵素活性穩定。 
  
關鍵詞：Meiothermus pigtungensis sp. nov. 
I40、魚鱗膠原蛋白、微生物、
蛋白酶 
 
Abstract 
Meiothermus sp. strain I40 (I40), a 
newly identified bacterium isolated from a 
hot spring located at southern Taiwan, is 
used to degrade fish scale collagen in this 
study. The Plackett–Burman (PB) design 
was used to screen the factors essential for 
proteinase production. Four factors 
including fish scale, tryptone, yeast extract, 
and temperature were then studied the 
effects of proteinase production in 3-day 
experiments by using response surface 
methodology (RSM). Results of RSM 
presented the effects of various factors for 
proteinase activity and optimum 
conditions for proteinase production. A 
maximal proteinase production of 20.8 U 
ml
-1
 activity was obtained with the 
following condition: 0.16% tryptone, 
0.27% yeast extract, 1.8% fish scale, and 
incubation temperature at 51.7 
o
C. By 
using statistical analysis involving PB and 
RSM, a 13.9-fold increase in proteinase 
production by Meiothermus sp. I40 was 
achieved. The optimal pH of the 
proteinase produced from I40 was 8.5, 
while the optimal temperature was 70 ˚С. 
The proteinase from I40 showed very 
stable in enzyme activity either storage in 
4˚С or -20˚С 
 
Keywords: Meiothermus pigtungensis sp. 
nov. I40, fish scale collagen, 
microorganism, proteinase
