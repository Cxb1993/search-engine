Abstract 
Low-temperature atmospheric pressure (AP) plasma technology had widely used in the 
surface treatment process of various industries such as glass substrate of LCD, flexible substrate, 
plastic film printing treatment, mineral electrostatic separator, ozone generator, surface 
modification, surface cleaning, radioactive waste materials, volatile organic compounds (VOCs) 
and so on. Meanwhile, the AP plasma deposition technology applications are seldom found and 
still studied in research and development period. Possessed these priorities of large market 
applications, more clean characteristics, low cost, atmospheric pressure operational convenience, 
and easily integrated with following processes, we determined to develop low-temperature AP 
plasma-jet deposition system and technology, not only build up the accurate and convenient 
measurement for deposition treated conditions, but also set up the relative database. Based on the 
above targets, we will design and manufacture more competitive novel low-temperature AP 
plasma-jet deposition system, and develop high extra benefits for various products. The purpose 
of this study is to investigate and develop the reasonable low-temperature AP plasma-jet 
deposition technology and system within three-year research stages. Within the 2nd year of the 
project, we had mostly finished design of experiments (DOE) to find out the optimal parameters 
for deposition of silicon oxide, polyethylene, titanium oxide and zinc oxide thin films. 
Simultaneously, we will try to deposit the optimal performance of the hydrocarbon hydrophobic 
thin films and DLC thin films by using DOE method. We consider that adopt these films
