摘要 
 
    高效率、安全舒適、低光害的「綠色照明計畫」是當前照明規劃設計之目標，不僅能節約能源，
同時也能降低夜間照明對整體環境的各種衝擊。然而過去台灣的戶外照明常有使用低效率燈具、或是
燈具超量設計之情形，加上未有具體的戶外照明節能規範，故此部分有檢討改進的空間。 
    本研究以住宅社區為調查研究對象，探討周邊道路照明與戶外活動廣場照明節能應用之可行性與
設計建議。路燈設置之節能依據，以「照明功率密度（LPD）」做為主要評價對象；基本照明品質的部
分，則以現行的路面輝度或照度做為依據；低光害的部分，則選用向上溢散光較低之遮蔽型燈具，同
時結合照明控制手法，透過電腦模擬得出光源類型、照明品質維護與節能效益之理想設計方式。 
　  最終提出社區戶外照明 LPD 之建議基準值，可做為照明節能之評估依據，提升都市能源效益與降
低 CO2 排放量；綠色照明手法之應用效益與可行性之探討，則可做為日後規劃設計與現況改善對策之
參考，促進照明產業升級，創造優質的夜間照明環境。 
 
關鍵詞：綠色照明、照明功率密度、照明模擬、節能減碳 
 
Abstract 
 
The Green Lights Program is aimed at lighting design with high energy efficiency, safe and safety, and 
reduction of light pollution. It means more energy conservation and less environmental effect, and has become 
the sustainable purpose of lighting design. However, outdoor lighting in Taiwan is usually designed with low 
energy-efficient luminaires or over designed. Moreover, there is no energy standard for outdoor lighting. 
    To improve lighting environment and save energy, this study investigates the feasibility of green lighting 
and propose design suggestions. Road lighting and outdoor lighting in community are chosen as the samples 
to evaluate lighting power density, outdoor illuminance level and emitting upward light. The lighting 
simulation software DIALux is also used to analyze lighting quality and to provide ideal lighting design 
consisted of high-efficient light source and energy control method. 
The results of this study are not only supply LPD suggestion for evaluating energy consumption of 
community outdoor lighting, but also help improve urban energy efficiency and decrease CO2 emission. 
Furthermore, the application of green lights program will benefit nighttime lighting environment and promote 
lighting industry in the future. 
 
Key words：green lights program, lighting power density (LPD), lighting simulation, energy conservation and 
CO2 reduction

四、研究方法 
（一）社區道路照明節能與綠色照明評估 
1. 道路照明功率密度（LPD）之計算 
照明功率密度（LPD）定義為單位路面面積上的照明安裝功率（包含鎮流器功耗），以道路照明為
例，若照明安裝功率為 P，路寬為 W 燈具間距為 S，則路面照明面積 A=S*W，LPD=P/A。一般先以
「流明計算法」決定燈具流明數（F）、燈具數（N）、燈具間距（S）等資料，確認平均照度與總安裝
功率之後，即能求得 LPD 值。 
6 
2.  深夜時段之調光模擬 
考量到將夜間的照明光害降到最低，在燈具的選用上，均選擇「完全截光型（full cut-off）」燈具，
避免不必要的光線向天空溢散。同時考慮到深夜時段（23：00 之後）道路人車使用率下降的情形，本
研究以「調光」的方式，將原有燈具之光通量分別調整為 80％、60％及 40％，並比較調光後住宅光侵
擾之情形。 
（二）社區戶外廣場照明節能與綠色照明評估 
戶外廣場照明功率密度（LPD）定義與前述相同，以燈具總安裝功率除以總照明面積。由於戶外
廣場的照明面積一般難以確切定義，故此處以燈桿兩倍高度距離內的面積認定之，同時以此做為照明
模擬計算範圍（圖 3）。至於照明品質檢視的部分，模擬結果參照 CNS 規範、經濟部「路燈設計規定」
（1986）、內政部營建署「市區道路工程規劃及設計規範之研究」（2002），以及國際照明委員會 CIE、
北美照明協會 IESNA 制訂的照度標準與均齊度要求。由於社區內廣場與住宅距離較遠，因此較無光侵
擾問題，故在光害減量的設定上，直接選擇「完全截光型（full cut-off）」燈具進行模擬。 
五、結果與討論 
（一）社區道路照明節能與綠色照明模擬分析 
1. 道路照明功率密度（LPD）與照明品質需求之合宜性 
社區道路照明模擬結果如表 7 所示。一般照明品質基準主要參考表 6「支路」部分，LPD 則參照
表 2 及表 3，考量次要道路的寬度在 8m~15m 之間，將參考值訂為 0.60。整體觀之，三種燈具均能符
合基本的道路平均照度需求，然而在輝度均齊度（U0）大部分未能達到標準，進一步模擬顯示，若改
為「雙側交錯」排列，則可提升 U0 值。再比較不同路寬，以 8m 時的照明品質較佳，15m 時僅能維持
平均照度。燈具裝設間距則以 25m 時之照明品質較為理想。 
    進一步比較水銀燈、高壓鈉氣燈與 LED 燈三種燈具效能，常見的水銀路燈不僅較難達成道路照明
品質需求，同時也較為耗能，約為 LED 路燈的 1.4 倍。高壓鈉氣燈之 LPD 雖然約為 LED 路燈的 1.2
倍，然而其在平均輝度、輝度均齊度、平均照度與照度均齊度的表現最佳，仍有使用上的優點。LED
路燈雖然有較低的 LPD 值，然而其主要缺點在於光源所能提供的道路均齊度不佳，尤其路寬越寬越為
明顯，顯示容易因為明暗對比強烈而產生「眩光」問題。 
    在照明節能部分，若僅考慮合乎最基本的路面平均照度需求（8 lx），則 LED 路燈之 LPD 可降至
0.31，高壓鈉氣燈之 LPD 為 0.39（表 8）。然而為了行車行人之安全，道路照明不能單以節能為首要目
標，必須要兼顧照明品質，故在全部合乎基準的要求下，此時高壓鈉氣燈之 LPD 為 0.59，LED 路燈之
LPD 為 0.58，兩者之節能效率不相上下（表 9），與表 2 及表 3 的 LPD 基準值接近但略微偏高，日後
在道路照明節能要求上，可暫以 0.6 做為最高限制值。此外，LED 路燈因均齊度較差的關係，較適用
於路寬較窄之 8m 道路，高壓鈉氣燈則適用於 10～15m 之道路。

效率、照明功率密度、照明品質與光害影響之情形，在兼顧綠色照明的概念下，得出住宅區道路合宜

使用高發光效率光源、採用具有良好截光設計的燈具避免光源裸露造成眩光，同時為了降低住宅光

～）降低燈具的光通量輸出，可以有效降低住宅窗面的垂直照度，同時在確保基本照明需求的同時，
也能因此達到節能的效果。

 2. Research Method 
 
2.1 Definition of light trespass 
 
Light trespass occurs when unwanted light enters one's property  [1]. When unwanted light enters 
the houses, the nighttime environment is disturbed and the quality of life diminished. Especially in 
dimly-lit bedrooms, the outdoor light can affect sleep quality. In addition, too bright light can inhibit 
melatonin production, cause reproductive system of reproductive system [2] and increase 
incidence of breast cancer and colorectal cancer [3]. Thus, light trespass has adverse impact on 
physiology and psychology. 
 
2.2 Light trespass limitation benchmark 
 
Based on CIE150 technical report, light trespass evaluation benchmark refers to vertical 
illuminance of house surface or potential relevant boundary, especially location of windows [4]. The 
relevant boundary refers to residential property boundary potentially affected by lighting, such as 
building boundary line or building line. During measurement of light trespass, vertical illuminance 
(Ev) is calculated 1.5m above the ground or the facade or window [1] [4].  
 
In light pollution prevention, CIE has outlined four environment zones to establish a basis for 
outdoor lighting regulations, from E1 to E4  [4] (Table 1). The lighting designers can determine the 
lighting objectives suitable to their buildings. For example, vertical illuminance of E3 zone should 
be lower than 10 lux during pre-curfew and lower than 2 lux during curfew.

2.3     Light trespass measurement  
 
To know the impact of outdoor lighting on houses, this study uses vertical illuminance (Ev) on 
windows as light trespass evaluation basis. Because the height of outdoor lighting fittings is limited, 
light trespass has greater impact on GF~2F. Thus, facade illuminance is recorded until 2F. The 
illuminance on window above ground is measured by using illuminance meter. Due to height and 
privacy of residents, for the part which cannot be measured by the illuminance meter, the 
measurement is replaced by measuring facade luminance, and then the luminance is converted to 
illuminance. First, it is assumed building facade material meets Lambert's cosine law, and 
luminance is equal from all directions. Under the premise, the material surface illuminance and 
luminance can be converted through the following equation:   
 
Luminance（L） = Illuminance（E） * Reflectance（ρ） / π                                                 （1） 
 
To reach a certain degree of accuracy for the measurement results, the individual readings of 
photometer should be within ±20% of the predictions and the average levels should be with in ±
10% of the calculated mean [5].Outdoor lighting measurement uses the illuminometer with 0.1 lux 
accuracy and cosine and colour temperature correction. Meanwhile, cloudy and rainy days should 
be prevented during survey. The measurement would start after discharge lamps work for 1 hour.

4. Investigation of light trespass caused by advertising light boxes  
 
4.1   Current installation position of advertising light boxes   
 
Currently, the common advertising signboard in Taiwan includes light boxes, neon light, projection 
lamp and large LED screen advertisings. The light box advertising features easy making, low cost 
and uniform distribution of the luminance. It is selected by 95% of businesses and the most 
common advertising way. It is used as horizontal signboard on the building surface and vertical 
signboard on the building facade. If there is more commercial floors, the building facade may have 
many light boxes. The adverting light box projecting range is subject to the building laws.  
 
Generally, advertising light boxes are often turned on during night operation and tuned off during 
closing. But some shops may turn on the light boxes during closing for publicity. In Taiwan, there 
are many mixed residential commercial buildings in big cities. The ground floor is used for shops 
and the floors above it are used for dwelling. The adverting signboards are often erected outside 
the windows on the higher floors. The diffuse light ray may affect residents. For this reason, it is 
necessary to discuss advertising signboards and light trespass.  
 
4.2   Sample investigation  
 
This study surveyed mixed residential commercial streets in Taipei and Tainan. The advertising 
light boxes are regarded as the major light trespass source. Type and location of advertising 
signboards, building height and mixture of residential commercial functions were recorded during

window of the 1F~2F houses. Luminance measurement can be conducted for the place which 
illuminance cannot be measured. In the six commercial districts, 495 building facades were

 5.  Light trespass in residential blocks of compact cities   
 
5.1   Comparison with the Impact of  light trespass from different light sources 
 
5.1.1 Roadway lighting and light trespass  
 
Road lights are the necessary lighting facilities. However, in the compact cities, residential 
buildings are constructed adjacent to the streets. It is difficult to avoid light trespass, especially in 
narrow lanes. Roadway lighting is the major source of light trespass in residential blocks. Due to 
street lamp height, the floors above the street lamps will not suffer light trespass.  
 
5.1.2   Advertising light box and light trespass  
 
The advertising light box is installed on the facade beside the windows and has direct impact on 
increasing  illuminance on windows. Its impact is more serious than road lamps. As long as one 
advertising light box is installed on building facade, the illuminance on window can be increased to 
17 lux. Being different from street lamps, advertising light box is not always set on the lower floor, if 
the light box is installed on higher floor, the residents may also suffer light trespass. Advertising 
light box has greater impact on the residents than roadway lighting. Fortunately, many businesses 
are closed before 11: 00 pm, and only a few of them continuously turn on their advertising light 
boxes to maintain commercial image.  
 
5.2   Discussion of light trespass in Taiwan’s residential blocks   
 
Due to high residential density of the compact cities, installation of light fittings may cause high 
illuminance on facade. In Taiwan, 1F houses of the common residential houses are the principal 
bedrooms which are mostly affected by light trespass. If the residential houses are located in the 
urban residential blocks, illuminance on the windows of principal bedrooms is 6 lux due to impact 
of road lamps during night; if the houses in mixed residential commercial district, the light from 
street lamp and advertising light boxes would be received. The illuminance on window surface will 
increase to more than 23 lux. The more the signboards, the higher the illuminance is (Table 4).
