生物可分解高分子是目前非常熱門之話題，世界各國皆注目在此類高分子的
研究上。本研究在利用微生物生產可解決環境問題以及可以利用於再生醫療
之材料，所以是非常有意義之研究。其價值不僅可以解決台灣的環境問題也
可以邁向再生醫療產業，幫助台灣產業往更尖端的行業前進。生物可分解高
分子的生產或推廣，可以讓台灣居民了解環保的重要等，改善台灣的居住環
境，讓我們更注意及維護台灣的環境，讓台灣世代能夠永續的經營，所以其
影響是非常的深遠。本研究除了探討利用微生物生產的條件之外，也試著應
用於再生醫療上，其應用價值今後會更寬廣，更有價值。今後將持續往再生
醫療之組織再生做應用研究，其發展的可能性是非常大。

no accumulation of polyesters in the cells was 
observed.  
To evaluate the polyester synthesis under 
the different culture condition, about 1.5 g and 
4.5 g quantities of the rinsed cells were 
transferred to a mineral medium (pH 7.0 & 7.5) 
containing the different concentration of carbon 
sources. The different culture conditions were 
shown in Table 1. The cells were cultivated in 
these media at 30 °C for 72 hours, harvested by 
centrifugation (4000 rpm, 15 min), rinsed with 
water and methanol and finally vacuum-dried at 
room temperature.  
The polyester was extracted from the 
dried cells with hot chloroform and purified by 
precipitation with n-heptane. The polymers were 
then vacuum-dried for 48 hours. The polymer 
yield was measured using the following formula 
(1). 
 
Y＝(Wa / Wd) × 100       (1) 
 
where Y is the polymer content (wt%), Wa is the 
polymer weight (g) and Wd is the dried cell 
weight (g). 
   
2.4. Analysis 
The compositions of the poly(hydroxyl- 
alkanoate) samples were determined by 
analyzing the 500 MHz 1H-NMR spectra, which 
were recorded using a JEOL α-500 spectrometer. 
The 1H-NMR spectra were recorded at room 
temperature for a CDCl3 solution of the 
poly(hydroxyalkanoate) (10 mg ml-1) with a 4.7 
µs pulse width, 5-s pulse repetition, 5000 Hz 
spectral width, 16K data point and 32 
accumulations. Tetramethylsilane (Me4Si, δ = 0) 
was used as the internal chemical shift standard.  
Gel permeation chromatography (GPC) 
was carried out using an HLC-802A high- 
performance liquid chromatograph (Tosoh) at 
38°C equipped with a series of four columns of 
TSK-gel and an RI-8 differential refractometer. 
Chloroform was used as the eluent at the flow 
rate of 0.1 ml min-1, while the sample solution of 
1 ml was used at a concentration of 1.0 g l-1. 
Polystyrene standards with a narrow 
polydispersity were used to generate a 
calibration curve, and the apparent molecular 
weights were then calculated. 
 
3. Results and discussion 
3.1. Effect of polymer content and composition of 
polymer at different cell concentration 
Table 2 lists the polymer content and 
composition ratio of polymer at various cell 
concentrations under 1.5 g/L and 4.5 g/L. The 
concentrations of carbon source affected the 
mechanism of bacterial synthesis produced 
polymer was observed. The composition 
distribution of copolymer was around at 80 
mol%, when the cell concentration of culture at 
1.5 g/L. The composition of copolymer was 
reached over 90mol%, when the cell 
concentration of culture increasing at 4.5 g/L. 
But the molecular weight was not clearly 
changed, both of cell concentration at 1.5 g/L or 
4.5 g/L. It is suggesting that the composition of 
copolymer was affected by cell concentration.
