中英文摘要 
(一) 中文摘要 
隨著網路科技之快速進展，訊息之提供呈
現更多樣性，除了文字之外，圖形更是大
量的在網頁中扮演重要的訊息傳送角色，
如網路書店、氣象網站和股市理財資訊網
站等。網際網路中提供的數位資訊服務，
隨著資料 e 化程度愈高而益趨蓬勃，在龐
大的資料流中，能有效率的搜尋和瀏覽是
提高資訊交換的關鍵。在本計劃中，我們
針對快速序列視覺呈現（rapid serial 
visual presentation，簡稱 RSVP）之影
像呈現技術的使用性(usability)，提出動
態影像呈現方向和移動速度之主觀性偏好
和辨認性績效評量，實驗中模擬網路書店
搜尋書籍書目的人機介面，共有 42 位受試
者參與此計劃。主觀性偏好問卷結果顯
示，使用者偏好物架型 RSVP 的移動方向為
由左下收縮至右上、移動速度為影格速率
20-30 FPS；旋轉木馬型 RSVP 則偏好順時
針方向旋轉、速度為 10 和 15 FPS；然而
針對辨認性進行績效評量實驗，邏輯斯迴
歸模式的配適結果顯示，RSVP 影像呈現模
式和移動速度對於辨認性具統計顯著性，
其中以旋轉木馬型 RSVP 和移動速度為 30 
FPS 有最佳之辨認性，正確辨認機率之預
測值為 0.9839；但是當物架型 RSVP 和移
動速度為慢速之 15 FPS 時，正確辨認機率
之預測值只有 0.6060，為所有組合中最低
者，顯示物架型 RSVP 和移動速度為 15 FPS
有最差之可辨認性。此外搜尋結果的 RSVP
動態影像呈現滿足使用者的經驗、美學與
極簡的設計、最少的動作、和易學性等可
用性評估準則。研究結果應可廣泛應用於
網際網路和行動網路之數位資訊傳輸服
務，藉此提供數位資訊傳輸服務之最佳呈
現介面。 
關鍵詞: 人機介面、主觀偏好、快速序列
視覺呈現、可用性評估

usability of rapid serial visual presentation 
(RSVP) image interface using subjective 
preference questionnaire and performance 
measurement methods. Forty-two 
undergraduate and graduate students (21 
males and 21 females) from Tunghai

2.1 受試者 
共有 42 名 23 至 29 歲之東海大學學
生（其中 21 名男性，21 名女性）參與本
研究，背景為管理學院者 37 人，背景為創
意設計暨藝術學院者 5 人，皆有網路購
物、瀏覽過網路書店之經驗。 
2.2 可用性評估問卷設計 
Nielsen (2005) [9]提出十個可用性
原則，研究中另外加入「易學性」原則，
評估 RSVP 介面應用於搜尋結果頁面的可
用性。茲將符合可用性原則的項目列於表
1，並依此原則設計主觀性偏好問卷。問卷
設計分為五個問題，依序為:「可吸引住我
的目光」、「覺得有美感」、「有效率的找到
目標書目」、「很快找到目標書目」以及「介
面是容易使用的」，對照於 Nielsen 的可用
性評估準則為：「滿足使用者的經驗」、「美
學與極簡的設計」、「最少的動作」和「易
學性」。此外依據資策會「2006 年我國家
庭寬頻、行動與無線應用現況與需求調
查—家戶連網應用」[2]，於此問卷中附加
調查受訪者使用網路之目的、使用網路書
店的主要目的、與使用網路書店之優缺點。 
2.3 移動式資訊模擬系統之設計 
圖 1和圖 2為研究中使用的網路書店
模擬系統，搜尋結果頁面將以兩種 RSVP
模組出現，針對旋轉木馬型 RSVP 設計順時
針與逆時針兩種圓形軌跡，圖片由下方資
料夾出現，至橢圓軌跡的長軸端點時放大
至最大，待移動到長軸另一端點時開始收
縮，至資料夾時影像恰好收縮為原來之
36%，約為最大圖片之 1/3（圖 1）；此外針
對物架型 RSVP，設計影像由左下至右上收
縮（物架型 I）、由右下向左上收縮（物架
型 II）、由左上至右下放大（物架型 III）、
由右上至左下放大（物架型 IV）等四種方
式，圖片由端點出現，順對角線移動，其
中物架型 I和 II 至一半時開始收縮，至資
料夾時影像恰收縮為原來之 36%(圖 2)；表
2 為主觀性偏好問卷和效能評量所採用之
設計參數和其因子分類之規格。 
RSVP之移動速度乃以Flash軟體中的
影格速率 (frames per second，簡稱 FPS)
評量[8]，影格速率是指動畫播放的速度，
以每秒鐘的影格數目為測量單位。動作越
複雜的動畫則需要越高的影格速率。實驗
以一張影像由開始出現至結束消失共 200
影格，移動速度為 10, 15, 20, 30, 和
40FPS，則每張影格的曝光時間分別為 20, 
13.3, 10, 6.67, 和 5 秒，圖 3為網路書
店之模擬系統以每分鐘 20 張影格、順時針
播放之旋轉木馬型 RSVP 顯示介面，圖 4
則為網路書店之模擬系統以每分鐘 20 張
影格、右上至左下播放之物架型 RSVP 顯示
介面。

design decisions on the ability of users to effectively perceive the displayed contents 
for quantitative data is our particular concern. The conventional mode and four modes 
of RSVP are briefly summarized below [6]: 
Conventional mode 
This approach displays a grid of pictures and allows the same size of the thumbnails to 
be shown on the screen at one time. 
Keyhole RSVP mode 
In the keyhole mode, only one image is visible at any moment and all the images 
appear in the same location in the same size. 
Carousel RSVP mode 
In the carousel mode, each image describes an approximately circular trajectory from 
its emergence at small size, increasing to maximum area at the top of the circle and 
then reducing again until ‘reentering’ the folder from which it emerged. 
Collage RSVP mode 
In the collage mode, a set of images is deposited, in rapid sequence, on a table top in 
such a way that all the images are visible at any one time. Resulting images will 
inevitably ‘cover up’ preceding ones. 
Shelf RSVP mode 
In the shelf mode, a set of images is displayed in catalog pages for immediate viewing. 
Applying the RSVP principles, we design a visual interface of statistical graphics on the 
small screen devices. In this study, using RSVP interfaces provides a rich set of 
multiple-objects information on the small screen devices. Three types of graphical 
displays, including a line graph, bar graph, and map, and five modes of RSVP 
interfaces, including a carousel, collage, shelf, keyhole, and conventional modes will be 
considered as the design factors. The researcher’s hypotheses proposed below will be 
discussed for two experiments. Does the interaction of RSVP modes and graphical 
display types influence the visual decoding performance of quantitative information on 
the small screen devices for the four tasks--reading an exact value, identifying the 
trend, comparing two values, and reading the maximum value? The objective is to 
evaluate the optimal visualization interface of quantitative data on the small screen 
devices. We propose the design guidelines of statistical graphs interact with RSVP 
interfaces on the small screen devices, especially on the mobile devices. 
 
RESEARCH METHODOLGY 
To design the RSVP interface on the small screen devices, a contextual inquiry was 
used to explore the subjective preference of luminance contrast at the beginning of 
this study. Two factorial experiments with blocks were used to collect the data of visual 
performance. 
Contextual Inquiry 
Contextual inquiry is a structured field interviewing method [2]. It is useful in the early 
stages of development, since much of the received information is subjective. Here, we 
use it to understand users’ subjective preference of luminance contrast. According to 
the result of the contextual inquiry, the subjective preference of luminance contrast is 
used to design a simulated mobile phone system. 
Participants 
Twenty undergraduate and graduate students (10 females and 10 males) from Tunghai 
University voluntarily participated in the contextual inquiry. Their age distribution 
ranged from 22 to 30 years old (mean age of 25.6 years old and standard deviation of 
1.9 years old). They were interviewed individually and their subjective preferences

extraction tasks including reading the exact value, identifying the trend, comparing 
two values, and reading the maximum value (“TASK” factor for short) were used as 
the design factors. Response time is defined as the time to completing the assigned 
task consisting of correctly perceiving the target and correctly deciding. In Experiments 
1 and 2, each participant had to randomly perform 30 trials including 5 RSVP × 3 TYPE 
× 2 TASK treatment combinations. The order of trials within a block was completely 
randomized for each participant. 
Apparatuses and Stimuli 
The apparatuses of the experiments consisted of: a Pentium IV desktop personal 
computer (256MB RAM) with a 17-inch TFT-LCD screen (1280×1024 pixels) and a web 
camera (Logitech’s Quick Cam Pro5000) with a built-in microphone. In addition, Cyber 
Link Stream Author 3.0 is used to record the process of operation displays. A digital 
video camera recorder (SONY DCR-PC330) is used to record the overall process of 
experiments. 
A simulated mobile phone system based on Sony Ericsson T630 mobile phone with the 
screen height of 3.65 cm and width of 2.85 cm is used to illustrate the small screen 
devices. Through the pilot study of fifteen participants, the mean viewing distance 
from eyes to the screen of mobile phone is 23cm and the mean viewing distance from 
eyes to the TFT-LCD screen is 80 cm. The corresponding screen height of the 
simulated mobile phone system is 12.7cm (3.65*80/23) based on considering the 
same visual angle for the simulated system. 
Based on combining RSVP and TYPE factors, Figures 2-4 indicated the simulated 
displays applied in Experiments 1 and 2. There are six images simultaneously shown 
on the small screen in random order. The participants have to choose the given target 
image and control the pace by moving the direction keys. The contents of the question 
bank came from the public information announced by Central Weather Bureau of 
Taiwan (http://www.cwb.gov.tw) and Weather Underground 
(http://www.wunderground.com). Mean daily temperatures of six cities including New 
York, London, Paris, Berlin, Shanghai, and Tokyo are cited from the historical data 
banks.

The results of ANOVA of log response time for Experiments 1 and 2 are shown in Table 
2. It indicates the significant differences between the interaction of TYPE and RSVP 
(“TYPE×RSVP” for short), the interaction of TYPE and TASK (“TYPE×TASK” for short), 
as well as their main effects. However, the three-factor interaction of 
TYPE×RSVP×TASK is not statistically significant. Duncan multiple range tests provide 
subsequent post hoc pair-wise comparisons of the significant interactions. In 
Experiment 1, Figure 5 (a) points out the mean response times of bar and line charts 
are almost the same for all RSVP modes, however, the mean response time of map 
TYPE is significantly longer than both the bar and line charts. For the map × keyhole 
design, it is notably worse than the other TYPE×RSVP designs. In general, line and bar 
charts are significantly better than map for any kind of RSVP designs. In addition, 
Figure 5 (b) indicates the mean response time of map is significantly longer than both 
the bar and line charts for reading the exact value. However, the map × trend design 
is better than the map × exact ones. 
Similarly, in Experiment 2, Figure 6 (a) points out the bar × carousel, bar × keyhole, 
bar × shelf, line × keyhole, line × shelf, map × collage, map ×conventional, and map × 
shelf designs have significantly shorter mean response times, however, line × collage 
and map × keyhole designs have significantly longer mean response times. For the 
line ×  collage design, it is notably worse than the other TYPE × RSVP designs. In 
addition, Figure 6 (b) indicates the mean response time for reading the maximum 
value is consistently shorter than comparing two values for both bar and line charts. It 
also indicates the mean response time of map × maximum is longer than both bar

In Experiment 1, for identifying the trend, the line-type is significantly better than

significantly worse than others. One possible reason is a statistical map has to be 
encoded by different color hues and users have to decode their real values against the 
levels of color hues. An elementary perceptual task of judging color hue has been 
performed which falls in the bottom of the perceptual hierarchy [4]. However, for 
comparing two values, collage × map, shelf × map, and conventional × map designs 
have relatively better performance than other designs. An in-depth investigation on the 
functionality of map design in other web browser or small screen devices is worthwhile 
since the Global Positioning System (GPS) was presented to the public. 
 
CONCLUSION 
The results show both the interaction of RSVP and TYPE as well as the interaction of 
TASK and TYPE are statistically significant in Experiments 1 and 2. For reading the 
exact value and identifying the trend, line and bar charts are significantly better than 
map for any kind of RSVP designs. For comparing two values, collage × map, shelf × 
map, conventional × map, carousel × bar, and keyhole × bar designs have relatively 
smaller mean response times. It should not be recommended for collage × line design 
for comparing two values. For reading the maximum value, carousel × bar, keyhole × 
bar, and keyhole × line designs are recommended, but keyhole × map design is not 
recommended for the small screen devices.
