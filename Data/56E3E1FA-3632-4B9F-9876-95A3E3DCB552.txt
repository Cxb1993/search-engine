一、中文摘要 
 利用廣泛用於微機電製程之負型光阻材
料 SU-8來做為製作奈米孔隙之材料。在本篇
文章中成功的利用控制聚合反應而完成孔隙
材料的製作。搭配使用螢光檢測的方式來檢
測孔隙材料對於分子級螢光的穿透性，以及
利用光催化聚合反應來完成可以藉由 UV 光定
義之孔隙材料製作。在檢測結果發現可以容
許 70kDa 以下之分子螢光透過本研究方式所
製備之奈米孔隙材料。此負型光阻 SU-8 配合
172nm 波長的 VUV 光搭配臭氧(O3)做表面修
飾，可以藉由簡單的表面處理方式將 SU-8 的
表面修飾成為親水的表面，搭配這兩種技術
組合可以使 SU-8 從一個廣泛使用於微機電系
統的光阻系統轉變成為一個可以用在 DNA 單
一分子在侷限孔隙內傳送控制孔隙材料。結
合此 SU-8奈米孔隙材料所製作之 DNA 單分子
侷限控制傳送過濾之 DNA 分子搭配使用介電
泳(DEP)電場控制開關之虛擬孔道來達成 DNA
分子單分子在流道中操控的目的。

Abstract 
 SU-8 is a widely used negative type 
photo-resist in MEMS. This research proposes a 
facile method to fabricate nanoporous 
microstructures by a photo-patternable SU-8 
photoresist, to serve as a molecular filter in 
micro fluidic systems.  The fabrication process 
involves the solvent-controlled nanoporous 
formation combined with standard 
photolithography steps for microstructure 
fabrication.  The self-formed nanoporous 
morphology embedded inside the microstructure 
exhibits a sufficient mechanical strength and 
eliminates complex processes or protocols for 
the integration/assembly among nano and 
microstructures.  This simple process provides 
a novel way to integrate the nanofiltration 
capability into micro structures while 
maintaining a sufficient mechanical strength for 
molecular level filtration in Lab-On-Chip (LOC) 
systems. 
 The second technology is related to modify 
the SU-8 structure surface by VUV/Ozone 
treatment and analyze the possible functional 
group generated inside closed channel. This 
technology is suitable to fabricate the 
monolithilic SU-8 close channel without any 
chemical contaminations. The modification of 
the surface with SU-8 materials can last for at 
least 2 weeks. Combined nano porous 
fabrication process and SU-8 and surface 
modification will make SU-8 a potential 
material for molecular filter. 
 The third technology involves the 
application of DEP inside a very narrow nano 
channel and make the DEP force large enough 
to control the size of nano channel as a virtual 
nano channel for DNA stretching. These three 
technologies can be combined to control the

的 SU-8 奈米孔隙材料是否適合應用在 DNA
及蛋白質分離系統上。

圖 7 利用原子力顯微鏡測量之 SU-8 表面(a)
經過標準製程之 SU-8 (b)經過溶劑鑄造法製

 由原子力顯微鏡掃描得知在一般標準製
程下的 SU-8光阻具有相當緊實的微結構，因
此缺乏使螢光分子移動的路徑，相較於利用
溶劑鑄造法製程之 SU-8而言，溶劑鑄造法製
程的 SU-8具有較鬆散的微結構，此微結構提
供分子在其中移動的路徑而使分子在此材料
中的擴散移動速度有所不同。 
 借由上述實驗可以驗證得知 SU-8經過溶
劑鑄造法的製程可以有效做成具有分子級孔
隙大小的材料，而此材料對於整合入微晶片
中因為是利用 UV作為鏈結手段具有相當優
勢。
