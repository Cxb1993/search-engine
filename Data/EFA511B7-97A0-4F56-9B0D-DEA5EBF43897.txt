中文摘要 
 
近年來，有許多學者成功地運用圖形與和積演算法來解決不同的問題。其中一個在通訊系
統上最著名的應用就是低密度同位檢查碼。低密度同位檢查碼實質上已將通訊頻道的容量
推到最高可能達到的Shannon 極限。藉助低密度同位檢查碼成功地運用在錯誤更正碼領域
的啟發，在這個計畫提案中，我們提出運用圖形理論與和積演算法來把不同通訊系統中棘
手的排程問題轉成類似像低密度同位檢查碼的解碼問題。在這個計畫提案中，我們所考慮
的通訊系統的排程問題包括：1)在多輸入多輸出通訊系統中的多用戶排程問題，2)低軌衛
星通訊系統中的廣播排程問題。我們相信運用圖形與和積演算法來解通訊系統中的排程問
題是一個全新的突破，而其影響將十分深遠。 
 
關鍵詞：正規圖形、因式圖形、和積演算法、多輸入多輸出的多用戶排程、衛星廣播排程 
 
Abstract 
 
Recently, there have been a lot of researches which have successfully applied graphs and the 
sum-product algorithm to different applications. One famous recent application of graphs and the 
sum-product algorithm is on the LDPC (Low Density Parity Check) code, which virtually pushes 
the channel capacity to the Shannon limit. Inspired by the huge success of the LDPC codes in the 
field of error control coding, in this project, we propose transforming the different 
communication scheduling problems into an LDPC-like problem using a graph and the 
sum-product algorithm. These communication scheduling problems we consider in this project 
including: 1) the mutiuser scheduling problem in a MIMO-based (Multiple-input multiple-output) 
wireless communication system, 2) the satellite broadcast scheduling problem for low-altitude 
satellite communication systems. We believe this approach is revolutionary and its impact is 
significant.  
 
Keywords: Normal Graph, Factor Graph, the Sum-Product Algorithm, Mutiuser MIMO 
Scheduling, Satellite Broadcast Scheduling
