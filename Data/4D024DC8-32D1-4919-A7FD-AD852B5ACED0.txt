新式複合無鉛銲錫之研發及其機械特性之研究 
A Study of Novel Composite Lead-free Solder and Its Mechanical Properties 
計畫編號：NSC96－2221－E－218－027 
執行期限：95 年 08 月 01 日至 96 年 07 月 31 日 
主持人：李洋憲 副教授

一、摘要 
複合銲料是一種有效改善並能強化銲
錫的機械性質之方式。本研究利用散佈強
化概念，分別於 Sn－3.5Ag 的錫膏內添加
單壁奈米碳管（SWCNTs）、多壁奈米碳
管（MCNTs）及二氧化鈦（TiO2）粉末，
然後以機械攪拌方式使粉末顆粒均勻散佈
於錫膏內，藉以形成複合銲料來強化 Sn－
3.5Ag 之基地組織。接著，將試件經由熱儲
存 處 理 來 觀 察 金 屬 間 化 合 物 層
(Intermetallic Compound)之形貌及成長機
制，然後利用光學顯微鏡（OM）、掃描式
顯微鏡（SEM）、EDS、微硬度試驗機、
微負荷拉伸試驗機來探討銲點間所形成之
金屬間化合物層之結合強度、剪切強度等
機械性質，以觀察添加 SWCNTs、MCNTs、
TiO2 對無鉛複合銲錫之微結構變化和拉伸
破斷行為之影響。本研究經實驗結果發現
到 於 Cu/Sn-Ag-0.01wt% SWCNTs 
as-soldered 的條件下進行剪切強度測試，
其破斷面為延性破壞；但隨著持溫時間增
加後其破斷面於介面層開始破壞且呈現劈
裂狀的脆性破壞；此外，當添加 0.1wt%以
上 MCNTs 於 Sn－3.5Ag 內和 Cu 基板結合
其反應介面層的顆粒明顯的變小，並且可
以抑制 IMC 層的成長；而隨著添加二氧化
鈦（TiO2）粉末的增加，其微硬度也隨之
增加，且受到冷卻速率的影響。 
關鍵字：複合銲料、單壁奈米碳管
（SWCNTs）、多壁奈米碳管（MCNTs）、
二氧化鈦(TiO2)、金屬間化合物（IMC）、
微結構、銲點 
 
Abstract 
Solder composition is a method that can 
effectively improve and strengthen the 
mechanical properties of lead-free solder. 
Depending on a concept of distribution of the 
reinforcing phase, this study aims at 
strengthening the matrix of Sn－3.5Ag through 
solder composition. First, SWCNTs, MCNTs, 
and TiO2 powder were appended to Sn－3.5Ag 
lead-free solder paste and were stirred so that 
they were evenly distributed in the paste. Next, 
the sample was stored through heat treatment and 
the microstructure features and growth 
mechanism of its Intermetallic Compound were 
observed. By means of OM, SEM, EDX, a 
Vickers hardness tester, and a universal testing 
machine, the mechanical properties of the IMC 
formed during soldering were investigated, such 
as compressive strength and shear strength, and 
effects of the appendence of SWCNTs, MCNTs, 
and TiO2 powder had on changes of 
microstructures and shear stresses failure 
behaviorof the lead-free composite solder was 
discovered. The results of the study were as 
follows. First, a shear strength test done under the 
condition of Cu/Sn-Ag-0.01wt% SWCNTs 
as-soldered found that broken surface of the 
lead-free composite solder displayed on the 
ductility destruction.However, as aging time 
increased, its broken surface began to crack from 
the surface, which was a cleavage of brittle 
failure. Second, when more than 0.1wt% of 
MCNTs was appended to Sn－3.5Ag and mixed 
with Cu base, Pellet of the IMC became smaller 
and its growth was depressed. Third, the 
microhardness of the IMC was increased as a 
growing amount of TiO2 powder appended and 
was influenced by the cooling rate.  
Key words: Composite solder 、 SWCNTs 、
MCNTs、TiO2、interfacial intermetallic（IMC）、
Microstructure、Solder joints
