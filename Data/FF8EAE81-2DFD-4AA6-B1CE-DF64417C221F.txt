研究摘要(500 字以內)： 
    The development of polyurethane in Taiwan is found to be lack of the key technique to 
obtaine stable and high quality polyurethane. In this proposed project, the new generation 
polyurethane is designed to meet the environmental-friendly materials and upgrade the 
commercial products to compete with international componies. The different molecular 
weight of poly(lactic acid) (PLA) diol is proposed to be synthesized from latic acid through 
esterification reaction. To adjust the obtained molecular weight of PLA diol, the esterification 
reaction time is controlled. The stable and high quality novel PLA based polyurethane are 
designed to be prepared from  prepolymer, diisocyanates and chain extender. The polymer 
film formed through drying of the ink is expected to exhibit good property at low temperature, 
high gloss, high resistance to water and high thermal stability…etc. 
 
Keyword: poly(latic acid) diol, polyurethane, biodegradable, PLA

技術特點說明： 
  In summary, we have successfully prepared biodegradable polyurethanes elastomer with 
poly lactic acid-based diol without catalysts and initiator. Polymerization of LA by 
uncatalyzed direct polycondensation under vacuum was studied with the goal of adapting the 
method to on-site production. These experiments involved controlling only LA and EG feed 
rate, temperature and pressure. Using 4,4-diphenylmethane diisocyanate (MDI) or Toluene 
2.4-diisocyanate  (TDI) ,TMP and 1,4BD as a chain extender. Encouraging results disclosed 
improved thermal stability and enhanced mechanical property. Using PBA and PTMG. The 
result of recovery study demonstrated that the recoverability of PU could be controlled by 
changing the structure. 
  
 
可利用之產業及可開發之產品： 
  主要可應用於環保印刷油墨，環保白板筆油墨開發與生產。本合作案預計可以合成
出不同分子量之聚乳酸二元醇，在學術研究方面，目前市面上無法取得小分子量之聚乳
酸二元醇，因此限制了聚乳酸聚胺酯材料之開發與研究，因此本合作案預期能製備出不
同分子量之聚乳酸二元醇，並且合成出市面上還未出現之聚乳酸聚胺酯材料，預期此材
料會有更優異之機械性質，而且也是本質性生物可分解之新一代環保塑膠材料。在水性
聚乳酸聚胺酯乳液與分散劑搭配下，勢必可製得可穩定使用之高性能環保油墨。對合作
企業而言，本計劃可以讓合作企業由進口貿易商提升至生產擁有自我品牌之水性聚乳酸
聚胺酯乳液、顏料分散劑與水性油墨材料。 
 
推廣及運用的價值： 
在本計劃案的執行結束後，我們會提出一個可行性的研究與商業化的計劃給合作企
業，在雙方達成目標的共識後，我們將會以本研究成果來協助業者發展，計劃性生產新
型的聚乳酸二元醇、分散劑與水性聚胺酯材料；同時也會協助業者將這些材料應用來嘗
試分散即有之顏料中以形成新型之環保油墨材料，藉以提升產品的獨特性與自我研發之
優勢，相信這會為合作企業帶來為數可觀的利潤。在這同時，若是業者在推廣新產品的
過程中遭遇到技術方面之困難，也可以共同商討問題之解決方法。
