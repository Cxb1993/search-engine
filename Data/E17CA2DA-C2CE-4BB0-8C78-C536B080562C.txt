研究主題一、清洗油品污染土壤 
ㄧ、研究背景及目的 
近年來台灣多處土地受油品污染，因油品通常儲存於地下儲油槽，當輸送管
線破損或儲油槽老舊腐蝕等途徑，常導致油品洩漏污染土壤及地下水。環保署於
2008年調查台灣土地之污染情形得知，台灣土壤污染主要為受總石油碳氫化合
物(Total Petroleum Hydrocarbon, TPH)之污染居多，因此對於 TPH污染之整治技
術開發為一重要課題。 
處理土壤油品污染土壤之整治方式眾多，而土壤清洗為一項快速且經濟效益
上可行之離址處理油品污染土壤方式。此外，土壤清洗可用於整治大面積之污染
場址，因其操作容易處理效率高，且其處理設備可依場址需求而移動具高機動性，
因此對於污染場址土壤急需完成整治以進行土地轉移買賣時，土壤清洗法為值得
加以評估的整治技術(蘇及陳, 2008)。 
TPH污染土壤之整治，當使用離址土壤清洗方式進行整治時，因污染場址
之土壤特性(例如：土壤質地)、污染程度、以及清洗之操作參數(例如：清洗液之
濃度)將會影響整治成效，因此本計劃研究目的為清洗 TPH污染土壤進行操作條
件之評估，篩選適當清洗液，提供應用於現址實場處理時所需的操作參數。 
 
二、文獻探討 
土壤清洗－TPH污染土壤 
土壤清洗處理 TPH污染土壤常使用界面活性劑(Surfactant)作為清洗液之主
要配方，界面活性劑有四種類型，分別為：陰離子、陽離子、非離子、兩性界面
活性劑等四類。 
界面活性劑為兩性物質，常由 8~18碳原子以直鏈或支鏈之結構組成並附加
一極性分子組合而成，因此同時具有親水端及疏水端，可溶於水及油相中，界面
活性劑濃度較低時，界面活性劑以單體存在，使界面張力下降，然而當濃度大於
臨界微胞濃度(Critical micelle concentration, CMC)時，則會形成微胞，對於不同
之污染物質或不同之界面活性劑所需之 CMC值會有所不同，而當微胞形成時疏
水端會朝內，親水端則朝外，疏水性污染物會溶解於疏水端，形成溶解之假相
(Pseudo-phase)，此現象將可使污染土壤中之 TPH自土壤洗滌分離。

五、結論 
    實驗結果，當 SDS濃度增加至 10 %以上，雖然對 TPH去除率有上升
之趨勢，但 SDS濃度 10 %已接近水中最大溶解度，因此無法配製較高濃
度 SDS之溶液； AEO對 TPH去除率則不佳，且 AEO內含有許多的珍珠
岩物質，當配製高濃度 AEO時，會造成固體物懸浮於表面之現象；SPAN 
80在濃度為 10~20 %時，界面活性劑會殘留在土壤中，且經由 RO水洗程

2.2 Experimental procedure 
A fixed soil to liquid ratio of 1 to 3 (w/v) was used in the batch studies.  100 g 
samples of composite soil were added into a series of 1L beakers and each soil sample 
was mixed with 300 mL of washing solution.  The resulting soil slurries were then 
stirred using a six-paddle jar tester, equipped with 1” × 3” stainless steel paddles, 
which was set at a speed of 200 rpm for 5 mins.  Thereafter, the slurries were 
allowed to settle for 30 mins and then were again stirred at a lower speed of 50 rpm 
for 60 mins.  After washing, the slurries were set to equilibrate for 60 mins and 
turbidity was measured at 10-min intervals. 
In the experiments designed to screen for a suitable washing solution, 
concentrations of between 10 and 300 mM for HCl, H2SO4, and CA and of between 
10 and 100 mM for EDTA solutions were tested.  Upon the determination of a 
suitable chemical reagent, based on the removal efficiency of total metals, higher 
concentration levels (i.e., up to 1000 mM) were used in order to establish an optimum 
operational concentration.  
2.3 Analytical methods 
The heavy metals in the soil samples were acid digested according to the 
analytical method of the Taiwan National Institute of Environmental Analysis (Taiwan 
NIEA) S321.63B.  The heavy metals were measured using a Perkin Elmer Analyst 
100 flame atomic absorption spectrophotometer in accordance with the method of 
Taiwan NIEA W306.52A.  The pH was measured using a pH meter (Thermo Orion 
720A+) equipped with a ROSS pH combination electrode.  Turbidity was measured 
using a Hach 2100 N turbidimeter.

4. Conclusion 
 The efficiency of the removal of Cu, Ni, Zn and Cr was influenced by the type of 
washing solutions used, i.e., strong acids or chelating agents.  Separation of heavy 
metals from the soils using by HCl and H2SO4 occurred mainly due to acid dissolution 
at an acidic pH (around 2) while removal of heavy metals using CA and EDTA was 
the result of a complexation mechanism.  Among the washing reagents studied, the 
usage of H2SO4 achieved the highest removal efficiency in the batch study and 
therefore it was selected for further pilot scale tests.  Removal efficiencies recorded 
during the washing of field contaminated soils in the pilot scale tests were comparable 
to those achieved in the batch study.  Evaluation of both laboratory- and pilot-scale 
tests demonstrates the feasibility of soil washing using H2SO4.  However, the 
relationship between metal separation and fractionation has not yet been determined.  
The usage of other potential washing reagents and the effects of soil particle size 
should be further explored and the determination of an optimum soil to liquid ratio 
must also be undertaken before full scale on site remediation is attempted.  In this
