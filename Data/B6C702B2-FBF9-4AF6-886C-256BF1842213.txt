ABSTRACT
The isolation and characterization of the indigenous dioxin-biotransformation
bacteria of Taiwan was investigated in this study. After that, the culture enrichment,
and strains acclimation were also conducted for the isolated bacteria for
bioremediation. The soil samples, Tainan#1, Tainan#2 and Tainan#3 were gathering
from the downstream of the announced dioxin contamination site in southern part of
Taiwan, and analyze PCDDs/PCDFs concentration to confirm the contamination of
the soil. According to the results, Tainan#3 has the highest total toxic equivalence,
2450ng- TEQ/kg of the contaminants. After go through ten sub-culture inoculum, four
bacteria strains which can survive in dioxin media were isolated. The identification
results shown the strains were Achromobacter xylosoxidans 、 Ochrobactrum
anthropi、Ralstonia mannitolilytica and Agromyces sp.. The morphology of the strains
shown that all of the strains are gram’s negative. The configuration of the strain
presented that Ochrobactrum anthropi and Agromyces sp. obtain the length and width
of 2μm and 1μm, which are bigger than others. Phylogenetic analysis showed that
Agromyces sp. is the closest with Mycobacterium chlorophenolicum strain PCP-1,
which is a PCP-degrading bacterium that with bootstrap value of 95.
After the growth test, it decrease inhibition of growth by add 1000 mg/L glucose
in the culture contain 10 μg/kg of 2378PCDD. According to the kinetic analysis,
bacterium Achromobacter xylosoxidans and Ochrobactrum anthropi achieve the best
growth condition with the maximum specific growth rate of 0.115h-1. Ralstonia
mannitolilytica has the smallest Ks with the value of 43.2 mg/L. In substrate inhibitory,
Ochrobactrum anthropi has the greatest inhibition coefficient of 0.948 mg/L.
Concerning the dioxin degradation, strain Achromobacter xylosoxidans 、
Ochrobactrum anthropi、Agromyces sp., can degraded 2,3,7,8-TCDD in lag phase,
however, the result for the strain of Ralstonia mannitolilytica is not observed. For
Ralstonia mannitolilytica, log phase is the major decomposition period for
2,3,7,8-TCDD. In these strains, Agromyces sp. has the better degradation efficiency
than the others, and the 2,3,7,8-TCDD was decline by 97%. The biotransformation
enzyme for the toxicant 2,3,7,8-TCDD request more study.
Key words: bacteria, biotransformation, dioxin, isolation, screening, soil

三、研究方法
本研究的碳源濃度與生長動力分析，主要是以2,3,7,8-TCDD為主要碳源，葡
萄糖做為輔助碳源，觀察菌株生長情況，而尋找出最佳的初始碳源濃度與分析菌
株生長動力。以最佳碳源濃度做為培養基質，量測2,3,7,8-TCDD濃度隨菌株生長
變化，本研究的分析，以菌株生長的適應期、生長期等階段做為探討。蛋白質體
的分析，係以聚丙烯醯胺膠體電泳(SDS-PAGE)做為分析方法，瞭解分解菌之蛋
白質因2,3,7,8-TCDD產生的變化。詳細研究方法與材料如下所述：
1. 分解菌生長碳源濃度分析
本實驗主要是觀察分解菌株在含有 2,3,7,8-TCDD 的環境下的生長情況，藉此
瞭解菌株生長的最佳碳源環境。由於 2,3,7,8-TCDD 對於微生物有極高之毒性，
有文獻指出，當 2,3,7,8-TCDD 含量超過10μg/L後，菌株生長會有明顯的抑制情
形發生(Ishii et al., 2008)，故在本實驗中 2,3,7,8-TCDD 濃度固定在10μg/L並添加
不同濃度葡萄糖(glucose)做為輔助碳源，觀察其生長情況之變化。
在含有10μg/L 的 2,3,7,8-TCDD 及 MSB 混和液中添加葡萄糖，其添加的濃度
分別為 0、10、100、1000、10000mg/L 並填裝至 20×150 的試管中，蓋上透氣塞
後，送入滅菌釜進行滅菌。將菌體倒入滅菌且冷卻後的培養基中，利用試管振盪
器(Vortex Mixer)將菌體與培養液混和均勻後，以 30℃、125rpm 恆溫震盪培養。
本研究以 OD600 做為菌株生長之指標，以 BioLog turbidimeter 做為量測儀器。
2. 分解菌生長動力分析
本研究生長動力係以 Monod model 和 Haldane model(Simkins, S., & Alexander,
M., 1984)為主要分析模式。兩模式之差異為，Monod model 主要針對分解菌在單
一碳源環境下的生長動力分析；Haldane model 則是以基質高濃度抑制為主。
3. 2,3,7,8-TCDD 降解測試
取 50uL 菌液，將其置於內含正己烷(Hexane)的 20mL 試管裡搖晃。混和均勻
後，以注射針添加內標準品(M1613_IS_L：10/20 pg/uL，20uL)及替代標準品
(Clup：5 pg/uL，20uL)，放置於震盪機震盪數秒，充分混合均勻，再置於離心機
離心，吸取有機層進行淨化。有機層淨化需經(a)酸性矽膠管柱淨化、(b)活性碳
管柱淨化及(c)氮吹轉移至 KD 管後上機分析，實驗步驟以環檢所公告戴奧辛及呋
喃檢測方法（NIEAM801.11B）進行。
