by using the methyl trimethoxy silane (MTOS) and 
trimethyl phosphate (TMP) as the precursors, which 
underwent hydrolysis and condensation in the 
dispersant at room temperature. Accordingly, the SiOx 
and PxOy nano-particles were obtained as dispersion, 
respectively. The PMMA composites were then prepared 
by a traditional casting and thermal polymerization 
process using polymer/monomer syrup mixed with the 
dispersion.  
The particle size and distribution of the nano-
particles as-prepared and after time aging were 
measured by particle size analyzer (PSA) and scanning 
electron microscopy (SEM). Thermogravity analyzer 
(TGA) and modulated differential scanning calorimetry 
(MDSC) were used to measure the thermal stability and 
glass transition temperature (Tg) of the composites. 
UV/Vis spectroscopy, as well as the visual, was used 
to observe the transmittance of each samples. 
Finally, the flame-retardant of composites were 
examined by limited oxygen index (LOI). 
Experimental results showed that the solvent-free 
sol-gel process is successful for a nano-particle 
dispersion production and consequent cast and thermal 
polymerization are successful for the composites 
preparation. The obtained nano-particles reinforced 
PMMA composites can be high transparent and flame-
retardant. The average LOI of SiOx-MAA system was 27, 
SiOx-HEMA system was 26, PxOy-MAA system was 29, and 
the PxOy-MAA system was 28, respectively. 
Consequently, the SiOx and PxOy nano-particles could 
enhance the LOI of the prepared composites when 
compared with the neat PMMA which has a LOI of 18, 
and keeping the reinforced composites transparent.

摘要 
本研究係以溶膠凝膠法 (sol-gel process) 製備耐燃之甲基丙烯酸酯
(poly(methyl methacrylate)；PMMA)複合材料。實驗中，以丙烯酸類化合物取代
溶膠凝膠法中之溶劑，作為反應型溶劑並作為分散劑以均勻分散溶膠凝膠溶液中
之奈米無機顆粒，以完成無溶劑之溶膠凝膠反應。 
在分散劑中，將甲基三甲基矽烷(Methyl trimethoxy silane；MTOS)及三甲基
磷酸酯(Trimethyl phosphate；TMP)分別經水解(hydrolysis)、縮合(condensation)
後，可各自製得無機奈米顆粒(氧化矽、氧化磷)之溶膠凝膠溶液。再將所製得之
無機奈米顆粒添加進 PMMA/MMA 漿液中，經澆注及熱處理後可製得複合材料。 
實驗結果顯示：依本計畫研究所提之無溶劑溶膠-凝膠法可製得高分散性之
奈米顆粒分散液，而應用傳統的澆注方式與熱聚合法可製得 PMMA/奈米顆粒複
合材料。所製得之複合材料具有高透明性，且其 LOI 值均有所提升：SiOx-MAA
系統之 LOI 值平均為 27、SiOx-HEMA 系統之 LOI 值平均為 26、PxOy-MAA 系
統之 LOI 值平均為 29，而 PxOy-HEMA 其平均值為 28。相較於 PMMA 之 LOI
值 18，各系統之 LOI 值均有顯著提升，可說明本計畫研究所得之 PMMA 複合材
料具有耐燃性。

材料、耐燃性、極限氧指數

四. 結論 
以本研究所提之方法- 以無溶劑溶膠-凝膠法製備奈米無機顆粒溶液(分散
液)，再將所製得之奈米無機顆粒溶液與 Mo/Po 漿液進行混合，再經熱聚合可製
得無機奈米顆粒/PMMA 複合材料。據此，成功地高值化(甲基)丙烯酸甲酯(MMA; 
methyl methacrylate)在奈米顆粒/PMMA 透明材的製造與應用上。所製得之複合
材料經過耐燃性能 LOI 測試後，可知含 SiOx系統之各樣品其 LOI 值介於 25-28
之間，依 LOI 值對難燃性之規範可歸類為可自行消火性材料。而含 PxOy系統之
各樣品其 LOI 值為 27-30，可使得複合材料提升其耐燃性而成為難燃性之材料。
因此，本研究中所製得之各樣品之耐燃性皆比一般級之 PMMA 之 LOI 值 18 及
具交鏈 PMMA 之 LOI 值 20 提升許多。因此，藉由本方法可製得高透明級之難
燃 PMMA。據此，已將此技術與產品申請發明專利 1 份，並已取得發明專利(台
灣發明專利號碼：I 363781(2012/05/11)，如附件 1 所示)。
