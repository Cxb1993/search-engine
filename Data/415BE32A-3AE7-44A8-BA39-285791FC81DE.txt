細胞活性之測試 (WST assay) 
利用WST-1 kit來測試細胞生長活性。將待測活性
細胞之樣品中的培養液吸除後，以PBS清洗三
次。加入180 μl之新鮮培養液及20 μl之WST-1 
溶液，置於37℃、5% CO2之細胞培養箱內避光反
應1.5小時，利用ELASA reader於波長450 nm下測
量吸光值。 
 
鹼性磷酸酶活性測試 
鹼性磷酸酶酵素在體外於鹼性環境下，能催
化水解單酯磷酸(monoester phosphate)。故利用此
原理測試鹼性磷酸酶水解單酯磷酸受質後，釋放
出p-nitrophenol的量，進而評估鹼性磷酸酶之活
性。將MG63類骨母細胞株培養於待測樣品上於第
7，14，21天後取出，以無菌PBS沖洗數次後，加
入150 μl的PBS溶液 (pH10.5,含0.1 M glycine、1 
mM MgCl2以及1% Trixon X-100)，於室溫下靜置1
小時，添加200 μl含p-nitrophenylphosphate 之
Alkaline Phosphatase Yellow Liquid Substrate，置於
37℃恆溫水槽中30分鐘，隨後加入50 μl之3N 
NaOH。利用ELASA reader於波長405 nm下測量吸
光值，並以未含有細胞之樣品作空白測試。。

西方轉漬點墨法(Wesrern blotting)

internal control。 
 
礦化分析測定(Alizarin red-based assay of 
mineralization) 
將 MG63 類骨母細胞株培養於膠原蛋白與
明膠電氣紡織纖維基材上，於 7、14、21 天後，

固定後，加入 2% Alizarin Red S 染劑於室溫下作
用十五分鐘。再加入適量之 acetic acid，最後將細
胞刮下並移至離心管中進行震盪。之後，在離心
管中加入適量之礦物油，在 85°C 下加熱十分鐘，
再進行離心。離心後，取下層液體並加入 200ul
的 ammonium hydroxide (10% v/v)與之反應，並以
可見光 405nm 進行測定分析。

INTRODUCTION 
Hepatocytes are anchorage-dependent cells and 
highly sensitive to the extracellular matrix and known 
to rapidly lose their phenotype in vitro. Numerous 
studies demonstrated that the phenotype and several 
differentiated functions of hepatocytes are better 
maintained in three-dimensional than in 
two-dimensional culture systems (1). Over the past 
decades, many three-dimensional culture hepatocytes 
in vitro systems were established including collagen 
sandwiches, Matrigel™ cultures (2) and 
microencapsulation system (3).  
Besides, there were several studies also shown that 
material surface conjugated with galactosed ligands 
can improve hepatocyte attachment and maintain most 
of the cellular functions (4). Pectin is one of the 
naturally biopolymers has been used frequently in both 
bio-industrial and biomedical application. Compared 
with polysaccharides from animals, pectin is a more 
inexpensive and uncomplicated purified polysaccharide 
source. Pectin is a heterogeneous polymer of 
α-(1–4)-D-galacturonic acid of varying degrees of 
esterification, neutral sugar substitution and molecular 
weight. 
In our work, we utilize pectin as matrix to 
encapsulate hepatocytes compared previous works 
modified the cultured surface with galactose ligands for 
enhanced hepatocytes attachment. A variety of 
techniques could be employed to fabricate microbeads. 
Electro-spraying is one of the methods that could 
produce the microbeads with diameter less than 300 
μm and with narrow distribution. Therefore, the aim of 
the study is to estimate the cellular functions of 
hepatocytes encapsulated in pectin compared in 
alginate.  
 
EXPERIMENTAL METHODS 
Human hepatoblastoma (HepG2, BRBC 60025) 
was used in the experiments. The pectin solution was 
mixed cells to attain the final concentrations of 4% 
w/v pectin and cell density of 2.5×106 cells/ml. The 
encapsulation of cells in alginate was prepared by 
the same procedure as stated above instead of 
concentration of alginate was 2% w/v.  
An electrostatic droplet generator was employed 
to prepare microcapsulations. The syringe was placed 
in a syringe pump at the solution flow rate of 25.4 
ml/hr and a high voltage electrostatic system was 
applied. The positive electrode of the electrostatic 
system was connected to the needle, while the negative 
electrode was placed in the collection solution 10 cm 
away from the needle tip. 
The WST assay used to determine cell viability. 
The capsules were collected at various time intervals 
after inoculation. The reagent WST-1 was added and 
incubated further for 1.5 hours. The level of dye 
formed was then immediately measured by a 
microplate reader at a wavelength of 405 nm. The 
amount of urea was determined according to the 
method of Jung et al (5).
