三、研究方法 
本計畫實驗執行可以分成兩部分，第一利用是利用有機金屬化學氣相沉積法(MOCVD) 
在 c 平面氧化鋁基板上先進行 ZnO 晶種層磊晶成長，隨後在氧化鋅晶種上以低溫水熱法成
長氧化鋅奈米柱磊晶。並發現由於氧化鋅與氧化鋁有較大晶格不匹配性，導致氧化鋅奈米
柱與氧化鋁基板有不只一種磊晶方位關係。並且進一步發現，氧化鋅奈米柱之磊晶方位會
受到氧化鋅晶種成長條件影響，並可以隨著製程條件控制呈現不同磊晶方位關係。第二部
分，經由控制化學氣相沉積法的成長動力學和基板的表面結構，可以直接透過有機金屬化
學氣相沉積(MOCVD)沉積法，能直接在(0001)平面的氧化鋁表面上不需事先透過黃光微影
等圖案定義製程處理，成長出自我組裝週期排列氧化鋅奈米結構。 
利用有機金屬化學氣相沉積(MOCVD)在 c 平面氧化鋁基板上磊晶成長 ZnO 晶種層。
在成長 ZnO 晶種層之前 c-plane 氧化鋁基板會先以 1200 oC 的溫度退火 4 小時，然後用丙
酮震盪清洗。利用低壓熱壁型MOCVD 來進行磊晶成長，採用Zinc acetylacetonate (Zn(acac)2, 
99.995 purity)和 O2 (99.999 purity)分別用來當作 Zn 與 O 的來源。待氧化鋅晶種磊晶成長完
成之後，在此氧化鋅磊晶晶種上繼續以水熱法進行 ZnO 奈米柱的成長。而水熱法之成長液
則是將 Zinc nitrate hexahydrate 溶解在去離子水中，濃度為 0.013 M，並放置在密封的鐵氟
龍壓力鍋中，加入 ammonium hydroxide 將 PH 值調整到 10.8。將已成長晶種層的基板放置
在鐵弗龍的載台上，表面向下懸掛在溶液中。然後將壓力鍋密封後放入烘箱。水熱法的製
程在 95 oC 的溫度下持溫 90 分鐘。之後，將基板從壓力鍋中取出，用去離子水沖洗後放在
室溫中乾燥。觀察改變氧化鋅晶種不同的成長溫度及氧化鋁基板表面前處理，對氧化鋅奈
米柱與氧化鋁基板磊晶方位的影響。ZnO 奈米柱與氧化鋁基板之間 Out-of-plane 和 in-plane
磊晶方位關係可以分別用 X-ray 繞射(XRD, Philips X’Pert diffractometer with a Cu K 
radiation source) 2/-和-scans來檢測。再利用場發掃瞄電子顯微鏡(FESEM, Hitachi S-4700)
來觀察形態。並透過穿透電子顯微鏡(TEM)的觀察氧化鋅在氧化鋁基板上成長介面結構。

重要的因素，因為在 CVD 成長過程中原子的吸附、擴散和沉積在基板表面，是非常依賴成
長溫度。影響形成自我週期排列奈米結構成長的條件，此刻仍然是不明確的，將在持續於
未來工作中進行探討。 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
在本研究中，自我週期排列氧化鋅奈米結構，可以直接經由 MOCVD 被成長在 c 軸氧化鋁
基板上，且不需要透過的微影製程。根據 XRD 和 TEM 特性描述的結果，這些氧化鋅奈米
結構磊晶成長在基板，遵循著(0001)ZnO||(0001)Al2O3 和(11-20)ZnO||(10-10)Al2O3 關係，經由 AFM
的觀察週期性氧化鋅奈米結構，已經被證實自我週期排列氧化鋅奈米結構的成長和氧化鋁
基板表面階梯結構有關。因此氧化鋁週期階梯結構提供了自我週期性排列之奈米結構磊晶
成長的自然樣板。自我週期性排列氧化鋅奈米結構成長，依賴在初始氧化鋁表面結構和
CVD 成長動力學。 
 
五、結論及建議 
本計畫實驗執行可以分成兩部分，第一部分是利用有機金屬化學氣相沉積法(MOCVD) 
在 c 平面氧化鋁基板上先進行 ZnO 晶種層磊晶成長，隨後在氧化鋅晶種上以低溫水熱法成
長氧化鋅奈米柱磊晶。在本研究中進一步發現，氧化鋅奈米柱之磊晶方位會受到氧化鋅晶
種成長條件影響，並可以隨著製程條件控制呈現不同磊晶方位關係。可以發現除了大部分
的 ZnO 奈米柱都與氧化鋁的表面垂直之外，也發現大量氧化鋅奈米柱呈現規則 38o傾斜，

七、計畫成果自評 
本研究為計畫參與碩士班研究生李政偉碩士論文之一部分。由實驗結果來看，本研究
已經初步掌握，可以透過控制氧化鋅晶種磊晶製程條件及氧化鋁基板前處理等製程參數對
於氧化鋅奈米柱在氧化鋁基板上各種磊晶方位關係。另外也發現經過適當MOCVD製程條件的
控制，可以不需透過微影製程，在氧化鋁基板上直接磊晶成長出自我週期排列氧化鋅奈米
結構。這些研究結果可供作為來年進一步進行磊晶成長氧化鋅奈米結構之研究計畫所需。
透過最佳化條件成長，進而控制氧化鋅奈米結構排列及成長方向將對於將氧化鋅奈米結構
在實際應用應有相當程度之幫助。

Epitaxial growth of self-arranged periodic ZnO nanostructures 
on sapphire substrates grown by MOCVD 
Hou-Guang Chen*, Sheng-Rui Jian, Zheng-Wei Li, Hong-De Lian, Jhih-Cheng Li 
Department of Materials Science and Engineering, I-Shou University, Kaohsiung 840, Taiwan 
 
This article reports an investigation on the growth behaviour of ZnO epitaxial nanostructures 
and thin films grown by metalorganic chemical vapour deposition (MOCVD). Self-arranged 
periodic ZnO nanostructures consisting of a large number of ZnO nano-columns can be 
directly grown on bare sapphire surface without any lithography or other pre-patterning 
processes. The spacing of periodic nanostructure was ~117 nm. The measurement of XRD 
2 and  scans indicated that epitaxial and non-epitaxial ZnO grains coexisted on the same 
substrate. According to cross-sectional transmission electron microscopy observation, these 
periodic ZnO nanostructures were epitaxially grown on sapphire substrates and separated by 
non-epitaxial ZnO grains. However, the in-plane periodic arrangement of ZnO nanostructures 
disappeared while increasing the growth temperature. Initial sapphire surface structure and 
CVD growth kinetics closely relate to the self-arranged periodic ZnO nanostructures growth.

While wide range of substrates has been used for growing high quality ZnO epitaxial thin 
film [13-15], c-plane sapphire is more favored substrate for the epitaxial growth of ZnO thin 
films or nanostructures, owing to low cost and large size single-crystal being commercially 
available. In this article, we show a self-arranged periodic striped ZnO nanostructures can be 
directly grown on the (0001) plane sapphire surface by controlling CVD growth kinetic and 
substrate surface structure without any complicated lithography or sophisticated process. The 
mechanism of the growth of self-arranged periodic ZnO nanostructure on sapphire is 
proposed and discussed. 
2. Experimental procedure  
A reduced pressure hot-wall type MOCVD apparatus was employed to implement the 
epitaxial growth of ZnO nanostructures on c-plane sapphire substrates. Zinc acetylacetonate 
(Zn(acac)2, 99.995 purity) and O2 (99.999 purity) were used as the zinc and oxygen source, 
respectively. The precursor of Zn(acac)2 was heated at 90 oC before introduction into the 
chamber by carrier gas of nitrogen (99.99 %), which was separated from oxygen flow before 
reaching the substrate surface to avoid to direct reaction of oxygen with zinc source. Prior to 
ZnO growth, as-received c-plane sapphire substrates in 1x1 cm2 size was annealed at 1200 oC 
in air for 4 hr and then were ultrasonically cleaned by acetone. The ZnO nanostructures were 
grown at temperatures of 450 and 500 oC, respectively, for 120 min.

4. Conclusion 
In this article, a self-arranged periodic nanostructure consisting of epitaxial ZnO 
nano-columns have been directly grown on bare c-plane sapphire substrates by MOCVD 
without any pre-patterning processes. According to the results of XRD and TEM 
characterization, these periodic ZnO nanostructure were epitaxially grown on substrates 
following the relation of (0001)ZnO||(0001)sapphire and  ZnO0211 || sapphire]0110[ . The periodicity 
of ZnO nanostructure was closely related to the mean terrace width which was verified by 
AFM observation. Periodically faceted surface of annealed sapphire provide a natural 
template for the epitaxial growth of self-organized periodic nanostructures. The self-arranged 
periodic ZnO nanostructures growth significantly depends on the initial sapphire surface 
structure and CVD growth kinetics.
