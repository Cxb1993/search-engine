2made from carbon materials. However, the PEMFC carbon materials/components
possibly contain toxic polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons (PAHs), and probably the
carbon-adsorbed PAHs may desorb and be emitted from PEMFC off-gases during
operation. Therefore, this study investigated the PAH content in several carbon
materials commonly used for PEMFC components and the PAH emission from a single
H2-O2 PEMFC.
Among 12 tested carbon materials, the plasma-generated carbon blacks exhibited
the highest total-PAHs content (7466 ± 1374 μg/g) corresponding to a total-BaPeq of
265 ± 74.8 μg/g; in contrast, a water-proofed carbon paper displayed the lowest
total-PAHs content (9.66 ± 1.29 μg/g) corresponding to a total-BaPeq of 1.13 ± 0.01
μg/g. The content of the 9 types of carbon grains followed the order of plasma carbon
black > CSCC graphite > MWNT > SWNT > Alfa > XC-72 > CSCC carbon > XC-72R
> bamboo carbon.
Within the tested anode and cathode flowrate ranges, the anode gas-phase
total-PAHs concentrations were in order 30 > 100 > 200 > 52 sccm, with a
corresponding total-BaPeq concentration order 52 > 30 > 100 > 200 sccm, whereas
both anode water-phase total-PAHs and total-BaPeq concentrations showed the order
30 > 52 > 100 > 200 sccm. For the cathode, both gas-phase total-PAHs and total-BaPeq
concentrations were in order 18 > 35 > 100 > 200 sccm; the water-phase total-PAHs
concentrations were also in order 18 > 35 > 100 > 200 sccm but the corresponding
total-BaPeq concentrations were in order 35 > 100 > 18 > 200 sccm. When the cell
operating temperature rose from 65 to 90 oC, both gas-phase total-PAHs and
total-BaPeq concentrations slightly increased; on the contrary, both water-phase
total-PAHs and total-BaPeq concentrations slightly decreased. Despite the variation of
flowrate and temperature, the emission of total-PAHs was higher for the cathode than
for the anode.
The gas- and water-phase total-PAHs and total-BaPeq emission concentrations
decreased with the increase of Nafion loading (0.6–0.92 mg/cm2) on carbon paper, so
brushing Nafion (solution) on the diffusion layers may lower the emission of PAHs
from the single cell. With increasing Nafion loading, the reduction of gas-phase PAH
emission from the anode and cathode was in order LMW-PAHs > MMW-PAHs >
HMW-PAHs, while that of water-phase PAH emission was in order LMW-PAHs >
MMW-PAHs≈HMW-PAHs.

2.2 PAHs之致癌性及致突變性
現今 PAHs 已被證實具有致癌性，Gimmer (1983)指出，若是長期暴露在
PAHs 環境之中，可能會造成皮膚病、呼吸道疾病與神經系統上的損傷，並有致
突變性及致癌性之風險。 Davis (1987)研究發現若長期曝露在 PAHs 的環境下可
能導致急性或慢性疾病。廖永生 (2000)亦指出 PAHs 中之 Bap 會造成肺腺癌及
鱗狀細胞癌，其接觸方式主要仍是以呼吸系統進入人體。 PAHs 中所含主要致癌
物為四至七環的化合物，其中最具代表性者為 BaP，它已被證實具有致癌性與致
突變性。曾有研究指出，以 BaP 對實驗動物(大老鼠)施以皮膚塗抹，以每週塗抹
200 μg 之 BaP 於小鼠皮膚上，持續 24 週後會使小鼠產生鱗狀細胞癌、乳頭狀
瘤，其產生腫瘤率高達 66％ ，因此可證明 BaP 明顯的具有致癌及致突變潛勢之
毒理反應（Shimizu et al., 2000）。有鑑於此， BaP 常被用為 PAHs 毒性指標。
表 2 為 PAHs 毒性當量係數，依據此表可將其他 PAHs 物種濃度換算成 BaP 之
當量濃度，進而作為人體健康風險評估之依據（Nisbet et al., 1992）。除了其 PAHs
本身之毒理反應之外，倘若 PAHs 與強氧化性之污染物起光化學反應時，將會產
生毒性更高之產物。例如 Perylene (PER) 本來不具致癌性，但若是與大氣中的 NO2
反應，則會生成高致癌性之硝化 PAHs－3 Nitroperylene （Usha, 1989），因此對
人類健康的危害便大幅提升。另有研究指出，二、三環之 PAHs 本身並無明顯之
致突變性，但形成硝基衍生物（Nitro-PAHs）後則具有強烈的致突變性，不需酵
素活化過程，即能對人體健康產生危害，因此造成都市大氣污染物於冬季期間之
致突變性比例增大（Tuominen et al., 1988）。

此次2008年歐洲氣膠研討會於民國97年8月24~29日在希臘塞薩羅尼基(Thessaloniki)
舉行，本研究團隊計發表(一)“Carbons and PAHs emitted from a diesel generator fuelled
with soy-biodiesel blends”、(二)“Photochemical performance for nitrate and sulfate in
nano and ultrafine particles”、(三)“Effects of traffic loading upon atmospheric coarse/fine
particulates”及 (四 ) “Polychlorinated dibenzo-p-dioxins/dibenzofurans from incense
burning”等四篇論文。論文主要內容旨在探討使用生質柴油對引擎排放污染物特性之探
討、不同採樣地點與交通量對大氣粗、細及奈米微粒組成特性影響之探討、微粒上硝酸
鹽與硫酸鹽之光化學作用特性之探討及機動車輛尾氣與拜香燃煙中戴奧辛及呋喃化合物
特性之探討。筆者於97年8月23日下午15:00搭乘高鐵自左營站到桃園站再轉搭接駁公車
