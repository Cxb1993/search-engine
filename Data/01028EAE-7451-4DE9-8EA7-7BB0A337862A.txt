Volatile organic compounds (VOCs) are the most common air pollutants emitted from
pharmaceutical, printing, shoe-making, painting industries that cause great harm to the environment,

factories. If acetone is discharged without treatment, it will cause serious environmental problems.

Resolution Transmission Electron Microscope (HRTEM). MSM was applied in the acetone adsorption,
studied in the flow system. Several parameters such as initial concentration, the flow rate, adsorbent
dosage and adsorbent regeneration were systematically investigated. Reaction kinetics and mechanism
were derived. The research of microscopic structure illustrated the synthetic materials retained
long-range order mesoporous structure. The results showed that the surface area, pore volume and pore
size was roughly ranged from 880 to 1060 m2 g-1, 1.05 cm3 g-1 and 4.0 nm, respectively. The adsorption
equilibrium revealed that adsorption capacity of MSM was 59.6, 65.7, 69.6, 84.9 mg g-1 while the

acetone follows Langmuir equation better which is a monomolecular adsorption model. Dynamic
analyses show that the adsorption of acetone fits well with the Pseudo-second-order dynamic equation.

capacity of MSM was 65.7, 73.56, 97.58mg g-1 while the adsorbent dosage was 0.25, 0.8 and 1.0g
respectively. As the adsorbent dosage increased, the time of breakthrough was extended.

VOCs 具有滲透、脂溶及揮發等特性，大部分的 VOCs 不但帶有毒性，可藉由接觸、吸入與食入

處理 VOCs 的方法大致可分為：吸收法、活性碳吸附法、冷凝法、熱焚化法、觸媒焚化法及生物
處理法等。吸收法、活性碳法皆有產生液相、固相的二次污染困擾；冷凝法及生物處理則其處理
效率有限；熱焚化法由於需要較高的溫度來破壞 VOCs，所以需要大量的燃料，但是對於處理低濃
度的揮發性有機污染物較不經濟；而觸媒焚化法可以較短的滯留時間及較低的溫度下來操作，而
且設備較不佔空間、亦較節省燃料、處理量大、轉化率也高，也不會因高溫而產生 Thermal NOx 二
次污染物的困擾，故觸媒焚化法是目前處理 VOCs 技術中最具發展潛力的。

（二） 吸附模式之建立
等溫吸附曲線是在固定溫度下，變化不同吸附分子的壓力所得的曲線圖，如 Freundlich 方程式
假設吸附劑表面為不均勻，由圖形中便可判別出利用 Freundlich 模式所回歸出的吸附材吸附性能優
劣。Freundlich 方程式(如下)適用高濃度溶液下吸附行為之描述，在低濃度下，較不符合實際的吸
附現象，此與 Langmuir 方程式(如下)之適用範圍恰好相反。本研究於第二年初期先嚐試使用此兩
種模式進行吸附模式建置，但因固體表面的原子或分子所受的力是不對稱的，當氣體分子與其接
觸時有一部份受表面吸引而暫時停留，因而使固體表面上氣體的濃度增加，這種現象即所謂固、
氣界面的吸附。一般而言，吸附是一種極為複雜的界面化學現象，是與吸附劑表面對吸附質之親
和力作用有關，因此擬利用測試結果修正所建置等溫吸附模式。

技術說明
(中文) 研究以廢棄矽藻土作為矽源，CTAB為範本劑，通過水熱法合成介孔材料(MSM)，
並使用XRD、N2吸脫附、FT-IR、TGA、SEM-EDS、HR-TEM等進行特徵分析，鑒定材
料的物化特性。使用合成的介孔材料MSM對揮發性有機物丙酮進行吸附試驗，考
查了丙酮濃度、流量、吸附劑劑量的操作條件下，MSM對丙酮的吸附能力，並進
行反應動力學和機理的推導。本文成果為對材料微觀結構的研究表明，所合成的
材料為介孔結構，孔道排列較為整齊，MSM的比表面積為1060.2 cm2 g-1，平均
孔洞體積約為1.05 cm3 g-1，孔徑為3.95 nm，孔徑分佈窄。丙酮吸附實驗結果
表明實驗結果表明當丙酮濃度為600、800、1000及1600 ppm時，其吸附量分別為
59.6、65.7、69.6和84.9 mg g-1。
(英文) This study used spent diatomaceous earth (SDE) from drink processing as source of Si 
and cationic surfactant (CTAB) as a template for the synthesis of mesoporous silica 
Materials (MSM) through hydrothermal method. MSM was applied in the acetone 
adsorption, studied in the flow system. Several parameters such as initial concentration, 
the flow rate, adsorbent dosage and adsorbent regeneration were systematically 
investigated. Reaction kinetics and mechanism were derived. The research of 
microscopic structure illustrated the synthetic materials retained long-range order 
mesoporous structure. The results showed that the surface area, pore volume and pore 
size was roughly ranged from 880 to 1060 m2 g-1, 1.05 cm3 g-1 and 4.0 nm, 
respectively. The adsorption equilibrium revealed that adsorption capacity of MSM was 
59.6, 65.7, 69.6, 84.9 mg g-1 while the acetone concentration was 600, 800, 1000 ppm, 
1600 ppm respectively.
