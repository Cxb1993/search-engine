Abstract 
Maximum power point tracking (MPPT) 
is one of the key technologies of power 
converter interface for the wind power 
generator system. This project proposes a 
MPPT method for the permanent magnet 
synchronous wind power generator. The 
proposed method only adjusts the amplitude 
of the DC/AC inverter’s output current and 
observes the output DC voltage of AC/DC 
power converter. Then, the MPPT of wind 
power generation system can be obtained. A 
prototype is developed and tested to verify the 
performance of the proposed method. The 
experimental results show that the 
performance of the proposed method is as 
expected.
