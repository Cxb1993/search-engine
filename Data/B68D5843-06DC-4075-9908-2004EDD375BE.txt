三、研究方法及成果 
下圖一顯示單根氧化銦奈米線熱電量測
晶片的場發射掃描是電子顯微鏡(FESEM)影
像，晶片上方是量測時提藉由施加電流供奈米
線電極兩端溫差的加熱裝置，因此，奈米線必
須垂直於加熱裝置。在氧化銦奈米兩端接上四
個電極，藉由四點量測基礎測量其電性質。在
接近奈米線兩端處，製作量測其溫差的溫度
計，此溫度機必須在制成允許下接近奈米線，
並且平行所要量測的位置。通過量測，我們可
以得到Seebeck係數，藉由量測奈米線兩端的
電壓差與溫差的關係。

圖二顯示氧化銦奈米線的熱傳導係數，由
於熱傳導可以分成電子或是聲子作為傳遞載
體，因此我們可以將熱傳導係數分成電子以及
聲子熱傳導係數，圖二中的藍色三角形、紅色
圓形、黑色正方形分別代表熱傳導、電子熱傳

導係數比起聲子小很多，因此氧化銦奈米線的
熱傳機制是由聲子為主導。除此之外，在295
到375 K量測溫度區間中，熱傳導與聲子熱傳
導係數皆隨著溫度上升而下降，其值在高溫區
間可以低到2 W/mK以下。

四、結論與討論 
單根氧化銦奈米線的熱電量測晶片已經
可以藉由電子束微影技術成功地製作出來，其
氧化銦熱傳導係數約為 10 到 2 W/mK，且隨著
溫度增加而降低熱傳導係數，除此之外，氧化
銦的熱傳導是由聲子主導，也就是說，是由聲
子間的碰撞傳遞熱能。氧化銦的 Seebeck 係數
值約為-75 to -50 μV/K，且隨著溫度上升而增
加。由於目前仍然是初步的結果，其熱電轉換
效率，ZT值仍然不高，但是我們相信藉由改變
氧化銦結構，在進一步降低其熱傳導係數，必
定可以增進其熱電轉換能力。 
在 Ni2Si/C54-TiSi2 核殼狀奈米線部分，我
們成功地藉由固液固成長過程合成出直徑約
200奈米，長度 2 微米的核殼狀 Ni2Si/C54-TiSi2
核殼狀奈米線。我們並詳細地探討其成長機

Field Emission of Core-Shell Ga2O3 Nanowires 
 
Kai-Yuan Cheng, Chia-Wei Hsu, Chin-Hua Hsieh, and Li-Jen Chou＊ 
Department of Materials Science and Engineering, National Tsing Hua University, 
No. 101, Section 2, Kuang-Fu Road, Hsinchu 30013, Taiwan, Republic of China. 
＊Corresponding Author e-mail: ljchou@mx.nthu.edu.tw 
 
Field emission devices, exhibiting high current density and narrow energy distribution, have been widely 
used in the electron source applications. The field emission properties were dominated by the morphology and 
electronic properties of the emitters. During the last decade, one-dimensional nanostructures were proposed as 
the most promising candidate for next generation field emission devices due to their extremely high aspect 
ratio. Among all the one-dimensional nanostructures, heterostructure such as peapod and core-shell has 
attracting growing interesting due to the novel properties in physical and chemical aspects. 
In the previous work of our group, a one-step method of unique Au-Ga2O3 core-shell nanowires were 
synthesized, and works demonstrating their fascinating photoresponse phenomenon were also carried out. In 
this study, we investigated the field emission properties of Au-Ga2O3 core-shell nanowires, which showing 
better field enhancing characteristic compare to previous reports. TEM analyses were utilized for identifying 
the geometry and the microstructure of the zenith of these core-shell nanowires. A thin layer of Ga3O on the 
surface of Au tip was observed in the analyses, which was considered as the main reason for the enhancement 
in field emission measurement. A growth model was proposed to explain the exist of Ga3O layer. 
In summary, measurements were carried out to investigate the field emission properties of Au-Ga2O3 
nanowires, which showing an interesting enhancement. The improvement in field emission was thought 
attributed to the low electron affinity Ga3O layer on the surface of Au tip.
