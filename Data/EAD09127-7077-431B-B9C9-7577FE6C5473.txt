 III
Abstract and Keywords 
We reported our studies on InAs-based materials and devices grown by gas-source 
molecular-beam epitaxy (MBE). First, we have overcome the issue of immiscibility and 
successfully grown InAsPSb epilayers nearly lattice-matched to InAs substrates. For 
samples with high As mole fraction, photoluminescence (PL) resulting from band-to-band 
recombination is observed when the temperature is high than 100 K. While as the As mole 
fraction is low in the miscibility gap, two PL bands, ascribed to band tail recombination and 
deep level transition, are observed. The latter transition coincides with the configuration 
coordination model and with an amorphous-like thermal quenching behavior. The defects 
responsible for the luminescence are most likely complexes consisting of carbon and an 
indium vacancy. 
In collaboration with Surrey University and Lancaster University, mid-infrared 
photomodulated reflectance (PR) spectroscopy was used to study the energy gap and spin 
orbit splitting energy in our InAsSb alloys. The most important finding is on the spin orbit 
splitting energy which is temperature independent and with a negative bowing on 
composition dependence. These results suggest that InAsSb has a splitting energy larger 
than its energy gap, indicating that CHHS Auger recombination does not exist in this alloy, 
and is thus a promising active medium for mid-infrared emitter.  
We grew a series of InAs1-xSbx/InAs0.67P0.23Sb0.10 (0.05 < x < 0.13) multiple quantum 
wells (QW), and determined their unstrained conduction-band and valence-band edges by 
fitting the PL peak energy in the temperature range 10 – 300 K. The results reveal that the 
QWs exhibit type-I band alignment and the valence band of InAsSb accounts for 65% of the 
energy-gap bowing. A valence-band anticrossing (VBAC) model is used to explain the 
unusual valence band bowing InAsSb as well as the spin-orbit splitting energy measured by 
the aforementioned PR spectroscopy. 
We also have grown and fabricated p-InAsPSb/i-InAs/n-InAs photodetectors. The 
detectors are able to operate at room temperature with a peak responsivity of 1.64 A/W at 3 
m, corresponding to an external quantum efficiency of 67%. Since the devices have no 
anti-reflection coating and the reflectance measured from an InAsPSb layer is ~35% at 3 
m, the estimated internal quantum efficiency is close to 100%. The peak 
Johnson-noise-limited detectivity is as high as 5.4×109 cm-Hz1/2/W, which is comparable 
with the best results measured from devices grown by liquid phase epitaxy. 
 
Keywords: molecular beam epitaxy, mid-infrared, InAs, InAsPSb, spin orbit splitting 
energy, band alignment, photodetector

結果與討論 
InAsPSb 薄膜的光學特性 
過去我們以不同長晶條件成長一系列 1 m 厚的 InAsPSb 薄膜於(1 0 0) InAs 基板
上，並以 HRXRD、PL 以及 scanning electron microscope 研究其磊晶品質、光學性質和
surface morphology，其結果發表於期刊 Journal of Crystal Growth [24]。對於 As 含量少的
樣品，其成份落於 miscibility 之內，因此其 HRXRD 呈現相分離的特性。隨著 As 成份
增多，HRXRD 結果轉變為單一相位，且其 surface morphology 獲得改善漸趨平緩。PL
光學特性上，As 含量 67%的樣品，我們測量其 10 ~ 300 K 的 PL 頻譜。PL 峰值對溫度
做圖，其圖形呈現 S 形走向。當溫度升高至 100 K 以上時，其 PL 峰值扣除 kT/2 之後可
以和 Varshni’s equation 推測的數值相符。我們認為在高溫的時候，PL 放光為 InAsPSb
的 band to band transition 主導。低溫時的 PL 峰值，比 Varshni’s equation 計算的數值要低
了 33 meV。我們在低溫 10 K 進行改變激發光源強度的 PL 測量，在激發光源由弱轉強
300 倍之時，其 PL 能量峰值共藍移了 18 meV。我們認為在低溫時，其 PL 放光為材料
的 localized state 放光主導。由於 localized state 的 density of state 較容易填滿，因此低溫
時增強激發光源的強度，可以將載子填到能量較高的 state 放光，才會產生 PL 峰值藍移
現象。 
我們研究 As 含量少落於 miscibility gap 中的 InAs0.04P0.67Sb0.29 PL 放光頻譜，並投
稿發表於期刊 Journal of Applied Physics [29]。在 10 K 低溫時，PL 的能量峰值比
Varshni’s equation 預測值低了 0.223 eV。圖一為 InAs0.04P0.67Sb0.29 的 PL 放光頻譜。頻

PL 特徵波包，我們以 Gaussian 曲線來擬合出 E2。再將原本的 PL 頻譜扣除 E2來獲得

這種 band alignment 與我們先前以 InAs/InAsSb 多重量子井中觀察到的相符合[49]。文
