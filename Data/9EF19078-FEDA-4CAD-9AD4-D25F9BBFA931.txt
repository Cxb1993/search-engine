ABSTRACT 
Stellite12 cobalt base alloys with different diamond contents were coated on SK3-carbon tool 
steel by laser cladding. The microstructures and wear behaviors of poly crystalline diamond and 
stellite12 composite coatings with rapid solidification were investigated. The X-ray diffraction 
analyses indicated the existence of ?-Co(F.C.C matrix enriched with cobalt), and the metallic 
compound carbides like M23C6, M6C and M7C3?M?W, Cr, Co, Fe?and poly crystalline diamond 
in the laser cladded layer. Metallurgical bonds were formed between poly crystalline diamond 
and the matrix of stellite12 alloys. The diamond provided the coating with excellent resistance 
during dry sliding wear test. A higher content and bigger particle of diamond imparted a more 
excellent wear resistance to the coating. 
Keywords: Laser surface cladding, poly crystalline diamond, Stellite12 cobalt base alloy
