 1
摘要 
 
廣泛運用在各種產業的價值工程(Value Engineering, VE)是一個被用於工程設計階段的高度系統化的管
理方法，價值工程(價工)一般以機能為前提再藉由降低成本來提升研析整體效益。國外推動價工的整體
效益甚為顯著，而我國的價工導入效益卻始終未達預期，此一現象亟待改善。本研究擬應用文獻回顧、
專家訪談、問卷蒐集、敘述性統計及因素分析等工具，從價工應用之推動環境面及價工研析案之執行層
面兩面向，探討我國營建業推動價工應用的關鍵成功因素，並深入剖析這些因素之間的互動關係。其次，
透過價工追求兩大根本構面－機能與成本的統合應用，建構價工研析需求性評估決策模式協助工程業
主，評估工程設計階段進行價工研析之需求性，以降低其對於工程是否施行價工研析決策之困擾。研究
成果可供政府部門推動價值工程之重要參考，亦可協助設計及監造單位掌握專案標的之機能與成本，甚
至可做為營造廠商研擬替代方案之參考依據。價工研析需求性決策輔助模式亦具備下列三點貢獻：(1)供
工程業主評估專案執行價工研析或進行相關決策之參考；(2)於設計階段迅速檢視專案，針對機能欠佳或
成本不合理之面向進行檢討及改善；(3)本模式評估結果可增進工程業主對價工之認同，有助於普及推動
我國價工之應用。 
 
關鍵詞：價值工程、關鍵成功因素、決策輔助模式 
 
Abstract 
 
Value Engineering (VE), applied in various industries, is a kind of highly systemized management used in the 
design stage of constructions. Generally, VE takes functions as the prerequisite and heightens whole benefit by 
lowering the cost. The entire benefit of applying VE is quite remarkable in foreign countries, but the benefits of 
applying VE in Taiwan are never as good as the expectation. This phenomenon is needed to improve urgently. 
Therefore, the research project will investigate the issues of the enhancing VE implementation in Taiwan by a 
three-year time table. Being the first year of the research project, this study will utilize descriptive analysis and 
factor analysis to analyze the data collected via experts interviewing and questionnaire survey. From the 
viewpoint of the overall environment of VE and VE workshop implementation, the study aims to investigate 
current problems of applying VE and identifying critical success factors (CSF) of implementing VE and 
performing VE workshop in Taiwan. The mutual relationship between the CSFs will be analyzed in-depth. The 
related strategies and actions of managing these CSFs will be suggested. In addition, the study will integrates the 
concepts of function and cost, the most important fundamentals of VE, to develop an evaluation model to assist 
project owner’s decision-making regarding the urgency of performing VE workshops for the specific project. 
The proposed model aims to decrease the difficulties of the decision regarding whether the project should go for 
VE workshop or not. The study could provide important references to public sectors regarding the VE 
implementation. It could also assist designers and supervision units to manage project’s functions and costs. 
Contractors will be benefited by referring the study for alternatives study.The contributions of the proposed 
evaluating model include: (1) providing important references for owner＇s decision-making for if the project 
should go for VE workshop, (2) identifying and improving those handicap functions and unreasonable costs 
during the early phase of the project, and (3) increasing the acknowledgement of project owners of public sectors 
will facilitate the VE implementation in Taiwan. 
Key words: Value Engineering, Critical Success Factors, Decision-Aided Model

2.2 建構價工研析需求性決策輔助模式之研究方法 
資料處理與統計分析法 
如同前述，於建構價工研析需求性決策輔助模式
時皆發放一般問卷資料，並使用 SPSS 套裝軟體處理
(敘述性統計、信度分析、交叉分析與卡方獨立性檢定
及母體平均數檢定等)，主要在了解資料群的分佈趨勢
及檢定等應用，相關部份介紹如下：  
一、交叉分析與卡方獨立性檢定  
卡方交叉分析（Chi-square Test）原用來檢定雙
重屬性變數條件之下，樣本間是否存在顯著差異？本
文以卡方交叉分析檢驗題組間關係來自不同背景專家
意見有無明顯差異，若交叉表的卡方機率值小於顯著
水準 α值時，則可認定兩變數間並非獨立。  
二、母體平均數檢定  
探索兩變項間關係時，若為等距尺度資料進行平
均數比較時，則需使用變異數分析。變異數分析係將
總變異分解為組間變異、組內變異兩個來源，其分析
原理即在求取組間及組內變異的比例，如果組間變異
數明顯大於組內變異數，則顯示各組的平均數中，至
少有兩組以上具有顯著差異，如果無顯著差異，則各
組的平均數亦無顯著不同。 
模糊德爾菲法 
將模糊理論導入德爾菲法中，運用累積次數分配
與模糊積分的觀念，將專家意見整合成模糊數，並以
幾何平均數作為群體決策篩選評估準則之依據，避免
極端值的影響，以使評估因子的選取效果更佳，稱之
為模糊德爾菲法。本文以模糊德菲法模式為基礎，建
立適用本研究主題之評估模式，其步驟如下：  
步驟一、建立評估因素集合  
依據國小普通教室之構面及多種機能之需求，進
行文獻蒐集並訪談專家，彙整出研究所需的不同構面
之隸屬因素集合。  
步驟二、蒐集專家意見  
專家需針對每一影響普通教室機能因素之重要程
度，在 1 分(不重要)至 10 分(絕對重要)的區間，給予
最小影響程度認知值(min)與最大影響程度認知值
(max)，分數愈高代表影響力愈高，分數愈低代表影響
力愈低。接著分別統計專家群體對於每一項評估項
目，所給予之 min 與 max，並剔除落於「2 倍標準差」
以外之極端值。

3.2價工研析之主要影響因子 
本研究從『施行價工因素』及『影響價工研析成
功要素』兩大角度，研擬影響我國價工研析主要成功
因子，針對我國施行價工環境進行適切性調查。本問
卷採用Cronbach’ s α 係數來衡量問卷信度，係數值越
大表示內部一致性越高，該因素就越足以代表該部分
變數。本文 α =0.9278，顯示本文之問卷可信度甚高。 
依據因素分析判斷準則，針對20項變數進行因素
分析，將20項變數組構成四大特徵植大於1的構面(因
素)，其累積解釋變異量為68.965%，符合所萃取出構
面(因素)總累積解釋變異量大於40%之建議(Zaltman 
and Burger, 1997)，故因素分析係屬可行。表3.4之CSFs
因素分析萃取出四個構面，如表3.5。 
構面一有5個因子，其特徵值為4.186，解釋變異
量為20.928%。由表3.5可了解為何需要施行價工，故
此構面命名為「施行價工之原因」。構面二有7個因子，
其特徵值為3.746，解釋變異量為18.731。由表3.5可得
知，施行價工流程時人為控制的重要，故此構面命名
為「工作計畫控制」。構面三有5個因子，其特徵值為
3.072，解釋變異量為15.361%。由表3.5可得知，研析
團隊成員之所屬領域專業經驗及知識、研析組員的個
性、落實機能分析、研析團隊成員間之互動及研析的
導入時機，進而影響整體研析過程的進度，故此構面
命名為「領隊能力」。構面四有3個因子，其特徵值為
2.789，解釋變異量13.945%。由表3.5可得知，研析的
目標清楚明確、業主支持和積極的參與與工程相關部
門的合作程度，進而影響整體研析過程的進度，故此
構面命名為「各部門參與」。

圖 3.1 評估模式流程圖 
 
國小教室工程機能評估指標 
評估指標之建立，經文獻回顧整理機能相關影響
因子後，並分為「機能」與「成本」兩面向探討，其
中「成本」面向擬以「單位教室之造價」進行估算；「機
能」面向包含「規格與形式」、「物理環境」、「空間規
劃」、「附屬設施」及「使用與維護」五大層級項目及
十六項評估因子，其所屬各項因子之架構圖 3.2 所示。 
問卷設計與統計方式 
問卷內容分為成本與機能兩部份。成本概估部份
係將以「施工單位」與「總務主任」兩受訪群體評估
國小教室工程單位面積之成本為主；機能部份包含：
因子重要性評估(藉模糊德爾菲問卷篩選因子)、構面
問重要性比較(以層級分析-成對比較轉換重要性為構
面之相對權重)、參數適切值範圍之評估(獲取受訪共
識之參數最可能範圍)三大部分。 
一、因子重要性評估 
採用模糊德爾菲法(Fuzzy Delphi Method)受訪問
卷方式進行整合並篩選經文獻回顧、受訪訪談所整理
之「影響國小教室工程機能」之因子。 
二、構面重要性比較 
為了分別獲知兩面向、五個機能構面間相對重要
性強度，以兩兩比較法、共分 9 個等級評估。由於每
個準則對目標的影響程度不盡相同，各準則對目標之
達成有其相對權重，所以能顯示出各準則的權重比
較，受訪者可就各準則評比勾選出最適當比值。

五、結論與建議 
本研究以文獻回顧、專家訪談、問卷蒐集、敘述
性統計及因素分析等工具，從價工應用之推動環境面
及價工研析案之執行層面兩面向，探討我國營建業推
動價工應用的關鍵成功因素，並深入剖析這些因素之
間的互動關係。其次，透過價工追求兩大根本構面－
機能與成本的統合應用，建構「價工研析需求性評估
決策模式」協助工程業主，評估工程設計階段進行價
工研析之需求性，以降低其對於工程是否施行價工研
析決策之困擾。 
本研究成果可供政府部門推動價值工程之重要
參考，亦可協助設計及監造單位掌握專案標的之機能
與成本，甚至可做為營造廠商研擬替代方案之參考依
據。價工研析需求性決策輔助模式亦具備下列三點貢
獻：(1)供工程業主評估專案執行價工研析或進行相關
決策之參考；(2)於設計階段迅速檢視專案，針對機能
欠佳或成本不合理之面向進行檢討及改善；(3)本模式
評估結果可增進工程業主對價工之認同，有助於普及
推動我國價工之應用。

5missing, or worse, distorted. Traditional accounting system leads to poor decisions. Today, it is not
unusual to find that overhead costs exceed 50 % to 60% of total spending, especially in high-tech
manufacturing and large financial institutions that have significant costs (Higgins and Young, 2001). It
is imperative to find an alternative costing system to provide proper information.
One of the drawbacks in traditional cost system lies in the categorization of traditional cost
accounting systems into direct and indirect cost in cost calculation. This division causes
product/service cost distortion, and emphasizing on output, product and total cost is not able to respond
to the change of environment nowadays. All theses may bring about a loss of important cost
information and make wrong decision. There is therefore a need to design a new system that enables
financial and business information to reflect operation performance, improve manufacturing process
and product, eliminate waste and implement company strategy and operation. Existing cost
management system tends to define product/activity cost by standard cost. However, it is difficult to
make correct decision because of out-of-dated data analysis, which might be distorted, lack of timely
efficient, and too vague. Management often makes wrong decisions from incorrect cost information on
their sales activities. It therefore causes loss on sales. By contrast, a new cost management system
should be able to provide critical management information to facilitate operation planning control and
implement enterprise operation activities. Among the candidates, Activity Based Costing (ABC)
system is designed to improve the deficiency of the traditional system.
ABC was promoted by Cooper and Kaplan in 1980 by integrating cost management techniques to
provide strategy evaluation and by conducting improvement activity to maximize its function on cost
management. With ABC, accurate and reliable cost information can be provided and business
management improvement activities can be supported. Several scholars promote ABC system, there are
a few companies applying ABC system globally. However, little attentions are given to ABC
incorporating VE for service improvement.
This paper will take a Taiwan Company as a case to validate the VE/ABC model in its process of
enhancing performance and operation efficiency. We will elaborate the application of ABC in the
information phase and development of the VE job plan and demonstrate the integrated model for
business solutions that balance business function, stakeholder issues, cost and profitability, and
business process. This integrated model will sharpen ABC, to identify in-depth the hidden and shadow
costs other than non-value-added activity cost that drain the organization of its resources and
profitability. It also tries to demonstrate this model’s capability to identify opportunities necessary for 
the organization to improve financial and operation performance without sacrificing quality, customer
loyalty and employees satisfaction.
VE is a managing technique focusing on functions, cost and schedule to seek optimal alternatives
and assist decision-making. VE is defined as the “statutory and regulatory definitions [that] encompass 
analysis of functions performed by a team of qualified personnel directed at improving performance,
reliability, quality, safety, and life cycle costs of products, systems or procedures (Department of
Energy, 1997). VE to be sure is not a conflict orientated design review, a cost cutting exercise, and a
standardization exercise (Leeuw, 2001), and VE can be applied to all areas of human endeavor.
VE is to look into a Job Plan to effectively analyze a product or service in order to develop a
maximum number of alternatives to achieve quality product or service (Lee, 1997). Following the Job
Plan the value engineering effort will assure maximum benefits and greater flexibility. VE takes
Functional Analysis Systematic Technique (FAST) diagram as the primary tool, which helps a team to
understand the hierarchical relationships of functions and focus their creative energy on finding better
ways to accomplish a given function. Typically, some functions on the FAST diagram will be
highlighted as promising areas for brainstorming (Lewis, 2001). During FAST diagramming, it is
important to involve every member of the team especially the inexperienced in the functional
discussions, because this breaks down some of the barriers, helps to think in terms of functions; and
provides a complete overview of the entire problem with facts. Team members should be encouraged
to project themselves into role-playing, in order to inject different points of view on the problem
(Parker, 1977).

the asymmetry of the information regarding facility design/management, manufacturing system or
service system. VE Methodology is applicable to hardware, building or other construction projects, and
to "soft" areas such as manufacturing and construction processes, health care and environment services,
programming, management systems and organization structure (SAVE, 1998). More broadly, Value
