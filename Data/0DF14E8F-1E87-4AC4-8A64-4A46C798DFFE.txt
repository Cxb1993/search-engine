一、中英文摘要 
    此報告在探討不確定性區間時變延遲基因轉錄調控網路的強健穩定化問
題，系統的不確性參數為時變有界。時間延遲且時延的上下限有關假設出現在傳
信核糖核酸與蛋白質中，轉錄調控函數假設為全域性利普西茨(Lipschitz)連續。
強健時延範圍相關控制器的區間範圍是使用更佳有界技巧設計。接著使用線性矩
陣不等式(linear matrix inequality)呈現一個充分條件來解決這個問題，經由線性矩
陣不等式法，可得到所要需要設計狀態回授控制器的詳細呈現。最後，在此提出
兩個範例詳細說明報告中所提的理論。 
This paper investigates the problem of robust stabilization for genetic regulatory 
networks with interval time-varying delays, which are subject to norm-bounded 
time-varying parameter uncertainties. The time delays including lower and upper 
bounds of delay are assumed to appear in both the mRNA and protein. The regulatory 
functions are assumed to be globally Lipschitz continuous. The resulting 
delay-range-dependent robust controller with interval range is designed in terms of 
improved bounding technique. A sufficient condition for the solvability of the 
problem is obtained via a linear matrix inequality (LMI). When this LMI is feasible, 
an explicit expression of a desired state feedback controller is also given. The theory 
developed in this paper is demonstrated by two numerical examples. 
Keywords–Delay-range-dependent, genetic regulatory networks, interval time-varying 
delay, linear matrix inequality.

由權矩陣來降低此條件的保守性，並可以確定所設計的控制器可以使系統漸進強
健穩定化。我們舉了兩個例子來說明結果的可應用性及有效性。因此我們可以預
期人造的機轉錄調控網路使系統過濾更大的外在環境干擾以及遭受更多內在的
波動，並且系統可以更為可靠的運作。
