英文摘要 
 
 
This research employed the photocatalysis and electrokinetic (EK) process for the 
remediation of nonylphenol polyethoxylates (NP-9) in the aqueous phase and nonylphenol (NP) in 
the soil phase, respectively. Several process parameters were investigated for their degradation 
efficiency during the aqueous phase photocatalytic process. These process parameters included the 
pH environment, types and amounts of composite photocatalyst, addition of enhancing reagent 
(H2O2) and initial concentration of NP-9. Besides, the degradation pathway of nonylphenol 
polyethoxylates (NPnEO) was analyzed. EK process was used for the remediation of 
NP-contaminated soil. Additional parameters affecting the EK process included the processing 
fluid, potential gradient, processing time and initial concentration of NP. The removal mechanism 
of NP using the EK process was also investigated.  
For the experiment of removing NP in soil phase using the EK process, the suitable potential 
gradient was shown at 1.0V/cm. As the current was more stable, the electroosmotic flow rate was 
more efficient. During the EK process, the total removal rate for the extension of treatment periods 
to 8-15 days was respectively 1.14-1.34 times as compared to the total removal rate on day 5. The 
extension of processing time had effectively enhanced the removal rate of NP. The experiments 
with different processing fluids generated the following sequence for the NP removal rate: 
Methanol (52.8-68.8%) >NaOH (42.8-53.7%)> CA (35.8-38.7%)>D.I. Water (29.6%). During the 
removal of NP by the EK system, the main proportion of NP was collected on the cathode 
reservoir, which accounted for 88.3 – 95.1%. The total amount of NP collected on the cathode 
reservoir increased as the removal efficiency improved. Therefore, the main removal mechanism 
was the migration of NP from the anode to cathode terminal by electroosmosis for the EK/NP 
system in soil phase. 
  The overall result showed that composite catalyst could degrade NP-9 effectively. The 
degradation efficiency was greater than using TiO2 alone. During the degradation process in 
photocatalysis, NP-9 was broken down from long-chain electro-optical (EO) substrates to shorter 
chain EO substrates and NP. Additionally, for the removal of NP in soil phase using the EK 
process, the NP removal efficiency may be enhanced by the conditional selection of other 
processing fluids and electrode materials. 
 
Keyword: Electrokinetic process, Processing Fluid, Photocatalysis, Nano composite photocatalyst, 
Nonylphenol polyethoxylate, Nonylphenol

第三章 結果與討論 
在電動力管柱實驗模組，主要需討論其 pH 值、電流密度、土壤中殘留之濃
度，藉由以上數據探討去除效率與去除機制。而於壬基苯酚之實驗中多加討論其
在土壤中之移動性。詳細討論分別附錄之文章中，此文章將陸續發表下列簡述幾
個基本條件討論。 
 
3.1 pH 值之變化 
於實驗期間，每日收集操作流質槽測其 pH 值發現，於電動力進行時，產
生之電解反應會使得陽極槽液於氧化環境下持續產生大量氫離子而呈酸性，而
陰極槽液於還原狀態下也因氫氧根離子之持續產生而呈鹼性，且陰、陽兩極槽
液之酸鹼值可達 1.63-2.4 及 11.54-12.6，顯示電場之電解反應均相當激烈。陰
陽兩極產生電解反應如下： 
Anode ：  2H2O → O2(g) + 4H+ + 4e-    (1) 
Cathode：  2H2O + 2e- → H2(g) + 2OH-    (2) 
於上述二式可知，於陽極不斷釋放出氫離子，故靠陽極端為呈現酸性；而陰
極則釋出氫氧離子，靠陰極端則呈現酸性。其詳細討論皆於各附錄的文章中。 
 
3.2 電流密度與電滲透係數(ke)之變化 
系統之電滲透係數(ke)由處理過程中每天收集之電滲透流量累積後求得，
可經由下式計算得知： 
Qe = ke × ie × A      (3) 
上式  ke=電滲透係數(EO permeability, cm2 V-1 s-1)，  
ie=電位坡度(potential gradient, V cm-1)， 
A=接觸面積(area, cm2)。 
 
電動力實驗一開始電流密度漸升高，約於 40 小時後開始下降，然後趨向穩
定，此因土壤中的重金屬與氫氧化物產生反應而沉澱阻塞，亦隨著電動力試驗的
進行將導致土壤之合水率降低，土壤孔隙增大使電滲逶流係數減低。在反應過程
中無論反應牆設置之有無，電動力技術在處理污染土壤時，電流密度會隨時間而
降低，其主要原因係反應過程中系統之阻力增加其中包括：(1) 土壤中之帶電顆
粒經離子遷移於兩極端造成阻塞，(2)操作流質與土壤中含有之離子產生鍵結或
沉澱，(3) 酸鋒遷移時所導致土壤中帶電離子如 Ca2+、Mg2+ 等物質，因電場效

staying in environment; (2) low degradation; (3) bioconcentrated and 
bioaccumulative; (4) biotoxic. Among those, Nonylphenol (NP), a kind of 
environmental hormone applied in detergent and shampoo, has been confirmed to 
cause breast cancer for human. Consequently, more researches were focus on the 
treatment of NP in environmental matrix.  
This study was aimed to investigate the electrokinetic removal of nonylphenol from 
soil matrix under potential gradient of 1V/cm for 5-day treatment. The EK 
experiments were conducted with three processing fluids of NaOH, methanol, and 
citric acid in a Pyrex glass cylindrical cell (4.2 cm in diameter) consisting of 
three compartments: anode reservoir, soil specimen chamber, and cathode 
reservoir. Results showed that the elcetrokinetic removal efficiency of NP at

highly related to the solubility of NP in processing fluid. Approximate

processing fluids of NaOH, methanol, and citric acid in a Pyrex glass cylindrical cell (4.2 cm in 
diameter) consisting of three compartments: anode reservoir, soil specimen chamber, and cathode 
reservoir. Results showed that the elcetrokinetic removal efficiency of NP at potential gradient of 1 
V/cm under 5-day treatment for processing fluid of methanol, sodium chloride, citric acid and DI water 
was 52.8-68.8%, 42.8-53.7%, 35.8-38.7%, and 29.6%, respectively. It was found that the removal of 
NP in EK system was highly related to the solubility of NP in processing fluid. Approximate 
88.3%-95.1% of NP was collected in the cathode reservoir after EK treatment, which was 7.5-9.4 times 
greater than that collected in the anode reservoir. It was concluded that NP was mainly removed from 
anode to cathode by electroosmosis flow. The electrokinetic phenomenon of current density, 
electroosmistic permeability, and power consumption were also investigated.   
Keywords: Electrokinetic process, environmental hormone, nonylphenol, POPs, processing fluid, soil 
remediation.
