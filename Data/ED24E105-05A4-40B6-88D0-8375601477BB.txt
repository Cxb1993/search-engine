1.2文獻回顧 
玻璃纖維 
玻璃纖維(Glass Fiber)實際上是由
許多氧化物混合在一起的纖維，其中
以二氧化矽(SiO2)為主，所以結構非常
複雜，在物性、化性都會因為製造方
法不同跟內含的氧化物成分的不同，
而有所不同，其中使用最廣泛的玻璃
纖維是 E-玻璃纖維，為矽酸硼鋁玻
璃，含有 0.8％以下的鹼金屬氧化物，
其主要用途為玻璃纖維強化塑膠
（FRP）與印刷電路版 PCB（Printed 
Circuit Board）。 
    目前各類複合材料中，纖維強化
塑膠是種類最多，用量最大，發展最
快的複合材料，其中玻璃纖維強化塑
膠，由於價格低廉且具有極高的機械
強度、耐熱與耐燃性佳、電氣絕緣性
高及尺寸安定等特性，是工業上主要
的工程材料之一。 
酸性染料 
在合成染料之領域內，酸性染料是屬
於發明比較早的產物，被廣泛的運用
於天然蛋白質纖維及聚醯胺纖維的染
色。酸性染料化學構造上主要可分成
偶氮染料、蒽醌染料、碳離子染料等
三類，最初是以鹼性染料磺酸化之形
式而被採用，接著含有偶氮基之酸性
染料漸漸開發，由於偶氮染料之製法
較為簡易，因而往後有新的酸性染料
製成，而奠定了在合成染料中的地
位。1900 年左右，酸性染料之均染性
及氈合性開始區別。然而，含有蒽醌
構造之酸性染料(如圖 1.2)被發明，逐
漸補足了原先欠缺之藍~綠色色相
NaO3S，同時跨進濕堅牢度酸性染料的
第一步。 
矽烷偶聯劑 
玻璃纖維與基材所構成的複合材
料，由於玻璃纖維表面極為光滑，並
有吸附水膜的現象，與樹脂的接著力
不佳，如欲改善纖維與樹脂的接著，
必需將纖維進行所謂的表面處理，增
加表面官能基的數目與樹脂之親和
性。 
胺基矽烷類化合物過去常常被
合成為觸媒或吸附劑和分離劑上，大
部分從矽烷製備的Silsesquioxanes 都
有相同的結構式RSiX3，其中R代表有
機分子團，X代表可被水解部分，此種
形式的矽烷化合物通常被當做連結有
機化合物和無機化合物的方法，舉例
來說RSiX3可連結有機化合物和無機
化合物，其中可水解部分X 包括了鹵
素，烷氧基類化合物，和乙醯基類化
合物，當在無機材料的表面縮合矽醇
和氫氧根時所形成的化學鍵會緊緊將
矽烷和無機材料包圍。而R 代表胺烷
基，甲基丙烯類化合物，乙烯基類化
合物，環氧基類化合物，以及硫醇類
化合物，將其於酸、鹼為觸媒合成網
狀結構，再進行染料或螢光物之接合。 
有機無機混成過程中，兩種不同
相所形成之界面，即為了避免不同相
之材料產生相分離，其作用得在續加
工的程序上，扮演著重要的角色，1940
年，玻璃纖維經過矽烷之表面改質，

了讓玻璃纖維與 3-氨丙基三乙氧基烷
的作結合。3-氨丙基三乙氧基矽烷必需
先經過水解才能與玻璃纖維作結合，
我們會設定兩種 3-氨丙基三乙氧基矽
烷的水解方式： 
方法一：90%的酒精加上 10%的去離子
水，用醋酸將 pH 值調至 4 再
將 3-氨丙基三乙氧基矽烷加
入水解 60 min。 
方法二：90%的酒精加上 10%的去離子
水，將 3-氨丙基三乙氧基矽烷
加入後，再用醋酸將 pH 值調
至 4，水解 60 min。 
烘乾 
在玻璃纖維經過含浸 3-氨丙基三 
乙氧基矽烷之後，3-氨丙基三乙氧基矽
烷與玻璃纖維在經過 80 ℃的溫度烘乾
去除水分後，會形成共價鍵結及氫鍵
鍵結。 
 
三 結果與討論 
3.1 紅外線光譜圖(F.T.I.R.) 
圖 3.1 為含浸 3-氨丙基三乙氧基
矽烷前之三種處理方式紅外光譜圖，
可以看到在三種玻璃纖維樣品在都有
在 3550 ~ 3200 cm-1 的-OH 伸縮振動吸
收峰、1040 cm-1 附近的 Si-O-Si 伸縮振
動吸收峰及 750~650 cm-1 的-OH 面外
彎曲振動吸收峰，由此紅外光譜圖可
證實三種玻璃纖維表面都有可與 3-氨
丙基三乙氧基矽烷結合的-OH 基團，
經蝕刻後的玻璃纖維，其-OH 基團的
吸收峰沒有變化，但在經過電暈後在
3550 ~ 3200 的-OH 伸縮振動吸收峰消
失了，可知電暈處理對於玻璃纖維並
不會使-OH 基團增加反而會減少。
 
圖 3.1 含浸 3-氨丙基三乙氧基矽烷前之三種處理方式紅 
外光譜圖 
由圖 3.2 可知經含浸 3-氨丙基三
乙氧基矽烷後之玻璃纖維多了 1650 ~ 
1560 cm-1 的 NH2 變形振動吸收峰及
1380 cm-1 附近的-CH3 對稱變形振動吸
收峰，且在 3550 ~ 3200 cm-1 的-OH 伸
縮振動吸收峰在經過含浸處理後其幅
度變小，可證實在經過含浸 3-氨丙基
三乙氧基矽烷此過程後，3-氨丙基三乙
氧基矽烷有與玻璃纖維作結合使-OH
基減少並且讓玻璃纖維多了可與酸性
染料結合的染色席位，而有檢測出
1380 cm-1 附近的-CH3 對稱變形振動吸
收峰推測是 3-氨丙基三乙氧基矽烷在
水解過程中水解不完全，還有些許的
-CH3 未水解成-OH 基。

Supercritical fluid is defined as fluid processed beyond the critical point which is indistinguishable as liquids or gases.[1,2,3]
Gases and liquids could become supercritical fluids when they are compressed and heated above their critical pressure and
temperature. In this state it can demonstrate properties intermediate between those of typical gases and liquids. Supercritical
carbon dioxide dyeing method has several benefits, such as nonflammable, nontoxic, easy to use, economic and environmentally
acceptable. The study became attractive since Saus et al. studied the dyeing of polyester and modified natural fibers with disperse
dyes in supercritical carbon dioxide.[4,5,6,7] In their context, the authors reported that temperature and pressure were the main
factors which could affect dyeing performance. It has been demonstrated that supercritical carbon dioxide is a good medium for
polymer dyeing, but some factors which affect the results such as pressure, temperature, dye concentration, pH value and the
addition of assistant agents still need to be understand. We used supercritical fluid dyeing technology to dye nylon6.6 and Tencel®
with a 180ml capacity dye pot, raising pressure and temperature simultaneously.[8]

3.2 Color strength
At pressures below 200 bar, dyestuff dissolved in the fluid is low. When pressure reaches 250 bar to 300 bar, the dyestuff dissolves
at the best conditions set for this experiment because lower pressure means better economic efficiency and safety but dyeing result
must be concerned. The low viscosity and high density of supercritical fluids make the dyestuff penetrating easily into the Tencel®
and nylon66 fibers. When Tencel® and nylon66 were dyed with RRB 4 and RRB 19 at 200 bar to 300bar with 4 % (o.m.f.) of dye
concentration for 10 min, the dyestuff attached not only to the outside of the yarn bundle and penetrates to the inner side of the
yarn. As the dyeing procedure was completed, the fabric was then immersed in a boiling pyridine-water (25:75) solution to extract
any unfixed dye and dried at 80 ℃ for 30min. Table 2 & Table 3 showed the color strength (K/S) with hydrophobic reactive dyes
on Tencel® and nylon 66 fabrics.
At dyeing temperature (140 ℃), these phenomena become more serious on nylon66 fibers because of thermal properties. Dyeing
results with hydrophobic dyes indicated that dye absorbance increases with an increase in pressure. Comparing results from the
trials of two dyestuffs, RRB 19 showed better penetration efficiency. As compared with water dyeing specimens whose K/S values
are 1.15 and 1.20 for RRB 4 and RRB 19 at 100 ℃, dyeing with supercritical fluid will have higher exhaustion for RRB 4 and
RRB 19 at pressure upon 300bar. In the experiment, we can conclude that both of temperature and pressure affect the penetration
and hydrophobic dyestuff selection is an important factor in achieving optimum dyeing results. In other words, it is practicable that
the recycling reactive dye is used by supercritical processing in the dyeing technology.

4 Conclusions
Using supercritical carbon dioxide as a dyeing medium for Tencel® and nylon66 fabrics can eliminate the
pollution of water treatment and save time. Cost of investment can be balanced by pollution and processing
profits. The dyeing of Tencel® and nylon66 fabrics with hydrophobic reactive dyes synthesized in our
laboratory was quite feasible. The work indicated both of temperature and pressure are the factors which
affect the color strength of Tencel® and nylon66 with hydrophobic reactive dye using supercritical carbon
dioxide as solvent system. Rubbing fastness was satisfactory for normal applications and washing fastness
was excellent as hydrophobic reactive dyes were used to dye Tencel® and nylon66 fabrics. In accordance
with the results stated above, we can deduce that the potential benefit of supercritical carbon dioxide as
solvent system is widely proved. This experiment demonstrated the possibility of dyeing polar fabric with
supercritical fluid dyeing method. Supercritical fluid dyeing process is an effective replacement for
traditional water process dyeing for synthetic polymers. Thus the development of this technology is available
and great progress may be expected for another polymer fiber in future.
