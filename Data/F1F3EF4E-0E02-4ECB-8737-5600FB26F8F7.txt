1摘要
3C產業為整合技術與知識密集產業，在我國屬於發展成熟且是出口的主力產業之一。然
而，隨著環境保護及永續發展的需求，企業對於生產時所引發之環境領域衝擊占了絕對關鍵
的角色。世界各國便制訂並實施各種環境法規，將生產者的責任延伸至整個產品生命週期。
綠色供應鏈之建構已成為當今提供綠色產品與邁向永續發展原則的主要挑戰及潮流，而為健
全此一體系，對企業而言即推動綠色協同供應商管理。
本研究之主旨在探討3C產業綠色供應商評選議題，運用票選層級分析法(VAHP)與資料包
絡分析法(DEA)作為供應商評選之方法，以協同供應鏈觀念為基礎，利用專家訪談的方式及
參考國內外關於供應商評選模式之文獻，建立一套適用於3C產業的綠色供應商評選準則與流
程。具體而言，本研究之目的為：1.歸納整理3C產業綠色供應商評選之現況。2.以VAHP與DEA
建構綠色協同供應商評選準則。3.依據評選模式設計協同評選流程。
結果發現：1.透過實際建置的綠色供應商評估準則模式，擷取出環境管理、製程技術及
設計績效等三大綠色準則構面。2.運用層級程序式架構擴充此三大構面下各次準則共15項，
並以VAHP計算各準則的權重。3.篩選出綠色供應商績效評估指標，投入指標為產能、綠色管
理系統、廢棄物處理、環保與替代材料、環保設備升級與採購、訓練環保人力及總資產共七
項，產出指標為稅後淨利與營業收入二項，並據以對供應商進行評選。
關鍵詞：綠色協同供應鏈、供應商評選、票選層級分析法、資料包絡分析法、3C產業
ABSTRACT
The 3C industries, including computer, communication and consumer electronic sectors, are in
the developed and mainly export stage in Taiwan. However, environment impacts caused by
manufacturing play the key role of sustainably operating businesses. Many environmental
protection laws and regulations are released and enforced globally. The responsibility of
environmental protection for manufacturers is extended through the product life-cycle to suppliers.
Green supply chain management thus becomes more important to ensure that production network
could provide environmentally conscious (green) products. In order to pursue a pleasing green
supply chain, green supplier management is an essential concept for business sustainability.
Primary intention of this research is on the selection of green suppliers for 3C industry via the
utilization of voting analytic hierarchy method (VAHP) and data envelopment analysis (DEA). The
research collaboratively established a set of green suppliers' evaluation criteria and consequently
designed evaluation processes, by literature review and field interviews. In short, the objectives of
this research are as follows: (1) to assess the current operation of green supplier evaluation in 3C
industry, (2) to establish evaluation criteria of green supplier utilizing VAHP and DEA, and (3) to
collaboratively design the evaluation process according to the set of criteria.
This research established 15 green supplier evaluation criteria under three green categories,
namely environment management, production techniques, and design performance. The evaluation
process was deployed by six stages. With the aid of VAHP, weight of each criterion was calculated
to present the priority of criteria. A DEA evaluation process consists of green criteria allocation and
computation procedure was constructed to facilitate the supplier performance evaluation task. The
input criteria for DEA include production capacity, green management system, waste treatment,
substitute material, procuring environment friendly equipment, environmental manpower training,
and total asset, and the output criteria for DEA are profit and sales amount.
Keywords: Green Supply Chain, Suppliers Selection, Voting Analytic Hierarchy Process, Data
Envelopment Analysis, 3C industry
1. 前言
近年來我國高科技產業蓬勃發展，尤其是以資訊電子、通訊為主的3C產業，無論在產值、
出口值、雇用員工人數，或研究發展經費上，占全體製造業的比重均大幅提升，成為經濟成
長的主要動力產業之一。然而，隨著環境保護、永續發展的需求，全球為因應此綠色浪潮，
32-2-1 歐盟環保指令
歐盟環境處為減低電子電機產品的廢棄物對環境引起的衝擊，提出廢電機電子設備指令
(Waste Electrical Electronic Equipment, WEEE)該指令已於2003年2月正式通過，成為歐盟EU
2002/96/EC指令，該指令主要內容包括：電機電子物品分類收集、回收、處理行為及使用者
資訊提供的規定，其涵蓋範圍計有10大類，超過百項產品。而其主要宗旨係藉由EEE（電機
電子設備）產品回收率的提升，來達成以下目的：1.削減EEE廢棄物的產量；2.避免過多的EEE
廢棄物進入掩埋場；3.提升資源再利用之比率，以降低產品對環境之衝擊。
伴隨著WEEE指令一同公告的為有害物質限用指令(Restriction of Hazardous Substance,
RoHS)。該指令主要管制電機電子產品中使用重金屬：鉛、鎘、六價鉻、汞、多溴聯苯(PBBs)
及多溴化二苯乙醚(PBDEs)等6種化學物質。自2006年7月1日起，上述電子電機設備不得含有
此六種有害化學物質；未能遵守此規範之電子電機設備產品將禁止於歐盟各國銷售。「危害物
質限用指令」的最重要關鍵，乃在於管制作業必需配合對供應鏈體系的管理，逐層管制與查
核這些限用物質的使用。由於RoHS是屬於一個調和性的指令(Harmonization Directive)，其目
的是希望歐盟各國之間對電機電子物品的管制趨於一致；一旦物品符合標準，便可以自由地
流通。因此，各國落實法規的相關規範，並沒有太大的變化空間。不過，由於RoHS對於物品
相關的檢測與查核未作深入的規定，短期之內OEM/ODM廠商要注意的符合要件及作業方
式，將是遵循各大型的國際公司、或品牌大廠對於達成限用物質相關檢測以及驗證的作業規
範。
歐盟地區成員國於2002年10月推出「最終使用設備生態化設計」(Eco-Design of End Use
Equipment, EuP)指令的草案，並於2003年8月正式向歐盟議會提出「使用能源產品生態化設計
指令」草案審讀，經二讀通過。依據該指令，製造商必須評估產品於整體生命週期中的環境
考量，並將與產品相關的環保特性，以量化的方式建置成產品的生態說明書(Ecological
Profile)。此外，製造商亦必須由產品生命週期之評估，尋求其他可能的替代技術，藉以提昇
產品的環境績效，開發出更佳環境友善(Environmental Friendly)之產品。
2-3 供應商評選
於1960年代製造商在選擇供應商時最重視的因素為品質、交期及過去績效等三項。
Wilson(1994)的研究分析70至90年代供應商選擇評估標準的相對重要性變化情形結果顯示：品
質及服務對於供應商選擇重要性相對的提高，主要原因是供應商與製造商的關係已漸由競爭
轉變為合作，不再以價格為選擇供應商的最主要因素。Goffin et al.(1997)的研究中針對過去傳
統的供應關係與現行供應鏈環境下對供應商評選準則做比較，研究結果可發現，過去選擇供
應商時所強調的重點是價格、品質及交貨速度等幾項，隨著環境的轉變，全球化競爭下的供
應鏈環境除了原有的成本、品質等考量外，對供應商的要求重點更為廣泛，包括技術能力、
財務能力、售後服務及策略性考量等。由以上分析得知，在不同時期與不同環境下，重視的
供應商評選準則會有所不同。

3-3 資料包絡分析法
資料包絡分析法(Data Envelopment Analysis, DEA)是一種衡量多項投入與多項產出的決
策制定單位（Decision Making Unit, DMU）之相對效率方法。亦是一套可包含量化與質化，
結合效率與效能變項的一種整體生產力衡量方法。其特性如下(Charnes et al., 1981)：1.可處理
多項投入、多項產出之效率評估問題。2.單位不變性(Units Invariance)。3.可以單一綜合指標
衡量效率。4.權重之決定不受人為主觀因素的影響。5.可同時處理比率資料及非比率資料。6.
可處理組織外之環境變數。7.可獲得資源使用狀況之相關資訊。基於DEA具有上述的特性，
故Lewin and Minton (1986)因而認為DEA為一效率評估之優良方法。應用在供應商評選準則的
選擇上可以避免主觀因素的影響，並將可行供應商進行效率比較，以執行供應商評選之最佳
決策。
綜合上述分析流程為：1.搜集訪談資料與整理相關文件。2.歸納出3C產業關鍵的供應商
評選決策需求，並確定供應商評選內容與範圍。3.以VAHP計算評選準則之權重；DEA求得綠
色供應商之績效。4.依據評選模式設計協同評選流程。

5. 結論
3C產業為技術與知識密集的產業，在我國屬發展成熟但競爭劇烈的產業。其在產業經營
方面，多偏重於技術發展，本研究提供3C產業評選供應商與經營管理之理論概念與實務應用
技術，使得在競爭白熱化及不斷改變的3C市場環境中，建立一套有效的綠色供應商評選準則
與流程。由於目前有些企業在進行供應商評選時，主要焦點還是在著重在傳統的準則上，例
如價格、交期、品質或服務，但在面對日益高漲的環保意識，於評選供應商方面實需考慮環
境保護概念與符合國際法規之需求。
為了建立綠色評選準則架構與綠色協同供應商評選模式，本研究使用VAHP作為供應商評
選分析方法。VAHP將成對比較的方式改為順序性的排列，在待評準則較多的狀況下，減少評
選者的錯亂感覺，使評選作業準確性提高。另外，輔以資料包絡分析法，使用客觀資料分析，
避免對準則評估之主觀判斷，進而篩選出合適的供應商。經由本研究調查發現歸納出下列結
論：
1. 本研究透過專家的訪談與文獻的搜集，找出適合3C產業供應商的評選準則，建立環境管
理、製程技術與設計績效此三大構面的指標。
2. 透過VAHP分析方法與群體決策計算之後發現3C廠商目前評選綠色供應商方面，於環境管
理構面以綠色產品的保證、環保認證、遵守環境法規為主要考量，其次為：教育訓練、環
境政策與綠色採購形象。在製程技術構面則是以廢氣排放處理、取得環境友善技術為首要
考量，其次為有害物質限禁用、標準檢驗技術與廢水排放處理。而設計績效構面，主要以
研發環境友善材料為主要考量，再來為綠色包裝、使用產品別設計能力、彈性變更設計。
此結果與訪談3C產業廠商及文獻資料相互驗證。
3. 經由DEA分析法的輔助可知若供應商屬於規模報酬固定（CRS）則表示營運已達最適生產
規模；但若供應商是處於規模報酬遞減（DRS）的階段，代表其資源投入相對產出已顯過
度，因此，應減少其資源的投入量，管理者可考慮降低規模，以提升效率；反之若供應商
處於規模報酬遞增（IRS）的階段，對於這類規模報酬遞增的供應商，管理者可考慮將規
模擴大，進而提升該公司的效率。

4. SYSTEM ANALYSIS AND DESIGN
4.1 System Analysis
The user demand of a R&D document classification and
retrieval system was firstly analyzed, and system information
requirement and data flow are secondly defined. Finally, the
system structure, functional operation and system features are
established and presented using IDEF0, a structured system
analysis tool. The document classification and retrieval
system comprises three sub-systems, namely document
classification system, document retrieval system, and
maintenance & management system. Figure 4 depicts the
system structure constructed by IDEF0.
Figure 4: IDEF0 Architecture of Document Classification &
Retrieval System
A document classification system (A1) enables the users
to upload documents for archiving. Then, the documents are
categorized into a knowledge dimension for subsequent
document retrieval. As a result of document classification, a
document retrieval system (A2) allows users to look up
documents according to knowledge dimension, category, or
keywords; maintenance and management system (A3) is
designed to meet the personal requirements of system users,
configure the system functions, update the preset keyword
sets, etc.
The document classification system consists of four
modules, i.e. process classification (A11), document
judgment (A12), stemming (A13) and technology and
equipment & material classification (A14). The process
classification module enables domain experts to define the
category of documents, according to the document’s
relationship. The document judgment module distinguishes
document type to literal or graphic documents. In the case of
literal documents, stemming is triggered and performed; in
the case of graphic documents, keywords set for the
document must be defined manually. The keywords are then
compared via VSM through technology and equipment &
material classification module. Then, the degree of
correlation between document and knowledge dimension is
calculated and consequently the document categorized.
The document retrieval system consists of three
modules, i.e. process dimension retrieval (A21), technology
dimension retrieval module (A22) and equipment & material
dimension retrieval module (A23). A document retrieval
system primarily aims to retrieve documents according to the
desired classification results of process, technology and
equipment & material. In the case of retrieval by process
dimension, system is required to list the property of process
category; in the case of retrieval by technology or equipment
& material dimension, only the degree of correlation between
documents and category shall be listed.
The maintenance and management system is primarily
used to manage users’ access authorization (A31) and system
configuration (A32). This sub-system empowers the system
administrator to define different member groups, of which
common member groups have only the right to update their
own documents, and domain expert groups have the right to
change the document property. The keyword set of
technology and equipment & material dimension can be
supplemented by the domain experts (A33).
4.2 System Design
The document classification & retrieval system is a
three-tier information processing structure. The users’ 
interface and system features are integrated by JSP. The
accessing to database is interpreted and performed by Tomcat
and JDBC. The prototype system is configured and designed
to be installed onto the servers at R&D site, which is
accessible via Intranet or Internet. Figure 5 illustrates the
system architecture.
1. Client: the researchers in R&D department are major
system users. The Client is linked to the system via
internet browsers and networked computers.
2. Web Server: responsible for integrating the clients with
database, playing the role of data collection,
manipulation and transfer.
3. Database Server: responsible for storage of documents
and knowledge dimension data based.
4.3 Database Design
The design of a database for the prototype system is
mainly based on relational database. The setting of primary
keys and database normalization are inevitable for
implementing a database application system successfully.
The primary key is a field that uniquely describes each record.
In the document classification system, for instance, the
document ID number is set to be the primary key in the data
table storing document information. This field is unique and
generated by the system automatically. The system has ten
defined data tables, such as: equipment & material
correlation, keywords of equipment & material, documents of
equipment & material, technology correlation, keywords of
technology, technology documents, project process
documents, document types, users and category vector. They
are all defined in the SQL database server.

5. CONCLUSION
Traditional document classification is heavily
dependent upon manual efforts, which cannot deal
extensively with the management of electronic documents in
tune with the growth of enterprises and information
technology. This research expanded the knowledge structure
concept of space vector model, and proposed an integrated
and process-oriented document classification and retrieval
structure for the R&D activities of semiconductor industry in
Taiwan. It assists document classification, and thus improves
classification efficiency. It also provides precise retrieval on
multi-dimension knowledge and documents.
We defined knowledge dimension for document
classification into project process dimension, technology
dimension and equipment & material dimension, allowing
multiple document classification. Only highly-related
documents were retrieved and thus improving the efficiency
of traditional full-text retrieval via keywords. We also
analyzed and designed a document classification & retrieval
prototype system using space vector model, such that the
inquiry results are collated according to the degree of
correlation. With the objective document evaluation
mechanism, the document classification and retrieval
efficiency can be dramatically enhanced.
As for the continuation of the exploitable and
extendable part of this study, we are seeking ways to improve
