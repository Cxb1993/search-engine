       執行期限 ： 95/8/1 - 98/7/31

一、中文摘要  
本研究係三年計畫，第一年之研究重點為＂低序化溫度L10 FePt薄膜之研製＂，首先採
用直流磁控濺鍍，在室溫將FePt合金薄膜濺鍍在表面自然氧化的矽晶圓上。初鍍薄膜為fcc 
FePt軟磁相，初鍍膜經過適當溫度的退火再爐冷至室溫後，無序的fcc FePt軟磁相會轉變為有
序的fct L10 FePt硬磁相。我們發現將基板預熱至300 ℃後再爐冷至室溫，可將L10 FePt相的
序化溫度降至350 ℃。而FePt的晶粒尺寸也隨著序化溫度的降低而下降，其中膜厚100 nm的
Fe52Pt48合金薄膜，在400 ℃的低溫下退火1小時，即可獲得良好的平行膜面磁性質，其平行
膜面頑磁力Hc//約為7.6 kOe，晶粒尺寸為21.9 nm。 
第二年之研究重點為＂垂直磁異向性FePt合金薄膜之研製＂，本實驗採用磁控濺鍍，在
表面自然氧化的(100)指向矽晶圓及7059系列的康寧玻璃為基板，鍍製FePt/Pt/Cr 三層薄膜，
實驗結果得知，FePt / Cr雙層薄膜並不會直接引出FePt (001)的垂直磁異向性，因為FePt薄膜
會與Cr底層反應而在介面處形成Cr rich的CrFePt的磊晶阻礙層，造成Cr (002)的磊晶效應在介
面處被CrFePt合金阻斷，而當Pt緩衝層加入後，Pt緩衝層便扮演一座橋樑的角色，除了阻擋
Cr底層原子擴散進FePt磁性層外，半整合性介面還會順利的承接Cr底層的(002)結構，並長成
Pt(001)的優選方位，最後FePt磁性層便會沿著Pt(001)的方位而長成L10 FePt (001)的優選方
位，使FePt薄膜表現出垂直磁異向性。在實驗中，L10 FePt phase的序化溫度約300 oC。 
    第三年之研究重點為＂ FePt垂直記錄媒體之軟磁穩定層研究＂，對於垂直記錄而言，
已知在記錄層底部加上一層易磁化軸平行膜面的軟磁薄膜，將具有高紀錄密度的潛力，此一
軟磁層扮演著flux sink的角色，使磁化pattern成為連續的horse-shoe磁鐵，垂直的部分位於記
錄層而水平的部分則位於軟磁底層，依此將可大大的減低薄膜之消磁場而增進記錄媒體之熱
穩定性。我們在L10 FePt (001)/Pt(001)/Cr(002)薄膜中加入FeTaC或CoCr軟磁底層，而加入
FeTaC軟磁層後，會破壞FePt (001)的優選方位，因此須再加入一層非晶質的SiNx薄膜於Cr

Semi-coherent epitaxial growth was initiated from the Cr (002) underlayer, continued through the

The study of third year focuses on “the study of soft magnetic stability layer in perpendicular 
recording media”. It has been known that adding a soft magnetic under-layer whose easy axis 
parallel to the film plane to perpendicular recording media is promising candidate for high-density 
magnetic recording. The soft magnetic under-layer plays a role of flux sink which lets 
magnetization pattern like continuous hose-shoe magnet. The perpendicular part is in the recording 
layer and longitudinal part is in the soft magnetic under-layer. This will reduce the demagnetizing 
field and increase the thermal stability of the recording media. In this study we added FeTaC or 
CoCr soft magnetic under-layer to the L10 FePt(001)/Pt(001)/Cr(002) films. However, the addition 
of FeTaC may destroy the preferred orientation of L10 FePt (001) magnetic layer. Therefore, it 
needs to add another non-magnetic SiNx amorphous film between Cr(002) layer and FeTaC 
under-layer to hinder the diffusion between them during substrate heating sputtering. The FeTaC or 
CoCr film with various film thickness are deposited on corning glass substrates or nature oxidized 
Si wafer substrates by dc magnetron sputtering at various substrate temperatures in order to get 
good soft magnetic properties. An amorphous SiNx layer is deposited on the FeTaC film by rf 
magnetron sputtering of Si3N4 target to avoid interlayer diffusion. The Cr film with various film 
thickness is deposited on SiNx layer by dc magnetron sputtering at various substrate temperatures 
in order to obtain Cr(002) texture. Then FePt film is deposited on the Cr(002) layer by dc 
magnetron co-sputtering of Fe target and Pt target at various sputtering rates and substrate 
temperatures in order to get L10 FePt(001) texture. Effects of sputtering rate, substrate temperature 
and film thickness on the magnetic properties and microstructure of the FeTaC and CoCr 
underlayers, and effect of SiNx layer thickness on the preferred orientation of FePt/Cr films are 
investigated.

代磁記錄媒體材料。原子百分比接近1：1的L10 FePt phase因具有極高的磁晶異向性常數Ku 
(接近 108 erg / cm3)，高的矯頑磁力(實驗證明已可達100 kOe以上)，高的飽和磁化量及良好
的抗腐蝕性等優點[4,17]，已被認為是極具潛力的新一代磁記錄媒體材料，目前世界各國都
積極參與L10 FePt合金薄膜應用於磁記錄媒體上之研究。相較於CoCrPt系合金，由於L10 FePt 
phase合金薄膜具有更高的磁晶異向性常數Ku及飽和磁化量Ms，因此L10 FePt phase將會有
更小的超順磁粒子尺寸，而能得到較高的磁記錄密度。雖然L10 FePt薄膜具備許多優異的性
質，但他也有一些先天的限制而減緩了他被應用於磁記錄媒體的速度，其中最主要的兩點
就是(1)晶粒之易磁化方向[001]不易垂直膜面成長及(2)形成L10 FePt phase所需的序化溫度
過高，若要使FePt薄膜能應用於垂直磁記錄媒體，必須先克服以上兩點，由於初鍍的 FePt
薄膜為fcc結構的γ-FePt，其最緊密堆積平面(111)面具有最低的表面能，因此初鍍的γ-FePt
薄膜其(111)面將會平行於膜面成長。γ-FePt薄膜經過退火後會轉變為L10 FePt phase，其為fct
結構，因此L10 FePt (111)面仍將平行於膜面，即 [111]方向垂直於膜面。fct結構的L10 FePt 
phase之易磁化軸為[001]，而[001]方向與[111]方向之夾角約為55o，所以[001]易磁化軸與膜
面夾角約為35 o，如圖2-1所示。因此，若要使FePt薄膜能應用於垂直磁記錄媒體，必須使易
磁化軸方向[001]垂直膜面，而FePt薄膜通常可藉由底層的引入來誘發磁性層之易磁化軸
[001]垂直膜面，以呈現垂直磁異向性。

然XRD告訴我們結構並不是FePt(001)的優選方位，但卻無法知道FePt薄膜與Cr底層之間的介
面性質，然而由圖(c)我們發現在FePt薄膜及Cr底層的介面處有不同於Cr底底層結構的區域
(如圖(c)中所選取的區域)，並阻斷了FePt磁性層沿著Cr底層結構成長的路徑。在此我們稱此
有別於Cr底層與FePt磁性層結構的組織為『磊晶障礙層(Epitaxial barrier)』。為了徹底瞭解此
層，我們將圖(c)中所選取的區域以TEM放大，其結果顯示於圖(d)，由圖(d)得知磊晶障礙層
的結構似乎與Cr底層及FePt磁性層不同，反倒像是非晶質(amorphous)的感覺，而且大小及分
佈似乎也沒有一定的規則，有的區域大於10 nm (圖中A區)，有的則是5 nm的大小(圖中B區)，
但可以確定的是，此磊晶障礙層的確會阻斷FePt磁性層沿著Cr底層結構成長的路徑，也就是
因為此磊晶障礙層的存在，才會使FePt薄膜無法順著Cr(002)底層長成具有垂直磁異向性的
(001)優選方位。 
    圖4-2-5是FePt(20 nm)/Cr(90 nm)雙層薄膜的AES縱深元素分佈圖，鍍製FePt薄膜的基板

膜的表面。同時，FePt中的Fe原子與Pt也會擴散進Cr底層，而且Fe原子的擴散速度似乎比Pt

間相互擴散的能量已足夠，開始有明顯的層間擴散發生，使Cr原子、Fe原子及Pt原子相互擴
散進薄膜內部，也因此破壞了FePt薄膜的磁性質。 
前面的FePt/Cr(002)雙層結構已經確定FePt薄膜直接鍍在Cr(002)的底層上是無法得到L10 
FePt(001)垂直磁異向性的性質，因為在Cr底層與FePt磁性層間會形成一層磊晶阻礙層而阻斷
FePt薄膜沿著Cr底層的(002)方向磊晶，因此，為了要引出FePt薄膜的垂直磁異向性，則必須

排列，而FePt(001)[100]則會沿著Pt(001)[100]排列而得到FePt(001)的優選方位。

II. EXPERIMENTS 
First, the Ag under layer (30nm) was deposited on glass substrates at room temperature. Then different volume 
percents (0~55.1 vol. %) of SiNx ceramic material are added to the CoPt films (16 nm) by co-sputtering of SiNx and 
CoPt on the Ag under layer (30nm) at room temperature. The CoPt and Ag films were fabricated by dc magnetron 
sputtering (base pressure was 5×10-7 torr) of pure Ag (99.95%), Co (99.95%) and Pt (99.95%) targets. The addition of 
SiNx was fabricated by rf-magnetron sputtering of Si3N4 target. Finally, the CoPt - SiNx / Ag films were annealed at 
700 ℃ for 30 minutes. The composition and thickness of the film were determined by an energy dispersive X-ray 
spectrometer (EDS) and an atomic force microscope (AFM), respectively. magnetic properties of the films were 
measured by using a vibrating sample magnetometer (VSM) and a superconducting quantum interference device 
(SQUID). The film structure was examined by an X-ray diffractometer (XRD) and a field emission gun high resolution 
transmission electron microscope (FEG-TEM). 
 
III. RESULTS AND DISCUSSION 
According to the past researches [5, 8, 14], it has been reported that the lattice parameter of Ag under-layer is larger 
than that of CoPt, they have 5.7 % misfit and result in the expanding the CoPt [010] and [100] axes. The in-plane 
expansion causes the shrinkage of the CoPt [001] axis which is perpendicular to the film plane. This is implied that the 
distortion of the CoPt unit cell may enhance the ordering of L10 CoPt and aiding the formation of the L10 CoPt (001) 
whose magnetic easy axis is perpendicular to the film plane. Therefore we introduce the Ag films as the under layer first 
to get perpendicular magnetic anisotropy CoPt film. 
Fig. 1 is the M-H loops of the CoPt (16 nm)/Ag (30 nm) film annealed at 700 ℃ for 30 minutes. It shows that the 
magnetic CoPt film exhibits perpendicular magnetic anisotropy with an out-of-plane squareness and in-plane squareness 
of about 0.95 and 0.42, respectively. The Ms and Hc⊥ values are about 390 emu/cm3 and 18 kOe, respectively.  
Fig. 2 is the X-ray diffraction patterns of the (CoPt)1-y–(SiNx)y /Ag films which annealed at 700 ℃ for 30 minutes. It is 
found that the (001) superlattice peak of ordering phase appears in the CoPt /Ag film which has no SiNx. When the SiNx 
is added, the intensities of CoPt (001) and (002) superlattice peaks of the (CoPt)1-y–(SiNx)y/Ag films are decreased. It is 
implies that the SiNx will break up the epitaxy between CoPt (001) and Ag (111) and lower the perpendicular magnetic 
anisotropy of the film. 
Fig. 3 shows the effect of SiNx content on the Hc of (CoPt)1-y–(SiNx)y/Ag films which annealed 700 ℃ for 30 
minutes.  We can compare these results with the FEG-TEM images of Fig. 4. In the Fig. 4 (a), the grain size of the 
CoPt/Ag films without SiNx addition annealed at 700 ℃ for 30 minute is about 130 nm. As the SiNx content is 6.3 vol. 
%, the CoPt particle sizes are not uniform, as shown in Fig. 4 (b). It is suggested that SiNx will break up the epitaxy 
between CoPt (001) and Ag (111). Moreover, the amount of ordering phase of (CoPt)93.7–(SiNx)6.3/Ag film is lesser than 
that in the CoPt/Ag film, as showon in Fig. 2. The particle size distribution is broad and some CoPt particles are smaller 
than the single domain diameter cause a decrease of Hc┴ and in-plane coercivity (Hc//) of (CoPt)93.7–(SiNx)6.3/Ag film.

VI. EXPERIMENTS 
First, the Ag under layer (30nm) was deposited on glass substrates at room temperature. Then different volume 
percents (0~55.1 vol. %) of SiNx ceramic material are added to the CoPt films (16 nm) to form a granular 
microstructure of CoPt film by co-sputtering of SiNx and CoPt on the Ag under layer (30nm) at room temperature then 
annealed at 700 ℃ for 30 minutes. In order to reduce the transformation temperature of CoPt films from fcc to fct 
structure, different volume percents (0~67.3 vol. %) of Ag is added into the CoPt- SiNx films. The CoPt, additive Ag 
and Ag under layer were fabricated by dc magnetron sputtering of pure Ag (99.95%), Co (99.95%) and Pt (99.95%) 
targets (base pressure was 5×10-7 torr). The addition of SiNx was fabricated by rf-magnetron sputtering of Si3N4 target. 
Finally, the (CoPt-SiNx)1-yAgy /Ag/glass films were annealed at 400, 500 and 600 ℃ for 30 and 60 minutes. The 
composition and thickness of the film were determined by an energy dispersive X-ray spectrometer (EDS) and an 
atomic force microscope (AFM), respectively. Magnetic properties of the films were measured by using a vibrating 
sample magnetometer (VSM) and a superconducting quantum interference device (SQUID). The film structure was 
examined by an X-ray diffractometer (XRD) and a field emission gun high resolution transmission electron microscope 
(FEG-TEM).  
VII. RESULTS AND DISCUSSION 
Fig. 1 is the X-ray diffraction patterns of the (CoPt)1-y-(SiNx)y /Ag films which annealed at 700 ℃ for 30 minutes. It 
is found that the (001) superlattice peak of ordering phase appears in the CoPt /Ag film which has no SiNx. When the 
SiNx is added, the intensities of fct-CoPt (001) and (002) superlattice peaks of the (CoPt)1-y-(SiNx)y/Ag films are 
decreased. This implies that the SiNx will break up the epitaxy growth between fct-CoPt (001) and Ag (111) and lower 
the perpendicular magnetic anisotropy of the film.

VIII. CONCLUSIONS 
We have investigated the magnetic properties and microstructure of co-sputtered nanocomposite (CoPt)1-y-(SiNx)y/Ag 
films over the varieties of SiNx contents. Granular structure of ordered CoPt-SiNx films with average grain size smaller 
than 10 nm could be obtained by annealing the (CoPt)53.8-(SiNx)46.2/Ag films at 700 ℃. We try to decrease the ordering 
temperature of CoPt by adding Ag. It is found that the 59.2 vol. % Ag addition to the CoPt-SiNx/Ag film would reduce 
the ordering temperature of the film more than 100 ℃ approximately. This [(CoPt)53.8-(SiNx)46.2]40.8Ag59.2/Ag films ill 
be a candidate of ultra-high density magnetic recording media.
