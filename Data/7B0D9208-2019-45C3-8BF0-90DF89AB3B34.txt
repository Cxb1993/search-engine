一、中文摘要 
考慮當今無線感測網路的應用於節點
數眾多，在實務上並考量佈建時，低成
本、低功率的感測節點便成為不可避免
的功能。為了提升系統效能，合作式通
訊經由分集增益，已經被認可為其中一
項重要技術，用以幫助實現無線感測網
路抵抗路徑衰減以及干擾，而且同時有
效節省能源的方法。在本篇結案報告
中，主要計畫執行方面，將我們提出的
一個么正空時調變碼技術，其特徵為適
用於在具有頻率選擇性的通道傳輸的
單載波塊狀傳輸方式，而對應的簡單接
收器卻可萃取出空間與多路徑分集增
益。經由數值模擬，我們得到此多感測
節點具有此優勢的驗證。除此之外，此
技術被拓展應用於合作式通訊，目的為
考慮由中繼點的合作來達到節能與降
低中斷機率。我們利用可以在叢集式架
構下，萃取出虛擬的多傳輸、多輸入模
式，在無線感測網路上開發出分集增
益，來解決感測節點的低成本、低功率
的限制。也就是說，感測節點會被先群
組化成叢集，然後連續兩個鄰近的叢集
裡感測節點的以合作式通訊的模式做
資料傳遞，類似一次跳躍。緊接著從來
源到目的一般需要做多次跳躍，解決環
境上對訊號傳輸的限制。本計劃結果顯
示以叢集為基礎的實體層傳輸機制，經
由電腦模擬後可以發現，中斷機率確實
與分集大小有冪次方的關係。除此之
外，中斷機率與設計的空時塊狀碼的編
碼分集有關，也就是說使用較多的節
點，空時塊狀碼的限制會將中斷機率拉
高，但是空間分集增益卻可將系統效能
提升。

For each hop it involves a distributed

clusters with potential cooperation, which 
was illustrated in three-node single-hop 
transmission above. In contrast with that 
case, the crucial clustering enables MIMO 
channel indeed. Data transmission is split

principle of time division in half-duplexing. 
Indeed, the two channels can be further 
referred to as intracluster (ITA) channel

corresponding to broadcasting nature to

3 Receiver Processing
To enable cognitive radio concept, we dissect the re-
ceiver processing into a few essential steps, which will be
individually addressed below. It is noteworthy that prior to
being subjected to the following processing, the received
signal is ﬁrst down-converted into intermediate frequency
(IF) band and then down-sampled to a sampling rate, which
is assumed four times the channel bandwidth with an aim at
performing ﬁne timing synchronization.

formance loss, which is nonetheless diminished as SNR in-
creases. Meanwhile, a 10−4 BER at 20dB SNR under mul-

receiver algorithms along with advanced hardware calibra-
tion are likely to be employed at the request of better system
performance.
4 Conclusion
We investigate CR-featured WiMAX systems and de-
velop feasible algorithms through considerations in numer-
ical simulation to veriﬁcation by DSP implementation in
SDR platform. Indeed, discerning availability of WiMAX
service and extracting critical information for access to net-
work are explored separately. DSP software implementa-
tion implies room for performance improvement using ad-
vanced processing, though this work demonstrates feasibil-
ity of CR concept in SDR is no longer beyond reach.
