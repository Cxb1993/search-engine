壓電超晶格極子的能量組成與電磁幅射 
The Energy Constituents and Electromagnetic Radiation of Polaritons in 
Piezoelectirc Superlattice 
計畫編號：NSC 97-2221-E002-106 
執行期限：97 年 8 月 1 日至 98 年 7 月 31 日 
主持人：周元昉  國立台灣大學機械工程學系 
 
中文摘要 
超晶格平板的極子行為來自週期結構中
機械波與電磁波的耦合，在某些頻率此耦合
現象十分強烈，在分析上必須兼顧Newton’s 
2nd Law與Maxwell’s equations，將此二者聯立
來解出壓電平板對應於Lamb wave的極子頻
譜與場形，由所獲得的場形可以求得極子的
機械能與電磁能的分佈。本研究並探討PZT
平板的週期極化方法，經由在試片兩面的特
定位置施加適當的電壓後，可達到週期極化
的目標。由實驗顯示PZT超晶格平板吸收電
磁能的能力遠較單向極化平板為強，且因振
幅較大故應較易於輻射電磁波。  
關鍵字：極子, 壓電超晶格, 頻譜, 能量 
Abstract 
The polariton behavior in piezoelectric 
superlattice is originated from the coupling 
between mechanical wave and electro-magnetic 
wave. Since the coupling is very strong in some 
frequencies, both Newton’s 2nd Law and 
Maxwell’s equations have been included in the 
analysis. The simultaneous equations is solved 
to obtain the spectrum and field patterns of 
polaritons in the piezoelectric superlattice plate 
corresponding to that of Lamb waves. The 
acoustic energy and electromagnetic energy 
distribution of polaritons are found from their 
field patterns. The method of fabricating 
periodically polarized specimen is studied. PZT 
superlattice plates have been fabricated 
successfully by applying appropriate voltages 
on specific locations. Experiments show that 
PZT superlattice plates can absorb much more 
EM energy than uniaxially polarized plates. 
Due to large vibration amplitude, these PZT 
superlattice plates should also radiate more EM 
wave than uniaxially polarized plates. 
Keywords: Polariton, Piezoelectric superlattice, 
Spectrum, Energy 
1. 緣起 
鐵電材料可經由週期性的極化，使某一
特定頻率的聲波與電磁波耦合。在 1999 年，
Lu[1]等人將兩種極化相反的 LiNbO3 鐵電晶
體以週期排列成超晶格結構，導致對微波的
吸收以及介電性質產生非常行為，並激發出
類似存在於離子晶體內的極子行為。Zhu 等
人[2]在 2003 年探討在此種壓電超晶格內電
磁波的傳遞，發現可在某些特定的頻率範圍
使機械波與電磁波發生強烈的耦合而產生一
種新型極子。Zhang[3]等人在 2004 年探討壓
電超晶格內極子的能隙。同年他們也研究準
週期結構的壓電超晶格內極子的行為[4]。前

6. 計畫成果自評 
對於複雜的跨領域研究，訓練研究生是
非常費時費力的工作，因此若非長期的計
畫，跨領域研究的投資報酬是很不合算的。 
一年期計畫與三年期計畫的研究標的與
研究方法的考量完全不同。本研究主題原係
申請三年的專題研究計畫，但只獲核定一
年，致使研究目標混亂、執行困難。本年的
工作在理論推演方面，已基本完成，但因式
(7)與式(9)內的元素數值相差巨大，導致數值
計算非常困難，因此未能獲得頻譜圖與計算
出能量的分佈。在實驗方面尚須製作一天線
來接收輻射的信號，才能確實比較其結果，
這些都需要人力與物力的支援。

CONCLUSIONS 
By comparing displacement profiles of finite and infinite periodic 
structure, their attenuation coefficients are found almost identical 
provided the number of alternating layers is adequate. Therefore 
referring the design of reflection layers to the band structures of the 
corresponding phononic crystals is reasonable although the attenuation 
coefficient of a finite periodic structure can not be calculated directly. 
For one dimensional phononic crystals, the attenuation coefficient 
becomes larger as the first band gap gets wider. The characteristic 
impedance ratio and density ratio between two interlaced materials 
increase simultaneously; the first band gap width also increases. This 
character can be adopted as a guideline in the design of solidly 
mounted resonators. 
Based on this guideline, Al and W are chosen as materials for the 
reflection structure. By calculating its electric impedance, the resonant 
frequency is found to be the same as the center frequency of first band 
gap of the corresponding phononic crystal. It shows that employing 
this stop band character to design the reflection structure of SMR is 
adequate and efficient.
