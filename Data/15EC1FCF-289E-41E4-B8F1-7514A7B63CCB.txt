The present research used home-made anodic aluminum oxide (AAO) membranes as the 
template to grow Fe-Ni alloy nanowire arrays and investigated the effects of externally applied 
magnetic field during wire growth on the product characteristics, particularly the magnetic 
properties. Fe-Ni alloys are soft magnetic materials with low coercivities, and they may be affected 
during the electrochemical growth process if subject to externally applied magnetic fields. 
    We successfully produced AAO membranes with pore diameters of 20 and 50 nm. The AAO 
membranes of 50 nm diameter can be used for preparation of nanowire arrays of large areas. The 
resulting Fe-Ni nanowire arrays did exhibit differences in magnetic properties, particularly for the 
Fe:Ni molar ratios of 1:3 and 3:1. Further study is underway to understand the reasons behind the 
differences. 
 
Key words：anodic aluminum oxide (AAO)template、external magnetic field、nickel-iron 
alloy、nano-arrays 
 
一、前言 
近幾年來奈米材料因其尺度之微小而使材料產生與塊材不同的特性，造成很多特殊功能
應用，使得奈米材料已漸漸受到各界重視並應用於廣泛領域。毋庸置疑，材料科技朝奈米級
尺度發展已是大勢所趨。而在這個資訊發達的時代裡，電子資訊設備已普遍使用，而在這些
資訊中需要應用多種資訊之磁功能材料，例如：磁記錄材料、磁存儲材料、磁光材料等。因
此奈米磁性材料已成為今日資訊科技發展先進國家重要的研發課題之一。

第二年成果 
 
摘要 
    在資訊發達的時代裡，開發具有更高密度的磁紀錄元件是必然的趨勢。許多研究者發現
陣列式磁性奈米金屬線在磁記錄元件中有其應用之相當潛能。而欲製備陣列式磁性奈米線之
方法很多，電化學沉積法具有其它技術所沒有的優點，例如製程簡單、成本低、可複製性高、
損失少及污染少，尤其藉由改變電位、溶液組成等變因可有效控制結晶的形狀以及晶格成長
方向等特點。而模版法具高度規則性，因此本研究採用電化學沉積法搭配模版使用來作為製
備磁性奈米線陣列之主要方法。 
    本研究以自製陽極氧化鋁模板(AAO)來作為模版，並搭配成長過程中施加外加磁場，成
長大範圍的規則排列鎳銀合金之奈米線陣列。使用同時含有磁性及非磁性之鹽類先驅物之電
解液，在外加磁場引導下進行電化學沉積，並成功地單一步驟製備出50nm 核殼磁性-非磁性
之奈米線陣列。 
 
關鍵字： 陽極氧化鋁、外加磁場、奈米線陣列、核殼結構

technological issue. Researchers discovered that nanoscale magnetic metallic wire arrays possess 
great potential as magnetic recording media. There are a number of ways to prepare nanoscale 
magnetic metallic wire arrays. Among them, electrochemical depositions have some merits over 
others, for example, easy preparation, cost effectiveness, and controllable arrays characteristics 
through adjustment in applied potential and electrolyte formulation.

三、文獻探討 
    一維奈米材料除了傳統由單一成分所組成之一維奈米結構外，尚有其它由雙成分以上所
組成的一維複合材料，如同軸核殼結構，因其表現出與以往單一成分一維結構於相關性質上
不同之特性，近年來亦引起相當多的研究投入。一般而言，對於同軸核殼奈米線的製備乃是
利用所謂的多步驟沈積法(multi-step deposition)來獲得，例如先於第一步驟製備出核心奈米
線，再於其上成長第二種材料；或者，將核心奈米線暴露於特定化學環境中，使其表面與環
境物料相互反應而生成另一物質於其上，進而形成一維同軸核殼結構。而近來亦有學者利用
化學反應性差異之概念而進行一步驟合成一維同軸核殼結構奈米線。例如 Hsu等人 1利用有
機-金屬化學氣相沉積法(Metal-Organic Chemistry Vapor Deposition, MOCVD)一步驟製備出
CdS-ZnS及 Cd1-xZn xS-ZnS之奈米線。其結構如圖 1所示。Wang 等人 2於電化學共沈積之
系統內，使用含有 Ni(H2NSO3)2、CuSO4 與 H3BO3之電解液，成功地於 AAO 模版內製備出
核-殼(銅-鎳)複合奈米線陣列，其合成流程如圖 2所示，其反應機制示意圖如圖 3所示，AAO
模版之表面會受到電解液之親水化的作用而形成-OH鍵，此時，電解液內之 Ni2+離子則會與
氧原子產生鍵結，因而選擇性地吸附於 AAO 模版之表面；反之，Cu2+離子則雜亂地分佈於
AAO通道內之液相環境中。當施以足夠之電化學電位時，Ni2+會率先在模版之表面進行還原
反應而生成 Ni晶體，而 Cu2+則順勢地於所生成之 Ni晶體表面上進行還原而沈積出 Cu。因
此，當電化學反應結束後，奈米線之組成會以 Cu為核心，其外層包覆著一薄層之 Ni。

Cobalt oxide aerogels of ideal supercapacitive 
properties prepared with an epoxide synthetic route

Abstract 
Mesoporous structures of high specific surface areas and high porosities, such as 
aerogels, are ideal for supercapacitor applications. This idea was successfully demonstrated 
for the first time by taking cobalt oxide aerogels as an example. Cobalt oxide aerogels of 
excellent supercapacitive properties, high specific capacitances (the highest ever reported 
for cobalt oxides, > 600 F/g at a high mass loading of 1 mg/cm2) and onset frequencies, and 
excellent reversibility and cycle stability, were successfully synthesized with the epoxide 
addition procedure for the first time by using cobalt nitrate as the precursor. The 
outstanding supercapacitive properties of the products were attributed to the beneficial 
mesoporous structures of the aerogels. Furthermore, cobalt oxide is a promising low cost 
transition metal oxide for supercapacitors of the asymmetric type, and the epoxide synthetic 
route enables preparation of transition metal oxide aerogels from low cost and stable metal 
salts instead of the much more expensive, moisture and heat sensitive alkoxides. The 
development of the present work makes possible the low cost production of high 
performance supercapacitors of the asymmetric type. 
 
KEYWORDS: cobalt oxide, aerogel, supercapacitor, epoxide synthetic route

12 hrs. The wet gel was then washed with ethanol several times to exchange the methanol 
and by-products from the pores of the wet gel. The wet gel was then dried with a 
supercritical dryer (SAMDRI-795, Tousimis, USA). The sample was first immersed in 
liquid carbon dioxide for 5 hrs. Then, the temperature of the dryer was raised to 45oC with 
the pressure maintained at 1200 psi for 2 hrs. After that, the system was depressurized at a 
slow rate to ambient pressure to obtain the dried aerogel. The dried aerogels were cobalt 
hydroxide in composition and need to be further calcined at a temperature equal to or 
higher than 200 oC for 5 hrs to convert to cobalt oxide. Note that, if CoCl2．6H2O (0.38 g 
1.6 mmole) was taken as the precursor, precipitates formed within 6 min after the addition 
of propylene oxide. The porous cobalt oxide containing both micropores and mesopores 
was prepared by using MCM-41 powders as the template. An amount of 0.2 g of MCM-41 
powder was dispersed in 2 ml of a 0.8 M Co(NO3)2．6H2O/EtOH solution and stirred at 
room temperature for 1 hr. EtOH was removed by heating the solution to 80 oC for 24 hrs. 
After the EtOH removal, the sample was calcined at 200 oC for 5 hrs to obtain the porous 
cobalt oxide. The MCM-41 template was removed by immersion in 1M NaOH for 1 day, 
followed by DI water rinsing. Finally, the rinsed sample was dried at 60 oC for 1 day. 
Characterizations.  
The pH values of the solution were measured with a pH meter (TS-100, Suntex). The 
XRD patterns of the samples were recorded with an X-ray powder diffractometer (CuKα, 
Ultima IV, Rigaku). The samples were ground into fine powders for the measurements at a 
scan rate of 2o/min. The grain sizes of the cobalt oxides were determined with the Scherrer 
equation. The BET specific surface areas and average BJH pore sizes were obtained from 
the N2 adsorption/desorption analyses at 77K (NOVA e1000, Quantachrome). The BET 
specific surface areas were calculated from the adsorption isotherms at the relative pressure 
range of 0.1 to 0.3, and the BJH pore sizes were determined from the desorption isotherms. 
The thermogravimetric analysis was run from room temperature to 500 oC at a heating rate 
of 5 oC/min (TGA951, Dupont instruments).  
Cyclic voltammetric measurements (CHI660C, CH Instruments Inc.) were conducted 
in a three-electrode system in a 1M NaOH solution. One milligram of the sample was 
coated on a graphite electrode as the working electrode. The reference electrode and 
counter electrode were Ag/AgCl and platinum, respectively. The potential was scanned 
between -0.1 and 0.55V with variable sweep rates. An impedance spectrum analyzer, IM6 
(ZAHNER), equipped with Thales software was employed to measure and analyze the 
impedance spectra. The potential amplitude of the ac is equal to 10 mV and the frequency 
range was from 0.1 to 10,000 Hz. 
Results and Discussion 
The gel formation mechanism of the epoxide synthetic route has been intensively 
studied.20-26 Briefly, epoxides act as a proton scavenger to deprotonate hydrated metal ions, 
which can then proceed with further hydrolysis and condensation to form gels under weak 
acidic conditions. The protonated epoxide undergoes ring opening, by attacks from 
neucleophiles such as water, nitrate, and chloride. Protons may be formed at the ring-
opening step, depending on the kind of neucleophile present. To favor gel formation, slow 
and uniform increase of the solution pH to weak acidic conditions is necessary.20-26 In this 
work, two different cobalt salts, nitrate and chloride, were tested with five different polar

The pore size distributions of sample PCO-micro were also displayed in Fig. 1(d). 
Because of the co-existence of micropores and mesopores, we plotted the pore size

Both pore size distributions are single modal with the mesopores centering around 4.8 nm 
and micropores centering around 1.7 nm.  
    To evaluate the potential of the present products for supercapacitor applications, 
the specific capacitances of the samples were determined from corresponding cyclic 
voltammograms (CV). The cyclic voltammograms were conducted in a three-electrode 
system. The samples were coated on a graphite substrate as the working electrode. The 
reference electrode and counter electrode were Ag/AgCl and platinum, respectively. The 
CV curves of samples A-200, A-300, A-400, and PCO-micro are shown in Fig. 2(a). The 
potential range spanned from -0.1 to 0.55V, and the sweep rate was set at 25 mV/s. All CV 
curves show an anodic peak at around 0.47V and a cathodic peak at about 0.4V. These two 
peaks indicate the redox couple of Co3O4/CoOOH. All CV curves appeared quite 
symmetric, indicating high reversibility of the redox reaction on all oxide surfaces. The 
corresponding specific capacitances of the samples were calculated from the CV curves and 
tabulated in Table 1. Evidently, for the aerogel samples, the specific capacitance decreased 
with increasing the calcination temperature, consistent with the trend of the BET specific 
surface area. If one examines the two sets of data carefully, the correlation is quite strong, 
indicating the dominant influence of the specific surface area on specific capacitances.  
The specific capacitance of 623 F/g of sample A-200 is much higher than those 
achieved by other mesoporous cobalt oxides, such as the loose-packed cobalt oxide 
nanocrystals3 and the cobalt oxide xerogels.1 More importantly, the sample loading of 1 
mg/cm2 used in the present work is much higher than the common practice of the field, 
often around 0.1 mg/cm2. Higher sample loadings increase the sample thickness when 
coated on the substrate, thus increasing the difficulty for the diffusing electrolytes to access 
the internal pore surfaces of the porous electrode and increasing the pathway distances that 
the electrons need to travel. Consequently, higher sample loadings often lead to lower 
specific capacitances. The present product nevertheless still achieves high specific 
capacitances at high loading conditions, indicating its strong applicability. Also note that 
the present specific capacitances were reported at a relatively high sweep rate of 25 mV/s, 
much higher than the common sweep rate of 5 mV/s. Furthermore, the voltammograms 
recorded from our samples are much more symmetric than those from Refs. 1a and 1c, 
indicating much better reversibility, one of the critical characteristics for supercapacitors. 
As to the specific capacitance of sample PCO-micro, the value was much lower than those 
of the aerogel samples, mainly because of the low specific surface area. The existence of 
micropores in sample PCO-micro, contributing half of the specific surface area, is also part 
of the reason for the low specific capacitance.
