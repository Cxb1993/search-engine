二、 計畫架構： 
為因應未來大量電動車電能補充需求，本計畫將進行配套措施探討，研擬考量電動車電
能補充之電網供電架構、界面及法令規範等相關配套措施。藉由配電系統模擬軟體建構衝擊
評估環境，以期能符合未來實際狀況，精確有效地分析 G2V 及 V2G 的衝擊與效益。未來電動
車電能補充系統可配合智慧電表輔助，對電動車充電站進行電能供給品質監測，本計畫將發
展適當的通訊架構對充電站智慧型變壓器及充電機進行管理，達成充電機之互相協調。本計
畫亦將進一步考量需量反應及時間電價機制，對充電站進行最佳電能管理。圖 1 所示為本計
畫所構思之智慧電網結合智慧電動車管理的可能架構。本計畫將利用資通訊技術開發以智慧
電表輔助之電動車充電站電能管理，透過智慧電表與所開發之系統，即時地對充電站之供電
變壓器進行管理，並與充電站之各充電機相互協調，在考量需量反應及時間電價等可能功能
下，對充電站進行最佳管理。為對充電站進行電能管理並維持供電品質，本計畫亦將設計智
慧型車載充電機及充電柱，以達到四象限實虛功控制、功率因數調整及諧波抑制等功能。本
整合型計畫的主要研究項目包括： 
(A)電動車電能補充對電網之衝擊分析 
(B)充電站管理及通訊架構研擬 
(C)充電機設計研發

2. 充電機電能管理策略設計之主要目標為開發一適用於充電機之電能管理策略，並據以進

式控制之功能，及過電壓、限電流及過溫度保護功能。為了與充電站能源管理系統進行
協調與溝通，本研究項目亦將針對使用 ZigBee 技術作為通訊媒介之可能性與電路設計
進行研究。此外，為了提高所開發充電策略的便利性，本研究項目亦擬針對人機介面進
行開發，並提供數種不同充電模式。最後，擬針對電池充電技術進行研究，探討不同充
電剩餘容量(State of Charge, SOC)與不同電池動態阻抗時，充電電流線上自動調整之
解決方案。

計模擬平台，利用此平台開發智慧型的電動車充電管理技術與管理程式，其可透過電
力調整，達到最大的平均充電狀態，並模擬和評估整體效能。另外，透過無線通訊方

交換站)。

玖、 檢討與展望 
(一) 由充電站實測資料可明顯看出，電壓不平衡率的大小隨著各相充電電流的增加而增加，
即採用單相慢充充電機之充電站確實會影響系統三相平衡。未來若大量建置這類型充電
站於台電低壓配電系統，三相電壓不平衡問題將會更加嚴重。未來充電站普設時，充電
設施大量使用對電網影響評估，除安全考量因子外，諧波抑制、三相電壓平衡等電力品
質技術問題亦需顧及，以兼顧國家能源政策發展目標及供電安全與品質。在電動巴士量
測方面，考慮未來電動巴士的營運成本，可能會在某段時段內集中充電，對配電系統之
衝擊將會產生嚴重影響，其程度與充電電氣標準與併接點位置與電動巴士數量習習相關，
如何充分運用再生能源資源，配合儲能裝置及先進控制技術，有效管理電動巴士充電站
電能補充將是未來重要的研究課題之一。 
(二) 本計畫已提出確定性參數模式，與隨機參數模式，分析電動車對於配電系統衝擊影響，
並且考慮控制型，及不控制型的充電模式。測試結果顯示，確定因素的模式分析中，以
峰值負載曲線模擬時，可以反應系統可能出現的低電壓、過電流的問題，但無法反應其
發生的頻率，也無法反應系統的平均損失、平均電壓降等指標，若以平均負載曲線模擬
時，可以反應系統平均損失、平均電壓降等指標，但無法反應系統可能出現的低電壓、
過電流的問題。本計畫所提出之隨機模式，可以反應系統平均損失、平均電壓降等指標，
亦可以反應系統可能出現的低電壓、過電流的問題，及其發生的頻率。此外測試結果顯
示，Controlled Charging 可以有效提高電動車的 Penetration Rate。目前已經在整理充電
站的實測資料，將利用這些資料，建立以實測資料為基礎之電動車衝擊分析參數之模
型。 
(三) 以往戶外充電站通常設置在街上或商場的停車場，以及車道旁停車位和現有的加油站。
本計畫針對戶外充電站進行最適配置規劃分析，期望藉由策略性配置充電機來降低配
電系統衝擊。本計畫以台中州廳停車場充電站電能補充實測資料為基礎，建立逐時隨
機充電矩陣，配合季節性逐時負載變動資料，以隨機三相電力潮流計算及人工智慧搜
尋技術，推導慢充充電站最適配置策略。分析結果顯示透過策略性配置充電站，可有
效降低電動車對配電系統之衝擊。未來可對既有充電站裝設位置研究如何透過充電排
程管理以降低對系統之衝擊。 
(四) 本計畫針對住商工不同用戶類型日負載需求搭配電動車電能補給時間，以饋線重組達
到最佳化系統損失及開關切換成本，分析結果証實有效的規劃現有系統之連絡開關位
置，可減緩電動車對配電系統之衝擊。未來可進行充電站及配電系統之整合型規劃。

國科會補助專題研究計畫項下出席國際學術會議 
心得報告(PEDG 2012) 
                                     
日期：101 年 7 月 15 日 
 第三屆電力電子與分散式發電系統研討會(IEEE 2012 The 3rd International
 Symposium on Power Electronics for Distributed Generation Systems)由丹麥
阿爾堡(Aalborg)大學主辦。大會論文審查嚴謹，思慮周全，規劃完善。雖然地
點遙遠，飽受長途飛行與轉機轉車之苦，但實不虛此行。 
在哥本哈根卡斯楚機場，就深深感受到北歐的設計風格，處處充滿設計感。
尤其是，第一航站的雪白通道，鋪天蓋地，迎面而來，令人驚艷。從阿爾堡回程
轉機時，又特地繞道穿過，再次體驗丹麥風情。 
丹麥人熱心、親切、開朗，有別於俄勒海峽對岸的瑞典人的冰冷，常常未開
口，先有笑容。有人說，全世界的都會人都很冷漠，東西方皆然，但北歐第一大
城的哥本哈根人卻不是，跟阿爾堡人一樣親切。路上，只要你拿著地圖，或是在
路口觀望，就會有人主動上來幫你。火車誤點，或者換月台，都會有人主動告
知。但是，這並不表示在丹麥旅遊很安全。從卡斯楚機場開往哥本哈根的火車
上，背包就差點被搶。但是，那幾個劫匪看起來像南歐的希臘人或土耳其人，更
可能是義大利人。 
丹麥的物價實在嚇人，高過於荷蘭瑞典。旅館貴，餐廳貴，市區公車一上車
就要 20 克朗，超過 100 新台幣。可是我們生活費的補助標準似乎沒有反映這個
事實。 
丹麥人愛騎腳踏車，在哥本哈根可免費租用，可是時間稍晚就借不到了。他

Abstract—The aircraft obstacle lights on the power transmission 
towers, which are located at the places without the utility mains, 
are powered mainly by solar cells and subordinately by 
rechargeable batteries. The power supply with solar cells and 
batteries are configured and coordinated according to the 
weather conditions. The designed power capacities are to keep 
the aircraft obstacle lighting system uninterruptable either under 
the drastic weather variation or a long period of insufficient 
sunlight. An exemplar case is planned on the basis of the local 
weather data in recent 6 years. A more economical and precise 
planning is made for the capacities of solar cells and batteries. 
The design has demonstrated, through simulations, the feasibility 
and the accuracy in reality. 
Keywords-aircraft obstacle light; solar cell; battery 
I.  INTRODUCTION  
Flight safety is a serious issue since pilots’ lives and 
aircrafts may be destroyed immediately once a mistake 
happened during the flights. Although the bad weather is a 
major factor, brightly eye-catching warning sign or lighting can 
effectively prevent the accidents from happening. To ensure 
the flight safety during low visibility and low illumination, 
aircraft obstacle lightings are used to mark the objects on the 
ground, not only to alert the pilots but also to protect 
transmission towers and power system from damage. 
Since most transmission towers are located on the suburb or 
in the mountain area, where the utility mains are hard to reach 
for powering the aircraft obstacle lighting. In this case, the 
most convenient solution is the use of photovoltaic power 
generated by solar cells [1]. However, the photovoltaic power, 
which is dependent mainly on the intensity of solar radiation, is 
inconstant and unpredictable. Therefore, a number of 
rechargeable batteries are essential to sustain the required 
power for aircraft obstacle lighting when photovoltaic power is 
not available. The batteries store the photovoltaic power during 
sunny daytime, and then discharge to power the aircraft 
obstacle lighting during night time or the daytime with 
insufficient sunlight. 
Nevertheless, the photovoltaic power produced by solar 
cells depends on the sun radiation which is different from not 
only season to season but also, and more critically, region to 
region. For this reason, it is hard to specify a capacity of the 
photovoltaic power that is reasonable and economical. This is 
also true for the subordinate power source, batteries. 
This research attempts to set up an effectively operated 
aircraft obstacle lighting system with more reasonable designs 
of the power supplies with solar cells and the batteries in 
accordance with the regional weather profile in recent 6 years. 
An exemplar design for the area of Kaohsiung, Taiwan, is 
given to illustrate the power planning of the system. 
II. SYSTEM CONFIGURATION 
The configuration of the obstacle lighting system is shown 
in Fig. 1. It is a stand-alone photovoltaic power system which 
is not connected to the utility mains [2-5]. A light emitting 
diode (LED) lamp is adopted as the aircraft obstacle lighting. A 
photo sensor is used to detect how much sunlight is available, 
and accordingly to switch on or off the aircraft obstacle light. 
Based on the regulation of Taiwan Civil Aeronautics 
Administration, the obstacle lights have to be turned on to warn 
the aircraft pilots when the solar radiation density is less than 
500 cd/m
2
, which can cause blurry vision to human eyes [6]. 
The power for the obstacle lighting is mainly from solar 
cells and subordinately from rechargeable batteries. A solar 
panel assembly formed by solar cells converts the energy from 
sunlight into electricity. The generated electrical power is in 
turn delivered to the LED lamp, or stored in a battery bank 
consisting of a number of batteries connected in series or 
parallel according to the load requirements. The power 
conversion circuit can perform the function of maximum power 
point tracking (MPPT) as well as coordinate the power flowing 
in and out among the solar cells, batteries and the load [7-10]. 
During the daytime with plenty sunlight, the batteries store 
the energy generated from solar cells since the LED lamp does 
not consume power. When sunlight is insufficient and the 
obstacle lamp has to be turned on, batteries and solar cells 
supply the obstacle lighting simultaneously. The batteries have 
to cope with the required power during the night and under the 
bad weather without sunlight.

The power produced by solar cells depends mainly on the 
intensity of solar radiation, which is different from not only 
season to season but also, more critically, region to region. 
For this reason, the capacities of the solar cells and the 
batteries have to be designed in accordance with the regional 
weather profile. 
In this research, the weather profile of Kaohsiung, Taiwan 
is studied as an example. Fig. 4 shows the variations of annual 
solar energy densities, which are converted from the weather 
data of 2005 to 2010 obtained from Taiwan Central Weather 
Bureau [9]. 
The solar radiation is constantly minor on the winter days 
of January and December, but is diverse year by year. Drastic 
changes occur always during the typhoon season in summer. A 
dramatic weather took place in 2007. The highest solar 
radiation in the 5-year period happened in the mid June of this 
year. The solar radiation remained high through July to early 
August, but then decreased drastically since it had been 
continuously rainy for 9 days. 
V. BATTERY CAPACITY 
When it is continuously rainy for several days, only little 
solar power is generated or even worse is entirely not available. 
To cope with a period when solar power is unavailable, the 
minimum battery capacity of the aircraft obstacle lighting 
system is designed as 
                                QBAT(min)=IL H D α                               (1) 
Where IL is the rated current of the aircraft obstacle lamp, H is 
the daily operating hours, D represents the continuous days 
without solar power, and α is the security coefficient 
considering aging and self-discharging of the battery. 
According to the specifications given by Tai-power 
Company, the minimum battery capacity has to sustain the 
system operation of the obstacle lighting for at least 5 days 
without sunlight. 
In the design case, a 16 W LED lamp is used as the aircraft 
obstacle light. The battery set is rated at 24V, and the security 
coefficient α is chosen as 1.25. Then, the minimum battery 
capacity QBAT(min) can be calculated as 
               QBAT(min)=(16 24) 24 5 1.25＝100 (Ah)             (2) 
It should be noted that the battery capacity obtained from (2) is 
based on the assumption that the battery has been fully charged 
at the beginning. This means that such a battery can success 
fully deal with 5 continuous days without sunlight only when 
the battery has been fully charged by high solar energy 
densities. 
In practice, the battery SOC can be uncertain, depending 
on the previous weather and the capacity of the solar cell. The 
system may be operated with insufficient sunlight for a long 
term. The battery SOC will decrease by degrees day by day, as 
illustrated in Fig. 3, and will finally decrease to zero.

VIII. CONCLUSIONS 
The planning of the power supply for the aircraft obstacle 
light has been proposed in this paper. The capacities of the 
solar cell and the battery are designed based on the solar 
energy density which is converted from the local sun radiation. 
The investigation results indicate that increasing the 
capacity of solar power is helpful for raising the minimum 
battery residue capacity, but has a less effect for 
supplementing the short-term insufficient sunlight caused by 
drastic weather change. An exemplar design is given for the 
area of Kaohsiung Taiwan. Such a design can only be applied 
to the specified area with the similar weather profile.

locally. Most importantly, the sum of the power of all the
branches should not exceed the available power for the main
line. This development is particularly beneficial to a complex
power network with multi-layer hierarchical power constraints.

In using the proposed procedure, the tie switches selection 
criterion is applied to make the feeders operate in a radial 
structure each hour. In the criterion, with all tie switches closed 
temporarily, the distribution network becomes a mesh grid and 
the load flow analysis is used to evaluate the branch currents. 
The tie switch on the feeder section with minimum branch 
current is selected to open to make a new network structure. 
And then, it is tested to investigate the connectivity and, if it is 
feasible, the load flow analysis is performed for the new 
network; else, abandon it. And also, if any constraint violation 
is detected, the new network structure is also considered to be 
infeasible and abandoned. Once the new network structure is 
abandoned, the branch ranked next is selected and the above 
process to detect the feasibility of a new network structure is 
repeated until the distribution system is detected to be a feasible 
radial structure.

IV. TEST RESULTS 
A simple test system with three feeders is shown in Figure 2. 
The nominal voltage is 11.4 kV, the current rating of each 
feeder is 600 A and the minimum voltage magnitude of each 
node is 0.95 p.u.. Daily mean loads of all nodes are shown in 
Table I [8], in which the mean loads are represented as the 
ratios of the corresponding load values to their normal values [5] 
respectively. The standard deviation of each node’s load is set 
to 3% of the mean node’s load. Under the base state, the tie 
switches on feeder sections S5, S11 and S16 are opened.

The probability distribution of the STC made by EPRI is 
shown in Figure 3 [4]. To facilitate the STC sampling for an EV, 
the probability distribution of the STC is converted to the 
uniform distribution [0, 1], as shown in Figure 4. For example, 
if for an EV the randomly sampled number is 0.5, as can be 
seen in Figure 4 that the start charging time of the EV is at hour 
19. In addition, the probability distribution of LCT which was 
tie switches at hour 1, 11 and 12 are identical, which are S7, S9 
and S4. Also, under the original network structure, the branch 
currents at hour 12, 18, 19, 20 and 21 are 620.4 A, 621.9 A, 
661.6 A, 655.9 A and 629.8 A, respectively, all of which violate 
the branch current limit (600 A). After performing the proposed 
method, the currents on these branches are reduced to 550.6 A, 
554.3A, 590.7 A, 585.0 A and 562.7 A respectively and the 
minimum voltage magnitude is improved to 0.960 p.u. 
corresponding to 0.957 p.u. under the original network 
configuration. Obviously, the performance of the proposed 
method is validated with these comparisons.    
Table III shows the results derived from solving the problem 
for 30 times with and without performing the proposed network 
reconfiguration criterion. The average system loss is 15435.2 
kW under the original network structure while the average total 
loss is reduced to be 14106.2 kW under the network structure 
made by the proposed feeder reconfiguration criterion. In 
addition, the average number of branch current violations is 5.5 
under the original network structure while it is 0.4 under the 
radial feeders determined by the proposed criterion. 
Accordingly, the performance of the proposed method is 
validated with less system loss and much smaller number of 
security constraint violations. 
V. CONCLUSION 
In the paper, the problem to minimize the loss of the 
distribution system with EVs by feeder switching controls is 
formulated as a discrete nonlinear optimization problem. The 
daily load value of EVs charging is sampled form the 
probability distributions of STC and LCT. The demands of EVs 
charging can increase the load and, especially, the evening load 
of the distribution system. Simulation results obtained from this 
study have demonstrated that, with the network reconfiguration 
criterion, the proposed loss minimization procedure can 
effectively reduce the system loss and branch currents, and

技術說明
(中文) 反流器為主的分散式發電由孤島運轉變為市電併聯時，若偵測到反流器輸出電流
超過限制，則反當以反流器為主的分散式發電由孤島運轉變為市電併聯運轉時，
若偵測到反流器輸出電流超過預先設定好之電流限制，則反流器控制策略會由下
降控制模式轉入過渡模式，於過度模式中，反流器會先將輸出電流在一段預先設
定好之時間內控制為零，於此同時，反流器會啟動鎖相迴路更新下降控制器之相
位，過了這段預先設定好之時間之後，反流器控制會轉變回下降控制模式，同時
並啟動動態虛擬電感以抑制控制模式轉變瞬間因些許的相位誤差造成的暫態環流。
(英文) If the inverter output current exceed the limitation when the inverter based distributed 
generation (DG) switched to grid-connected operation from islanding operation. The 
control algorithm changes from droop-control mode to riding-through mode. In the 
riding-through mode, the inverter will control the output current as zero for a specific 
time delay. At the same time, the phase-lock loop(PLL) will update the phase angle of the 
droop controller in the time delay. After the time delay, the inverter switched back to the 
droop-control mode to output rated power, at the same time, the virtual inductance was 
also engaged to suppress the transient current.

一.經濟效益(產業經濟發展)  
1.透過電動車電能補充量測系統，以最經濟且有效方式，允許電動車示範運行
推動辦公室、使用單位、車廠及電力公司掌握電動車充電行為及電力運轉資料，
建立提高先導運行專案之提出，藉由電動車選取，來降低因為大量電動車導入，
對於電力系統的衝擊，本計畫所提出的電動車充電管理架構及通訊架構具專利
價值。 
2.與高雄客運進行產學合作，協助高雄客運進行電動巴士充電站建置與用電品
質監測，並依據量測資料，與客運公司之行車計劃，提出契約容量規劃，以節
省電能支出費用。 
3.與鎰福電子合作項目確認，以併網控制（孤島偵測、功率潮流控制）與低電
壓渡過控制（電流控制、功率控制），合計 4項技術，透過產學合作與技術移轉
方式進行細部洽談與合作內容，協助業界發展充電椿與相關測試平台實驗室，
預計於明年進行。 
4.完成工研院綠能所勞務採購案簽約，購案名稱：三階層中性點箝位脈波調變
器研製，計畫內容為交直流電力轉換器電路製作與脈波寬度調變器控制策略分
析。 
5.訪談電動車推動辦公室、高雄市政府交通局、高雄市政府經濟發展局、高雄
市政府環境保護局等單位，討論電動車充電站設置之法規與建議。預計將訪談
結果納入高雄市政府充電站設置相關法規建議研究報告中。 
6.與台達電子洽談合作，並預計明年度合作建置電動車充電站示範場。 
7.完成充電站能源管理系統及數位控制充電機之架構，並提出社區型電動車充
電管理技術，同時也提出可實現此管理技術的通訊架構，有助電動車產業發展。
所提出的社區型電動車充電管理及通訊架構，已初步藉由小型模型驗證其可行
性，具專利價值。 
二.非研究類成就(人才培育、法規制度、國際合作、推動輔導) 
1.歸納及分析電動車充電站之相關規範，有助電力公司及充電站產業業者對各
國和國際組織之規範和標準的了解，讓充電站產業有依循之規範和標準。 
2.參考美國能源署贊助電力公司發表之「電動汽車充電基礎設施的部署指南」，
彙整充電站設置之考慮因素和設置步驟，可做為建立台灣電動汽車充電站設置
規範之參考。 
3.建議家庭式充電機、小型、大型和重要型充電站之供電架構。 
4.拜訪 Quanta Technology Inc, PG&E 及 SDG&E 討論 AMI 及電動車管理有關議
題，有助國際合作。 
5.藉由充電站電能補充的實場量測，培育相關人才並提供政府有關單位及電力
公司電動車電能補充資訊，瞭解電動車充電行為及其問題，從中獲取經驗，加
速擴大推廣。 
三.其它效益(科技政策管理及其它) 
本計畫研提充電站設置相關法規建議，可供未來推廣電動車充電站管理的參
考。對目前政府補助之電動車示範運行系統進行量測，建立完整之充電站電力
品質資料庫，對於台電評估充電站對配電系統的影響具參考價值。
