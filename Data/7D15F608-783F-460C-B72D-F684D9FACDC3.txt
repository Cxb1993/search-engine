摘要：氧化鋅奈米線（ZnO-NWs）一維結構具
有高光電感應效果，具有開發成高感應度、低製
作成本、使用方便紫外線感元件之優勢，本計畫
提出以 ZnO-based 奈米結構研製新穎之紫外線感
測器，使紫外線造成傷害的預警溶入生活當中，
使人們可以即時清楚知道其所在環境之紫外線強
度而能做適當防護，而使紫外線造成身體的傷害
降到最低。 
本計畫開發低成本紫外線感測元件之元件
設計及製程技術，研製攜帶方便或安裝容易之紫
外線感測器。本計劃執行「採用 ITO 玻璃基板結
合氧化鋅奈米結構研製高效率之紫外線感測元
件」，研發重點為(1)低成本氧化鋅奈米線/奈米柱
陣列成長技術之研發，整合以水熱法成長
(hydro-thermal growth，HTG) ZnO 奈米線合成長
於 ITO 玻璃基板，(2)應用 ZnO-NWs 於 P 型之金
屬氧化物/n-ZnO 異質接面奈米結構之研製，藉以
提升 1-D 光電量子效應製備與特性分析。 
綜合言之，本計劃提出以 ITO 玻璃基板結合
HTG 成長 ZnO 奈米線研製紫外線感測元件，以提
供人們對紫外線造成傷害的預警溶入生活當中，
使人們可以即時清楚知道其所在環境之紫外線強

Abstract： Due to 1-D quantum effect of Zinc oxide 
(ZnO) nanowires (NWS) hetero structure with high 
photoelectric response, it provides the availability of 
developing high sensitivity, low cost, and wearable 
devices. This project proposed the use of a 
ZnO-NW-based heterojunction structure for 
developing of a novel ultraviolet (UV) sensor on 
ITO glass substrate, which aimed at the design and 
fabrication of low-cost, high sensitivity, and easily 
use sensor. 
The major targets in research activities are:  
(a) The development of low cost technologies for 
synthesizing ZnO-NWs: by using hydrothermal 
growth (HTG) method to produce ZnO-NWs on 
ITO glass substrate. The key techniques 
including methods of concisely controlling 
diameter, length, and density of ZnO-NWs has 
been developed. 
(b) The use of a ZnO-NW-based heterojunction 
structure to promote the efficiency of 1-D 
quantum：by employing p-type metallic oxide 
integrated with  ZnO-NWs, a high 
performance 1-D nano heterojunction structure 
was developed. p-type metallic oxide NiO was 
well surveyed to form high efficiency 1-D nano 
heterojunction structure. 
The UV sensor with ZnO-NW-based hetero 
structure has been developed on ITO glass substrate 
by conjugating all the accomplishments of each 
technique developed in this project proposal. With 
the above-mentioned technologies developed in this 
project, a high sensitivity UV sensor was designed 
and fabricated. It is highly expected that the 
developed technologies would provide people 
forecasting about UV harm.

標題目為"A Novel Method of Developing ZnO-based Nano Hetero Structure on 
Flexible Substrate for UV Photo Detecting Application"。主要是講述一種兩階段水
熱法(hydro-thermal growth, HTG)成長氧化鋅奈米線（Zinc Oxide Nano Wires, 
ZnO-NWs）結合 p型半導體特性之氧化鎳（Nickel Oxide, NiO）形成 ZnO-基異
質奈米接面結構（Nano Hetero Junction Structure, NHJS），因 NHJS之一維量子效
應對於紫外線有良好反應，此一 NHJS 正可以應用於紫外線之感測。一般 HTG
成長 NWs 需有成核晶種層，本論文所提兩階段 HTG 則可以完全克服晶種層問
題，直接於 p-NiO薄膜上成長 NWs，而產生 p-NiO/n- ZnO-NWs之 ZnO-基異質
奈米接面結構。所發表論文另一部份為 PET 軟性基板熱重朔製程。PET 為塑膠
材質，質軟容易熱脹冷縮，熱重朔製程成功提升 PET 軟性基板平整度及熱穩定
度。本研究團體成功將 p-NiO/n- ZnO-NWs之 ZnO-基異質奈米接面結構研製在
PET軟性基板，此為軟性電子之一大進度，12月 19日參與大會舉辦之成都市區
旅遊。

III. RESULTS AND DISCUSSIONS 
The row PET substrate as known is curled, soft, and 
unstable with temperature increasing. Due to PET substrate 
expanding and shrinking with temperature change, the ZnO-
NWs/p-NiO structures, even the HTG conducted in 70－
90oC low temperature process, were found splitting as Fig. 
3(b) shown. Enhancing the flatness, toughness, and stability 
of PET substrate is indispensable. A thermosetting technique 
is developed in this work. The row PET substrates were first 
clipped by two pieces of flat glass substrates. The clipped 
PET substrates, shown as Fig. 2, were placed in 90°C hot 
water 30 minutes to be softened and shaped. Then the PET 
specimens were placed immediately into 15°C cold water 10 
minutes to enhance toughness.  Figure 3(a) shows the top 
view of SEM image of ZnO-NWs/p-NiO structures produced 
on PET substrate treated with thermosetting. Compared with 
that shown on Fig. 3(b), the ZnO-NWs/p-NiO structures is 
without any splitting found even PET substrate bent. This 
result indicates that PET substrate treated with thermosetting 
have gotten improvement in flatness, toughness, and stability. 
 
 
Figure 3. SEM images of HTG results, ZnO-NWs grown on PET substrate 
(a) with and (b) without thermosetting treatment. 
ZnO-NWs grown by HTG need some materials as seed 
layer[10-11]. For comparing the affect of seed layer, HTG 
was conducted on AZO and p-NiO film, respectively. As Fig. 
4 shown, it can be clearly found that on AZO film, part (A), 
HTG process can synthesize ZnO-NWs well but on p-NiO 
film, part (B), it only can produce tiny nano spots (NSPs). In 
this study, a 2-step HTG was developed which the NSPs 
were employed as pseudo seed layer. First, p-NiO film 
deposited PET substrates were placed in a mix solution of 
low consistency Zinc nitrate hexahydrate (Zn(NO3)2⋅6H2O) 
and high consistency HMT at 85°C 30 minutes to synthesize 
ZnO-NSPs. Second, samples were then washed in DI water 
and then baked on 90°C hot plate to solidify ZnO-NSPs. 
Finally, amples with ZnO-NSPs as pseudo seed layer were 
then conduced in another consistency solution for second 
step HTG.

氧化鋅奈米線（ZnO-NWs）一維結構具有高光電感應效果，具有開發成高感應度、低製作
成本、使用方便紫外線感元件之優勢，本計畫提出在 ITO 玻璃基板上以水熱法研製
ZnO-based 奈米結構紫外線感測器，使紫外線造成傷害的預警溶入生活當中，使人們可以
即時清楚知道其所在環境之紫外線強度而能做適當防護，而使紫外線造成身體的傷害降到
最低。 
綜合言之，本計劃執行完成 
1. 學術成就方面完成異質接面奈米結構研製，尤其在 p-NiO 薄膜上利用無晶種層水熱法
成長 n-ZnO-NWs。 
2. 技術創新方面，傳統技術大多 n-ZnO-NWs 成長之後再批覆 p-NiO 薄膜，造成接面不完
整，或 NiO 墜落於 ZnO-NWs 間，欠缺完整 1-D 接面結構，產生光電感應特性不穩定及特性
重現性差之問題，本研究在水熱法技術上突破傳統研製完成無晶種層水熱法成長
n-ZnO-NWs 技術，可以在平整 p-NiO 薄膜上成長 n-ZnO-NWs，產生良好均勻之
p-NiO/n-ZnO-NWs 異質接面奈米結構。 
3. 本計畫參與人員在水熱法成長 ZnO-NWs 及 p-NiO 薄膜研製技術上獲得良好技術訓練，
在參數粹取及製程規劃均經歷完整訓練，對將來投入產業界助益斐然。 
4. 本計畫成功研製良好之 p-NiO/n-ZnO-NWs 異質接面奈米結構，證實此結構對紫外線確
實有敏銳之光電感應，因其具有低溫製程之特性，本計畫將繼續努力將此異質接面奈米結
構研致在軟片基板上，使得 UV 感測器得以具有方便攜帶及可以安置於各種扭曲表面。
