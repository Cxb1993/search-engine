Abstract 
In this project, we established the 
simulation model combining molecular 
dynamics (MD) with FEM for copper nano 
thin film. MD was used to simulate the atomic 
displacement and equivalent strain for the 
proportional tensile and compressive tests 
under the conditions of the size 
of 000 a6a6a14 ×× , 2016 atoms, the tensile 
and compressive rates of 0.001/step and 
-0.001/step, and the system temperature of 
293K and then equivalent stress could be 
calculated by using atomistic stress formula. 
Next, flow stress-strain curve was plotted. 
Based on shape function, the equivalent strain 
of atom was obtained and the equivalent stress 
was then calculated by 
th3  order stress-strain 
polynomial regressive equation. The results 
showed that for proportional tensile simulation: 
(1)when being zero strain, the stress is 
-0.7952GPa for the truncated radius ( cr ) of 2.5 
times equilibrium radius ( 0r ), that is, 
pre-stress exists exactly. Due to proportional 
increase of distance between atoms, the stress 
and strain distributions increase gradually
