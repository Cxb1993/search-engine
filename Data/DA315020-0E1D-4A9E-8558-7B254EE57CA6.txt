2一、中文摘要
近年來，非侵入式拉曼光譜與螢光光
譜被視為一前瞻性醫學診斷技術。螢光光
譜和拉曼光譜可診斷人體組織生化組成
與組織結構，可提供組織變異相關資訊，
作為早期病變組織診斷工具。
本研究目標建立子宮頸離體正常組
織與異常組織臨床診斷系統。我們成功
的建立高解析度拉曼光譜、螢光光譜系
統、積分球系統量測組織各項光學特
性，並結合蒙地卡羅組織光學模擬方
法，探討組織內光學特性表現。
研究結果發現我們可以利用拉曼光
譜的 1250cm-、1585 cm-1 、1602 cm-1 和
1656 cm-1 波數進行子宮頸正常組織與癌
病組織診斷，診斷率達到 80%以上，我
們也利用蒙地卡羅演算法模擬以不同入
射角度照射子宮頸組織的螢光光譜，可
以把早期癌病變與正常組織分離出來。
配合我們所開發的多變數分析系統，找
出不同病變組織光譜特徵差異性，提供
組織內組成的診斷資訊，作為早期癌組
織輔助診斷的利器。
關鍵詞：螢光光譜、拉曼光譜、Monte carlo
光學模擬、子宮頸組織
Abstract
Recently, non-invasive Raman and
fluorescence spectroscopic techniques have
been identified as powerful techniques in
medical diagnosis. Raman and fluorescence
techniques provide information about
biochemical composition and molecular
fingerprints of dyplasia tissues and can serve as
precancer diagnostic tools. The purpose of this
study is to develop a noninvasive diagnostic
system for cervical dysplasia tissue screening.
We have successfully established high
resolution Raman spectra system, fluorescence
spectra system, and integrated sphere system to
measure the optical characterizations of
various tissues at different dysplasia stages. We
have also developed the Monte Carlo
autofluorescent simulation to predict the
emission of autofluorescent signals from
different dyplasia stages under different
excitation conditions .The results indicate that
we can utilize Raman signals at 1250, 1585,
and 1656cm-1 to distinguish dysplasia tissues
from normal tissue, and its diagnostic accuracy
is higher than 80%. Furthermore, we have
used Monte carlo simulation to simulate
fluorescence spectra at different incident
angles. With the application of PLS analytic
method developed in this study, we have
successfully differentiated precancer tissues
from normal tissues using Raman and
fluorescence spectroscopies; they can serve as
assisted precancer diagnostic tools for screening
applications.
Keywords: Monte Carlo, auotfluorescence ,
Raman, cervical tissue
二、緣由與目的
在臨床醫學的診斷上，早期診斷技術
的發展與非侵入式/低侵入式的診斷技術
間，有著密不分的關係；非侵入式技術的
發展，對於早期診斷的推廣以及病人福祉
的照顧，都極為重要。近年來光電技術的
快速成長，其在醫學上的應用與投入也越
來越廣泛而深入。以光電的技術，開發非
侵入式或低侵入式的醫學檢驗方法也被各
研究團隊列入優先研發的項目。
近年來，伴隨著生醫光譜學的發展，
反射光、吸收光、螢光以及拉曼散射光光
譜學的創新研究與其診斷分析能力快速發
展。以現代醫學來看，人類發生疾病主因
在於生化分子變異。因此我們建立一個有
效及穩定的診斷系統，不只單靠病理醫師
肉眼觀測細胞形態變化的方式，來進行正
常及異常細胞的檢驗；而根據這些光學方
式診斷細胞結構及化學成份的改變，有效
提供生物組織診斷的資訊[1]。

C.拉曼光譜研究
本研究使用的拉曼光譜儀系統為
Jobin Yvon LabRAM HR800，搭配 Olympus
BX-41 顯 微 鏡 系 統 ， 使 用
10X(N.A.=0.25) 、 50X(N.A.=0.75) 與
100X(N.A.=0.9)的物鏡，雷射激發光源為
632.8nm的氦氖雷射，以及共軛焦(confocal)
顯微裝置，具有取得共軛焦拉曼散射訊號
能力;使用 1800g/mm 的光柵，因此具有
1cm-1 的高解析度能力，及含有液態氮冷卻
CCD(charge couple device)系統，使用的軟
體是由 JY 公司所提供的 LabSpec 4.14 版，
儀器架構如圖三。

片，雷射光將被濾除，只有 Raman 散射光

以選擇具空間解析的 Raman 散射訊號，藉

號傳入電腦處理。
本實驗中相關子宮頸組織樣本，由台
北馬偕紀念醫院提供，主要來自16位病

D.組織樣本處理
我們的樣本切割方法如圖四示。經冷凍
切片，取中間 50um 的切片進行拉曼光譜量
測，量測樣本前後的 6um 切片進行染色，
經由病理醫師定義病變區域。在量測之前
一律置放在零下 20 度的低溫冰箱中保存，
並不添加任何外加藥劑，維持樣本的純淨。

Development of Infrared Thermal Imager for Dry Eye Diagnosis 
Huihua Kenny Chiang1,2, Chih Yen Chen1, Hung You Cheng1, Ko-Hua Chen3, David O. Chang4 
1Institute of Biomedical Engineering, National Yang-Ming University, Taipei, Taiwan 
2Department for Research and Education, Taipei City Hospital, Taipei, Taiwan

4United Integrated Services Co. Ltd., Taipei, Taiwan 
 
 
Abstract 
This study aims at the development of non-contact dry eye diagnosis based on an infrared thermal imager system, 
which was used to measure the cooling of the ocular surface temperature of normal and dry eye patients. A total of 108 
subjects were measured, including 26 normal and 82 dry eye patients. We have observed that the dry eye patients have 
a fast cooling of the ocular surface temperature than the normal control group. We have developed a simplified 
algorithm for calculating the temperature decay constant of the ocular surface for discriminating between normal and 
dry eye. This study shows the diagnostic of dry eye syndrome by the infrared thermal imager system has reached a 
sensitivity of 79.3%, a specificity of 75%, and the area under the ROC curve 0.841. The infrared thermal imager 
system has a great potential to be developed for dry eye screening with the advantages of non-contact, fast, and 
convenient implementation.  
Keywords: Dry eye, Infrared, Thermal imager, Tear film 
 
1. Introduction 
  Dry eye syndrome (Keratoconjunctivitis sicca) is one of the most common problems treated in ophthalmic outpatient 
clinic. This syndrome is usually caused by the tear film quality. The prevalence of the dry eye syndrome may be closely 
linked to the widely use of computer, contact lens, air conditioning, and air pollution problems. The common 
symptoms of dry eye patients involve the feelings of irritation, itching, burning, redness, blurred vision, excessive 
tearing, the sense of a foreign body, and the inflammation. 
  There are many pathological reasons causing the dry eye. According to the literatures, the dry eye syndrome is above 
10% in adults in developing countries; however, it can reach as high as 25% for elderly eyes. Elderly eyes tend to 
produce less oil; the deficiency in oil affects the tear film stability, and the tear film evaporate faster with less oil 
sealing. Some other diseases, including preocular and lacrimal gland disorder, thyroid conditions, hormone change, and 
vitamin deficiency may also cause ocular or lacrimal gland inflammation and lead to tear secretion with deficiency, 
non-uniform distribution, fast evaporation, and dry eye syndrome1-3.  
  The diagnosis of the dry eye syndrome is usually based on measuring the tear film production. In clinics, Schirmer 
test is the most widely used method. Although it can directly provide the volume of tear production over a fixed time

2.2 Physiology of Ocular temperature 
  Tear film is composed by three layers, the inner mucus layer, the middle aqueous layer, and the outer lipid layer. 
Figure.1 shows the structure of the tear film. The mucus layer coats the cornea to form a fundamental cover of the 
epithelium cells.  The middle aqueous layer provides nutrients and oxygen supply for the cornea. 98% of this layer is 
made of water. The outer lipid layer seals the tear film to prevent excessive evaporation. According to previous studies, 
an average of 40% tear film evaporates in the air. The evaporation cools down the ocular surface temperature. The 
difference in the ocular temperature decay may help discriminate between normal and dry eye patients8,9.
