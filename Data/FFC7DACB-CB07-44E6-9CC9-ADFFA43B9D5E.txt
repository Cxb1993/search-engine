綠色快速模具技術研發與大型件矽膠模具應用 
計畫編號：NSC 99－2628－E－131－001 
執行期限：99 年 08 月 01 日至 100 年 07 月 31 日 
             
 
中文摘要 
於快速模具應用中，矽膠模具為最常使
用技術，主要原因為矽膠模具具備良好的柔
軟性與彈性，能夠翻製出特徵結構複雜、花
紋精細、無拔模斜度以及具有深凹槽的零
件。然而，幾何形狀複雜矽膠模具製作技巧
與一般簡單外形矽膠模具製作技巧差異性很
大，主要的原因為矽膠模具分模面(parting 
plane)會影響矽膠模具製作成功率，當製作幾
何形狀複雜矽膠模具時，其分模面很難延用
一般簡單外形矽膠模具製作技巧。因此，於
矽膠模具製程中，如何提昇幾何形狀複雜大
型矽膠模具製作成功率即變成一個重要議
題。本研究以風扇前護網研究載具，提出適
合運用於幾何形狀複雜之大型矽膠模具製作
之技術，以作為未來製作幾何形狀複雜大型
矽膠模具之專門知識(know-how)。 
 
  
關鍵詞：快速模具、大型矽膠模具、綠色製
造 
 
Abstract 
Rapid tooling technology is regarded as an 
important method of reducing the cost and time 
to market in a new product development 
process. Silicone rubber mold is frequently 
employed in the indirect tooling. Based on 
green manufacturing, an environment friendly 
process for making large size silicone rubber is 
developed and implemented in this work. This 
method provides a new, simple, and green 
manufacturing to produce large size silicone 
rubber mold without loss of dimensional 
accuracy, geometrical accuracy, and surface 
roughness. The advantages of this method 
include high yields of the mold fabrication, 
reducing the cost in the mold fabrication, and 
environmentally conscious manufacturing. The 
saving in the cost of the rapid tooling is up to 
66.41%. 
Keywords: Rapid tooling, large size silicone 
rubber mold, green manufacturing 
 
前言 
如何有效的縮短產品開發時間，以最新
最符合市場需求的產品保持市場的領先，是
各廠商不斷努力的方向，因此誰能在最短的
時間及最少的經費下推出新產品誰就是贏
家。在目前的工業界運用上，產品開發的流
程可以分成三個階段：設計、試作驗證及正
式量產(mass production)，而在正式量產前常
需投入大量的經費與人力從事設計與試作的
工作，試作完成後常又因為圖面與實際物體
認知上的差異而需做設計上的變更，而此時
通常已投入大量的時間與經費，若能減少此
階段的開發時間及費用，則對於產品的時效
性及成本競爭性將會有非常大的幫助，因此
發展出快速原型技術(Rapid Prototyping, RP) 
[1-5]，快速原型技術可以在設計完成後能立
刻有實際的物體，對於設計者研發思緒的延
續及各部門間同步工程的進行都是一大助
益。目前快速原型機台售價皆在數百萬至數
千萬間，若其功能僅限於上述則顯得大材小
用，故如何將其功用繼續延續就又是另一門
重要的課題，因此發展出快速模具技術
(Rapid Tooling, RT) [6]。快速模具技術可以視
為快速原型技術的延伸,讓快速原型技術本
來所具有的開發階段的利益直接地延續到生
mm。表 2 為前護網 RP 製程參數，層厚設定

容室，其具有一空氣通口及一液面感測器，其中該液面感測器之
下方空間係用以容置一容器，且該液面感測器係經由穿過該密閉
容室之一第一導線輸出一液面感測信號；一密閉管路，其具有第
一開口、第二開口、第三開口及第四開口，其中該第一開口係與
該空氣通口套接；一手動控制閥門，其係與該第二開口套接，且
具有一控制把手；一電磁閥，其係與該第三開口套接，且具有與
一第二導線連接之一開、關信號輸入端；一真空泵浦，其係與該
第四開口套接，且具有與一第三導線連接之一啟動信號輸入端；
以及一可程式控制器，其具有與該第一導線耦接之一第一輸入
端，與該第二導線耦接之一第一輸出端，及與該第三導線耦接之
一第二輸出端。

Engineers and Scientists: Propellers of Technological Innovation for Competitive Advantage 
 
Abstract 
Whether a country can move forward or fall behind in the world order is decided by many factors 
led by its ability to educate, produce, and utilize engineers and scientists. Science, technology, and 
innovation are the propellers of our infinitely evolving world. The past decade witnessed unprecedented 
global economic changes brought about by the power of a steady stream of emerging technologies. New 
technology becomes obsolete at a much faster rate; and disruptive innovation has become the currency of 
the day. Risk-averse organizations have little chance to make it big in the market place. Critical success 
factors necessary for an organization to thrive are primarily dependent on their ability to create and 
deliver technologies. Creating new technology is the driver of the strategic entry and positioning in 
global markets. In order to compete effectively, companies must leverage their existing infrastructure 
knowledge assets, stimulate innovation, and align their dynamic capabilities processes. There is a need 
for corporations to understand the complexity of the strategic integration of information technology 
capabilities, knowledge management programs, along with inventive corporate initiatives for enhancing 
value creation and improving their competitive advantage.

System Identification: The Path from Data to Model 
 
Abstract 
System identification is the art and science of building mathematical models of dynamic systems 
from observed input-output data. It can be seen as the interface between the real world of applications 
and the mathematical world of control theory and model abstractions. As such it is a ubiquitous necessity 
for successful applications. The area has many facets and there are many approaches and methods. The 
presentation aims at both giving an overview of the "science" side, i.e. basic principles and result and at 
illustrating the practical, "art", side of how to approach a real problem. System identification is a very 
large topic, with different techniques that depend on the character of the models to be estimated, linear, 
non-linear, hybrid, nonparametric etc. At the same time, the area can be characterized by a small number 
of leading principles, e.g. to look for sustainable descriptions by proper trade-offs in the triangle of model 
complexity, information contents in the data, and effective validation. The fundamental character of this

(英文) Vacuum casting machine is usually employed to de-gas in the manufacturing of silicone 
rubber mold. The operations of de-gas process using vacuum casting machine are 
accomplished by manual operations until now. The drawback of operating vacuum 
casting machine to de-gas in the manufacturing of silicone rubber mold for operator 
include human error of operator and suffer from both noise and air pollution derived from 
the motor of the vacuum casting machine. In order to enhance the efficiency of rapid 
tooling fabrication, reduce human error, noise and air pollution that is harmful to health 
of operator under long operation time. An automotive de-gas system for rapid tooling 
application is designed and implemented in this study. The inner structure and vacuum 
tube of the vacuum casting machine are reconstructed. Photoelectric sensor is employed 
to detect the signal change in de-gas process using vacuum casting machine.
