Abstract
In this paper we present Polypropylene(PP)/
metallocene polyethylene(mPE)/clay nanocomposites.
Metallocene polyethylene was grafted with vinyl triethoxy
silane first and then followed by the mixing of which with
PP and clay. The clay incorporation was 2, 5, and 9 parts
per hundred resins of mPE. The crosslinking reaction was
conducted at 70 in the presence of water.℃ The effect of
curing on the mechanical properties and thermal properties
was investigated.
Keywords: Polypropylene, mPE, clay, silane, mechanical
properties, thermal properties, nanocomposites

一、前言
材料是人類賴以生存和發展的物質基礎，近幾年
有機、無機奈米複合材料受到非常多的注意不論是工
業界還是學術界[1]。奈米複合材料是指以高分子為基
材(連續相)，而補強材(分散相)以奈米級的程度分散於
基材中，其分散力徑大小定義是介於 1~100nm 之間
[2]，當尺寸縮小至奈米尺寸，有別於一般材料的物理
與化學特性。黏土在近年來特別受到重視，因為黏土
為奈米尺寸之無機層狀結構，層間帶有陽離子，經澎
潤改質後可以增加其層間距離(D-spacing)，有利於高
分子進入層間並分散以形成高分子奈米複合材料
[3]。黏土在自然界中藏量豐富、具有高物理特性等優
點[4-5]，因此被視為極具市場潛力的重要奈米材料。
交聯技術有許多種類，包括輻射交聯、硫化交聯、
酚醛樹脂交聯、過氧化物交聯及矽烷水交聯等[6]。其
中矽烷水交聯法是將 silane 接枝於主鏈上，其末端基
會行縮合反應脫去 H2O 生成 Si-O-Si 的鍵結產生交聯
的作用。
熱塑性彈性體一般簡稱為 TPE(Thermoplastic
elastomer)，TPE 有兩大類，一為嵌段共聚合物如聚苯
乙烯-丁二烯-苯乙烯共聚合物(SBS)，聚胺基甲酸酯
(TPU)等；另一類為橡塑膠混成合膠(TPO)及交聯型
TPO(TPV)[7]。TPE 兼具塑膠和橡膠的雙重特性，使
其在當今材料領域獲得極大的應用[8]。
本實驗以熔融混煉的方式將 clay掺混於mPE接枝
silane(mPE-g-silane)中，再掺混 PP，最後進行水交聯，
預期 clay参與水交聯反應，可撐開其層間距，增加 clay
在此複合材料之分散程度。並經由機械性質、熱性質和
細微形態分析，來觀察交聯和未交聯之間的差異。
二、文獻回顧
2-1、mPE 接枝矽烷水交聯
Sirisinha, K. ; 等人在 2004 年以 mPE 為基材，比
較過氧化物交聯和矽烷水交聯在機械性質和熱性質的
影響。在熱性質方面，由 DSC 的分析結果發現純 mPE
和過氧化物交聯都在 60~85℃有一寬闊的吸熱峰，而
矽烷水交聯則是在 78℃有一吸熱峰[9] 。Sirisinha, K. :
等人在 2004 年以 Ethylene-octene copolymer (EOR)為
基材，利用雙螺桿混煉機將 dicumyl peroxide (DCP) 和
vinyl trimethoxysilane (VTMS) 接枝上，再浸於 7 0 ℃
的 水 進 行 水 交 聯 。此研究發現水交聯 60 hr 有最
大凝膠量達 77 %，但超過 60 hr 後凝膠量不會再增
加。隨著水交聯時間和凝膠量的增加，抗張強度也隨
著增加[10]。Sirisinha, K. : 等人在 2004 年以 dicumyl
peroxide (DCP) 和 vinyl trimethoxysilane (VTMS) 接
枝在 Ethylene-octene copolymer(EOR)上，用不同比例
EOR/LDPE 混合來觀察凝膠量和 FTIR 在 1192 cm-1 吸
收峰的變化。發現隨著水交聯時間增長，凝膠量和
-Si-O-Si-的吸收峰也隨著增加，但 Si-OCH3 的吸收峰
隨之減少[11]。
2-2、高分子奈米/clay 複合材料
Zhang, Q. ; 等人在 2000 年經由 18 碳銨鹽改質之
蒙脫土，和蒙脫土、PP-g-MA、PP 進行熔融混煉，型
成 PP/MMT 奈米複合材料。由 XRD 分析發現，
C18-MMT 之層間距離比未改質之蒙脫土由 1.4nm 增
加到 3.6 nm，至於 PP/C18-MMT 複材之層間距離則為
4.4 nm，近似脫層，但 TEM 圖中發現許多蒙脫土重
疊，因此不屬於完全脫層。在抗張強度上，
PP/C18-MMT 和純 PP 比較並沒有太大的差異[12]。

Styenene-ethylene-butylene-sytrene block copolymer (SEBS) is one of the most widely used
thermoplastic elastomer, which exhibits balanced elasticity and processibility, good thermal stability,
etc. Thus, it is applied in adhesives, the automotive and sports industries. To improve its properties,
the intercalated clay was incorporated into SEBS in some studies. Most importantly, Szazdi et al.
pointed out that a high exfoliation extent may not guarantee high strength for layered silicate
nanocomposites demonstrated by PP/clay nanocomposites using PP-g-MA as a compatibilizer. This
work attempts to elucidate this important finding further and focuses on mechanical properties in
detail, including tensile strength, and tear strength by varying inter-phase properties using two
different compatibilizers for SEBS/clay nanocomposites.
Experimental. Styrene-ethylene-butylene-styrene triblock copolymer was Kraton G1652 with 29 wt %
styrene (Shell Chemical). The commercial O-MMT clay (Cloisite 20A, denoted as 20A in the
following) obtained from Southern Clay Products, Inc. SEBS-g-maleic anhydride (Shell, Kraton
FG-1901) with 29 wt % of styrene and the grafting level of maleic anhydride is 1.7 wt %. PP-g-maleic
anhydride was Du Pont commercial grade under the trade name of Fusabond 613(MD 613-5). The
grafting level of maleic anhydride is 0.55 wt %. In our preparation procedure, SEBS and organic
modified clay were mixed with various amounts of compatibilizers in a batch mixer (P& L Industries)
at a rotor speed of 100 rpm for 10 min. at 180 oC. The clay content was kept at 5 wt% and the
compatibilizer was loaded at 5, 10, 15, and 20 phr, respectively. The prepared batch was then hot
pressed at 180 oC to obtain a ca. 1 mm thick specimen.
For the characterization, X-ray diffraction (XRD) equipment and transmission electron
microscopy (TEM) were employed to evaluate the dispersion of O-MMT in the composites.
A Siemens D5005 X-ray unit operating at 40 kV and 40 mA was used to carry out the
experiments at room temperature. The X-ray source was CuKα radiation at a wavelength of
0.154 nm. The diffractograms were scanned in the 2 range from 1.5° to 10° at a rate of
0.02°/sec. The TEM observation was performed on ultrathin sections of cryo-microtomed
thin composite films with a JEOL JEM-2000EX II system using an acceleration voltage of
100 kV. The optical properties were evaluated using a UV-Visible light spectrophotometer
(Varian, Cary 50) at a scanning wavelength from 400 to 1000 nm. Tensile measurements were
conducted based on ASTM-D638 at a crosshead speed of 20 cm/min using a Universal

Mechanical Properties. To further compare the effectiveness of compatibilizers on the tensile
properties of the nanocomposites, tensile strength is plotted against the compatibilizer dosage,
as shown in Figure 4. Generally, tensile strength for the nanocomposites containing
PP-g-MA or SEBS-g-MA was significantly different, especially at high dosage levels. This
finding reflected the important role of compatibilizer types and dosages in terms of tensile
strength for a large deformation in comparison to a small deformation in the case of Young’s
modulus. As the SEBS-g-MA content increased, tensile strength of composites decreased
slightly and reached a value of 28.1±1.0 MPa, as tensile strength of SEBS-g-MA at 20.51.8
MPa was a little bit lower than that of the SEBS matrix. For nanocomposites containing
20 phr of PP-g-MA, tensile strength increased 30%, reaching a value of 39.0 ±2.5 MPa. As
discussed in the X-ray diffraction patterns, the expansion degree of clay was slightly larger in
the SEBS-g-MA compatibilized system. Surprisingly, the PP-g-MA compatibilized system
conferred higher tensile strength than the SEBS-g-MA compatibilized system, even though a
higher dosage of SEBS-g-MA was of benefit to expand the interlayer spacing of the clay.
This unexpected result was in line with the work of Szazdi et al. on PP/clay nanocomposites
using 20 vol% of PP-g-MA as a compatibilizer [10]. In addition, the complex morphology
derived from incorporated compatibilizers also played an important role in our system, as
described earlier. A similar finding on PP-g-MA compatibilized cases was found for tear
strength.
