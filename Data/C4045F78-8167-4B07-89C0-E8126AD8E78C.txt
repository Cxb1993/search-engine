Abstract 
The advance of technology has boosted the 
economic growth. But mass production, 
consumption and discharge of toxic materials 
will cause exhaustion of resources and global 
pollution, which hindered the perpetual 
existence of the human race. Among them, the 
greenhouse effect result on the earth is serious 
increasingly and the Petroleum of energy is 
exhausted day by day which are the global focus. 
In accordance with statistics of the BP Statistical 
Review of World Energy, reserve of the 
petroleum can only be used 40 to 50 years more 
at most with the speed that the petroleum 
consumes now. For this reason, new energy and 
relevant technology for development is pressing, 
the technology, such as fuel cell, solar energy, 
and wind energy are in the alternative energy to 
stand out, and become the global focus. Most 
countries stand side by side fuel cell, 
semiconductor science and technology and 
biotechnology for the three revolutionary science 
and technology in the 21st century. Semi Active 
Open Structure II(SAOSII) to present with the 
open structure, help researchers understand the 
principle of fuel cell, the researchers are easy to 
replace every spare part in this platform and test 
the efficiency of the fuel cell. This paper used 
figure component on MATLAB/SIMULINK to 
build up a control model for SAOSII.
