Abstract 
The mechanical grinding processes is the most popular way to fabricate the end face of 
optical fibers, although there are other methods like chemical etching and laser machining.   
In this study, the optical fiber grinding mechanism is redesigned and implemented to 
overcome the restraint of the traditional optical fiber grinding machine that cannot control the 
angle between the fiber and the diamond film during the grinding process. By redesign and 
implement the optical fiber grinding mechanism, the relative position (fiber rotating angle φ、the 
angle θ and the vertical distance H between the fiber and the diamond film) and the material 
removal rate (MRR) are simultaneously controlled to change the normal pressure and MRR in the 
grinding process.  The fiber envelope end-face is constructed based on the three parameter φ, θ, 
H of the related position. 
In the first year, the machining theorem of optical fiber end-face, the requests and limitations 
of the grinding mechanism are studied. The prototype of a new optical fiber endface grinding 
machine has been developed. In the second year, this machine will be improved and fine turned, 
it’s going to design and implement a Mechanism for grinding optical-fiber endface with 
double-variable curvatures, which can control Material Removal Rate by adjusting the relative 
position and the normal pressure between the fiber endface and the grinding film simultaneously 
and periodically. This study is composed with reviewing the anterior references, including the 
papers and the patents, and a series of engineering design methods, which involve the design 
requirements and constraints, conceptual design, evaluating alternatives, detail design, assembly 
and calibration. The mechanism, the research result, and those needed to be ameliorated will be 
demonstrated in the conclusion and discussion part, so as to offer the investigating direction in 
the future. It is believed the grinding machine system developed in this study can be successfully 
applied for fabricating the optical fiber lenses as well as different types of micro probes, micro 
electrodes, and micro spectroscope for other applications. 
 
Keywords: Optical Fiber Endface, Grinding Machine, Mechanism, Grinding Processes,

1. 改善機構作動精度： 
    由於先前市售研磨機於研磨過程中其震幅惡化至 10μm 以上，嚴重影響研磨精度，使

區域震幅小於 1μm 之研磨機台（圖 3 、圖 6） ，取代市售舊機台。 
    自轉軸部分，先前作動一圈會上下震盪兩次，且震動幅度約 40~50μm，已嚴   重影

圖 4 )目前自轉軸上下約 1 μm 以下，已成功解決震盪問題。 
    圓弧型滑軌部分，由於使用一段時間後電動缸與線性滑軌之間的平行度偏移導致其作

五、結論 
    本研究在耦光設計端即投入最佳耦光的微透鏡（Micro Lens）的設備開發，不但成功
開發出具有五軸(自轉軸角度φ、光纖與磨盤夾角θ及光纖與磨盤之鉛直距離 H、扭力控制
α軸、以及研磨盤轉軸)的加工平台。成功突破「市售傳統光纖研磨機」、「非對稱光纖端面
研磨機」及「扭力控制光纖端面研磨機」之機械限制與研磨性能，且成功地將頭髮般粗細
之玻璃光纖研製成各種光纖端面，如錐式楔型光纖端面、多角錐型光纖端面及雙變曲率光
纖端面。本研究所研發之「雙變曲率光纖端面研磨機」，除可用於製造各式光纖端面，以供
光纖通訊系統使用外，僅須更換適合之夾具及套管，即可用於各式微探針、微電極及微分
光鏡之研磨加工。
