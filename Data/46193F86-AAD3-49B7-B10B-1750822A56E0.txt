中文摘要 
    台灣總督府專賣局為台灣日治時
期最重要的官營事業機構，經營之專賣事業
含括菸、酒、鹽、樟腦、鴉片…等民生產業，
專賣局所屬廳舍、工場、鹽田與官舍遍布全
台各地，成為提供就業機會與維持地方經濟
的重要命脈。現存台灣日治時期建築中，專
賣局體系建築之數量眾多且各具代表性，除
已有部分案例獲得列級國家古蹟或登錄為
歷史建築以外，未來並有部分案例將轉型為
五大「創意文化園區」，專賣局體系建築顯
已逐漸獲得重視與再生利用。 
本研究係以 2003年底開放使用的「台
灣總督府專賣局公文類纂」為研究對象，由
萬餘卷原始檔案中，篩選屬於規劃配置與建
築設計部分之圖面史料，經彙整後作為解析
各地專賣局體系建築組群發展變遷歷程之
基礎資料，並對各建築群組轄下廳舍、工
場、鹽田與官舍等四類建築，就其平面與構
成特性分別加以探討。 
關鍵詞：台灣總督府專賣局公文類纂 
Abstract 
The Taiwan Soutokufu Senbaikyoku was 
an important nationalization organization 
during the Japanese period. The organization 
was in charge of the most indispensable 
people’s livelihood industry, which included 
tabacco, wine, salt, camphor and opium. 
These buildings of Senbaikyoku system 
included official hall, factory, salt pan and 
official residence, which were set up 
everywhere in Taiwan. Because of serving the 
employs and holding the enconomics, their 
values were playing a decisive role. 
Nowadays, there are still a significant 
numbers existing buildings of Senbaikyoku 
system which were built during the Japanese 
period, these buildings are varieties and 
representatives. Some cases were turned into 
the national historic monuments or registered 
as history constructions now, some of these 
cases will turned into five major “cultural park 
of originality”. Nowadays, the buildings of 
Senbaikyoku system are gradually become 
valued and recycled in the future. 
 The research will focus on the Taiwan 
Soutokufu Senbaikyoku Archives which 
disclosure since 2003, to choose some original 
documents about planning and architectural 
design in buildings of Senbaikyoku system, 
and to make the database for further analysis. 
The building types will subdivide to official 
hall, factory, salt pan and official residence as 
the ruling of classification, and to discuss with 
the planes and the characteristic of buildings.
