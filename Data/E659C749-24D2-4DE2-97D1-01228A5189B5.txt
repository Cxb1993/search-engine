一、中文摘要（關鍵詞︰薄膜電晶體液晶顯示
器、感光性高分子、碳黑、樹脂型黑色矩
陣） 
黑色矩陣可用來提高薄膜電晶體液晶顯示器的
色彩對比性及避免薄膜電晶體產生光電效應，因而
成為平面顯示器技術相當重要的課題之一。本研究
為延續 NSC96－2221－E－005－047 計畫旨在研發
環境友善高性能彩色薄膜電晶體液晶顯示器黑色矩
陣材料。首先，利用共聚高分子改質劑在分散程序
中將高碳黑粒子(原始粒徑小於 16nm)表面修飾使其
二次粒徑達到奈米尺度並均勻且安定地分佈在有機
溶液中，然後不經固液分離程序而直接與含有鹼液
可溶環氧壓克力樹脂、光起始劑、及反應性單體進
行摻合，並經由旋轉塗佈與紫外線曝光和顯影程
序，製作同時具有高光學密度(OD>3/µm)及低導電性
(表面電阻抗>1012Ω/□)之高解析度(線寬<10µm)樹脂
型黑色矩陣。 
英 文摘 要 (Keywords: TFTLCD; Photosensitive 
polymer; Carbon black; Black matrix resin) 
With double functions of enhancing the contrast of 
color spectra and protecting thin film transistor from 
opto-electrical effect, the black matrix (BM) is one of 
the critical issues in the flat panel display (FPD). 
Following the project (NSC96－2221－E－005－047), 
This study aims to research and develop the advanced 
material of black matrix for environment friendly and 
high performance of color thin film transistor liquid 
crystal display (TFTLCD). Firstly, the co-polymeric 
dispersant is employed to modify and uniformly stable 
the nano-phase of carbon black in solution, and then 
without separation of solid and liquid blend directly 
with alkali soluble polymer in the presence of photo 
initiator and reactive monomer to fabricate 
photosensitive black matrix with high optical density 
(OD>3/µm), low conductivity (>1012Ω/□), and high 
resolution (line width<10µm) in the lower amount of 
carbon black through spin coating and photolithography 
process. 
二、計畫的緣由與目的 
薄膜電晶體液晶顯示器(TFT-LCD)為非主動發
光元件，故須透過內部的背光模組提供光源並搭配
驅動積體電路(IC)與液晶控制形成黑、白兩色的灰階
顯示，再透過彩色濾光片的紅（R）、綠（G）、藍
（B）三種彩色層提供色相，以形成彩色顯示畫面，
因此彩色濾光片可說是液晶顯示器彩色化之關鍵零
組件。而在彩色濾光片結構中，佔成本比例最大及
技術需求層次最高的即是黑色矩陣。黑色矩陣在彩
色濾光片內的功能為[1]：(1) 防止紅，綠，藍三色混
色﹔(2) 提高紅，綠，藍三顏色之對比值﹔及(3) 保
護 TFT。 
目前主要以重金屬鉻為黑色矩陣材料，其原因為
鉻所形成的黑色矩陣具有膜厚薄且高光學密度，耐
熱、耐化性佳，此外，亦能得到解析度較高的圖案。
雖然如此，仍有一些缺點，即加工的過程複雜、製
作成本昂貴、高反射率，同時也因為鉻金屬的蒸鍍
或濺鍍程序、蝕刻程序等會產生重金屬鉻的廢棄
物，對環境的污染相當大，因此由鉻所形成的黑色
矩陣在未來將會漸漸被禁用[2]。另外，近年來金屬
鉻型黑色矩陣為了解決其高反射率的問題便是在金
屬鉻薄膜上再鍍上一層或數層之氧化層，如 CrOx 等
[3, 4-5]。此方式可以大幅降低反射率，但由於現今
玻璃基板大型化趨勢後，其所需的設備與材料成本
提高，且對環境亦有更大的污染。此外，由於鉻的
低阻抗，使得以鉻所形成的黑色矩陣不能直接做在
TFT 基板上，為了克服此問題，一種以有機高分子
為主體的樹脂型黑色矩陣也開始被廣泛的加以研究
[6]。樹脂型黑色矩陣主要是將碳黑或黑色染料與感
光性樹脂作均勻性的摻合以形成黑色光阻，接下來
便依照一般的微影製程序，即利用旋轉塗佈，將含
有碳黑之感光性樹脂塗佈在玻璃基材上，再經曝光
顯影製成黑色矩陣，此具有製程簡單、成本低且對
環境污染最少等優點，但光學密度不高是其最大的
缺點[7, 8]。 
樹脂型黑色矩陣可分為黑色染料及碳黑兩種系
統，其中黑色染料吸光性較差，且對高分子樹脂或
溶劑溶解性不佳﹔此外，耐熱、耐光及耐化性也比
碳黑差。而目前的樹脂型黑色矩陣都以碳黑為主，
原因為其有優良的光色澤度[9, 10]，但由於碳黑的凝
聚力強，易導致光散射，使得穿透度及消色性變差，
因此以碳黑為主的樹脂型黑色矩陣，其分散安定化
和提高光學密度為一項重要的課題。 
樹脂型黑色矩陣另一關鍵組成為感光性高分
子，其主要功能為：(1)形成光微影圖案；(2)增強附
著力；及(3)膜厚控制。目前形成黑色光阻有酸交聯
和自由基兩種感光聚合系統。其中，感光性酸交聯
聚合系統是以紫外光照射酸性物質產生氫質子在高
分子結構中擴散並尋找可交聯的單體進行反應，此
系統的優點為因氫質子擴散力強，故碳黑粒子在高
分子中的填充量可增加以提高樹脂型黑色矩陣的光
學密度[11]，雖然如此，膜厚增加會造成後續彩色層
的平坦性及解析度不良，且殘餘的氫質子會破壞高
分子結構 [12]，導致黑色矩陣的信賴度下降。 
綜合上述，為了克服現階段樹脂型黑色矩陣材
料與製程所遭遇之困境，即光遮蔽率、電阻抗、附
著力、微影解析度與環保同時必須兼顧的問題，本
計畫提出一項研發構想，即藉由零維奈米結構的操
控技術，使二次碳黑粒子在鹼液可溶感光性樹脂中
達到奈米程度且均勻地分散形成高比表面積的狀
態，期望能夠獲得低碳黑填充量(SC<10wt%)但高光
學密 (OD>3/µm)且低導電性(>1×1012Ω/□)的黑色光
阻。 
三、研究方法及成果 
接續 96 年度(NSC96－2221－E－005－047)的
研究成果，即碳黑奈米分散液及高解析度鹼液可溶
感光性高分子的製備技術，本研究進一步利用摻合
方法配製低固含量與環境友善的黑色光阻。在本實
驗中，分別利用電子穿隧顯微鏡(TEM)、場發電子掃
描顯微鏡(FE-SEM)、原子力顯微鏡(AFM)、高阻抗
四點探針與光學密度儀進行(1)碳黑奈粒子在鹼液可
溶感光性高分子中之分散狀態與結構形貌觀察﹔及
(2)黑色光阻光微影解析度測試與其光電特性鑑定。 
本計畫所獲得重要成果歸納如下: 
(1) 經由 TEM、AFM 及 FE-SEM 觀察得知，本研究
之碳黑奈米分液與鹼液可溶感光性高分子可均
勻摻合，且成膜後碳黑粒子在高分子材料中呈現
低結構狀態其顆粒大小平均約 48nm。 
另外，圖 4-2-3 為 9.45 wt % M-1000 之黑色光阻
膜厚對光學密度關係圖，從圖中可以觀察出來，當
膜厚達 0.7 µm 以上時 M-1000 黑色光阻的光學密度
超過 3。而 M-1000 黑色光阻可以在低膜厚、低固含
量達到高光學密度，這是由於 M-1000 碳黑分散液有
較小的二次平均粒徑，導致所製備出的黑色光阻具
有高比表面積性質，因而可以同時達到低固含量、
低膜厚與高光學密度。

2. EXPERIMENT 
 
Cobalt acetate (Co(CH3COO)2．4H2O) was manufactured by SHOWA. Palladium chloride (PdCl2), 
Polyvinyl- pyrrolidone (PVP, molecular weight= 10,000), Dodecylamine (DDA) and Tri- octylphosphine 
Oxide (TOPO) were manufactured by Aldrich Chemical Company. Ethylene glycol and ethyl alcohol 
were the products of Tedia Corporation. All reagents are reagent grade and used without further 
purification. 
 
The recycle microwave process was conducted to reduce cobalt acetate with ethylene glycol and 
capping agents. A domestic microwave oven (2.45MHz, SAMPO MOP-201R, Taiwan) was modified as 
follow: a 2mm ID of helical quartz tube located inside the cavity of microwave oven and connected to 
silicon tube. The tube outside the microwave oven was covered by quartz wool to prevent heat loss. The 
reaction system is schematically shown in Fig 1. The reactant was placed in the three-neck bottle and 
transported into the microwave oven by peristaltic pump and then recycle. Reacting temperature could be 
controlled precisely within ±2℃ by senor. 
 
For the synthesis of cobalt nano- particle, the metal precursors were mixed with ethylene glycol, 
nuclear agent of palladium chloride and the capping agent of PVP, TOPO and DDA, respectively, and 
heated by microwave at 190℃ in the recycle polyol  process. The various amounts of chemicals, 
reacting time and temperature of treatments are given in table1 to 3.

3.2 Binary capping agent formula 
 
Compare the molecules structure of PVP, TOPO and DDA. PVP is a kind of polymer, which the 
nitrogen atom bonds with cobalt ion (Xiong et al., 2007), resulting in the long chain to cover surface of 
cobalt and five-membered heterocycles as steric hindrance to prevent aggregation of as-synthesized Co 
nano-particles. TOPO has three alkyl group chains each with eight carbon atoms. DDA is linear structure 
with one nitrogen atom and one alkyl chains with twelve carbon atoms. The steric hindrance from DDA is 
smaller than from PVP and TOPO respectively. This may be the major reason in the binary capping agent 
with DDA, causing different grow rate in different direction for an anisotropic shape, as shown in Figs. 4 
to 6 and summarized in Table 3. At the initial period of synthesis reaction, the small and isotropic shape 
of cobalt nano-particles is formed, and  cobalt ions bonded with the smaller molecule of DDA could 
collided with the cobalt nano-particle at the surface of smaller steric hindrance. Finally, the nano-particle 
starts to grow in particular direction and produce nano-fiber in the recycle microwave polyol process. 
 
magnetic anisotropy energy barrier is controlled by thermal fluctuations, so the moments ease rotates among 
different directions, leading to that the magnetization of each particle vanishes and superpara- magnetism occurs 
(Held et al., 2001).

4. CONCLUSION 
 
This work has successfully synthesized cobalt nano-particles by recycle microwave assisted polyol process. As 
shown in the results, the capping agents play an important role on controlling the sizes and shapes of as-synthesized 
cobalt nano-phase. It is found that the particle size of as-synthesized Co can be controlled by varying the amount of 
single capping agent and the shape of as-synthesized cobalt nano-structure may be dominated by using the molar 
ration of binary copping agents in this study.

SYNTHESIS OF COBALT NANO-PHASE BY USING RECYCLE MICROWAVE POLYOL PROCESS 
 
 
W. T. Cheng* and  Y. L. Wang 
 
 
Department of Chemical Engineering, National Chung Hsing University, 
250 Kuo Kaung Rd., Taichung 402, Taiwan, R. O. C 
 
 
Abstract: This paper aims to report manipulating the size and shape of cobalt nano-phase by different capping agent in 
the recycle microwave-polyol process. The cobalt nano-phase were synthesized by using cobalt acetate with ethylene 
glycol in the presence of palladium chloride as nuclear agent and polyvinylpyrrolidone (PVP), trioctylphosphine oxide 
(TOPO) and dodecylamine (DDA) as capping agent through recycle microwave-polyol process. As shown in the results, 
with single capping agent of PVP, TOPO and DDA, respectively, the mean particle size of as-synthesized cobalt can be 
effectively controlled within 3 nm to 25nm. In the binary capping agent system, the suitable ratios of PVP and DDA as 
well as TOPO and DDA can be used to synthesize cobalt nano-fiber, which the diameter is about 10nm to 25nm and the 
length is about 50nm to 120nm. In addition, it is found that the PVP and  TOPO may be only employed to synthesize 
nano-particle-like of cobalt in this work.  
 
Keywords: Recycle process; Microwave; Ethylene glycol; Cobalt Nano-particles; Polyol   
 
 
1. INTRODUCTION 
 
The definition of nano-materials is a characteristic length scale less than 100nm in one dimension at least. 
According to the structure, it can be divided into three types: particle-like, rod-like or wire-like, and layer-like. Cobalt 
nano-materials display new interesting fundamental properties because of its specific magnetic property and potentially 
in many nanotechnology applications, including high-density information storage (Lodder, 1996; Hisano and Saito, 
1998), magnetic fluid (Moskowitz and Casciari, 1995), catalyst (Huh et al., 2005) and drug delivery system (Bao and 
Krishnan, 2005). 
 
Cobalt nano-materials have been prepared by various techniques where microwave-polyol process is one of those. 
Microwave-polyol process is polyol reduction combining microwave heating technique. Polyol reduction is using liquid 
polyols, such as ethylene glycol, working as solvent and reducing agent at the same time (Carotenuto et al., 2000) and 
microwave can heat polar solution to high temperature instantaneously and uniformly without heating the glass 
container (Liu, 2000). This method, originally developed by Fiévet et al., is now referred to as the microwave-polyol 
process (Cushing et al., 2004). 
 
For small size and narrow distribution, how to manipulate the shape of nano-particles is another important topic. 
The structures of different shape exhibit different properties. For example, one of the major factors that cause magnetic 
anisotropy is shape anisotropy. In point of geometry, the shape of hexagons and triangles has the advantage that it 
enables close-packing of particles in the unit area (Shukla et al., 2006), improving the ability of magnetic recording 
media. Puntes and co-workers (Puntes et al., 2001) mixed two different capping agent with Co2 (CO)8 in o-dichloro- 
benzend to obtain anisotropic shape of cobalt nano-phase. Komarneni and co-workers (Komarneni et al., 2004) also 
combine different capping agent with the microwave-polyol process for anisotropic shape of metal nano-phase. They 
concluded that it is a good strategy to control the shape of cobalt nano-phase by mixed different capping agents. 
 
In this paper, we manipulate the size and shape of cobalt nano-phase by using single and binary capping agents, 
respectively, in the recycle microwave-polyol process. The temperature of system can be controlled precisely within 
±2℃ in varying volume flow rate, improving the size distribution helpfully and reducing the danger of superheated 
solution in sealed pipe.
