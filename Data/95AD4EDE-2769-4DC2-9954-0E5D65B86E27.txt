添加奈米TiO2對Sn3.5Ag0.5Cu無鉛銲錫 
應用於先進微電子構裝之可行性及界面反應的研究 
 
Research of the Solderability of Adding Nano-TiO2 to Sn3.5Ag0.5Cu 
Solders on Advanced Electric Packaging  
 
計畫編號：NSC 97-2218-E-020-004- 
執行期限：97 年 10 月 1 日至 98 年 9 月 30 日 
主持人：曹龍泉   國立屏東科技大學材料工程系 
計畫參與人員：孫瑋宏、許建志、李俊毅、張明進、劉俊宏、
王偉全 
  
 
一、中文摘要 
 
許多無鉛銲錫成分中，共晶SnAgCu 系
合金已被電子產業廣為接受，然而三元
SnAgCu 銲錫仍存在一些缺點，針對相關之
問題，添加強化相元素為一解決之道。本
計畫預計研製具有優異潛變阻抗和高強度
特性的新型奈米複合-無鉛銲錫合金。其製
程選定以歐系及日系廠商所建議的SnAgCu
合金為基材，並以傳統Au20Wt.%Sn共晶銲
錫合金為對照比較,本計畫是探討添加奈
米TiO2對Sn3Ag0.5Cu 無鉛銲錫的可形性及
特性分析的研究。其顯微組織、機械性質
均明顯地改善。 
 
關鍵詞：奈米- TiO2 、SAC複合銲錫、顯
微組織、機械性質 
 
Abstract 
 
Many kind of Pb-free solder 
compositions, the eutectic SnAgCu ally 
system has gained popularity in the 
electronics industry. However, the 
employment of a ternary SnAgCu solder still 
presents a handful of disadvantages. The 
addition of the strengthen phase into the 
SnAgCu solder has been considered as an 
effective solution to these problems. 
The project, as a whole, will be 
exploratory study on uniformed dispersion 
of nanopowder in lead-free solders and that 
is a nano-composite solders, with that 
having excellent creep resistance and 
significantly enhanced strength properties. 
In search of the setup, Sn3.5Ag0.5Cu 
solders are chosen for base material ，
concurrent with the traditional Au20Sn 
serving as a contrasting counterpart during 
the same period. Metallographic 
observations of the lead-free SAC composite 
solders revealed a reduction in the grain size 
of β-Sn, Ag3Sn phase and Ag3Sn phase 
located between the spacing lamellae.  In 
terms of mechanical behavior, the addition 
of larger weight fractions of TiO2 
nanopowders in lead-free SAC solder matrix 
led to an improvement in microhardness, 
0.2%YS and UTS. 
 
Keywords: Nano-TiO2 ; Composite solder; 
Microstructure; Mechanical 
properties
