 I 
摘要 
 
二維凝膠電泳影像(Two-Dimensional Gel Electrophoresis Image, 2DGE Image)
是一種用來偵測與比對蛋白質點的重要生物影像。但二維凝膠電泳影像容易受限
於擷取設備的低動態範圍影響，導致二維凝膠電泳工具無法有效地正確偵測出微
量蛋白質點。本研究針對二維凝膠電泳影像進行一系列的相關研究，希望能協助
生物學家進行蛋白質研究，有效整合工作，提升研究效率。首先以程式批次設定
取代人工手動設定，完成二維凝膠電泳影像批次擷取的工作，達到省時與避免人
為因素影響影像品質的優點。接著利用所得的影像，以類似高動態範圍影像 
(High Dynamic Range Image) 的觀念，設計出 HDB (High Dynamic Bit)，以提升
影像的位元數，完整保留影像在不同明亮度的特徵。最後為了解決高動態範圍影
像的儲存問題，以 JPEG-LS 為基礎，提出了無失真壓縮的方法。經由實驗證明，
除了可以幫助二維凝膠電泳工具找出原本偵測不到的微量蛋白質，與 WinRAR、
WinZip 及 7-Zip 三種檔案壓縮程式比較，可獲得較好的壓縮效果。 
 
 
關鍵字：影像擷取、高動態範圍影像、無失真壓縮、JPEG-LS。

1. 前言 
在蛋白質體學 (Proteomics) [2]研究中，生物學家們常利用二維凝膠電泳影像 
(Two-Dimensional Gel Electrophoresis Image, 2DGE Image) 來尋找疾病的致病機
轉。在取得蛋白質二維凝膠電泳影像後，接著進行蛋白質點「偵測」與「比對」
的工作，其目的在於找出異常的蛋白質，因為這些蛋白質有可能就是造成病患機
能異常的致病機轉。 
在眾多的蛋白質二維凝膠電泳工具軟體中，ImageMaster 2D Platinum (之後
簡稱為 IM) 是在學術界與軟體廠商合作之下共同開發而成，學術界部份包括了
Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics 以及 GE Healthcare，在軟體廠商部份則是
Amersham Pharmacia Biotech。IM 所得之偵測結果可直接與知名蛋白質資料庫
SWISS-2DPAGE 進行連線，交換彼此的資訊，並可取得其它特定種類蛋白質的
相關資訊，與其它獨自開發作業的工具軟體相比，具有更高的學術價值。 
然而，利用蛋白質二維凝膠電泳工具軟體進行偵測與比對蛋白質時，其結果
會被事先設定的各種參數值影響 (如解析度、明亮度、角度…等等參數值)。為
了改善偵測比對的結果，生物學家需要反覆調整各項參數值，整個步驟相當繁雜
且費時。因此，若影像擷取設備能有愈大的動態範圍，愈能表現出影像明暗間的
細膩層次，將更有利於蛋白質二維凝膠電泳工具軟體正確的偵測與比對蛋白質。 
即使影像擷取設備的動態範圍問題，透過提升硬體能力而獲得改善，只要儲
存影像的檔案格式仍然採用常見的 R、G、B 三原色，三者各用 8 位元方式來呈
現的話，仍然會造成部分影像資訊的失真，使得蛋白質二維凝膠電泳工具軟體的
偵測效果不佳。 
另外，合成一張適合二維凝膠電泳工具軟體使用的高動態影像，需針對二維
凝膠電泳影像的灰階影像特性，提升影像的灰階範圍，用更多的位元數來描述影
像中的每一個像素，如此才能有效提升二維凝膠電泳工具軟體的偵測與比對效
果。然而相對的，會造成儲存檔案時需要更多空間的問題。 
為了達到有效的壓縮影像的檔案空間，各種影像壓縮技術相繼被提出。影像
壓縮的方法有很多種，這些方法大致上可分為「無失真 (Lossless)」及「有失真 
(Lossy)」壓縮二種。為了盡可能保留影像的資訊，提升二維凝膠電泳工具軟體的
偵測與比對結果，適必得使用無失真的壓縮方式來儲存檔案。可惜的是，目前仍
沒有符合超過 8 位元且無失真壓縮的影像儲存格式，因此，設計符合此需求的影
像儲存格式，將是件相當重要的事情。 
雖然使用超過 8 位元來描述的影像，能保留更多的資訊，幫助二維凝膠電泳
工具軟體有更佳的結果，卻也造成了另一個問題，那就是現在的顯示設備與二維
凝膠電泳工具軟體大多只支援到 8 位元影像格式。因此，想要在螢幕與工具軟體
上正常呈現出超過 8 位元的影像內容，勢必得設計一個轉換方式，使它能正常地
使用於一般的環境下。

再將它們合併呈現。 
 
3.3. 影像壓縮 
所謂的影像壓縮，是指將影像透過各種編碼的技術，減少資料的冗餘性
(Redundancy)，達到降低資料儲存時所需空間的目的。影像壓縮的格式又可分為
無失真性影像(Lossless Image)與有失真性影像(Lossy Image)兩種方式。 
無失真性影像解壓縮後還原出來的圖，是與壓縮前的原始影像完全一致，無
任何細微的差異；有失真性影像的壓縮原理是利用人的肉眼對於細小的差異無法
分辨出來的特性，只要肉眼對於壓縮後的影像品質可以接受即可。一般的影像壓
縮技術大多為有失真性壓縮的方法，如 VQ(Vector Quantization) [15]、JPEG(Joint 
Photographic Coding Expert Group)、JPEG2000(Joint Photographic Coding Expert 
Group 2000) [12-13]等格式皆為有失真壓縮技術。少數如 PCX[15]、GIF、TGA 
[15]、PNG(Portable Network Graphics)[11]、JPEG-LS[14]、CALIC[7-10]為無失真
壓縮技術。 
 
3.4. 高動態範圍影像呈現 
為了將高動態範圍影像呈現於一般顯示裝置(低動態範圍)，人們開發了各種
將高動態範圍影像「轉換」成可以查看的影像的方法，這些方法統稱為「色調映
射(tone mapping)」目前的作法可分為 Tone reproduction curve[1, 4, 6]與 Tone 
reproduction operator[3-4]兩種。 
Tone reproduction curve 的作法是計算出一個像素轉換對應圖，依據轉換對應
圖將低動態範圍影像中場景的明亮度轉換成低動態範圍裝置所能呈現的明亮
度。該轉換對應圖的數值對應是固定不變動的，這代表著輸入同樣的像素值，即
使像素值座標不同，仍會得到同樣的轉換數值結果。轉換對應圖的優點是計算只
需要很短的時間，但缺點是轉換後的結果，會造成極小影像細節與對比的遺失。 
Tone reproduction operator 與 Tone reproduction curve 的作法相比，兩者有很
大的差異。Tone reproduction operator 認為即使是輸入同樣的像素值，應該根據

4.3. 高動態位元影像無失真壓縮 
本研究提出的高動態位元影像無失真壓縮方法，是以 JPEG-LS 為基礎進行
改良，執行步驟說明如下： 
 
步驟1：計算表示index count所需的位元數 n 
因為高動態影像的像素範圍已超過 JPEG-LS 所適用的一般 8 位元的影像，
即像素值介於[0, 255]間的範圍，所以在進行高動態影像無失真壓縮前，需要先
根據 ImgPixelRow'的長度，即 index count 的值，計算出最少需要 n 位元才足夠表
示 index count 的最大數值，計算公式請見公式 3。 
 
min(2n) ≧ index count (3) 
 
步驟 2：依據 n 的大小，重新設定 JPEG-LS 的像素值範圍，再進行壓縮。 
在上一步驟中，我們得到了表示 index count 的最大範圍的位元數 n，接下來
利用 n，重新設定 JPEG-LS 的像素值範圍。 
 
步驟 3：依照 JPEG-LS 的處理流程，進行壓縮。 
最後按照 JPEG-LS 的壓縮流程進行高動態影像無失真壓縮處理，得到壓縮
後的檔案。

Preface 
 
The past few years have witnessed a rapid development of intelligent systems in the theory and 
practice of artificial intelligence, biologically inspired computing (evolutionary computing, 
neural networks, artificial immune systems, ant colony optimization, swarm intelligence), 
decision support systems, fuzzy logic, data mining and knowledge discovery, human-machine 
interaction, machine learning, intelligent control, intelligent measurement, ontology, semantic 
web, etc.. The 2009 Global Congress on Intelligent Systems (GCIS’09) aims at providing a 
unique platform for researchers, industrialists and practitioners from all over the world to 
exchange ideas and achievements on their recent research in intelligent systems. 
 
The Proceedings include 464 papers selected by the Programme Committee from 1137 
submissions from all over the world, with an acceptance rate of 40.8%. Every paper has been 
carefully reviewed by the members of Programme Committee and independent reviewers. 
 
I deeply appreciate the support of the World Research Institutes (WRI) for all organisation issues 
and the IEEE Computer Society for publishing the GCIS’09 proceedings. Special thanks go to 
the authors of the papers in the Proceedings and all people who have submitted their research 
work to the GCIS’09. I would like to express my sincere gratitude to the members of Programme 
Committee and independent reviewers. Without their professional supports to review the papers, 
the GCIS’09 would not have been possible, and the quality of the conference would not have 
been guaranteed. The last but not the least, I would also like to thank all other people who are 
involved in this conference.

Janusz Kacprzyk is a Professor 
of Computer Science at the 
Systems Research Institute, 
Polish Academy of Sciences. 
He is an Academician of the 
Polish Academy of Sciences 
(Member of the Polish 
Academy of Sciences). 
Professor Kacprzyk was also 
visiting professor at many 
universities in the USA, United 
Kingdom, Italy and China. His 
research interests include soft 
computing, fuzzy logic, decision 
making, decision analysis and 
decision support, database 
querying, information retrieval, 
data analysis, data mining, etc. 
His publication record is: 5 
books, 30 volumes, 300 papers. 
Professor Kacprzyk is the 
President of IFSA (International 
Fuzzy Systems Association), 
Fellow of IEEE and IFSA. He 
received The 2005 IEEE CIS 
Fuzzy Pioneer Award for 
pioneering works on multistage 
fuzzy control, notably fuzzy 
dynamic programming, and The 
Sixth Kaufmann Prize and Gold 
Medal for pioneering works on 
the use of fuzzy logic in 
economy and management. He 
is the Editor in Chief of 3 
Springer’s book series, and is 
on editorial boards of ca. 25 
journals. Professor Kacprzyk 
has been invited to be a 
member of the IPC at more 
than 200 conferences.

“Intelligent Systems for 
Intelligence Data 
Analysis” 
 
Abstract: 
Failures in the detection of 
serious crime, including 
terrorist activity are not 
necessarily due to insufficient 
data, but rather to difficulties 
in interpreting the available 
intelligence. Automated 
software systems that model 
and analyse intelligence data 
will provide useful means for 
the assessment of emerging 
scenarios for plausible crimes. 
This offers assistance in 
rapidly responding to the 
need of devising and 
deploying preventive 
measures. This talk will 
describe the important 
challenges which arise in this 
area, and which offer great 
opportunities for the 
development of intelligent 
software systems. It will focus

The SRSI protected database has the following 
advantages: (a) It can be manipulated as normal. (b) Illegal 
users are unable to differentiate between the actual and 
interference data records since the interference records have 
the same format as the real ones. (c) Legal users before using 
the database will know the original database accuracy and 
may therefore manipulate the parameters to expected lower 
accuracy in respect to the ratio of interference data records. 
(e) Only the correct parameters can be used to recover the 
original database from the protected database. 
B. Lifting database protection 
Fig. 2 shows the flow for lifting database protection. 
Steps for the process are as described below. 
 
Figure 2.  Flow for lifting database protection 
Step 1: Use the SRSI protected database PDB for the lifting 
algorithm. 
Step 2: Input the three parameters, namely, R, key and E. 
These must the original parameters to be able to 
recover the correct original database. 
Step 3: Use R to find the ratio of interference data used. 
Step 4: Use key to locate the interference data. 
Step 5: According to R and key to find the last loop used to 
generate interference data. Delete interference data. 
Step 6: Check for the number of loops to be greater than E 
in the lifting process; otherwise repeat steps 3 thru’ 5. 
After completion, PDB will be recovered to ODB. 
The number of loops completed must be E. 
Performing loops greater than E will cause more 
data records to be deleted while a smaller E leave 
some interference data in the database. An incorrect 
R will cause incorrect ratio of data records to be 
deleted while a wrong key will cause records to be 
deleted in a wrong location. 
III. EXPERIMENTAL RESULTS AND ANALYSIS 
Experimental testing will be conducted on the following 
well known classification algorithms: MySVM[8], 
LibSVM[10], CLC[11] and KNNR[12]. The experimental 
data used are taken from the UCI Machine Learning 
Repository[6] website. There are altogether 31 databases. 
The databases are classified as having two class and more 
than two class databases. There are 17 two class databases 
and 14 with more than two class databases.  
For the experimental testing, the method used to calculate 
accuracy is a modified version of k-fold CV. The database 
will be used to simulate training past database and future 
database for prediction use. The past database is the original 
database (ODB) with its accuracy before protection set as 
OA which makes use of the classification algorithm to train 
the model ODBM. The future data is used for making 
prediction. The protection accuracy PA applies the 
classification algorithm to train a model PDBM. If the future 
and the before protection databases are the same, no 
interference data is added. Previously k-fold CV is used to 
added interference to the whole database rendering useless.
