Abstract 
To avoid the damage of the high-rotational-speed shaft by the poor thermal dissipation, this 
study provides a new configuration of the 180-deg turning channel with a perforation divider. The 
perforations cross the divider let the coolant at the first duct to bypass into the second duct early. 
Such design provides a uniform cooling, preventing from the local high temperature. Additionally, 
the perforation-induced disturbing flow should enhance the total heat transfer. This work 
experimentally investigates the heat transfer characteristics. The variable parameters are the 
diameter of perforations, the angle of perforations, the number of perforations, the location of 
perforations and the Reynolds number. The results indicated that the distribution of local Nusselt 
number was much changed when the diameter of perforation was big and the angle of perforation 
was positive. Besides, the number and location of perforations did not obviously influence the total 
heat transfer. But increasing the diameter of perforations decreased the total heat transfer, especially 
at the positive angle of perforations. When the diameter of perforations was small, whose Nusselt 
number was 10% higher than the non-perforation one, the effect of the angle of perforation on heat 
transfer was insignificant.   
Keywords：Heat transfer, 180-deg turning channel, Perforation.

圖十二顯示n、L1/L、d/Dh與θ對整體熱傳
增益的影響， 0DNu 表示無穿孔時的整體平均
Nu值，結果發現穿孔數(n)與穿孔位置(L1/L) 
對整體熱傳增益的影響不顯著，這是因為當n
增大時，擾動氣流能力增加可增益熱傳，但整

熱傳，L1/L的值愈小，將使第一流道氣流愈早
有機會旁通到第二流道下游出口處，此對整體
熱傳不利，但也同時使直通道氣流被穿孔擾動
的範圍增大可增益熱傳，此外，穿孔孔徑(d/Dh)
愈大時，氣流愈容易旁通到第二流道，增強第
二流道的熱傳時也同時降低整體熱傳能力，這
在穿孔為正角度時尤為明顯、穿孔角度為0時
次之、穿孔為負角度時影響最小，但當穿孔孔
徑較小時(如d/Dh=1/4)，因穿孔流阻較大，旁
通不足，但對氣流擾動的效應反而較顯著，有
益整體熱傳，而此時穿孔角度對整體熱傳的影
響則不明顯。 
五、結論 
本研究以實驗法量測中間分隔具多個穿
孔之 180 度轉彎通道之熱傳特性，變動參數包
括穿孔孔徑、穿孔角度、穿孔孔數、穿孔位置
與雷諾數，實驗結果指出穿孔孔徑較大且穿孔
為正角度時對局部紐塞數分布影響極大，此
外，目前的實驗結果指出穿孔孔數與穿孔位置
對整體熱傳影響不明顯，而穿孔孔徑愈大會使
整體熱傳下降，尤其在穿孔為正角度時，但穿
孔孔徑較小時，整體熱傳會比無穿孔時高約
10%，且此時穿孔角度的影響不顯著。
